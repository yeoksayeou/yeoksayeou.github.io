Korean Colonial Period Periodicals

Digital Archive of Major Korean Periodicals (1920-1945)

開闢
Kaebyŏk

A leading monthly magazine founded in 1920, published until 1926. It covered literature, politics, and social issues during the early colonial period.
삼천리
Samch'ŏlli

Popular monthly magazine published from 1929 to 1941, known for its comprehensive coverage of Korean culture, society, and current events.
대동아
Taedonga

Magazine that represented the late colonial period (1940s), reflecting the complex cultural and political environment of wartime.
동광
Tonggwang

Literary and cultural magazine published from 1926 to 1933, associated with nationalist and progressive intellectual movements.
별건곤
Pyŏlgŏn'gon

Popular magazine (1926-1934) known for its eclectic mix of literature, satire, entertainment, and modern cultural content.
