**Body Text Translation:**
Prospectus for the Kaebyŏk Publishing Company Expansion Support Association

To all those who love our Kaebyŏk Publishing Company, both within the country and abroad, please rejoice together! The "Kaebyŏk Publishing Company Expansion Support Association" has been formed to expand the Kaebyŏk Publishing Company even further than it is now.

It is generally known that the Kaebyŏk Publishing Company was founded in the summer of the Kyŏngsin Year (1920) and has exerted itself day after day for the past seven years. However, in these times of daily renewal, our demands can no longer be satisfied by merely maintaining the status quo of this institution.

There is no need for lengthy words regarding our work, which proceeds solely with sincerity and determination. The only question is whether we will expand this institution, the "Kaebyŏk Publishing Company," with our heart and blood, just as we founded it with our heart and blood.

Everyone, iron is struck when it's hot, and work is done when it comes to mind. We must expand the "Kaebyŏk Publishing Company," and expand it with the material support of like-minded comrades – that is all. Comrades residing within the country and overseas, please unite your hearts and strength and become members of this association. <102>

Details of Business Expansion
1. Establishment of a Printing Press
2. Publication of Printed Materials
3. Dispatch of Mobile Reporters Domestically and Overseas
Other

Bylaws
1. This association shall be called the Kaebyŏk Publishing Company Expansion Support Association (開闢社擴張期成會).
2. The office shall be located within the Kaebyŏk Publishing Company at 88 Kyŏngun-dong, Kyŏngsŏng.
3. Members shall consist of Regular Members and Special Members; those contributing 3 Won or more shall be Regular Members, and those contributing 10 Won or more shall be Special Members.
4. Any unsettled matters not specified herein shall be decided by the Promoters' Meeting.

**Summary**

This article from the May 1926 issue of *Kaebyŏk* magazine announces the formation of the "Kaebyŏk Publishing Company Expansion Support Association." It appeals to readers domestically and abroad to contribute financially to expand the company's operations, citing seven years of effort and the need to grow beyond the status quo. The expansion plans include establishing a printing press, increasing publications, and dispatching reporters. The article details membership tiers based on donation amounts (3 Won for Regular, 10 Won for Special) and lists the names, locations (primarily from northern Korea), and contribution amounts of early donors. From a historical perspective, this document vividly illustrates the grassroots fundraising efforts supporting a prominent Korean nationalist cultural and intellectual enterprise during the Japanese colonial period. The extensive list of donors, including specific amounts and locations, offers valuable data for social historians studying regional support networks, economic conditions, and participation in nationalist movements.

開闢社擴張期成會趣旨
國內에서나 國外에서나 우리 開闢社를 사랑하는 여러분은 다-가티깃버해주십시오 開闢社를 現在보다도 더한층 擴張하자는「開闢社擴張期成會」가 생겻슴니다
開闢社가 지난 庚申年녀름에 創立되여 그間七年의 歲月을 하루가티 盡瘁해온 것은 一般이다-아는바이어니와 나날이 새로워가는이에 우리의 要求는 이 機關을 現狀維持하는 것만으로는 滿足하지못하게되얏슴니다
오즉 精誠과 覺悟만으로써 나가는 우리의 일에 긴말이 必要치아니함니다다못 問題는 「開闢社」라는 이 機關을 우리의 心血로써 創設하엿슴과가티 이제  우리의 心血로써 擴張하겟는가아니하겟는가 그것임니다
여러분 쇠는 더운에 치는 것이오 일은 생각나는 에 하는 것임니다 「開闢社」를 擴張하자 擴張하되 우리의 가튼동무의 物質로써하자는 그것임니다 海內海外에게신 同志여러분 다-가티 마음과 힘을 모아 이 會의 會員이되여주십시오＜102＞

事業擴張의 內容
一, 印刷所設置
一, 印刷物刊行
一, 海內海外流動記者派遣
其他

會則
一, 本會는 開闢社擴張期成會라함
一, 事務所는 京城慶雲洞八八番地開闢社內에두기로함
一, 會員은 普通會員과 特別會員으로하되 金參圓以上을 損出하는 이 普通會員, 金拾圓以上을 損出하는 이를 特別會員이라함
一, 여긔에 나타나지이니 한 未備事項은 發起人會에서 決定함
