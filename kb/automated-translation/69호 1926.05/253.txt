**Summary**

This text is the colophon, or publication information page, for the 69th issue of the Korean magazine *Kaebyŏk*, published on May 1, 1926. It lists the key dates (printing and publication), the editor (Yi Tonhwa), publisher (Yi Tusŏng), printer (Min Yŏngsun), printing company (Taedong Printing Co., Ltd.), and publishing house (Kaebyŏksa), along with their addresses in Kyŏngsŏng (colonial Seoul). Contact information like the telephone number and postal transfer account is also provided, and it notes that reprinting is welcomed. For historians, this colophon provides valuable data points about the publishing infrastructure and personnel associated with a significant Korean intellectual journal during the Japanese colonial period. The specific addresses within Kyŏngsŏng and the mix of Korean names with Japanese era names (Taishō) illustrate the operational context of Korean cultural production under colonial rule.
