**Company Announcement**

**Chŏnju Branch Newly Established**
Previously, the Chŏnju Sub-branch (全州分社) was managed by Mr. Kim Hae-ch'ŏn (金海天), but now a Chŏnju Branch (全州支社) has been newly established. Under the management of the persons listed below, various affairs concerning reader recruitment and advertisement handling will be expanded. We earnestly hope for your increased patronage and support as before.
Location: Building No. 9, Ponch'ŏng Ich'ome (本町 二丁目 - Honmachi 2-chōme), Chŏnju (全州)
Area: The entire Chŏnju County (全州郡) and its vicinity
Staff:
Branch Manager: Pak T'ae-hwa (朴泰華)
Reporter: Yu Po-gyŏng (柳寶敬)
Reporter: Sin Si-ch'ŏl (申時澈)
Reporter: Kim Un-yŏng (金雲英)

**Hoeryŏng Sub-branch Newly Established**
Location: Il-tong, Hoeryŏng-myŏn (會寧面), Hoeryŏng County (會寧郡)
Area: The entire Hoeryŏng County (會寧郡)
Staff:
Sub-branch Manager: Sim Pyŏng-gi (沈柄基)
Reporter: Im Pyŏng-ho (林秉昊)

**Taegu Branch Staff Dismissed**
General Affairs Manager & Reporter: Kang Sin-uk (姜信旭)
Reporter: Sin Ch'ang-ho (申昌浩)
Clerk: Yi Wŏn-ho (李源浩) <64>

**Summary**

This company announcement from the magazine *Kaebyŏk* details organizational changes. It announces the establishment of a new branch office in Chŏnju, upgrading from a sub-branch, and lists its location, coverage area, and staff. It also announces the creation of a new sub-branch in Hoeryŏng, providing similar details. Finally, it lists three staff members who have been dismissed from the Taegu branch. For historians, this article provides concrete evidence of the magazine's operational expansion and regional network development (Chŏnju, Hoeryŏng, Taegu) during the colonial period. The specific names of personnel and the use of Japanese-style addresses like "Ponch'ŏng Ich'ome" (Honmachi 2-chōme) reflect the administrative reality of the time and show a Korean cultural entity functioning within that system.
