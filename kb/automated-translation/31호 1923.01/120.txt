**Body Text Translation**
Basic Survey of Korean Culture, Publication of Each Province Issue

It is good for us to have a movement with deeper grounds, and it is good for us to have endeavors filled with more sincerity. But is this something to be wished for through coercion?

Whenever we open our mouths or lift our pens, we all say together that we are doing it for Chosŏn (朝鮮), that we are doing it for society. But can society or the nation, which is not one's own single body nor one's single household, be served so easily?

The person who serves a household best, better than anyone else in the world, is the head of that household. This is because only the head of the household knows the circumstances of that house best. One cannot be the person who is most moved without being the person who knows best, and one cannot be the person who serves best without being the person who is most moved. Believing this alone to be the true word (眞言) would likely not be wrong.

Under such thoughts, we now intend to implement this task. That is, we intend to fundamentally survey the general phenomena of Chosŏn and make the findings public to our brethren. This will involve surveying one province per month, and the findings of that survey will be made public as a supplement to the 『Kaebyŏk』(開闢) of the following month. <102> What special meaning could there be? It is to impress the current circumstances of Chosŏn as they are upon the minds of anyone who is a blood descendant of Chosŏn, thereby making all of us now clearly the head of the household (戶主) of Chosŏn, and making each one a person who marries Chosŏn.

The standards by which we will actually conduct the survey in each province are:
The state of human feelings and customs (人情風俗), the condition of industry and education (産業敎育), the causes and trends (原因 及 趨向) of various social problems (諸社會問題), introduction and critique (紹介 及 批評) of central figures (中心人物) and major business organizations (主要事業機關), exploration of famous sites and historic remains (名勝古蹟) and legends (傳說), observation and critique concerning other general conditions (其他의 一般狀勢).

We will generally conduct the actual survey of each province and each county under standards like those above, starting on the 1st day of the coming spring month of March by revealing the actual conditions of Kyŏngsangnam-do (慶尙南道). Following that issue, we plan to proceed to Kyŏngsangbuk-do (慶尙北道) and Chŏllanam-do (全羅南道), completing the survey covering the entirety of Chosŏn by March 1st of the following year, Kapcha (甲子 - 1924). Detailed matters regarding this will be announced again according to the circumstances.

First day of Kyehae (癸亥 - 1923)
Kaebyŏk-sa (開闢社) <103>
<102-103>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the January 1923 issue of *Kaebyŏk* magazine announces an ambitious project by the publisher, Kaebyŏk-sa. They plan to conduct a comprehensive, province-by-province survey of Korea (Chosŏn) covering social customs, industry, education, social issues, key figures and organizations, historical sites, and legends. The findings for each province were to be published monthly as a supplement to the magazine, beginning with Kyŏngsangnam-do in March 1923 and concluding in March 1924. The stated goal is to foster a deeper, more informed understanding and emotional connection to Korea's reality among its people, likening this connection to being the "head of the household" or "marrying" Chosŏn. For historians, this announcement is significant as it reveals a major effort by Korean intellectuals during the colonial period to systematically document and analyze contemporary Korean society and culture, independent of official Japanese colonial narratives. It reflects a desire for self-understanding and national consciousness-building through empirical investigation, highlighting the cultural nationalist activities undertaken within the constraints of colonial rule. The detailed scope of the planned survey provides insight into what aspects of Korean life were considered most crucial by intellectuals of the time.

朝鮮文化의 基本調査, 各道道號의 刊行
더 좀 근거 깁흔 운동이 우리에게 잇서 조코 더 좀 성의 넘치는 作事가 우리에게 잇서 좃타. 그러나 이것이 억지로써 바랄 일일가.
우리는 입만 열면, 붓만 들면 다―가티 가로되 조선을 위한다 하며 사회를 위하노라 한다. 그러나 자기의 한몸도 아니오 또 한 집도 아닌 사회나 민족이 그러케 쉽게 위해질 것인가.
한 집을 위하는 사람은 천하의 어떠한 사람보다도 그 집의 家長된 그 한 사람이 가장 잘 그 집을 위한다. 이것은 家長된 그 사람 뿐이 그 집의 情形을 가장 잘 아는 故이다. 가장 잘 아는 사람이 아니고 가장 잘 감동되는 사람이 될 수 업스며 가장 잘 감동된 사람이 아니고 가장 위하는 사람이 될 수 업는 것이다. 이 것뿐은 眞言이라 미더도 잘못은 아니겟지.
우리는 지금 그러한 생각 미테서 이러한 일을 시행하기로 한다. 즉 조선의 일반현상을 근본적으로 답사하야써 그 소득을 형제에게 공개하기로 한다. 이것은 한 달에 한 道를 답사할지오 답사한 그것은 그 翌月號의 『開闢』에 부록으로써 공개할 것이다.＜102＞ 무슨 別한 뜻이 잇스리오. 조선의 血孫된 누구의 머리에나 조선의 今日情形을 그대로 감명케 하야써 우리들 일반 이제 各히 분명한 조선의 戶主되게 하자 함이며 제각히 조선과 결혼하는 者 되게 하자 함이다.
우리가 各道에 잇서 실지로 답사할 표준은
人情風俗의 如何 産業敎育의 狀態  諸社會問題의 原因 及 趨向 中心人物 及 主要事業機關의 紹介 及 批評 名勝古蹟 及 傳說의 探査  其他의 一般狀勢에 관한 觀察과 批評
우리는 대체로 右와 가튼 표준의 미테서 各道 各郡의 실지답사를 행하되 오는 春 3월의 제 1일로써 먼저 慶尙南道의 실황을 表出할지며 다시 號를 딸아 慶尙北道, 全羅南道로 及하야 明年 甲子의 3월 1일로써 조선전면에 대한 답사를 종료할 계획이라. 이에 대한 상세한 사항은 형편을 딸하 다시 발표하리라.
癸亥 첫날
開闢社 ＜103＞
