기사제목 着着 進行되는 朝鮮 文化의 基本 調査!! (The Basic Survey of Korean Culture Progressing Steadily!!)
기사형태 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editorial Postscript)

Steadily Progressing Basic Survey of Korean Culture!!

Wherein lies the intention of Kaebyŏk Magazine in daringly undertaking this great project now, disregarding economic difficulties, lack of effort, and all other obstacles!

In all the world, there is no ignorance like not knowing one's own affairs while knowing others', and even more ignorant is the person who lives without knowing the state of their own livelihood. Look! How many among us Korean people truly know the state of our own livelihood, which is the situation of Korea? There are many among us who know others' affairs well but are relatively ignorant of our own, and many who critique others' livelihoods well but do not know how their own is faring. Deploring this point greatly, we have undertaken the Basic Survey of Korean Culture as a new project this year, and through this, we decided to publish provincial issues for each province. This is purely for Koreans to understand Korea well, and solely for Koreans to know the state of their own livelihood well and gain the wisdom to manage and organize it with their own hands.

However, providing the guidance and direction to enable Koreans to possess the knowledge to understand Korea well is by no means an easy task. To know well the livelihood of a territory called three thousand *ri* and a population called thirty million, one must have a long period of one to two years. Therefore, we decided to divide Korea into 13 provinces and survey the content of these 13 provinces over 13 months.

Starting from March 1st, we finished Kyŏngsang North and South Provinces and P'yŏngan North Province, and now in August, we will move to Kangwŏn and P'yŏngan South Provinces, sequentially covering the entire land of Korea. May the prominent figures of all provinces strive to meticulously arrange the materials and their substance. And wait for what kind of descriptions and judgments of right and wrong will appear on the pages of "Kaebyŏk" or "Puin." Thus, may our general brothers and sisters rejoice that through these efforts today, they will, for the first time, come to know their own livelihood in detail, and thereby engrave the entirety of Korea in our minds. The standards by which we are now conducting field surveys in each province are:

Causes and Trends of Various Social Problems
Introduction and Critique of Central Figures and Major Business/Institutions
The Actual State of Human Feelings and Customs
Conditions of Industry, Education, and Religion
Exploration of Scenic Spots, Historical Sites, and Legends
Observation and Critique Regarding Other General Conditions

Kyŏngsŏng Kaebyŏk Company

**Summary**

Kaebyŏk Magazine announces its ambitious project: a comprehensive survey of Korean culture and conditions, province by province, over 13 months. Despite acknowledging financial and logistical hurdles, the magazine justifies this undertaking by stressing the critical importance for Koreans to understand their own "livelihood" (살림살이) – their social, economic, and cultural situation – rather than focusing only on others. The goal is to equip Koreans with the knowledge needed for self-awareness and self-management. The survey covers social issues, key figures/institutions, customs, industry, education, religion, historical sites, and general conditions. The article highlights the nationalistic undertones and the push for self-understanding within the Korean populace during the colonial period. Historians might find this significant as it reflects a conscious effort by Korean intellectuals to document and analyze their own society under Japanese rule, framing it as essential for future self-determination, despite the constraints of censorship and colonial oversight. The use of terms like "our own hands" and the focus on self-knowledge point to a desire for agency.

기사형태	사고·편집후기

着着 進行되는 朝鮮 文化의 基本 調査!!
開闢雜誌가 今此 經濟의 困難, 努力의 困備, 其他 모든 難關을 돌아보지 아니하고 敢히 이 大事業을 斷行하는 것은 그 뜻이 어대 잇나뇨!
天下의 無識이 남의 일은 알되 自己의 일을 모르는 것 만치 無識한 일이 업고 그 보다도 더 無識한 것은 自己네의 살림살이 內容이 엇지 되어 가는 것을 모르고 사는 사람가티 無識한 일이 업다. 보라 우리 朝鮮 사람이 朝鮮 形便이라 하는 自己네의 살림살이의 內容을 아는 사람이 얼마나 되는가? 우리는 남의 일은 잘 알되 自己의 일은 比較的 모르는 사람이 만흐며 남의 살림살이는 잘 批評하되 自己의 살림살이는 어찌 되어 가는지 모르는 사람이 만흐다. 우리는 이 點을 甚히 慨嘆하게 보아 今年 新事業으로 朝鮮文化의 基本調査에 着手하며 니여써 各道 道號를 刊行하기로 하얏나니 이는 純全히 朝鮮사람으로 朝鮮을 잘 理解하자는데 잇스며 朝鮮 사람으로 自己네의 살림살이의 內容을 잘 알아 가지고 그를 自己네의 손으로 處辨하고 整理하는 聰明을 가지자 하는데 잇는 것 뿐이다.
그러나 朝鮮 사람으로 朝鮮을 잘 理解하는 知識을 가지게 할만한 指導와 方針은 決코 容易할 일이 아니다. 적어도 三千里라 하는 疆土와 三千萬이라 하는 食口의 살림살이를 잘 알고저 하면 1年 乃至 2年의 長歲月을 가지지 아니하면 안될 것이다. 그래서 우리는 朝鮮을 13道에 논하 가지고 13個月에 13道의 內容을 調査하기로 하얏다.
지난 3月 1日로부터 첫번 始作하야 慶尙南北道와 平北을 맛치고 今 8月에는 江原, 平南 兩道로 옴기여 順次로 朝鮮 全 土에 及할 것이다. 全道 人士는 奮發하야 材料와 品質을 一一히 整頓하라. 그리고 어떠한 形容과 어떠한 是非曲直이 「開闢」또는 「婦人」의 紙上에 나타날 것을 기다리라. 그래서 우리 一般 兄弟들은 今日의 此等에 因하야 처음으로 自己네의 살림살이를 仔細히 알게 되며 그리하야 朝鮮의 全般을 우리들 머리 속에 銘記하게 될 것을 깃버하라. 지금 우리가 各道를 踏査하는 標準은
諸 社會 問題의 原因 及 趨向 中心人物 及 主要事業機關의 紹介 及 批評 人情 風俗의 實際 如何 産業 敎育 及 宗敎의 狀況 名勝古蹟 及 傳說의 探査 其他 一般 狀勢에 關한 觀察과 批評
