餘滴 (Postscript Drops)

This month's issue is the commemorative issue marking the full 3rd anniversary since the founding of this magazine. According to our initial plans, we intended to achieve unprecedented novelty beyond regular standards to repay the kind favor of readership from all our readers under heaven. However, as it is said that good deeds encounter many obstacles, our company's chief editor, Mr. Kim Kich'an (金起瀍), developed a poisonous boil (毒瘇) on his right thumb, his sole weapon, and being in the midst of suffering, he had no leeway to oversee the editing. Furthermore, other staff members were away on regional business trips, causing numerous difficulties. Please generously forgive the meagerness of the content, and we intend to apply even greater effort in the next issue to compensate for the shortcomings of this one (桑楡의 失).

Regarding the critique of newspapers and magazines within the article, as this is the first time such a thing has occurred in our lonely journalistic circle (我操弧界), it is not entirely absent that the contributor's words may have transgressed the line of critique and committed an offense of attack. However, even an attack might serve as material for reflection for the one attacked or for third parties, we don't know. Also, rather than harboring discontent behind the scenes, cursing and slandering each other with rumors and street talk (流言巷說), and misunderstanding through exaggeration (針小棒大), we thought it would be more beneficial—whether for the newspapers and magazines themselves or for the public society—to openly present this to the general public and let the readers judge the rights and wrongs for themselves. Therefore, we have resolutely published the contributor's words as they were, and we especially hope for your understanding on this point.

Furthermore, the "Critique of Current Cultural and Social Movements" could not be included in this issue due to lack of space, for which we offer a word of apology to the contributors. This magazine, not limited to this issue, will henceforth welcome any proper critique on any issue.

We intended to publish the latter part of our Kyŏngbuk Province (慶北道) special issue in this month's issue, but not only were the investigations incomplete, but especially because this month's issue is a commemorative expanded issue, the space did not permit it, so it has been unavoidably postponed to next month's issue.

**Summary**

This editorial postscript apologizes for the shortcomings of the magazine's 3rd-anniversary issue, attributing the thin content to the chief editor's illness and staff absences. It defends the decision to publish a potentially controversial critique of other publications, arguing that open discussion, even if critical, is preferable to hidden gossip and serves the public good. The editors also announce the postponement of articles on cultural/social movements and the conclusion of the Gyeongbuk Province special due to space constraints. For historians, the article offers insight into the operational challenges (staffing, illness affecting production) faced by Korean periodicals in the 1920s. It also highlights the burgeoning, albeit perhaps contentious, media landscape where critiques between publications were emerging, and the editorial stance favoring public debate over private slander within the Korean intellectual community under colonial rule.

餘滴
今月號는 本誌 창간후 만 3주년되는 기념호이올시다. 처음에 예정까지는 規例없는 嶄新을 極하야 만천하 독자제씨의 애독하시는 호의를 酬코자 하얏나이다. 그러나 好事는 魔가 多하다고 본사 주간 金起瀍군이 그의 유일한 무기인 右手母指에 毒瘇이 발생되야 呌苦中에 在함으로 편집을 돌아볼 여지가 업섯고 기타 사원도 지방출장 중에 在하야 다대의 곤란이 잇섯나이다. 내용의 빈약함은 물론 厚恕하시려니와 來號에는 今號보다 일층 노력을 가하야 桑楡의 失을 補코자 하나이다.
기사중 신문잡지에 대한 비판은 적막한 我操弧界에 在하야 처음으로 발생된 사실인 고로 투고하신 이의 언론이 혹은 비판의 선을 脫하야 공격에 犯한 점이 不無함니다. 그러나 공격도 공격을 바든 그이나 제삼자에 대하야 혹은 각성의 재료가 될지도 不知하겟고 또한 이면에 불평을 抱하고 流言巷說로 호상 저주하며 호상 비난하야 針小棒大의 오해하는 것보다는 차라리 이것을 일반에게 공개하야 독자로 하야금 시비를 自判하게 하는 것이 신문잡지자체에 대하야서던지 사회공중에 대하야서던지 유익할가 하야 투고하신 이의 언론 그대로 감연이 기재한 것이니 此点을 특히 양해하시기를 바라나이다.
그리고 「현하의 문화운동 及 사회운동에 대한 비평」은 지면이 불허하는 관계로 今號에 기재치못하게 되얏슴으로 투고하신 제씨에게 一言으로 謝하나이다. 本誌는 今號에 한하지 안이하고 이후부터는 何문제에 대하야서던지 정당한 비판이면 환영하겟습니다.
본지 慶北道號의 下는 今月號에 발표코자 하얏사오나 조사에 미비한 것이 잇슬뿐 안이라 특히 今月號는 紀念增大號인 고로 지면이 許치 안이하야 부득이 來月號로 연기되얏나이다.
