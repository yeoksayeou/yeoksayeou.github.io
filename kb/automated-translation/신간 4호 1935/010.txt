Kaebyŏk Associate System Great Expansion

The purpose of our Kaebyŏk Company (開闢社) is not merely the publication and sale of ordinary magazines and books. To achieve a more intimate connection between people who share the same aspirations and, beneath that, to obtain more substantial results, we established the Associate (社友) System in October 1922 (壬戌), the third anniversary of our founding [Note: the text incorrectly states 1920년(壬戌), but 壬戌 corresponds to 1922, which was the 3rd anniversary if founded in 1920]. We broadly sought like-minded individuals throughout the world, and over the subsequent ten-plus years (星霜), thanks to the fervent support and backing of Associates both at home and abroad, the company's fortunes have progressed daily, leading to the prosperity we see today. Now, coinciding with the publication of *Kaebyŏk*, our company is further renewing and expanding this system. We dare to commemorate the flourishing fortune (隆運) of our company and express gratitude for the goodwill of our brethren, while simultaneously planning for this system to become the sole institution that brings about (齋來) great achievements in our cultural movement. If, fortunately, motivated individuals everywhere respond to this, reaping extremely practical results under an extremely simple method, how could this benefit only the relationship between the Company and its Associates, or the Associates and the Company? Herein, we solely (애오라지) present the details and dare to beg for the endorsement of the public's earnest intent (大方篤志).

Associate Rules (社友規則)

Article 1: An Associate refers to a person of earnest intent (篤志) who approves of this company's principles and objectives and maintains an organic (有機的) connection with the company.
Article 2: Associates may request the company to investigate or devise solutions for various issues arising on the surface (波面) of contemporary society that they deem significant or special. Upon receiving such a request, the company will consider the extent and nature of the issue and respond to the request as much as possible (可及).
Article 3: Associates within the same region (e.g., 1 Prefecture (府), County (郡), etc.) may, with the joint signatures of 28 or more members, request the company to provide lectures (演說) or talks (講話) on special local issues. Upon receiving such a request, the company will respond immediately (直히).
Article 4: The company will present Associates with the *Kaebyŏk* magazine or other magazines free of charge and provide all other publications related to the company's publishing activities at actual cost (實費).
Article 5: Associates possess the basic qualification to become employees of the company headquarters, branches (支社), or sub-branches (分社).
Note: This is because a clause exists in the company's regulations stating that company employees shall be selected from among the Associates.
Article 6: Should any Associate unfortunately suffer a sudden disaster (災變), the company will provide appropriate consolation and aid (慰助). Furthermore, should an Associate's human rights be violated, the company will devise (講究) suitable protective measures (擁護策).
Article 7: Associates shall contribute (出演) 10 Won annually as a sign of agreement with the company's principles and objectives.
However, those contributing a lump sum of 100 Won or more are permanently exempt.
Article 8: Associates shall observe the progress of all business activities undertaken (施爲) by the company and may present opinions regarding business development or provide reference materials to the company at any time (何時).
Article 9: Associates shall, as much as possible (可及), investigate and consider special facts or trends arising in their surrounding society and transmit (送致) them to the company.
Article 10: The company shall, as much as possible (可及), seek (圖) the convenience of Associates at any time (何時) and respect their honor.
Article 11: Associates shall, as much as possible (可及), seek (圖) the convenience of the company at any time (何時) and respect its honor.

Appendix: Company Rules Summary (附 社規摘要)

Article 1: This company shall be called Kaebyŏk Company (開闢社).
Article 2: This company aims to pioneer (開闢) the most rational and novel path for life's development by researching, critiquing, introducing, and advocating concerning all phenomena of human society under pan-human nationalism (汎人間的民族主義).
Article 3: To achieve the objectives of Article 2, this company may publish magazines and books and undertake all activities deemed necessary for realizing the company's principles (社是).
Article 4: With the intention of socially accomplishing (期成) the objectives of Article 2, this company may separately implement an Associate (社友) system.
However, regulations concerning Associates shall be determined separately.
Article 5: This company shall establish its headquarters in Kyŏngsŏng (京城), Chosŏn (朝鮮), and establish branches (支社), sub-branches (分社), and distribution points (分賣所) in various key locations (樞要地) as needed.
Article 6: To carry out the duties of Article 3, this company shall establish administrative execution bodies such as (如) the following:
1. Editorial Bureau (編輯局)
a. Research Department (調査部) b. Politics and Economics Department (政經部)
c. Social Affairs Department (社會部) d. Academics and Arts Department (學藝部)
2. Business Bureau (營業局)
a. Accounting Department (經理部) b. Sales Department (販賣部)
c. Advertising Department (廣告部) d. Agency Department (代理部)
3. General Affairs Section (庶務課)
Article 7: This company shall have employees such as (如) the following:
a. Advisor (顧問) b. President (社長)
c. Editor-in-Chief/Manager (主幹) d. Editorial Bureau Chief (編輯局長)
e. Business Bureau Chief (營業局長) f. Chiefs of each Department (部) and Section (課)
g. Presidents and Staff of each Branch (支社) and Sub-branch (分社)
Article 8: Company employees may only be appointed from among those who are Associates.

**Summary**

This 1935 article from *Kaebyŏk* magazine announces a significant expansion and renewal of the Kaebyŏk Company's "Sawoo" (Associate) system, first established around 1922. It outlines the reciprocal relationship: Associates support the company's cultural and nationalist aims ("pan-human nationalism") financially (10 Won annually or a 100 Won lifetime fee) and intellectually (providing local information, suggestions), while receiving benefits like free magazines, discounted books, potential employment preference, mutual aid in times of disaster, and defense of their rights. The company pledges to respond to Associate requests for investigation into social issues or local lectures if enough members request it. The article includes summaries of both the Associate rules and general company regulations, detailing Kaebyŏk's structure and mission to "pioneer" societal development through publishing. From a historical perspective, the article highlights how a Korean cultural organization under Japanese colonial rule structured itself, fostering a sense of community and mutual support tied to nationalist cultural objectives. Historians might find the formalization of this associate system, blending financial contribution, intellectual participation, mutual aid, and preferential hiring, an interesting example of Korean civil society organization and nationalist expression within the constraints of the colonial period. The emphasis on mutual aid and rights protection hints at the precariousness faced by Koreans.

開闢 社友制 大擴張
우리 開闢社는 그 목적이 보통잡지 及 도서간행 판매에만 잇는 것이 아닌지라 뜻을 가티하는 우리 사람과 사람이 보다 더 친밀한 결연 그 밋헤서 보다 더 착실한 성과를 엇기 위하야 창사 제 3주년 되든 1920년(壬戌) 10월에 社友制를 창설하고 천하의 뜻 가튼 분을 넓이 구하야 이래 10여 星霜에 海內海外에 社友 諸氏의 열렬한 원조 지지하에 社運이 날노 진전되야 금일의 성황을 보게 되엿슴니다. 이제 본사는 開闢을 발간하는 동시 斯制를 더욱 갱신 확장하야 감히 본사의 隆運을 기념하며 형제의 호의를 감사하는 동시에 進하야 이로써 우리의 문화운동의 대성과를 齋來하는 유일한 기관이 되게하기를 기약하노니 다행히 도처에 有志가 이에 향응하야 극히 간단한 방식하에서 극히 실질잇는 공과를 수확하는 무엇이 된다하면 이것이 어찌 本社 對 社友 社友 對 本社의 幸益뿐을 위함이리오. 이에 애오라지 그 내용을 들어서 감히 大方篤志의 찬동을 乞함이다.

社友規則
第1條 社友라 함은 본사의 趣意, 목적을 찬성하야 본사와 유기적 연락을 취하는 篤志의 人을 謂함이다.
第2條 社友는 현 사회의 波面에 起하는 각종 문제의 중대 또는 특수하다 認할 자에 대하야는 此에 조사 혹은 해결의 방편을 본사에 의뢰할 수 잇스며 右에 의뢰를 受한 본사는 그 문제의 정도와 성질을 고찰하야써 可及의 한에서 그 의뢰에 응할 것이다.
第3條 본사는 동일지역 (예하면 1府, 郡과 여함) 내의 社友 중 28인 이상의 連名으로써 그 지방의 특수문제에 대한 연설이나 講話를 본사에 의뢰할 수 잇스며 右의 의뢰를 受한 본사는 直히 此에 응할 것이다.
第4條 본사는 社友에게 대하야 開闢잡지 혹은 기타잡지를 증정하며 其外 본사의 간행에 관한 일체의 출판물을 실비로 제공할 것이다.
第5條 社友는 본사, 支 分社의 사원될 기본 자격을 有함이라.
비고. 본사의 사규 중에 본사의 사원은 社友 중에서 선발함이라 한 조항이 有한 故이다.
第6條 본사는 社友 중에 혹 불시에 災變을 당한 이가 有할 時에는 상당한 慰助를 與하며 又  혹 社友의 인권이 유린되는 경우에는 此에 상당한 옹호책을 강구할 것이다.
第7條 社友는 본사의 趣意 목적을 찬성하는 의미로써 년 10원을 출연할 것이다.
但 일시금으로 金 100원 이상을 출연할 시는 영구 면제함.
第8條 社友는 본사의 施爲에 속한 일체사업의 진행되는 그것을 주시하야 사무의 발전에 관한 의견 혹은 참고의 자료를 何時던지 본사에 陳示 혹은 제공할 것이다.
第9條 社友는 자기의 주위 사회에 起하는 특수사실 혹은 경향에 대하야는 可及의 한에서 조사 고찰하야 본사에 송치할 것이다.
第10條 본사는 可及의 한에서 何時던지 社友에 대한 편익을 圖하고 명예를 존중할 것이다.
第11條 社友는 可及의 한에서 何時던지 본사에 대한 편익을 圖하고 명예를 존중할 것이다.
