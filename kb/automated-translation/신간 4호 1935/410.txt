Publication Date: 1935년 03월 01일
Article Title: 黎明의 作業, 『正初첫아츰』 (Work of the Dawn, "First Morning of the New Year")
Author: 李應洙
Article Type: 시 (Poem)

As the cock, fulfilling its duty on the last night of the twelfth month, finishes its crowing
On the ground of the first day of the new year, by the custom since the creation of heaven and earth
The younger sibling of the coastline, the boundary between sea and land (陸)
The dawn, the boundary line between night and day, walks forth again

All things, buried in the black light during the night
Having made a "cocktail" of the black universe
Each separates its self from chaos again <26>
And erects its true form towering upon the ground;
Following the sunlight surging up in the east
They busily weave their images, their shadows again

Behold, the fields and mountains, even on this morning of the new year's first day
Are adorned with the splendid and complex furnishings and decorations
Of this daily, familiar, inexplicable
Scarecrow, riddle, designs, and embroidery.

I too open the window and go out under the blue sky
Drinking deeply of the fresh air
In this morning's work that creates these two images
I participate as a duly qualified "member" (一員). -(End)- <27>
<26-27>

**Summary**

The poem by Yi Ŭngsu describes the arrival of dawn on New Year's Day, portraying it as a daily act of creation where the world emerges from darkness. The poet observes nature taking shape in the morning light and feels a sense of participation in this renewal by stepping outside. For historians, the poem offers a glimpse into the expression of cyclical renewal and individual connection to nature during the colonial period. The blend of Hanja and older Korean orthography, along with the theme of finding one's place in the "morning's work," might reflect subtle undercurrents of continuity and participation despite the political context.

섯달 금음 밤 천직을 다하는 닭이 홰우름 다하매
새해 첫날의 따우에는 천지개벽 이래의 관습
바다와 묻(陸)의 境界線인 해안의 동생
밤과 낮의 境界線인 려명이 또 걸어오도다

밤동안 검은 빛속에 무치여
흑색 우주의 『칵텔』을 맨들엇든 萬物은
다시 제가끔 혼돈에서 我를 떼여 ＜26＞
웃뚝웃뚝 따우에 實像을 세우드니
동켠에 불끈 솟는 태양빛 달아
다시 분주이들 그 映像 그름자를 짖다

보라 드울과 산은 오날 새해의 첫날 아츰도
매일의 낫익은 이 설명못할
허재비와 수수꺽기의 두문이와 자수로
찬란하고 복잡한 설비음 장식을 하엿고나.

나도 창문을 열고 푸른 하늘 아래 나가
신선한 공기를 한껏 마시며
二像을 맨드는 이 아츰 작업에
당당이 자격잇는 『一員』으로 참가하도다. -(끗)- ＜27＞
