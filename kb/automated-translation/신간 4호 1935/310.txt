France - Money Spent by Travelers
-France - Money Spent by Travelers

According to a recent investigation announced by the French Ministry of Finance (大藏省), the total amount of money left behind in the country each year by the various gentlemen, young ladies, madams, and young sirs—foreigners (에트란제) from all over the world who visit France, starting with the flowery Paris (巴里)—is approximately one billion francs.

This is the total sum that 500,000 foreigners (에트란제) and their families generously sprinkle like sweet dew (甘露) in France, averaging two thousand francs per person.

So, into which sectors of France does this huge amount of money flow?

While no other country's Ministry of Finance investigates such things, according to our investigation, first, various procedural fees, taxes, etc., that go into the hands of the government account for 40%, approximately four hundred million francs. Next, related to ■■ships (船舶), ■■ etc., that is, transportation facilities, is twenty to twenty-five percent. Following that, rent for hotels, restaurants (레스트랜), etc., and food expenses also account for twenty to twenty-five percent, generally between two hundred million and two hundred fifty million francs. Lastly, for the guests' purchases, sightseeing, amusement, etc., that is, flowing into the citizens' ■■rooms (■ㅅ방), casinos (카지노), and theaters (극장), is approximately [one hundred million?] to twenty percent, with money pouring in between one hundred million and two hundred million francs.<83>

**Summary**

This 1935 article reports on findings by the French Ministry of Finance regarding the economic impact of foreign tourists. It states that approximately 500,000 visitors spend a total of one billion francs annually (averaging 2,000 francs each). The article breaks down this spending: 40% goes to government fees and taxes, 20-25% to transportation, 20-25% to accommodation and food, and 10-20% to shopping, sightseeing, and entertainment like casinos and theaters. For historians studying Korean colonial history, this article is interesting as it demonstrates the type of international news (specifically European economic and social details) being disseminated to a Korean audience during Japanese rule. It reflects an awareness of global tourism and its economic significance, potentially serving informational or even aspirational purposes for readers in colonial Korea, contrasting the situation in occupied Korea with the perceived glamour and economic activity of a major European nation.

佛蘭西- 旅行者의 흘리는 돈
-佛蘭西- 旅行者의 흘리는 돈
最近 佛蘭西 大藏省이 發表한 調査에 依하면 年年 꽃의 巴里를 筆頭로 佛蘭西를 訪問하는 世界 各國의 에트란제 諸君 孃 令夫人 令息이 이 나라에 흘리고 가는 돈은 總額 約 十億프랑이다.
이것은 五十萬人의 에트란제 及 그 家族이 이만한 甘露를 앗김없이 佛蘭西에 뿌려놋는 總額이니까 一人이 平均 二千프랑씩이 된다.
그러면 이 巨額의 돈이 佛蘭西의 엇더한 方面으로 흘러가는가?
엇더한 나라의 大藏省도 이런 것은 調査 안하지만 우리의 調査에 依하면 위선 政府의 手中으로 드러가는 各種의 手續費, 稅 等이 四○% 約 四億프랑. 다음은 ■■船舶, ■■ 등 卽 交通機關의 關係로 二十에서 二五%. 또 그 다음으로 호텔, 레스트랜 等의 집세 食費가 亦是 二○에서 二五% 大槪 二億과 二億五千프랑 사이 最後로 客들의 買品, 구경, 遊興 等, 卽 市民諸君의 ■ㅅ방으로 카지노로 劇場으로 約 一■■에서 二○% 以內■ 一億과 二億프랑 사이의 돈이 쏘다지는 것이다.＜83＞
