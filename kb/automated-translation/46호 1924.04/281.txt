祝 開闢忠南道號 發行
Congratulations on the Publication of the Kaebyŏk Ch'ungnam Province Special Issue

瑞山郡 泰安面
Sŏsan County, T'aean Township

李基升
Yi Ki-sŭng

**Summary**

This is a brief congratulatory message from Yi Ki-sŭng, residing in T'aean-myŏn, Sŏsan-gun, celebrating the publication of a special issue of the magazine *Kaebyŏk* dedicated to Ch'ungch'ŏngnam-do Province. The message itself is simply the title repeated, followed by the sender's location and name. For historians, this short piece indicates the magazine's effort to engage regional audiences by producing special provincial issues and demonstrates reader participation and regional identity affirmation within the context of colonial Korea.
