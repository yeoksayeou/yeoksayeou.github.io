Afterword

Kaebyŏk April World Turbulence Issue (世界風雲號)! Just seeing the title makes it seem like winds are rising from all directions. But how is the content? Truthfully, sitting in the tiny Kaebyŏk editorial office in a corner of Seoul, it is indeed difficult work to see, hear, send, and entrust each and every aspect of the world's turbulence (風雲). We can only ask for your understanding on that point.

Due to limited page space (紙面), setting aside the World Turbulence theme to put out a South Ch'ungch'ŏng Province Issue (忠南道號) and publishing Spring Musings (陽春隨想) is, in fact, difficult due to lack of space. Therefore, regrettably, we could not proceed as previously announced.

It is particularly regrettable that we could not publish Mr. O P'a's (O波氏) "Scientific Communism and Anarchism" and "Activities of Koreans Residing Abroad (해외에 在한 조선인의 활동)." It is also extremely unfortunate that we could not publish the precious manuscripts (玉稿) from various writers as planned, and that the contributions received from Messrs. Sin T'aeak (辛泰嶽), Yi Unhyŏk (李雲赫), and Yi Sŏnghwan (李晟煥) also could not be published. We are deeply sorry.

As announced, the May issue will be presented to you as the Kyŏnggi Province Issue (京畿道號). Please wait and see how it turns out. And another word we humbly ask about is how this magazine (本誌) has become rather frequent/crowded (번잣게) since the start of this year. As you know, perhaps this is the reason (까달인가) why worldly affairs (世事) are so troublesome (多煩)? Even after increasing the page count, there are still many points of regret (遺憾處). Please understand.

One more thing to add: the prediction contest (豫言募集) for the Kapcha (甲子) year, which was announced in this magazine's (本誌) New Year issue, cannot have its results announced due to circumstances (형편). Regarding the circumstances (形便), we believe you can likely infer (斟酌) the reason. <71>

**Summary**

This editor's postscript from the April 1924 issue of *Kaebyŏk* magazine apologizes to readers for deviating from the announced "World Turbulence" theme and for being unable to publish several planned articles. These included features on South Ch'ungch'ŏng Province, spring essays, an article on communism and anarchism by "O P'a-ssi," a piece on overseas Koreans' activities, and submissions from three named writers, all primarily due to space constraints. The editor also notes the magazine has become busier or more crowded since the year began and announces the postponement of the results of a New Year's prediction contest due to unspecified "circumstances." For historians, this piece reveals the practical challenges (limited space, possibly resources) faced by a Korean cultural magazine under colonial rule and hints strongly at external pressures or censorship ("circumstances" preventing the publication of prediction contest results, cancellation of articles on sensitive political topics like communism/anarchism and overseas Korean activities).

뒷말슴
開闢 4월 世界風雲號! 題號만 보아도 四圍에서 바람이 이는 듯 함니다. 그러나 내용이 엇덜넌지요. 사실이지 서울 한귀퉁이 조고마한 개벽 편집실에 안저서 세계의 風雲을 일일이 보고듯고 보내고 맛기는 실로 어려운 일이웨다. 그쯤 諒解해 주실 밧게 업구요.
적은 紙面에 世界風雲을 버리고 忠南道號를 내놋코 陽春隨想을 실어 놋키는 사실 지면이 부족됨니다. 그래서 유감이나마 예고대로는 못 되얏슴니다.
특히 O波氏의 「과학적 공산주의와 무정부주의」를 못 싯게 된 것과 「해외에 在한 조선인의 활동」을 못 싯게 된 것이 더욱 유감이 오며 文人諸氏의 玉稿도 예정대로 못실니고 辛泰嶽
李雲赫
李晟煥 세분의 주신 것도 못 실리게 된 것이 極키 미안함니다.
예고함과 가티 5월호는 京畿道號로써 여러분께 보일 터이외다. 엇지 될넌지 기다려 주십시요. 그리고 한말슴 다시 엿줍는 것은 금년부터의 本誌가 番잣게 된 그것이외다. 아시는 바와 가티 世事가 多煩한 그 까달인가 함니다. 지면을 늘려도 오히려 遺憾處가 만슴니다 그려. 양해해 주십시요.
한말슴 더 할 것은 本誌 신년호에 예고하얏든 甲子 1년에 대한 豫言募集은 형편에 의하야 발표를 못하게 되얏슴니다. 形便이라면 斟酌하실듯 함니다.＜71＞
