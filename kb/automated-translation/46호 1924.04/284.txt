**Summary**

This short piece is a congratulatory announcement from the staff of the T'aean Youth Association in Sŏsan-gun, T'aean-myŏn, celebrating the publication of a special issue of the magazine *Kaebyŏk* focused on Ch'ungch'ŏngnam-do province. Historically, it highlights the engagement of local youth organizations with national-level cultural publications like *Kaebyŏk* during the Japanese colonial period. It demonstrates the magazine's practice of dedicating special issues to specific regions and the local pride or interest this generated, offering a glimpse into regional consciousness and civil society activity at the time.
