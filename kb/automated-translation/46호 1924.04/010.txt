기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial/Editor's Postscript)

事必歸正 (Things inevitably return to the right path / Justice will prevail)

"Let's know the world" – this is what we previously asserted as the first thesis in the inaugural issue, Issue 1, of this magazine. The reason is that if we do not first systematically understand the state of this world, and especially if we do not recognize the historical turning point of this world, which twists and turns moment by moment, we will inevitably repeat errors akin to scratching where it doesn't itch or blowing the trumpet after the procession has passed. In other words, it is because our precious, precious efforts would be misplaced.

The Great War in Europe, where the powerful cliques of nobles, royals, soldiers, bureaucrats (the traditional types), and the plutocrats such as company presidents, bank presidents, and shop owners (the newly emerging class) colluded, mindlessly exploiting the flesh and blood of the populace, suddenly transformed into domestic revolutionary movements and social movements towards its end. Revolutions in Russia, followed by Hungary, Austria, and Germany, especially the victorious advance of the Russian Red Army in 1920, gave the sense that all of Europe was being revolutionized.

However, is the state of the world not simple, and is reaction easy to occur? Just as the high tide of revolution dyed the world red during 1920, it began a slight retreat from 1921. Entering 1922, reactionary forces suddenly surged greatly: Hungary turned white (counter-revolution), Germany hesitated, the Conservative Party in Britain, the National Fascist Party (國粹黨) in Italy, the Republican Party in America, and the Revanchist Faction (復讎派) in France each displayed their power. It seemed as if social movements and revolutionary movements were beginning a general retreat across almost the entire front.

However, reaction is reaction. It is not something of creation. As long as it is not something of creation, no matter how strong its power may seem, it will eventually disappear. From the latter half of 1923, that is, last year, reactionary forces have shown a marked tendency to diminish, and the movement of the naked human being, the social movement, flies a new banner of resurgence (捲土重來). Events such as the formation of the Labour Party cabinet in Britain, the Farmer-Labor movement in America, and the preparations for the Second Revolution in Guangdong, China, could perhaps be called examples that speak of the world's return to the right path (歸正).

But one must not misunderstand. When we say this, we absolutely do not mean that the world's return to the right path will happen in the time it takes for idle talk. Truly, we must be prepared for the final struggle that will renew the fate of this world lying ahead. That reactionary spoiled child (驕兒) – France – becoming the cause of the Balkanization of all of Europe, and the troubles of the Eastern nations facing Russia, the kingdom of ideology – what indeed could these signify? <1>

**Summary**

This editorial from 1924 reflects on the ebb and flow of revolutionary and reactionary forces in the world following World War I. It notes the initial surge of revolutions (Russia, Central Europe) inspired by socialist ideals, followed by a strong reactionary backlash around 1922 (Hungary, Italy, UK, US, France). However, the author sees signs of a progressive resurgence in late 1923, citing the UK Labour government, US Farmer-Labor movement, and developments in China as evidence that things are "returning to the right path" (事必歸正). Nevertheless, it warns that the ultimate struggle is yet to come, hinting at future conflicts involving France and Russia. For historians, this piece is interesting as it reveals Korean intellectuals' close observation of global political currents (revolution, reaction, socialism) and their interpretation of these events through a lens of hope for eventual justice and potentially, national liberation. It shows an internationalist outlook and a belief in historical progress despite the constraints of colonial rule.

기사형태	사고·편집후기

事必歸正
『세계를 알쟈』 - 이것은, 우리가 일즉히, 본지 創刊 제1호에 첫 論文으로써 주장한 것이다. 그 까닭은, 우리가 몬져, 이 세계의 되여가는 꼴을, 체계적으로 알지 못하면, 더욱히 순간 순간으로 뒤틀녀가는, 이 세계의, 歷史的轉機를 인식하지 못하면, 우리는 속졀업시, 가렵지도 아니한 곳에 손을 대이거나, 또 혹은 행차뒤에 나팔을 불음과도 가튼 오류를 반복하겟는 故이다. 다시 말하면, 우리의 그 艱貴, 艱貴한 노력을 잘못함이 되겟는 故이다.
귀족, 왕족, 군인, 관료 등(在來種의) 權閥과 會社長, 銀行長, 商店主 등(新興級)의 재벌이 서로 姦을 通하야, 속졀업시 민중의 혈육을 取盡하던  歐洲의 大戰은, 그 말기에 니르러, 문득 국내의 革命運動, 社會運動으로 轉化하야, 露西亞를 爲始한 凶牙利, 奧太利, 獨逸의 혁명, 특히 1920년, 露西亞 赤軍의 乘勝長驅는, 全歐羅巴를 혁명화하는 感이 잇섯다.
그러나 단순치 아니한 것은 世相이오, 닐기 쉬은 것은 反動인가. 그와 가티 1920年間을 赤染하던 혁명의 滿潮도, 21년 으로브터 적히 退轉을 試하더니, 22년에 入한즉, 反動勢力은 홀연 大振하야, 凶牙利는 白化하고, 獨逸은 躕躇하고, 英國에는 保守黨, 伊國에는 國粹黨, 米國에는 共和黨, 佛國에는 復讎派가 제각히 세력을 발휘하야, 社會運動, 革命運動은 거희 全線에 亙한 總退刻을 始함과 如한 觀이 업지 아낫다.
그러나, 反動은 反動이다. 創造의 무엇은 아니다. 創造의 무엇이 아닌 以上, 그 세력은 如何히 강대하다 할지라도, 필경은 소실되는 것이다. 1923년, 즉 작년의 하반기로부터, 反動勢力은 현저히 소실되는 경향을 呈하며, 赤裸裸의 인간운동, 社運운동은 捲土重來의 새 旗幟를 날닌다. 英國의 勞働黨組閣, 米國의 勞農聯合運動, 中國廣東의 第二革命準備와 가튼 일은, 천하의 歸正을 말하는, 한, 例證이라고나 할가.
그러나 오해하여서는 안된다. 우리가 이러케 말함은, 결코, 천하의 歸正이 談笑間에서 되리라함은 아니다. 진실로 이 세계의 운명을 更新할 최후의 鬪爭은 이 압헤 잇슬 것을 覺悟하지 아나서는 안될 것이다. 뎌 反動的 驕兒 - 佛國을 火因으로 한 全歐洲의 巴爾幹化와 또 主義의 왕국인 露西亞를 對手로 한 東方諸國의 多事는 과연 무엇을 의미함일고. ＜1＞
