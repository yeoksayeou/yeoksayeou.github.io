Magazine Title:	개벽 제46호
Publication Date:	1924년 04월 01일
Article Title:	謹告 (Public Notice/Announcement)
Author:	車相瓚 (via 京城開闢社)
Article Type:	사고·편집후기 (Announcement/Editorial Note)

Regarding my recent business trip to the South Ch'ungch'ŏng Province region (忠南地方) for a Cultural Foundation Survey (文化基本調査), I received great support and care from the various gentlemen (諸氏) of that place. I hereby express my gratitude.

April, Year of Kapcha (1924)
Kyŏngsŏng Kaebyŏk Sa (京城開闢社)
Ch'a Sangch'an (車相瓚)

**Summary**

Ch'a Sangch'an, representing the Kyŏngsŏng (Seoul) office of Kaebyŏk Sa (the publisher of Kaebyŏk magazine), formally thanks the people ("gentlemen") of the South Ch'ungch'ŏng Province region for their significant support during his recent business trip there. The purpose of the trip was a "Cultural Foundation Survey." This brief notice highlights the activities of Korean cultural organizations like Kaebyŏk Sa during the colonial period, specifically their efforts in conducting regional surveys potentially related to documenting Korean culture or society. For historians, the mention of a "Cultural Foundation Survey" by a prominent nationalist magazine like Kaebyŏk in 1924 is noteworthy, suggesting organized efforts to understand and preserve Korean culture under Japanese rule. The formal language and use of Hanja reflect the writing style of the era.
