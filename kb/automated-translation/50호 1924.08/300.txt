Leftover Words

They say tomorrow or the day after is *Ch'obok* (初伏, the first of the three hottest days of summer). We have truly entered the *Sambok* season (三伏時節). One cannot avoid fighting the heat and sweat. Ah— what can console us in this atrocious (至毒) heat! Cooling off (納凉), escaping the heat (避暑)! How shall we do it! Thus, we wish to offer this August issue as something for our readers to cool off with.

If you pick it up and sit down, you will soon know it is no lie, but this August issue, truly— breaking away from all formalities (形式), we have put in much miscellany (*同散異, tongsan'i*) or tall tales (噓風旋이, *hŏp'ungsŏn'i*), in the manner of spreading a straw mat under a tree where three or four friends gather and chat idly (閑談) while swatting flies. There are savory ghost stories (怪談), humorous tales (笑話) that could make your sides split, dreamlike stories of immortals (神仙), and besides these, impressions (感想), poems (詩), novels (小說), etc.—we have filled it entirely with things solely on the side of sentiment (情趣). Don't just idly say it's hot; pick up a copy and see. Herein are fans (붓채), electric fans (旋風器), shaved ice (氷水), cider, beer (삐루)—all such things are here.

As you can see, this August issue, exceptionally (例外), has come out early. You may wonder (疑訝), "Why is this issue so early?" but there is no need to wonder. From now on, we will always be this early. This must customarily (依例) be done due to your demands (要求) and also the duty (義務上) of our company (本社). Whether in the Kyŏngsŏng area (京城地方) or not (母論), we will ensure you see the magazine without fail by the end of that month (금음).

Because we rushed the editing like this, for this issue only, current affairs (時事問題) or general social issues (一般社會問題) were cut off at the entrance (入口), stopped in the middle (中間)—it naturally (自然) had to turn out that way. Matters from after the 15th have been pushed to the next issue (次期). We especially request (囑望) this of all contributors (寄稿諸氏). Please do not forget (닛지 마라 줍시요) that the 15th of each month (每月) is the manuscript deadline (原稿締切期日).

The heat bears down, and the rainy season has arrived (臨하얏는데); lonely is the editor (編者) battling with the pen (붓대), but also the circumstances (情景) of brothers Ch'ŏngo (靑吾) and Soch'un (小春), who are wandering arduously through mountains and waters (山山水水) for the P'yŏngnam Province issue (平南道號), are extremely (極키) worrying. We repeatedly entrust (付托) this to the people of P'yŏngnam (平南人士). Please give much care and protection (愛護럴 줍시사) to the two workers from our company (本社).

We hear that our brethren (兄弟) in Southern Korea (南鮮) weep because of drought (旱災), and our brethren in Western Korea (西鮮) are alarmed again by floods (水災)—how can the times and human affairs (天時人事) be so uneven! We hope that this journal might perhaps (幸여나) become even a momentary comfort (一時慰安).

**Summary**

This editor's postscript from the August 1924 issue of *Kaebyŏk* magazine explains the light, entertainment-focused content designed as a "cooling" read for the hot summer, filled with stories, humor, and poetry rather than serious social issues. The editors apologize for omitting current events due to an earlier publication deadline, reminding contributors of the 15th-of-the-month deadline. They express concern for two colleagues traveling for a special P'yŏngan Province feature and lament the concurrent drought in southern Korea and floods in western Korea. For historians, the piece offers insights into colonial-era magazine production (deadlines, content strategy tailored to season/reader mood), the use of specific terms like Namsŏn/Sŏsŏn for regions of Korea, and contemporary awareness of regional hardships like natural disasters (旱災/水災), framing the magazine as a potential source of comfort amidst difficulties. The mention of specific contributors (靑吾, 小春) and a regional feature (平南道號) also provides concrete details about the magazine's operations and scope.

남은 말슴
來日 모래가 初伏이라 함니다. 졍말 三伏時節에 드러왓슴니다. 더위와 땀과 안이싸울 수 업슴니다. 아- 이 至毒한 더위에 무엇으로써 위로를 삼을고! 納凉 避暑! 그 무엇으로써 할고! 이에 우리는 讀者 여러분의 納凉거리로 이 8月號를 바치려 함니다.

들고 안즈시면 거짓말 안인 것을 곳 아르실 터임니다마는 이번 8月號야말로 모든 形式을 뚝- 떠나서 나무아래 멍석깔고 3,4 친구가 모야 안저 파리채를 날니며 閑談하는 格으로 *同散異 또는 噓風旋이를 만이 집어 너엇슴니다. 구수한 怪談도 잇고 허리가 부러질만한 笑話도 잇고 꿈갓흔 神仙 니악이도 잇고 其他 感想, 詩, 小說 等 아조 情趣의 方面뿐 실엇슴니다. 空然히 덥다는 말만 마시고 한책씩 들어 보십시요. 여긔에는 붓채도 잇고 旋風器도 잇고 氷水도  잇고 사이다 삐루 그런 것이 다- 잇슴니다.

보시는 바와 가티 이번 8月號는 例外에 일즉 나왓슴니다. 「이번 號는 엇더케 이리 이른고?「하고 여러분이 疑訝하리이다마는 疑訝할 것 업슴니다. 이제부터는 늘 이러케 일즉하겟슴니다. 그것은 여러분의 要求와 또는 本社의 義務上 依例로 그리해야 되겟슴니다. 京城地方 母論하고 그 달 금음이면 꼭꼭 冊을 보시도록 하겟슴니다.

이러케 編輯을 닥아친지라 이번 號뿐은 時事問題 또는 一般社會問題가 入口에서 끗치고 中間에서 멈추고- 自然 그리될 밧게 업섯슴니다. 15日 以後ㅅ 것은 次期로 넘어가개 되얏슴니다. 特히 寄稿諸氏에게 囑望함니다. 每月 15日이 原稿締切期日인 것을 닛지 마라 줍시요.

더위는 닥처오고 또 장마는 臨하얏는데 외로히 붓대와 싸우는 編者도 編者러니와 平南道號를 爲하야 山山水水에 괴롭게 해매이는 靑吾小春 두분 兄님의 情景이 極키 걱정됨니다. 平南人士에게 거듭 付托함니다. 本社의 두분 일꾼에게 만흔 愛護럴 줍시사고-.

南鮮兄弟는 旱災로 울고 게시고 西鮮兄弟는 水災에 또 놀내신다 하니 天時人事가 엇지면 이러케 고루지 못한고! 幸여나 本誌가 一時慰安이라도 되게 되면-합니다.
