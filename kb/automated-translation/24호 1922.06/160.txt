기사제목	草笛(創作) (Grass Flute (Creative Work))
필자	岸曙 (Ansŏ)
기사형태	시 (Poem)

草笛
						岸曙
By the riverside,
As new buds sprout on the willow branches,
Washed by the river water flowing with a gentle laugh,
When the spirit of the new spring hangs like mist on the riverbank dike,
"Think of me," you whispered and went away.

When the setting sun's crimson, blood-like evening glow
Smiles from the late-returning girl's basket of greens,
And warmly illuminates the heart of an old man whose dreams have come true,
"Think of me," you whispered and went away.

When the beautiful dream path of melody follows the gentle breeze twice,
Crossing the distant, far-off sea, releasing a new fragrance,
You, who said "Think of me," are nowhere to be found. <12>

Night after night, day after day, singing songs,
As the memory of the waves seeps into the white sandbanks,
You, who said "Think of me," are nowhere to be found.

On the third day of the third month,
When leaves unfurl and flowers are about to bloom,
Do not forget the city of flowers, P'yŏngyang of joy.
On the calm Taedong River, drifting wild geese,
On Nŭngna Island, willows sprouting new shoots,
Look, among the pine trees near Moran Hill,
Hesitantly, words like whispering peonies
Seep from a lover's lips and wander, don't they?
Today is the first third day of the third month,
The day even the swallows of Kangnam return, not forgetting their old nests,
A lover's first third day is not just in the human world!
Look, the first third day in the sky too.

Does memory not die?
The pleasure that melts the heart,
Even in deep pleasure, still "loneliness" <13>
Hesitantly, ceaselessly comes and goes in my head,
Ah, is this the old melody of having lost you?
Love and joy that arose suddenly
Suddenly vanished without a trace,
And again, following the wind,
Like the spring season, like the flowing river water,
So charmingly, so pitifully, so tenderly,
It clutches my all-but-dead heart and sobs,
Ah, is this a scene from days gone by, or memory?
If it is memory, does memory not die?

Is my world water, or clouds?
Lying alone on the waterside dike of Nŭngna Island,
White waves silently flow down, bend after bend,
And there, far away in the clear sky, that endless place,
White clouds drift peacefully, cluster upon cluster.
A heart fading without a trace like the waves,
Thoughts drifting idly like the clouds.
Is my world water, or clouds?
Yesterday and today, it flows without end. <14>
<12-14>

**Summary**

This poem, "Grass Flute," by Ansŏ expresses deep longing and sorrow for a departed lover associated with spring, P'yŏngyang, and the Taedong River. The speaker recalls the lover's parting words, "Think of me," contrasting the beauty of nature's renewal—willow buds, spring festivals (Samjilnal), peonies on Moran Hill—with the persistent ache of absence and the haunting nature of memory. The poem concludes with a sense of aimlessness and dissolution, questioning whether the speaker's existence is ephemeral like water or clouds. From a historical perspective, the poem is interesting for its lyrical depiction of Korean landscapes (P'yŏngyang, Taedong River, Nŭngna Island, Moran Hill) and traditional customs (Samjilnal) during the colonial period, offering insight into personal sentiments and the enduring connection to cultural identity amidst external pressures. The use of older Korean orthography also provides linguistic data from the early 1920s.

草笛
						岸曙
江가에서
실버드나무가지에 새 눈이 도다나오며,
해죽해죽 웃으며 흐르는 江물에 시(洗)치우는
江두던에는 새 봄의 氣運이 안개가티 어리울 때,
「나를 생각하라」고, 그대는 소삭이고 갓서라.

넘어가는 새빩안 피빗의 저녁노을이,
느저가는 少女의 나물광주리에서 웃으며,
꿈을 이룬 늙은이의 가슴을 덥허 비추일 때,
「나를 생각하라」고, 그대는 소삭이고 갓서라.

樂調의 고흔 꿈길이 두 번 보들어운 바람을 딸하,
저멀리 먼바다를 건너 새 芳香을 놋는 이 때,
「나를 생각하라」신 그대는 찻기조차 바이 업서라.＜12＞

밤이면 밤마다, 날이면 날마다 노래부르며,
물결의 記憶이 흰모래밧을 숨여드는 이 때,
「나를 생각하라」신 그대는 찻기조차 바이 업서라.

三月에도 삼질날
닙피고 꼿 열리랴는 때가 되면은
꼿의 서울, 歡樂의 平壤을 닛지 말아라.
잔잔한 大同江우에는 떠도는 기럭이,
綾羅島에는 새엄을 도치는 실버드나무,
보아라, 牧丹峰가의 소나무일에는
삼가는듯이, 소삭이는 牧丹꼿가튼 말이
愛人의 입슐로서 숨이어 헤매지안는가.
오늘은 三月에도 첫삼질날,
江南의 제비도 녯깃을 안닛고 오는 날,
愛人의 첫삼질은 人世뿐만 아니여!
보아라, 空中에도 첫삼질.

記憶은 죽지도 안는가
맘을 녹기는 悅樂의,
썩 깁흔 悅樂에도 오히려 「외롭음」은＜13＞
삼가는듯이, 끈치도 안코 내 머리속을 오가나니,
아아, 이는 그대를 일흔 녯 曲調런가.
문득스럽게 생겨난 사랑과 깃븜이
문득스럽게 자최도 업시 슬어저서는
또다시 바람결을 딸하,
때의 봄철, 흐르는 江물과가티
아양스럽게도, 애처롭게도, 살틀하게도
다 죽은 나의「맘」을 붓잡고 흐득이나니,
아아, 이는 간날의 光景이런가, 記憶이런가.
記憶이라면, 記憶은 죽지도 안는가.

내 世上은 물이런가, 구름이런가
혼자서 綾羅島의 물가두던에 눕엇노라면
흰물결은 소리도 업시 구비구비 흘러내리며,
저멀리 맑은 하늘, 끗업는 저 곳에는,
흰구름이 고요도하게 무리무리 떠돌아라.
물결과 가티 자최도 업시 슬어지는 맘,
구름과 가티 한가도하게 떠도는 생각.
내 世上은 물이런가, 구름이런가.
어제도 오늘도 흘러서 끗남업서라.＜14＞
