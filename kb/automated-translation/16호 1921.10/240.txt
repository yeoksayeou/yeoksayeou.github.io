十月의 古今 (October's Past and Present)

1st Confucius is born (Year Keng-hsü of King Ling of Zhou).

3rd Un'yang Kim Yun-sik is born (440th year of Kaeguk).

8th Empress Myŏngsŏng, Lady Min, is assassinated (32nd year of Kojong, Year Ŭlmi).

12th Columbus crosses the Atlantic Ocean and discovers Cuba (玖馬) and the islands of Haiti (하이치).

13th At the International Meridian Conference, the Greenwich (끄리니치) meridian of the British Observatory is made the Universal Prime Meridian (1884).

15th German philosopher Nietzsche is born (1889) [Note: Nietzsche was born in 1844, not 1889].

16th Irish poet Oscar Wilde is born (1854).
His Sayings:
The past and present are not important. We shall only touch the future. The past is sought only for what is useless to people, and the present is sought only for what people cannot do without. The future is truly the place where the artist will live.

17th The Kapsin Coup occurs along with the Post Office completion banquet, and on the following day, the cabinet of Kim Ok-kyun's faction is formed. (21st year of Kojong).

18th French philosopher Bergson is born. (1859)
His Sayings:
We are free when our actions emanate from our whole personality, when they express it, when they possess that indefinable resemblance to it which one sometimes finds between the artist and his work (作品).

20th Kojong ascends the Wŏn'gu Altar, performs rites to Heaven, assumes the imperial throne, and proclaims the country name as Taehan (Great Han Empire) and the era name as Kwangmu.
On the same day, His Highness Crown Prince Ŭn of the Yi Royal Family is born.

21st British Naval Admiral Nelson dies in battle. (1805)
His dying words:
Thank God, I have done my duty (余는 余의 任務를 盡하얏다).

26th An Chung-gŭn shoots Itō Hirobumi dead at the Harbin station platform. (3rd year of Yunghŭi).

27th Japan's Yoshida Shōin is executed. (6th year of Ansei).

28th Founder of Ch'ŏndogyo, Master Suun Ch'oe Che-u is born. (433rd year of Kaeguk).
On the same day, the Hwangsŏng (Seoul) Young Men's Christian Association is established. (7th year of Kwangmu).

29th The Bank of Korea (韓國銀行), the predecessor of the current Bank of Joseon (朝鮮銀行), is established. The establishment of this bank, along with the Oriental Development Company established the previous year, was the beginning of economic management regarding Korea and was Itō Hirobumi's final policy. (3rd year of Yunghŭi).
On the same day, Japan's Liberal Party holds its formation ceremony. (14th year of Meiji).
Folk song = composed by Komuro Kutsuzan
In heaven, be a spirit of freedom; on earth, be a person of freedom. Freedom, oh freedom! Ah! Freedom, between you and me is the promise of Heaven, Earth, and Nature.

31st Luther advocates for the Religious Reformation. (1517)

**Summary**

This article from the Korean magazine *Kaebyŏk* (개벽) in 1921 lists significant historical events associated with the month of October. It presents a chronology mixing major figures and events from Korean history (Confucius's birth year based on Zhou dynasty, Kim Yun-sik, assassination of Queen Min, Kapsin Coup, Kojong's declaration of the Korean Empire, birth of Crown Prince Ŭn, An Chung-gŭn's assassination of Itō Hirobumi, birth of Ch'oe Che-u, establishment of Seoul YMCA and Bank of Korea/Oriental Development Company) with those from world history (Columbus, Greenwich Meridian, Nietzsche, Oscar Wilde, Bergson, Nelson, Yoshida Shōin, Japanese Liberal Party, Martin Luther). Quotes from Wilde, Bergson, Nelson, and a Japanese folk song about freedom are included. For historians, the article is interesting as it reveals how a Korean publication during the colonial period constructed a historical narrative for its readers, juxtaposing recent traumatic national events (Queen Min's assassination, Kapsin Coup failure, colonization implicitly linked to Itō, the Bank of Korea, and Oriental Development Company) alongside world events and figures associated with philosophy, art, exploration, and political change (including freedom and revolution). The inclusion of An Chung-gŭn and the explicit mention of Itō Hirobumi's role in establishing economic control mechanisms are particularly notable reflections of Korean consciousness under Japanese rule.

그의 錄語
過去와 現在는 다 重要한 것이 아니다. 우리는 다못 未來에뿐 接觸할 것이다. 過去는 사람에게 잇서 쓸대 업는 것 뿐으로써 찻고 現在는 사람에게 잇지 아니할 수 업는 것뿐으로써 찻다. 未來야말로 藝術家의 살을 곳이다.
17日 郵政局 落成宴과 가티 甲申政變이 起하며 그 翌日로써 金玉均 一派의 內閣이 成하다.(高宗21年)
18日 佛國哲學者 벨구손이 生하다.(1859)
그의 綠語
吾人의 行爲가 吾人의 全人格으로부터 나오는 때, 全人格을 表現하는 때, 全人格과 그의 言語로써 說明치 못할 類似, 即 藝術家와 作品의 間에 때때로 認함과 가튼 類似의 點을 가지는 때, 그때에 吾人은 自由이다.
20日 高宗이 圜邱에 登하야 天에 祭하고 皇帝位에 卽하며 國號를 大韓, 年號를 光武라 하다.
同日 李王世子 垠陛下-誕生하다.
21日 英海軍將 「넬손」이 戰死하다.(1805)
그의 臨終語
神에게 謝하노니 余는 余의 任務를 盡하얏다.
26日 安重根이 伊藤博文을 哈爾賓驛頭에 銃殺하다.(隆熙3年)
27日 日本 吉田松陰이 處刑되다.(安政6年)
28日 天道敎 元祖 水雲先生 崔濟愚-誕生하다.(開國433年)
同日 皇城基督敎靑年會가 設立되다.(光武7年)
