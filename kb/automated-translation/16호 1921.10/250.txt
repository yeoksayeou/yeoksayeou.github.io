**Body Text Translation:**
To Those Who Submit Articles
						Announcement from the Editorial Department

We would like to offer a word to those who show their special love for this magazine (本誌) by submitting articles.

One. The scope of the writing should be limited to introductions of others' works, presentations of one's own research (自己硏究), or matters related to one's observations (觀察) or impressions (感想), but please ensure the writing definitely has substance (lit. 'seed'). Something with only a fine appearance (허우대) but lacking substance (實속) is something we strongly avoid (大忌).

Two. For our brothers (兄弟) residing in foreign countries (外國) who grace us with their submissions, we would be even more delighted if, rather than any other writing, you could realistically describe (實寫) and send us the general situation (情形) and its changes (推移) concerning that country or region (地方). This is because doing so provides the most interest (趣味) and practical benefit (實益) to our compatriots in the homeland (內地同胞).

Three. Furthermore, even for those residing in regions within Korea (朝鮮內), we would be grateful if you could write articles concerning the conditions (形便) and changes (推移) of that region, or commentary (論評) regarding individuals (個人) or groups (團軆) there that does not lean towards slander (中傷) or exaggerated praise (誇讚).

Four. Among the articles submitted by everyone during that time (그間), although there were not a few pieces that were worth taking (可取할 것) when viewed in part (一部), as whole pieces (全篇) they were absolutely unpublishable (到底히 揭載할 수가 업사와), leaving us with the desire but unable to fulfill it (有念莫遂). However, from now on, we have decided to change our editorial policy (編輯上 方針) regarding this, such that even if only a part is usable, we will take what can be taken (取할 것은 取하야써) to show the intention (意思) therein, so please be aware of this.

Five. The writing style (文軆) must absolutely be written in vernacular language (말글). Even if mixing Korean and Chinese characters (國漢文), it would be better to write in pure vernacular language rather than adopting the style (式) of adding Korean grammatical particles (吐) to Chinese text (漢文). For example (例), instead of writing 「一葉落而天下知秋」 as 「一葉이 落하야 天下가 秋됨을 知한다」 (One leaf falls and the world knows it becomes Autumn – a direct Korean rendering/parsing of the Chinese),
write it like 『한 닙이 떨어짐을 보와 天下가 가을됨을 알겟다』 (Seeing one leaf fall, I know the world is becoming autumn – a natural Korean expression). <146>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from *Kaebyŏk* magazine outlines submission guidelines for contributors in 1921. It emphasizes the need for articles with substance, discouraging superficial pieces. The editors specifically request reports on conditions and changes from Koreans living abroad and in various regions within Korea, urging objective commentary. Acknowledging past difficulties in publishing submissions entirely, they announce a new policy to publish excerpts if parts are valuable. Crucially, the notice strongly advocates for writing in pure vernacular Korean ("말글") rather than the traditional style of adding Korean grammatical particles to Sino-Korean sentences derived from Classical Chinese. From a historical perspective, this notice is significant for its explicit promotion of vernacular Korean prose at a time when mixed script (Hanja-Hangul) and more literary styles were common, reflecting growing nationalist sentiments and a desire for accessible, practical content relevant to the Korean populace under colonial rule. The call for reports from abroad and the regions also points to an effort to connect dispersed Koreans and foster a shared understanding of their diverse situations.

投稿하시는 이에게
						編輯局啓告
本誌를 特히 사랑하시사 投稿하야 주시는 이에게 한 말삼을 들이겟나이다.
一. 쓰는 글의 範圍는 남의 것의 紹介나 自己硏究의 發表이나 또는 自己의 觀察, 感想에 係한 것에 限하되 그 글에는 반듯이 씨가 들게 하야 주십시오. 허우대만 조코 實속 업는 것, 이것은 우리의 大忌이올시다.
二. 外國에 계시는 兄弟로서 投稿의 사랑을 주시는 이는 다른 무슨 글보다도 그 나라 或은 그 地方의 一般에 亘하는 情形, 及其 推移를 實寫하야써 보내 주시면 더욱 깃겁겟습니다. 이리함이 內地同胞에게 가장 만흔 趣味와 實益을 줌이 되는 故이외다.
三. 그리고 우리 朝鮮內 地方에 계시는 이일지라도 그 地方의 形便 及 其 推移에 關한 글과 또는 그곳 個人이나 或은 團軆에 對하야써 中傷, 誇讚에 기울지 안는 論評의 글을 적어 주시면 感謝하겟습니다.
四. 그間 여러분들의 投稿하신 글 中에는 一部로써 보면 可取할 것이 적지 아니하얏사오나 全篇으로서는 到底히 揭載할 수가 업사와 有念莫遂하얏나이다. 그런데 이제부터는 이에 對한 編輯上 方針을 달리하야써 비록 一部일지라도 取할 것은 取하야써 그 意思의 잇는 바를 示하기로 하얏사오니 그리 아십시오.
五. 文軆는 꼭 말글로 써주셔야 되겟습니다. 비록 國漢文을 석거 쓴다 할지라도 漢文에 朝鮮文으로써 吐다는 式을 取하지 말고 純然한 말글로써 씀이 조켓습니다. 例를 들어 말하면 「一葉落而天下知秋」라 하면 「一葉이 落하야 天下가 秋됨을 知한다」 함과 가티 쓰지를 말고
『한 닙이 떨어짐을 보와 天下가 가을됨을 알겟다』라고 씀과 가틈이외다.＜146＞
