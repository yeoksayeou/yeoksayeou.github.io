**Body Text Translation:**
A New Phase is About to Open

As summer goes, autumn comes. A cool feeling will newly stir in the Northern Hemisphere of the Earth; as the doors of the Supreme Council are about to close, the curtain of the Pacific Conference is about to rise, and the world's gaze shall for a while be concentrated there. Will its results be temporary? Or continuous? Though we cannot predict this, the minds of the multitude will somehow turn with this, and will its scene be wind or cloud? Though we cannot foretell this, the world's situation will surely change with this.

Then, what should be our way of dealing with this phase? Regarding this, those who are related nations or related peoples will all have made appropriate preparations together. There is nothing for us to particularly prattle on about, but the one thing we wish to say is that, at this time when celestial timing and human affairs align to seek renewal, there is also one thing in our Korean society where a new phase seeks to open. What is that one thing? It is the industrial awakening that has recently been emerging from the minds of our general Korean brethren, and it is the industrial movement suddenly erupting from this earnest awakening.

Looking back quietly, we have, in recent years, come to possess a vast awakening regarding life itself for the first time. However, that awakening, as vast as its scope was, its content was also extremely abstract. Afterwards, upon obtaining the goal of establishing a 'New Culture', there was undeniably a sense of it becoming somewhat more concrete, but that goal of 'New Culture' was also abstract, and the majority among our brethren found considerable difficulty in grasping its significance.

However, was it the circumstances of the times that naturally led to this, or was it that the guidance of our seniors attained its appropriateness? Rolling into the spring of this year, the general compatriots together cried out the urgent need for education, publicly recognizing that the great path for our lives lies first in the development of knowledge and ability. Then, reaching the summer of this year, they cried out the urgent need for industrial development, keenly feeling that after the development of knowledge and ability, striving for the increase of wealth is the way to make our lives whole. Reaching this point, the vast awakening of recent years has found its path forward, unfolding one by one, and from now on, our activities concerning various fields will become more organized and practical. Therein shall lie a distinctly true or poignant taste. We call this the newest phase opening upon each of our hearts, and we say that the light we anticipate will shine where this phase unfolds best. <1>

**Summary**

The editorial discusses the changing international landscape, particularly the upcoming Pacific Conference, suggesting it marks a turning point. It argues that Korea is experiencing its own internal shift, moving beyond abstract concepts like 'life' or 'New Culture' towards more concrete goals. Following a recent emphasis on education, the current focus is now sharply on industrial development and economic self-strengthening ('increase of wealth') as the practical means to improve Korean lives. This 'industrial awakening' is presented as the most significant 'new phase' for Korean society, promising tangible progress and a path towards 'light'. For historians, the article clearly documents the evolution of Korean nationalist thought in the early 1920s, showing a pragmatic turn towards economic self-reliance (linked to the burgeoning *mulssan changnyŏ undong* or Promote Korean Products Movement) as a key strategy under colonial rule, moving beyond purely cultural or broadly political aspirations.

기사제목	새로운 局面은 열니려 하도다
기사형태	논설

새로운 局面은 열니려 하도다
여름이 가거늘 가을이 오도다. 地球의 北半面에는 서늘한 氣分이 새로 動할지며 最高會議의 門은 닷기려 하거늘 太平洋會議의 幕은 열니려 하나니 世間의 凝視는 한참동안 그로 集注하리라. 그 功果가 一時的일가? 繼續的일가? 우리가 그를 豫察치 못하나 多衆의 心機는 如何間 이로써 一轉할지며 그 場面이 風일가 雲일가? 우리가 그를 先占치 못하나 世界의 局面은 想必 이로써 一變할 지니라.
그러면 이 局面에 處하는 道가 어떠할가? 이에 對하야는 關係國 或은 關係民族된 자로서 다 가티 相當한 準備가 잇섯슬 것이라. 우리가 別로 呶呶할 것이 업거니와 다못 한가지 말하고 십흔 것은 이가티 天時人事가 아울러 새롭고저 하는 이때를 당하야 우리 朝鮮社會에도 또한 새로운 局面이 열니고저 하는 한가지가 잇는 그것이니 그 한가지라 함은 무엇이냐? 最近 우리 朝鮮 一般 兄弟의 머리 속으로부터 쪽 이어 나오는 産業的 自覺 그것이며 이 切實한 自覺으로부터 문득 勃發하는 産業的 運動 그것이로다.
가만히 돌아보면 우리는 近年 以來로 처음 生에 對한 尨大한 自覺을 가지게 되엇섯다. 그러나 그 自覺은 그 範圍가 廣大한 그만큼 그 內容도 甚히 抽象的이엇다. 그 後 新文化의 樹立이라는 目標를 得하야써 얼마큼 具體化한 感이 업지아니 하얏스나 新文化라는 그 目標가 亦是 抽象的인 바 兄弟 中 대다수는 그 意義의 捕捉에 자못 困難하얏도다.
그런데 時勢의 시킴이 스스로 그리됨인가. 또는 先輩의 指導가 其宜를 得함인가. 구을러 今年 봄에 이르러는 一般同胞가 고로히 敎育의 急務임을 絶呌하야 우리의 生을 爲하는 大道가 먼저 知能의 啓發에 잇슴을 公認하얏스며 다시 今年 여름에 이르러는 産業發展의 急務임을 絶呌하야 知能啓發의 다음으로는 富力의 增進을 圖함이 우리의 生을 온전히 하는 道임을 懇切히 느끼게 되엇나니 이에 이르러 年來의 그 尨大한 自覺도 하나 둘식 풀려 나아갈 길을 어덧스며 이제부터 各 方面에 對한 우리의 活動은 一層 組織的 또는 實地的이 될지요. 거긔에는 別로 眞實 또는 幸酷한 맛이 잇스리라. 우리는 이것으로써 우리들 各自의 마음우에 열니는 가장 새로운 局面이라 하며 이 局面을 가장 잘 展開하는 곳에 우리들의 期待하는 光明이 비추리라 한다.＜1＞
