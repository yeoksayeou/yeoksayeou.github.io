**Summary**

This document is the colophon (publication details) for the 58th issue of the magazine *Kaebyŏk*, published on April 1, 1925. It lists the editor (Yi Ton-hwa), publisher (Yi Tu-sŏng), printer (Min Yŏng-sun), printing company (Taedong Printing Co., Ltd.), and publishing house (Kaebyŏk-sa), along with their respective addresses in Kyŏngsŏng (colonial Seoul). It also includes the printing and publication dates, telephone number, postal transfer account number, and a note encouraging reprints. For historians, this colophon provides valuable specific information about the personnel, locations, and infrastructure involved in Korean publishing during the Japanese colonial period. The mix of Korean district names (e.g., Samch'ŏng-dong, Iksŏn-dong) and a Japanese district name (Ch'ŏngsu-ch'ŏng/Shimizu-chō) within Kyŏngsŏng reflects the administrative reality of the time.
