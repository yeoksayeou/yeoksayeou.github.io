Challenge of the Barbarian
						Paek Ki-man
I love the Madonna
I love the pure Madonna.<42>
You temptresses wrapped in clothes of false silk!
Quickly, retreat from the holy temple of my spirit.

Though your mouths call out for Truth,
Your hearts constantly scatter into Falsehood,
Floundering in the great contradiction of Sin—
This dirty body, this heart.

I am a Barbarian—a child of rebellion,
Crimson, thick blood boils and rises.
In the heavens and earth of the twentieth century, turbulent with trickery and chaos,
I will sprinkle this crimson blood.

Then, cry! While pounding the Earth,
While pounding the dark Earth—
Until the sun of Light shines,
Until the Barbarian's hymn rings out.

Behold! In the heart of the Brave,
The mad blood of challenge leaps wildly,
You devils dancing in the night shadows!
Quickly, get away from the holy temple of my spirit.
-End-<43>

**Summary**

This poem by Paek Ki-man is a fierce declaration of rejecting societal hypocrisy ("Falsehood," "Sin") and embracing a passionate, uncorrupted identity, symbolized by the self-proclaimed "Barbarian." The speaker contrasts this pure, rebellious spirit ("crimson blood," "mad blood of challenge") with the deceitful "temptresses" and "devils" representing the corrupt modern world (the "twentieth century, turbulent with trickery and chaos"). The poem calls for a transformation, anticipating a future where the "sun of Light" shines and the "Barbarian's hymn" prevails. From a historical perspective, the poem is interesting for its powerful expression of defiance and alienation within the context of colonial Korea. While not explicitly political, the passionate rejection of falsehood and the embrace of a "barbarian" identity against a corrupt establishment could resonate with anti-colonial sentiments or reflect the internal struggles of intellectuals navigating modernity under oppression. Historians might find the use of the "Barbarian" trope as a source of strength and authenticity particularly noteworthy.

蠻人의 挑戰
						白基萬
나는 마돈나를 사랑한다
純眞한 마돈나를 사랑한다.＜42＞
虛僞의 비단옷을 감은 妖女들아
빨리 내 靈의 聖殿을 물너서거라.

입으로는 眞實을 부르지즈며
마음은 줄곳 虛僞에 허터지느니,
罪惡의 큰 矛盾에서 허우적이는
더러운 이 몸을, 이 마음을.

나는 蠻人이다-反抗兒이니
싯뻙언 굵은 피가 끌어 오른다.
둑갑이 작란이 擾亂한 二十世紀의 天地에
싯뻙언 이 피를 뿌려 주리라.

그러면, 울어라! 大地를 두다리면서
黑暗의 大地를 두다리면서-
光明의 太陽이 빗날 때까지
蠻人의 聖歌가 울릴 때까지

보아라! 勇者의 가슴에는
挑戰하는 밋친 피가 가로뛰느니,
너이들 夜影에 춤추는 惡魔들아
빨리 내 靈의 聖殿을 물너가거라.
-끗-＜43＞
