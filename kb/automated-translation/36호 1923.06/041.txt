Location: Sŏnch'ŏn Town Center

**Summary**

This brief notice announces the establishment of a new branch office for the *Kaebyŏk* magazine in Sŏnch'ŏn (宣川). It details the office's location within the town center, its geographical area of responsibility covering Sŏnch'ŏn, Ch'ŏlsan, and Yongch'ŏn counties, and lists the key personnel appointed: Kim Sanggyŏm as Branch Manager, Kim Hŭich'ak and Yi Ch'angsŏk as Advisors, and Wŏn Sangnim handling External Affairs and reporting. For historians, this notice illustrates the organizational expansion and regional network of influential Korean nationalist publications like *Kaebyŏk* during the Japanese colonial period, showing efforts to disseminate ideas and maintain a presence despite colonial constraints. The listed personnel might also be figures of local or national importance worth further investigation.
