The Dreadful Rampage of Financial Power and Parental Authority
						One Reporter
The Shamelessness of Kaesŏng Millionaire Kim Kyŏngbae
When one mentions the Kaesŏng millionaire Kim Wŏnbae, likely anyone near Kyŏngsŏng would know of him. However, he has a younger brother named Kim Kyŏngbae, who possesses wealth and reputation not much less than his elder brother.

Mr. Kim Kyŏngbae! He is a young man, still under 30 years old. Having studied abroad in Japan early on, he is, so to speak, a young gentleman of wealth and knowledge, truly a person possessing the power and means to do whatever he wishes in the realm of Kaesŏng. However, today (setting aside other matters), there is a strange and wicked deed associated with him, certainly not something expected from Mr. Kim's usual self. (Although Mr. Kim himself insists the matter is not wicked at all, but rather quite natural).

Being under 30, he is at an age where men of other places and classes might still be unmarried bachelors. However, being abundantly wealthy, he early on took one wife and had a son and a daughter, and then took another wife (the first wife having died) and likewise had a son and a daughter. One could say his luck with women was considerable. Yet, for Mr. Kim, this was still insufficient.

This affair began last spring. In the house neighboring Mr. Kim's lived a maiden named Ko XX, 18 years old this year, attending the third year at Hosudŏn Girls' High School. Mr. Kim, who had taken a first and second wife and had two sons and two daughters, now fell for her. Consequently, he courteously gave bribes to her mother, and provided opium and money to her father, Ko Sanghu, an opium addict, making the request, "Give your daughter to me as a concubine." Ko's family lived in poverty, and of course, they had borrowed money from Kim not just once or twice.
Money! With thoughts of money swaying their minds, the parents <47> eventually readily consented. After the maiden advanced to the fourth year in March, her parents (actually Kim Kyŏngbae) promptly made her stop attending school. They then tried to immediately choose a date and hold a ceremony, but Ko XX refused, vowing death. Her parents tried to obtain her consent through beatings and torture, but that too was futile. She only wished for death. Mr. Kim requested they bring her over anyway, saying, "Women are like that at first, but once she comes to my house and spends a few days without dissatisfaction in a splendid room, her mind will soon change." Thus, he immediately sent numerous wedding gifts such as silks, fabrics, fruits, cakes, etc., to the Ko residence as a bride price. Seeing this, Miss Ko was greatly shocked and lost her color, truly not knowing what to do. Fortunately, she had a young male relative studying in Japan, and other relatives in Osaka, Pusan, and Taegu, so she quickly asked them for rescue. Through their arrangement, she decided to go to Tokyo and secretly left home. During an overnight stay in Pusan, the aforementioned Kyŏngbae learned of this and provided travel expenses to the girl's father, making him hurry there. Enacting a great drama in Pusan, the father pulled out a dagger, threatened her, saying, "How can I not do as I please with the daughter I bore?" and brought Miss Ko back. While beating and coercing her daily, Mr. Kyŏngbae, perhaps intending to show his face, said he would meet her personally once and went to her house. At this time, Miss Ko, in a tone like a shower of arrows, invoked even Kim's deceased father and grandfather, scolding and insulting his shamelessness. Hearing this insult, Mr. Kim, rather finding her witty, still returned home in a seemingly good mood. Afterward, her father was caught in a roundup of opium addicts and went to the police station. Her mother alone is guarding her and employing tactics, but now she too, perhaps lacking confidence, occasionally visits Mr. Kyŏngbae and advises him. However, Mr. Kim says, "Well, isn't it frustrating? What do I lack? Whether it's money, house, or studies, isn't it more than sufficient to satisfy that one woman?" and to someone else, he reportedly said, "Because that woman loves me," thus remaining stubborn and unyielding to this day. Furthermore, it is said that Mr. Kim has recently been threatening his current wife, forcing a divorce.

This rumor became an issue in Kaesŏng society for some time, and there was no one who did not know about it. However, the matter continues to unfold. Mr. Kim still tries to pursue the maiden, Mr. and Mrs. Ko Sanghu still try to hand over the maiden, and the maiden still fights against this injustice today, it is said. Mr. Kim Kyŏngbae possesses financial power (金權), supreme in the world, and Mr. and Mrs. Ko Sanghu possess parental authority (親權), like an unbroken lineage for all ages, openly and imposingly suppressing her, but the only pitiful one is Miss Ko XX.

Alas! Has the world indeed gone astray and have people truly perished? Is today's world nothing but the existence of the rich, convention, and injustice, with nothing else besides? Just become rich, and one can take ten, even a hundred, of those playthings called women, and other things follow suit. Just become parents, and one can donate or even sell off that property called children—is this today's world? Alas! If this can be tolerated, what cannot be tolerated? <48>
<47-48>

**Summary**

This 1923 article from *Kaebyŏk* magazine reports on a scandal in Kaesŏng involving a wealthy, married young man, Kim Kyŏngbae, attempting to forcibly take an 18-year-old schoolgirl, Ko XX, as his concubine. He bribes her impoverished, opium-addicted parents, who then try to coerce their daughter through confinement and violence. Despite her fierce resistance, escape attempts, and public denunciation of Kim, he persists, backed by his wealth (金權), while her parents assert their parental authority (親權). The article decries the power of money and traditional authority to override individual will and morality. For historians, this article offers a stark glimpse into social issues in colonial Korea, highlighting the clash between traditional patriarchal structures (parental rights, concubinage) and emerging modern sensibilities (female education, individual autonomy), the corrosive influence of wealth, the vulnerability of women and the poor, and social problems like opium addiction within the colonial context. The reporter's impassioned critique reflects contemporary anxieties about social justice and moral decay.

可恐할 金權·親權의 亂動
						一記者
開城富豪 金慶培氏의 無廉
開城 一富 金元培氏라 하면 京城 近方에서는 아마 누구라도 斟酌할 것이다. 그런데 그에게는 實弟 金慶培氏란 이가 잇서 別로 兄에게 못지 아는 財産과 名望을 가지고 잇다.

金慶培氏! 그는 아죽 30歲未滿의 靑年이다. 일즉히 日本에도 留學하야 말하면 有産有識의 靑年 紳士로써 開城의 天地에 잇서서는 實로 무엇을 하려면 얼마라도 할 수 잇는 힘과 便宜를 가진 사람이다. 그런대 今日의 그(다음일은 別問題이고)에게는 實로 氏의 平日에 期望하던 바가 아닌 一種의 怪惡한 行事가 잇다. (勿論 氏 自身으로는 그 일이 怪惡하기는 姑舍하고 도리혀 當然한 事라고 主張하지마는).

30歲未滿인 그는 다른 곳 다른 級의 사람 가트면 아직 쟝가도 들지 아니 한 總角 그대로도 잇슬만한 身分이다. 그러나 財産이 豊富한 그는 일즉히 1女를 取하야 1男 1女를 두엇고 또 다른 1女(前妻는 死亡)를 取하야 亦 如是 1男 1女를 두엇다. 그만하면 계집 福도 어지간 햇다고 할 수 잇다. 그러나 氏에 잇서는 이것이 오히려 不足햇다.

지나간 봄 때에부터 생긴 일이다. 氏의 隣家에는 好壽敦女子高等普通學校 3學年에 다니는 高00이란 今年 18歲의 處女가 잇섯다. 1妻 2妻를 取하고 2男 2女를 둔 氏는 또 여긔에 반하엿다. 그래서 其 母에게 慇懃히 賂物을 與하며 阿片中毒者인 그 父 高尙厚에게는 阿片과 金錢을 與하야 「자네 딸 내 妾으로 주게」하는 請을 건내엇다. 高의 집은 貧窮한 生活이라 其 問에 金에게서 借金한 일도 勿論 1, 2次뿐이 안이엿다.
金錢! 金錢생각에 마음이 비칠 비칠하야 그 父母는＜47＞ 이윽고 快히 承諾하얏다. 그 後 3月에 그 處女가 4學年으로 進級한 後는 그 父母(實은 金慶培)로부터 곳 通學을 停止케 하얏다. 그리고는 卽時 擇日하야 禮式을 擧行코자 하얏스나 高00은 誓死不應하엿슴으로 其 父母는 歐打 拷問으로서 承諾을 바드랴 하얏스나 그도 徒勞이엇다. 다못 죽기를 願하얏다. 慶培氏는 「女子란 처음에는 그런 것이지만 내 집에 와서 華麗한 房안에서 不滿이 업시 數日間만 지내이면 곳 마음이 돌려질 터이라」고 그대로 다려오기를 請하얏다. 그리하야 卽時 納幣로 數多의 幣帛 布木 果餠 等을 高氏의 집으로 보내엇다. 00은 이것을 보고 大驚失色하야 正히 엇더케 할 바를 몰랏다. 多幸히 그의 親族되는 靑年 한 사람이 日本에 留學하고 그 外의 大阪 釜山 大邱에도 親族들이 잇섯슴으로 곳 그들에게 救援을 請하야 그의 周旋으로 東京에 가기로 하고 그윽히 집을 나와 釜山에 1泊하는 中例의 慶培가 이것을 알고 女子의 父에게 旅費를 出給하야 急行케 하니 釜山서- 大活劇을 演하야 其 父는 短刀를 끄내서 威脅하며 「내가 나흔 내 딸을 엇재 내 마음대로 못 하랴」하고 00을 다리고 와서 日日歐打하며 强誘하든 中 慶培氏가 그 容貌를 보이려 하얏슴인지 한 번 親히 對面하겟다 하야 그의 집으로 갓섯다. 이 때의 00處女는 빗발가티 모라치는 語調로 金氏의 亡父祖父까지 걸어 그의 無廉함을 叱辱하엿다. 이 辱을 드른 金氏는 오히려 才弄스럽다 하며 그래도 조흔 마음으로 歸家하얏다. 그 后 其 父는 阿片中毒者 檢擧에 걸려 警察署에 가게 되고 其 母 혼자가 그를 직히고 手段을 불이는 中인데 이제는 그 母- 亦是 自信이 업는지 때로 慶培氏를 訪問하고 忠告하낊� 氏는 「글세 딱하지 안소. 내가 무엇이 不足하오. 돈이던 집이던 工夫로 하던 그 女子하나를 滿足식히기에야 充分할 것이 안이요」 하며 또 엇던 이에게는 「그 女子가 나를 사랑하니까」하고 하야 如前히 固執不下하는 今日이라 한다. 그리고 金氏는 最近에 그 妻(後妻)를 脅迫하야 離婚을 强求하는 中이라 한다.

이 所聞은 한참 동안 開城 社會의 問題가 되야 그 누구가 모르난 사람이 업섯다. 그러나 그 問題는 그대로 進行되여 잇다. 金氏는 如前히 그 處女를 取尋하려 하고 高尙厚夫妻는 如前히 그 處女를 引渡하려 하고 그 處女는 如前히 그 無道와 싸우는 今日이라 한다. 金慶培氏에게는 天下第一인 金權이 잇고 高尙厚夫妻에게는 萬世一系인 親權이 잇서 堂堂하게 公然하게 壓下하지마는 다못 볼상한 이는 高00處女뿐이다.

嗚呼라. 世는 果然 遠하고 人은 確實히 亡하엿는가. 富者가 잇고 因襲이 잇고 無理가 잇는 以外에 다시는 아모러한 것도 업는 것이 今日의 今世인가. 富者만 되엿서라 女子라는 저! 愛物을 열이라도 百이라도 取할 수가 잇고 다른 것은 거긔에 準하게 된다. 父母만 되엿서라 子女라는 그 所有物을 寄贈할 수도 잇고 賣却할 수도 잇는 것이 今日의 今世인가. 嗚呼 是可忍也 孰不可忍也＜48＞
