Magazine Title: 개벽
Publication Date: 1923년 06월 01일
Article Title: 正誤 (Errata/Corrections)
Author: None specified
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editorial Postscript)

In the supplement Gyeongnam Province issue attached to this magazine's April issue, page 32, second column, fourth line, 'Mr. Yi I-o' (李以吾氏) is an error for 'Mr. Chŏng I-o' (鄭以吾氏). Likewise, on page 33, upper column, second line, 'Yi I-o' (李以吾) is an error for 'Chŏng I-o' (鄭以吾).

In this magazine's May issue, page 12, within the article titled 'What Kind of Month is May?' (五月은 어한 달인가), the statement that China's National Humiliation Memorial Day (國恥記念日) is the 4th is an error for the 7th.<31>

**Summary**

This brief article lists corrections for previous issues of the magazine *Kaebyŏk*. It rectifies a misspelling of a person's name, changing Yi I-o to Chŏng I-o in two places within the April issue's Gyeongnam Province supplement. It also corrects the date mentioned for China's National Humiliation Memorial Day in the May issue, stating it should be May 7th, not May 4th. The mention of China's National Humiliation Day (related to the Twenty-One Demands) indicates the magazine's and its readership's awareness of significant political events and anti-imperialist sentiment in neighboring East Asian countries during the Japanese colonial period.
