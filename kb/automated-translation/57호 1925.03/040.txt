Commemoration of the Proletarian Women's Day
						SY

1
March 8th! This day is the International Proletarian Women's Day. Just as May 1st was designated as the memorial day for the world's proletariat class, and just as the first Sunday of September is Proletarian Youth Day, March 8th is the World Proletarian Class Women's Day.
The significance, emotion, and goal of these memorial days lie in the fact that they are not limited to any one nation or region but are international memorial days that broadly transcend national borders and races. Therefore, this is an expression of the common interests of the international proletariat class and their will to liberate themselves from the oppression of the international capitalist class; it is the forging of battle, a demonstration, and also the harmony and response of the human feeling of solidarity of the new society to come. Thus, it is a memorial day that emphasizes the united cooperation of the world's proletarian workers, women, and youth, and promotes their opinions.

2
March 8th, 1925, this year's day is the 4th commemoration. Like May 1st, it does not yet have a long history, nor has it been widely publicized, but since the 1st commemoration day, a profound impression, emotion, and also the joy of the heart for commemorating this day in unity in the not-too-distant future must already be, and cannot but be, in the minds and hearts of the proletariat class of nearly every country in the world.

At the International Proletarian Women's Conference held in Mosŭk'ŭba in June 1921, a proposal was submitted by a comrade from Pulgaria to designate March 8th of every year <23> as International Proletarian Women's Day. The 82 representatives from 28 countries present at the time sincerely expressed their agreement, and it was passed unanimously.
Now, this March 5th is the birthday of the famous Rosa Luksembŭrg, who devoted her entire life to the liberation movement and finally met her end at the hands of counter-revolutionaries in Pŏllin. Thus, the proletarian women created a truly profoundly significant historical memorial day by making the seven days centered around this honorable and emotion-filled 5th and 8th into Women's Week.
Therefore, the proletarian women will utilize this hard-won, ideal opportunity to express and promote the spirit of marching in step on the class line in unity and cooperation with the men of the entire world's proletariat class to strive for the improvement of the economic, political, and social status of proletarian women.

3
At the 1st commemoration in 1922, the momentum of activities in the proletarian state of Russia goes without saying, and besides that, the activities of the awakened proletarian comrades residing in capitalist countries, celebrating this day which gives meaning and emotion for this purpose, are said to have had truly great power, even if their number was small.
In Tongil (Germany), the magazine "Communist Party Women" published a special issue, carrying articles about the miserable living conditions of working women and opinions on the matter, raising their spirited voices. Also, they separately published a pamphlet titled "To Working Women and Girls," distributing and publicizing it in various areas, thereby encouraging participation in the demonstration movement of this Women's Week. As this pamphlet fluttered through the streets, it goes without saying that the momentum of the class movement became even fiercer amid the overwhelming welcome of proletarian workers, women, and youth, and on the other hand, there were also confiscations due to prohibition and oppression by the authorities.
Also, since the 1st commemoration day, one can see the situation of activities by proletarian women in Ilbon (Japan) to commemorate this day considerably expanding, so it will likely not be difficult to predict that there will be remarkable movements on this day this year.
I do not know how things are in Chosŏn (Korea). Perhaps it is due to my limited knowledge, but anyway, I do not know. <24>
<23-24>

**Summary**

The article commemorates the 4th International Proletarian Women's Day (March 8th), established in 1921 at a Moscow conference and linked to Rosa Luxemburg's birthday (March 5th), forming a "Women's Week." It emphasizes the day's international, class-based nature, aiming to unite proletarian women worldwide (alongside men) against capitalist oppression and promote their social, political, and economic advancement. The author notes commemorative activities, such as publications and demonstrations, in Russia, Germany, and increasingly in Japan, but professes ignorance about any similar activities in Korea. For historians, this piece is interesting as it shows the relatively early awareness and discussion of international socialist feminism and class struggle within colonial Korea (1925). It highlights the connection Korean intellectuals or activists felt to global movements, while the author's concluding remark about Korea hints at either a lack of organized activity or the difficulty of conducting/reporting on such movements under Japanese surveillance and control.

기사제목	無産婦人日의 紀念
필자	SY
기사형태	소식

無産婦人日의 紀念
						SY
1
3月 8日! 이날은 國際無産婦人紀念日이다. 5月 1日을 世界의 無産階級의 紀念日로 定한 것 가티 또 9月의 第一 日曜日이 無産靑年日인 것 가티 3月 8日은 世界無産階級婦人日이다.
이들의 紀念日은 엇더한 國家 或은 地方에 局限된 것이 아니라 널리 國境과 人種을 超越한 國際的 紀念日인 곳에 그 意義가 잇고 感激이 잇고 目標가 잇는 것이다. 그럼으로 이는 國際的 無産階級의 共通한 利害關係와 또 그 國際的 資本階級의 억압에서 解放하려는 意思의 表白이오 戰鬪의 鍜鍊이오 示威요 또 將來 할 新社會의 人類的 連帶感情의 調和오 嚮應이다. 그리하야 世界의 無産勞働者 婦人 靑年의 一致的 協同을 高調하는 또 그 意見을 發揚하는 紀念日이다.

2
1925年 3月 8日 今年의 이날은 第4回의 紀念日이다. 5月 1日과 가티 아즉 長久한 歷史도 업고 또 널히 宣傳 되지도 아니 햇지마는 第1回 紀念日 以來 거의 世界 各國의 無産階級의 腦裏와 胸底에는 임이 업시랴 업실 수 업는 深刻한 印象과 感激과 또 멀지 아니 한 將來에 一致하게 이날을 紀念히기 爲한 맘의 歡喜를 가티 할 때가 올 것이다.

1921年 6月 모쓰크바에서 開催된 國際無産婦人大會에서 뿔가리아의 同志의 發案으로 每年 3月 8日을＜23＞ 國際無産婦人日로 하자는 案이 提出되매 當時 出席한 28個國의 82名의 代表者는 眞心으로 이에 贊成의 意見을 表明하고 滿場一致로써 可決되엿다.
그런대 이 3月 5日에는 一生을 解放運動을 爲하야 盡力하고 맛참내 伯林에서 反動革命者의 凶手에 그의 最後를 맛친 저 - 有名한 로자·룩센붙크의 誕王日이다. 그래서 無産階級 婦人은 이 榮譽와 感激이 찬 5日과 8日를 中心으로 한 7日間을 婦人週間으로 하야 眞實로 意味深長한 歷史的 紀念日을 맨늘엇다.
그래서 無産階級婦人은 이 엇기 어려운 絶好의 機會를 利用하야 無産階級婦人의 經濟的 政治的 또는 社會地位의 向上을 圖謀하기 爲한 全世界의 無産階級 男子와 一致 協力하야 階級線의 步武를 가티 할 氣勢를 表白發揚할 것이다.

3
1922年 第1回 紀念에는 無産國家의 로시아에서의 活動의 氣勢는 말할 것도 업는 일이오 그 外의 資本國家에 在한 覺醒한 無産同志의 이 目的을 爲한 意義와 感激을 엇게 하는 이 날의 紀念을 祝賀하는 活動은 그 數는 비록 적엇다 하드래도 實로 偉力이 잇섯다 한다.
獨逸에서는 「共産黨 婦人」이란 雜誌의 特別號를 刊行하야 勞働婦人의 悲慘한 生活狀態와 그에 對한 意見을 揭載하야 氣焰을 놉혓고 또 別로히 「勞働婦人 及 少女들에게」라는 小冊子를 發行하야 各 方面에 配布 宣傳하야 그래서 이 婦人週間의 示威運動에 叅加하기를 勸誘하엿다. 이 小冊子가 街頭로 휘날니매 無産 勞働者 婦人 小年들의 絶大한 歡迎裡에 階級運動의 氣勢를 加一層 熾烈한 것은 勿論이엇고 그 反面에는 官憲의 禁止와 壓迫으로 押收도 잇섯다.
또 第1回 紀念日 以後의 日本의 無産婦人도 이 날을 紀念키 爲하야 活動하는 形勢는 자못 擴大되는 것을 볼 수 잇스니 今年의 이 날에는 可觀할 運動이 잇슬 것을 豫測하기가 어렵지 아니 할 것이다.
朝鮮에서는 엇더케 되어 잇는지 나는 알지 못한다. 그것은 나의 寡聞인지 모르나 엇젯든 나는 모른다.＜24＞
＜23-24＞
