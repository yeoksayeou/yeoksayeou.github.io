It seems spring is already drawing near. Thinking about it brings back memories of a year ago. I don't mean my own memories. I mean memories that will come to those heads of households who are about to receive the title of 'father'. I speak of Kim Kijin and Pak Chonghwa, who continue to be highly active in literary circles, each set to have a lovely second-generation baby. For Mr. Chonghwa, this is not his first attempt. It's actually his third time. After his first son passed away, five years passed, then came his second son who is now four years old, and this time, a smooth delivery has brought a precious daughter. For Mr. Kim, however, this is truly the first time, the beginning. Whether a future general or a female musician will emerge is still a great secret, but the urgent matter for now is for the baby to come into the world quickly. Who would wish for someone to quickly enter this troubled world, yet the anticipation of the father and mother must be so great, so please, please come quickly.

The P'asŭk'yura (파스큐라) Literary Lecture somehow came and went, but it was indeed an interesting event. The highlight among highlights that night was Mr. Sŏ Yŏng's (夕影君) oratorical style and Mr. Kim Ŏk's (金億君) poetry reading. There were some who spoke of rights and wrongs, but I believe there is absolutely no reason to discuss such things. In a world full of talk, it's difficult to speak, but if one can laugh off such matters, there might still be some generous room left in the heart. It is interesting enough that this first step by P'asŭk'yura, which introduces and studies literature and thought, achieved even that much.

Mr. Yi Sanghwa (李相和君), who came up to Seoul the other day, is inherently a sensitive poet, and his mind is also noble. Staying in an upper room at Ch'wiun Pavilion (翠雲亭), looking down upon the dusty world (紅塵世上), his emotions must be profound, and his poetic inspiration seems to have sharpened. With a mineral spring in the back garden (後庭) and white flowers scattering in the front garden (前庭), his heart must be quite pleased, yet sorrowful sighs likely emerge when he covers himself alone under a cold blanket. Never mind, they say "sorrow is the root of happiness."

Recently, Mr. Pyŏnghŏ (憑虛君), as things at the newspaper company are not going as he wishes, says he will strive for more reading and research. He has always been greatly indignant that the state of criticism in Korea is completely worthless, and he plans to write essays from now on. This is welcome news, delightful news, and reassuring news.

It is said that the Chosŏn Mundan Sa (朝鮮文壇社) held its Joint Review Meeting at Yŏngdo Temple (永道寺) on February 15th.<60>

**Summary**

This short piece from 1925 offers gossip and commentary on the Korean literary scene. It notes the impending fatherhood of writers Kim Kijin and Pak Chonghwa, reflects on a recent literary lecture event ("P'asŭk'yura") featuring Sŏ Yŏng and Kim Ŏk, describes poet Yi Sanghwa's reflective mood during a visit to Seoul, mentions Pyŏnghŏ's (likely Hyŏn Chin-gŏn's) dissatisfaction with journalism and his turn towards literary criticism, and reports a meeting held by the Chosŏn Mundan Sa group. For historians, the article provides a valuable glimpse into the personal lives, professional activities, and intellectual concerns of prominent Korean writers during the colonial period. It highlights the vibrancy of literary circles, their organized events, and the ongoing debate about the quality and direction of Korean literature and criticism in the mid-1920s.

文壇雜談
벌서 봄철이 갓가워 보인다. 생각하면 1년전 일이 새삼스럽게 생각난다. 내가 난다는 말은 아니다. 장차 아버지라는 명칭을 밧게 될 가장들이 생각나겟딴 말이다. 문단에서 대활약을 짐즛 계속하는 金基鎭, 朴鍾和 兩君에게는 각각 제2세의 귀여운 애기를 갓게 되여딴 말이다. 鍾和군에게는 이것이 첫 솜시가 안이다. 그래도 이번이 제3차이란 말이다. 장자 사별 5년간에 次子가 잇서 年이 4세이며 이번 순산엔 고명딸이다. 그러나 金군에게는 비로소 이것이 처음이며 시작이다. 장차 장군이 나올지 여류음악가가 나올지는 아즉 대비밀에 붓처 나 두고 위선 급한 것은 얼는 세상에 나오는 일이다. 괴로운 세상에 얼는 나오기를 누가 조화하랴마는 아버지 어머니 되는 이의 기다림이 너무도 과할 터이니 제발 좀 빨리 빨리.

『파스큐라』 文藝講演은 그럭저럭 지내고 말엇스나 하기는 자미도 잇는 일이다. 當夜 夕影군의 웅변체와 金億군의 讀詩가 快事 중 快事란 말이다. 시비를 말하는 사람도 잇기는 잇드라만는 그것은 시비를 말할 아모러한 원인이 업슬 줄 안다. 말 만흔 세상에 말하기도 어렵다마는 그런 일은 웃고 지내도 오히려 마음속에 너그러운 틈이 잇슬 것이다. 문예와 사상을 소개하며 연구하는 『파스큐라』의 第一步가 그만큼 된것만일지라도 자미 잇는 일이다.

일전에 상경한 李相和군은 원래부터 다감한 시인이려니와 마음조차 고결해서 숙소를 翠雲亭에도 上坐에 坐하야 紅塵世上을 俯視할 때 감개도 무량하려니와 詩想조차 날카로와진 듯 하다. 後庭에 약수가 잇고 前庭에는 白花가 분분하니 마음 짐즛 즐거우려니와 그래도 석을푼 한숨은 고독히 찬 이불을 덥흘 때 다다 나오려니. 두어라 『비애는 행복의 근본』이란다.

근래에 憑虛군은 신문사 일도 뜻대로 되지 안음으로 그는 더 만흔 독서와 연구에 노력하겟다 한다. 늘 군은 조선의 평론계가 아모 보잘 것이 업는 것을 매우 분개해서 압흐로는 논문도 쓰실 작정이라구. 구마운 일이며 즐거운 일이며 마음히 든든해지는 일이다.

朝鮮文壇 社主催合評會를 永道寺에서 2월 15일에 개최하엿다 한다.＜60＞
