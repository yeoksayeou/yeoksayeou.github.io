**Summary**

This text provides the colophon or publication details for Issue 62 of the Korean magazine *Kaebyŏk*, published on August 1, 1925. It lists the editor (Yi Tonhwa), publisher (Yi Tusŏng), printer (Min Yŏngsun), printing company (Taedong Printing Co., Ltd.), and the publishing house (Kaebyŏk-sa), along with their respective addresses in Kyŏngsŏng (colonial Seoul). It also includes the telephone number and postal transfer account information. For historians, this colophon is valuable as it documents the key personnel and infrastructure involved in producing a prominent Korean cultural and intellectual magazine during the Japanese colonial period. The specific addresses and company names offer concrete details about the publishing landscape in 1920s Seoul, including the use of Japanese place names like Ch'ŏngsu-ch'ŏng (Shimizu-chō) alongside Korean ones.
