Errata

Previous issue, Novel 'The Fallen One', page 73, bottom section, 7th line: '自由' (Freedom) should be '有望' (Promising) (Printing Error).

Page 75, top section, 13th line, last character '도' (to/also) and 14th line, first character '하' (ha/do) should be removed (Manuscript Error).

Page 77, bottom section, 6th line: '羊' (Sheep) should be '乳' (Milk) (Printing Error).

Page 80, bottom section, 13th line: '줄을 골라도' (even if choosing a line/rope) should be '줄도 골랏고' (also chose a line/rope and...) (Manuscript Error).

Page 83, top section, from the 1st line to the 4th line should be deleted (Manuscript Error).

Page 86, top section, 13th line: Regarding '를럿다' (was dragged) and '틀하엿다' (was tilted/lifted?) (Manuscript Error) [Implies correction needed].

Page 86, top section, 21st line: '잠앗든 팔' (sleeping arm) should be '감앗든 팔' (wound arm) (Manuscript Error).

Page 87, top section, 7th line: '깁흔 발길' (deep footsteps) should be '생각 깁흔 발길' (thoughtful/deep-in-thought footsteps) (Printing Error).

Ibid., 11th line: '보들모들한 나의 목' (soft my neck) should be '보들보들한 두 팔이 나의 목' (soft two arms my neck [encircling my neck]) (Printing Error).＜30＞

**Summary**

This article is an errata list detailing corrections for the novel "墮落者" (T'arakcha - The Fallen One) published in the previous issue (No. 19) of the magazine *Kaebyŏk*. It specifies corrections on various pages, citing both printing errors and mistakes originating from the manuscript. The corrections range from single characters or words to the deletion of entire lines. For historians, this piece provides insight into the practical challenges and editorial processes of magazine publishing in 1920s colonial Korea. It also offers specific examples of language usage, including older Korean spellings (e.g., 깁흔, 잠앗든, 골랏고) and Hanja terms, reflecting the linguistic landscape of the period.
