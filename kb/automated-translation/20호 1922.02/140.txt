Errata
For the January Issue:
On page 121, 千思萬態 (Ch'ŏnsamant'ae) should be 千思萬度 (Ch'ŏnsamando).
On page 122, 春堤의 暗黑 (Ch'unjeŭi amhŭk) should be 春堤의 暗黃 (Ch'unjeŭi amhwang).<83>

**Summary**

This brief article lists two corrections for the previous month's (January 1922) issue of the magazine *Gaebyeok*. It specifies word changes on pages 121 and 122. For historians, this notice offers a small glimpse into the editorial practices of a prominent Korean cultural magazine during the early colonial period, demonstrating a commitment to accuracy. The specific terms corrected (千思萬態 to 千思萬度, and 暗黑 to 暗黃 in the context of 春堤) might also be of minor linguistic interest regarding word usage at the time.
