May First
"May Day" and "Children's Day" - while these are annual demonstrations (年中示威) for the whole world concerning May First, for our Chosŏn (朝鮮), their significance (基義) is particularly profound (深長). Look! In today's world, which takes people living by eating other people as the sole natural law (天則) of social survival (社會生存), the situation (境遇) of which nation's populace could be smooth (順坦)? Yet, where else are there people (百姓) so thoroughly sucked dry and left to die like the people of Chosŏn? And in today's society, which takes the reciting (祖述) of tradition (傳統) and the rejection (拒否) of creation (創造) as the sole iron rule (鐵案) of human ethics (人間倫理), the growth (生長) of which land's children could be natural (天然스러우랴만)? Yet, where else is there an example of children being trampled (짓밟히고) and ignored (無視되는) like the children of Chosŏn? "May Day" and "Children's Day" - Labor Commemoration (勞働紀念) and Children's Blessing (祝福) - this day, deeply impressing upon the circumstances (處地) and aspirations (抱負) especially of the Chosŏn masses (民衆)! May it indeed be significant (意義)!<1>

**Summary**

This editorial reflects on the profound significance of May First, encompassing both May Day (Labor Day) and Children's Day, for the Korean people under Japanese colonial rule in 1925. It argues that while labor exploitation ("people eating people") and the suppression of children's natural development ("reciting tradition, rejecting creation") exist elsewhere, the situation in Chosŏn is uniquely dire, with its people being "sucked dry" and its children "trampled and ignored." The author sees these commemorations not just universally but as particularly poignant symbols reflecting the specific suffering and hopes of the Korean masses. For historians, the article is revealing of the intertwined nationalist and social consciousness in 1920s Korea, highlighting the perceived dual oppression of labor and the younger generation under colonialism, and framing international commemorative days within a specifically Korean context of suffering and aspiration.

五月一日
「메-데이」와 「어린이날」-이것은 五月一日에 대한 전세계로의 年中示威인 동시에 우리 朝鮮에 있어서는 別로히 基義가 深長한 것이다. 보아라 사람이 사람을 먹고 사는 것으로서 社會生存의 유일한 天則을 삼는 오늘 세상에 잇서 어느 나라 민중이라고 그 境遇가 順坦하랴만 우리 朝鮮 사람가티 요리케도 전적으로 빨리워 죽게 된 百姓이 어대 잇스며 傳統의 祖述, 創造의 拒否로써 人間倫理의 유일한 鐵案을 삼은 오늘 사회에 잇서 어느 땅의 어린이라고 그 生長이 天然스러우랴만 우리 朝鮮 어린이가티 짓밟히고 無視되는 例가 또 어대 잇슬가. 「메-데이」와 「어린이날」-勞働紀念과 어린이 祝福―특히 朝鮮民衆의 處地와 抱負에 잇서 感銘이 깁흔 이 날! 부대 意義아 잇슬지여다.＜1＞
