Publication Date: 1923년 10월 01일
Article Title: 訂正廣告 (Correction Notice)
Author: Not specified
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editorial Postscript)

In the congratulatory advertisement for the third anniversary of this magazine's founding in the July issue, "Asil Yanghaeng (朝日洋行) of Sangbu, Hamhŭng-ŭp" should be corrected to "Chowŏl Yanghaeng (朝月洋行)," and "Chief Ch'oe K'yusŏng (崔奎成)" should be corrected to "Ch'oe Kyusŏng (崔圭成)." <112>
<108-112>

**Summary**

This brief notice corrects two errors that appeared in a congratulatory advertisement published in the July issue of the magazine "Kaebyŏk" (개벽) commemorating its third anniversary. Specifically, it corrects the name of a business in Hamhŭng from "Asil Yanghaeng" (朝日洋行) to "Chowŏl Yanghaeng" (朝月洋行) and corrects the name of the chief/manager from Ch'oe K'yusŏng (崔奎成) to Ch'oe Kyusŏng (崔圭成). From a historical perspective, this article provides specific names of a business (Yanghaeng - 洋行, an older term for a trading company) and an individual in Hamhŭng in 1923. The correction itself, particularly the change from 朝日 (often associated with Japan) to 朝月 (using the Korean 'moon' character), might subtly reflect naming preferences or corrections related to identity during the colonial period. It also shows the magazine's practice of publishing corrections.
