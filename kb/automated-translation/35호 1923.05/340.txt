After Setting Down the Pen

This is the May issue. If things had gone according to plan, this month's issue should have been the Kyŏngbuk Province (慶北道) special issue. However, due to survey circumstances, we ended up postponing it to the upcoming June issue. When we think about how everything too often goes according to our own convenience, we feel very apologetic, but it is the result of us doing our best effort. We increasingly feel that getting anything done is difficult, and effort is a noble thing. Anyone among our readers who has personally managed something and agonized over it will surely understand.

Our company's stalwart legs (健脚) and also what could be called active hands (活手), Mr. Ch'a Sangch'an (車相瓚) and Mr. Yi Chaehyŏn (李在賢), already went to the Kyŏngbuk region (慶北地方) a month ago. It seems they are quietly exploring every nook and cranny of Kyŏngbuk without any particular notice. We feel endlessly sorry for them, striving with utmost devotion (鞠躬盡瘁) on their journey away from home (逆旅), where their only solace must be the moon and stars at night and the mountains and rivers by day. Even if it's just in spirit, please pray for their constant health, both mind and body.

We don't know yet on which day they will depart, but it has been decided that sometime this May, we will survey the P'yŏngbuk region (平北地方). It seems Mr. Han Chungjŏn (韓重銓) of the Sinŭiju (新義州) branch has been carefully preparing for this for some time. From Kyŏngbuk to P'yŏngbuk, shall we call this a giant leap? It's nice that we have so much freedom like this. Haha.

This happened in February. We read "The Returned Heretic" (도라간 異端者) sent by Mr. O Yŏnghwi (吳英輝) with great pleasure. We believe it will be read together with the readers before long. Mr. O, please know this.

Mr. Son Hyojun (孫孝俊) in Tokyo (東京) keeps requesting the establishment of a section related to sports. He insists that doing so would only benefit the Chosŏn (朝鮮) sports world, even if just a little, and would cause no harm. We are thankful to the point of tears for the sincerity of Mr. Son, who, being far away, cannot forget the sports world of his homeland (故國) like that. This time too, he has already sent three manuscripts. However, we feel sorry that due to editorial circumstances, we could not publish them all. We especially ask for your generous forgiveness (雅量잇는 寬恕), Sir. <155>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the May 1923 issue of *Kaebyŏk* magazine explains the delay of the planned Kyŏngbuk Province special issue to June due to survey difficulties. It describes the fieldwork of two reporters, Ch'a Sangch'an and Yi Chaehyŏn, in Kyŏngbuk, expressing sympathy for their hardships. The editors announce upcoming plans to survey P'yŏngbuk Province in May, mentioning preparations by Han Chungjŏn in Sinŭiju. They also acknowledge receipt of a manuscript from O Yŏnghwi and multiple submissions concerning sports from Son Hyojun in Tokyo, apologizing for not being able to publish all of Son's work due to space constraints.

This article is interesting for colonial Korean history as it reveals the practical challenges faced by publications like *Kaebyŏk* in producing content, particularly regional features requiring extensive fieldwork under difficult conditions. It highlights the dedication of journalists traveling across the peninsula and the engagement of readers and contributors, even those abroad like Son Hyojun in Tokyo, who showed keen interest in homeland affairs like the sports scene. The piece offers a glimpse into the editorial process, resource limitations, and aspirations of a major Korean cultural magazine during the colonial period.

擲筆之餘
5月號이다. 豫定대로 하엿스면 이 달 號는 慶北道號가 되엿서야 할 것이다. 그러나 調査의 形便으로 오는 6月號로 미루고 마럿다. 每事가 너무나 우리의 任意가 되야 생각하면 罪悚한 바가 만흐나 우리로서는 最善의 努力을 하노라는 것이 그러하다. 大軆 일 햐기란 어려운 것이며 努力이란 高貴한 것임을 작고 작고 느끼게 된다. 무엇이라도 손수 經營해보고 苦心해본 이면 讀者 中에서도 斟酌해 줄 일이라 한다.

우리 社의 健脚이오 또 活手라도 할만한 車相瓚
李在賢 兩君은 벌서 한 달 前에 慶北地方을 갓섯다. 그들은 아모러한 所聞도 업시 가만 가만히 慶北의 구석구석을 探索하는 貌樣이다. 다못 밤의 月星과 낫의 山河가 그들의 벗이 되는 것밧게 아모러한 慰藉가 업슬 그 逆旅에서 鞠躬盡瘁하는 그들의 모양이 限업시 가엽게 생각된다. 마음만이라도 조흐니 그들의 心身을 爲하야 恒時 健康이나 비러 주소.

어느 날에 떠날는지 그것은 아즉 未定이나 이 5月 中에는 平北地方을 踏査하기로 되겟다. 新義州 支社의 韓重銓君은 언제부터 그 準備에 用心하는 中인가 보다. 慶北으로부터 平北에, 이것을 껑충 거름이라고나 할가. 우리에게 이러케 自由가 만하서 좃타. 呵呵.

2月 中의 일이다. 吳英輝君의 보내준 「도라간 異端者」는 퍽 조흔 마음으로 닑엇다. 오래지 안하서 讀者와 가티 닑게 될 것을 밋는다. 吳君이여 그리 알어주쇼.

東京에 잇는 孫孝俊君이 작고 運動에 關한 欄의 設施을 請한다. 그러케만 하면 조곰이라도 朝鮮 運動界에 利益이 잇슬지언졍 害는 업스리라고 조른다. 먼 대에 잇서서 故國의 運動界를 그러케 못 니저하는 君이 誠意가 눈물이 나도록 感謝하다. 이번에도 벌서 세 通의 原稿를 보내 왓섯다. 그러나 編輯의 形便으로 그것을 다- 싯지 못한 것이 未安하다. 特히 君의 雅量잇는 寬恕를 빈다.＜155＞
