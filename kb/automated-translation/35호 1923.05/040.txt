五月은 엇더한 달인가

The month of May, historically speaking, is an extremely significant month and at the same time, an interesting month. The first day of this month is May Day, a Labor Memorial Festival day celebrated throughout the world, and in our Chosŏn, starting this year, this day became "Children's Day," poised perhaps to become the youngest movement, stretching out year after year like the surging movement of life at the century's end. The 2nd is the anniversary of the start of the Spanish Uprising; the 3rd is the festival commemorating the discovery of Brazil in South America; the 4th is China's National Humiliation Day (the day the Twenty-One Demands treaty was concluded with Japan); the 5th is the Orient's Dano Day and Mexico's National Day; the 13th is the anniversary of the abolition of slavery in Brazil; the 14th is Labor-Farmer Russia's International Labor Festival Day; the 17th is Norway's Independence Day; the 19th is the memorial day (Hwanwŏnje) for Son Pyŏnghŭi, a giant of modern Chosŏn; the 23rd is Romania's National Day; the 25th is Ascension Day and also Argentina's Independence Day; the 28th is the Armenian Republic's Independence Day; the 30th is the Memorial Day (Ch'ohonje) observed simultaneously by forty states in the USA. Labor memorials, youth memorials, uprising memorials, discovery memorials, revolution memorials, independence memorials, memorials of life, memorials of death—it is no coincidence that so many days of various kinds, yet all possessing great significance, are clustered together like this. The month of May! May it become ever more sacred as it goes. <12>

**Summary**

This article lists numerous significant historical dates occurring in May from around the world and within Korea. It highlights May Day (Labor Day) and the newly designated Children's Day in Korea, alongside international events like the Spanish Uprising, Brazilian independence milestones, China's National Humiliation Day, Mexican and Romanian national days, Norwegian, Argentinian, and Armenian independence days, and commemorations in Russia and the USA. It also notes the anniversary of the death of Son Pyŏnghŭi, a key Korean figure. The author finds the concentration of such diverse and meaningful days in May remarkable and wishes the month growing sanctity. For historians, the article is interesting for establishing Korea's first Children's Day in 1923, linking it explicitly to May Day, and placing Korean events (Children's Day, Son Pyŏnghŭi's memorial) within a global context of labor movements, national struggles, and historical commemorations, reflecting an outward-looking perspective even under colonial rule. The mention of China's "National Humiliation Day" related to Japan also subtly reflects the contemporary political atmosphere.

五月은 엇더한 달인가
5月 한달은 歷史的으로 보아 甚히 意義 잇는 달인 同時에 興味잇는 달이다. 이 달의 첫 날은 메- 데- 勞働記念 祭日로 擧世가 同然이며 우리 朝鮮에서는 今年부터 이 날이 『어린이날』이 되야 世紀末의 들끄러 오르는 生의 運動과 가티 年年歲歲로 뻣쳐 나아가는 가장 졂은 運動이 될지오 2日은 西班牙 暴動 開始 紀念日이오 3日은 南米의 「뿌라질」을, 發見한 記念祭日이오 4日은 中國의 國恥紀念日(日本과 一 二十一個條 締結日)이오 5日은 東洋의 端午日 墨西哥의 國祭日이오 13日은 「뿌라질」의 奴隷廢止紀念日이오 14日은 勞農 露國 國際勞働祭日이오 17日은 諾威의 獨立祭日이오 19日은 朝鮮 近代의 巨人 孫秉熙氏의 還元祭日이오 23日은 루마니아의 國祭日이오 25日은 基督昇天節인 同時에 알센친의 獨立祭日이오 28日은 알메니야의 共和國 獨立祭日이오 30日은 米國의 四十州가 一齊로 擧行하는 招魂祭日이다. 勞働紀念 少年紀念 暴動紀念 發見紀念 革命紀念 獨立紀念 生의 紀念 死의 紀念 이와 가티 가지 各種 그런 中에도 모다 크다라한 意義를 가진 日字가 이만큼 몰아 붓기도 偶然한 일이 아니다. 5月의 달! 갈스록 더 거룩함이 잇스라.＜12＞
