Article Title: 近作四章 (Four Recent Pieces)
Author: 金石松
Article Type: 시 (Poem)

近作四章
						金石松

Pitch-Black Man.
One winter day.
One Sunday morning (日曜日 午前),
Hurrying to the workplace, I,
Hearing the bell sound with my right ear,
Along the crumbling castle wall path,
Mixed with the churchgoers (信徒) heading to the chapel (禮拜堂),
Pass by, thinking of Heaven (天國).

Something bumps into me from the front,
Startled, I look up, and
A man whose whole body is bound tightly,
Whose entire body is pitch-black,
Rolling his nickel-bright eyeballs,<135>
Goes past, muttering.

Ah! Chimney (煙突) sweeping friend!
Do not resent me.
Your nickel-bright eyeballs are
The light (光) of rebellion (叛逆) cursing (咀呪) Heaven (天國)!
Your pitch-black whole body (全身) is
The immeasurable power (無量之力) to conquer (征服) reality (現實)!

Little Explorer (探索家).
In front of the east (東)-facing window (窓),
Where the morning sunlight brightly enters,
As the father (父) smokes his tobacco,
A newly awoken two-year-old,
Saying, "Daddy! Daddy!",
Crawls onto his father's lap,
Sits quietly without a word, then
Spreading his adorable small hands,
Tries to catch the tobacco smoke
Coming from his father's mouth,
Sitting down and standing up - with zeal.<136>
The father, discovering this sight,
Intentionally blows out more smoke,
With smiling lips,
Ah! Little explorer (探索家)!,
Are you seeking the truth of smoke?,
You, a lump of instinct (本能),
Whose bud of reason (理知) has not yet sprouted.

Puppy.
The morning sun - its benevolent rays full of love - fills our yard.
The neighbor's puppy, not yet three months old since birth, visits our house again like a child coming for a morning greeting (問安).
(Whenever the puppy comes, I stroke it, with a hand of love detached from utility (功利).)

The puppy playing in the yard finally comes up onto the wooden floor,
And dirties the cleanly swept floor with its muddy paws.
"Hey, you puppy!"
The scolding voice of the ill-fated (八字) old woman is heard.

The old woman chasing the puppy!<137>
The puppy being chased by the old woman!
When I see them, together with them,
I feel a hunger for people, receiving the rays of love.

Joy (喜悅) of Completion (完成)
A powder bottle (粉甁) on a glass paperweight (琉璃 文鎭), a toy wooden bell (木鐸) on top of that.
Having built a three-story pagoda (三層塔), the little one,
Claps his hands, saying, "Ah! Good, good!"

"Ah! Good, good!" I too cried out.
Seeing the three-story pagoda (三層塔) inside the child's head,
Seeing the "Joy (喜悅) of Completion (完成)" dancing in the human heart.
- Between January and February, 1923 -<138>
<135-138>

**Summary**

This work by Kim Sŏksong consists of four short poems depicting vignettes of everyday life in 1923 Korea. The first poem contrasts the speaker's contemplative walk towards a church with a sudden encounter with a soot-covered chimney sweep, whose appearance is interpreted as a symbol of rebellious power against harsh reality. The second observes a toddler's innocent fascination with catching smoke. The third portrays a scene with a puppy, an old woman, and the speaker, reflecting on shared human loneliness and the need for connection. The final poem captures the simple delight of a child successfully stacking objects, representing the universal human joy of accomplishment. From a historical perspective, the poems offer glimpses into the texture of colonial Korean life – the presence of manual laborers like the chimney sweep, the mix of tradition (old woman) and modernity (church), and the simple, perhaps escapist, focus on innocent moments and universal emotions amidst a likely difficult socio-political environment. The description of the chimney sweep as a figure of "rebellion" and "power" to "conquer reality," even if metaphorical, is particularly striking, potentially hinting at subdued social commentary or a search for strength in the marginalized.

近作四章
						金石松
샛검은 사람.
겨을 어느 날.
어는 日曜日 午前에,
일터로 밧비 가는 나는,
올흔편 귀로 鐘소리를 드르며,
흔허진 城밋 길로,
禮拜堂 가는 信徒와 석겨,
天國을 생각하며 지나간다.

무엇이 압헤 와 부다치기로,
깜작 놀라 치어다 본즉,
대톄를 서리서리 얼메인,
온 몸이 샛검은 사람이,
白銅 빗나는 눈알을 굴리며,＜135＞
중얼중월하고 지나간다.

아! 煙突 쑤시는 친구야!
너는 나를 원망치 말아라.
白銅 빗나는 너의 눈알은,
天國을 咀呪하는 叛逆의 光!.
샛검은 너의 全身은,
現實을 征服하는 無量의 力!.

어린 探索家.
아츰 해빗이 밝게 드는,
東으로 난 窓 압헤서,
아비가 담배를 피울 적에,
갓 깨인 두살박이가,
아버지! 아버지! 하며,
아비의 무릅으로 기어와서,
아모 말도 업시 감안히 안젓더니
앙징한 작은 손을 버리고,
아비의 입에서 나오는,
담배 연기를 잡으랴고,
안젓다 섯다 한다 - 熱心으로.＜136＞
이 꼴을 發見한 아비는,
일부러 연기를 더 뿜으며,
微笑를 띄운 입살로,
아! 어린 探索家여!,
너는 연기의 眞理를 찻느냐?,
理知의 움이 돗지 아니한,
本能의 덩어리인 네가.

강아지.
아츰 해가- 사랑에 넘치는 거륵한 光線이- 우리집 뜰에 가득하다.
나흔지 석 달도 못되는 이웃집 강아지가 아츰 問安 오는 아해가티 또 우리집을 차저왓다.
(나는 강아지가 올 때마다 그것을 쓰다듬는다, 功利를 떠난 사랑의 손으로.)

뜰에서 놀든 강아지는 마츰내, 마루에까지 올나서,
흙발로, 정히 치운 마루를 더럽힌다.
「네 요 강아지!」
八字 사나운 할멈의 호령소리가 들닌다.

강아지를 쫏는 할멈!.＜137＞
할멈에게 쫏기는 강아지!.
나는 그들을 볼 때에, 그들과 함끠,
사람에 주림을 늣긴다, 사랑의 光線을 바드며.

完成의 喜悅
琉璃 文鎭 우에 粉甁, 그 우에 작란감 木鐸.
三層으로 塔을 싸허 놋코, 어린것이,
「아! 좃타 좃타!」하고 손벽을 친다.

「아! 좃타 좃타!」 나도 부르지젓다.
어린것의 머리 속에 든, 三層塔을 보고,
사람의 마음 속에 춤추는 「完成의 喜悅」을 보고.
-1923, 1월로 2월사이-＜138＞
