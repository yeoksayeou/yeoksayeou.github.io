Publication Date: 1923년 05월 01일
Article Title: 淸川江 以北 형제에게 (To the Brothers North of the Ch'ŏngch'ŏn River)
Author: Not specified (likely Kaebyŏk editorial staff)
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice / Editorial Postscript)

To the 1.2 million brothers of P'yŏnganbuk-to (平北), scattered evenly across 1 *pu* (府; city), 19 *kun* (郡; counties), 194 *myŏn* (面; townships), and 1,438 *tong* (洞; villages), evenly enjoying the pleasures of your native land – we humbly wish you every blessing and, furthermore, wish to express our sincere feelings in a word.

As you already know, our Kaebyŏk Publishing Company (開闢社), recognizing this year as the deeply meaningful Year of Kyehae (癸亥年), has already embarked upon the task of surveying all the culture of our Hibiscus Domain (槿域; Korea), so that we may appreciate it together with you and take pride in it together with you. Two months have passed since we began this work, covering two provinces (道).

During the survey of the two provinces of Kyŏngsangnam-do (慶南) and Kyŏngsangbuk-do (慶北), thanks to the abundant affection and help of the brothers there, we were able to investigate the culture there generally, as far as our minds allowed, although [the coverage was] unsatisfactory.

Therefore, although the travel route feels somewhat out of order, it seemed there might be a special appeal in introducing the South and North alternately, and so the company has unanimously decided to proceed with a visit to P'yŏnganbuk-to (平北) this May.

When going to one's own region to do one's own work, meeting one's own brothers, and speaking of one's own affairs, what other requests or worries could there be? However, the strength of an individual and the strength of the masses are different, and accordingly, the work of an individual and the work of the masses are different.

Because universality versus partiality, thoroughness versus negligence, completeness versus deficiency depend on the collective effort of the many, we rely solely on the fraternal affection of our brothers and dare to earnestly request: Brothers, as this is our work and you are our brothers, please guide us and help us.

Regarding P'yŏnganbuk-to (平北) – the causes and trends of industry, education, religion, and other social issues; the introduction and critique of famous scenic spots, historical sites, local sentiment, customs, central figures, and major institutions, etc. – please, brothers, voluntarily investigate all these things, past and present, according to the actual facts and actual figures, and then introduce and present them to us one by one on the day our company delegation arrives in your esteemed region.

Especially, P'yŏnganbuk-to (平北) is the border region, and are there not also the inexhaustible forest treasures on both sides of the Yalu River (鴨綠江)?

Is it not something we all commonly know that, geographically and historically, there are indeed many things worth seeing and many things to explore?

Let us, the publishing company and all of you, combine our strengths to achieve thorough results.<1>

**Summary**

This article serves as a notice from the Kaebyŏk Publishing Company to the residents ("brothers") of North P'yŏngan Province (P'yŏnganbuk-to). It announces the company's upcoming cultural survey of the region in May 1923, part of a larger project covering all of Korea initiated in the significant "Kyehae Year." Having already surveyed the Kyŏngsang provinces, Kaebyŏk appeals for enthusiastic local cooperation, asking residents to proactively gather detailed, factual information on the province's industry, education, religion, social issues, historical sites, customs, key figures, and institutions, both past and present, to share with the visiting survey team. The article emphasizes the power of collective effort for achieving a thorough understanding and highlights the unique importance of P'yŏnganbuk-to as a border region near the Yalu River, rich in history and resources. From a historical perspective, the article is significant as it showcases a Korean cultural nationalist effort during the colonial period to systematically document Korean society and culture region by region. Historians might find the call for grassroots data collection ("actual facts and actual figures") and the framing of the survey as a collective endeavor ("our work," "our brothers") particularly interesting, reflecting attempts to foster national consciousness and conduct social research under colonial rule. The specific focus on the resource-rich border region also hints at contemporary interests.

淸川江 以北 형제에게
一府 19郡 194面 1,438洞에 골고루 훗터져 골고루 그 - 鄕土의 樂을 누리시는 120만 平北의 兄弟시여 저의는 삼가 여러분의 萬福을 祝하오며 아울너 一言으로써 眞情을 아뢰나이다.
여러분이 이미 아시는 바와 가티 저의 開闢社는 今年이 意味 - 깁흔 癸亥年으로써 우리 槿域의 모든 文化를 踏査하야써 여러분과 가티 翫味하고 여러분과 가티 자랑하고자 하야 이미 그-일에 着手한지 달로는 2個月이요 道로는 2個道엿슴니다. 這間 慶南 慶北 2個道 답사에는 그곳 兄弟의 만흔 사랑과 만흔 도음으로써 그 곳의 文化는 不滿하나 마힘 밋는 限에서는 大槪로써 査實하얏나이다. 이에 路次는 좀 越等되는 感이 잇스나 南北을 番갈라 紹介하는 깃도 일종의 特味가 이슬 듯 하야 今番이 5月로써는 平北 訪問을 斷行하기로 社議가 一致되얏나이다.
내 地方을 가서 내 일을 하며 내 兄弟를 뵈오고 내 事情을 말하기에 무슨 딴 付託이 잇스며 또 무슨 딴 걱정이 잇겟슴니까 마는 힘은 個人의 힘과 衆人의 힘이 다르오며 딸하서 일도 個人의 일과 衆人의 일이 다릅니다. 普遍과 一遍, 徹底와 疎忽, 圓滿과 欠缺이 多衆의 共力如何에 잇싸옵기 이에 우리는
兄弟의 情誼만 밋고 敢히 懇請하오니 兄弟시여 내 일이요 내 兄弟오니 잇끌어 주시며 도아 주시옵소서. 平北에 對한 産業 敎育 宗敎 其他 社會問題의 原因 及 趣向이며 名勝古蹟 其他 人心 風俗 中心人物 及 主要機關의 紹介 及 批判 等 모든 것의 過去 及 現在를 實事대로 實數대로 兄弟께서 自進하야 査實하셧다가 저의 社員 一行이 貴地에 到着하는 날 일일히 紹介하시며 提示해 주시옵소서. 特히 平北은 國境이오며 또 鴨綠江 左右의 無盡한 山林寶庫가 잇지 아니함니까. 地理로 歷史로 果然 볼만한 것이 만흐며 探索할 것이 만흠은 吾人이 共知하는 바 안임니까. 本社와 여러분과 力을 合하야 周到한 成績을 엇도록 하옵시다.＜1＞
