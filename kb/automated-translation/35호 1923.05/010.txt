A Fundamental Survey of Korean Culture!!
						Kyŏngsŏng Kaebyŏk Publishing Company

Our company's two major plans for this year: The publication of provincial issues for each province based on a survey of Chosŏn's current situation. The gathering of a task force for the Thirteen Provinces accompanying the establishment of a supporter system.

The survey team has already departed from Kyŏngsŏng.
The sound clamoring throughout the land is the cry that Kaebyŏk Publishing Company is undertaking provincial issues as its new project for the year Kyŏhae (1923), embarking on a fundamental survey of Korean culture.
Wherein lies the intention of *Kaebyŏk* magazine in daringly carrying out this great undertaking, disregarding financial difficulties, the exhaustion from effort, and all other obstacles?

In all the world, there is no ignorance like that of knowing others' affairs but not knowing one's own, and even more ignorant than that is the person who lives without knowing how the substance of their own livelihood is faring. Look! How many of us Chosŏn people know the substance of our own livelihood, which is the situation of Chosŏn? We have many people who know others' affairs well but are relatively ignorant of their own, and many who critique others' livelihoods well but do not know how their own is faring. We view this point very gravely and have embarked on a Fundamental Survey of Korean Culture as this year's new project, and through this, we have decided to publish Provincial Issues for each province. This is purely for the purpose of having Chosŏn people understand Chosŏn well, and solely for having Chosŏn people know the substance of their own livelihood well and gain the wisdom to manage and organize it with their own hands.

However, providing the guidance and direction to enable Chosŏn people to possess the knowledge to understand Chosŏn well is by no means an easy task. To properly know the livelihood of a territory called Samch'ŏlli (Korea) and a population called 30 million, one must have a long period of one or even two years. Therefore, we have decided to divide Chosŏn into 13 provinces and investigate the substance of the 13 provinces over 13 months.

Starting from the 1st of this month, the first beginning was made with Kyŏngsangnam-do, and from the 1st of next month, it will move to Kyŏngsangbuk-do, sequentially reaching the entire territory of Chosŏn. People of all provinces, exert yourselves and arrange the materials and details one by one. And wait for what kind of descriptions and what rights and wrongs will appear on the pages of *Kaebyŏk* or *Puin* (Woman). Therefore, let our general brethren rejoice that, due to this undertaking today, they will for the first time come to know their own livelihood in detail, and thus, the entirety of Chosŏn will be engraved in our minds. The criteria by which we will survey each province this time are:
▲ Causes and Trends of Various Social Problems ▲ Introduction and Critique of Central Figures and Major Business Organizations ▲ Actual State of Human Feelings and Customs ▲ Situation of Industry, Education, and Religion ▲ Exploration of Scenic Spots, Historical Sites, and Legends ▲ Observation and Critique Concerning Other General Conditions

**Summary**

The Kaebyŏk Publishing Company announces a major year-long project for 1923: a fundamental survey of Korean culture and current conditions. They plan to publish special provincial issues of their magazine (*Kaebyŏk*, and possibly *Puin*) for each of Korea's thirteen provinces, based on field surveys conducted by their teams. The stated goal is to combat the perceived ignorance of Koreans about their own situation ("household affairs") and empower them with the knowledge to understand and manage their own circumstances. The first survey begins in Kyŏngsangnam-do. Historically, this article is significant as it demonstrates a strong nationalist drive for self-awareness and cultural documentation during the Japanese colonial period. It highlights the role of publications like *Kaebyŏk* in fostering Korean identity and undertaking ambitious projects despite political and economic constraints, providing valuable insight into the cultural and intellectual currents of the time.

朝鮮 文化의 基本 調査!!
						京城 開闢社
本社의 今年 中 二大計劃. 朝鮮의 現狀 調査에 依할 各道 道號의 刊行. 社友制의 期成에 伴할 十三道 役軍의 紏合.

踏査員 一行은 旣히 京城을 出發하다
天下가 악자하고 떠드는 소리는 開闢社가 癸亥年의 新事業으로 各道 道號를 한다, 朝鮮문화의 기본조사에 착수한다 부르짓는 것이라.
開闢 雜誌가 經濟의 困難, 努力의 困憊, 기타 모든 난관을 돌아보지 아니하고 敢히 이 大事業을 斷行하는 것은 그 뜻이 어대 잇나뇨.
天下의 無識이 남의 일은 알되 自己의 일은 모르는 것만치 無識한 일이 업고 그보다 더 無識한 것은 自己네의 살림살이 內容이 엇지 되어 가는 것을 모르고 사는 사람가티 無識한 일이 업다. 보라 우리 朝鮮 사람이 朝鮮 形便이라 하는 自己네의 살림살이의 內容을 아는 사람이 얼마나 되는가? 우리는 남의 일은 잘 알되 自己의 일은 比較的 모르는 사람이 만흐며 남의 살림살이는 잘 批評하되 自己의 살림살이는 어찌 되어 가는지 모르는 사람이 만흐다. 우리는 이 點을 甚히 **하게 보아 今年의 新事業으로 朝鮮文化의 基本調査에 着手하며 니여써 各道 道號를 刊行하기로 하얏나니 이는 純全히 朝鮮사람으로 朝鮮을 잘 理詳하자는 데 잇스며 朝鮮사람으로 自己내의 살림살이의 內容을 잘 알아 가지고 그를 自己네의 손으로 處辦하고 整理하는 聰明을 가지라 하는 데 잇는 것 뿐이다.
그러나 朝鮮사람으로 朝鮮을 잘 이해하는 知識을 가지게 할 만한 指導와 方針은 결코 容易한 일이 아니다. 적어도 三千里라 하는 강토와 3천만이라 하는 食口의 살림살이를 잘 알고저 하면 1年 乃至 2年의 長歲月을 가지지 아니하면 안 될 것이다. 그래서 우리는 朝鮮을 13道로 논하 가지고 13個月에 13道의 內容을 調査하기로 하얏다.
今月 1日로부터 첫 번 始作이 慶尙南道로 되게 되얏스며 來月 1日로부터 慶尙北道로 옴기여 順次로 朝鮮 全土에 及할 것이다. 全道 人士는 분발하야 材料와 品質을 일일히 整頓하라. 그리고 어떠한 形容과 어떠한 是非曲直이 「開闢」 또는 「婦人」의 紙上에 나타날 것을 기다리라. 그래서 우리 一般 兄弟들은 금일의 此擧에 因하야 처음으로 自己네의 살림살이를 仔細히 알게 되며 그리하야 朝鮮의 全般을 우리들 머리 속에 銘記하게 될 것을 깁버하라. 이번에 우리가 各道를 답사할 標準은
諸 제  諸社會問題의 原因 及 趍向 ▲ 中心人物 及 主要事業機關의 紹介 及 批評 ▲ 人情風俗의 實際 如何 ▲ 産業敎育 及 宗敎의 狀況 ▲  名勝古蹟 及 傳說의 探査 ▲  其他의 一般 狀勢에 關한 觀察과 批評
