Magazine Title: 개벽 제56호
Publication Date: 1925년 02월 01일
Article Title: 新春이냐 窮春이냐, 救濟를 要할 가장 緊要한 時機는 왓다. (Is it the New Spring or a Destitute Spring? The Most Crucial Time Demanding Relief Has Come.)
Author: Not specified
Article Type: 논설 (Editorial/Argumentative Essay)

Whenever the spring season arrived, we used to write phrases like "Ipch'un taegil" (立春大吉 - Great luck on the onset of spring), "Kŏnyang tagyŏng" (建陽多慶 - Establishing yang brings many blessings), "Kukt'ae minan" (國泰民安 - May the nation be peaceful and the people tranquil), "Kagŭp injok" (家給人足 - May households be supplied and people content) on our home's gates and pillars, celebrating our peaceful lives and praying for peace and abundance in the new year. If you examine our door lintels (門楣), even now you would likely find those faded ink stains (墨㾗). However, brothers, what if we consider our current situation (目下 情形)? What if we look at our present lives? The nest is overturned and the eggs are broken (覆巢卵破)—for more than ten years now (于今十有星霜), oppressed and plundered, defeated and pursued, wanting to live but having no land, wanting to work but having no place—how many have scattered outside the borders (境外 離散), supporting the old and leading the young (扶老携幼)? How many of our compatriots are now wandering the streets (路中) in worn-out clothes and bare feet (弊衣跣足)? Adding frost upon snow (雪上加霜), last year's famine (饑凶) has once again struck the frail backs of these pitiable (愛憐) compatriots. Alas, amidst old and new famines, what can be done for the three million lonely figures (隻影) who do not know where to turn?

Moreover, now is the New Spring (新春)—no, the Destitute Spring (窮春). The few sŏm and mal of grain have all disappeared during the harsh winter snow and cold (嚴冬雪寒). Trying to go out to sell labor, there is no way to pack food for even a single day's journey (餘程). Trying to undertake the spring plowing (春耕) planned for the year (計營), there is no way today to obtain even a few mal of grain seed (穀種). While relief for the destitute is always essential, the most urgent time among urgent times is surely this moment.

Brothers, did we not previously establish a Famine Relief Association (饑饉救濟會) and raise our voices for relief? The most critical time demanding relief—the Destitute Spring (窮春)—has come. The time has come to save one person and thereby save a whole family, to provide relief for one season (節期) and thereby save the entire year. What shall we do?

Facing the Ipch'un season and reflecting on our circumstances, my heart aches particularly. "Let's die together if we die, let's live together if we live." We can only have this kind of resolve. <1>

**Summary**

This editorial from the February 1925 issue of *Kaebyŏk* magazine contrasts the traditional hopeful spirit of *Ipch'un* (the start of spring) with the grim reality of widespread poverty and famine, termed *Kungch'un* (Destitute Spring), facing Koreans. The author laments over a decade of oppression, land loss, displacement, and the devastating impact of recent famine, emphasizing the desperate situation of millions. It highlights the immediate, critical need for relief as people lack food for daily survival and seeds for the crucial spring planting season, urging action and solidarity through the previously established Famine Relief Association with a sentiment of shared fate. For historians, this article provides a poignant snapshot of the socio-economic hardship under Japanese colonial rule in the mid-1920s, detailing the effects of colonial policies (land loss, displacement), recurring famines, and the emergence of Korean self-help/relief organizations. The stark language ("Destitute Spring," "nest overturned, eggs broken") reveals the depth of despair and the call for communal resilience ("live together or die together").

新春이냐 窮春이냐, 救濟를 要할 가장 緊要한 時機는 왓다.
봄쳘만 잡히면, 우리들은 우리네 집의 대문과 대문, 기둥과 기둥에 『立春大吉, 建陽多慶, 國泰民安, 家給人足』이란 등 글구를 써부치여, 우리의 태평생활을 츅복하는 동시에, 새해 1년의 歲和時豊을 비러 왓섯다. 試하야 우리네 門楣를 살핀다하면, 우리는 지금 당장이라도 그 지적지적한 墨㾗을 차저낼 것이다. 그러나 형제들아, 目下의 우리네 情形을 생각한다하면 엇더며 당장의 우리에 생활을 돌본다하면 엇더냐. 覆巢卵破-于今十有星霜에, 눌리고 앗가고, 패하고, 쪼기여, 살으려 하나 땅이 업고, 일하려 하나 터가 업서, 扶老携幼-일즉이 境外로 離散한 者는 얼마며, 弊衣跣足으로 지금 막 路中을 헤매는 동포는 얼마냐. 雪上에 加霜, 작년의 饑凶은 이 애련한 동포의 간여윈 등을 또 한번 습격하엿다. 아아 녯 기근 새 기근에 돌아갈 바를 모르는 3백만의 隻影을 엇지하면 可할고.
더욱히 지금은 新春-아니 窮春이라, 몃 섬 몃 말의 곡석도 嚴冬雪寒을 지내는 그 間에 다 업서지여, 품을 팔려 나서려 하나 단 하루 여정의 양식을 쌀 도리가 업고, 1년 計營의 春耕을 하려 하나 단 몃 말의 穀種을 구할 길이 업는 오늘이니 업는 사람에게 대한 구제가 어느 때에라고 긴요치 아느리요만, 긴요한 중에도 가장 긴급한 때는 이 때이라 할 것이다.
형제여 우리는 일즉히 饑饉救濟會를 니르켜, 구제의 소리를 놉히지 아니 하엿는가. 구제를 요할, 가장 긴중한 시기-窮春-는 왓다. 한 사람을 구제하야 왼 가족을 한 節期를 구제하야 왼 1연을 구제할 시기는 왓다. 엇지할고.
立春時節을 마즈며, 우리의 지내는 형편을 돌보니 마음이 별로 상한다. 『죽어도 가티 죽고 살아도 가티 살쟈』 우리는 다만 이러한 각오가 잇슬 뿐이리라.＜1＞
