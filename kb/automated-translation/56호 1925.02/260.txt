Publishing the Readers' Literary Works
						Hoe Wŏl

While soliciting contributions from many readers (諸氏) via the "Call for Readers' Literary Works" advertisement in the January issue of this magazine, we received many submissions. However, regarding novels more so than poetry, many exceeded the stipulated page limit by two or three times, and due to space limitations (紙面關係), we could not publish them. Of course, if the power of their content had been abundant, they would certainly have been readily included even in this section, but the content did not reach that level. Consequently, what is regrettable is that the content of the creative works relies too heavily on imitation (模倣), and no effort whatsoever is evident regarding the talent of individuality (個性). Since it is different from the outset under the name of "readers' literary works," it might differ from those who professionally study literary arts, but still, there is somewhat of a lack of anguish from individual contemplation (思索), or any light in creative reason (理智) or emotion. Among the poems published this time, Mr. Kim Ch'ang-sul's (金昌述氏) "Taedohaeng" (大道行) could be called a relatively strong work (力作), but one might suspect, to the point of doubt (疑心), whether it did not perhaps imitate the same feeling from Whitman's (휫트만) "Poem of the Road" (大道의 詩). However, I believe that if the author researches and practices somewhat more on that poem, there will be much future potential. Also, regarding the poem "Chosŏnhon'a" (朝鮮魂아) by Sangmu (想無氏), while its form is very exquisite (微妙) in some respects, it is regrettable (遺憾) that the meaning of the words lacks unity here and there (군대군대). Nevertheless, it is a very good piece. Furthermore, Mr. Chŏng Nae-dong's (丁來東氏) "Nun onun ach'im" (눈오는 아침 - Snowy Morning) is a poem that has run too far down the path of imagination (想像). It would be better if it had a bit more sense of reality (現實味). Also, the poem "Net Hwan'gyŏngŭrobut'ŏ" (넷 環境으로부터 - From Four Environments) by Kim Yŏng-su (金榮秀) does not evoke any emotion (感激) whatsoever, just as the title suggests. If possible, I wish a newer title had been considered.

Besides these, there were a few more poems that could have been published, but because the "debate on class literature" is particularly important for the literary section this time, there was not enough spare space (餘分의 紙面) for them, so we concluded the editing with just these for this issue.＜87＞

**Summary**

The editor, Hoe Wŏl, expresses disappointment with reader-submitted literary works, particularly novels that exceeded length limits and lacked quality. The main criticism is the prevalence of imitation and the absence of individual creativity and effort. Specific critiques are given for several published poems, praising Kim Ch'ang-sul's potential despite suspected imitation of Whitman, noting Sangmu's formal skill but lack of coherence, Chŏng Nae-dong's excessive imagination, and Kim Yŏng-su's lack of emotional impact. Publication space was limited due to a feature on the "class literature debate." From a historical perspective, this piece is interesting as it reflects editorial concerns about literary originality and individuality in colonial Korea, shows the influence of Western authors like Whitman, and highlights the contemporary relevance of ideological debates about class or proletarian literature in the mid-1920s literary scene.

讀者文藝를 發表하면서
						懷月
本誌 1월호에 「독자 문예모집」 광고를 내면서 만흔 독자 諸氏의 투고를 바덧다. 그러나 詩보다도 小說에 이르러서는 일정하게 규정한 폐지가 초과하기 2,3배에 이르러서 紙面關係로 기재하지 못하엿다. 그러나 그것의 내용의 힘이 풍부하면 물론 본란에라도 싯게될 것이지만 내용이 그러케까지는 못되엿다. 따러서 섭섭한 것은 창작의 내용이 너무도 모방에 심하고 개성의 재능 여하에는 아모러한 노력이 보이지 안는단 말이다. 독자문예라는 일홈부터 다르니깐 문예를 전문으로 연구하는 사람보다는 다르겟지마는 역시 다소간이라도 개성의 사색에 고민이나 또한 창작적 이지나 감정에 무슨 광명이 업다. 이번에 발표한 詩 가운데는 金昌述氏의 「大道行」이 좀 역작이라고 하겟스나 혹 휫트만의 「大道의 詩」라는데서 가튼 감정을 모방하지나 안이•˜엿나 할만치 의심스러웁니다. 그러나 그 詩에는 다소간 더 필자가 연구하고 연습하면 만흔 미래가 잇을 줄로 안다. 또한 想無氏의 「朝鮮魂아」라는 詩는 어느 점에서는 형식이 매우 미묘한 점이 잇스나 말의 뜻이 군대군대 통일되지 못한 것이 유감이다. 그러나 매우 조흔 작품이다. 또 丁來東氏의 「눈오는 아침」은 너무도 상상에 길로 다러난 詩다. 좀더 현실미가 잇섯스면 좃겟다. 또 「넷 環境으로부터」라는 金榮秀의 詩는 제목부터 그럿치만 아모러한 감격을 주지 못한다. 할 수 잇섯으면 좀더 새로운 제목을 생각하엿으면 한다.
그 외에도 좀더 발표할 詩도 잇섯스나 이번 문예란에는 특별히 「계급문학 시비론」이 중요한 것임으로 그것으로 여분의 紙面이 넉넉지 못해서 이번에는 이것으로써 편집을 맛첫다.＜87＞
