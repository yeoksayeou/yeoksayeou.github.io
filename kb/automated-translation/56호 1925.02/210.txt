Magazine Title: 개벽 제56호
Publication Date: 1925년 02월 01일
Article Title: 나는 이 나랏사람의 자손이외다 (I am a descendant of this nation's people)
Author: 梁柱東
Article Type: 시 (Poem)

I am a descendant of this nation's people
						Yang Chu-dong

The people of this nation
Their hearts are whiter than their clothes
And wine and song
They love like their wives.
I am a descendant of this nation's people

Good and humble
Full of dreams and full of laughter
But powerless and bloodless
These people of this nation—
Ah, I am a descendant of this nation's people<41>

The people of this nation
Their hearts are poorer than their homes
And peace and freedom
They love like their brothers.
I am a descendant of this nation's people.

Lonely and desolate
Full of suffering and full of tears
But with breath and with life
These people of this nation—
Ah, I am a descendant of this nation's people
(1924)<42>

**Summary**

This poem by Yang Chu-dong is a poignant affirmation of Korean identity and heritage during a period of hardship (likely the Japanese colonial era, given the date). The speaker expresses deep connection and pride in being a "descendant of this nation's people," highlighting perceived national characteristics: inner purity (whiter than clothes), love for culture (wine and song), peacefulness, and valuing freedom. Despite acknowledging the people's suffering, poverty, powerlessness, and sorrow, the poem emphasizes their enduring spirit, dreams, and fundamental life force ("with breath and with life"). For historians, the poem offers insight into the resilience of Korean national consciousness under colonial rule. It showcases an attempt to define and cherish Korean identity through cultural traits and inner virtues, contrasting with the external conditions of subjugation and hardship, reflecting a blend of pride and lament common in the era's literature.

나는 이 나랏사람의 자손이외다
						梁柱東
이 나랏사람은
마음이 그의 옷보다 희고
술과 노래를
그의 안해와 가티 사랑함니다.
나는 이 나랏사람의 자손이외다

착하고 겸손하고
꿈 만코 웃음 만으나
힘업고 피업는
이 나랏사람-
아 나는 이 나랏사람의 자손이외다＜41＞

이 나랏사람은
마음이 그의 집보다 가난하고
평화와 자유를
그의 형뎨와 가티 사랑함니다.
나는 이 나랏사람의 자손이외다.

외로웁고 쓸쓸하고
괴로움 만코 눈물 만으나
숨ㅅ결잇고 생명잇는
이 나랏사람-
아 나는 이 나랏사람의 자손이외다
