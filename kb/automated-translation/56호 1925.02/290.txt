Magazine Title: 개벽 제56호
Publication Date: 1925-02-01
Article Title: 餘言 (Postscript / Additional Remarks)
Author: 春坡 (Ch'unp'a)
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice / Editorial Postscript)

The January issue achieved better-than-expected results, and we are pleased, along with our readers, that we issued a second printing just 10 days after publication.

Just as the January issue was that much more substantial and vigorous, fitting for having aged one year, we are confident that the February issue, having passed one more month, will have developed that much further.

Just as the creative writing section of the January issue received a great welcome from readers, we believe that no one will be able to resist reading the debate on class literature in the February issue.

Not to mention the overall review of the January creative writing scene,

We had planned to publish in this magazine Tonggok-kun's "Revolutionary Literature," Yi Sŏnghwan-kun's "Measures for the Relief of the Masses," Na San-kun's "Japan's Proletarian Parties and Korea," and Chŏng Paek-kun's "A Letter to Rural Youth." However, unfortunately, all four of these * were subjected to full censorship (全文 삭제) and were lost. There is nothing more to say.

We are extremely curious as to where Cha Sangch'an-kun, who left for the North Ch'ungch'ŏng Province issue, might be now. Is he receiving much affection from the local figures there? Amidst the white moon and white snow, is he free from the sorrows of being away from home (客愁)? As a veteran of a hundred battles, his mind and body must surely be robust. In any case, we trust in the goodwill (厚意) of the local figures.

In the March issue, the North Ch'ungch'ŏng Province fieldwork report (忠北査 踏記) will naturally be published. (Following 4 lines deleted)

It is tiresome to say this repeatedly. We would appreciate it if requests for manuscript returns ceased from now on. Having announced the primary formal [selection/rules?], we absolutely cannot employ personal feelings of favoritism or discrimination (何厚何薄의 私情). Our company absolutely will not comply with such requests. We ask that you submit manuscripts with this understanding.

Many requests like this come from readers: Please write articles in a popular, simple style so that everyone can read them. That is, requests to mix in Hanmun (漢文), provide annotations for technical terms (術語), clearly separate phrases and clauses—and thus eliminate any sense of tediousness. Indeed, that is so. The editor will pay particular attention, and it would be good if contributing gentlemen (諸氏) also understand this point as much as possible. (Ch'unp'a)＜90＞

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the February 1925 issue of *Gaebyeok* magazine celebrates the success of the January issue, mentions upcoming features like a debate on class literature and a report on North Ch'ungch'ŏng Province, and outlines editorial policies regarding manuscript returns and the need for clearer, more accessible writing for a wider readership. It bluntly states that four planned articles covering topics like revolutionary literature, mass relief, Japanese proletarian parties and Korea, and rural youth were entirely censored. Historically, the article is significant for its open admission of Japanese censorship (全文 삭제 - full text deletion) targeting specific political and social topics, highlighting the constraints on intellectual discourse during the colonial period. The discussion about making content accessible (using fewer difficult Hanja terms, annotating jargon) also reflects the ongoing efforts in literacy and intellectual popularization movements of the time.

餘言
1월호는 豫期以上 好成績으로 발행 10일만에 재판을 발행한 것은 독자와 가티 깁뻐하는 바이다. 1월호가 한 살 더 먹으니 만치 그러케 묵직하고 또 씩씩하얏스니 2월호는 한 달 더 지낸 것 만치 그만콤 더 발전되리라고 자신한다.

1월호의 창작란이 독자의 대환영을 밧은 것과 가티 2월호의 계급문학 시비론은 누구든지 안이 닑고는 못 백이리라 한다. 1월 창작계 총평은 말 말고라도

本誌에다가 東谷君의 「革命文學」 李晟煥君의 「民衆救濟策」 挐山君의 「日本의 無産政黨과 朝鮮」 鄭栢君의 「農村靑年에게 보내는 글월」을 심엇든 터이 그러나 불행히 右四*이 모다 全文 삭제를 당하고 마럿다. 다시 무어라고 할 말이 업다.

忠北道 號를 爲하야 떠난 車相瓚君은 지금 어대쯤 가섯는지 몹시도 궁금하다. 當地人士의 사랑이나 만히 밧고 지내는지 月白하고 雪白한데 客愁나 업는지? 百戰老將이라 심신이 俱健하겟지 여하간 當地人士의 후의만 밋는다.

3월호에는 母論 忠北査 踏記가 발표된다.(이하 4行 삭제)

번번히 말하기도 귀찬은 일이다. 원고 반환요구는 이제부터 그만두어스면 조켓다. 1차 정규를 발표한 이상 何厚何薄의 私情은 도저히 쓸 수 업다. 本社는 절대로 그 요구에 응치 안으련다. 이런 양해를 가지고 투고해 줌을 발란다.

讀者로부터 이런 요구가 만히 드러온다. 제발 통속으로 평이하게 만인이 다 보도록 글을 써 달나고. 즉 漢文들 석고 術語는 註釋하고 구절구절을 꼭꼭 때고-그리해서 지리의 감이 업도록 해 달나는 말이다. 果然 그럿타. 편집자도 특히 주의하려니와 기고 諸氏가 이것을 만츰 諒解함이 조켓다. (春坡)＜90＞
