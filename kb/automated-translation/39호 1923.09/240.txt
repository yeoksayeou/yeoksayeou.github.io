餘墨 (Afterthoughts/Editorial Notes)

Again, due to page constraints, many items announced in the previous issue could not be included. More than feeling sorry to the readers, we feel remorseful (罪悚하다) towards those who sent in the writings.

Due to the author's circumstances, the continuation of "When the Eyes Barely Open" in the Literary Arts section could not be published. However, it is certain to be concluded by the next issue.

Regarding the frequent inclusion of serialized works in this magazine, we ourselves cannot say it is ideal. However, when we believe that publishing a certain piece is beneficial for the readers, it is troublesome that 7 or 8 out of 10 of those turn out to be serials. Understanding this situation, we hope you will appreciate (諒解해 주기를) why serial works are included.

Starting from the next issue, we intend to establish a special column titled "Editor's Room and Readers" to facilitate the exchange of opinions between the editors and readers, or among readers themselves. That is, if many of you send in contributions, it will begin right away.

The collection of famous literary quotes (文豪名句集) related to Mr. Chŏng Yŏngt'ae's (丁榮泰) selections could not be included in this issue. We ask for his forbearance (寬恕).

The articles for the P'yŏngbuk Province (平北道) special issue have concluded for now with this issue. The reporter who explored (踏査) that area wrote down a great deal (수둑하게 만핫스나), but due to editorial circumstances, only that much has been published for now (爲先).

Among the regional correspondents (地方踏査員), Mr. Ch'a Sangch'an (車相瓚), head of our company's Political and Economic Department (政經部主任), departed early for Kangwŏn Province (江原道). Actually, he should have left a bit earlier, but he was slightly delayed (느젓다) due to the weather. We hope that the care (愛護) of our brethren (兄弟) will be upon him in all the places he visits (所經各處).

Starting from the October issue of this magazine, we will publish "Outline of Socialist Doctrines" (社會主義 學說의 大要), an easy-to-understand (一覽暸然) explanation of socialism based on Mr. Sakai Toshihiko's (堺利彦) explanations. Although countless writings and words about socialism, this way and that, have been published during this time, there likely hasn't been (업섯스리라) an explanation as systematic and clear as this.<151>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the September 1923 issue of *Kaebyŏk* addresses several housekeeping matters. The editors apologize for unpublished articles due to space limits, justify the inclusion of serial works despite drawbacks, announce a new reader interaction column, mention delays for specific contributions (Chŏng Yŏngt'ae's quotes), conclude the P'yŏngbuk Province feature, report on correspondent Ch'a Sangch'an's fieldwork trip to Kangwŏn Province, and announce an upcoming serialized explanation of socialism by the Japanese socialist Sakai Toshihiko. For historians, the piece reveals the practical challenges of magazine publishing (space constraints), editorial strategies (justifying serials), reader engagement efforts, the magazine's focus on regional reporting (P'yŏngbuk, Kangwŏn), and importantly, the introduction and planned systematic explanation of socialist thought to a Korean audience in 1923, reflecting the intellectual currents of the time under Japanese rule. The mention of Sakai Toshihiko highlights the influence of Japanese intellectuals on Korean discourse.

餘墨
또 頁數의 관계로, 前號에 豫告한 것도 못 실은 것이 맛핫다. 독자에게 미안하다함 보담도 그 글을 보내주신 이에게 罪悚하다.
文藝篇 중 『눈을 겨우 뜰때』의 續稿가, 작자의 사정으로 해서 揭載되지 못하엿다. 그러나 來號까지에 완결될 것은 확실하다.
本誌 중에, 繼續品이 작고 실리게 되는 것은 우리 스스로 생각할 때에도, 좃타고 하지 못하얏다. 그러나 엇던 것을 꼭 揭載함이, 독자를 위해서 利가 될 것을 밋는 때에, 엇던 그것이, 10에 7,8. 繼續物이 되는 데에는 괴롭다. 이쯤 생각하는 밋헤서, 繼續品이 編入되는 것을 諒解해 주기를 바라는 바이다.
來號브터는 『편집실과 독자』라는 欄을 特設하야, 編者와 讀者, 혹은 讀者와 讀者와의 의견교환을 行햇스면 한다. 즉 여러분이 만히 써보낸다 하면 곳 시작할 것이다.
丁榮泰씨의 抄錄에 係한 文豪名句集은, 本號에 들지 못하게 되엿다. 氏의 寬恕를 빈다.
平北道號의 기사는 이번 號로 爲先 끗이낫다. 그 곳을 踏査한 기자의 써 노은 것은, 수둑하게 만핫스나, 편집형편상 爲先 그만큼 발표하얏다.
地方踏査員 중 본사의 政經部主任 車相瓚군은 일즉이 江原道를 향하야 떠나섯다. 사실은 좀 더 전에 떠낫슬 것이나, 기후의 관계로 조곰 느젓다. 所經各處에, 兄弟의 愛護가, 그의 身上에 잇기를 바란다.
본지 10月號부터 堺利彦氏의 설명에 係한 『社會主義 학설의 大要』라는, 一覽暸然의 社會主義 설명문을 발표하겟다. 그 동안 社會主義에 대한, 이러니 저러니 한 글이나 말이 수 업시 발표되엇스나, 이만큼 체계적이오 분명한 설명은 업섯스리라.＜151＞
