開闢社 廣告部 校正係＜105＞

Correction Notice

Regarding the congratulatory advertisement for the publication of the Kyŏngbuk Province Issue featured in our 38th issue, the character '院' (Wŏn) in Taegu Talsŏng Hospital (大邱達城醫院) was mistakenly printed as '海' (Hae), and for Taegu Kŭmnŭng Hospital (大邱金陵醫院), the character '院' (Wŏn) was not clearly printed due to a typesetting issue. We hereby express our apologies for this oversight.

Kaebyŏk Company, Advertising Department, Proofreading Section <105>

**Summary**

This short article is a correction notice published by Kaebyŏk Company. It addresses two errors found in a congratulatory advertisement in the previous issue (No. 38), which celebrated a special "Kyŏngbuk Province Issue". The errors involved misprinted or unclear Hanja characters for the names of two hospitals in Taegu: Taegu Talsŏng Hospital and Taegu Kŭmnŭng Hospital. The notice apologizes for the lack of attention during proofreading. For historians, this notice provides insight into the operational practices of a major Korean magazine during the colonial period, including its interaction with regional advertisers (hospitals), the challenges of printing technology (typesetting errors), and the existence of special regional-focused issues.
