Additional Remarks

These are long, long summer days. These are recent days when even a single layer of hemp clothing feels unbearably burdensome on the body. On top of that, the early heat continues. The toil and worries of our sweating comrades must be all the more severe.

We, who have taken on a particular duty and strive only in that duty to be true friends to our compatriots, endeavoured to put in extra effort for this month's issue, but how did it actually turn out?

We believe that translating and publishing the masterpieces of various literary giants as a special supplement in this issue is significant in many ways. Readers, too, might find it more pleasant than reading rather uninspired original creations.

Summer is an unusual season among the four. It demands灑脫 (carefreeness) and 解放 (liberation) from everyone. Clothes worn are shed, and hidden skin is revealed. One seeks the breeze by the 淸江 (clear river) and whistles in the 녹음 (green shade). If it's good, it's a truly wonderful time. Let's try to spend it exceedingly well. Haha. <78>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the July 1922 issue of *Kaebyŏk* reflects on the summer heat and its effect on people, acknowledges the effort put into the current issue, and highlights the inclusion of translated literary works as a supplement, suggesting readers might prefer these to mediocre original pieces. The author ends with a cheerful wish for readers to enjoy the liberating aspects of summer. For historians, this piece offers a glimpse into the editorial voice and priorities of a major Korean cultural magazine during the colonial period. The mention of favouring translations over potentially subpar original works might reflect the state of Korean literary production or editorial strategy at the time, navigating constraints and seeking reader engagement amidst difficult conditions.

餘言
長長 夏日이외다. 베옷 한 껍흘이 몸에 겨워서 못 견듸여 하는 近日이외다. 게다가 또 早炎가 繼續합니다. 땀 흘리는 동무들의 苦勞와 우려가 一層 甚할 것이외다.

別로 이 마튼 바가 잇서 오즉 그 마튼 바의 일에서 同胞의 眞實한 벗이 되어오노라 한 우리는 本月號에 잇서 一段의 努力을 더하노라 하엿사오나 事實 어찌 되엇는지오.

本號의 附錄으로 特히 여러 文豪의 名作을 譯載한 것은 여러 가지로 생각하야 意義가 잇는 일이라 自思합니다. 讀者 여러분으로도 돌이어 시원치 못한 創作品을 닑는 것보다 快할 것이외다.

여름이란 4節 中에도 異常한 節이외다. 萬人에게 灑脫, 解放을 要求합니다. 입엇던 옷도 벗어 부티며 감추엇던 살도 들어내입니다. 淸江에 바람을 찻고 녹음에 휘파람을 불게 됩니다. 됴타면 썩 됴흔 때이지요. 썩 됴히 지내보기로 합시다. 呵呵.＜78＞
