Magazine Title: 개벽 第25호
Publication Date: 1922년 07월 10일
Article Title: 大同江 (創作) (Daedong River (Creative Work))
Author: 岸曙 (Ansŏ)
Article Type: 시 (Poem)

My Sorrow

On the banks of Nŭngna Island (綾羅島),
When the flowers of the willow tree
Are blown by the idle wind,
Making ripples on the water's surface,
My "sorrow" was born.

The scent of the willow flowers, still yet,
Mixed with my leaf-like "sorrow,"
Far away, aimlessly wanders
The blue, blue flower of the May sky.

Moon

Tonight too, <116>
Quietly, lonely,
Treading the same path, rising,
O Moon, O eternal traveler.

In the round, wide sky,
How many times have your steps been taken!
On your pale, vexed
Face,
(Look, still yet,)
A smile of weariness (倦怠) lingers.

Recollection

At dusk when the autumn wind cries,
A single flower bloomed profoundly,
Its lofty fragrance (芳香) scatters unknowingly,
Oh, flower of lost thoughts.

Flower tormented through the whole night,
Flower that will wither tomorrow,
Your name is a dirge (弔歌) of the past. <117>

In the quiet night, on the sea resting quietly,
Like a faint, flickering fire,
For a while, it will be a flower in the heart.

Oh, the whispering sound of that vanishing flower,
Burdened heart, listen attentively,
And cherish it deep inside.

Theory of Yawning

The chaotic yawn of fatigue,
The yawn emerging from broken thoughts,
And the strangeness of the yawn of love,
The yawn of life's unbearable heavy burden,
If there is still a yawn after that,
That must indeed be the Buddha's leisurely yawn.

Mouth

All manner of calamities and illnesses (禍病) enter through the mouth,
All manner of calamities and illnesses arise from the mouth,
Indeed, the song recited by this mouth of mine
Is the groan (呻吟) of the Malady appearing at the end of the century, <118>
The strange breath burying the corpse (死體) of love.

Night in Andong County (安東縣)

In Andong County (安東縣), white snow falls all night long.
Beautifully tonight, lying on the snow, it is silent.
The darker the night becomes, the whiter it gets.

In Andong County, the pale lamplight flickers deep into the night.
The coolie (苦力), tonight too, wanders wrapped in snow.
The dimmer the light becomes, the more it seems about to go out.

In Andong County, the snow falling without a sound,
In Andong County, the light shining vacantly,
In Andong County, the night growing deeper,
My heart weeps endlessly. <119>
<116-119>

**Summary**

This collection of six short poems by Ansŏ, published in 1922, explores themes of sorrow, the passage of time, memory, weariness, the duality of the mouth (source of sustenance and trouble), and social observation. The poems often employ natural imagery (willows, moon, flowers, snow) contrasted with human emotions like melancholy, fatigue, and a sense of loss or burden. The final poem, "Night in Andong County," offers a stark, atmospheric depiction of hardship, contrasting the silent, beautiful falling snow with the suffering of a wandering coolie under a flickering lamplight, evoking a sense of quiet despair. For historians, the poems, particularly the last one, offer insight into the somber mood and perhaps the social realities experienced by some during the early Japanese colonial period in Korea. The mention of "Andong County" and the "coolie" provides a specific geographical and social marker, while the overall tone reflects a pervasive sense of melancholy and fatigue potentially linked to the era's atmosphere.

大同江 (創作)
						岸曙
내서름

綾羅島 기슭의
실버드나무의 꼿이
한가롭은 바람에 불니어서
水面에 잔무늬를 노흘 때,
나의 「서름」은 생기엇서라.

버들꼿의 香내는, 아즉도 오히려,
落葉가튼 나의 「서름」에 석기어,
저 멀니, 새파란 새파란 五月의
하늘 꼿을 方向도 업시 헤매고 잇서라.

달

오늘밤에도＜116＞
고요히 외롭게
가튼 길을 밟으며 오르는
달이어, 오오 永久의 길손이어.

둥굴고 넓은 하늘에는
그대의 거름이 몃 번이든고!
핼금하게도 역정난
그대의 얼굴에는
(보아라, 아즉도 오히려,)
倦怠의 微笑가 띄어 잇서라.

追憶

가을 바람이 울부짓는 저녁 때,
그윽하게도 피어난 꼿한송이,
놉흔 芳香은 모르게 흐터지어라,
오오 일허진 생각의 꼿이어.

왼하룻밤을 시달닌 꼿이어,
來日이면 슬어질 꼿이어,
너의 이름은 過去의 弔歌러라.＜117＞

고요한 밤, 고요히 쉬는 바다에,
번듯거리는 희미한 불과도 가티,
한동안은 맘속에 잇슬 꼿이어.

오오 업서질 그꼿의 소삭이는 소리를,
바리운 맘이어, 맘하여 들으라,
그리하고 속깁히 貴해하여라.

하픔論

뒤숭숭한 疲勞의 하픔,
슬어진 생각때문에 나오는 하픔,
그리하고 사랑의 하픔의 야릇함이어,
人生의 참을 수 업는 무거운 짐의 하픔,
그 다음에도 오히려 하픔이 잇다 하면은,
그야말로 佛처님의 한가롭은 하픔이러라.



입

온갓의 禍病은 입으로 들어가고,
온갓의 禍病은 입에서 생겨라,
그러하다, 나의 이 입으로 을퍼진 노래는
世紀끄테 생기는 Malady의 呻吟,＜118＞
사랑의 死體를 파뭇는 야릇한 숨소리러라.

安東縣의 밤

安東縣에 하얀 눈이 밤새도록 내립니다.
곱게도 오늘밤은 눈우에 누어 잠잠합니다.
볼스록 캄캄한 밤은 볼스록 희여만집니다.

安東縣에 보얀 燈불은 밤깁도록 깜박입니다.
苦力은 오늘밤도 눈속에 싸여 해매입니다.
볼스록 희미한 불은 볼스록 꺼질듯합니다.

安東縣에 소리도 업시 내리는 눈,
安東縣에 속도 업시 반득이는 불,
安東縣에 볼스록 감해지는 밤,
내맘에는 하염업시 눈물집니다. ＜119＞
＜116-119＞
