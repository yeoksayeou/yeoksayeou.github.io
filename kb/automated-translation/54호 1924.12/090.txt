**Body Text Translation:**

Kyŏngsŏng Miscellaneous Talk

When Maruyama, the Police Affairs Bureau Chief, was replaced and left Chosŏn, Pak Chungyang, the Governor of Ch'ungbuk Province, saw him off as far as Ch'up'ungnyŏng Pass and gave him one mountain raccoon dog (山狸) pelt as a commemorative gift. Now, in the current wind of administrative restructuring, it's said that Mr. Pak will also face dismissal (馘首). If there were a friend in Ch'ungbuk as loyal as Mr. Pak, they might give him a wild dog (野犬) pelt to commemorate his having eaten seaweed soup [idiom for being dismissed] as the "Wild Dog Extermination Lord Mayor." However, in Chosŏn custom, mountain dog meat is originally a great taboo (大忌物), so what can be done?

Speaking of rumors of governor dismissals (知事 馘首說), Mr. Yi Kyuwan, the Hamnam Governor who managed to cling to his governorship through threats against the Government-General after being targeted for dismissal several times during restructuring periods, could not exert his foolhardiness any longer this time and ended up going to Ch'unch'ŏn to become an apple (苹果) merchant. Mr. Yun Kappyŏng, the Kangwŏn Governor and a remnant (餘物) of the Ilchinhoe, also completely lost his spirit (꽁지가 빠저서) and had to bid farewell to the Sinyŏn'gang ferry landing. However, there is still no news about Kim Kwanhyŏn, the Governor of Ch'ungnam, known as the "Kalsipan Governor" (갈시판知事). Although he milks the story of being hit in the arm by a stray bullet during the Russo-Japanese War and having the broken bone heal like an old dog's bone, if he gets left out again in this restructuring wave, the people of Ch'ungnam will truly have a wretched fate. However, Mr. Kim Chin-*, the county magistrate in Tangjin who does all sorts of things relying on Governor Kim's influence and is the younger brother of his concubine (妾弟), seems safe for now. (Governor Kim is ignorant and calls the bulletin board [게시판] a 'kalsipan' [갈시판].)

Everyone would be surprised if a dog became president or emperor. However, Mr. Cho Pongam of the Sinhŭng Youth Association (新興靑年會) calls his dog "Kaiser," and Mr. Yi Ch'ŏl of the Kŏnsŏlsa (建設社) calls his dog "Wilson." Whenever they call "Kaiser" or "Wilson," the dogs come running, wagging their tails. Behold! How contemporary people truly disdain the military cliques (군벌) and capitalist cliques (자본벌).

An old fogey (老物) named Kim Sugyŏng, born in Tonch'ŏn County, P'yŏngnam Province, claiming to express gratitude for the Governor-General's grace (恩), sent several dozen home-grown fresh pears (自家産生梨) along with a letter of appreciation to the Governor-General. The Governor-General reportedly expressed thanks and then gave the old fogey a tour of Kyŏngsŏng city in a government-sponsored (어용) automobile. It is indeed an honor for a countryside old fogey to tour Kyŏngsŏng in a government car just for a few fresh pears, but one wonders when his stomach, which produced those pears, will be begging again.
＜48＞

**Summary**

This satirical piece from 1924 comments on the recent administrative restructuring in colonial Korea, mocking several pro-Japanese Korean officials facing dismissal. It highlights the perceived sycophancy of officials like Pak Chungyang and Kim Sugyŏng towards Japanese authorities, contrasts it with the precarious positions of others like Yi Kyuwan and Yun Kappyŏng, and criticizes the alleged corruption and ignorance of Kim Kwanhyŏn. The anecdotes about dogs named "Kaiser" and "Wilson" serve as a commentary on the public's disdain for established power structures. For historians, the article offers a contemporary, critical Korean perspective on colonial administration, collaboration, political maneuvering, and public sentiment during a period of bureaucratic change under Japanese rule. The specific names, incidents, and satirical tone provide insights into the social and political atmosphere of Kyŏngsŏng (Seoul) in the mid-1920s.

京城雜話
忠北도지사 朴重陽군은 遞任된 丸山警務局長이 조선을 떠날 때에 秋風嶺까지 餞送하고 山狸 1필을 기념품으로 주더니 금번 행정정리 바람에 朴군도 또한 馘首를 당하게 된단다. 忠北에 만일 朴군처럼 의리 잇는 친구가 잇다하면 野犬이라도 1필 주어 野犬 撲殺大監의 미역국 먹은 기념품을 삼을터이다. 그러나 조선 습관상에 산에 개고기는 원래 大忌物이니 奈何.
知事 馘首說이 낫스닛가 말이지 멧 번식이나 정리통에 드렷다가 총독부를 위협한 덕분에 知事位를 억지로 가지고 잇던 咸南知事 李圭完군도 이번에는 그 만용을 더 부리지 못하고 春川에 가서 苹果 장사를 하게 되고 一進會 餘物인 江原知事 尹甲炳군도 아주 꽁지가 빠저서 新淵江 배터를 하직하게 되얏다. 그러나 갈시판知事 忠南 金寬鉉군은 아즉 아모 소식이 업다. 日露전쟁시 지내가는 총알에 팔 한 번을 맛고 부러진 뼈를 묵은 개뼈 모양으로 잘 울겨먹는다마는 이번 정리통에 또 빠지고보면 忠南의 인민이야 참 팔자가 구질 것이다. 그러나 金군의 세력을 밋고 唐津에서 별별 일을 다하는 金군의 妾弟 金振* 군수는 아직 안심할 듯.(金지사가 무식하야 게시판을 갈시판이라함)
개가 대통령이나 황제가 되얏다면 누구나 다 놀날 것이다. 그런데 新興靑年會의 曹奉岩군은 자기집 개의 이름들을 「카이젤」이라 하고 건설사의 李鐵군은 또 자기집 개의 이름을 「웰손」이라 하야 아모때라도 「카이젤」 「웰손」하면 그 개가 꼬리를 치고 잘 다러온다. 보라! 현대인의 군벌 자본벌을 천시함이 과연 如何.
平南 頓川郡 출생인 金洙京이란 老物은 총독의 恩을 謝한다 칭하고 自家産生梨 수십개와 사례장을 동봉하야 총독에게 밧치엿더니 총독은 감사하다고 또 그 老物을 어용 자동차에 태워서 京城 시가를 한박퀴 구경식혓단다. 향촌 노물이 生梨 몃 개를 가지고 어용 자동차로 京城 구경한 것도 영광은 영광이다만은 그 자의 준 배속은 언재 또 비러먹을는지.
＜48＞
