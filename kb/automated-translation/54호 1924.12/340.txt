餘言 (Epilogue)

It goes. The year Kapcha (甲子) goes. It passes by swiftly, without pause. It cannot be held back, nor pleaded with; it just rushes by as it pleases. The crowds of falsehood and the crowds of deceit offer their usual year-end greetings (送年辭) and lament the usual passage of time (歲月去). But it is a stern fact. Even *Kaebyŏk* (開闢子) could not avoid attaching the name "Year-End Issue" (送年號).

Indeed. It is the Year-End Issue. Because it is the Year-End Issue, we have comprehensively reviewed all external and internal matters concerning the Kapcha year. However, due to page limitations or unfinished matters, we only managed to cover the first half this time. It is no exaggeration to say you can learn of Chosŏn's gains and losses, and the world's changes—here. We confidently state that you will find no other place to look for this besides this magazine (本誌).

We have included the continuation of Ch'ŏng'o's (靑吾君) Hamnam Travelogue (咸南踏査記), which was incomplete (未*) from the previous issue (前號). While the usual statistical articles may hold little interest, if you sit quietly holding this magazine amidst the hundred types of people centered around Hamhŭng (咸興) and Wŏnsan (元山), the diverse figures of northern Chosŏn (北鮮人物) will reveal themselves one by one, some laughing, some crying—all sorts of lively dramas will unfold. Who then would feel the length of the long winter night (冬夜長)?

Along with the year's end (歲暮), human society becomes quite busy. There's sending off the old year, and with preparations for welcoming the new (*上), there's barely time to blink. Despite this busyness, we offer many thanks to Brother An Sŏkchu (安碩柱 兄) who provided 24 cartoon topics (漫畵 24題) for this magazine, and how can we express our gratitude to Brothers Na Pin (羅彬), Pak Chonghwa (朴鍾和), Kim Kijin (金基鎭), and Pak Yŏnghŭi (朴英熙 諸兄)!

We are especially sorry that quite a few articles, though advertised, had to be unavoidably postponed to the next issue (來號) due to page constraints. Above all, it is a matter of shared frustration with our readers that Brother Yang Paekhwa's (梁白華 兄) play "Wang So-gun" (王昭君) will be published in two parts. Brother Yŏm Sangsŏp's (廉尙燮 兄) review of the literary scene over the past year was snatched away by the *Sidae Ilbo*'s (時代報) year-end/new-year feature, and the review of the past year in the labor-farmer movement (勞農운동) also has no choice but to be included in the New Year Issue (新年號).

We will leave the Year-End Issue at this and, from this moment (œ부터 - typo for 이제부터?), devote all our energy to the New Year Issue. We are planning something quite grand, in our own way, to make the New Year Issue truly worthy of its name. Whether it becomes a lie or reality, let's wait and see, readers. Also, starting from the New Year Issue, we have realized the reference section (참고문단), which was a pending task shared with readers. Let's combine our strengths by sending many fine manuscripts (玉稿) and try to build a new literary forum (신문단) in the New Year! (Ch'unp'a 春坡)<163>

**Summary**

This editor's postscript from the December 1924 issue of *Kaebyŏk* reflects on the end of the Kapcha year, introducing the "Year-End Issue." It highlights the issue's review of the year's events (though only the first half), promotes Ch'ŏng'o's travelogue on northern Korea, thanks contributors like An Sŏkchu and others, and apologizes for postponing several articles, including Yang Paekhwa's play and reviews of the literary scene and the labor-farmer movement, due to space limits. The editor, Ch'unp'a, concludes by promising a significant New Year Issue and announcing the launch of a reader contribution section to foster a "new literary forum." For historians, the piece offers insights into the editorial practices and content priorities of a major Korean cultural magazine during the colonial period. The specific mention of delayed articles on the literary world (Yŏm Sangsŏp) and the burgeoning labor/farmer movements provides clues about contemporary intellectual and socio-political concerns, while the call for reader manuscripts hints at efforts to cultivate a national literary sphere under Japanese rule.

餘言
간다. 甲子년은 간다. 것침도 업시 훨훨 넘어 간다. 붓잡을 수도 업고 사정할 수도 업시 제멋대로 훨훨 넘어간다. 허위의 무리 간교의 무리들은 예의 送年辭를 하며 예의 歲月去를 嘆한다. 그러나 엄정한 사실이다. 開闢子도 送年號라는 名을 안이 붓칠 수 업섯다.

그러타. 送年號다. 送年號이기 때문으로 甲子 1년에 대한 대외 대내의 모든 事를 總觀해 노핫다. 그러나 頁數의 관계 또는 未盡의 事로써 상반기만 이번에는 보고 말앗다. 朝鮮의 得失, 세계의 변동-그것의 聞知는 과언이 안이다. 本誌가 안이고는 차자볼 곳이 업스리라고 장담해 둔다.

前號의 未*인 靑吾君의 咸南踏査記를 첨부햇다. 例의 통계적 기사야 별 흥미가 잇스랴마는 咸興, 元山을 중심한 人物百態下에 고요히 本誌를 들고 안즈면 형형색색의 北鮮人物이 낫나치 或笑或泣에 별 활극이 다 나올 것이니 뉘- 冬夜長을 感하랴.

歲暮와 共히 인간사회는 퍽도 밧브다. 送年도 하려니와 迎新 준비에 *上 눈코 뜰 새가 업다. 이러케 밧븜을 불구하고 本誌를 위하야 漫畵 24題를 주신 安碩柱 兄에게 多謝하며 또는 羅彬, 朴鍾和, 金基鎭, 朴英熙 諸兄에게는 무엇으로 감사를 表할넌지!

광고는 해놋코 頁數 관계로 부득이 來號로 미루게 된 기사가 적지 안음이 더욱 미안하다. 무엇보다 梁白華 兄의 희곡 「王昭君」을 두 번에 싯게됨이 독자와 共히 궁금한 일이다. 廉尙燮 兄의 문단 1년은 時代報의 送舊迎新이 물어가고 말앗고 勞農운동의 1년도 新年號에 실을 밧게 업다.

우리는 送年號는 이만 해두고 이 œ부터 新年號에 전력을 注한다. 新年號야말로 新年號답게 하랴고 우리 딴은 굉장히 벼루고 잇다. 거짓이 되냐 사실이 되냐 독자야 지내만 보자. 그리고 新年號부터는 독자와 共히 숙제도 잇든 참고문단을 실현하얏다. 玉稿를 만히 보내다고 힘을 아울러 新年에는 신문단을 세워 보기로 하자! (春坡)＜163＞
