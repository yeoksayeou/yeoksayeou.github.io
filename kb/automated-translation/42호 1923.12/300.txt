餘感 (Lingering Thoughts)

At first, the thought was to make this issue (本號) the year-end issue. That is, we intended to do a general reckoning (總勘定) concerning politics, economics, ideology, and literature. However, because we were putting out a special issue for Kangwŏn Province (江原道), we stopped everything and only included a few pieces regarding the literary world (문단). Various other articles were also all pushed to the next issue.

Mr. Ch'a Sangch'an (車相瓚), who went to Kangwŏn Province (江原道), and Mr. Pak Talch'an (朴達成), who went to Hamgyŏng North Province (함경북도), both returned safely late last month. The local gifts they brought back with them—every single one left us astonished. Last year is already the old days; the situation in the provinces—no, *our* situation—changes urgently moment by moment (刻一刻急). What must be done to mend our livelihood's collapse (생활파탄) and pioneer (개벽) a new phase? Our problem truly seems to lie solely here.

We hope you will read "Europe's (歐洲) Unrest," printed at the beginning of the volume (卷首), to the end, even if it seems tedious. Of course, we cannot guarantee there are no errors or inaccuracies (杜撰) whatsoever, but we believe it certainly has value worth reading at least once. Following that, the "Explanation of Historical Materialism (物惟史觀解說)" is a piece from which one can find new meaning in words often heard. Entering the literary section, the critical essay "Ruins of the Heart (마음의 廢墟)" is truly a piece one cannot read without tears. A magazine volume coming out month after month—if a third party were to glance at it, they might perhaps think it quite amusing, but in producing this book each month, there are various harsh and bitter experiences (經綽). Those who know will know the hidden suffering (苦裏) of the staff involved, who lie awake tossing and turning (憂心展輾) at night, clutching their heads.

To do one's utmost with all available strength and only cease after death—this is truly the fate of us humans. It is the fate of us humans who know no compromise, know no adherence to convention (因循), and only, only forge ahead anew (매진). How wonderful it would be if this small book, "Kaebyŏk" (開闢), could become like the leading voice (선소리) of these people.

Now, we shall likely meet again in the new year. At this time when things change smaller and smaller [Translator's note: meaning unclear, possibly 'rapidly' or 'incrementally'], let us see at the dawn of the new year (신년 원단) how much stronger the spirit (氣魄) of the many people welcoming the new year will become, and how much the appearance (면복) of this journal will be renewed.
＜163＞

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from *Kaebyŏk* magazine discusses the changes made to the current issue, prioritizing a special on Kangwŏn Province over the planned year-end review. It reflects on the grim reports brought back by correspondents from the provinces, highlighting the rapidly deteriorating living conditions and the urgent need for change ("kaebyŏk," or pioneering a new era). The editors recommend specific articles on European politics and historical materialism, acknowledge the hardships involved in publishing the magazine, and express a determined resolve to persevere and strive for progress, looking forward to a renewed spirit in the new year. Historically, this piece is interesting as it reveals the operational difficulties faced by Korean publishers under Japanese rule, their awareness of and concern about the worsening socio-economic conditions in the Korean countryside, their intellectual engagement with international affairs (Europe) and contemporary ideologies (Marxism/Historical Materialism), and the persistent nationalist undertone of striving for a "new era" (개벽) despite adversity.

餘感
처음 생각에는, 本號로써 송년호를 맨들려고 하엿다. 즉 정치, 경제, 사상, 문예의 각 방면에 관한 總勘定을 하려고 해섯다. 그러나 江原道에 道號를 내기 때문에, 모다 중지하고, 單히 문단에 대한 몃 편만을 실엇다. 그 외의 여러 가지 기사도, 모다 다음으로 밀고 말엇다.

江原道에 갓던 車相瓚군이며 함경북도에 갓던 朴達成군은 다가티 지나간 하순으로 무사히 도라왓다. 그들의 싸가지고 온 지방의 션물은, 어느 것이 우리를 경악게 아니함이 업섯다. 작년이 녯적이라, 지방의 형편 아니 우리는 刻一刻急 轉한다. 엇지하여야, 써, 우리의 생활파탄을 枚하고, 새 국면을 개벽할고. 우리의 문제는 오죽 여긔에 잇스리라 한다.

卷首에 실은 「歐洲의 不安」은, 지리할지라도 끗까지 보와주기를 바란다. 물론 다소의 杜撰이 업기를 保할 수 업스나, 반드시 一讀할 가치는 잇스리라고 한다. 그 다음의 「物惟史觀解說」은, 늘 듯는 말이나 새로운 의의를 차저낼 수 잇는 글이며, 문예편에 드려와 「마음의 廢墟」 한 감상문은 실로 눈물이 업시는 못닑을 글이다. 다달이 나오는 잡지 한 권, 제3자로서 문득 보면, 아조 우수운 것이라 생각할넌지도 모르나, 이 책을 매달 맨들어 내이는 거긔에는, 여러 가지로의 맵고 쓰린 經綽가 잇는 것이다. 憂心展輾하는 밤에, 머리 집고 잠 못드는 관계자의 苦裏을, 아는 이는 알 것이다.

잇는 힘을 다하야 죽은 후에 마는 것, 이것은 실로 우리 사람의 운명이다. 타협을 모르고, 因循을 모르고, 오즉 오즉 새롭케 매진하는 우리 사람의 운명이다. 「開闢」이란, 이 조그마한 책이, 이 사람들의 선소리 모양이 되엿스면, 얼마나 조흐랴.

이제는 새해에나 대할 것이다. 작고 작고 달라가는 이즘에 잇서, 새해를 맛는, 여러 사람의 氣魄이 얼마나 더 장해지며 本誌의 면복이 얼마나 새로워질가는 신년 원단의 그때에 보쟈.
＜163＞
