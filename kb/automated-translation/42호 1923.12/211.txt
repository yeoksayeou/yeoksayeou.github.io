Notice of Thanks

When we recently undertook a field survey of both Hamgyŏng North and Kangwŏn Provinces for the purpose of a basic investigation of Korean culture, we received the support of local figures in various ways. Although this is highly informal, we first wish to express our gratitude through this publication.

Kaebyŏk Publishing Company Editorial Department
Cha Sangch'an
Pak Talch'ŏng<105>

**Summary**

This brief notice is an expression of gratitude from the Editorial Department of Kaebyŏk magazine, specifically mentioning Cha Sangch'an and Pak Talch'ŏng. They thank the local people in Hamgyŏng North and Kangwŏn provinces for their support during a recent field survey conducted as part of a basic investigation into Korean culture. For historians, this short piece indicates active efforts by Korean intellectuals associated with *Kaebyŏk* to research and document Korean culture during the colonial period, specifically involving fieldwork in the northern and eastern regions. It also highlights the reliance on local support networks for such endeavors.
