The League of Nations General Assembly in Recess

While Germany's admission to the League of Nations, a byproduct of the Locarno Treaties, has recently become a major focus of attention in European politics, the issue of admitting Soviet Russia or expanding the scope of the permanent members of the Council (due to hopes from second-tier nations, i.e., weaker small nations like China, Poland, Sweden, Brazil, Czechoslovakia, etc., to become permanent members) arose. The League of Nations Extraordinary General Assembly, convened in Geneva to decide on this admission issue, proceeded somewhat smoothly as Sweden and Czechoslovakia withdrew their candidacies for Council seats. However, due to the strong insistence of Brazil <67> and China, it inevitably ended up being postponed until the coming September. Only a proposal by the Japanese delegate to establish a committee to study the organization of the Council and the election method for Council members and report back to the September General Assembly was adopted, bringing the session to a close.

Thus, Germany's admission is also postponed until September, and the admission of Brazil, China, and other various nations must also wait for the September General Assembly. Originally, the League of Nations was nothing more than a combination policy of the Great Powers, established after the Great European War under the pretext of "Justice and Humanity," primarily aimed at maintaining the power of the European Great Powers and enjoying great power status and dominance.

However, the world is not solely the world of the strong, nor is it a world where only the weak submit. What can be done when the assertions of all the weaker small nations, rising up one after another, saying "Me too, me too," are not just one or two, nor just once or twice? The old great nation of the East, China, is rising; the small nation of South America, Brazil, is rising; the newly emerged nation, Poland, is rising; all small and weak nations mutually join forces to forcefully argue for permanent Council seats.

No matter how much the strong freely rampage in the world, there is no helping it when faced with the assertion of just rights. No matter how much Great Britain, France, Italy, and Japan tried to fulfill only their own desires, even sacrificing nations like Sweden and Czechoslovakia to eliminate smaller, weaker nations like China and Brazil, in the end, they inevitably had to scratch their heads and back down in the face of Brazil's justified demands.

What will happen in September? We have no choice but to wait and see what unfolds next time. <68>
<67-68>

**Summary**

This news report details the postponement of Germany's admission to the League of Nations and the expansion of the Council's permanent members until September 1926. The delay was caused by disputes arising from the demands of nations like Brazil and China for permanent seats, despite attempts by major powers (Britain, France, Italy, Japan) to manage the situation, including pressuring Sweden and Czechoslovakia to withdraw. The article critiques the League as a tool for Great Powers but notes the rising influence and successful resistance of smaller nations asserting their rights. From the perspective of Korean colonial history, the article is interesting as it reflects an observation of international power dynamics from a potentially subjugated viewpoint. The cynicism towards the Great Powers' "Justice and Humanity" rhetoric and the emphasis on weaker nations like China and Brazil standing up for their rights could mirror Korean aspirations for self-determination and resistance against dominant forces during the colonial period.

休會中의 國際聯盟總會
「로카로느」 條約의 副産物인 獨逸의 國際聯盟 加入案이 최근 歐洲政局에 一大 注目거리가 되야 잇는 一面에 赤露의 引入說 又는 常任理事局 範圍擴大 문제가 起하야(一流 이하의 국가 즉 中國, 波蘭, 瑞典, 「뿌라질」, 「체크슬로바키아」 등 弱小國家로서 常任理事局을 희망하는 데서) 此加盟問題를 결정하랴고 「제네바」에 召集한 國際聯盟臨時總會는 瑞典 及 「체크슬로바카아」가 理事國을 辭退함으로써 다소간 順度로 진행되더니 「뿌라질」＜67＞ 及 中國의 强硬한 주장에서 別數 업시 來 9월까지 延期하기로 되고 다만 理事會의 조직 及 理事國의 선거법을 연구하야 來 9월 總會에 보고케 할 委員會만 創設하자는 日本대표의 提案으로써 終幕을 고하고 마럿다 한다.
그리하야 獨逸의 加盟도 9월로 延期되고 「뿌라질」 中國 기타 諸國의 加盟도 9월 總會를 기다릴밧게 업시 되얏다. 본래-國際聯盟이란 歐洲大戰 후 「正義人道」라는 구실하에서 歐洲列强의 權力保持를 주로 하야 大國家主義 及 大支配權을 享有하자는 列强의 組合政策에 불과한 것이다.
그러나 세계는 강자만의 세계도 안이요 또 약자만 屈服하는 세계는 안이다. 「나도 나도 하야 이러나는 모든 弱小國의 주장도 한아 둘 한 번 두 번이 안인데야 엇지 하랴. 東洋의 老大國 中國도 니러나고 南米의 一小國 「뿌라질」도 니러나고 新興國 波蘭도 니러나고 모든 小弱國은 相互히 擧勢하야 常任理事國을 力說하얏다.
아무리 강자의 自由橫行하는 세계나 정당한 권리의 주장에는 別數가 업는 것이다. 英佛伊日 諸國이 아무리 자기의 욕망만 채우려고 中國 及 「뿌라질」 가튼 小弱國을 제거하랴고 瑞典 及 「체크」 등을 犧牲까지 시키면서도 결국은 「뿌라질」의 정당한 强要에는 別數가 업시 머리를 쓸고 물너 안게 되얏다.
9월은 如何히 될넌지? 下回를 볼밧게 업다.＜68＞
