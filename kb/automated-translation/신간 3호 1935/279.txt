World Sightseeing Record

Training for Universal Conscription

Military reinforcement has now become like a constant topic throughout the world. The head of the Fascist house, Mussolini, gave the following speech at a meeting of generals on September 3rd last year:
"We must become a martial people. No, we must become a militaristic, belligerent people."

Less than a month later, he showed the significance of his speech – that is, as the first stage of preparation, he summoned regimental commanders and higher officers from various regions and held a conference. Thus, he established a new "plan" for universal conscription and announced it with the following "slogan":
"In a Fascist state, the duties of a citizen and the responsibilities of a soldier cannot be distinguished."

Italy's military responsibilities over the past seventeen years were training intended to contribute (供) to the territory of the pre-war Hohenzollern (호-헨시쓰오레룬) dynasty. However, under the new plan, the Italian people are forced to carry wooden rifles (木銃) on their shoulders as soon as they turn eight years old. The military training (軍事敎鍊) of the new plan is divided into three stages:

"Preliminary Era" – This is further divided into two stages, from age eight to eighteen, and from eighteen to twenty-one. In the earlier period, they are made to have frequent contact with the military to instill a belligerent tradition and sense of glory, and are educated to arouse enthusiasm for military life. Additionally, for training above this level, 20 hours are set aside in high school specifically for teaching military history specially selected by the Fascists, how to read maps, and various other military drills. Then, upon turning eighteen, students become members of the Fascist National Army and receive special education in military training.

"Active Duty Era" – Upon reaching age twenty-one, they receive a summons, leave the National Army, and live in the military for eighteen months.

"Reserve Era" – After finishing the active duty era, they return to the National Army for a second time for ten years and must review their military training for twenty days each year using holidays and festival days.

The Sighing Queen

Last July, Queen Wilhelmina (월헬미나) of the Netherlands, whose tears had not yet dried after losing her consort Prince Henry (헨리-)殿下, encountered a melancholy incident entirely unexperienced during her thirty-six years as Queen and thirty-three years on the throne.
She was proceeding along the main street of The Hague (하-구), which she always traveled, just like any other time, accompanied by the Duke of Mecklenburg, to attend Parliament. <108>
While dressed in pure white mourning clothes and responding to each of the people's cheers, suddenly dozens of cars belonging to Dutch communists rushed close to the royal procession (鹵簿). A clash occurred with the escorting police force, preventing further progress, and she returned to the palace.

Not only this, but a series of various unfortunate incidents considerably troubled the Queen, who had lost her peace of mind over the past year. This is not just limited to the Queen of the Netherlands, but has been a trend for queens since 1830.

×

Regarding the gold export ban due to the inflation policies of various world countries, the Netherlands insists on the gold standard to the very end. Meanwhile, in Amsterdam (암스텔담) and Rotterdam (롯텔담) last summer, riots by the unemployed occurred, causing much distress to the Queen. Listening to the Queen's words regarding this matter, they are as follows:
"Our country too has been swept into the moral and economic chaos that plagues the world even more than a year ago. Despite being stuck in the mire (泥沼) of the economic crisis, there is no room for tax increases – therefore, expenses must be economized. The results of economic consultations over the past year are not fully satisfactory. Regarding this point, thinking about the future again, rather than improving, there is a greater tendency for things to gradually worsen. In light of this, we must pay even more attention to the advancement of the domestic market."

The domestic market includes not only the eight million Dutch people but also the sixty million people of the colonies. Of course, the Queen does not mean for the nation to be entirely self-sufficient in oil, sugar, rubber, quinine (기니-네), coffee (커-피-), etc., but it goes without saying that the bitter experience of last year's warship mutiny incident related to the colonies has not yet faded from her mind. Furthermore, she also says, "We must pay full attention to the trend of global military expansion."

Hearing this advice (諭告), the parliamentarians are planning to bolster the East Indies Navy by allocating one-eighth of this year's budget of ninety-three million guilders (그르덴). Of course, a few communists opposed this and heckled (야지), but they were arrested as soon as the Queen left the throne (玉座).

Even for the Queen, thus gripped by sorrow, there is one single joy: the engagement of Princess Juliana (유리야나) to Prince Bernhard (베르나르), the third son of the Swedish Crown Prince [Note: Bernhard was actually a German Prince]. Thus, the time is not far off when a spring of joy will visit the Dutch Royal House. **Summary**

This article from 1935 reports on two main international topics: Mussolini's comprehensive plan for the militarization of Italian society, mandating military training from age eight through adulthood under Fascist ideology, and the difficulties faced by Queen Wilhelmina of the Netherlands. The Queen grapples with political unrest (communist demonstrations), economic crisis forcing austerity despite adherence to the gold standard, colonial troubles (referencing a past naval mutiny), and the global trend of military expansion, while finding solace in the engagement of Princess Juliana. For historians of Korean colonial history, the article is interesting as it reflects the global rise of militarism and nationalism in the 1930s, mirroring trends imposed by Japan in Korea. The discussion of colonial management and unrest (Netherlands East Indies) might also resonate with Korea's own colonial context under Japan.

世界見聞錄
國民皆兵의 訓練
軍備强化는 지금 世界의 한 常議과갓치되엿다 팟쇼의 本家「뭇소리니」는 지난 九月三日將官會議席上에서 아래와 가튼 演說을하엿다.
「우리들은 尙武的國民이되지안으면 안된다 아니 軍國主義的, 好戰的國民이되여야할것이다」라고.
그后 一個月이못되야 그는 演說의 重要性을 보여주엇다. -즉 準備의 第一階程으로 各地의 聯隊長以上을 召集하고 會議를 옅엇다. 그리하야 그는 國民皆兵에 對한 새로운 「프란」을 세우고 左의 「스로캉」과 갓치 發表하엿다.
「파스시트 國家에 잇서는 市民으로서의 職務와 兵士로서의 責務를 分別할 수가 업다」
伊太利의 過去十七年間의 軍事上의 責務는 戰前의 「호-헨시쓰오레룬」家의 版圖에 이바지(供)하려는 訓練이엿다. 그런데 新計劃下에잇는 伊太利國民은 八歲가되기무섭게 木銃을억개에메이지안으면 안되게되엿다. -新計劃의 軍事敎鍊이란 三層段으로난우어 잇다

「初步時代」-이것은 다시 二層段으로난우엇스니 八歲로 十八歲-二十一歲에 이르기지이다. 前期에잇서는 각금각금 軍隊와 接觸케하야 好戰的인 傳統과 光榮心을 불어늣케하고 軍隊生活의 熱情을이르키게 敎育하는것이고  그우에 訓練으로는 高等學校에서 二十時間을 개여 特別히 「파시스트」特選의 軍史와 地圖의 보는法 其他여러가지의 軍事敎鍊을 식힌다 그리다가 十八歲가되면 學生은 「파시스트」國民軍의 一員이되고 軍事敎鍊의 特別敎育을 밧게한다.
「現役時代」-二十一歲-가되면 召集을바더 國民軍을 脫退하고 十八個月間軍隊生活을 하게한다.
「後備時代」-現役時代를 맛치고 十年間은 두 번째 國民軍에도라가 每年祭日과 休日을 利用하야 二十日間은 軍事敎鍊의 復習을 하지안으면 안되게되엿다.

難息하는 女王
昨年七月- 皇配「헨리-」殿下를 일코 눈물이 아즉도 마르지 안은 和蘭女王「월헬미나」는 女王이된지 三十六年-在任三十三年間에는 全然經驗치못하엿든 憂鬱한 事件을 맛닥드렷섯다.
議會에 臨席하려 다른와갓치 늘단이는 「하-구」의 큰길을 「멧크렌부르그」公과갓치 進行＜108＞途中이엿다.
純白의 喪服을입고 國民의 喚呼에 一一히 應答해가는中 突然히 和蘭共産主義者의 自動車數十臺가 鹵簿갓가히 달려들어 警護警察隊와 衝突이 이러나자 더 進行치 못하고 還宮한 것이다
이아니라 여러 가지의 不幸한 事件의 連繪은 마음의 진정을 일흔 壹年의 女王을 적지아니괴롭히엿다 이것은 비단 和蘭의 女王아니라 一八三○年以來의 女王의 傾向이다.

×
世界各國의 「인프레」政策에 依한 金輸出禁止에 對해서 和蘭은 지 金本位를 主張하고 잇다. 그러는 一方「암스텔담과」「롯텔담」에서는 昨年녀름에 失業者의 暴動이일어 女王의 마음을 편안치못하게 한일이 만엇다. 이일에 關해서 女王의 이야기를 들어보면 아 와갓다.
「一年前보담도 더욱더 世界를 괴롭히는 道德上과 經濟上의 混亂에 우리나라도 휩싸여들엇다. 經濟的危機의 泥沼에 저잇슴에 不拘하고 增稅의 餘地는업고-그럿키믄에 經費는 節約하지안으면 안된 것이다 過去一年間의 經濟的商議의 結果는 十分滿足할수가업다. 이 點에 關해서다시 將來를 생각하면 良好하게된다기보다 도리혀 점점 險惡해질 傾向이 더만타. 이것에 鑑하야 國內市場의 進展에보담한층 注意를하지안으면 안될것이다」라고-.

國內市場이란 單純히 八百萬의 和蘭人만아니라 六百萬人의 植民地人도 包含되여잇다. 勿論女王은 國民이 石油, 砂糖고무, 기니-네, 커-피-等을 모도 自治自足하자는 것은 아니나 植民地에 依한 昨年戰鬪艦反亂事件의 쓴 經驗이아즉도 念頭에서 살어지지안은 것만은 두말할것도업는일이다. 더구나 「世界的 軍備擴張의 趨勢에도 十分注意하지안으면안된다」고말한다.
議員은 이 諭告를 드소 政府가 今年度의 豫算九千三百萬「그르덴」內에서 八分一을 던저서 東印度海軍의 充實을 計劃하고 잇다. 勿論二三의 共産主義者가이에 反對하고 「야지」를 하엿스나 女王이 玉座를 나자 곳 逮捕되고 말엇다.
如斯히 哀傷에사로잡혀잇는 女王에게도 단한가지의 깃븜이 잇스니 그것은 「유리야나」皇女와 端典皇太子의 第三皇子인 「베르나르」皇子와의 婚約이다. 이리하야 和蘭皇室에도 깃븜의 봄이 차저올도 머지안엇다(寫眞上은 獨身獨裁者「케말·파샤」와 (下)婚談이잇는「루마니아 皇妹」＜109＞)
