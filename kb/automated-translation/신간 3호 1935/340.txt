Magazine Title: 개벽
Publication Date: 1935-01-01
Article Title: 窓 (Window)
Author: 金起林
Article Type: 시 (Poem)

Kim Kirim

Going up to the second floor
Where there is a window overlooking the sea...

Like the ears of this house, anxiously
Waiting for the order of departure to fall from anywhere,
I try to throw open that window facing south...

And then, I shall mock the islands bound to the sea, not knowing how to flow away.
Also, the hungry cat and withered chrysanthemums hoping to receive the sun's caress <22>
Let's move them to the balcony (露台) again today.

A window where the pigeons, messengers of angels (天使),
Have never flown to nor flown away from.
On this side of the "curtain," happiness has never slept either.

Leaning on that window, I scold.
- O Life (人生)!
I know you are plotting
Unhappiness (不幸) and cruelty (殘忍), hidden
Beyond the wall of this season,
Trying to startle me into fainting.

This year too, I have never suffered the doctor's (醫師) worry.
Now, in order not to collapse under your blow (打擊),
I command my heart to arm (武裝) itself.- <23>
<22-23>

**Summary**

The poem depicts a speaker observing the world from a window overlooking the sea, feeling confined yet waiting for a "departure order." The speaker expresses disdain for stagnation (the islands), cares for fragile life (cat, flowers), and acknowledges a lack of happiness within. Ultimately, the speaker directly addresses "Life," recognizing its potential cruelty but declaring a resolute inner strength and readiness ("arming the heart") to withstand hardship without succumbing to weakness or illness. For historians, the poem reflects a mood of waiting, perhaps for liberation or change, common among intellectuals in colonial Korea. The themes of confinement versus the desire for departure, and the assertion of inner fortitude against anticipated external "blows" (打擊) and "conspiracies" (陰謀), offer insight into the psychological resilience cultivated amidst the oppressive atmosphere of the era.

金起林
바다가 바라보이는 窓이 잇는
2층으로 올라간다 ...

어디서든지 出發의 命令이 떨어지기를 기다리는
안타까운 이 집의 귀인 것처름
나는 南쪽으로 뚤린 그 窓을 열어 제치려 ...

그렇고는 바다에 억매여 흘러갈 줄을 모르는 섬들을 비우서 줄 게다.
또 해뼏의 愛撫를 받기를 바라는 주린 고양이와 시드른 菊花꽃들을＜22＞
오늘도 露台에 옴겨 놓자.

天使의 심부름꾼들인 비닭이들이 날어와 본
일도 날어가 본 일도 없는 窓.
『커-틴』의 이쪽에서는 幸福이 자본 일도 없다.

나는 그 窓에 기대여 꾸짓는다.
-人生아
나는 네가 나를 놀래여 기절시키려고 하야
수었는 不幸과 殘忍을
이 季節의 담벼락 넘어서 陰謀하고 잇는 것을 안다.

올에도 나는 醫師의 걱정을 기처본 일은 없다.
지금 너의 打擊에 꺽꾸러지지 않기 위하야
나는 나의 마음에 향하야 武裝을 命한다.- ＜23＞
＜22-23＞
