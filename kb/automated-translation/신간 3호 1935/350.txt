Selected Translations of Tang Poems
						Yang Sang-ŭn

Passing a Tavern (過酒家)

Is drinking today for my own sake?
Everyone in the whole world is drunk.
Let it be, how could this one body not get drunk?

Original Poem by Wang Ji (王績作)
此日長昏飮. 非關養性靈. 看眼人盡醉, 何忍獨爲醒.

Resentful Feelings (怨情)

A beautiful woman sits with the pearl curtain rolled up, frowning with her moth-like eyebrows.
Traces of two streams of tears linger on both cheeks.
Alas, no one knows the deep resentment in her heart.

Original Poem by Li Bai (李白作)
美人捲珠簾, 深坐噸蛾眉. 伹見淚痕濕, 不知心恨誰

Midnight Spring Song (子夜春歌)

The willow branches on the bank sway in the spring breeze.
Thinking of my beloved far away, my insides melt away.
I wonder how my beloved’s burning heart must feel.<24>

Original Poem by Guo Zhen (郭振作)
陌頭楊柳枝己被春風吹妾心正斷絶君懷那得知

Seeing Off a Friend (送兄)

Where my friend departed, clouds idly rise.
That goose flying high follows, flapping its wings.
I lament deeply that I alone cannot follow.

Original Poem by a Seven-Year-Old Girl of Tang (唐七歲女子作)
別路雲初起, 離亭葉正飛所嗟人異雁, 不作一行歸

Cold Food Festival on the Road (途中寒食)

Riding on horseback, the spring season grows late.
My hometown Jiangpu is visible far away.
Why is it that I cannot meet the people of Luoqiao?

Original Poem by Song Zhiwen (宋之問作)
馬上逢寒食途中屬暮春, 可憐江浦望, 不見洛橋人

Crossing the Han River (渡漢江)

Winter passed and spring came, yet not a single letter arrived.
Approaching my longed-for hometown, my heart grows more troubled.
Even if I meet people, I cannot ask for news of home.

Original Poem by the Same Author (前人作 - referring to Song Zhiwen)
嶺外音書斷經冬寑歷春, 近鄕情更怯, 不敢問來人＜25＞
＜24-25＞

**Summary**

This article presents Korean translations of six Tang Dynasty Chinese poems, rendered by Yang Sang-ŭn. The selected poems, originally by poets like Wang Ji, Li Bai, Guo Zhen, Song Zhiwen, and an anonymous young girl, explore universal themes such as revelry and societal conformity ("Passing a Tavern"), hidden sorrow ("Resentful Feelings"), longing for an absent loved one ("Midnight Spring Song"), the pain of parting ("Seeing Off a Friend"), and homesickness mixed with apprehension ("Cold Food Festival on the Road," "Crossing the Han River"). From a historical perspective, the publication of these translations in a Korean magazine during the Japanese colonial period is significant. It demonstrates a continued engagement with classical Chinese literature, a shared East Asian heritage, possibly serving as a subtle expression of cultural identity distinct from the colonizer's influence. The prevalence of themes like sorrow, longing, and displacement might have resonated particularly strongly with Korean readers facing the hardships and uncertainties of colonial rule.

唐詩選譯
						楊相殷
過酒家
오늘날 술 마시니 이 몸을 위함인가
왼 세상 사람들이 모도 다 醉하엿네
두어라 이 한 몸으로 아니 취코 어이리
原詩 王績作
此日長昏飮. 非關養性靈. 看眼人盡醉, 何忍獨爲醒.

怨情
발것고 안즌 美人 蛾眉를 찡그렷네
두 줄 눈물흔적 兩볼에 어리엿다
그대의 깁픈 원한을 알니 업서 하노라
原詩 李白作
美人捲珠簾, 深坐噸蛾眉. 伹見淚痕濕, 不知心恨誰

子夜春歌
언덕 우 버들가지 봄바람에 한들한들
멀니 게신 님 생각에 이내 창자 다 녹는다
우리 님 애타는 심사 엇덜넌가 몰나라＜24＞
原詩 郭振作
陌頭楊柳枝己被春風吹妾心正斷絶君懷那得知

送兄
님 떠난 그 자리에 속절 업시 구름닌데
놉피 뜬 저 긔럭이 펄펄 나라 따라간다
내 혼자 못 따라감을 못내 슬퍼 하노라
原詩 唐七歲女子作
別路雲初起, 離亭葉正飛所嗟人異雁, 不作一行歸

途中寒食
말 타고 오는 길에 봄시절도 느저 간다
江浦라 우리고향 저멀니 보이는데
어찌타 洛橋사람은 맛나볼수 업서라
原詩 宋之問作
馬上逢寒食途中屬暮春, 可憐江浦望, 不見洛橋人

渡漢江
겨을 가고 봄이 와도 편지 한장 업섯고야
그린 고향 다다르니 마음 더욱 산란하다
사람은 맛나보아도 집 소식은 못 무를네
原詩 前人作
嶺外音書斷經冬寑歷春, 近鄕情更怯, 不敢問來人＜25＞
＜24-25＞
