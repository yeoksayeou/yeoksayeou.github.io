Looking Back Eight Years
Everyone, please rejoice together.—(Here, twenty-one characters unavoidably omitted - original text)—
If someone who had died came back to life in this world, how welcome and joyous would that be? Moreover, this past July marked the 14th anniversary of our Kaebyŏk Company's (開闢社) founding. That *Kaebyŏk* (開闢) has come out again coinciding with that commemoration is, like a person celebrating a birthday who also has a son, undeniably a double joy.

However, human joy is not solely composed of joy. Just as there is joy, there is also sorrow. At the same time as the joy of the new *Kaebyŏk* emerging—(Here, thirteen characters unavoidably omitted - original text)—it freshly arises. Through eight years (八個星霜) of autumn winds and sad rains (秋風悲雨), worldly affairs (世事) have changed radically (激變), giving a sense of great transformation (滄桑), and human affairs (人事) are also impermanent (無常). The brave figures (諸勇士) Min Yŏngsun (閔泳純), Yi Tusŏng (李斗星), Pak Talch’ŏng (朴達成), and Pang Chŏnghwan (方定煥), who previously struggled intensely (苦心血鬪) for *Kaebyŏk* magazine, have already departed this world without achieving their long-held wishes (素志). Furthermore, comrade Kim Kijŏn (金起田) was struck by illness (病魔) and has been bedridden in a corner of Haesŏ (海西一隅 - likely Hwanghae Province) for four or five years (四五星霜). Of the people from the time of *Kaebyŏk*, only Yi Chŏngho (李定鎬) and I remain to edit this journal again, and I cannot suppress the feeling of loneliness and solitude, like defending a castle alone (獨守孤城).

However, we have become aware and made our resolve. We intend to strive hard and fight bravely (奮鬪勇戰) for this journal until the last person, the last moment. Even if the company's situation (社勢) today may be subdued (沈寂), if our principles (主義) and spirit (精神) do not change, then comrades throughout the land (滿天下 同志) will all support (應援) and back (支持) us. Therein, we find self-confidence (自信) and self-consolation (自慰) again, and intend henceforth, with courage a hundred or a thousand times greater, to fulfill the mission of *Kaebyŏk* magazine, which possesses a glorious history. Comrades throughout the land (滿天下 同志)! Please rejoice together and offer encouragement and support (鞭撻聲援). (Cha)

**Summary**

The author, likely a key figure named Cha, expresses profound joy at the relaunch of *Kaebyŏk* magazine after an eight-year hiatus, timed with the publisher's 14th anniversary. This joy is tempered by sorrow over the passage of time and the loss of several colleagues (Min Yŏngsun, Yi Tusŏng, Pak Talch’ŏng, Pang Chŏnghwan, Kim Kijŏn) who were vital to the magazine's earlier run. Despite the reduced team and challenging circumstances, the author reaffirms a strong commitment to the magazine's principles and mission, confident that their enduring spirit will attract nationwide support from comrades, and calls for collective celebration and encouragement. Historically, this piece is significant as it highlights the resilience of Korean cultural and intellectual activities during the Japanese colonial period, despite evident hardships like the long publication gap and censorship (indicated by the omitted text). The mention of specific individuals and their fates offers a glimpse into the personal costs and networks involved in maintaining a Korean voice, while the emphasis on unchanging "principles and spirit" suggests a persistent underlying nationalist or ideological commitment.

回顧八年
여러분 다가티 깃버하야 주십쇼.-(此間二十一字不得已畧-원문)-
이 세상에 만일 죽엇던 사람이 다시 살어나왓다면 그 얼마나 반갑고 깃뿌겟슴닛가. 더욱히 금년 7월은 우리 開闢社가 創建한지 第14周年 돌마지 紀念이엿슴니다. 그 紀念을 際會하야 開闢이 다시 나오게 된 것은 마치 생일 잔치를 하는 사람이 아들까지 낫케된 것과 맛찬가지로 二重의 깃붐이라 안이할 수 업슴니다.

그러나 사람의 깃붐이란 어듸까지 깃붐만 잇는 것은 안임니다. 깃붐이 잇는 반면에는 슬픔이 또한 잇는 것임니다. 새 開闢이 나온 깃븜이 잇는 同時에-(此間十三字不得已畧-원문)-새삼스럽게 남니다. 秋風悲雨八個星霜에 世事는 激變하야 滄桑의 感이 잇고 人事 또는 無常하야 전날 開闢誌를 위하야 苦心血鬪하던 閔泳純
李斗星, 朴達成
方定煥 諸勇士가 素志를 未成하고 벌서 이 세상을 떠나고 金起田同志가 또 病魔에 걸리여 四五星霜을 海西一隅에 누워 잇고 開闢 당시에 人으로 다만 나와 李定鎬군이 남어 잇서서 本誌를 다시 編輯하게 되니 獨守孤城과 가티 쓸쓸하고 외로운 感을 스사로 금할 수 업슴니다.

그러나 우리는 覺悟하고 決心하얏슴니다. 最後一人, 最後一刻까지라도 本誌를 위하야 奮鬪勇戰하려고 一.오늘에 社勢가 비록 沈寂하다 할지라고 우리의 主義와 精神이 변치 안는다면 滿天下 同志가 다 우리를 應援하고 支持 할터인즉 우리는 거긔에 다시 自信이 생기고 自慰가 생겨서 압흐로 百倍天倍의 勇氣를 내여 光榮의 歷史를 가진 開闢誌의 使命을 다하랴 합니다. 滿天下 同志여 다가티 깃버하고 鞭撻聲援하야 주시어지이다.(車)＜1＞
