讀者原稿募集 (Call for Reader Manuscripts)

Kaebyŏk does not belong to any single individual or any single group; it is yours, the readers'. Therefore, whether this book succeeds or fails also cannot happen without your passionate enthusiasm and firm support. In this regard, we are recruiting your manuscripts. Regulations are not announced separately, but please send us anything you yourself think suitable for this book. Of course, length is also at your discretion, but please be sure to use manuscript paper for the writing paper. A deadline will not be set separately, so please send them whenever you wish to send them. Excellent submissions will be rewarded with a token of appreciation (*謝). Please be sure to send them to: -Kaebyŏksa Kaebyŏk Editorial Department (開闢社開闢編輯部)- <94>

**Summary**

This article is an announcement from the editors of Kaebyŏk magazine calling for manuscript submissions from its readers. It emphasizes that the magazine belongs to the readers and relies on their support and contributions. No specific guidelines or deadlines are given, encouraging readers to submit any work they deem suitable, written on manuscript paper, at any time. A reward or token of appreciation is promised for outstanding submissions. From a historical perspective, this call reflects the magazine's effort to foster a sense of community ownership and solicit diverse content directly from its Korean readership during the colonial period, likely navigating publishing challenges and seeking to sustain engagement despite potential restrictions. The informal tone and lack of strict rules might suggest an attempt to encourage broad participation.
