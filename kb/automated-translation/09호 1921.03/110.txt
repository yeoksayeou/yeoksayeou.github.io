The one who will be the final victor will not be the *nation* (國民) that dares to commit the greatest evil, but that nation which endures hardship and perseveres until the very end.
Makswini of Ireland (愛蘭 막쓰위니)

Knowing how to fight and how to be victorious is something only a person strong in spirit can possibly do. All great efforts, all outstanding achievements are the products of a strong spirit (强魄). No matter how calm reason may be, it alone has never yet brought about any remarkable great work. We cannot possibly conquer passion unless we make passions confront each other; a truly wise person controls themselves in this way, just as a skilled boatman (梢工) safely steers the hull (船體) amidst opposing winds.
French (佛國) thinker Rousseau (루-소)

Complete courage must possess a special quality that makes it indistinguishable between the moral and physical aspects; any kind of chivalrous daring (義俠的 敢行) can be accomplished even with temporary strength. However, the true essence of real courage (眞勇) is not revealed in perhaps one event, but manifests itself throughout one's entire life (生涯).
American (米國) great man Roosevelt (루스벨트) <63>

**Summary**

This short article presents three quotes emphasizing endurance, spiritual strength, and lifelong courage as the keys to ultimate success and greatness. MacSwiney highlights perseverance through suffering for national victory, Rousseau stresses the power of spirit and controlling passions for achievement, and Roosevelt defines true courage as a consistent quality manifested throughout life, not just in isolated acts. From a Korean colonial history perspective, publishing these quotes, especially MacSwiney's on national endurance, in a magazine dated March 1st (anniversary of the 1919 independence movement) is highly significant. It served as a coded message of encouragement to Koreans under Japanese rule, advocating for resilience and inner strength in their struggle for liberation, framing it as a path to eventual victory despite hardship.

最後의 勝利者가 될 이는 最大의 惡을 敢行하는 *國民이 아니요 끗끗내까지 苦且忍하는 그 國民이 될 것이니라.
愛蘭 막쓰위니

如何히 싸우고 또 如何히 勝捷할가를 知하는 것은 오즉 精神이 强한 사람 뿐 能할 바이라. 모든 偉大한 努力, 모든 卓越한 行蹟은 다 强魄의 所産이라. 理性이 如何히 冷靜할 지라도 그것 뿐으로는 아즉 何等의 보암즉한 偉業을 끈긴 일이 업다. 우리는 情慾을 서로 對抗케 함이 아니면 도저히 그 情慾을 制服할 수 업는 것이니 眞個의 賢人은 그리하야 己를 持하는 것이라. 마치 熟達한 梢工은 相反風이 吹하는 其間에서 船體를 安全히 進行케 함과 가티.
佛國 思想家 루-소

完全한 勇氣는 道德肉體의 兩面에서 區別할 수 업스리 만콤 特殊한 性質을 具有치 아니치 못할지니 如干한 義俠的 敢行은 一時의 力으로써도 能行하는 것이라. 그러나 眞勇의 眞髓라는 것은 혹 一事에 現함이 아니요 전 生涯를 通하야 現顯하는 것이다.
米國偉人 루스벨트＜63＞
