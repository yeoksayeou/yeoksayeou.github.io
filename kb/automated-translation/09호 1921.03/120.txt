필자	林蘆月
기사형태	문예기타 (Literary Miscellaneous)

異端者의 警句
						林蘆月

Life is abstract and impersonal, therefore difficult to appreciate. Life is merely crude material for the artist.

Life means bewitchment and the sacrifice of the self (自我의 贄澤).

Only those who commit love suicide (情死者) can create the immortal moment (刹那).

Our virtue (德行) resides entirely in fantasy (空想) and imagination.

Long ago, the worldview of the Eleatic School (에레나 學派) in Greece (希臘) was this: "Only what exists is real; non-reality cannot exist, nor can it be thought of." To this assertion, I respond thus: "Fantasy is thought. For this fantasy, reality merely exists... true reality (참 實在) can be recognized through fantasy."

The artist should be an imaginer (空想家) in all things. For only imagination is creative.

Apart from descriptive prose (敍景文), there is no Nature.

Nature is not so much outdated (時代遲) as it is a corpse (死骸).

A poet's admiring (嘆美的) phrases about Nature are merely self-praise, showing how elegant and abundant (贍富하다) their own emotions (精緖) are.

Self-abandonment (自暴自棄) is a virtue (美德). Why? Because it exterminates the root (根底) of desire (慾).

Those hitherto called wicked people (惡人) are generally those who were utterly true (忠直) to themselves.

Drama (戱曲) possesses more reality (實在性) than life.

Life always fails when it tries to imitate (模倣하다) art.

The artist can beautify (美化할) all things.

The artist writes history with short songs (小唄) and paints (繪具).

Politicians or militarists try to write history with swords (劒) and blood.

Honor (名譽) is like an unchaste prostitute (無貞操한 娼妓).

In success and possession, there is regret and weariness (倦怠).

There are two eras in this world now most important to watch. One... when art performs astonishing miracles (奇蹟). The other, when life comes to imitate art completely, that is, when art will rule the entire world in place of reality.

Art always saves life from labor (勞動).
(End)
(The above excerpted from my notes (手記))

**Summary**

This article presents a series of aphorisms arguing for the supremacy of art, imagination, and individual artistic sensibility over life, reality, and conventional morality. The author, writing as a "heretic," dismisses life as crude material, praises imagination as the only path to true reality and creativity, elevates art (like drama) as more real than life, and even finds virtue in self-abandonment and perceived wickedness (if it represents being true to oneself). Art is presented as the ultimate force, capable of beautifying everything, writing history more meaningfully than politics or war, and ultimately destined to rule the world and save humanity from labor.

From a historical perspective, this text is interesting as an example of aestheticism or decadent thought appearing in colonial Korea in the early 1920s. Published in *Gaebyeok*, a significant nationalist magazine, these aphorisms, with their radical prioritization of art over life and reality, could be interpreted as an intellectual escape from the harsh political realities of the time, or perhaps a subtle form of cultural resistance emphasizing inner consciousness and artistic creation in a period where overt political expression was suppressed. The embrace of the "heretic" stance and the questioning of conventional values might also reflect a broader intellectual ferment and search for new values among Korean intellectuals during this era.

기사제목	異端者의 警句
필자	林蘆月
기사형태	문예기타

異端者의 警句
						林蘆月
生은 抽象的이요 非個性的인 故로 鑑賞키 難함. 生은 藝術家에게 粗末한 材料에 不過하다.
生은 蠱惑과 自我의 贄澤을 意味함.
不滅의 刹那를 創造할 者는 오즉 情死者뿐일다.
우리의 德行은 全혀 空想과 想像에 在하다.
大昔 希臘 에레나 學派의 宇宙觀은 이러하다. 「잇는 것은 實在 뿐 非實在는 잇슬 수가 업슬 뿐더러 또 思惟할 수가 업다.」此說에 대하여 나는 이러케 對答한다.「空想은 思惟라. 이 空想을 위하야는 實在가 잇슬 뿐...참 實在는 空想에 依하야 認識할 수가 有함.」
藝術家는 모든 것에 空想家가 될지라. 그는 空想뿐이 創造的인 까닭이니라.
敍景文을 떠나서는 또 自然이 업느니라.
自然은 時代遲 보다도 차라리 死骸니라.
詩人이 自然에 對한 嘆美的 文句는 自己의 精緖가 얼마나 우아하고 贍富하다는 자기 칭찬에 불과하다.
自暴自棄는 美德이다. 何故오 하면 慾의 根底를 絶滅케 함으로.
至今까지 惡人이라고 불리엇던 사람들은 大槪 自己한테 忠直을 다한 者라.
戱曲은 人生보다도 더- 實在性을 가젓다.
人生은 藝術을 模倣하다가 언제던지 失敗한다.
藝術家는 모든 것을 美化할 수가 잇다.
藝術家는 小唄와 繪具로 歷史를 쓰지마는.
政治나 軍事家는 劒과 血로 歷史를 쓰고저 한다.
名譽는 無貞操한 娼妓와 갓다.
成功과 所有에는 後悔 倦怠가 잇다.
이 世上에 至今 第一 重大하게 볼 二時代가 잇다. 하나는...藝術이 驚嘆할 만한 奇蹟을 行할 때요. 또 하나는 人生이 藝術을 完全히 模倣하게 될 때 곳 藝術이 現實의 代身으로 全世界를 支配할 것.
藝術은 언제던지 人生을 勞動에서 救濟한다.(終)
