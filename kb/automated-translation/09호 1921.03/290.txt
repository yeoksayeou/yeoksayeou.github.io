Errata

In the previous issue, page 100, within the second section of the piece titled 'The Beauty of Death,' the text "몃 사람의 부르지즘은 옛사람의 부르지즘," (Some people's bourgeoisism is the old people's bourgeoisism,), and at the end of the ninth section, the text "아! 1920年 10月 25日-" (Ah! October 25, 1920-), should be placed at the beginning of the tenth section, before the phrase 'to eliminate the noun 「弱者」(Weakling)'. <133>

**Summary**

This article is an errata notice correcting the placement of two text fragments within an article titled "The Beauty of Death" published in the previous issue (No. 8) of the *Kaebyŏk* magazine. It specifies that text mentioning "bourgeoisism" from section two and a date from the end of section nine should actually appear at the start of section ten. For historians, this notice provides a glimpse into the editorial practices and potential printing challenges of Korean magazines under Japanese rule. The specific content being corrected (mentioning 'bourgeoisism', 'weakling', and a date) might subtly indicate the sensitivity of certain topics or simply reflect common typesetting errors.
