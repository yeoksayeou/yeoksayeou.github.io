Magazine Title: 개벽 제21호
Publication Date: 1922년 03월 01일
Article Title: 編輯局消息 (Editorial Department News)
Author: Editorial Department
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editorial Postscript)

It is March, a fine season. Though the time and the day are good, there is nothing particular we can call work for us. It just feels rather languid.

However, one joyful phenomenon amidst this is seeing the spirit of self-cultivation (自修) filling everywhere, as the general awakening gradually heads towards deeper places.

Responding to this newly arising momentum, we too have aimed to provide practical help in the construction of the new life that our brothers (兄弟) are each preparing, pouring seeds and affection into even a single character of writing or a single phrase of speech; if you think about it, you will understand this yourselves.

It is not our intention to announce things with words before revealing the facts, so we cannot speak of it here, but for our part, we are preparing something to meet the expectations (企待) of you, our readers, so please wait patiently.

This month, amidst preparations for launching the "Woman" (婦人) magazine, there were also incidents such as the head of the Arts and Sciences department, Mr. Hyŏn Hŭiun (玄僖運君), suffering from illness for many days, and the head of the Society department, Mr. Pak Talsŏng (朴達成君), rushing to a distant place (遠地) for his father's funeral (父親喪), which made us quite busy (奔忙). In the midst of this, things like the cover turned out somewhat hastily (어물적하게), but we are confident (自信) that next month (來月), an increased level of effort will be added to this magazine (本誌).<72>

**Summary**

This editorial note from the March 1922 issue of *Kaebyŏk* reflects on the current atmosphere, noting a widespread trend towards self-cultivation among Koreans, which the editors hope to support through the magazine's content. They mention ongoing challenges, including staff illness and bereavement, which have affected production quality (like the cover) and made them busy, particularly with preparations for launching a new "Woman" magazine. The editors ask for readers' patience, hinting at future developments they cannot yet reveal, and promise greater effort in the next issue. For historians, this piece offers insight into the mood and activities of Korean intellectuals and cultural nationalists shortly after the March 1st Movement, highlighting the shift towards cultural strengthening ("self-cultivation") as a form of resistance or nation-building under colonial rule. The operational challenges faced by the magazine staff also shed light on the difficulties of publishing under constrained circumstances.

編輯局消息
春三月 好時節이외다. 때도 조코 날도 조흐나 우리에게는 別로 이것이라 할 일도 업습니다. 그저 녹은한 셈이외다.
다못 그 中에 한가지 깃거운 現像은 一般의 自覺이 漸漸漸 깁흔 곳으로 向하야 第一로 自修의 氣分이 到處에 充溢함을 보는 그것이외다.
우리도 새로이 일어나는 이 氣勢에 應하야 비록 한 字의 글과 한 句의 말에 일지라도 씨를 너코 情을 부어써 우리 兄弟가 다-各기 장만하여 잇는 새 生命의 建造에 實地의 도움이 잇기를 期하얏사오니 생각고 보시면 스스로 알으시리이다.
事實을 나타내이기에 먼저하야 말부터 發表함이 우리의 하고저 하는 바-아니 옴으로 여긔에 말슴은 못하겟사오나 우리만으로는 讀者 여러분의 企待에 副應키 爲하야 무엇을 準備함이 잇사오니 고히 기대려 주옵소서.
이 달에는 特히 「婦人」 雜誌의 發行 準備가 잇사온 中 또 學藝部 主任 玄僖運君이 病으로써 여러 날을 고생하엿스며 社會部 主任 朴達成君이 父親喪으로써 遠地에 奔喪한 等의 事故가 잇서 자못 奔忙하엿나이다. 그리되는 中에서 表紙가튼 것도 어물적하게 되엇사오나 來月은 一段의 努力이 本誌에 加添될 것을 自信합니다.＜72＞
