Respectful Notice

We respectfully announce hereby that Reporter Mr. Im Kye-ho (林啓鎬) of this branch office has resigned due to circumstances.

September, Kaebyŏk Year 5
Kaebyŏk Kaesŏng Branch Office<119>

**Summary**

This brief notice announces the resignation of Reporter Im Kye-ho from the Kaesŏng branch office of the Kaebyŏk magazine in September 1924, citing unspecified "circumstances". For historians, this notice provides a small data point regarding personnel changes within a significant Korean cultural magazine during the colonial period. It also confirms the existence and operation of regional branch offices like the one in Kaesŏng, shedding light on the magazine's organizational structure and reach.
