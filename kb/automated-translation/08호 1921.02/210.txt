Characteristics of Trade with Foreign Countries

Traditionally, the trend in our Chosŏn's trade with foreign countries did not show much progress in terms of trade value, but the types and range of export and import goods spanned a wide variety. The same imported goods were brought in from numerous countries, and similarly, the same export goods also had numerous countries as counterparts, making the scope of trade relatively international. However, recently, (1) the export value has increased more than the import value, (2) although the number of trading partner countries has not significantly increased or decreased compared to before, there have been changes in volume, meaning that while trade was generally international before, in recent years, large volumes of trade have been conducted with specific countries (United States of America, China, United Kingdom, etc.), becoming more singular and specialized, and (3) the trade volume has rapidly increased over the last 3 years. Characteristics of Trade with Japan

As noted above, Chosŏn's export-import trade volume at the end of Taishō 8 [1919] reached over 540 million Won, showing an increase of about tenfold compared to 10 years prior. However, a characteristic feature within this increase is the expansion of exports rather than the inflation of imports, and also the expansion of exports and imports with Japan rather than the increase in exports and imports with countries other than Japan. That is, the total import volume the year before last showed only a 7-fold increase compared to Taishō 1 [1912], but the total export volume for the same year showed a 36-fold increase. Furthermore, the proportion of trade with Japan relative to the total export-import value, which was only 64 out of 100 in Taishō 1, increased to 77 out of 100 by the year before last. <91> The primary reason for this is that the general development of Chosŏn's industry and economy recently, and especially the soaring prices since the Great War, generally inflated Chosŏn's trade value. The second reason, namely the particular increase seen in the trade value with Japan, is that although Chosŏn's commercial relations with foreign countries following the Japan-Korea Annexation continued based on previous treaties (條欸), the gradual increase of Japanese residents in Chosŏn and other relationships naturally led to increased trade with Japan. Furthermore, one special phenomenon that will arise in Chosŏn's trade starting this year is the change in the tariff system. Previously, Chosŏn's tariffs were collected based on treaties concluded between the Korean government and various foreign countries. Even at the time of the annexation, it was decided to follow the existing commercial treaties for the next 10 years. The foreign tariffs based on the treaties concluded by the Korean government at that time were, of course, set at low rates. However, adherence to these rates expired last year, and from this year, tariffs will be based on Japanese customs regulations. The previously independent customs territory of Chosŏn has been incorporated into Japan's customs territory. Trade goods entering or leaving between Chosŏn and Japan, except for several special items, will have tariffs completely abolished. In contrast, export and import goods with countries other than Japan will entirely follow Japanese customs regulations and, at the same time, be subject to higher tariff rates than before. As a result of this, Chosŏn's export-import trade volume with Japan will likely increase greatly, especially the import trade volume. That is, more Japanese goods than before will be imported. Why? Because the question of whether exports or imports between Chosŏn and Japan will prevail depends on the productive capacity of Chosŏn products heading to Japan (from Chosŏn) and the consuming power for Japanese products heading to Chosŏn (in Chosŏn). For these forces to manifest actual effects in terms of exports and imports, <92> they cannot but rely on the convenience of tariffs handling exports and imports between Japan and Chosŏn. Currently, the Chosŏn products with the most significant production capacity for Japan are agricultural products like rice (米), beans (豆類), cocoons (繭), cotton (綿), mineral products like gold (金), silver (銀), iron (鐵), and others like cattle (牛), fish (魚類), etc. Among these, those corresponding to goods imported into Japan are rice grain (米穀), minerals (礦物), cocoons (繭), cotton (綿), etc. Even if the productive capacity of these increases, it cannot increase infinitely. In contrast, Japanese goods entering Chosŏn that have huge consuming power in Chosŏn include cotton yarn and cloth (綿絲布), paper products (紙類), stationery (文具), metal goods (金屬用), pottery products (陶器製品), alcoholic beverages (酒類), and other miscellaneous goods (雜貨). These saw large import volumes even when import taxes were levied, accounting for about 70% of Chosŏn's total consumed goods. If these tariffs are abolished this time (actual abolition from next year), even goods that were intended for China or elsewhere will pour into tariff-free Chosŏn. Furthermore, regarding Chosŏn's trade with countries other than Japan henceforth, the import value will decrease compared to before, while the export value seems likely to increase somewhat. However, the effects of the tariff changes on Chosŏn's trade will be manifold, but since this is not the place to discuss them, I will omit further details and conclude here. (End) <93>

**Summary**

This article analyzes the dramatic increase in Chosŏn's foreign trade volume in the decade leading up to the early 1920s, noting a nearly tenfold rise since 1911. The author highlights two key trends: a shift in foreign trade away from diverse international partners towards specialization with a few key nations (China, US, UK), and a disproportionate surge in trade with Japan, particularly in exports from Chosŏn to Japan. The author attributes the overall growth to economic development and price inflation (especially post-WWI) and the specific growth in Japan trade to the effects of annexation, increased Japanese presence, and upcoming changes to the tariff system which will abolish duties between Japan and Chosŏn while applying higher Japanese tariffs to trade with other nations, predicting a further flood of Japanese goods into Chosŏn. For historians, the article provides a contemporary perspective on the mechanisms integrating Chosŏn's economy into Japan's, particularly the strategic use of tariff policy following the expiration of treaty-based tariffs. It clearly shows the expectation that these changes would further bind Chosŏn economically to Japan and potentially disadvantage trade with other nations.

對日本貿易의 特徵

右에도 記함과 如히 大正 8年 末의 朝鮮 輸出入 貿易高는 5億 40餘 萬圓에 達하야 自今 10年 前보다 約 10倍의 增加를 示하엿다. 그런데 此 增加의 內容 中에 特徵된 點은 輸入의 膨脹된 것보다도 輸出의 膨脹, 又는 日本以外國에 對한 輸出入의 增加보다도 日本에 對한 輸出入의 膨脹된 것이 是이라. 即 再昨年 中의 全輸入高는 大正 元年보다 7倍의 增加를 見함에 不過하나 同年의 全輸出高는 36倍의 增加를 示하얏스며 大正 元年度의 全 輸出入額에 對한 對日本輸出入의 比例는 100分 中의 64分에 不過하던 것이 再昨年度에 至하야는 100分 中의 77分으로＜91＞ 增加하얏다. 是는 第一의 原因은 近來 朝鮮의 一般的 産業 經濟의 發達과 特히 大戰 以來 物價의 騰貴는 一般的으로 朝鮮의 貿易額을 膨脹케 하얏스며 第二의 原因 卽 對日本貿易額의 特別增加를 見한 것은 日韓合倂 以來로 朝鮮對外國의 通商關係는 從來의 條欸에 依함에 不拘하고 朝鮮在住日本인의 漸增과 其他 關係는 自然 日本과 貿易을 多行하게 된 故이다. 此를 統計로 示하면

그리고 朝鮮 貿易에 今年으로부터 한가지 特別한 現像을 生할 것은 關稅制度의 變更이라. 從來 朝鮮의 關稅는 韓國政府와 諸外國間에 締結된 條約에 依하야 徵收하던 바 合倂 當時에도 向後 10個年間은 從來의 通商條約에 依하기로 하얏는데 韓國政府 當時에 締結된 條約에 依한 對外關稅는 勿論 그 稅率이 輕하엿던 것이라. 그런데 該稅率의 依遵은 昨年까지 滿期되고 今年부터의 關稅는 日本關稅 規則에 依하여 되는 바 從來로 獨立하얏던 朝鮮의 關稅區域은 日本의 關稅區域에 編入되어 朝鮮 日本 兩地間에 出入하는 貿易品은 幾種의 特殊品을 除한 外에 關稅가 全혀 撤廢되고 此에 反하야 日本以外國에 對한 輸出入品은 全혀 日本關稅 規則에 依하는 同時에 從前보다 高率의 關稅를 拂하게 되엇다. 이리된 結果로는 朝鮮對日本의 輸出入貿易高는 크게 增加할지요. 中에도 輸入貿易高가 別로 增加할 것이다. 卽 日本物品이 從前보다 多數 輸入될 것이다. 何故오 하면 朝鮮 日本間의 輸出輸入 中 何者가 勝할가 함은 日本向朝鮮物産의 生産力(朝鮮에서) 如何와 及 朝鮮向日本의 消費力(朝鮮에서) 如何에 關係하는 것인데 此等의 勢力이 輸出入 方面에서 現實的 效果를 現出함에＜92＞는 日鮮兩地間에 在한 輸出入을 取扱하는 關稅의 便益에 依치 아니치 못할 것이다. 그런데 現在 日本向朝鮮産物로 最主要한 生産能力을 有한 者는 米·豆類·繭·綿 等의 農産物과 金·銀·鐵 等 鑛産物 其他 牛·魚類 等으로서 此 中에도 日本으로 輸入되는 物品에 相對될 것은 米穀·礦物·繭·綿 등인 바 此等은 비록 生産力을 增加한다 할지라도 無限히 增加할 수 업는 것이요 此에 反하야 朝鮮入日本品으로서 朝鮮에서 巨大한 消費力을 有한 것은 綿絲布 紙類·文具·金屬用 及 陶器製品·酒類 其他 雜貨인데 此는 輸入稅를 課하던 時에도 多量의 輸入을 見하야 朝鮮 全消費品의 約 7割을 占하얏는데 此가 今回 關稅가 撤廢(實際의 撤廢는 自明年乎)되면 中國 其他로 向코저하던 品까지라도 關稅 업는 朝鮮으로 注入될 것인 故이며 尙且 今後의 朝鮮對日本以外國의 貿易에 在하야는 輸入額은 從前 減少될 것이요 輸出額은 多少間 增加될 듯하다. 그런데 關稅變更의 朝鮮 貿易에 及할 影響은 多端할지나 玆에는 論할 것이 아님으로 이만 略하고 말겟다.
