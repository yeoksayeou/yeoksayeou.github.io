기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Announcement/Editorial Postscript)

活動은 吾人의 運命
Activity is Our Destiny

We always wish to ask one thing of the many people in the world. Your beautifully made houses, your majestically constructed great ships, the convenient steam or electricity you utilize, the artworks or music pleasant to see and hear, and all other such things – did you acquire them by making them yourselves? And your body and mind, which you boastfully put forward as the most sentient of all creatures whenever you get the chance – were these too built up within your own generation? If we ask this, perhaps some among you will answer thus: “Of course. We obtained all such things by paying the appropriate price for them, and as for this mind and body, we acquired them by learning and polishing them ourselves from childhood to adulthood, so we feel not the slightest dissatisfaction in our hearts.” However, can you truly feel not the slightest dissatisfaction in enjoying those conveniences or possessing that mind and body, just because you paid some price or performed some cultivation? If that were so, what compensation should the many inventors and strivers who dried their blood and offered their lives to create or refine such things receive? And what should the many sacrificial victims (from amoebas up to the stage of humankind) and scholars of the Way (from primitive times to the present day), who arose and fell under the laws of natural selection or under man-made persecution to advance the function of human life just a bit more, to elevate the degree of conscience just a bit more – what should they possess as their compensation? If one thinks deeply, anyone should acknowledge it: all the civilization we currently possess and this mind and body we boast of were not attained by our sole ability; they are merely the inheritance passed down righteously by the ancestors of successive generations. This being the case, if we happen to possess this inheritance – that is, this civilization and this mind and body – yet cannot share it with our compatriot brothers, or if we merely exhaust this inheritance without adding some net assets to it to pass on to the future, with what face shall we meet our ancestors of successive generations when we pass away, and with what courage can we stride boldly upon the main streets while alive? That today we bustle about, whether in small or large ways, for our compatriot brothers, for human society, or for our own fulfillment – the intention lies entirely herein, and indeed, it cannot but lie herein. This is not theory but fact; it is not coercion but destiny. Because it is destiny, we cannot ask about success or failure, gain or loss; we can only exert ourselves to the utmost with dedication and diligence.

**Summary**

This editorial argues that contemporary human civilization, knowledge, and even our physical and mental capacities are not self-made but inherited legacies built upon the sacrifices and efforts of countless ancestors, inventors, and thinkers throughout history. Therefore, individuals have an inescapable duty or "destiny" (運命) not merely to consume this inheritance but to actively contribute, add to it, and share it with contemporaries and future generations. From the perspective of Korean colonial history, this piece is interesting as it reflects a strong sense of collective responsibility and historical consciousness, urging Koreans to actively participate in progress despite circumstances. The emphasis on shared inheritance and the duty to contribute beyond personal gain can be seen as a subtle call for national unity, self-strengthening, and a forward-looking perspective, framing societal contribution not as a choice but as an unavoidable fate, potentially empowering in a context of limited agency.

開闢 第八號 卷頭言
活動은 吾人의 運命
우리는 世間 뭇사람에게 늘 한가지 뭇고저 함이 잇도다. 그 制度 아름답게 된 당신의 家屋, 그 搆造 거룩하게 된 당신의 巨船, 利用하기에 便한 蒸氣나 電氣, 보기 듯기에 조흔 美術品이나 音樂, 其他 그러한 모든 것이 다- 당신네 스스로가 지어 어든 것이며 그리고 萬物 中의 靈長이라고 툭하면 내세우는 당신의 그 身과 心, 그것이 또한 당신 自代에 싸하올린 것이냐? 고 우리가 이러케 무르면 당신네 中에는 이러케 對答할 분도 계시겟지요. 「암 그러치 우리는 모다 그러한 것을 取하기에 맛가즌 代金을 出하야 어든 것이며 또 이 心身으로 말할지라도 自幼至長으로 우리가 스스로 배우고 硏磨하야써 어든 것인즉 우리는 少毫라도 於心에 慊然함이 업노라.」고. 그러나 당신네가 多少 代金을 出하얏노라 하야 或은 多少 修養을 行하얏노라. 그 便益을 享有하기에 그 心身을 所存하기에 少豪라도 慊然함이 업슬 수 잇슬가. 만일 그러타 하면 그러한 것을 刱造 或은 精製하기에 피를 말리고 목숨을 바친 幾多 發明家 努力家는 그 代로 무엇을 取하여야 可하며 이 人間의 生의 機能을 더 좀 進化케 하기 爲하야 良心의 程度를 더 좀 向上케 하기 爲하야 或은 自然淘汰의 法則下에서 或은 人爲的 迫害下에서 나고 꺼진 幾多의 犧牲者(아미빠로부터 人類의 階段에 至하기까지) 道學者(原始時代로부터 今日에 至하기까지)는 그 代로 무엇을 所有하여야 可할가. 그윽히 생각하면 누구라도 認할 것이어니와 現在 吾人의 所有한 全文明과 자랑할 이 心身은 吾人의 單獨能力으로써 做得한 것이 아니요. 歷代 祖先의 義롭게 남겨주신 遺産을 相續한 것뿐이다. 이러하거늘 吾人이 苟或이 遺産, 卽 이 文明과 이 心身을 所有하야 同胞兄弟와 더불어 난흐지 못하며 또는 다못 이 遺産을 消盡할 뿐으로서 多少의 淨資를 此에 添하야써 後日에 傳함이 업스면 돌아가서는 무슨 面目으로 歷代祖先를 對하며 살아서는 무슨 勇氣로써 大道街上에 濶步함을 得하리요. 今日 吾人이 同胞兄弟를 爲하야, 人類社會를 爲하야 或은 自己의 充實을 爲하야, 작게나 크게나, 꿈적어리는 것은 其意 全혀 此에 在하며 또한 此에 在치 아니치 못할 것이다. 이것은 理論이 아니라 事實이요, 强作 아니라 運命이다. 運命인지라 成敗利鈍을 뭇지 못하고 다못 鞠躬盡瘁할 것뿐이다.
