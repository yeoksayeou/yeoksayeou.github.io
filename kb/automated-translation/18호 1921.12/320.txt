기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editorial Postscript)

送年號의 編輯을 마치면서
						개벽사

How could we see what others haven't seen, and think what others haven't thought, pouring passion (情熱) even into the same words, and inserting substance even into the same arguments (主張), so as to always face you, our brothers (兄弟), in a fresh and most deeply moved state of mind (感激한 氣分)? This has been the effort of this magazine (本誌) throughout the year Shin'yu (辛酉, 1921).

But what is the reason for this? Now, standing at the peak of this year and calmly reflecting upon this magazine's past year, everything is nothing but unsatisfactory. Brothers (兄弟)! What is the reason for this? What is the reason for this?

There is no benefit (益) in looking back on past events (往事) that only hurt the heart; we only hold the tragic determination (悲壯한 決意) that we must once again rouse our sincerity (誠) and courage (勇) to face the advance of tomorrow, no, the New Year (新年).<162>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the magazine *Kaebyŏk* reflects on the past year's (1921) efforts. The editors express deep dissatisfaction with their work, despite their goal to present fresh perspectives and passionate arguments. They lament their perceived shortcomings and resolve not to dwell on the past but to muster renewed sincerity and courage to face the challenges and progress of the upcoming New Year with strong determination. From a historical perspective, the text is interesting for its candid expression of frustration and ambition under the constraints of Japanese colonial rule. While not explicitly political, the language of striving, dissatisfaction, and "tragic determination" can be interpreted as reflecting the broader nationalist sentiments and the difficulties faced by Korean intellectuals and publishers in expressing themselves freely and effectively during that period. Historians might find the tone and word choice revealing of the atmosphere and the coded ways dissatisfaction might have been voiced.

그러나 이것이 웨인 일입니까. 이제 이해의 絶頂에 올라 가만히 本誌이 1年을 回想하오면 모든 것이 不滿한 것뿐이외다. 兄弟여 이것이 웨인 일입니까. 웨인 일입니까.
마음뿐 傷하는 往事는 돌아보아 益이 업스며 오즉 우리는 다시금 誠과 勇을 鼓奮하야써 明日아니 新年의 前進에 當하리라는 悲壯한 決意를 가질 뿐이외다.＜162＞
