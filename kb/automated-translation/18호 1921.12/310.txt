Errata
						Kaebyŏk Publishing Company

In the Business Introduction section on page 125 of the previous issue, Im U-sik (林友植) was an error for Pak U-sik (朴友植). <161>

**Summary**

This short article is an errata notice issued by the Kaebyŏk Publishing Company. It corrects a name that appeared in the "Business Introduction" section of the previous issue (No. 17, p. 125), stating that "Im U-sik" should have been printed as "Pak U-sik". For historians, this brief notice illustrates the standard practice of corrections in early Korean periodicals during the colonial period. It underscores the importance placed on accuracy, even in potentially commercial sections, and offers a glimpse into the editorial process of correcting errors related to personal names.
