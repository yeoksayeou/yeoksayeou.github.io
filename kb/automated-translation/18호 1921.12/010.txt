**Body Text Translation:**
Our Sending Off and Welcoming

1. The Sinyu year (1921) is trying to leave again. If it goes, it seems we shall certainly never be able to meet it again. By what method shall we commemorate this one year that we cannot meet again?
During this one year, there might have been vaguely joyful things for us. There must have been stingingly painful things, things newly managed, things further expanded, things frustrated midway, and things that ended in failure. Not only that, but while there were times we, as individuals or groups (團軆), acted with true hearts for ourselves and for others, there must also have been times when, quite conversely, with petty feelings (些少한 感情), we secretly harmed (陰害) others and obstructed (沮戱) their work. Besides these, depending on each person's circumstances (境遇), there must have been various thoughts conceived, words spoken, and deeds performed.

Here, there is one word we must tell our brethren (兄弟). Namely, although the time of this one year passes, the traces (痕跡) left behind by this one year do not easily depart; they (1) become distinct achievements (歷然한 功程) guarding our great land, rivers, and mountains (大地山河), and (2) become faint memories (熹微한 記憶) occupying a corner (一隅) of our hearts (心情). Thus, much of it becomes a kind of relic (遺物), a latent power (潛勢力), forever working (働作) behind the scenes of our society and our hearts. Therefore, we must fully reflect (回顧) upon our words and deeds (所云爲) of this past year, first discarding (絶棄) what should be discarded and preserving (保持) what should be preserved, thereby quickly making preparations (準備) to greet the new year. Doing so seems to be the best way to commemorate this Sinyu year and simultaneously the best way to welcome that Imsul (1922) year.

Let us speak frankly (肉談). Let's use the last few days (幾日間) of the year-end (年末) to hold this departing Sinyu year right before our eyes (眼前), discard what should be discarded, and take what should be taken. Once this task is finished, let's bring forth the arriving Imsul year, make new resolutions (決心), and plan our budgets (豫筭). This is all we have to say, and to make this easier to put into practice (實行), we attach some related materials (資料) (in this volume (卷中)). <1>

**Summary**

This editorial from the December 1921 issue of *Kaebyŏk* reflects on the passing year (Sinyu, 1921) and the upcoming one (Imsul, 1922). It urges readers to introspectively review the year's events, both positive and negative, personal and collective. The author emphasizes that the past year's actions leave enduring "traces"—achievements or memories—that become a "latent power" influencing the future of society and individuals. The piece advocates for using the year's end to consciously discard negative aspects and preserve positive ones, thereby preparing thoroughly to welcome the new year with fresh resolutions and plans. This reflective process is framed as the proper way to bid farewell to the old year and greet the new. From a historical perspective, the article is interesting as it shows the intellectual climate of early colonial Korea, focusing on self-assessment, moral reflection, and future-oriented planning within the Korean community ("our brethren"). The call to consider the impact of actions on "our great land, rivers, and mountains" subtly invokes national consciousness, while the concept of past actions forming a "latent power" (潛勢力) suggests a belief in the cumulative effect of individual and collective efforts towards shaping the nation's destiny, making it valuable for historians studying Korean thought during this period.

우리의 送迎
1. 辛酉 1年이 또 가고저 합니다. 가면 應當 다시는 못 對할 듯 합니다. 우리는 무슨 方法으로써 이 다시 對하지 못할 一年을 紀念하겟습니까.
이 1年 동안에는 우리에게 어섬프러하게 나마 깃거운 일도 잇섯슬지오. 쓸아리게 압흔 일도 잇섯슬 것이며 새로 經營한 일, 一層 擴張한 일도 잇섯슬지오 中途 挫折된 일, 結局 失敗한 일도 잇섯슬 것입니다. 뿐 아니라 우리는 個人으로나 團軆로나 참마음으로 나를 爲하고 남을 爲한 일이 잇섯는 同時에 이와 正反對로 些少한 感情으로써 남을 陰害하고 남의 일을 沮戱한 적도 잇섯슬 것이며 其他 사람 사람의 境遇에 依하야 가지 各樣의 생각한 것, 말한 것, 行한 것이 잇섯슬 것입니다.
여긔에서 우리는 한 말슴으로써 兄弟에게 告할 것이 잇습니다. 卽 이 1年의 歲月은 갈지라도 이 1年의 남겨 노혼 痕跡은 오히려 가지 아니하고 그것은 (1) 歷然한 功程이 되어 우리의 大地山河를 지키고 잇스며 (2) 熹微한 記憶이 되야 우리 心情의 一隅를 占據하고 잇다 함이며 그래서 그것의 多部는 一種의 遺物이 되어 潛勢力이 되어 永遠히 우리 社會와 우리 心情의 裏面에서 働作할 것이라 함이외다. 그러니까 우리는 이 1年間의 所云爲를 完全히 回顧하야 먼저 絶棄할 것을 絶棄하며 保持할 것을 保持하야써 새해 마즐 準備를 急히 하여야 할 것입니다. 이리함이 이 辛酉 一年을 가장 잘 紀念하는 道인 同時에 저 壬戌 1年을 가장 잘 歡迎하는 道일가 합니다.

아조 肉談으로 말슴합시다. 年末의 幾日間을 引用하야 이 가고저 하는 辛酉 一年을 우리의 眼前에 그대로 붓들어 노코 그저 버릴 것을 버리고 取할 것을 取합시다. 이 일이 마치거든 다시 오고저 하는 壬戌 1年을 그대로 끌어다 노코 새로 決心할 것을 決心하며 豫筭할 것을 豫算합시다. 우리가 할 말은 이것뿐이며 이것을 實行하기에 便케 함이 될가 하와 이에 關連된 多少의 資料를 摘記(卷中에) 합니다.＜1＞
