Editor's Postscript

For the editing of this issue, the editor, Mr. Yi (李君), was on a journey to observe the Western Korea region (西鮮地方), so our office affairs were particularly busy. Consequently, there are many aspects of the article editing that are inherently disorganized. The statistics presented within the various articles documenting the economic power of foreigners are all based on those published by the hand of the Government-General (總督府). While this is unsatisfactory to our minds, we cannot abruptly change this. We intend to make renewed efforts towards this kind of investigation. Furthermore, although we intended this time to illustrate the influence of foreigners in various fields as much as possible, due to space limitations (紙面), we have only recorded a few issues for now (爲先). Details (詳細) will be presented later (追後). We would be glad if even this rough (踈粗) record could serve as some small help (一助) to stimulate our many brethren towards producing prompt facts. It seems the New Year issue (新年號) will turn out quite decently for once. Setting aside (除止) all other reasons, we cannot but make special (恪別) efforts for the sake of the dignity (체면) of the magazine world (雜誌界) produced by Korean hands (朝鮮人). Something we must mention again concerns those who frequently send precious manuscripts (玉稿) to our company (本社). Please send writings that show traces of more effort, and send those that contain substance (씨; lit. seeds) based on reality (실제) and the true state of affairs (실상). If it is not so (그러치), it would be better (조켓습니다) to refrain from the outset. Apologies for these rash words (妄言多悚).<69>

**Summary**

This editor's postscript from *Kaebyŏk* apologizes for potential disorganization in the current issue due to the editor's travel. It notes the use of Government-General statistics regarding foreign economic influence, acknowledging their inadequacy but citing necessity, with plans for future independent research. The piece highlights space constraints limiting coverage but promises more detail later. It expresses hope the issue stimulates readers and anticipates a strong New Year issue, driven by the need to uphold the reputation of Korean-run publications. Finally, it urges contributors to submit well-researched, reality-based articles. For historians, this piece reveals the operational challenges faced by Korean publishers under colonial rule, including reliance on official (Japanese) data sources despite recognizing their limitations. It underscores the conscious effort to document foreign economic penetration and the strong sense of national pride tied to producing Korean-language media ("the dignity of the magazine world produced by Korean hands"). The call for more substantial, fact-based submissions also reflects a desire for intellectual rigor and relevance within the nationalist discourse of the time.

編輯餘言
이번호의 편집에는 編輯人되는 李君이 西鮮地方을 見學하는 노정에 올라 우리집일은 별로 奔忙하엿습니다. 딸하서 기사의 편집상 스스로 整齊되지 못한 것이 만습니다. 外國人의 經濟的 勢力을 기록한 諸問題의 중에 나타난 통계는 모다 總督府의 手로 발표된 그것에 의하엿습니다. 마음에 깨끗하지 못하나 갑작히 할 수가 업습니다. 이러한 조사의 방면에 향하야 우리는 세삼스럽게 노력하려 합니다. 그리고 이번에는 외국인의 세력을 할 수 잇는대로 각방면으로 표시코저 하엿스나 紙面의 관계로 爲先 몃 문제뿐을 기록하엿습니다. 詳細는 追後로 표시하겟습니다. 다못이 踈粗한 기록이나마 여러 형제의 신속한 사실을 作出하는데 대한 자극의 一助가 되엇스면 깃브겟습니다. 新年號는 한번 그럴듯하게 될 것 갓습니다. 다른 이유는 다 除止하고라도 朝鮮人의 手에 成한다는 雜誌界의 체면을 위하야 恪別努力하지 아니할 수 업습니다. 다시금 말하지 아니할 수 업는 것은 本社에 가끔 玉稿를 주는 여러분에게 대한 것이외다. 좀 더 노력한 자최가 잇는 글을 보내시며 실제와 실상에 의거한 씨가 들은 것을 보내시오. 그러치 아니한 것이면 처음부터 그만두는 것이 조켓습니다. 妄言多悚.＜69＞
