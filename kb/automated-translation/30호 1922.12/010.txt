Intent and Regulations Regarding the Establishment of the Kaebyŏk Company Membership System

I. Fundamental Intent for Establishing the Membership System
We hope that our long-advocated "Revitalization Movement" (更生運動) will achieve the most substantial results under the close connection with each and every one of our compatriots. Prior to this, we hope that all people who have had thoughts, words, or actions concerning the advancement of this "Revitalization Movement" will form some organic connection as quickly as possible.

This Company (本社) was founded in the summer of the Kyeongsul year [庚申, 1920], dedicating its efforts solely to the promotion of the cultural movement. After several years of painstaking effort and sincerity (苦心殫誠), we feel that its foundation has now begun to take shape. Therefore, with extremely significant meaning, this Company specially establishes the Membership (社友) system. We dare to commemorate the flourishing fortune of this Company and express gratitude for fraternal goodwill (好誼), while also advancing hereby to ensure the results of our pan-humanistic national movement (汎人間的 民族運動) become [missing character/illegible]. If, fortunately, committed individuals (有志) everywhere respond to this, and under an extremely simple format, something that yields extremely substantive achievements is harvested, how could this be solely for the benefit of this Company towards Members, or this Company towards itself? Thus, we humbly draft this small plan (小案) and dare to request the endorsement of the devoted patrons (大方 篤志).

II. Member Regulations
Article 1. A Member (社友) refers to a devoted individual (篤志) who approves of the intent and purpose of this Company and maintains an organic connection (有機的 聯絡) with it.

Article 2. Among the various problems arising on the surface of current society, Members may request this Company to investigate or devise solutions for those deemed significant or special. Upon receiving such a request, this Company shall consider the extent and nature of the issue and respond to the request as far as possible (可及).

Article 3. Members within the same region (e.g., one prefecture '府', county '郡', or similar) may, with the joint signature of 20 or more Members, request this Company to provide speeches or lectures on special issues of that region. Upon receiving such a request, this Company shall respond directly.

Article 4. This Company shall present the Kaebyŏk magazine or the Women's magazine as gifts to Members and provide all other publications issued by this Company at cost (實費).

Article 5. Members possess the basic qualification to become employees (사원) of this Company or its branch offices (支分社).
Remark: This is because there is a clause in the Company's regulations (社規) stating that company employees are selected from among the Members.

Article 6. If any Member suffers unforeseen disaster (災變), this Company shall provide appropriate consolation and aid (慰助). Also, if a Member's human rights are trampled upon (蹂躪), this Company shall devise appropriate protective measures (*護策).

Article 7. As a token of approval for the Company's intent and purpose, Members shall donate (出捐) ten Won (拾圓) annually.
However, those who donate one hundred Won (金백원) or more in a lump sum shall be permanently exempted from the annual ten Won support contribution (贊助金).

Article 8. Members shall pay close attention to the progress of all undertakings (施爲) belonging to this Company and may present opinions regarding the development of affairs or provide reference materials to this Company at any time.

Article 9. Members shall, as far as possible, investigate and research special facts or trends arising in their surrounding society and send them to this Company.

Article 10. This Company shall, as far as possible, strive for the convenience of Members and respect their honor at all times.

Article 11. Members shall, as far as possible, strive for the convenience of the Company (社社) and respect its honor at all times.

Appendix: Summary of Company Regulations (社規摘要)
Article 1. This Company shall be called Kaebyŏk Publishing Company (개벽사).
Article 2. This Company aims, under pan-humanistic nationalism (범인간적 민족주의), to research, comment on, introduce, and advocate regarding all phenomena of human society, thereby opening up rational (合理) and novel (嶄新) paths for the development of life.
Article 3. To achieve the objective of Article 2, this Company shall publish magazines and books, hold speeches and lectures, and may undertake various other activities deemed necessary for the realization of the Company's principles (社是).
Article 4. To socially promote the objective of Article 2, this Company may separately establish a Membership (社友) system. However, regulations concerning Members shall be determined separately.
Article 5. This Company shall be located in Kyŏngsŏng (京城), Chosŏn (朝鮮), and may establish branch offices (支社), sub-branch offices (分社), and distribution points (分賣所) in various key locations (樞要地) as needed.
Article 6. To carry out the duties of Article 3, this Company shall establish administrative execution organs (事務執行機關) as listed below (左記):
1. Editorial Bureau (편집국)
   a. Research Department (조사부) b. Politics and Economics Department (政經部) c. Social Affairs Department (사회부) d. Arts and Sciences Department (학예부)
2. Business Bureau (영업국)
   a. Accounting Department (경리부) b. Sales Department (판매부) c. Advertising Department (광고부) d. Agency Department (대리부)
3. General Affairs Section (서무과)
Article 7. This Company shall have employees (사원) as listed below:
   a. Advisor (고문) b. President (사장) c. Chief Editor/Manager (주간) d. Editorial Bureau Chief (편집국장) e. Business Bureau Chief (영업국장) f. Head of each Department/Section (각 部,課 주임)
   g. Presidents and employees of each branch office (지사 분사 사장 及 사원)
Article 8. Employees of this Company may only be appointed from among those who are Members (社友).

**Summary**

This article from the December 1922 issue of *Kaebyŏk* magazine announces the establishment of the "Sawoo" (社友), or Company Membership system. The purpose is to create an "organic connection" between Kaebyŏk Publishing Company (開闢社) and its supporters to further its goals, framed as a "Revitalization Movement" and a "Pan-humanistic National Movement." The regulations detail the benefits for members (receiving magazines, requesting research/lectures, potential employment, aid during disasters, protection of rights) and their obligations (an annual donation of 10 Won, providing local information, supporting the company). From a colonial history perspective, this initiative is significant as it shows a major cultural and intellectual organization attempting to build a structured, nationwide network of engaged supporters under Japanese rule. The language emphasizes mutual support, cultural advancement, and national consciousness (albeit veiled in terms like "Revitalization Movement" and "Pan-humanistic National Movement," likely to navigate censorship). The explicit mention of protecting members' rights if "trampled upon" (蹂躪) hints at the challenging political environment and the company's perceived role in defending its constituents.

開闢社社友制의 設行에 關한 趣意와 規定
一. 社友의 制를 設行하는 根本意
우리는 우리의 標榜한 年來의 「更生運動」이라 그것이 우리 동포의 사람 사람으로 더불어 결연한 그미테서 最着實한 성과를 어듬이 잇기를 기대하며 이에 先하야 우리는 다시 이 「更生運動」의 進成에 대한 생각이나 말이나 또 행동을 가저본 모든 사람과 사람이 1일이라도 速히 무슨 機的 聯結을 지음이 잇기를 기대하는 바이라.
本社-業을 庚申의 夏에 創하야 力을 문화운동의 促進에 專할새 苦心殫誠-數年을 經하야 이제 비롯오 其基가 成한 감이 업지 못한지라. 이에 本社는 極히 중대한 의미에서 社友制度를 特設하야 敢히 本社의 隆運을 기념하며 형제의 好誼를 감사하는 동시에 進하야 이로써 우리의 汎人間的 民族運動의 성과를 **********되게 하기를 기약하노니 다행히 到處에 有志가 이에 響應하야 極히 간단한 方式下에서 極히 實質잇는 功果를 수확하는 무엇이 된다하면 이것이 어찌 本社 對 社友, 本社 對 本社의 幸益 뿐을 爲함이리오. 이에 애오라지 小案을 起하야써 감히 大方 篤志의 贊同을 乞함이라.

二. 社友規定
第1條. 社友라 함은 本社의 趣意, 목적을 찬성하야 本社와 有機的 聯絡을 取하는 篤志의 人을 謂함이라.
第2條. 社友는 現社會의 波面에 起하는 각종의 문제 중, 重大 또는 特殊하다 認할 者에 對하야 此에 對한 조사 혹은 해결의 방편을 본사에 依賴할 수 잇으며 右에 依賴를 受한 本社는 그 문제의 程度와 성질을 고찰하야써 可及의 限에서 그 依賴에 응할 것이라.
第3條. 社友는 동일 지역(例하면 一府, 郡과 如함) 내의 社友 중 20人 이상의 連名으로써 그 지방의 특수문제에 대한 演說이나 講話를 본사에 의뢰할 수 잇으며 右의 의뢰를 受한 본사는 直히 此에 應할 것이라.
第4條. 本社는 社友에게 對하야 개벽잡지 혹은 부인잡지를 贈呈하며 기타 本社의 刊行에 관한 一切의 출판물을 實費로 提供할 것이라.
第5條. 社友는 本社, 支分社의 사원이 될 기본자격을 有함이라.
備考. 본사의 社規 중에 본사의 사원은 社友 중에서 選拔함이라 한 조항이 有한 故이라.
第6條. 本社는 社友 중에 或 不時에 災變을 당한 이가 有할 時에는 상당한 慰助를 與하며 又 或 社友의 인권이 蹂躪되는 경우에는 此에 상당한 *護策을 講究할 것이라.
第7條. 社友는 본사의 趣意, 목적을 찬성하는 表로써 年拾圓을 出捐할 것이라.
但 一時金으로 金백원 이상을 出捐할 時에는 年 10원의 贊助金을 永久免除함이라.
第8條. 社友는 본사의 施爲에 속한 一切事業에 진행되는 그것을 注視하야 사무의 발전에 관한 의견 혹은 참고의 자료를 何時던지 본사의 陳示 혹은 제공할 것이라.
第9條. 社友는 자기의 周圍社會에 起하는 특수사실 혹은 경향에 대하야는 可及의 限에서 조사, 考究하야 본사에 送致할 것이라.
第10條. 본사는 可及의 限에서 何時던지 社友에 대한 편익을 圖하고 名譽를 존중할 것이라.
第11條. 社友는 可及의 限에서 何時던지 社社에 대한 편익을 圖하고 名譽를 존중할 것이라.
