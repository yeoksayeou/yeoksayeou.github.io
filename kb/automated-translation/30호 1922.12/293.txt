Yesan, Ch'ungch'ŏngnam-do
Ch'ungnam Commercial Co., Ltd. <70>

**Summary**

This short piece is a congratulatory message from the Ch'ungnam Commercial Co., Ltd., located in Yesan, South Ch'ungch'ŏng Province, celebrating the significant development of the magazine *Kaebyŏk*. It appeared in the December 1922 issue. For historians, this advertisement demonstrates the reach and influence of *Kaebyŏk* beyond Seoul into the provinces, and shows regional businesses engaging with and supporting prominent cultural and intellectual publications during the Japanese colonial period. It suggests the magazine was seen as an important voice representing Korean interests or modernity, worthy of public support even from commercial entities.
