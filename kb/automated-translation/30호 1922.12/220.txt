Article Title: 가을 바람과 함께 (With the Autumn Wind)
Author: 金岸曙
Article Type: 시 (Poetry)

With the Autumn Wind
						Kim Ansŏ
Beyond the sea
Across the sea, across the blue sea,
Far, far away beyond the distant sea,
A boat with white sails faintly seen, going.

Across the sea, across the blue sea,
Over the horizon of that distant sea,
My complaining dream, going alone without end.

Untitled
Joyful morning sunlight shines upon people,
And smiles of gladness are upon people's faces.
Everything, so beautifully, so peacefully,
Treads the path given by God and passes by, but,

Ah, how sorrowful, I want to tear at it!
My person, my love,<27>
In my heart, limitlessly, tears fall.

November Evening
Through the grove of leafless trees
Blown by the wind,
As small birds fly away,
Heavy clouds drift in the sky,
And the evening sun sets ever so quietly.

Stand quietly, listen, and watch,
Dim, sorrowful "Regret" (悔恨), along with the darkening night,
Descends upon the heart awaiting Rest,
And doesn't the colorless, pale moon rise once again?

My Spirit, you, today as yesterday,
Alone bow your head, and remain huddled.

Autumn
Clean and desolate autumn,
Clear and gloomy sky,
Golden Sunlight seemingly without any strength,<28>
Water Surface like a wiped mirror.

Trees and trees, singing of love and hatred in the wind,
And Fallen Leaves and Fallen Leaves.

My heart, left alone in solitude,
Truly finds no place to lean on,
Look there, even the sun, fallen alone,
Hides behind the clouds, sobbing and weeping.

Sad Comedy
Buy the bone gambling tiles,
However,
Do not gamble with the bone tiles,
- The Law has thus decreed.

Buy a wife with money,
However,
Do not buy a woman with money,
- Morality has thus spoken.<29>
<27-29>

**Summary**

This collection of poems by Kim Ansŏ evokes themes of longing, melancholy, loneliness, and a subtle critique of societal hypocrisy, all set against autumnal and evening backdrops. The poems use imagery of the sea, wind, leafless trees, pale moonlight, and the weeping sun to convey deep personal sorrow and a sense of isolation, contrasting inner turmoil with the seemingly peaceful exterior world. The final poem, "Sad Comedy," uses sharp irony to point out contradictions in law and morality concerning gambling and the commodification of women. From a historical perspective, these poems are interesting as they reflect the somber and perhaps suppressed atmosphere of Korea under Japanese rule in the early 1920s. The pervasive sense of sorrow, isolation, and the critique of arbitrary rules (even if metaphorical) might resonate with the experiences and sentiments of intellectuals during that period.

가을 바람과 함께
						金岸曙
바다 저 便
바다를 건너, 프른 바다를 건너,
저 멀리 머나먼 바다의 저便에,
그윽하게도 보이는 흰돗 달고 가는 배.

바다를 건너, 프른 바다를 건너,
머나먼 저 바다의 水平線 우으로
끈치 안코 혼자 가는 하소연한 나의 꿈.

失題
즐겁은 아츰볏은 사람의 우에 빗나며,
깁븜의 웃음은 사람의 얼굴 우에 잇거라.
모든 것은, 이리도 곱게, 이리도 평화롭게,
한우님의 주신 길을 밟으며 지내가건만,

아, 설어라, 물어뜻고 십허라!
나의 사람이여, 나의 님이여,＜27＞
내 맘에는 限끗도 업시, 눈물 지여라.

11月의 저녁
바람에 불리우는
옷벗은 나무 수풀로
적은 새가 날아갈 때,
한울에는 무겁은 구름이 떠돌며,
저녁 해는 고요히도 넘어라.

고요히 서서, 귀 기울이며, 보아라,
어득한 설은 「悔恨」은, 어둡어지는 밤과 함께,
安息을 기다리는 맘우에 내려오며
빗갈도 업시 핼금한 달은 또 다시 올으지 안는가.

나의 靈이여, 너는, 오늘도 어제와 가티,
혼자 머리를 숙이고, 쪼구리고 잇서라.

가을
깨끗하고도 寂寞한 가을,
맑지고도 엇득스러한 한울,
힘이라곤 족음도 업는듯한 금색의 日光,＜28＞
거울을 씨서노흔 듯한 水面.

바람결에 사랑과 밉움을 노래하는
나무와 나무, 그리하고 落葉과 落葉.

혼자 孤寂하게 남기운 내 맘은
참말로 依支할 곳도 업서진다,
저것 보아, 태양조차, 혼자 떠러저,
구름 뒤에 숨어서, 흐득여 울고 잇다.

설은 喜劇
골패짝은 사거라,
그러나,
골패질은 말아라,
- 法律은 이러케 定하엿서라.

안헤를 동으로 사거라,
그러나,
게집을 돈으로 사지말아라,
- 道德은 이러케 말하엿서라.＜29＞
＜27-29＞
