Chinju Correspondence
In the Kyŏngnam region, there are currently two major issues: (tenant farmer movements) are one, and "education promotion" is the other. Among these "education promotion projects," the Fundraising Movement (期成運動) for the Chinju Ilsin Higher Common School (晋州一新高普) has already succeeded. An application for authorization of a foundational juridical person (財團法人認可願) with a fund of 500,000 *wŏn* was submitted on the 12th of last month. From December 31st of last year to January 12th of this year, with a general mobilization of Chinju citizens, construction work for leveling the Chinju Ilsin Higher Common School building site was carried out. The situation of their obligatory labor service (義務出役) during that time was as follows:

Day 1: 171 people
Day 2: 179 people
Day 3: (Rest)
Day 4: 90 people
Day 5: 466 people
Day 6: 577 people
Day 7: 661 people
Day 8: 573 people
Day 9: 150 people
Day 10: 546 people
Day 11: 488 people
Day 12: 394 people
Day 13: 377 people
Total: 4,672 people

Furthermore, from the first day of construction until the seventh day, the women of Chinju performed obligatory cooking duty (義務出炊) at an average value of 20 *chŏn* per person. The fundraising support association for the same Higher Common School performed the cooking duty from the eighth day to the thirteenth day at an average value of 13 *chŏn* per person. Through this, one can see how magnificent the passion of the Chinju citizens for the promotion of education is.

P'yŏngyang Correspondence
On a terribly cold night, some day in January, in Changbyŏl-ri, Kyŏngch'ang-ri, outside P'yŏngyang city, the wife of a poor man gave birth. When the husband (夫) went to the kitchen, there was nothing to burn. Quickly thinking, he went to the neighboring house, explained the situation, and pleaded for them to lend him just one bundle of firewood. However, the owner of that house—perhaps unable to trust him for even one bundle of firewood—flatly refused. So the poor man stole one bundle from the place where that house stored its firewood and ran away. While doing so, he was caught by a patrolling officer (夜巡하던 나리님) and taken to the police substation. No matter how he explained his family's situation, the policeman also did not trust him. That night, in that poor family's home, the mother, the newborn baby, and even a three-year-old child reportedly froze to death. It is said that only when the policeman went to check the next morning was the man released (放送). Is it the fault of the one who wouldn't lend<78> the wood? The fault of the officer (나리님)? Or the fault of the one who stole? This is the underside of today's society. The P'yŏngyang Labor Alliance (勞働同盟會), boasting 2,500 members, held its founding ceremony last August. Advocating for the progressive livelihood of workers and the solidarity (握手團結) of the propertyless (無産者), it had been active in various fields. The South P'yŏngan Provincial Police Department (平南警察部), observing the internal state of the Alliance, determined that its methods of activity not only closely resembled communism (公産主義), but also that the Alliance managed the Chosŏn Ilbo Branch Office (朝鮮日報支局) and a branch office (支社) of the New Life Company (新生活社) as its organs, utilizing some publication of correspondence, and furthermore, that it was engaged in means intended to resist capitalism (資本主義). For these reasons, on the morning of January 5th, a swarm of detectives suddenly searched the Alliance's office (below, in Suguchi-ri 水口里), the home of Chairman Kim Hong-jŏk (金鴻爵), the home of Chosŏn Ilbo Branch Manager Kim Kyŏng-bin (金景彬), and the homes of several other executives. After discovering about 40 copies of *New Life* (新生活) that had already been confiscated, around 10 a.m. the same day, all the Alliance executives were summoned to the Higher Affairs Section (高等係) of the P'yŏngyang Police Station. They were admonished (說諭) on the grounds that not only was their labor activism turning into communism, but there was also internally an attitude dreaming of the independence movement (獨立運動), which was deemed seditious (不穩當). In response, the Alliance executives reportedly explained that they did not embrace any form of Red ideology (赤化主義).-
P'yŏngyang's particular problems seem likely to arise centered around this organization. What will the future trend be?
(P'yŏngyang One Reporter) <79>

**Summary**

The article presents two contrasting local reports from colonial Korea in 1923. The first, from Chinju, highlights the community's enthusiastic mobilization and voluntary labor to fund and build a new higher common school, showcasing a strong desire for education. The second, from P'yŏngyang, recounts a tragic story of a poor family freezing to death after being denied firewood and the father's subsequent arrest, alongside a report on the P'yŏngyang Labor Alliance facing police investigation for suspected communist and pro-independence activities due to its labor organizing and association with publications. Historians might find the juxtaposition of community self-help for cultural advancement (education) against the backdrop of extreme poverty, social indifference, and state suppression of organized labor and nationalist sentiment particularly revealing of the complex social dynamics and political pressures of the era.

晋州來信
慶南 지방에는 現今 두 가지의 큰일이 잇나니(소작운동)이 그 하나이며 「교육진흥」이 그 하나이다. 그 「교육진흥사업」 중의 하나인 晋州一新高普의 期成運動은 벌서 성공을 하야 기금 50만원의 財團法人認可願을 지난 12일로써 제출하고 작년 12월 31일로부터 금년 10월 12일까지 晋州 시민의 총출동으로써 一新高普 建築基地整理의 공사를 행하얏는데 그네들 그 당시 義務出役 상황이 이러 하엿다.

平壤來信
1월 몃친 날인지 그 몹시 치운 날 밤에 平壤市外 景昌里 將別里에서는 한 빈민의 집에서 그 처가 해산을 하엿는데 그 夫되는 이가 부억에 나아가 보니 때일 것이 하나도 업다. 얼는 생각하고 엽집에 가서 그 사정을 고하고 나무 한 뭇만 꾸여 달나고 간청하엿다. 그러나 그 집 주인은ㅡ나무 한 뭇의 신용을 못하겟던지ㅡ斷然히 거절하엿다. 그래서 그 빈민은 그 집 나무 싸아둔 곳에서 한 뭇을 훔쳐가지고 다라낫다. 그리다가 夜巡하던 나리님에게 붓들려서 파출소까지 잡혀갓다. 아모리 家用의 사정을 말하나 순사 역시 불신용하엿다. 그날 밤에 그 빈민가에서는 산모와 산아와 3세 먹은 아해까지 동사하엿다는 말이다. 이튼날 아츰에 순사가 가 보고서야 그 이를 방송하엿다고 한다. 꾸이지 안흔 者의＜78＞ 탓인지 나리님의 탓인지 도젹질한 이의 탓인지, 이것이 오늘 사회의 이면. 회원 2,500명을 有한 平壤의 勞働同盟會는 작년 8월에 창립식을 거행하고 노동자의 향상적 생활과 無産者의 握手團結을 주창하야 다방면으로 활동하야 오든 중 平南警察部에서, 同會의 내부 국면을 주시하야 그 활동하는 방법이 공산주의에 흡사할 뿐 아니라, 同會에서는 기관으로 朝鮮日報支局과 新生活社의 支社를 경영하야 다소의 통신발표를 이용하며 또한 일면에 잇서서, 자본주의를 저항코져 하는 수단에 在하다 하는 이유로 1월 5일 아츰에 공연히 同會事務所下(水口里)와, 회장 金鴻爵씨와 朝鮮日報支局長 金景彬씨 이외 간부 몃 사람의 집을 전부 수색한 형사의 떼 이미 압수를 당하엿던 新生活 40여부를 발견한 후 同日 오전 10시 경에 同會 간부 일동을 平壤警察署 高等係에서 소환하야, 노동주의를 공산주의로 변할 뿐 아니라, 내부 일면에, 독립운동을  몽상하는 태도까지 有함은 불온당하다는 이유로서 說諭한즉, 이에 대하야 同會 간부측에서는 하등의 赤化主義를 포함치 안엇노라 변명하엿다고.-
平壤의 특수한 문제는 이 단체를 중심하야 일어나것다. 장래의 추이가 如何?
(平壤 一記者)＜79＞
