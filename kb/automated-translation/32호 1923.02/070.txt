天道敎北京敎堂의 建築, 在外天道敎徒의 活躍
						一記者

For years, the activities of Ch'ŏndogyo followers residing in China, Japan, and Russian territories have been quite noteworthy. Particularly, the followers of the same religion (仝敎) in the China region resolved to construct a Ch'ŏndogyo Beijing Church and selected and sent Mr. Ch'oe Tong-o (崔東旿) as a representative to seek the approval of Ch'ŏndogyo followers within Korea proper regarding this matter. Amidst this, on December 23rd of last year (客年), the Preparatory Committee for the Construction of the Beijing Church (北京敎堂建築期成會) was organized and is said to be steadily proceeding with activities today.

The fact that Korea's unique religion is extending its hand of activity across national borders does not fail to be a somewhat significant phenomenon for us in recent times. (一記者)＜44＞

**Summary**

This short news report from February 1923 highlights the growing activities of Ch'ŏndogyo followers outside of Korea, specifically in China, Japan, and Russian territories. It focuses on the plan to build a Ch'ŏndogyo church in Beijing, mentioning the formation of a preparatory committee in late 1922 and the dispatch of Ch'oe Tong-o to gain support from followers within Korea. The author notes the significance of this transnational religious expansion for Korea. For historians, this piece reveals the outward-looking activities and organizational efforts of a major Korean indigenous religion during the colonial period, potentially reflecting both religious fervor and nationalist aspirations extending beyond Korea's borders, even amidst Japanese rule. The mention of activities in Russian territory is also notable.

天道敎北京敎堂의 建築, 在外天道敎徒의 活躍
						一記者
年來로 중국, 일본, 露領에 在한 천도교도의 활동은 자못 주의할 바가 잇섯다. 특히 중국지방에 잇는 仝敎의 교도는 天道敎北京敎堂의 건축을 결의하고 崔東旿씨를 대표로 選送하야 그에 대한 조선내지에 잇는 천도교도의 찬성을 구하던 중 客年 12월 23일로 北京敎堂建築期成會가 조직되야 착착 활동을 진행한다는 금일이다.
조선 고유의 종교가 국경을 넘어 활동의 손을 펴는 그 일은 근래의 우리에게 잇서 일종의 의의잇는 현상됨을 不失한다.(一記者)＜44＞
