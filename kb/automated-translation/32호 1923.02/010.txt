**Body Text Translation:**
To our 1.74 million brothers residing within the Kyŏngnam region. Now, we head far, far southeast to seek you out. Our traveling party has already departed from Kyŏngsŏng Station.

You likely already know that our project for the year starting last autumn is to conduct a fundamental survey of Korean culture, whereby we personally undertake field investigations (踏査) in various places of each province, and report what we have investigated and perceived in each monthly issue of "Kaebyŏk" in the format of a Provincial Issue (道號). The traveling party that left Kyŏngsŏng Station! That party is solely for the purpose of carrying out this task. For that task, the very first step is seeking the 1.74 million brothers of Kyŏngnam.

A Fundamental Survey of Korean Culture! A Fundamental Survey of Kyŏngnam Culture! The task is indeed great, and the meaning is truly deep. However, whether our meager strength (微力) will be capable of handling this is, in truth, our worry day and night (晝宵).

"First, let's know Chosŏn. Let's know it clearly. And so, let each and every person of Chosŏn become the master of the great work of finding 'Chosŏn's glorious tomorrow.'" Our general spirit is bound to this. We know that your spirit is surely the same. In the place where this spirit and that spirit meet, there our meager strength will surely no longer be meager. With that power, we dare to undertake the great work of the Fundamental Survey of Kyŏngnam Culture (in sum, the Fundamental Survey of Korean Culture). This is merely the one word we offer in advance to our 1.74 million brothers.

What we will investigate together with you this time are:
The Causes and Trends (趨向) of Various Social Problems (諸社會問題)
Introduction and Critique of Central Figures (中心人物) and Major Business Organizations (主要事業機關)
The State of Industry, Education (産業敎育), and Religion (宗敎)
Inquiry (探究) into Scenic Spots and Historical Sites (名勝古蹟) and Legends (傳說)
The Nature of Human Feelings and Customs (人情風俗)
Other observations and critiques regarding the general situation (狀勢) <1>

**Summary**

This editorial notice from the February 1923 issue of *Kaebyŏk* magazine announces a major year-long project: a "Fundamental Survey of Korean Culture." The editors inform readers, particularly those in South Kyŏngsang Province (Kyŏngnam), that a team has departed Seoul (Kyŏngsŏng) to begin the survey there. The goal is to investigate various aspects of regional life—social issues, key figures and organizations, industry, education, religion, historical sites, folklore, and customs—to foster a deeper understanding of Korea ("Chosŏn") and empower Koreans to build a "glorious tomorrow." The notice appeals for cooperation, framing the survey as a collaborative effort between the magazine and the local populace, driven by a shared spirit. For historians, this article is significant as it demonstrates a concrete effort by Korean intellectuals under colonial rule to systematically study and document Korean culture and society on a regional basis, likely as a form of cultural nationalism and self-strengthening. It highlights the magazine *Kaebyŏk*'s role in promoting national consciousness and outlines the specific areas deemed crucial for understanding contemporary Korea in the 1920s.

百七十四萬의 兄弟를 찻는 마당에, 이 글을 特히 慶南人士에게 부칩니다
174만의 慶南域內에 계신 형제들이여. 이제 우리는 당신네를 차자 멀리 멀리 東南下합니다. 그의 일행은 벌서 京城驛을 떠낫소이다.
우리 가을해 1년의 사업으로 조선 문화에 대한 기본조사를 행하되 우리들이 몸소 各道의 各地를 實地로 踏査하야 그 査實한 바와 그 感得한 바를 매월호의 「開闢」에 道號의 형식으로써 공개한다 함은 여러분이 일즉 아르섯슬 것입니다. 京城驛을 떠난 그의 일행! 그의 일행은 專혀 그 일을 하기 위함이외다. 그 일을 위하야 第一着으로 174만의 慶南 兄弟를 찻는 것이외다.
朝鮮文化의 基本調査! 慶南文化의 基本調査! 일은 과연 크오며 뜻은 사실 깁습니다. 그런데 우리의 微力이 능히 이를 堪當할넌지 晝宵의 우려는 진실로 여긔에 잇슴니다.
「먼저 朝鮮을 알자. 분명히 알자. 그래서 朝鮮의 사람사람이 제각히 「朝鮮의 光榮잇는 明日」을 차저 내이는 큰 일의 임자가 되게 하자」. 우리의 일반정신은 여긔에 멧첫슴니다. 여러분의 정신도 역시 그러할 줄 압니다. 이 정신과 정신이 서로 합치되는 자리에 그 자리에서는 우리의 微力은 벌서 微力이 아니겟슴니다. 그 힘으로써 慶南文化의 基本調査 (합하야 말하면 朝鮮文化의 基本調査)ㅡ그러한 큰 일을 감히 하려 합니다. 174만여의 형제에게 미리 한 말씀 들이는 것은 다못 이것이외다.

우리가 이번에 당신네들과 가티 査實하올 것은
諸社會問題의 原因 及 趨向
中心人物 及 主要事業機關의 紹介 及 批評
産業敎育 及 宗敎의 狀況如何
名勝古蹟 及 傳說의 探究
人情風俗의 如何
其他一般 狀勢에 관한 觀察과 批評＜1＞
