社告 (Company Announcement)

As previously announced, this company, having various thoughts this year, will conduct a basic survey regarding the culture of Chosŏn (朝鮮) and, along with this, publish special provincial issues for each province (各道).

Our company's executive staff members, Mr. Kim Ki-hyŏng (金起瀅) and Mr. Ch'a Sang-ch'an (車相瓚), have now departed for the Kyŏngnam region (慶南地方) with that mission.

They plan to meticulously survey the Kyŏngnam territory (慶南 境內) over approximately thirty days.

People of Kyŏngnam, please give them special care and convenience.

February 1st
Kaebyŏksa <84>

**Summary**

This announcement from the Kaebyok Company (Kaebyŏksa) informs readers that, as part of a plan to conduct a basic survey of Korean culture, they are dispatching two staff members, Kim Ki-hyŏng and Ch'a Sang-ch'an, to the Gyeongsangnam-do (Kyŏngnam) region for approximately 30 days. The purpose of their visit is to conduct fieldwork for upcoming special provincial issues of the magazine. The company requests the support and assistance of the local people for their researchers. For historians, this article is significant as it documents an organized effort by a Korean cultural organization during the colonial period to systematically research and preserve Korean regional culture. It reflects a form of cultural nationalism and intellectual activity aimed at understanding and documenting the nation despite colonial constraints.
