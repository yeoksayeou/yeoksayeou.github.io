From the Editorial Department

This issue primarily aimed to write about liberation (해방). Not only is bringing up (捉來함) this issue at the beginning of the New Year not without meaning, but it is also because we know that our lives today must rightfully (맛당히) be lives based on liberation.
Therefore, articles not closely related (갓갑지 아니한) to the editorial spirit of this issue were largely pushed back (밀엇스며), and there were also many precious manuscripts (玉稿) submitted by several others that we could not handle as we wished (만핫다). On this point, we particularly ask for the generous forgiveness (寬恕) of those who submitted articles.

As announced several times in separate notices (別紙로), we have decided to undertake a basic survey (基本調査) of the current conditions (現狀) of Chosŏn (朝鮮) as a new project for this year. A team (一隊) of investigators has already (벌서) departed for the Kyŏngsangnam-do (慶南) area.
As we roughly surmise (짐작하는 바와 가티), Kyŏngsangnam-do is a place where the gap between rich and poor is particularly severe (別로 심하며), and it can be called a place where Japan's (日本) economic management power (경제적 경영력) is strongest. At the same time, the various issues (諸문제) arising there today greatly draw (惹起함이 甚多하나니) our attention. For instance (가론), the tenant farmer movement (소작운동), for instance (가론) the movement to promote domestic products (국산운동), for instance (가론) the fishing rights issue (어업권 문제), and similar matters (가튼 것) are undoubtedly (不失하는 바이거니와) prominent examples (顯例) among them. Besides these, it is a fact that many (幾多) serious problems (重大問題) are contained (包藏되어 잇는) within. We wonder (모르괘라) what the results of investigating (査實한) the entire Kyŏngsangnam-do area will indeed be.

Along with (共히) embarking on this "Basic Survey of Present-Day Chosŏn" (朝鮮現在의 基本調査), there is one request (한 말의 부탁) we must make. It is a matter concerning (事이라) the organizations and esteemed members/friends (社友諸賢) in various regions. That is, since this current undertaking (今回의 此擧) cannot, in reality (기실), achieve complete results (완전한 성과를 엇지 못할 것인즉) unless it relies on the strength of nationwide unity (擧國一致의 力), we ask that you please provide active convenience/assistance (적극적의 편의를 도모해줍시요) regarding this. This, in fact (사실상으로), we cannot help but request (원하지 안흘 수 업다). We venture to ask, esteemed sirs, what are your thoughts (敢問諸賢意中如何)?<68>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the February 1923 issue of *Kaebyŏk* magazine discusses the focus of the issue on "liberation" and explains that some submitted articles were postponed. It announces a major new project: a "Basic Survey of Present-Day Chosŏn" aimed at understanding the current conditions across Korea. The first investigation team has been dispatched to Kyŏngsangnam-do, noted for its significant wealth disparity and strong Japanese economic influence, highlighting issues like tenant disputes, the promotion of domestic products, and fishing rights. The editors appeal to organizations and associates nationwide for cooperation, emphasizing that the survey's success depends on collective effort. For historians, the article is interesting as it explicitly links the magazine's work to the concept of "liberation" (해방) in 1923, indicating early nationalist or anti-colonial sentiment articulated through social analysis. The plan to conduct a nationwide socio-economic survey, starting with a region heavily impacted by Japanese economic policy (Kyŏngsangnam-do), reflects a systematic attempt by Korean intellectuals to document the realities of colonial rule and mobilize public awareness and potentially resistance, highlighting specific points of conflict like tenant farming and resource control (fishing rights).

編輯局으로부터
이번 호는 주로 해방에 관한 것을 적으려 하엿다. 신춘벽두에 한 번 이 문제를 捉來함이 의미 업는 일이 아닐 뿐 外라 우리의 오늘 생활은 맛당히 해방을 근저로 한 생활이 되지 아니할 수 업슴을 알은 故이다.
딸하 본호의 편집정신과 갓갑지 아니한 기사는 만히 뒤로 밀엇스며 그 외의 몃 분의 주신 玉稿도 마음대로 하지 못한 것이 만핫다. 이 점은 특히 투고하신의 寬恕를 비는 바이다.
別紙로 여러번 발표한 바도 잇거니와 우리는 금년도의 새사업으로 朝鮮의 현상에 대한 기본조사를 행하기로 하엿다. 조사원의 一隊는 벌서 慶南方面을 향하엿다.
우리가 대강이라도 짐작하는 바와 가티 慶南은 빈부의 차가 別로 심하며 일본의 경제적 경영력이 가장 강한 곳이라 할 만한 동시에 그 곳에서 일어나는 금일의 諸문제는 우리의 注意를 惹起함이 甚多하나니 가론 소작운동, 가론 국산운동 가론 어업권 문제 가튼 것은 그 중의 顯例됨을 不失하는 바이거니와 그 외에도 幾多의 重大問題가 包藏되어 잇는 것은 사실이다. 모르괘라 慶南一帶를 査實한 결과가 과연 무엇일고.
이번 「朝鮮現在의 基本調査」에 착수함과 共히 한 말의 부탁을 아니할 수 업는 바는 각지의 단체와 社友諸賢에 대한 事이라. 즉 今回의 此擧는 기실 擧國一致의 力에 의함이 아니면 완전한 성과를 엇지 못할 것인즉 여긔에 대한 적극적의 편의를 도모해줍시요 하는 그것이다. 이것만은 사실상으로 원하지 안흘 수 업다. 敢問諸賢意中如何,＜68＞
