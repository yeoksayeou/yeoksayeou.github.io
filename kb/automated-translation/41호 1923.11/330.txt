餘墨 (Remaining Ink/Afterthoughts)

Time really flies; after just one more monthly issue, we will be preparing the New Year's issue. We have decided to put forth our absolute effort for the New Year's issue to truly publish a 『開闢』 (Kaebyŏk) worthy of the New Year. However, we hope that many readers will send in manuscripts for the New Year's issue.

Our company's 『朝鮮文化調査』 (Chosŏn Cultural Survey) is, in fact, proceeding little by little, but it could not be included in the paper this time either. This is because the survey of Kangwŏn Province, with its many mountains and rough roads, did not proceed as planned. Generally, the more things don't go according to plan, the more suffering there is. How great must be the distress of Mr. Ch'a Sang-ch'an, who is exploring Kangwŏn Province? Readers, before you reproach us, please sympathize with our situation.

A branch office has newly been established on Cheju Island, and the existing branch office in Taegu has been temporarily closed. However, it seems something will happen in Taegu soon as well. Another thing to mention is that the Ch'ŏrwŏn Bureau in Kangwŏn Province has now been expanded into the Kwandong Branch Office, and Mr. Chŏng Chae-hwa has newly become the Branch Manager at the Chinju Branch Office. What I want to mention even more than this is that Mr. Kim Chŏng-gi, the Kando Branch Manager, who was imprisoned (囹圄) for a long time, was released a short while ago. If our intentions resonate, let us rejoice together.

Although I cannot say definitively yet, it seems likely that around the beginning of November, we will depart for the 『文化調査』 (Cultural Survey) concerning Ch'ungnam Province. The days are getting shorter and colder, and this year's prospects are becoming meager, so we must push forward without delay. If we do depart, we will announce it again at the time of departure. I add a word especially for our Ch'ungnam brothers. <44>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the November 1923 issue of *Kaebyŏk* discusses the magazine's upcoming New Year's edition, delays in their ambitious "Chosŏn Cultural Survey" project due to difficulties encountered in Kangwŏn Province, and updates on the status of various regional branch offices, including closures, expansions, new appointments, and the recent release of the Kando branch manager from prison. It concludes by announcing tentative plans to survey Ch'ungnam Province soon. From a historical perspective, the article offers insights into the operational challenges faced by Korean cultural publications under colonialism, the geographic scope of their activities (including remote areas like Kangwŏn, Cheju, and Kando), and the political risks involved, highlighted by the imprisonment and release of the Kando branch manager, Kim Chŏng-gi, hinting at the surveillance and suppression faced by nationalist-leaning organizations.

餘墨
세월이란 정말 빠르다, 한달 號만 더 하고는, 新年號를 하게 되겟다. 新年號에는 절대의 노력을 드려, 정말 新年號다운 『開闢』을 내이기로 햇다. 그런대 독자들 중에서, 新年號 원고 좀 만히 보내주엇스면 됴켓다.
본사의 『朝鮮文化調査』는 사실로는, 작고 작고 진행되는대, 紙上에는 이번에도 못되엿다. 산 만코 길 험한 江原道의 조사가 마음대로 진행되지 못한 탓이다. 대개 일이란, 예정대로 못될사록 고통은 더 되는 것이라, 江原道를 답사하고 잇는 車相瓚군의 고심은 과연 얼마나 할 것인고, 독자여, 우리를 나물하기 전에 우리의 사정을 憫憐해주소.
濟州島엔 새로히 支社가 되엿고 大邱에는 잇던 支社가 잠간 업서지게 되엿다. 그러나 大邱에도 日間으로 무엇이 될 모양이다. 또 한가지 말할 것은 江原道의 鐵原分局이, 이번에 關東支社로 확장되고, 晋州支社에는 鄭載華씨가 새로히 支社長이 되엿다. 이보다도 더 말하고 십흔 것은 間島支社長 金正祺氏가, 오래동안 囹圄중에 잇다가, 얼마 전에 出獄된 것이다. 뜻이 통하는 이어든 서로들 깃버해주소.
아직 결정적으로는 말할 수 업스나, 11月 初旬 限하고, 忠南道에 대한 『文化調査』를 떠날 듯 십다. 날은 작고 추워가고, 금년도 세색이 열버가는대, 그냥 모라치지 아느면 안되겟는 故이다. 만일 떠나거든, 떠나는 때에, 다시 알리게 될 것이다. 특히 忠南兄弟를 위해서 一言을 加한다.＜44＞
