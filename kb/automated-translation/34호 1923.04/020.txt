To Those Reading the April Issue

There are various contents in this April issue of our magazine (本誌). Accordingly, if we were to say something to those who will read the April issue, those words would also be varied. However, what we wish to speak of now is not about those various things, but only about the article concerning the "Kyŏngsang Province Feature" (慶尙道號) in this issue (本號). We will state this in items (項), so please pay attention and read carefully.

1. The "Basic Survey of Korean Culture" (朝鮮文化의 基本調査) that our company is carrying out this time (今回) is, as shown by its title, a basic survey. In other words, it is a foundational survey (기초조사). That is, the primary intention of this step is not to complete a concrete survey of the entire face of Korea all at once this time, but rather first to gain a basic concept of the general phenomena of that region and to establish foundational contact with the brethren (형제) of that region. Accordingly, the Kyŏngnam (慶南) articles in this month's issue also only record the general outline (大軆) concerning it first. All concrete investigations and records will continue to appear successively (續出) following later (後).

2. Besides making the results of this investigation (今回) public so that each of our brethren (형제) may gain what they can from it, it is not without the intention for us ourselves, by tacitly keeping and storing (黙契藏存) a portion of it, to use it as a resource (資하쟈) for the great, world-creating work (개벽적 대사업) of Korea henceforth (今後). Accordingly, among the Kyŏngnam (慶南) articles published in this issue (本號), while that which was to be announced (發布) was announced, there is also that which was not to be announced (發布) that was not announced.

You must read this issue (本號) of our magazine (本誌) after acknowledging the two conditions above (右). We specially announce (特告) this intention to our brethren (형제) residing in Kyŏngnam (慶南) or outside of Kyŏngnam. <1>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript addresses readers of the April 1923 issue of *Kaebyŏk* magazine, specifically regarding its feature on Kyŏngsang Province (referred to as 慶尙道 and 慶南). The editors explain that their "Basic Survey of Korean Culture" is foundational, aiming to establish general understanding and connections rather than provide exhaustive detail immediately. Importantly, they state that not all findings are published; some information is deliberately withheld (黙契藏存) to serve as a resource for the future "great, world-creating work of Korea." For historians, this piece is interesting as it reveals the magazine's strategic approach to cultural research under colonial conditions, balancing public dissemination with the covert preservation of knowledge potentially deemed sensitive or useful for future nationalist or social transformation projects ('개벽적 대사업'). The explicit mention of withholding information highlights the constraints and perhaps the ambitions of Korean intellectuals during this period.

四月號를 닑는 이에게
4월호의 本誌에도 여러 가지의 내용이 잇습니다. 딸하서 4월호를 닑을 이에게 무슨 말슴을 들인다 하면 그 말슴도 여러가지이겟습니다. 그러나 지금 말슴코저 하는 것은 그 여러 가지에 대한 것이 아니요 오즉 本號의 「慶尙道號」 기사에 對한 그것이외다. 項을 지어 말슴 들일 것이니 유의해서 보아주시요.
1. 본사의 今回에 집행하는 「朝鮮文化의 基本調査」는 표방 그것에서 보임과 가티 기본조사. 다시 말하면 기초조사이외다. 즉 이번 한 번에 조선 전면에 대한 구체적 조사를 완성하쟈 함이 아니요 먼저 그 지방의 일반현상에 대한 기본개념을 엇고 그 지방의 형제와 더불어 기초적 연락을 취하쟈 함이 이번 거름의 주요한 뜻이 됩니다. 딸하서 本月號의 慶南 기사에도 먼저 그에 대한 大軆를 적엇슬 뿐입니다. 구체의 모든 조사며 기록은 後를 딸하 그냥 續出될 것입니다.
2. 今回의 조사는 조사한 그것을 일반에게 공개하야 형제 각자가 그 어들 바를 엇게 하는써 외에 우리 자신이 그것의 일부분을 黙契藏存함에 의하야써 금후 조선의 개벽적 대사업에 資하쟈는 그것이 업지 아니합니다. 딸하서 本號에 발표된 慶南의 기사도 發布할 그것을 發布한 동시에 發布치 아니할 것을 發布치 아니한 그것이 잇습니다.
右의 두 가지의 조건을 인정하신 후 本號의 本誌를 닑어주셔야 되겟습니다. 이 뜻을 慶南 또는 慶南 이외에 계신 형제에게 特告합니다.＜1＞
