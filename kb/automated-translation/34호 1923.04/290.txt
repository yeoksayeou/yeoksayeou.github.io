After Putting Down the Pen

As I am about to put down the pen for editing this issue, several thoughts come to mind.

1.  As articles related to Kyŏngnam (慶南) increased, it couldn't help but affect other articles. We tried as much as possible not to let this happen, but beyond a certain point, it became impossible. Accordingly, the articles submitted by various contributors inevitably could not be published. It is truly regrettable, but it couldn't be helped.

2.  Some readers might not think well of the play titled "The Apostate" (「背敎者」), published in the literary section of this issue. However, that is a matter that cannot be left to the common sense and virtue of those readers.

3.  Despite our repeated statements, some contributors, when their submissions are not accepted, request their return, and so forth (云云). However, they will absolutely not be returned, so it would be good for both parties to be aware of this from the very beginning (自初).

4.  Among the submissions, those related to literature are the most numerous, and among literary submissions, those centered on romance are the most numerous. We do not welcome writings belonging to such categories and hope that contributors will instead focus on somewhat more practical (實地잇는) areas. <74>

**Summary**

The editor reflects on completing the 34th issue of *Kaebyŏk*. Key points include: prioritizing Kyŏngnam-related articles led to rejecting other submissions; defending the publication of a potentially controversial play ("The Apostate") against reader sensibilities; reiterating the policy of not returning rejected manuscripts; and expressing a preference for submissions focusing on "practical" matters over the numerous romance-themed literary pieces received. From a colonial history perspective, the editor's notes reveal the magazine's regional focus (Kyŏngnam), its willingness to publish potentially sensitive content despite anticipated reader reaction, and its editorial stance favoring practical, possibly socially relevant, writing over popular romance themes, reflecting the intellectual and social currents of the time.

擲筆之餘
本號編輯의 붓을 노흐려 하니 몃가지 생각되는 것이 잇다.
1. 慶南에 관한 기사가 만하짐을 딸하 다른 기사가 영향을 아니 밧지 못하는 것이다. 될 수  잇는대로 아니 그리하고저 하엿스나 어느 정도까지는 할 수가 업게 된다. 딸하 투고한 여   러분의 기사는 스스로 내지 못하게 된다. 적이 미안하나 할 수 업는 일이오.
2. 本號의 문예편에 게재된 「背敎者」라는 각본은 독자 중의 엇던 분은 조치 못하게 생각할지도 모르겟다. 그러나 그것은 독자 그분네의 常識과 德養에 맛길 수 업는 일이오.
3. 우리가 매양 말해두엇슴에도 불구하고 투고하는 이 중 엇던 이는 그것을 채용치 아니할  때에 반송하라고 云云한다. 그러나 그것은 절대로 반송치 아니할지니 그 점에 대해서는     自初로 각오함이 피차에 조흘 것이며
4. 투고 중에는 문예에 관한 것이 제일 만흐며 문예의 투고 중에도 연애중심의 것이 제일   만흔 바 우리는 그런 부류에 속한 글을 환영치 아니하며 될 수 잇는대로는 더 좀 實地잇    는 방면에 착안함이 잇기를 바라는 바이다.＜74＞
