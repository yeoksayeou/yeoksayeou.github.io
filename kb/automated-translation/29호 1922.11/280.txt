Company Announcement

Mr. Pak Pong-ŭi (朴鳳儀), the Kyŏngnam Branch Manager of Kaebyŏk, has resigned due to circumstances (事勢), and Mr. Chŏng Chae-hwa (鄭載華) has been newly appointed. General affairs have been further expanded (擴張).

Therefore, we ask the esteemed readers (愛讀者位) within the jurisdiction (管內) to please understand (洞亮하시옵).

Kaebyŏk Company <36>

**Summary**

This is a company announcement from Kaebyŏk Company informing readers that the Kyŏngnam (South Kyŏngsang Province) Branch Manager, Pak Pong-ŭi, has resigned and been replaced by Chŏng Chae-hwa. It also states that the branch's general affairs have been expanded and asks for the readers' understanding. For historians, this notice provides insight into the organizational structure (regional branches) of a major Korean cultural magazine during the colonial period, personnel changes, and the company's direct communication with its readership in specific regions.
