As Heard and Seen

Everyone acknowledges Mr. Yun Ch'iho's (尹致昊氏) extraordinary intelligence (怜悧) and agility (敏捷), but it's said that he hardly ever looks up to listen to anyone else's words. However, it is said he almost unconditionally obeys (聽從) only the words of his mother (母親) and elder brother, Mr. Ch'iso (致昭氏). Anyway, he's the number one person in Chosŏn for intelligence! Incidentally, there's a rumor (說) that he will assume the position of Principal at Songdo Higher Common School (松都高等普通學校). Of course, that too is conditional (有條件).

Maya (馬野), the Kyŏnggi Province (京畿道) Police Chief appointed in September (9月), is quite tall and slender, and his face also looks quite presentable. When meeting him briefly, his amiable manner quite intoxicates (魔醉) people. However, seeing as his distinctive masterpiece, the "empty laugh" (헛웃음), is forcefully deployed in almost every conversation (話題), one wants to acknowledge that there must be something within that laughter that one cannot help but be wary (警戒) of. In any case (如何間), we shall know by observing over time.

The weapons (武器) that serve as the physical evidence (實物) of the Vladivostok (海參威) arms issue—which proved Japan's dual diplomacy (二重外交), causing it to lose national credibility (國家의 信義) internationally and inducing an imminent cabinet crisis (內閣의 危機) domestically—are actually weapons from the Czech (「체크」) army. These were arranged to be sold to the Independence Army for a price of one million Won (百萬圓) through negotiations (交涉) led by the subordinate of Kim Hasŏk (金河錫), known as a leader (巨頭) of the overseas Korean Independence Group (在外 朝鮮獨立團). After carrying out the contract procedures (契約節次), they were about to deliver a ten thousand Won (十萬圓) deposit (契約金) (from the 150,000 Won in Bank of Chosŏn notes (朝鮮銀行券) that had been forcibly seized (强取)), but this ended in failure (不成功에 歸한) due to the breakdown (破綻) of the aforementioned 150,000 Won incident (十五萬圓 事件). Whether it happened this way or that, those weapons were destined only to cause trouble (말썽) with Japan. Generally, that's the result of deploying an army without justification (無名의 軍), one might say, and leave it at that.

This year's flood damage (水害) in Hwanghae Province (黃海道) was truly tragic upon tragic (悽慘). However, within Hwanghae Province, there is a fact perhaps even more dreadful (끔찍한) than this flood damage. This is the statement that the land owned by 87 Japanese landowners (日人地主) in the province accounts for 47 percent (四七割分) of Hwanghae Province's total cultivated land (全耕地). Such a situation is surely not limited to just Hwanghae Province, yet while voices calling for flood relief (水害救濟) are widely heard (喧傳) everywhere, why is it that we do not hear voices calling for relief measures (救濟策) regarding this issue? (借間是何故) If a person has no foresight, they will surely face immediate worries (人無遠慮면 必有近憂).

When one mentions Mr. Chang Tuhyŏn (張斗鉉氏), President of the Tongyang Products Company (東洋物産會社), people in the business world (實業界) of Kyŏngsŏng (京城) can generally guess (斟酌) what kind of person he is. But now there is something from which to infer (推察) his character in simple terms. The past October 3rd was the first birthday celebration (晬宴日) for Mr. Pak Hŭido's (朴熙道氏) father. Due to an issue with banquet expenses (宴會費問題) for some association, he opened his wallet (紙匣). After taking out the required amount (所要金), the remaining money (殘餘金) was exactly fifteen chŏn (十五錢). He suddenly said, "With this, I can still share a glass of liquor each with two friends (동무)." That in a casual remark (내치는 말) he thought of two friends suggests how much Mr. Chang likes close friends (親友) and is rich in sociability (社交性).<69>

**Summary**

This article presents several unrelated news tidbits from late 1922 Korea. It comments on the perceived character of Yun Ch'iho, the new Kyŏnggi Police Chief Maya, and businessman Chang Tuhyŏn. It also discusses the Vladivostok arms incident involving Czech weapons, a failed deal with Korean independence groups, and its implications for Japanese diplomacy. Finally, it highlights the severe flood damage in Hwanghae Province but contrasts it with the arguably more alarming issue of Japanese landownership concentration (47%) in the same province, questioning the lack of public outcry compared to flood relief efforts. For historians, the article offers valuable contemporary perspectives on prominent figures under colonial rule, insights into the complex international dealings related to the Korean independence movement (Vladivostok arms), and stark data on the socio-economic reality of increasing Japanese land control, reflecting anxieties about colonization's deeper impact beyond immediate disasters.

隨聞見隨
尹致昊氏의 남다른 怜悧와 또 敏捷은 누구나 다- 認하는 바이거니와 氏는 그만치 누구의 말이라도 처다보아 듯는 일은 업다는 말이다. 그런데 自己의 母親 及 兄 致昭氏의 말뿐은 거의 無條件으로 聽從하다고 어찌햇던 怜悧하기로는 朝鮮의 第一人! 그런데 氏는 松都高等普通學校의 校長職에 就하리라는 說이 傳한다. 그 亦 有條件인 것은 勿論.

9月 中에 新任한 馬野京畿道警察部長은 키가 자못 후리후리하고 얼굴이 또한 그럴듯하게 보이어써 暫間 對하면 그 서글서글한 맛이 자못 사람을 魔醉함이 잇다. 그러나 그의 남달은 傑作인 「헛웃음」이 거의 話題마다에 强發되는 것으로써 보면 그 웃음 속에는 반듯이 警戒하지 아니치 못할 무엇이 잇는 것을 認하고 십다. 如何間 두고 보면 알일.

日本의 二重外交를 立證하야 外로는 國家의 信義를 天下에 失하고 內로는 內閣의 危機를 目前에 誘來케 한 海參威武器問題의 實物되는 그 武器는 即 「체크」軍의 武器로서 이것은 일즉히 在外 朝鮮獨立團의 巨頭라 稱할 金河錫部下의 交涉을 以하야 百萬圓의 價金으로 獨立軍에게 賣渡하기로 되어 契約節次를 行하고 十萬圓의 契約金 (朝鮮銀行券 十五萬圓을 强取한 그 中에서)을 先渡하려 하던 中例의 十五萬圓 事件의 破綻으로 不成功에 歸한 그것이라 한다. 저러케 되엇던 이러케 되엇던 그 武器는 日本과는 말성을 닐으키게만 된 物이다. 大槪 無名의 軍을 出한 結果란 大槪 그러한 것이니라 하면 그만.

黃海道의 今年 水害는 實로 悽慘에 悽慘을 極하엿다. 그러나 黃海道 안에는 이 水害보다도 오히려 끔즉한 事實이 잇다. 이것은 該道內의 日人地主 87人의 所有한 土地가 黃海道 全耕地의 四七割分을 占有하엿다는 말이다. 이러한 일은 반듯이 黃海道뿐만 아닐 터인데 水害救濟의 聲은 四處에 喧傳함에 不拘하고 이에 對한 救濟策의 聲을 不聞함은 借間是何故, 人無遠慮면 必有近憂.

東洋物産會社長 張斗鉉氏라 하면 京城의 實業界에서 大槪로 그가 如何한 人임을 斟酌할 바이거니와 이제 簡單한 말로써 그의 性格을 推察할 거리가 잇다. 지나간 10月 3日 朴熙道氏의 父親 晬宴日이다. 그는 어떠한 會의 宴會費問題로 하야 돈 紙匣을 열엇는데 그 所要金을 除하고 殘餘金이 正히 十五錢이라 그는 문득 가로되 「이것도 동무 두 사람과 가티 술 한잔式을 난흘 수가 잇다」고 하엿다. 문득 내치는 말에 동무 두 사람을 생각하야 말함과 가틈은 氏가 如何히 親友를 조하하며 또는 社交性에 富함을 알 수 잇다.＜69＞
