誓願 (Sŏwŏn - Vow/Pledge)

1. How can we make each and every one of our Brothers a subject who possesses the most systematic wisdom (智) and organizational power (力), unwavering and perfectly ordered (萬撓不惑, 一絲不紊 - man'yo pulhok, ilsa pulmun), in all the movements and actions they undertake?

1. How can we make these most sound individuals come together and advance, entwined in the bliss of shared purpose (同歸一體의 法悅下 - tonggwi ilch'eŭi pŏbyŏlha), without the slightest flaw of arrogance or dependency?

There is no other way. First, there is only the path of making each of our people possess the clearest consciousness (最 明瞭한 意識 - ch'oe myŏngnyohan ŭisik) and the most sincere awakening (極眞摯한 覺悟 - kŭk chinjihan kak'o) regarding the environment surrounding them or the various problems of real life (實生活 - silsaenghwal) that bind them, so that they truly face the challenge of pioneering their own destiny (運命開拓 - unmyŏng kaech'ŏk) with a tense spirit. Who then will be the one to first raise a blazing torch (炎炎한 炬火 - yŏmyŏmhan kŏhwa) in these confusing and indistinct, vast streets (紛紜無分의 漠漠한 街頭 - punyun mubunŭi mangmak'an kaedu)? Our company (本社 - Pon Sa), having taken upon itself the belief that "there is only the utmost devotion and effort (殫誠盡瘁 - tansŏng jinch'oe) as our organ," relying on the foundation built through over two and a half years of hard-fought struggles (惡戰苦鬪 - akchŏn kot'u), and sensing the rapid turn of the situation (局面의 急轉 - kungmyŏnŭi kŭpchŏn)—referring to the daily shrinking life-destiny of our compatriots (萎縮되는 同胞의 生活運命 - wich'uktoenŭn tongp'oŭi saenghwal unmyŏng)—which attacks with an intensity that worsens daily and monthly (剝床切?, 日甚月酷 - paksang ch'ŏl?, il sim wŏl hok), takes a step further beyond reforming the contents of this magazine as previously announced＜2＞ on the first page of the last issue, establishing a new era from now on:

1. To establish and hold monthly lectures on the current situation (時局講座 - Siguk Kangjwa), working in parallel with the articles of this magazine, to foster the clearest judgment and thorough attention regarding all current affairs surging domestically and internationally (國內 國外 - Kungnae Kuk'oe).

2. To realize the system of company friends (社友의 制度 - Sauŭi Chedo) according to our company regulations (本社의 社規 - Pon Saŭi Sagyu), thereby aspiring to the completion of the rebirth movement (更生運動 - Kaengsaeng Undong) we advocate, through the mutual seeking and response (相求相應 - sanggu sang'ŭng) between comrades (志士 - chisa).

We have now expressed, albeit briefly, our humble intentions and small plans in a few words. What other meaning could there be? It is solely about how we might form the most resolute individuals or groups through connection with more comrades (동무 - tongmu).

Brothers! If you discover even one iota of insincerity in what we plan or implement today, cut out our tongues and break our pens the moment you find it. However, if you recognize that there is truly a basis and an overflow of deep emotion (感激 - kamgyŏk) in what we are doing, then grasp our hands and communicate with us (聲氣를 通하라 - sŏnggirŭl t'onghara) at that very moment. It is either that or this! There should be no hesitation (躊躇 - chuch'ŏ).＜3＞
＜2-3＞

**Summary**

This editorial from *Kaebyŏk* magazine, titled "Vow," expresses the publication's renewed commitment to galvanizing the Korean people. It poses questions about how to foster strong, organized individuals and unite them into a powerful collective force, free from arrogance or dependency. The only path, it asserts, is through achieving clear consciousness and sincere awakening regarding their oppressive reality. The magazine vows to lead this effort by introducing monthly lectures on current affairs and establishing a membership system to connect like-minded individuals ("comrades") and advance their declared "rebirth movement." It passionately urges readers to join their cause if they believe in its sincerity, or condemn it if not, emphasizing the urgency of choosing a path. For historians, this piece vividly illustrates the intellectual fervor and organizational strategies employed by Korean nationalists during the colonial period. It highlights the perceived crisis ("shrinking life-destiny"), the emphasis on individual enlightenment and collective action as solutions, and the crucial role media like *Kaebyŏk* played in cultivating national consciousness and resistance under Japanese rule. The language reflects the urgency and determination of the movement.

誓願
1. 어떠케 하면 우리 兄弟의 사람사람이 다- 各各 그 云爲하는 一切의 運動이며 動作에 잇서 萬撓不惑, 一絲不紊의 最系統的 智와 組織的 力을 所持하는 主體가 되게 할가.
1. 어떠케 하면 이와 가튼 最 健全한 個人과 個人이 小毫라도 驕傲와 依賴의 瑕累가 업시 다못 同歸一體의 法悅下에서 엉키어 나아가는 群團이 되게 할가.
다른 道里가 업다. 먼저 우리의 사람사람으로 하여금 自己를 에워싸고 잇는 그 環境 또는 自己를 얽어매고 잇는 實生活의 諸 問題에 對한 最 明瞭한 意識과 極眞摯한 覺悟를 有하야 정말 緊張된 氣分으로써 自己의 運命開拓의 衝에 當하게 하는 한길 밧게는 업다. 그러면 이 紛紜無分의 漠漠한 街頭에 先하야 炎炎한 炬火를 들 者가 누구냐. 오즉 「우리 機關으로의 殫誠盡瘁가 잇슬 뿐이라」 自任한 本社는 惡戰苦鬪 2年有半의 時日로써 이뤄진 基礎의 完成에 仗하고 剝床切?, 日甚月酷의 形勢로써 덥쳐오는 局面의 急轉(나날이 萎縮되는 同胞의 生活運命을 指稱함이라)에 感하야 前號 卷頭에서＜2＞ 일즉히 聲明한 바와 가티 本誌內容의 革新으로써 自今의 新紀元을 삼는 以外에 다시 一步를 進하야
1. 月 1回의 時局講座를 設行하야 本誌의 記事와 並勢하야써 國內 國外에 奔騰하는 一切의 時事에 대한 極明瞭한 判斷, 周到한 注意를 가지게 하며
2. 本社의 社規에 依한 社友의 制度를 實現하야써 志士와 志士가 相求相應하는 그 間에서 우리의 標榜한 更生運動의 完成을 期하고저 하는도다.
우리는 이제 두어 마듸의 말로써 簡單하게나마 爲先 우리의 微意와 小計를 表白하엿다. 무슨 다른 뜻이 잇스리요. 엇더케하면 좀더 만흔 동무와의 聯結미테서 最 唐突한 個人이나 또는 群團을 지여 볼가함 뿐이라.
兄弟여 우리의 今日 計劃 또는 實施하는 바로써 萬에 一分일지라도 眞實로 純摯치 못함이 잇는 무엇이던 저를 發見하거던 發見하는 卽時로 우리의 舌을 斷하고 筆을 折하라. 그러나 우리의 하는 이 일에 眞實로의 依據가 잇고 感激이 流露함을 認하거던 認하는 그 때로 곳 우리와 손을 붓잡고 聲氣를 通하라. 彼가 아니면 此! 躊躇할 것은 업스리라.＜3＞
