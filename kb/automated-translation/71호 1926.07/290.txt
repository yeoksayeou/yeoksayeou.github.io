謹白
Humbly Report

본지 창간 6주년 기념축하광고는 본사는 물론 각 地支, 分社까지 과반 모사건으로 하야 광고모집에 착수한 시일이 너무 短急한 사정상 다시 모집기를 今 7월 15일까지 연장하야 來 8월호의 면목을 일층 번영케 하려 하오니 독자 諸位는 輪亮하십시요.
Regarding the commemorative celebratory advertisements for this magazine's 6th anniversary, due to circumstances where the time available to start collecting advertisements was too short because of a certain incident (모사건) affecting more than half of not only our main office but also each regional (地支) and branch office (分社), we intend to extend the collection period again until this July 15th to make the appearance of the upcoming August issue even more prosperous. We ask all readers (독자 諸位) to please understand (輪亮).

開闢社廣告部＜66＞
Gaebyeoksa Advertising Department <66>

**Summary**

This is an announcement from the Gaebyeoksa Advertising Department informing readers that the deadline for submitting advertisements celebrating the magazine's 6th anniversary has been extended to July 15th. The reason cited is a delay caused by "a certain incident" (모사건) that affected the main office and a majority of its branches, leaving insufficient time for ad collection. The extension aims to ensure the August issue is sufficiently filled with celebratory ads. The mention of an unspecified "incident" impacting operations across multiple offices is historically interesting, hinting at possible external pressures, political interference, or significant internal disruption common during the Japanese colonial period, which could affect publishing activities. The formal language and specific reference to enhancing the magazine's "appearance" (면목) also reflect the business concerns of the time.
