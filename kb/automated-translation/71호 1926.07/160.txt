Regarding the Songdo High Common School Strike
						赤烽 (Chŏkpong/Red Beacon)

At Songdo High Common School (Songdo Kobo), when the American principal, Mr. Wi Imse (魏任世), took office, he made various further efforts to train Christian workers, but the general student body avoided religion. Consequently, when prayer meetings were held during the first period each day, many students would only attend classes starting from the next period. Therefore, the prayer meeting time was not only changed to the second period each day, but various punishments were also imposed on students who did not enter the prayer meeting! There had been many hidden clashes between the principal and the students. Whenever this happened, Mr. Wi would repeatedly give admonitions to the effect that this school (Songdo High) is run by the Christian church, so students who dislike believing in Christianity are not welcome. Whether Mr. Wi thought that Korean students were ungrateful whenever this occurred is unknown, but rumors of school closure<114> had even spread.

Students do not enter religious schools in order to believe in Jesus or to become Christian workers. In Korea, where educational institutions are severely inadequate and insufficient, students had no choice but to pretend to believe in Christianity, even if they disliked it, in order to receive an education. Look! Even though the school employed a dedicated student pastor and diligently instilled Christianity for 4 or 5 years, how many graduates actually said they would work for Christianity after graduation? Even if there were some, it is seen as merely pretending to believe in Jesus as before, in order to find employment in Korea where the fervor for getting a job is extreme. In other words, isn't the teaching meant to produce Christian workers actually resulting in an anti-Christian movement? Korean students, who are implicitly experiencing the principle that social life governs the spirit, not the other way around, will not be able to simply believe and follow the teachings of Christianity which force them to endure the bitterness of all these realities and be satisfied—that is, to avoid reality, dream of illusory happiness, and be utterly obedient (唯唯服從) to the ruling class. Is this all?

Since they (the students) have come to understand that the economy is the foundational work of human society and that the superstructure is determined by changes in the foundational work! They are seen as too clever (怜悧) to pursue this passive! illusory happiness.

They (the students) know the Korean masses crying out for "bread." And they know the struggle centered around "bread." They know that the superstitious sophistry (迷信巧說) of going to heaven in a dim afterlife is a temptation to annihilate the spirit of struggle and demand docile obedience (順順服從) to the ruling class. At the same time, they have come to thoroughly know the causes that produce the current irrational social life and realize they must fight powerfully for its resolution. Korean students, who have many dissatisfactions with social life, have resolved to fight firmly to find happiness in reality. Given that Korean students are growing up in this kind of social environment, isn't it natural to see avoiding religion, and taking a step further, denying religion, as a logical movement?

Despite this, the school authorities have repeatedly forced faith. This is seen as the starting point of the recent Songdo High strike incident (松高盟休事件). Although the students have many demands, the main condition among them is the rejection of the stubborn Principal Wi, who forces faith. Therefore, the origin (發論) of the recent Songdo High strike incident can be seen as the first shot (第一矢) in the fight between the school authorities who compel religion and the student group that refuses the coercion of faith.
(June 19, 1926) <115>

**Summary**

This editorial discusses the student strike (盟休, maenghyu) at Songdo High Common School in 1926. The author, "Chŏkpong," argues the strike's root cause was the American principal Wi Imse's forceful imposition of Christian practices, like mandatory prayer meetings, on students who largely sought secular education due to the lack of alternatives in colonial Korea. The author contends that students, increasingly aware of socio-economic realities ("bread") and Marxist ideas (base determining superstructure), rejected the "illusory happiness" and passive obedience promoted by Christianity, viewing it as a tool of the ruling class. The strike is framed as a justifiable act of resistance against forced faith and a reflection of Korean students' growing consciousness of social injustice and their desire for tangible, real-world change. For historians, this article offers insight into student activism in colonial Korea, the friction caused by missionary-run schools attempting religious indoctrination, the penetration of socialist/materialist thought among students, and the framing of religious resistance as intertwined with anti-colonial sentiment and class consciousness. The explicit linking of resistance to religious coercion with a materialist critique of society and the ruling class is particularly noteworthy.

松都高普校盟休에 際하야
						赤烽
松都高普校에서는 교장 미국인 魏任世가 취임하자 예수교 일군 양성하기에 여러 가지로 더욱 노력을 하엿스나 일반학생은 종교를 기피하엿다. 그리하야 매일 첫 시간에 기도회를 開하면 다음 공부시간부터 출석하는 학생이 만음으로 기도회 시간을 매일 둘재시간으로 변경하엿슬 뿐 안이라 기도회 시간에 드러가지 안는 학생에게는 여러 가지 처벌하는 등! 교장과 학생간에 여러번 암암리에 충돌이 잇섯든 것이다. 이리할 때마다 魏씨는 말하되 본교(松高)는 예수교에서 경영하는 학교이니 예수교를 밋기 실흔 학생은 환영치 아는다는 의미의 훈시가 여러번 잇섯스며 魏씨 자신이 이러할 때마다 조선 학생들은 배은한다고 생각하엿는지는 모르나 폐교의＜114＞ 風說까지 전파된 바가 잇섯다.
학생들이 예수를 밋기 위하야 또는 예수교 일군이 되기 위하야 종교학교에 입학하는 것이 안이다. 교육기관의 不備不足이 심한 조선에서는 교육을 밧기 위하야서는 밋기 실은 예수교라도 할 수 업시 밋는 쳑하고 공부를 하지 안을 수 업섯든 것이다. 보라! 학교에서 學生專担牧師를 두고 4, 5년간이나 열심으로 예수교를 주입하얏스되 졸업 후에 과연 幾人이나 예수교를 위하야 일하겟다고 하든가. 설혹 잇다 하면 그것은 취직열이 酷甚한 조선에서 취직하기 위하야 그저 前 모양으로 예수를 밋는 쳑하는데 불과한 것이라고 본다. 밧구워 말하면 예수교 일군이 되라고 가르친 것이 도로혀 반예수교운동을 하게 되는 현상이 안인가? 정신이 사회생활을 지배하는 것이 안이라 사회생활이 정신을 지배한다는 원리를 은연중 체험하고 잇는 조선학생은 이 모든 현실의 쓰라림을 참고 견대며 스스로 만족하라고 강요하는ㅡ즉 현실을 회피하고 환상적 행복을 꿈구며 지배계급에 唯唯服從하라는 예수교의 가르침을 그대로 신앙하며 행할 수는 업슬 것이다. 엇지 이 뿐이랴?
그들(학생)은 경제가 인간사회의 기초공사이며 상부건축은 기초공사의 변혁에 따라서 좌우되는 줄을 알게 된 이상! 이 소극적! 환상적 행복을 추구하기에는 너무나 怜悧한 것이라고 본다.
그들(학생)은 「빵」을 부르짓는 조선 대중을 안다. 그리고 「빵을 중심으로 싸우는 것을 안다. 희미한 내세에 천당 간다는 迷信巧說은 투쟁 기세를 말살식히여 지배계급에 順順服從하라는 유혹인 것을 아는 동시에 현재와 가튼 불합리의 사회생활을 나아 놋는 원인을 삿삿히 알고 또 그의 해결을 위하야 힘잇게 싸워야 할 것을 알게 되엿다. 사회생활에 불만족이 만흔 조선 학생들은 현실의 행복을 찻기 위하야 굿세게 싸와야 할 것을 각오한 것이다. 이러한 사회의 주위 환경을 가지고 자라나는 조선 학생들이라 종교를 기피하며 한거름 더 나아가서 종교를 부인하는 것이 당연한 운동이라고 볼 수 잇지 안을가?
이러함에도 불구하고 학교 당국자는 신앙을 강제하기를 수업시 하엿다. 이것이 今般에 니러난 松高盟休事件의 발단이라고 본다. 학생들의 요구 조건이 만으나 그 중 제일 주되는 조건이 신앙을 강요하는 완고한 魏교장을 배척한다는 것이다. 그럼으로 今般 松高盟休事件의 發論이 종교를 강제하는 학교 당국자와 신앙의 강요를 거부하는 學生群과의 싸움의 第一矢로 볼 수 잇는 것이다.
(1926. 6월 19일)＜115＞
