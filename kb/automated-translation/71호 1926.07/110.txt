Strangely

Whether an adult or a child, the strange magazine that you cannot put down once you see it is 『Ŏrini』.
And while being interesting, the 『Ŏrini』 magazine imparts fresh and beneficial knowledge to anyone.
Even if you are an adult, just take a brief look. You will be surprised that what catches your eye immediately becomes knowledge, while being endlessly interesting.
Among them, this July issue has become even more interesting, giving away world country photos and the 『Ŏrini Sinmun』 (Children's Newspaper) for free, and also giving a pair of shoes as a prize.
The price is only 15 *chŏn*. Try ordering it today, even just as a trial, and read it. You will understand why 『Ŏrini』 sells the most in Chosŏn. <89>

**Summary**

This text is an advertisement for the children's magazine 『Ŏrini』 (Children) published in July 1926. It promotes the magazine as captivating and educational for both children and adults, highlighting its engaging content and knowledge-building potential. The ad emphasizes a special offer for the July issue, including free supplements (world photos, a children's newspaper) and a prize draw for shoes, all for the low price of 15 *chŏn*. It concludes by asserting that reading the magazine will reveal why it is the best-selling publication in Korea (Chosŏn). For historians, this advertisement offers insights into early Korean magazine publishing and marketing strategies during the colonial period, particularly targeting children and families. It reflects contemporary views on children's education, the use of promotional incentives, pricing structures, and the claim of market dominance within colonial Korea.

이상스럽게
어른이던지 어린이던지 한번만 보면 다시 놋치 못하게 자미잇는 이상한 잡지가 『어린이』임니다.
그리고 지미잇는 중에 아모에게라도 산뜻하고 유익한 지식을 너어 주는 것이 『어린이』 잡지임니다.
어른이라도 한번만 잠간 드려다 보서요. 눈에 뜨이는 것이 곳 지식이 되면서 한이업시 자미잇는데 놀라실 것임니다.
이번 7월호는 그 중에도 더 자미잇게 된 중에 세계각국 사진과 『어린이신문』을 그저 껴주고 구두 한켜레 상품도 줌니다.
갑은 단 15전. 시험삼아라도 오늘 곳 주문하야 닑어 보십시오. 엇지하야 『어린이』가 조선서 제일 만히 팔리는 이유를 아실 것임니다. ＜89＞
