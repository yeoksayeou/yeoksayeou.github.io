The Kaebyŏk Company Expansion Preparation Committee Steadily Progressing

To all comrades under heaven. Please rejoice together. The "Kaebyŏk Company Expansion Preparation Committee" (開闢社擴張期成會), aiming to expand the Kaebyŏk Company even further than its current state, is steadily progressing under the endless help of you, our comrades.

Regarding the purpose of the expansion, as it has already been widely explained in various ways, there is no need for repetition today. Just as the Kaebyŏk Company was founded and has been maintained by the heart and blood of like-minded comrades like us, the expansion of the Kaebyŏk Company will also be achieved through the material contributions of tens of thousands of like-minded comrades like us. That is all.

"A good beginning is half the battle," and we have already gained several hundred, nearly a thousand comrades. We rejoice that in the future, there will be tens of thousands, hundreds of thousands of comrades. Please gather your hearts and strength together and become members of our committee. The ways to become a member are as follows.

Details of Business Expansion
1.  Establishment of a printing house
2.  Publication of printed materials
3.  Dispatch of mobile correspondents domestically and overseas
Others

Bylaws
1.  This committee shall be called the Kaebyŏk Company Expansion Preparation Committee (開闢社擴張期成會).
2.  The office shall be located within the Kaebyŏk Company at 88 Kyŏngun-dong, Kyŏngsŏng.
3.  Members shall consist of ordinary members and special members; those donating Gold 3 Won (金 參圓) or more shall be ordinary members, and those donating Gold 10 Won (金 拾圓) or more shall be special members.
4.  Any unspecified matters not covered herein shall be decided by the promoters' meeting.

**Summary**

This article from the July 1926 issue of *Kaebyŏk* magazine announces the ongoing fundraising campaign by the "Kaebyŏk Company Expansion Preparation Committee." It appeals to "comrades" for donations to support the company's growth, outlining plans to establish a printing press, increase publications, and dispatch correspondents. Membership tiers are defined based on donation amounts (3 Won for ordinary, 10 Won for special). The article then lists numerous donors from various regions across Korea, detailing their names, locations, and contribution amounts, demonstrating the broad base of support the initiative had already garnered. From a historical perspective, this piece highlights the significant role of cultural and publishing enterprises like Kaebyŏk Company in fostering national consciousness and networks during the Japanese colonial period. The extensive list of donors, spanning many towns and cities, offers valuable insight into the geographical reach and social strata of support for such nationalist-leaning cultural activities, illustrating a collective effort funded by Koreans themselves.

着着 進行되는 開闢社擴張期成會
만천하 동지 여러분. 다ㅡ가티 깃버해 주십시오. 개벽사를 현재보다도 더 한층 확장하자는 「개벽사확장기성회」는 여러분 동지의 끗업는 도음 아레서 착착히 진행 중에 잇슴니다.
확장의 취지에 대해서는 이미 각 방면으로 널리 알켜들인 今日에 重言을 할 것은 업슴니다. 개벽사를 우리의 뜻가튼 동무의 심혈로써 창설하고 또 유지해옴과 가티 개벽사 확장에도 또한 우리의 뜻가튼 수만흔 동지의 물자로써 확장하자는 그것 뿐임니다.
「시작이 반」이라고 발서 수백명 근 천명의 동지를 어덧슴니다. 장차로 수만 수십만의 동지가 게실 것을 깃버함니다. 다ㅡ가티 마음과 힘을 모도아 우리 會의 회원이 되어 주십시오. 회원되시는 방식은 아레와 갓슴니다.
