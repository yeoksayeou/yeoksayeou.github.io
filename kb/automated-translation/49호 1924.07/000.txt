△Urgent Company Notice△

Our company (弊社), which has hitherto striven diligently with a reader-first principle (讀者本位), has frequently and irregularly published enlarged issues (倍大號, 增大號) or temporary/special issues (臨時號, 特別號) whenever we managed to capture something, whether spatially or temporally. As a result, errors in the magazine fees (誌代) and postage (送料) sent daily by readers in various regions (各地方讀者) have become extremely numerous (夥甚), causing mutual confusion (互相間의 煩惑) layer upon layer. Truly, recognizing this as a tremendous obstacle (莫大한 障碍) to the improvement of the company's fortunes (社運向上), and simultaneously feeling the urgent necessity (至急히 必要) to rectify this, we are hereby decisively implementing (斷行) the revision of the magazine's fixed price (雜誌定價의 改正), a long-pending issue (多年懸案), as follows. Accordingly, we attach points of caution regarding remittances (送金에 對한 注意件). We sincerely hope (懇望) that our esteemed readers (愛讀者 諸賢) will pay special attention (特別用意) to this notice henceforth.

(Kaebyŏk Fixed Price Revision Table)

**Summary**

This urgent notice from the Kaebyŏk magazine company addresses confusion among readers regarding subscription fees and postage. Due to the frequent publication of various special and enlarged issues, readers were making errors when sending money. The company states this confusion hinders its operations and announces a revision to the magazine's fixed price, urging readers to pay careful attention to the new pricing and remittance instructions. For historians, this article highlights the operational challenges faced by a prominent Korean nationalist magazine during the colonial period, particularly the logistical difficulties of irregular publishing schedules and managing finances with a geographically dispersed readership under constrained circumstances. It reflects both the magazine's ambition (frequent special issues) and the practical hurdles encountered.
