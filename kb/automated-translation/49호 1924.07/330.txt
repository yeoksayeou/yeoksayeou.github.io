Concluding Remarks

Suddenly, it is July. The scorching season of intense heat (熱炎期) has arrived again. Along with the heat, mosquitoes, bedbugs, and fleas will cause us to frown for quite some time. The interest in reading books will likely lessen. One cannot help but struggle with tediousness, anguish, and anxiety. Therefore, as a way to pass the time (逍遺策) during summer, we have edited this 4th-anniversary commemorative issue with a focus on popular taste and amusement.

Regarding the literary contest for the 4th-anniversary issue, although the number of submissions unexpectedly exceeded expectations, the judging results were quite disappointing, with only one second-place winner, one third-place winner in fiction, and one third-place winner in drama being selected. We feel particularly sorry and apologetic to all the participants who put effort into their submissions. Please continue to exert much effort in the future.

We intended to publish the appendix for the 4th-anniversary issue as a separate volume if possible, but due to various circumstances at our company, it has been attached to the end of this issue. Although there could have been various other materials for the appendix, we have first published a glossary of new terms (新術語辭典) covering 18 words in the field of literature for the reference of those newly engaged in literature. Next, we will introduce terminology related to thought and philosophy.

Furthermore, we extend our gratitude to Mr. Yŏm Sangsŏp (廉尙燮) and Mr. Kim Unjŏng (金雲汀) who, despite their busy schedules, diligently judged nearly 70 submissions for the literary contest, and to Mr. Pak Yŏnghŭi (朴英熙) who compiled the nearly 40-page glossary of new terms.

Throughout the entire issue, while there are no particularly dreadful articles, we are confident that there are also no truly meaningless ones. Please accept this commemorative issue as it is. As announced on a separate page, while holding a commemorative celebration, we will immediately begin work on the P'yŏngnam Province issue (平南道號). We especially request much support from the people of P'yŏngnam.

Due to circumstances, the continuation of the Inch'ŏn article (仁川記事) and the Kanghwa article (江華記事) have been postponed to the August issue. We offer our apologies to the people of those areas. <37>

**Summary**

This editor's postscript from the July 1924 issue of *Kaebyŏk* magazine discusses the content of the 4th-anniversary issue, apologizing for its lighter, popular focus necessitated by the summer heat. It expresses regret over the low number of winners in the anniversary literary contest despite many submissions and thanks the judges (Yŏm Sangsŏp, Kim Unjŏng) and the compiler of the new literary terms glossary appendix (Pak Yŏnghŭi). The editors announce plans for the upcoming P'yŏngnam Province special issue and apologize for postponing articles on Inch'ŏn and Kanghwa. Historically, the text reveals the challenges of publishing (adjusting content for seasons, managing contests, appendices), the active literary scene (contests, glossary creation), the involvement of prominent literary figures, and the magazine's regional focus strategy (province-specific issues). The language itself, with its older spellings and grammar, is a valuable snapshot of Korean linguistic evolution in the 1920s.

끗말슴
언으듯 7월이외다. 홧홧한 熱炎期는 또 닥처 왓슴니다. 더위도 더위러니와 모긔 빈대 벼룩 그것들 담으네 또 한참 얼굴을 찡글게 되얏슴니다. 책 볼 흥미도 덜 할 것이외다. 支離와 번민과 안이 싸울 수 업슴니다. 그래서 우리는 여름의 한 逍遺策으로 이번 4주년 기념호는 통속적으로 취미를 중심하야 편집하얏슴니다.
4주년 기념호에 대한 현상문예는 예상외에 응모 點數는 만어스나 考選한 결과―당선된 點數가 소설 2등 1, 3등 1과 각본 3등 1 밧게 안이된 것에 대하야는 크게 섭섭함니다. 특히 얘써 써주신 여러분 응모 諸氏에게 극키 미안함니다. 압흐로 만히 힘써 주십시요.
4주년 기념호 부록은 될 수 잇는 대로 別刊을 하려 하얏더니 본사의 여러 가지 사정으로 本號의 末에 첨부케 되얏슴니다. 그리고 다른 여러 가지 부록거리도 잇겟지만 새로 문학에 종사하는 여러분의 참고를 위하야 新術語辭典 文學部 18語를 爲先 發布하얏슴니다. 다음은 사상 及 철학 방면에 대한 術語를 소개해 드리겟슴니다.
그리고 多忙하심을 불구하고 70여 편에 近한 懸賞文考選에 노력해 주신 廉尙燮
金雲汀 두 분과 40頁에 近한 新術語辭典을 編述해 주신 朴英熙씨의게 감사를 드림니다.
全篇을 통하야 별로 끔즉한 기사는 업스나 또 그닥 무의미의 것도 업다 자신하오니 이번 기념호는 이러케 마저 주시고 別頁에 예고함과 가티 기념 잔채를 츠리면서 곳 平南道號에 착수하겟슴니다. 특히 平南人士의게 만흔 후원을 비러 둠니다.
仁川記事의 續과 江華記事는 사정에 의하야 8월호로 넘기게 되얏슴니다. 當地 人士의게 미안을 엿줍슴니다. ＜37＞
