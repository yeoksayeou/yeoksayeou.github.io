Three Poems -
						Yi Sanghwa

Everyone I see, yesterday or today, seems suffocated.
Without even the joy of meeting after a long time
A forced smile builds its home on faces like melon flowers.
Without even the feeling of a blizzard-raging winter
Sweat streams from fists like fern shoots.
Shall I pierce a small window in that sky? I am suffocating.
-Korean Sickness (朝鮮病)-


The water seller came into my ears and opened my eyes.
Look! <118>
A magpie cries on a bare tree branch.
Why is this?
Does the frost also shed tears along the edge of the eaves?
I am just glad. It will be warm today.
-Winter Heart-


It's sorrowful. O City afflicted with amnesia (健忘症)!
Even if you've put aside even the will to live since yesterday
At least the old dream from hundreds of years ago when your body came into being
Think of it one last time before you stop.
O Sŏul! City born of rebellion (叛逆)!
-Invocation of the Soul (招魂)- <119>
<118-119>

**Summary**

This publication presents three poems by Yi Sanghwa from 1925. "Korean Sickness" expresses a deep sense of oppression and suffocation pervading society. "Winter Heart" offers a brief, contrasting moment of simple observation and slight optimism amidst a bleak winter scene. "Invocation of the Soul" is a direct address to the city of Sŏul, lamenting its forgotten past ("amnesia") and urging it to remember its origins, specifically referencing its birth from "rebellion." From a historical perspective, these poems vividly convey the atmosphere of despair and cultural suppression under Japanese colonial rule. "Korean Sickness" reflects the stifling political and social climate, while "Invocation of the Soul," with its call to remember a past possibly linked to resistance or founding, critiques the present state and hints at a yearning for lost identity and sovereignty, making them valuable for understanding the psychological landscape of colonized Korea.

어제나 오늘 보이는 사람마다 숨결이 막힌다.
오래 간만에 맛나는 반가움도 업시
참외 꼿 가튼 얼골에 선웃음이 집을 짓더라.
눈보라 모라치는 겨울 맛도 업시
고사리 가튼 주먹에 진땀물이 구비치더라.
저한울에다 봉창이나 뚜르랴 숨결이 막힌다.
-朝鮮病-


물쟝사가 귀 속으로 드러와 내 눈을 열엇다.
보아라!＜118＞
까치가 뼈만 남온 나무 가지에서 울음을 운다.
웨 이래?
서리가 덩따라 추녀 끄트로 눈물을 흘리는가.
내야 반가웁기만 한다. 오늘은 따쓰겟구나.
-겨울 마음-


설업다. 健忘症이 든 都會야!
어제부터 살기조차 다- 두엇대도

마즈막으로 한 번은 생각고나 말어라.
서울아 叛逆이 나흔 都會야.
-招魂-＜119＞
