Remaining Words

Amidst the cruel heat and the tedious rainy season, how is everyone's health? Under a clear sky and a new breeze, we present to you, our readers, Volume 3 of this magazine, hoping to deeply console your minds and spirits. Please accept it with affection.

It seems there is no such thing as satisfaction in this world. We tried infinitely hard to make this issue somewhat satisfactory. However, looking at the finished product, it is still unsatisfactory. We once again declare that dissatisfaction is precisely our life force.

Even amidst the dissatisfaction, we are confident that several items, such as "Our New Era (吾人의 新紀元)," "The New Age and New Figures (新時代와 新人物)," "Faithful Duke Kim Okkyun (忠達公 金玉均)," and the script "The Night Before (隔夜)," are worth looking at from our current standpoint. We hope you will not glance through them cursorily like viewing mountains from a galloping horse (走馬看山).

The portrait of Teacher Kim Okkyun is unexpectedly crude. There is a sense that it is quite insufficient for imagining his character. Thinking about it, this portrait seems to be from the time when his appearance was extremely haggard after returning from Hokkaidō. Perhaps seeing his face of loyal indignation (忠意顔) will evoke more feeling in us than seeing him in his prime.

Where the spirit of this magazine lies is something we believe our readers, who have not turned away from this magazine up to Volume 3, already clearly understand without us having to state it explicitly. We merely intend to do our utmost until the very end based on that spirit, and furthermore, we can only ask that you, the readers, prove through future issues of this magazine that these words are not a kind of cliché (一種套語).

However, one thing that worries us is that the authorities (当局者) are always prone to much confusion. Although we strive to do our best from our perspective, there will always be imperfections and shortcomings. Regarding this point, we ask that those of you who love this magazine frankly point out things as you feel them and also contribute articles.

As I finish drafting this text on August 17th, the sky is high, the wind is cool, and the surrounding scenery has become exceptionally clear (洒落), with the feel (氣味) of autumn definitely floating about. Ah, autumn is coming. The autumn of harvest is coming again to our mountains and rivers (山河)... <154>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript reflects on the publication of the third issue of *Kaebyŏk* magazine amidst challenging weather. The editors express humility about the issue's quality, stating that dissatisfaction fuels their drive. They highlight key articles, notably one on Kim Okkyun, justifying the inclusion of a perhaps unflattering portrait. The piece reaffirms the magazine's guiding spirit and appeals to readers for continued support, understanding, and critical feedback, subtly acknowledging the constraints imposed by "the authorities." Historically, this text reveals the challenges faced by Korean intellectuals publishing under Japanese colonial rule – the need for careful expression, the appeal to national sentiment (mentioning Kim Okkyun, a controversial reformist, and the "mountains and rivers"), and the reliance on reader support to navigate censorship and maintain publication. The mention of Kim Okkyun is particularly interesting as evaluating historical figures was a way to comment on contemporary issues indirectly.

남은 말
酷毒한 더위 支離한 장마에 여러분의 健康이 엇더하심닛가 말숙한 하날 새로운 바람아레에서 저의들은 本誌 3號로써 여러분 압헤 드리오며 여러분의 心神을 즉으기 慰勞코저 하오니 情으로 밧아주소서.
이 世上에는 滿足이라는 것은 업나보이다 이번 號는 좀 滿足하게 하야볼나고 애는 無限히 썻슴니다 그러나 하여놋코 보닛가 亦是 不滿이외다 不滿은 곳 우리의 生命이라고 저의는 가시금 브르지즈나이다.
不滿足한 그 中에서라도 「吾人의 新紀元」「新時代와 新人物」「忠達公 金玉均」 脚本「隔夜」 等 멧가지 問題는 現下 우리의 立場으로서는 한번 볼 必要가 잇다 自信하오니 走馬看山에 돌녀 보내지마옵기 바라나이다.
金玉均 先生의 肖像은 意外에 엉성함니다 先生의 그 人格을 想像하기에 만히 不足한 感이 잇나이다 생각컨대 이 肖像은 先生의 北海道로부터 도라오와 形容의 憔悴가 正히 甚할 그때의 것인 듯 함니다 先生의 得意時를 봄보다도 그의 忠意顔을 봄이 우리에게는 느끼이 反多할 듯.
本誌의 精神이 那邊에 在한가 함은 저의가 구타여 말치 안이하야도 임히 本誌 3號까지 물니치지 안이하신 讀者여러분께서 발키 諒察하실 줄 밋나이다 저의는 다못 그 精神에 據하야 最後까지 最善을 盡코저 하오며 아울너 이 말이 一種套語가 안임은 今後의 本誌에 依하야 여러분께서 證明하야 줍소서 할뿐이외다.
그런대 한가지 걱정되옵는 곳은 當局者에게는 언제던지 迷가 만사온바 저의딴으로는 힘을 다한다 하오나 不美와 不足이 언제던지 잇슬것이오니 이 點에 對하야는 本誌를 사랑하시는 여러분께서 느끼는대로 醇醇히 가라켜 주시며 또 投稿하여 주서야 하겟나이다.
이 글의 草를 맛치는 8月 17日 하날이 놉고 바람이 서놀거리며 四圍風色이 別로 洒落하야 가을 氣味가 確實히 浮動하나이다 아아 가을이 오나이다 거둠에 가을이 우리 山河에 또 오나이다… ＜154＞
