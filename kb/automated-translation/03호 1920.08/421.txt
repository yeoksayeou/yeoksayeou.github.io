▲Company Notice▲

＜154＞

**Summary**

This brief notice announces that the magazine *Kaebyŏk* is establishing local distribution points (分賣所 - punmaeso) in various regions outside the central publication area to better serve its readers. It states that the locations listed below have been designated according to the company's internal rules. For historians, this notice highlights the expansion efforts and growing reach of Korean-language publications like *Kaebyŏk* in 1920, despite colonial rule. It points to the development of distribution networks necessary for disseminating cultural and potentially nationalist content to a wider audience across the peninsula.
