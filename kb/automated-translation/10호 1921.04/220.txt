Closing Words

Regarding the March issue of this magazine, there were not a few regrettable matters due to an unexpected event, causing inconvenience to our dear readers. However, as it was something beyond the thoughts of the editorial staff, we hope for your deep forgiveness.

For this April issue, we put in much effort seeking something meaningful, but it is regrettable that it did not turn out quite as we wished. Nevertheless, we think it is a most welcome development to be able to feature new writings from the gentlemen who graduated from various vocational schools this spring. We hope you will read with interest what new voices these individuals, who diligently studied through several years of hardship at the firefly window and snow desk, intend to offer our society in the future.

As announced in the March issue, the play, which is a special feature of this magazine's literary section alongside the novels, was intended to begin serializing the world-renowned masterpiece "Hamlet" starting this month, but due to space constraints, we could not proceed as planned. This is another regrettable matter, but we will definitely continue with serialization starting next month.

Specifically, the editorial staff respectfully addresses the 27 representatives of the 314 members of the Hongsŏng Youth Association. We received with heartfelt joy the manuscript you sent, titled "After Reading the Magazine Sŏgwang, To Mr. Hwang Sŏk-u." We intended to publish it in this issue, but due to editorial circumstances, we could not proceed as planned. However, as there will be a next month's issue, we ask that you not think too impatiently. Our company staff will definitely not betray your goodwill.

Many articles that would have added distinction to this month's literary section could not be published, either due to space constraints or time constraints. The person responsible considers this most regrettable, but as it was also not our true intention, we sincerely hope for your complete forgiveness.

Especially among them,
Mr. Kim Wi-bang's "Pioneer Life"
Mr. Yi Ik-sang's "Our Literary Scene Lacking Artistic Conscience"
—these three beautiful writings will brilliantly adorn our literary section in the May issue, and we inform our readers of this in advance. <147>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the April 1921 issue of *Kaebyŏk* apologizes to readers for unspecified issues with the March issue and expresses mild disappointment that the current issue didn't fully meet their ambitions. It highlights the inclusion of works by recent vocational school graduates and promises the serialization of Hamlet, delayed due to space. The editors also apologize directly to the Hongsŏng Youth Association for postponing their submission and announce two specific articles planned for the May issue. For historians, the text reveals the practical challenges faced by Korean publishers (space constraints, potential external pressures hinted at by "unexpected event"), the magazine's effort to promote new voices (graduates), its engagement with specific reader groups (Hongsŏng Youth Association), and its literary ambitions (serializing *Hamlet*, featuring critical essays on the literary scene). The apologies suggest sensitivity to reader expectations and the difficulties of consistent publication.

끗말
本誌 3月號에는 意外의 일로 愛讀者 諸位에게 未安한 일이 적지 아니하엿습니다. 그려나 그것은 編輯同人의 생각밧 일이오니 깁히 容恕하시기를 바라는 바이올시다.
이번 4月號에는 좀 意味만흔 무엇을 求하랴고 애는 만히 써 보앗스나 그처럼 마음과 가티 되지 아니한 것이 遺憾이올시다. 그러나 今春에 卒業한 各 專門學校 卒業生諸君의 새로운 글을 올리게 된 것은 무엇보다도 반가운 일이라고 생각합니다. 幾個星霜을 螢窓雪案에 刻苦勉勵한 그네들의 새소리는 우리社會에 將且 무엇을 주랴고 하는지 興味잇게 보시기를 바라는 바이올시다.
本誌 文藝部의 小說과 한가지로 特色되는 戱曲은 三月號의 豫告한 바와 가티 世界의 名作이라고 이름이 놉흔 『하무테트』를 今月號부터 譯載하랴고 하엿더니 紙面의 關係로 뜻과 가티 되지 못하엿습니다. 이것도 한가지 未安한 일이나 來月號부터는 斷然코 繼續 連載하겟습니다.
特히 編輯同人으로부터 洪城靑年會 314人 代表者 27人 諸位에게 삼가 이 말슴을 들입니다. 보내신 原稿『雜誌曙光을 읽고 黃錫禹君에게』라고 한 글은 滿腔의 깃븜으로써 잘바닷습니다. 그 글을 이번 號에 낼려고 하엿더니 마츰 編輯上 形便에 依하야 뜻과 가티 되지 못하엿습니다. 그러나 來月號가 또 업지 아니하오니 넘우 燥急히 생각하지 마시기를 바랍니다. 本社 同人은 決코 諸氏의 好意를 저버리지 아니하는 바이올시다.
今月號의 文藝部에 異彩를 낼 글들이 或은 紙面의 形便上이나 時日의 形便上으로 揭載되지 못한 글이 만습니다. 그것은 責任者의 가장 遺憾으로 생각하는 바이오나 또한 本意가 아니오니 萬萬容恕하심을 바랍니다.
