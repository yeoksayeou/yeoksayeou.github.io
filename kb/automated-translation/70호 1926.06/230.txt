Article Type: 시 (Poem)

現實과 民衆
						金宙遠

The heart of the century is a ballroom afflicted with fever.
The soul's (령혼) instinctive rebellion (本能的 反逆) has raised the banner (긔ㅅ발) of nerves.
-To make the flower (꼿) of life bloom with fire and blood-
-On the battlefield of new creative destruction (創造的 破壞).

The sharp fangs (니발) of a pack of wolves, starving (굼주린) in the dark night,
Like loving the pig-belly-like (도야지 배가튼) nape of a ruddy-faced 'gentleman (신사)',
-Onto the promenade of docile shame (恥辱), despicably-
-Towards the windpipe that has lost (일허바린) faith (미듬) and truth-
Bestows the baptism of a flood-like 'poison gas (독와사)'.

Reality strolls (산보한다) the North Pole (北極) with a single layer of 'flesh (솨ㄹ)' wind.
The life of freedom and rebellion breathes red breaths beneath (미테서) the grave.
The masses (민중) are the steam whistle (긔적) of reality, singing the march of the volcano (火山).

Let's stop just the foolish oaths (맹서) of words and tears.
Saying there are only one or two bitter tastes in the world...
Contradiction (모순) and discontent (불평) alone are far from finding (찻기엔) again the old days (녯날) of joy (깃붐) and laughter (웃슴).
To drive away today's misfortune of starving to death and dying shedding blood (피타저),
In chests like the inside of a chimney (굴둑속), the 'gunpowder (화약)' of resentment (분푸리) is loaded.
Let body and mind match steps (거름을 마추자) towards the century like lightning fire (번개불가티).
We are the masses (민중) of the eternal sun (太陽).<13>

**Summary**

This poem by Kim Chu-wŏn portrays a bleak and oppressive reality ("fever," "shame," "poison gas") where the suffering masses ("starving wolves") are contrasted with the comfortable ("ruddy-faced 'gentleman'"). It passionately rejects passive suffering and calls for a violent, revolutionary uprising ("instinctive rebellion," "creative destruction," "volcano's march," "gunpowder of resentment") to overcome the current despair and reclaim a future of freedom. From the perspective of Korean colonial history, this poem is a powerful expression of the intense anger, suffering, and radical revolutionary sentiment present among intellectuals and activists in the mid-1920s. Historians might find the raw, violent imagery and the explicit call for destructive action significant in understanding the mood of resistance and the ideological currents opposing Japanese colonial rule during this period.

現實과 民衆
						金宙遠
世紀의 심장은 열병에 걸린 무도장이다.
령혼의 本能的 反逆이 신경의 긔ㅅ발을 세웟다.
-불과 피로 생명의 꼿을 피우려고-
-새로운 創造的 破壞의 싸움터에다.

어둔 밤 굼주린 이리 때의 날카로운 니발이
血色 조흔 「신사」의 도야지 배가튼 목덜미를 사랑하듯
-비열하게도 온순한 恥辱의 산보장으로-
-미듬과 진실을 일허바린 숨통을 향하야-
홍수가튼 「독와사」의 세례를 나린다.

현실은 한겹의 「솨ㄹ」 바람으로 北極을 산보한다.
자유와 반역의 생명은 무덤미테서 붉은 숨을 쉰다.
민중은 火山의 행진곡을 부르는 현실의 긔적이다.

말과 눈물의 어리석은 맹서만은 그만 끈치자.
세상에 쓴맛이라구 하나 둘뿐이라구...
모순과 불평만이 깃붐과 웃슴의 녯날을 다시 찻기엔 사이가 멀다.
굶어죽고 피타저 죽는 오늘의 불행을 물리치기 위하야
굴둑속 가튼 가슴속에선 분푸리의 「화약」을 재운다.
몸과 마음은 번개불가티 世紀를 향하야 거름을 마추자.
우리는 영원한 太陽의 민중이다.＜13＞
