Regarding the Second Printing

As everyone likely still freshly remembers, after our New Year's Issue No. 19 was confiscated, we published a special provisional issue (臨時 特大號) to offer our apologies and respects to readers everywhere. The contents of that provisional issue were greatly welcomed by the public, achieving the great success of selling out within just a few days.

Amidst this, even today, with days still remaining in January, the month this provisional issue corresponds to, orders for this issue continue to pour in. Now, responding to the warm requests of you, our brothers, we are carrying out a second printing of the provisional issue.

This is the second time we have experienced the joy of carrying out a second printing of the current month's issue. We are grateful that the deep affection our fellow brothers bestow upon our magazine grows thicker day by day, and we are especially pleased that this situation has become particularly prominent entering the New Year.

Kaebyŏk Publishing Company <1>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from *Kaebyŏk* magazine announces the second printing of a special provisional issue. The original New Year's issue (No. 19) had been confiscated by authorities, prompting the publication of the special issue as a substitute. This special issue proved highly popular, selling out quickly, and continued demand led to the decision for a reprint. For historians, this article vividly illustrates the censorship faced by Korean publications under Japanese colonial rule (confiscation of Issue 19) and simultaneously highlights the strong public demand and support for such journals despite the risks, indicating a resilient nationalist sentiment or intellectual curiosity among the readership.

再版을 行하면서
本誌 新年號 第19號가 押收되고 臨時 特大號를 發行하야써 삼가 天下 讀者에게 謝禮하온 것은 一般의 記憶이 오히려 새로움 줄 밋삽거니와 그 臨時號의 內容이 크게 世間의 歡迎한 바 되와 몃날을 지나지 못하야 絶版되는 盛績을 어덧나이다.
그러하온 中 이 臨時號의 當月되는 1月의 日字도 오히려 남음 今日에 잇서 本號의 注文은 依然히 踏至하옵는 바 우리는 이제 여러분 兄弟의 따수한 要求에 應하야 臨時號의 再版을 行하나이다.
本誌가 當月號의 再版을 行하는 깃븜을 엇기는 이번으로써 두 번째이외다. 同胞兄弟로부터 本誌에부텨 주는 至情이 날로 두터워 감을 感謝하오며 이 現狀이 新年에 들며 特히 著大한 바 잇슴을 別로 깃버합니다.
開闢社＜1＞
