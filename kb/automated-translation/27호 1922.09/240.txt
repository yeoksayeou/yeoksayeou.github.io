餘墨 (Remaining Ink)

Who knows how the world intends to end things, but chaotic events continue to pile up one after another as time goes by.

Look, the London Conference (倫敦會議) has broken down, and it seems there are signs of division in the friendly ties between Britain and France (英佛). Regarding Germany's (獨逸) reparations, despite the amount already collected reaching 8.1 billion yen (81億萬圓), the Allied Powers (聯合國) have refused even the defeated nation's final request for a payment deferral (支拂延期). Germany's internal situation is becoming more chaotic by the day, the Mark falls daily, and dark clouds (暗雲) hang low over the lands of Europe (歐洲).

Ireland (愛蘭), after a fierce struggle (惡戰苦鬪), seemed to have settled the chaotic situation for a time with the establishment of the Irish Free State (愛蘭自由國). However, conflicts arose between the Free State faction and the Republic (共和國) faction, leading to bloodshed among compatriots. Recently, the President of the Free State, Mr. Griffith (끄리푸이쓰氏), died of illness, and his successor, the giant of Ireland, Mr. Collins (코린쓰氏), was shot dead by irregulars of the Republican army. The future of Ireland, having lost both heroes (兩雄), seems likely to fall into further turmoil (紛紏).

Turning back to the recent situation in our Korea (朝鮮), the Niigata Prefecture (新瀉縣) massacre issue and the clash between police officers and T'aeŭlgyo (太乙敎徒) followers in Kohŭng (高興) greatly agitated the public mind. Amidst this, unprecedented flood damage (水害) has occurred repeatedly, further increasing the level of anxiety (不安).

The talk since last year, "Money is scarce, money is scarce," reached its peak (其極) entering this summer. While the general economic downturn (沈衰) was severe, recently a new trend (新機風) of lowering prices and conserving consumption crossing over from Japan (日本) seems to have become somewhat of a topic, but to what extent it will achieve actual results (實效) is a major question (疑問中 疑問).

Meanwhile, the unprecedented heatwave (酷暑), which was a precursor (前兆) to the recent heavy rains (大降雨), has directly and indirectly struck our elbow (肘 lit. elbow, fig. affected us) in many ways. Therefore, the substance (本質) of this magazine will be just this much for now. From now on, as the cool autumn air arrives (新凉이 到하는), we intend to forge a new path (新局面을 開拓) and redouble our efforts (一段의 努力을 重하려) for the magazine starting from the October issue (10月號).

One last thing to mention is that the Kyeryongsan Travelogue (鷄寵山遊記), a kingdom of strange tales (異說), has been postponed to next month's issue (來月號) due to space constraints (紙面의 關係上). We sincerely express our gratitude (謝意) to the contributor. <71>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from September 1922 reflects on the tumultuous state of global and local affairs. It mentions the breakdown of the London Conference, Germany's reparations crisis, the internal conflict in newly formed Ireland following the deaths of Griffith and Collins, and recent upsetting events in Korea including a massacre in Niigata, a police clash with T'aeŭlgyo followers in Kohŭng, and severe flood damage. The piece also notes the severe economic downturn, skepticism about Japanese-led price controls, and concludes by apologizing for delaying a travelogue and promising renewed efforts for the magazine in the cooler autumn weather. For historians, the article provides a contemporary Korean intellectual perspective on international events (post-WWI Europe, Irish Civil War) alongside domestic colonial issues (violence involving Koreans, economic hardship, natural disasters, Japanese economic influence). The mention of the Niigata massacre and the T'aeŭlgyo incident offers specific data points on colonial tensions and social issues.

餘墨
世上이 어떠케 끗을 맷고 말랴는 셈인지, 어즐어운 일은 갈수록에 層生疊出이외다.
보시오, 倫敦會議는 破裂되어 英佛간의 聯合의 誼도 分裂의 兆가 잇는듯하며 獨逸에 對한 賠償額은 旣히 徵收한 額이 81億萬圓에 達하엿슴에 不拘하고 聯合國의 側은 戰敗國의 最後 要請인 支拂延期까지를 不許하야 獨逸의 內情은 日紛하고 馬克은 日落하야 歐洲의 天地에는 暗雲이 低迷하며
惡戰苦鬪의 愛蘭은 先者愛蘭自由國의 成立을 得하야 一時의 亂局을 收拾하는 듯하더니 自由國便과 共和國便의 意思가 相衝햐야 同胞間의 流血을 見하던 中 近者에는 自由國의 大統領 끄리푸이쓰氏가 病死하고 其後를 繼한 愛蘭의 巨人 코린쓰氏가 共和軍의 不正規軍에게 射殺되어 兩雄을 失한 愛蘭의 前途는 一層 紛紏中에 陷할듯하며
도리켜 우리 朝鮮의 近狀을 보면 新瀉縣의 虐殺問題와 高興의 警察官 對 太乙敎徒 衝突事件은 一般人의 心思를 興奮케 함이 甚하던 中 例에 업는 水害가 다시금 疊出하야 不安의 度를 益加하엿스며
昨年以來로 「돈이 귀해셔 돈이 귀해셔」하던 소리는  今夏에 들며 其極에 達하야 一般 經濟界의 沈衰가 甚하던 中 近者에 日本으로서 건너 오는 物價引下, 消費節約의 新機風이 動하야 多少의 問題거리가 되는 듯하나 어느 程度까지의 實效를 收할가 함은 疑問中 疑問이라.
그런 中 그間 大降雨의 前兆이던 無前의 酷暑는 直接 間接으로 우리의 肘를 擊함이 多하야 本誌의 本質도 이번에는 爲先 이만큼하고 新凉이 *到하는 今後부터 新局面을 開拓하야 10月號 부터의 本誌에는 特히 一段의 努力을 重하려 한다.
끗으로 한 말할 것은 異說의 王國인 鷄寵山遊記와 紙面의 關係上 來月號로 밀게 된 그것이다. 삼가 寄稿者에게 謝意를 表한다.＜71＞
