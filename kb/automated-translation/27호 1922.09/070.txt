Special Notice

Regarding the articles in this September issue, within "Random Jottings from Toseonam Hermitage, Sixteen Topics" (道詵菴中의 漫筆十六題) by Saegŏmjŏng (洗劒亭), the last five lines on page 5, page 17, or page 24, and the entire text of "New Basis for the Development of the Korean Nation" (朝鮮民族發展의 新根據) by Pukchok Tonggok (北族東谷), pages 47-57, incurred the displeasure of the authorities (当局) and were confiscated. We are now issuing a temporary replacement issue.

While our company takes responsibility for the delay in publication and the roughness of the content for this issue, if only we have the forgiveness of all our brothers who have long cherished our publication, we—though lacking in strength—will not hesitate to exhaust all that we can do. We will not tire. <47>

**Summary**

This notice from the publisher of *Kaebyŏk* magazine informs readers that parts of the September 1922 issue were censored and confiscated by the Japanese colonial authorities (当局). Specifically, lines from an article by Saegŏmjŏng and the entirety of an article by Pukchok Tonggok titled "New Basis for the Development of the Korean Nation" were removed. The publisher apologizes for the resulting delay and incomplete content but reaffirms their commitment to continue publishing despite the challenges. For historians, this text is a direct primary source documenting Japanese censorship practices in colonial Korea, revealing the specific articles targeted (likely for nationalist content), and showcasing the publishers' constrained yet persistent position under colonial rule.

特告
本九月號의 記事中 洗劒亭의 「道詵菴中의 漫筆十六題」中의 五頁一七頁又는 二四頁의 末五行과 北族東谷의 「朝鮮民族發展의 新根據」의 全文 四七頁-五七頁은 當局의 忌諱에 觸하야 押收를 當하고 이제 다시 臨時號를 發行하나이다.
本號에 對한 發行時日의 遲延과 記事內容의 粗踈는 本社-스스로 其責을 負하옵거니와 幸히 일즉부터 本社를 愛護하시는 兄弟 여러분의 寬恕만 這間에 잇다하면 우리-비록 微力이나 그 盡할 바를 盡하기에 躊躇치 안켓나이다. 斁할줄을 모르겟나이다. ＜47＞
