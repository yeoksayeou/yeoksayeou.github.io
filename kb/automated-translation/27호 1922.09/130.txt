Magazine Title	개벽 第27호
Publication Date	1922년 09월 01일
Article Title	社會日誌(八月) (Social Diary (August))
Author	一記者 (One Reporter)
Article Type	소식 (News/Events)

社會日誌
						一記者

July 26th
Mr. Yun Yŏng-sŏn (尹永善, son of Mr. Yun Ch'i-ho 尹致昊氏), who graduated from the Agricultural Department of Ohio State University in the United States, and Mr. Yi Ch'un-ho (李春浩氏), who graduated from the same university's Liberal Arts department, both returned to Kyŏngsŏng (京城).

27th
The 9th International Prison Congress convened in Bern (뻬룬), Switzerland (瑞西), scheduled for about one week.

29th
Heavy rain began falling on the Kyŏngbu Line (京釜線) from dawn this day, causing damage to the railway lines in over 20 locations north of Choch'iwŏn (鳥致院). In the Kyŏnggi (京畿) jurisdiction alone, flooded houses reached 7,300, and the water level of the Han River (漢江) rose to over 34 ch'ŏk (尺 - Korean foot).

August 1st
Former Italian Prime Minister Facta (팍타) formed a new coalition cabinet without including the Socialist Party (社會黨).
▲ News of the massacre of Korean laborers (朝鮮人 勞動者) by the Shin'etsu Electric Power Company (信越電力會社) in Niigata Prefecture (新渴縣), Japan (日本) arrived, causing public sentiment (民心) to reach a peak of excitement.
▲ The Shanghai Football Team (上海蹴球團), composed of students studying in China (中國), landed in Pusan (釜山) this morning.
▲ An alumni association (後援會) composed of graduates of the private Sungdŏk School (私立崇德學校) in P'yŏngyang (平壤) was organized, because the school's management had fallen into difficulty (困難).
▲ The budget assessment meeting (豫算査定會議) for the next fiscal year of the Government-General of Korea (朝鮮總督府) concluded.

2nd
▲ British Prime Minister Lloyd George (로이드쪼-지氏) delivered a speech on the Indian political situation (印度政局) in the House of Commons, stating that Britain (英國) would by no means abandon its responsibility for the Indian Empire (印度帝國). This caused a sense of contradiction with the British government's previous guarantees regarding Indian self-government (印度自治), deeply angering various political factions (各政派) in India (印度) and leading to attempts to unite the extremist and moderate factions (極端派와 溫和派).
▲ Yi Pok-nae (李馥來), living in Che-dong (齋洞), Kyŏngsŏng (京城), claimed to have newly invented the Pogyŏng Electric Lamp (普景電燈), but it was actually nothing more than a fraudulent scheme (詐欺手段) to extort money (金錢) from local people. Having defrauded approximately 60,000 Wŏn (六萬圓), he was previously arrested (撿擧) by the police, and his house was searched (家宅搜索) on this day.
▲ Dr. Graham Bell (끄라함,베-ㄹ博士), inventor of the telephone (電話發明家), passed away at the age of 76. Born in Edinburgh (에징바라), Britain (英國) in 1847, he later emigrated to the United States (米國). He studied the principles of sound (音聲의 原理) and in March 1876, at the age of 29, invented and completed the modern telephone (電話). He immediately obtained a patent from the U.S. government (米國政府) (Patent No. 174465) and subsequently founded a telephone company with six million dollars (六百萬弗), thus laying the foundation (端緖) for the worldwide spread of the telephone as we see today.
▲ The Korean Student Federation (朝鮮學生大會) sent a letter of inquiry (質問書) to the principals of seven middle schools in Kyŏngsŏng. Prior to this, the principals of the seven middle schools in Kyŏngsŏng—Posŏng Normal School (普專), Hwimun (徽文), Chung'ang (中央), Posŏng (普成), Chungdong (中東), Kyŏngsin (敬信), and Yangjŏng (養正)—had resolved upon three conditions with the aim of tightening control (團束) over students more thoroughly. Among these, the first article stated, "A student of middle school level who participates in the Student Federation will be ordered to withdraw (退學)." In response, the Student Federation inquired as to the reason (理由) for this.

3rd
▲ The West Sŏn (西鮮 - Western Korea) Boys' Football Tournament (少年蹴球大會), hosted by the Chinnamp'o Wŏlsŏng Club (鎭南浦月城團), held its final match this day. The eventual victors were the Yongsadan (勇獅團 - Brave Lions Club) of P'yŏngyang (平壤).

4th
▲ Japan (日本) ratified all of the Washington Treaties (華府條約). The main points of the conditions are: (1) The Four-Power Treaty concerning the Pacific and supplementary agreements (太平洋에 關한 四國條約並追加協定), (2) The treaty concerning the limitation of naval armaments (海軍軍備制限에 關한 條約), (3) The Five-Power Treaty concerning submarines and poison gas (潛水艦及毒瓦斯에 關한 5國 條約), (4) The Nine-Power Treaty concerning China (支那에 關한 9國 條約), (5) The treaty concerning Chinese customs tariffs (中國關稅에 關한 條約).
▲ President Li (黎總統) of China (中國) sent two official letters to the House of Representatives (衆議院), resigning from his post and describing the painful circumstances (苦衷) of the dissolution of the House of Representatives in June of the 6th year of the Republic (民國 6年 6月), seeking their understanding (諒解).
▲ The All-Russian Communist Party Congress (全露共産黨大會) was held in Moscow (莫斯科). Mr. Lenin (레닌氏), who was ill in bed, dispatched Political Bureau Director Mr. Stalin (스타-린氏) as his temporary representative (臨時代理) to express gratitude for the goodwill (好誼) in holding the conference and conveyed his intention that the day he could grasp political affairs (政務) together with everyone was not far off, indicating his recovery (慚快) from his chronic illness (宿病).
▲ The Yan cabinet (顔內閣) in China resigned, and a new cabinet under Tang Shaoyi (唐紹儀) was organized. (Premier Tang Shaoyi 唐紹儀, Foreign Affairs Gu Weijun 願維鈞, Internal Affairs Tian Wenlie 田文烈, Finance Gao Lingwei 高凌霨, Army Zhang Shaozeng 張紹曹, Navy Li Dingxin 李鼎新, Justice Zhang Yaozeng 張耀會, Education Wang Chonghui 王寵惠, Agriculture and Commerce Lu Xin 盧信, Communications Gao Enhong 高恩洪).

5th
▲ The issue of the massacre of Koreans in Niigata Prefecture (新渴縣의 朝鮮人 虐殺問題) caused public opinion (輿論) to boil fiercely. Amidst this, at 8 PM this day, people from various fields gathered at the Chongno Youth Hall (鍾路靑年會館). They decided to first investigate the truth (眞相) of this incident, selected 10 committee members (委員), entrusted them with all matters of investigation (調査事項), and dispatched Messrs. Na Kyŏng-sŏk (羅景錫) and Kim Sa-guk (金思國) as investigation committee members (調査委員).
▲ In the week from July 31st to August 5th, the number of people who received suspension of transaction orders (取引停止處分) from the Kyŏngsŏng <86> Clearing House (手形交換所) reached a total of 21. This number doubled compared to the previous week, and among them were prominent businessmen (實業家).

6th
▲ The Kyŏngbu Line (京釜線) between Pyŏngjŏm (餠店) and Osan (烏山), which had been cut off due to the heavy rain at the end of last month, was reopened.

7th
▲ The London Conference (倫敦會議), composed of the prime ministers of the five Allied powers (聯合5國 - Britain 英, France 佛, Italy 伊, Belgium 白, Japan 日), opened this day at the British Prime Minister's residence. This conference aimed to resolve the current chaotic financial situation (財政上 混沌狀態) in Europe, but due to the excessively harsh conditions demanded by the French (佛國側) side regarding the deferment of German reparation payments (獨逸賠償金支拂猶豫), the conference eventually broke down (破裂) on the 14th of this month.
▲ Anti-Socialist Party riots (反社會黨의 暴動) erupted in major cities of Italy (伊太利).
▲ The verdict (判決) of the Appeals Court (覆審法院) regarding the four Buddhist youths (佛敎靑年)—Kang Ch'ang-sin (姜昌信), Kim Sang-ho (金尙昊), Chŏng Maeng-il (鄭孟逸), and Yang Mu-hong (梁武弘)—who had denounced Kang Tae-ryŏn (姜大蓮), the abbot (住持) of Suwŏn Yongju Temple (水原 龍珠寺), as a demon (惡魔) of the Korean Buddhist world (朝鮮佛敎界) and attacked him with drums (鳴鼓攻之), was delivered on this day. Kang, Kim, and Chŏng were each sentenced to 6 months of imprisonment (役), and Yang to 4 months, but all four received suspension of execution (執行猶豫).
▲ Various gold and silver merchants (金銀商業人) in Kyŏngsŏng city organized the Gold and Silver Merchants' Craft Guild (金銀商細工組合) to regulate prices (物價) and wages (工賃). However, one of the guild members (組合員) violated this regulation (規定).
▲ The matter of Baron Han Ch'ang-su's (韓昌洙男) disrespectful incident (無嚴事件), which arose in connection with the issue of the Yi Royal Family's personal funds (李王殿下의 親用金間題), had attracted much public attention (注目) due to suspicions of various hidden scandals (黑幕) lurking within. On this day, 76 Confucian scholars (儒林) including Hong U-sun (洪祐淳) submitted a petition (上書) to His Highness King Yi (李王殿下) and a long letter (長書) to the Government-General (總督府), bitterly denouncing (痛論) the disrespect (無嚴) of Han and his clique (韓一味).

8th
▲ 53 political prisoners (政治犯) held in Taegu Prison (大邱監獄) conducted a one-day hunger strike (絶食同盟). The reason was their inability to endure the unreasonable harsh treatment (虐待) at the prison.

9th
The Shanghai Student Football Team (上海學生蹴球團) delegation, consisting of Sin Ki-jun (申基俊), Yu Ki-jun (劉基峻), Chu Yo-han (朱耀翰), and 14 others, who had been touring areas such as South Chosŏn (南鮮 等地), arrived in Kyŏngsŏng (入京). In Japan, *Motoharu Hira* (*本卵平* - transcription uncertain), Nakano Seigō (中野正剛), and about twenty other members of the New Man Faction (新人派) formed a type of political association (政治的 結社) called "Shinsha" (新社 - New Society). Their stated goal is to offer the current Japanese society (日本의 現社會), dominated solely by politicians (政治家), to the entire populace (國民全般), liberate thought (思想을 解放), arrange efficiency (能律을 按排), correct the nation's posture (國家의 姿勢), and open a dynamic path (動的 進路) in international life (國際生活).
▲ The Chinese thinker Chen Duxiu (陳獨秀氏) was arrested (速捕) by French detectives (佛人探偵) in the Shanghai French Concession (上海佛相界) on suspicion of connections (連絡한 嫌疑) with radicals (過激派).
▲ An Anti-Christian Federation (反基督敎聯盟) arose in Fengtian (奉天), China. This was reportedly because the American head (米人靑年會長) of the local Christian YMCA (基督敎...靑年會) conspired (協策) with Zhang Zuolin (張作霖) to deceive (愚弄) the local youth (靑年).

11th
▲ Mr. Joffe (묫폐氏), departing as the plenipotentiary head (全權主班) for the Japanese-Russian Conference (日露會議) representing Soviet Russia (勞農露國) and the Far Eastern Republic (極東共和國), arrived in Beijing (北京), China. He was welcomed (歡迎) by Peking University President Cai Yuanpei (北京大學長 蔡元培氏) and others.
▲ On the 3rd anniversary of the promulgation of the German Republic's Constitution (獨逸共和國 憲法發布 第 3周年 記念日), German President Ebert (獨逸 大統領「에벨트」) issued a proclamation (布告) encouraging the national spirit (國民의 意氣), stating in effect, "The German people (獨逸國民) cannot abandon the hope (希望) of saving not only Germany (獨逸) but the entire world (全世界)."
▲ Mr. Arthur Griffith (아-사끄리푸이스氏), founder of the Irish Sinn Féin party (愛蘭의 씽페인 黨開祖) and President of the Provisional Government of the Irish Free State (愛蘭自由國假政府의 大統領), suddenly passed away. He was one of the leaders of the South Irish people (南愛蘭國民領袖), born in Dublin (데불린市) in 1867. He became a journalist (新聞記者) for many years and, inspired (暗示) by the Hungarian independence movement (凶牙利의 獨立運動), organized the Sinn Féin (씽페인) party. Sinn Féin is generally translated as "Ourselves Alone" (吾等, 自身), but its original spirit (本來의 精神) means "By relying solely on Ireland's own efforts (愛蘭自己의 努力), we must seek our true position (眞個의 地位) as a nation of the civilized world." Although he faced criticism (攻駁) from some Irish people for supporting the earlier Anglo-Irish Treaty (英愛條約), he was undoubtedly a fervent patriot (熱烈한 愛國者) and a courageous administrator (勇敢한 執事者). His passing is undeniably a grave loss (重大한 損失) for Ireland (愛蘭).

12th
▲ While the American coal miners' strike (米國의 坑夫罷業) still showed no glimmer of resolution (瑞光), the four major brotherhoods (四大友愛會) of railway employees (鐵道從業員) initiated a general railway strike (鐵道總罷業) as a sympathy strike (同情罷業). (The Four Great Brotherhoods (四大友愛會) refers to the combination of the Brotherhood of Railroad Trainmen (鐵道乘務員組合), Brotherhood of Locomotive Firemen and Enginemen (機關手 及 火夫組合), Order of Railway Conductors (車掌組合), and Brotherhood of Locomotive Engineers (機關車機械工組合).)
▲ Police Affairs Bureau Director Maruyama (丸山 警務局長) summoned <87> the responsible persons (責任者) of each newspaper company (新聞社) in Kyŏngsŏng and the special correspondents (特派員) of each paper. He warned (警告) them, stating, "Recently, there has been much discussion (言論) regarding radical thought (過激思想) and communism (共産主義). At this year's judicial officers' conference (司法官會議), the policy for controlling (團束) this was discussed. Ultimately, it was decided that judicial power (司法權) would proceed independently (獨立) from administrative power (行政權). Therefore, from now on, in addition to administrative measures (行政處分) against speech (言論), judicial power will also be independently activated (發動)."

14th
▲ Following the death of Irish Free State President Griffith (끄리푸이스氏), Mr. Collins (고련스氏) succeeded him (其任을 襲하다). Lord Northcliffe (노-스크리푸卿), known as the "Newspaper King" (新聞王), passed away. The Lord was born near Dublin (떠블린), Ireland on July 15, 1865. Aspiring to be a journalist (新聞記者) from childhood, he became an assistant to a publisher (出版業人) and independently managed to publish a weekly magazine (週刊雜誌). Later, achieving great success (功) in this field (斯界), he at one time published 60 types of periodicals (定期刊行物), with their circulation (發行部數) reportedly reaching 20 million (2千萬).
▲ The Chŏngnonhoe (正論會 - Righteous Discussion Society) was initiated (發起) in Kyŏngsŏng. Its purpose is to rectify (匡正) erroneous opinions (邪論) in society. Key figures involved are Sŏ Kwang-jŏn (徐光前), Chŏn Il (全一), and several dozen others.

15th
▲ The noon gun (午砲) in Kyŏngsŏng was abolished (廢止).
▲ The Representative Committee (代表委員會) of local parishes (地方 敎區) and direct mission offices (直接傳敎室) of Ch'ŏndogyo (天道敎) convened inside the main church (仝敎堂) (in Kyŏngsŏng).

16th
▲ Gabriele D'Annunzio (따눈쵸氏) of Italy (伊太利) fractured his skull (頭蓋骨을 挫하다) after falling from a high place while strolling in his garden (庭園).
▲ In Kŏhŭng County (高興郡), South Chŏlla Province (全南), over 80 T'aeŭlgyo believers (太乙敎徒) clashed (衝突) with a group of policemen (一團의 警官). The police immediately shot and killed (銃殺) believer Pak Pyŏng-je (朴炳濟) and seriously injured (重傷) a woman with the surname Kim (金姓女). This incident gradually escalated afterward, and investigations (調査) by both the authorities (當國) and private citizens (民間側) are ongoing.

18th
▲ The Allied Reparations Commission (聯合國賠償委員會), composed of commissioners from four countries (四國委員)—France (佛), Britain (英), Belgium (白), Italy (伊)—decided to dispatch two commissioners (委員) to investigate Germany's financial situation (獨逸의 財政狀態).
▲ Enver Pasha (엔벨바샤氏), leader of the Young Turks (土耳其靑年黨領袖), was shot and killed (射殺) while commanding anti-Bolshevik forces (過激派討伐軍 指揮中).
▲ The Japanese government (日本政府) announced 19 measures (物價策 19項目) regarding prices. Conservation of consumption (消費節約), increase in production (生産增加), and price reduction (物價引下) were its main spirit (主精神).

22nd
▲ The continuous rain (霖雨) that started around the 20th continued for days. The Kyŏngŭi Line (京義線路) collapsed in over 60 places. Areas like Sinmak (新幕) and Mulgae Station (勿開驛) were completely flooded (浸水), resulting in many casualties (死傷) among people and livestock (人畜). The Han River's water level (漢江의 增水) also exceeded 30 ch'ŏk, causing extensive flooding (浸水) along the banks (沿岸). The Kyŏngwŏn Line (京元線) was also cut off (不通), creating a flood disaster (水亂) unprecedented in recent times (近來 未曾有).

26th
▲ The withdrawal (撤退) of Japan's Siberian Expeditionary Forces (四伯利波遣軍) began. <88>

**Summary**

This "Social Diary" from September 1922 chronicles events from late July to late August 1922, covering a mix of Korean domestic news and international affairs. Domestic events include the return of US-educated Koreans, severe flooding and infrastructure damage, the formation of school alumni associations, controversies involving alleged fraud and Buddhist temple leadership, the suppression of student activism, economic troubles reflected in bankruptcies, a violent clash between police and members of the T'aeŭlgyo religious group, and government warnings to the press about reporting on radical ideologies. International news covers Italian politics, the Niigata massacre of Korean laborers in Japan and the subsequent investigation, the ratification of the Washington Naval Treaties, political shifts in China, updates on Lenin's health in Russia, the London Conference on German reparations, the death of Irish leader Arthur Griffith and newspaper magnate Lord Northcliffe, US labor strikes, and the beginning of the Japanese withdrawal from Siberia. The article reveals significant social and political tensions within colonial Korea, including nationalist responses to Japanese actions (Niigata), conflicts between traditional/religious groups and authorities (Buddhist youth, T'aeŭlgyo), government efforts to control dissent (student rules, press warnings), and the impact of natural disasters. For historians, the diary offers valuable snapshots of colonial administration concerns (budget, control of thought), Korean social responses (protests, organizations), economic conditions, and the ways international events were reported and perceived in Korea during this period. The juxtaposition of local Korean issues with major global events provides context for understanding Korea's position under colonial rule.

社會日誌
						一記者
7月26日
米國 오하이오州大學 農科를 卒業한 尹永善(尹致昊氏의 令息)氏와 同文科를 卒業한 李春浩氏가 共히 歸京하다.

27日
第 9回의 國際監獄會議가 約 1週間 豫定으로 瑞西의 「뻬룬」에 開催되다.

29日
이날 새벽부터 京釜線에 大雨가 降하야 鳥致院 以北의 線路破損이 20餘 個所에 達하고 京畿管內의 浸水家屋만이 七千三百戶에 達하엿스며 漢江의 增水가 三十四尺餘에 達하다.

8月 1日
伊太利 前 首相「팍타」는 社會黨을 不加하고 新聯立內閣을 組織하다.
▲日本新渴縣信越電力會社로부터 朝鮮人 勞動者를 虐殺한 消息이 傳하야 一般 民心의 興奮이 極에 達하다.
▲中國의 留學生으로 成한 上海蹴球團一行이 이날아츰 釜山에 上陸하다.
▲平壤私立崇德學校의 졸업생으로 成한 同校後援會가 조직되니 그 학교의 經營이 困難에 陷한 故이라.
▲朝鮮總督府의 來年度豫算査定會議가 終了되다.

2일
▲英國首相「로이드쪼-지」氏는 印度政局에 對한 演說을 下院에서 行하되 英國은 決코 印▲度帝國에 對한 責任을 抛棄하는 者가 아니라하야 從來 英政府의 聲明한 印度自治에 대한 保障과 矛盾한 感이 有하매 印度의 各政派는 甚히 憤慨하야 極端派와 溫和派의 連結을 見하려 하기에 至하엿다.
▲京城齋洞에 사는 李馥來의 自稱 新發明의 普景電燈은 其實이 地方人의 金錢을 騙取코져하는 詐欺手段에 不過한 것으로 約 六萬圓의 金額을 詐取한 바 일즉이 警察의 手에 撿擧되야 이날에 家宅搜索을 하다.
▲電話發明家「끄라함,베-ㄹ」博士가 逝去하니 享年 76이였다. 翁은 1847年 英國「에징바라」에 生하야 그後 米國에 移住하엿는대 일즉히 音聲의 原理를 硏究하고 1876年 3月 即 그의 29歲 時에 今日의 電話를 發明完成하야 곳 米國政府의 特許(特許番號는 174465)를 得하고 仍히 六百萬弗의 電話會社를 起하야 遂히 今日과 如한 世界的의 電話普及을 促하는 端緖를 지엇다.
▲朝鮮學生大會로부터 京城의 7中學校長에게 質問書를 送하다. 是에 先하야 京城內의 普專, 徽文, 中央, 普成, 中東, 敬信, 養正의 7中校長은 學生에 對한 團束을 一層 徹底히 하리라는 目的으로써 3個의 條件을 決議한 中 第1條에「中學程度의 學生으로서 學生大會에 參加할 時는 그에게 退學을 命한다하엿는대 이에 對하야 學生大會로부터 그 理由의 那邊에 在한가를 質問한 것이다.

3日
▲鎭南浦月城團의 主催에 係한 西鮮少年蹴球大會는 이날노써 最後의 決勝을 行하얏는대 結局의 勝利를 得한 이는 平壤의 勇獅團이엇다.

▲中國의 黎總統으로부터 衆議院에 2通의 公文을 送하야 其職을 辭하고 民國 6年 6月 衆議院 解散의 苦衷을 述하야 其諒解를 求하다.
▲莫斯科에 全露共産黨大會가 開催되얏는대 病臥 中의 「레닌」氏는 政治局長「스타-린」氏를 臨時代理로 差遣하야 同會議開催의  好誼를 謝하고 諸君과 共히 政務를 掌握할 日이 不遠하다는 旨를 傳하야 宿病慚快의 意味를 表하다.
▲中國의 顔內閣이 辭職되고 唐紹儀의 新內閣이 組織되다. (總理의 唐紹儀, 外交願維鈞, 內務 田文烈, 財政 高凌霨, 陸軍張紹曹, 海軍李鼎新, 司法張耀會, 敎育王寵惠, 農商盧信, 交通 高恩洪)

5日
▲新渴縣의 朝鮮人 虐殺問題는 社會의 輿論을 沸騰케함이 甚하던 中 이날 午后 8時 鍾路靑年會館에 各 方面의 人事가 會合하야 爲先 이事件의 眞相을 調査하기로하고 10名의 委員을 選하야 調査事項의 一切를 委任하야 羅景錫, 金思國 兩氏를 調査委員으로 派送하다.
▲7月 31日로부터 8月 5日에 至하는 一周間에 京城＜86＞ 手形交換所의 取引停止處分을 受한 者가 合計 21名에 達하얏는대 前週보다도 其數가 倍加하엿스며 其中에는 有數한 實業家가 有하엿다.

6日
▲前月末의 大雨로 因하야 不通되던 餠店烏山 間의 京釜線이 開通되다.

7일
▲聯合5國( 英, 佛, 伊, 白, 日)의 各 首相으로 成한 倫敦會議가 이날 英國首相 官邸에 開會되다. 이 會議는 現下 歐洲의 財政上 混沌狀態를 解決하자 함이엿스나 獨逸賠償金支拂猶豫에 關한 佛國側의 對償條件이 過重하야 드대여 이달 14日로 會議의 破裂을 見하다.

8日
▲大邱監獄에 在한 政治犯 53人은 一日間의 絶食同盟을 行하다. 그 原因은 同監獄의 無理한 學大에 耐치 못함이엿다.

9日
南鮮 等地를 歷回하던 上海學生蹴球團 一行인 申基俊, 劉基峻, 朱耀翰 等 17人이 入京하다. 日本의 *本卵平, 中野正剛 等 二十餘名의 新人派「新社」라는 一種의 政治的 結社를 起하다. 그의 標榜은 政治家에 뿐 依하야 支配되는 日本의 現社會를  國民全般에게 提供하야 思想을 解放하고 能律을 按排하고 國家의 姿勢를 正히 하야 國際生活에 動的 進路를 開하자 함이라 한다.
▲中國의 思想家 陳獨秀氏가 過激派와 連絡한 嫌疑로 上海佛相界에서 佛人探偵에게 速捕하다.
▲中國의 奉天에 反基督敎聯盟이 起하다. 이는 同地 基督敎米人靑年會長이 張作霖과 協策하야 當地의 靑年을 愚弄코저 함에 在하엿다한다.

11日
▲勞農露國 及 極東共和國의 日露會議全權主班으로 出發한 「묫폐」氏가 中國의 北京에 入하얏는대 北京大學長 蔡元培氏 以下의 歡迎이 有하다.
▲獨逸共和國 憲法發布 第 3周年 記念日에 當하는바 獨逸 大統領「에벨트」는 『獨逸國民은 獨逸은 勿論이오 全世界까지라도 救濟하겟다는 希望을 抱棄치 못할 것이라』는 意味의 布告를 發하야 國民의 意氣를 激勵하다. ▲愛蘭의 씽페인 黨開祖이오 愛蘭自由國假政府의 大統領인 「아-사끄리푸이스」氏가 突然 逝去하다. 氏는 南愛蘭國民領袖의 一人이니 1867年 데불린市에 生하야 多年 新聞記者가 되엿스며 凶牙利의 獨立運動에서 暗示를 得하야 「씽페인」黨을 組織하엿다. 「씽페인」은 一般으로 「吾等, 自身」이란 意味로 譯하나 其 本來의 精神은 오즉 愛蘭自己의 努力에 뿐 依하는 政策을 執하야써 文明世界의 國民으로서 眞個의 地位를 求하지 아니치 못할지라」는 意味이다. 先者의 英愛條約을 贊成함에 因하야 一部 愛蘭人의 攻駁을 受하얏스나 如何間 氏는 熱烈한 愛國者이엿스며 勇敢한 執事者이엿나니 그의 逝去가 愛蘭의 重大한 損失임은 無違하다.
