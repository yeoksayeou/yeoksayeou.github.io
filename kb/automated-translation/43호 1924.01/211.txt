Musan and Onsŏng, Left with Regret

Though it was something I did, it is a strange affair. When I went to P'yŏnganbuk Province (平北), somehow I failed to see the two counties of Huch'ang (厚昌) and Wŏn (渭原), leaving a great regret (恨). This time, in Hamgyŏngbuk Province (咸北), in exactly the same way, I have ended up leaving the regret of not seeing two counties.

"Is it enough just to say 'I did wrong and it turned out wrong'? You rascal—" It feels as though a stern reprimand (責) is descending upon me from the brothers of those two counties. However, thinking I might be better than a rascal who does wrong and then stubbornly denies it, I apologize to the brothers of the two counties.

The fault of the responsible reporter is, needless to say (母論), the issue, but it is said the counties themselves are also at fault. Musan (茂山), why did you establish your location beyond the great peaks of T'aesan Chullyŏng (泰山峻嶺), beneath the fearsome and terrifying, tiger-filled Pektu Mountain (白頭山)? Onsŏng (穩城), why did you choose a location at the very northernmost, the very back edge of Chosŏn (朝鮮)? Did you settle yourselves there, hiding slightly, so that trembling young hands and feet (手足) could not reach, could not perceive you? Be that as it may, the responsibility lies with the reporter... but still.

O Musan. Musan where I left my regret. Please forgive me. I resolve to find a special opportunity and definitely go see you. More than anything, I want to see Pektu Mountain. Also, I want to sleep with the tigers in the great forests of Musan. If I go to Musan, I cannot avoid seeing our guardian mountain (主山), Pektu Mountain, so how could I dare even conceive (生心) of going without sufficient days and sufficient preparation? That is why I could not stop by this time. Please understand (諒恕).

Ah—I want to grasp the hand of the artless Musan elder brother, and I want to eat the meal personally prepared by the innocent Musan auntie (아즈머니). Furthermore, the steaming hot, ox-testicle-like sweet potatoes (甘藷)—and the savory fresh kimchi with pork in noodle soup—I miss it all. I will surely go once. Please do not treat me poorly (薄待). And

O Onsŏng. If I speak more, you will call me a cunning fellow, so I have no choice but to present myself saying, "It was my fault." On the road from Chongsŏng (鍾城) to Kyŏngwŏn (慶源), why couldn't I manage to visit? It is true that I lacked sincerity. That's right. Looking back now, my regret is beyond measure. I can only plead the urgency of the schedule—that is all.

Brothers of Musan and Onsŏng. Take care of yourselves (保重). As you are on the border (국경), take even greater care. If you have news, please send it from time to time. <188>

**Summary**

The author, a reporter for the magazine *Kaebyŏk*, expresses deep regret for failing to visit the counties of Musan and Onsŏng in Hamgyŏngbuk Province during a recent trip, just as he had previously missed Huch'ang and Wŏn in P'yŏnganbuk Province. He apologizes profusely, blaming both his own shortcomings (lack of time, preparation) and jokingly, the remote locations of the counties themselves near Pektu Mountain and the northern border. He longs to visit Musan, see Pektu Mountain, experience its nature and local life (food, people), and promises to visit both counties eventually, asking them to take care, especially being border regions. The article is interesting for its personal, almost sentimental tone regarding travel within colonial Korea, the specific mention of Pektu Mountain as a national symbol ("our guardian mountain"), and the reference to the strategic importance and perhaps vulnerability of the northern border counties ("As you are on the border, take even greater care"). Historians might find the blend of personal reflection and the underlying context of movement and identity within the colony noteworthy.

恨을 남겨둔 茂山과 穩城
내가 단닌 일이지만 기괴한 일이외다. 平北을 갓슬 때에 엇지엇지하야 厚昌 渭原 두 고을을 못보고 큰 한을 남겻더니 이번 또 咸北에서 꼭 마챤가지로 두 고을을 못본 한을 남기게 되얏슴니다.
『잘못하고 잘못되얏슴니다 하면 그 뿐일가. 이놈-』하고 두 고을 형제로부터 엄하신 責이 내리는 듯 하외다. 그러나 잘못하고도 안이 잘못되얏다고 뚝뻐치는 놈보다는 나을 듯 하야 두 고을 형제에게 사과함니다.
책임진 기자의 잘못은 母論이외다마는 두 고을은 고을 자신부터 잘못이라함니다. 茂山은 왜그리 泰山峻嶺을 넘어 무섭고 무섭은 호랑이 만흔 白頭山下에 위치를 정햇슴닛가. 穩城은 왜그리 北하고도 찰 北 朝鮮의 뒷꼭댁이 끗테 위치를 잡앗슴닛가. 보르르 떠는 어린 手足이 미츨랴 야밋지 못하리만치 살작 피해 안젓슴닛가. 그러컨 저러컨 책임은 기자에게 잇지만...그런데
茂山이여. 내가 한을 둔 茂山이여. 용서하소서. 기어코 특별 기회를 어더 가보기로 결심함니다. 무엇보다 白頭山이 보고 십슴니다. 또는 茂山의 大森林속에서 호랑이와 자고 십슴니다. 茂山 가면 우리 主山 白頭山은 안이 볼 수 업스니 여간 日字 여간 준비로야 감히 生心이나 하겟슴닛가. 그래서 이번은 못들넛소이다. 諒恕하소서.
아-틔업는 茂山 형님의 손목도 쥐고 십고 천진스러운 茂山 아즈머니의 손수지은 밥도 먹고 십슴니다. 더군다나 김이 무럭무럭나는 소볼알갓흔 甘藷-또 구수한 갓김치에 도야지고기 친 국수-그것이 다- 그립소이다. 꼭 한번 갈게. 부대 薄待는 마라주소서. 그리고
穩城이여. 말하면 간사한 놈이라 하겟기 더-말치 안코 『잘못이외다』고 대령할 밧게 업슴니다. 鍾城서 慶源가는 길에 무엇이엇지 하야 못가보여스랴. 성의업는 것이 사실이외다. 그럿소이다. 지내놋코 보니 후회가 밋츨것이 업소이다. 다만 日字의 促急-그것에 訴하고 말 뿐이외다.
茂山 穩城 형제여. 保重하소서. 국경이니 더욱 保重하소서. 기사잇스면 때때로 보내주소서.＜188＞
