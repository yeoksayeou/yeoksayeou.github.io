My Humble Wish

Though we tried to do a somewhat better job, calling it a New Year's issue, the planned schedule went all wrong.

□

It might have been better if we had made it a double-sized issue, but spending a lot of money here seemed likely to excessively burden the readers, and doing so didn't seem like the right thing to do in these times of lacking money. So, we tried to limit it to around 250 pages, but it simply didn't go as planned. Especially after including over 50 pages of articles from provincial branches (道號 記事), we were truly at a loss about what to do (措手足).

□

Unavoidably (不得已), we closed our eyes, picked out manuscripts, and did this much with what we grabbed, postponing the remaining manuscripts for later. Special thanks are expressed to brothers Gobong (孤峰), Gimseong (金星), Dohyang (稻香), Woltan (月灘), and Dongin (東仁).

□

What did the passing year give us? I want to say this: it gave us at least one great ideological change (一大 思想上 變化). What mission (任務) did *Kaebyŏk* perform in relation to this? We don't want to chatter on (呶呶) about it.

□

What will the new year give us? This is something we cannot know. However, the so-called half-baked liberals (얼칙이 自由主義者) and superficial proponents of self-strengthening (반지리 實力主義者) will likely make more noise. But the self-awareness (自覺) of the masses (民衆) probably won't forgive (容恕) the insincerity (不眞面) of these types down to their core.

□

Whether it succeeds or fails, whether there was sincerity (誠意) or not, thinking about having laughed and cried over this one *Kaebyŏk* for the past year and now facing another year, these are not mere conventional words, but truly, the feelings (感懷) are boundless (無窮). Comrades! The dawn of the world (世界) is breaking, yet our self-awareness (自覺) and unity (結束) still have shallowness (淺薄). Let's stop the useless empty cries (空嘯) of merely being brothers (兄弟) or compatriots (同胞). Can we not truly know ourselves, know others, and stand in that place of simply being human—common to the menial worker (행낭사람), the day laborer (막살이 사람), and even the servants (드난드는 사람들)—and step onto the profound (深刻) and great (偉大) path of Kaebyŏk (開闢)? This is my humble wish (區區所願).

**Summary**

The editorial postscript for the January 1924 issue of *Kaebyŏk* expresses regret for not meeting the original plans for the New Year's edition due to financial constraints and space limitations, necessitating the postponement of several articles. The editor reflects on the past year's ideological shifts and *Kaebyŏk*'s role, looking ahead with uncertainty but cautioning against superficial liberalism and self-strengthening ideologies. The piece concludes with an emotional call for genuine self-awareness, unity, and a focus on shared humanity, transcending empty slogans, to embark on the "profound and great path of Kaebyŏk." For historians, this article offers insight into the practical challenges faced by Korean publishers under colonial rule, the ongoing ideological debates within the nationalist movement (liberalism vs. self-strengthening), and the intellectual elite's call for deeper popular consciousness and unity beyond factionalism in the early 1920s. The mention of specific contributors and the frank admission of editorial difficulties provide a glimpse into the inner workings of a significant cultural-nationalist magazine.

區區所願
新年號라 하야, 좀 잘해본다 햇더니, 豫定計劃이다-틀렷다.
□
倍大號  햇으면, 그래도 나을 듯한대, 여긔서 돈을 만히 드리면, 보는 이의 負擔이 過해질듯도 십고, 그러케 하는 것이 돈 업는 이에 잘하는 일도 못되는 듯 십허서, 限 二百五十頁 限定하고 해보려고 하니, 도모지 마음대로 되지가 안는다. 中에도 五十餘頁의 道號 記事를 싯고 보니, 正말 措手足을 할 수가 업섯다.
□
不得已, 눈감고 原稿를 집어내여, 잽히는대로 이만큼 하고, 그 남아지 原稿는 그대로 뒤로 미럿다. 特히 孤峰, 金星, 稻香, 月灘, 東仁 여러 兄에게 謝意를 表한다.
□
지나가는 해가 우리에게 무엇을 주엿너냐. 적어도 우리에게 一大 思想上 變化를 주엇너니라 나는 이러케 말하고 십다 開闢이 여긔에 즈음해서, 무슨 任務를 햇너냐, 우리는 呶呶히 말하고 십지가 안타.
□
새해는 우리에게 무엇을 줄 것인고, 이것은 알 수가 업는 일이다. 그러나 所謂 얼칙이 自由主義者와 반지리 實力主義者는, 좀더 들터이겟지. 그러나 民衆의 自覺은 이들의 내종지의 不眞面을 容恕치는 안흘터이겟지.
□
되나, 못되나, 誠意가 잇섯스나, 업섯스나 이 『開闢』하나를 가지고, 지난 한해를 웃고 울다가, 이제  한해를 맛는 생각을 하니, 例套의 말이 아니라, 正말로 感懷가 無窮하다. 동무들아, 世界의 새벽은 동터오는데, 우리의 自覺은, 結束은 오히려 淺薄함이 잇서라, 한갓 兄弟이니, 同胞이니, 쓸대업는 空嘯단 말고, 正말로 自己를 알고, 사람을 알아, 행낭사람과, 막살이 사람과, 이와, 드난드는 사람들지에 共通한 오즉 사람이란 그 자리에 서서, 深刻, 偉大한 開闢의 길거리에 나설 수가 업슬가. 區區所願이 이것이다.
