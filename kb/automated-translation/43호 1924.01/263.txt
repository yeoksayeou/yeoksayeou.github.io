**Summary**

This document provides the colophon or publication details for Issue 43 of the magazine *Kaebyŏk*, published on January 1, 1924. It lists the printing and publication dates, the editor (Yi Tonhwa), publisher (Yi Tusŏng), printer (Min Yŏngsun), printing company (Taedong Printing Co., Ltd.), and the publishing house (Kaebyŏk Publishing Company), along with their respective addresses in Kyŏngsŏng (colonial Seoul). It also includes the magazine's price (though unspecified), telephone number, postal transfer account number, and explicitly states that reprinting is welcomed. For historians, this colophon offers valuable concrete details about the publishing infrastructure in colonial Korea, including names of key personnel, companies involved, locations within Kyŏngsŏng, and the use of Japanese era names (Taishō) alongside Western dates, reflecting the administrative context of the time. The explicit permission to reprint could also indicate the magazine's desire for wider dissemination of its content.
