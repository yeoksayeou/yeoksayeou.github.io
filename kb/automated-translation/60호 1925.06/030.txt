**Body Text Translation:**
The Implementation of the Peace Preservation Law and the Future of the Korean Social Movement

It is not that the authorities have ever been unable to regulate due to a lack of laws, nor is it that any matter or movement in the world is determined solely by a piece of legislation. Therefore, regarding the implementation of the Peace Preservation Law this time, there is nothing new for us to make an issue of, but it seems mutually acceptable to discuss it at this opportunity. We asked for the opinions of several individuals active in the social movement or the press, based on three questions: 1. What is the future trend of the Korean social movement? 2. What should be the future direction of the Korean social movement? 3. What will be the future relationship between the social movement and the nationalist movement in Korea? However, it is regrettable that the words are inherently rough, and as they are transcribed from spoken words, they often fail to fully convey the speaker's intended meaning.

Chosŏn Labor-Farmer General Federation (朝鮮勞農總同盟)

Kwŏn O-sŏl (權五卨)

1. Regarding the trend of the Korean social movement following the implementation of the Peace Preservation Law? The fact that this law is being implemented in Chosŏn proves how intense the social movement is. The intensification of the movement is certainly not <10> solely due to the activities of a few activists; the hardships of the masses' lives and various circumstances have brought the movement to its current state. The intensification of the movement as the masses awaken is based on the inevitable laws of social evolution, so how could the Peace Preservation Law possibly stop this great trend? Look at the flowing water! No matter how huge the reef, will the flowing water not flow? The more obstacles there are ahead, the more fiercely the waves will surge. I declare that our movement from now on will advance ever more powerfully, like that flowing water.

2. The future direction of the movement? Just because the Peace Preservation Law is implemented, will there be a different direction? Originally, the labor movement and the peasant movement lie in organizing many unions, and the core of union organization lies in solving immediate problems. In other words, the sole goal is that life is absolutely impossible unless sharecropping fees are reduced or labor wages are raised. As long as it is not about denying the private property system or implementing communism, I believe the Peace Preservation Law will have no impact whatsoever on union organization. However, looking at the declarations and platforms of current labor-farmer organizations, there is undeniably some vagueness. Such things will be corrected to be concretely suitable for practical movement, and at the same time, the movement will gradually shift from a propagandistic, mood-driven movement to a practical one!

3. Relationship with nationalist activists? Order No. 7 (制令第7號) has suppressed nationalist activists in the past, and the current Peace Preservation Law will suppress social movement activists. Therefore, being in a position of receiving the same oppression, I believe there will be many points of convergence where both types of activists will draw closer.

Chosŏn Youth General Federation (朝鮮靑年總同盟)

Yi Yŏng (李英)

1. The social movement is a movement for the producing class to acquire the right to exist, so it is not a movement that will be swayed by laws. Only some superficial sentiment might be affected to some extent, but if the core movement is affected, politically it will rather promote an organized movement that has evolved to a new stage.

2. We cannot specifically state the direction of our movement at the present time. However, no matter how much pressure this evil law exerts, <11> we know that we will not deviate from the essence of Marxism.

3. Cooperating with the non-compromising nationalist movement is something our Youth General Federation has already declared. Therefore, regardless of what laws are implemented, we believe both movements will advance based on this principle.

Sinhŭng Youth League (新興靑年同盟)

Cho Pong-am (曹奉岩)

1. The bourgeoisie clamors that once the Peace Preservation Law is implemented, the social movement will be eradicated or wither away. They place their final hope on this and rejoice infinitely. Thus, they believe that with such a peculiar legal net, they can indeed stop the social movement commanded by historical fact.
The social movement is a mass life movement of the majority populace; it is the pulsation of the life consciousness of the laboring masses. Therefore, such a mass life movement, a product of historical necessity, cannot be stopped by any means. If one attempts to obstruct this movement by force, it cannot but be called a foolish experiment. In Germany, where Bismarck used policies to eradicate communism, the social movement is even more fierce today; in Italy, where the Fascist reactionary movement is intense, the social movement shows signs of not ceasing. The spirit of the legislators, in essence, is said to lie in regulating secret societies that aim to deny private property and transform the national structure (國軆). This will merely end up clarifying the scope and method of regulation for general regulators; in reality, it will have no effect whatsoever on the substance of the mass movement.
However, as regulatory methods become specialized, the tactics of the movement will also unfold in peculiar forms. In any country, the social movement is determined by the development of capitalism. If the capitalist offensive is peaceful, the social movement will also appear in a peaceful form; conversely, if the capitalist offensive develops [aggressively], [so will the social movement].

2. If the consolidation of the vanguard unit of the social movement front is the first step of the movement, this consolidated small unit must mobilize the masses from within the masses; hence, they must learn the method of mobilizing the masses. To mobilize the masses, one must accurately observe the immediate problems of the proletariat and struggle. Thus, departing from the past movement of activist versus activist, one must take the path of a realistic movement where the masses move. <12> Through this, the front of the movement will gradually expand, and accordingly, it will become a practical movement based on reality.

3. The relationship between the social movement and the nationalist movement will become even closer. As harsh laws are enacted to obstruct the movement, the relationship between activists will inevitably become deeper than before. Cooperation is necessary, of course, to confront the opposing side, and currently, the two major movements have various points of agreement to form a cooperative front. However, it must be stated that compromising elements within the nationalist movement must be rejected.

Sŏul Youth Association (서울靑年會)

Yi Chŏng-yun (李廷允)

1. (a) Will the social movement stagnate or be obstructed due to legal sanctions? If so, due to the laws implemented half a century ago by figures like Tsar Alexander I of Russia or Duke Bismarck of Germany to suppress social movements, the world would have already been decided according to their wishes, and such laws would be unnecessary in Japan today. Also, the fuss like today's Treaty of Basic Relations between the Socialist Soviet Federal Republic and the Yokosamurai Empire (요고사무라이 帝國), or the struggle for the presidential seat between General Hindenburg and Communist Party member Thälmann, akin to the leg-play of naked children (裸大童의 脚戱), would not have occurred. In any case, as a result of this law's implementation, while the social movement may show a somewhat depressed atmosphere on one hand, on the other hand, the movement will become more profound, leading to a path where the social hurricane approaches quickly, showing the tension of strong atmospheric pressure.
(b) From now on, tributary movements (支流運動) will become prevalent.

2. To reduce losses and gain benefits in large quantities or rapidly, for the time being, it will be necessary to strive for the internal organization and unification of the movement line as much as possible.

3. For the genuine liberation of Chosŏn in the true sense, nationalism and socialism ultimately cannot coexist, but as both movements become more organized, there will inevitably be situations where they must adopt a cooperative political front.

Hwayohoe (火曜會 - Tuesday Society)

Kim Ch'an (金燦) <13>

1. The future trend of the social movement? It is indeed difficult to definitively say how it will turn out, but I do not think, as newspapers or some predict, that 50 existing organizations will be dissolved or that the social movement will decline due to harsh oppression. Rather, I want to be optimistic. Why? Because when anyone encounters something, if they lack the necessary compass (standard) to judge it, they feel undue fear and recklessly charge in according to their own strength. However, in traditional Chosŏn or Japan, there were no laws to regulate the newly emerging movement, so the police authorities regulated according to their own will. Who, not being a god, can be absolutely free from mistakes or misunderstandings? How much more so for the police authorities, whom the world recognizes as lacking common sense and being obstinate, especially regarding the rapidly changing new thought and emerging movements, lacking any understanding or knowledge? Without a fixed legal standard, they arbitrarily carried out dissolutions, prohibitions, and arrests according to their own intentions, causing our social movement to suffer truly many blows and persecutions. However, the Peace Preservation Law being implemented this time, not only its legislative spirit but also its articles, clearly aims to regulate associations formed to change the national structure (國軆) or absolutely deny private property. In other words, it is a bill to regulate the secret societies of communists or anarchists. Is it not an undeniable fact that the existing social movement organizations in Chosŏn – namely, the Labor Federation (勞總), Youth Federation (靑總), various ideological groups, and other youth, Hŏngp'yŏng (衡平 - leveling/equality movement), women's, etc., organizations – are not secret societies of communists or anarchists? Therefore, I believe that from now on, not only will all previously banned gatherings be permitted, but also speech other than that advocating for changing the national structure or absolutely denying private property will not be unreasonably stopped, banned, or oppressed as in the past. Therefore, I predict that the future trend of society will develop more publicly and towards the masses. How will it really turn out?

2. The future direction of the social movement? It will probably conform to the future trend of the social movement. If so, lectures, seminars, speech meetings, and various other mass meetings will be held frequently, many publications will be issued, and on the memorial days of our proletariat, large demonstrations will be held, etc. While conducting propaganda, training, and demonstrations, our vanguard units will begin skirmishes (散兵戰), entering villages, factories, and mines to organize and train unions, and fighting for the immediate interests of our proletariat will be the direction we take. <14>

3. The future relationship between the social movement and the nationalist movement? I believe it will become closer, and at the same time, the quality [of the relationship/participants] will be strictly selected.

Socialist League (社會主義同盟)

Kim Hae-kwang (金解光)

1. What is the future trend of the social movement? I do not think that such a noteworthy new trend will emerge due to the implementation of the Peace Preservation Law. It is true that the Peace Preservation Law is a bill to oppress the social movement, but it cannot change the ultimate goal demanded by the proletariat. Furthermore, changing the demands of the current Chosŏn proletarian masses will be entirely impossible. Therefore, the future situation of the Chosŏn social movement will be to maintain the current state while struggling against the evil law called the Peace Preservation Law.

2. The future direction of the social movement? Until now, it has been purely a movement of ideological propaganda. However, from now on, emphasis will be placed on "unity and organization," and economic and other interest issues will also be considered to some extent.

3. What is the future relationship between the social movement and the nationalist movement? The nationalist movement and the social movement originally have common interests. Therefore, it is needless to reiterate that the movements will "be related to each other" in the future as well. If, in the future, Neo-Japanists (新日本主義者) emerge and the leaders of the hitherto incomplete nationalist movement join Neo-Japanism, we will simultaneously treat them as adversaries and assist in the revival of the nationalist movement.

Pukp'unghoe (北風會 - North Wind Society)

Sin Ch'ŏl (辛鐵)

1. Just as it is easier for people under the sanction of codified law to take certain actions, and their actions become more calculated, compared to people under unwritten regulations, so too in Chosŏn, under the vague phrases of their so-called subversive Koreans (不逞朝鮮人) or dangerous thought (不穩思想), we have rather received more ambiguous and harsher regulation. However, this Peace Preservation Law (治維法) can be said to have some standard in its articles regarding the scope and degree of regulation. Accordingly, the social movement, which previously held a vague anxiety with an undefined degree and scope, will henceforth find it rather easier to act on certain points and will make more calculated progress. "Transformation of the National Structure (國體の變革)," "Denial of the Private Property System (私有財産制度の否認)" – these elements belonging to the platform of the social movement will become widely known to the general populace within the coming six or seven years. And <15> the social movement in Chosŏn, regardless of the implementation of the Peace Preservation Law, is attempting to transcend the realm of the first-stage mood-driven movement and transition to a second-stage organized movement. Therefore, even if the banner belonging to the maximum program – which the Peace Preservation Law makes the focal point – is temporarily folded, by focusing more on the practical interests of the masses and emphasizing organization and strategic issues, I believe it will achieve more systematic and substantial development. That is, the social movement, betraying the mistaken expectations and imagination of some individuals that it will suffer a major blow due to the implementation of the Peace Preservation Law, will see its foundation become firmer and its steps more steady (丁丁). To say it once more, since the social movement did not arise due to the laxity of existing laws but possesses irrefutable reasons and causes, the foundation of the movement will not change just because the existing laws become somewhat harsher. Rather, that harshness will result in heightening the fervor of the movement, and the masses will, through the emergence of such laws, more directly perceive the anxiety and fear of the legislators. However, for the time being, I think the so-called underground trend (潛行の勢) and the overt struggle (表面の爭勢) may tend to be somewhat obstructed partially.

2. Regarding the future direction of the social movement, rather than the implementation of the Peace Preservation Law having a major influence, I believe a landmark change will occur due to the progress of the movement itself or the substantial demands of the masses. That is, henceforth, more effort will be devoted to the practical interests of the masses than to fomenting moods and propagating ideology. Particularly, movements like the labor-farmer union movement will become more practical based on this direction. And for the time being, I think the so-called level (에벨) of the movement will be somewhat lowered.

3. Henceforth, the two movements, regarding immediate practical interest issues – especially economic issues – will achieve mutual cooperation and partnership. However, this (or the incessant practical movement - 又는 不絶의 實地運動) will only be realized among the hardline nationalists who continue the unceasing movement for changing the status quo, the social movement activists who adopt stage-based policies (階段政策), and the masses who support these kinds of movements.

Tong-a Ilbo Company (東亞日報社)

Song Chin-u (宋鎭禹)

1. What is the future trend of the social movement?
First, underground movements (裏面運動) will become more intense than surface movements. Second, the previously disordered and confused movement <16> lines will, due to external pressure, promote self-reflection among members and foster a mood of unification and solidarity.
2. What is the future direction of the social movement?
Isn't it necessary to focus on internal organization rather than external propaganda to establish practical power?
3. What is the relationship between the social movement and the nationalist movement?
Due to shared external oppression and current shared livelihood instability, relationships of partnership and cooperation will increasingly arise.

Lawyer (辯護士)

Yi In (李仁)

Even with the implementation of the Peace Preservation Law, the social movement in Chosŏn seems unlikely to be affected as much as in Japan. Originally, Chosŏn has had the Security Law (保安法) and Order No. 7 (制令第7號), which can be called incomparably evil laws in the world, suppressing and sanctioning political, economic, and all other movements. Therefore, no matter how newly the Peace Preservation Law is implemented, there will likely be no further suppression or sanctions beyond that. Consequently, all movements will likely continue to proceed and develop as before. Especially since an ideologue (主義者) is someone who acts according to their own principles regardless of the legal sanctions of the time, if they were to bend their principles or stop their movement depending on the law, what would make them an ideologue? The issue is merely whether there are thorough ideologues in Chosŏn or not; the law itself is not the issue. However, now that the Peace Preservation Law has been enacted, political crimes will not be conflated and sanctioned as before, but will be strictly distinguished and sanctioned. Therefore, the social movement and the political movement will also proceed distinctly, and their tactics and various other aspects will be markedly different from before. Also, since there is still no clear distinction between nationalists and socialists in Chosŏn, it is difficult to say whether they will unite or not. If there were ideologues with clear colors, their movements would remain different in the future, and there seems no reason for them to unite because of this law.

Sidae Ilbo Company (時代日報社)

Hong Myŏng-hŭi (洪命熹)

It is likely true that the social movement in Chosŏn will face some obstacles due to the implementation of the Peace Preservation Law.
However, one cannot be pessimistic that the movement, which has progressed this far, will decline again because of those obstacles. In some respects, the movement might become more solid and sincere, establishing a more powerful foundation than today.
Regarding the future direction, the ideologues themselves must have determined <17> their course, so there is nothing particular to say. However, whether nationalist or socialist, in Chosŏn, the situation and circumstances are unique compared to other countries or peoples. Therefore, rather than distinguishing between 'you' and 'me' based on respective ideologies and forming factions, it would be better to join hands and advance together.

Kyŏngsŏng Youth Association (京城靑年會)

Song Pong-u (宋奉瑀)

1. The future trend of social movement activists. I believe it will be no different from before its implementation. Rather, in a certain sense, one could say they have found a more convenient path forward than before. Why? Because before the implementation of the evil law, there was an unidentified reef called the Order (制令), causing many shipwrecks (坐礁). Truly, the Order's scope was vague, allowing it to cater entirely to the mood and feelings of the authorities. However, this evil law has clear articles, so if one avoids them, there should be no danger of shipwreck as before.
2. Future direction. The 'level' (에뻴 - likely 'level' or 'Ebene') will naturally be lowered. Not because the evil law is implemented, but because we clearly saw our goal during the mood-driven movement phase, and struggling for immediate interests from now on is something we have always advocated. This is not the main current of the movement, but it is an unavoidable, necessary process.
3. How about the future connection with the mass movement (民衆運動)? Needless to say, they must stand on a common front temporally. In the future, it must become organic. Ah, the sad thing is that since the March First Movement, we have regrettably not found mass movement organizations or individuals who have taken to the streets domestically.

Chosŏn Ilbo Company (朝鮮日報社)

Sin Il-yong (辛日鎔)

1. The Peace Preservation Law is a product of the times. It is because the so-called peace and order could not be maintained without enacting such an evil law. Since we have already been subjected to the harsh regulations of the existing Security Law and Order No. 7, we just need the resolve that we will now be subjected to somewhat more organized regulation. In a certain sense, it can also be seen as a potent stimulus for deepening our movement.
2. Our social movement has hitherto not escaped the initial realm of being conceptual and emotional. That aspect must be corrected, and it must approach the real life of the masses.
3. The nationalist movement and the social movement are, by nature, unlikely to cooperate. However, strategically, there will likely be times of temporary cooperation, that is, facing a common enemy on a common front in times of emergency. <18>

**Summary**

This 1925 survey in *Kaebyŏk* magazine gathers opinions from prominent Korean socialist, labor, youth, and press figures on the likely impact of the newly implemented Japanese Peace Preservation Law (治安維持法) on the Korean social movement. Respondents generally express defiance, arguing that the movement stems from the masses' suffering and historical necessity, which laws cannot ultimately stop. Many believe the law, while oppressive, might paradoxically clarify regulatory boundaries (compared to vague previous orders), force the movement to become more organized, practical, and focused on immediate popular interests (like labor/tenant issues), potentially shifting from overt propaganda to deeper organization and underground activity. There's a consensus on the need for closer ties, or at least a tactical united front, between the social and (non-compromising) nationalist movements due to shared oppression, though some note inherent ideological differences.

For historians, this article is valuable as it captures the immediate reactions and strategic thinking of key Korean leftist and nationalist figures to a major piece of repressive colonial legislation. It reveals their analysis of the law's intent (targeting communism/anarchism), their assessment of its likely practical effects (potentially driving movements underground, forcing tactical shifts), and the complex, evolving relationship between socialist and nationalist currents in colonial Korea, particularly the debate around cooperation and united fronts against Japanese rule. The confidence, and sometimes optimism, expressed despite the harsh law is also noteworthy.

기사제목	治安維持法의 實施와 今後의 朝鮮 社會運動
기사형태	설문

治安維持法의 實施와 今後의 朝鮮 社會運動
當局의 取締가 어느 때에나 法令업서서 못한 것이 아니오 世上의 엇던 일이나 또 運動이 一片 法令 때문에 左右되는 것도 아닌 바 이번 治安維持法實施에 對해서도 우리가 새삼스럽게 問題삼을 것도 업지만 이런 機會에 한번 니야기나 하고 지나가는 것쯤은 彼此에 괸치아늘 듯 십허서
1. 朝鮮社會運動의 今後趨勢 如何
2. 朝鮮社會運動의 今後方針 如何
3. 朝鮮의 社會運動과 民族運動과의 今後關聯 如何오 하는 세 가지 條件을 들어써 社會運動 또는 言論界에 나선 몃 분의 意見을 무럿다. 그런대 그 말됨이 원체 거칠性이 만코 또 드러가지고 적는 말이 되야서 말한 그이의 辭意를 盡치 못함이 만흔 것은 遺憾이다.

朝鮮勞農總同盟

權五卨

1. 治安維持法이 實施됨에 딸아서 朝鮮社會運動의 趨勢?말이오닛가. 이 法이 朝鮮에도 實施되는 것만치 社會運動이 激烈함을 證明하는 것이올시다. 運動이 激烈하야가는 것은 決코 一部運動家의 活動만으로써 그러케 된 ＜10＞ 것이 안이오 大衆의 生活苦가 또한 온갓 事情이 運動을 今日에 이르게 한 것이올시다. 大衆이 自覺함에 딸아서 運動이 激烈하야감은 社會進化의 必然的 法則에 基因함인즉 엇지 治安維持法 그것으로 大勢를 막을 수 잇겟슴닛가. 보시오 저 흐르는 물을! 아모리 巨大한 岩礁가 잇다고 흐르는 물이 흐르지 안이하겟슴닛가. 압헤 障碍物이 잇스면 잇슬사록 波勢는 더욱 激仰할 것이올시다. 從今 以後의 우리 運動은 저 흐르는 물과 가치 더욱더욱 힘잇게 進展되리라고 斷言함니다.
