People from Each Province

Traditionally in Kyŏngsŏng, people from each province have been assessed as follows: Hwanghae-do, basket peddlers (because many women were merchants of distilled spirits (燒酒), oil (油), and pickled clams); P'yŏngan-do, kisaeng; Kangwŏn-do, outer servants; Hamgyŏng-do, water sellers; Ch'ungch'ŏng-do, yangban; Kyŏngsang-do, scholars; Chŏlla-do, entertainers; from Kyŏnggi, Kwach'ŏn wood sellers, Ogang boatmen, Kwangju wild vegetable sellers, Suwŏn stewards (쳥직이), Yangju grooms (말군), Koyang A-frame carriers, Yŏju and Ich'ŏn rice sellers. However, with the changing times, these assessments have also changed, and today it is said: Kyŏngsang-do prostitutes, Chŏlla-do vagrants (浮浪者), Kangwŏn-do firewood sellers, Ch'ungch'ŏng-do land speculators (땅흥정군). <115>

**Summary**

This short piece outlines traditional stereotypes associated with people from various Korean provinces as perceived in Kyŏngsŏng (Seoul). It lists occupations or social roles historically linked to each region, such as scholars from Kyŏngsang-do or entertainers from Chŏlla-do. The article then contrasts these older views with contemporary (1924) stereotypes, noting a significant shift, often towards more negative or economically marginal roles like prostitutes (Kyŏngsang-do), vagrants (Chŏlla-do), and land speculators (Ch'ungch'ŏng-do). From a historical perspective, this article is interesting as it documents shifting social perceptions and regional biases during the Japanese colonial period. The drastic change in stereotypes, particularly the negative turn for regions like Kyŏngsang-do and Chŏlla-do, could reflect socio-economic disruptions, migration patterns, or evolving prejudices influenced by colonial policies and modernization, offering historians insight into colonial-era social commentary and regional tensions.

各道各人
自來 京城에서 各道人을 評하기를 黃海道 광주리 장사(여자의 燒酒, 油, 조개젓 商이 多한 故) 平安道 기생, 江原道 행랑살이, 咸鏡道 물장사, 忠淸道 양반, 慶尙道 學者, 全羅道 광대, 京畿로는 果川 나무 장사, 五江 배사공, 廣州 산나물 장사, 水原 쳥직이, 楊州 말군, 高陽 통지게 장사, 驪州 利川 쌀장사라 하얏다. 그러나 시대의 변천으로 그 評도 변해서 今日에는 慶尙道 娼妓 全羅道 浮浪者 江原道 장작장사 忠淸道 땅흥정군이라 한다. ＜115＞
