기사제목	荷哇伊朝鮮學生故國訪問計劃 (Plan for Hawaiian Korean Students to Visit the Homeland)
기사형태	잡저 (Miscellaneous Article)

Welcome news has arrived that a group of 25 male and female Korean students from Hawaii (荷哇伊朝鮮人男女學生團), who have yearned for their homeland from a distant land across the sea for over 20 years (星霜), plan to visit their homeland in mid-June to embrace its mountains and rivers and enjoy time with their brothers and sisters in the homeland.

Brothers and sisters of the Paedal nation (배달국), who have longed for each other for many years across the vast ocean, a joyful handshake is not far off. Let those coming prepare for their arrival, and let those receiving prepare to welcome them, doing their utmost. <82>

**Summary**

This short article announces the planned visit of 25 Korean students (both male and female) from Hawaii to Korea in June 1923. Having lived abroad for over two decades, they are described as longing for their homeland. The article encourages both the visiting students and the Koreans in the homeland to prepare for this reunion. From a historical perspective, this piece highlights the continued connection and longing for Korea among the early Korean diaspora in Hawaii during the Japanese colonial period. It reflects the strong sense of national identity maintained abroad and the anticipation within Korea for interaction with overseas Koreans.

荷哇伊朝鮮學生故國訪問計劃
이역만리에서 20여 星霜을 두고 애답게 고국을 그리우든 저ㅡ태평양 중에 잇는 荷哇伊朝鮮人男女學生團 25명은 고국의 산천에 안기여 고국의 형제들과 한번 즐겨보기 위하야 오는 6월 중순을 기하야 고국을 방문하겟다는 반가운 소식이 왓다.
大洋을 隔하야 다년간 서로 그리우든 배달국의 남녀 형제여 반가운 악수가 不遠에 잇슬 것이니 오는 이는 오실 준비에 맛는 이는 맛는 준비에 그ㅡ최선을 盡하라.＜82＞
