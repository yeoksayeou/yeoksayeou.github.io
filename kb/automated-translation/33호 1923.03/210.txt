						One Reporter

Past movements were like this, and looking at the prohibition (禁酒) and smoking cessation (斷煙) movements arising in the last one or two months, this fact is proven even more clearly. Look how tremendous this is - even a rough count yields 140 alliances. Besides these, there must be countless alliances omitted from the survey, and who knows how many thousands or hundreds of alliance associations will arise in the future. Perhaps in just one or two more months, all of Chosŏn might become an alliance, and the 20 million people of Chosŏn might all become alliance members. <84>

Ah - a movement formidable and fearsome to outsiders, yet for us, a welcome and necessary movement. How much will our lives improve, and how much substance will be gained through this movement? Let alone the improvement in material aspects, how clean and refreshing (爽快) will our spiritual aspect become? Furthermore, how much benefit will there be in terms of time and work?

It is said that the amount spent on tobacco (烟草) in Chosŏn annually is 20 million won. If we all completely cease smoking, we will gain 20 million won. If we substitute work during smoking time, we will gain another 20 million won or more. Not only tobacco, but the annual cost of alcohol (酒價) in Chosŏn is likely consumed at a rate exceeding that of tobacco. Also, the time spent drinking alcohol is many times that of smoking time. If that time is utilized, it amounts to tens of millions of won. Let's set aside for now how to live and how to spend this money.

Calculating the gains just for January and February from the current 140 alliances, assuming conservatively 30 members per alliance, and each person saves 5 chŏn on tobacco and 5 chŏn on alcohol daily, totaling 10 chŏn, which is 3 wŏn per month, then for 30 people, it's 90 wŏn. One association has profited 90 wŏn. For 140 associations, it's 12,600 wŏn. For two months, it's 25,200 wŏn. This 25,200 wŏn has already entered our pockets. If we calculate this for just one year for these alliances alone, it's 151,200 wŏn. If we add all the general alliances constantly emerging, what a large sum it will reach!

By all means, let's see this through from beginning to end (有始有終). Let the whole country unite (擧國一致). For the sake of our livelihood, we cannot but do this; moreover, in our current circumstances, if we don't do it, we will perish. Brothers - since you self-awaken (自覺) and proceed voluntarily (自進), there is no need for special requests (別付託).

Added to this, there are Self-Production Societies (自作會), Societies for the Promotion of Native Products (物産獎勵會), and Consumption Saving Societies (消費節約會) in various places, constantly propagandizing, self-producing, promoting, and saving day by day, so auspicious light (瑞光) has shone (照臨) everywhere in Chosŏn's economic sphere.

As mentioned above, the benefit gained in our spiritual aspect through this movement is, above all, joyous, welcome, and refreshing (爽快). This truly cannot be bought even with ten thousand pieces of gold (萬金). By all means, let's see this through from beginning to end (有始有終)- If we falter, we will not escape the ridicule (鼻笑) of others. Even without that, it is said that a certain official (當局者), whoever it may be, remarked to some reporter in an observant, no, sneering (鼻笑) manner, "Since it's a movement by Chosŏn people, who knows how it will turn out..." We must resolve to fight even more fiercely (奮戰猛鬪) against this. "Because it's something Chosŏn people are doing?" Huh!<85> <83-85>

**Summary**

The article celebrates the rapid spread of prohibition (anti-alcohol) and smoking cessation movements across Korea in early 1923, listing 140 specific local alliances formed within just two months. The author emphasizes the potential economic benefits (saving millions of won spent on tobacco and alcohol, plus reclaiming productive time) and, more importantly, the spiritual uplift and refreshment these movements bring. The piece urges nationwide unity and perseverance (有始有終), linking temperance to economic self-strengthening (mentioning native product promotion and savings societies) and national survival. It concludes by noting dismissive attitudes from authorities ("Since it's a movement by Chosŏn people...") and calls for renewed determination in response.

This article is historically interesting as it showcases a widespread grassroots social movement in colonial Korea focused on self-improvement and economic nationalism. It reflects the cultural nationalist drive to reform perceived social ills (drinking, smoking) as a path to national strengthening, distinct from direct political action. The extensive list of local groups provides valuable data on the movement's geographic reach and organizational forms (youth groups, labor unions, village associations, etc.). The mention of official skepticism highlights the tense relationship between Korean society and the Japanese colonial authorities, even regarding seemingly apolitical self-help movements.

보라 얼마나 굉장한가를- 얼풋 꼽아도 140동맹이다. 此外에 조사상 漏落된 동맹도 亦 부지기수이겟고 장차 起할 동맹회가 不知幾千幾百일 것이다. 아마 이제 一二개월만 지나면 조선 전체가 동맹이 될넌지도 不知며 조선 2천만이 동맹회원이 될넌지도 모르겟다.＜84＞
아- 남 보기에는 무서운 운동, 전율할 운동, 우리로서는 반가운 운동, 당연히 할 운동, 이 운동으로 말미암아 우리의 생활은 얼마나 향상이 되며 얼마나한 실속이 생길 것인가. 물질방면으로 향상은 고사하고 우리의 정신방면으로 얼마나 깨긋하고 爽快한가 또는 시간상 사업상으로는 얼마나 이득이 될넌가.
조선에서 1개년간 소비되는 烟草額만 2천만원이라 한다. 그러면 우리가 전부 斷烟이 되면 이천만원은 得할 것이다. 흡연의 시간에 사업을 代하면 또 이천만원 이상을 得할 것이다. 烟草만 그러할 뿐 아니라 조선에 대한 1년의 酒價는 煙草 이상으로 소비될 것이다. 또 술먹는 시간은 흡연시간의 멧 배이다. 그 시간을 이용하면 몃 천만원이다. 이 돈으로 엇더케 살고 엇더케 쓸 것은 아직 두고.
현재 140동맹으로 一二월간의 이득만 計한다 해도 1동맹에 少하게 30인 잡고 1인이 매일 煙草代 5전 酒價 5전 합계 10전을 잡아 1개월 3원이라 하면 30인에 90원이다. 1會에서 90원은 利하얏다.  140會이면 1만2천6백원이다. 2개월이면 2만5천2백원이다, 이 2만5천2백원은 벌서 우리의 주머니에 들엇다. 이 동맹 뿐 1개년만 쳐도 15만1천2백원이니 此에 작구 이러나는 총동맹을 다 計하면 얼마나한 多額에 至할 것인가.
아모조록 有始有終하자. 擧國一致하자. 생을 위하야는 아니할 수 업스며 더욱이 우리의 금일 형편으로는 아니하면 죽겟다. 형제- 자각하야 自進하나니 별부탁이 업겟다.
여긔다가 自作會가 잇고 物産獎勵會가 잇고 각처에 消費節約會 등이 有하야 時로 日로 선전을 하며 자작을 하며 장려를 하며 절약을 하니 조선의 경제계란 도처에 서광이 照臨하얏다.
우에 말하얏거니와 이 운동으로 말미암아 우리의 정신방면으로 생기는 이득이야말로 무엇보다 깃뿌고 반갑고 爽快하다. 이야말로 萬金으로써 못 박구겟다. 아모조록 有始有終하자- 만약 잘못하다가는 타인의 鼻笑를 면치 못할 것이다. 그러치 안어도 당국자 누구는 『조선인들의 운동이니까 어떨는지 무엇-』하고 어떤 기자에게 방관적 아니 鼻笑의 態로써 말햇다 한다. 우리는 여긔에 더- 한층 奮戰猛鬪를 期하얏겟다. 『조선인들이 하는 일이니까?』에-＜85＞ ＜83-85＞
