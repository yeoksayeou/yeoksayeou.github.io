**Body Text Translation:**
Three Thousand Hearts/Thoughts in a Day
						Kim Ansŏ
Prayer

O Author of the Universe, who lacks nothing that cannot be given when asked,
O Almighty One, who multiplies eight by eight to make eighty-eight,
Yesterday I went to the market and with a cup of wine,
I bought a monster called Friendship and made it my own.
But this morning, I lost it over a cup of wine.
How desolate and sorrowful my heart must be!
I earnestly pray, please find that lost thing called Friendship
and make it mine once again.
O Author of the Universe, who lacks nothing that cannot be given when asked.

Devalued Tears

Confusing gold and love,
my lover, skilled at making dishes,<14>
with the skill of peeling a crabapple (림금알) and dividing it piece by piece,
took the "Dish of Love," which amounts to only one bowl,
and divided it so evenly, so skillfully,
then with a gentle song and a smile,
upon the flower-adorned table placed before several people,
served it out, one bowl at a time.
From the moment those many people ate that dish,
everything changed, the Earth began to turn uselessly,
and tears, which previously had the value
of a single drop being like the lofty words of a Saint,
became devalued,
so that thereafter, a single drop of tears was not even worth five *chŏn*.

Crimson

When the crimson azalea flowers fall,
When the sorrowful heart's azalea flowers fall,
When the vexing evening sun sets,
When the dimming day darkens even in the waning spring,
When the village lamps shine brightly,
Helplessly, my spirit weeps alone.

Early Month Moon <15>

Between the end of a dying day
and the beginning of a nascent day,
neither dark nor bright,
in the twilight's western sky,
like a short life,
like a sorrowful love,
appearing for a while then fading away,
like the character 'ㅁ', the crimson
crescent moon of the early month.

Lingering Scent

In the easily falling flower of "Joy,"
hides the scent of endless "Sorrow."
The flower is far too unreliable,
and the lingering Scent is far too poignant.<16>
<14-16>

**Summary**

This collection of five short poems by Kim Ansŏ explores themes of ephemerality, loss, and disillusionment. "Prayer" laments the easy loss of friendship. "Devalued Tears" criticizes the cheapening of love and sorrow when shared superficially. "Crimson" evokes a mood of deep loneliness amid the fading beauty of spring twilight. "Early Month Moon" uses the crescent moon as a metaphor for fleeting existence and love. "Lingering Scent" reflects on the fragility of joy and the persistent poignancy of sorrow. From a historical perspective, these poems, published in the prominent magazine *Kaebyŏk* shortly after the March 1st Movement, offer insight into the personal and emotional landscape of intellectuals during the Japanese colonial period. The pervasive sense of melancholy, transience, and disillusionment might reflect broader societal anxieties and the search for meaning under oppressive circumstances, making it valuable for historians studying the psychological impact of colonialism on Korean artistic expression.

祈禱
求하면 주지 못할 것이 업는 宇宙의 著者시여,
八을 八倍 하야 八八되게 하시는 全能者시여,
어제 제가 쟝에 갓다가 友情이라는 怪物을
술 한잔으로 사서 제의 所有를 만를엇습니다.
만은 오늘 아츰에는 술 한잔에 그것을 잃엇습니다.
엇지나 제의 맘이 설게도 서오하겟습닛가!
간절히 비옵나니, 잃어진 友情이라는 그것을
다시 차자 제의 것을 만들어 주시옵소서.
求하면 주지 못할 것이 업는 宇宙의 著者시여.

低落된 눈물
림금과 사랑을 混同하는
솜씨조케 料理를 만드는 愛人은＜14＞
림금알을 벳겨 조각조각 난호는 솜씨로,
한그릇밧게 아니되는 「사랑의 料理」를
골고롭게도 솜씨잇게 난호아서는
곱은 노래가락에 微笑를 띄우며,
여러 사람의 압헤 노힌 꼿食卓 우에
한그릇식 한그릇식 내여 노핫습니다.
여려 사람이 그 料理를 먹엇습 때부터,
모든 것은 一變하야 地球는 쓸데업시 돌아가게 되며,
以前에는 한방울이, 聖者의 놉흔 말과 가튼
그만큼한 價値가 잇는 눈물이 低落이 되야,
그때부터는 눈물 한방울에 五錢도 못가게 되엿습니다.

새빩안
새빩안 피빗의 진달내꼿이 질 때,
애닯은 맘의 진달내꼿이 떨어질 때,
속을 복기게 하는 저녁볏이 넘을 때,
저므는 봄에도 저믐날이 저갈 때,
村집의 燈뿔이 빤하게 빗날 때,
어이업시도 내 靈은 혼자 울고 잇서라.
