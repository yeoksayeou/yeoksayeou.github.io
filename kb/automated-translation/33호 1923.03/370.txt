Upon Finishing Editing

It is rather embarrassing to repeat words of apology regarding editing. We find ourselves relating these irresponsible words once again.
Things in the world truly do not go according to one's heart. As briefly mentioned at the beginning of the volume, we intended for this journal to appear on this March 1st under the name of the Kyŏngnam-do issue. However, due to various circumstances, it has ended up appearing in this current form. Whether it was due to our insufficient activity, the complexity of the investigation's facts, or a miscalculation of the timing, whatever the reason, we are thick-skinned but have broken our promise for the March issue. If, instead, the content had been complete, we might have consoled ourselves, and it would be less regrettable to our readers, but the content also seems mediocre. This too was due to unavoidable circumstances. Three of the editors were away on trips, and with only one person remaining in charge, fumbling through, how could the result be presentable?

However, we cannot but be grateful for the kindness of all those who contributed manuscripts. February is a busy month, and despite undoubtedly having no time to spare due to your own affairs, you disregarded this and provided many opinions from various fields and wrote much, enabling this journal to face the world with a visage not overly shameful; truly, we cannot bear the weight of our gratitude.

One more regrettable matter is that due to editing deadlines or lack of space, we were unable to include the precious manuscripts sent from afar in Peking and Tokyo, and also due to lack of space, the plays sent by several individuals from P'yŏngyang and Kaesŏng have been held over this month. Please forgive us.

Lastly, one thing we wish to ask about is the requests for manuscript responses, which you make even while knowing [our policy]. Setting aside inquiries about acceptance, these are requests for return. As this journal has already announced in a company notice, the return of manuscripts is not customary, so we hope that both parties will refrain from exceptional procedures.

**Summary**

The editorial postscript for the March 1923 issue of *Kaebyŏk* magazine is primarily an apology to the readers. The editors express regret for failing to deliver the promised special issue focused on Kyŏngnam Province (慶南道) and for the perceived mediocrity of the current issue's content, attributing these shortcomings to "various circumstances" and the absence of three editors, leaving only one person to manage the publication. They thank contributors for their submissions despite the difficulties and apologize for being unable to publish contributions from Peking, Tokyo, P'yŏngyang, and Kaesŏng due to space or time constraints. Finally, they reiterate the magazine's policy of not returning submitted manuscripts. For historians, this piece offers a glimpse into the practical challenges faced by Korean publishers during the colonial period, including staffing issues, logistical difficulties in coordinating special issues, and managing contributions from both within Korea and abroad (Peking, Tokyo). The mention of "various circumstances" might subtly hint at external pressures or censorship, common concerns for publications at the time. The established network of contributors across East Asia is also noteworthy.

編輯을 마치면서
편집상 謝告의 말슴도 넘우하니까 돌이여 미안합니다. 이 무책임한 말슴을 또 알외게 되얏습니다.
세상의 일이란 참말 사람의 마음과 가티 아니됩니다. 卷頭에도 잠간 말슴하얏습이다마는 本誌는 이 3월 1일로써 慶南道號의 名으로써 現出하랴고 하얏습니다. 그리한 것이 여러 가지 사정으로 그만 이 모양으로 現出되옵니다. 저의 활동이 未及함이온지 調査의 사실이 넘우 복잡함이옵든지 日時의 예산을 잘못함이옵든지 엇잿든 3월호는 顔厚하오나 失信이 되엿소이다. 대신에 내용이나 완전하엿스면 오히려 自慰도 하겟고 독자에게도 미안이 들하겟는데 내용 역시 평평한 듯 하외다. 此亦 엇절 수 업는 사정이엿습니다. 편집자 중 3인이 출타하옵고 惟一人이 當局하야 어믈어믈 하엿싸오니 꼴이 보잘 것이 잇겟습니까.
그러나 寄稿諸氏의 厚意는 감사치 안흘 수 업소이다. 2월은 분주한 달이라 自家自事에도 여념이 업스실 터인데 불구하시고 다방면으로 만흔 의견을 주시고 만히 집필해 주셔서 本誌가 과히 부끄럽지 아니한 면목으로써 세상에 나서게 되얏슴은 실로 감사를 不堪하나이다.
또 한 가지 미안하온 것은 멀리 北京 東京에서 보내주신 玉稿를 편집일자 又는 지면의 관계로 싯지 못하게 됨이오며 平壤 開城에서 몃 분이 보내신 희곡도 지면의 不許로 이 달을 그대로 묵이게 됨이외다. 寬恕하옵소셔.
끗흐로 한 가지 엿줄 것은 여러분이 아시면서도 하시는 원고독촉이외다. 기재 여부의 독촉은 고사하고 반환의 독촉이외다. 本誌-이미 社告한 바와 가티 원고 반환은 예에 업기로 되얏사오니 피차-예외의 수속이 업기를 바라옵니다.
