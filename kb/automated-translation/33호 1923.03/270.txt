**Body Text Translation:**
A Fundamental Survey of Korean Culture!!
Our Company's Two Major Plans for This Year—The Publication of Provincial Issues Based on a Survey of Korea's Current State; The Rallying of Representatives from the Thirteen Provinces Accompanying the Establishment of the Membership System

The Survey Team Has Already Departed from Kyŏngsŏng

The world is clamoring and buzzing with the news that Kaebyŏk Publishing Company, as its new project for the year Kyehae (癸亥年, 1923), will publish special issues for each province and has embarked on a fundamental survey of Korean culture.

Why does the Kaebyŏk magazine dare to undertake this great project, disregarding economic difficulties, exhaustion of effort, and all other obstacles? Where does its intention lie?

In the world, there is no ignorance like not knowing one's own affairs while knowing the affairs of others, and even more ignorant than that are those people who live without knowing how their own livelihoods are faring. Look, how many among us Koreans know the details of our own livelihoods, which constitute the Korean situation? We have many people who know others' affairs well but are comparatively ignorant of their own, and many who are adept at criticizing others' livelihoods but do not know how their own are faring. Deeply lamenting this point, we have now embarked on a fundamental survey of Korean culture as our new project, and through this, we have decided to publish special issues for each province. This is purely for the purpose of helping Koreans understand Korea well, and for Koreans to know the details of their own livelihoods well, thereby gaining the intelligence to manage and organize them with their own hands.

However, providing the guidance and policy sufficient to equip Koreans with the knowledge to understand Korea well is by no means an easy task. To truly know the livelihoods of the territory called Samch'ŏlli (三千里, Three Thousand Ri) and the population called twenty million, one must have a long period of one to two years. Therefore, we have decided to divide Korea into 13 provinces and survey the details of the 13 provinces over 13 months.

Starting from the 1st of this month, the first beginning will be made with South Kyŏngsang Province (慶尙南道), and from the 1st of next month, it will move to North Kyŏngsang Province (慶尙北道), sequentially reaching the entire land of Korea. May the people of all provinces rouse themselves and meticulously prepare materials and data. And await what kind of description and what judgments of right and wrong will appear on the pages of "Kaebyŏk" (開闢) or "Puin" (婦人). Therefore, let our general brethren rejoice that through this undertaking today, they will, for the first time, come to know their own livelihoods in detail, and thereby engrave the entirety of Korea in our minds. The standards by which we will survey each province this time are:

▲ Causes and Trends of Various Social Problems ▲ Introduction and Critique of Central Figures and Major Project Organizations ▲ The Actual State of Human Sentiment and Customs ▲ The Status of Industry, Education, and Religion ▲ Exploration of Famous Sites, Historical Relics, and Legends ▲ Observation and Critique concerning Other General Conditions

Kyŏngsŏng 开闢社 (Kaebyŏk Publishing Company)

**Summary**

The Kaebyŏk Publishing Company announces an ambitious year-long project for 1923: a "Fundamental Survey of Korean Culture." Deploring Koreans' lack of knowledge about their own country and circumstances compared to their awareness of others', the magazine plans to conduct detailed surveys of each of Korea's thirteen provinces, one per month. The findings, covering social issues, key figures/organizations, customs, economy, education, religion, historical sites, and legends, will be published in special provincial issues of "Kaebyŏk" or "Puin" magazines. The stated goal is to empower Koreans with self-knowledge to manage their own affairs. The survey team has already departed Kyŏngsŏng (Seoul), starting with South Kyŏngsang Province.

From a historical perspective, this announcement is significant as it reflects a strong nationalist consciousness and desire for self-understanding and self-determination during the Japanese colonial period. The project itself, aiming for a comprehensive, province-by-province documentation of Korean society, culture, and economy from a Korean perspective, represents a major intellectual and organizational undertaking under colonial constraints. Historians might find the project's stated aims, methodology (the listed survey points), and the very act of undertaking such a survey revealing about Korean intellectual responses to colonialism, efforts towards cultural preservation and national consciousness-raising, and the perceived needs of Korean society in the early 1920s.

기사형태	사고·편집후기

朝鮮文化의 基本調査!!
本社의 今年中 二大計劃ㅡ朝鮮의 現狀調査에 의할 各道 道號의 刊行 社友制의 期成에 伴할 十三道 役軍의 糾合

踏査員 一行은 旣히 京城을 出發하다

천하가 왁자하고 떠드는 소리는 개벽사가 癸亥年의 신사업으로 各道 道號를 한다. 조선문화의 기본조사에 착수한다 부르짓는 것이라.
개벽잡지가 경제의 곤란, 노력의 困憊, 기타 모든 난관을 돌아보지 아니하고 감히 이 대사업을 단행하는 것은 그 뜻이 어대 잇나뇨.
천하의 무식이 남의 일은 알되 자기의 일을 모르는 것 만치 무식한 일이 업고 그보다 더 무식한 것은 자기네의 살림살이 내용이 엇지 되어가는 것을 모르고 사는 사람 가티 무식한 일이 업다. 보라, 우리 조선사람이 조선형편이라 하는 자기네의 살림살이의 내용을 아는 사람이 얼마나 되는가? 우리는 남의 일은 잘 알되 자기의 일은 비교적 모르는 사람이 만흐며 남의 살림살이는 잘 비평하되 자기의 살림살이는 어찌 되어가는지 모르는 사람이 만흐다. 우리는 이 점을 심히 慨嘆하게 보아 지금의 신사업으로 조선문화의 기본조사에 착수하며 니여써 各道 道號를 간행하기로 하얏나니 이는 순전히 조선사람으로 조선을 잘 이해하자는 데 잇스며 조선사람으로 자기내의 살림살이의 내용을 잘 알아가지고 그를 자기네의 손으로 處辦하고 정리하는 총명을 가지라 하는데 잇는 것 뿐이다.
그러나 조선사람으로 조선을 잘 이해하는 지식을 가지게 할 만한 지도와 방침은 결코 용이한 일이 아니다. 적어도 삼천리라 하는 疆土와 2천만이라 하는 식구의 살림살이를 잘 알고저 하면 1년 내지 2년의 長歲月을 가지지 아니하면 안될 것이다. 그래서 우리는 조선을 13도로 논하 가지고 13개월에 13도의 내용을 조사하기로 하얏다.
今月 1일로부터 첫 번 시작이 慶尙南道로 되게 되얏스며 來月 1일로부터 慶尙北道로 옴기여 順次로 조선 全土에 及할 것이다. 全道 人土는 분발하야 재료와 품질을 일일히 정돈하라. 그리고 어떠한 형용과 어떠한 是非曲直이 「開闢」 또는 「婦人」의 紙上에 나타날 것을 기다리라. 그래서 우리 일반 형제들은 금일의 此擧에 인하야 처음으로 자기네의 살림살이를 자세히 알게 되며 그리하야 조선의 전반을 우리들 머리 속에 銘記하게 될 것을 깁버하라. 이번에 우리가 各道를 답사할 표준은
諸社會問題의 原因 及 趨向 ▲中心人物 及 主要事業機關의 紹介 及 批評 ▲人情風俗의 實際如何 ▲産業敎育 及 宗敎의 狀況 ▲名勝古蹟 及 傳說의 探査 ▲其他의 一般狀勢에 관한 觀察과 批評
京城  開闢社
