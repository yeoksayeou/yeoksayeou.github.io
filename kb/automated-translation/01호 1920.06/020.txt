創刊辭 (Founding Editorial)

A sound exists, spreading throughout the world, and all humanity responds, beginning to cry out. The strong cry out and the weak cry out; the superior cry out and the inferior cry out. East, West, South, North—the four seas and eight directions—are all submerged in sound. Is it a thunderbolt (霹靂)? An earthquake (地震)? Divine music (神籟)? Demonic wailing (魔哭)? We cannot yet judge whether this sound is right or wrong. However, it is clear that it is the voice of the people (人民), which the majority longs for and demands.

A philosopher (哲人) said, "The voice of the majority of the people is the voice of God (神)." God has no demands of His own, so He responds to the voice of the people to express their demands. God has no longings of His own, so He responds to the voice of the people to also manifest their longings. The sound longed for and demanded by the majority of the people is the sound longed for and demanded by God; this is the sound of the world's creation (世界 開闢).

God began evolution from the One Thing of Nothingness (無何有之一物). He initiated the division of nothingness (無有를 肇判), organized the Solar System (太陽界), and brought forth all things (萬物); this is the creation of the universe (宇宙의 開闢). Humans, as beings evolved from God, represent all creation. They started with fishing and hunting (漁獵), managed agriculture (農業), and initiated commerce and industry (商工業), adding evolution upon evolution, and have now come to experience this spirit of reform (革新의 氣運) called the Great World Transformation (世界大改造); this was the creation of creation (開闢의 開闢).

At this time (此際) when time is creating, affairs are creating, and people are creating, for us (吾人) to write this history of creation (開闢史) is truly fitting for the time, fitting for affairs, and fitting for the spirit—it cannot but be called a demand from God. The voice of the people will grow louder, wider, and more thorough because of this creation.

Alas (嗚呼)! Today, hundreds of thousands of years after the birth of humankind (人類), how could the emergence of this *Gaebyeok* (開闢) magazine be a mere coincidence (偶然)? <2>

**Summary**

This founding editorial proclaims the launch of the magazine *Gaebyeok* (Creation/Genesis) amidst a perceived global transformation. It interprets the widespread calls for change ("the people's voice") as a divine mandate, equating it to the voice of God and linking it to the ongoing process of cosmic and human creation (開闢). The magazine positions itself as a chronicler and participant in this new era of 'creation.' For historians, this text vividly illustrates the intellectual climate of 1920 Korea under Japanese rule. Its blending of traditional cosmology (God, creation) with modern political ideas (the people's voice, world transformation) and its messianic, quasi-religious tone reflect a potent mix of nationalism, spiritualism (influenced perhaps by movements like Cheondogyo, the publisher), and revolutionary fervor, offering insight into how some Koreans sought to understand and navigate their challenging reality.

創刊辭
소리-있어 넓히 世界에 傳하니 온 世界 모든 人類-이에 應하야 부르짖기를 始作하도다. 强者도 부르짖고 弱者도 부르짖으며 優者도 부르짖고 劣者도 부르짖도다. 東西南北, 四海八方이 다같이 소리中에 묻혀 있도다. 霹靂이냐 地震이냐 神籟이냐 魔哭이냐 우리는 아즉이 소리의 正邪를 判斷할 수 없도다. 左右間 多數가 渴仰하고 多數가 要求하는 人民의 소리임은 明白하도다.
哲人은 말하되 多數 人民의 聲은 곳 神의 聲이라 하엿나니 神은 스스로 要求가 없는지라 人民의 소리에 應하야 其 要求를 發表하는 것이요, 神은 스스로 渴仰이 없는지라 人民의 소리에 應하야 또한 其 渴仰을 나타내는 것이라, 多數 人民의 渴仰하고 且 要求하는 소리는 곳 神의 渴仰하고 要求하는 소리니 이곳 世界 開闢의 소리로다.
神은 無何有의 一物로 붙어 進化를 始作하엿도다. 無有를 肇判하고 太陽界를 組織하고 萬物을 내엇나니 이 곳 宇宙의 開闢이며 사람은 神의 進化한 者로 萬物을 代表하야 漁獵을 始하며 농업을 營하며 商工業을 起하야 進化에 進化를 加하는 中 오늘날 이 世界大改造라 하는 革新의 氣運을 맛보게 되엇나니 이 곳 開闢의 開闢이엇도다.
時-開闢하고 事-開闢하고 人物이 開闢하는 此際에 吾人으로 이 開闢史를 쓰게됨은 實로 時에 適, 事에 適, 精神에 適하는 神의 要求라 아니할 수 없도다. 人民의 소리는 이 開闢에 말미암아 더욱 커지고 넓어지고, 徹底하야지리라.
嗚呼라 人類의 出生 數十萬年의 오늘날, 처음으로 이 開闢 雜誌가 나게됨이 어찌 偶然이랴. ＜2＞
