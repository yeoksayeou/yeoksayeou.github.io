**Body Text Translation:**
Praise of Death
						No Wŏl (蘆月)

Sir Poet-
Who are you? But then
When was it that I, together with you,
Leaving humanity, leaving pleasure (歡樂),
And floating between the Great Sky (大空) and the Great Sea (大海),
Feeling beauty (美) and sorrow (哀愁), just simply
Chasing the vast (茫茫) horizon (水平線),
To the limitless (際涯업는) world (世界),
To the world seen in childhood and dreams,
Wandered (漂泊) like duckweed (浮萍)? To the East (東) and West (西)?

And amidst wonder (驚異) and admiration (嘆美),
When watching the ethereal (縹渺) miracle (奇蹟) of sunset (日沒),
Gold floats in the Great Sky,
Birds fly to the limit (際涯),
And when all phenomena (萬像) pour towards there,
Ah- Sea of eternity (永遠) and infinity (無限).
Saying, "Ah, desolate (寞寞) dream world (世界) transcending (超越) time (時間) and space (空間),"
When extremely excited (興奮), when ecstatic (狂喜);

Suddenly, black clouds (黑雲) shatter the peace (平和) of the Great Sea,
When a storm (暴風) overturns the waves,
When receiving the sentence (宣告) of shipwreck (破船),
Ah- When only death (死) and despair (絶望) are visible,
Ah- When the hereditary disease (遺傳病) of terror (恐怖) drives the whole body (全身) to madness (狂亂),
Our clothes (衣服) are torn by the storm (暴風雨),
And we became naked (裸體).
Ah- In order to hide the shame (羞恥) of this nakedness (裸體),
We embraced (抱擁) each other, closing our eyes.
And you, calling me,
Saying, "My love, my life (生命), my hope (希望),"
Planted the most fervent (熱烈) kiss on my cheek, my neck;
And then, as we entered the place (地) of mutual death (共死) together,
The words that I confessed (告白) all secrets (秘密),
Are they all really true?

Ah- Sir Poet.
This is fantasy (空想). It is your own thought (生覺).
This is a dream. It is merely sympathy (同情) for fragments (破片) of legend (傳說).
I do not love you.
Outside the dream world (世界 以外), I do not love you at all.

(June 5th)
<85>

**Summary**

The poem vividly recounts a shared, dreamlike experience between the speaker and a "Poet," where they faced a terrifying shipwreck and impending death together. Amidst the chaos and fear, they shared an intimate moment, culminating in the Poet calling the speaker their love, life, and hope, and the speaker supposedly confessing secrets. However, the speaker abruptly retracts this memory, declaring it the Poet's fantasy or a dream born of sympathy for legends, and firmly states they do not love the Poet in reality. From a historical perspective, published in *Kaebyŏk* shortly after the 1919 March First Movement, the poem's intense emotional swings between shared crisis, intimacy, and ultimate denial might resonate with the disillusionment, psychological strain, and fractured sense of reality experienced by Korean intellectuals under the oppressive early colonial regime. Historians might find the juxtaposition of existential despair and the negation of connection interesting as a potential reflection of the complex psychological landscape of the era.

잡지명	개벽 제1호

기사제목	死의 讚美
필자	蘆月
기사형태	시

死의 讚美
						蘆月
詩人님-
당신은 누구셔요? 그런데
언제 제가 당신과 한께
人間을 떠나서 歡樂을 떠나서
그리고 大空과 大海 새에 떠서
美와 哀愁에 느끼면서 그냥
茫茫한 水平線을 차서
際涯업는 世界에
어릴 때와 꿈에서 보든 世界에
浮萍가티 漂泊한 일이 잇서요. 東과 西에?
그리고 驚異와 嘆美속에서
日沒의 縹渺한 奇蹟을 볼 때
黃金은 大空을 떠
새는 際涯를 날아서
그리고 萬像이 그리로 쏠려 들어갈 때
아- 永遠과 無限의 바다여.
時間과 空間을 超越한 寞寞한 꿈 世界여하고
몹시 興奮할 때 狂喜할 때
돌연, 黑雲이 大海의 平和를 깨트리고
暴風이 물ㅅ결을 뒤집어 업흘 때에
그만 破船의 宣告를 당할 때
아- 다만 死와 絶望만 뵈일 때
아- 恐怖의 遺傳病이 全身을 狂亂케 할 때
우리의 衣服은 暴風雨에 찌키여
그리고 裸體가 되엇다.
아- 이 裸體의 羞恥를 가릴 양으로
서로 서로 눈을 감고 抱擁하엿다.
그리고 당신은 절다려
「내 사랑아 내 生命아 내 希望아」하고
가장 熱烈한 키쓰를 제 볼에 목에
그리자 서로 서로 共死의 地로 들어갈 때
제가 모든 秘密을 告白햇다는 말이
그래 다- 정말이셔요?
아- 詩人님.
이는 空想입니다. 당신의 저 혼자 生覺입니다.
이는 꿈입니다. 傳說의 破片을 同情함이외다.
저는 당신을 사랑하지 안어요.
꿈 世界 以外에서는 도모지 사랑 안이합니다.
(6월 5일)＜85＞
