Magazine Title: 개벽 제5호
Publication Date: November 1, 1920 (Inferred)
Article Title: 編輯을 마치고서 (After Finishing Editing)
Author: Unspecified (Likely Editor)
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice / Editorial Postscript)

Bewildering times (悾惚한 歲月) have become even more bewildering in our much-suffering land (山河), and this year too, autumn has already passed, and we enter the winter season. At this time, we wonder if the various esteemed readers (兄님) who love this journal are in good health and circumstance (氣體情祥), and if the affairs you oversee and manage are proceeding smoothly.

We have ended up delivering this journal's 5th issue to you, our readers, on November 1st, 5 days later than previously. The reason for changing the publication date in this way will already have been understood (諒察) by you through this issue's company notice (社告). Please just feel the difference in mood between the former days when you barely received the journal towards the end of the month, and today, when you suddenly encounter it on the first day of the new month.

Among the contents of this issue, topics such as "Discussing Our National Character (民族性)" and "An Urgent Motion (緊急動議) for Rural Improvement (農村改善)" are matters whose subjects themselves are significant, and moreover, they are not things that can be adequately discussed through the temporary reflections or opinions of a single individual. We have merely opened the beginning (端緖) of the discussion.

And, though it may be repeating the same thing, Rousseau's (루소) theory of equality (平等論) and view on education (敎育觀), as well as "China's (中華) Literary Revolution (文學革命) Centered on Mr. Hu Shih (胡適氏)," are especially worth reading. Furthermore, the creative work (創作) "Sacrificial Flower (犧牲花)" seems to be a piece that holds its own without much inferiority (遜色업슬 品) in the recent literary scene.

On this day of finishing the editing, the sky is high and the air is clear (天高氣淸), and the sound of wild geese heading south is also desolate (凄凉). The dark red leaves (紺붉은 한닙 두닙) of the sakura (사구라) tree outside the gate, which began to fly off and fall a few days ago, brush powerlessly against our study window (書窓).

We humbly pray (禱하야) to the gods above and below (上下神祗) that the spiritual (精神上) and material (物質上) lives of our various esteemed readers enter an ever more favorable state (佳境) moment by moment, and with this, we lay down our pen (閣筆하나이다).<152>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from Gaebyeok Issue No. 5 announces a delay and a permanent shift in the publication date from the end of the month to the first of the following month, asking readers to note the change in feeling this brings. It highlights key articles on Korean national character, rural reform, Rousseau's philosophy, Hu Shih's literary revolution in China, and a fictional piece titled "Sacrificial Flower," suggesting these are starting points for larger discussions. The piece concludes with a melancholic description of late autumn and a prayer for the readers' well-being. For historians, the text reveals the operational challenges (delays) and strategic decisions (publication date change) of publishing in colonial Korea. It also shows the intellectual currents of the time, engaging with Western philosophy (Rousseau), contemporary Chinese thought (Hu Shih), and pressing domestic issues like national identity and rural conditions, reflecting the concerns and aspirations of Korean intellectuals under Japanese rule. The seasonal imagery adds a layer of cultural sentiment.

編輯을 마치고서
悾惚한 歲月이 일 만흔 우리 山河에 더욱 悾惚하와 今年도 벌서 가을이 지내고 겨을 節氣에 드나이다. 이때에 本誌를 사랑하야 주시는 여러 兄님의 氣體情祥하시며 보시는 일 經營하시는 일도 順調로 잘 進行되나잇가.
그런데 저이는 旣往보다 5日間을 遲하야 11月 1日로써 本誌 5號를 여러분 압헤 드리게 되엇나이다. 이와 가티 發行 日字를 變更하온 것은 本號의 社告로써 여러분이 이미 諒察하셧슬 것이외다. 다못 가튼 本誌이오나 그 달 末頃에 겨우 對하던 前日과 새달 첫날에 문득 對하는 今日의 
氣分이 如何한 것만 늑겨주십시오.
本號 
中의 「우리의 民族性을 論하노라」와 「農村改善의 緊急動
議」의 兩題와 如함은 그 問題 自體가 重大함과 共히 一個人의 一時的 思究 或은 意見으로써 能히 論議할 바가 아니외다. 爲先 論議의 端緖를 開한 것뿐입니다.
그리고 다시 가튼 말이라면 식은 말이나 「루소」의 平等論·敎育觀과 밋 『胡適氏를 中心으로 한 中華의 文學革命』은 特이 一讀할만 하오며 
創作 「犧牲花」와 如함은 近日 創作界에 別로 遜色업슬 品인가 하나이다.
編輯을 마치는 이날 天高氣淸하며 南으로 向하는 기럭이 소리도 凄凉하온데 며칠 前부터 날아 떨어지기 始作한 門 압 사구라의 紺붉은 한닙 두닙이 힘업시 우리 書窓을 스치나이다.
삼가 上下神祗에 禱하야 여러 兄님의 精神
上 또는 物質上의 살림이 刻一刻 佳境에 들기만 願하오며 이만 閣筆하나이다.＜152＞
