기사제목	諸名士의 朝鮮女子 解放觀에 對한 余의 疑問 (My Doubts Regarding Various Notables' Views on the Liberation of Korean Women)
필자	辛泰嶽
기사형태	논설 (Editorial/Argumentative Essay)

Whether it is the influence of the new trends or arises from the concept of equality of personhood, recently in our society too, the number of those who recognize (?) women's personhood and call for their equality has increased, which I greatly rejoice in and welcome. However, regarding the slogan "women's liberation" (女子解放?), I cannot help but harbor deep suspicion, and moreover, I speak of the lack of thoroughness in its meaning. Why is this so? It is because we men (myself and others) possess absolutely nothing with which to liberate women. In other words, that men and women are equal in personhood is a principle ordained by Heaven. That men can absolutely neither restrict nor liberate this is what I mean. In this sense, if men were to liberate women, meaning if men had the right to be the liberators of women, this would still be a male-centric social system as before. I think it is a clear fact that under such a social system, men and women cannot possess absolute freedom and equality with each other.

Furthermore, there must certainly be a reason for liberating women. If it is said that women lack originally possessed equal rights and gain these through men's liberation, this would truly be temporary and, like fire, obviously not permanent. Also, if it is said that their originally possessed equal rights were deprived by men midway, and now that women have become self-aware, they seek to recover these, then this is not liberation but the restoration of rights (復權) by women themselves.

While the purport is thus, in Volume 4 of this magazine, regarding the opinions of various notables on the view of Korean women's liberation, some advocated the argument that it is still too early, some attached theories of limitation, and some proposed the theory of absolute liberation, showing truly that under each person's different viewpoint, the conclusions did not converge. For example, to simply cite one phrase each, Mr. Yu Pyŏngmin's "Liberation is not an absolute urgent task," and
Mr. Han Kyusŏl's <87> "Today is the preparatory period for liberation," etc.—even setting aside such theories that claim even the spirit of liberation is premature—theories of limitation like Mr. Yu Kŭn's "Liberate within an appropriate scope" made me feel keenly that they excessively disregard women's personhood and view them as mechanical animals. Next, regarding Mr. Yu Sŏngjun's "Liberation is already not the issue,"
the statement beginning with the dragon's head, "Regardless of the person, people are absolutely equal. If one has the right to equality, one has absolute freedom," ending with the snake's tail, "Korea too, from now on, should respect humanitarian justice and grant appropriate personhood to women," caused me great regret for its lack of thoroughness in intention. Why is this? If everyone possesses the right to equality, how can men grant personhood to women?

Therefore, how can men liberate women? That is, this phrase "liberation" clearly cannot be used as a term for realizing women's personal freedom and equality. However, what is excessively daunting is that the issue of liberation is something discussed by the prominent figures of our society, and not only that, it is terminology that is one of the major issues stirring the entire world, so for someone like me, who knows nothing, to criticize and refute it, might it not be too presumptuous? But again, this is not criticism; I attach my main purpose, which is merely to state and assert my opinion and to wish to respectfully receive and hear the results (solution) of sincere research, and with that, I stop.
(From Unni-dong) <88>

**Summary**

Sin T'aeak argues that the term "women's liberation" (女子解放), as used by male intellectuals, is fundamentally flawed because men do not possess the authority to "grant" liberation or equality, which are inherent rights. He contends that using this term perpetuates a male-centric view where men are bestowing rights, rather than acknowledging women's inherent equality. He suggests that if rights were previously denied, the appropriate concept is "restoration of rights" (復權) achieved by women themselves. The article reveals the nuances within the early Korean feminist discourse, showing internal critique among intellectuals regarding the framing of women's rights. For historians, it highlights the conceptual struggle between viewing women's rights as something to be granted versus an inherent status to be reclaimed, reflecting the complexities of adopting modern ideas within a patriarchal colonial context.

諸名士의 朝鮮女子 解放觀에 對한 余의 疑問
						辛泰嶽
新風潮의 影響인지 아니 人格平等의 觀念에서 出함인지 如何間 近日에 와서 우리 社會에서도 女子의 人格을 是認(?)하야 그 平等됨을 呌呼하는 者만 짐은 甚히 깃버하고 歡迎하는 바이올시다. 그러나 그 標語인 女子解放(?)이라는데 對하야는 甚히 疑心함을 마지아니할 뿐 아니라 그 意義의 不徹底함을 말합니다. 웨그러냐하면 우리 男子(余나 其他)는 絶對로 女子를 解放할 아모것도 가지지 못한 까닭이올시다. 다시 말하면 男女가 人格上 平等됨은 天定한 原理이라. 이것을 男子가 拘束하며 或은 解放할 수는 絶對로 업다함이올시다. 이 意味에 잇서서 萬若 男子가 女子를 解放하는 即 男子가 女子에 對한 解放者가 될 權利가 잇다 하면 이것은 如前한 男子 本位의 社會制度라. 이러한 社會制度 알에에서는 男女가 서로 絶對의 自由와 平等을 가질 수 업슴은 明瞭한 事實이라 생각합니다.
또한 女子를 解放함에는 반듯이 理由가 잇슬지니 萬若 女子가 元有의 平等權이 업는 것은 男子의 解放에서 이것을 어떠타 하면 이것은 實로 一時的이오 永久치 못할 것은 如火한 事實이겟지오. 또한 元有의 平等權을 中途에서 男子의 剝奪을 當하얏다가 女子가 自覺한 今日에 이르러서 이것을 回復하자 함이다 하면 이것은 解放이 아니라 女子自體의 復權이겟습니다.
意致
가 이러한데 本誌 第4號에 朝鮮女子 解放觀에 對한 諸名士의 意見을 揭함에 或은 是의 尙早論을 唱하얏스며 或은 是에 制限說을 附하얏스며 或은 絶對解放論을 提唱하야 實로 各人各樣의 見解下에 그 結論이 歸一치 아니함을 보앗습니다. 例로 簡單히 1句式을 擧示하면 劉秉敏 氏의 『解放은 絶對的 急務가 아니라.』와 
韓圭卨 氏의＜87＞ 『今日은 解放準備時代』라는 等 即 解放이라는 精神까지도 尙早하다는 說 等은 度外하고라도 柳瑾 氏의 『相當한 範圍內에서 解放하라.』는 制限論 가튼 것은 넘우나 女子의 人格을 無視하고 機械的 動物視함이 甚함을 感하얏습니다. 그 다음 兪星濬 氏 『解放은 旣히 問題가 아니오.』라는 가운
대 『어던 사람을 勿論하고 사람은 絶對平等입니다. 平等의 權利가 有하면 絶對 自由가 有합니다.』라는 龍頭에 『朝鮮도 今後로는 人道正義를 尊崇하야 女子에게도 相當한 人格을 付與하야써』라는 蛇尾엿슴은 그 意思의 不徹底함에 甚히 遺憾하는 바이올시다. 위 그런가 하면 누구나 다 平等權이 잇는데 어떠케 男子가 女子에게 人格을 付與하겟습니까?
딸아서 男子가 어찌 女子를 解放하겟습니까? 卽 이 解放이라는 句語는 女子의 人格上 自由平等을 實現함에의 術語로 用할 수 업슴을 明케 합니다. 그런데 넘우 惶悚한 것은 解放問題는 적어도 우리 社會에 諍諍한 諸位의 論한 바이고 뿐만 아니라 온世界가 떠드는 大問題 中 一인 術語인데 이것을 아모 것도 모르는 제가 論斥함은 넘우나 唐突치 아니한가(?) 생각합니다. 그러나 다시 이것은 批評이 아니라 저의 意見을 陳述하며 主張함에 不過하고 眞
摯한 硏究의 結果(解決)를 밧들어 듯기를 願하는 本旨임을 附하고 그만 끄칩니다.
(雲泥洞에서)＜88＞
