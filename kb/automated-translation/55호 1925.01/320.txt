餘言 (Postscript)

Whether it is substantial or poor is left to the reader's judgment, but anyway, the Editor does not feel particularly dissatisfied. Why is that? Because satisfaction knows no bounds. Therefore, this is said for self-consolation.

Speaking of what has passed, regarding last year, the Year of the Rat (甲子年), this journal firstly lacked essays. Moreover, the literary section was weak. This was a mutual regret between the readers and the Editor. However, can it remain like that forever!

As you will see, starting with Mr. Yi Tonhwa's (李敦化氏) "Chŏkchajuŭi" (赤子主義 - Doctrine of the Innocent Child) at the beginning of the volume, the essays by Messrs. Kim Kijin (金基鎭) and Pak Hyŏngbyŏng (朴衡秉) seem weightier and newer, as befits being one year older. And Mr. An Chaehong's (安在鴻氏) "The World Standing on the Line of Reaction and Its Trend" (反動線上에 立한 世界와 밋그 趨勢) is said to be definitely worth reading, both in quality and quantity.

Needless to say about the Miscellany (雜俎), but in the Literary section, it is no exaggeration to say that the formation is well-ordered, with all the stars of the literary world taking up their pens for six creative works*, besides essays and critical reviews, which is said to be the great pride of the *Kaebyŏk* New Year issue.

One way or another, I express my gratitude to all contributors (投稿諸氏). In this year-end period when everyone, individually and socially, is extremely busy (눈코 뜰새 업는), the fact that they still exerted themselves for this journal deserves thanks beyond thanks, for which I bow deeply in gratitude (拱手謝禮). Among these, due to the deadlines (期日) and page limits (頁數), it is mutually regrettable that Mr. Tonggok's (東谷氏) New Year essay, Mr. Cho Myŏnghŭi's (趙明熙氏) story "Into the Ground" (땅속으로), and Mr. Yang Chudong's (梁柱東氏) poem "I am a child of this nation" (나는 이 나랏자손이외다) have all been postponed to the next issue. Also, Okch'ŏnsaeng's (玉川生) History of Social Thought will be continued from the February issue, and notably, due to the short period for submissions to the readers' column, we have decided to publish them fully in the February issue.

Starting with the New Year issue, it is a joyous matter for both the readers and us that the literary section of this journal will be directly associated with Hoewŏl Pak Yŏnghŭi (懷月 朴英熙君). How could the connection between the gentleman and this journal, and between this journal and the readers, have ever been neglectful (踈忽)? Nevertheless, how can we not feel even more delighted and happy!
-Ch'unp'a (春坡)-
<100>

**Summary**

The editor (signed Ch'unp'a) reflects on the past year's publication of *Kaebyŏk*, admitting shortcomings, particularly the lack of essays and a weak literary section. However, the editor expresses satisfaction with the current New Year issue (No. 55, Jan 1925), highlighting strong essays by Yi Tonhwa, Kim Kijin, Pak Hyŏngbyŏng, and An Chaehong, and a revamped literary section featuring numerous contributions. Apologies are made for postponing works by Tonggok, Cho Myŏnghŭi, and Yang Chudong due to deadlines and space. The editor also announces that Pak Yŏnghŭi will be newly involved with the literary section. This postscript is interesting to historians as it provides insight into the editorial process, content priorities (essays, literature), and the network of prominent intellectuals and writers (like An Chaehong, Pak Yŏnghŭi, Yang Chudong) contributing to a leading Korean journal during the Japanese colonial period, reflecting the vibrant, albeit constrained, cultural and intellectual life.

餘言
충실과 빈약은 독자의 판단에 맷기리니와 엇잿든 編者로서는 그닥 불만은 안이 늣긴다. 왜 그런고 하니 만족이란 限이 업다는 말이다. 故로 自慰삼아 하는 말이다.
지냇스니 말이지 甲子年 中의 本誌는 첫재 논문이 부족하얏다. 더군다나 文藝便이 빈약하얏다. 이것은 독자와 編者間 相互의 遺感이엇다. 그러나 언제까지든지 그럴 수야 잇스랴!

보면 알려니와 卷頭의 李敦化氏의 『赤子主義』를 비릇하야 金基鎭 朴衡秉 諸氏의 논문은 한 살 더 먹은이만치 묵직하고 새로워 보인다. 그리고 安在鴻氏의 『反動線上에 立한 世界와 밋그 趨勢』는 質로나 量으로나 반듯히 一讀의 値가 잇다고 한다.

雜俎는 말할 것 업고 文藝便에 잇서서 文壇明星이 모다 붓을 드러 創作 六* 이외에 隨想과 論評이 알울러 陣形이 整正한 것은 과언이 안이라 開闢 新年號의 큰 자랑이라고 한다.

이러커나 저리커나 投稿諸氏에게 功을 謝한다. 개인으로나 사회로나 두루 눈코 뜰새 업는 이 歲末에 잇서서 그래도 本誌를 위하야 힘을 다해 주심은 감사이상의 감사로 拱手謝禮한다. 그런 중에도 期日이 期日이오 頁數가 頁數인지라 東谷氏의 新年論文과 趙明熙氏의 「땅속으로」의 소설과 梁柱東氏의 「나는 이 나랏자손이외다」의 詩를 다-來號로 미루게 된 것이 서로 섭섭한 일이다. 그리고 玉川生의 社會思想史도 2월호부터 계속되게 되얏고 특히 독자문단은 발표의 기간이 短함에 鑑하야 2월호로써 충분히 발표하기로 하얏다.

新年號부터 本誌 文藝便은 懷月 朴英熙君과 직접 인연을 맷게 됨이 독자와 共히 즐거운 일이다. 君과 本誌 本誌와 讀者間의 인연이 從來-ㄴ들 엇지 踈忽하얏스랴마는 그래도 한층 더 반갑고 깃븐데야 엇지하랴!
-春坡-
＜100＞
