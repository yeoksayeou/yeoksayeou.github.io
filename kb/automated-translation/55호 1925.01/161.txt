**Summary**

This brief notice lists the newly appointed staff members—reporters, general affairs managers, branch heads, and distribution point chiefs—for various regional offices (支社), sub-branches (分社), and distribution points (分賣所) of the magazine *Kaebyŏk* (開闢) as of January 1, 1925. The listed locations include Kaesŏng, Wŏnsan, Tanch'ŏn, Namwŏn, Anju, Mansŏng, P'ihyŏn, and Anbyŏn. For historians, this article provides concrete evidence of the magazine's organizational network and geographical reach across Korea during the colonial period. The specific names and locations can help map the personnel structure and distribution system of a significant Korean publication in the mid-1920s.
