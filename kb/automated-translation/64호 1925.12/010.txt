Magazine Title: 개벽 제64호
Publication Date: 1925년 12월 01일
Article Title: 謹謝二千萬同胞 (Deep Gratitude to Twenty Million Compatriots)
Author: N/A
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Announcement/Editorial Postscript)

We respectfully inform the twenty million brothers and sisters of Chosŏn's 13 Provinces, 12 Prefectures, 2 Islands, and 218 Counties.

Our 13-Province survey project (13道踏査事業), which we have dragged on for three years, although unsatisfactory and insufficient, has now almost come to an end.

From the beginning of this work until its end, we have tried infinitely hard, with what little strength we have, to somehow present the entirety of Chosŏn in decline (頹廢) and Chosŏn in revival (부흥) before our compatriots. But, as mind and work always failed to align, we have ended it with this much dissatisfaction.

Meanwhile, when we think about how to express our gratitude to the twenty million brothers [and sisters] who gave us so much support, both directly and indirectly, materially and spiritually, we only feel choked up (抑塞).

Through these pages (紙面), we respectfully express our thanks for your past kindness (厚意) and request even more affection (愛顧) in the future. <1>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the magazine *Kaebyŏk* announces the near completion of a three-year nationwide survey project covering all administrative divisions of Korea (Chosŏn). The editors express humility about the project's shortcomings despite their efforts to capture the state of both "Chosŏn in decline" and "Chosŏn in revival." They extend profound gratitude to the "twenty million compatriots" for their material and spiritual support and ask for continued affection. For historians, this piece offers insight into nationalist intellectual activities during the colonial period, specifically the ambitious effort by *Kaebyŏk* to document the entirety of Korea. The mention of "Chosŏn in decline and Chosŏn in revival" reflects the complex reality and contemporary perceptions of Korean society under Japanese rule, acknowledging both hardship and potential for resurgence. The specific enumeration of administrative districts (13 Provinces, 12 Prefectures, 2 Islands, 218 Counties) underscores the comprehensive scope of their intended survey.

謹謝二千萬同胞
삼가 朝鮮 13道 12府 2島 218郡 2천만 형제자매에게 告함니다.
저의가 3개년이나 두고 끌고 끌어오든 13道踏査事業은 불만족하고 불충분하나마 이제 거의 끄틀 막엇슴니다.
저의는 이 일을 시작할 때부터 이 일이 끗날 때까지 엇더케 하든지 頹廢의 조선 부흥의 조선 그것의 總量을 그대로 들어 동포의 前에 소개하려도 업는 힘이나마 애는 무한히 쓰노라고 하얏슴니다. 마는 항상 마음과 일은 일치되지 못하야 이만한 불만을 두고 끄틀 막엇슴니다. 這間에 저이들을 위하야 직접 간접으로 또는 물질과 정신으로 만흔 후원을 주신 이천만 형제에게 무엇으로써 감사를 표할넌지 생각하면 抑塞할 뿐이외다. 紙面을 통하야 삼가 과거의 厚意를 謝하오며 장래 더 만흔 愛顧를 비나이다. ＜1＞
