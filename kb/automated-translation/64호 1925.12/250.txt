Concluding Remarks
Whether we lived well or poorly, another year is passing anyway, what can be done! Reflecting on the year, it's not that there weren't joyful things, but there are more sorrowful thoughts. Considering the principle of evolution, one might say there must have been that much harvest, but there is also the law of elimination (淘汰), so wasn't there also that much loss? This is the way of the world (世道) and human affairs (人事). Let's send off the departing year and greet the coming year again-.

As this is the year-end issue (送年號), reflecting on the year made editing truly difficult. We tried to fully assess all circles (各界), but that also seemed commonplace, so we selected only a few important items and stopped. One year of the political situation (政局), one year of the economy (經濟), one year of thought (思想)—we know that alone will be sufficient for our reference.

The provincial survey (各道 踏査), which had been dragged out for three years, is considered finished within the year Eulchuk (乙丑年 - 1925), although it is incomplete. As the common saying goes, it's bittersweet (시원 섭섭하다). In the meantime (這間), there were certainly things where our ability was insufficient (毋論力不及) and also many irresponsible things. Since the task itself (原體) was large and broad, even though we exerted our best efforts, it ended up causing such dissatisfaction. We apologize (謝한다) to the twenty million brethren of the thirteen provinces (13道).

Moreover, this time the Chŏnbuk survey (전북답사) truly became insincere and irresponsible. (Chŏnnam (全南) too, though.) Because we intended to finish it within the year Eulchuk (乙丑年) and start new work from the New Year, we rushed into it like fire despite the short time. We can only hope for the tolerance (寬容) of the people of Chŏnbuk (全北).

There is nothing more to say about the past. Let's resolve to muster new strength (振作) from the New Year onwards and open up an even newer phase. Above all, let "Kaebyŏk" (「開闢」) itself do so. Let's try to do so starting from the New Year issue (新年號). Let's muster new energy (氣力) and open a new phase.

It goes without saying (毋論) that the New Year issue will be a double-sized issue (倍大號) according to custom (例). What will be included to make it worthy of a New Year issue cannot be said yet. There's nothing to do but wait, thinking, "It must be tremendous!" Like previous years, there will certainly (毋論) be a New Year "gift" (선물) too. As New People in the New Year (新年 新人), let's greet the New Year's New Kaebyŏk (新年 新開闢)-.

Let me add one more word regarding the year Eulchuk (乙丑年). It is a request to send in more local articles starting from the New Year. It is regrettable that there were few of them in the year Eulchuk. We earnestly request this of the local branch offices (支分社). Let's pay attention to local articles-.

We sincerely pray for "All wishes come true" (萬事如意) starting from the New Year, and with this, we bring the year Eulchuk to a close.

**Summary**

This editorial postscript reflects on the passing year (1925, Eulchuk) with mixed feelings, acknowledging both progress and shortcomings. The editor notes the difficulty of summarizing the year but highlights reviews of politics, economy, and thought. A major point is the bittersweet completion of the three-year provincial survey series, with apologies offered for its incompleteness, especially concerning the rushed Chŏnbuk and Chŏnnam sections. Looking forward, the editor promises renewed effort for *Kaebyŏk* magazine in the New Year, including a special double-sized issue with a gift, and makes an urgent plea for more local news contributions from branch offices. For historians, the text offers insight into the editorial challenges and ambitions of a major Korean cultural magazine during the colonial period, its self-assessment of a significant long-term project (the provincial surveys), and its direct engagement with readers and regional branches, reflecting efforts to foster national consciousness and connection despite limitations.

기사형태	사고·편집후기

餘言
잘 살던 못 살앗던 여하간 또한 해 넘어가는데야  엇져랴! 일년을 회고하니 깃븐 일도 업는 바 안이지만 비장한 생각이 더 만타. 진화의 원칙을 생각하면 그만한 수확이 잇서스리라 하겟지만 淘汰되는 법도 잇스니 그만한 손실도 잇섯슬 것 안이냐. 이것이 世道요 人事란 말이다. 가는 해 보내고 오는 해 또 마저 보자-.

送年號 인지라 1년을 회고 하랴닛가 편집이 실로 곤란하엿다. 各界를 통하야 충분히 감정 해 보려 햇스나 그 亦 예사 가태서 중요한 멧 가지만 추려 보고 마럿다. 政局의 1년 경제의 1년 사상의 1년 그것만도 吾人의 참고에 넉넉할 줄 안다.

3년이나 두고 끌고 끌어 오든 各道 踏査는 불충분 하나마 乙丑年內로써 끗츨 막은 셈이다. 常말로 시원 섭섭하다. 這間에는 毋論力不及한 일도 잇고 또 무책임한 일도 만하섯다. 原體-일이 크고 또 넓엇는지라 최선의 힘을 다한다 하나 그러케 불만을 끼치고 말앗다. 13道 2천만 형제에게 謝한다.

더구나 이번 전북답사는 정말 무성의 무책임 하게 되얏다. (全南도 그렇지만) 乙丑년으로써 끗츨 내고 新年부터 새 일을 하랴닛가 短時日 임을 불구하고 불치듯 이러케 덤비인 것이다. 全北 人士의 관용을 바랄 뿐이다.

지난 일은 더 말할 것이 업다. 압흐로 新年 부터 다시 새로운 힘을 振作하야 한 층 더 새로운 국면을 열기로 하자. 무엇보다 「開闢」 이것이 그러케 하기로 하자. 新年號 부터 그러케 해 보자. 새 氣力을 振作하야 새 국면을 열기로 하자.

新年號는 例에 의하야 倍大號로 됨은 毋論이다. 엇더한 것이 실려서 新年號담게 될는지는 아즉은 말할 수 업다. 「그저 굉장하려니!」 하고 기다리는 수 밧게 업다. 예년과 가티 新年 「선물」 도 毋論 잇슬 것이다. 新年 新人으로서 新年 新開闢을 맛기로 하자-.

乙丑년에 잇서 한마디 더 해 두자. 新年부터는 지방 기사를 만히 보내라는 말이다. 乙丑년에 잇서서는 그것이 적어 유감이엿단 말이다. 지방 각 支分社에 간졀히 원한다. 지방 기사에 주의하자-.

삼가 새해부터 「萬事如意」 하기를 빌고 乙丑년은 이것으로 끗막고 만다.
＜54＞
