**Summary**

This company notice from the December 1, 1925 issue of *Kaebyŏk* magazine details organizational changes in its branch network during November 1925. It announces the establishment of a new sub-branch in P'yŏnggang, the re-establishment of the Wŏnsan branch, and changes to the Yŏngdong sub-branch, listing the locations, coverage areas, and key personnel (including advisors) for each. For historians, this notice offers insights into the organizational structure and geographical reach of a significant Korean magazine during the colonial period. The listing of specific individuals, including local figures serving as advisors, hints at the magazine's efforts to build networks and integrate with local communities or elites across different regions of Korea.
