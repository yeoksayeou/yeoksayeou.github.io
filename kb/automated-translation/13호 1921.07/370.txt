Article Type: 시 (Poem)

(Feeling of the Past, Prize Essay Announcement New Poetry 3rd Place)
						Pyŏlsaem Han Hyŏnsang

P!
Oh! My past love!
Oh! Thread of love (戀絲), tougher than anything!
Oh! Love's affection (戀情), that doubted mulberry fields turning into blue sea (桑田碧海)!
Oh! Dream that was happiness in the paradise (樂園) of the past (過去)!
Does that, instead, become the seed of heartbreak (斷腸)!

P!
Paralyzed (痲痺) by Satan's poison gas (毒瓦斯)!
Memory (追憶) of happiness (幸福) that will never come again!
Oh! My chest (胸部)?
Pitiableness that is the ruins (廢墟) of reality (現實)!
First startlement of an innocent (天眞) young spirit (靈)!
Oh! What to do with this wound (傷處)!
The world is all spring
But in my body, it is still winter... .

Critique: A short piece depicting the sorrow (哀傷) of one heartbroken. It would have been <22> better if it were richer in originality (獨自性). Please strive to avoid using too many commonplace words (文字). <23>

**Summary**

This third-place winning poem from *Kaebyŏk* magazine expresses the intense sorrow and disillusionment of a speaker lamenting a lost love, referred to as "P." The speaker contrasts past blissful memories with the bleakness of present reality, feeling paralyzed and wounded while the world seems to move on ("The world is all spring / But in my body, it is still winter"). A brief critique follows, suggesting the poem could benefit from greater originality and less common phrasing. From a historical perspective, the poem exemplifies the emergence of personal, lyrical themes in early modern Korean poetry during the colonial period, focusing on individual emotional experience rather than solely collective or nationalistic ones. The critique reflects contemporary literary standards valuing originality and careful diction within the "New Poetry" (新詩) movement, offering a glimpse into the development of modern Korean literary aesthetics.

						별샘 韓晛相
P야!
오! 나의 옛사랑아!
오! 무엇보다도 질기던 戀絲야!
오! 桑田碧海를 疑心하던 戀情아!
過去의 樂園에서 幸福이던 꿈아!
돌이어 그것이 斷腸의 씨가 되노나!

P야!
사탄의 毒瓦斯에 痲痺 되엿고나!
永遠히 오지 안흘 幸福의 追憶아!
오! 나의 胸部야?
現實의 廢墟인 애처롭음아!
天眞의 어린 靈의 첫 놀램아!
오! 이 傷處를 어찌할고!
世上은 모도 다 봄이다
그러나 내 몸엔 아즉도 겨을... .
