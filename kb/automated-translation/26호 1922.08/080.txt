**Body Text Translation:**
Population of Ch'ŏngjin at the Beginning of July

Koreans 1,861 households, 10,774 people
Japanese 1,021 households, 4,949 people
Chinese approx. 300 people
Russians 5 people
Total 16,000 people<74>

**Summary**

This short report provides population statistics for the city of Ch'ŏngjin as of early July 1922, broken down by nationality. It lists the number of households and individuals for Koreans and Japanese, and the approximate number of individuals for Chinese and Russians, summing to a total of 16,000 people. For historians, this data offers a snapshot of the demographic makeup of a significant northern port city during the Japanese colonial period, clearly illustrating the substantial Korean majority alongside a large Japanese settler population and smaller Chinese and Russian communities, reflecting colonial administration and regional migration patterns.
