-編輯後告- (Editorial Postscript)

□ We had planned to make this issue the "Thought Critique" (思想批判) and "Critique of Contemporaries" (時人月旦) issue, but we have decided to postpone the "Critique of Contemporaries" for now.
The reason for this is primarily that we intended to clearly show the ideological tendencies of those prominent in current society, thereby clarifying the divisions within our intellectual world (思想界). However, in reality, when one looks closely, the vast majority of contemporaries have not yet established clear ideological boundaries and are in the midst of daily changes. Therefore, there is no need to rashly label them, saying "so-and-so is such-and-such," and pigeonhole them prematurely. Thus, we have temporarily suspended it and decided to observe how their thoughts continue to evolve. Additionally, even while discussing pure thought, the article concerning Christian (基督敎) thought could not be written because the assigned writer (맡은 사람) became ill.

□ As advertised in the previous issue, for the sake of our literary world (文壇) and also considering the benefit (健益) of those interested in this area, we are soliciting novels and scripts (脚本) for a contest, investing a particularly large sum (巨額). We hope that those with ideas will pick up their pens as soon as inspiration strikes.

□ The manuscript from Mr. Pyŏn Hŭi-yong (卞熙瑢) in Tokyo (東京) arrived early, but it is extremely regrettable that we could not publish it in this issue either because it was "incomplete" (未完).

□ In some ways, this whole damn world (이 놈의 世上) seems to be stirring uneasily (음음하는 듯십다). Accordingly, the next issue will be the "World Turmoil Issue" (世界風雲號). There will also be essays (隨筆) from various writers welcoming the fine spring season (陽春佳節) and a supplement featuring South Ch'ungch'ŏng Province (忠南道號).

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the March 1924 issue of *Kaebyŏk* explains changes to the planned content. A feature critiquing contemporary figures was postponed because the editors felt people's ideologies were still evolving and shouldn't be prematurely labeled. An article on Christian thought was also omitted due to the writer's illness. The editors promote a literary contest for novels and scripts and apologize for not publishing an incomplete manuscript from Pyŏn Hŭi-yong in Tokyo. The next issue is announced as a "World Turmoil Issue," reflecting global unease, and will include spring essays and a supplement on South Ch'ungch'ŏng Province.

From a historical perspective, this piece is interesting as it reflects the cautious intellectual climate of colonial Korea, where defining ideological stances was complex and perhaps risky. The postponement of the "Critique of Contemporaries" hints at the fluidity or sensitivity surrounding public figures' affiliations. The announcement of a "World Turmoil Issue" shows awareness of and engagement with international affairs. The literary contest and the regional focus (Ch'ungnamdo) highlight efforts to cultivate national culture and explore local identities under colonial rule. The mention of an individual in Tokyo points to the networks connecting intellectuals across the Japanese empire.

-編輯後告-
□이번에 「思想批判」「時人月旦」號를 하기로 햇섯는데, 時人月旦은 爲先延期하기로햇다
그 닭은 主로 現社會의 表面에선 사람들의 思想傾向을 明示하야 우리 思想界의 分野를 明瞭히 하려함이엿는데 事實로, 다 닥처보면 時人의 多大數는 아즉 思想 上 分界가 確立치 못하야, 나날히 變邊되는 中에 잇는바, 여긔에 가부야히 손을 대여 그 사람은 엇더엇더한 者이라하고 미리 판박아 노을 必要가 업는 故로 爲先中止하고, 그 사람들의 思想이 變遷되여가는 모양을 보기로 한다. 그리고 純思想만을 니야기하는 中에도 基督敎의 思想에 關해서는 마른 사람어 病을 들켯기 문에 쓰지 못햇다.
□前號에 廣告한바와가티, 우리는 우리의 文壇을 爲하고 는 그 便에 생각을 가진이의 健益을 생각하야, 特히 巨額을 드려가면서 小說,, 脚本을 懸賞으로 募集함이니 생각잇는 이는 생각나는 卽時로 붓을 잡으면 됴켓다
□東京 卞熙瑢 氏의 原稿가 일즉히 到着되엿스나「未完」으로 해서 이번 號에도 揭載치 못함은 至極히 未安한 일이다.
□엇더케 보면 이 놈의 世上의 全體로 음음하는 듯십다. 이에 써 來號는 世界風雲號로하게 되얏다. 그리고 陽容佳節을 맛는 여러 文人의 隨筆과 忠南道號의 附錄이 잇겟다.
