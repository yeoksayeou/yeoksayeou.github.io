Fragments from the Border Region
						A Reporter at the Border

Recently in Hamgyŏng Pukto (咸北), regulations for vocational youth associations, drafted by the Hamgyŏng Pukto Provincial Governor (咸北道知事), have been issued. Youth associations in various regions have begun to critique these regulations, and it has become a significant issue with arguments for and against. The gist of the Hamgyŏng Pukto Governor's assertion is that Chosŏn (朝鮮) is an agricultural nation, a poor country, so Chosŏn youth should not concern themselves with political issues or social movements, but just dig the earth and barely even breathe loudly. On the other hand, the main point that the youth here do not accept (首肯치 안는 要旨) is that even if they were to engage in vocational work, it cannot be done apart from the political background or institutional relationships, so shouldn't their path be naturally separate? This is the major issue near the border (挽近國境) recently. What will happen in the future?

In Hamgyŏng Pukto, there is the Hamgyŏng Pukto Youth Group Federation (咸北靑年團聯合會). On October 27th last year, coinciding with the completion ceremony for the Hamgyŏng North and South Province Products Exhibition Hall (咸鏡南北道 物産陳列舘 落成式), a joint conference was held in Nanam (羅南). At that time, the Nanam Police Station (羅南署) regarded it with extreme displeasure. However, it was somehow barely opened, and resolutions were made on all matters. This conference plans to hold another meeting in Kyŏngsŏng-ŭp (鏡城邑) sometime in January to make decisions on everything on a large scale, and they are conducting a fervent campaign. How will the authorities (當局) act this time? Still, having eaten at least one more bowl of rice cake soup than last time, perhaps their views (所見) will be somewhat different. It will be quite a spectacle (可觀可觀).

In Nanam, the center of Hamgyŏng Pukto, even two years ago, one couldn't witness such things as associations (會), but counting the associations and groups (會團) that have recently emerged, there are 14 or 15. Let's list some: Nail Youth Association (羅一靑年會), Kwanbuk Buddhist Association (關北佛敎會), Buddhist Youth Association (佛敎靑年會), Women's Association (婦人會), Christian Church (基督敎會), Diligence Youth Association (勉勵靑年會), Boys' Association (少年會), Uri Friendship Association (우리 親睦會), Labor Savings Association (勞働貯蓄會), Craftsmen's Friendship Association (職工親睦會), Labor Union (勞働組合), Logging Sawyers' Cooperative (引鉅組合), Women's Night School Association (婦女夜學會), Confucian Scholars' Association (儒林會) – there are this many. Magnificent, magnificent (壯哉壯哉)!

The Nanam Kindergarten (羅南幼稚園) is finally getting established, even forming a preparatory committee (期成會). Last spring, Mr. Song U-sun (宋禹淳氏) immediately contributed a sum of 300 Won (一金 三百圓), and while the Nanam Christian Church (羅南基督敎) kept saying they would do it, a full year passed, and the matter seemed to fade into uncertainty (有耶無耶). However, about a month ago, the Kwanbuk Buddhist Association (關北佛敎會) and the Nanam Christian Church vied to undertake it, but the executives (간부) reached an agreement, combined their efforts under the name "Nanam Kindergarten," and held a preparatory committee meeting within Nanam Elementary School (羅南普校) on January 6th. Now it is set to proceed smoothly. Very fortunate, very fortunate (多幸當幸).

Money panic! Where is this money panic (錢慌) not severe? But it is particularly severe in Hamgyŏng Pukto. However, one part, Ŏdaejin (漁大津) in Kyŏngsŏng County (鏡城郡), has newly become a gold village (黃金村). In less than a month, 170,000 Won worth of gold sprang from the sea. That is, mackerel (鯖魚) leaped out. In a place with fewer than 400 households and a population of 3,000-4,000, they are living it up like spreading mats under Namsan, wrapping new janggu drums around their heads, seated and just saying, "Pour and drink!" Also, hundreds of thousands of *sŏk* of soybeans (豆太) have come down, money is boiling (돈이 끌코), and an ice company called Sŏnbin Sanghoe (鮮氷商會) is said to employ 2,000 laborers (人夫) daily. From here, over 1,000 Won flows into Ŏdaejin daily via these laborers. But isn't it frightening? The development of Ŏdaejin, on one hand, leads to the development of restaurants (料理店) and houses of prostitution (色酒家). Caution, caution (注意注意).

Amidst this, through the dedication (誠意) of Messrs. Sŏk Hyŏng-gŭn (石亨根), An Yŏng-gŭn (安永根), Hyŏn Yong-gyu (玄用珪), Kang Sŏ-hwang (姜瑞璜), No Ch'i-su (廬致秀), Kim In-jun (金寅濬), Ch'oe Chi-nan (崔智爛), and Kim Chu-sŏng (金鑄成), 15,000 Won in donations (寄附) were collected to newly build a private elementary school (私立 普校), creating an institution to educate the young children (子弟). This is truly a blessing (幸福) for Ŏdaejin.<39>

**Summary**

This article from 1924 reports on various social and economic developments in the Hamgyŏng Pukto border region of Korea. It highlights a conflict between the provincial governor, who wants youth to focus solely on vocational training, and the youth groups, who believe social and political awareness is necessary. It notes the proliferation of civic associations in Nanam, the successful, cross-religious establishment of a kindergarten there, and the stark economic contrast between general hardship ("money panic") and the sudden, potentially destabilizing wealth brought to the fishing village of Ŏdaejin by a massive mackerel catch. Despite the boomtown vices emerging in Ŏdaejin, the article concludes positively by mentioning the community's effort to fund and build a new private school. For historians, the article offers insights into colonial administration's attempts to control youth activities, the burgeoning civil society (associations, education initiatives) among Koreans despite restrictions, the volatile nature of the colonial economy, and the social changes (both positive and negative) accompanying economic booms in specific localities. The tension between imposed directives and local agency is a key theme.

國境片片
						在國境一記者
近者 咸北에는 咸北道知事의 起案인 실업 청년회 준칙이 나서, 각지방 청년회에서는 제각금 준칙비평을 하기 시작하여, 曰是曰非로 중대한 문제가 되여잇다. 朝鮮은 농산국이다. 貧寒한 나라다, 그러면 朝鮮 청년 이 너희들은, 정치문제니 사회운동이니 무엇 무엇 할 것 업시, 땅이나 파먹어 가면서, 숨도 크게 쉴 것이 업다는 것이 咸北 知事의 주장의 요점이오. 설혹 실업을 해 먹는다 할지라도, 정치적 배경이나 제도의 관계를 떠나서는 할 수 업는 일인즉, 우리의 갈길은 스사로 따로 잇슬 것이 아니겟느냐 하는 것이, 이곳 청년의 首肯치 안는 要旨이다. 이것이 挽近國境의 一大 문제다. 장차 如何.
咸北에는 咸北靑年團聯合會가 잇다. 去 10월 27일이다. 咸鏡南北道 物産陳列舘 落成式을 거행하는 때를 따라 羅南에서 연합대회를 개최하엿다. 그때 羅南署에서는 심히 불쾌이 역엿다. 그러나 이렁저렁 간신히 열어, 모든 것을 결의하엿다. 이 대회가 정월중으로 鏡城邑에서 개최하여, 모든 것을 대대적으로 결의하겟다고 猛熱한 운동을 한다. 금번에는 당국에서 如何이 할까, 그래도 그때 보다는 떡국이라도 한 그릇 더 먹엇스니, 所見이 좀 다르겟지. 可觀可觀.
咸北에 중앙인 羅南에는 2년 전에도 會라는 구경을 할 수 업더니, 近者까지 일어난 會團을 손곱아 세면 14, 5개다. 어듸 좀 곱어보자, 羅一靑年會, 關北佛敎會, 佛敎靑年會, 婦人會, 基督敎會, 勉勵靑年會, 少年會, 우리 親睦會, 勞働貯蓄會, 職工親睦會, 勞働組合, 引鉅組合, 婦女夜學會, 儒林會, 이와 가티 만타. 壯哉壯哉.
羅南幼稚園은 요지간이야 바로되여, 期成會까지 되엿다. 작년 봄에 宋禹淳씨가 一金 300圓을 당장에 내어노면서부터, 羅南基督敎에서 한다한다 하면서, 1년을 훌젹 지나와 有耶無耶 중에 돌아갓던바, 月餘前이야 關北佛敎會와 羅南基督敎와 서로 하기를 다투다가, 간부 간부가 합의하야 「羅南幼稚園」이란 名下에서 힘을 합하여 去 1월 6일 羅南普校내에서 期成會를 열엇다. 이제야 원만이 되게 되엿다. 多幸當幸.
錢慌! 이 錢慌이 어느 곳이라고 심하지 아느리요만은, 특히 咸北에는 심하다. 그러나 일부인 鏡城郡 漁大津은 새로이 황금촌이 되엿다. 1개월 미만에 해중으로 17萬圓의 황금이 뛰여 나왓다. 즉 鯖魚가 뛰여 나왓다. 400戶 미만에 3, 4千명 인구는 南山아내 베닷칠나 하면서, 새장구 머리에 둘너 안져, 「부어라 먹자」고만 한다. 또 수십만석의 豆太가 내려와, 돈이 끌코, 鮮氷商會라는 어름회사가 잇서, 매일 人夫 2,000名式 쓴다고 한다. 이곳으로부터 매일 人夫등을 넘어 1,000圓 돈이 漁大津에 와서 깔린다. 그러나 무섭지 아느리오, 漁大津의 발전은, 一面으로 요리점의 발전, 色酒家의 발전이 된다. 注意注意.
이러한 中石亨根, 安永根 玄用珪 姜瑞璜 廬致秀 金寅濬 崔智爛 金鑄成 諸氏의 성의로, 15,000圓의 寄附를 모집하여, 사립 普校를 신축하고 어린 자제를 교육식힐 기관이 새로이 되엿스니, 이것은 참으로 漁大津의 幸福.＜39＞
