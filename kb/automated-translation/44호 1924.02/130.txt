Correction
In issue 43 of this magazine, on page 169, line 14, 车淳荣 (Ch'a Sun-yŏng) is corrected to 车荣淳 (Ch'a Yŏng-sun), and 曹百云 (Cho Paek-un) is corrected to 池百云 (Chi Paek-un).<72>

**Summary**

This brief notice serves as an erratum for the previous issue (No. 43) of the magazine *Kaebyŏk*. It corrects two names that were misspelled on page 169: Ch'a Sun-yŏng should be Ch'a Yŏng-sun, and Cho Paek-un should be Chi Paek-un. For historians, this small correction notice provides insight into the editorial practices of a major Korean magazine during the colonial period. The specific names corrected might also be significant if these individuals were notable figures, offering a data point about their activities or mentions in the press at the time. The use of older spelling conventions ('함니다' instead of '합니다') is also noteworthy for linguistic studies of the period.
