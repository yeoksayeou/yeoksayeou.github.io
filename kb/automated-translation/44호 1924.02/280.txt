Article Title:	餘言 (Epilogue/Closing Remarks)
Author:	(Not specified, likely editorial staff)
Article Type:	사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editorial Postscript)

This issue has become a magazine focused entirely on literature. By putting out this one issue, we intended to show a new spirit to the Korean literary scene, which is filled with a mood of vulgarity (卑野) and decadence (頹廢), but it did not turn out quite as we wished. However, it will surely be different from not having done it at all. And regarding future matters, how about we wait and see for a bit?

As much as possible, we planned to compile and publish the works of various writers in this issue, but due to page limitations, we could not do so. Tomorrow is limitless (明日 來無窮), so we have no choice but to look to the future.

We are truly sorry for not being able to serialize several continuing articles. However, they will certainly resume starting from the next issue. The work submitted by Brother Na Tohyang (羅稻香兄) could not be published in this issue because it was "Unfinished (未完)," and the manuscripts by Mr. Yi Ch'ŏngu (李靑愚) and Mr. Yi Ok (李鈺) arrived just after editing was completed. We apologize.

The upcoming March issue will specifically be a "Thought Issue (思想號)." We are confident that the contents, even based on what is currently planned, will be quite substantial.

The world truly changes quickly. It has changed quite a lot in the meantime. Mr. Lenin, who was a planet (惑星) of the 20th century, has truly passed away, and in Britain (英國), a Labour Party cabinet, unprecedented in its kind, has been formed. In Japan (日本), the government and the public (朝野) are in an uproar over the lèse-majesté incident (不敬事件) and the resulting issue of the formation of a special privilege cabinet as a reaction to this incident. In Korea (朝鮮), liberal-style (自由主義流) connection movements (聯結運動) and socialist-style (社會主義流) propaganda movements (宣傳運動) are tremendous. Living is the world, but perhaps because life has become unlivable, things are taking these shapes. As the world goes this way, we will just carry on with our small Kaebyŏk movement (開闢運動).<218>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the February 1924 issue of *Kaebyŏk* magazine explains that the issue focused on literature but fell short of the editors' ambition to counteract the perceived "vulgarity and decadence" of the Korean literary scene. It apologizes for omitting works by Na Tohyang, Yi Ch'ŏngu, and Yi Ok due to incompletion, late submission, or space constraints, and promises to resume serialized articles in the next issue, which will be a special "Thought Issue." The editors then comment on significant recent world events: Lenin's death, the formation of a Labour government in Britain, political turmoil in Japan over the lèse-majesté incident, and the rise of both liberal and socialist movements within Korea, attributing these developments to the hardships of life. For historians, this piece offers insight into the editorial self-perception and goals of a prominent colonial-era Korean magazine, its engagement with contemporary literary figures, and its awareness of both international political shifts (Lenin, UK Labour, Japanese politics) and burgeoning ideological movements (liberalism, socialism) within colonial Korea itself, framing these within a context of societal hardship. The mention of specific movements like "liberal-style connection movements" and "socialist-style propaganda movements" is particularly noteworthy.

餘言
本號는 온젼히 문예를 중심으로 한 잡지가 되엿슴니다. 우리가 이 한 號를 내임으로해서, 卑野, 頹廢의 기분으로 충만된 조선의 문단에, 한번, 새로운 기세를 보히려 햇더니, 그러케 뜻과 가티는 못되엿슴니다. 그러나, 아니한 것 보다는 확실히 다른 것이 잇겟지오. 그리고, 이후 事는 조곰 두고 보는 것이 若何.
될 수 잇는 대로는 本號에는, 여러 작가의 작품을 網羅하야 실어볼 작뎡이엿스나, 頁數의 관계로 그만 그러케 못 되엿슴니다. 明日이 來無窮하오니, 장차 볼 밧게 업슴니다.
몃 종의 繼續文을 連載치 못한 것은 진실로 미안함니다. 그러나 來號부터는 확실히 계속될 것임니다. 羅稻香兄이 주신 작품은 그것이 「未完」이기 때문에, 李靑愚, 李鈺씨의 원고는 편집 직후에 도착되엿기 때문에 本號에 못 실엇슴니다. 미안함니다.
來 3월호는 특히 「思想號」가 되겟슴니다. 내용은 지금 豫期한 것으로도 자못 상당하올줄로 자신함니다.
세상은 졍말 잘 변함니다. 그동안에도 퍽 변햇슴니다. 20세기의 惑星이던 「레닌」씨도 졍말 세상을 떠낫고 英國에는 類例에 업든 勞働黨 내각이 조직되엿슴니다. 日本에는 不敬事件, 이 사건의 반동으로 생긴 특권내각 성립의 문제로 朝野가 떠들셕하고, 朝鮮에는 自由主義流의 聯結運動, 社會主義流의 宣傳運動이 굉장함니다. 사는 것이 세상인대, 못살게 되닛가, 이 모양들이겟지오. 세상이 이래 갈사록, 우리는 작고 開闢運動이나 하겟슴니다.＜218＞
