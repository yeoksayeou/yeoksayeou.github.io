餘言 (Postscript)
△ As is always said, time truly flies.
Now, if we edit just ten more volumes of this book, the year Pyŏngin (丙寅年, 1926) will also be over. Just as individuals accumulate worries as they age, for this journal, the older it gets, the more keenly all sorts of responsibilities are felt. Thoughts on how to meet society's [*]望 (expectations/hopes) are many, but [*]墳 (efforts/circumstances?) prevent things from going as intended.

△ February is an ordinary month, so all feelings are as flat as the beginning of the year [*]. Does that mean this journal is also flat? No. With the major essays by Messrs. Yi Tonhwa (李敦化), Yi Ch'ŏngu (李靑宇), [Name obscured], and the three creative works by Messrs. Cho Myŏnghŭi (趙明熙), Ch'oe Sŭngil (崔承一), and Yi Kiyŏng (李箕永) as the main features, whether it's addressing the [*][*]点 (focus?) and points of joy regarding the current [*] situation from various perspectives and individuals, or recalling Marx (칼), Rosa [Luxemburg] (로자), Thomas (토마쓰), [and] Lenin (레닌), or the new trends in Russia (露國), Zhang Zuolin's (張作霖) [*][*][*], the second proletarian gov[ernment?] ([*][*]정**), [or] current affairs commentary, we believe we have [*][*][*] fulfilled our duty (其任).
