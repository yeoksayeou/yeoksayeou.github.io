餘言 (Epilogue/Postscript)

A 5th-anniversary commemorative issue! How should it be edited to feel like a commemorative issue? Being the 5th anniversary, it should have value commensurate with that... What should be done? Alright, that's it. Until now, we have exposed many of Chosŏn's shortcomings, but we haven't spoken of Chosŏn's strengths. This time, let's make a special effort to "Praise Chosŏn." Let's look at Chosŏn from its bright side (光明面). Alright, that's right. Let's do that—this was the editorial foreword.

Worldly affairs (世事) are such that things get done if you do them, and things are achieved if you set your mind to them. While we cannot say we achieved satisfaction as planned, the thought occurs that this much is not particularly flawed. From geography and history to figures, products, public sentiment, customs, and traditions, all points of pride have been brought to light. Especially, the segmented praise for the eight provinces is fine material (好 재료) unobtainable outside this issue (本號), and similarly, the praise for Chosŏn as seen by foreigners—such fine points (玉條) could not be obtained outside this issue. Readers—treasure this book together.

While the praise for Chosŏn is quite grand, the recent international political situation and China's (中國) great resistance movement clearly reveal foreign affairs (外情), and Chosŏn's rural village problem and Chosŏn's Christianity problem clearly reveal domestic affairs (內情). Also, regarding news of our brethren in the north, the past and present situation of fellow Koreans' farming villages in Russia (在露) and Yi Tonhwa's (李敦化) journey to South Manchuria (南滿洲行) can be clearly known.

In the literary section, besides the two winning creative works from the commemorative contest, three creative works by Messrs. Yŏm Sangsŏp (廉想涉), Hong Sayong (洪思容), and Kim Kijin (金基鎭) have been added, making a total of five pieces and over 100 pages of new literature. However, one disappointment is that while the literary contest entries showed good results beyond expectation (豫期) in quantity, the quality was unexpectedly poor. Only one second-place and one third-place piece were obtained. This is regrettable, as it is for all the entrants (諸氏).

As planned, the field survey of Chŏnnam (全南) has begun. On June 19th, Mr. Ch'a Sangch'an (車相瓚) departed first. Mr. Pak Talch'ŏl (朴達成) was also set to leave soon after, and it was later decided that Mr. Yi Ŭl (李乙) would go as well. While asking for much support from the figures of Chŏnnam, we also wish for the strong legs (健脚) of the advance member, Mr. Ch'a. Ah—the bamboo forests (竹林) of Chŏnnam! How vigorous and refreshing they are!

Finally, one more word. Mr. Yi Minch'ang's (李民昌) "Theory of Chosŏn's Economic Ruin (朝鮮의 經濟破滅論)," which has continued for two installments, will be suspended before its conclusion. This is due to the previous confiscation (압수). Also, among the articles praising Chosŏn this time, Mr. Yi Pyŏngdo's (李丙燾) piece on King Sejong the Great and Chosŏn Culture (世宗大王과 朝朝鮮文物) and the biography of General Im Kyŏngŏp (林慶業 장군 전기) are postponed to the next issue. The esteemed manuscripts (玉稿) of Messrs. Yi Tonggok (李東谷), Yi Ŭihwan (李儀煥), and Kim Kyŏngjae (金璟載) are also postponed to the next issue. <94>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript reflects on the creation of Kaebyŏk's 5th-anniversary issue (No. 61, July 1925). The editors decided on the theme "Praise Chosŏn," aiming to highlight the nation's strengths across various domains like geography, history, culture, and regional distinctiveness, including perspectives from foreigners. The issue also covers contemporary international (China) and domestic (rural issues, Christianity, Koreans in Russia/Manchuria) affairs, and features a literary section with contest winners and established writers. The editors express mild disappointment with the literary contest's quality despite high participation and announce the commencement of a field survey in Chŏnnam. Finally, it notes the suspension of Yi Minch'ang's series due to "confiscation" and the postponement of several other articles to the next issue.

From a colonial history perspective, the article is revealing. The explicit focus on "Praising Chosŏn" can be seen as an attempt to foster national consciousness and pride amidst Japanese colonial rule. The mention of the "confiscation" (압수) leading to the suspension of an article on Chosŏn's economic ruin directly points to the reality of censorship faced by Korean publications during this period. The coverage of diverse topics like rural problems, Christianity, and the diaspora reflects the complex social and political landscape the magazine navigated.

餘言
5주년 기념호! 엇더케 편집해야 기념호다울고? 5주년이니만큼 그만큼 갑이 잇서야 될 터인데... 엇더케 할고? 올타 되얏다. 여태것 朝鮮의 단점은 만히 들추어 보앗지만 朝鮮의 장점은 말해보지 못햇스니 이번엘낭 특별히 「朝鮮자랑」이나 좀 해보자. 光明面으로 朝鮮을 보아보자. 올타 그럿타. 그러케하자- 이것이 편집 前言이엿다.

世事는 하면 되는 법이요. 마음먹으면 일우는 법이다. 생각대로 만족을 어덧다 할 수는 업스나 이만하면 그닥 흠결도 안이라는 생각이 난다. 지리로 역사로 인물로 산물로 인심으로 습관 풍속에 이르기까지 자랑거리는 다 들추어 내엇고 특히 8도의 分科 자랑은 本號가 안이고는 어더 보지 못한 好 재료이고 또는 외국인이 본 朝鮮의 자랑도 本號가 안이고는 그만한 玉條를 어더 내지 못할 것이다. 독자-다 가티 이 책을 寶藏하라.

朝鮮자랑은 그만큼 굉장하거니와 또한 최근의 국제정국과 中國의 대반항운동은 外情을 알기에 역력하며 朝鮮의 농촌문제 朝鮮의 기독교문제는 內情을 알기에 역역하다. 그리고 북방형제의 소식을 알기에는 在露동포 농촌정황의 昔今과 李敦化君의 南滿洲行으로 역역키 알 수 잇다.

문예란에 잇서서는 기념현상의 창작 2편 외에 廉想涉
洪思容
金基鎭氏의 창작 3편을 倂하야 合 五題 百餘頁의 신문예이다. 그런데 한 가지 섭섭한 것은 현상문예가 양으로는 豫期 이상의 好 성적이엿스나 질로는 상상 이외의 不 성적이다. 이등 1편 삼등 1편을 어덧슬 뿐이다. 응모 諸氏와 갓치 섭섭한 일이다.

예정과 갓치 全南 답사는 시작되얏다. 6월 19일로써 車相瓚君이 먼츰 떠낫다. 朴達成君도 뒤밋처 떠나기로 한 후 특히 李乙君도 가기로 되얏다. 全南 인사의 만흔 후원을 비는 동시 선발 車君의 健脚을 祝한다. 아- 全南이 竹林! 씩씩도 하고 시원도 하구나!

최후로 한 말 더한다. 2차나 계속되는 李民昌君의 「朝鮮의 經濟破滅論」은 종결을 두고 休載된다. 그것은 前次의 압수로 因함이다. 그리고 이번 朝鮮자랑 기사중의 李丙燾氏의 世宗大王과 朝鮮文物이란 것과 또 林慶業 장군 전기도 來號로 밀어둔다. 그리고 李東谷
李儀煥
金璟載 諸氏의 玉稿도 來號로 밀어둔다.＜94＞
