Remittance Notice

One, When remitting the total payment for *Kaebyŏk*, *Sin Yŏsŏng*, *Ŏrini*, and other publications all at once, please itemize each publication (各種을 細別하야) in the communication column (通信欄).

One, When sending money, be sure to make the payment via postal transfer (振替) to Kyŏngsŏng (京城) 8106. However, stamps or revenue stamps are absolutely refused.

One, Subscription fees (誌代) must be paid entirely in advance. If the advance payment runs out (盡할 時), delivery of the magazine will be stopped.

One, Requesting magazines via cash on delivery (代金引換) or collection mail (集金郵便) is absolutely refused.

One, For the address, be sure to note the County (郡), Township (面), Village (里), and street number (番地).

One, When changing your address, be sure to clearly state both the old address (舊住所) and the new address (新住所).

One, Especially for readers in the China region (中國지방), if you do not clearly state your address (down to the small place name; 小地名까지) and full name (氏名) when remitting money, we cannot send the publications.<28>

**Summary**

This article provides detailed instructions for readers sending payments to the publisher for subscriptions to *Kaebyŏk* and other affiliated magazines like *Sin Yŏsŏng* and *Ŏrini*. It specifies that payments must be itemized if multiple subscriptions are paid together, sent via postal transfer to a specific account in Kyŏngsŏng (Seoul), and paid entirely in advance. The notice explicitly refuses payment by stamps, cash on delivery, or collection mail, and warns that delivery will stop if advance payment runs out. It also stresses the need for complete addresses, including administrative divisions (County, Township, Village, Number), and requires both old and new addresses for changes. A special instruction is given for readers in China to provide very detailed addresses and full names. From a historical perspective, this notice reveals the logistical operations of a major Korean publishing house during the colonial era, including its reliance on the Japanese postal transfer system (振替), the specific administrative geography used (郡, 面, 里), and its subscriber base extending to China. The strict payment terms and methods reflect the challenges of managing subscriptions and distribution during that period.

送金注意
一, 開闢, 新女性, 어린이 기타 출판물의 대금을 총합하야 일시에 송금할 시는 각종을 細別하야 通信欄에 기입하여 주시요.
一, 送金할 時에는 반듯이 振替 京城 8106番으로 拂込하여 주시요.
단, 우표나 인지는 일체 사절함.
一, 誌代는 일체 선금으로 하되 만일 선금이 盡할 時는 送本을 정지함.
一, 代金引換, 集金郵便으로 잡지를 講求함은 일체 사절함.
一, 주소는 반듯이 郡, 面, 里, 番地를 상기하시요.
一, 주소를 변경할 時는 반듯이 舊住所와 新住所를 明記하시요.
一, 특히 中國지방 독자에 限하야 송금하실 時는 주소(小地名까지) 氏名을 明記치 아니하면 간행물을 발송치 못함.＜28＞
