社告
特別히 各地 分賣所 當務者와 열어분 讀者에게

一, 本誌代金 及 郵稅를 五月 以前 未納額은 上半期 決算 前에, 五月 以後의 것은 先金(三個月分ㆍ六個月分ㆍ一個年分)으로 보내주시오 先金이 아니면 책을 보낼 수 업습니다

一, 各 敎區에서 送金하실 에는 振替口座 京城 八一○六 加入者 開闢社 名義로 直接 보내주시오 萬一 振替口座 京城 一三六三 加入者 盧憲容 名義로 間接 보내시면 受理할 時間에 受理치 못하야 相違되는 點도 업지 아니합니다

一, 振替로 送金하실 에는 반듯이 振替用紙 裏面 通信欄에 送金하는 事由 即 一金 圓 錢은 開闢自 號至 號 幾冊代金 及 郵稅라고 昭詳히 記入하여주시오 通信欄에 아모런 記事가 업스면 金錢處理 上에 대단이 困難합니다

一, 本社에 通信하실 에는 葉書와 封緘을 勿論하고 반듯이 發信人의 氏名과 住所 道 郡 面 里 番地를 正確히 記送하여 주시오 住所가 不分明하면 무르신 事件의 答書와 請求하신 物件의 送品을 할 道理가 업습니다

開闢社 經理部＜41＞

Company Notice
Especially to Personnel at Regional Distribution Points and Esteemed Readers

One, Regarding unpaid amounts for this magazine's subscription fee and postage from before May, please send them before the first-half fiscal closing. For amounts from May onwards, please send them as advance payments (for three months, six months, or one year). If payment is not made in advance, we cannot send the magazine.

One, When sending money from each district (敎區), please send it directly via postal transfer account Kyŏngsŏng 8106 under the name of the subscriber, Kaebyŏk Publishing Company (開闢社). If you happen to send it indirectly via postal transfer account Kyŏngsŏng 1363 under the name of the subscriber No Hŏnyong (盧憲容), it may not be received at the expected time, and discrepancies may arise.

One, When sending money via postal transfer, please be sure to clearly write the reason for the remittance in the communication section on the back of the postal transfer form, specifically stating, "X Won X Chŏn is payment for Kaebyŏk issue number X to issue number X, X copies, including postage." If there is no information in the communication section, it causes significant difficulties in processing the payment.

One, When communicating with our company, whether by postcard or sealed letter, please be sure to accurately write and send the sender's full name and address, including Province, County, Township (Myŏn), Village (Ri), and House Number. If the address is unclear, there is no way for us to send replies to your inquiries or the items you requested.

Kaebyŏk Publishing Company Accounting Department <41>

**Summary**

This company notice from Kaebyŏk Publishing Company outlines strict payment and communication procedures for its distributors and readers in 1921. It demands advance payment for subscriptions, specifies the correct postal transfer account for payments (warning against using an alternative associated with No Hŏnyong), insists on detailed information on payment forms, and requires complete sender addresses for all correspondence to ensure proper handling and delivery. From a historical perspective, the notice reveals the practical business challenges faced by Korean publishers during the colonial period, including managing finances across different regions, the reliance on the postal transfer system (振替口座), and the emphasis needed on clear communication and record-keeping for smooth operations. The specific mention of two different postal accounts suggests potential internal organizational complexities or changes.
