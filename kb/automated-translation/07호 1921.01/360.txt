Like the Snow Moon
						Pŏwŏlsaeng

The mystery of the Universe, the creation of Nature,
The heavens are white, and the earth also,
Is it a pearl? Is it a rose?
My love flowing in my heart,
Ah, just like you.

Silence above, silence below,
In a place quiet like a mute,
Like a virgin secretly met,
The sound of my beloved whispering in the silence,
Ah, just like you.

Whether the wind blows or the rain comes,
Neither extinguished nor cooled,
Burning without flame, hot without heat,
That which surges in my beloved's breast,
Ah, just like you.

No matter how deep, no matter how high,
Crossing the sea, 넘어 over Mount T'ai (泰山),
The love of my beloved stretching without limit,
Like this thing hidden in my heart,
Ah, just like you.

The world has harsh classes,
Humans have differences of rich and poor,
But having no high or low, no noble or base,
The white and clear bundle of love,
Ah, just like you. <149>

Eating greens and drinking water,
Lying down using an arm as a pillow,
Only love, without dislike,
Seeks me out without hesitation,
Ah, just like you.

Worldly ways are the devil's hand, love is an infant's hand,
When we are equally enticed (籠絡),
That which is joyful amidst sadness,
Is only you, for whom I am thankful,
Ah, just like you.

Without parents knowing, without siblings knowing,
Known only to you and me, just the two of us,
Our precious and important thing,
Until the world forever ends,
Ah, just like you.

Though the cup of the heart is full,
Do not let it overflow and spill,
I worry it might get stained by the dirt on the ground.
So that dislike never arises,
Ah, just like you.

It is not only coal briquettes (油炭) that melt and burn,
Without smoke and without flame,
To whom can I show this heart of mine?
Secretly from anyone, only you know,
Ah, just like you.

The life of a person is long and short,
Until life itself ends,
Whether others laugh or insult,
Paying no attention at all, that pure and elegant thing is
Ah, just like you.

Whether spring returns and the snow melts or not,
Whether the moon waxes and wanes by degree, present or absent,
Warm love is boundless with heaven and earth (與天地無窮),
Whether bitter or sweet, that pure white thing is
Ah, just like you.
...(End)...<150>

**Summary**

This poem by Pŏwŏlsaeng, titled "Like the Snow Moon," repeatedly compares the speaker's profound love to the purity, constancy, and transcendent nature of a bright moon shining on snow. Love is depicted as a mysterious, silent yet potent force, capable of overcoming physical distance and social barriers like class and wealth. It provides solace amidst hardship, remains steadfast regardless of external circumstances or judgment, and is portrayed as a secret, precious, eternal bond known only to the lovers. From the perspective of Korean colonial history, the poem's publication in *Kaebyŏk* (a prominent intellectual magazine of the era) is significant. The intense focus on pure, transcendent love that ignores societal divisions (class, wealth) and remains constant despite external pressures (hardship, judgment, changing seasons) could be interpreted as an allegory for yearning for purity, freedom, or national resilience in the face of colonial oppression and societal constraints. The emphasis on love's power to exist outside conventional structures might resonate with anti-establishment sentiments common in *Kaebyŏk*'s readership.

雪月과 가티
						步月生
宇宙의 神秘 自然의 造化,
한울도 히고 땅도 또한,
眞珠이냐 薔薇이냐,
가슴에 흐르는 나의 戀愛도,
아아 한가지 너와 가티.
우에도 沈默 알에도 沈默,
벙어리가티 잠잠한 곳에,
남몰래 맛난 處女처럼,
沈默에 숙살거리는 님의 소리도,
아아 한가지 너와 가티.
바람이 부나 비가 오나,
꺼지도 안코 식지도 안는,
불꼿업시 타고 熱업시 뜨거운,
님의 가슴에 북바친 그것도,
아아 한가지 너와 가티.
아모리 깁거나 아모리 놉거나,
바다도 건너고 泰山도 넘어,
限업시 뻐치는 님의 사랑도,
가슴에 감춘 이 내 것처럼
아아 한가지 너와 가티.
世上은 모진 階級이 잇고,
人間은 貧富의 差異가 잇스나,
上下도 업고 貴賤도 업는,
히고도 맑은 사랑의 뭉치는,
아아 한가지 너와 가티.＜149＞
나물을 먹고 물을 마시며,
팔을 비고서 누워 잇서도,
오즉 戀愛는 실흠이 업시,
나를 차저서 끄림이 업다,
아아 한가지 너와 가티.
世態는 惡魔손 戀愛는 幼兒손,
한가지 우리가 籠絡될 때에,
슯흔 가운대 깃거운 것도,
다만 感謝한 너뿐이로다,
아아 한가지 너와 가티.
父母도 모르고 兄弟도 모르게,
너와 나와 단 둘이 아는,
貴하고 重한 우리의 것은,
世上히 永遠히 다 마치도록,
아아 한가지 너와 가티.
마음의 잔이 가득 차도록,
넘쳐 붓지를 행여 말아라,
땅바닥 흙에 물들가 念慮다.
언제던 실흠이 나지 안토록
아아 한가지 너와 가티.
눗고도 타는 것이 油炭뿐 아니다,
煙氣도 업고 火焰도 업는,
이 나의 가슴을 누게 다 보일가,
아모도 몰래 너만 알지,
아아 한가지 너와 가티.
人生의 一生은 길고도 짤느다,
목숨이 부터 잇는 때까지,
남이야 웃거나 辱을 하거나,
족음도 關係말고 淸雅한 그것은,
아아 한가지 너와 가티.
陽春이 돌아와 눈이야 녹던 말던
盈虧가 度數잇서 달이야 잇건 업건,
따뜻한 사랑은 與天地無窮으로,
쓰거나 달거나 潔白한 그것은,
아아 한가지 너와 가티.
