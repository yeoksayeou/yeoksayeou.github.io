上下七十一辛酉
						權悳奎
Although the founding of the nation by Tan'gun may be several hundred years before the Common Era, here the Common Era is followed. While facts of recent times can be known in more detail than those of high antiquity, they have been deliberately summarized. Regarding high antiquity, it is unavoidable that there is speculation involved.

Year 54 (Tan'gun Era)
In the *Tongsa* (Eastern History), it is said that when Tan'gun encountered a nine-year flood, he commanded P'eng U (彭虞) to stabilize the high mountains and great rivers. This Tan'gun was one Tan'gun among the successive generations of Tan'gun rulers. This year was two years before China's Xia Yu (夏禹) controlled the waters and reached Youjieshi (右碣石), so P'eng U's control of the flood likely occurred in this year as well.

Year 474 (Tan'gun Era)
At this time, Wang Chomyŏng (王祖明) improved agriculture and the housing system.

Year 534 (Tan'gun Era)
At this time, Wang Haewŏl (王海月) was known for shipbuilding skills.

Year 654 (Tan'gun Era)
At this time, Wang Myŏngji (王明知) was known for calendrical arts.

Year 774 (Tan'gun Era)
At this time, King U (王僪) improved weaponry.

Year 1074 (Puyŏ Era)
The border was fixed with the former Kalsa state (曷思國) (the later Kalsa was the state where the younger brother of Taeso (帶素), King of East Puyŏ (東扶餘), killed Haedu (海頭) and made himself king).

Year 1194 (Puyŏ Era)
Historians say that Paeg'i (伯夷), hearing that Tan'gun had founded a nation on the sea and possessed a benevolent and wise character, fled from King Chu (紂) and resided on the shores of the North Sea (北海). He inquired about local customs while placing his hands on the ground (以手據地) and translated and read the divine proclamations of the Chŏnggyo faith (倧敎神誥). Hearing that King Wen (文王) was adept at caring for the elderly, he again headed west and reached Zhou (周). Since this Sinyu year was two years after King Chu released King Wen, it is unknown whether this was the year Paeg'i went west.

Year 1374 (King Sŏ Yŏn Era)
According to the inscription on the King's shrine stele, those from the surrounding feudal states who had disputes had nowhere to seek proper judgment... Those who offered tribute of jade, silk, and items of life and death (玉帛死生之物) at the court of Sŏ (徐) numbered 36 states.<102> Perhaps even in this Sinyu year, the Cho-Han peoples (朝鮮民族) were pursuing deer across the Central Plain (中原), unable to open their eyes due to flying arrows and stones.

Year 1394 (China, Zhou Regency 2nd Year)
King Li (厲王) of Zhou (周) in China was immoral. When the Huai people (淮人) from our colonial territory (我殖民地) came to punish him, King Li fled, and the two Dukes, Zhou and Shao (周召二公), administered the government. This is what they call the Gonghe Regency (共和時代); this period lasted for 40 years starting from the year before this one. It was an era when the activity of our people reached its peak.

Year 2274 (East Puyŏ, King Kŭmwa Era)
King Kŭmwa's (金蛙王) eastward move occurred one year after this year, and the Han abolition of the Four Commanderies (漢廢四郡) was 23 years before this year. Therefore, it seems this 24-year period was the busiest time for the Puyŏ dynasty. In this Sinyu year too, there must have been considerable turmoil. Thinking again, the Han abolition of the Four Commanderies 23 years prior might also have been due to the pressure from Puyŏ's eastward move. Thus, the doubt arises whether Puyŏ's eastward move occurred 23 years before this year.

Year 2334 (Koguryŏ, King Yuri 20th Year)
At this time, Cho Wŏlli (曹元理) of Okchŏ (沃沮) was known for mathematics.

Year 2394 (Silla, King T'arhae 5th Year)
It was 54 years after Paekche (百濟) annexed Mahan (馬韓). During this long period, the Mahan general Maeng So (孟召) defended the isolated fortress of Pogam (覆巖孤城). Helplessly, he surrendered the fortress to Saro (斯盧), that is, Silla (新羅). The remaining Mahan faction was thus eradicated.

Year 2454 (Koguryŏ, King T'aejo 69th Year)
Koguryŏ (句麗) led the armies of nations like Ye (濊) and Maek (貊) to attack Han China (支那漢).

Year 2754 (Karak, King Sin 15th Year)
The 6th King of Karak (駕洛), Sinwang Chwaji (神王坐知), passed away, and Crown Prince Ch'wihŭi (太子吹希) ascended the throne, becoming King Hyewang (惠王).

Year 2814 (Silla, King Soji 3rd Year)
Kyerim (鷄林), that is, Silla (新羅), along with Paekche (百濟) and Kaya (加耶), defeated the Koguryŏ (高句麗) and Malgal (靺鞨) armies west of the Niha River (泥河西).

Year 2874 (Silla, King Chinhŭng, Kŏnwŏn 6th Year; Paekche, King Sŏng 19th Year)
A Doctor of the *Mao Shi* (毛詩博士, Book of Odes) and the meaning of the *Niepan Jing* (浧槃經義, Nirvana Sutra) were requested and brought from Liang China (支那梁). ○ At this time, it had been over ten years since the Paekche monk Kyŏmik (儈謙益) returned from Central India. Residing at Hŭngjŏn-sa temple (興轉寺), he translated 72 volumes of the Vinaya Pitaka (律部).

Year 2914 (Koguryŏ, King Yŏngyang 12th Year)
At this time, the monk Hyenye (儈 慧涅) was living in seclusion in Harima Province (播磨國), Japan. Responding to a request from Soga no Umako (蘇我馬子), he took up residence at Ishikawa shōja (石川精舍).

Year 2994 (Silla, King T'aejong Muyŏl 8th Year; Koguryŏ, King Pojang 20th Year)
It had been over ten years since the Silla monk Ŭisang (儈 義湘) was in China. He secretly transmitted Tang's (唐) policy towards the East back to his home country. ○ Tang launched a major invasion into Koguryŏ (句麗). Kae Sowmun (蓋蘇文) dispatched his son Namsaeng (男生) and achieved a great victory.

Year 3054 (Silla, King Sŏngdŏk 20th Year)
The Tripiṭaka master Hyech'o (三藏慧超) was traveling in India after arriving from China. (The Tripiṭaka master's *Wang o ch'ŏnch'uk chŏn* (往五天竺傳, Memoir of the Pilgrimage to the Five Regions of India) was excavated from Dunhuang (燉煌), China, in the past Yunghŭi 1st year [1907]).

Year 3114 (Silla, King Sŏndŏk 2nd Year) <103>
Rites were performed at the divine palace (神宮). ○ I Hwoeok (李懷玉), originally from Koguryŏ, died in China.

Year 3174 (Silla, King Munsŏng 3rd Year)
Ilgilch'an (一吉飡) Hongp'il (弘弼) plotted rebellion, but the plot was discovered, and he fled. ○ The monk Tosŏn's (僧 道詵) age at this time was 15. Since it was 114 years after the death of Yi Xing (一行) of Tang, the claim that Tosŏn personally received Yi Xing's arts is a mistaken transmission (誤傳).

Year 3234 (Silla, King Hyogong 5th Year)
Kung Ye (弓裔) declared himself king, stating he would avenge Koguryŏ against Silla and Tang (羅唐). ○ King Kyŏng (景王) of Parhae (渤海) passed away, and King Ae (哀王) ascended the throne.

Year 3294 (Koryŏ, King Kwangjong, Chunp'ung 2nd Year)
There was a great flood, and houses were swept away.

Year 3354 (Hyŏnjong 12th Year)
Envoys from the Ch'ŏlli state (鐵利國) came, requesting submission. ○ Minister of War (兵部尙書) Kang Minch'ŏm (姜民聸) passed away. ○ The pagoda at Hwangnyong-sa temple (皇龍寺塔) was repaired.

Year 3414 (Munjong 35th Year)
A chieftain of the Western Jurchen (西女眞) came and presented weapons.

Year 3474 (Injong 19th Year)
The Jin (金) captured Ko Yŏngch'ang (高永昌), and Taewŏn (大元) fell.

Year 3654 (Ch'ungsuk Wang 8th Year)
The Directorate for Investigating and Rectifying Wrongs (察理辨違都監) was re-established.

Year 3714 (Deposed King U 7th Year)
In this year, raids by sea pirates (海寇, Wokou) were rampant.

Year 3774 (Chosŏn, King Sejong 23rd Year)
King Tanjong (端宗) was born. Kusŏnggun Chun (龜城君浚) was born. ○ An envoy was sent to Japan (日本) to present azaleas (躑躅花).

Year 3834 (Lord Yŏnsan 7th Year)
Ryukyu (琉球) sent envoys. ○ I Hwang (李滉) and Cho Sik (曹植) were born.

Year 3894 (Myŏngjong 16th Year)
The king personally tested the scholars on leave (賜暇學士) at Ch'wihwajŏng Pavilion (翠華亭) with composition, exposition, and lectures on the classics. ○ Kim Sangyong (金尙容) and I Tŏkhyŏng (李德馨) were born.

Year 3954 (Lord Kwanghae 13th Year)
Pak T'aehaeng (朴太行), together with I Ich'ŏm (李爾瞻), attempted to stage a coup at Kyŏngun Palace (慶運宮) but did not succeed.

Year 4014 (Sukchong 6th Year)
The Capital Garrison (禁衛營) was established. The Office for Musical Instrument Production (樂器造成廳) was established. ○ The Confucian scholars I I (李珥) and Sŏng Hon (成渾) were enshrined in the National Confucian Shrine (文廟).

Year 4074 (Yŏngjo 17th Year)
The punishment for theft was not to be applied to renowned Confucian scholars (儒名者). ○ An Kungnin (安國麟) and others obtained books such as eclipse tables by Westerners (西洋人). ○ The practices of *Chŏllang t'ongjŏng* (銓郞通情, collusion in personnel appointments) and *Hallim hoech'ŏn pŏp* (翰林回薦法, re-recommendation by the Office of Royal Decrees) were abolished.

Year 4134 (Sunjo 1st Year)
Palace slaves (內奴婢) and temple slaves (寺奴婢) were abolished. The slave registers (奴婢案) of various palaces (各宮) and government offices (各司) were burned outside Tonhwamun Gate (敦化門), totaling 66,067 individuals. ○ Ch'oe P'ilgon (崔必恭), I Sŭnghun (李承薰), Chŏng Yakchong (丁若鍾), I Kahwan (李家煥), and others were all executed for the crime of "Heterodox Learning" (邪學罪, Catholicism). Chŏng Yagyong (丁若鏞) was exiled to Kangjin (康律). ○ The *Tongwang chŏmgyŏng* (東王占鏡, Eastern King Divination Mirror) was found in Pyongyang (平壞).

Year 4194 (Ch'ŏljong 12th Year)
Posthumous ranks were bestowed upon the Eight Righteous Men (八義士) of the Hyŏnch'ung Shrine (顯忠祠) in Hoeryŏng (會寧). ○ Kim Paeg'on's (金伯溫) *Taedong yŏjido* (大東輿地圖, Map of the Great East) was completed. ○ Son Pyŏnghŭi (孫秉熙), Pak Yŏnghyo (朴泳孝), Yu Kŭn (柳瑾), and Kwŏn Tongjin (權東鎭) were born.

Year 4254 (- Year)
In January, *Kaebyŏk* (開闢) No. 7 was published.<104>

**Summary**

This article by Kwŏn Tŏkkyu presents a chronology of significant events purported to have occurred in the Sinyu (辛酉) year of the sexagenary cycle throughout Korean history, starting from the mythical Tan'gun era (using a specific dating system) up to the publication year, 1921 (Year 4254 in the author's system). It covers events related to politics, warfare, culture, religion, technology, and notable figures across various Korean kingdoms (Tan'gun Chosŏn, Puyŏ, Koguryŏ, Paekche, Silla, Karak, Koryŏ, Chosŏn) and interactions with China and Japan. The author blends legend with recorded history, sometimes offering interpretations or questioning traditional accounts.

From the perspective of Korean colonial history, the article is interesting for several reasons. It reflects a strong nationalist sentiment by constructing a continuous, long history originating from Tan'gun, implicitly asserting Korea's ancient and independent civilization. The use of a Tan'gun-based era count itself was a nationalist practice. Particularly revealing is the claim of ancient Korean influence extending into China, describing the Huai people as being from "our colonial territory" (我殖民地) and the "Cho-Han peoples" (朝鮮民族) competing in the "Central Plain" (中原), which reinterprets history to emphasize ancient Korean power and reach. The inclusion of figures like Son Pyŏnghŭi (Cheondogyo leader), Pak Yŏnghyo (reformer/pro-Japanese collaborator), Yu Kŭn (independence activist), and Kwŏn Tongjin (independence activist) born in a Sinyu year connects this long history directly to contemporary figures involved in the struggles and transformations of the colonial period. Historians might find the specific historical interpretations, the blend of myth and history, and the underlying nationalist framework valuable for understanding intellectual currents and historical consciousness among some Koreans in the early colonial period.

上下七十一辛酉
						權悳奎
檀君開國은 公用紀元 數百年 前이 될지나 此에는 公用紀元을 從한 것이며 近代의 事實은 上古보다 昭詳함을 得할 지나 짐짓 畧히 하얏스며 上古에 在하야 推測이 有함은 免치 못할 事實이로다.
紀元54(檀-年)
東史에 檀君이 9年의 洪水를 當하야 彭虞를 命하야 高山大川을 定하얏다 하니 이 檀君은 歷代 檀君의 一位이신 檀君이요. 是歲가 支那夏禹의 水를 治하야 右碣石에 至하던 2年 前이니 彭虞의 洪水를 治함이 是歲에도 有하얏스리로다.
仝474(檀-年)
時에 王祖明이 農業과 家屋制度를 改良하니라.
仝534(檀-年)
時에 王海月이 造船術로 聞이러라.
仝654(檀-年)
時에 王明知가 曆術로 聞이러라.
仝774(檀-年)
時에 王僪이 兵器를 改良하니라.
仝1074(扶餘-年)
前曷思國(東扶餘王 帶素의 弟가 海頭를 殺하고 自王한 國은 後曷思)과 定界하다.
仝1194(扶餘-年)
史氏ㅣ 云하되 伯夷가 檀君이 海上에 開國하야 仁賢의 風이 有함을 聞하고 紂를 避하야 北海의 濱에 居하야 以手據地하고 俗禁을 問하며 倧敎神誥를 譯讀하다가 文王이 善養老함을 聞하고 다시 西走하야 周에 至하다 하니 이 辛酉가 紂가 文王을 釋한 後 2年인즉 伯夷의 西走한 年이 是歲인지도 知치 못하리로다.
仝1374(徐偃王-年)
王의 廟碑文에 四方 諸侯의 爭辯하는 者ㅣ 質正할 바가 無하야… 玉帛死生의 物을 徐의 庭에 贄한 者ㅣ 36國이라＜102＞ 하니 아마 이 辛酉에도 朝漢民族이 中原一幅에 鹿을 逐하노라 走矢飛石에 眸子를 開치 못하얏스리로다.
仝1394(支那周共和二年)
支那周의 厲王이 無道한지라. 我殖民地의 淮人이 問罪하매 厲王이 出奔하고 周召二公이 行政하니 彼의 謂하는 바 共和時代니 이 時代는 是歲前年으로부터 40年間이라. 我人의 活躍이 極度에 達한 時代러라.
仝2274(東扶餘 金蛙王-年)
金蛙東遷이 是歲 一年 後요, 漢廢四郡이 是歲 23年 前인즉 想컨대 이 24年間은 扶餘朝의 가장 多事한 時라. 이 辛酉에도 뻑뻑이 騷擾가 少치 아니하얏슬 것이며 다시 생각하면 이 23年 前의 漢廢四郡도 扶餘東遷의 壓迫인지도 知치 못하리니. 扶餘東遷이 是歲 23年 前인가 하는 疑도 無치 아니하도다.
仝2334(高句麗 琉璃王 20年)
時에 沃沮人曹元理ㅣ 數學으로 聞이러라.
仝2394(新羅 脫解王 5年)
百濟가 馬韓을 倂한지 54年이라. 韓將孟召가 이 長年月間에 覆巖孤城을 守하다가 無柰히 城으로써 斯盧 곳 新羅에 降하니 馬韓 餘黨이 玆에 根絶하니라.
仝2454(高句麗 太祖王 69年)
句麗가 濊, 貊 等 國의 軍을 率하고 支那漢을 擊하다.
仝2754(駕洛 神王 15年)
駕洛 第6代王 神王坐知가 昇遐하고 太子吹希가 立하니 곳 惠王이러라.
仝2814(新羅 炤智王 3年)
鷄林 곳 新羅가 百濟, 加耶로 더불어 高句麗, 靺鞨軍을 泥河西에 破하다.
仝2874(新羅 眞興王 建元 6年, 百濟 聖王 19年)
毛詩博士와 浧槃經義를 支那梁에 求來하다. ○時에 百濟 儈謙益이 中印度로부터 歸한지 十餘年이라. 興轉寺에 在하야 律部 72卷을 譯하니라.
仝2914(高句麗 嬰陽王 12年)
時에 儈 慧涅이 日本 播磨國에 隱하얏더니 蘇我馬子의 請에 應하야 石川精舍에 居하니라.
仝2994(新羅 太宗武烈王 8年, 高句麗 寶藏王 20年)
新羅 儈 義湘이 支那에 在한지 十餘年이라. 唐의 對東主義를 本國에 密傳하니라. ○唐이 大擧하야 句麗에 入寇히거늘 蓋蘇文이 그 子 男生을 遣하야 大破하다.
仝3054(新羅 聖德王 20年)
三藏慧超가 支那로부터 印度에 至하야 旅行中이러니라. (三藏의 往五天竺傳은 去 隆熙 元年에 支那燉煌에서 發掘되다.)
仝3114(新羅 宣德王 2年)＜103＞

仝3174(新羅 文聖王 3年)

仝3234(新羅 孝恭王 5年)
弓裔가 高句麗를 爲하야 羅唐에 報讎한다 하고 王을 稱하다. ○渤海 景王이 昇遐하고 哀王이 立하다.
仝3294(高麗 光宗 峻豐2年)

仝3354(顯宗 12年)

仝3774(朝鮮 世宗 23年)
