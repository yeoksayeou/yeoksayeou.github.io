Magazine Title: 개벽 제14호
Publication Date: 1921-08-01
Article Title: 夏蟲의 囈語 (Murmurings of a Summer Bug)
Author: Not specified
Article Type: 잡저 (Miscellaneous Essay)

No matter how I think about it, there is no animal as cunning (狡猾) as a human. Weaving a small pouch of wisdom (智慧), they create appearances in all matters (於千萬事), babbling carelessly as they please about this being good or that being good, making noise arbitrarily according to their thoughts, yet still unsatisfied, they engage in things like competition (競爭) and war (戰爭), killing their own kind (同族相殺), treating the victor preferentially and the loser with contempt (賤待), while holding their heads high and calling themselves the "lord of all creation" (萬物의 靈長) or saying "only humans are most noble" (唯人이 最貴). It is truly a ridiculous thing.

Just because humans have speaking organs (機關) and written characters to express their thoughts (意思), should they speak so carelessly? What makes them the "lord," and where is the proof (證明) that they are the "most noble"? According to some people, they claim to be the "lord" or "most noble" because humanity has the Five Relations (五倫). If that's the case, then honoring their ancestors (祖先) must also be one boast of those people called "lord" and "most noble," but since they don't know their ancestors and progenitors (始祖), such specimens (物件) called "lord" and "most noble" are truly pitiful, lamentable, and ridiculous (可笑로운).

No matter how pretentious they act, they fail to realize they are our descendants (後裔), our posterity (子孫), and they treat us poorly (薄待) and with contempt (賤待). The proverb (俗談) "Rearing a tiger invites trouble" (養虎爲患) truly refers to something like this. We do not use cunning tricks (手段) like you to proclaim ourselves "lord" or "most noble"; this is proven by words spoken amongst yourselves, which you yourselves recognize (認定) as most correct. It is nothing other than this: look at Darwin's (따알위인) theory of evolution (進化學說). Among all living beings (生物), what did it say their ancestor was? You shouldn't get so carried away just because you turned out well; try to remember your tadpole (올챙이) days too. Oh well, is there any point in blaming you? Let's just resign ourselves (斷念) to our own carelessness (不察) in having raised bad descendants. You ridiculous things. Haha.<133>

**Summary**

This satirical essay, written from the perspective of a "summer bug" (representing earlier life forms), critiques human arrogance, hypocrisy, and violence. Humans boast of being the "lord of all creation" and the "most noble," citing things like the Five Confucian Relations, yet they engage in constant conflict and mistreat others. The narrator mockingly points to Darwin's theory of evolution, reminding humans of their humble origins ("tadpole days") and calling them ungrateful descendants who have forgotten their true ancestors (earlier life forms), thus proving the proverb "rearing a tiger invites trouble." From a historical perspective, this piece from *Kaebyŏk* magazine in 1921 is interesting for its use of satire and evolutionary theory to critique human self-importance. While not explicitly political, its themes of arrogance, power dynamics (victor/loser), and forgotten origins could be interpreted by historians as an allegorical commentary on societal hierarchies, the perceived failings of modern civilization, or even reflecting disillusionment within the context of colonial Korea, where claims of superiority and the reality of subjugation were starkly contrasted.

夏蟲의 囈語
아모리 생각하여도 사람과 가티 狡猾한 動物은 업다. 족으마한 智慧 주머니를 짜서 於千萬事에 태를 맨들어 노코 이것이 조흐니 저것이 조흐니 마음 드는 대로 생각 나는대로 함부로 짓거리고 任意로 떠들면서도 오히려 不足하여 競爭이니 戰爭이니 하여 同族을 相殺하야 勝한 者를 優待하고 敗한 者를 賤待하면서 얼굴을 크게 들고 萬物의 靈長이니 唯人이 最貴이니 하니 참 가소로운 일이다.
아모리 사람에게 말하는 機關이 잇고 意思를 發表하는 文字가 잇다고 하기로니 그가티 함부로 말을 할 것인가 무엇이 어떠하니 靈長이며 어대가 어떠하니 最貴라고 하는 證明은 우웨 못하엿는가. 어떤 사람의 말을 들르면 人類에게는 五倫이 잇다고 靈長이니 最貴이니 하엿스니 그리고 보면 그 祖先을 爲하는 것도 아마 靈長이요 最貴라는 사람의 한 자랑꺼리겟는데 祖先과 始祖를 모르니 그러한 靈長과 最貴라는 物件들도 참 불상하고 애달고 可笑로운 일이다.
아모리 저이들이 젠체 하여도 우리의 後裔이요 우리들의 子孫인 줄을 깨닷지 못하고 우리를 薄待하고 賤待하니 俗談에 養虎爲患이라는 것이 참 이런 것을 두고 한 말이다. 우리는 너이들과 가티 狡猾한 手段으로 自稱 靈長이니 最貴이니 하는 것이 아니고 너이들 中에서 짓거린 말이고 또 너이들도 가장 올타고 認定하는 것으로만 證明하는 것이다. 그것은 다른 것이 아니라 『따알위인』의 進化學說을 보와라. 온 生物 中에 그 祖先이 무엇이라고 하엿나. 너이가 잘 되엇다고 그러케 날뛸 것이 아니라 올챙이 때 생각도 좀 하여 보아라. 아서라 너 나무렐 것이 잇나. 子孫 잘못 둔 우리네의 不察이라고 斷念하지. 可笑로운 이것들아. 하하.＜133＞
