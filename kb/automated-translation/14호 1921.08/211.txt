Article Title: [무제] ([Untitled])

Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editorial Postscript)

"Modern Literature" (「近代文藝」) by Mr. Ansŏ (岸曙 君), which has been serialized since the previous issue, is unavoidably omitted due to the author's illness. We ask for the deep understanding of all our readers. <133>

**Summary**

This brief editorial notice informs readers of *Kaebyŏk* magazine that the continuation of the serialized article "Modern Literature" by Ansŏ Kun (Kim Kijin) is absent from issue 14 because the author is ill. It asks for the readers' understanding. From a historical perspective, this small notice reflects the practical challenges faced by colonial-era Korean publications, such as maintaining regular contributions amidst potential personal difficulties like illness. It also shows the direct communication style between the editorial staff and readers common in periodicals of the time.
