**Body Text Translation:**
『Soviet』 Literary Arts Conference

At the end of last August, a grand Literary Arts Conference was held in Moscow, 『Soviet』, with the participation of 500 writers representing 80 languages.

On that day, the opening address was given by 『Kolk'i』 (Gorky), who called for the study of Russia's (露西亞) classical literature. 『Sŭt'allin』 (Stalin) greatly welcomed them, calling them engineers who supply power to the 『Human Spirit (人間精神)』.

Therefore, the country's newspapers, echoing 『Sŭt'allin』, asserted in unison that the purpose of the conference was 『to apply scientific methods (科學的 方法) to literary creation (文學創作)』. As 『Llenin』 (Lenin) had recommended the study of classics (古典研究), it is thought to be a story worth listening to at this time when the sounds of 『Politics and Literature (政治와 文學)』 are clamorous. (Photo shows the main meeting of the conference) <84>

**Summary**

The article reports on the First Soviet Writers' Congress held in Moscow in August 1934. It notes the participation of 500 writers representing 80 languages. Maxim Gorky gave the opening address, emphasizing classical Russian literature, while Joseph Stalin welcomed the writers, famously calling them "engineers of the human spirit." The conference aimed to apply "scientific methods" to literary creation, following Lenin's earlier encouragement of studying classics. The author suggests this event is noteworthy amidst contemporary debates about politics and literature. For historians, this brief report in a colonial Korean magazine is interesting as it shows awareness of and potential interest in Soviet cultural policy and socialist realism during a period of intense Japanese censorship and anti-communist sentiment. It reflects how international ideological currents reached Korea, even under colonial rule.

『싸벳트』文藝大會
지난 8월말 『싸벳트』의 『모스코-』에서 80*語를 대표하는 文學者 500인의 참가로 *大한 文藝大會가 개최되엿다.
당일 개회사는 『골-키-』가 하엿는데 그는 露西亞의 古典文學의 연구를 喚起하엿다. 「스타-린」은 『人間精神』에 動力을 공급하는 技師라고 그들을 크게 환영하엿다.
그래서 同國의 신문은 일제히 『스타-린」과 갓치 입을 모아서 그 회담의 목적은 『문학창작에 과학적 방법을 적용하는데 잇다』고 내세웟다. 『레-닌』은 고전연구를 推獎하엿든 바 『政治와 文學』의 소리가 식그러운 이 때 한번쯤은 귀를 기우려 볼 이야기라고 생각한다.(사진은 동대회의 주요會議) ＜84＞
