기사형태	잡저 (Miscellaneous writing)

What is the Third Empire?

The Third Empire is called "The Third Empire" in English. The meaning of this term is that human life is not governed solely by instinct. We are not formed only of the world of flesh, the ugly material world, like animals. However, neither are we made only of the world of ideal spirit, like what Christianity calls Heaven. Therefore, if we call the First Empire the Empire of Flesh and the Second Empire the Empire of Spirit, then the Empire we hope for in the future is the Empire of Spirit and Flesh, namely the Third Empire.<74>

**Summary**

The article defines the concept of the "Third Empire" (第三帝國) as a future ideal state that synthesizes the physical/material realm ("First Empire") and the spiritual/ideal realm ("Second Empire"). It posits that human existence is neither purely instinctual/materialistic like animals nor purely spiritual like the Christian concept of Heaven, but should strive for a future integration of both spirit (靈) and flesh (肉). For historians, this piece offers insight into the intellectual and philosophical discussions occurring in early colonial Korea. The adoption and reinterpretation of the "Third Empire" concept, possibly derived from European thought, reflects a search for new societal models and ideals amidst the challenges of colonialism, attempting to forge a future vision distinct from pure materialism or detached spiritualism. The use of "Empire" (帝國) is itself interesting, suggesting a grand, perhaps utopian, future construction.

第三帝國主義는 무엇인가?
第三帝國主義는 英語로 The third Empire라고 한다. 이 말의 뜻은 人間의 生活이 本能 그대로 左右되는 것은 아니다. 우리가 動物과 가티 肉의 世界 醜惡한 物質的의 世界로만 成立된 것은 아니다. 그러나 또 基督敎의 天國이라고 하는 것과 가티 理想靈魂의 世界로만 된 것도 아니다. 그러면 第一의 帝國은 肉의 帝國, 第二의 帝國은 靈의 帝國이라고 하고 장차 우리가 바라는 帝國은 靈과 肉의 帝國 卽 第三帝國이라고 하는 것이다.＜74＞
