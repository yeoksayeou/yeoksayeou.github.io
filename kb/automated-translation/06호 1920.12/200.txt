Koreans Who Love Being the 'Original Founder'

The term "Wonjo" (元祖 - original founder) is the closest word to invention, discovery, creation, founding, or genesis (開闢 - kaebyŏk, also the magazine's name). This "Wonjo" is often used by merchants, stemming from the ambition to gain credibility for their products with the implication 'I started this first'. Using "Wonjo" in its true sense would not be particularly strange, but it is laughable how we Koreans often pride ourselves, calling transplantation or imitation "Wonjo". However, not only does that "Wonjo" have no relation whatsoever to oneself, but it also merely exposes one's own ignorance. For example, the Hyŏksindan (革新團) prides itself as the "Wonjo" of performance (演劑), Aengbun (櫻紛) prides itself as the "Wonjo" of the cosmetics world, and Hyech'ŏn'gwan (惠泉館) prides itself as the "Wonjo" of cuisine. There are countless other various "Wonjos", but omitting them here, I would like to make one request: instead of priding oneself on being the "Wonjo", how about priding oneself on improvement and progress? <91>

**Summary**

The article criticizes the trend among Koreans in 1920, particularly businesses, of claiming to be the "Wonjo" (original founder) for products or services. The author argues this term is often misused for mere imitations or transplants of existing things, revealing ignorance rather than genuine originality. Specific examples like a performance troupe (Hyŏksindan), a cosmetic brand (Aengbun), and a restaurant (Hyech'ŏn'gwan) are cited. The author suggests that focusing on "improvement and progress" would be more commendable than falsely claiming to be the first. For historians, this piece offers insight into early colonial Korean commercial culture, the anxieties surrounding originality versus imitation in the face of foreign influence (implied, likely Japanese or Western), and the emerging discourse on modernity and progress. It reflects a contemporary critique of superficiality and a call for substantive development.

元祖 조하하는 朝鮮사람
元祖라고 하는 말은 發明, 發見, 創造, 創始, 開闢에 가장 갓가운 말이다. 이러한 元祖를 往往 商家에서 利用하여 내가 第一 먼저 始作하엿다는 뜻으로 商品에 대한 信用을 엇고자하는 野心에서 나왓다. 眞正한 意味下에서 元祖를 使用하는 것은 別로 怪異한 것이 업겟다마는 우리 朝鮮 사람은 往往이 移植, 模倣을 元祖라고 自矜하는 일이 可笑이다. 그러나 그 元祖라고 하는 것이 自己에게 何等의 關係도 업슬 뿐만 이니라 돌이어 無識한 自己를 暴露하는데 不過한 것이다. 一例를 들면 演劑의 元祖라고 革新團이 自矜하고 化粧品界의 元祖라고 櫻紛이 自矜하고 料理의 元祖라고 惠泉館이 自矜하고 其他에 別別元祖가 다 잇지마는 이에 略하고 한마디 付托할 것은 元祖로 自矜함보다도 改良進步로 自矜하얏스면 엇더할는지.＜91＞
