**Body Text Translation**
Editorial: The Sun is Setting!!

This year of Gyeongsin (庚申), from which we, the public, earnestly expected some new meaning (新意義) to be bestowed upon this world (世間), is also, like previous years, fading away amidst suspicion (猜疑), disputes (紛糾), reflection (反省), and curses (詛呪), and its curtain is about to fall in the not-too-distant future.

Brothers! As your companion who wishes to share the same fate (運命) with you, it is my wish (*願*) that today, at the very peak of this year, we must surely reflect upon our achievements (業績) of the past year. Thus, let us earnestly calculate how much further our education has spread, how much further our industry has been promoted (振興), to what point our words (言) and actions (行) have aligned, and how much closer we have approached our ideals (理想) regarding various other matters.

It is not that I specifically wish to point out the dark side (暗黑面), but speaking for ourselves, this past year saw many plans (企劃) formulated in various aspects (方面), yet failures were almost as numerous; countless things were realized (實現), yet setbacks (挫絶) were also not few. Of course, we are not such ignoramuses (沒常識漢) as to recognize the construction of Rome (羅馬) as being accomplished in a single day, but thinking with our truly anxious hearts (情), aren't our accomplishments (事績) of this Gyeongsin year far too disappointing?

However, however, true ideals (理想) are established upon experience (經驗), and great hope lies across the path of failure (失敗). What we must simply consider here is not to merely forget the past like forgetting the heat once food has passed the throat (過喉忘慹), but to meticulously settle the various ledgers like diaries (日記帳) and account books (元帳), write the profit and loss statement (損益表) on each of our persons (紳), and cross the final meridian (經線) of this year with unhindered light attire (輕裝), that is, with a sturdy new outfit.

Ah, the sun is setting. But the New Year of hope is right ahead. Fortunate are we who will enter its embrace with a meaningful new outfit. <1>

**Summary**

This editorial reflects on the passing year (1920, Gyeongsin), expressing disappointment that despite high hopes, the year was marked by internal strife, unmet goals, and numerous setbacks in areas like education and industry. The author acknowledges the difficulty of achieving grand aims quickly but laments the perceived lack of progress. However, concluding on a hopeful note, the editorial urges readers not to simply forget past failures but to learn from them, settle accounts, and prepare with renewed determination and a "new outfit" to embrace the coming New Year. From a historical perspective, this article is interesting as it voices a sense of national frustration and disappointment with the progress under Japanese colonial rule, subtly criticizing the conditions that led to failures while simultaneously trying to inspire continued effort and hope among Koreans for national betterment. The call for reflection and preparation resonates with the nationalist movements seeking self-strengthening during this period.

기사제목	권두언 : 해는 저믈도다!!
기사형태	논설

권두언 : 해는 저믈도다!!
그래도 무슨 新意義를 이 世間에 부텨 주리라고 우리 一般이 간절히 기대하던 이 庚申 1年도 例와 가튼 猜疑, 紛糾, 反省, 詛呪의 中에 그 色이 紗薄하고 멀지 못한 時日 中에 그 幕은 나리고저 하도다.
兄弟들이여 당신네와 더부러 運命을 가티하고저 하는 이 동무의 *願이니 이해의 絶頂인 今日에 在하야 期히 過去 一年의 우리 業績을 回想함이 有하라. 그리하야 우리의 敎育은 얼마나 더 普及되엇스며 産業은 얼마나 더 振興되엇스며 그리고 우리의 言과 行은 어느 點까지 一致하얏고 其他 무엇 무엇에 對한 理想에는 얼마콤이나 더 갓가히 부텃는가를 切切히 精算함이 有하라.
구태혀 暗黑面을 指고저 함이 아니나 우리로서 말하라 하면 가고저 하는 이 一年은 各 方面으로 企劃한 것이 만은 代에 失敗가 거의 그만하얏스며 實現한 것이 無數한 代에 挫絶이 또한 一二가 아니엇도다. 勿論 우리는 羅馬의 建設이 1日에 在하얏다 認함과 如한 沒常識漢은 아니나 하도 안타까운 우리의 情으로 생각하면 庚申 1年의 우리 事績이 넘우도 섭섭치 아니한가.
그러나 그러나 참 理想은 經驗의 上에야 立하는 것이며 큰 希望은 失敗의 途에도 橫하는 것이니 여긔에 다못 우리가 생각할 것은 過喉忘慹로 한갓 過去를 잇고저 말고 日記帳, 元帳의 諸 帳簿를 着實히 精算하야 그 損益表를 各自의 紳에 書하야써 거칠것 업는 輕裝으로 即 튼튼한 새 차림으로써 이 해의 最後 經線을 越할 것뿐이다.
아아 해는 저물도다. 그러나 希望의 新年은 저 압헤 잇도다. 뜻잇는 새 차림으로써 저의 품에 들 우리는 多幸하고니.＜1＞
