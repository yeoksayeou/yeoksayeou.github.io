Concluding Words

The fresh coolness enters [?]! It is truly the time to gaze upon the lamplight.
When savoring this booklet, produced while battling the fierce heat, along with the new coolness, who would not feel memories and prospects—the spirit of severing the old and reforming the new (斬舊革新)? As I try to lay down the brush for the September issue, the wind blowing from the outskirts just happens to scatter the manuscript paper. I am made to feel anew the passage of time like [?] (如[?]).

September 1st! I remember this day last year. Fear even arises. The sea of fire (火海) across the water becomes vivid before my eyes. Already one year has passed to this day. Again, International Youth Day comes to mind. Having repeated its 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and 4th iterations, Youth Day is now said to celebrate its 10th anniversary. I wonder what kind of demonstration it will show us; that is what I am curious about.

Having spent over a month (月[?]) far away in the mountains and rivers of P'yŏngnam, battling the heat, sweat, and the rainy season, Soch'un (小春) and Ch'ŏng'o (靑吾) toured their destinations and returned safely around August 10th. They are said to hold back tears of gratitude (感淚) for the warm sympathy of the P'yŏngnam personages, and to continuously dream sweet dreams of the warm charm of P'yŏngnam's mountains and rivers. Ah, how could our relationship be so affectionate and sentimental! Ah, may we think fondly of each other (相[?]相[?]) for ten thousand years. We repeatedly pray for the good fortune of the P'yŏngnam personages.

The unexpected, tremendous heat (酷暑) beyond imagination (想[?]) in the August issue likely turned the stomachs of not only us writers (問人) but also you, our readers. Although it is certainly not our fault, considering the circumstances and situation, let me say that we should promise each other to endure for now. We do not forget our sense of responsibility. Please be generous (雅量).

The October issue will be a regular special enlarged issue. Especially, the new writing brushes of the students, who have gained real feelings and real experiences during the summer vacation, will display distinctiveness. For us who live trusting in the future, we rejoice in advance that this will be greater gospel than anything.

Flood and drought disasters strike, threatening us. It seems the vitality (半生氣) of Chosŏn's living souls has diminished due to this threat. I wonder if this too is a trial for our lives. All the more reason, let us pledge not to waver until we pass over that peak called extremity, planting our roots firmly in this layer (잇層). <43>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the September 1924 issue of *Kaebyŏk* reflects on the transition to autumn, the challenges of publishing during the summer heat, and recent events. The editor recalls the horrors of September 1st, 1923 (implicitly referencing the Great Kantō Earthquake and Korean massacre), anticipates International Youth Day, shares news of staff members Soch'un and Ch'ŏng'o returning from a trip to P'yŏngnam, apologizes for potential issues with the previous issue due to the heat, and announces a special October issue featuring student contributions. The piece concludes with a somber acknowledgment of natural disasters plaguing Korea and a call for resilience. For historians, the article offers a glimpse into the publishing world of colonial Korea, reflecting anxieties about contemporary events (Kantō Earthquake aftermath, natural disasters), hinting at intellectual networks (P'yŏngnam visit), possibly signaling leftist leanings (International Youth Day), and expressing a determination to persevere despite hardships under Japanese rule. The oblique reference to the Kantō Earthquake and massacre is particularly significant.

끗말슴
新凉이 入**! 正히 燈火를 觀할 때입니다.
炎熱과 싸와 *낸 이 冊을 新凉과 더부러 음미할 때에 누가 추억과 전망- 斬舊革新의 氣가 안이 생기랴. 구월호의 붓을 던지려할 때 맛츰 교외로 오는 바람이 원고지를 날녀버립니다. 세월의 如*를 새삼스럽게 늣기게 됩니다.
9월 1일! 작년의 이 날이 기업됩니다. 공포까지 생깁니다. 물건너 火海가 눈에 훤해집니다. 어언 1년이 지난 금일입니다. 다시 國際靑年데이가 생각남니다. 1회, 2회, 3회, 4회를 거듭한 청년데이는 이제 10회의 기념을 맛는다*니다. 엇더한 시위로서 우리에게 보여줄는지 그것이 궁금합니다.
月*를 두고 멀니 평남의 산천에서 더위와 땀과 장마와들과 싸우든 小春과 靑吾는 목적지를 두루 도라 8월 10일께 무사히 도라왓슴니다. 평남인사의 뜨거운 동정에 그들은 感淚를 含하며 평남의 산천의 따스한 정취에 그들은 단꿈을 連해 꾼다합니다. 아- 엇지면 우리의 관계가 이러케 다정다감하던고! 아 억만년 두고 相*相*이 되여지이다. 평남인사의 다복을 거듭 빔니다.
8월호의 想* 이외의 엄청난 酷暑는 우리 問人뿐 안이라 독자 여러분도 심정이 뒤집혀즐듯 합니다. 우리의 잘못은 無論안이지만 경우와 처지를 생각하야 아직 참어가자는 것을 서로 약속하자는 것을 말슴해둠니다. 책임관념은 닛지안슴니다. 雅量해 주십시오.
10월호는 정기특대호가 되겟슴니다. 특히 하기방학동안에 實感實得한 학생제군의 새로운 필봉이 이채를 낫타낼 것임니다. 장래를 밋고 사는 우리로서는 무엇 이상의 복음일 것을 미리 깃버함니다.
水災旱災가 건드려 우리를 위협합니다. 조선의 생령들은 그것의 위협에 半生氣는 주러든 듯합니다. 이것도 우리의 생에 대한 시련인가 함니다. 그럴사록 더욱 잇層에 뿌리를 짝 박고서서 극단이란 저 고개를 남기까지 흔들니지 안키를 맹세합시다.＜43＞
