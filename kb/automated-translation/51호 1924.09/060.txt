Article Title: 七八月의 世相 (Worldly Affairs of July-August)

Article Type: 소식 (News/Dispatches)

Worldly Affairs of July-August
July 11th to August 10th

(July 11th) △The Japanese Government approved the transfer of the Bank of Chosŏn (朝鮮銀行). △Japanese Minister to China, Mr. Yoshizawa (芳澤氏), departed to inspect North Sakhalin (北樺太) as a requirement for Japan-Russia negotiations. △Japanese Ambassador to the U.S., Mr. Hanihara (埴原氏), resigned and returned home as a result of the U.S. implementation of Japanese exclusion. △The French Senate passed a vote of confidence in Prime Minister Herriot (「에리오」氏) by 246 votes to 18. △The U.S. Department of Labor issued a strict prohibition order against Japanese residents in Hawaii (布哇) transferring to the mainland.

(July 12th) △The P'yŏngyang (平壤) Review Court delivered sentences for Cho Chunnyong (趙俊龍), Song Yŏngjin (宋英珍), Song Yŏngsu (宋英秀), and 21 others in the Hŭich'ŏn (熙川) Myŏngmun-dong (明文洞) police substation attack case (imprisonment for 5 years or less). △Egyptian Prime Minister Zaghloul Pasha (「작클파-사」氏) was seriously injured by an assassin. △Korean Independence Group (朝鮮獨立團) death squad member Kim Yongdŏk (金龍德) was arrested in Sinŭiju (新義州). △Over 300 Persian cavalry troops were massacred by Turkish soldiers. △In Sinan-ri (薪安里), Pogae-myŏn (寶蓋面), Ansŏng County (安城郡), several hundred peasants engaged in a major fight over the *yangban*-commoner issue. △In Sŭngbang-dong (勝芳洞), Kongbung-myŏn (公北面), Kanggye County (江界郡), Kim Yongjun (金龍俊) and his wife were shot dead by an Independence Group (獨立團) on suspicion of being spies (密偵).

(July 13th) △Over 2,000 printing workers in Kyŏngsŏng (京城 - Seoul) established the Printing Workers' Union (印刷職工組合) under the banner of the labor movement. △Over 500 tenant farmers in Amt'ae (岩泰), Muan County (務安郡), engaged in renewed negotiations with landlord Mun Chaech'ŏl (文在哲), leading to a disturbance (粉紏) and the arrest of another 10-plus people. △In Hwangch'ŏng-dong (黃淸洞), Chongsŏ-myŏn (從西面), Kanggye County (江界郡), 3 members of an Independence Group (獨立團) were shot dead by a police unit. △In Beijing Central Park, China (中國 北京), representatives from over 50 countries, along with members of parliament and over 300 students, gathered to establish the Anti-Imperialist League (反帝國主義聯盟會), declaring the unity of oppressed peoples from Korea (朝鮮), India (印度), Russia (露國), Germany (獨逸), etc. △Heavy floods occurred in the Yongch'ŏn (龍川) and Ch'ŏlsan (鐵山) areas, causing much damage to crops and village trees.

(July 14th) △In Kyŏngsŏng (京城), a discussion meeting (討議會) of concerned citizens regarding the Sidae Ilbo (時代日報) newspaper opened within the Chosŏn Education Association (朝鮮敎育協會). △An exchange of ambassadors was decided between China and Russia, with Mr. Karakhan (「카라한」氏) appointed as the Russian Ambassador to China. △The 1st International Advertising Convention (國際廣告大會) was held outside London, UK. △In the Yonghŭng River (龍興江) below Sŏn'gwansan (船冠山), Yŏnghŭng County (永興郡), 3 members of an Independence Group (獨立團) drowned while being hastily pursued by police.

(July 15th) △All Chinese workers employed by foreigners in Shameen (沙面), Guangdong (廣東), launched a general strike (同盟罷業) to protest the harshness (苛酷) of new control regulations (revised after the incident involving Governor-General Merlin). △The All-Russian Young Communist League (renamed the Leninist Communist League) convention was held in Moscow, Russia (露國 莫斯科). △In Ŭijin-dong (義眞洞), Kokha-myŏn (曲河面), Kanggye County (江界郡), 5 or 6 members of an Independence Group (獨立團) appeared again, cut telephone lines, and shot dead one spy (密偵).

(July 16th) △An Allied Powers (聯合國) conference centered on the issue of German reparations (對獨賠償問題) opened in London, UK. △In Chingnye-dong (直禮洞), Samp'ung-myŏn (三豊面), Chasŏng County (慈城郡), another member of an Independence Group (獨立團) was shot dead by a police unit. △In Kyŏngsŏng (京城), a meeting of branch managers of the Sidae Ilbo (時代日報) was held regarding the newspaper's issues. △Mr. de Valera (「때벨레나」氏), leader of the Irish Independence Party who had been arrested by Free State forces, was released. △Floods occurred in the Beijing (北京) region of China, causing many casualties among people and livestock and damage to buildings.

(July 17th) △In P'yŏngyang (平壤), heavy rain caused excessive flooding (浸水) of houses, leading to great public turmoil; in the Samnam region (三南地方), which had been suffering from severe drought (旱魃), welcome rain (甘雨) brought comfort (慰安) to the people. △The general strike in Guangdong (廣東) expanded, with over 10,000 water transport workers (水上勞働者) also striking simultaneously.

(July 18th) △The ministers of Japan, Britain, the U.S., and France stationed in China held a meeting regarding the issue of rights to the Chinese Eastern Railway (中東鐵道利權問題) in relation to the Sino-Soviet agreement (中露協約) and resolved on joint action (共同動作). △The British House of Commons rejected the government's Unemployment Protection Bill (失職者保護法案). △On the main road from Kanggye (江界) to Manp'ojin (滿浦鎭) in Sichung-myŏn (時中面), Kanggye County (江界郡), a major incident occurred where over 30 members of an Independence Group (獨立團) seized and burned (奪取 消盡) mail and cut the telephone lines again. △On the same day, in Hwangch'ŏng-dong (黃淸洞), Chongsŏ-myŏn (從西面) of the same county, peasants Son Yongjun (孫用俊), Ch'oe Yunhak (崔允學), and Pak Hakpin (朴學彬) beat the spy (密偵) Hong Inhwa (洪仁化) to death in revenge (報復) for Independence Group member Chang Ch'anghŏn (張昌憲) who was killed in action (戰死) there on the 13th, and then fled (逃走).

(July 19th) △The newly appointed Chosŏn Governor-General (朝鮮 政務總監), Shimooka Chūji (卜岡忠治氏), arrived in the capital (入城). △Japan's 49th provisional Diet session closed (閉會). △Heavy rain fell in the Haeju (海州) region, flooding over 500 households (33)戶. △The U.S. Consul stationed in Tehran (대하란), Persia (波斯), was killed (被殺) by locals while photographing the local sacred site, the "Magic Spring." △On Uhangnyŏng Pass (牛項嶺) in Chongnam-myŏn (從南面), Kanggye County (江界郡), an Independence Group (獨立團) again seized and burned (奪取 燒盡) mail (between Kanggye and Huch'ang).

(July 20th) △In Kyŏngsŏng (京城), the Ironworkers' Union (鐵工組合) was founded, based on over 3,000 ironworkers (鐵工). △Heavy rain fell in the Chaenyŏng (載寧) and Pongsan (鳳山) regions, flooding 6,000 households, causing much damage (被害) to crops and roads. △In the Kaesŏng (開城), P'aju (坡州), and Munsan (汶山) regions too, due to heavy rain, more than half of the cities were flooded, many lives were lost (傷害) (Kaesŏng 4, Munsan 18, P'aju 12, others unknown), and the Kyŏngŭi Line (京義線) was blocked (不通). △The French (佛) Prime Minister [likely Poincaré before Herriot, or Herriot reiterating a stance] rejected (拒絶) the British Prime Minister's proposal for troop withdrawal (撤兵) from the Ruhr (루얼).

(July 21st) △In P'ungho-dong (豊湖洞), Chusŏ-myŏn (州西面), Hamhŭng County (咸興郡), over 10 armed (武裝) Independence Group (獨立團) members appeared and collected (取去) military funds (軍資金) from the houses of Kim Kijŏng (金基鼎), Ch'oe Chaesun (崔在純), and others. △In Tehran (대하란), Persia (波斯), over 200 rioters (暴徒) involved in the killing of the U.S. Consul were arrested. △The British House of Commons again rejected the government's proposed Housing Bill (住宅法案). △Near Kwanhŭisa Temple (觀喜寺) in Chuimyon (朱伊面), Chŏngp'yŏng County (定平郡), a battle (交戰) occurred between an Independence Group (獨立團) and police, resulting in one member being killed (被殺).

(July 22nd) △In São Paulo (「싼바우로」), Brazil (伯刺西爾), South America (南米), over 3,000 residents were killed (被殺) due to revolutionary warfare (革命戰). △In Taegu (大邱) too, the entire city was flooded due to heavy rain, causing great chaos (大混亂) among residents. △Due to Chinese exclusion (中國人 排斥), the U.S. refused landing (上陸を拒絶) to Chinese students who arrived in San Francisco (米桑港). △In Sosu-dong (小水洞), Komyŏn (古面), Ch'osan County (楚山郡), district head (區長) Kim Ik-su (金翊洙) was shot dead by an Independence Group (獨立團) on suspicion of being a spy (密偵).

(July 23rd) △The Special Committee of the Allied Conference held in London resolved (決議) to implement the Dawes Plan (「또쓰」案) for reparations and abolish (撤廢) the Franco-Belgian economic administration (經濟管理) of the Ruhr (「루얼」) region. △The All-Russian Pastors' Conference currently being held expressed great joy at the Russian government's recognition of religion (宗敎認定). △Systematic anti-Japanese (排日) sentiment intensified in California (米加州), with the K.K.K. Party (K.K.K黨) attacking Japanese farms (日人 農園); Japanese people were preoccupied with evacuation (避亂).

(July 24th) △Over 30 Korean printing workers at the Chosŏn Printing Company (朝鮮印刷) inside Sŏsomun Gate (西小門內), Kyŏngsŏng (京城), began a strike (同盟罷業), unable to bear the harsh treatment (苛酷な待遇) by the company supervisor named Matsuhisa (松久). △The strike in Shameen (沙面), Guangdong (廣東), grew increasingly perilous (尤益 險惡化), with the U.S. and France preparing for battle (戰鬪). △The Japanese Cabinet decided on a 6-point policy towards Russia (對露方針): (1. Demand recognition of management rights (經營權) for coal mines (炭坑), especially oil fields (油田), invested in by private capitalists under Japanese military administration (軍政) during the occupation (占領), in exchange for withdrawing troops (駐兵 撤廢) from North Sakhalin (北樺太), requiring guarantees (保證). 2. Leave sufficient options acceptable to Russia for the method of apology (謝方法) regarding the Nikolayevsk Incident (尼港事件). 3. Japan shall declare its autonomous withdrawal of troops (自主的 撤兵) from North Sakhalin. 4. Regarding old treaties (舊條約), the Treaty of Portsmouth (「포스마스」條約) must be recognized and continued (其他 條約은 必要에 依하야 臨時 改訂 - other treaties to be temporarily revised as needed). 5. The issue of old debts (舊債問題) shall be deferred to a future international conference (國際會議), and the issue of prohibiting propaganda (宣傳禁止問題) shall be referred to a future Japan-Russia expert committee (日露 專門委員). 6. Upon the conclusion (成立) of an agreement (協定) based on the above outline (大體), Japan will recognize (承認) Russia, and diplomatic relations (國交) between Japan and Russia will be restored (恢復) concurrently (共히) with the signing (調印) and ratification (批準) thereof.)

(July 25th) △Areas near Kyŏngsŏng (京城) were also flooded due to heavy rain, inundating places like Ich'on-dong (二村洞) and Map'o (麻浦) along the Han River (漢江) banks. △The Profintern (?) Trade Union Congress (「인터나소날」勞働組合大會) being held in Moscow resolved to hold a World Congress of Trade Unions and selected 17 committee members. △Mr. Liu Jingren (劉境人氏) was selected as China's Ambassador to Russia. △Ch'oe Sihŭng (崔時興), leader of the Kwangboktan (光復團), was sentenced to death (死刑言渡) at the Sinŭiju (新義州) District Court.

(July 26th) △The revolutionary turmoil (革命亂) in Brazil (伯國), South America, grew increasingly fierce (去益 猛烈), with the Federal Army (聯邦軍) declaring a decisive battle against the Revolutionary Army (革軍) and demanding the withdrawal (撤退) of residents. △In Xingjing County (興京縣), South Manchuria (南滿), a clash (衝突) occurred between the Chosŏn Independence Army's Righteous Government (朝鮮獨立軍統義府) and Chinese troops over the issue of forced seizure (無理奪取) of firearms, resulting in the deaths (被死) of 3 of our Independence Group members and 4 Chinese soldiers (date unknown 日字 未詳). △In Songmyo-dong (松廟洞), Nam-myŏn (南面), Ch'osan County (楚山郡), it is reported (傳) that the Independence Army (獨立軍) shot dead 14 pro-Japanese Koreans (親日鮮人) and 1 Chinese person. Also, they shot dead Kim Pyŏngik (金炳益) of Sŏngsŏ-dong (城西洞), set fire (放火) to the house of Yi Ch'andŭk (李贊得) in Ch'angudong (倉圩洞), and abducted (押去) his wife, Kim Kiman (金基萬?). △The British House of Commons finally passed (遂 通過) the government's proposed Housing Bill.

(July 27th) △The Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs submitted a protest (抗議) to the Japanese government regarding Japan's increase (引上) in luxury goods taxes (奢侈品稅). △A steamship collision (衝突) occurred off the coast of Sakhalin (樺太), Japan, resulting in the drowning (溺死) of over 260 crew members (船員) and passengers (乘客). △The Anti-Imperialist Movement Grand Alliance (反帝國主義運動大聯盟會) in Beijing invited press corps (記者團) from various places, and the press corps participated (加擔) in publicizing this movement.

(July 28th) △In the Huch'ang County (厚昌郡) border area, a battle (交戰) occurred between an Independence Group (獨立團) and police, resulting in the death in action (戰死) of Independence Group member Kim Hyŏn'guk (金賢國). △The Allied Conference ongoing in London, UK, approved inviting German representatives (獨逸代表) and the Reparations Commission (賠償委員).

(July 29th) △A treaty (協約) was signed (調印) between Russia and Germany. The Mexican (墨西哥) government officially (正式) recognized Soviet Russia (勞農露國). △The Central Executive Committee of the Japanese Peasants' Union (日本農民組合) resolved (決議) on the establishment (樹立) of a proletarian party (無産政黨) and land nationalization (土地國有). △Four British airplanes (飛行機) dropped bombs (爆彈を投下) on the Wazir (「와질」) tribe in the area southeast of Razmak (?) (「라즈야소크」) and all crashed (墜落) during their return. (34)

(July 30th) △In Yongam-ri (龍岩里), Sŏnp'o-myŏn (仙沼面?), Sunch'ŏn County (順川郡), a ferry boat (渡船) capsized (顚覆), and of the over 60 people on board (乘船人), 24 tragically died (慘死) by drowning (溺水). △In the northern China regions of Zhangjiakou (張家口), Beijing (北京), and Tianjin (天津), massive floods caused thousands of fatalities and washed away (流失) 400,000 houses. △The Allied Conference reached consensus (一致) on organizing (組織) an arbitration committee (仲裁委員會) regarding the question of which country would arbitrate (仲裁) should Germany default (不履行) on the Dawes Plan (「또스」案). △A conspiracy (陰謀) for a major uprising (大暴動) was discovered (發覺) in Romania (羅馬尼), and nationwide (全國的) martial law (戒嚴令) was proclaimed (布告).

(July 31st) △The German government submitted proposals (案) to the Allied Conference, including resolution of non-League of Nations (國際聯盟) rules, non-violent resolution, establishment of an arbitration tribunal (仲裁所), and general arms limitation (軍費制限). △The ministers of Japan, Britain, the U.S., and France stationed in China held a meeting regarding the Chinese Eastern Railway rights issue (中東鐵道利權問題). △Russian Ambassador to China, Mr. Karakhan (「카라한」氏), presented (捧呈) his credentials (國書) to the Chinese government. △Over 90,000 electricians (電工) and workers (職工) in Tokyo, Japan (日本 東京), carried out (決行) a general strike.

(August 1st) △In Panghyŏn Namsi (方峴南市), Kusŏng County (龜城郡), a battle (交戰) occurred between an Independence Group (獨立團) and police, resulting in 11 Japanese policemen (日巡査) suffering serious injuries (重傷). △The French Chamber of Deputies went into indefinite recess (無期休會). △In the Sinŭiju (新義州) District Court, the public trial (公判) opened for Pyŏkch'ang Volunteer Corps (壁昌義勇團) members Yang Sŭngu (楊承雨) and Han Tongjin (韓東振), who had shot dead numerous spies (密偵). △The Pacific Conference (太平洋會議) opened in Honolulu (「호놀루」), U.S. (regarding territorial preservation (諸領 保存) and commerce issues (通商問題) - likely the Institute of Pacific Relations conference). △The Chinese Chamber of Deputies, currently in session, fell into great disorder (大混亂) over the speaker issue (議長問題) and adjourned (流會).

(August 2nd) △The Allied Conference adopted (採用) the French proposal and simultaneously issued an urgent request (至急孤送狀) to Germany to send representatives. △The Anglo-Soviet negotiations (英露交涉), which had been temporarily recessed, reconvened (再會議).

(August 3rd) △The Catholic Church (天主敎堂) in Chungnim-dong (中林洞), Kyŏngsŏng (京城), was damaged (破壞) by a lightning strike (落雷). △Germany accepted the Allied Conference's invitation, and representatives including Chancellor Marx (「막쓰」氏) and four others headed to London. As this day was the anniversary (記念日) of the start of the Great European War, Germany flew flags at half-mast (半旗を揭) to remember (追憶) the nation's downfall (祖國 沒落). △The police substation in Tokso-myŏn (篤所面), Musan County (茂山郡), was attacked (襲擊) by an Independence Group (獨立團), and its security weapons (警備武器) were seized (被奪).

(August 4th) △Britain refused (拒絶) China's request regarding the issue of elevating the rank (昇格問題) of its minister stationed in China, citing (標榜) the need for consultation (商議) with other powers (列國). △Anglo-Soviet negotiations (英露交涉) once again fell into crisis (危機に陷). △In Ch'angjŏn (菖田), Huch'ang County (厚昌郡), 3 members of an Independence Group (獨立團) drowned (溺死) due to their boat capsizing (顚覆). △Residents of Pongsan (鳳山) and Chaenyŏng (載寧) counties submitted a petition (陳情書) to the Governor-General authorities (總督當局) requesting river embankment construction (河川堤防) as a countermeasure (對策) against the perennial flood damage (年來 水災).

(August 5th) △The Japan-Russia negotiations (日露交涉), which had been temporarily stalled (停滯), reconvened (再會議) in Beijing. △The Kyŏngsŏng Sidae Ilbo Discussion Meeting (京城時代日報 討議會) held another general meeting, changed its name to the Poch'ŏn'gyo Denunciation Society (普天敎聲討會), and resolved (決議) to investigate and eradicate (調査撲滅) the Poch'ŏn'gyo Church (普天敎會) and those associated with it (關係者). △In Sangsŏ-ri (上西里), T'aep'yŏng-myŏn (泰平面), Nyŏngbyŏn County (寧邊郡), a battle (交戰) occurred between an Independence Group (獨立團) and police, resulting in the deaths in action (戰死) of 2 Independence Group members and 1 policeman. △France also opposed (反對) elevating its minister's rank in China, giving the reason that it was premature (時機尙早). △Soviet Russia (勞農露國) proclaimed (布告) the nationalization of real estate (不動産國有) in its Far Eastern provinces. △Over 3,000 Korean compatriots (朝鮮同胞) residing (在住) in Osaka (大阪) held a large speech and discussion meeting (大演討會) denouncing (彈劾) the suppression (壓迫) of speech and assembly (言論集會), during which 25 speakers (演士) were arrested (儉束).

(August 6th) △In Mundŏk-dong (文德洞), Ch'osan County (楚山郡), a battle (交戰) between an Independence Group (獨立團) and police resulted in one Independence Group member being killed (被殺). △A strong earthquake (强震) occurred again in the Tokyo area, causing great commotion (大騷動). △In Kowloon City (九龍市), Guangdong (廣東), China, a clash (衝突) erupted between Yunnan troops (雲南軍) and the Merchant Corps Army (商團軍), leading to violence including arson (放火), looting (掠奪), and rape (姦淫), leaving the city in a state of total destruction (全滅狀態). △Pak Kyŏngsŏng (朴庚星), head (面長) of Ch'ŏnma-myŏn (天摩面), Kusŏng County (龜城郡), and one other person were shot dead (銃殺) by an Independence Group (獨立團). Also, in Chosan-dong (造山洞), T'aep'yŏng-myŏn (泰平面), Nyŏngbyŏn County (寧邊郡), a battle (交戰) occurred between an Independence Group (獨立團) and police, resulting in the deaths in action (戰死) of 2 members and 1 policeman.

(August 7th) △The Allied summit meeting (聯合國 巨頭會議) discussed (討議) the German proposals (獨逸提案). The German proposals were: 1. German representatives shall also attend (列席) deliberations (審議) on the issue of German debt default (債務不履行). 1. Withdrawal (「루얼」地方撤退) from the Ruhr region shall be implemented more quickly (速키) than the period designated by the Allied Powers. - Release (放免) of political prisoners (政治犯人) from the Ruhr region and permission for exiles abroad (國外亡命人) to return home (歸國許可). 1. Refusal (拒絶) to deliver substitute goods (代用品 引渡) of a fixed amount (一定額) after the period set by the Treaty of Versailles (「벨사이유」條約).

(August 8th) △Pyŏkch'ang Volunteer Corps (壁昌義勇團) member Yang Sŭngu (楊承雨) was sentenced to death (死刑), and Han Tongjin (韓東振) to life imprisonment (無期懲役) (at the Sinŭiju District Court 新義州 地方法院). △The long-negotiated Anglo-Soviet Treaty (英露條約) was signed (調印).

(August 9th) △An Independence Group (獨立團) attacked (襲擊) the Kŭmgwang (金鑛 - gold mine) in Choak-tong (造岳洞), Kwansŏ-myŏn (館西面), Kusŏng County (龜城郡), leading to a major incident involving the shooting death (銃殺) of 1 policeman, arson (放火) of the office (事務所), and abduction (押去) of 11 miners (鑛夫). △The Hawaii Expedition Baseball Team (布哇遠征 野球團) from the Kyŏngsŏng Central YMCA (京城 中央基督敎靑年會) returned home (歸國). △In Yumoto-machi (陽本町?), Fukushima Prefecture (福島縣), Japan, a gas (瓦斯) explosion (爆發) occurred in a coal mine (炭鑛), causing the tragic deaths (慘死) of over 100 miners. △Japan-Russia negotiations (日露交涉) stalled (停滯) again over the issue of oil field rights (油田 利權問題). △In the Yangdŏk-tong (陽德洞) gold mine, Ch'ŏngsan-myŏn (靑山面), Ch'angsŏng County (昌城郡), 5 policemen were instantly killed (卽死) by an Independence Group (獨立團), and T'aech'ŏn Township Head (泰川面長) Pae Tonggyun (裵東均) and the Ch'ŏngsan Township Head (靑山面長) (name unspecified 某) suffered serious injuries (重傷).

**Summary**

This article from the September 1924 issue of *Kaebyŏk* magazine chronicles major news events from July 11th to August 10th, 1924. It covers a wide range of topics including international diplomacy (Japan-Russia negotiations over Sakhalin, Allied reparations talks with Germany, Sino-Soviet relations, Anglo-Soviet treaty), political events (French confidence vote, Irish leader's release, Brazilian revolution), social issues (labor strikes in Korea and China, US anti-immigrant policies), natural disasters (major floods across Korea and China, earthquake in Japan), and significant activities of the Korean independence movement, primarily armed clashes, assassinations of suspected collaborators, and fundraising efforts in northern Korea and Manchuria. The article juxtaposes events within Korea under Japanese rule with global developments, highlighting both local resistance and the broader international context. For historians, this article provides a valuable snapshot of the intense anti-Japanese resistance activity, particularly the armed struggle by various "Independence Groups" (獨立團) in the northern border regions and Manchuria, detailing specific incidents like attacks on police stations, ambushes, assassinations of officials and alleged spies (密偵), and clashes with Chinese forces. It also reflects the growing labor movement (printing/ironworker unions), social tensions (landlord-tenant disputes, *yangban*-commoner conflicts), the impact of natural disasters, and the close monitoring of international affairs relevant to Korea's situation (e.g., Japan's diplomatic maneuvering with Russia and China, anti-imperialist movements).

七八月의 世相
7月 11日부터 8月 10日까지
(7月 11日) △日本政府는 朝鮮銀行 移管을 可決하고 △日駐中公使 芳澤氏는 日露交涉의 要件으로 北燁太視察에 出發하고 △日駐米大使 埴原氏는 米國이 排日實施의 結果-辭任 歸國하고 △佛國 上院은 18票 對 246票로 首相 「에리오」氏 信任案을 可決하고 △米國 勞働省은 布哇 在留日人의 大陸轉航嚴禁令을 發하다.

(同 12日) △平壤覆審法院에서는 熙川 明文洞 駐在所 襲擊事件의 趙俊龍, 宋英珍, 宋英秀 外 21名의 判決 言渡가 有하고(懲役 5年 以下) △埃及 首相 「작클파-사」氏는 엇던 刺客에게 重傷을 負하고 △朝鮮獨立團 決死隊員 金龍德은 新義州에서 逮捕를 當하고 △波斯의 300餘 騎兵隊는 土耳其 兵에게 慘殺을 當하고 △安城郡 寶蓋面 薪安里에서는 農民 數百名이 兩班 상놈 問題로 一大 格鬪를 起하고 △江界郡 公北面 勝芳洞의 金龍俊 夫婦는 密偵의 嫌으로 獨立團에게 銃殺을 當하다.

(同 13日) △京城의 印刷職工 2,000餘名은 勞働運動의 旗幟下에 印刷職工組合을 創立하고 △務安郡 岩泰 小作人 500餘名은 地主 文在哲과 再次 交涉하다가 粉紏를 起하야 다시 10餘名이 檢擧되고 △江界郡 從西面 黃淸洞에서는 獨立團 3名이 警官隊에게 銃殺을 當하고 △中國 北京 中央公園에서는 50餘國 代表 及 議員 學生 300餘名이 會集하야 反帝國主義聯盟會를 創立하야 朝鮮, 印度, 露國, 獨逸 等의 被壓迫民族의 聯合을 聲明하고 △龍川 鐵山 方面에는 大洪水가 出하야 農作 及 村木의 損失이 多하다.

(同 14日) △京城에는 時代日報에 對한 社會有志의 討議會가 朝鮮敎育協會內에 開하고 △中國과 露國間에는 大使交換이 決定되야 露國의 駐中大使는 「카라한」氏가 被任되고 △英國 倫敦市外에는 第 1回 國際廣告大會가 開催되고 △永興郡 船冠山下 龍興江에는 獨立團 3名이 警官의 追從에 急하야 溺死하다.

(同 15日) △廣東 沙面에 在한 外國人 使用 中國 勞働者 一同은 新取締規則(멜란總督 遭亂後 更定) 苛酷에 反對하야 同盟罷業을 起하고 △露國 莫斯科에는 全露靑年共産黨(레린共産黨으로 改稱) 大會가 開催되고 △江界郡 曲河面 義眞洞에는 又 復 獨立團 5, 6名이 出現하야 電線을 斷絶하고 密偵 1名을 銃殺하다.

(同 16日) △英國 倫敦에는 對獨賠償問題를 中心한 聯合國 會議가 開催되고 △慈城郡 三豊面 直禮洞에서는 獨立團 1名이 又 警官隊에게 銃殺을 當하고 △京城에는 時代日報問題로 同報 支局長會議가 開催되고 △愛蘭 獨立黨 首領으로 自由國軍에게 逮捕되얏든 「때벨레나」氏는 放釋되고 △中國 北京 地方에는 洪水가 有하야 人畜의 死傷 及 建物의 損失이 多出되다.

(同 17日) △平壤에는 大雨로 因하야 家屋의 浸水 過多로 人心이 大騷動되고 旱魃이 甚하던 三南地方에는 甘雨로 因하야 人心이 慰安되고 △廣東의 同盟罷業은 擴大되야 1萬餘名의 水上勞働者도 一齊 罷業하다.

(同 18日) △駐中 日英米佛 4國 公使는 中露協約에 對한 中東鐵道利權問題로 會議를 開하야 共同動作을 決議하고 △英國 下院은 政府의 失職者保護法案을 否決하고 △江界郡 時中面 江界서 滿浦鎭 一等路上에는 獨立團 30餘名이 郵便物을 奪取 消盡하고 又 전선을 斷한 大事가 出하고 △同日 同郡 從西面 黃淸洞에서는 農民 孫用俊, 崔允學, 朴學彬 3名은 13日 同地에서 戰死한 獨立團 張昌憲의 報復으로 密偵 洪仁化를 打殺 逃走하다.

(同 19日) △新任 朝鮮 政務總監 卜岡忠治氏 入城하고 △日本의 第49臨時議會는 閉會되고 海州地方에는 大雨가 降하야 民家 500餘(33)戶가 浸水되고 △波斯 「대하란」駐在 米國領事는 當地 靈書 「魔力의 泉」을 撮影하다가 當地人에게 被殺되고 △江界郡 從南面 牛項嶺에는 又 復 獨立團이 郵便物을 奪取 燒盡(江界 厚昌 間)하다.

(同 20日) △京城에는 3,000餘 名의 鐵工을 基礎로 하야 鐵工組合이 創立되고 △載寧 鳳山地方에는 大雨가 降하야 民家 6,000戶가 浸水되고 農作物 及 道路의 被害가 多出되고△ 開城 坡州 汶山地方에도 大雨로 因하야 全市가 半以上이 浸水되고 人命의 傷害(開城 4名, 汶山 18名, 坡州 12名, 其他 未詳)도 多出되고 京義線은 不通되고 △佛白 首相은 英 首相의 提案인 「루얼」 撤兵을 拒絶하다.

(同 21日) △咸興郡 州西面 豊湖洞에는 武裝獨立團 10餘名이 出現하야 金基鼎, 崔在純 等 家에서 軍資金을 取去하고 △波斯 「대하란」에서는 米國 領事 被殺事件의 暴徒 200餘名을 逮捕하고 △英國 下院은 政府提案인 住宅法案을 又復 否決하고 △定平郡 朱伊面 觀喜寺 附近에서는 獨立團 對 警官의 交戰이 有하야 團員 1名이 被殺되다.
