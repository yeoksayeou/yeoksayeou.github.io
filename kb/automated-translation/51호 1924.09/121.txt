西京夜話 (Night Tales of Sŏgyŏng [P'yŏngyang])

◇P'yŏngyang, like other cities, has both old and new city districts. The old district is inhabited by Koreans and Japanese who came during the old Korean Empire era, while the new district is inhabited by newly arrived Japanese. However, if we dissect and examine these two districts further: Suyuk-ri has many inns (旅舘業); along the old and new roads of Nammun-t'ong, there are many Western clothing merchants (洋服商) and rubber shoe merchants (고무신 商); Ia-gyo has a food market (食料品塲); Sŏmun-nae (Haktang-kol) has many sellers of meat (獸肉販賣) and general goods dealers (雜貨業者); Ch'aegwan-ri and Sŏram-ri are kisaeng quarters (妓生窟); Kang'an-t'ong (Sinch'ang-ri) has restaurants (料理店) and lumber merchants (材木商); Sinch'ang-ri has fruit merchants (果物商); outside the Ch'ilsŏngmun earth fortress (土城外) live the poor (貧民); Kyoguch'ŏng has rubber factories (고무工塲); Changbyŏl-ri has sock factories (洋襪工塲); Sŏmun-naeoe has schools (學校); Chin-jŏng has the licensed prostitution district (遊廓); Chŏn'gu-ri, Sŏmun-oe, and Kyoguch'ŏng have laborers (勞働者); Nammun-t'ong has antique dealers (古物商); and Sangha Suyuk-ri has many unlicensed brothels (密賣春家).

◇P'yŏngyang has traditionally been famous for its kisaeng and kwari artisans/vendors (과리). However, recently, the kwari artisans/vendors have moved to Chinnamp'o, and their numbers <66> have greatly decreased. The things newly increasing are only beggars (乞人), Japanese, paegi (小弓) gambling, and unlicensed prostitutes (密賣者). It is impossible to know whether this is a sign of prosperity or decline.

◇Since the topic of kisaeng came up briefly, let me elaborate. P'yŏngyang is the seedbed (苗床) for kisaeng. Since earlier times, anyone of reasonable means would customarily enroll their daughter in a kisaeng school (妓生書齋), dreaming of future wealth and nobility, as if preferring daughters over sons (不重生男重生女格). However, while in the past they might become concubines (妾) of governors (監司) or magistrates (守令), enjoying luxury and accumulating wealth, today they merely prey on the spines of vagrants (浮浪者). Therefore, when the financial world (財界) experiences a panic (恐慌), they are the first to be affected. Lately, business has been so bad that, let alone going to Kangsŏ Yaksu for summer retreat (避暑), they cannot even afford to buy a mountain-heap-like bowl of cold noodles (冷麵) for fifteen chŏn, nor can they freely drink ice (어름), sold at five chŏn per gourd dipper, leaving their throats quite dry, it seems. Nevertheless, perhaps because that's all they can do, even now there are over eighty child kisaeng (童妓) attending kisaeng schools, and over one hundred forty kisaeng with business licenses (營業狀).

◇Anyway, among them, those who are particularly cherished and popular are said to be: the composed and graceful Wŏnsanwŏl; the heavy-drinking Ch'oe Nanhong (alias Sudden Storm); the passionate and sorrowful Kang Yŏnhwa; the skillful dancer Wang Kihwa; the lively Kang Tohwa; Chang Haksŏn, Kim Kŭmhwa, and Kim Nongwŏl who are good at singing the 'Song of Melancholy' (愁心歌); Cho Wŏlsăek who is good at Southern Provincial folk songs (南道雜歌); the charming Hong Ch'unhong; the easily weeping O Sanwŏl; the snorting No Sanwŏl; the dry-lipped, tooth-baring Chŏng Yŏngwŏl (talented in calligraphy and painting); the veteran kisaeng Mun Myŏngok; the simple Yi Ch'wihong; the demure Im Sanwŏl; Kim Ongnan, who resembles a painted boy from a mountain spirit shrine (山神閣畵童); <67> and others like Yi Unson, Kim Yŏngwŏl (talented in calligraphy and painting), and so on. However, a certain man of refined tastes remarks that since the short-haired beauty Ch'ae Kŭmhong quit the kisaeng profession to sell cold noodles, and since Kim Okyŏp – who was considered first-rate in P'yŏngyang, rumored to have charmed the entire P'yŏngan Namdo Provincial Office (平南道廳), and nearly committed a love suicide with Governor Yoneda (米田知事) on the Taedong River – went to Kyŏngsŏng (Seoul) for studies (when Governor Yoneda first arrived, while boating on the Taedong River, he saw Okyŏp in a nearby boat and tried to grab her hand, nearly falling into the water), and since Kang Kihwa, skilled in calligraphy and painting, went to Kyŏngsŏng, the P'yŏngyang world of flowers and willows (花柳界 - i.e., the kisaeng world) has become desolate.

◇While chatting idly, let me add another word. When Police Affairs Director Maruyama (丸山警務局長) was accompanying Governor-General Saitō (齋藤總督) on a tour of the border region, he stopped in P'yŏngyang and went to Changch'un-gwan restaurant at someone's invitation (招得). Being drunk, he went into another room and complained about not being given beer (麥酒), nearly getting his gourd-head smashed into a dozen pieces by the guests (座客) there. Fortunately, those guests were associated with the courthouse (裁判所), and they also realized it was Mr. Maruyama, so the incident ended without issue.

◇Furthermore, famous local foods include fish and abalone soup (魚鰒장국) before meals, meat broth (맥물[肉汁]) at night, fish porridge (魚粥) during boat excursions (船遊), and nŏch'i (노치 [부침이] - pancakes) in autumn (秋期). Cold noodles (冷麵) and distilled spirits (燒酒) are available in all four seasons. Also, ice (氷), corn (玉蜀藜), melons (참외 [Kangsŏ apples]), dog meat stew (狗醬), and watermelon (수박 [from Nŭngna-do]) are plentiful in summer. Taedong River smelt (秀魚) and P'yŏngyang beef (牛肉) and pork (猪肉) are also famous. Additionally, straw sandals (집신), cord/string (목), and belts (腰帶) are famous local products (名産), and there are quite many makers/sellers of tanggŏn hats (宕巾장이) on the streets. <68>

**Summary**

This article from 1924 describes the city of P'yŏngyang, detailing the distinct characteristics of its old and new districts and the types of businesses or populations found in specific areas like Suyuk-ri, Nammun-t'ong, and Sŏmun-nae. It notes the decline of traditional 'kwari' artisans/vendors and the perceived rise of beggars, Japanese residents, gambling, and unlicensed prostitution. A significant portion discusses the state of P'yŏngyang's kisaeng, lamenting their declining economic fortunes despite their continued presence and training, contrasting their current struggles with past prestige. It names several prominent kisaeng of the time and recounts anecdotes involving famous kisaeng like Kim Okyŏp and Japanese officials like Governor Yoneda and Police Director Maruyama. The piece concludes by listing P'yŏngyang's famous foods, drinks, and local products.

For historians, this article offers a valuable glimpse into the socio-economic landscape of colonial P'yŏngyang in the mid-1920s. It highlights the spatial segregation between Koreans and Japanese, the changing urban economy, the precarious situation of traditional entertainers like kisaeng under colonial modernity, and provides specific details about local culture, neighborhoods, notable figures (both Korean and Japanese), and everyday life, including food and trade. The anecdotes involving Japanese officials offer insights into colonial power dynamics and social interactions.

◇平壤도 다른 都會가티 新舊兩市街가 잇는데 舊市街는 朝鮮人과 舊韓國時代에 온 日本人이 살고 新市街에는 新入한 日本人이 산다, 그런데 그 두 市街를 다시 解剖하야 살펴보면 水玉里에는 旅舘業이 만코 南門通 新舊路邊에는 洋服商, 고무신 商이 만코 二衙橋에는 食料品塲이 만코 西門內(學堂골)에는 獸肉販賣及雜貨業者가 만코, 釵貫里와 薛岩里는 妓生窟이오, 江岸通(新倉里)는 料理店과 材木商, 新倉里는 果物商 七星門土城外에는 貧民, 橋口町은 고무工塲, 莊別里는 洋襪工塲, 西門內外는 學校, 賑町은 遊廓, 磚九里, 西門外, 橋口町은 勞働者 南門通는 古物商, 上下水口里는 密賣春家가 만타 ◇平壤은 自來로 妓生과 과리가 名物이엿다 그런대 近來에 과리는 鎭南浦로 移徙를 가서 數가 퍽 ＜66＞減해지고 새로 느러가는 것은 乞人, 日本人, 백이(小弓)賭博, 密賣者이니 이것이 잘되는 徵兆인지 못되는 徵兆인지 알 수 업다 ◇妓生말이 조곰 낫스닛가 말이지 平壤온妓生의 모자리판(苗床)이다 前日부터 웬만한 사람이면 依例이 을 妓生書齋에 느어서 不重生男重生女格으로 將來의 富貴를 귀고 잇다, 그러나 前日에는 監司守令의 妾이나 되야서 호강도 하고 돈도 모앗지만은 今日에는 但只 浮浪者의 등골이나 치여다 보는 故로 財界가 恐慌이 생기면 몬저 影響을 밧는데 요새이는 아주 흥정이 업서서 江西藥水에 避暑가는 것은 구만두고 十五錢에 山덥이가튼 冷麵 한 그릇도 잘 사먹지 못하고 五錢에 한 박아지식 되는 어름도 마음대로 못먹어서 목이 밧작 마르는 모양이다 그래도 분는 것이 그이닛가 그러한지 只今도 妓生書齋에 다니는 童妓가 八十餘名이오 營業狀가진 妓生이 百四十餘名이다 ◇何如間其中에도 귀염만이 밧고 잘불리기는 沉着하고 얌잔한 元山月, 술잘먹는 崔蘭紅 (別名猝地風波) 多情多恨의 姜蓮花, 춤잘추는 王琪花, 活潑한 康桃花, 愁心歌 잘하는 張鶴仙, 金錦花, 金弄月 南道雜歌 잘하는 趙月色, 愛嬌만흔 洪春紅, 울기 잘하는 吳山月, 코웃둑이 盧山月, 乾脣露齒 鄭暎月(有書畵才) 장수妓生 文明玉, 수수한 李翠紅, 얌잔한 任山月, 山神閣畵童가튼 金玉蘭 ＜67＞其他 李雲仙, 金暎月(有書畵才) 等이란다 그러나 短髮美人 蔡錦紅이가 妓業을 구만두고 冷麵 장사를 하게 되고 平壤에서 第一流로 平南道廳을 다 드러먹다 십히 하고 米田知事와 大同江에서 情死할번하던 金玉葉이가 京城에 가서 留學을 하고 (米田知事到任初에 大同江에서 船遊하는데 隣船에 잇는 玉葉을 보고 손을 잡다가 一足이 물에 진 일이 잇다) 書畵잘하던 姜琪花가 京城으로 간 後에는 平壤花柳界가 寂寞하다고 某風流郞이 말한다 ◇雜談하는 中에  한마듸하자 丸山警務局長이 齋藤總督과 國境巡廻하러 갈 에 平壤에 들녓다가 누구의 招得로 長春舘에를 갓는데 술이 醉해서 다른 房에를 가가지고 麥酒안이 준다고 투정하다가 座客에게 하마터면 뒹박머리가 열아문조각이 날번하얏는데 多幸이 其座客은 裁判所축들이고  丸山君인줄 알게되야 無事하얏다구 ◇ 飮食의 名物은 食前에는 魚鰒장국 밤에는 맥물(肉汁), 船遊에는 魚粥, 秋期에는 노치, (부침이) 冷麵 燒酒는 四時에 다잇고, 그러고 氷, 玉蜀藜, 참외(江西사과) 狗醬, 수박(綾羅島) 이 여름에는 만흐며 大同江秀魚와 平壤牛肉猪肉도 著名하고, 尙又집신, 목, 腰帶도 名産이오, 거리에 宕巾장이도 퍽 만타,＜68＞
