**Body Text Translation:**
Song of Twenty Years Old
						Sŏktong
Last summer, while climbing "Yŏnjuam" with Mr. S from Kap neighborhood in Seoul

1
"This rice, or whatever it is, is all dried out and hard,
My tongue feels rough, how can I eat this?"
Hey, man. Is that such a big deal?
Let’s just soak it well in cold water and eat it.
At our age of twenty, in our prime,
Even if we chewed stones, wouldn't they digest?

2
"These clothes, or whatever they are, are all tattered,
Flapping in the wind, how can I go anywhere?"
Hey, man. Is that such a big deal?
Let's just look straight ahead and keep walking.
At our age of twenty, in our youth,
Even wearing rags, where could we not go?

3
"These shoes, or whatever they are, are all loose,
How can I walk, especially on this steep path?"
Hey, man. Is that such a big deal?
If it's really a problem, let's just go barefoot.
At our age of twenty, in our green years,
Is there any path where we would stop along the way? <18>

**Summary**

This poem depicts a conversation between two young men, likely the speaker and his friend "Mr. S," facing minor hardships like unpalatable food, tattered clothes, and worn-out shoes during a climb. The speaker consistently rebuffs his friend's complaints with youthful bravado, emphasizing their resilience and unstoppable spirit at the age of twenty. The poem celebrates youthful energy, optimism, and the ability to overcome obstacles, dismissing material discomforts as trivial. For historians, the poem offers a glimpse into the mindset of Korean youth during the colonial period. Despite the implied backdrop of potential hardship (poor food, clothes, shoes), the focus is on internal strength, perseverance, and a forward-looking attitude, which could reflect a coping mechanism or a subtle expression of national resilience under occupation.

스무살노래
						石童
지난 여름, 서울서 갑동네 S군과 「戀主岩에」 오르면서

1
『밥인지 뭣인지 온, 꼬드라저서
혓바닥이 깔깔해 어디 먹겟나』
아따, 이 사람아. 좀, 그러면 대순가.
찬 물에 푹푹꺼서 먹세나그려.
우리나아 스믈이라 함창시절에
차돌을 까먹은들 아니 삭을까.

2
『옷인지 뭣인지 온 다 떠러저서
바람에 펄렁이니 어디 가겟나.』
아따, 이 사람아. 좀, 그러면 대순가.
그저 앞만 바라보고 걷세나 그려.
우리나이 스믈이라 젊은 시절에
누덱이를 입엇기로 어딜 못갈까.

3
『신인지 뭣인지 온, 헐렁거려서
더구나 비탈길을 어디 걷겟나.』
아따, 이 사람아. 좀, 그러면 대순가.
정, 뭘하면 맨발로 가세그려.
우리나이 스믈이라 푸른 시절에
가다가 멈출 길이 어디 잇는가.＜18＞
＜18＞
