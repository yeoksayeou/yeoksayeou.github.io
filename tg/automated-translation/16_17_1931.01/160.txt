**Body Text Translation**
**On Hearing of the Revival of *Tonggwang***

**Praying for Eternal Life from Resurrection**
Chosŏn Products Promotion Association (朝鮮物産獎勵會)
Myŏng Che-se (明濟世)

When *Tonggwang* (東光), which was the light of our East Asia (東亞), was temporarily suspended, it is fair to say that our OO groaned in near darkness, it was so desolate. Therefore, I, as one molecule of OO, sincerely prayed day after day for its resurrection to break through personal distress and the desolation of OO. As the proverb says, "sincerity moves heaven," perhaps moved by the sincerity of the public, identical to mine, I now sincerely pray again that it will be healthy going forward and live eternally. Lastly, I also pray for the health of all the members [of the magazine staff].

**May It Become the Light of the East**

I congratulate *Tonggwang* for coming out into the world again. I was ordered to write on "Greeting the Sinmi Year (辛未年)" or "Random Thoughts (隨感)," but due to a change of address, I only received the postcard yesterday, and there is nothing I can write hastily to meet the deadline. Also, I have nothing particular written down. From the next opportunity, I hope to gain the honor of having something, however unworthy, published in your esteemed journal.
It is said that several new magazines will come out this winter. Compared to the declining autumn,<40> I believe there will be much more vitality. Among them, *Tonggwang* shows various strengths. First, though young, it has an impressive history; second, it has a definite principle and viewpoint; third, the editorial members are masters in this field (斯界), and at the same time, their personal lives are relatively stable; and fourth, the fact that there are reserve funds (積立金) might be a strong point. The final balance sheet of 1930 shows nothing more than decline and decay. Entering the Sinmi Year, I sincerely hope it will be a year of construction with immortal vitality. I pray and celebrate that the life of your esteemed journal *Tonggwang* will be long, that it will develop greatly, truly become the flesh and blood of the populace, and extending further, become the light of the East (東方). I substitute this simple message for a greeting.

**Perseverance and Endurance**
P'yŏngyang O Kyŏng-suk (平壤 吳敬淑)

*Tonggwang*! *Tonggwang*! *Tonggwang*, which I thought was dead. The pulse of life (生脉) was just submerged. Hearing that *Tonggwang* is being revived as we greet the Sinmi Year, we, who do not know what brings joy even when greeting the new year, are truly thankful and happy. Moreover, for us comrades, it is even more cause for joyful celebration. As the red sunlight rises in the Eastern Sky (東天) on New Year's Day (元旦) of the Sinmi Year, *Tonggwang* reappears with all the preparation and experience from after its suspension, offering boundless hope, like the progress of that time, aiding our aspirations. Everything must balance income and expenditure. Passivity and activity are the same. Match spiritual income with material expenditure. Everything else is also the same. Esteemed members of the *Tonggwang* executive staff! Strive for Perseverance and Endurance (堅忍持久), making balancing the accounts (收支) the first priority (先務). <41>

**Summary**

This article comprises three short congratulatory messages celebrating the republication (*pokkan*, 復刊) of the magazine *Tonggwang* in January 1931. The contributors (Myŏng Che-se of the Chosŏn Products Promotion Association, an unnamed writer, and O Kyŏng-suk from P'yŏngyang) express immense relief and joy at the magazine's return after a period of suspension, likened to darkness. They convey high hopes for its future, emphasizing its potential role as a vital source of enlightenment ("light of the East") and sustenance for the Korean populace ("flesh and blood of the populace") in the new Sinmi year. The second piece highlights *Tonggwang*'s perceived strengths: its history, clear ideology, respected contributors, their stable lives, and financial reserves, contrasting this potential with the general decline observed in 1930. The final piece urges the magazine's staff towards perseverance and fiscal responsibility.

From a historical perspective, the article is interesting as it reflects the precariousness of publishing ventures in colonial Korea, the intellectual community's reliance on such journals, and the palpable sense of hope tied to their survival and revival. The explicit mention of the Chosŏn Products Promotion Association connects the magazine to nationalist economic movements, while the emphasis on financial stability (reserve funds, balancing accounts) highlights the practical challenges faced. The language used ("our OO," "flesh and blood of the populace," "light of the East") reveals the strong nationalist and enlightening aspirations associated with the magazine during this period.

東光 復刊을 듣고
復活에서 永生을 祝함
朝鮮物産獎勵會
明濟世
우리 東亞에 光明이든 『동광』이 잠시 휴간함에 우리OO는 거이 암흑에 呻吟하엿다고 할만콤 적막하엿다. 그럼으로 OO의 一分子인 필자도 개인의 울민과 OO의 적막을 타파하기 위하야 매일매일 其 復活을 지성으로 축원하였다. 속담에 지성이면 감천이라더니 필자와 동일한 대중의 至誠에 感應이 되엇음인지 동시에 앞으로 건전하야 永生하기를 또 至誠으로 빌고 끝으로 同人諸氏의 건강을 아울러 빈다.

東方의 빛이 되기를
東光이 다시 세상에 나온다는 것을 慶賀합니다. 弟에게도 「辛未年을 맞음」 혹 「隨感」을 쓰라고 下命하섯으나 주소이전관계로 엽서를 昨日에야 받앗고 急作히 기한에 마춰서 쓸만한 것이 없읍니다. 또 써둔 것도 별로 없습니다. 다음 기회부터는 변변치 못한 것 이나마 귀지에 실리는 榮을 얻을까 합니다.
이 겨울에 새로히 數種의 잡지가 나온다고 합니다. 凋落적이든 가울에 비해서＜40＞ 훨신 활기가 잇을 것을 믿습니다. 그 중에서 東光은 여러 가지 강점을 보여줍니다. 첫재는 쩗엇을망정 인상 깊은 역사를 가젓고 둘재는 일정한 주의주장을 가젓고 셋재는 편집 同人 諸氏가 斯界의 대가들이시며 동시에 諸氏의 사생활이 비교적 안정되어 잇고 넷재는 적립금이 잇다는 점이 강점일까 합니다. 1930년의 총결산은 凋落과 쇠퇴밖에 더 보이지 안습니다. 辛未年에 들어서는 불멸의 활력을 가진 건설의 일년이 되기를 바라서 마지 안습니다. 귀지 東光의 생명이 길고 크게 발전되여 참으로 민중의 살과 피가 되어 주고 뻗어서는 동방의 빛이 되기를 빌고 축하하는 바입니다. 간단한 말슴으로 인사를 대신합니다.

堅忍持久
平壤 吳敬淑
東光! 東光! 죽은 줄 알앗든 東光. 生脉의 움은 그냥 그냥 잠기워 잇엇다. 신미년을 맞이하면서 東光이 다시 복간된다하니 새해를 마지하여도 무엇이 깃븐지 모르는 우리로는 참 감사하며 깃브도다 더욱이 우리 동지로서는 더욱 歡賀할배라 辛未年 元旦에 붉은 햇빛이 東天에 돋을 때 「東光」이 休息된 뒤에 모든 준비와 경력을 가지고 다시 출현하니 무한한 희망으로 저 때의 전진과 같이 우리의 소망을 援助하련다. 아무것도 수입지출이 맞아야한다. 消極積極이 一樣이다. 정신적 수입과 물질적 지출을 맞추어라 其 외도 다 같다. 東光 幹部 諸位여 收支先務 堅忍持久의 노력을 기하라. ＜41＞
