**Body Text Translation:**
This magazine published a total of 16 issues, from its inaugural issue in May 1926 until its suspension after the August 1927 issue. During that time, this magazine truly achieved an unprecedented circulation in the Chosŏn magazine world and received the trust and welcome of the general readership.
However, unfortunately, due to unavoidable circumstances, we were forced to suspend publication for over three years.
Now, gathering the strength of our comrades, we have decided to resume publication of this magazine. It goes without saying that there will be a great struggle, like making a comeback after defeat (捲土重來), for our members, but we trust that our esteemed readers will also give us double the previous loving readership and support.

Upon resuming publication of this magazine, there is one thing we must announce to our esteemed readers: that is, while the newly continued *Tonggwang* remains consistently unchanged in its principles and assertions, there will be a remarkable change in its content.
*Tonggwang*'s principle is to strive for the national prosperity of the Korean people, discarding falsehood (虛僞), empty thoughts, and empty theories (空想空論), and to do so through striving for substance and practical action (務實力行). To achieve this:
1. Let us have an accurate understanding and appropriate critique of the current situation (時局).
2. Let us investigate our national character (민족적 본성).
3. Let us establish a national ideal (민족적 理想) and find the path forward to achieve it.
4. Let us heighten the civic (士(公民)) training of the nation.
5. Let us inspire the spirit of national love (民族愛) and unity.
6. Let us encourage the learning and acquisition of science and technology.
7. Let us reform practical life with a spirit of innovation and progressiveness.
8. Let us disseminate a pure and orderly Korean language (朝鮮語) and Korean writing (朝鮮文).
9. Let us become an institution that cultivates sound and noble moral and artistic sentiment (情操).
10. Let us become an institution that supplies the thorough common knowledge required of a civilized person (文明人).
These 10 Great Principles arise from this.
As for what the content will be like, it is quicker to show you with the actual product. However, it is a matter of course that every single letter and phrase in this magazine is for the sake of the 10 Great Principles listed above.
Another thing to mention regarding this magazine is our effort to comprehensively cover important domestic and foreign current events (內外時事) and also introduce important commentary from domestic and foreign newspapers and magazines, so that by reading this magazine alone, one can sufficiently gain a broad overview of the domestic and foreign situation and grasp the pivot (樞機) of its changes. This is the first time such an experiment is being undertaken in Chosŏn. (Yi Kwangsu) <1>

**Summary**

This editorial announces the resumption of the magazine *Tonggwang* after a three-year hiatus. Written by Yi Kwangsu, it reaffirms the magazine's core goal: the national prosperity of Koreans through practical action, rejecting empty theories. While the underlying principles remain, the content will change significantly. It outlines ten guiding principles focusing on understanding the times, defining national identity and ideals, civic training, unity, science, practical reform, language purification, moral cultivation, and disseminating knowledge. A new feature will be comprehensive coverage of domestic and international news and commentary to provide readers a broad understanding of current events, claimed as a first for a Korean magazine. For historians, this article is significant as it reflects the nationalist intellectual currents of the era, particularly Yi Kwangsu's emphasis on self-strengthening, cultural nationalism (language, morals), and practical improvement ('務實力行') within the constraints of colonial rule. The explicit mention of analyzing the "current situation" (時局) and the goal of national prosperity, alongside the ten principles, subtly outlines a program for national development and resilience under Japanese occupation. The attempt to become a comprehensive news digest also points to the evolving media landscape and information needs.

續刊辭
本誌는 1926년 5월에 창간호를 내어서 1927년 8월호를 마즈막으로 휴간하기까지에 도합 16호를 발행하얏다. 그 동안에 본지는 실로 朝鮮잡지계에서는 未曾有한 발행부수를 가지엇고 또 일반 독자의 신뢰와 환영을 받앗다.
그러나 불행히 부득이한 관계로 3개년 이상이나 휴간하지 아니치 못하얏다.
이제 동지 힘을 鳩聚하야 본지를 속간하기로 하니 同人에게 捲土重來이상의 大奮鬪가 잇을 것은 毋論이지마는 讀者諸位께서도 倍前의 애독과 성원을 주실 것을 믿는다.
본지를 속간함에 當하야서 독자제위께 고하지 아니치 못할 한마디가 잇으니 그것은 곳 새로 계속되는 東光은 그 主義主張에 잇어서는 一貫不變하지마는 그 내용에 잇어서는 괄목할 변화가 잇다는 것이다.
東光의 主義는 朝鮮人의 민족적 번영을 圖하되 虛僞와 空想空論을 버리고, 務實力行으로써하자 하는 것이니 그리하기 위하야
1. 時局에 대한 정확한 이해와 妥當한 비판을 가지자.
2. 민족적 본성을 究明하자.
3. 민족적 理想을 확립하고 그를 達하는 進路를 찾자.
4. 민족의 士(公民)的 훈련을 高調하자.
5. 民族愛와 단결의 정신을 고취하자.
6. 과학과 기술의 學得을 장려하자.
7. 혁신, 진취의 정신으로 실생활을 개조하자.
8. 순결하고 整齊한 朝鮮語와 朝鮮文을 보급하자.
9. 건전하고 고상한 도덕적, 예술적 情操를 涵養하는 機關이 되자.
10. 文明人으로의 周到한 상식을 공급하는 機關이 되자.
하는 10大綱領이 派生하는 것이다.
내용이 어떠할 것임은 實物로 보여드림이 빠르다. 다만 본지의 1字 1句는 다 前揭한 10大綱領을 위한 것임은 물론이다.
또 한가지 본지에 관하야 할 말은 內外時事의 중요한 것을 망라하고 또 내외 신문잡지의 중요한 논평을 소개하야서 본지 하나만 보고도 족히 內外 時局을 大觀하야 그 변천의 樞機를 파악하도록 노력하는 것이다. 이것은 朝鮮에 잇어서는 본지가 처음으로 하는 시험이다.(李光洙) ＜1＞
