Knowledge Repository (智識庫)

In America, which boasts of having the world's best in everything, the Maroro-ho (마로로號), currently under construction at the Kŭrap’ŭ shipyard (그라프 조선소) in Ppilladelppia (삘라델삐아), has a length of 582 ch’ŏk (呎) [feet] and a tonnage (噸數) of 22,050 tons. This steamship (기선) is expected to be completed next year and used on the T’aep’yŏngyang (Pacific Ocean) route.

However, the Ppat’ellandŭ-ho (빠텔란드號), built in Hamp’uk (함붉), Togil (獨逸) [Hamburg, Germany] in 1914, was a steamship (기선) with a displacement (排水) of 63,000 tons and a length of 905 ch’ŏk (呎) [feet].

According to the latest research, the total number of stars up to the 20th magnitude (20等星) is as many as one billion (10억).

The hardest wood in the world is said to be the “*ch’angmok*” (瘡木) [likely Lignum vitae], found in the tropical regions of Namyang (南洋) [South Seas/Southeast Asia], which even mice with sharp teeth (니) reportedly cannot easily gnaw.

A large locomotive recently manufactured in the eastern United States is said to have used two hundred tons (2백돈) of steel (鋼鐵). The world's largest locomotive.<69>

**Summary**

This article presents a collection of trivia focused on "world's best" or largest items: the new American passenger ship SS Malolo (though noting the older German SS Vaterland was larger), the estimated number of stars, the hardness of Lignum vitae wood, and a massive new American locomotive. From a historical perspective, the article reveals a Korean interest in global technological advancements and superlatives, particularly from the West (USA, Germany), during the colonial period. It reflects an outward-looking curiosity about modern scale and power beyond the immediate confines of the Japanese Empire.

智識庫
무엇이던지 세계제일이라고 자랑하는 미국서는 목하 삘라델삐아의 그라프 조선소에서 건조 중인 마로로號는 배의 길이는 582呎이고 噸數가 22,050톤인데 이 기선은 내년에 완성이 되어 태평양항로에 사용할 모양이다.
그러나 1914년 독일 함붉에서 건조한 빠텔란드號는 排水 6만 3천톤, 배의 길이가 905呎의 기선도 있었다.
최신의 연구에 의하면 20等星까지의 별의 총수는 10억이나 된다.
세계에서 제일 딱딱한 나무는 남양의 열대지방에 있는 「*瘡木」이라고 하는데 날카로운 니를 가진 쥐도 잘 못 바쉰다고.
최근 미국 동부에서 제조된 대형 기관차는 2백돈의 강철을 썻다고. 세계최대의 기관차.＜69＞
