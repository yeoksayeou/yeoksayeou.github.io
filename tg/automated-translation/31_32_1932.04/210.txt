**Body Text Translation:**
Condemning the Masked Patriots Through Writing: Personality and Principle

What is personality (人格)? It is neither the licentious hero nor the consistently submissive slave. It is 『an individual natural person who recognizes and also practices the complete rights and duties of a member of society』. Inasmuch as they are a person with rights, they can freely preserve their dignity, and at the same time, being a person with duties, they must also willingly accept the constraint of respecting others.

What is principle (主義)? It is 『the expression of life』 that accompanies a personality, and it signifies 『the determination of views and beliefs as a member of society』. Therefore, that principle must necessarily run parallel with personality, and depending on the differences in individual viewpoints—whether Red, White, Yellow, individual, class, nation, or even the world—distinctions of left/right coloration and broad/narrow scope arise. As it is the perspective unique to one individual, Red and White cannot coexist simultaneously. If Red and White were to coexist simultaneously without a time difference, that principle must be deemed inconsistent, eaten by the moth of doubt, and having incurred rupture.

In other words, principle is the radiance of personality, so it is something that can only be universalized through that personality, possessing an outward-acting force (遠心的 作用) that can only be embodied through personal effort and sacrifice. At the same time, it also possesses an inward-seeking nature (求心性) where Principle A and Principle B build ramparts against each other and can absolutely not compromise. <78>

Since personality and principle possess an inseparable nature, inferring from this, when a personality is in a state of confusion (錯亂狀態), its principles and even consciousness cannot definitively escape confusion. An inconsistent principle signifies the ruin (破滅) of personality, because they are inherently of a nature to coexist or perish together. If, perchance, beautiful words arise accidentally from a confused personality, they are like the bluster of a madman—not inevitable but accidental, not principle or consciousness. They are, to the last, merely an irregular concoction of ravings to be ridiculed and spat upon. They make noise, but like a 'crying brass gong' (우는 銅囉), they are meaningless; they are beautiful, but like the poisonous smile (毒笑) of a seductive woman (妖艶한 게집), they possess a fatal charm. Consequently, not only do they lack even a speck of life or value, but on the contrary, they deceive and prey upon society and poison the nation.

However, a phenomenon commonly seen recently is that among the so-called intellectuals (識者) who position themselves as the leadership stratum of society, many individuals with utterly confused consciousness can be found. They are simultaneously incurable sleepwalkers (夢遊病者) who have fallen into the ruin of personality. To be indifferent by making the excuse that this is caused by a society in its final stages is too grave and lamentable an affair.

So, by what standard do we designate them as those with confused principles and ruined personalities? It is the name to be given to the despicable ones (卑劣漢) who, despite being adherents of Principle B which cannot be reconciled with Principle A, willingly submit before Principle A for private gain (私利); it is for those who, for their own slightest profit (毫利)... (omitted section in original text) ...it is the collective term for those who quite loudly call out to the masses but, in reality, only deceive society and fish for fame (榮名), while in their hidden conduct (裏面行爲), they are utterly corrupt and ××-like, and for those of petty-bourgeois character (小市民性者) who advocate a temperamental flunkeyism (氣分的 事大主義) without any real understanding.

We believe their masks (假面) will be stripped away before long. And we trust that the emergence of true personalities will take their place. <79>
<78-79>

**Summary**

The article defines personality as the recognition and practice of societal rights and duties, and principle as the consistent expression of one's beliefs stemming from that personality. It argues that personality and principle are inseparable; a confused or compromised personality inevitably leads to confused and worthless principles. The author strongly condemns contemporary intellectuals and leaders ("masked patriots") who betray their professed principles for personal gain or out of subservience ("flunkeyism"), deceiving the public while being internally corrupt. The author expresses hope that these hypocrites will eventually be exposed and replaced by individuals of true integrity. From a historical perspective, this article reflects the intense internal debates and moral anxieties within Korean society under Japanese colonial rule. It highlights the pressure on intellectuals and leaders, criticizing those perceived as collaborators or opportunists who compromised nationalist or other principles. The condemnation of "flunkeyism" (事大主義) is particularly pointed, likely referencing subservience to Japanese authority or perhaps other powerful factions, making it a valuable commentary on the ethical dilemmas faced by Koreans during this period.

假面志士를 筆誅함, 人格과 主義
人格이란 무엇이냐? 그것은 放縱者인 英雄도 아니요 屈服一貫의 奴隷도 아니다. 그것은 『一個 自然人이 社會人으로서의 완전한 權利와 義務를 認識하고 또 實行하는 者』이다. 그가 이미 權利人인 만큼 자유롭게 자신을 尊保할 수 잇는 동시에 또한 義務人인지라 他人을 尊重할 拘束도 甘受해야 할 者이다.
主義란 무엇이냐? 그것은 一個 人格에 따르는 『生의 표현이며 社會人으로서의 見解와 信念의 決定을 意味』하는 것이다. 그럼으로 그 主義란 것은 반드시 人格과 並行할 者로서 혹은 赤 혹은 白 혹은 黃 혹은 個人 혹은 階級 혹은 國家 乃至 世界... 이러케 개인의 觀點의 相異를 따라서 左右의 色別과 廣狹의 구별이 生起하는 것이다. 그것은 1個人의 1家的 見解니 만큼 赤白이 동시에 混在할 수는 없는 것이다. 만일 赤白이 시간의 차가 없이 동시에 混在하다면 그 主義는 벌서 徹底치 못한 것이며 懷疑의 좀이 먹고 破綻을 生한 것이라 할 수 밖에 없다.

換言하면 主義란 人格의 光彩인지라 그 人格을 통해서만 普遍化시킬 수 잇고 人格的 노력과 犧牲에 의하여서만 具現시킬 수 잇는 遠心的 作用을 하는 동시에 甲主義와 乙主義가 彼此에 城壁을 쌓고 절대로 妥協할 수 없는 求心性도 가진 者이다.＜78＞

이미 人格과 主義는 不則不離의 성질을 가진 것이니 이에서 推理하면 人格이 錯亂狀態에 잇을 時는 그 主義 乃至 意識이란 것 조차도 決定的으로 錯亂을 免치 못할 것이며 主義가 不徹底한 것은 즉 人格의 破滅을 의미하는 것이니 그는 완전히 共存共滅할 성질의 것인 까닭이다. 혹시 錯亂된 人格 중에서 遇發하는 美辭가 잇다면 그것은 狂人의 氣焰같아서 必然이 아니고 遇然이며 主義와 意識이 아니다. 어데까지든지 變則的이며 噴飯唾棄할 妄發의 調合에서 不過한 것이다. 그것은 소리를 내이되 『우는 銅囉』와 같이 無意味한 것이며 그것은 아름다우되 妖艶한 게집의 毒笑의 魅力인 것이다. 따라서 그것은 一毫의 生命도 價値도 없는 것은 물론이요 反히 社會를 欺瞞餌食하고 民族을 毒針주는 것이 된다.

然而 近日에 흔히 보는 現狀은 소위 社會의 指導層이라고 自處하는 識者間에서 전혀 意識의 錯亂者를 多數 발견할 수가 잇으니 그들은 동시에 救援할 수 없는 夢遊病者며 人格破滅에 陷入한 것이다. 그것을 末期的인 社會가 시키는 일이라고 憑藉함으로써 無關心하기로 너머나 중대하고 寒心한 일이다.

그러면 무엇으로써 主義의 錯亂者며 人格의 破滅漢이라고 指稱하겟느냐? 그것은 甲主義와 調和될 수 없는 乙主義者가 私利를 위하여 甲主義 앞에 屈服을 自進하는 卑劣漢에게 줄 命名이며 그것은 자기의 毫利를 위하여서는 (中畧-원문) 그것은 제법 大衆을 외처 부르면서 그 實은 社會를 欺瞞하고 榮名을 釣할 뿐으로 裏面行爲에 잇어서는 완전히 腐敗하고 ××的인 者들과 아무 認識도 없이 氣分的 事大主義를 唱道하는 小市民性者들의 總稱인 것이다.
吾人은 그들의 假面이 未久에 剝奪되리라고 본다. 그리고 眞實한 人格의 出現이 此에 代할 것을 믿는다.＜79＞
