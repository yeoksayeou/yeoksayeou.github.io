My Personality as I See It
Survey

Please tell us about the greatest flaw in your personality and the efforts you are making to correct it.

A Trusting Nature     Kim Changhwan, Dong-A Ilbo Co.

Although I clearly know that there is no one in the world worth trusting, I still keep trusting people. I trust, then fail, and after failing, I regret it, yet I trust again. It seems my greatest flaw is unconditionally trusting others if they show no ill will towards me. I try hard not to, but it seems one cannot help one's personality.

Impatient Tendency     Kim Ŭrhan, Maeil Sinbo Co.

My innate personality has a rather impatient side, so I am trying hard to keep calming it down.

Rushing for Quick Results     O Pongbin, Chosŏn Art Museum

The greatest flaw in my personality was impatience (燥急性). I rushed to achieve quick results in everything. Gradually, as I aged <78> and gained more experience, I came to think that there is definitely an order and a process (途程) in things, and I am slowly correcting it.

Unable to Distinguish Left from Right
Ms. Kim Iryŏp

When I become absorbed (沒中) in one thing, I am negligent and careless (疎放), unable to distinguish left from right. Through efforts to correct it, it seems to be temporarily fixed, but knowing that it cannot be fundamentally corrected anyway, if I have a strong point (長技), I intend to foster (助長) that instead.

Self-Confidence and Arrogance     Kim Ch'angje, Ewha College

Excessive self-confidence and self-esteem are my biggest flaws. If not careful, this can appear as stubbornness (固執), prejudice (偏見), and arrogance (傲慢). Consequently, I rarely trust people I have misjudged once, which has led to many failures. I have tried hard to correct this and believe it has improved significantly now.

A Blow to the Wicked
Pyŏn Kwangho

Among my personality traits, there is a flaw where I fiercely (峻烈히) attack wicked people superficially, yet internally (內容的으로), I don't know how to hate them much. However, I intend to continue living with it as it is.

Prudence upon Prudence     Cho Myŏngsik, Sungin Commercial School

My personality has many flaws, but impatience (燥急) was the greatest one. I have put much effort into correcting this, always striving for prudence (愼重), prudence, and being careful, so it is somewhat better now. <79>
<78-79>

**Summary**

This article compiles responses from several Korean intellectuals and public figures in 1932 regarding their self-perceived greatest personality flaws and their efforts to amend them. Common themes include impatience, excessive trust, overconfidence leading to arrogance, and difficulty focusing. Respondents describe various coping mechanisms, from conscious effort and learning from experience to accepting the flaw or focusing on strengths instead. From a historical perspective, the article offers a glimpse into the self-reflection of educated Koreans during the Japanese colonial period. The affiliations of the respondents (newspapers, art museum, schools) indicate their positions within the colonial society's structures. The candidness about personal shortcomings, framed within a traditional understanding of character (性格) and self-cultivation, juxtaposed with their roles in modern institutions, is noteworthy for historians studying colonial modernity and intellectual life.

내가 본 내 性格
設問

先生의 性格 중 最大 缺陷과 그것을 고치기 위한 노력을 말슴하여 주십시오.

헛 믿는 性質     東亞日報社 金章煥

세상 사람 중에는 하나도 믿을 사람이 없는 것을 번연이 알면서도 그대로 작고 믿습니다. 믿엇다가는 실패를 하고 실패를 하고는 후회를 하면서도 또 믿읍니다. 남이 나에게 악의만 표시치 않으면 남을 무조건하고 믿는 것이 나의 최대의 결점인 듯 합니다. 그러지 않겟다고 애를 쓰나 성격은 하는 수 없는 것 같습니다.

急急한 便     每日申報社 金乙漢

타고난 性格이 좀 急한 편이 잇으므로 그것을 작고 눅이려고 노력하고 잇습니다.

速成을 덤벼     朝鮮美術舘 吳鳳彬

나의 性格 중 최대 缺陷은 燥急性이엇나이다. 무엇이나 速成하고저 덤비엇습니다. 차차 나쌀이＜78＞ 먹어가며 經驗이 늘어감을 따라 일에는 반듯이 順序가 잇고 途程이 잇는 것이 생각되어 점점 고치게 됩니다.

左右를 못 分別
金一葉 女士

한 가지에 沒中하면 左右를 分別치 못하는 게으르고 疎放한 것. 고치랴는 노력으로 一時的 고처지는 것도 같지만 어차피 根本的으로 고치지 못한 줄 아는 나는 長技가 잇다면 그것이나 助長해 볼가 합니다.

自信과 傲慢     梨花專門 金昶濟

自信과 自尊心이 너무 과한 것이 나의 缺陷 중의 가장 큰 것입니다. 이것이 까딱하면 固執, 偏見, 傲慢처럼 뵈여집니다. 따라서 한번 잘못 본 사람은 좀처럼 믿어주지 안는 까닭에 이로써 실패도 많이 하고 고치랴고 노력도 많이 하야 지금은 많이 고치어진 줄로 생각합니다.

惡人에 一棒
卞光昊

나의 性格 중에는 惡人에 대하야 表面上으로는 峻烈히 攻擊을 加하면서도 內容的으로는 그다지 미워할 줄 모르는 缺点이 잇소. 그러나 그대로 가지고 지나려 하오.

愼重에 加愼重     崇仁商業 趙明埴

나의 性格은 缺陷이 많으나 그 중 燥急한 것이 최대 缺陷이엇습니다. 이를 고치기에 많이 노력하야 항상 愼重 愼重하며 注意하므로 지금은 좀 나갑니다.＜79＞
＜78-79＞
