Urgent Telegrams, Part 1

To Mr. Kim Tong-hwan (金東煥):
Question: Following the publication of "Peace and Freedom (平和와 自由)," would you not consider publishing a comprehensive collection of writers' works? (Yi In-ho [李麟鎬] from Anju [安州])
Answer: Following "Peace and Freedom," our company (本社) has already decided to publish a New Literature Anthology (新文學選集), which is currently being printed, and it will likely be published in early April. I believe this will show one aspect of the comprehensive collection of writers' works you mentioned. (Kim Tong-hwan [金東煥] of Samch'ŏlli-sa [三千里社])

Yŏ Un-hyŏng's Address
Question: 1. What kind of work is Yŏ Un-hyŏng (呂運亨) currently engaged in?
2. Please provide Yŏ Un-hyŏng's current address.
3. To travel to Shanghai (上海), which route is the safest and most convenient?<52>
4. What is the travel cost to Shanghai? (A student from Chŏngju [定州生])
Answer: Yŏ Un-hyŏng's current location is Taejŏn Prison (大田監獄). His occupation is net weaving (?).
To go to Shanghai, taking a boat from Inch'ŏn (仁川) is the safest and most convenient way.
The 3rd class fare from Inch'ŏn to Shanghai is 15 Won (圓). (A Reporter [一記者])

To Mr. An Ki-yŏng (安基永):
Question: If one wants to learn music composition through self-study, what kind of books should one read? And which books should one read first to proceed safely and quickly? (A Music Enthusiast [音樂狂])
Answer: It would be very difficult to learn music composition through self-study. No matter how much of a genius you may be, you will need to receive corrections from someone knowledgeable in that field and gradually build experience.
That is, if you wish to compose, you must naturally be well-versed in music theory (樂理), so after reading books on music theory and understanding them all, you should learn about harmony (和聲法), and after that, I believe you should learn knowledge about melody composition (旋律作法).
Through this opportunity, I would like to ask all you music enthusiasts (音樂狂 諸氏) like yourself that if you have any questions regarding music or vocal music (聲樂), please do not hesitate (躊躇) to come and speak with me. (An Ki-yŏng [安基永])<53>

**Summary**

This article features a Q&A section from the April 1932 issue of *Tonggwang* magazine, responding to reader inquiries. The questions cover literary publishing plans at Samch'ŏlli-sa following their "Peace and Freedom" publication, the current status and location of the prominent nationalist figure Yŏ Un-hyŏng, practical advice on traveling to Shanghai (route, cost), and guidance on self-studying music composition. Historically, the article is revealing as it casually mentions Yŏ Un-hyŏng's imprisonment in Taejŏn Prison and his forced labor ("net weaving?"), reflecting the political realities of colonial Korea. It also shows continued cultural interest in literature and music, and provides practical details like travel costs (15 Won to Shanghai) under Japanese rule.

至急電報 其一
金東煥씨께
問 「平和와 自由」를 發刊함과 같이 文人綜合全集을 發刊하지 않으시렵니까. (安州 李麟鎬)
答 「平和와 自由」에 次하야 本社에서는 이미 新文學選集을 發刊하기로 되어 方今 印刷 중 에 잇는 바 아마 4月 初旬에는 發刊될 것 같습니다. 이것이 말슴하신 바 文人綜合全集의 一面을 보여주는 것일 줄 아옵니다. (三千里社 金東煥)

呂運亨 住所
問 1. 呂運亨은 현재 어떠한 사업을 하고 게십니까.
2. 呂運亨의 현재 住所를 알려 주십시오.
3. 上海로 渡航할여면 어떠한 방면으로 가면 安全, 便利할까요.＜52＞
4. 上海 渡航費用 幾何. (定州生)
答 呂運亨의 현재는 大田監獄. 職業은 그물뜨기(?).
上海로 가시려면 仁川서 배를 타시고 가는 것이 제일 安全, 便利합니다.
仁川서 上海까지 3等船費가 15圓입니다. (一記者)

安基永씨께
問 獨學으로 作曲을 할려면 어떠한 책을 읽어야 합니까? 그라고 무슨 책부터 읽어야 제일 안전하며 빠릅니까. (音樂狂)
答 獨學으로 作曲을 하시려면 매우 힘들겟습니다. 아무리 天才시라도 그 방면의 아는 이에게서 校正을 받으서서 점차로 經驗을 쌓으서야 될 것입니다.
즉 作曲을 하시고저 하시면 물론 樂理에 通達하서야 될 터이니까 樂理에 관한 책을 보서서 다 아시게 되거든 和聲法에 관한 것을 아서야 될 것이고 그 후에는 旋律作法에 대한 知識을 배우서야 될 줄 압니다.
이 기회를 통하야 貴下와 같으신 音樂狂 諸氏에게 엿줍는 말슴은 音樂이나 聲樂에 대하야 問議하시고 싶은 것이 잇으면 躊躇마시고 오서서 말슴하시기 바랍니다. (安基永)＜53＞
＜52-53＞
