Uprising of the Muslims

The number of Muslims (回回敎徒) in the whole world is at least 234 million. It is no ordinary matter that this great power has begun to disintegrate. Firstly, the traditional joint head of church and state, the "Caliph" (칼리프), has been abolished, and the joint capital has also departed from Constantinople (컨스탄틔노폴). Their holy scripture, the "Quran" (코란), has been stripped of its special status as sacred law by order of the Turkish (土耳其) President, Mustafa Kemal (무스타파케말). Permission has now been granted for Christians, who previously could not enter the Holy City (聖市) of Mecca (멕카), to enter. The "Harem" (하렘) (inner chamber) system, which was a prison for Turkish women, has been abolished, polygamy has been legally prohibited, and the traditional red fez has also been discarded. In other words, Turkey (土耳其) and Islam (回敎) are undergoing Westernization (洋化).<27>

**Summary**

This 1926 news report details the significant socio-political and religious changes occurring in Turkey and their impact on the broader Islamic world. It highlights the abolition of the Caliphate, the secularization of law (demoting the Quran), the end of the Harem system and polygamy, the discarding of the fez, and increased access to Mecca for non-Muslims under Mustafa Kemal Atatürk. The article frames these changes as the disintegration of traditional Islamic power and the "Westernization" of Turkey and Islam. For historians of colonial Korea, this article is interesting as it shows Korean awareness of global modernization movements, particularly in a non-Western context like Turkey. The focus on secularization and "Westernization" might reflect contemporary Korean discourse on modernity and national identity under Japanese rule, possibly serving as a point of comparison or inspiration, albeit reported neutrally here.

回回敎徒의 궐기
전세계에 잇는 回回敎徒의 수효가 적어도 2억3천4백만은 된다. 그 큰 세력이 분해되기 시작한 것은 尋常한 일이 아니다. 첫재로 전통적이던 교회와 국가의 공동원수이던 「칼리프」가 폐지되고 공동首府도 「컨스탄틔노폴」府를 떠낫다. 그들의 성경인 「코란」은 土耳其 대통령 「무스타파케말」의 명으로 신성법전의 特待를 빼아끼엇다. 聖市 「멕카」에 못드러가던 예수교인이 지금은 드러갈 수 잇는 허락이 나렷고 土耳其 여자의 감옥인 「하렘」(내실)제도가 폐지되어 일부다처제가 법률로 금지되고 전통적의 赤 감투도 폐기되엇다. 다시 말하면 土耳其와 回敎가 洋化를 하여간다.＜27＞
