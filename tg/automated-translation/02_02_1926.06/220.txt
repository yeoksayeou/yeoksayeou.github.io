**Body Text Translation**

Storehouse of Knowledge

This February, a young man in America regained his sight after 19 years thanks to surgery by Dr. C.M. P’osŭ.

Recently displayed in a New York electronics store is a newly invented electric spanking machine (전기 打擲器) used for punishing children.

According to a railway ministry investigation, among the tracks of England, America, France, Germany, Belgium, Russia, and Japan (英米佛獨白露日), American tracks are said to wear out the fastest, in 10 years.

The director of the P’op’ŭ Research Institute (포프 연구소) in Calcutta, India, has discovered that plants also have muscles.

Calculate it using dynamics, everyone: when an express locomotive stops, it requires twice the force as when it starts.

An airship, the world's largest, has been built in England, featuring an entertainment room for dancing in the air, a kitchen, and 32 beds; it is scheduled to fly to India on the same day.

According to recent statistics, there is an average of one shipwreck (破船) per day throughout the world.

The radio station (무전국) on P’ik.sŭ.midei Mountain (삑.스.미데이 산) in France is at an elevation of 9,439 feet, the highest radio station in the world.

A student at the University of Pennsylvania in America has invented a method to measure plant growth using radio. <44>

**Summary**

This article presents a collection of miscellaneous news items and scientific/technological facts from around the world in 1926. Topics include medical advancements (restoring sight), new inventions (electric spanker, radio plant growth measurement), engineering facts (rail track durability, locomotive force), biological discoveries (plant muscles), large-scale construction (British airship), global statistics (shipwrecks), and geographical records (highest radio station). For historians studying colonial Korea, this piece reveals the kind of global information being disseminated to a Korean audience through magazines like *Tonggwang*. It reflects an interest in modernity, Western science and technology, and world affairs, juxtaposing trivial inventions with significant scientific discoveries and engineering feats, offering a glimpse into the global consciousness being shaped in Korea during that period.

智識庫
금년 2월에 미국의 一靑年은 19년만에 C.M.포스 박사의 수술로 소경눈이 떳다.

최근 紐育 어느 전기점에 진열된 신발명품은 아이를 벌하는대 쓰는 전기 打擲器가 잇다.

철도성의 조사로 보면 英米佛獨白露日 등의 궤도 중 미국제가 제일 먼저 10년만에 해진다고.

인도 칼커타의 포프 연구소장이 식물에도 근육이 잇다함을 발견하엿다.

급행 기관차가 설 적에는 발차한 때의 2배의 힘이 든다고 여러분 역학으로 계산하여 보시오.

공중에서 딴스하는 오락실도 잇스며 취사실도 잇고 32개의 침대도 잇는 비행선을 영국에서 만들엇는데 세계 제일이요 인도를 同日에 날라갈 예정이다.

최근의 통계에 의하면 세계를 통하여 매일 평균 一艘식의 破船이 잇다.

불란서의 삑.스.미데이 산에 엇는 무전국은 해발 9439척. 무선국으로는 세계 최고라.

미국 펜실베니아 대학의 학생이 라듸오로 식물의 성장을 측하는 법을 발명하엿다.＜44＞
