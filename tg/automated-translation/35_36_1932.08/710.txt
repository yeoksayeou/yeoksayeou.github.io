**Body Text Translation:**
Urgent Telegram: Esperanto and Mr. Kim Ŏk

Question: Please speak about the following matters.
1. Esperanto: Which country and person created it?
2. What is the nature of the Esperanto language?
3. Has Esperanto become an international language? Since when?
4. After learning Esperanto grammar, what should one learn next?
5. In which countries worldwide is Esperanto most widespread? What kinds of publications in Esperanto exist, and how can one purchase them?
6. Please tell us Mr. Kim Ŏk's birthplace, age, alma mater, current occupation, works, and religious beliefs. (Sŏ Man-sun)

Answer 1. The creator of Esperanto was Dr. L. L. Zamenhof, an ophthalmologist from Poland.

Answer 2. Asking "what is the nature of the Esperanto language" is a vague question. Esperanto is not the arbitrary creation of one individual; it is an arrangement of natural languages sharing the same linguistic roots. It goes without saying that the vocabulary of Esperanto possesses internationality stemming from its etymological origins.

Answer 3. When Esperanto might become an international language, this we do not know. If Esperanto has value as an international language, then above all, efforts must be made for its promotion and dissemination. However, in one sense, Esperanto has already become an international common language, inasmuch as it has achieved a certain status through practical use. The issue, I believe, is when Esperanto will be included as a regular part of the academic curriculum.

Answer 4. Esperanto grammar is simple and clear. In any case, after learning the grammar, it would be good to read any easy book. "Aesop's Fables" or short stories (소화) are likely good because they are easy.

Answer 5. The countries where Esperanto is widespread are the weak and small nations (약소국); Germany and Russia seem to have a considerable presence. However, this does not mean it is absent in countries like England, America, or France; they even have Esperanto schools. All kinds of books are published. If there is a publication you desire, please inform the agency department of the Tonggwang Company (東光社), and we will obtain it for you. (Kim Ŏk)

Answer 6. Mr. Kim Ŏk's birthplace is Kwaksan, Chŏngju County, North P'yŏngan Province (平北 定州郡 郭山); his age this year is 38; he graduated from the literature department of Keiō University (慶應大學) in Japan; his occupation is a life of writing (文筆生活); his works include poetry collections such as "Songs of Spring" (봄의 노래), "Ansŏ Poetry Collection" (岸曙詩集), "Song of the Jellyfish" (해파리의 노래), etc., and besides these, he has many other writings such as "Esperanto Short Course" (에쓰페란토단기강좌), etc. His religion is none (無). (One Reporter (一記者)) <78>

**Summary**

This article, formatted as an "urgent" Q&A, addresses reader Sŏ Man-sun's questions about Esperanto and the prominent Korean intellectual Kim Ŏk in 1932. It explains Esperanto's origins (L.L. Zamenhof, Poland), its nature as an organized language based on common roots rather than arbitrary invention, its practical use suggesting a de facto international status despite not being in official curricula, and learning progression (grammar then simple reading). Kim Ŏk himself answers the question about Esperanto's prevalence (noting its popularity in "weak and small nations" and presence in major powers) and how to acquire publications (via Tonggwang Company). The final question, answered by a reporter, provides Kim Ŏk's biographical details: birthplace, age (38), education (Keiō University), occupation (writer), major works, and lack of religion. From a historical perspective, the article highlights the interest among some colonial Koreans in Esperanto as a potential neutral international language, perhaps reflecting a desire for global connections outside the Japanese empire's sphere. It also provides a valuable contemporary profile of Kim Ŏk, a significant literary figure and Esperantist during this period, and notes the perception that Esperanto found more traction in less powerful nations.

至急電報, 에쓰페란토와 金億 선생
문 左記에 대해서 말슴하여 주십시오.
1. 에쓰페란토語는 何國何人의 창작?
2. 에쓰語의 내용 여하?
3. 에쓰語가 국제어가 되엇는지오. 원제부터.
4. 에쓰語는 문법을 학습한 후에는 무엇을 배워야 함니까.
5. 에쓰語는 세계 어떤 나라에 제일 만히 보급되엇습니까. 에쓰語로 된 출판물은 어떤 것이 잇으며 그것을 살려면 어떠케 하여야 합니까.
6. 金億 선생의 출생지, 연령, 출신학교, 현직, 저작, 신앙을 알려주십시오.(徐萬淳)

답 1. 에쓰페란토 창안자는 폴란드국인 안과의사 「라자로.루도비코.자엔홉」박사외다.
2. 에쓰語 내용의 如何란 망연한 질문이외다. 에쓰語는 한 개인의 독단적 창작이 아니요, 같은 어원의 자연어를 정리해 놓은 것이외다. 에쓰語의 단어가 어원으로의 국제성을 가진 것은 물론이외다.
3. 에쓰語가 언제부터 국제어가 될는지 이것은 모릅니다. 에쓰語가 국제어로의 가치가 잇는 것이라면 무엇보다도 그 선전 보급에 노력하지 아니하여서는 아니 될 것이외다. 다만 한마디 에쓰語가 실용으로 그만한 지위를 잡은 것 만치 어떤 의미로는 발서 국제공통어가 된 것이외다. 문제는 이 에쓰語가 학과의 正課의 하나로 언제쯤 넣게 되겟는가 그것인 줄 압니다.
4. 에쓰語의 문법이라야 간단 명료하외다. 여하간 문법을 배운 뒤에는 아무것이나 쉽은 책을 읽는 것이 좋겟지오.
「이솝의 이애기」 또는 소화 같은 것이 쉬어서 좋을 줄 압니다.
5. 에쓰語가 많이 보급된 나라는 약소국들이요, 獨露가 상당한가 보이다. 그러타고 英, 米, 佛 같은 나라에는 없는 것이 아니요, 에쓰語 학교까지 잇습니다. 발행되는 서적은 무엇이든지 잇습니다. 원하는 책자가 잇으면 東光社 대리부로 말슴하시면 구하여 들입니다.(金億)
6. 金億 씨의 출생지는 平北 定州郡 郭山, 연령은 금년이 38, 日本 慶應대학 문과 출신, 직업 문필생활, 저작는 시집 「봄의 노래」 「岸曙詩集」 「해파리의 노래」 등등이 잇고 그밖에 「에쓰페란토단기강좌」 등 저서가 많소. 종교는 무.(一記者) ＜78＞
