**Body Text Translation:**
Science Fragment, The Character of Mr. Wright

Among the Wright brothers, inventors of the airplane, Wilbur Wright was known for his strict character. He strictly observed Sundays, did not associate closely with women, did not smoke tobacco or drink alcohol, and would only eat simple food.

When the King of Spain came to watch (this was when they were experimenting with flight techniques in France), he asked to be shown on a Sunday. The Emperor's request would have been tantamount to a command, but Mr. Wright replied asking if they could please arrange it for another day instead of Sunday. The Emperor inevitably had no choice but to arrange it for another day. <56>

**Summary**

This short article highlights the famously strict character of Wilbur Wright, one of the inventors of the airplane. It notes his adherence to religious observance (keeping Sunday holy), abstinence from women, alcohol, and tobacco, and preference for simple food. The main anecdote describes how he politely refused a request from the King of Spain to demonstrate his airplane on a Sunday while in France, forcing the king to reschedule, thus emphasizing Wright's unwavering principles even in the face of royalty. For historians, this piece reflects the Korean interest in Western scientific figures and technological advancements during the colonial period. The focus on Wright's strict morality and discipline might also subtly reflect prevailing social values or desired traits emphasized at the time.

科學斷片, 라이트 군의 성격
비행긔 발명가 라이트 형제 중에 윌보, 라이트 군은 엄격한 성격으로 이름이 잇엇다. 그는 일요일을 엄격히 지키고 여자와 가까이 아니하며 담배, 술 아니먹고 음식도 검소한 것이 아니면 아니 먹엇다.
서반아의 황제가 구경을 오는데(그때 불란서에서 비행술을 실험할 때다.) 일요일날 구경을 시켜달라고 햇다. 황제의 요구는 명령이나 다름이 없을 것인데 라이트 군은 일요일보다 다른 날로 해줍시사고 회답을 햇다. 황제도 부득이 다른 날로 정할 수 밖에 없엇다. ＜56＞
