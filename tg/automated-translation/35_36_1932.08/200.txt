**Body Text Translation**
Science Fragments, The Use of Poison Gas

Poison gas, which kills people during wartime, is also utilized in various ways during peacetime.

Banks and jewelry stores install tear gas devices. When a robber enters, this gas is stealthily released, and no matter how strong the robber is, they end up covering their eyes and fleeing. It is said that the holes from which the gas emerges are extremely small, making it difficult to quickly discern where they are embedded, so the thief cannot block them beforehand.

If household gas is faulty and leaks out, people can inhale it and die. To prevent this, gas companies mix in sneeze-inducing poison gas. Then, if the gas leaks, sneezing occurs, providing a warning and waking even sleeping people.

Various types of poison gas are also used on farms to exterminate harmful insects. <39>

**Summary**

This short article from 1932 discusses the peacetime applications of poison gas, originally developed for warfare. It highlights its use in security systems for banks and jewelers (tear gas), as a safety measure by adding sneeze-inducing gas to household gas supplies to warn of leaks, and for pest control in agriculture. From a historical perspective, the article is interesting as it reflects the normalization and perceived utility of a potent weapon technology for civilian purposes during the Japanese colonial period in Korea. It showcases how technologies associated with modern warfare were being integrated into everyday life and commercial applications, potentially indicating shifts in societal attitudes towards security, safety, and technological solutions promoted during that era.

科學斷片, 毒瓦斯의 이용
전쟁 때에 사람 죽이는 독까쓰가 평화시절에도 여러 가지 방면으로 리용이 된다.
은행이나 보석상점에서는 눈물나는 독까쓰를 장치하고 강도가 들어오면 슬그먼이 이 까쓰를 터트려 노으며 제아모리 강도라도 그만 눈을 싸매고 달아난다. 그 까쓰 내보내는 구멍이 아초 적어서 어디 박이어 잇는지 얼는 알 수 없는고로 도적이 미리 막을 수가 없다 한다.
가정에서 사용하는 까쓰가 잘못되어 새어 나오면 사람이 마시고 죽는다. 이것을 방지하기  위하야 까쓰회사에서는 재채기 하는 독까쓰를 섞어 보낸다. 그러면 까쓰가 새어나오면 재채기가 나서 경고하고 잠들엇든 사람도 깨어나게 된다.
농원에서 해로운 벌레를 구제하는데도 각 종류의 독까쓰를 사용한다. ＜39＞
