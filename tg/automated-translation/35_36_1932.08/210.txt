Publication Date: 1932년 08월 01일
Article Title: 第6官, 東亞日報의 奔忙 (The Sixth Official, the Tonga Ilbo's Bustle)
Author: Not provided
Article Type: 소식 (News/Tidings)

With Chungang suspended and Chosŏn hesitating, among the three private newspapers, the Tonga Ilbo alone has become busy. Its circulation must surely have increased, but that is a business secret and cannot be known; however, it is said that what has made it particularly busy are the sponsorship requests for various performances.

Since it is now handling the sponsorships that the three newspapers used to handle all at once, the person in charge is reportedly so busy with allocating page space for those matters that they have no time to even blink, as sponsorships keep pouring in.

Furthermore, the company (同社) seems to have gained considerable financial leeway in recent years, and it is heard that around next spring (明春), provided there isn't another suspension disturbance, they plan to expand the company building, completing the office building which is currently only half of its original design (元設計).＜40＞

**Summary**

The Tonga Ilbo newspaper found itself exceptionally busy after its competitor, Chungang, was suspended, and another, Chosŏn, became hesitant. This increased activity stemmed largely from handling sponsorship requests for performances, a task previously shared among the three papers. The article also notes the Tonga Ilbo's improved financial situation and its plans to expand its office building the following spring, contingent on avoiding further suspensions. For historians, this piece highlights the volatile environment of the colonial Korean press, subject to suspensions (censorship), the competitive dynamics between newspapers, and offers insights into their business operations, including reliance on sponsorships and aspirations for physical growth despite the challenging political climate.

第6官, 東亞日報의 奔忙
中央이 휴간되고 조선이 망서리는 바람에 민간 3신문 중 東亞日報가 홀로 분주하게 되엇다. 부수도 미상불 늘엇겟지마는 그것은 영업의 비밀이라 알 수 없으나 특히 분주하게 된 것은 여러 가지 공연물의 후원신청이라 한다. 세 신문이 하든 후원을 한꺼번에 하니 담임자는 그 사고 들어갈 지면배정에 눈코 뜰 새가 없으리만치 후원이 자꾸만 들어온다고.
그리고 同社는 근년에 재정적 여유가 꽤 생긴 모양으로 明春 쯤에는 다시 정간소동이나 없는 한에서 사옥을 증축하야 원설계의 반분 밖에 안되는 사옥을 완성할 게획이라고 들엇다.＜40＞
