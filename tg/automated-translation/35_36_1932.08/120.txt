**Body Text Translation:**
News After Posŏng College's Sixth Bureau Phase

Posŏng College (普成專門學校) became a topic of conversation for a time when it was said that the Kim (金) clan threw down 500,000 won to bring it back to life, but how have things progressed since then?

Kim Sŏngsu (金性洙) has assumed the position of Principal, Ch'oe Tuson (崔斗善) is the administrative director of the incorporated foundation, Professor Ok Sŏnjin (玉璿珍) has been appointed Dean of the Law Department, and Professor Kim Yŏngju (金泳柱) has been appointed Dean of the Commerce Department. The lineup has been organized by inviting rising young scholars as lecturers, among whom are Mr. Yu Chino (兪鎭午) and Mr. Ch'oe Yongdal (崔容達), both graduates of the Imperial University (帝大), as well as Mr. O Ch'ŏnsŏk (吳天錫), who returned from the United States (米國). People are joking that Posŏng College (普專) now has ten thousand *sŏk* (만석), combining the Kim family's five thousand *sŏk* (五千石) [of wealth] and the lecturer O Ch'ŏnsŏk (吳天錫) [whose name sounds like five thousand *sŏk*].

Furthermore, Mr. Hyŏn Sangyun (玄相允), the former principal, has newly taken up the post of Principal at Chungang High School (中央高普). People are amazed, saying his complete recovery from tuberculosis is due to his absolute self-discipline.

There was talk that the new campus for Posŏng College (普專) would be located outside Tongdaemun (동대문) at An'gamch'ŏn (安甘川), but it was deemed too cramped with only 12,000 *p'yŏng*. Consequently, the decision was made to search again for a suitable location. It is said that lately, Director Ch'oe Tuson (최두선) and other related personnel are traversing every mountain near Kyŏngsŏng (京城), just like people searching for auspicious grave sites (뫼짜리) in the old days.

News of Musicians

After Hong Nanp'a (洪蘭坡), famous for the violin, and Pak Kyŏngho (朴慶浩), who plays the piano, went to America (渡米) one after the other, there was no news. However, according to recent rumors, it seems both are studying composition extensively, and both are reportedly striving to create works based on Korean folk songs (조선민요).

It appears highly likely (십상팔구) that Mr. Pak (朴) will be employed by the Music Department of Ewha College (梨花專門) starting this autumn.
＜28＞

**Summary**

The article reports on the revival and reorganization of Posŏng College (later Korea University) following a significant donation from the Kim family (specifically Kim Sŏngsu, who became Principal). It details new administrative and faculty appointments, including notable scholars like Yu Chino and O Ch'ŏnsŏk, and mentions the ongoing search for a larger campus site. It also updates readers on the activities of musicians Hong Nanp'a and Pak Kyŏngho, who are studying composition with Korean folk music influences in the United States, with Pak potentially joining Ewha College. For historians, the article offers insights into the private funding and rebuilding of Korean educational institutions under Japanese rule, the integration of both Japanese-educated (Imperial University) and US-educated Korean intellectuals into these institutions, and the concurrent efforts by Korean musicians abroad to engage with national identity through folk music. The mention of land searching referencing traditional geomancy ('grave site searching') adds a cultural layer to the practicalities of institutional development.

第6官, 普成專門의 其後 소식
普成專門學校는 金씨 문중에서 50만원을 탁 내던지어 재생을 하게 된다 해서 일시 이애기거리가 되엇거니와 그후에 일은 어떠케 진행이 되고 잇는가.
교장에는 金性洙씨가 취임햇고 崔斗善씨가 재단법인의 사무이사로, 法科 科長에 玉璿珍 교수, 商科 科長에는 金泳柱 교수가 취임햇으며 신진 청년학자들을 강사로 초빙하야 진용을 정제 햇는데 帝大 출신의 兪鎭午, 崔容達 兩君도 그 중에 들엇고 米國 출신의 吳天錫 군도 들엇는데 우슴꺼리 하는 사람이 普專은 金씨의 오천석(五千石)과 강사에 오천석(吳天錫) 합하야 만석이 되엇다고 한다.
그리고 中央高普校長으로는 元 校長 玄相允 군이 新就햇는데 씨의 폐병이 아주 완쾌된 것은 씨의 절대의 극기력에 의함이라고 사람들이 놀란다.
普專의 新校舍는 동대문 외 安甘川에 된다는 말도 잇엇으나 1만 2천평 가지고는 너무 협착하다고 하야 다시 적합한 곳을 골르기로 하야 최두선씨 이하 관계자들은 요새는 옛날 뫼짜리 골르는 사람 모양으로 이산 저산으로 京城 부근 산은 모조리 밟아 단닌다 한다.

음악가의 소식
바욜린으로 유명한 洪蘭坡, 피아노 치는 朴慶浩, 이 두 음악가 전후하야 渡米한 후 소식이 없더니 최근 풍문을 들으면 두 분이 다 작곡에 대해서 공부를 많이 하는 모양인데 두분이 다 조선민요를 가지고 창작에 노력중이라 한다.
朴군은 십상팔구 梨花專門 음악과로 今秋부터 취직하게 될 모양이라고.
＜28＞
