발행일: 1932년 08월 01일
기사제목: 우리말로 그려내리라 (I Shall Depict It in Our Language)
필자: 李無極
기사형태: 시 (Poem)

I Shall Depict It in Our Language
Yi Mugŭk

Like white clouds blooming in the haze of the blue sky,
Like black smoke billowing up,
Ah, within my narrow chest,
Something is always bubbling and boiling up.

This mysterious feeling seeping deep into my heart,
With this body that has even the tip of its tongue stolen away,
How can I express it thoroughly, inside and out?
My heart only shudders in frustration.

Then I shall become mute.
For this fertile land where I snored to lullabies on my sister’s back,
For the colors and sounds I see and hear before my eyes,
I shall endure and endure, becoming a precious mute.

Then later, when a harsh wind opens the door of my heart,
And the mysterious emotions that piled up and piled up inside my chest
Explode my silence without mercy,
Oh, then I shall depict the cries of beasts, the songs of the mad,
Clearly, clearly in our language. <84>

**Summary**

This poem by Yi Mugŭk vividly expresses the poet's inner turmoil and frustration at being unable to fully articulate deep, "mysterious" feelings, metaphorically described as something boiling within. The speaker attributes this inability to having their "tongue stolen away," a clear reference to the suppression of the Korean language under Japanese colonial rule. The poet resolves to endure this imposed silence ("become a precious mute") for the sake of the homeland, bottling up the emotions until they inevitably erupt. When that moment comes, the speaker vows to unleash these pent-up feelings, depicting even primal expressions like the "cries of beasts" and "songs of the mad," forcefully and authentically in "our language" (Korean). For historians, the poem is a poignant artifact of cultural resistance during the colonial period, illustrating the profound connection drawn between national identity, personal expression, and the native language, even when its use was restricted. It showcases the determination to preserve and ultimately reclaim linguistic sovereignty as a vital form of expression.

우리말로 그려내리라
						李無極
푸른 하늘 아지랑 속 힌구름이 피여오르듯
검은 연기 뭉게뭉게 솟아 오르듯
어 내 좁은 가슴속엔
늘 무엇이 부글부글 끓어오르네.

이 마음 속 깊이 숨여드는 신비의 이 마음을
혀 끝 마자 빼아낀 이 몸으로
어떠케 속속드리 표현할 수 잇을까
마음만 안타까이 몸서리를 치나니.

그러면 나는 벙어리가 되리라
누나의 등에서 자장가에 코골든 기름진
이 땅을 위하야
내 눈앞에 보이는 들리는 채색을 음향을
참고 참고 값진 벙어리가 되리라.

그랫다가 후일 모진 바람이 마음문을 열어
가슴속에 쌓이고 쌓엿든 그 신비의 감정이
내 침묵을 여지없이 폭발시킬 때
오오 나는 그때 즘생들의 웨ㅅ침을 미친 자의 노래를
분명히 분명히 우리말로 그려내리라. ＜84＞
