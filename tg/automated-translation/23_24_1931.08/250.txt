잡지명	동광 제24호
발행일	1931년 08월 04일
기사제목	버드나무 그늘(8월의 수필), 消燈雜詠十首 (Willow Shade (August Essay), Ten Miscellaneous Poems on Putting Out the Lights)
필자	作家 全武吉
기사형태	시 (Poem)

1. City Maiden
City maidens,
Where do you go every dawn?
Proud of your blooming bodies,
Applying rouge (연주) and powder,
You are the factory girls,
Your beauty (美) already belongs to others.

2. City Moon
On these old castle ruins,
Even the crescent moon was bright.
On nights when lonely souls rejoiced,
They were unleashed beneath that moon.
Today, oppressed by electric lights (電光),
Even the full moon has no radiance.

3. Traveler's Heart
When this body was strong,
Being alone and adrift was comfortable.
But when I fall ill and lie down,
I long again for my old home.
This heart's loneliness, riding the clouds,
Fears the hand of the blowing wind.

4. Suffering
They tell us to endure even when hungry,
And block even true words.
Though our whole bodies are bound,
If we try to raise our will,
We still have no comrades;
This I lament endlessly.

5. Mosquito
Following the lamplight and the smell of flesh,
Swarms of mosquitoes fly in.
Chase them away, they come again,
Swat them, and revenge follows.
Their way of life will be eternal;
Our path lies here.

6. Lover
Was her face so pretty,
Her words like an angel's?
Before love deepens,
Control the poison first.
Consider her fate being set right
As your own happiness.

7. Sweet Rain
Upon all the fields of this land,
The rain of drought relief pours down.
By planting rice and weeding fields,
These people have survived.
Cheers! A bountiful year arrives,
How could we starve when it's plentiful?

8. Literature and Class
This land has limits,
And each person's strength differs.
Classes are divided
Based on land and strength, but
Can there be class in literature?
That is a sword dropped by the enemy.

9. Decadence
The words desired by the 'Moppo' (모뽀):
They dislike Heaven and seek Hell.
Heaven has no cafes,
Hell's women are alluring, but
The sun shines brightly in the sky,
And humankind has the Way (道).

10. Fragments of Thought
This night too, I sit alone <53>
Shedding tears until dawn.
Let only the cuckoo cry,
Why should the pointless moon shine?
Rather, even if I go to that moon,
If only this country's master would come...
(1931. 7. 1) <54>

**Summary**

This collection of ten short poems by Chŏn Mu-gil, titled "Ten Miscellaneous Poems on Putting Out the Lights," reflects on various facets of life and society in 1931 Korea. The poems touch upon the hardships of urban factory girls, the dimming of tradition (moonlight) by modernity (electric lights), personal loneliness and longing for home, suppressed expression and the lack of solidarity, the resilience of even bothersome creatures like mosquitoes, cautionary thoughts on love, hope spurred by rain ending a drought, a rejection of class division in literature, a critique of modern decadence (Moppo culture), and a final expression of solitary sorrow and patriotic yearning. From a colonial history perspective, the poems offer glimpses into the social conditions (factory labor, suppression), the tension between tradition and imposed modernity, and subtle expressions of national consciousness and desire for liberation ("this country's master"). The poem explicitly addressing literature and class (No. 8) is particularly interesting, rejecting a class-based view of literature during a period when proletarian literature was influential, framing it instead as divisive ("a sword dropped by the enemy").

1. 도시의 처녀
도시의 처녀들아
새벽마다 어델 가노
피는 몸 자랑스뤄
연주 찍고 분 발라도
공장의 큰 애기라네
美는 벌서 남의 것

2. 도시의 달
옛날의 이 성터엔
초생달도 밝앗어라
외론 넉 흥겨운 밤
저 달 아레 풀엇거니
오날은 電光에 눌려
보름달도 빛 없네

3. 旅心
이 몸이 굳세일젠
홀로 뜬 몸 편하더니
병들어 누어지면
옛 집 다시 그리워라
구름 탄 이 맘의 외롬
바람칠 손 두리네

4. 고통
주려도 참으라고
참된 말도 가로 막네
온 몸을 얽어 매도
우리의 뜻 높히려건
아즉도 동지 없음을
끝내 아숴 하노라

5. 蚊
불빛과 살내 따라
모기의 떼 날러드네
쫓아도 다시 오고
맞고 나선 복수 오네
사는 법 영원하리니
우리의 길 예 잇다.

6. 애인
그 얼골 어여쁘고
천사의 말 같으더냐
사랑이 깊기 전에
독을 먼저 制할진저
그 운명 바로 잡힘을
이내 행복 삼거라

7. 甘雨
이 강산 온 벌판에
감음의 비 나려붓네
모 심고 김매서만
이 백성들 사라왓건
지화자 풍년이 드네
흔하고야 굶으랴

8. 문학과 계급
이 따에 한정 잇고
사람마다 힘 다르네
계급이 갈리기를
땅과 힘에 말미암건
문학에 계급 잇으랴
원수 노친 칼일세

9. 퇴폐
모뽀의 원하는 말
천당 싫고 지옥 찾네
천당엔 카페 없고
지옥 게집 요염타나
하날에 태양이 밝고
사람에게 道 잇건

10. 斷想
이 밤도 홀로 앉아＜53＞
눈물지며 두샐진대
두견만 울렴으나
뜻 없은 달 웨 밝으리
차라리 저 달 가서도
이 나라 님 오시면
