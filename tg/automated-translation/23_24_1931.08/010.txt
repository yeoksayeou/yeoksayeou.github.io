Special Announcement

Dear Readers, how hot it must be for you all. However, we trust that you will press forward even more vigorously amidst the heat. We trust that you will advance with even greater passion and perseverance during these times when all circumstances are harsh.

Amidst the silence in the magazine publishing world, we have resolutely published the August issue. We have published it thus, with significantly increased content. Please note that not only has the number of pages increased, but more importantly, the content has significantly increased. At least 180 more characters have been added per page. Calculated by page count, this is equivalent to an increase of about ten pages.

*Tonggwang* (東光) is not an entertainment magazine. It is not a magazine that gathers only articles meant for a quick laugh and then forgotten. Therefore, the circulation of *Tonggwang* is naturally limited. We cannot resort to any means necessary just to sell copies. For that reason, criticisms that *Tonggwang* is expensive or has fewer pages compared to other magazines are harsh. We are doing our utmost. We are still operating at a loss each month. Our only regret is not being able to make the content more valuable.

Everyone, let us increase the fellowship (*tang*, 党) of *Tonggwang*. If each of you introduces just one new reader, our *Tonggwang* will become a greatly distinguished magazine. To make a better magazine, more supporters are needed.

Everyone, we do not run excessive newspaper advertisements. We are even considering abolishing them entirely at some point. However, we are always publishing steadily. We never skip any month throughout the year. Knowing this, please do not forget to send your payment when the end of the month arrives.

Though it may sound like self-praise, we believe that not a single article included in this issue should be carelessly discarded. Among them, "Outline of National Socialism" (民族社會主義論綱), "Roosevelt's Policy Towards Korea" (루스벨트의 조선정책), "Review of the Salary Increase Issue" (加俸問題檢討), and "Solutions to the Problems of Compatriots in Manchuria" (在滿同胞問題解決策) are original and enlightening major articles (大文字). The two theses published in the Tonggwang Forum (東光大學) are also articles from which much can be gained. We ask for your careful reading. We are confident that "Reminiscences of the Annexation Period" (合邦當時 回顧談) and the "August Essays" (8月隨筆) are readings cooler than iced water (氷水) on a hot day.

The September issue is planned as a "Manchuria Problem Special Feature" (滿洲問題 特輯), intending to be fully loaded (滿載) with articles from various perspectives in Korea, China, Japan, and the world concerning this issue. Additionally, we plan a brilliant editorial lineup including research papers on national issues, responses from various gentlemen regarding the reorganization plan for nationalist organizations, articles on education, monthly critiques of figures (人物 月旦), practical articles of interest on health and science, and countless rare news items (珍消息) from overseas dispatches.

Starting from the autumn term (秋期), as planned, the Tonggwangsa (東光社) Publishing Department is set to operate at full capacity (大馬力). We intend to publish many excellent books. We will also further expand (廣張) the Agency Department (代理部). We will also issue a book catalogue (圖書目錄). Everyone, please support us.

August 1st, Tonggwangsa (東光社)

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the August 1931 issue of *Tonggwang* addresses readers directly, acknowledging the difficult times (heat, harsh circumstances) while urging perseverance. The editors justify the magazine's price and page count by emphasizing its serious, non-entertainment nature and the increased content per page, despite operating at a loss. They appeal for readers' support in expanding readership and ensuring timely payments, highlighting key articles in the current issue (on national socialism, Roosevelt's Korea policy, salary issues, Koreans in Manchuria, annexation memoirs) and previewing the September issue focused on the "Manchuria Problem," along with plans for expanding the publishing house's activities. From a historical perspective, the text reveals the economic struggles of independent Korean publishing under colonial rule, the magazine's explicitly nationalistic and serious intellectual orientation (contrasting itself with 'entertainment'), and its focus on contemporary political and social issues relevant to Koreans, particularly the situation in Manchuria and critiques of colonial policy (implied in the article titles), making it a valuable source for understanding Korean intellectual currents and nationalist discourse in the early 1930s.

特告
독자여러분 얼마나 더우십니까. 그러나 더운 가운데 더욱 씩씩하게 나가실 줄 믿습니다. 모든 정세가 악착한 이 때에 더욱 열잇게 끈기잇게 나아가실 줄 믿습니다.
잡지 출판게가 적막한 가운데 우리는 단연코 팔월호를 발행햇습니다. 이러케 내용까지 훨신 늘려 발행햇습니다. 페지 수도 늘엇거니와 그보다도 내용이 훨신 는 것을 주의하십시요. 한 페지에 적어도 180자 이상이 늘엇습니다. 이것을 페지 수로 따지면 약 열 페지가 늘어나간 세음입니다.
東光은 오락잡지가 아닙니다. 한 번 픽 웃고 말 기사만 모아서 내는 잡지가 아님니다. 따라서 東光의 발행 부수는 자연이 제한을 받습니다. 팔기 위해서는 무슨 수단이라도 가리지 안코 나갈 수는 없는 것입니다. 그런 고로 東光의 값이 많다. 다른 잡지와 비겨서 페지가 적다하는 비평은 혹평입니다. 우리는 최선을 다하고 잇습니다. 우리는 아직도 다달이 미찝니다. 오직 그 내요을 더 가치잇게 못하는 것이 유감입니다.
여러분 東光의 党을 늘립시다. 여러분이 한 분식만 신독자를 소개하시드라도 우리 東光은 크게 훌륭한 잡지가 되겟습니다. 더 좋은 잡지를 만들기 위하야는 더 많은 후원자가 필요합니다.
여러분, 우리는 신문광고를 과대적으로 내지 안습니다. 어느 시기에 가면 아주 전폐하려고도 생각합니다. 그러나 언제던지 우리는 꾸준히 발행하고 잇습니다. 年中 어느 달이나 건느는 법이 없읍니다. 그런줄 아시고 월말만 되거든 잊지 마시고 송금하시옵소서.
자화자찬같으나 이번 호에 실린 기사는 하나도 무심히 버릴 것이 없다고 생각합니다. 그 중에도 『民族社會主義論綱』 『루스벨트의 조선정책』 『加俸問題檢討』 『在滿同胞問題解決策』들은 독창적, 계발적 大文字들입니다. 東光대학에 실린 兩 논문 역시 크게 얻음이 잇을 기사입니다. 숙독을 바랍니다. 『합방당시 회고담』 『8월수필』들은 더운 날에 빙수보다 더 시언한 讀物임을 자신합니다.
9월호는 『만주문제 특집』으로하야 이 문제에 관한 조선, 중국, 일본 세계의 각 방면 기사를 滿載하고저 합니다. 그리고 민족문제 연구논문, 민족주의단체 재조직의 복안 諸氏답안이며 교육문제, 인물 月旦, 건강, 과학 등의 취미실익기사 그리고 해외단신의 무수한 珍消息을 넣어 찬란한 편집을 할 예정입니다.
秋期부터 예정과 같이 東光社 출판부는 大馬力을 내게 되엇습니다. 훌륭한 책을 많이 출판하고저 합니다. 그리고 代理部도 더욱 廣張하겟습니다. 도서목록도 발행하겟습니다. 여러분, 지지하여주십시오.
8월1일 東光社
