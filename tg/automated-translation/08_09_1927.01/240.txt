Songs of Home
						Kim An-sŏ

Drizzling Rain

Drizzle, drizzle
On Hwangp'o, the fine rain falling
Today too, for half a day,
Has only made the grains of sand cry out.

It doesn’t come when I wait,
But on a sorrowful day, drizzle, drizzle—
Could it be the tears of that person
Parted from without even meeting?

On a sorrowful day, drizzle, drizzle,
Even the seagulls of Ŏyŏngdo,
Their wings heavy with damp,
Fly in flocks.

A body drifting three hundred *ri* by waterway—
Oh, boatman of Namp'o,
In what place right now
Might you be struggling through tears in the drizzling rain?

Autumn

On the water where the boat path is severed,
Only a seagull, alone, <17>
Sings a sorrowful song.

The chilly autumn wind
Ruffles the water's surface
And goes off somewhere.

Letting out a "kkaa-kkaa" cry,
When the seagull weeps,
Tears form in the clear sky.

The autumn wind blows gently, gently—
Everything is a dream within a dream;
Will it all just scatter like this? <18>

**Summary**

These two short poems by Kim An-sŏ, titled "Drizzling Rain" and "Autumn," evoke a sense of sorrow, longing, and displacement, using imagery associated with coastal life and changing seasons. The speaker reflects on separation, perhaps from a loved one or a hometown, symbolized by the rain, the crying sand, the dampened seagulls, and the solitary seagull in autumn. The poems convey a melancholic mood possibly reflecting the atmosphere of loss or hardship during the colonial period. For historians, the poems offer insight into personal expressions of sadness and nostalgia from the 1920s, using specific place names (Hwangp'o, Ŏyŏngdo, Namp'o) that ground the sentiment geographically, possibly hinting at themes of wandering or exile common in literature of that era.

故鄕의 노래
						金岸曙
보슬비
黃浦에 보슬보슬
내리는 실비는
오늘도 한나절을
모래알만 울려 노핫소.

기다릴 때엔 안 오다가도
설은 날이면 보슬보슬
만나지도 못하고 갈라진
그 사람의 눈물이랄가.

설은 날이면 보슬보슬
魚泳島 갈매기도
나래가 축진해서
떼를 지어 날아드오.

水路 삼백리를
배ㅅ길로 떠도는 몸이라
南浦의 사공님
지금쯤은 어느 곳에서
보슬비에 눈물 겨울꼬.

가을
배ㅅ길 끈허진 물우에
갈매기만 혼자서 ＜17＞
설은 노래 부름니다.

싸늘한 갈ㅅ바람은
물우에 물쌀을 돗치고
어대론지 가고 맘니다.

「가가」목을 노하서
갈매기가 울으면
맑은 하늘엔 눈물 짐니다.

가을바람은 산들산들
모든 것은 꿈속에 꿈이라
이대로 흐터지고 말까요.＜18＞
＜17-18＞
