Special Notice

With the thought of acquiring a more general readership, Tonggwang (東光) is striving to become an easier and more interesting magazine. This issue expresses that thought to some extent. From the next issue, we intend to do so even more.

However, this does not mean we intend to fall completely into being entertainment-focused. We only aim to express things worth knowing about our reality as plainly as possible, to the maximum extent expression allows. At the same time, we also believe a more fundamental reform is necessary and are currently considering it. We expect to announce new plans in the autumn.

A major treatise by Mr. Yi Chŏng-sŏp (李晶燮), titled "The General Situation in the East (東洋大勢) and the Future of Korea (朝鮮の 將來)," was supposed to be published in this issue but ended up not being published permanently. Also, due to a flood of articles, the two fascinating serials, "The Story of Nameless" (無名氏傳) and "A Russian Literary Giant's Travelogue on Korea" (露文豪의 朝朝鮮紀行), could not be included. The fact that there is only one piece from Tonggwang University (東光大學) is for the same reason.

Starting with this issue, we have decided to solicit articles from our readers with prizes in every issue. For the article topics, we intend to select, as much as possible, immediate and urgent issues, issues that can provide benefit, and issues of profound ideological significance. We hope that you will all prepare your brushes and submit many entries. Besides these, if you send any writing, we will publish the well-done pieces. There are still few writers in Korea. We await the emergence of hidden geniuses.

Meanwhile, we experimentally published the "Anthology of Korean Poems and Paintings" (조선시화집) and the "Literary Reader" (문예독본). Gaining confidence from the performance of those books, we are considering starting a publishing business on a large scale going forward. Details will be announced next month, but we ask for your support starting now.

The barley harvest season has passed, and it is now the steaming summer. We hope that those of you going to the water or mountains, and those of you working hard in the fields, dripping with sweat, will all go forward with a vigorous spirit.

Everyone! In this reality where putting out a single issue is more difficult than giving birth to a son, we are currently striving to release the August issue right on schedule. We only ask that you observe our steady efforts.

There are those seeking old issues of Tonggwang. We have issues from No. 1 to No. 16 bound into books. They are in two volumes, upper and lower. Each volume costs 2 *wŏn*. Those who wish to purchase them, please make a request.

July 1st
Tonggwangsa (東光社) Paek

**Summary**

This editorial notice from *Tonggwang* magazine outlines its new direction towards becoming more accessible and engaging for a wider audience, while still covering important contemporary issues. It announces plans for fundamental reforms, apologies for unpublished articles due to space constraints (including a major piece by Yi Chŏng-sŏp and two serials), and the launch of a reader submission contest with prizes. The notice also mentions the company's experimental foray into book publishing ("Anthology of Korean Poems and Paintings," "Literary Reader") and plans for larger-scale publishing ventures. It concludes with seasonal greetings and an appeal for understanding regarding the difficulties of publishing regularly under current conditions, while assuring readers of their commitment. For historians, the text offers insights into the editorial strategies of a Korean magazine during the colonial period, attempts at reader engagement, the challenges faced by publishers (hinting at potential censorship or resource limitations causing articles to be pulled or delayed), and the nascent expansion of Korean publishing houses into book formats beyond magazines. The mention of specific unpublished works and authors is also of interest.

特告
좀더 일반적인 독자를 획득하자는 생각으로 東光은 더 쉽고 더 재미잇는 잡지가 되려고 노력중입니다. 이번호는 어느 정도까지 그 생각을 표현한 것입니다. 다음호부터는 좀더 그리하랴고 합니다.
그러나 그러타고 아주 오락본위로 떠러지자는 것은 아닙니다. 오직 우리 현실에서 알아야할만한 것을 표현할 수 잇는 최대한도에서 가장 평이하게 해보자는 것입니다. 그러는 동시에 일층 근본적인 개혁이 필요하다고도 생각하여 지금 고려중입니다. 가을에는 새로운 게획을 발표하게 될 줄을 압니다.
이번호에는 李晶燮씨의 大論文 『東洋大勢와 朝鮮의 將來』라는 것이 실릴뻔하다가 영영 못실리게 되엇습니다. 또 기사의 폭주로 흥미진진한 兩大 게속기사 『無名氏傳』과 『露文豪의 朝鮮紀行』을 못실렷습니다. 東光大學이 僅一篇인 것도 같은 이유입니다.
이번호부터 매호에 현상으로 독자제위의 글을 모집하기로 되엇습니다. 글 문제는 될 수 잇는대로 당면의 긴급한 문제, 유익을 줄만한 문제, 사상적 뜻깊은 문제 등을 택하려합니다. 여러분께서 붓을 다리어서 많이 응모하시기를 바랍니다. 그밖에 무슨 글이던지 보내시면 잘된 것은 게재하겟습니다. 조선에 글쓰는 사람이 아직 적습니다. 숨은 천재의 출현을 기다립니다.
그동안 우리는 시험적으로 『조선시화집』과 『문예독본』을 출판햇섯습니다. 그들 책의 성적에 자신을 얻어 앞으로 대대적으로 출판사업을 시작해볼까 합니다. 자세한 것은 래월에 발표하겟습니다마는 지금부터 여러분의 지지를 구해둡니다.
보리 가을이 지나가고 찌는 여름이외다. 물로 산으로 가시는 여러분이나 또는 비지땀 흘리며 일터에 잇는 여러분이나 다같이 씩씩한 정신으로 나가기를 빕니다.
여러분! 한호 내보내기가 아들나키 보다도 더 어려운 이 현실에서 우리는 지금 팔월호를 제긔일에 꼭 내노랴고 애를 쓰고 잇습니다. 오직 우리의 꾸준한 노력을 보아주시기를 바랍니다.
옛날 동광을 찾는 이가 잇습니다. 一號부터 十六號까지 책으로 맨 것이 잇습니다. 上下 兩卷입니다. 한 권에 2원식입니다. 원하시는 이는 청구하십시오.
7월 1일
東光社 白
