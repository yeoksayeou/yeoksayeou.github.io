Magazine Title: 동광 제14호
Publication Date: 1927년 06월 01일
Article Title: 徽文高等普通學校 校歌 (Hwimun Higher Common School School Song)
Author: 崔南善 作歌 (Written by Ch'oe Nam-sŏn)
Article Type: 시 (Poem/Song Lyrics)

1. Gazing down upon the myriad houses (萬戶) of Sŏul (阿峴),
Meaningful is our lofty home on Polchae hill.
Youthful children gathered in the forest of learning (學林),
Meaningful is the diligent study in this home.

2. Containing the great light that illuminates the world (世),
This home is precious as it spreads it forth.
Striving to become vessels that increase brightness,
Our very pride is giving our utmost devotion.

3. These arms and these legs, this heart and this soul,
Will we rest even a little from forging and polishing?
Intending to make the sounds born here spread widely,
Intending to cover this home with glory.

4. Only with cleanliness and diligence,
Carrying this heavy burden lightly,
Passing through this difficult world easily,
May we and our home always have purpose/meaning.
Kim Su-yŏng submitted <38>
<38>

**Summary**

This article presents the lyrics for the school song of Hwimun Higher Common School, written by the prominent intellectual Ch'oe Nam-sŏn in 1927. The song evokes pride in the school's location overlooking Seoul, emphasizes the importance of diligent study and enlightenment ("spreading light"), calls for the physical and mental training of students, and encourages cleanliness, diligence, and bringing glory to the school. From a historical perspective, the song reflects the values promoted by a leading Korean private educational institution during the Japanese colonial period. Historians might find interest in the emphasis on self-improvement, enlightenment, and contributing to the "world" as potential expressions of Korean aspirations within the constraints of colonial rule, as well as the use of older Korean language forms and the authorship by Ch'oe Nam-sŏn, whose legacy is complex due to his later collaboration.

徽文高等普通學校 校歌
						崔南善 作歌
一. 잘집(萬戶)의 서울(阿峴)을 눈아레 깔고서
뜻 있네 볼재에 우뚝한 우리 집
즘음의 어린이 숲속(學林)에 모여서
뜻 있네 이 집에 힘쓰는 배움들

二. 누리(世)를 빛의 일 큰 빛을 담아서
퍼지게 난우매 이 집이 귀할 사
밝음을 느리는 그릇이 되려고
성성을 다함이 제 자랑 이거라

三. 이 팔과 이 다리 이 맘과 이 넋은
단렪고 닦이를 좀이나 쉬울가.
예서난 소리가 넓이게 하련이
이 집에 영화를 덮이게 하련이

四. 오직 깨끗함과 부즈런 함으로
무겁은 이 짐을 가볍게 나르며
어렵은 이 누리 쉬웁게 지나가
우리와 우리 집 늘이뜻 있으라.
