Article Title:	新計劃進行中의 朝鮮鐵道, 今後 20年後가 되면 3千 5百哩가 된다고 (Chosŏn Railway Under New Plans, Said to Reach 3,500 Miles in 20 Years)
Author:	
Article Type:	소식 (News)

Regardless of East or West, as the world progresses, transportation systems develop further. In our Chosŏn as well, we can see that railway lines have increased considerably in recent years.

Now, while the existing railway lines in Chosŏn are generally well-known, it would likely not be without benefit to investigate how much the railway lines will be extended in the future. Looking at the planned lines scheduled by the Railway Bureau:

Tumen Line: Between Unggi and Tonggwanjin, 97 miles (哩)
Hyesan Line: Between Sŏngjin and Hyesanjin, 88 miles
Manp'o Line: Between Sunch’ŏn and Manp’ojin, 178 miles
East Sea Line: Between Wŏnsan and P'ohang, and Ulsan and Pusan, 341 miles
Kyŏngjŏn Line: Between Chinju and Chŏnju, and Wŏnch'on and Tamyang, 156 miles
Total: 860 miles

Besides these, there are several more lines, but these are merely the ones highlighted that require comparatively large expenses and are expected to yield significant results. The Tumen, Hyesan, and Manp'o lines are intended to provide great convenience for transporting the inexhaustible coal and timber resources piled up in the Kando (關此) area, and also to establish economic contact with adjacent regions. The East Sea Line is for developing and transporting coal, timber, and other minerals from the T'aebaek Mountain Range (脊梁山脈), as well as marine products from the East Sea, in addition to pioneering undeveloped coastal areas, and also connecting with the Hamgyŏng Line to form the great Eastern Longitudinal Line. The Kyŏngjŏn Line aims to connect the Honam region via the Pusan area, forming a transversal line for Southern Chosŏn.

Simultaneously with planning these new lines, there are ongoing efforts to purchase the currently intervening private railway lines, totaling over 200 miles, and attach them to state management. The complete purchase will require five years. The current private railway lines are as follows:

Kyŏngnam Line: Between Masan and Chinju, 43.5 miles (435 is likely a typo, 43.5 miles or 70km is correct)
Chŏllam Line: Between Songjŏng-ri and Tamyang, 22.7 miles (227 is likely a typo, 22.7 miles or 36.5km is correct)
Kyŏngdong Line: Between Taegu and *san (likely Hayang 경산), between Sŏak and Ulsan, 92.0 miles (920 is likely a typo, 92.0 miles or 148km is correct)
Above belong to the Chosŏn Railway Company (朝鮮鐵道會社)

Chŏnbuk Line: Between Iri and Chŏnju, 15.5 miles (155 is likely a typo, 15.5 miles or 25km is correct)
Above belongs to the Chŏnbuk Railway Company (全北鐵道會社)

Hamgyŏng Line: Between Hoeryŏng and Tonggwanjin, 36.1 miles (361 is likely a typo, 36.1 miles or 58km is correct)
Above belongs to the Tumen Railway Company (圖們鐵道會社)
Total: 209.8 miles (2098 is likely a typo based on adding the corrected mileages, or the individual mileages are significantly different than known historical lengths)

After the acquisition, the remaining private railways will include the Chosŏn Railway Company, Kyŏngnam Railway Company, and Kŭmgangsan Electric Railway Company, etc. Among these, the Chosŏn Railway Company, once a part of its lines are sold, will likely use the considerable surplus funds to add about 200 miles of track. Including the Kyŏngnam Railway and Kŭmgangsan Electric Railway, it is predicted that about 600 miles will be laid over the next 12 years.

According to the investigation at the end of last year, the total operating mileage of all Chosŏn railways was merely 1,829 miles, combining 1,342 miles of state-owned and 487 miles of private lines. However, after the next 12 years pass, the total length combining state-owned and private lines will reach 3,501 miles, which is an increase of 91 percent compared to the total operating mileage at the end of last year. <33>

**Summary**

This 1927 article details the ambitious plans for expanding the railway network in colonial Korea over the next 12 to 20 years. It outlines five major new lines (Tumen, Hyesan, Manp'o, East Sea, Kyŏngjŏn) totaling 860 miles, primarily aimed at extracting resources like coal and timber, developing coastal areas, and connecting different regions. The plan also includes the government acquisition of over 200 miles of existing private railway lines within five years. The article projects that, combined with expected private railway expansion, the total network will grow from 1,829 miles to 3,501 miles in 12 years, a 91% increase. For historians, this article clearly illustrates the colonial administration's focus on infrastructure development for resource extraction and strategic control, integrating Korea more tightly into the Japanese empire's economic and transportation network. The specific lines mentioned highlight the targeted regions for resource exploitation and the goal of creating longitudinal and transversal connections across the peninsula.

기사형태	소식

新計劃進行中의 朝鮮鐵道, 今後 20年後가 되면 3千 5百哩가 된다고
東西洋을 물론하고 世運의 진보를 딸아 교통기관이 더욱 발달하나니 우리 朝鮮에서도 근년에 이르러 철도선로가 꽤 증가하여 감을 볼 수 있다.
그런데 현재 朝鮮 철도의 기성 선로에 대하여는 일반이 다 잘 아는 바어니와 장차 철도선이 얼마만콤 연장될는지 이를 한 번 알아 보는 것도 또한 무익한 일이 아닐까 한다. 이제 철도국으로서 계획하는 예정선을 보면
圖們線 雄基, 潼關鎭間 97哩
惠山線 城津, 惠山鎭間 88哩
滿浦線 順天, 灣浦鎭間 178哩
東海線 元山, 浦項及蔚山釜山間341哩
慶全線 晉州, 全州間及院村潭陽間 156哩
計 860哩
이 밖에도 幾個線路가 더 있으나 그 중에도 비교적 거대의 경비를 요하며 다대한 효과를 收할 것만을 듦에 不外하다. 그런데 圖們, 惠山, 滿浦의 三線은 關此 일대에 무진장으로 쌓이어 있는 석탄과 목재를 반출하는대 막대한 편을 주게하며 또 인접지대와의 경제상 접촉을 시키기 위함이요. 東海線은 脊梁山脈의 석탄, 목재 기타 광물이며 東海의 해산물을 개발 반출하는 이외에 海岸未發의 地를 개척하게 되고 또 咸鏡線과 연락하여 東部 大縱貫線을 형성하려 함이요. 慶全線은 釜山 방면의 경유로 湖南 방면을 연락하여 南朝鮮의 횡단선을 형성하려고 한다.
이와 같이 新線을 계획하는 동시에 기간에 개재한 현재의 사설 철도선 200여리를 매수하여 국영에 붙이려 하는 중이다. 전부 매수는 5개년간을 요할 것이다. 현재의 사설 철도선은 아레와 같으니
慶南線 馬山, 晉州間 435哩
全南線 松汀里, 潭陽間 227哩
慶東線 大邱, *山間
西岳, 蔚山間 920哩
右는 朝鮮鐵道會社所屬
全北線 裡里, 全州間 155哩
右는 全北鐵道會社所屬
咸鏡線 會寧, 潼關鎭間 361哩
右는 圖們鐵道會社所屬
計 2098哩
매수된 후의 사설 철도는 朝鮮 철도회사 京南 철도회사 및 金剛山 전기철도회사 등이 있으리니 취중 朝鮮 철도회사는 그 소속선의 일부가 매각되면 곳 상당한 여유 자금으로써 약 200리의 선로을 증설할찌요 기타 京南 철도와 金剛山 전철까지 넣어서 今後 12년간에 약 600리을 부설하게 되리라 예측한다.
전년 말 조사에 의하건대 全朝鮮 철도의 영업 哩數가 국유 1,342리, 사설 487리을 합하여 모도 1,829리에 불과하다. 그러나 今後 12년을 경과한 시는 국유와 사설을 합하여 총 연장이 3,501리에 이를 것이니 곳 작년말의 총 영업 哩數에 비하면 9할 1분이나 증가하는 셈이다.＜33＞
