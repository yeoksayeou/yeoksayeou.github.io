Sorrowful Love
						餘心

Love is the round moon flickering in the water
A drunken I Paek trying to scoop up the moon in the water

"Forgive me. I have a man I've been engaged to since childhood!"
She wrote in her last letter.
Yet still, the drunken heart
Tries to scoop up the moon in the water!

When the moon in the water was shattered
The drunken Poet wept
But when the moon became round again
The Poet, arms outstretched, toward the river
Toward the water's edge overflowing with moonlight
Sought death. Found repose.
But a broken love has no way
Of becoming round again
The sobered heart weeps and laments for days gone by
Wandering alone above the water foam, searching everywhere
But the flickering moonlight is a bygone dream
The tide envelops, dark and sorrowful
Ah! The cold and sorrowful water foam soaking the whole body.
1926. 11 <32>

**Summary**

This poem by Yŏsim uses the metaphor of the drunken poet Li Bai (I Paek) trying to grasp the moon's reflection in the water to depict the pain of unattainable and lost love. The speaker receives a final letter ending a relationship due to a prior engagement. Despite knowing the love is lost (like the moon's reflection), the speaker cannot let go, ultimately finding only despair, likened to the cold, enveloping water foam. The poem contrasts the cyclical nature of the moon (shattering and reforming) with the finality of the broken love, which cannot be restored. From a historical perspective, the poem's intense expression of personal despair and disillusionment, published during the Japanese colonial period (1927), might resonate with or subtly reflect the broader societal feelings of loss and hopelessness prevalent among Koreans at the time, even though its theme is personal. The use of a classical Chinese reference (Li Bai) also points to the continuing influence of classical East Asian literary traditions.

섧은 사랑
						餘心
사랑은 물 속에 얼른거리는 둥글헌 달
물속엣 달을 건지려는 술 취한 李白

『용서하서요. 내게는 어려서붙어 약혼한 사내가 있어요!』 하고
그는 마즈막 편지를 썼다.
그래도 술 취한 마음은
물속엣 달을 건지려고!

물속엣 달이 으지러진 때
술취한 詩人은 울엇더라
그러나 달이 다시 둥그러진 때
詩人은 팔 벌리고 강물로
달빛이 흘러 넘치는 물걸우로
죽엄을 찾다. 안식을 얻다
그러나 이지러진 사랑은 다시
둥그러질리 바이 없어
지나간 날을 울고 탄식하는 선술깬 가슴이
물거품 우로 혼자 헤매여 두루 찾으나
얼른 거리는 달빛은 지나간 꿈
어둡고 섧게도 휩싸는 潮流
아! 왼 몸을 적시는 차고도 섧은 물거품.
1926. 11 ＜32＞
＜32＞
