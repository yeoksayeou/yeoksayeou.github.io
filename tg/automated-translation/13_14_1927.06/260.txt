**Body Text Translation**
Tonga Ilbo Editor-in-Chief Yi Kwangsu

Pen names: Ch'unwŏn (春園), Changbaeksanin (長白山人). Born February 1st, Kae'guk 501 (1892). Born in Chŏngju (定州). Original Domicile: 127 Sŭngsam-dong (崇三洞), Kyŏngsŏng Prefecture (京城府). Current Residence: Same.
Father: Agriculture. Wife: Hŏ Yŏngsuk (許英肅). Married 1921.

1910: Graduated from Meiji Gakuin (明治學院) Middle School Department, Tōkyō (東京), Japan (日本). Same year: Teacher at Osan School (五山學校), Chŏngju (4 years).
1919: Studied at Waseda University (早稻田大學), Tōkyō, Japan. Same year: Head of Arts and Sciences Department, Tonga Ilbo Company (東亞日報社) (5 years).
1922: Teacher at Kyŏngsin School (儆信學校), Kyŏngsŏng (京城) (3 years).
Current Position: Editor-in-Chief, Tonga Ilbo Company.

Director, Suyǎng Tonguhoe (修養同友會).
Authored works: Novels such as *Mujŏng* (無情), *Kaech'ŏkcha* (개척자 - Pioneer), *Chaesaeng* (再生 - Rebirth), *Sŏndocha* (先導者 - Leader), *Hŏ Saeng Chŏn* (許生傳), *Chŏlmŭn Kkum* (젊은 꿈 - Young Dream), *Ch'unwŏn Tanjip'yŏn* (春園단편집 - Ch'unwŏn Short Story Collection), etc. Also *Kŭmgangsan Yugi* (金剛山遊記 - Travels in the Kŭmgang Mountains), *Chosŏn ŭi Hyŏnjae wa Changnae* (조선의 현재와 장래 - Korea's Present and Future), *Sinsaenghwal-lon* (新生活論 - Discourse on New Life), etc.
Travelled to Russia (露西亞) and China (중국). Favorite reading: Works of Tolstoy (톨스토이) 及 (and) the Bible, Buddhist scriptures, etc. Hobby: Travel.
