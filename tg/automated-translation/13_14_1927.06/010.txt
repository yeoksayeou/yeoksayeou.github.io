**Body Text Translation**
Apology Notice

Last time, the May issue received unexpectedly high praise and was completely sold out within just one week of publication, causing much inconvenience to several hundred new readers who had requested advance payment. What is even more distressing is that we were even unable to send copies to our contributing writers. Therefore, for those who wish to subscribe to this magazine, requesting it in advance via "Postal Transfer Kyŏngsŏng No. 4" (振替京城4番) is the safest method.

Furthermore, at the time of the May issue's publication, due to the post office's extremely meticulous handling, the dispatch was unavoidably delayed by several days. We apologize for this as well.

Mr. U Hoik's (禹浩翊) "Mugunghwa ko" (無窮花考 - Study on the Rose of Sharon) has unavoidably been temporarily suspended, and the same applies to "P'ip'agi" (琵琶記 - Tale of the Pipa). However, we will definitely publish them starting from next month's issue.

**Summary**

This editorial notice apologizes to readers and contributors because the previous month's issue (May 1927) sold out extremely quickly, preventing many from obtaining a copy, including advance subscribers and even writers. It encourages readers to subscribe in advance via postal transfer to ensure they receive future issues. The notice also apologizes for a mailing delay caused by "extremely meticulous handling" at the post office and announces the temporary postponement of two articles, promising their publication in the next issue. For historians, the notice highlights the magazine's popularity despite potential distribution challenges. The mention of postal delays due to "meticulous handling" is particularly interesting, potentially hinting at scrutiny or censorship by colonial authorities, a common experience for Korean publications during this period.

謝告
지난번 5월호는 예상외의 대호평으로 발행된지 불과 1주간에 全數 매진되었음으로 인하여 先金請求하신 수백 신독자 제씨에게 대하여 미안한 일이 매우 많았음니다. 더욱이 불안한 것은 기고하신 여러분에게 보내들이지 못하기까지 하였음이외다. 그런즉 本誌를 구독하실 분은 「振替京城4番」으로 밀이 청구하여 두시는 것이 가장 안전함니다.
더욱이 5월호 발행 당시에는 우편국에서의 몹시 까다라운 취급으로 말미암아 할 수 없이 발송이 幾日  지연되었음니다. 이에 아울러 사과들임니다.
禹浩翊씨의 「無窮花考」는 부득이 잠깐 休揭하게 되었사오며 「琵琶記」도 그리하었사오나 來月號붙어는 꼭 게재하겠음니다.
