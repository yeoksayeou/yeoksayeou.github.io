Magazine Title: 동광 제25호
Publication Date: 1931년 09월 04일
Article Title: 炎天苦吟十首 (Ten Poems of Anguished Muttering Under the Burning Sky)
Author: 全武吉
Article Type: 시 (Poetry)

1. Lighthouse
From the distant sea lighthouse
A light flashes, brightening the way.
The gratitude for that light,
Only a boatman (船夫) might know, but
If only this body's benefit to the world
Could be as much as the lighthouse.

2. Sea Bathing
As if to fill the sea,
Crowds of men and women surge forth.
Saltwater benefits the body,
And ultraviolet rays are said to be good, but
Looking after ruined livelihoods,
It cannot be better than tilling the fields.

3. Cooperation (合力)
Starving and without a path,
The wandering people—
Even if they were to enlighten this whole land,
Would the deep-blue resentment disappear?
But let's endure and gather our strength,
To use it together someday.

4. Compatriots in Manchuria (在滿동포)
The wide fields of the land of Manchuria
Were created without an owner.
Whoever goes there to till the fields,
Why should it be called a crime?
Being trampled upon because they are weak—
What is the right of the strong?

5. Licensed Prostitution District (公娼街)
Whether an acquaintance or a stranger,
As if there's no need to distinguish,
She grabs the sleeve of a passing drifter (浪人)
And puts on airs.
A body sold for 1 won, yet
She frets that no one comes.

6. Westernization (洋化)
The street vendor shouts out,
"Western pears! Western strawberries!"
Just because the character 'Yang' (洋 - Western) is applied,
Did you think it was good?
The taste of Chosŏn radish, Chosŏn eggplant—
Knowing one's own taste comes first.

7. Thinking of a Friend (思友)
In associating with people,
Deep association causes pain.
In the several years since they left, <80>
There hasn't been a day they were forgotten.
Today again, there is no news;
Resentment towards them arises naturally.

8. Atmosphere
The atmosphere of this year
Breaks the writers' brushes.
The art school (藝術派) wanders,
And the proletarian writers (푸로 작가) dislike legality.
This way and that, they quietly die.
Are the rivers and mountains going with them?

9. Novice Drawer (그림배기)
Being a novice drawer at 30,
I cannot help but feel shy.
Blaming the lack of daylight,
Why is some woman peeking?
Flustered, I hide it, and then gone—
Not knowing where she went, I feel regret.

10. Ordinary
Though I make loud pronouncements,
There is no change upon this land.
Being talented or untalented—
How many feet apart is the distance?
Those who live deceived, thinking they are great,
I truly hate to see them.
1931. 8. 2 Evening <81>

**Summary**

This collection of ten short poems by Chŏn Mu-gil, written in 1931, captures the author's anguished reflections ("苦吟") on various aspects of life and society under the "burning sky" (炎天) of colonial Korea. The poems touch upon themes of individual purpose versus societal contribution (Lighthouse), the contrast between superficial leisure and underlying poverty (Sea Bathing), the need for collective action amidst suffering (Cooperation), the injustice faced by Korean migrants in Manchuria (Compatriots in Manchuria), the degradation of poverty (Licensed Prostitution District), the critique of unthinking Westernization (Westernization), personal longing (Thinking of a Friend), the stifling intellectual and artistic climate (Atmosphere), personal insecurity (Novice Drawer), and disillusionment with boastful mediocrity (Ordinary). For historians, the poems offer valuable insights into the social consciousness of the time, reflecting widespread economic hardship, anxieties about cultural identity amidst foreign influence (Japanese and Western), the plight of Koreans displaced to Manchuria, and the constrained environment for intellectuals and artists under colonial rule. The mention of specific groups like "proletarian writers" also points to the ideological currents of the era.

炎天苦吟十首
						全武吉
1. 등대
먼 바다 등대에서
길 밝히 불 반짝이네
그 불빗 감사함을
船夫라야 알리마는
이 몸이 세상 이롭힘
등대만큼 되어도

2. 해수욕
바다를 메울 듯이
남녀의 떼 밀려가네
짠물이 몸에 利코
자외광선 좋다마는
묺어진 살림 돌보아
밭 갈기만 못하리

3. 合力
주리고 갈 길 없이
헤매우는 백성들이
이 땅을 다 깨친들
푸른 원한 슬어지랴
그러나 참고 힘 모세
한 때 함께 쓰려면

4. 在滿동포
만주땅 넓은 벌은
임자 없이 생겨난 곳
뉘 가서 밭 갈기로
무삼 죄라 이름지랴
약자라 뭇지름받네
강자 권리 무엇고

5. 公娼街
아는 손 모르는 자
가려볼 것 없다는 듯
지나는 浪人 소매
부여잡고 아양피네
1원에 파는 몸이건
안오는가 애태네

6. 洋化
장사군 외처 부름
洋배차요 洋딸기네
洋字만 발리우면
좋은 줄로 알앗드냐
조선 무 조선 가지 맛
입맛 알자 첨일네

7. 思友
사람을 사괴이되
기피 사굄 괴롭일네
그 간 뒤 수 년 동안＜80＞
잊처질 날 없엇거니
오날도 소식이 없네
그의 원망 절로 나

8. 분위기
이 해의 분위기가
작가들의 붓을 꺽네
예술파 헤매이고
푸로 작가 합법 싫대
이저래 가만히 죽네
강산 함께 가느냐?

9. 그림배기
30에 그림배니
수집어 못할네라
날 없는 틈을 탓건
웬 안악네 엿보노나
당황해 감초고 나니
간 곳 몰나 아쉬워

10. 평범
큰 소리 치고 나도
이 땅 우에 변화 없네
잘 나고 못난 것이
몇 자 거리 머다드냐?
잘난 줄 속아 사는 이
참아 보기 싫어라
1931. 8. 2夕＜81＞
＜80-81＞
