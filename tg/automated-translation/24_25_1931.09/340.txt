Doctor and Writer
Paek Injē (白麟濟), M.D.

Did I have thoughts of becoming a doctor (博士)? Yes, even back then I heard that being a doctor was good. But among doctors, they said a Doctor of Philosophy (철학박사) was good.

And since Mr. Yi Kwangsu (李光洙) was a teacher at Osan (五山) during my middle school days, I wanted to become a literary man (文士).

(Reporter's comment: A medical doctor (醫師) and a literary man (文士) are not so far apart, so it seems there may still be many opportunities.) <68>

While Doing Mining Practice
Professor Yi Ch'unho (李春昊) of Yŏnjŏn

My friends said I would become an educator (敎育家), and in the end, it turned out that way. When I went to America (美國), I said I would study engineering (工學), but when I went to a mine (鑛山) for practical training (實習), I saw two people die instantly when a mine wall (礦壁) collapsed, and I changed direction.

(Reporter's comment: If you had become a mine owner (鑛業家), there's no law saying you wouldn't have become the second Ch'oe Ch'anghak (崔昌學). It's a pity.) <73> <68, 73>

**Summary**

This article presents brief memoirs from Dr. Paek Injē and Professor Yi Ch'unho about their youthful career ambitions. Paek initially considered becoming a Doctor of Philosophy or a writer, influenced by the prominent writer Yi Kwangsu who taught him, before ultimately pursuing medicine. Yi Ch'unho intended to study engineering in America but shifted to education after witnessing a traumatic fatal accident during mining practice.

For historians, the article provides glimpses into the aspirations and formative experiences of educated Koreans during the Japanese colonial period. It highlights the influence of major intellectual figures like Yi Kwangsu, the opportunities and risks associated with studying technical fields (including abroad in America), and the awareness of successful Korean entrepreneurs like Ch'oe Ch'anghak, reflecting the complex social and economic landscape of the time.

박사가 될 생각이 잇섯느냐고요. 예 그 때도 박사가 좋다는 말은 들엇섯지오. 박사는 박사래도 철학박사가 좋다고 합디다.
그리고 중학에서는 李光洙씨가 五山 선생으로 잇든 시절이니 만큼 문사가 되려 햇소.

광업실습을 하다가
延專교수 李春昊

나는 교육가가 되리라고 동무들이 그러더니 결국 그대로 된 세음입니다. 미국 가서는 공학을 배운다고 햇는데 광산에 실습 갓다가 礦壁이 묺어저서 2명이 즉사하는 것 보고 방향을 전환햇지오.
