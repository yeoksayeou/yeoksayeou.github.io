Public Notice

This issue is unavoidably a little late. Next month's issue will definitely be published in the latter part of this month. We intend to make the content even more lively. The cool autumn air has entered the outskirts, so it is the season for reading. Please look forward to it greatly.

The valuable opinions (高見) from various seniors regarding "The Pros and Cons and Methods of Forming National Movement Organizations" (『민족운동단체결성가부급방법』), which were prepared for this issue, unfortunately had to be suppressed (말살하지 않을 수 없게 되엇습니다). We especially apologize to the teachers (선생들) who contributed their precious manuscripts (옥고) during their busy schedules.

Regarding the Manchuria issue, the most important reference material also suffered a miscarriage (流産이 되엇습니다). Therefore, the content of the special feature did not turn out to be very satisfactory. We ask our readers for their full understanding (諒察하시옵소서).

September 1st
Tonggwangsa, Paek

**Summary**

This editorial note from Tonggwangsa apologizes to readers for the delay of the current issue (No. 25) and promises the next issue will be out promptly and feature more lively content. More significantly, it explains that prepared articles on the formation of Korean national movement organizations and important materials regarding the "Manchuria issue" had to be suppressed ("miscarried" or "erased") and could not be published. The publisher apologizes to the contributors and asks for the readers' understanding regarding the resulting unsatisfactory special feature section. For historians, this brief notice is revealing about the censorship environment in colonial Korea in 1931, explicitly mentioning the suppression of politically sensitive topics related to Korean nationalism and the volatile situation in Manchuria, likely due to pressure from Japanese authorities.

謹告
이번호는 부득이 조곰 늦엇습니다. 來月號는 今月 下旬에 반드시 出世시키겟습니다. 내용은 일층 생기발랄하게 하고저합니다. 新凉이 入郊햇으니 독서할 시기외다. 많이 支待하십시오.
이번호에 실리려고 준비햇든 『민족운동단체결성가부급방법』이란 제선배의 高見은 부득이 말살하지 않을 수 없게 되엇습니다. 분주하신 시간에 옥고를 주신 선생들께 특히 사죄하옵니다.
만주문제에 관한 것도 가장 중요한 참고재료가 流産이 되엇습니다. 그래서 특집의 내용이 그리 만족하게 못되엇습니다. 독자는 십분 諒察하시옵소서.
9월 1일
東光社 白
