Cancellation

The article "Record of Male Student Reputations" (『男學生評判記』) published on page 73 of this magazine's November issue last year contained factual errors and is therefore fully retracted. <55>

**Summary**

This is a brief notice from the editors of *Tonggwang* magazine retracting an article titled "Record of Male Student Reputations" published in the previous November issue. The reason cited for the retraction is factual errors within the article. For historians, this notice is interesting as it indicates editorial oversight and the correction of errors, potentially hinting at the sensitivity or controversial nature of evaluating students publicly ("評判記" implies reputation or critique) during the colonial period, which might have led to complaints or pressure necessitating a retraction.
