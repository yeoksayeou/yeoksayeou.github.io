Youth
					Pak Yŏngsan

Youth

Where has the boy I used to see gone?
Suddenly, I am twenty – is this youth?
My reflection mirrored in the pond,
Looking back, my heart just pounds.

Yesterday it rained, today snow flutters,
Even though this lonely heart has nowhere to turn,
The deeply beautiful dream of hope
Flickers far away at the edge of the sky.

In Kyŏngju

1. Sitting alone on Panwŏlsŏng deep in the night,
When I gaze upon the moon hanging over Sŏak,
The sound of silver flutes played by carefree children
Wrings the heart of this different-minded traveler.

2. Is the dream of glory enjoyed for a thousand years
Fluttering at the tip of a withered blade of grass?
The splendid old palace is nowhere to be found,
Only a cuckoo (접동새) that has lost its way cries mournfully.

3. Even the Pongdŏksa temple bell has become mute,
To whom shall I ask about the old legends of Kerim?
Only Pulguksa and Ch'ŏmsŏngdae
Still boast of their ancient light. <57>

**Summary**

This publication features two poems by Pak Yŏngsan. The first, "Youth," captures the fleeting nature of time as the speaker reflects on reaching the age of twenty, feeling both bewildered and hopeful despite present loneliness and hardship. The second poem, "In Kyŏngju," expresses a deep sense of loss and melancholy while visiting the historical sites of the ancient Silla capital. The poet contrasts the past grandeur of the kingdom with the desolate present, finding only ruins and mournful sounds where glory once stood. For historians, these poems offer insight into the sentiments of Korean intellectuals during the colonial period: a personal introspection on youth combined with a poignant nostalgia for a lost national past, lamenting the decline symbolized by the ruins of Kyŏngju under foreign rule.

청춘
					朴永山
청춘
예전 보든 소년은 어대로가고
이십이라 어느 덧 이 청춘인가.
연못 속에 비최인 이 내 그림자
마주보니 가슴만 뛰노는구나.

어제는 비 오늘은 날리는 눈에
외로운 이 맘 조차 둘 곳 없어도,
그윽히 아름다운 희망의 꿈은
저 멀리 하늘 끝에 가믈거리네.

慶州에서
1. 깊은 밤 半月城에 홀로히 앉아
西岳에 비낀 달을 바라볼 적에
철없는 아이들의 은피리 소리
맘다른 나그네의 애를 끓는다.
2. 일천년 누려오든 榮華의 꿈이
시드른 풀닢 끝에 나부낌인가.
찬란한 옛 궁궐은 간 곳이 없고
깃 잃은 접동새만 구슬피 운다.
3. 奉德寺 인경 조차 벙어리되어
鷄林의 옛 전설을 뉘게 물을고
다만 지 佛國寺와 瞻星臺만이
아직도 묵은 빛을 자랑하누나. ＜57＞
