Company Announcement

Once the advance notice of this magazine's first issue was made public, letters of welcome and sympathy poured in frequently from various individuals at home and abroad. We, the associates (同人) of this company (本社), are endlessly grateful (感荷不已) and hereby express our gratitude through this publication.

Furthermore, while the delay of the publication date beyond the schedule like this, due to an unforeseen misfortune (不意의 厄), is an unavoidable matter given our current circumstances, we sincerely apologize (期必 謝禮). Therefore, the June issue is scheduled to be published around the 10th, so we hope for your understanding (下諒).

We pray for your continued vigorous efforts for the people (爲族彈精), and furthermore, we earnestly hope (伏願) that you will cherish (愛顧) this magazine even more and cooperate wholeheartedly (一心合力) with us associates (同人) for its development. We conclude here.

The Associates of the Tonggwang Company (동광사 동인), Respectfully <32>

**Summary**

This announcement from the associates of the *Tonggwang* magazine expresses deep gratitude for the public's supportive response to their planned first issue. It apologizes sincerely for an unexpected delay in publication, attributed to an "unforeseen misfortune," and informs readers that the June issue will now appear around the 10th. The editors ask for understanding and appeal for continued patronage and wholehearted cooperation for the magazine's future development, praying for the readers' own efforts for the nation. From a historical perspective, the mention of an "unforeseen misfortune" causing a delay is particularly interesting. In the context of 1926 Korea under Japanese rule, this likely hints at censorship, financial difficulties, or other pressures faced by Korean cultural or intellectual publications, offering a glimpse into the challenging environment for nationalist or independent expression during the colonial period.

社告
본지 창간예고가 한 번 세상에 나매 해내외의 諸位로부터 비일비재의 환영과 동정의 글이 답지함을 본사 同人 등은 感荷不已하야 지상으로 사의를 표하오며 또 불의의 액으로 창간일자가 예정보다 이가티 지연됨은 오늘날 우리의 경우상 부득이한 일이라 하나 기피 사례하옵는 바이오며 딸아서 6월호는 10일경에 발행할 예정이오니 下諒하시기를 바라나이다. 諸位의 더욱 爲族彈精함을 祝하옵고 남아로 본지를 한층 더 愛顧하사 그 발전을 위하여 同人 등과 일심 합력하옵기를 복원하고 그치나이다.
