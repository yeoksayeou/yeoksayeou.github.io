Article Title: 여름 (Summer)

Article Type: 시 (Poem)

Summer
						Son Chŏnghwa

The lovely sound of birds singing of summer
Rolls like jewels over the quietly flowing stream water,
And the clear cicada song, drawing out the long midday
Without complaint, renews my heart.

The farmer's tune, heard faintly from afar,
Joyfully forgets the scorching sun of July and August,
The Buddha farmer (부처농부) who straightens his back while weeding, sending smiles to the growing grain—
Gazing upon this, a savory scent stirs my own delight.

I stretched my four limbs wide, yawning upon the grass.
A piece of leisurely drifting cloud creeps into my chest,
And at the crow of the red rooster announcing noon (午正),
Embracing the cloud, my thoughts run—wherever they please.

When my body, weary from the daily grind, seeks the shade of the mountainside,
This revived coolness makes me forget my troubles.
How could I not long more for the mountains and sea, the more they wear me down?
Oh, this refreshing day's life—when shall I taste it again?
(At Kŭgyo-Paengmasansŏng (舊橋-白馬山城)) <84>

**Summary**

This poem by Son Chŏnghwa paints an idyllic picture of a Korean summer day. It evokes sensory details like birdsong, the sound of a stream, cicadas, a farmer's song, and the scent of the countryside. The speaker finds personal renewal and peace in observing nature and the contented labor of a farmer, contrasting this with their own daily weariness. The poem culminates in a longing appreciation for the simple, refreshing moments found in nature. From a historical perspective, the poem offers a glimpse into the appreciation of nature and rural life during the colonial period. The depiction of the "Buddha farmer," finding serene joy in toil despite the heat, and the speaker's escape into nature might reflect a desire for solace and connection to Korean landscapes amidst the hardships of the era, potentially serving as a subtle form of cultural expression.

여름
						孫貞華
여름을 노래하는 뭇새의 고운소리
한적히 흐르는 시내물 우에 구슬을 구을리고
한 낮을 길다 않고 길게 내어 빼는
청아한 매암 소리에 내 마음 새롭네.

저 멀리 아득하게 들리는 농군의 노래가락
7, 8월 찌는 볓을 흥겨워 잊어버리고
기음매다 허리펴 자라는 곡식에 웃음을 보내는 부처농부
이러틋 바라보니 구수한 내음새에 내흥도 돋네.

네 활개 쭉 뻗어 잔디 우에 기지개를 켯네.
한가히 떠도는 구름쪼각 내가슴에 기어 들고
午正을 알리는 붉은 숫탉의 꼬끼오ㅅ소리에
구름을 얼싸 안고 내 생각 어대로인가 달리네

내 하로 같이 고된몸 산기슭 그늘 쪽을 찾으면
이러케 되살은 듯 시원한 머리 괴로움을 잊거늘
산과 바다가 내 어찌 시달릴쓰록 더 그립지 않앗으랴
오오 이 하로의 시원한 삶이어 언제나 또 다시 맛볼까나.
(舊橋-白馬山城에서) ＜84＞
