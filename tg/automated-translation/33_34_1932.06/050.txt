Strong Power

The Inukai Cabinet has collapsed.
It must be a "National Unity" Cabinet. It must be a "Strong Power" Cabinet. Elder statesmen, bureaucrats, the military, the young officers faction, fascists... it's an undeniable fascist march. Even the Genrō (元老) have finally yielded to this sound.

Hitler occupies the Prussian Cabinet. How long will Hindenburg's future last?

East and West, "Strong Power" is omnipotent. Corrupt political parties, contradictory economic systems, parliamentary systems that can do nothing, decadent morality, the indulgence of the propertied class... thus, the current ruling structure faces a predicament, attacked from both left and right.

"Strong Power!" "Strong Power!" Isn't this a phrase that catches the eye in this generation?

We do not intend to speak here of "Strong Power" in that sense. Our social environment has weak grounds for the growth of that kind of fascist spirit. The "Strong Power" we advocate for is of a different meaning. In our daily lives, we advocate for "Strong Power." From powerlessness, indecision, and lack of courage to a life of strong power with conviction and bravery.

From a life of ease, debauchery, and servility to a life of strong power.

Let our bodies become as sturdy as reinforced concrete (鐵筋콩크리트).
Let our spirit also be as immovable as a great rock (盤石).

A life of strong power puts the interest of the whole before the interest of the individual as the first principle. A life of strong power considers life as light as a goose feather (鴻毛) when it comes to faith and righteousness (信義).

"Strong Power life"—this is the new signal for the youth of Chosŏn. (Song'a)<1>

**Summary**

The article discusses the global political trend towards "Strong Power," exemplified by the fall of Japan's Inukai Cabinet and the rise of Fascism, referencing Hitler and Hindenburg. The author observes this trend but argues that Korea lacks the conditions for such political Fascism. Instead, the author calls for a different kind of "Strong Power" among Korean youth: physical strength, mental fortitude, discipline, prioritizing collective good, and unwavering conviction, transforming from passivity and indulgence to a proactive, robust way of life. For historians, the article is interesting as it shows a Korean intellectual responding to the rise of global Fascism in the early 1930s, rejecting its political form for Korea but appropriating the language of "strength" and discipline for a nationalistic purpose focused on youth self-cultivation and collective spirit within the constraints of colonial rule. It reflects the search for agency and national revitalization amidst perceived weakness and external pressures.

기사형태	문예기타

强力
犬養內閣이 꺼꾸러젓다.
『擧國一致』 內閣이라야 된다. 『强力』 內閣이라야 된다. 重臣, 官吏, 軍部, 少壯泥, 파쑈...속일 수 없는 파쑈 행진곡이다. 元老도 마츰내 이 소리에 지고 말앗다.
히룰러가 푸로시아 內閣을 占據한다. 힌덴부륵의 앞날이 얼마나 될까.
東에도 西에도 『强力』 萬能이다. 부패한 政黨, 모순 많은 경제제도, 아모것도 할 수 없는 議會제도, 頹廢한 도덕, 有産계급의 放逸...이리하야 現支配조직은 좌우 挾攻의 難局을 당하게 된다.
『强力』! 『强力』! 이 世代에 눈이 번쩍 띠우는 글짜가 아닌가.
우리는 그러한 의미의 「强力」을 여기서 말하고저 아니한다. 우리의 사회환경은 그런 의미의 파쑈정신이 生長될 근거가 薄弱하다. 우리가 高潮하는 「强力」은 딴 의미로서다. 우리들의 일상생활에 우리는 「强力」을 高潮한다. 무기력, 無定見, 無용맹에서 신념잇고 용기잇는 强力생활로.
安逸, 放蕩, 卑㤼의 생활에서 强力의 생활로.
우리들의 육체는 鐵筋콩크리트처럼 단단하야저라.
우리들의 정신도 盤石과 같이 不動하여라.
强力생활은 個體의 이익보다도 전체의 이익을 第一義로 한다. 强力생활은 信義에 잇어서 생명을 鴻毛로 아는 것이다.
「强力생활」―이것은 朝鮮의 청년의 新信號다. (頌兒)＜1＞
