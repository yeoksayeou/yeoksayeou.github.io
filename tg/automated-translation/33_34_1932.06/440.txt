**Body Text Translation**
Reading Room

*Yŏin* (女人), by Kim Tong-in (金東仁)

Titled "An Expository Autobiography," this is a record in which the man Kim Tong-in very explicitly describes his experiences with women he encountered throughout half his life. Moreover, most of them are *kisaeng* (female entertainers).

As confessional writing, these works are too superficial and artificial, lacking depth. Also, viewed from the perspective of novelistic value, they completely disregard the elements of a "story," so they are not even worth considering.

If there are points that draw interest, firstly, it's because they are almost entirely factual, allowing real figures to appear, doubling the interest in identifying the models. Secondly, it's the occasional light sarcasm scattered here and there and the bold brushstrokes of partial psychological descriptions. Beyond that, a kind of "sex appeal," to put it harshly, is characteristic of soft literature (軟文學的), and sentimental scenes appear here and there, which merely elicit a smile.

The first half, dealing with experiences with the opposite sex during his youth, is worth seeing inasmuch as it shows sharp observation regarding psychological development. However, the latter half amounts to nothing more than a very commonplace recounting of a philanderer's path of philandering. It only depicts the pleasure-seeking mood of an ignorant young man who can spend money. There is neither the sexual exposé seen in naturalist works nor a depiction of psychological anguish.

This book will sell well. The names of once-famous *kisaeng* are written in it, and literary figures appear under their real names. Above all, Kim Tong-in's stark nakedness writhes within it. There is nothing that draws interest quite like nakedness.

However, readers seeking anything more than that will be disappointed. Why is that? Because we demand tragedy from a literary autobiography, but this work offers comedy. (Price 50 *chŏn*. Published by Samunsa [三文社], 248 Nagwŏn-dong [樂園洞], Kyŏngsŏng [京城]. Postal Transfer Kyŏngsŏng 9756)

*Collection of New Korean Children's Songs, Volume 1* (朝鮮新童謠選集 第一輯), edited by Kim Ki-ju (金基柱)

This book collects over 200 children's songs (童謠) recently created and published in Korea (朝鮮). It encompasses nearly 100 authors, famous and unknown alike, so it might as well be called a complete collection (全集) rather than a selected anthology (選集).

Since it's called Volume 1, we assume there will naturally be a Volume 2 and Volume 3. If so, this could be considered a kind of yearbook for children's songs. It wouldn't be an exaggeration to say that it has gathered works representative of all trends since the New Children's Song movement began in Korea. Rather, if one were to criticize, the fault might be that too many have been gathered. There's a feeling that the selection standard could have been narrower. One feels they should have applied a principle of strict selection (嚴選主義) based on the works themselves, regardless of whether the author was famous or unknown.

The works are generally divided by Spring, Summer, Autumn, and Winter, so this will be a good reference book for those intending to use it for educational purposes. It is also a great reference material for researchers seeking to understand the trends (推勢) in the world of children's songs. We hope for continued publication sequentially from Volume 2 onwards from the standpoint of a children's song yearbook.

As reading material for children, it is regrettable that it wasn't strictly selected as mentioned earlier, but there is no doubt that finding and reading the many hidden jewels (寶玉) within will be infinitely interesting. (Price 40 *chŏn*. Published by Tonggwang Sŏjŏm [東光 서점], 85 Kyŏngchang-ni [景昌里], P'yŏngyang [平壤]. Postal Transfer Kyŏngsŏng 4295) (Chu [朱]) <93>

**Summary**

This article presents two book reviews from 1932. The first critiques Kim Tong-in's autobiographical work *Yŏin*, finding it superficial, lacking literary merit as a novel, and relying on scandalous real-life portrayals and "sex appeal" for sales rather than genuine psychological depth or tragic insight. The second review covers Kim Ki-ju's *Collection of New Korean Children's Songs, Volume 1*, praising its comprehensiveness as a near-complete collection representing the New Children's Song movement but criticizing the lack of strict selection, suggesting it's more of a yearbook than a curated anthology suitable for children, though still valuable for educators and researchers.

For historians, the reviews offer insights into the literary tastes and critical standards of the time in colonial Korea. The critique of Kim Tong-in reveals contemporary debates about autobiography, realism, sensationalism, and the perceived moral or artistic obligations of literature. The review of the children's song collection highlights the burgeoning cultural movement focused on children's literature and education, reflecting efforts to cultivate a modern Korean identity, while also showing concerns about quality control in publishing. 讀書室
『女人』, 金東仁著
題하야 曰 「暴露的 자서전」이라. 이것은 사람 金東仁이 그 半生에 接觸한 여인과의 경험을 극히 노골적으로 記述한 기록이다. 더구나 그 대부분이 妓女다.
이 작품들은 告白文으로서는 너무 평면적이요 가식적이매 深刻味가 적다. 또 소설적 가치로 본다면 「스토리」로서의 요소를 전혀 無視하니만치 문제가 아니된다.
흥미를 끄는 点이 잇다고 하면 첫재로는 그것은 거이 十分 사실적이기 때문에 실제 인물들이 등장하여 모델 흥미를 곱을 수 잇고 둘재로는 여기 저기 散在한 가벼운 빈정거리와 부분적 심리描寫의 꿁다란 筆致라 할 것이다. 그 외에 일종 「썩스 아필」은 심하게 말하자면 軟文學的이요 또 간간이 쎈티멘탈한 장면이 나오는데 이것은 미소를 지어낼 뿐이다.
前半 즉 소년시대의 異性에 대한 경험을 그런 것은 그 심리적 發展에 대한 예리한 관찰을 보이어 주는 이만큼 볼만한 가치가 잇다. 그러나 후반에 이르러서는 오입장이의 오입 經路를 극히 평범하게 陳述한데서 지나지 안는다. 거기는 오직 돈을 쓸 수 잇는 無識청년의 道樂的 기분을 그려낸 것 밖에 없다. 자연주의 작품에서 보는 性慾的 폭로가 잇는 것도 아니요 심리적 고민을 그린 것도 아니다.
이 책은 잘 팔릴 것이다. 거기는 이름 날리든 妓生의 이름이 적혀 잇고 또 文士들이 本名대로 가지고 등장한다. 무엇보다도 金東仁의 적나라한 나체가 움즈긴다. 나체처럼 흥미끄는 물건이 없는 것이다.
그러나 그 以上 무엇을 구하는 讀者는 실망할 것이다. 웨 그러냐. 우리는 문학적 자서전에서 비극을 요구하는데 이 작품은 희극을 주기 때문이다. (定價 50錢 京城樂園洞 248번지 三文社발행 振贊京城9756番)

『朝鮮新童謠選集 第一輯』, 金基柱編
최근 朝鮮에서 창작 발표된 童謠 2百餘篇을 모아 놓은 것이 이 책이다. 作者로는 有名無名을 물론하고 近百篇을 網羅햇으니 選集이라기보다도 全集이라고 할 만하다.
第一輯이라 하엿으니 물론 第二輯 第三輯이 잇을 줄 안다. 그러면 이것은 一種의 童謠 年鑑이라고 할 것이다. 朝鮮에서 新童謠운동이 생긴 이래로 모든 경향을 대표할 만한 것을 다 모아놓앗다 해도 과언이 아닐 것이다. 도리어 비평을 하자고 하면 너무 많이 모은 것이 탈이 아닐까 한다. 조곰 더 선택의 표준이 좁앗더라면 하는 感이 없지 아니하다. 有名無名을 막론하고 작품本位로 하야 嚴選主義를 썻드라면 하는 느낌이 잇다.
작품들은 대개 春夏秋冬別로 갈라 놓앗으니 이것을 교육용으로 쓰려 하는 이에게는 좋은 참고서가 될 것이요. 또 童謠界의 推勢를 알려는 연구가에게 큰 참고거리다. 第二輯 以下로 順次 童謠年鑑的 입장에서 繼續 刊行이 잇기를 바라는 바다.
兒童讀物로서는 前記와 같이 嚴選이 아니기 때문에 유감이 잇으나 그러나 거기 숨긴 많은 寶玉을 찾아 읽는 것은 무한한 흥미가 될 것을 의심치 안는다. (定價40錢 平壤景昌里八五東光 서점발행 振贊京城四二九五) (朱) ＜93＞
