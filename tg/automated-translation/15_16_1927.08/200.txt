Article Type: 시 (Poem)

Preparing for Plowing
						Pak Aji

Ch'ŏngmyŏng is near,
Hey people,
Aren't you going to prepare for plowing?

Look after the cow feed,
Take out the seed pouch,
Let's prepare and procure the tools. - End -<61>

**Summary**

This short folk-style poem by Pak Aji encourages farmers to prepare for the upcoming plowing season as Ch'ŏngmyŏng (a traditional solar term signifying the start of farming) approaches. It lists essential tasks like feeding the cows, preparing seeds, and readying tools. From a historical perspective, the poem reflects the importance of agriculture in colonial Korea and the rhythm of rural life tied to traditional seasonal markers. It offers a glimpse into the everyday concerns and activities of ordinary people, emphasizing diligence and preparation within the agricultural cycle, potentially serving as a subtle form of encouragement during a period of external rule.
