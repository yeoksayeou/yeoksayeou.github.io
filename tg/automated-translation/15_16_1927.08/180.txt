Article Title: 北關 아씨(民謠詩) 외 2편 (Pukkwan Lass (Folk Poem) and 2 Others)

Article Type: 시 (Poem)

Pukkwan Lass
						Kim Ansŏ

Pukkwan Lass

Hey there, Pukkwan lass,
At Yŏngmyŏng Temple and Moran Hill,
Today too, the setting sun burns brightly red.

Fire catches on the western hills,
Fire catches on the eastern hills,
And in the middle of the Taedong River, the firelight glows red.

Hey there, Pukkwan lass,
In this eighteen-year-old body of mine,
The flames of a plaintive heart are burning.

Star

At the harbor at dusk,
All the songs sung
Cling heavy on the heart.

One star, one me,
Even counting on fingers,
That person has no way of coming.

The more I think,
This sorrowful heart,
Try not to cry, I cannot help but cry.

Willow Catkin

In the indifferent spring breeze,
A flower bloomed,
But vainly in that same breeze,
It withers, you see.

Isn't it sorrowful?
Young Pukkwan lass.

Today again at Nŭngna Islet,
The willow catkin
Drifts alone upon the water,
Floating away.

Isn't it pitiful?
Young Pukkwan lass.<59>

**Summary**

This publication features three short poems by Kim Ansŏ, presented in a folk song style ("民謠詩"). The poems, titled "Pukkwan Lass," "Star," and "Willow Catkin," explore themes of youthful sorrow, longing, and the transient nature of beauty. The speaker often addresses a "Pukkwan Lass" (a young woman from the northern Hamgyŏng region), connecting personal feelings of heartache and loneliness to natural imagery like sunsets over P'yŏngyang landmarks (Moran Hill, Taedong River), stars at a harbor, and willow catkins drifting on water. For historians, the poems offer insight into the lyrical sensibilities and common emotional expressions (like *han*, or sorrow/resentment) in Korean literature during the colonial period. The blending of regional references (North Hamgyŏng and P'yŏngyang) and the adoption of a folk song style might reflect contemporary interests in national identity and traditional forms amidst colonial rule.

北關 아씨
						金岸曙
北關 아씨
여봅소 北關 아씨,
永明寺 모란봉엔
오늘도 넘는 해가, 빩앟게 불이 붙소.

서산에 불이 붙고
동산에 불이 붙고
대동강 한복판에, 불빛이 붉소 그려.

여봅소 北關 아씨,
열 여듧의 내 몸엔
하소연한 심사의, 불ㅅ길이 타는구려.

별
어둡을녁 포구의
노래란 노래는
모도 다 맘에 맺힘니다.

별도 하나 나 하나
손곱아 헤어도
그 사람은 올ㅅ길 없구요.

생각에 생각쓰록
설업은 심사는
안 울랴 안 울 수 없구려.

버들개지
무심ㅎ다 봄ㅅ바람에
꽃은 피었다가
헛되히 그 바람에
시듭니다려.

설업지 않을까요.
젊으신 北關 아씨.

오늘도 능라도라
버들개지는
물우를 혼자 돌며
흘러 갑니다

가엾지 않을까요
젊으신 北關 아씨.＜59＞
＜59＞
