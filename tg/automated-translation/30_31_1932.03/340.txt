Magazine Title: 동광 제31호
Publication Date: 1932년 03월 05일
Article Title: 針大棒小 (Making Mountains out of Molehills)
Article Type: 세태비평 (Critique of the Times)

It is said that 70% of the world's population has abandoned the gold standard. Can it be said that the countries still holding onto the gold standard are truly falling behind the times? If the remaining 30% also divorce themselves from gold, will we then return to the primitive era of bartering?

Even in France, which claimed to be aloof from the economic panic everyone else was experiencing, demonstrations by the unemployed are frequent. Perhaps they are developing some sympathy for Germany, based on their own experience.

The Chosŏn Cultural Association (朝鮮文化協會) has been established in New York. It is meaningful work to properly educate ignorant white people who ask where the island of 'Korea' is.

Regarding the Songjŏng-ri Police Substation (松汀里 駐在所) torture-to-death incident, evidence was apparently discovered without torture, resulting in a sentence of less than two years imprisonment.

Inoue Junnosuke (井上準之助) has been assassinated. Assassinations of opposition party figures are rare. To say this is unrelated to the election would be a truly enormous misperception.

The market price of Manchurian millet is soaring. Yet, the polished rice (正米) of the wealthy only piles up in their granaries. This is a fine example of modern economics.

China insists on the absolute indivisibility of the Shanghai Incident and the Manchurian Incident. Does this mean they intend to create a free state in Shanghai as well?

After crushing Chang Hsüeh-liang (張學良), the Great Manchu State (大滿洲國) emerged. After rounding up all the rebellious Koreans<92> (不逞鮮人), the Minsaengdan (民生團) was born. It's the same tune played differently (同曲異調也).

Luoyang (洛陽) is the capital, Zhengzhou (鄭州) and Shanghai (上海) have National Government (國民政府) offices, and the Foreign Ministry remains in Nanking (南京). Is this a plan to confuse the world powers with a four-headed snake?

France's proposal for League of Nations armed forces is merely becoming a laughingstock among the powers. How about having the magician Briand summon straw soldiers instead?

Twenty thousand troops are concentrated in the streets of Shanghai. On top of that, refugees are flooding in from Hubei (湖北). It seems the ones profiting from the Sino-Japanese conflict are primarily the daily goods merchants within the foreign concessions (租界).

Ten thousand *koku* (石) [approx. 1.8 million liters] of Japanese sake cross the Hyŏnhae Strait (玄海灘) annually. How much of that is brewed using Korean rice (朝鮮米)?

The sardine oil (鰮油) issue continues to cause dispute. The Yŏnghŭng (永興) oyster farms are also still in turmoil. It seems they are still stubbornly attached to the Ŏjidun Water Cooperative (於之屯水組). If public opinion (民意) were respected, this issue would have been resolved already.

Regarding the Mangwŏl Irrigation Association (望月水利組合), a movement for direct management by landowners (地主) has emerged. The era of all-powerful government-appointed directors (官選理事萬能時代) must surely change someday. This applies to water cooperatives (水組) and financial cooperatives (金組) alike.

America's inflation policy. Two billion dollars is over four billion won, an enormous amount just to hear about.

The President encourages the utilization of 1.3 billion dollars of hoarded money (死藏) among the populace. Even with money, it fails to circulate; even with food, people starve to death. The disease seems to have penetrated the marrow. Can this illness truly be cured by a single broadcast from the President?

Female divers (海女) have attacked a policeman. What a gentleman blessed with amorous fortune (艶福).

They say there are 5,000 arrested, including Gandhi (깐디). The trouble is that they cannot imprison all 300 million people of India.<93>

**Summary**

This article presents a series of brief, cynical commentaries on various global and local events in early 1932. Topics range from the abandonment of the gold standard and economic woes in France and the US, to political instability in China (Shanghai Incident, Manchuria, fragmented government), the assassination of a Japanese politician, social issues in Korea (torture case, sake imports using Korean rice, fishery and irrigation cooperative disputes), and the independence movement in India. The overall tone is one of skepticism towards political leaders, economic systems, and international relations, often highlighting perceived absurdities or injustices. For historians, the piece offers a contemporary Korean perspective on major world events intertwined with colonial realities. Particularly interesting are the comments on the Manchurian situation, including the mention of rounding up "rebellious Koreans" (不逞鮮人) and the formation of the Minsaengdan, the critique of Japanese sake production using Korean rice, the disputes involving Korean cooperatives potentially clashing with colonial administration or landowner interests (Songjŏng-ri, Yŏnghŭng, Mangwŏl), and the mention of the Korean Cultural Association in New York, reflecting both global awareness and specific colonial grievances.

기사제목	針大棒小
기사형태	세태비평

針大棒小
세계인구의 7割이 金本位制를 포기햇다 한다. 그냥 金本位制를 가지고 잇는 나라는 그야말로 시대유행에 뒤떠러젓다고 할까. 남저지 3割까지 金과 離婚하면 그 다음은 물물교환의 원시로 돌아갈 것인가.

남이 다 받는 경제공황을 혼자서 초연한다든 佛國에도 실업자의 시위가 잦다. 당해 본 경험으로 獨逸에 대한 동정심이 좀 생기려만.

뉴욕에 朝鮮文化協會가 창립. 코리아란 섬이 어디 잇느냐고 묻는 무식한 백인들을 잘 교육함이 有意義.

松汀里 駐在所 拷問致死 사건은 고문 아니하고도 證據判出돼서 2년 이하 징역.

井上準之助가 암살되엇다. 野黨의 인물암살은 그 例가 드물다. 선거에 관계가 없다고 하면 그야말로 엄청난 인식착오.

滿洲 좁살 시세가 붓적든다. 그래도 부자의 正米는 곡간으로 싸여들 뿐. 이것이 현대경제의 好典型.

中國은 上海事件과 滿洲事件을 絶對 不分離主義라고. 그러면 上海에도 자유국을 만들란 말인가.

張學良을 따려부쉬고 大滿洲國이 출현. 不逞鮮人을＜92＞ 총 검거한 뒤에 民生團이 출생. 同曲異調也.

洛陽은 首都요 鄭州와 上海에는 國民政府 辦事處가 되고 外交部는 그냥 南京에 두엇다. 四頭蛇로 列國의 정신을 흘릴 게획인가.

佛蘭西의 國際聯盟 武裝案은 列國의 우슴거리만 사고 잇다. 마술사 브리앙으로 하여금 벳집 병정이나 불러냄이 如何오.

上海市街에는 2萬 大軍이 집중. 게다가 闈北으로부터 피난민의 殺到. 日中衝突에 利보는 자는 우선 租界내의 일용품 상인일 듯.

日本酒 1萬石이 1년간에 玄海灘을 건너온다. 그 중에 朝鮮米로 釀造한 것이 얼마 얼마.

鰮油問題가 그냥 분분. 永興 굴漁場도 그냥 뒤숭숭. 於之屯水組를 아직도 미련을 부치는 모양. 民意를 존중한다면 벌서 해결되엇을 문제.

望月水利組合은 地主가 직영할 운동이 대두. 官選理事萬能時代는 언제든지 바뀌어야 할 것이다. 水組가 그러고 金組가 그러하다.

米國의 인플레슌 정책. 20억불이면 40억원 이상이니 말만 드러도 엄청나다.

民間에 死藏한 돈이 13억불이나 된다고 그것 활용을 대통령이 권장한다. 돈이 잇어도 유통이 못되고 먹을 것이 잇어도 餓死者가 생기니 병은 골수에 든 모양. 대통령의 放送一片으로 과연 이 병이 나을까.

해녀들이 순사를 襲擊. 艶福많은 나으리로군.

깐디 이하 被捕者 5천이라고. 3億의 印度人을 다 가둘 수 없음이 탈.＜93＞
