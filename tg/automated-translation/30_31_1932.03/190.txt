That Lost Spring
						Kim Ansŏ

Oh God of Omnipotence and Omniscience, yesterday I lost my youth. Please, somehow find that Youth for me. I know it must surely exist somewhere on this Earth. Please, find it for me.

The boat that carried my Youth drifts along,
The waves rise and fall in ten thousand layers,
Looking out upon the wide sea of Life,
Everyone seeks their own path with their own sail.

On the hill, the grass was green, and in the fields, the flowers,
Cherishing their single season of new spring,
Danced with the wind, spreading a drifting fragrance,
My Youth, losing its senses, just wandered aimlessly.

Where has the aimlessly drifting boat come to?
Waking up, it's pitch dark; the flower petals have fallen,
In early autumn, the tree leaves forlornly play.

Having lost it, only Memories remain; alone and lonely,
I long for the spring that passed, but there's no way back,
Under the yellow sunlight, this day too just darkens into evening.<69>

**Summary**

The poem "That Lost Spring" by Kim Ansŏ expresses a deep sense of regret and longing for the speaker's lost youth. Using metaphors of a drifting boat and the changing seasons, the speaker laments the irretrievable passage of time and the aimlessness with which their youth was spent. The poem concludes on a melancholic note, emphasizing the loneliness and finality of this loss as another day ends. From a historical perspective, the poem's themes of loss, aimlessness, and regret, while personal, might resonate with the broader sense of powerlessness or lost direction felt by Koreans during the colonial period under Japanese rule. Historians might find the expression of individual sorrow and the specific linguistic forms of the 1930s of interest.

바요린니스트 安柄玿氏 ◇靑春아 봄의 노래 부르세◇

잃어진 그 봄을
						金岸曙
全能全智으로의 하느님이에 어제 제가 저의 청춘을 잃어버렷사외다. 아모조록 그 靑春을 찾아주옵소서. 이 地上 어느 곳엔지 분명이 잇을 줄 아옵니다. 부대 그것을 찾아 주옵소서.

靑春을 고이 실고 흘러가는 배
물결은 높나지의 千萬층일제,
바라보니 人生의 넓은 바다엔
모도다 제 돗대에 제 길을 찾네.

언덕엔 풀 파랏고 들엔 꽃들이
제 한 철의 새 봄을 아끼노라고
바람과 춤을 출제, 떠도는 香내,
내 靑春은 얼 잃고 잘 돌고 마네.

方向없이 흘은 배 어디를 왓노.
깨고보니 깜하다, 꽃닢은 지고
첫가을에 나무닙 쓸쓸이 노네.

잃고 나니 追憶 뿐, 혼자 외로이
지낸 봄을 그리나 올길은 없고
노랑 볓에 이 날도 해만 점으네.＜69＞
