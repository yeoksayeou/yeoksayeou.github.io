My Beloved
						Yang Chu-dong

My beloved,
My beloved, look,
My clothes are worn out like a beggar's,
Do you still love me?

For my sake,
You have starved and suffered for a long time.
Do you still love me?

Because I am a man of no account,
Many women have abandoned me,
Ah, but you alone, do you still love me?
(A Ma Femme. 10. 1926)<61>

Ten Years Ago

Thinking deeply on an autumn night,
About a love lost ten years ago,
Before I know it, tears wet both my cheeks.

If the memory of ten years ago is like this,
Ah, the heart, the tears of that day,
How hateful must it have been, how blood-mixed must it have been.

They say the path of life is so cruel,
Who knows, on an autumn night ten years from now, for whom will I weep again?

(Autumn 1926)<62>

A Passage of Prayer Offered on an Autumn Night

Please burn the pages of Memory,
Please paint over the picture of Illusion with black ink,
Lord, from the Record of this "life" full of Misprints,
Please Delete the Chapter of "past love." (1926)<63>
<61-63>

**Summary**

This document presents three poems by Yang Chu-dong published in December 1926. The poems explore themes of enduring love amidst poverty and societal rejection ("My Beloved"), the lingering pain and sorrow of past love ("Ten Years Ago"), and a desperate plea to erase painful memories ("A Passage of Prayer Offered on an Autumn Night"). The poems convey a strong sense of personal anguish, regret, and weariness with life. From a historical perspective, these poems reflect the personal struggles and emotional landscape of an intellectual during the Japanese colonial period. While not overtly political, the deep sense of loss, suffering, and the desire to erase the past could resonate with the broader context of colonial oppression and the hardships faced by Koreans during that era, offering historians insight into the individual psychological responses to the time.

사랑하는 이여
						梁柱東
사랑하는 이여
사랑하는 이여 보라,
내 옷이 해어저 거지갓노리,
그대는 오히려 나를 사랑하는가.

그대 나를 위하여,
오래ㅅ동안 굶주리고 고생하도다.
그대 오히려 나를 사랑하는가.

볼 것 업는 사내인지라,
만흔 게집이 나를 바리엇나니,
아 그대만은 오히려 나를 사랑하는가.
(A Ma Femme. 10. 1926)＜61＞

십년전
십년전 일어진 사랑을,
가을ㅅ밤에 곰곰히 생각하노라면,
어느 듯 눈물이 두뺨을 적시우나니.

십년전의 회상이 이러하거던,
아 그 날의 가슴이야 눈물이야,
얼마나 미워젓스랴, 얼마나 피석겻스랴.

인생의 길이 하도 얄굿다 하니
모르괘라, 십년 후 가을ㅅ밤엔, 나는 또 누구를 위하여 울음 울는지.

(1926 가을)＜62＞

가을밤에 올린 祈願의 一節
追憶의 페-지를 불살워 주소서,
幻影의 그림을 먹칠해 주소서,
주여 이 誤植 만흔 「삶」의 記錄에서
「지나간 사랑」의 章을 削除해 주소서. (1926)＜63＞
