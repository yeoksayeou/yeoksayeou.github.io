Magazine Title: 동광 제13호
Publication Date: 1927년 05월 01일
Article Title: 失題詩 (Untitled Poem)
Author: 餘菩出小猿
Article Type: 시 (Poem)

Even if I don't forget the departed love,
What use is thinking of them?
Once gone, they say it's like that,
So I should know that's how it is.
When my love left me,
They said they would come again,
But there's truly no way they will come,
So I shall not wait.

Still, this sorrowful heart
Cannot forget.
On long, long autumn nights (秋夜長),
When sleep will not come,
I gaze vacantly
At the moon shining in the gauze window (紗窓),
How many tears have fallen
Onto the pillow?

In the moonlight that fills the window bars,
Piercingly cold,
When the image of my love faintly
Seems to gather,
Even though I clearly know
My love cannot come,
Hoping, "Perhaps it is them?"
How many times have I looked again? <32>

**Summary**

This poem expresses the deep sorrow and lingering attachment of a speaker whose beloved has departed. The speaker oscillates between trying to accept the finality of the separation, knowing the promise of return is likely false, and being unable to forget. Sleepless nights filled with tears and illusory visions of the beloved in the moonlight highlight the persistent pain and futile hope. From a historical perspective, this poem, published in 1927 during the Japanese colonial period, captures a personal sentiment of loss and longing. While ostensibly about a departed lover, such expressions of sorrow and separation could resonate with broader societal feelings of loss – loss of sovereignty, separation from homeland, or the general melancholic atmosphere felt by intellectuals and artists under colonial rule. Historians might find it interesting as an example of personal lyricism potentially reflecting or refracting larger historical anxieties.

失題詩
						餘菩出小猿
가신님 아니 잊고
생각한덜 무엇하리
한번가면 그렇다니
그런 줄로 알을시고
님이 나를 두고 갈제
다시 오마 하더라만
정말 올듸 만무하니
기다리지 말을세라

그래도 애닯은 맘
잊어지지 못하여서
秋夜長 긴긴 밤에
잠이 아니 오량이면
紗窓에 빛인 달을
시름없이 바라보며
운눈물 벼개우에
떠러짐이 얼마련고

창살이 가득토록
사무차인 달빛 속에
님의 모습 어렴푸시
모이는 듯 하량이면
님이 오지 못한 줄을
반연히 알면서도
행여나 그인가 하여
거듭 봄이 몇번인고 ＜32＞
