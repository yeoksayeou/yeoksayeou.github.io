**Summary**

This article from the May 1927 issue of *Tonggwang* magazine lists the names of individuals and organizations who contributed five Won to the Tonggwang Society, up to April 25th of that year. The list is organized geographically by province and major city within Korea, and also includes supporters from China, Japan (Kyoto), and Hawaii. The total number of new supporters listed is 239, bringing the cumulative total to 928. For historians, this list offers valuable insight into the geographic distribution and scale of support for *Tonggwang*, a significant cultural and intellectual magazine during the colonial period. It reveals the names of specific individuals, businesses, schools, and associations across the Korean peninsula and even among the diaspora who financially supported the publication, potentially indicating networks of intellectuals, nationalists, or culturally engaged individuals.
