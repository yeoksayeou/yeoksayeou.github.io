社告 (Editorial Notice)

We sincerely apologize to our esteemed readers for making you wait quite a while, as this issue has been significantly delayed. Furthermore, we feel even more apologetic for having to suspend the serialization of "Research on Ancient Korean Culture" (朝鮮上古文化硏究) and the "Play: The Lute Story" (戱曲 琵琶記).

Although this issue marks our first anniversary since founding, the reason it doesn't appear particularly different from a regular issue is simply that we decided not to concern ourselves with special formats.

There may also be those who reproach us for a perceived lack of literary content, but knowing that in our current circumstances, it is better to advance towards scientific or academic fields rather than things that are merely pastimes, we intend to focus on practical areas as much as possible. We intend to concentrate our efforts in this direction in the future as well.

The treatises included in this issue, such as "A Study on the Rose of Sharon" (無窮花考), "A Consideration Regarding Freedom" (自由에 對한 一考), and "New Humanism" (新人間主義), are all presentations of unique research by students overseas who have dedicated many years solely to academic study; therefore, we believe they will serve as valuable research materials.

We hope that our esteemed readers will continue to provide much support in the future.

**Summary**

This editorial notice from *Tonggwang* magazine apologizes to readers for the delayed publication of its first-anniversary issue and the suspension of two serialized features ("Research on Ancient Korean Culture" and "Play: The Lute Story"). The editors state they intentionally avoided a special format for the anniversary, choosing instead to shift focus away from literary content, deemed a mere "pastime," towards more practical scientific and academic articles. They highlight three such articles in the current issue written by students studying overseas and express their intention to continue prioritizing academic content. For historians, this notice reveals the magazine's editorial direction in 1927 colonial Korea, emphasizing a move towards practical knowledge and academic research, possibly reflecting broader intellectual trends or perceived societal needs under colonial rule. The explicit downplaying of literature in favor of academic/scientific pursuits is particularly noteworthy.

社告
이번호는 퍽 늦게 되어 愛讀諸氏로 한여곰 많이 기다리게 한 것은 매우 죄송함니다. 또 게다가 종래 연재하던 「朝鮮上古文化硏究」와 「戱曲 琵琶記」를 휴게하게 되어 더욱 미안한 바외다.
이번호는 창간 1주년기념이라면서 普通號보다 별로 다른 점이 보이지 않는 것은 다만 우리는 別般 형식이라는 것을 돌아보지 않기로 한 것이기 때문임니다.
또 문예가 좀 부족하다고 責하실 이도 게시리다마는 우리의 오늘 처지로서는 잠시 소일꺼리에 불과한 그것보다 더욱 과학이나 학술방면으로 나아가는 편이 낫다고 앎으로 가급적 실제방면에 힘쓰려하는 것임니다. 장래에도 이 방면으로 주력하고저 함니다.
이번호에 실린 「無窮花考」와 「自由에 對한 一考」와 「新人間主義」 같은 논문은 다 해외에서 다년 학술연구에 전력ㅎ는 학도의 독특한 연구을 발표한 것이오니 크게 연구의 자료가 되리라고 믿습니다.
애독제씨로서 장래에도 많은 성원을 주시기를 바람니다.
