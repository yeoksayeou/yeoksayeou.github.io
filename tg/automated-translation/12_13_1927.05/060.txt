Knowledge Store

How to Revive a Dead Person

When a person dies, don't they stop breathing and moving, becoming just like wood or stone? Recently, a scientist in Russia says that even a dead person's heart can be made to beat, bringing them back to life.

It is called the electrical transport method (전긔수송법), which involves putting blood into the heart. When this was tested on a dead dog, its eyes opened wide and its ears pricked up, making it active just like when it was alive. Isn't that truly amazing?

How to Catch Ducks Barehanded

If someone says they can catch ducks playing in the river barehanded, wouldn't everyone think it impossible? However, people in Mexico use a clever method to catch them enjoyably.

The method is quite easy. Prepare about two boxes, like kerosene crates, and drill a round hole in one side of each. Put grain that ducks like into one box and float it on the water. A person covers themselves with the other box, immerses their body in the water, and quietly waits for the ducks to come. As they approach to eat the grain, the person grabs them with both hands. It's said they catch about fifty ducks a day this way. What a curious method this is! <25>

**Summary**

This short article from 1927 presents two pieces of trivia: a report about a Russian scientist supposedly able to revive the dead (tested on a dog) using an "electrical transport method" for blood, and a description of a Mexican technique for catching ducks barehanded using strategically placed boxes, one with bait and one for concealment. For historians, the article reflects the type of popular science and world trivia being disseminated in colonial Korea, showing interest in modern scientific advancements (even sensational ones) alongside curiosity about foreign customs or practical tricks. The language itself, with its older spellings and grammatical forms, is also of linguistic interest.

죽은 사람 살리는 법
사람이 한번 죽으면 호흡과 운동을 정지하여 마치 나무나 돌과 같이 되지 아니함니까. 근일 러시아에 한 과학자는 죽은 사람이라도 심장이 뛰게 하며 다시 살릴 수 있다고 함니다.
그것은 심장에 혈액을 넣어 주는 전긔수송법(電氣輸送法)이라는데 이를 죽은 개에게다 실험하여 본즉 눈을 뻔히 뜨고 귀를 쫑긋거리어 꼭 살았을 때나 다름없이 활똥하였담니다. 참 신긔하지 않슴니까.

맨손으로 물오리 잡는 법
강물에 노는 물오리를 손에 아무것도 가진 것 없이 산채로 잡는다 하면 누구나 다 되지 않을 것으로 생각하겠지오? 그러나 맥시코 사람들은 교묘한 방법을 쓰어서 재미있게 그를 잠는담니다.
그 방법인즉 퍽 쉽슴니다. 석유상자 같은 궤짝을 두엇쯤 예비하여 각기 한쪽에 둥글언 구멍을 뚫고 한 개에는 물오리가 잘먹는 나락을 넣어서 물에 띄우고 한 개에는 사람이 뒤집어 쓰고 몸을 물에 담근 채 가만히 물오리 오기를 기다리고 있어서 그들이 나락 먹으러 오는 쪽쪽 두 손으로 웅키어 잡슴니다. 이렇게 잡는 것이 매일 오십마리씩은 된담니다. 이 얼마나 야릇한 법임니까. ＜25＞
