Magazine Title: 동광 제38호
Publication Date: 1932년 10월 01일
Article Title: 汽車는 구을고 잇나니 (The Train Rolls On)
Author: 盧再憲
Article Type: 시 (Poem)

The train rolls on
Through wide fields of red, yellow, and blue light.

Like an old man following his young wife's bier,
Huffing and puffing, it climbed the mountain bend,
Now like an angry bull, tail erect, it dashes forth.
Unimpeded across the wide fields, over the mountain, as if to pierce the sky—

Comrades!
Seeing the rushing train and the billowing smoke,
Your panting breaths and flowing bloody sweat.
In that suffocating pit (堀坑中) where even the sky cannot be seen,
It clearly appears before my eyes even as I cross over it,
I want to smash the speeding train.

The train rolls on
Towards the lion's den-like mine, the coal mine (炭坑),
So that swollen heels harden once again.
Today too, in leggings and straw shoes (감발집신), 200 ri a day.
Comrades whose bodies are weary and lack travel money
Are countless,
Yet the enemy train properly rolls on.<70>

**Summary**

This poem by No Chae-hŏn depicts a train powerfully traversing the landscape, contrasting its relentless movement with the suffering of laborers ("comrades"). The train, initially climbing slowly, now rushes like an "angry bull," symbolizing perhaps industrial progress or the force driving laborers. The poet expresses empathy for the workers, imagining their "bloody sweat" in the "suffocating pit" of the mines, and feels an urge to destroy the train taking them there. It highlights the exhaustion of workers traveling long distances ("200 ri a day") in inadequate footwear towards harsh mine labor, while the "enemy train" continues indifferently. From a historical perspective, the poem offers a poignant glimpse into the harsh realities faced by Korean laborers during the Japanese colonial period, likely referencing the mobilization of workers for mining industries. The depiction of the train as an "enemy" underscores the resentment towards forces facilitating exploitation and hardship.

汽車는 구을고 잇나니
						盧再憲
기차는 구을고 잇나니
넓은 들 붉고 누르고 푸르런 빛을 뚫고서.

젊은 안해의 상여를 따르는 늙은이 같이
허득허득 산굽이를 추어 오르드니
이제는 성난 황소 같이 꼬리 벋고 내닫네.
넓은 들을 거침없이 산 넘어 하늘을 껴뚜를 듯이―

동무여!
닷는 기차와 뿜는 연기를 보니
그대들의 가쁜 숨결과 흐르는 피ㅅ땀.
하늘도 못 보는 그 질식할 堀坑中
넘어도 눈 앞에 또렷이 나타나
내닫는 기차를 부시고 싶다.

기차는 구을고 잇나니
부풀은 발굼치가 다시 굳어지도록
사자굴 같은 광산, 炭坑을 향하여
오늘도 감발집신에 하로 200리
몸은 피곤하고 노자 없는 동무는
수없시 많은데
원수의 기차는 제대로 구을고 잇고나.＜70＞
