Price of the British Emperor's Crown (英皇의 冠價)
The crown used when the Emperor of Great Britain ascends the throne is entirely adorned with diamonds. Among the ten large ones, the two in the center are the most valuable, each worth 20,000 Won. The next most valuable are worth 15,000 Won each. Therefore, calculating the value of all the diamonds there, it is said that even at a wholesale price (歇갑스), it would exceed 1,000,000 Won.

Amount of Salt in the Sea (海中의 鹽量)
How much salt is there in total in the seawater of the entire Earth? If the salt content is 2.7 percent of the seawater, 104.35 million tons of salt can be obtained from one square *ri* (平方里) of seawater. Furthermore, if the area of the Earth's oceans is considered to be 435 million square *ri*, the total amount of salt contained therein is said to be 45 *gyeong* (敬) 3,755 trillion 500 billion tons.

America's Great Book (美國의 大書籍)
The United States government compiled a history of the American Civil War, with the total cost amounting to 5,600,000 Won. The number of volumes reached 110 books, and it was compiled over a long period of ten years. It is said that it would take more than ten years to read through these books once.

Sunlight Firing Cannon (日光 發射砲)
Nowadays, a cannon that fires using sunlight has emerged, and it is said to be best used for the noon gun. It has been installed (備買하엿는데) on a 2,000-*ch'ŏk* (尺) high mountain in the Sintra (新도라) region near the capital of Portugal. The firing method involves the sun's rays gradually reaching their zenith at noon; as the light beam concentrates at the focal point of a receiving mirror (受光鏡), it automatically contacts a fuse (導火線), causing the cannon to fire.

Monument to Animals' Patriotism (동물의 愛國碑)
The Royal Society in London, England, is said to have erected a memorial monument for the warhorses (戰馬), carrier pigeons (傳信鴒), and scout dogs (偵探犬) that were active and died on the battlefields during the Great European War years ago. It is said they did this believing that the animals sacrificing their bodies for the nation is a contribution no less than that of humans.

Life-Saving Device Kills (活人器가 殺人)
Although the X-rays <40> invented 30 years ago by the German Dr. Röntgen (먼도젠 博士) and the "Radium" (「라듸움」) discovered 26 years ago by the French Mr. and Mrs. Curie (께리씨 夫婦) have made great contributions to saving human lives, they also become things that harm human life. While X-rays and Radium kill toxic germs (毒菌) within the human body (人体), they also destroy the tissue (組織) of human cells (細胞). It is said that the number of scholars who sacrificed their own lives after using these two inventions for a long time has reached over 140 people.

Money Floating in the Air (空中에 뜬 金錢)
According to statistics from the US Post Office (郵便局) for the last year, the number of letters that could not be delivered (分傳치 못한) due to unknown addresses of both sender and receiver reached 21 million envelopes (封). Among them, it is said there were money orders (送金票) for currency (通貨) worth over 3,000,000 Won.

Rights and Money (權利와 金錢)
A famous racecourse (跑馬場) owner in England named Henry Waterson (헨리 화타슨) filed a lawsuit demanding compensation for damages (損害賠償 請求訴訟) against a railway company, claiming they had damaged (破損) his leather trunk (皮箱) during travel. He lost the first trial, appealed again, and finally received compensation of 56 *jŏn* (錢). However, the new litigation costs incurred to receive this 56 *jŏn* amounted to 38,000 Won. For him, respecting his rights had a value beyond money.

America's Cosmetics Expenses (美國의 化裝費)
In New York City (紐育市), one of the world's largest cities (城市), women compete with each other to apply makeup well. Therefore, *[unclear word, possibly 機者 - entrepreneurs/opportunists]* seeking to make money set up so-called "Beauty Parlors/Cosmetology Clinics" (美容術化裝所) and conducted business by grooming faces, applying powder (粉 바르고), and putting on rouge (臙脂짓는 것) to suit women's tastes. According to figures released by the New York Statistics Bureau, in the year before last (1925), the total number of women who visited "Beauty Parlors/Cosmetology Clinics" in New York City alone was 400,000, and the income generated from this was 400 million US dollars. This means that the average annual cosmetic expense (化粧費) per woman was 1,000 dollars (equivalent to 2,000 Won in East Asian currency - 東洋貨).<41>

**Summary**

This 1927 article from the Korean magazine *Tonggwang* presents a collection of miscellaneous news briefs from around the world. Topics include the value of the British crown, the amount of salt in the oceans, a massive US publication on the Civil War, a solar-powered cannon in Portugal, a London monument for war animals, the dual life-saving and life-taking nature of X-rays and Radium, undelivered mail containing money in the US, an expensive lawsuit over a small claim in the UK, and the high cost of cosmetics for women in New York City. From a historical perspective, the article reveals the types of international news and trivia deemed interesting to a Korean readership during the colonial period. The mention of currency conversion (US dollars to "East Asian currency" - likely Japanese Yen/Korean Won) and the selection of topics focusing on Western modernity, science, wealth, and peculiarities offer insight into the cultural and economic reference points of the time.

英皇의 冠價
英皇의 冠價 英國 皇帝가 卽位할 때에 쓰는 冠冕은 전부 金剛石으로 꾸미엇는데 其 중 큰 것 10개 중에 중앙에 잇는 두 개가 제일 갑만흔 것인데 그것은 한 개에 20,000圓 짜리가 되고 그 다음으로는 15,000圓 짜리가 됨으로 거긔 잇는 金剛石 가치를 다 계산하면 歇갑스로도 100萬圓 이상이 되리라 함니다.

海中의 鹽量 全 地球에 잇는 海水 중에 鹽分이 모도 얼마나 될가? 鹽分이 海水의 100分의 2.7이라 하면 1平方里 海水 중에 1억 435만 頓의 鹽을 어들 수 잇고 또 이 地球上 海洋의 面積을 4억 3,500만 平里라 하면 거긔 잇는 鹽量이 모도 45敬 3,755兆 5,000億 噸이 되리라 함니다.

美國의 大書籍 美國 政府에서 南北戰爭史를 編纂하엿는데 그 費用이 모도 560萬圓이라 하며 卷數는 110책이나 되고 10년 동안에나 되는 긴 세월을 두고 編纂하엿는데 이 책들은 한 번 내려 보려면 10여 년만에야 마치리라 함니다.

日光 發射砲 지금은 日光을 應用하여 發射하는 大砲가 생기엇는데 이것을 午砲에 사용하는 것이 제일 조타 함니다. 葡萄牙 首附 부근 新도라 지방의 2,000尺 高山에 備買하엿는데 그 發射하는 法은 太陽 光經이 점점 正午에 이르면 그 光線이 受光鏡 焦点에 집중되어 이것이 절로 導火線에 接觸되면 大砲가 發射된다 함니다.

동물의 愛國碑 英國 倫敦에 잇는 王立協會에서는 年前 歐州 大戰亂 時에 戰場에서 活動하다가 죽은 戰馬와 傳信鴒과 偵探犬들을 위하여 紀念碑를 세웟다는데 동물들도 國家을 위하여 제 몸을 犧牲한 것은 그 功이 사람만 못하지 안타 하며 그 가티 하엇다 함니다.

活人器가 殺人 30년 前 德國 먼도젠 博士가 발명한＜40＞ X光線과 26년 前 法國 께리씨 夫婦가 발명한 「라듸움」으로 人命을 救하는데 그 功이 위대하건마는 이것이 또 人命을 害하는 물건도 됨니다. X光線이 「라듸움」이 人体 內에 잇는 毒菌을 죽이는 그 대신에 또한 人體細胞의 組織을 破壞함으로 이 두 가지 發明物을 오래동안 사용하다가 자기의 생명을 犧性한 學者가 140人 이상에 達하엿다 함니다.

空中에 뜬 金錢 작년 1년 동안 美國 郵便局 統計에 의하면 發信人과 受信人의 住所不明으로 分傳치 못한 書信이 2,100萬 封에 達하는데 그 중에는 通貨 300萬圓 이상의 送金票가 잇다 함니다.

權利와 金錢 英國에 유명한 跑馬場 主人 「헨리 화타슨」이란 이는 여행할 때에 鐵道會社에서 자기의 皮箱을 破損하엿다 하여 그 鐵道會社를 걸어 損害賠償 請求訴訟을 提起하여 1審에서는 지고 다시 上訴하여 마침내 56錢의 賠償을 바닷는데 이 56錢을 밧노라고 消費한 新訴訟 費用은 38,000圓이 들엇다 함니다. 그는 자기의 權利를 尊重히 함에 金錢에 다 할 수 업슴이 잇슴니다.

美國의 化裝費 世界의 최대 城市 중의 1인 美國 紐育市에 사는 여자들은 化裝을 서로 다투어 가며 잘 하려 하는 고로 *機者들이 돈 벌 궁리를 하여 가지고 所謂 「美容術化裝所」를 내고 여자들의 비위에 맛게 얼굴을 닥달하고 粉 바르고 臙脂짓는 것으로 營業을 하엿는데 紐育 統計處에서 發布한 것을 보면 再昨年(1925) 1년 동안에 紐育市에서만 「美容術化裝所에 드나든 여자가 모도 40만 人이요 이에 收入된 金額은 美貨로 4億萬圓이라 함니다. 그러면 여자 한 사람의 1년 동안 消費한 化粧費가 평균 1,000圓(東洋貨로는 2,000圓)식이 되엇음니다.＜41＞
