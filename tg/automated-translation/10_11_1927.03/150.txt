Magazine Title:	동광 제11호
Publication Date:	1927년 03월 05일
Article Title:	同志여! (Comrade!)
Author:	鄭昞淳
Article Type:	시 (Poem)

Comrade!
						Chŏng Pyŏng-sun
Comrade!
For years
I searched for you.

Through the rough and empty fields
As I wandered alone
Hoping, “Perhaps here?”
How many times did I walk in vain?
Asking, “Are you the one?”
How hoarsely I cried out?

Now I have found you.
But my body is already moldy.
Comrade!
Receive at least my will.
January 22, 1927
To the Rural Village Friends (農村會友)

Happiness

Just follow, it called,
Happiness beckoned.
A long road, an unknowable road,
How far I walked.

Crossing over one pass,
I looked behind me.
Ash-colored smoke drifts hazily,
A bygone childish dream!
Even in there, happiness calls.
192[7?]. February, New Year’s Eve (除夕)

Reminiscence

Meeting on the street,
You lowered your head.
Meeting in the room,
You hid in the kitchen.
You, back then,
With your braided hair let down,
Somehow, I miss you.
August 10, 1925 <51>

**Summary**

This document presents three poems by Chŏng Pyŏng-sun published in 1927. The first, "Comrade!", expresses a long and difficult search for comrades, culminating in finding them but feeling worn out, dedicated to "rural village friends." The second, "Happiness," reflects on the elusive pursuit of happiness, looking back at the past while still being called forward. The third, "Reminiscence," evokes nostalgia for a shy person encountered in the past. From a historical perspective, the poems, particularly "Comrade!", offer insight into the feelings of isolation and the yearning for solidarity potentially experienced by intellectuals or activists involved in social or nationalist movements in rural areas during the colonial period. The dedication "To the Rural Village Friends" might hint at connections to rural enlightenment or cooperative movements common at the time.

同志여!
						鄭昞淳
同志여!
해를 두고
내 그대들을 찾았노라.

거츨고 뷔인 들을
내 홀로 헤매일 때
행여나 여긘가? 하고
몇 번이나 헛걸음 걸었으며
그대가 그대이더냐고
내 얼마나 목메어 웨첬으랴

내 인저 그대들을 찾았노라
그러나 내 몸이 벌서 곰팡났나니
同志여!
뜻이나마 받으소서
1927. 1. 22日
農村會友에게

幸福
딸아만 오라고
幸福이 부르기로
멀ㄴ 길 알지 못할 길을
나는 얼마나 걸었다네.

한 고개 넘어갈 때
내 뒤를 바라보니
잿빛 烟氣가 아득이는
지나간 얼인 꿈!
그 속에서도 幸福이 부르네
192. 2 除夕

追憶
길에서 만나면
고개를 숙이고
방에서 만나면
부억에 숨던
머리 땋아 늘인
그 때엣 당신이
웨 그런지 그립슴니다.
1925. 8월 10일＜51＞
