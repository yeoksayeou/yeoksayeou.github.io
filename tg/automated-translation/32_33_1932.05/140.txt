Urgent Telegram
World War Prediction?
(Q). German General Ludendorff predicted that a World War would break out on May 1, 1932. What is the reason for this? (Yu Hyŏng)
(A). According to General Ludendorff, a scientific analysis of the existing military armaments of European nations indicates that war would break out on May 1, 1932. The basis for specifying this date, according to General Ludendorff's opinion, is that Jews hold great power in various European countries, and in the secret spells of Judaism, the sacred number promising fortune is "15". Therefore, the time when various organizations in which Jews hold worldwide influence can achieve victory is the year when the sum of its digits equals 15, that is (1+9+3+2=15) 1932. The recent Great European War also adds up to 15 (1+9+1+4=15). Furthermore, May 1st is considered most likely because it is a worldwide Jewish festival day. (One Reporter)<33>

To Mr. Kim Tongin
(Q). How far behind is the Chosŏn literary world compared to the world literary scene? (Anju . Yi Hanmin)
(A). I think the ratio between world culture and Chosŏn culture can be applied here as well. (Kim Tongin)

Beekeeping Master
(Q). I intend to start beekeeping soon. Please provide the name and address of someone knowledgeable in both the theory and practice of beekeeping, whom a beginner could consider a master beekeeper (養蜂大家). (Yi Sihwa)
(A). If you inquire with Mr. Sŏng Chubok (成周復), who operates Samsan Mirwŏn (三山密園) at 174 Sŏngbuk-ri (城北里), outside Tongsomun Gate (東小門), Kyŏngsŏng (京城), you can learn everything about beekeeping.

Music Teacher
(Q). I want to learn the violin ("payoring") and piano ("p'iano"). Are there places that teach them in Seoul (Sŏul)? (A Female Reader in Kyŏngsŏng)
(A). Ms. Kim Chŏngsun (金楨洵), a graduate of Tokyo Musashino Music School (東京 武藏野音樂學校), has organized the Taedong Music Association (大東音樂協會) at 143 Kwansu-dong (觀水洞), Kyŏngsŏng-bu (京城府) and is currently teaching. Please inquire there for details. (One Reporter)＜35＞

**Summary**

This article from the May 1932 issue of *Tonggwang* magazine features a Q&A format addressing diverse reader inquiries. Topics include a prediction of World War II based on General Ludendorff's interpretation of Jewish numerology, detailed listings of the famous "Eight Views" scenic locations in Korea (Kwandong, Kwansŏ, Tanyang, Hansan) and China (Hsiaohsiang), stereotypical characterizations of people from Korea's eight provinces attributed to Ch'a Sangch'an, a question to prominent writer Kim Tongin about the status of Korean literature, and practical advice on finding experts for beekeeping and Western music (violin and piano) instruction in Kyŏngsŏng (Seoul).

For historians, the article is interesting for several reasons. It reflects the circulation of international news and even anti-Semitic conspiracy theories (Ludendorff's prediction) within colonial Korea. The provincial stereotypes offer a glimpse into contemporary social perceptions. Furthermore, it documents the growing interest in Western cultural pursuits like music and provides specific names and locations related to literature (Kim Tongin), traditional culture (Eight Views), and modern services (beekeeping, music schools) in 1930s Kyŏngsŏng.

至急電報
世界大戰 預言?
(問). 獨逸 루덴돌프將軍의 豫言에 1932年 5月 1日에는 世界大戰이 勃興되리라 하니 그 理由如何? (柳亨)
(答). 루덴돌프將軍의 말에 의하면 歐洲 諸國의 現存한 軍備를 科學的으로 分析하면 1932年 5月 1日에 戰爭이 나리라 하는 것입니다. 그 날자까지 밝힌 根據는 루將軍의 의견에 의하면 歐洲 各國에 큰 勢力을 가지고 잇는 것이 猶太人인데 猶太敎의 秘呪에 幸福을 約束하는 神聖한 數字가 「15」라고 합니다. 그래서 猶太人이 世界的으로 勢力을 가지고 잇는 각 團體가 勝利를 얻을 수 잇는 때는 各個의 數의 合이 15가 되는 해 즉 (1+9+3+2=15) 1932年이라고 하는 것입니다. 요전 歐洲大戰도 (1+9+1+4=15) 合하면 15가 됩니다. 그러고 猶太人의 世界的 祭日인 관계로 5月 1日이 가장 有望하다고 하는 것입니다. (一記者)＜33＞
