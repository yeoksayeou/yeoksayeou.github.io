Overseas Review

Early and Late Marriages of Londoners
Among the 4.5 million people in London, the capital of England, those who married early include 2 brides aged 14, 55 aged 15, 448 aged 16, and 2,499 aged 17. For grooms, the youngest was 15, with 21 aged 16 and 167 aged 17. The national average marriage age in England is 25 for men and 24 for women.
The record for the oldest late marriage is 90 years old. It is said that a surprising 76 people married after turning 80. These were men, but there were two women who married after turning 80. Furthermore, there was reportedly one instance of a 50-year-old woman marrying a 24-year-old man.

Book Publishing in Soviet Russia
Russia's book production reached 500 million copies in 1930, compared to 120 million copies before the war. It is projected to reach 625 million copies this year, an increase of 24 percent compared to last year.

130,000 Nurseries
The People's Commissariat of the Russian Republic has decided to increase facilities for small children following the advancement of female laborers into the industrial sector. That is, the number of nurseries, which was 50,980 in 1930, has increased to 130,000 this year. This means that 41 percent of the children of female industrial laborers will be raised in nurseries.

Organization of Writers' Defense Committee
Prominent American writers, artists, and other intellectuals, spurred to action by racial and political persecution, have reportedly organized a National Committee for the Defense of Political Prisoners. Its chairman is Theodore Dreiser. The committee's first challenge was directed at the Governor of Alabama, demanding the release of Black youths sentenced to the electric chair. The electric chair execution is a barbaric murder intended to prevent the revolutionary organization of the Black masses living in slave-like conditions in the United States. The death sentences for eight of the nine Black youths in Scottsboro, Alabama, were averted due to the cooperative protest and indignation of the international laboring masses.

Miss Fredson Awaits the Hero, Mr. Moyle

Miss Frances Fredson, the fiancée of Mr. Don Moyle, reportedly chartered an airplane and departed from Riverside City airfield, rushing to Seattle to meet Mr. Moyle. However, the K'ŭrasina Match'i-ho (크라시나 맛치號) carrying Messrs. Moyle and Allen, which embarked on the great undertaking of a non-stop Pacific crossing starting from Hamayori (淋代), Japan at 5:28 AM on September 8th, has still not shown its airframe at its destination, Seattle, or anywhere else, even after 49 hours have passed since leaving Hamayori. Did they fall and die? An emergency landing on a deserted island? Oh, devilish Pacific Ocean, return the young lady's sweetheart, the hero Moyle. <48>

**Summary**

This article presents several international news briefs from late 1931. It covers social statistics about marriage ages in London, the significant increase in Soviet book production and nursery facilities (highlighting the role of women in industry), the formation of a committee by American intellectuals led by Theodore Dreiser to defend political prisoners (specifically mentioning the Scottsboro Boys case and condemning racial injustice in the US), and the ongoing uncertainty surrounding the fate of aviators Don Moyle and Cecil Allen during their attempted trans-Pacific flight from Japan to Seattle, awaited anxiously by Moyle's fiancée. From a historical perspective, this collection reveals the scope of international news reaching Korean readers during the colonial period, reflecting interest in Western social trends, Soviet developments (presented somewhat neutrally or positively regarding industrial/social progress), awareness of racial and political issues in the US, and fascination with modern technological feats like aviation and their inherent dangers. The inclusion of these varied global reports in a Korean publication under Japanese rule highlights an outward-looking perspective amidst colonial constraints.

해외레뷰
倫敦人의 早婚과 晩婚
英京 倫敦人 450만 중에 早婚한 자로는 新婦 14세가 2人, 15세가 55人, 16세가 448人, 17세가 2,499人. 新郞은 제일 어린 것이 15세, 16세는 21人 17세가 167人, 영국의 전국 평균 결혼 연령은 남자가 25세, 여자가 24세.
晩婚者의 최고 기록은 90세. 80세 이상이 되어서 결혼한 사람이 무려 76人이라고 한다. 그것은 남자이지마는 여자는 80세 이상이 되어서 결혼한 사람이 두 사람. 그런데 50세 된 여자가 24세 된 남편을 가진 일이 한번 잇엇다고.

쏘벳트露西亞의 서적 출판
러시아의 서적 생산은 戰前의 1억 2천만 부에 비하야 30년도에는 5억 부에 달하얏다. 그러고 금년은 6억 2,500만 부에 달하리라고 하니 작년에 비하야 2割4分의 증가다.

탁아소 13만
러시아 공화국 人民委員部는 부인노동자의 공업 부문에의 진출을 따라서 소아용 시설의 증가를 결정하엿다. 즉 1930년 5만 9천 80이든 탁아소는 금년도에 이르러서는 13만으로 증가하엿다. 이것으로 보면 공업부인노동자의 소아의 41퍼센트는 탁아소에서 양육하게 되는 것이다.

작가의 防衛委員會 조직
米國의 저명한 작가, 예술가, 기타 지식층은 인종적, 정치적 迫害에 奮起하야 정치범 防衛를 목적으로 國民委員會를 조직하엿다고 한다. 그 委員長은 레오돌·드라이자. 이 委員會의 최초의 도전은 알라바마 知事로 향하엿는데 그것은 電氣死刑을 선언한 흑인 청년의 해방을 요구한 것이다. 電氣死刑이라 하는 것은 合衆國에서 奴隷的생활을 하는 흑인 대중의 혁명적 조직을 방지하기 위한 野蠻的 殺人이다. 알라바마洲 「스코츠버로」의 흑인 청년 9인중 8명의 사형은 국제적 노동 대중의 협동적 항의와 憤激에 의하야 구출되엇다.

勇士 모일君을 기다리는 부孃

돈·모일君의 약혼자 푸란시스· 푸렛손孃은 돈君을 마중하기 위하야 비행기 1대를 대절하고 「리버사이드市」 비행장을 출발하야 「씨아틀」로 급행햇다고 한다. 그런데 9월 8일 오전 5시 28분 「日本 淋代」를 출발점으로 하야 太平洋 無着 횡단의 壯途에 오른 모일, 아렌 兩氏의 크라시나 맛치號는 淋代를 떠난 지 이미 49시간을 경과하야서도 그 機体를 목적지 씨아틀에는 물론 아무데도 나타내지 않는다 한다. 떠러저 죽엇는가 孤島의 불시착인가. 魔의 태평양아 부孃의 애인, 용사 모일을 돌려보내라. ＜48＞
