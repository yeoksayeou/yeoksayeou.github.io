Author of Tragedy
						By I Ŭng-su

O Chosŏn! All the plays you create
Always conclude with a miserable result, a "sorrow-end."
While America's are renowned for their "happy-ends,"
Yours always begin and end as "sorrow-ends."

O Chosŏn! You are an author of tragedy.
You have grown old in that profession for three or four thousand years,
And now you are creating an even greater masterpiece,
You are a sorrowful author, full of sentiment and melancholy (憂爵).

O Chosŏn! Then the tens of millions of actors who have appeared only in tragedies for 4,000 years
Are now exhausted by fatigue and weariness.
Now I beseech you, please try creating comedies sometimes.
How much we yearn for them!

O Chosŏn! Take pity on these gentle actors of yours,
I beseech you, please create comedies now.
Really, where is there such a narrow-minded elder (어룬)
Who just keeps writing only tragedies for hundreds of millions of years? <93>

**Summary**

This poem personifies Korea (Chosŏn) as a playwright who has only ever created tragedies ("sorrow-ends") throughout its long history (symbolized as 3-4 thousand years). The speaker, representing the Korean people ("actors"), expresses profound exhaustion and weariness from constantly living through these tragedies. They earnestly plead with Chosŏn to break this pattern and create comedies ("happy-ends") instead, criticizing the persistent focus on sorrow and suffering. For historians, the poem is a poignant expression of the national despair and fatigue felt under Japanese colonial rule. The personification of Korea itself as the source of tragedy, albeit one the people wish would change, reflects a complex internalisation of suffering, while the plea for "comedy" powerfully symbolizes the deep yearning for liberation, joy, and a future free from oppression, implicitly condemning the colonial reality.

悲劇의 作者
						李應洙
조선이시여! 당신의 창작하시는 모든 연극은
언제나 비참한 결과 『쏘로엔드』로 끝맺엇나이다
『아메리카』 彼女의 것이 『햅피엔드』로 이름높거니와
당신의 것은 언제나 『쏘로엔드』로 始終하엿나이다.

조선이시여! 당신은 비극의 작자입니다.
3,4천년을 그 직업으로 늙어 왓고
지금엔 또 더 큰 대작을 창작하고 게신
감상과 憂爵에찬 비창의 작자입니다.

조선이시여! 그럼 4천년을 비극만에 출연하여온
수천만의 배우는 이제 피로과 권태에 지첫나이다.
이제 비옵나니 당신도 더러 희극을 나어보시구려
우리는 얼마나 그것을 열망하고 잇사오리까.

조선이시여! 당신의 이 유순한 배우들을 불상이 녁이시여
비옵나니 인제는 희극을 나어주소서
글세 억만년을 두고 그저 비극만을 지으시는
그러한 편협한 어룬이 어데잇단 말입니까.＜93＞
