Overseas Review

Writers' Activities
America's left-wing writers - Cruden, Pasos, Waldo Frank, Gibson, Michael Gold, etc. - are reportedly conducting large-scale fundraising to provide relief for the fighters who have been sacrificed to the class struggle and are in prison, and for their families. Their goal is to provide a subsidy of 10 Won per month to each person in prison and 40 Won per month to their dependent families. It is said that currently, about 90 militant workers in prison will receive this relief.

Writers' Total Mobilization
The International Union of Revolutionary Writers in Moscow reportedly requested anti-imperialist war materials from all American writers and artists through the "John Reed Club" in America. These materials refer to sketches, short stories, poems, and paintings concerning the preparations by the Great Powers for an imperialist war of plunder (帝國主義 略奪展), the working class's anti-war struggle against it, and various other problems related to war. American left-wing writers were reportedly in a state of total mobilization leading up to the Anti-Imperialist War Day on August 1st. <12>

**Summary**

This news brief reports on the activities of American left-wing writers in 1931. It highlights their efforts to raise funds for imprisoned activists ("fighters") and their families affected by the "class struggle." It also notes that the International Union of Revolutionary Writers, based in Moscow, requested materials (stories, poems, art) from American writers via the John Reed Club to document preparations for an "imperialist war of plunder" by the Great Powers and the working class's anti-war resistance, mentioning a "total mobilization" for Anti-Imperialist War Day. For historians studying colonial Korea, this article is interesting as it shows Korean awareness (via the magazine *Dong-gwang*) of international leftist and anti-imperialist movements. It reflects the global discourse on class struggle and opposition to imperialism, which would have resonated strongly within a colonized nation like Korea, potentially informing or inspiring local intellectuals and activists opposing Japanese rule.

海外 레뷰
作家의 活動
米國의 左翼作家- 크루덴, 파소스, 왈도·푸랜크, 깁손, 마이켈·골드 등등은 階級鬪爭에 희생을 당하야 獄中에 잇는 鬪士와 그들의 가족을 구원하기 위하야 대대적으로 자금을 募集 중이라고 한다. 그들의 목표는 獄中에 잇는 한사람 앞에 매월 10원, 그에게 달린 가족에게 매월 40원을 보조하는데 잇다. 현재 獄中에 잇는 90여명 전투적 노동자가 이 구원을 받게 되리라고 한다.

作家의 總動員
모스코바에 잇는 국제적 革命作家聯盟에서는 米國에 잇는 「죤·리드·클랍」을 통하야 全米국작자와 예술가에게 反帝戰 資料를 구하엿다고 한다. 그 자료라고 하는 것은 列强이 帝國主義 略奪展을 준비하고 잇는 것과 거기 대한 勤勞階級의 反戰鬪爭과 기타 전쟁에 대한 여러가지 문제에 대한 스켓취 短篇小說, 詩, 회화를 의미하는 것이다. 米國 左翼作家는 지난 8월 1일 反帝國主義戰爭日을 향하야 總動員狀態에 잇엇다고 한다. ＜12＞
