Article Title:	新作路, CO에게 (New Road, To CO)
Author:	金岸曙
Article Type:	시 (Poem)

New Road, To CO
						Kim Ansŏ
1
Travelers come and go, go and come
Footprints trample footprints into mere dust
The wind blows, and even that dust flies away
My love, shall this life take the New Road?

2
The New Road makes this heart of mine so restless.
Day and night, travelers never cease.
Footprints are disordered in the dust
How can I find my love, who passed by in a dream?

3
The New Road, paved in hopes my love might come,
Why do unfamiliar travelers come and go?
Useless footprints just stir up dust
My awaited love's procession does not come today either.

4
On the New Road, automobiles flee.
Ships cross the trackless sea.
Birds fly on the blue path of the empty sky.
My love, which road shall I take?

Over there, in the grassy field, there is a path.
It is messy with the tracks of a narrow single path.
As no one travels it, it is quiet.
My love, let us go; that is the path. -1931, 10, 29 - <96>

**Summary**

This poem by Kim Ansŏ contrasts the bustling, impersonal "New Road" (新作路), likely symbolizing modernity and rapid change, with a quiet, overgrown grassy path. The speaker feels lost and alienated on the New Road, unable to find the longed-for "beloved" amidst the dust and constant traffic of strangers and machines (cars). Ultimately, the speaker proposes escaping to the quiet, untrodden path, suggesting a preference for intimacy, tradition, or a retreat from the disorienting pace of modern life. For historians, the poem reflects the ambivalence and anxieties surrounding modernization in colonial Korea during the 1930s. The "New Road" could represent imposed development or societal shifts that feel alienating, contrasting with a desire for a more authentic or traditional connection symbolized by the overgrown path.

新作路, CO에게
						金岸曙
1
行客은 오고 가고 가고 옵니다
자욱은 자욱밟아 띠끌이외다
바람부니 그나마 띠끌납니다
님이어, 이 한 生을 新作路탈가.

2
新作路는 이내 맘 분주도 하이.
밤낮으로 行客은 끊일 때 없네.
먼지속에 발자욱 어즈럽거니
꿈타고 지내신 님 어이 찾을고.

3
幸여나 님오실까 닦은 新作路
낱설은 行客들은 뭐라 오갈고,
쓸데없는 자욱에 먼지만 일고
기두는 님 行次는 이 날도 없네.

4
新作路엔 自動車 달아납니다.
길도 없는 바다를 배는 갑니다
빈하늘 프른길엔 새가 납니다
님이어, 어느 길을 저는 가리까.

저기저 풀밭속에 길 잇습니다.
외마대 자욱길로 어즈럽쇠다
아모도 안단니니 고요하외다
님이어, 가십시다 저 길이외다. -1931, 10, 29 - ＜96＞
