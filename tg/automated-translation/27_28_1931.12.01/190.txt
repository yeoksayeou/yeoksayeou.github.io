Urgent Telegram No. 1

"Do you not have just one word you must say to the figures active in our society or to those who have not stepped forward?" Thus, the telegrams received are this Urgent Telegram No. 1 and its replies, which now appear before you. For those from whom replies have not yet been received, they will be published in the next issue as soon as they arrive.
Furthermore, Urgent Telegram No. 2 seems poised to arrive from all directions, and we now widely solicit contributions from our readers. If it is a telegram that our general readership wishes to hear, we will promptly publish it along with the reply. (In order of telegram arrival)

To Mr. Yi Kwangsu
Leave that place, read social science, and become the Gorky of Chosŏn. Your life will live forever. - From Kim Myŏngsik, Osaka

Reply
Thank you for the advice. I wish to become Gorky and live forever, but I hesitate for the sake of living in the present. Sooner or later, I intend to find a way to live forever. - Yi Kwangsu

To Mr. Ch'oe Namsŏn
How about returning to the "Ch'ŏngch'un" (Youth) era? - From Kim Sŏnnyang, Anak
Can you not, with the beating heart of one undergoing rebirth (更生), return once more to the masses? From O Kiyŏng, P'yŏngyang

Reply to Both
I will continue doing the work permitted in the present. I do not wish to speak of the future. - Ch'oe Namsŏn

To Mr. O Igŭn
Stop hesitating and undertake some kind of business (實業). Act proactively. - From O Chŏngsu

Reply
Due to personal circumstances, I have hitherto been unable to undertake any business. Henceforth, I am thinking of trying to act proactively. - O Igŭn

To Mr. Kim Kŏnhyŏng
Much is expected of you; please enter the publishing world. - From Sippansaeng (十半生), Kyŏngsŏng

Reply
I keenly feel that the enterprise you advise is greatly demanded by our society, and I also recognize it as the most suitable enterprise for myself. However, the reason I cannot undertake it immediately is the issue of funds (資金問題), so it remains on hold for the time being (當分間). - Kim Kŏnhyŏng <59>

**Summary**

This article presents a series of "urgent telegrams" sent to prominent Korean intellectuals and figures (Yi Kwangsu, Ch'oe Namsŏn, O Igŭn, Kim Kŏnhyŏng) by readers or acquaintances, along with their replies. The telegrams contain pointed advice or requests, urging Yi Kwangsu towards social critique ("become the Gorky of Chosŏn"), Ch'oe Namsŏn to return to his earlier nationalist stance, O Igŭn to start a business, and Kim Kŏnhyŏng to enter publishing. The replies range from cautious agreement (Yi, O, Kim) to deflection (Ch'oe). For historians, this piece offers a fascinating glimpse into public expectations and critiques directed at leading intellectuals during the colonial period. The calls for specific actions (social science, business, publishing, returning to past ideals) reflect contemporary concerns, while the often evasive or constrained replies hint at the difficult positions these figures occupied under Japanese rule, balancing personal circumstances, public pressure, and political realities.

至急電報 第一號
「貴下는 우리 社會에서 活動하는 人士 또는 나서지 않은 人士에게 꼭 한마디 하실 말슴이 없습니까?」 이리하야 들어 온 電報가 여러분 앞에 나타나는 이 至急電報 第一號와 그 回電입니다. 아직 回電이 없는 곳은 回電이 들어오는대로 來號에 揭載하겟습니다.
그리고 至急電報 第二號는 四方으로 들어 올 모양이나 이제 널리 독자 諸位에게 구합니다. 一般 우리 독자가 듣고 싶어하는 電報면 지체없이 回電을 아울러 실리기로 합니다. (電報到着順)

李光洙君
그 자리를 버리고 社會科學을 읽어서 朝鮮의 꼬리키가 되라. 저의 生命은 永遠히 살 것이다. - 大阪 金明植 發

回電
忠告는 고맙습니다. 꼬리키가 되어 永遠히 살고도 싶으나 當場 살기를 위하야 주저하고 잇습니다. 早晩間 永遠히 살 道理를 하려 합니다. - 李光洙

崔南善氏
「靑春」시대로 도라가심이 어떠하십니까. - 安岳 金善亮 發
貴下는 更生하는 者의 뛰는 心藏을 가지고 다시금 民衆에게로 도라올 수 없습니까? 平壤 吳基永 發

둘에 대한 回電
現在에 허락되는 일을 아직 그대로 하겟습니다. 앞에 일은 말하고 싶지 안습니다. - 崔南善

吳翊殷君
그만 躊躇하고 무슨 實業에든지 着手하시오. 적극的으로 活動하시오. - 吳楨洙 發

回電
個人의 事情에 의하와 至今껏 무삼 業에나 着手치 못하엿습니다. 앞으로는 積極的 活動을 하여 보려고 생각합니다. - 吳翊殷

金建亨君
君에게 囑望하는 바 많으니 出版界에 나오세오. - 京城 十半生 發

回電
警告하시는 事業은 우리 社會에서 絶大하게 要求된는 바도 切實히 느끼며 아울러 내 自信으도 가장 適合한 事業으로 認識합니다. 다못 즉시 着手치 못함은 資金問題이니 當分間 保留 중에 잇습니다. - 金建亨 ＜59＞
