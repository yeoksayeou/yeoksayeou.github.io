Publication Date: 1932년 11월 01일
Article Title: 行進잃은 나의 行進曲 (My March Without a Procession)
Author: 李瀅敎
Article Type: 시 (Poem)

My March Without a Procession
						Yi Hyŏnggyo

Figuratively, my song is a melody without sheet music (曲譜)-
Figuratively, my outcry (絶叫) is a trumpet (喇叭) blown after the procession (行次)-
Figuratively, the staff I grasp is also a useless stick
after falling down-
Figuratively, my repeated injection (注射) is but ineffective meddling (作亂깜) sent
into a broken life, into cooled blood vessels (血管)-

Oh, friend! Behold,
Are not all these things a march without a procession – the resonance
of a geomungo with broken strings?

Today again, I wander these castle ruins (城터) where the sun has set.
And so, once more, picking up broken castle stones (城돌), patting
the moss-covered neglected tombs,
Aimlessly, endlessly, endlessly, I utter a sorrowful lament (痛哭). <91>

**Summary**

This poem by Yi Hyŏnggyo uses a series of metaphors to convey a profound sense of futility, loss of purpose, and despair. The speaker compares their efforts (song, outcry, actions like using a staff or giving an injection) to things that are inherently useless or have missed their moment, like music without notes, a trumpet after the parade is over, or medicine for the already dead. This feeling culminates in the image of wandering aimlessly through sunset ruins, weeping without direction. From a historical perspective, the poem powerfully reflects the deep disillusionment and sense of paralysis experienced by some Korean intellectuals during the Japanese colonial period. The imagery of a "march without a procession," broken instruments, and wandering ruins can be interpreted as expressing the loss of national direction and the inability to act meaningfully under oppressive circumstances, making it a poignant piece for historians studying the psychological impact of colonialism.

行進잃은 나의 行進曲
						李瀅敎
비유컨대 나의 노래는 曲譜없는 노랫가락-
比喩컨대 나의 絶叫는 行次後에 부는 喇叭-
비유컨대 나의 부여잡은 지팽이도 너머진
뒤에 用處없는 막대기-
비유컨대 연겊 놓는 나의 注射래야 분질러
진 목숨에 식어진 血管에 보내는 効力없는 作亂깜-
오 벗이어! 보라
그 어느것이 모다 行進잃은 行進曲-줄 끊어진
거문고의 울림이 아니뇨.

오늘도 나는 또다시 이 해떠러진 城터를
헤매이노라.
그리하야 다시금 부서진 城돌을 줏어 이끼
앉은 허튼 무덤을 두다리며
支向도 없이 끝없는 끝없는 구슬픈 痛哭을
엽드노라. ＜91＞
