Science Fragments
						O Chŏng-su

The Debut of the Double-Decker Train
It is reported that the Pennsylvania Railroad Company in the United States recently debuted one double-decker train. If test results are good, the company plans to make all its trains double-deckers. This new style of car can accommodate approximately 50% more passengers with the same weight and volume, which is expected to bring enormous savings in power and great convenience for passengers.

Not 5 Senses, but 10
Hearing, sight, taste, smell, touch - the 5 senses. It has been said until now that higher animals possess these 5 senses. However, recent theories speak of 10 senses. - Namely, the sense of temperature (hot/cold), kinesthetic sense (movement), sense of verticality, sense of pain, and interoception (internal organ sense). These are said to be possessed by higher animals - especially humans. The sense of temperature distinguishes hot and cold through the action of special nerves <76> contacted by the skin; the kinesthetic sense, located in the joints, reports the position of the limbs; the sense of verticality perceives whether the body is vertical through the action of a leveling mechanism near the inner ear; the sense of pain is the action of special nerves located in the skin, muscles, and blood vessels. Interoception is the sensing of internal organ functions such as respiration and pulse.

X-rays Shortening the Maturation Period of Fruit Trees
There has been an instance of a citrus tree flowering when it was merely 2 weeks after germination and only 2 inches long. This was the result of an experiment by the EG Company in the United States; normally, that tree takes 5 to over 10 years to flower. The seeds were exposed to X-rays for 2 minutes before sowing. Although the exact relationship between X-rays and the maturation period of fruit trees is not yet fully understood, if the fruiting period can be revolutionarily shortened, how great might the impact on humanity be?

Artificial Sugar
Swiss scientists Pictet and Vogel are said to have invented a method for artificially making sugar from carbon and water. The sugar made this way is said to have properties exactly the same as cane sugar (Cane Sugar). It is still questionable whether this method will develop to the point where it can be produced cheaply enough for commercialization. <77>

**Summary**

This article presents four brief reports on recent scientific developments: the introduction of double-decker trains by the Pennsylvania Railroad for increased capacity, the expansion of the traditional five senses to ten senses in higher animals (including temperature, movement, verticality, pain, and internal state), the use of X-rays by the American EG Company to dramatically shorten the time for a citrus tree to flower, and the invention of artificial cane sugar from carbon and water by Swiss scientists Pictet and Vogel, though its commercial viability is uncertain. For historians, the article reflects Korean intellectuals' awareness of and interest in global scientific and technological advancements during the colonial period, showcasing a desire to engage with modernity despite the political circumstances. The selection of topics—transport efficiency, human biology, agricultural technology, and synthetic chemistry—points to key areas of contemporary scientific interest worldwide.

二層汽車의 出世
米國 펜실바니야 鐵道會社에서는 最近 二層 汽車 壹臺를 出世시켯다 한다. 試驗 結果가 좋으면 其 會社 汽車는 全部 二層으로 만들 計劃이라 한다. 이 新式車는 같은 重量과 容積에 客을 收容하기는 約 5割이나 더 할 수 잇으므로 動力의 經濟와 客의 便宜가 莫大하리라고 한다.

5覺이 아니라 10覺
聞, 見, 味, 嗅, 觸의 5覺. 高等動物은 이 5覺을 가젓다고 지금까지 말하여 왓다. 그러나 最近 學說은 10覺을 말한다.-曰 寒暖覺, 動覺, 垂直覺, 痛覺, 內臟覺이 이것이니 高等動物-特히 人生이 반드시 가지고 잇는 것이라 한다. 寒暖覺은 皮부가 접촉된 特別神經의＜76＞ 作用으로 寒과 暖을 분간하는 것이요 動覺은 關節에 所在한 것으로 手足의 位置를 報告하는 것, 垂直覺은 內耳 部近에 잇는 水平機의 作用으로 身體의 垂直與否를 覺하는 것, 痛覺은 皮膚, 筋肉 及 血管에 所在한 特別神經의 作用이다. 內臟覺은 呼吸 脈搏 等의 內臟作用을 覺하는 것이다.

果樹의 成熟期를 縮少시키는 X光線
發芽한지 不過 2週間이요 長이 不過 2寸 되는 귤나무가 꽃 핀 일이 잇다. 米國 EG社에서 試驗해 본 結果인대 普通 그 나무는 5年 以上 10年이 되어야 結花된다. 播種하기 前 種子를 X光線에 2分間 비쵠 後 심엇든 것이다. X光線과 果樹의 成熟期와 꼭 어떠한 關係가 잇는지는 아직 다 알지 못하지마는 結實期를 革命的으로 縮少시킨다 하면 人類에 及하는 影響은 얼마나 클는지?

人造砂糖
瑞西의 科學家 픽테와 보겔은 人工으로 炭素와 물을 가지고 砂糖 만드는 法을 發明하얏다 한다. 이같이 만든 砂糖은 甘蔗, 糖(Cane Sugar)과 性質이 꼭 같다 한다. 이 方法이 發達되어 商品化하리만큼 低價로 만들 수 잇게 될는 지는 아직 疑問이라 한다.＜77＞
