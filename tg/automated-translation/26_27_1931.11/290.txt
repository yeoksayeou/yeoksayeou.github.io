An Urgent Notice to Compatriots in Manchuria
						Yi Kwangsu

Compatriots in Manchuria must absolutely not align yourselves with any particular political force. Because the political forces in Manchuria lack stability and are always changing, even if you align with Regime A (甲政權) to gain temporary convenience, when Regime B (乙政權) replaces it, you will inevitably be swept away and oppressed as collaborators of Regime A. Do we not have the bitter experience of Chiang [Kai-shek] (蔣氏) and the National Government developing ill feelings towards all Koreans just because a few Koreans participated in the anti-Chiang uprising in Kwangtung (廣東)?

Therefore, it is right for us compatriots in Manchuria to transcend all political regimes and strive only to put down deep economic and cultural roots. Since we are residing as guests in another country's land, it is necessary to clearly understand that jumping into the midst of that country's internal political disputes brings a hundred harms and not a single benefit.

This time too, as a result of the Japanese military dispatch (日本出兵), we cannot know what changes may come to the Northeast [Manchurian] regime (東北政權). It is crucial for our compatriots in Manchuria to keep your mouths firmly shut, maintain a solemn and wise silence, and exercise meticulous care so as not to give the officials and people of that country any pretext for suspicion (猜疑).

What is most desired is that, on this occasion, even if compatriots in Manchuria cannot unify, they should form two or three Korean resident associations (교민단체) according to their respective conveniences. Firstly, to protect already acquired rights and interests; secondly, to control the words and actions of compatriots; and thirdly, to actively plan for the establishment and improvement of economy and culture, transcending political power. I believe this is the wisest course of action for compatriots in Manchuria facing the current situation. If not, and if even one step is taken incorrectly, there is no small concern that the foundation built by compatriots in Manchuria over half a century may be reduced to nothing (歸於虛地).

Thinking of our compatriots amidst the chaos, I offer these words of earnest advice (苦諫) from afar. <53>

**Summary**

Yi Kwangsu writes an urgent editorial in November 1931 advising Koreans living in Manchuria to maintain strict political neutrality amidst the turmoil following the recent Japanese military intervention (Manchurian Incident). He warns against aligning with any Chinese political faction, citing past negative repercussions, and urges them instead to focus on establishing strong economic and cultural foundations. He advocates for the creation of Korean resident associations to protect their interests, manage community conduct, and foster non-political development, fearing that any misstep could destroy the community's hard-won position. For historians, this article is revealing as it reflects the precarious position of Korean immigrants caught between Chinese nationalism, internal Chinese power struggles, and Japanese expansionism just after the Manchurian Incident. It also showcases Yi Kwangsu's pragmatic, potentially accommodationist, survival strategy for the diaspora during the colonial period, urging dissociation from political struggles and focusing on community self-preservation and socio-economic consolidation, a stance that foreshadows his later controversial path.

在滿同胞에게 急告
						李光洙
在滿同胞는 결코 어느 정치적 세력에 偏倚하여서는 안됩니다. 滿洲의 정치적 세력이란 安定性이 없이 매양 변하는 것이기 때문에 甲政權에 偏倚하야 一時의 便宜를 圖得한다 하더라도 乙政權이 代立할 때에는 甲政權의 連累者로 掃盡, 壓迫을 당하지 아니하면 아니됩니다. 廣東서 反蔣暴動에 幾個朝鮮人이 가담하기 때문에 蔣씨와 國民政府에서 朝鮮人 전체에 대하야 惡感을 가지게 된 쓴 경험도 잇지 아니합니까.
그러니까 우리 在滿同胞는 마땅이 모든 정권에 초월하야 오직 경제적 문화적으로 뿌리를 깊이 박도록 노력하는 것이 옳습니다. 우리는 남의 나라 땅에 가서 寓居하여 잇으니 그 나라의 內政紛爭의 와중에 뛰어들어가는 것은 百害가 잇고 一利도 없는 것을 分明히 알아둘 필요가 잇습니다.
이번에도 日本出兵의 결과로 東北政權에 무슨 變動이 올는지도 알 수 없거니와 우리 在滿同胞는 굳게 입을 다물어 엄숙하고 지혜로은 침묵을 지켜서 저 나라 官民에게 猜疑의 구실을 주지 아니하도록 細心의 注意를 하심이 긴요합니다.
가장 원하는 것은 이번 기회에 在滿同胞가 통일은 못하더라도 각기 便宜를 따라 2, 3개의 교민단체를 만들어서 첫재로 旣得한 권익을 옹호하고 둘재로 同胞의 言動을 團束하고 셋재로 적극적으로 政權에서 초월한 경제와 문화의 扶植, 向上을 圖할 것입니다. 이것이 今日에 處하는 在滿同胞의 가장 지혜로은 일인가 합니다. 그렇아니하고 만일 一步를 잘못하면 在滿同胞의 半世紀間 쌓아놓은 地盤이 歸於虛地가 될 염려가 없지 아니합니다.
혼란중에 게신 동포를 생각하면 멀리 一言의 苦諫을 들이는 바입니다.＜53＞
