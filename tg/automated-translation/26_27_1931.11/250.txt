**Body Text Translation:**

An Autumn Night's Sickbed Lament
						Chŏn Uhan

The papered door trembles in the wind like rapids,
Crickets cry noisily, announcing autumn,
But my gaunt body lies ill in a foreign land;
Tonight feels especially sorrowful—how can I not weep?

Tonight, this room is desolate as a tomb,
The moonlight slanting through the window lattice is unusually blue;
Even an untroubled heart (무단한 心情) would weep on such a night,
How much more so a sick, aching heart at a traveler's window (客窓)?

Where has sleep gone? Has it fled thousands of miles away?
Closing my eyes, ten thousand thoughts bloom like clouds.
Ah, who is the beloved one to touch my feverish brow?
To become like a pebble kicked by the foot's edge is even sadder.

In a world where one close in the morning becomes a stranger by evening,
My body knows there is no one at all to trust,
Ah, yet I yearn, for that person alone I yearn,
Only for that person who would touch my brow and soothe my chest, I yearn.
-(Autumn 1931, in Hanyang)-<112>

**Summary**

This poem by Chŏn Uhan expresses the deep sorrow, loneliness, and physical discomfort of being sick and alone in a foreign place (specifically Hanyang/Seoul) during an autumn night in 1931. The speaker laments the sounds of autumn, the desolate room, sleeplessness filled with anxious thoughts, and a profound longing for a specific loved one's care, contrasting this with a general disillusionment about human relationships. For historians, the poem offers a personal glimpse into the emotional landscape of an individual, possibly an intellectual or student, experiencing illness and alienation in the colonial capital, Hanyang (Seoul), during a period of significant social and political change. The sense of displacement ("foreign land" - 客地) and yearning might reflect broader sentiments among Koreans under Japanese rule.

秋宵病吟
						全佑漢
문풍지는 여울물처럼 바람에 떨고
귀뚜라미 요란히 울어 가을을 알외건만
여윈 몸 客地에 病들어 누엇으니
이 밤이 더욱이 설어워라 아니울고 어이랴.

오늘 밤 이 방안이 무덤같이 쓸쓸쿠나
窓쌀에 빗긴 달빛 유달리 푸르르니
무단한 心情도 울고야 말 밤이어던
하물며 客窓에 病든 아픈 마음이랴.

잠조차 어데간나 千里萬里 다라난나
눈감으니 千萬생각 구름되어 피어나네
아아. 끌는이마 짚어줄 님이 그 누구냐
발끝에 채이는 조악돌 신세됨이 더욱이 슬프구나

아침에 친튼 사람 저녁에 남되는 세상이라
미들 사람 전혀 없음을 내 몸음 아니언만
아아, 그래도 그립구나 그이만은 그립구나
내 이마 짚어주며 내 가슴 어루만질 그이만이 그리워라.
-(1931, 가을 漢陽서)-＜112＞
