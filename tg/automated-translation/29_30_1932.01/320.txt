**Body Text Translation**
Warriors of the World
						Song Sunil

Souls huddled by the fireplace, by the brazier,
Lumps of flesh sliding towards the warmest part of the ondol floor—
They are all cowering.
Like flies dried onto the ceiling.

Yet the children, in the yard, on the street,
Flail their limbs and leap about.
Even when the wind blows, even when the blizzard rages.
Ah, they are the stout Warriors (勇士) who defy and swallow the world.

Rolling on the frozen ground, the children run along.
Holding sleds, holding skates,
To the ice rink, to the ice rink—
Even though the sun has set and hidden in the clouds,
They wave their red fists,
Boasting, "Let's have a snowball fight."

The dim room is not their playground;
Shaking off their gentle mothers' hands,
They ride the world and romp about.
Ah, stout Warriors!
Where do your pulsing veins lead—?

Grow up well and strong, stout warriors,
To become the masters of the coming day and sound the victory drum.—
-1931.12- <84>

**Summary**

This poem contrasts the lethargic, withdrawn state of adults indoors during winter with the vibrant, irrepressible energy of children playing outside despite the cold. The children are hailed as "stout warriors" who embrace the world and hold the promise for the future. The poem concludes with an exhortation for these children to grow strong and become the "masters of the coming day," sounding the "victory drum." From a historical perspective, the poem, written during the Japanese colonial period, can be interpreted as a metaphorical call for the younger generation of Koreans to maintain their spirit and vitality, implicitly hoping they will lead the nation to a future "victory" or liberation. The emphasis on resilience and future mastery resonates with nationalist sentiments often expressed subtly in literature of the era.

世界의 勇士
						宋順鎰
난로까에 화로까에 쭉으린 목슴들
아렛묵으로 미끄러지는 고기덩이들-
그들은 모도다 움츠러지도다.
천정에 말라붙은 파리 같이도.

그래도 아이들은 마당에서 거리에서
활개치며 날뛰어라.
바람이 불어도 눈포래처도.
아 그들은 세계를 비앗고 삼키는 씩씩한 勇士.

언 땅을 굴르며 아이들은 달리어가도다.
설매들고 스켙들고
어름판으로 어름판으로-
해도 치워 구름속에 숨엇는데
그들은 붉은 주먹 휘두르며
눈쌈하자 뽑내어.

컴컴한 방 속이 그들의 노리터 아니어니
다사로운 어머니 손낄 뿌리치고
그들은 세게를 타 앉고 뛰놀거니
아 씩씩한 勇士들이어!
그대들의 팔닥이는 핏줄은 어데로-.

잘 크고 자라거라 씩씩한 용사들
오는 날의 주인 되어 승전고 울리도록.-
-1931.12- ＜84＞
