Modern March

UK's New Marriage Law
Despite various difficult issues, the British Parliament has submitted a new bill to regulate matters concerning marriage. It is said that men can marry relatives in the following relationships:
1. Daughter of a deceased wife's brother.
1. Daughter of a deceased wife's sister.
1. Wife of a deceased paternal uncle.
1. Wife of a deceased maternal uncle.
1. Sister of a deceased wife's father.
1. Sister of a deceased wife's mother.
1. Widow of a nephew (brother's son).
1. Widow of a nephew (sister's son).

A Very Modern Maiden Who Fought for Her Marriage
In Tanggayŏng, Namhae, Kyŏngnam Province, a great marriage battle unfolded in 1931, and the protagonist, Ch'oe Hakpong (18), suffered defeat, resulting in the conclusion of a marriage treaty on the same day with a 17-year-old maiden. Hearing the details, the family of Ch'oe Hakpong, residing in Sangju-ri, Namhae, had proposed marriage to the second daughter, Pak Soche (17), of a Mr. Pak living in the same village, but ten months passed without any news. Meanwhile, an engagement was arranged with the daughter of a Mr. Song from Hangdo-ch'on, Namhae, and the groom was on his way carrying the wedding gifts (폐물). Hearing this news, Pak Soche resented her parents for not arranging the marriage at such an appropriate time, went out, grabbed the groom, insisted he take the wedding gifts to her house, and ultimately, Soche won. She took the groom to her house, and her parents had no choice but to hold the wedding ceremony that very day. After the ceremony, the bride loudly proclaimed that the forced marriage was her parents' fault. The family of Mr. Song received 8 wŏn for the wedding preparation costs, and the incident was resolved peacefully.

Memory Power
In Iowa, USA, the famous tenor musician Paul Kraitschpild continuously broadcast 160 songs he had memorized. The broadcast continued from 10:35 PM to 12:30 AM.

Poch'ŏn'gyo Superstition Causes Freezing Deaths
Believing in the superstitious faith of Poch'ŏn'gyo (普天敎) to cure illness, Yi Namhan (61), Kwŏn P'arhan (19), O Kison (19), and Han Sŏkcho (13) were heading towards Ma-ri, Chungnam-myŏn, Yŏngil-gun, Kyŏngbuk Province on January 9th. Their bodies were discovered only after 20 days, having tragically frozen to death lying side-by-side in snow piled 7 or 8 ch'ŏk (feet) deep in a mountain valley near the old temple Pogyeongsa (寶鏡寺). Since the four believers were sacrificed to freezing death, perhaps the superstition froze to death along with them.

New Collection Method Invented by Pastor
Due to the worldwide economic depression causing Sunday offerings to decrease daily, a pastor at a Christian church in Middletown, North America, devised a clever plan. If someone donated 1 wŏn or more, the collector would blow a trumpet. As a result, the offerings experienced a recent boom, and consequently, the collector's lips reportedly became chapped.

Handsome Man's Deceptive Robbery
Miss Wells, living in Chelmsford, England, was robbed of money and valuables by a certain handsome man. He woke the sleeping Miss Wells, claiming to be a police officer investigating a suspected burglary, and after receiving permission to check downstairs, he somehow sneakily stole a money chest containing 600 wŏn in cash.

Swiss Militarism
In Switzerland, famous for peace conferences and disarmament talks since the World War, the number of national servicemen has increased by a staggering 60,000 since 1921, and the militaristic (武斷的) spirit is reportedly growing daily.

Triplets with Different Birthdays
Mrs. Sisson, living in a place called Middleboro, North America, gave birth to triplets. The first baby was born around 11 PM, and the other two babies were born the next day, resulting in the three sibling sons having different birthdays.

Escaping the Depression
Imagawa (49), arrested by the Tokyo Fukagawa Police Station for dining and dashing, told the officer, "I have nothing to eat, and I thought life would be stable in prison, so I committed the crime. By the time I finish my sentence and get out of prison, the economy will have recovered, right?"

Unusual Beer Names
Dr. Schlanka of Germany authored a book titled "Beer" in 1886. Among the strange and unusual beer names recorded in it, a few are:
Ouch (Auweh)
Bite the Guy (Betz den Kerl)
Holy Father (Heilige Vater)
Dog (Hund)
Scared (Angst)
Injured Female Cat (Bletzkatze) etc.<94>

**Summary**

This article compiles various short news items from around the world and Korea in early 1931. Topics include changes to British marriage laws, a dramatic incident in rural Korea where a young woman intercepts her intended groom who was engaged to another, a feat of memory by an American tenor, the freezing deaths of four Poch'ŏn'gyo followers due to superstition, a novel church fundraising method in America, a robbery in England by a man posing as police, rising militarism in Switzerland, triplets born on different days, a man committing a crime in Japan to seek refuge from the depression in prison, and peculiar German beer names. From a historical perspective, the article reveals contemporary global interests and anxieties, such as the economic depression. The Korean marriage story highlights tensions between tradition and emerging modern agency for women, while the Poch'ŏn'gyo incident and the man seeking jail reflect the harsh realities, desperation, and influence of religious/superstitious beliefs in colonial Korea.

英國의 新結婚法
英國議會에서는 여러 가지 難問題가 잇음에도 불구하고 結婚에 관한 問題를 정리하기 위하야 新法案을 제출하엿는데 男子는 左記關係의 姻戚하고는 결혼할 수 잇다고 한다.
一. 사망한 妻의 兄弟의 딸.
一. 사망한 妻의 姉妹의 딸.
一. 叔伯父가 죽은 三寸댁.
一. 外三寸이 죽은 外三寸宅.
一. 妻죽은 丈人의 姉妹.
一. 妻죽은 丈母의 姉妹.
一. 조카의 未亡人.
一. 생질의 未亡人.

結婚을 戰取한 超尖端處女
慶南 南海 당가고개(堂加峙)에서는 1931년 結婚大戰이 연출되고 主人公 崔學奉(18)은 敗北를 당한 결과 芳年 17세 처녀와 卽日 結婚條約을 締結하얏다. 이제 그 내용을 드르니 南海 尙州里에 사는 崔學奉의 집에서는 같은 동리에 사는 朴某의 次女 朴小悌(17)와 약혼을 하자고 청혼을 하엿으나 열달이나 지나도록 소식이 없엇다. 마츰 南海項島村宋某의 딸과 약혼을 하고 그 날 신랑이 페물을 지고 가든 길이다. 이 소식을 드른 朴小悌는 그같이 적당한 자리에 혼인을 아니한 자기의 父母를 원망하고 나아가서 신랑을 붓잡고 페물을 가지고 자긔 집으로 가자고 승강하다가 결국 소제가 이기어 신랑을 데리고 처녀의 집으로 가서 그 父母도 하는 수 없이 당일로 결혼식을 거행하엿다 한다. 成禮後 신부는 강제결혼은 父母의 무리라고 大氣焰을 吐하고 宋某의 집에서는 結婚準備費用 8원을 받고 事件은 無事解決이라고.

記憶力
米國 아이오와洲에서 테너로 유명한 樂家 「파울, 크라치필드」는 기억한 노래 160을 계속 放送하엿다. 繼續時 午後 10시 35분부터 午前 12시 반까지.

普天敎迷信凍死
普天敎의 迷信的 信仰을 믿고 병을 고친다하야 지난 1월 9일 慶北 迎日郡 竹南面 馬里로 향하든 李南漢(61) 權八漢(19) 吳基善(19) 韓錫祚(13) 4명은 古刹 寶鏡寺에서 가까운 山谷間에서 7, 8尺이나 쌔운 눈 우에 머리를 나란이 하야 慘酷히 凍死한 것이 20일 만에야 발견되엿다. 四敎徒가 凍死로 희생을 당하엿으니 迷信도 같이 凍死하엿을 것이다.

新集金術牧師가 發明
세계를 휩싸는 전황때문에 일요일의 연보금이 날로 줄어 들어가는데 대하야 北米 미들타운 야소교회에서는 목사님이 妙策을 강구하엿다. 1원 이상을 내는 사람이 잇으면 集金하는 사람이 나발을 불게 하엿음으로 연보금은 근래에 드문 호경기를 일우엇고 그 결과 集金하는 사람은 입이 부르텃다고 한다.

美男子의 說敎强盜
英國 「체름스포-드」에 사는 「웰스」양은 어떤 美男子에게 금품을 강탈당하엿다. 그것은 잠자고 잇는 「웰스」양을 깨우며 자긔는 경찰관인데 도적이 들어 온 모양이니 아래층을 조사하겟노라고 허락을 받은 후에 어느 틈에 감쪽같이 現金 600원이 든 금궤를 훔처갓다 한다.

瑞西의 軍國主義
世界大戰 이래 平和會義 軍縮會議로 유명한 瑞西國에는 1921년 이래 國民軍 增加數 무려 6萬 武斷的 精神은 날로 높아가는 상태에 잇다.

生日이 相違한 三胎子
北米 「미들보로」라는 곧에 사는 「싯손」여사는 三胎子를 낫는데 맨 먼저 아이는 오후 열한시경에 나코 다른 두 아이는 그 이튼날 나케 되어서 쌍둥 아들 3兄弟는 生日이 달라젓다고 한다.

不景氣避難
無錢取食으로 東京深川署에 검거된 今宮某(49)는 警官에게 말하기를 『먹을 것은 없고 刑務所에 가면 생활이 안정되겟기에 죄를 범하엿소. 服役하고 형무소를 나올 때는 景氣가 회복되겟지오.』
