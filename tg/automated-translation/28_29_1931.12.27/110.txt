To Those Wishing for Corrections of New Poetry

For all of you striving in new poetry (新詩), the poet An Sŏ (岸曙), Mr. Kim Ŏk (金億先生), has begun providing personal correspondence instruction in poetry according to the regulations noted below. We hope you will make much use of this service.

Regulations:
1. Poetry manuscripts will be corrected/critiqued and returned.
1. Mr. Kim Ŏk (金億先生) takes full responsibility.
1. On the envelope, address it to Mr. Kim Ŏk, Tongkwang-sa (東光社), 1-2 Sŏdaemun-jŏng (西大門町), Kyŏngsŏngbu (京城府).
1. Enclose the manuscript return fee. Those without the return fee will not be returned. Items with insufficient postage (郵稅) will not be accepted.
1. To limit the number of submissions (投稿數), a correction fee of 30 *chŏn* per 50 lines is required (要함).
1. Correction fees paid with postage stamps (郵票) are also invalid (無効).
1. Outstanding works will be published (揭載) in the Tongkwang (東光) journal. End. <32>

**Summary**

This article is an announcement in the magazine *Tongkwang* advertising a new poetry correction service offered via correspondence by the established poet Kim Ŏk (pen name An Sŏ). It outlines the rules for submission, including the address, the requirement of a return fee, and a correction fee of 30 *chŏn* per 50 lines (payable not by stamps), adding that excellent submissions may be published in the magazine. From a historical perspective, this piece reveals the active literary mentorship occurring during the colonial period, the formalized ways poets sought guidance, and the economic aspect of literary services, showing that even established poets like Kim Ŏk engaged in paid instruction. It also highlights the role of literary magazines like *Tongkwang* in fostering new talent.

新詩의 添削을 원하시는 이에게
新詩에 精進하시는 여러분을 위하야 左記의 規正으로 詩人 岸曙 金億先生이 詩의 通信個人敎授를 開始하게 되엇습니다. 많이 이용하시기 바랍니다.
규정 1. 詩의 원고를 添削批評하야 반송함.
1. 金億先生이 모든 책임을 가짐.
1. 皮封에는 京城府 西大門町 一ノ二 東光社交金億先生으로.
1. 원고반환료를 첨부할 것. 반환료 없는 것은 반환치 아니함. 郵稅不足되는 것은 받지 아니함.
1. 投稿數를 제한하기 위하야 50行따라 30錢식의 添削料를 要함.
1. 添削料는 郵票로도 無効함.
1. 우수한 작품은 東光誌上에 揭載함. 이상. ＜32＞
