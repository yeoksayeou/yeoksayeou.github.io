News from Old Newspapers, Snippets of News from America

Dr. Kim Ch'angse (金昌世) is currently active across the United States fundraising for the Tuberculosis Prevention Association based in Shanghai, China (中國 上海). Dr. Kim is a graduate of Kyŏngsŏng Medical College (京城 世專), practiced medicine in Shanghai for a long time, obtained a doctorate in public health from Johns Hopkins University in Baltimore, USA, worked at his alma mater, and then went back to China where he became a figure in China's public health movement, having toured various regions in the North and South, as well as the South Seas (南洋). His family is currently in Shanghai.

Miss Maekkeiri, who is in Chosŏn (朝鮮) for religious work, is serializing a novel titled "Glory of the East" in the "Christian Herald" magazine. The novel's subject matter is life in Chosŏn.

Four Chosŏn female students are currently enrolled at the University of Michigan. They are Ko Hwanggyŏng (高凰京), Kim Tongjun (金東駿), Kim Meri (金메리), and one other.

Mr. Ch'oe Kyunam (崔규남), currently studying at Ann Arbor University, has been nominated as a member of the national scientific society, Ssŏgŭma Ch'ai (Sigma Xi).

Mr. Chŏn Cheik'ŏp (田제이컵) is on a lecture tour regarding "Far East Issues" in the California (加州) area.

Mr. J.M. No (J.M.盧) of Duke University (듁대학) is reportedly fundraising with the intention of establishing a new secondary school upon returning to Chosŏn.

Dr. Sin Hŭng'u (申興雨), General Secretary of the Chosŏn YMCA (朝鮮基靑), stopped over in New York for three days in April on his way back home after attending the Methodist Conference held in Atlantic City.

Dr. Kim Myŏngsŏn (金鳴善) presented his research at the medical conference held in Philadelphia in May.

Mr. Pak Kyŏngho (朴慶浩) has reportedly been invited to be a professor in the Music Department of Ewha College (梨專) and will be returning home soon.

Dr. Yun Sŏngsun (尹誠淳) received a Doctor of Philosophy degree in Education from American University in Washington.

Mr. Allen, who was stationed in Kyŏngsŏng (京城) as the U.S. Consul at the time of the Annexation of Korea (朝鮮合倂), is said to be spending the rest of his life in Toledo, Ohio, USA. He was originally a doctor who came to Chosŏn via China and served as a royal physician. His medical skills were always needed during events like the Kapsin Coup (甲申事變) and the Queen Min Incident (閔妃事變). Later, he spent his life as a diplomat, serving as Legation Secretary and Consul General, and is said to have been a friend to Koreans from beginning to end.
＜19＞

**Summary**

This article compiles short news items about Koreans active in the United States and related figures in 1932. It covers activities ranging from fundraising for public health in China (Dr. Kim Ch'angse), academic achievements (students at Michigan, Ch'oe Kyunam, J.M. No, Dr. Yun Sŏngsun), professional presentations (Dr. Kim Myŏngsŏn), religious and cultural work (Miss Maekkeiri, Chŏn Cheik'ŏp), organizational representation (Dr. Sin Hŭng'u), and faculty appointments (Pak Kyŏngho). It also includes an update on Horace N. Allen, a former US diplomat in Korea. From a historical perspective, the article is interesting as it highlights the significant transnational activities and achievements of Koreans during the colonial period, particularly in education, medicine, and social activism within the US. It reveals an active network of Korean elites engaging with American institutions and contributing internationally, despite the limitations imposed by Japanese rule.

新聞舊聞, 米國消息片片
金昌世 박사는 中國 上海에 잇는 결핵방지회의 자금 모집차로 米國 각지에서 활동중이다. 박사는 京城 世專 출신으로 上海에서 오래동안 의업에 종사햇고 米國 볼티모어의 쨘스 합킨쓰대학에서 공중위생학의 박사학위를 얻고 모교에 와서 시무하다가 다시 中國으로 가서 中國의 공중위생운동의 一역군으로 남북각지와 南洋을 순행한 일이 잇엇다. 그의 가족은 목하 上海에 잇다.

朝鮮에 종교사로 와 잇는 「맥게이리」 양은 「크리스챤, 헤랄드」 지상에 「동방의 영광」이란 소설을 연재 중이다. 소설의 제재는 朝鮮의 생활이다.

미시간대학에는 4명의 朝鮮여학생이 재학 중이다. 그들은 高凰京, 金東駿, 金메리 외 1명이다.

안아보대학에 재학 중인 崔규남 군은 전국적 과학학회 써그마.차이의 회원으로 추천되엇다.

田제이컵 군은 加州방면에서 「극동문제」에 관한 강연여행을 하고 잇다.

듁대학의 J.M.盧 군은 朝鮮 나와서 중등학교를 신설할려고 목하 재정을 운동 중이라고.

朝鮮基靑총무 申興雨 박사는 4월달에 아틀란틱 씨티에서 열린 감리교대회에 참석하고 귀향하는 길에 뉴욕에 3일을 경유햇다.

金鳴善 의사는 5월에 필라델피아에서 열린 의학대회 석상에서 자기의 연구를 발표햇다.

朴慶浩 씨는 梨專음악과 교수로 초빙되어 近近 귀국한다고.

尹誠淳 박사는 워싱톤에 잇는 아메리칸대학에서 교육학으로 철학박사의 학위를 받앗다.

朝鮮合倂 당시 米國 영사로 京城에 주재하든 알렌 씨는 현재 米國 오하요주 톨레도시에서 여생을 보내고 잇다 한다.
氏는 원래 의사로 中國을 거처 조선에 와서 왕실의원으로 잇엇다. 甲申事變과 閔妃事變 등에 그의 의술이 항상 소용이 되엇다. 뒤에 그는 공사관 서기로 총영사로 외교관 생활을 지냇고 자초지종 조선인의 친구엿다 한다.
＜19＞
