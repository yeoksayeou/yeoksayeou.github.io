The Reincarnation of Nongae, the Reason for Discontinuation
						Kim Tongin

I have decided to cut short the serialization of "The Reincarnation of Nongae" (논개의 환생), which was being published in this magazine, leaving it unfinished (未完).

The original plan for "The Reincarnation of Nongae" was to have Nongae (논개)—who, during the Japanese invasion in the year Chŏngyu (정유년 왜난; 1597), embraced a Japanese general at Ch'oksŏngnu Pavilion (촉석누) and threw her body (몸) into the Namgang River (남강)—reincarnate in Kyŏngsŏng (경성) in the year 1932, and through this reincarnated Nongae, observe and critique the current state of affairs in Chosŏn (조선). Up until the June issue of this magazine, I wrote according to the original plan. However, starting with the third installment in July, the author unavoidably decided to discard the original plan. This is because, sitting in present-day Chosŏn, even if I were to write according to the original plan, it would be utterly impossible to have it published in print (活字化)... Not only that, but I am in such an unfree situation that I lack the liberty even to tell the readers today the outline of that original plan. In other words, present-day Chosŏn as reflected in the eyes of the righteous Kisaeng Nongae (의기 논개) is something that you, the readers, can each imagine only in your own minds, but it seems impossible to publish it in writing, as readers likely cannot fail to guess. At this point, the author abandoned the original plan and attempted, through Nongae, a superficial observation (피상적 관찰) that could sufficiently be published in print even under this current system (制度). What was published in the July and August issues was precisely that. However, the pen of the author, having discarded the original plan into which he had poured his energy, had already lost its passion. Try as I might, the author's pen refused to move. The author's mind resisted setting up a new plot. Thus, having abandoned the original plan, the author wielded the pen aimlessly, not knowing what to write. Now, the author has decided to throw down that irresponsible pen. I apologize to the readers who have been reading and to the editor who has been publishing this, but frankly, I am fed up with continuing to wield an irresponsible pen. Along with discontinuing "The Reincarnation of Nongae" henceforth, I also hereby cancel the continuations published in the July and August issues of this magazine. And so, I lay down this penitent pen, waiting for an opportunity to someday write a new "Reappearance Chapter" (재세편) that would follow that "Reincarnation" (환생) story. <75>

**Summary**

Kim Tongin announces the abrupt discontinuation of his serialized work, "The Reincarnation of Nongae," explaining that the original plan—to have the historical figure Nongae critique 1932 colonial Chosŏn—was impossible to publish under the current censorship regime ("system"). He attempted a more superficial version but found he lacked the passion after abandoning his true intent and felt the resulting work was irresponsible. He apologizes to readers and the editor, cancels the previous two installments, and hopes for a future opportunity to write a true continuation. This article is revealing about the pervasive censorship in colonial Korea and its direct impact on literary creation. Historians would find it interesting as a primary source documenting authorial self-censorship and the constraints on expressing critical views of the colonial reality, even through fictional or historical allegories.

論介의 환생, 중단하는 까닭
						金東仁
본지에 싯든 「논개의 환생」은 미완「未完」인채로 끊어버리기로 작정하엿다.
본지 「논개의 환생」은 정유년 왜난에 촉석누에서 왜창을 쓸어안꼬 남강에 봄을 던진 논개를 1932년도에 경성에 환생케 하여 그 환생한 논개로 하여금 지금의 조선의 정경을 보고 비판케 하려 하든 것이다. 본지 6월호까지는 원 플란에 의지하여 썻다. 그러나 7월로 제 3회에 이르면서부터 작자는 할 일 없이 원안을 내여버리기로 하엿다. 지금의 조선에 앉어서는 원안대로는 비록 쓴다 할지라도 도저히 활자화(活字化) 할 수는 없겟으므로... 뿐만 아니라 그 원안의 대략 조차 지금의 독전에게 말할 자유가 없느니만치 부자유한 처지이다. 말하자면 의기 논개의 눈에 비최인 지금의 조선은 독자 제위가 각각 당신네의 마음뿐으로 상상할 수는  잇지만 글로서 발포할 수는 도저히 없을 것임은 독자도 짐작 못하실 것이 아니겟다. 여기서 작자는 원안을 버리고, 지금의 이 제도(制度)아레서도 넉넉이 활자화 할 수 잇을만한 피상적 관찰을 논개을 통하여 시험해 보려 햇든 것이다. 7월, 8월호에 실은 것이 즉 그것이다. 그러나 정력을 부엇든 원안을 내여버린 작자의 붓은 이미 정열이 식엇다. 쓰려도 쓰려도 작자의 붓은 음즈기기를 거부하엿다. 작자의 머리는 새로 플롤을 세우기를 저월하엿다. 이리하여 원안을 포기한 작자는 무엇을 써야할찌 아지 못하면서 되는대로 붓을 놀리든 것이다. 지금 작자는 그 무책임한 붓을 내던지기로 결심하엿다. 읽으시든 독자 제위와 실어주시든 편즙인에게는 죄송하나 책임 없는 붓을 그냥 놀리기는 사실 질쭝이 난다. 장차 「논개의 환생」을 중지함과 동시에 본지 7월, 8월 두달의 게속까지 아울러 취소한다. 그러고 그 「환생」 서편에 게속될 새로운 「재세편」을 언제 쓸 긔회가 이르기를 기두르면서 이 사죄의 붓을 놓는다.＜75＞
