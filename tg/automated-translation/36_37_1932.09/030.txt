Monthly General Knowledge Test
Submissions Welcome (However, please enclose answers too)
1 point per question, 5 points to pass, 8 points for honors

1. Who is the author of the play "A Doll's House"?

2. What does "Tamhap" (談合) mean?

3. How many counties (郡) are there in Chosŏn?

4. Name three cities in Chosŏn where streetcars are installed.

5. Name the host city for this year's British Empire Conference.

6. What are the "Nazis"?

7. For the discovery of what principle is Dr. "Einstein" famous?

8. For what is Ch'oe Sŭng-hŭi famous?

9. Does the raw material for rubber come from plants or minerals?

10. What is the meaning of the English word "modern"?

(Answers on page 31) <5>

**Summary**

This article is a short general knowledge quiz from the September 1932 issue of the magazine *Tonggwang*. It tests readers on a range of topics including Western literature, current terminology ("Tamhap," "modern"), Korean geography and infrastructure, international politics (British Empire Conference, Nazis), science (Einstein), famous Korean figures (Ch'oe Sŭng-hŭi), and basic material knowledge (rubber). For historians, the quiz offers a glimpse into the knowledge considered "common sense" or desirable for educated Koreans during the colonial period, reflecting an awareness of both local administrative facts and global events, figures, and concepts. The inclusion of a contemporary Korean dancer like Ch'oe Sŭng-hŭi alongside international figures is also noteworthy.
