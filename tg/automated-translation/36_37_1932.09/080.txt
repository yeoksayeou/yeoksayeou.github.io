News and Old News, News from the Korean Student Community in America

Recent news from the Korean student community in America, extracted from the 『North American Student Newsletter (北米學生 通信)』, is as follows.

Student Conferences
The 7th Eastern Conference of the Federation of Korean Students in America (留米學生聯合會) was held in New York City on June 4th and 5th. The conference chair was the Federation's Vice-Chair, Ms. Kim Maria (金마리아). In the discussions, under the guidance of Dr. Kim Toyŏn (金度演) of American University, Mr. Han Sŭngin (韓昇寅) of Columbia University, and Mr. Pi Wai Hŏ (삐,와이,허) of the University of Pennsylvania, there was a debate titled "Methods for the Economic Improvement of Korea."

The speakers at the banquet were Reverend Yang Kyesam (梁桂三), the General Superintendent of the Korean Methodist Church who is visiting America, and Dr. Kim Ch'angse (金昌世) of Johns Hopkins University. In addition, Mrs. Kim Pessi (金베씨) of Chicago, Professor Ŏm Yang (엄孃) of Ewha College, Dr. Yi Sŭngman (李承晩), and Mrs. K'imbŏllan (킴벌란) are said to have participated in the program.

The Midwestern Conference was set to open in Chicago from June 24th, with the discussion topics reportedly being:
"Korean Nationalism"
"Korean Socialism"

Student Association Officers
The officers for the Student Federation for 1933 were reportedly elected as follows:
Vice-Chair: Kim Maria (金마리아)
General Secretary: Han Sŭngin (韓昇寅)
Corresponding Secretary: Chŏng Ilhyŏng (정일형)
Secretary/Recorder: Kim Ŭnsŏk (김은석)
Head of Religious Affairs: Kang Il (강일)
Head of Social Affairs: C. Yi (C, 李)
Head of Athletic Affairs: Kim Saehyŏng (김새형)
Head of Publications: Y. H. Chŏng (Y, H, 정)
Treasurer: Kŭresŭ Cho (그레스, 조)
Secretary/Manager: M. S. Kim (M, S, 김) <11>

**Summary**

This article reports on the activities of Korean students in the United States in 1932, focusing on two regional conferences held by the Federation of Korean Students in America. The Eastern Conference in New York discussed Korea's economic improvement and featured prominent speakers like Rev. Yang Kyesam and Dr. Kim Ch'angse. The upcoming Midwestern Conference in Chicago planned to discuss Korean nationalism and socialism. The article also lists the newly elected officers of the Federation for 1933. For historians, this piece offers a valuable glimpse into the organized activities and intellectual concerns (economic betterment, nationalism, socialism) of Korean expatriate students during the colonial period. The presence of figures like Yi Sŭngman (Syngman Rhee) and the specific discussion topics highlight the political and social consciousness within this community abroad.

新聞舊聞, 米國留學生界 소식
최근의 米國유학생계의 소식을 『北米學生 通信』에서 摘記하건대 다음 같다.

학생대회
留米學生聯合會 제7회 동부대회는 6월 4, 5 양일 뉴욕시에서 거행되엇다. 대회주석은 同연합회 부회장 金마리아양이엇고 토론에서는 아메리카대학의 金度演 박사, 컬럼비아대학의 韓昇寅군, 펜실베니아대학의 삐,와이,허 군의 지도 하에 「조선의 경제적 개선의 방법」이란 題로 토론이 잇섯다.
만찬회 석상의 연사는 도미 중인 조선 감리교 총리사 梁桂三 목사와 쩐스 합킨스대학의 金昌世 박사이엇다. 그 밖에 시카고의 金베씨 부인, 梨專교수 엄孃, 李承晩 박사, 킴벌란 부인 등이 푸로그람에 참여하엿다 한다.
중서부 대회는 6월 24일부터 시카고에서 열리게 되엇는데 토론제목은
「조선의 민족주의」
「조선의 사회주의」
의 兩題라 한다.
