**Body Text Translation:**
The Master Said

Chŏng Se-gwŏn
-Industry does not necessarily need a political background; if one only has entrepreneurial power like the Jews (猶太人) or the Chinese (中國人), one can succeed. ("Real Life" [「實生活」])

Yi Chŏng-sŏp
-The temperament of the French people (佛國民) is precisely cheerfulness. Their ideal is to reject pessimistic philosophy, praise optimistic philosophy, and seek the flourishing of life amidst the flowers of laughter. (Kyewu [桂友])

Mun Il-p'yŏng
-Attempting reforms by borrowing the strength of others, even if it stems from unavoidable, temporary expedient means (權道), is very dangerous because one can easily end up doing someone else's work. (Kyewu [桂友]) <22>

Yun Ch'i-ho
-There are three causes for the impoverishment of rural villages: 1. Land annexation by large capital; 2. The concentration of the middle class and above in cities; 3. The departure of capable young people (有爲 청년) from rural villages. (Nongmin [農民])

Kim T'ae-hŭp
-Human life must thoroughly recognize and believe in religion; denial, resistance, or rejection is impossible. ("Buddhism" [「佛敎」])

Sin Pong-jo
-To take charge of such a small part of a job that others hardly recognize its existence, and to be faithful to it, is quite difficult and rare, whether viewed from our experience or considered from human nature. (Sinsaeng [新生]) <23>

Ch'ŏn Chae-gap
-The existence of the unemployed army (실업군) was an indispensable condition for the emergence, development, and persistence of capitalism, and only with its existence did capitalist production for profit become possible. ("Uriddŭl" [「우리들」])

Kim Chun-hwang
-In all matters under heaven, bringing things to a successful conclusion (有終) is harder than starting them; it is easy to fail by one fathom in ten fathoms of water, and it is also easy to fall short by one basketful when building a nine-fathom mountain (九仞 造山). (Ch'ŏndogyohoe Wolbo [天道敎會 月報])

Kim Tong-chun
-Henceforth, the populace will not always hold blind faith in newspapers but will critically guard against and examine newspapers with sharp eyes. (Sin'gan'gan [新人間]) <24>

Yi Man-gak
-If women wear Western clothes, this pushes even women into the pit of a double life, causing them to wander lost. ("Paehwa" [「培花」])

Yi Kwang-su
-Babies are not only the flowers of human life, not only the flowers of the earth, but truly the flowers of the universe. (Yun Sŏk-chung Children's Song Collection [尹石重 동요집])

Tangsŏng (Ch'ŏndogyo Ch'ŏngudang "New Faction" [「新派」] Organ)
-We cannot help but see this time as a great period of transformation (變覆時期), as spoken of by Master Suun (水雲 선생), when the Early Heaven (先天) society passes and the Later Heaven (後天) society is about to unfold. <26>

**Summary**

This article presents a collection of aphoristic statements attributed to various Korean intellectuals and publications from 1932. The quotes cover a wide range of topics, including the nature of industrial success independent of politics, the French national character, the dangers of relying on external forces for reform, the causes of rural decline, the necessity of religion, the difficulty of humble work, the role of unemployment in capitalism, the importance of perseverance, the need for media criticism, the perceived negative impact of Western clothing on women, the celebration of children, and a Ch'ŏndogyo perspective on the current era as a transition to a new world. From a historical perspective, the piece offers valuable glimpses into the diverse concerns and viewpoints circulating among Korean intellectuals during the colonial period. Historians might find the varied commentary on economic models (Jewish/Chinese entrepreneurship vs. large capital), social issues (rural flight, gender roles), political thought (reliance on external powers), religious interpretations (Buddhism, Ch'ŏndogyo eschatology), and cultural critiques (media literacy, Western influence) particularly interesting as reflections of the complex intellectual landscape under Japanese rule.

子曰
鄭世權
-산업이란 반듯이 정치의 배경이 잇어야 하는 것이 아니요 猶太人이나 中國人과 같은 기업력만 잇으면 성공할 수 잇는 것일세.(「實生活」)

李晶燮
-佛國民의 기질은 경쾌, 바로 그것이다. 염세철학을 배격하고 낙천철학을 예찬하야 생명의 榮華를 우슴의 꽃 가운데 구하려는 것이 그들의 이상이다.(桂友)

文一平
-남의 힘을 빌어 개혁을 꾀함과 같음은 설사 그것이 부득이한 일시적 權道에서 나온 것이라 하야도 까딱하면 남의 일을 해주기 쉬우므로 매우 위험한 것이다.(桂友)＜22＞

尹致昊
-농촌피폐의 원인이 세 가지가 잇는데 1曰, 대자본의 토지겸병이요 2曰, 중산계급 이상의 도시집중이요 3曰, 有爲 청년들의 농촌이탈이 그것입니다.(農民)

金泰洽
인생은 종교를 철저하게 인식하며 믿을지어정 부인, 반항, 거절은 불가능한 일이다.(「佛敎」)

辛鳳祚
-그 자리의 존재를 다른 사람이 잘 인식도 못할 만큼 적은 부분의 일을 맡아가지고 이에 충실하기는 우리의 경험으로 보든지 인정으로 생각하든지 퍽 어렵고 드문 일이다.(新生) ＜23＞

天載甲
-실업군의 존재는 자본주의의 발생과 발전, 존속에 없지 못할 조건이엇으며 이것이 잇고서야만 비로소 이윤을 얻기 위한 자본주의적 생산을 할 수 잇게 되엇든 것이다.(「우리들」)

金俊璜
-천하만사는 시작보다 有終하기가 어려운 법이요 열길 물에 한길 못 피기가 쉽고 아홉길 造山에 한 소구리 이끄리기가 또한 쉽습니다.(天道敎會 月報)

金東俊
-今後의 민중은 언제든지 신문에 향하야 맹목적 신뢰를 갖고 잇을 것이 아니라 날카로운 눈으로 비판적으로 신문을 경계하며 검토할 것이다.(新人間) ＜24＞

李萬珏
-여자가 양복을 입는다면 이는 여자를 마자 이중생활이라는 구렁에다 씰어 넣어 해매게 하는 것이다.(「培花」)

李光洙
-아기네는 인생의 꽃일 뿐더러 지구의 꽃일 뿐더러 실로 우주의 꽃이다.(尹石重 동요집)

당성(天道敎 靑友黨 「新派」 기관지)

-우리는 이때를 水雲 선생의 말슴하신 先天사회가 가고 後天사회가 전개되려는 일대 變覆시기라고 볼 수 밖에 없다. ＜26＞
