Magazine Title: 동광 제15호
Publication Date: 1927년 07월 05일
Article Title: 古宮短詠 (Short Songs of the Old Palace)
Author: 詩牧 (Simok)
Article Type: 시 (Poem)

1
In the gentle spring breeze,
I came looking for the old Palace;
Kwanghwamun Gate is gone,
And a stone house stands tall.
Only an unfamiliar traveler
Wipes away tears and leaves.

2
Ancient Buddhist statues from Silla times
And pottery from Koryŏ days,
Though they ask us to look
At the traces praised and cherished,
Even looking, I cannot truly see;
For this, I grieve.

3
Passing before Kŭnjŏngjŏn Hall,
I followed the back gate in;
Oh, indifferent stone *haet'ae* (돌해태),
Buried and lying in the mud,
Do you know, I wonder,
Of the magnificent deeds of five hundred years?

4
In the deep pond of Kyŏnghoeru Pavilion,
The spring ice first melts,
And in the pine trees' empty pavilion,
Only magpies chatter.
Even when the wind strikes the gate,
It feels heart-rending.
March 14th
-In Kyŏngbok Palace- <10>

**Summary**

This poem, written by Simok in 1927, expresses deep sorrow and lament while visiting Kyŏngbok Palace. The poet observes the changes, such as the absence of Kwanghwamun Gate replaced by a "stone house" (likely the Japanese Government-General Building), the neglected state of artifacts and structures like the stone *haet'ae* and Kyŏnghoeru Pavilion, and feels a profound sense of loss and inability to connect with the palace's former glory. The poem captures the feeling of cultural displacement and grief under colonial rule. For historians, the poem is a poignant reflection of Korean sentiment regarding the desecration of national symbols like Kyŏngbok Palace during the Japanese colonial period, highlighting the psychological impact of colonization on cultural identity and heritage, particularly the specific mention of the missing gate and the intrusive "stone house".

古宮短詠
						詩牧
1
수수한 봄바람에
옛 宮殿 찾아드니
光化門 간 곳 없고
돌집 하나 높아 있네
낫설은 길손 하나만
눈물 싯고 가더라

2
新羅적 옛 佛像과
高麗적 陶器들은
기리고 기린 자취
보래라 하것만은
보아도 보지 못하니
그를 설어 하노라

3
勤政殿 앞을 두고
뒤문 좇아 들어가니
진흙 속에 묻혀 눕은
무심한 돌해태야
五百年 거륵한 공을
너는 알까 하노라.

4
慶會樓 깊은 못에
봄어름 처음 녹고
소나무 빈 정자에
鳥鵲만 찌적인다.
바람이 문에를 처도
애끗는 듯 하여라
3. 14.
-경복궁 안에서- ＜10＞
＜10＞
