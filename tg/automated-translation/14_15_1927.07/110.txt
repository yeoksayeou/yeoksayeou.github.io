Students Returning to Rural Villages: Vacation is Not a Time for Play, Summer Vacation Special Feature
						By Hong Pyŏng-sŏn, Rural Department, Central Christian Young Men's Association

Summer is the peak season for all things. It is also a time when people work a lot. To think of summer as a time for escaping the heat, vacationing, or playing is a big mistake. Look at the farmers in the rural villages. For them, summer is the most strenuous period of activity. Students who were studying at school will be heading back to their home villages for the break.

There can be no object for life's teacher apart from this Great Nature. Leaving behind the complex, dusty, and vexing civilized cities, what should one do upon returning to the countryside, which makes the mind and body healthy and refreshed?

Since they are students, wherever they go, they should take books to research and read. However, it seems good to get a taste of real life as well.

(一) Cooperating with Parents and Elders in Actual Farming. Farming during the summer period will not only benefit one's body but also yield significant effects for spiritual cultivation.

(二) Rural Training Centers
If one cannot directly engage in farming, one should gather children in the village who do not attend school and do not know literature, and provide them with simple educational training during the summer period.
The method for conducting summer training should be as follows:
(1) Location... Find a guest room (舍廊房) convenient for gathering, or if not, gather at a pavilion or under a tree.
(2) Period and Time
The period should be one month, and the time could be daily from 8 PM to 10 PM, or from 4 PM to 6 PM, whichever is convenient for the children (兒孩) to gather.
(3) Curriculum
Korean language (朝鮮語), Arithmetic (算術), Calligraphy Practice (習字), Singing (唱歌), Children's Stories (童話), Exercise (運動)
(4) Textbooks
The teacher will teach orally, and the children (兒孩) should be equipped with paper (조히) and pencils.<15>

(三) Youth Club (少年俱樂部)
Gather all the village youths for one or two hours during the day, organize a Club (俱樂部), play together, go mountain climbing, swim, sing, hold children's story sessions, have debate meetings, and do various interesting things for the village, such as cleaning the roads or catching insects harmful to crops.

As mentioned above, I hope that students in rural villages during the summer period will use their summer vacation most usefully for themselves and for others. It is absolutely not a vacation for playing.<16>
<15-16>

**Summary**

This article by Hong Pyŏng-sŏn of the YMCA's Rural Department argues that students returning to their rural homes for summer vacation should not waste time on leisure. Instead, they should engage in productive activities: assisting family with farming, establishing short-term schools (講習所) to teach basic literacy and numeracy to village children, or organizing youth clubs (少年俱樂部) for recreational, educational, and community service activities like cleaning roads or pest control. The author emphasizes that summer is a time for work and self-improvement, benefiting both the student and the community. For historians, the article highlights the social concerns of the educated class during the colonial period, particularly the emphasis on rural enlightenment, self-reliance, practical education, and community development, often channeled through organizations like the YMCA. It reflects a desire to modernize rural areas and instill a work ethic and sense of social responsibility in the younger generation returning from urban schools.

農村에 돌아가는 學生 休暇는 노는 때가 아니다, 夏期休暇特輯
						中央基督敎靑年會 農村部 洪秉璇
夏期는 萬物의 全盛時代다. 사람도 일을 많이 할 때다. 夏期에 避暑니 休暇니 노느니 하는 것은 큰 착오라 하겠다. 저 農村에 있는 農夫들을 보아라. 그들은 夏期 가장 많이 힘쓰는 活動時代다. 學校에 工夫하던 學生들이 방학을 당하여 자기 사는 農村으로 돌아갈 것이다.
人生의 선생은 이 大自然을 떠나고는 對象物이 없을 것이다. 복잡하고 먼지 많고 煩悶 많은 文明 都市를 떠나서 心身을 건전하게 시원하게 하는 農村에 돌아가서 무엇을 할까.
學生들이니깐 어대로 가던지 冊 가지고 연구도 하고 독서하여야 할 것이다. 그러나 實生活의 맛을 좀 보는 것도 좋을 듯 하다.
(一) 父兄과 같이 실제 農事를 協力할 일. 夏期間에 농사를 하는 것이 그 신체에 대하여 有益될 뿐만 아니라 精神 修養에도 다대한 효과를 얻을 것이다.
(二) 農村講習所
直接 農事를 할 수 없으면 그 農村에 있는 學校도 다니지 않고 文學도 모르는 兒童들을 모아서 夏期間 簡易敎育講習을 시킬 것이다.
하기 강습을 하는 방법은 如左히 할 것이다.
(1) 場所... 어대던지 모이기 좋은 舍廊房을 얻던지 그렇지 않으면 정자나 나무 밑에 모일 것이다.
(2) 期間 及 時間
期間은 1개월 간으로 하고 時間은 매일 하오 8시로 10까지 하던지 下午 4시로 6시까지 하던지 兒孩들 모이기 좋은 데로 할 것이다.
(3) 課程
朝鮮語, 算術, 習字, 唱歌, 童話, 運動
(4) 敎科書
敎師가 口述로 하고 兒孩들에게는 조히와 연필을 갖게 할 것이다.＜15＞
(三) 少年俱樂部
낮에 한 두 시간식 동내 모든 少年들을 모으고 俱樂部를 組織하여 같이 놀기도 하고 登山도 하고 수영도 하고 唱歌도 하고 童話會, 討論會도 하고 동내 일을 위하여서도 道路를 淸潔히 한다던지 農作物의 벌레를 잡아 준다던지 이렇게 여러 가지 滋味있는 일을 할 것이다.
우에 말함과 같이 夏期間에 農村에 있는 學生들은 자기를 위하여 남을 위하여 가장 有用하게 夏期休暇를 사용하기를 바란다. 결ㅎ고 노는 休暇는 아니다.＜16＞
＜15-16＞
