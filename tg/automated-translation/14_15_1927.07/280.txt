New Method for Longevity, Follow These Steps to Live 15 Years Longer

Professor Fisher (핏춰-ㄹ氏) of Yale University (에이열쓰大學) in America has listed the following 15 rules as a method to live 15 years longer. If you follow all 15 rules, you will live 15 years longer. If you follow just one rule, you might live one year longer.

1.  Wherever you live or work, circulate fresh air (공긔).
2.  Wear light and loose clothing.
3.  Go outside for some air every day, even if just a little.
4.  When sleeping, open the window to let fresh air come in freely.
5.  Breathe deeply.
6.  Never eat too much.
7.  Do not eat too much meat and eggs (닭알).
8.  Eat a balanced diet with various kinds of food.
9.  Eat slowly.
10. Make sure to have a bowel movement (大便) once every day.
11. Whether standing, sitting, or working, always keep your body straight.
12. Do not become dependent on poisonous medicines (藥品).
13. Do not get sick. (Always practice prevention)
14. Work hard when working, but rest well with a relaxed mind when resting.
15. Always keep your mind joyful and discard the habit of constantly worrying about trivial matters. <43>

**Summary**

This article presents 15 health recommendations attributed to Professor Fisher of Yale University, promising an additional 15 years of life if followed. The advice covers fundamental aspects of health including fresh air, appropriate clothing, outdoor activity, sleep hygiene, breathing, moderate and balanced diet, eating habits, regular bowel movements, posture, avoiding harmful substances, disease prevention, balancing work and rest, and maintaining a positive mental attitude. From a historical perspective, the article shows the introduction and popularization of Western (specifically American) health and longevity concepts in colonial Korea during the 1920s through print media. It reflects an interest in modern hygiene, scientific approaches to health, and self-improvement, potentially serving as a point of interest for historians studying cultural change and the adoption of Western ideas in this period.

增壽新法, 15年 더 살려면 이와 같이 행하라
미국 에이열쓰大學 교수 핏춰-ㄹ氏는 15년간 더 사는 法으로 左의 15條를 들었다. 15條를 다 지키면 15년을 더 사니까. 그 중 1條만 지키어도 1年은 더 살겠지.
1. 그대가 사는 곳, 일하는 곳. 어대나 신선한 공긔를 流通시키라.
2. 의복은 가볍고 큰 것을 입으라.
3. 매일 조곰 식이라도 문밖 바람을 쏘이라.
4. 잘 때에 窓門을 열어 신선한 공긔가 맘대로 들어오게 하라.
5. 숨은 길게 쉬라.
6. 언제나 넘우 많이 먹지 말라.
7. 고기와 닭알을 넘우 많이 먹지 말라.
8. 음식은 여러 가지를 고루 고루 찾아 먹으라.
9. 천천히 먹으라.
10. 매일 大便을 꼭 한번식 보라.
11. 서거나 앉거나 일하거나 늘 몸을 곧게 가지라.
12. 독이 있는 藥品에 인백이지 말라.
13. 병 들리지 말라.(언제나 예방하라)
14. 일할 때 힘써 일하되 쉬일 때에는 맘놓고 잘 쉬라.
15. 언제나 맘을 즐겁게 가지고 조고만 일에 걱정을 작고 하는 버릇을 내버리라.＜43＞
