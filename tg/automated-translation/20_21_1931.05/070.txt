Overseas Review

**In 1940**
Mr. Norman Bel Geddes, who previously left many achievements as a portrait painter and stage designer and is now devoted to the industrial field, predicted the state of the world 10 years later as follows:
- Ten years from now, even the predictions written below will be outdated.
- Rooftop gardens will be found in every house.
- The ultraviolet rays of sunlight will enter through closed window panes.
- The air inside rooms will be artificially freshened, and the temperature inside rooms will be adjusted from a central plant according to the season.
- Aviation speed will be 6 miles per minute.
- The work week will be 4 days, and a 6-hour workday system will be implemented.
- Women will wear short clothes, then long clothes, then short clothes, then long clothes again.
- Humans will make it rain by their own power.
- With only 2 *jŏn*, one will be able to buy world literary masterpieces.
- Through the artificial use of science, bountiful harvests will be achieved.
- Every room will have soundproofing installed.
- Ten years later, there will be cruelty and stubbornness, but also generosity and selflessness. There will be workers and citizens.
- In other words, there will still be men, and there will still be women.
- Readers Digest -

**Moon World Travel**
Austrian scientist Professor Hermann Oberth recently published research on sending mail using rockets (火箭). According to Professor Oberth's research, mail could be transported from Europe across the Atlantic Ocean to America in just 20 to 30 minutes. This rocket, 6 feet 56 inches long, uses liquid fuel, achieves a speed of 11,000 feet per second, and can reach an altitude of 60 miles. If this succeeds, the next step would be to manufacture an 8-meter long double rocket capable of reaching an altitude of over 100 miles. It is said that after 15 more years of repeated research, reaching the Moon world could also be possible.

**Kissing Ban**
At the New York Tuberculosis Prevention Health Association meeting, Mr. Basil Ibos(?) (베질, 이부스) presented a peculiar theory: "Absolutely ban kissing (接吻). Then, the tuberculosis bacteria that claim tens of thousands of lives will be eradicated." Many scholars expressed agreement.

**Ten *Ri* Per Minute** (*Ri* is approx. 0.39km)
The British fighter plane "Eagle" (독수리號) is an ideal aircraft that embodies the marvels of modern science, decidedly overwhelming other planes, with its speed said to be over 200 miles per hour.

**German-Austrian Customs Agreement**
The contents of the customs agreement established between the German and Austrian governments on March 21st are:
1.  Tariff rates towards all foreign countries shall be identical.
2.  Customs administration shall be handled separately by the two countries.
3.  Customs revenue shall be distributed by agreement between the two countries.
4.  Trade treaties with foreign countries can be made independently, but joint exchange should be pursued as much as possible.
5.  The effective period is 3 years, and it can be renounced with 1 year's notice.
6.  If opinions differ on customs administration, an arbitration committee shall be organized with 6 members each from both countries.
Regarding this agreement, France, in particular, is strongly opposing it, stating that this agreement recreates the situation before the Great War.

**Mr. Mosley's New Party Organization**
Mr. Mosley's faction, which had advocated organizing a minority ruling cabinet to implement protection for British industry, withdrew from the Labour Party and issued a statement on February 28th. This new party opposes liberalism and, at the same time, battles against protectionist policies that benefit employers at the expense of workers and consumers. The new party advocates for protectionist policies encompassing all sectors, maintenance of stable markets and social living standards, and a thorough reorganization plan incorporating all Dominions and Britain into a new economic structure.

**The Bankruptcy of Fascism**
In Italy too, with the increase in unemployment, decline in trade, successive bankruptcies, foreign debt of 9.5 billion dollars, and financial difficulties, it seems even Mussolini can do nothing against the laws of economic progress. Near Turin (도리노), graffiti saying "Give bread to the children, or else Mussolini's life" is prevalent, suggesting that Italian Premier Mussolini's fate may not be far off.<32>

**Summary**

This article from May 1931 compiles various news snippets from around the world. It includes futuristic predictions for 1940 by Norman Bel Geddes (rooftop gardens, climate control, faster planes, shorter work weeks, weather control), Hermann Oberth's rocket research aiming for transatlantic mail delivery and eventual moon travel, a call to ban kissing to prevent tuberculosis, details on a fast British fighter plane, the terms of the controversial German-Austrian customs union opposed by France, Oswald Mosley's formation of a new protectionist party in Britain after leaving Labour, and reports of economic hardship and anti-Mussolini sentiment in Fascist Italy. From the perspective of Korean colonial history, the article reveals the kinds of international news and technological/social predictions Koreans under Japanese rule were exposed to. It shows an awareness of global political shifts (rise of Fascism, post-WWI tensions, economic struggles), technological advancements (aviation, rocketry), and even social discussions (public health, fashion), reflecting Korea's connection, albeit filtered, to global currents despite its colonial status. The inclusion of predictions about future technology and social changes might have resonated with a population experiencing rapid, often imposed, modernization.

海外레뷰
1940年에는
일즉이 肖像畵家로 舞臺裝置家로 많은 공적을 남기고 지금은 工業方面에 傳心하고 잇는「노-만, 벨, 게더스」氏는 10년후 世相을 이러케 예언하엇다.
- 이제부터 10年後에는 다음에 쓰는 豫言도 舊式이 될 것이다.
- 屋上庭間은 집마다 잇게 되리라.
- 日光의 紫外線이 닫은 영창문으로 드러오게 될 것이다.
- 房안의 空氣를 人工的으로 新鮮하게 하고 時節을 따라서 房안의 溫度를 中央工場에서 調節하리라.
- 航空 速力은 1分間에 6哩.
- 1週日의 勞働日은 4日이 되고 6時間 勞働制가 되리라.
- 女子는 옷을 짧게 입고 길게 입고 또 짧게 입고 또 길게 입으리라.
- 사람의 힘으로 비를 오게 하리라.
- 2錢만 가젓으면 世界的 文學作品을 살 수 잇게 되리라.
- 人工으로 科學을 利用하야 豊年이 지게 하리라.
- 房마다 防音裝置를 하게 되리라.
- 10年後에는 殘酷, 頑固가 잇고 寬大와 無慾도 잇으리라. 勞働者와 市民이 잇으리라.
- 다시 말하면 亦是 男子도 잇고 女子도 잇으리라.-Readers Digest-

月世界 旅行
墺太利 과학자 「헬만, 오-벨드」敎授는 最近 로켓트(火箭)로 郵便物을 郵送하는 硏究를 發表하엿는데 「오-벨드」敎授의 硏究에 依하면 不過 20分 내지 30分內에 歐羅巴에서 大西洋을 건너 亞米利加에 郵便物을 輸送할 수 잇다고 한다. 이 「로켓트」의 長은 6呎 56吋인데 液体燃料를 使用하야 一秒에 1만1천呎의 速力을 發揮하고 60哩 上空에 達할 수 잇다 하며, 이것이 成功하는 날이면 第二次로 長 8米의 二重 「로켓트」를 製造하야 百哩 以上 高度에 達할 수 잇으리라 한다. 今後 15年間 硏究를 거듭하면 月世界에도 達할 수 잇으리라 한다.

키스 禁止
베질, 이부스氏는 紐育結核防止 健康協會席上에서 「絶對로 키스(接吻)를 禁止하라. 그러면 數萬人의 生命을 빼앗는 結核菌이 消滅하리라」하는 珍奇한 學說을 發表하엿는데 多數 學者가 贊意를 表하엿다.

一分間에 十里
英國戰鬪機 독수리號는 現代科學의 妙를 極한 理想的 飛行機로 斷然 다른 飛行機를 壓倒하는데 그 速力은 1時間 200哩 以上이라 한다.

獨墺關稅 協定
3月 21日 獨逸 墺地利 兩國 政府 사이에 成立된 關稅協定 內容은
一. 諸外國에 對한 稅率은 同一하게 할 것.
二. 關稅行政은 兩國이 달리 할 것.
三. 關稅收入은 兩國이 協定하야 分配할 것.
四. 外國과의 通商條約은 單獨히 할 수 잇으나 可及的 共同 交換할 것.
五. 有效期間은 3年이고 1年의 豫告로 抛棄할 수 잇음.
六. 關稅行政上 意見이 틀리면 兩國에서 各各 委員 6名式으로 仲裁委員會를 組織할 것.
이 協定에 對하야 特히 佛國은 이 協定은 大戰以前의 狀態를 再現하는 것이라 하야 極力 反對하고 잇다.

「모즐리」氏의 新黨組織
小數執政內閣을 組織하야 英國의 産業保護를 實行하자고 主張하든 「모즐리」氏 一派는 勞働黨을 脫退하고 2月 28日 聲明書를 發表하엿다. 이 新黨은 自由主義에 反對하는 同時에 勞働者 及 消費者를 犧牲하고 雇主를 利롭게 하는 保護政策에도 排戰한다. 新黨은 各 部門를 綱羅하는 保護政策, 堅實한 市場 及 社會生活 標準의 維持, 各 自治領과 英國을 新經濟經織에 包含하는 徹底的 改造案을 主張한다.

「파씨즘」의 破産
伊太利에도 失業者의 增加 貿易의 減退, 破産者 續出. 外價 95억弗 財政難으로 「뭇소리니」도 經濟的 進展法則앞에는 하는 수가 없는 모양이다. 「도리노」附近에서는 「어린애들에게 빵을 줘라. 不然이면 「뭇소리니」의 「목슴을」하는 落書가 盛行하니 伊相 「뭇소리니」의 運命도 멀지 안앗으리라 한다.＜32＞
