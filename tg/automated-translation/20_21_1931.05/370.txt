Magazine Title: 동광 제21호
Publication Date: 1931년 05월 01일
Article Title: 내가 만일 罪人이라면 (If I Were a Sinner)
Author: 靈風 許翿
Article Type: 시 (Poem)

1
If I were a sinner (罪人),
I would be one deceived by money,
Worth less than money.
A person worth less than money,
One who ignored the spirit (靈) with the flesh (肉),
A sinner who killed truth with falsehood.
If ignorance and knowledge
Are both sins,
Though I may not know how to deceive money,
Knowing well that I have been deceived by money, yet again
Knowing dependence (依賴) but not freedom (自由),
Ah, I am a biased (偏僻) sinner.

2
If I were a sinner,
I would be a sinner who does not know how to teach others.
Deceived only by what others have written,
After knowing how to learn,
Though the scheme to find my own things
And teach others should come first,
Suddenly (幡然), while knowing I have learned,
I do not know how to find sufficiently what others
Perhaps could not teach,
And teach it to others.
Still unable to unfold the dream of learning,
Ah, I am an ignorant (愚昧) sinner without creation (創造).

3
If I were a sinner,
I would be a sinner who has made enemies (怨讐).
Knowing only how to live and fight
With ease (安樂), love, happiness (幸福), luck (僥倖), joy (喜悅),
Satisfaction (滿足), refreshment (爽快), and extravagance (豪康),
But not knowing how to die
With pain (苦痛), hatred, derailment (脫線), sorrow (悲哀), contradiction (矛盾),
Anguish (煩悶), conflict (撞着), and anger (憤怒),
A sinner who knows yet does not know.
Though I do not know regret,
Still, I know well how to be self-important (自矜),
Knowing yet not knowing,
Ah, I am an arrogant (倣慢) sinner.

4
If I were a sinner,
I would be a sinner who has made thieves (盜賊).
If survival is
The highest instinct (最高本能) of growth,
For the life destined not to survive,
How can the last desperate gesture
Be punished as pretense (虛飾)?
If there was nothing made without becoming,
Not enabling the struggling state of the world (世相) to live well,
Ah, I am a stingy (吝嗇) sinner.

5
If I were a sinner,
I would be a sinner who has
Forged (僞造) the frame of the spirit (精神).
This forged frame of the spirit,
At times, daily, and yearly,
Makes beggars, the disabled, and death.
Sounds of cruelty (殘忍), violence (强暴), and injustice (無道)
Are heard chaotically (搖亂하게도) and detestably (可憎하게도).
Ah, I am a wicked (姦惡) sinner.
-From an old manuscript (舊稿)- <92>

**Summary**

The poem is a confessional piece where the speaker contemplates various ways they might be considered a "sinner." These include being subservient to money, lacking originality and failing to teach others meaningfully, embracing only positive experiences while rejecting suffering, contributing to social inequality that creates thieves, and forging a corrupted spirit that perpetuates harm. From a historical perspective, the poem reflects a deep introspection and critique, potentially mirroring the struggles and self-examination within Korean society under colonial rule. The focus on subservience (to money, to others' knowledge), the lack of creative agency ("creation"), the existence of social ills ("thieves," "beggars"), and the forging of a flawed "spirit" could be interpreted as indirect commentary on the constrained and often demoralizing conditions of the time, making it of interest to historians studying the intellectual and emotional landscape of colonial Korea.

내가 만일 罪人이라면
						靈風 許翿
1
내가 만일 罪人이라면
나는 돈에게 속아지낸
돈만도 못한
사람이 돈만도 못한
肉으로써 靈을 모르는체 한
거즛으로써 참을 죽인 罪人이외다
모르는 것도 아는 것도
모도가 罪라면
돈을 속일 줄은 모을라도
내가 돈에게 속은 줄은
잘 아을고도 다시
依賴는 아을고 自由는 모올으는
아아 나는 偏僻된 罪人이외다

2
내가 만일 罪人이라면
나는 남에게
가라치일줄을 모르은 罪人이외다
남이 쓰은것에게만 속아
배흐는 줄을 아을은 그 뒤에는
나의 것을 찾어
남에게도 내가
가라치일 꾀가 앞을 서것마는
幡然히 내가
배호은 줄은 아을면서도
넉넉히 내가 남에게는
남이 아마도
가라치지 못한 것을 찾어
가라치일 줄은 모을은
아즉도 그대로
배홈의 꿈을 푸을지 못한
아아 나는 創造가 없는 愚昧한 罪人이외다

3
내가 만일 罪人이라면
나는 怨讐를 만든 罪人이외다.
安樂과 사랑과 幸福과 僥倖과 喜悅과
滿足과 爽快와 豪康으로
자올줄만 아을과 사올줄만 아을고
苦痛과 미읍음과 脫線과 悲哀와 矛盾과
煩悶과 撞着과 憤怒로
죽을 줄은 모을으는
아을고도 모을으는 罪人이외다
후회할 줄은 모을으건마는
그래도 自矜할 줄은 잘 아으는
모을으고도 아으는
아아 나는 倣慢한 罪人이외다

4
내가 만일 罪人이라면
나는 盜賊을 만든 罪人이외다
사을자는 그것이
자람의 最高本能이라면
못사을게 되인 목숨의 사을자는
마즈막 몸부림의 손짓을
어찌 虛飾이라 罰하리까
만듬이 없는 되어지은 것이 없엇다면
못사는 世相을 잘 사을게 못한
아아 나는 吝嗇한 罪人이외다.

5
내가 만일 罪人이라면
나는 精神의 틀을
僞造한 罪人이외다
이 僞造되인 精神의 틀은
때로 날로 그리고 해마다
乞人과 病身과 죽음을 만드는
殘忍과 强暴와 無道한 소리가
搖亂하게도 可憎하게도 드올리어지나니
아아 나는 姦惡한 罪人이외다.
-舊稿에서- ＜92＞
