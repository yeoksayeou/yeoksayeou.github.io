Modern March

A 254-Year-Old Man
What is the essence of "Modern"? While the academic definition is left to scholars, elements such as "ero," "gro," "nonsense," "smart," "chic," "speed," "cutting edge," etc., are among its components. Besides these, the strange social phenomena born of the Third Period also have considerable grounds to be included. To highlight the march of "modern" as a single red spot amidst a myriad of records, here is the story of a 254-year-old man. In a rural area within the small town district of Shibaozhai (石堡塞), Kaixian (開縣), Sichuan Province (四川省), Republic of China, there lives an old man who is 254 years old this year. Hearing his history, it is as follows.
His ancestor was a eunuch official (宦臣) of the Ming Dynasty, and his family status was affluent. He had expertise (所長) in classical literature, passed the state examination (登科), and of his three brothers, two died young (早世), leaving him as the eldest.
His first wife died before he was 40, and his second wife died in the year he turned 80. He had two sons and one daughter. Although his spirits were still robust in the year his second wife died, he did not remarry for fear of being ridiculed (恥笑) by people.
From the year he turned 100, he developed a desire to worship Buddha (拜佛), reading Buddhist scriptures (佛書) and making seated meditation (靜坐) his daily routine (課程). By this time, his children had all grown up, so he entrusted all household affairs to them and did not concern himself with anything.
He ate three meals daily, cooking them himself (親手 烹調), eating only vegetables (채소); he did not consume meat (육류) at all. When eating, he chewed for a long time, so it always took over an hour.
He has already seen up to his ninth-generation descendants (九代孫), but they all live together in one hall (一堂) without having separated households (分家), forming several dozen families within a single household (一門戶內). On every holiday or ancestral rite day, the nine generations of ancestors and descendants gather together in one hall to eat and drink (餐飮). Although nine generations live together, they are extremely harmonious (화목), and there has never once been a conflict (충돌). His entire household is sincere and lives a rural life.
Even today, at over 200 years old, his gait (步履) is still robust (健壯), allowing him to walk 100 *ri* a day on foot without feeling fatigue. Besides seated meditation and chanting Buddhist prayers (靜坐念佛), he enjoys taking walks in the outskirts (교외) leaning on his walking stick (手杖). He says that he has rarely been ill since birth (生來) and has not often encountered sorrow (憂愁).

Airplane Suicide
With a photo of his lover inscribed, "My beloved Tsuyako, Farewell forever. On this path flying through the sky, I only pray for your happiness," a cutting-edge youth threw himself from an airplane flying at 1,000 meters above ground, leaving this world full of regrets. The reason: a broken heart (失戀). The airplane was a Japan Air Transport Company passenger plane bound for Ōsaka, which departed from Tachikawa, Tōkyō at 50 minutes past noon [Note: likely an error, likely meant PM or AM, given the discovery time] on March 6th. His body was reportedly discovered at 4:30 PM on the same day in Kawachi Village, Mie Prefecture.

Graduation Cancellation Curiosity
At Chŏnju Agricultural School, it is reported that five graduates had their diplomas, which had already been given, confiscated and their graduations canceled because they allegedly delivered subversive (불온) fiery speeches (열변) during the graduation farewell party.

Elementary School Teachers' Advance
At Nishiōra Village Elementary School in Kasa District, Kyōto Prefecture, the principal and all teachers reportedly resigned *en masse*, submitting their resignation letters (사표) to the village authorities (村當局). As a result, 150 students (生徒) had no choice but to take a leave of absence (休學).
The reason: absolute opposition to a 20% salary cut. This is a tragedy of the Third Period.

Gambling Bill Passed, Divorce Freedom
As a result of the passage of a gambling bill (賭博法案) in the Nevada State Assembly (洲會) in the United States (米國), Reno City is said to be extensively building (盛築) gambling houses to attract (誘引) the world's gamblers (賭博軍). In Reno City, one can file for divorce (離婚訴訟) after residing there for only three weeks, and it's said one can enjoy pleasures (享樂) of 100 percent "ero," surpassing Monaco. <57>

**Summary**

This article presents a collection of news items under the theme "Modern March." It contrasts the story of a 254-year-old Chinese man living a simple, traditional, and harmonious life with several contemporary incidents reflecting perceived "modern" issues: a dramatic airplane suicide over lost love, the cancellation of students' graduations for expressing potentially subversive views, an elementary school faculty resigning *en masse* over pay cuts, and the legalization of gambling and easier divorce in Reno, Nevada. The juxtaposition highlights anxieties and negative aspects associated with modernity—individualism leading to despair, political tension, economic hardship ("Third Period tragedy"), and perceived moral decline—contrasted with an idealized, stable past embodied by the long-lived man. For historians, the article offers insights into how "modernity" was perceived and framed in colonial Korea during the 1930s, showcasing social anxieties, the impact of global events (like news from the US), colonial education tensions, and economic struggles affecting even educators.

모-던行進曲
二五四歲 老人
「모던」의 本質은 如何? 學術的 定義는 學者에 맡기거니와 「애로」 「그로」 「넌센스」 「스마-트」 「시-그」 「스피-드」 「尖端」 等等等等이 그 要素중의 하나이다. 이밖에 第三期가 나은 社會 奇象도 한목 끼워 줄 상당한 근거가 잇거니와 萬錄叢 中에 一点紅 「모-던」행진을 선명히 하기 위하야 이것은 254세된 老人의 이야기. 中華民國 四川省 開縣 石堡塞라는 적은 市鎭境內 어떤 鄕間에 한 노인이 잇으니 금년이 254세이다. 이제 그의 역사를 들으면 이러하다.
그의 先人은 明朝의 宦臣이요, 家勢는 富饒하엿다. 그는 舊文學에 所長이 잇어 壯元에 登科하고 형제 3人 중 2人은 早世하고 이이는 長兄이다.
그의 初娶妻는 40전에 죽고 再娶妻는 80세 되던 해에 죽엇다. 그는 2男1女를 두고 後娶妻 죽은 당년에도 기분은 아직 건장하나 사람의 恥笑를 살가바서 다시 娶妻를 아니 하엿다.
100세되던 해부터는 拜佛할 생각이 나서 佛書를 읽다가는 靜坐하는 것으로 일상의 課程을 삼엇다. 이때에는 자녀들도 다 장성하엿음으로 가사는 다 자녀에게 마끼고 일절을 不關하엿다.
그는 매일 三食하되 親手 烹調하고 채소를 먹을 뿐이요 육류는 일절 드리지 아니 하엿다. 그는 밥먹을 때에 오래 씹어 먹는 고로 매양 일시간 이상이 걸리엇다.
그는 이미 九代孫까지를 보앗으나 다 一堂에 동거하고 분가한 일이 없고 一門戶內에 수십 가정을 일우엇다. 每節日이나 祭日에는 九代 祖孫이 다 一堂에 모여 餐飮하며, 九代가 동거하되 심히 화목하야 충돌된 일이 한번도 없엇다. 그의 온 집안은 성실하고 농촌 생활을 하고 지난다.
그는 200여세 된 오늘에도 步履가 아직 健壯하야 도보로 日行 100里를 하되 피로를 느끼지 아니하며 靜坐念佛하는 외에는 手杖을 집고 교외에 산보하기를 즐긴다. 그는 生來에 병난 일이 별로 없고 憂愁에 부닥긴 일이 많지 안타 한다.

飛行機自殺
『사랑하는 「츠야고」야
永遠이 잘 잇어라.
空中에 나는 行路
그대 행복 빌 뿐이다.』
라고 쓴 愛人의 사진을 품에 안고 千「메돌」 상공에 나는 비행기에서 몸을 던져저 한 많은 이 세상을 떠난 尖端靑年이 잇다. 理由는 失戀. 비행기는 東京 立川 3월 6일 오후 50分發 日本航空輸送會社 大阪行 旅客機. 시체는 同日 오후 4시반 三重縣 河內村에서 발견하엿다고 한다.

卒業取消珍曲
全州農業學校에서는 졸업생이 송별회 석상에서 불온한 열변을 토하엿다는 이유로 졸업생 다섯명에게서 주엇든 졸업장„ 빼앗고 하고 난 졸업을 취소하엿다고 한다.

小學敎員의 進出
京都府 加佐郡 西大浦村 小學校에서는 校長 이하 敎員全部가 同盟하야 村當局에 사표를 제출하고 生徒 150명은 休學할 수밖에 없엇다고 한다.
이유는 봉급 2割減에 절대 반대. 第三期의 비극이다.

賭博法案通過, 離婚自由
米國 네바다洲 洲會에서 賭博法案이 통과한 결과 레노市에서는 도박장을 盛築하고 天下의 賭博軍을 誘引하리라 한다. 레노市에서는 3주간만 거주하면 離婚訴訟을 제기할 수 잇고 「에로」 100파센트의 享樂도 누릴 수 잇는 것이 「모나코」以上이라고. ＜57＞
