**Body Text Translation:**
Our Will
						Ch'unwŏn

Let us not lie. Let us forget deceit.
Even if our tongues are cut and we become mute, even if our breath stops and we die,
Would we ever lie or deceive again?

If others do well, let us rejoice as if it were our own deed.
Even if they face misfortune or error, shall we hate them though we grieve?
Among our own people (同族), let us love deeply.

What is this body of mine? It will die someday.
Shall I dwell on joys and sorrows? It is all but a dream.
This humble life, let me dedicate it to our people (겨레).

Do not grieve over the Present (現在). Can you not see what lies ahead in the Future (將來)?
Do not rely on others. The only one to act is myself.
Let us hope for the Future (將來) and unite with one mind (一心團結).

The mushroom lasts but a day, the Toon tree (椿나무) five hundred autumns (五百春秋).
Can we expect a Ten-Thousand-Year Plan (萬年之計) to be realized in an instant (一旦)?
If we just persevere steadily, there will be a day of achievement.

What we must do is neither this nor that.
Nor is it all the things the world clamors about.
Say that it is only this: cultivating the Individual (個人) and Unity (團結).

For how many decades have we planted trees (남) without roots?
Stop the futile talk (空論) of building a house without laying a Foundation (基礎).
Though you say you are busy (밭브다), first cultivate strength.

What is strength? The strength of the Individual (個人), the strength of Unity (團結).
Strong Individuals (個人) firmly bound in great Unity (團結),
When that time comes, precisely then, great deeds will naturally be accomplished. (Ch'unwŏn) <1>

**Summary**

This poem by Ch'unwŏn (Yi Kwangsu) is a call for moral fortitude (truthfulness), national unity, self-sacrifice, hope, self-reliance, perseverance, and the cultivation of both individual capability and collective strength among Koreans. It urges readers to look beyond present hardships towards a future built through steady effort and deep bonds among the Korean people (동족, 겨레), emphasizing that true progress requires strong foundations and unified action, not superficial efforts or reliance on others. For historians, the poem reflects the nationalist sentiment prevalent during the Japanese colonial period, focusing on internal strengthening and unity as a path forward for the Korean people. Written by Yi Kwangsu, a major yet controversial literary figure, it showcases the emphasis on self-cultivation and national consciousness promoted by some intellectuals under colonial constraints, avoiding direct anti-colonial confrontation while advocating for national empowerment.

우리의 뜻
						春園
거짓말 마사이다. 속이는 일 잊사이다
혀 끊어 벙얼어도, 숨 멎어 죽사와도
거짓말 속이는 일을 다시 하올 우리리까

남이 잘 하옵거든 내 한 듯이 깃소리라
불행 잘못해도 슳어한들 미워하랴
도모지 동족끼리는 사랑깊게 하리라

내 몸이 무엇이오, 한 때에는 죽을 것이
고락을 혜오리까 한바탕 꿈이로다
조고만 목슴이나마 겨레 위해 바치리라

現在를 슳어마소, 將來 앞에 못보는가
남을 믿지마소, 하올이는 나뿐일세
우리는 將來 바라고 一心團結하오리라

버슷은 하로라도 椿나무는 五百春秋
萬年之計를 一旦에야 바라리까
꾸준히 하여만 갈진댄 이룰 날이 잇으리다

우리가 하올 일이 이도 아니 저도 아니
세상이 떠드는 일 그것도 다 아니로다
個人과 團結을 기룸 이뿐이라 하시오

뿌리없는 남을 심어온지 멫十년고
基礎 안논 집을 세울 공론 그만하소
밭브다 하옵길래로 힘을 먼저 기르소

힘이란 무엇인고 個人의 힘 團結의 힘
힘 가진 個人이 굳게 뭉친 큰 團結이
이는 때 바로 그 때에 큰 일 절로 일리라.(春園) ＜1＞
