Magazine Title: 동광 제35호
Publication Date: 1932년 07월 03일
Article Title: 短詩一束 (A Bundle of Short Poems)
Author: 李石山
Article Type: 시 (Poem)

I. A Life Like a Flame

Just one moment,
Even if it's such a short life,
A life like a blazing flame.
Oh, I want to live like that!

II. A Madman's Scream

Sound the drum!
All will respond!

Strike it!
It will shatter!

Run!
You will arrive!

Oh, the temptation of a worthy life!

III. That Silence

Being still,
Like a shadow, but
If only fire touches it, it explodes—
The silence of that bomb!

IV. The True Form of Melancholy

The melancholy that sometimes bites my heart,
Does it seep in from this place?
Uncontrollable like the wind,
A black shadow, persistent like a venomous snake!
—From an Old Manuscript— <94>

**Summary**

This collection of four short poems by Yi Sŏksan expresses intense emotions and desires. The poems touch upon the longing for a passionate, impactful life, even if brief ("A Life Like a Flame"), a fervent call to action and breakthrough ("A Madman's Scream"), the potential for explosive force hidden beneath quiet stillness ("That Silence"), and the pervasive, inescapable nature of melancholy ("The True Form of Melancholy"). From a historical perspective, these poems, written during the Japanese colonial period (1932), are interesting for their raw emotional intensity. Historians might find the themes of constrained energy threatening to explode ("That Silence," "A Madman's Scream") and the underlying sense of despair or angst ("The True Form of Melancholy") reflective of the socio-political atmosphere and the suppressed spirit of the time. The desire for a meaningful, albeit short, life could also be interpreted in the context of resistance or national consciousness.

短詩一束
						李石山
一. 불꽃같은 삶
오직 한순간
그 짧은 생앨지라도
作熱하는 불꽃같은 삶
오 나는 그러케 살기 원한다!

二. 狂人의 絶呌
북을 울리라
모다 응하리라

두들겨라
부서지리라

달려라
다다르리라

오 가치잇는 삶의 유혹이어

三. 그 침묵
잠잠하기
그림자같지만
불만 다으면 폭발하는
폭탄의 그 침묵!

四. 憂鬱의 正軆
때로 나의 마음을 깨무는 우울
이레 부로터 스며드는 것일까?
바람과 같이 것잡을 수 없고
추근추근하기 독사와 같은 검은그림자!
―舊稿에서― ＜94＞
