Student Life March
Summer is July, July is summer vacation – As soon as vacation starts, I'll head to the mountains, the waters, back to my beloved hometown. But hold on. They say the examination system has changed and been revised to an assessment (考査) system, but still, preparing for the semester exams brings sweat and drowsiness.
Anyway - the Student Life March introduced here, composed of the two parts 1. Girls' School Review (女學校 評判記) and 2. Hurrah for Our School (우리학교 만만세), is the mischief of a 10-minute break. But in student life, what time is more meaningful than a 10-minute break, and in life, what period is better than student days?
1. Girls' School Review is a view of girls' schools as seen by male students. Being young and full of vigor, it's not impossible that there might be some playful words, but no matter how much they boast, when they actually face a female student, they are innocent misters whose faces turn red for no reason. It's a review without malice, so don't take offense, perhaps some "self-reflection" (一省) would be good.
2. Hurrah for Our School is the pride of one's own school. Let's re-recognize the value of our school and make our student life meaningful.
Thus, let's enrich and give meaning to our student life. This is the reason (所以) for publishing the Student Life March in this magazine. We express our gratitude to the students (學生諸君) who agreed with this plan (期圖) and sent in their manuscripts.
This time, it was limited to some boys' schools within the city, but subsequently... a Boys' School Review will also be published, and we plan to continue publishing the Student Life March. We request (仰請) students of all middle schools and below throughout Chosŏn (全朝鮮), both male and female, to see the separate regulations at the end of this text and submit many contributions. (One Reporter) <76>

Characteristics and Atmosphere (氣風) of Girls' Schools as Seen Through the Eyes of Male Students - (Appendix) Popularity Poll of Girls' Schools in the City
Kyŏngsŏng Imperial University... Ch'ŏlsaeng
A. Ewha Women's College (梨花專門) - Even considering the "handicap" of it being a professional school, it's extravagant (奢侈). The Girls' High School seems to lack vitality.
Sukmyŏng (淑明) - Their focus on appearance catches the eye.
Generally, female students lack a girlish, "innocent" quality and adopt the attitude of noble ladies (귀부인 然), which is a flaw (欠點).
B. I like the liveliness of Ewha Women's College.

Higher Commercial School... Kongjŏngan (公正眼 - Fair Eye)
A. Ewha Women's College - Stripping away the substance, they only imitate the Western shell (겁질), so their spirit floats in the air.
Ewha Girls' High School (梨花女高) - Has the same flaw as Ewha Women's College but possesses a cheerful charm (快活味); their style is number one.
Kyŏngsŏng Childcare (京城保育), Chungang Childcare (中央保育) - Need to cultivate a child-like heart (童心).
Tongdŏk Girls' High School (同德女高) - Relatively beautiful; more obedient (順) than cheerful.
Sukmyŏng Girls' High School (淑明女高) - The military-style uniform (軍隊服) with a hat is very awkward to look at. Obedient (溫順).
Kyŏngsŏng Girls' High School (京城女高) - Neither particularly good nor bad; makes one imagine an intellectual (인테리) housewife.
Girls' Commercial School (女子商業) - The school song is good; it would be better if discipline (風紀) improved.
Chinmyŏng Girls' High School (進明女高) - Conservative (保守的) and thus diligent.
Chŏngsin (貞信), Paehwa (培花) - Christian influence, gentle (溫厚), old-style housewife type (舊式家庭婦人型).
B. I like Tongdŏk Girls' High School.

University Preparatory Course... H-saeng
A. Ewha Women's College, Ewha Girls' High School - Too American style (아메리카 風), but encouraging the wearing of colored clothes (色服着用獎勵) is O.K. Kyŏngsŏng Girls' High School, Tongdŏk Girls' High School, Chinmyŏng Girls' High School, Sukmyŏng Girls' High School have a continental atmosphere (大陸的 氣風) and don't seem to show feminine temperament (여성적 氣質).
Paehwa Girls' High School (培花女高), Kŭnhwa (槿花), Chŏngsin Girls' High School (貞信女高) have the feeling of nuns (修女). Girls' High School (女高 - likely referring to Kyŏngsŏng Girls' High School again or generally), Girls' Commercial School seem simple and honest (質朴).
B. Shall I say Ewha Women's College?

Pharmaceutical College... Kim T'aesaeng
A. Ewha's (梨花) liveliness.
Paehwa's (培花) discipline. <77>
Girls' Commercial's (女商) frugality.
Chinmyŏng's (進明) appearance (姿色).
Sukmyŏng's (淑明) academics (學業).
Girls' Commercial's (女商) vigor.

Buddhist College... Sonsaeng
A. Ewha Women's College - Seems conceited (자만심).
Chinmyŏng Girls' High School - Has strict discipline and is lively.
Sukmyŏng Girls' High School - Lively.
Tongdŏk Girls' High School - Puts on too many airs (점잔을 뺀다).
Girls' Commercial School - A bit extravagant.
Kyŏngsŏng Childcare, Chungang Childcare - Seems dignified (으젓해 보인다).
B. Chinmyŏng Girls' High School looks good.

Chungdong School... Sŏsaeng
A. Sukmyŏng Girls' High School - Has the best attire and a modest atmosphere (氣風).
Chinmyŏng Girls' High School - Atmosphere is good.
Kyŏngsŏng Girls' High School - Seems to go sightseeing often. There's never a time I go to the theater and don't see students from this school. The atmosphere is a bit Westernized (洋風).
Tongdŏk Girls' High School - Generally modest. Seems like they study a lot.
Ewha Girls' High School - Too high-collar/fashionable (하꾸라이). Has an air of being stuck-up (건방진 感). In Japanese, "sharē" (샤례 - stylish/dandy).

Chungdong School (中東學校)

3. General Ondal (溫達將軍) Hong-kun (洪君) - When this gentleman acts like Ondal in his youth during the Silla period, even Keaton (키튼), the king of smileless comedy (無笑喜劇王), would split his sides laughing (抱腹絶倒).
3. The school friendliest with ours might be Chungang School (중앙학교). We've been close for many years through sports like tennis and basketball, and many teachers who were at Chungang have come to our school.

Central Buddhist College (中央佛敎專門)
1. Our school's pride - The main building located in the mountain forest and the medicinal spring (藥水) in the back garden (後園).
2. Our school's famous things/people
1. A monk student from Pongwŏnsa (봉원사) famous for his large head (頭大).
2. A student from T'ongdosa (통도사) in South Chŏlla Province who acts 100% like a monk (승려).
3. A Christian (기독교신자) student whose appearance (외양) is that of a monk.
3. Friendly schools include our sibling schools Posŏng Kobop (보성고보), Myŏngsŏng Girls' Industrial School (명성여자실업학교), Hwagwang School (和光學校), ETC. - (Sonsaeng)

Pharmaceutical College (藥學專門)
1. Our school's points of pride - The vigorous spirit of young men. Producing "chemists" (케미스토). Strong teams in rugby (럭비) and tennis.
2. Our school's famous things/people
1. Ammonia gas (암모니아까스). 2. Mountaineering team (등산대). 3. Short people.
3. The school friendliest with ours is the Pharmacy Department (藥學科) at Tōdai (東大 - Tokyo Imperial University?), where many of our seniors are.
4. Rival schools (競爭校) exist only as job rivals (취직 경쟁교) in the same "category" (카데고리). - (Kim T'aesaeng)

Kyŏngsŏng Imperial University Preparatory Course (京城帝大豫科)
1. Our school's points of pride - Freedom (자유) 100%, Academics (학업) 95%, Sports (스포츠) 80%.
2. Our school's famous things/people - 1. Long Hair Club (長髮俱樂部), 2. Asceticism Club (修道俱樂部).
3. The school friendliest with ours is the Kyŏngsŏng University Main Course (京城大學本科).
4. There is no rival school (對敵하는 경쟁校) to ours in all of Chosŏn. - (H-saeng)

Kyŏngsŏng Higher Commercial School (京城高等商業)
1. Our school's points of pride - Cheerful character (쾌활한 氣品), Round cap (丸帽).
3. Rival school in sports is the Medical College (醫學專門學校).

Kyŏngsŏng Imperial University (京城帝國大學)
1. Our university's pride - The university library (대학도서관), currently holding over 370,000 volumes (藏書), increasing by over 20,000 volumes annually. Especially the collection from the former Kyujanggak (舊奎章閣藏本), numbering over 160,000 volumes, is a distinctive collection (特色잇는 藏書) of this university (本學).

**Summary**

This 1932 article from *Tonggwang* magazine presents a survey of student life in Kyŏngsŏng (colonial Seoul). It features male students' impressions and critiques of various girls' schools, covering aspects like fashion, atmosphere, perceived intellect, and adherence to tradition versus Western influences. The second part allows students from different boys' schools (secondary and tertiary) to boast about their own institutions' strengths, famous figures, friendly schools, and rivals, particularly in sports. The tone ranges from playful and judgmental (in the girls' school reviews) to proud and competitive (in the boys' school self-portraits).

For historians, this article offers a fascinating glimpse into the social dynamics, cultural values, and inter-school relationships within the Korean student population under Japanese rule. The critiques of girls' schools reveal contemporary male perspectives on female education, modernity ("Western shell," "American style"), and traditional roles ("old-style housewife type"). The boys' school sections highlight the importance of sports rivalries (especially basketball and soccer), institutional pride (libraries, specific programs), student self-governance, and connections to Japanese institutions (like Kyŏngsŏng Imperial University and Kyushu Imperial University), reflecting the complex realities of colonial education and youth culture. The casual mention of Japanese loanwords and comparisons also underscores the cultural milieu of the time.

學生生活行進曲
여름은 7월, 7월은 여름방학 - 방학만 되면 산으로 물로 그리운 내 고향을 찾아 돌아가리라. 그러나 말마오. 시험제도는 변하야 考査제도로 改正되엇다하지마는 역시 학기시험준비에 땀이 나고 조림이 오오.
각설-여기에 소개하는 1. 女學校 評判記 2. 우리학교 만만세의 2部曲으로 된 학생생활 행진곡은 10분간 휴식시간의 장란이다. 그러나 학생생활에 잇어서 10분간 휴식시간보다 더 뜻잇는 시간이 어데 잇으며 인생생활에 잇어서 학생생활보다 더 좋은 시절이 어데잇으랴?
1. 여학생 評判記는 남학생이 본 여학교觀이다. 젊은 혈기라 다소 장란의 말이 없을 법도 하지 않지마는 제아무리 뽑낸다 하여도 막상 여학생을 면대하면 공연히 얼굴이 뻘개지는 無邪氣한 미스터라 악의없는 評判이니 그리 허물말고 「一省」이 어떨가 한다.
2. 우리 학교 만만세는 내 학교의 자랑이다. 우리 학교의 가치를 再인식하고 우리 학생생활을 意義잇게 하자.
이리하야 우리 학생생활을 윤택하게 하고 뜻잇게 하자. 이것이 학생생활 行進曲을 本誌에 揭載하는 所以니 이 期圖에 찬성하야 원고를 보내주신 學生諸君에게 감사를 마지안는다.
이번은 市內一部 남학교에 限하엿으나 이어서... 남학교 評判記도 실리고 학생생활 行進曲을 계속 揭載하려고 하니 本文末 別記 규정을 보고 많이 投稿하여 주기를 全朝鮮남여中等 以下 학교학생 諸君에 仰請하는 바이다. (一記者)＜76＞
