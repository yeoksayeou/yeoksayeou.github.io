Reading Room (讀書室)
*Numerical Study of Korea* (數字朝鮮硏究), Vol. 3
By Yi Yŏsŏng (李如星) and Kim Seyong (金世鎔), Co-authors

The third volume of this valuable work, the first of its kind in Korea (朝鮮), has been published. The contents of this edition include "Financial Transactions Between Korea (朝鮮) and Foreign Territories," "An Analysis of the Korean (朝鮮) Tax System," "The Inside Story of the Monopoly System," "The Problem of the Unemployed," "Trends in Thought Crimes," and "A General Overview of the Religious World." Except for the final chapter, all the topics starkly reveal the state of Korea (朝鮮) engulfed in the machinery of capitalism, thus making them all the more interesting to us.

When we introduced the first and second volumes, we mentioned that their content was merely a collection of various numbers, but we feel this statement requires some explanation. Why? Because this book is by no means like an almanac that merely lists numbers from beginning to end. Of course, it is true that even a simple listing of numbers can imply a unique perspective to the reader depending on the order and manner of the listing. The effort of Messrs. Kim and Yi primarily holds its unique characteristic in classifying scattered statistical figures under appropriate headings, and this alone represents an achievement for which we should be grateful. However, they certainly did not stop there but added sharp interpretations or explanations within the permitted scope.

When we say this book is a listing of statistics, apart from the meaning above, it simply means that it is not a systematic research paper on the reality of Korea (朝鮮). Of course, it is true that the statistics collected under each heading and their explanations are internally unified under a consistent philosophy. However, each individual study is still an independent small research paper, and the book as a whole cannot be called a single unified thesis. Nevertheless, we do not disregard the achievements of the two authors. Perhaps under our current circumstances (due to the inadequacy of materials or the lack of freedom of publication), it might be impossible to write a more unified critical thesis than this. For the time being, we may have no choice but to imply the whole issue by discussing only partial problems. We have no choice but to be satisfied with the semi-processed product that the two gentlemen have provided.

As they have already published the third volume and are said to be preparing the fourth, we cannot help but express our utmost respect for their steady efforts. At the same time, it is certain that this series will form a unique highlight in the history of Korean (朝鮮) publishing and also become a significant achievement in the history of Korean (朝鮮) culture. We believe that the efforts of the two gentlemen, though perhaps not immediately recognized, will surely be rewarded in the future (if not materially). (Price 60 chŏn, Published by Segwangsa (世光社) at 42 Hwa-dong, Kyŏngsŏng-bu. Distributor Chinch'an Kyŏngsŏng (振贊京城) 1923, Number 2) <122>

**Summary**

This article reviews the third volume of "Numerical Study of Korea" by Yi Yŏsŏng and Kim Seyong, published in 1932. The reviewer praises the book for meticulously compiling and classifying scattered statistics on crucial socio-economic issues in colonial Korea, such as finance, taxes, monopolies, unemployment, thought crimes, and religion. While acknowledging the authors' insightful commentary within permissible limits, the reviewer notes the work is a collection of individual studies rather than a single, unified critical thesis. The reviewer attributes this limitation potentially to censorship ("freedom of publication") and lack of materials, yet highly values the book as a significant "semi-processed product" and a landmark contribution to Korean publishing and culture, anticipating future recognition for the authors' efforts. For historians, the review is interesting as it reflects the intellectual climate under Japanese rule, highlighting both the drive to document colonial realities through data and the constraints faced by Korean scholars in offering overt political or economic critiques. The specific topics covered in the reviewed book (finance, tax, monopoly, unemployment, thought crime) provide direct insight into key areas of colonial administration and its impact.

讀書室
數字朝鮮硏究 제3집
李如星·金世鎔 共著

朝鮮에서 처음으로 되는 이 귀중한 著書의 第3輯이 나아왓다. 이 편의 내용은 『朝鮮과 外地와의 金融往來』 『朝鮮租稅制度의 解剖』 『專賣制度의 內幕』 『失業者問題』 『思想犯趨勢』 『宗敎界 總觀 등』으로 되어잇다. 최종의 一章을 除하고는 全休의 제목이 자본주의의 기계 속에 陷沒된 朝鮮의 姿態를 적나라하게 나타내는 것이니만치 一層 우리에게 흥미를 주는것들이다.
우리가 第1輯, 2輯을 소개할 때에 그 내용이 各種數字의 집합에 불과하다고 하엿거니와 이 말은 좀 설명을 要한다고 생각한다. 웨 그러냐하면 이 책은 결코 數字의 羅列에 終始하는 年鑑類의 그것이 아니라는 점이다. 물론 단순한 數字의 나열이라 하드라도 그 나열의 순서와 방식에 따라 특이한 관점을 讀者에게 암시할 수 잇는 것이 사실이다. 金, 李 兩氏의 노력은 첫째로 散在한 통계數字를 적당한 제목하에 분류한데 그 특이점이 잇고, 그것만으로도 우리가 감사해야될 업적을 나타내고 잇다 할 것이다. 그러나 그들은 결코 이에 그친 것이 아니라 허용된 범위내에서 예리한 해석 又는 설명을 加註하고 잇는 것이다.
우리가 이 책을 통계의 나열이라고 하는 것은 以上의 의미를 떠나서 一步進하야 朝鮮의 현실相에 대한 계통적 연구논문이 아니라는 뜻에 불과하다. 물론 그 個個의 제목하에 수집된 통계와 및 그 설명이 一貫한 철학하에서 內在的으로 통일되고 잇는 것은 사실이다. 그 個個의 연구가 아직은 各其 독립한 연구小논문이요 책 전체가 일개의 통일된 논문이라고는 할 수 없다. 그러타고 우리가 兩氏의 업적을 등한시하는 것은 아니다. 혹 우리의 현실사정下에서는(재료의 未汲한 관계로 또는 發表자유의 관계로) 이 以上 통일된 비판논문을 써내기가 불가능할지도 모른다. 우리는 당분간 오직 부분문제를 論難하므로써 全休문제를 암시할 수 밖에 없을지도 모른다. 우리는 兩氏가 제공한 반 가공품에 만족할 수 밖에 없다.
이미 3輯을 내고 또 第4輯을 준비중에 잇다고 하거니와 우리는 兩氏의 꾸준한 노력에 絶大의 敬意를 表하지 않을 수 없다. 동시에 이 叢書가 朝鮮出版史上에 一 異彩를 이루고 또 朝鮮文化史上에 一收穫이 될 것은 확정된 사실이라고 할 것이다. 兩氏의 노력이 당장에는 모르나 장래에 반듯이(物質的으로는 아니라도) 갚음받을 때가 잇을 것을 믿는다. (定價60전 京城府 花洞 42번지 世光社 發行 振贊京城1923 二番)＜122＞
