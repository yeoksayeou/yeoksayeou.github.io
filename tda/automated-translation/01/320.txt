기사제목	해뜨는 나라의 兵士를 讚美함 (Praising the Soldiers of the Land of the Rising Sun)
필자	伊太利首相 뭇소리-니
기사형태	논설 (Editorial/Opinion Piece)

해뜨는 나라의 兵士를 讚美함
						伊太利首相 뭇소리-니

Comrades! A day of solemn decision in Italian history, and a day that will set a new path in the history of the European continent, has arrived. The two nations, Germany and Italy, bound even more closely by the cooperation of steel, together with brave Japan, have today entered a state of war with the United States, and the Japan-Germany-Italy Tripartite Pact has become a military alliance. Right now, around the flags of these three nations, 250 million people, anticipating all victory in their hearts, are forming ranks. Neither the Axis nor Japan desired an expansion of the conflict. However, a single tyrant of democracy, lacking fairness, deceived his own people through a series of provocations, desired war, and thus prepared for it daily with devilish persistence. The terrible blow dealt to the United States military in the vast waters of the Pacific demonstrates the might of the soldiers of the Land of the Rising Sun in the East.

I declare to you all that it is a privilege for Italy to fight alongside Japan.

Today, the three nations of Japan, Germany, and Italy possess high morale and formidable means of war, guaranteeing victory and also guaranteeing the establishment of a just peace for humanity and the construction of a New Order. Italians have risen again. We will achieve victory.<157>

**Summary**

This article is a statement by Italian Prime Minister Mussolini, published in early 1942, celebrating the formation of the military alliance between Japan, Germany, and Italy and their entry into war against the United States following the attack on Pearl Harbor. Mussolini praises the Japanese military's strength, frames the war as a response to American provocation ("tyrant of democracy"), expresses confidence in the Axis powers' eventual victory, and reaffirms the goal of establishing a "New Order." From the perspective of Korean colonial history, the publication of this piece by a major Axis leader in a Korean magazine is significant. It shows the extent of Axis propaganda disseminated within Korea, aiming to legitimize Japan's war effort (portrayed as part of a global struggle alongside European allies) and integrate Koreans ideologically into the wartime mobilization under the banner of the "Greater East Asia Co-Prosperity Sphere" and the fight against Allied powers.

기사제목	해뜨는 나라의 兵士를 讚美함
필자	伊太利首相 뭇소리-니
기사형태	논설

해뜨는 나라의 兵士를 讚美함
						伊太利首相 뭇소리-니
戰友여, 伊太利역사에 있어서의 嚴肅한 決斷의 날, 그리고 歐洲大陸의 역사에 새로운 길을 줄 날이 到來하였다. 더욱 더 緊密히 맺어진 獨伊, 鋼鐵의 協力에 의해서 맺어진 兩國은 勇敢한 日本과 함께 今日 米國과의 交戰狀態에 들어갔으며, 日獨伊 3국동맹은 軍事同盟으로 되였다. 지금이야말로 이 3국기의 周圍에는 모든 勝利를 가슴에 期한 2억5천만의 사람들이 隊列을 짓고있다. 樞軸도 日本도 紛爭의 擴大를 欲求하지는 않았다. 그리나 公正을 缺한 單 한 사람인 民主主義의 暴君이 一聯의 挑發行爲에 의해서 自國의 人民을 欺瞞하면서 戰爭을 欲求하고 따라서 每日 惡魔와 같은 執拗로써 準備를 하여온 太平洋의 廣大한 水域에서 米國의 軍隊에 준 무서운 一擊은, 東方의 해뜨는 나라의 兵士의 威力이 如何하다는 것을 示하고 있다.
余는 諸君에 對해서 伊太利가 日本과 함께 싸우는 것은 特權인 것을 言明한다.
今日 日獨伊 3국은 旺盛한 士氣와 무서운 戰爭手段을 가지고 있으며, 勝利를 保障하고 있고 또한 人類에 있어서의 正當한 平和確立과 新秩序建設을 保障하고 있다. 伊太利人은 다시 일어섰다. 우리들은 勝利를 狀²得할 것이다.＜157＞
