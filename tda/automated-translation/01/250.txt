A Brief History of Marshal P'ap'un Who Shoulders Thailand
						By Han Minhun

Previously, Thailand was the "ham" in a "sandwich." Squeezed between the bread of Britain and France, unable to move – this was the reality of the Land of the White Elephant.
The first step in Thailand's new beginning as a truly independent nation, making progress, started with the bloodless revolution of 1932. And the driving force of this revolution was indeed the youth stratum of Thailand, centered around Tuan, P'ip'un, and Songk'ŭram. That Thailand took on the form of a modern nation is entirely due to the efforts and passion of Mr. P'ip'un. Leading the Thai people, who possess the listlessness characteristic of tropical peoples and appear quite idle at first glance, and calling for reform in a corner of the East and West, Mr. P'ip'un's path was truly a path of thorns. Today's Thailand has come into being only after overcoming many difficulties alongside this Mr. P'ip'un.

Born in the Buddhist country of Thailand, Mr. P'ip'un received his elementary education in a temple, following the customs of the country at that time. In this country, where there is one monk for every 65 people, it is not particularly unusual that a future Marshal of the Army and Navy received education in a temple. After finishing elementary education, he entered the Military Academy at age 12, completed six years of study, and was immediately commissioned as an officer. Thereafter, he studied at the Artillery School and the Army War College, and finally went to study abroad in France.
This period of study in France was a time that provided a turning point in Mr. P'ip'un's life. His character as a nationalist sprouted entirely during this time abroad. At that time, just after World War I, four young Thai men were studying in Paris. It was indeed during this time that he formed a firm pact with Mr. P'urajitt'ŭ, the current Minister of National Defense; Mr. P'urom, the Vice Minister of National Defense; <142> Mr. Ujittŭ, the Minister without Portfolio; these men who are striving for the construction of a newly rising Thailand, assisting Mr. P'ip'un. The words "National Self-determination, Rise of Small Nations" could not but resonate in the hearts of the young Thais. They pledged to realize a "Land of Freedom, People of Freedom."
Returning after finishing his studies in France, concealing an iron resolution in his heart, Mr. P'ip'un became the Inspector General of Army Artillery, and subsequently held key posts in the National Defense Forces, gradually solidifying his position within the military. Meanwhile, he organized the general youth stratum with Mr. P'urajitt'ŭ as a leader, watching for an opportunity to realize reform.
Finally, the time came. On June 24, 1932, rallying behind the Commander-in-Chief of the National Defense Forces, P'ia P'ahon, they rose up to overthrow the royal autocracy and establish constitutional government. The complacent dreams of the royalty and aristocracy, who had monopolized politics, amassed enormous wealth, and indulged in being a semi-colony of Britain, were shattered, and they finally surrendered.
From then on, Thailand has trodden a path of steady improvement. Under Mr. P'ip'un's leadership, the opposing forces were swept away, and taking advantage of France's surrender, the long-standing objective of recovering lost territories was achieved.

However, now the Great War for the Liberation of East Asia has broken out in the Pacific. The future of Thailand, still like a child, will likely be fraught with difficulties. Nevertheless, with Thailand now joining hands with Our Country (我國 - Japan) and declaring war on the US and Britain, and before the burning patriotic passion of Marshal P'ip'un, who shoulders the destiny of Thailand as one wing of the East Asia Co-Prosperity Sphere, a bright tomorrow will surely dawn.<142>

**Summary**

This 1942 article by Han Minhun profiles Plaek Phibunsongkhram (referred to as P'ap'un), portraying him as the driving force behind Thailand's modernization and independence from Western influence. It details his education, key role in the 1932 revolution overthrowing the absolute monarchy, and his leadership in consolidating power and recovering territory from France. The article concludes by praising Thailand's decision to ally with Japan ("Our Country") against the US and Britain in the Pacific War, framing it as a step towards a bright future within the Greater East Asia Co-Prosperity Sphere under P'ap'un's patriotic leadership. From a Korean colonial history perspective, it's revealing to see a Korean author writing positively in a Japanese-controlled publication about an Asian nation joining Japan's imperial project against Western powers. It reflects the propaganda narratives of the time, positioning Japan as the liberator of Asia and figures like Phibunsongkhram as nationalist heroes aligning with this vision, potentially highlighting the complex pressures and perspectives within colonial Korea.

泰國을 걸머진 파푼 元帥의 略史
						韓民薰
이전의 泰國은 「샨드윗찌」의 「햄」이었다. 英佛의 빵에 끼워서 움지기지 못했던 것이 이 白象의 나라의 實狀이었었다.
이름뿐이던 獨立國 타이가 새로운 發足을 遂行하여 躍進의 제 일보를 내여드딘 것은 1932년의 무혈혁명에서 시작된다. 그리고 이 혁명의 원동력이야말로 「투안」, 「피푼」, 「손끄람」을 중심으로 한 泰國의 靑年層이었다. 泰國이 近代國家의 形態를 가춘 것은 전혀 피푼氏의 노력과 열정에 의한 것이라고 할 것이다. 熱帶人 特有의 無氣力, 一見 도무지 怠慢하게 보이는 泰國人을 引率하여 東西의 一角에 革新을 부르짖은 피푼氏의 길은 實로 가시의 길이었다. 今日의 泰國은 이 피푼氏와 함께 많은 困難을 넘어서 겨우 생겨난 것이다.

佛敎國 타이에 出生한 피푼氏는 當時의 이 나라의 習慣에 따라 初等敎育을 寺院에서 받었다. 65인에 한 사람의 僧侶가 있는 이 나라에서는 後日의 陸海軍 元帥가 寺院에서 敎育을 받었다고 해도 별로 異常할 것이 없다. 初等敎育을 끝맞후고 12세에 陸軍士官學校에 入學하여 6개년의 修學을 끝낸 후 즉시 士官에 任官되었다. 그 후 砲兵學校, 陸軍大學에서 工夫하고 드디어 佛蘭西로 留學하였다.
이 佛蘭西 留學時代야말로 피푼氏의 生涯에 한 개의 轉機를 준 時代였다. 그의 國家主義者로서의 性格은 전혀 이 留學時代에 싻헜다. 당시 제 1차 大戰 직후의 巴里에는 4명의 젊은 泰國靑年이 留學하였었다. 現國防大臣 푸라짓트氏, 國防副大臣 푸롬氏, 無任所大臣＜142＞ 우짓드氏, 피푼氏를 도와 新興泰國의 建設에 邁進하고있는 이들과 굳은 盟約을 하게 된 것은 실로 이 시대였다. 「民族自決, 弱小國家의 擡頭」라고 하는 말이 젊은 泰人의 마음에 울리지 않을 수 없었다. 그들은 「自由의 나라, 自由의 百姓」을 實現하려고 盟誓하였다.
佛蘭西留學을 끝마추고, 鐵石의 決意를 가슴에 秘藏하고 돌아온 피푼氏는 陸軍砲兵監이 되었으며, 다시 國防軍의 要職을 歷任하여 漸次로 軍의 속에 그 地步를 굳게했다. 그리고 一方 푸라짓트氏를 지도자로 한 一般 靑年層을 組織하여 革新의 實現의 機를 엿보고 있었다.
드디어 때는 왔다. 1932년 6월24일, 그들은 國防軍의 總帥 피아·파혼氏를 擁하여 王族專制의 打破, 立憲政治의 確立을 完成하려고 蹶起하였다. 政治를 獨占하고 巨萬의 富를 쌓아가지고 英國의 半植民地에 甘醉되어 오던 王族과 貴族의 倫安의 꿈은 깨뜨려저서, 그만 屈服하고 말었다.
그로부터의 泰國은 一路 向上의 길을 밟고있다. 피푼氏의 統率下에 反對勢力의 一掃가 이루어젔고, 다시 佛蘭西의 降復을 타서 年來의 失地回復의 目的을 達成하였다.

그러나 지금은 태평양에 東亞解放의 大戰이 버러젔다. 아직 어린아이와 같은 泰國의 前途는 多難할 것이다. 그렇지만 이번 泰國이 我國과 손을 잡고 米英에 대하여 宣傳을 布告했으며, 東亞共榮圈의 一翼으로서의 泰國의 運命을 걸미진 피푼元帥의 불타는 愛國의 情熱앞에는 반드시 明朗한 明日이 찾어오리라.＜142＞
