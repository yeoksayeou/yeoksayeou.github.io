Overseas Information

**The Comedy of the US President's Family**

Regarding the family of the war instigator President Rujŭbelt'ŭ, a reader's letter published in the newspaper called the "Sik'ago Tribune" tells the following story. Mrs. Rujŭbelt'ŭ recently said in Rosŭaenjaelsŭ, "The nation demands combatants; those who do not take up arms are not citizens," but it seems she did not think about the Rujŭbelt'ŭ family's situation at that time. Why is that? The current president did not serve in the Spanish-American War (米西戰爭) when he was 17, nor in World War I (第1次 世界大戰) when he was 32. The president's father, Cheimsŭ Rujŭbelt'ŭ, also did not serve in either the Mexican-American War (米墨戰爭) or the American Civil War (南北戰爭). Furthermore, the president's grandfather, Isak'ŭ, despite having great wealth (富), did not gain experience in battle. The president's great-grandfather, Cheimsŭ, also did not try to enlist in the battles of Wasingt'on and K'oŭnuol.

**British Ambassador Harip'aksŭ's Incident**

When the British Ambassador to the US, Harip'aksŭ, came out of a hotel in Ttaet'ŭroit'ŭ, he was suddenly surrounded by women from an anti-British demonstration. On the flags (旗) they held were written slogans such as "Oppose the dispatch of American youth to Kurop'a (歐羅巴)" and "Isolate Harip'aksŭ from American politics." Harip'aksŭ panicked and jumped into his car, but the women pounded on the windows with their fists, threatening him.

**The US and UK Deceiving India's Kkandi**

Regarding a profile in the American magazine "Ruk'ŭ" which opined that Kkandi held the view that post-war India would be satisfied with the current situation under British Empire (英帝國) rule, Mahat'ŭma Kkandi announced in the newspapers that this article had no basis (根據) whatsoever. According to Kkandi, there can be no satisfaction without the complete independence (獨立) of India. Moreover, he said he had never even heard the name of the magazine "Ruk'ŭ."

**The Number of Islands in the Pillyup'in**

Calculating all the large and small islands of the Pillyup'in shows the truly large number of 7,083 islands (島). <85>

**Summary**

This article compiles several pieces of "overseas information" painting the US and UK in a negative light. It accuses President Roosevelt and his family of hypocrisy regarding military service, reports on an anti-British protest targeting the UK ambassador in the US, and highlights Mahatma Gandhi's refutation of a US magazine claim, emphasizing his demand for India's complete independence. It concludes with a factual note on the number of islands in the Philippines. From the perspective of Korean colonial history, this article is significant as it clearly reflects the wartime propaganda disseminated by the Japanese empire in its colonies. It aimed to undermine the legitimacy and popular support of the Allied powers (US and UK) by focusing on internal dissent, alleged hypocrisy of leaders, and anti-colonial sentiment (in India) against the British, thereby bolstering the narrative of Japan's "Greater East Asia Co-Prosperity Sphere" as a liberating force against Western imperialism.

海外情報
米國大統領 一家의 喜劇
전쟁도발자 루즈벨트 대통령 一家에 대하여 「시카고·트리뷰운」이라는 신문에 게재된 독자의 투서에 다음과 같은 이야기가 있다. 루즈벨트 부인은 최근 로스앤잴스에서 『국가가 전투원을 요구하는데, 총을 잡지않는 자는 국민이 아니다』고 말했지만 그때 그 부인은 루즈벨트 일가의 일을 생각지 못한 모양이다. 웨 그런고하니, 현재의 대통령은 17세때의 米西戰爭, 32세 때의 第1次 世界大戰에는 출전하지 않었으며 대통령의 부친 제임스·루즈벨트도 米墨戰爭과 南北戰爭 중 아무데도 출전하지 않었고, 또 대통령의 조부 이삭크도 많은 부를 가지고도 싸움의 경험을 가지지 믓했다. 대통령의 증조부의 제임스도 또한 와싱톤과 코은우올의 전투에 응모하려고 하지 않었다.

英國大使 하리팍스의 逢變
英國의 駐米大使 하리팍스가 「때트로이트」의 여관에서 나온즉 反英떼모의 부인들이 막 둘러쌋다. 그들이 가진 旗에는 「米國청년의 歐羅巴 파병 반대」「하리팍스를 米國정치로부터 隔絶하라」등의 표어가 씨어저있었나. 하리팍스는 당황하여 자동차에 뛰여들어갔는데, 그 여자들은 주먹으로 창을 두둘기며 하리팍스를 脅威하였다.

印度 깐디를 欺瞞하는 米英
米國잡지「룩크」의 인물평론에 깐디는 戰後의 印度는 英帝國 지배의 현상에 만족하고 있다는 의견을 가지고 있다고 평한데 대하여 「마하트마·깐디」는 이 기사는 아무 根據가 없는 것이라고 신문에 발표하었다. 깐디가 말한 바에 의하면 완전한 印度의 독립이 없이는 만족은 있을 수 없다고 한다. 더욱 그는 「룩크」라는 잡지의 이름조차 들은 일이 없다고 한다.

比律賓의 島嶼數
比律賓의 大小섬을 모두 계산하면 그 수가 7,083島라는 실로 큰 數字를 보이고 있다. ＜85＞
