Literary World

The Passing of Mr. Yi Hyosŏk
Mr. Yi Hyosŏk, the writer who wielded his talented pen through long and short stories such as 'When the Buckwheat Flowers Bloom,' 'Pollen,' and 'Pig,' sadly passed away on May 25th at the age of 36 after suffering from an illness for about 20 days. The entire literary world cannot cease its sorrow and condolences.

The Return of Mr. Yi Ŭnsang
Nosan Yi Ŭnsang, known for his elegant and beautiful poetry and prose, had hidden his traces from the literary scene for various reasons, reaching ten years of contemplation, but recently published a collection of poetry and prose, the 'Nosan Collection (鷺山文集)'.

Recent Publications
P'albong Kim Kijin is said to be publishing a new work titled 'Random Thoughts (隨想錄),' a collection of criticism, essays, and poetry, soon from a certain bookstore.
Wŏlt'an Pak Chonghwa published the essay collection 'Blue Moss Collection (靑苔集)' in late April and subsequently published the new novel 'The Eve (前夜)' recently.
Mr. Pak Kyeju is currently back in his hometown of Sŏnch'ŏn, North P'yŏngan Province (平北 宣川), to write the sequel to his full-length novel 'Record of Martyred Love (殉愛譜).'
It is said that Inmunsa (人文社) is preparing to publish collections of vernacular creative works several times a year.
P'ain Kim Tonghwan's poetry collection 'Rugosa Rose (海棠花)' was published in early May.

Writers' Recent Activities
Messrs. Chang Hyŏkchu, Yu Ch'ijin, and Chŏng Int'aek departed Kyŏngsŏng (京城) the other day as special dispatches of the Development Section (拓務課) for a cultural inspection tour concerning Manchurian (滿洲) settlers, returning in mid-June.
The novelist Ms. Ch'oe Chŏnghŭi has been working at the Kyŏngsŏng Broadcasting Station since early May.

Recommended Book Committee Members

**Summary**

This article provides news from the Korean literary world in mid-1942. It reports the death of Yi Hyosŏk, the publication comeback of Yi Ŭnsang, new works by Kim Kijin, Pak Chonghwa, Pak Kyeju, and Kim Tonghwan, and the activities of other writers, including a government-sponsored trip to Manchuria and Ch'oe Chŏnghŭi joining the Kyŏngsŏng Broadcasting Station. It concludes by listing prominent Japanese cultural figures serving on the Japan Publishing Culture Association's Recommended Book Committee, overseeing literature, art, and film under the wartime "new system."
For historians, the article offers insights into the state of Korean literature during a peak period of Japanese colonial control and wartime mobilization. The mention of writers participating in activities sponsored by colonial entities (the Manchuria trip by the 拓務課, Ch'oe Chŏnghŭi at the broadcasting station) illustrates the complex interactions between Korean cultural figures and the colonial regime. The list of Japanese committee members highlights the mechanisms of cultural control Japan implemented to guide publishing and arts according to its national policies.

文壇
李孝石氏의 長逝
「모밀꽃 필 무럽」 「花粉」 「豚」 등 장단편의 작가로써 그 才筆을 날니든 李孝石씨는 20여일의 병환에 신음타가 5월 25일, 그만 36세를 일기로 별세하고 말었는데 全文壇은 애석과 추도를 마지 않는다.

李殷相氏의 複活
流雅, 艶麗한 詩文으로 알니우든 鷺山 李殷相씨는 그간 여러가지 사정으로 문단의 外表에서 자최를 감처 사색 10년에 미첬더러니 최근에 詩文集 「鷺山文集」을 발간하엿다.

近刊諸書
八峰 金基鎭씨는 평론, 수필, 시문을 모아 「隨想錄」이란 題로 新著를 某 書肆로부터 近刊하리라한다.
月灘 朴鍾和씨는 수필집 「靑苔集」을 4월 하순에 간행하였는데 계속하여 근일 소설 「前夜」를 신간하였다.
朴啓周씨는 장편 「殉愛譜」의 속편을 집필차로 故卿인 平北 宣川에 귀향중
人文社에서는 언문창작집을 년 수차 간행코저 준비중이라고
파인 金東煥씨의 詩集 「海棠花」는 5월 초순에 발행되었다.

文人近況
張赫宙, 柳致眞, 鄭人澤의 3씨는 滿洲 개척민 문화시찰차로 拓務課 특파가 되어 전일 京城 출발, 6월 중순 귀향.
소설가 崔貞熙여사는 5월 초부터 京城 방송국에 근무
