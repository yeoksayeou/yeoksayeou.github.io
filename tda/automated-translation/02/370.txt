Publication Date: 1942년 07월 01일
Article Title: 編輯後記 (Editorial Postscript)
Author: Editorial Staff
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editorial Postscript)

May on the Peninsula (半島) was a good month that will remain in history. That is because on May 8th, there was the Cabinet decision for the implementation of the long-awaited Conscription System (徵兵制度實施). From now on, all fathers and mothers of Chosŏn have become "fathers and mothers of the military state," and all the youth of the Peninsula have become "members of the Imperial Army (皇軍)," shouldering rifles and marching side-by-side. From now on, indeed, as subjects (臣民) of the Empire of Japan (日本帝國), we must train our "spirit and physique" so that we are not left behind at this time when the nation's fortune of a thousand years is about to open up.

"Regular Use of the National Language (國語常用)" is something we must uphold most sincerely at this time. Compatriots (半島同胞) on the Peninsula who still do not know the National Language (國語), please learn it with haste. The time will come later to soar heroically to the south, to the north, and throughout the entire world, but what will you do if you don't even know the language? Please devote all your energy to learning the National Language right away. This magazine, "Great East Asia (大東亞)," will also specially establish a "National Language Course" on its pages starting next month.

Also, entering May, Governor-General Minami (南總督) and Director-General Ōno (大野總監), who were here for seven years, left Chosŏn, and the new Governor-General Koiso (小磯總督) and Director-General Tanaka (田中總監) arrived to take up their posts. At this time, having seen off and welcomed the new and former Governors-General who are like benevolent fathers (慈父), we know we must strive even harder to do our duty as subjects under wartime conditions (戰時下 臣民).

This magazine has slightly reduced its page count (頁數) starting this month. It was difficult to turn away the rapidly increasing number of devoted readers (愛讀者), so we reduced the pages and increased the circulation (部數); please understand (諒解).

Our company's acclaimed publications, starting with the poetry collection "Haedanghwa (海棠花)" by Kim Tong-hwan (金東煥), have come out in large numbers as shown in the separate page advertisement (別頁廣告). Under the current situation (時局下), paper (用紙) is again difficult to obtain (求키 어려워), making reprinting impossible (再版不能), so please order many copies before they are sold out (品切되기前).<156>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from July 1942 celebrates the recent implementation of the Japanese military conscription system for Koreans, framing it as an honorable step towards becoming true subjects of the Japanese Empire. It strongly urges Koreans to learn Japanese ("National Language") for future opportunities within the expanding empire and announces a Japanese language course in the magazine. The postscript also notes the recent change in the Governor-General and Director-General of Political Affairs for Korea and explains a reduction in page count due to paper shortages, while promoting other publications. For historians, this piece vividly illustrates the peak of wartime mobilization and forced assimilation policies in colonial Korea, showcasing the propaganda used to encourage cooperation with Japanese imperial aims, particularly conscription and language adoption. The mention of specific high-ranking Japanese officials and a Korean poet publishing under the regime provides valuable context.

編輯後記
半島의 5월은 역사에 남을 좋은 달이엇습니다. 그것은 5월 8일에 대망하는 徵兵制度實施의 閣議決定이 있었습니다. 이제부터는 조선의 부형은 모도 다 「군국의 아버지, 어머니」가 되었고, 半島의 청소년은 모도 다 총을 메고, 엇둘, 엇둘 발마처 나가는 「皇軍의 一員」이 되었습니다. 이제부터야말로 우리는 일본제국의 신민으로서 「정신과 체력」을 수련하여, 천년국운이 트려는 이때 뒤떠러짐이 없어야 할 것입니다.

「國語常用」은 이때에 있어 우리가 가장 진심으로 밧뜨러 행할 일이외다. 半島同胞, 아직 국어를 모르는 이는 하로 바삐 습득하소서, 이 뒷날 남방으로 북방으로 전세계에 웅비할 시기가 올터인데 말도 모르고 었저자고 그러심니까 어서 국어습득에 전력하셔요, 본지 「大東亞」도 내월부터 지상 「國語講座」르 특설하겠습니다.

또 5월에 드러 7년간 계시든 南總督, 大野總監이 朝鮮을 떠나가시고, 새로 小磯總督, 田中總監이 도임하셨습니다. 慈父와 가튼 新舊總督을 送迎한 이지음 우리들은 戰時下 臣民으로서의 할 일을 더욱 힘써 하여나가야 할 줄 아옵니다.

본지는 이달부터 頁數를 조금 주렸습니다. 격증하는 애독자 제씨를 물니치기 어려워 頁數는 주리고 부수를 증가시킨 것이니 양해하여주소서,

본사의 호평간행물은 金東煥시집 「海棠花」를 비롯하여 別頁廣告와 여히 많이 나왔습니다. 時局下用紙는 다시 求키 어려워 再版不能하니, 품절되기前 많이 주문하여 주옵소서.＜156＞
