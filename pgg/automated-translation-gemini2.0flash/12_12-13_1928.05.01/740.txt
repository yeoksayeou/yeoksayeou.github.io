Emperor Taizong of Tang's Eye and Emperor Yang of Sui's Chest

It is widely known that Emperor T’aejong (唐太宗) of Tang, Li Semin (李世民), invaded Ansi Fortress (安市城) of Koguryŏ, and lost an eye to an arrow from our soldiers. However, the fact that Emperor Yang (隋煬帝) of Sui was struck in the chest by an arrow when he was defeated by Koguryŏ is not well known because it is not recorded in the official history (正史). According to the Samguk Yusa, when the Sui emperor fought with Koguryŏ, one of our people concealed a small crossbow (小弩) in his bosom and followed the envoy carrying the national letter (國書) into the enemy camp. When the Sui emperor read the letter, he skillfully shot the Sui emperor in the chest with the hidden crossbow. Afterward, when the Sui army was completely defeated and retreated, Emperor Yang, looking around, said, "I became the emperor of the world and personally attacked a small country, and ended up in this state, which is truly shameful for all ages. However, the bravery of the Koguryŏ people is truly unmatched in the world." This is what was said. ＜169＞

唐太宗의 눈과 隋煬帝의 가슴
唐太宗 李世民이 高句麗의 安市城을 침범하다가 우리 군사의 살에 마저서 한 눈이 멀게 된 것은 세상 사람들이 대개 알지만은 隋煬帝 楊廣이 高句麗에 패할 때에 활살에 가슴을 마진 일은 正史에 나지 안은 까닭에 세상 사람들이 잘 알지를 못한다. 그런데 三國遺事를 보면 隋帝가 高句麗와 싸울 적에 우리 사람 중 한 사람이 小弩를 품 속에 감안이 감추어 가지고 國書가지고 가는 使臣을 따라서 적진에 갓다가 隋帝가 그 국서를 읽을 즈음에 감추엇든 小弩로 맵시 잇게 隋帝의 가슴을 쏘와 맛치엿는데 그 뒤 隋軍이 참패하야 全軍을 퇴각할 때에 廣이 좌우를 돌녀 보며 하는 말이「내가 천하의 임군이 되어 親이 小國을 치다가 이 모양이 되엿스니 실로 만대에 붓그러울 바이나 엇잿든 高句麗 사람의 용맹은 참 천하무적이라 하얏다」고 云云하얏다. ＜169＞
