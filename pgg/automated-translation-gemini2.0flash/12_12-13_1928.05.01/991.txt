We are Seeking Anecdotes of Beautiful Deeds

Regardless of whether the material is from ancient times or the present, please send us many stories about local anecdotes (美談) concerning ambition, success, loyalty, valor, chivalry, virtue, and human kindness. We will publish the best submissions in this magazine.

Submissions should be sent to the Byeolgeongeon Editorial Department.
