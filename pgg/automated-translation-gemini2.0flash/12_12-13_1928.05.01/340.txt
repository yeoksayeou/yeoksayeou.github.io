This and That
This and That
A greedy and stingy man was buying a train ticket and tried to get a discount of just five jeon.
"What are you doing? Is it worth being like that over just five jeon? Every store gives discounts on fractions."

While he was doing that, the train started to leave, whistling. The man, feeling desperate, chased after it, shouting,
"This is a disaster! Hey... hey, I'll pay the five jeon, please stop the train!" <76>

A Baffling Advertisement
A quick-witted young man was riding a streetcar. Seeing an advertisement posted above each window that read, "Do not smoke," he said, "Those damn guys! If they tell me not to smoke, I'll smoke even more. Anyway, the guy who sells tobacco is good at advertising."

A country fellow sitting next to him pointed to the sign on the other side that read, "Be careful, as it is dangerous to stick out your hands or head." The country fellow asked, "What kind of advertisement is that?" The quick-witted man replied, "Uh-huh, that? It's probably a reverse promotion (逆宣傳) by a surgeon." <76>

이것저것 다 글넛다
이것저것 다 글넛다
엇던 욕심쟁이고 인색한 사람이 차표를 사면서 작고만 닷돈을 깍거날라고 졸낫슴니다.
「무엇 그러심니까 고까짓 닷돈에 고다지 그럴 것 잇슴닛가. 어늬 상점에서나 우수리는 감해주는 걸요.」
그러는 동안에 긔차는 고동을 뛰기면서 떠나기를 시작하엿슴니다. 이 사람은 속이 달아서 쫓처나가며
「이것 큰일낫구나 여… 여보 닷돈 마자 냇게 긔차 좀 머물너 주오!」 ＜76＞

뚱딴지 광고
입 빠른 젊은 사람이 전차를 탓슴니다. 차창 위마다에「담배 먹지 마시오」하고 한 광고가 부터 잇는 것을 보고「망할 놈들 먹지 말나면 붓석 더 먹을 터니까 하여간 담배 파는 놈이 광소술은 용한데」
겻헤 안잣는 싀골 사람 하나이 저 편에
「손이나 머리를 내여 밀면 위험하니 주의하시오」라고 썻스니 저근 무슨 광고요 입빠른 사람 「으-ㅇ 그것 말임니까 아마 외ㅅ과의사의 역선전(逆宣傳)이겟지요」 ＜76＞
