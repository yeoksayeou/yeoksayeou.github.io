잡지명	별건곤 제41호
발행일	1931년 07월 01일
기사제목	新아리랑 (New Arirang)
필자	浩然堂人
기사형태	시 (Poem)

Arirang, Arirang, Arariyo
Arirang, let's play well
Where did this Arirang come from?
Everyone is playing with Arirang

Arirang, Arirang, Arariyo
Arirang, let's play well

Look at that wicked woman walking over there
The girder of Gyeongbokgung Palace (경북궁) is not enough

Even if you plant seeds, you'll harvest five bags
Why is that guy's back so wide?

A horned hat, gold buttons, and a knife-edged pair of pants
Selling ten bags of rice is just enough for that

Even the doorkeeper of a girls' school is arrogant
A student in a horned hat is standing guard

Tempura, potato noodles, and japchae in a bowl
The Amnok River (압록강) iron bridge bends and cracks

Who eats the oily white rice?
Only farts come from eating barley and millet

Don't say a thousand words
Even if I look like this, I'm a modern woman

I've sold everything this way and that
All that's left is a princess in my kidneys
