The Exact Number of the Korean Population

The preliminary report of the national census (國勢調査) conducted in Chosŏn on October 1 of last year announced the population as 21,057,969 people. However, after a detailed investigation of the reports, the completely confirmed population is 21,058,305 people, an increase of 336 people from the preliminary report. Comparing this confirmed population with the simplified national census (簡易國勢調査) of 1925, there is an increase of 1,535,360 people. Looking at this by province, Kyŏngbuk (慶北) has the highest population with 2,416,712 people, and Hamgyŏngbuk-do (咸北) has the lowest with 745,124 people. Astounding Telephone Bill

Due to the agreements between the United States and France (米佛兩國), while the U.S. representative was staying in Paris, the telephone bill for contacting the home country was, don't be shocked, a whopping 170,000 francs (approximately 13,600 won). The total call time was 7 hours and 45 minutes. It is said that this is the first time such a long call and high telephone bill have been paid between Europe and America. <17>

놀날만한 電話料
