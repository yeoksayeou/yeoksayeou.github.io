靑天白日下에 無罪釋放된 少年殺害犯 高玉丹 (Ko Ok-tan, a Juvenile Murderer Acquitted Under the Clear Sky and White Sun)

Ko Ok-tan, a Juvenile Murderer Acquitted Under the Clear Sky and White Sun
Kim Il-gu

Subsequent Story of the Pakseoksan Murder Incident

Ko Ok-tan (高玉丹), a concubine of Han Paek-won (韓百源) of Maesan-ri, Hwaseong-myeon, Cheongyang-gun, Chungcheongnam-do, was running a restaurant in Yongcheon-ri, Bibong-myeon, in the same county. On the morning of April 26, 1930, her husband, Han Paek-won, scolded her severely, saying, "You have been having an affair with a man named Ri Ki-mun (李起文) who lives in that neighborhood since long ago, and now you have promised to run away with him to Kanggyeong (江景). Do you have such bad habits?" It was said that Pak Chang-su (朴昌銖), who did errands at her house, had informed on her about her secret. She immediately called Pak Chang-su and beat him twice. Still not satisfied, she conspired with Jo Ki-jun (趙起俊), who worked as a servant in her house, to take Pak Chang-su to Pakseoksan (朴石山) and kill him. That evening, Ko Ok-tan lured Pak Chang-su to Pakseoksan, saying she was going to gather wild vegetables, and waited for Jo Ki-jun to come. Ko Ok-tan tied Pak Chang-su's neck with a towel and knocked him down, and together with Jo Ki-jun, they strangled Pak Chang-su to death. Ko Ok-tan received a 15-year prison sentence for this crime, and Jo Ki-jun received a 10-year prison sentence and were serving their sentences in prison. Half a year later, Pak Chang-su, who was said to have died on Pakseoksan, came back alive. Herein lies the problem. Chang-su, who was said to have died on Pakseoksan, came back alive, and Ko Ok-tan is serving 15 years and Jo Ki-jun 10 years of imprisonment for killing him, so what happened in the incident?

Pak Chang-su was only 16 years old at the time, and his family was poor, so he did errands at Ko Ok-tan's house to barely make ends meet. Although his family was poor, the affection between mother and son was extraordinary. Her beloved son had been brutally strangled at the hands of Ko Ok-tan, a promiscuous concubine of another man; how could she bear it? The local police station dissected the body found on Pakseoksan, and his mother also testified that it was definitely her son. The court accepted this and sentenced Ko Ok-tan to 15 years and Jo Ki-jun to 10 years. <18> For some reason, Jo Ki-jun remained in prison, but Ko Ok-tan appealed to the Gyeongseong (京城) Appellate Court, but the 15-year sentence was upheld. Thinking there was nothing she could do, she was serving her sentence in Seodaemun Prison. In the meantime, Pak Chang-su, who was said to have died, returned to his home alive after more than half a year. It is a lie-like fact that he came back alive today, when he was removed from the registry as dead and two people are imprisoned for murder. So where had Pak Chang-su, who was said to have been murdered on Pakseoksan, been all this time? After being beaten twice by Ko Ok-tan that day, he could no longer stay in that house, so he left the house without saying anything and went on a wandering journey without saying anything to his mother. He begged from house to house and did errands at a temple. Coincidentally, on the night that Pak Chang-su was beaten by Ko Ok-tan, left the house, and went missing, a boy's body was found on Pakseoksan. The police constructed a murder case claiming that it was Pak Chang-su's dead body, and that Ko Ok-tan and Jo Ki-jun conspired to kill him.

However, now that Pak Chang-su has come back alive, Ko Ok-tan and Jo Ki-jun cannot simply remain imprisoned for murdering Pak Chang-su. So, the prosecutor started working again, and the issue was brought back to court. The first trial was held at the Gyeongseong Appellate Court on the 21st, presided over by Judge Mal Song (末松) with Prosecutor Juken (酒見) present.

裁 "Is it true that you had a relationship with Ri Ki-mun?"

被 "I followed him, but I never thought of killing him."
裁 "Did you know that there was a boy's body on Pakseoksan on April 16th?"
被 "I didn't know."
裁 "Did you know that the body was Pak Chang-su?"
被 "At that time, Pak Chang-su was not seen in my house, so I suspected that it might be Pak Chang-su's body."
裁 "Does the defendant know that Pak Chang-su is alive?"
被 "I don't know if he's dead or alive."
裁 "If you didn't kill Pak Chang-su, did you kill someone else?"
被 "No, I didn't."
裁 "Then why did the defendant confess to killing him? According to the police investigation, the defendant clearly killed Pak Chang-su."
被 "The police investigation was so harsh that I had to confess, even if it was a lie. Considering the facts before and after, it seemed like I had killed him, so I had to say that I had killed him."

And the prosecutor requested an acquittal for Ko Ok-tan, and a verdict of innocence has already been delivered. Then who is the boy's body on Pakseoksan, and who is his murderer? Let's leave that as a separate matter, but where can Ko Ok-tan and Jo Ki-jun, who are unjustly accused of murder and suffering unfairly, appeal? I think the National Compensation Act (國家報償法) has not yet been finalized. How many such unfair facts are there in Joseon (朝鮮) now? But unfortunately, there is no way to uncover and defend their unfair facts one by one. <19>

磚石山 殺人事件 後聞
충남 청양군 화성면 매산리 한백원(韓百源)의 첩 고옥단(高玉丹)은 동군 비봉면 룡천리에서 음식영업을 하고 잇든 중 소화 5년 4월 26일 아츰에 그 남편되는 한백원으로부터
네가 그 둥리에 사는 리기문(李起文)이란 자와 일즉부터 간통하고 잇고 이제는 그와 함께 강경(江景)으로 도망하기로 약속이 잇다하니 그런 좃치 못한 년의 버릇이 잇느냐 하고 톡톡한 꾸지람을 드럿다. 이럿케 자긔의 비밀을 알게된 것은 집에서 심부름하는 박창수(朴昌銖)란 자의 밀고이라 하야 즉시로 박창수를 불너다 놋코 전후 두 차레나 매질을 하고 그리고도 부족하야 자기집에서 머슴사리하는 조긔준(趙起俊)과 공모하야 전긔 박창수를 박석산 우에 끌고가서 죽이기로 작정하고 그날 저녁에 고옥단은 산나물을 캐러 가자고 박창수를 달래여 박석산으로 유인하야 다리고 가서 조긔준이가 오기를 기다려 고옥단은 박창수를 수건으로 그의 목을 매어 넘어트리고 조긔준과 합력하야 박창수의 목을 졸라 맛츰내 즉이여 버리엿다는 죄목으로 고옥단은 15박년의 증녁. 조기준은 10년의 증녁을 밧고 감옥에 복역하는 중이엿다. 그후 반년이 지나서 박석산 우에서 죽엇다는 박창수가 사라서 도라왓다. 여기에서 문제는 이러난다. 박석산 우에서 죽엇다는 창수가 사라서 도라왓고 또 그를 즉이엿다 하야 고옥단은 15년 조기준은 10년의 증역 생활을 하는 중이니 대체 그 사건은 엇지된 일일가.
박창수는 그때에 겨우 16세 되는 소년이엿고 그의 가세는 빈한하야 고옥단의 집에서 심부름 해주고 겨우겨우 그날을 보내고 잇섯다. 비록 가세는 구차하다 할지라도 모자간의 정의야 남다름이 잇스랴. 자기의 사랑하는 아들이 고옥단이란 행실부정한 남의 첩의 손에서 무참히 교살되엿스니 그의 분원이 과연 엇더하엿스리요. 소관 경찰서에서는 박석산 우에 잇는 시체를 해부하고 또 그의 어머니도 이것은 분명히 내 아들이라고 립증햇다. 재판소의 판결은 그를 그대로 시인하고 고옥단에게 15년 조기준에게 10년 언도(言渡)가 나리엿다.＜18＞ 엇진 까닭인지 조기준은 그대로 복역하고 고옥단은 경성 복심법원에 불복 공소하엿섯스나 역시 일심대로 15년의 언도가 나리여 이제는 할 수 업다고 생각하고 이래 서대문 감옥에서 복역하고 잇섯다. 그러하든 중에 죽엇다고 하든 박창수가 반년이 넘은 오날에 시퍼럿케 사라서 자기 집을 차저왓다. 죽엇다 하야 민적에서 제적되고 두 사람이 살인죄로 증역하는 오날에 그가 사라서 왓다는 것은 거짓말 갓흔 사실이다. 그러면 박석산 우에서 살해되엿다는 박창수는 그동안 어듸가서 잇섯든가 그날에 고옥단에게서 두 차레나 매를 맛고는 그 집에 그냥 잇슬 수 업다하야 아모말 업시 그 집을 떠나서 자기의 어머니에게도 아모말 아니하고 방랑의 길을 떠낫다. 이집 저집에서 걸식하고 도라다니다가 어느 절에가서 심부름 해주고 지냇다. 일이 공교하게 되느라고 박창수가 고옥단에게서 맛고 집을 떠나 행방불명이 된 그날 밤에 전긔 박석산 우에는 소년의 시체가 잇섯다. 그것이 곳 박창수의 죽은 송장이요 ꊳ�옥단과 조긔준이가 공모하야 죽이엿다고 경찰은 살인죄를 구성식키엿다.
