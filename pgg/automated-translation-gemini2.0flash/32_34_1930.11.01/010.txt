Increased Harvest of 6 Million Seok
Due to the bountiful harvest, there was an increased yield of 6 million seok, but there is nowhere to sell it. The friend who used to trade until last year came with a bag the size of a child's diaper to buy it. But still, "buy it"! Seeing this, an elder of the village lamented, "With such a plentiful harvest, we are starving; this is unprecedented. It's a sign that the end of the world is near!" <1>

增收 600萬石
풍년의 덕으로 6백만석이 증수되엿스나 팔을 곳이 업다. 작년까지 거래하든 친구는 어린 아희의 기족이만한 자루를 들고와서 사자고 한다. 그러나마 『買得』으로! 이것을 보고 鄕老가 탄식 曰 「풍년에 배가 곱흐니 전에 업는 일이다. 末世가 갓가워온 증조로구나!」＜1＞
