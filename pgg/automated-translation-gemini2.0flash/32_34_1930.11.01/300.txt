Separation for Three Years
						Kim An-seo

Separation for Three Years
Ah, it's been three years since I parted with you
My longing grows stronger when the moon is bright
I look at the bright moon in vain
Thirty-six times the moon has become round

Original Poem

悠悠一別己三年
相望相思明月天
膓斷靑天望明月
別來三十六回圓
-Bai Juyi

White Heron
Is it because of my many worries that I am aging? <152>
My hair has turned completely white from worries.
Why is it that the heron by the water
Has no worries and its head feathers are white?

Original Poem
人生四十未全衰
我爲愁多白髮垂
何故水邊雙白鷺
無愁頭上亦垂絲
-Bai Juyi

Staying at Changjeong Post Station
Night deepens at the passing post station
Unable to sleep, I look towards my hometown
What can be seen in the bright moon?
Only the white sea water reaches the sky

Original Poem
夜半樟亭驛
愁人起望鄕
月明何所見
潮水白茫茫

Untitled
Your heart is the color of the evening glow
My heart is the color of white marble
Marble is always white
It's a pity the sunset fades as the day ends

Original Poem
君心如紅雲
妾思如白石
白石長**
紅雲不待夕

Yuaisa Temple
Sitting by the stream, I play leisurely
Walking around the temple looking at flowers
How welcome are the occasional songs of birds
Everywhere, springs flow <153>

Original Poem
弄日臨溪坐
花尋繞寺行
時時聞鳥語
處處是泉聲
