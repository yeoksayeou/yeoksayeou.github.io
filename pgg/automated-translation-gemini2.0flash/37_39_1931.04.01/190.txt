Increase in Print Run

Due to a shortage of the inaugural issue, we frantically increased the number of copies for this second issue. Not only "Hyeseong" (慧星), but also Pyŏlgŏn'gon (別乾坤) is incredibly popular, and because the books disappeared in just five days, we suffered greatly and posted an apologetic advertisement on various newspapers saying, "We are out of books, so please do not place any more orders." <24>

**Named Entities**

慧星	Hyeseong
別乾坤	Pyŏlgŏn'gon

大部數增加
