笑話三篇 (Three Anecdotes, Miscellany)

Kindness

A friend riding his bicycle for a while was passing through a village as night fell. He asked a young man in the village for directions, but the young man, looking very sorry, scratched his head and said he didn't know. Having no choice, he went about 20 li (里) further and finally entered an inn, settled his lodging, and was about to sleep when the young man from the village came panting after him. "Oh, aren't you the one who asked for directions in front of our village earlier?" he suddenly asked. When he replied that he was, the young man said, "I'm very sorry, but I asked my brother, and he also said he didn't know."

Funny

The owner's wife, with a very displeased look, stood in front of the kitchen door and said to the maid, "Why do you drag people all the way into the kitchen? What were you all cackling and chattering about last night? I couldn't sleep a wink!" The maid replied, "But Madam, it's because this morning we were telling the story of when you were making cookies, and everyone started laughing..."

Complaint

A young, modern woman went to a flour store and complained, "The flour I bought here last time was too hard." "What do you mean, the flour was hard...?" "Well, I made bread and gave it to my family, but they said it was so hard they couldn't eat it at all!" <89> <89-89>

親切
자전거를 타고 한참 모라가던 친구가 날이 저무러서 어느 동리를 지나다가 그 동리 젊은 친구에게 길을 무르닛가 아조 미안하다는 드시 머리를 득득 글그면서 모르겟다고 대답하는지라. 할일 업시 한 20리 더 가다가 겨우 주막에 드러서 사관을 정하고 자랴닛가 힐덕 헐덕하며 뒤를 쫏처온 앗가 동리 젊은이 「아이그 당신 아니심닛가. 악가 우리 동라 압헤서 길을 무르시던 이가」하고 별안간 대드러 무름으로 그럿다고 대답한 즉 「참 미안함니다마는 제 형에게 무러 보아도 역시 모르겟다고 하던데요.」

웃기는
주인 마누라님 한 분이 잔득 상을 찝푸리고는 부엌문 압헤 와 서서 「애 웬 사람을 부엌에까지 그리 끌어드리나. 어제ㅅ 저녁가튼 때는 무얼 가지고 그리 여러이 깔깔대고 짓거리는지 원 잠 한잠 못 잣네」하고 행랑어멈을 남으랫다. 「그럿치만 마님 어제 아침에 마님께서 과자 만드시던 니약이를 하닛가 동무들이 우서싸서...」
