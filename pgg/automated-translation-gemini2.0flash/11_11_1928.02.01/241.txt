Warning Regarding Fake Reporters

Recently, there have been frequent rumors that individuals are appearing in various regions claiming to be "Kaebyŏksa reporters" (開闢社記者) and engaging in various fraudulent activities. We hereby inform you not to be deceived by such individuals. Currently, with the exception of Mr. Chŏng Il-chang (鄭日彰君) of this company, who has returned to the company from Chŏlla North Province (全北地方), there are no reporters on local assignment.

Kaebyŏksa (開闢社)
