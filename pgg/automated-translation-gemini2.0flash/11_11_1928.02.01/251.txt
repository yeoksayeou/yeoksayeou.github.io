Local Color and Postcard Communication

Due to the abolition of the Idu (吏讀) column for this issue, it is not published, but we will continue to solicit submissions in the future, so please send us many manuscripts and write them as freely as possible.

The editorial board especially hopes that readers will submit many other folk songs, Sijo (時調), and school reports.
