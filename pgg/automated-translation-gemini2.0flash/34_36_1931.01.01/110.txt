기사제목	一問一答記4 (Questions and Answers, Part 4)
기사형태	대담·좌담 (Dialogue/Talk)

Reporter: You have continued cold water bathing and mountain climbing for over ten years; what has been the effect on your health?

Chang: The effect has been very great. The stomach ailment (胃病) and head pressure symptoms (머리 압흡 症) that used to trouble me are now gone, and I don't even know the name of something like a cold, even in winter. <51>

Named Entities
