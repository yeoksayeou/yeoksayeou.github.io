기사형태	대담·좌담 (Dialogue/Talk)

When is the happiest time of day for you as you live your daily life at the newspaper company?

The happiest time is when the newspaper is all made and I hear the sound of the rotary press (輪轉機) running. Once the newspaper meets the sound of the rotary press, that day's work is all done. <45>
