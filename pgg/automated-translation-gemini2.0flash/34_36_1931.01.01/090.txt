대담·좌담 (Conversation/Interview)

Isk Al-Bett

Reporter: What was the most joyful thing that happened while you were managing the school?

Shin: It was when our school was approved (認可). As you well know, our school has a history of over 10 years, but because everything was inadequate (不備), we had only the qualification of a training center (講習所資格) until now. After much hard work (苦心努力), we finally obtained the qualification of a school last March, and for me, there was no happier event than that. <46>
