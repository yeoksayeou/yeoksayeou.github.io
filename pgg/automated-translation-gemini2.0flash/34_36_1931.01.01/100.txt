一問一答記3,
Kuk Gi-ryeol

Reporter: I hear you like loach soup (chueotang 鰍湯), so which chueotang restaurant in Seoul is the best?

Kuk: While people say Hwadong Hwang Chueotang (花洞黃추탕) and the Brothers Chueotang (弟兄 추탕집) outside Dongdaemun are good, I think Jangchunok (長春屋) chueotang is the best. <48>

**Named Entities**

花洞黃추탕	Hwadong Hwang Chueotang
東大門	Tongdaemun
弟兄 추탕집	Brothers Chueotang
長春屋	Changchunok
鞠琦烈	Kuk Gi-ryeol
