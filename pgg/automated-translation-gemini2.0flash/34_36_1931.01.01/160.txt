Article Title: 一問一答記 9 (Questions and Answers 9)
Author: 李一 (Yi Il)
Article Type: 대담·좌담 (Dialogue/Discussion)

The reporter asks: Mr. Yi, you seem to really enjoy skate (스켓) skating. How many hours a day do you do it?

Yi replies: On days off from school, I start around nine in the morning and usually go until nine at night, so I skate for about 12 hours a day. If you were to calculate the distance I travel on the ice (氷上), it would easily be 500 or 600 li (里). <60>
