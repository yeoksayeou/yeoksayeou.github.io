The Total Korean Population Exceeds 22 Million: First Announcement of Census Results

Only the important parts of the results of the national census, which was implemented simultaneously throughout Korea on October 1 of last year, such as the number of households, the total number of men and women, and the population density (密度), were announced on December 9. According to this:
Number of households 3,987,742
Population 21,057,969
Men 10,763,230
Women 10,294,739
As such, the total population is 21,057,969. Since the racial statistics have not yet been completed, the number of Japanese and foreigners is unknown, but referring to the statistics from the end of Showa 4 (approximately 488,476 Japanese and 58,146 foreigners), if we consider it to be about 550,000, the Korean population reaches more than 20.5 million, far exceeding the traditionally called "twenty million compatriots." Comparing the statistics with the increase from the past few years, there is an increase of 1,535,240 people (an increase of 78.6 per 1,000 people) compared to the simplified national census in Taisho 14, and an increase of 1,726,809 people compared to the statistics at the end of last year. <38>

The Most Populous is Kyŏngbuk, the Highest Population Density is Chŏnbuk, the Least and Most Sparse is Hamgyŏngbuk

The most populous is Kyŏngsangbuk-do with 2,416,752 people, and the least populous is Hamgyŏngbuk-do with 745,107 people. The population density (密度) is highest in Chŏllabuk-do with 2,719.2 people per square *ri*, and lowest in Hamgyŏngbuk-do with 564 people. The average population per household is 5.3 people, but Hamgyŏngnam-do, Hamgyŏngbuk-do, P'yŏnganbuk-do, Kangwŏn-do, Ch'ungbuk, and Ch'ungnam have more than 5.4 people, while regions like Kyŏngnam, Hwanghae, and Chŏnbuk have less than 5.1 people.

1.5 Million Compatriots Residing Overseas

The Korean population living in Korea exceeds 20.5 million, far surpassing the so-called 20 million compatriots, as mentioned in the separate article. For the past 20 years, many Koreans have emigrated abroad, with the number reaching approximately 2 million. Although accurate statistics are unknown, the statistics released by the Nanjing government some time ago stated that there were 108,974 households with 1,354,685 people. Approximately 160,000 compatriots reside in Siberia, and about 20,000 in North America, Mexico, and other countries, bringing the total to 1,534,000 people. Therefore, the total Korean population living on Earth is 22,330,000.
According to this survey, the current total number of Korean households is 3,987,742, and the total population is 21,057,969. When divided by gender, there are 10,763,238 men and 10,294,739 women. Therefore, the average population per household is 5.6 people, and the ratio of men to 100 women is 140.6. Compared to the results of the simplified national census of Taisho 14, there is an increase of 266,969 in the number of households and an increase of 1,535,024 in the population. Since the above increase occurred over 5 years, the average annual increase is 307,005 people, and the average annual increase rate is slightly over 15.8 per 1,000 people. These statistics do not distinguish between Koreans, Japanese, and foreigners, but only provide the totals. In any case, the above population growth rate is comparable to the average annual growth rate of 20.8 in New Zealand and 20.1 in Australia, showing the highest rate in the world. <40> Let us try to show the recent world population growth rate on the left.

海外在住同胞百五十萬

조선 안에 사는 조선인 인구가 2천50만 명 이상에 달하야 속칭 2천만 동포를 훨신 초과하얏다 함은 별항 긔사와 갓거니와 조선인은 만근20수년래 외국으로 건너가는 사람이 만허 그 수흐가 약 2백만 명에 달한다고 한다. 정확한 통게는 모르겟지마는 얼마전 남경정부에서 발포한 통게가 10만8천974호에 135만4,685인이라 하얏스며 서백리에 거주하는 동포가 16만 명 가량, 기타의 북미(北米) 묵서가(墨西哥), 기타 각국에 약2만 명이 잇다고 함으로 그 총수가 153만4천여 명에 달하야 지구 우에 사는 조선족이 2천2백3만여 명이다.
이 조사결과에 의하면 현재 조선 總살림수는 398만 7742요, 총인구수는 2105만7969인이다. 그리고 이것을 남녀별로 볼 때는 男이 1076만3238이오, 女가 1029만4739인이다. 그럼으로 한 살림 평균인구는 5人6分이오 女百에 대한 男의 비는 140인6分이다. 이리하야 이것을 대정 14년 簡易國勢調査의 결과에 비하면 살림수에 잇서서 26만6969, 인구수에 잇서서 153만5024인의 증가다. 5년간에 右의 증가임으로 一個年의 평균증가수는 30만7005인이오, 일개년의 평균중가율은 千人에 대하야 15인8分强이다. 이 통계는 조선인, 日本人及外國人을 구별하지 안코 총계만을 들은 것이지만 여하튼 如上의 인구증가율은 新西蘭의 일년 평균증가율 20인8분 濠太利의 20인1분에<40> 버금하야 세계의 최고율을 示한다. 試하야 左에 최근세계인구증가율을 시하자.
