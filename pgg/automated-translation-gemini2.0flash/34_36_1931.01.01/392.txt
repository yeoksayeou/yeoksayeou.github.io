別件坤 第36號
1931년 01월 01일
年頭頌 (New Year's Ode, Poem)
劉道順
시

Magazine Title: 別件坤 第36號
Publication Date: 1931년 01월 01일
Article Title: 年頭頌 (New Year's Ode)
Author: 劉道順
Article Type: 시 (Poem)

As the chrysanthemum petals fall and I vaguely see tears,
The thought of eating and not working gnaws at my heart.
In this rough world of floating worries, when did the new year come?

Even with a festering heart, I stand before the new year's dawn,
The thought of swallowing others brings forth new sorrows.
Carrying a heavy burden of silence, tears fall first.

Still, hoping that I might live when I shouldn't,
Clinging desperately to the weariness of not dying,
I pour out my heart to greet the new year and rush out agitated.
---On the morning of the first day--- <150>
