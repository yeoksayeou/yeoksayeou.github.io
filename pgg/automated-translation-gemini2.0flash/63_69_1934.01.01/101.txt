Correction

Regarding the article about Mr. Incheon 森勝波 (Incheon 森勝波氏) in the sorrowful story of Chŏng Kŭn-sun (鄭艮順) published in the June issue of this magazine last year, we correct it as there are some discrepancies with the facts.

Pyŏlgŏn’gon Editorial Department <31>

Body Text Translation:

Correction

Regarding the article about Mr. Incheon Sŭngch’ampa (仁川森勝波氏) in the sorrowful story of Chŏng Kŭn-sun (鄭艮順哀話) published in the June issue of this magazine last year, we correct it as there are some discrepancies with the facts.

Pyŏlgŏn’gon Editorial Department <31>

Named Entities

鄭艮順	Chŏng Kŭn-sun
仁川森勝波氏	Incheon Sŭngch’ampa
