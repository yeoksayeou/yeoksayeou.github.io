Film Overseas Correspondence

Viewing Tax in the United States

According to the announcement by the U.S. tax authorities, the total collected amount of film viewing tax for the year 1926 (from July 1925 to the end of June 1926) was 23,276,883 dollars and 44 cents. Broken down by state, New York State leads with over 7.3 million dollars, and Illinois State with over 2.4 million dollars. However, out of the 20 million dollars, 279,177 dollars and 30 cents were not collected from permanent theaters, but from incidental businesses.
