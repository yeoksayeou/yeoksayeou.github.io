Superstitions of Marriage

In some parts of America, there is a custom of pouring money on the foreheads of the bride and groom when they come out of the church. It is said to be a wish for them to become rich!

Also, if a cat sneezes the night before the wedding, it is said to be a good match (緣分)!

In Germany, on the wedding day, they pick up several old plates and throw them on the street. If any of them don't break into pieces, it is said to be unlucky!

In Scotland, it is said to be unlucky if the bride does not cry at the wedding. If she doesn't cry that day, she will have something to cry about in the future! <45>

또 결혼식 前 밤에 고양이가 재채기를 하면 조흔 緣分이란대요!

넷날에 獨逸서는 헌 접시 몃 개를 집어내다가 길거리에다 팽게친답니다. 만일 하나라로 깨여지지 안는 것이 잇스면 불길하다고 햇다나요!

넷날 스코트랜드에서는 새색시가 혼인식에 울지 안으면 불길하다고 햇답니다. 그 날 울어두지 안으면 장래에 울 일이 생긴다고요!＜45＞
