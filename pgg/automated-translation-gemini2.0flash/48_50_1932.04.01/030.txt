To talk about the brief history of the famous figures among the ministers:
Jeong Hyo-seo (Prime Minister) - Until now, he had not emerged much in political circles, but he had once received requests for political entry from Yuan Shikai (袁世凱), Li Yuanhong (黎元洪), and Duan Qirui (段祺瑞), but he refused. A person who buried himself in his study, he single-heartedly held the thought of the Qing Dynasty's restoration (淸朝復興), and with this opportunity, he followed Puyi (溥儀) and took on a heavy responsibility in the new nation. He is 73 years old.
Jang Sik-ui (Deputy Prime Minister) - A graduate of the Japanese Army Academy. He was the chief of staff of the Jilin (吉林) Military Governor. After Zhang Zuolin (張作霖) moved to Beiping (北平), and after Zhang Zuolin was assassinated, Yang Yuting (楊宇霆), whom he believed was the boss, was shot by Zhang Xueliang (張學良). The National Name is Manzhou, and the Era Name is Datong.
From the moment the Manchurian Incident (一中事變) occurred, the so-called Manchurian-Mongolian independence issue, which was being fiercely promoted, centering on figures such as Jang Sik-ui (張式毅), Won Geum-gae (袁金凱), Hui Heup (熙洽), and Jang Gyeong-hye (張景惠), was finally materialized, and on March 1st, the establishment of the new nation was formally declared both domestically and internationally. The name of the nation was initially discussed as the Manchurian-Mongolian Federation of Provinces Republic (滿蒙聯省共和國) or the Datong State (大同國), but ultimately it was decided as Manchukuo (滿洲國), and the era name was proclaimed as Datong (大同), which is said to have taken the meaning of "Great Harmony of the World" (天下大同) from the I Ching (周易). Its territory encompasses the four northeastern provinces (東北四省) of Fengtian (奉天), Jilin (吉林), Rehe (熱河), and Heilongjiang (黑龍江), with an area of approximately 77,554 square li and a population of approximately 34 million. The national system is nominally a republic, but in reality, it is a monarchy, with the former Qing Dynasty's Emperor Puyi (宣統帝溥儀) being promoted as the head of state under the name of Chief Executive (執政), and the capital was decided as Changchun (長春).

A New National Flag with Five Splendid Colors
The new national flag (國旗) of this Manchukuo has yellow as its base with blue (靑), red (赤), white (白), and black (黑) added. Yellow symbolizes the great unity of the yellow race (黃色人種), red symbolizes **, blue symbolizes fresh energy (긔운), white symbolizes peace and ** (平和 **), and black symbolizes solidity (堅實), symbolizing the new nation.

Content of the New National Organization and Four Major Principles of National Foundation
Regarding the organization of the nation, the Chief Executive (執政) holds the highest position (like a monarch), and below it are the Legislative (立法), State Affairs (國務), and Audit (監察) branches. Furthermore, under the State Affairs branch are seven departments: Civil Affairs (民政), Foreign Affairs (外交), Military Affairs (軍政), Finance (財政), Industry (實業), Transportation (交通), and Justice (司法). The administrative districts (行政區域) are divided into ten administrative districts, with four provinces (四省) – Fengtian, Jilin, Heilongjiang, and Rehe – and six special cities (特別市) – Changchun, Fengtian, Jilin, Harbin, Rehe, and Qiqihar (齊齊合爾).
The principles of the independent nation consist of four articles:
1. The Chief Executive, as the head of state, governs Manchukuo under a constitutional system (立憲制) based on the election by all the people.
2. The politics of Manchukuo shall be based on democracy.
3. Manchukuo shall establish a constitution in the nearest future based on the general will of the people.
4. The Chief Executive's government shall be in the form of a transitional government until a formal constitution is enacted and implemented.

Leaders of the New Government
According to the decisions announced at the Changchun Conference (長春會議) on March 3, the Prime Minister (國務總理) is Jeong Hyo-seo (鄭孝胥), the Chairman of the Council (參議府議長) is Jang Gyeong-hye (張景惠), and the Chairman of the Autonomous Committee (自治委員會長) is Yu Chung-han (于沖漢). The Minister of Civil Affairs (民政總長) is Hui Heup (熙洽), the Minister of Finance (財政總長) is Jang Sik-ui (臧式毅), the Minister of Military Affairs (軍政總長) is Ma Jeom-san (馬占山), the Minister of Industry (實業總長) is Jang Yeon-gyeong (張燕卿), the Minister of Foreign Affairs (外交總長) is Sa Gae-seok (謝介石), the Minister of Transportation (交通總長) is Jeong Gam-su (丁鑑修), the Minister of Justice (司法總長) (concurring with the Speaker of the Legislative Yuan (立法院長兼任)) is Jo Heun-baek (趙欣伯), and the Speaker of the Board of Audit (監察院長) is Won Geum-gae (袁金凱). Won Geum-gae was originally a scholar who served as an advisor to Zhang Zuolin (張作霖) and briefly became the chairman of the Fengtian Autonomous Council during the recent Sino-Japanese Incident. Jang Gyeong-hye is a valiant general from a bandit background who was under Zhang Zuolin and is 60 years old this year, recently serving as the Administrative Governor of the Harbin Special District (哈爾賓特別區行政長官). Hui Heup is originally from Shenyang County (瀋陽縣) and graduated from the cavalry department of the Japanese Army Academy, currently serving as the Governor of Jilin Province, 48 years old. Yu Chung-han (于沖漢) is also from Liaoyang County (遼陽縣), Fengtian Province, and ** in Japan, a leading figure in the civilian faction at 61 years old.
Jo Heun-byeok is from Wanping County (宛平縣), Zhili Province (直隷省), a middle-aged man of 45 years old, graduated from Meiji University in Japan, earning a doctorate in law. Jang Sik-ui (臧式毅) is a direct subordinate of Yang Yuting (楊宇霆), who was assassinated by Zhang Xueliang (張學良) a few years ago, from Shenyang, Fengtian Province, and graduated from the infantry department of the Japanese Army Academy. Prime Minister Jeong Hyo-seo (鄭孝胥) is a former Qing Dynasty official with considerable scholarship, having been a teacher to Emperor Puyi (宣統帝) from an early age. (Omitted Below) <13>

國號는 滿洲, 年號는 大同
만주의 일중사변이 이러나던 즉시부터 장식의(張式毅), 원금개(袁金凱), 희흡(熙洽),장경혜(張景惠)등 일파의 인물을 중심으로 하야 맹렬이 운동하던 소위 만몽독립 문제는 그여히 구체화하야 지난 三月 一日에 정식으로 새국가의 수립을 내외에 선포하얏다. 그 국가의 이름은 처음에 만몽연성공화국(滿蒙聯省共和國)이니 또는 대동국(大同國)이니 하고 여러 의론이 잇섯스나 최후에 만주국(滿洲國)으로 결정되고 년호(年號)는 대동(大同)이라 층하게 되엿스니 대동은 주역(周易)에 천하대동(天下大同)이란 의미를 취한 것이라 한다. 그 국토(國土)는 봉천(奉天), 길림(吉林), 열하(熱河), 흑룡(黑龍)등 구동북의 사성(東北四省)을 총괄하야 면적이 약 七萬七千五百五十四方里에 달하고 人口는 약 三千四百萬이요 국체(國體)는 형식상 공화제이나 실질은 군주제(君主制)로 되여서 그 원수(元首)는 집정(執政)이란 명의로 전날 청조의 선통제부의(宣統帝溥儀)씨가 추대되고 국도(國都)는 장춘(長春)으로 결정되엿다 한다.

五色찬란한 新國旗
이 만주국의 새 국긔(國旗)는 누른 바탕에 청(靑), 적(赤), 백(白), 흑(黑) 사색을 가하엿스니 누른 것은 황색인종의 대동단결을 의미한 것이요 적색은 **을 의미한 것이요 청색은 청신한 긔운을 의미하고 백색은 평화와 **(平和 **)을 의미하고 흑색은 견실(堅實)을 의미한 것이요 신국가를 상증(象徵)한 것이라 한다.

新國家組織의 內容과 立國의 四大原則
그리고 그 국가의 조즉에 잇서서는 집정(執政)을 최고의 지위로 하고 (군주와가티) 그 밋헤는 립법(立法), 국무(國務), 감찰(監察), 삼원을 두고 또 국무원 밋헤는 민정(民政), 외교(外交), 군정(軍政), 재정(財政), 실업(實業), 교통(交通), 사법(司法)등 일곱부를 두엇스며 행정구역(行政區域)은 봉천, 길림, 흑룡강, 렬하, 사성(四省)외에 장춘, 봉천, 길림, 할빈, 렬하, **하루(齊齊合爾)등 여섯 곳의 특별시(特別市)를 실시하야 십개 행정구역에 구분하엿다.
독립국의 원측은 四개조로 되엿스니
一. 원수(元首)인 집정은 전 인민의 추대에 의하야 립헌제(立憲制)에 의하야 만주국을 통치함.
二. 만주국의 정치는 민본주의로써 함.
三. 만주국은 가장 갓가운 장래에 인민의 일반 의사에 의하야 헌법을 제정함.
四. 집정 정치는 정식의 헌법을 제정 실행하기 전까지는 과도긔적 통치의 형식으로 합니다.
