Literary World Rumors

New Student Company of Ewha Normal School

Mr. Yi Tae-jun, who was with the New Student Company (新生社), has recently moved his position to the Ewha Women's College office. Instead, Mr. Pang In-gŭn, who was with the Christian Daily (基督申報), has become a professor of literature at Ewha Women's College (梨花專門). Seeing that Mr. Yi Tae-jun has also gone to Ewha School, could it be that the New Student Company is a training center for professors and staff of Ewha School? If so, it seems it will be Mr. Pang's turn next. Let's be busy preparing for that from now on.

Mr. Ch'oe Hak-song's Two Kinds of Disease

Mr. Ch'oe Hak-song of the Maeil Shinbo, has been suffering from severe toothache (齒痛) and hemorrhoids (痔疾) lately, making it difficult to eat and very painful to defecate. However, a jobless friend who saw Mr. Ch'oe coming from the hospital said, "Is the suffering because you are allergic to your teeth? Anyway, toothache (齒疾) is a terrible disease." Mr. Ch'oe quickly replied, "Ha! If it were just toothache (齒疾), that would be good! I also have other hemorrhoids (痔疾)."

Mr. Yi Kwang-su's Tooth Extraction

Mr. Yi Kwang-su of the Dong-A Ilbo had one of his upper front teeth extracted recently. It couldn't have been extracted for the sake of elegance, but if it was a charming tooth, it would be very disappointing not only for himself but also for the people he meets. However, how about brushing it off with an interpretation like, "He sacrificed a tooth (年齒) because he was worried about eating even one more," or "Ch'unwŏn (春園) is already old, so an old tooth (老齒) fell out on its own"? Anyway, it is regrettable, so if there is a skilled dentist specializing in life insurance and dental services, he should rush to negotiate. <29>

文壇風聞
梨花師範科新生社
신생사(新生社)에 잇든 李泰俊씨는 요번에 자리를 리화전문사무실로 옴겨가게 되엿다. 그 대신 신생사에는 긔독신보(基督申報)에 잇든 方仁根씨가 리화전문(梨花專門)의 문과교수로 갓섯고 이번에 또 李泰俊씨가 리화학교로 가게 된 것을 보아 신생사가 리화학당의 교수 사무원의 양성소나 아닐는지! 그러면 다음은 方씨의 차레일 듯. 이로부터 그 준비에 밧블지어다.

崔鶴松氏의 兩치疾
매일신보의 崔鶴松씨, 요 근래 치통(齒痛)과 치질(痔疾)로 병고(病苦)가 자심하야 식사하기도 어려웁지만 뒤 보기에도 매우 고통이라고. 그런데 병원에 갓다오는 씨를 보고 실업슨 친구 하나이「원 니빠리를 알르라고 그 고생인가 하여간 치질(齒疾)이란 고약한 병이야」하니까 최씨 얼른 대답해 가로되
「흥 그 치질(齒疾) 뿐이면 조케! 다른 치질(痔疾)까지 잇다네.

李光洙씨의 발치
동아일보 李光洙씨 요전에 오른 쪽 압니 한 개를 발치하엿다. 운치로 빼엿슬리 만무하겟지만 애교잇는 니이엇다면 본인은 물론 대하는 사람도 매우 섭섭할 일이다. 그러나 「한나이라도 더 자시는 것이 안타까워서 년치(年齒) 한 개를 희생하엿느니라」. 「이미 춘원(春園)이 늙은지라 노치(老齒) 한 개가 절로 빠젓다」쯤의 해석으로 물려버림이 엇덜까? 하여튼 유감된 일이니 생명보험의 교권만공의 수완 잇는 입치업자가 잇거든 조속 달려가서 교섭해 볼 일이다.＜29＞
