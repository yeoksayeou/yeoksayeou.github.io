Correction Notice
Byeolgeon-gon Editorial Department

We would like to correct an error in the 1st Prize Winning Ballad (第1回 懸賞小曲) by Eul Paso (乙巴素) published in last month's issue. In “Ownerless Ferryboat (임자업는 나룻배),” (A) the phrase "The abandoned sorrowful fellow flows away (바리운 설은 놈이 흘너가노나)" should have (놈) been (몸). (C) The second line, "Today I sleep here, tomorrow where? (오날은 여기 자면 내일은 어듸)" should have read "Where do I sleep today, where tomorrow? (오날은 어듸자고 내일은 어듸)". We apologize to the writer and our readers for these errors.
Byeolgeon-gon Editorial Department <57>
