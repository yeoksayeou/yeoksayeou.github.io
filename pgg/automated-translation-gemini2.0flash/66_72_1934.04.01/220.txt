Children's Song: Moonlit Night
Yun Sŏk-chung, Translator

Knock, knock, knock
Please open the door
Who is it?
It's a tree leaf
Clatter, clatter

Knock, knock, knock
Please open the door
Who is it?
It's the wind
Clatter, clatter

Knock, knock, knock
Please open the door
Who is it?
It's the moon's shadow
Clatter, clatter<45>
