Apology

We at Pyŏl Kŏn'gon (別乾坤) were confident that our editing style and printing quality would be excellent, but for this issue only, we were unable to do so. We rushed to publish the New Year's issue by the end of the year, so we couldn't wait for the photographic plates to be developed and just rushed through the process. This is because we need to ensure regular publication from now on. Please understand and forgive us this time. <21>

謝告
□編輯體裁 印刷맵시조키로 충찬도 만코 自信도잇는 別乾坤이 이번號만은 그것을 다음대로 못하엿슴이다 엇더케던지 年末안으로 新年號를 내이려고 모타치느라고 寫眞製版도되기를 끼처기다리지못하고 그냥모타치기만한 닭임니다 그러케 해야 압흐로는 定期發行이되겟는 닭임니다 만히짐작하시고 이번만은 눌너보아주소서.＜21＞
