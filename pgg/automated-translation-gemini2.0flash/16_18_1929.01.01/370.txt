Samnyong wore the pouch that the mistress had given him and paraded around, showing it off. The neighborhood women, trying to create something to talk about... The whole village began to gossip. Hearing this, Kwigong was enraged and unable to contain his anger, beat Samnyong half to death, snatched the pouch, and then interrogated his wife, demanding she confess the truth, treating her as if she were a great criminal. But Samnyong couldn't figure out why and only felt sorry for the mistress who was being abused. Seeing Kwigong's violent behavior, Samnyong couldn't stand it, snatched the fainted mistress, held her, and barely managed to escape that situation. Kwigong's suspicion of his wife was very high. From that day on, strict orders were issued that Samnyong could not even cast a shadow in the inner courtyard.

Loyal and innocent Samnyong was trembling outside the main gate in the chilly autumn wind. But he didn't know how to resent it and was always worried about the mistress's safety, but there was no way to find out. Finally, he overheard from a maid going to get medicine that the mistress was lying in bed, groaning. <145>

紙上封切 벙어리 三龍이(4), 羅雲奎 主演
三龍이는 主人아씨가 준 주머니를 차고 단이며 자랑을 하엿다. 말거리를 작만하려는 동리의 녀편네... 온동리가 수군거리기 시작하얏다. 이 말을 드른 貴童이는 怒氣가 불갓치 닐고 분함을 참지 못하야 三龍이를 죽도록 따리고 주머니를 빼아슨 후 안해를 두드리며 事實을 自白하라고 맛치 큰 罪人을 달으는 것 갓치 하엿다. 그러나 三龍이는 무슨 까닭인지 알어내기가 어려웟고 구박을 당하는 주인 아씨가 불상하고 가엽슬뿐이엿다. 貴童이의 橫暴한 짓을 보고 잇든 三龍이는 참다 못하야 긔절한 주인아씨를 빼아서 안고 나와 겨우 그 당장은 모면은 식엿다. 貴童이가 안해를 의심하는 度는 놉핫다. 그날부터 三龍이는 內庭에 그림자도 못빗치도록 嚴한 분부가 나렷다.
忠實할 뿐이고 罪업는 三龍이는 쌀쌀한 가을 바람에 大門밧게서 떨고 잇다. 그러나 원망할 줄을 모르고 오히려 늘 불상한 主人아씨의 신변을 염여하엿스나 그러나 알어볼도리가 업섯다. 맛친 藥지러가는 女僕에게 主人아씨가 자리에 누어 신음하는 것을 들어 알앗다.<145>
