Constitution of Love
1. Anyone who reads this Constitution and does not laugh will be severely punished.
2. Any man or woman who does not comply with this Constitution will be sentenced to love imprisonment for more than one year.
3. This Constitution shall be effective from the date of its promulgation.

Chapter 1: Love
Article 1: Love is a fever that erupts from the impulse of instinct.
Article 2: Love is how the children of all living beings continue their lineage.
Article 3: Love is sacred and cannot be violated by a third party.
Article 4: Love is considered ideal when it complies with this Constitution.
Article 5: Love has the obligation of marriage.
Article 6: Love is limited to once in a lifetime.
Even cats are stipulated to love twice a year, so how can human love be excessive? True love only happens once in a lifetime.
Article 7: Love should have equal maturity, so do not engage in unrequited love (片戀). It ruins oneself.
Article 8: Love must maintain silence to have an intimate taste. Love that is done in a glass box does not know its true flavor.
Article 9: Love cannot be double-sold. This is the cause of love tragedies.
Article 10: The value of love is determined by chastity.
Article 11: The motive of love is not always pure, and love that is purely instinctual is vulgar. It becomes noble after washing away impatience and cultivating oneself, and after undergoing trials.
Article 12: Love is freedom. However, it is precious when it is not free.
Article 13: Love is blind. However, it must have reason.
Article 14: Love must last a lifetime.
Article 15: Love is not sympathy.
Pitying and feeling sorry for someone easily turns into real love. However, such love is short-lived. Love with the responsibility of marriage should not be mixed with simple sympathy.
Article 16: Love should have similar ages.
Goethe loved a young girl in his 80s. Love has no age. However, happy love should have similar ages.
Article 17: Love that is easily achieved is easily broken.
Article 18: True love exists in true people.
Article 19: Love is a bottomless pit.
Article 20: Love tastes like honey. However, it is very bitter.
Article 21: First love is when one's heart burns with longing for the opposite sex for the first time in the spring when one's instincts blossom.
Article 22: Once you get love, you don't become immune. Rather, you are more likely to get it multiple times. In today's world, where a love disease immunization method has not been discovered, one should not carelessly approach the opposite sex who are carriers. Extremely dangerous!
Article 23: Love is easy to ignite and easy to cool down.
Article 24: Love is an interesting novel, and marriage is a dry and dull thesis.

Chapter 2: Precautions for Lovers
Article 1: Talkative people
The more someone expresses the truth of their love in words, the less they have in their heart than in their words. Choose someone who can impress you with their actions rather than their words. However, be careful of pretense!
Article 2: People who cater to your digestion well
Wiping dust off your lover's skirt, giving stomach medicine when they burp, patting their back when they cough - even catering too much to their digestion is 싫어하는 (disliked). A person who is so attentive during the dating period will definitely become a tyrant in married life.
Article 3: Repeat offenders
In the society of love, there are many habitual love thieves called repeat offenders (前科數犯). They grant to others what they have promised to you. They entice others with the same means they used to seduce you. What has touched my lips has also touched the lips of others. Love, no, protect what is more precious than that from thieves! Be careful, be careful!
Article 4: Hypocrites
Hypocrites will be more reputable as lovers than straightforward people. There are many 바람쟁이 (flirts/ 바람둥이) women, but they cannot compare to 바람쟁이 men. People who become material for the third page of newspapers are myopic people who have failed to see through these 바람동이 (flirts).
Article 5: People with too much self-esteem
People who unconditionally submit to others are fools. However, people with too much self-esteem are even more foolish. You cannot be happy with someone who has a sense of superiority over you and덮비는 (covers) you.
Article 6: People who are too kind
A man's kindness and pufferfish soup are 㥘이 난다 (cause trouble) in the end. However, pufferfish soup is a bit safer compared to a man's kindness. A truly kind man is rather unkind to the opposite sex. There is nothing more dangerous than a woman in the dating period liking a man's kindness.

Chapter 3: Unacceptable Love
Article 1: Unreasonable love
A married woman is fascinated by the appearance, wealth, or art of a man other than her husband, and leaves her family, abandoning her children, to pursue a joyful dream. It may be sweet and refreshing for a while. However, incurable pain in her conscience will torment her for a lifetime. The power of love is so great that it can mask pain. However, lifelong regret will always block her path. Regret is useless!
Article 2: Love that is 맡기는 (forced).
A merchant from Hamgyeong Province 맡겨놋고 (deposits) a bolt of 北布 (northern cloth) and retrieves it during the autumn pasture season. 외상 (Credit) devours even young children, so can it not be paid off! If it cannot be paid off by autumn, the 용솟 (interest) is lost. Many women, and even men, 떼여 맛기는 (force) love with half-pleading and half-threat. Unless you want to be a lifelong partner in misfortune, it is safest not to buy such 외상 (credit) love.
Article 3: Love that cannot be seen.
It is common for people who have only heard rumors or are fascinated by the art of a painter or writer to fret and worry when the seeds of love sprout. The expectation of the former and the result of the latter are inversely proportional to how many unfortunate mummies (木乃伊) have been sacrificed to love. Remember this. <154>
Article 5: Receiving too much love is also unhappiness.
Like the song "If you and I come to love each other, even if we eat potatoes and millet rice!", the greatest ideal of lovers is to give and receive love even in such places. However, there will be times when they sigh deeply, saying, "Why did we love each other so intensely?" After receiving someone who is 지나치는 (excessive), disappointment is several times greater.
Article 6: Between teacher and student
Love has no class and no hierarchy. However, if it is not legitimate love, people may forgive, but heaven will not forgive. Teacher and student in love! Master and servant in love! Have you seen it last long?
Article 7: Love is not marriage.
Like butterflies sleeping in flowers and bees sucking honey, lovers want to fully enjoy the 청춘 (youth) that will never return. However, one must endure. Doing love like 맥주 (beer) with the lid off before marriage causes 곳 (soon)倦怠 (weariness). Marriage is when the delicious taste of 감주 (sweet rice wine) is preserved in a bottle of chastity.
Article 8: Do not be daring in love.
Just as Myeongsa workers die in the water, those who are daring in love always fail. Do not flaunt your position, honor, or wealth. Those who are cautious in mutual love will gain victory.
Article 9: Do not do love like sightseeing.
Article 10: Do not have much experience in love.
There is nothing more precious than experience. Human affairs can be perfected through experience, but only in love does misfortune follow. What is there besides Roman-s manufacturers?
Article 11: Do not issue dishonored promissory notes of affection. Do not pretend to have non-existent passion and then face great ruin when the reckoning comes.
Article 12: Do not become addicted to love.
The power of love is sacred. However, there is a greater power in life than that. An apostle who is only fulfilled by love will become miserable in middle age. A love addict is more pitiful and hateful than an opium addict.
Article 13: Do not compete in love.
If you lose, the sorrow is multiplied, and even if you win, the curse is several times greater than the joy.

Chapter 4: The Canon of Love
Article 1: Do love like eating dumplings (饅頭).
감칠맛 (Savory) and 달큼 (sweet) passion is 理性 (reason) wrapped in a 밀가루껍풀 (flour dough). Eating the filling first is not how to eat dumplings. A lover must not eat the filling first.
Article 2: Walk a clean path in love.
Only 빠저서 허덕어리는 (drowning) in 情慾 (lust) is not true love. The "beautiful love" that you can tell your sons and daughters about - this is the 상등 (high-class) love that this Constitution prescribes and desires.
Article 3: Do love like flying a 연(紙鳶) (kite).
If the other side is猛熱 (fierce), loosen the 戀糸 (kite string), and if the other side is 느러지 (slack), tighten the 戀糸 (kite string). 잘 操縱하라 (Operate well) so that it does not break in the autumn wind.
Article 4: Do love 비타산적 (non-calculatingly).
Love with 利益 (profit) as its purpose disappears at the same time as the purpose is lost.<155>
Article 5: Know the strengths and weaknesses of your lover in advance.
Article 6: Do not respond quickly to requests in love.
Even when buying a 질뚝백이 (earthenware pot), if you pay whatever price is asked, you 의심 (suspect) that it is damaged. If you value love, do not respond lightly.
Article 7: Have the same 생활력 (living power) in love. Whether love succeeds or fails depends on whether you have the 힘 (power) to live. It is 거룩한 詩 (holy poem) to love when you are hungry. However, love without the power to live 亡치 나니라 (ruins) the body.
Article 8: Always have the same 열도 (fervor) in love.
It is not 행복 (happiness) to only 귀엽 (cuteness) like a doll. A wife who only receives 단순한 (simple) love knows how much 寂寞 (loneliness) there is. The 힘 (power) of love must be 돌아보내 (returned) to keep 사랑 (love) 핑핑히 켱겨잇서야 (bouncing vibrantly) - especially for couples.
Article 9: Make someone who adores children your lover.
There are no 악인 (bad people) who adore children. However, there are those who use it as a 수단 (means) to buy 歡心 (joy). 엄밀한 鑑別 (strict discrimination) is necessary.
Article 10: Maintain 체면 (face) even between lovers.
The deeper the love, the easier it is to act without 체면 (face). For a while, it can be 용서 (forgiven) with "lovely spitefulness," but 귀여운 끗헤 미워지나니라 (it ends up being hated). 조심조심 (Be careful)! <165>

敗하면 그 설움이 곱이나 되고, 勝利를 하여도 그 咀呪가 깃붐에 몃곱이나 된다.

第4章 戀愛典
第1條 戀愛는 饅頭먹듯 하여라.
감칠맛 잇고 달큼한 정열이란 속을 理性이란 밀가루껍풀이 싸고 잇다. 속부터 먹는 것이 饅頭를 먹는 것이 아니다. 戀愛子는 모름직이 속부터 먹지 말라.
第2條 戀愛는 淸潔한 길을 걸어라.
情慾에만 빠저서 허덕어리는 것은 참 戀愛가 아니다. 아들과 딸에게도 일러 줄만한 『악름다운 戀愛』 이것이 이 憲法이 規定하고 바라는 바 上等戀愛이다.
第3條 戀愛는 연(紙鳶)날이 못하여라.
저 便에서 猛熱하거든 戀糸를 늣구어 주고, 저 便이 느러지거든 戀糸를 켱기여라. 가을바람에 끈허지지 안토록 잘 操縱하라.
第4條 戀愛는 非打算的으로 하여라.
利益을 目的한 戀愛는 그 目的物이 업서서는 同時에 사라진다.<155>
第5條 戀人의 長處와 短點을 미리 알아 두어라.
第6條 戀愛는 請求에 빨리 應치 말라.
질뚝백이 하나를 사는 데도 부르는 대로 밧고 팔면 도리혀 破損이 나지 안엇나 하고 疑心하는 것이다. 戀愛를 重히 녁이거든 輕率히 應치 말라.
第7條 戀愛는 生活力이 한가지 하여야 한다. 戀愛의 成否도 生活力 有無에 좌우되는 世上이다. 배가 곱하 가지고는 戀愛할 거룩한 詩이다. 그러나 生活力이 업는 戀愛는 몸을 亡치 나니라.
第8條 戀愛는 恒常熱度가 가타야 한다.
人形과 갓치 귀엽만 밧고 잇는 것이 幸福이 아니다. 單純한 사랑만 밧고 잇는 안해가 남모르는 寂寞이 얼마나 만흔지 모른다. 바든 사랑을 돌녀보내 일만한 사랑의 힘이 핑핑히 켱겨잇서야-더구나 夫婦에 잇서서 더욱 그럿타.
第9條 어린아희를 귀여워하는 사람을 戀人으로 하라.
어린이를 귀여워하는 사람에게 惡人은 업다. 그러나 歡心을 사기 爲한 手段으로 쓰는 者가 잇다. 嚴密한 鑑別이 必要하다.
第10條 戀愛人끼리도 體面은 차려다.
戀情이 깁흘사록 體面업시 굴기 쉬웁다 한동안은 『사랑스러운 얄미움』으로 용서할 수 잇스나 귀여운 끗헤 미워지나니라. 操心操心! <165>
