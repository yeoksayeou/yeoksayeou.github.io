기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editorial Note)

We welcome reports on the exploration of various cities.

As the wind of civilization blows in daily, the number of so-called metropolitan areas (都會地) is also increasing daily. Until now, we have only explored such demon dens (魔窟) in the Kyŏngsŏng (京城) area, but from now on, we intend to make an effort to explore local metropolitan areas as well. We hope that those who have the will will provide us with a lot of reliable data. <133>

**Named Entities**

京城	Kyŏngsŏng
