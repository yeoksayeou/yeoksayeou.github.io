Park Tal-seong's Tobacco Business

Park Tal-seong, who called himself the emperor of the self-supporting (自層) Joo (주) state and used to drink over 300 times a day, doing a liquor business wouldn't surprise anyone, but doing a tobacco business would be unexpected. After suffering from a nervous disorder for several years, he completely cut ties with alcohol, and doesn't even think about going near a brewery (麥廛) , let alone getting drunk. He only enjoys fruit, rice cakes, and tobacco. These days, because he's bored and doesn't have other income, he opened a small tobacco shop in his newly moved house in Jae-dong (齋洞), like Buddha running a tobacco business. If he, a cheerful man, drank alcohol well like before and ran a liquor business, he would invite his close friends, give them a drink, and ask them to sell his liquor well. But he quit alcohol, became ill, his chivalrous spirit declined, and now he's running a stingy and strict tobacco business. How could he offer a cigarette for someone to try like he would with liquor? If someone jokingly asks to try a cigarette, he shakes his head and says, "No, we don't have any profit (利文)," showing an awkward charm. <25>

萬華鏡
朴達成氏의 담배장사
자층 주국천자라 하고 일일수경 300배 이상을 하던 박달성씨가 술장사를 한다면 누구나 괴이치 안케 생각하겟지만 담배장사를 한다면 의외로 생각할 것이다. 그는 신경병으로 수삼년 고생을 한 뒤로는 술과는 아주 인연을 끈어서 과맥전대취가 아니라 맥전이라면 그 근처에 갈 생각도 하지 안코 과실과 떡 담배만 잘 먹는데 요새는 심심하고 다른 수입도 업고 하니까 재동 거리에 새로 이사한 집에다 조고마한 담배가게를 새로 내고 부처가 담배장사를 한다. 쾌남아인 그가 만일 전과 가티 술을 잘 먹고 술장사를 한다면 친한 친구들을 불너서 한 잔식 주며 술을 잘 팔어달나고 하겟지만은 술을 끈코 중병지여가 되야 호협풍도 감소한데다가 리가 박하고 빡빡한 담배장사를 하고 보니 그 담배야 술처럼 한 대 먹어보라고 할 수 잇스랴. 누가 작난으로 담배 맛 좀 보자면 「안되야 우리 리문이 업서 하고 머리를 휘휘 두르며 무자긔한 애교를 부린다고」.＜25＞
