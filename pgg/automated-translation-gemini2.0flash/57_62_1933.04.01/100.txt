Silence is Golden!

A conductor hired a violinist for his orchestra, unaware that he was partially mute.
Conductor: "Mr. Hari, how about it? If you officially become a member of our orchestra, I'll give you 40 won a week..."

However, "Hari" was not satisfied with 40 won, so as he was about to refuse, mumbling with his mouth, the conductor spoke first.
Conductor: "Then I'll give you 60 won."

Only then did "Hari" greatly mumble with his mouth, and the conductor shouted again.
Conductor: "Okay, let's just decide on 80 won, whether it's too much or too little."
"Hari": "T-t-thank you."
Conductor: "Oh, my god, what are you...?"
