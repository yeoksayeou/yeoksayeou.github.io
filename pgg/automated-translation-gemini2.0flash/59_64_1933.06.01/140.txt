A Story About Wanting to Devour
						Horangdang In
Characters - Wife, Husband, Baby

Wife: Honey.

Husband: Uh.

Wife: Please stop it.

Husband: Uh, uh.

Wife: I said, please get up. Phew—the smell of alcohol is still coming out of your mouth.

Husband: (Snores)

Wife: My goodness, where did you go and what did you do to get your suit in this state... You fell asleep without even taking it off, how am I supposed to take care of this now… Hey…

Husband: (Snores)

Wife: Hey, honey.

Husband: Uh, uh.

Wife: Please get up.

Husband: Uh… a little… more sleep…

Baby: Waa, waa, waa.

Wife: ………

Husband: Uh, uh.

Baby: Waa, waa, waa.

Husband: Why are you making the baby cry before mealtime and not letting me sleep!?

Wife: What do you mean, before mealtime?

Husband: Stop talking back and go do your work. Make some soup to sober me up…

Wife: Seriously! There's no rice for breakfast, let alone soup… What happened to the salary you got yesterday?

Husband: If there's no rice, why didn't you say so earlier?

Wife: Didn't I? Didn't I tell you yesterday morning when you went out that there was no rice for dinner?

Husband: I didn't hear anything like that…

Wife: Oh my, what should I do… You go out and eat well like this, but I went hungry last night… How am I supposed to have milk! You can't even bear to see the baby crying because it's hungry?

Baby: Waa, waa, waa.

Husband: Ugh.

Wife: Seriously, how are you going to go to work today looking like this?

Husband: Don't worry.

Wife: I won't worry, so give me the salary you got… If you keep this up, the baby and I will starve to death…

Husband: Here, take it all.

Wife: How much is this?

Husband: How would I know how much it is!

Wife: You spent all 60 won and only left this much?

Husband: I must have had a reason to spend it.

Wife: Seriously, you left me with 1 won and a few jeon, how am I supposed to manage for a month? What about the firewood bill?

Husband: Postpone it.

Wife: What about the electricity bill that's been postponed for three months!?

Husband: Postpone it for a few more days.

Wife: The rice price has been delayed for two months.

Husband: They said they'd give it to me tomorrow.

Wife: The landlord already charges 18 won, and now they won't even give me vegetables.

Husband: Don't eat them.

Wife: I have to pay the pawnshop interest.

Husband: I won't let the belt cool down for a month (œ질 아니식혀).

Wife: The tailor is coming to the house to collect payment now.

Husband: Send him to the company.

Wife: You don't have any underwear, what are you going to do?

Husband: Don't worry.

Wife: I don't have anything to wear anymore, what should I do?

Husband: Cut something out of that and make something.

Wife: What am I supposed to buy for breakfast today!?

Husband: Buy seolleongtang (설넝탕) and eat it.

Wife: Who would give me seolleongtang if I didn't pay?

Husband: Then we'll have to go hungry.

Wife: Aren't you hungry?

Husband: Who wouldn't be hungry if they didn't eat!

Wife: Seriously, why did you leave this? Why didn't you spend it all?

Husband: Give it to me… I'm going to go get a hangover cure.

Wife: Oh my! Seriously!

Husband: Hurry up and buy rice with that and cook.

Wife: I don't know. I don't know… Oh my!… I just… I just… want to devour you! <37>
