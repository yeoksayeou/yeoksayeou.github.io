Let’s dig a grave
Let’s dig a grave, let’s dig a grave
Let’s dig a grave on Waryong Yongdu
If you build a grave on Waryong Yongdu
You will have numbness for twelve years

-Grave is 墓. Waryong Yongdu means the dragon's head of Waryong Mountain (臥龍山). In the vicinity of Chungcheong Province, there is a superstition that if you build a grave on a mountain with the character Dragon (龍), you will get numbness, so farmers sing this song.

Let’s dig a grave, let’s dig a grave
Let’s dig a grave on Waryong Yongdu
If we can’t plant in the dog days of summer
How can we live through the three winter months and three spring months?

Let’s dig a grave, let’s dig a grave
Let’s dig a grave on Waryong Yongdu
What is the story behind building this grave?
They say it will produce many state councilors and ministers.

Let’s dig a grave, let’s dig a grave
Let’s dig a grave on Waryong Yongdu
Even if it produces state councilors and ministers
What will we, the common people, eat to live?

Let’s dig a grave, let’s dig a grave
Let’s dig a grave on Waryong Yongdu
If you build a grave on Waryong Mountain
Water will spring from the mountains and rivers of the eight provinces.
