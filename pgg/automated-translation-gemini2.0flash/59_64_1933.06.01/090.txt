잡지명	별건곤 제64호
발행일	1933년 06월 01일
기사제목	朝鮮兩班의 妾타령 (A Discourse on Concubines of the Joseon Yangban)
필자	風流郞
기사형태	잡저 (Miscellany)

Concubines! This is a major ill custom that has continued from the East. This ill custom was even more severe when it came to Joseon, causing families to lose their homes and livelihoods, and leading to both disgrace and advancement. It would be quite interesting to uncover the stories of other people's concubines.

Kim, the concubine of Yulgok (栗谷), a mountain and forest scholar, Lady Ri, the concubine of Sin Dokjae (愼獨齋), and Lady Jeong (鄭氏), the concubine of Sagye (河溪) were all well-known. Yulgok did not have a son until the end, and at the time, the national law stipulated that adoption was only permitted if there were no legitimate or illegitimate sons (嫡庶俱無子). Therefore, in order to have a son, Kim, who was from a commoner background, was taken as a concubine, and she bore him three sons. She devoted herself to serving her master and his legal wife as well as her widowed sister-in-law, and to this day, she is praised. Furthermore, her descendants have flourished considerably in Haeju, Hwanghae Province. Lady Ri, the concubine of Sin Dokjae, was Yulgok's illegitimate daughter, born to Kim mentioned above. At the time, national law allowed the daughter of another man's concubine to marry as a concubine. Sagye (河溪), Sin Dokjae's father, was Yulgok's most esteemed disciple, and Sin Dokjae also frequented Yulgok's house, following his father's footsteps, receiving Yulgok's affection. Sin Dokjae's wife, Lady Yu (兪氏), was the daughter of Songdang Yu Hong (松堂兪泓), and she was reclusive and indecisive. Even when she sewed socks in the shape of pouches, Sin Dokjae regarded them as unique treasures, which shows her character. Because she could not produce any offspring, Yulgok gave his illegitimate daughter to him and made him his son-in-law, resulting in offspring. Even now, their descendants are quite prosperous in areas such as Yeonsan (連山) and Dugye (豆溪). Sin Dokjae's son wrote a statement (單子) on his civil service examination paper (試紙) stating that his father was Sin Dokjae, his grandfather was Sagye, his great-grandfather was Hwanggang (黃岡), and his maternal grandfather was Yulgok. The four ancestors (四祖) were the best in Joseon, but because he was an illegitimate child, he was not given an official position, so he vomited blood and his family did not take the civil service examination for generations. Sagye remarried late in life and, after the age of sixty, took Lady Jeong and had six sons. The group known as the "Yulbang faction" (률방파) refers to the descendants of those brothers. Just by looking at them, one can see that they were refined scholars with ample means, and their descendants continue to serve in high positions to this day. There are also some concubines of powerful figures that are worth mentioning, such as the concubine of Hong Yunseong (洪允城), a meritorious subject of Sejo (世祖), and the concubine of Yun Wonhyeong (尹元衡).

Hong Yunseong's concubine was the daughter of a clerk from Jeonju. Although she unwillingly became Hong Yunseong's concubine due to his power, she was virtuous and exceptionally intelligent. Hong Yunseong abused his power, causing much criticism at the time, but she skillfully dissuaded him, appeased him, and corrected his mistakes. On the other hand, Yun Wonhyeong's concubine, Lady Ranjeong (蘭貞), was arrogant and relied on her favor, playing a significant role in the massacres of the Eulsa (乙巳) year [1545]. This shows that even in the same position, the difference between good and evil can be vast. Yang Bongrae (楊蓬萊)'s mother is famous as the mother of a distinguished figure. She was born to an unknown person in a remote village in Jeolla Province. Yang Bongrae's father noticed her while having a meal on his way to his post as the magistrate (郡守) of Yeonggwang (靈光), and after exchanging gifts (淸扇紅扇) at the age of ten, she defied her parents' orders to marry and came to Yang Bongrae's father as a concubine (副室). As a result, she acted as a stepmother and raised Bongrae's brothers into famous figures, passing on their names to the world. Furthermore, on the day of her husband's funeral (成服), she immediately followed him (下從), allowing her son to be known as a legitimate son and preventing herself from being called someone's concubine. She was a lady of outstanding talent, virtue, and integrity.

The two concubines of Jin Dynasty scholar (柳進士) Jakyeon (綽然) were born in the same year, month, and day in neighboring houses in the same village as his wife. They became concubines after promising to serve one husband together. As a result, they had the then-Prime Minister Gwon Daeun (權大運) as their concubine's father-in-law and a high-ranking member of the middle class (中人) as their concubine's father-in-law, living a life of luxury without lifting a finger (十指不動). This was an unusual occurrence (奇緣). Lady Ri, the concubine of Yi Sang'eon (李尙彦), received all the wealth of a millionaire's only daughter because of a dream, marrying the impoverished Yi Seobang. She never had to worry about food or clothing, which can be called a concubine who hit the jackpot (橫財). The concubine of Yi Dong'ak (李東岳) and the concubine of Hong Injae (洪忍齋) both turned misfortune into fortune (轉禍爲福). Entering a dangerous situation (虎穴) after being seduced one night or becoming drunk, they fortunately were able to honestly convey their innermost thoughts and made a lifelong commitment in a life-or-death situation. How miraculous is this? Oksoseon (玉簫仙) of Seong Sechang (成世昌) and Iltahong (一朶紅) of Sim Huisu (沈喜壽) were the work of the creator (造化翁), who brought together talented scholars and beautiful women. By chance, they recognized each other's true selves and did not change their minds despite their poverty, living together for a hundred years with intertwined episodes that made them a perfect match.

The women from Ganggye (江界) in Kim Uhang's (金守杭) household and from Nyeongbyeon (寧邊) in No Okgye's (盧玉溪) household upheld their chastity in entertainment venues and sought men who could recognize the value of their reputations. They chose men who were impoverished and burdened with debt, recognizing their inner worth rather than their outward appearance. They rescued them from their unfortunate circumstances, sacrificing their bodies and possessions to fulfill their earnest promises. They even went into deep mountain valleys to become nuns, unable to protect their chastity from powerful local officials (수령) or helpless against their ignorant mothers. After enduring solitude (空閨) in snow-covered huts (白雲寒屋), they fortunately met again in glory and lived a life of comfort together. This is a rare occurrence among prostitutes who sell laughter. Uncho (雲樵), a concubine of Kim Iryang (金履陽), was a gisaeng from Pyeongyang. At the age of 20, she entrusted herself to a 70-year-old governor (監司) for life. This was an unusual event, and she was also famous for her writing skills, earning praise (得譽) among the female writers (女流) of the time. Rahab (羅閤) is probably the concubine who most influenced the political scene for five hundred years.

<22> When Yi Seogu (李書九), Minister Yi, was appointed as the governor of Jeolla Province, he sent an envoy to Rahab on the day of her birth, saying that if she gave birth to a son, he would dispose of him immediately, and if she gave birth to a daughter, he would simply return, worrying that she would disrupt the court (濁亂朝廷). Just hearing this legend, one can understand that her appearance was related to the fate of Joseon. As she entered the household of Kim Jwageun (金佐招) as a concubine, she held such immense influence over her husband's appointments that there was even a popular saying (俗謠) that it was Rahab's power, not Haok's power (荷玉 - 金佐招's 別號). One can imagine how great her power was. When Haok was building the Gyodong house (now Min Yeonghwi's house), he tried to build the main reception hall (큰사랑) high, but Rahab protested, saying that Mount Namsan was blocked from view in the main hall (大廳) of her house, so the main hall's pillars were cut down by one foot. There is also a story that even after Jo Dusun (趙斗淳) was appointed to a high position (大拜), he would first greet Rahab before visiting Haok when he went to their house. If that was the case, who in the court, whether civil or military officials, could ignore her? Whether it is true or not, there is a rumor that when Haok asked for his ceremonial attire (官듸) to enter the palace, a shaman's robe (장삼) was brought instead. This suggests that Rahab favored shamanistic rituals (不供) more than anyone else. In recent times, Min Yeonghwi's principal concubine, Haeju Mama, was born with a fortunate destiny (사주). She was chosen by her father-in-law, Grand Minister Chunryu (春留) as a concubine and daughter-in-law (첩며누리). Among the concubines of Joseon nobles, she was considered the luckiest in terms of wealth, honor, long life, and having many sons (수부귀다남). Min Yeonghwi's minor concubine, Pyeongyang Mama (already deceased), did not have as fortunate a destiny as Haeju Mama, but she wielded even more power than Rahab in her heyday. In recent times, times have changed and her influence has waned, so she could not appoint or dismiss local officials (수령방백), but many of her requests were granted when she removed officials (사음떼고 내는데), and she used this to exercise power that rivaled Rahab. With over 10,000 bags of rice worth of wealth, officials from the capital visited her house as frequently as they visited the Ministry of War (兵判門前). It seems that the land is destined to be a place where concubines wield power. As for accumulating wealth and changing husbands, Sanwol Mama, who lived with Min Byeongseok (閔丙奭) and then with Yi Yunyong (李允用), would be second to none. Gangseok Han Gyuseol's (江石韓圭卨) Nyeongbyeon (寧邊) Mamas were just like Emperor Shun's two wives, Ahwa and Yeoyeong (娥媓女英). However, Gangseok did not take the two concubines at the same time. The younger Nyeongbyeon Mama was originally the wife of Gangseok's in-law, Sim Sanghyeon (沈相薰), whom he took as his concubine after Sim Sanghyeon's death. The Nyeongbyeon Mamas' scheme to prevent each other from losing the old man's love was clever, but Gangseok's reputation was somewhat damaged. As a result, Han Gyuseol's entire being – his mind, body, and wealth – was devoted to them, to the point where he didn't even recognize his own son. His ability to monopolize her husband's affection surpassed everyone else.

Among the recent gisaengs, Ryu Saek, the concubine of Yi Punghan (李豐漢), first enjoyed the love of Yun Deokyeong (尹德榮) and is now the second wife of Baron Yi Punghan, a wealthy yangban. She enjoys wealth and pleasure to her heart's content in her newly built large house in Gahoe-dong. No concubine among today's concubines would complain about being second to her. Ryu Sanhong Mama, the mistress of a modern newspaper publisher, lives no differently than the concubines of other wealthy or noble men. Moreover, because her husband is a modern celebrity, she seems to dream of even more fervent love. Bae Jeongja (裵貞子), who was deeply involved in the rise and fall of the late Joseon Dynasty, had famous tactics and a famous perverted sexuality. First, she brought prosperity and ruin to Hyeon Yeongun (玄英軍). Now, she is helping to promote Park Yeongcheol (朴榮喆), the president of a certain bank, and she whispers love to younger men, sometimes acting like a grandmother (손쟈ㅅ벌) or a mother (아들벌). After squandering all the dirty money, she is now begging somewhere, a perfect match (好一對) for a man who spends on his mistress and ends up begging. In any case, she can be said to be the best of the women of fortune in the five hundred years of Joseon and the most notorious of the female adventurers (女流오입쟁이).<23>

朝鮮兩班의 妾타령
						風流郞
첩! 그것은 동양 자래로 잇는 큰 페습이다. 이 페습이 조선 와서는 더욱 어느 때 보담 심하야 일로 인하야 집이 일코 망하기도 하얏스며 망신도 발신도 업지 안햇스니 남의 첩들의 이야기를 들추는 것도 여간 재미잇지 안흘 것이다.
산림학자의 첩으로 률곡(栗谷)의 첩 김씨와 신독재(愼獨齋)의 첩 리씨와 사계(河溪)의 첩 정씨(鄭氏)가 다 이름이 잇섯다. 률곡이 끗까지 아들을 두지 못하고 그 때 국법이 적서구무자(嫡庶俱無子)라야 립후를 허락하는 까닭으로 아들을 두기 위하야 려럼출신의 김씨를 량첩으로 어더 아들을 삼형제나 두고 률곡과 가티 싀서모 섬기기와 홀로된 맛동서 섬기기에 극진한 성력을 다하야 지금까지 아름다운 칭송이 전하야올 뿐 아니라 그의 혈속이 황해도 해주에서 상당한 번창을 이루고, 신독재의 첩 리씨는 즉 률곡의 서따님이니 우에 말한 김씨의 소생이라 그 때 국법은 남의 첩의 딸이 남의 첩으로 싀집가는 수도 만엇스니 신독재의 아버지 사계(河溪)가 률곡의 제일 놉흔 제자이요 신독재도 그 아버지의 귀를 따라 률곡문하에 출입하든 까닭으로 률곡의 신애를 바닷다. 신독재의 부인 유씨(兪氏)는 송당유홍(松堂兪泓)의 따님인데 위인이 불출이어서 아무 주착이 업스며 버선을 주머니모양으로 지어도 신독재는 유일무이한 귀물로 알앗스니 그만하야도 그의 위인을 알 것이다. 그런 중 사속을 두지 못하니 률곡이 자긔의 서딸을 주고 사위를 삼아 사속을 보앗스니 지금도 련산(連山) 두계(豆溪) 등지에 그 혈속이 여간 번연하지 안타. 신독재 아들이 과거보는 시지(試紙)에 단자(單子)를 쓰고 본즉 아버지는 신독재, 하라버니는 사계, 증조는 황강(黃岡), 외조는 률곡으로 사조(四祖)는 조선 제일이나 서자인 까닭으로 청환을 주지 안하 피를 토하고 대대로 과거를 보지 안핫다. 사계는 늣게 상처하고 환갑지난 후 정씨를 어더서 아들 6형제를 나엇스니 속칭 률방파라는 것이 그 률형제 자손을 이름이니 그만 보아도 그네들이 품행과 덕성이 점잔흔  학자와 살기에 넉넉함이 잇서 지금도 자손이 엄연히 대성노림을 한다. 세도군의 첩으로 조흔 대되는 이가 하나 잇스니 세조(世祖)공신 홍윤성(洪允城)의 첩과 윤원형(尹元衡)의 첩일 것이다. 홍윤성의 첩은 전주 아전의 딸로 권세에 견듸지 못하야 홍윤성의 첩으로 비록 드러 왓스나 위인이 덕성이 잇고도 총민이 출중하야 홍유성이 권세를 밋고 폭학을 부리어 당시에 득담이 여간 아니엇스나 그가 능히 눈치잇게 말리고 비위를 마추며 간하야 허물을 기운 것이 여간 만치 안타. 그 반면으로 윤원형의 첩 란정(蘭貞)은 방자무긔탄하고 시총자사(恃寵自恣)하야 을사(乙巳)년대 살육(殺戮)에 크나큰 주인공이 되엿스니 이것은 가튼 자리에 잇서 석악의 차이가 천리뿐이 아니라 명사의 어머니로는 양봉래(楊蓬萊)＞의 모친이 유명하니 전라도 궁촌 무명씨의 소생으로 양봉래의 부친이 령광(靈光)군수로 도임길에 밥 사먹을 때 눈에 들어 청선홍선(淸扇紅扇)으로 10여 세에 서로 폐백을 하고 자란 후 부모의 싀집보내려는 명령을 거역하야 양봉래 아버지에게 부실(副室)로 와서 인하야 겸부인 노릇을 하고 봉래형제의 명인을 난하 세상에 이름을 전할 뿐 아니라 자긔 남편 죽어 성복(成服)날에 바로 하종(下從)하야 아들로 서자의 이름을 들리고 자긔도 남의 첩소리를 전치 안엇스니 이야말로 재조와 덕향과 절개가 구비한 숙녀라 아니할 수 업다. 류진사(柳進士) 작연(綽然)의 두 첩은 자긔 안해와 동년 동월 동일 동시에 한 동리 알에웃집에서 태여나 가티 자랄 때 한 남편을 섬기자는 약속으로
