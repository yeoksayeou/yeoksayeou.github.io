To Our Readers,

To those readers who have been avidly reading Pyŏlgŏn’gon (別乾坤) from before and to those who have pre-ordered Pyŏlgŏn’gon (別乾坤), we will send you this book and also Hyesŏng (彗星). The total price for both books is 35 chŏn; we will send them until the remaining money is exhausted. If the remaining money is insufficient, we ask that you quickly send the 부족되는 돈 (deficient amount). If you have any objections to this, please notify the main office's dispatch department (本社發送係) immediately. <9>

**Named Entities**

別乾坤	Pyŏlgŏn’gon
彗星	Hyesŏng
本社發送係	Head Office Dispatch Department
