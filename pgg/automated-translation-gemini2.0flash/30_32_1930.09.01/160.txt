Humor, Satire, Extraordinary Exhibition of Bride Candidates
						Ssang S Hosted

Because this exhibition has been going on for several months, I've been getting so many letters that it's become a problem. "I'm reading it with great interest, but aren't you being too harsh on the women?" Most of these letters are from women. They say it's interesting, but it stabs them in the heart too much, so please soften it up a bit... "If you're so picky, you'll never get married." This also seems to be written by a woman. On the other hand, some people write, "Thank you for Ssang S's refreshing satire," or "I specially request Ssang S to write even more explicitly and refreshingly if possible." These letters probably come from men who have been scolded or deceived by women once or twice. "As a single man looking for a bride, I am grateful for your articles, which are very helpful," these are the most sincere letters.

Anyway, I have to continue the exhibition as I initially promised and planned. Whether it's more refreshing, too harsh, lukewarm, helpful, or not, is up to the readers, not my concern. Now, let's continue the exhibition.

Han-nyang
22 years old. The younger sister of the owner of a large pharmaceutical company (製藥會社), a beauty who graduated from a commercial school last spring. <58> From her face and figure to her voice and actions, everything is so refreshing that she must be a very pleasant woman in the summer, even if I don't know what she's like in the winter. She gives the feeling of sitting next to a block of ice. After graduation, she refused a job introduction as a clerk (店員) at a store in Jingogae, saying, "Who would do such a thing?" which was also a cool thing to do. It's refreshing to throw the abacus (珠板) away and turn around if it's even a little annoying, saying, "I can't do it." And another cool thing that no one else can imitate is that she's good at forgetting things without hesitation. She easily forgets whatever she's carrying in her hands, whether it's a towel, a fan, or a powder case. It's rare not to find something she left behind wherever she sits and gets up.
"Miss, isn't this what you left behind?" She's a woman who graduated with honors from hearing this sound. It's a mystery how she can be like this even after graduating from a commercial school, where she should be meticulous.
"Isn't it because it's not like the old days when girls would organize sewing kits or fidget with notebooks? If modern women remembered everything, they'd get nervous breakdowns, wouldn't they? I like cleanly and neatly forgetting things; I can just buy them again when I need them."
This woman must also be dropping love letters she receives from others all over the place, and if she's this clean, she's likely to go to the beach and forget which one is her husband.

An-nyang
21 years old. This woman is a beautiful woman who looks like she came out of a painting. She's kind, has a good heart, graduated with honors, her father is the principal of XX school, and she's the most well-behaved candidate, but this pitiful beauty can't be without medicine bottles (藥甁) for some reason. It's like a person wearing clothes sitting next to a medicine bottle. They say that if there are no illnesses in the family for three years, anyone can become rich... To marry this woman, you have to marry her as if you're marrying a medicine bottle.

Bak-nyang
21 years old. A beauty who graduated from OO Nursery School and works as a nanny (保姆) at XX Kindergarten in the city. Not only is her face cute and her figure beautiful, but her voice is also lovely, so her words are so pretty. Listening to this woman cheerfully talking with her charming mouth is like listening to a canary's song. Seeing this woman smiling with her cute face, dancing with her lovely figure for the kindergarten children, and singing with her lovely voice is like seeing a fairy (仙女) wandering in a flower garden. If you dreamed of a home with such a woman, it would be a wonderful paradise (樂園). And if this woman gave birth to a child, she would be a grateful and faithful good mother... She was such an excellent beauty, such a qualified good mother <59>, and the most promising candidate, but one day, I saw something I shouldn't have. It was when I stopped by her house on the way.
"Are you still not going? You little brat, I bought you candy yesterday and snacks today, and you ate them all, so why aren't you going? If you're not going to do errands, give it all back. Give it back, you little brat, still not going? Still not going? You little urchin, still not going?"
She was picking and pinching the cheeks of her crying niece (少女), using only the most fragrant words. It seemed that the tenant didn't have laundry or stamps to send letters and was telling the girl, who was playing seesaw with the neighborhood kids, to buy them. How could a woman who is so gentle and cares for children in kindergarten pick and pinch the cheeks of a crying child like that...? Standing by the gate and seeing that sight I shouldn't have seen, my disappointment was truly great. A woman who learned something at nursery school but acts differently at home is dangerous in other ways as well.

Sin-nyang
23 years old. A knowledgeable woman who went to Japan and finished Normal School (高師) and came back. She's not employed, but she's a good writer. With considerable skill, she is a rare reader as a woman, and her thoughts are very philosophical. Compared to the common women who are like noisy night market merchants without anything inside, she is like a white crane (白鶴) among chickens (群鷄), an outstanding woman. She is a woman worthy of respect. As much as she has the skills and her thoughts are calm, she doesn't talk much usually, but sometimes when she writes and publishes something, amazing ideas pop out. But if you marry this woman, you have to live a married life only through letters from afar, and if you sit down and meet her in person for even just 5 minutes, you feel like you've fallen into a deep pit of heavy worries. She seems like a person full of worries, anxieties, and melancholy, like nursing and sitting next to a dying patient or praying next to a corpse. Because of that, it's a pity that a deep, invisible melancholy is clouding her clean face like the rainy season sky. If you marry this woman, the fortune that had come up to the doorstep will surely run away in terror.

Jeong-nyang
22 years old. This woman is the daughter of an executive at a trading company. She is a close friend of Sin-nyang, whom I introduced earlier. People call her a literary girl, and she considers herself to be one, but I have never seen her writing published anywhere. She is a very high-collar (하이카라인) woman, lively, and dresses well, a showy beauty like a foreign actress. She knows how to socialize with men, is good at entertaining guests, and meeting this woman never makes you feel stuffy or worried. She has a good physique, <60> is lively, not too ignorant, and her family is well-off, so she doesn't lack the money needed to live luxuriously. She is a flawless modern beauty. And this woman has the talent to play the mandolin well. One day, on a moonlit night, I was sitting in the courtyard of her house, listening to her mandolin, and casually said,
"A friend of mine who writes novels named K is quite good at playing the mandolin." She immediately said, "Oh, do you really know that K? The K who writes those sentimental novels! Oh my... I love the sentimental parts of his novels the most. By the way, what does K look like? Is he handsome? How old is he? Is he still young? Is he married? I heard he's very romantic. Is he really that romantic? He's a novelist, so of course he's very romantic, right? I wish I could meet him. Mr. Ssang S, please come to our house with him sometime. I would be so grateful. I'll even treat you."
It seems she doesn't even know what the word "sentimental" means, yet she throws it around carelessly, not knowing where the sentimental parts are in K's novels. And she's a terribly vain woman who wants to try out that rumored romance, so she's a truly dangerous woman. Who can guarantee that this woman won't abandon this kind of psychology even after marrying me?

Im-nyang
24 years old. This woman is a great beauty and has a great education, but her personality is too stiff, so she's been diligently looking for a marriage but hasn't been able to resolve it yet. A woman without personality is truly unbearable. But a woman who respects her personality too much and thinks only her own personality is important, like this woman, is unsuitable for the three-legged race (량인삼각) called married life. It's easy to fall because the feet don't match, so even if it's a pity, for the sake of her personality, it's best to recommend a single life for a woman with such a stiff personality.

Yu-nyang
20 years old. She's a beautiful woman with a virtuous appearance, but like Cha-nyang, whom I introduced before, her flaw is that she's a philanthropist. Just by moving from one chapel to another without attending one diligently, her unrefined nature is revealed.

Jang-nyang
24 years old. Rumor has it that OO Chapel is being run by "this woman," so many people admire "this woman," and there are so many single people attending the chapel because of this woman. But the lucky man who monopolized this woman among those many people unfortunately died last year. She is good at singing, but she is even better at playing the piano, so she is now giving private piano lessons while going to a wealthy home. Her husband is dead, but her body is at the height of its youth <61>, so because of her beauty that is not yet fresh but fully ripe, the young men who had admired her from the beginning are starting to admire her even more sincerely, and on top of that, new admirers are being born, truly making her the target of hope for countless people. Judging from her makeup and the way she dresses, she is by no means dull, and although she never publishes anything, judging from the letters she writes, she has considerable knowledge and writing skills. Although everyone says that love letters are all masterpieces, this woman's letters are not only masterpieces but also show the power of reading many books in every chapter.
As much as she has read many books, this woman naturally thinks that she should wait for a good match and remarry. She is a new person and a strong modern woman in that she does not have weak and foolish thoughts such as, "What's the point of someone as unlucky as me getting married again?" or "Remarrying is not something to be proud of." And this woman is quickly paying attention to my personal matters. If I'm busy and don't change my tie, she says, "That tie doesn't suit the season anymore," or "The new one you're wearing doesn't look as good as the previous one." I am always grateful that she cares as if it were her own business, such as, "Why haven't you gotten a haircut when it's time?" And judging from how she remembers what I like and dislike from my careless words, or from the letters she sends me, I believe that she will never shake her head if I ask her to marry me. But there is only one thing that bothers me: she often mentions her dead husband without realizing it whenever she speaks. It must be true that a woman cannot forget her first man for her entire life, whether for good or bad. But that is never a welcome thing for the other man. Furthermore, it would be a big problem for each other if she compared everything to her ex-husband even after getting married.

Sin-nyang
21 years old. She is good at Japanese, good at writing, good at giving speeches, worked as the president of the student council when she was in school, and is working as the secretary-general of the alumni association after graduation. Judging from the fact that her cheekbones are protruding or that her hair is not soft, you can tell that her will is firm and she has some stubbornness. She is not particularly beautiful, but she is not ugly either. Her strong will and active, adaptable personality, almost comparable to a man, more than make up for the lack of beauty. However, it seems ridiculous that this woman's nose is big and her personality is close to masculine, but she herself will experience many hardships and make her husband unhappy. Whenever I see this woman, I quietly think that among the older women I know, those with big noses and masculine faces and personalities are often widows or live like widows. Ssang S doesn't do great things <62>, but I can't bear to die early enough to make my wife a widow.

Son-nyang
19 years old. Her family is good and her appearance is good, but she has the same conditions as Park-nyang, the kindergarten teacher I introduced earlier. The only difference is that her father has a lot of money and no sons.

Go-nyang
23 years old. She went to Japan to study dentistry and came back, but she hasn't opened a practice yet and is looking for a groom to get married before opening one. As such, she suddenly socializes with men frequently and puts great effort into her face, figure, and clothes, making her a clear and bright woman like a polished mirror. But this woman has curly hair, buck teeth, and was born in Hamgyeong Province. She's a modern woman who likes to dress up, but her hair is naturally curly, so she'll save money on perms, but having buck teeth on top of that is scary. A friend from Hamgyeong Province whom I know personally divorced his wife and started a new life with a new woman, and that new woman had curly hair and buck teeth. Everyone envied the man's happiness because she was friendly, good at socializing, and knew a lot, but the man was always horrified whenever he talked about his wife because she had a strong will and fought a lot. They say that fighting is sometimes fun in a marriage, but when this woman gets a hysterical fit even in her sleep, she kicks off the blanket, gets up naked, bites and tears at her husband, and screams. So she can only feel relieved by kicking her naked husband out of the room, and if she can't kick her husband out naked, she opens the door and runs out naked, so the husband always surrenders in any case. While that man was in jail for 3 years, that woman picked another man who was easy to undress and left, so after that man came out of jail, his friends congratulated him, saying, "The only thing that came out of the 3 years in jail was that the naked ghost ran away," so the reader can imagine how scary she was. I know that not all people with curly hair and buck teeth are like that, but even thinking about it makes me shudder. Just imagine. What would happen if a woman screamed in the middle of the night and ran out the door naked?

Byeon-nyang
22 years old. A woman who suddenly became famous after giving a lecture at the Youth Hall during the OO competition last fall. She has a plump, modern type of body, and has a wonderful and attractive appearance, but it is an unusual characteristic for a woman these days that she is indifferent to what she wears or her shoes. She knows a lot, is good at recitation, and her father is also a great celebrity, but it is a cute characteristic that she does not act arrogant or boast about herself at all. By the way, I don't know where she learned it, but she started wearing perfume, showing off a chic taste. And another characteristic of this woman is that she talks well and imitates others well when she is in a good mood. She imitates gestures, hand gestures, eye gestures, and even voices perfectly after seeing or hearing them only once. She imitates Yun Chi-ho's face-shaking speeches, Yu Seong-jun's charming sermons, and Ok Seon-jin's seductive lectures, and lately she has made it a habit to imitate me everywhere people gather. Anyway, she is a wonderful beauty and a charming female figure.
"I will willingly become the slave of the man I find to be my ideal (理想). I don't care if he doesn't have money, I don't care if I have to suffer." That's a great thing to say. But she never says what kind of person her ideal man is. So it has become a big homework (宿題) among the men and female friends who surround and admire her.
Once, when she came to visit me, strangely enough, not only was it just the two of us meeting quietly that day, but her mood seemed to have sunk. So I took the opportunity to ask her politely.
"A person who is trustworthy and receives reliable trust from individuals and many people, a person who has the ability to lead not only his wife but also many people, a person who does not easily lose heart, a person who can fully express his ambitions and intentions in front of others in any case and convince many people, either by giving a speech or by writing to that extent, a person who is a worker and understands hobbies," she says. That's a very solid thought.
But before I could find out whether I met those conditions, I unfortunately started to hear words that made me lose affection.
I don't know if this woman recently read a novel by Korontai, but she said,
"Sexual hunger between men and women is exactly the same as hunger for food, so just as it is not a sin for a hungry person to get cold rice from anyone, it is not a sin for a person hungry for sex to satisfy that hunger from someone else, and it is not defiling chastity. Don't you think that's right? How can you blame a hungry person for getting cold rice from someone else...?"
It's good to hear when someone other than your wife says it, but there's no one who would like it and be happy to hear his wife say such things... I'm old-fashioned, so I started to retreat slowly as soon as I heard those words.
Now, let's end the exhibition here. There are about 10 more people <64>, but they are mostly similar to the people I have introduced above, so there is no need to introduce them separately.
I thank the readers who have continued to listen to the tedious introduction until now, and I will add one excuse, which is that those who read this article are likely to misunderstand that "Ssang S is a person who boasts a lot about himself." I have only selected and written about people who initially had goodwill toward Ssang S, and those who did not have goodwill were not included in the number, and I have listed the shortcomings one by one and made it so that "there is no one who says it's good, so you won't have a place to go after all." As I initially promised, this side is better or that side is better, both have advantages and disadvantages, so it is difficult to choose, so I am writing frankly without hiding even small flaws, as I am leaving it up to the readers to choose.
If this not-so-funny prank has contributed even a single laugh to the readers suffering in the midsummer heat, it would be a fortunate thing, and if this uninteresting trivial writing (雜文) has been of some reference to some unmarried men and women, it would be even more fortunate.
However, such flattery is unnecessary for this trivial writing, and the reader still has the condition to choose which woman is "still the best among them" as promised. It is a matter of personnel (人事), so it is impossible to publicly ask for a vote, and if there is a tasteful person who sends a subtle secret letter, I plan to invite them to the wedding someday and treat them to a simple cup of rice wine. Well, stay in peace and see you at the wedding. - (End) - <65>

諧譃·諷刺·奇拔 新婦候補者展覽會
						雙S 主催
이 뎐람회가 여러 달 걸치게 되닛가 끗도 나기 전에 쌍 에쓰에게 편지가 만히 와서 탈이 낫다. 『대단히 흥미 잇게 닑고 잇스나 쓰시는 솜씨가 녀자들에게 넘어 심하지 안슴닛가』 이따위 편지는 대개 녀자들의 보낸 것이다. 자미는 잇스나 넘어 가슴에 찔니는 곳이 만흐니 조곰 부드럽게 해 달나는 말이겟지...『당신 성미가 그러케 심하게 고르다가는 평생 장가들기는 어려우리다.』 이러케 써보낸 것도 암만해도 녀자의 필적 갓다. 그런가 하면 또 한편으로는 『쌍 에쓰씨의 통쾌한 풍자를 감사함니다.』 하거나 『쌍 에쓰씨에게 특청 하오니 긔왕이면 더 로골뎍으로 더 통쾌하게 써 주기를 바람니다』하는 편지가 제일 만히 오는대 이것들은 아마 한 두번 쯤은 녀자에게 핀잔을 밧엇거나 속임을 당한 남자들인 것 갓고 『저는 아즉 미혼 남자로서 신부를 물색중이온대 선생의 쓰시는 긔사가 만히 참고가 되야 감사 함니다.』하는 편지들은 그중 진실한 편지다.
하여커나 나는 나대로 아초의 약속대로 아초의 방침대로 진렬을 계속해 가지 안으면 안된다. 더 통쾌한지 넘어 심한지 미지근한지 조흔 참고가 되는지 안되는지 그것은 독자의 일이지 내가 계관 할 일은 안인 까닭이다. 자아 진렬의 계속이다.

咸孃
22세. 어느 커다란 제약(製藥)회사 주인의 누이로 상업학교를 작년 봄에 졸업한 미인이다.＜58＞얼골이며 맵시며 말소리 행동까지 엇지 싀원스런지 겨을에는 엇덜지 몰나도 녀름철에는 굉장히 조흔 녀자다. 거의 어름 덩어리 엽해 안젓는 늣김을 주는 녀자다. 졸업한 후에도 진고개 어느 상뎜에 뎜원(店員)으로 취직 소개가 잇는 것을 『그 까짓걸 누가 단여요』하고 외마듸 소리로 차버린 것도 서늘한 짓이요. 주판(珠板)을 노타가 조곰만 거북하면 『나는 못놋켓서』하고 거침엄시 업허 버리고 도라 안는 것도 싀원스럽다. 그러고 또 한가지 다른 사람이 도뎌히 흉내내지 못하게 싀원스러운 짓은 아모것이고 조곰도 긔탄업시 이저 버리기를 잘하는 것이다. 수건 부채 분갑 손에 들고 단이는 것은 무엇이던지 싀원스럽게 이저 버린다. 그가 안젓다가 이러나는 자리에 떠러저 잇는 물건이 업는 때가 희귀할 만큼 표적을 남겨 놋는다.
『아가씨 이것 아가씨가 노으신 것 안임닛가?』 이 소리를 듯기에 우등 졸업을 한 녀자다. 착은 착은 해야 할 상업학교를 졸업하고도 이 지경이닛가 까닭을 모를 일이다.
『예전 처녀처럼 반짓고리나 정리하고 잇거나 수-첩이나 만즈고 안젓슬 때는 안이닛가요. 현대 녀자가 이것저것을 일일이 긔억하고 잇스려서야 신경쇠약에 걸니지 안켓서요? 한 엽흐로 척-척- 이저 버려버리는 것이 깨긋하고 가즈런해서 나는 조와요. 필요한 때 다시 사면 그만이지요.』
이 녀자 남에게서 온 련애편지도 질질 흘리고 단일 터이니 이러케 깨끗하다가는 해수욕장 가튼데 갓다가 어느 것이 자긔 남편인지도 이저 버리기 쉬울 녀자다.

安孃
21세. 이 녀자야말로 그림 속에서 빠저 나온 듯 십은 어엽븐 미인이다. 상냥스럽고 마음씨가 착하고 O명을 우등으로 졸업하엿고 아버지는 XX학교 교장이시고 가장 얌전한 후보자인데 이 가엽슨 미인이 읏전 일인지 약벙(藥甁)을 떠나지 못한다. 마치 약병에 사람의 옷을 입혀 안친 폭이다. 3년만 집안에 병이 업스면 부자 안될 사람이 업다는대 ...이 녀자하고 결혼을 하려면 약병하고 결혼을 하는 셈치고 해야겟다.

朴孃
21세. 22세. 작년 가을에 OO대회 때에 청년회관에서 강연을 한 번 하고 갑작이 유명하여진 녀자다. 토실토실한 현대뎍 미인타입의 륙톄를 가젓고 훌륭한 매력잇는 용모를 가젓것만은 닙은 옷이나 구두 가튼 데에 무관심한 것도 요사이 녀자치고는 남다른 특성이요 아는 것이 만코 독창도 잘하고 아버지도 훌륭한 명사요 하것만은 조곰도 건방지게 굴거나 자긔 자랑을 하지 안는 것도 귀여운 특성이다. 그런대 이마적은＜63＞어데서 배워 왓는지 일설에 향수를 바르기 시작하야 씩-크한 맛을 보인다. 그리고 이 녀자의 또 한가지 특성은 긔분이 조흔 때는 이야기를 잘하고 남의 흉내를 잘 내는 것이다. 몸짓 손짓 눈짓이며 목소리까지 한번만 보거나 듯고도 여전하게 흉내를 내인다. 윤치호씨의 얼골 흔드는 연설 유성준씨의 앙징한 설교 옥선진씨의 간드러진 강연 한 번 듯고 흉내 내지 못하는 것이 업는대 요사이는 여러 사람 모인 데서마다 내 흉내를 내이는 것이 버릇이 되엿다. 좌우간 훌륭한 미인이요 구수한 귀염성 잇는 녀류 인물이다.
『나는 내가 리상(理想)하는 남자를 발견하기만 하면 자진하여서 그 남자의 노예가 될 터 이야요. 돈이 업서도 조와요 고생을 하여도 조아요.』 한다. 그것은 훌륭한 말이다. 그러나 자긔의 리상의 남자가 엇던 성질읫 사람이라고는 결코 이약이하지 안는다. 그래서 그를 에워 싸고 돌면서 찬미하는 남자들이나 녀자 동무사이에는 그것이 커다란 숙뎨(宿題)가 되여 잇는 것이다.
한 번 그가 나에게 놀너왓슬 때 이상하게도 그 날은 단 둘이 맛나 조용 할 뿐 안이라 그의 긔분이 폭- 가라 안즌 듯 하기에 그 긔회에 그것을 정중히 무러 보앗섯다.
『묵에가 잇서서 개인에게나 여러 사람에게나 미덤성이 잇는 신뢰를 밧는 사람, 자긔 안해뿐 안이라 만흔 사람을 지도할 만한 능이 잇는 사람, 용이히 락심하지 안는 사람, 엇던 경우에던지 남의 압헤서 자긔 포부와 의사를 충분히 발표하야 여러 사람을 설복식힐 수 잇슬만큼 강연을 할 줄 알거나 그 만큼 글을 쓰는 사람, 이러케 일꾼이면서 취미 생활을 리해하는 사람 』 이라 한다. 퍽 튼튼한 생각이다.
그러나 나는 내가 그 조건에 합격이 되는지 못되는지 이 녀자의 의견을 알아보기 전에 불행히 정 떠러지는 말을 듯기 시작하엿다.

『남녀간에 성(性)에 주린 것은 밥에 주린 것과 꼭 맛찬가지라지요 그래서 배곱흔 사람이 아모에게나 찬밥을 어더 먹은 것이 죄될 것 업는 것과 가티 성에 주린 사람이 다른 사람에게서 그 긔갈을 채우는 것이 허물 될 것도 업고 뎡조를 더럽히는 것도 안이라고 한다는 말이 나는 올흔 말이라고 생각되여요. 그러치 안슴닛가 배곱흔 사람이 남에게 찬밥을 어더 먹엇다고 엇더케 그것을 책망 할 수가 잇겟슴닛가...』
이런 말은 자긔 안해 안인 사람이 할 때는 조와도 직접 자긔 안해가 그런 말을 하는 것을 조와하고 깃버 할 사람은 업슬 것이닛가... 나는 구식이라 그런지 그 말을 듯기 시작하면서부터 슬금 슬금 퇴각하기 시작하엿다.
자아 이것으로 진렬을 끗막기로 하나, 이외에 또 ＜64＞ 10여 인이 잇스나 대개 우에 소개 해 온 사람 중에 어느 사람과 공통하는 조건의 인물들인즉 따로히 소개할 필요가 업슬 것 갓다.
이때까지 지리한 소개를 계속해 들어 준 독자들께 감사하면서 한 가지 변명을 첨부해 두겟스니 그것은 이 글을 닑은 이가 잣칫하면 『쌍에쓰가 자긔 자랑을 굉장히 하는 사람이라』고 오해하기 쉬운 그것이다. 애초에 쌍에쓰에게 호의를 가진 사람만을 추려서 쓴 것이라 호의를 가지지 안이한 이는 수에 넛치를 안엇고 일일히 단뎜을 들어서 『한 사람도 조타는 사람이 업스니 결국 장가 갈 곳이 업지 안느냐』 하는 말이 나게 한 것은 처음 약속처럼 이 편이 나을가 저 편이 나을가 일장일단이 다 달러서 선택이 곤란하닛가 독자 여러분께 선택해 보아 달나고 내여 막기자니 조곰아한 험이라도 숨기지 안코 솔직하게 쓴 것뿐이다.
이 우슴거리도 못되는 붓 작난이 삼복더위 중에 부닥기는 독자들께 단 한 번의 우슴이라도 이바지 하엿다면 다행한 일이요 이 조흔 긔사 되지 못하는 잡문(雜文)도 혹 일부 미혼 남녀에게 다소의 참고라도 되는 뎜이 잇섯다면 더욱 다행할 일이겟다.
그러나 그런 수작은 이 잡문으로서는 안이해도 조흔 인사치레이고 독자는 약속대로 이 중에서 어느 녀자가 『그래도 그 중에 나으리라』고 골라 주어야 할 조건이 남엇다. 인사(人事)에 관한 일이니 공공연히 투표하여 달나할 수는 업는 일이고 넌즛이 투서를 하여주는 풍치객이 잇다면 어느 때 할는지 몰라도 혼인날 초대 하여다가 박주 일배라도 대접을 할 요량은 가지고 잇다. 자- 평안히들 계시다가 혼인날 맛나십세.-(끗)-＜65＞
