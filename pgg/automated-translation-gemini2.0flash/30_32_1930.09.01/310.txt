소식 (News)

Lee Ki-young often boasts that he can host a round of gambling even with an empty paper box and not a penny to his name when he's drunk. Recently, at a gambling place, someone was supposed to pay up, but didn't do it properly. So, as usual, Mr. Lee made a grand statement, saying he would pay if that guy didn't... His friends were skeptical and checked his wallet again. It seemed like it had been a long time since any money had been in that wallet, as only mold was growing even in the corners (旱). Everyone burst out laughing.

It's well known that Kim Hwal-ran went to the United States recently. According to recent news, she has enrolled in a university and plans to study for about three years before returning to Korea. She is determined to obtain a doctorate this time. What about marriage? <104>
