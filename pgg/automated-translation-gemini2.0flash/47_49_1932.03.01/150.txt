A Blind Man's Suspicion

A blind man was walking down the street wearing a tanggeon (宕巾, a type of Korean hat) and looking back.
A passerby said, "Hey, wouldn't it be better to take off that tanggeon and look back?"
The blind man smiled and said, "You probably want to take my tanggeon if I take it off."
Shin Sun-beom

Blind Man Watching a Ssireum Match

A friend said, "Hey, since you can't see, I'll poke you in the ribs. When I do, say that guy is good at ssireum." So they watched the ssireum match, and as they were about to go home, he poked the blind man in the ribs.
The blind man exclaimed, "Ah, that guy is good at ssireum!"

Dried Persimmons, Dried Persimmons

A while ago, a poor yangban (nobleman) had no choice but to become a dried persimmon seller with a few dried persimmons. However, he couldn't bring himself to say "Buy dried persimmons!" So, he just carried them around. As he entered a village, a potter was passing by, shouting, "Buy pots! Buy pots!"
The dried persimmon seller thought, "Alright, now I can do it!" and, with a trembling voice, barely followed the potter, saying,
"Dried persimmons too, dried persimmons too (고깜두 고깜두)."
Haha—he meant to say, "Dried persimmons too, buy them!"
Yang Geun-i

A Strange Question

On a very hot day, a Sunday school teacher droned on and on with a sermon.
Teacher: "Everyone! The sermon is over. Does anyone have any questions?"
Student: "Yes! Um, teacher, what time is it now?... ..."
Pyeongyang Kim Yang-su

Arithmetic Problem

A student went to a store and said,
Student: "I'd like to order ten 2-jeon pencils, two 8-jeon brushes, and four 5-jeon notebooks. How much is that in total?"
The clerk was calculating on the abacus,
"That's 56 jeon in total."
Student: "Thank you for your trouble. I just needed to know the answer for my arithmetic homework." And he left.
Lee Seon-ha

No Talking

Jeong-dong: "Let's have a contest to see who can stay silent for an hour."
Bok-dong: "That's a good idea."
Su-dong: "Whoever speaks is a son of a bitch."
And so they decided to stay silent. Then, Su-dong's father came to find Su-dong.
"Su-dong-ah, are you here?"
Su-dong: "Yes."
Jeong-dong: "You're a son of a bitch now."
Bok-dong: "I'm the only one who hasn't spoken. You're all sons of bitches."

"Eat, please."

A man went to the market and asked a pine nut seller,
"What is that?"
Seller: "Eat, please (자시오)."
Man: "Thank you, I can't eat if you say 'eat, please.'"
Seller: "Don't you dare eat without paying."
Man: "You said 'eat, please.' Pay me."

장님에 疑心
장님이 길에서 탕건쓴체 뒤을 보고잇섯습니다.
지나가던 行人「여보시오, 그 탕건좀 버서놋코 뒤좀보는거시 좃치안소」한즉
장님 빙긋우스면서「내탕건 버서노으면 당신이 가저 갈 모양이구려」하엿소.
愼淳範

盲人씨름구경
친구「여보게. 자네는 눈이 어두우니 내가 자네 엽구리를 쿠-ㄱ찌르거든 그놈 씨름 잘한다 하게」그리하야 씨름구경을 다하고 집에 가자고 봉사의 엽구리를 찔넛더니
봉사「앗다 그놈 씨름 잘한다.」

고깜두- 고깜두
조금 오래 전에 어떤 가난한 양반 한 분이 할수할수 업서서 고깜 몃고치를 가지고 고깜장수로 나서기는 하엿스나 참아 「고깜사려-」소리는 나가지아너서 그냥들고 도라 다니는 판에 마츰 어느동리에 들어가니 독장사가
「독사려- 독사려-」
하고 웨치며 지나가는지라 이 고깜장수 양반「올치 인제 됏구나」하고 떨니는 소리로 억지로 독장사의 꽁문이를 따라가며
「고깜두 고깜두」
하드랍니다. 하하- 하제 고깜두 사란말이겟지요!
羊近而

별 質問
몹시도 더운날 주일학교 선생이 지리하게 한바탕 설교를 하고나드니
선생「여러분! 설교는 끗나스니 무슨 질문할 것 업습니까?」
생도「네! 저. 선생님. 지금 시게는 몃십닛까?... ...」
平壤 金養洙

算術問題
한 생도 어느 상점에 가서
생도「주문이요. 2전짜리 연필 열자루 8전짜리 붓 두자루, 5전짜리 공책 네권 모다얼마요?」
점원이 주산을 놋타가
「모다 56전이요」
생도「수고햇소. 그만하면 산술 숙제는 알엇소」하고 가버렷다.
李善夏

말안하기
정동「우리 한시간을 말하지 안키 내기를 하자.」
복동「그것 좃타.」
수동「만일 말하는 사람은 개아들이다.」
하고 말 안하기로 되엿다. 그때 수동아버지 수동을 차자와서.
「수동아. 여긔잇니?」
수동「네.」
정동「너는 인제는 개아들이다.」
복동「말 안하는 사람은 나하나뿐이다. 너의들은 다 개아들이다.」

「자시오」

한사람이 장에 가서 잣파는 사람에게
「그것은 무엇이요」
상인「자시오」
한사람「고맙소, 자시오 하는데 못먹겟소」
상인「돈도 아니내고 자시오」
한사람「당신이 자시오 햇지. 돈내」 ＜30＞
