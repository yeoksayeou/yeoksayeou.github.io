一丁目一九一ノ二 金大甲 (Ichome 191-no-2 Kim Tae-gap) (Apology/Editor's Note)
사고·편집후기 (Apology/Editor's Note)

Ichome 191-no-2 Kim Tae-gap

Regarding the article "Secret Family Exploration" in the February issue of this magazine, it seems many people have misunderstood the address Seodaemun-jeong 1-jeongmok 19xx beonji (西大門정 一정목 一九××번지) etc., as referring to the family of Mr. Kim. We would like to clarify that this is not the case. <15>

Named Entities

西大門정	Sŏdaemun-jŏng
金大甲	Kim Tae-gap
