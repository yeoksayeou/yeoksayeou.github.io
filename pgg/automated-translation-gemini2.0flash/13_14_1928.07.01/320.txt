발행일	1928년 07월 01일
기사제목	내가 만일 斷髮娘과 結婚한다면 (If I Were to Marry a Short-Haired Woman)
기사형태	설문 (Questionnaire)

If I Were to Marry a Short-Haired Woman
Only After Receiving Five Promises

Go Han-saeng

If I were to marry a bobbed-hair beauty - it's quite an unusual question. However, when I hear this, an old memory comes to mind. It was when I was involved in the management of a theatrical troupe called 『Towolhoe』 (토월회).

One of the actresses suddenly cut her hair. She had cut her hair, but she didn't have money to buy Western clothes. Frustrated, she ordered Chinese food on credit and complained all night. In her drunken anger, she scolded her colleagues, took a taxi on credit, and wandered around all night, only to be left with fatigue and debt. When she cut her hair, it was because she was upset that the person she loved didn't trust her.
"So, even if I cut my hair, you won't trust me?"
She cut her hair in the same way as the bobbed-hair beauties that were popular in the world. But she didn't seem to regret it much.

The next morning, she opened her eyes and immediately went to the mirror and said to the man,
"Honey, go to the dressing room behind the stage and bring me the curling iron."
She said. When he brought the curling iron, this time,
"Pour some alcohol on it, set it on fire, and heat up the curling iron."
She said. After curling her hair with the iron,
"I can't wear Korean clothes with this hair." <118>
She said. He did all the errands that didn't cost money, but he was dumbfounded because the talk of Western clothes cost money.
"Well, Western clothes are good, but where's the money?"
"Oh, then what should I do?"
She said, almost despairingly, stretching out her legs and almost crying.
"If you really needed Western clothes, why didn't you discuss whether I could get the money for them before you cut your hair so rashly?"
"Ah - so you let me cut my hair, and now you're going to upset me again."
She frowned like an angry queen. Her lover, blinded by love, pawned other things and barely made a few tens of won to buy her a Western suit and a hat.

Now that she had a Western suit and a hat,
"Honey, let's go for a walk."
The sound of her nagging increased. But he didn't want to go out. One night, mischievous children threw stones at them from behind, shouting,
"Bobbed-hair idiot, bobbed-hair beauty."
The stone hit his shoulder and bruised it. And sometimes he met friends and heard them say,
"You're really living the good life. Walking around with a bobbed-hair beauty in Western clothes..."
He was ridiculed a lot. One day, he was even interrogated by a police officer for being out late.

Since then, he shook his head at the mention of a walk.
But if his lover nagged too much, he had no choice but to take her out. His widowed mother, who used to say before she cut her hair,
"Now that you're attached, bring her home to live with you."
But since she cut her hair, she said,
"Is that even a woman?"
She didn't even look at her anymore. During those few weeks, the late summer passed and it was time to wear autumn clothes.

But unlike summer clothes, women's autumn clothes cost forty or fifty won. But there was no way to raise such a large sum of money.
She didn't want to go out because she didn't have Western clothes, and eventually disappeared one night.

I still have vague memories of this. And I think this memory has become the core of my view of bobbed-hair beauties.
Of course, I believe that among the bobbed-hair beauties scattered around the world, there are sincere ones and simple ones. But it is also a fact that there are bobbed-hair beauties like this, and it seems that the trend of the world is to take these kinds of frivolous bobbed-hair beauties as the standard for bobbed-hair beauties. <119>
Therefore, first of all, if I were to live with a bobbed-hair beauty, I would need the following promises:
"Even if you can't get Western clothes, you won't run away."
"Don't bother your husband by curling your hair with a curling iron morning and night."
"Even if you go for a walk with other women, don't walk side by side until your hair grows back."
"When you go to see your parents-in-law or when your parents-in-law come to visit, you must hide your bobbed hair by tying your hair at the nape of your neck."
"No matter how much you uplift your family, don't shake your head and create the impression that it's hard to tell whether you're a woman or a ruffian."

If these few contracts are not strictly adhered to, it seems difficult to even act like a human being, let alone a mother.
I feel a sense of guilt for being too stubborn and making it seem like I'm criticizing just for the sake of criticizing. But this is my opinion from the most solemn standpoint of a wife who will live together for a hundred years.
If you're not looking for a wife to spend a hundred years with, but a bobbed-hair woman as a temporary companion to go to cafes or wander around aimlessly to temples or something like that, then I'm saying that beauty is the only unconditional requirement.

Various Things to be Grateful For

Pa-yeong

Instead of explicitly saying "bobbed-hair woman" (단발랑), let's just call her a modern girl (모던-껄) and think about marrying one of them.

A woman who doesn't know what to call her father's nephew but thinks she's great just because she frequents the Jinogae (진고개) fabric store. A woman who, when her beloved father comes to visit her at the inn, says, "The errand boy (말음이) from our house has come," and asks her father, whom she calls errand boy, to buy her silk socks. A woman who can't read a newspaper properly but boasts of her worldwide knowledge when it comes to knowing the names of active male actors. I can't even dream of marrying such a great modern girl.
But if someone like me were to die ten or twelve times and be reborn with such excellent qualifications, I would think that way. <120>

First, I need to have enough wealth to spend as we please without any special income, or at least appear to have it even if I don't.
Second, I need to make my face look like an active actor with powder and whatever my occupation is.
Third, I need to publish articles in magazines so that they don't forget me, and when I go to lectures, I need to be a part of the speakers and give good lectures so that applause continues. When I go to concerts, I need to be a welcomed musician, and when I go to sports events, I need to be a popular athlete there too. When I go to swing, I need to be the best at swinging (because modern girls are attracted to popular people in every field they go to, and they want to be close to them regardless of whether their husbands are next to them or not).
Fourth, I should never insist on my own way and always be a faithful servant to my wife.
If the woman says, "Is that a bean?" (어게 콩이지요)
"Yes, that's a very good bean."
"No, it's not a bean, it looks like a red bean."
"Yes, that's right, it's not a bean, it's a great red bean."
"Oh, it's not a red bean, it looks like mung bean (for making green bean jelly)."
"Yes, it's definitely mung bean. It's a good mung bean."
"Oh, no, it's not mung bean, it's millet."
"Yes, it's definitely millet. There's even red millet."
Like this......
Fifth, if my parents are alive, I should secretly treat them well, but in front of my wife, I should be prepared to forget about my parents and live with her.
Sixth, whenever we go to see anything, I should have arrangements to get invitation tickets instead of regular tickets.
Fortunately, if I have all these seven qualifications and marry a modern girl, there are a few things that I must, must do.

First, even if I can't buy a pot (釜) or set the table, I need to prepare a piano, a phonograph, a radio, a trump card, and mahjong (麻雀).
Second, I need to open credit accounts (通帳) at the meat shop, rice shop, Chinese restaurant, side dish store, laundry, cosmetics store, and fabric store.
Third, I should eat breakfast alone and not wake up my sleeping wife.
Fourth, I should research and implement contraception (避妊法) and take tonics (補陽劑) regularly. <121>
Fifth, I should designate Wednesdays and Saturdays as movie days, Sundays as walking days, and Tuesdays and Thursdays as days when my wife goes out.
Sixth, when the man goes to a "party" (연회) that doesn't have any special business, or when he goes to a house of courtesans, he must bring his wife with him, and when the man has to go alone and inevitably stay overnight, he should notify his wife in advance so that she can also engage in free activities and avoid future complaints.
Seventh, when there are female or male guests visiting only the woman, the man should go out even if he doesn't want to, according to his wife's orders.
In addition to these grateful things, there is one more grateful thing, which is the divorce and the aftermath of the divorce.
In a world where there are so many wives who say, "I am the ghost of this house" and hang themselves because the man wants to live apart more than death, this one will kick first and say, "I'm leaving, let's meet again," and leave refreshed. It may be ruthless, but there's nothing to be annoyed about. What's really grateful is that even after the divorce, she doesn't complain or curse at this side at all, but even while living with another husband, she sometimes sends a nonchalant invitation letter to come and visit quietly.
Oh, immature modern boy! Throw away your bones and serve these holy queens!

Only Men Who Fulfill 10 Major Conditions

Gangchon Gu-bu

What if I were to marry a "modern girl" (모던껄)? What is the life of a husband who marries a "modern girl" like? I, for one, do not have the confidence to write about this topic. This is because I have never been able to associate with a "modern girl" or even come into contact with one. Therefore, I do not know their behavior, character, psychology, or actions, and even less so their domestic life. However, based on my superficial observations of them, I have a vague feeling that they are not all that bad, so I am now picking up my pen. <122> Before I get into the main text, there are a few words I want to write as a digression. It's like this: When you look at women these days, 8 or 9 out of 10 seem to be "modern girls." Therefore, I don't want to give the noun "modern girl" specifically to women who "cut their hair and walk with their buttocks swaying." I want to collectively call "modern girls" those who walk down the street with "tremerries (트레머리)," "stiletto heels (송곳구두)," "short skirts (깡둥치마)," and "silk parasols (비단양산)," saying, "With my face and attitude like this, I can say that I am a new woman without being too embarrassed!" That's why I say that there is some distance between ordinary new women and so-called "modern girls," but it is extremely difficult to distinguish them exactly. This is what is meant by "Who knows the gender of the bird (誰知鳥之雌雄格)?"
Recently, women are very fond of "extravagance" (豪奢). They call this the narrow-mindedness (險口) of men, but how can we deny the undeniable fact? Of course, they may have a reason for being "extravagant," but in a word, it seems to be expressed from the consciousness of wanting to strongly impress their existence on the earth through "self-expression" (自己表現). In any case, it is a strange fact that the boundaries between wives and women, female students and female office workers, virgins and entertainers are so blurred these days. They spend their days doing their best to fill their minds and bodies with makeup, extravagance, going to the theater today, a concert tomorrow, an exhibition in the morning, and a walk in the evening. The premise is too long, so I will now enter the main text.
If I were to marry a "modern girl"! If I were married to a "modern girl"! What would their domestic life and the life of the husband be like? If you were to marry them, there would be many conditions in the first place. 1st, there must be abundant material to satisfy her desires every day, 2nd, her orders must be absolutely obeyed, 3rd, parents-in-law must be isolated, 4th, the family's lifestyle must be cultural and Western, 5th, clothing stores, laundries, barber shops, grocery stores, and restaurants must be designated, 6th, she must be made to attend theaters, concerts, and entertainment, 7th, she must sleep until noon and go for walks and entertainment until two in the evening, 8th, chastity must be absolutely liberated, 9th, her actions must be left entirely to freedom and not restricted in any way, 10th, the man must always respect, treat well, and praise her.
In general, marriage with a "modern girl" will be established only after agreeing to the above 10 conditions. If marriage is established under these conditions, one can easily guess the life of the husband without asking, and one can know the family's circumstances formed by the combination with her without seeing them.
If even one of the above 10 conditions is not fulfilled, not obeyed, or resisted, the family, which was established under the solid oath of not being satisfied, will be broken in an instant. And when it comes to a husband with this kind of wife, he will only become a "sleeping partner" (잠동무) in common parlance, and he will never find the individuality of a spouse. When she goes out, just like when a great nobleman used to go out in those days, he must follow her, carrying her gloves or a pouch with cosmetics in it, as if he were carrying a floor servant (床奴), a tobacco pouch (담배쌈지), and a tobacco pipe (담배ㅅ대), to be praised as a kind "husband" (허스뺀드), and if he cooks rice at home like a fool, he must wash the dishes if the wife washes them, and the husband must wash the rice and cook it, and if the wife sweeps the room, the husband must roll up his sleeves and mop the floor, so that he will hear the praise of "my sleeping partner is as lovely and gentle as a sheep (羊)," and even if the wife goes out and does evil deeds with other men, he must close his eyes and pretend not to see it and just sit at home as a watchman to be a truly good husband. If you are hungry, you must endure it, and if your clothes are dirty, you must not say that they are dirty. Why? Because you originally got married under 10 conditions... If you ever go against or resist the wife's orders or demands, you should only do so after being prepared to divorce her. The orders of one's own parents can be disobeyed, but the orders of one's wife must be sacred and inviolable.
Now - only a man with this kind of resolve and this kind of heart will be qualified to marry a "modern girl," and husbands who marry "modern girls" must recognize that they will be "idiots" (등신) and "scarecrows" (허수아비), and that their life will be nothing more than that of a "sleeping partner" (잠동무). (End) <124>

잡지명	별건곤 제14호
발행일	1928년 07월 01일
기사제목	내가 만일 斷髮娘과 結婚한다면
기사형태	설문

내가 만일 斷髮娘과 結婚한다면
다섯가지 承諾을 밧고야

孤帆生

단발 미인과 결혼을 하면-좀 긔발한 문뎨이다. 그러나 나는 이 소리를 드를 때 한 가지 녯 긔억이 떠오른다. 그것은 내가 즉 일히 『토월회』라는 연극단톄 경영에 관계하얏 슬 때일이다.
녀배우의 한 사람이 돌연히 머리를 깍갓다. 머리는 깍갓스나 돈이 업서서 양복을 못해 입는다. 그것이 원통하야 외상으로 청요리를 차려다 노코 백알을 기우렷다. 술 김에 화가 나닛가 동모들을 껄고 외상 자동차를 타고 밤이 새도록 도라 다니다가 날이 새고 보니 남은 것은 피로와 외상갑 뿐이엇다. 그가 머리를 깍글 때에는 그가 사랑하는 사람이 그를 못 미더주는 것이 섭섭하다는 리유로
『자 내가 머리를 깍가도 나를 못 미들 터이요?』
세상에 류행되는 단발미인과 가튼 수작으로 머리는 깍가바렷다. 그러나 그는 그다지 앗가워하는 것 갓지는 안앗다.
그 이튼 날 아츰에 그는 눈을 뜨며 즉시 석경을 향하야 안더니 그 남자더러
『여보 저 무대 뒤 화장실에 가서 머리지지는 인두 좀 갓다 주어요.』
하얏다. 인두를 갓다 주니 이번에는
『알콜을 좀 부어서 불을 부처 가지고 인두를 좀 데워주어요.』
하얏다. 인두를 갈아가며 머리를 지지고 나더니
『암만해도 이 머리에 조선 옷은 못 입겟지』<118>
하얏다. 돈 안드는 심부름이야 하라는 대로 다 하얏스나 양복 타령은 돈드는 일이닛가 어안이 벙벙하얏다.
『글세 양복도 좃치만 돈이 어대잇나』
『아이 그러면 엇더케 한담.』
하여 그는 거의 절망뎍으로 두 다리를 뻐드며 거의 울 것 갓하얏다.
『양복을 꼭 입어야 될일 가트면 왜 나더러 양복갑 변통이 되겟느냐고 상의나 하고 깍지 함부로 깍갓단 말이요 』
『아-  그래 머리까지 깍게 해놋코 당신은 또 배 비위를 상해주겟소.』
하며 성낸 녀왕가티 아미를 쯩그렷다. 사랑에 눈이 어두운 그 애인은 다른 물건 뎐당을 잡혀서 겨우 몃 십원을 맨드러가지고 양복 한 벌과 모자 한 개를 사주엇다.
양장에 모자가 생기더니 이번에는
『여보, 우리 산보갑시다.』
조르는 소리가 곱장이는 늘게 되얏다. 그러나 그는 잘 나아가랴고 하지는 안앗다. 어느 날 밤에는 작란군 아해들이 뒤에서 돌을 던지며
『더벙머리 단발미인』
하는 소리를 드럿다. 돌이 엇개를 따려 멍도 드럿다. 또 어는 때는 친고들을 맛나
『자네도 여간 호강이 안일세 그려. 양장 한 단발미인 만다리고 다니며......』
하며 몹시 비웃는 소리도 드럿다. 어느 날은 늣게 도라다니다가 순사에게 취톄까지 당한 일이 잇섯다.
이런 일이 잇슨 이래로 그는 산보라면 머리를 흔들게 된 것이다.
그러나 애인이 너모나 몹시 조르면 하는 수 업시 껄녀 다니는 일도 잇섯다. 과부된 모친도 그가 단발을 하기 전까지는
『이왕, 정이 드럿거든 집에 다려다 살나.』
고 하더니 그가 단발을 한 이래로는
『온 그것이 계집이냐』
다시 도라보도 안는 형편에 이르고 마랏다. 그리하는 몃주일 동안에 느진녀름도 가고 가을 양복 입는 때가 되얏다.
그러나 녀름 옷과도 달나 여자의 가을 양복은 갑이 사오십원은 든다. 그러나 그럿케 그럿케 큰 돈은 주선할 도리가 업섯다.
양복이 업서서 나아가랴고 하지 안튼 그는 결국 하로 밤 어대로인지 자최를 숨겨 바리고 마랏다.

나는 이 가튼 긔억이 아즉도 어렴풋이 남아잇다. 그리하야 이 긔억이 나의 단발 미인관의 중추가 되야 바렷는가 한다.
그야 세상에 흣터저 떠도는 단발미인 중에는 진실한 분도 잇고 질소한 분도 잇슬 줄은 밋는다. 그러나 이 가튼 단발미인이 잇는 것도 사실이며, 세상의 대세가 이 가튼 오입장이 단발미인으로 단발미인의 표본을 삼는 것도 사실인 것 갓다.<119>
그러함으로 첫재 단발미인을 다리고 살냐면 나는
『비록 양복을 못어더 입드라도 다라나지 안을 일』
『아침이고 저녁이고 머리에 인두질 하는데 남편의 수고를 끼치지 말 일』
『비록 동부인 산보는 다녀도 머리를 다시 기를 동안은 나란히 서서는 다니지 말 일』
『싀부모를 뵈오려 갈 때에나 싀부모가 오시는 때에는 다러 꼭지를 뒤덜미에다가 붓드러 매서라도 단발을 감출 일』
『아모리 집안을 드려 업드라도 머리를 흔드러 계집인지, 망난인지 분간키 어렵은 악감을 이릇키게 하지 말 일』
이 몃 가지 계약은 굿게 직히지 안코는 산아희 노릇은 커냥 사람 노릇도 하기 어려울 것 갓다.
말이 너모 완고하야지고 맛치 흉을 보기 위해 흉을 본 글 갓게 된 렴의를 스사로 늣기게 된다. 그러나 이것은 백년을 해로할 안해라는 가장 엄숙한 견지에서 나아온 나의 의사이다.
그럿치 안코 카푸에나 껄고 다니고 글이나 절 가튼데로 닥치는 대로 놀너 단니는 일시 소일 동모로서의 단발랑을 구한다면 얼골만 묘하면 절대 무조건인 것을 말하야 둔다.

감사할 살림 여러 가지

波影

『단발랑』이라고 꼭 명토를 박지 말고 그냥 모던-껄이라고 하고 그중의 하나와 결혼할 생각을 해보기로 한다.

『모던껄』과 결혼을 한다면 엇더켓느냐? 『모던껄』과 결혼하면 그 남편의 생활이 엇덜고? 나라는 사람은 足히써 이 제목으로는 쓸 자신이 업다. 웨 그러냐 하면 『모던껄』과 더부러 교제도 못하야 보고 접촉쪼차 업기 때문이다. 그런 까닭에 그네들의 소행과 그네들의 성격 심리 행동도 모르거니와 더구나 가정 생활에 일으러서는 알 길이 업다. 그러나 피상적으로 나의 관찰한 바 그네들을 표준삼어 고찰하여 본다면 그다지 못쓸 것도 업스리라는 막연한 생각이 남으로 이제 붓을 들어보는 것이다.<122> 그런데 내가 본문에 들어가기 전에 두어마듸 여담이 나마 먼저 쓰고저하는 것이 잇다. 그것은 이가튼 것이다. 근래 여성을 보면 10에 8,9는 모다 『모던껄』인 것 갓다. 그럼으로 특히 나는 『머리나 깍고 응등이로 거러 다니는 』 그 여성만을 지칭하야 『모던껄』이라는 명사를 주고 십지 안타. 『트레머리』『송곳구두』『깡둥치마』『비단양산』 이런 것을 가지고 큰 길로 『 내 얼골이 이러코 내 태도가 이만하면 가위 신여성이라고 하기에 그다지 북그럽지 안켓지오! 하고 돌아다니는 그네들을 미러통 『모던껄』이라고 하고 십다는 말이다. 그러키 때문에 나는 말하되 보통 신여성과 소위 『모던껄』과의 사이에 다소 그 距離는 좀 잇다고 보지만 아조 딱 구별하기에는 極難하다. 此所謂誰知鳥之雌雄格이란 말이다.
