						Sangsusaeng, Seokhwa Saeng
The above article, which not only readers but also colleagues within the company had great interest in and expectations for, was completed by the eccentric pens of both Sangsusaeng and Seokhwa two gentlemen, consisting of approximately 560 lines and 11 pages, but unfortunately, it was not permitted for publication due to the reason of "urban insult." <104>
