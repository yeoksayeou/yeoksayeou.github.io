기사제목	新語大辭典(六) (Dictionary of New Words, Part 6)
기사형태	잡저 (Miscellany)

Pragmatism: Actualism. Utilitarianism. A theory advocated by James of the United States, which says that truth is only what is useful in our lives and changes according to the times and people.

Platonic Love: Pure and noble love devoid of carnal desire. Ideal love. A term derived from the love philosophy of the philosopher Plato.

Prosit: A word used when offering a toast. (Latin)

Production: A work. Or, a privately run film studio.

Propaganda: Promotion. The dissemination of doctrines by socialists through printed materials, and the informing of the public by the government on matters of hygiene through printed materials or lectures, etc.

Promide: Non-fading matte photograph.

Promiscuity: The theory that in primitive human times, there was no marital relationship and there was an era of indiscriminate mating like animals. There are many opposing arguments to this theory.

Peppermint: Mint. Blue liquor. An aphrodisiac drunk by high-collar types.

Pepton: What is created when protein dissolves into peptone (胃腋) in the stomach and intestines.

Piccadilly: A bustling place in London, England. Because many beautiful prostitutes appear there, it is also used to refer to women with suspicious behavior and fancy attire.

Personalism:
A philosophical view of personality. A way of thinking that views everything's unifier, such as reality or psychological state, as the same or similar to personality.

Petit-Bourgeois (佛語):
French. A derogatory term for the middle class. Independent small farmers, small manufacturers, small merchants, small capitalists, writers, lawyers, and high-ranking salaried workers belong here.

Propertariat:
The opposite of proletariat, meaning the propertied class (bourgeoisie). German word.

Profil (佛語):
French. Translated as profile, half-face, side, outline, etc. In this sense, it refers to the longitudinal section or side view of all things.

Proletcult:
An abbreviation of Proletarian Culture, further shortened to Prolocult. Meaning proletarian education. A claim that based on Marxist ideas, proletarian class education should be organized independently.

Prologue:
The counterpart to epilogue. Foreword. Preface. Especially in theatrical terms, it is the custom of ancient theater where, right before the curtain rises, an actor sings a poem introducing the whole play. Even today, although the form is different, it can often be seen. Also called Foreplay.

Prompter:
English. Also a theater term. A person who reads the script in a low voice from behind to remind an actor who has forgotten or is stammering over their lines.

Pageant:
Translated as public play or outdoor play. English. Refers to a play performed by a public organization. Or, a play where actors use outdoor nature as a backdrop and open it to the general public. In recent years, it is often used in the latter sense.

Closed Union:
A union that restricts its members in order to monopolize employment, such as a vocational labor union that has entered into an employment contract with a capitalist in a certain factory or region. <123>

잡지명	별건곤 제33호

기사제목	新語大辭典(六)

新語大辭典(六)
프 部

푸라그미틔즘(Pragmatism)   實際主義. 實用主義. 米國 쩸스의 主唱한 바 卽 眞理란 우리들 生活에 所用되는 것뿐으로 時代와 사람에 따라 어떠케든지 變하는 것이란 說.

푸라토닉러브(Platonic-love)  高潔淸淨한 肉慾을 떠난 戀愛. 理想的 戀愛. 哲人 푸라톤의 戀愛 思想에서 나온 말.

푸로짓트(Prosit)  祈盃등을 올닐 때 쓰는 말.(拉語)

푸로떡숀(Production)  作品. 또는 個人 經營의 活動寫眞撮影所.

푸로파간다(Propaganda)  宣傳. 社會主義者가 印刷物로써 主義를 傳播하고 政府가 衛生上 注意하기 爲하야 印刷物이나 講演을 通하야 民衆에 알니는 것等.

푸로마이드(Promide)  不變色粗面寫眞.

푸로미스퀴틔-(Promiscuity)  人類의 原始 時代에는 婚姻關係란 것이 업고 禽獸와 가튼 亂婚時代가 잇섯다는 說. 이 說에는 만흔 反對論도 잇다.

페퍼민트(Pepermint)  薄荷. 푸른 술. 하이칼라 黨이 마시는 性慾 昻騰制.

페푸톤(Pepton)  胃膓속에서 蛋白質이 페푸론-胃腋-에 溶解되여서 되는 것.

피카데리-(Picadilly)  英國倫敦의 繁華한 곳. 아름다운 賣春婦가 만히 나오는 곳임으로 行動이 좀 수상한 美裝의 婦人을 가르치는 말도 된다.

퍼-쇼너리즘(Personalism)
人格說. 一切의 統一者로써 哲學的 意味로 卽 實在나 心理狀態 等을 人格과 同樣 或은 同一한 것으로 보는 思考方法.

푸틔부르조아(Petit-Bourgeais)
佛語. 中間階級에 對한 嘲罵的 名稱. 自作小農, 小製造業者, 小商人, 小資産家, 文士辯護士, 高級 살러리맨들이 여긔 屬한다.

푸로파-타리아-트(Propertariat)
푸로레타리아-트의 反對語로 有産階級-부르조아지-란 말. 獨語.

푸로필(Profil)
佛語. 橫顔, 半面, 側面, 輪廓等으로 譯한다. 이 意味에서 모든 事物의 縱斷面 乃至 側面圖를 일옴.

푸로렛트컬트(Proletcult)
푸로레타리안컬츄어(Proletarian Culture)의 縮語로 더 줄여서 푸로컬트라고도 한다. 無産者 敎育이란 말. 맑스主義에 立脚하야 푸로階級的 敎育 獨立的으로 組織하자는 主張이다.

푸롤로그(Prologue)
에피로그의 對 英語. 序言. 序詞란 뜻. 特히 演劇用語로써는 開幕直前 俳優가 全篇을 紹介하는 詩를 노래한 古代演劇의 遺風으로 今日에 와서도 形式은 달을 망정 종종 볼 수 잇는 것이다. 前藝라고도 한다.

푸롬푸터(Prompter)
英語. 亦是 演劇用語. 俳優가 臺詞를 니저 바리든지 더듬든지 할 때 그게 생각나도록 뒤에서 적은 소리로 脚本을 읽어주는 役.

페-졘트(Pageant)
公共劇 又는 野外劇이라고 譯한다. 英語. 公共團體에 依해서 演出되는 劇을 일옴. 또는 俳優가 野外의 自然을 利用하야 그것을 背景으로 하고 一般에게 公開하는 演劇. 近年에는 後者의 意味로 만히 쓴다.

閉鎖組合
어느 工場이나 또는 어느 지방의 資本家와 雇傭契約을 맷고 잇는 職別勞働組合가튼 것이 그 雇用을 獨占하기 爲하야 組合員을 制限하고 잇는 組合을 일음.<123>
