잡지명: 別乾坤 제33호
발행일: 1930년 10월 01일
기사제목: 善竹橋 (Sijo)
필자: 李裕宿
기사형태: 시 (Poem)

Serving the lord, would I begrudge even death?
Has a lonely spirit become the head of the collapsed bridge?
Even now, a sincere hand presses my chest in sorrow.

It is a place where resentment is cherished and blood was shed,
Yet it is a place where heartless women wash clothes.
Bridge, do you know when it will be a time of luxury?

As the setting sun slants, it is the path of the shepherd boy.
The traces left by the loyal subject are too faint to seek.
Child, do not wipe it away on purpose; the mind's eye sees it.
-26. 9- <163>
