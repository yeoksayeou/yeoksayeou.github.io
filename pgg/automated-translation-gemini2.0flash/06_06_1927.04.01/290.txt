기사제목	西王母(續篇) (Seo Wangmo (Sequel) / Queen Mother of the West (Sequel))
필자	夜雷 (Yare)
기사형태	소설 (Novel)

Queen Mother of the West
						Yare

King Mu's party set up camp on the eastern shore of the lake and decided to spend several days devising a plan to safely pass through the man-eating country. This place had cliffs on both the left and right banks, so there was no other way to go around, and only by crossing the lake could they find their way to Seo Wangmo. However, crossing this lake meant entering the territory of cannibals, so it was not easy to cross the lake without worry. After several days of thinking, Jopu (造父) came up with a plan. From that day on, the party relied on this plan and began preparations.

The party climbed the mountain and cut down trees to make dozens of rafts. Then, they made 70 or 80 scarecrow people, put them on the rafts, and floated them on the lake. King Mu and his party all made scary masks and built dugout canoes, hiding on an island in the middle of the lake. When dozens of rafts floated near the island, hundreds of cannibals saw the scarecrow people sitting on the rafts from the other side and thought they had found something to eat. They quietly paddled their dugout canoes and climbed onto the rafts, at which point dozens of warriors hidden on the island simultaneously shot arrows, killing dozens of cannibals. The cannibals thought that heavenly arrows were falling from the sky, and they scattered in confusion, half of them falling into the water and drowning. At that moment, the party put on their scary masks, lay down on the rafts in their dugout canoes, and shouted, "We are warriors descended from heaven to punish your sins. Those who surrender will live, and those who do not will be killed!" The remaining cannibals immediately prostrated themselves, rubbing their hands and begging for mercy. So, King Mu, with three warriors as interpreters, said, "We will forgive you and let you go, but you must return and tell your chief that warriors who work for heaven have come down, so bring plenty of delicious beast meat to welcome them." Then, they paddled the rafts to the western shore of the lake. The cannibals listened to the words, bowed a hundred times, and scattered into their villages. After a while, a chief who was nine feet tall brought his subordinates and carried meat, prostrating himself before King Mu. He looked up at the masked faces of the party and, startled again, fell to the ground, begging for mercy. King Mu, intentionally showing his majesty, shook the fierce and scary mask from side to side and said, "Behold the creation of heaven!" He drew his bow and shot a distant target, knocking it down. The chief bowed again and asked, "General, what is that?" "This is the arrow of punishment sent by heaven, and if you shoot with this arrow, you can kill even a million people," he said. The cannibals believed that King Mu's party was truly terrifying generals who had come down from heaven, and they brought a lot of meat to supply the party. With this plan, the party safely passed through the land of the cannibals. <138>

Entering the Territory of Seo Wangmo

Riding a thousand-mile dragon horse, they tirelessly galloped west for seven days. Suddenly, they reached a place where distant mountains overlapped, nearby mountains were sparse, the long river flowed, and the white sand and green pines created a beautiful and scenic landscape before their eyes. King Mu stopped his horse and looked around at his party, saying, "This must be the border of Seo Wangmo's divine land. Let's slowly drive our horses and enjoy the scenery." The party followed the long river for about a hundred li, where peach blossoms floated on the water, mingling with the ripples, flowing peacefully, and rows of villages were visible on the left bank of the river. The party slowly entered the village, step by step, and saw two or three children playing by the roadside. King Mu jokingly said, "Do they also have children in the land of the gods?" He turned to the children and asked, "Is your father home?" The children smiled awkwardly and replied, "We don't know what our father is." King Mu, dumbfounded, smiled and asked again, "Where is there a child without a father?" The children laughed among themselves, saying, "What is a father? This is a strange thing to hear." They looked at King Mu strangely. King Mu asked, "Then do you know your mother?" The children replied in unison, "Yes." King Mu exclaimed, "Ah, so this country only has mothers and no fathers!" As they entered the village, countless fruit trees surrounded it. These fruit trees were not of one kind but were mixed with hundreds of different kinds of trees. Peach trees were the most numerous, and there were also apple, pear, and persimmon trees, not to mention strange fruit trees with unknown names, some with ripe fruits ready to be picked, some with fruits just beginning to form, some with flowers in full bloom, some with buds just bursting, and some still standing like winter trees, showing thousands of different forms. <139> Under the fruit trees were herds of deer, roe deer, and pigs, and birds such as peacocks and parrots flew freely, not afraid of people at all. King Mu felt as if he had truly entered a secluded paradise. As they approached the village, cross streets formed squares, and beside the road, flowers of five colors – blue, yellow, red, and white – bloomed in disarray, with swarms of butterflies and bees following people's footsteps, carrying the scent of flowers. Suddenly, they saw shadows of people in the forest and heard voices and singing. The party hastened their steps and went to that place, where there was a large pond with lush green grass growing by the pond. Dozens of men and women were either dancing or singing. King Mu went before them and was about to greet them when a woman of about 20 years old came forward and, without asking anything, said, "Let's dance together once." King Mu, dumbfounded, stepped back and said, "I don't know how to dance," and smiled awkwardly. Only then did the woman ask, "Where are you from?" King Mu replied, "I am from the Kingdom of Zhou." Only then did they gather around, surrounding King Mu's party and examining their appearance with strange eyes. King Mu continued, "I am a guest from afar who has come to see your country." As soon as they heard this, they said, "We must be hungry, so ask for something to eat." The way they spoke seemed entirely like innocent children to King Mu. Their innocent actions and the milky-white complexions of both children and adults were very lovely. Moreover, their clothes, whatever they were, were the same for men and women, with the upper part half-naked and the lower part wearing neatly tied skirts, making them look like tall children. Anyone who saw them would want to embrace them and kiss them. As the day drew to a close, King Mu entered a large house with them. The house was made entirely of wood, but it was very clean inside and out, which was very refreshing. At dinnertime, they led King Mu to a communal dining hall. This was where the people of the village gathered to eat together. On the table were fruits, meat, and rice porridge. They gathered in one place without any distinction between men and women, eating happily. At the table where King Mu sat, there were dozens of people, but strangely, there was not a single elderly person. King Mu initially thought nothing of it, but after finishing the meal and going to the communal playground, he still couldn't see any elderly people. This playground was a communal activity that all hundreds of people in the village did together after dinner. The activities mainly included dancing, singing, wrestling, and various games that King Mu had never seen before in his life. In the midst of the activities, they laughed more than they spoke. They liked to laugh like children, and it was their habit to laugh at the slightest thing. Also, the games were orderly, with one group playing and then leaving, and another group coming in. After the adults played, the children played, so there was never any disorder. This was not because they had any rules or orders, but it was a natural result of the customs that had been passed down from ancient times, as King Mu later realized. King Mu, curious about why he hadn't seen any elderly people at the dining table, looked around in the playground to see if he could find any elderly people, but he still couldn't find a single one. King Mu asked a person nearby, "Why are there no elderly people here?" The young man next to him smiled and simply replied, "We don't have many elderly people in our place." King Mu was very curious about the lack of elderly people, so he pointed to one of the people who looked the oldest and asked, "How old are you this year?" That person also replied like a child, "I don't know how old I am. Not just me, but no one here knows their age." King Mu's party was so dumbfounded that they thought, "Ah, is this the land of the gods? Are those all thousands-of-years-old gods?" However, seeing that the gods also had children and that there were men and women, they didn't seem like gods. They began to wonder what the reason was. King Mu asked that person again, "What is your name?" The man pointed to a pear tree in the yard and said, "My name is the same as that tree. It's called 'Pear Tree' (Bae Namu, 배나무)." King Mu said, "You are called Pear Tree?" He also said, "My mother gave birth to me under a pear tree, so I am called Pear Tree." King Mu thought to himself, "Ah, so the customs of the ancient Three Sovereigns (太古三皇) still remain here." King Mu then asked "Bae," "Is your father still alive?" "Bae" also replied like the children he met on the street, "We don't know who our father is. We know our mother, but we don't know our father. How can we know our father?" King Mu asked, "Then does your country not have something called 'husband and wife' (夫妻)?" Bae asked in return, "What is 'husband and wife'?" King Mu replied, "Husband and wife are a man and a woman who get married and live in the same house, having sons and daughters." Bae said, "Ah, is that what you call 'husband and wife'? We have men and women in our country, but we don't have 'husband and wife'. If a man and a woman like each other and want to live together, they can live together as much as they want, but they don't live in the same house. Men go here and there, hunting mountain goats, catching fish, and planting trees, so they can't stay in this village all the time. That's why we don't have 'husband and wife'. That is, there is no father." King Mu said to himself, "There are no 'husband and wife', so there is no father," and he couldn't help but burst out laughing. King Mu asked, "Then who is the richest person in your village?" The man also asked in return, "What is a rich person?" King Mu replied, "A rich person is someone who has a lot of money." Then he asked again, "What is money?" <141> King Mu couldn't say anything. He asked various other things, but the man didn't know anything. It seemed that there was no one as clever as the pear tree among them, and he didn't know anything at all like a natural fool. When he asked other people, he got the exact same answer as the pear tree. King Mu was so strange that he wondered if he was having a big dream. He turned to Jopu and said, "Is this not a dream? There is nothing in this country, so what is the reason? There are no old people, no fathers, no deaths, no money, no rich people, no officials. There is nothing to see in the world in this country. What is going on? And how do they live? They seem to live very well, but there is nothing." King Mu spent that night in doubt. Thinking that he wouldn't be able to find out the situation of this country from the people there, King Mu got up early the next morning, said goodbye to them, and left to find another village.

Seeking Seo Wangmo the Queen

In order to find out the details of this divine land, King Mu traveled from village to village, but each village was the same, and each village's situation was exactly the same, so he couldn't figure out the reason why they lived even though there was nothing. After a few days, the party reached a place where they saw a surprisingly scenic spot. Mountains and lakes mingled with each other, and the mountains and waters surrounded them like a picture, hundreds and thousands of times over. The mountains surrounded the water, and the water surrounded the mountains again, looking as lovely and beautiful as young men and women embracing each other and dancing. The mountains were all made of granite, with occasional transparent jade stones embedded in them, clear and beautiful, without a speck of dust. When King Mu reached the lake and was about to cross it, a small boat quickly came from the other side and asked King Mu, "Are you going to cross this water?" King Mu got on the boat and quickly reached the western shore. The person rowing the boat was a girl of about 20 years old, and her face alone made it impossible to see her as an ordinary human being. King Mu turned to the girl and asked, "Who are you?" The girl smiled and replied, "I am a person living on this island, and I am the woman who is in charge of crossing this ferry." When he asked, "How much is the fare for crossing the boat?" the girl didn't seem to understand the words and hesitated before asking again, "What is the fare?" King Mu asked, "Is there anything you receive for crossing the boat?" The girl looked at King Mu with strange eyes and said, "It is my duty to cross people over, so what is there to receive?" She then turned to King Mu and asked, "Where are you from, and where are you going?" King Mu said, "I am a person from the Kingdom of Zhou, and I have come to see this country." The girl then asked, "Then will you meet the Queen?" <142> King Mu was happy to know that there was indeed a queen in this country and eagerly said, "I am on my way to meet the Queen. Please guide us and let us meet the Queen." She replied, "That is not a difficult thing. Let's go together." She led King Mu's party through the island for about three li, and they were surprised. There, houses made entirely of jade stones lined up, connecting the mountains. It was impossible to tell whether the mountains were houses or the houses were mountains, and they crossed a small stream in the midst of the mountains and houses to reach a house. As soon as King Mu's party entered the house, dozens of women welcomed them and asked them to sit comfortably. King Mu was like having a dream within a dream and couldn't say anything. He looked around for a while and then asked them, "Who is the Queen here, and how can I meet the Queen?" One of the women stepped forward and said, "Our Queen's name is Seo Wangmo. Because the name of this country is Seo Wangmo, the Queen's name is also Seo Wangmo. And you will be able to meet the Queen at noon tomorrow." King Mu then asked, "How old is the Queen?" The woman replied, "No one here knows their age." King Mu thought, "Ah, it's the same here," and asked again, "Since there is a Queen, there must be many subjects?" The woman said, "There is a Queen in this country, but there are no subjects." King Mu asked, "What does the Queen do?" The woman replied, "She doesn't do anything. However, because the Queen of this country has high virtue, she makes all the people live well through her virtue." King Mu asked everything he could here, but they also didn't know much like the people in the village, so he couldn't figure out the essentials. At noon the next day, King Mu, guided by the women, reached a place to meet the Queen. After riding a boat along a long pond for a while, they climbed up a jade stone railing, and there was a woman there, serving tea. They led King Mu to a seat, and then the beaded curtain from the upper railing was half-opened, and a Queen gracefully moved down. A strange fragrance filled the room, and the Queen's figure was briefly visible within the five-colored clouds outside the railing. Then, all the women guided the Queen to King Mu. King Mu briefly looked up at the Queen and was so stunned that he couldn't say anything and stood there like a stone statue. The Queen also briefly looked at King Mu and then stood like a stone statue without saying anything. No one in the room knew what was going on. The reason for this was that the Queen was none other than Zhou Seongseon (周成仙), the heroine of King Mu's old love affair that he had been thinking about day and night. Only after Queen and King Mu barely opened their mouths to exchange a greeting <143> did the Queen order all the women to return to their rooms, leaving only the Queen and King Mu in the room. King Mu and the Queen exchanged tearful greetings and were silent for a long time before the Queen spoke first. "Your Majesty, Your Majesty, what is going on? I still feel like I am dreaming and cannot come to my senses. The Kingdom of Zhou is ten thousand li away from here. It is truly impossible for Your Majesty to travel this far unless it is a dream." She said, shedding tears. King Mu also thought it was a dream and couldn't say anything for a while. "I can only say it is a dream. I heard that Zhou Seongseon went to the Western Paradise, but it is a complete surprise to meet you here." He continued, telling the whole story from beginning to end without omitting anything. "But, Your Majesty, please tell me first how you came to this place. I am so anxious that I cannot bear it." As he waited for the Queen's words, the Queen took a long, regretful sigh and slowly opened her mouth. "After studying immortal techniques under the monk Jingwan (眞觀道士), it has been five years since I came here with the monk. This place is known as the Western Paradise, the land of the gods, from ancient times, and there has been a Queen who has ruled this place with virtue for generations. When I came to this country, the previous Queen relinquished the throne to me and went to the Pure Land (極土)." So, I am here as the Queen instead. The Queen told King Mu about the situation since Zhou Seongseon followed the monk Jingwan in detail. King asked, "Then there is no separate prince in this country, but anyone with high virtue can become the king?" Queen said, "That's right. The king of this country is not like the king of Zhou, who has subjects and soldiers. The people simply follow the king's actions when he is king. Therefore, anyone with high virtue becomes the king, and the king relinquishes the throne to that person and goes to the Pure Land. King asked, "Is there another country called Pure Land besides this country?" Queen said, "No. When people in this country get old, they leave this place and go to a place called the Pure Land, which is also a village in the West Queen Mother Country. King asked, "When I came here, I tried to find out about the situation of this place, but the people of the village only replied that they didn't know, so I still don't know the reason. Please tell me the situation." The queen smiled and said, "I will tell you slowly later." She ordered her maid to serve the divine food. King Mu was now able to see the Western Paradise, which he had always wanted to see, and he was also able to meet Zhou Seongseon, so he was happy and joyful in his heart, spending pleasant days with the West Queen Mother Queen Zhou Seongseon, enjoying the immortal banquet. <144> After a few days, the West Queen Mother Queen hosted a great banquet for King Mu and his party, inviting them. When the party arrived at the West Queen Mother's banquet and ate all the divine foods, the Queen especially brought a bright red peach and presented it to King Mu, saying, "This peach is a specialty of this country, and it is called the Millennium Jade Peach (千年碧桃). There is a legend that the fruit ripens only once every thousand years, so it is called the Millennium Jade Peach." When King Mu received the peach and ate it, the taste was so clear and refreshing that his spirit was renewed, and his bones seemed to be reborn. After the banquet, the Queen ordered all the women to perform Hwarak (化樂), and the sound of the zither and jade flute was clear, and flocks of birds such as magpies and peacocks danced on the trees, and all the women danced the fairy dance, as if the universe were in the Great Harmony Prime Energy (泰和元氣). The banquet continued until night. At the end of the banquet, everyone dispersed and returned, and King Mu and the West Queen Mother spent the night in one room. A few days later, the West Queen Mother Queen took King Mu, just the two of them, to the Fairy Platform (玉仙臺), which was called the Jade Immortal Platform.

The Contents of the West Queen Mother Country

King Mu, who had ascended to the Jade Immortal Platform, somehow felt that he was no longer an ordinary human being. So, he quietly held the Queen's wrist, thinking about the time when the two of them had stood on the Hermit Platform (隱仙臺) of the Gwangseongdo Hall (廣成道館) in the old Kingdom of Zhou, and smiled to himself. King Mu was eager to know the situation here and urged the Queen to tell him the details. The Queen smiled and said, "If Your Majesty wants to know the situation here, please ask the suspicious things first." King: "The people here all don't know their fathers, what is the reason?" Queen: "Because they don't live in family units here, and men and women live freely, they only know their mothers and don't know their fathers." King: "Ah, is that so? And there are no old people here, what is the reason?" Queen: "When people in this country get old, they don't stay in that village but go to a place called the Pure Land. Therefore, the people here are always only young people, and there are no old people." King: "Then do the old people gather together? What do those many old people do when they gather in one place?" Queen: "What do the old people do in the Kingdom of Zhou? The old people in the Pure Land are all old, so playing is what they do." King: "They say that they don't know about death in this country, is that really true?" Queen: "Isn't that the only way? Since the old people are gathered together, you can never see anyone dying in the village." King heard these words, clapped his knees, and said, "Indeed, that's how it is. So it looks like they are immortal." He then continued, King: "There are no thieves in this country, is that true?" Queen: "Of course. The people in this country have no private property, and the production of the village is owned by the village in common, so there is no reason for thieves to exist." King Mu listened to the Queen's words and was silent for a while before continuing, "Then how is the distribution of food and clothing in the West Queen Mother Country?"

西王母
						夜雷
穆天子 일행은 호수의 東岸에서 천막을 치고 멧칠 동안을 묵우면서 食人國을 무사히 통과할 계획을 열어가지로 생각하엿다. 이곳은 左右兩岸이 다 절벽이어서 다른 길로 돌아갈 데는 업고 반다시 호수를 건너야 西王母로 가는 路便을 잡을 것이엇다. 그러나 이 호수를 건너면 곳 식인종의 部落이기 때문에 안심하고 호수를 건너기는 여간 위험한 일이 안이엇다. 여러 날 생각하든 결과-造父는 한게구를 생각한 것이 잇다. 일행은 그날부터 그 게책에 의지하야 着着 준비를 실행하엿다.
일행은 산에 올나 나무를 찍어 떼를 數十隻을 제조하엿다. 그리하야 허수아비 사람을 7,80명을 맹그러 떼우에 태여서 호수에 뜨웟다. 그리고 일행은 穆天子 以下로 너나 할 것 업시 무섭게 탈을 맹그러 쓰고 獨木船을 맹그러 타고 바로 호수 복판에 잇는 섬 속에 숨어 잇섯다. 數十隻의 떼가 호수에 흘너 섬 갓가히 올 때에는 저편으로부터 식인종 수백 명이 떼우에 안즌 허수우비 사람을 보고 정말 먹을 것이 생겻다고 살살 獨木船을 타고 살가치 저어 떼우에 올나 설 때에 섬 속에 숨어 잇든 수십 명의 壯士가 일시에 할을 쏘아 식인종 수십 명을 죽엿다. 그런 즉 신인종들은 하늘에서 天살이 떨어진다하야 각각 헛틀어저서 졀반은 물에 빠저 죽엇다. 그때 맛츰 일행은 무서운 탈을 집어쓰고 獨木船을 타고 떼-우에 누면서 『우리은  한울로 내려온 壯士덜인데 너의들의 죄를 다슬이라 왓스니 항복하는 자는 살이고 그러치 안으면 모졸이 죽이리라』고 호령을 한 즉 남아 잇든 식인종은 일시에 업들이며 손을 비비고 살여주기를 청하엿다. 그래서 穆天子는 三壯士를 通辯으로 하고 하는 말이 「우리가 너이를＜138＞ 용서하야 노아 줄 터이니 너이는 돌아가 너이의 酋長의게 한을에서 힘쓰는 壯士가 내려왓스니 맛잇는 줌성의 고기를 만히 가지고 와서 마즈라 하여라」하고 떼를 저어 호수 西岸에 대엿다. 한즉 식인종들은 말을 듯고 百拜 졀하고 모다 部落으로 헛터져 들어가더니 한참 잇다가 키가 九尺이나 되는 추장이 부하를 덜이고 고기를 실고 穆天子의 압헤 졀하고 낫츨 들어 일행의 탈쓴 얼골을 보더니 다시 놀내여 따에 업데여 살여주기를 청한다. 穆天子는 일부러 위염을 보히기 위하야 험하고 무섭게 멩근 탈을 좌우로 흔들며 「너의는 우늦�의 조화를 보라」하고 활을 당겨 먼 곳에 잇는 사심이를 맛처 꺽구러 뜰인 즉 추장은 다시 졀하고 「장군님 그것이 무엇이냐」고 묻는다. 「이것은 한울에 보히는 벌의 살인데 너이도 이 살로 쏘면 백만 명이라도 다 죽인다 하엿다. 식인종들은 穆天子 일행을 정말 한울로 낼온 무서운 장수인 줄 알고 만흔 고기를 가저다 일행의게 供饋하엿다. 일행은 이 게책으로 무사히 食人國을 통과하엿다.

西王母 國境을 들어서면
千里龍馬를 타고 쉴새업시 살가치 달아나 西으로 西으로 7일 동안을 들어 갓다. 문득 한 곳에 이른 즉 遠山은 疊疊, 近山은 落落, 長江은 滾滾, 白沙靑松 山明秀麗한 곳이 눈압헤 전개하엿다. 穆天子는 말을 멈추고 일행을 돌아보면서 「이곳이 필연 西王母의 神仙國 국경인가 십흐니 말을 천천이 몰아 山川求景을 하면서 가자」고 분부하엿다. 일행은 長江을 끼고 한 百里 가량을 간 즉 물에 복숭아꼿이 點點이 떠서 물더품과 한데 어울너저 한가히 흐르고 강 左便에는 즐비한 部落이 보힌다. 일행은 部落을 차자 한거름 한거름 천천이 들어간 즉 길엽헤 數三兒童이 놀고 잇는 것을 보앗다. 穆天子는 우습게말로 神仙國에도 역시 아동이 잇는가 하면서 아해들의게 향하야 「너의 아부지가 집에 잇느냐」고 물엇다. 아해들은 해죽해죽 우스면서 「우리 아부지가 무엇인지 몰나요」하고 대답한다. 穆天子는 어이가 업서서 우스면서 「아부지 업는 아해가 어대 잇단 말이냐」하고 다시 물은 즉 아해들은 저히끼리 서로 우스면서 「아부지가 무슨 물건이냐. 참 별 말삼도 들어보겟다」하고 穆天子를 보고 이상히 처다보고 잇다. 穆天子는 「그러면 어머니는 아너냐」한 즉 아해들은 한갈가치 「네」하고 대답하엿다. 穆天子는 하- 하고 올치 이 나라은 어머니만 잇고 아부지는 업는 나라이로구나」 하엿다. 部落를 잡아들자 수업시 密立한 과실나무가 部落를 싸고 잇다. 그리하야 그 만흔 과실나무는 한 종류의 과실이 아니오 몃 백 종의 가지각색의 나무가 한데 석겨 잇다. 그 중에 복숭아나무가 제일 만코 그 외에 林檎, 배, 감 가튼 나무는 말할 것도 업고 이름도 아지 못한 이상한 과실나무에는 금방 열매가 익어 따먹게 된 것, 열매가 시재 매치늊� 것, 꼿이란 만히 핀 것, 꼿망울이 가주터지는 것, 겨울나무와 가치 아즉도 그대로 서 잇는 것의 千態萬像의 나무이엇다. ＜139＞ 과실나무 아래는 사슴, 노루, 도야지 가튼 무리며 새로는 공작, 鸚鵡 가튼 새들이 조곰도 사람을 무서워하지 안코 自由自在로 이리로 뚸고 저리 나라가는 것을 본 穆天子는 진실로 世外別區에 드러온 感想이 이러낫다. 部落이 점점 갓가워 옴에 조차 十字길이 方井이 나고 길 엽헤는 靑黃赤白 五色의 꼿이 헛틀어지게 피여잇는 우에 나비와 벌이 떼가 사람의 발자최를 따라 꼿행기를 날이고 잇다. 문득 본 즉 저편 樹林 속에서 사람의 그림자가 보히고 말소리와 노래소리가 들인다. 일행은 거름을 재촉하야 그곳까지 나아가 본 즉 거기에 큰 못이 잇고 못가에는 錄陰芳草가 욱을어젓는데 수십 명의 남녀가 혹은 춤추고 혹은 노래하고 잇섯다. 穆天子는 그들의 압헤 가서 먼저 인사를 청하고저 한 즉 그 중에 나히가 한 20세 가량되는 여자 한 사람이 뒷여 나오더니 不問曲直하고 「나하고 하번 춤을 가치 추어 보십시다」하고 말을 눗치겟다. 穆天子는 어이가 업서 몸을 피하면서 「나는 춤을 출 줄을 모르오」하고 슬그먼히 우서 벌인 즉 그 여자는 그제야 「당신은 어대서 온 사람이냐」하고 물어 본다. 穆天子는 「나는 周나라에서 온 사람이오」하고 대답한 즉 그들은 그제야 하나 둘 모혀 들어 穆天子 일행을 둘너싸고 이상한 눈으로 일행의 행색을 살피고 잇다. 穆天子는 다시 말을 이어 「나는 먼 곳에서 온 손으로 당신네 나라를 구경을 왓소」하고 그들의 대답을 기다린 즉 그들은 그 말을 듯자마자 「위선 시장하겟슨 즉 먹을 것을 달나너냐고 한다」 이러케 말하는 그들의 모양이 穆天子의 눈에는 순전히 어린 劅�孩들 가치 보혓다. 그 無邪氣한 동작이라 든가 어린이나 어른을 물론하고 낫빗치 豊秀하야 우유빗 가치 뽀얏게 피여올우는 肉態가 심히 귀여워 보인다. 게다가 그들의 입은 의복은 무엇인지는 몰으되 남녀가 한가지로 웃동은 절반 벗고 아래도리에 치마가치 맨 끈을 맵시잇게 둘는 모양까지 키 큰 어린 兒孩로박게는 불 수 업섯다. 누구든지 그들을 한 번 보면 단박 허리를 쓸어안고 입을 맛출이만치 귀여워 보인다. 그럭저럭 날은 저물엇다. 穆天子는 그들과 가치 엇던 큰 집을 들어간 즉 집은 모두 木製로 지엇스나 안박히 심히 청결한 것이 마음에 심히 상쾌하엿다. 전역 시간되자 그들은 穆天子를 인도하야 공동 식당에 들어 갓다. 여기는 그 部落 사람들이 한가지로 모혀 음식 먹는 곳이 엇다. 식탁 우에는 과실과 獸肉과 살이粥이 노혓다. 그들은 남녀의 구별이 업시 한 곳에 모혀 질겁게 음식을 먹고 잇다. 穆天子의 안즌 식탁에는 사람이나 수십 명 가량이 잇는데 이상하게도 한 사람의 노인도 업다. 穆天子는 처음에는 무심히 생각하얏스나 畢竟 식사를 맛치고 共同遊戱場에를 나와 본 즉 여기에도 노인은 도모지 볼 슈 업다. 이 遊戱場은 그 부락 사람 수백 명 전부가 한 곳에 모혀 져력 후에 의려히 하는 共同遊戱엿다. 遊戱에는 대개 춤추기와 노래하기와 씨름하기와 또는 穆天子가 평생 보지 못하든 가지각색 노름이 만히 이섯다. 遊戱하는 가운데 그들은 말보다 우슴소리가 만핫다. 그들은 어린 兒孩들과 가치 우슴 ＜140＞ 웃기를 엇지 조와하는지 조곰만 일에도 하하하고 웃는 것이 그들의 습관이엇다. 그리고 노름도 질서가 整然하게 한 派가 놀고 나가면 다른 한 派가 드러와서 어른 논 뒤에은 어린이들이 놀고 하야 一應紊亂한 일이 업다. 이것은 그들의게 무슨 규칙이나 명령이 잇서 그러한 것이 아니오 녯날로 내로오는 습관에 의지하야 天然自然으로 그러하게 되는 것을 穆天子는 후에 드러 잘 알앗다. 穆天子는 식탁에서 노인을 보지 못하든 일이 궁금하든 끗에 遊戱場에서도 노인을 볼까 하고 살펴 보아스나 거기에도 노인이라구는 한 사람도 업는 것을 본 穆天子는 겻헤 사람의게 「여기에는 웨 노인이 업느냐」고 물엇더니 겻헤 잇든 청년이 우스면서 「우리 곳에은 노인들이 원체 업슴니다」하고 무심히 대답하야슬 뿐이다. 穆天子는 이곳은 노인이 업는 일이 하 궁금하야 그 중에 제일 나히 만하 보히는 사람 하나를 指揮하야 가지고 「당신은 금년에 나이가 멧치나 되엿소」하고 물른 즉 그 사람 역시 어린 아해 모양으로 「나는 내 나히 얼만지 몰으지요. 나뿐 안이라 이곳 사람은 다 나를 몰우고 삼니다」하고 대답한 즉 穆天子 일행은 너무도 어이가 업서 올치 이곳이 신선의 나라이라든니 저것들이 그리면 다 멧천년식 묵은 신선들이로구나 하고 생각하엿다. 그러나 신선도 兒孩를 낫코 남녀가 잇는 것을 보면 신선 갓지도 안이하야 보히는데 대체 엇진 까닭인가하고 萬端 유심히 들어가기를 시작하엿다. 穆天子는 다시 그 사람의게 「당신 어름이 무엇이요」하고 물엇다. 그 남자는 「뜰에 선 배나무를 가리치며」 내 이름은 저 나무와 갓소. 「배나무」라 하오 한다. 穆 「져-이름 배나무라지엇소」 또 「또 우리 어머니가 배나무 아래서 나를 나앗는 고로 배나무라 함니다」 한다. 穆天子는 혼저 생각에 올치 이곳은 아즉 太古三皇쩍 풍속이 그대로 남아 잇고나 하고 생각하엿다. 穆天子는 다시 「배」씨의게 향하야 당신의 아부지가 아즉 까지 살앗슴니까」튕� 즉 「배」씨는 역시 길에서 만낫든 兒孩들과 가치 우리는 아부지가 누군 줄을 남자와 여자가 뜻이 맛자 함께 살고 십흐면 얼마든지 살 수 잇스나 그들의 한 집에 잇는 것이 안이오. 남자는 이리로 저리로 산양도 당기고 고기도 잡고 나무도 심우고 하니까 어대 이 部落에만 일정하게 이서야지요. 그러니까 夫妻라는 것이 업지요. 즉- 아부지가 업슴니다」 한다. 穆天子는 혼자말로 「夫妻도 업서 그러니까 아바지가 업구나」하고 호올로 우슴이 중에는 배나무 만치 똑똑한 사람이 업는 것 가튼데 그는 天痴 모양으로 도모지 아는 것이 엄섯다. 다른 사람의게 무러보아도 또한 배나무와 꼭 가튼 대답을 어덧다. 穆天子 너무도 이상하야 내가 큰 꿈이나 꾸지 안나하고 잇섯다. 그리고 造父를 돌아보며
