New Prize Contest Problem

The people in the above photographs are from China, Russia, Turkey, and Italy. What are their names?

▲ If you know, cut out the paper below with scissors, write the answer there, clearly write your address and name, put it in an envelope, and send it by the end of March.

First Prize: One person will receive a fall overcoat.
Second Prize: One person will receive a summer overcoat.
Honorable Mention: Forty-eight people will receive a special notebook made by this company.
