기사제목	支分社新說 (New Branch Office Announcement)
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Notice · Post-Editing Notes)

Gwangju Branch

Area: The entirety of Gwangju County, excluding Songjeong-myeon (松汀面).

Location: Sugiya-cho (須奇屋町), Gwangju.

Branch Manager: Jeong Tae-ho (鄭泰好)

Sincheon Branch

Area: The entirety of Sincheon County.

Location: Jeokseo-ri (滌暑里) in Sincheon-eup.

Branch Manager: Bae In-shik (裵仁植)

Yongin Branch

Area: The entirety of Yongin County.

Location: Gimnyangjang-ri (金良場里), Yongin County.

Branch Manager: Choe Sang-eo (崔尙虞) <176>
