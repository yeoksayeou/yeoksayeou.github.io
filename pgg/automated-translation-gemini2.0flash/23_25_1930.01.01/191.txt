To Our Readers: A Special Request

1. Whenever you have something to ask this company, please be sure to send the return postage.
(If it is a postcard, please use a reply postcard; if it is a letter, please include a three-jeon stamp).

2. When sending letters or order forms to this company, please clearly write your address and name. Because you write in a hurry, the company, which receives four to five hundred letters a day, faces difficulties and time loss. In severe cases, the sent books or replies sometimes return. <106>

讀者諸位 特別히 願함니다
一, 如何한일던지 本社에 몰어보실일이잇는에는 반듯이 返信料를보내주십시오.
(葉書면 往復葉書로 封書면 三錢切手너어보내주십시오)
二, 本社에 便紙를 보내시거나 注文狀을 보내실에는 貴住所와 氏名을 明白하게 써 주십시오 急하게 흘려쓰시는믄에 一日四五百枚씩 便紙를 밧는 本社에서는 困難과 時間損失할아니라 甚한는 보낸 冊이나 回信이도로도라오는가 만슴니다.＜106＞
