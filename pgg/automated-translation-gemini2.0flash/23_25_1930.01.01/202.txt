New Year Grand Prize

I. Quickly identify the photo on the opposite page. Look closely. Is it winter, summer, spring, or autumn?

II. How many picture fragments are there on the New Year's issue supplement game board of the children's magazine?

Regulations and Notes

▽ Don't deceive yourself, look and look again carefully.
▽ Answers must be written on the paper on the opposite page (tear it off with scissors).
▽ Deadline is January 23rd.
▽ The envelope must be written as "Kyŏngsŏng Kaebyŏksa Pyŏlgŏn'gon Hyŏnsang Kye" (京城開闢社別乾坤懸賞係).
