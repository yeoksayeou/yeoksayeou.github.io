사고·편집후기 (Notice / Editor's Note)

A Big Prize Question: One Bag of White Rice
Something easy that anyone can know. Please submit quickly before the deadline.
Among the four pages of photographs at the beginning of this magazine, look closely at the Western actress in the last photograph. Which of the four women next to her is the naked woman in the middle?
(Pay attention to the eyebrows, nose, and mouth.)

▽Those who know the answer should cut out the paper below with scissors, write the woman's name on the paper, write your address and name clearly, put it in an envelope, and send it by July 15th.

▽We will send special prizes made by our company to 100 people who get the answer correct (if there are too many, we will draw lots), and we will also draw lots from among them to give one bag of high-quality white rice (we will send the equivalent in cash to regions without a branch office). <183>
