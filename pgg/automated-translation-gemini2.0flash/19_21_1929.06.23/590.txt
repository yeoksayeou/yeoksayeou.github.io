기사제목	別乾坤을닑으시는 讀者諸位 特告함니다 (Special Announcement to Readers of Byulgeongon)
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editor's Postscript)

A special announcement to the esteemed readers of Byulgeongon:

We presume that there are some readers of Byulgeongon who have not yet seen "Children" (어린이), but we earnestly recommend that even those who have seen it before should be sure to read the June issue of "Children" (어린이) at least once. Despite considerable restrictions (規審), the Gaebyeok Publishing Company (開闢社) is putting its heart and soul into it, so much so that its printing technology is unrivaled by foreign magazines and its content is of such high quality that it is universally praised. It is being eagerly read not only in discerning households but also in various schools both domestically and abroad. It would be a great mistake to think that "Children" (어린이) is only for young people; once you read it, you will understand how even scholars and elders (人士老人) can experience the education of their character. Please send eleven 1-sen postage stamps to request a copy. <153>
