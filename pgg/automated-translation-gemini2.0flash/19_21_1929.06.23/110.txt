기사형태	시 (Poem)

Young People
						Kim Tong-hwan

Young people, let's go to the mountains.
Let's go to the mountains filled with poison.
With these arms where red blood boils,
Let’s chop wood and nurture our strength.

Only when we nurture strength will good days come.
Are heroes and brave men special people?
They are the ones with great strength and abundant blood.
Young people, let's go to the mountains. <29>

Named Entities

金東煥	Kim Tong-hwan

젊은이들아
						金東煥
젊은이들아 산으로 가자.
독기나 차고 산으로 가자.
붉은 피 끌는 이 팔뚝으로
나무나 패여 긔운 길느자.

긔운 길러야 조흔 날 오지.
영융 호걸이 별 사람인가.
긔운이 장코 피 만흔 이지.
젊은이들아 산으로 가자.<29>
