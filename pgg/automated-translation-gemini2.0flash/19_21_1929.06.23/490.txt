The First Invention of Powder

Powder has been used in the East and West since before the Common Era. In the East, China had already invented it during the Zhou Dynasty, and later, Qin Shi Huang (approximately 350 BCE) was one of the admirers of white powder. He had it applied to the three thousand beautiful women of the Afang Palace, and he also had them apply rouge and dai (黛, eyebrow pencil), enjoying their beautiful appearance. <125>
