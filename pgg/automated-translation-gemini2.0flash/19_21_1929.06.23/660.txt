기사제목	진달네 (Jindallae - Azalea)
기사형태	시 (Poem)

When the azaleas bloom,
They say it is spring.
不如歸不如歸 (Pulgwibulgwipulgwi)
The cuckoo cries.
아이공아이공 (Aigongaigong)
How wonderful it is!

Even in my hometown mountains,
The azaleas will bloom.
不如歸不如歸 (Pulgwibulgwipulgwi)
The cuckoo cries.
아이공아이공 (Aigongaigong)
How wonderful it is! <160>
