萬華鏡 (Kaleidoscope; Miscellaneous)

India person's injection of an English woman

You all know very well from just reading the newspapers that the movement to reject England is intense in India these days. However, according to recent news, it is said that Indian people carry around syringes with poisonous bacteria and, when there is some spectacle or other place where many people are gathered, they slip in and watch for an opportunity. If there is an English person among them—especially a frail woman—they go next to her and secretly inject her, leaving no trace, and then run away. The women who are injected die in just twenty or thirty minutes without knowing why, thinking they were just stung by some sort of bug, and the number of victims is already said to have reached several dozen. If Indian people are all doing such things, it might be quite difficult for the English.

North Pyeong's (北平) beautiful policewoman squad

People who don't know recent circumstances in China easily think that Chinese women all bind their feet and waddle when they walk, like ducks, and without any knowledge or work, just remain locked up in their homes. But in fact, when you see the recent awakening and activity of Chinese women, it is truly astonishing. Everyone well knows that during the Shanghai Incident last year, female students around twenty years old, like flowers, gathered a suicide squad and bravely fought on the front lines, falling and dying dozens and hundreds at a time. These days, the Bukpyeong Public Security Bureau (北平公安局) is recruiting one thousand female police officers, all of whom have short hair, of course, and most of whom are students who studied in Europe and America, fluent in English and surpassing most men in other knowledge, causing both domestic and foreign people to stick out their tongues in amazement. Korean women! Especially those who came out as athletes and even cut their hair short, making a fuss for a while about going here and going there, and then grew their hair out again and became protection soldiers for their husbands and young children in the inner room—how can you not feel ashamed when you see these Chinese women? However, this doesn't mean that Korean women should also become police officers, so be careful about that.

Only 5 million dollars in broadcasting fees for the presidential election battle

During the presidential election in America this time, the Republican Party (共和黨), the Socialist Party (社會黨), and the Democratic Party (民主黨) each competed to elect a president from their own faction, and because it was so fierce, it is said that the broadcasting fees for propaganda alone from the three factions amounted to a whopping five million dollars. If you consider current exchange rates, how much money is five million dollars? However, when you see that there was a case where five hundred tobacco pipes (煙竹) were given as gifts in a myeon council (面協議員) election campaign in Korea, it’s not that surprising.

The Second Ma Jansan (馬占山)

People didn't really know whether Ma Jansan (馬占山) in North Manchuria had died or was still alive, and recently, it was said that So Byeongmun (蘇炳文) was defeated by the Japanese army and fled to Russia. It was said that Russia had captured him and would hand him over to the Japanese-Manchukuo army (日滿軍), but then I heard again that Russia had simply released him and he was once again wandering around all of Manchuria. I wonder if that's another lie. It's a similar situation to Ma Jansan's (馬占山) dying and coming back to life. <42>

北平의 美人警官隊
최근 중국의 사정을 잘 모르는 사람들은 중국 녀자라 하면 의레히 발을 동여서 거름을 거를 때이면 오리 모양으로 되똥되똥하고 아모 지식도 하는 일도 업시 그저 집안에만 잠겨 잇는 줄로 알기 쉽지만은 사실 근래 중국 녀자의 각성한 것과 활몽하는 것을 보면 참으로 놀날 만하다. 작년 상해사변 때에 꼿 가튼 이십 내외의 녀학생들이 결사대를 모와 가지고 전지에 나와서 용맹스럽게 사우다가 수십 수백식 한꺼번에 꺽구러저서 죽은 것 가튼 것도 누구나 잘 알거니와 요새 북평 공안국(公安局)에서는 녀자 경관 일천 명을 모집하는데 전부 단발을 한 것은 물론이고 대개가 구미유학생으로 영어를 유창히 하고 그의 다른 지식도 모모 남자를 능가하야 내외국 사람들이 모도 혀를 헤헤 내두른다고 한다. 조선의 녀성 제씨 그 중에도 운동선에 나서서 단발까지 하고 한참동안 예 간다 제 간다 하고 떠들다가 머리까지 다시 길으고 안빵으로 남편과 어린 아이들의 보호병정이 된 녀성 제씨―이 중국 녀자들을 볼 때에 엇지 붓그럽지 안을가. 그러나 조선 녀자도 이럿케 순사 노릇을 하란 말은 아니니 그것만은 주의―.

大統領 選擧戰에 放送料만 五百萬 弗
이번에 미국에서 대통령을 선거할 때에 공화당(共和黨)과 사회당(社會黨) 민주당(民主黨) 세 패에서 각각 자긔파 사람으로 대통령을 선거하랴고 경쟁이엇스나 격렬하엿던지 세 패에서 선전방송한 방송류만이 작그만치 오백만 불이라 한다. 요새에 위체 시세로 한다면 오백만 붉이란 그 돈이 그 얼마나 큰돈이냐. 그러나 조선에서 면협의원(面協議員) 한아 선거운동에 담뱃대(煙竹) 선사가 오백 개나 된 일이 잇는 것을 보면 그다지 참할 것도 업다.
