A Runaway Story of Romantic Luck
						Kim Neung-in

…The place is Gyeongseong (京城), a major city in Joseon, at the foot of Bukaksan Mountain, in a clean, modern cultural residence! The time is a clear spring afternoon.

Without knowing that this small play is unfolding in a corner of Seoul, cars are still honking and streetcars are clanging as they rush down the main street today.

Now! The play begins when a young, well-dressed housewife returns to the living room of this cultural residence (currently empty) with a rustic man from the countryside…

Woman: "Now, take off your hat and sit comfortably here."

Man: "Is it alright to sit?"

Woman: "Yes. Make yourself at home as if you were in your own house."

Man: "But!"

Woman: "What are you hesitating about?"

Man: "Then, where should I sit?"

…The man, flustered and bewildered, finally sat down awkwardly…

Woman: "How do you like the room?"

Man: "It's really nice. I've never been in a room like this before."

Woman: "Do you like the room?"

Man: "Yes. I like it very much. When I was in the countryside, I heard that Seoul rooms were all small and cramped, but I never knew they could be so spacious and wonderful."

Woman: "Oh my, is this your first time in Seoul?"

Man: "Yes. I came up from the countryside this morning."

Woman: "Oh dear, really?"

…The woman blinked her pretty eyes as if surprised. And quickly took off her overcoat and jacket, leaving only a thin and simple inner garment that revealed her curves, and sat closely beside the man…

Woman: "Oh my! How could you be so bold?"

Man: "I… …"

Woman: "Tell me."

Man: "What is it that's bold?"

…This man is very foolish…

Woman: "Oh, come on! Isn't everything you're doing now bold?"

Man: "Is this bold?"

Woman: "Yes."

Man: "Is being scared out of my wits bold?"

Woman: "No. You don't understand me. How did you get the courage to talk to me so brazenly as I walked lively in the middle of Jongno? How can someone who has just arrived in Seoul act so boldly?"

Man: "But I hesitated for a long time before talking to you..."

Woman: "Still, I think you are a man with a boldness that is rarely seen these days... To be honest, I've become interested in your boldness. In other words, I've fallen for you."

Man: "Haha."

Woman: "Why?"

Man: "Oh, that must be a lie, right?"

Woman: "What?"

Man: "How could someone as wonderful and pretty as you fall for a country bumpkin like me? Oh, you must be joking."

Woman: "No, it's the truth."

Man: "Really, really?"

Woman: "Yes, what's wrong with that?"

Man: "Hehe. If I had known this would happen, I would have come to Seoul sooner. Even after I talked to you, I was worried that I might have offended a noble lady."

Woman: "Honey, are you saying I'm not a noble lady?"

…The woman is very angry…

Man: "A noble lady... hehe."

Woman: "Honey, then what kind of woman do you think I am?"

Man: "Oh, I'm sorry. I misspoke. Then, noble lady madam... goodbye."

…The man tries to get up and leave…

Woman: "Where are you going?"

Man: "I have to leave because I can't be disrespectful to a dignified noble lady."

Woman: "Alright, stay and play! I pity you."

Man: "But..."

Woman: "Why are you acting so foolishly? Sit down and relax. Do you dislike me?"

Man: "Oh, not at all."

Woman: "Then why are you doing this?"

Man: "Then, are you one of those women?"

Woman: "What kind?"

Man: "You know, that kind of woman."

Woman: "Listen here. That's disrespectful to a noble lady."

…The man tries to open the door and leave without saying anything…

Woman: "Honey, where are you going?" <44>

Man: "I have to go! I can't stay and play if you're a noble lady."

Woman: "Haha, you're really a naive country bumpkin."

…Suddenly, the woman's anger melted away like snow…

…The man stands there blankly, not knowing what's going on…

Woman: "Listen, honey, don't be like this. Think of this house as your own. But there's a time limit. Just pretend until six o'clock this afternoon. My husband will be back from work at six."

Man: "Then, is it alright if I think of you as my wife?"

Woman: "Haha, look at him. What a bold man! You can think that way until then, okay?"

Man: "Seoul women have such strange hearts. Being so generous to a man they've just met."

Woman: "I can't figure out country men either. I thought country bumpkins were all clueless and dull, but I never knew you could be so bold and courageous."

Man: "By the way."

Woman: "What is it?"

Man: "By the way, you're not a fox spirit (여호), are you?"

Woman: "Honey, what kind of joke is that? Hahaha."

…The woman laughed sweetly and tapped the man's shoulder…

Woman: "Don't be like that. Let's talk."

Man: "What about?"

Woman: "How did you get the courage to talk to me so boldly in the middle of Jongno today?"

Man: "Yes. That story. I'm good at that story."

Woman: "Then tell me."

Man: "This morning, I got off the train at Gyeongseong Station and took the streetcar, right? There weren't many passengers in the streetcar, and a gentleman was sitting next to me. So, I gathered my courage and asked him."

Woman: "How?"

Man: "Like this. 'I'm visiting Seoul for the first time from the countryside, and where should I go to see pretty Seoul women?'"

Woman: "That's interesting. What did the gentleman say?"

Man: "So, the gentleman looked at my face for a while and said, 'If you want to see pretty Seoul women, go to the middle of Jongno and stay there for two or three hours.'" So, since I was already there, I gathered my courage again and asked him."

Woman: "Yes. How?"

…The woman was so captivated by this man's story that she leaned closer to him.

Man: "Just like this. 'Actually, I want to play with that pretty woman,' I said."

Woman: "You're a really bold man. And then?"

Man: "So, the gentleman looked at my face again as if he was amazed and said, 'Then do this. Go to the middle of Jongno and wait, and if you see a woman who is pretty and heavily made up, don't hesitate to talk to her. Women who put on a lot of makeup are usually available to play with.'"

Woman: "Hahaha. Oh, that's how you ended up talking to me so brazenly."

Man: "Yes."

Woman: "Haha. I'm going to die laughing."

Man: "Hey, is that what you call being bold?"

Woman: "Of course. You succeeded in being bold, which is the first thing women want from a man."

Man: "What success?"

Woman: "Of course. It's a great success. What kind of reward should I give you for that?"

Man: "Then I..."

Woman: "Yes. What is it?"

Man: "Pour me a drink with your own hands."

Woman: "Why are you asking so hesitantly? Is it just alcohol? I'll even add a song to encourage drinking as a side dish."

Man: "Ah, am I dreaming of the Dragon Palace?"

Woman: "Don't act so foolishly and come here."

…The woman pulled the man closer, her blue charm brimming between her bright red lips… <45>
Someone's voice is heard outside.

Woman: "Oh no, it sounds like my husband. This is a disaster."

Man: "Yes, then what should we do?"

…Imagine the bewildered expression on this country bumpkin's face at this moment…

Woman: "Quickly, climb up on this table."

Man: "Why?"

Woman: "Just do it. And touch the light switch. Pretend you're from the electric company..."

In a panic, the man managed to climb onto the table.

In the meantime, the husband has already entered the room…

Woman: "Why are you home so early? You're earlier than usual today."

Husband: "Yeah, I came home a bit early today. But who is this person?"

Woman: "He's from the electric company. The light was flickering and broken, so I called them, and it seems like he just arrived."

Husband: "Oh, good job. That light has been flickering and breaking a lot lately."

Woman: "Yes, so I called them today to have it fixed."

…So far, things have gone well. But what follows is chaos…

Husband: "By the way."

Woman: "What is it?"

Husband: "Why is that person acting like that?"

Woman: "Well, what's wrong?"

…The woman's heart is shrinking to the size of a bean, wondering if she has committed a sin…

Husband: "Something's strange."

Woman: "What do you mean?"

Husband: "Look closely. That person has been fiddling with the light, but he hasn't fixed anything."

…At this moment, the country bumpkin's heart sank. So, unconsciously, he ended up looking at the face of the woman's husband.

And so, from above to below, the eyes of the two men met perfectly…

Man: "Ah?"

Husband: "Huh?"

Woman: "Oh, I'm going to die laughing. What's wrong with you two?"

Husband: "That person is the man from the countryside I met on the streetcar earlier."

Woman: "What?"

…In the meantime, the country bumpkin jumped off the table, opened the door, and ran away. The woman's face turned pale, and she sat there as if rooted, only breathing heavily…

Husband: "Oh, so that's how it is."

…The husband looked at the woman's face, smiled, and calmly lit a cigarette… <46>
