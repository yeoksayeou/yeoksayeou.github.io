유모어 小說, 뚱뚱보 女先生의 失戀 (Humorous Novel, The Fat Female Teacher's Disappointment in Love)
李啞夫
소설 (Novel)

An Teacher, chased into the faculty room, was so upset that she collapsed into her seat, and immediately began to cry loudly.

"Those mouths! Those mouths! Those devils who should be taken away by goblins..." she exclaimed, venting her anger alone, weeping bitterly.

The truth was, she had attended the joint male and female faculty "Nickname Naming Ceremony" (別名命名式) held in the K Girls' School office that afternoon, and after suffering unbearable humiliation, she stormed out and was now weeping like this.

The unbearable humiliation she received from the male and female teachers was as follows:

As they went through each teacher's name to assign nicknames, she was given five nicknames.

Because her name was Uji (雨止), she was called Teacher Uji.
Because she was large, she was called Kkakji-ttong Teacher.
Because she always laughed a lot, she was called Nikko-nikko Teacher.
Because she was short and stout, she was called Ottukki Teacher.
Because she ate hotteok (호떡) well, she was called Hotteok Teacher.

"Am I crazy? If I had left as soon as they started, nothing would have happened! Why did I linger around, giggling, only to suffer this humiliation?"

Her crying grew louder.

"It's all my parents' fault! Why did they make my face like this and only feed me fat! Why did they raise me so fat?"

"From tomorrow, how can I face the school! If this rumor spreads, what will the students say? ... ... They'll laugh and sneer, saying, 'Because her face looks like a caricature (漫畵的), she can't get married even though she's over thirty... She's destined to be a teacher forever!'"

"Oh, God! Have mercy! Mother! Have mercy! What sin have I committed to be given this bitter cup (盞)?"

"Ah! I am 'Uji of Suffering (受難)!’ Don't I have a wretched woman's life? Ah ah ah ... !"

Her crying continued without stopping, rising and falling in a mournful rhythm.

Twenty minutes, thirty minutes, forty minutes passed. The crying stopped. An Teacher, with swollen eyes, smoothed her bangs and sat up.

She stared at the wall and began to think.

"No! This is no time for this! They're looking down on me! They look down on me because I'm an old maid, and that's why they're doing this?!"

... ... ...

"Humph! If I were a married woman, would such insulting words come out? Who would dare utter such vulgar words to a woman with a husband without getting their legs broken?! No way! It's all because I'm not married!!"

... ... ...

"You ugly thing! This is no time to cry! This is no time to stand blankly! It's time to raise the flag of aggressive male conquest (男性征服)! That's right! This is my youth's emergency! Will I give up my female status helplessly? Let's fight to the end! And let's seize a man (男性)!"

... ... ...

"But—there has to be someone? Oh! There is! There is! Surely, among the eight hundred million men, there will be a man for me—" <47>

Thus, An Teacher, who had been writhing from anger to despair, suddenly felt something and made a resolution. She returned to her former cheerful mood and greeted the young students who came to her that night one by one.

One night—beneath the pine trees of Namsan (南山), where white snow was falling.

An Teacher, having graduated from a women's physical education school in Tokyo (東京) and now working as a recreational gymnastics teacher at the school, did not pay much attention to the weather—sunny, cloudy, overcast, rainy, or snowy. On this cold day, she was speaking earnestly to the other person.

"Don't you think so? In the end, life is a tragedy!"

The other person was a young man named Choi, twenty-five years old this year, who was also in charge of gymnastics at the same school—Teacher Lee. His face was also far from being considered handsome.

"Why? Why are you so pessimistic? Did you ever think it was a comedy?"

"Who said it was a comedy?"

"Then?"

"It's just that nothing goes my way, so I can't help but think that way, can I?"

"..."

"Ultimately, the reason I asked to see you today is not for anything else. It's to try to accomplish something that doesn't go my way with the help of a teacher!"

"You're very ambitious! Where is there anything that goes as you wish? But tell me what it is. If it's something I can help with..."

"Are you married, Teacher?"

"No!"

"Why aren't you?"

"Why do you ask?"

"I need to know that to have the courage to speak."

"..."

"Why are you silent? Huh?"

"I still ... want to remain a bachelor."

"Haha! Is it difficult to say that?"

"Well, what I wanted to say is not something else. ... It's about marriage ... ...

"What? Marriage? Whose are you talking about?"

"I'm talking about you, Teacher."

"Then are you going to introduce me to someone suitable?"

"No! Absolutely not! ... ... ... Let me be frank. Until now, women have been passive and men have been active in thinking about and deciding on marriage, but tonight, I want to break away from that old-fashioned idea and think about it the other way around. ... ... ... Teacher? ... Teacher?"

"..."

"Why are you silent?"

"Yes?"

"How about marrying me?"

"? ?"

"Of course, you might think my words are unexpected! But I am saying this with the heart of wishing for your happiness and my happiness ..."

"Two people's happiness, you say?"

Lee Teacher, who had been silently observing An Teacher's words and actions until now, finally began to retort.

"You mean combining you and me would be happiness? You mean a thirty-one-year-old woman and a twenty-five-year-old man have to combine to be happy?" <48>

"Yes! I want to affirm and guarantee that."

"So, you mean in the sense of Wakai Tsubame (젊은 제비)!"

"Absolutely not! How can you say such a rude thing? I'm saying this from a eugenic point of view!"

"Huh! Because both of us are physical education teachers, healthy bodies and healthy bodies will combine!"

"Wouldn't we give birth to a healthy jade child (玉童子)?"

"What next!"

"What do you mean, what next?"

"Don't you know what's next? What about ten years or twenty years from now? Can't you understand that?"

"After that, we'll grow old together!"

"What a pity!"

"Why! Is it bad to grow old together, until black hair turns white?"

"Anyway, An Teacher lacks understanding. She lacks understanding of men in general. Men absolutely do not want to marry older women."

"Why is that?"

"Because women age faster than men!"

"Then should we intentionally postpone the union of our healthy bodies because the difference in aging is so great?"

Here, An Teacher's attack and Lee Teacher's defense are reaching a climax.

"An Teacher, from the beginning, you are being unreasonable. Also, you have too simplistic an idea about marriage. Marriage is not that easy. Mood, emotion, age, personality, character, and so on..."

"Stop it! I understand without you saying more. But I never knew that your and my views on marriage were so different! Lee Teacher, whom I have been yearning for, admiring, and thinking of so fondly..."

With that, An Teacher collapsed on the spot and burst into tears.

"An Teacher! What is this? Huh?"

"I don't know! I'm a discarded person now!"

"Discarded? Who did what?"

"If I am abandoned even by Lee Teacher, I am no different from a dead person!"

An Teacher's crying showed no sign of stopping.

"Then what do you want me to do?"

"Love me!"

"I can't love you!"

"Kill me!"

"Kill you? So I can become a murderer?"

"Then do as you please. Dispose of me as you please. What's the point of taking this body, which is no different from a dead body, back?"

"Don't do this foolishly and let's go back. You wrote in your letter as if there was some important discussion, but what is this now? Even in the sense of respecting each other's personality or thinking about our position (身分)..."

"Personality? Position? Do you think those things will scare me when I've made my final decision?"

In this situation, the two of them were in a very difficult situation. Even though Lee Teacher had a strong body, he was greatly troubled by how to comfort An Teacher, who had been sobbing for two hours, and send her away, and An Teacher couldn't awkwardly retract the foot she had already extended. But Lee Teacher couldn't bear this any longer either. Eventually, he came up with a way out.

"An Teacher?"

"Yes?"

"I can't stay here any longer."

"Why?"

"First, it's unpleasant, second, I can't stand the cold, and third, I'm actually afraid of the National Guidance Alliance (保導聯盟員)."

"..."

"What if we are discovered by them!"

"Why would the National Guidance Alliance come to Namsan (南山) in this cold?"

"Why? They come sometimes. They bring Sepa-do (세파-도) and patrol this place. Didn't you see it in the newspaper last summer? They say the police station is raising Sepa-do (세파-도) to crack down on the morals of men and women... and that will be realized from this New Year's Day. Moreover, to crack down on secret meetings like ours."

"Stop saying such cowardly things. Who do you think will believe that?"

"If you don't believe me, are you going to spend the night here?"

"Until I get Lee Teacher's approval."

Lee Teacher was speechless at this last word.

He said to An Teacher, who was shedding tears and crying,

"Then I have no choice. Excuse me."

With that, he disappeared into the darkness. An Teacher stared at the spot where Lee Teacher had hastily disappeared, like a dog looking at the roof after chasing a chicken...

"I did something foolish again..."

"All men are cowardly bastards!"

"I'll have another nickname!"

"Disappointment in Love (失戀) Teacher!" <49>
