Humor Short Story, Telephone 4471
						Choe Kong-won (Writer)

He had seen others use the telephone countless times, but this was the first time in his life he had tried it himself.
Confidence comes from experience—what he had easily dismissed when watching others now felt unfamiliar, awkward, and bewildering when he faced it himself.

Before picking up the receiver, he pursed his lips and held them very close to the transmitter, as if sucking on it. This was not because he was more eager to send his words than to receive them, but simply a posture he adopted to avoid mistakes in the order of communication.

Next, to clear the passage in his throat through which sound waves generated by the vibrations of his vocal cords would escape into the vast universe, he coughed loudly once. Only then did he finally lift the receiver to his ear.
Ting-. Swish-

"Hello- hello?"

There was no response.

"Hello- hello?"

Then, a click sounded in the receiver, followed by a buzzing sound like a mosquito near his ear.

"Nambang?" (남방?)

Even in this confusion, his imagination worked wonders, thinking, "Aha! That clicking sound must be the operator closing the door as she enters the exchange!"

"Hello- hello, 4471..."

The telephone number he had memorized until it was worn out suddenly became blocked, so he pulled out his notebook from his pocket.

"Hello- 4471 desu."

"Dochira no?"

"Hello- 4471 desu."

"Dochira no?"

"Huh? Ha-"

"Dochira no 4471 desu ka?"

The operator's tone became sharper than before.

"Ah- sō desu. 4471."

"Dochira no 4471 desu ka to iu no ni... (Unintelligible muttering)"

The operator's voice was even higher, faster, sharper, and impossible to understand. His head was spinning, and he felt a tingling sensation down his spine, so he quickly hung up the receiver.

"I spoke clearly, so why is the operator acting like that? Does she think I'm mocking her and is angry?" He tilted his head, examining the number in his notebook again.

"Dochira no, dochira no... what is dochira? The number 4471 is correct."

As a young man from the special zone called Joi (조이), he was not particularly deaf to Japanese or lacking in discernment. However, the simple word dochira (何處), or "where," was incomprehensible to him because it seemed irrelevant and disconnected from the context. Rather, he could only think of it as an idiom outside his realm of knowledge.

"4471, I clearly said it, but there's no reason to ask dochira. What on earth could that dochira mean?"

No matter how logically he thought about it, there was no reason for dochira to be involved in making a phone call. Therefore, he blamed all the errors and mistakes on the operator and attempted a second call. <50>

"Hello- hello."

"Nambang?"

It was a different operator's voice.

"Shikkari tanomimasu. 4471."

"Dochira no?"

His back tingled again. But this time, he was determined to stand his ground, find out what dochira meant, and finally achieve his goal.

"Saki kara 4471 to itte iru no ni, dochira ga nan desu ka, nani?"

"Dochira no 4471 desu ka?"

"Ha- wakarimashita, wakarimashita. Ano- anpan desu. Oku no heya desu."

He was greatly satisfied, as if he had finally understood the true nature of dochira. In his mind, he thought,

"I knew it. There couldn't be another dochira."

"Eh?"

"Anpan desu. Meishuku (明淑) san no heya desu yo?"

"Nan desu tte? Iyakashi nanka shicha ikemasen yo!"

Click.

"Nani? Iyakashi?"

Suddenly, he became irritated. He couldn't help but be irritated.

"Nani? Iyakashi? Baka."

"... ... ..."

There was no response. No sound. He shook the receiver violently, as others often did.

"Hello- hello."

Click. A faint, buzzing sound came, and the same voice returned. It sounded like the voice from the first time he called.

"Nambang?"

"Hello, you're being very unkind. Why won't you connect me to 4471?"

"Yoboseyo..." (여보서요.)

The operator's sweet voice unexpectedly changed to Korean.

"Where are you talking about? 4471? Are you saying Ko-gyamong? Honggyokku? Ryu-san?"

"Oh, right, right. Now I understand. I'll say it again. Hello, hello. Kant’aesŭdo (貫鐵洞) 4471."

"Shiyonai ne!"

"Sō desu. Shi (四) yo (四) na (七) i (一) desu." -End- <51>

자신(自信)이란 체험(體驗)에서 우러나는 것이라- 남하는 것 보고 예사로 역엿든 것이 정작 대하고 보니 자신이 업서 서먹서먹하고 얼떨떨 하엿다.
수화기(受話器)를 떼여 들기 전에 두 입술을 쭝그-ㅅ해 가지고 송화기(送話機)라 팔을 빨듯이 밧작 갓가이 대엿다. 그것은 오는 말보다도 보낼 말이 급해서가 아니라 통화(通話)상 순서를 차리는데 실ㅅ수 안 생기게 하자는 생각에서 취한 자세에 지나지 안엇다.
그 다음에는 성대의 진동으로 말미암아 생기는 음파가 광대무변한 우주의 공간을 향하야 빠저 나가는 인후(咽喉)의 관문을 소제하기 위하야 그는 큰기침을 한번 컹 하엿다. 그런 다음에야 비로소 수화기를 손에 떼여 드럿다.
뗑-. 솨-
『에- 모지 모지』

『에- 모지 모지』

『남방?』
그는 그 총중에도 놀라울 만한 상상력이 활동하야 올치 딸각 소리는 교환수가 교환실로 드러오면서 문닷는 소리로구나? 하엿다.
『에- 모지모지 아노- ...』
그는 입이 달토록 외여 두엇든 전화번호가 갑작이 맥혀서 호주머니에서 수첩을 끄어내 들엇다.
『아노- 욘센 온햑구 나나주-이찌방』

『도찌라노 욘센 욘햑구 나나주-이찌방뎃스까?』
교환수의 말은 악가 보다도 매운기가 된다.
『아- 소데주 욘센 욘햑구 나나주-이찌방』
『도찌라노 욘셍 욘햑구 나나주 이찌방데스깟 도유노니 ... (此間지지 재재 자셔이 못드를 소리?)』
교환수의 목소리는 한칭 더 놉고 빨르고 날카롭고 아라드를 수 업섯다. 그는 머리속이 멍하고 등줄기의 심쭐의 뻐치는 것 가태서 얼른 수화기를 거러버렷다.
『분명히 말햇는데 엇재서 교환수가 그럴까? 히야까신 줄 알고 노한 모양이지?』 그는 고개를 갸웃둥 해 가지고 수첩에 적은 번호를 다시 똑바로 보면서 만지적 거렷다.
-도찌라노 도찌라노 햇스니 또찌라가 무엇인고 번호는 4471이 틀리지 안는데-
그가 조이라는 특수지대에서 자라난 도시의 청년이니만치 일본말에 그다지 귀가 어둡거나 감악눈은 아니엇다. 그러컷만 도찌라(何處)라는 이 쉬운 말이 그에게는 경우를 무시하고 연락에 다치안는 말이기 때문에 해득이 못 되엿다. 아닌 그의 지식권위 박게 잇는 일개의 숙어(熟語)로 생각할 박게 업섯다.
