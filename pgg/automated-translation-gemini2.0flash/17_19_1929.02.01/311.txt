기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Notice/Postscript)

Special Announcement

Recently, various magazines have been appearing, but some are making announcements as if they have an indirect—or even direct—relationship with our company in order to promote themselves, even though they have no connection to us. This is causing confusion among the general public and creating difficulties for our company. We ask that you please pay special attention. The only magazines currently managed by our company are *Byeol Geon-gon*, *Eorini*, and the newly published *Haksaeng*. <125>

Named Entities

어린이	Eorini
學生	Haksaeng
