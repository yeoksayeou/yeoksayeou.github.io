Marriage Age for Men and Women: What Age is Appropriate for Marriage for Men and Women?
						Medical Doctor I Gap-su

The marital system created through marriage can be said to be an industrial association that produces us, humankind. Therefore, only when a man and a woman, with fully matured sexual development as a man and woman respectively, marry each other as husband and wife, can they fully achieve their instinctive purpose. Hence, complete physical development is necessary for marriage, and to fully develop physically, an appropriate age is required. In other words, it is extremely legitimate for men and women to marry in their mature, adult years, and this will be a marriage based on the natural laws of humankind.

In our Joseon, the evil practice of premature marriage is prevalent. In severe cases, children who are not even 10 years old and cannot distinguish east from west are forced into restrictive marriages, creating nominal couples. Or, young boys around 10 years old are paired with women who are 17 or 18 years old and nearing maturity, sadly wasting the women's blossoming youth. It is truly an unspeakable human tragedy. The harm caused by this premature marriage is not limited to this. Among the countless other issues, the most important is that it is extremely harmful to the health of the men and women themselves. To use an analogy, it is no different from planting immature seeds and expecting them to grow into fruit trees that will bear good fruit in the future. The seeds will rot before sprouting, and even if they do sprout, they will not become complete fruit trees. Next, the offspring, the descendants, will not be strong, and their constitutions will be weak, leading to the frequent occurrence of various diseases, which will be an enemy to human eugenics. Therefore, in ancient Greece, in order to obtain healthy citizens, the law stipulated the marriage age as 35 to 40 years for men and over 30 years for women. Also, in Germany, in order to prohibit premature marriage, marriage was not legally permitted unless men were over 25 years old and women were over 22 years old. As a result, they produced citizens who were superior in terms of their physical strength and were the most robust. Furthermore, unlike our Joseon, the actual marriage age in various European countries is actually 10 years later than the age stipulated by law. This is because the hygienic awareness and general mindset of the people in those countries have progressed, and the time to discuss the impossibility of premature marriage has already passed centuries ago. Furthermore, they have advanced to the point where they consider adulthood to not be the right time for marriage, and they wait for the peak of reproductive ability in adulthood. For reference, the actual marriage age in various European countries is, on average, 26 years old for men and 25 years old for women in England. In France, it is 28 years old for men and 25 years old for women on average. In Norway, it is 28 years old for men and 26 years old for women on average. In the Netherlands, it is 29 years old for men and <61> 28 years old for women on average. In Belgium, it is 30 years old for men and 28 years old for women on average. Averaging all of these countries, it is about 28 years old for men and 26 years old for women. From this, it can be said that it is a suitable age for men, but from a physiological point of view, it cannot be said that it is too late for women. The reason is that the development of reproductive function is faster in women than in men, and it declines earlier. Then, what age is most suitable for marriage? I would like to briefly introduce my humble opinion. The period of life when our life abilities are most vigorous is from about 20 to 38 years old for women and from about 28 to 48 years old for men. Therefore, we must seek the most suitable age combination within this period and marry. As mentioned above, since women's general physical or sexual development is faster than men's, there must be a difference in age between men and women. Then, another question is, how much age difference between men and women is appropriate? There are several opinions from scholars on this, but generally, women should be 6 to 10 years younger than men, with an average of 8 years, which would be the most appropriate age difference between men and women. However, couples who marry relatively late should have a greater age difference between men and women than this, and couples who marry relatively early should have a smaller age difference between men and women than this. The reason can be understood in detail by looking at the marriage age combination table below. And, according to the marriage age combination table of Mr. "Zelheim" explained in Dr. Bagwon's marriage medicine introduced in the Osaka Asahi Shimbun the other day, it can be said to be almost suitable, but the age of women is slightly later than the age of men, and the age difference between men and women seems to be small. Then, what age combination is the most appropriate? The combination of a 28-year-old man and a 20-year-old woman, a 29-year-old man and a 21-year-old woman, and a 30-year-old man and a 23-year-old woman is the most reasonable. In other words, the period from 28 to 30 years old for men and from 20 to 22 years old for women is the most suitable marriage age. At the same time, it is possible to smoothly realize the marital life, which is the natural law of monogamy, and first, infertility is extremely rare, and the pregnancy rate will be high, and next, it is possible to avoid both eclampsia, which occurs frequently in young primiparas, and dystocia, which occurs frequently in older primiparas. If we marry earlier than the age discussed above, we can call it relatively early marriage, or if we marry later than that age, we can call it relatively late marriage, so we should try to find our partner within this period and have a rational marriage as much as possible. 결혼으로써 생겨난 부부제도는 우리 인류를 제작하야 내이는 산업조합이라 하겟슴니다. 그럼으로 남자는 남성으로서의 성적 발육이 완전히 성숙되며 여자는 여성으로서의 삀±적 발육이 완전히 성숙된 후에 서로 결혼된 부부라야 자기의 본능인 목적은 원만히 達할 수 잇는 것임니다. 是故로 결혼에 대하야는 완전한 신체의 발육이 필요하며 완전히 신체가 발육하자면 이에 적당한 연령이 필요함니다. 즉 남녀가 서로 성숙기인 장년기에 결혼하는 것이 극히 합법적인 동시에 따라 이것이 인류의 천연적 법칙에 의한 부부가 될 것임니다.
우리 조선에는 조혼의 악습이 유행되야 심한 경우에는 10세도 못된 아직 東西를 분별치 못하는 아해로 하야금 구속적 결혼을 식히여 허명만의 부부를 맨드러 주며 또는 10세 가량된 어린 남자과 17,8세나 된 거히 성숙기에 달한 여자와 배필을 삼어 여성의 꼿다운 청춘을 애석히 허송케 하는 등 실로 형언할 수 업는 인간의 비극을 연출하얏슴니다. 이 조혼으로 인하야 초래하는 해가 비단 이것뿐 안이라 그 외에 비일비재한 중 극히 중요한 것을 말하면 첫재 그 남녀 자신의 위생상 극히 해로운 것이니 譬諭하자면 아직 성숙치 못한 종자를 심어 장래 양호한 과실이 맷칠 果樹가 되라는 것과 少許도 차이가 업는 바, 出芽 전에 그 종자가 부패될 것이요 설혹 출아된다 할지라도 완전한 과목이 되지 못할 것은 사실이라 하겟슴니다. 다음에는 그 후계자인 자손이 강건치 못하며 체질이 연약하야 제반 악병이 다수히 발생하는 것이니 이는 인류 優生에 대한 적이 될 것임니다. 그럼으로 고대 希臘에서는 건강한 국민을 엇기 위하야 법률상으로 남자는 35세 내지 40세, 여자는 30세 이상이라는 결혼의 연령을 제정하얏섯스며 또 獨逸에서는 조혼을 금하기 위하야 남자는 25세 이상 여자는 22세 이상 이 안이면 법률상 결혼을 허락치 안이 하얏슴니다. 그리한 결과 성적이 우량하야 가장 강건한 국민을 산출하게 되얏다 함니다. 그뿐 안이라 歐洲 各國은 우리 조선과 정반대로 실제 결혼하는 연령이 법률상 규정한 연령보다도 도리혀 10년이나 더 느즘니다. 이는 그 나라 인민의 위생적 관념과 일반 정신이 진보되야 조혼의 불가를 논할 시기는 발서 세기를 밧귀엿고 일보 더 進하야 성년으로도 오히려 결혼의 시기가 아니라 하야 생식능력의 최고도인 장년긊�를 기대하는 까닭임니다. 참고로 歐洲 各國의 실제 결혼연령을 말하면 英國에는 평균 남자는 26세, 여자는 25세요 佛蘭西에난 평균 남자는 28세, 여자는 25세요 諾威에는 평균 남자는 28세, 여자는 26세요 和蘭에는 평균 남자는 29세, 여자는＜61＞ 28세요 白耳義에는 평균 남자는 30세, 여자는 28세인 바, 이 列國의 전체를 평균한 즉 남자는 28세 여자는 26세 가량임니다. 이것으로 보면 남자로난 적합한 年期라 하겟스나 여자로는 생리학으로 보와서 만혼이라 안이할 수 업슴니다. 그 원인은 여자는 남자보다. 그 생식기능에 대한 발육이 신속하며 따라 일즉이 쇠퇴하야지는 까닭이라 하겟슴니다. 그러면 엇던 年期가 결혼에 가장 적합할 것이냐 나의 愚見를 잠시 소개하려 함니다. 우리 인생으로서 가장 생활능력이 왕성한 年期는 여자로는 약 20세로부터 38세까지요 남자로난 약 28세부터 48세까지의 기간이라 하겟슴니다. 그런 즉 우리는 이 기간 내에 제일 적합한 연령의 配合을 구하야 결혼하여야 할 것인바 상술한 바와 가티 여자는 남자보다 일반 신체 또는 성적 발육이 速함으로써 그 남녀의 배합에 반다시 年齡의 차이가 잇슬 것은 물론임니다. 그러면 또 한가지 문제되는 것은 그 남녀연령의 얼마만한 차이가 잇서야 적합할 것이냐?
