Welcome Secret Orders
We hope that all readers of this magazine will become editorial advisors. The secret investigation team reporters of this magazine are always ready to be dispatched and are awaiting the orders of all readers. Whether it's, "Investigate the mischief of a villain in a certain place in a certain house," or, "Investigate the inner workings of a strange demon cave in a certain place in a certain house," or, "There is a strange person or a suspicious event in such and such a place, so please investigate," please notify the "Pyŏl Kŏn'gon Investigation Department (別乾坤調査部)" discreetly, and we will immediately dispatch for a secret investigation and report it in the magazine.

Even though we protect secrets in the magazine, please specify the location, address, the person's name, and if a woman, her age, to the headquarters, and please also let us know the address of the person who is writing so that we can contact them for investigation purposes.

秘密命令歡迎
本誌를 닑으시는 讀者가 모다 編輯顧問이 되어주십시오 本誌의 秘密調査班記者가 恒常出動準備를하고 讀者諸氏의 命令을기다리고잇슴니다 아모곳아모집에 독갑이작란이잇스니 調査하라던지 아모곳아모집에 恠異한 魔窟이잇스니 그 內面을 調査하라던지 어느곳어느곳에 怪常한 人物이잇거나 疑心나는 事件이잇스니 調査하라고 넌즛이 『別乾坤調査部』로 通知해주시면 卽刻出動秘密調査하야 誌面으로 報告하겟슴니다.
誌上에는 秘密을지히드라도 本社지는 그 場所番地 人物이면 女性年齡을 明記하시고 投書하시는이의 住所도알려주서야 調査上連絡할수잇겟슴니다.
