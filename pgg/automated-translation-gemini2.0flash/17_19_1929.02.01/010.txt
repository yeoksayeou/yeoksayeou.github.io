Plum Blossom
Lee Byeong-gi

Plum Blossom
Even with a low eaves, the sunlight always shines in,
In the aged atmosphere, the plum blossoms are about to bloom.
How is it different from the usual, yet it feels new every time I see it.

Spending the lonely winter days in sorrow,
A single plum blossom shows me the spring.
This body becomes a butterfly and I wake up to find it's a dream.

Narcissus
The plum blossom bud is like a bright lip,
The cluster of narcissus is like an alluring face,
The hidden fragrance it emits is also like a jade.

After the chrysanthemums wither and the citron also dries up,
The narcissus blooms and is particularly beautiful,
I quietly try to paint it, just staying up all night.＜1＞

梅花
						李秉岐
梅花
나즌 첨아라도 벼티매양 비처들어
낡은 분위에 매화는 피려하네
해갈어 다르랴만은 볼제마다 새로워.

쓸쓸한 겨을날을 시름으로 보낼러니
매화꼿 한송이에 봅나라를 보여주네
이몸은 나비가되여 깨어보니 꿈이라네.

水仙花
매화 붕오리는 밝안한 입술가티
수선화 슥은송이 아양스런 얼굴가티
으늑이 피우는향내 또한누와 가트리.

국화 시든뒤에 柚子또한 마르더니
수선화 피어나며 유달리도 아름다워
고요히 그리려다가 밤을그저 새우네.＜1＞
