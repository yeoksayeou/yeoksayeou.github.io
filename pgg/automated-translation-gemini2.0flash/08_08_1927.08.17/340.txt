Strange Incident?! A Bizarre Couple
						Hokkyokusei

Ring, ring, ring.

The telephone rang suddenly and loudly, and the Chappuring Inspector (a nickname earned because his beard resembled Chappuring, the cartoon character) on night duty, who had been half-dozing, threw down his newspaper, grabbed the telephone, and put the receiver to his ear.

"Hello, hello, is that Inspector Kim?"

He was momentarily taken aback by the unexpectedly lovely woman's voice.

"Yes, yes, this is Kim," he replied, pretending to be Kim.

"Oh, I'm so glad. I was worried about what would happen if someone else answered the phone... I was anxious even as I called... It's been cutting off and connecting me to the wrong places since earlier, and I was about to die of frustration."

The lovely voice was being incredibly coquettish over the telephone, but the Inspector, of course, wasn't Kim, and he had no idea who the owner of that voice was.

Aha! This must be a kisaeng (妓生) calling her lover, but the operator mistakenly connected her to this police station. Well, it's not exactly proper, but on a night like tonight when I'm on night duty, it's not unwelcome, the Inspector thought, and he simply continued to listen.

"Hello? Why aren't you talking much today? Do you have another woman next to you? That's why you're not answering properly and hesitating... You shouldn't be flirting with other women behind my back."

"No, no, no other woman," the Inspector said, playing along mischievously.

"If there isn't, I forgive you, hahaha. By the way, I have something urgent to tell you. (Then, in a sudden, low voice) The boss (團長) says he's going to that place tomorrow night to do a job, and he's wondering whether he should take you along."

"Hmm..." The Inspector swallowed hard and said, "That would be appreciated," and then he pulled the telephone closer.

"But what the boss is talking about doing sounds really dangerous. It's too risky, so if the boss tells you to go with him, you should make an excuse and quit this time. I'm worried. Do you understand what I'm saying? Huh?"

"Yes, yes, I understand," the Inspector said, forcibly suppressing the curiosity and ambition that were bubbling up inside him at the prospect of single-handedly uncovering such a big case and gaining great merit... so that he wouldn't be discovered to be someone else, he asked in a low voice, playing it cool.

"By the way, where is that place you're talking about?"

"Oh, why don't you know? It's the XX Company in the OO district (00애 잇는 XX회사) that we've been planning for a while. I heard that they've recently received some important items there, and tomorrow night at 9 o'clock, when the company finishes work and closes its doors, the boss will go into the manager's office and hide, and when the manager tries to put the document box in the safe (金庫), he'll jump out and snatch it away and run. Then you're supposed to be waiting outside the window with the car, and you'll both escape together. Isn't that dangerous?"

"It is a rather dangerous plan..."

"That's why I'm telling you not to go this time and make some other excuse. And..." The woman stopped abruptly, as if she was about to say something else. "Oh dear, I think the boss is back. I'll call again later. Anyway, be sure to do as I say."

Click!! The call ended abruptly.

<140>

That's him! The fact that there have been signs of a strange person roaming around the city lately must be him.

It's definitely him. It's a call from the boss's mistress or lover (情婦) who is secretly communicating with the subordinate she loves, deceiving the boss.

Anyway, it's very fortunate that I found out about this secret in the middle. I'm going to be able to achieve a great feat, thought the Chappuring Inspector, leaping and dancing with joy.

The night of the problem! The next night arrived, and from early evening, the Chappuring Inspector had his subordinate officers dress in plain clothes and stationed them at various points around the XX Company, and then he went into the manager's office.

The manager, as 9 o'clock gradually approached, was pounding his chest and unable to relax his face, looking bewildered.

<141>

"Don't worry about anything now. I've brought ten detectives to this company and had them stationed everywhere. If those guys come in without knowing, we'll arrest them on the spot. It's almost 9 o'clock. They'll be here soon. Now, Manager, pretend to go home and hide in a place like the night duty room. Don't let the employees notice, and have them all go home. If the employees notice, things will get noisy and messy. And on a night like tonight, don't put valuable items in the safe (金庫); leave them in the night duty room and have yourself guard them."

"Yes, yes, I'll do that," said the manager, hiding the box of valuables and pretending to go home, and he went into the night duty room.

Nine o'clock came. Nothing happened. An anxious, fearful time, as if a terrible war was about to break out, passed minute by minute, making their hearts pound.

Five minutes, ten minutes, fifteen minutes passed, but nothing happened. The employees had all gone home, and the company's main gate was heavily locked like an iron wall, and inside the large, quiet company building, only the detectives were lurking, either behind doors or under staircases, like cats watching for mice.

Eighteen minutes, nineteen minutes, twenty minutes. At that moment, the door of the night duty room suddenly opened, and the manager ran out with hurried steps to the Inspector, and in a worried, trembling voice, he said,

"I forgot something in my panic. There's a secret staircase that leads up to the manager's office in the back warehouse. I wonder if those guys know about it and are coming that way." The Inspector, who until now had thought that all the places that needed guarding were being guarded, was startled by those words.

"A secret staircase from the warehouse to the manager's office? That's bad. You should have told me about such a secret passage sooner. Then let's go investigate it now," he said, and he and the trembling manager turned on their flashlights and went down the staircase behind the manager's office.

After about seven or eight minutes, the Inspector came back up with a dissatisfied look, grumbling.

"Ugh, so-called managers are so foolish and cowardly! They just get scared and confused and even go to a warehouse that's not even a concern—"

He was grumbling when, at that moment, the door of the night duty room, which should have been empty, burst open, and a detective came running out, pounding his feet.

"Something terrible has happened! I heard a clattering noise in the night duty room, so I ran in, and the manager was lying there, having had his coat taken off and <142> wearing only his underwear, with his mouth, arms, and legs tied up. And when I looked out the window, there was a car next to the road, and a beautiful woman was sitting in it."

"What? Then the guy who pretended to be the manager and tricked me and took me to the warehouse was someone else. Is that who it was? Is the guy still in disguise as the manager in the warehouse? Everyone chase after him! I'll go out the window and arrest the woman in the car!"

The Inspector shouted, and he hurriedly jumped out the window himself.

As the detectives, following the Inspector's orders, rushed down the staircase and past the warehouse, they found the manager's clothes thrown on the warehouse floor, and next to it was none other than the Chappuring Inspector, with all his clothes taken off and wearing only his underwear, his entire body tightly bound.

They rushed over and untied the Inspector, who was stamping his feet and shouting,

"That guy came out of the manager's office, took the manager's clothes, tricked me, dragged me into the warehouse, took my clothes, and left alone!"

"Then that's the guy who was on the upper floor in the hallway, pretending to be the Inspector, and telling us to go this way!"

"Ugh! That's him. Quickly, chase after him and catch him! Quickly, quickly!"

The detectives rushed back upstairs and out the window, but it was already too late. From the beginning, the boss, called the Chief (團長), had a woman lure the inspector out with a seemingly accidental phone call, bound the manager in the night duty room and stole the valuables, disguised himself as the manager and lured the inspector to the warehouse, tied up the inspector and disguised himself as the inspector, jumped out the window, and drove away with the woman who had been waiting for a long time.

형사들은 또다시 우루루루 몰러 올러가서 들창밧게까지 쫏차 나왓스나 그러나 벌서 느젓다. 아초에 녀자를 식여서 일부러 잘못 걸닌 뎐화처럼 하야 경부를 꼬여 내고 오늘 숙직실에서 지배인을 결박하고 물건을 빼슨 후에 지배인으로 변장하고 나와서 경부를 꼬여서 창고에 다리고 가고 경부를 결박하고 경부로 변장한 단장이란 인물은 들창 밧그로 뛰여나가 기다리고 잇던 녀자와 함께 자동차를 몰아간지가 오래된 후엿다.＜143＞
