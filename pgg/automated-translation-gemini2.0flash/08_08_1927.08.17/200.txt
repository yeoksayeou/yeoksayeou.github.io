Article Title: 人間諷刺 食人蟲四頭會議 (Human Satire: A Four-Headed Conference of Anthropophagous Insects [Bedbug, Flea, Mosquito, Fly's Great Secret Meeting])
Article Type: 世態批評 (Critique of Social Trends)

Human Satire: A Four-Headed Conference of Anthropophagous Insects

A fellow named Nanjing Bug Second Gentleman (南京蟲次郞) (a bedbug’s modern official title), having somehow devoured someone’s neck and filled himself to bursting, crawls along in a dignified manner. His gait is like that of someone who has eaten a lot of fertilizer, or of a pregnant woman near her due date, or of a large jar belonging to a madam in J-dong. He mutters to himself, likely boasting, "Today, I cleverly and nimbly avoided the human's venomous hands and enjoyed their salty-sweet blood until my rear end nearly burst."

As if suffering from post-meal drowsiness, he settles behind the desk, muttering to himself again, "Humans are clever and venomous, but also foolish and clumsy. They try to control us by spreading poison, covering themselves with nets, and digging deep trenches, creating defenses more formidable than any empire's 금성탕지 (金城湯池, geumseongtangji) [impregnable fortress]. But, they are still the ones being eaten. We may lose dozens, hundreds every night to them, but hahaha, I'm full! Even if I die soon..." He continues to ramble like a drunkard.

Just then, a fellow named Flea Student (蚤生員) (Joe Saeng-won) appears, his whole body flushed red as if he had enjoyed a generous amount of wine. He approaches the desk, humming and performing 광대노름 (gwangdae noreum) [a clown's act], even doing a bit of a dance, as people say nowadays. He spots Nanjing Bug Second Gentleman. With a very frivolous demeanor, he jumps and bounces, saying, "Oh, Mr. Bedbug! Fancy meeting you here. Were you well on your way? Oh! You look like you've eaten heartily. What did you eat? Oh! Look at that belly..." and bursts into a fit of giddiness. Nanjing Bug Second Gentleman, maintaining his composure, replies, "Ah! Flea Student, is that you? I was feeling lonely. Good to see you. Was your income good today? You seem very drunk! You say I ate well? You've surpassed me in success, showing both skill and courage in preying on the ruthless human society. Anything special happen today? Where did you find a victim? Since I'm full, let's chat until the humans wake up, then head back on the mountain path at dawn, singing."

Flea Student relieves himself, exclaiming, "Ah! That's refreshing!" He approaches Nanjing Bug Second Gentleman and begins to recount his tale of attacking humans. "Mr. Bedbug! Haven't we always risked our lives for food? But tonight, I nearly died five times. Humans are truly ruthless… Perhaps due to the humidity, I’ve developed a bit of 脚氣症 (gakgi jeung) [beriberi], so I can’t jump as well as before. And since I haven’t been eating well due to 滯氣 (chegi) [indigestion] since the other day, I set out early last night. I left the corner behind the wardrobe and hopped toward the human kingdom. I hadn’t gone far when I found a young human sleeping soundly in front of the wardrobe. When you're hungry, do you care whether it's a child or an adult? To be honest, sticking to children is safer and more delicious. So, I quietly crawled under the covers. I pondered whether to bite the neck, the chest, or the soft area beneath the 사차귀불알아레 (sachagwi bulare) [scrotum]. But hunger knows no choices. So, I decided to just start at the entrance and sucked at its heel. The blood tasted milky, not too salty or bitter, but sweet, which would be quite nourishing. Since I was there, I thought I'd eat something softer and tastier, so I crawled up to the 사차귀 (sachagwi) [groin]. I attached myself just under the 불알 (bural) [testicles] and started sucking again. But the little rascal started to wake up. He squirmed and then rubbed his 사차귀 (sachagwi) [groin] a couple of times. If I hadn’t been careful, I would have been injured by that little hand. So, I was chased away from there and moved to his chest. I buried my mouth in the soft nipple and sucked like a calf nursing. But the little rascal woke up again, whimpering a couple of times and crying, 'Mommy!' Then his mother or grandmother—it must have been his grandmother—rushed over and said, 'Baby! What's wrong? What bit you? Where does it hurt? Is it a bedbug or a flea?' She quickly picked up the child and searched under his blanket and in his 옷 (ot) [clothes]. I thought, 'Oh no, if I’m discovered by this adult, I'm done for!' So, I hid in the dark under his armpit. The old woman meticulously examined the child’s 적삼 (jeoksam) [undershirt], searching every inch of the front, back, and sleeves. That’s when she spotted me. 'Run for your life!' I thought, as I hopped away. Then the woman shouted, 'Oh, flea—' and pointed a finger like a viper's head at me, chasing after me. No matter how much 脚氣症 (gakgi jeung) [beriberi] I have, how could she, an old human in the middle of the night, catch me? I jumped two or three times and landed in the middle of the room. It would be easier to find rice in the ocean than for her to catch me. So, she seemed to give up and said, 'Agh, frustrating. I missed him! Ugh, he bit him badly!' She examined the child’s chest, 사차귀 (sachagwi) [groin], and heels. I thought, 'I'll see you later,' and jumped again, still a good distance away, to escape. But what misfortune! I fell into the 빈대홈 (bindae hom) [bedbug pit] you guys dug to trap us. I swam for a while, swallowing water, and barely managed to crawl out. When I looked back, I saw many of Mr. Bedbug's friends trapped there, pitifully crying out. So, I escaped this treacherous place and crawled under the wardrobe, resting for a while until the big humans fell asleep again. Then I went out again, wanting revenge on the human who had scolded me earlier. I attacked her back, hoping to make it difficult for her to use her hands. She was sound asleep, so no matter how much I sucked, she didn't move. So, I sucked to my heart's content, and then, full, I went to her 넓즛한 (neoljeushan) [wide] chest and did a little dance. That's when I met my younger brother, Iron Flea (鐵蚤). He was already full and excited, and he asked me for a dance. So, we danced together for a while. That’s when she woke up. She groaned and rolled over, saying, 'Ugh! These fleas are making me 설네인다 (seolleinda) [itch]!' She opened her chest and tried to find us. I thought, 'Oh no, let's run!' Like a deer fleeing from a hunter, we jumped and ran in all directions. My brother was caught, but I luckily survived, though my heart is still pounding."

Nanjing Bug Second Gentleman, who had been listening to Flea Student's story with interest, burst into laughter, saying, "You really had a hard time. That's what happens when you try to eat others. I nearly died three times myself. I almost fell into a pit, the poison fumes almost made me faint, and my rear end got injured by their thrashing around. Anyway, if you want to eat well and avoid danger, the best time is when they're first falling asleep. Don't bother them when their eyes are wide open. And don't even think about attacking when they're waking up, like now, at dawn. It's too risky. I have a lot of experience, so I know. Humans have all sorts of tricks… Hey, Flea Student! Where is that singing coming from? Who's singing at this hour?"

"Well, that's General Mosquito (蚊將軍) singing. He's a great singer. Let's invite General Mosquito to join us and chat together…"

At that moment, General Mosquito flew in with an "Eeng!" and sat next to Nanjing Bug Second Gentleman, saying in a frivolous manner, "Oh my! Nanjing Bug and Flea Student are here too. How did you all gather? You both look like you've eaten well. I'm just hungry…" and made a buzzing sound again.

"We only have mouths and feet, yet we're not hungry. How can General Mosquito, who even has wings, be hungry? You must be teasing us…" The bedbug and flea both said mockingly.

"No, really, I haven't eaten. Even if you have wings, like an airplane, it's no use. Humans are truly ruthless. Let me tell you. In the early evening, they use things like fans and fly swatters to keep us from getting close. Then they use things like mosquito coils and mugwort to create a terrible smell. And after that, they cover themselves with nets and crawl inside. They really have a lot of tricks. So, what can you do but just circle outside, drooling over their plump, white flesh... Hahahaha, it's laughable. I started attacking when the humans were just getting drowsy in the early evening, before they were prepared. But they just heard my sound and slapped their own cheeks and smacked their own shins. It was so funny. I teased them by going 'Eeng' by one ear and 'Eeng' by the other ear, and they would slap one ear and then the other, saying 'Eeng!' It was so funny. Anyway, it's easier to tease humans than to tease a crazy cat…"

"Teasing is good, but you need income. Anyway, General Mosquito, your voice is your 병 (byeong) [weakness]. If you didn't make any noise, they might not even dream of you. Anyway, that voice is your downfall."

Suddenly, there's a "Wing" sound, and something falls onto the desk. The bedbug, flea, and mosquito all raise their heads and look up, and there sits Detective Fly (蠅探偵), rubbing his hands together as if in prayer, saying, "Oh, merciful Hanu (하-느님) [God]! Why is there such a difference in fortune among living beings? I fly around all day and night, but all I get to eat is dung, and all I smell is rotting things. How can it be so harsh? I'm so frustrated. In the early evening, I went to the humans' dinner party, hoping to get some 고기 (gogi) [meat], 사탕 (satang) [candy], 떡 (tteok) [rice cake], or 밥 (bap) [rice], but I only got slapped and had to come back empty-handed. I thought I might steal something while they were sleeping, so I flew around the ceiling, walls, and floor, but the humans were so careful to put the food they ate into containers and close the lids tightly. I just swallowed my 침 (chim) [saliva] and had no choice but to go to the 수챗구멍 (suchaetgumeong) [drain] and eat some of the 밥찍기 (bapjikkireul) [leftover rice] they had thrown away. Other than that, I only ate dung. I'm so frustrated. Why is there such discrimination among living beings…?" He lamented his fate.

Listening to this, Nanjing Bug Second Gentleman, Flea Student, and General Mosquito burst into laughter, saying, "Oh, Detective Fly is complaining about his fate. He must be having a hard time today… Hey, Detective Fly, come here. Don't mutter to yourself, come and chat with us." They invited him over.

The bedbug, flea, mosquito, and fly, these four anthropophagous insects, gathered in one place. A conference was opened.

Bedbug: "It's a good thing we've gathered together unexpectedly. Let's chat to our hearts' content and then part ways."

Flea: "That's good. Let's each talk about the inside view of human society."

Mosquito: "That's right, but let's also figure out how to eat well, live well, and preserve our species without being caught by the humans."

Fly: "More than that, let's start by understanding our own shortcomings. If we're going to attack others, we need to know our own shortcomings and strengths first…"

Bedbug: "That's a very good point. Then let's each speak honestly about our shortcomings and strengths. First, I'll talk about Detective Fly. Don't be angry if I go too far… Detective Fly, I think your only strength is that you can smell well. You somehow know when someone is defecating, even in the deepest mountains and valleys, so that's your strength. And if being good at 색기 (saeggi) [procreation] is a strength, then that's also a strength. And the way you raise your front paws and rub them together as if praying, even in the most urgent situations, means you'll probably get to heaven before the humans' missionaries. Haha! And your shortcomings are that you're as filthy as you are good at smelling, and as 너저분 (neojeobun) [messy] as you are good at 색기 (saeggi) [procreation]. You go to all the dirty and 너저분한 (neojeobunhan) [messy] places—dog dung, cow dung, latrines, urine bowls, rotting corpses of snakes, dead chicks—and then you sit on people's good, clean clothes without washing your feet or brushing your teeth. Who would like that? Especially when you sit on the soft cheeks and red lips of beautiful women. Isn't that too shameless? Perhaps it doesn't matter if it's on the 검버슷 돗은 (geombeoseut doseun) [liver-spotted] face of an old 노축 (nochuk) [old lecher]… And you often defecate in rice bowls, soup bowls, 장독 (jangdok) [crocks for sauces], and 반찬 항아리 (banchan 항아리) [side dish containers], causing maggots to swarm immediately. Who would like that? And you spread 독균 (dokgyun) [harmful bacteria] and 세균 (segyun) [germs], causing 호열자 (hoyeoljareul) [cholera] and 장질부사 (jangjilbusa) [typhoid fever]. Aren't you a troublemaker to humans? And you go to the faces of sleeping children and suck their lips, cheeks, and even 콧딱지 (kottakji) [boogers] and 눈꼽 (nunkkop) [eye boogers], and then you always defecate and leave. You follow new and pretty clothes around and make them dirty, and you always cause trouble when people are sleeping or eating… Anyway, your habits are the worst…"

Fly: "Mr. Nanjing, I've heard your words well. But what about your faults? You launch a total assault on men, women, young, and old, even babies, in the middle of the night when everyone is sleeping soundly. Where can you find such a cruel act? And, more than anything, your smell is 괴악 (goeak) [horrible]. Who wouldn't cover their nose and turn away from your horrible, rancid smell? And you go into the places where couples are sleeping sweetly together and bite their necks, backs, thighs, calves, and heels. Where can you find such a cruel and shameless act? And you 피칠하기 (pichilhagi) [smear blood] on freshly washed clothes, and 남의 갓발는 벽에다 난초치기 (namui gatbalneun byeogeda nanchochigi) [draw orchids on freshly washed walls]. Who would like you when you only do such dirty and nasty things…"

Flea: "Both of you gentlemen's criticisms are reasonable. They hit the mark exactly. Now, let me criticize Mr. General Mosquito. As I said before, Mr. Mosquito, your voice is your 병 (byeong) [weakness]. Who wouldn't get angry when they hear that 앵-앵 (aeng-aeng) [buzzing] sound? Agh, how 요망스럽기도 하지 (yomangsreopgido haji) [bewitching]! Even Xiang Yu (項羽) would draw his sword at that sound, and even a young woman would slap her own cheek. Is there any sound as 요망스런 (yomangsreun) [bewitching] that would never get you a single spoonful of 밥 (bap) [rice], even if you starved for thirteen days? And the 침 (chim) [saliva] in your mouth is the most 독함 (dokham) [poisonous] of our four major anthropophagous insects. It looks as flimsy as 갈품 (galpum) [reed flowers], but how is it so 독살맛게 (doksalmatge) [poisonous]? And you're good at spreading malaria (瘧疾)…"

Mosquito: "Hey, Mr. Flea, what about you? You're no bigger than a 담배씨 (dambae ssi) [tobacco seed], and you boldly stick out your whiskers, hopping around and making people's fingertips disappear. And you go under people's armpits and 불알 (bural) [testicles], 바스락바스락 (baseurak baseurak) [rustling] and 설네며 (seolleimyeo) [itching] and defecating. Who would like that? 엑기 요-망난이 (ekgi yo-mangnan-i) [Ugh, you wicked thing]…"

Bedbug: "Okay, that's enough criticism of each other's shortcomings. We'll start fighting if we go too far. Let's stop and talk about how we can eat well, live well, and preserve our species without being caught by the humans. Detective Fly, you have the most connections and the best 비위 (biwi) [stomach], so you go first."

Fly: "Well, there's not much we can do. Humans are cleverer and stronger than us, and they have all sorts of 약 (yak) [medicine] and 기구 (gigu) [devices]—flea medicine, bedbug medicine, mosquito nets, fly traps—so it's impossible to confront or attack them head-on, day or night. We have to deceive them, fool them, and embrace 강 (gang) [strength] within 柔 (yu) [gentleness]. We have to attack quietly and without a trace. The best time to do that is when they're sleeping soundly. And the safest way to attack is from behind, rather than from the front where they can see us. And once we attack, whether we succeed or not, we should run away immediately and give them enough time to forget about it and relax before attacking again. If we try to attack again right away, we'll get into big trouble. The reason why thousands or millions of us four anthropophagous insects are sacrificed every day is mostly because we're not good at that method…"

Everyone agreed and applauded.

Fly: "Well, it's getting late, and the humans will be waking up soon, so let's say a few more words and part ways. Let's talk briefly about the 裏面 (rimyeon) [inner workings] or 꼬락산 (kkoraksan) [true nature] of the humans that we've seen. Since I'm already talking, I'll go first. Ahem…"

Humans are truly 웃기 (utgi) [funny], 맹랑 (maengnang) [absurd], 망측스럽고 (mangcheukseureopgo) [outrageous], and 괴약한 (goeyakhan) [bizarre] animals. There's no one as 위선 (wiseon) [hypocritical] as humans. During the day, they talk about 도덕 (dodeok) [morality], 인륜 (illyun) [ethics], 체면 (chomyeon) [face-saving], and 례의 (ryeui) [etiquette], and they dress up in clothes, hats, and shoes, pretending to be the most 점잔은 (jeomjaneun) [dignified] and 사람 (saram) [human]. But at night, they strip naked and 막 딍굴며 (mak dwinggulmyeo) [roll around wildly], doing all sorts of 해괴망측한 작란을 (hoegoemangcheukhan jakran-eul) [bizarre mischief]. When I pretend to sleep on the ceiling and watch them, it's so funny I could die. The 어린 색씨 (eorin saekssi) [young wives] and 새서방 (saeseobang) [newlyweds] are the funniest, and I can't stand watching the old 영감 (yeonggam) [old men] and 마누라 (manura) [wives]… And at night, they gather together and talk about stealing, taking things from others, fighting, and killing. All they talk about is that kind of stuff. Look at this: 'Bring the fly swatter, bring the mosquito coil, bring the bedbug medicine, 뿌려라 (ppuryeora) [spray] the flea medicine…’ Isn't that all talk about killing? 'Tomorrow morning, let's catch a chicken, bring beef, buy 무 (mu) [radish], buy 배추 (baechu) [cabbage]…' Isn't that all about killing 食物 (sigmul) [food]? And who doesn't want to survive by making others 못살게 (motsalge) [uncomfortable] by taking something from someone, 속키고 (sokkigo) [deceiving] someone, slapping someone, or suing someone? They're truly 暴惡한 (poageuhan) [violent] animals. We suck the blood of humans, but humans suck the blood of their own comrades. Which human with a little money or power isn't sucking the blood of others?"

Bedbug: "That's right. I've nicknamed humans 'lying animals.' They're so good at lying. They go out and pretend to be good, talking about society, the nation, love, and compassion, and pretending to be good for the sake of the community. But when they come home and whisper to their women under the blanket, almost all of it is about eating others. I know that best, because I'm right there in their 벼갯귀 (byeogatgwi) [pillow corners] and 니불속 (nibul 속) [inside blankets], so I hear everything from beginning to end without missing a single word."

Mosquito: "That's right. They call us mosquitoes 독하다 (dokhada) [poisonous] and this and that, but which one of those bastards doesn't have a 칼 (kal) [knife], 총 (chong) [gun], 독기 (dokgi) [poison], or 송곳 (songgot) [awl]? Even if they don't have them physically, they all have at least one 칼 (kal) [knife] hidden in their hearts. Not a single one of them—whether they're a girl or a child—who has the skin of a human doesn't have one hidden. And yet they talk so much about good and evil."

Flea: "Okay, let's part ways. If that fat pig of a human finds out we're criticizing them, they'll start 뒷첫다 쳇첫다 (dwitcheotda cheotcheotda) [scratching and clawing]…"

Bedbug: "That's right! Let's part ways. It'll be a big problem if we don't. Especially since we're slow and have no wings…"

Mosquito: "Okay, then let's meet again tonight. Eeng!"

Flea: "See you later. Kkangjung!"

Fly: "Will I only get dung again today? Wing!"

Bedbug: "Those with wings can go easily! Eonggiteonggit (엉깃엉깃) [Crawling]!"

人間諷刺 食人蟲四頭會議
南京蟲次郞(빈대의 신식관명)이란 자가 엇던 놈의 목덜미를 뜻어먹엇는지 엇잿든 똥구멍까지 터질 듯이 마룩마룩하게 잔득 어더먹고서 둑겁이 거름으로 안이 제법 키적은 양반 비지 먹은 거름으로 엇더케 보면 똥똥한 색시 만삭된 거름으로, 또 엇더케 보면 J동 마님 절구통 거름으로 제법 점잔케 엉깃엉깃 기여오면서 무어라고 혼자서 주둥이를 놀니고 잇다. 그것은 아마 『오늘은 인간놈의 그 독수에 걸니지 안코 묘하게 민활하게 인간놈의 그 쓰고 짭잘한 피를 똥구멍이 터지도록 잘 어더먹엇다』는 수작인 듯하다. 역시 식곤증이 나는지 책상밋 쓰러박기 뒤에 턱-젯치고 서더니 또 혼자ㅅ말로 『엇잿든 인간놈이란 꾀도 만코 독도 만치만 愚하기도 하고 물넝물넝도 하여 우리들 제어하랴고 독약을 뿌리고 망사를 두루고 심연을 파고 그야말로 엇던 제국의 金城湯池보다도 더 굉장히 별별 방법의 시설을 다해 노앗지만 엇잿든 먹키긴 우리에게 먹키는 놈들이야. 우리도 하로저녁 멧십 멧백명식 저놈의게 희생이 되지만. 하하하 엇잿든 배부르니 좃타! 죽을 때 죽는 셈치고라도...』
술주정군 모양으로 이럿케 혼자 떠들고 잇다. 잇때 맛츰 蚤生員이란 자가 역시 어대서 포도주 잔이나 톡톡히 한 것가티 전신이 빨갓케 무루 녹아가지고 그것도 흥이 나는지 혼자서 광대노름을 하면서 시속말로 제법 딴쓰를 하면서 책상 밋트로 기여든다. 맛츰 蟲次郞을 맛낫다. 몹시 경망한 태도로 깡충 뛰엿다 내려서더니 蟲次郞을 보고,
『아구! 아젓씨 여긔서 뵈옵겟슴니다 그래. 一路에 무사하섯소? 아구! 무엇 톡톡히 잡수신 모양이군. 무얼 그럿케 잘 잡수섯소. 아구! 저-배보아...』하고 한바탕 까부러 쌋는다. 蟲次郞은 제법 점잔을 빼면서

빈대 『그것 참-조흔 말이요. 그럼 우리 각각 숨기지 말고 피차의 短處 及 長處를 말하기로 합시다. 爲先 내가 蠅探偵네 足下부터 말할께. 혹-말이 과해지드라도 노여워마소...蠅足下는 엇잿든 냄새 잘 맛는 것은 유일의 長處로 아오. 深山幽谷에서 누가 똥을 싼다 해도 엇더케 아는지 당장 아러내니까 그것이 장기이고 또한 색기 잘치는 것도 長處라면 長處이지. 그리고 아무리 급한 때라도 압발을 드러 싹싹 비비며 기도하는 것은 인간의 傳道군보다 천당에 먼저 가겟데. 하하 그리고 短處로는 냄새를 잘 맛는이 만치 그만큼 너저분하고 색기를 잘치는 이만치 그만콤 너저분하지. 개똥밧 쇠똥밧 뒷깐 오줌깐 독사죽엄 병아리죽엄 더럽고 너저분한 데는 다 차저 단이다가 발도 안씻고 양치도 안하고 남의 조흔자리 산듯한 의복에 서슴지 안코 함부로 올나 안즈니 누가 조와하겟나. 더구나 입분 색씨들의 고- 보드라운 빰이나 고- 앵도가튼 입살에 올나안는 것이야 넘우나 렴치 업는 즛이 안인가. 혹시 老축의 그 검버슷 돗은 얼골은 관게치 안치만...그리고 밥그릇 국그릇 장독 반찬 항아리 할 것 업시 걸핏하면 쉬를 쓰러붓쳐 금시 곳 구덕이가 와글와글-하게 하니 누가 조타 하겟나. 그리고 독균 세균을 함부로 뭇처다가 虎列刺니 膓窒扶斯니 그따위 전염병을 전파식키니 누가 조와하겟나. 엇잿든 자네는 심술쟁이로 인간에게서 크게 말성거리이데. 그 뿐인가 어린애 곱게 자는 얼골에 가서 입살 빰쌀 심지어 콧딱지 눈꼽...다 빠라 먹고는 의례 똥을 내갈기고 오지. 새옷이나 고흔 옷은 따라 단니며 더럽피고 잠잘 때나 밥먹을 때면 긔여히 말성을 부리고...엇잿든 자네 버릇이 제일 고약하느니...』
파리 『南京足下말은 잘 드럿네. 그러나 자네 허물은 또 엇던가. 深夜三更에 남들이 곤하게 자는 틈을 타서 남녀노소 갓난색기까지 총출동을 하야 동서남북 土下左右로 무리습격을 하니 그런 잔인한 일이 어대잇나. 그리고＜89＞ 자네는 무엇보다 그 냄새가 괴악하느니 아이구 자네의 괴약한 그 노린 냄새에야 누가 코를 막고 도라서지 안켓나. 그리고 남 부처끼리 자미낫게 자는 자리에 드러가 목덜미를 물고 등댁이를 물고 불기짝을 물고 장단지, 발꿈치 함부로 무러주니 그런 잔인 몰염치한 즛이 어대잇겟나. 그리고 나의 갓빠른 縟에다 피칠하기, 남의 갓발는 벽에다 난초치기. 그럿케 더럽고 못된 즛만 하니 누가 조타겟나...』
벼룩 『두분 아저씨의 두분 평이 다 그럴 듯하오. 꼭꼭 내 마음에 드러맛소. 그런데 내가 蚊將軍네 아젓씨 평을 할께 드러보오. 蚊씨 아젓씨야 말로 악가도 말하엿지만 고-목소리가 병입늰다. 고-앵-앵-하는 소리를 듯고야 누가 성을 안이 내겟소. 아구 요망스럽기도 하지. 고 소리에는 項羽라도 칼을 빼고 새앗씨라도 제빰을 제가 때리고야 말지. 글세 고런 열사흘 굶어도 밥 한 술 안 생길 요망스런 소리가 엇대 잇겟소. 그리고 고-주둥이 침도 우리 四大食人蟲에 제일 독함늰다. 갈품(芦花)가티 보기에는 엉성한 것이 엇지면 그리 독살맛게 생겻는지. 그리고 瘧疾을 一手 잘 전하겟지...』
모긔 『애애 벼룩네 足下야 너는 엇더냐. 담배씨만도 못한 것이 게다가 검방지게 수염을 딱 뻣치고 호독독 호독독 뛰여단니며 남의 손가락심만 업새주고 그리고 남의 겨드랑이나 불알 밋테 드러가 바스락바스락 설네며 똥만 내갈기니 그것을 누가 좃타겟늬. 엑기 요-망난이...』
빈대 『자-그만하면 피차의 短處평은 되얏소. 너무하면 싸홈 나겟소. 그만하고 우리 인간놈들께 해밧지 안코 잘 먹고 잘 살고 종족보전할 도리를 니약이 합시다. 蠅探偵네 足下가 제일 발이 넓고 또 비위가 조흐니 먼저 니약이하시오』
파리 『그거야 뭐 별수 잇소. 인간놈들이란 엇잿든 우리보다 꾀도 만코 힘도 만코 벼룩약 빈대약 모기장 파리통 별에별 약과 별 기구가 만흐닛가 그를 정면으로 대적한다거나 습격하기는 주야불문하고 도저히 용이한 일이 안이고 엇잿든 그 놈들은 속켜야 되고 얼녀야 되고, 柔중에 强을 품어야 되고 無聲無跡의 중에 가만히 살작 습격을 해야되네. 그리하기에는 그놈들이 곤하게 잘 때가 제일 좃코 그리고 습격방면은 그놈들의 눈이 박킨 압면보다 후면습격이 제일 안전한 방법일세. 그리고 한번 습격하고는 成功不成功간 곳 다라낫다가 그놈들이 니저버리고 안심할만한 여유를 주엇다가 다시 습격하야지 그 당시 곳 재습격을 하다가는 큰 봉변일세. 우리네 사대 식인충이 날마다 몃천몃만 머리 희생되는 것도 대개 그 방법이 서틀너서 그리되는 걸세...』
일동은 올타고 모다 拍掌을 한다.
파리 『자-시간도 꽤 가고 또 조곰 잇스면 인간놈들이 니러날 터이니 얼는 멧마듸 더하고 헤여지세. 우리가 본＜90＞ 인간놈들의 裏面 꼬락산이나 간단간단히 니약이 해보세. 말하든 끗이니 내가 먼저 하지. 에헴...』
인간놈들 이란 참말 우습고 맹랑하고 망측스럽고 괴약한 동물들이데. 세상에 위선 위선하야도 인간놈들 가티 위선자는 업겟데. 글세 이놈들이 낫에는 소위 도덕이니 인륜이니 체면이니 례의이니 하고 떠들면서, 옷을 입느니 모자를 쓰느니 신발을 신느니 하야 가장 점잔은 체 가장 사람인 체하다가 밤이면 뻘거벗고 막 딍굴며 별별 해괴망측한 작란을 다 하데 그려. 천정에 가만히 붓터자는 체하고 엿보노라면 참말 우슴이 나서 죽을 지경이야. 그 중에 어린 색씨 새서방의 작란이 더 우슴나고 늙은 영감 마누라의 작란은 가업서 못보겟고...그리고 이놈들은 밤이면 모여드러 도적질할 니약이 남의 것 빼아슬 니약이 남과 쌈할 니약이 남 죽일 니약이 그따윗 니약이 뿐이데 그려. 자-이것 보게. 파리통 가저 오너라 모긔향 가저 오너라 빈대약 가저 오너라 벼룩약 뿌려라...이 따위 소리가 말금 죽이자는 니약이가 안인가. 내일 아츰은 닭을 잡자 쇠고기를 가저오자 무를 사자 배추를 사자...하는 것이 모다 食物 죽이자는 것이 안인가. 그리고 누구에게 무엇밧고 엇던 놈을 엇더케 속키고 또 누구는 빰을 때리고 누구는 고소를 하는 등- 모다 남을 못살게 하고 자기만 살자는 것이 안인가. 참말 暴惡한 동물들이지. 우리는 사람놈들의 피를 빠라먹지만 사람놈들은 자기의 동무의 피를 빠라먹고 살데 그려. 사람놈 치고 돈푼이나 잇고 권세개나 잇는 놈치고 누가 남의 피를 안이 빨아내인 것인가.』
빈대 『참말 그럿치오. 나는 인간들을 거짓말하는 동물이라고 별명을 지엇지오. 놈들이야말로 거짓말도 참 잘합듸다. 놈들이 밧게 나아가서는 제법 사회니 민족이니 사랑이니 동정이니 하고 가장 공동을 위하야 선한 체하다가도 집안에 드러와 니불속에서 녀편네하고 속은거리는 거야 거의 전부가 남 잡아먹을 니약이임니다 그려. 그거야 내가 제일 잘 알지오. 그놈들의 벼갯귀나 니불속에 쑥 드러가 자초지종 한 마듸도 빼지 안코 드른 사실 니약이니까요.』
