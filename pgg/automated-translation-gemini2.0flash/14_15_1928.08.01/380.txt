Barbershop Vote Preliminary Results

The barbershop vote for six cities, which was announced and solicited in the previous month's issue, did not yield very good results, perhaps because of the summer vacation. First, if we announce only the number of votes received by July 25th, it is as follows:

Kyŏngsŏng: Chungang (Chongno 2-ga) 115 votes, Tunan (Chongno 3-ga) 107 votes, Tongch'ang (An'guk-tong) 82 votes, Kwangch'ŏn (Sŏrin-tong) 14 votes
P'yŏngyang: Tongyang (Taedongmun-tong) 17 votes, Ilmi (Sŏram-ri) 11 votes, Kisŏng (Chŏn'guri) 6 votes
Kaesŏng: Kodŭng (Pukponjŏng) 17 votes, Koto (Nammun-tong) 37 votes, Songak (Pukponjŏng) 9 votes, Kaesŏng (Pukponjŏng) 7 votes, Nanggung (Taehwajŏng) 7 votes
Wŏnsan: Nodong (Sŏgu-dong) 33 votes, Chungang (Pukch'on-dong) 33 votes, Mihwa (Sang-dong) 7 votes
Taegu: Invalid votes 3 votes
Inch'ŏn: Chosŏn (Saji-machi) 5 votes

O The final deadline for submissions is September 20th, so please cut out the form on page 32 of this issue and vote a lot.
O You may cast new votes for barbershops among the barbershops listed above that have not yet appeared.
O Votes for barbershops outside of Kyŏngsŏng, P'yŏngyang, Kaesŏng, Wŏnsan, Taegu, and Inch'ŏn will be invalid (places not run by Koreans are also invalid).

Prize Regulations

The top 7 barbershops from each of the 6 cities will be selected (42 shops in total), and the winning barbershops will be introduced in the magazine with articles and photos.
The votes from each city will be collected separately, and lots will be drawn for the voters, selecting 1 first-place winner (6 in total), 1 second-place winner (6 in total), and 10 third-place winners (60 in total) from each city.
1st place will receive a high-quality razor (with 2 additional prizes)
2nd place will receive high-quality hair oil and anti-dandruff medicine (with additional prizes)
3rd place will receive a special prize from the company. <163>

상기 6도시 각지방마다 1등으로부터 7등까지 定하고(6도시 合42店) 당선돤 이발소를 기사와 사진으로 誌上에 소개함.
투표자에게는 各都 투표 따로따로 모아서 제비를 뽑아 1등1인씩(6都合6인) 2등1인씩(合
6인) 3등10인씩(合60인)을 定하야 당선자로 하고
1등에는 上等面刀1個進呈(外副賞品2種添呈)
2등에는 上等香油 비듬약進呈(外副賞品添呈)
3등에는 本社特別賞品進呈 <163>
