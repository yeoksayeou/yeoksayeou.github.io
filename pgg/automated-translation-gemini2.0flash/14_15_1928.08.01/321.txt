◇Notice Regarding Remittances

Regarding magazine subscription fees or other remittances to our company, we have reluctantly accepted the use of postal money orders (切手代用) with a 10% increase for convenience. However, there have been frequent instances of people sending stamps, which creates difficulties in processing. Therefore, we ask that you refrain from sending stamps as payment in the future. <141>
