Welcome to Reports from Various City Investigations

As the wind of civilization blows in daily, the so-called urban areas are increasingly plagued by evil winds, and those who wear all sorts of masks and are active in the dark are increasing daily. We have only investigated such dens of evil in the Kyŏngsŏng (京城) area so far, but from now on, we intend to make an effort to investigate even local cities. We hope that those who are interested will provide us with a lot of responsible data. ＜72＞
