기사제목	놀내긴 (Ggakseuk-nolnaegin / A surprising thing)
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Notice · Post-editing note)

Surprised, surprised, surprised.

The front house is surprised, and the back house is also surprised. In the countryside and in Seoul, the old and the young are all surprised, surprised, surprised... The reason why you and I ordered the amazing July/August issue of "Eorini (어린이)" and looked at that amazing book. I'm surprised that the three-color photograph on the cover costs only ten jeon (十錢) and I'm surprised at the forty articles that are new, interesting and fun. And I'm surprised at the pretty and amazing newspaper supplement that is given away for free. How amazing is this issue of "Eorini (어린이)" that makes everyone so surprised? You should quickly send just ten jeon (十錢) to Kaebyoksa (開闢社).
