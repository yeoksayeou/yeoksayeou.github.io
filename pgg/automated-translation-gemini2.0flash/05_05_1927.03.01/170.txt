A Personal Story
						Kŭnhwa Girls' School, Kim Mi-ri

Recently, when looking at graduates of girls' schools, one can see that many tend to have a great deal of self-esteem and vanity. That is, even with a small amount of knowledge, they act as if they are the only ones with learning in this world. They also harbor desires and extravagance beyond their means, and ultimately fail and fall without achieving any success. This is a phenomenon that exists in any country in a period of transition, but we must pay close attention and be aware, first considering what our families and surrounding circumstances are like. It seems good to abandon useless vanity and self-esteem and do whatever we do with more composure and sincerity. <40>

一人一話
						槿花女學校 金美理士
近來 女學校의 卒業生을 보면 대개가 自尊心과 虛榮心이 만흔 경향이 잇다. 卽 조고마한 智識을 어뎌가지고도 이세상에는 자기밧개는 學識이 잇는 사람이 업거니하고 또 분수이상의 慾望과 奢侈心을 가서서 결국 아모 성공도 못하고 失敗, 墮落하는 사람이 만타. 그것은 過渡期에잇서서 어느나라든지 잇는 現狀이지만은 깁히 注意하고 覺悟하야 먼저 우리의 가정과 周圍事情이 엇뎌한 것을 생각하야 無用의 허영, 自尊을 다버리고 좀더 沈着하고 진실하게 무슨일 이든지 하얏스면 조흘 것 갓다.<40>
