Quiz
① What is it that comes even if you try to stop it, and goes even if you try to hold it back?
② What is the cheapest and yet the most distant place on this Earth we live on?
* This time, we've presented very easy questions. The number of entries decreased significantly because last month's issue was seemingly easy yet difficult. So, this time, we've chosen very easy questions that anyone reading this book can solve. We hope and wait for even those who haven't sent in entries before to take the initiative and send them in this time. -(Quiz Department)-
Important Information when Sending in Your Quiz Answer
★ Must arrive at the headquarters by June 28th.
★ Write the answers simply on a postcard-sized paper, and be sure to write your address and name clearly.
★ Be sure to attach the reader certificate on the edge of this page to the answer sheet. It will be invalid if not attached.
★ Write 『京城開闢社別乾坤懸賞係』 (Kyŏngsŏng Kaebyŏksa Pyŏlkŏn'gon Hyŏnsanggye) on the envelope and also write 『原稿在中』 (wŏn'go chaejung) in red ink next to it and seal one side and attach a 2-sen stamp. You will be fined (for insufficient postage) if you do not write 『原稿在中』. <66>

懸賞問題
① 오지말라고 막어도 긔어히오고 가지말라고 암만 붓잡어도 긔어히 가고야 마는 것이 잇슴니다 무엇이겟습니?
② 우리가 사는 이 地球우에서 제일 갓찹고도 먼-곳이 잇습니다 어느곳이겟습니?
* 이번에는 아조쉬운 것을 내엿습니다. 지난달호가 비교적 쉬운듯하고도 어려웟든 관게로 응모수효가 훨신줄엇습니다. 그래서 이번에는 이책을 읽으시는 분은 누구나 풀수 잇슬만치 쉬운 아조 쉬운 문제를 골라서 내인것입니다. 다른 안해보내시든분지 이번에는 솔선하야 보내주실 것을 밋고 기다려보겟습니다. -(懸賞係)-
