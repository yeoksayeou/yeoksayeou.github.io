기사제목: 제5회 「新流行小曲」 大懸常募集!! (The 5th "New Popular Songs" Grand Open Call!!)
기사형태: 사고·편집후기 (Notice and Post-Editorial)

The 5th "New Popular Songs" Grand Open Call!!
The winning works will be immediately composed and recorded in this magazine and on records!!
Prizes are 10 won for each selected piece... 5 won for honorable mentions, deadline June 25th.

The selection of song content is at the discretion of the author, but overly vulgar content should be absolutely avoided. And, as much as possible, avoid difficult characters and profound content, and write in easy words and easy content that anyone can understand.

The song should be written in 345 or 435 rhythm, 44 rhythm, 334 rhythm, or 3443 rhythm, with one verse consisting of 3 to 4 lines and one song consisting of no more than 3 verses (however, 4 verses are acceptable for 44 rhythm).

Submissions for the 5th call must arrive at our office by June 25th. Any submissions received after that date will be considered as submissions for the 6th call and will be kept.

The announcement of the 4th selection will be made in the next month's issue of this magazine, and the winning works will be commissioned to experts for composition and will be specially recorded not only in this magazine but also on popular records for promotion.

There is no limit to the number of winning works; we will select as many good ones as possible. And we will give the following prizes: 10 won (cash) for each selected piece, 5 won (cash) for each honorable mention. Prizes will be sent within 2 weeks after the announcement (all copyrights and recording rights of the winning works are owned by our company).

The selection will be handled by the editorial staff of this company and the authority in this field. When sending, be sure to write "Prize Song Manuscript (懸賞小曲原稿)" on the envelope and send it to the Byeolgeon-gon Editorial Department of Gaebyeoksa (開闢社) in Gyeoungun-dong, Gyeongseong.

Submitted manuscripts will not be returned at all. <25>

제5회 「新流行小曲」 大懸常募集!!
당선작은 즉시 작곡-本誌上과 레코-드에 취입!!
상금은 매편 특선 10원 ... 입선 5원, 6월 25일까지
노래의 내용선택은 작자의 자유입니다만은 너무 야비한 것은 절대로 피합니다. 그리고 되도록 어려운 문자와 심원한 내용은 피하고 누구나 아라드를 쉬운 말과 쉬운 내용으로 쓰십시요.
노래는 345 又는 435調나 44調, 334調, 3443調로 지으시되 한 절은 3행 내지 4행 한편은 3절 이내로 하십시요(단, 44調는 4절까지도 무방).
제5회 모집은 6월 25일가지 본사에 도착된 것으로 합니다. 그리고 그 이후의 것은 제6회 모집할 때에 응모된 것으로 하고 보관하겟습니다.
제4회 발표는 來月號 本誌上에 하고 당선작은 곳 전문가에게 의촉하야 작곡케 한다음 本誌上은 물론 현금 평판 조흔 레코드에 특선 취입하야 선전하겟습니다.
당선작품의 수는 따로 제한치 안코 얼마든지 조흔 것을 뽑겟습니다. 그리고 다음과 가티 상금을 드리겟습니다. 특선 매편 10원(현금), 입선 매편 5원(현금)상금은 발표후 2주일내로 보내 드립니다(당선작품의 판권과 취입권 일절은 본사에서 소유합니다).
考選은 본사 편집국원과 斯界의 권위수氏가 담당하겟습니다. 보내실 때 皮封에는 반드시 「懸賞小曲原稿」라고 쓰시고 京城慶雲洞 開闢社 別乾坤 편집부로 보내십시요.
