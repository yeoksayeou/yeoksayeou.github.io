Three Dramatic Poems (Rhymed)
Kim An-seo

I. One Gold 30 Jeon Also

Living was impossible, so Dŭgnam fled at night,
Even if he lives well in vast Manchuria, it’s a sorrowful thing that he died as a traveler.
A fatherless child, spending 30 jeon (錢) of my money, squandering it all.
If he died on the spot instead of as a traveler, who would know what happened to that fellow?

II. A Certain Friend

Flowers bloom, and days are all joyous.
Not being able to spend money as I please looks awful.
Shall I put a field () up for collateral and play around?
Eating, wearing, living, it’s all according to precedent.

III. Small Night Scene (Of Seoul)

In every alley, Yi Doryeong and Seong Chunhyang maiden,
Their shadows stretch long side by side.
My love, your love, whispering together,
East, West, South, North, their hearts are all different.
-1930.4.28-

劇詩三篇(押韻)
						金岸曙
Ⅰ 一金參拾錢也
살길업서 得男이 夜間逃亡 하드니
넓은滿洲 살진들 客死라니 가엽지。
홀애자식 參拾錢 내돈아주 는걸
客死안야 卽死-ㄴ들 녀석일을 뉘알리。

Ⅱ 엇던 親故
도피고 날조와 모다興겹다
맘대로 돈못쓰니 모양안인것
마지기 잡혀다 놀아볼가
먹고입고 살거야 依例잇는것。

Ⅲ 小夜景(서울의)
골목마다 李道令 成春香아씨
가즈란이 그림자 길게늘이고
내사랑 네사랑은 소군거려도
東西南北 마음은 모다각가지。
-一九三〇‧四‧二八-
