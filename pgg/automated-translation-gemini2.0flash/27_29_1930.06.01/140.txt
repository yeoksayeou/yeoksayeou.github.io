Two Jokes

Fencing

Barber: "Oh dear, I'm so sorry. I've cut your face for the third time."

Customer: "It's alright. It doesn't seem too deeply injured this time... but lend me a razor."

Barber: "What for?"

Customer: "Because you're trying to stab me with your razor, so I have to defend myself."

Intuition

Poet: "Is the editor in?"

Receptionist: "He's not here."

Poet: "Then throw this manuscript in the trash." <84>
