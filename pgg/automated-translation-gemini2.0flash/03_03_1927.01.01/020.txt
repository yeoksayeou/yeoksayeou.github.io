잡지명: 別乾坤 第3號
발행일: 1927년 01월 01일
기사제목: 孔子와 子路 (Confucius and Zilu)
기사형태: 잡저 (Miscellany)

Confucius and Zilu were walking together. They were crossing some Taeryeong (泰嶺) mountain range. Zilu suddenly became thirsty. No matter how much he looked, there was no water. "The Sage knows everything, so I'll ask Confucius," he thought and asked Confucius, "Where is there water?" Confucius replied quickly, "Water is usually in low places and concave places, so go look for a low place or a concave place." "Alright," he said, and looked around for a low place. Indeed, there was a deep valley. He went to find it. Indeed, there was the sound of water gurgling. "That's right, Confucius really knows," he said, and lay down in the stream, drank a bowlful, and stood up. Oh my, a great event right before him! Suddenly, a big tiger squatted down where he was drinking water, wagging its tongue, and pounced with a "Woof!" Zilu was naturally very strong (though he couldn't avoid the tiger that was biting him), so he grabbed it in a flash and started a fight. When the tiger wrestled with Zilu, it became terrified that it would be eaten instead of eating, so it ran away, intending to fight again later. Enraged, Zilu followed the fleeing tiger, grabbed its tail, and slammed it against a rock a few times, so the tiger's tail came right off. The tiger just pooped over a dozen times, threw down its tail, and ran away. Zilu, enraged and furious, waving the tiger's tail, ran toward Confucius, grinding his teeth, saying, "Confucius, this guy tried to kill me by pretending to point out where the water was and told me where the tiger was. I should go and beat him to death right now. Am I the kind of guy who would be killed by a tiger? Let's fight, you rascal!" He came running at Confucius with a tiger's tail in one hand and a rock the size of a gourd in the other, shouting, "You bastard Confucius, what grudge do you have against me that you pretended to point out the water but pointed out the tiger? Do you want to be eaten by a tiger, you old fox? Die!" and was about to bring the rock down on Confucius' head. Confucius, who was standing there absentmindedly, waiting for him to finish drinking and return, was in a completely unexpected, disastrous situation. Confused and at a loss, he quickly said, "Hey, Zilu, I guess I'm going to die at your hands. But let me say one thing before I die. Just wait a moment," he pleaded. Zilu, being so cheerful, said, "Alright, what is it? Hurry up and take this rock!" Then Confucius slowly said,

"The best bravery (上勇) is catching a tiger and bringing it back alive (生摛而來), the medium bravery (中勇) is catching a tiger and bringing back its tail (持尾而來). And furthermore, the best scholar (上士) kills someone with words (以言而殺), and the inferior scholar (下士) kills someone by raising a stone (擧石而殺)."

Hearing these words, Zilu immediately threw the stone away, fell to the ground, and said, "Indeed, you are my teacher!" and he renewed the division between teacher and disciple. Later, he became a superior scholar among the 72 disciples. <9>

기사형태	잡저

孔子와 子路
孔子와 子路가 길을 갓치 가것다. 엇던 泰嶺을 넘어가것다. 子路가 갑작이 목이 마르것다. 아무리 살펴도 물이 업것다. 「聖人은 萬事知라니 어디 孔子에게 무러 보겟다」 하고 孔子에게 「물이 어느 곳에 잇겟느냐」 고 무럿것다. 孔子 얼핏이 대답하되 「물이란 나즉한 곳에 잇고 오묵한 곳에 잇는 법이니 나즉한 곳 오묵한 곳을 차저가 보구려-」 하얏것다. 「그러렷다.」 하고 이리저리 나즉한 곳을 살피것다. 과연 깁숙한 골작이가 잇것다. 차저가겟다. 과연 물소리가 놀놀 나것다. 「올타구나 孔子가 과연 안다」 하고 개쳔에 업듸려 한동의 드리마시고 이러섯것다. 아이고 大事當前! 난데업는 大虎가 물먹든 當前에 쭈구리고 안저 혀를 휘휘 내저으며 「으앙!」 하고 달녀들녀것다. 子路 본래 힘 개나 쓰든 판이라 (물녀고 드는 호랑이를 피할 수도 업지만) 단바람 쓰러 안고 한바탕 결투를 하것다. 호랑이란 놈이 子路와 한바탕 쓰름을 해보닛가 먹기는 고사하고 먹킬 듯한 겁이 대발하야 후일 再鬪할 심치고 다라나것다. 다라나는 호랑이를 분노김에 따라가 꽁지를 잡아 둘너 멧다치닛가 호랑이 꽁지가 쑥 빠젓것다. 호랑이는 그냥 똥 열너 말 싸고 꽁지 빼던지고 다러낫것다. 子路-노발대발 분기충천하야 호랑이 꽁지를 내여 두루며 씩얼거리며 孔子에게로 달녀오면서 「孔子 이 놈이 나를 죽이려고 물 잇는 곳을 가리치는 체하고 호랑이 잇는 데를 말햇구나 여 이놈을 당장 가서 때려 죽여야지. 그래 내가 호랑이에게 죽을 놈이냐. 이놈 격거바라.」 하고 한 손에 범의 꽁지를 들고 한 손에는 함박 가튼 돌뭉이를 들고 달어와서 孔子에게 대들며 「이놈 孔子야 나와 무슨 원수 잇기에 물을 지시하는 척하고 호랑이를 지시하얏늬. 이놈 늂�가 호랑이에게 물더 죽을 상 십흐냐 이놈 죽어바라.」 하고 孔子의 머리에 벼락을 내리려 하것다. 무심코 서서 물먹고  도라오기만 기다리든 孔子 천만의외 대봉변이엿다. 蒼惶罔措라가 얼핏이 「여보 子路 내가 노형의 손에 죽기는 죽게 되얏소. 그러나 말이나 한 마디 하고 죽겟소. 잠간만 참으오」  하고 간쳥하얏것다. 본래 쾌활한 子路인지라 「그래라 무슨 말이냐. 얼핏하고 이 돌뭉이를 바더라.」 하닛가 孔子 천천히 말하되
「上勇은 捕虎하되 生摛而來하고 中勇은 捕虎하되 持尾而來니라. 그리고 또 말하면 上士는 살인하되 以言而殺하고 下士는 살인하되 擧石而殺이니라.」
이 말을 듯든 子路 얼풋이 돌을 던지고 땅바닥에 따러 업드리며 「과연 선생님이올시다.」 하고 師弟의 分을 매젓다 한다. 후에 72제자 중 上士가 되얏다 한다.＜9＞
