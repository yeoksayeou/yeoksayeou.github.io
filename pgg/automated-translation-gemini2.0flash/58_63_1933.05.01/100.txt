잡저 (Miscellaneous Essay)

One year later, around September of Imsin (壬申) year, I thought I was getting close to a complete recovery and that the day I would feel the joy of full recovery was not far off. However, misfortune struck again, and in mid-October of the same year, a minor cold caused my fever to rise to nearly 40 degrees, my appetite decreased, and my cough worsened. Within just 4 or 5 days, I became noticeably emaciated, so my family worried that I might have consumption (p'yebyŏng 肺病), and they immediately called a doctor. After examination, the doctor said that I had contracted wet pleurisy (sŭpsŏng jomak'yŏm 濕性助膜炎) and that it was incurable, which made me very worried that it might already be too late.

I was told to rest absolutely and was even forbidden from reading, but because I originally enjoyed reading, one day about eight days later, I met a struggling student (kosaeng 苦學生) on my way to the toilet. I bought a copy of the *Byŏlgŏn’gŏn* 別乾坤 November issue that the student was selling, and while lying in my room reading it, I saw an article titled "Tuberculosis and Stomach Disease Cured with Cheap Medicine after 10 Years of Suffering." It said that I was cured with "Jōzai Kawamoto" (錠劑カワモト) so I decided to try it and went to the nearest pharmacy to buy it.

Because it was my first time, I took it in conjunction with my medicine for about a week. After that, when I had a medical examination on November 12, my fever was only 37.2 degrees (only in the morning), and my appetite had improved somewhat. Believing that it was definitely the effect of "Jōzai Kawamoto" (錠劑カワモト), I continued to take six tablets each time, and I gradually got better. By the end of November, my temperature stayed below 37 degrees in both the morning and afternoon, my appetite was normal, and I didn't cough at all. After a doctor's diagnosis in late December, he was surprised, saying, "The day you will be completely cured is not far off," and "This is the fastest I've ever seen!"

I am deeply grateful that this is entirely thanks to "Jōzai Kawamoto" (錠劑カワモト). Since then, I have been taking "Jōzai Kawamoto" (錠劑カワモト) continuously and maintaining a good condition without change. Caries (kariesŭ 가리에스) which had developed at the same time as the pleurisy and was close to being completely cured, became severe again, and I felt severe pain when I woke up in the morning. However, after taking "Jōzai Kawamoto" (錠劑カワモト), it improved at the same time as the pleurisy improved. Now, my health has been restored to that of a normal healthy person. The disease that had been tormenting me for about two years was defeated by "Jōzai Kawamoto" (錠劑カワモト), so I will continue to rely on "Jōzai Kawamoto" (錠劑カワモト) as a companion and devote myself to my beliefs.
I express my gratitude by providing the above information. <26>
