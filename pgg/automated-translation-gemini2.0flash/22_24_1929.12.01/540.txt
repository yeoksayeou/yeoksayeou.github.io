별건곤 제24호
1929년 12월 01일
「달」과「센틔멘탈이즘」 (Moon and Sentimentalism, Poem)

My heart…
That never trembled even before guns and swords,
Why does it shake so tonight!
As if meeting an old lover in a dream,
Neither smiling
Nor getting angry,
Just staring without a word at the moon, at the moonlight…

Drawing branches of memories on the bright face of the moon,
This sleepless heart!
Embracing dewdrops asleep on grass leaves, a grasshopper cries,
This heart that cannot cry with it!
Not knowing how far the road is, a firefly flies toward the moon,
This heart that cannot follow!
Ah, looking at the moon, shall I laugh loudly? Shall I sing? Shall I grab my head and stomp!

Weary heart! But go to sleep
Before dawn tomorrow, in this land
Isn't the suffering you will receive great?!
Before this day fully dawns,
Count again the bullets left in your chest! Sharpen the bayonet!
Before dawn tomorrow, in this land
Isn't the suffering you will receive great?!
-1929.9.15- <111>
