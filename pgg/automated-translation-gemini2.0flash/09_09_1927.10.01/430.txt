Article Title: 入選時調, 無題 (Selected Sijo, Untitled)
Author: 開城 朱德鍾
Article Type: 시 (Poetry)

The plane of the knife is the same steel,
Yet the steel scrapes and eats away at the steel,
The steel inside is grass,
Grinding away at the block of wood.

Alas, our lives too,
Are carved and scraped away.

Critique - In this world full of suspicion, hatred, strife, and murder, one cannot help but lament like this. <134>
