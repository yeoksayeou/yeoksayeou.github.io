Postcard Communication Welcome

The "Postcard Communication Column" is going to be interesting. As I have said before, the content is absolutely free: a word, satire, magazine review (誌評), play review (劇評), news, or anything else is acceptable (we only accept postcards).

O Be sure to write "Postcard Communication" on the envelope.

O Must be received by the 10th of each month to be published in the following month. <140>
