기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Notice/Post-Editorial Note)

Request for Return of July Issue

Due to each issue of this magazine being out of print, the July issue is once again out of print, with not a single copy remaining. As a result, we are sending other issues in place of the July issue for local orders. We no longer have any copies even for internal use at the company. Therefore, we will send the November issue of this magazine, or the next issue, as desired, to those who send the July issue to this office by November 10th. <131>

**Named Entities**

7月號	July Issue
