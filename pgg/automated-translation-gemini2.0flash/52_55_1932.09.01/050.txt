Passing by Aegi's Tomb
						Yun P'ung-dae

Wandering aimlessly, pushing through the forest,
Fanning myself with a paper fan and asking where this is,
Stopping on the path, I realized it was Aegi's Tomb.

With no place to rest my heart, I straightened my legs and spoke of the past,
Thinking of my young love, we both wept,
Only the reeds in the sunset quietly burned.
1932, 6, 25, (From a quiet street) ＜12＞

엽선손 죽르며 예어데오 무르시기
가던길 멈처물리니 애기陵이 더니라.

맘둔곳 어이엄노 다리고 옛말할제
어린님 더욱그려 그도나도 울다보니
석양에 풀들만은 고히타고 잇더라.
