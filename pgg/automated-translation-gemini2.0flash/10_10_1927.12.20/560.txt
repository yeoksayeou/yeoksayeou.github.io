발행일	1927년 12월 20일
기사제목	『암만 부즈런한들』 (No matter how diligent)
필자	李象範
기사형태	만화 (Cartoon)

No matter how diligently one works,

李象範

Even when sick (炳), one cannot rest, and with a year of hard work (부즈런), one barely receives a year-end bonus (年末賞與) of 30 won, such a grateful existence (身世). <93>

**Named Entities**

李象範	I Sang-pŏm
