잡지명	別乾坤 第10號
발행일	1927년 12월 20일
기사제목	무슨 餘裕가 잇소, 歲暮에 안저서 새해를 볼 때에 생각되는 일 (What Leisure Do We Have? Thoughts While Sitting at the End of the Year and Looking Towards the New Year)
필자	韓基岳
기사형태	논설 (Essay)

What leisure do we have? Unless there is some leisure in life, what is the point of talking about taxes or anything else? In our situation, what room is there to say anything? Even the New Year's Day, which we can barely manage to celebrate, along with the ideal of observing it according to the solar calendar, is helplessly broken down and abandoned due to the customs of the family. <69>
