From Beijing, Recent Correspondence from Famous Figures Overseas
Shin Chae-ho

To Brother Cha:

I don't remember when or where we met, but I vaguely recall you reciting *sijo* poems and me being shy. Time flies like an arrow, and after 18 years of wandering overseas, my youthful hair is turning gray. I'm curious to know how you are doing. The reason I'm writing is that a Chinese friend here who understands Korean is asking for the history of Cheondogyo (天道敎) and the names of the main branch locations of the Cheondogyo Jongriwon (天道敎宗理院本支部), and the main branch locations of the Cheondogyo Youth Party (天道敎靑年黨本支部). I earnestly request that you write them down and send them to me. Perhaps we may have the chance to meet again. That's all.

Your brother, Shin Chae-ho
November 18

Please let me know if you have any information about Brother Park Ki-dong. Tell him that I have been suffering from an eye disease for several months now. <127>

						申釆浩
車兄惠鑑
우리가 언제? 어대서 맛낫던지 그 處所와 時日은 記憶되지 안코 다만 그때 兄의 時調하던 것과 弟의 羞澁하던 일이 어렴풋할 뿜임니다. 光陰이 살과 같어 弟는 伊來海外流離 18星霜에 當日靑年의 머리텰이 희끗희끗하랴 함니다. 兄은 엇더하신지 알고저 함니다. 살우고자 하는 말삼은 此間에 朝鮮語를 了解하는 一中國親友가 天道敎史와 天道敎宗理院本支部 各所在地名과 天道敎靑年黨本支部所在地名을 알어달나고 함니다. 切托이오니 부대 적어 보내주시압소서. 혹 맛나보일 天緣이 잇슬는지요. 이만.
弟 申釆浩
11월 18일
朴起東兄의 信息이 잇는지요. 弟가 至今 累朔間 眼疾로 고생함을 말삼하야 주시오. <127>
