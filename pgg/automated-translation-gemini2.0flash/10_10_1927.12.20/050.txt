名物 세가지, 午鍾소리, 葉書通信 (Three Famous Things, Noon Bell Sound, Postcard Communication)
淸雲洞 側目生
잡저 (Miscellany)

They say, "There are three unique famous things in Kyŏngsŏng: one is the attire of OO Office employees, another is the Salvation Army uniform, and another is the attire of the employees of the British and American consulates."

I don't know who said that, but what's not mentioned is certain, and it also goes on to say:

"It is pitiable to see the old, bent-over OO Office employees wearing yellow-rimmed hats and yellow Western suits, as poverty dictates their actions. It is detestable that the old attire of the British and American consulate employees comes from the repulsive curiosity of white people, but it is truly something no one can imagine that someone would run away at night, fed up with married life, wear rubber shoes under a long skirt, put epaulettes on the collar of their jacket, and stand there captivating and clinging to the attention of all men. It can only be called reckless." <155-155>

『京城에는 색다른 名物 세가지가 잇스니 하나는 曰 OO職雇員들의 服裝이요 又曰 救世軍服裝이요 又曰 英米國領事舘雇人들의 服裝이라 하시니라』
