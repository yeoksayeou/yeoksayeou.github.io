Summer Resort Night Talk - Whose Fault?
						Lee Seok-hun

Characters
Western Man, Woman, A, B (4 people)
Korean Young Men Gap, Eul, Byeong (3 people)
K Hotel Owner, Police Officer, etc.
The Scene: K Hotel in Jangan Temple, Geumgang Mountain, around 10 PM on a summer night.

K Hotel is two stories high. In the room on the left side of the first floor, three Korean young men, Gap, Eul, and Byeong, are lying down as if asleep. In the room on the right side, a young Western couple is whispering passionately, lost in love. On the left side of the second floor, Westerners A and B are staying, quiet as if asleep.
-In the room on the right side of the first floor-
Western Man (in a sweet tone): For three years, I...
Western Woman (in an even sweeter tone): I too, for three years, I...
Western Man: Have secretly admired you.
Western Woman: I have had a crush on you.
Western Man: A kiss!
Western Woman: Love!
Western Woman: (Silently pouts her lips)
Western Man: (Embracing her tightly) 쪽! 쪽! (Jjok! Jjok!)
Western Woman: (Simultaneously with the man) 쪽! 쪽! (Jjok! Jjok!)
-In the room on the left side-
Korean Young Man Gap (springing up): Hey! What's with the "jjok"?
Korean Young Man Eul: I don't know...
Korean Young Man Byeong (raising his head and looking at Gap): Isn't that the sound of kissing, "jjok"?
Gap: Hahaha, naruhodo. (Then he peeps through a gap in the paper door)
Gap (gesturing): Shh! Quiet, quiet. (Lowering his voice)
Eul (moving closer): Hey, stop watching and let me see!
Gap: Jotto matte...
Byeong (moving closer): Let me see, me...
-At this moment, from the room on the right again-
Western Man, Woman: 쪽! 쪽!... (Jjok! Jjok!...)
Gap (taking his face away from the paper door): Ugh, this is disgusting. (Stepping aside) Anmyeon banghae-n geol!
Eul, Byeong (simultaneously pressing their faces against the paper door and looking): Che! Shakuda! Jerun! Danyeon! Anmyeon banghae-da!
Gap: Hey guys, this is disgusting, let's make some noise. What do you think? Huh?
Eul, Byeong (stepping back): Good! Good! Let's cause a ruckus! Damn it!
Gap (deliberately singing louder): Ae-ra, this is love-
Eul, Byeong (following): Ae-ra, this is a nuisance-
Gap (in an even louder voice): To the sound of the yankobaegi kissing jjok jjok- the young people in the next room can't sleep at night- Aerang aerang aeheya- Eo-ya deo-ya dongguraemi ga wonsuroguna-
-Because of this noise, Westerners A and B, who were sleeping on the second floor, wake up and throw water down to the hallway on the first floor-
Westerner A: God damn! Be quiet!
Westerner B: God damn!
-The water that fell into the hallway splashes on the Korean young men Gap, Eul, and Byeong-
Gap (shouting): Who is it! Who threw the water...
Eul, Byeong: It must be the yankobaegi...
Gap (running out to the hallway and shouting towards the upper floor): God damn! Why are you throwing water! (Shouting)
-There is silence from above-
Gap: Hey, are you going to just stand by after being insulted like this by yankobaegi? Huh?
Eul, Byeong: That's right! Charge!
-Because of this, the Western man and woman in the room on the right side of the first floor let go of each other and their eyes widen-
Gap: Let's go to the second floor...
-Gap, Eul, and Byeong go up to the second floor and bang on the door where Westerners A and B are-
Gap: Open the door!
Eul, Byeong: Hurry! You won't open it!
Westerner A (opening the door and stepping out): Why are you making noise? Huh!
Gap: Huh! What is this, you old monkey! Why did you throw water?
Westerner A: Because you were causing anmyeon banghae.
Gap: You son of a bitch, what did you get a mouth for?
Byeong: You bastard, did you think we were dogs or pigs?
Westerner A: Are you people a nation that doesn't know manners?
Gap: Who doesn't know manners? We suffered anmyeon banghae first...
Eul: The young man and woman from your country in the next room were kissing so loudly that we couldn't sleep!
Westerner A (smiling bitterly): We don't know about that! After 10 PM, it's okay to kiss loudly.
Byeong: Then why did you throw water? Isn't that treating us like we're not people?
Westerner B (popping out): God damn! Go down, go down, we need to sleep!
-Because of this noise, the hotel owner runs up in a panic-
Hotel Owner (with a pale face): Why are you doing this? Please, don't make noise.
Westerner A: These people are complete XX people. If you don't get them out of here, we're leaving.
Gap: What? XX people? You son of a bitch (slaps Westerner A's cheek)
Eul, Byeong: You bastard! XX people? (Protesting)
-At this moment, Westerner B runs down in a fluster-
Westerner A (standing dazed after being slapped): Ugh! XX people! Very bad XX people!
Eul: You bastard! You still think you did well, XX people? (Slaps him again)
Westerner A: Ugh- I'm leaving!... Isn't there a police officer here? Huh! Police officer (goes down)
Hotel Owner: Why, why are you doing this?
-At this moment, a police officer runs up-
Police Officer: Why is there violence! Huh? To foreign guests! God! To the police station... (Leads the three men away)
Gap: Let's go!
Eul, Byeong: I feel refreshed after venting a little. Those guys were so arrogant!
-Gap, Eul, and Byeong go down with a refreshing smile-
Hotel Owner: I lost a guest, but I feel refreshed. How dare those yankos act so arrogant!
(End) <37>
