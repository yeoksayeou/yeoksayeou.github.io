慶北民謠, 사승歌 (Gyeongbuk Folk Song, Hemp-Weaving Song) (시, Poem)

This song is very popular in Andong and Uiseong in Gyeongbuk. "Saseung" means weaving hemp (續麻), and the area is famous for ramie cloth production, so women often sing this song when weaving hemp in the summer.

I
- This verse is sung when weaving hemp at night -

Yeonghae Yeongdeok long hemp stalks - "gari" means tip
Jinbo Cheongsong resinous pine branches - "gwansol" means torch
Our father chops resinous pine
Our uncle lays down resinous pine
I strip hemp
Our brother beats hemp
Weaving hemp all night long
My ten fingers are half-soaked
Andong neighborhood, twelve neighborhoods
Every neighborhood, let's file a lawsuit
Even if we file a lawsuit for no reason
- "Am" means wrong -
You or I will win
Our father is a scribe and head of the household
Our uncle is a magistrate of Dongnae
- This is a boast of one's power - <2>
Concubine courtesan, younger half-sibling
- "Eol" means illegitimate -
Head gisaeng, chief inspector, second cousin
You or I will win

II
- This verse is sung when weaving hemp at dawn -

The moon has already set
Why does the rooster crow again?
My mother-in-law, who talks too much
Wakes me from my sleep again
Genuine Cheongsong long hemp stalks
Yeonghae Yeongdeok resinous pine branches
What's with all the affection, all fluttery
Visiting morning and evening
A woman who weaves hemp at dawn
Only wears a tattered dress

III
Carrying parched rice powder on my back
Crossing the Sanyang Market
- Sanyang is Mungyeong Market -
Look at the hearts of the Sanyang people
They ask for five jeon, five pun
In the short nights of May, June, and July
I can't even sleep soundly
Weaving hemp this way and that way
Both my knees are worn out
The young child cries for milk
The older child cries for rice
Look at the behavior of Kim Dong-ji from the back house
He looks at me and smiles insincerely <3>

慶北民謠, 사승歌
-이 노래는 慶北의 安東, 義城 等地에서 만히 유행하는 노래이다. 사승은 삼삼는다(續麻)는 뜻이니, 그 지방은 자래 麻布名産地로서 여름철에 婦女들이 삼을 삼을 때에 흔이 이 노래를 부른다.

一
-이 節은 밤에 삼삼을 때에 부르는 것-
盈海盈德 긴삼가리-가리는 꼭지라는 뜻
眞寶靑松 관솔가지-관솔은 松明
우리아배 관솔패고
우리올배 관솔노코
이내나는 비비치고
우리형님 나러치고
밤새도록 삼고나니
열손가리 반을축여
安東동내 열두동내
동내마다 송사가자
암만업시 송사가면
-암은 잘못이란 뜻-
네익이나 나익이지
우리아배 吏房戶長
우리올배 東萊府使
-이 말은 자긔의 세력을 자랑하는 말이다-＜2＞
골방妓生 얼동생아
-얼은 庶-
行首別監 얼三寸에
너익이나 나익이지

二
-이 節은 새벽에 삼 삼을 때에 부르는 것-
달은벌서 다젓는대
닭은웨이 또우는다
잔말만흔 싀어머지
이내잠을 또깨우네
眞實靑松 긴삼가지
盈海盈德 관솔가지
널캉날캉 웬情만어
아츰저녁 따러드나
새벽길삼 지기는년
사발옷만 입더란다

三
미수가리를 걸머지고
山陽場을 건너가니
-山陽은 聞慶市場-
山陽놈의 人心봐라
오돈오푼 바드란다
五六七月 짜른밤에
담잠을랑 나못자고
이삼저삼 삼을적에
두무릅이 다석엇다
어린아희 젓달나고
큰아희는 밥달난다
뒷집金동지 거동보소
나를보고 헛우슴치네＜3＞
＜2-3＞
