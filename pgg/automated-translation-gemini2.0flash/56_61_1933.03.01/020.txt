Modern People's State of Mind
1) If you were to be reborn, what kind of person would you be, and what kind of work would you do?
2) What kind of person (male or female) do you like?
3) What has film taught you?
4) How do you imagine Korea ten years from now?
5) If you knew your lifespan in advance, what would you do?
6) If you could express love in one word, what would it be?
7) Which books influenced your youth?
8) What is the most missed among the things that have been lost? Korean Style - Korean Sentiment
9) What is the first thing to consider and preserve in Korea?
10) What would be the most inconvenient if it disappeared?

Novelist... Yi Tae-jun

1) I'm not old yet, so even now, I can become the person I want to be and pursue the work I want to do.
2) A person who doesn't use tactics in everything.
3) I still have more to give than I have received, and that is time.
4) If I knew this, I wouldn't be sitting here holding a pen.
5) I will prepare flowers at my bedside.

10) The Dongsomun (東小門) bus that disappeared.

Newspaper Reporter... Shin Kyong-seop

1) I want to become a strongman (滄海力士) and smash Qin Shi Huang's carriage with an iron club. Right in the middle...
2) Regardless of gender, I like people with humor and flexibility. I don't enjoy people who are excessively rigid. Even as a woman, I don't welcome prudes.
3) One year, I saw "Lullaby" (子守唄) in a cheap popular movie at the Yumei Theater (優美館), and I was deeply moved by the heightened maternal love. It must be because I've been starved of maternal love since I was young...
4) I saw Korea ten years ago, and I'm seeing it today, so who wouldn't be able to guess what it will be like ten years from now? But what's the point of making long speeches? ... ...
5) What else can I do?
6) It's a natural process of life.
7) The biography of General Hannibal powerfully stirred and inspired my heart when I was a student.
8) The sight of a girl carrying a water jug, wearing a scarlet jacket and navy skirt with a white apron tied around it, under the peach blossoms (碧桃樹) on the mountainside in spring, allows me to fully enjoy the Korean style and Korean sentiment (朝鮮의 멋 朝鮮의 정조). It's a deluxe version of sentiment.
9) The public health of the people would be the first.
10) If a prohibition were to arise in Korea and "bars" were to close, I think it would be the most difficult thing to bear.

Poet... Pak Pal-yang

1) I want to be born as a genius artist and create beautiful works of art, or otherwise, as a man (or woman) with the ability and sincerity to make life happier through some kind of business.
2) A person who is truthful, sincere, healthy, diligent, cheerful, and optimistic in everything, regardless of gender. However, one more condition is added for women: a person who possesses the above conditions and is also beautiful in appearance. If so, it would be the icing on the cake (금상첨화).
3) Movies have become like an essential item for modern city dwellers. Only while watching a movie can we wander in a dream world separate from the real world. But what has it given us? I can only tilt my head and think, "Well..." Of course, new movies in the future will bring us many gains. However, the kind of unrealistic, low-grade taste movies we've seen in the past haven't given us anything. When will the kind of movies we demand unfold before our eyes? We can only wait for that day.
4) It's a very difficult topic. It's hard to say.
5) It's impossible to know in advance, and there's no need to know in advance. We just live until we die, and then we die when the day comes. "Something worth doing once or twice, but not worth doing from the third time onward."
7) Also, literary books.
8) There's not much.
9) Only a "People's Enlightenment Movement (民衆敎養運動)."
10) In the spiritual aspect, it's belief. In the material aspect, it's money. (However, this is true in today's society.)

2) People who only know how to laugh or people who only know how to cry.

Newspaper Reporter... Hong Jong-in

1) A politician (政治家), but it wouldn't be appropriate to call them a common politician, but a man with a more passionate aspect of "Byron (바이론)".
2) A person who is passionate and honest, indifferent to money and women, gentle yet strong. Especially a woman who is not vain.
3) The influence that film has given is particularly small.
4) Korea ten years from now? I don't think we can talk about Korea separately. I am sure that it will move exactly in line with the movements of Japan, China, Manchuria, etc. in the Far East (極東), but I cannot say anything more than that.
5) There is no reason to be more pessimistic because you know the day your life will end, and there is no reason to be more optimistic because you imagine a vague future without knowing the day your life will end. Therefore, knowing or not knowing the exact time of your lifespan will have no influence on your current life. There may be some psychological impact, but if it is certain that I cannot live more than ten years, I think I will have the mind to accomplish the work I had planned for twenty years within that time. I think life should be viewed like a task.
6) Goethe's love between "Werther" and "Lotte" is a brilliant record and flower of life.
7) Natsume Sōseki's "Botchan (坊ちやん)", then "Tagore (타고어)". Then, the materialist dialectic.
8) Korean sentiment, the most missed thing? There are many sentimental and picturesque scenes mixed up in my mind like spring haze, but... I will never forget the sad folk song (民謠) of the farmers I heard in Ryonggang (龍岡) and Kangso (江西), Pyongan Province (平安道).
9) The first thing to think about is where are Koreans going? How are they becoming? If we pay attention to where the majority (多大數) is going from what standard point, we will be able to think about what we should do in each direction.

1) 정치가(政治家) 그러나 시속 정치가라는 말로서는 당치 안을 것 갓습니다만은 좀더 정열로 뭉친 「바이론」의 일면을 가진 남아(男兒).
2) 열이 잇고 순직한 사람 돈과 게집에 담박한 사람 온후하면서도 강한 사람. 특히 녀자는 허영이 업는 사람.
3) 특히 영화가 끼처 주엇다 할 영향은 적습니다.
4) 십년후의 朝鮮? 조선만을 따로히 말 할 수 업슬줄 압니다. 일본, 중국, 만주 등등 극동(極東)<12>의 움즉임에 딸아 꼭가티 움즉일 것만은 확실성이 만타고 보나 그외는 더 말 할 수 업습니다.
5) 생명이 끈어지는 날을 미리 알엇다고 더 비관하고 생명이 끗날 날을 몰으고 막연한 미래를 상상하면서 산다해서 좀 더 락관적일리는 만무합니다. 딸아서 수명이 진할 시일을 미리 알고 몰은다는 것은 현재생활에 조금도 영향할 바 업슬 것 갓습니다. 다소 심리적 영향이 잇슬는지 몰으나 만일 십년이상 더 살 수 업다는 것이 확정적이라면 이십년 예정하엿든 일을 좀더 빨리 其內에 성취하여야 되겟다는 마음을 가지게 될 뜻 합니다. 일생도 사무와 가티 볼 바가 크다고 생각함으로.
6) 꾀-테의 「베르테르」와 「롯테」의 사랑은 인생의 찬란한 긔록이요 꼿입니다.
7) 夏目瀨石의 「坊ちやん」, 그 다음은 「타고어」. 다음은 유물변증법론으로.
8) 조선의 정조 가장 그리운 것? 봄 아즈랑이 가티 눈 속에서는 센틔멘탈한 회화적 장면이 만히 뒤박긔고 잇스나... 平安道 龍岡, 江西 방면에 듯든 농민의 애끗는 기나리(民謠)는 닛기지 안는 한 긔억이 되어 잇습니다.
9) 몬저 생각해야 할 것은 조선 사람은 어데로 가고 잇는가? 엇떠케 되여가고 잇는가? 多大數라는 표준점에 어데서부터 어데로 가고 잇는가를 주목하면 우리는 무엇을 하여야 할 것인가를 각자 방면을 딸아 생각할 수 잇슬 것입니다,
