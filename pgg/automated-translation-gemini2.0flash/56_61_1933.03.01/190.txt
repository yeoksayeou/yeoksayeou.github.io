胃腸病과 神經衰弱 (Stomach and Intestinal Diseases and Neurasthenia)
大杉司朗
잡저 (Miscellaneous Essay)

At first, I worked diligently, but after two or three months, for some reason, even though I was young, my head felt heavy, and I had wandering thoughts while working every day. Among other things, I had no appetite and dreamed every night, so I told the owner, "Maybe it's just a cold; it'll get better if I work..." and they didn't tell me to rest. I couldn't help but work for about twenty days while feeling miserable and bitter, and eventually, I got a headache and had to lie down.

While spending time in such despair and unpleasantness, one day, I saw an article about "Jōzai Wakamoto (錠劑わかもと)" in a magazine. I thought it was just another advertisement for medicine, but I felt like this medicine would cure my illness, so I bought it without telling my parents.

So, after taking the first dose of "Jōzai Wakamoto (錠劑わかもと)" (four tablets), I slept well that night, and I was surprised at how effective it was. After diligently continuing to take it, on the tenth day, my painful stomach ailment was cured, and my headaches and dizziness disappeared. I naturally wanted to work, so I told my family, and my grandmother was so happy that she even shed tears.

Commentary

In general, when we get sick or weakened, it is caused by a deficiency of nutrients (榮養素) necessary for the body's composition and the decline of enzyme action (酵素作用). Therefore, if we sufficiently supply these, we can naturally recover from illness or weakness. However, until recently, this was very difficult, so the number of people who get sick does not easily decrease.

However, the recently discovered "Jōzai Wakamoto (錠劑わかもと)" was painstakingly researched by Dr. Sawamura Makoto (澤村眞), Professor Emeritus of Tokyo Imperial University, to make "Hefe (ヘー훼)" bacteria into an internal medicine. It is said to have been appropriately made to replenish all the "enzymes (酵素)" and "nutrients (榮養素)" without any harm and aim to restore health.

Then, the question is what role do these enzymes play in the body? There are very complex actions, so it is difficult to explain them simply, but in summary, they first give vitality to the tissue cells of the stomach, which mainly digest and absorb all nutrients, as well as the cells that make up the entire body, restore various abnormalities, improve their functions, or kill harmful bacteria that have invaded the body and increase white blood cells. As a result, the metabolism is also carried out perfectly, resistance is increased, and the body's healing ability can be fully demonstrated. In addition, "Jōzai Wakamoto (錠劑わかもと)" contains "carbohydrates (함수탄소)," "fats (지방)," "proteins (담백)," various "vitamins (비타민)," and "nutrients (영유)," which are essential nutrients for body health, in an easily absorbable form, which helps each other and leads to treatment.

As such, "Jōzai Wakamoto (錠劑わかもと)" is a biological preparation that is clearly different from conventional symptomatic or chemical products.

Range of Applicable Diseases

The range of uses is very wide, and it is said to have unique effects on various diseases such as all types of "tuberculosis (결핵성)," as well as "stomach ailments (위장의 환질)," "pleurisy (늑막염)," "neurasthenia (신경쇠약)," "beriberi (각긔)," and "scrofula (선병질)" in pregnant women and infants. <33>

講評

대체 우리들이 병을 알는다든가 쇠약해진다는 것은 신체조성에 필요한 영양소(榮養素)의 결함과 효소작용(酵素作用)의 쇠퇴로부터 일어나는 것입니다. 그럼으로 이것들의 보급만을 충분케하면 자연 병긔나 쇠약도 회복식힐수 잇는 까닭이지만 어제에는 이것이 퍽 어려움으로 해서 병에 걸니는 사람이 졸연이 끈처지 안는 것입니다.
그런대 최근에 발견된 「錠劑와까모도」는 『헤-훼』균을 내복약으로 맨들기 위해서 동경제대 명예교수 澤村眞박사가 고심연구한 것으로 그 성분이 조금도 해가 업게 전긔 『효소』와 『영양소』를 보급해서 건강의 회복을 목적해서 적당하게 만든 것이라고도 하겟습니다.
그러면 이 효소는 신체에서 어떠한 역할을 하느냐는 것이 문제입니다. 대단복잡한 작용이 잇서 간단하게 말슴을 다하기는 어려우나 일괄해 말한다면 먼저 일체의 영양의 소화흡수를 주로 하는 위장의 조직세포를 비롯해서 신체전체를 조직한 세포에 활녁을 주어 여러 가지 이상을 회복식히고 그 긔능을 올니며 혹은 체내에 침입한 유해의 균을 죽이는 작용을 하여 백혈구를 늘켜줍니다. 그 결과 신진대사작용도 유감업시 되어 저항력이 증대해저서 신체의 치료능력도 충분히 발휘될 수 잇스며 그외에 「錠劑와까모도」는 『함수탄소』, 『지방』, 『담백』 각종 『비타민』 『영유』등 신체보건상 필수의 영양소를 흡수하기 용이한 형태로 되여잇서 서로서로간 도음이 되여 치료케 되는 것입니다.
이와가티 「錠劑와까모도」의 재래의 대증적이나 화학적 제품과는 판연이 달은 생물제재입니다.
