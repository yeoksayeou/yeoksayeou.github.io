Let's Go Get Mountain Greens, New Folk Song
Yi Sŏ-gu

Hey yara dwiyeora
Let's go pick mountain greens
Pink skirt, Chinese angelica
The eldest daughter is collecting
Flower basket, small knife worn
Let's cross the mountain, toddling.

Hey yara, let's go get mountain greens
Let's search through this valley and that valley
A virgin, is angelica
Only flushed with flower-colored rain?
A swarm of butterflies
Flies up.

Hey yara, dwiyeora
Let's go pick mountain greens
Whether we pick greens or not
Let's go to the mountain for spring play
Flower basket, small knife worn
Let's cross the mountain, toddling. <2>

Hey yara, dwiyeora
Let's search through this valley and that valley
Elecampane or wheat stalks
Put them all aside
Pick cherry blossoms
To take in our sleeves

Hey yara, dwiyeora
Let's go pick mountain greens
Even on the yuzu tree branches
A fire-like color is full
Wouldn't it be in our baby's heart
Restless, anxious?

Hey yara, let's go get mountain greens
Let's search through this valley and that valley
A spring maiden's heart
Is it the moonlit color of drunkness?
Even seeing twin butterflies
She gets drunk.
