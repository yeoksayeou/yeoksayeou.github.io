Street Women Scenery
						Chang Tŏk-cho

One for two?
The pavements of Chongno! There is no place more suitable for cheerful and lively women to secure than there. Judging by the fact that they are already wearing what is called the '33-year style' dresses and carrying one hundred thousand things made of imitation leather, they seem to be walking at the same "tempo" as the latest trend.

"Hey, that looks good!" The place where they exclaim in admiration is not the department store "show window," where handbags convey the new trend, or the front of a confectionery store, where gold and silver chocolate coins are piled up like mountains, but the entrance of a Chinese restaurant that boasts 100% of nourishing deliciousness (滋養美味), which seems quite reasonable.
"Hey, I'm getting fat."
"Just adjust it again, is that all you're worried about?"
"In this recession, weight loss pills will go up in price."
"In that case, let's endure it with laxatives."
"That's not right; you're always acting like an economist day and night."
"Anyway, let's go in."

If they are "mademoiselles" with no shortage of money to spend, they might be carefree even with "biftek" costing 2 or 3 won a piece, but among the many, there are also people whose wallets are not as fragrant as their outfits suggest.
Even in such a bitter (?) situation, they never lose their oath-like courage.
"One dumpling, two cocoas."
Occupying a table in the store with a confident expression, they first hang their scarves on the backs of their chairs and slowly issue their order.
"Hey! Is it one for two?"
"Yes, one and two."
"Yes!"
"Don't get it wrong, not two for one, but one for two."

Soon, the ordered items, steaming white steam, appear with soy sauce.
"One more of these."
"Yes? Do you mean a dumpling?"
"No, this," and what is held out towards the flat nose of the server is not a wooden chopstick covered in a thin paper skin?
"Ah, chopsticks?" Even if they were inwardly dismayed, the restaurant owner, whose top priority is "service," cannot show even a trace of such a feeling.
"Here you are." The server obsequiously hands over the chopsticks, which they arrogantly accept.
"Hey, eat before it gets cold."
"Merci (Thank you) very much."
In this way, the dissected dumpling is passed into the mouths of the two young women, half at a time.
And this kind of sound will eventually be added, but I hope that even the mobos (modern girls), who only calculate their pocket budget and freeze their red noses in the blizzard, can quickly revert to this much cleverness.

The Beauty Has Disappeared
"Oh, I'm sorry. The wind was blowing directly in my face, so I was backing up and accidentally bumped into you! Please forgive me."
Shoes of the same tone as "nylon," which has trimmed the demand for rayon to a minimum, faintly emit the fragrance of "snakeskin" "medium heels"—(It would be impressive to include one or two names of movie actors around here, but I am unfortunately a country bumpkin. My knowledge in that field is so shallow that I once stubbornly insisted that the famous "Dietrich" was a man, and I was miserably corrected by a friend who is a movie buff, so I respectfully decline.)
"Oh, not at all! Haha, people don't have eyes in the back of their heads."
"Still—why is the wind blowing so fiercely?"
"Are you going this way?"
"Yes."

They started walking side by side.
"I still like winter streets a lot. I don't know why, but I don't like it when people are too crowded. That's why I deliberately come out to wander around like this." <20>
"Yes, me too. I was coming out to find a 'present' because it's a friend's wedding tomorrow, but I can't find anything suitable."
"Oh my! You came out to buy something. For a friend—"
His eyes gleamed. He has three eyes.
One is shining with a deep, crimson focus at the tip of his left ring finger. The third eye, guarding his most precious possession—
If the saying "he dominates that person with his eyes" is true, it would fit that jewel perfectly.
"Let's go over there. How can they be shouting so hard in this cold?"
"Come on, come on. We're selling fountain pens. A fountain pen that was left over after a fire in the warehouse. Pick and choose any you want at 20 jeon a piece. Come on, fountain pen dumping! Fountain pens left over from the fire—"
"Huh? My wallet, where did my wallet go? Oh, and my watch too. Oh dear, the beauty, the beauty has disappeared—"
"Dumping, dumping... Excuse me? The person you were with went that way a little while ago."
The merchant, intoxicated by the senseless sophistry, and the gentleman's clear eyes! Find the beauty thief, the three-eyed one, based on the roadside merchant's argument (露店商人) !

NO! TWICE
Summer night streets—going through the crowd with sweat dripping from your forehead is too close to utilitarian health cultivation (功利的保健修養). Moreover, the streets in autumn and spring would only be groping for a dull "fashion show."
Considering this, it seems that the real taste of the night street is ultimately in this winter street.
"Only 5 minutes left." The impatience of the "rendezvous" (데-쁘) waiting for approaching footsteps in the dark alley on a snowy night! The pleasure of feeling your heart pounding as your whole body is nestled warmly in your lover's large coat! And... but this kind of thing is not something that a country bumpkin can dare to depict (while slyly being humble).

However, it seems that the women who are striding in the extremely dynamic street of 1932 are bringing abnormalities to this rendezvous front (密會戰線) and that going out for a walk, changing partners on the long winter nights, satisfies their curiosity instead.
He, even if he is a "salaryman" (쎌러리. 맨), certainly does not live on that alone! But in reality, he may be just a student wearing a cap (角帽). (These days, students want to wear what is called a "sebiro" suit anyway.) His grandly waving buttocks are flying in the wind, and yet he is being dragged along.
Then, if they meet a close friend on the street, they bump shoulders with each other and
"Which one are you?"
"Confess first, only one?"
"No- twice!"
"Great, me too, twice!"
"Take good care of them. I'll win no matter what..."
"Well, I won't be only two forever either."
"We'll see when we settle the accounts."
Thus, to be honest, they are being treated like pieces in a marching chess game (行軍將棋).
Mobos (modern girls) of the world! Put on your coats, wear your gloves, and if you have even a few pennies on top of that, don't wander around in that "Quo Vadis" and come out to the street without saying a word.
If possible, to the honor of their third "horse"! <21>
