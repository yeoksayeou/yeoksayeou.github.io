Weak One, Your Name is Man,
A Spineless Fellow is a Man
Cold-blooded Man

There is nothing as amusing as a man. There are also spineless fellows.
It was once said that “the weak are women,” but in reality, the weak are men, and furthermore, spineless fellows are male.
Knowing full well that the woman is engaged in secret prostitution (密賣淫), men give her nicknames like “flour” or “silver gentleman (銀君子),” and cannot help but seek her out.
And a fellow calling himself a “gentleman” slyly follows women’s behinds in the evening.
Of course, it is not only men who do such things. In other words, women are stronger than men in some ways. Women may be inferior to men in terms of maternal love (母性愛), but in reality, women are more intense in their sexual desire (性慾) or in pursuing something they want to do.
Once a woman decides to dump a man, that’s it. She cuts him off immediately, as if snapping a string. If you don’t know this and try to find her, you’ll be humiliated.
Eventually, you will always see a guy who claims to have seduced and abandoned hundreds of women end up groveling before a woman.
Think about it. Where does all the increasing "luxury" come from? It would be a big mistake to think that women are earning that much to indulge in "luxury." It's money that comes from their husbands, if they have them, or from their lovers, if they have them.
Moreover, lately, with the spread of "Red Love" by Kolontai from Russia in Korea, women divorce as easily as drinking water or eating rice. <72>
Despite this, men persistently cling. In severe cases, one guy puts a woman in the inner room, another puts her in the room under the yard, and they blunder and flatter around, eventually melting their hearts and revealing everything.
And is that all? Even knowing that the woman is married to a friend, he stealthily follows her, engaging in “conquest” or begging, which leads to one of them getting a beating.
If they lived well together after that, it would be good, but that doesn’t happen either. After a while, the guy who claimed to have “conquered” her is actually "conquered (被戰取)" and talks about dying, the Hangang Bridge, or rat poison (猫いらず). But he doesn’t die. It’s all an empty show.
Soon, this guy “conquers” another woman and goes around with her. And he is "extremely elated (氣高萬丈)," saying this and that.
These are the scenes of social interaction (社交相) between young men and women these days that I hear and see repeatedly. Some friends might get angry, saying that “social interaction” is too much to say, but what can I say when that’s the reality? Also, it would be good if exposing such things could purify social interactions even a little.
However, nowadays, you can find many young men and women of the 1930s doing this in cafes (가페), inns (旅館), parks (公園), and streets (街頭).
They seem like they are desperately trying not to miss a chance and getting burned and blundering around. In this regard, women have already regarded the concept of chastity (貞操觀念) as something from the old century (世紀).
The day before I wrote this, after midnight, a "student-like woman" and a gentleman were caught at the Pagoda Park police box, dragging their noses on the ground and saying "apology, apology (사죄, 사죄)." Only the man was allowed to go first, and a innkeeper came and a accommodation register (宿泊名簿) came for what would happen to the woman, and after scolding, scolding “Since it is first time, we are sending you back!” After receiving a generous disposition (?), she runs away, lamenting that she cannot put her face, which turned ashen, into her chest.
If men didn’t seduce women, why would such things happen, and if women didn’t seduce men, why would they? In the end, it is a mutual interaction (交互作用), but only men have a strange psychology – a spineless psychology. That is, they want their wife or the woman they associate with to maintain chastity (貞操) until the end, but they want to mess around with other men's wives or other women. What kind of contradiction (矛盾) is this, and what kind of spineless behavior is this?
And if they don't like someone, they criticize and insult them, and eventually slander them. Seeing this, men have little truth. They have little face. Therefore, they are much weaker than women.

Americanism Poisoning
Chŏng In-yŏk

Modern Korea seems to be in conflict with two major principles. One is what is called "Americanism (아메리카니즘)" <73> and the other is "Russianism (러시아니즘)..." Moreover, in relationships between the sexes, these two principles are indeed prevalent and frightening. It is possible that this is just a hallucination of the author, but even within that, "Americanism (아메리카니즘)"-like relationships between the sexes seem to be growing more dominant than "Russianism (러시아니즘)." The young people who are called the New People of modern Korea... among them, this "Americanism (아메리카니즘)"-like relationship between the sexes seems to be regarded as a very ordinary and natural conclusion, as if it has come with historical inevitability. The "Americanism (아메리카니즘)" of relationships between the sexes that I am talking about here is, to put it easily, a tendency toward female dominance and male subordination. After Korea had been free from the feudal shackles of male dominance and female subordination (男尊女卑) for so long, it is not unnatural that the extreme conclusion of female dominance and male subordination came as a backlash against this, but I don't know if it is because I am a man, but I can't help but have concerns that if the "Americanism (아메는카이즘)" of relationships between the sexes grows even more, it will eventually bring about the same evils as in the old days of male dominance and female subordination. A few days ago, I heard this story in a hobby lecture by a Japanese detective novelist.
During the last European War, the troops dispatched from the United States were all temporary volunteers, but the recruitment results were very poor. Well, that's natural, since war is a place of slaughter, and who would want to jump into death voluntarily? Unless the state forces them to do so, it is more than natural that the results of voluntary dispatch are not good. However, it was said that the poor recruitment results were a very serious problem, so the government incited all the alluring women in the United States. They were told to encourage young men to bravely apply for dispatch for the sake of the nation, for the sake of world peace, and for the sake of the reputation of the United States. In response, young women became government stooges, wrinkling their brows and opening their scarlet lips to encourage young men. They even threatened that if they did not apply for dispatch, they would be forever banished from the soft embrace of women. As things turned out this way, big things happened. The men who had not enjoyed being dispatched competed to volunteer bravely for the sake of the beautiful young women of America. Only for women... neither the nation nor humanity nor peace mattered. It was as if they had a natural duty to absolutely obey women, and as if women had absolute power over men...
Now, this kind of "Americanism (아메리카니즘)" has begun to shake up the relationships between the sexes in Korea. Should we pity the men of the Korean era who have come to resemble the men of "America (아메리카)" who complain about the tyranny of women and cry out for liberation from the oppression of women! If shouting for female liberation and demanding the expansion of women's rights means persecuting men and putting them in shackles, then that is a big problem. It may be good for women to be liberated and gain power, but what is there to welcome for men who are caught again? To put it easily, the "Americanism (아메리카니즘)"-like tendency of relationships between the sexes cannot but be a shift from the old evils of persecuting women to new evils. What's funny is that this tendency is noticeable among the intellectuals and new people who are equipped with contemporary knowledge, so should we say, "Weak one, your name is intellectual man of Korea!" This is certainly a new trend that has arisen from their contemporary learning and insight. The story written below is a sufficient example to explain the tendency of female dominance and male subordination. The protagonists are all people I know personally. I am sorry to expose them even anonymously, but in this case, I believe that giving this example is enough to prove that this article is not empty talk, however clumsy it may be.
P gets up at dawn when it is bright and cleans. He fetches water, chops firewood, cooks rice, and even wipes the floors. During this time, P's wife lies in bed reading several pages of reference books for today's teaching material. Next is makeup - when the makeup is finished, P carries in the breakfast table. The wife takes out a few Japanese-style dried side dishes from the closet and puts them on the table. When he throws away the wife's used wash water and comes in, the wife has already picked up her spoon and is sitting and kindly offering him food. The couple finishes breakfast as harmoniously as pigeons and goes to work, with P in the lead, without washing the dishes. It is rare that the wife comes home first in the evening. P also comes home first, does the dishes, and cooks rice. Doing things like this is always the same, whether it's cold or hot. But P never finds it bothersome. The wife never feels sorry. They are like something that is taken for granted...
Of course, I and my friends mock P when we see him. P always uses love as a shield when that happens. I retort by asking, "Then is it only you who has love?" P says that his wife has it too. If his wife has it, then why does P do all the labor? P says that it is because his wife is a weak woman. Because his wife is a weak woman, she has become strong, and because he is a strong man, P has become weak. Everyone, this is a fact that eloquently speaks about the current relationships between the sexes in the new power that is Korea. Because they have knowledge and are strong, men are becoming weak. And they use love as a shield for their excuses...

Men are Weak
Kim Mal-bong

If you are going to say "men are weak," you will be refuting Shakespeare's words, "Weak one, your name is woman," although it is commonplace. The reason is that it is written as a counterpoint to the words, "Strong one, your name is man." However, look at those who have graduated from vocational schools or universities after wasting their intelligence, health, and vast sums of money, and even wasting ten or twenty years of time. What does it mean that their resumes, which are several sheets of fancy paper neatly filled out, end up in the dusty corners of drawers in company factories, banks, and schools, or even in the trash? Look at the crowds of young workers who have no place to use their well-developed limbs and concentrated energy and are turning yellow in the narrow alleys of the big city. Look at the tailor who trudges along empty-handed with his shoulders drooping low again today. Every time the faces of the innkeeper and the image of his sighing wife pass through his head, he can only shudder and close his eyes. Man! Strong man! Sturdy man! But this can only be seen under certain circumstances and conditions. The murderous depression caused by the contradictions of modern social organization has drained the energy from all men! It has dried up the blood vessels of all young people! Ah, weak one, your name is Korean man! <75>

쓸개빠진 놈은 남자다
冷血洞人

남자가티 우수운 것은 업다. 또한 쓸개 빠진 놈들은 업다.
일즉이 ⾀Œ약한 자는 녀자다」라고 하얏스나 실상인즉은 약한 자는 남자이고 더 나가서 쓸개 빠진 놈은 사나희 놈이라는 것이다.
번연히 그 녀자가 밀매음(密賣淫)을 하고 잇는 줄 알면서도 저희들끼리 「밀가루」이니 「은군자」이니 하는 층호를 주면서 그 녀자를 차저가지 아니하면 못 백이는 것이 남자이다.
그러고 제왈 「신사」라고 하는 놈이 저녁이면 녀자의 궁둥이를 슬슬 따라스는 것이다.
물론 순전히 남자만이 그런다는 것은 아니다. 말하자면 녀자는 돌이어 어느 편으로 보든지 남자보다 강하다. 녀자는 다소 모성애(母性愛)에 잇서서는 남자보다 못할는지 몰으나 실상 「성욕」(性慾)이라든지 또는 한 번 하고자 한 일은 녀자가 일층 더 하다고 할 수 잇다.
녀자는 한 번 어떠한 남자를 차버리고자 할 진댄 그만이다. 대번에 맷고 끈흔 듯이 차버린다. 섯불리 그런 줄은 몰으고 그 녀자를 차저 단이다가는 욕을 보는 것이다.
결국 어느 때든지 보면 제왈 녀자는 멧백명이든지 홀여내고 버려쥰다고 한 놈이라로 녀자 압헤 가서 굽히지 안는 놈이 별노 업다.
가만히 생각하여 보아라. 근자에 늘어가는「사치」가 죄다 누구에 돈인가 녀자가 그러케 버러서 「사치」를 하는 줄 알엇다 가는 큰 탈이다. 그것은 남편이 잇스면 남편의 손을 것처서 애인이 잇스면 애인의 손을 것처서 나온 돈이다.
더욱이 요 근자에는 「러시아」에 『코론타이』의 「붉은 사랑」이라는 것이 조선에도 쫙 퍼치자 녀자들은 離婚을 물 마시듯 밥먹듯 한다.＜72＞
이럼에도 불구하고 끈준끈쥰 덤비는 것이 남자이다. 심한 것은 가튼 남자를 한 녀석은 안방에 안처 노코 한 녀석은 뜰 아레방에 안처 노코 이러구 저러구 엉벙뗌하고 얼너 마치는 바람에 그냥 마음이 녹아서  모엇이든지 다 터러 내노코 나오고 한다.
그나 그 뿐인가. 그 녀자가 번연히 어느 친구하고 결혼한 것까지 알면서 슬슬 따라 단이며 『戰取』니 비러 먹을 것이니 하는 것을 하야가지고 한 놈을 고랑땡을 안긴다.
그러케 되엇스면 둘이서 잘 살엇스면 조켓는데 또 그도 그러치 못하야 얼마 후에 보면 『戰取』하얏다는 놈이 돌이어 『被戰取』을 당하야 죽느니 사는니 한강철교이니 『猫 いらず』 이니 하고 떠든다. 그러면 그 자가 또 죽는 것도 아니다. 그것은 벌서 헛풍이다.
얼마 후 이 자는 또 엇던 녀자를 『戰取』하야 데리고 단인다. 그리고 『氣高萬丈』하야 어떠니 어떠니 하고 잇다.
이것들이 내가 듯고 보고 밤낫 되푸리 하는 요새 청춘남녀의 사교상(社交相)이다. 사교상이라면 너무 과한 말이라고 노할 친구도 잇슬는지 몰으나 현실이 그런데야 무엇이라 말하랴. 또한 이러한 것을 폭로(暴露)시키어 저윽이 사교가 쟁화(淨化)한다면 고마운 일일 것이다.
그러나 요사히 『가페』와 『旅館』과 『公園』과 『街頭』 어느 곳을 가든지 이러한 일천구백 삼십년대의 청춘남녀가 만흔 것이다.
밤낫을 치고 잇지 못해 애쓰는 놋칠까바 애쓰는 놈 놋치고 설어서 덤벙거리는 놈 이것들 가티 보인다. 이러한 뎜에 잇서서 녀자들은 벌서 정조 관념(貞操觀念)이란 것은묵은 세긔(世紀)의 것으로 치고 말게 되엇다.
이 글을 쓰든 전날도 자정이나 넘어서 「파고다」공원압 파출소에는 『녀학생비슷』과 신사 한 분이 잡히어 코를 땅에 끌코 『사죄, 사죄』하야 남자만은 먼저 가고 녀자가 어찌 되는지 여관 주인이 오고 숙박 명부(宿泊名簿)가 오고 하드니 꾸지람 꾸지람 『처음 갓튼 일이니 돌여보낸다!』 관후한 처분 (?)을 밧고 얼굴이 붉다 못 하야 재가치 된 것을 가슴속에다 집어 너치 못하는 것을 한하며 달아난다.
남자가 녀자를 유혹치 아니하든들 녀자가 웨 그런 일이 잇스며 녀자가 남자를 유혹치 아니하든들 웨 그러랴. 결국 이것은 피차의 교호작용(交互作用)이겟스나 오즉 남자에 한하야는 이상한 심리-쓸개빠진 심리-가 잇다. 그것은 자긔의 안해나 자긔와 상관 상종하는 녀자는 어느 때까지든지 (貞操)를 직하고 자긔는 남의 안헤나 다른 녀자를 상광하랴고 한다. 이 무슨 모순(矛盾)의 짓이며 쓸개 빠진 놈의 짓인가.
그러고 자긔의 마음에 맛지 안는다고 비평하고 욕하고 나종에는 중상을 한다. 그러고 보니 남자라고 하는 것은 참이 적다. 체면이 적다. 따라서 녀자보다 활신 약하다.

아메리카니즘 中毒
