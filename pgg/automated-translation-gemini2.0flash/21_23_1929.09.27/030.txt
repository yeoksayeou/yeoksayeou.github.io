Overview of Gyeongseong

Seoul is the capital of Joseon and the place where the Li Dynasty established its 500-year capital. It is now called Gyeongseong, but in the past, it was called Namgyeong (南京), Hanyang (漢陽), or Hanseong (漢城). It is located at 126 degrees 59 minutes east longitude and 37 degrees 34 minutes north latitude, occupying the central part of the Korean Peninsula. To the north, it is backed by the steep peaks of Bugaksan (白岳山), which is 143 meters high, and Samgaksan (三角山), which is 636 meters high. To the south, it faces Mongmyeoksan (木覓山), also known as Jongnamsan (終南山), which is 265 meters high. To the east, Tarakson (駝駱山), and to the west, the peaks of Anhyun (鞍峴) at 338 meters surround it, forming a natural fortress. To the southwest, the fields and mountains unfold, and the Han River (漢江), one of the five major rivers of Korea, flows grandly to the south, truly a magnificent capital with mountains and rivers. The fortress is now mostly destroyed, but its circumference was 9,975 steps (approximately 4 ri 26 cho), and its height reached 41 cheok 1 chon. Originally, there were eight gates, but currently, only Namdaemun (南大門), Dongdaemun (東大門), Changui (彰義), Hyehwa (惠化), and Gwanghui (光熙) remain, but one can imagine its magnificent appearance in the past. (Details about the gates and fortress walls will be discussed in another section.) The administrative divisions were originally divided into 5 bu (部) and 49 bang (坊), with dong (洞) and gye (契) under the bang, but after the merger, Sinnyongsan (新龍山) and Guryongsan (舊龍山) were incorporated into Gyeongseong and divided into 85 dong and 101 jeong. Its breadth is 1 ri 33 cho from east to west and 3 ri 12 cho from north to south, with an area of 2,035 square ri. The circumference is 7 ri 26 cho, which amounts to a total of 10,629,500 pyeong when calculated in pyeong. The climate is characterized by severe heat and cold, with the highest annual temperature reaching 36.3 degrees Celsius and the lowest reaching -21 degrees Celsius. The annual rainfall and snowfall amount to 1171.9 mm, which is the highest rainfall in Korea, about 80% of Busan (釜山) and Wonsan (元山), and 1.5 times that of Hamgyeongbuk-do (咸鏡北道), the driest region. The maximum rainfall in one day and night is 335 mm, which is unparalleled in Korea and Japan. The first frost usually occurs around October 12th or 13th, and the first snow usually occurs around November 3rd or 4th. During the winter, the Han River freezes to a thickness of about one cheok, and the snow sometimes reaches a thickness of about one cheok.

The number of households is 70,288, and the population is 315,006. Among them, the number of Korean households is 48,454, with 115,742 men and 110,091 women, totaling 225,833 people. The number of Japanese households is 20,866, with a population of 43,032 men and 41,144 women, totaling 84,176 people. There are 968 foreign households, with 4,179 men and 816 women, totaling 4,997 people. [Showa 3rd year survey]

京城의 大觀
서울은 朝鮮의 首府오 李朝 500년 國都를 奠하엿든 곳이다. 지금은 京城이라 하지만은 往時에는 혹은 南京 혹은 漢陽 又는 漢城이라 稱하얏스니 동경 126도 59분 북위 37도 34분에 位하야 朝鮮半島의 中部를 占하얏다. 北은 高가 143米되는 白岳山과 636米 되는 三角山의 重疊한 奇峯을 등지고 南은 265米 되는 木覓山(終南山)을 낫하며 東은 駝駱山 西는 338米의 鞍峴諸峯이 둘너 서서 천연의 城府를 이루고 西南으로 山野가 展開하는 동시에 朝鮮 5대 강에 한나인 漢江이 基南으로 洋洋이 흘너가니 실로 山河襟帶의 雄都다. 성은 지금에 多部分 破毁돼얏스나 주위 9,975步(日四里 26町餘) 高가 41척 1촌에 달하고 문은 원래 八門으로서 현재 南大門 東大門外 彰義 惠化 光熙 등이 殘餘할 뿐이나 往時의 雄偉壯觀인 것을 可想하겟다. (門及 성벽은 他項에 詳論함) 행정구역은 自來 5部 49坊에 分하고 坊下에 또 洞, 契가 잇섯스나 倂合 후 新舊龍山을 京城에 편입하야 85洞101町으로 分하얏스니 廣袤는 동서가 1里33町 남북이 3里12町 면적은 2,035方里 一周의 里程은 7里26町인데 此를 坪數로 따지면 총계 1062만9,500평이다. 기후는 寒暑가 심하야 연중 최고 기온은 섭씨36도 3분 최저 기온은 영하 21도다. 雨雪의 年量은 1171,9粍이니 전 朝鮮 내 최다 雨域 釜山 元山에 비하면 약 8할에 상당하고 最寡 雨域인 咸鏡北道에 비하면 1배 반이 되니 一晝夜의 최다량 335粍은 朝鮮 각지 及 日本 각지에 其比가 업다. 初霜은 대개 10월 12,3일경, 初雪은 대개 11월 3,4일 경이니 冬節에 漢江이 결빙하야 尺餘에 달하고 雪도 尺餘에 달하는 時가 잇다.
戶數는 70,288戶 인구는 315,006人인 中 內에 朝鮮人 戶數는 48,454戶로 男이 115,742人 女 110,091人 計225,833人이오 日本人은 戶數 20,866으로 인구 男43,032人 女41,44 計84,176人이오 외국인은 968戶로 男4,179, 女816 計4997에 달한다. [昭和3年調]<1>
