1929년 09월 27일
능청마즌 人力車軍과 아궁이의 燭불, 서울은 낭이다! 京城와서 속아 본 이약이 (Experiences of Being Deceived in Seoul by Rickshaw Drivers and Candlelight in the Furnace, Seoul is a Den of Thieves!)
全州 張完植
회고·수기 (Reminiscence/Memoir)

Last year, on a certain day in December, at 9:X at night, I arrived at Kyŏngsŏng Station (京城驛). The electric lights, mocking the sunlight, were bright, but as a first-timer, I didn't know the direction and had no one to consult on which way to go. So, I easily hailed a rickshaw and told him to take me to the Hwanggeum-chŏng (黃金町) XX Inn where Mr. Yi was staying.

Not knowing whether Hwanggeum-chŏng was to the east or west, I was shaken as the rickshaw pulled me along, just waiting for him to tell me to get off. He kept pulling, but I wondered how far it was, as he kept going for quite a while without telling me to get off, just pulling and pulling. "They say Seoul is big, but is it really this tedious to travel? Perhaps Hwanggeum-chŏng is at the very end of Seoul." Thinking this, I endured the tedium and kept going. When I asked, he said it was still far. After pulling for a while, he finally stopped at a place and let me off. I jumped out and asked how much the fare was. He didn't mention a price but just said to give him what I thought was fair. Considering he had come a long way and it was late, I gave him one won (1圓) with a big show of generosity. He said, "Do you think I'd come this far for just one won?" and refused to accept it. Surprised that one won was too little, I asked what he meant, checking the time to see that it was past twelve and already 1:20 AM. I thought that he had come a long way, so I gave him an additional 50 chŏn. Still, he asked for more. Then, I started shaking the inn door, saying I would ask Mr. Yi (李氏) and pay him after finding out. The rickshaw puller hastily left. The owner came out, and when I looked for Mr. Yi, alas, he had moved to another place two or three days ago, leaving me very disappointed. But with nothing else to do, I decided to stay there that night and unpacked my luggage. Afterwards, I asked the owner how far it was from the station to here. He said it wasn't far at all. I then asked how much the rickshaw fare would usually be. The owner replied that 40 chŏn would be sufficient. Only then did I realize that the rickshaw puller had guessed I was new to the city and had been circling around unnecessarily, using the excuse of the long distance to try to get more money. Still, he had some conscience and couldn't dare to ask for an outrageous amount, so he had asked me to give him what I thought was fair, hoping to get more if he could. But when I started knocking on the door and calling for the owner, he took what he had and fled in a hurry.

While sleeping at that inn, the room was so cold that I told the servant to start a fire, but he said the wood was all gone, so I should just bear with it and sleep as is. The innkeeper's way of treating guests was so atrocious that I wanted to swear at him, but fearing what misfortune might befall me in scary Seoul, I gave him 20 chŏn and asked him to buy wood and start a fire. Now hoping it would get warm, I lay down and tried to sleep. However, it was still cold, and I only dozed off for a moment before waking up again and feeling the floor, which was as cold as ever.
<123>
In my sleep, I could hear the sound of wood breaking, but the room wasn't getting warmer. Thinking the fire had died down, I went to check and saw the fire glowing brightly in the furnace. It was burning, but it wasn't getting warmer, which was strange, so I went down and looked into the furnace, and it was just unbelievable. A one-chŏn candle (洋燭) was almost completely burned out.

Brothers who come to Seoul without knowing the situation and the people! Seoul is such a place. Whether you come for business or sightseeing, I urge you to learn about Seoul well before coming.

능청마즌 人力車軍과 아궁이의 燭불, 서울은 낭이다! 京城와서 속아 본 이약이 (各地各人의 實地經驗談)
						全州 張完植
昨年 12월 어느날 밤 아홉시 X분에 京城驛에 나리엿슴니다. 해ㅅ빗을 비웃는 전등불은 발것스나 初行인 나는 방향을 알 수가 업고 갈 길을 의논할 곳도 업서서 손쉬웁게 人力車를 불너 타고 내가 차저가는 李씨가 묵고 잇는 黃金町 XX여관으로 가자고 하엿슴니다.
黃金町이 동쪽에 잇는지 서쪽에 잇는지 아지 못하는 나는 인력거가 흔드는 대로 흔들니며 내리라고 할 때만 기다리고 끌을녀 가나 대체 얼마나 먼 곳인지 한참을 가도 내리란 말이 업시 작고 끄을고 갈 뿐임니다. 『서울이 널다 하기고 이러케도 지리하게 갈가. 아마 서울 끗다은 곳이 黃金町인가보다.』 이러케 생각을 하고 지리한 것도 참고 가다가 물어 보면 아즉 머럿다고 작고 가다가 이윽고 한 곳에 나려 놋키로 나는 뛰여 나려서 삭을 얼마나 달나느냐 무르니 돈 말은 안이하고 그저 처분을 해 달나기로 멀니 오기도 햇고 밤도 느젓기로 큰 생색을 내는 셈으로 1圓짜리 한장을 주엇드니 『돈 1圓 밧자고 이 먼 곳을 와요』하면서 밧지를 안이하엿슴니다. 나는 1圓이 적다는데 놀나서 무어요 하면서 시간을 보니 열두시가 지나서 새로 한시 20분 前이엿슴니다. 나는 생각에 멀니 오기는 햇구나 하며 50錢을 더 주엇드니 그래도 더 달나기에 그때는 여관집 문을 흔들며 李씨를 불너서 무러 보고 주마 하닛가 그 인력거꾼은 황황이 가버리고 마럿슴니다. 주인이 나왓기로 李씨를 차즈니 앗불사 2,3일 전에 다른 곳으로 옴겨 갓다고 하야 매우 낙심이 되엿스나 할일 업시 그 밤은 그 곳에서 자기로 하고 旅裝을 푸른 뒤에 주인에게 정거장에서 여기까지 얼마나 되느냐고 무르니 불과 얼마 안이 된다 하기로 인력거 삭은 대개 얼마나 줄것이냐고 다시 무럿습니다. 주인은 대답하야 40錢이면 족하다고 하엿슴니다. 그제야 그 인력거꾼이 내가 처음길인 것을 짐작하고 공연히 빙빙 도라단이며 길이 멀다는 것을 빙자하고 더 돈을 바들냐고 하얏스나 그래도 양심이 잇서서 감히 엄청나게 달날수는 업슴으로 처분을 하라고 하야 내가 주는 것을 보아서 될수 잇스면 더 조르랴 한 것이 대문을 두드리며 주인을 부르닛가 그것이라도 바다가지고 황횡히 간 것임니다.
그 여관에서 잠을 자는데 방이 엇지도 치운지 하인을 불너서 불 때기를 일넛드니 나무가 맛침 떠러젓스니 참고 그대로 자라는 것이엿슴니다. 여관하는 놈의 손대접하는 태도가 괴악하야 욕설을 하고 십헛스나 무서운 서울에 처음 왓다가 무슨 봉변을 당할지 몰나서 20錢을 내여주며 나무를 사다가 때여 달나하고 인제는 뜨듯해 오려니 하고 누어서 잠을 청하엿슴니다. 그러나 원래 치워서 잠간 잣는 듯하다가 다시<123> 깨여 방바닥을 만저 보니 여전히 차기는 한가지엿슴니다.
잠결에 나무 꺽는 소리는 낫는데 방이 더웁지는 안이 하기로 불이 죽은 줄 알고 내여다 보니 아궁이예서 훤하게 불빗이 뵈엿습니다. 불은 타는데 안 더웁는 것이 이상하야 나려가서 아궁이를 드려다보니 말하기도 기가 막힘니다. 1錢짜리 洋燭 한가락이 거진 다 타드러 가고 잇섯슴니다.
서울의 형편과 인심을 모르시고 오시는 형님네여! 서울이란 이런 곳임니다. 혹 볼일이 잇서 오시든 구경을 오시든 서울에 오시기 전에 반다시 서울을 잘 알고 오시기를 부탁함니다.<124>
