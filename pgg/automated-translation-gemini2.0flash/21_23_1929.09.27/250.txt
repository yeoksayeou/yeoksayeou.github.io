-The Most Central Place in Old Kyŏngsŏng-

In the middle of the Tea House Alley (茶坊골), there remains a single thatched-roof house (草家집)... It is said that this is the very center of old Kyŏngsŏng (京城), the middle of the turtle-shaped area, and is called "belly button house" (뱃곱집) because it's the turtle's navel (龜形). If you put a tiled roof (개와), there will be a fire, so it is always kept as a thatched roof, but because the police do not allow thatched roofs (草家를 不許), they recently put a tiled roof on it, but a fire broke out, and it is said that they rebuilt it as a thatched roof again. <74>

-녯날 京城의 最中心地-
가운데 茶坊골에 단 하나 남아 잇는 草家집...... 녯날 京城의 그 中 복판의 복판이요 龜形으로 된 지역의 한복판이라 하야 거븍의 뱃곱이라고 『뱃곱집』이라 하는대 개와를 니으면 火災가 잇서서 언제던지 草家로만 두논대 警察이 草家를 不許하는 고로 近年에 개와를 언젓더니 火災가 나서 다시 草家로 고치엇다 한다.<74>
