Instructions for Ordering the Magazine

One, write your address and name clearly without blurring.
(The magazine is returned if it cannot be delivered.)

One, clearly specify the month or issue number.
(If you don't write the month, the wrong book may be sent.)

One, don't forget to add and send the postage.
(It is slow and a loss to send a postcard to request the shortage instead of sending the book.)

One, please send money orders by postal transfer as much as possible instead of cash or postal stamps.
(There is no concern about intermediate loss, and the remittance fee is cheaper.) <105>
