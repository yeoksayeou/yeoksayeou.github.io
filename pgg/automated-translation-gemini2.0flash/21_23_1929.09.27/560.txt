기사형태	희곡·시나리오 (Play/Scenario)

Apartment

Narrator (As the curtain opens): Ladies and gentlemen! The scene I am about to show you is, I think, the most suspenseful drama in the world. Our heroine seems to be waiting for someone in her bedroom alone. It is past midnight. (Hides to the side of the stage.)

Woman (Suddenly gets up and listens. Then goes to the window and looks outside. A soft knock is heard on the door. Without turning around) Come in. (A man in evening dress enters.) Sit down. Tonight, you must do everything I tell you to do.

(He sits down with a strange look. The woman remains turned away) Mr. Reginald! (The man is startled.) Tonight, you must confess everything. (The man is even more surprised.) You won't see that woman again, will you? You promise you won't?

Man (In a subdued voice) Yes, I promise I won't.

Woman You promise? You kissed her here, didn't you? (Pointing to her lips—but not looking at the man.) What did she say then? (The man doesn't know what to do.) Didn't she slap you?

Man Yes, she did.

Woman You have confessed everything you have done so far, without leaving anything out? So that you feel relieved! That's why I like you.

Man Not at all.

Woman Really! Mr. Reginald! I always want to do that with you.

Man (In a subdued voice) Of course.

Woman Mr. Reginald! We mustn't have any secrets between us! Right? I'm going to tell you everything too!

Man Of course—<163>

Woman The first person who ever kissed me in my life was a truly terrible villain! That evil man stole my innocence....

Man That murderous bastard!

Woman First he kissed me here (pointing to her cheek), then here (lips), and then here (neck). He just kept kissing me?

Man And then....

Woman Then I let him. And the second person who kissed me was a stableman (馬夫).

Man That son of a bitch!

Woman That guy took my pure and innocent self.... first here, then here, and then here—

Narrator To let you know that 2 minutes have passed here, I will turn off the lights for 2 seconds.

Woman And then the ninth man kissed me here—

Man Oh!

Woman First here—

Narrator To let you know that 4 minutes have passed, I will turn off the lights for 4 seconds.

Woman And then the twenty-third man kissed me here—

Man That damned bastard—

Woman That guy also deceived me when I was innocent—

Narrator To let you know that an hour has passed here, I will turn off the lights for 5 seconds.

Woman And then the two hundred and sixty-seventh man kissed me here.

Man That immoral bastard!

Woman First here and then—

Narrator To indicate the passage of several hours, I will turn off the lights for about 7 seconds. (Straining) Well, it's dawn.

Woman And then, darling, the 77,777th man kissed me right here? That terrible guy, how awful he was, also aimed for my innocent self— (A moment of silence—rubbing her eyes) Ah—I've confessed everything! Now there are no secrets between you and me, right? Mr. Reginald! Now— (The woman stretches out her arms and goes to the man. The moment she looks at his face for the first time, her expression changes and fills with horror. The woman screams) Oh dear, you heard everything! (And the woman faints prettily in front of her husband. (Curtain) <164>

說明者 (開幕과 동시에) 여러분, 紳士淑女諸君! 이제 보혀드릴 한 幕은 이 세상에서 가장 긴장된 드라마가 안일가 생각함니다. 우리들의 헤로인(여주인공)은 혼자 자긔 침실에서 누구인지 기다리고 잇는 모양임니다. 지금은 한밤ㅅ중을 지난 때임니다. (무대 녑흐로 숨는다.)
女 (별안간 니러서서 귀를 기우린다. 그러고 창 녑흐로 가서 밧갓을 내여다 본다. 뚜-어를 가만히 두드리는 소리가 난다. 도라다 보지도 안코) 드러오서요. (夜會服을 입은 남자가 드러온다.) 안즈십시요. 오늘밤은 꼭 나 하라는 대로 해야 해요.
(그는 이상한 낫빗을 하며 거러 안는다. 여자는 그대로 도라슨 채로) 레듸날드氏! (남자는 감짝 놀낸다.) 참 오늘은 모든 것을 토파해 주시지요. (남자는 더욱 놀랜다.) 이제는 그 여자와는 다시 안 맛나실테야요? 그러치 꼭 안 맛나지?
男 (옴으러저 드러가는 목소리로) 네- 그러케 하지요.
女 그러치? 여긔다 킷쓰를 햇지? (자기의 입을 가르치며- 그러나 남자를 도라보려고는 안는다.) 그때  그 여자는 무어랍듸까! (남자 엇절줄 모른다.) 빰이나 맛지 안엇소?
男 네- 그저 그랫지요.
女 당신은 이때까지 한 일을 하나도 뻬놋치 안코 다- 고백을 햇지요? 속이 시원하도록! 내가 당신을 조화하는 까닭이 그 점이라우.
男 천만에.
女 정말! 레듸날드씨! 나도 당신에게 늘 그러고 십허요.
男 (속으로 드러가는 소리로) 그러코말고요.
女 레듸날드씨! 우리 사이에 무슨 비밀이 잇서서는 안되지요! 응? 나도 무엇이고 무엇이고 다 말해 버릴테예요!
男 조코말고-<163>
女 이때까지의 내 평생에 나에게 맨 처음으로 킷스를 한 사람은 그건 참 말도 할 수 업는 악한이엿다우! 그런 악한 놈이 그만 나의 순결한 것을.... .
男 저런 죽일 놈!

男 그러고....
女 그 다음에는 내가 해주엇지. 그러고 두번재 킷스를 한 사람은 馬夫엿다우.
男 비러먹을 놈!
女 그 놈은 나의 순결하고 텬진한 것을.... 처음에는 여긔 그 다음에는 여긔 그러고 여긔-
