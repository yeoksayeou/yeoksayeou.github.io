기사형태	雜著 (Miscellaneous Essay)

The Beginning of the Stone Embankment Construction of Gyeongseong's Stream

For over 300 years since the establishment of the capital, Gyeongseong's (京城) stream had not been properly dredged (濬川), resulting in annual floods (洪水) during the rainy season (潦霖), causing unforeseen disasters (不虞의 患) for the people of the city (都內 人民). In the 39th year of King Yeongjo (英祖), the dredging project (濬川의 役) finally commenced in the 3rd month of the year Gyeongjin (庚辰) and was completed in the 4th month. However, without stone embankments (石築) on either side, and only willows (柳) planted, the stream collapsed annually, requiring significant national expenditure (國費) for repairs. In the 49th year of King Yeongjo, the year Gyesa (癸巳) (157 years ago from now), Royal Guard Commander Yi Gyeong-u (李景祐氏) (from Yongin 龍仁) petitioned the throne, and each army division was assigned a section to construct stone embankments. This was the beginning (嚆矢) of the stone embankment construction of Gyeongseong's (京城) stream. <81>

京城 開川石築의 始
京城의 開川은 設都 후 300여 년이 되도록 濬川을 안이하야 매년 潦霖의 時에는 洪水가 汎濫하야 都內 人民이 不虞의 患을 免치 못하던 바 英祖39년  庚辰3월에 비로소 濬川의 役을 始하야 同4월에 完了하얏다. 그러나 左右에 石築이 업고 但히 柳를 種하얏슴으로 매년 崩潰하야 此를 修補하기에 國費가 不少하더니 英祖49년 癸巳(距今 157년 전)에 守禦使 李景祐氏(龍仁人)가 上奏하야 諸軍門으로 구역을 정하야 石築을 하니 此가 京城 開川石築의 嚆矢다.<81>
