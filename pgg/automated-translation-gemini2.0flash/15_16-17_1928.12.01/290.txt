기사제목	男女大討論, 子女結婚에 干涉을 할가 自由로 둘가 (Great Discussion Between Men and Women: Should Parents Interfere in Their Children's Marriages, or Leave Them Free?)
기사형태	설문 (Questionnaire)

Interference Side
Shin Albert

I will get straight to the point without much preamble. In present-day Joseon, I believe it is absolutely necessary for parents or guardians to interfere in the marriages of young men and women. As the saying goes, even if everything else is Westernized and good, a Westernized daughter-in-law is not good (as Mr. So-and-so experienced last year when he married a Qing woman, and his parents opposed it), just as everyone likes Western things but not Western-style rice. Everyone likes freedom, but freedom of marriage alone is absolutely impossible. Of course, if the man and woman are of considerable age and knowledge, and if they have sufficient understanding and are prepared to marry freely, there would be no greater happiness, and no one would oppose it. However, in Joseon, which is still in a transitional period, where there is a great difference in the level of knowledge between men and women, and where social and family systems have not been fully improved, if young men and women, without any understanding or preparation, merely follow the trends of the times and are swayed by temporary feelings or emotions, and engage in so-called free love or free marriage, how harmonious will their family be, and how happy will the couple be? Just yesterday, people who were dreaming sweet dreams of wedded bliss in the bridal chamber are often grieving over broken relationships in just a few days. I don't need to cite examples to illustrate how common this is in our society. Generally speaking, in marriages with interference, because others are interfering, there are many things that do not suit the opinions of the two parties involved, which can easily lead to discord between the couple. On the other hand, free marriages are based on the free will of the parties involved, so it seems that the two people will always be able to unite well, but in reality, it is the opposite. If you look at the statistics of divorcees recently published in newspapers, the number of divorces among free marriages is higher than among arranged marriages. This is because, as mentioned above, in free marriages, men and women do not have far-sighted and thorough thoughts, but rather marry based on temporary emotions and feelings, so when those emotions and feelings fade, the relationship immediately breaks down. In contrast, in arranged marriages, supervisors think calmly and fairly, and plan for the maximum happiness and benefit of the two people, so even if there are some dissatisfactions between the two people, it does not lead to a final tragic ending. In conclusion, in the current state of Joseon, whether viewed from the perspective of the individual, the family, or society, free marriage is harmful and useless.

Freedom Side
Shin Heung-woo

In marriage, it seems no exaggeration to say that it is a new principle to associate with and approach each other, in order to find out each other's personality, ideals, health, and everything else.
Marriage is not mainly about momentary pleasure arising from the physiological impulses of men and women meeting temporarily.
If you think small, it starts with an individual's life problem, and if you think big, it is a problem related to society and the nation.
I think it is a big consideration to blindly follow and carry out customs that have been passed down from the old days without any awareness of such important issues.
A good example is the matchmaking marriage (媒介結婚) that is commonly practiced in Joseon.
If the parties' intentions and personalities are ignored and others interfere absolutely to carry out the event, it makes one wonder where the meaning of marriage lies.
Marriage is an individual's life or personal union, so I don't think a third party should go deep into the content and interfere.
Therefore, I think free marriage is the most ideal method of marriage, but when it comes to realizing this, there are many difficult problems to face. First, there are several difficult things, such as morality, circumstances, and social mores.
In order to realize free marriage in our society, we must first create a suitable place, or consider the issue of morality between men and women.
For example, if it is a place where parents or relatives are present, the men and women who are about to marry cannot have a relationship to find out their personality or ideals, but just pick up strange decorum at the end of the collection <139> .
However, if they meet in a secret place, they will talk about all kinds of things and have a relationship to the extent that it looks unsightly to others. Therefore, it cannot be regarded as an ideal that young men and women in our society have recently engaged in romance and free marriage.
It can be seen as a common aspect of free marriage for young men and women in our society to repeat secret meetings and secret meetings, and when they try to avoid and avoid them, and there is no place to hide, and things become urgent, they take the superficial form of marriage.
Free marriage is, of course, a good thing, but I would like to add that you should be aware of the various difficulties in realizing it and pay attention to this point.
Finally, there is also the racial improvement theory of Mr. Colt (꼴트), who values ​​family or faction and denies free marriage, but I have been denying this theory since I was in school, so I will say again that I will not deal with it today.

Freedom Side
Lee Don-hwa

Regarding the question of which of the two sides, parental interference in marriage and the absolute freedom of the parties involved, is closer to the ideal marriage, I don't think this is a question in the first place.
Why? This is the same problem as when I am hungry and want to eat, but if there is no parental interference, I will not be able to eat what I like and what tastes good. Of course, if the person eating is a child or a patient, there is of course a need to interfere. But is there a fool anywhere who would be interfered with by his parents even when eating food as an adult or as a healthy person? The same goes for the issue of sex. Sexual desire is not a problem of acquired nature like appetite, but a problem of innate personality that comes out as a free-spirited, God-given character. What kind of shame do parents have to interfere with, or what is the need to interfere? The fact that these two issues are being discussed in society shows how infantile and pathological that society is.
As much as society is infantile and pathological, that much the society shies away from free marriage. For example, let's look at our Joseon. As you all know, there was absolutely no free marriage in Joseon, and there was the evil practice of parents marrying off young children who were still 口尙乳臭 (gu sang yu ch'u - smelling of milk) according to their own wishes, wasn't there? So, there was nothing but parental interference here, and parental interference was necessary. Why? Because they had to marry off young children who didn't even know the difference between men and women, so parents had to go on an indirect marriage on behalf of the bride and groom, didn't they? In this case, parental interference is absolutely necessary. It's only natural. But this is when society is in a pathological state. That's why Joseon has become like this. If you still want to marry off young children and have parents indirectly marry them off, parental interference is of course necessary. That goes without saying. However, if we are talking about adults who are in a physiological state of nature and living a natural sexual life, it would be the same as parents interfering with their adult children's eating and feeding them while going through rituals if parental interference is necessary. What are you worried about? Isn't it extremely natural that sexual desire is innate, that two bodies like each other innately, and that those who like each other join together to become a couple? So, why would parents need to interfere with it by being grumpy? Parents should only help to make it smooth and only do the marriage procedure. Some might say this: Because young people don't know what is water and what is fire and rush into things recklessly, they may not choose their partners well and ruin their future, so parental interference is necessary. Look at this. In our Joseon, parents have interfered and have done marriages so well that society is in this state. What does it matter to parents? Marriage is a matter of innate personality, so absolute individual freedom must be allowed. In the extreme, marriage is a matter for the individual to solve with his or her own freedom. It is never a matter for parents and siblings to interfere with. Even if they fail, they have to leave it to their own solution. Even if they succeed, they have to leave it to their own solution. So, some will fail. Some will succeed. Does that mean that if parents interfere, there will be no absolute failure and only success? As mentioned above, that becomes the root cause of the pathological phenomenon of society and only invites a major failure that violates the principles of nature. Even what is considered a success is not a success but only a success that enslaves individuality.

Interference Side
Byun Seong-ok

If asked what my opinion is on free marriage and arranged marriage, I would say that I have the opinion that neither type of marriage is desirable. Arranged marriage overlooks the personality and intentions of the parties concerned, so there is a major defect in the ideal and reality of marriage, and free marriage tends to flow in an emotional direction, so there is a flaw in that the outcome and continuation of the marriage do not proceed smoothly.
For example, in our society, marriage has traditionally been based on the opinions of parents, which has caused problems with affection between couples, peace in the family, and even social morals, which is a fact that everyone can see.
However, should arranged marriages be abolished overnight and free marriages be advocated and implemented? This is not something to be decided rashly.
Looking at the United States, which is famous for free marriage, the divorce rate is 25 out of 100 marriages. It is something to think about why there are divorces when free marriages should have been made because the personalities, thoughts, and everything else of the two people are in harmony. Free marriage should be done in the sense that it respects the opinions and personalities of the parties involved, but since it is not subject to any restrictions or interference, emotions are easily heightened or it is easy to get caught up in the heat, and the functions of reason and reflection become dulled, making it impossible to judge the right and wrong of things. At the same time, when the heat of emotions cools down, what they thought was most beautiful looks ugly, and defects that they had not recognized before are discovered, leading to unexpected failures.
Therefore, I am against both absolute free marriage and absolute arranged marriage. I am most in favor of a marriage law that harmonizes emotion and reason by compromising these two types of marriage and selecting the strengths of each. To be more specific, while respecting the intentions of the people who are getting married, the people themselves should follow the teachings and guidance of parents or seniors who have a lot of experience in society and human relationships.
I have the opinion that both free marriage and arranged marriage are undesirable, but if I were to say which type of marriage in our current society would give more benefits and less harm to individuals and society, I think arranged marriage would have fewer disadvantages than free marriage.
Since our society is still uncivilized and the issue of young men and women's cultivation is still in progress, if we transplant free marriage as it is in foreign countries, I think there is great concern about social morals and the future of young men and women. Therefore, if we were to judge the pros and cons based on understanding today, I would say arranged marriage.

적게 생각하면 개인의 생활문제로 비롯하야 크게 생각하면 사회나 민족에게까지 관계가 잇는 문제이다.
