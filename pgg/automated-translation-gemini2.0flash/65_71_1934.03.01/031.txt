Imjin War and the Japanese Army
First Installment
-Landing at Pusan on April 4th, 25th year of Seonjo's reign [1592]-

The number of Japanese troops dispatched during the first invasion of the Imjin War was approximately 300,000. The deployment of the advance guard was as follows:

First Division - Konishi Yukinaga, Sō Yoshitoshi, Matsura Shigenobu and 6 other generals (18,700 soldiers)

Second Division - Katō Kiyomasa, Nabeshima Naoshige, Sagara Yorifusa and 3 other generals (22,800 soldiers)

Third Division - Kuroda Nagamasa, Ōtomo Yoshitō and 2 other generals (21,000 soldiers)

Fourth Division - Mōri Yoshinari, Shimazu Yoshihiro and 6 other generals (14,000 soldiers)

Fifth Division - Fukushima Masanori, Chōsokabe Motochika, Hachisuka Iemasa and 6 other generals (25,000 soldiers)

Sixth Division - Kobayakawa Takakage, Mōri Hidemoto, Tachibana Muneshige and 5 other generals (15,700 soldiers)

Seventh Division - Mōri Terumoto (30,000 soldiers)

Eighth Division - Ukita Hideie (10,000 soldiers)

Ninth Division - Hashiba Hidekatsu, Hosokawa Tadaoki and 2 other generals = stationed at Iki (壹岐在陣) = (11,500 soldiers)

Second Installment
-Jeong-yu War (丁酉再亂), 30th year of Seonjo's reign [1597]-

First Division - Second Division, Katō Kiyomasa, Konishi Yukinaga, Sō Yoshitoshi, Matsura Shigenobu, Arima Harunobu and 7 other generals (Katō's troops: 10,000, Konishi and others: 14,100)

Third Division - Kuroda Nagamasa, Mōri Yoshinari, Mōri Katsunaga, Shimazu Toyohisa and 8 other generals (10,000 soldiers)

Fourth Division - Nabeshima Naoshige, Nabeshima Katsushige (12,000 soldiers)

Fifth Division - Shimazu Yoshihiro (10,000 soldiers)

Sixth Division - Chōsokabe Motochika, Tōdō Takatora, Katō Yoshiaki, Ikeda Hideaki and 7 other generals (13,300 soldiers)

Seventh Division - Hachisuka Iemasa, Ikoma Kazumasa, Wakiya Yasuharu and 3 other generals (11,100 soldiers)

Eighth Division - Mōri Hidemoto, Ukita Hideie (Hidemoto: 30,000, Hideie: 10,000)

In addition, Kobayakawa Hidekane's (小早川秀) troops stationed in Pusan (釜山) numbered 10,000, Tachibana Muneshige's (立花宗茂) troops at Angolpo (安骨浦) numbered 5,000, Takahashi Naotsugu (高橋直次) and Tsukushi Hirokado's (築紫廣門) troops at Gadeokdo (加德島) numbered 1,000, Mōri Hidemoto's troops at Jukdo (竹島) numbered 1,000, Asano Yoshinaga's (淺野幸長) troops at Seosaengpo (西生浦) numbered 3,000, for a total of 141,500 soldiers. <7>

壬辰亂과 日本軍
第一回
-宣祖二五年壬辰四月四日釜山上陸-
壬亂時日本軍의 第一回出兵數는 約三十萬으로 그 先發隊의 部署는 如左함니다.
第一陣-小西行長, 宗義智, 松浦鎭信等六將(兵卒一萬八千七百人)
第二陣-加藤淸正, 鎬島直茂, 相良油賴房三將(兵卒二萬二千八百人)
第三陣-黑田長政, 大友義統二將(兵卒二萬一千人)
第四陣-毛利吉成, 島津義弘等六將(兵卒一萬四千人)
第五陣-福島正則, 長會我部元親, 蜂須賀家政等六將(兵卒二萬五千人)
第六陣-小早川隆京, 毛倉秀包, 立花宗成等五將(兵卒一萬五千七百人)
第七陣-毛利輝元(兵卒三萬人)
第八陣-宇喜多秀家(兵卒一萬人)
第九陣-羽柴秀勝, 細川忠興二將=壹岐在陣=(兵卒一萬一千五百人)
第二回
-宣祖三十年丁酉再亂-
第一陣-第二陣, 加藤淸正, 小西行長, 宗義智, 松浦鎭信, 有爲晴信七將(加藤兵一萬人, 小西以下一萬四千百人)
第三陣-黑田長政, 毛利吉成, 同勝永, 島津豐久等八將(兵卒一萬人)
第四陣-鍋島直茂, 同勝茂(一萬二千人)
第五陣-島津義弘(一萬人)
第六陣-長會我部元親, 藤堂高虎, 加藤嘉明, 池田秀七將(兵卒一萬三千三百人)
第七陣-蜂須賀家政, 生駒一正, 脇均安治三將(兵卒一萬千百人)
第八陣-毛利秀元, 字喜多秀家(秀元三萬人, 秀家一萬人)
此外釜山駐屯小早川秀兵一萬, 安骨浦의 立花宗茂兵五千, 加德島의 高橋直次, 築紫廣門兵一千竹島의 毛利秀包一千, 西生浦의 淺野幸長兵三千, 總兵數十四萬千五百人이엿습니다.＜7＞
