Kumgang Ballad, Butterfly and Lily of the Valley
Kimnyeongin

It was a late spring evening when an ominous wind, symbolizing a storm, was blowing!

The place is a desperately wretched and beautiful story that occurred in the middle of a precipitous cliff in the valley of Kuryongyon (九龍淵), a world-famous scenic spot in Mt. Kumgang (金剛山).

It is a story so old that even billions of years would be too short to describe it. After the creation of the universe, in the middle of a terrifying cliff where no human hands or feet had ever reached, a single, small lily of the valley flower (鈴蘭꼿) bloomed alone in a rock crevice less than an inch wide. It bloomed and grew unnoticed. But on this evening, it suddenly encountered an unpleasant, ominous wind, and a shadow of melancholy filled its pure and lovely face.

At that moment, a butterfly (범나븨) with tattered wings, flew in from somewhere to find this lonely lily of the valley, a pure maiden who had never met a bachelor butterfly. It had searched so much for a place to rest that its wings were now almost torn, and it seemed unable to fly even an inch further. Seeing this, the young lily of the valley felt deeply sympathetic and worried. However, being in the presence of a strange man for the first time, she lowered her head, blushing, and barely managed to breathe softly without saying a word.

Now! The play begins here.

Butterfly: "Excuse me, Lily of the Valley (鈴蘭) Miss! As you can see, I am a tired butterfly (나븨) who cannot move even one step further. Oh, endlessly sacred and endlessly beautiful Lily of the Valley (鈴蘭) Miss! Could you have pity on me and let me stay for a while?"

Lily: "Oh, how embarrassing..."

Butterfly: "That is understandable. It is only natural that you are very embarrassed to suddenly encounter such a change, having been born and blooming unnoticed in this spiritual mountain and sacred place. But because you bloom unnoticed like this, Lily of the Valley (鈴蘭) Miss! Your body is clearer than the Milky Way and purer than a white pearl!

Ah! But what evil fate is this? Who would have known that a being as worn out and defiled by the waves of the world as I am would come to find such a pure lady? But if everything is fate, it is fate, and if it is destiny, it is an unavoidable situation that can also be said to be so!

Lily of the Valley (鈴蘭) Miss! Please bend your jade-like and pine and bamboo-like integrity once and let this tired body into your arms for a while. In doing so, allow this sick and withered body to have a night's rest as sweet as honey and regain new courage from tomorrow."

Lily: "Oh, what to do!"

Butterfly: "Well, it is natural to hesitate, but isn't the time different now? Look over there. Dark clouds like ink are gathering in the sky. Over there on the mountain, a terrible wind is blowing!... Now-- Miss! Please make a decision quickly."

Lily: "Oh, what to do!"

Butterfly: "Then it seems that you cannot possibly take in this unfortunate body. You will not let this withered and tired body sleep in your silken arms for one night and give me the grace of rebirth. Then, I cannot help it. I will go. I will go, dragging my broken wings and flying aimlessly to wherever. I will die if I die... Now! Lily of the Valley (鈴蘭) Miss. Please stay in peace!"

Lily: "Oh, what to do!... Oh dear, the butterfly (나븨) is gone! He really left!"

Lily: "Ah! That roaring sound. This rain and wind! Oh, I was a bad girl. I was too cold! How could I have chased away such a tired butterfly (나븨) at this terrible time? Excuse me. Butterfly (나븨) sir! Butterfly (나븨) sir! Oh, where could he have gone? He couldn't have gone far! Butterfly (나븨) <23> Butterfly (나븨) sir!"

Butterfly: "Yes!"

Lily: "No! Where did you answer from? But why can I only hear your voice and not see your body?"

Butterfly: "I have fallen under your feet on this rock."

Lily: "Is that true? What a pity. You must have become like this because you couldn't fly. Excuse me, Butterfly (나븨) sir! I think I was too harsh. Now! Hurry up and climb up and come into my arms!"

Butterfly: "Thank you. But I don't have the strength to climb up. I will just die like this."

Lily: "Oh, no! You can't do that. Hurry up and come up. Now-- I will bend down like this, so just move your body a little and crawl up!"

Butterfly: "Thank you. I will never forget this grace, even in death."

Lily: "Put those words aside and hurry up! Oh-- you finally came up. Now-- I will cover your body with my sleeve like this, so don't worry about anything and sleep comfortably."

Butterfly: "Ah. I am alive now. Oh, now that I have received this touching love from you, I have no regrets even if I cannot live."

Lily: "Rest quickly. Now that I have allowed my integrity once like this, I have firmly resolved to share death and life together!"

Butterfly: "Thank you. This is truly a sacred love that will be etched for billions of years in this spiritual sacred ground (靈山聖地)."

Thus, on this windy evening in a corner of this spiritual mountain, the short but sacred scene of love unfolded.

Ah, but it was a pitiful and tragic event. The fierce and merciless storm continued to pour down all night long. And so, the lily of the valley (鈴蘭꼿) and the butterfly (나븨), clinging to each other and gritting their teeth, tried to endure this cruel fate. But the power of the lily of the valley (鈴蘭꼿) was too weak against the violence of the great nature.

Eventually, the lily of the valley's (鈴蘭꼿) waist broke, and the butterfly (나븨) fell from within the petals to the ground. <24>

But the sacred love still continued. As the butterfly (나븨) fell to the ground, it gathered its faint spirit and, after untold hardships, crawled under the roots of the lily of the valley (鈴蘭꼿), trying to leave its body to the rocky cliff, and dug deeply into the roots.

The next day came.

The day cleared up beautifully.

But the lily of the valley (鈴蘭꼿) was still unconscious. Two or three days passed. The sunlight was bright. The lily of the valley (鈴蘭꼿), its broken waist healed, regained consciousness and bloomed prettily again.

But she was tearful because she could not find where the butterfly (나븨) had gone. This was because the lily of the valley (鈴蘭꼿) herself had no way of knowing that the butterfly's (나븨) dead body had dug into her roots and provided fertilizer for the roots to be revived.

Moreover, no one will ever know this fact for thousands of years. Only the spiritual mountain (靈스러운 山) will soar high for billions of years, and only the sacred water will flow long for tens of thousands of years. <25>

곳은 금강산(金剛山)에도 긔절참절한 천하명승지 구룡연(九龍淵) 골자기 깍거질은 듯한 절벽 한 가운데서 생긴 영겁의 비참하고도 아름답고 고흔 이야기 입니다.
억千년도 오히려 짧다할 만큼 우주(宇宙)가 창생이후(蒼生以後) 한번도 인간의 손발이 밋치지 못한 무서운 절벽 한 가운데 단 한 치도 못되는 바위틈을 뚤코 어린 鈴蘭꼿 한 송이가 외롭게 피여잇섯스니 남모르게 피여나 남모르게 자라나고 잇다가 이날 저녁 때 별안간 유쾌하지 못한 음산한 바람을 맛나고 보니 순진하고도 귀여운 그 얼골에는 가련하게도 수심의 빗이 가득차 잇섯든 것입니다.
