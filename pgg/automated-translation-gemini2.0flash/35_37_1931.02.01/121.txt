新年號懸賞當選發表 (Announcement of Winners for the New Year's Issue Contest) (Notice)

The last New Year's issue was very popular in many ways, so the number of applicants for the prize was truly 16,876. However, what is most unfortunate is that the number of correct answers was small, perhaps because the question was difficult. From now on, we will think carefully and put out questions that anyone can guess if they think about it. We hope you will continue to apply. The correct answers are as follows.

Many of you who see this will slap your knees and say, "Oops!" Many people correctly replaced the roof tiles, but unfortunately, it was not the correct answer, so you were unsuccessful.
