50 Crippled People Hold Tomb Completion Ceremony for Beggar Leader

On April 5th, at the public cemetery in Jinae-ri (池內里), Ido-myeon (二道面), Eunryul-gun (殷栗郡), Hwanghae Province (黃海道), about fifty beggars, men and women, young and old, with various physical impairments such as crippled arms and legs, gathered to tend to the tomb of Kim, who had been buried the previous winter, and held a memorial service. Kim, the protagonist of the tomb, was the leader of the beggars scattered throughout the various counties of Haeseo (海西各郡) and had received considerable respect from the general beggar population. He died in that region last winter and was buried in the local public cemetery. Because it was winter, the tomb completion had not been fully carried out. They held the ceremony on the day of Cheongmyeong (淸明節) to tend to the tomb. This has become a major topic of conversation among the general public. <20>

황해도 은률군 이도면 지내리(黃海道 殷栗郡 二道面 池內里) 공동묘지에는 지난 사월오일에 팔병신 다리병신 등 신체가 온전한 사람이 별로 업는 남녀노소의 걸인 오십여명이 모여들어 작년 겨울에 매장한 김모분묘에 때를 주며 추모제를 거행하엿다는데 그 분묘의 주인공되는 김모는 해서각군(海西各郡)에 널려 잇는 걸인들의 단장으로 일반걸인군에게 상당한 존경을 바다오든 중 작년 겨울에 그 지방에서 사망하야 그곳 공동묘지에 매장하엿든 것인데 동절이 되기 때문에 성묘가 완전치 못하엿든 것을 이번 청명절을 당하야 그와 가티 혹 때를 하며 추도제를 행한 것이라는 바 일반에게 잇서서 큰 화제(話題)꺼리가 되어잇다.<20>
