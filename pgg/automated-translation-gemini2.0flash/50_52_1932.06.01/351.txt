Prize Question

"Seon-sa-seup-mu-jo-hae-ni-sal-lam-eun eol-ya-kka-get!" This is a scrambled saying. If you unscramble it correctly and send it in, it will become the prize question. Send in your answer in five letters or less.

Prize

Special Prize: One Person - Art Clock
Special Extra: Ninety-nine People - Magazine

Rules

◇ Write the answer on a postcard-sized piece of paper, and clearly write your address and name at the end.
◇ Be sure to cut out and attach the reader's certificate in this month's issue to the postcard.
◇ Be sure to write "Gyeongseong Gaebyeoksa Byeolgeongon Prize Section" (京城開闢社別乾坤懸賞界) on the envelope.
◇ Write "Manuscript Enclosed" (原稿在中) on the envelope, and if you cut a corner of the envelope's opening with scissors, you can send it with a 2-jeon stamp.
◇ It is convenient for everyone and saves on postage if you send multiple entries in one envelope. However, you must attach a reader's certificate to each answer.
