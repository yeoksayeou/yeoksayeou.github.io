The Three Most Recent Major Assassination Attempts in the World
Yun Pong-gil Incident at Shanghai New Park

(Omitted) Yun Pong-gil was the eldest son of Yun Hwang (尹僙-42), residing at 109 Silyang-ri (柿梁里), Deoksan-myeon (德山面), Yesan-gun (禮山郡), Chungcheong Province (忠南), and his given name was Yun U-ui (尹禹儀). At the age of 11, he attended the local public elementary school (公普校) for two years, and then studied Chinese characters (Hanmun) for three years. Afterward, he joined the Woljin Association (月進會) and was active there, and also taught literacy to the illiterate in his village at night school. On February 7th of Showa 4 (1929), he went to Sinuiju (신의주), then to Qingdao (靑島), where he worked at a tailor shop (洋服洗濯所) for a year. He then went to Shanghai, where he worked at a sundry goods workshop (雜品工作所), receiving a salary of 45 won per month. He also participated in the Korean OOOO and, as a member of the Sino-Korean National League (中韓民族同盟), was involved in the Sino-Korean Anti-Japanese Alliance. He participated in this Anti-Japanese Alliance under a certain name, and there were 13 articles of agreement (盟約). It is said that on the 25th, he was going to carry out a major plot as an assassin at the Jingguoshensha Grand Festival (靖國神社大祭) in Tokyo (東京), but failed, and Yun Pong-gil was one of those involved.

In his family, there are his 44-year-old mother, Kim (金氏), and two younger brothers, Pong-deok (奉德-23) and Yong-seok (永錫-16). He married Bae (裵氏-26) at the age of 15, and they have two sons, Mo-sun (模淳-6) and Dam (淡-3).

As mentioned above, the criminal went to Shanghai, contacted certain individuals, and was always planning 0000. On that day, he boldly committed the crime under the instigation of someone behind the scenes. According to experts, the hand grenade he used was not crudely made but seemed to have been manufactured by a skilled professional. There are various rumors about its origin, either China or the home country (Korea), but since nothing has been officially announced, I will refrain from writing about it. When committing the crime, he cleverly hid one grenade in a water bottle and another in a "bento" (벤도) box, disguising them as belongings, so the guards didn't suspect anything and let him pass. Regarding this incident, there are suspicions of numerous accomplices both inside and outside the country, and dozens of Chinese and Russian people have been arrested. Several Koreans, including Yu Pil (李裕弼) and An Ch'ang-ho (安昌浩) in the French Concession (法租界), have also been arrested. Inukai Prime Minister Assassination Incident

Attack on the Metropolitan Police Department

At around 5:25 PM on May 15th, a car suddenly stopped in front of the main entrance on the left side of the Tokyo (東京) Sakurada Gate (櫻田門) Metropolitan Police Department (警視廳). XXXX one person and XXXX three people got out of the car. One of them ordered the three people, and the three threw hand grenades at the General Affairs Division (總監室) on the third floor from the entrance. However, it only caught on the dedicated telephone line and the line broke. When they threw another one, it hit a telephone pole and exploded with a bang, scattering the branches of the roadside trees. As the four were trying to escape, Clerk Nagasaka (長坂) of the Metropolitan Police Department was surprised and looked out the window, firing two shots with a pistol, wounding them, and then fleeing while threatening the gathering petitioners with a pistol.

Following that, around 5:30 PM, two cars, one carrying XXXX two people and the other carrying XXXX, attacked the Metropolitan Police Department again. Two of them, brandishing pistols, broke into the police station (亂入). When someone tried to stop them, they fired a pistol, wounding Mr. Takahashi (高橋), a Yomiuri Shimbun (讀賣新聞) reporter who happened to be there. The other two ran up to the second floor and fired four shots in front of the Security Department (保安部) Public Peace Section (安寧係), smashed the glass window, and leisurely escaped back down the stairs they had come up.

Attack on the Seiyukai Headquarters and Marquis Makino's Residence

At the same time, the Seiyukai (政友會) headquarters in Yamashita-cho (山下町), Kojimachi Ward (麴町區) was bombarded with explosives. A person wearing a XXXX uniform drove up in a car, stopped the car, and without even getting out, threw two hand grenades. One of them exploded with a loud bang, destroying part of the entrance. Around the same time, two XXXXXX also drove up in a car to the residence (邸) of Interior Minister Marquis Makino Nobuaki (牧野伸顯伯) in Mita-daimachi (三田臺町), Shiba Ward (芝區) and threw two hand grenades in front of the entrance. One of them exploded, and a petitioner (請願) police officer was shot and injured.

Attack on the Bank of Japan

At around 5:55 PM that day, a person wearing the same uniform drove up in a car to the Bank of Japan (日本銀行) in Honryogae-cho (本兩替町), Nihonbashi (日本橋). He threw a hand grenade into the courtyard (中庭), which was more than 30 feet away from the area where the carriages turn around, and detonated it. Mitsukoshi (三越) and Mitsui (三井), which were right in front of it, closed their iron gates tightly and were on guard.

Attack on the Prime Minister's Official Residence

At around 5:30 PM, two XXX people came to the front entrance of the official residence (官邸) and requested a meeting. While they were doing so, four XXXX people came and tried to go up to the entrance of the back Japanese-style room (日本室). When police officers Hirayama (平山) and Tanaka (田中), who were guarding the area, stopped them, they said, "Get out of the way!" and two of them shot and seriously injured the two police officers. Two people went inside and heard the family members saying, "Please evacuate immediately." The Prime Minister (首相), who was listening to this, slowly came out of the entrance of the Japanese-style room and spoke to the two people.
"Let me hear what you have to say first, and then shoot me if you want."
At those words, the two hesitated a bit, and the other two ordered, "Shoot!" and then two bullets were fired in quick succession. One bullet penetrated the Prime Minister's forehead, and the other went from his left jaw to his right cheek. The four criminals ran away to Sanno-dai (山王臺) bus stop, concealed their tracks by car, and the two who were at the entrance came close to the Japanese-style room, and seeing that everything was done, escaped through the back door.
The 17 perpetrators of the incidents listed above scattered leaflets at the scene and other places, and rushed to XXXXX and surrendered.

Assassination of the French President by a White Russian

It was May 6th, when the city of Paris (巴里) was freshly adorned with verdant greenery. At the "Salomon Rothschild" Hall (會館), built by Baron Rothschild (男爵), famous as a world-class millionaire, the literary exhibition (文藝展覽會) of writers (作家) who had served in the Great War in Europe was being held for the first time. French President (佛國大統領) Paul Doumer (폴-·쯔-메루) was invited to the exhibition and went to see it. While the President was meeting and talking with the great figure of French literature, Claude Farrère, at a distance of about three meters between the first and second exhibition rooms, a villain fired a pistol at the President, causing the President to collapse on the spot. Claude Farrère was also seriously injured in the upper arm, and the chief of the guard (경무부장) who was guarding the President was also injured. At that moment, Minister of National Defense "Pietri" and Minister of Pensions (恩給大臣) Doumergue (도·리-푸) were surprised by what happened and embraced the President. The perpetrator was arrested on the spot, and the collapsed Doumer was immediately transported to the Beaujon Hospital (뽀-존병원), where he received emergency treatment after being examined by the great surgeon (外科大家) Professor Gosset (코세). The severely injured areas were near the right temple and the right ribcage. Initially, there was much concern due to his advanced age, but the surgery to repair the severed brachial artery (上膊動脈) was effective in preventing bleeding. After a blood transfusion, the President briefly regained consciousness and recognized his wife and family at his bedside and even spoke a few words. Prime Minister Tardieu (탈루듸유) also rushed to his side upon hearing the sudden news, but his condition rapidly worsened, and at 1:30 AM on the 7th, he became completely critical. All the citizens of Paris were agitated, lamenting and grieving. At 4:45 AM, he passed away.

The perpetrator (犯人) is Paul Gorguloff (폴- 보오루구루푸), a Russian, who is undergoing intense interrogation at the Paris Police Department (파리경시청). He spoke with an untroubled attitude, stating:
"I am a patriotic Russian. I am an admirer of Hitler and Mussolini, but I have absolutely not received any money from them." He had two pistols and a blue notebook titled "Confession of Paul Gorguloff, Who Killed the President of France." At first, everyone thought he was a madman, but the results of the investigation showed that this was not necessarily the case. It is not yet clear whether he is a White Russian (白系露人) or a communist, but the perpetrator did not have any personal grudge against the President. It seems that his intention was to disrupt the alliance between France and Russia and to encourage France to declare war on extremists. He also insisted that there were no accomplices in the crime and that it was his sole action. This person, who used the name Paul Fred, was a former soldier who established the White Russian People's Fascist Party (白系露人의 民衆파시스트黨) in Monaco (모나코), and had recently been deported from Czechoslovakia. Considering this, it seems he is not an ordinary person but someone driven by political fanaticism. Regardless of who he was, the assassination of President Doumer, a veteran of French politics and a figure revered by the nation, inevitably caused great outrage among the French people. When they tried to transport the perpetrator to the police station, a large number of people surrounded the car, shouting and trying to drag him out and execute him. During the Great War in Europe, he sacrificed four sons to the country, and having served as the Speaker of the Senate (上院議長) for many years, the passion of the French people for the aged, virtuous President and their anger towards the perpetrator was truly heartfelt. At the time, various European newspapers viewed the incident as a significant event, commenting that just as the assassination of the Austrian Crown Prince (墺國太子) by a Serbian youth (塞維耳) triggered World War I, this incident could lead to World War II. It is true that assassination incidents have been occurring frequently in various countries recently, but since there are few precedents for assassinating the president of another country, everyone is paying close attention. It remains to be seen what the impact will be in the future, but here, I would like to briefly introduce Doumer's biography for general reference.
Paul Doumer was born on March 22, 1857, in a poor family working as servants in the southern ancient city (古都) of Aurillac (오리얏ㅋ), France. At the age of 14, he came to Paris (파리) and studied while working to pay for his education, completing Sorbonne University. He worked as a school teacher and newspaper reporter, and in 1888, he was finally elected as a member of the National Assembly (代議士), beginning his political career. In 1895, he became the Minister of Finance (藏相) in the Bourgeois cabinet, and in 1897, he became the Governor-General of Indochina (印度支那總督). Later, he broke away from the Radical Party (急進派) and joined the Right Wing (右黨). In 1930, he became the Speaker of the House of Representatives (下院議長), in 1912, a member of the Senate (上院議員), and during the Great War, he participated in the Painlevé cabinet. In 1927, he became the Speaker of the Senate (上院議長), and on May 13, 1931, he overwhelmed Foreign Minister Briand (뿌리앙) in the presidential election and took the seat of the President with glory.
He is an old man of 75 this year, and he and his wife, Mrs. Freyssinet (프랜슈부인), had 5 sons and 3 daughters. During the Great War in Europe, all four sons went to war and died. His book (著書), "Letters to My Sons," was written in memory of those fallen sons.

白系露人의 佛國大統領暗殺事件

물빗가튼 신록(新綠)이 꼿도시의 파리(巴里)을 파릇파릇하게 새로 단장한 5월 6일이엿다. 세게의 부호로 유명한 「로스차일드」남작(男爵)의<47> 건설한 「솔로몬 로스타일드」회관(會館)에는 구주대전 때에 출정하엿던 작가(作家)들의 문예전람회(文藝展覽會)가 처음으로 열리엿다. 불국대통령(佛國大統領) 폴-·쯔-메루씨는 그 전람회의 초청을 바더서 구경을 가게 되엿섯다. 대통령은 그 회장에서 불국 문단의 거장인 클로도 파렐씨를 만나서 서로 이야기를 하는 찰나동안에 제일전람실과 제이전람실 새- 약 삼 미돌 되는 거리에 일개 흉한이 권총으로 대통령을 향하야 발사하니 대통령은 그 자리에서 혼도하고 클로도 파렐씨도 또한 웃팔에 중상을 당하고 대통령을 호위한 경무부장도 부상하얏다. 그리고 그 장소에 잇는 국방대신 「피에트리」씨와 은급대신(恩給大臣) 도·리-푸씨는 그 순간에 생긴 일에 놀나서 대통령을 끌어 안엇섯다. 범인은 그 장소에서 체포되고 혼도한 즈-메루씨는 즉시 뽀-존병원으로 운반되야 외과대가(外科大家) 「코세」교수의 진단을 바더 응급수당을 하엿섯다. 중상한 개소는 오른편 관자노리밋과 오른편 갈비대밋 두 곳이엿스나 원래 년로한 탓으로 처음에 만흔 걱정들을 하엿더니 수술로 상박동맥(上膊動脈)의 끈어진 것을 결합한 효과로 출혈을 방지하고 다른 피를 수혈한 결과 대통령은 잠시 의식이 회복되야 머리맛헤 잇는 부인과 그의 가족을 알어보게 되야 몃 마듸의 말까지 하엿섯다. 그 급변을 접한 탈루듸유 수상(首相)도 겻헤까지 왓섯스나 그 뒤 용태가 급작히 악화되야 7일 오전 1시 반에 전연 위독에 빠지매 파리의 전시민들은 물끌듯시 격앙하야 통분 애석하는 중에 - 오전 4시 45분에 그만 절명을 하고 말엇다.
범인(犯人)은 폴- 보오루구루푸라하는 로서아사람인데 파리경시청에서 엄중한 취조를 바드며 태연자약한 태도로 호언하되
「나는 대로서아의 애국자이다. 나는 히트러와 뭇소린이의 숭배자이나 그들에게 절대로 돈을 바든 것은 아니다」라 하고 두 자루의 권총을 가지고 「불란서대통령을 죽인 바오루구루푸의 고백」이란 청색의 수첩을 가지고 잇섯다. 처음에는 그 사람을 누구나 일개 정신병자로 알엇스나 취조의 결과는 반듯시 그럿치도 안엇다. 그 게통은 백게의(白系露人) 로서아인인지 혹은 공산당원인지 아즉 그 본질이 분명치 안으나 범인은 대통령에 대하야 개인으로 하등의 원한을 품은 것은 아니요 다만 불란서와 로서아의 제휴를 방해하야 불란서로 하야금 과격파에 선전을 포고를 하도록 하랴는 음게인 듯 하다고 한다. 그리고 그 범죄에는 전연 공명자가 업고 자긔 한 사람의 행동이라고 호언하얏다. 그리고 그 사람은 포-루 푸레드란 문명을 가지고 전날의 출정군인으로 백게로서아사람(白系露人)의 민중파시스트당(黨)을 「모나코」에 설입한 것이라던지 또는 체크 스로바키아로부터 최근에 국외추방을 당한 경력이 잇는 것을 보면 어떠튼 보통인물은 아니요 일종 정치적 열광에 날뛰는 사람인 것 갓다고 한다. 그 인물이야 여하한 인물이던 불국정게의 원로이요 국민숭배의 인격자인 쯔-메루대통령을 살행한 것은 불국 국민의 큰 격앙을 이르키지 안을 수 없섯다. 범인을 경시청에 호송하랴고 할 대에 다수한 민중이 자동차를 에워싸고 아우성을 치며 범인을 끌어내여 사형을 하자 죽이자 한 것도 무리가 안일 것이다. 구주대전 때에 맛아들로부터 4형제씩이나 사랑하는 아들을 나라일에 희생식히고 여러해ㅅ동안 상원의장(上院議長)으로 잇던 로령덕망가 대통령에 대한 불란서 국민의 품은 열정과 범인에 대한 분개는 참으로 통절하엿섯다. 당시 구주의 각 신문들은 그 사건을 크게 중대하게 보아 전날 써비아(塞維耳)청년이 오지리 황태자(墺國太子)를 암살한 동긔로 세게대전이 이러난 것과 가티 이번 사건으로 말미암아 제이세게대전이 이러나게 될는지도 모르겟다고까지 론평하엿섯다. 최근에 어느 나라던지 암살사건이 만히 발생하는 것은 사실이나 이러케 남의 나라대통령을 암살하는 것 가튼 일은 그 례가 별로 업스니만치 누구나 크게 주목하는 바이다. 그 영향이 장차 어떠할 것은 아즉 하회를 기다려 보와야 알겟거니와 여긔에는 다시 쯔-메루씨의 략력을 잠간 소개하야 일반의 참고에 도리고자 한다.
포-루·쯔-메루대통령은 1857년 3월 22일 불국의 남방 고도(古都) 「오리얏ㅋ」의 빈한한 남의 집 고용사리하는 가정에서 태여낫섯다. 14세 때에 파리에 와서 고학을 하야 소루본대학을 맛치고 학교 교원, 신문긔자 등 생활을 하다가 1888년에 비로소 대의사(代議士)에 봅혀서 정치적 활약을 하게 되엿다. 1895년 부루조아내각의 장상(藏相)이 되고 1897년에는 인도지나총독(印度支那總督)이 되엿스며 그 뒤에는 라지칼(急進派)과 갈니여서 우당(右黨)으로 드러가고 1930년에는 하원의원(下院議長) 1912년에는 상원의장(上院議員)이 되고 대전 중에는 빤루베내각에 참례하고 27년에는 상원의장(上院議長)이 되고 31년 5월 13일 대통령선거전에 외상(外相) 뿌리앙씨를 압도하고 영예의 대통령의자를 차지하게 되엿다.
그는 금년 75세의 늙은이요 그 부인 프랜슈부인 몸에서 5남 3녀를 낫섯는데 구주대전 때에 아들 4형제가 모도 출정하엿다가 전사하엿스니 그의 저서(著書) 「아들에게 주는 글」이란 것은 그 전사한 아들을 추억하며 쓴 것이다.<48>
