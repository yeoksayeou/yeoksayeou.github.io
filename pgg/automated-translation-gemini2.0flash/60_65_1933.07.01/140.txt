그 뒤에 오는 것, 첫번에는 조왓스나 나종에는 켱기는 이야기 (What Comes After: The Story of Pleasure First, and Trouble Later)
鄭五星 (Chŏng Osŏng)
회고·수기 (Memoir/Personal Essay)

After Drinking on Credit

They say that one cup of alcohol is when a person drinks alcohol, two cups is when alcohol drinks alcohol, and three cups is when alcohol drinks the person...
No matter how much it is drinking on credit without paying right away, eventually it adds up little by little. I had to go back at night and do work, so I was just going to have a couple of drinks and stop. My face flushed, and the world seemed dark and hazy.

"Have another drink!"

"Come on, everyone!"

The words sounded particularly cheerful.

Of course, drinking is good! After downing a glass, I picked up a piece of radish kimchi from the side dishes, and looking at the food table, there was short-rib that I had only planned to eat every time I got my paycheck, but never had the chance to.

"Let's stop now."

When my friends said that,

"What's wrong with you? It's not over yet!"

I don't know what wasn't over yet. My friends were just pretending to save face, of course, they didn't really want to stop. After a while, their voices got louder and their nagging got worse. They were drinking without any pretense or hesitation, and before I knew it, they were putting on their hats and getting excited and rowdy. It was obvious that they were in a good mood.

By this time, they were completely drunk. They were all shaking and shivering from the alcohol. The group left, since it was on credit, and the last one to leave lingered behind, whispering with the owner and nodding repeatedly, then came out with a hearty laugh.

The next morning when I woke up - <38>

When I opened my eyes and came to my senses, the first thing out of my mouth was

"Oh, no!"

I could vividly remember drinking on credit, but I had no idea what happened after that.

Several of us were drunk and walking down the street when one of our friends suggested going to a cafe and having another drink, and we all thought it was a great idea and went.

We ordered drinks at the cafe, sat with the waitresses, and started singing and drinking, but what happened after that?

That's right, we must have gotten into a fight. The waitresses grabbed our arms and said,

"What are you doing? What are you doing?"

But it turned into a fight anyway. I don't know who got hit or who hit whom, but after the fight calmed down, we all sat back down, but one person was missing. We looked for him for a while, but he was gone, so

"This is a big problem!"

We must have thought, even in our drunken state. The missing person was the most important person in our group, the one who could get us credit at this cafe. We were too drunk to get up, but we couldn't even if we wanted to. Who had the money to pay the bill?

And then I don't know how I got home at all. My wrist is throbbing, so I must have gotten involved in the fight and done my fair share. When I looked in the mirror, I had two or three cuts on my face. I felt ashamed thinking about my wife nagging me and what people would think, so I pulled the blanket over my face.

"Ugh, I swore I wouldn't drink again, but I did!"

I regretted it, but it was too late. I lay in bed all day feeling sick, and when I went to the office the next day, the phone kept ringing, it was the cafe calling to collect the bill.

It was a whopping 15 won and 60 jeon.

To think that we were just going to have a couple of drinks at a bar that gave credit, as a treat from our friends, who would have imagined it would turn out like this?

Credit at the bar,

Fighting and tearing my clothes, causing trouble, making mistakes in front of people, lying down all day and not being able to eat, getting sick, and then getting stuck with a 15-won cafe bill, it broke my heart. I realized that it wasn't good to just drink and act crazy.

After Buying a Suit on Installments

Still, wearing a gray spring and autumn suit and walking around the warm streets, it makes life worthwhile.

The days are short and getting hotter, and wearing that winter suit until May was not only hot, but also the sweat was terrible, and I was too embarrassed to pull out a towel to wipe it off.

But now I'm wearing a decent spring suit, even if it's on installments, and strutting around admiring myself. From the outside or the inside, it's a 40-won suit, so my appearance is truly splendid from any angle.

But there's one thing I absolutely hate. When I'm walking down the street like it's my world, and I meet the person who collects the installments for the suit, it's not like it's unexpected, but I just want to collapse. My mood can be so cheerful, but as soon as I see that person, I become pessimistic. What's worse, I often run into him when I need to look my best. At those times, I really wish I could just disappear into the ground.

Ah, once, when the cherry blossoms were in full bloom at Ch'anggyŏngwŏn (昌慶苑) and the gentle breeze of the south wind made the hearts of young people flutter? No, wait, I didn't have my spring suit yet! But don't be surprised, I was still wearing an installment suit. Not an installment spring suit, but an installment winter suit.

Anyway, I had some money and took my "sweetheart" to Ch'anggyŏngwŏn, strolling under the trees and by the flowers, and our romance was in full bloom, when that damn enemy saw me from across the way, shaking his tall frame and coming closer and closer!

"Hey, how about that suit payment?"

Inevitably, he'll say something like that as usual, and what can I do? I'll just have to say, "Please, have mercy," with a desperate heart. So please think about it and have some sympathy. If I were to be humiliated like that in front of my beloved, what would happen? How would our beautiful love end in such a sad way?

Now that terrible man is walking towards me. In that instant, I pretended not to see him, even though I did. When things get really desperate, I look around wildly. Since there's no chance of finding a rat hole, I look for a public restroom. I'm going to run in there quickly!

The story is too miserable, so I'll stop there. But is there such a thing as a ten-month installment suit? Buying a suit and paying for it in ten installments? But the one I'm wearing now is really a ten-month installment suit, so what am I supposed to do? How did it happen? The winter suit I bought earlier was a five-month installment, and the one I'm wearing now is four months, so that's nine months, almost a ten-month installment. Maybe I paid two months already? Anyway, I haven't paid much. I haven't been able to pay. So I can't help but feel sorry for that guy who's chasing after me with such determination.

But what can I do? I can't help it. Once, this happened.

I was just about to leave the office when someone came to see me!

"Someone wants to see you."

When the office boy came in and said that,

"Tell them I'm not here."

The office boy's eyes widened as he left. It must have seemed strange to lie when I was clearly there. But I had looked out the window facing the street and knew that it was the suit installment collector coming.

The suit maker went back. But I thought he was gone and left the office after finishing my work, and oh, what am I going to do?

I clearly knew I had ditched him, but the friend had been waiting for me and glared at me when he saw me, wasn't he?

I don't know how I escaped that situation or what I said, what would you do in this situation?

This is also too miserable a story, so I'll stop.

It's good to be able to wear a suit like everyone else, even if it's on installments! But in the end, you end up in this kind of situation.

After Going Astray

Going to drink is good, but going to see the flower-like face of the woman pouring the drinks was also good.

"Please have a drink."

Saying these words while pouring the rice wine, her white and soft arm, and when you looked up, her slender face wasn't very pale, and she had a lot of freckles, but she had a very sexual and attractive face, and her eyes were rolling around.

Everyone who went to drink was lusting after the woman sitting at the counter.

"Where is your home?"

"Do you have a husband?"

"I wish I could have an affair with you."

"She's a good woman."

"If I could take her as a concubine, it would cost a lot of money."

The woman in white sitting and pouring drinks made the customers whisper these words.

Anyway, because of that woman, the bar was very lively, and it was true that people went to see her more than to drink, and there were even people who were in love with her, and even people who came in and if they didn't see her, they would call her out, and if she had gone somewhere, they would eat a few drinks and leave with a sigh. <41>

It was spring. I was walking alone in Chongno when I felt like drinking, so I went into the alley next to me and was about to go into a bar when

"Oh, no."

I thought of that woman and went to the cheap bar where she was sitting and selling drinks. But I entered happily, but the woman was nowhere to be seen.

I thought she would come out soon, so I ate and drank while waiting, but she didn't come out. I felt subtly disappointed. Eventually, I couldn't stand it anymore and

"Where did she go?"

I asked the old man who was grilling the side dishes, but he said,

"I don't know."

"Oh."

I was a bit drunk, and just then the owner's wife passed by, so I grabbed her and asked, and she laughed and said,

"She went to her hometown."

"Why?"

"Who knows? Maybe she went to her husband."

The words that she had gone to find her husband were really disheartening. I felt suffocated and left.

But a few days later, I discovered something truly shocking, I found the woman in a brothel in XX neighborhood.

It was a terrible fact. That woman who had made my heart ache so much was in a place like a brothel...

That night, of course, I sat there and spent all the money I had, drinking. (12 lines omitted from this section in the original)

Everyone, don't be surprised or laugh... I can't even find the time to look for advertisements for remedies for gonorrhea (淋疾) when I open the newspaper every day.

Ugh, this damn disease is such a pain. Can't urinate freely. Can't walk around with strength. The medicine doesn't seem to cure it. When I think about it now, that dreamy and intoxicating scene seems disgusting.

It was good at first, but later it was a disaster! Could there be anything more terrible in the world? I am now a patient with third-stage gonorrhea, and I am in a terrible state worthy of the name. Aah- <42>

그야말로 이때쯤은 잔뜩 얼엇다. 서로들 술이 취하야 푸-푸-하고 떨며 잇는 것이다. 일행은 외상술이니까 먼저 나오고 술내든 사람이 맨 뒤에 떠러저서 주모하고 뭐라고 숙덕숙덕하며 연송 고갯짓을 끄덕어리드니 껄껄대며 나왓다.
