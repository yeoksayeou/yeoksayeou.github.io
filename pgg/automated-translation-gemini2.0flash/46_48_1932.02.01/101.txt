Unusual Customs of Unusual Races (3)

This is King ‘Gajiga Abangura,’ the king of the famous cannibalistic savage tribe living in the Congo region of Africa. His subordinates roam around Matchuri mountain like goats, catching people every day and offering them to the king. The king then divides the flesh of the captured people among his various subordinates, allowing them to live. <25>
