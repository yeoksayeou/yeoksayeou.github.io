Business World Happenings
The 'Dong-A' Department Store and Hwashin Trading Company
When one thinks of "department store" (大百貨店) in Chongno, Seoul, until now, the first one that northerners (people north of the Han river) would mention was 'Hwashin Trading Company' (화신상회). But, as the saying goes, someone better always comes along, so right next to it, they built a large building and opened the 'Dong-A' Department Store (동아). So, Hwashin Trading Company, which had been called the best until now, couldn't hold its head up and became a little flattened.

However, Hwashin Trading Company, not to be outdone, is undertaking a major plan (大企圖) to repair its existing building and extend its store to the side of the Daechang Trading Company, so it remains to be seen how the two will compare when that is completed.

In any case, the owner of 'Dong-A' is Choe Nam, the manager of the former 'Dong-A Women's Store' (동아부인상회), who is said to have achieved today's success with nothing, in other words, a veteran businessman. And the new owner of 'Hwashin Trading Company' is Park Heung-shik, who is said to hold the reins of the Joseon fabric business and display his skills in the Joseon business world. With such figures facing each other across the eaves, the competition will be considerable, which is not difficult to imagine. But, as the saying goes, the shrimp's back breaks in a whale fight, and it has already caused anxiety among the shopkeepers along Jongno who can't even make a few pennies a day, and there seem to be many old men worried. If they are going to compete, instead of consolidating the power of small hole-in-the-wall shops, don't they have the wherewithal to compete against large Mitsukoshi or Jeongjaok?

Anyway, it was noticeable as a gift that didn't forget neighborly loyalty when 'Hwashin Trading Company' donated a congratulatory wreath to 'Dong-A' at the opening and put it in front of the store where customers were entering.

Also, I heard that on the first day 'Dong-A' opened, they closed their eyes and donated 1/100th, or 111 won and 8 jeon, of the sales (賣上高) of 11,108 won to a certain newspaper company for a poverty relief project. Perhaps the new, capable manager will have the ability to set aside about 1/10th and use it for social projects when Hwashin Trading Company's extension is completed and opened.

Anyway, the emergence of even such a department store in the insignificant northern Seoul commercial district can only be seen as a good thing. Hopefully, it will give the opportunity for the footsteps of modern housewives or luxury-loving female students to turn away from the east side of Namdaemun and come this way, which would be even better. <31>

商界往來
大百貨店 『東亞』와 和信商會
서울 종로ㅅ거리에서 『대백화점』하면 지금까지는 의레 북촌의 조선사람에게는 『화신상회』가 첫손가락을 곱히더니 나는 놈 우에 뛰는 놈이 잇다고 바로 그 여페다가 커다란 삘딍을 지어노코 대백화점 『동아』를 열게되니 지금껏 제일이라고 일컷든 화신상회가 머리를 못 처들고 좀 납작해지게 되엿다.
그러나 화신상회 역시 거기에 지지 안흘 대기도(大企圖)로 재래의 집을 수선하는 한편 대창무역회사의 엽구리에까지, 잇대여서 점포를 증축하는 중이니 그것이 락성되는 날이면 서로 비교가 엇덜는지 좀 두고 볼일이다.
하여튼 『동아』의 주인은 재래 『동아부인상회의 경영자 최남(崔楠)씨로서 적수공권으로 오늘의 성공을 햇다는 말하자면 백전로장의 상업가요 『화신상회』의 새 주인은 역시 조선지물상게에 패권을 잡고 조선실업게에 가진 수완을 뽑낸다는 박흥식(朴興植)씨가 채를 잡게 되엿다니 인물도 인물이려니와 소도장도로서 처마를 맛대고 안게 되엿스니 경쟁도 상당할 것은 상상키에 어렵지 안커니와 속담에 고래싸움에 새우등 터진다는 격으로 그러지 아니해도 하로에 몃푼 버리를 못하든 종로연변 소매상들의 공포ㅅ중을 이르켜 노케 되야 저윽이 걱정하는 감투쟁이 영감님들이 만흔 모양인데 긔왕이면 조그만 구멍가개의 세력을 구축할 것이 아니라 경쟁을 해볼 맘이면 커다란 삼월·정짜옥과 한번 대항해볼 뒷심은 더 업는지?
엇잿든 『동아』가 개점하는 벽두에 『화신상회』에서 개점축하의 화환을 긔증하야 손님 드러오는 점포 아페 내세운 것은 이웃의 의리를 잇지 안흔 선물로 눈에 띄웟젓다.
또 드른즉 『동아』가 개점하든 첫날의＜30＞ 매상고(賣上高) 일만일천일백팔원에서 백분일 즉 일백십일원팔전을 눈 딱 감고 때여서 모모신문사에 빈민구제사업비로 주엇다는 말이 잇스니 화신상회 증축락성 긔렴 개점 때에는 한 십분일 뭉떵 때여서 사회사업에 써볼 력량이 수완잇는 새 경영자에게 잇슬지도 모르지.
아모려나 조잔한 북촌 상업게에 이만한 백화점이나마 생긴 것은 조흔 일이라고 볼 수 밧게 업다. 요행 모던 가정의 새각씨님이나 호사 조와하는 녀학생 아씨들의 발자욱이 남대문동편을 덜 향하고 이쪽으로 발을 옵겨드듸게 되는 긔회를 지어주엇스며 더욱 조흔 일이라할까.＜31＞
