The Strange Customs of Strange Races (Part 1)

The sight of women dancing among the natives living near Cape Town, South Africa. Isn't what they wear on their heads just like a *gokgal* (곡갈) [Korean traditional hat] if you think of it as a penis? What's so different from the revue girls' dances that have come from the West these days?

The women in northern Portugal in Europe carry their children on their heads just like the photos show. It's a big mistake to think they're selling children like peddlers in Joseon! <18>

珍奇人種의 珍風俗(一)

南아리카「케이프ㆍ타운」근처에 사는 토인(土人)의 여자들의 춤추는광경 머리에 쓴 것은 좃ㄴ으로치면 곡갈세미지요 요샛날 서양에서 건너온 레뷰-열들의 춤과 그 다지다를 것이무엇잇습니?


구라파에잇는「포류갈」(葡菊牙)나라북의부인들은 자녀들을 사진과 가티머리에니고다닙니다 조선의 광우리 장사셈치고 아이들을 팔러다니는 줄아럿다는 큰잘못!＜18＞
