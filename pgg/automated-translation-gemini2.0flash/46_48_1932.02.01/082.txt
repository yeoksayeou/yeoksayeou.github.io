기사제목	珍奇人種의 珍風俗(二) (Strange Customs of Strange Races, Part 2)
기사형태	잡저 (Miscellaneous Essay)

The women of the "Kaidetsch" tribe (카이뎃슈족) in Australia knock out the front teeth of their sons and daughters with stones when they reach a suitable age. This serves as a sign that their sons and daughters have grown up sufficiently. Therefore, once their front teeth are knocked out, they will no longer receive the treatment of children.

<19>

Among the "Bagobos" (바고보스) women living on the island of "Mindanao" (민타나오) in the Philippine Islands (比律賓群島), they file down their upper teeth with a saw until they are like awls in order to decorate themselves.

珍奇人種의 珍風俗(二)

오-스트랄리아(濠太利)의 「카이뎃슈」족(族)의 여자가 삼당한 년령에 달한 자긔의 아들의 니리를 돌맹이로 려는 것이 니이것으로 그의 아들이 충붆 장성하엿다는 표가되는것업니다. 라서 니리가 지게되면 어린애대접은 안바들것입니다.


비률빈군도(比律賓群島)중의 한섬「민타나오」도에사는 「바고보스」안의녀자이니모양내느라고 웃니는 줄로쓰러송곳가치맨듭니다.＜19＞
