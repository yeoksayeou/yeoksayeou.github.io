기사제목: 懸賞募集 (Prize Competition)
기사형태: 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Post-editing)

Prize Competition

Creative Sijo
We are seeking new creative sijo (創作時調) of modern people.
○ Up to five poems per person
○ A small token of appreciation will be presented for published works
○ There is no deadline

Judge: Karam Yi Hun-gi (考選 가람 李薰崎氏)

Ancient Folk Songs
Each region has its own unique folk songs. We are seeking ancient folk songs (古來民謠) that Korean youths have sung for generations.
(A small token of appreciation will be presented)

Creative Folk Songs
We are seeking creative folk songs (創作民謠) that originate from the lives of modern Koreans. (Adaptations of ancient melodies are also welcome).
○ The number of lines in the title is free
○ A small token of appreciation will be presented for outstanding works among the published works

Judge: Pa'in Kim Tong-hwan (考選 巴人 金東煥氏)＜79＞

Named Entities

李薰崎	Yi Hun-gi
金東煥	Kim Tong-hwan
