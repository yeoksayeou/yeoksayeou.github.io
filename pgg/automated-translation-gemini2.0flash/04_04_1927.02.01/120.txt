A Laughing Matter

Having heard the rumor that her husband went to the theater with another woman,
Wife: "Honey, why did you take my friend to the theater?"

Husband: "I know you went to the theater with my friend last Sunday, so what are you complaining about?"

Wife: "Even so, I made him pay for my ticket (入場券), but did you pay for that woman's ticket?"<44>
