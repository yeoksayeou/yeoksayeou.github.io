Academic World Anecdotes

Kim Hyŏng-pae, the principal of the former Hwimun High School, has apparently been working at the Ch'ungmu-gong Historical Site Preservation Society in the Chosŏn Education Association Hall in Sup'yo-chŏng, but recently, he has expanded from there to establish the Higher Preparatory Institute (高等準備學院). Considering this, one can imagine how limited the future is for Chosŏn educators and also surmise that the wave of unemployment is looming everywhere.

Chang Ŭng-jin, formerly the academic supervisor of the former Hwimun High School and the vice-principal of Kyŏngsŏng Girls' High School, has recently moved to the position of school inspector in the Education Affairs Bureau. There is a funny story about him from his days at Hwimun School (before it became a high school). The gymnastics teacher was sick and unable to come to work, so a gymnastics class in one grade was left vacant. Mr. Chang apparently had no homeroom duties at that time, so he called the students out to the schoolyard and had them do gymnastics training that he had never done before.
"Attention!" "Number off!" "Right face!" "Forward march!" went well, but he didn't know "About face forward march" and "Halt!". The students marched, but they approached the end of the sports field and the order to go out onto the street was not given. The students were puzzled. Then the order "Ji!!" was given in a fluster. The students hesitated, confused by this unprecedented command "Ji!!". One student asked Mr. Chang,
"Teacher! What is Ji-?"
"Ji!! Don't you know the stop (止) character Ji? I mean, stop marching!" he said.

Pak Sŭng-bin, the president of Posŏng Professional School, has been devoted to his unique research on the Korean language for a long time, and recently, in the name of the school, he has written and published books titled "Essentials of Korean Language Lectures" (朝鮮語學講義要旨) and "Rules for Transcribing Korean into Roman Letters". <26>

學界漫話
전휘문고보 교장인 김형배(金亨培)씨는 그간 수표정 조선교육협회 회관에 잇는 충무공 유적보전회에서 시무하신다더니 요새는 그곳에서 늘러서 고등준비학원(高等準備學院)을 설립 하섯다고 한다. 이것으로도 조선교육자의 말로가 얼마나 갈 데 업는 것을 생각할 때 또한 실업의 물결이 곳곳마다 드리인 것을 짐작할 수도 잇다.

역시 전휘문고보의 학감으로 경성녀자고보 교감을 지나신 장응진(張膺震)씨 최근에 학무국 시학관으로 옴겨 가섯다. 그런데 씨에게는 우수운 이야기가 하나 잇스나 휘문의숙(고보되기전)때 일. 체조선생이 병으로 출근을 못해서 어는 학급에 체조시간이 뷔게 되엿다. 그 시간에 마침씨에게는 담임시간이 업섯는지라 학생들을 교정으로 불러 내리워 전에 해보지 못한 체조훈련을 씨가 하게 되엿다.
「기척!」「번호!」「우향웃!」「압흐로 갓」까지는 조왓스나「도라 우편 압흐로 갓」과 「전대립!」을 몰랏다. 학생들은 행진하얏다. 그러나 운동장 끗까지 다가서 다시 거리로 나가도록 호령이 나리지 안는다. 학생들은 의아하얏다. 그러자「지!!」하는 호령이 황망히 나렷다. 학생들은 이 듯도 보도 못한「지!!」란 호령에 우물쭈물하고 섯섯다. 한 학생이 씨에게 무럿다.
「선생님! 지-가 뭐얘요!」
「지!! 그칠 지ᄉ자(止) 몰라. 행진을 정지하란 말이야.」하엿다고.

보성전문학교장 박승빈(朴勝彬)씨는 자래로 독특한 조선어연구에 몰두해 오시든 바 최근에는 학교명의로 「조선어학강의요지」(朝鮮語學講義要旨)와 「조선어를 라마자로 긔사함의 규태」라는 책자를 저술 출판하섯다고 한다. ＜26＞
