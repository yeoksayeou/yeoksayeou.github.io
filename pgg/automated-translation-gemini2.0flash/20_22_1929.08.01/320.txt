Apology
We would like to express our gratitude for the special kindness of various local colleagues who provided much convenience for the North-South Team reporters who were dispatched for the exploration of the Gyeongui Line and Gyeongbu Line. We are extremely sorry that several of the articles from our explorations had to be deleted and could not be published.
