Manners Cartoon, Modern Daughter-in-law and Old-fashioned Mother-in-law
2. Because she missed the daughter-in-law who hated her and lived separately, the old-fashioned mother-in-law went to see her modern daughter-in-law, but the door was closed, and she saw shadows of a man and a woman hugging and dancing (OO대면서 이리왓다 저리갓다) this way and that. Thinking how outrageous it was, she couldn't even say "Oh, my goodness!" (에그 망칙해라) and ran away. Later, she asked her son, "What kind of outrageous thing is that you are doing?" "What..." "What what, closing the door and hugging..." "Oh, that's a ballroom dance practice." "Now, stop with the ballroom dancing and cabbage fields (배추도고)! Did you insist on living separately just to do that?" ＜113＞
