Review of Beauties Military Drill

A famous, famous general was called before the king and was enthusiastically lecturing on military strategy. The king, however, couldn't get excited by just talk and made a special request, saying, "Ah, a practical drill right now is best!" But since it was within the palace grounds, it wasn't possible to gather soldiers so suddenly. In his haste, the king asked, "How about women?"

If it were women, there were several hundred, and indeed, the entirety of his cherished concubines and favored consorts, a bevy of beauties, were teeming like goldfish (金鮒魚) right there within the palace walls.

"That would be fine," the general, his interest piqued, replied. "Please issue the summons immediately."

Anyway, these were representative beauties, selected and gathered from every corner of the country by this king. Suddenly summoned from this room and that, there were undoubtedly several hundred. Pretty as they were, their chattering was quite something.

"That fellow may have a name, but he's truly a madman."

"But really, what does he intend to do by calling us?"

They were making a racket like new brides under the eaves on a spring day.

"Ahem—" Suddenly, a voice like a cracked bell rang out from the fearsome general's mouth. "Do you all know your own hearts, your left and right hands, and your backs?" he asked firmly.

"As if we'd only know that!"
"Does he think we wouldn't know such things?"
"Hee hee, does he think we're kindergarten children?"
They chattered here and there.

"Alright, then you know. Now, when I give the command 'Forward!' look at your hearts. When I say 'To the left!' look at your left hand. When I say 'To the rear!' look at your backs! Understood?" The explanation ended here, and the agreement with the beauties was made.

The general, holding a drumstick in his right hand, struck it once, shouting "Rear!" and simultaneously commanded "To the right!" At this, the beauties, who had thought it hilarious from the start and couldn't hold back, burst into laughter as if they couldn't stand it anymore.

The general glared fiercely at the beauties and said, "The initial agreement was not thoroughly understood by you, and your failure to follow the command is my fault as the commander." This time, holding the drumstick in his left hand, he struck the drum, "Thump!" and again gave the command, "To the left!" However, the beauties still clutched their stomachs, making no attempt to turn left.

The general's anger finally reached the top of his head. "Alright! If the first command not being thoroughly understood was my fault as commander, then failing to obey the command again, even after it was made clear, is your fault as soldiers!" As soon as he shouted this, he seized the two beauties who were squad leaders and lunged forward to cut them down with a single stroke.

It wasn't just the beauties who were shocked. The frightened king cried out, "Wait just a moment, General! I know well that your military methods are sound, but these are originally women, aren't they? Please be patient. Besides, these two are concubines I hold particularly dear among my countless court ladies. If these court ladies were to die, I wouldn't even be able to eat sweetly starting tonight," he said dejectedly. However, the general wouldn't even listen to the king's words.

"Having become a general by Your Majesty's command, when it comes to commanding (銃御 - ch'ongŏ, lit. gun-control, but here meaning commanding/controlling) subordinates, there are times when even the king's command cannot be heard."

He refused flatly and, with one stroke of the sword, cut down the two beauties.

"This was unnecessary training I ordered," the king muttered regretfully, but he couldn't reprimand the general at all, so pretending not to see, he disappeared somewhere. Regardless, this general selected new beauty squad leaders and conducted the drill with an even more solemn commanding voice, "Right— Left— Forward—" This time, thinking they might die too if they made the slightest mistake, the beauty troops, far from laughing, trembled nervously, awkwardly kept step, and moved solemnly according to the commands, becoming as freely maneuverable as the general's own hands and feet. Then the general sent a person to the king, reporting, "The troops are completely trained, please come and see." The king, who had gone to a corner shedding tears after losing his two beloved court ladies, had no choice but to mutter under his breath, and thus, the remarkable "Review of Beauties Military Drill" commenced. Every hand movement, every footstep, marching back and forth, left and right, perfectly matched the military principles and commands. Only then did the king, anew, admire the general's skill, appointing him commander-in-chief of the entire nation's army on the spot. Using that military strategy, he came to hold his head high among the 16 feudal lords of the Warring States period in China (支邦戰國). That general was Sun Tzu (孫子), the king was King Ho-lü of Wu (吳王 闔閭), and this is the laughable, frightening, regrettable, and lamentable immortal legend from China (支邦), the "Review of Beauties Military Drill." -(End)-<49>

**Summary**

The article retells the Chinese legend of Sun Tzu demonstrating military discipline to King Ho-lü of Wu. When the king requests a practical demonstration, Sun Tzu uses the king's concubines. Initially, the women disobey commands and laugh. Sun Tzu executes two lead concubines despite the king's pleas, asserting military authority over royal favor. Subsequently, the terrified women perfectly execute the drills. Impressed, the king appoints Sun Tzu commander-in-chief, leading to Wu's rise during the Warring States period. For historians of colonial Korea, this retelling of a classic Chinese story about absolute military authority and discipline in a popular Korean magazine from 1926 is interesting. It might reflect contemporary discussions or anxieties about order, authority, and perhaps even national strength or weakness, potentially using a historical Chinese context to comment indirectly on the Korean situation under Japanese rule.

美人觀兵式
有名 有名한 장수 하나가 님금님 압헤 불녀가서 한창 신이나게 兵法을 講義하고 잇것다. 님금님 암만해도 이약이만으로는 흥이 나지를 아니하야 『에라 당장에 實地演習이 제일이다.』하고 특청을 하얏스나 짬맛침 宮闕안 일이니 졸지에 군사를 그러케 모을수도 업는처지라. 님금님 급하신 마음에 『女子는 엇덜가?』 하고 무러보앗다.
女子일 것 가트면 몃 백명, 그리고도 그게 전의 전부가 愛妾寵妃의 美人떼, 目前에 대궐안에서 金鮒魚 뛰듯 오글바글 한터이다.
『괜찬습지요.』하고 이 장수도 귀가나서 『곳 소집령을 내리십시오.』 하엿다.
아모려나 이 님금님, 전국의 방방곡곡에서 가리고 추려온 代表的 美人들 뿐이라. 이房 저房에서 별안간에 모혀든 것이 틀님업시 數百名, 엡부기도 엡부지만 고대신 고떠드느 것이란.
『저자가 일홈은 잇더라만 참 밋친 녀석이여.』
『그런데 도대체 우리들을 불너서 무얼 할심인구.』
하고는 봄날 첨하 끗헤 새색시기 야단내듯 작장구를 치고 잇다.
『그착-』 갑작이 무서운 장수의 입으로부터 깨어진 종소리가튼 음성이 울녀나오며 『너의들은 다각기 재맘과 좌우의 손과 등을 알고 잇지!』 하고 다저무럿다.
『알기만 할리라구.』
『그런 것 모를줄 알고 그러나.』
『히히 幼雅園 어린앤 줄 아나뵈.』
하고 여긔저긔서 짓거린다.
『그래 그러면 아럿지. 자아 내가 압흐로ㅅ 구령을 부르거던 맘을 본다. 좌로ㅅ 하거던 좌편손 뒤로ᄉ 하거던 등을 보아라! 알엇니?』 여긔서 설명은 끗나고 미인들과의 약속도 맛치엇다.
북을 우편손에 잡은 장수, 『등!』 하고 한번 치는 동시에 『우로ᄉ』 하고 호령한다. 그런즉 처음부터 우수워서 못 견듸겟다던 미인Ep는 그만 참을 수 업다는드시 우슴ᄉ보가 떠저 바렷다.
장수는 미인들을 잔-뜩 흘겨보며 『처음 약속이 너의들에게 철저치 못하야 구령대로 안 된 것은 그건 대장된 내의 허물이다.』 하고 이번은 북을 좌편 손에 잡고 『둥!』 하고는 다시 『좌로ᄉ』하고 구령을 불넛다. 그러나 미인들은 역시 배를 끄러안고 좌편을 향하랴고도 아니한다.
장수의 노염은 그만 머리끗까지 올나가게 되얏다. 『그래! 처음구령이 철저치 못한 것은 대장된 내의 허물이지만 다시 구령이 철저 한데도 명령대로 안는 것은 군사된 너의의 허물이다.』 하고 소리를 질느자마자 隊長인 미인 두명을 잡어가지고 단칼에 배히랴고 덤빈다.
놀낸 것은 미인측 뿐이 아니엇다. 겁낸 님금님 『족금만 기다리게 장관! 경의 병법이 확실한 것은 잘 알엇스나 이거야 원래가 여자 아닌가. 참어주게 그러고 이 두사람은 수만흔 궁녀에도 특별히 내가 생각하고 잇는 궁녀로서 이 궁녀가 죽을 것 가트면 나는 오늘밤부터 밥도 달게 못 먹겟네.』 하고 풀이 죽는 소리를 한다. 그러나 장수는 님금의 하는말을 드르랴고도 아니한다.
『저는 왕의 명령에 의지하야 장수가 된 이상, 부하를 銃御하는데는 님금의 명령이라도 드를 수 업는 ²½우가 잇슴니다.』
하고는 단연히 거절하고 한 칼아래 두 미인을 베혀 바렷다.
『이건 긴치 안흔 조련을 식혓구나.』 하고 님금님은 돌탄돌탄 하지만 그 장수에게는 한말도 책망할 수 업고해서 그만 보는 듯 안 보는 듯 하는중에 어대로 드러가 바렷다. 아모러거니 이 장수는 다시 미인대장을 뽑아내 가지고 전보다도 더 儼然한 호명소리로 『우로- 좌로- 압흐로-』 하고 練兵을 식히는데 이번이야 말로 깟닥하면 또 죽는 판이 다하고 미인군들, 웃기는 커녕 벌벌 떨면서 엇둘 엇둘 발을 맛추어 으젓하게 구령대로 동작하야 장수의 손발가티 자유자재로 놀니게 되얏다. 그래 장수는 님금님에게 사람을 보내여 『군대가 완전히 조련되얏스니 와 보시라.』고 전햇더니 그중 사랑하는 두 궁녀를 일코 한편 구석에 가서 눈물을 흘리고 잇던 님금님도 할 일업서 속소리로 두덜거리며 여긔서 기발한 미인관병식이 열녓다. 손 한번, 발 한번, 좌우전후로 行步起伏 하는 것이 병법대로 구령대로 턱턱 마저 나가닛가 그제야 님금님도 새삼스러히 그 장수의 수완에 탄복하야 그 당장에서 일국 전군의 도대장을 삼고 그 병법을 써서 支邦戰國 16제후간에 머리를 번쩍 처들게 되얏는데 그것이 장수로는 孫子 님금으로 吳王 闔閭로서 우숩고 무섭고 앗갑고 원통한 千古不朽의 支邦傳說 『美人軍 觀兵式』이다. -(끗)-＜49＞
