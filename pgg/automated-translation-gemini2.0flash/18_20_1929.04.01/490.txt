발행일	1929년 04월 01일
기사제목	우리아기 (Our Baby)
필자	春園
기사형태	시 (Poem)

Our Baby
						Chunwon

Yesterday was a good day, the day our baby was born.
There is no shade for ninety li (나무리 90리).
Tonight, the snowstorm rages at Surijae (수리재),
But our baby rests comfortably in Hanyang (漢陽).

As our baby goes, the spring breeze blows,
As our baby sleeps, the waves also sleep.
Wherever our blessed baby goes,
He gives peace and joy to the world. (End) <145>

**Named Entities**

春園	Chunwon
漢陽	Hanyang
수리재	Surijae
