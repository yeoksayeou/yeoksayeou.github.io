Prize Question

One, Han Ŭn Pyǒl Ko Ik Chung Jap Mi Kǒn Dae Cha Ha Kon Il Ji It Chǒn Je Yu
This is a long sentence with the order mixed up, so please correct it and send it in a way that makes sense.

Special Prize: One person, Art Desktop Clock
Regular Prize: Ninety-nine people, one book each

Regulations
◇ Write the answer simply on a postcard, and be sure to write your address and name at the end.
◇ Be sure to write "Kyǒngsǒng Kaepyǒksa Pyǒl Kǒn Gon Hyǒnsang Gye" on the envelope. (Other manuscripts or other prize answer can be enclosed together)
◇ Write "Manuscript Enclosed" on the envelope and cut one corner of the envelope with scissors, you can send it with a 2 jeon stamp.
◇ Send it within 20 days.
◇ It is convenient and saves on postage if you send several people's entries in one envelope. This time, we have decided not to attach a reader card.

Notify Us of Your Address and Name

New Magazine Delivered Free of Charge
Anyone who simply writes down their address and name and notifies "Kyǒngsǒng Kaepyǒksa", will receive a new magazine called "New Economy," which increases knowledge and informs about world news, for free. Please tell your acquaintances a lot, but please make sure the notification is by return postcard.

懸賞問題
一, 한은별고익중 잡미건대자하곤일지잇천제유
이것은 기다란말한마디를 선후를 석거논것이니 바로 잡어서 말이 되게 적어보내시오.

特賞一人 美術卓上時計
普通賞 九十九人 書籍一册式

規定
◇엽서에다 대답을 간단이 쓰시되 그 헤 자긔주소와 성명을 쓸 것.
◇피봉투에는 반듯이 京城開闢社別乾坤懸賞係라고 쓸것. (다른 原稿나다른 懸賞答案과 同封해도 可)
◇피봉에는 原稿在中이라고 쓰고 봉투주둥이의한귀통이를 가위로 비면 二전우표를 부처서 올수잇습니다.
◇二十日以內로보낼 것.
◇되도록 한봉투안에여러사람의 것을 너어보내시면 피차간에 편리하고 우표갑슬절약할수잇습니다 이번은 독자증을 안부치기로 햇습니다.

住所姓名通知하라

新雜誌無代進呈
누구든지 自己의 住所姓名만 이적어서 「京城開闢社」로 通知하시면 知識늘고 세상 消息아는 「新經濟」라는새雜誌를 거저보내드립니다. 아시는 친구에게 만히 말슴하야주시되 通知는 반드시 往復葉書로하라하십시오.
