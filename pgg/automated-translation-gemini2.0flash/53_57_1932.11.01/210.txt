Gaeseong Night Tales, A Tail of Following Gisaeng
						Songaksanin

Songdo is not a modern city.

Buildings made of stone, iron, concrete, and factory chimneys are huge and fixed in a modern style, but it remains a conservative city with an old-fashioned atmosphere.

The fierce sound of sirens and the wheels of streetcars are unheard of, and even the disorderly and strange sounds of running cars are rare. The exploitative bus management system of 10 jeon per zone cannot but be called 10 to 100 times the unearned income compared to 5 jeon from Dongdaemun to Hangang Bridge in Gyeongseong. In today's world where scientific mechanical civilization is developing at a high speed, transportation in Gaeseong is like this, so what about other things....

There is no place to apply new words such as "Robot," "Mannequin," "Department," "Neon Sign," "Illumination," "Asphalt," and "Pavement," and it is a city without even a decent "Show Window" to attract the eyes of passersby. However, while modern cities are all artificial, Songdo is extremely natural, with flowers planted in every house and fresh air to breathe. To sketch the night scenery of such a natural city, an old-fashioned city - a city without even a "cafe" or "restaurant" - one cannot help but deal with the existence of gisaeng.

On the night of the full moon when the moon is silently bright, the time is good and the heart flows with subtle emotions that cannot be grasped, but there is no way to taste pleasure and joy, and even walking in the countryside and appreciating the cheerful moonlight becomes tiresome, so with a novel suggestion to disguise and explore, C and I made a "plan" and went to the dark side of Songdo - the red-light district - at ten o'clock at night, wearing caps and sunglasses and dragging a stick.

It is said that there are a total of about 100 gisaeng belonging to the two geonbeon (權番) in Gaeseong's night district, so it is impossible to visit them all one by one, and even if we did visit, it wouldn't be very interesting, so we decided to walk around the entire Seobonjeong area to find a character to introduce.

Park Seon-cho

As I tried to go up along the Seobonjeong stream, reminiscent of the Teahouse Town (茶屋町) riverside in Seoul, a tall and slender woman with a good figure and a pretty face (in the moonlight) appeared from the opposite side, fluttering her colored skirt. She stared at our group and passed by, but the subtle scent of a woman mixed with powder gave a strange impulsive tickling to the five senses of a hungry bachelor. I poked C, who was standing next to me, and asked,
"How about it?"
"That's Seon-cho (仙草 假名)," he says.
"Then do you know that woman well?"
"Not really."
"Anyway, let's follow her."
According to C, Seon-cho attended Seoul Jung X Boheonschool (中X 保育學校) 4 years ago, and she was pretty and talented, becoming an object of burning desire for many men. However, like a lovely pink rose that leaves an ugly sight after being struck by a storm, she was tricked by the sweet words of B or K, who were teachers at the school, and provided her chastity, which is an absolute and primary condition in the egg of capitalism, and then, as if throwing away worn shoes, she repeatedly fell into depravity, wandering around Cheorwon and Gongju, and finally became a gisaeng and rolled into Songdo.

The fact that she is beautiful, talented, and even attended the nursery department (保育科) makes her a unique existence among the outdated gisaeng, so a small number of Gaeseong intellectuals compete to call her, and the competition becomes fierce, so it is ridiculous how many victims or injured will come out.

Seon-cho, who is surrounded by wealthy men, may not be affected by the economic downturn, but as a frail woman, isn't she tired of sweetly accepting the flirtations of many men and selling her charm, coquetry, smiles, and flesh?

As I slowly followed Seon-cho and circled around the front of her gate, Mr. H, who is over 60 years old, was shaking Seon-cho's gate. Since he knows me well, I wanted to stop following and pretend not to know him, but as the investigator is responsible, I couldn't help but eavesdrop.

From inside the room, coquetry, laughter, and the slow voice of old Mr. H occasionally leaked out. Standing behind a paper door and eavesdropping, I had these thoughts in my head.

Does she really have love for old Mr. H? Isn't it simply because of money? What is her feeling of selling smiles that are not in her heart for money, money?

It is said that Seon-cho is pregnant with Mr. H's child. When Mr. H heard this directly from Seon-cho, he was greatly shocked and jumped up, saying that it was inappropriate for a 60-year-old and offered her 2,000 won, 3,000 won, 5,000 won ... ... 20,000 won to get rid of it, so Seon-cho! Before your true colors are revealed and you lose everything, you should take it ... ... .

Kim Wol-seon

Coming back out of the alley and walking along the same stream, 전망 (jeonmang - to command a view) of the blue sky and bright moon, a woman with a plump figure and a plausible physical beauty passes by. Although it was night, her big eyes, nose, face, style, and jade-like hands were well harmonized, and the way she was wearing a purple skirt and jacket made her look like an 8 out of 10 at first glance. With the expectation that I could get a material, I followed her, and as if she noticed that she was being followed, she turned around and looked at us, and her smile was perfect.

She turned into an alley and entered a dead-end alley, then entered a fancy new house with a large gate and locked the gate. Instead of being disappointed, I took out the battery I had prepared in advance and shone it on the gateplate, which read Kim Wol-seon (金月仙- 假名) - the most successful girl in the XX geonbeon. Although I have little experience in investigation, I have become bold, so I grabbed the bolt rope that came out of the gate and pulled it, and the ring was caught. Then, the sound of shoes came from behind, so I turned around and saw Mr. B, a heavy hitter in the Gaeseong business world and the president of X Company.

We had no time to hide and were hesitating under the eaves, when Mr. B, who was drunk and staggering, saw our group and stopped. Perhaps misunderstanding that he had been deprived of his preemptive rights, he muttered and sadly turned away, his two shoulders drooping under the electric light. I felt like I had lost a good fish in my net, and I was extremely frustrated.

As I was urinating in the gutter, I heard the sound of shoes coming into the alley again, so I quickly entered the neighbor's gate that was slightly open and looked around. Mr. R of the Gaeseong county office appeared, adjusting his fly.

"A big catch!" I whispered in C's ear, and C also laughed.

Mr. R shakes the gate and shouts,
"Wol-seon! Wol-seon!" He calls Wol-seon three or four times, but there is no answer. Finally, on the fifth time, the mother's indifferent voice is heard, and unexpectedly,
"She's not here."
and brushes him off. My curiosity is rising 100 percent.
"Mother! Open the door, open the door..." Mr. R's voice is without any strength. Since he already stepped on the banana peel, he should have slipped earlier to avoid being embarrassed.

"Come back tomorrow night," the mother finally says. Looking at Mr. R turning away,
"Don't be intoxicated with decadent pleasures, but be ashamed of your ignorance and be friendly with your desk... and look down on the deformed reality and have understanding and awareness..." I wanted to shout.

Around the time when Mr. R turned away, Mr. Y, a handsome man and sportsman, entered with a cheerful walk, so we listened carefully, expecting to see him get taken again, but unexpectedly, the door opened quickly and the woman who greeted him was Wol-seon herself. After they locked the gate and went inside, I looked at C and asked, "It seems like they had a prior appointment?" and he shook his head.
"Then?"
"It seems like a patron..."
"You're very knowledgeable in that area..."
"Hey, you... Anyway, it seems like Wol-seon doesn't lack fame, status, or wealth..." he says meaningfully.
"He takes everything from those who have it and gives pleasure to those who don't, so it's the same kind..."
Anyway, we wished the two of them a peaceful night.

The night grows deeper. The night of an unknown other world is now growing deeper.

The melody of a phonograph (留聲機) was flowing out from Wol-seon's window, breaking the silence.

Li Haejung-wol

With the thought that I might find something to investigate if I went to the theater, I bowed to the ticket seller and "passed" to the second floor and sat down. The teenagers sitting side by side with their knees together, cracking watermelon seeds, came into view. Among them, the most plausible one was named Haejung-wol (海中月- 假名).

She had her bangs cut at the beauty salon and styled her disheveled hair. Her prominent nose, round and flat pale face, white teeth revealed whenever she smiled, deliberately inlaid gold teeth, charming body, mottled skirt, and every move filled with charm and craftiness would have melted the hearts of young men.

Waiting for the movie to end, I followed her. Three vagrants followed her - walking alongside her, laughing and poking each other among the bankers... They entered a secluded alley, crossed a ditch, opened a gate, and went inside.

We peeked through the gate and saw a small house with two rooms in the main building and a sarangbang (사랑방 - guest room) on the head.
"It's cold, please come inside."
"Mr. S, you go inside... we're going to slip anyway."
"Oh, come on, you guys, we came together..."
"Stop talking and go to bed... It seems like the lady has already spread the blankets..."
"Oppa is just..."
"Don't do that. Hey, Kimsang is really..."
"Okay, we're leaving. Good night, Mr. S."
"Well, I'm sorry..."
"Not at all..."
"I'm sorry. Goodbye... Come visit again."
"Yeah..."

Mr. S, who fell into Haejung-wol's house, was fixed, but where are the other friends going? With this doubt, I decided to follow the two people who left the gate. As we imagined, they didn't break up and passed in front of Daeseonggwan (大成館), crossed the stream, circled around the Euljong (乙種) houses, and entered a decent tavern near Nambu Burojeong (南部 不老亭) restaurant. Seeing them harmonize, it seemed like their limbs were twisted and they were trying to drink. Since I didn't think I could get any more special material from them, I came back to Haejung-wol's house and saw that the lights were off and only peaceful breathing was heard...

Mr. S, who is dreaming sweet dreams in Haejung-wol's soft arms, doesn't know how deep the night is, but how can our clumsy pen write about the heart of his wife, Mrs. K, who is waiting for him to return from the bank and is suffering after six months of married life? How many times would she have cursed the disorder and indifference of heaven and lamented her body, which was no different from a sacrifice on a bewitched night, and how much would she have plotted to escape from the traditional male-centered morality, that is, the doll-like family, and become free! Not only is she a wife, but also a wage earner who receives a salary of 4 or 50 won is having an affair every day, so Mr. S, are you not going to clear your past slander and at least make your position as a banker solid?

Since Haejung-wol's words are better, let me tell you that a wealthy man in Gaeseong, Mr. X, compensated her with 2,000 won. However, Haejung-wol, who melted Mr. X with her charm, kindness, and vixen's mouth, has now dumped X. Since the gentleman can't receive the compensation due to his face, he only dug in vain,

Before getting 2,000 won from Mr. X, there is a famous anecdote. Last winter, she complained about her situation to a delicious 12-year-old middle school student at a restaurant, and the extremely moved 20-year-old - no, 12-year-old boy sent a telegram to his hometown asking them to send 2,000 won. However, his family mistook "2,000 won" for "20 won" and replied,
"XX! Are you studying hard? Seeing that you sent a telegram, it seems like you are short on money, so we are selling the last remaining cow and sending you 20 won, so please study hard..." As a result, the romantic dream of the boy and Haejung-wol vanished. If it goes on like this, how will the world turn upside down?

開城 夜話, 妓生尾行의 尾行記
						松岳山人
송도(松都)는 근대적 도시가 아니다.
돌, 쇠, 콩크리드제의 건물, 시가공장 연돌가튼 것이 근대식으로 거대화, 고정화 긔게화(巨大化, 固定化, 機械化)를 등지고 잇는 보수적 옛 풍도의 도시 그대로이다.
싸이렌의 사나운 소리, 전차의 바퀴소리는 고사하고 달리는 자동차의 무질서한 괴상한 소리조차 흔치 못하며 한 구역 10전의 폭리적 뻐스 경영조직은 대경성 동대문에서 한강교까지 5전임에 비하야 실로 10배 100백의 불로이득(不勞利得)이라 하지 안흘 수 업다. 과학적 기게문명이 고속도로 발전되여 나가는 오늘에 교통기관이 이러한 개성(開城)이니 다른것이야 ....
「로봇트」 「마네킹」 「데파트맨트」 「네온싸인」 「일류미네슌」 「아스팔트」 「페프멘트」가튼 새 말은 적용할려야 적용할 곳이 업스며 행인의 안목을 유인하는 「쇼원드」하나 변변한 것이 업는 도시다. 그러나 근대적 도시가 모다 인공적임에 비하야 송도만은 극히 자연적이어서 집집이 화초를 심엇스며 신선한 공긔를 호흡할 수 잇다. 이러한 자연적 도시, 고풍의 도시- 「카페」나 「레스트랜」조차 업는 도시의 밤 풍경을 스케취하려면 불가불 기생이란 존재를 상대로 하지 안흘 수 업다.
달이 말 업시 밝은 십오야 날 조코 때 조커니 마음이 것잡을수 업시 미묘한 감정으로 흘으나 향락과 환희를 맛볼길 바이 업고 전원으로 거닐면서 명랑한 월야를 감상하는 것도 실증이 남으로 변장이나 하고 탐사나 해볼까? 하는 기발한 제의로 C군과 「플랜」을 세워가지고 캡과 색안경을 쓰고 스틱을 질질 끌면서 송도의 암흑가- 화류항을 차저들기는 밤 열시엿다.
개성(開城) 야명(夜明) 두 권번에 속한 기생이 도합 백여 명이라 하니 그들을 일일히 방문할 수도 업고 방문한댓자 별로 신통치도 안켓기에 서본정(西本町) 일대를 한박휘 휘- 돌면서 등장시킬 인물을 찾기로 하얏다.

朴仙草
서울 다옥정 천변(茶屋町川邊)을 연상케 하는 서본정 천변을 끼고 올너가려니 탐사자 색안경 안으로 키가 후리후리하고 몸맵시가 잇고 어엽버 보이는(달빗테) 껄 하나이 수색치마를 펄럭이며 마즌 편에서 나타나더니 우리 일행을 주시해 보고는 지나가 버리는데 분 냄새에 석긴 여성의 야릇한 냄새가 굼주린 총각의 오관에 이상한 충동적 감족을 준다. 엽헤 슨 C군을 꽉 찌르며
「어때?」 하고 물엇더니.
「저게 선초(仙草 假名)라네 ...」
한다.
「그럼 자네 저 여자를 잘 아나?」
「잘은 몰나」
「좌우간 대서 보세.」
C군의 말을 듯건대 선초는 4년전에 서울중X보건학교(中X 保育學校)에 다녓섯는데 얼골이 입브고 재조가 잇서 뭇남자의 가슴을 태운 대상물이 되엿섯다 한다. 그러나 고옵게 핀 연분홍 장미화도 하로아츰 엄습해 오는 폭풍우에 여지업시 추악한 꼴을 남기고 말 듯이 학교 교사로 잇는 B라던가 K의 감언리설에 꼬여 자본주의 알에 절대적이요 제일의적 조건인 정조를 제공하고는 헌신짝 가티 그만 거더 채운 것을 시호로 하야 타락에 타락을 거듭하야 철원(鐵原)으로 공주(公州)로 떠도라 다니든 끗헤 필경 기생이 몸이 되야 송도로 굴러왓다 한다.
재색이 겸비할뿐더러 보육과(保育科)까지 단엿다는 조건은 시대에 뒤떠러진 기생들 중에서 한 이채적 존재라하야 소수의 개성 인테리군은 다투어 그를 부르며 쟁탈전이 격력화 한다 하니 희생자나 부상자가 멧명이나 나려는지 가소로운 일이다.
재산가를 끼고 도는 박선초이니 불경긔의 타격이야 업겟지만 잔약한 여자의 몸으로서 뭇남성의 히야까시를 달게 바드며 가진 아양과 애교와 우슴과 살을 팔으랴면 피곤치나 안흘는지!
선초의 뒤를 슬슬 밟어 그의 드러간 대문 압흘 빙빙 돌고 잇스려니까 H씨- 60을 넘은 늙은 몸임에도 불구하고 선초네집 대문을 흔든다. 씨를 잘 아는 나이라 등장을 중단하고 모르는척 피해주고 십것만 탐사가 책임이니만치 엿보지 안흘 수 업섯다.
방안에서는 아양과 우슴과 그리고＜50＞ 늙은 H씨의 느린 음성이 간간이 새어나올 때 판장 하나를 격하고 서서 엿듯는 나는 이러한 생각이 머리에 떠올렷다.
과연 그가 늙은 H씨에게 사랑이 잇서 그럴까? 단순히 돈 때문이 아닐까? 돈, 돈을 위하야 마음에 업는 우슴을 파는 그의 심정은 어떠할는지?
선초는 H씨의 애를 배엿다 한다. 이 말을 선초의 입으로 직접 들은 H씨 대경실색하야 펄쩍뛰며 60 늙은이로서 가당치도 안타고 부정타 못해 2천원, 3천원, 5천원 ... ... 2만원을 주께 떠러지라고 한다 하니 선초군! 본색이 탄로되어 궤도구럭도 다 일키전에 바더둠이여 하오 ... ... .

金月仙
골목을 되돌아 나와 예의 천변을 거닐면서 푸른 하늘, 밝은 달을 전망하고 잇스려니까 오동통한 몸집에 육체미가 그럴듯해 보이는 여자가 지나간다. 밤이엇스나 큼직한 눈, 코, 얼골, 스타일, 옥가튼 손이 잘 조화된데다가 보라빗 치마저고리를 입은 폼이 언뜻보아 8점이상이다. 이만하면 재료를 하나 어들 수 잇겟지 하는 기대를 가지고 그의 뒤를 밟으니 뒤를 밟힘을 눈치채엿는지 우리를 작고 도라다보며 살짝 웃는 그 얼골에 애교야말노 만점이다.
골목을 도라 막다른 골목으로 드러스더니 큰 대문 달린 번뜻한 새집으로 드러가며 대문 빗장을 잠거버린다. 실망할게 아니라 하고 미리 준비하엿든 건전지로 문패를 빗처보니 김월선(金月仙-假名)- XX권번에서 제일 잘 풀린다는 아가씨다. 탐사에 경험이 적으나 대담하여진 우리는 박그로 나온 빗장 줄을 잡어다러 보니 고리가 걸엿다. 그러자 등뒤에서 저벅저벅하는 구두소리가 남으로 홱 도라서 보니까 우리 압헤 나타나는 사람은 의외에도 개성실업게의 중징이요. X주식회사 사장인 B씨.
