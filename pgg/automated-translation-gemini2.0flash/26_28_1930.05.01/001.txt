Flower Sijo
Flower Compilation
The peony is the king of flowers, the sunflower is a loyal subject, the chrysanthemum is a hermit, and the plum blossom is a poor scholar. The gourd flower (匏花) is an old man, and the carnation (石竹花) is a young boy. The apricot flower is a shaman, and the crabapple is a courtesan. Among them, are the red peach, the green peach, and the tricolored peach blossoms romantic gentlemen?

Spring Night
Because the spring night is painfully short, I wake up from my daytime nap and open the bamboo window. In the night, with the sound of wind and rain, how many flowers have fallen? Child, don't sweep. Even fallen flowers are still flowers.

Peach Blossom Waters
Having heard about the waters on both ends of Mt. Turyu (頭流山) for a long time, now I see them. In the clear water with peach blossoms floating, the mountain's shadow is reflected. Boy, is this Wuling? I am a fisherman.

Oh Butterflies
Oh butterflies fluttering in the small garden's flower bushes, savor the fragrance and don't land on every branch. In the sunset, the mischievous spider is setting its web and peeking. <1>
