Fragment
						Kim Hwa-san

On the white snow-covered pavement
Writing and erasing all night long
Erasing and writing sad stories

The wind howls and passes by
The neighboring man goes silently (뭇흐로) XX
Young children XX XX and cry XX XX and cry. (1930.1.16) <124>
