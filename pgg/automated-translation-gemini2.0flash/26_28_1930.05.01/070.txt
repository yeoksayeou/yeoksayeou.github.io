Article Title: 메리듸트의 結婚觀 (Meredith's View on Marriage)
Author: Not specified
Article Type: 잡저 (Miscellany)

George Meredith (1838-1907), the English poet and novelist, said, "Marriage should be done in periods of 5 years. If, after living together for the first 5 years, they think they cannot live together, they should make a new promise for another 5 years, making it the second term. In this way, they should live with a new contract every 5 years, and when the couple reaches 50 years old, they should not be allowed to separate." ... ... so. <28>

메리듸트의 結婚觀
