You Are Needlessly Angry
A terribly cross-eyed judge sat down three criminals, Gap, Eul, and Byeong.

Judge: "Gap, what is your name?" But his eyes were directed at Eul.

Eul: "Yes, it's XXX."

The judge, hearing Eul answer the question he asked Gap, shouted, "You rascal, did I ask you?" Then Byeong said, "You are needlessly getting angry. I didn't say anything." <63>

**Named Entities**

甲	Gap
乙	Eul
丙	Byeong
