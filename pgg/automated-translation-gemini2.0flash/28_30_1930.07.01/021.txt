기사제목	慶州人士의게 (To the People of Gyeongju)
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Notice · Editor's Note)

To the People of Gyeongju

As you all know, this year is the 10th anniversary of our company's founding, and we have been widely recruiting branch offices in various locations both domestically and internationally. Most major locations already have branches. However, we regret that Gyeongju, a famous scenic spot in Korea renowned even overseas, does not yet have a branch office of our company. Therefore, we are now making a special announcement. We hope that those who are interested will consult with our Business Bureau by the end of this month. <7>

**Named Entities**

慶州	Gyeongju
