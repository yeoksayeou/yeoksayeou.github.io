기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editor's Note)

New and Old Magazine Exchange

Due to the unprecedented popularity of Byeolgeon-gon (別乾坤) "Issue 5," "Issue 7," and "Issue 11," they were sold out immediately upon publication. Recently, we have received special orders from fellow Koreans residing overseas. Therefore, we hereby announce that if anyone has the aforementioned issues of the magazine, please send them to our company. We would be extremely grateful and, at the same time, will exchange them for this month's new magazine or any issue you desire. However, the deadline is July 15th.
(Pages that have fallen out are invalid)
