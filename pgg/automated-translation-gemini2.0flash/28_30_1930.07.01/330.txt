濯足 (Soaking Feet; Poem)
로아
기사형태: 시

Soaking Feet
						Roa

Turning coarse stones and digging in the sand,

The flowing stream, the flowing sound,
I want to send my heart even a thousand li.
Beyond a thousand li, my hometown and mother's grave,
Even in front of it, the stream flowed...

Half-ripe fruit is open in the water,
A young minnow nibbling on it,
The water moss flicks its tail, has it hidden?
I am washing my feet, don't be surprised.
I'll pick ripe peaches for you, come out and play.
