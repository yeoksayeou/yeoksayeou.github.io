Special Announcement

A person named Kang Che-hwan (姜齊煥), with an unknown address, is falsely claiming to be "Kang Che-hwan (姜齊煥), a traveling representative handling advertisements for the 10th anniversary celebration of Kaebyoksa's founding" and is soliciting advertisements for our company in areas such as Ŭiju (新義州) and Antong County (安東縣). This person is not only not an employee of our company but also has no connection to us, directly or indirectly. Please beware of being deceived.
