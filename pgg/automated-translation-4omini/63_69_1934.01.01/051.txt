**Summary**  

This article provides a comprehensive overview of the historical dynasties of Korea, listing the rulers and the duration of their reigns, from Dangun Joseon to the Joseon dynasty. It also includes a detailed table of the area of each province in Korea. This information is particularly significant as it reflects the Korean perception of their own history and territorial identity during the colonial period under Japanese rule, revealing aspirations of nationhood and a rich historical narrative at a time when Korea was seeking to assert its cultural and historical distinctiveness.
