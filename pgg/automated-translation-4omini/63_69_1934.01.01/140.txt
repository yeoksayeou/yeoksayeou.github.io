珍奇消息  

An explosion occurred from a bomb that exploded after 15 years. This strange incident happened in "Yu-Gosravia," where a bomb and bullets that had not exploded during World War I suddenly detonated after 15 years, killing nine people and injuring twelve others.   

Additionally, there was a case of a person named "Svetcha," who suffered serious injuries when a bullet exploded while he was picking it up in a mountain area called "Gatchi."  

The emergence of track flying machines.   

The era of merely knowing airplanes as something that flies in the sky has passed. Due to the advent of automobiles on land and airplanes in the air, trains are now being practically replaced since customers are gradually diminishing. Therefore, the company "Polman" has collaborated with "Start Aircraft Manufacturing" to create something similar to a track flying machine, and it has reportedly performed admirably in preliminary test flights. <36>  

**Summary**  

The article discusses bizarre incidents involving unexploded bombs from World War I that only detonated after 15 years, leading to casualties. It also highlights the development of track-based flying machines as a novel form of transportation, reflecting technological advancements during the early 20th century. This article captures a moment of transition in transportation technology and raises questions about the lingering effects of war on civilian life in Korea, emphasizing the unexpected dangers from unresolved wartime remnants.

珍奇消息
15년에 터진 爆彈
이것은 『유-고스라비아』에서 생긴 이상한 일인데 세게대전 때 터지지 안코 그대로 전장에서 딍굴든 탄환과 폭탄이 15년이 지난 최근에 와서 폭발이 되야 아홉명을 죽이고 열두명을 부상식혓다 한다.
그리고 또 한 가지 『스벳차』라는 사람이 『깟치』라는 산중에서 탄환을 줍다가 손바닥에서 탄환이 폭발되야 빈사이 중상을 입은 일이 잇답니다.

軌道飛行機 出現
비행긔하면 으레히 공중을 나르는 것으로 아는 시대는 발서 지나갓다. 륙지에는 자동차- 공중에는 비행긔 때문에 긔차 갓흔 것은 실증을 내여 손님이 차차 주는 고로 『폴만』이란 회사에서는 손님을 그는 한 방책으로 이번에 『스타-트』 긔게제작소에 주관하야 궤도비행긔 비슷한 것을 만들어 내여 임의 시운전까지 맛추엇는데 그 성적이 매우 량호하다 합니다.＜36＞ ＜36＞
