★ How to Order the Magazine  
I feel a bit ashamed to reiterate how to order a magazine, but it is worth mentioning as it often causes great inconvenience to both the subscribers and the company involved. Looking at the current situation, there are still quite a number of people who send their subscriptions via remittance, but also many who send stamps or, sometimes, cash tucked inside an envelope. Sending in this manner often leads to issues such as loss in transit or other errors, so it is highly recommended to use a remittance account (振替口座). It is likely a bit more expensive than sending stamps but is far more secure. This also requires careful writing of your address and order details, or else there might easily be mistakes. (The remittance account for Kaep'ŭk Company is Kyŏngsŏng 8106.)
