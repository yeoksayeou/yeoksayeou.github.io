Single Great Men  

The mathematician renowned for his contributions to algebra, "Arisakgu Nyuton," spent his entire life completely oblivious to love. Once, a woman proposed marriage to him during a conversation, but while he was distractedly smoking a cigarette, he mistakenly mistook the woman’s hand for a cigarette holder, leading to a huge embarrassment and ultimately the dissolution of the engagement.  

If we were to name the most prominent single artist, "Mikeran Jero" would definitely be in the running; he seems to have been born with an extraordinary disposition that renders him indifferent to women. As such, among his several masterpieces, only two or three feature women.  

The great scientist "Kadeitsyu" has a peculiar reputation as an example of a man who is indifferent to women. He lived with a maid in his house but insisted that she always stay upstairs to avoid encountering him directly.  

The highly acclaimed philosopher "Rune Nekaldo," known for the maxim "I think, therefore I am," was so absorbed in philosophical contemplation that he completely neglected thoughts of marriage until he turned well over the age of 50.  

The great economist "Adam Smith" once stated, "My beloved is a book." He never married throughout his life. He left behind a fine offspring in his book, "The Theory of Moral Sentiments."  

  

**Summary**  

This article reflects on the lives of various notable individuals who remained unmarried, highlighting their preoccupations with their respective fields, such as mathematics, art, science, philosophy, and economics. It reveals the tension between personal relationships and professional accomplishments. Historians might find the portrayal of these figures interesting as it mirrors societal attitudes towards marriage and intellectual achievement in the context of Korea's colonial period, offering insights into how intellectuals navigated personal and professional domains during challenging times.

獨身偉人
대수학자로 유명한 『아리삭구 뉴-톤』은 사랑이란 것을 전연 모르고 그 일생을 맛초앗다. 어느때 한부인이 그에게 결혼을 요구한 일이 잇섯는데 이야기 하는 중에 담배를 자조 태우노라고 결혼에 관한 일은 입체 이저바리고 괴상하게도 그 부인의 손을 담배잿터리로 잘못 알어 큰 망신만하고 혼담은 그냥 파괴되엇다고.

독신미술가로 첫손가락을 뽑으면 『미케란 제로』를 쳐들 수 잇는데 그는 선천적으로 녀자를 실혀하는 특이한 성질을 타고 난 모양이드라고... 그러기에 그의 수십가지 명작중에도 녀자가 끼인 작품은 불과 두셋 밧게 안되엿단다.

대 과학자인 『카데잇슈』는 국단의 녀자실혀하는 표본이라고도 할수 잇슬 만치 괴벽한이로 자긔집에서 부리는 하녀와 얼골을 맛대하기도 실혀하야 항상 뒤ㅅ층게로 올너 단이도록 명령하엿단다.

『나는 생각한다. 고로 나는 존재한다』라는 모로에의 하야 유명해진 대철학자 『루네 네칼도』는 항상 철학상의 사색에 골몰하야 안해를 어들 생각까지 전혀 잇고 지내다가 급기야 어들 생각이 낫슬때는 임의 50의 고개를 훨신 넘은 때이란다.

대 경제학자 『아담 스미스』는 『나의 연인은 서책이다』하고 몸이 밧도록 결혼치 안엇다. 그는 그 서책이라는 연인과의 사이에 『當國論』이라는 훌륭한 아들을 후세에 남겨논 셈이다.＜51＞
