主婦手帖  

During this time of year, what housewives need to pay the most attention to is the selection of food items. It is necessary to avoid foods that are rich in fat and to save fat for energy storage purposes. This not only prevents illness but also serves as an energy source that can be stored in the body. 

To achieve this, it is important to primarily consume carbohydrates and foods rich in fats in moderation while avoiding overly fattening foods.

These days, fish are quite plentiful. However, many of us do not have enough discernment to choose fish properly. Let me simplify the method for you. When selecting fish, look for those that have a fresh sheen, the grains of the wood (referring to the scales) should be distinctly visible, and the flesh should be firm to the touch, producing a sound when pressed. 

And one more thing to note is that even if you find good fish, when purchasing a large quantity at once, it is essential to check whether they are cooked evenly. 

Concluding on the topic of fish, I will share one more tip. To use charcoal efficiently in a brazier, consider placing three or four small stones of a suitable size in the brazier. These stones will heat up together with the charcoal, producing additional heat beyond the charcoal's own heating ability, thus providing economic benefits. <39>  

**Summary**  

The article emphasizes the importance of careful food selection for housewives, particularly regarding the choice of fish and the efficient use of charcoal. It highlights the need for discernment in selecting fresh fish, providing tips for recognizing quality. This reflects the domestic and economic considerations faced by women during the colonial period, where access to quality food was vital amidst changing societal roles. Historians might find it interesting how practical advice for domestic life reflects broader trends in colonial Korean society and the pressures on women to maintain household stability.

치운 요 때에 잇서 주부로서 가장 주의해야 할 것은 음식물의 선택입니다. 가튼 갑이면 되도록 피하에 지방을 저축케 되야 치위를 막는대 필요한 것과 그것이 또한 「에네르기」의 급원(給源)으로서 몸가운데 저장되게 하는 것입니다. 그러기 위해서는 제일은 함수탄소가 석긴 음식과 지방질이 만흔 음식을 취하지 안으면 안되는 것입니다.

요즘은 숫이 만히 씨워질 때입니다. 그러나 우리들은 보통으로 숫이 좃코 그른 것은 분별할 줄은 모릅니다. 간단히 그 방법을 아르켜 드리지요. 숫을 볼 때 쌓는 자리가 껌엇케 반짝이고 또 나무결이 분명히 보이고 숫텅이와 덩이가 맛브트러서 석소리가 나는 것이라야 가장  조은흔 숫입니다.
그리고 특히 한 가지 즈의할 것은 아모리 조흔 숫이라도 한새번에 만히 살때에는 전체가 잘 구어잇는지 아닌지를 똑똑이 검사한 뒤에 사는 것이 필요합니다.

숫이야기 끗헤 한 가지 더 아르켜 드리겟습니다. 화로에 숫불을 경제적으로 쓰기 위하는 한 방법으로 화로에는 조고만 큼찍한 바둑돌을 새개 쯤 니어보십시오. 숫불에 그놈이 셋빩아케 달어서 숫불화력 이외의 화력이 더 생겨질 것이니 다소 경제가 되리라고 밋습니다.＜39＞ ＜39＞
