Correction

In the last June issue of this magazine, an article regarding Mr. Seung-bo Lim (森勝波) was published which contained inaccuracies. We are issuing this correction.

別乾坤 編輯部＜31＞ 

**Summary**

This article serves as a correction regarding an earlier publication that inaccurately reported on a person named Mr. Seung-bo Lim. Such editorial corrections highlight the attentiveness to factual reporting even during the colonial period in Korea, and they demonstrate the challenges faced by publications in maintaining accuracy amidst pressure for content. Historians might find the implications of journalistic integrity amid censorship during colonial rule to be particularly revealing.
