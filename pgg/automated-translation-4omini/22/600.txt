Please write your thoughts on a postcard and send it to us.  

The content is without restrictions, so feel free to write anything you’d like to send to our magazine’s postcard communication column. It seems that the distinctive feature of this column is to share whatever comes to mind, no matter what it is.  

 

**Summary**  

The article invites readers to express their thoughts on postcards and send them in, highlighting the freedom of expression and the lack of restrictions on what can be shared. This initiative fosters a sense of community and encourages interaction among the readership. Historians might find the article interesting as it reflects the cultural currents of the time and offers insight into how communication was encouraged within the context of colonial Korea, showcasing a blend of modernity and tradition in the public discourse.
