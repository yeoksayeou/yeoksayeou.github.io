Famed Delicacies of Chungcheong Province: Jincheon Memul-muk, A Tribute to Eight Regional Delicacies  
朴瓚熙  
As various food showcases emerge, let’s talk about the pride of Chungcheong Province's memul-muk.  

In Korea, there is hardly a place that does not eat some kind of muk, but when remembering the taste of Chungcheong Province's memul-muk, one cannot help but feel pity for those who have to settle for what they call 'muk'.  

The muk from other places is often cut into chunks the size of a small pillow, merely serving as a side dish alongside rice, but it cannot be compared to the delicate taste of Chungcheong Province's memul-muk.  

First, in Chungcheong Province, you will find numerous muk shops dotted throughout alleys like in Seoul's taverns or seolleongtang (beef bone soup) shops.  

When you walk in, you will see bowls filled to the brim, and at first glance, it can easily be mistaken for a bowl of noodle soup.  

This is mainly because the memul-muk is thin, just like the noodles. Usually, they are mixed with meat broth to eat, but the unique taste of Chungcheong Province's muk, combined with its elegant seasoning and the flavor of the accompanying meat, is simply exquisite—so delightful that one might not even notice if they were to eat it until their eyes closed.  

On winter nights, taking the opportunity to drink a couple of glasses of anju (side dish served with alcohol) and have a couple of bowls of this dish creates a unique and charming experience. The price is 1.5 won.  

Flavor, fragrance, and charm—indeed, it has all three elements in harmony. <66>  
<65-66>  

**Summary**  

This article extols the virtues of Jincheon memul-muk, a type of food that is distinctly celebrated in Chungcheong Province of Korea. The author highlights how this delicacy stands out compared to other varieties of muk found in Korea, emphasizing its refined taste and the experience of enjoying it, especially during winter nights. The significant cultural aspect of enjoying food in communal settings during the colonial period gives historians insights into the social practices of Koreans at this time, reflecting their resilience and cultural pride in the face of colonial rule.

忠淸道名物 鎭川메물묵, 珍品·名品·天下名食 八道名食物禮讚
						朴瓚熙
다각기 음식자랑이 나오니 忠淸道의 메물묵 자랑이나 하여보자.
朝鮮에서 어데치고 여러가지 묵을 먹지 아니하는 곳이 업스나 忠淸道의 메물묵을 먹은 입맛으로 그것을 볼 때에 져것도 묵이라고 먹는가 십허 가엽슨 생각이 난다.
달은 곳에서 먹는 묵들은 그져 목침뎅이만큼 씩하게 뚝뚝 잘너서 장을 질넘 쳐가지고는 밥반찬으로 한두졋갈 지번거리고는 그래도 묵을 먹엇느라고 한다.
그러나 忠淸道의 물묵은 그러케 운치가 업고 승겁고 툭명스럽지가 안타.
第一 忠淸道에 가면 서울의 선술집이나 설넝탕ㅅ집처럼 골목골목에 묵집이 잇다.
쳐들어가면 한주발 그득하게 갓다놋는데 쳠경보면 국수장ㅅ국인가하고 속기가 쉽다.
그것은 무엇보담 묵이 국수ㅅ발과갓치 가늘은 까닭이다. 비비기는 대개는 고기장ㅅ국에 비벼서 먹는데 그 忠淸道의 묵이 아니면 맛볼 수 업는 固有한 맛에다 얌젼한 양념 맛과 고기만을 가미한 맛이란 그야만로 둘이 먹다가 하나가 죽어도 몰을 지경이다.
겨울밤이 깁흔 뒤에 속이 허츌한 틈을 타서 한두잔 안쥬 삼어 두어그릇 먹는 운치도 또한 그럴 뜻하다. 代金은 一金五錢也라다. 헐키와 風味와 韻致--그야말로 三拍子가 드러마졋다.＜66＞
