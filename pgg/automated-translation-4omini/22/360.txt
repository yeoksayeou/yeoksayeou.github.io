Pride of Daegu: Daegu's Daegu Soup Rice, Treasures, Famous Products, and World's Famous Food: Eight Culinary Delicacies Tribute

"There's no famous dish that tastes good," says the Japanese proverb "kodo wa za." There is some truth to this saying, for famous dishes are often disappointing in taste due to the excessively high expectations we have as a culture that favors new flavors. Additionally, there are reasons why the dish purveyors, seeking profits by leveraging their fame, are hesitant and mixed up, resulting in the gradual banalization of what was once famous.

Nevertheless, despite such circumstances, let us honor Daegu Soup Rice and give it the respect it deserves by trying its taste.

Daegu Soup Rice is originally known as Yukgaejang (육개장). Generally, dog meat is a delicacy considered a nurturing ingredient, which is a common trait among some Koreans. Especially in rural areas of the southern provinces, it is a big event to prepare dog meat when affines visit, as there is a saying that "if the in-laws come, dog meat is prepared." The preference for dog meat and the perception of people who cannot consume dog meat, coupled with recent changes causing dogs to become rarer, have led to the emergence of Yukgaejang. To put it simply, it is a dish made with beef instead of dog meat, and it has flourished, eventually spreading from its birthplace in Daegu to Seoul.

In a pot, they place a generous amount of meat and boil it thoroughly, adding chili powder and sesame oil liberally. The thick soup, with green onions floating on top and oil dripping, offers a savory aroma that tickles the senses. When one approaches it, with its heat swirling up and the red soup drawing closer, one's anticipation is undeniable, causing saliva to accumulate at the thought of enjoying it. In the severe cold of winter, even a frozen face warms up, helping the entire body enjoy the dish.

Ultimately, Daegu Yukgaejang is characterized by the special preference of its local eaters through the liberal use of chili powder and its unique way of cooking. Eating it carelessly can lead to one’s mouth swelling up, hindering romantic interactions, and can even cause one to shed tears.

When I was in middle school in Daegu, after enjoying a famous painting at the "Intolerant" exhibit, I rushed back home in the freezing cold, my hands and feet numb, shaking and running. Entering a beloved Yukgaejang restaurant, I swiftly emptied a bowl and, leaving afterward, fell into deep sorrow from the hunger pangs. I recall how, each time the pot lid was lifted, a steaming, monstrous aroma reminiscent of the breath of some beast wafted up. I remember the owner’s wife, who, wearing a slightly undercooked tomato-seed apron, I unintentionally leaned upon, and I remember dozing off in that state. Even now, I yearn for the Yukgaejang I once had back then during my youthful days.

  
**Summary**  

The article celebrates Daegu Soup Rice, particularly its form as Yukgaejang, detailing its cultural significance, unique preparation methods, and how it has evolved amidst the changing perceptions of dog meat. The author shares a personal nostalgic experience linked to the dish, emphasizing its warmth especially in winter. Historians may find this article interesting as it reflects the cultural landscape of Korea in the 1920s under colonial rule, with food becoming a focal point of identity and nostalgia amidst changing societal norms.

大邱의 자랑 大邱의 大邱湯飯, 珍品·名品·天下名食 八道名食物禮讚
						達城人
「명물치고 맛난 것 업다」 이런 日本 「고도와자」가 잇다. 一理가 잇는 말이니 명물이란 일홈에 홀이여 日常 새맛을 추구하야 마지안는 우리들의 미각이 넘우나 과민한 企待를 가지는 까닭도 잇고 또는 명물업자들이 亦如是名物에 藉勢하야 暴利를 꿈꾸고 우물쭈물 날님으로 주무럭거리기 시작하야 점점 명물이 平凡化하는 것도 한가지 理由가 된다.
그러나 그런 것은 엇젯든 명물을 명물로 대접하야 이에 大邱湯飯을 한번 嘗味해보기로 하자.
大邱湯飯은 본명이 육개장이다. 대체로 개고기를 한 별미로 補身之材로 조와하는 것이 一部 朝鮮사람들의 通性이지만 특히 南道地方 村間에서는 「사돈량반이 오시면 개를 잡는다」고 개장이 여간 큰 대졉이 아니다. 이 개장 嗜好性과 개고기를 먹지 못하는 사람들이 私情까지 살피고 또는 요사이 점점 개가 귀해지는 긔미를 엿보아서 생게난 것이 곳이 육개장이니 얼는 말하자면 소고기로 개장쳐름 만든 것인데 時方은 大發展을 하야 本土인 大邱에서 서울까지 진출을 하얏다.
셔말지기 가마에다 고기를 만히 넛코 곰곳틋 푹신 고아서 울어난 물노 국을 끄리는데 고초가루와 소기름을 흠벅 만히 넛는다.
국물을 먼져먹은 굵다란 파가 둥실둥실 뜨고 기름이 뚝뚝 뜻는 고음국에다 고은 고기를 손으로 알맛게 찌져너흔 국수도 아니요 국밥도 아닌 혓바닥이 딀만치 뜨겁고 김이 무렁무렁 떠올으는 싯뻘건 장국을 대하고 안즈면 위션 침이 꿀걱 넘어가고 아모리 嚴冬雪寒에 언(凍) 얼굴이라도 저절노 풀니고 왼몸이 녹아서 근질근질해진다. 엇젯든 大邱육개장은 죠션사람의 특수한 구미를 맛초는 고초가루와 개장을 뽄뜬데 그 본래의 특색이 잇다. 갓딱 잘못 먹엇다간 입설이 부풀어서 애인하고 키쓰도 못하고 애매한 눈물까지 흘니리라. 내가 大邱서 中學時節에 인토레런스란 名畵을 구경하고 열두시나 되여 손과 발이 얼어서 모통기름으로 벌벌 떨고 뛰여오다가 그때 친해 단이든 육개장집에 들어가서 단숨에 한그릇을 비우고＜67＞ 나서는 그만 食困症에 취하야 서말지기 뚜껑을 열 때마다 무슨 괴물의 입김쳐름 확확 내치는 장국김에 설여서 반만 익은 토마도빗가치 된 주인마누라 무릅을 비고 그대로 잠이 들엇든 일을 생각하면 지금도 그때 먹든 육개장이 새롭고 쳘업는 어린 그때가 그리워진다.＜68＞
