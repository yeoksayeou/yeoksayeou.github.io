기사형태	문예기타 (Other Literature)  

The delicious and inexpensive Jinju bibimbap cannot be compared to Seoul bibimbap, especially with large pieces of meat piled on without care, and the mung bean sprouts being about three inches long compared to the finely trimmed pieces. The white rice is topped with beautifully arranged colorful vegetables, such as vibrant green fernbrake and yellowish soybean sprouts. After carefully arranging various vegetable toppings, shredded meat is added, and rich broth is poured over it before thorough mixing. Finally, a delicately cut layer of yellowish sweet potato noodles goes on top, followed by finely sliced raw meat, and a little bit of spicy sauce is added to awaken the appetites. The aroma wafting from the dish tickles one’s nose and is truly appetizing. Remarkably, this delightful meal costs only 10 jeon. Aside from the recommendation for healthy eating, one can easily avoid a heavy stomach as well. Such a rich and pleasing bibimbap reflects the artistic touch of the young people of Jinju while conveying the spirit of camaraderie and loyalty.

Summary  

The article describes the culinary delight of Jinju bibimbap, emphasizing its affordability and flavorful components, which include a vibrant array of vegetables served over rice accompanied by meat and a spicy sauce. Notably, the text highlights not only the aesthetic and taste qualities of the dish but also the cultural significance it holds for the young people of Jinju, symbolizing artistic pride and strong communal values. Historians might find the intertwining of food with local identity and social values particularly revealing in understanding Korean society during the colonial period.

맛나고 갑 헐한 晋州비빔밥은 서울비빔밥과 갓치 큰 고기졈을 그냥 노흔 것과 콩나물발이 셋치나 되는 것을 넝쿨지게 노흔 것과는 도져히 비길 수 업슴니다. 하-얀 쌀밥 우에 色을 조화식혀서 날늘듯한 새파란 야채 엽헤는 고사리나물 또 엽헤는 노르스름한 슉쥬나물 이러한 방법으로 가지각색 나물을 둘녀노흔 다음에 고기를 잘게 닉여 끄린 장국을 붓어 비비기에 젹당할만큼 그우에는 유리됴각갓흔 黃쳥표 서느 사슬을 노흔 다음 엽헤 육회를 곱게 썰어놋코 입맛이 깩금한 고초장을 조곰 언슴니다. 여긔에 니러나는 향취는 사람의 코를 찌를 뿐 안이라 보기에 먹음직함니다. 갑도 단돈 10錢. 상하게급을 물논하고 쉽게 배곱흠을 면할 수 잇는 것임니다. 이럿케 소담하고 비위에 맛는 비빔밥으로 길녀진 晋州의 젊은이들은 미술의 재질이 만흔 것임니다. 또한 義氣의 烈烈한 XX졍신을 길너주는 것임니다.＜65＞
