Summary  

This article announces the establishment of new branch offices for the publication, specifically detailing the locations and leadership of the An'gang and Gwangju branches. The article reflects the expansion efforts of media outlets during this period in Korea. It is particularly interesting to note the establishment of regional offices as it showcases the push for broader communication and regional engagement during the colonial era.
