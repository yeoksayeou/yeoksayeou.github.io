기사제목	朝鮮語辭典과 朝鮮人 (The Korean Language Dictionary and Koreans)  
필자	李克魯 (I Kyu-ryu)  
기사형태	논설 (Essay)  

The Korean peninsula is desolate in all aspects. However, the field of the Korean language is an even more undeveloped wasteland. Our language, which we use frequently, has never been thoroughly investigated. Just listen to the fishermen by the sea; they possess names for every fish and every type of shellfish. Speak to the shepherds from isolated villages, and you will discover countless names for water, plants, and trees that we ordinarily do not know. All of these words remain confined to their dialects and have not achieved any academic recognition.

It is not disgraceful that we lag behind in natural sciences or crafts, but it is truly embarrassing for a great nation that has lived a cultural life for tens of thousands of years, with recorded history spanning four to five thousand years, to have not organized a language that serves as the foundation for public culture and for expressing its thoughts. One person remarked at a banquet about a foreigner that "You lack a dictionary, yet you speak so boldly!" This insult motivated him to commit to the dictionary project. The author, too, felt profound pain and shame when he attended the Korean language course at the University of Berlin four years ago and found no standard Korean dictionary available. How many others share this same pain? Perhaps some are too thick-skinned to feel shame, but I believe everyone experiences this discomfort to some degree. 

Consider the Mongolian herders in the Gobi Desert or the Manchus hunting in the forests of Baekdu Mountain; they have had standardized dictionaries that they have used for several hundred years. In Silla, some clusters used Korean or literary Chinese characters, while King Sejong of the Joseon dynasty invented Hangul to record the Korean language, resulting in many texts and various literary works. Yet, even at that time, they did not produce dictionaries. Following that period, for thousands of years, ignorant masses oppressed by the authority of classical Chinese have not engaged in the development of the Korean language. Initially, the French missionaries who came to spread Christianity in Korea learned Korean, and as a byproduct, they published the Korean-French dictionary in Paris in 1880. Subsequently, in 1890, the Englishman Underwood published the Korean-English dictionary in Yokohama, and in the ninth year of Taisho, the Government-General of Korea published a Korean dictionary. All of these works were created by foreigners intending to learn Korean. Thus, no publication by Koreans for the purpose of creating a Korean-language dictionary has been achieved to date. However, we are not without effort. Seventeen or eighteen years ago, members of the Gwangmun Association, such as Kim Jaebong, Yi Kyu-yong, and Kwon Deok-kye, began working on a Korean language dictionary and have exerted effort for many years. Three years ago, the Kyemyeong Club was established with Choi Nam-seon, Yi Yun-jae, Jeong In-boo, Lim Gyu, Byeon Yeong-ro, and Han Jeong, all labored for the same endeavor, while Kim Jaebong in Shanghai has collected tens of thousands of vocabulary items over six or seven years. Lee Sang-cheon from Kaesong has also gathered numerous words. Yet, to date, none of these works have been published. The dictionaries created by foreigners only encompass a portion of vocabulary and cannot confidently be called standard dictionaries.

Our dictionary compilation differs in one significant way from others: elsewhere, standard languages and orthographies have already been established, allowing the contents of dictionaries to be merely expanded or condensed. In our case, however, nothing in our language has been finalized yet. Therefore, it is vital to have a unified and authoritative standard dictionary created at any moment. To achieve this, at the very least, a unified organization must be established. This is why we have organized the Korean Language Dictionary Compilation Association. Our undertaking must not be viewed as a first step but rather as reaching a final resolution of the accumulated efforts made by numerous predecessors over the past twenty years. It aims to establish a great authority from many smaller authorities.

**Summary**

This article highlights the deficiencies in the development of the Korean language and the absence of a comprehensive Korean language dictionary during the colonial period, which reflects a greater struggle for cultural identity under Japanese rule. It underscores the efforts by Koreans to rectify this gap despite the historical suppression of the Korean language and culture. Historians may find the discussion regarding the lack of standard authority in the Korean language and the initiative to compile a dictionary particularly revealing as it reflects the cultural aspirations and national consciousness during a time of colonial domination.

朝鮮語辭典과 朝鮮人
						李克魯
우리 朝鮮에는 모든 方面이 다 荒蕪地라할수잇다 그러나그가온대에도 朝鮮語方面에는 더욱 開拓 못된 荒蕪地다 우리가 쓰는 우리의말이퍽만컷마는 누가 그것을 한번 調査하야보앗는가 海邊漁民에게서 들어보라 모든 물고기와 모든 조개들의 이름이다잇으며 窮鄕■村에 사는 椎童牧竪에게서들오보라 우리가 普通모르는물, , 나무이름을 얼마나 알게되는가 이모든 말은 다그들의 헉끄테만 돌아다니고 이죽감이것는 學術的말이 되지못하얏다.＜156＞
다른 自然科學이나 工藝가남보다 뒤떨어진것을 그러케부끄러운것은 아니다 그러나 無史幾萬年과 有史四五千年에 한곳에서 文化生活을하야오는 一大民族으로 自己의 뜻을 通하고 一般文化의 基礎가되는 말하나를 整理못하고 살아오는것은 참아 남의압헤 얼골을 들지못할부끄럼이 아니라 할수업다 어느사람은 한번 酒席에서 某國人에게서『너이는 字典도 한권 업는것들이 무슨 큰소리냐!』하는 侮辱을 當하고 字典事業에 奮鬪하기로 決心하얏다한다 執筆者도 年前에 伯林大學에서 四年間 朝鮮語講座를 맛타볼때에 朝鮮語標準字典이 업서서 참아 못當할 苦痛과 羞恥를 어찌 한두사람만 當하얏으리오 얼골 가죽이 두텁어서 부끄럼은 업슬는지 모르지만는 누구나 글슬때에 苦痛은 다잇스리라고 생각한다 보라 저 蒙古沙漠에서 遊牧하는 蒙古族에게도 白頭山森林속에서 狩獵하는 滿洲族에게도 여러 百年동안 써오는 標準字典이 잇다.
新羅에 群聽이 吏讀文으로써 或朝鮮말을 썻스며 李朝에 世宗大王이 朝鮮文字를 만들어 朝鮮말을 記錄케하시매 經傳과 其他多少의 諺解가낫스며 或創作도 잇엇스나 그때에도 辭典은 만들지못하얏다 그뒤에는 더욱 數千年동안에 漢文威力에 壓伏된 無知한 무리는 朝鮮語開拓에는도 지 못하얏다 처음에 佛蘭西 宣敎師들이 天主敎를 傳하려 朝鮮에와서 朝鮮말을 배호게되매 그 副産物로 그들의손에서 韓佛字典이 一八八〇年에 巴里에서 出版되엿고 其後一八九〇年에 英人 언더우뜨 의 韓英字典이 橫濱에서 出版되엇고 大正九年에 朝鮮總督府에서 朝鮮語辭典을 出版하얏다 이우에 말한것은 다 外人이 朝鮮語를 배호랴고 만든 冊이다 그러니 朝鮮사람이 朝鮮말을 배호고저 쓴 字典은 아즉 하나도 出版되지 못하얏다 그러나 우리도 勞力을 아니하는것은 아니다 十七八年前에 光文會에서 金枓奉, 李奎榮, 權悳奎諸氏가 朝鮮語辭典編纂에 着手하야 多年勞力하얏스며 三年前에 啓明俱樂部에서 崔南善, 李允宰, 鄭寅普, 林圭, 卞榮魯, 韓澄諸氏가 同一한 事業에 勞力하얏고 上海의 金枓奉氏가 六七年來에 數萬語彙를 蒐集하얏스며 開城의 李常春氏가 亦是 數多의 語彙를 蒐集하얏다한다 그러나 아즉 出版되지 못하얏스니 말할것도 업소 外人이 만든 그것들도 多少의 語彙를 刊行함에 不過하고 참 標準字典이라고 말하기는 어렵다.
우리의 辭典編纂은 다른나라에서 그것과 다른 點이잇다 다른나라 에서는 이미 標準語와 綴字法이 定하야젓슴으로 所用에딸하서 字典內容만 伸縮하야 編纂할뿐이지만은 우리말은 아즉 모든 것이 定하야지지 못하야슴으로 어느때에나 統一이되어서 權威잇는 標準字典이 나야될것이다 그러자면 적어도 統一機關을 特設하여야 되겟다 그러므로 우리는 이번에 朝鮮語辭典編纂會를 組織하게된것이다 우리의 事業은 決斷코 첫거름부터 걸어가는것이아니오 여러 先輩들의 二十年來에 努力하야 오는 積功을 總決算하자함이며 數多의 權威로부터 一大權威를새우자하는것이다.＜157＞
