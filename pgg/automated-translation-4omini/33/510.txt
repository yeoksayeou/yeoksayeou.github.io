Special Notice  

The women's magazine "新女性" (New Woman), which has been on hiatus for a while, will be resumed publication by Shin Yeong-ho. During the time "新女性" was in circulation, it served as a significant companion and beneficial friend to Korean women, and its impact still remains vividly in memory.  

Thus, renewing "新女性" with a complete overhaul of content to be presented to you is both a great delight and a source of pride for us.  

We ask that you look forward to "新女性" being fully available for everyone throughout Korea around December 20.  

Kaepiksa  

**Summary**  

The article announces the resumption of the women's magazine "新女性," highlighting its historical importance as a companion for Korean women and its benefits. It emphasizes the recommitment to innovate the magazine's content, conveying a sense of pride in its revival. Historians may find this announcement significant as it reflects the cultural shifts and the role of women in colonial Korea during the 1930s, showcasing the intersection of media, gender, and social transformation.

特告
한동안 휴간 중이든 부인잡지 「新女性」을 신영호부터 속간하게 되엿슴니다. 新女性이 간행되는 시절에 얼마나 조선 여성들의 갓가운 동무요. 또한 실익의 벗이엿든가 하는 것은 아직도 인상 깁히 남어 잇슬 것임이다.
그러하든 「新女性」을 다시 한번 내용에 대 혁신을 가하야 여러분의 압헤 내어노케 된 것은 여러분과 한가지로 무어라 할 수 업는 깃붐이요 자랑인가 함니다.
12월 20일경에는 「新女性」이 전 조선의 여러분의 수중에 모조리ㅏ 잽히게 될 것을 기다려 주시기 바라나이다.
開闢社 <161>
