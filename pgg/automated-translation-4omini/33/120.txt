一問一答記(3)  

記  先生은 술을 잘 잡수시는 모양인데 한거번에 얼마나 잡수심닛가.  

徐  正宗이면 보통 한 되쯤은 먹고 其外 麥酒가튼 것은 대중이 업슴니다. <31>  

 

**Summary**  

In this brief article, the author engages in a light conversation about drinking habits, specifically inquiring about how much alcohol the interviewee typically consumes. The interviewee humorously notes that when drinking good sake, they usually consume about a "do," and they express that they do not drink beer and similar beverages. This snippet reveals cultural attitudes toward alcohol in the 1930s, which might interest historians studying social practices during the Japanese colonial period in Korea, illustrating both a traditional appreciation for certain drinks and the avoidance of imported options like beer.
