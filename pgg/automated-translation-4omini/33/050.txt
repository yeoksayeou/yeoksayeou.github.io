葉書通信大歡迎  
In order to inform you about general criticism and hidden facts, this magazine has set up a special "Postcard Communication Section" since the day before, and has published postcards sent by readers in each issue. We express extraordinary welcome to readers. From the New Year issue, we aim to strengthen this section and will be soliciting postcards on a large scale, so anyone can feel free to share their thoughts and, if there are any factual matters, please do not hesitate to send them.  
(Refer to this magazine's postcard communication section for content and article format) <16>  

**Summary**  

This article announces the establishment of a "Postcard Communication Section" in the magazine to encourage reader participation and the sharing of opinions and hidden truths. It emphasizes the desire for robust engagement from readers by inviting them to contribute their postcards freely. This reflects a period of increasing public engagement and the search for truth during the colonial era in Korea, which may hold significance for historians studying media and communication under Japanese rule, revealing how vehicles of expression were being adapted in response to societal needs.

葉書通信大歡迎
本誌에서 一般의 批評거리와 숨은 事實을 여러분에게 알이기 위하야 전날부터 「葉書通信欄」을 特設하고 每號에 여러분의 投書한 엽서를 그대로 발표한 바 讀者에게 비상한 환영을 바덧슴니다. 新年號부터는 이 欄을 더욱 힘쓰기로 하야 대대적으로 葉書通信를 모집하오니 누구시던지 보시고 드르신대로 말슴하시고 십흔 사실이 잇사오면 조곰도 忌憚치 마르시고 곳 投書하야 주십시오.
