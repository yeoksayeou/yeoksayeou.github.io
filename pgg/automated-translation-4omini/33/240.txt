**Summary**  

The article presents a detailed observation of the bleak economic conditions in the streets during the year-end season, reflecting the struggles faced by individuals amidst the financial downturn. It employs satirical illustrations to convey a commentary on social behaviors and expectations during this time. Historians might find the depiction of economic hardship and its impact on societal norms particularly revealing, as it sheds light on the lived experiences of ordinary Koreans under colonial rule and the varying perceptions of prosperity and poverty during this era.
