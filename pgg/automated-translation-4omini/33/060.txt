Prize of One Hundred Won = Two Major Prize Recruitment, Manuscripts for "Song of Students" and "Debate Conference"  
Our magazine "Students" has been recruiting for the "Song of Students" since April of this year and the Debate Conference announced in October has not yet been completed and has been closed. We sincerely apologize to those who sent in submissions and to our readers who have supported the student magazine. However, these two recruitments will continue under the "別乾坤" section to ensure that they are successfully concluded. We hope that you will continue to love "別乾坤" just as you loved "Students" and that you will submit your excellent works as before.  

Recruitment Regulations  

Song of Students  
Title and length are free. Deadline is by the end of December. Prize money is one hundred won (10 pieces of work to be collected, 10 won each).  
Send to: 京城 開闢社 別乾坤部 학생노래募集係.  
Conditions: The song should bring joy to our Korean students. Winning works will be published in "別乾坤" next spring along with music composition.  

Debate Conference Recruitment Regulations  
Title is free. One line should be around 23 characters, and the total length around 150 lines. Include your address or school name and your name. Deadline is by the end of December.  
Send to: 京城 開闢社 別乾坤部 학생雄辯係. Prize money: 1st place 15 won, 2nd place 10 won, 3rd place 5 won (one for each).  
Judges: Seongsaeng Lee Kwang-su, Seongsaeng Park Hee-do, Seongsaeng Bang Jeong-hwan, Seongsaeng Seo Jun, Seongsaeng Ok Cheon-jin <17>  

**Summary**  

This article announces two major competitions for students: a songwriting contest and a debate competition in the "별건곤" magazine. It emphasizes the importance of student creativity in expressing national sentiments under colonial rule. Historians might find interest in how cultural activities were promoted within the constraints of colonial society, reflecting the aspirations and frustrations of Korean youth in the 1930s.

賞金壹百圓=二大懸賞募集, 『學生의 노래』와 『雄辯大會』 原稿
본사 발행 『學生』 잡지에서 今年 4월부터 모집하여 오든 「학생의 노래」와 10월달에 새로 발표한 懸賞全鮮學生誌上雄辯大會은 아즉 완료되지 못하고 폐간이 된은 만흔 응모를 보내주신 여러분께와 아울너 學生誌를 애호 편달하여 주신 여러 독자께 심히 미안한 일이오나 이 두 가지 모집은 「別乾坤」部에서 引續하야 원만하게 맛치려고 하옵니다. 「學生」을 사랑하시든 마음으로 「學生」의 뒤를 밧히어 주는 우리 「別乾坤」을 사랑하여 주시고 前과 가튼 用念으로 만흔 力作을 속속 던저주시기 바랍니다.

모집규정

학생의 노래
제목과 용지와 長短 자유. 期日 12월 말일까지. 賞金 백원(10篇 모집, 1篇 10원씩)
皮封 京城 開闢社 別乾坤部 學生노래募集係. 條件 우리 朝鮮學生들의 마음에 마즐 즐거운 노래-, 당선 작품은 작곡을 부처서 明春 別乾坤誌에 발표한다.

募集規正
제목 자유 1행 23자 150행 내외. 주소 또는 校名과 성명 명기. 期日 12월 말일까지
皮封 京城 開闢社 別乾坤部 學生雄辯係. 賞金 1등 15圓, 2등 10圓, 3등 5圓(각 1인)
審判 李光洙 선생, 朴熙道 선생, 方定煥 선생, 徐椿 선생, 玉璿珍 선생 <17>
