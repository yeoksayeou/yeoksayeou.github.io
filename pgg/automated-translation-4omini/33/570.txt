Editorial Notes  

The editorial notes for the New Year’s issue still have traces that have not yet faded, and already we are beginning to edit the Year-End issue. As an editor of a magazine with a month’s worth of work pressing down on us, we inevitably become more conscious of how quickly time is passing.

Although time passes normally in whatever one does, for us, who should be engaged in more than just ordinary activities, it is a sobering thought that this year as well we are only managing to publish a few issues of the magazine, thus spending it in vain. In these days, it is difficult to hold back the feeling of sorrow over wasted time. (Cha)

Last month’s issue was quite substantial in content, attracting significant popularity in various spheres, and even amidst this economic downturn, it became almost sold out within just a few days of publication. While I express my gratitude to our readers, I also hope that this upcoming issue will be loved even more. (Choi)

Since this is the Year-End issue, we have included a summary of the year's developments in various fields in Korea. To understand what occurred in the national circumstances throughout the year, everyone should certainly read this article. Moreover, the discussions included in the Economic Review roundtable featuring experts in economics are essential for us, who endure hardships due to the economic downturn.  

Additionally, the continuation of Mr. Ma-u’s rural policy and Mr. Hong Seung-ha’s current state of Korean industry, which drew considerable interest in the previous issue, should be read in continuation. Furthermore, the article on the uprising of the Taiwanese indigenous population must be read by those with compassion, for it is filled with blood and tears. (Park)

Due to the temporary suspension of the Student Journal and the continuing publication of New Women, the articles that were published in Student have all been moved to the separate Student section of Byeolgeongon, and many student articles will continue to be published there. I hope that general student readers will love reading both Byeolgeongon and New Women alongside the student section. (Choi)

Mr. Park Roar has gotten married and goes diligently to meet his wife at Suwon every Saturday. These days, the king has so many engagements that going to Suwon nine times a month has become troublesome. However, Mr. Park visits his in-laws four to five times a month, so the calls he receives from his wife at Suwon have become quite frequent. As a result, there is indeed an additional task added to his workload. Nevertheless, don’t forget to give him a generous bonus of buckwheat noodles at year-end. (Park Clerk) <176>  

**Summary**  

This article offers reflections by the editors on the transition from a New Year’s issue to a Year-End issue, highlighting the challenges faced in publishing during a difficult economic climate. It praises the previous issue's success and describes the planned contents of the upcoming issue, including topics like economic discussions and social issues affecting Koreans. This piece is indicative of the sentiments of the time, showcasing the struggles of Korean intellectuals and the impacts of colonial policies on their publishing efforts, which speaks to broader themes in colonial history regarding cultural expression and economic hardship.

編輯落書
新年號의 編輯落墨이 아즉까지 그 餘痕이 살어지지 안엇는데 벌서 送年號를 또 編輯하게 되니 한달 일을 닥어하는 雜誌 編輯者로서는 남보다 세월의 빠른 것을 더욱히 더 늣기게 된다.

어느 일을 하던지 세월을 보내기야 일반이지만은 당연이 보다 이상의 활동을 할 우리로서 환경에 진눌이어 금년도 또 잡지 몃 冊만 내이고 헛되이 보내게 되니 녓날 사람의 泣髀의 歡을 禁하기 어럽다. (車)

前月號는 내용이 자못 충실하니 만치 각 방면의 인기가 非常하야 이와 가티 불경기한 중에도 발행한 지 불과 몃칠에 거의 絶版이 되게 팔이엿다. 讀者 여러분에게 感謝하는 동시에 이번 호도 더 一層 愛讀하야 주시길를 바란다. (蔡)

이번 호는 送年號인 까닭에 금년 1년 朝鮮 각 방면의 總決算을 揭載하게 되엿다. 금년 중 朝鮮의 總現狀이 엇지된 것을 알자면 누구나 이 기사를 읽어야만 될 것은 물론이고 經濟學 專門大家 여러분을 總網羅한 不景氣檢討座談會 記事는 불경기에 대 고통을 밧는 우리로서 꼭 읽어야만 되겟다.

그리고 前月號에 대인기를 끌은 馬嗚氏의 農村政策, 洪性夏氏의 朝鮮工業의 現階段... 續論은, 누구나 繼續하야 읽을 만하고 臺灣 蕃族의 反亂記는 피와 눈물이 잇는 同情人으로서는 반드시 읽어야 하겟다. (朴)

學生志를 臨時 休刊하고 新女性을 續刊하게 된 까닭에 前날 學生에 게재하던 기사는 전부 別乾坤 學生欄에 옴겨 실고 압흐로도 別乾坤 學生欄에 학생 기사를 만히 실게 되엿다. 일반 學生讀者는 학생과 가티 別乾坤과 新女性을 愛讀하야 주기 바란다. (崔)

朴露兒 先生임은 장가를 가시더니 每 土曜日이면 그 부인을 만나러고 꼭꼭 水原을 가신담니다. 녓날 正宗大王은 한 달에 水原 거등이 시물 아홉 번이나 되기 때문에 陵參奉이 困難을 당하더니 이 朴先生님은 한 달에 妻家行을 4, 5차식 하시기 때문에 박선생을 찻는 전화 대답 또 하루가 멀다 하고 水原에서 하시는 그 부인의 전화 밧기에 밧분 중 일 한아가 더 생겻슴니다. 그 대신에 年末 賞與로 국수나 한탁 잘 주시지요. (朴給仕) <176>
