Snow Scene  
						方仁根  

Last night, I saw the snowflakes falling gently. When I woke up in the morning, the window was bright. Half-awake, I opened the window and saw that the yard was covered in snow, piled high. The morning sunlight was dazzling against the snow. What a change has occurred overnight...  

The wind is blowing fiercely. The falling snow dances in the air, drifting this way and that. The snow falls like a waterfall. Yet there is no sound; it is quiet. In a world that is usually noisy, I find the silent pouring of snow delightful.  

The snow falls thickly like fluffy cotton balls, falling merrily in the night. The branches of the bare trees are adorned with snowflakes, and even the dirty, cold earth is gently covered with the white, fluffy embrace of snow, making it heartwarming.  

When the Christmas bells ring out softly, the snow falls dazzlingly, and children's laughter dances—on the night of December 20, it feels even more delightful when the snow falls.  

I long for the North Country where the snow falls thickly like Mount Tai (泰山), where there are no people, no smells, and only the white snow covering the fields, and I wish to walk through that expanse all at once. <160>  

**Summary**  

This article captures the beauty and tranquility of a snowy landscape through poetic imagery. The author's reflection on the snowfall evokes feelings of nostalgia and longing for a serene winter scene. The mention of Christmas and the contrast of joy against the backdrop of snow may resonate with the experiences of individuals in Korea during the colonial period as they celebrated traditional and modern festivities amid changing societal conditions. Historians might find interest in how nature and seasonal changes are connected to emotional and cultural sentiments during this era, revealing the Korean people's longing for peace and beauty despite historical disruptions.

雪景
						方仁根


어제ㅅ밤에 눈발이 흿득흿득 날니는 것을 보고 갓는데 아츰에 이러나니 窓門이 환-하다. 반쯤 이러나 창문을 열어잣드리니 눈이 마당에 집웅에 소복히 싸혓는데 아츰 햇빗이 눈이 부시게 빗최고 잇다. 하로 저녁 사이에 이러케도 변화가 잇나니...



바람이 몹시 분다. 쏘다지는 눈이 공중에서 춤을 추며 가로세로 날린다. 폭포처럼 눈발은 나려질닌다. 그러나 소리가 업시 삿분삿분 고요하다. 떠들기 조화하는 세상에 소리업시 퍼붓는 눈이길래 나는 사랑스럽다.



주먹 가튼 솜뭉치 가튼 눈이 펑-펑-펑- 탐스럽게 쏘다지는 밤-. 여위고 말는 나무 가지에도 눈송이로 살이 찌고 드럽고 추한 언 땅에도 하얏케 솜니불 덥허주는 다정한 눈이길래 귀엽다.



크리쓰마쓰 鍾소래 은은히 울릴 때 눈은 펄펄 날니고 어린이들 깃버 춤추는 12월 20일 밤 반든시 눈이 와야 더욱 興致잇더라.



눈이 泰山처럼 푹푹 싸히는 北國이 그립고 그립네- 사람도 업고 냄새도 업고 빗갈도 업는 흰 눈만 보히는 그 벌판을 한업시 걸어가고 십네. <160>
