						朴瓚熙  
記  당신은 眼鏡을 항상 써고 기시니 짐으실 때에는 엇지하심닛가.  
朴  밤에도 電灯을 끄기 前까지는 늘 쓰고 잇습니다. 나는 원래에 眼力이 不足하야 中學 時代부터 眼鏡을 썻스닛가요.  

**Summary**  

In this brief exchange, the author, Park Chan-hee, discusses his habit of wearing glasses all the time, even before turning off the electric light at night. He explains that he has had poor eyesight since middle school and has thus been using glasses since that time. This simple article reflects the everyday experiences and challenges faced by individuals in colonial Korea, shedding light on the impact of modernization and the spread of new technologies like electric lighting on daily life. Historians may find the shifting perceptions of vision and the significance of personal health in the context of colonial society particularly interesting.
