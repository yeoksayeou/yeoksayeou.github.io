Nonsense Club

The Cat's Words  
At midnight, the mother cooking rice opened the cupboard and secretly stole something to eat, when the owner woke up and asked,  
"Who is it?"  
The mother responded nonchalantly,  
"Meow."  
"Oh, it’s a cat."  
"Yes."

Money Fixes  
Doctor: "In any case, your urgent task right now is to improve your living conditions."  
Patient: "Yes. Then, what kind of job..."  
Doctor: "Well, even if you don't go that far, you should be able to pay five won with a single check-up."

Where the Ice Went  
"I've lost it."  
"Why is that?"  
"What do you mean, 'why is that?' The ice was too dirty, so I put it in hot water, and now I don't know where it went."

Still Suspicious  
A lady from the countryside rode a tram for the first time, and the conductor was very kind, so she said appreciatively,  
"You’re different from young people these days. You’re truly wonderful. When I go back, I’ll complain again."

My Stomach is More Beautiful  
Wife: "You’re just idling around, so your stomach is beautiful. You should work like others and make some money."  
Husband: "Who hasn’t worked? My stomach was more beautiful when I worked."

The Original Skin  
The nurse, taking care of a patient with high fever, was instructed to wet a cloth with water every three hours and rub the patient's skin. However, after the second time, when about to do it the third time, the patient weakly said,  
"Excuse me. It's not because my body is dirty. It's just my original skin."

Who is the Bride?  
A woman who recently got married boasted,  
"My husband Park is quite serious. He doesn’t smoke or speak nonsense, and he never does anything wrong." Her friend replied,  
"Indeed, there are all kinds of people in this world! So how would you explain the reason for an annulment?"

Photo Enlargement  
Customer: "Do you enlarge small pictures to the actual size?"  
Photographer: "Yes, that’s our specialty."  
Customer: "Then please enlarge this picture of Baekdu Mountain to the actual size."

Charity  
Young-sook: "Look at me. Is that person over there the one you applied for marriage?"  
Moosun: "Well, I thought for a while that it might be that person. I'm quite sure it’s him."

**Summary**

This article presents a series of humorous and absurd dialogues, reflecting the everyday lives and social interactions during the 1930s in Korea. Each vignette captures a glimpse of human nature, societal expectations, and individual quirks within a colonial context. Historians might find the portrayal of traditional roles, particularly marriage and work ethics, interesting as it provides insights into the cultural values and changes during the colonial period.

고양이의 말
밥 짓는 어멈이 밤중에 찬장 문을 열고 몰래 무엇을 훔쳐먹는 판에 주인이 깨여서
『누구야』
어멈이 시침을 뚝 따고
『냐-웅』
『아 고양인가.』
『네.』

돈이 고친다
의사 『하여간 목하의 당신의 급무는 생활상태를 고치서야 할 것입니다.』
환자 『네. 그러면 어떠한 직업이... ...』
의사 『아니 그러케까지 아니하시더라도 한번에 진찰로 오원은 내실 수 잇도록.』

어름의 행방
『어-렵소.』
『웨 그러나?』
『웨 그러나가 뭔가. 어름이 너모 더러워서 지금 더운물에다 시첫더니 어데로 갓는지 업서젓네.』

또 탐세
시골서 올러와 처음으로 전차를 탄 마나님, 차장이 퍽 친절히 구니까 대단히 조와서
『자네는 요새ㅅ날 젊은 녀석과 달라 참 훌륭하이. 잇다가 도라가는 길에 또 탐세 응.』

배가 더 곱하
안해 『빈들빈들 놀고 잇스니까 배가 곱흘 박게. 남들 모양으로 로동해서 좀 버러요.』
남편 『누가 로동을 안해봣나. 일하니까 배가 더 곱흐던대.』

원래의 살빗
엇던 간호부가 고열(高熱)의 륵막념 환자를 마텃는데 세 시간마다 해면을 물에 축여서 환자의 피부를 문지르라는 명령을 바덧다. 그런데 두번은 하고 세번째 할 판에 환자가 히미한 소리로
『여보시오. 내 몸이 더러워서 그런게 아니요. 원래의 살갈이 그럿소이다.』

단처는?
막 결혼한 녀자가 자랑삼어서
『우리 박갓양반은 여간 점잔치 안어슬, 담배 다 안 먹지 말소리 놉히고 해퉁해퉁하는 일이 도모지 업서.』 그의 동무 모던껄
『참 이 세상에 별 사람이 다 잇군! 그러면 장챠 리혼할 리유를 무얼로 부칠테야.』

사진확대
손님 『당신네 댁에서 적은 사진을 실물만큼 확대해 주십니까.』
사진사 『네네. 그것이 우리의 장기입니다.』
손님 『그러면 이 백두산 사진을 실물대로 확대해 주십쇼.』

박애(博愛)
