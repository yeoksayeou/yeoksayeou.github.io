學界漫話  
Having just returned from a tour of Europe, Mr. Kim Seong-su has taken up the position of principal at the Central High School (中央高普), while former principal Mr. Choi Du-seon has been elected as the school's permanent director, marking a decision to switch from unpaid to paid directors, where Mr. Kim holds a prestigious but honorary unpaid position. The fact that Mr. Kim, who has just returned from a European observation tour, is taking the principal's post in a Korean middle school is indeed noteworthy. There are considerable expectations from the public regarding what new facilities might emerge under his leadership.

Young educator Mr. Choi Jin-soon has left the Middle East School (中東學校) and taken over the Seoul Childcare School (京城保育學校), concurrently enlisting almost all members of the Saengdong Society (색동회) to wield the teaching baton. As his first major initiative, he has dispatched Mr. Choi Yeong-joo, who was previously affiliated with our former Women's Department, to Japan for an inspection of childcare institutions across various regions, which raises the hope that a childcare institution centered on the Saengdong members, who have conducted research for a decade on child issues, will soon be established.

In the private school community of Korea, during the inception period of the Middle East School, a pact was made by the trio of Liu-kwan-Jang. For some reason, Choi Gyu-tong, An Il-yeong, and Sim Jae-deok, who had made a firm promise together, have since seen Mr. Sim retire five years ago, and Mr. An Il-yeong is slated to return to Gimcheon High School (金泉高普) this year, which leads one to reflect on the past amidst a sense of nostalgia when looking at the school grounds of the Middle East School, as frequently expressed by former alumni.

Mr. Baek Nam-kyu, who had once taken the position of math teacher at Central High School and subsequently fell from grace about a year ago as the head of the teaching affairs at Dongdeok Girls' High School (同德女高), has recently devoted himself passionately to the study and promotion of Esperanto. He has taken up a lecture column on Esperanto in the Dong-A Ilbo and is earnestly propagating knowledge even to elderly men sporting topknots, indicating that he has joined the Kehmeng Club (啓明俱樂部). It would be worth inquiring about the progress on the Korean dictionary project, which was all the rage in the same club a few years back. If the Korean dictionary does not come out, at least an Esperanto dictionary should be possible. <19>  

**Summary**  

This article discusses recent developments in the Korean educational landscape, particularly highlighting shifts in leadership at various schools, including the Central High School and the Seoul Childcare School. It notes the changing roles and expectations of educators, along with efforts to promote childcare initiatives in Korea. Notably, it reflects the impact of European perspectives on education and the nostalgia of early founders in the private school system. Historians might find the reference to the challenges and transitions within educational institutions during the colonial period particularly revealing, shedding light on the broader socio-political dynamics at play in Korean society.

學界漫話
歐洲 시찰을 맛치시고 갓 도라오신 金性洙 氏가 中央高普의 교장으로 나오시고 前 교장 崔斗善 氏는 同校의 常任理事로 被選되시어 재래의 無給 理事를 崔氏에 한하야 有給으로 새 결정을 하고 김씨만은 無給의 名譽職으로 게시게 되엿다고 한다. 아모려나 일 만코 問題 만흔 조선의 中學校에서 歐洲 시찰을 하시고 온 김씨가 교장으로 나오심은 매우 깃분 일이다. 무슨 새로운 施設이 나올는지 一般으로는 기대와 주시도 적지 안흘 듯.

少壯敎育家 崔瑨淳 氏가 中東學校에서 나와 가지고 京城保育學校를 引繼해 맛는 동시에 색동會 一派의 會員을 거의 망라하야 敎鞭을 잡게 하는 한편 第一着의 사업으로 前 本社 新女性部에 게시든 崔泳柱 氏를 일본에 派遣하야 각지의 保育機關을 시찰케 하엿다니 兒童 問題에 10년간 연구를 거듭하야 온 색동會員을 중심으로 하야 保育機關 하나가 서 나가게 된다는 것은 매우 깃보고도 미덤성 잇는 일이다.

朝鮮私學界에 이름 노픈 中東學校 창립시대에 일즉이 劉關張 3인의 桃源結諠 격으로 손을 마조 잡고 굿게 약속을 하엿다든 崔奎東·安一英·沈在德 三氏도 어인 일인지 심씨가 퇴직한 지 이미 사오 년이요 安一英氏가 금년에 또 다시 金泉高普로 낙향을 하시게 되야 옛날을 회상하고 中東의 교내를 드러다 보면 엇전지 좀 쓸쓸한 기색이 돈다는 것은 옛날 校友들의 입에서 흔이 나오는 소리라고.

中央高普의 수학 선생님으로 一朝에 同德女高의 敎務主任 의자를 차지하섯다가 거기에서 실각한 지 일년여가 되는 白南奎氏는 近間에 에쓰페란토 연구와 선전에 極力熱中하시여 東亞日報 紙上에 에쓰페란토 講座欄을 擔任하시고 길까에서 상투 짠 노인에게까지 熱心宣傳하시드만 말슴이 잇섯는데 요점에는 啓明俱樂部에 드러가시게 되엿다고 한다. 年前에 同俱樂部에서 떠들든 朝鮮語辭典編纂事業은 엇지나 되엿는지 일 조와하시는 백씨가 드러가섯스니 朝鮮語辭典이 못 나오면 에쓰페란토 辭典이라도 나옴직한 일이다.＜19＞
