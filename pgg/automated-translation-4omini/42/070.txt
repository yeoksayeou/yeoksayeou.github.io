文壇風聞  
Mr. Lee Tae-jun has been in Kaesong for some time, but recently he and his wife have moved to Seoul, where he has taken a position at the Gyeongseong Childcare School, teaching Korean language, while his wife, Mrs. Lee Soon-uk, serves as a dedicated music teacher. Their residence is at the rather uncomfortable address of 3-7-2, Seodaemun-dong.  

Mr. Kim Luk-soo (金鹿水), also known as Park Pal-yang, had been unwell for a while and had not been seen in the newspaper office. He, along with Mr. Yoo Kwang-ryeol and Mr. Bae Seong-ryong, submitted a resignation and was later reinstated. However, it turns out that his overdue salary has accumulated for several months. As they say, one must eat to live as a poet. Friends jokingly suggested that if he were to receive his back pay, he could treat them to a meal.  

Mr. Yeom Sang-seob (廉想涉) has already quit drinking and is now proudly a proponent of sobriety, to the extent that he wishes it for everyone. It was a focus of attention in the host country as to how this change occurred, and it was recently revealed that it was not due to health or financial issues but because of the persuasive lectures given by his very stern wife, who, after he returned home late after drinking, would weep and give him a stern rebuke the next morning. This led to a significant realization for him, and he has since firmly committed to abstinence. It is rather pitiful that the owner of a cafe would only have cider as a choice when his friends would drag him along.  

Mr. Jeon Mu-gyeong (全武吉) has recently joined the Chosun Ilbo newspaper. He seems to be quite fortunate. Recently, while returning late due to a breakdown of his car, his boarding house owner, not knowing where he had gone, caused him to worry about not being able to eat as dinner had passed. Feeling pressed for food, he ended up cooking rice and eating with the water seller, who had been urging him for rice, without the owner knowing; the owner felt so sorry not to have asked him anything and went to bed.

文壇風聞
李泰俊 氏는 그간 개성에 가 게시더니 요즘에 내외분이 함께 올라와서 京城保育學校에 교편을 잡게 되섯는데 氏는 조선어를 가르키게 되고 부인 李順玉氏는 專任 음악 선생으로 게시게 되엿다. 사택은 西大門町 二丁目 七의 三의 다號. 대단히 거북한 번지다.

金鹿水 朴八陽氏는 한동안 몸이 불편하다고 신문사에도 아니 나오더니 柳光烈, 裵成龍 氏 등과 함께 사직원을 제출하야 수리가 되엿다고. 그런데 드른즉 밀리운 월급이 여러 달 분이라고. 먹어야 시인도 산다. 밀린 월급을 한목 밧는다면 한 턱쯤은 내여도 조켓다고 가까운 친구들이 농담.

廉想涉 氏는 벌서부터 술을 끈어 이제는 당당하게 검주론자가 되여 가지고 누구에게나 그러하기를 원할 만큼 되엿다. 엇재서 끈케 되엿나 하고 주인국에서 크게 주목하던 바 최근에 와서 드듸어 발각이 되엿다. 즉 건강과 돈이 문제가 되여서 그런 것이 아니라氏의 부인 무던하신 부인이 대취하야 늣게 도라온氏를 곱게 재웟다가 이튼날 아침에는 반드시 눈물을 흘리면서 훈시를 한바탕 하신다. 이 바람에氏도 크게 깨다른 바 잇서서 그후부터는 절대로 검주를 단행하얏다. 친구에게 끌리며 카페를 가면 마지못해 사이다로 대작하는 것은 석일의 대주인氏로는 여간 가여운 게 아니다.

全武吉氏는 최근에 朝鮮日報社에 入社하엿다. 매우 밧브신 모양.
일전에는 하숙 주인이 문박게 어듸를 갓다가 자동차의 고장으로 밤늣게야 도라오게 되엿다. 그런 때 저녁 먹을 때가 지나도록 주인이 오지를 아느니까 냉면 한 그릇으로 때울 판인데 물장사가 밥을 내라고 조른다. 할 수 업시 평소에 묵중한 그가 쌀을 퍼내여 가지고 밥을 지어서 물장사하고 가티 먹엇다고 주인은 그런 줄도 모르고 미안해서 아모 말도 못하고 자버렷다고.＜15＞
