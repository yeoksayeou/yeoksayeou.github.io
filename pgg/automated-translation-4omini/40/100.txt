The results of the national census (國勢調查) conducted in Korea on October 1 of last year were reported as a population of 21,057,969 people. However, upon thorough examination of the reports, the officially confirmed population is 21,058,305 people, an increase of 336 from the provisional figure. Compared to the simplified national census of 14th year of Taisho (大正 14年 간이(簡易) 국세조사), this represents an increase of 1,535,360 people. Britain: A Woman's Country - There are More Women than Men by Over 1.8 Million  

As of April 26 this year, the census (國勢調査) in Britain revealed a total population of 44,790,485. Compared to ten years ago in 1921, this shows an increase of over 2 million, and among that population, the number of women exceeds that of men by 1,861,063. To the single men in Korea! Do not worry at all and just move to the UK. You will be warmly welcomed by the women during this bountiful time of women....  

  

Startling Telephone Charges  

Due to the treaty (協約) between the United States and France, the telephone charges (電話料) incurred while American representatives were in Paris (巴里) to discuss matters with their homeland are shocking. The total charge was around 170,000 francs (approximately 13,600 won) for a total duration of 7 hours and 45 minutes, marking the first time such a long duration and significant telephone charges have been incurred between Europe and America.  

  

**Summary**  

The article provides statistics on the confirmed population of Korea following a national census, with notable increases compared to previous data. Additionally, it presents a curious perspective on the gender ratio in Britain, suggesting opportunities for unmarried men in Korea, as well as the unforeseen costs of long-distance communication. Historians might find the details regarding population changes significant, reflecting the demographic shifts during the colonial period in Korea.

朝鮮의 人口確數
작년 10월 1일에 시행한 조선의 국세조사(國勢調査)의 결과 속보(速報) 인구를 21057969人으로 발표하얏스나 그후 신고서를 정세히 조사한 결과 완전이 확정된 인구는 21058305人으로 속보 인구보다 336인이 증가되고 이 확정 인구를 大正 14년 간이(簡易) 국세조사와 비교하면 1535360人이 증가 되엿스며 이것을 다시 각 도별로 보면 경북(慶北)의 2416712人이 제일 만코 함북(咸北)의 745124人이 제일 적다고 한다. 도별의 자서한 것은 다음과 갓다.

놀날만한 電話料
이번에 미불랑국(米佛兩國)의 협약(協約)관게로 미국대표가 파리(巴里)에 체재한 중 본국과 렬락한 전화료금(電話料)은 놀나지 마시오. 작으만치 17만 푸랑(略13600원)이요 전화한 시간은 합게 7시 45분이라는데 구라파와 아메리카 새이인 만큼 긴 시간과 만흔 전화료를 지불하기는 이번이 처음이라고 한다.＜17＞
