Notice

We respectfully announce that the principal of our company, Mr. Pong Jeong-hwan, passed away in the Kyongseong University Hospital on July 23.

This article serves as an announcement regarding the death of Mr. Pong Jeong-hwan, indicating a notable loss for the publishing company.
