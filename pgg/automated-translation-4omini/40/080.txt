萬華鏡  
今世大力士  
When referring to the Buddhist leader Han Yong-un (佛敎社長 韓龍雲氏) as a Zen Master, no one would disagree, but calling him a strongman (力士) might draw doubt. However, it turns out that he possesses an unknown phenomenal strength capable of overpowering about thirty people. This is not just hearsay; it is an account witnessed by his close acquaintances. Several decades ago, while he was in the town of Goseong, Gangwon-do, he witnessed a robbery where riches were plundered and women were dishonored, which greatly incensed him. Armed with a handful of wooden sticks, he charged into the fray and chased off dozens of men. There are reports of others having witnessed similar feats. Anyone who treats him merely as an ordinary person and ignores his capabilities would surely rue the day, so one should exercise particular caution!

文盲老先生  
Joseon Education Association  
Yu Jin-tae (朝鮮敎育協會 兪鎭泰氏) may appear prominent due to his name and long service in the education sector, suggesting he is well-educated and influential in the field. However, the reality is that he has never even served a day as a primary school teacher and struggles to read a single letter, sometimes resorting to asking others to read letters for him. In essence, he is a literal illiterate (文盲). Yet, who could say he is uneducated? His extensive knowledge when socializing and his fervor for education surpass that of many formally educated individuals. This indicates that merely knowing how to read or write does not fundamentally define one's education or intelligence.

文士家의 海軍化  
Chunwon Lee Kwang-soo (春園 李光洙氏) has relocated his entire family to Incheon’s coast (仁川海岸) for summer vacation and the recuperation of his wife. Remarkably, every day he also conducts business with the Dong-A Ilbo (동아일보社). This transformation into a sailor-like family seems fitting, especially since Chunwon is writing a novel about Korea's naval hero, Yi Sun-sin (리순신氏), portraying every aspect of his life in a naval theme. Speaking of which, it would be quite flavorful if Chunwon's pen name (호) were to be changed to Chunhae (春海) while temporarily merging with Bang In-geun (方仁根氏), adding a touch of grace, ha ha...

北도 七星·南도 七星  
Geunwoo Club  
Jeong Chil-seong (槿友會 丁七星氏) established a textile shop named "Bunok Suya" (粉玉手藝舍) in Nakwon-dong (樂園洞) last year and was simultaneously running club activities while operating his business. However, as his plans weren’t aligning as desired, he has decided to leave for Geongnam Tongyeong (慶南統營) to pursue new ventures. It seems the Northern Seven Stars (北斗七星) are transforming into the Southern Seven Stars (南斗七星). Whatever the case—may the stars shine brightly!

獨鵑不獨鳴乎  
Dukgyeon Choi Sang-deok (獨鵑 崔象德氏) has been leading a solitary life like a mourning dove after breaking ties with Yu Yeong-suk (劉英淑), yet rumors suggest that he is now in discussions for marriage with Baek Jeong-jin (白貞鎭) from the Central Child Care School (中央保育學校). If these rumors hold true and don't merely remain gossip, the owner of this kaleidoscope should once again extend their congratulations for their marriage and offer another view at the wedding banquet.

  
**Summary**  

This article features a series of vignettes showcasing various remarkable or ironic traits of individuals within Korean society during the colonial period. It emphasizes the unexpected contrasts between one's public respectability and private realities—such as highlighting that an influential educator is functionally illiterate. The anecdotes reflect social struggles and personal battles, revealing a society in flux, where perceptions of strength and education are often superficial. Historians might find interest in how these narratives underline the complexities of identity and societal expectations in a colonized Korea, especially in challenging the conventions of what it means to be 'educated' or 'strong' during this time.

불교사장 한용운씨(佛敎社長 韓龍雲氏)를 선사(禪師)라고 하면 누구나 이의가 업겟지만은 력사(力士)라고 하면 잘 고지 듯지 안을 것이다. 그러나 알고보면 그는 남모르는 굿센 힘이 잇서서 한 몸으로 약 30여 人을 능당한다고 한다. 그것은 누가 지여낸 말도 아니요 그의 친한 사람이 목도한 일인대 지금으로부터 약 수십년 전에 그가 강원도 고성 땅을 지내다가 어떤 촌락에 강도가 드러서 재물을 략탈하고 부녀를 함부로 능욕하는 것을 보고 크게 분개하야 방맹이 한아름 가지고 단심으로 뛰여 드러가서 수십여 명을 따러 쫏찻다 한다. 그뿐 아니라 그 외에도 여러 가지 일을 목도한 사람이 잇다고 하는데 누구나 그를 그냥 보통 사람으로 알고 육박전 가튼 것을 함부로 하랴다는 큰 코를 닷칠 것이니 특별 주의할 일!

文盲老先生
조선교육협회
유진태씨(朝鮮敎育協會
兪鎭泰氏)는 그의 이름난 것으로 보나 여러 해 동안 교육회에 종사하는 것으로 보아 누구나 퍽 유식하고 교육계에 리력이 만흔 이로 알 것이다. 그러나 실상 알고 보면 그는 학교라고는 소학교 교원 한아도 지내본 일이 업고 글자라고는 남이 편지 한 장을 잘못 보는 까닭에 자긔에게 오는 편지를 각금 남을 주는 일이 잇다고 한다. 말하자면 문맹로선생(文盲老先生)이다. 그러나 그를 누가 무식하다고 하랴. 남과 교제하고 말할 때에 풍부한 상식이＜14＞ 잇는 것이라던지 교육에 렬심하는 것은 보통 유식하다는 인물보다 훨신 초월하다. 그러고 보면 이 세상에서 다만 문ㅅ자를 알고 모르는 것만 가지고 유식무식이라 하는 것은 월래 잘못인 듯.

文士家의 海軍化
춘원 리광수씨(春園 李光洙氏)는 피서 겸 아든임들의 요양(療養)을 식힐겸 하야 너름 동안에 전 가족이 인천 해안(仁川海岸)으로 이주를 하고 동아일보사에도 매일 인천에서 통행을 한다. 그러고 보면 문사의 전가족이 해군화(海軍化) 한 세움이요 더구나 춘원씨는 소설까지 조선의 해군신(海軍神)인 리순신氏를 쓰니 전 생활이 해군화한 것 갓다. 이왕이면 춘원이란 그의 호(號)도 너름 동안만 춘해 방인근씨(春海 方仁根氏)와 잠간 박구워서 춘해라 하엿스면 더 격이 맛겟지. 하하.......
