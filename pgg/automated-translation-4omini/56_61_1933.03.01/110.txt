발행일	1933년 03월 01일  
기사제목	新聞에도 發表안된 現代의 『루팡』 女怪盜, 白晝鍾路에서생긴 大膽한 犯行 (A modern "Lupin" female phantom thief, a bold crime committed in broad daylight on Jongno)  
기사형태	회고·수기 (Retrospective and Memoir)  

This story is a true crime incident that recently occurred at a gold and silversmith shop in Seoul. It is so audacious and clever that it would make even the phantom thief Lupin drop his jaw in surprise. Moreover, the fact that the perpetrator is a woman makes this a one hundred percent intriguing crime story. -Author-

In broad daylight—what is more, right in view of the police station—this tale unfolded in Jongno, at the largest gold and silver shop in Seoul. 

Given the boldness of the crime and the amount involved, which was a staggering fifteen hundred won, the victim shop vehemently denied the incident, leading this story to remain unpublished in the newspapers.

On the afternoon of January 20X, at around 1 PM, a middle-aged woman in her forties, somewhat more than thirty years old, arrived in a taxi at the shop. Judging by her appearance, she seemed to be the concubine of a rich man, yet presented herself with a solemn demeanor. 

Upon entering, she solely engaged in negotiating for jewelry made entirely of precious metals, including necklaces, rings, bracelets, and earrings, for a total value of fifteen hundred won. The owner, feeling greatly pleased with such a significant amount in these times of economic hardship, was taken aback. 

After selecting the items and deciding what to purchase, the problematical woman spoke in an unflustered manner. “So, the total is fifteen hundred won, correct?” 

“Yes, yes, that is correct.” 

“Since everything is made of metal, I cannot say much. In fact, I came from XXdong OO pharmacy, and since it was the birthday celebration of the head of the family, I wished to buy gifts for my relatives to give to them. Bringing cash seems dangerous for a woman, so I thought I would negotiate and promise to pay when I brought the goods with me.” 

The owner, whose mind was somewhat dazed, listened to her words and, not having anyone less knowledgeable about the store’s renowned reputation, gently acquiesced to her request. 

“Yes, that seems alright. Especially since you chose to come to this store and made such a large purchase, it shouldn’t be too difficult a task. I will have someone accompany you, so please wait a moment.” 

“Then please do that.” 

“I’m afraid I must ask for a taxi.” 

“Yes, yes, I will call one for you.” 

Based on the calm and composed demeanor of the woman, the owner confidently agreed, called a taxi, and sent a shop assistant with the purchased items. 

At OO pharmacy in XXdong! 

After alighting from the taxi, this woman, in a leisurely manner, paid the taxi fare and then turned to the assistant who was carrying the goods. 

“Please wait here for a moment while I show these items to the family members inside. If they all like them, I will pay you without fail.” 

Extending her hand, the young assistant, now having reached the pharmacy, thought nothing of it and handed the bundle of gold into the woman’s grasp. 

Once inside, this woman immediately sought the owner of the pharmacy whom she had agreed to meet beforehand. 

“I’m sorry for being slightly late. As I mentioned earlier, I forcibly deceived my sick child and came here, but right now he is at the door. But first, please do not worry about how much the total comes to. My goal is only that the child gets well, so just charge me whatever it costs, and I will step out for a moment and return in an hour or so, so please see that medicine is prepared in the meantime.” 

The pharmacy owner agreed without a second thought, thinking that there was no issue after having heard earlier from the woman, who seemed to be a very dignified lady, and she ushered the woman into the back door of the shop. 

After some time! 

The owner sent someone out to fetch the assistant waiting by the door. 

“I apologize for making you wait so long.” 

“Not at all!” replied the assistant. 

Seeing that the assistant was around twenty, bright and youthful, the owner thought to himself, “This is a remarkably good-looking and seemingly unfortunate young person.” 

“Please step into this room for a moment.” 

“No, I don’t really need to go in. Just give me the money, and I will leave.” 

Well aware of what the woman had said, the owner insisted, “Come on now, you shouldn’t… Would you mind stepping in a bit?” 

There had been no indication that anything untoward was happening, and the assistant reluctant yet unable to refuse, followed the owner into the room, and

“What exactly do you want with the money you claim?” asked the owner. 

“What do you mean, what do I want? Didn’t we agree on the amount?” 

“The amount… what did we agree on…?” 

“Of course it’s already agreed upon!” 

With regard to the patient’s request for soothing, the owner acted coyly. 

“So then, what do you intend to do with the money?” 

“What do you mean, what will I do? I will take it to the shop, as stated!” 

“Shop! Shop! If you don’t hand it over…” 

“What? You won't give it? Then I will take back the gold items.” 

“Clearly, you must have met some agreement in person before, and it is certainly worth considering!” The owner clicked his tongue in disbelief. 

“Now let’s stop this talk and tell me what’s been going on.” 

“What’s going on! Don’t you see it’s you who’s the culprit! Hurry up and give me the money, or I will call the police!” 

Realizing that suspicion was growing, the assistant became frightened and anxious. The owner, perceiving that this urgency could not end well in such a circumstance, gave a quick warning glance at the assistant to restrain him. 

Using the surprise of this situation, the woman began to shout wildly, but despite her tantrum, it became evident to the owner how profoundly the situation had transformed into something sinister altogether. 

However, a healthy person’s pulse wouldn’t demonstrate a drastic change for no reason, and still, the chaos intervening in her mind arose in both apprehension and disappointment, driving her heartbeat to quicken. 

After a moment, the owner focused on the patient’s health. Meanwhile, what sprung forth from the woman was none other than a receipt from the store, which utterly shocked the owner. 

Upon seeing this, the owner could hardly come to terms with the absurdity of the revelation! 

The anxiousness of dealing with this situation weighed heavily on the owner, and yet here, she had successfully executed a heist to the tune of fifteen hundred won at the presentation of false goods. 

What a wild tale, yet from now until the moment they apprehend her—this would remain an unsolvable case! 

**Summary**

This article narrates a daring theft at a gold and silversmith shop in Seoul, committed by a woman posing as a wealthy client. Despite the audacity of her actions, crime remained hidden due to the victim's denial of the incident. The story highlights the clever methods employed by criminals even during the colonial period, illustrating the social dynamics of women’s roles and criminality in Korea at the time. This incident could pique the interest of historians studying criminal activity and gender roles during Korea’s colonial era, as it reflects both resilience and the complexities of societal expectations.

그는 들어오든마다 순전히 금속으로만 비나·반지·가락지·팔찌·귀개 등 장신구로만 시가 일천오백원어치의 흥정을 하엿다.
주인은 요즘가튼 불경긔에 일천오백원이란 엄청난 흥정에 마음이 흡족하야 너무도 당황하엿다.

『자아 이것의 갑이 모다 일천오백원이라지요.」
『네 네 그럿습니다.』

주인은 어대까지 침착평연한 그 녀인의 태도로 보아 쾌연히 응낙을 하고 택시를 불으고 점원하나를 뽑아 물건을 들려 보내엇다.

XX동 OO약방압!
자동차에서 내린 그 녀인은 유유한 태도로 택시갑을 무러준 후 약방대문압까지 이르러 물건 가저온 점원에게

주인은 사람을 내 보내여 문 밧게서 기다리는 점원을 불너 드리엿다.
『너무 오래 기다리게 해서 미안합니다.』
『온 천만에 말슴을 다 하십니다.』
주인이 보니까 나희가 인제 겨우 스물안팟게 안된 새파랏케 젊은 사람이라 속으로
『생기기도 잘 생긴 사람이 더구나 나희로 보아 가엽기도 하다.』
하고 동정이 끄러 올낫다.
『자아 잠깐 이방으로 드러오시지요.』
『아니요. 들어갈 것까지는 업습니다. 돈만주시면 그냥 가겟습니다.』
주인은 녀인에게 들은 이야기가 잇는지라.
『흥 딴은 ...돈 때문에 밋친 사람이 분명한걸!』
『글세 돈은 드릴터이니 잠깐 이리 들어오시오』

하고 물엇다.

『자아 인제 한담은 그만두고...대관절 언제부터 그럿소』
하고 그의 손목을 잡엇다.
『이 냥반이 밋첫나. 뭐가 언제부터 그럿탄 말이요...어서 돈이나 줘요. 나는 밧븐 사람이외다.』

놀나움과 실망 또 흥분으로 심장과 호흡에 급격한 변화로 인하야 급히 뛰는 맥박을 주인은 어대까지 병적으로만 녁이게 되엿다.
얼마후 주인은 확실히 병으로만 따지고 처방까지 생각을 하엿슬 때 환자의 몸에서 튀여나온 것은 상점의 령수증이다.
