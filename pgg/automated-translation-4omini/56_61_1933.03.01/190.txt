기사제목	胃腸病과 神經衰弱 (Diseases of the Digestive System and Nervous Exhaustion)  
필자	大杉司朗 (Osugi Shirō)  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

In the beginning, I worked diligently, but after a month or two, I realized that for some reason, despite being young, my body felt heavy, and I couldn't focus on my daily tasks; my thoughts wandered. Among these, I lost my appetite and began to have restless nights full of dreams. When I mentioned this to my employer, he said, "It must be fatigue; if you work more, you'll feel better," and didn't really offer me rest. Unable to bear the torment and burdened with anxious thoughts, I worked hard for about twenty days, only to end up suffering from a headache and having to lie down.

Amidst this despair and unpleasantness, one day I stumbled upon an article in a magazine about "Tablets for Digestive Health." Initially thinking it was just another medicine advertisement, I read it; however, I began to wonder if this medicine might cure my illness without telling my parents. 

So I took the "Tablets for Digestive Health" (四錠) for the first time, and that night I surprisingly slept well, astonished at its effectiveness. After continuing to take them for ten days, my once-troublesome digestive issues, headaches, and listlessness disappeared, and I felt a natural desire to work again. I shared this news at home, and my grandmother was so pleased that she even shed tears.

The reason we recognize illness or experience weakness arises from a deficiency in the nutrients necessary for body constitution and a decline in enzymatic action. Therefore, by adequately supplying these, it is natural that both illness and weakness can be recovered. However, in the past, achieving this was quite challenging, resulting in many people suffering from illnesses without relief.

Recently discovered "Tablets for Digestive Health" were developed by Professor Sawamura Makoto of Tokyo Imperial University, who painstakingly researched to create an oral medicine from the "He-Way" (ヘ-훼) strain. It is designed to supply essential enzymes and nutrients without any harm, with the goal of restoring health.

The issue then arises: what role do these enzymes play in the body? The interactions of these enzymes are quite complex, making it difficult to explain easily; however, generally speaking, they primarily energize the gastric tissue cells involved in the digestion and absorption of all nutrients and invigorate various cells throughout the body to recover different dysfunctions and enhance their capabilities, while also acting to kill harmful germs that invade the body, increasing the white blood cells. As a result, metabolism is enriched, resistance is boosted, and the body's ability to heal is fully manifested. Additionally, "Tablets for Digestive Health" are formulated in a way that allows easy absorption of essential nutrients such as "carbohydrates," "fats," "proteins," vitamins, and minerals, all of which aid each other in the healing process. Thus, they are distinctly different from traditional symptomatic or chemical products, as they are biological preparations.

Scope of Applicable Diseases

The range of uses is extremely broad, and various diseases such as "tuberculosis," "digestive disorders," "pleuritis," "nervous exhaustion," "scarlet fever," and others related to pregnant women and infants, are all significantly certified to have unique effects. 

  
**Summary**  

The article discusses the author's personal experience with digestive issues and nervous exhaustion, leading to the discovery of a medicine named "Tablets for Digestive Health," which claims to effectively restore health by supplying essential nutrients and enzymes. This highlights the intertwined nature of physical and mental health and reflects on the medical practices of the era, where traditional remedies and new scientific approaches were beginning to merge. Historians may find the references to contemporary medicinal developments illuminating, especially in terms of how they intersected with the broader context of health during Korea's colonial period, where access to effective medical treatments was unevenly distributed.

胃腸病과 神經衰弱
						大杉司朗
(전략-원문) 처음에는 열심히 일을 봐왓지만 이삼개월이 되고 본 즉 어쩐 일인지 나 젊은 내 몸이지만 뭐라고 말할수 업시 머리가 무겁고 매일하는 일에 귀치 안흔 생각이 떠돌앗습니다. 그 중에도 밥맛이 업스며 매일밤 꿈만 뀌게 됨으로 주인에게 말을 하니까 『멀...감긔가 든것이니 일하면 절노 낫겟지...』하면서 별로히 쉬라구도 아니합니다. 할수 업시 괴롭고 쓸아린 생각을 하면서 이십일가량 무리를 해서 일을 하니까 결국은 두통이 나서 자리에 눕게까지 되엿습니다.
(중략-원문) 이러한 절망과 불유쾌한 즁에서 세월을 보내든 중 어떤 날인가 모잡지에 「錠劑와까모도」의 긔사가 잇슴으로 이것도 역시 매약광고겟지 하면서 읽어 보앗지만 어듸까지 이 약이 내 병을 고처줄 것 가튼 생각이 들어서 양친에게도 말슴을 엿줍지 안코 삿습니다.
그래서 제일회에 「錠劑와까모도」(四錠)를 먹엇드니 그 밤엔 잠이 잘 와서 이처럼 효력이 대단한가 하고 놀나다 십히 햇습니다. 열심히 게속해서 복용한후 열흘째 되는 날은 참으로 괴로웟든 위장병도 낫고 두통, 현긔 다 업서저서 자연 일해보고십흔 생각이 남으로 집안에 이야기를 햇드니 할머니는 하두 깃버서 눈물까지 흘니섯담니다.(후략-원문)

講評

대체 우리들이 병을 알는다든가 쇠약해진다는 것은 신체조성에 필요한 영양소(榮養素)의 결함과 효소작용(酵素作用)의 쇠퇴로부터 일어나는 것입니다. 그럼으로 이것들의 보급만을 충분케하면 자연 병긔나 쇠약도 회복식힐수 잇는 까닭이지만 어제에는 이것이 퍽 어려움으로 해서 병에 걸니는 사람이 졸연이 끈처지 안는 것입니다.
그런대 최근에 발견된 「錠劑와까모도」는 『헤-훼』균을 내복약으로 맨들기 위해서 동경제대 명예교수 澤村眞박사가 고심연구한 것으로 그 성분이 조금도 해가 업게 전긔 『효소』와 『영양소』를 보급해서 건강의 회복을 목적해서 적당하게 만든 것이라고도 하겟습니다.
그러면 이 효소는 신체에서 어떠한 역할을 하느냐는 것이 문제입니다. 대단복잡한 작용이 잇서 간단하게 말슴을 다하기는 어려우나 일괄해 말한다면 먼저 일체의 영양의 소화흡수를 주로 하는 위장의 조직세포를 비롯해서 신체전체를 조직한 세포에 활녁을 주어 여러 가지 이상을 회복식히고 그 긔능을 올니며 혹은 체내에 침입한 유해의 균을 죽이는 작용을 하여 백혈구를 늘켜줍니다. 그 결과 신진대사작용도 유감업시 되어 저항력이 증대해저서 신체의 치료능력도 충분히 발휘될 수 잇스며 그외에 「錠劑와까모도」는 『함수탄소』, 『지방』, 『담백』 각종 『비타민』 『영유』등 신체보건상 필수의 영양소를 흡수하기 용이한 형태로 되여잇서 서로서로간 도음이 되여 치료케 되는 것입니다.
이와가티 「錠劑와까모도」의 재래의 대증적이나 화학적 제품과는 판연이 달은 생물제재입니다.
