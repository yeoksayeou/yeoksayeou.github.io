Prize Question  
In this issue, we have a special reading material: "The Strange True Story"『피무든 手帖』 and the detective novel "The Jealous Demon"『嫉妬하는 惡魔』. How many main characters are there in each of these two selected readings? Please simply indicate the number of characters from each, writing only "the number of characters in The Strange True Story" and "the number of characters in The Jealous Demon" and send that back to us.  

Rules for Prize Submission  
★ Responses must reach the head office by the eighteenth of next month (March).  
★ Write your answer concisely on a postcard, ensuring to include your address and name clearly.  
★ You must write the reader's certificate provided on this page in your answer sheet as well. If you do not include this, your entry will be invalid.  
★ On the envelope, you must clearly write 'Kyungseong Gaepiksa Prize Division' and on the top left write 'Manuscript Enclosed' in dark ink, then seal it. Place a two-jeon stamp on it. If 'Manuscript Enclosed' is not written, you will incur a penalty (insufficient funds).  

Prizes  
First Prize for one person: Specially made decorative table clock  
Secondary Prizes for ninety-nine people: A copy of a book  
Awarding  

Correct Answers from Previous Issue  
[Question] "A youth named Lee Dong-sik and another youth named Park Soon-deok were both in love with a woman named Kim Eun-hee. Despite their deep friendship, when it came to this love, neither would yield, and they continued to secretly compete to win her heart. However, Kim Eun-hee, who was pure-hearted, thought of both of them. How could a woman like her simultaneously love two men? As a result, both youths received a letter at the same time, strangely requesting to meet at the same time and place. Rather than despairing, she confidently agreed to meet both at that time and place. The response needed to explain how this could happen simply requires a straightforward answer in ten characters."  

[Correct Answer] "It is absolutely impossible.”  
This was the correct response. In this issue, as we released the special phenomenon from the prize division for the first time, we received over 60,000 entries, yet not a single person answered correctly. Perhaps they overthought the problem. For a woman to meet two men at the same time and place is simply impossible. Although this was not the correct answer, some of the answers published below fittingly matched and were thus classified among the correct entries. Prizes will be sent accordingly.
