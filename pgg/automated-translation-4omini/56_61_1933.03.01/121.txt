連作漫談, 女子의 一生  
The newly initiated serial conversation "A Woman's Life" (女子의 一生) in last month's issue could not continue in this issue due to special circumstances affecting the writer. However, it will continue in the next April issue, so we kindly ask you to understand and look forward to the next issue!

별건곤편집부백

**Summary**

The article announces the continuation of a serial called "A Woman's Life," which was interrupted in the current issue due to the writer's unforeseen circumstances. It establishes a connection with readers, urging them to anticipate the next installment. This article reflects the challenges faced by writers under colonial rule and highlights the struggle for women's voices in Korean literature during this period. Historians might find the interruption of the serial and the emphasis on women's life narratives particularly revealing about the socio-political context of the time.
