Even if Deprecated, the Value of Classical Literature and Poetry  
						I Kwang-jong  
Since the beginning of history over 4,000 years ago, to write one line that can be boasted about to the world, let us inscribe the heroic strategies of Eulji Mundok and Yang Man-chun, who, with their bold and great plans, did not return to the Sui and Tang dynasties and dared not gaze eastward. Shall we write about Kim Yu-shin's miraculous schemes and divine might that unified the three kingdoms and expelled the neighboring countries? The achievements of Dae Joyeong, the might of Gaiso Mun, the endeavors of Gang Han-chan, and the annihilation of enemies by Chungmugong are all matters that can be recorded in stone and bamboo without the need for me to elaborate further, as they are already known to the public. I, the author, am an old scholar who was born and raised in peaceful times, and in this peaceful era, as military power and martial prestige are lacking due to the lack of attacking and defending strategies, I can only admit my inability to wield the brush and ink courageously. Thus, I wish to write about the literature and arts of Silla, the potteries of Goryeo, and the Hangul of Joseon... This is something worth noting. Before I embellish it, the world is already in admiration of these matters, so there is no room for me to add further embellishments.

Now there is one matter that can be appreciated, recited, and is worth contemplating. However, when I describe this matter, those who see it will surely spit at me, those who hear it will surely curse me, the elderly will laugh at me, and the young will mock me, thereby casting me away in great disdain. Among ten million people, whether one person will sympathize with me is questionable; even to affirm or support my words is out of the question, and rather, I expect to be criticized as a futile thinker. Nevertheless, I boldly write without hesitation. This determination should not be alarming; it concerns the so-called classical poetry and prose (科文) of Korean literature. This poetry and prose constitutes a unique category of Korean literature. Refuting or denouncing the fundamental law of this classical genre is another matter. However, the poetry and prose themselves, with their intricacies, transitions, tightness, and grand arrangement, are indeed a singular literary form in the world.

In this era of isolationism, which has lasted for hundreds of years, the techniques for advancing the nation and strengthening the military have been tossed aside, leaving heroes without a place to showcase their martial skills, and the teachings of Song Confucianism have bound our minds with iron wire, blocking even an inch of progress and thought. In the midst of this, the competition for power has formed factions, leading to continuous bloodshed.

Humans are creatures of instinct, while thoughts are living entities. The innate talents of our people, often emerging from the mountainous rivers of the northern temperate peninsula, have compelled us to engage in literature since our youth. Hence, we focused our efforts on creating poetry and prose during those times. Consequently, the innate talent of artistry was deeply embedded in our lives as poetry and prose, our ideals were poetry and prose, and our skills were also poetry and prose. Therefore, the masterpieces that are acknowledged today as great works, even when examined in the context of the Far East (支那) or distant Western Europe, stand unrivaled as selections of exquisite literature. Who would affirm these words? Nonetheless, I cannot help but lament. This literary form of poetry and prose encompasses literature, history, politics, and morals, while also encompassing philosophical thoughts, each shining brightly as a magnificent literary form.

Critics may ask if poetry can satisfy one's hunger or if prose can bring warmth, declaring that such useless recitations have left heroes with white hair. This is a childish remark that shows ignorance of the times, so it should not be harshly criticized. I, too, harbor no expectation to continue to advocate for poetry and prose in our time. Although the predecessors of Korea struggled tirelessly over the years amidst humble surroundings to create literary masterpieces, it is now almost impossible to acknowledge their spirits or recite their names in the changing times. Preserving the literature is essential for fostering our national identity, and gathering the fragmented remnants of the so-called Eastern people's masterpieces, we can collectively present their historical and unique brilliance to the world through an encyclopedic literary collection.

  
**Summary**  

The author laments the decline of classical Korean literature, particularly poetry and prose, asserting their inherent value even in the face of modern criticisms. He emphasizes that these literary forms encapsulate profound knowledge spanning several fields, arguing against the dismissal of their significance. This article serves as a critique of colonial attitudes toward Korean culture and highlights the enduring legacy of Korea's literary heritage, which historians may find insightful as it reflects the tension between cultural pride and colonial subjugation.

묵어도 價値 높은 科文詩賦
						李光鍾
有史 4,000餘載 以來로 세계에 誇張할 其一의 一言을 기술코자 하는 筆頭에 隋와 唐이 片甲이 不還하고 감히 東으로 睨視치 못하게 하든 高句麗 大將 乙支文德과 梁萬春의 雄韜大略을 書하야 볼가. 三國을 倂合하고 隣邦의 감시를 축출하든 金庾信의 奇謨神威를 大書特書할가. 大祚榮의 開拓, 蓋蘇文의 威勢, 姜邯贊의 樹功, 忠武公의 殲敵은 以上 皆 金石의 文字와 竹帛의 丹靑이 筆者의 必을 借하야 贅言을 不待하고 世人의 所共和임으로 更히 기술치 안이할 뿐 안이라 筆者는 迂踈한 老學究라 太平에 生하고 太平에 長하야 兵力과 武威는 涉獵의 攻苦가 素乏함으로 此에는 敢히 霑毫弄墨의＜115＞ 能力이 無함을 자인할 뿐이다. 然則 新羅의 文藝와 高麗의 窯品과 朝鮮의 한글…… 등의 此를 기술코자 함에는 筆者가 과장하기 전에 世人이 이왕 경탄하는 바이라 筆陣圖에 개칠할 여지가 無하다.
今에 一件事가 有하니 此可以詠嘆이오 此可以傳誦이오 亦 可以 思慮할 만한 一件事이다. 然이나 此 一件事를 筆者가 記述함에는 見者-必唾之오 聞者 -必罵之오 老的은 笑之오 小的은 擬之하야 嘩然哄然에 筆者를 遠惡地로 投棄코자 하리라. 1,000만人 中에 1個人이나 筆者와 同情하리가 有할는지 一例로 筆者의 言을 認定 又는 擁護는 고사하고 反히 中毒胎弊等語로 呶呶히 능멸함을 각오한다. 然이나 筆者는 大膽으로 筆을 주저치 안는다. 此 주저치 안는 筆은 勿驚하라. 즉 朝鮮 文藝의 所謂 科文의 詩와 賦이다. 此 詩와 賦는 文學의 一格을 別成한 朝鮮의 特有物이다. 根本的 科擧의 法을 부인하거나 폐단을 唾罵하는 것은 別問題이다. 但 詩와 賦의 자체 卽 其 文藝에 在하야 其 奧妙, 迭蕩, 緊着, 豪雄의 排置規模가 과연 世界의 유일한 文藝일다. 時代가 시대인 鎖國主義 幾 100年間에 進攻退守 富國强兵의 術은 笆籬邊에 付하야 英雄이 用武之地가 無하고 宋儒의 學說은 鐵絲로 頭顱를 縛하야 一寸의 思想이 向上 進展의 道를 墻壁가티 막엇섯다. 개중에 權利競爭은 偏黨을 成하야 殺戮이 陸續의 時代이다.
人은 動物이오. 心은 活物이라. 北溫帶 半島 山河에 往往 배출하는 吾民族의 天賦才藝는 其 精藝를 何處에 露出하얏노 不得已 自幼로 文學에 從事하얏다. 그리하야 당시에 發身하는 詩와 賦에 專力하고 마럿다. 그리하야 天賦의 才藝를 필생토록 寓情이 詩와 賦오 理想이 詩와 賦오 技能이 詩와 賦엿다. 所以로 屈指하든 巨擘의 一唱三歎의 傑作은 大陸 支那이나 遠地 西歐에 考證하야도 匹儔가 無할만한 精選의 文藝일다. 誰가 此言을 首肯하리오마는 筆者는 哀惜하기를 마지 안는다. 此 詩賦의 文藝는 科學인 文學, 史學, 政治, 道德을 包括하고도 又 以上 哲學의 思想이 전혀 其 自體가 燦爛한 一大 文藝이다.  駁論者 曰 詩可飽乎아 賦可暖乎아 하야 無用의 吟詠으로 英雄이 盡 白頭라 하리라. 此는 時代를 不知하는 幼稚의 言임으로 苛責치도 안이하다. 筆者인들 今日에 詩와 賦를 獎勵 繼續하자는 意見이 안일다. 朝鮮의 先輩가 松燈土壁에서 兀兀窮年하든 傑作의 文藝를 時代의 變遷으로 更히 其 魂을 弔하거나 其 名을 誦하느니가 天地를 回顧고하야 1인도 不可見이라. 文의 文藝를 保存하고 我民族을 憧憬함에는 幾 100人의 巨擘大匠의 傑作인 所謂 東人의 斷簡殘篇을 俱收並蓄하야 百科全書 文學篇中에 其 歷史的 唯一한 光輝를 世界에 公開하기 自足한다. ＜116＞
