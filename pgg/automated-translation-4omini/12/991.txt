美談을 募集합니다  

We invite contributions of beautiful stories, whether from ancient or modern times. Please send us many local beautiful stories regarding ambition, success, loyalty, courage, righteousness, chastity, and human emotions. Successful submissions will be published in this magazine.  

Submissions should be sent to the Editing Department of Byeolgeon-gon at <230>  

  

**Summary**  

The article is a call for readers to submit inspiring local stories that reflect values such as ambition, success, and loyalty. These narratives will be shared in the magazine to promote positive values in society. From a historical perspective, this initiative highlights the attempts during the colonial period to build a sense of national identity and cultural pride among Koreans as a response to foreign rule, showcasing literature's role in fostering communal values.
