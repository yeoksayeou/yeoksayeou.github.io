기사제목	萬波息笛 (Manpo Sicted, "The Flute That Calms the Waves")  
저자	-  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

In the time of Shilla's (新羅) Sinmunwang (神文王), a mountain appeared in the middle of the East Sea, floating along with the waves, moving this way and that. There was also a large vessel (大) present there, which seemed strange to the king. He consulted the Dayan (日官) and asked for an omen. The Dayan responded that if the king moved to that place, a great treasure would be assured to him. The king, filled with excitement, moved closer to the area and looked at the mountain. Suddenly, the heavens and earth trembled, and a great storm arose, making it as dark as night, only calming down after seven days. After the wind had subsided and the waves calmed, the king crossed the sea and ascended to the mountain, where he encountered a strange person. Inquiring about the large vessel's behavior, he asked why it floated during the day and submerged at night. The stranger replied that striking it with one hand would yield no sound, while using two hands would produce sound. The wise king was to govern the world through sound, and by taking the flute and playing it, peace would return to the kingdom. The king, greatly rejoicing, took the flute and named it Manpo Sicted (萬波息笛).  

**Summary**

This article narrates a mythological story from the Shilla (新羅) period about a king discovering a mysterious mountain and a flute that could bring peace to his kingdom. It reflects the importance of items like the flute as symbols of governance and harmony in Korean culture. Historians might find this narrative interesting as it illustrates the blending of myth and governance, portraying how mystical beliefs were utilized to reinforce the authority of rulers during Korea's ancient history, particularly in a colonial context where such narratives could serve as a counter-narrative to foreign domination.

萬波息笛
新羅 神文王 때에 동해 중에 山 한아이 떠서 물결을 따러가지고 이리 왓다 저리 갓다 하는데 그 산우에는 또 대가 잇슴으로 王은 그것을 이상이 녁이고 日官을 물너서 占을 치니 日官이 말하기를 王이 만일 그곳에 거동을 하시면 반드시 큰 보배를 어드리다 하니 王이 깃버하고 그 근처로 거동을 하야 산을 바라보니 별안간에 天地가 진동하고 風雨가 大作하야 아주 밤중가티 어둡더니 7일만에 겨우 바람이 개고 물결이 잠으로 왕이 바다를 건너서 그 산에 올으니 異人이 잇서 王에 뵘으로 王이 물어 갈오되 이 山의 대가 엇지하야 낫이면 난호어지고 밤이면 합하는고 하니 이인이 대답하되 此컨대 한 손으로 치면 소리가 업스나 두 손으로 치면 소리가 잇는 것과 가트니 聖王이 소리로써 천하를 다스릴 상서이니 대를 취하야 笛을 맨기러 불면 국내가 태평하리다 하거늘 王이 크게 깃버하야 從臣으로 대를 비혀가지고 나오니 異人이 忽然히 뵈지 안엇다. 王은 그대로 笛을 맨들고 이름하야 가로되 萬波息笛이라고 하얏다. ＜128＞
