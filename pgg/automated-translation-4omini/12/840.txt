발행일	1928년 05월 01일  
기사제목	본지기자二만명 (20,000 Reporters of This Magazine)  
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Opinion and Editorial Postscript)  

This magazine is particularly your publication, dear readers. Anyone can become an editor of this magazine. If you send in local news or interesting facts that you know, we will gladly include it in the magazine. 

In that sense, all of our readers are considered reporters, making a total of 20,000 editors. So, reporters! Hurry up and send in your articles. Local legends, local news, new romances flitting through the streets, thrilling investigative reports, tailing reports, accounts of women fleeing from their homes, narratives of young men committing suicide—anything that is a true story is acceptable. If your writing style is clumsy, that's not an issue. Even if it does not make it into the magazine, please send us details about the incident such as the place of occurrence, names, ages, etc., as clearly as possible.

**Summary**  

The article invites readers to contribute to the magazine and emphasizes that they can become part of its editing team. It encourages submissions of various types of stories, suggesting a community involvement in the publication. This highlights an era when magazines sought to engage their audiences actively, revealing a shift in media culture during the colonial period in Korea, where local voices could momentarily be amplified through print. Historians may find the engagement of everyday citizens in journalism reflective of broader social dynamics in colonial Korea.

기사형태	사고·편집후기

本誌記者二萬名
어느 雜誌던지 그런 말을 하겟지만은 특별히 더 別乾坤은 독자 여러분의 잡지임니다. 여러분이 누구나 본지 編輯員이 되신다는 말임니다. 여러분이 여러분의 지방 소식을 써 보내시던지 여러분이 아시는 진기한 사실을 써 보내주시면 곳 책에 내여드린다는 말임니다.
그러한 意味에서 본지 독자를 모다 기자로 처서 편집원 2만명이란 말임니다. 자- 기자여러분! 부즈런히 부즈런히 기사를 써 보내십시오. 地方傳說, 地方消息, 巷間에 니러난 新로맨스, 넌즛한 探査記, 尾行記, 洞里女子逃走記, 리옷靑年 自殺記 아모 것이라도 실재 사실이면 기사 안되는 것 업슴니다. 文體 서투르면 곳처서 냄니다. 冊에가지는 안 내드라도 本社에까지는 사건 發生場所, 氏名, 年齡 등 될 수 잇는대로 明記해 보내십시오. ＜193＞
