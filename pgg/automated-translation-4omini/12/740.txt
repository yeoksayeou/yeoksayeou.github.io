기사제목	唐太宗의 눈과 隋煬帝의 가슴 (The Eye of Emperor Taizong and the Heart of Emperor Yang of Sui)  
저자	불명  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

Emperor Taizong (唐太宗) of the Tang Dynasty (唐) lost one eye when engaged in battle with Goguryeo (高句麗) at Ansi Fortress (安市城). This is well-known to the public, but the fact that Emperor Yang (隋煬帝) of the Sui Dynasty (隋) was struck in the heart by an arrow during his defeat by Goguryeo is not common knowledge, as it is not preserved in the official histories. However, the Samguk Yusa (三國遺事) records that during an encounter with Goguryeo, one of their people concealed a small crossbow (小弩) within his clothing and followed the envoys carrying the national document to the enemy camp. As Emperor Yang read the document, the concealed crossbow was fired and struck his heart. Subsequently, the Sui army suffered a disastrous defeat and retreated. When Yang looked around at his surroundings, he lamented, "As the ruler of the world, I have come to assault a small nation, and yet this is the outcome. Truly, the bravery of the people of Goguryeo is unmatched in the world." (云云). <169>

**Summary**

This article contrasts two historical figures, Emperor Taizong of the Tang and Emperor Yang of Sui, emphasizing their respective military failures against Goguryeo. It draws attention to the lesser-known account of Sui's defeat, which is overshadowed by Taizong's better-documented experience. Historians might find this comparison significant as it highlights the military prowess of Goguryeo and its historical significance in resisting powerful neighboring dynasties, reflecting the complexities of Korean history during this period of colonial encounters and conflicts.

唐太宗의 눈과 隋煬帝의 가슴
唐太宗 李世民이 高句麗의 安市城을 침범하다가 우리 군사의 살에 마저서 한 눈이 멀게 된 것은 세상 사람들이 대개 알지만은 隋煬帝 楊廣이 高句麗에 패할 때에 활살에 가슴을 마진 일은 正史에 나지 안은 까닭에 세상 사람들이 잘 알지를 못한다. 그런데 三國遺事를 보면 隋帝가 高句麗와 싸울 적에 우리 사람 중 한 사람이 小弩를 품 속에 감안이 감추어 가지고 國書가지고 가는 使臣을 따라서 적진에 갓다가 隋帝가 그 국서를 읽을 즈음에 감추엇든 小弩로 맵시 잇게 隋帝의 가슴을 쏘와 맛치엿는데 그 뒤 隋軍이 참패하야 全軍을 퇴각할 때에 廣이 좌우를 돌녀 보며 하는 말이「내가 천하의 임군이 되어 親이 小國을 치다가 이 모양이 되엿스니 실로 만대에 붓그러울 바이나 엇잿든 高句麗 사람의 용맹은 참 천하무적이라 하얏다」고 云云하얏다. ＜169＞
