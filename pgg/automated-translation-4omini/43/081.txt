기사제목	學生諸君에 忌憚업는 投稿 (Unreserved Submission to Students)  
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Commentary)  

Students! There are those who call "Byeolgeongon" an agricultural magazine. However, that is absolutely not the case. Do you know how much this is welcomed by the poor and uneducated in Joseon? Dear students! Have you thought about sending a copy of "Byeolgeongon" to your rural homes? Write honestly about your experiences at school, in society, and on the streets, and send them in for publication. Let us publish them so that we may learn from one another. <15>  

**Summary**  

The article addresses students in Joseon, urging them to recognize the value of "Byeolgeongon" beyond its categorization as an agricultural magazine. It encourages them to contribute writings about their lives and experiences, fostering a sense of community learning and support. This call for engagement highlights the significance of grassroots sharing of knowledge among young people during a period marked by colonial oppression, which historians might find indicative of the emerging self-awareness and resilience among Korean youth.
