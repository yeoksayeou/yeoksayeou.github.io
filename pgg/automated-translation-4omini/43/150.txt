기사형태	소식 (News)  

The principal of Jeonhuimun High School, Kim Hyeongbae, has reportedly been working at the Chungmugong Heritage Conservation Society located at the Shupyojeong Joseon Education Association headquarters. Recently, it is said that he established a Higher Preparatory Academy. Considering the lack of prospects for educators in Joseon, one can also infer the presence of waves of unemployment sweeping across various sectors.

Similarly, Jang Eungjin, who served as the academic supervisor at Jeonhuimun High School and then the vice principal at Kyungsung Girls' High School, has recently moved to the Department of Education as an inspector. He has a humorous story to share from his time at Hui Mun School (before it became a high school). Due to the gymnastics teacher being unable to attend because of illness, a gymnastics class was unexpectedly skipped in one of the grades. Since he had no advisory period during that class, he called the students out to the courtyard and conducted a physical training session that they had never done before. 

“Attention! Number! Right turn! Halt!” was somewhat understood, but when he attempted to say, “Turn right!” and “Resume marching!” there was confusion. When they reached the end of the playground, a command to proceed back onto the street was not given either. The students were puzzled. Suddenly, the command “Stop!” was issued. With unfamiliarity regarding the command “Stop!,” the students hesitated and stood frozen. One student asked him, “Teacher! What does ‘Stop!’ mean?” “Stop! Don’t you know it refers to halting? It means to stop marching,” he replied.

Park Seungbin, the principal of Boseong Technical School, has long been devoted to unique research on the Korean language. Recently, he published a book under the school's name titled “Essentials of Korean Language Lectures” (朝鮮語學講義要旨) and "Guidelines for Writing Korean Using the Latin Script” (조선어를 라마자로 긔사함의 규태).

**Summary**

This article discusses recent developments within Korean educational institutions, highlighting the establishment of a new academy by Kim Hyeongbae and the career transition of Jang Eungjin to the educational administration. It also features a humorous anecdote illustrating the challenges faced by educators and students amidst a lack of proper communication in physical education. The publication of works by Park Seungbin further points to the ongoing efforts in Korean language research amid colonial constraints. Historians may find the mention of employment challenges and the evolution of educational frameworks during the colonial period particularly revealing, reflecting broader societal issues and efforts toward preserving Korean identity.

學界漫話
전휘문고보 교장인 김형배(金亨培)씨는 그간 수표정 조선교육협회 회관에 잇는 충무공 유적보전회에서 시무하신다더니 요새는 그곳에서 늘러서 고등준비학원(高等準備學院)을 설립 하섯다고 한다. 이것으로도 조선교육자의 말로가 얼마나 갈 데 업는 것을 생각할 때 또한 실업의 물결이 곳곳마다 드리인 것을 짐작할 수도 잇다.

역시 전휘문고보의 학감으로 경성녀자고보 교감을 지나신 장응진(張膺震)씨 최근에 학무국 시학관으로 옴겨 가섯다. 그런데 씨에게는 우수운 이야기가 하나 잇스나 휘문의숙(고보되기전)때 일. 체조선생이 병으로 출근을 못해서 어는 학급에 체조시간이 뷔게 되엿다. 그 시간에 마침씨에게는 담임시간이 업섯는지라 학생들을 교정으로 불러 내리워 전에 해보지 못한 체조훈련을 씨가 하게 되엿다.
「기척!」「번호!」「우향웃!」「압흐로 갓」까지는 조왓스나「도라 우편 압흐로 갓」과 「전대립!」을 몰랏다. 학생들은 행진하얏다. 그러나 운동장 끗까지 다가서 다시 거리로 나가도록 호령이 나리지 안는다. 학생들은 의아하얏다. 그러자「지!!」하는 호령이 황망히 나렷다. 학생들은 이 듯도 보도 못한「지!!」란 호령에 우물쭈물하고 섯섯다. 한 학생이 씨에게 무럿다.
「선생님! 지-가 뭐얘요!」
「지!! 그칠 지ᄉ자(止) 몰라. 행진을 정지하란 말이야.」하엿다고.

보성전문학교장 박승빈(朴勝彬)씨는 자래로 독특한 조선어연구에 몰두해 오시든 바 최근에는 학교명의로 「조선어학강의요지」(朝鮮語學講義要旨)와 「조선어를 라마자로 긔사함의 규태」라는 책자를 저술 출판하섯다고 한다. ＜26＞
