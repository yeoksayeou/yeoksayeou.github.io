기사제목	모르는 男子에게 바든 편지 (Letter to an Unknown Man)  
저자	仁載  
기사형태	문예기타 (Other Literary Genre)  

To XX,

I apologize for being so bothersome. I cannot help but think that this may cause you significant distress. Am I not pitiful, loving you (even if it is unrequited) and unable to avoid causing you pain? I hope you consider this even a little bit. 

Let’s give up! Stop writing! Yet, I cannot help but spend the beautiful autumn nights filled with falling leaves in tears, so I find myself again taking up the pen, only to tear the paper apart again and again until I finally write hopelessly. But what should I write? All the laments filling my heart have already been written in seventeen letters. Restating those laments will only bring my anguish anew, so I ask you to think deeply about this. 

I cannot help but confess that my entire life is tied to you, even the matters of life and death. 

Will you save this fading body that is about to breathe its last in hopes of your life, or will you just leave me as I am? 

Even if you are as unyielding as iron towards me, do you truly find satisfaction in the death of a young man spouting blood (even though you have not asked for it) because of you? 

If you do not wish to show me even the tiniest hint of kindness for one such as me, then please pierce my heart with your own hands and let me die. That would bring me happiness. I would close my eyes then, for you will see how much my blood burned for you. 

I will no longer bother you more than this. This may be my final letter. Please just write even one letter word to me, however simple. 

With sadness,  

仁載 (Injae) 

Sister Yu-ram,  

Regardless of everything,  

Your precious and significant essence,  

The only treasure of its essence,  

If you have made up your mind, I beg you to guard it even more strictly and pay attention to it once again.  

Sister,  

I sincerely believe that for the sake of this world,  

You will be able to see the absurd existence of this farcical society filled with laughter.  

Sister, let’s laugh greatly.  

Indeed, is there not a great deal of joy in letting out a hearty laugh?  

Ah ha ha ha  

Sister,  

Even if tears melt away, my distant eyes are still teary.  

Sister,  

But that person has not yet been met. With infinite conversation, I align them for advice and guidance, wishing to share this with you. 

Am I just a foolish dreamer?  

Still? This is my heart.  

Is it possible that dreams are, in fact, realities?  

I will not speak more, but I wish for,  

Sister, let us be together more joyfully.  

The end.  

This insignificant existence may be for your sake...  

It may be a page of history that you can hold for your own future.  

Please contact me at the address below.  

All my like-minded acquaintances have returned home, and I alone am studying diligently.  

To Cheongjin-dong 22X, Park In-seon.  
  

**Summary**  

The article is an emotional letter expressing deep affection and despair directed at a woman who remains unnamed. The writer conveys feelings of hopelessness and longing while pleading for a sign of recognition or kindness from her. This heartfelt correspondence highlights the complex emotional struggles of individuals during the colonial period in Korea, reflecting broader themes of love, suffering, and the longing for personal connections amidst the backdrop of societal constraints. Researchers may find it significant as it provides insight into the personal sentiments of individuals in a turbulent era, showcasing how personal and national identity were often enmeshed.

「이줄수 업는 XX씨께」

너모 추근추근하게 구러서 미안합니다. 이것이 당신에게 대단한 괴로움이 될 것을 웨 모르겟습니까? 알면서도 사랑하는 (외쪽 사랑이지만) 당신에게 괴로움을 드리지 안코 못 백이는 이 가엽슨 사람이랍니다. 이 점을 조곰이라도 생각해 주시는지요.
단념하자! 쓰지마자! 하면서도 락엽 지는 소소한 가을밤을 그대로 눈물로만 지낼 수가 업서서 쓰다가는 찟고 또 쓰다가는 찟고 하다가 기어히 실업슨 붓을 또 들고 마럿습니다. 그러나 무엇을 쓰겟습니까. 가슴에 가득한 모든 하소연은 벌서 열일곱 번 편지에 다 쓰고 마럿습니다. 그 하소연을 또 되푸리하야 괴로움을 낏치지 안켓사오니 생각하야 주십시요.
저의 일생의 모든 것 더 나아가서 죽고 사는 것까지도 XX씨 당신에게 매워 잇는 것을 진심으로 고백하지 안을 수 업게 되엿습니다.
XX씨 인생의 희망에 대하야 숨이 끈어저 가는 이몸을 살리시럅니까 그대로 두어 버리시럅니까?
아모리 리지가 철석가튼 당신일지라도 당신으로 인하야 (당신이 요구한 일이 아니지만) 새파란 젊은이 하나가 피를 토하고 죽어 너머진다면 그리 속 시원하실 것이야 무엇 잇습니까.
그러케까지 「가다고이」하는 저에게 악착한 형벌을 내리지 아느시랴거든 이 못난 놈에게 조고마한 표적이라도 보여 주십시요 네?
만약 그것이 못 된다면 차라리 당신의 손으로 저의 심장을 찔러 죽여 주십시요. 이것은 저로서 행복이겟습니다. 눈을 감고 죽겟습니다. 그 때는 나의 피가 얼마나 당신을 위하야 불타 올랏든가를 당신의 눈으로 보실 터이니까요.
XX씨 이이상 더 추근추근히 구러 드리지 안으려 합니다. 아마 이것이 최후로 드리는 글 일는지도 모릅니다. 조고마한 조희쪽에 글자 한자만이라도 적어 보내 주십시요.
