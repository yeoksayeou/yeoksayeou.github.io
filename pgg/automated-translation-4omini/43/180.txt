기사제목	號外의 號外 (Extra of the Extra)  
기사형태	세태비평 (Social Critique)  

As the Manchurian Incident quieted down, the extra editions of various newspapers that had frequently appeared for a while suddenly became scarce for several days. However, after the air raid incident in Jinzhou occurred, the political situation reverted back to tumultuousness, bringing about several complex issues, and the extras of newspapers began to reappear. Nevertheless, in some newspaper extras, there are more articles on tennis, sponsored by Jaegisa (自治社), than those related to the Manchurian Incident. As a reader, one is grateful for even a blank page, but during this sensitive time, how about limiting such articles to an extent in the extras?

Not long ago, there was an article in a social section of a newspaper about a local person who was bitten by a rabid dog and died due to poisoning after taking tiger bone (호골) and a "half cat" (반묘). They prominently featured "poisoned by tiger bone and half cat" as a major headline, while the minor headline confusingly stated they had ingested tiger bones and cats. While "tiger bone" is indeed tiger bone, the term "half cat" can literally be interpreted as "cat," but in reality, it refers to insects called "gyeolchung" (갈충) or "gyeolgui" (갈귀).

In the spring, there are often reported cases in newspapers under the title of "fugu poisoning" (하돈중독), stating someone got poisoned after eating fish that had been provoked — seemingly absurd articles published by writers. It seems they might also erroneously interpret half cats as cats. <27>  

**Summary**  

This article critiques the irresponsibility of newspapers in reporting sensational stories, particularly in the context of the Manchurian Incident and its aftermath. It notes the odd juxtaposition of serious news like the political upheaval being crowded out by trivial stories, revealing how media priorities did not align with broader socio-political developments. Historians might find it interesting how media narratives shaped public perception during a crucial period in Korea’s colonial history and how trivialization of serious issues could reflect societal conditions.

號外의 號外
만주사변이 잠잠하게 되니까 한참동안 쏘다저 나오던 각 신문의 호외도 몃칠동안 볼 수가 업더니 일번비행긔의 금주폭격(錦州爆擊)사건이 생긴 뒤로 시국은 다시 돌번하야 여러가지의 복잡한 문제가 생기며 신문의 호외가 다시 쏘다지기 시작하얏다. 그러나 어떤 신문의 호외에는 만주사건보다 자긔사에서 후원하는 정구운동(庭球運動) 긔사가 아모때에 발표하야도 조흘 허드래 긔사를 더 만히 실는 일이 잇다. 독자로서야 백지 한 장을 그저 주어도 고맙지만은 여러사람의 신경이 예민한 이 때에 호외에 다 그런 긔사쯤은 약하는 것이 어떠할지.

조곰 얼마전 일이다. 모 신문 사회면 긔사중에는 어떤 지방 사람이 밋친개(狂犬)에게 물니어서 약으로 호골(虎骨)과 반묘(班猫)를 먹고 중독되야 죽엇다는 긔사가 잇는 데 큰 제목에다 호골과 반묘에 중독이라고 대서특서를 하고 작은 제목에는 엉뚱하게 호랑이 뼈와 고양이를 먹고라고 하얏다. 호골이야 물론 호랑이 뼈이니까 문제가 아느 되지만은 반묘는 글자로 보면 알녹알록한 고양이로도 볼 수 잇스나 실상은 반묘는 고양이가 아니요 「갈충」 혹은 「갈귀」라고 하는 곤충(昆蟲)의 이름이다.

봄이면 신문에 흔이 나는 긔사중에 하돈중독(河豚中毒=河豚은 복어의 별명)이란 제목밋헤 물도야지 고기를 먹고 중독되엿다고 엄청난 긔사를 쓰시는 긔자선생님들이 종종 잇는 터이닛가 반묘를 고양이로 알기도 용흥무괴일 듯. ＜27＞
