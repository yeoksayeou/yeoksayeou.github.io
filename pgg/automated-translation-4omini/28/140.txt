기사형태	소식 (News)  

A certain Mr. Dodges, Bernard, and Shaw's observations on America recently mentioned to magazine correspondence reporters that friends are urging them to visit America without any "reason," and that just trying to bravely make the voyage would not be a bad idea.  

On May 31, in commemoration of the 25th anniversary of Whitman's birthday, the Walt Whitman Society in Chicago is preparing to hold a commemorative dinner and ceremony at the Morrison Hotel on that day.  

The distinguished speakers at the commemorative event on that day include Mr. Frederick Babcock of the "Trial" newspaper in Chicago, and Mr. Huelan Jones, literary section chief of the Chicago evening newspaper, among eight others. Mr. Jones, known as a critic of popular literature, is highly anticipated for his speech that evening. The famous professor Robert Park from Chicago University and the well-known skeptic Mr. Clarence Taro are also notable speakers — the event is expected to be a grand affair.  

Dovid Herbert Lowrance has passed away at the age of 45 on March 3 in Vence, France (southern). His departure from the English literary world is a considerable loss to the current literary scene, but more so, it is a cause of deep sorrow for the Poetry Society and Harriet Monroe. It is worth noting that since the Poetry Society was established in 1912, Mr. Lowrance greatly assisted their eight members, and the fact that Miss Harriet Monroe is currently editing Mr. Lowrance's "Life and Letters" is also quite fitting. <68>  

**Summary**  

This article provides insights into the British literary landscape in 1930, focusing on key figures and events surrounding Walt Whitman's 25th birthday commemorative celebration. It highlights the camaraderie within literary circles, the support for emerging voices, and reflects on the loss felt due to the death of Dovid Herbert Lowrance, an influential literary figure. For historians studying Korean colonial history, the article emphasizes the connections and cultural exchanges between Korean intellectuals and Western literary movements during a time of significant societal change.

英國文壇片信
쪼지. 뻐나드. 쑈-의 渡米觀  近者 氏는 雜誌 通信記者들의게 말하기를 諸 友人들이 米國으로 한번 오라구 하나 『理由』업시 渡米하기는 실타고. 하기는 한번 뱃심 부리다가 오는 것도 그럴듯할 일-.

휘트맨-의 25주년 기념을 5월 31일은 『왈트 휫트맨』(Walt Whintan)의 생일 즉 25주년을 맛는 기념으로 市加古 「휫트맨」友悌會에서는 당일 기념 夕餐과 同 禮式을 「모리슨호텔」에서 거행하기로 준비중에 잇다.
당일 기념 석상에서 연설할 諸 名士는 市加古 「트리엘」 신문사 『푸레드릭. 빱콕』씨와 又는 市加古 석간신문사 문예과장 『휘엘닌. 쫀스』씨 등 외 8명이다. 『쫀스』씨는 平民文學批評家로 當夜氏의 연설을 기대하는 바도 不少하다. 그 市加古 대학 『로벨트. 팍』교수 또는 不思議論者로 유명한 『클. 라엔스. 따로-』씨 등으로- 모다 기념회는 성황을 豫期하고 잇다.

「로-렌쓰」-逝去  Dovid Herbert Lowrance 씨는 去 3월 3일 벤스(Vence)라는 곳에서 (佛國 南方) 45세를 一期로 하고 逝別하엿다. 氏까 英文壇에서 떠난 것이 現文壇의 손실도 크려니와 무엇보다도 『詩社』 「해이옛. 몬로」(Harriet Monroe)가 더욱 슬퍼한 일이다. 1912년 『詩社』가 생기면서 1914년에 와서 該社 同人 8인과 함께 『로렌스』씨가 極力 도움을 준 것을 생각하면-.
그럼으로 방금 「해리엣. 몬로」가 「로렌스」씨의 『生活과 書信集』을 편집 중에 잇는 것을 생각하면 역시 그럴 듯하다. ＜68＞
