Foreword  
It is delightful. July is the month when "Gaebyeok" (開闢) finally emerged in the world a decade ago. However, that delight is an illusion. "Gaebyeok" has already perished, leaving behind an empty place where the master (主人) once resided! A lament arises, shedding tears of regret.  

The sorrow of parents preparing for the burial of their deceased child is like a deep, somber reflection!  

Looking back, the brief life of "Gaebyeok" was a life in the midst of dust and turmoil, a life filled with suffering. It was a life of fierce battles and solitary struggles, a life without support. Yet the birth of "Gaebyeok" was magnificent; its advancement was grand, and its end was glorious. It was born from the essence of righteousness and fought for justice, and it perished while steadfast in its principles.  

The end of those who fight for righteousness is indeed tragic!  

However, "Gaebyeok" has perished, yet its shining spirit lives on, and the rich nourishment it has provided remains. If future endeavors cultivate that spirit with the nourishment it has left, then the work will continue, and the pace of progress will be even stronger; thus, we only wish to recall the perished "Gaebyeok" and ardently continue along the path.  

This fervent spirit is amply demonstrated by the new magazines to be issued soon, along with the current "Byeolganggun" (別乾坤), "Haksaeng" (학생), and "Eorini" (어린이), which center around the absolute support of two hundred thousand readers, and the ten major new plans are steadily being implemented. <1>  

**Summary**  

The article reflects on the ten-year anniversary of the magazine "Gaebyeok" and expresses longing and regret over its demise while highlighting the enduring spirit it leaves behind. The author emphasizes the tragic yet heroic nature of fighting for justice, suggesting that the legacy of "Gaebyeok" can inspire future endeavors in Korean publishing and activism. From the perspective of Korean colonial history, the text provides insight into the struggles faced by Korean intellectuals and publishers under Japanese rule and emphasizes the resilience of the Korean spirit through collective memory and ongoing efforts.

기사형태	논설

卷頭詞
반가웁다. 七月은 『開闢』이 十年의 옛날에 비로소 世上을 나아오든 제돌의 그 달이다. 그러나 반가움은 幻影이다. 『개벽』은 이미 沒하여 업고 돌마지를 바들 主人의 자리는 쓸쓸히 뷔이다니! 소스치는 感悔가 눈물을 자어낸다.
죽은 子息의 나희를 헤아리며 돌차림을 차리는 어버이의 情과도 가튼 寞寞한 哀想이여!

돌으켜 생각하면 길지 못한 『開闢』의 一生은 風塵中의 一生이요 苦難中의 一生이엿섯다. 惡戰苦鬪의 一生이요 孤立無援의 一生이엿섯다. 그러나 『開闢』의 출생은 雄雄하엿스며 그의 步武는 堂堂하엿스며 그의 最後는 赫赫하엿다. 그는 正의 精氣로 出生하엿스며 義를 爲하야 싸웟스며 節을 직히여 殉하엿다.
義를 爲하는 이의 最後가 悲壯함이여!

그러나 『開闢』은 沒하엿스되 그의 빗나는 精神은 살어잇스며 그의 끼친바 기름진 거름은 남어잇다. 後業이 때를 작만하야 그 거름에 그 精神을 培養하량이면 그 일은 뒤가 이어지며 나아가는 步調는 한層 더 굿세여지는 것임을 밋나니 이에 吾人은 沒한 『開闢』을 追憶하는 한便 압날에 펼칠 壯한 氣운을 힘겨워 미드며 오로지 압길을 걸으려 할 뿐이다.

이 熱熱한 氣勢는 末久에 發行될 新雜誌와 現在의 別乾坤, 學生, 어린이의 四大誌를 中心으로 二十萬 讀者의 絶對 支持 하에 十大新計劃이 着着 進行되는 것으로써 넉넉히 立證하고 남는 바이다.＜1＞
