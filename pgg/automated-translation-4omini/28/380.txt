前月 縣賞은 25,065人이라는 超人氣的 응모가 잇슴에도 불구하고 正解答이 겨우 3인 밧게는 안이 되엿슴니다. 여러분과 한가지로 매우 유감으로 녈이는 바임니다. 부득이 그 3인 중 추첨으로 특등 1인을 뽑앗슴니다.  

正解答  병아리  

特賞(모시周衣次一件)  
元山 銘石洞 105  金舜德  

普通賞(本社特製手帖)  
(京城)  朴完緖  
(牙山)  洪泰裕  

**Summary**  

The article announces the results of a county prize contest with an overwhelming 25,065 entries, but only three correct answers were submitted. As a result, a lottery was held among those three to select the grand prize winner. Notably, the correct answer to the contest was "chick" (병아리). This contest reveals the engagement of the population in activities despite the limitations imposed during the colonial period, illustrating both creativity and community spirit in Korean society at the time.
