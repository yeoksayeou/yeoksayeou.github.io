新懸賞問題  
This month is the tenth anniversary of Kaebyoksa (開闢社), so the prize question has been particularly planned with intention.  

Please think carefully and we look forward to receiving as many correct answers as possible.  

Question:  
1. What was the first newspaper published in Joseon?  
(You can see it in this issue's commemorative content.)  

2. When was the publication date of the Kaebyok magazine?  
(You can find it in this issue's articles.)  

3. Name five or more types of cloth produced by Koreans in the summer.  

If you know the answers, please write them on the form below and send them to the Kaebyoksa Prize Department by the 15th of this month, including your address and name.  

Prize: 1st place gets one piece of a traditional outfit (周衣) and 50 ordinary prizes (special notebooks) <175>  

**Summary**  

This article announces a prize contest held by Kaebyoksa to celebrate its tenth anniversary, encouraging readers to answer questions about the history of Korean newspapers and local textile production. It highlights a significant moment in Korean publishing as Kaebyoksa marks its decade, emphasizing participation and knowledge about Korean cultural history. Historians might find interest in the specific questions posed, reflecting the intertwining of cultural identity and colonial history during this period.
