기사제목	本號附錄그림 (Appendix Illustration of This Issue)  
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Announcement · Editorial Afterword)  

This issue's special appendix was to create a photo album of immense practical value with around 80 photos collected. However, due to the discussions concerning mail regulations and the time required for photo plate production not being completed by the publication date, this has been postponed to the next issue. As the summer season approaches, this edition focuses on illustrations that provide coolness. 

This is the first trial for a four-color print using Korea's only machine capable of adding a fifth color, which sacrificed six times the time and cost compared to other photographs. 

If you attach this illustration on larger black or dark green paper and affix it to the wall, the indoor atmosphere will become even more refined. 

(In the July issue for children, an additional booklet titled "Children's Song Sheet Music Collection" was included, and in the July issue for students, an additional booklet titled "Enlightenment Reader" was included.)<99>  

**Summary**  

The article discusses the postponement of a special photo album due to production delays while providing an illustrated focus for the current issue. It highlights the innovative use of a four-color printing technique, emphasizing the aesthetic improvements for indoor spaces through decor. This reflects trends in colonial Korean culture where there was an interaction between art, technology, and daily life. Historians may find the exploration of media and artistic expression during the colonial period particularly revealing, showcasing an era of creativity despite restrictions.

기사제목	本號附錄그림
기사형태	사고·편집후기

本號附錄그림
이번 號의 特別附錄은 實益萬重의 XX사진첩을 제작하기로 하야 실로 80여 사진을 모앗섯스나 우편물 규정에 관한 打合과 사진판 製版의 시일이 발행 기일까지에 未及하야 이것은 次期로 미루고 夏節案頭에 納凉됨즉한 그림을 골는 것임니다. 4색 인쇄를 한 것으로 朝鮮 안에 단 하나밧게 업는 기계로 加一色을 하기는 이결로 첫 시험을 한 것이요, 다른 사진에 비하야 6배의 시간과 비용을 희생한 것임니다.
이 그림보다 조곰 더 큰 黑色紙나 暗綠色紙 우에 부처서 壁에 부치면 실내가 一層 싀원해질 것임니다.
(어린이 7월호에는 별책 『童謠曲譜集』을 첨부하엿고 학생 7월호에는 별책 『啓蒙讀本』을 첨부하엿슴니다.)＜99＞
