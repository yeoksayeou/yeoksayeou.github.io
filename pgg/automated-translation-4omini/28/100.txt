新語大辭典  
(ㅋ) (고)部  
카-니봘 (Carnival) is a festival held before the Roman Catholic Lent, generally used to refer to parties and celebrations.  

껄-스카우츠 (girl-scouts) refers to the Little Girl Volunteer Corps, a new movement aimed at teaching young girls the significance of social life, similar to the Boy Scouts.  

캎셀 (Kapsel) is a German term. In Korean, it means capsule, which is used to ingest unpleasant-tasting medicines.  

카랏트 (Carat) is a unit measuring the purity of gold. Pure gold is defined as 24 carats, so 18-carat gold contains 18 parts gold and other metals making up the remainder.  

크레프트메니어 (Cleptomania) is related to the Japanese term for shoplifting, describing a type of thief who steals from shops or department stores.  

크레오소-트 (Kreosot) is a type of disinfectant that has recently been selling well.  

크로-붜- (Clover) is a plant resembling forage that grows in gardens or pastures and has clover-shaped leaves. The four-leaf clover is a symbol of love that lovers exchange in love letters for enjoyment.  

過激法案 is an abbreviation for the law aimed at suppressing radical social movements, severely targeting the propagation of communism and anarchism.  

群衆心理 refers to the psychological phenomenon where individuals, when part of a crowd, perform actions they would not consider when alone, indicating a tendency toward emotional impulsivity and conformity.  

國粹會 is an anti-socialist movement group in Japan, organized by individuals such as traditional fighters, rural soldiers, and construction contractors, known for their skill in combat and propensity for violence.  

國際主義 opposes imperialism, advocating for national activities based on the great principle of coexistence among all nations around the world.  

코로타이푸 (Collotype) is a type of photographic plate made by applying glue to a copper plate and using a photosensitive chemical for printing.  

코록케 (Croquette) refers to a Western dish made from finely chopped meat or ingredients like potatoes, shaped into circles and fried.  

콘트라딴스 (Contra dance) is a type of dance with participants performing to a 2/4 beat, originally from France and initially with two dancers, now typically involving eight.  

誇大妄想狂 refers to a type of mental illness characterized by grandiose delusions that often manifest during periods of intense neural stimulation, where the individual believes they are extraordinary.  

코닥크 (Kodak) is a portable camera using film, popular among women and reporters.  

콘쿠리-트船 (Concrete-ship) is a recently devised creation due to steel shortages, a type of boat encased in concrete, which provides benefits not only from being cheap material but also in minimizing damage from torpedo attacks. <51>  

**Summary**  

The article presents a variety of new vocabulary terms with definitions, reflecting the changing linguistic landscape during a time of modernization in Korea. It highlights cultural exchanges, technological advancements, and societal developments in the 1930s, such as the rise of movements like Girl Scouts and changes in social mores. Historians may find the inclusion of terms related to anti-socialist sentiments and public psychology indicative of the political climate of colonial Korea, revealing a society grappling with modern influences and identities during Japanese occupation.

新語大辭典
(ㅋ) (고)部
카-니봘(Carnival)  羅馬敎의 四旬祭 前에 잇는 祭로 一般 宴會와 祝祭에도 通用되는 말이다.

껄-스카우츠(girl-scouts)  小女義勇團 小女斥候隊로 뽀-이스카우츠와 가치 少女들에게도 社會生活의 意義를 理解식히고자 하는 새로운 運動.

캎셀(Kapsel)  獨語. 우리말로 膠匣이라 한다. 먹기 거북한 쓴 藥을 內用하는대 쓰는 膠囊.

카랏트(Carat)  金의 品位에 대한 單位. 純金을 24카랏트로 정햇슴으로 18金이라면 24分의 18分만 金이고 남어지는 다른 金屬이 석기인 것이다.

크레프트메니어(Cleptomania)  日本말로 소위 「만빗기」(萬引)라는 것으로 商店이나, 데파-트멘트 店頭에서 行하는 鼠賊..

크레오소-트(Kreosot)  요사이 잘 팔니는 殺菌劑의 一種.

크로-붜-(Clover)  牧草와 가티 생긴 植物이니 庭園이나 牧場가튼데 匐生하야 크라부形의 입분 三葉草 四葉 크로-붜-는 愛의 象徵으로 러브레터-속에 너어서 戀人들이 주고 밧고 하면서 즐거워하는 풀이다.

過激法案  過激社會運動 取締法案의 略稱. 共産主義. 無政府主義 等의 宣傳을 嚴重히 取締하기 위한 법안.

群衆心理  한 사람 한 사람 따로 떼여서는 생각할 수 업슬 만한 일이라도 群衆은 實行한다. 卽 反省力을 일코 感覺的으로 흘너서 熱하기 쉬운 附和雷同的 心理作用의 一種.

國粹會  日本에 잇는 反社會主義運動團體로 古來의 俠客, 在鄕軍人, 土木請負人 等의 一部 同志로써 組織된 것이다. 칼질 잘하고 暴力 잘 부리기로도 有名하다.

國際主義  帝國主義의 反對. 世界 萬國 共存의 大義에 의하야 國家活動을 營爲해 나가자는 주의.

코로타이푸(Collotype)  銅板에 膠칠을 하고 그 우에 感光藥을 써서 燒光하는 一種의 寫眞板.

코록케(Croquette)  잘게 썰은 肉. 감자(馬鈴薯)가튼 것을 동그랏게 만들어서 지저 낸 西洋料理의 일흠.

콘트라딴스(Contra dance)  舞踏하는 사람이 마조 보고 추는 2/4 拍子의 딴스. 佛國에 始作된 것인데 처음에는 춤추는 사람이 두 사람이든 것인데 시방은 普通 여들 사람으로 되엿다.

誇大妄想狂  神經刺戟이 强한 時節에 만히 생기는 精神病者. 自己가 대단히 훌륭하게 된 것처름 妄信하고 그런 類의 誇大忘訖을 하는 狂人.

코닥크(Kodak)  懷中用 輕便 필름 寫眞械. 婦人用. 記者用으로 잘 쓰인다.

콘쿠리-트船(Concrete-ship)  鐵材缺之으로 하야 最近 考案된 것인데 콘쿠리-트로 둘러싼 汽船인데 材料가 低廉한 것은 勿論이나 水雷의 攻擊을 밧고도 그 被害가 一局部에 긋치는 이익이 잇다. ＜51＞
