Special Notice 

An individual named 姜齊煥 (Kang Chi-hwan) with a fraudulent business card stating "Traveling Advertiser for the 10th Anniversary Commemorative Advertisement of Gaebyeoksa" is soliciting advertisements for our main office in regions such as Shin-ŭiju and Andong County. This individual is not an employee of our office and has no indirect connection whatsoever. We hope that you will not be deceived.  

**Summary**  

This article serves as a warning about an impostor posing as a representative of a publication to solicit misleading advertisements, emphasizing the importance of verifying the legitimacy of such claims. It reflects on the contexts of trust and deception in advertising during the colonial period, illustrating the potential exploitation faced by businesses and individuals amidst the complexities of Korean society under Japanese rule. Historians may find the article significant as it reveals the fraught relationships between businesses and the challenges they faced navigating a colonial economy.
