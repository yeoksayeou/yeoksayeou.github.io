기사제목	胎中奇談 (Tales from the Womb)  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

A 27-year-old young gentleman's claim to still be inside his mother's womb has recently astonished the academic world in Japan. A woman named Kobayashi Shizuko (小林靜子), aged 46, from Kobe has been pregnant for 27 years. However, despite reaching full term, she shows no signs of giving birth even after one, two, five, ten, or fifteen years, all the way up to this 27th year. Consequently, she has decided to undergo a cesarean section (切開手術) at Osaka Medical University (大阪醫大) to finally deliver the child still within her.

Upon examination, her abdomen appeared swollen like a balloon, and it was discovered that the development of the fetus was comparable to a normal one. However, due to excessive calcification, the fetus has turned into a "mummy" consisting only of dry flesh, skin, and bones. It was determined to be an ectopic pregnancy (子宮外姙娠), resulting in it being unreachable for a natural delivery. Over the years, the maternal body absorbed the amniotic fluid, leaving the fetus in a "mummified" state, which explains how it has remained in its mother's womb for this lengthy period. If this baby had been born, it would now have been 27 years old, potentially with siblings in the world. 

**Summary**  

This article discusses a remarkable case of a woman who has been pregnant for 27 years without giving birth, leading to the discovery of a calcified fetus in her womb. This extraordinary narrative caught the attention of the Japanese academic community and exemplifies the intersection of human biology and the medical advancements of the time. Historians of Korean colonial history may find this article interesting as it reflects the state of medical knowledge in the early 20th century, showcasing how health and societal perceptions of medical phenomena were shared across cultures, particularly under Japanese rule.

胎中奇談
이십칠세 된 청년신사 한 분이 아직도 어머니 어머니 배ㅅ속에 잇다는 거짓말 가튼 사실이 요지음 일본의 학게를 경탄 식히고 잇다. 일본신호(神戶)에 잇는 소림정자(小林靜子)라는 마흔 여섯 된 여자는 지금으로부터 27년 전에 임신을 하얏는대 만삭이 되여도 아모 소식이 업고 한해, 두해 오년, 십년, 십오년 결국 스물일곱해가 되는 금일까지도 의연히 소식이 업서 부득이 이번에는 배ㅅ속에는 아이를 그내기 위하야 대판의대(大阪醫大)에서 절개수술(切開手術)을 하기로 하엿다는 것이다.
이 녀자의 배는 풍선과 가티 불른 대「렌도겐」으로 본 결과 보통 태아와 가티 발육은 되어 잇스나 가석하게 되 석아(石亞)가 되여 마른 살과 피부 뼈 뿐으로 「미이라」가 되어 잇고 자궁외 임신(子宮外姙娠)이 엿기 때문에 나올 길이 업서 그 사이에 모체에 수분(水分)은 흡수되여 「미이라」가 되어 소위 석아(石亞)로써 지금까지 어머니 배ㅅ속에 잇섯든 것이라는 대 그 나희를 치면 스물 일곱 세상에 나왓드라면 아들딸을 몃 형제 식이나 두고 잇슬 것이다.＜29＞
