The World's Highest Climbing and Fasting Record  
Kandyi, the leader of the Indian national movement, undertook a fast for 21 days at the age of 70, in order to break the national burden of the so-called "Unexplainable Untouchability System" (不可解賤民制). The world was astonished at the completion of his mental strength. Compared to the highest fasting record of 71 days set by the world fasting athlete from Ireland, Leatinso Maguswini, Kandyi's fasting was not to be underestimated. Maguswini, who was formerly the mayor of a district and a national figure in Ireland, supported the "Singpuen" movement and was arrested by the British authorities. He went on a hunger strike in the prison, ultimately resulting in his death due to the consequences of a hunger strike that lasted for 71 days before his passing, four days after collapsing. This remarkable fasting record prompted significant attention in the medical community, leading German physician Dr. Arbita to conduct immediate research, concluding that the conditions of his prison life, such as the diet and temperature, were very reasonable for the continuation of fasting.＜57＞  

**Summary**  

This article highlights the fasting records of notable figures, particularly Kandyi and Leatinso Maguswini, emphasizing their remarkable endurance in the face of social and political challenges. The comparison between their fasting durations reveals the extreme measures taken by leaders to challenge oppressive systems. Historians might find the intersection of such personal sacrifices with broader national movements particularly significant in understanding the dynamics of colonial resistance and the ways in which fasting was employed as a tool for activism.

世界最高의 攀·食記錄
인도(印度) 국민운동의 지도자 깐듸씨가 70에 갓가운 로쇄한 몸으로도 소위 불가해천민재(不可解賤民制)의 민족적 폐중을 타파하기 위하야 21일간의 단식단행을 하야 정신력의 완성함에는 세게가 놀내엿스나 단식의 세게선수권 보지자 아일랜드의 지자, 레-틴소 마구스우이니-씨의 최고 긔록 71의 단식 긔록에 비하면 안듸옹의 단식은 감심 할 것이 아니다. 마구스우이니-씨는 일직이고- 구사의 시장까지를 지낸 아일랜드의 국사로 『싱푸엔』 운동을 지지하다가 영국관헌에게 채포 되야 논돈형무소에 드러가 기아전술로 행쟁하야 단식전후 71일에 실신하야 버리엿다가 드듸여 4일 후에 사망하얏다. 이 놀낼만한 단식긔록은 당시의 의학게에 일대 충동을 이르키여 독일의 의학자 아-빗다 박사가 즉시 연구를 한 결과 씨의 형무소 생활에는 수연이나 온도 광성 환긔 등의 모든 것이 절식 속행에 매우 합리하얏다는 것이다.＜57＞
