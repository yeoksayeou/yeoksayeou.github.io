기사제목	高句麗古墳 (Goguryeo Ancient Tombs)  
기사형태	잡저 (Magazine Article)  

The Goguryeo ancient tombs, known as the "Three Tombs of Gangseo" located in Uhyun-ri (遇賢里) of Gangseo-gun (강서군), are a global treasure showcasing the splendid culture of the Goguryeo era expressed through murals. These tombs attract both domestic and foreign visitors who seek the historical sites of Pyeongnam. However, due to the fading colors of the murals in the Donggobun (동고분) that have almost lost their original appearance, a model of the murals in the style of Mongolian tombs has been commissioned to a lecturer at the Tokyo National University of Fine Arts. The representations of the Zhuque (朱雀), Qinglong (靑龍), and Baihu (白虎) aspects have been completed, leaving only the Xuanwu (玄武) yet to be finalized. The completed model is displayed at the Pyongyang Museum, and it is said that the Gangseo tombs will forever remain sealed.

Regarding architectural value, the Cheonwangsasinchong (天王四神塚) located in Songgye-dong (松溪洞) of Suncheon-gun (순천군) is now undergoing restoration due to the significant damage caused by rain and rats, making it difficult to maintain its original state. This site is considered a relic from the Goguryeo era.

Currently, the eighth tomb in Jeonbaek-dong (정백동) of the Pyongyang Daan Ancient Tombs Region is under excavation as commissioned by the colonial government. While it is a wooden coffin (木槨), it has entirely decayed, and no funerary goods (副葬品) have been found. There are five large and small wooden coffins (木棺). In the southern chamber, there are five silver hairpins and one black object similar to a "diamond."

Excavation has also commenced for the thirteenth and seventeenth tombs, with findings from the 221st tomb revealing three bronze six jewelry (漆器), six clay goods (土器) rings, and ancient artistic and cultural treasures such as glass earrings. The 219th tomb yielded artistic value with five bottles featuring color decorations, symbolizing the abundant craftsmanship of the era.  

**Summary**  

The article discusses the Goguryeo ancient tombs, particularly the "Three Tombs of Gangseo," emphasizing their cultural significance and the ongoing restoration and excavation projects. It highlights the deterioration of the murals and the efforts made to preserve them as well as the discoveries within various tombs symbolizing Goguryeo's rich heritage. From the perspective of Korean colonial history, historians may find interest in the colonial government's involvement in archaeological attempts to preserve Korean history and culture amidst external pressures during the colonial period.

高句麗古墳
강서군강서면 우현리(遇賢里)에 소재한전세게적 자랑인 고구려평원왕능(高句麗平原王陵)인 속칭 강서 세무덤은 고구려시대의 찬연한문화를 벽화로 표현하고 잇거니와 평남의 고적을 찾는자 내외국인을 물론하고 반다시 이곳을 구경하게되여 동고분의 벽화는 손삼변색하야 원형을 일허버일 지경임으로 몽고분의 벽화의 모형을 동경미술하교 소장(小場)강사에게 위촉하여 묘산중이든바 발서주작(朱雀) 청룡(靑龍) 백호(白虎)의 삼면은 준공되엿고 목하현무(玄武)만이 남어잇스나 근일전부 완성을고하게되엿다 준공된모형은 평양박물관에 진렬하고 강서고분은 영원히 열지안토록 무더버릴 때 정이라한다.
◇
건축상가치로서 세게적으로 자랑한다는 순천군북창면송게동(順川郡北倉面松溪洞)에잇는 천왕사신총(天王四神塚)은 최근에는 비가새고 귀렴이생기여 원상을일허버릴정도에 달햇슴으로 수선을하기로 되엿다는대 갑빗싼고구려시대의 유적이라한다.
◇
평양대안고분지대 정백동(桓栢洞)에잇는 제팔호고분은 소장총독부촉탁이 목하발굴중이든바 목관(木槨)이되엿스나 전부썩고 부장품(副葬品)은 업섯고 목관(木棺)이 대소다섯개가 잇섯다 동측남관에서는 은으로맨든빗 가락지다섯개 흑색「다이야」와 흡사한것한개가 잇섯다.
◇
십삼호분과 십칠호분도 발굴을개시햇고 이백이십일호분을 발굴하엿는대 금동을 올닌 칠긔(漆器) 토긔(土器)세개 가락지여섯개와 고대의 예술과 문화를 자랑하는 유리귀고리가 나타낫고 이백십구호고분에서는 색 채를가한 이잇는 미술가치만점의 병 다섯 개를어덧다고한다.＜53＞
