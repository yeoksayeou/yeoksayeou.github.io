Extra Edition of Extra  
What is this "extra edition"? I thought there was a flood or a war in South Joseon, but it seems there is nothing happening.  

Lately, every newspaper has been dealing with cartoons. The Maeil Sinbo has juvenile cartoons, the Chungang Ilbo has Jeong Man-seo, and the Chosun Ilbo has Eongteongguri and Byeokchangho.  

The juvenile cartoons in Maeil Sinbo—since the author is just a boy and it is a practice work, it is not worth much debate.  

Jeong Man-seo in Chungang Ilbo—if the lines and explanations were more "cartoonish," it might be better. But even then, it is somewhat difficult to be defined clearly as a cartoon.  

Byeokchangho in Chosun Ilbo—while it is true that some readers find it popular, it doesn't seem to resonate with the general public; it tends to be too sophisticated and can easily be misunderstood when trying to grasp its humorous aspects.  

Eongteongguri—it truly is a mess. It is only somewhat funny, but it is presented as humorous, which is absurd. The author, Kim Hwa, who is also in charge of current affairs cartoons for Chosun Ilbo, is certainly admirable for his technique in satirical cartoons, but he seems utterly unable to handle the nonsensical humor of Eongteongguri. Lately, Eongteongguri has even been criticized for insulting its "ancestor."  

The Chungang Ilbo has moved into the former Chosun Ilbo building and expanded its pages from four to six, greatly increasing its advertisement capacity and raising its profile.  

This is certainly good news.  

However, the real issue lies more in the tightness of the pages rather than just gaining momentum through advertisements.  

To be honest, if you look at the Chungang Ilbo's pages, they seem a bit loose. There is no tightness or freshness visible.  

If things continue like this, the sacrifice of increasing from four to six pages while also taking on the burden of an increased expenditure of sixty won might end up being pointless.  

I hope the pages will maintain more of a focused and engaging essence.  

The Chosun Ilbo has established its subsidiary, the Stock Company Chosun Ilbo. With this (capital of three hundred thousand won), the financial basis appears solid. I hope for further growth.  

As for being a stock company, the names of the board of directors presented do not seem to be well-known figures; it is curious who they actually are.  

Large newspapers proceed with a news-centered approach. However, the Dong-A Ilbo is considered a large newspaper, leading to a curious news-centered direction.  

What an excellent three-part argument!  

Things like academic sections are reserved for second-rate and third-rate newspapers that lack advertisement. Look at large papers like Japan's Yomiuri Shimbun or UK's The Times; they are entirely dedicated to a news-centered format.  

Yet, when a heron is carrying a stork, that often leads to trouble.

The Sunday supplement allows for some academic content, which could be seen as a white flag of surrender for the academic section of the Great Dong-A Ilbo.  

**Summary**  

This article discusses the current state of cartoons and editorial content in various newspapers of the time, with a particular focus on the Chosun Ilbo and Chungang Ilbo. The author critiques the humor and artistic quality of the editorial cartoons, praising some while finding faults in others. There is also a commentary on the development and expansion of these newspapers during this period, suggesting an emphasis on advertisements and the financial structure regarding stock companies. Historians might find the discussion of expanding newspaper formats and the cultural significance of humor in media during 1930s Korea particularly interesting, as it reflects on the evolving nature of journalism in a colonial context.

號外의 號外
호외라니 제-니  남조선지방에 수재나 난줄알고 작 놀날것은엄다.
◇
요지음 각신문이 다루어 만화를 실닌다. 매일 신보에는 소년만화 중앙일보에는 정만서 그리고 조선일보에는 엉텅구리와 벽창호.
매일신보의 소년만화――이것은 그저 작자가 소년이오  습작(?)인 만큼 별로 시비들 켈것업고.
중앙일보의 정만서――선과 설명이 좀더 「만화」다웟스면 조켓다. 그래가지고는 만화로 생세하기는 약간어려울.
조선일보의 벽창호――일부 독자에게 인기가잇는 것만은 사실이나 일반적은 되지못하는 감이잇다 넘우 어려워서 그 유모러스함을 리해치 못하기가 쉬움단말이다.
엉텅구리――참 엉텅구리다 조곰도 우습지아니한 것을 우수운것이라고 내노흐니우수운일이다. 필자 김화(金華)씨는 역시 조선일보의 시담당자인데 시사만화에는 그수법의 묘함에 탄복하나 넌센스의 유모어만화이 엉텅구리에는 아주 무능한 것갓다. 요지음 엉텅구리는 그 「조상」을 욕하는늣김이잇섯다.
◇
중앙일보가 전 조선일보사옥으로 이사를하고 지면을 四면에서 六면으로 확장하고 그리하야 선전을 크게하며 기세를 올닌다.
물론 조흔일이다.＜48＞
그러나 문제는 선전으로 기세를 올니는 것보담 지면의 긴장여하에 잇는 것이다.
실례의 말갓지만 중앙일보의 지면을 보고잇스랴면 조롬이 오는것갓다. 투긔가 보이지아니한다.
이대로 나가다가는 가튼 六十전을 바드면서 四면을 六면으로한 귀한 희생도 보람이업서진다.
바라건대 좀더 지면에 바드득 죄이는 맛이엇도록――.
◇
조선일보가 주식회사조선일보의 동족을 맛치엇다. 이것으로(자본금 三十만원)재정엔기초가 온전한셈이다 압흐로더욱성장하기바란다.
주식회사라니 말이지 그취제역감사역들의 성함이 발표된 것을 보아도 대개가 모를분들이니 대관절 누구들인지.
◇
대신문은 뉴-스중심주의로 나간다. 그런데 동아일보는 대신문이다 그러니 아 뉴-스중심주의로 나간다.
훌륭한 三단론법이다!
학예면가튼 것은 광고가 모자라는 二류 三류신문들이 하는 것이다 일본의 대판 매일 대판조일이나 영국의 론돈타임스 가튼 신문들을 보아라 얼마나 철두철미 뉴-스 중심으로 나가는가――.
그러나 백새가 황새를 어가라다가는 가랑이가 저지는 법이야.
일요일마닥 일요부록 이라고해가지고 학예를 좀 돌아보게 되는 것은 대동아일보의 학예학대의 백기(白旗가 아닐는지?)＜49＞
