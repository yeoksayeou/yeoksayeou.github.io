Gyeongbuk Folk Song, Sassengga  
- This song is widely sung in places like Andong and Uiseong in Gyeongbuk. "Sasseng" means "to continue hemp," and since this area is known for its hemp fabric production, women often sing this song while working on hemp during the summer.

1  
- This verse is sung at night while working with hemp -  
The abundant sea and abundant virtue, the long hemp twine - "garin" means cap  
True treasure and green pine, candles made from pine -  
Our father beats the pine torch,  
Our mother weeps over the pine torch,  
While I play and laugh,  
My brother comes to hit me,  
I work all night with the hemp,  
My hands get tired,  
In Andong town, through twelve different neighborhoods,  
Every neighborhood sings a complaint,  
No matter how many complaints there are,  
Is it your fault or mine?  
Our father is the local headman,  
And our mother is the governor of Dongnae -  
This statement boasts of one's power.  

In the corner room, there is a courtesan sister -  
A girl of humble origins -  
The head supervisor says,  
Is it your fault or mine?  

2  
- This verse is sung at dawn while working with hemp -  
The moon is already setting,  
The rooster crows once more,  
Do nothing but whisper,  
And I will wake up again.  
True treasure and green pine, the long hemp twine,  
The abundant sea and abundant virtue, the pine pole -  
What's this connection to you?  
I want to go to dinner this evening,  
While going to work at dawn,  
I only wear a simple garment.  

3  
I carry a hemp bag and cross over the Wonyang Market -  
- Wonyang refers to a market in Munkyeong -  
Look at the heart of the people of Wonyang,  
They say it's worth five or six pennies.  
In the months of May, June, and July,  
I can't sleep at night -  
While working diligently,  
Both knees are already swollen.  
Little children ask for milk,  
And the older children ask for rice.  
Look at the actions of Kim Dongji from the back house,  
And see if they are pretending not to see me.  

**Summary**  

This poem, "Sassengga," intricately interweaves the daily lives of women in Gyeongbuk who engage in hemp production, reflecting their struggles and communal experiences. It also emphasizes the social hierarchy and dynamics at play within the community, revealing both the pride and challenges of the local populace. The boasting of local authority figures and the mention of economic conditions provide insights into social structures during Korea's colonial era, underscoring how local songs were a means of processing and voicing collective experiences and grievances.

慶北民謠, 사승歌
-이 노래는 慶北의 安東, 義城 等地에서 만히 유행하는 노래이다. 사승은 삼삼는다(續麻)는 뜻이니, 그 지방은 자래 麻布名産地로서 여름철에 婦女들이 삼을 삼을 때에 흔이 이 노래를 부른다.

一
-이 節은 밤에 삼삼을 때에 부르는 것-
盈海盈德 긴삼가리-가리는 꼭지라는 뜻
眞寶靑松 관솔가지-관솔은 松明
우리아배 관솔패고
우리올배 관솔노코
이내나는 비비치고
우리형님 나러치고
밤새도록 삼고나니
열손가리 반을축여
安東동내 열두동내
동내마다 송사가자
암만업시 송사가면
-암은 잘못이란 뜻-
네익이나 나익이지
우리아배 吏房戶長
우리올배 東萊府使
-이 말은 자긔의 세력을 자랑하는 말이다-＜2＞
골방妓生 얼동생아
-얼은 庶-
行首別監 얼三寸에
너익이나 나익이지

二
-이 節은 새벽에 삼 삼을 때에 부르는 것-
달은벌서 다젓는대
닭은웨이 또우는다
잔말만흔 싀어머지
이내잠을 또깨우네

三
미수가리를 걸머지고

-山陽은 聞慶市場-
山陽놈의 人心봐라

이삼저삼 삼을적에
두무릅이 다석엇다
어린아희 젓달나고
큰아희는 밥달난다
뒷집金동지 거동보소
