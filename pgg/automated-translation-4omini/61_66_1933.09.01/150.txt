Summer Resort Night Talk - Whose Sin?  

K Hotel's second floor consists of rooms, with three Korean youths, 甲, 乙, and 丙, seemingly asleep in the left room downstairs, while in the right room, a young Western couple delightfully whispers sweet nothings to each other in a drunken state. On the second floor, there are two more Westerners, A and B, who seem to be quietly asleep.  

- In the right room downstairs -  

Western Man (sweetly) I've loved you for three years...  

Western Woman (even sweeter) For three years, I've loved you...  

Western Man I secretly adored you.  

Western Woman I've been in love with you.  

Western Man Kiss!  

Western Woman Love!  

Western Woman (silently pursing her lips)  

Western Man (holding her in his arms) Chul! Chul!  

Western Woman (simultaneously with the man) Chul! Chul!  

- In the left room -  

Korean Youth 甲 (suddenly waking up) Hey! What’s that noise?  

Korean Youth 乙 Probably...  

Korean Youth 丙 (lifting his head and looking at 甲) Isn’t that the sound of lips meeting, “chul”?  

甲 Haha, just a bit more...  

甲 (gesturing) Shh! Quietly (speaks softly)  

乙 (getting closer) Hey, let me see!  

甲 Come on...  

丙 (getting closer) Let me see too...  

- At this moment, from the right room again -  

Western Man, Woman Chul! Chul!...  

甲 (pulling his face away from the door) Ugh, that’s disgusting. (shakes his head) What a nuisance!  

乙, 丙 (pressing their faces against the door) Check! What a shame! The audacity! What a nuisance!  

甲 Hey, let’s make some noise while they’re being disgusting. How about it, huh?  

乙, 丙 (hesitant) Sure! Sure! Let’s make a ruckus!  

甲 (deliberately singing louder) Ah- this is love—  

乙, 丙 (following) Ah- this is a tragedy—  

甲 (singing even louder) The young white people making smooching sounds— Keeping the young men awake at night— Ah-ra-rah— Oh-ya- more- how the eastern quarrels are resented—  

- With such noise, the Westerners A and B, who were sleeping upstairs, wake up and come down, throwing water down in irritation.  

Western A Quiet!  

Western B Quiet!  

- The water splashes on Korean youths 甲, 乙, and 丙.  

甲 (yelling) Who threw that water!...  

乙, 丙 Must be those white people...  

甲 (runs into the hallway shouting at the upstairs) Quiet! Why are you throwing water?!  

- The people upstairs remain silent.  

甲 Are we just going to put up with this humiliation by the West’s youth?  

乙, 丙 Indeed! Charge!  

- Meanwhile, the young Western man and woman in the right room are startled and find their embrace interrupted.  

甲 Let’s go upstairs...  

- 甲, 乙, 丙 rush upstairs and bang on the door where Westerners A and B are.  

甲 Open up!  

乙, 丙 Hurry! Or we’ll break it down!  

Western A (opening the door and stepping out) What is this commotion?  

甲 What on earth is wrong with you old monkey? Why are you splashing water around?  

Western A You lot are so rude that...  

甲 Hey! What do you want to do by this?  

丙 You think we’re dogs or pigs?  

Western A Don’t you folks know courtesy?  

甲 Who’s not being courteous? You’re the ones being rude...  

乙 You young couple from your country making all that kissing noise have kept us from sleeping!  

Western A (smiling mockingly) We don’t mind making noise after 10 PM.  

丙 Then why throw water? That’s treating us like we’re not human!  

Western B (suddenly stepping out) Quiet! Let’s go down; we need to sleep!  

- As they are arguing, the hotel owner, looking anxious, rushes upstairs.  

Hotel Owner (with a frightened expression) What’s going on? Please stop making noise.  

Western A These people are complete XX people. If you don't kick them out, we will leave!  

甲 What? XX person? You bastard! (slaps Western A)  

乙, 丙 You jerk! XX person? (retaliates)  

- At this moment, Western B hastily goes down.  

Western A (stunned from the slap, standing there confused) Uy! An absolute XX person!  

乙 You jerk! You still think you did the right thing saying XX person? (slaps him again)  

Western A Uy- I’m leaving!... Is there a policeman here? Huh! Policeman (heads downstairs)  

Hotel Owner Why are you all like this?  

- Just then a policeman arrives.  

Policeman Why the violence? Huh? You can’t assault foreign guests! To the police station... (takes the three away)  

甲 Let’s go!  

乙, 丙 We just got some venting out; that’s not fair. Those people are completely insolent!  

- 甲, 乙, 丙 leave with satisfied smiles.  

Hotel Owner The guests should be the ones to apologize. Those white people are so insolent!  

(End) <37>  

  

**Summary**  

The play humorously depicts a late-night altercation between Korean youths and a Western couple at a hotel, as cultural misunderstandings lead to escalating tensions. The Korean characters react to the Western couple's intimate noises, while the West perceives their actions as culturally permissible. This moment brings to light the complexities of Western influence and the tensions of colonial dynamics present in 1930s Korea, revealing insights into the cultural conflicts of the era that historians may find noteworthy.

**Glossary**  

쭐	쭐 (chul)	Smooching sound, indicative of intimate actions, potentially an older term.  
양코백이	양코백이 (yangkobaegi)	Term used disparagingly for Westerners or foreign people, reflecting cultural tension.  
안면방해	안면방해 (anmyŏnbanghae)	Rude interruption, particularly in social contexts, highlighting offensive behavior.
