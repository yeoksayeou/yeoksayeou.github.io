기사제목	男女大討論, 子女結婚에 干涉을 할가 自由로 둘가 (Great Debate on Whether to Interfere in Children's Marriages or Allow Freedom)  
기사형태	설문 (Survey)  

干涉便  
申알베트  

I believe that parental or guardian interference in the marriages of young men and women is absolutely necessary in present-day Korea. Recently, there has been a saying that "all things are good, but the son-in-law of a house is unacceptable" (referring to a case last year where a certain man married a woman, but her parents opposed it). It is similar to the notion that while everyone agrees on freedom, it is commonly accepted that marriage freedom cannot be left entirely to individuals. If both parties are of adequate age and knowledge and enter into marriage freely after sufficient understanding and commitment, then such a marriage may indeed bring happiness and there will be no objections against it. However, in our current transitional phase in Korea, where the level of knowledge between men and women varies greatly, and societal and familial systems have not yet fully improved, it is unlikely that young individuals, without understanding and commitment, will come together solely based on the trends of the times or momentary feelings and emotions to engage in so-called free love or free marriages. In such cases, how harmonious will their family truly be, and how happy will the couple be? It is not uncommonly observed that those who once dreamt of a blissful union end up experiencing separation just a few days later. Such occurrences are well-known in our society. 

Generally speaking, interference in marriage means that it does not solely depend on the two parties involved, which can often lead to dissatisfaction between the couple and cause a breakdown in their relationship. On the other hand, free marriages, while claiming to be the result of the free will of the individuals, often lead to the exact opposite. Recent statistics on divorces presented in the newspapers indicate that the rate of divorce among free marriages exceeds that of interfered marriages. This is due to a lack of deep thought on the part of both parties, as they tend to marry purely based on fleeting emotions and feelings, and when those feelings diminish, the marriage can immediately fall apart. Interfered marriages are assessed more rationally by the supervising party, allowing for the two parties to weigh their greatest happiness and interests, which minimizes their dissatisfaction and prevents a complete breakdown as frequently.

In summary, in the current state of Korean society, whether from an individual, familial, or societal perspective, I believe that free marriages are utterly detrimental.

방임便  
申흥雨  

When it comes to marriage, it is not an exaggeration to say that it is a principle to know everything about each other, including personality, ideals, and health, through courtship and interaction. 

Marriage should not be merely about fleeting pleasure arising from the physiological impulses of momentary encounters. If looked at from a personal perspective, it starts as a matter of individual living, but from a larger view, it is a matter related to society or even the race. 

Considering how significant this matter is, it is something that should not be conducted blindly through old traditions passed down without conscious thought. The customary marriage process commonly practiced in Korea is a good example of this. 

If someone completely ignores the will and character of the parties involved and lets others intervene completely in their marriage like this, it raises a doubt about the very meaning of marriage. Marriage is an individual life and an interpersonal bonding; therefore, I believe that a third party has no place to meddle in such matters. 

Thus, in terms of the method of marriage, I see free marriage as the most ideal, but realizing this will undoubtedly lead to several challenges. Firstly, issues like morals, circumstances, and social customs pose various difficulties. 

To realize free marriage in our society, above all, there must be proper spaces created, and the issues surrounding personal character must also be considered. For example, if marriage happens in a place with parents or relatives present, the man and woman intending to marry cannot have the necessary interaction to know each other’s character or ideals and will only collect superficial information. 

However, if they meet in a private location, they will engage in richer conversation, bringing up diverse topics, even those that may not be socially acceptable. Thus, the idea that young men and women in our society commonly engage in love and free marriage is not fully realized. 

The continuation of secret meetings and the effort to avoid exposure leads them to a point where they lack a place to hide and ultimately take on the formal structure of marriage. This definition can be considered the common phenomenon of free marriages among young men and women in our society. 

Although free marriages are indeed a positive concept, I further point out that they harbor numerous obstacles in their realization. 

Finally, I assert that those who focus on lineage or cliques to deny free marriage, such as "cultists," with theories about racial improvement, I have rejected since my school days, so I will not discuss them further today.

방임便  
이돈화  

I believe that the question of whether parental interference or the absolute freedom of the parties in the marriage will lead to an ideal union is inherently not a problem. 

Why do I believe this? Because it is comparable to a scenario where I wish to eat tasty food but, without parental interference, I would not be able to eat what appeals to my stomach. Of course, if the person eating is a child or ill, intervention may be necessary. However, for an adult, why should there be any interference in the food they choose to consume? 

The same applies to issues regarding sexual desire. Sexual desire, like appetite, is not a result of external influence, but a natural intrinsic characteristic expressed from innate disposition. How can parents intervene in such matters, and is there even a need for such interference? 

Generally speaking, the mere fact that societal discussion revolves around these two issues indicates how immature and pathological that society must be. The more the society is immature and exhibits pathological symptoms, the more it hinders the realization of free marriage. 

Let us take Korea for instance. As we know, there is no free marriage at all, and the evils of parents deciding on marriages and forcing children into them exist. 

Is it not true that in such cases parental intervention is absolutely necessary? When a child does not even understand gender distinctions, parents must act on behalf of the groom and bride for their marriage decisions. 

In this scenario, parental interference is absolutely necessary and extremely justified. However, as society does not remain pathological, this situation should not continue. Even today, if parents decide to marry off their children indirectly, their interference is indeed necessary. But if we consider that adults are adapting to physiological natural states and are engaging in natural sexual lives, then there should be no need for parents to interfere, just as they would not serve food and guide the young adult's hand while eating. 

What is there to worry about? Sexual desire is a natural matter; harmony between two individuals with innate compatibilities leading to marriage is entirely natural. Therefore, why should parents intervene and impose restrictions? 

Parents should merely support the smooth process of marriage and carry out administrative tasks. Some might say that in the youth period, one does not know something from nothing and hastily runs into marriage, leading to poor choices that result in negative consequences, thus necessitating parental intervention. 

Look at it this way. With parental interference in Korea, how many successful marriages have resulted, given the condition of this society? 

What does that indicate? Marriage is an issue of intrinsic individuality, and absolute freedom of individuality must be permitted, or else it cannot be resolved successfully. In extreme terms, marriage issues should be resolved by each individual’s freedom. The failure or success is their own to manage. Thus, even if there are failures, they should serve as personal lessons rather than external judgments, and the same goes for successes. 

Interfering parents does not guarantee only success without failure; rather, it becomes the fundamental source that leads to great failures that contradict the natural order. 

干涉便  
변성옥  

As for the opinions on free marriage and mediated marriage, I hold the view that neither is ideal. Mediated marriage has significant flaws as it disregards the individuality and will of the parties involved, while free marriage tends to flow too emotionally, causing issues with outcomes and sustainability. 

For instance, in our society, marriage has traditionally adhered strictly to the wishes of the parents, leading to problems in affection between couples and harmony within families, ultimately influencing even social morals. 

Should we simply abolish mediated marriage overnight and advocate for free marriage? That would also be a rash decision not deserving of consideration. 

In the case of free marriage, looking at America, out of every 100 marriages, there is a 25% divorce rate. If free marriages are supposed to be based on the compatibility of the characters, thoughts, and circumstances of the individuals, then how is it that divorces occur? 

While free marriage is said to respect the will and personality of the individuals, this very absence of restraint or intervention often leads to emotional high points where rational judgment falters, mixing feelings and blurring boundaries, such that things previously thought beautiful become ugly upon reflection, with flaws being discovered that had not been acknowledged before, leading unexpectedly to breakdowns. 

Therefore, I oppose the idea of absolute free marriage and absolute mediated marriage. Rather, I support a balanced approach that incorporates the strengths of both, creating a system of marriage where emotion and reason harmonize. 

To elaborate, I advocate for recognizing the will of the individuals looking to marry, while at the same time, those individuals must comply with the teachings and guidelines of parents and elders who have extensive experience in society. 

Although I express the opinion that both free marriage and mediated marriage are unfavorable, considering the current situation in our society, mediated marriage will cause less harm than free marriage.

Today, our society has not yet evolved, and the issues regarding the cultivation of young men and women remain in a process, so if we were to transplant the idea of free marriage exactly as it exists abroad, it would greatly worry about the moral state of society and the future of young men and women. For now, if we were to make a decision based on understanding, we would lean towards mediated marriage.  

**Summary**  

This article presents a debate on the necessity of parental interference in the marriages of young people versus advocating for free marriage. Proponents of parental interference argue that young couples lack the maturity and understanding to make such significant decisions independently, particularly in the transitional societal context of Korea at the time. Conversely, supporters of free marriage emphasize the importance of personal choice and individuality, arguing that societal restrictions inhibit natural relationships. This discussion highlights the clash between traditional familial authority and emerging modern individualism in Korea during the colonial period, as well as the broader implications for social norms as Korea navigated its changing identity.

Historians may find the attitudes expressed in this debate particularly revealing of the cultural tensions faced by Koreans under colonial rule, where traditional values conflicted with modern sentiments toward individualism and personal freedom.

나는 여러 말슴도 할 것 업시 단도직입으로 現下 朝鮮에 잇서서는 청년 남녀의 혼인은 절대로 그 부모이나 혹은 감독자가 간섭을 하는 것이 필요하다고 생각함니다. 근래의 말로 唐것이 다 조와도 며누리 唐것은 좃치 안코 (前年에 某氏가 淸女에게 장가를 들엇더니 그 부모가 불가하다고 반대한 말) 洋物이 다 조와도 洋쌀밥은 좃치 안타는 말과 맛찬가지로 자유라는 것을 누구나 조와하는 것이지만은 유독 혼인의 자유만은 절대 불가한 줄로 암니다. 그것도 남녀가 다 연령과 지식이 상당하고 서로 충분한 이해와 각오가 잇서서 자유로 결합하는 혼인이 된다면 물론 그만한 행복이 업고 또 찬성치 안을 이가 업겟지만은 우리 朝鮮과 가티 아즉 과도기에 잇서서 남녀의 지식정도가 차이가 만코 사회제도와 가정제도가 완전하게 개선이 되지 못한 때에 청춘의 남녀가 아모 이해와 각오가 업시 다만 시대풍조에 딸이우고 일시적 기분이나 감정에 끌리여서 소위 자유연애이니 자유결혼이니 하고 자긔네끼리 결혼을 한다면 그 가정이 과연 얼마나 원만한 가정이 되며 그 부부가 또한 얼마나 團樂한 부부가 되겟슴닛가. 엇그적게 洞房華燭에서 鴛鴦의 단꿈을 꾸던 사람들이 불과 몃칠에 파경의 恨心을 쉬게 되는 일이 종종 만슴니다. 이러한 일은 내가 누구의 예를 드러 말슴치 안어도 우리 사회에 얼마나 만슴닛가. 보통으로 생각하면 간섭의 혼인은 남이 간섭을 하는 것이닛가 여러 가지가 당사자 두 사람의 의견에 맛지 안는 일이 만허서 그 부부간에 破綻이 생기기 쉬웁고 자유결혼은 당사자간의 자유의사의 결함이닛가 언제던지 두 사람이＜138＞ 잘 단합할 것 갓지만은 사실은 그와 정반대임니다. 최근 신문상에도 이혼자의 통계발표한 것을 본다 면 간섭 혼인자보다 자유혼인자의 이혼건수가 만습듸다. 그것은 다른 까닭이 안이라 자유혼인에는 우에 말슴한 것과 가티 남녀가 다 遠大周到한 생각을 가지지 못하고 다만 일시의 감정과 기분적으로 결혼을 하는 까닭에 그 감정과 그 기분이 살어지는 때에 즉시 파탄이 생기는 것이오. 간섭혼인은 감독자가 냉정공평하게 생각하하야 두 사람의 최대행복과 최대이익될 점을 원만하게 打算하는 까닭에 두 사람 새에 다소 불만이 잇슬지라도 그리 최후의 破綠曲을 부르기에까지는 이르지 아는 것임니다. 摠而言之하면 現下朝鮮형편에 잇서서는 개인으로나 가정 또는 사회의 어느 방면으로 보던지 자유결혼은 백해무익한 것으로 생각함니다.

申興雨

결혼하는 데 잇서서 남녀가 피차에 성격이라던지 이상이라던지 건강이라던지 이 밧게 모든 것을 알어보려고 교제하며 접근하는 일은 결혼의 신조라고 해도 과언이 안인 듯하다.
결혼은 남녀가 일시 만나서 생리적 충동으로 니러나는 순간적 향락을 爲主하는 것이 안이다.
적게 생각하면 개인의 생활문제로 비롯하야 크게 생각하면 사회나 민족에게까지 관계가 잇는 문제이다.
이와 가티 중대한 문제를 아모 자각업시 녯날부터 전해 나려오는 풍습을 墨守하야 이행한다는 것은 크게 고려할 일이라고 생각한다.
朝鮮에 잇서서 보통적으로 성행하고 잇는 媒介結婚가튼 것은 好個의 예이다.
당사자들의 의사나 인격을 무시하고 타인이 이에 절대간섭을 하야 거사를 한다면 결혼의 의의가 자못 那邊에 잇는지 疑訝케 된다.
결혼은 개인의 생명 내지 인격적 결합인즉 제삼자로 내용에까지 깁히 들어가 간섭할 바 안이라고 생각한다.
그럼으로 결혼하든 방법에 잇서서는 자유결혼이 무엇보다도 이상적이라고 보나 이것을 실현하는 데 이르러서는 적지 안은 어려운 문제를 당하게 된다. 첫재에 정도, 경우, 社會風紀, 문제등 여러 가지 난처할 일이 잇다.
우리 사회에서 자유결혼을 실현하려면 무엇보다도 몬저 적당한 장소를 맨들어야 하겟고 또는 남녀의 정도문제를 고려하여야 하겟다.
가령 예를 들어보면 부모나 친척이 입회한 장소이면 결혼하려는 남녀는 성격이라던지 이상이라던지를 알어볼 만한 교제를 못하고 그저 수집은 끗에 이상한 점잔을 뽑고 말게＜139＞ 된다.
그러나 그들이 달은 비밀 한 장소에서 만나다면 별별 니야기를 다하고 남이 보기에 흉한 정도까지의 교제를 하게 된다. 그럼으로 최근 우리 사회에서 흔이 보는 청년남녀들이 연애를 하야 자유결혼을 한다는 것은 그리 理想으로 되엿다고 看做할 수가 업다.
밀회와 밀회를 거듭하고 忌避와 忌避를 힘써하다 듸데여 피신할 곳이 업시 일이 窮過하여지면 결혼이라는 표면형식을 취하는 것이 우리 사회의 청년남녀의 자유결혼의 通相이라고 볼 수 잇다.
자유결혼은 물론 됴흔 일이나 실현하는데 니르러서는 여러 가지 난관이 잇슴을 짐작하고 이 점에 유의하여야 할 것을 부언한다.
끗으로 말하는 것은 가문이나 파벌가튼 것을 위주하고 자유결혼을 부인하는 『꼴트』씨의 인종개량학 가튼 것도 잇스나 이 학설은 내가 학창에 잇슬 때부터 부인하여 온 것이니까 오늘에 문제 삼지 안음을 다시 말하여 둔다.
