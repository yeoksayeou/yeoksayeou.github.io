Secret Orders Welcome

Readers of this magazine, please become our editorial advisors. Our Secret Investigation Team reporters are always prepared for action and are waiting for the commands of our esteemed readers. If there are any disturbing incidents or strange happenings at any place or house, or if there are bizarre individuals somewhere, please notify our ‘별건곤 조사부’ (Bbyeolgeongon Investigation Department). We will deploy immediately, investigate secretly, and report back in our magazine.

In the magazine, please state the details, including the location, address, and the name and age of the individuals involved, even if it means disclosing secrets. This information will help connect our investigation team with what you report. 

 

**Summary**

The article invites readers to actively participate in the publication by reporting unsettling or suspicious occurrences, which the magazine's investigative team promises to investigate and report back on. It reflects a societal atmosphere in colonial Korea where public concerns regarding the unusual and secretive were intertwined with the media. Historians might find the engagement of the public in reporting on social issues particularly interesting, revealing a dynamic between the press and community involvement during a time of colonial rule.

秘密命令歡迎
본지를 닑으시는 독자가 모다 편집고문이 되어 주십시오. 본지의 비밀조사반 기자가 항상 출동준비를 하고 독자 諸氏의 명령을 기다리고 잇슴니다. 아모 곳 아모 집에 독갑이 작란이 잇스니 조사하라던지 아모 곳 아모 집에 괴이한 마굴이 잇스니 그 내면을 조사하라던지 어느 곳 어느곳에 괴상한 인물이 잇거나 의심나는 사건이 잇스니 조사하라고 넌즛이 『別乾坤調査部』로 통지해 주시면 즉각 출동 비밀조사하야 誌面으로 보고 하겟슴니다.
誌上에는 비밀을 직히드라도 본사까지는 그 장소 번지 인물이면 성명 년령을 명기하시고 投害하시는 이의 주소도 알려 주서야 조사상 연결 할 수 잇겟슴니다.＜47＞
