기사제목	굉장한 몸둥이 (The Amazing Body)  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

According to the research of Professor Ueongga, a professor at the university, it has been found that just wearing underwear for a single day allows about 400,000 germs to grow per square inch, and if one wears a pair of socks for six days without washing them, about 10 million germs proliferate. It is said that if they are washed thoroughly every day, underwear and socks will not allow germs to grow in thousands. The human skin is thus truly a breeding ground for a terrifying amount of germs. <89>  

**Summary**  

This article discusses the alarming proliferation of germs on clothing, particularly on underwear and socks, revealing the hygiene issues of the time. It emphasizes that even minimal exposure can result in a significant number of germs, highlighting the lack of cleanliness standards in the era. This notion relates to Korean colonial history as it illustrates both the social awareness of hygiene and the prevailing conditions faced during that period, offering insights into public health perceptions under colonial rule.
