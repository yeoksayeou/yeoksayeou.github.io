社員年賀狀  

◇無祝爲祝  
차상완  

To wish someone well is to express a hope for what they lack. For instance, wishing for someone to save money implies that they typically struggle in that regard, and wishing for a son indicates a desire for a child because sons are rare for them. This applies to everything. Thus, we Koreans can be said to already find satisfaction in life; thus, it is not special to wish for something. Given this, I wish that this year I might arrive at a point where I have nothing left to wish for and can find contentment instead.  

◇나의소원  
송계월  

New Year’s Day! I cannot express how much I am looking forward to it. My heart, which is full of anticipation, is linked with my entire life, and the desire for a new day ignites a fervor in my heart, making me both excited and nervous.  
―――I sincerely hope the new year brings me good health―――.  

◇긔렴됨이만키를  
라소  

May you receive great fortune in the New Year, and I wish that this year's edition of our magazine will prosper as well. Furthermore, I sincerely hope that there will be remarkable achievements for you personally and socially during this year.  

◇편즙으로의 써비스를  
최족桂  

This year, I will return to our main task. I will brighten your days and enhance the enjoyment of our magazine. By doing this, I hope to make this year’s Byeolgeongon more interesting and more famous, and I am grateful for your continued support.  

◇왜시름업시게십니?  
김규택  

You must be eating too much! Here’s some medicine to relieve your distress. Do not let worry hinder your enjoyment! Embrace the new year with joy, and may your path be clear. Indulging in the joys of the new year will most certainly lead to a fulfilling year ahead, everyone...  

◇希望하는 것 몃가지  
안회남  

As I contemplate, I wish I could say thank you to the one who nurtured my heart well, always guiding me in my life. Moreover, I hope good fortune comes to my suffering friends, and I resolve more than anything to refrain from troubling words and to act according to my decisions starting this new year.  

◇不賀新年  
소만식  

The saying that one must read the four books and three classics to be considered knowledgeable is often said, and you readers may be hearing the phrase "no New Year greetings" for the first time. It's not particularly extraordinary; I merely mean to refrain from celebrating the New Year. I feel as though the new year will not come for me, nor for you. However, let's have a wonderful world in the future, where we can celebrate and bow to each other. <45>  

**Summary**  

This piece reflects on the sentiments of New Year wishes in the Korean colonial context, conveying a mix of personal aspirations and communal sentiments. The authors express their hopes for health, prosperity, and fulfillment, as well as reflections on the nature of wishes. The nuanced exploration of satisfaction in life and the reluctance to extend New Year's greetings can reveal the resignations felt among Koreans in the context of colonial rule, presenting them as both hopeful and reflective of their state.

◇無祝爲祝
車相瓉

사람마다 무슨축원을한다는 것은 그부족한 것을 축원하는 것이다. 가령돈을 잘모으라고하면 그사람이평소에 돈어엄기문에 돈을 만히모으라고 축원하는것이요 아들을나라고하면 그사람이아들이귀하기 문에 한 아들낫키를축원하는 것이다. 그 외모든것이다 그럿타 우리조선사람은 모도가볼만족이니 특별이 무엇을 축원한다고할수도엄고 축원한대야 한 축원대로되지도안는 것이다. 그럼으로 나는 금년에는 근본으로 그무엇이던지 다시축원할것이업슬만한 정도에 이르기지만족을 엇게되기바란다.

◇나의소원
宋桂月

새해새날! 얼마나 기대럿는지 모른답니다. 햇슥한얼골 상관한마음 이것이이해에 나의생활전체이엿스니 새날을긔대라느마음은 원슥한얼골과 부산하마음을 불질르고 황망피여오릅니다.
―――원컨대 새날이여 나에게 건강을 가져다다오―――.

◇긔렴됨이만키를
羅笑

금년새해 복만히바드시고 올해 一년도 본지의 발전과아울너 만혼에호가잇서중시기를바람니다.
그리고 금년중에는 당신개인그보나 는 사회적으로나 특긔할만한 긔렴됨이더 만혀지기를 충심으로바래마지안습니다.

◇편즙으로의 써비스를
崔逐桂

올에는 밧부게돌아단이겟습니다. 그리고 귀지를 날마당 우벅내여서 귀를더밝게하겟습니다. 이리하야 금년의 별건곤을 좀더재미잇게 좀더 有名하게만드러서 여러분에게 평소애호(平素愛護)해주심에 갑흐려합니다.

◇왜시름업시게십니?
金奎澤

국을 너무잡수신게로군요 속풀릴약을드릴아요 아니 못잡수서서 시름엄시게 신모양입니다그려! 너머 걱정만마시고 박그로 여나가십시오 새해의 공긔를가슬마서보신시오 박하사랑보다 더시워―ㄴ합니다. 이공긔을 잡수시면 금년에 속시원한일만으실것입니다. 여러분서는…….

◇希望하는 것 몃가지
安懷南

내가 새각할사록 머리가 숙어지는 오날날아지의 나의생활에잇서서 나의마음을 잘안허주고길러준 그녀성에게 복이잇스라.
 가난하고 불상한 나의만흔동무들에게도 조흔운수가돌기를 바라며 내자신은 새해부터는 슬코 절대로입에다대지안코 결심대로 실행이잇기를무엇보담도바란다.

◇不賀新年
蘇萬植

四서三경을다읽어야 ■리분자는 럼듯는다는 준수로 독자여러분도 노소물논하고 불하신년(不賀新年)이라는말은 처음들을것입니다.
그러냐 별로 깁흔잇튼업습니다. 그저 신년축하를 아니한다는 것입니다. 내자신이 새해가 깃블것도업스려니와 여러분도 역시 그럴것이니요 후일 조흔세상맛나서그나축하도하고 새배도합시다.＜45＞
