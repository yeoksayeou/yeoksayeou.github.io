기사제목	中國留學을 갈려면 이것을 알어두라 (What You Should Know Before Going to Study in China)  
필자	在南京 申基彦  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

Before talking about Nanjing, I would like to mention a few things that those wanting to study in China should know. First, the educational system and academic qualifications in China are different from those in Korea. The academic year starts in September and the summer vacation is generally from late June until August. The entrance examinations, for most parts, take place in July. Therefore, if students finish a school year in Korea and come to China in April, those who do not know Chinese will not be able to sit for the entrance examinations in July of that year. For instance, in order to enroll in July next year, they must at least come to China within this year and prepare by learning Chinese.

Next, China's educational system consists of six years of middle school and four years of college. Middle school is divided into three years of junior middle school and three years of senior middle school, with senior middle school corresponding to Japanese high schools. Therefore, it is somewhat difficult to enter college after graduating from middle school in Korea without attending senior middle school in China for one or two years (they are eligible to take the entrance examination). This is something that needs to be known before coming to study in China. I have seen many students who graduated from middle school in Korea come to study in China and find it unexpectedly difficult to enter senior high school and end up returning home or enrolling in various colleges in Shanghai or Beijing, which often they are able to enter simply by paying a large sum of money (these colleges are referred to by Chinese people as 野鷄大學, "Yejida Xue").

This arises from a desire to become college students as quickly as possible.

Next, I want to address the issue of studying hard in China that many friends who have not been here fantasize about. Can anyone study hard if they go to China without money? They might be disappointed when they realize that studying hard is impossible in China without money. We will certainly not conclude that it is absolutely impossible to study without money. Generally speaking, the cost of studying here is less than in Gyeongseong (경성, now known as Seoul).

One thing I especially want to point out is the issue of public safety in China. As a country where wars are prevalent, many may think that life is at risk or that there are no safe places. Of course, war zones are like that, but places that are not war zones are relatively safer and freer than those under the law of public security maintenance.

Now let me talk about studying in Nanjing. Nanjing, located in the plains by the Yangtze River, is surrounded by ancient city walls and has a population of around 600,000, making it the capital of China. Although, compared to modern cities, it is still less developed than Shanghai, it is a wonderful place for students to study compared to Shanghai and Beijing. First of all, Nanjing is a city with a village-like atmosphere and excellent natural scenery. The Yangtze River banks are vibrant throughout most of the year. Moreover, on Sundays, when passing through quiet bamboo groves, the sounds of vendors selling snacks can be heard from a distance, which gives a sense of escape from the bustling urban life. However, due to geographical reasons, most of our students come to China through Beijing or Shanghai, and very few come to Nanjing. Currently, there are about twenty of our students in Nanjing, three at the university, and five at a special school, with the rest being middle school students. There is a student union established for our Korean students.

Now, let me briefly introduce the universities and special schools in Nanjing. The universities include Central University (中央大學), Central Political University (中央政治大學), Jinling University (金陵大學), and Cultural University (文化大學) (all of these schools are coeducational), and there is also Jinling Women's University (金陵女子大學). The special schools include the Central Army Officer Academy (中央陸軍軍官學校) (formerly Huangpu Military Academy). To enroll in those universities, one must graduate from senior middle school, but it is possible to enter the military academy without graduating from senior middle school.

The study period is three years. However, the enrollment period is not fixed. Generally, students are recruited around March or April every year, so if students come to Nanjing within this year and prepare for the entrance examination in Chinese, they will be able to sit for the exam. The Chinese universities do provide special consideration for Korean students. Among various universities, I would particularly like to introduce Central Political University. Central Political University is a public university (公費學校), meaning students do not pay tuition, and living necessities as well as lectures are provided by the school. This is the only university in China where students can study without having money. The departments offered include law (法律系), economics (經濟系), education (敎育系), diplomacy (外交系), finance (財政系), administration (行政系), and military (陸系), and each of these subjects can be concentrated on more specifically than in other universities, although in terms of breadth, it is much narrower than in Central University or Jinling University.

This is what I wanted to convey to those interested in studying in Nanjing. However, it has become somewhat simplified. For those who wish to know more details, I hope you will inquire at the Nanjing Central University mailbox No. 778, Nanjing Korean Students Association.＜58＞

**Summary**

This article provides a comprehensive overview for Korean students considering studying in China during the early 1930s, focusing on the differences in educational systems, cost of study, and the atmosphere in Nanjing. It reveals the challenges faced by Koreans in accessing higher education in China, along with safety considerations during a turbulent period marked by conflict. Historians may find the insights into the educational aspirations amid colonial rule significant, reflecting the broader context of Koreans navigating foreign education systems in search of opportunity.

中國留學을 갈려면 이것을 알어두라
						在南京 申基彦
남경(南京)을 말하기전에 중국류학(中國留學)을하고저하는분으로써 알어야할 몃가지를 말하자 첫재 中國의 학력(學歷)과 학제(學制)는 朝鮮의 그것과 다르다. 학력은 매년 九月에 학년(學年)이 시작되여서 이듬해 六月하순부터 八月금음지는 방학이다. 그리고 입학시험(入學試驗)은 대개 七月에 치르게된다. 그럼으로 朝鮮에서 학년을 마치고 四月에 中國엘오면 中國말을모르는 사람은 그해 七月에 입학시험을 치루지못하게된다. 이를테면 내년 七月에 입학할려면은 적어도 금년내에 中國엘와서 중국어를 준비하여야 한다.
그다음 중국학제는 중학(中學) 六年 대학(大學)四年인데 중학을 초급중학(初級中學)三年 고급중학(高級中學)三年으로 노나서 고급중학(日本高等學校에상당함)을 졸엄하고 대학(大學)에 드러가게된다. 그럼으로 朝鮮에서 중등학교를 졸업하고 中國에와서 고급중학에 드러가서 一年혹은 二年을 공부하지안코 대학에 입학하기에는 좀어렵다(시험볼자격은준다) 이것은 中國류학을오기전에 반드시 알어야 할바이다. 필자는 만히보앗다. 朝鮮에서 중등학교를 졸엄하고 중국류학을 왓다가 새삼스럽게 中學(高級)엘드러가기가 실혀서 귀국하거나 상해(上海) 북경(北京)등지에 허다한 돈만주면 입학할수잇는 대학(중국 사람들은 야게대학(野鷄大學)이라고한다)에 입학을하는 만흔사람들을…….
이것은 어서리 대학생이되여야겟다는데서 생긴 결과가아닌가한다.
그다음말하고저하는 것은 中國엘와보지 못한동무들이 생각은 만히갓는 고학(苦學) 문제다 돈엄는동무는누구나 中國엘가면 고학을할수가잇느냐? 고뭇는다. 돈엄시 와볼려고하는동무는 실망을하겟느나 中國에서는 고학을 할 수가엄다. 그러타고 돈엄는 우리는 절대로 공부할수가엄다고는 생각안한다. 자세한 것은 나종에 말하겟스나 하여간 학비는 경성보다 적게든다.
예서한가지 특별히 말할 것은 중국의 치안문제(治安問題)다 중국은 전쟁이만흔나라이니 생명이위험하다든가 안전한곳이 엄다고 생각하는사람이 적지안흘것갓다. 물론 전쟁지대(戰爭地帶)는 그러하다그러나 이전쟁지대가 아닌곳은 밤낫으로 치안유지법(治安維持法)의 법방에 걸리고잇는 그곳보담은 안전하고 자유롭다.
이제 남경(南京)류학에 대하야 말하자 양자강(楊子江)남 벌판에 주저안즌 주위팔실리(周圍八十里) 고성(古城)에 싸힌 남경은 六十萬시민을가진중국의 서울이다. 비록현대의도시(都市)로서는 상해(上海)에 비하야 아직도보잘것엄스나 학생이 공부하기에는 상해ㆍ북경보담도 훌륭한곳이다. 첫재남경은 향촌미(鄕村味)를 가진 도시로써 산수(山水)의 풍경이구비하엿다. 긔후는 朝鮮남부보담도 오란하야 양자강언덕은 一年의 대부분이 푸른비슬 이고잇다. 더구나 일요일마다 시가를 난 고요한대숩(竹林)속을 거러가면서 멀리서부터오는 엿장사의 호래소리를 드를에는 벌서 번잡한도시생활을 이저버리게된다 그러나 지리상관게(地理上關係)로 中國엘오는 우리학생은 대개 북경이나 상해에서 모물게되고 남경지 오는분은 극히 적다. 지금남경에 우리학생이약 二十名잇는대 대학 三名 특종학교(特種學校) 五名외에는 모다 중학생이다. 이곳에 우리의 류학생회(留學生會)가 조직되여잇다.
이제 남경각대학과 특종학교를 간단히 소개하자 대학으로는 중앙대학(中央大學) 중앙정치대학(中央政治大學) 금능대학(金陵大學) 문화대학(文化大學)(이상의 각교남녀공학임) 그리고 금능여자대학(金陵女子大學) 이잇고 특종학교로는 중앙륙군군관학교(中央陸軍軍官學校)-(전의 黃浦軍官學校)가잇다. 이상각대학은 우에서도 말한바와 가티 고급중학을 졸엄하고 드러가나 군관학교는 고급중학을졸엄하지안코도 입학할수가잇다.
그러고 수학년한(修學年限)은 三年이다. 그러나 입학긔(入學期)는 일정해잇지안타. 대개 매년 三四月에 학생을 모집할듯하니 금년내로남경엘와서 중어(中語)를 준비하면입학시험을볼수가잇다.  조선사람에게는 동정하여준다. 각대학중에는 특히 중앙정치대학을 소개하고 전한다. 중앙정치대학은 공비학교(公費學校)이다. 돈을내지안는 것은 물론 의식주(衣食住) 강의(講義)지도 학교에서 공급한다. 이것이 중국에서 다만 하나인 돈엄시공부할수잇는 대학교이다. 그대학내용은 법률게(法律系) 경제게(經濟系) 교육게(敎育界) 외교게(外交系) 재정게(財政系) 행정게(行政系) 륙게로노나서 매게를다시수조로 노낫슴으로 다른대학보다도 전공적(專攻的)이라고하겟스나 농(農) 공(工) 의(醫)등과가엄스니 범위에 잇서서는 중앙대학 금능대학에 비하야훨신좁다.
이상은 남경류학을 하고저 하는동무에게 올린바이다. 그러나 너무간단하게 되엿다. 좀더자세한 것을 알고저 하는분은 남경중앙대학신상(南京中央大學信箱)七七八號 남경한국류학생회(南京韓國留學生會)에 하문하기를 바란다.＜58＞
