기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)  

Mongdang Iron Pen  

Hello, everyone. How have you been? This year, may you receive many blessings and achieve your wishes.

On New Year's Day, according to the old customs of Joseon, people make what is called a "bokjori" to go door to door selling them, while many people buy them to hang above their doors for a year.

Instead of bokjori, we have created the Byeolggeongon magazine to spread widely across the entire Joseon Peninsula and even to the farthest reaches of the world.

We may be proud, but Byeolggeongon magazine is indeed the bokjori of the Joseon people. It opens the eyes of those who are blind, it awakens the ears of the deaf, it makes the crying person laugh, and it turns despair into optimism.

Families that read Byeolggeongon are truly happy families. So everyone—if you truly want to receive copious blessings this New Year, without hesitation read Byeolggeongon.

From now on, starting with Byeolggeongon, continue to read it in the New Year, and if there are those who do not read it, please encourage them to do so. Once our whole nation reaches a level where Byeolggeongon can be widely read, our hopes for great happiness will naturally come to fruition.

And everyone, may you be diligent. This year, among our various magazines published by Gaebyeoksa, we will greatly expand Byeolggeongon and actively promote it even to the most remote villages, encouraging them to read it. To foster closer ties with our dedicated readers, Han Yeong-chang (韓永昌), who has been actively engaged in our organization's activities in Tokyo for many years, will return to our headquarters and begin a regional tour from this New Year. Please be diligent and offer your support.  

**Summary**  

This article discusses the importance of the Byeolggeongon magazine, likening it to a traditional New Year charm, 'bokjori', that brings blessings and happiness. It emphasizes the magazine's role in enlightening its readers and fostering national unity among Koreans. The call to action stresses the significance of widespread readership to address social issues and cultivate a hopeful national spirit. Historians might find the promotion of cultural publications during the colonial period interesting, showcasing efforts to maintain Korean identity and cohesion amidst Japanese oppression.

잡지명	별건곤 제59호
발행일	1932년 12월 30일
기사제목	몽당鐵筆
기사형태	사고·편집후기

몽당鐵筆
여러분 과세안녕이하셧습닛가 금년에는 특히 복을 만히드시고 소원성취하십쇼.
◇
정월초하루날이면 조선의 녯풍속에는 소위복조리란 것을 만드러서 조리잠사가집집으로도라다니며 복조리를 사라고위치고 여러사람들은 그것을 사서방문우에 다 일년내애다러두지안습닛가.
◇
우리는 복조리대신에 이별건곤잡지를 만드러서 조선팔도방방곡곡내지 원천하세게에아지 넓이퍼트립니다.
◇
우리가 우리의 자랑갓지만은 별건곤잡지야말로조선사람의 복조리올시다. 눈에둔사람의 눈을 워주고귀먹은 사람의 귀를 틔워주고 우는 사람을 웃게하고 실망하는 사람을 낙관으로 만드러 놋습니다.
◇
별건곤을 읽는가정은 참으로 행복의 가정이올시다. 여러분-새해에 참으로 복을 만히 바드시랴거던 두말마르시고 이별건곤을 보십쇼.
◇
긔왕부터 별건곤을 보시거던 새해에도 게속하야 보시고 안보는 사람이 잇거던 보도록 권하십쇼 우리전민족이별건곤한아만잘읽을만한정도에 이르면 우리의 희망하는 큰행복은 저절로 굴너서라도 드러울것입니다.
◇
그러시고 여러분은 깃버하십쇼 금년에는 우리개벽사잡지그중에도 우리별건곤을 대대적으로 확장하고 면면촌촌야지라도다읽으시도록 선전하며 애독자여러분과 좀더 친밀한련낙물엇기위하야 다년간동경에 가서 우리지사일로만흔활동을 하던 한영창(韓永昌)씨가이신년부터 본사로도라와서 지방순회를하게되엿습니다. 다가티깃버하시고 만흔 원조를 하야주십쇼.＜68＞
