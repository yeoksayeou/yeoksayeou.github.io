Prize Question  

1. Which article published in this issue of 星乾 (Byeolgeongon) is considered the "most enjoyable"?  
Special Prize: One person will receive a fountain pen.  
Normal Prize: Ninety-nine people will receive one book each.  

Regulations  
◇ Please write your answer briefly on a postcard, clearly stating your address and name.  
◇ On the envelope, please write "Seoul Gaebyeoksa Byeolgeon Prize Department" (It is allowed to send together with other manuscripts or prize answers).  
◇ Write "Manuscript Enclosed" on the envelope, and you may affix two won worth of stamps using scissors on the top of the envelope.  
◇ Send it within twenty days.  
◇ If possible, it is more convenient to send the answers of several people in one envelope to save on stamps. From now on, readers will no longer need to send their regards.  

New Magazine Free Distribution  
Please request via reply postcard.  
Anyone who simply writes their address and name and notifies "Seoul Gaebyeoksa" will receive a new magazine called "New Economy" at no charge, which increases knowledge and keeps one informed of world news. Please inform your acquaintances, but notification must be done via reply postcard.

一, 이번 벌건곤(신년호)중에서 제일「자미잇게 읽은 글」은 어한 글 임니?
特賞 一人 萬年筆一柄
普通賞 九十九人 書籍一册式

規定
◇엽서에다 답안을 간단이 적어보내시되자긔의주소성명을 히 쓸 것.
◇봉투에느어보내실는 반듯이 京城開闢社別乾坤懸賞係라고 쓸 것. (다른 原稿나다른 懸賞答案과 同封해도 可)
◇피봉에는 原稿在中이라고 쓰고 봉투주둥이의한귀통이를 가위로 비면 二전우표를 부처서 올수잇습니다.
◇二十日以內로보낼 것.
◇되도록 한봉투안에여러사람의 것을 너어보내시면 피차간에 편리하고 우표갑슬절약할수잇습니다 이제부터 독자중은 안부치기로햇습니다.

新雜誌無代進呈
往復葉書로 請求하시요
누구든지 自己의 住所姓名만 이적어서 「京城開闢社」로 通知하시면 知識늘고 세상 消息아는 「新經濟」라는새雜誌를 거저보내드립니다. 아시는 친구에게 만히 말슴하야주시되 通知는 반드시 往復葉書로하라하십시오.
