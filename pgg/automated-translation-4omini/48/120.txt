文壇風聞
Yihwa Women's Normal School's New Life Society  
Mr. Lee Tae-jun from Shin Saengsa has recently moved his position to the Yihwa Women's Normal School office. In turn, Mr. Bang In-geun from the Gidog Sinbo (Christian Herald) has taken the position of literature professor at Yihwa Women's Normal School. Seeing that Mr. Lee Tae-jun is now heading to the Yihwa School, one wonders if Shin Saengsa is perhaps a training ground for faculty members of Yihwa Academy! If this is the case, it seems it will soon be Mr. Bang's turn. So, let the preparations be made in advance.  

Mr. Choi Hak-song's Two Ailments  
Recently, Mr. Choi Hak-song of Maeil Sinbo has been suffering from toothache and hemorrhoids, causing him such discomfort that eating has become difficult and he is in considerable pain even in other respects. When he returned from the hospital, a business friend remarked, "Is it really worth it to suffer this much for just your teeth? In any case, hemorrhoids are terrible." To which Mr. Choi quickly responded, "Hmph, as if tooth decay is the only thing! There are also those hemorrhoids to deal with."  

Mr. Lee Kwang-soo's Tooth Extraction  
Recently in Dong-a Ilbo, Mr. Lee Kwang-soo had a tooth extracted from the right side. He surely didn’t extract it for fun, but if it were charming, it would be regretful not only for him but for everyone who interacts with him. However, he remarked, “It is a pity to lose a year of life, even if it means sacrificing a tooth.” Did he mean to say, “Now that Chunwon (Spring Garden) is getting old, one tooth has fallen out on its own”? In any case, it's a regretful matter; if there is a life insurance agent with appropriate skills, they should rush over to negotiate.

  

**Summary**

This article reports on recent developments in the Korean literary community, including personnel changes at Yihwa Women's Normal School and the health issues of notable figures. Mr. Lee Tae-jun's move and subsequent appointments may reflect opportunities for teachers within Korean educational institutions under colonial rule. The humor surrounding the ailments of Mr. Choi and Mr. Lee reveals societal attitudes towards health and personal issues during this turbulent period in Korea’s history. Historians may find the mention of these literary figures and their ailments representative of the complexities of their personal lives amidst the broader context of Korean colonial experiences.

梨花師範科新生社
신생사(新生社)에 잇든 李泰俊씨는 요번에 자리를 리화전문사무실로 옴겨가게 되엿다. 그 대신 신생사에는 긔독신보(基督申報)에 잇든 方仁根씨가 리화전문(梨花專門)의 문과교수로 갓섯고 이번에 또 李泰俊씨가 리화학교로 가게 된 것을 보아 신생사가 리화학당의 교수 사무원의 양성소나 아닐는지! 그러면 다음은 方씨의 차레일 듯. 이로부터 그 준비에 밧블지어다.

崔鶴松氏의 兩치疾
매일신보의 崔鶴松씨, 요 근래 치통(齒痛)과 치질(痔疾)로 병고(病苦)가 자심하야 식사하기도 어려웁지만 뒤 보기에도 매우 고통이라고. 그런데 병원에 갓다오는 씨를 보고 실업슨 친구 하나이「원 니빠리를 알르라고 그 고생인가 하여간 치질(齒疾)이란 고약한 병이야」하니까 최씨 얼른 대답해 가로되
「흥 그 치질(齒疾) 뿐이면 조케! 다른 치질(痔疾)까지 잇다네.

李光洙씨의 발치
동아일보 李光洙씨 요전에 오른 쪽 압니 한 개를 발치하엿다. 운치로 빼엿슬리 만무하겟지만 애교잇는 니이엇다면 본인은 물론 대하는 사람도 매우 섭섭할 일이다. 그러나 「한나이라도 더 자시는 것이 안타까워서 년치(年齒) 한 개를 희생하엿느니라」. 「이미 춘원(春園)이 늙은지라 노치(老齒) 한 개가 절로 빠젓다」쯤의 해석으로 물려버림이 엇덜까? 하여튼 유감된 일이니 생명보험의 교권만공의 수완 잇는 입치업자가 잇거든 조속 달려가서 교섭해 볼 일이다.＜29＞
