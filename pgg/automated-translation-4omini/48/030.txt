The True Nature of the Newly Established Manchukuo  
Cui Yunsheng

Among the prominent figures in this cabinet are a few noteworthy individuals.  
Jeong Hyo-seo (Prime Minister) was someone who had not participated much in political life until now; he was often sought for positions by figures such as Yuan Shikai (袁世凱), Li Yuanhong (黎元洪), and Duan Qirui (段祺瑞), but he had refused. A person committed to the restoration of the Qing dynasty, he has now embraced his heavy responsibilities in this new nation at the age of 73.  

Jang Sik-ui (Vice Premier) is a graduate of the Japanese Military Academy and was previously the chief of staff to the governor of Jilin. Following the relocation of Zhang Zuolin (張作霖) to Beiping, and after the assassination of his successor and commander of the Fengtian faction, Jang Sik-ui was killed by Zhang Xueliang (張學良) when he attempted to take leadership of the Yangwu Regiment.  

The national title is Manchukuo, and the era name is Daedong (大同).  
Since the outbreak of the Incident of 1 September in Manchuria, Jang Sik-ui, Won Geum-gye (袁金凱), Hee Heup (熙洽), Jang Gyeong-hye (張景惠) and others from the same faction have been vigorously advocating for the so-called independence of Manchuria, which materialized when the formal establishment of this new nation was announced publicly on 1 March. The name of this country was initially discussed as either the Republic of Manchurian-Mongolian Federation (滿蒙聯省共和國) or the Daedong Nation (大同國), but it was ultimately decided to be Manchukuo (滿洲國), with the era name also decided to be Daedong (大同), which carries the meaning of “universal harmony” from the Zhouyi (周易). The territory comprises the four provinces of the Northeast: Fengtian (奉天), Jilin (吉林), Rehe (熱河), and Heilongjiang (黑龍) with an area of about 77,554 square miles and a population of about 34 million. Although the form of government is ostensibly a republic, it is actually a monarchy, with the leader designated as a ruling head (執政) after being supported by the last emperor of the Qing dynasty, Puyi (溥儀). The capital was determined to be Changchun (長春).  

The splendid new national flag of Manchukuo features a yellow background with blue (靑), red (赤), white (白), and black (黑) colors. The yellow represents the unity of the yellow race, the red symbolizes the struggles, blue signifies fresh vitality, white represents peace, and black implies solidity, thereby symbolizing the new nation.  

The structure of the new nation places the ruler in the highest position (similar to a monarchy) with legislative (立法), executive (國務), and supervisory (監察) branches. Under the cabinet, there are seven ministries: civil affairs, foreign affairs, military affairs, finance, industry, transportation, and justice, with ten administrative divisions established by dividing the four provinces and special cities such as Changchun, Fengtian, Jilin, Harbin, Rehe, and Qiqihar (齊齊合爾).  

The four fundamental principles of this independent nation are as follows:  
1. The ruling head shall govern Manchukuo through constitutional means, being elected by the people.  
2. The politics of Manchukuo shall be based on the principle of popular sovereignty.  
3. Manchukuo will enact a constitution based on the general will of the people in the near future.  
4. The ruling system shall take a transitional form until a formal constitution is established and implemented.  

Key Figures of the New Government  
According to a decision announced at the Changchun Conference on 3 March, the Prime Minister is Jeong Hyo-seo (鄭孝胥), the Chairman of the Advisory Council is Jang Gyeong-hye (張景惠), the Chair of the Autonomy Committee is U Chung-han (于沖漢), and the Minister of Civil Affairs is Hee Heup (熙洽), the Minister of Finance is Jang Sik-ui (臧式毅), the Minister of Military Affairs is Ma Jeom-san (馬占山), the Minister of Industry is Jang Yeon-gyeong (張燕卿), the Minister of Foreign Affairs is Sa Gae-seok (謝介石), the Minister of Transportation is Jeong Gam-su (丁鑑修), and the Minister of Justice is Jo Heon-baek (趙欣伯). Won Geum-gye is originally a scholar who had served as an advisor to Zhang Zuolin and temporarily became the Chairman of the Fengtian Autonomy Council during the 1 September Incident. Jang Gyeong-hye was a leader emerging from the bandit ranks under Zhang Zuolin and is 60 years old, having been a former administrative head of Harbin Special District. Hee Heup is originally from Shenyang, graduated from the Japanese Military Academy, and is currently the governor of Jilin. He is now 48 years old. U Chung-han is from the Ouyang District (遼陽縣) of Fengtian and has been educated in Japan, currently being 61 years old, and is a prominent figure among the scholars. Jo Heon-baek, a young man of 45 from Wanping County (宛平縣) in Hebei Province, graduated from Meiji University in Japan with a doctorate in law. Jang Sik-ui is a military officer and was involved in Zhang Xueliang's assassination of Yang Wuting (楊宇霆), and is from Fengtian, graduating from the Infantry division of the Japanese Military Academy. Prime Minister Jeong Hyo-seo is noted for his scholarly achievements and had been a teacher to Puyi since his childhood.  

**Summary**  

This article discusses the establishment and structure of Manchukuo, a puppet state created by Japan in Northeast China, highlighting its political hierarchy and notable figures. It illustrates the tension of identities in colonial contexts, where the revival of a past dynasty (Qing) coexists with modern governance structures. The arrangements for authority in Manchukuo reflect complicity and resistance, showing the complexities of colonial politics. Historians may find the nuanced representation of collaboration and nationalism of key interest, as this was a period of significant geopolitical upheaval in East Asia.

國號는 滿洲, 年號는 大同
만주의 일중사변이 이러나던 즉시부터 장식의(張式毅), 원금개(袁金凱), 희흡(熙洽),장경혜(張景惠)등 일파의 인물을 중심으로 하야 맹렬이 운동하던 소위 만몽독립 문제는 그여히 구체화하야 지난 三月 一日에 정식으로 새국가의 수립을 내외에 선포하얏다. 그 국가의 이름은 처음에 만몽연성공화국(滿蒙聯省共和國)이니 또는 대동국(大同國)이니 하고 여러 의론이 잇섯스나 최후에 만주국(滿洲國)으로 결정되고 년호(年號)는 대동(大同)이라 층하게 되엿스니 대동은 주역(周易)에 천하대동(天下大同)이란 의미를 취한 것이라 한다. 그 국토(國土)는 봉천(奉天), 길림(吉林), 열하(熱河), 흑룡(黑龍)등 구동북의 사성(東北四省)을 총괄하야 면적이 약 七萬七千五百五十四方里에 달하고 人口는 약 三千四百萬이요 국체(國體)는 형식상 공화제이나 실질은 군주제(君主制)로 되여서 그 원수(元首)는 집정(執政)이란 명의로 전날 청조의 선통제부의(宣統帝溥儀)씨가 추대되고 국도(國都)는 장춘(長春)으로 결정되엿다 한다.

五色찬란한 新國旗
이 만주국의 새 국긔(國旗)는 누른 바탕에 청(靑), 적(赤), 백(白), 흑(黑) 사색을 가하엿스니 누른 것은 황색인종의 대동단결을 의미한 것이요 적색은 **을 의미한 것이요 청색은 청신한 긔운을 의미하고 백색은 평화와 **(平和 **)을 의미하고 흑색은 견실(堅實)을 의미한 것이요 신국가를 상증(象徵)한 것이라 한다.
