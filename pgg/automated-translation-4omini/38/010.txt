기사제목	새나라로! (To a New Country!)  
필자	李光洙 作詞 (Li Kwang-su, Lyricist)  
기사형태	시 (Poem)  

To a New Country!  
					Li Kwang-su, Lyricist  
Come, come, come, let us go,  
Old country, stay well,  
For I am leaving to a new country.  

Come, come, come, let us go,  
On the boundless ocean, on the long road,  
I am leaving to a new country.  

Come, come, come, let us go,  
When shall I return? There is no promise in this new country.  

Come, come, come, let us go,  
I am well, I am well,  
For I am leaving to a new country.  
(Music score is on page 20)  
An Ki-yong, Composer  

**Summary**  

The poem "To a New Country!" expresses a longing for change and a new beginning, symbolizing a journey away from the old towards something hopeful and uncertain. It captures a sense of nostalgia for one's homeland while also highlighting the aspirations for renewal and transformation often felt during the colonial period in Korea. Historians may find interest in how this piece reflects the cultural shifts and the yearning for independence and a brighter future among Koreans during their Japanese colonial rule.
