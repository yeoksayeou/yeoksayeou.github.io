統計室  
朝鮮米外地輸出  
Last year, the total amount of rice exported from Korea was 4,915,090 seok, of which 2,584,906 seok was brown rice, 2,169,684 seok was polished rice, 1,259 seok was regular rice, 99,313 seok was broken rice, and 59,928 seok was other types of rice. This represents a decrease of 625,865 seok compared to the previous year, with most exports going to various locations in Japan.

生絲의 生産高  
In Korea last year, the production of raw silk was 232,542 kan from machine silk and 39,485 kan from hand-spun silk, totaling 271,027 kan. Of this, the export volume was 1,968,727 geun with a value of 16,834,355 won for customs-cleared exports, and 48,416 geun valued at 424,692 won for small packages.

今年에 보리는 얼마?  
The authorities estimate this year's barley yield to be about 10 million seok. If the harvest goes well as projected, this would significantly exceed last year’s actual yield of 9,960,000 seok. Eating barley rice is said to promote good digestion, thus it seems likely that the smell from barley rice in rural areas will be stronger this year than last.

煙草消費高  
In March, the total tobacco sales figures from the Monopoly Bureau were as follows: the main store in Kyŏngsŏng had sales of 510,300 won, the Busan branch 202,000 won, the Daegu main branch 197,300 won, the Jeonju main branch 171,500 won, the Pyŏngyang main branch 142,400 won, the Mokpo branch 131,000 won, the Sarijun branch 121,600 won, the Daejeon branch 105,500 won, and other points under 100,000 won included 17 locations, totaling approximately 2,622,000 won. Cumulatively, since April of last year, the total sales for this year have reached 32,580,000 won, with the breakdown by monopoly region being Kyŏngsŏng 12,398,000 won, Jeonju 7,801,000 won, Daegu 6,409,000 won, and Pyŏngyang approximately 5,972,000 won. From these statistics, it can be inferred which regions consume tobacco more heavily.

全鮮 형무소 재감인원  
As of the end of February this year, the total number of inmates in various prisons and sub-stations was 16,719, which included 16,013 Koreans, 507 Japanese, and 199 foreigners. Categorized by offense, there were 14,027 sentenced prisoners, 1,918 criminal defendants, and 594 labor detainees. When categorizing by offense type, the major offenses were:  
- Theft / 13,892 / 423  
- Robbery / 2,522 / 5  
- Fraud / 1,156 / 6  
- Injury / 542 / 24  
- Homicide / 392 / 202  
- Embezzlement / 336 / 1  
- Forgery / 221 / 1  
- Arson / 1,900 / 77  
- Adultery / 112 / 15  

Political offenders included 229 men and 2 women for violations of the Maintenance of Public Order Act, 44 men for violations of the Security Act, and 15 men for disturbing public peace, among other charges. Comparing this to the 7,021 individuals recorded in the year of annexation, Meiji 43, this represents an increase of 9,698 individuals. With the prison population consistently increasing, the overcrowding has reached 6,000 beyond the intended capacity of 11,000. Consequently, there are places where up to 6 individuals are cramped into a single space of one pyong (3.3 square meters), compared to 1.3 for Japan’s mainland and 1.4 for Taiwan. This situation poses challenges due to difficulties in prison expansion or budget constraints; thus, starting from Showa 6 (1931), including plans to construct solitary cells for pre-trial detainees at the Seodaemun Prison, however, this would not effectively resolve the issue, as around 1,000 individuals are expected to be released on bail this year.  

**Summary**  

This article discusses various statistics regarding agriculture, exports, tobacco consumption, and the prison population in Korea during the year 1931. Notably, it outlines a significant drop in rice exports to Japan and an increase in the incarcerated population surpassing prison capacity, reflecting the socio-economic conditions under Japanese rule. Historians may find the analysis of decreasing exports amidst growing detention figures indicative of the underlying tensions and strategies of colonial governance during this period.

生絲의 生産高

今年에 보리는 얼마?
당국에서 예상하는 금년의 보리는 약 1,000만 석인데 다른 해만 업고 예상대로 잘 수확이 된다면 작년 實收 996만 석보다는 훨씬 增收이다. 보리밥을 먹으면 방구가 잘 나오는 법이닛가 결국 보리밥을 만히 먹는 농촌에는 방구 냄새도 작년보다 훨신 더할듯.

煙草消費高
