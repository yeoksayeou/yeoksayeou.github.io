Editorial Postscript

By nature, Byeolgeongon bears a peculiar name, and because it is sold for just 5 jeon, it has also earned the nickname "5 jeon magazine." Additionally, due to its policy of being out of print for only three days each month, it has garnered the names "three-day magazine" or "out-of-print magazine."

Thus, when referring to Byeolgeongon, it is often called interchangeably as the "5 jeon magazine," "three-day magazine," and "out-of-print magazine." To reiterate, this translates to out-of-print magazine = 3-day magazine = 5 jeon magazine = Byeolgeongon.

Out of print! Out of print! If we increase the number of copies printed each month, it seems we face more instances of being out of print. As the editor, this brings considerable stress, and I feel quite sorry for those who wish to order it later because I cannot send them the magazine.

This time, we’ve increased our print run by an additional 20,000 copies compared to before. However, as the content is more engaging and beneficial than ever, and the writing has been simplified for both knowledgeable and novice readers alike, there is a concern that it may soon go out of print again. As a dedicated reader, the best course of action to ensure you receive a copy is to order in advance.＜28＞ 

**Summary**

The editorial discusses the peculiarities surrounding the magazine Byeolgeongon, particularly its frequent out-of-print status due to its low price and printing policies. The editor expresses both anxiety over the magazine's availability and encouragement for readers to pre-order due to its increasing popularity. This insight into the challenges faced by publications during the colonial period reflects the broader struggles of media and cultural dissemination in Korea under Japanese rule, illustrating how market dynamics and consumer behavior influenced literary culture.

編輯後記
별건곤은 원래에 이름도 별스럽거니와 갑이 단 5전으로 나린 까닭에 또 5전잡지라는 별명을 듯고 게다가 또 달마다 단 사흘 동안에 절판이 되야 사흘잡지 또는 절판잡지라는 별명까지 엇게 되얏다.

그리하야 별건곤 하면 으레 5전 잡지, 사흘잡지=絶版잡지 그럿케 부르게 되엿다. 이것을 다시 각구루 되푸리 한다면 절판잡지=3일잡지=5전잡지=별건곤이다.

절판! 절판!하니 말이지 달마다 부수를 늘니면 늘릴사록 또 절판이되니 편즙자로서는 대단히 깃분 일이나 추후 주문하시는 이에게는 책을 못보내 드리니 미안이 적지 안타.

이번에는 전보다 일약 2만 部를 더 增刷하엿다. 그러나 내용이 전보다 더 자미잇고 유익하고 남녀로소 유식무식간에 누구나 다 읽을만치 글이 평이하게 된 까닭에 조금만 느지면 또 절판될 염려가 잇다. 애독자로서 안심하랴면 미리 주문하는 것이 제일 조흘 것갓다.＜28＞
