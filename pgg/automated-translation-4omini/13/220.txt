기사제목	開闢社의 支分社大募集 (Large Recruitment for Branch Offices of the Gaebyeok Company)  
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Article)  

Large Recruitment for Branch Offices of the Gaebyeok Company  
For those who are enthusiastic about regional culture, we invite you to establish branch or sub-branch offices of our company and to collaborate in our endeavors. You will become a part of this noble enterprise, enhance your status, acquire knowledge that will keep you from falling behind the times, and ultimately become a distinguished figure who contributes to the development of local culture.  

If you are interested, please enclose 10 jeon in a postage-paid envelope and request a guide from our headquarters. We will send detailed regulations. <96>  

**Summary**  

This article from 1928 encourages enthusiastic individuals to contribute to local culture by forming branch offices of the Gaebyeok Company. It highlights the potential benefits for participants, such as improved social status and educational opportunities. Historians may find this piece revealing regarding the efforts made by Korean cultural institutions during the colonial period to foster local engagement and identity amidst the Japanese occupation.

기사제목	開闢社의 支分社大募集
기사형태	사고·편집후기

開闢社의 支分社大募集
지방 문화를 위하야 有志하신 이는 本社의 支社나 分社機關을 設하시고 본사의 사업에 협력활동을 하여 주십시오. 좃코 존귀한 사업 큰 기관의 일원이 되는 동시에 따라서 자기의 지위가 향상되고  세상에 뒤떠러지지 안는 지식을 갓게 되는 한편으로 지방의 문화를 개척식혀 주는 고귀한 인물이 될 것입다.
有意하시면 10錢切手封入하야 본사로 안내서를 청구하십시오. 자세한 규정을 보내드리겟습니다.<96>
