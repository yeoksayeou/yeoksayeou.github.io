Dear Readers  

A terrifying heat wave is approaching that even your stillness cannot prevent; how do you plan to ward off the heat? Without a doubt, make sure to order the August issue of our magazine, "The Great Secret Disclosure Issue," in advance. Just imagine how refreshing it will be; you can look forward to it eagerly...<135>  

 
**Summary**  

This editorial piece expresses concern over the upcoming heat and encourages readers to prepare for it by ordering a specific issue of the magazine that promises to provide refreshing content. It reflects the publication's strategy to engage its audience during oppressive weather conditions, hinting at the importance of timely and anticipated content. Historians may find it significant as it shows how magazines at the time sought to maintain reader engagement amidst the challenges of colonial life, highlighting both social concerns and the evolving media landscape in Korea during the Japanese occupation.
