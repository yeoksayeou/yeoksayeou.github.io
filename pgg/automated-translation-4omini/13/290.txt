발행일	1928년 07월 01일  
기사제목	特히 女性讀者께 (Especially for Female Readers)  
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)  

특히 여성독자께  
We are deeply sorry that the published magazine "新女性" (New Woman) has unfortunately been temporarily suspended; I hope that it has not entirely faded from your memories. We earnestly ask that you continue to read as we await the great activity of our magazine's female section.  

**Summary**  

This article expresses regret regarding the suspension of the magazine "新女性", appealing to female readers to remain engaged and hopeful for its future content. It emphasizes the importance of the magazine's women's section and signals its potential upcoming revival. Historians might find this moment significant as it reflects the challenges faced by feminist publications under colonial rule and the ongoing struggle for female voices in the 1920s Korean society.
