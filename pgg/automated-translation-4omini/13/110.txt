기사제목	갈팡질팡하는 자들 (The Confused Ones)  
필자	동대문병원 부인의사 吉貞姬  
기사형태	문예기타 (Literary Other)  

When things go wrong, people are confused; when there is no place to go, they are confused; when someone is about to collapse, they are confused.

Seeing someone who is confused makes me feel sorry, but even more pitiful is the sight of someone who is so easily overwhelmed that they start sweating profusely. This usually happens when they are bringing a sick patient with them to a hospital. 

Lately, during this hot season, there are many people who bring their children suffering from severe digestive issues to the hospital, panicking as their children seem to be nearing death. When the illness is light, they pretend not to notice, merely feeding the child whatever they can, but as the condition worsens, they start panicking. Many seem to pass from digestive disorders to depletion (消耗症) and eventually to pneumonia (肺炎) or meningitis (腦膜炎), only realizing the seriousness of the situation when it's almost too late.

I believe that the majority of these situations arise not from a lack of ability to consult a doctor, but rather from a lack of proper attention and preparation. It seems to me that people who are really organized and proactive do not resort to such chaotic behaviors.  

**Summary**  

The article discusses the confusion and desperation of parents bringing their severely ill children to hospitals, specifically during a hot summer, highlighting a cycle of neglect leading to increasingly serious illnesses due to a lack of awareness or preparation. This illustrates broader themes of public health and medical awareness in colonial Korea, where access to healthcare and understanding of medical issues were crucial yet often ignored, especially among the less educated segments of society. Historians may find it interesting as it reflects the struggles of colonial subjects navigating both cultural and healthcare systems while confronting modern medical challenges.

갈팡질팡하는 자들
						동대문병원 부인의사 吉貞姬
일이 모라칠 때 갈팡질팡, 갈 곳이 업슬 때 갈팡질팡, 사람 숨넘어 갈대 갈팡질팡
갈팡질팡하는 사람보고 딱하게 녁이지 안는 이는 업겟지만 더욱이 가엽고도 보기에 너무도 딱해서 등에서 진땀이 붓젹부젹 흐르게 듸는 때는 병에 걸닌 환자를 끌고 갈팡질팡하는 사람과 부듸철 때이다.
요새가티 더운 때에 데일 심한 아해들의 소화불량중으로 다-죽게 된 아해를 병원에 다리고 와서 쩔쩔매는 이가 부지기수이다.
병이 좀 가벼웟슬때에는 모르는 척하듯이 아해가 달나는 대로 주착업시 내리 먹이기들만하다가 차차 병이 더해진다. 소화불량에서 소모증(消耗症)으로 건너서 합반증(合班症) 폐염(肺炎) 뢰막염(腦膜炎)에까지 이르러서 마즈막 판을 당하고서야 정신이 나서 이리저리 헤매이는 이들이 보통인 듯하다.
사실상 나는 의사를 차저 볼 수 업는 사정으로 그러케 되는 이가 만은 것보다 평시에 주의하는 점이 부족한 택에 이러나는 일이 만타고 본다.
모든 일에 준비성잇고 부즈런한 사람치고 갈팡질팡하는 추태를 부리는 이는 아마 업슬 것으로 나는 밋는다.<54>
