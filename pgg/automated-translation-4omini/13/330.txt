기사형태	잡저 (Magazines)  

懸賞募集 理髮所投票  
Is there anyone in the city who does not go to a barbershop? A man's hairstyle is something he cannot neglect as if it were someone else's matter.  

Which barbershop in Gyeongseong is the best? Which one in Daegu, Pyongyang, Wonsan, Kaesong, or Incheon is the top choice? Please cast your votes.  

Let us know the first, second, and third place in Gyeongseong, separately from Pyongyang, Daegu, and so forth. The purpose of this is not just to determine which barbershops are well-known but also to enhance the quality of barbering; therefore, do not disregard this request and cast your votes—one for each place, please.  

Voting Regulations  

Each person must vote for only one place and must carefully cut out the voting form located at the back of this magazine and write your choice on it. Voting will take place from July 7 until August 20 (the votes collected by July 21 will be announced first in the August issue, and the results of the votes up to August 20 will be added to announce the final winners in the September issue).  

Vote is limited to the six places: Gyeongseong, Pyongyang, Kaesong, Incheon, and Wonsan, and you may write and send only one barbershop from your residing area.  

Prize Regulations  

From each of the six cities, prizes will be awarded from first to seventh place (a total of 42 shops across six cities), and the winning barbershops will be introduced in articles along with photographs.  

For those who voted, a draw will be held separately for each city, designating one person for first place (total six), one person for second place (total six), and ten people for third place (total sixty).  

The first prize will receive a premium razor and two additional prizes. The second prize will receive quality hair oil and dandruff treatment products, along with additional prizes. The third prize will receive special products from the magazine’s office. <124>  

**Summary**  

This article is a call for public participation in voting for the best barbershops across several cities in Korea, presenting a competitive spirit among local businesses. It illustrates how everyday practices such as grooming were not just personal choices but also community-focused events. Historians might find it interesting as it highlights the blend of consumer culture and local business competition during the colonial era in Korea, reflecting early forms of marketing and public engagement.

지금 도시 남자치고 이발소에 안다니는 이가 잇겟슴닛가 이발소 이약이는 결코 결코 남의 이약이 하듯 지내처 버리지 못할 것임니다.
京城 안에서 어느 이발소가 제일나은가. 大邱, 平壤, 元山, 開城, 仁川, 서는 어는 곳이 제일 나흔가?......투표하여 주십시오.
그리하야 京城 따로 平壤 따로 大邱 따로 그곳 그곳에서의 일등 이등 삼등을 알고 지내게 하십시다. 이리하는 일은 현재 잘 아는 집을 널니 알게 되는데만 긋치는 것이 안이라, 압흐로 더욱더욱 조와지게 하는 일이니 결코 등한히 녁이지 말고 다 가티 1票씩을 던저 주시기 바랍니다.

投票規定

한 사람이 한 곳만 쓰시되 반듯이 이 책 맨 끗장 구텽이에 잇는 투표용지를 가위로 오러서 거긔에 쓰실 일.
투표는 7월 7일로부터 8월 20일까지(7월21일까지의 투표 수를 우선 8월호에 발표하고 그 후 8월 20일까지의 것을 가산하야 당선 결과를 9월호에 발표함)
京城, 平壤, 開城, 仁川, 元山
상기 6地方에 限하되 각각 자기 거주지방 이발 한 곳만 적어 보낼 것.

賞品規定

상기 6都市 각 지방마다 1등으로부터 7등까지 定하고(6都市 都合 42店) 당선된 이발소를 기사와 사진으로 誌上에 소개함.
투표하신 이에게는 각 都 투표 따로따로 모아서 제비를 뽑아 1등 1인씩(6都 合6인) 2등 1인씩(合6인) 3등 10인씩(合60인)을 정하야 당선자로 하고
1등에는 上等면도 1個進呈(외 副賞品 2種 添呈)
2등에는 上等香油 비듬약 進呈(외 副賞品 添呈)
3등에는 본사 특별상품 進呈<124>
