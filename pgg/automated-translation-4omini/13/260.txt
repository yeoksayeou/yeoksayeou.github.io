기사제목	大讀刺記事 (Great Read on the Stabbing Incident), 大京城狂舞曲 (狂舞曲 of the Great Capital City)  
필자	雙S生, 石火生  
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Commentary)  

The articles that were eagerly anticipated not only by readers but also among fellow members of the magazine were completed by the curious pens of 双S生 and 石火生, spanning over 560 lines across 11 pages. However, unfortunately, due to the reason of “Urban Insult,” they could not be published.  

**Summary**

This piece reflects the tension and censorship prevalent in colonial Korea during the late 1920s. The title suggests a critical or satirical examination of urban life, indicating an emerging literary trend that challenged authority and social norms. Historians may find the mention of censorship particularly revealing as it illustrates the dynamics of power and resistance in a colonized context.
