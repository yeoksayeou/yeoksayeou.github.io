Mokkeul and the Old Man  

1. On the day the break commenced, at the station,  
Mokkeul said, "This time I really must go and do it. It’s a matter of marriage."  
Moppo replied, "This time I will definitely meet my father (父) and obtain his consent. Please don't eat too many candies or snacks during the trip and behave well." They parted with these words.  

2. Not three days later, Mokkeul wrote a letter saying, "Oh, my beloved Mr. OO, I cannot bear to be away from your warm embrace, and I long for your presence to comfort my lonely frame." Mokkeul sent it in a pink envelope.  

3. Due to some delaying matters, Moppo made a detour, and the letter arrived before him, in the hands of his father. The old man read the letter and became enraged, exclaiming, "This kid better come back!"  

4. Five days later, Moppo returned, having shown even more respect than before, and spoke, "As for Mokkeul, she comes from a respectable family and is known for studying well and having good manners. She is the finest young lady in Gyeongseong..." Look at the old man's face as he was beaming.  

5. "Now you have said everything you needed to say."  
"Yes, just that."  
"This family has lost its way. Is a girl who sends such filthy letters to another family's son considered an upright person in Gyeongseong? Is she, under the pretense of studying, consorting with such a shameless woman, then coming to boast about it to her father? Go back and read this letter aloud to him."

Thus, everything was ultimately rejected.<97>  

**Summary**  

This article portrays a dramatic interaction surrounding courtship and family honor in colonial Korea, highlighting the strict societal norms regarding propriety and the expectations placed on young women. The rejection of Mokkeul's relationship due to her letter showcases the severe scrutiny faced by women and the roles they were expected to play within their families. Historians may find the depiction of familial pressure and gender norms in the colonial context revealing, as it illustrates the complexities of love and societal expectation during this period.

모껄 『이번에는 꼭 하고 와야해요. 무언 무어야 리혼수속 말이지』
모뽀 『이번에는 꼭 늙은이(父) 보고 당신 이약이를 해서 승낙을 엇어 가지고 올터니.』 하고 고 동안에 딴사탕이나 과자 사먹지 말고 얌전히 잇스라고 부탁하고 작별하엿다.
2. 헤여진 사흘이 못지나서 『아이고 나의 사랑 OO씨여 당신의 땃듯한 품을 떠나서는 하로도 못 지내겟슴니다. 속히 올나오서서 이 쓸쓸한 몸을 O어 주세요...』 모껄이 편지를 써서 분홍꼿 봉투에 너보냇다.
