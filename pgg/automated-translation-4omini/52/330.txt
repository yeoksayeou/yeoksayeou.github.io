Prize Questions

Regular Prize: Ninety-nine people, a book  

Rules  
◇ Please write your answers briefly on a postcard-sized inquiry while also including your name and address.  
◇ Readers must cut out (from this month's issue) and send it.  
◇ On the envelope, write to the "Kyŏngsŏng Kaep'ŭksa Byeolgŏn Prize Division." (It is acceptable to include other manuscripts and prize answers.)  
◇ Write "Manuscript inside" on the envelope and affix a two-won stamp by cutting the envelope flap with scissors so it can be mailed.  
◇ Send it within twenty days.  
◇ If possible, it is more convenient for everyone to send several answers in one envelope, saving each other postage; however, each person's answer must include one reader certificate cut out.
