기사제목	무엇하러오는가을인가 (What Comes This Autumn?)  
필자	申■澈 (Sin Cheol)  
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Commentary/Editorial Postscript)  

What comes this autumn?  

It was unavoidable that I could not see([26]) it.  

  

**Summary**  

The article reflects on the changing season and its impact on human emotions and activities. The author contemplates the essence of autumn and the unfamiliarity it brings, suggesting a sense of introspection and questioning of one's actions as nature transitions. In the context of Korean colonial history, it may reveal how individuals grappled with their identities and feelings during a time of turbulence and change, highlighting the emotional resonance of nature amidst societal challenges.
