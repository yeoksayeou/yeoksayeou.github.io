Newspaper Advertising Is Useless  

At a time when promotion is necessary, the application of newspapers is significant. However, our company has reached a point where there is no need to place magazine advertisements in newspapers. This is because the five magazines published by Gaebyeoksa (개벽사) significantly exceed the circulation of daily and monthly newspapers by more than ten times. Therefore, our company is now in the position of deciding against placing magazine advertisements in newspapers. We kindly inform our readers to understand this in advance. Moreover, the new publication from Gaebyeoksa, "Sin Gyeongje" (新經濟), is only 2 jeon (전) and the annual prepayment is 20 jeon, so you will be able to learn in detail about the news and contents of the large magazines, so do not delay in ordering "Sin Gyeongje." <34>  

**Summary**  

The article discusses the transition from newspaper advertising to magazine advertising, emphasizing the superior circulation of the magazines published by Gaebyeoksa compared to newspapers. This reflects a significant shift in media consumption during the colonial period in Korea, illustrating changing dynamics in marketing and readership preferences. Historians might find it interesting that this article highlights the growing influence of magazines during a time of colonial suppression, suggesting a cultural assertion and resilience through media.

新聞廣告 無用
선전이 필요한 이 때에 잇서서 신문의 응용은 대단한 것이다. 그러나 본사에서는 잡지광고를 신문에 낼 필요가 업는 시기에 도달하얏다. 그것은 개벽사에서 발행하는 다섯 가지 잡지와 부수가 굉장히 만어저 일간과 월간의 구별은 잇슬지언정 신문 부수 몃 10배 이상으로 나가게 되는 까닭이다. 그럼으로 본사에서는 압흐로 신문에 잡지광고를 내지 안을 방침을 취하랴 한다. 그것을 미리부터 량해하야 두기를 독자 제위께 고한다. 그리고 개벽사에서 새로 발행하는 「新經濟」는 단 2전이요. 1년 선금 20전이니 대 잡지의 소식과 내용을 자세이 알게 될 것이니 하로라도 지체말고 「新經濟」를 주문해 두라.＜34＞
