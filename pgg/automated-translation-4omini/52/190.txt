기사제목	農業上의 新發見 (New Discoveries in Agriculture)  
기사형태	잡저 (Magazine Article)  

Agricultural innovations have been remarkably discovered, allowing for yields of eight bushels of brown rice per 1 jeong (反步), and twelve bushels of wheat, as well as the potential for profits ranging from three to four hundred won per 1 jeong through various crops. 

This secret method has been developed through years of careful study and experimentation. It has been demonstrated that anyone can succeed with this method, making it not only advantageous but also reliable. Reports from various practitioners across regions confirm its efficacy. The guide can be obtained free of charge by applying for it via postcard.

Tokyo City, Kōjimachi Ward, 2–10  
Agricultural Education Association <33>  

**Summary**

This article introduces a new agricultural method that promises significant increases in crop yields, suggesting that it has been validated through extensive experimentation. The promotion of this method during the colonial era reflects an interest in improving agricultural productivity, potentially to bolster economic self-sufficiency in Korea. Historians might find the focus on agriculture notable, illustrating how colonial policies and innovations were marketed to Korean farmers during this period.

農業上의 新發見
案內書無代進呈
지금 놀 날 만큼 하게 신식의 농업 법이 발견 되야 토지 1反步에서 玄米로 8석 나고, 小麥이면 12석을 實收하고, 기타의 농작물이면 1 反步 3,4백 원의 이익을 엇는 비법이 잇다.
此秘法은 다년간 고심하야 실험한 결과로 발견 된 것인데 이것이면 어떤 이가 하든지 잘 되는 것이고 또는 유리하고도 확실하다는 것이 실험되얏슴으로 今回에 공개하기로 된 것인데  이 방법으로써 농경을 하게되면 누구나 물론하고 농작을 잘 하게 된다. 各地의 實驗者로부터의 報告와 禮狀이 山과 가티 싸이는 것을 보드래도 이것을 證明하게 된다. 안내서은 엽서로 申込하면 곳 無科로 보냅니다.
