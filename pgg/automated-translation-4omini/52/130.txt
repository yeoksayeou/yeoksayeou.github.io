기사제목	學界漫話 (Talks on Academia)  
기사형태	소식 (News)  

In recent months, there has been a notable silence in Korean academia, but new achievements have emerged as several individuals have recently received doctorates. Among these, there are those who are particularly distinguished, including those who took their degrees abroad, which is especially honorable and a source of pride. However, it is concerning that alongside one doctorate, there is also an alarming absence within the academia.

Music Doctorate 桂貞植 (Kwon Jeong-sik)
Kwon Jeong-sik, a violinist since his youth, is a well-known figure among music fans in Korea. He went to Germany to pursue his studies in music and graduated last year from the National University of Würzburg in Germany, successfully defending his dissertation last June, which earned him the esteemed title of Doctor of Music. He plans to return to Korea within this year. Our musician, who has left scars in his heart from a deep love, is urged not to forget his old passion but to actively engage in life for Korean art instead.

Philosophy Doctorate 崔允浩 (Choi Yun-ho)
After seventeen years, he has returned from the United States. Although not many might know him, Yun-ho, who graduated from the Dae-seong School in Pyongyang and moved to the U.S. at twelve years of age, studied for nearly twenty years. He obtained a Ph.D. in education from Indiana University and plans to return to Korea this month. Dr. Choi has vowed to dedicate his life to the establishment of farmers' culture and intends to run the Bong-san Farmers’ Academy alongside his wife, Lee Seon-haeng. It is admirable if he becomes a real ally of the farmers in Korea.

Economics Doctorate 金度演 (Kim Do-yeon)
Kim Do-yeon was well-known during his studies in Tokyo for his eloquence and writing abilities. After a long absence from the news, he was imprisoned for over a year during the Gyeongmi Uprising. He moved to the United States in 1922, and after ten years, he is returning this month (August), bringing back a doctorate in economics from the American University. It seems his once lush hair has grown even more disheveled.

Tragic Fate of 医学博士兪日濬 (Yu Il-jun)
Dr. Yu Il-jun, a leading bacteriologist and a promising young scholar at thirty-eight, was recently reported to have drowned while swimming with his family in the Han River last August. The calm and composed demeanor he displayed as a professor in the medical school will forever be missed, as it is a significant loss for Korea’s medical community.

**Summary**

The article highlights several academic achievements in colonial Korea, particularly the awarding of doctoral degrees to notable figures in music, philosophy, and economics. It also mourns the tragic loss of a key scholar in bacteriology. This showcases the complexities of cultural and intellectual life during the Japanese colonial period, highlighting both advancements in education and the personal tragedies faced by these scholars. Historians might find the juxtaposition of achievements and losses particularly revealing as it reflects the broader struggles of Korean intellectuals under colonial rule.

學界漫話
적막하든 조선학게에 최근두어달을두고 박사세분이 새로생기고 박사중에도 모다 특색잇는박사 그리고 모다 외국에서 학위를 어덧다는 것이 더욱명예스럽고 자랑스럽다 그는 그러타하거니와 한박사를 일흔 것은 몹시도 가석하다 학게에도 깃븜과 스름이 석기어잇다할는지.

音樂博士 桂貞植氏
게정식(桂貞植)씨는 소년시대부터 바욜리니스트로 조선안음악팬으로는 누구나 거의 그이름을 긔억하고잇지만 씨는 독일에 건너가서 십개성상간이나 음악을 전공하고 작년에 독일국립 「울쓰뿔크」대학을 졸업하고 논문을 제출햇든바 그것이 지난 六月에 통과되야 음악박사의 영예스러운 학위를 엇고 구미를 만유한후 금년안으로 귀국하리라한다 날 사랑으로 가슴에 상처를 바든 우리 음악가는 도라오는어리에도 감개가 무량할 더욱이 감상성만흔예술가 로서는 잇지못할긔억이 잇겟지만 옛날의사랑을 생각지말고 조선의예술을 위하야 생명잇는 활동을 해보라고 미리부탁이나 해둘.

哲學博士崔允浩氏
十七년만에 미국에서 도라왓스니 그의 이름을 아는이도 별로업겟지만 옛날평양대성학교(大成學校)출신으로 스믈두살에 미국에건너가서 근 二十년간이나 체류하며 여러곳에서 수학하고 인디아나주립대학에서 교육학으로 박사의 학위를 어든 최윤호(崔允浩)씨가 금월중에 귀국하신바 최박사는 무엇보다도 농민문화수립에 일생을 희생하시겟다는 정신으로 그의 부인리선행(李善行)녀사와가티 손을 잇글고 봉산농민학원장(鳳山農民學院長)이되야 가시겟다하니 박사로는 참고마운일이다. 상투튼 조선 농민과 손을 붓들고 정말 그들의 동무가 되야준다면 더욱고마운일.

經濟學博士金度演氏
김도연(金度演)씨하면 한참당년동경유학시대에는 연설잘하고 글쓰기로 이름이쟁쟁하든분인데 한참동안 그의소식이 전연무문이든중 긔미운동당시 동경유학생대표로일년여의 철창생활을 치르고 一九二二년에 미국으로건너가서 십년만에 이달(八月)로귀국하게되엿고 선물은 미국와상통아메리칸대학에서 경제학박사의 학위를 어더가지고 도라오시는것이란다. 하하 버서진머리가 더버서지섯겟군.

醫學博士兪日濬씨의 不幸
의학박사유일준(兪日濬)씨 과연조선의 세균학자요  三十八세라는소장학자로서 이름이놉고 기대도만튼바 지난 八月중에 가족과가티 한강에가 수영을하시다가 잘못하야 익사를하섯다. 이제는 의학전문학교교수실에서 그의 아존하고 침착한 자타입이 백퍼센트나 풍부하든 유씨의 면영도 다시는 영구이보지못할 듯 조선의학게를위하야 얼마나 아운일인지 모르겟다.
