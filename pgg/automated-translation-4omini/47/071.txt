1-Chome 191-2 Kan Taekang  
In the article titled "Secret Family Visit" in last February's issue, it seems that some readers misunderstood the address "Seodaemun-Jeong 1 Jeongmok 19xx" as belonging to the aforementioned family. However, I would like to clarify that this is contrary to the facts. 

**Summary**  

This brief note clarifies a misunderstanding from a previous article regarding the address of a family that was mentioned. The clarification reflects the sensitivity of personal information during the colonial period in Korea, highlighting the importance of accuracy in journalism at a time when scrutiny of private lives could have serious implications. Historians may find it significant as it illustrates the delicate balance between public interest and personal privacy in early 20th-century Korea under Japanese rule.
