발행일	1927년 10월 01일  
기사제목	全鮮女子庭球大會를 보고 (Report on the Nationwide Women's Tennis Tournament)  
필자	一記者 (A Reporter)  
기사형태	소식 (News)  

The fifth nationwide Women's Tennis Tournament, which has nearly become an annual event sponsored by the Dong-A Ilbo, was held on October 1st this year at the Gyeongseong Sports Field.

Although I could not grasp a "naget" or a "pat," still, when there is an athletic meeting, it seemed like I had to engage in it actively. How could one not get excited over a nationwide Women's Tennis Tournament? It felt like an event of great significance, and I rushed to participate from early on, together with my friend.

This day happened to be October 1st, "Sports Day." At the Gyeongseong Sports Field, tens of thousands of students from public and private schools in Gyeongseong all showed up. Besides the unified athletic competitions, there were gymnastics, dance, baseball, and tennis - truly a spectacle beyond description. Moreover, the women's tennis tournament coincided with this, making it quite a sight for both male and female spectators!

"Well, today I’ve seen quite a crowd. Moreover, the scent of women's perfume must be prevalent. Let's hurry up, but why is the weather like this? Is it because it's a gathering of women, that even the sky seems to be weeping? In any case, as long as it doesn’t rain, it will be a fine day for watching the matches and for competition as well, although tears might flow this year. Alas, it's true that when men lose in a match, they do not cry."

While I was making such idle comments, the tram arrived at the training center.

After briefly stopping at the joint athletic competition venue to watch a short-distance race, I entered the tennis competition venue, but it was relatively empty, resembling a poor household's gathering. According to the program, there were as many as twelve schools participating, including regional schools like Ishin Girls' High School from Jinju, Jeongui Girls' High School from Pyongyang, Ryu Girls' High School from Wonsan, and Hohsootun Girls' High School from Kaesong. From Gyeongseong, schools like Ewha, Jinmyeong, Jeongsin, Yonggok, Women's Commercial School, Public Women's High School, First Girls' High School, and Second Girls' High School were also participating. Regardless of whether their skills had improved, the increase in participating schools compared to previous years proves progress in the women's tennis sector. It was disappointing not to see the shadow of Shukmyung, Paehwa, and Dongdeok participating this year, and even more disappointing was not being able to see the exquisite silk dresses from the Yongmyeong in Gongju.

"I wonder which school will win this year?" I thought, filled with curiosity, and upon observing the players, they all seemed quite plump and robust, truly appearing like athletes. Their shoes, socks, and uniforms were dazzlingly white, and each player was standing on the white chalk line, ready to hit the white ball into the white net, creating a refreshing scene. Furthermore, whether they succeeded or failed, the display of red lips showcasing their white teeth was even more enjoyable to behold.

The uniforms were generally different, with the skirts of Jinju being distinctive, and the ties were also unique, particularly the red ties from Pyongyang stood out. Among them, the unique color of Ewha's general uniform was impressive. Just like soldiers wear differing uniforms, the uniform color of the general's group must also vary. This was a well-thought-out plan.

At nine o'clock, the matches indeed began. Jeongui and Jeongsin played against each other, Ishin and Second Girls' High School matched up, Ewha met First Girls' High School, and Hohsootun faced off against the Women's High School, while Ryu, Jinmyeong, Public Women's Commercial School, and Yonggok received first-round byes. Whether to watch Court A or Court B? During such times, having four eyes would have been beneficial.

Having a basic understanding of tennis was lacking, and while I couldn’t grasp the technical aspects, I could ascertain that Ryu, Jeongsin, and Ishin were weak in terms of skill, while Women's Commercial School and Yonggok exhibited the demeanor of newcomers. It was quite unexpected that First Girls' High School and Public Women's High School lost in their first matches. Although Ewha's general group performed well, they committed mistakes due to careless play. Regardless, the left-handed player Yi Baenang left quite an impression on the spectators. Jinmyeong's first group was commendable in both skill and performance, and Hohsootun's general group also exhibited decent skills. After quite a few fierce exchanges, Ewha ultimately secured victory in the end, bringing the audience to a place of near-ten-year sorrow.

However, one remarkable phenomenon that day was that neither the players nor the cheering squad displayed any signs of distress after a loss. It was indeed a curiosity, for at last year’s tournament, they had all burst into tears upon losing—this year, they merely left in a detached and composed manner. Whether their emotions have dulled, or they have been washed over by societal changes, losing one's sensitivity remains an unanswered question.

Cheering was generally group-oriented and organized, although occasionally there were instances of unruly behavior resembling middle school high spirits, which weren’t bothersome. However, the coolness from the local cheering squads was disheartening. How disappointing it must have been for the regional players!

In any case, this year's women's tennis tournament was a grand success. Next year will surely be even more spectacular. I’d like to conclude by wishing the players continued efforts.  

**Summary**

This article reports on the fifth nationwide Women's Tennis Tournament held in Gyeongseong in 1927, highlighting the large participation from numerous schools and the excitement generated among spectators. The competition not only showcased athletic skill but also reflected social dynamics among women during the colonial period, illustrating how such sporting events brought forward a sense of unity and enthusiasm. Historians may find it interesting that even amidst colonial rules, women were actively participating in sports, which often served as a platform for asserting cultural identity and social change.

기사형태	소식

全鮮女子庭球大會를 보고
						一記者
거의 年中行事化한 東亞日報社主催의 第五回全朝鮮女子庭球大會는 금년도 10월 1일로써 京城運動場에서 열니게 되얏다.

자신이 「나겟」하나 「뺏트」하나를 쥐여보지 못하면서도 엇쨋든 운동회라면 머리를 싸매고 따라단이는 성미엿다. 함울며 全朝鮮女子庭球大會라니 안이 갈 수 잇스랴? 무슨 굉장한 數나 떠러질 듯이 雀躍之勢로써 일즉부터 덤벼들엇다. 微笑君과 가티.

이날은 맛츰 10월 1일 「體育데-」엿다. 京城運動場에는 경성내 공사립 남녀학교 수만명 학생이 총출동하야 聯合競技외에 체조, 딴스, 야구, 정구가 잇서 실로 장관이상의 장관을 볼 수가 잇섯다. 게다가 여자 정구대회까지 겹해 열녓스니 남녀 관중이 여간 할 수 잇스랴!

『여보게 오늘은 사람개나 톡톡히 보게 되얏네. 더구나 여자의 향수냄새는 슬컷 맛틀 수 잇겟네. 자- 속히 가세. 그런데 일기는 왜 이 모양인가? 여자들 모임이닛가 한울도 눈물을 짜 내려는가. 엇쨋든 비만 안오면 구경도 좃코 경기에도 조흔 일기일세.금년도 눈물동의나 흘닐 터이지. 아이그 대톄 울기들도 잘도 하지 하기야 경기애 패하면 남자는 안우나.』
뭇지도 대답도 안하는 말을 혼자서 이럿케 수선을 떠는 새에 전차는 발서 훈련원에 다엇다.

각 학교 연합 경기장에 들러서 잠간 단거리 경주를 보고나서 정구대회장으로 드러가니 아직 일너 그런지 가난한 집 잔채가티 관중이 별로 만치 안타. 「프로글람」을 어더보니 작으만치 열두 학교나 되는데 지방으로는 晋州의 一新女高, 平壤의 正義女高, 元山의 樓氏女高, 開城의 好壽敦女高가 출진하얏고 京城으로는 梨花, 進明, 貞信, 龍谷, 女商, 公立女學普, 第一女高, 第二女高가 출진하얏다. 기술의 진보는 엇던지 아직 모르나 참가교가 예년보다 만흔 것은 여자 정구계의 진보를 증명하고 잇다. 年來로 출진하든 淑明, 培花, 同德의 그림자가 업는 것이 섭섭하얏고 그보다도＜78＞ 公州永明의 모시치마 적삼을 못 보게 된 것이 섭섭하얏다.
『금년은 엇던 학교가 우승을 할텐고?』하는 몹시 궁금한 생각을 가지고 선수들의 觀容察色부터 해보니 모다 배나무 목침가티 퉁퉁하고 튼튼히 된 법이 과연 운동가다워 뵈인다. 구쓰, 양말, 복장이 눈이 부시게 하-얀데다가 엇던 선수는 분까지 발느고 石灰白線을 타고 서서 백색나껫으로 백색 넷트를 향하야 백색 뽈을 주고 밧고 하는 것이 엇지도 淸快한지. 게다가 성공, 실패간에 紅脣을 여러 박씨 가튼 하-얀니빨을 관중의 압헤 자랑하는 것은 더욱 보기 조왓다.
운동복은 대개 상이한데 晋州의 아랑지 치마가 특색이엿고 넥타이도 상이한데 平壤의 빨간 넥타이가 눈에 띄웟다. 그 중에도 한가지 이약이거리는 梨花軍大將組服色이 특제품인 것이엿다. 군인에도 소장 대장의 복색이 판이한 것가티 大將組의 복색은 물론 달너야 될 것이다. 잘한 생각이엿다.

아홉시가 되자 정말 경기는 시작되얏다. 正義와 貞信이 걸니고 一新과 第二女高가 걸니고 梨花와 第一女高가걸니고 好壽敦과 女高가 걸니고 樓氏, 進明, 公立女商, 龍谷女校는 제일회는 부전승이엿다. A코-트를 볼가 B코-트를 볼가? 이런 때는 눈이 넷이 엿스면 조켓다.
정구에 대한 상식이 본래 缺如하닛가 기술 여하는 잘 알 수가 업스나 하여간 樓氏와 貞信과 一新은 기술이 약하얏고 女商과 龍谷은 더구나 初出의 態가 그대로 뵈엿고 第一女高(日)와 공립여고가 제일회에 패배을 당함은 상상밧기엿다.
梨花의 大將組는 하기는 잘하나 기술을 밋고 着心을 들하야 실수가 만엇다. 엇쨋든 왼손잡이 李寶貝孃은 관중에게 만흔 인상을 주엇다. 進明의 제일조는 기술로나 성적으로나 과연 찬사를 들일만하고 好壽敦의 大將組도 기술이 상당하얏다. 梨花와 一勝一敗甘戰數十回에 결국 최후일공으로 승리를 得하는데는 과연 관중으로 하여금 십년감수는 식키고 마럿다.

그런데 이날의 한 奇觀은 선수나 응원단간에 패배을 당하고 나서도 우지 안는 것이 한 奇觀이엿다. 작년만해도 지고 나오면 나껫을 메다치며 모다 대성통곡을 하얏든 것이 사실이엿는데 今年은 그저 평범하게 태연하게 예사로 슬적 도라서는 것이 일대 의문이엿다. 감정이 鈍해젓는지 세태물정에 씻기고 달녀서 심정이 寒凉해 젓는지 엇쨋든 상상밧기엿다.
응원도 대개 단체적이요 규칙적이엿는데 간혹 중학생들의 작란 비슷한 「야지」가 잇서서 심심치 안엇고 지방응원을 넘우도 냉냉함은 매우 섭섭하얏다. 지방선수들이 얼마나 섭섭하얏스랴?

엇잿든 금년의 여자정구대회는 대성황이엿다. 명년은 이보다 더 장관이겟지. 끗흐로 선수諸君의 부단한 분투를 祝하고 고만둔다.＜79＞
