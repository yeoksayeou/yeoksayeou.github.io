Yal-gu-jŭn Trend refers to the slow-moving marathon practice<89> of tourists.  

**Summary**  

This article discusses the slow marathon practice among tourists, highlighting a growing trend and the leisurely nature of the activity during that period. Historians may find the focus on leisure activities during the colonial period revealing, as it reflects the cultural shifts and adaptations of Korean society under Japanese rule, indicating a blend of traditional values with newfound modern leisure pursuits.
