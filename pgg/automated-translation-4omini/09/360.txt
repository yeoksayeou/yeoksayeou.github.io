What With

I should apologize to the readers, yet there is no matter to discuss.

This is a phrase I often say, and although it may seem like I am merely evading responsibility, having barely finished the August issue, I began work on the September issue only to find that Shin Yeong-cheol, who had been ill for a long time, returned from the countryside. The weather was also turning cool with autumn, making us particularly tense as we attempted to gather plausible content. However, during the censorship process, we unexpectedly faced deletions, leading to insufficient manuscripts. At the same time, a matter arose, causing Cha Sang-jam to go on a business trip, while Bang Jeong-hwan was bedridden due to illness for more than a month, groaning in pain. In the end, we barely managed to edit it after adding the third and fourth manuscripts, but Park Dal-seong also had to go on a business trip, leaving our already small team feeling completely depleted.

In these circumstances, we finally issue this after two months, and we ourselves are significantly dissatisfied with it as well. While expressing our grievances might be taken as excuses, we only hope that those who understand us will forgive us.

 

**Summary**

The editorial note expresses the difficulties faced by the publication team in putting together the magazine due to illness and unforeseen circumstances, including censorship challenges that led to a lack of sufficient content. The tone reveals a sense of resignation and frustration with the ongoing struggles of producing quality content during a challenging period, reflective of the constraints faced by intellectuals in colonial Korea. Historians might find interest in examining how these external pressures affected literary expression and the functioning of the press during Japanese colonial rule.

무엇으로
독자 여러분께 사과해야 할 터이고 할 말슴은 업고 그럿슴니다.
언제나 하는 말이여서 책임만 회피하랴는 것 갓슴니다 만은 겨우 8월 호를 내여노코 9월 호 편집에 착수하자 오래 병중에 잇던 申瑩澈군도 시골로부터 도라오고 때도 서늘한 가을이라 가장 긴장한 태도로 그럴듯한 내용을 모아보려 햇던 것이 검열의 際에 의외의 삭제를 당하야 원고는 부족한데다가 마침 일이 생기어 車相瓚군이 지방에 출장하고 方定煥군이 병으로 누어 月餘나 呻吟하는 중에 3차 4차 원고를 추가하야 겨우 편집을 맛치고나니 朴達成군이 또 지방 출장을 가게되야 그러지 안해도 적은 손에 그만 社안이 텅 뷔인 것 가티 되엿슴니다.
그런 속에서 겨우겨우 두 달만에 이것을 내여 노코보니 우리로도 또한 불만이 업지 안슴니다. 苦情을 말슴하면 핑계라 하시겟고 다만 아시는 분이나 우리의 뜻을 짐작하여 용서해 주시기나 바람니다. (謝告)＜119＞
