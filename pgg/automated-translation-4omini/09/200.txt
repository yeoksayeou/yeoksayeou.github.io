Apology  

It seems that when people from the provinces order this magazine and send their remittance, they mistakenly write 『別乾坤社』, which causes confusion at the Remittance and Deposit Section regarding how to handle it, resulting in some occurrences of funds being returned. In future orders, please ensure to use the Remittance Account Kyŏngsŏng 810 (경성 810番) under the 開闢社 and direct all communications regarding this publication to 『開闢社別乾坤部』 (Kaibyaku-sha Byeolgeon-gwon Department).<77>  

**Summary**  

This article addresses the confusion caused by incorrect remittance details provided by readers ordering the magazine from provincial areas, leading to funds being returned. It emphasizes the importance of using the correct account information during transactions to ensure smoother operations. Historians might find it interesting as it reflects the bureaucratic challenges during the colonial period, highlighting the interactions between domestic consumers and the administrative structures imposed by Japan.

미안한 말슴
지방에서 본지를 주문하시는 분이 송금하실 때 『別乾坤社』라고 모르고 쓰시기 때문에 振替貯金課에서 엇더케 취급할지 몰나서 도로 返金식힌 일이 잇섯다고 합니다. 이 다음 주문시에는 반드시 振替口座京城810番 開闢社로 하시고 본지에 대한  통신은 전부 『開闢社別乾坤部』로 하여 주십시오.＜77＞
