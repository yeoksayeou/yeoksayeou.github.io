Postcard Communication Welcome  
The "Postcard Communication Section" is open for contributions. The content may include personal impressions, satire, critiques, play reviews, news, and anything else, as previously mentioned, with absolute freedom (except postcards cannot be accepted).  

- Write "Postcard Communication" on the envelope.  
- Contributions must be submitted by the 10th of each month in order to be published in the following month. <140>  

**Summary**

This article invites readers to submit their thoughts and contributions via postcards, emphasizing freedom of expression in various forms such as personal impressions and critiques. This practice reflects a burgeoning participatory culture in early 20th century Korea, allowing individuals to voice their opinions despite the restrictive colonial context. Scholars might find this piece interesting as it signifies an early attempt to foster public discourse in a period characterized by colonial suppression.
