기사제목	자미잇는 傳說 (A Beautiful Legend)  
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)  

A beautiful legend, a splendid legend! A romantic and yet delightful legend! Singing "Ganggangsullae" as autumn comes, every year when autumn arrives, the colors of Jeolla and Gyeongsang provinces dance and sing in the earthy fragrance of Joseon, and the legend of "Fufu Rock" is scribed by the talented brush of Ba Ren Kim Dong-hwan, appearing on page 152 of this book. <145>  

**Summary**  

This article celebrates the legend of "Fufu Rock," intertwining it with the cultural practices of singing and dancing associated with the traditional Korean song "Ganggangsullae." The piece reflects the regional vibrancy of the Jeolla and Gyeongsang provinces, presenting folklore as a form of artistic expression and communal joy. From a historical perspective, the article is notable as it encapsulates elements of Korean identity during a time of colonial subjugation, highlighting the resilience of cultural traditions against external pressures.
