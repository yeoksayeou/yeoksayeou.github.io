잡지명	별건곤  
발행일	1927년 10월 01일  
기사제목	入選時調 (Selected Time Tune)  
필자	유문  
기사형태	시 (Poem)  

In the mountains beyond, between the pines,  

Who do you see gazing?  

In other places, the tears drawn like a painting  

Drench the collar.  

My friend, would you know  

This heart towards the clouds?  

評 - This writing also peeks beyond the mountains and between the pines, just as the clouds do. Perhaps you understand the heart towards those clouds. <136>  

**Summary**  

The poem reflects on longing and emotional connection, expressed through natural imagery and metaphor. The speaker conveys a sense of yearning for a friend, intertwining feelings of love with the beauty of nature. Historians might find significance in how such expressions relate to the broader emotional landscape of Koreans during colonial rule, revealing a blend of personal and collective sentiment that can underpin cultural identity amidst adversity.

入選時調, 내 벗아
						유문
뫼 넘어 솔 사이로
엿보느니 누구런고
외론 곳 그린 눈물
