기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)

7月號返送希望  
This magazine is currently out of print with each issue, and the July issue has also been reprinted and there is not a single copy left. Therefore, we are sending the orders from different regions with substitutes of other issues, as there are no records remaining at the head office. However, those who send us the July issue by November 10 will receive either the November issue or a subsequent issue, as desired, in return. ＜131＞

**Summary**

This article addresses the issue of the July edition of the magazine being out of print, emphasizing the difficulties faced by the publication in maintaining stock and fulfilling subscriber orders. The editor offers a temporary solution by substituting other issues for the missing one and encourages readers to return their copies by a specific date to receive future editions. This reflects the broader challenges of media production and distribution during the colonial period in Korea, providing insight into the logistical struggles of publishers under Japanese rule.
