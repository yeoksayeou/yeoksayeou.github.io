**Summary**  

This article discusses the results of a prize question contest held the previous month, highlighting the significant engagement from participants, with over eight thousand individuals submitting correct answers. The winners range from various cities, indicating a broad interest among the public. This reflects not only the popularity of such contests but also a community engaged in intellectual activities during a time when colonial pressures were intense. Historians might find interest in how events like these allowed for cultural participation and a semblance of entertainment in colonial Korea, providing insight into the social dynamics of the period.

**Glossary**  

懸賞問題	현상문제	A prize question; a question posed for a contest where correct answers may win prizes.  
人氣	인기	Popularity; the state of being liked or admired by many people.  
應募者	응모자	Participants in a contest or competition; those who submit answers for consideration.  
抽籖	추첨	Lottery; a method for selecting a winner from among participants.  
特賞	특상	A special prize; an award given to a top participant in a contest.  
普通賞	보통상	A regular prize; standard awards given to multiple participants in a contest.  
正解	정답	Correct answer; the solution that meets the criteria set forth in a question or problem.  
発表	발표	Announcement; a public declaration of results or findings.
