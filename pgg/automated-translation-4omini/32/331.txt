**Summary**  

This article announces the establishment of several branch offices in different locations, detailing their addresses and the names of the respective branch heads. The listed branches include Samsoo (삼수), Go-eup (고읍), Gwak-san (곽산), and Dongnae (동래). Additionally, there are congratulatory notes for businesses and a medical institution in the Chungnam region. This article reflects the organizational developments within the Korean media landscape during the colonial period, showcasing a growing effort to decentralize operations and connect more closely with local communities. Historians may find it interesting as it indicates a shift towards more localized and perhaps independent structures in response to the colonial administration's influence.
