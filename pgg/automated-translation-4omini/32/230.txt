別의 別乾坤  

Military Expansion  
According to a report from the U.S. Bureau of Information, the purchase rate of pistols in Washington D.C. has remarkably increased; that is, the number of pistols sold in 1918 was 94,011, but this year it has risen to 272,555, which is a surprising increase. The U.S. politicians should slow down on international disarmament and start implementing household disarmament first.

The Theory of Adaptation  
U.S. Labor Bureau industrial commissioner Lawrence Purrkins has proposed a famous theory for preventing war, stating, "Artificial silk is made from cotton gunpowder. Therefore, if many women wear artificial silk, there will be a shortage of cotton gunpowder, and thus, there will be no war. Thus, women should wear synthetic silk to establish world peace..."

The Price of Abuse  
In a modern Don Quixote incident, a French friend named Eric Legy was fined 50 francs and sentenced to six months in prison for cursing while riding a train.

War as the Norm  
Professor Fera, the Romanian representative in the League of Nations, announced a famous theory stating that "war is the normal condition of humankind, and peace is an anomaly. When we look back on world history, there have only been approximately 248 years of peace in the last 3,400 years..."

Ineffective Prohibition  
At Dartmouth College in the U.S., a survey of the senior class revealed the following results. 300 students married for money, 221 for love as a condition for marriage, 43 due to prohibition, and 1 reported liking alcohol.

The Lawsuit Against Mr. Wells  
Mr. H.G. Wells has been sued by the Canadian writer Florence Deeks for plagiarism regarding his famous international bestseller "The Outline of History." According to the Eastern statement, he had requested to publish the manuscript of his work "History" with the Macmillan Company in 1918, but it was returned in 1919 when Wells's "The Outline of History" was published, and the content had many identical phrases with her work.

A Ridiculous Damages Claim  
A schoolteacher in Indiana, Mr. Wilson, dreamed of being hit by a car and, upon waking, jumped out of bed and hit his face against a window, causing serious injury. Although this incident might have won him a lawsuit, Mr. Wilson immediately claimed damages against a car company because he had been startled by being hit by one of their cars the day before. If such an incident had not happened, he surmised he would not have dreamt that way and thus would not have incurred such injuries... The verdict resulted in a substantial amount of monetary compensation.

Number of Automobiles in the U.S.  
The annual production of automobiles in the United States is 6.5 million, employing 4.72 million workers in manufacturing.  

**Summary**  

The article discusses various contemporary issues related to military expansion, theories on preventing war, and societal attitudes towards marriage, censorship, and automobiles in the United States during the 1930s. It touches on the irony of increasing civilian armament despite calls for international disarmament and the challenges and absurdities of wartime and peacetime society. Historians might find it revealing to examine how the observations and cultural phenomena of that era mirror earlier fears of war and societal change during the colonial period in Korea, especially with regard to international relations and modernity.

軍備擴張
米國國內 情報局의 보고에 의하면 외싱톤시에서 피스톨의 구매율이 비상히 놉하졋다고, 즉 1918년에 피스톨세가 94,011弗이든 것이 금년에는 272,555弗이 되엿스니 놀라운 증가다. 米國위정자의 국제군축은 좀 두엇다가 하고 자네들의 가정군축부터 시행함이 若何.

변통업는 理論
米國勞働局의 공업위원 로렌스뻐-킨스양께서는 전쟁방지의 유명한 이론을 주창하여 가로되 『人造絹은 綿火藥의 원료로 만드는 것이다. 그럼으로 人造絹을 만히 입으면 綿火藥이 부족할터이니까 전쟁을 못하게 될 것이다. 그럼으로 해서 부인들은 인조견을 만히 입어 세계평화를 확립하라...』云

욕한마듸의 代償이
현대의 똥·키호테 뭇소리니군이 기차를 타고 지나가는 것을 에리-케·리기-라는 佛蘭西친구가 욕을 하다가 붓잽혀서 여섯달 징역에 50뿌랑의 벌금!

戰爭이 常態
國際聯盟의 루마니아 대표 페라교수 령감께서는 유명한 학설을 발표하되 『戰爭은 인류사상 보통상태요, 평화가 되리어 異狀이다. 우리가 세계역사를 도르켜 볼 때에 3,400년간 평화로운 시간은 겨우 248년밧게는 아니된다...고.

禁酒無效
米國 따-트마우드 대학에서는 最上級生에게 질문을 해서 어든 결과가 다음과 갓다.
300명은 돈 때문에 결혼을 햇고, 221명은 사랑을 결혼의 조건으로 햇고, 43명은 금주가요, 1  명은 음주가라고.

被訴된 월스氏
H·C웰스氏는 그의 유명한 세계적 저서 『世界文化史大系』로 인하야 카나다의 閨秀作家 푸로렌스·데-크스양에게 표절이라는 혐의로 피소되엿다고. 동양의 진술에 의하면 그의 저서 『史*』의 원고를 맥그리인 회사에 출판하도록 부탁한 것이 1918년인데 그것이 반환되기는 웰스씨의 世界文化史大系가 발행된 1919년으로 그 내용이 자기의 저작와 문구가지 동일한 점이 만타...는 것이다.

기맥힌 損害賠償
米國 인듸아나洲의 소학교 교원으로 잇는 웰손군은 자동차와 충돌되는 꿈을 구다가 놀나 깨여 침대에서 뛰여내리다가 유리창에 얼골을 부듸치고 중상을 당하엿다.
이약이가 이것만이면 승검겟지만 웰손군은 즉시 어느 자동차부를 걸어 손해배상을 청구하엿스니 그 이유는 그가 그 전날 거리에서 그 자동차부의 자동차에게 치여서 대단 놀난 일이 잇섯다. 그런데 그러한 일이 업섯스면 그러한 꿈을 꾸엇슬 리가 업고 따러서 그러케 중상을 당햇슬 리도 업다...는 것이다.
판결의 결과 아주 만흔 돈이 생겻다고.
