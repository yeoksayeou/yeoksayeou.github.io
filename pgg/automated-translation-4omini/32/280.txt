Modern - Fuku-Tei  
A herd of deer strolls on the asphalt.  
Modern - Schu-Modern - C-C, Itte, Mien, Mien, Mien.  
The colors of the century-end decadence light up and change. The five senses of modernists, paralyzed, desperately crave violent strong stimuli, concentrating on bizarre originality. Thus, captivated by the visual allure of this modernity, they become blind and overly sensitive idiots.  

Now, they have begun to take an interest in the magic of heavy makeup and flamboyant clothing, reaching even to their own bodies. How will their physique transform? Is this the sheer cliff of the avant-garde? If anyone doubts this old modernity, take a moment to glance at the fashion trends sweeping through Golden Country America this autumn. The trends that now dominate the world have long since moved from the originator, the casinos and malt houses, to Broadway in America.  

Just as heads have grown longer again, the wide thighs now risk showing dangerous areas above knee-length skirts, which are beginning to lengthen to lengths comparable to French brides or nuns’ dresses, while the waistline has gloriously risen from the hips to just below the breasts, completing the autumn fashion of these modern Yankee gals.  

Moreover, when it comes to evening wear and hats, they fit tightly against the body. Stockings are thick, but to make them appear less noticeable, they are so sheer that they seem to meld with bare legs. And those who dare to wear them without stockings have their thighs bare.  

Now, their cheeks are painted with large and round, deep red, while their lips are smeared in a bright, blood-red akin to that of a cannibal ready to feast on people. Their nails are sharpened, polished brightly, and they attempt to minimize their food intake as much as possible. Recently, a young woman committed suicide because she thought her body had gained weight, but these modern Yankee gals, though not reaching suicide, are nonetheless deeply troubled, avoiding local foods or starches. Instead, they recklessly smoke the drug of the weak, feeling compelled to do so. Thus, adorned only in calf-length stockings and clunky shoes, with waists swaying like slender willows, they walk briskly down tree-lined streets on asphalt like patients of cachexia, creating a poignant ambiance against a backdrop of autumn colors. This woman seeking only unparalleled beauty is said to be ardently studying the new book "Women's Beauty Secrets" by Helena Rubenstein as if it were a holy text. Ah! Will there come a day when she proclaims, while climbing a mountain, "I love you"?  

In the 1930s, sharp entertainment had reached its peak.  

American girls, tired of jazz dancing, speed driving, and intense cocktail parties, have discovered a striking new kind of entertainment.  
Parachuting! By saving money meant for a lavish dinner or silk stockings, they pay the fee of $25 (50 won) to jump from a plane with a parachute, which is the spirit of the 30s. Thus, on Saturdays or Sundays, the airports around New York are bustling with numerous women, lured by the thrill of parachuting. Letters praising parachuting arrive at airlines daily, with one such letter ending with the sentiment: "Even if it means saving on clothing and meals, I will gather my savings and come to your airfield to merge in the excitement of parachuting."  

In America, where no tempest blows, one wonders how these holy parachutes could drift down to Seoul and what kind of children could emerge from this chaos; perhaps we could catch a glimpse of their appearances!  

A black man with a peculiar sexual obsession  
- Lived in a brothel for seven years -  
Among the black apparitions with lips like a slit and eyes sparkling like a thin dawn, there is a unique friend. In tropical regions, most inhabitants mature early; even mothers who have given birth at ages as young as five or six are plentiful. The notion that some can achieve full physical development and marry by the age of three or four seems plausible, yet in their midst, we hear whispers of those whose sexual preference is neurotic and perverse, traitors to their own people.  

Among them, a 32-year-old man, Hamit, who is fading, recently caused a stir one night by acting suspiciously, leading the high-speed police to question him. Upon searching his belongings, what did they find? About twenty strips of a dark purple fabric worn by Japanese women (koshimaki, 腰巻), all piled up. As if that weren't enough, he confessed to making cushions from Japanese women's delightful koshimaki and even garments for his nightly rendezvous. Since coming to Japan seven years ago as a circus clown, he has immersed himself in these artfully colored and patterned fabrics of Japanese women, indulging in a near-obsessive collection to satisfy his perverse inclinations. By the night's end, our black friend Hamit sings softly in his jail cell: "I have a wish. No matter what, I can't sleep, so please return my treasures." Over those seven years, what thoughts and dreams did he harbor while entangled within the layers of koshimaki?  

Individuals Going to Animal Paradise  
In Osaka’s Nishi Ward, there is a man named Tsujiyoshinosuke, who has dedicated the last twenty years of his life to the care of animals. Because of this devotion, he has attained the honorary position of a director in the Osaka Animal Protection Society, despite being an ignorant merchant.  

He has posted signs around the streets, urging, "Let’s love the working cows and horses without a single complaint. Let them eat slowly." He diligently carries bags overflowing with premium sweet potatoes, soybeans, and other treats, ensuring that weary cattle and horses are well-fed. The cattle and horses seem almost heavenly, joyfully nudging him and eagerly devouring the food. Over twenty years, he has provided beans, ginseng leaves, and sweet potatoes from his own resources, feeding street cows and horses. There are times when the family's work is overwhelmed with the visiting cattle and horses that they come to aid him in serving. This past spring at Shitenno-ji temple, for the starving, neglected cattle and horses, he donated 300 sweet potatoes, 6,000 ginseng leaves, 70 sweet potatoes, and five shi of soybeans across the city. While mere humans may be on the brink of starvation, we could never comprehend the value of his compassion; surely, through this time of kindness, those cows and horses will find a glorious place in animal paradise. But who knows if Tsujiyoshinosuke harbors an inclination towards the unique scent of horse manure?  

**Summary**  

This article captures the essence of 1930s modernity and the fashion trends among women in the West while contrasting them with the societal issues reflected in the lives of individuals like Hamit and Tsujiyoshinosuke. The blend of frivolity in the fashion world with underlying themes of mental and ethical crisis illustrates the complexities of modern life during this period. Historians might find the juxtaposition of the pursuits for modern beauty against acts of altruism and moral struggle particularly revealing regarding the ambivalence of progress in colonial Korea as it grapples with modernization while structured by a European and American aesthetic.

이제야 그들은 농후한 화장과 화려한 의상이 가진 마법에도 흥미를 일코 나서 타고난 體軀에까지 손을 대이게 되엿다. 그들의 체형이 엇덧케 변해갈 것인지! 이것이 첨단의 千仞絶壁인가 부다. 만일 이 舊모던의 말을 의심적게 생각하는 분이 잇거던 黃金國 아메리카의 올가을 유행풍을 잠간만 살펴보라. 이제야 천하를 지배하는 유행을 그 本尊이든 가지노나몬말트 호리벨젤에서 아메리카의 뿌로-드웨이로 넘어 온지 오래어던.
잘넛든 머리가 다시 길어지듯이 넙적다리가 다소 위험한 곳까지 들어 나게 짤든 치마는 다시 불란서 신부나 수녀들 옷과 가치 졸도 할 만치 길어지고 뻴트(허리띄)는 엉둥이에서 번쩍 승격을 하야 젓가삼 우에까지 올너오게 된 것이 모던-양키-껄들의 가을 옷맵씨란다.
그리고 빗갈은 엇더냐하면 야회복과 갓고 허리에 와서는 몸에 착 들어붓게 되엿다. 양말은 빗갈만 진한 것을 쓰나 터문이 업시 엷어서 얼는 보기에는 맨다리와 가티 보인다. 그리고 양말업시 단이는 모꺼돌도 만타고.
또 그러고 두 볼에는 크고 둥글고 진하게 빨강이를 칠하고 입술에도 당장 사람이나 잡어 먹고 난 喰人鬼가티 샛빨갓케 칠하는 것이 첨단모꺼이요. 손톱도 뽀족하게 맵시를 내여 밝각케 물들이고 음식은 될 수 잇는대로 적게 먹는다. 최근 살이 나리지 안는다고 자살한 젊운 녀자가 몃사람 잇섯지만 이들 모던 양키-껄들도 자살까지는 이르지 안을 망정 최대의 고심을 하야 지방분이나 澱粉을 피한다. 그러고 瘐瘠之劑라 하야 담배를 함부로 피운다. 이리하야 초ㅅ대가튼 두다리에 양말을 신은 둥 만 둥 굽놉흔 구두를 답어 신고 細柳가튼 허리를 양낭거리면서 담배를 피여 물고 가로수 늘어선 아스팔트 우를 껑충껑충 걸어가는 瘐瘠症患者들의 데리케-트한 품이 단풍을 배경삼아 뛰여 나온 암사슴가튼 감개무량한 정취를 짜아낸다 한다. 이리하야 絶世美만을 구하는 그 여인네는 헤레나·루빈수타인의 新著 「婦人美容法」을 聖典과 가티 탐독한다고 한다. 嗚呼! 나종에는 야산에 올나 암사슴을 아고 「나는 당신을 사랑함니다」할 때가 닥처오려나부다.
