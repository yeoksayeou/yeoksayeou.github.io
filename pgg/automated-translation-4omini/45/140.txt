기사제목	나는 왜 이럿케 됏나, 그는 왜 중이 되엿나 (Why Did I Become This Way, and How Did He Become a Monk?)  
필자	韓龍雲 (Han Yong-un)  
기사형태	회고·수기 (Memoir)  

Why did Han Yong-un become a monk? Was it because he, unlike others, lost his parents and had nowhere to turn? Not at all. Was it because he left home due to family discord? Then why did he become a monk?  

He was originally born in Hongseong, Chungcheong Province, into a fairly well-off family for those times. He had a wife and children and had specialized in classical Chinese studies until the age of 27. His remarkable writing skills and rich knowledge of Confucianism were all learned during his time as a student. Then, at the age of 27, while studying the I Ching at a temple in Hongju, he came upon a Buddhist text titled "Seonchae" (禪妻). In the preface of the book, he read the phrase, "But see the finger pointing at the moon, and you do not see the moon itself" (但看標月之指,未見當天之月) and it awakened a great understanding in him. He was also moved by the infinite vows of Bodhisattva Samantabhadra from the Avatamsaka Sutra. With this realization, he threw away Confucian texts and resolved to take refuge in Buddhism.  

Without saying a word to his family, he traveled around famous mountains and eventually went to Seoraksan (雪嶽山) in Inje County, where he shaved his head and became a monk. If he had a wife and son, some might assume this was after the recent allowance for monks to marry. However, as mentioned, she was indeed his wife and child. At the time when he decided to leave home to become a monk, his son was still in the womb. For several years they did not meet, and it was only a year ago that he was able to reunite with them.  

  

**Summary**  

This memoir reflects on the life journey of Han Yong-un as he transitions from a scholar with a family to a Buddhist monk, emphasizing his internal struggles and transformative encounters with Buddhist texts. The anecdote reveals the personal complexities faced by individuals in colonial Korea, particularly regarding family obligations and spiritual calling. Historians might find his story indicative of the broader societal shifts during the period, including the tension between traditional Confucian values and emerging Buddhist ideologies.

나는 왜 이럿케 됏나, 그는 왜 중이 되엿나
						韓龍雲
한룡운씨는 왜 중이 되엿나? 씨는 남처럼 조실부모하야 의탁할 곳이 업서서 중이 되엿는가? 그도 아니다. 그러면 가정의 불화로 집을 떠나 중이 되엿는가? 그러면 왜 중이 되엿나?
씨는 원래 충청도 훙성(洪城)에서 그 시절로 상당한 가정에서 태여낫섯다. 그리하야 안해가 잇고 자식이 잇는 몸으로 27세 까지 한문 공부를 전공하엿섯다. 그의 웅건한 붓대라던지 풍부한 한학은 다 속게 유생시대에 배운 것이엿다. 그러다가 27세 되든 해에 홍주의 어떤 절(寺)에 가서 주역(周易)공부를 하는대 우연히 그 절에 잇는 불서(佛書)가운대 「선처」(禪妻)라는 책을 읽다가 그 서문 가운대 『但看標月之指,未見當天之月』(이 말의 뜻은 단지 달을 가르치는 손고락만 보고 하늘의 정말 달은 보지 못한다.)이란 글ㅅ구를 보고 크게 깨다른 바가 잇는데다가 또 화엄경행원품(華嚴經行願品)중에 보현보살의 행원 무궁한 것을 감탄하야 드듸여 유서(儒書)를 불 지르고 불법에 귀의 하기로 결심하얏다＜19＞. 그리하야 집 안 사람들에게는 아모 말도 업시 그야말노 운심수성(雲心水性)으로 가진 명산을 차자 다니다가 강원도 린제군 설악산 설악사(麟蹄郡 雪嶽山 雪嶽寺)에 가서 머리를 깍고 중이 되엿다. 씨에게 안해가 잇고 아들이 잇다면 모르는 사람은 요새 중에 장가가는 것을 허한 후의 일로 알겟지만은 기실은 우에 말한 것과 가치 그가 속게에 잇슬 때에 잇든 처자이다. 중이 되려 집을 떠낫슬 때는 아드님은 잉태중 이엿다. 여러 十년을 두고 만나지 못햇다가 년 전에야 겨우 상면하게 되엿다.＜20＞
