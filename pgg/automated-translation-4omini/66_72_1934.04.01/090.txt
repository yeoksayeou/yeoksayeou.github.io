기사형태	잡저 (Miscellaneous)  

Feed is highly beneficial for the body as it is similar to digested rice. It is particularly helpful for young people, even outside of holidays.

When visiting someone, it is considered etiquette to first inquire about the host's well-being. One should always introduce oneself before requesting a meeting.

During cleaning, it is advisable not to give water to pots. It is better to neglect it than to overwater it out of laziness.

When boiling something, removing the lid causes unnecessary waste of heat. It is better to slightly adjust the fire instead of taking off the lid.

When writing an important letter, it is common to write "親展" (for personal delivery), but "必親展" (must be personally delivered) is seen as overly obsessive and may raise suspicions from family members.

Chickens are always busy keeping watch over the house through the night, so one must not disturb them with noise when they are asleep.

Socks made of cotton are sturdier than those made of mixed fibers. Likewise, if nails are long, they tend to tear easily. Gloves also share this commonality.

Shelves in cabinets are easy to be damaged. The danger is greater the more hidden the entrance.

The reason water is beneficial during cleaning is because it contains fewer bacteria and fresh minerals, making it extremely refreshing. <17>

**Summary**

The article provides various pieces of practical advice on health, etiquette, home maintenance, and cleanliness. It reflects the societal norms and practices of the time, such as the emphasis on proper behavior when visiting others and detailed domestic advice. This piece can give historians insight into the daily life and cultural standards during the Japanese colonial period in Korea, highlighting how these factors influenced Korean domestic practices and social interactions.

식해는 밥이 소화된 것과 가튼고로 몸에 대단히 좃습니다. 특히 명절때가 아니라도 어린 사람에게 먹이면 리롭습니다.

누구를 방문하든지 주인의 잇고 업슴을 먼저 뭇는 것은 실레니 반듯이 자기가 누구임을 알린 후에 면회를 청함이 레의.

치운때에는 화분 가튼데 물을 전혀 주지 안는 것이 좃습니다. 외그러냐하면 게을러서 너머 지나치게 주는 해보다 낫기 때문에.

무엇을 끄릴 때 넘는다고 뚜게를 벗기는 것은 화력을 쓸데업시 낭비하는 것입니다. 뚜게보다 불을 조금 재로 덥흘 것.

중요한 편지를 쓸 때 「親展」이라고 쓰는 것이 보통이나 「必親展」은 너무 집념이 지나처서 집안사람에게 의심밧기 쉽다.

개란 놈은 항상 밤을 새워가며 집을 직히는 것이니 낫에 잠잘 때 결코 작난으로 놀내 깨게 하지 말어야 합니다.

양말은 순털로 된 것보다 교직이 더 튼튼합니다. 그리고 발톱이 길면 쉬상하는 것. 장갑도 역시 맛찬가지.

긔차속 선반은 도적맛기가 쉽습니다. 더구나 출입구가 갓차운 곳일수록 위험률은 더 만습니다.

옛날부터 치운때 물이 좃타는 리유는 세균이 적고 또 유효한 광물질이 석겨 극히 신선한 때문.<17>
