Mosaic  

Mr. Kim Se-yong, who was managing the Digital Joseon (수짜조선) along with his sister Lee Yu-seong, could not see him for quite some time. I was wondering whether he had gone to Shanghai or perhaps somewhere like Russia, but I heard that he has returned to his hometown of Daegu to run the postal service business with his father. It seems that he regretted not having actually calculated postal statistics when preparing Digital Joseon.

Mr. Baek Gi-man and the gold mining industry  

Mr. Baek Gi-man, who had published many poems in Baekjo Magazine (白潮雜誌) and other newspapers during the past Baekjo magazine era, has recently relocated to Daegu to work at the Choyang Community Center (朝陽會官) and has redirected his focus to the mining sector, achieving very good results. If things continue like this, he would be well qualified to enter the literary magazine market. If Mr. Baek is involved in gold mining, it is likely that Baek Gold Mine (백금광) will be more valuable than ordinary gold mines.

Mr. Kim Il-yup and Bongoong Temple  

Last year, Mr. Kim Il-yup went to Sudeoksa (수덕사) in the Yesan County of Chungcheongnam-do to study Zen meditation and then moved to Jikji Temple (직지사) in Gimcheon, Gyeongbuk. Recently, he has relocated again to Bongoong Temple (鳳凰寺) in Daegu. His name is Il-yup, so he is known for frequently moving around, but since the temple's name is Bongoong, one might wonder if he might inadvertently sing "Bongoong Song" (봉구황곡) during meditation. 

67 and Lee Kwang-jong  

Mr. Lee Kwang-jong (李光鐘), who taught many students for several decades at the Central High School (중앙고등보통학교), was affectionately called various nicknames such as 'the Great Teacher' and 'the Master of Wits' by many students. He resigned from the school this academic year. It is said that the reason he resigned is due to his age of 67 this year, coinciding precisely with the age at which his father retired. The term “retirement” at 70 seems to be a tradition his family follows. 

Mr. Lee Il's position as Anak County Governor  

In spring, Mr. Lee Il was so busy with mountain climbing that his family found it hard to see him except for meals and sleep. After marrying last year, he has been imprisoned in the prison of love and has been spending less time outside the school. Recently, he is busy taking care of his loving wife and looking after their daughter, so much so that he hardly has time even on pleasant days like these to engage in mountain climbing and has been mainly focused on his duties as the Anak County governor (안악군수).

  
**Summary**  

The article provides updates on several notable figures in the community, including their educational, vocational, and family developments. Notably, it discusses Kim Se-yong's return to Daegu to engage with postal services, Baek Gi-man's shift towards mining, and Lee Kwang-jong's retirement after decades of teaching. From a historical perspective, these stories reflect the shifting social and economic landscapes of Korea during the colonial period, highlighting the impact of personal choices within broader societal changes.

萬華鏡
金世鎔氏와 郵便所

이여성(李如星)씨와 남매인 동시 수짜조선(數字朝鮮)을 가티 경영하던 김세용씨는 한참동안 얼골을 볼 수가 업기에 그와 인연이 깁흔 상해나 혹은 로서아 가튼 곳으로 가지나 안엇나 하엿더니 전하는 말을 들은즉 그는 자긔의 고향인 대구에 가서 그의 부친과 가티 우편소 용업을 한다고 한다. 수짜조선을 편즙할 때에 우편소 통계수자를 실지로 계산 못해본 것이 큰 유감이엿던 모양이지.

白基萬氏의 金鑛業

전날 백조잡지(白潮雜誌)시대에 그 잡지와 그외 신문잡지에 만흔 시(詩)를 발표하던 백긔만씨는 그후 대구에 나려가서 조양회관(朝陽會官)의 일을 보고 잇더니 근자에는 다시 광업게로 방향을 전환하엿는데 성적이 매우 양호하야 그대로 잘만 나간다면 문예잡지사 시장자격은 넉넉히 될만 하다고 한다. 백씨가 금광을 한다면 백금광이닛가 보통 금광보다도 가치가 나갈 뜻.

金一葉氏와 鳳凰寺

김일엽씨는 작년에 충남 레산군 수덕사(忠南禮山郡修德寺)에 가서 참선공부를 하다가 경북 김천군 즉지사(慶北金泉郡直持寺)로 옴겨갓다더니 요새에는 또 대구 봉황사(鳳凰寺)로 전거하얏다. 그 이름이 일엽이니만치 날너다니기를 잘하지만은 본 절 이름이 봉황이니 참선중에 혹여 봉구황곡(鳳求凰曲)이나 또 부르지 안을가.

67과 李光鐘

중앙고등보통학교에서 수십년 동안 교편을 잡고 만흔 학생을 가르치고 또 만흔 학생에게 대곰보선생님이니 재담선생님이니 하며 여러 가지 별명을 드르며 만흔 경모를 밧던 이광종(李光鐘)씨는 이번 학긔에 그 학교를 사임하얏는대 그가 사임한 이유는 그의 년령이 금년 67인데 그의 부친이 퇴관(退官)하던 년령과 꼭가틈으로 특히 금년에 퇴직을 한 것이라고 한다. 70에 치사(致仕)란 말은 그의 부자분이 꼭 실행인 듯.

李一氏의 안악郡守

봄이면 등산운동 여름이면 해수욕과 수영, 겨울이면 스켓 스키 등 여러 가지 운동으로하야 그 집 사람들도 밥먹고 잠잘 시간 외에는 얼골을 볼 수 업다고 하던 이일씨는 작년에 신혼을 한 뒤로 사랑의 감옥에 가치여서 학교 이외에는 별로 출입이 적더니 요새에는 또 따님을 나아가지고 사랑하는 산모 구원하랴 따님 보와주랴 더구나 겨를이 업서서 요새와 가티 땃뜻한 날에도 그 조와하는 등산운동을 못하고 아주 안악군수 노류만 한다고.<33>
