(Night Cherry Blossom Points - National Edition)

Due to circumstances that could not be avoided, only illustrations could be included alongside the prose of Choi Yeong-su's (崔永秀) essay "Night Cherry Blossom Points" (夜櫻點景). The author sincerely apologizes to both the writers and the readers for this situation. <8>

  
**Summary**  

This article discusses the inclusion of illustrations in place of the prose from Choi Yeong-su's essay due to unavoidable circumstances. It highlights the significance of cherry blossoms as a cultural symbol during the colonial period, reflecting deeper themes in Korean colonial history. Historians may find it interesting how the choice of content was influenced by colonial censorship and the need to navigate artistic expression under Japanese rule.
