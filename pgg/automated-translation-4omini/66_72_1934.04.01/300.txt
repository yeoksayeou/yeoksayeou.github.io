Reader Club  

Dear teachers! How are you all doing? There is one thing I want to ask you. I won a prize in the contest twice last year, but the prizes have not come yet. What is the matter? Also, if I want to write a novel for newspapers or magazines, what are the regulations? Please let me know. (Chungang Jin Hong Bong-chi)  

That’s impossible. You should inquire about the contest; they said everything had been sent out immediately! As for your novel, there are no special regulations. Just submit it, and if it’s good, we will publish it. (Reporter)  

Dear teachers, how are you? Is there a magazine called 『樂園』 (Paradise) being published? If so, how many issues have come out, and what is the address of its publisher? (Tokyo Kanai San’in)  

I’ve never heard before that the magazine 『樂園』 is being published, according to what you just said. Did you perhaps misremember the title? (Reporter)  

Dear teachers! I hope you are all doing well. The books you send every month are received well, but I’m very sorry; the boys in my neighborhood come running when the books arrive and read the children's stories in the section. Is there any way to have fewer of these stories published? (Gyeongcheon Ro Seong-ho)  

Thank you. The children’s section is very popular among young readers everywhere. However, because there are few pages and the sections are limited, it is currently difficult to increase that section. (Reporter)  

The books you send are truly beautiful. They are truly a separate world. However, I request that you include more stories about our current world situation and children’s songs in the children’s section. (Uiju Kim Yeong-jun)  

There is no separate response to the requests made by the two individuals, but given the circumstance, I will try to fulfill your requests as best as I can. (Reporter)  

I congratulate the health of all the teachers and express my gratitude for how deeply you are invested in your work. Every time I receive the magazine, I feel increasingly thankful. Additionally, I would like to particularly thank teacher An Seok-yeong for the beautiful covers each month. Now I have a few inquiries; I hope you can respond despite your busy schedule. First, how many days or months after the announcement are the prizes sent? Second, are poems published in your magazine? Third, are general submissions (except for contests and club-related submissions) not published? (Wonsan Chun In)  

I will respond in order to your inquiries. 

First, prizes are sent within one month after the announcement. 

Second, why are they not published? If there are good poems, we will publish several each month. 

Third, we have not implemented that yet. However, we will start accepting submissions from next month. (Reporter)  

Dear teachers! How are you? I read in the newspaper that Park Geum-ri committed suicide by poisoning on March 2nd, but is it true that her love interest was Jeong Hong-gyeong? (Omitted)=Dongnae Il Sangsaeng=  

It was Jeong Hong-gyeong. As for your next question, I already answered that above. (Reporter)  

Dear teachers! I hope you are all doing well during this busy season. As I think about how hard you are working on this magazine, I feel deep regret in my heart. However, I have one question. Previously, when submitting contest entries, there were no such things as postal issues, but lately, the new postal worker told me that the postal fee needs to be 3 jeon instead of the 2 jeon as per regulations. Please inform me of the rules for sending contest entries. Also, I am truly grateful that there is a way for our readers to communicate through the reader club every month. Fellow readers! Through this valuable section, let us share our unfettered feelings with each other. (Seosan Choi Myeong-u)  

The postal regulations I mentioned previously through Byeolgeongon (별건곤) are absolutely correct. If there is such a contradictory treatment in the future, please directly complain to the postmaster; perhaps the new worker is just unfamiliar with the rules. (Reporter)  

Dear teachers! I have something to tell you. Some may know this, but for those who don't, I want to share a remedy for warts. Warts can be difficult to remove once they grow on the hands or feet, and they look very unsightly to others, making one hide their hands or feet away. However, there is a very simple and effective method to remove them. To illustrate just how easy it is, it’s easier than making bean paste from soybeans and even easier than boiling rice water for porridge. The value of this remedy could be worth 100 won, but I will teach it to you for free. Once you use this remedy and the wart miraculously goes away as I’ve said, please write to 『별건곤』 and tell them about it. Now, let me stop rambling and just mention the remedy. When a woman has just given birth, there is a lot of blood. Don’t you think that blood is a bit embarrassing and dirty (You say it’s shameful and dirty? Well, what is shameful or dirty when it comes to healing?). Apply that blood to the wart and leave it for 2 or 3 days without washing that area. It won’t hurt and you won’t even notice it, but the wart will dissolve. Finally, as we live in the countryside, please write something informative or beneficial about laws in a simple manner, even if it’s just a half-page one each month. (Pyeongwon Kim Ho-gyeon)  

Thank you for the remedy you shared. Your request is very good. However, if I were to just publish it vaguely, it might be difficult. If you have something specific you would like to know, please send that particular inquiry to our office. We will request an authoritative individual in the field to provide you with a kind and accurate answer. (Reporter) <63>  

**Summary**  

This article presents a compilation of inquiries and responses from readers of the magazine, highlighting their interactions regarding literary contributions, contests, and various concerns surrounding the publication. It reflects the readers' desires for more stories and poems, as well as their appreciation for the magazine's content. The article showcases the communal aspect of readers reaching out to each other and the magazine for guidance and connection. This exchange emphasizes the literary enthusiasm during the colonial period in Korea and the readers' quest for cultural engagement under Japanese rule, which is a critical area of study for historians examining the cultural history of Korea during its colonial era.

감사합니다. 무르신 아동란은 도처 어린 사람들에게 대인긔올시다. 그러나 원래 페지가 적은데다 달리 쪼개는 『란』이 만어서 지금 형편으로는 이란만 더 늘리기는 어렵습니다.(記者)

보내주시는 책은 참말 자미가 오글오글 합니다. 참말 별건곤입니다. 그런데 압흐로는 우리에게 직접 리로운 세상 형편 이야기와 아동란의 동요를 좀더 만히 실도록하여 주십시요.(義州 金英俊

우엣 분의 요구하신 바와 별다른 차이가 업서 따로히 할 대답은 업습니다만 압흐로 형편보아 요청대로 하여 드리지요.(記者)

여러 선생님들의 건강을 축하오며 편즙에 얼마나 골몰하십닛가? 귀지를 접할 때마다 층일층 감사를 늣기는 바입니다. 그리고 매달 아름다운 표지를 보여주시는 安夕影선생께 특히 감사합니다. 끗흐로 문의와 긔대하는 바 잇사오니 분망하시겟지만 대답해 주십시요. 첫재 상품은 발표후 몃칠 혹은 몃달만에 보내주십닛가? 둘재 귀지에는 詩는 실지 안습닛가? 셋재 일반의 투고는(懸賞, 俱樂部地方欄外에) 밧지 안습닛가?(元山 春人)

무르신 순서대로 대답해 드리겟습니다.
첫째, 상품은 발표후 1개월안에 보내드립니다.
둘째, 왜 실지 안켓습닛가? 조흔 詩만 잇스면 매달 몃편씩 실겟습니다. 셋째, 아즉까지 실행치 못햇습니다. 그러나 내달부터 모집해 보겟습니다.(記者)

丁洪敬씨입니다. 그리고 그 다음 문의하신 것은 이우에 대답하엿습니다.(記者)

긔자 여러 선생님! 봄날 고단하신데 별건곤 편즙하시기에 얼마나 뇌심을 하십닛가? 우리는 편안히 안저서 책을 바드며 이런 것을 생각할 때 저윽이 마음속으로 미안을 늣기는 바입니다. 그런데 한가지 문의할 것이 잇습니다.
『그전에는 현상답안을 보낼 때 아모런 사단이 업더니 요즘 신임하신 이곳 우편소 사무원님은 아시는게 만어서 그러함인지 2월호 현상답안을 전 규정대로 보내려한즉 2전 절수는 부족이니까 3전을 붓처야 된다고 하시니 이 현상답안 보내는데 대한 우편규정을 아르켜 주십시요.』
그리고 우리 독자들 사이에 따로 어떠한 통신련락을 할 무엇이 업더니 다달이 독자구락부를 통하야 통신신을 하게 되와 참으로 감사해 마지 안습니다. 독자 제씨여! 이 귀중한 란을 통하야 우리들의 숨김업는 소회를 서로 실파하고 지냅시다.(瑞山 崔明宇

무르신 우편규정은 본사에서 별건곤을 통하야 말슴드린 그 규정에 결코 틀림이 업습니다. 다시 그러한 모순된 처사가 잇스면 우편소장에게 직접 항의하십시요- 아마 새로 오신 분이되여서 잘 몰라 그러는 것이겟지요.(記者)

여러분 긔자 선생님과 애독자에 말슴드릴 것이 잇습니다. 혹 아시는 분도 게실지 모르나 모르시는 분을 위하야 사마귀약을 한가지 아르켜 드리겟습니다.
이놈이 한번 수족에나면 좀처럼해서 잘 떠러지지도 안치만 제일에 다른 사람이 보기에 대단 더럽게 보이고 또 자긔 스사로도 제면쩍어서 늘 수족을 감추어 가지고 단이게 됩니다. 그런데 이 사마귀를 떼는데 아조 쉬웁고 조흔 약이 잇습니다. 쉽다고 해도 이보다 더 쉬운 것은 업슬 것입니다. 콩으로 메주쑤기보다도 쉽고 물로 백비탕 끄리기보다도 쉽습니다. 이약의 가치로 말하면 돈 100원이나 밧고 아르켜 드려도 싼 것이지만 나는 무료로 이 비약을 가르켜 드겟습니다. 이약을 한 번 쓰시고 사마귀가 내말대로 떠러지거든 이 『別乾坤』에다 감쪽갓치 떠러젓다고 적어보내십시요. 이제 잔소리는 그만하고 약이름을 적겟습니다. 녀자가 해산을 하면 그 자리에 피를 만히 쏫지 안습닛가?
그 피(뭐라고요 창피하고 지저분하다고요 병낫게 하는데 창피가 뭐고 지저분이 다 뭡닛가)를 찍어 바르십시요. 그리고 2, 3일동안 그 자리를 씻지 말고 그냥 내버려 두십시요. 그리 아프지도 안코 업서지는줄 모르게 떠러저 바립니다- 끗흐로 우리는 농촌에 사는 사람들인만큼 다른 유익한 긔사도 좃치만 제말 法律에 대한 이야기를 아조 쉬웁게 써서 단 한 페지씩이라도 매달 내여주십시요.(平原 金虎健
