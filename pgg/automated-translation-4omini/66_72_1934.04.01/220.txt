잡지명	별건곤  
발행일	1934년 04월 01일  
기사제목	童謠 달밤 (Children's Song: Night on the Moon)  
필자	尹石重 譯 (Yun Seok-jung, translator)  
기사형태	시 (Poem)  

Knock, knock, knock  
Please open the door  
Who is it?  
It’s the tree leaf.  
Clatter, clatter  

Knock, knock, knock  
Please open the door  
Who is it?  
It’s the wind.  
Clatter, clatter  

Knock, knock, knock  
Please open the door  
Who is it?  
It’s the moon's shadow.  
Clatter, clatter  

**Summary**  

This poem reflects a child's innocent perspective as it explores elements of nature and night through rhythmic knocking at a door. The imagery of a tree leaf, wind, and the moon's shadow evokes a tranquil yet mysterious atmosphere reminiscent of a traditional children's song. Historians may find interest in the simplicity and playfulness of the language as a representation of Korean culture during the colonial period, contrasting with the complexities of societal issues at the time.
