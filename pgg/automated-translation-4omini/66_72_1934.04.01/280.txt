Correction Announcement  
Byeolgeongon Editorial Department  
In the last month's issue, it has been pointed out that in the first prize-winning song, Yulbatsu's "My Dear Boat," the phrase "the man who carries the barge" (놈) should be corrected to "the body" (몸), and that in the second line "Today, if I sleep here, where will I be tomorrow?" should be corrected to "Today, where will I sleep, and where will I be tomorrow?" We apologize for these mistakes to both the author and the readers.

  
  

**Summary**  

This correction announcement addresses errors in a previously published song in the magazine. The changes highlight the importance of accuracy in cultural representation and provide insight into the editorial practices of the time. Historians might find interest in how such publications served as a medium for shaping national identity and cultural expression during the colonial period, reflecting the tension between traditional and modern influences in Korean society.
