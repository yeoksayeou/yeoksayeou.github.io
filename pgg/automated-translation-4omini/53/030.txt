기사형태	만화 (Cartoon)  

Sstickgeol

Seeing a Western man and woman walking together, two female students commented,  

"Look, sister, isn’t that the Sstickgeol?"  

"Oh! That’s right, they just hang on each other’s arms." <5>  

**Summary**  

The article provides a brief and comical observation of a cultural encounter between Korean female students and a Western couple, highlighting the girls' curious reaction to what they perceive as an affectionate display. This glimpse into the attitudes towards Western customs during the Japanese colonial era showcases the complexities of cross-cultural interactions and reflects societal changes in Korea that were influenced by Westernization. Historians may find this depiction indicative of shifting social norms regarding relationships in a colonial context.

**Glossary**  

스띡껄		Sstickgeol		Social and cultural term used to signify an embrace or close physical proximity, likely derived from a Western influence.  
언니		unni		Older sister; term of endearment or respect used among females.  
녀학생		nyuhaksaeng		female student; term for a girl enrolled in educational institutions.
