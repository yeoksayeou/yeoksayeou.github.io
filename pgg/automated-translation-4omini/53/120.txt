기사제목	이 업는 老人 (The Toothless Old Man)  
저자	알 수 없음  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

A child: "Grandmother, do you have any teeth left?"  
Old man: "I don't have even one."  
Child: "If so, I'm relieved. Although you’re having difficulties, please go to the apple store and have fun. I’ll come back after playing."  
Old man: "..."<21>  

**Summary**  

The short dialogue presents an interaction between a child and an elderly man about his dental state. The child expresses concern but tries to reassure both the old man and himself by encouraging him to go out and enjoy life, despite the hardships he faces. This piece reflects the social dynamics of the time, indicating a child’s innocent understanding of aging and perhaps also hints at the isolation or neglect often faced by the elderly during the colonial period. Historians might find the portrayal of generational relationships and the elderly's status in society significant given the context of Korean colonial history.
