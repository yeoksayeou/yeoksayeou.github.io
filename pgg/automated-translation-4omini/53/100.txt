Unprecedented Great Tragedy

(Urgent Telegram) At the famine relief center, due to the malfunction of the chemical food distribution device, a person suffering from famine was filled with hydrogen and ascended to heaven as the distribution ended. <19>  

**Summary**

This article reports a bizarre and tragic incident at a famine relief center where a mechanical failure resulted in a person being mistakenly filled with hydrogen. It highlights the desperation faced by the impoverished during this time and the dangers of technological reliance. The use of humor in a dire situation reflects societal attitudes towards suffering under colonial rule, and historians may find it of interest as it illustrates the severity of famine conditions and the absurdities of life in 1930s Korea.
