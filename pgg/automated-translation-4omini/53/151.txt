기사형태	문예기타 (Literary Other)  

Money! They say even an infant can sense it! 

Chasing after a criminal in a dead-end alley, the policeman shouted, “Hey, hurry up! It’s money! Come out quickly, money, money, money!”

<29>  

**Summary**  

The article highlights the pervasive influence and significance of money in society, to the extent that it can even be recognized by infants. The vivid portrayal of a policeman chasing a criminal emphasizes the desperation and urgency associated with money. This reflects the societal dynamics during the colonial period in Korea, illustrating how the pursuit of wealth can lead to morally ambiguous situations. Historians might find the juxtaposition of innocence and criminality particularly revealing when examining economic conditions in colonial Korea.
