百貨店見學  
A group of students on a field trip are sightseeing at a department store. One student stops alone at the cafeteria and notes, "Ah - this is katsuretsu (cutlet), vegetable salad, rice curry. I should jot this down for a moment."

  
**Summary**  

The article describes a visit by students to a department store, capturing a moment where one student reflects on the various food items available, showcasing a glimpse into life during this era in colonial Korea. It emphasizes the spread of modern conveniences and culture under Japanese rule, which may intrigue historians studying the influence of colonialism on everyday life and consumer habits in Korea at that time.
