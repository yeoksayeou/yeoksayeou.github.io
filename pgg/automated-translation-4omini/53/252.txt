This is a long phrase to be arranged in order, so please send it back corrected to make sense.

Rules  
□ Write your answer simply on a postcard, including your address and full name.  
□ On the envelope, be sure to write "Kyungseong Gaebyeoksa Byeolgeonkons Prize Division." (It is permissible to include other manuscripts or respond to other prize answers.)  
□ On the envelope, write "Manuscript Enclosed," and if you cut a slit in the top of the envelope, you can affix a 2-jeon stamp and send it.  
□ Send within twenty days.  
□ If possible, sending multiple responses in one envelope is convenient for all and saves postage. This time, no reader's coupon is required.  

Please provide your address and full name.  

New Magazine Gift  
Anyone who simply writes their address and full name and notifies "Kyungseong Gaebyeoksa" will receive the new magazine called "Sin Gyeongje" for free, which increases knowledge and informs about world news. Please inform many friends of this, but notifications must be made with a return postcard.
