기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

가튼 病  
"I've been thinking a lot lately, and it seems to me that women are afflicted with a common malady when it comes to spending money."  

"Why wouldn't it be so? In our house, if my wife doesn't go out to shop even for a day, she calls for the doctor." <30>  

  
**Summary**  

The article discusses the notion that women have a peculiar tendency to spend money excessively, humorously portraying the domestic life of a certain household where a wife's lack of shopping triggers concern akin to illness. This reflects societal attitudes toward gender roles during the colonial period in Korea, and historians may find the commentary indicative of the era's gender norms and the intersection of consumer culture and masculinity.
