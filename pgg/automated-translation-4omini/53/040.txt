離婚式  
A wedding ceremony can take place, but a divorce ceremony cannot be overlooked. The divorce ceremony is a solemn occasion that officially cuts the ties of connection (恋縁) between the two individuals, and it is conducted with utmost seriousness. <7>  

**Summary**  

The article discusses the concept of a divorce ceremony, highlighting its significance in severing the marital bond between two individuals. It underscores the solemnity and seriousness that such an event entails, contrasting it with a wedding ceremony. This reflection on divorce during the early 1930s suggests cultural attitudes towards marriage and separation, potentially providing historians with insights into societal norms and changes in Korea under colonial rule and the evolving perspectives on relationships during this period.
