기사제목	廣告無用 (Advertising is Useless)  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

"Are you saying that your child has run away from home and you don’t know where he has gone? Then why don’t you place an advertisement in the newspaper today and see if you can find him?"

"But as you know, that child is only six years old, so he hasn’t even started school yet. How would it be of any use for him to be able to read the newspaper?"

  

**Summary**  

This brief dialogue reflects a parent's concern about a missing child and critiques the effectiveness of newspaper advertisements to help find missing children, especially when the child cannot read. The conversation highlights social issues of the time, such as the lack of education and the struggles of families during the colonial period in Korea. Historians might find it interesting as it showcases the intersection of family challenges and communication methods in early 20th century Korea under colonial rule.
