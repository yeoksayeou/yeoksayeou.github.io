봄새의 各色소리  
노구지의 飛行曲  
노구지 (雲雀) is a bird that ascends gradually to the sky following the earth's atmosphere, hence it is also called a geographic bird. This bird is akin to a noble aviator among humans. It flies not in a lateral manner but in a vertical one, and as it performs its flight, it sings a flight song. Truly, its song is magnificent.  
“Cheerful lark, cheerful lark, let us fly, cheerful lark, let us go. Whether the wind blows cheerfully, even if it rains cheerfully, I look at the vast blue sky and see the white sandy ground. Cheerful lark lays its eggs by the sandy shore and only watches with wonder, cheerful lark.”

杜鵑새의 望鄕歌  
The cuckoo (杜鵑) is associated with the legend of the late leader of Shu, representing a bird filled with longing. At any time, it thinks of its homeland and cries out “Returning to Shu, returning to Shu”; at other times it laments, “Restoration of the nation, restoration of the nation, restoring my nation as I go over this mountain, restoring my nation as I go over that mountain.” It cries for its restoration. <60> 

**Summary**

This article poetically describes various spring birds, focusing on the lark and the cuckoo. The lark symbolizes a joyful ascent into the sky, while the cuckoo mournfully laments for its homeland and the restoration of its nation. From a historical perspective, the imagery and emotions expressed may resonate deeply with Koreans during the colonial period, reflecting a sense of yearning for national identity and the natural beauty of their homeland, which was being overshadowed by foreign domination.

봄새의 各色소리
노구지의 飛行曲
노구지(雲雀)는 地氣를 따러서 점차 上空으로 올나가는 까닭에 從地理새라고도 한다. 그 새는 사람으로 치면 高貴 飛行家다. 飛行을 하되 橫斷飛行은 안이하고 縱斷飛行만 하며 飛行機 춤을 맛추워서 飛行曲을 불은다. 그 노래야말로 참으로 멋잇다.
『노구지구 졸졸 노구지구 졸졸 올나가자 졸졸 나러가자 졸졸 바람이 불어도 졸졸 비가 와도 졸졸 치여다 보니 萬里는 蒼空 나러다 보니 白沙地땅이라 노구지구 졸졸 모래툽에 알을 낫코 바라만 보니 조흘시고 노구지구 졸졸.』

杜鵑새의 望鄕歌
杜鵑새는 蜀主의 後身이란 전설이 잇느니만치 구슬푼 새다. 언제이나 故國을 생각하야 『歸蜀道 歸蜀道』하고 울며 또 엇던 때에는 나라를 回復하랴고 『復國 復國 復復國 이 山으로 가며 復國 저 山으로 가며 復國.』 復國하고 운다.＜60＞
