Article Title: 斷章 (Fragment)  
Author: 金華山 (Kim Hwa-san)  
Article Type: 시 (Poem)  

Fragment  

The white snow-covered pavement,  

All night long, I write and erase,  

Erasing and writing, the traces of my sadness.  

The wind whistles as it passes by,  

In the neighboring houses, many weep,  

Little girls cry XX XX and cry XX XX. (1930.1.16)<124>  

**Summary**  

This poem reflects on feelings of sadness and the passage of time through imagery of snow and the act of writing and erasing. The mention of children weeping adds a layer of community emotional experience. From a historical perspective, it is interesting as it captures the melancholy sentiments that might resonate with individuals during the colonial period in Korea, reflecting broader feelings of loss and yearning.
