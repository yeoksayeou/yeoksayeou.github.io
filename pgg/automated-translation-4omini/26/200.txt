Toothless Animals  

Animal Science Teacher: "Give me an example of a toothless animal among mammals."  
Young Child: "I know."  
Teacher: "Oh, good. What is it?"  
Young Child: "My grandmother." <78>  

**Summary**  

This article presents a humorous exchange between a teacher and a child regarding toothless animals, using a personal anecdote to illustrate the concept. The child responds that their grandmother is an example of a toothless animal, which adds a personal and light-hearted touch to the subject. This type of playful dialogue reflects the social norms of the time, potentially highlighting societal views on aging and wisdom in colonial Korea.
