기사제목	봄날의 가난한 牧童 (The Poor Shepherd of Spring Day)  
필자	로아  
기사형태	시 (Poem)  

The Poor Shepherd of Spring Day  

Today, instead of a bowl of rice,  
my little shepherd is loaded with spring on his back  
and has gone up the hill.

He walks carefully, stepping on the newly awakened grass,  
holding the blooming twigs from the water-soaked trees. 

As the sound of the flute from the distant hill rises,  
it shakes the spring sky,  
and in my little shepherd's heart, spring breathes.

With a small sickle in hand,  
he cuts the old grass,  
running through the dry branches on the left hill.

Throughout the long spring day, he skips lunch,  
filling his belly with the two-dog flower,  
wondering if he will come across any fruit,  
my little shepherd has touched flowers a few times.

When he wakes from his long nap,  
he hears the angry sighs of frustration,  
and he strikes and kicks the tree trunk of the young shoots dozens of times. 

As the mountain birds sing their songs in soft tones,  
my little shepherd cannot hear them.

On the way back, bending at the waist,  
he gives up the beautiful haunts of the mountain and the load,  
clenching his fists, he cries out—the poor shepherd of spring day! (1930.3.11)  

**Summary**  

This poignant poem depicts the life of a poor shepherd in spring, amid nature's beauty and simplicity. The shepherd’s struggles and experiences reflect the challenges faced by those living in poverty during a time of colonial rule. Historians may find interest in how this work illustrates the socio-economic conditions of rural Korea in the 1930s, highlighting both the innocence and hardships of peasant life under Japanese occupation.

봄날의 가난한 牧童
						로아
오늘도 밥동구리 대신

내 어린 牧童은 뒤ㅅ산에 올너갓네.＜126＞

선잠깬 어린풀 밟을가
조심조심 발을 옴겨드듸고
물오른 나무가지 휘여잡으면서. ... ...

뒤ㅅ좃차오는 둥무의 피리소래
봄하늘을 흔들때
내 어린牧童의 마음에도 봄은 숨이여드네.

손에든 작은낫 한자루
묵은 풀닙 헷치고
마른 사리나무차자 왼산을 다름질치면서. ... ...

긴긴 봄날에 점심도 굶고
두견화 따먹고 배를 채우다 행여나 열매매젓나
내 어린牧童은 두세번 복사꼿흘 만저보앗다네.

노곤해 들엇든잠 깨나고 보니
엉엉울고 십흔마음 분한설음이 복바처
아람도리 나무둥치를 열번스무번 낫으로쫏고 발로 차고. ...

낫닉은 산새는 날너와서
고흔 노래를 제마다 불너도
내 어린牧童의 귀에는 들니지를 안엇네.

저물어가는 길에 허리꼬부라저 돌아오다가
산 님자맛나 지게 뺏기고 뺨어더 맛고
두주먹쥐고 울고섯는 봄날의 가난한 牧童!(1930.3.11)＜127＞
