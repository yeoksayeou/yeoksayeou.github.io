기사형태	문예기타 (Literary Miscellaneous)  

Paper Premiere: The Mute Samryong (1)  
Original Work---Late Ra Dowang  
Screenplay Director---Chun Si  
Starring---Ra Un-gye, Ryu Sin-bong  

The mute Samryong! He was ignorant but earnest and sincere in facing the world, serving his master. However, his master considered him no better than livestock.  

The only son of Mr. Oh, the wealthy child was spoiled by his parents and known as a rascal among the older men in the village! He was also the subject of mockery among his friends! Yet, he found the mute to be strong and strictly authoritative.  

The wealthy child, intending to go gambling in the village, sneaked into his father's room to steal money and ended up in trouble.  

"They say hitting the mute will make him get up."  

The evil troublemakers mocked the wealthy child like this. The wealthy child, furious, beat the mute even more savagely than he would a horse or cow. However, from then on, the mischievous boys who had been teasing the wealthy child with the mute no longer found him funny.<125>  

**Summary**  

The article discusses a theatrical work centered around the character of a mute man named Samryong, who, despite his ignorance, is diligent and trustworthy, only to be mistreated by those around him, including his master. The narrative highlights themes of social class and childhood bullying, particularly how the wealthy child, who is both spoiled and ridiculed, reacts to the mute character's authority. This portrayal reflects the societal structures and hierarchies present in Korea during the colonial period, providing insight into interpersonal dynamics shaped by class and power, which may interest historians examining the complexities of colonial-era social relations.

잡지명	별건곤 제18호
발행일	1929년 01월 01일
기사제목	紙上封切 벙어리 三龍이(1), 羅雲奎 主演
기사형태	문예기타

紙上封切 벙어리 三龍이(1), 羅雲奎 主演
原作---故 羅稻香
脚色監督---春史
主演---羅雲奎, 柳新芳
벙어리 三龍이! 무식한 그는 부지런하고 진실한 것으로 세상을 대하며 主人을 섬겨왓다. 그러나 그의 主人은 그를 마소와 갓치 치부여 왓다.
吳生員의 외아들 貴童이는 父母에게는 응석으로만 자라낫고 洞里老人에게는 호래자식! 친구에게는 놀림감! 그러나 벙어리에게는 君主와 갓치 强하고 嚴하엿다. 貴童이는 동리로 노름을 하러가기 위하야 아버지의 방에 드러가 돈을 훔치려다가 야단을 맛나고 나온다.
『벙어리를 때리면 다리고 가겟다』
성악한 작란꾼들은 貴童이를 이러케 식키엿다. 철업는 貴童이는 그 벙어리를 소나 말보다도 더 몹시 때렷다. 그러나 근번부터 貴童이와 벙어리를 조롱하고 놀려주는 것으로 자미잇게 아는 악소년들이 貴童이와 갓치 갈 理가 업섯다.<125>
