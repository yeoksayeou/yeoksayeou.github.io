**Summary**  

The article presents reflections and thoughts from various authors in celebration of the New Year, featuring personal insights and perspectives on contemporary literary aspirations and figures that have influenced them. Among the highlights are strategies for revitalizing the literary community, which reflects a growing desire for cultural and intellectual engagement among writers during this time. Historians may find interest in how these reflections encapsulate the sentiments of Korean intellectuals under colonial rule, illustrating their endeavors to foster a collective cultural identity amidst oppressive circumstances.
