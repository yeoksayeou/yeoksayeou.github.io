Recruitment  

We welcome reader submissions...  

Depending on the content, it will be published in 『本誌』(this magazine), 『어린이』(Children), and 『學生』(Students) <146>  

**Summary**  

This brief article invites readers to submit their writings for publication in various sections of the magazine. It emphasizes the importance of reader contributions to fill the pages of the publication, indicating a collaborative relationship between the magazine and its audience. From the perspective of Korean colonial history, this engagement reflects a growing interest in literature and education among the populace during a time of cultural upheaval and identity formation under colonial rule. Historians might find it interesting as it showcases the early stages of popular literary participation and the importance of written expression in a colonial context.
