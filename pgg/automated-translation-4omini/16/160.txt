Trends! New Trends in the New Year! Desired Trends, Anticipated Trends  
The Trend Leading to the Great Bear Star

Ri Ryang

Europe, America, Asia— the whole world is shaking. Even more so, Joseon is caught up in this and is living a life of "shaking, shaking" without a root.  
I am currently writing this manuscript on a shaking train in Kaesong. The shaking train, my shaking body, and my shaking thoughts—everything is trembling.  
Someone once spoke highly of Joseon spirit, but now even that Joseon spirit has become a trending commodity, and things are shaking along on a different frequency. While "shaking" and "trending" have different meanings—  
But, Brother Cheong-o!  
You want me to write about trends?  
That's quite an overwhelming request. This is more like a large-scale trend. If the owner doesn't mind this, you might as well sing the Jongno March; advertisers would surely propose something interesting and charming. That is their personal flair. Also, it is a glorious lifestyle. However, I am neither a merchant nor an advertiser. I am not a department store clerk or a newspaper reporter. It's hard for me to speak about the true nature of trends.  
Nevertheless, the state of trends arises from individuals' discontent with current realities and the emergence of new desires, which represents a remarkable act of creation. It is also a surge of new life.  
So, Brother Cheong-o! What has been trending in Joseon now? What will become trendy in the future?  
Everything is fair game. Whether it’s love discussion meetings trending or organizing fashionably dressed groups of men and women, whether there are no goods but just big banners put up, or the so-called modern beauty—short-haired girls or short-haired women hanging out on the streets at night. There are great dining houses, and due to the high price of lumber, brick buildings are being built; rubber shoes are so trendy that they fill trash cans; there is no place without them. Everything is trending as it is meant to trend. We are wishing for this trend and that trend and what would be trending—these thoughts no longer come to us. This is because we understand well the economic relationships that are the foundation of modern life, the chaotic means of production, and their ideologies and tendencies, which inform our lifestyle and its forms. The lifestyle of the time is made of the things of the time, and thus it shakes and trends; it dances about amidst the chaotic mask dance, just like clouds drifting in a starry sky, heralding new currents of spirit, calling upon the greatness of labor, and aiming for the Great Bear Star.

Back to the Middle Fold

Ri Seung-man

=What would have been=  
In modern style, the shapes and colors are not coordinated at all, and what's peculiar is that modern women are broiled and boiled from that. It’s like a dough mass colored with flour, and wondering what design it is, not fitting to anything at all, whatever it is that they are flaunting as modern. If the makeup or clothing can be presented modestly, then we could call it a modern woman.  

=What seems probable=  
It has become the fashion for thin women now to wear shoes with high heels in good seasons. Since shoes originated from Westerners, they might need to follow that example, but seeing them wearing shoes that are possibly uncomfortable is quite disconcerting, like they are going downhill from a hillock. Though they are accustomed to it now, this new year we can expect the heels to get lower. When a new woman raises her leg to board a tram, her buttocks are visible, and with skirts that are high, they were supposed to be longer.  

=Once more=  
Although one might dress in an outer coat and wrap a scarf, not wearing it would make it seem difficult to show off; thus, nowadays, women typically wear this too. The skirt is short, and the scarf reaches down to the calf, leading one to wonder if it's not only unpleasantly long but that to avoid a filthy appearance it needs to be made shorter. It’s just an observation, really. Moreover, back to modern literature, regarding the young people stirring about, they’ve brought back the top hats with the middle crease.  

Tea and Handshakes

Ri Tae-jun

=What seems probable=  
Although a thatched cottage might hide behind the signboards, in Seoul, times being what they are, it presents a fair urban scene of the end of the century. Yet, it is somewhat awkward that so-called modern things amuse some who, while having tea, might suddenly throw on operatic voices in front of hot rice and pastry.  

It is indeed evident that even in the clock street where only businesses with no vitality exist, tea houses will soon emerge, and modern trends will naturally spread, generally to the extent of the tea enjoyment culture.  

=What should have been=  
Naturally, waiting for trends is not enough; only through persistently reaching out and showing respect can one fulfill one’s emotional wishes to find satisfaction. One can’t imagine that the new ladies will not be so unreservedly affectionate, yet it is rather accurate that the public generally regards these women as misfits. Long times ago, asking if a good woman is well, or simply addressing acquaintances has become distasteful, and for those when hats are removed and sitting becomes difficult, perhaps a handshake can serve as appropriate greeting. This awkwardness when greeting a close female friend who hasn’t been seen in a long time is something I assume everyone experiences. I wish that the handshake between men and women becomes a great trend and that it is universally accepted and commonplace.  

Strange Male Students and Good Folklore

Ri Seo-kyu

=What should have been=  
We young ones lack a sense of unity. Although we are friendly with one another, there are not many opportunities for us to grow closer. Thus I hope there will be some rule established or some format laid aside so that we can naturally meet each other in a friendly atmosphere.  

=What seems probable=  
What’s being called modern now means middle school students are dressing oddly and flamboyantly. I suspect this year, students will further indulge skirt lengths and hats and might even wear pants that are wider than 4 people’s shoulders combined.  

=Once more=  
Nowadays children in schools are pretty dull. They seem to have learned to sing nursery rhymes and catchy melodies; however, they could not understand deeper meanings. When walking down the streets, when I hear a flute playing "Oh, it's so hot," it brings about a feeling of loneliness in me. Not being able to hear those songs makes one feel even lonelier. The old songs are being reshuffled, perhaps we can create new tunes from them that the children can sing.  

Tent Life and Military Style Physical Training

Hyeon Dong-wan

=What should have been=  
Regarding sports, we ought to engage more in baseball, which costs a little in our community’s economy. While promoting baseball at struggling high schools is good for the students, due to economic difficulties, we should instead choose soccer, volleyball, or basketball, which can be done at relatively low costs and allow for a more inclusive participation from all.  

=What seems probable=  
While tent life has been realized abroad from early times, it’s still regarded merely as a hobby here. In winter, due to weather concerns, it cannot truly be conducted, but in the summer, it is considered very beneficial. It’s worth expecting in terms of economy, health, hygiene, or hobbies. It would be a great blessing for those without housing. For various reasons, I predict that tent life will not just trend but will become concrete and practical.  

=Once more=  
Whether it be cultural education or something else, we have transitioned to a cultural-style of teaching, even for military-style physical training. However, I believe that physical education should still be taught in the traditional military style. Back in our school days, students had robust physiques, yet these days, students’ physical fitness seems less appealing. Currently, it seems like physical activity is only a modest passing of time! We should still demand that physical training be strict and create strong and healthy physiques as befitting a student.  

Traditional Festivals and Korean Traditional Festivals

Kim Dong-jin

=What should have been=  
In our society, when young people want to gather or communicate through meetings or for entertainment, there are many times where we must resort to dining establishments, since there are few other places. If one wishes to maintain a respectable appearance, they ought to go to a restaurant as there are rarely other options available, which presents a significant issue given our impoverished economic situation. Clubs may exist, but they are mainly for specific people and are not of general use, so they are not effective. It would be best if there were places where everyone can enjoy themselves and talk freely while creating a gentlemanly atmosphere.  

=What seems probable=  
In the past, if people of higher societal status associated with a woman, it was often regarded as being informal and not leading to a proper public marriage. Such occurrences, in the past, would be considered scandalous; however, nowadays times have changed, and people may think differently. Even following the example of Western individuals, those who associate with women of ill repute or actresses—those kinds of relationships are no longer condemned. I saw this in the case of a friend’s marriage recently. There seems to be a burgeoning trend where such partnerships might become trending and where church halls may serve as wedding venues.  

=Once more=  
Yet, these days, they sing and dance in outrageous fashion, with Western dances and like, and only pretend to dress nicely while seeking attention. Obviously, these modern performers are what the new educated youth (modern women) are asking for, but if they indulge in superficial modernity, there’s a clear concern about Korean tradition fading away.  

Korea's Own Style

Ryu Yeong-gi

=What should have been=  
We should research and embrace Korean identity and practice in both spirit and material. When people studying in Western countries are seen, they often claim that if they do not read literature or philosophy in the language of that country, it seems shameful. However, those who return from Japan do not regard their own literature as valuable, apparently considering it childish and insignificant. They seem to view foreign cultures with admiration, almost as if they were idolized.  

Of course, if one is to deeply study a specific culture, familiarity with its language is crucial. Nonetheless, it feels that people today often neglect the importance of their own literature and history, and they become enamored with foreign things while being ignorant of their own. It would be better if we could study and appreciate our own culture, elevating our spirits so that we might also present and take pride in our identity before foreigners. Furthermore, in material aspects, there is a need to cherish our own things.  

Although saying this while wearing Western clothes feels somewhat odd, I think that wearing Western garments only for the sake of appearance or to show off is not the way forward. Even as women, if they have enough of their own good materials to dress well, there’s no need to be excessively reliant on expensive foreign fabrics. These trends suggest an urgent need for us to reevaluate and return to cherishing our own identities in both material and spiritual lives.  

=What seems probable=  
In the United States, there might have been a trend where women cut their hair short, but I believe this age has passed. Meanwhile, in China, the government has encouraged various hairstyles.  

Depending on perception, it could be necessary from one angle, but in any case, the idea that short hair means equality between genders appears misleading. I believe that men and women possess essential differences, and they should each strive to be their best. For women, the aspect of beauty is inherent to identity, and hair plays a vital role; thus, when I see a woman with short hair, it does influence how I view femininity. This short hair trend may very well appear in the new year, but I hope it doesn’t.  

=Once more=  
In times past, the passion and fervor among students were so potent, that even female students threw off their high-heeled shoes, abandoning their Western-style dresses for traditional Korean attire. Sadly, nowadays one cannot even listen to this desire, and they have returned to high heels once again. My hope is that we can make a concerted effort to revive traditional Korean products and love our own culture deeply in the new year.

  
 
**Summary**  

The article reflects on various aspects of trends present and anticipated in Joseon society in 1929, showcasing the intertwining of modern styles with traditional values amidst colonial pressures. It reveals a complex negotiation of identity where elements of Western influence clash with indigenous desires, highlighting how clothing, interactions, and lifestyles are becoming markers of modernity while also indicating a deeper longing for cultural authenticity. Historians might find the commentary on shifting gender norms, particularly in the context of increasing Western influence, especially revealing as it illustrates societal transformations at a time of colonial subjugation.

流行! 新年 새流行! 希望하는 流行·豫想하는 流行
大熊星으로 가는 流行

李亮

유롭, 亞美利加, 亞細亞-全世界는 흔들리고 잇소. 더욱 朝鮮은 그름에 끼워서 『흔들흔들』뿌리가 업는 生活이요.
나는 只今 이 原稿를 開城行 列車에서 쓰오. 흔들리는 汽車, 흔들리는 몸, 또 흔들거리는 생각-모든 것이 흔들흔들 뼈가 업구려.
누구 한분은 퍽도 朝鮮心을 말슴하더니 지금은 그 朝鮮心도 한 流行인 商品이 되고 또 흔들거리는 물건이 되야 그 生活과 그 마음이 딴거리에서 흔들거리고 잇다하오. 흔들거린다는 말고, 流行된다는 말이 한가지 의유가 아니지마는-
그런데 靑吾兄!
流行에 대한 이약이를 쓰라고요?
원 當치 안으신 말슴이요. 이것은 오히려 큰 떼파-트 主人의게 무르시던지 그렂치 안으시면 종로행진곡을 부르고 잇는 모더-ㄴ -廣告業者가 滋味잇고도 魅力잇는 新案을 提起할 것임니다. 그것은 그사람들의게 한 個性이요. 또 得意한 生活임니다. 그런데 나는 商人도 아니요. 廣告業者도 아니요. 百貨店店員도 아니요. 또 新聞記者나 무슨 스트리트 뽀이가 안인이상, 참말 流行性의 眞實을 말슴하기에 거북합니다.
그러나 流行이라는 그 世態가 엇던 사람의 시작으로 또 엇던 사람들의 追隨를 바더오던지 其 動機와 動機만은 다 자기가 가지고 잇는 現實不滿에서 새로운 慾求의 發作이니, 이것은 훌융한 創作이요. 또 新生의 衝動이외다.
그런데 靑吾兄! 무엇이 朝鮮에서 流行되엿느냐고 只今 流行되느냐고<100> 또 將來 엇던 것이 流行되엿스면 좃켓느냐고요-.
무엇이던지 좃슴니다. 戀愛座談會가 流行되여도 좃코 性에 주린 男女便衣隊를 組織하여도 좃코, 商品은 업서도 看板만 크게 달어노아도 좃코, 또 소위 現代美라는 斷髮娘이나 斷髮婦人이 밤낫 길거리에서 헤고 단이엳 좃슴니다. 큰 料理집 代身에 簡易食堂이 생기고 材木이 高價가 되야 煉瓦建物이 생기고 고무신은 유행되다 못해 쓰레기통마다,  업는 곳이 업슴니다. 무엇이던지 流行됨대로 流行되는 것이 좃슴니다. 우리는 이 流行이 납부고 저 流行이 좃코 또 엇던 流行을 바라고 -이러한 생각을 아니 가지게 됨니다. 그것은 現代生活의 基礎인 經濟關係와 또 無秩序한 生産手段과 其 思潮와 傾向에 따르는 其 生活形態와 形式을 잘 아는 까닭이외다. 其 時代의 生活은 其 時代의 사물이니까 흔들리우고 유행되고, 까불고-이러케 끗업는 탈춤속과 星雲갓치 도라가는 過卷 가운데서 새로운 精神의 流行을 볼것이니 그것은 좌편으로 左便으로 勞動力의 偉大를 부르며 大熊星을 갈 것임니다.

다시 中折式으로

李承萬

=되엿스면=
모더-ㄴ式이면 모양도 色彩도 相關업시 덥허놋코 이상하기만 하면 주서바르고 주서입는 것이 요사이 新女子이다. 밀가루반죽에다 色彩칠한 것 갓쳐 그게 무슨 모양인지 어울이지도 안코 맛지도 안은 것을 모더-ㄴ이라고 휘두르고 단이는 꼴은 볼 수가 없다. 化粧이나 衣服빗이나 모양이나 수수하게 차려서 어되까지 됴선의 新女子라고 볼 수 잇게 하엿스면 좃켓다.
=될 듯 한 것=
됴선 마른 신만 신고 지낼 색시들이 時절이 조와서 구두를 신게 되엿는대, 구두는 원래가 西洋사람의 것이니ㅅ가 그 번을 따라야 하겟스나 짝없이 굽을 놉히 맨드러 뼘가옷이나 되는 것을 신고 단이여 보기에도 퍽 거북해 보이고 똑 언덕빗탈길을 나려가는 것 갓드니 요새는 퍽 익숙해서 다름질도 하게 되니ㅅ가 인제는 굽이 차차 야터저서 새해에는 아마 퍽 얏허질 것 갓고 電車에 탈때한다리를 들면 엉덩이까지 보이고 무릅까지 내여 놋튼 新女子의 스카-트가 좀 기러질 것 갓슴니다.
=다시 되엿스면=
치우니ㅅ가 外套입고 목도리를 두루고 단이는 것이지만 목도리를 걸지 안코는 행세하기 어려웁다는 듯이 일제하게도 요새 女子들은 안이걸고 단이는 이가 업는 중 좀 심한 것은 新女子의 목도리이다. 치마는 짧고 목도리는 신등까지 나려오니<101> 보기도 실치만 길다고 더 더러운 것이 아닌 바에야 실갑 더 드리고 보기실케 작고 기러만 가니 질질 땅에다 끄을리도록 길어저야만 고만 둘야른지 이만콤 길엇슬 때 다시 오구라젓스면.
또 新文學靑年이라고 휘젓고 단이는 이의 帽子꼭지를 동굴게 접어쓰고 다니는 것을 다시 中折式으로 햇스면-.

喫茶와 握手

李泰俊

=될 듯한 流行=
아즉도 초가집웅은 看板뒤에 숨어잇슬지언정 서울에도 時代는 時代라 제법 世紀末의 都市風景을 갓초려하는 것 갓다. 그러나 至尊이 소위 모껄이니 모뽀이니 하는 분들로 구쓰를 끄르고 湯飯에 오폐라빡쓰를 시루꼬 집테불갓흔데 버서 놋는 것은 암만하여도 初忌飜譯劇을 구경하는 것과 갓치 어색한 감이 업지 안은 것이다.
이를 보아 아모리 無神經한 商人들만 사는 鐘路라 하드라도 不遠하야 喫茶店 골목이 생길 것과 판백이 모더-ㄴ들은 勿論이려니와 一般的으로도 喫茶道樂이 流行되리라고 생각한다.
=되엿스면 하는 流行=
勿論 유행을 기다리지 안코도 선선히 손을 내여 밀어 情分이 깁흔 그만큼 滿足한 예를 보히는 新女性이 아조 업슬 것은 아니나 아직도 一般化되지 안을분더러 이러한 女性을 特히 말괄냥이視하는 것이 事實이다.
오래간만에 맛난 누이들에게 혹은 그러한 女子친구들에게 너 잘잇섯니 아무개 잘잇섯소 하기에는 情에 막히며 너무나 무뚝뚝한 일이고, 그렂타고 帽子를 벗고 허리를 굽힐 수도 업는 경우에는 말인사와 함끠 握手가 조흘 것 갓다. 더욱 오래간만에 맛난 女子親구에게 情分대로 인사하지 못하는 딱한 경우는 나뿐이 아니리라 생각한다. 어서 男女間에 握手禮가 大流行을 지어 一般化하고 平凡視되엿스면 한다.

괴상한 남학생과 조흔 동요

李瑞求

-되엿스면 하는 流行-
우리 靑年間에는 너모도 우의적 기분이 업다. 아모리 서로 친하고 십허도 친해질 機會가 업는 것이 유감이다. 그럼으로 무슨 規則을 定한다든지 무슨 形式을 버서나서 그저 서로서로 親友的 氣分으로<102> 會合할 만한 社交機關이 잇섯스면 한다.
=될 듯한 流行=
所謂 모더-ㄴ뽀이니 모더-ㄴ껄이니 하야 괴상야릇하게 맵시를 타리고 단이는 것을 요새 中學生들이 이번을 뜨나 새해에는 中學生들의 모양내는 것이 더욱 심아여 질 듯하다. 가령 머리를 깍는 식이라든지 모자를 쓰는 식이라든지 바지통이 서너사람 목이나 넓다든지 하는 것 이외에 더욱 이상야릇하게 모양을 낼 듯하다.
=다시 되엿스면=
