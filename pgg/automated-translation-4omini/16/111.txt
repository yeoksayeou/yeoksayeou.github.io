秘密命令歡迎  
To all readers who support this magazine, the editorial team wishes to inquire. Our secret investigation team is always ready to dispatch and is waiting for your commands. If you have any suspicions about any particular item or location, such as where there are disturbances or strange occurrences, please inform us. If you want us to investigate a certain place, or if there are peculiar characters or suspicious incidents in any location, notify us, and we will promptly launch a secret investigation and report back in the magazine.

Even if you disclose secrets in this magazine, our company can only process your request if you provide the location, street address, and the authors' names and ages. Additionally, please share your own address so we can contact you regarding the investigation. <68>  

**Summary**

This article is an invitation for readers to report suspicious activities or strange phenomena, prompting the publication of secret investigations in response to public inquiries. The piece showcases the magazine's engagement with its readership and its role in uncovering hidden truths within society. This reflects the atmosphere of uncertainty and intrigue in colonial Korea, where citizens might have been concerned about abnormal events or figures amidst the sociopolitical changes of the time.

秘密命令歡迎
本誌를 닑으시는 讀者가 모다 編輯願問이되여주십시오 本誌의 秘密調査班記者가 恒常出動準備를하고 讀者諸氏의 命令을 기다리고잇슴니다 아모곳 아모집에 독갑이 작란이잇스니 調査하라던지 아모곳 아모집에 怪異한 魔窟이잇스니 그 內面을 調査하라던지 어느곳 어느곳에 怪常한 人物이잇거나 疑心나는 事件이잇스나 調査하라고 딘즛이 『別乾坤調査部』로 通知해주시면 卽刻出動 秘密調査하야 誌面으로 報告하겟슴니다.
誌上에는 秘密을 직히드라도 本社아지는 그 場所番地 人物이면 姓名年齡을 明記하시고 投書하시는이의 住所도 알려주서야 調査上連絡할수잇겟슴니다.＜68＞
