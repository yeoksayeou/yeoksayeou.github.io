기사제목	一無名女性의 名士罵倒錄 (A Record of the Defamation of Notable Figures by an Unknown Woman)  
필자	P. M. S  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

I am an unnamed woman.

I have not followed a formal path and have not practiced diligently; I have only managed to escape illiteracy by learning a little from newspapers, magazines, and pamphlets. 

However, I have a keen understanding of the realities in various sectors of society, and my childhood suffering has made me adept at discerning the nuances of human feelings. 

Having endured hardships as a child and as one connected to the women’s movement, I am confident in my ability to make judgments about significant contradictions in society. 

Initially, I only made a superficial contact with societal realities out of necessity for the movement. 

However, during that time, I came to understand the hidden truths lurking underneath. Realizing this, I actively sought to delve deeper into those matters. 

Among them, I particularly focused on the behavior of individuals that society deems as notable figures towards women, and I scrutinized, observed, and sought to understand their actions. 

I found countless instances of wrongdoing. 

Moreover, I see that numerous instances continue to form to this day. 

With each discovery, one sense of righteous indignation arises; finding two brings two such feelings—thus, a stream of molten righteous anger fills my heart, swirling chaotically.

Yet, I have no means to express this. 

Public speaking or broadcasting on the radio could expose their ugly truths, and I yearn for the freedom to unleash my thoughts, but who will grant me the ability to voice such claims? 

With no other choice, I have opted for this form of submission. Of course, whether this will even see the light of day is questionable, and even if it does, it is unlikely to receive the handling it deserves. Although that is disheartening, even this will at least serve to relieve half of my grievances.

To my earnest and bright younger sisters, heed this crime—I wonder if it is not an offense worth beheading in order to apologize to the world?

Our Korea is currently in a transitional period towards the liberation of women. 

As such, ordinary young or adolescent women do not yet possess a clear sense of reason and critical evaluation in the real world. 

They can be easily misled by pretty face cards or colored candies and are like children with tender hearts and weak wills. 

They tend to be amazed unconditionally by any action or character, failing to distinguish right from wrong. Rather than viewing realities through a lens of consequences and benefits, they first allow their undeveloped feelings to fill them, exuberantly giving praise and ultimately placing themselves in the claws of a poisonous demon. 

On one hand, a vile predator hides its ferocious claws beneath fine feathers, and as one is embraced, it will indiscriminately grasp and claw like a beast. 

General society, upon seeing such predators regards their merits as outweighing their crimes, thus turning a blind eye and tacitly approving of their behavior.

Then, whom might these predators be? One should naturally ask. 

Shall I name them here? Is it truly necessary to do so? 

Even if I were to name them, their power is such—that is, I must indeed name them. 

I have more than ample evidence in hand to confidently state their names clearly. 

However, if I were to declare it on this paper, the world would turn a blind eye. 

The magazine establishment itself belongs to these same ilk. They would never harm their own kind. 

Moreover, whether this submission will even be printed on their pages is dubious, let alone that their names might surface. 

However, I believe disclosing their professional affiliations—their occupations—might be permissible.

I will name their professions without revealing individual names, as this will at least ensure the guilty parties cannot rest easy and will feel uneasy about their reputation in the darkness.

Yet, eternal peace shall not be theirs. Whenever the opportunity arises, I will again pick up my pen and add ink to their names.

Now—

Among those at the forefront of Korean newspapers and magazines, and the flowering reporters, earnest public figures, eloquent speakers, overseas agents, popular sportsmen, musicians, and writers... 

Of these, if we exclude about 30%, the remaining 70% have seduced the daughters of others, from three to four individuals. 

It may be somewhat harsh to call them "those," but as my anger swells, it naturally leads to that expression. 

Take heed: 70% of them have indeed seduced the daughters of more than three or four other men. If there are those who disagree, stretch your neck and listen. 

To the hypocrites, I shall first strike this pen against your necks.

Those so-called "notable figures" are regarded by society as the heroes leading the charge and revered as fathers and brothers, while they use vile means to lure earnest, naive young women, greedily sucking their essence and spitting out the remains in the streets like phlegm.

Certainly, it is not my intent to solely blame men for these faults.

When meetings organized by newspapers or magazines occur, even in cases of directors or heads of departments leading the discussion, a single polished article or mere attendance at foreign events including purchasing Japanese books, or a home run hit—their reputation is easily elevated, with praise flooding in soon after that.

The claim that praise floods in might exaggerate it a bit; however, it is undeniable that industrious women often act this way, and such behavior is not without its inherent absurdity.

However, it is the duty of society to correctly guide and rectify this disarray, yet instead, they brand it as a profitable venture, freely wielding their poisonous hands.

You, whom society deems "notable figures," with fine weather, how can you not fear retribution?

Yet that's not all, as women are often incapable of tackling this issue alone; recognizing their weakness, you advance even further, turning this opportunity into a trap for women.

This is your miracle—how each of you is wholly devoted to these bodies.

They indeed act proactively, engaging in clandestine activities. 

They fabricate pretexts to gather women for opportunities, or they establish personal connections for individual encounters. 

Among them, they mark women of interest for their own desires. 

They do not care what kind of women they pursue, be they young maidens in school, working women, or common wives... they do not discriminate at all.

Consequently, the so-called "notable figures," having their mistresses, openly conduct marriages with the daughters of others in front of crowds. 

Within the same group, a few will take turns in abusing the same woman, trading off parental duties for the offspring. 

Upon seeing women in revealing clothing on the street, the men are assured that their expectations shall not be challenged as they hear of a prominent lady’s divorce, leading them to feel "calm."

While this description may sound harsh, they are nothing but men without direction to their lives.

General society condones their misdeeds. 

The reason is that the sins they commit are sufficiently offset by their merits—some maintain that their achievements are so great they compensate for their crimes, while others view them simply as contradictions of a transitional period.

Indeed, this is a nonsensical attitude.

To begin with, what claims do they have to their achievements?

In reality, they trivialize as they advertise their work, claiming to be involved in public profit for the community. 

Under the pretense of nationalism, they hijack the collective power of the crowd still blinded by their interests and intend to establish a kingdom of liberty for selfish agendas. 

They looted farmers' properties and, after returning from overseas, boastfully parade about, covering up their foul deeds while revealing hollow truths.

Underneath their deluded pursuits of glory, in grand stands, claiming empty platitudes as speakers, they earnestly articulate utter selfishness while openly disregarding the children of others.

The achievements of athletes who swim against the current of time!

That of a musician who can hardly earn a living in life!

The artist or literary figure who takes on a third wife amongst the boasts of glory!

Once you grasp the root of it, they merely resemble the colonial government with their refined manners and sinister motives lurking beneath the surface.

These perpetrators lay claim to such achievements, yet society turns a blind eye.

The optimistic wait only for the inevitable, dismissing contradictions as transitional.

Misinterpretations abound, insistent on declaring inevitability.

To conclude, I will now recount certain true occurrences.

Last year, I had a girlfriend who graduated from school and was in search of employment. 

At 20 years old, she was utterly naive, lacking the discernment of society. 

Without proper supervision from her family, she lived in Seoul boarding houses while engaging in find work.

While I was aware of the dangers akin to walking on thin ice, I helped her network to find opportunities.

(This passage has been omitted for brevity, spanning twenty lines - original omitted content)

This is an example of women involved in a group where men take on roles as joint owners of concubines, all sharing one female.

The woman in question certainly holds little fault for her own, yet undeniably delivers a wretched scene that defies description.

Initially, she played the part of a concubine to the leader of this group (the subsequent passage, totaling forty-five lines, has also been omitted).

Although locations and names cannot be explicitly mentioned here, I hope readers can vaguely infer the hidden truths with some understanding.

Indeed, countless materials lie in my possession.

Especially concerning the above accounts, I have firsthand experience with scenarios that simply cannot be fabricated. If any believe that I have concocted fictitious attacks against others to obscure my shortcomings, I do not feel the need to justify myself. 

However, if my words seem untrustworthy, dive in to explore the underbelly of this masked performance.

Whatever emerges can be witnessed with one's own eyes.

My true intention is to expose the entire reality step by step to the public; yet, as I am unable to accomplish this, I harbor deep resentment.

If, by this alone, I can alert my innocent younger sisters, who unaware, would rashly throw themselves into danger, it will be counted as a grand success.  

**Summary**  

This article expresses the author's grievances as an unnamed woman in early 20th-century Korea, exposing the moral corruption of male figures in society, especially those identified as public intellectuals or notable figures. Through her observations and personal experience, the author critiques the patriarchal dynamics that manipulate young women, ultimately calling out these men who exploit their positions of power. Historians will find this piece significant as it provides insight into women's struggles during the colonial period, highlighting the social injustices faced by women and the ways in which gender dynamics were contested amid a rapidly changing socio-political landscape.

이유는 그들이 지은 죄가 그들의 功勞로써 능히 相殺하고도 남은 功이 크다는 一派와 過渡期이니 엇절 수 업는 矛盾이라고 보는 一派다.
과연 구역이 나는 수작이다.
첫재 무엇을 가지고 그들의 공로라 하는가?
아닌 게 아니라 그들은 적거나 크거나 민중과 사회를 위한 사업을 標榜하고 서서 잇다.
갑시헐한 民族主義의 큰 간판을 내어걸고 아직 利害의 打算에 눈이 어두운 민중의 團結力을 橫領하여 그것으로 뿔조아自由主義의 王國을 建設하려는 新聞雜誌業者들의 공로!
농민의 財物을 노략한 것을 물녀바더 가지고 洋行ㅅ개나 하고 도라와서 속이 텡뷔인 소리를 짓거리고 다니는 그들의 공로!
어렴풋한 氣分만 가지고 壇上에 올나 頭序 업는 소리를 짓거리다가 中止나 당하고 그것을 雄辯家라 하야 新聞雜誌에서 비행기를 태우기 때문에 공연히 남의 자식을 바리개된 애송이 雄辯家의 공로!
시대에 逆流하는 스포-쓰맨의 공로!
평생 가야 제 일밧게는 못하는 音樂家의 공로!
뿔조아의 第三夫婦人인 藝術派 文人의 공로!
모도가 거트로 보면 총독부의 연돌가치 꼿꼿한 것 가트나 속은 새캄앗다.
사람의 옷을 입은 사면바리다.
이러한 者들의 이러한 공로가 잇다 하며 그것을 묵인한다.
必然만 기다리는 樂觀패들은 矛盾은 過程이라 하야 묵인을 한다.
『필연』의 해석도 잘못하면서 必然必然한다.

끄트로 멧 개의 구톄뎍 事實을 들어 말할 터이다.＜57＞
나에게는 昨年에 학교를 맛치고 求職하는 여자 친구 하나가 잇다.
사람이 나히는 스무살이나 먹엇스되 도모지 철이 업고 世上 分別을 하지 못한다.
그러한 사람이 家庭의 감독도 업시 서울서 하숙 생활„ 하며 求職運動을 하고 잇다.
나는 그가 엷운 얼음을 밟는 듯한 안탁가운 마음은 잇스면서도 이곳저곳에 주선을 하여주엇다.
(此間二十行略 - 원문)
이것은 어느 남자들이 만흔 구루푸 속에 들어가서 일을 하다가 사오人의 共同所有妾노릇을 하든 여자의 이약이다.
이 여자에 대하여서는 물론 여자 편에도 큰 잘못이 업는 것이 아니나 과연 그네의 醜惡한 짓이란 무어라 形言할 수가 업다.
최초에 그 녀자는 그 그루푸의 대가리되는 한 者의 情婦노릇을 하다가 (以上四十五行略 - 원문)
以上의 實例가 물론 시간과 장소와 各其姓名을 쓰지 못하기 때문에 몽롱하기는 하나 그래도 半쯤은 內幕이 엇떠하엿다는 것을 讀者도 짐작하엿슬 것이다.
그 밧게도 얼마든지 나의 수중에는 材料가 山가치 만히 싸여잇다.
더구나 우에 들은 實例 중에 내 自身이 당한 것 가튼 것은 秋毫 半푼엇치도 속임이 업는 事實이다. 혹은 사람들이 『남을 공격하기 위하야 제 일홈을 숨기고 虛構를 만들엇다』고 解釋한다면 나는 구지여 변명하려고 아니한다.
그러나 만일 나의 하는 말이 미덥지 못하거든 한 몸을 바려 黑幕을 探査할 셈치고 뛰여들어 보아라.
무엇이 나오는지는 그 때에 눈으로 불 수 잇슬 것이다.

나의 本뜻은 기다란 前提 가튼 것은 내어노코 사실에 관한 全體만을 일일히 들어 세상에 발표하려는데 잇스나 그야말로 뜻을 두고 이루지 못하니 恨이 매친다.
이것만으로써도 불을 보고 죽엄인 줄 알지 못하고 날어들려는 나의 귀여운 동생들에게 警戒를 주는 도음이 된다면 큰 성공으로 알겟다.＜58＞
