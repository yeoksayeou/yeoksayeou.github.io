기사제목	이것도 한失手, 내가 실수한 이약이 (This is also a Mistake, My Mistaken Article)  
필자	蔡萬植 (Choi Man-sik)  
기사형태	회고·수기 (Memoir/Personal Narrative)  

I often make mistakes, and I'm rather clumsy, resulting in a constant feeling of unease. Recently, I made another significant blunder that is particularly embarrassing because it has negatively impacted the reputation of an intimate friend I've known for many years. This has made me feel extremely uncomfortable and guilty, leading me to feel I cannot endure the situation any longer.

This revolves around an article published in the New Year issue of this magazine titled "Good People or Bad People to Be Friends With," which includes a section by Kim Ya-bak from the Dong-A Ilbo titled "Based on Class or Region." Within that article, there's a segment that implies that people from Yeongnam and Gyeongbuk are easier to befriend, while those from Gyeonggi, Honam, and Gwanseo are somewhat difficult. 

While this observation may not completely lack basis, the essence is that Kim Ya-bak has never been one to not have friends across any region in Joseon. If people from those areas were to read the article, how might they view Kim? Eight or nine out of ten would likely feel indignant, thinking, "This guy has been shunning me all this time while pretending to be friendly." Not to mention, Kim, being from Yeongnam, is saying that one can befriend people from other regions freely, so how can he say such a thing?

However, there is an underlying story here. When I went to request a draft from Kim for the article titled "Good People or Bad People to Be Friends With," we both were very busy at that time. Kim had just finished working on the day’s newspaper, and I had a deadline for drafts looming just a day away. Therefore, Kim was in no mood to really write or even engage in a conversation.

"Come on, let’s at least discuss it. What about friendships based on region?" 

"Region? What a silly thing! Who among us finds friends based on region? Even with people from other countries, if there’s mutual understanding, we can become friends. Region-wise, what does that even mean? Just hurry up and make the New Year’s issue and send me a copy."

"That's not it! Seriously, this is all about making the New Year’s issue. Come on—if we were to divide by region... hypothetically speaking, what about that?"

"Sure, let's say hypothetically—but bear in mind, if I’m someone who only has friendships among Eight Provinces, how can that be any different? First, talking about region after region like this gives me the chills. We can talk about personalities, and that can be differentiated. Generally, both people from Yeongnam and Gyeongbuk are straightforward and down-to-earth, while people from Gwanseo are more flamboyant and talkative; and the Gyeonggi people... Honam people... no suitable adjectives seem to come to mind! Goodness. You’re from Honam? Why would you write it that way? Regardless, personalities vary. The joy of friendship varies too! Some people have certain vibes, others have different vibes—that’s just how it is! What does it matter to talk about regions or anything else?"

"The esteemed grand reporter of a major newspaper purposely made me ask these questions, and the replies are ridiculous!"

"If they are ridiculous, then read Jeong Do-jeon's Profiles of Figures of Eight Provinces. And just get out of here with this nonsense!"

"Then, generally speaking, what kind of people are good to be friends with?"

"Seems like a major reporter such as yourself is easily misled! Once you take a person like you out of the equation, then everyone else is terrific! Haha..."

This was one of the things Kim said at that moment. Just from that alone, Kim wouldn’t bear any fault. But with all this, I, in my clumsy eagerness, let my pen run out of control, forgetting to think about the whole matter beforehand and ended up creating such a mess of an article. Since my relationship with Kim was somewhat close, he didn’t press me too hard, but it was evident that he sensed a bit of resentment. 

If by chance someone who is also close to Kim were to seek out Kim in anger, they would probably rather punch me to vent his frustration rather than retain it.

  
**Summary**  

The author reflects on a past mistake regarding an article published in a magazine that unintentionally offended a friend by making generalized statements about people from different regions in Korea, suggesting that the relationships varying by location were not as harmonious as they could be. The article poses an intriguing insight into issues of regional stereotypes and social ties during the colonial period in Korea, highlighting how perceptions of social interactions among different groups could affect personal relationships, thereby reflecting broader societal sentiments in that era. Historians studying this article may find it interesting to examine how such perceptions of social networks and friendships were influenced by the colonial context.

이것도 한失手, 내가 실수한 이약이,
						蔡萬植
남보담 갓득이나 실수를 잘 하는 농판이기 때문에 노상 오금이 저릿저릿 하는데 최근에 큼직한 실수를 또 하나 저걸너 노앗다.
더구나 이 번 실수는 나의 다시 업시 친한 친고의 체면에 적지 아니한 영향을 끼처 주게 된 만큼 한 십 년 굴속에 가 숨어 잇고 십게 무안하고 미안하여서 도모지 견댈 수가 업다.
바로 本誌 신년호에 발표된 『사귀기도 조흔 사람 납분 사람』이라는 기사가 잇고 그 중에 東亞日報社 金枓白 君의 『階級 따러 地方 따러』라는 部分記事가 잇다.
그 기사 가운데는 嶺南과 關北 사람은 사귀기가 조코 京畿와 湖南과 關西 사람은 좀 거북하다는 의미의 부분이 잇다.
이 관찰은 어느 정도까지는 근거가 업는 것이 아니나 본래 金枓白 君은 전 조선의 어느 道를 치고 멧 사람씩의 친한 친구를 가지지 아니한 道가 업는데 만일 그 사람들이＜96＞ 그 기사를 읽는다 하면 金 君을 어터케 생각할 것인가?
十中八九는 『이 녀석이 그 동안 나를 한 팔 제처 노코 사귀여 왓구나』라고 憤慨를 할 것이다.
하물며 金 君자신이 嶺南사람이면서 嶺南사람은 그저 조코 달은 곳 사람은 낫부다고 하엿스니 그런 괘씸할 도리가 잇나!
그러나 사실은 한 겹의 內幕이 숨어 잇다.
내가 金 君에게로 『사귀기 조흔 사람 낫분 사람』이라는 제목의 원고를 청하러 갓슬 때는 피차에 퍽 분주한 때엇섯다. 金 君은 그 날의 신문 편즙에 밧벗고 나는 하루를 압헤 남긴 원고 모집 기한이 밧벗고... 그래서 金 君은 원고 쓰기는커녕 말대답도 잘 해주지 안을 형편이엿섯다.
『이 사람 그러면 말대답이나 하게. 爲先 地方別로 본다면 엇던가?』
『地方別? 밋친 사람! 누가 친구를 地方別로 사귀는 사람이 잇든가? 다른 나라 사람이라도 뜻만 마즈면 사귀여지는데 地方別이라니? 이 사람 밧분데 어서 가서 신년호나 잘 만들어 한 권 보내주게.』
『아니냐! 정말일세. 이게 다 신년호 만드는 일일세. 자- 地方別로 본다면... 가령 본다면 말일세...』
『글세 假定도 분수가 잇지. 나 가튼 『水湖志』패는 八道가 아니라 八十道 사람이라도 다 친하고 십흔데 地方別이 다 무씀¸가? 첫재 地方別 地方別 하는 소리좃차 나는 딱 듯기가 실테. 성질이 엇더타고는 말할 수 잇겟지. 대체로 우리 嶺南치나 關北 사람은 털털하고 뚝뚝하고 關西 사람은 선선하고 콸콸하고 畿湖 사람은... 湖南 사람은... 적당한 形容詞가 얼는 나오지 안네 그려! 올치. 자네가 湖南치기? 좃토록 써 넛케. 엇쨋든 성질이 各各이닛가. 사귐 맛도 各各이 아닌가? 이런 사람은 이런 맛으로 저런 사람은 저런 맛으로 사귀는 게지. 무얼 地方別이니 무어니 하고...』
『大新聞의 大記者 양반이라고 일부러 차저왓드니 대답이 신겁소 그려』
『신겁거든 鄭道傳의 八道 人物評이나 읽어보게. 밧분 사람 붓잡고 공연히 쓸데업시...』
『그러면 일반적으로는 어떤 사람이 사귀기 조흔가.』
『자네 名記者 노릇을 하노라고 이러케 축은축은 덤비는 모양일세 그려! 자네 가튼 개땅쇠를 빼 놋코는 다 조위 다 조와! 하하하... ...』
以上이 金 君의 한 말이엇섯다. 이 만큼 한 것을 가지고는 金 君에게는 아모 것도 허물 될 것이 업다.
그러한 것을 내가 쓰느라고 제일은 나의 붓끗치 둔해서 제이는 총망 중에 전후를 생각하지 못하고 붓대에 열이 나서 그러한 沒人事한 데 각가운 기사를 만들어 노앗다.
金 君은 나와의 친험이 친험인 만큼 모지게 추궁은 아니하나 속으로는 매우 쓰린 눈치다.＜96＞
만일 나와 한 가지로 金君과 친한 분이 잇다면 金君에게 노염을 가지는 대신 나를 큼직한 주먹으로 한 번 훔처 때리고 그 노염은 풀리기를 바란다.＜97＞
