기사제목	開闢社創社十周年紀念計畫 (10th Anniversary Commemorative Plan of Kaebiksa)  
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)

開闢社創社十周年紀念計畫  

1. Establishment of New Sister Enterprises  
   As it is currently an urgent matter that requires attention and has yet to be set in motion, we will take the lead in launching new enterprises.

2. Dispatch of Readers Abroad  
   Through three magazines, we will select one or two individuals by means of soliciting essays and send them to inspect emerging foreign cities.

3. Increase in Publications of Three Magazines  
   In addition to the three magazines currently published, we plan to launch one new magazine, aiming to advance to a total of four major magazines.

4. Hosting of Commemorative Exhibitions  
   In addition to holding monthly commemorative gatherings throughout the year, we will particularly conduct a large exhibition that will be beneficial to the public.

5. Organization of a National Readers' Association  
   We aim to organize nationwide readers' associations for "Students" and "Children" to foster closer ties.

6. Establishment of a Traveling Lecture Group  
   We will organize a traveling lecture group composed of our staff and volunteers to visit local areas as needed, focusing on enhancing local culture centered around the readers' association.

7. Establishment of a New Agency Department  
   To cater to more than 200,000 readers, we will set up a large agency department in a cooperative model to facilitate practical living.

8. Commemorative Publication of Popular Korean Historical Tales  
   As part of our 10th-anniversary publication, we will release a text on modern Korean history written by Cha Sang-chan in the most accessible manner.

9. Commemorative Publication "Sources of Civilization"  
   For students and youth, we will distribute a 10th-anniversary gift: a translation by Ko Young-hwan that describes the path of human culture.

10. Commemorative Publication of Popular Parenting Talks  
    We assert that it is a must-have book for every household, consisting of parenting talks by Bang Jeong-hwan, which have long been awaited in every locality.

With such youthful vigor, we are preparing for a new path as we mark our 10th anniversary this year. The Kaebiksa plans to feature grand special editions in its three magazines monthly while steadily advancing the aforementioned ten plans.  

We earnestly hope for the support of our members and readers!  

We cannot express enough our gratitude that we have been able to fight for ten years thanks to the support of our readers. At the same time, we earnestly request your even stronger encouragement for our new endeavors as we enter our eleventh year.  

**Summary**  

This article outlines the ambitious plans of Kaebiksa as it celebrates its 10th anniversary. These plans include establishing new subsidiaries, enhancing reader engagement, and expanding publication efforts, along with organized events aimed at cultural enrichment. The document reflects the proactive role that cultural publications were taking to promote education, community, and cultural dialogue among Koreans during the colonial period, providing insight into the efforts to foster a sense of national identity amidst external pressures.

開闢社創社十周年紀念計畫
1 新姉妹事業創設
目下最新緊急을 要하는 일이면서 아즉지니저버려지고잇는 新事業에 率先着手함니다
2 讀者外國派遣
三雜誌를 通하야 論文募集의 方式으로 一人或二人을 選出하야 外國新興都市를 視察케함
3 三雜誌一種增判
現在發行하는 三雜誌外에 한가지 새 雜誌를 刊行하야 四大雜誌로 奮進하려함니다
4 紀念展覽會開催
十週年紀念集會를 今年中一年두고 每月開催하는 外에 特히 民衆에게 有益할 大展覽會開催
5 全國讀者會組織
『學生』과『어린이』의 讀者會를 全朝鮮的으로 組織하야 一層緊密한 關係를 지여나아가려는것임니다
6 巡廻講演班組織
本社社員及囑托中으로서 巡廻講演班을 組織하야 隨時地方에 出張하야 讀者會中心으로 地方文化向上에 努力
7 本社代理部新設
二十餘萬讀者와 더부러 消費組合式의 一大代理部를 設置하야 實生活의 便宜를 圖하기로함
8 紀念出版 通俗朝鮮史話
十週年紀念出版其一, 아모나 가장 쉽게 알수잇게 著述한 近代朝鮮史車相瓚氏著
9 紀念出版 「文明의 淵源」
學生級靑年級을 向해보내는 十週年紀念선물노 人類文化의 길을 細術한 高永煥氏譯著
10 紀念出版 通俗育兒講話
地方마다 집집마다에서 苦待한지 오래인 方定煥氏의 育兒講話!! 萬家必備할 著書임을 斷言한다

一騎當千 奮戰奮戰 十一年재의 새길
今年으로 十週年을 마지하는 開闢社는 우선 三雜誌부터 每月에 大大特輯을 하거니와 다시  上記十大計劃을 세워 그 準備가 着着進行되고 잇슴니다.＜100＞
