발행일	1930년 02월 01일  
기사제목	朝鮮 歷史日誌(第二回) 二月錄 (Korean Historical Diary, Second Edition, February Records)  
기사형태	잡저 (Periodical)  

朝鮮 歷史日誌  
1st (Old January 3rd)  
◇ The Daengpyeong Petition is submitted, criticizing the evils of factionalism and announcing that thereafter high-ranking officials in the political sphere will transcend all factions and will be appointed based on merit. (Yeongjong, Eulsi)  

◇ The currency regulations are announced. (Gojong, Gwangmu 5th year)  

2nd (Same 4th)  
◇ The Astronomical Bureau is abolished. (Sunjong, Runghee 1st year)  

◇ Yi Seong-gye leads troops against Liao. (Goryeo, Gongmin Wang 19th year)  

3rd (Same 5th)  
◇ An agreement on whaling rights between Japan and the three provinces of Gangwon, Gyeongsang, and Jeolla is signed. (Gwangmu 4th year)  

◇ An unusual insect descends in Seohyeon. (Silla, Hyeondeok Wang Eulsi)  

4th (Same 6th)  
◇ A major agricultural festival is held at the Wonkyudan (Round Mound Altar). (Gwangmu 2nd year)  

5th (Same 7th)  
◇ General Yi Geun-taek is attacked by an assassin on Serdong Road. (Gwangmu 10th year)  

6th (Same 8th)  
◇ Ming General Yi Ruso defeats the Japanese army at Pyongyang and recaptures the city. (Seonjo, Gyesi)  

7th (Same 9th)  
◇ Zen Master Jin-gam Hoesa passes away. (Silla, Munseong Wang Gyeongmu)  

◇ Various cases of palace servants are burned. (Sunjong, Sinyu)  

◇ The national taxation collection law is announced. (Runghee 2nd year)  

8th (Same 10th)  
◇ The island chief is first appointed on Ulleungdo. (Gae 54)  

9th (Same 11th)  
◇ Heungwang Temple is completed. (Goryeo, Munjong, Jeongmi - began construction, took twelve years to finish)  

10th (Same 12th)  
◇ Jin soldiers capture Uiju. (Injo, Jeongmyo)  

◇ Heungryeo Kingdom requests aid. (Goryeo, Hyeonjong, Gyeongmu)  

11th (Same 13th)  
◇ Jo Chong defeats the Mongol general Hajin and suffers heavy losses against the Dongdan army at Gangdong Fortress. (Goryeo, Gojong, Gimai)  

12th (Same 14th)  
◇ King Joong-hui ascends to heaven at Yueyang County.  

◇ The publication law is promulgated. (Runghee 3rd year)  

13th (Same 15th)  
◇ The founder of Silla, Hyeokgeose, ascends to the throne. (Gapsi)  

◇ Sticky rice is offered to birds. (Silla, Jochiwang, 10th year - this is the origin of Joseon's medicinal cuisine.)  

14th (Same 16th)  
◇ Japanese Minister of Justice Kuroda Kiyotaka and Inoue Kaoru come to Ganghwa regarding artillery fire and diplomatic inquiries.  

15th (Same 17th)  
◇ An You, Choi Yeong, and others defeat the Red Bandits. (Goryeo, Gongmin Wang, Imjin)  

◇ A white rainbow pierces the moon. (Yeongjo, Imsin)  

◇ The area for the state treasury of the bank and post office is established. (Runghee 2nd year)  

16th (Same 18th)  
◇ Yi Seong-gye defeats the Red-headed Bandits and recaptures Songjing. (Goryeo, Gongmin Wang, 11th year)  

◇ Hundred coins are in circulation. (Gojong, Jeongmyo)  

17th (Same 19th)  
◇ Kim Cheong-yin Sangheon rents the peace treaty with Qing and weeps bitterly. (Injo, Jeongchuk)  

18th (Same 20th)  
◇ Kim Yang defeats the royal army at Daejeon and punishes Kim Myeong. (Silla, Hyegong Wang, Gimyu)  

◇ The industrial apprenticeship rules are published. (Gwangmu 1st year)  

19th (Same 21st)  
◇ Anju fortress falls to Jin soldiers, and Nam Yi-hyeong dies for loyalty. (Injo, Jeongmyo)  

20th (Same 22nd)  
◇ Yi Sit initiates a revolt. (Injo, Gapsi)  

◇ Gangdu falls to the Qing soldiers, and Seonyuan Kim Sang-yong self-immolates in loyalty. (Injo, Jeongchuk)  

◇ The Busan underwater cable law is established with five provisions. (Gae 490)  

21st (Same 23rd)  
◇ Kim Yu-shin crosses the Seven Heavy Rivers and defeats the Goguryeo soldiers. (Silla, Munmu Wang, Imil)  

◇ The regulations on Japanese students studying abroad (Gwangmu 10th year) and Persona Law (Yunhui 3rd year) are issued.  

22nd (Same 24th)  
◇ General Jang Man hears of Yi Sit's rebellion and begins to mobilize troops against him. (Injo, Gapsi)  

◇ The regulations on national forest and mountains are promulgated, and the salt industry testing law is established. (Runghee 2nd year)  

23rd (Same 25th)  
◇ The 6th article of the Korea-Japan agreement is established.  

◇ The Qing soldiers attack Pyeongsan. (Injo, Jeongmyo)  

◇ The legal training school rules are published. (Runghee 2nd year)  

24th (Same 26th)  
◇ Yeongjo visits Sinil Castle. (Yeongjo, Jeongchuk)  

◇ The rules on the manufacture and sale of promissory notes are established. (Gwangmu 11th year)  

25th (Same 27th)  
◇ Injo is relocated to Gangdu. (Jeongmyo Hurandan)  

◇ The Unified Control Camp is established at Nanyang. (Gojong, Gyesi)  

◇ Artillery is installed in coastal areas. (Gwangmu 5th year)  

26th (Same 28th)  
◇ Jeong Dong-kyu learns of the peace with Namhan and cuts open his stomach with a sword. (Injo, Jeongchuk)  

◇ A cotton cultivation site is established in Mukpo. (Runghee 2nd year)  

27th (Same 29th)  
◇ The peace treaty with Qing is completed.  

28th (Same February 1st)  
◇ Jiang Han-jam defeats the Khitan army at Gyeongju. (Goryeo, Hyeonjong, Gimyu)  

◇ Kim Yu-shin defeats the Tang army at Jangseok. (Silla, Munmu Wang, Imil)  

◇ The middle harmony ruler is promulgated. (Jeongjo, Byeongjin)  

**Summary**  

This article presents a chronological record of important events in Korean history for the month of February, which includes political developments, military actions, cultural activities, and significant deaths. The content illustrates the socio-political landscape of Korea during the periods mentioned, reflecting the various conflicts with foreign powers as well as internal governance issues. Historians may find notable patterns in how factional disputes and foreign invasions shaped the national identity and state response, providing insight into Korea's colonial struggles and the legacy of governance amid adversity.

잡지명	별건곤 제26호
발행일	1930년 02월 01일
기사제목	朝鮮 歷史日誌(第二回) 二月錄
기사형태	잡저

朝鮮 歷史日誌
一日(舊正月三日)
◇ 蕩平訴를 下하야 由來朋黨의 惡弊를 痛論하고 伊後政界要官은 各 朋黨을 超越하야 採用할 事를 宣布하다.(英宗 乙巳)
◇ 貨幣條例를 發表하다.(高宗 光武 五年)
二日(同 四日)
◇ 觀象監을 廢止하다.(純宗 隆熙 元年)
◇ 李太祖가 攻遼의 軍을 率하고 發程하다.(高麗 恭愍王 19年)
三日(同 五日)
◇ 日本과 江慶咸三道捕鯨權合同協約이 調印되다.(光武 四年)
◇ 西原에 異蟲이 降下하다.(新羅 憲德王 乙巳)
四日(同 六日)
◇ 圓丘壇에서 祭穀大祭를 行하다.(光武 二年)
五日(同 七日)
◇ 軍大 李根澤이 寺洞路上에서 刺客에게 遐難하다.＜2＞(光武 十年)
六日(同 八日)
◇ 明將 李如松이 平壤에 日兵을 大破하고 平壤을 回復하다.(宣祖 癸巳)
七日(同 九日)
◇ 眞鑑禪師 惠昭가 歸寂하다.(新羅文聖王 庚午)
◇ 內司各宮奴婢案을 梵燒하다.(純祖 辛酉)
◇ 國稅徵收法을 발표하다.(隆熙 二年)
八日(同 十日)
◇ 울릉도에 島長을 始置하다.(開五四)
九日(同 十一日)
◇ 興王寺가 落成되다.(高麗 文宗 丁未-起工 凡 十二年 竣工)
十日(同 十二日)
◇ 金兵이 義州를 陷하다.(仁祖 丁卯)
◇ 興遼國이 上表乞援하다.(高麗 顯宗 庚午)
十一日(同 十三日)
◇ 趙沖이 蒙將 哈眞으로 더부러 東丹兵을 江東城에서 大破하다.(高麗 高宗 己卯)
十二日(同 十四日)
◇ 忠惠王이 岳陽縣에서 昇遐하다.
◇ 出版法이 頒布되다.(隆熙 三年)
十三日(同 十五日)
◇ 新羅始祖赫居世가 卽位하다.(甲子)＜3＞
◇ 糯飯으로 鳥에 祭하다.(新羅 炤智王 十年이니 朝鮮藥食의 始初다.)
十四日(同 十六日)
◇ 日本辨理大臣黑田淸隆과 井上馨이 雲揚艦砲擊問質事로 江華에 來到하다.
十五日(同 十七日)
◇ 安祐, 崔瀅 等이 紅賊을 擊破하다.(高麗 恭愍王 壬寅)
◇ 白虹이 月을 貫하다.(英祖 壬申)
◇ 금고 及 郵署國庫金出納區域이 施政되다.(隆熙 二年)
十六日(同 十八日)
◇ 李太祖가 紅頭賊을 大破하고 松京을 回復하다.(高麗 恭愍王 十一年)
◇ 當百錢을 施用하다.(高宗 丁卯)
十七日(同 十九日)
◇ 金淸陰尙憲이 朝淸講和書을 裂하고 大哭하다.(仁祖 丁丑)
十八日(同 二十日)
◇ 金陽이 王軍을 達伐에서 破하고 金明을 誅하다.(新羅 僖康王 己未)
◇ 공업전습소규칙이 반포되다.(光武 一年)
十九日(同 二十一日)
◇ 安州城이 金兵에게 陷落되고 南以興이 殉節하다.(仁祖 丁卯)＜4＞
二十日(同 二十二日)
◇ 李适이 反亂을 起하다.(仁祖 甲子)
◇ 江都가 淸兵에게 陷落되고 仙源 金尙容이 自焚節死하다.(仁祖 丁丑)
◇ 釜山해저전선법 五隷이 성립되다.(開 490年)
二十一日(同 二十三日)
◇ 金庾信이 七重河을 渡하야 高句麗兵을 대파하다.(신라 文武王 壬戌)
◇ 일본유학생규정(광무 십년)민적법(융희 3년)이 반포되다.
二十二日(同 二十四日)
◇ 도원수 張晩이 李适의 반란이 起함을 始聞하고 討适兵을 起하다.(仁祖 甲子)
◇ 국유산림산야부분임규칙이 반포되고 鹽業試驗法이 始定되다.(隆熙 2년)
二十三日(同 二十五日)
◇ 韓日議定書 6조가 성립되다.
◇ 淸兵이 平山을 來襲하다.(仁祖 丁卯)
◇ 法官養成所學則이 發表되다.(隆熙 二年)＜5＞
二十四日(同 二十六日)
◇ 英祖가 辛日城尉第에 幸行하다.(英祖 丁丑)
◇ 手形用紙製造及賣下規則이 始布하다.(光武 11年)
二十五日(同 二十七日)
◇ 仁祖가 江都에 播遷하다.(丁卯胡亂)
◇ 南陽에 統禦營를 설치하다.(高宗 癸巳)
◇ 연해지방에 포대를 설치하다.(光武 5년)
二十六日(同 二十八日)
◇ 鄭桐溪蘊이 南漢講和의 報를 聞하고 劍으로 腹을 割하다.(仁祖 丁丑)
◇ 木浦에 면화재배소를 설치하다.(隆熙 2年)
二十七日(同 二十九日)
◇ 淸과 講和節目이 成하다.
二十八日(同 二月 一日)
◇ 姜邯贊이 龜州에서 契丹兵을 대파하다.(高麗 顯宗 己未)
◇ 金庾信이 獐塞에서 唐兵을 擊하다.(新羅 文武王 壬戌)
◇ 中和尺子宰執을 頒하다.(正祖 丙辰)＜6＞
