A Different World

Fashion has a tremendous heat force. For a while, knitting was considered a women's exclusive trend, but recently in England, even men have taken to knitting. The most notable example is that Prince of Wales, Edward, is enthusiastic about knitting scarves.

Kiss Ban

In Kansas, USA, the Public Health Association has issued a kiss ban. The content says that one should not kiss in crowded places, avoid kissing when there is excessive heat or if feeling ill, and do not kiss in windy places.

National Treasure Sale

The government of France, facing financial difficulties, has decided to sell Rodin's famous marble sculpture 'The Kiss.'

Patriotic Long Hair

Two leaders of the Philippine independence movement have landed in San Francisco, USA, with well-defined long hair and beards. This is asserted to be a symbol of patriotism, declaring that they will not cut their hair or beards until the Philippines achieves independence.

Another Kiss Ban

A kiss ban has also been issued in Hankou, China. A newlywed couple was fined for kissing in a car.

Egg-eating Tiger

A man named Max Heynel, living in Berlin, Germany, known as 'Egg-eating Tiger,' holds the world record for eating 75 eggs in just 30 seconds. He is 22 years old, less than five feet tall, and weighs around 86 pounds.

Ancient Pahlawan Worth 1.3 Million Yen

Recently deceased American department store king Rodman Wanamaker had amassed a collection of ancient Pahlawan that is not only rare but is valued at 1.3 million yen at current market prices.

Germany is number one in book publishing

Even though Germany was devastated in World War I, it still ranks first in book publishing. Last year alone, 37,000 copies were published, meaning a book every twenty minutes. What about Korea?

Robbers

There has been a surge of robbers in the Beckenham area of England. In a home that experienced several difficulties, the owner left a note on the table with whiskey and glasses, saying, "There are no valuable items in this house, so please drink this whiskey and go." When the owner returned, he found signs that a robber had indeed entered, and the note led to a rather amusing reaction from the intruder, who expressed gratitude after having a drink and mentioned he wouldn't have entered if he knew there was nothing of value.

Scientific Begging Instruction

In Moscow, a beggar association is teaching scientific begging, with instructors claiming that their begging results were significantly better than their teaching records.

**Summary**

This article provides an amusing yet telling overview of various peculiar social trends and regulations from different countries in the 1930s, such as the ban on kissing in public places and the embrace of knitting by men, including royalty. The juxtaposition of these trends with the Philippine independence movement highlights the colonial landscape of the time, an era when cultural developments, yet steeped in traditional views, were taking place amidst ongoing struggles for sovereignty and self-identity.

別의 別乾坤
英國 皇太子가 編物

流行이란 굉장한 熱力을 가지고 잇다. 한동안 編物이라 한면 女子專屬流行이든 것이 最近 英國에서는 놈팽이들까지 編物을 한다든가. 그 가장 有數한 一例로는 푸린쓰·오부·웰스(英國 皇太子)가 목두를 뜨기에 精神이 업다는 것이다.

키스 禁止
米國 칸사스州 衛生組合에서는 키스 禁止令을 發布하엿는데 內容은 여러 사람이 모힌 곳에서 키스하지 말 것. 相對便이 熱이 놉거나 감긔가 들엇슬 때에 키스하지 말 것. 찬바람이 부는 곳에서 키스하지 말 것.

國寶 放賣
돈이 업서 쪌쪌매는 佛蘭西 政府에서는 로단의 有名한 大理石 彫刻 『키스』를 팔기로 햇다고.

愛國 長髮
比律賓 獨立運動의 首領 두 사람이 米國 산푸란씨스코에 上陸을 하엿는데 長髮 長髷에 형용이 긔괴하다고. 그 이유는 比律賓이 독립을 現實하기까지는 愛國의 表徵으로 절대로 머리와 수염을 깍지 안켓다고 한다든가.

또 키스 禁止令
中國 漢口에에도 키스 禁止令이 내렷다. 방금 新婚한 한 쌍 夫婦가 自働車 속에서 키스를 하다가 그만 들녀나서 罰金이 一金 五十圓也.

닭이알호랑이
獨逸 伯林에 사는 막스하이넬이라는 친구는 號曰 닭이알호랭이인데 十分 三十秒 동안에 닭이알 일흔다섯 개를 먹어바린 世界新記錄保有者. 나희는 스물두 살. 키는 五尺 未滿. 體重은 十三貫.

古代 빠요린 百三十萬園엇치
最近에 죽은 米國의 百貨店王 로드만·와나메-커는 생전에 모아둔 고대 빠요린이 모다 珍品일 뿐 아니라 時價로 一百三十萬圓엇치나 된다고.

書籍出版은 獨逸이 第一 歐洲大戰에 쫄딱 망할 줄만 알어도 그래도 書籍 出版으로는 獨逸이 第一. 일년 전 三萬七千 部. 즉 이십 분만에 冊部씩 出版하는 셈. 朝鮮은 若何?

도적놈 各位
영국의 벳켄남 地方에서는 한동안 강도가 만이 出沒하엿다. 여러 차례 곤란을 밧든 한 집에서는 테불 우에다 우이스키와 술잔을 노코는 그 엽헤다 告示를 써놋퇴
『이 집에는 하등 귀중한 물품이 업스니 이 술이나 한잔 마시고 도라가시요. 더뎍놈 各位』
이래 노코는 主人이 밧게를 나아갓다가 돌아와 보이니까 과연 도적이 들어왓든 흔젹이 보이는데 술을 노아두엇든 테-불 우에는
『조흔 것을 주어서 고맙슴니다. 한잔 먹고 도라갑니다. 이러케 아모 것도 업는 집인 줄 알엇스면 애초에 들어오지 아니할 것을... 섭섭함니다. 그래도 원 사람 사는 집이라는 체면으로 하녀라도 하나쯤은 두어두시기를 바람니다.』

科學的 거지질 敎授
모스코바市 乞人組合에서는 科學的 거지질을 敎授하는데 授業者는 未授業보담 거지질 成績이 大大的으로 好良云云.＜59＞
