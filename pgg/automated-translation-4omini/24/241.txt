**Summary**  

The article announces the results of a prize contest from the previous issue of the magazine "별건곤," which had an unprecedented participation of 48,052 entrants, of whom 12,252 answered correctly. The successful responses were published, along with a list of winners including various individuals from different regions of Korea. This engaging contest reflects the magazine's attempt to promote readership and interaction during the colonial period, showcasing how cultural publications fostered a sense of community among Koreans despite the broader context of colonial oppression. Historians may find it interesting that such efforts were made to maintain cultural identity and interaction among readership amid colonial challenges.
