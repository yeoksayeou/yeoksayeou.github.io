Postcard Communication

1. Limited to postcards

2. Inquiries will not be answered

3. The deadline is by the 20th of every month


Dear Teacher Yukdang!

It seems that the great teacher Choi Nam-seon has probably awakened from his long slumber, right? Since when has the strange and bizarre made its entrance, and what is being written in the newspapers...

I suppose the teacher's stature must have grown considerably? 

How does the taste of the native mulberry leaves differ from the old taste of mulberry leaves? 

Nevertheless, do you still have the audacity to dip your hands into the manuscript fee?

Ah, the horses weep in the northern winds, and the birds of Yue are accustomed to the southern ladies… What do you think, Teacher?

(Pyŏngyang, KKsaeng)

A Proposal

It is somewhat of an old story, but I must bring it up. There is one matter related to the exhibition that has been stuck in my head as if branded in fire since the time surrounding the event, and if it can even be called an achievement, it is indeed a significant one.

To see great numbers at the exhibition, the students who had been living at home for two or three years were suddenly gathered back by their landlords who claimed that they would treat the students like family in the advertisements they put up on the streets at the end of the exhibition. 

As for the students who got sent away from their lodging houses after returning home for vacation and wandered around the streets with their desks on their backs, how much suffering did they endure? What sort of people are these landlords who beat their own kin repeatedly? I propose that we should clearly exclude such folks from the 22 million people.

(Kyŏngsŏng, Kimseoksan)

Hope for the Kinhyow Association

I am a not-so-knowledgeable woman. I have joined the Kinhu association because I want to understand more about the world; however, much of what they talk about in their meetings is beyond my comprehension due to my lack of vocabulary, so naturally, I cannot engage. I wish that we unknowledgeable people could be included somehow. 

(Daegu, A Woman)

Advertising Psychology in Japanese

There are all sorts of unusual friends in the world. Even though one can clearly understand that the customer is Korean and knows that the commercial nature developed will not be beneficial for Japanese people, advertisements are still published in newspapers and magazines in Japanese. 

For example, the book merchant O-dang does this, as does the sporting goods merchant San-O-dang. How would this be dissected if we were to analyze this with psychology?

(Kyŏngsŏng, Jiankōse)

To the Head Teacher of the Middle School

Kyŏnghyŏng is blossoming, yet we have become ignorant country folks who cannot even read. We might not understand what is happening, but it seems that your responsibilities are worse than ours. I hear that our child, who is as good as a precious dumpling, is trembling in detention. What does this mean? 

There is a saying, “trust the axe, but it stabs the foot,” is this a saying that applies here? From now on, I will have to go to the police station to pay for my car, and surely my own child will not die of cold.

(Sangju, An Old Man) <89>

**Summary**

This article presents various opinions and letters written by readers addressing diverse social issues and thoughts on cultural developments during the colonial period in Korea. The sentiments reflect a degree of frustration with the circumstances of life under Japanese rule, especially pertaining to education and social inequalities. Historians might find the commentary on the duality of Korean and Japanese identity, as well as the treatment of students by landlords, illustrative of broader struggles within Korean society during this time.

六堂先生게!
육당
崔南善대대선생님에서는 아마 중취원 잠안이 깨신 모양이지? 怪奇가 망측하게 나오고 신문에 무엇을 쓰고 또 무엇을 쓰고.....
한겁질버섯스니 선생님 몸집도 꽤 자라섯겟지?
선생님 재래종의 桑葉味와 舊來의 桑葉味가 어터케 달읍더이까?
선생님 그래도 넷맛이당지서 슬거머니 원고료에 손을 대이심니까?
흥 胡馬는 북풍에 울고 越鳥는 南妓에 길든다더니....
선생님 생각이 엇더하오?
(平壤 KK생)

제의한가지
조곰 묵은 이야기외다만은 박람때 전후에 보고드른 이약이 중에 이때까지 이처지지안코 내머리속에 불로지저 부친것처럼 박혀 잇는 한가지 소득? 소득이라면 대단한 소득이잇슴니다.
박람회에 큰 수를 보려고 2년 3년 내집에 잇던 학생들을 앗김없시 붓그럼없시 내여 뜻츤 하숙주인들이 박람회 끗나는때 길거리에 써부친 광고에 하였스되 학생을 친가족가치 대우함니다고. 방학때 귀향햇다가 도라오는 길로 하숙에서 내여 쫏긴 학생들이 길거리로 책상을 짊어지고 방황하면서 얼마나 고생을 하엿는가 대체 하숙영업자들은 친족들을 몇 번씩이나 때엿다 부첫다 하는 인종들인가...이 따위 인종들을 단연히 2천 2백만 속에서 제외해 평겨칠 일을 제의한다.(경성 金石山)

槿友會에 대한 희망
나는 싀골 무식한 녀성임니다. 세상 일이 좀 알고 십허서 이곳에 근우회가 생겻기에 나도 입회를 하엿슴니다만은 그들이 모여서 하는 말 중에는 술어도 몰을 것이 만흐니 자연히 별자미를 못부치게됨니다. 엇더케 우리가치 무식한 사람도 축에 들도록하여 주엇스면 조켓습듸다.(大邱 一女性)

일문광고심리
세상에는 별의별 이상야릇한 친구가 만타. 번연히 고객이 조선사람인 것을 알며 또 일본사람 상대로 발전될 성질의 상업이 아닌 것을 자타가 다가티 알고 잇는데도 신문이나 잡지에는 일본말로 광고를 내인다. 曰 書籍商 以O堂이 그러코 運動具商 三O堂이 그러타. 이것을 정신병학으로 해부한다면 답은 어테케될고.(경성 尖口生)

중학교 교장 선생 각하
京鄕이 落落하고 또 우리는 목불식정의 우매한 촌부가 되고 보니 무엇을 알겟소만 귀하의 책임관렴이 우리만도 못하외다그려 당신에 찰떡가치맛긴 우리 자제가 유치장에서 떨고 잇다니 이게 웬말이요. 속담에 信斧斫足이라드니 이걸 두고 이른말인지 이다음부터는 자동차 신세를 질 것업시 아주 경찰서에 갓다 맛기겟고 제 아비 혈육을 밧고 난 놈이 설마 얼어 죽기야 하겟소.(尙州 一老夫)＜89＞
