Magazine Title: 별건곤  
Publication Date: 1930년 02월 01일  
Article Title: 沈亨弼氏 夫人, 訪問 가서 感心한 婦人 (Mrs. Shin Hyung-bul, A Woman Impressed During a Visit)  
Author: 崔瑨淳  
Article Type: 소식 (News)

Mrs. Shin Hyung-bul is one of the most impressive women I know among the few women with whom I have special rapport. She comes from a wealthy family and was educated under the new teaching methods, yet she is also skilled in household management. She adeptly applies the new knowledge gained from school in areas such as child-rearing, family economics, and managing the household on the fly. Her diligence and frugality are also aspects that I find very commendable. 

Even carrying her little child, she handles all the household chores herself, and it is no trifling matter that she oversees all family expenditures as well. She is the most capable woman I know of, which leaves me in awe. 

Additionally, I must mention that since Mr. Shin and I are close friends, our meetings often lead to lively conversations and even a big drink together. Yet, she welcomes guests without any apparent fuss, which I consider rather admirable for a so-called new woman. If one were to ask whether this is indeed remarkable, I could find it hard to articulate an answer. However, it is true that in living her life as a model new woman, she brings joy and life to those around her, which certainly makes her noteworthy. 

**Summary**

This article highlights Mrs. Shin Hyung-bul’s exceptional capabilities in both traditional household roles and modern education. The author admires her ability to balance family duties while exhibiting qualities of a modern woman. This depiction of women's roles during the colonial period offers insights into societal expectations and the evolving identities of women in Korea, showcasing the tension between traditional domesticity and modernity that historians might find revealing.

沈亨弼氏 夫人, 訪問 가서 感心한 婦人
						崔瑨淳
특별히 만흔 교제가 업스며 더욱 여자와의 교제는 더 엷은 터이나 나의 아는 婦人＜37＞ 중에 中央高普 沈亨弼君의 夫人이 나의 가장 感心한 婦人이다. 氏는 일즉 부유한 가정에서 新敎育을 밧으신 분이나 家事에 熟達하시며 育兒라던가 가정경제라든가 집안 규모에 대하야 학교에서 어드신 新知識을 適宜하게 臨機하야 잘 응용하시며 퍽 근면하시면서 검소하신 것들이 모다 나의 感心한 바이다. 어린 아기까지 다리시고도 손수 집안 일을 모다 처리하시는 것은 勿論이오 加計家用까지 전부 마타보아 가시는 것이 나의 아는 부인 중에서 제일 탄복할이 만큼 능하시게 하는 것이다. 끗흐로 한가지 더 말슴할 것은 沈君과 나와는 절친한 만큼 각금 놀너가게 되면 자연 떠들게 되고 大飮까지 하게 되는 터이나 언제든지 실허하시는 빗치 업시 접대하는 것도 일반 新女性으로 보기에 듬은 일이라고 생각한다. 이것이 과연 감심할 것이냐고 뭇는다고 하면 무엇이라고 할 지 모르겟스나 사실에 잇서서 新女性으로 이 만큼 模範되게 살님을 하시며 재미를 붓치시고 지내시는 분은 듬을다고 하는 것만은 사실일 것이겟다.＜38＞
