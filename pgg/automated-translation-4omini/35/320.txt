A collection of humorous anecdotes, including a story about a schoolgirl 

K was a relatively young man of considerable intelligence. He was composed by nature, often lost in thought, and tended to shy away from arguments or engaging in trivial conversations, giving him a scholarly appearance. However, what was peculiar was his ability to hold his liquor; unlike many who become rowdy or unruly when intoxicated, K remained unchanged in demeanor.

Yet, unexpectedly, K found himself in an embarrassing situation one day after having consumed some alcohol while out with friends. Perhaps the fatigue from several days of toil contributed to his decision to overindulge. As usual, K boarded a tram without any incident, but suddenly felt a wave of nausea wash over him. 

Holding it in was becoming increasingly tough. K could no longer manage his discomfort, and upon stepping off the tram, he was met with an unexpected sight: a particularly demure schoolgirl standing before him. 

The amusing nature of the incident was not lost on anyone; K, supposedly sober and well-mannered, had just comprehended the absurdity of an intoxicated person embarrassingly falling before a schoolgirl on a tram! This situation was nothing short of a significant error in judgment on his part.

Fortunately, the young lady, far from offended or disgusted, merely brushed herself off and walked away. There was something deeply admirable about the schoolgirl’s calm demeanor amidst such chaos. The intention was not to belittle K’s blunder, but to praise the admirable qualities of the schoolgirl.

Stories in front of a house of courtesans 

K and S were both thoroughly intoxicated. After leaving the house of a courtesan, H, they bid farewell as it was past midnight. The two friends parted ways, K heading north while S went towards Jongno.

As they walked, their friendly exchange turned amusing. K suggested that S should take a taxi, to which S replied, "I’m fine, do I look drunk?" They assured each other to take care and agreed to meet again the next day. However, K, still inebriated, meandered down local streets and found himself near H’s house.

A few moments later, S, now also in the area, caught sight of H. Though initially surprised, he smiled and continued on, aiming to avoid any awkward encounters with K. 

On the other side, K, wishing to sidestep any confrontation with S, found himself returning to H’s house.

Eventually, both K and S crossed paths again, whereupon laughter erupted between the two of them. They parted once more, only to meet again after an hour, sharing another round of laughter before heading towards a nearby tavern.

The life story of courtesans 

Rumors about courtesans courting actors have circulated for quite some time. It is true that courtesans would often develop romances with actors. In the past, there were also tales of connections between courtesans and various professions: gangsters, melodramatic actors, journalists, musicians, and even taxi drivers. 

Indeed, the era saw a rise of exceptional students and actors as well. Presently, courtesans have their share of suitors as well, including a notable example in K, a courtesan known for her charm, who was caught up in a whirlwind romance with An Chang-nam, a famous aviator. 

In the earlier days, even baseball players and soccer players were popular among courtesans, demonstrating the evolving targets of affection based on status and renown. Despite the obvious variances in physical appeal, there was something about the charisma and fame of these men that captivated the hearts of courtesans.

An instance of unexpected humor occurred when a courtesan named H was asked by a customer if they could go out for a ride to enjoy the evening. As the man appeared to be sincere, she readily complied. 

Later, when a taxi was called for, there would often be amusing misunderstandings when the courtesan would get a ride with someone else. 

Such instances might not appear extraordinary in their essence, but they highlight how intertwined the lives of courtesans were with their clientele, often leading to comical outcomes. 

An important anecdote follows about one of the entertainers who fell short, losing his dignity not as a result of drunkenness but rather through unforeseen circumstances.

This anecdote only serves to showcase the healthy mix of comedy and severity inherent in the lifestyles of these courtesans. 

The stories reveal a dynamic period when societal roles were heavily intertwined with relationships rooted in affection, financial backing, or mere survival. 

During this era, such portrayals of life reveal how deeply skewed societal perceptions were and lead to various chronicle-worthy tales. 

Finally, as humorous anecdotes unfold within the daily lives of these individuals, the history of Korea during this colonial oppression reveals poignant insights into human behavior, socio-economic conditions, and the nuances of relationships among the classes.

**Summary**

The article presents a collection of humorous anecdotes focusing on a variety of social interactions that emphasize the comical yet serious nature of relationships, particularly within the milieu of courtesans and their encounters with men. The situations depicted reflect the complexities of human relationships and the behavior adjustments required in a colonial society. The humor juxtaposed with social critique highlights the intersections of class, gender, and culture during a time of colonial oppression that historians might find revealing.

그러닛가 술을 먹는다고해도 아모런 실수가 업섯슴니다.
그런데 우연히도 K군이 술을 먹엇다가 생각지 안이한＜135＞ 실수를 한 일이 잇섯슴니다. 그런 것도 運數라 할는지!

어느날 K군은 여러 동무들과 한가지로 문 박게에를 나아갓섯슴니다.
여러 날 일에 피곤한 몸이 문밧 깨끗한 공기 속에서 마음을 노코 놀게 되엿스니 상쾌햇슬 것은 不問可知임니다.

인제는 項羽라도 더 참을 수가 업시되엿슴니다. 그대로 배속에 들은 것이 와락 목구멍으로 넘어왓슴니다.
그런데-그런데 말임니다. 그때에 K군의 압헤 누구가 안젓겟슴닛가? 누구가 그 이바지를 바덧겟슴닛가? 얌전스럽게 생긴 여학생 한 분이엇슴니다.
