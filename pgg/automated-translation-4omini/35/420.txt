기사제목	消閒四篇-漢詩押韻 自由譯 (Four Leisure Poems - Free Translations of Chinese Poems)  
필자	金岸曙 (Kim An-so)  
기사형태	시 (Poem)  

梅花 (Plum Blossom)  
Snow fills the empty mountain, the moon brightens the city,  
I drink alone, reveling in the company of night.  
The lamp flickers, lonely and dim,  
On the plum tree, this year’s end is subtly marked.  
Original Poem: 雪滿空山月滿城, 獨吟獨酌到深更, 晝燈睡熟殘燈冷, 一樹梅花歲暮情.-尹秉緩 (Yun Byeong-hwan)  

詠梅 (Ode to the Plum)  
Oh Plum blossom, do not mourn the passing of the years,  
Your blossoms are just beginning to unfold.  
Since ancient times, the spring breeze has been just,  
In the mountain home, there is no lack of tranquility.  
Original Poem: 梅花休歎歲華垂, 正是梅花欲放時, 自有春風公道在, 山家未必到來遲.-金綺秀 (Kim Gi-su)  

對酒有感 (Reflections While Drinking)  
When drunk, the sorrow of this white hair becomes clear,  
Time flows like water, never stopping.  
Do the mountain birds know spring is upon us?  
Let the falling flowers be as they are, mournfully lingering.  
Original Poem: 對酒還憐白髮多, 年光如水不停波, 山島傷春春己暮, 百般啼奈落花何.-吳擎華 (Oh Gyeong-hwa)  

奉別西溪 (Farewell to West Creek)  
Every day, I open my door and wait alone,  
It is a delight that you have come and the swallows have arrived too.  
Tonight, if you depart once again,  
I will see the moon on the mountains and the platform.  
Original Poem: 城西日日小堂開, 之子來時燕子來, 送君今夜傷心處, 花滿春山月滿臺.-具鴻書 (Gu Hong-su)  

**Summary**  

This article presents a collection of four poems translated into Korean from classical Chinese, highlighting themes of the changing seasons, the ephemeral nature of life, and the beauty of plum blossoms. The translations reflect a longing for tranquility and connection to nature. The poets evoke a sense of nostalgia and introspection, resonating with the struggles of identity and cultural heritage during the colonial period. Historians may find this reflection of traditional aesthetics particularly compelling, as it underscores the cultural resilience of Koreans amidst colonial pressures.

消閒四篇-漢詩押韻 自由譯
						金岸曙
梅花
눈은 산에 달빗은 城에 찻슬제
잔잡고 흥어리니 밤도 깁헛네.
비인방에 등잔불 쓸쓸히 타고
梅花꼿엔 이 한해 날도 점으네.
原詩 雪滿空山月滿城, 獨吟獨酌到深更, 晝燈睡熟殘燈冷, 一樹梅花歲暮情.-尹秉緩＜176＞

詠梅
梅花야 해넘는다 탄식을 말게
그대의 꼿필 날은 이제부터리.
예로부터 봄바람 공평하것다.
山家라고 느즐理 만무할 걸세.
原詩 梅花休歎歲華垂, 正是梅花欲放時, 自有春風公道在, 山家未必到來遲.-金綺秀-

對酒有感
술 취하니 이 백발 서름이나네,
가는 세월 물이라 올길 업거니
산새의 우는 것을 봄야 알것가,
지는 꼿을 그대로 지랄 수 밧게.
原詩 對酒還憐白髮多, 年光如水不停波, 山島傷春春己暮, 百般啼奈落花何.-吳擎華-

奉別西溪
날마다 문을 열고 홀로 기둘제
반갑다 자네 오고 제비도 오네.
이날밤에 다시금 자네 떠나면
산엔끗 臺엔달을 눌과 나보리.
原詩 城西日日小堂開, 之子來時燕子來, 送君今夜傷心處, 花滿春山月滿臺.-具鴻書-＜177＞
