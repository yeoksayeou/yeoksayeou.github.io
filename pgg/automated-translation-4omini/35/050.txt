Smoking Room  

From the new school term, it has been said that all middle schools will introduce vocational subjects and that 13 vocational schools will be newly established throughout Joseon. It seems that the adage about the son of labor is quite fitting in this current time.

This winter, possibly due to a drastic drop in the price of wool goods or the indulgence of women’s vanity, the fashion of wearing wool clothing among women has become very popular. It is not surprising if wool (毛物) has come into vogue.

The new headquarters of the New Korean Congress imposed disciplinary measures on local members in Gyeongseong and the local branch also issued either expulsion or disciplinary measures against the executive committee members. In these times, the fundamental calls for dissolution mean that such actions are to be expected. What is it that they cannot do to each other?

In the New Year editions of various Joseon newspapers, remarks from authorities, which were rarely seen in previous years, were abundantly featured. Given the current situation, shouldn’t the words of the authorities be the most credible? For a discerning newspaper, that would indeed be a waste of time.

The Gyeongseong District Court's Prosecution Bureau has lost a significant complaint related to the so-called 900,000 won case. If they did not hang a ban on “newspaper reporters,” but instead hung a sign that read, “ban on document thieves,” such an issue would not arise.

Within the city of Gyeongseong, there are now already 37 Mahjong clubs, and the number of applications for licenses has reportedly reached over 100. In this era, are we just going to spend our time playing Mahjong or are we going to wish for more?

Even though rumors of dissolution have circulated since before, the Joseon Infantry Corps is set to disband at the end of February. Whether the presence of unnecessary flower pots matters to the general populace is inconsequential, but the thought of the real-life scenarios they may face without the flourishing flowers of May and June is truly heartbreaking.

Given that money is scarce at this time, it is natural for there to be a movement to reduce monthly tuition fees, but the police even prohibited that gathering. If that is the case, then they should announce strict regulations on the parents of students who cannot pay tuition and are expelled.

It has not been long since the solar New Year, but the lunar New Year is approaching, burdening those without money with an unbearable double hardship. This year’s tax season is incredibly harsh.

Whenever individuals run newspapers or magazines, even when they attend meetings of foreigners, they are referred to as representatives of the Joseon people. Is this how the people of Joseon are so cheapened?

While great consolidations of groups happen, such as the unification of Heaven's Dao, the Christian alliance, and the cooperative of fabric merchants, discussions of dissolution arise from groups like the New Korean Congress, the Kimyo Association, and the local New Youth League. Is this not a contradictory principle?

The Chungnam provincial office, embroiled in a struggle with one woman and four sons-in-law in locations like Gongju, Taejeon, and Cheongju, has finally been relocated to Taejeon. Ah—well, it can’t be helped; Taejeon is originally the gateway to Chungnam.

If a woman's complaint leads to a man being charged with the crime of adultery, this clause is said to have been discussed by the committee revising the Japanese criminal code. If this law is implemented, it would likely lead to a great panic on the part of males.＜19＞  

**Summary**  

This article highlights various contemporary social issues in 1931 Joseon, including changes in women’s fashion, educational reforms introducing vocational subjects, and public sentiment regarding economic hardships and shifting cultural norms. Of particular interest to historians is the reflection on the fragmentation of social groups and the emerging pressures on patriarchal structures in the context of colonial rule. The mention of a proposed law affecting men's legal responsibilities in adultery cases points to tensions in gender dynamics and colonial legal structures.

기사제목	吸煙室
기사형태	세태비평

吸煙室
新學期부터는 각 중등학교에 실업과목을 만하늣코 全조선에 13개 실업학교를 신설한다고 한다. 속담에 실업의 아들이란 말이 只今에 와서 꼭 맛는 모양인가.

今年 겨울에서 毛物갑이 너무 헐해 그런지 여자들의 사치심이 느러서 그런지 女子界에 毛物입는 風이 매우 유행한다. 모껄=毛껄이닛가 그도 容或無怪.

新幹會본부에서는 京城支會員에게 停權處分을 하고 同支會에서는 본부 집행위원에게 除名또는 停權處分을 하얏다. 근본적 解消論이 이러나는 이때에 그것쯤이야. 서로 못할 것은 무엇이잇다.

朝鮮文 各新聞의 新年號에는 예년에 못보던 당국자의 談이 滿載하얏다. 지금 형편에야 누구보다도 당국자의 말이 가장 유력한 터이닛가 怜悧한 신문사로서야 도로혀 晩時의 일이지.

京城地方法院 檢事局에서는 소위 90만원사건의 중대한 고소장을 紛失하얏다. 『新聞記者御斷り』라는 禁札을 붓치지 말고 『書類盜賊御斷り』라는 禁札을 붓첫더면 그런 일이 업섯겟지.

京城市內에는 벌서 麻雀俱樂部가 37개나 생기고 方今 허가 출원을 한 것도 1백여 건에 달한다고 한다. 이 시대에 잇서서  麻雀들만 하고 말터인가 麻雀乎望雀乎.

벌서부터 해산설을 전하던 朝鮮步兵隊는 期여히 2월말에 해산히기로 되엿다. 無用의 화초병이 잇스나 업스나 일반 민중에개는 하등 관계가 업는 것이지만은 眞所謂 五六月火爐도 업새면 섭섭하다고 생사를 회고하면 感慨無量.

돈이 귀한 이때에 月謝金 減下運動을 하는 것은 당연한 일이지만은 경찰은 그 집회까지 금지를 한다. 그럴터이면 無錢退學生學父兄取締規則을 발표하야 月謝金 못내고 퇴학 당하는 학생의 학부형들 엄중 取締하지.

陽曆설을 지낸 지가 얼마 안이 되는데 陰曆설이 또 닥쳐오니 돈업는 사람의 이중고통이야 참으로 형언 할 수 업다. 세도지난이 難於上靑天.

몃 개인이 신문잡지를 하야도 日민중의 대표기관, 외국인 집회에 참석하야도 日朝鮮民衆대표, 遊戱的집회를 하야도 日民衆大會라 하니 조선민중은 그럿케 歇價인가.

天道敎합동, 기독교합동, 布木商組合의 합동, 京仁取合同 등 天下大合同이 되는 반면에 신간회를 비롯하야 槿友會, 靑總, 지방의 新興靑盟 등 解消說이 擡頭하니 此是相反性原理乎.

一女四婿格으로 公州, 太田, 鳥致院, 天安 등지에서 쟁탈전을 하던 충남 도청은 그여히 太田으로 이전하케 되엿다. 암-그리되고 말고 太田이 원래 忠南진고개이지.
