投稿大歡迎  
Please send us your poetry, satires, short stories, plays, and other literary works. Any work is highly welcomed.  
Make sure to write "Submission Manuscript" in red on the envelope.  
Manuscripts that have been submitted will absolutely not be returned. <163>  

**Summary**  

This article expresses an open call for various forms of literary submissions, indicating a welcoming attitude towards contributions from any author. It reflects the cultural milieu of the time, where local literary expression was encouraged, particularly during a period of heightened nationalism and cultural identity amidst colonial rule. Historians may find interest in how such publications served as platforms for emerging voices in Korean literature, reflecting both creativity and resistance against the colonial situation.
