別에 別乾坤  
In 1931, a new type of glasses was born. They have a thin frame and legs and are made of very thin lenses that are placed right on the eyeball. Slightly adjusting the lenses doesn't even make them noticeable on the pupil... and the process of putting them on and taking them off only takes 12 seconds.

Artificial Aviators  
In England, artificial aviators have successfully completed a nighttime flight of 400 li. Now, wearers have to go out in their heavy coats, eat, and relieve themselves without any inconvenience, and the era of aviators who don’t fear falling to their death is not far off.

Alexander the Great's Golden Coffin  
In a world that even denies the existence of Alexander the Great, there is a scholar who claims he will excavate that crown. He is White Carter, who, like Robert Carter, discovered the tomb of the Egyptian king Tutankhamun. The next episode may be absurd, but it doesn't seem completely unrealistic.

The Plight of the Former German Crown Prince  
The former German crown prince has become the talk of the town for selling his famously hidden paintings. The said painting depicts the meeting between Wellington and Blücher at Waterloo, painted by "Angel." Initially, no one knew he had sold it, but once it was published in the inventory of a Dutch art dealer named Godstekka, it became known to the world.

From Justice Comes Order  
The story is a bit convoluted, but it has a detective-like flavor, so it is about 60-70% interesting. A friend named Burg who was doing car sales outside New York City committed suicide. However, there is a very peculiar reason behind the suicide. 

This friend was living with his wife while having an affair with a nineteen-year-old girl who lived nearby. They had even agreed to leave their respective families and run away together. Yet, he suddenly committed suicide. The method was very meticulous; after immersing himself in a bathtub filled with water, he first ingested poison, then stabbed himself, and finally strangled himself with a tie. 

If the story ended there, it wouldn't have been particularly sensational, but a few hours before his death, he had brought the waitress from his regular restaurant to his home. He had intended to kill her with a single blow to the back of the head, but somehow she managed to escape. 

The friend had a face strikingly similar to that of the waitress, almost like twins. So he planned to kill the person who looked exactly like him and pretend to commit suicide, leaving that girl to become a corpse in his place. This plan seemed to falter, so fearing that he would end up eating prison "rice" (serving time), he despaired and decided to commit suicide.＜149＞  

**Summary**  

The article reflects on various oddities and events of 1931, focusing on advances in technology, archaeological claims, artworks, and a dramatic personal tragedy. Of note is the portrayal of changing societal norms, such as the acceptance of artificial aviators and the tragic consequences of infidelity. Historians might find the discussion of the former German crown prince's sale of paintings indicative of the socio-economic climate of the time, showcasing the impact of World War I and transitions in nobility and their treasures during colonial pressures in Europe.

別에 別乾坤
1931년式
1931년식 안경이 생겨낫다. 그것은 테도업고 다리도 업는 아주 대창가치 얇은 유리알-그놈을 눈뚜덩을 떠들고 눈동자에다 씨어놋는 것이다. 약간해서 눈동자에 유리 한겹이 씨워진 것도 보이지 아니하고......그리고 끼엿다 빼엿다 하는 수속은 12초.

人造飛行士
영국에서는 인조비행사를 식혀 4백리의 야간비행에 성공하엿다. 압흐로는 치워서 털옷을 입고 현긔가 나고 먹어야하고 대소변을 보아야 할 불편도 업고 또 떠러저 죽을 염녀가 업는 비행사시대가 不遠할 뜻.

알렉산데 大帝의 黃金棺
알렉산더대제의 존재조차 부인하려는 세상에 그 금관을 파내이겟다고 장담하는 학자가 잇다. 로버-트·카-너본과 한가지로 애급의 쓰-턴커-덴왕의 묘를 발견한 화이트·카터-가 즉 그다. 下回가 엇더할는지 모르나 그다지 허황한 소문도 아닌듯은 하다.

前獨皇太子의 窮狀
前 독일황태자는 그 秘藏해둔 유명한 그림을 팔은 것이 말이 퍼저서 한참이야기거리가 되어잇다. 그 그림이라는 것은 웰링톤과 부류켈 두사람이 와텔로-에서 회견한 것을 『엔젤』이 그린 것이다. 팔어먹은 줄을 처음에는 아모도 몰낫는데 和蘭의 美術品商 고-도스텍카-라는 사라므이 賣品목록에 뚜럿이 발표가 되면서부터 일은 세상이 알게 되엿다.

事必歸正
이야기는 좀 지리하나 약간 探偵味가 잇서 자미가 6,70％는 된다. 미국 뉴-욕시 외에서 자동차 장사를 하고 잇는 뿌로크라는 친구가 자살을 하엿다. 그런데 자살을 한데는 아주 신통한 원인이 잇다.
친구는 마누라와 가치 살면서 근처에 사는 열아홉살 먹은 게집애와 연애를 하엿다. 오래쟌허 집과 마누라를 바리고 두리서 다러나기로 약속까지 하고 잇섯다. 그런데 갑작히 자살을 하엿다. 방법은 아주 周到해서 넘실넘실이 물이 담긴 목욕통 압헤 안저 처음에는 독약을 먹고 목을 찔으고 마즈막에는 넥타이로 목을 졸나매엿다. 이야기가 여기까지만이면 별로 신통할 것이 업지만 친구는 그날 자살하기 멧 시간전에 그가 단골로 다니는 식당의 뽀이를 데리고자기 집으로 왓다. 그래서 맛치로 뒤통수를 한번 갈겨 죽이려고 한 것을 뽀이는 어터케 해서 虎口를 버서저 나왓다. 친구는 원래 그 뽀이와 얼골이 쌍둥이 이상으로 꼭 가텃다나. 그래 자기와 얼골이 꼭 가튼 사람을 죽여노코 자기가 자살한 체하고 저는 정돈 게집과 산송장이 되어 揷十六計를 하랴한 것닌듯하다. 그린 것이 계획이 실패가 되니까. 오래쟌허 실안미수죄로 콩밥을 먹게 될 것이 두려워서 「에-라」하고 내친거름에 자살을 해버린 것이라고.＜149＞
