卷頭言  
According to the news reported last month by the Nanjing correspondent, based on the plans for Manchuria and Mongolia in Northeast China, the strategy for utilizing foreign capital is progressing steadily, as the capital entities of the major powers are competing for various investments.  

Among these, the establishment of a loan contract worth 20 million yuan between the French Aeronautics Society and the Northeast Government, the advancement of the road construction and vehicle supply contracts with the American General Motors Company, the successful negotiations for the establishment of a bank by British capitalists in Fengtian, and the progress of negotiations for the management of the north-south Manchurian transport route are significant factors. It is a well-known fact that Japan has already established a solid power focusing on the South Manchurian Railway.  

The aforementioned matters represent the new expansion of the major powers into Manchuria, and their influence in mainland China has already been firmly established for a long time. In other words, China, this massive lump of flesh, serves as a nurturing substance for the capitalist powers, while simultaneously providing ample opportunity for them to suck out its sweet juice.  

Despite being thoroughly exploited as a class by the great powers on a national level, the Chinese people are still clamoring for superficial claims of rights, such as the reclamation of concessions and the abolition of extraterritoriality. This reflects the lax attitude of the 'National Government,' which is the representative of the emerging bourgeoisie, displaying hollow posturing.

  

**Summary**  

This article discusses the encroachment of foreign powers into China, particularly in Manchuria, focusing on financial investments and negotiations involving various nations. It points out the exploitation of the Chinese populace by these powers while they demand superficial rights that do not address their deeper issues. Historians may find it interesting how this perspective critiques the nationalistic movements of the time, revealing a significant socio-political commentary on colonial dynamics and the struggles of the Chinese populace against foreign domination.

卷頭言
中國의 虛勢
月前新聞이 보도된 南京통신에 依하건대 中國 東北側의 滿蒙計劃에 基한 外資利用策이 착착진행되어야 세계열강의 자본단은 각종 투자에 先을 다투는 중이라 한다.
그 중 佛國航空社會와 東北政權 간에 이천만원의 借款계약성립 미국 제너랄·모터스 회사의 道路修築及車臺공급계약의 진행 영국의 자본단의 奉天에 은행설립 교섭의 성공과 남북 滿洲貫通항로경영 교섭의 진행 등이 그 중요한 자이며 일본은 滿鐵을 위시하야 이미 確乎한 세력을 扶植하고 잇는 것은 주지의 사실이다.

이상은 열강 자본의 滿蒙방면의 새로운 진출이요 중국본토에 잇서서는 그 세력이 확립된 지 이미 오랫다. 즉 중국 이러하는 일개의 거대한 肉塊는 자본주의 열강의 한 조흔 滋養物이며 동시에 압흐로 그 甘汁을 빨아먹을 여지가 상당히 잇다하는 것을 볼 수가 잇는 것이다.

이와 가티 중국국민은 全민족적으로 열강의 被搾取階級化하여 잇슴에도 불구하고 皮相的 權利主張인 租界 回收니 치외법권 철폐이니 하고 떠드니 이것이 新興뿌르죠아지의 대변자인 『國民政府』의 虛張聲勢가 안일넌지.＜1＞
