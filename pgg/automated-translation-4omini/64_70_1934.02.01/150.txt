To Our Readers in the Region  
The magazine Byeolgeongon has received overwhelmingly positive feedback after its systems and content were renewed, resulting in an increase in readership by about double. Thus, we are dedicating more effort to the development of our magazine and striving to create more reader-oriented content that is friendlier to everyone. Starting from the March issue, we will also establish a separate regional section to feature communications from our readers, encouraging our readers in the regions to actively contribute, thus making this section your own while also utilizing our modest efforts gratefully.  

Any article concerning the region is welcome. Please write and send it to us.  

**Summary**  

The article communicates the successful revitalization of the magazine and an increase in its readership, particularly emphasizing the magazine's commitment to becoming more reader-friendly and inclusive. It calls for contributions from regional readers to create a dedicated section, reflecting a move towards greater engagement with its audience. This is interesting from a historical perspective as it highlights the ways publications sought to connect with local audiences during the colonial period, possibly serving as an avenue for regional voices amidst the suppressive cultural environment of colonial Korea.

地方讀者께
						編輯部白
별건곤은  체제와 내용을 일신하게 곳친 후 그 호평이 대단하야 독자도 약 배 이상이나 늘엇습니다. 그리하야 본지의 발전과 아울너 좀더 여러분 독자와 친하고 또는 여러분 본위의 책을 만들려 정성을 다하고 잇습니다. 그리하야 오는 3월호부터는 또한 地方欄을 따로 두고 여러분 독자의 통신을 바더 실기로 한바 地方에 게신 독자 여러분은 만히 분발하셔서 이 난을 여러분의 것을 만드는 동시에 본지의 적은 정성이나마 고맙게 리용하여 주시기 바랍니다.
무엇이나 그 地方에 관한 기사이면 그만입니다. 만히 적어 보내 주십시요.＜25＞
