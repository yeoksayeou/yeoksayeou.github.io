Accident

Starting last month, we have decided to establish a new column titled "Reader's Club" in order to maintain closer and more intimate communication with you. The content will be entirely based on your impressions after reading books, suggestions on what we should publish, or general inquiries, all of which we intend to handle in a friendly and sincere manner. We will strive to ensure that this column provides you with significant convenience and benefit, so we kindly ask you to contribute significantly and allow this column to be rewarding. It would be appreciated if you could write your comments or inquiries in as concise a manner as possible. (Editorial Department White) <7>

  
**Summary**

This article outlines the launch of a "Reader's Club" section in the magazine, aimed at fostering closer interaction between the editorial team and its readers by inviting them to share thoughts, feedback, and inquiries. The effort reflects a desire to engage the audience and enhance community participation in literary discussions. From the perspective of Korean colonial history, this initiative might indicate a shift towards more participatory forms of media, enabling expression among readers during a time of intense political repression and cultural change, offering scholars insight into the evolving relationship between literature and society in colonial Korea.

사고
지난달부터 본지는 여러분과 좀더 친하고 좀더 긴밀한 련락을 취하기 위하야 『讀者俱樂部』라는 새로운 난을 넛키로 하엿슴니다. 내용은 순전히 여러분이 책을 읽으신 후의 소감과 또는 어떠케 하여 주엇스면 좃켓다든지 누구의 글을 실어 달나든지 일반 질의를 바더 가장 친절하고 또 정녕하게 취급하여 드릴 작정임니다. 아모쪼록 이 난을 통하야 여러분에게 크듸큰 편의와 이익이 잇도록 노력할 것이니 만히 투고하여 주셔서 이 난으로 하여금 특별한 보람이 잇도록 하여 주시기 바람니다. 뭇는 말슴이나 참고의 말슴을 되도록 간단하게 적어 보내주시면 고맙겟습니다.(編輯部白)＜7＞
