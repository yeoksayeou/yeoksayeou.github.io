기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)  

The first selected entries for the "New Popular Songs" (新流行小曲), which started collecting submissions after being announced in the last issue, reached a total of 250 entries. Among them, the two pieces listed below have been selected as winners. The lyrics and detailed stories will be included in the next issue, so please wait.

Selected Entries: (임자 업는 나루배 : 京城 金鍾漢, 나도 몰라요! : 開城 高馬夫)＜51＞

  

**Summary**  

This article announces the results of a popular song contest, highlighting two winning entries from a large pool of submissions. It reflects the cultural engagement and artistic expression during the colonial period in Korea and shows how public participation in the arts was encouraged, potentially as a means of fostering a sense of identity under colonial rule. Historians may find the list of winners significant to understanding popular sentiment or cultural trends of the era.
