기사제목	科學, 떡과 밥은 엇더케 다른가 (Science, How Are Rice Cakes and Cooked Rice Different?)  
필자	三山人  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

Dear readers! These days during the lunar calendar, the opportunities to eat rice cakes are fewer than at other times. So how are rice cakes and the cooked rice we eat on a daily basis different? Let me explain that to you. Whether it is rice cakes or cooked rice, the components they contain—namely carbohydrates (함수탄소), crude fat (조지방), crude ash (조회분), total nitrogen ( 총질소), total caloric value (총렬량)—are almost the same, although rice cakes may contain relatively lower quantities of said components. Specifically, despite some variations in types, rice cakes generally have about double the amount of components found in cooked rice. This is the reason why, after eating rice cakes, one does not feel like eating rice anymore, and after eating rice, one may not crave rice cakes.

However, if one eats too many rice cakes, digestion can become difficult, and the stomach may be adversely affected. Therefore, it is necessary to ensure that after consuming rice cakes, one also eats fruits or other refreshing items to replenish vitamins (you may ask elders or teachers about this).

  

**Summary**  

This article examines the nutritional differences between rice cakes and cooked rice, noting that while both foods share similar components, rice cakes are denser in calories and nutrients, leading to quicker satiety. The author warns of potential digestive issues associated with overconsumption of rice cakes and suggests balancing them with fruits for proper nutrition. Historically, this discussion reflects the culinary and dietary habits of Koreans in the 1930s, shedding light on food practices during a time of colonial rule, which might interest historians studying Korean food culture and health practices during this period.

科學, 떡과 밥은 엇더케 다른가
						三山人
여러분! 요즘은 음력이라 하야 다른 때보다 떡을 먹을 긔회가 만치 안습니까?
그럼 그 떡과 우리가 일상 먹는 밥과 엇터케 다른지! 그것을 이야기 해 드리겟습니다. 떡이든지 밥이든지 그 속에 포함해 잇는 성분-즉 함수탄소(含水炭素), 조지방(粗脂肪), 조회분(粗灰分), 총질소(總窒素), 총렬량(總熱量) 등은 거의 비등하나 떡은 량이 적어도 밥보다는 그 성분이 훨신 만흔 편입니다.
즉 보통으로 종류가 좀 다르다고 하드라도 떡은 거의 죄다가 밥의 약 2배에 상당한 성분이 잇습니다. 그럿키 때문에 떡을 실컨 먹으면 밥 생각이 업고 또 밥을 대해도 먹고 십지도 안은 것이 그 까닭입니다.
그러나 떡은 만히 먹으면 소화가 잘 안 되야 위장을 상하기가 쉽습니다. 그러니까 되도록 떡 먹은 후에는 반듯이 과일이나 기타 청신한 것을 먹어 비타민(이것은 웃어른이나 선생께 물어 보십지요)의 보충을 하는 것이 필요합니다.＜42＞
