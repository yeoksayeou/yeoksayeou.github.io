10th Anniversary Commemoration Plan of Kaebuksa  

1. Establishment of New Sister Enterprises  
Currently, this is a matter that urgently requires attention, and it has not yet been abandoned; we will take the lead in initiating the new venture.  

2. Dispatching Readers Abroad  
Through the three magazines, we will collect essays and select one or two individuals to send abroad to observe emerging cities.  

3. Expansion of Publications  
In addition to the three magazines currently published, we plan to launch one new magazine to advance to four major publications.  

4. Hosting Commemorative Exhibitions  
In addition to holding the anniversary gathering twice this year, we will specifically hold a major exhibition that will be beneficial to the public.  

5. Organization of National Reader Groups  
We aim to organize reader groups for 'Students' and 'Children' throughout all of Korea to foster a closer bond.  

6. Establishment of Traveling Lecture Groups  
We will organize traveling lecture groups composed of our staff and associates to visit various regions, focusing on local cultural development with the reader groups.  

7. Establishment of a New Agency Department  
With over 200,000 readers, we plan to establish a large agency department based on a cooperative consumption model to improve practical living conveniences.  

8. Commemorative Publication: Popular History of Korea  
As part of the 10th anniversary publications, this is a book written by Cha Sang-jin that presents modern Korean history in the most accessible manner.  

9. Commemorative Publication: The Source of Civilization  
For high school and youth readers, we will distribute a 10th-anniversary gift book translated by Ko Yeong-hwan that details the path of human culture.  

10. Commemorative Publication: Popular Parenting Talks  
This is a long-awaited parenting book by Bang Jeong-hwan, which has suffered in various households! I assert that it is an essential book for every family.  

We, at Kaebuksa, that mark our 10th anniversary this New Year, have not only significantly featured the New Year edition of the three magazines but also added an impressive appendix. Again, we have established the above ten plans, and preparations are steadily advancing. <108>  

We sincerely wish for the support of our fellow members and readers! It is entirely thanks to the readers’ support that we have been able to endure for ten years with our limited strength, and we cannot express our gratitude enough while hoping for even stronger encouragement as we embark on our second decade. <109>  

<108-109>  

**Summary**  

This article outlines the initiatives planned by Kaebuksa to commemorate its 10th anniversary, focusing on new business ventures, reader engagement, and cultural contributions. Notably, the plans reflect an intent to enhance Korean literacy and cultural awareness during a period of colonial rule. Historians might find this emphasis on self-education and cultural progress significant, revealing a response to the limitations faced under Japanese colonial governance.

開闢社創社十周年紀念計畫
1 新姉妹事業創設
目下最新緊急을 要하는 일이면서 아즉지니저버려지고잇는 新事業에 率先着手함니다
2 讀者外國派遣
三雜誌를 通하야 論文募集의 方式으로 一人或二人을 選出하야 外國新興都市를 視察케함
3 三雜誌一種增判
現在發行하는 三雜誌外에 한가지 새 雜誌를 刊行하야 四大雜誌로 奮進하려함니다
4 紀念展覽會開催
十週年紀念集會를 今年中一年두고 每月開催하는 外에 特히 民衆에게 有益할 大展覽會開催
5 全國讀者會組織
『學生』과『어린이』의 讀者會를 全朝鮮的으로 組織하야 一層緊密한 關係를 지여나아가려는것임니다
6 巡廻講演班組織
本社社員及囑托中으로서 巡廻講演班을 組織하야 隨時地方에 出張하야 讀者會中心으로 地方文化向上에 努力
7 本社代理部新設
二十餘萬讀者와 더부러 消費組合式의 一大代理部를 設置하야 實生活의 便宜를 圖하기로함
8 紀念出版 通俗朝鮮史話
十週年紀念出版其一, 아모나 가장 쉽게 알수잇게 著述한 近代朝鮮史車相瓚氏著
9 紀念出版 「文明의 淵源」
學生級靑年級을 向해보내는 十週年紀念선물노 人類文化의 길을 細術한 高永煥氏譯著
10 紀念出版 通俗育兒講話
地方마다 집집마다에서 苦待한지 오래인 方定煥氏의 育兒講話!! 萬家必備할 著書임을 斷言한다

一騎當千 奮戰奮戰 十一年의 새길
이번 新年으로 十週年을 마지하는 開闢社는 우선 三雜誌新年號를 大大特輯한 外에 宏壯한 附錄지 添附하엿거니와 다시  上記十大計劃을 세워 그 準備가 着着進行되고 잇슴니다.＜108＞

社友諸氐 讀者諸位의 後援을 切願!!
