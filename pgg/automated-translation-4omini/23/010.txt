10th Anniversary of the Publication  
The New Year. This New Year marks the 10th anniversary of the Gaebyeoksa (開闢社). With over 530 branches and subsidiaries both domestically and abroad, and 210,000 readers across the nation, we are excited to celebrate this 10th anniversary together.

As the saying goes, ten years is like an era, yet the past decade for Korea has been tossed by wild winds and storms. How much has Gaebyeoksa endured amidst these tribulations? The suffering of the Korean people over the last ten years is incomparable; the hardships faced by Gaebyeoksa are also immeasurable.

As the winds and frost of Korea have swept through these ten years, there seems to be no parallel in history. Our solitary struggle through this decade feels as if it has endured thirty or fifty years of hardship.

Oh, what words can express the emotions and excitement we feel on this day? Even after ten years of fierce battles, we still lack the freedom to wield this pen, so what can we say? We can only acknowledge that the road ahead will be fraught with peril.

However, the only thing I can say is that despite the scars of ten years of suffering, we have managed to illuminate a path together with the Korean people, and with tens of thousands of readers, we boldly take our steps into the eleventh year, overflowing with excitement.

Thus, as Gaebyeoksa joyfully concludes its 10th anniversary, my feelings towards the spirits of the deceased contributors who fought alongside us are deeply heartfelt and sincere. At the same time, it brings to mind our shared resolve to continue fighting along this difficult path. 

**Summary**  

This article celebrates the 10th anniversary of the Gaebyeoksa, reflecting on the struggles and hardships faced by both the magazine and the Korean people during this turbulent decade. It emphasizes the perseverance and determination to continue contributing to Korean society despite adverse conditions. Historians may find interest in how the publication framed its identity and resilience during Japanese colonial rule, highlighting the role of media in Korean cultural and social life during this period.

기사형태	잡저

創刊 十週年
새해. 이 새해로써 開闢社는 創社 十週年을 마지한다. 海內 海外 五百三十餘의 支社 分社와 天下 二十一萬의 讀者와 더브러 오늘 十週年에 새해를 마지한다.

俗言에도 십 년은 한 옛날이라 하거니와 朝鮮의 십 년이 終始 狂風暴雨에 飜弄되엿나니 開闢社의 마즌 비 그 얼마엿스며 朝鮮 사람 십 년의 受難이 그 比할 바가 업섯나니 開闢社의 바든 苦難이 그 얼마엿는가.

朝鮮의 십 년 風霜이 일즉이 史上에 달리 類例가 업섯는지라 其間에 외로히 싸와 온 우리의 십 년이 실로 삼십 년 오십 년의 困境을 지나온 感을 가지게 되는 것이다.

아아 무슨 文字로써 이 날의 우리 感懷와 感激을 表할 것이랴. 십 년 力戰 그러고도 오히려 이 날 이 붓을 달닐 自由가 업스니 무슨 할 말이 잇스랴. 오즉 將次 갈 압길이 또한 險峻할 것을 알 뿐이다.

다만 한 가지 십 년 苦難에 節介를 더럽힘 업시 朝鮮과 더부러 朝鮮 사람과 더부러 한길을 밝혀올 수 잇섯슴과 오늘 수십 만의 讀者와 함께 십일 년째의 새길을 씩씩하게 내딋는 感激에 넘치는 깃붐이 잇슬 뿐이다.

끗흐로 開闢社가 호을로 十週年을 마지함에 當하야 開闢志의 죽은 나히를 혜이는 情이 懇切함과 同時에 함께 또 뒤따라 갓흔 뜻으로 나서서 奮戰 途中에 戰沒한 수만흔 同業諸誌를 생각함이 그윽히 懇切하다.＜1＞
