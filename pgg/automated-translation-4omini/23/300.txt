Great Satirical Techniques for Social Success  

The Secret to Becoming a Literarian  

Oh, literarians! Just hearing that brings a romantic resonance to the ears with an alluring harmony, echoing a musical value. Literarians! Literary figures! The objects of admiration for literary youth, the authorities of the world of romance... ... The secret to becoming a literarian with a rich meaning of life... ... 

As I describe this, it may sound incredibly difficult, but in reality, it is merely lacking in shame and going at it boldly.  

If you, gentlemen! Or you, ladies! If you want to become a literarian or a literary woman, then faithfully follow this path:  

First, buy a thousand sheets of manuscript paper and then buy five thousand stamps, writing and submitting them indiscriminately to newspapers and magazines. In writing poetry, it's enough to say “Oh! It is raining,” “Ah! There is a piercing sound,” “Oh! The leaves are falling,” “Ah! My heart feels heavy,” “Oh! Life is desolate,” “Ah! My beloved!” – these would suffice. In novels, you need not write anything more than just a concept or a brief description.  

Secondly, do not specialize in just one thing. Poets, novelists, dramatists, screenplay writers, critiques, translations of foreign works... ... 

If you persist in these two practices for a couple of months, you will have already become 50% of a literarian. At that time, you should form friendships with magazine staff, casually insert your name in the section titled “Authors contributing to this magazine” within their announcements. Otherwise, you may ask a literarian to feast on a plate of sweet and sour pork and request that they write about you in the newspaper's section titled “Impressions of a Literarian.” Then, you will have truly completed the transformation into a 100% literarian.  

However, this is all referring to artificial literarians. To genuinely become a literarian, one must possess the observant eyes of a scientist, the thoughtful mind of a philosopher, and the expressive techniques of an artist. 

This requires 100% study and 100% talent.  

The Secret to Becoming Beautiful  

To all the ordinary women who endure the bitter resentment of broken mirrors and live a gloomy life night and day! In modern times, your worries are merely unfounded fears.  

You should go out to the Han River, perform a suicide drama that won’t catch a cold, and the next day you will be reported as a beautiful suicide! Can’t a woman become a social beauty, at a time when a packet of powder costs 10 yang and a streetcar fare is 5 yang?  

And if you cut your hair, you become a short-haired beauty, if you wear Western clothes, a Western-style beauty; if you run away, a fugitive beauty... ... All of these are very modern!  

But if you want more difficult methods to become beautiful, then first, sleep well, eat well, and ensure proper digestion (drink a cup of cold water three times daily). Second, eat vegetables for breakfast. Third, do not skip baths. Fourth, regularly have massages. Fifth, wash your eyes well with water. Sixth, sleep with a bit of powder on. Seventh, keep your nails clean. Eighth, tie your hair up nicely looking in the mirror for five minutes. Ninth, apply light makeup that is only 70-80% heavy. Tenth, express your feelings in your facial expressions. Eleventh, make your voice gentle. Twelfth, pay attention to the fabric as well as color of your clothes. Thirteenth, wear clothes that suit your body. Fourteenth, remember to have a modest gait. Fifteenth, pay attention to your posture. Sixteenth, take care of your hands.  

The Secret to Becoming a Famous Reporter  

A renowned reporter, a celebrated reporter, a star reporter… just thinking about it makes my legs tremble with excitement. Especially in Korea, the authority of a journalist is genuinely tremendous; who wouldn't want to become one with youthful passion?  

Writing exciting articles that make even ghosts cry or taking on a challenge without fear of ridicule… ... Anyway? Do you want to try it too? Then I will reveal the secrets.  

First, go to a newspaper that doesn't pay wages and obtain a nameplate for two months with a sob story; use that to roam around and engage in networking, enjoying sumptuous meals, bribing reporters, and submitting articles to various newspapers... ... 

However, if you're not talented, there are also paths. Be diligent (write articles with thought rather than verbally), be nimble, research the experiences of others, handle articles impartially... ... 

The Secret to Winning at Romance 

Oscar Wilde, in the beginning of his work “The Model Millionaire”, states, “Romance is the privilege of the rich, not the profession of the unemployed.” 

But that is a complete lie. Can one not romance without money? 

Even if you hear your stomach growl and share a house with a landlady who begrudges food, don't worry! While it's not ideal to have winter clothing when you can only afford spring outfits, you can still attend school concerts while accompanying tapestry and stamps to craft a connection or flirting with your previous crush... 

However, if you genuinely desire to pursue romance honestly, several essential conditions must be met:  

First, you must be in a mutual romantic feeling (unrequited love won't do). Second, do not act until passion is fully ignited. Third, love must grow amidst challenges. Fourth, there should be no rivals in sight. Fifth, keep your soul noble. Sixth, love must be clandestine. Seventh, there should be few words exchanged. Eighth, love must abstain from physical relations. Ninth, love should not be pursued through sorrow or coercion. Tenth, engage in love that can lead to marriage. Eleventh, do not be seduced by beauty alone... ... Such love can only truly succeed.  

The Secret to Amassing Wealth  

Money! Money! For those who find it difficult to save or lack money, do lend me your ears!  

The world is no longer suitable for the miserly and their grasping of public funds, but instead, if you have an ancestral asset, use it for a mortgage and head to Incheon to participate in trade, or pay close attention as you walk down the street. Carrying a hammer at your hip and heading deep into the mountain to explore can also lead to wealth. It is also recommended to seize a fortune through inheritance or becoming a board member of a newspaper if there's a sudden lack of heirs.  

If such heavenly fortune is unavailable, spend 70% of your total income as your total expenses. (However, discussions about income deficiencies in specific areas are a separate topic).  

The Secret to Maintaining Affection in Marriage  

In a person's lifetime, the most intimate relationship is that of a husband and wife. They are one body. As they spend their lives together, it is fundamental that their affection is deep and their relationship is harmonious, hence their practical life should reflect this.  

However, there are exceptional cases that can be quite tragic. 

To revive the sweet relationship between couples, I introduce a number of techniques that can inject sweetness into the lives of unfortunate couples.  

It’s favorable for the man to meet his wife coming back from a cooking class while the wife returns from music lessons in the alley. It’s good for the man to buy a cape when financial status allows or even praise the wife for her appearance.  

It should also be noted that bringing up the past can be detrimental and lead to divorce. But ensuring a harmonious marriage, the following techniques are essential:  

The husband must, firstly, not love the wife too much. Secondly, he should not spy on her while she is getting ready. Thirdly, he should honestly compliment her new clothes. Fourthly, arguments should only happen once a month and should be resolved within five minutes post-conflict. Fifth, express appreciation for a good stew occasionally. Sixth, do not be overly kind to the wife's family. Seventh, do not coldly treat your wife's relatives. Eighth, take the wife for a walk when she seems upset. Ninth, be especially warm when she is sick. Tenth, do not disregard the wife's cleanliness. Eleventh, uphold the husband’s dignity. Twelfth, love from the heart.  

As for the wife: First, she should understand humor. Second, she should treat her husband like a child to some degree. Third, don’t just be a good wife, but a loving wife. Fourth, do not love children more than your husband. Fifth, maintain a bit of jealousy. Sixth, do not argue with your husband. Seventh, do not go out when the husband is angry. Eighth, do not praise other men in front of him. Ninth, do not demand direct favors from your husband. Tenth, do not carelessly reveal your husband’s secrets. Eleventh, cultivate a shared interest. Twelfth, do not outspend your husband. Thirteenth, sincerely love your husband.  

The Secret to Becoming a Youth Leader  

Once upon a time - there was a princely figure... ... and there was a strange young person. If you were to write a nursery rhyme that doesn’t make sense and ends with “The moon feels merry and the stars don’t sleep...” that itself would turn you into a youth leader (of course becoming a writer of children’s stories).  

Going a step further, if you possess some small change, you can lead a youth magazine, filling it with romantic stories to extract money from the naive kids. 

Gentlemen, do you not wish to try? If you really want to, I will genuinely provide the assistance required.  

When dealing with children, there is no responsibility greater than this. If done wrongly, one may easily plant bad habits in their young minds. Therefore, one must take up the role of a wise guardian while also maintaining an innocent heart towards them.  

The Secret to Becoming a New Woman  

New Woman! C’mon! Having lived in imprisonment for thousands of years, you’ve now entered a bright world where everything is vibrant and all is freedom. Why should you fear anyone or hold back?  

Chastity? Tch – where does such an old notion still exist? Is there a law against a single woman living life with all her might?  

Now, tonight, I am ready to passionately deliver my speech titled “The Social Examination of Women’s Issues.”  

Is the modern woman anything? She is the New Woman.  

Yet, there is a façade of so-called emancipated women who defend absolute freedom while being only children in the eyes of younger girls. They continue to wander in the shadows of a past they have supposedly overcome. 

These women are mere mannequins of transition. They present confusion to those who genuinely seek true female emancipation.  

One must have a firm belief and understanding equivalent to that of a newspaper. Can there be absolutes in any truth, let alone female liberation? Are we to build a nation on the Moon where only women exist?  

Dressed in miniskirts to show off their bodies emulates the streets of the West, yet high heels still restrict their freedom of movement.  

The Secret to Becoming a Doctorate  

The method to become a doctorate - the secret – is surprisingly simple.  

You have graduated from elementary school, attended six middle schools over three years, been somewhat involved in food and romantic pursuits. Then go abroad to the Pacific or the Atlantic, where you must write documents for high amounts of money and become friendly with prostitution.  

If you return with lots of books that you’ve only pretended to read and claim to have earned a degree, producing articles that appear in newspapers saying “X country’s X doctoral candidate XXX returns home,” you have essentially become a doctorate without anyone being able to dispute it. 

However, genuine scholars have often spent many years wrestling with a microscope as well as ancient texts, and the prestigious academic title that is hard-earned through genuine effort is something you must not forget.  

The Secret to Becoming a Corporate Executive  

Becoming an executive is as easy as drinking water, so long as you hold a cash of 10,000 won or so, live in a rented house, ride a private vehicle, and keep subordinates who you can take out to eat frequently.  

Yet if you are someone who has ambition and cares for the core of the business, such lofty dreams must be carefully thought through.  

Those who follow the previously mentioned path may end up a mere puppet executive, exploited as a tool of the company.  

The Secret to Becoming a Celebrity  

To become a celebrity! There's a very easy method. Make just one journey overseas – particularly to the West or China. Even if internally your actions amount to merely breaking seeds, announce loudly to the society that you are serving.   

Become a regular at banquets and mingles and keep close contacts within the media so that you can be featured on occasions, volunteering to host farewell or welcoming parties.  

Acquiring at least a director or executive title would be very beneficial. This way, even in the face of any wrongdoing, you will not be scorned.  

This is the only method to create a false celebrity. However, it is essential to be aware that true celebrities cannot be manufactured simply like this.  

If you wish to be recognized as a celebrity, you need to genuinely work for society, and then the world will naturally recognize you in that role.  

The Secret to Preventing a Husband from Taking a Concubine  

If your husband has taken a concubine, follow this advice and do not act out like a strong robber. If you cause a mess, you may even lose your life, and if you stage a suicide drama, you’re likely to waste your effort on sorrow.  

But enduring duty requires that you navigate through it when facing resentment. If affection has faded and hope for the couple's relationship has diminished, it is wise to separate quietly.  

However, if your husband’s disloyalty was a temporary folly, it requires careful consideration and calm action.  

Aside from specific exceptions, your husband's infidelity typically hints at a lack of satisfaction at home. Understanding this deficiency is crucial for rekindling excitement in domestic life while keeping your husband focused on loyalty towards you.  

The specific details can be found in the earlier advice on maintaining harmony in marriage.  

The Secret to Becoming a Socialist  

If you wish to become a socialist, extraordinary means are needed!  

First, become accustomed to referring to others as “our proletarians” or simply “comrades” and carry two pamphlets with you at all times. Make certain to display pictures of Marx, Engels, and Lenin on the wall. Within your comrades, one must engage collectively.  

However, to genuinely embody socialism, one requires thorough understanding and courageous committed action, along with a clear class consciousness.  

The Secret to Running a Magazine  

You’ll find that educational background, ideology, or experience are not necessary; you mainly need some money and a bit of free time.  

Inquire amongst those interested to secure about 3,000 won, find a small house, place a huge sign outside, and assume the title of Chief Editor of a significant academic or mass magazine while sending out flyers to local influential individuals to pay in advance for subscriptions or advertisements.  

In your inaugural issue, include your photograph or a statement on founding the magazine. One must also expect that within one or two issues your endeavor may reach an end.  

However, those are not the true secrets to running a magazine. The genuine secret is that more than just money, it requires unwavering effort and knowledge pertinent to the publication.  

The Secret to Overcoming Unrequited Love  

Unrequited love! Oh, the bitterness of unrequited love! To all the young gentlemen and ladies enduring that pain, hear my words well.  

Drinking alcohol to oblivion isn’t the answer nor is holding onto an ex-lover’s photograph while dreaming of the past.  

Finding a quiet space to read past letters and crying does not address the truth.  

But if the pain is too much, eat a portion of tranquilizers and fall ill to require hospitalization.  

However, that only leads to a condition after a century has passed; surely you, who are walking the forefront of modernity, cannot allow this to continue... ... 

Thirty minutes after the break-up, secure a new partner and walk back to your previous lover's home hand in hand. 

How refreshing this seems!  

Romance must be recognized as a full-fledged aspect of life; hence, all tragedies (while the youth may cry, it is in fact a comedy) are a part of living.  

Sentimentalism has long been dispelled. Anyone who weeps about sentimentality is nothing more than an aged soul in the body of a vibrant youth.  

Romance rooted in sentimentality is excessive and theatrical. It should be done clearly and, if necessary, ended quickly – only then does one find the ability to engage in romance. Do not model yourself on the tales of old!  

 

**Summary**  

This article humorously outlines satirical techniques for achieving various social statuses and personal attributes (such as becoming a literarian, a beautiful woman, a leading journalist, etc.) in 1930s Korea. It presents an exaggerated and ironic view on societal roles, particularly emphasizing the absurdity behind the pursuit of such roles. Historians may find interesting reflections on the gender expectations and the mocking of class and romantic ideals prevalent during this colonial period in Korea. The use of humor to address serious issues can offer insights into the social dynamics of the time.

이러케 늘어노코 보니까 아주 썩 어려울 줄 알어도 실상 알고 보면 팔 집고 헤염치기다.
제군! 혹 諸裙! 제군 등이 만일 文士나 혹은 文女가 되고 십거든 다음의 經路를 충실하게 밟어라.
第一로 원고용지를 한 만장 사다노코 2錢 우표를 한 오천 장 사다노코 그저 마구 써서 그저 마구 신문사로 잡지사로 보내라.

第二로는 한 가지 것만 전문으로 파서는 안 된다.
시∙소설∙드라마∙시나리오∙評∙三重四重의 외국작품 번역... ...
이상의 두 가지를 한 두어 달 불굴의 정신으로 계속하면 이미 諸君 등은 50%의 文士가 된다.
그때에는 제군 등은 곳 잡지사 사람과 親交를 매저 가지고 그 잡지의 豫告欄에 『本誌에 執筆하는 文人 諸氏』라는 곳에 제군 등의 성명 3字를 집어 너어라. 그러치 아니하면 달은 문사 하나를 탕수육에 백알ㅅ잔이나 대접하고는 신문에 『文人 印象記』를 써 달나고 간청을 하여라. 그러면 그때에는 발서 제군은 100%의 문사로 완성이 된다.
단, 以上은 人造絹式 문사란 말이다. 일홈만 엇자면 그러케 해도 된다 그 말이다.
제군이 만일 진정하게 문사가 되려면 이미 옛사람이 갈으킨 바와 가치 과학자의 눈으로 관찰하고 철학자의 머리로 생각하고 예술가의 수법으로 표현을 해야 한다.＜144＞
그리하자면 百分의 공부와 百分의 재능이 필요한 것이다.

한강에 나아가서 감기가 돌지 아니할 정도로 自殺劇 一幕을 實演하여라. 이튼날에는 미인의 투신자살이라고 버젓하게 나지 안나. 朴家粉 한 갑에도 10錢하는 시절에 電車삭 5錢이면 一躍 사회적 미인이 되지를 안는가!

名記者 되는 秘訣
명기자, 명기자, 大新聞의 명기자, 花形記者. 듯기만 해도 다리가 웃줄거려지고 긔운이 나는 듯하다. 더구나 조선에 엇서서는 기자의 권위란 참 굉장한 지라 누가 젊은 긔운으로 한번 되여보기를 바라지 아니하랴.
귀신이 哭을 하고 독개비가 오좀을 싸고 달어날 만큼 신통하게 기사 어더내는 것, 오나가나 대접을 극진히 밧는 것... ... 엇대? 제군도 한번 되여보고 십지 안소? 그러면 秘方을 공개하지.
一 曰, 월급 못 주는 신문사에 가서 돈은 일 업스니 名義나 두어 달나고 사정 사정으로 간청을 해서 爲先 記者名函을 박어 둘 일, 그 놈을 가지고 걸이나개나 막 돌아다니며 교제를 할 일, 外上 료리를 먹고 ヰノゴリ를 잽힐 것, 電通이나 帝通 기자에게 돈푼이나 먹여두고 記事를 어더낼 일, 黨派를 딸아 이 신문사 저 신문사로 옴겨다닐 일, 사장이나 중역이 일본이나 어데를 가거든 차 속에 대고 『선생님 안녕히 다녀오십시요.』라고 電報를 댓 장씩 칠 일, 洋服エリ에는 반듯이 빼지를 부치고 다닐 일, 일주일에 열네 번씩 극장에를 다닐 일, 미운 놈을 기사로 욕할 일, 文士業을 겸할 일... ... 등등이다.
그러나 혹 성가지지 아니하면 다음의 法도 잇다.
부지런할 일(손으로 기사를 쓰려고 말고 말로 쓰려고 할 것), 민첩할 일, 先進의 경험을 연구할 일, 기사를 공평하게 취급할 일... ... 등등이다.＜145＞

戀愛 成功秘訣
오스카-∙외일드는 그의 作 『千萬者의 모델』이라는 소설의 冒頭에 Romance is the privilege of the rich, not the profession of the unemployer―(연애도 돈 잇는 사람의 特權이지 失業者의 할 일이 못 된다.)―라고 말하엿다.
그러나 그것은 새빨간 거짓말이다. 돈이 업다고 연애를 못해?
