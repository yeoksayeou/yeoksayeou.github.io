A year’s worth of liquor consumed by 140 million Russian citizens amounts to 6.9 million stones, valued at 1.5 billion won. The labor government has decided to restrict liquor annual production to 2.6 million stones, citing that it is unsustainable.

Recently, an advertisement in a British newspaper called for 20 bald gentlemen to attend public events such as church, theater, and concerts, with the expectation that advertisements would be painted on their bald heads to promote the store’s sales. The ad promises adequate protection against any humiliation.

Edgar de Wells, a linguist from Estonia, has invented a universal language that can replace Esperanto. It is named "Occidental," and aside from a few exceptions, anyone can understand it after hearing it only once. The grammar is said to be simple, having no complicated irregular verbs.

A young man in England, unable to bear his mother's suffering from an incurable disease, shot and killed her. Though the prosecutor sought a five-year prison sentence, the judge declared, “In such a case, for a child, taking their mother’s life is the only option,” and he was acquitted.

Glara Po, a renowned beauty in the world, receives about 30,000 love letters a month, including around 1,000 daily, from admirers.

In Chicago, a new club called the "Men's Rights Association" has formed. This is a coalition of men who feel oppressed by their wives. Their demands include the following: 

1. A mandatory one-night outing per week without explanations.
2. No housework, cooking, or chores unless they have three children or their wives are aware.
3. A daily allowance of one dollar, not to be reported back.

A Spanish worker suffering from a toothache joked about wanting to die rather than endure the pain, and so took a pistol and shot himself in the mouth. Surprisingly, this shattered two of his teeth, immediately alleviating his toothache, and he felt relieved as if he had gone to heaven.

In New York, a tailor who was struggling with his business seemed to have developed a mental illness, as he couldn’t control himself and would push strangers onto the subway tracks. He was arrested after having pushed a wealthy gentleman and another lady, stating, “Standing on the platform, it’s inexplicably unbearable to resist pushing people.”

**Summary**

This article discusses various peculiar stories from around the world, including limits on alcohol production in Russia, an odd advertisement seeking bald men for publicity, and a strange new universal language. It also features a tragic case of justified homicide in England. These tales reflect the societal issues and the absurdities of everyday lives in the early 20th century. Historians may find the account of the acquitted son particularly illuminating regarding societal attitudes toward maternal suffering and moral justification during the colonial era.

火酒 制限

勞働政府에서는 이거 안되엿다고 화주 年産額을 260만 石으로 제한.

대머리 新利用
최근 영국의 어느 신문 광고를 다음에 소개한다.
『대머리 紳士 諸君 이십 명을 구함. 用途는 본 상점의 판매를 擴張하기 위하야 대머리 紳士 諸君의 버서진 이마에 그림 그린 광고문을 記入하여 가지고 교회 극장 음악회 등 공개한 會席에 出席을 요구함. 모욕에 대하여는 충분한 보호를 함.』

新世界語 發明
에스토니아의 언어학자 에드가-·데웰스 씨는 최근에 에스패란토에 代用될 世界共通語를 발명하엿다.
명칭은 『옥시덴탈』이요 바보와 젓멕이 이외에는 한 번만 닑으면 누구나 알 수 잇게 되엿다. 문법도 썩 간단하고 복잡한 불규칙동사의 法도 업시 만들엇다고 한다.

어머니를 쏘아 죽이고 無罪
영국의 어느 청년이 자기 어머니가 불치의 難病으로 몹시 부댁기는 것을 참아 볼 수 업서 피스톨로 쏘아 죽엿다.
검사가 五五年 禁錮를 구형하엿는데 판사는『그 경우에 자식으로써 취할 방법은 모친의 생명을 뺏는 것밧게는 달은 도리가 업다』고 하야 무죄 판결.

戀愛 편지가 每日 數百 張
세계 일의 곡선미의 소유자 그라라·포-에게로 한 달에 오는 연애 편지는 돈 달나는 편지와 합해서 삼만 장. 하로에 일천 장.

안房郡守同盟
최근 미국 시카고에 『男便擁護同盟』이라는 얄구즌 會合이 생겻다.
녀편네의 방등이에 눌녀 긔운을 펴지 못하는 놈팽이들의 동맹이다. 所期의 요구조건은 다음과 갓다.
一, 일주일에 한 번씩 야간외출(단 이유 설명을 불허함). 二, 자식이 셋 이상 잇고 또는 마누라가 알는 때 이외에는 집안 掃除와 부억일과 허잽이 일을 아니할 일. 三, 매일 一弗씩의 용ㅅ돈을 어더내일 일.(단 용돈 보고를 불허함)

齒痛 治療法
西班牙의 어느 노동자가 치통이 생겨서 엇지나 압흐든지 에라 차라리 죽어나 바리겟다고 피스톨을 입에 대이고 압흔 니ㅅ발을 겨누어 쾅!
한즉 웬걸 압흐든 니ㅅ발 두 개가 산산 부서지며 치통이 단번에 개며 천국에 간 것 가처 가슴이 시원하엿다고.
