**Summary**  

This article details the establishment of new branch offices across various locations, including Technically Tongyeong, Gangneung, Osaka, Yongsan, and Pandan. Each branch has its own address and appointed branch head, indicating an effort to expand operations or perhaps communication networks during this period. Historians might find the establishment of these branch offices of interest as they reflect the organizational strategies of Korean businesses or media in the colonial context, contributing to the understanding of colonial infrastructure development.
