기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)  

Submission Welcome

Local Color! This is to taste the flavor of local readers and encourages submissions at will. There must be strange customs, weird habits, and bizarre historical legends throughout the thirteen provinces. Write about people, landscapes, and local specialties as well. Anyone can write about their local experiences and send them in. However, there should be this kind of so-called limitation.

1. Do not write in a cliched introduction; instead, write in an interesting and engaging manner.

2. Write with fifteen characters per line, within sixty lines.

3. Write in broken sentences.

4. It is even better to send along photographs or similar items. 

Editing and selection will be left to the discretion of this company, but a small reward will be given for those parts that are published. <129>  

**Summary**

The article invites readers to submit their writings about local customs, legends, and landscapes to the magazine, emphasizing the importance of presenting unique and interesting local flavors. The restrictions on submission format suggest an effort to maintain a standard in writing that captivates the readers. This reflects the cultural dynamics of the colonial period, as it encourages local expression in a time when many voices were suppressed. Historians might find it interesting as it shows how cultural documentation was attempted amidst the colonial framework, possibly providing insights into local identity during Japan’s occupation.

投稿歡迎
地方色! 이것은 地方讀者의 페지를맛겨둔다 멋대로마음대로 投稿하라 十三道坊坊曲曲에는 가지가색의 이상한 風俗, 야릇한 習慣駭怪한 歷史傳說이만흘 것이다 人物도좃타 山水風景도좃타 風土産物도좃타 그것을 쓰란말이다. 누구나 自己地方의 것을 얼마던지 써보내라 그러나 요만한 所謂制限은 드러주어야한다.
一, 陳腐한 紹介文가티쓰지말고 좀 멋잇시 興味잇게읽을수잇도록쓸일
一, 原稿紙 一行十五字 六十行內外로쓸일
一, 句節을  여서 쓸일
一, 寫眞가튼 것을 添附하여보내는 것은 더욱 조흔일
添削과 取捨는 本社에 一任하되 揭載되는 分에 對하야는 薄謝를 드리기로 함니다.＜129＞
