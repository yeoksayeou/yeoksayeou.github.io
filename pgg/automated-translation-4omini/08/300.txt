The Missionary of the Wind  
A certain American missionary who had long been in China, after returning, spoke about China with the following words: "Everyone, there is no country in the world like China where many people are executed. They kill at any moment. However, if you have money, you can buy someone else to take your execution in your place. Thus, those who have money often buy poor people to die in their place. Therefore, in China, there are many people who take on the profession of being executed in place of others in order to lead a comfortable life."

  

The Alert Spirit  
Mr. Heollungi, who was good at being careless, one night needed to go out for something and, accompanied by his wife, when they were about to leave the front door, noticing it might rain, his wife said, "Dear, it looks like it will rain soon, so please go quickly and put on your old padded coat before coming out." He replied, "Okay, I will," and hurriedly went inside, but he did not come out all night long. Unable to wait on the street, his wife went inside to see what had happened, and there, Mr. Heollungi, instead of wearing the padded coat, had taken off everything down to his underwear, and in the end, he was lying down comfortably with a blanket on top of himself. <127>  

**Summary**

This article features a commentary on the harsh realities of capital punishment in China, as described by a returning American missionary who highlights the prevalence of bribery in evading justice. In the second part, it whimsically illustrates the foolishness of Mr. Heollungi, who, in a humorous failure to prepare for the rain, ends up fully undressed and cozy instead. The discussions around capital punishment and societal conditions reflect broader themes of moral ambiguity and socio-economic disparity, offering insight into life during the colonial era, which may intrigue historians studying the period's social dynamics.

풍장이 선교사
풍장이 선교사
중국(中國)에 오래 잇다가 도라 온 미국선교사(宣敎師) 한 분이 긔도 올닌 후에 연설하는 말 『여러분 세계에서 중국이란 나라처럼 사람 만히 죽이(死刑)는 나라는 업슴니다. 툭하면 죽여버리닛가요. 그러나 돈만 잇스면 다른 사람을 사서 대신 사형을 밧게 할 수 잇는고로 대개는 돈 잇는 사람은 가난한 사람을 사서 대신 죽게함니다. 그래서 중국에는 남의 대신 사형 밧는 것으로 직업을 삼어서 안락한 생활을 하는 사람이 만히 잇슴니다』

알뜰한 정신
덤벙대기 잘하는 헐넝이 선생이 느진 밤에 볼일이 잇서서 부인과 함께 대문 밧글 나스는데 비가 올 듯한지라 부인이 『여보 비가 올가 무서우니 드러가서 헌 두루마기와 밧구어 닙고 나오시요』하닛가 『응 그러지』하고 뛰여 드러가더니 밤이 다 깁도록 영영 다시 나오지 안는지라 행길에서 기다리다-못하야 부인이 드러가 보닛가 엇지 헐넝이든지 두루마기 버슨 김에 족기까지 벗고, 족기 버슨 김에 버선까지 벗고, 버선 버슨 김에 바지저고리까지 벗고, 발가버슨 김에 이불을 덥고 자리에 누어 편안히 자드란다.＜127＞
