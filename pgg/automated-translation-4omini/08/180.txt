기사제목	불란서 사람의 말 (Words of a French Person)  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

A French person said, "A man will surely have two good days in his life: the day he gets married and the day he buries his wife..." <79>  

**Summary**  

This article presents a whimsical saying attributed to a French perspective on marriage and life events, reflecting a stark view of marital relationships and death. The quote encapsulates humor and cynicism regarding the constraints of marriage, contrasting joyful union with the eventual loss of a spouse. From a historical viewpoint, this observation could intrigue historians studying relationships and societal norms in Korea during the colonial period, highlighting the cultural exchange and perception of marriage influenced by Western ideas.
