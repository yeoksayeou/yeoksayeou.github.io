(Untitled - National Issue)  
Creative Folk Songs…………Selected by Kim Dong-hwan  
Creative Sijo…………Selected by Lee Hun-gi  
The winning entries among those submitted will be published in the September issue. Please send your new works immediately. Folk songs are limited to three pieces per person, and sijo are limited to five entries per person. Winning works will be featured in the magazine, and a small token of appreciation will be presented. <119>  

  
**Summary**  

This article serves as a notice regarding the submission of entries for creative folk songs and sijo poems. It outlines the limitations on submissions and informs contributors that selected works will be published in the next issue, with some form of recognition provided to authors. Historians may find it interesting as it reflects efforts to promote indigenous Korean cultural expression during the colonial period, emphasizing the consolidation and preservation of Korean identity despite Japanese oppression.
