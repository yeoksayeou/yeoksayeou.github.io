Strange Incident Occurs!? A Strange Man and Woman Duo  

The abrupt ringing of the telephone jolted the sleepy duty officer, nicknamed 'Chaatpuring' (his beard gave him that title), from his dozing state as he tossed away the newspaper and grabbed the receiver to his ear.

“Hello, hello, is this Officer Kim?”  

For a moment, he was astonished by the charming voice of a woman, and then he responded, “Yes, yes, this is Kim.”  

“Oh, thank goodness! I was worried that it might have gotten through to someone else when I called... It's me! Do you understand? Huh? You know... for some reason, I got so mad that I thought maybe I had dialed a different number."  

The charming voice was overly flirtatious, and the officer, of course, did not think it was the real Kim nor did he even know which woman owned that voice. 

Well, this is likely a call from some gisaeng (the term for courtesan) who misdialed while trying to get through to her regular man. Though it’s a rather annoying thing to deal with, I think it may provide some entertainment for me while I’m on duty tonight, the officer thought, and decided to continue the conversation.  

“Hey, what’s wrong? Why are you so quiet today? Are you perhaps with another woman? That must be why you’re stuttering and hesitating... You shouldn’t be bringing another woman to my attention without my knowledge.”  

“No, no, what are you talking about!” the officer interjected, playing along.  

“Ah, if that’s the case, then forgive me! Hahaha. But you know, I have something urgent to tell you. (And then suddenly in a lower voice) The leader said that tomorrow night at 9, he’ll be going there to do a job, and said he’ll bring you along.”  

“Ah...” The officer swallowed hard and replied, “That sounds like a meaningful job.” He then pulled the telephone closer.  

“But what the leader said is very serious. It's really dangerous, so the leader asked you not to go this time under any pretense. I really don't feel good about it. Hey! Do you understand what I am saying? Huh?”  

“Yeah, yeah, I got it,” the officer responded, struggling to suppress his curiosity about the major case he might be uncovering, and trying to feign casualness in his replies.  

"Where exactly is this place you’re talking about?”  

“Oh, don’t you know? It’s the XX Company located at 00 from the star. Recently, a very important item is supposed to arrive, and tomorrow night at 9, when the office has finished up and they’re about to lock the doors, the leader will hide in the manager’s room and when the manager is ready to place the document box inside the safe, we’ll jump in and take off with it. So, isn’t that dangerous?”  

“Well, that sounds like a risky plan...”  

“Exactly! That’s why I told you to make some excuse this time and not go.” And then the woman stopped suddenly and exclaimed, “Oh no! It seems like the leader has returned, so I’ll call you again later. You must do as I say.”  

Click! The call ended just like that.  

It’s him! There have been signs of strange men activity lately in the city; it must surely be that guy.  

That must be it. The lover or concubine of that 'leader' is calling on the phone to deceive him while making contact with the man she loves.  

It’s quite the coincidence that I came to know this secret. Now I have the chance to gain some great merit, the Chaatpuring officer thought, thrilled.  

On that fateful night! The officer arrived early in the evening, dressed in civilian clothes with multiple subordinate officers, prepared for a stakeout at the XX Company.  

As it approached 9 PM, the manager began to appear increasingly nervous, fidgeting without a clue, no doubt spellbound by fear.  

“Don't worry anymore. We’ve hidden detectives throughout this company. If they arrive, we can apprehend them smoothly. It should be around 9 o’clock. They will come soon. So you, the manager, should go home and hide in the duty officer's room. Make sure the employees don’t catch on and let them all go home. If the employees get suspicious, it could cause a ruckus.”  

“And what about the valuable item? Tonight, it’s safer to have it kept in your own room rather than in the safe.”  

“Right, right! Then we’ll do that,” the manager remarked as he took the box and headed home to hide in his office.  

9 PM strikes. Nothing happened at all. The frightening minutes stretched out painfully, each second tightening the officer’s heart with anxiety.  

5, 10, 15 minutes passed, but still nothing came up. All the employees had gone home, the company's front gate closed tightly, and it became eerily quiet. Only detectives remained, waiting like cats hunting mice.  

At 18, 19, and 20 minutes after the hour, suddenly, the duty officer's door burst open, and the manager rushed in, trembling with worry.   

“I just totally forgot! There's a secret staircase that leads up from the office to the back warehouse. What if those guys know about it and come this way?”  

The officer was stunned to hear this, as he thought all possible hiding spots had already been secured.  

“A secret passage from the warehouse to the manager's room? That's terrible! Why didn’t you tell me sooner? Let’s hurry and check it out.”  

And so, the officer, quaking with fear, grabbed the lantern and followed the manager down the staircase.  

After about 7 or 8 minutes, the officer returned with a vexed look on his face, muttering.  

“Seriously? The so-called manager is so clumsy and cowardly! He unnecessarily freaked out and on such frivolous thoughts, went to that careless warehouse... What nonsense.”  

While he was grumbling, again, the duty officer’s room burst open, and one of the detectives blurted out, “It’s a disaster! I heard strange noises from the duty room, and when I checked, the manager was there, all bound up wearing only his underpants.”  

“He went to the warehouse and dressed up as the manager to fool me like that?” the officer shouted as they quickly rushed him up. “What? He even stripped me of my clothes and went out dressed like a manager!”  

“Then it must be him who was telling you to come here just now!”  

“Quick! After him! That’s the guy! Hurry, hurry!”  

The detectives rushed up the stairs, but alas, they were too late. It was all a ruse. The woman on the phone made a mistake to lure the officer in, as the leader had tied up the real manager, taken his clothes, and escaped via the window with the woman in the waiting car long before.  

  

**Summary**  

This story follows a police duty officer who naively engages with a woman on the phone, only to become a victim of a carefully planned theft scheme. The clever misdirection results in chaos, where the officer’s desire to prove himself leads to his embarrassment and failure. This narrative sheds light on themes of deception and the nature of crime during the colonial period, revealing a yearning for agency amidst the oppressive structure of society, which historians may find significant as it reflects the vulnerabilities of colonial administration against burgeoning crime.

형사들은 또다시 우루루루 몰러 올러가서 들창밧게까지 쫏차 나왓스나 그러나 벌서 느젓다. 아초에 녀자를 식여서 일부러 잘못 걸닌 뎐화처럼 하야 경부를 꼬여 내고 오늘 숙직실에서 지배인을 결박하고 물건을 빼슨 후에 지배인으로 변장하고 나와서 경부를 꼬여서 창고에 다리고 가고 경부를 결박하고 경부로 변장한 단장이란 인물은 들창 밧그로 뛰여나가 기다리고 잇던 녀자와 함께 자동차를 몰아간지가 오래된 후엿다.＜143＞
