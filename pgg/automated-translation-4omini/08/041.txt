**Summary**  

This article announces the establishment of new branch offices for the company, indicating their locations, leadership, and staffing. The establishment of these branches indicates an expansion strategy aimed at improving distribution and engagement with local communities during the colonial period. Historians may find interest in the involvement of local figures in the management of these offices, reflecting a blend of Korean and Japanese colonial governance structures.
