Welcome to Secret Orders  
Dear readers of this magazine, we invite you to become editorial advisors. Our secret investigation team reporters are always prepared to be dispatched and are waiting for your orders. If there are strange occurrences in any specific area or if there is any mysterious dungeon, please instruct us to investigate. If there are unusual individuals or suspicious incidents in any place, please notify us immediately at the "별건곤 조사부" (Byeolgeongon Investigation Department), and we will promptly carry out a covert investigation and report back in the magazine.  

In the magazine, even if you keep it confidential, please provide the location, address, and details of the individuals, such as gender and age. You must also provide the address of the person submitting the letter to facilitate contact during the investigation.

**Summary**  

This article invites readers to participate in the investigative work of a publication by reporting strange occurrences or individuals in their vicinity. The magazine's call for collaboration engages its audience in a pseudo-journalistic effort reflecting an era when media played a significant role in shaping public discourse. Historians studying this period may find it revealing of the public's engagement with media and the prevalent fears or curiosities regarding the unknown, especially in a colonial context where such reporting could serve to highlight societal tensions or issues.

誌上에는 秘密을지히드라도 本社지는 그 場所番地 人物이면 女性年齡을 明記하시고 投書하시는이의 住所도알려주서야 調査上連絡할수잇겟슴니다.
