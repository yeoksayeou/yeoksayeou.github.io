기사형태	잡저 (Miscellaneous Article)  

After being elected as the President of the United States, a letter of congratulations was sent to Mr. Hoover from the students of Washington University, measuring nine feet in width and five feet in height; it indeed is something typical of the American people.  

**Summary**  

This article discusses the congratulatory letter sent to Herbert Hoover after his election as President of the United States. The sheer size of the letter is noted, symbolizing the American people's enthusiasm. Historians might find this exchange significant as it reflects the American political culture during the late 1920s and how Korea at the time was observing and engaging with global politics under colonial rule.
