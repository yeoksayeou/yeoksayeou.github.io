【募集】  
We welcome readers' manuscripts for publication...  

Content will be included in "This Magazine," "Children," and "Students" <67>  

**Summary**  

This brief announcement invites readers to submit their writings for publication in various sections of the magazine. The inclusion of content aimed at "Children" and "Students" reflects the magazine's efforts to engage younger audiences and promote literary participation among readers. Historians might find the encouragement of creative expression during Japan's colonial rule in Korea an interesting aspect, as it illustrates a cultural dynamism that contrasts with the oppressive political environment of the time.
