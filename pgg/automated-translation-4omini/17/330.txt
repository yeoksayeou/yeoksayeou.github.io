Controversy Regarding Men  
Men are driven only by ambition  
Jeong Jong-myeong  

Those who insult the yangban are lowborn, and those who insult the rich are the poor. In the same line of reasoning, if I were to speak of men's shortcomings, it would typically be said that I am female and thus degrading men, and that I am committing various deceptions and oppressions against men. However, I do not do this out of animosity as a woman. I shall speak of men's shortcomings from a standpoint that transcends all emotions, fully detached from the status of men or women, aiming to express things in the most detached and equitable perspective.  

To begin with, men, towering in their pride (though some may have short stature), act with a sense of superiority as they consider themselves. They tend to disregard others’ dignity and instincts solely to satisfy their own desires. While they demand chastity from women, men engage in licentious and unprincipled behavior, indulging in liquor to the point of disgrace, shedding their garments and spiraling into drunkenness, causing distress to many. Yet they readily slander women for their farming endeavors. On encountering a woman, rather than treating her with dignity, they start with a critical or ambitious perspective, using every means of temptation they can muster to have her sacrifice her entire being. Once they have violated a woman's chastity, they show no sense of shame or responsibility, discarding her like a worn shoe and never looking back.  

Even in traditional households, women are expected to prepare meals, sew clothes, do laundry, raise children, and contribute all in their effort, while men consider it of little consequence and perceive it as some burden they alone need to bear. Jealousy, envy, and factionalism seep in from their heels to the very tops of their heads, and upon noticing that someone else may slightly excel or have some power or be successful in any venture, they will scheme to crush and trample it down. It is often said that behind every crime, a woman is lurking, but are not all evils presented openly by men?  

Marking how the past has shaped us, it cannot be overlooked that even today, men are the ones destroying peace and disrupting order in the world. To put it bluntly, men are all ambition—a lump of sin. In the old days, it was said that when a man transgresses, he deserves a beating. However, even today, if women were to seize power and reinstill corporal punishment, men would not dare to mutter a word of protest, even if they faced the same beating as in the past.

Men are Cold-Blooded Creatures  
U Hwang-un  

Women, like cats, will come to you if treated with kindness, whereas men, like snakes, are slick and adept at carrying and biting. An ancient saying went that of all snakes, men resemble pythons, cold and cunning, eyeing women like a python would a frog or egg, always poised to swallow them whole. They skillfully chase after power and profit, living in both water and on land, causing all types of storm and strife in the world, yet they hibernate in winter and emerge in spring, ready to seize every opportunity to further their own interests. Their guile is as deep and impenetrable as that of a snake, whose length cannot be gauged; they appear amicable but harbor intentions to harm once a rift occurs. 

One who possesses little knowledge pretends to be wise, even while being inept—a disposition that seems common to men, who always display a façade of ambition. They do not care for the needs of others and thus resemble a dragon aspiring to rise to the heavens, oblivious to the struggles of those around them. Indeed, men are nothing other than cold-blooded creatures.

My Observations on Men  
Kim Sun-yeong  

I am still a virgin and have not had much interaction with men or collaborated on work with them. Nonetheless, I understand that criticizing men may seem presumptuous, yet since many voices are expressing opinions, I shall share a few personal reflections based on my feelings and considerations.  

First, regarding men's strengths, they exhibit greater ambition and capacity for action compared to women. However, these strengths pale against their numerous shortcomings. Chief among these is their arrogance, which comes from the belief that they alone possess superiority, as they preach gender equality yet continue to treat women based on past traditions with contempt. They fail to see women as individuals and instead adopt a critical or ambitious approach from the outset, often dismissing their spouses’ opinions and asserting their own will. This selfishness tends to overlook others’ welfare. These observations represent what I perceive to be the essence of masculinity.

Two Strengths and Four Weaknesses  
Yu Gyeok-kyeong  

The current state we are in, concerning both genders, indicates that we are in a transitional phase, and it is difficult for anyone to definitively state their strengths and weaknesses, as there are also personal limitations. Even so, because you have earnestly requested it, I will record some thoughts, though I cannot guarantee their universality, and I would appreciate readers reflecting upon them.  

Strengths  
1. Abundant tolerance.  
2. A pronounced decisiveness that drives them to pursue their objectives with a disregard for familial, moral, or life considerations.  

Weaknesses  
1. Excessive ambition leads them to believe they are the natural leaders while adhering relentlessly to their own standards.  
2. A lack of patience or endurance, resulting in a tendency toward destruction rather than construction.  
3. A deficiency in compassion, often leading to immoral conduct.  
4. Although they profess to be warriors, they historically succumb unconditionally to those with weapons.  

Three Strengths and Three Weaknesses of Men  
Hwang Sin-deok  

Strengths  
1. Generally, men are believed to possess a strong decisiveness. While women often ponder over minor decisions for too long and end up failing, men tend to resolve matters promptly and conclusively.  

2. Courage may be considered a strength of men; this may be attributed to their strong bodies fostered by a long history. Women often defer in situations where they are aware of their moral standing, while men boldly charge fearlessly when they believe they are right.  

3. Their overwhelming desires could be interpreted negatively as ambition, but positively it can be perceived as a strong drive towards ideals. Their desire to achieve is vital for their vitality and purpose in life, and it is believed that a life devoid of desires would be meaningless.  

Weaknesses  
1. A lack of self-control. While they may be decisive and courageous, they frequently lack the discipline to restrain themselves from doing what they know is wrong. Whether this stems from a lack of self-control or indulgence is unclear, yet it is undeniable that it is a weakness.  

2. An excessively inflated sense of self-esteem. This could reflect their feelings towards women, but their desire to dominate others can come off as somewhat unbecoming.  

3. Inconsistency, though common amongst Korean men, is particularly pronounced in men, where their fervent desires and resolutions to achieve are often not pursued with the same vigor over time.

**Summary**  

This article provides a stark critique of masculinity in early 20th century Korean society, highlighting various negative traits attributed to men, such as arrogance and ambition, contrasted against the expected submissiveness of women. It reflects a deep concern over gender roles and the systemic inequalities women faced while living under male hegemony. Historians might find interest in how these sentiments echoed the broader struggle for gender equality during the colonial period, as women's voices began to emerge amidst oppressive societal norms.

男子는 冷血動物

여자는 고양(猫)이 가터서 각가이 하면 품속으로 드러오고 남자는 뱀(蛇)과 가터서 각가이 할사록 차고 물기를 잘한다고 엇던 녯사람은 말하얏다. 과연 그럿타. 남자는 뱀 중에도 구렁이 갓다. 냉정하고도 능글능글하야 여자를 보면 개골(蛙)이나 계란을 본 구렁이처럼 늘름거리며 집어 삼취랴고 하고 水陸兩處에 서식하덧이 세력과 이익을 따라서 이리追逐 저리追逐하며 몸과 꼬리를 처서 물결을 일으키듯이 이 세상의 모든 풍파와 시비를 다 일으키고도 겨을에 동면을 하다가 봄철이 되면 쓱 나오덧이 기회를 따러 자기이익을 圖得하랴 한다. 속이 깁고 음흉하기는 진소위 궁긔에 든 뱀이 길이를 알 수 업는 것과 가티 남이 그 深淺을 엿볼 수 업고 친하다가도 한번 틀니면 아주 피차에 정을 끈코 해칠 마음까지 두는 것은 구렁이의 몸이 냉하고 니(牙)가 毒한 것과 갓다. 그리고 아모 것도 아는 것이 업스면서도 아는 척하고 못나고도 잘난 척 하면 속으로는 별짓을 다하면서도 것으로는 有志한 척하며 명예심, 허욕심만 갓득 한 것은 마치 이묵(螭)이가 제 몸 생각은 하지 안코 龍이 되여 하눌로 올나가랴는 욕심을 가지고 잇는 것과도 갓다. 엇지 하엿던 남자는 냉혈동물이다.

男子의 三長三短
黃信德

長點
1. 일반뎍으로 결단성(決斷性)이 풍부하다고 생각한다. 여자들은 적은 일 한아를 處決하는데도 이리저리 너무 생각을 만히 하다가 실패하는 일이 만흐나 남자들은 엇더한 일을 당하던지 능큼능큼 결말을 지여 좃는 果斷性이 퍽이나 시원스럽다.
2. 용감(勇敢)스럽다는 것도 아마 남자의 장점이 될 듯하다.  이것은 물론 그들의 심신을 건장하게 길너준 긴 력사의 덕택이라 할 수 잇다. 여자들은 흔히 변연히 자긔 한 일이 올흔 줄 알면서도 머리를 숙일 때가 만타. 말하자면 정의(正義)에도 용감스럽지 못한데 비하야 남성들은 자긔가 올타고 생각하는 일이면 물에나 불에나 주저하지 안코 용감스러운 태도로 돌진하는 것이 통쾌하다.
3. 욕망(慾望)이 만타. 이것을 악의로 해석하면 야심이 만타고도 할 수잇스나 선의로 해석할 적에는 욕망이 만흘사록 리상이 크고 강하다. 무엇이든지 하고저 하는 마음! 만일 사람에게서 이것을 빼여버리면 무미맹량하다 나무나 돌보다 별노 나을 것이 업슬 듯 십다. 그런데 남성들은 어릴때로나 나이 만허진 후까지도 욕망이 만흔 까닭에 그 일생이 녀자보다 퍽이나 씩씩하다.

短點
1. 자제력(自制力)이 업는 것이다. 과단성이 잇고 용긔는 만흐나 스사로 제어하는 힘이 부족한 탓으로 자긔가 지금 하고 십흔 일히 분명히 조치 못한 일인 줄 알면서도 참지를 못한다. 이것은 사실 자제력이 업는 까닭인지 방종(放縱)인지 자세히 모르나 엇던 것이든지 단처인 것만은 사실이다.
2. 자존심(自尊心)이 너무 과하다. 이것은 녀자에게만 향하는 남자의 심사인지 몰나도 아는 체하고 다른 사람을 지배하랴 하는 것은 좀 아니껍살스러운 일이다.
3. 시종(始終)이 한결갓지 못하다. 이것은 조선사람의 공통한 단처라 하지만은 특별히 하고저 하는 욕망과 하겟다 하는 결심 그리고 시작이 용감스러움에 비하야 꾸준이 계속하야 나가지 못하는 것이 남자에게 더욱 심하야 보인다.＜133＞
