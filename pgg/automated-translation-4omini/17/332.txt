**Summary**

This editorial announces a special reward of five hundred won for the readers in celebration of surpassing thirty thousand copies in circulation. The publication invites participation from the public, emphasizing various meanings behind this call to action. Historians may find the context notable as it suggests a vibrant readership and engagement during the colonial era, reflecting a connection between media and public participation in Korea under Japanese rule.
