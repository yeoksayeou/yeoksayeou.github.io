기사제목	表紙그림說明 (Cover Illustration Description)  
기사형태	잡저 (Miscellaneous Article)  

The cover illustration of this issue depicts a beauty (美人) playing the lute (琵琶) from about fifteen hundred years ago during the Three Kingdoms (三國) era. The hairstyle (머리) and clothing (衣服) are very different from today's customs (只今風俗), as can be seen.

  

**Summary**

This article provides a brief description of a historical illustration featured on the cover of the magazine, emphasizing its connection to the Three Kingdoms period in Korea. Historians might find it interesting as it reflects the cultural aesthetics and societal norms at that time, contrasting sharply with the 1920s fashion and beauty standards during the colonial period in Korea.
