各都市探査記歡迎  
As the winds of civilization blow increasingly into the so-called urban areas, harmful influences are also permeating, leading to a significant rise in groups that actively operate in the shadows, wearing various masks. We have recently explored several magical dens in the capital, but now we are striving to investigate the regional cities to the utmost. We earnestly request those who are willing and able to provide us with responsible and substantial materials <102>.  

**Summary**  

This article serves as a call for participation in a collective investigation of urban life and its hidden underbelly, reflecting the changing socio-cultural landscape during a time of colonial rule in Korea. The increase in clandestine activities in urban areas signifies a complex interplay of modernity and societal issues that historians may find crucial for understanding urbanization in colonial Korea. The article captures crucial moments of transformation and highlights the need for responsible documentation of social phenomena during this era.
