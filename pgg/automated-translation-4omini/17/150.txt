기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)  

This year, we promised to publish regularly every month, and as a special undertaking, we worked through the nights at the end of last year to prepare the New Year's issue with all employees mobilized. Immediately thereafter, we began editing the February issue. However, the circumstances around us have not allowed us to proceed smoothly. Thus, the February edition was finally completed and is now released. Regardless of the circumstances, I apologize to our readers and pledge to work harder so that future issues will be published on time.

  
**Summary**  

The article expresses an acknowledgment and apology from the editorial team for the delay in the publication of the February issue of the magazine. It highlights their intentions to publish monthly and their efforts in preparing the New Year’s issue under challenging circumstances. This reflection of struggle for regular publication amidst external pressures could interest historians studying the cultural and social dynamics of Korean periodicals during the colonial era, particularly in understanding how colonial rule influenced local media operations and editorial policies.

기사형태	사고·편집후기

謝告
금년에는 꼭 달달이 정기발행을 하랴고 별으로 또 뼐너서 작년말에 밤을새워가며 전사원의 총동원으로 신년호을 내여놋고 즉시또 2월호편집에 착수하얏습니다. 그러나 말슴할수업는 주위의 사정은 우리의 일을 망ㅁ대로 하게 許치안슴니다. 그리하야 2월총 편집한것이 이제 겨우 나오게 되엿슴니다. 사정이야 如何하던지 독자여러분에게 미안을 謝하오며 또 이후는 더욱 奮發하야 꼭 정기에 발행하길ㄹ 기약함니다.
