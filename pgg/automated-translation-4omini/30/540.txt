기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Notes)  

주문하실 때의 注意  
When ordering this magazine, please send a deposit to振替京城 8106 and also send your address and name. Requests for credit or cash on delivery are strictly prohibited.<178>  

  

**Summary**  

This article provides specific instructions for ordering the magazine, emphasizing the importance of prepayment and the prohibition of credit payment options. This reflects the broader economic conditions of the colonial period, where financial transactions were cautious due to unstable economic factors. Historians might find this detailing of payment practices significant as it sheds light on the business practices during Japan's colonial rule over Korea and the challenges faced by publications at that time.  

**Named Entities**
