Rumor Report

Mr. I Gi-yong often boasts that even after drinking, he can confidently present a table with nothing more than an empty paper box without a single penny. Recently, at a certain gaming house, someone claimed they would distribute liquor but was unable to do so. As usual, Mr. I made grand claims, saying, "If they don't distribute, I will!" His friends, half-convinced, examined his wallet and found it to be so empty that, after several times of feeling it since he bought it, they saw it was only filled with mold, leading them all to burst into laughter.

Mr. Kim Hwal-ran's recent trip to America is well-known to everyone. However, I heard recently that he has enrolled in a university and plans to study for about three years before returning. This time, he intends to come back with a doctoral degree. When will he get married? <104>  

**Summary**

This article provides a humorous anecdote about Li Gi-yong's imprudent behavior and boasts regarding his finances along with a brief update on Kim Hwal-ran's academic pursuits in America. The references to social activities and aspirations provide insight into the social dynamics and aspirations of individuals during the colonial period in Korea. Historians may find the informal nature of social interactions, coupled with the aspirations tied to education abroad, indicative of changing societal values in colonial Korea during the 1930s.

風聞帖
李箕永씨는 醉後이면 피천 한 푼 업시 텅텅 븨인 紙匣을 가지고도 호기스럽게 한 卓을 낸다고 장담하시는 일이 종종 잇는데 요전에도 엇던 노리터에서 누가 맥부를 낸다고 하고는 시원치 안케 내이닛가 例의 李君는 豪談을 하며 그까짓 놈이 안내면 내가 낸다고...가튼 친구들은 반신반의하야 그의 지갑을 또 들처 본 즉 돈이라고는 그 지갑을 산 뒤에 몇번이나 느어 보앗는지 그 감을(旱)통에도 곰팡이만 가득 세엿서서 일단은 모다 呵呵大笑를 하얏다.
金活蘭씨가 요전에 米國에 간 것은 누구나 다 아는 바이다, 그런데 최근 소식을 드를즉 그는 모 대학에 입학하야 약 3년간 공부를 하고 귀국할 예정인데, 이번은 기어히 박사 학위를 어더 가지고야 온다고요. 결혼은 언제?＜104＞
