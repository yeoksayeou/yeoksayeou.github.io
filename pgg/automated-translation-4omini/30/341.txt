**Summary**  

This article presented a prize quiz where readers were invited to guess an item depicted in an accompanying photograph that was neither alive nor dead, rewarding correct answers with prizes. The high level of engagement from participants reflects the interest of the time in both intellectual challenges and the potential for rewards. This engagement also indicates how toys, stationery, and personal items like clothing served as significant interests during Korea's colonial period, revealing hints of social life and consumer culture within the context of the Japanese occupation.
