기사제목	地方紹介欄을 新設 하면서 (Establishing the Local Introduction Section)  
저자	別乾坤編輯部 (Byeolgeongon Editorial Department)  
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)  

With this issue, we have newly established a Local Introduction section. If this can serve even a little to inform our readers about the circumstances and customs of various regions, it would be quite a great fortune. As this is the initial issue, we kindly ask for your understanding regarding the many shortcomings present. Going forward, we hope to receive many contributions from readers concerning cities such as Daegu (大邱), Incheon (仁川), Wŏnsan (元山), Busan (釜山), Jeonju (全州), and Gunsan (群山) among other local cities.

  

**Summary**  

This article announces the establishment of a new section dedicated to showcasing the local customs and conditions of various Korean regions in the magazine. The editorial expresses hope for reader contributions about different cities, indicating an intention to broaden awareness of regional diversity within Korea. From a historical perspective, this initiative can be seen as an effort to foster a sense of national identity and understanding among Koreans during the Japanese colonial period, highlighting the importance of local culture amidst colonial suppression.

기사형태	사고·편집후기

地方紹介欄을 新設 하면서
이번 號부터 地方紹介欄을 신설하엿다. 이것으로써 독자에게 각 지방의 事情風物을 알니게 하는 도움이 조곰이라도 잇게 된다면 적지 아니한 다행일가 한다. 금번호는 시초인 만큼 부족한 점이 만히 잇는 것을 양해하고, 다음부터 계속될 大邱, 仁川, 元山, 釜山, 全州, 群山등 기타 지방도시에 관한 독자의 투고가 만히 잇기를 바라는 바이다.
別乾坤編輯部
