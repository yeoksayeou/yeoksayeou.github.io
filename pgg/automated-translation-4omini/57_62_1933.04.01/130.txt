萬華鏡  
Mr. Park Dal-seong's Tobacco Business  

Mr. Park Dal-seong, who referred to himself as a "nation's silver" and made more than 300 daily service engagements, would raise eyebrows if he turned to liquor sales, but his venture into tobacco sales surprises few. After suffering from nerve disease for three years, he completely severed ties with alcohol, and rather than "flying high with alcohol," he now has no intention even of approaching it; he enjoys fruits, rice cakes, and tobacco instead. Lately, feeling bored and without other sources of income, he opened a small tobacco shop in the house he newly moved into on Jeongdong Street. 

This amiable man, if he were still to drink as he used to and run a liquor store, would surely invite his close friends over and sell drinks while offering a toast. However, now that he has forsaken alcohol due to his chronic illness and seeing that his health has deteriorated, he has taken on a brisk tobacco business. One cannot simply sample a cigarette like they would a drink. If someone asks to taste the tobacco, he playfully shakes his head, saying, "No, I don't have it; I'm brimming with affection without it."

**Summary**  

The article discusses Mr. Park Dal-seong's unexpected transition from a renowned drinker to a tobacco seller due to health reasons. His previous affinity for alcohol has been replaced by a new source of livelihood in tobacco. This story reflects the broader behaviors of individuals navigating health and societal roles during the colonial period in Korea, marking a shift in lifestyle choices and economic adaptations. Historians may find it revealing about the societal pressures and personal struggles faced by individuals during the Japanese colonial era, particularly how they altered their livelihoods in response to health and colonial circumstance.

萬華鏡
朴達成氏의 담배장사
자층 주국천자라 하고 일일수경 300배 이상을 하던 박달성씨가 술장사를 한다면 누구나 괴이치 안케 생각하겟지만 담배장사를 한다면 의외로 생각할 것이다. 그는 신경병으로 수삼년 고생을 한 뒤로는 술과는 아주 인연을 끈어서 과맥전대취가 아니라 맥전이라면 그 근처에 갈 생각도 하지 안코 과실과 떡 담배만 잘 먹는데 요새는 심심하고 다른 수입도 업고 하니까 재동 거리에 새로 이사한 집에다 조고마한 담배가게를 새로 내고 부처가 담배장사를 한다. 쾌남아인 그가 만일 전과 가티 술을 잘 먹고 술장사를 한다면 친한 친구들을 불너서 한 잔식 주며 술을 잘 팔어달나고 하겟지만은 술을 끈코 중병지여가 되야 호협풍도 감소한데다가 리가 박하고 빡빡한 담배장사를 하고 보니 그 담배야 술처럼 한 대 먹어보라고 할 수 잇스랴. 누가 작난으로 담배 맛 좀 보자면 「안되야 우리 리문이 업서 하고 머리를 휘휘 두르며 무자긔한 애교를 부린다고」.＜25＞
