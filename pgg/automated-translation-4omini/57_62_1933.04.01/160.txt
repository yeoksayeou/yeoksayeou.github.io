**Summary**  

This comic depicts a reunion between two individuals, reflective of social interactions during the cherry blossom season. The character's expressions convey nostalgia and the significance of seasonal gatherings. Such interpersonal relationships hint at larger cultural practices during the Japanese colonial period in Korea, which would interest historians studying the social dynamics and cultural exchanges during that era.
