Prize Question  
This month's prize question is something more unusual. That is, "What is it that adds one to one and becomes one?" It is a principle that 1+1=2, yet somehow it becomes 1. The answer is simply ■■■.  

Prize Submission Regulations  
★ It must reach the headquarters by the 25th of this month (April).  
★ Write the answer simply on a postcard-sized inquiry and be sure to include your address and name.  
★ You must also write the reader's certificate included in this issue on the answer sheet. Not writing it will result in invalidation.  
★ On the envelope, be sure to write "京城開闢社別乾坤懸賞係" and on the flap write "原稿在中" in red ink. If you do not write "原稿在中," you will incur a penalty (shortage).  

Prizes  
1st place: 1 person - Custom-made decorative table clock  
2nd place: 2 people - Custom-made notebook set  
3rd place: 99 people - A book each  

Last month’s correct answer  
[Question] In the selected reading materials "피무든手帖" and "嫉妬하는 惡魔" (Jealous Demon), how many main characters are there?  
[Correct Answer] "피무든手帖" has three people, and "嫉妬하는 惡魔" has four people.  

Out of 59,600 submissions, only a quarter got the correct answer. It seems this confusion arose from mixing up the total number of characters mentioned in both readings with the main characters. We conducted a drawing, and the winners are as follows:
