기사제목	졸업을 하고 나니! (After Graduation!)  
필자	熊超  
기사형태	만화 (Cartoon)  

After graduation, it seems I need to find something to do! So, I got an unpaid job standing in front of the window display at a department store for 30 minutes.  

<39>  

**Summary**  

The article humorously reflects on the uncertainty and challenges faced by individuals after graduation, particularly in the context of finding employment during that era. Through a light-hearted illustration of securing an unpaid position, it captures the socio-economic realities of the time, especially concerning youth employment and the impact of the colonial situation in Korea. Historians may find this piece revealing in how it highlights the struggle for independence and self-sufficiency amid limiting opportunities during the colonial period.
