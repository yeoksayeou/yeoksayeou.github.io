발행일	1928년 08월 01일  
기사제목	本誌九月號는 (The September Issue of This Magazine)  
저자	없음  
기사형태	소식 (News)  

The September issue of this magazine will focus on how, in this progressing age, one could live solely on thoughts and intellect. Is it possible for anyone to continue living in such a manner? The upcoming September issue will be titled “New Life Issue,” aiming to introduce everyone’s criticisms and opinions, as it is our duty as Koreans to participate in this matter. The fixed price remains at 52 jeon, so please place your orders in advance.

  

**Summary**  

The article announces the upcoming September issue of the magazine, which seeks to engage Korean readers in discussions on living a fulfilling life through the collective sharing of ideas. It suggests that intellectual exploration is vital in the context of the times, inviting readers to contribute their perspectives. This reflects the period's broader movements toward modernization and the struggle for identity within the cultural framework of colonial Korea.
