**Summary**  

This article serves as an announcement regarding the establishment of new branch offices in various regions. It details the names of the branch offices, their locations, and leadership personnel, indicating a growing organizational structure to enhance local coverage. Notably, it emphasizes the importance of regional reporting during the colonial period by designating specific individuals to lead these branches, ultimately suggesting an effort to strengthen communication and information dissemination in Korea during Japanese rule. Historians may find this insight significant as it reflects the administrative strategies employed by Korean entities at a time of colonial oppression.
