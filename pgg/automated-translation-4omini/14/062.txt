**Summary**  

This article announces the establishment of the new branch named "靈光分社" (Spirit Light Branch), located in Jeonnam (全南) at Yeonggwang (靈光邑). Kim Kil-seong (金吉成) has been appointed as the branch manager. The inception of this branch may indicate expanding media presence in Korea during a time when Japanese colonial control attempts to tighten its grip on cultural domains. Historians might find interest in this article as it reflects the colonial administration's strategies in promoting local governance and media as a means of control over Korean society.
