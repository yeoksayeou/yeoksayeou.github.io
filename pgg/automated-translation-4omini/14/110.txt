기사제목	準敎員懺悔錄 (Confessions of a Trainee Teacher)  
필자	ESC  
기사형태	회고·수기 (Memoir)

I was an authoritative middle school teacher almost ten years ago. However, perhaps because I was the eldest son of another family carrying other's debts, my family frequently faced financial troubles. Therefore, out of necessity, I began giving private English lessons to several students in the evenings. 

At first, there were three young ladies around twenty years old who came to me by chance. As they began to come and go often, the parents of these young women started to gossip, claiming it was inappropriate for a man to visit at night, which led to this becoming an exclusive matter for the girls. So, over time, around five female students regularly attended. 

As the days progressed, some students were diligent in their studies for a while, but more and more of them began to simply quit after some time. However, among them, there were two girls who consistently studied diligently, regardless of harsh nights or blizzards. 

I feel very uncomfortable using their names now, but I will refer to these two women as 'A' and 'B.' A was a particularly clever girl, and I was sometimes impressed by her. It could be said that she had a talent for languages. She was also relatively pretty, yet there was one notable drawback; she had a seriously shortened leg, making her a complete cripple. Both her parents were not living, and she lived under the burden of her brother’s family until adulthood.

In contrast, B was the daughter of a well-off family from Seoul. While her features were not striking, she seemed to have a certain charm and an air of a well-bred daughter of a large household. 

Therefore, when I met these two women—each with very different characters and backgrounds—every evening, rather than merely thinking 'let's treat them diligently according to their wishes,' I frequently found myself lost in all sorts of strange philosophical thoughts.

For several months, alternating between writing letters for A and B, in addition to teaching English texts, I began to occasionally share stories from novels and my subjective critiques on them. Through these experiences, it was quite certain that a 'teacher-student bond' had been formed among the three of us.

On the rare occasions when B was unable to attend due to illness, A would come by herself and, without reservation, express her views similar to her life philosophy. I often found myself engaging in deep discussions about current events and foreign customs with her. A was quite sharp and would pose refreshing questions. Each time this happened, I experienced a satisfaction beyond words.

As time went on, I would eagerly await the evenings when I could meet A at the school. My affection towards A had grown to such an extent that my previously strong rationality began to fade. Forecasting her arrival, I feigned illness at home and pretended to rest.

However, the situation turned out to be quite mischievous and unfortunate. On that particular evening when I was expecting A, B unexpectedly arrived quite early, which was unusual for her. At that moment, the pleasant conversational tone I'd usually had with her was nowhere to be found. 

As time passed with no sight of A, B ultimately decided to excuse herself, saying, “Please take care of your health, Teacher.” 

While I felt relieved at B's departure, I was still filled with a growing agitation. The resentful thoughts like, “Why on such an evening had A not come?” tormented my entire body. The devilish temptation strengthened itself, and a primal urge began to torment me without mercy. 

At this moment, I distinctly heard A's voice mixed in with my wife's gentle tones, which suddenly silenced my feelings of resentment. I simply wanted to throw off my inner weariness and run to A, even if it was just in my underwear. 

However, for some reason, I felt overwhelmingly scared of my wife at that moment. I could only manage to whisper, “Please come in quickly.” 

“Oh, Teacher? Are you sick? I came to help with my older sister’s guests from the countryside who just arrived. I'm taking care of them.” 

Upon hearing this, I was entirely transfixed on A’s smiling mouth, my fervent gaze akin to that of a madman. 

“Are you in pain?” A asked me, as if she was genuinely trying to assess the burning desire that had been igniting within me. 

After that moment, my actions became a blur. I really lost myself, completely unaware of how I ended up grasping her hand or what sort of embrace we shared. 

At one point, after A had left me in a bewildered state, I finally realized what I had done when I saw her tearfully rushing out, unable to speak amidst her intense excitement. 

Once I regained my clarity of mind, I leaped up in a panic, searching for A in every corner of my house, calling her name. However, all I received in response was the unwelcoming voice of my wife asking, “Who are you calling?” from deep inside the room. 

After such an incident, I continued to feel regret towards A, blaming her for never answering me again. Despite using every means to send her letters countless times, even discussing rumors in exchange for her messages, I only ended up cursing her. 

One day, B said, “Maybe something strange happened with A’s family. I haven’t seen her lately, no matter where I go. It seems she doesn’t come around anymore. What is going on?” I responded vaguely, but I still thought poorly of A, calling her foolish and not fitting for a modern woman.

While I looked down on her, deep down, I felt considerable pain. I kept thinking about how pitiful A must be. 

Afterwards, even up until that point, I still didn’t have the courage to stop teaching B. 

As months passed amidst remorse and mental anguish, I continued being a gloomy private tutor for four to five months until one day, B, who had been continuously studying harder than ever, suddenly appeared at my house without any plausible reason.

The fact was that those who knew my situation had likely found out. 

When I learned of a rumor floating around that A had gone missing after experiencing a sudden crisis in her life and had run away from her brother’s home, I prepared my feeble belongings and went out, pretending I was off on a trip to find A. 

I searched for her for a month, but how she had been faring was beyond my comprehension. When I finally returned to my own house exhausted, another significant thing had happened.

On a particularly bright autumn evening, my wife discreetly left a note for me after putting our child to bed, and then suddenly disappeared. 

After that, I left the school as well. I am not sure if I resigned or was dismissed. Even after ten years, I remain ignorant of A’s whereabouts, and my wife’s situation is just as bleak, while I dwell helplessly in my current unfortunate state of being a mere beggar. 

Enduring all this suffering, it has come to the point where I am nothing but a worthless individual managing to survive. Yet, every time I see a sign related to “private tutoring,” it instills in me immediate suspicion and conjures horrifying images of the tragedy that unfolded in my life, alongside memories of my sins resurfacing, mercilessly hollowing out what remains of my spirit.

Dear readers! To all you privileged people who engage in private tutoring, do you truly possess the sort of wisdom to hear my honest confession and comprehend its implications? 

**Summary**

The author reflects on his past as a middle school teacher and the complicated relationships he formed with two female students, A and B, leading to a personal crisis. His unacknowledged feelings of love towards A culminate in a moment of overwhelming passion, resulting in a series of regrets and eventual separation. The narrative reveals the struggles of a man dealing with the pressures of society, emotional complexities, and the painful consequences of unreciprocated love. This article provides insight into gender dynamics and the cultural constraints of the colonial period, illustrating how personal desires could clash with societal expectations, a point of interest for historians exploring the societal changes during Korea's colonial era.

나의 죄를 풍편에 듯고 보니 A는 배 불은 몸이 되여 그의 헝님 집에서 부댓기다 못해 몃칠 전에 거긔서 뛰여나와 행방불명이 되엿다는 긔맥히는 소문 허설인지? 사실인지? 나는 살필틈 업시 나의 눈 압흔 캄캄하엿젓슴니다. 나는 곳 빈약한 행구를 차려가지고 『려행 갓다 오마』는 구실 아래 A의 갓슬듯한 곳을 차저 나갓슴니다.
한달 동안이나 두고 아모리 두루 차젓스되 알 길은 업섯슴니다. 모든 넉을 다-일코 집에 도라 오니 또 한가지 큰 일이 생기여 잇섯슴니다.
초가을 달이 유심히도 밝든 팔월 열사흔날 밤중에 나의 안해는 어린 아해를 재워놋코 편지 한 장 남겨 두고 돌연히 자최를 감초엿든 것임니다. 그 후로 나는 학교에서도 나왓슴니다. 내가 사직을 한 심인지? 면직을 당한 심인지?
10년 후 이때것 A의 소식은 암흑 중에 싸힌 채이고 안해의 소식은 듯기는 듯고 잇스나 맛나 보지 못할 헝편에 처하고 잇슴니다.
전일 이 중학교 선생이고 개인 교사이든 내가 지금은 맥업시 떠도는 불상한 일개의 병신이 되고 말엇슴니다. 이 고생 저 고생 다 달게 밧으면서 막치기 장사나 해먹는 보잘 것 업는 신분에 이르고 말엇지만 지금이라도 『개인교수』 한다는 간판을 보면 나는 당장에 의심을 품게 되며 엇더한 무서운 비극을 머리에 그리게 되는 동시에 나의 죄의 추억이 나의 다-허터진 가삼을 새롭게 찌저 갈기는 듯한 감을 늣기게 됨니다.
