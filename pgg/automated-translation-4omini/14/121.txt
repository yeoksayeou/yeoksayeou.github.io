기사제목	開闢社의 支分社大募集 (Call for Major Recruitment of Branch Offices of the Gaebyeok Society)  
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Article)  

The Gaebyeok Society is seeking enthusiastic individuals to establish branch or sub-branch organizations to actively collaborate in our projects for local culture. By becoming a member of this esteemed and grand organization, you will simultaneously enhance your own position and gain knowledge that keeps you from trailing behind in the world, while also becoming a noble figure who contributes to the advancement of local culture. 

If you are interested, please enclose a ten-won postal money order and request an information booklet from our office. We will send you detailed regulations. <55>

  
**Summary**  

This article serves as a call to action for individuals interested in contributing to local cultural initiatives by joining or establishing branches of the Gaebyeok Society. It emphasizes the dual benefits of personal advancement and cultural development. Historians might find it interesting as it reflects the efforts during the colonial period to engage citizens in cultural endeavors, showcasing a blend of local empowerment and colonial oversight.
