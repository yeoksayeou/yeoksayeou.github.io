An Unbearable Hobby, Kiss  

There is an old man who drinks well, and he was told that if he stops drinking, he would become blind.  
"Would you rather choose to stop drinking or become blind? Which will you choose?"  
"Yes. I am already sixty years old. I have not seen much of the world, so it's fine. I can always drink..."  

Kiss  

One day, a young man was caught for kissing a young woman. Thus, the woman was summoned for questioning.  
"Did that man kiss you against your will and forcefully?"  
"Yes. Ten times..."  
"Wow, then you must not have kissed that man yourself?"  
The woman was left speechless.  
"Speak truthfully. If you don't..."  
Finally, the woman managed to answer,  
"I also kissed him. However, for self-defense..."  
Thus, the trial was canceled. <108>  

**Summary**  

The article discusses the themes of indulgence and the societal expectations surrounding actions deemed inappropriate, such as alcohol consumption and kissing. The anecdote illustrates how personal desires conflict with societal standards, showcasing a casual attitude towards both topics in a humorous light. This reflects a broader social context in 1920s Korea where traditional values were increasingly challenged, pointing towards changing gender dynamics and evolving norms in public behavior. Historians might find interest in how such narratives reveal the complex interplay between personal freedoms and societal restraints during Korea’s colonial period.

술 잘먹는 로인 하나이 술을 끈치 안으면 소경이 되리라는 선고를 밧앗것다.
『당신이 술을 끈턴지 소경이 되던지 둘 중에 하나를 고르시요. 어느 편을 취하겟슴닛가?』
『네. 벌서 나희 륙십이라. 세상일을 별로 못 본 것이 업스니 넉넉하지요. 술만은 암만해도......』

키스

엇던 젊은 사내가 남의 처녀에게 키쓰하얏다고 잡혓것다. 그래서 그 처녀도 심문을 밧게 되얏다.
『저 사내는 네의 의지(意志)를 반대하고 억지로 키쓰하엿단 말이냐?』
『네. 열번씩이나......』
『허허 참 그럼 너는 그 사내와 키쓰하지 안엇겟지?』
처녀는 말이 막혓다.
『얘 실상대로 말해 보아라. 그럿치 안으면......』
처녀는 겨우 대답하기를
『나도 하엿담니다. 그러나 자긔 방어(自己防禦)를 위하야서......』
그래서 재판은 취소되고 말앗다.<108>
