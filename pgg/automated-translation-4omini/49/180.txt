短想二三  
								  
戀愛  
To be unaware of love between men and women is no trait of humanity. One might argue that even a cold stone could exhibit such ignorance. 

However, it is also not human to charge into life believing that love constitutes the entirety of one’s existence. Such behavior might only align with the fleeting satisfaction of carnal desires, akin to the instincts of a dog or pig.

藝術  
Just as "licorice" is essential in traditional Chinese medicine, beauty in all forms of art cannot be fully realized without effort and elements to denote it. 

In literature, there is the beauty of rhetoric; in drama, the beauty of movement, and so on... "Beauty" ultimately signifies something that can be simply understood as "done well" or "skillfully executed." Therefore, one could conclude that a "well-made" beauty might well represent a masterpiece of natural artistry.

矛盾  
In ancient times, soldiers would set out to war grasping a spear in one hand and a shield in the other, suggesting that those who engage in combat have long practiced contradictory behaviors. 

While calling for peace, they expand military spending and advocate for societal improvements while pursuing their private gains and other interests... Perhaps the truth lies in this contradiction. <33>  

**Summary**  

This text reflects on the themes of love, art, and contradiction in human behavior, offering philosophical insights. It critiques the notion of love as a singular focus in life and emphasizes the necessity of effort in the pursuit of beauty in art. Moreover, it draws a parallel between ancient military practices and contemporary societal contradictions. Historians might find the commentary on Philippine social conditions and human nature, especially under colonial rule, intriguing as it hints at broader philosophical reflections pertinent to societal struggles in colonial Korea.

短想二三
						盧良根
戀愛
男女間에 戀愛를 모르는 것은 사람이 아니다. 차듸찬 돌맹이의 四寸은 될지 모른다.
그러나 또한 戀愛를 自己生活의 全部로 알고 덤비는 무리도 사람이 아니다. 그것은 一時的 性慾의 滿足만을 채우랴고 날치는 개나 돼지의 兄弟는 될는지 모른다.

藝術
漢藥에 「甘草」가 빠지고는 되지 못하드시 모든 藝術에는 이러니 저러니 해야 美라는 것이 들지 안코는 일우어지지 못할 것이다.
文藝에 修辭美 劇에 動作美 등등. ...
「美」라는 것은 결국 얼는 알기 쉽게 말하면 「조타」「잘되엿다」「잘한다」라는 것을 意味한 것이니까. ... 그러타면 「잘낫다」는 美人은 天然的 藝術의 傑作品의 하나인지도 모른다.

矛盾
옛적에 戰爭에 나가는 병사는 한 손에 矛(창) 한 손에 盾(방패) 두 가지를 함께 다 물고 나갓다 하니 남과 싸화 익이려는 者는 예나 이제나 矛盾的 行爲를 잘하는 가 부다. 平和를 부르지즈며 軍費를 擴張하고 社會를 云云하면서 自己 私服을 위하며 其他. ... 등등.
아마도 矛盾에 眞理가 잇는 게다.＜33＞
