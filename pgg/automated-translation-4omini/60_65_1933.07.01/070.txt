기사제목	다구진 處女市長 (The Maiden Mayor of Dagujin)  
기사형태	잡저 (Magazine Article)  

The newly elected mayor of the city of "Palcos" in the Spanish Republic is the 23-year-old maiden "Teodora Medeabira." In a world where men are not lacking, the choice of a maiden as a mayor seems to imply that the situation must have been particularly grave. However, upon investigating the reality, there appears to be a plausible reason. The maiden has demonstrated a boldness that surpasses even that of men, so much so that during the turmoil between over half of the "Catholic" faithful and socialist workers, she boldly intervened without regard for her personal safety and worked hard to restore order. While there were certainly numerous casualties, it is a stroke of fortune that Miss Medeabira emerged with only minor injuries. With such courage, she has garnered a reputation as an outstanding mayor. <22>

  
**Summary**  

This article discusses the election of a 23-year-old woman, Teodora Medeabira, as the mayor of a city in Spain during a tumultuous period marked by conflict between various factions. It highlights her bravery and ability to emerge as a leader despite the gender norms of the time, offering insight into the growing recognition of women's roles in politics. Historians might find this particularly interesting as it reflects broader trends of women's empowerment in a global context during the early 20th century, an era that witnessed significant social change.

다구진 處女市長
스페인(西班牙) 공화국의 「팔고스」시(市)에 갓가운 「그리할비」의 시장은 방긔(芳紀) 23세의 묘령(妙齡) 처녀 「테오드라.매데아뷔라」양이다. 남자가 모자라는 세상도 아니건만 처녀를 시장(市長)으로 취대(推戴)함은 너무도 작난이 심햇다고 보겟다. 그러나 실상을 조사해 본즉 그럴듯한 리유가 잇다. 처녀는 연약한 녀자의 몸으로서 남자도 따르지 못할 만큼 대담스러워 과반 「카도릭크」敎徒와 사회주의 로동자와 사이에 란투(亂鬪)가 이러낫슬때 그 가운데 뛰어드러가 긔의 몸을 돌보지 안코 秩序회복에 노력하엿다. 그대에 死傷者도 물론 만히 낫지만 「메데아뷔라」양은 조고만 상처도 밧지 안은 것은 천행이엇다. 이만한 용긔를 가진 녀자인지라 명시장(名市長)의 평을 듯고 잇다.＜22＞
