기사제목	號外의 虛外 (The Supplement of the Supplement)  
기사형태	세태비평 (Social Critique)  

The last month saw newspapers furiously publishing extras as they rushed to deliver urgent news. Especially notable was the case of the five-year prison sentence in Taesan Myeongdong related to a father being held accountable for the death of his child, which made for particularly sensational extras. However, the inspiration for this extra of Byeolgeongon seems to have caught a cold and thus took a break.

While one newspaper published a novel titled "The Era of Extras", today’s newspapers could just as easily be called the "Era of Extra Mania" (號外狂時代). Running out of content, they resort to publishing extras... even if Jiang Jieshi dies from a lie, they’ll publish an extra... claiming it's a falsehood. At this rate, even if Jiang Jieshi claims he was in hiding, will they cease to publish an extra?

Oh, dear newspapers! What relevance does Jiang Jieshi being injured by a bomb, or the rumor that it was a hoax, have to the Korean people?

Kim Dong-jin from the social department of the Dong-A Ilbo has reportedly been promoted to a managerial position at the Chosun Ilbo. I've heard he’s being taken to the Tokyo bureau, which is sure to stir up considerable advertising competition in Japan through his diplomatic activities. Likewise, Seo Beom-seok of Dong-A Ilbo has resigned to join the Manwung Ilbo. Just because these two have left, does it mean no one else will step up? Nowadays, Dong-A Ilbo is losing ground, with ongoing competition in both local and city markets, and Chosun Ilbo seems to be absorbing all the "Da" newspapers; what do we say about this?

Chosun Ilbo is extremely diligent about familial and academic articles. Please stop criticizing these sections just because advertisements are increasing; rather, it would be better to slow down the publication than to undermine the integrity of academic and family content. The reason the Japanese "Yomiuri" newspaper stands strong in the academic and family sections is something Korean newspapers may or may not realize.

A newspaper modeled on a news-centric format may indeed be necessary at times. However, has Korean journalism actually reached such heights?

As the Chosun Ilbo prepares to relocate back to its previous home, I hope you will get more energized while you’re at it. I hear the Chosun Ilbo is moving into a temporary office now located in Taepyeong-dong?  

**Summary**  

The article critiques the proliferation of extras published by newspapers in 1933 Korea amidst sensational news, highlighting the absurdity of their content and questioning the relevance of events like Jiang Jieshi's bomb injury to the Korean populace. It reflects upon the state of Korean journalism, lamenting the shift away from meaningful content focused on community and academic matters in favor of clicks and sensationalism. Historians might find this piece notable for its commentary on media practices in the colonial context and the pressing need for responsible journalism during a tumultuous period.

뭇노니 여보 신문*들이여! 장개석이가 폭탄을 맛고 부상한 것이나 *는 그것이 헛소문이라 *것이 조선의 민중과 무슨 그리 큰 관련이잇소?

동아일보의 사회부 노장 김동진군은 조선일보의 영업차장으로 영전을 하엿겟다. 들으니 김군은 동경지국으로 보낼 양으로 데려갓다니 군의 외교수단으로 하면 동경의 광고게에 상당한 쎈쎄-순을 일으키겟지.
또 동시 동아의 서범석군이 만웅일보로 가느라고 동아를 사직햇다.
두분이 나갓다고 다시 사람이 업슬리야＜24＞ 업겟지만 요지음 동아일보는 지방것이고 시내것이고 굴다란 「다*」를 모조리 조선일보에 「누아사레루」하니 뭇건대 그 무삼일인고?

조선일보는 가정연과 학예연에 매우 충실하다. 제발 압흐로 광고가 는다고 누구처럼 가정연, 학예연을 괄세하지 말고 차라리 지면을 느릴지언정 학대는 하지 말기를-. 일본의 「요미우리」신문이 대조대*와 겉우어 나가는 것이 학예연, 가정연이 충실한 때문인 것을 조선의 신문씨들은 아는지 모르는지.
뉴-스 본위의 대신문식의 신문도 어느시기에는 필요하다. 그러나 조선의 신문이 과연 그처럼 대신문의 역에 달하엿는가?
장개석이 폭탄마진것이 거짓말이라는 호외를 내일 성의는 잇스면서 정작 독자에게 고마움 학예, 가정의 주연을 괄세하는 것은 민중의 목탁」이 아니라 차라리 「민족의 공탁」이다.

중앙일보가 조선일보의 녯날 집으로 옴기어 간다. 기왕 갈길이니 집도 널지하고 좀더 기운을 내시요.
