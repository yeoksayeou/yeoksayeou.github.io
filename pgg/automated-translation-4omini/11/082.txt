New Spring March Grand Special Issue on Korean Pride  
Korea's pride! Korea's pride! Indeed, we also have pride. We have shining pride! The power that allows us to take pride! It is the tremendous power that overcomes all misfortunes of our nation and creates new life. Indeed! We have that tremendous pride. Let us stop crying, wipe away our tears, and let us celebrate our pride in this book!!

An essential addition to every household, a grand record to be handed down for ten years and even a hundred years!  
An unprecedented special issue with greatly increased pages, priced at seventy coins.  
Not only to be enjoyed by oneself but also to be shared with the elderlies, with women, with laborers, with the servants, and to be handed down to growing boys and girls.

"Korea's Pride!" has been around before, and indeed, there will still be people who cherish it today. However, how this editorially unique "별건곤" (Byeolgeon-gon) will joyfully and delightfully treat this matter is yet to be revealed. Collecting the pride from our history of half a million years, and comparing it to the pride of various countries and nations around the world, it will reveal an extraordinary and exquisite pride article that is not to be missed in an entertaining magazine. It is only mentioned here that this will be a record that the writers, even more than the readers, will take care to preserve and cherish <59>.

  
**Summary**  

This article in the special issue celebrates Korea's heritage and pride, urging its readers to embrace their national identity positively. It emphasizes the significance of sharing Korean culture across generations and highlights its vast historical context with pride. The publication reflects the early 20th-century effort to foster a sense of nationalism amidst colonial rule and may indicate early movements towards cultural preservation in Korean history, which historians might find fascinating in examining Korea's struggle for identity and autonomy during this period.
