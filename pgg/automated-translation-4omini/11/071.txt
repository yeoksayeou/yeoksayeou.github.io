Submission Welcome  

There are strange customs hidden in every nook and corner, so we kindly ask local readers to write about such customs from their hometown and submit them to our magazine's "Local Color" section. (Editorial Department) <53>  

**Summary**  

The article invites readers, particularly from local areas, to share unique cultural customs specific to their regions by submitting articles to the magazine's "Local Color" section. This highlights a growing interest in cultural documentation during the colonial period, reflecting an effort to capture and preserve local traditions amidst broader changes. Historians may find it interesting that this call for contributions emphasizes the importance of regional identity in contrast to the overarching narrative of colonial dominance.
