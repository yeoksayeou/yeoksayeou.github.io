기사제목	朝鮮初有의 創作大衆讀物 名士連作大講談 (The First Original Mass Publication of Korea: Collaborative Tales by Notable Figures)  
기사형태	소식 (News)  

The first original mass publication in Korea is a special collaborative work consisting of tales written by various notable figures, surpassing both essays and novels in its grand scope. The authors participating in this project are as follows, in alphabetical order: 

Kim Gi-chon, Lee Sang-hyeob, Lee Dun-hwa, Park Hee-do, Hyon Yu-jeon, Song Jin-woo, An Jae-hong, Jeong Dae-hyeon, Choi Nam-seon, Hong Myeong-hee.

Within "Byeolgongon," this unprecedented new project is unfolding — a special collection of tales that are more captivating than novels. Each author contributes a unique story, creating an engaging narrative that discusses events previously created by others. It will be interesting to see how the participating authors handle these events and how they develop their work. 

As the authors are not only writers of fiction but also significant figures in the public discourse, it is worth noting how they will tackle various events and how they choose to portray individuals and their activities. Those who consistently ponder the realities of Korea — considering “Korean life” in their works — aim to present their thoughts to the broader population. This implies that the work is indeed a significantly larger narrative than typical novels, simultaneously serving as a more substantial essay than ordinary scholarly papers. This aspect marks the piece as something that the multitude of readers should pay keen attention to.

The titles and order of the contributions will be announced in the next issue.

**Summary**

This article discusses a unique collaborative anthology set to be published in Korea, featuring notable figures from various fields contributing stories that engage in discussions about Korean life and events. This anthology highlights the blending of storytelling and social commentary, suggesting that it will attract significant public interest. Historians may find this compilation relevant as it showcases the intellectual engagement of authors during the colonial period, reflecting their perspectives on Korean society and its challenges.

『別乾坤』안이면 計劃하지못할 空前의 新計劃인 이 特別大連作讀物은 小說보다 興味잇는 講談을 各方面의 名士諸氏가 一人一回式에 을 連달어 創作해가는것이니 남의 創作한 內容事件을 彼此相議엄는 다음 執筆者들이 엇더케 取扱하야 엇더케 展開식여나갈넌지……興味잇기로 天下에 比類가업는 讀物인데 더욱 執筆者諸氏가 小說創作家가안인만큼 非創作家의 創作인 點에 이 連作이 한層의 興味를 더하는 것이외다.
그러고 執筆者諸氏가 或은 民衆言論機關의 主幹이요 或은 民衆大團體의 幹部요  或은 敎育界의 巨人들이니 그들이 將次 엇더한 事件을 取扱하야 讀者의압헤 公開할넌지………엇더한 人物을 지여서 엇더케 活動을 식일넌지………平生을다밧처 朝鮮을 생각하고 「朝鮮의 生活」을 생각하는그들이 이 作品에 그생각해온바를 다쏫아서 朝鮮의 大衆에게 提示하려는것이슬것이라 이 作品은 實로 小說以上의 大小說인 同時에 한 論文以上의 大論文이외다. 이 點에서 이 作品은 千萬大衆이 다갓치 注意하야닑어야할것이외다.
