笑話三篇  

亲切  
While a friend was riding a bicycle and passing through a village as the day was drawing to a close, he asked a young man from that village for directions. Apologetically, the young man scratched his head and replied that he didn't know. After traveling about another 20 li without a plan, he finally arrived at a tavern, and after ordering some food and drink, he was approached by the young man from the village who said, "Aren't you the one who asked for directions in our village?" The friend replied, "I am sorry, but when I asked my brother for help, he also did not know."

웃기는  
One lady, the wife of the owner, was frowning and stood at the kitchen door saying, "Why do you drag such people into the kitchen? Last night around dinner time, I couldn't sleep a wink because of all the noise and laughter." She remarked to the attendant. The attendant responded, "But ma'am, you were the one who was making sweets that morning, and your friends were gathering around..."  

분푸리  
A young modern lady went to a flour shop and complained, "The flour I bought last time was too hard." "How can flour be hard...?" "Then? I made bread to give to my family, and it turned out too tough to eat at all, and they all complained." <89> <89-89>  

**Summary**  

This article presents three humorous anecdotes that illustrate various social interactions and misunderstandings in daily life. The stories touch upon themes common in colonial Korea, such as the clash of traditional and modern lifestyles, and serve as a lighthearted commentary on social etiquette and the struggles of communication. Historians might find the representation of social norms and the evolving roles of women during this period particularly interesting, as they reflect the complexities of life in colonial Korea.

親切
자전거를 타고 한참 모라가던 친구가 날이 저무러서 어느 동리를 지나다가 그 동리 젊은 친구에게 길을 무르닛가 아조 미안하다는 드시 머리를 득득 글그면서 모르겟다고 대답하는지라. 할일 업시 한 20리 더 가다가 겨우 주막에 드러서 사관을 정하고 자랴닛가 힐덕 헐덕하며 뒤를 쫏처온 앗가 동리 젊은이 「아이그 당신 아니심닛가. 악가 우리 동라 압헤서 길을 무르시던 이가」하고 별안간 대드러 무름으로 그럿다고 대답한 즉 「참 미안함니다마는 제 형에게 무러 보아도 역시 모르겟다고 하던데요.」

웃기는
주인 마누라님 한 분이 잔득 상을 찝푸리고는 부엌문 압헤 와 서서 「애 웬 사람을 부엌에까지 그리 끌어드리나. 어제ㅅ 저녁가튼 때는 무얼 가지고 그리 여러이 깔깔대고 짓거리는지 원 잠 한잠 못 잣네」하고 행랑어멈을 남으랫다. 「그럿치만 마님 어제 아침에 마님께서 과자 만드시던 니약이를 하닛가 동무들이 우서싸서...」

분푸리
