To the Readers, We Express Our Gratitude  
                                                   Editorial Note  
The commemorative issue for the first anniversary of our magazine, published in December, sold out a large number of extra prints within just three days, and the reprint that followed also sold out in a week, showing a remarkable increase in readership that is truly unprecedented since the founding of Gyeonggaksa.

The delay in issuing the book that was meant to be published on January 1st, which many have waited for throughout January and February, is something we cannot adequately express our apologies for. We hope you can understand that there have been circumstances that were out of our control.

With the great success of the December commemorative issue, all staff have been rejuvenated, tirelessly working day and night, not realizing we were exhausted, in hopes of achieving results that exceeded expectations. However, suddenly, the magazine called "Children," which was in circulation, was seized, resulting in the confiscation of our magazine and around 300 other publications nationwide. We went through a great deal of commotion dealing with the authorities and attending interrogations. After spending nearly a half-month in turmoil, several manuscripts concerning special circumstances finally received valid approval, but when considering the parts that were deleted (the sections not permitted), it became impossible to print the book as it was without changes. Thus, we had to conduct a third round of editing as there were still sections disallowed. During that time, January passed, and now half of February has gone by, which has caused the New Year’s articles to be too late, to the point where we made the decision to remove them ourselves. 

In saying all this, our only request is for your understanding regarding the unknown hardships we faced, and we hope for your compassion as well as any criticism you may have.

To those of you who placed advance orders and sent numerous letters of inquiry, we initially tried to send responses, but as the staff thought, "Will the book actually be published?" we were more focused on sending the book first rather than replying. Had we known it would be this delayed from the start, it would have been better to inform you in advance through a letter... We ask for your forgiveness for not being able to notify you individually as well.

Now, this book will be a combined issue for January and February. The March issue, as advertised separately, is set to be published as a special large issue filled with essential articles that will be necessary for every household, aiming to be released on March 20th for the first time since our publication. Please do not worry, and placing advance orders is still the safest approach. <178>  

**Summary**  

This article expresses gratitude to the readers for their patience and support during the publishing delays experienced by the magazine. It outlines the unfortunate circumstances they faced, including the seizure of their magazine and the disruption it caused. The challenges brought about by censorship during the colonial period are highlighted, reflecting the volatile environment for publications in Korea at that time. Historians might find the details of governmental censorship and its effects on cultural production particularly noteworthy, revealing how such restrictions influenced public discourse.

讀者 諸氏께 謝告함니다.
						社告
지난 12월에 발행한 본지 창간 1주년 기념호는 단 3일에 增刷大部數가 매진되고 곳 재판발행한 것이 단 1주간에 또 매진, 일거에 독자가 倍加되야 실로 개벽사 창설 이래 처음 되는 발매 성적을 示하얏슴니다.
뒤밧처 1월 1일에 발행할 이 책을 1월 한달 또 2월 한달 궁금히 기다리시게 하고 이제야 발행하게 된 것은 여러분깨 무어라고 사죄할 길이 업슴니다. 다만 그 간에는 이럿케 말못하게 딱한 사정이 잇섯슴을 짐작해 주시기 바랄 뿐임니다.
12월 기념호의 好成績에 사원 일동의 원기백배되야 疲困한 줄 모르고 連日連夜 徹夜活動을 계속하야 예고 이상의 收穫를 엇어서 편집종요! 허가가 속히 되기만 기다리고 잇는데 돌연히 「어린이」(발매 중에 잇던) 誌가 압수를 당하야 본지는 물론이요 전국 300여처에서 발매중이던 책을 몰수당하느라고 대소란을 격고 뒤니여 한편으로 당국으로 交하랴 또 한편으로 取調를 당하랴 ... 반개월여를 수선히 지내고 나서도 또 얼마를 지내 涉여 別乾坤의 원고가 겨우 허가되여 나왓스나 삭제된 것(不許可된 部分)이 생각밧게 만허서 도저히 그냥 그대로 뼈업는 책을 인쇄하여 내일 수가 업섯슴니다. 그래서 재차 활동 재차 편집 그래도 不許可된 부분이 잇서서 3차 편집을 곳처하엿슴니다. 그러는 동안에 1월이 지나고 2월이 또 半이나 지낫스니 신년기사는 때가 느저저 노아서 이번에는 우리 손으로 빼여 버리게까지 되엿슴니다. 이런 말을 길다락케 하면 무슨 소용이 잇겟슴닛가. 오즉 이러한 남모르는 苦情을 삶혀 주시고 꾸지람의 한편에 동정을 주시기 바라는 것 뿐임니다.

선금으로 미리 주문해 두시고 여러번 독촉의 편지를 보내주신 여러분께 처음에는 回報를 드렷섯스나 나종에는 사원들도 「곳 책이 발행되게 되겟지 되겟지」하고 밋고 잇섯든 고로 답보보다 책을 먼저 보내드리려고 오늘, 내일하고 잇섯던 것임니다. 아초부터 이럿케 느저질 줄 알앗드면 진즉 ＠書로라도 미리 통고해 드려두엇슬 것이 안이겟슴닛가... 이 점을 양해해 주시고 일일이 통지 못드린 것도 함께 용서해 주시기 바람니다.
이제 나와도 이 책은 1월, 2월 합호요. 3월호는 따로히 광고한 바와 가티 대대특집 家家戶戶에 必備할 중요기사를 모아 創刊後 처음 特別增大號로 기필코 3월 20일 발행되겟슴니다. 염려마시고 역시 주문은 미리 해 두시는 것이 안전함니다.＜178＞
