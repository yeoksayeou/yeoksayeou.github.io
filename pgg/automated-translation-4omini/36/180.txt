기사제목	奇案妙答 (Strange Cases, Ingenious Answers)  
기사형태	잡저 (Miscellaneous)  

A student in Seoul needed to ask for money to be sent to him, but he was strictly told that unless he specified a clear purpose, he would not receive any. He first claimed he needed it for medicine because his ears were frozen, then that he needed treatment for an injured arm. He kept inventing excuses, and eventually ended up breaking a school window, asking for 2 won to cover the cost. Then he needed 5 won to replace a broken blackboard.

As half a year passed, he ran out of excuses and found himself in a bind. One day during physical education class, they were lined up in a row, and when he stepped forward, the teacher's voice thundered, "Don't let the line break!" He thought to himself that it was his fault, so he wrote home saying, "Today in gym class, I broke the line, and I need you to send me 7 won to cover the expenses." 

The letter was received by his father in the countryside, who didn’t understand what "line" meant. He showed the letter to the local elementary school teacher, who laughed upon reading it and replied, "Don't worry. There is a way to cover it without money." He then wrote back, "Just take a step forward!"  

**Summary**  

The article recounts a humorous tale of a student creatively inventing various excuses to solicit money from his parents, revealing the absurdity and desperation of his situation as he tries to navigate through his school life. This narrative provides insight into the challenges faced by students during the colonial period, where financial pressures and the need to maintain appearances could lead to such comical situations. Historians might find it interesting as it reflects not only the socio-economic struggles of families under colonial rule but also highlights the resourcefulness and creativity of youth during that era.

기사제목	奇案妙答
기사형태	잡저

奇案妙答
서울 류학생이 돈을 보내 달나고 해야겟는데 분명한 용처를 적어 보내지 안으면 절대로 안 보내는지라. 치위에 귀가 얼어서 병원에 단기니 약갑을 보내라. 운동하다가 팔을 상햇다. 발목을 삐엿스니 치료비를 보내라. 생각나는 핑계를 차례차례 모조리 써먹고 나종에는 작난하다가 학교 유리창을 깨트려서 물어노아야 하겟스니 2원만 보내라. 칠판을 깨트려 노앗스니 5원만 보내 달나고까지 하엿다.
이러케 한 반년 동안 지나닛가. 더 써먹을 핑계가 업서서 쩔쩔매게 되엿다. 하로는 운동장에서 톄조를 하는 중에 엽흐로(橫隊)로 쭉 느러서서 전진을 하는데 한 거름 더듸 거럿드니 톄조선생의 호령이 벽력갓다.
「列ヲ落シジヤイカソ 렬을 떠러트리면 안된다.」 올탓구나 하고 그는 그날 집에 편지하되 「오늘 톄조하다가 렬을 떠러트려서 곳 물어노아야 겟스니 7원만 뎐환으로 부처 주서야 겟슴니다.」하엿다.
그 편지를 밧은 시골 아버지가 렬이라는 것이 무언지 몰나서 동리 보통학교 선생에게 가서 편지를 보엿더니 선생이 보고 우스면서 「럼려 마십시요. 돈 안드리고 물어 놋는 방법이 잇슴니다.」하고 곳 뎐보문을 써주되 「一步ススメ。 한 거름 더 나스라!」고 하엿다.<19>
