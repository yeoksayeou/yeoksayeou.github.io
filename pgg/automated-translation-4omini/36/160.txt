始初이야기  
「담배」의 始初  

The tobacco that we consume daily has a history that seems quite long, akin to that of rice or alcohol. However, in reality, the history of tobacco being imported into Joseon is rather short. According to the book "Jibong Yusol" (芝峯類說) authored by Lee Ji-bong (李芝逢), written in the 6th year of King Gwanghae of the Joseon Dynasty (李朝光海朝六年), it states that tobacco was first introduced to Joseon from Japan in recent years. Thus, it is evident that tobacco came to Joseon from Japan for the first time only about 330 years ago, in the early years of the Qing Dynasty (관해조). From the mention of "Dongnae Ulsan's tobacco" in "Damba-gwi Tale," it appears that it was first imported into the Gyeongsangnam-do region as well.

In Manchuria (만주), there are records indicating that tobacco was imported from Joseon during the time of King Injo's (仁祖) Byeongja War (丙子호란). Furthermore, the first Joseon person to smoke tobacco was Zhang Gye-gok (張溪谷), who was the son-in-law of Kim Cheong-eum (金淸陰). He was said to have suffered a great reprimand from his father-in-law for smoking tobacco. At that time, smoking tobacco was likely considered more strange than using opium today. Yet now, we consume tobacco worth as much as 200 million won annually.

  
**Summary**  

This article discusses the early history of tobacco in Korea, tracing its introduction from Japan to Joseon and the social perceptions surrounding its use. Notably, it highlights the stark contrast between the past views on tobacco, likening it to taboo substances like opium, and the present-day consumption that had grown significantly by 1931, suggesting a normalization of tobacco use in society. This illustrates changing social norms during the colonial period and how they reflect broader cultural imports and shifts.

始初이야기
「담배」의 始初

우리가 날마다 먹는 담배도 잠간 생각하면 밥이나 술과 가티 그 력사가 퍽 오랜 것 갓다. 그러나 실상 알고 보면 우리 조선에 잇서서는 담배의 수입된 력사가 퍽 짜르다. 리조 광해조 6년 (李朝光海朝六年)에 저슬(著述)된 리지봉수광선생(李芝逢晬光先生)의 「芝峯類說」이란 책을 보면 근년에 왜국(죽 日本)으로부터 담배가 츰으로 조선에 드러와 심으기를 시작하얏다 하엿슨즉 담배가 츰으로 조선에 오기는 관해조 초년-지금으로부터 약 330년 전에 비로소 일본으로부터 조선에 온 것이 분명하고 담바귀 타령에도 동래울산 담바귀라는 구절이 잇는 것을 보면 경산남도로 먼저 수입이 된 것도 사실인 것 갓다. 만주(滿洲)에는 또 仁祖 丙子호란 시대에 조선에서 수입해 갓다는 말이 여러 긔록에 잇고 또 조선사람으로 맨 먼저 담배를 먹은 사람은 김청음선생(金淸陰先生)의 사위되는 장계곡(張溪谷先生維) 선생이엿는데 그는 담배를 츰으로 먹다가 그 장인에 큰 책망을 드럿다는 말이 또 엇던 긔록에 잇다. 그때에는 담배 먹는 것이 아마 지금에 아편 먹는 것보다도 더 괴상하게 녁엿던 것갓다. 그런대 지금은 매년 2억원(二億)어치 씩이나 먹고 잇다.<18>
