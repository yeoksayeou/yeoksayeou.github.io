기사제목	더 싸게 보는 法 (Ways to View More Cheaply)  
저자	없음  
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Opinion/Editorial)  

The world's cheapest magazine, costing only 5 jeon, can be received even cheaper. By paying only 50 jeon, you will receive a year’s subscription for 12 months starting from that month. The cost of sending is profitable, and at 4 jeon per issue, it is extremely beneficial.  

**Summary**  

The article offers a promotional subscription deal for a magazine priced at 5 jeon, where readers can receive a full year's worth of issues for just 50 jeon. This reflects an early example of marketing strategies used to boost readership during the colonial period in Korea. Historians might find the cost and accessibility of information dissemination notable, especially considering the economic conditions and the impact of colonial rule on both prices and public engagement with media.

더 싸게 보는 法
5전짜리라는 세계 제일 싼 本誌를 더 싸게 보는 법이 잇슴니다. 50전만 내여 두시면 그달브터 1년동안 12달 두고두고 보내 드림니다. 送金料金 利益되고 一冊 4전꼴 밧게 안되니 대단 유익함니다.<19>
