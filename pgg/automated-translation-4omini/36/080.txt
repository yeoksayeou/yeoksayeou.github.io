기사제목	讀者諸位께 (To the Readers)  
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)

To the readers,

To those who have avidly read 別乾坤 and those who have pre-ordered 別乾坤, we will send you this book along with the comet (彗星). The combined cost of the two books is 35 全（錢), and I will send them until the remaining funds are exhausted. If there are those who are short on funds, please send the missing amount promptly. If there are any objections to this, please notify the dispatch department of our company immediately.

  
**Summary**

This piece serves as an editorial note to the readers of 別乾坤, expressing gratitude and explaining the procedure for receiving a book they have ordered. The cost and billing procedure is clarified, reflecting the transactional nature of publications in colonial Korea. Historians may find it interesting as it highlights the early media engagement strategies and the commercial aspects of publications under Japanese rule, showcasing how media was a channel for both entertainment and financial exchanges during this period.
