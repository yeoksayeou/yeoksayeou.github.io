Q&A  

Reporter: Readers want to know your real name. Is it okay to reveal it?  
金華山: It would be fine to inform you via private message, but please refrain from publishing it in the magazine. There are some troubling issues regarding my surrounding circumstances, so for now...  

廉想涉  

Reporter: Is there a model for your recent long work "三代" (Three Generations)?  
廉: There is no model. However, the characters are drawn from "realistic" figures in reality.

This exchange illustrates the complexities of authorial identity and the relationship between literature and reality in Korean colonial history, shedding light on how authors navigated their public personas amid societal pressures.
