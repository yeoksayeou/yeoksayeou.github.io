警風錄  
Mungdungi's Answer  

Lawyer: "When two automobiles collide, from what distance do you observe?"  
Witness: "18 paces and three inches!"  

Lawyer: "How do you know such a precise distance?"  
Witness: "Surely there's no one as ugly as that to ask such a thing."  

Plaque of Recognition  

Doctor: "There is nothing as precious in life as a doctor, yet I wonder why the world does not recognize our merit. For example, isn’t there any custom of erecting a plaque in honor of physicians?"  
Guest: "Absolutely not. That’s a different matter. Visit a graveyard. The stakes placed at every grave are your plaques of recognition, aren’t they?"  

Wise Wife  

As the country wife says farewell at the station, "When you arrive, please send me a telegram."  
"Yes, I will send it, but should I send about 10 won?"  
"... ..."

The Outlaw's Will  

A famous outlaw, when he was ill and on the verge of death, said to his dear friend, "Now, now, I’ll give you a priceless jewel as a keepsake."  
"Do you think that such a thing is necessary? But to think and give it to you is something that I will remember."  
"At 000 Cheonggyecheon, in Jongno, when you stand at the back wall of the house with the signboard of 000, jump over the back wall, sneak into the kitchen, and behind the back window, you’ll find it hidden in the upper shelf; please take it." <22>  

**Summary**  

This text features humorous dialogues that reflect societal norms and perceptions in early 20th-century Korea. It illustrates how professions, such as law and medicine, grappled with public acknowledgment while simultaneously providing witty observations about human interactions. The dialogue also hints at the lack of respect and recognition for professions critical to society during the colonial period, suggesting a disconnect between essential services and societal appreciation. Historians may be intrigued by the underlying commentary on colonial attitudes and the social status of professions within Korean society.

警風錄
뭉둥이 대답

변호사 「자동차와 자동차가 충돌할 때 당신은 얼마 거리(距離)나 되는 데에서 보섯슴닛가.」
증거인 「18자하고 세치!」
변호사 「엇더케 그러케 확실한 거리를 아심니까.」
증거인 「필시 나종에 엇던 못생긴 놈이 그런 것을 무러볼 놈이 잇슬 줄 알고」

긔렴비

의사 「사람의 생활에 의사처럼 고마운 것이 업겟는데 세상에서는 읏재서 우리들의 공적을 몰나주는지 모르겟서요. 가령 말하면 엇던 의사를 위해서 긔렴비 하나 세우는 법이 업지 안슴니까.」
손님 「천만에요. 딴 말슴이지요. 공동묘지에를 가보십시오. 무덤마다 꾜친 말뚝이 당신네들의 긔렴비가 안이고 무엄니까.」
