Three Hopes: Criticism and Requests Regarding 別乾坤  
						By: 東亞日報社 朱耀翰  
The emergence of a magazine like 別乾坤, centered on taste and pleasure in the midst of the dreary, desolate reality of our Joseon society, feels like a flower blooming in the desert, and the remarkable development it has accomplished in just one year since its founding can be viewed as a great success for your company. Additionally, presenting historical facts about Joseon in a manner akin to storytelling every issue is quite beneficial and significantly enhances public interest. However, I find that at times, in critiquing certain figures, there is a tendency toward personal attacks, and I wonder if there is not a slight over-sensationalism in the handling of the article material. Regarding your magazine, I have three hopes:

1. That the materials for articles are selected to be as realistic as possible within the available range and made more appealing to taste. 

2. That even humorous articles, as much as possible, are chosen to be grand and useful.

3. That articles on taste are chosen to present a slightly higher level of sophistication.

  

**Summary**  

The article expresses appreciation for the magazine 別乾坤 while also providing constructive criticism aimed at improving its content selection. The author stresses the importance of realism and elevated taste in articles, calling for material that is both engaging and educational. This commentary reflects the broader cultural aspirations of Joseon during the colonial period, indicating a desire for intellectual and artistic refinement amidst oppression. Historians might find interest in this article as it showcases the complexity of cultural production and intellectual discourse in colonial Korea, highlighting the attempts to navigate identity and modernity under Japanese rule.

세 가지 希望, 別乾坤에 對한 批判과 要望
						東亞日報社 朱耀翰
아모 취미도 쾌락도 업시 간조적막한 우리 조선사회에 「別乾坤」과 가튼 취미중심의 잡지가 잇는 것은 맛치 사막에 꼿핀 것과 가티 조흔 일로 생각하며 또 別乾坤이 창간한지 불과 일년에 그와 가티 발전된 것은 귀사의 한 성공이라 할 수 잇슴니다. 더욱이 조선의 력사뎍 사실을 강담 비슷하게 매호마다 긔재하는 것은 일반에게 유익도 하고 흥미를 퍽 늣기게 됨니다. 그런데 나로서 생각할 때에는 어느 인물을 비평할 때에 혹 가다가 다소 인신공격에 각가운 듯한 때가 잇고 또 記事재료를 조곰 엿게 취급하는 感이 잇지 안은가 함니다. 貴誌에 대하야 내가 희망하는 것은 대개 세 가지가 잇스니
1. 記事재료는 될 수 잇는 範圍내에서 실제문뎨로 선택하야 취미化하엿스면 하는 것이요.
2. 우슴거리 記事도 아모쪼록은 장쾌하고 유익할 것으로만 선택하엿스면 하는 것이요.
3. 취미도 조곰 더 高尙한 취미의 記事를 선택하얏스면 하는 것이 올시다.<111>
