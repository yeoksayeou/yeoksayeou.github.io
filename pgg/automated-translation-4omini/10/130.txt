필자	漢城券番 崔東垣  
기사형태	회고·수기 (Retrospective/Personal Account)  

As for our entertainment industry, it is indeed a downturn compared to last year. During this time last year, there were about a hundred gisaeng (妓生) working each night, but this year there are only about sixty to seventy. Currently, if we consider the number of operating establishments, there are approximately one hundred fifty to one hundred fifty-three, and including those that have ceased operations, it totals around four hundred. However, the number of gisaeng has been higher last year compared to this year, and this year certainly has fewer than last year. This decline seems to reflect the overall hardship of living where everyone is struggling for survival. 

It is particularly noteworthy that students from the women's school are frequently seen turning to gisaeng work, which clearly indicates this troubling trend. Concerning the revenue of the association, the commission taken from the fees last year was around twelve thousand won, with the total revenue for gisaeng hours approximately one hundred twenty thousand won. This year, however, it seems likely that figures will be lower than those. The highest revenue earned by a gisaeng last year was three thousand won by Song Yeonhwa (宋蓮花), but as for this year, we have not concluded the figures yet, but it is certain that very few have earned such amounts.

Regarding businesses that have closed this year, around fifty establishments have ceased operations, and there are about fifty new establishments that have received licenses, showing little difference from last year. In terms of location, the highest number of gisaeng comes from Seoul, followed by Pyeongyang, and then Daegu. <178>  

**Summary**

This article presents a retrospective view on the changes in the gisaeng industry over two years, highlighting significant economic difficulties that have led to a reduction in the number of active gisaeng. It portrays a concerning situation where even students are turning to gisaeng work, indicating broader societal challenges during the colonial period. Historians might find the mention of the economic impact on traditional occupations particularly revealing, showcasing the intersections of gender, class, and colonial exploitation.

우리 화류계로 말슴하야도 작년에 비하면 역시 불경긔 임니다. 작년 이때로 말슴하면 하루밤에 노물 바더가는 기생이 약 백여명 내외엿섯는데 금년에는 불과 륙칠십명 내외올시다. 현재 영업자로 말슴하면 일백 오십 이삼명이나 동기까지 합하면 약 사백 여명이올시다. 그런데 기생 수는 재작년보다 작년이 더 만코 작년보다 금년이 더 만슴니다. 그것은 모도 생활이 곤난하닛가 당장에 버러먹으랴고 그러는 것이 올시다. 녀학교에서 상당한 공부를 하든 학생으로서 기생출원을 만히 하는 것을 보와도 알 수 잇는 것이올시다. 조합의 수입으로 말슴하면 화대의 십분일을 떼는 것이 작년도에 약 일만이천원 가량이 되엿스닛가 기생의 시간대의 총수입으로 말슴하면 약 십이만원이나 되엿슴니다. 그러나 금년은 아마 이활은 감소된 듯 함니다. 작년도에 기생 개인의 수입으로는 송련화(宋蓮花)의 삼천원이 최고엿슴니다만은 금년은 아즉 결산을 안이 하얏스닛가 자서이는 알 수 업스나 물론 그만한 수입된 기생이 별로 업슬 것 갓슴니다. 금년도에 폐업자로 말슴하면 약 50명이고 새로 영업허가를 맛튼 자도 약 50명 되는데 그것은 작년과 별로 차이가 업슴니다. 디방별로는 서울사람이 제일 만코 그 다음이 평양이요 그 다음이 대구올시다. <178>
