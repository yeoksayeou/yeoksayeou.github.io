발행일	1927년 12월 20일  
기사제목	収穫 만흔 一年이엿다, 今年一年 朝鮮사람의 生活은 엇더하엿나, 여러 方面으로 모아 본 昨年과 今年의 比較 (The Harvest of a Year, How have the Lives of Koreans been this Year? A Comparison of Last Year and This Year from Various Aspects)  
필자	東亞記者 李吉龍 (Dong-A Journalist Lee Gil-ryong)  
기사형태	회고·수기 (Retrospective)

Time has flowed and passed, and before we know it, the year of the 丁卯 (Jeong-Myoh) is coming to a close. What have the healthy children of the peninsula accomplished during this year? How has it adorned the pages of Korean sports history? Now, I would like to introduce a few of those achievements.

Originally, since it is Korea, there is a sense of having made some progress, but this year, amidst the inadequacies, there have been particularly significant harvests. If these can reasonably develop from now on, this year can indeed be said to be a year of harvest akin to a transformation from one generation to another. Why? Because this year, the 8th Far Eastern Olympic Games, the palace of Eastern sports, was held in Shanghai, where Japan remarkably achieved 2nd place in football for the first time in their history. On their way back, they shamefully suffered a defeat to the Korean football team at the Kyungsung Stadium. This too is one of the significant harvests. Additionally, in the autumn of this year, during the Sports Day and Children's Competition held in Kyungsung, Korean school children demonstrated excellent results, surpassing the Japanese participants. This vividly proved that Koreans are not inferior in sports by nature. 

These are just two of the great harvests; nonetheless, football and sports skill have proven how deeply ingrained these activities are within the essence of Koreans and the territory of Korea. Aside from these, how about tennis? This year has seen significant harvests in tennis as well, particularly among women rather than men. It can be observed that at this year's large event, the tennis skills of Korean women have reached a new level and can be regarded as their first significant breakthrough. The achievements of the teams from Ewha Womans University, including Kim Lee and Haosyoosun, as well as the players from Jinnam High School, are commendable.

Moreover, this year, in track and field events, with new records being set, the first official marathon run of 26 miles and 4 minutes, which was accomplished by the Korean athlete Ma Bong-uk, was an exhilarating victory.

Thus, we have recorded our first records and created eight new records in track and field events, achieving a remarkable milestone in sports history. Now, it is challenging to introduce more examples in this limited space, so I will save them for the next opportunity. I would like to conclude by stating one last point regarding the closing pages of this year's sports history. This relates to the establishment of the Korean Wrestling Association to unify and reward the most culturally significant folk sport in Korea, as initiated by those who have devoted themselves to this endeavor, and the hosting of the first nationwide wrestling tournament by the Central Christian Youth Association, which has made the conclusion of this year's sporting history even more meaningful.

**Summary**

This article reflects on the achievements of Koreans in sports during the year of 1927, highlighting significant events such as their participation in the 8th Far Eastern Olympic Games and the first nationwide wrestling tournament. The author emphasizes that despite Japan's colonial dominance, Koreans showed considerable competitive spirit and capabilities in sports, thereby challenging the prevailing stereotype of inferiority. Historians might find the article significant as it underscores the growing sense of national identity and pride among Koreans during Japanese rule, alongside the resilience evident in their sporting accomplishments.

필자	東亞記者 李吉龍
기사형태	회고·수기

收穫 만흔 一年이엿다, 今年一年 朝鮮사람의 生活은 엇더하엿나, 여러 方面으로 모아 본 昨年과 今年의 比較
						東亞記者 李吉龍
세월은 흘르고 또 흘러 丁卯年도 어느듯 기우럿다. 『今年 一年동안엔?』 하고 決心튼 半島의 健兒는 과연 얼마마한 收穫을 어덧스며 또 朝鮮의 體育史의 페이지를 裝飾하엿는가? 이제 몃마듸 紹介를 하야보려고 한다.
원래가 朝鮮땅이라 얼마쯤 進步된댓자 不具의 感이 업지 안흐나 그러나 今年 한해 동안에는 不具한 그 중에서도 자못 劃時期的의 큰 收穫이 잇섯스니 만일 合理로 이것이 자라난다면 今年이야말로 隔世의 收穫을 거둔 해이엇다. 왜? 今年에는 東洋 스포스의 殿堂인 제8회 극동올림픽대회가 上海에서 열렷고 이에서 日本이 蹴球에 평생 처음으로 2위를 차지하야 가지고 조하서 돌아가는 길에 京城에서 轉戰하야 朝蹴에게 延專에게 無慘히 敗하고 돌아간 그것도 큰 收穫의 하나이오 今年 가을에 京城에서 열린 體育데이와 밋 神競大會의 兒童競技에 朝鮮人 學童이 日本人 그거보다 越等한 好成績으로 走力을 나타내여 결코 朝鮮人 本質이 體育에 劣等아닌 것을 歷歷히 發明케 하엿다. 이것은 큰 收穫 중의 둘에 불과하지만 엇잿든 蹴球와 밋 蹴球技 이것이 얼마나 朝鮮사람과 朝鮮땅에 알마즌 運動이며 因緣 깁흔 運動이라는 것을 굿게 밋게 하엿다. 그 외에 庭球는 엇더한가. 今年의 庭球는 蹴球 다음가는 收穫을 거두엇스니 그것은 男子보다도 女子에게서 더욱 그러하다. 今年 大會에는 거의 朝鮮女子의 庭球技術이 搖籃의 때를 버러난 처음 해이라고<157> 볼 수 잇게 되엇스니 梨花女高의 金李組와 好壽敦 밋 進明의 選手들의 活躍은 稱讚할 갑이 잇다. 그러고 陸上競技에 今年度 新記錄 중 트랙의 中長距離와 今年 最初의 定規마라손 즉 26哩4分1의 走破에 잇서서 朝鮮人 馬鳳玉君이 一着을 하엿슴은 痛快한 勝利이엇다.
그리하야 最初記錄을 우리 손으로 지엇고 또 8종의 新記錄을 우리 손으로 지어 陸上競技의 初有盛事를 이루엇다. 이제 좁은 紙面에 낮낮치 例를 들어 紹介하기는 어려움으로 다음 機會로 밀우고 끄트로 今年 運動史의 마즈막 페이지를 더욱 燦爛하게 꿈이어진 것을 한마듸 더붓치고 말련다. 그것은 朝鮮에 가장 傳來되는 民衆的 競技 씨름을 統一的으로 獎勵하자는 意味에서 이에 平素부터 뜻을 둔 이들의 發起로 朝鮮씨름協會를 組織한 것과 또 中央基督敎靑年會主催로 제1회 全朝鮮씨름大會를 열게 된 것은 今年 競技史의 막음을 더욱 意味잇게 하는 것이다. <158>
