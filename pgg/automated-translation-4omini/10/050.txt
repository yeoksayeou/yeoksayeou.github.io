Three Unique Local Products  
                     淸雲洞 側目生  
In Kyŏngsŏng (京城), there are three distinctive local products. One is the clothing of OO employees, another is the uniform of the Salvation Army, and the last is the attire of workers from the British and American consulates.

It is unclear who first said this, but it is certainly true. Furthermore, it is noted that the aging bodies of OO employees, adorned in yellow hats and yellow western suits, is a reflection of their poverty, making them deserving of pity. The old-fashioned uniforms of employees from the British and American consulates stem from the contemptible curiosity of white people, making them rather loathsome. However, the sight of those who have escaped from a difficult life, wearing long skirts and rubber shoes with military-style jackets and shoulder boards, captures the attention of many men. This is truly something one could not have imagined, and it can be called foolish bravery. <155-155>  

**Summary**  

The article discusses unique cultural aspects observed in Kyŏngsŏng during the colonial era, focusing on the clothing styles of various groups, including local employees and diplomatic staff from Western consulates. The author critiques the economic hardship reflected in the clothing of local workers and reflects on the peculiarities of foreign diplomatic attire. This offers insights into both social stratifications and the cultural exchange, or lack thereof, during Korea's colonial period, highlighting the intersection of poverty and external influences.

名物 세가지, 午鍾소리, 葉書通信

『京城에는 색다른 名物 세가지가 잇스니 하나는 曰 OO職雇員들의 服裝이요 又曰 救世軍服裝이요 又曰 英米國領事舘雇人들의 服裝이라 하시니라』
누가 그럿케 하시니라 하섯는지 몰나도 그것 말 안인 것은 確實한대 그 아래에 또 하혓스되
『OO職雇員의 늙어 굽는 몸에 노랑테帽子 노랑동洋服을 닙는 것은 貧寒이 식이는 일이니 可憐타 할 것이요 英米國領事雇人의 옛날 服裝은 白人들의 얄미운 好奇心에서 나온 것이니 可憎타 할 것이어니와 싀집살이 실타고 밤에 逃亡해 와서 긴-치마 밋에 고무구두 신고 저고리 동정에 肩章을 띄고 나서서 뭇男子의 이목을 붓들고 매여달니는 것은 실로 아모도 想像도 못하는 것이니 勇盲타 할 것이니라』<155-155>
