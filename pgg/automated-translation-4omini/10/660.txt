Article Title: 울음 속에서 우슴을 구하는 우리에게, 別乾坤에 對한 批判과 要望 (To Us Seeking Comfort Amidst Tears: Critiques and Requests Regarding 別乾坤)  
Author: 中外日報社 李相協  
Article Type: 설문 (Survey)  

Amidst various hardships, I am not able to thoroughly read 別乾坤, but recently I have seen many positive reviews about it. Perhaps the number of copies distributed is quite substantial? It is an entertainment magazine! I believe it has emerged at an opportune time. Korea is worn out and parched in every aspect, and the surroundings are notably desolate and bleak. Regardless of whether young or old, in such a desolate state, we are still trying to seek out some consolation. In other words, we are scrambling to find smiles from within our tears. Our current state is indeed one of forcing ourselves to try to smile.

For those of us with this sentiment, the successful development of 別乾坤, an entertainment magazine with diverse aspects, is truly no coincidence. I think it is essential for it to put more effort into the avenues of entertainment to lift our spirits.

 

**Summary** 

This article reflects on the struggles and emotional state of the Korean people during the colonial period. It highlights the desire for entertainment and comfort amidst pervasive hardship and desolation. The author argues that the rise of the magazine 別乾坤 is a testament to the cultural need for joy and distraction in difficult times. Historians may find it interesting as it illustrates the efforts of cultural productions to provide solace during Korea’s colonial struggles, showcasing the importance of media and entertainment in fostering resilience.

울음 속에서 우슴을 구하는 우리에게, 別乾坤에 對한 批判과 要望
						中外日報社 李相協
여러 가지로 밧븐 몸이라 別乾坤을 號號히 通讀하지는 못 함니다만은 갓금 보아도 좃코 또 到處마다 매우 好評이 드구만요. 아마 部數가 相當히 나가지요? 趣味雜誌! 時勢를 잘 타고 나온 줄 암니다. 朝鮮은 모든 것이 疲廢하고 枯渴하닛가 四圍가 여간 蕭條寂寬하지가 안슴니다. 靑年老年할 것 업시 이와 가티 蕭條寂寬한 중에서도 그래도 엇더케든지 慰安을 엇고저 함니다. 즉 울음 가운데서 우슴을 차즈려 함니다. 억지로라도 우서보려고 애쓰는 것이 現下 우리의 心情임니다. 이러한 心情을 가진 우리에게 趣味多端의 別乾坤誌가 잘 發展케 됨은 실로 偶然한 일이 안이라고 생각함니다. 더 一層 趣味의 方面에 努力하서서 우리의 心情을 깃겁게 해주서요.<108>
