기사제목	『암만 부즈런한들』 (Am I Not Busy?)  
필자	李象範 (Li Sang-bam)  
기사형태	만화 (Cartoon)  

Even if I am exhausted, I cannot rest, and after a year of being busy, I am truly thankful for the year-end bonus of 30 won that I received. <93>  

**Summary**  

The article discusses the struggles of work and the relief brought by a year-end bonus, portraying a common sentiment of workers during that era. Historians might find it interesting as it reflects the social conditions and economic realities of Koreans under colonial rule, highlighting the contrast between hard work and meager rewards, indicative of the broader exploitative economic structures in place.
