謝告  
This issue contains a total of 178 pages, which is more than 250 pages in content compared to other magazines. Despite this, due to the abundance of articles, the following two pieces regrettably could not be included and will be published in the New Year edition. We apologize for this in advance.  
  
◇Men and Women Who Can Marry and Those Who Cannot………………Kang Gyu-bok  
  
◇Good Marriages are Better than Bad Marriages……………………Choi Du-seon＜154＞  

**Summary**  
  
This article serves as an editorial apology in the magazine "별건곤" for the exclusion of two articles from the current issue due to space limitations. The pieces focused on marriage involving theoretical discussions about suitable partners and the merits of different types of marriages. This reflects ongoing societal discussions in 1920s Korea about marriage norms and personal relationships which might interest historians studying the evolution of social perspectives during the colonial period.
