저자	-  
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)

地方通信  
If you send school or youth group news and other local anecdotes in a lively manner, we will publish them in this magazine. Please submit a lot. <127>  

 
**Summary**  

The article encourages submissions of news from schools and youth organizations, stressing the importance of lively writing. This call for local communications indicates an effort to foster dialogue among Korean communities during the colonial period, highlighting a sense of collective identity. Historians may find the push for local engagement particularly interesting, as it reflects the cultural efforts to assert a distinct Korean voice within the confines of colonial rule.
