葉書通信歡迎  
The "Postcard Communication Column" is warmly welcomed. As mentioned before, the content can include immediate impressions, satire, critiques, news, and anything else, as long as it is not outside the scope of postcards (葉書).  

On the cover, it is necessary to write "Postcard Communication" (葉書通信). <159>  

**Summary**  

This article invites readers to contribute to a communication column that is accepting a variety of inputs, emphasizing absolute freedom of expression. It reflects a call for broader engagement and interaction, characteristic of the period’s shifting attitudes toward communication mediums in colonial Korea. Historians may find the emphasis on freedom of expression particularly interesting in the context of the increasing restrictions imposed by colonial authorities at the time, signaling a yearning for greater autonomy in public discourse.
