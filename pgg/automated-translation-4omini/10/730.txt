필자	朝鮮日報社 韓基岳  
기사형태	설문 (Survey)  

1. There is little to praise about hobby magazines, but the fact that they do not cover politics or current affairs is a source of distress for the editors and a regret for the readers. After all, a magazine should be suitable for reading on a train or tram, and in that regard, I cannot understand how, compared to other magazines, it is so superior.

2. Originally, this magazine's intention is centered around hobbies. Of course, the content reflects that focus, but I think it would be better if more articles beneficial to hobbyists were included than is currently the case. Additionally, it would be ideal if the magazine included more substantial articles related to rural areas and the economy.

**Summary**

The article discusses the limitations of hobby magazines, criticizing their lack of content on politics and relevant contemporary issues, while emphasizing their role as reading material for commuters. The author suggests that enhancing practical and meaningful content about rural and economic topics could improve the magazine's value. From a historical perspective, this highlights the tension between cultural entertainment and the need for engagement with pressing social issues during the colonial period in Korea.
