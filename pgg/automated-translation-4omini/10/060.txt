기사제목	꿈에 본 하나님!(追憶) (God I Saw in a Dream!)  
필자	江華 一讀者 (Kanghwa Il Reader)  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

When I was eight years old, this was something that happened 17 or 18 years ago. It was back when I was at Kanghwa Habong School, and every morning I would attend with a neighbor named Kim Jeong-soon. However, his family and father were known for their “playful” nature. Therefore, amidst the flavors, they also had such a daunting nature. His younger brother, recently, is known for his good physique and eloquence, clearly evident in the children’s stories he broadcasts on the radio. One day he came to pick me up, and we had this unsettling experience.

“Hey, Jireong. Did you see God? I tasted it last night!” he said, sounding truly elated and boasting about it… I responded like this… “Jeong-soon! How big is God?” To which he replied, “Well, he isn’t very big, just about the size of a cigarette box!” So, this unusual incident occurred. From then on, I always… hmm! I would think that God is just about the size of a cigarette box. Later, at a Catholic Church, I saw a statue of God made just the size of a cigarette box. <155-155>  

**Summary**  

The article recounts a childhood memory from when the author was eight years old, centered around a conversation with a friend regarding the size of God, humorously comparing it to a cigarette box. This recollection highlights the innocence and imaginative perceptions of children in interpreting spiritual matters. Historians may find this article interesting as it reflects the cultural and religious dynamics of the time in Korea, particularly the role of childhood innocence within discussions of faith.

꿈에 본 하나님!(追憶), 葉書通信
						江華 一讀者
내가 여덜살 먹엇슬 때에 일이니까 발서 17, 8년전 옛날 이약이다. 江華合一學校에 단일 때인데 每日 아침이면 이웃 琴貞淳이란 동모와 가티 단일 때다! 그런데 그 집안 사람들이 그의 아바지랑 왼 식구가 『익살』로 생겻다. 그래서 滋味도 잇거니와 우서운 이약이를 잘한다. 그의 아우님은 요사히 「라듸오」 放送局에서 童話를 갓금 放送하는 琴徹君만 보아도 體格이며 말솜씨也를 잘 알리라. 그는 어느 날도 나를 데리러 왓다가...... 이런 우서운 이약이를 하엿다.
『야 智榮아. 너 하나님 보앗니? 나는 어제밤에 맛낫섯단다!』 하고 정말 맛낫든 것처럼 悅高萬大하야 자랑하길래...... 나는 이럿케 무럿다...... 『貞淳아! 大關節 하나님이 크기가 얼마나 하든?』 하닛가 『그런데 크진 못하고 저- 똑 卷煙匣만 하드라!』 그래서 우섯든 일이 잇섯다. 그후 나는 늘... 응! 하나님은 卷煙草匣만 하거니 하고 지냇섯다. 그후에 어느 天主敎會 聖殿에서 하나님의 銅像이 똑 卷煙草匣만 하게 만드러 노흔 것을 보앗다.<155-155>
