佛國巴리에서, 海外에 잇는 名士의 最近通信  
崔麟  
To begin with, I am fortunate to report that I have arrived in Paris without any incident. This is largely due to the protection of the Heavenly Master and the grace bestowed upon me by the combined goodwill of everyone. As for Paris, it is known to be one of the most splendid and beautiful cities in the world, not only that, but it is also historically the center of European culture, where everything from monuments to artworks possesses exceptional beauty and the smiles of ladies and the steps of gentlemen are imbued with emotion. 

However, Paris is more than just a beautiful and poetic city. It is the burial ground of past geniuses, great poets, and major literary figures and has been a source of inspiration for heroes, like Raphael, who influenced European rivers and mountains through his works. Paris is also a city that carries the lingering pains of the world wars, as well as a site marked by the historical agreements of the Three Aliances of the World War, in which the German artillery inflicted deep sorrows on the French people, leaving a lasting resentment throughout history.

Due to the exceptional complexity of Paris, the lives of its inhabitants are not straightforward either. On the surface, Parisians lead a flamboyant life, but underneath they are very economical. Currently, the price of a French franc (佛貨) is about 8 pennies in our currency. However, the perception of a franc among the French people is quite different. In comparison to other nations, the general savings of the French people are the highest. It is said that 1.7 million French civilians died in the First World War, which illustrates the horrors of war.

At present, the French people seem to be making considerable efforts to recover from the losses of the war. Additionally, the French people's fear of war and their thoughts on peace seem to be very genuine. Consequently, numerous organizations dedicated to peace (both internal and international) have been established by notable figures from both the political and social spheres. I have met many of these distinguished individuals and have also met several dedicated women, including Madame "Intrigue-Brun," the president of the International Women's League. We have met multiple times to share meaningful discussions.

Among them was a scholar named "Perry Seiyangcholle" who traveled to Korea. During one of our gatherings, the topic of the truth of the Heavenly Way (天道敎) came up, leading to an intriguing question-and-answer session about the philosophy of the religion. One of the friends present asked whether there were any books that translate the truths of the Heavenly Way into French, English, or German. At that time, my discomfort over this question could only be imagined by you. Their warm regard left me deeply moved. Madame Ju-Brun gifted me a note saying, “As a sister from Korea, I wish you every success.” Although there are many such precious acquaintances here, this is just a brief mention. 

  
**Summary**  

The article presents an account of the author’s experiences in Paris, emphasizing the city's rich cultural heritage and the impacts of historical events, particularly the First World War. The author expresses both admiration for the aesthetic beauty of Paris and reflects on its darker past, including the human cost of conflict. Historians might find it significant that conversations about peace and cultural exchange were ongoing in the period following the war, highlighting the efforts of individuals to promote understanding across national boundaries during colonial tensions in Asia.

佛國巴里에서, 海外에 잇는 名士의 最近通信
						崔麟
前略 弟는 巴里到着後 아즉것 一身이 無故하오니 다행임니다. 이것은 곳 天師의 庇護하오심과 여러분 同德의 念慮하야 주신 德澤으로 밋슴니다. 이 巴里城으로 말슴하면 아시는 바와 가티 世界中 가장 華麗秀美한 都市일 뿐만 안이라 過去 歐羅巴文化의 中心地인만큼 尺樹拳石이 어느 것이 美的이 안인 것이 업고 老婆의 우슴과 公子의 거름이 어느 것이 情的이 안인 것이 업슴니다. 巴里城은 藝術品으로 一大傑作인 同時에 遊興地로 一大劇場임니다. 그러나 巴里는 다만 美的詩的의 巴里만이 안임니다. 過去의 모든 天才 즉 巨詩人 大文學者의 靈骨埋葬地요 歐羅巴 山川을 一手로 飜覆하든 豪雄 라파륜의 策源地며 普佛戰爭의 城下三盟과 世界大戰當時에 獨軍砲丸이 爆落하야 佛國人의 千古遺恨을 품게하고 肝膽을 破喪하든 巴里城임니다. 橫으로 縱으로 이만큼 複雜한 巴里인 까닭에 巴里人의 생활도 따라서 단순치 안슴니다. 巴里人의 생활은 表面으로는 風流的이면서도 裏面으로는 經濟的임니다. 佛貨 一法의 現在價格이 우리의 通貨로<126> 약 8錢 가량임니다. 그러나 佛人의 一法에 대한 觀念이 如干이 안임니다. 佛國人民의 一般貯金은 각국 중에 제일 만타고 함니다. 世界大戰에 佛人戰死者가 170만이나 된다고 함니다. 戰爭의 慘禍를 이로써 알 수 잇슴니다. 現下 佛國人民은 大戰의 損失를 回復하기 위하야 如干한 奮鬪努力이 안인 모양임니다. 뿐만 안이라 佛國人民의 戰爭을 恐怖하는 마음과 平和를 謳歌하는 생각은 무엇보다 切實한듯 함니다. 따라서 朝野名士들로 組織된 各種의 平和(內外)的 機關이 만히 設立되여 잇슴니다. 弟는 그 方面의 名士들을 만히 만나보고 또는 國際女子聯盟會 會長 「안트레쥬-브」라는 夫人以下 여러 有志夫人들도 만나보왓슴니다. 그리고 여러 번 會合하야 滋味잇게 논 일도 잇섯슴니다. 其中에는 朝鮮에를 댕겨온 「뻬리씨양쫠레」라는 學者도 잇슴니다. 그런데 그 會席에서 우리 天道敎問題가 話題가 되어 天道敎眞理에 대한 問答이 자못 興味를 이르킨 일도 잇섯슴니다. 其時에 잇든 친구가 이런 말을 물엇슴니다. 「天道敎의 眞理를 佛文이나 혹은 英獨文으로 飜譯한 書籍이 잇느냐」구요. 그때에 나의 거북하든 생각은 여러분의 상상으로도 짐작하실듯 함니다. 그리고 그들의 땃듯한 情은 나로 하야금 感服치 안을 수 업게 하엿슴니다. 쥬-브 女史는 나에게 이런 글을 써서 주엇슴니다. 「나는 朝鮮의 누이로서 공경하는 당신의 성공을 빈다」구요. 그밧게도 자미잇는 이약이가 만이 잇슴니다만은 구만 두젯슴니다.
