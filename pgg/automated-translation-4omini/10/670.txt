Magazine Title: 별건곤  
Publication Date: 1927년 12월 20일  
Article Title: 家家戶戶에 뵈이코 십소 (An Appeal to Every Household)  
Author: 啓明俱樂部 崔南善  
Article Type: 설문 (Survey)

別乾坤! I am truly in admiration. How is it that the editorial methods are so thorough? Compared to magazines like those from Japan, I believe it does not fall short at all. Among them, the account of a survey into the female student recruitment group is indeed astonishing. I wish for it to be disseminated among households with both male and female students. Please make even more efforts.

**Summary**

The article expresses admiration for a publication's editorial quality, particularly praising a specific piece about the recruitment of female students. The author encourages wider dissemination of such articles among households. This reflects a growing concern regarding education and societal roles of women during the colonial period in Korea, underscoring the changing dynamics in gender roles and education that historians may find significant within the context of Korean colonial history.

別乾坤! 나는 실로 嘆服함니다. 엇지면 編輯手段이 그럿케 周到함닛가? 日本의 무엇이니 무엇이니 하는 雜誌에 比해서도 결코 遜色이 업슬 줄로 암니다. 그 중에 특히 언제인가 女學生誘引團探査記는 실로 놀내지 안을 수 업섯슴니다. 男女學生을 둔 家家戶戶에 傳하고 십헛슴니다. 더 만히 努力해 주십시요.<108>
