기사제목	무슨 餘裕가 잇소, 歲暮에 안저서 새해를 볼 때에 생각되는 일 (What Abundance Is There, When Thinking of the New Year at the Year-End)  
필자	韓基岳  
기사형태	논설 (Essay)  

What abundance is there, when thinking of the New Year at the year-end?  

After all, the taxes and everything really can be discussed only after there is some leisure in life. In our surroundings, is there any place to really talk about it? Even if we attempt to celebrate the New Year according to the solar calendar, it has become a habit of the household to be unable to execute it properly without being broken down in the process.  

  

Summary  

This article reflects on the concepts of leisure and abundance in the context of celebrating the New Year during a time of hardship in colonial Korea. The author expresses frustration at the inability to properly engage in traditions due to the burdens imposed by life under colonial rule. Historians might find interest in how such reflections illustrate the impact of colonial policies on daily life and traditional practices, shedding light on the socio-economic struggles faced by Koreans during this period.
