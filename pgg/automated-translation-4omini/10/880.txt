Recent Correspondence from a Notable Figure in Brazil  
Sin Hak-mun  
The letter sent on August 27th reaches me today (October 10th) from Batizato. I wish your health is well and that the club activities are proceeding as planned, for which I express my gratitude. Since my arrival here, both my son and mother are suffering from local diseases. (Omitted text - original)  

Since coming here, there have been many humorous incidents. Yet, no matter how amusing it may be, it is of no use. It seems that this place, at least, is not meant to be my residence. I have moved from the previous house to a nearby one, but it feels uncertain. I have precisely 8 coins left. I gaze blankly at the distant mountains, feeling uneasy and confined. I cannot tell whether I wrote this letter or if someone else did.  

Among all matters, the most troubling is the inconsistency of life standards. While others achieve success, what kind of success are we measuring it against?  

So, please stay healthy. I wonder if there will be a letter sent back from you in this country.  

I am curious if you received the postcard I sent on July 9th and the letter dated August 17th from Yukdo and Kaesong. <126>  

**Summary**  

This correspondence from Sin Hak-mun highlights the personal and societal challenges faced by Koreans abroad during the colonial period. He reflects on health issues, limited finances, and the ambiguity of success in a foreign land, expressing a sense of isolation. Historians might find it interesting how the experiences of individuals like Sin encapsulate the broader struggles of Koreans in exile, dealing with illness and the quest for identity amidst colonial pressures.

뿌라질에서, 海外에 잇는 名士의 最近通信
						秦學文
8월 27일에 부치신 글월은 오늘(10월 10일) 밧자왓소. 連하야 貴體萬旺하시고 俱樂部 事業도 計劃대로 進行된다하오니 感祝하외다. 나는 이곳 온 후 곳 奇兒를 일코 奇母 역시 風土病으로 苦生 중이요.(中略-원문)
이곳에 와서 우스운 일 격근 것이 한두 가지가 아니요. 그러나 그까지 이약이한들 所用잇소. 다만 이곳은 적어도 나의 잇슬 곳이 아닌 듯하오. 전에 잇던 집에서 나와 그 近處에 집을 비러가지고 잇는데 곳 뜨라니 돈이나 잇서야 하지 아니하오. 지금 時財가 꼭 8錢이요. 茫然히 먼 산만 바라보고 안젓스며 긔만 막히오. 이 편지를 내가 썻는지 남이 썻는지 나는 알 수 업소.
무엇이니 무엇이니 하는 중에도 생활의 標準업는 것이 제일 기맥힌 일이요. 남들은 成功 成功하지만 成功이란 무엇을 標準한 成功이요?
자 그러면 부듸 健康히 게시요. 또 글월을 밧자올 긔회가 잇슬는지(이 나라에서).
諸益에 안보해주오.
7월 9일에 부친 葉書와 8월 17일에 六堂과 開城으로 한 편지 보앗는지 궁금하오.<126>
