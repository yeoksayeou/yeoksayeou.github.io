Trends in Money Observed at the Tavern  
호텔업 황창환  
Last year, the average sales in my tavern reached over 100 won, with at least 60 to 70 won daily. This was quite a profitable price.  

This year, it is not close to that. Even on busy days, I'm lucky to make around 60 to 70 won if I work hard to sell. Thus, it seems my income has definitely dropped by a third.  

If taverns experience a drop in earnings, one might assume it is due to fewer customers. It's easy to guess that the decline in customers could be attributed to either improved drink quality or a decline in the preparation of side dishes, or perhaps customer service is less friendly than before. However, anyone who has visited the tavern would know that nothing has deteriorated in comparison to last year. So why is my income down? That is indeed a puzzling question.  

The lifestyle of Korean people mostly consists of hardships and suffering; those who can afford it may say, "Let's have a drink and forget about it." If more people adopt that mindset, society must be in a difficult situation. Perhaps the world is turning into a dire circumstance where tavern owners cannot thrive due to a downturn in overall economic conditions. Yet the fact remains that my tavern’s situation diverges from last year's. Who can anticipate how next year will unfold? Unless something changes, there isn’t much hope.  

It’s uncertain whether there are more people who are willing to say, “Let’s drink and forget everything,” but what is clear is that my money has indeed become less, making it impossible to spare a drink, so I am forced to reduce my consumption from two drinks to one due to financial circumstances. Furthermore, the tavern is small and busy, with customers coming in steadily, which naturally leads to a decrease in income.

Looking back over the year, autumn was the time when prices were highest, and generally, the evening hours are busiest. So, my business is greatly affected by the overall plight of ordinary people's living conditions. What should I do to survive? It is truly a lamentable situation.<158>  

**Summary**  

This article reflects on the declining earnings of a tavern owner, Hwang Chang-hwan, throughout one year, highlighting the economic struggles faced by ordinary Koreans under colonial rule. Despite believing that the quality of services has not deteriorated, Hwang notes a notable decrease in customers and profits. This reveals the broader theme of economic hardship during the colonial period, where the cost of living and societal conditions significantly impacted even small businesses like taverns. Historians may find this illustrative of the everyday realities and resilience of Koreans in the face of economic downturns and colonial exploitation.

선술집에서 본 돈 경향, 今年一年 朝鮮사람의 生活은 엇더하엿나, 여러 方面으로 모아 본 昨年과 今年의 比較
						酒店業 黃昌煥
하로에 적어도 60원 70원 만흐면 백원이 훨신 넘엇던 것이 우리집에서 판 작년 평균통게엿슴니다. 무던히 팔린 가격이지요.
금년에는 어림도 업슴니다. 하로에 만히 팔려야 작년 적게 팔리던 날만큼 되야 한 6, 70원 가량밧게 아니됨니다. 그저 확실히 3분 1은 수입이 준 셈임니다.
술집에서 수입이 줄엇다하면 물론 손님이 덜드시닛가 그러코 손님 덜드는 것은 술맛이 전보다 낫버젓다거나 안주준비가 전만 못하다거나 혹은 손님대접이 전보다 불친절하다거나 무슨 원인이 잇던지 반드시 원인이 잇기는 잇슬 것이라고 추측하시겟지요. 그러나 와보신 손님은 다 아시겟지만 족금도 전보다 나으면 나엇지 못하게 하는 것은 하나도 업슴니다. 그러면 엇재서 수입이 주느냐? 그게 의문임니다.
우리 조선사람의 생활이라는 것이 돈 만흔 사람은 말할 것 업지만 대개는 고통과 번뢰로 지내지 안습닛가. 『에라 한잔먹고 니저버리자.』 깟닥하면 그러케 되기쉽지요. 그러는 사람이 는다면 사회야 엇더케 되던지 세상이야 엇잿던지 하여간 불난데 도적질하랴는 셈으로 술장사의 호주머니밧게 살찔 것이 업슬 것 갓지만 사실에 잇서서는 요러케도 작년과 금년이 틀너 넘어 갑니다그려. 명년에 가서는 요보다도 더 틀닐넌지 누가 압닛가. 딴수가 생기기 전에는 별수 업지요.
『술이나 먹자. 그리고 다 니저버리자.』 하는 패가 늘으섯슬는지는 모릅니다마는 그 소리보다 호주머니가 더 줄어든 것만은 사실이어서 그나마도 한잔 기우릴 힘이 업고 전에 두잔 먹든 것을 주려서 한잔으로 절약하지 안이하면 안될 엇절수 업는 사정에 박도한 까닭, 게다가 다른건 못하여도 선술집은 작고 느니 손님은 주는데다가 겹처서 길이 갈리게 되니 자연히 수입이 적어지지 안켓서요.
1년을 두고 보면 가을머리가 제일 시세가 낫고 하로로 보면 저녁때가 제일 복잡한데 하여간 우리 일반의 살림살이가 궁해오닛간 내 영업가지 영향이 이러케 큼니다그려. 엇더케 살어야 올습닛가. 참 딱한 일이여요.<158>
