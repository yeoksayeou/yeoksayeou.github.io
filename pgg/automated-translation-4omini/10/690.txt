Two Hopes: Critique and Requests Regarding 별건곤  
                                勞働運動社 姜友  

In our society, which is crying over a difficult and painful life, it is certainly a delightful and heartwarming matter that the peculiar and beautiful monthly magazine 별건곤 has come into existence. 

Moreover, what the masses appreciate and joyfully feel is not only the review of historical heinous figures but also the effort to detect and expose the evil hidden within society. This is something for which the general populace cannot help but express gratitude. At the same time, what I desire from your magazine is a reduction in the complexity of the writing and the simplicity of its style.<109>  

**Summary**  

The article expresses appreciation for the magazine 별건곤, highlighting its contribution to society by exposing hidden evils along with discussing historical figures. The author also requests improvements in the writing style for better accessibility. From a historical perspective, this article reflects the desire for media to combat societal issues during the colonial period, suggesting a burgeoning awareness and demand for social accountability in Korea.

希望 두 가지,  別乾坤에 對한 批判과 要望
						勞働運動社 姜友
쓰리고 압픈 생활에 울고 잇는 辛酸한 우리 社會에 웃음이 나오고 趣味가 잇는 妙하고도 아름다운 月刊誌 別乾坤이 잇게됨은 여간 귀엽고 반가운 일이 안인 줄로 암니다.
더구나 大衆이 痛快히 생각하고 감사히 녁이는 바는 歷代陰凶人物을 여디업시 檢討하는 것뿐 안이라 社會의 裡面에 숨어 잇는 罪惡을 摘拔하야 세상에 暴露하는 것은 一般大衆이 感謝를 마지 안는 바이다. 동시에 貴誌에 바라는 바는 誌代의 引下와 文體의 平易다.<109>
