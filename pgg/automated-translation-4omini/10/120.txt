기사제목	銀行에서 본 朝鮮人 經濟力, 今年一年 朝鮮사람의 生活은 엇더하엿나, 여러 方面으로 모아 본 昨年과 今年의 比較 (Economic Power of Koreans Seen from the Bank, How Has Korean Life Been This Year, A Comparative Analysis of Last Year and This Year from Various Aspects)  
필자	韓一銀行 李恒鍾  
기사형태	회고·수기 (Memoir)

The income and expenditure of the bank show that there is little difference between this year and last year. However, this statement only reflects the digits formally and does not accurately represent the reality; in terms of cash transactions, it is considerably sluggish compared to the same time last year. As everyone knows, our Korea is an agricultural country, and as such, the overall economy significantly affects the prices of grains. Since the transactions of the bank mainly involve landowners, the prices of grains have drastically plummeted this year, causing landowners to absolutely refrain from selling their grains, resulting in a lack of monetary circulation and simultaneously reducing the general purchasing power, leaving the commercial sector entirely sluggish. 

Regarding the bank, September to the following year’s February and March is generally the peak time for transactions, but this year has been extremely quiet, almost like summer. As mentioned earlier, the bank's income and expenditure do not show much difference numerically when compared to last year; however, it is noticeable that promissory notes have been deferred, and the recovery of other loans has been significantly delayed. In summary, this year has seen a reduction in general purchasing power due to the plummeting grain prices and the tightness of finances, making the bank considerably sluggish compared to the previous year.<159>  

**Summary**  

This article reflects on the economic situation of Koreans as perceived by a bank official, noting the stagnation resulting from plummeting grain prices and diminished purchasing power due to the prevailing agricultural economy. It emphasizes how financial transactions slowed notably compared to the previous year, indicating deeper economic struggles faced by Koreans during the colonial period. Historians may find the observed decline in purchasing power and its impact on daily life significant in understanding the broader economic conditions and challenges faced by Koreans under colonial rule.

銀行에서 본 朝鮮人 經濟力, 今年一年 朝鮮사람의 生活은 엇더하엿나, 여러 方面으로 모아 본 昨年과 今年의 比較
						韓一銀行 李恒鍾
은행의 수입지출로 보면 금년도와 작년도가 별로 차이가 업슴니다. 그러나 그것은 행식상 수字만 따저서 하는 말이지 실제에 현금거래로 말하면 작년 이때에 비하야 퍽이나 한산함니다. 일반이 다 알으시는 바와 가티 우리 조선은 농업국인 까닭에 일반의 경제가 모도가 곡물가격 여하에 큰 영향을 밧슴니다. 은행의 거래도 대부분이 地主들인대 금년에는 곡가가 아주 참욱하게 폭락케되야 地主들이 곡물을 절대로 팔지를 안이하니 따러서 금전의 융통이 되지 안는 동시에 일반의 구매력(購買力)이 또한 감소하야 상업계가 모다 한산함니다. 銀行으로 말슴하야도 9월로부터 翌年 2,3월까지가 가장 取引이 빈번하고 밧분 때인대 금년에는 아주 한산하야 녀름이나 맛찬가지올시다. 먼저도 말슴한 것과 가티 銀行의 수입지출이 數字上으로는 昨年과 별차이가 업스나 대개는 手形도 延期換書가 만코 其他貸出金回收가 아주 지연함니다. 총이 말슴하면 금년은 곡가폭락의 영향으로 일반 구매력이 감소하고 금융이 경색하야 작년에 비하면 銀行도 퍽 한산한 모양임니다.<159>
