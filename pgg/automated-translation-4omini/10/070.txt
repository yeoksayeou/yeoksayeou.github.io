기사제목	알 수 업는 몃 까지, 꿈에 본 하나님!(追憶)(Unknown Until What, God I Saw in My Dream!)  
필자	慶雲洞 梧城生  
기사형태	잡저 (Miscellaneous Writing)  

The hot summer is difficult to endure, but even more unbearable is the profuse sweating; there is no way to avoid this. However, bathing in cold water during the chilly winter is also an inexplicable matter! 

Blind people, unable to see well, try to manage their lives while unwittingly wearing glasses, which is also a mystery! It is evident that even students who can hardly pay their monthly tuition fees prioritize their appearance. 

Young women who may appear demure at first glance have unspeakable secrets beneath the surface, yet they pretend to be aloof. What’s more, the sight of women strolling with parasols in the cold winter is also an enigma!<155>  

**Summary**  

The article reflects on the peculiarities and contradictions found within society, highlighting various human behaviors that may seem illogical or inconsistent. This showcases the complexities and uncertainties of life during the colonial period in Korea, where societal expectations and personal struggles intersect. Historians may find the focus on the superficial appearances and underlying truths of individuals to be particularly revealing about the tensions of identity in a colonized environment.

알 수 업는 몃 까지, 꿈에 본 하나님!(追憶), 葉書通信
						慶雲洞 梧城生
더운 녀름에 그냥 견듸지도 못할 터인데 그보다 더욱 뜨업고 더운 汗증하는 것도 할 수 업는 일이여니와 치운 겨울에 冷浴하는 것은 알 수 업는 일!
소경이 압흘 못 보아서 집행이로 짐작대고 다니면서도 의려히 眼鏡을 쓰고 다니니 그도 알 수 업는 일이여니와 月謝金도 제대로 못내는 學生들이 몸치장만 하니 그도 알 수 업는 일!
남이 보기에는 얌전해 보이는 女學生님들도 內容을 알아보면 말못할 XX가 잇스면서도 거죽으로만 새침이를 뚝 따는 것도 알 수 업는 일이어니와 치운 겨울에 陽傘쓰고 다니는 婦人들이 만흔 것도 알 수 업는 일!<155>
