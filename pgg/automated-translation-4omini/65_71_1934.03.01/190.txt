First New Popular Song Contest Results Announcement

The boat that carries you, when the wind blows,  
The waves, the waves, will push and pull me along.  
Though the fair wind may keep the sail full,  
It is the weary soul that will drift away.

The boat that carries you, this body too,  
Drifting as the wind blows, no matter where.  
Longing for those past days too much,  
I cry alone at the bright moon floating on the water.

The boat that carries you, even without a destination,  
Today here, tomorrow elsewhere.  
Though my heart is drawn along, my body drifts,  
Will my dreams return to those old days?

Kaesong "Horseman"

The silvers of spring fluttering in the breeze,  
The lark’s song, as I wander alone and pause,  
Letting out a long, unclear sigh softly,  
My heart cries for no reason, I don’t know why.

The forsythias are blooming, smiling in spring,  
I softly circle them smiling shyly,  
Blushing, I approach like a breeze,  
My heart smiles softly, I don’t know why.

Along the roadside, the green grass, beautiful spring,  
Every time I walk by with him, my heart races,  
Bowing my head shyly,  
My heart trembling softly, I don’t know why.

Post-Contest Reflection

As mentioned in our advertisement, it has become increasingly apparent that today’s popular songs have drifted towards vulgarity, a significant concern for us. This is an issue all wise readers are well aware of. The emergence of well-composed popular songs is yearned not only by the educated class but the entire populace. Hence, we have undertaken a project since the New Year’s issue to solicit the newest and most artistically refined songs from readers. To our surprise, we have received an overwhelming response for our first contest, so we have selected two songs as the winners from over 300 submissions, after careful scrutiny.

Of course, among those submitted, there were many wonderful songs that did not fit our specific contest criteria. Regrettably, none received special mention, but we are optimistic more fine works will emerge soon. We encourage your continued participation in our efforts to maintain high standards. The two winning pieces will be entrusted to our highly reputable companies, Pakta and Siyeon, for composition, and we expect their songs will spread widely soon.

Representatives from our editorial department and the two record companies attended the ceremony including Go Pan, Lee Gam-gu. We can assure you that even if only two new clean popular songs have emerged that might overshadow existing records, it is a momentous occasion.

  
**Summary**  

This article announces the winners of the First New Popular Song Contest, highlighting the degradation of popular music and the desire for artistic refinement among the populace. It reflects the concerns of the time regarding cultural standards amid colonial influences in Korea. Historians might find interest in the push for artistic purity and cultural identity during the 1930s as a response to colonial pressures, indicating a yearning for a cultural renaissance amidst oppression.

選後感
임의 모집광고에도 말슴한 바와가치 지금의 유행가는 점점 야비하게만 치우처 그 영향이 크게 우리네들의 고려를 요하기까지에 이른 것은 현명한 독자 여러분이 더 잘 알고 게실 문제이겟습니다. 그리하야 조흔 내용을 갓추운 류행소곡의 출현은 일부 지식게급 뿐 아니라 민중 전체가 이를 갈망해 마지 안는 바입니다. 그래서 우리는 뜻한 바 잇서 지난 신년호부터 만천하 독자께 가장 새롭고 또한 예술적으로 고아(高雅)한 노래를 모집하엿든 바 의외에 예거 이상으로 성황을 보게 되야 우선 제1회로 우에ㅅ 두분의 노래를 입선으로 뽑앗습니다. 응모총수가 300여 편에서 가장 엄선의 엄선을 하야 책임잇게 선정한 것이 이 두 편입니다. 물론 이 중에는 락선식히기에 앗가운 조흔 노래가 만엇습니다만은 본지가 특별히 생각하고 모집한＜36＞ 규정에 어그리진 것은 읏절수 업시 죄다 빼여버렷습니다. 특선이 업섯슴이 유감이나 머지안어 더 조흔 작품이 출현될 것을 밋고 우리는 게속하야 초지를 관철코저 하오니 만히 응모해 주시기 바랍니다. 이번 당선된 두 편은 우리가 가장 신임하는 박타와 시에론 두 회사에 부탁하야 임의 작곡중에 잇스니가 이것이 노래가 되야 전선적으로 퍼저나갈 날도 머지 안엇습니다. 압흐로도 게속하야 이 두 회사와 협약하고 당선작품은 이 두 회사의 보반(譜盤)을 통하야 퍼저나갈 것이오니 여러분은 좀더 분발하야 만흔 노력의 결정을 보내주시기 바람니다.
이번 고선에 잇서 본사 편즙국은 물론이요 두 레코-드회사를 대표해서는 孤帆 李堈求씨가 참례를 하엿습니다. 하여간 열분이 읽어보셔도 알겟습니다만 종래 레코-드를 압도할 새로운 노래- 깨끗한 류행소곡을 단 두 편이라도 엇게 된 것을 크게 깁버하는 바입니다.＜37＞
