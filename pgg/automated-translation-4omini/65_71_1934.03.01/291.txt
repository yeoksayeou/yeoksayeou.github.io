Several days ago, while cleaning, a certificate like this came out. However, it has become somewhat damaged due to age, so I am unable to ascertain the amount. Nevertheless, it is an important document, and I need to find out how much it is. Please let me know the amount and send it to me.

Points to Note for Sending Prize Entries

★ Write a simple answer on a postcard-sized inquiry, ensuring to clearly include your address and name.  

★ A reader's certificate included in this issue must be attached to the answer sheet. If not attached, the entry will be invalid.  

★ The envelope must be marked “Kyŏngsŏng Kaep'ipsa Byeol Gyeonk'ŭn Prize Affairs” and also on the postcard write “Manuscript Inside” in red ink. If “Manuscript Inside” is not written, a penalty (deficiency) will be incurred.

**Summary**

This article presents a prize question, inviting readers to uncover the value of a certain certificate. It emphasizes the importance of adherence to rules regarding submission and provides an enticing reward structure for participants. It notably reflects the publication's engagement with readers during the colonial period, highlighting the era's cultural environment and the types of contests that appealed to the public. Historians may find value in examining how such participatory media addressed social and economic challenges faced by Koreans under Japanese rule.
