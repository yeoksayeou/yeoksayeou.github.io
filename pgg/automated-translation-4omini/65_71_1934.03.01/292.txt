★雜誌注文하는 法  
It may sound a bit silly to mention how to order magazines at this point, yet there are safe methods that cost little money. However, there have been occasions where there have been complications for those who ordered books or for the headquarters. ---- Judging by the current statistics, the number of people sending actual postal money orders is not small, but there are also those who, in an attempt to be more convenient, send stamps or, alternatively, send cash simply stuffed in an envelope, sometimes as little as three won. However, sending in this manner can sometimes lead to loss in transit or other mistakes, causing great difficulties. Thus, it is best to use the postal account (振替口座) when possible. Compared to just sending stamps, it may seem more tedious, but it is the most economical and safe way to do so. Furthermore, if the address and the name of the magazine being ordered are not clearly written, it is easy for mistakes to occur. (開闢社振替口座振京城八一○六番)  

  
**Summary**  

The article provides guidance on safely ordering magazines, emphasizing the use of postal accounts over sending cash or stamps to avoid complications. It reflects the challenges faced by consumers during the colonial period in Korea, highlighting the trust issues surrounding postal services. Historians might find this insight into consumer behavior during colonial rule revealing, particularly in how economic practices shifted due to colonization.
