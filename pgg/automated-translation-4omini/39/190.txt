大建物 구경  
This photo is of 徽文高普 (Huiwen High School). The site of the school has significant historical value as it was formerly the site of the 觀象監 (Gwanseonggwan) and has a favorable location. The playground and the school buildings are well-constructed, making it the best among the schools run by Koreans.  

Land area: 5741 pyeong  

Main building: 3 stories, brick construction, area of 360 pyeong  

West building: 2 stories, brick construction, area of 262 pyeong  

Lecture hall and special classrooms: 2-story wooden construction, area of 210 pyeong  

Auxiliary buildings: 1-story wooden construction, area of 78 pyeong  

Playground: 4000 pyeong  

The first construction of the school began in August of the 10th year of 光武 (Gwangmu). Subsequently, there were expansions in July of the 2nd year of 大正 (Taisho), April of the 11th year, and June of the 2nd year of 昭和 (Showa), totaling three expansions at a cost of approximately 100,000 won.
