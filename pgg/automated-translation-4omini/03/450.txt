The Handbag Game  
A well-dressed modern woman boarded the tram. Reader, pay attention and focus.  

1. The woman chooses her seat.  

2. The conductor comes to collect the fare.  

3. The woman takes off her gloves.  

4. She opens her handbag.  

5. She pulls out her wallet.  

6. She closes her handbag.  

7. She opens her wallet.  

8. She takes out a ten-jeon coin (10전).  

9. She closes her wallet.  

10. She opens her handbag and puts her wallet in it.  

11. She closes her handbag.  

12. She pays ten jeon and receives five jeon in change.  

13. She opens her handbag.  

14. She takes out her wallet.  

15. She closes her handbag.  

16. She opens her wallet and puts in five jeon.  

17. She opens her handbag and puts her wallet in it.  

18. She closes her handbag.  

19. She puts her gloves back on.  

"Oh dear, I’ve come all the way down," she says. <123>  

**Summary**  

This article humorously details the series of actions a fashionable woman performs while riding a tram, focusing on her handling of money and her handbag. Historians might find this article interesting as it reflects the changing roles and behaviors of women in 1920s Korea, illustrating how modern influences were manifesting against a backdrop of colonial rule and shifting societal expectations.

가방노름
모양 잘 낸 최신식 녀자 한 분이 뎐차에 올너 탓다. 독자여 정신차려 닑으시요.
1, 녀자가 자리를 골나 안는다.
2, 차장이 와서 표를 내란다.
3, 녀자가 장갑을 벗는다.
4, 손가방을 연다.
5, 돈지갑을 끄낸다.
6, 손가방을 닷는다.
7, 지갑을 연다.
8, 10전 짜리 은전을 집어 낸다.
9, 지갑을 닷는다.
10, 가방을 열고 지갑을 넛는다.
11, 가방을 닷는다.
12, 10전 짜리 주고 5전을 거슬러 밧는다.
13, 가방을 연다.
14, 지갑을 끄낸다.
15, 가방을 닷는다.
16, 지갑을 열고 5전을 넛는다.
17, 가방을 열고 지갑을 넛는다.
18, 가방을 닷는다.
19, 장갑을 다시 낀다.
『아이고 발서 다 왓스니 나려 주시요-』＜123＞
