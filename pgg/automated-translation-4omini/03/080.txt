A Rare Tragedy of the World, Piano Duel  
When we think of a duel, it usually involves sword fighting in the past, or gunfights in the present, but this is a piano duel! An immensely rare and tragic duel that one could not even dream of.  

In the spring of 1880, in the Palace of Parets in Spain, a musician was insulted grievously by another musician. Angered, he challenged the other to a duel. Being a musician by profession, he proposed that they duel with pianos instead of swords or guns.  

One might doubt how a piano could be used to harm an opponent! However, it was presumed that they intended to engage in a competition of skill to resolve their issues.  

Dear readers, you are encouraged to read the following remarkable account slowly.  

The duel between the two musicians lasted 48 hours (two full days) without drinking water, eating food, or even taking a break to rest. They sat continuously at the pianos for 48 hours straight.  

During that time, one of them played a single piece 150 times, while the other played it 151 times. However, in the middle of the contest, he became dizzy, fainted, and died. The other player, who played 150 times, survived until the end but fell into a near-comatose state and was later hospitalized before passing away. Additionally, the four friends who were witnesses to the piano duel all went insane, and the two pianos were severely damaged in the process. <23>  

**Summary**  

This article recounts an extraordinary event from 1880 in Spain, where two musicians engaged in a highly unusual duel using pianos instead of weapons. The contest lasted for an astonishing 48 hours, resulting in tragic outcomes including madness among witnesses and the deaths of the participants. This bizarre episode reflects aspects of cultural expression during a time when artistic integrity was often at odds with personal honor, offering a unique lens on international interactions and the cultural dynamics during Korea's colonial period.

天下의 珍悲劇, 피아노 決鬪
결투라 하면 전갓흐면 의레 칼싸홈, 지금 갓흐면 의레 륙혈포 싸홈이지만은 이것은, 피아노 결투! 꿈에도 생각 못할 大珍悲결투이다.
西曆 1880년 봄에 西班牙의 파레쓰-府에서 한 音樂家가 갓흔 어느 音樂家에게 불의의 큰 侮辱을 당하고 분개하야 결투를 청하얏는데 本職이 音樂家인 만큼 칼로도 안이고 총으로도 안이고 피아노로 결투를 하자고 하엿다.
대체 피아노로 엇더케 적을 죽이노! 하고 疑心해서도 못쓰고 『피아노 競彈을 하야 기술의 우승으로 적의 名譽를 죽이는 것이겟지』 하고 지레 짐작을 해서도 못 쓴다.
독자여 그대는 천천이 아래의 珍記錄을 읽으라
두 音樂家의 決鬪는 48시간(滿二晝夜) 물도 안 먹고 밥도 안 먹고 잠ㅅ간 쉬지도 안코 뛰지도 안코 안즌채로 고대로 안저서 48시간을 내리 계속해 타기 내기엿다.
그 중의 한 사람은 그동안에 어느 한가지 곡을 150회를 彈奏하엿고 또 一人은 151회를 彈奏하다가 중도에 눈깔이 핑핑 돌아 기절하야 卽死하엿고 150회만 한 이는 죽기까지는 안이하엿스나 거의 人事不省이 되야 병원에 입원하엿다가 곳 사망하엿다. 그리고 그 피아노 결투에 증인으로 엽헤 入會하고 잇든 友人 4명은 모다 광인이 되얏고 피아노 두 채는 몹시 破損되여 버렷다.＜23＞
