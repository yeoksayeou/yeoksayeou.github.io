Fifteen Jeon  

Consider it as if you've bought a pack of cigarettes and send only 15 jeon (15 pieces of 1 jeon postage stamps) to Kaebiksa. We will send you three extraordinary items that will genuinely surprise you. Since the magazine culture has begun in Korea, this is the first time we have seen something remarkable in terms of the printing technique, dazzlingly done to a point where even professional printers will be astonished, which is the 'New Year’s Issue' of 'Children' published on January 1st.  

It is a misconception to think that 'Children' is only for the young. Once you see this New Year’s issue, you will realize that 'Children' is not just delightful for children, but also beneficial and enjoyable for adults as well.  

Moreover, enclosed within the children's book as a first appendix is 'News' which includes various New Year’s knowledge, and the special New Year appendix which is distributed for free, 'Sullangmalpan,' is a splendid toy for family entertainment worth 20 jeon if bought.  

Even if you are deceived, send just 15 jeon to Kaebiksa and request 'Children.' You will truly be surprised by the three amazing items delivered to you on that very day. Please send the 15 jeon to Kaebiksa, treating it as if you've paid for a pack of cigarettes. <61>  

**Summary**  

The article is an enthusiastic promotion for a children's magazine, 'Children,' encouraging recipients to send a modest fee in return for an issue that promises to be remarkably printed and beneficial for all ages. It highlights the misconception that children's content is only for kids and suggests that the New Year’s edition contains valuable knowledge for adults too. Historians may find interest in how such publications sought to influence cultural perceptions and promote literacy during the Japanese colonial period, demonstrating an emerging publishing industry and the broader engagement with educational materials.

잡지명	별건곤 제3호
발행일	1927년 01월 01일
기사제목	十五錢만
기사형태	사고·편집후기

十五錢만
담배 한 갑 사먹은 셈 치고 15錢만(1錢 郵票 15枚만 사서) 開闢社로 보내 보십시요. 아모라도 깜짝놀낼 굉장한 것 세 가지를 보내 드립니다. 朝鮮에 雜誌란 것이 생긴 후 처음보게 굉장한 맵시를 내고 印刷術에 잇는 技術을 다 하야 專門印刷業者가 보기에도 안이 놀낼 수 업시 찬란히 된 것이 이번 1월 1일에 발행된 『어린이 新年號』 임니다.
『어린이』 는 어린 사람만 볼 것이라고 하는 것이 잘못된 생각임니다. 이번 新年號를 보시면 「어린이」는 어린이에게만 자미잇는 것이 아니라 어른에게도 엇더케 자미잇고 有益한 것인 줄 아실 것임니다.
그러고 어린이 冊 속에 一部씩 끼여 進呈하는 第一附錄 『新聞』 은 여러 가지 正月知識이 갓득히 실려 잇고 新年特別附錄으로 無料進呈하는 『出世말판』 은 20錢 주고는 사지 못할 新春一家團樂의 大遊戱具임니다.
속는 셈치고라도 15錢만 開闢社로 보내고 『어린이』 보내라 해보십시요. 그 잇흔날로 配達되는 세 가지 굉장한 물건을 밧아가지고 참말로 놀내실 것임니다. 답배 한 갑 사 먹은 셈치고 이번 한 번 15錢을 開闢社로 보내십시요.＜61＞
