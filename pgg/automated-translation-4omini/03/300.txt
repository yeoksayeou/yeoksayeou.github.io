New Year’s Reflections  
Chao Dong-sik  

1. Set firm goals. It is the order of things for a person to organize all that is unorganized in every matter, and we are gradually entering a time of organization from all the confusion of our era. Therefore, we must set a certain standard in some direction for our advancement and must strongly fight for it.  

2. Then, everyone should strive in their respective roles. This effort in the tasks they are responsible for represents faithfulness to oneself. Ultimately, the effect of this will be reflected in society as well. <85>  

**Summary**  

In this article, the author emphasizes the importance of setting clear goals and dedicating oneself to their responsibilities as the new year begins. These reflections convey a sense of urgency for individuals to organize their lives and actively contribute to society's improvement. From a historical perspective, the article reflects a mindset during a tumultuous period in Korea, where fostering personal responsibility and community engagement was vital amid colonial challenges. Historians might find the call for self-improvement and socio-political engagement particularly relevant to the evolving national consciousness of Koreans under colonial rule.

年頭感
						同德女高普 趙東植
一, 목표를 단단히 세울 것. 사람이 무슨 일에나 정리되지 못한 것을 정리하여 나가는 것이 순서인 바 우리도 모든 혼란한 시대로부터 차차 정리 시대에 드러가게 되엿슴니다. 그러면 우리는 압흐로 나가는데 어느 방면에 일정한 표준을 세우고 굿건히 싸워 나가야 할 것임니다.
二, 그러고 누구나 자기 직무에 분투할 것. 그것은 자기가 담당해 나가는 일에 奮鬪 努力함은 먼저 자기에게 충실함이요. 때라서 그 효과는 사회에 밋처 가는 것임니다.＜85＞
