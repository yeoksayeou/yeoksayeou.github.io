年頭感  
						朝鮮日報社 安在鴻  
1. Until now, what we have mainly asserted and commented upon has mostly been abstract principles, vague and broadly discussed in a global and human context, but we must now move towards a more detailed exploration of the actual problems and circumstances of Korea.

2. Even if the current reality is at the brink of destruction and in a deplorable state, we should not merely express sorrowful cries with our voices or pens, but should encourage ourselves to strive with greater courage to fight back. To do so, everything must ultimately return to the fundamental issues, but I will avoid discussing them here.  
<47>  

**Summary**

The article reflects on the need for a shift from abstract discourse to practical discussions regarding the reality of Korea at the time. It emphasizes the importance of courage in the face of despair, calling for a proactive response to the dire circumstances rather than just expressions of sorrow. This perspective is significant in the context of Korean colonial history as it highlights the growing awareness among intellectuals of the need to confront the harsh realities of Japanese rule and to activate a spirit of resistance, something that historians may find revealing about the emerging national consciousness during this period.
