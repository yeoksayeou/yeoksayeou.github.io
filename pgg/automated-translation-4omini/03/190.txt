잡지명	별건곤  
발행일	1927년 01월 01일  
기사제목	年頭感 (New Year Thoughts)  
필자	朝鮮日報社 韓基岳  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

We lack the ability to implement, which is our most significant fault. Currently, we young people often talk about rural issues, yet few actually go into the countryside to work. Even those who advocate for certain principles tend to speak only theoretically, with little effort to engage in practical activities. This general lack of implementation is evident. Personally, every New Year, I tell myself that I will accomplish something this year, but when I look back later, I realize that nothing has been executed. Thus, everyone hopes to resolve to finally take action in whatever venture they pursue.

  

**Summary**  

This article reflects on the inadequacy of action among the youth in colonial Korea, emphasizing the gap between discussion and real engagement with rural issues. The writer notes the tendency for individuals to make resolutions without following through, highlighting a significant cultural challenge during this period. Historians might find it interesting as it reveals the aspirations and frustrations of Korean society under colonial rule, showing how young people were conscious of societal issues yet struggled to effectively address them.

年頭感
						朝鮮日報社 韓基岳
우리는 實行力의 不足한 것이 무엇보다도 缺點인가 함니다. 現下 우리 靑年으로서 입으로는 農村問題를 만히 말하지만 實際로 農村에 드러가서 일을 하는 사람은 적고 主義者하여도 역시 理論으로만 말하고 實際 運動에 努力함이 적은 것을 보아도 一般으로 實行이 不足함을 알 수가 잇스며 나 個人으로 보아서도 해마다 新年에 안자서는 今年에는 무엇을 좀 해보려니 하지만 나종에 打算하여 보면 하나도 실행된 것이 업슴니다. 그러면 누구나 무슨 일에던지 새로 실행하기를 決心하는 것이 가장 希望하는 바입니다.
＜59＞
