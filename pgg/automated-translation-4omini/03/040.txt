Staying Up All Night  
The title of this book is written as "Staying Up All Night." I have spent many nights awake to compile this book, and I am also staying up late to have it printed. The printing house will be closed on the 30th, but we arrived at the printing house on the afternoon of the 28th, so we must make it work somehow.  

The printing house is quite large, and it was exceedingly slow, so we had to hassle them quite a bit; they promised to have it ready by the night of the 31st (the first day of the new month) by staying up all night every day until then.  

So it’s all our fault, and we are also staying up at the printing house like this. We don’t blame anyone, but we are unable to arrange the layout of the print as we wished, which has become a problem.  

As for the photos and illustrations, fortunately, we had many plates prepared ahead of time, but we could hardly wait even two hours to get those finished and rushed off without waiting.  

Those who have supported the second issue can probably imagine that 別乾坤 (Byeolgeongun) is a hobby magazine, and we have been trying hard not to harden the eyes of our readers with the layout. Unfortunately, we could not accomplish that while staying up at night like this.  

This month's edition is meant to focus on being published quickly, so we ask for your understanding. (Editorial Note) <13>  

**Summary**  

This editorial note reflects the challenges faced by the magazine's editors in producing the third issue while staying up multiple nights to ensure timely printing. It reveals the dedication and commitment of the editors to maintain quality and aesthetic standards, despite difficulties. Historians might find this insight into the working conditions and publishing culture of the colonial period significant, highlighting the pressures of colonial publishing as well as the cultural aspirations of Korean intellectuals during this time.

잡지명	별건곤 제3호
발행일	1927년 01월 01일
기사제목	밤을 새워서
기사형태	사고·편집후기

밤을 새워서
『밤을 새워서』 라고 이번 이 책의 제호를 붓치고 십슴니다. 잇흘 밤을 꼼박 새워서 편즙한 이 책이 인쇄하느라고 또 밤을 새임니다. 인쇄소에서는 30일날 휴업에 한다는데 우리가 인쇄소에 오기는 28일 오후엿스니 될 수 잇겟슴닛가.
인쇄소가 원래 큰지라 몹시 졸랏더니 한 턱 쓰는 폭으로 31일(섯달 금음날) 밤 열두 시까지에 날마다 밤을 새여서 해 놋켓다고 함니다.
그래서 올탓구나하고 우리도 이럿케 인쇄소에서 밤을 새우고 잇슴니다. 올탁구나 하고 새우는 것이닛가 누구에게 원망은 업슴니다만은 인쇄 맵시를 우리의 마음대로 못 해서 탈낫슴니다.
사진이며 그림을 만히 製版에 착수해 놋코도 다행히 먼저 된 것만 넛코 두 시간만 기다리면 될 것도 못 기다리고 그냥그냥 모라처 갑니다.
2호를 보아주신 이는 짐작하시겟지만은 別乾坤은 취미잡지인 만큼 맵시를 깻긋이 하야 독자의 눈을 苦롭게 아니하려고 만흔 고심을 하는 터인대 이럿케 밤을 새워 가면서 그것을 못하게 되엿슴니다. 이달치는 속히 발행된 것만을 깃버해줍시사는 말임니다.(社告)＜13＞
