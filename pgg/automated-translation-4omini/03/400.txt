기사제목	現代 學生의 눈 (Modern Students' Perspective)  
기사형태	만화 (Comic)  

現代 學生의 눈  
Even while studying for exams, students go to the photo studio to see romantic pictures, female students, and prostitutes. No matter how many eyes they have, it is never enough.  
<109>  

**Summary**  

The article humorously critiques the distractions faced by modern students who prioritize visiting photo studios over their studies. It highlights the temptations and societal influences that students encounter, reflecting a blend of youthful desires and academic pressures. Historians may find this portrayal insightful as it underscores the struggles and cultural dynamics of students during the Japanese colonial period in Korea, revealing insights into youth culture and social expectations of that era.
