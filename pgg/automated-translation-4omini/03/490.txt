발행일	1927년 01월 01일  
기사제목	年頭感 (New Year's Reflection)  
필자	東亞日報社 崔元淳  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

As we move into the new year, it would be beneficial for the youth movement to take on a more significant meaning. In other words, it is preferable to not drift away into vague fantasies detached from reality, but rather to advance upon a scientific foundation. <128>  

**Summary**  

The article discusses the importance of grounding youth movements in reality and science rather than letting them become fanciful or disconnected from practical concerns. This perspective is particularly interesting in the context of Korea's colonial situation, as it highlights an emerging call for pragmatism among the intellectual youth of the time, which may reflect broader aspirations for social and national reform amidst Japanese rule.
