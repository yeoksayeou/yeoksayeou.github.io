Year’s Perspective  
						By Min Tae-won of the Chosun Ilbo  
It is well known to everyone that one cannot speak of any affairs concerning the rural villages in Korea. Therefore, it should be that everyone is earnestly trying for the sake of the rural populace. This can be done through lectures or in the form of night schools.  
<65>  

**Summary**  

The article reflects on the importance of addressing the needs of rural communities in Korea, emphasizing the responsibility of individuals to contribute to this cause through educational initiatives. This perspective sheds light on the societal challenges of the time, particularly in the context of Japanese colonial rule, where rural areas suffered from neglect. Historians might find it interesting how the author advocates for grassroots efforts to uplift rural populations, highlighting a form of early social activism amidst colonial oppression.

年頭感
						中外日報社 閔泰瑗
朝鮮에 잇서서는 農村을 노코서 무슨 일을 말할 수가 업슴은 누구나 다 아는 일. 그러면 누구나 압흐로 農村의 民衆을 爲하야 努力함이 잇섯스면 함이다. 그는 講習會도 조코 夜學가튼 것으로도 조켓지요.
＜65＞
