기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

Year's Beginning Reflection  
						東亞日報社 李光洙  
Our Korean people look at life with pessimism, and thus they despair and do not struggle at all. Therefore, I believe that if we live with a little more hope for the beauty of life and strive in whatever we do, our path to survival will open up.

<107>  

**Summary**  

The article reflects on the negative outlook of Koreans toward life during the colonial period, calling for a shift in mindset. The author emphasizes the importance of hope and struggle in overcoming despair and finding a path for survival. This perspective is revealing of the psychological state of Koreans under colonial rule, highlighting the necessity for resilience and proactive efforts to improve their circumstances. Historians might find this appeal for hope and action significant in understanding the broader social sentiment during Korea's struggle against colonial oppression.
