**Summary**  

This article announces the winners of a prize contest from the previous month, highlighting various articles deemed significant or beneficial, including discussions on modern inventions and water management. It reflects the growing focus on technological and utilitarian progress during the colonial era. Historians may find it particularly interesting as it showcases not only the voices and contributions of Koreans during this period but also the attempt to foster innovation amidst colonial constraints.
