기사제목	珍奇人種의 珍風俗(三) (Strange Customs of Strange Races (3))  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

This is the king of a famous cannibalistic tribe called 'Gajiga Abangura' located in the Apurika (?지역) region. The lower members of the tribe hunt for people daily and bring them to the king. The king shares the meat of the captured individuals with his subordinates, allowing them to sustain themselves. ＜25＞

**Summary**

The article discusses the practices of a cannibalistic tribe in Africa, emphasizing the role of the king and the communal sharing of human meat among tribe members. This reflection on such extreme customs presents a stark contrast to the contemporary global norms and highlights the ethnographic interest in colonial attitudes towards 'exotic' cultures during Korea's colonial period. Historians may find the portrayal of these customs significant in illustrating how colonial narratives often sensationalized and dehumanized colonized peoples.
