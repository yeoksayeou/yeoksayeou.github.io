Anyone Can Send In Their Writings!!

「別乾坤」is calling for submissions. Starting from this issue, we will have a reader section where the writings of our readers will be published. Whether it is poetry (詩), short stories (小説), essays (隨筆), local discussions (地方論壇), reader correspondence (讀者通信), humor (笑話), or anything else, feel free to submit and enhance the glow of 「별건곤」. This magazine belongs to you, the readers.

Living Notes: Please send in your "My Daily Life Diary." Write earnestly about the "bitter life experiences" you are facing. Please avoid sweet love stories as much as possible.

Exposé: Share strange events, shocking happenings, thrilling incidents, or things that cannot usually be left as they are in your locality. The more unknown to others, the more interesting it is.

◇ Guidelines: Please write on manuscript paper (原稿紙) using a standard size of 23 characters per 10 lines, and limit your submission to 20 pages. Place your manuscript in an envelope with a 2-jeon stamp attached and be sure to write "原稿在中" (manuscript inside) and "別乾坤係" (for the magazine 「별건곤」) on it.

◇ Deadline: Submissions should be sent at least five days before the end of the month to be considered for the following month. Manuscripts submitted will be kept by our office, but even if not published, they will not be returned.

◇ Reward: For outstanding manuscripts, we will give a small token of appreciation to the authors.

Kaihaku-sha, Byeolgeon-gon Division  

**Summary**

This article invites readers to contribute their writings to the magazine, highlighting an interest in diverse forms of literature, from poetry to exposés about life experiences. It reflects an effort to engage with the readership actively and gather local narratives, which can be significant in terms of cultural history. For historians, the solicitation of personal accounts and local news provides insight into the socio-political climate of the time, especially in the context of Korean society under Japanese rule in the early 1930s.

暴露文 귀지방에서 이러나는 괴상한일 팻심한일 통쾌한일
公開狀 그대로두고볼수업는일 등을 폭보하고 공개하야 주십시오 될수잇는대로남이알지못하는일이 더욱흥미잇습니다.
