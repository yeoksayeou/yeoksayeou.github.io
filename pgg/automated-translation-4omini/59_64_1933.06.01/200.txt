발행일	1933년 06월 01일  
기사제목	갑 헐한 놈은 (The Cheap Guy)  
기사형태	잡저 (Miscellaneous Writing)  

客 이 양복걸이는 얼마요?  
店員 네 50전입니다.  

客 또 이놈은  
店員 네 30전입니다.  

客 더 헐한 놈은 업소?  
店員 네 오전짜리도 잇습니다.  

客 더 헐한 놈은?  
店員 네 의복을 사다가 손님께서 늘 입고만 게시면 양복걸이를 사실 필요는 업습니다.＜49＞  

**Summary**  

The article humorously portrays a dialogue between a customer and a shopkeeper discussing the prices of coat hangers, with the customer persistently asking for cheaper options. The dialogue reflects the economic conditions of the time, where affordability was a significant concern for consumers. This exchange offers insight into the everyday lives of individuals during the colonial era in Korea, showcasing their struggles with affordability under Japanese rule, which can be revealing for historians examining consumer behavior and economic hardships in colonial Korea.
