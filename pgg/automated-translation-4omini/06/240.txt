To the Readers!

The previous editions of 『別乾坤』 have received overwhelming enthusiasm from our readers, and we have already published an enormous number of copies. However, we regret that due to unavoidable circumstances, each issue has had to end before satisfying the expectations of our readers. Therefore, we hope you will place your orders before each issue's final release. Thus, to cater to the tastes of our regional readers, we will also be addressing local articles frequently from now on. <118>

**Summary**

In this editorial note, the editorial team expresses gratitude to the readers for their support while also apologizing for the premature discontinuation of issues due to unavoidable circumstances. They emphasize the importance of placing orders in advance and highlight their intention to diversify content by including articles that resonate with regional interests. This piece reflects the struggles of intellectual publications during the colonial period in Korea, as they navigated reader expectations and marketplace realities amidst a challenging socio-political environment, which historians might find indicative of broader cultural shifts.
