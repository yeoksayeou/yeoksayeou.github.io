기사제목	忠州의 儁龍이 (The Illustrious Dragon of Chungju)  
저자	저자 미상  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

The phrase "Illustrious Dragon" refers to a well-known figure in the Chungbuk (Chungcheongbuk-do) region, particularly in the areas of Chungju, Hwasan, and Imseong. There is an old saying that wisdom is akin to foolishness. It raises the question of whether he truly acts foolishly or if he is simply mocking the world around him. Regardless, he is infamous in the region for his foolish antics. 

For instance, he is known to drink excessively, which causes local troublemakers to mock him by asking, "Hey, Illustrious Dragon, are you planning to drink yourself to death?" He immediately responds by exposing his backside. They would taunt him, saying, "No matter how hard you try, raising your voice is futile!" and then they would choose a large stick and hit him once. He would bite his lips in response, painfully holding back, and would let out a deep groan. 

On another occasion, he had some debt at a tavern and would go out of his way to avoid passing by it. However, due to unavoidable circumstances, he found himself needing to go past that tavern. Covering his eyes with both hands to avoid seeing anything, he ended up catching the attention of the tavern owner, who exclaimed, "Aren't you the Illustrious Dragon?" He retorted, "Hey, your eyes are like thorns... you can see everything!" 

 

**Summary** 

This article humorously depicts a local character from Chungju known for his foolish behavior and drinking habits, highlighting how he is both ridiculed and recognized in his community. Notably, such portrayals may reflect social dynamics during the colonial period in Korea, as figures like the Illustrious Dragon could symbolize the complexities of identity and societal roles amidst the pressures of colonial life. Historians might find insights into local culture and resistance through humor as indicative of the people's resilience in the face of colonial rule.

忠州의 億龍이
충북 지방의 忠州, 槐山, 陰城 등디에 가서 억룡이라고 한면 모를 사람이 업다. 大智는 大愚에 갓갑다는 녯말이 잇다. 그는 텬품이 정말 바보로 나서 그런지 혹은 이 세상을 조소하느라고 그런지? 엇겟든지 바보의 짓만 골너 가면 하는 까닭으로 그는 愚名으로 그 지방에 명성이 자자한다. 그의 일화의 두어 가지의 예를 들면 그는 술을 대단히 조와하는 때문에 그 몹슬 작란군들이 그를 놀려대느라고 『애! 억룡아 너 술 사죽게 볼기 한 대 마즈려늬?』 하면 대번에 얼는 볼기를 까고 업더린다. 『너- 암만 압허도 아야 소리만 내면 소용업다』 하면서 큼직한 매를 골라서 냅다 한번 때리면 그는 그 니를 응 물고 입을 꼭 깨물엇다가 니를 부득 갈며 가장 웅숭깁게 『끙!』 한다. 그는 또 어는 술집에 외상이 조곰 잇서서 그 집 근처로 가지도 못하다가 한번은 부득이한 사정으로 꼭 그 압흐로 지나게 되어 그는 무한 궁구하다가 두 손으로 자긔 눈을 가리고 지나다가 주모의 눈에 띄여서 쪼차 나와 『너- 억룡이 아니냐?』 한니까 그는 『애- 당신 눈은 송곳 눈인개벼. ... ... 남의 손바닥을 다 뚜르고 보게. ... ...』 ＜37＞
