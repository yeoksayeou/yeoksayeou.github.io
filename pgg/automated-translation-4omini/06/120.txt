趣味의 統計室  
The relationship between parental drinking and the dental development of offspring is a topic I will discuss further, but it is reported that there are certain differences in the development of children's teeth depending on whether the parents consumed alcohol or not. In a study of children who develop teeth at the age of eight months, among those with completely abstinent parents, 72 out of every 100 children had developed teeth, while 66 out of 100 children with parents who drank about one glass had developed teeth, and only 57 out of 100 children with parents who consumed more than that had developed teeth. Furthermore, within eight months, the number of teeth was found to increase in the following order: 2.5 (for abstinent parents), 2.1 (for light drinkers), and 1.5 (for heavy drinkers), indicating that children of alcohol consumers are indeed developing teeth less than those of non-drinkers.

The Ratio of Male to Female Newborns  
Due to the effects of war, there are discussions in society about the gender ratio at birth during and after the war, and it is commonly believed that more males are born. This is purportedly a natural adjustment mechanism. Current reports indicate that the typical ratio is 106 males for every 100 females. A researcher named Lu-ge from Berlin University found an even higher ratio of 116 males to 100 females, thus proving that the number of male births increased during wartime. Another researcher named Ji-gel discovered ratios of 100 to 122 or 100 to 117.

The Lifespan of Animals  
While it is generally believed that humans rarely live beyond seventy years, there are many animals that live far longer, and conversely, some species, like certain rodents, may live only to five years or even experience miscarriage much earlier. **Summary**  

This article presents statistical observations about parental habits, the gender ratio of newborns, and the lifespan of various animals. Particularly notable is the assertion that parental alcohol consumption may significantly impact the dental development of children, with abstinent parents’ offspring showing better development. This reflects societal concerns during the era regarding health and familial well-being, especially in the context of Korean society under colonial rule, which may have drawn heightened attention to the behaviors of parents as indicative of larger social issues.

生兒의 니(齒)와 父母의 飮酒
술을 먹어서 조흔 것인지 낫분 것인지는 여긔서 다시 말할 것이 업지만 술 먹는 부모와 안 먹는 부모의 새이에는 자식의 니ㅅ발 발육에 이런 차가 잇다고 한다. 생후 8개월 째에 니ㅅ발나는 아이를 조사해 보면 절대 금주한 부모의 자녀는 百人에 대하야 72인, 삐-루 한 잔쯤 먹는 부모의 자녀는 百人에 대하야 66인, 그 이상 먹는 사람의 자녀는 百人에 대하야 57인의 比요. 그리고 8개월만에 니의 수효는 右의 순서대로 해서 2개 5, 2개 1, 1개 5이어서 음주자의 자녀는 술 안 먹는 사람의 자녀보다 발육이 더듼 것이 확실하다.

生兒男女의 比
전쟁으로 인하야 初生男女 중 어느 편이 만이 출생하느냐 하는데 대하야는 俗間에서 전후에는 남자의 출생이 만타고들 하는 바 이것이 자연 조절에 의함이라고 한다. 지금 여긔에 관한 보고를 종합하여 보면 평시에는 여자 출생수 100에 대하야 남자 106의 비인데 루-게 씨는 伯林大學에서 여자 100에 대하야 남자 출생수가 116으로서 戰時 남자 출생수가 여자 출생수보다 증가한 것을 증명하고 지-겔 씨는 다시 그 비를 100 대 122 혹은 117이라는 것을 발견하엿다 한다.
