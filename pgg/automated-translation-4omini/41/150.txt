기사제목	이런 이야기를 아십니까 男子牧師가 애난 이야기 (Do You Know This Story? A Male Pastor's Strange Tale)  
필자	天外生  
기사형태	잡저 (Miscellaneous Article)  

Do you know this story? You probably have never heard it before. For the sake of keeping it a secret, I'll keep the place names concealed as well. It was a stormy night in November when K, a resident of a small village, was soundly dreaming, while the thunder rumbled like a snore. Suddenly, there came a loud banging on the door that made the chaos of the night even more agitating. K had been filled with dreadful thoughts while sleeping, and the loud knock jolted him awake. “What an exhausting situation! Who on earth would need a doctor at this hour? If it were someone from the village, surely they'd ring the doorbell instead of banging on the door… maybe it’s a robber?” 

With terrifying footsteps outside, K cautiously peeked through the window. “It's urgent, sir! This is a matter of life and death! Please, come out to the front! There's a car waiting! Hurry!” The urgency in the voice didn’t seem to belong to dangerous men, and seeing a car made K think someone from the city must have visited and fallen seriously ill, which put his mind at ease. However, in his haste, he had forgotten to dress properly and, wearing just his pajamas with his socks in hand, climbed into the car, where two well-dressed individuals were already waiting.

“Who is so ill…” K began to ask, but before he could finish, the car suddenly sped off, leaving him unable to inquire further or respond. As they drove along the rough, bumpy road, he was overwhelmed by fear and worry. Just as they neared the outskirts of the village, K could not hold back and shouted, “Where on earth are we going?” 

Unable to restrain himself, the other two in the car suddenly clamped their hands on him and tied him up, even gagging him. K, still in a daze from sleep, realized he was in deep trouble when one of them calmly said, “Please don’t be alarmed, sir. I’m truly sorry for all this, but we must take you to the house of a noble. The reason is that the young lady at that house unexpectedly needs to give birth tonight, and we’ve come to summon you. However, you must keep this matter a secret and you will be responsible for the baby after it’s born. In return, I promise you one hundred won.” Finally, K sighed with relief. Now understanding the situation, he thought he could manage the secret and the responsibility and rationalized that any noble's daughter must come with her own complications.

After three hours of work, as K played the role of the doctor, a newborn baby lay in his coat, quietly sleeping. “What am I going to do raising this child I’ve taken responsibility for just over money? I can’t abandon it nor put it to death; a hundred won is certainly hard to come by,” he thought anxiously, pacing back and forth in his residence. Just then, there was another loud knock on the door, and he wondered who else could be coming at such an hour. 

“Sir, are you sleeping? Please open the door! The pastor is in critical condition!” It was an elderly woman from the village church. “The pastor! What in the world has happened to him?” K felt a sudden rush of guilt for leaving the child behind and hurried to open the door while still carrying the wrapped baby in his arms. Arriving at the pastor’s house, he gently placed the child in a corner and saw that the pastor indeed looked very ill. The issue was not another sickness; his abdomen was bloated with fluid, which could be fatal.

K thought for a moment and sent the family of the pastor out before starting the necessary surgery alone. The pastor, after having nearly lost his life, finally regained consciousness and said, “Thank you, doctor! I’ll be alright now. But what was going on with my abdomen?” 

“Shh! Please, don’t panic. Just don’t be alarmed,” K said as the daughter of the noble came forward, holding the newborn and said, “Actually, this baby was inside me.” K's remark, stressing that the child had indeed been inside the pastor, took him by surprise. Since a newborn baby born during surgery perfectly proved the situation, he had no further words. It was impossible for him to deny the truth any longer, but he soon became deeply concerned about the future. 

“The fact that a man has given birth poses a significant issue within medical contexts,” K maintained a composure as he seemed to have uncovered a great discovery. “Sir, what will you do about this situation moving forward?” 

“Well, on the surface, it might not seem significant. But if you are to raise it, oh, the effort involved will be tremendous.” With that, K left the pastor with the newborn and swiftly returned home. Meanwhile, the mystified pastor, who found it hard to cope with the situation, began to treat the discovery of the baby as divinely inspired, raising it with a holy reverence. 

Twenty years passed swiftly. On the pastor’s deathbed, the child he had cherished tightly held his father’s hand just before departing, exclaiming, “Father, I know that I am not a man and that you are not my father, but rather my mother.” 

“That’s right. I am your mother.” Within this, the poor mother could not express a single word as her father was summoned to heaven.  

 

**Summary**

This article presents a bizarre story of a male pastor who, through a series of unfortunate events, ends up unintentionally giving birth after a young noblewoman summons a doctor to help her deliver. The absurdity of a man experiencing childbirth raises questions around gender and societal norms in a deeply patriarchal society. This story reflects the pressing issues of gender and identity during the colonial period in Korea, with implications on societal expectations and the medical understanding of gender roles.

K씨는 잠을 자면서도 무시무시한 생각이 잇섯는데 별안간에 뚜드리는 문소리에 곤히 자던 잠을 번쩍 깨엿습니다.『아니 이 밤ㅅ중에 웬 급한 병잔고 의사질도 참 고달프구나. 그러치만 동리 사람 가트면 초인종을 누를텐데 문을 뚜드리는 것을 보니 웬처ㅅ사람인 듯 습흔데....혹시 도적놈일가?..』K씨는 무시무시한 발자국을 창압흐로 옴겨 가만히 밧겻을 내여다 보앗습니다.『큰일 낫습니다 선생님. 생명에 관게가 잇는 일이니  빨니 좀 나와 주십시오. 현관압헤 자동차가 왓스니 급히 나와 주십시오. 선생님 빨니.』이 급히 부르는 폼이나 말솜시나 별노 위험한 자들은 아닌 것 갓고 자동차까지 가지고 왓다는데는 어느 도회사람이 려행을 왓다가 급한 병이 난 모양이라고 생각하고 K씨는 안심하고 따라가기로 하얏습니다. 그러나 엇지나 급히 서둘느는지 미처 옷도 입지 못하고 와이샷스는 뒤집어 입고 양말은 손에 쥐인 채로 자동차 안으로 드러안고 보니 자리에는 풍채가 점잔은 두 사람이 기다리고 안젓섯습니다.『누가 그리 병환이 위중.....』K씨가 막 물어보려니까 자동차가 왈칵 떠나버리는 바람에 혀끗을 물려서 더 말도 못하고 대답도 못 드럿습니다. 길이  엇지나 웃틀둣틀한지 키질하는 이상 까부는 데다 질풍가티 몰고가는 바람에 말을 몇 번이나 꺼내랴다가 혀끗만 깨물니고 눈만 찝흐리고 잠잣고 잇슬 수 밧게 엇전 까닭을 몰낫습니다.그러나 어듸로 데리고 가는지 똑바로 다라나는데 거진 그 촌락끗까지 왓슬 때 K씨는 겁이 와락 나서 『대체 어디ㅡ로 가는 셈이요.』-견듸다 못하야 버럭 니러나랴고 하얏드니 좌우에 안젓든 친구들이 왈칵 주저안치며 준비햇든 끈으로 잔뜩 묵거노코 입에다 재갈까지 물려노니 꿈꾸는 K씨도 이제는 경첫구나 하고 가슴이 벌컥 내려안젓스나 하는 수 업시 하는 대로 당햇습니다. 그러나 뜻밧게 점잔은 목소리 『선생님 너무 놀내지 마십시오. 너무 무레한 짓을 해서 죄송스럽습니다만은 이제 선생님을 뫼시고 가랴는 데는 이 다음 촌 어느 귀족댁입니다. 실상은 지금 가는 그 귀족댁 아가씨가 어느 틈에 배가 불너서 오늘밤에 별안간 산긔가 잇는 까닭에 선생님을 뫼시려 온 것이며 그러고 이 사실을 절대로 비밀을 지켜주시고 장차 나올 아이의 처치까지 선생님께서 책임을 지시라는 것입니다. 그러면 사례로 돈 만원을 드리겟습니다. 그러면 이 일을 입밧게 내이시면 안됩니다.』K씨는 그제야 겨우 휘-하고 한숨을 내쉬고 안심을 하엿습니다. 그러고 보니 아닌게 아니라 묵는 품이 퍽 느슨한 것을 깨달엇습니다. 더구나 돈 만원이＜24＞생길 것을 생각하니 이까짓 결박쯤이야 얼마든지 견듸겟고 그까진 비밀쯤이야 얼마든지 직힐 수가 잇다고 생각하며 『귀족에 딸도 별 수 업군.』생각하며 하여간 어느 귀족의 딸인지가 궁금하엿습니다. 그뒤 세시간이 지난 후 호질과로 의사의 직책을 다한 K씨의 외투 속에는 새ㅅ발간 피덩이의 갓난아이가 안겨서 쌕-쌕-잠이 들어 잇섯습니다.『돈 만원 바람에 책임을 지고 다리고 왓스나 호라비 살님을 하는 내 가 이 아이를 장차 엇지 기르노. 그러타고 죽일 수도 업다. 버릴 수도 업고 만원버리가 톡톡하게 어렵구나』하며 엇지할 줄을 모르는 딱한 처지의 K씨 방안으로 왓다갓다 하며 이리 궁리하고 잇스랴니까 또 별안간에 인제는 초인종소리가 요란스럽게 나며 밧게서 누가 찻습니다.『허허 또 누가 왓노. 오늘밤중은 웬일로 이리 병난 이가 만흔가.』외투속에서 잠자는 아기를 엇다가 채 눕히지도 못한 판인데 또 누가 온 모양이니 엇지할 줄을 모르고 허둥거리고 잇섯습니다.『선생님 주무십니까. 얼는 문 좀 여러 주십시오. 목사님이 위중하십니다.』그는 그 동리 교회의 목사님 집에 잇는 노파의 목소리엿다.『목사님!
