기사제목	누구나 알어두어야 할 世界大勢 이야기 (Everyone Should Know the Story of the World Situation)  
필자	金光宇 (Kim Kwang-u)  
기사형태	소식 (News)  

The term "Hoover Economy" has recently become popular. It even appeared in exam answers. So what exactly is the Hoover Economy and what results does it bring?

President Hoover of the United States proclaimed that to eradicate the current worldwide economic depression, the Allied nations should postpone the reparations from Germany for one year, and in return, they should also postpone the payment of the war debts owed by the Allied nations to the United States for one year. As this policy emerged, various opinions arose, and countries considered their own interests, with some countries supporting it while others supported it conditionally. Regardless of the circumstances, most nations today appear to be in favor of it, with no objections. The reason for this is clear; the price of rice has risen, stocks have gone up, the prices of goods have increased, and it is indeed a fact that the market has somewhat revitalized. More notably, the most evident effect is the emergence of several newspaper companies that have been struggling due to a lack of materials during this summer season. Just like Japanese newspapers, the issue of the Hoover Economy held a prominent place in discussions.

Germany has been defeated in the war; this is a universally acknowledged fact. The countries that participated in the war have not only taken land from Germany but also seized its colonies, and on top of that, they decided to impose heavy reparations. However, Germany does not have the strength to pay these reparations. Therefore, it has been assigned an annual payment amount over many years. Yet, the shattered economic state of Germany makes it impossible for it to meet these obligations. Thus, the United States lent money to Germany to help rejuvenate its industry so that Germany could pay off the reparations with the income generated from its recovery. This became known as the Dawes Plan (道斯案). As a result, the economic condition of Germany is gradually recovering. However, this restoration of Germany's economic state translates into an increased strength for Germany, which leads to a potential rebellion against undue oppression. Furthermore, neither France nor England would be pleased to see Germany flourishing under America’s influence. Obviously, both nations are unwilling to keep a resurgent Germany firmly in America's grasp.

Now, how does the Hoover Plan affect the relationships of the concerned countries? England receives payments from Germany as reparation, so there are no issues involved, while France pays half of what it receives from Germany as reparations to foreign countries, keeping the remaining half for itself, which is certainly beneficial for them. Moreover, since America has a large amount of capital, it stands to gain significantly from Germany's economic improvement. Thus, the most advantage is derived by France.

The Decision on Kyŏngdŏng's Electric Utility  

The Kyŏngsŏng Electric Company has been seeing huge profits, and there has been a prolonged discussion about the operation of this company. Recently, there has been fierce activity in the municipal council and among the citizens regarding the proposal, which is centered around the electric utility, creating a division among the council members. One faction supports passing the utility proposal, while another faction insists that the proposal should be researched for its potential benefit before making a decision. After a lengthy discussion on the 9th of last month, the proposal passed with 31 votes for the utility and 19 votes for further investigation.

The Education Labor Workers' Union  

Recently, incidents related to ideology have been discovered, leading to overcrowding in prisons, and specialized judges and prosecutors have emerged. However, the case that has particularly caught the attention of the public is the recent report about the Education Labor Workers' Union. This union has its headquarters in Tokyo, with branches in Korea, mainly opposing XXX-style education and advocating for the elimination of the wage gap between Japanese teachers and Korean teachers. The leader of the Korean branch of the union, Sanggŏmi Taeran (上甲米太郞), reportedly spends only thirty won of his salary on living expenses, dedicating the rest to feeding impoverished students who come to school hungry. Furthermore, he vehemently opposed government policies, specifically against the joint cotton sales system. The local parents and general public highly respected him. He organized the Education Labor Workers' Union, promoting it among the teaching staff in normal schools and initially spreading the idea at Kyŏngsŏng Normal School. He was arrested while actively campaigning, and the only Korean involved was Cho Han-chul (趙判出), a student from Kyŏngsŏng Normal School. Formerly, Sanggŏmi Taeran was the principal of Gwonmyeong Public Elementary School and was also known to be a junior officer in the army.

The Second Round Table Conference of India and England  

While the date has not yet been set, it is anticipated that the second round table conference between India and England will be held in London this autumn. They require that all representatives attend by the end of September, implying that the conference might take place in October. Before the formal meeting, two important subcommittees appear to be convening; one concerns the consideration of the federal organization proposal, while the other will address the issues of minority groups. The members of the National Congress will also participate in this process, increasing the expected attendance from 89 members last time to 108 members this time. Notably, Sarojini Naidu is also expected to attend this meeting.

Mr. Jin Wuyin Visits Japan  

Jin Wuyin, the Foreign Minister of the Guangdong government, visited Japan to meet with the Minister of Foreign Affairs and the military leadership, drawing significant public attention. Recently, various factions opposing Jiang Jieshi's Nanjing government have gathered in Guangdong to establish a government, leading to military actions in the north against Jiang Jieshi by Shi You san and Feng Yuxiang. During these turbulent times, Jin Wuyin's visit to Japan to meet with government officials should not be taken lightly. According to reports, he aims to procure weapons from Japan. It is said that he has expressed strong anti-Japanese sentiment, asking for understanding, but given the broader political context in China, his visit is of considerable importance, making it impossible to overlook.

  

**Summary**  

The article discusses the Hoover Economy initiated by President Hoover, aimed at addressing the global economic depression by postponing reparations and war debts. It also touches upon the economic recovery of Germany, the implications for international relations, and the significant developments in education labor movements in Korea and political discussions regarding India's future. Of particular interest to historians may be the intricate dynamics of international economic policies during the colonial period and the emergence of Korean resistance movements against Japanese rule.

						金光宇
후-버- 景氣는 무엇
요사히 후-버- 경기란 소리가 유행한다. 시험문제 답안에까지 나온다. 그러면 후-버- 경기란 무엇이며 그는 엇떠한 결과를 가저올가?
미국의 대통령 후-버-는 주창하야 가로되 오늘의 세게적인 불경기를 퇴치하는 때는 렬국이 독일에서 바들 배상금을 일년간 유예하고 그 대신에 렬국이 미국에 지불하여야 할 전채(戰債)를 또한 일년간 유예한다고 햇다. 그러한 주°½이 세상에 나오자 여러 가지로의 관측이 잇고 각국은 또한 자기의 리해관게를 생각하고 그 입장에서 찬성하기도 하고 찬성은 하되 조건부로 하는 나라도 잇다. 그는 엇지 되엿건 이제 와서는 관게 렬국이 대개는 그에 찬성하고 이의(異議)가 업는 모양이다. 그 까닭인지 쌀갑도 오르고 주식도 오르고 그 타의 물건갑이 오르며 시장에는 다소 활기가 띄여온 것도 사실이다. 그보다도 가장 그 효과가 현저한 것은 이 여름철에 긔사 재료는 업고 하야 곤경을 치루고 잇든 신문사가 수가 생겻다는 것이다. 일본 신문과 가튼 것은 후-버- 경긔 문제가 한때은 전면을 덥고도 남엇다. 그것으로 해서 후-버-란 사람은 신문사에도 긔사 경긔를 주엇다.
독일은 전쟁에 젓다. 그는 다- 아는 사실이다. 그 전쟁에 참가햇든 렬국은 드러부터 독일의 령토를 빼앗고 식민지(植民地)를 빼앗고 그리고도 부족하야 막대한 배상금을 바더내이기로 햇다. 그러나 독일은 그 배상금을 지불할 힘이 업다. 그래서 여러 해를 두고 일년에 얼마씩 배정하야 밧기로 햇다. 그러나 독일의 파멸된 경제 상태는 그를 감당하는 수 업섯다. 그래서 미국은 독일에는 돈을 대여주고 그것으로 산업의 부흥을 도모하야 거긔에서 나는 힘으로 밧기로 하고 거대한 돈을 독일에 빗으로 주게 되엿다. 그가 이른바 또-스 안(案)이란 것이엿다. 그로 해서 독일의 경제게는 점차로 회복되여 간다. 그럿케 경제 상태가 회복하여 간다는 것은 곳 독일의 힘의 증대이니 그 증대되는 힘은 무리한 억압에 반항하게 되는 것이다. 뿐만 아니라 불란서와 영국이 독일을 미국의 손에 너코는 유쾌하여 할 리가 업는 것이다. 영국은 물론이요 불란서도 구라파에 잇서 금융자본국(金融資本國)으로 손곱기에 드는 처지에 린방의 독일을 그대로 미국의 손안에 언제나 두고 십지는 안은 것이다. 그래서 영안이란 것이 생기엿다. 그럴사록 독일의 힘은 증대하고 잇는 것이다.
이제 후-버- 안으로 인하야 관게 렬국의 리해관게는 엇떠한가?
영국은 독일에게 밧는 그 만큼 전채로 지불하는 것이 잇스니 아무런 문제가 업고 불란서는 독일에서 밧는 액수의 절반을 전채로서 외국에 지불하고 남어지의 절반은 자긔 주머니에 남든 것이니 그에는 리해관게가 잇는 것이다. 미국은 그리하야 독일의 경제 상태가 증진하고 잇슬사록 미국의 자본이 크고 잇는 것이니 그야 물론 리익이 막대한 것이다. 그러니 골는 나라는 불란서이다.
