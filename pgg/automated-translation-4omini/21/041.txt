기사제목	京城오시면 (When You Come to Gyeongseong)  
저자	미제 (Unknown)  
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)  

When you come to Gyeongseong,  

please be sure to visit the Kaebyuk Company.  

When you come, you will not only see all our company operations but also have the chance to view the first building in Gyeongseong and the major institutions in Gyeongseong apart from our company.  

If you have any inquiries while in Gyeongseong, please come to our company for discussions, and we will guide you so that you won't be misled. Be sure to visit us. <5>  

  

**Summary**  

This article invites visitors to Gyeongseong (now Seoul), encouraging them to visit the Kaebyuk Company to learn about its operations and view significant buildings in the city. It reflects a PR strategy aimed at showcasing the company's prominence and the city's notable sites. Historians may find the significance in how such articles reflect the efforts during the colonial period to promote economic and infrastructural development in Korea under Japanese rule, revealing the interplay between business, colonial governance, and urban identity.
