葉書通信  
Please write whatever you think on a postcard and send it to us.  

Observations  

Short Reviews  

News  

There are no limitations on content, so you can send anything you think of. We will compile everything you send into this magazine's postcard communication section. The unique characteristic of this section is that we gather whatever thoughts come to mind. <94>  

<94>  

**Summary**  

This article invites readers to share their thoughts and observations through postcards without restrictions on content. The initiative encourages community engagement and contributes to the magazine's character by showcasing diverse opinions. From the perspective of Korean colonial history, this reflects a moment of emerging public voice and sentiment during a period of political oppression, highlighting how avenues for communication were sought despite restrictive circumstances.
