기사제목	京城의 大觀 (The Grandeur of Gyeongseong)  
기사형태	논설 (Essay)  

Gyeongseong is the capital of Joseon and is a place where the 500 years of the Yi Dynasty were established as the national capital. Although it is now called Gyeongseong, it was formerly referred to as Nanjing or Hangyang or Hansung. It is located at 126 degrees and 59 minutes east longitude, and 37 degrees and 34 minutes north latitude, occupying the central part of the Korean Peninsula. To the north, it is backed by the overlapping peaks of Baegak Mountain, which rises to 143 meters, and Samgaksan, reaching 636 meters. To the south lies Mukhya Mountain (also known as Jongnamsan), which stands at 265 meters, while to the east, the Daryoksan, and to the west, the 338-meter heights of Anhyeongjuryong create a natural fortification. The Han River, one of the five major rivers of Korea, flows majestically, creating a true grand city of mountains and rivers. 

The fortress, although largely in ruins now, originally extended around 9,975 paces (approximately 26 town blocks). The wall’s height reached 41 cheok (about 12.5 meters). There were originally eight gates, of which only the South Gate, East Gate, and remnants of the Changui, Hyegwa, and Gwanghui gates remain. One can imagine the grandeur of the past. (The gates and walls will be discussed in detail in another section). 

The administrative districts are divided into 5 bu (departments) and 49 bang (wards), and within these wards are further divisions into dong (neighborhoods) and kye (blocks). After consolidating, New and Old Yongsan were incorporated into Gyeongseong, resulting in a total of 85 dong and 101 town blocks. Its total area is 2,035 square ri, with the east-west width measuring 1 ri and 33 town blocks, and the north-south length at 3 ri and 12 town blocks. The total circumference of the city is 7 ri and 26 town blocks, which amounts to about 10,629,500 pyeong in area. 

The climate is extreme, with the highest recorded temperature reaching 36.3 degrees Celsius and the lowest dropping to -21 degrees Celsius. The annual average rainfall is 1171.9 mm, which is about 80% of what is recorded in Busan and Wonsan, the wettest areas in all of Joseon, and 1.5 times the amount in the least rainy area, Hamgyeongbuk-do. The maximum recorded rainfall in a single day is 335 mm, which is unmatched across Korea and Japan. The first frost typically occurs around October 12 or 13, and the first snowfall around November 3 or 4. During winter, the Han River can freeze to depths of over a foot, and snow accumulation can reach similar depths at times.

As of the survey conducted in Showa 3 (1928), there are 70,288 households with a total population of 315,006. Among these, 48,454 households are Korean, comprising 115,742 males and 110,091 females, summing up to 225,833 people. Japanese residents number 20,866 households, with 43,032 males and 41,144 females, totaling 84,176 people. Foreign residents amount to 968 households, consisting of 4,179 males and 816 females, resulting in a total of 4,997.

**Summary** 

This article provides a detailed description of Gyeongseong (Seoul), emphasizing its historical significance as the capital of Joseon and its geographical features that contribute to its grandeur. It offers demographic information and insights into the climate and administrative divisions of the city during the late 1920s, illustrating its importance during Korea's colonial period. Historians may find the statistical data on the population composition particularly interesting, highlighting the presence of both Korean and Japanese residents, which reflects the socio-political dynamics of Korea under colonial rule.

京城의 大觀
서울은 朝鮮의 首府오 李朝 500년 國都를 奠하엿든 곳이다. 지금은 京城이라 하지만은 往時에는 혹은 南京 혹은 漢陽 又는 漢城이라 稱하얏스니 동경 126도 59분 북위 37도 34분에 位하야 朝鮮半島의 中部를 占하얏다. 北은 高가 143米되는 白岳山과 636米 되는 三角山의 重疊한 奇峯을 등지고 南은 265米 되는 木覓山(終南山)을 낫하며 東은 駝駱山 西는 338米의 鞍峴諸峯이 둘너 서서 천연의 城府를 이루고 西南으로 山野가 展開하는 동시에 朝鮮 5대 강에 한나인 漢江이 基南으로 洋洋이 흘너가니 실로 山河襟帶의 雄都다. 성은 지금에 多部分 破毁돼얏스나 주위 9,975步(日四里 26町餘) 高가 41척 1촌에 달하고 문은 원래 八門으로서 현재 南大門 東大門外 彰義 惠化 光熙 등이 殘餘할 뿐이나 往時의 雄偉壯觀인 것을 可想하겟다. (門及 성벽은 他項에 詳論함) 행정구역은 自來 5部 49坊에 分하고 坊下에 또 洞, 契가 잇섯스나 倂合 후 新舊龍山을 京城에 편입하야 85洞101町으로 分하얏스니 廣袤는 동서가 1里33町 남북이 3里12町 면적은 2,035方里 一周의 里程은 7里26町인데 此를 坪數로 따지면 총계 1062만9,500평이다. 기후는 寒暑가 심하야 연중 최고 기온은 섭씨36도 3분 최저 기온은 영하 21도다. 雨雪의 年量은 1171,9粍이니 전 朝鮮 내 최다 雨域 釜山 元山에 비하면 약 8할에 상당하고 最寡 雨域인 咸鏡北道에 비하면 1배 반이 되니 一晝夜의 최다량 335粍은 朝鮮 각지 及 日本 각지에 其比가 업다. 初霜은 대개 10월 12,3일경, 初雪은 대개 11월 3,4일 경이니 冬節에 漢江이 결빙하야 尺餘에 달하고 雪도 尺餘에 달하는 時가 잇다.
戶數는 70,288戶 인구는 315,006人인 中 內에 朝鮮人 戶數는 48,454戶로 男이 115,742人 女 110,091人 計225,833人이오 日本人은 戶數 20,866으로 인구 男43,032人 女41,44 計84,176人이오 외국인은 968戶로 男4,179, 女816 計4997에 달한다. [昭和3年調]<1>
