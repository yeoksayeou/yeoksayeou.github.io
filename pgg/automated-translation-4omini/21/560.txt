過去  
						(米) P·와일드  

Apartment  
Narrator (as the curtain rises) Ladies and gentlemen! The act you are about to see is the most tense drama in this world, I believe. Our heroine seems to be waiting for someone alone in her bedroom. It is just past midnight now. (The narrator hides to the side of the stage.)  
Woman (suddenly leans forward and listens closely. Then she goes to the window and looks out. There is a gentle knocking sound. Not looking back) Come in. (A man in evening wear enters.) Please, you must do as I say tonight.  
(He archly offers a strange smile while approaching her. The woman remains still.) Mr. Raynald! (The man is taken aback.) Really, tonight you must confess everything. (The man looks even more surprised.) Surely, you won’t be seeing that woman again, right? Wouldn’t you avoid her, right?  
Man (in a shy tone) Yes— I suppose so.  
Woman Is that so? You kissed here, right? (Pointing to her own lips—but still not looking at the man.) That time, what did the woman do! (The man is at a loss.) Wasn’t she a trouble?  
Man Yes— that’s how it was.  
Woman Until now, you confessed everything without omitting a single thing, right? To ease my mind! That’s exactly why I’m here to see you.  
Man Absolutely not.  
Woman Really! Mr. Raynald! I also wish to tell you everything.  
Man (quietly) Well, yes.  
Woman Mr. Raynald! There must be no secrets between us, okay? I’ll tell you everything!  
Man That’s alright—  
Woman Until now in my life, the very first person who kissed me was indeed an unthinkable villain! Such a wicked fellow took my purity....  
Man That cursed knave!  
Woman At first, here (pointing to her cheek) and then this (lips), and then this (neck) until he kissed me?  
Man Then....  
Woman After that, I returned the kiss. And the second person who kissed me was a horseman.  
Man That scoundrel!  
Woman He deceived me about my purity.... At first, here, then here, and then here—  
Narrator In order to recognize that two minutes have passed, I will turn off the lights for two seconds.  
Woman And then, the ninth man kissed me here—  
Man Good heavens!  
Woman At first, here—  
Narrator To mark the passage of four minutes, the lights will be off for four seconds.  
Woman And then the twenty-third man kissed me here—  
Man Cursed son of a...  
Woman He too tricked me about my purity—  
Narrator Ladies and gentlemen, it has been over an hour; the lights will be off for five seconds now.  
Woman And then the 77,777th man just kissed me here, right? That one, too, was he a terrible fellow? I mean, he wasn’t after my purity— (pauses, rubs her eyes) Oh dear— I have exposed all of it! Now, there really are no secrets between you and me, right? Mr. Raynald! So— (The woman throws her arms wide, approaches the man, and right at the moment of touching his cheek, the expression of fright covers her face. The woman screams) What have you heard from me! (Then she surprisingly faints in front of her husband. (Curtain)  

  
**Summary**  

The play "Past" revolves around a tense encounter between a woman and a man named Raynald, who seems to be hiding secrets about their past romantic connections. The woman reveals a series of intimate encounters with various men, suggesting both a lost innocence and a plea for transparency in her relationship with Raynald. The most intriguing aspect of this piece is its exploration of themes such as betrayal, romantic secrecy, and societal norms regarding female purity in the 1920s context, which may catch the attention of historians studying gender and social dynamics during the colonial era in Korea.
