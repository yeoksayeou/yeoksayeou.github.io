怪常한 競爭  

The person who smokes a cigarette from Luzon the longest wins first place. A strange competition was recently held at the Luna Park in Berlin. Among the competitors was a young lady who stood out and ultimately, a man named August Scott won first prize by smoking for 2 hours and 54 minutes. It seems that smoking well has become a global venture, so all tobacco aficionados, be enthusiastic.  

Weaker than Appendicitis  

A young man from Kentucky, Albert Jock (21), suddenly fell ill with acute appendicitis while headed towards an extravagant wedding venue in a car. When he saw a doctor midway, he was informed that without surgery, his life would be in danger. However, the groom thought, "Love overcomes even pain," and drove straight to the venue to hold the bride's warm hand. But at the end of the ceremony, the wedding car, which was supposed to carry the bride and groom, turned into a lonely ambulance, racing off to the hospital. That night, the new groom held his bride close, even while being transported.  

Mother Koala's Carrying  

The adorable koala dog from Australia climbs trees better than monkeys. It is said that carrying its young on its back has become its specialty. Therefore, even when Amory climbs to a tree's top, the mother carries her baby. Mother koalas are known for being very efficient; they hold the baby by wrapping their arms around its waist and take care of it while looking for food. Such cute creatures are rare, and it is unfortunate that we cannot see them in person. There must be all sorts of curious things in this world.  

Fifty Years of Hunting  

A Duke in France recently celebrated his 50th birthday at home. Not only does he hunt goats well, but he has also captured 1,680 deer over the years. He has decorated his rooms with the heads of the captured deer, which now fill the walls and ceiling. This reflects not just a hobby but an extreme passion for it, and the world is full of curious pastimes.  

The Three-Eyed Cod  

A three-eyed cod, a deep-sea monster, was caught in the South Seas. Over ten kan (about 600 pounds) in size, it had three distinct eyes, making it a previously unheard-of monster in nature. However, consider the plight of this cod. In the deep ocean where sunlight doesn’t reach, having merely two eyes wasn’t sufficient, so it seems a third eye sprouted right in the center of its forehead. It is said that this peculiar eye functions like a lamp, illuminating the dark surroundings, making it an interesting sight indeed.  

The World's Seawater  

If the world’s seawater were to dry up completely and the current rivers continued flowing as they do, it would take 40,000 years for the seas to return to their former state!  

Each person inhales about 50 stone (approximately 1000 liters) of air a day.  

American women spend about one hundred million won annually on cosmetics.  

The Eleven-Year-Old With Facial Hair  

This February has brought forth a youth who astonished the Japanese medical community. This boy, residing in a certain district of Osaka, has been growing at an incredible pace since the age of nine and now has a physique comparable to that of a young adult, with facial hair resembling an older man. Such rapid and effortless growth raises concerns among his parents, who are unsure how to cope with this unique situation. Doctor Shimizu from Osaka Medical University examined the boy and attributed this to the abnormal development of the pineal gland. This case is regarded as extraordinarily rare and is currently being studied in the medical community.  

**Summary**  

The article touches on various unusual competitions and notable news stories, showcasing a mix of human resilience, peculiar animal behavior, and extraordinary occurrences in nature. The interview with a rapidly maturing boy reflects societal concerns during a time of medical curiosity, particularly in colonial Korea, where Western influence and scientific exploration were shaping health perceptions. Historians might find the juxtaposition of traditional views on medicine with modern understandings during the colonial era to be particularly poignant.

盲腸炎보다 弱해

小犬의 어부바

濠洲産의 코아라라는 귀여운 개는 나무에를 원숭이보다도 잘 올는다. 그러고 나어서부터 엄마 등에 업피는 것이 선수라 한다. 그래서 아모리 놉흔 나무 꼭댁이라도 엄마가 어부바를 하고 올나간다. 엄마 코아라도 원체 이력이 나서 아가의 두손을 억개 우로 둘너서 목에다 걸고 애기를 보아 주며 밥버리를 하러 단인다. 귀여운 품이나 강가루가티 보기 실치가 안타니 우리가 이루 보지를 못해서 그럿치 세상에는 별물건 별김승이 다 잇서......

五十年間의 獲物

세눈깔 대구(鱈)

深海의 怪物 세눈백이 대구가 南洋갸삼 水道에서 잡혓다. 10貫이 넘는 몸집에 커다란 눈이 셋이 또렷하게 박엿다는 前代未聞의 괴물.. 造化의 神의 造化여.. 그러나 여러분 그 鱈君의 신세가 되여 생각해 봅시다. 깁고 깁흔 바다물 속에서 태양의 광선이 드러오지 안는 깁흔 속에서 도뎌히 그 큰 놈이 兩便에 잇는 두 눈으로만은 부족해서 이마때기에 가서 한눈이 더 생겻는 듯하다. 이 가온대 눈은 마치 전등가티 스스로 빗을 내여서 근방의 어두운 것을 발킬 수가 잇다하니 구경 좀 햇스면.
