Aramachi-myeon is offering a grand prize of white rice.  

Question (1): Examine this photograph carefully. Is it winter, autumn, spring, or summer? Look at it closely again and describe what you see among the trees and the people.

Please write the answers to the above two questions on the provided paper and send it after cutting it out with scissors.

(If from a distant area, monetary compensation will be sent).  
The remaining 99 will each receive a special prize from our magazine.  

Out of those participating, a drawing will select the hundred winners from a larger group of participants.

**Summary**  

The article outlines a prize contest organized by Aramachi-myeon for readers who correctly answer questions about a photograph and the historical year of the Namdaemun's construction. It also announces winners from a previous contest regarding foreign actresses and results from a voting contest related to a North-South team visit. Notably, this context reflects early Korean efforts at cultural engagement and competition during the colonial period, showcasing the zeitgeist of that era in consumer culture and media interaction.
