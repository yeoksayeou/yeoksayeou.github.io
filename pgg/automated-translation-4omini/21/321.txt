地方色  
We will revitalize Local Color. The unique charm of each region, please send us your good writings to be included in the book. It must be within 17 characters per line and 60 lines total. <85>

  
**Summary**  

This article expresses a desire to rejuvenate the concept of "Local Color," which emphasizes the unique cultural and environmental characteristics of different regions. Contributors are encouraged to submit their reflections or descriptions, indicating a community engagement in preserving and celebrating regional diversity. Given Korea's colonial status during this period, the article may reflect attempts to assert identity and cultural pride amidst external pressures. Historians might find interest in how such initiatives served as means of cultural resistance and identity formation under colonial rule.
