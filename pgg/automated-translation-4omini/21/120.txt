**Summary**

The article is a vivid portrayal of Seoul's unique atmosphere in the late 1920s, blending humor and nostalgia while encapsulating the various scents associated with the city, from food to urban life. Through colloquial dialogue and sensory-rich descriptions, it highlights both the charm and the grim realities of urban living, reflecting the complexities of a city undergoing rapid change amidst colonial influences. Historians may find the depiction of everyday life in Seoul particularly interesting, as it reveals the cultural and social dynamics present during the colonial era, influencing modern perceptions of Korean cities.
