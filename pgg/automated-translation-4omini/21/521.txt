謝告  
The serialized story 『아츰』 which received favorable reviews in every issue has, due to the circumstances of the author, been repeatedly suspended for a while, causing dissatisfaction among readers. Starting from the next issue, it will resume serialization.  
Editorial room <157>  

**Summary**  

This announcement informs readers about the resumption of the serialized story "아츰," which had been paused temporarily due to the author's circumstances. The article highlights the challenges faced by authors during the colonial period, reflecting on how external factors can disrupt literary production and impact audience engagement. Historians might find interest in the author's difficulties as indicative of the broader struggles encountered by artists under colonial rule, shedding light on the cultural landscape of Korea during this era.
