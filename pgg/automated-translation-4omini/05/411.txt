**Summary**  

This short editorial explains the postponement of the planned special feature column from February to April due to time constraints in the editing process. For historians studying Korean colonial history, such editorial decisions can reflect the pressures faced by publications under colonial rule and the limitations on timely dissemination of information during this period.
