讀者의 投稿를 歡迎함니다  
We welcome readers' contributions to our magazine.  

Any thoughts or hopes regarding our publication, local colors, news from youth associations, school communications, current trends, folk songs, or anything else is most welcome.  

There are no restrictions on current trends and folk songs; however, for other submissions, a manuscript should not exceed ten pages, with a maximum of ten lines of twenty-three characters per line. There is no deadline,  

(Contributed articles with prizes are also welcomed) <89>  

**Summary**

This article expresses an open invitation to readers to submit their writings and thoughts for publication in the magazine, encouraging contributions from various themes like local culture and youth news. It reflects the magazine's desire to engage its audience further during a time when colonial Korea was grappling with cultural identity and expression. Historians may find the openness to public contribution significant as it highlights local voices in an era dominated by colonial narratives.
