논설  
Our Korea is seeing a significant number of graduates from secondary education every year, which is something that everyone welcomes. However, alongside this welcomed development, there is a considerable hardship: the issue of employment. It is a fact that there are no facilities for our Korean people in terms of politics or various other fields to accommodate graduates. Nevertheless, it does not mean that there is nothing to be done. If someone who has studied medicine returns to the countryside with a long-term vision instead of merely thinking about a salary, they could provide rounds of treatment to rural areas or promote hygiene ideas to the general populace. Likewise, if someone has studied law or agriculture, if they go to rural areas and engage directly with the people to enlighten and guide them based on what they have learned, the actual benefits would be immense, though difficult to quantify. Graduates should not be merely lamenting the difficulties of job hunting in the cities. It would be better if they quickly returned to the countryside and became true companions of the farmers. The fundamental issue in all of Korea lies in awakening the farmers.  

**Summary**  

The editorial discusses the growing number of graduates from secondary education in Korea and the pressing employment issue they face, emphasizing the importance of returning to rural areas to help and educate farmers rather than seeking city jobs. It reveals an interesting perspective on societal responsibility during the colonial period, where graduates were encouraged to contribute to rural communities, which reflects the broader implications of colonial policies on education and labor dynamics. Historians might find the call for rural engagement as notable in understanding aspirations for social change amid colonial constraints.

기사형태	논설

논설
						中外日報社 柳光烈
우리 朝鮮에도 해마다 중등이상의 졸업생이 만히 느러가는 것은 누구나 반가워하는 일이다. 그러나 그 반가운 반면에는 큰 고통이 잇스니 즉 취직문제가 그것이다. 우리 朝鮮사람은 정치방면은 물론이고 기타 여러 방면의 시설이 업스닛가 졸업생을 수용할 곳이 업는 것이 사실이다. 그러나 그럿타고 아주 할 일이 엄는 것이 안이다. 다 각각 목전의 월급생활을 할 생각을 말고 영원한 이상을 가지고 농촌으로 도라가서 의학을 공부한 사람이면 농촌으로 순회치료을 한다든지 일반민중에게 위생사상을 선전하는 것도 조켓고 또 법학이면 법학, 농학이면 농학 기타 여러 가지의 자기 배운 그것을 향촌에 가서 민중과 접촉하야 직접으로 깨우처도 주고 지도도 하야준다면 기 실 익이 얼마나 클는지 알수 업슬 것이다. 졸업한 이들은 공연히 도회에 잇서서 취직난의 고통을 부르지질 것이 아니라. 하루라도 속히 농촌으로 도라가서 농민의 참된 동무가 되엿스면 조흘가 한다. 朝鮮의 모든 일은 근본적으로 농민을 각성식히는데 잇다.<23>
