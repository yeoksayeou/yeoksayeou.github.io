Great Prize Contest  
In the February and March issues of this magazine, the article on the fierce competition between the South and North teams (the South team being 阿片窟 (Opium Den) in the February issue, and the North team being the 女學生誘引團 (Female Student Inducement Group) in this issue) has indeed captured the attention of thousands and now announces its success.  
Readers will review the articles from both the South and North teams and provide their evaluations, so please fill out the voting paper located at the bottom of the table of contents.  

The team with the most votes will be declared the winner, and from among all the voters, 100 people will be chosen by lottery to receive equal prizes, with one person being specially selected to receive an even greater reward.  
(The voting deadline is April 10, and the results will be announced in the May issue.) <141>

  
**Summary**  

This article announces a prize contest based on reader evaluation of two competing teams covered in the magazine. It fosters reader engagement and highlights the competition's popularity, offering incentives through prizes. For historians examining the colonial period in Korea, this example of reader involvement through entertainment and competition reflects societal trends and the media's role in shaping public opinion during a time of turmoil.
