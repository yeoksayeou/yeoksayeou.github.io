기사형태	잡저 (Essays)  

One cannot neglect the state of the finances of a country and the conditions of its people. Even though there are considerable investments in hospitals in Korea, with magnificent buildings and excellent facilities that could attract worldwide attention, our economic decline does not allow for it. Moreover, there are many who criticize that Koreans only go to hospitals when they are about to die. This is indeed a lamentable statement. Who does not cherish their own life? But in this world, money determines whether one can receive medicine. Thus, the costs of medicine, hospitalization fees, and surgery fees are all burdensome, leading to a situation where the members of the medical community must carefully measure their decisions against the realities faced by the populace. Nowadays, hospital managers must thoughtfully consider the actual living conditions of our people while striving for content-rich practices. <147>

  
**Summary**  

The article discusses the challenges faced by hospitals in Korea during the colonial period, emphasizing the disconnect between the lavish facilities and the harsh economic realities of the Korean populace. It critiques societal attitudes toward healthcare, noting that patients often delay seeking help until they are severely ill due to financial constraints. This piece highlights the struggles of Koreans under colonial rule, offering insight into the social determinants of health during this era and revealing the complexities of healthcare accessibility, shedding light on the broader implications of colonial influence on local healthcare systems.

一人一話
						醫師 崔永在
내 남 할 것 업시 그 나라의 시세와 그 나라 사람의 뎡도를 무시하고는 될 수 업슬 줄 암니다. 지금 조선 안에 잇는 병원 즁에 막대한 자금을 들이여 굉장한 건물과 훌늉한 설비로써 세상의 이목을 놀내일만한 데가 적지 안을 것임니다만은 나날이 몰락 되여가는 우리의 경제가 허락치 못하는 데야 엇지 함니까. 더구나 조선 사람들은 아조 죽게 되어야 병원을 차저 온다고 비난하는 사람이 만슴니다. 참으로 긔맥힌 말입니다. 뉘가 제 목숨 앗가운 줄 몰으겟슴니까만은 돈 업고는 약도 먹을 수가 업게 된 세상이구려. 그래서 약갑이 얼마니. 입원료가 얼마니, 수술료가 얼마니, 긔록한 의사회의 협뎡도 반드시 상대편의 쳐디를 도라 본 뒤의 일일줄로 생각함니다. 엇잿던 오늘날 우리 병원 경영자도 우리 민중 생활의 실제 문뎨데를 신중이 생각하는 동시에 자톄의 내용 충실에 힘써야 할 생임니다. <147>
