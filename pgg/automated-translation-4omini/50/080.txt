기사제목	큰일날 計量器 (A Serious Measuring Device)  
기사형태	잡저 (Magazine Article)  

A new measuring device has recently been invented in France. It is a device that determines how truthful a husband is to his wife and how faithful a wife is to her husband. If this device is secretly installed under the bed, it is said that if the husband goes out and behaves in a certain way, the wife will not be able to engage in unfaithful activities while he is away. When both the husband and wife are on the bed, the weight will be the husband + wife. However, if the wife is alone on the bed, the husband's weight will naturally be subtracted from the total. If the wife invites another man during this time, the measuring device will show a significant deviation.

Moreover, what is even more sophisticated is that this measuring device is equipped with something akin to a seismometer (地震計), which indicates the vibrations of the bed. Therefore, it will be different when an inanimate object is placed on the bed and when a person is on it and moving about. Hence, whether the wife or husband is out, inviting someone else will not be possible. This is the characteristic of this measuring device. The actions of people in that situation are truly embarrassing. Can this device accurately reveal the infidelities of a husband or wife? <16>  

**Summary**  

The article describes a fictional measuring device invented in France that aims to track the fidelity of spouses through weight measurements and bed vibrations. Its humorous representation of surveillance in marriage highlights societal concerns about trust and infidelity in relationships during the early 20th century. This reflects the broader cultural anxieties of the time, possibly linked to the changing dynamics of gender roles and societal values in Korea under colonial rule, providing insight into early modern marriage concepts and societal expectations.

큰일날 計量器
최근에 불란서에서는 큰일날 게량기가 발명되엿다. 그것은 두 내외간에 남편이면 안해 안해면남편이 얼마나 진실한가를 칙정하는 기게다. 이 기게를 몰래 침대미테 장치해두면-가령 남편이 이러케 해노코 어데를 나간다면 안해는 남편업는새 부정한 일을 못하게 된다는 것이다. 남편과 안해가 동시에 침대우에 잇슬경우면 그 중량이 남편+안해=이러케 될 것이나 안해혼자만 잇다면 둘의 중량에서 남편의 중량이 당연이 빠질 것이다. 그런줄도 모르고 안해가 딴 남자를 끄러드리는 때는 게량기에 지침이 사뭇 도라갈 것이다. 그뿐 아니라 더욱 정교한 것은 이 게량기 우에 지진게(地震計)와 가튼 것이 잇서서 침대의 진동을 표시하게까지 되여 잇다. 그러므로 움직이지 안는 물체를 침대우에 올려노을 때와 사람이 올러잇서서 부스럭거릴때와는 판이 다를 것이다. 그럼으로 안해든지 남편이든지 한편이 외출한 동안에 딴 사람을 끄러드릴수가 업슬 것이다.-이것이 이 게량기의 특색인 것이다. 코큰놈들의 하는 짓이란 이러케 쑥스럽다. 과연 이 기게로 남자나 안해의 부정을 정밀히 알 수 잇슬까?  <16>
