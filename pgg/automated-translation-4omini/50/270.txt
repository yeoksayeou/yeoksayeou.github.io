기사형태	세태비평 (Social Critique)  

In the few months since its revival, the Jongang Ilbo (中央日報) once again faced suspension due to management difficulties.

The names of its publication have varied, as it changed from Sidai Ilbo to Joonguk Ilbo and then back to Jongang Ilbo, but the issue of management has remained consistent, indicating that the problem lies not in the title itself, but in the competence and financial issues of the management. It would be better if the name could just stay the same regardless of who manages it.

A senior official of the Chosun Ilbo has been imprisoned due to an incident, and a new management team has been organized. However, the new president, Yu Jin-tae (兪鎮泰), has recently been accused of embezzlement due to the Min Byeong-ju (閔丙疇) affair shortly after his appointment.

Although the outcome of that case is uncertain, it seems that the position of president at Chosun Ilbo is not a very pleasant one.

It could be said that former president Shin Seok-u (申錫雨) has not been able to see the light of day for a long time; recently, perhaps out of sheer boredom, he has reportedly been active at the management of Kyongsu Automobile Company (京水自動車會社).

While it is regrettable to lose the cushy position of a newspaper president and take up residence in a hard-floored automotive company, perhaps it is some consolation to be called a “teacher” or “mister” by the circle of genial gentlemen.

A master of the newspaper industry, Min Tae-won (牛步 閔泰瑗), was said to have taken a trip to Manchuria recently, and upon hearing reports, it appears he did not go merely for leisure but to discuss the establishment of a new newspaper. If that plan progresses well, it is expected to be realized soon.

However, the birth of that newspaper has caused ripples in the newspaper industry in Korea, leading to several personnel changes at various newspapers, and important figures at other newspapers are also said to be agitated. 

**Summary**

The article reflects on the turbulent state of the newspaper industry in Korea during the 1930s, highlighting management issues, leadership changes, and the precarious position of media outlets under colonial circumstances. It is particularly revealing of how the political and financial instability of the time impacted not only the success of newspapers but also individual careers within the industry. Historians might find the commentary on management problems and personal stories of leadership shifts a valuable insight into the broader social and political struggles faced by Koreans under Japanese colonial rule.

號外의 號外
속간 당시부터 말성만턴 중앙일보(中央日報)는 불과 몃달에 경영곤난으로 또다시 정간이 되엿다.

시대일보를 중외일보로 중외일보를 또다시 중앙일보로 몃번식 명의를 곳첫스나 경영곤난은 맛찬가지인즉 결국 문제는 명의란 그것보다 경영자의 인물문제 또는 재정문제인즉 이 압흐로는 누가 다시 경영하던지 제발 명의만은 그대로 두엇스면 조흘 것 갓다.

조선일보의 중요간부가 불상사로 감금이 되고 새간부가 조직되더니 새사장 유진태(兪鎭泰)씨는 취임한지 미구에 민병주(閔丙疇)사건으로 사긔횡령(詐欺橫領)의 고소를 당하얏다.

그 사건이 엇지 귀결될지 알 수는 업거니와 조선일보의 사장 자리도 그리 조흔 자리는 못되는 모양

조선일보 사장 말이 낫스니 말이지 전사장 신석우(申錫雨)씨는 한참동안 연명을 볼 수 업더니 요즘에는 너무 심심한 탓인지 그의 경영하는 경수자동차회사(京水自動車會社)로 각금 나온다고 한다.

푹신푹신한 우단방석의 신문사장 자리를 빼앗기고 딱딱한 장판바닥의 자동차회사 방구석 차지를 하게된 것은 섭섭한 일이지만은 오라이 스텁을 부르는 어엽분 뻐쓰껄들의 선생님 령감님 소리에 적이나 위안될 듯 하다.
