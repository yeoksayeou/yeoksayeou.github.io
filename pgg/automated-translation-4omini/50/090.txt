기사제목	新婦一人에 三新郞登場 (A New Bride with Three Suitors)  
기사형태	잡저 (Miscellaneous)  

A new bride has attracted three suitors. A cute son’s daughter, who unknowingly got engaged to a man, has her father thinking about her future and choosing a suitable son-in-law while her mother also picks her favorite. Choosing a son-in-law isn’t a problem, but how did it happen that an engagement occurred among them without their knowledge, making it so there must be three suitors for one bride? 

On the day of the wedding, without being able to get together with the son (age 14) chosen by the father, there arose an unexpected turmoil at the wedding invitation because the son that the mother selected had already gotten engaged to the bride herself. The three fiancés found themselves in the middle of this comic situation, arguing, “I am the fiancé; who are you guys?” Eventually, the three suitors, at a loss, could only lick their lips in dismay while blinking their eyes. This was a nonsensical drama that unfolded in Yeongheung (永興).<16>  

**Summary**  

This article presents a humorously chaotic situation where one bride finds herself engaged to three different suitors, each oblivious to the others. The amusing conflict arises during the wedding preparations, reflecting on the misunderstandings and complexities surrounding marriage customs during that era. The article's portrayal of familial expectations and the young age of the suitors adds a layer of depth, indicating societal norms and gender roles in a colonized Korea.

新婦一人에 三新郞登場
귀여운 자긔의 딸이 엇던 남자와 정이 드러 자기네끼리 약혼한 줄도 모르고 아버지는 딸의 장래를 생각하야 사위될 감을 고르고 어머니는 자기의 마음에 드는 사위를 고르고 햇다. 사위고르는 것쯤은 문제가 업겟지만 서로모르게 약혼을 덜컥해노앗스니 신랑이 셋이 될박게!
아버지가 정해준 신랑(14세)과 엇절 수 업시 결혼하게 되는 날 초래청에는 이로 말미암아 뜻도 아니한 분란이 생겻스니 즉 어머니가 정해노은 신랑 신부자신이 약혼해 노은 신랑 세 신랑이 신부하나를 가운데 노코
「내가 신랑인데 댁들은 웬놈들이냐」하고 서로들 야-단이엇다. 급기야 세 신랑은 어이가 업서 입맛만 쩍쩍 다시고 눈만 꿈벅꿈벅 이것이 영흥(永興)에서 생긴 일막의 넌쎈스극이다.<16>
