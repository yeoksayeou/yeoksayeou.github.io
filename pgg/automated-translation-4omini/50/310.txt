발행일	1932년 06월 01일  
기사제목	말 잘쓰는 며누리 (A Daughter-in-law Who Uses Words Well)  
필자	開豐 玄錫夏  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

말 잘쓰는 며누리  
시어머니 「얘야 지금부터는 겻말을 쓰지 말어라」  

며누리 「엽말이 겻말이요」  

시어머니는 골이나서  
「요년 것말 쓰지 말내도 또 겻말쓰냐?」  
하고 이마를 딱 따렷습니다.  

며누리 「차포오졸은 다 빼고 면상부터 치는구료」  

시어머니 「... ...」<45>  

**Summary**  

This article presents a humorous exchange between a mother-in-law and her daughter-in-law centered around language and its usage. The mother-in-law disapproves of her daughter-in-law's speech, insisting that she avoid using certain words. The daughter-in-law cleverly responds to each critique, showcasing her wit. This interaction reveals the dynamics of familial relationships during the period, highlighting cultural attitudes towards language, authority, and rebellion within the household. Historians may find the underlying social commentary significant, reflecting the tensions experienced in Korean society under colonial rule, where language can signify power and identity.

**Glossary**

요년	요년	Older term for 'young woman,' often used in a derogatory way.
