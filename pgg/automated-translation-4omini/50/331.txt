기사제목	◇盲人의 安全通行 (Safe Passage for the Blind)  
필자	金奎澤 (Kim Kyu-jak)  
기사형태	문예기타 (Literary Miscellaneous)  

◇盲人의 安全通行  
교통사고가 빈번한이 장님의 대로통의묘책  
「여붑시오 무슨 구루마요 어데지가십니」  

「우편차(郵便車)요」  

「네 네그럿습니 내집이게동××번지니 기왕이면 좀 바라다주시겟습니!」  

  

**Summary**  

The article discusses the safety and navigation challenges faced by blind individuals in urban traffic, highlighting a fictional dialogue between a blind person and a mail cart driver. It portrays the difficulties of securing safe passage regardless of the prevalent risks of traffic accidents. This insight into the struggles of disabled individuals during a time of rapid urban development reveals the broader societal challenges in colonial Korea, where infrastructural inadequacies and social neglect were compounded by the daily realities of colonial rule.
