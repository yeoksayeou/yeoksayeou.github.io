기사제목	漫畵 아가야 우서라! (Cartoon: Baby, Laugh!)  
필자	熊超  
기사형태	만화 (Cartoon)  

漫畵 아가야 우서라!  
熊超  
1. It’s not that I’ve lost my mind. I’m putting aside my pride and making every effort to amuse my beloved child.  

Baby, laugh! Ching ching ching chirra ching ching... Look at this guy, still not laughing!  

2. Baby, laugh! This is scary, isn’t it?  

A-huh, look at this little guy, still not laughing!  

3. Baby, would you please laugh a bit? Look at this, the sheep would die, right? Still not laughing!  

4. Baby, please laugh a bit. Now, look at this, isn’t it funny? Could you laugh a bit?  

5. What’s wrong with this little guy? What a naughty boy! Where is he trying to go?  

6. Hey, look! He is recognizing his mother. Hey, he laughed. Now he’s laughing, ha ha ha, now he’s laughing. Ha ha ha...<21>  

**Summary**  

The article presents a whimsical and humorous dialogue as a parent attempts various antics to make their baby laugh, showcasing the tender dynamics of parenting. This cartoon captures both the struggles and joys in the relationship between a parent and child, reflecting societal expectations placed on parenthood during the colonial era. Historians could find the humorous take on domestic life particularly interesting, as it sheds light on cultural behaviors regarding parenting in Korea during this time.
