기사제목	郵票뒷等에 7045字를 써 (Writing 7045 Characters on the Back of a Postage Stamp)  
저자	불명  
기사형태	잡저 (Magazine Article)  

A certain prisoner in a French prison wrote 7045 characters on the back of a postage stamp. To what extent did he write that it is barely visible without a magnifying glass? However, this was in competition with another German prisoner who wrote 6000 characters on a similar postage stamp, and this is likely to become a world record. <20>  

**Summary**  

The article discusses an extraordinary feat by a French prisoner who managed to write 7045 characters on the back of a postage stamp, highlighting a competition with a German prisoner who wrote 6000 characters. This record showcases human ingenuity and endurance under confined circumstances, which reflects on human creativity and the spirit of competition even in dire situations. Historians might find interest in how such acts of creativity were manifestations of resilience among prisoners during the political oppression of the time.

郵票뒷등에 7045字를 써
불란서 어떤 감옥죄수는 우표뒷등에다 7045자의 글ㅅ자를 썻다. 얼마나 적게 썻기에 그럴는지 확대경 아니면 좀처럼 못볼 것이다. 그런데 이것은 독일의 어떤 죄수가 역시 그만한 우표에 6천자를 쓴 일이 잇는데 경쟁적으로 한 것인데 이것이 아마 세게적긔록이 될 것이다. <20>
