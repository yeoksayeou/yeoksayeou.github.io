The Cause of Divorce is a Large Mass Inside the Belly

The human body, though small in size—approximately six zhang in length and two zhang in width—consists of intricate tissues, and as such, there are countless diseases that can afflict it. A grotesque and alarming mass weighing twelve and a half kun, about the size of a large pumpkin, was excised from a woman's abdomen, which has become a subject of amazement in Wonsan. The owner of this mass is a woman named Kang, from Insok-ri in Deukgi-myeon, Yeongheung-gun, currently fifty-five years old. She began experiencing swelling in her abdomen since the age of fifteen, and at one point, she became the subject of dubious rumors. After marrying, she faced divorce due to her illness, leading to an increasingly solitary and lonely life, while suffering from her ailment for over twenty years filled with tears and sighs. Eventually, the mass within her belly grew to such an extent that she could no longer move freely, and she was brought to Wonsan Guse Hospital. Upon hearing her heartfelt moans, the hospital director and Dr. Seo Byeong-su felt compassion and admitted her. After examination, she was diagnosed with a rhabdomyosarcoma, and on the first of the month, an abdominal surgery was performed, resulting in the removal of a massive meat lump weighing twelve and a half kun. In medical circles, this is referred to as a large mass of rhabdomyosarcoma, which is rarely seen in practice. 

**Summary** 

This article recounts the story of a woman suffering from a large mass in her abdomen for over twenty years, highlighting her struggles with illness and the eventual medical procedure that removed the mass. The tale reveals societal attitudes towards illness and divorce in 1930s Korea, emphasizing the stigma that women faced, particularly with health issues that affected their marital status. Historians might find the intersection of medical practices and societal norms during the colonial period noteworthy, reflecting on how health and gender played critical roles in personal lives during this time.

소박의 原因이 腹中의 大肉塊
사람의 몸이 기리로는 엿섯자 널피로는 두자 네치에 지나지 못하는 적은 몸동어리나마 조직이 복잡한이만큼 이루 세일수업는 수만흔 병이 잇는 것이다. 괴상하고도 끔직한 중량이 열두근반이나 되는 크다란 호박덩어리만한 혹을 배가온대에서 빼여내엿다함이 원산에서는 이약이거리가 되여 잇다. 그 비여낸 혹의 주인공은 영흥군 덕기면 인석리(永興郡 德岐面 仁石里)에 사는 녀자 강씨로 지금 나히는 설흔다섯살인데 처녀시대인 열다섯때부터 배가 점점 불너오기 시작하야 한때는 수상한 소문이 수군수군하엿스나 때마처 시집을 갓다가 그 병으로 말미암아 고만 남편에게 소박을 맛고 쓸쓸하고 외로움에다가 날날히 더하야 가는 병세로 이십여년이라는 기다란 세월을 눈물과 한숨으로 보내다가 그 혹은 배속에서 점점 커서 운신조차 마음대로 못할 지경에 일으러서 멀니 원산구세병원으로 차저와서 눈물석긴 하소연에 동병원장과 의사 서병수(徐秉守)씨가 불상이 역여서 입원식힌 후 진찰한 결과에 근육종(筋肉腫)으로 판평이 되여서 지난 일일에 복부수술을 하고 열두근 반이 되는 크다란 고기덩어리를 비여내엿다는데 의학게에서 일즉이 물어본 일이 업는 근육종의 대육괴(筋肉腫의 大肉塊)라고 한다.<11>
