Prize Question  
What is the answer to "선사습무조해니살람은 얼야겟!"? This is a phrase that jumbles the word "말하마듸" (malhamade) and if you rearrange it correctly, it forms a prize question. Please write your answer in five characters or less and send it in.  

Prizes  
Special Prize: 1 person, Artistic Clock  
Secondary Prize: 99 persons, Magazine Issue  

Regulations  
◇ Please write your answer on a postcard-sized paper and clearly write your name and address.  
◇ An issue of the magazine must be cut out and attached (the one from this month's issue).  
◇ Write "Gyeongseong Gaepiksha Byeolgeun-gon Prize World" on the envelope.  
◇ Write "Manuscript Inside" on the envelope and attach a 2-jeon stamp to the corner of the envelope.  
◇ If possible, send multiple entries from one person in a single envelope for convenience and to save on postage. However, make sure that for each entry, a separate copy of the magazine must be attached.

**Summary**  

This article presents a prize question for readers of the magazine and recounts the responses received for a previous query regarding admired figures among the Korean populace, revealing a large response rate of over 75,000 participants. The article highlights the interest and engagement of the population in cultural figures during that time, albeit unable to announce specific names due to the variety of responses. This reflects a moment in Korean colonial history where public opinion and admiration were tracked and possibly influenced by the prevailing societal conditions under colonial rule.

◇되도록한봉투안에 여러사람의 것을 너어보내시면 피차간에 편리하고 우표갑을 절약할수잇습니다. 그러나 반듯이 한사람대답에하나씩 독자증을 오혀부쳐됩니다.
