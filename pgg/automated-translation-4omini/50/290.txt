필자	金奎澤 (Kim Gyu-jak)  
기사형태	만화 (Cartoon)  

처녀지 개척  
사랑하는 남녀는 그들의 안전한 밀회(密會)를 위하야 언제던지 처녀지를 개척한다. 그곳에서 녀자는 다시 사랑하는 남자로 인하야 자기의 처녀지를 개척당하는 수도 잇다.<39>  

**Summary**  

The article discusses the metaphorical significance of "opening virgin land" in the context of romantic encounters, illustrating how lovers establish secret places for their meetings. The woman in the relationship is portrayed as an object of conquest, implying that men reclaim her "virgin land" as a symbol of love. This perspective raises intriguing discussions about gender dynamics and romantic relationships during the colonial period in Korea, where societal constraints often dictated personal interactions. Historians might find the sexual metaphor particularly revealing as it captures the intersection of traditional values and modern influences under colonial rule.
