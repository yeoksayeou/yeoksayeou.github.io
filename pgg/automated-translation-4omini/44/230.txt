K traveled to Russia for a long time and arrived in Uradio at some point during winter. He had heard from rumors about the famous bathhouses in Uradio, and it was his friend S, who had been living there for a long time, who suggested to K to visit the well-known bathhouse. K, who originally intended just to try it out, did not hesitate and decided to go with S.

When the lights of the street came on in Uradio’s harbor, K set out in a car driven by S from their hotel. 

“What kind of bathhouse is this you speak of?” K asked S during the ride in the car, which was moving slowly. 

“Just like an ordinary bathhouse!” 

“What? An ordinary bathhouse?” 

“Of course! What’s wrong with that?” 

“I thought there would be something special!” 

“Don't worry! There will be something special to see. If you desire, a woman can join us!” 

“Okay!” 

While the two conversed, the car made several turns before stopping at a certain venue in the outskirts. The two got out of the car and entered a narrow, dimly lit street where a sign read “Moscow-style Bath (모스코-프쓰ㅡ키! 바아니아-)”. 

K and S, with excited expressions, seemed to be whispering something at the entrance. 

“By the way, do you prefer a young one or one around thirty?” S asked K with a casual tone. 

“I guess I prefer someone young!” 

“Alright!” 

The two opened the door and entered. At the entrance stood a man dressed in a rubashka, around forty years old. 

“I’ll just step inside for a moment, so please wait here!” 

“Okay,” K responded as S went into the bathhouse. K found himself waiting in a small reception-like room when the man brought a strange-looking beverage while mumbling something in Russian. 

Before long, S emerged from inside, wearing a smile. 

“Guess what? There’s quite a stunning beauty here! Shall we take a look?” 

At that moment, a dancer appeared and asked them to step inside. When K paid the fee requested, which was thirty rubles (approximately twenty won), they were directed to different rooms. 

The first room they were shown was small, accommodating about two people, with just a sofa, a round table, two chairs, and a mirror. K took off his suit and prepared to take a bath. 

He then opened the next door. 

That place was a bath decorated entirely in marble, a spacious room reminiscent of other bathhouses. It had bath towels, soap, and perfume available. K sank into the filled marble bath and felt a wave of contentment. 

“I wonder how the woman is doing?” he mused aloud. 

Just then, he heard the sound of someone opening the door. 

Someone was entering! 

K tightened up a bit. The door opened slightly, and a twenty-two-year-old with alabaster skin and perfect features stepped in. 

K nodded briefly, and she approached him, displaying her alluring curves without holding back. 

Initially, K asked her about her past, but she barely entered the bath and lay on the marble tub, resembling a mythical heavenly maiden as described in old tales. The beautiful curves sparkled softly on the marble surface. 

“Aren’t you cold lying there?” K asked. 

“No,” she replied calmly. She even lay back on the marble floor to prove she wasn’t cold. 

“Oh...?” 

K got out of the tub and tried lying down as she did. Surprisingly, the marble was warm. 

“Haha!” K chuckled. 

She encouraged K with “Please lie down.” 

The man complied and reclined, feeling a bit nervous.

본래부터 한번 시험한다고 생각을 하고 잇든 차이라 서슴지 안코 K는 S에게 가기로 약속햇다.
K는 가두의 등불이 우라듸오 항구에 켜질 때 S의 안내로 호텔에서 자동차를 몰앗다.
「그런데 말야. 자네가 말하는 그 목욕탕이란 대체 어떤 것인가!」

「뭐? 보통 목간이라구?」
「그럼! 그런데 외 무엇이 잘 안됫나?」
「잘만 되고 말구. 이사람! 나는 또 특별한 무엇이나 잇는 줄 알앗드니.」
「념녀말게! 특별한 것을 보여 줄 터이니. 자네가 원한다면 녀자가 함께 달닌다네!」
「흐-ㅇ!」
두사람은 이러케 대화를 하는 동안에 자동차는 몃 번이나 거리ㅅ모퉁이를 돌아들어서 어떠한 시외거리에서 스톱하엿다. 거긔서 자동차를 내린 두 사람은 좁고 침침한 한모퉁이 길가를 거러 들어갓다. 「모스코-프쓰ㅡ키! 바아니아-」라고 써잇다. 바아니아-란 말은 러시말로 모스근-식 욕탕이란 의미이엿다.

K와 S는 흥분된 상으로 입구에서 무엇인가를 속살거리고 잇섯다.
「한데 자네는 절문 것이 조흔가? 또는 한 삼십가량 된 것이 조흔가?」
S는 손씨익은 말시로 K에게 물엇다.
「글세! 나는 나희는 엣젯든 비교적 육감적인 것이 조흔데!」
「오-라이!」
두사람은 쑤-ㄱ문을 열고 들어섯다. 입구에는 루바시카를 입은 약 사십된 남자가 서 잇섯다.
「내가 잠간 안에 갓다 올 터이니 거긔서 잠시동안 기다려주오!」
「오-케이」
S는 K를 남겨노코 그대로 안으로 들어가 버렷다. K는 녑헤 잇는 응접실 비슷한 조고만 방에서 멍한히 S를 기다리고 잇는 동안에 루바시카 입은 남자가 이상한차-비슷한 것을 가저다 부어주면서 무엇이라고 러시아말로 중얼거렷다.
얼마 안하여 S는 안에서부터 미소를 띄우면서 나왓다.

그때에 뽀-이가 나와서 몬저 회게를 츠러 달나고 청하는 것이엿다. 얼마인가를 무른즉 30루-블(약 이십원)이라고 뽀-이는 대답햇다.

K는 그 방에서 양복을 벗고 몬저 목욕을 할 작정으로 준비를 햇다.
