Editorial Note  

This year marks the halfway point of the year. Perhaps it is because people's endeavors do not align with their intentions that I have become acutely aware of how fast time flies. In this season of lush greenery, I humbly wish for the prosperity of our readers and begin with an apology.  

Since last autumn, after our launch was prohibited, we have tried to contribute to society with our humble efforts, yet for some reason, we seem to be incapable of achieving our goals. The environment in Korea does not seem to accommodate us as we had hoped, and it is indeed a troublesome situation. 

Everyone—how long have you been waiting for the May issue? I can imagine that you have been counting the days and anxiously waiting, from early May until the end of June—wondering whether it would ever come out. "Ah—maybe 별건곤 is done for now. But it shouldn't end like this," you might have felt discouraged or perplexed. Indeed, it was a troubling time for us. Every day, our hearts felt heavy as we received inquiries from you via phone, letters, and telegrams.

Those who are aware through the newspapers will certainly know. Currently, our editorial team has been living in a different world due to matters that do not align with our dignity or reputation, resulting in either a 20-day or 30-day absence from the publication. The hardest worker among us, Mr. Shin, is currently hospitalized and has been bedridden for two months due to his illness. Such difficulties have led to these delays, but if we are unable to help but apologize, is it solely our fault? We only ask for your forgiveness. 

Also, during the manuscript review process, deletions were made, and additional editorial adjustments were completed, so we have just now been able to present to you the old layout. Regarding the contents, they are not what you might expect from a political and current events magazine; while there is little to discuss on that front, we have hastily gathered a variety of topics in the realms of entertainment and practical interest, producing the April issue under duress and inadequacy. Everything has been postponed to the next month's issue, and we hope for your understanding.

Starting from the next issue, provided there are no unforeseen winds or rains, you can trust that we will publish daily. Moreover, we guarantee that we will present a truly innovative editorial style in the upcoming edition, which will feature unique articles from 별건곤 that you could never imagine, ensuring that the expectations of our readers will not be disappointed. Coincidentally, this marks the 8th anniversary of our founding. The groundbreaking new plans announced in the 별건곤 will be accompanied by content in the August issue that is sure to satisfy you.

The worldwide attention-drawing topic of "Silver Flies" that has continued to be written in 별건곤 will unfortunately be unable to proceed for the time being. This is dictated from above, so please understand that this is not our preference. There is no need for lengthy explanations; we too feel discomfort about this situation for the time being. However, please look forward to the great reading material that will replace it in the August issue.  

-(Spring)- <168>  

**Summary**  

The editorial note reflects on the struggles faced by the publication after being prohibited earlier in the year, leading to delays in issues and difficulties within the editorial team, including illness among key staff. It serves as both an apology and a promise to improve future publications by introducing innovative content and addressing reader concerns. The observations on the hardships faced by the publication team provide insight into the challenges of media under colonial rule, highlighting the tensions between creativity, societal contribution, and external pressures that historians may find valuable for understanding the era's publishing landscape.

編輯後言
今年도 발서 절반이나 갓슴니다. 사람의 일이 뜻과 가티 되지 안어 그런지 새삼스럽게 세월이 빠른 것을 늣기게 됨니다. 綠陰이 우거진 이 때에 삼가 讀者諸位의 萬福을 祝하며 爲先 미안한 말슴 한 마디를 하게 되얏슴니다.
昨秋開闢이 禁止된 후 우리는 微力이나마 잇는 힘을 다하야 社會奉貢에 盡悴하려 하얏스나 우리의 힘이 부족하야 그런지 조선의 환경이 우리를 容納하지 안는지 뜻한 대로 도저히 안이 됩니다. 우리에게는 公私間 旋風과 暴雨가 엇지면 그다지도 만슴닛가 조선놈의 例套의 말이 안이라 實로 난처한 일이 만엇슴니다.
여러분-本誌 5월호를 얼마나 기다렷슴닛가 오늘이나 날가 내일이나 날가 손 곱아 기다리는 것이 5월 초순으로 6월 하순까지 繼續 하시지 안엇슴닛가 그러다가 終是 안이 나오닛가 「아-別乾坤도 이제 그만인가 보다 그래도 그럴수는 업는데-하고 낙심도 하고 의아도 하섯슬 것임니다. 과연 그리섯슬 것임니다. 날마다 궁금히 물어 주시는 전화와 편지와 또 전보를 밧을 때 우리의 마음은 얼마나 압핫겟슴닛가
신문지를 통하야 아시는 이는 아섯겟슴니다. 這間 우리 編輯局員一同은 소위 名譽毁損이니 뭐니 하는 일 답지 안은 일로 혹은 20일 혹은 30일 社를 뷔다십히 하고 別世界 생활을 하얏슴니다. 그리고 가장 부즈런한 일꾼인 申君은 그 말는 몸이나마 지탱하지 못하고 병원에 입원하야 두 달재나 누어 잇슴니다. 이러한 곤란으로 이럿케 느젓슴니다요 만일에 뒤밧칠 힘이 업는 고들로서 사과니 뭐니 할 것이 뭐 잇겟슴닛가 만은 이것이 엇지 우리만 못생긴 탓이겟슴닛가. 寬恕를 바랄 뿐임니다.
또한 原稿檢閱 중에 削除가 만어 세 번재 追加編輯을 해가지고 이제야 여러분을 차저 舊面을 닛게 되얏슴니다. 내용에 잇서서는 原來 政治時事誌가 안이닛가 그 방면은 말도 할 것 업고 趣味와 實益方面은 골고루 모아 노려든 것이 疲困한 힘으로 급하게 모라친 것이라 억지로 발행한 4월치와 가치 不備한 것이 만슴니다. 모든 것을 來月號로 미루엇사오니 諒察하심이 잇기 바람니다.
次號 부터는 不時의 風雨만 업스면 기억코 一日 發行을 할 것은 물론이요 내용에 잇서서 嶄新한 編輯術로 실로 想像不到의 「別乾坤」지 獨特의 特種記事를 公開하야 讀者諸氏의 企待에 어그러지지 안켓슴을 장담해 둠니다. 맛츰 開闢社 創立8週年 긔렴이라 別乾坤誌 上에 발표될 破天의 新計劃과 함께 8월호 내용은 반듯이 여러분께 滿足을 드릴 것도 밋고 잇슴니다.
開闢新女性으로 別乾坤에 까지 繼續해 쓰든 天下의 注目거리『銀파리』는 당분간 못싯게 됨니다. 저긔서 그리하라는 것이니 自意가 아닌 것만 아러 주십시요. 말을 길게 할 필요는 업고 우리로도 당분간 일망정 섭섭함니다. 그러나 그에 代할 조흔 讀物이 8월호에 잇슬 것을 기다려 주십시요.
-(春)- ＜168＞
