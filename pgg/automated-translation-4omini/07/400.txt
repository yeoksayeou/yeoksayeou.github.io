필자	申瑩澈  
기사형태	회고·수기 (Memoir)  

The time when I was in elementary school, wearing a gold-rimmed hat and carrying a black knife, I was a bit more chubby than the skinny man I am now. At that time, I would drink occasionally and act a bit boorishly. So, just think about it. How could a teacher be involved in something so shameful? But luckily, I avoided the greatest embarrassment.

One of the students invited the teachers to his home for some sort of banquet or ceremony. So, along with four or five colleagues, I visited the student's house. The teachers were treated to an extravagant spread of food and drink. In that circumstance, without realizing it, I drank as much as I was given. We ate and drank from around 3 PM until long into the night, consuming who knows how much.

Eventually, completely intoxicated, I started singing and dancing, losing myself in the moment until the night grew late. When I came to my senses, the other students had all returned home safely to their own places, whereas I, all alone, got lost in the dark of night, stumbling through the fields and finding myself collapsing into a rice paddy, unconscious. I lost track of everything, and when I finally realized what was happening, I found myself rolling in the mud at the bottom of the paddy. How embarrassing it was! Having collected my thoughts, I found my body completely covered in mud, but I had no idea where my hat and shoes had ended up. I wondered if anyone had seen me? How truly shameful this was.

So I washed off some of the mud in clear water and, before anyone could see me, hurried towards the school. Was it because of the alcohol that my throat felt so parched? Therefore, despite my embarrassment, I picked mulberries and ate them like a child. After that, I hesitated to go directly to school, so I went to the home of a classmate and quietly explained my situation while sneaking into the house to wear his clothes and lay down for a nap.

Ah! Just look at this. During this time, the teachers who had left were searching for me all night, roaming the hills and fields, but they couldn't find me. Out of concern, the students and the parents had gathered to search for me as well. And yet they couldn't find me; as a last resort, they decided to report me missing to the local police as a search process. In the meantime, my classmate had informed the others, and they all came rushing to my location. How embarrassed I was! What would this mean for a teacher's dignity? If they had submitted a missing persons report, what might have happened?

Since that incident, I have taken great care with alcohol. 

**Summary**

This article is a humorous recounting by Shin Yeong-cheol of an embarrassing incident from his youth involving excessive drinking at a student's home. He narrates the events leading up to him getting lost after a night of revelry, culminating in a misadventure that caused concern among his peers and teachers. The article highlights the impact of social expectations on educators during the early colonial period in Korea, showcasing the tension between personal conduct and societal roles, which historians might find reflective of the broader challenges faced by individuals in a colonized society.

금테 모자쓰고 烏銅칼 차고 보통학교 훈도 노릇을 하든 때엿소. 내가 지금은 이럿케 빼빼 마른 골 샌님 갓지만 그 때는 제법 술잔이나 마시고 작탄태나 하든 때엿소. 자- 이것 보시오. 선생님으로 이런 창피가 어대 잇겟소. 그러나 요행 대창피는 면햇지요.
엇던 학생 집에서 무슨 잔채인가 제사인가 잇다고 선생들을 쳥해 갑듸다. 그래 同伴 四五人과 갓치 그 학생의 집을 갓구려. 선생들이라고 술이며 떡이며 아주 굉장히 대접합듸다. 이 바람에 멋도 모르고 조와라고 술을 주는 대로 바다 생켯구려. 오후 3시쯤해 가서 밤이 들도록 먹엇스니 얼마나 먹엇겟소.
곤두래 맨두래 취해 가지고 춤을 추며 노래를 하며 한참이나 질알발광을 하다가 밤이 깁흔 뒤에야 밋친 놈 갓치 넘어지며 잇츨빗츨 도라 오게 되얏소. 그런대 엇더케된 셈인지 다른 동무들은 다 각각 正路를 차저 제 숙소로 도라 갓는데 나 혼자뿐 橫路로 드러 캄캄한 밤즁에 산 덤벙 물 덤벙 이리저리 헤매이다가 그만 氣盡脈盡하야 논뚝에 걸녀 넘어저서 잠이 드럿소 구려 정신 모르고 자든 바람에 그만 논바닥 즌탕 가운데 떠러지고 마럿소. 무엇이 선뜻하기에 정신을 차려 보니 논바닥 감탕 속에서 딍굴고 잇겟지요. 엇지도 창피하든지오. 그래 정신을 다시금 차려가지고 이러서 보니 읜몸이 전혀 감탕 박아지를 뒤집어 썻는데 모자며 구두는 어데가 떠러젓는지요 누가 보지는 아엇스나 참말 창피햇슴니다. 맑은 물 차저서 다소간 감턍을 씻처버리고 발기전 남 보기 전에 간다 그 빗출빗츨 학교근처로 갓슴니다. 술이 깨이는지 목이 엇더케 마르던지요. 그래 염치불구하고 어린애 모양으로 뽕나무 열매를 한 박아지 따먹엇지요. 그리고 나서 학교로 바로 가기는 무엇해서 엇던 소사의 집을 차저가서 가만히 사정을 말하고 방 안에 드러가 남모르게 소사의 옷을 엇어 입고 아렷목에 누어 잠을 한잠 잣어요.
아! 이것 보시요. 이러는 동안에 갓치 갓든 선생들은 나를 찻느라고 밤을 새여가며 산과들을 왼통해매이다가 종시 찻지를 못하고 할 수 업스닛가 학생들과 부형까지 총출동을 식켜 차젓다 합니다. 그래도 못 찻가스닛가 이제는 최후 수단으로 혹시 큰 일이나 낫나하야 주재소에 수색원 까지 제출하기로 햇다함니다. 이러는 통에 소사가 나의 소식을 알켜 노흐닛가 모다 조아라고 우루루 몰켜 왓슴니다 그려. 얼마나 창피헷켓슴닛가. 선생의 체신으로 이게 무엇임닛가. 만약 수색원만 제출하야 순사 까지 출동했드면 엇지 되얏겟소. 그후 부터는 술에 대해서 몹시 주의를 햇슴니다. ＜136＞
