Submission Welcome  

Local Colors! This is a space for local readers to express their thoughts. Feel free to submit anything at will. Each of the 13 provinces (道) holds various strange customs, unusual habits, and shocking historical legends. From notable figures to scenic landscapes, and local products, write about them. Anyone can send in articles regarding their local area. However, certain limitations need to be addressed.  

1. Do not write in a clichéd introduction; make it interesting and engaging to read.  

2. Write in 15 characters per line for about 60 lines.  

3. Write clearly and distinctly in your sections.  

4. It is even better if you attach photographs or similar materials.  

The editing and selection will be at the discretion of our company, but those whose submissions are published will receive a nominal compensation. <146>  

**Summary**  

This article is a call for submissions from local readers, urging them to share unique aspects of their regions, including customs, personalities, and landscapes. The specific instructions for submissions suggest a push for creative and engaging writing. From a historical perspective, the encouragement of local expression highlights the desire for a diverse literary scene during Korea's colonial period, reflecting a growing consciousness around regional identity and heritage amid Japanese oppression.

投稿歡迎
地方色! 이것은 지방 독자의 페지로 맛겨둔다. 멋대로 마음대로 投稿하라. 13道 坊坊曲曲에는 가지각색의 이상한 풍속, 야릇한 습관, 駭怪한 역사 전설이 만흘 것이다. 인물도 좃타. 山水風景도 좃타. 風土産物도 좃타. 그것을 쓰란 말이다. 누구나 자기 지방의 것을 얼마던지 써 보내라. 그러나 요만한 소위 制限는 드러 주어야 한다.
1. 진부한 소개문 가티 쓰지 말고 좀 멋잇시 흥미잇게 읽을 수 잇도록 쓸 일.
2. 원고지 1行15字 60行 내외로 쓸 일.
3. 구절을 똑똑 떼여서 쓸 일.
4. 사진 가튼 것을 添附하여 보내는 것온 더욱 조흔 일.
添削과 取捨는 本社에 一任하되 揭載되는 분에 대하야는 薄謝를 드리기로 함니다.＜146＞
