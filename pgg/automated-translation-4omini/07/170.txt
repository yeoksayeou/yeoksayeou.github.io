기사제목	두 달 동안 (Over Two Months)  
저자	안흔 기자 (An Heun)  
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)  

Over the past two months, among the journalists, half of them have been subjected to arbitrary detention, and the editorial manager, Shin Yeong-cheol, has fallen ill and been hospitalized, causing the editing for the May issue to be delayed by over two weeks. Eventually, the edition, which was belatedly edited, faced censorship from the authorities and had much of its content deleted. As a result, we hastily had to conduct a second round of editing, but unfortunately, that too was subjected to further deletions, making it impossible to publish as it was. In light of this situation, after much consideration, we had no choice but to gather articles for a third time, and we barely managed to pass through censorship without deletions.

During this time, the date of May 6th simply passed by unnoticed. If we had known this would happen in advance, we might have notified our readers. However, believing it wouldn’t take long, we just let time slip by. We hope you will understand these circumstances, and that rushing to print through sleepless nights has resulted in this issue not being as polished as the previous April issue. We ask for your forgiveness and seek your kind consideration for the August issue. (Thank you)  

**Summary**  

The article discusses the challenges faced by the magazine during a tumultuous period, with many journalists detained and the editorial team stricken by illness. The delays in publication due to censorship and deletions highlight the tense climate under colonial rule. Historians might find the account revealing of the oppressive atmosphere for intellectuals during this era of Japanese colonialism in Korea, emphasizing the struggles for freedom of expression.

기사형태	사고·편집후기

두 달 동안
만치 안흔 기자중에 半數나 되는 사람이 自由拘束된 중에 編輯 責任者인 申瑩澈君이 또 身病으로 입원해 잇게 되야 5월호의 編輯이 2주여나 느저 젓난대 늣게야 편집된 그것이 當局의 검열을 밧는 중에 만흔 삭제를 당한 고로 제 2차의 편집을 속히 하야 추가 검열을 밧는 중 불행히 또 삭제를 당하야 도저히 그대로 발행하게 못 된 고로 생각다 못하야 다시 제 3차의 기사를 蒐集하야 간신히 삭제 업는 검열을 밧앗슴니다.
그리하는 동안에 5월 6일이 그냥 넘어 가고 말엇슴니다. 이리 될줄 미리 알엇드면 일즉이 讀者諸位에 통고라도 할 것 이엿스나 不日間 되려니 하고 잇섯든 고로 그냥 그냥 밀니여 왓슴니다. 이러한 사정을 삶혀도 주시려니와 급히만 인쇄 하노라고 밤을 새여 한 일이라 體裁에 用意 만히 하는 本誌가  지난 4월호와 본호가 함께 그다지 뛰여나게 깨긋하지 못한 것을 아울너 용서하시고 모든 것을 8월호에 긔대해 주시기 바람니다. (謝告)
