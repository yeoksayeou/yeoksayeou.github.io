Greetings for Postcard Communications  

We will establish a 'Postcard Communication Column' to provide readers with communication. Please use it freely. The contents may include complete freedom, scathing criticism, articles, play reviews, news, or anything else (absolutely prohibited outside of postcards).  

○ To be written in postcard communication  

○ Must be submitted by the 10th of each month to be included in the following month's issue <41>  
<42>  
<43>  

**Summary**  

The article announces the establishment of a communication column dedicated to readers, inviting submissions on a wide variety of topics, strictly via postcards. It underscores the editorial team's commitment to frank and diverse opinions, encouraging the free exchange of thoughts among readers. This piece reflects the cultural openness sought in Korean society during the colonial period, emphasizing the desire for unfiltered expression in a time of oppression and censorship.
