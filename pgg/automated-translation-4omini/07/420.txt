Article Title: 編輯局員總出 내가 第一 창피하얏든 일, 記者 1人 1話, 사돈집 處女 (The Editor’s Office Reflects on My Most Embarrassing Experience, One Story from One Reporter, The Virgin at the In-Law's House)  
Author: 金起田 (Kim Gi-cheon)  
Article Type: 회고·수기 (Memoir)  

The life I have lived has never been particularly bold or daring; at the same time, I have not experienced many genuine moments of refreshment or great joy in my life, nor have I ever had the chance to truly enjoy life. Reflecting on it, my life has been quite fine and thin, almost lukewarm.

If I were to force myself to state an instance, it would be when I was fourteen years old. I accompanied my mother to her parents' house, which was my in-law's family. At their home, they welcomed me with open arms, treating me as a young lord from their family and showering me with affection. 

As I played with some boys, there were also several younger male relatives there, and one girl around the same age as me (my sister-in-law). Whenever I played rambunctiously with their younger brother, the girl usually stood by and laughed cheerfully at us. It seemed as if all her actions were aimed at observing me and expressing her admiration.

But then something happened that I can hardly describe. While I jumped and played, my excitement suddenly caused my pants to come down, halfway to my feet, leaving me utterly embarrassed. Even with my young heart, I felt so ashamed that I froze on the spot, struggling to pull my pants back up, and I could not face the girl ever again. I quickly returned home, not daring to go back even when my mother suggested we visit again. Perhaps this could be considered one of my embarrassing moments. Ha ha.

  

**Summary**

In this recollection, Kim Gi-cheon reflects on a moment of childhood embarrassment that left a lasting impact on him. He recalls a visit to his in-laws, where a slip-up caused him great shame in front of a girl he found charming. This personal anecdote illustrates the complexities of youthful social interactions and the feelings of awkwardness that often accompany them. From a historical perspective, this account provides insights into the societal expectations and familial interactions of the time, highlighting the emotional lives of children in colonial Korea and how they navigated relationships within their extended families.

編輯局員總出 내가 第一 창피하얏든 일, 記者 1人 1話, 사돈집 處女
						金起田
한 번도 걸젹걸젹하게 대담하게 살아 보지를 못한 나의 생활＜136＞에는 무엇 시원한 유쾌를 늣긴 때가 업섯는 동시에 여시 톡톡한 창피를 당해 본 긔역도 업다. 도라 보면 얼마나 가늘고 기다라한 뜨뜨미지근한 생활이 엿는가.
억지로 말하면 이런 것이라고나 할가 열 네 살 낫슬 때에 아즈머님을 쪼차 아즈머님의 친정 즉 사돈집을 가섯는대 그 집에서는 나를 사돈집의 도령님이 왓다고 하면서 극진히 대우해 주고 사랑해 주엇다. 그래 자미가 나서 몃 츨을 묵으면서 노는 대 그 집에는 아즈버님의 남동생도 여러 사람이엿거니와 나와 꼭 년갑쯤된 녀동생(형님의 처데)이 한 분 잇섯다. 내가 날마다 그를 (아즈머님의 남동생 되는)군형데와 더부러 갈캠질하고 뛰놀 때에는 그 처녀는 매양 우리 겻헤 서서 해쥭해쥭 웃고 잇는 것이 보통이 엿스며 그리고 그의 모든 동작은 나를 쥬의해 보는 듯 하엿고 나를 동졍해 주는 듯 하엿다. 그런대 이게 무슨 일임가. 내가 그의 압헤서 뛰염을 하며 신이 나게 놀다가 엇더캐된 셈판이 갑작이 허리띄가 뚝 끈어지며 바지가 발등 우에 까지 나려오고 마럿다. 나는 어린 마음에도 엇지 창피하던지 그만 그 자리에 꽉 주저 안저서 겨우 바지를 추어 입고는 다시 그 찀˜녀의 편을 도라다 보지 못하고 아조 나의 집으로 다라 오고 마럿다. 그 후에는 아즈머님이 작고 가자고 해도 그 생각이 나셔 다시는 가지 못하고 마럿다. 이런 것도 창피츅에 하나가 될넌지. 하하.＜137＞
