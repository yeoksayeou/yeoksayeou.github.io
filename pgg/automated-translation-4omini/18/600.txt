기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Commentary)  

In the March issue, due to various painful circumstances, we could not publish it on time, and thus we released the so-called combined issue (March-April). I refrain from repeating my apologies too many times lest I impose further regrets. Instead, I ask everyone to kindly understand our situation.

This issue has been edited with March as the standard, so the content is entirely reflective of March. As you all know, March is the season of revival and regeneration, a time when sick recover, the dead come back to life, and scattered beings unite. It is filled with articles on rejuvenation and success that, upon a single reading, can lift one's spirits just like spring snow. I sincerely hope every Korean can read this.

The previous issue was a special edition on gender issues and a commemorative issue for surpassing a circulation of 30,000 copies. It was a remarkable achievement that garnered widespread popularity, surprising many. The editor is not only gratified but also hopes the readers will feel the same excitement.

However, there are some individuals displaying a lack of common sense by criticizing articles on gender issues without having read the content. If they express such opinions without engaging with the material, it reflects nothing but frivolity. If they have read it and still speak out, it indicates an urgent need for self-examination. It is particularly absurd and regrettable that someone who usually advocates for sex education dismisses serious articles on sex education published in this magazine by commenting on its moral implications instead. Furthermore, it is ridiculous that they would introduce advertisements for foreign obscene pictures just to save a few bucks while criticizing serious articles on essential sex education. If they continue to behave insensitively, they might attend a wedding and condemn it for the "frivolous gathering of young men and women" or criticize a family offering ritual drinks as "a drinking problem before family diseases." Oh dear! I hope you refrain from making rash statements from now on. (Chung)

Furthermore, it was mentioned in the previous issue that the editorial office was undergoing expansion, and it has completed its construction now. However, when it comes to additional requests like adding a telephone, I negotiated with the business director, and since a lot of money was spent on building the new office, further expenses seemed unlikely, and they dismissed the idea outright. Yet, we were not discouraged; through active efforts, we finally got a telephone installed in the editorial office! Just a small step forward, but we are now much better connected. Immediately when the phone rang, anyone who could get to that new phone first was said to have good fortune that day. Standing around, someone rang, saying, "Is this Mr. Pang?" And through the loud noise came, "Oh no! Did I make a turn for trouble?" With a beaming smile, Mr. Pang seemed to have his fortune for the day! Despite not eating lunch that day due to all the excitement, I spent a lot of money and completed both the house and telephone installation, and now I'm just sorry that editorial work continues to lag behind. 

However, though I offer no excuses, I promise that from this point onwards, we shall publish regularly. (Park) <179>  

**Summary**  

This article from the magazine reflects the challenges faced by the editorial team in publishing their issues timely due to various difficulties. It discusses the themes of regeneration in the March issue and touches upon public reactions to prior discussions on gender issues. The author expresses contempt for critics who disparage serious topics like sex education without understanding the material, revealing tensions in society regarding discussions of gender during the colonial period. Historians might find interest in the editorial's candid views on societal norms and the push for sex education amidst a backdrop of rigid moral expectations during colonial Korea.

編輯室 落書
3월 호로 꼭 나와야만 될 冊이 가슴 쓰라린 여러가지 사정으로 인하야 제 때에 나오지 못하고 글자부터 쓰기 실은 소위 合倂號(3, 4월)를 발행하게 되엿다. 미안의 말슴도 여러 번 하면 도로혀 미안을 끼칠가 하야 謝禮를 略하고 다만 여러분에게 우리 처지를 잘 諒解하야 달나고 청할 뿐이다.

이번 호는 본래 3월을 標準하야 편집하기 때문에 기사의 내용이 전혀 3월의 기분이 만타. 3월은 여러분도 잘 알으시는 바와 가티 方春和時 萬物更生하는 時期임으로 病者蘇, 死者生, 散者合, 其外 創建, 成功등 갱생활약의 기사를 滿載하야 한 번만 읽으면 모돈 落心이 春雪과 가티 다 살아지게 되엿다.
특히 朝鮮사람 된 우리로서는 누구나 한번식 잘 읽어 주기를 바란다.

前號는 兩性問題의 특집호요 本誌 3만부 突破의 기념호이니 만히 만천하의 대인기를 꿀어서 누구나 크게 놀날만한 破天荒의 大成績을 보게 되엿다. 편집자도 스사로 깃버함을 마지 안커니와 독자 여러분도 또한 가티 깃버하야 주기를 바란다.

그러나 일부의 沒常識 頑冥의 패가 잇서 兩性問題 기사를 非難하는 듯한 바 그들이 기사내용을 닑어 보지 안코 하는 짓이라면 但히 輕妄에 돌니고 말 것이어니와 닑고서 하는 말이라면 그 頭顔를 檢診할 필요가 잇다 한다. 자기자신이 平素에 성교육의 필요를 주장하면서 本誌가 될 수 잇는데까지 鄭重하게 성교육 材料를 取扱한 기사를 沒理解하야 風敎上 云云하고 橫堅說去하는 것은 可笑라는 것 보다 寧히 可惜하다 할밧게 업다. 성문제라 하닛가 내용도 보지 안코 不美 云云하는 愚가 識者中에 잇다는 것이 딱한 일이요 더욱 돈 몃푼을 위하야 外國奸商輩의 春畵圖 廣告를 소개하는 이로서 남의 愼重한 성교육 기사를 論難하는 데 至하야는 그 愚 가히 논할 바도 못된다. 그럿케 몰상식하게 굴다가는 남의 결혼식 구경을 하고 『禮拜堂內에 靑春男女 白畫野合』이란 대제목으로 風紀紊亂을 痛論하고 또 남의 祭祀 집에 가서 獻酌하는 것을 보고 『家病前一門*酒』라고 大書特書하야 禁酒必要를 絶叫할 것이다. 咄咄! 此後幸勿妄論如何. (靑)
