필자	白南奎  
기사형태	문예기타 (Literary Other)  

After graduating from high school, I think of my daughter, who has graduated from elementary school, as exactly the same. 

Just because she has spent four more years in high school, does that mean her ambitions are established and does she have an understanding of family or society? Not at all. Therefore, I think I should treat her the same as a daughter who has just graduated from elementary school and should supervise her accordingly. But how to supervise is a crucial issue here.

First, the supervision should be based on the perspective of the daughter herself rather than a rigid parental viewpoint. I must become one with her to understand how she feels and how to deal with her.

Next, in practical terms, we must guide her to become the housewife and mother of another family. Regarding the methods for this, I believe it is essential for young girls to study various aspects of life. They must learn from their mothers at home, read newspapers and books, and personally visit homes such as elementary schools or kindergartens to observe and learn. Before getting married, they should acquire knowledge about fairy tales or children’s songs and an understanding of child hygiene, among other things. <89>  

**Summary**

This article discusses the challenges of educating young women at home after their graduation from school, emphasizing the importance of guiding them to become competent housewives and mothers. The author argues for an approach focused on the daughters' perspectives, highlighting the need for practical education through observation and learning from various sources, including their mothers. This perspective reflects the societal values of the time regarding women's roles within the family and highlights the expectations placed upon women in Korean society during the colonial era, which historians may find revealing in terms of gender roles and education.

高等普通學校를 졸업하고 드러안즌 딸을 소학교를 졸업하고 드러안즌 딸과 나는 똑갓치 봄니다.
高等普通을 4년 더 하엿다고 그동안에 자기의 志氣가 서서  가정이나 사회에 대한 이해력이 잇느냐 하면 그럿치 못합니다. 그럼으로 그 딸을 대하기를 小學校 졸업한 딸과 일반으로 대하며 감독해야 되리라고 생각합니다. 그런데 그 감독이란 것이 여기서 문제이겟지요.
먼저 감독을 父兄本位로 하는 頑固를 떠나 딸 그사람 본위로 해야 될 것입니다. 내가 그 딸의 몸이 되여 가지고 생각하며 그 딸을 대할 것입니다.
다음에 구체문제로서는 남의 안해가 되고 남의 어머니가 될 한 가정의 주부로 인도해야겟지요. 그 방법에 잇서서는 어린 아희들의 생활을 만히 연구하도록 할 것이니 집에 잇서 어머님에게도 배우고 신문 서적으로 배우고 밧그로 小學校나 幼稚園 갓흔 데를 자조 가서 눈으로 보기도 하고 듯기도하여 出嫁하기 전에 집에 잇는 그동안 동화나 동요에 대한 상식을 배워 가지게 해야 할 줄 압니다. 아동위생에 대한 상식도 물론 그와 갓흘 것입니다.<89>
