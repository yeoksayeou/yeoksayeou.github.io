기사제목	우리아기 (Our Baby)  
필자	春園 (Chunwon)  
기사형태	시 (Poem)  

Our baby was born on a fine day,  
In a place 90 ri away, there was no wind,  
Tonight, in the valley of Suri, the snow and wind blow,  
Our baby lies peacefully in Han-yang.  

Wherever our baby goes, the spring breeze blows,  
When our baby sleeps, the waves also calm down,  
Blessed is our baby, wherever it goes,  
Peace settles upon the world. (End) <145>  

**Summary**

The poem reflects a simple yet profound affection for a newborn, capturing a moment of tranquility and hope amidst the natural landscape. It juxtaposes the joy of parenthood with the peacefulness of the world, suggesting a universal longing for peace and comfort during challenging times. Historians might find this portrayal significant as it encapsulates the sentiments of Korean families during the colonial period, where personal joys were often overshadowed by broader socio-political turmoil.
