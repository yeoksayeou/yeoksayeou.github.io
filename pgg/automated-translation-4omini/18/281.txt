二大懸賞  
In this book, there are two major prizes: a grand prize of five hundred yen and a prize of a bag of white rice. It is important for you to carefully check the table of contents to find out how to participate in these rewards. The first one is urgent, so please apply quickly. <79>  

 
**Summary**  

This article announces two significant prizes available to readers, highlighting a cash award and a supply of rice as very appealing rewards. The emphasis on urgency in applying for these prizes could reflect the economic hardships faced by many during the colonial period, demonstrating the magazine's understanding of its audience's needs. Historians might find this article interesting as it implies ways in which colonial publications tried to engage with their readers while also addressing the material concerns of everyday life under colonial rule.
