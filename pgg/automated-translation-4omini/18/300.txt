Severe Pain in the Waist, Blossoming of Flowers  
A Great Caution  

A boy named 씨동 from 압집 was a boy explorer. One day, a guest arrived, and when the guest tossed his hat, the boy explorer asked him. "Excuse me, isn't there a hair on your forehead?"  
"Um, because I am engaged in research, I use my brain every day, so my hair cannot grow, and even those that do grow have all fallen out."  
"Uh-huh. That's why the hair on my mother's lips is all gone."  

What is the reason for that?  

"It must be from doing research. It seems like you are losing more hair."  
"I have a concern."  
"What concern is that?"  
"It's a worry that my hair is getting smaller and falling out..."  

Which part of the neighborhood?  

When a train passed through a tunnel without lights, a man suddenly thought, "In such times, theft (窃盜) is frightening." As he reached into his coat pocket, he discovered another man's hand was already inside.  
"You! You thief! Take your hand out!" he shouted.  
"You! Take your hand out! Who do you call a thief?" Since both were shouting at each other, it turned into a confusion until a gentleman nearby shouted,  
"You two, take your hands out of my pocket! How is it that in such darkness, two people have their hands in somebody else's pocket?"  

The Reason for Coming Back  

A woman, after having a quarrel with her husband and running away to a inn, came back to her husband within just four days. When her friends asked why she returned so quickly, she said, "Everything was fine, but one thing—the signboard of the inn saying 'No lost items' was visible every time I tried to enter." So when they asked what it said, she replied,  
"It says there are no lost items."  

Strange Natural Law  

A Chinese person and a Korean went hunting with shotguns, and since the Korean was very poor at shooting, the cunning Chinese gentleman proposed a bet.  
"Look, if you shoot a bird from a distance of more than 50 feet, I will give you 5 yen. Of course, if you fail to do so, you have to give me 5 yen."  
"Alright, let’s see." When he responded, at that moment, a large bird was flying just above them. The Korean aimed and fired, and as smoke billowed, the bird fell. "Haha, I've just won 5 yen!" he exclaimed. The astonished Chinese gentleman stood there dumbfounded, then said,  
"Um, hold on a moment. If a bird were to fall from such a height, even without you hitting it, it would surely die; that's a natural law, isn't it?"  
The Chinese gentleman continued to be amazed.  

A Housewife's Kindness  

Although it was not very large, the inn was not small either. When the new guests were about to lie down at night, the landlady kindly said,  
"If you need to get up in the morning, just shout downstairs, and I will come up to wake you."  

The Same Thing  

"I would like to introduce my wife."  
"Oh no, that's unnecessary. I have one person too."  

Clear Cause  

A law school professor asked,  
"What is a significant cause of divorce?"  
A bright student replied,  
"That's marriage."  

In Place of That  

An elder said, "Your father has a mustache in the shape of '八字'."  
The child replied, "Yes, it looks impressive like a general's mustache, right?"  
The elder said, "Impressive, what are you talking about! That mustache is just like your father's."  
"Why?"  
"Think about it. With such a big mustache, while eating, wouldn’t the rice grains get stuck in it and make it dirty?"  
The child replied, "True, but…"  
"What do you mean 'but'?"  
"The foam from beer does not get stuck and get dirty, does it?"  
The elder remained silent.  

Strange Occupation  

As a doctor was examining a patient, he asked,  
"Do you talk in your sleep?"  
"No, absolutely not. It’s impossible for me to talk while sleeping since everyone else is asleep."  
"Haha, that’s a normal thing."  
"I am a professor at a vocational school."  

The Hair-Cutting Girl  

A young man said, "You always talk about being fashionable while cutting your hair, but can you even use a spatula?"  
The girl who cuts hair replied angrily, "Why wouldn’t I know? Just because I’m modern doesn’t mean I don’t know how to use a spatula!"  
The young man said, "Eat up."  

Early Maturity  

One young woman was nursing her child on the tram and as a gentleman stood in the middle without a place to sit, the well-mannered child jumped from her lap and pointed to her mother's lap while saying to the gentleman,  
"Excuse me, please come and sit here."  

Free Will  

Two strangers were fishing in the same spot. A caught small fry, while B did not catch a single fish before sunset. Puzzled, B asked out of frustration,  
"Excuse me, I brought the most expensive bait, yet I didn't catch a thing. What kind of bait do you use?"  
Feeling annoyed, A kept silent until B insisted, and finally A responded,  
"I just tied a 50-jeon silver coin to my fishing line."  
"A 50-jeon silver coin won't attract fish?"  
"Fish don’t eat it, but when they pull it, I can just buy my favorite bait with it later."  
B was left speechless.  

**Summary**  

This article is a collection of humorous anecdotes reflecting everyday life and social interactions during the 1920s in Korea. The scenarios range from light-hearted conversations between strangers to peculiar situations highlighting cultural quirks and social observations. For historians, these anecdotes may shed light on the social dynamics and humor that persisted in colonial Korea, indicating how ordinary people navigated life amidst significant political and social changes.

어느 구녁?

기차가 전등도 안켜고 굴속을 지나가게 되엿슬 때 한 남자가 언뜻 『이런때 스리(窃盜)가 무섭더라.』는 생각이 나서 양복 주머니에 손을 너어보앗다. 아이크머니 벌서 딴놈의 손이 그 속에 드러와 잇는지라.
『이놈아. 도뎍놈아. 손을 끄내여라!』 하닛가
『이놈아. 네 손을 끄내가거라. 누구더러 도리혀 도뎍이래.』 하고 그놈도 소리를 지르는 고로 읏지된 셈인가 하는 판에
『이놈들아. 두놈 다 내주머니에서 손을 끄내가거라. 아모리 어둔 중이기로 한꺼번에 두놈씩 남의 주머니에 손을 데민단 말이냐?』 하고 엽헤 잇던 신사가 소리를 지른다.

남편차저 온 이유

어느 부인이 부부싸홈을 하고 화ㅅ김에 뛰여나가서 여관생활을 하고 잇섯는데 단 4일이 못되야 도로 남편에게로 긔여 도라왓다.
읏재서 그리 속히 도로 왓느냐고 그 동무들이 무르닛가 『다른 것은 다 조흔데 똣 한가지 그 여관문 엽헤 써부친 것이 출입할 적마다 눈에 뵈여서 도로 왓다.』 고 하는 고로 무어라고 써붓첫기에 그랫느냐 무르닛가
『니저버린 물건은 업슴닛가.』고 써부친 것이 드란다.
