기사제목	大建物 구경(其二) 中央基督敎靑年會(京城 鍾路) (A Tour of the Grand Building (Part 2) Central Christian Youth Association (Seoul, Jongno))  
기사형태	소식 (News)  

This building is located in the very center of Seoul. It occupies a prime spot right in the middle of Jongno, which has the most convenient transportation, and until the construction of the Cheondogyo Hall (before 1909), it was the largest and most spacious assembly hall for Korean people. This building had its cornerstone laid on November 7, 1907, and after one year of construction, the completion ceremony was held on December 1, 1908. The completion ceremony was recently unprecedentedly grand in Korea, with approximately one thousand guests attending from both domestic and foreign circles, and at that time, His Royal Highness the Crown Prince, who was then His Royal Highness the King, especially attended the event. 

The land area is 966 pyeong, the building area is 1,731 pyeong, and the construction cost was about a hundred thousand won at that time. 

**Summary**  

The article describes a prominent building in central Seoul, used as a major assembly hall prior to the construction of the Cheondogyo Hall. It details the ceremonies associated with its construction and completion, showcasing the significance of the grand event and the notable personalities present, particularly the Crown Prince. This article highlights the cultural and social infrastructure of Korea during the colonial period, demonstrating its importance in fostering community and connectivity among Koreans, which historians might find fascinating regarding the era's national identity and colonial experience.

이 건물은 서울에서 정중앙지지요. 교통이 가장 편리한 종로 한복판을 차지하야 천도교당이 건축되기 이전(己未 이전)까지는 조선 사람의 유일한 집회소요 가장 크고 넓엇던 건물이다. 이 집은 1907년 11월 7일에 定礎式을 행하야 만 1년간 공사로 1908년 12월 1일에 낙성식을 행하얏섯는데 그 낙성식은 근래 조선에서 空前에 盛況을 이루어 참석한 內外國 손님이 천여 명에 달하고 지금 李王殿下께서 황태자로 기실 때에 특히 來臨까지 하시엿다.
