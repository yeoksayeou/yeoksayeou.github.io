기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)  

Increased Volume

Due to the shortage of the first issue, we have greatly increased the volume of this second issue. Not only has "慧星" (Hwaeseong) become popular, but "별건곤" (Byeolgeongon) has also gained immense popularity. Because it ran out of copies within just five days, we faced significant hardship and had to publish an urgent advertisement in various newspapers, pleading, "Please do not order any more books." <24>  

<24>  

**Summary**

The article discusses the increase in publication volume for the second issue of "별건곤" due to demand, particularly after experiencing a rapid sell-out of the first issue. The need to appeal to the public not to place orders indicates the high interest in the magazine. This reflects the cultural dynamics of the time, showing how colonial-era publications could attract attention and readership among Koreans during a period when media was becoming a vital vehicle for both information and identity.
