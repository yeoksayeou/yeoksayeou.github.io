百花塔  
Mr. Lee Jong-lin and his letter  
It is a complete lie that a person who drinks alcohol has tremors. Mr. Lee Jong-lin of the Cheondogyo Church cannot get drunk even if he tries to get pleasantly intoxicated with stories about wheat fields. However, he has had severe tremors since childhood, so he cannot even eat rice properly, and furthermore, he cannot write well at all. When he picks up a brush, he has trouble with his English studies, yet he manages to write in a twisted and difficult English style. Consequently, it takes him a long time to write. If you ask him to write a letter for some urgent matter, it will surely be a disaster, so be careful in advance. 

Mr. Ryu Gwang-ryeol’s manuscript handwriting  
Mr. Ryu Gwang-ryeol of the Chosun Ilbo has published many articles in newspapers and magazines, which everyone reads with interest. However, his manuscript handwriting is relatively sloppy; if at first glance, it seems like a gathering of Big Uncle and his brothers. Among our acquaintances, we always pay little attention to his handwriting, but once someone said upon seeing his handwriting that Mr. Ryu writes the manuscripts as if he were copying from a child's book or something like that. Haha... In reality, it turns out to be his own handwriting.

Mr. Kim Gi-jeon’s photograph  
Mr. Kim Gi-jeon of the Cheondogyo Youth Party is well-known for his runny nose. Recently, after he went to China and sent a photograph to a friend, the friend joyfully looked at it and remarked that the photograph turned out well; however, it was ruined because his signature runny nose wasn’t hidden in the picture, which caused everyone to burst into laughter. <17>  

**Summary**  

The article presents humorous anecdotes about three individuals: Lee Jong-lin, Ryu Gwang-ryeol, and Kim Gi-jeon, highlighting peculiarities in their lives and characteristics, particularly focusing on Lee's writing difficulties and Ryu's handwriting. It provides a glimpse into the personalities and social dynamics among the intellectual circles of 1930s Korea. Historians may find the portrayal of these individuals as a reflection of cultural attitudes towards education and social interaction during the colonial period, revealing how personal quirks could become subjects of light-hearted discussion, serving as informal documentation of social life.

百花塔
李鍾麟氏와편지
술 먹는 사람이 수전증잇다는 것도 아주 그짓말이다. 텬도교회 리종린씨는  과맥전대취(過麥田大醉)는 그만두고 밀밧 이야기만 하야도 취할만치 술을 못 자시는 이다. 그러나 그는 어려서부터 수전이 엇지 심한지 밥숫까닭을 바루 들지 못하는데 더구나 글씨가튼 것은 아주 잘 쓸 수가 업서서 붓을 들면 영어(英語)공부를 안이한 이로되 꼬불꼬불하게 영자가튼 글씨를 잘 쓴다. 따러서 쓰는 시간도 퍽 더듸다. 그더러 무슨 급한 일에 편지 써 달나는 청하다가는 대랑패할 터이니 미리 주의-.

柳光烈氏의 原稿 글씨
조선일보사 류광렬(柳光烈)씨의 글은 신문으로 잡지로 만히 발표가 되고 또 누구나 애독을 하는 신문이다. 그러나 원고(原稿)글씨는 비교적 졸력하야 일번에 낫스면 대외백(大偎伯)과의 형제를 모을만 하다. 우리 친지간에는 항상 보닛가 그의 글씨을 그리 유의를 하지 안치만은 엇던때 인소사람이 그의 글씨를 보고는 이류 선생은 원고를 써 가지고는 항상 자긔집 어린애로 복사를 식힌는 모양이라고 하더란가 하하.. 실상 알고 보면 그의 친필-.

金起田氏의 寫眞
텬도교청년당 김긔전씨는 콧물 잘 흘니기로 유명한 이엿다. 요전에 그가 중국에 가서 긔념으로 사진을 백히여서 엇던 친구에게 보냇더니 그 친구는 반가이 바더보고 하는 말이 그 사진이 되기는 잘 되엿스나 그의 특종인 콧물을 안이 백히여서  안되엿다 하야 일좌가 대소(大笑)하엿다고.＜17＞
