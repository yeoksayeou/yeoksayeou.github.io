Q and A

Record of questions and answers

You are not writing novels these days. Why is that?  
I have been busy with work. I am currently thinking of selecting better materials to produce a well-crafted novel.

Are you visiting Seoul? Are you living there?  
This year, I am planning to live in Seoul for a while. My husband, who attends Seonggyeongwan (성균관), is feeling quite lonely in this foreign place.

When you first came out of prison, what was the happiest thing for you?  
Meeting my wife, whom I hadn't seen for several years, was the happiest thing.

Mr. Kim, you seem to be over fifty years old, but you say you're still forty-nine every year. What is the reason for this?  
Ah, you cannot deceive you, but I have two reasons for lying about my age.  
1. The Youth Party (青年黨) excludes members over fifty.  
2. I still do not have a son, and since my wife is aging, I want to marry a younger woman.

Mr. Park, Spring Hill (春坡) is said to be suffering from neuralgia and alcoholism. Will you stop drinking?  
No, I plan to completely abstain from alcohol. The doctor says I absolutely must not continue drinking.

  

**Summary**  

This article presents a series of conversational Q&As with notable individuals discussing personal and societal matters during the colonial period in Korea. Particularly interesting is the concern over age and societal expectations reflected in Kim's reasoning for lying about his age as well as the significant loneliness and isolation expressed by wives and families of men living away from home for work and school. Historians might find the insights into personal struggles against the backdrop of colonial pressures especially illuminating.

一問一答記
李益相
記 당신은 요새에 왜 소설을 아니 쓰십니까
李 일이 밧버서 그랫습니다. 이 압흐로 조흔 재료를 선택하야 力作의 소설을 하나 내노랴고 생각중이올시다.

朴敬植
記 당신은 서울을 다니러 오섯습니까. 살너오섯습니까.
朴 금년에는 서울에 와서 좀 살어볼가 함니다. 남편되는 이가 城大를 다니는데 객지생활이 너무 고적해 하니까요. ＜10＞

鄭柏
記 감옥에서 처음 나올 때 제일 반갑던 일이 무엇이엿슴니까.
鄭 여러 해 못보던 妻子를 만나는 것이 제일 반가웟슴니다.

金秉濬
記 金先生은 벌서 50이 넘으신 듯한데 해마다 49세라니 웬 까닭임니까.
金 허허 당신들에게는 아모 것도 속일 수 업스나 나는 두 가지 이유로 나이를 속이지요.
1. 靑年黨에서 50이상이면 除黨을 하는 까닭.
2. 아들이 아즉 업고 마누라가 늙은 까닭에 젊은 여자에게 장가를 가려고.

朴達成
記 春坡선생은 이번에 신경통-알콜중독으로 그와 가티 고생을 하시엿다니 이 압흐로 斷酒를 하시겟슴니까. 계속하야 飮酒를 하시겟슴니까.
朴 아니올시다. 아주 斷酒를 할 작정이올시다. 의사 말이 절대로 斷飮을 하지 안으면 안된다니까요.＜27＞
