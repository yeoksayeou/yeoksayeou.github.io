Dramatic Special Issue  

The Huiseong magazine is experiencing an extraordinary level of popularity beyond imagination, and the reporters are full of energy to publish this second issue, which is exceptionally edited as you see. It is not just about the title but also has substantial content that has received overwhelming praise. The magazine has published only valuable articles that are necessary for everyone to read in Korea. Thus, this second issue has been compiled to include only significant articles currently being discussed in Korea, making it a treasure that every Korean should read. Please do not miss it; we sincerely hope you will read it. 『慧星』 is not a light magazine like ordinary others. Please take a moment to consider the title above once more.  

Summary  

The article presents the second issue of the Huiseong magazine, highlighting its surprising popularity and emphasizing the importance of its content, which includes essential articles relevant to Koreans. This promotional piece shows the effort of the editorial team to provide valuable insights and information at a time when such resources might have been limited during the colonial period. Historians might find the emphasis on the magazine's role in shaping public discourse and its encouragement for collective awareness among Koreans particularly significant as it reflects the media's function in a colonial context.

飛躍的 特輯
慧星잡지는 部數가 그러케 몹시 굉장한 우에 상상 이상의 대인기에 記者 一同 元氣百倍하야 제2호는 보시는 바와 가티 이러케 굉장하게 특별편집하엿슴니다. 제목만 느러놋는 것이 아니라 내용이 닑을 갑이 잇다고 호평이 대단함니다.＜24＞ 잡지는 닑고 엇는 것이 잇서야 하는대 慧星은 모조리 어들 것이 만흔 기사만 실엇다 하야 환영되는 바 이번 제2호는 目下 朝鮮안에서 문제되는 중요기사만 추려서 朝鮮사람치고는 누구던지 닑어야 할 보물이 되엿슴니다. 빠지시지 말고 당신도 꼭 닑으시기 바람니다. 『慧星』은 보통 다른 잡지처럼 가벼운 것이 아님니다. 이 우에 잇는 제목을 다시 한 번 마음넉여 보아주십시요.
＜24-25＞
