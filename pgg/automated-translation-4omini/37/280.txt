Editorial Remarks  

Since our magazine has reduced the price to just one won from the original cost, many people have been astonished and have asked in various places whether this is true or not, leading to an overwhelming number of inquiries and letters. Some employees even had difficulty managing the correspondence due to the volume.

Despite the fact that money is precious these days, selling for just one won is astonishingly cheap, and the content of the magazine is even more substantial and engaging than before, making it accessible for anyone who knows Korean, whether they are young or old, or women. As a result, just three days after publication, it sold out, and I apologize to those who placed orders a little late as we could not accommodate them.

This issue, of course, contains more content in the same format and has become richer and more enjoyable. I encourage everyone to place orders quickly before it sells out again.

In the previous issue, on page 13, the phrase regarding the most outstanding students might lead to misunderstandings among students, and I would like to correct the article in the cultivation of talent regarding the candidate Li Wan-yong as there were factual discrepancies with what was stated from the school. <32>  

Summary  

This editorial reflects on the significant reduction in the magazine's price and the overwhelming public response, leading to a swift sell-out of the latest issue. It emphasizes the accessibility and improved quality of content as key factors in the magazine's popularity. This instance offers insight into the socioeconomic environment of colonial Korea, highlighting both the inflation concerns and the growing demand for accessible reading material among the populace. Historians might find the juxtaposition of economic hardship alongside cultural engagement through literature particularly revealing of the complex dynamics faced by Koreans under colonization.

編輯餘言
우리 잡지가 전달부터 원가의 십분지 일을 감하야 단돈 오전으로 된 것은 누구나 깜작 놀나서 그것이 사실인가 안인가 하고 경향 각처에서 뭇는 바람에 전화통이 불이 나다십히 하고 편지가 산덤이가티 싸여서 그대 답하너라고 사원 몃사람은 편즙 일까지 잘 못보왓다.
안인게 안이라 아모리 돈이 귀한 이때라도 오십전 자리가 단 오전으로 된 것은 놀날만치 싸기도 하거니와 잡지 내용이 전보다도 더 마지밋고 글이 평이하야 조선글만 아는 사람이면 젋은이고 늙은이고 부녀자까지 잘 읽게 된 까닭에 발행한지 단 3일에 절판이 되야 조금 늣게 주문하신 이에게는 미안하나마 수용을 못하얏다.
이번호는 갑도 물론 가튼 갑에다 내용이 더 풍부하고 자미잇게 되얏다. 절판되기 전에 속히 주문하야 주기 바란다.
前號 13頁 普成高普 기사 중 가장 령리한 학생 운운 이하의 문구는 학생간 誤解가 잇슬 듯하고 培材高普 기사 중 李完用候가튼 大監 운운 기사는 同校에서 事實相違라기 訂正한다.＜32＞
