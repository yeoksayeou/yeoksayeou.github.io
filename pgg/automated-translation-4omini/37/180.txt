기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)  

News of Kibbun  

We announce the news of Kibbun. The first issue of "慧星" (Sei-sei) sold out in just nine days, and due to a shortage of copies, we regret that we are unable to send them to those who ordered late. However, we can only apologize for this inconvenience. The enormous popularity we have experienced is by no means a matter confined to our own Kibbun. It is certainly not just our issue that experiences such a high demand for good magazines.  

  
**Summary**  

This article discusses the swift popularity of the first issue of the magazine "慧星" (Sei-sei) and highlights the magazine's inability to fulfill all orders due to the overwhelming demand. The article suggests that the rising interest in quality magazines is not limited to the publication in question, reflecting a broader trend in the media industry at the time. Historians may find it interesting as it hints at the cultural dynamics during the colonial period in Korea, suggesting a burgeoning demand for diverse media that might have offered an alternative to mainstream narratives.

깃분 消息
깃분 消息을 告함니다. 『慧星』 創刊號가 단 9일만에 다 팔니고 책이 모자라서 늣게 注文하신 이에게는 보내드리지 못하게 되야 미안하나마 2호로 보내 드릴 밧게 할 수가 업는 대인기를 끄은 것은 결코 우리들만의 깃붐에 그치는 일이 아님니다. 조흔 잡지가 만히 닑혀진다는 것이 엇지 우리뿐만의 깃붐이겟슴니까.＜24＞
