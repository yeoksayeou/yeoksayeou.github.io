(Overview) One year later, around the 9th month of the year of the Water Snake, I thought that I was getting close to a complete cure and that the day of full recovery was not far off. However, misfortune struck again, and in mid-October of that year, a mild cold caused a severe fever of over 40 degrees Celsius, a poor appetite, and intense coughing, leading me to become remarkably emaciated within just four or five days. My wife was worried that I might have lung disease, so we called a doctor. Upon examination, the doctor said that if I contracted wet pneumonia, it could not be cured, which made me very anxious for a time.

I was advised to rest absolutely, even being forbidden to read, but since I originally enjoyed reading, one day, about eight days later, after returning from the restroom, I bought a copy of Bŭlgwon-gun November issue that an unfortunate student was selling. While lying in my room and perusing the article stating, "A cure for a ten-year struggle with tuberculosis and gastrointestinal diseases can be found with this medicine," I learned about "Tablet Kawai" and resolved to try it, so I went to a nearby pharmacy and bought it.

However, when I first took it along with my prescribed medicine and continued the medication for about a week, by November 12, when I had another check-up with my doctor, my fever had stabilized at around 37.2 degrees Celsius (only in the morning) and my appetite had improved somewhat. I was sure that it was the effect of "Tablet Kawai." I continued taking six tablets a day, which led to further improvement; by the end of November, my fever dropped below 37 degrees at all times, my appetite returned to normal, and my cough completely ceased. By the end of December, the doctor declared that "the day of full recovery is not far off" and was surprised, saying, "I've never seen such rapid progress before."

I owe this entirely to “Tablet Kawai,” and I am very grateful. Since then, I have consistently taken "Tablet Kawai," maintaining good health without any changes. Though I also previously suffered from pneumonia, a severe condition that could cause intense pain, after taking "Tablet Kawai," my pneumonia eased alongside it, and now I have returned to general health with an energy level that is markedly different from before. The illness that tormented me for about two years was miraculously defeated by "Tablet Kawai," so I intend to continue using it and dedicate myself to my beliefs. 

This is my numerical report along with my expression of gratitude.

The article recounts the personal experience of Choi Jung-geun, who suffered from pneumonia and found relief through the use of "Tablet Kawai." Highlighting his medical journey, the piece serves both as a testimonial and an endorsement for the effectiveness of the medicine.
