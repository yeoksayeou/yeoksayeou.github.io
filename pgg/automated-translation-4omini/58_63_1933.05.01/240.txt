Prize Question  
The last prize question received many good answers. This time, I will give you a similarly interesting one. "What is found between spring and summer?" Think carefully. If you get it right, there's a great prize waiting for you.  

How to submit answers  
★ The answers must reach our office by May 30th of the coming month.  

★ Write your answer simply on a postcard-sized paper and be sure to include your address and name clearly.  

★ This page's reader stamp must be attached properly to the answer sheet. If you don't attach it, your entry will be void.  

★ On the envelope, make sure to write, "Kyungseong Gaebiksa Byeolgeongon Prize Division" and, in small letters, "Manuscript Inside" using red ink and seal it. Failing to write "Manuscript Inside" will incur a penalty.  

Prizes  
1st prize: One person, special artistic table clock  
Non-ranked: 99 people, a selection of books  

Regarding last month's prize answers  
This time, the total number of entries exceeded 60,000, but among them, there were not many appropriate answers.  

Thus, we conducted a fair drawing among the entries, and the winning answer was "If you add a drop of water to a drop of water, what do you get?" The next interesting answer was the character "王" (King). If you add one and one, it becomes "王". Therefore, two second prizes were awarded based on this character. After that, there were 97 other answers that were nice but did not win a prize.

懸賞問題
지난번 현상문제는 매우자미가 잇섯습니다.
이번에도  자미잇는 것으로 내겟습니다.
「봄과여름사이에 잇는 것이 무엇입니?」
잘생각해보십시오. 알어 맛치기만하면 참자미가잇습니다.

현상해답하는법
★내달(五月)三十日지 본사에 와닷도록되여야합니다.
★엽서만한 조회에다 대답을 간단히 적어보내되 반듯이자긔주소와 성명을 이써야합니니다.
★이페지(頁)한해잇는 독자증을 답안용지에다 반듯이 부처야합니다. 안붓치면 무효입니다.
★피봉에는 반듯이 『京城開闢社別乾坤懸賞係』라쓰고 엽헤다 『原稿在中』이라고 안잉크로쓰고 한주둥이를 고 二錢우표붓처보내주십시오. 原稿在中이라고 안쓰면 벌금(不足)을 물게됩니다.

賞品
一等一人 特製 美術卓上時計
等外九十九人 書籍一部式

前月懸賞解答에 對하야
이번현상은 응모가 도합 六만명이상이엇스나 그중에 그럼직한 해답은 얼마되지 아니하엿습니다.
그리하야 현상에게서 정해로 골나 추첨한 것이 「물한방울에 물한방울을 보태면 물한방울」이라는 것입니다.
그리고 그다음으로 진기한 것은 왕「王」자입니다. 一十一하면 「王」자한자가 되지아니합니. 그래서 이 「王」자에서 이등을 둘만 앗습니다. 그런다음에 근사하게 마즌분으로 은 것이 九十七이 다 차지못하엿습니다.
