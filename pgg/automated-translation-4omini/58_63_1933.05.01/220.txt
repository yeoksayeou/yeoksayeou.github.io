Mosaic

A Signboard That Changes Well  
It would be rude to call someone else's house a haunted house, so let's simply refer to it as a signboard that changes well, and speak about a few houses in Seoul.

재동감옥 (Jaedong Prison)  
At the entrance of Jaedong (재동), formerly known as Namyeongwigung (남영위궁), the current nickname is Jaedong Prison. Originally, that house was a royal residence, and the character for "house" (궁) and the character for "to come to an end" (궁) perhaps gave rise to its name. Back when it was owned by the Yoon family (윤씨), it would also rent out apartments to about 70 households in intervals. Later, when it was purchased by Jo Seolhyeon (조설현), he expelled everyone and made significant renovations, thus earning it the names Jaedong Abangung (재동 아방궁) and Jaedong Prison. However, just a few years later, Jo also failed, and the house was once again occupied and rented out to various people.

안국동 까치집 (Ankukdong Magpie House)  
Then there's the Ankukdong (안국동) which has the Huh Shin Hospital (허신병원) and the shop formerly occupied by Bou Yu Jeon (鮮于全氏 운동구 상점자리) that resembles a magpie house; the owner, Park Gwi (박귀), often sees his signboard change about three to six times a year as he heads toward Insa-dong (인사동) from the Ankukdong Six Street (안국동륙거리).

仁寺洞 幻戱樓 (Insa-dong Phantom Theater)  
Going further from there toward the sinjak-ro (신작로) leading to Gyeonji-dong (견지동), at the triangular point where the Insa-dong street intersects, one may find a brick house which has also frequently changed its signboard, sometimes known as the Geumhui Musical Instrument Store (금희 악긔점) or the Asia Café (아세아카페), and now also called the Eden Café (에-덴카페), with its sign changing several times a year—almost like how photos change in moving pictures. This house could be nicknamed the Insa-dong Phantom Theater.

종로 좃도집 (Jongno A House with Changing Signs)  
Speaking of a house with a well-changing sign, the two-story house at the corner leading to the Jongno Pyongyang Cold Noodles House (종로 평양냉면집) is quite a place of change as well. A tavern, a cold noodles house, and a café—its sign changes multiple times a year, to the extent that it feels like it may not stick around for long due to its location. One might call such a place a temporary business, hence the nickname "A House with Changing Signs."

  
**Summary**  

This article discusses various houses in Seoul that are known for frequently changing their signboards, highlighting a mix of historical significance and contemporary commentary on their operations' success and failures. Particularly intriguing is the mention of Jaedong Prison, a site linked to royal lineage, emphasizing shifts in ownership and social status in colonial Korea. Historians might find the implications of these changes illustrative of economic instability during this period under Japanese rule, reflecting broader trends of urban development and identity.

萬華鏡
看板 잘 變하는 집
남의 집을 흉가라 하기는 미안하니까 그저 수수하게 간판 잘 변하는 집이라 하고 서울에 잇는 몃집의 이야기를 하기로 하자.

齋洞 監獄
재동(齋洞) 초입 전날 이름으로 말하면 남영위궁(南寧尉宮)이요. 지금의 별명은 재동감옥이다. 그 집은 원래 궁가(宮家)로 집 궁자(宮字)와 궁활궁자(窮) 음이 상사하야 그러한지 전날 윤씨(尹氏) 소유로 잇슬 때에도 남에게 잡혀먹고 간(間)마다 삭월세를 주어 그 대문안에 70여호가 살더니 조질현(曺설鉉)군이 사서 그만흔 사람을 다 내쫏고 굉대하게 개측하야 재동 아방궁이니 재동감옥이니 하는 말까지 들엿다. 그런데 불과 몃해에 조군이 또한 실패를 하야 그 집을 잡혀먹고 또 여러 사람에게 사글세를 놋케 되엿다.

安國洞 까치집
그리고 안국동(安國洞) 지금 허신씨병원(許信病院) 압집(鮮于全氏 운동구 상점자리)도 까치집 모양으로 어지간하게 주인이(영업주) 박귀고 안국동륙거리(安國洞六街里)에서 인사동(仁寺洞)으로 나려 가랴면 전날 三乎의원자리 1년에 삼사차 내지 오륙차는 간판이 갈린다.

仁寺洞 幻戱樓
또 그리로 나려가서 견지동으로 가는 신작로와 인사동 길모동이 삼각지점(三角地點)에 웃둑 소사잇는 벽돌집도 혹은 금희 악긔점(琴喜樂器店)으로 혹은 아세아카페로 현재에는 또 에-덴카페-그외에도 일년에 몃번식 간판이 변하야 맛치 활동사진에 사진 변하덧 한다. 이 집을 별명짓자면 인사동 환희루(仁寺洞幻戱樓)라고나 할까.

鐘路 좃도집
간판 잘 가는 집 이야기가 낫스니 말이지 종로 평양냉면집(宗老冷麪家)으로 드러가는 모퉁 이층집도 꽤 변화가 만흔 집이다. 선술집, 냉면집 또 식당으로 카페로 일년에도 몃차례-거짓말을 봇탠다. 한달에도 몃번식 간판이 갈닌다. 대체 그 집들은 집자리가 좃치 못하야 속히 실패를 하야 그만두는지 퍽 궁금하다. 이런 집은 별명짓자면 잠간 동안 영업처이니까 좃도집이라고도 할 것 수 잇다.＜59＞
