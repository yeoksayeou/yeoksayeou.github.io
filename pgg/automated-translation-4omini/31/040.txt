一問一答記(1)  
朴昌薰  

Reporter: How many times do doctors wash their hands in a day?  
Park: Oh, don't say such things. While doctors certainly wash their hands more often than others, it also varies depending on the patients’ conditions, so it cannot be standardized. As for myself, being a surgeon, on average, when patients are numerous, I probably wash my hands about 40 to 50 times a day, and sometimes even over a hundred times.

朴昌薰

記者, 醫師는 하루에 손을 몃 번이나 씻나요.
朴, 원-별말을 다 무르시우. 醫師가 물론 다른 사람보다는 손을 만히 씻지만은 그것도 환자의 만코 적은 것을 따러서 다르닛가 일정할 수야 잇슴닛가. 나로 말하면 外科醫요 환자가 만흐닛가 아마 평균치면 하루에 보통 4,50번 만흔 때는 백여 번은 되지요.
