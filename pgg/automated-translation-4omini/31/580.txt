Editorial Scribbles  

October 1 - It is the 10th day of the 8th month by the lunar calendar. Only five nights separate us from the first significant holiday of the year, the 8th month. In the countryside, farmers celebrate this festive occasion with new rice cake (송편) and fresh sweet liquor (신청주), while we present this special autumn issue to you.  

As this is the special autumn issue, its content is comparatively rich. Articles like "Busy People" by Sin Tae-ik about the Japanese Privy Council, "The Driving Force of Changes in the Korean Economy" by Ju Yo-han, "Record of Politics in Northern China" by Kim Se-yong, "Explaining the Red Movement of Chinese Farmers" by Yang Yun-sik, and the "Explanation of Theft Prevention Methods" by Yang are all worth reading. Particularly, Yang's explanation of theft prevention methods, having been in practice for just a few days, is certainly an article you must read to understand its contents. (Cha)  

The exhibition for prospective brides published monthly by our magazine has garnered enthusiastic responses from general readers. Having concluded in the last issue, this issue instead features "Prospective Grooms Exhibition," a series that will continue each month. This article will undoubtedly attract not just general readers but also those maidens in courtship who must read it for reference. (Park)  

Beginning from last month's issue, we started to publish regional marches monthly, which have received immense popularity among local readers. The previous issue featured the Pyongyang march, and this issue features the Daegu march. Since the writer is from Daegu, it discusses the past and present of Daegu without missing any details, providing a thorough discussion and review. Everyone in Daegu should read this; even those interested in the actual conditions of the city must take the time to read it. (Kim)  

In next month’s issue, we plan to feature the Kaesong march. We hope that the people of Kaesong will provide ample materials directly or indirectly for this article, and we encourage readers with any relevant material about Kaesong to submit it. (Choi)

**Summary**  

This editorial reflects on the publication of a special autumn issue while connecting the significance of the season with traditional celebrations in Korea. It highlights various articles discussing socio-political topics, including the implications of economic changes, local content that resonates with readers, and upcoming exhibitions tied to marriage prospects. This piece is of particular interest in colonial history, illustrating how cultural traditions intertwined with contemporary societal issues and the media's role in shaping public discourse during a period of Japanese colonial rule in Korea.

編輯落書
10月 1日- 陰曆으로 8月10日이다. 1年中 第一 조흔 名節인 8月가외도 겨우 닷새밤을 隔하얏다. 시골 農家에서는 올벼송편에 新淸酒를 비저서 이 名節마지를 하지만은 우리는 이 新秋特輯號를 내여 여러분에게 드리게 되엿다.

이번은 新秋特輯號이니만치 內容이 前보다 比較的 充實하다.『忙中閒人』의 日本 樞密院의 解剖 申泰翊 氏의 朝鮮 經濟 變動의 原動力 朱耀翰 氏의 中國 北方政 十日 天下記 金世鎔 氏에 中國農民의 赤色 運動 楊潤植 氏의 盜難防止法 解釋 等等은 누구나 한번 읽을 만한 評論인 중 特히 楊氏의 盜難防止 法解釋은 그 怪法이 實施된지 不過 몃칠인 오늘에 잇서 그 內容이 어떠한 것인지 반드시 읽어 알아야만 될 記事다.(車)

本誌에 每月 續載하든 雙S君의 新婦 候補者 展覽會는 一般 讀者에게 白熱的 歡迎을 밧다가 前號에 끗을 마치고 이번 號에는 그 대신으로 『新郞 候補者 展覽會』가 또 每月 續載하게 되엿다. 一般讀者는 勿論이어니와 求婚中에 잇는 處女 總角 等은 滋味보다도 參考로 꼭 읽어야 만 될것이다. (朴)

前月號부터서 始作하야 每月 揭載하게 된 地方 行進曲  記事는 特히 地方여러분에게 大歡迎을 밧게 되엿다. 前號 平壤 行進曲도 그랫지만은 이번 大邱 行進曲은 執筆者가 全部 大邱人士이니만치 大邱의 今昔實事情을 조곰도 틀님업고 숭김업시 자서히 들어서 討論 論評하얏다. 大邱의 여러분은 勿論이어니와 大邱 實況을 잘 알고저 하는 이 또한 이것을 꼭 읽어야 될 것이다. (金)

來月號에는 또 開城 行進曲을 揭載하기로 內定이 되엿다. 開城 人士는 直接 間接으로 記事의 만흔 材料를 供給하야 주기를 바라거니와 讀者中으로도 開城에 關한 記事 材料가 잇스면 投稿하야 주기를 바란다. (蔡)<176>
