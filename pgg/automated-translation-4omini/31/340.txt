葉書通信  
危險한 曲橋  

In our village, the curved bridge (the first bridge east of the Hong Bridge) that crosses the steel tunnel was repaired once about ten years ago and has since been left as is. Now, due to the decaying of the beams and boards, it has become quite dangerous, and anyone who is just a little inattentive while crossing might easily fall and be injured. Is the Construction Bureau of Seoul sleeping even in this autumn breeze? If the city hall enforced taxes as strictly as it does, it could certainly be done. My point is that the Seoul City Mayor or the Construction Bureau Chief should come out and try to see if they can perform acrobatics on the curved bridge as a test. (Sampo, Samgak-dong, Kim Saeng)

尖端은 다 무엇이요  

These days, newspapers and magazines are filled with the term "尖端" (suhda), but what exactly does尖端 mean? Is it something sharp, like a pointed nib, the tip of a high-heeled shoe of western-style girls, or the tip of a western man's nose? There’s this saying that if Christians (普天敎徒) find something sharp, they won’t cut their hair, yet nowadays, even writers are believed to be practicing this belief. They say wearing wide-legged pants makes one modern, but why do they say尖端? If they just use that term with a bit of refinement, it would be better. (Pyongyang, One Reader)

망측한 男子들  

At the water springs of Chwiun Pavilion or the water springs of Gajun Cave, the so-called cold baths are taken, and in the morning, men gather and go completely naked and cause a ruckus. How can we women go to drink that spring water in such embarrassment? While I understand men being men, if they had a bit of shame or civic consciousness, they wouldn’t behave so rudely as to be naked in front of others who drink from the same well. Such men should be grabbed and sent off to a nudist country like the South Sea Islands after getting their bottoms whipped 300 times. (Seoul, Gahoe-dong, One Woman)

속지마시요  

Recently, on the streets, there have been advertisements for various organizations claiming they are recruiting men and women with promises of a monthly salary of 50 to 100 won or more. Having suffered from the pain of not having a job, I felt this was a stroke of luck, as if I had come back to life like a resurrected parent; however, I soon found out they required a guarantee deposit of 200 won.  

But I must say, the guarantee deposit is returned later. The monthly salary is not what it seems; if one does their job as stated in the advertisement, the salary is only a deduction of what one amounts to earn in reality, and the so-called named organization is just a penniless outfit that lives off others' deposits. In the end, when I suffered a loss and tried to lodge a complaint, nothing could be done. Ultimately, everyone should be wary and not fall into such traps as I did. (Seoul, Jongno 2nd Street, Kim Saeng)  

**Summary**  

This article comprises a series of letters that express public grievances about various social issues such as the dilapidated state of infrastructure, societal mores, and deceptive employment schemes. The writers highlight concerns on unsafe public constructions, inappropriate public behavior among men, and the exploitation involved in job recruitment ads. These topics reveal the challenges and frustrations faced by Koreans under colonial rule in the 1930s, particularly the struggle against negligence from authorities and the financial hardships prevalent during this period, which would interest historians examining the societal impacts of Japanese occupation.

葉書通信
危險한 曲橋

우리 洞里에서 貫鐵洞을 건너가는 곱은 다리(廣橋東便 첫다리=曲橋)는 몃 10年前에 한번 修繕을 하고는 그대로 두어 只今에는 架木과 板木이 모도 썩어 문어진 까닭에 通行하는 사람이 조곰만 不注意하면 落傷하기는 念慮업시 되엿다. 京城府 土木課에는 이 가을바람에도 낮잠만 자는지 엇재 修繕할 생각이 업는가. 府民이 稅金안낸다고 强制執行하듯하면 그러케는 안이두겟지 내말이 正말이 안이거든 京城府尹이나 土木課長이 한번 出張을 하야 試驗으로 曲橋에서 曲馬團노름이나 하야보시요.(市內三角町 金生)

尖端은 다 무엇이요

요새 新聞이나 雜誌에는 前에 못보든 尖端이란 文字가 만흐니 대체 尖端이란 것이 무엇인가요. 송곳끗인가요 하이카라 女學生의 구두끗인가요. 西洋놈의 코끗인가요 普天敎徒들이 뾰죽한 수가 잇서야 산다고 상투를 안 깍는다더니 요새 글 쓰는 이들도 普天敎를 信仰함니까. 바지는 아래통이 넓다란 라팔바지를 입어야 모던式이라면서 아모데나 尖端소리는 웨 잘 쓰나요. 이 압흐로는 그것도 양렴으로 조곰식 썻스면 조흘 듯하외다.(平壤 一讀者)

망측한 男子들

翠雲亭 약수터에나 可潛洞약수터에는 所謂 冷水浴인지 무엇인지 한다압시고 아츰이면 男子들이 모도 벌개벗고 야단을 치니 우리가튼 女子들이야 어듸 창피하여서 약수물이나 먹으려 갈 수 잇나요. 女子도 女子이지만은 自己네 男子끼리라도 적으나 염지가 잇고 公德心이 잇스면 남이 먹는 우물엽헤서 벌개벗고 지저분하게는 할 수 잇글가요. 그런 男子는 그대로 업허 놋코서 볼기를 한 300度 따린 다음에 南洋群島나 물 건너 섬나라가튼 裸體國으로 定配를 보냇스면 조겟소이다.(市內嘉會洞 一女性)

속지마시요

요새에 거리마다 무슨社 무슨社라고 男女社員 大募集廣告를 붓첫스되 月收50圓以上 或은 100圓以上이라 하엿기에 職業이 업서 죽을 苦痛을 밧던 나는 이것이 웬 떡이냐하고 마치 죽엇다가 다시 사라온 父兄을 차자 가듯이 가보앗드니 及其也 身元保證金 200圓을 내라겟지요.
그러나 할슈잇슴니까. 保證金은 追後에 도로주고 月給50圓以上이 생긴다는 바람에 집에 잇는 如干 세간 나부랭이를 팔고 典當을 잡혀 갓다 주엇더니 웬걸요 月給이란 것은 月給이 안이라 自己가 廣告募集가튼 것을 잘 하면 그 割引이 그럿탄 말이고 또 名色社란 것은 아모 것도 업는 빈털털이로 남의 保證金만 살작 잘너 먹슴듸다. 也終에 損害를 보고 告訴를 하니 所用이 잇슴니까. 그저 누구나 츰부터 잘 注意하는 수 밧게는 업겟습듸다. 나처럼 꼭 속지말고 注意를 하십시요.(市內鍾路通 2丁目 金生)<91>
