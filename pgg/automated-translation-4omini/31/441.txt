저자	없음  
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Advertisement/Editorial Note)  

Recruitment for Branch Offices  
There are still several locations throughout all of Korea where there are no branch offices of the Kaebulsa (開闢社). This is an undistinguished situation for those regions. Please contact the main office now.  
Kaebulsa Local Department <145>  

**Summary**  

The article addresses the necessity for establishing branch offices of the Kaebulsa throughout various regions in Korea. Highlighting the lack of local representation, it underscores the company’s commitment to serve these areas better. The article reflects on the broader context of colonial Korea in the 1930s, shedding light on the challenges faced by Korean organizations operating under Japanese rule and their efforts to expand and provide services to local communities.
