**Summary**  

This article presents the new prize question for readers and announces the winners of the previous month's question. The overwhelming response shows significant engagement from the public, indicating a rather active participation in such activities despite the constraints of the colonial era. Historians might find interest in how these prize drawings provided a semblance of entertainment and involvement among the Korean populace under Japanese colonial rule, reflecting cultural expressions even amid oppression.
