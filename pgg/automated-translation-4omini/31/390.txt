푸라그미틔즘 (Pragmatism)   Practicalism. Utilitarianism. A doctrine championed by American James, stating that truth is only what is actually useful in our lives, and varies historically and by individual.  

푸라토닉러브 (Platonic love)  A love that is devoid of base carnal desires. An ideal love. The term derives from the love philosophy of the philosopher Plato.  

푸로짓트 (Prosit)  A phrase used when toasting, derived from Latin.  

푸로떡숀 (Production)  A work or an individual’s entrepreneurial activities, particularly in the context of a photography studio.  

푸로파간다 (Propaganda)  Dissemination of information. The socialist's way of spreading their ideology through printed materials or speeches, informing the public on matters the government deems necessary for public health, etc.  

푸로마이드 (Promide)  Non-decolorizing rough surface photography.  

푸로미스퀴틔 (Promiscuity)  The belief that in primitive human times, there were no marriages, and thus a chaotic mating system akin to that of beasts existed. Opposing theories are also present regarding this concept.  

페퍼민트 (Pepermint)  Mint. Green liquor. A stimulant for desire frequently consumed by the high-class party.  

페푸톤 (Pepton)  A substance formed when proteins dissolve in gastric juice.  

피카데리 (Piccadilly)  A bustling place in London, England. As it is known for its beautiful prostitutes, it can also refer to suspiciously dressed women acting in ways that draw attention.  

퍼-쇼너리즘 (Personalism)  The doctrine of personality. A philosophical view that considers being, or psychological states, in the same realm as personality or as the same entity.  

푸틔부르조아 (Petit-Bourgeois)  A pejorative term in French referring to the middle class, including small farmers, small manufacturers, small merchants, petty bourgeoisie, scholars and lawyers, and high salaried employees.  

푸로파-타리아-트 (Propertariat)  The antonym of proletariat, signifying the owning class or bourgeoisie.  

푸로필 (Profil)  The French term meaning profile, side view, or outline. It refers to the cross-sections or side diagrams of all things.  

푸로렛트컬트 (Proletcult)  An abbreviation for Proletarian Culture (Proletarian Culture), often shortened to Proculte. It advocates for the independent organization of proletariat education based on Marxism.  

푸롤로그 (Prologue)  The opposite of epilogue in English. It means preface or prologue. Especially in theatrical terminology, it refers to the introduction of the entire play sung by an actor just before it begins, a remnant of ancient drama that is still seen in some forms today, also known as the front art.  

푸롬푸터 (Prompter)  An English term, also used in theater. It refers to the role of someone who prompts an actor by quietly reading from the script when they forget their lines.  

페-졘트 (Pageant)  Translated as public theater or outdoor theater. An English term used to describe performances conducted by public groups or performances using the natural outdoor backdrop made available to the public. It has become frequently used in the latter sense in recent years.  

閉鎖組合 (Closed Union)  A labor union composed of workers bound by employment contracts with a specific factory or local capitalists, which limits union membership to monopolize employment opportunities. <123>  

**Summary**  

This article presents a compilation of newly introduced words, explaining their meanings and contexts. Among the terms discussed are philosophical concepts such as "Pragmatism" and "Personalism," as well as social terms like "Proletariat" and "Petit-Bourgeois." The significance of these terms lies in their relevance to the socio-political climate of the time, reflecting the ideological battles in colonial Korea. Historians may find the discussion of Marxist influences particularly interesting as it highlights the intellectual currents that shaped the discourse in this period under colonial rule.

新語大辭典(六)
프 部

푸라그미틔즘(Pragmatism)   實際主義. 實用主義. 米國 쩸스의 主唱한 바 卽 眞理란 우리들 生活에 所用되는 것뿐으로 時代와 사람에 따라 어떠케든지 變하는 것이란 說.

푸라토닉러브(Platonic-love)  高潔淸淨한 肉慾을 떠난 戀愛. 理想的 戀愛. 哲人 푸라톤의 戀愛 思想에서 나온 말.

푸로짓트(Prosit)  祈盃등을 올닐 때 쓰는 말.(拉語)

푸로떡숀(Production)  作品. 또는 個人 經營의 活動寫眞撮影所.

푸로파간다(Propaganda)  宣傳. 社會主義者가 印刷物로써 主義를 傳播하고 政府가 衛生上 注意하기 爲하야 印刷物이나 講演을 通하야 民衆에 알니는 것等.

푸로마이드(Promide)  不變色粗面寫眞.

푸로미스퀴틔-(Promiscuity)  人類의 原始 時代에는 婚姻關係란 것이 업고 禽獸와 가튼 亂婚時代가 잇섯다는 說. 이 說에는 만흔 反對論도 잇다.

페퍼민트(Pepermint)  薄荷. 푸른 술. 하이칼라 黨이 마시는 性慾 昻騰制.

페푸톤(Pepton)  胃膓속에서 蛋白質이 페푸론-胃腋-에 溶解되여서 되는 것.

피카데리-(Picadilly)  英國倫敦의 繁華한 곳. 아름다운 賣春婦가 만히 나오는 곳임으로 行動이 좀 수상한 美裝의 婦人을 가르치는 말도 된다.

퍼-쇼너리즘(Personalism)
人格說. 一切의 統一者로써 哲學的 意味로 卽 實在나 心理狀態 等을 人格과 同樣 或은 同一한 것으로 보는 思考方法.

푸틔부르조아(Petit-Bourgeais)
佛語. 中間階級에 對한 嘲罵的 名稱. 自作小農, 小製造業者, 小商人, 小資産家, 文士辯護士, 高級 살러리맨들이 여긔 屬한다.

푸로파-타리아-트(Propertariat)
푸로레타리아-트의 反對語로 有産階級-부르조아지-란 말. 獨語.

푸로필(Profil)
佛語. 橫顔, 半面, 側面, 輪廓等으로 譯한다. 이 意味에서 모든 事物의 縱斷面 乃至 側面圖를 일옴.

푸로렛트컬트(Proletcult)
푸로레타리안컬츄어(Proletarian Culture)의 縮語로 더 줄여서 푸로컬트라고도 한다. 無産者 敎育이란 말. 맑스主義에 立脚하야 푸로階級的 敎育 獨立的으로 組織하자는 主張이다.

푸롤로그(Prologue)
에피로그의 對 英語. 序言. 序詞란 뜻. 特히 演劇用語로써는 開幕直前 俳優가 全篇을 紹介하는 詩를 노래한 古代演劇의 遺風으로 今日에 와서도 形式은 달을 망정 종종 볼 수 잇는 것이다. 前藝라고도 한다.

푸롬푸터(Prompter)
英語. 亦是 演劇用語. 俳優가 臺詞를 니저 바리든지 더듬든지 할 때 그게 생각나도록 뒤에서 적은 소리로 脚本을 읽어주는 役.

페-졘트(Pageant)
公共劇 又는 野外劇이라고 譯한다. 英語. 公共團體에 依해서 演出되는 劇을 일옴. 또는 俳優가 野外의 自然을 利用하야 그것을 背景으로 하고 一般에게 公開하는 演劇. 近年에는 後者의 意味로 만히 쓴다.

閉鎖組合
어느 工場이나 또는 어느 지방의 資本家와 雇傭契約을 맷고 잇는 職別勞働組合가튼 것이 그 雇用을 獨占하기 爲하야 組合員을 制限하고 잇는 組合을 일음.<123>
