잡지명	별건곤  
발행일	1930년 10월 01일  
기사제목	가을들에 서서 (Standing in Autumn Fields)  
필자	로아  
기사형태	시 (Poem)  

In the place where field chrysanthemums bloom, the scent of water is fragrant. 

Has the rugged rock gone to float away in the summer rains? 

The blue sky reflects the rain, and the water is clear, pouring down.

Clamor, clamor, the sound of a new day rises in each field. 

The scarecrow shakes the bell. 

Who is it that planted this rice and weeded it out? 

The glutinous rice is ripe and has come to fruition.

On the mountain, the mulberries are far away. 

Last year at this time, 

With a towel on my head and a basket in hand, 

Where has the woman who picked mulberries gone? 

Has she gone to the distant village? To Bukgando, perhaps?

Oh, restless heart of the sleepless young man, 

How the Milky Way weeps at night, clinging to each drop of dew.


-1930. 9. 9-

<162>  

**Summary**  

This poem captures the essence of autumn in a rural landscape, touching upon themes of nature, nostalgia, and the human connection to the land through agricultural practices. It reflects the lives of those harvesting and their memories intertwined with the seasons. Historians may find this poem revealing in the context of Korean colonial history, as it showcases the everyday lives and struggles of Koreans during a time of deep political and social upheaval.

잡지명	별건곤 제33호
발행일	1930년 10월 01일
기사제목	가을들에 서서
필자	로아
기사형태	시

가을들에 서서
						로아
들국화 핀 곳에 물소래 향긔롭다.
험상구진 바위는
장마통에 떠나려갓나
푸른하늘 빗친하늘 물빗 맑아 몰을내라.

우여- 우여- 들마다 새날니는 소리
벼직히는 허재비 종을 흔드네
이 벼 심고 김매든 이 누구란 말가
찰베올베 다 익어 찻고 잇구나.

산에는 멀구다래 작년 이맘때
머리에 수건쓰고 바구미 안고
멀구다래 따든 처잔 어데로 갓나.
먼 고을 싀깁갓나 북간도 갓나.

잠 못자는 뒤ㅅ집총각 마음아라서
은하수 빗긴 밤을 풀버레만 우네.
-1930. 9. 9-
