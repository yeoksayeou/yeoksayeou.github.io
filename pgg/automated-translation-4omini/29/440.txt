前金  
Doctor: "Where do you feel discomfort?"  
Patient: "I just have a memory lapse and don't need a diagnosis. Please give me a prescription."  
Doctor: "Then pay the front money. Is your illness really an illness?" <176>  

**Summary**  

In this brief dialogue, a doctor and a patient discuss the concept of paying "front money" before medical treatment. The patient expresses a minor concern, suggesting it is not a significant illness, highlighting the transactional nature of medical consultations at the time. This gives insight into colonial Korea's healthcare system and the financial barriers that affected access to medical care, reflecting the broader socio-economic challenges of that era.
