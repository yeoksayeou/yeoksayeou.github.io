잡지명	별건곤  
발행일	1930년 08월 01일  
기사제목	으스름 달밤 (Twilight Moonlit Night)  
필자	로아 (Roah)  
기사형태	시 (Poem)  

Twilight Moonlit Night  

Starry maids   

Having put on their long skirts  
Are slow to come out.  

With their adorned faces,  
The moon draws them forth from the doorway.  

-7.4.-<109>  

**Summary**  

The poem captures the serene imagery of a twilight night as starry maidens, adorned in their traditional long skirts, slowly emerge under the enchanting moonlight. The delicate layers of visual and sensory details demonstrate a traditional Korean aesthetic, emphasizing beauty and the allure of nature during twilight. This piece reflects the gender roles and cultural expressions of the time, offering insight into the societal expectations of women in late colonial Korea. Historians might find the representation of women's attire and their presence in public spaces notable when studying the shifts in gender dynamics during Korea's colonial period.
