기사제목	銀魚 (Silver Fish)  
필자	로아 (Roah)  
기사형태	시 (Poem)  

Silver Fish

The creek water sparkles,  
The stones gleam.  

The stream is not even a hundred ri (里) long,  
But it's a boat that swallowed pearls, glistening silver. <109>  

**Summary**

This poem captures the beauty of a creek with sparkling water and shining stones, comparing its allure to a boat that has swallowed pearls, thereby suggesting a sense of richness and hidden depth in the natural world. The imagery evokes a serene yet vibrant environment that resonates with the reader's senses. From a historical perspective, the poetic expressions of nature reflect a longing for identity and cultural richness during the colonial period, emphasizing a complex relationship with nature and a yearning for beauty amidst hardships.
