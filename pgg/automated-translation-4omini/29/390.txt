**Summary**  

This article presents humorous comic strips that depict intriguing situations, such as a misunderstanding at a restaurant and a Chinese acrobat troupe engaging with Western culture by opening a café. The reference to coinage and the misprints reflect a playful attitude towards popular culture of the time. Historians might find the blending of Western and Eastern cultural elements significant in understanding the challenges and adaptations of Korea during the Japanese colonial period, showcasing a humorous cross-cultural interaction amidst a complex sociopolitical landscape.
