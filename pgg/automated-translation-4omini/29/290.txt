잡지명	별건곤

발행일	1930년 08월 01일

기사제목	噴水 (Fountain)

필자	로아 (Roah)

기사형태	시 (Poem)

噴水

Waterweed, behold!

Drooping and bending

The waterweed.

The shoots of the weed decorously

Tangled with goldfish. <109> 


**Summary**

The poem "噴水" presents a whimsical image of waterweed bending and curling, intertwined with goldfish. It captures a moment of serene beauty in nature, potentially reflecting themes of tranquility and the delicate balance of life. For historians, this poem offers a glimpse into the cultural perceptions of nature during the colonial period in Korea, illuminating how natural imagery was used to express emotions and aesthetics amidst the socio-political challenges of the time.
