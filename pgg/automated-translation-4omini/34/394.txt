잡지명	별건곤  
발행일	1931년 01월 01일  
기사제목	朱題 (Red Title)  
필자	하윤  
기사형태	시 (Poem)  

In the darkness engulfed by the stars,  
A single star twinkles brightly.  
All day long, I bow my head,  
Moving my feet, wondering where I might go. <151>  

**Summary**  

The poem expresses a sense of introspection and existential wonder, using celestial imagery to reflect on the human condition amidst darkness. The most revealing aspect for historians might be how such sentiments were articulated in a period of colonial rule in Korea, hinting at the struggle for identity and solace during times of despair. The use of nature and the cosmos can be interpreted as a subtle rebellion against the oppressive political climate.
