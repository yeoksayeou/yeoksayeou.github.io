Editorial Note  

From the New Year, for the sake of the new fortune of your household, please do these two things, no matter how difficult it may be.  

Instead of lamenting that your wife is suffocating, make sure to read "New Woman" every month. If you don't know it, go ahead and read it and introduce it to your daughters as well. In less than a year, you will become a knowledgeable housekeeper, and your family will be renewed.  

Do not be satisfied with school studies and encourage your children to read "Children's Monthly" every month. In no time, you will have individuals more knowledgeable than teachers. Furthermore, this New Year edition comes with a special supplement. <107>  

**Summary**  

The editorial note emphasizes the importance of educating wives and children through appropriate reading material, encouraging a progressive view on women's roles and children’s education. It illustrates a shift in social norms during the colonial period in Korea, highlighting a push toward modernization and enlightenment of household roles. Historians might find it revealing of the cultural shifts occurring in Korea during this time, as the publication promotes literacy and education as pathways to family renewal and societal advancement.

신년부터 당신 宅의 새로운 福運을 위하야 아모리 어려워도 이 두 가지만은 꼭 실행하십시오.
안해가 갑갑하다 한탄 말고 『新女性』을 매월 닑히십시오. 모르면 닑어서 들녀라도 주십시오. 단 일년 못가서 지식만흔 주부가 되고 당신의 가정이 새로워질 것임니다.
