誌上顧問欄  
A new column for advice has been established. Please use it in accordance with the following regulations.  

1. Advisory Matters - Excluding special issues such as law and medicine, only matters that are difficult to resolve alone in our daily life.  

2. Materials - Official postcards.  

3. Others - Inquiries should be simple and clear, and anonymity is allowed for names. <146>  

Summary  

This article introduces a new advice column aimed at addressing common issues faced in daily life, excluding specialized topics like law and medicine. It highlights the importance of providing a platform for readers who might struggle to resolve personal conflicts or dilemmas independently. This initiative may reflect the broader social conditions of the time, emphasizing the communal need for guidance during the colonial period in Korea, which is of interest to historians examining societal interactions and supports available to individuals under colonial rule.
