Q: I heard you visit fish soup places, so which is the best fish soup restaurant in Seoul?  
A: While all the fish soup restaurants around Dongdaemun (東大門) are good, I think the best one is Changchun House (長春屋) for fish soup. <48>

It reflects the intimate connection of Koreans with their culinary traditions, even during the colonial period.
