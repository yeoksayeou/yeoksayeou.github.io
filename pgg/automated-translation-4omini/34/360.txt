新春大懸賞  
As you enjoy looking at this photo on the peaceful night of the first day of the new year, try to guess the names of these beauties. (For detailed rules, please refer to this issue on page 128.) <135>  

**Summary**  

This article presents a contest for readers to guess the names of beautiful women featured in a photograph, popularizing engagement with the magazine's content. The contest reflects the cultural practices of the time, where beauty and social contests were common in Japanese-occupied Korea. Historians may find interest in how such activities showcase the blending of colonial influences with traditional Korean social practices, indicating a cultural interaction during the colonial period.
