발행일	1931년 01월 01일  
기사제목	瞬間 (Moment)  
필자	하윤 (Ha Yun)  
기사형태	시 (Poem)  

I want to live in a country where there are no newspapers,  
To live peacefully without knowing any news.  

I want to live in a country where there is no fear,  
This world is too talkative anyway. <151>  

**Summary**  

The poem expresses a longing for a peaceful existence away from the chaos of contemporary life, characterized by an absence of news and fear. It reflects a profound desire to escape from the burdens of reality, which resonates with the experiences of many during a challenging historical period in Korea. Historians might find this particularly revealing of the sentiments during Japanese colonial rule, illustrating the yearning for tranquility in the face of socio-political turmoil.
