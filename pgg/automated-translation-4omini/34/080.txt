Q&A Record 1

Song Jin-woo

Recorder: During the course of your daily life at the newspaper office, when is the most pleasant time?

Song: It is the moment when the newspaper is finished and I can hear the sound of the rotary press. When I hear the sound of the press, it feels like everything for that day has come together. <45>  

**Summary**

This article features a brief exchange between a recorder and Song Jin-woo, highlighting the satisfaction and excitement felt by journalists upon hearing the rotary press. This moment symbolizes a culmination of hard work and the anticipation of disseminating news. Historians might find this glimpse into the interplay of journalism and colonial life interesting, revealing the connections between daily experiences and the broader context of news production in colonial Korea.
