기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Advertisement/Editorial)  

New Year's Great Prize Recruitment  

◇On page 135 of this issue, there are five beautiful ladies. If you do not believe it, please look again; they are all modern beauties and renowned courtesans of our time.  

◇Do not just look at them, but try to uncover their ages.  

◇They are twenty-seven (27), nineteen (19), twenty (20), and eighteen (18) years old respectively. However, which lady corresponds to which age, you must determine.  

◇Those who have a guess should write it down on the answer sheet provided and clearly indicate your address and name, to be sent to this company by the 20th of this month.  

◇Thirty correct answers will receive a special "Yut" (윳) as a New Year’s game prize from our company, and one of those will be selected randomly to receive the grand prize, a "Serudurumaki" (세루두루막이).  

**Summary**  

The article announces a contest inviting readers to guess the ages of five beautiful women featured in the publication, promising prizes for correct answers. This reflects the societal norms and interests during the colonial era, showcasing the objectification of women and the blending of entertainment with consumer culture in the 1930s Korea. The mention of terms like "courtesans" indicates the continued prominence of traditional gender roles and entertainment forms under colonial rule, which could be of interest to historians studying the period.
