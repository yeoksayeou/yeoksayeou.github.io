발행일	1931년 01월 01일  
기사제목	안개의 誘惑 (The Temptation of Fog)  
필자	로아  
기사형태	시 (Poem)  

The fog whispers to the fog  
Gently, softly, it falls, entwines  
Clarity, clarity.  

On a suffocating winter night  
A sound of a creaking cart hangs above,  
A child’s laughter turns into a distant echo.  

Is it a playful girl?  
Covering her eyes and sealing her lips,  
Does she tell me not to leave……………  

In this heart-empty night without stars,  
Beneath the blanket of your smile,  
I wish to linger just more.  

Do not stir my heart,  
Before this night fades away, a thousand miles will remain,  
Do not betray my longing.  
1930, December – on a night thick with fog – <151>  

**Summary**  

The poem "The Temptation of Fog" expresses a poignant longing amidst a winter night filled with fog, evoking emotions of isolation and desire. The imagery and gentle tone showcase a yearning to remain in a moment of warmth and comfort while acknowledging the passage of time and inevitable departure. This piece may intrigue historians tracing the cultural expressions of emotions in colonial Korea, as it reflects the tender yet melancholic sentiments amidst the struggles of that era, highlighting how art served as an emotional refuge during challenging times.
