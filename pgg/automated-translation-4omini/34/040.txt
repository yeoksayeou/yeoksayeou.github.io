Smoking Room  
Even in times of recession, the end of the year and the beginning of the new year come around as abundantly as ever, without any difference from before, so it seems that the word recession is a term only used among us Koreans.  

Among them, Koreans age two years at once since the lunar year-end has just passed, and though the phrase about gently aging two years is merely a fine saying, truly, by next year my black hair may turn gray.  

The lunar new year is even more numerous, and those opposing the solar new year also attend year-end parties, so it seems they partake in the practices of the solar calendar while only pretending otherwise.  

Despite attempts at the Round Table Conference in the British Indian colonies, it seems that a complete resolution will not emerge, and it may be necessary to have a Corner Table Conference instead. As long as there is no restraint of British ambitions, such meetings will always remain unresolved; is resolving matters with words rather than with a sword a wise strategy?  

After enduring in vain and engaging in various movements, Feng Yuxiang and Yan Xishan of China are said to have fled to Japan. From now on, it seems Feng Yuxiang will establish power in Fengdu Hell while Yan Xishan may try to gain influence in Yanluo Kingdom.  

The Wushe Incident in Taiwan is said to remain unclear, even when one listens to reports from the Taiwan Governor-General's office; whether the incident occurred in Wushe or whether the truth is as murky as five miles in a fog is unclear.  

Not long ago, Yoon Sim-deok, who died tragically with Kim Yu-jin at Hyeonhaitan, is rumored to continue her studies, with Kim writing plays and Yoon studying vocal music in Italy, Rome, as their overseas activities spread.  

The truth of the matter remains uncertain as Kim's brother has reportedly submitted a petition to the External Affairs Office of the Governor-General. To find out the truth, one must check the results of their investigations, but while thorough searches may not retrieve the living, they might dig up the bones. However, thoroughly searching the seabed will likely be impossible.  

A certain group from the Five-Man Organization attacked the police station at the newly established Georyongsan, killing two and seriously injuring one; they are likely individuals dispatched from the southern province of Jeongdo.  

The governor of Changwon reportedly instructed the local chiefs not to impose duties on the citizens recklessly during these difficult times. Although he appears to be a unique governor from the perspective of the people, on the other hand, he may also be seen as an unyielding governor.  

At the Oshin Commercial Store, which is said to be the largest department store for Koreans in the Jongno area, it appears that since a while back, friends from abroad have been taking up nearly all the space, resembling the likes of the second Mitsukoshi or second Dingziya, which is truly disheartening.  

Rural farmers are feeling overwhelmed by taxes, leading them to sell rice at a loss; however, the authorities are still presenting discussions about rice price countermeasures in the news, implying that the countermeasures are preparations for next autumn—likely...<33>  

  

**Summary**  

This article reflects on the socio-economic conditions in Korea during the early 1930s, particularly touching upon the themes of recession, cultural practices regarding New Year celebrations, and the political turmoil affecting China and Taiwan. It critically assesses the local governance of Changwon and the struggles faced by the Korean populace under colonial rule. Historians might find the commentary on the interrelation between cultural practices and economic hardships, as well as the mention of significant political figures and events, particularly revealing in understanding the societal dynamics of colonial Korea.

吸煙室
不景氣-不景氣하야도 歲末 歲初에 진고개구 경을 하면 그 前보다 조금도 다름이 업시 풍성풍성하니 結局不景氣란 말은 우리 朝鮮사람끼리만 쓰는 말인 것갓다.

그 중에 조선사람은 이중으로 또 陰曆歲末이 갓차워오니 일년에 나히를 두 살식 먹어서 쉬 늙는다는 말은 才談에 不過한 말이지만 참말 빗달년에 검은 머리가 파뿌리가 될 지경이다.

二重過歲 말이 낫스니 말이지 陽曆過歲를 반대하는 사람들도 陽曆歲末에 所謂忘年會에는 참가하는 사람이 만히 잇스니 놀고 먹는 것만은 陽曆實行을 한단 말인가.

英印의 圓卓會議는 암만 하야도 圓滿한 해결이 나지 안으니 다시 角卓會議나 하야보지 圓卓이나 角卓이나 영국의 野慾이 업서지기 前에는 그 회의는 언제나 不圓滿할 것이니 舌보다 칼 解決이 上策이 안일가.

한참동안 野心을 버틔고 별 運動을 다 하던 중국의 馮玉祥과 閻錫山은 日本으로 亡命을 한다나. 이제부터 馮玉祥은 馮都地獄 閻錫山은 閻羅王國에나 가서 세력을 잡아볼가.

臺灣의 霧社事件은 臺灣總督府當局者의 보고를 드러도 역시 그 眞相이 不明하다고 한다. 事件이 霧社에서 發生되엿스닛가 眞相도 五里霧中인가.

몃해 前에 玄海灘에서 金祐鎭과 情死한 尹心悳은 事實죽지를 안코 男女가 共히 伊太利 서울 로-마에 가서 金은 劇作, 尹은 聲樂硏究를 한다는 所聞이 자자하다.

사실의 眞否는 金의 親弟가 이미 總督府外事課에 搜索請願을 提出하얏다닛가 搜索의 결과를 보와야 알겟지만은 수색을 철저하게 잘만 하면 산사람은 못 차저도 주근 뼉다귀는 찻겟지. 그러나 海底搜索까지는 어려울 터이니 奈何.

五人組의 某團이 鷄龍山新都警察官駐在所를 襲擊하야 二名을 죽이고 一명은 重傷케 하얏다니 아마 南朝鮮鄭道令國에서 特派한 사람들인게지.

昌原某郡守는 이런 不景氣時代에 人民에개 함부로 賦役을 식히지 말라고 各面長에게 訓示를 식혓다 한다. 民間으로 보면 奇特한 군수지만은 다른 편으로 보면 不逞郡守-

鍾路一帶에서 朝鮮사람 百貨店으로 제일 크다는 0信商店의 內面에는 별서부터 물건너 친구가 차지를 하고 잇서서 第二三越이나 第二丁子屋 비슷하게 되엿다니 역시 寒心千萬.

시골 농민들은 稅金에 쫄이어서 쌀을 헐가로 별서 다 팔아 업샛는데 當局에서는 只今에도 米價對策講究說을 新聞에 각금 發表하니 그 대책은 明年 가을 豫備對策인 게지-아마...<33>
