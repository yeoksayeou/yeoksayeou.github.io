별에 별건곤

Alcohol Consumption in the Prohibition Country

In America, which proudly calls itself a prohibition country, it is said that during the year from July of last year to June of this year, around 20 million 'seok' (석) of alcohol were consumed. Breaking it down, soju (소주) accounted for 1.55 million 'seok,' wine (葡萄酒) for 2.5 million 'seok,' and beer (맥주) for 15 million 'seok.' The average per person across the nation is about 4 'seong' (升)....

If it's a prohibition country, then what if it were not?

A Disgraceful Child

In Ohio, there is a house where the boy is barely six years old and is five 'chae' (치) tall... Until now, there hasn't been anything particularly amazing about this child, but he has a thick black mustache and even smokes a cigarette like a goat. Seeing that physique, behind the whiskers while holding a long cigarette, is certainly something one would want to see once. It's true that his physical strength and intelligence seem quite developed; he surely is qualified as a disgraceful child.

White Snow Near the Equator

Speaking of Bogotá, it is not far from the equator, about 300 ri (리) away. In that place, it does not snow or hail, and such nouns are virtually unheard of. However, recently snow and hail fell to such an extent that it reached three 'cheok' (尺). The Earth has undergone numerous changes, going from fields (桑田) to oceans (碧海) several times, but for hail and snow to occur near the equator is exceptionally noteworthy. Is our Earth about to enter another glacial age?

Ignorance of Americans

There are no people as superstitiously fanatical and thus ignorant as American friends. It's all the more laughable because mechanics (メカニズム) originated there. Recently, in Philadelphia, a Presbyterian elder was said to have held a gathering without any rainfall for prayer.

The House of 12,000 Years Ago

From the University of Chicago in America, an exploration to Asia Minor has uncovered the remains of wooden structures from about 12,000 years ago near a place called Arashur (아렛슈어).

Russian Sexual Morality

A certain Dr. Robert Paret from America, while traveling through various countries, returned and criticized the sexual morality of labor and farmer Russia (勞農露亞亞) saying, "In Russia, there are no restrictions regarding sexual issues. While it may seem that there is indulgence due to the lack of regulations, conversely, individual awareness is quite strong. Thus, Russia has developed the best sexual morality in the world." What is your opinion on this?

Women in India!

Recently in India, over sixty dancers staged a protest against the British. Korean women could feel a sense of superiority when looking at an Indian woman, whose face is flat, lips protruding, and nose is flat, compared to themselves, correct? What do you think? <147>

**Summary**

This article discusses the paradoxes surrounding alcohol consumption in the United States during Prohibition, highlights a peculiar case of a young child in Ohio, the anomaly of snowfall near the equator, and perceptions of morality in different countries, particularly Russia and India. It sheds light on the ignorance of Americans in religious and social matters, which reveals a cultural perspective during Korea's colonial period where Western influences were both imitated and critiqued. Historians might find the juxtaposition of Western lifestyle and local commentary relevant in understanding Korea's identity during colonial rule.

禁酒國의 酒消費量

자칭 금주국이라고 남더러 자랑을 하는 미국에서 작년 7월부터 금년 6월까지 만1갠ㄴㄷㅇ안에 소비한 술이 2천만여석이라고 한다. 이것을 세분하여보면 우리스키-와뿌란데가 155만석, 葡萄酒가 250만석 맥주가 1500만석이라고 그리고 전미국의 한사람압해 평균이 4升....
금주국이니까 이러치 만일 그러치 안타면?

후레자식

미국 오하이오개의 어는 집 자제는 금년에 나희 겨우 여섯살, 키는 석자다섯치...여긔까지는 별로 신기할것이 업스나 이애가 턱아리 밋테 수염이 검실검실하게 나고 또 거긔다가 담배까지 염소모양으로 질걱한다. 그 체격을 하여가지고 수염을 쓰다듬으면서 여송연을 가로물고 잇는 꼴은 과연 한번 보고 십다. 체력과 뇌수도 어룬과 가치 발달이 된것도 사실 이만하면 후레자식의 자격을 충분.

赤道附近에 白雪

보고타라하면 적도에서 300리 밧게 아니되는 곳이다. 그곳에서는 눈이나 우박이 오기는 곳하고 그러한 名詞조차말죠차업다. 그런데 요지음 눈과 우박이 폭풍우에 불녀내려 3尺가량이나 째엿다. 지구가 그동안 만흔 변천이 잇서 桑田과 碧海가 서로 밧귀임이 여러번 잇섯것스나 적도부근에 우박과 눈이 왓다는 것은 넘우도 뜻밧게 일이다. 우리 지구에 빙하시대가 다시 한번 오려는지?

米國人의 無知

세상에 미국친구들처럼 종교적으로 맹신적이요 따러서 무지한 인종들은 업다.
메카니씀의 원산지가 그러하니 더구나 우습다는 것이다.
그런데 요지음 필델피아에서는 비가 아니온다고 야소교 장로 영감을 식혀서 긔우게를 지냇다든가.

萬二千年前의 집

미국시카고 대학에서 小亞細亞 지방으로 탐험을 갓다가 아렛슈어!라는 곳에서 지금으로부터 일만2천년전에 니은 목조건축을 발굴하엿다.

露西亞의 性道德

미국 로버-트, 빠-레트라는 박사영감이 세계각국을 유람하다가 도라와서 勞農露亞亞의 성도덕을 다음과 가치 비판하엿다 「로서아는 성문제 대하야 아무런 제재가 업다. 제잭가 업슴으로 일견에 방종할섯 가트나 그와는 반대로 각개인의 자각이 매우 엄하다. 그리하야 로서아는 세계에서 제일 성도덕이 발달이 되여잇다」고
여러분 엇덧소?

印度의 女性은!
