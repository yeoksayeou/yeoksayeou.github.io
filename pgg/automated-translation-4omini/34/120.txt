一問一答記5  
劉英俊  
Reporter: What is the most enjoyable and desirable thing at home?  
劉: The most pleasant thing is raising children. However, at the age of 40, I only have one daughter and still do not have a son. I have the greatest desire to have a son.<52>  

 
**Summary**  

The article features a dialogue with Liu Yeongjun, where he discusses his enjoyment in raising children, particularly expressing his desire to have a son since he only has a daughter at the age of 40. This excerpt reflects the traditional societal values of the time, where having male heirs was highly valued, providing insight into family dynamics during the colonial period. Historians might find it interesting as it underscores the gendered expectations of parenthood and familial responsibilities in early 20th-century Korea.
