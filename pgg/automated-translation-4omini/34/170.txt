Readers' New Year Greetings from Distinguished Individuals, in Place of New Year's Cards

As the sun rises in the East like it does every year, my heart once again finds a new path.

No matter how much one travels, life remains unchangeable, and it's not that this New Year is anything special.

- Kim Anseo

1. High Awareness as a Member of Society  
2. Economic Awareness of All People  
3. Scientific Approach to All Aspects of Life  
Let us all strive towards this.  
- Ryu Kwangnyeol

I hope to strive diligently in both reading and writing. And if circumstances permit, I also hope to try my hand at drawing from the start of this New Year.  
- Lee Taejun

Though I may seem insignificant, as I grow a year older, I find myself pondering it with mixed feelings of exhilaration and sorrow.  
- Ko Yeonghan

I hope that each individual in our society respects sincerity and merits, and that we develop a proper authority in public opinion that is even more feared than legal adjudication.  
- Kim Yeoseok

As comrades, we should firmly uphold trust to avoid the delusions that led to our past self-destruction and seek better outcomes in our endeavors.  
- Park Heedong

In a society lacking central authority, no words carry weight; nevertheless, there is no other feeling of change.  
- Park Seungbin

This is the year of the rabbit. It may seem a bit ‘centennial,’ but personally, it feels like a time too fast to catch up with emotions. I want to boldly step forward without fear.  
- Kim Namju

I hope to reconnect more personally with those with whom I've shared a long-distance relationship.  
- Lee Ikseong

The conduct and trends among students have become quite concerning. Nowadays, students show no enthusiasm for studying and are filled with lethargy, making one nostalgic for the camaraderie and righteousness of past students.  
- Choi Kweidong

Whether as individuals or organizations, I hope we can unite and progress through contacts; indeed, it's welcoming to see signs of such energies emerging within our society, as without cohesion, no authority can rise.  
- Jeong Daehyeon

Although the New Year doesn't carry an abrupt change, it’s hard to express in words; thus, rather than voicing my sentiments, I wish for the prosperous growth of all of you.  
- Jeong Inseok

From this New Year, let's prioritize liberation, beginning with ourselves.  
- Choi Rin

Let us not linger in empty theories but instead commit to realizing at least one practical task. Year after year, what good are superficial discussions without any real utility?  
- Han Longun

In the New Year, I wish for the presence of the "shin" flavor (辛味).  
- Kwon Sangro

As the year turns, the feeling of boundless emotions floods over me. I hope for a life infused with conviction, and I encourage you, dear readers, to do the same.  
- Kim Ilhan

Starting this year, I will handle all letters the same day they arrive.  
- Jo Giwan

Let us not be disheartened but instead strive forward.  
- Park Munhee

Every New Year, I aspire to create a new work, but often it doesn’t come to fruition. This year, I will put forth effort to at least attempt it.  
- Lee Sangbeom

New Year's, New Year's brings me—no, us— new courage again. Why? Because we can’t let our courage fade away.  
- Lee Seokyu

In short, I have no particular plans, feelings, or words to express. While I've had plans and resolutions before, I haven't executed any yet.  
- Choi Hakseong

Until now, I have published various poems. However, with each publication, I have felt dissatisfaction with my work. Although there’s time, I wish to present more confident creations this year.  
- Kim Hwasan

Life doesn't change, and even if one has ambitions, I question what different life can be created from now on. From this New Year, I want to spend less time on trivial magazine writings.  
- Kim Jaebak

One must effectively seize opportunities.  
- Lee Inseok

In the New Year, I intend to work more actively for the unification and organization of our Korean script.  
- Lee Geunro

I will ensure to handle and organize all work and documents on the same day they arise.  
- Jeong Eungbang

Let us first agree on our rituals.  
- Myeong Jaeseo

Whatever the ideology, if Koreans unite in one place, it will be beneficial.  
- Kim Byeongjun

I hope to produce a remarkable book.  
- Lee Dunghwa

Today, I am very concerned about the chaotic state of our education system and wonder how we can remedy it; additionally, we shouldn't neglect our responsibilities and should unite wholeheartedly especially this year.  
- Jo Dongshik

From this New Year, I aim to focus on providing suitable education for Korean families. And one hope is that we could unify our greetings within the year.  
- Baek Namkyu

I wish for brighter lives surrounding me. The children are often seen with fewer smiles and solemn faces, which is truly hard to bear. I believe that self-reliance and robust education are paramount for all.  
- Jo Jaehwa

Every New Year brings with it aspirations, yet I have achieved none so far. This year, I am determined to accomplish something.  
- Eon Jongki

It is already the New Year. I wish all my long-held aspirations from last year and those before to be fulfilled this year.  
- Kim Gyeongjae

This summer, I plan to seize the chance to visit Mount Baekdu, and I also intend to inspect Osaka and Tokyo.  
- Lee Gwangsoo

The year of the sheep symbolizes peace, yet historically, great sacrifices have occurred in such years for Korea. How will this Year of the Metal Sheep (辛未) unfold?  
- Seol Uisik

Starting this New Year, I hope Koreans will carry out these two tasks:  
1. Abstain from alcohol and smoking.  
2. Avoid colored clothing.  
- Kim Jeongwan

With every New Year, I feel our goals become more distant.  
- Ju Yohan

From this year onward, I wish for Koreans to act on at least one practical matter rather than engaging in empty speculation.  
- Song Jinwoo

I plan to engage in rabbit breeding as a side business. As my backyard is relatively spacious, it seems feasible.  
- Gyeong Gilryeol

While I am unsure whether this will come to fruition, I consider establishing a women's hospital even with small funds among my fellow female doctors.  
- Ryu Yeongjun

I regret missing a trip to Mount Geumgang last time, but this summer, I will visit Mount Geumgang and, if time permits, also inspect Osaka or Tokyo.  
- Park Janhee

Currently, I am dissatisfied with the system of women's high schools. This year, I hope we can establish a home economics school that is directly needed for household life.  
- Bang Sinyeong

Having experienced the year turn for many years, I feel bittersweet passing an empty boundary of a New Year; "Life is about pursuing limited ideals," or so I wonder if that is truly the case.  
- Kim Wonju

**Summary**

In this article, renowned individuals share their reflections and hopes for the New Year as a form of greeting, echoing sentiments of both aspiration and concern for societal issues in Korea. Each contributor emphasizes the significance of personal responsibility, economic awareness, and the need for unity in the face of challenges. This collection highlights both the individual and collective hopes for a better future, revealing the aspirations of Koreans living under colonial rule during this period, along with their desires for liberation and progress. Historians may find it intriguing how these reflections not only encapsulate personal ambitions but also reflect the broader socio-political climate of Korea in the early 1930s.
