鼻下八寸  
Once upon a time during the reign of the Han Emperor Wu in China, there was a famous person known as Dongfang Shuo (東方朔), who no Korean could possibly be unaware of, even children and servants. At that time, a fortune-teller told Emperor Wu that his philtrum (the space under the nose) was one inch long, thus he would easily attain longevity of a hundred years. Whether in ancient times or now, if a person could live long, it was good news. The Emperor, proud of this prediction, boasted about his long life to everyone he met, causing him great delight. 

One day, the Emperor visited Dongfang Shuo and again proclaimed his longevity. Unexpectedly, Dongfang Shuo burst into a loud laugh, making the Emperor extremely angry and reprimanding him. However, Dongfang Shuo responded that laughing at the Emperor's words was not disrespectful. He elaborated, saying that since the ancient Peng Zu (彭祖) lived for eight hundred years, if with the theory that a one-inch philtrum would equal a hundred years, then the philtrum of Peng Zu must have been eight inches. If there existed a person in this world with an eight-inch philtrum, who wouldn't laugh at them? As he continued to chuckle at the thought, even the once angry Emperor found himself laughing, and the group was transformed into a sea of laughter. <41>  

**Summary**  

This article recounts an anecdote from the time of the Han Emperor Wu, highlighting the absurdity of believing that physical features could determine longevity as seen through the dialogue between the Emperor and Dongfang Shuo. The humor in the exchange serves to humanize historical figures and reflect the cultural values concerning longevity and wisdom. In the context of Korean colonial history, this narrative provides insights into the interactions between Confucian ideals and the imperial narratives shared in East Asia, which were influential during Korea’s colonial period.

鼻下八寸
녯날 녯적 支那의 漢武帝 때에 유명한 사람으로 三千甲子 東方朔이라 하면 우리 朝鮮 사람도 兒童走卒 할 것 업시 모를 사람이 업슬 것이다. 그 때에 엇던 觀相 보는 사람이 漢武帝를 보고 말하기를 迤下의 인중(鼻下)이 한치(一寸)나 되게 기니 百歲長壽는 無難다고 하얏다. 녯날이나 지금이나 사람이 오래 산다면 조와하는 法이라 武帝는 보는 사람마다 自己가 百年長壽를 하겟다고 반가워서 자랑을 입이 찌여지도록 하엿다. 하루는 武帝가 東方朔을 보고 例와 가티 또 自己가 長壽한다는 말을 자랑하니 東方朔은 별안간에 仰天大笑 함으로 武帝가 크게 怒하야 責하되 臣下가 君上의 말슴에 無嚴하게 웃는 것이 무슨 不敬한 일이냐 한즉 東方朔은 억지로 배를 붓잡고 우숨을 진뎡하면서 하는 말이 「녯적의 彭祖는 八百 歲를 사럿는데 인중이 한치가 되면 百歲를 산다는 理論으로 생각해보면 彭祖의 인중은 八寸이나 될터이나 萬一 이 世上에 잉중이 八寸이나 되는 사람이 잇다하면 누가 보고 웃지 안켓슴닛가. 저는 그 생각을 하고 우슴이 절로 나서 우섯슴니다 하고 또 깔깔 우슴을 치니 크게 怒하얏던 武帝도 當場에 우슴이 또 나서 전체가 우숨의 바다로 化하엿섯다.＜41＞
