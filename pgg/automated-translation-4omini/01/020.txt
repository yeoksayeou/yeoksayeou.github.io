Song of the Black Spot on Earth  
                        尙火  
It seems it will never change; even within the sun, a black spot burns --  
the world will grow old and wither, from summer onward it will not know. --  

Whether we experience or not, there is one concern,  
even on this precarious land where life and death contest in struggle,  
the time when even the blind moonlit night descends here,  
the sorrowful beings -- the pitiable lives that cannot live long --  
what do they see, how do they live, and what have they clutched in their heaving chests?  

Ah, the power of man is insignificant; boasting foolishly,  
to worry about ascending to the ten thousand-layered heaven is truly presumptuous.  
Generations merely consume the earth they have eaten through and think they can live with a single course --  
-- how laughably foolish the dreams of that foolish child were --  
Even after knowing, do they treat it as ordinary?  
Now when night falls, even the stars that resemble twinkling flames must come to me.  
Here, the suffocating clouds once again block my breath,  
crying without purpose -- lacking any way to live,  
what can I brush against, and what can I see on the ground before I die?  
Ah, the heart of man can hold nothing; it is something to be taken lightly.  
The ten thousand varieties of foolishness thrown about in this sky is indeed maddening.  

**Summary**  

This poem delves into themes of existential struggle, reflecting on the human condition in the face of overwhelming despair and the insignificance of human concerns in the grand scheme of the universe. The imagery of the "black spot" suggests a persistent blemish on the Earth, mirroring the sorrow and futility felt by individuals. From the perspective of Korean colonial history, this piece underscores feelings of helplessness and a critique of societal norms amidst the turmoil of the colonial experience, resonating with the broader struggles of identity and existence in a time of oppression.

地球黑點의 노래
						尙火
영영 변하지 안는다 밋든 해 속에도 검은 점이 돗처
--세상은 수이 식고 말려 녀름철부터 모르리라--
맛거나 말거나 덩따라 걱정은 하나마
죽음과 삶이 숨밧굼질하는 위태로운 땅덩이에서도
엇재 여게만은 눈빠진 금음밤조차 더 내려깔려
애닯은 목숨들이-길욱하게도 못살 가엽슨 목숨들이
무엇을 보고 엇지 살꼬 앙가슴을 뚜다리다 밋처나 보앗든가.
아 사람의 힘은 보잘 것 업다 건방지게 비웃고＜23＞
구만층 놉흔 한울로 올러가사는 해 걱정을 함이야말로 주제넘다
대대로 흙만 파먹으면 한갈가러 살려니 하든 것도
--웃으꽝스런 독갑이에게 홀런 긴 꿈이엇구나--
알어도 격거도 예사로 녀겨만 지는가
이미 밤이면 반듸불 가튼 별이나마 나와는 주어야지
엇재 여게만은 숨통막는 구름조차 또겁처끼여
울어도 쓸데업시-단 하로라도 살 듯 살어볼 꺼리업시
무엇을 밋고 니저볼꼬 땅바닥에 뒤궁글다 죽고나 말것인가
아 사람의 맘은 두릴 것 업다 만만하게 생각고
천가지 가즌 지랄로 잘까부리는 저 한울을 둠이야말로 속터진다＜24＞
＜23-24＞
