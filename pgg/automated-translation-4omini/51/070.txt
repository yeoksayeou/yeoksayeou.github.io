기사제목	드믄 孝子 (A Rare Filial Son)  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

A stout girl says to a pretty boy,  

"If I were your mother, how happy I would be."  

"Yes – I wouldn't know, but surely my father would be very pessimistic."  

<14>  

**Summary**  

This short dialogue captures a humorous and insightful exchange between a girl and a boy regarding the nature of familial relationships. The girl imagines the joys of being a mother, while the boy alludes to the societal pressures and expectations that come with parental roles. From a historical perspective, this piece provides a glimpse into the evolving discussions around family dynamics and gender roles in Korea during the colonial period, reflecting both traditional values and emerging modern attitudes. Historians may find the interaction indicative of shifting perceptions of filial duty and emotional expression within Korean family structures.
