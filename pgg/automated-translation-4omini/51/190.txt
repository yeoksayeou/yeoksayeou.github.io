"비극도 아닌데 웨 그리우나 울만한 사실은 조곰도 업지안어?"  
"아니얘요. 이까짓걸 구경이라고 돈 드려온게 분해서" <39>  

**Summary**  

This article captures a brief moment of dialogue between a husband and wife at a theater, with the wife crying not out of sorrow, but out of frustration about spending money to see something unworthy. The humor derives from the wife's unexpected emotional response to a trivial situation. From a historical perspective, this reflects the cultural perceptions and societal pressures in colonial Korea during the early 1930s, revealing the growing frustrations of ordinary people amidst the economic difficulties of the time.
