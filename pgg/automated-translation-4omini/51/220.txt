기사제목	괴롭다 (Suffering)  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

 괴롭다  
그녀자 「여보서요 내가 원하는 것이라면 무엇이던지 드러주시겟지요」  

그남자 「그러다뿐입니까 생명을 바처서라도 다해 드리겟습니다」  

「어이구 엇저면 저는 참 깁붐니다. 그러면 내일부터 나 집을 차저 주지마세요. 네-」  

 
**Summary**  

The article explores a conversation between a woman and a man, highlighting emotional conflict and the depths of their relationship. The woman's request for anything she desires contrasts with the man's willingness to sacrifice everything for her. This exchange not only captures personal anguish but also reflects the larger socio-cultural dynamics of the time, offering insight into gender roles and emotional expectations under colonial circumstances. Historians may find the portrayal of love and sacrifice particularly revealing in the context of 1930s Korea, during a period of colonial subjugation and identity struggle.

**Glossary**

깁붐니다	gipbumnida	An older form of expressing emotional distress or suffering.   
생명을 바처서라도	saengmyeongeul bacheoseorado	To the extent of sacrificing one’s life.
