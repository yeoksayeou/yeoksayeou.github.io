Empirical Philosophical Lecture, Unusual Baldness  
Starting this month, the Byeolgeongeon department of the Kaepiksa will publish "Empirical Philosophical Lecture."  

Originally, lacking the authority of sharp-tonguedness and having rather thick lips, I often find myself being pressed by my lips even if I try to sound sharp. This is truly a situation where I am stuck with a ridiculous boast.  

Anyway, since I have been granted permission to start, I will proceed with the lecture and if there is interest and enough participants, I will continue. If, however, that is not the case, I have no choice but to resign. This is my simple excuse.  

Time is gold, so instead of wasting it with such pointless chatter, I will now begin the lecture. - I hope everyone has their notebook ready?  

While researching lecture materials empirically (since this is empirical philosophy), I flipped through newspapers and magazines and was caught by the title "Regarding the Korean Newspaper and Magazine World" from the June issue of the "Nanggong" magazine.  

It's unknown whether it was written or spoken, but even highly regarded figures were unable to avoid mentioning trivial matters. So I turned the pages and found that contrary to what had been highlighted in the table of contents, the articles in the body were treated very poorly. Indeed, from the article, I felt it might be easy for someone to receive disappointing treatment. (If the Nanggong magazine’s intention was not this, I retract that statement). The content is a survey regarding "complaints and hopes about Korean newspapers and magazines" in which several eminent individuals responded, and I intend to summarize the responses an especially disappointing one.  

In’gu Pyo - is a well-known Doctor of German Literature. Seems like he is a Professor Jinjin for the "Gadagai" being considered a formal specialty, right? However, this Professor stated,  

"I did not purposely buy Korean newspapers at all because the reports in the entire newspaper detailed only negative aspects of Korea - hence, it prompted feelings of despair and anxiety within me." (from the audience, a sound of snickering can be heard)  

There should be no snickering as I quote the esteemed professor's remarks. In any case, if this professor had seen the situations of some unreported individuals directly, he might have fainted?  

Anyway, it is of that ilk - a type of comment where one tries to avoid filth with their ears and not look at what is ugly with their eyes. (From the audience: "Originally, professors are often exceptions, like one in a hundred in cases of archaeological presence.")  

Even though he was requested to revise the content and format, there was no particular instruction on what revisions should be made. If there happens to be a student with connections to Professor In, kindly bring it up next time for discussion.  

To conclude, I hear complaints that "true academic journals" are rare. This probably stems from the professor’s mistaken view due to the despair and anxiety he referenced, or does Professor In expect to pay a hefty sum once each month to give out hundreds of magazines for free, regardless of quality, to ease his own heart?  

Byong-gang Yoo - this seems to be someone new in the editorial office, and his demeanor is quite striking.  

The current newspaper and magazine folks say they have no understanding of character appraisal, implying the things they publish are rather dubious; however, it seems that at least he himself is not very dubious?  

So I suggest if you young folks are looking to work in magazines or newspapers later on, make sure to appoint this Byong-seng as the top adviser in character appraisal and introduce him as one of the noblest individuals in Korea.  

*Seung-nai - who said to write something that gives hope to the young folks; however, it seems he has mistaken the magazines or newspapers for a gospel book.  

An artist, *** - expressed complaints that the articles were lowered in quality and demanded an elevation of intellectual refinement. Would it not be ridiculous to create a ladder to ascend to heaven? In any case, he is very much a man far removed from "humanity."  

Regarding the Korean media, one educator attributed the absence of press freedom as solely complaining that "there's only bad reporting." This is Mr. Pumpkin Flower School's teacher. Is this not the very embodiment of what they call "saying a deer is a horse"?  

Sada-yuu - seems to be a professor of various specializations, but he ought to use historical approaches to examine the situation.  

You all know Professor Sada - notably still retaining the popularity of that lecture "Oh, the Youth Blooms." His statements are as follows:  

These days, magazines depict males with "vile disparagement" while portraying females as ** with “great bold type.” That’s just grimy and merely a strategy to sell magazines. Furthermore, does this actually not amount to a humiliation of women?  

With that, isn’t it truly inexplicable how magazine practitioners have not raised any protests against hearing such "vile disparagement"? (From the audience: "Professor Sada is a legal expert, so he should act according to the law and rebut with oratory. It’s unlikely anyone would be swayed by that." "Ah, a lawyer only excels at going to prison, and an orator is merely good at defining sentences.")  

In this case, it seems that Professor Sada is a considerable female idolator. It seems he sometimes goes to buy vegetables, carrying commercially acquired products (each time leaving his pocket knife right there and returning to them afterward?) and enjoys dining quietly at a fine restaurant while personally delivering manuscripts when requested.   

Considering this, isn't it excessive for magazine publishers to refuse to compliment women, valuing one’s self-image over the possibility of “an enticing glance” from a woman?  

With that, today’s lecture will conclude. What are your thoughts, everyone?  

(From the audience: "What’s the use of intestines that do not do anything!?")  

(Another from the audience: "Just send it to a sundae shop.")  

(Another student: "If you send it alive, it won't be of any use! They could just shape their own sundae!")<56>  

**Summary**  

In this article, the author presents a satirical lecture that critiques various brilliant but flawed opinions regarding the Korean newspaper and magazine world, as evoked by selected responses to a survey. Engaging in humor and sharp commentary, the lecture reveals a disconnect between elite perspectives and the public expectations. The article illustrates the tensions within Korean media culture during the colonial period as intellectuals like In’gu Pyo denote despair and negativity portrayed in the media, while the professor's observations skew toward critique of societal values regarding masculinity and femininity. Historians may find this commentary revealing of the elite’s struggle to maintain a voice amid colonial censorship and societal expectations of professionalism.

實證尖口 哲學講座, 떡떡 대머리스트
이달부터 開闢社 別乾坤部의 촉탁을 바더 「實證尖口 哲學講座」를 담님하게 되엇습니다.
본시 나는 첨구(尖口)의 권위는커녕 입술이 넘우 두터워서 혹시 뾰족한 소리가 나오다가도 입술에 눌니어 말삼을 당하는 터인데 이거야말로 천만에 수작을 하게 된 형편이지요.
어쨋거나 긔왕 승낙한 터이니 시작해봐서 인긔가 잇고 청강생이 만커나 하면 그대로 멧달 계속할 것이요 만일 그러치 못하면 사직을 햇지 별수가 잇습니까.
이것이 내 자신의 간단한 변명이외다.
시간은 황금이라니 그러한 공연한 소리로 황금을 허비할 것이 아니라 지금부터 강의에 들어가겟습니다. -노트는 다 준비 되엇겟지요?

강의재료를 실증적으로(實證哲學이니까) 하느라고  신문 잡지를 뒤적어리니까 「南光」잡지 6월호의
「朝鮮言論界에 對하야」라는 제목이 눈에 선뜻 띄엇습니다. 글로 썻는지 말로 하엿는지는 모르겟스나 전일 화함을 듯잡지 못하는 명사까지 들어 여러분이 게섯습니다. 그래 페-지를 넘겨가지고서 본즉 목차에서 잘 대접한 것과는 딴판으로 기사에 가서는 행랑아범 대우를 햇겟나요! 그래. 그 놈을 죽 읽어보니까 미상불 그러한 후대접을 밧기 쉬울만큼 내용이 좀 섭섭해 보엿습니다. (南光지가 그것이 본의가 아니엇다면 이 말만은 취소한다) 그 내용인즉
「조선의 신문 잡지에 대한 불평 희망 충고」라는 설문에 대하야 몃몃분 명사들께서 대답하신 것인데 지금부터 그 중에도 가장 섭섭한 대답을 한 분의 것만 멧개 추리어 가지고 강의를 해보려고 합니다.

殷愚票- 누구나 다 아는 독일문학박사이십니다. 「가다가이」가 普通專門이라고 되엿스니 진진교수이신 것이 분명하지요? 그런데 이 교수씨의 말슴에 왈
「조선신문을 일부러 사 보지 아니한 일은 잇섯는대 그 리유는 신문 전체가 비운(조선의)만 보도한 때문에- 그래서 자기에게 비관과 불안의 자극을 주엇기 때문에」라고 햇습니다.(청강생 중에서 우슴소리가 들린다)
여러분 권위 잇는 교수씨의 말슴을 인용하는데 그러케 킥킥 우서서는 아니됩니다.
그러고 보니 아마 이 교수씨는 신문의 보도보담 멧배나 긔맥힌 XX의 사정을 눈으로 즉접 보앗다면 기절이 되엇겟지요?
하여간 귀로 더러운 소리를 아니듯고 눈으로 추한 것을 아니보려는 류의 고색이 창연한 말습입니다.(청강생 중에서 「원래 교수라는 것은 백에 하나 열에 하나의 례외를 뻬어노코는 모도가 고고학적(中古考古學) 존재이니까요」)
내용과 체재를 개작하라고 하엿스나 어떠케 개작하라는 말은 업스니 이중에 혹 현재 殷교수와 친분이 잇는 학생이 잇거든 그 구체안이나 좀 들어가다 다음 시간에 말하시오.
끗흐로 「순 학술적 전문잡지」가 적은 것이 유감(-)이고」 하엿는데 아마 이것은 殷교수가 우에 말한 비관과 불안의 자극 때문에 착각이 생겨서 이러한 밀슴을 한 모양이나 혹 殷교수께서 돈이나 듬북내어 매월 멧만부씩 백여가지고 독자야 가저다가 도배를 하건 뒤지를 하건 무료배부를 하엿스면 속이 시언하다고 하실는지?

便狂尿氏 ... 이 분은 영사령에 처음 참여하시는 분인 듯한데 기세가 매우 시퍼럿트군요.
지금의 신문 잡지쟁이가 인물 감식에 관한 상식이 업서서 그 개려내는 인물이 보다 비슥하다고 하엿스니 적어도 자기 자신만은 그닥지 비슥하지는 아니할 것이 아니겟습니까?
그러니까 일후에 제군 가운데 혹 잡지나 신문에 종사하려거든 이 便선생을 모서다가 인물감식 최고고문으로 안치고 또 조선의 인물중 가장 고귀한 분으로 소개하십시요.

*勝來氏 ... 이분은 청년에게 희망 줄 가시를 쓰라고 하엿스니 그러나 그것은 잡지나 신문을 복음전도서로 오해하신 듯 십흔데 ... .
미술가 ***씨 ... 기사가 야비화 한 것에 불평을 가즈시고 지식적 고급취미화 하라고 하시엇다.
사닥다리나 놉직한 놈을 만들어 가지고 천당으로 이전하심이 어떨는지? 엇쨋거나 「인간」과는 인연이 대단한 양반이십니다.

조선언론게에 대해서 언론의 자유가 업다는 리유로 다만 「오식」이 만흔 것만이 불평이라 하신분이 게시니 박(호박)꼿학교의 철창객 선생님이십니다
이야말로 「사슴을 말」이라고들 「오식」된 존재가 아닐는지요.

砂多有氏- 가튼 *專의 교수씨지만 이분은 고현학적 방법을 빌어다 고찰을 해야하겟습니다.
제군도 잘 알지요- 웅변교수 砂교수씨를! 그리고 특히 「오회라- 청춘이 가려네」라는 강연으로 만도의 녀학생의 인기를 한 몸에 아직(?)도 보유하고 잇는 砂교수씨를-

요지음 잡지라는 것은 남자는 「악담패설」로 낫뿌게 소개하고 녀자는 **로 「대호활자」를 대서 소개한다.
