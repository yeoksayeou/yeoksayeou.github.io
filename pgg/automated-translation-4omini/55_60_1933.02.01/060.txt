Li Aeri-su's Tragic Love Incident, Exclusive Victor Popular Singer Li Aeri-su, the Flower of the Theater

The cheerful sounds of nightingales coming from the mountains and the melodious songs sung perfectly by Li Aeri-su—who captivates the stage with her charming figure—had an unfortunate incident on the night of the 9th of last month at 3:30 am. She consumed poison with her beloved, Yeonjeon Student Bae Dong-pil, and in a moment of despair, cut her artery with a sharp knife and resolutely took her life. Aeri-su performed a tragic act of love, leaving this world alongside her beloved. However, is it fortune or misfortune that Aeri-su, who left this world, regained her life in a dimly lit hospital room? What will unfold for the two of them who clung to life in the aftermath of this tragic love? Anyone who knows them feels a deep longing and concern for what might have led Aeri-su to such a fateful decision.

Born and raised in Kaesong, Aeri-su grew up in a troubled home, constantly harboring grievances in her young heart, which later drove her to the theater. After immersing herself in acting, Aeri-su's personality began to stand out, marked by her ability to act and empathize deeply. Since she had no one else to rely upon, the presence of men became significant in her life. This entanglement with male actors led to a tumultuous lifestyle.

Back when the theater industry was not established in Korea, she wandered without guidance, unable to find her path as a female actor. Thus, Aeri-su became ill in spirit. As the whispers of men surrounded her and time passed, Aeri-su finally secured her position as a popular actress in a theater company, just as the Korean theater scene began to advance. She transitioned from Columbia to become an exclusive singer with Victor, monopolizing popularity, but at the same time, her life became filled with regrets. Yet, behind Aeri-su's bright facade lies a painful story. Unable to nurture her own child, Aeri-su became a weeping mother. Laughing Aeri-su lived a double life of joy and sorrow.

Eight years ago, when she first took the stage, she deeply moved audiences. A fortuitous encounter occurred between Aeri-su and a young wealthy man, Li Gwang-ryeol, who joined the troupe. Yet, the people who surrounded her and those who sought her for financial reasons complicated their love. 

"I want to stop acting now."  
"Why?"  
"I want a more peaceful life."  
"Since when?"  
"Simply put, it's because I long for a family."  

The two eventually left the theater company to create a new household. However, Li was already married and had a child.  

"I'm married and have a child, but you mustn't think of me as a mistress."  
"When I first fell in love with you, I didn't consider the wife and child; why bring it up now?"  
"Then it's my fault."  

Thus, they settled into a life together, splitting living arrangements between Li's mother and his primary wife. Aeri-su began recording music and gave birth to a child. The inability to manage household affairs pushed Li into financial ruin, leading to conflict in their relationship. Aeri-su's love for Li started to sour.

"That's it between us."  
"Why?"  
"I must live happily with your respectable wife who blames you."  

Li’s wife—innocent and virtuous—loved her husband as much as anyone and was prepared to sacrifice her love. But Aeri-su's troubling past overshadowed everything once more, complicating her feelings.

Li decided he would divorce his wife and inform Aeri-su that he intended to make her his legal wife, but as this love faltered, he became a pitiable figure while Aeri-su found herself longing for a new life. As her popularity soared, vanity came to weigh heavily on her light spirit. 

"I must go. Be well."  
"Why go? If a couple devoid of love lives even for a millennium, what is the point? Please stay and live happily."  

Grasping Aeri-su's hand, he implored her not to leave.

"Yet I must go. If I linger on, what will be accomplished?"

"I was divorced, but I leave with no words. It's because I love them. Aeri-su must offer her love to her beloved."

The tragic conflict of two women in love! Yet, Li couldn’t part with Aeri-su and grieved over their separation.

"Aeri-su! At the first moment we met, did you anticipate such misfortune?"  
"..."  
Silenced Aeri-su! In the room's silence echoed the cries of two children born of different mothers—voice of life!  

Aeri-su, now a mere shadow of her former self, managed to save enough money from her records to move her household from Kaesong to Seoul, transitioning into a solitary life. However, the child she could not raise blossomed under Li's shadow, longing for her mother, fostering a bond difficult for anyone else to perceive.

Now, with the hopeful university student Bae Dong-pil, Aeri-su walks side by side on the streets, easily visible to all. Bae, who had twice been married, found himself in love with Aeri-su, longing for her nurturing spirit despite their tumultuous love. They promised to live together for a hundred years.

As time passed, passion seized Bae, and dissatisfaction with his current life grew, fueled by economic constraints and feelings toward his father. 

It was the 9th of January, a winter night. The two sat closely together, sharing the evening meal in deep silence. Upon returning home, Bae declared, "Aeri-su, I will die."  
"..."  
Aeri-su feared for his life. How could he shed the burdens of vanity?  

As they contemplated poison, they spoke in agony—"I will die too."  

They ingested the poison, their consciousness fading, holding one another tightly as they cut their arteries together. Their final breath found its purpose in shared despair. 

"Why must death lead to resolution?"  

Writing their will to the world, they succumbed to the night, the last of their days together. 

"Ah, it’s spinning," Bae uttered.  
"Now, it is our world."  
"Love, our love…"  

Aeri-su lost her consciousness, plunging into darkness with Bae.

Revived, they now lie in hospital beds, listening to each other's heartbeats. How will they face the world?

Tragedy, love entwined with death. Is there such a pure love at all? Or is it merely a joke, witnessing lives leading to demise? The weight of their past haunts faceless individuals, making the situation appear absurd. 

The reality of Li Gwang-ryeol, Aeri-su's child, and Bae Dong-pil shows that the paths of love seldom align in harmony, especially in a society like Korea which lacks fundamental understandings. The lack of social responsibility and duty leads to disgrace among neighbors.

As we wander through the night, bound with love, suffering, and sorrow, we must contemplate whether love can truly lead to death or if we, as young individuals, may yet step forth, learning the responsibilities and duties we as Koreans ought to embrace. 

-1933.1.18-  

**Summary**

The article follows the tragic love story of Li Aeri-su, a popular singer and actress, who attempts to take her life along with her lover, Bae Dong-pil, after enduring a complex love surrounded by societal pressures. Their story unfolds against the backdrop of early 20th-century Korean theater life and the struggles between ambition and personal relationships. This narrative not only captures the emotional turmoil of its characters but also highlights the societal expectations and personal sacrifices inherent in colonial Korean culture. Historians may find the reflections on love, sacrifice, and societal limitations particularly revealing about the lived experiences of individuals during the colonial period.

애리스는 정신을 빼앗기고 낭떠러지에서 떠러지는 거와 가티 배(裵)를 얼싸안고 딍구럿다. 이애리스와 배동필이 정사를 햇다.

죽엇다 사러난 두 사람은 산송이 되어 지금 대학병원 침대우에서 서서로 맥박의 소리를 세이고 잇스니 엇더케 세상 사람들을 대할 것인가.

정사, 정을 안고 죽는다. 그러면 사랑과 죽엄 그러케도 안타가은 순결한 사랑이 잇섯든가 현재 조선 남녀들의 머리를 흔드러 놋는 허영에서 어든 병이 정사를 한 개의 모던으로 생각하기 때문에 희극을 연출하게 되는 것이다. 정사를 한 사람들에게 슯흔 일이겟스나 엽헤서 보는 사람은 손고락질해서 웃지 안을 수가 업다.
리광렬(李光烈)-
애리수의 어린애-
배동필(裵東必)-
이 사람 사람의 거러갈 길이 사랑의 길밧게 업섯든 것이 너무나 조선 사람이 가저야할 상식이 업기 때문이다. 좀 더 사회(社會)인으로서 가저야할 책임(責任)과 의무(義務)를 다하여 주엇드면 얼마나 고마웟슬가. 이웃 나라 사람들 보기 붓구러운 일이다. 밤이 업는 거리를 헤메는 우리들이 지금 사랑을 안고 웃고 울고 나종엔 목숨까지 끄너야 할 것일가.
나종 끗흐로 바라는 것은 정사와 가티 사랑도 죽엇스니 다시 샘솟는 사랑을 끄너버리고 조선사람이 가저야 할 책임과 의무를 배우며 압길을 향개 나가는 젊은이가 되기를 빌고 바란다.
