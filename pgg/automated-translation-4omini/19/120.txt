기사제목	그리운 곡조 (Nostalgic Tune)  
필자	金東煥 (Kim Dong-hwan)  
기사형태	시 (Poem)  

Nostalgic Tune  

Oh, raindrop, that follows the water lily.  

From which cloud in the sky do you drift down?  

In the sky, there are pairs of clouds, yet.  

The rain that follows the water lily is but one.  

This melody softly caresses a young heart.  

From whose fingertips does it flow?  

Though melodies are plenty in this vast world.  

The melody that strikes this heart comes from but one. <29>  

**Summary**  

This poem evokes feelings of nostalgia and longing through imagery of nature, specifically rain and water lilies. It reflects on the personal connection between music and emotion, illustrating how a singular melody resonates deeply within the poet's heart despite the abundance of sounds in the world. The historical context of Korean colonial history during this period is significant, as it prompts reflections on identity and the cultural shifts occurring under Japanese rule, allowing historians to consider how personal experiences, such as those expressed in poetry, intersect with larger social and political themes.

기사형태	시

그리운 곡조
						金東煥
창포님 따리는 저 빗방울아.
어느 하눌 구름 속서 떠러 젓느냐.
하눌에는 구름쌍도 만키도 하나.
창포닙 따릴 비는 오직 하나라.

젊은 가슴 부드치는 이 곡조야.
어느 분의 손가락서 흘러 나오나.
너른 세상 곡조탈이 하도 만흐나.
이 가슴에 보낼 곡조 타는 이 한분.<29>
