기사형태	문예기타 (Literary Miscellaneous)  

The tide carried through the window, and as I awoke, my wife gently said, “Dear, while you said you would fix the window frame, you haven’t made any progress because you're short.” Please fix the window in this location. <155>  

  
**Summary**  

This article presents a mundane domestic exchange between a husband and wife, evoking the themes of responsibility and communication within a marriage. The wife's gentle request to her husband highlights the social dynamics and gender roles prevalent during the era. Historians might find this dialogue intriguing as it reflects on the everyday lives of families during the colonial period in Korea, illuminating the subtle familial tensions and societal expectations that were shaped by Japanese rule.
