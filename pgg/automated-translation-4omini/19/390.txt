세계의 麥秋期  
In April, the warm south winds bring the yellow barley, and in our Korea, generally, by the lunar month of April, barley matures, and wheat (小麥) is harvested around the end of April to early May. Thus, ordinary farmers in Korea know the harvest season of wheat only as May. However, now people around the world eat wheat so commonly, that regions cultivating wheat have also expanded widely, and there is hardly any month in the year when wheat harvest does not occur, except for the month of December. Now, to introduce this by month and region, we have it roughly as follows.

Australia and the Argentinian Republic are in January  

India and Upper Egypt are in January and February  

India, Lower Egypt, Syria, Cyprus, Cuba, and Mexico are in April  

Japan, China, Algeria, Texas, Morocco, and Central Asia are in May  

The western and southern regions of North America, Spain, Upper Turkey, Greece, Italy, Portugal, and southern France are in June  

Northern and southern regions of North America, Romania, Hungary, Southern Russia, Germany, Switzerland, and southern England are in July  

Central North America, Lower Canada, Belgium, Holland, England, Denmark, Poland, and Central Russia are in August  

Sweden, Norway, Scotland, and northern Russia are in September and October  

Southern Africa and South America, specifically Peru, are in November.  

In Korea, in mountainous areas like Changjin, it is said that they do the oat harvest in August, so if they were to farm wheat here, they too would not be able to harvest outside of July and August.  

**Summary**  

The article discusses the global wheat and barley harvest timings, contrasting regional agricultural practices worldwide. It highlights how wheat, now a staple around the globe, has widely expanded cultivation, with different harvesting months depending on the locality. For Korean colonial history, the mention of little wheat cultivation in Korea and the timing of harvesting oats suggest an agricultural landscape shaped by colonial constraints and the global dynamics of food production and consumption. Historians might find the article valuable for its insights into agricultural practices under colonial rule and how they compared to global standards.

世界의 麥秋期
4월 남풍 大麥黃이라고 우리 朝鮮에는 陰曆 4월이면 버리가 대개 성숙하고 밀(小麥)도 4월말 내지 5월 초순이면 收穫을 하게 된다. 그럼으로 朝鮮의 일반 농가에서는 小麥의 수확기를 5월로만 안다. 그러나 지금에는 全世界사람이 小麥을 常食하는이 만치 小麥農을 하는 지방이 또한 넓어서 1년중에 小麥 收穫을 안이 하는 달은 僅히 12월 한 달뿐이오 其外에는 어느 달이더지 小麥 收穫을 다한다. 이제 이것을 月別 地方別로 소개하자면 대약 左와 갓다.

濠洲 及 아루젠딘 공화국은 1월
印度, 上埃及은 1, 2월
印度, 下埃及, 씨리아, 사이부데스, 큐바 墨西哥는 4월
日本, 支那, 알제리아, 데기사스, 모롯고, 中央亞細亞은 5월
北米서부 及 南部, 西班牙, 上耳其, 希臘, 伊太伊, 葡萄牙, 佛國남부는 6월
北米남북부, 羅馬尼, 匈牙利, 南露西亞, 獨逸, 瑞西, 英國의 남부는 7월
北米중부, 下加奈太, 白耳義, 和蘭, 英國, 丁扶, 波蘭, 中央露西亞는 8월
瑞曲, 諾威, 蘇格蘭, 북부 露西亞는 9, 10월
남부阿弗利加, 南米페루는 11월이다.
조선에서는 長津가튼 고산 지대에서는 8월에 귀리 타작을 하는 일이 잇슨즉 小麥 농사를 한다면 역시 7, 8월이 안이고는 추수를 하지 못할 것이다.<84>
