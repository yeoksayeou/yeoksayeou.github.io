美人懸賞  
To overcome the heat, we have an interesting prize contest for beautiful women at the beginning of this magazine's photo page. If guessed correctly, the prize is a bag of white rice, so please take a look. There are also prize contests for wristwatches in 'Student' and 'Children'. <42>  

**Summary**  

This article introduces a contest for identifying beautiful women in photographs, with a prize of white rice for those who can guess correctly. It reflects the interwar period's cultural fascination with beauty contests and commercialization, particularly within the context of colonial Korea. Historians might find it intriguing how these contests were used to engage the public and promote magazines, as well as the implications of colonial identity and gender roles during this period.
