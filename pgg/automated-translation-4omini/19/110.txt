잡지명	별건곤  
발행일	1929년 06월 23일  
기사제목	젊은이들아 (Young People)  
필자	金東煥 (Kim Dong-hwan)  
기사형태	시 (Poem)  

Young people, let us go to the mountains.  

With poison in our hearts, let us go to the mountains.  

With these arms that draw red blood,  

Let us chop wood and foster the long path.  

Fostering the long path is good for the coming days.  

Is the heroic man really an extraordinary being?  

Fostering is not easy with blood dripping.  

Young people, let us go to the mountains.<29>  

**Summary**  

In this poem, the author calls upon the youth to venture into the mountains, symbolizing a journey towards strength and vitality. The imagery of the mountains and chopping wood evokes a sense of labor and fortitude, suggesting that fostering character and resilience is essential. Historians might find this call to action significant as it reflects the sentiments of young Koreans during a time of colonial oppression, potentially signaling a yearning for independence and a strong national identity amidst hardship.

발행일	1929년 06월 23일
기사제목	젊은이들아
필자	金東煥
기사형태	시

젊은이들아
						金東煥
젊은이들아 산으로 가자.
독기나 차고 산으로 가자.
붉은 피 끌는 이 팔뚝으로
나무나 패여 긔운 길느자.

긔운 길러야 조흔 날 오지.
영융 호걸이 별 사람인가.
긔운이 장코 피 만흔 이지.
젊은이들아 산으로 가자.<29>
