In a city’s underbelly, there was a villain named Frank Trent—the head of a gang. He appeared to be a gentle and handsome man but was always using his numerous subordinates to carry out wicked deeds. Recently, a murder incident occurred, and the police linked it to Trent’s actions. However, after three days of investigation, a key witness was killed, causing the case to vanish into darkness once again. The police, outraged by the violent and immoral actions of the gangsters, promised to arrest them, and the renowned sniper Nolan was appointed as the chief detective to resolve the case. 

What Nolan planned was to mobilize all detectives that night to round up all the gangsters in the city at once. However, only the gang's leader, Trent, evaded capture and cleverly concealed himself. Soon after, Trent posted bail for his subordinates with a hefty sum of money. Thus, Nolan's plan to catch them all failed, but through this, he realized that Trent was indeed the main perpetrator of the incident. He intruded into the cafe managed by Steve and ordered Trent to leave the city within twenty-four hours. However, Trent’s mistress, Mack Pie, had feelings that disturbed Nolan. After that, Mack Pie recalled her love for Trent and found herself thinking of Nolan.

Even after twenty-four hours passed, Trent did not leave the city. Instead, he reinforced his defenses and showcased the forces that would resist the authorities. Mack Pie pretended to be infatuated with Nolan and several times lured him to the police station to turn him in, but Nolan was too astute to fall for it. One evening, while Donovan was attempting a phone call from the base of the Riverside gang, he was shot and killed. Following the murder, Nolan resigned from his detective position. Upon hearing that Nolan had left his detective duties, the underworld criminals began to raise their heads again, and Trent held a celebratory feast.

Afterward, Nolan fell into despair, began drinking heavily, and showered all sorts of shame on Mack Pie, Trent, and his subordinates. However, Mack Pie sympathized with Nolan, revealing that it was actually Trent, not Nolan, who had killed Donovan. As a result, Trent assaulted Mack Pie and turned against Nolan swiftly, while Nolan rushed in to invade Trent's stronghold. At that moment, Steve mistook Nolan for Trent and vice versa, resulting in both men collapsing at the scene. Thus, by a strange twist of fate, Nolan saved his life and took Mack Pie as his wife.  

Explanation  
The nature of the film – An initial work by Mr. Gromit titled "Dark Streets" along with the same director and starring actors, presents a detective interest set against the backdrop of dark streets.  

Original work – The original author, Oliver H. P. Frost, was a journalist and thus well aware of the inner workings of society's underbelly.  

Director – Joseph von Sternberg, a genius who had released films such as "Dark Streets" and "The Last Command."  

Starring – Gromit, who displayed remarkable acting in "Dark Streets," suddenly rose to stardom.  

Production – Paramount Company  

  

**Summary**  

This article reviews the film "The Drag Net," highlighting its plot involving a detective's challenge against a cunning gang leader and his dynamics with his mistress. The film serves as a reflection of the period's societal issues, particularly regarding crime and moral decay. Historians may find the portrayal of law enforcement’s struggle against crime of interest, especially how it mirrors the broader social realities faced during Korea’s colonial period, suggesting a complex relationship between law, order, and societal decay under colonial rule.

◇
어느 都市의 下層界에 퍼-후랑크 트렌트라는 惡黨으 頭目이잇섯다 보기에는 柔和한 好男子이엿스나 항상 만흔 部下를 使用하야 가진 惡行을 하고잇섯다.
最近에 殺人事件이 니러낫다 그러고 이 事件은 트렌트의 所行이라고 警察의 눈에 빗치엿다.
그러나 사흘동안의 工判이 이난뒤에 有力한 證人이엇던 者에게인지 狙擊을 當하엿슴으로 事件은 그대로 다시 캄캄한 속으로 드러갓다.
그러나 警察은 暴戾非道한 無賴漢들의 行動에 憤激하야 그 惡黨들을 톄포하기를 장담하고 二挺拳銃의 名人노-란은 探偵長으로하야 이 事件을 解決하고저하엿다.
노-란을 무슨생각을하엿는지 그날밤으로 刑事를 總出動을식여서 市中에잇는 無賴漢을 一擧에 檢擧하여 버렷다 그러나 홀노 그들의 頭目 트렌트만이 그그물에 걸니지안이하고 교묘하게 자최를 감추엇다 그러고 얼마 後에 트렌트는 만흔돈을 내여서 그의 部下를 保釋出獄을 식엿다.
이리하야 노-란의 一網打盡的計劃은 고만헛되하되고 말엇다 그러나 이것으로 드듸여 트렌트가 그 事件의 主犯者인 것을 알고 스스로 븨 스티-브가 經營하는＜147＞ 카페-에 드러가서 트렌트다려 二十四時間안으로 그 市를 退去하기를 命令하엿다 그러나 그 트렌트의 情婦 막크파이-는 노-란의 마음을 을엇다 그런 後 막크파이-는 트렌트에게 쏠녓든마음이 노-란을 생각하게 되엿다.

二十四時間이지나도 트렌트는 그 市에서 退去치를 아니하엿다 도리혀 防備를 튼튼히하야서 官憲과 對抗하려는 勢力을 보혓다.
막크파이-는 엇더케하든지 노-란을 戀慕하는체하며 그를 저희으로 잡아올녀고하여 여러번 警察署로 노-란을 차저가기지하엿스나 그러나 노-란이 을닐닭이엄섯다.
어는날저녁 리버-싸이드 十二番 惡黨의 根據에서 探偵도나반이 電話을하든中 失手하야도나반에게 彈丸이날너 죽어버렷다.
노-란은 殺人을하엿다는 關係로 그후에 探偵을고만두엇다 노-란이 探偵을고만두엇다는 所聞을듯고 下層의 惡黨들은 다시 고개를 들고 트렌트는 祝宴을 베프럿다.
그후에 노-란은 自暴하야 술을먹기를 시작하야 막크파이-압헤서 트렌트와 그의 部下에게 온갖 羞恥를 當하＜148＞엿스나 막크파이-는 도리혀 노-란을 同情하야 도나반을 죽인 것은 노-란이아니라 트렌트라는 것을 일너주엇다.
이것으로 말미아마 트렌트는 막크파이-를치고 노-란을 첫스나 노-란은 猛進하야 트렌트의 牙城에 侵하엿다 그마츰 스트부는 노란을 차오다가 트렌트를 노-란으로알고 트렌트는 스티부를 노-란으로 알고 서로앗다 이리하야 두사람은 그 자리에 쓰러지고말엇다.
그럼으로 노-란은 묘하게 목슴을 救하야 드듸여 막크파이-를 안해로 삼엇다.
