**Summary**  

This announcement highlights the ongoing progress of the company's tenth anniversary commemorative project, which includes the establishment of a regional lecture team for public engagement. The intent is expressed for readers to utilize this initiative actively. From the perspective of Korean colonial history, the organization of local lecture teams reflects an effort to engage and educate the public within a framework that may have been influenced by colonial policies regarding education and propaganda. This can reveal insights into how cultural and intellectual movements were facilitated or controlled during the colonial period.
