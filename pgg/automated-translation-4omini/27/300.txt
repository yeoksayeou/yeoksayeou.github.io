100 Percent Modern  
-Modern Chastity-  
If they are beautiful lips,  
Kissing a hundred times would be just a matter of course.  
My lover. Even a thousand times,  
Love, through a hundred iterations, is something new.  

In the frenzy of the gale,  
Searching for my honey,  
In the honeycomb's inside, where it 
digs its own bite.  
The unruly fellows who’ve passed through here,  
Yellow bee, white bee, striped bee...  

-30, 5, 10-  

**Summary**  

The poem explores the theme of modern love and intimacy, juxtaposing the physicality of attraction with the notion of constant renewal in affection. It highlights an idealized modernity where love is depicted as a fresh experience, emphasizing a break from traditional restraint. This reflection on personal relationships during the colonial period can reveal how cultural influences challenged prevailing societal norms regarding love and expression, offering insights into the evolving identities and values among Koreans in the 1930s. Historians might find interest in the way literary expressions reveal the tensions between modernity and tradition during this era of colonial rule.
