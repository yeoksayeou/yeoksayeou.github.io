新舊雜誌交換 

別乾坤『第五號』『第七號』『第十一號』는 破天荒의 人氣로 發行卽時品絶되온바 近日 해외에게 신 동포여러분으로서 特別注文이 있삽기 玆에 特告하오니 누구시던지 前記號의 雜誌가 잇사오면 本社로 보내주시면 感謝를 마지않는 同時에 이번달 新雜誌나 그러치 안하면 所願하시는 號를 交換하야 드리겟슴니다 但期限은 六月十五日임니다. 

(落頁된 것은 無效) 

開闢社發送部＜42＞ 

Summary

This article announces a special exchange initiative for a magazine called 별건곤, encouraging readers to send in their old issues in exchange for new ones. There is an emphasis on connecting with fellow Koreans overseas and a deadline of June 15 for submitting the magazines. This exchange program reflects the broader trends of cultural connection among Koreans during the colonial period and highlights efforts to maintain a sense of community amid challenges posed by colonial rule. Historians may find this interesting as it showcases the active participation of Koreans in cultural activities despite their circumstances.
