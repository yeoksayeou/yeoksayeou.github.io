Special Notice  
From this issue onward, this magazine will particularly feature issues related to politics and economics. Dear readers, we kindly ask for your attention to this matter and would greatly appreciate it if you would send us your essays, commentaries, and other writings on the topic.

  
**Summary**  

This article serves as a notification to its readers about the inclusion of political and economic issues in the magazine from that point forward. It encourages submissions of related essays and commentary from readers. Historians studying this period may find it particularly interesting as it indicates a shift in the magazine's editorial focus, reflecting broader social and political changes in colonial Korea during the early 1930s.
