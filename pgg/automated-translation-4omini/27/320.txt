기사형태	문예기타 (Literary Other)  

The Temptation of New Green  
		方春海  
The first bloom of spring corresponds to the age of a fifteen or sixteen-year-old, while the late spring to early summer resembles the young adulthood of someone over twenty. This season feels energetic, much like young boys and girls in their youth, who are busy in their minds and in their activities. Although spring brings joy, underneath it all lingers a sense of unease, distractions, and a restless heart.

The wind blows fiercely, but people flock together like flies for sightseeing, spending a moment amidst the blossoms before being swept away in the gusts, losing their composure. 

However, as the flowers bloom and the splendidly grotesque phenomena emerge, the original color of green leaves sprouts anew; this soft green, as it pervades each branch, brings a tranquil state of mind, filling one with refreshing calmness and steadfastness.

When summer arrives and the leaves have fully unfurled, turning dark green akin to the strong years past forty, the new green of early summer represents a significantly beautiful young adulthood, wherein everything is delightful and hopeful.

Whether in the mountains, fields, or riversides, as we observe this atmosphere, we sing of how fortunate life is and how beautiful nature can be.

Green! I greatly admire this color. The green of grass and trees, the green of the sky, the green of the sea—all of it always delights my eyes and cools my heart. Each of these harmonizing shades of new green beckons me into a land of temptation and allure.

**Summary**

The article beautifully describes the transition from the vibrancy of spring to summer, metaphorically linking these seasonal changes to stages in life. The writer expresses a profound admiration for the color green, viewing it as a symbol of youth, hope, and happiness. This connection to nature reflects a longing for harmony and fulfillment during a time of societal upheaval under colonial rule. Historians may find the poetic expression of yearning for natural beauty amidst the hardships of the 1930s in Korea particularly illuminating, symbolizing the struggles and aspirations of the Korean people during this era of oppression.
