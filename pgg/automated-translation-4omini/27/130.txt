Modern Methods of Fighting Poverty  
Living Poverty Hall  

First Words  
In ancient times, Yan Zi practiced contentment, Han Tui-ji sent away poverty, and Yang Xiong pursued poverty. The terms "contentment," "sending away poverty," and "pursuing poverty" are all strategies for the poor and never for those with wealth. However, these words are not very applicable to the modern poor. When one is hungry and their wife is bedridden with illness, how can they be content with the teachings of Yan Zi or Confucius, and live comfortably? The inner life of Han Tui-ji and Yang Xiong was rich enough to compose literary works and songs, but these modern expressions of comfort seem to be mere frivolities of the well-fed. At the very least, a modern person suffering under the relentless assault of poverty must engage in the study of wealth creation or else they must know the methods of fighting poverty. However, we, like the calves, are too ashamed to study wealth creation, and even if we did study it, our circumstances may prevent us from achieving it, making it essential that we understand these methods of fighting poverty well. It follows that among the impoverished, we must all skillfully engage in this fight for survival until the last victory. We should fight with the resolve of Han Xin’s double encampment strategy. While those who lose in political struggles may have opportunities to recover, those defeated in the struggle against poverty have no chance of recovery. 

Oh, all you poor folks!  

Don’t resign yourselves to suffer in silence as if poverty is merely your fate, eventually resulting in despair and thoughts of suicide by jumping into the Han River after taking a train there. Instead, study these methods of fighting poverty. This may seem like a mere boast, but it is far more noble and interesting than plots to kill or seize wealth from others.

1. The Art of Living Rent-Free  
Among the essential needs of food, clothing, and shelter, the issue of housing in places like Seoul has become especially difficult. It is unthinkable for someone with no money to buy or rent a house. A poor person can manage to live in a house that may be rented without money. At present, since landlords have deteriorated, it has become challenging to find houses that can be rented without upfront payments. If someone has a connection, when moving from their rented house, they can obtain permission from the landlord and move in; this will not be considered trespassing, but can be viewed as a full rental transfer. Surely a person with no money cannot pay rent for that month. However, if viewed from the inability of the poor to pay, it may be a better discussion just to avoid paying altogether. After one or two months of non-payment, a benevolent landlord might cover moving expenses and more, while a malevolent one could apply to the courts for eviction. Thus, it may be possible to live rent-free for several months. 

2. How to Ride the Tram Without Paying  
This is not an issue for those who have special privileges, such as employees of electricity companies, police officers, or reporters, who can ride for free. For those without such privileges hoping to ride the tram without paying, you must be clever. When you board the tram, simply raise your hand a bit to indicate you are issuing a ticket in the air; the conductor may assume you are presenting a ticket that is not visible and allow you to pass without charging you. However, recklessness may lead to trouble, especially during busy times. Always keep at least five jeon on hand as a backup to prevent issues.  

3. The Fish-Without-Money Method  
Even a poor person can enjoy delicious fish. Nowadays, fishmongers have a variety of fresh fish available—who wouldn’t salivate at the sight? But don’t let an empty pocket discourage you. If you understand the methods of fighting poverty well, you can enjoy great fish without money. First, eat the roe of the fish; then, wait for the fishmonger to become busy selling and pick the largest fish. 

4. The Art of Assistance Without Money  
Assistance typically refers to helping someone with money or goods. However, a poor person may find it impossible to assist in this way without feeling ashamed. So, if you ever receive an invitation to a celebratory event, submit a list saying "One serving of rice" and "One bowl of honey rice cake." The host will assume it is complimentary food items, yet in reality, it means you're hoping to have free food for yourself.

First Effective Method of Fighting Poverty  
Yunjeong Gyuja

When a poet is asked to explain how to write poetry, they usually cannot articulate it well. The same applies to a poor person trying to articulate how they survive. While preparing to submit this to the editor at Byeolgeongon, before even sending it, I am already being told that "this isn’t enough."  

"I ask that you go borrow a hammer from the father of Bokdong at the back house and bring it back."  
  
Soo-nam goes but soon comes back empty-handed saying, "He said he can’t lend it."  

"That friend is too much. Isn’t it possible to borrow something? There’s no way we don’t have a hammer in our house."  

Is this a method of fighting poverty? This kind of person would likely remain poor even if they became a millionaire—this isn’t a strategy for fighting poverty. It has been quoted that the secret of fighting poverty lies in managing credit well. If one borrows more than they can handle, they would start getting refused by lenders, something that would turn into a benefit! However, how does it benefit a person when their prior creditor keeps coming back, asking for payment?

Even though this training is precious for becoming adept at not paying off debts, too much can lead to headaches or losing all your hair! There are methods for that too."  

"What methods?"  

"Engaging in a bath; it can completely wash away all worries."  

"Then that method needs bath expenses, making it not so effective for the poor."  

Navigating their thoughts on what constitutes strategy highlights the different challenges faced by the poor and strategies for coping with them. Understanding the nuances of managing financial circumstances becomes a critical aspect of survival.  

This piece, while humorous, humorously contrasts the dire straits of poor living with whimsical solutions representing a detachment from the genuine struggles of poverty. From a historical perspective, scholars may find interest in how such discussions reflect contemporary issues of class and economics under colonial rule in Korea through an ironic lens.

**Summary**  

This article explores various humorous and ironic strategies that the poor can employ to navigate their challenges without money, emphasizing a deep understanding of survival methods. The author's reflections highlight social issues surrounding poverty during the colonial era in Korea, shedding light on both individual struggles and societal perceptions of poverty that may be appealing for historical scholars examining socioeconomic conditions during this time.

첫말
녯날에 顔子는 安貧을 하고 韓退之는 送窮을 하고 楊雄은 逐貧을 하얏다. 安貧이니 送窮이니 逐貧이란 말이 모도다 가난한 사람의 수작이오, 결코 돈 잇는 사람의 수작은 안이다. 그러나 현대의 貧者에게는 아주 맛지 안는 말이다. 배가 곱하서 정신이 홰홰들니고 妻臥病床兒呼飢할 때에 顔子가 안이라 顔子의 선생 孔子이면 엇지 安貧을 하고 편이 누어 잇슬 수가 잇스며 韓退之, 楊雄과 가티 內面 생활이 풍부하면서도 文과 賦를 짓기 위하야 문자로만 送窮이니 逐貧이니 하는 것은 너무도 배부른 사람의 興타령과 갓다. 적어도 현대 사람으로 실제생활에 잇서서 시시각각에 襲來하는 가난에 고통을 當하고 신음하는 사람은 그까진 미지근한 수작을 가지고는 생활을 유지할 수가 업다. 적극적으로 致富術을 연구 하던지 그럿치 안으면 鬪貧術을 알지 안으면 안될 것이다. 그러나 우리는 뿔송아지派처럼 致富術을 연구하기도 창피하고, 연구한다 하야도 우리의 처지 경우에 치부할 수도 업슨 즉 결국 이 鬪貧術을 잘 아는 것박게는 當分間 별 도리가 업슬 것이다. 그러고 보면 우리 돈 업는 사람은 누구나 이 鬪貧術을 잘 알어서 최후 승리를 하기까지 잘 싸워야 하겟다. 싸우되 韓信의 배수진 이상의 각오를 가지고 싸워야 되겟다. 정치투쟁에 패배을 한 사람은 다시 捲土重來할 기회가 잇지만은＜78＞ 이 鬪貧에 패배한 사람은 다시 이러날 기회가 업는 것이다. 만천하 가난뱅이 제군!
가난한 것을 제군의 한 운명으로만 알고 혼자 고통을 하며 悲泣하다가 최후에 아주 낙심까지 하야 업는 돈에 電車까지 타고 漢江 철교에 가서 投水自殺할 생각을 말고 이 鬪貧術을 잘 연구하라. 이것이 엊지보면 한 噓言과 갓지만은 남을 죽이고 뺏는 致富術보다는 얼마나 신성하고 흥미가 잇는 일이냐.

第四, 돈업시 전차 타는 법
전기회사의 관계자라던지 刑事巡査나 신문기자 등과 가티 파스 또는 마크의 특권을 가지고 무료승차를 하는 것은 물론 여긔에 문제거리도 안이다. 파스도 업고 무슨 마크도 붓치지 안코 그양 곱다랏케 돈 업시 전차를 타랴거던 남 먼저 차에 나리되 그저 손만 번적 드러라. 그러면 차장은 그 뒤에 잇는 사람이 차표를 낸다는 의미로 알고 그양 통과를 식힐 것이다 .그러나 그럿타고 해서 경우와＜79＞ 형편을 살피지 안코 함부로 하다가는 큰 코 닷치고 대 망신을 할 것이다. 그것은 꼭 사람이 만히 탄 경우에 쓸 것이오, 사람이 적게 탈 때에는 좀 문제이다. 그리고 안전책은 鬪貧用 特別軍資金으로 최하 5전은은 보관하엿다가 여차하면 지불을 하여야 한다. 그리고 아주 突擊鬪將으로 나서랴거던 여러 사람과 가티 차에 나려서 시침을 떼고 가되 만일 車掌이 차표를 청구하거던 不問曲直하고 달녀드려서 차장의 뺨을 한 번 딱 따리고 손님에게 모욕도 분수가 잇지 차표를 두 번식 밧는 법이 어듸 잇느냐고 하여라. 그러면 여러 승객도 물론 차장의 잘못을 말할 것이오. 자기 亦 무사통과가 될 것이다. 그러나 이것이야 일시 전술에 불과한 것이오 鬪貧者中 惡鬪를 하는 자가 안이면 못할 것이다.

第五, 無錢食魚術

第一有效鬪貧術
雲庭居士

시를 잘 쓴다고 그더러 시 짓는 법을 설명하라면 한 마듸도 못한다고 엇던 시인은 말하엿다. 가난방이보고 가난방이의 살어 가는법을 설명하라면 할 말이 업는 것이다.
이러케 써서 別乾坤 편집 선생께 보내려 하난대 봉투에 우표도 부치기 전에 또 달겨 들어서 『이 말 한마듸만으로는 안된다』고 떼를 쓴다. 떼에 敗戰하여 노앗스니 쓰기는 무어던지 써야겟는데.......

『수남아, 뒷집 복동이 아버지한테 가서 못하나 박고 곳 가저 올 터이니 장도리를 잠간 빌녀 줍시사 해오너라』
수남이가 뒷집에 갓다가 그냥 븬 손으로 와서
『장도리가 달켓스닛가 못 빌녀 드린다고 그래요』
