기사제목	그리운 搖籃, 流行小曲 제3회 당선발표 入選 第1席 (Beloved Cradle, Announcement of the 3rd Selection of Popular Songs, 1st Place)  
필자	載寧 鄭興弼  
기사형태	시 (Poetry)  

Beloved cradle, announcement of the 3rd selection of popular songs, 1st place  
載寧 鄭興弼  

A  
On a fragrant day of lush green summer  
When the gentle clouds bloom brightly,  
Welcoming the flowing water from the river,  
Where we grew up, in the shade of silver birch.  

B  
When the cuckoo sings in the fragrant garden,  
The flowing water cools the spring,  
Holding a basket, we picked raspberries,  
Where we grew up, in the back hill of pine trees.  

C  
When the morning dew gnaws and the day dawns,  
We are terrified by our own voice and tremble,  
As the stream wades across,  
Where we grew up, a little creek. <24>  

**Summary**  

The poem depicts nostalgic memories of childhood, evoking the serene beauty of nature where the author grew up. It captures elements of summer, like blooming clouds, singing cuckoos, and playful activities such as picking raspberries. This piece reflects a yearning for simpler, more innocent times, likely resonating with experiences common to many during the colonial period in Korea. Historians may find this work intriguing as it illustrates the emotional landscape of Korean society amidst colonial challenges, signaling a longing for connection to the past.
