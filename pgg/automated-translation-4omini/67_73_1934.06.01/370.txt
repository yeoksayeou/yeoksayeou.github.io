기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)  

This month's issue has been published as a combined issue for May and June without a preface due to some unavoidable circumstances that cannot be mentioned. However, starting from July, we plan to publish it monthly as usual. 

This issue features mainly historical tales of science and a special focus on true stories, including tales of the world 100 years from now and extraordinary escape stories. Though we had initially planned for more content, due to special circumstances of the contributors, we are unable to provide as much as we wanted, yet I believe it will be interesting enough for our readers.

The next July issue is planned as a grand special edition to commemorate the 15th anniversary of our founding. Additionally, as part of our business initiatives, we have decided to issue "Kaebyeok" once again. We hope for your continued affection and support.

Regarding the lyric contest, this time we received an astonishing total of over 2,000 entries due to the great interest. However, it is a significant regret that we have not yet come across any outstanding work worthy of a "special selection." Nevertheless, do not lose heart and continue to strive for the opportunity to shine in the special selections! This is what I wish for. (Notice) <66>

**Summary**

This editorial note outlines the publication's circumstances, celebrating its 15th anniversary and encouraging participation in a lyric contest. The mention of stories set 100 years in the future and extraordinary real-life tales reflects the era's fascination with progress and survival. For historians, the issue exemplifies the challenges of publishing during colonial times, illustrating the interplay of creativity and constraints faced by Korean intellectuals under Japanese rule.

남은 잉크
이 달 호는 특별히 말못할 부득이한 사정으로 인하야 더구나 늣게되야 웃절수 업시 5, 6월 합병호로 내엿습니다. 그대신 다음 7월부터는 제달 제달 정긔발행을 할 작정입니다.
이번 호는 주로 과학긔사와 특집 실화호로 담엇습니다. 100년후의 세상 이야기와 비상선돌파 실화가 그것입니다. 웬만히 할 예정이엿스나 글 써주신분의 특별사정으로 충분히는 못되엿스나 그래도 흥미잇게 읽으시리라고 밋듭니다.
다음 7월호는 創社 15周年記念號로 大大 특집을 할 작정입니다. 그리고 긔업사업의 하나로써 로히 「開闢」을 또다시 발행키로 하엿습니다. 만흔 애호와 성원을 주시기 바랍니다.
류행소곡은 갈사록 그 인긔가 비상하야 금번에는 응모 홍수가 2,000여 건이라는 놀라운 수효를 어덧습니다. 그러나 아직까지 「특선」될 힘잇는 력작을 엇지 못한 것이 큰 유감입니다. 그러나 낙망마시고 게속 발분하여서 특선의 광명을 차지 하소서! 하고 바라는 바입니다.(社告)＜66＞
