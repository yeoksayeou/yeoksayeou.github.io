**Summary**  

The article announces the establishment of various branch offices across Korea and in Osaka, Japan, detailing their locations and branch heads. This reflects the administrative expansion of Korean businesses and organizations during the colonial period, highlighting the interconnectivity of Korean regions and diasporas. Historians may find the establishment of these branches significant as it indicates the growth of local governance and commerce amidst colonial rule, portraying a robust, organized effort to bolster local infrastructure.
