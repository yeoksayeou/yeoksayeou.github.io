Tears Flow, Announcement of Winners of the 3rd Popular Song Contest, Selected 2nd Place  
信川 康承翰  

A  
In the tranquil night when the waves of the sea dance,  
As the crescent moon tinted by rain weeps,  
To the flowing song like a dream,  
A nameless flower sheds tears.

B  
On a night when the blue branches sway in the silver,  
As the peach blossoms flutter in the gentle wind,  
To the tender song that flows like a stream,  
The wandering traveler sheds tears.

C  
In the tear-filled night of my heart,  
As the faint breeze flows on the riverbank,  
Yearning for the lost old dreams,  
I shed tears for the love that has been lost.

**Summary**  

This piece presents a poignant exploration of emotions tied to nature and unfulfilled dreams through a lyrical format. Each stanza conveys a deep sense of sorrow and reflection, illustrating how the beauty of the night and the gentle flow of music evoke tears. From a historical perspective, the poem reflects the emotional struggles of individuals during the colonial period, highlighting the impact of Japanese rule on personal identities and cultural expressions.

눈물지어요, 流行小曲 제3회 당선발표 入選 2席
						信川 康承翰
A
바다 물결 넘노는 고요한 그 밤
재빗 물든 초생달 울고 잇슬때
꿈결가티 흐르는 슯흔 노래에
이름모를 풀꼿이 눈물지어요.

B
푸른가지 실버들 나붓기든 밤
홀노피인 복사꼿 흐날니든때
흘너흘너 들니는 설은 노래에
지향업는 길손은 눈물지어요.

C
눈물 어린 내맘에 히미하든 밤
강 언덕에 간엷은 바람흐를때
일허버린 옛 꿈을 주서모으며
애닯헛든 사랑에 눈물지어요.
