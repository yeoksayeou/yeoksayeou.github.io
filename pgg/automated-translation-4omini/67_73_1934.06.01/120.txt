기사제목	흘르는 人生, 漫文 (Flowing Life, Prose)  
필자	金陵人  
기사형태	문예기타 (Literary Other)  

Is life inherently flowing?  

Is it like a wandering cloud drifting in the sky, without a clear direction?  

Today, I lean against the window, gazing at the distant future, sighing. - Oh! The pain of a troubled life!  

When I came to know 永愛, it was not by chance, and when I say that I am grateful beyond measure to have received her love - there is no doubt my feelings for 永愛 were truly profound.  

While one might say the purity of a young woman is precious and profound, the sincerity of a young man cannot be inferior. Love transcends everything; what use are status and gold? My affection for 永愛 was not based on her beauty, not her knowledge, nor her wealth.  

It was not her family background or her fame. It was her heart, as pure as autumn waters. It was her character, tender and heartfelt! 永愛 too, in my great attitude, was just like me. At least, I loved in such a way that my pure personality, and her clear heart, merged - and brighter than any poppy flower, our love bloomed.  

In this vast world, if just two people live and love deeply, even if the whole world forsakes us, won't we be lonely no more?  

Oh! Sky, rejoice!  

Earth, dance!  

Since the beginning of time, there has been no greater sensation than young men and women loving freely. - But time flows on.  

As time passes, life changes. Just as seasons change from the scorching summer to the refreshing autumn, the foolishness of thinking that the passion of love will last forever!  

With the cold sigh tossed aside, 永愛 departs!  

I was left in despair, as if my thoughts had been covered in darkness.  

Even if this world seems empty, how can it be so? But upon reflection, I realize I was never worthy of loving 永愛 in the first place.  

To love a daughter of a noble family, fair and cultured, and modern like 永愛, to take care of her, to satisfy her desires - first, I would need wealth!  

Second, I would need 100% health - to be complete!  

Third, I would need ample free time!  

However, I lacked all these attributes.  

永愛 has gone.  

She has completely discarded me.  

Even while harboring resentment, I sincerely wished for 永愛's happiness.  

As time goes by, a year passes.  

This spring, 永愛 graduated from her girls' school and is to marry a wealthy doctor.  

I received an invitation to her wedding.  

On that day, I sent a congratulatory message and sat in my dim boarding room, envisioning the vibrant scene of 永愛's wedding.  

Words cannot express my feelings!  

Yet, I bit my lip and held it in.  

I wished for 永愛's happiness again.  

Time flows -  

Two, three more years have swiftly passed.  

I, while wandering through the lonely world, looked into the mirror and saw my countenance, and even I found it terribly worn.  

At this moment, I believed my body would be well and that 永愛's married life had met with disaster, and I was overwhelmed with despair.  

How could that marriage not break down?  

How could her home not fall apart and drift north to the countryside?  

What will become of 永愛?  

Everything remains an unsolvable riddle, while time and life continue to flow.  

Year by year, I begin to forget.  

Yet, just last summer! I had to go on a business trip to 金剛山.  

After thoroughly exploring the world-renowned mountain, I stayed in 溫井里 for about a week, organizing manuscripts. One rainy, misty morning, while waiting for a train at 外金剛驛 coming from 高城, among the crowd I spotted a worn-down young woman!  

A woman holding a child!  

Ah! Is she not surely 永愛?  

Feeling a mixture of surprise and joy, I hesitated to approach her!  

"Is it possible?" I thought.  

Then, as if ignoring me - was I mistaken?  

It was only then I realized I had mistaken her identity and nearly faced disgrace in front of a stranger.  

But "This woman could not possibly be in a frail state!" I thought, as 永愛 is a health-conscious athlete!  

She should possess a vitality, not a pale face.  

And her expression would be sharp and full of vigor, not timid and cowering.  

While having such thoughts, I boarded the train.  

The train swiftly headed towards 安邊驛, gliding along the scenic eastern sea.  

I stared wide-eyed at the beautiful scenery around me, utterly absorbed in its beauty.  

The train passed 松田驛, heading even further east, yet such things hardly registered in my mind.  

"Teacher!"  

I was startled by a call from behind.  

It was that worn-out woman again!  

I could hardly comprehend.  

"Is it you, 永愛?"  

I was dumbfounded. So this woman really is 永愛?  

"Seeing that you recognized me, I deliberately pretended to ignore you! So you must have thought I was someone else."  

I still could not find the words.  

“But, meeting like this, if we pass by without acknowledgment, who knows when we'll meet again… perhaps I'll never get to see you again."  

Overcome with a myriad of emotions, I could only stay silent.  

"I have seen you several times in 溫井里. So I became aware of your purpose for being here. However, I silently observed you!"  

“Why don’t you come over here?”  

I finally spoke and shifted my place.  

"That's alright."  

"Why?"  

"I must get to the other side."  

"Why?"  

"Please do not ask."  

I was at a loss for words at 永愛's attitude.  

"Teacher! You look quite distressed."  

永愛 seemed emotional as she looked into my face, tears welling in her eyes.  

"You too, 永愛!"  

"That's only natural!"  

As she answered, she quietly sighed.  

I too felt a heavy yearning rising from the depths of my chest.  

Even if I harbored resentment towards 永愛, upon seeing that inscrutable expression on her face, my resentment seemed to dissolve like spring snow.  

“I must go!”  

When I saw 永愛 leaving, I could not say anything. I pressed my heart with my hand as the train reached 安邊驛.  

"I am getting off here."  

永愛 once again approached me and left with this brief message.  

"Then won't you go straight to 京城? Is 元山 your hometown?"  

"I can’t say for sure since it doesn’t matter."  

永愛 exited the train.  

As the train departed, I caught a glimpse of 永愛’s figure from the corner and it faded from view.  

I stared blankly out of the train window.  

The setting sun was accompanied by clouds drifting across the western sky.  

Ah - is life not like those flowing clouds?  

Is it not an unsolvable riddle!  

What has become of 永愛?  

What is she doing now?  

Who is that child; whose is he?  

Is she dreaming of life with another man?  

What did she come to 金剛山 for?  

All remains unknown.  

There is no one to ask about it.  

Yet, even that remains unnecessary.  

Life flows on, passing another year. Today, spring rains sprinkle gently on the asphalt road.  

The sound of spring rain lightly enters the heart of a troubled life, while I futilely try to cover my ears against its whispers.  

Strangely, the sound evokes threads of memories.  

But, what good does it do to think?  

It is originally a flowing life. -<65>  

**Summary**  

In this reflective prose, the narrator contemplates the nature of life and love, lamenting the passage of time that alters everything, including relationships. The story reveals a deep, unfulfilled love for a woman named 永愛, illustrated through his memories and the bittersweet encounter years later. The loneliness and emotional complexity of the narrator's feelings serve as a poignant reminder of the struggles faced in relationships during the colonial era in Korea, highlighting how societal expectations and personal desires intertwine. Historians might find the depiction of love amidst societal pressure particularly revealing of the emotional landscape during Korea's colonial period.

그 어린애는 누구의 애일가? 永愛는 다시 다른 男子와 생활을 꿈이엿는가?
金剛山엔 엇재 왓던가? 모다 알 수 업는 일이다. 누구에게 무를 수가 업는 일이다.
그러나 무를 필요조차 업는 일이다.
그것 역시 흘느는 세월은 다시 일년을 지나왓다. 오늘은 아스팔트우에 느진 봄날 구진 비조차 촉촉하게 나리고 잇다.
슯흠을 가진 인생의 가슴속에 속속 드리 숨여드는 봄비 소리! 듯지를 안으려고 귀를 막어도 아득히 속삭이는 봄비소리!
이상하게도 봄비소리는 한가닥 두가닥 추억의 실마리를 끄집어 내인다.
