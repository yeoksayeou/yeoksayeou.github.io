필자	개벽사  
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)

개벽사  
The upcoming July issue is a special commemorative issue celebrating the 15th anniversary, and it is a grand feature. Please promote it widely from now on and wait with great anticipation for how amazing it will be. <31>

**Summary**

The article announces that the forthcoming July edition of the magazine will mark its 15th anniversary with a special feature. It encourages readers to promote the issue and express excitement for its contents. This reflects the cultural efforts of the magazine during a period when Korean media was significantly influenced by colonial rule, highlighting the importance of community engagement and national identity in publications of this time.
