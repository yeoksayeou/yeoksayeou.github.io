Special Notice  

The Readers' Club is highly successful with each issue. However, due to the limited space available, it is regrettable that we cannot publish all of your correspondence. Please write as concisely and clearly as possible. This way, all one million readers can share their opinions through this medium. Additionally, please send in local column pieces as well. This month, we have featured four of them. Please make sure they are straightforward yet informative. <65>  

**Summary**  

The article informs readers about the success of the Readers' Club and encourages them to contribute their thoughts and local news features concisely. It reflects an active attempt to engage a large audience in sharing their views and suggests a communal spirit among readers. From a historical perspective, this highlights how cultural publications during the colonial period sought to create a sense of community and identity among the Korean populace amidst the restrictions imposed by colonial rule.
