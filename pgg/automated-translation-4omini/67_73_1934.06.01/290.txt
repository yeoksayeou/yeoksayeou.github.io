기사제목	종달새의 동리 (The Village of the Lark)  
필자	影童 (Yeongdong)  
기사형태	시 (Poem)  

The village of the lark  

The village of the lark  
is among the wildflowers -  

Droplets,  
glistening as dew, fill the landscape.  

Clusters,  
the signboards of the white flowers.  

The village of the lark  
is among the wildflowers.  
-(March)-<41>  

**Summary**  

This poem paints a vivid picture of the simple beauty found in a village inhabited by larks, emphasizing the natural elements such as wildflowers and dew. Its imagery conjures feelings of tranquility and pastoral life, suggesting a harmony with nature. From a historical perspective, this piece reflects the nostalgia for rural life which was often idealized during the colonial period in Korea; it may serve as a commentary on the tensions between modernity and tradition amidst the rapid change of the 1930s.
