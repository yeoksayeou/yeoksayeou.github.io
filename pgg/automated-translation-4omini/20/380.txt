기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

十錢避暑法  

This method of summer escape costs only ten jeon! This is a peculiar way of spending the summer that was collected last year.  

By sending ten jeon and obtaining a single issue of the Children's magazine for August, the family comes together in a circle to read aloud. The collection consists solely of cool photographs and articles, and from every page flows a refreshing breeze that cools the entire home.  

By sending eleven one-jeon stamps to Kyŏngsŏng Kaebyeoksah, the Children's magazine and its supplement will be sent the very same day. <165>  

  

**Summary**  

The article describes a simple and budget-friendly method to escape the summer heat by purchasing a children’s magazine filled with pleasant articles and pictures, which helps to create a cool atmosphere at home. This reflects the social context in Korea under Japanese colonial rule, where access to affordable recreation was vital for family bonding and coping with the summer heat, suggesting a communal activity amidst economic constraints.
