展望車  

When calcium chloride is spread on the ground, it absorbs moisture from the air, which is why a phenomenon occurs. Anyone can see this.  

The longest novel in the world is said to be "Hakkenden" (八犬傳全卷) by the Japanese author Ryūzō Bakin, comprising 10,600,000 characters; I do not know if there is any longer one.  

Recently, it has been reported that in England, a hotel and car combined has been invented, where five people can comfortably stay for £40.  

The seismologist "Davyson" claims that the next great earthquake will happen between 1951 and 1959; we shall wait and see.  

It is said that a person's height increases during the day and decreases at night, but an American doctor called "Spurinka" has invented a method to increase height using electricity. He passes current through the knee cartilage, causing the cartilage to expand, thus increasing height. This effect is especially significant in childhood. Swimming and gymnastics also have an effect on height.  

Glass Hats  

Ordinary glass blocks out some ultraviolet rays. However, within this filtered ultraviolet range, there are portions beneficial to the human body, which has posed a significant question among scholars worldwide. However, in quick response, a trendy shop in Paris has introduced women's hats made of glass that purport to filter beneficial ultraviolet rays for health and fashion at the same time. Of course, since they are made of glass, the hats allow the hair to be visibly bright, making them seem amusing at first glance. Nevertheless, trends can be powerful; now, one cannot assert themselves without wearing a glass hat for the sake of health?  

Chewing Gum  

In England, it is said that the annual waste from discarded chewing gum wrappers amounts to £2 million.  

Traffic Accidents  

I wonder if the increase in urban prosperity corresponds directly with traffic accidents, but in the grand metropolis of London, which ranks as the world's number one, there have been 23,212 traffic accidents in the past year.  

**Summary**  

This article discusses various intriguing inventions and phenomena, including the method of increasing height using electricity and the introduction of glass hats in Paris that filter ultraviolet rays. It highlights the cultural trends of the time, such as fashion intertwined with health consciousness. Historians might find the juxtaposition of technological advancement and societal trends particularly revealing of the anxieties and adaptations of life under colonial rule during this period in Korea.

염화칼슘을 주악돌에 석거서 道路面에다 깔면 空中의 水分을 흡수하는 까닭에 몬지가 안이 난다는 것을 發見햇다. 누구든지는 이저버렷스나.

세게에서 뎨일 길은 소설은 일본인 龍澤馬琴著의 八犬傳全卷 106책 字數가 3,600만자라니 이 외에 더 긴것이 잇는지는 모르나.

최근 영국에서 기리 40「피-드」에 다섯 사람이 넉넉히 묵을 수 잇다는 여관 겸 자동차를 발명햇단다.

地震學의 대가 「따뷔손」이 曰 다음에 올 세계대지진은 1951년부터 8년까지라니 두고 보자지.

사람의 身長은 낫에는 늘어나고 밤에는 조라든다하지만 미국 「스푸링카-」라는 의사는 전기를 이용하야서 身長을 늘니는 것을 발명하엿다. 그는 무릅의 연골에 전류를 통하는 것이며 그러면 연골의 활동이 늘며 따라서 키가 커진다는 것이다. 특별히 어렷슬 때에 그 효과가 현저하다한다. 수영과 기계체조도 키를 늘니는데 効果가 잇다.

유리로 帽子를

보통 유리(硝子)는 자외광선을 幾分 막는다. 그런데 이 막히는 자외광선에는 인체에 필요한 部分이 만타하야 세계의 학자들 간에서는 매우 問題꺼리가 되엿다. 그러나 재바른 巴里의 流行商은 보건과 유행을 겸한 수정 비슷한 자외광선을 잡아드리는 유리로 婦人帽子를 맨드러 내엿다. 물논 유리로 맨든 것인 까닭에 모자는 썻서도 머리털은 환하게 보히며 처음 볼때는 매우 우습지만은 그러나 유행이란 무서운 것이라 인제는 유리모자를 안쓰면 행세를 못한다니 보건으로? 위생으로?
