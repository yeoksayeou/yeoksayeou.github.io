謝告  
I would like to express my gratitude for the special kindness of various local associates who provided abundant assistance for the exploration trip conducted by reporters from the South-North Team along the Gyeongui and Gyeongbu Lines. I sincerely apologize for the few articles that were unable to be published due to deletions in the coverage made during the exploration.

Separate from the editorial department of Byeolgeongon,  

The appendix inserted in this issue was regrettably printed in a single color due to insufficient time for printing, and I apologize for that. <125>  

**Summary**  

The article is a brief acknowledgment from the editorial staff of the magazine expressing gratitude to contributors who assisted in a journalistic expedition for articles covering various regions along the Gyeongui and Gyeongbu railway lines. It also apologizes for the omission of some articles and mentions an issue with the appendix's printing quality. This note reflects the operational challenges faced by media within Korea during the colonial period, highlighting the resource constraints and pressure to produce timely content under such conditions.
