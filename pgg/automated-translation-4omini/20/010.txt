기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)  

Marking the Ninth Anniversary of Our Foundation

In the midst of battling against stormy winds, as we traversed through lessons of diligence and examination, we have never been idle, particularly in promoting the fulfillment of the Gaebyeok (開闢) Movement. The continuous publication of "Eorini" (어린이), "Sinyeoseong" (신녀성), "Byeolgeongon" (별건곤), and "Haksaeng" (학생) is proof of this, as well as the issuance of various publications, the movement concerning the "Thirteen Principles," and the expansion of the "Saudya" (사우뎨). As a result, today, the total number of Gaebyeoksa (개벽사) publications amounts to several hundreds of thousands, and our writings have spread to various parts of the world, thus becoming a solid source of support for our many comrades. 

However, along with this heartfelt and profound recollection, it is with a sense of sorrow that on this day of culmination, we cannot help but share in the melancholy that has been felt since the passing of "Gaebyeok" last summer, which was like losing one arm of our movement. It is a deep-seated regret that today we are unable to express fully the emotions that swell within our hearts. There is little more to say. Reflecting upon the past serves to remind us that we must gather our resolve, and as a substitute for "Gaebyeok," we are now greeted with the new magazine "Hyesung" (혜성) which is set to invigorate our movement once more. Alongside this, we are planning new initiatives as a complementary endeavor to the publication efforts, in order to bravely march forward together with our allies and pledge to create a new record that will span from this tenth year onward. 

**Summary**

This article reflects on the ninth anniversary of the founding of the publication organization, expressing both pride in its accomplishments and sorrow over the loss of the “Gaebyeok” publication, which was significant to their movement. It underscores the ongoing dedication to publishing and the promotion of the Gaebyeok Movement, while signaling new initiatives with the introduction of the “Hyesung” magazine. Historians may find interest in the emotional connection to the "Gaebyeok" publication, as it represents the struggles of Korean intellectual movements during colonial rule.

기사형태	사고·편집후기

創社九周年을 마즈며
- 十八行略 -
폭풍광우와 싸호면서 도산검수를 수업시 넘어 오면서 그중에서도 우리는 개벽 운동의 충실을 도모하기에 한 때도 게으르지 못하엿나니 『어린이』 『신녀성』 『별건곤』 『학생』의 계속 발행이 그 것이요 출판물 간행이 그것이요 십삼도 긔본됴사 운동이 그것이요 사우뎨도의 확충이 또한 그것이엿다. 그리하야 오늘 개벽사 출판물의 총독자는 수십만으로 혜이고 글발은 해외 각디에까지 퍼지게 되야 스스로 이 수만흔 동지 후원의 든든한 힘을 밋게 된 것을 깃버하기에 니르럿다. 그러나 이 슯흐고 깃브고한 회고를 어울러 가지고 구 주년을 마지하는날 텬하 독자와 함께 섭섭한 생각을 가티하게 되는 것은 지난 병인 녀름에 『개벽』이 죽어 우리의 운동의 한 팔이 떠러진 그것이요 그리하야 이날 우리의 가슴에 용소슴치는 감회나마 싀원히 이약이 할 자유가 업는 그것이다. 더 쓸 말이업다. 지나간 일을 말하는 것은 오즉 압길을 가다듬자는 것이니 『개벽』대신으로 운동 중에 잇는 혜성(慧星) 잡지가 새로히 활약할 날이 갓가운 압날에 잇스며 잡지 간행의 자매 사업으로 새로히 게획하는 것이 또 한 가지 잇는 바 이리하야 새로운 진용으로써 텬하 동지와 함께 더욱 용맹히 싸화나감으로써 십 년째부터의 새 긔록을 지을 것을 스스로 맹서할 뿐이다.<1>
