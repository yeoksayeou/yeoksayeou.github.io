기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Advertisement/Editorial Notes)  

학생 만화 모집  
Middle school students, female students, and specialized students are invited to submit humorous or satirical cartoons. If selected, we will publish them in 별건곤 (Byeolgeongon) and present a small reward. Please submit your entries generously. <49>  

**Summary**  

This article is a call for cartoons from students, highlighting the magazine's focus on engaging youthful creativity and humor. It reflects the cultural role of student expression during the colonial period in Korea, noting how those in educational institutions were encouraged to participate in artistic endeavors. Historians might find it interesting as it showcases a form of cultural output amidst the broader socio-political landscape, emphasizing the importance of creative expression during a time of repression.

學生 漫畵 募集
