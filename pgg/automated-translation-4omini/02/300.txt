Submission Welcome  

Local Color! This is intended to flavor the pages with the waste of local readers. Write freely and as you please. Across the thirteen provinces, there must be all sorts of peculiar customs, strange habits, and bizarre historical legends. Individuals too, I encourage you to write about those. Anyone can send in whatever they wish regarding their locality. However, there are a few requests and restrictions that must be stated.  

1. Please do not write in a dull introduction and instead, write it in a more interesting and engaging manner.  

2. Manuscripts should be around 15 characters per line and about 60 lines in total.  

3. Please quote passages clearly when writing.  

4. Sending in photographs or similar materials is even better.  

The editing and selection will be left to this office, but a small fee will be offered for those whose submissions are published. <87>  

**Summary**  

The article encourages submissions from readers about local customs, habits, and legends, fostering a sense of community and cultural sharing among different regions. It emphasizes creativity and personal engagement in writing, inviting contributions that capture the local essence. This showcases the colonial era's emerging interest in regional identities and cultural documentation, significant for historians studying how such expressions of local pride were manifested during Japanese rule.

投稿歡迎
地方色! 이것은 地方讀者의 폐지로 맛겨둔다. 멋대로 잇는대로 投稿하라. 13도 방방곡곡에는 가지각색의 이상한 풍속, 야릇한 習慣, 駭怪한 역사 전설이 만흘 것이다. 인물도 좃타 그것을 쓰란 말이다. 누구나 자기지방의 것을 얼마던지 써 보내라. 그러나 요만한 所請과 制限은 드러주어야 한다.
1. 진부한 소개문 가티 쓰지 말고 좀 멋잇시 흥미잇게 읽을수 잇도록 쓸 일.
1. 원고지 1행 15자 60행 내외로 쓸 일.
1. 구절을 똑똑 떼여서 쓸 일.
1. 사진 가튼 것을 첨부하여 보내는 것은 더욱 조흔 일.
添削과 取捨는 본사에서 일임하되 게재되는 분에 대하야는 薄謝를 드리기로 함니다.＜87＞
