路上의 人
李商在 老人
As I was walking leisurely down the main road in 교동 (Kyo-dong), attracted by the 中山 (Chūzan) hat and bundled up in a thick scarf, I spotted an elderly man slowly making his way. Drawn in by the charm of the old hat, I quickly sketched a picture and recognized him as our esteemed 李商在 (I Sangjae) elder. At the remarkable age of 78, the sight of him enjoying a normal leisurely stroll is nothing short of a pleasant sight! No one can overlook the mere thought of his 78 years full of endeavors when witnessing this elderly man’s leisurely pace. He passes through the 교동 (Kyo-dong) road daily to visit the 청년회관 (Youth Center) in 종로 (Jongno).

兪珏卿 女史
Even if people call me a bad youth for sketching pictures of women passing by with my drawing notebook, it cannot be helped. Perhaps the 편즙장 (Pyeonjujang) manager has ordered me to draw a woman, so it is not my fault. Behind 탑골공원 (Tapgol Park) in Seoul, I saw a charming young female student walking cheerfully with a blue umbrella! The umbrella draws just as much attention as her appearance. When I approached her with my drawing notebook, I learned that she was none other than 兪珏卿 (Yoo Gyeok-kyung), the chairperson of the women’s youth association! A sight to behold, she had two children at home. However, she looked like a typical 17 or 18-year-old student when seen outdoors. (Please forgive my impertinence) <25>

**Summary**

This article sketches the everyday lives of two individuals, an elderly man, I Sangjae, and a young woman, Yoo Gyeok-kyung, as they navigate the streets of Seoul in 1926. The mention of their leisurely activities highlights a sense of normalcy and social interaction despite the broader context of colonial pressures. Historians might find interest in this portrayal of daily life, shedding light on how individuals maintained a sense of community and engagement during a time of cultural and political upheaval in Korea.

路上의 人
李商在 老人
漫畵노트로 기사를 엇으려고 아츰에 校洞 큰 길로 거니르노라닛가 中山모자에 행전치고 목도리 퉁퉁히 둘으고 집행이 풀업시 집고 천천히 거러오는 노인 한 분 中山모자 옛날 행전에 마음이 끌려 번쩍하고 한 장 그리고 보니 우리의 존경하야 마지안는 李商在 老人이시다. 78이나 되는 高齡으로 이럿케 치운날이 건전한 거름거리! 노인의 거름거리 보고 그의 健鬪 78년 일을 잠시라도 생각지 안는 이가 업슬 것이다. 노인은 매일 한 차례 校洞길을 거처 鐘路 靑年會館을 들는다.

兪珏卿 女史
漫畵노트를 들고 지나 다니는 여자를 노린다고 不良靑年이라고 욕을해도 할 수 업는일 편즙장 각하가 꼭 여자 한 분을 그려오라는 엄명이닛가 내 책임은 아니요. 서울 탑골공원 뒤로 보라ㅅ빗 만ㅅ도를 닙고 아장아장 거러오는 앙징한 녀학생 한 분! 보라ㅅ빗만큼 남의 눈을 만히 끈다. 漫畵노트를 드리대고 보니 나-는 누구라고 녀자 긔독청년회장 兪珏卿씨! 딴은 아버님 달머서 엇지로 톄소하신지 구두를 보면 귀여운 아기구두 갓다. 아가를 두 사람이나 나으셧것만은 다리고 다니는 것은 본 일이 업고 이럿케 길에서 보면 17,8의 여학생 갓해 보인다.(실례 용서합시오)＜25＞
