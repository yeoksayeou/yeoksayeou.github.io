잡지명	별건곤  
발행일	1926년 12월 01일  
기사제목	(무제-국편) (Untitled - National Edition)  
기사형태	시 (Poem)  

第一年 別乾坤 十二月號  
靑山에눈이오니 蜂마다玉이로다 저山푸르기는 봄비에잇거니와 엇지타 우리의 白髮은 검겨볼줄잇스랴.  
無名氏  
In the green mountains, snow falls, every bee is like jade; that mountain green is from the spring rain, but how can our white hair still be seen?  

백설이자사진골에 구름이 메흐레라 반가운매화는 어느곳에 픠엿는고 석양에호을로서이서 갈곳돌나하노라.  
李牧隱  
Snow covers the field and clouds obscure the view; where have the beloved plum blossoms bloomed? As the sunset is beautiful, where shall I wander?  

朔風은나무에불고 明月은눈속에찬데 一長劒여들고 戌樓에놉히안저 긴파람 큰한소래에 거칠것이업세라.  
金宗端  
The north wind blows on the trees, the bright moon is cold in the snow; drawing a long sword, I ascend the dog tower. In the strong wind with a great sound, it cannot be rough.  

춘풍도리화들아 고흔樣子자랑마라 창송륵죽을 歲寒에보렴으나 貞貞코落落한節을 고칠줄이잇스랴.  
金裕器  
Oh, spring breeze, do not boast of your flowery appearance! Look upon the pines and bamboos in this cold season; can they alter their steadfast nature?  

白頭山에 놉히안저 압뒷굽어보니 南北萬里에 녯생각새로워라 간님이 精靈게시면 눈물질가하노라.  
無名氏  
Looking from the heights of Baekdu Mountain, glancing back at the thousands of miles north and south, old thoughts are refreshed. If the beloved appears as a spirit, will tears flow?  

泰山이놉다하되 하날아래뫼이로다 오르고오르면 못오를리업건마는 사람이 제아니오르고 뫼을놉나하도다.  
楊蓬萊  
Mount Tai is high, yet is one of the mountains under heaven; climbing and climbing one cannot reach the peak, yet can it be that a person does not ascend the mountain?  

第一年 別乾坤 十二月號＜1＞  

**Summary**  

The poem reflects on nature’s beauty and the inevitable passage of time, capturing the tension between youth and aging, the cycle of seasons, and the emotional resonance tied to landscapes. It poignantly conveys a sense of nostalgia and longing, emphasizing the connection to one's homeland. For historians, the poem represents the complex emotional landscape of the time, particularly within the context of Korean colonial history, illustrating how nature and regional identity were intertwined with personal and collective experiences during a period of cultural suppression.
