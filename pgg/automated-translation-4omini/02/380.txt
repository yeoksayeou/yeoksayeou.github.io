Folk Style Cartoon, Modern Stepmother and Traditional Daughter-in-law  
1. The complaint of a traditional daughter-in-law who, having become worried, has to serve a modern mother-in-law. "Other house’s stepmothers look after the children and do the cooking in the morning and evening. As for me, my fate is to have to taste a modern mother-in-law. In the morning and evening, she just washes her face and puts on makeup like a courtesan. What on earth does an old woman have to do with makeup? She spends day and night running around selling tickets to someone else's performance, thinking that is what modernity is." <112>  

 
**Summary**  

This article presents a satirical comic illustrating the concerns of a traditional daughter-in-law burdened with the modern expectations placed upon her by her progressive mother-in-law. The clash between old customs and new practices is depicted humorously, highlighting the frustrations that arise from this generational gap. This reflection on familial roles during the colonial period reveals how modernity affected traditional Korean family dynamics and could be of interest to historians studying the social changes during Korea's transition into the modern age under colonial rule.
