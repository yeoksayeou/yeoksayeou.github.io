The Grand Overview at the Start of the New Year  

A secret investigation by disguised reporters (First public release)  

Though the world may be dark, is it possible to guarantee that those who fall under the law are exempt from sin? A grievous sin can be overlooked without recognition, while the committed sinner can roam about with impunity; such abominations will not easily vanish. Our magazine intends to act with purpose, sending out disguised reporters each month—either one male or female—for the purpose of uncovering the shadows that linger over the sun. In this first installment, the chosen reporter will disguise themselves and reveal what they bring back for our delight! Readers will surely shout with joy upon perusing the New Year’s edition.  

The heartfelt discussions of social department reporters  

What other job could be as interesting as that of a newspaper reporter? Among them, social department reporters find themselves embroiled in exceptional intrigues. They venture even into fire, tackle murder cases, confront robbers, and engage in detective activities that often surpass those of police officers and detectives. Their notebooks contain more colorful and secretive records than those of actual detectives. Listening to their heartfelt narratives is more exciting than any detective novel.  

Major events hosted in the New Year’s edition  

Exhibition of January customs from thirteen provinces  
Who could dismiss our Korea, spanning three thousand ri of beautiful mountains and rivers, as narrow and small? Perhaps looked at from afar, it may seem so, but in terms of customs, habits, language, and systems, it is indeed richly endowed and diverse. From the southern island of Jeju's Tamna to the northern peaks of Baekdusan and the confluence of the two great rivers of the Tumen and Amnok, our people flourish! View the exhibition of New Year customs splashed across our magazine pages, portraying the eight provinces in detail.  

Exhibition of modern rare professions  
“Who said ‘a throat like a bunch of grapes’?” There are many strange professions in this world that we could never even conceive of for our livelihoods. New professions are continually emerging as the world evolves. In contemporary Korea, which is brighter today, how many astonishing rare professions have emerged? Watch for this unique exhibition in the New Year’s edition! A variety of imaginative and peculiar occupations will be featured, certainly revealing more than mere novelty.  

Evaluation of signboards from stores in Kyŏngsŏng  
How much has the commercial intelligence of Koreans developed? How open have their eyes become to commerce? Don’t just follow along with duality while merely thinking superficially. Earning a living requires not to hit a dead end with antiquated commercial approaches; a sign of failure is guaranteed under such regulations in today’s economic conditions. Thus, what designs and strategies are being utilized in commerce? Hence, behold the insights acquired from surveying the marketplaces in this evaluation, which surely will not be without significance in today's economic disaster.  

Critique of notable figures and families  
From educated Koreans to practical workers, writers, religious figures, foreign students, and even denizens of the pleasure quarters, we assess, investigate, observe, satirize, and commend the prominent figures in Korea! It will feel like an extensive portrait of Korean talent is being displayed on the page. Those curious about how various figures will appear on paper should wait in anticipation for the reveal of the grandeur and detail…  

A special prize competition for a thousand  
Men, women, the elderly, and youth alike can engage in this fascinating competition with a chance to win! Gather with family around the warmth of a stove on a snowy night; together, solve the problems and bid to win prizes. This remarkable competition in the magazine world, sure to be unlike any other, awaits you! Be sure to order the New Year’s edition and await its arrival. Detailed guidelines are inscribed therein. Do not forget the key to being the first to act!  

**Summary**  

This article serves as an announcement for the upcoming New Year’s edition of "Byeolgeongon," featuring a variety of intriguing topics such as rare professions, social issues, and cultural exhibitions across Korea. The innovative concept of disguised reporters aims to reveal hidden truths in society. Historians may find the reflections on evolving identities and societal roles during the colonial period particularly interesting, shedding light on changing perceptions of modernity in Korea.

各新聞社會部記者苦心談
『新聞記者』라는 것처럼 興味잇는 職業은 업슬것이요 新聞記者중에도 『社會部』記者처럼 興味만흔일은 업슬 것이다. 하로하로의 새로운 소식을 民衆에게 전하기 위하야 그들은 불난데도간다. 싸홈터에도간다. 殺人事件도 주무른다. 强盜와도 맛난다. 그리고 로는 警察刑事以上의 探偵活動도한다. 그리고 그들의 手帖은 探偵의 手帖보다도 더요란하고 더 秘密한 記錄을 가지고 잇는 것이다. 그들의 苦心談!! 探偵小說보다도 興味잇는 그들의 苦心을 듯는 것은 實로 興味津津한일이다.
新春三大 誌上主催

三千里江山의 우리 朝鮮을 누가 좁고적다하는가? 으로보면, 그럴딘지 모르지만 風俗習慣言語制度로보면, 얄굿게도 가지 各色으로 틀니고 달다. 南으로는 濟州島의 耽羅녯나라러진 섬으로부터, 北으로는 白頭山웃둑솟고, 豆滿鴨錄兩大江이 흘너가는 사이에 사는 우리 民族! 新年號紙上에 버려지는 各地의 正月風俗展覽會를보라. 안저서 八道江山을 다 求景할수잇슬것이니………….
現代珍職業街上展覽會
『목구녁이 포도텽』이라고는, 누가한말인지……먹기위하야는 우리들의 에도 못생각할 珍奇한 職業이, 이 世上에는 만타. 世上이 밟아갈스록, 차차로, 새로운 職業은＜88＞ 늘어가는 것이다. 이사이의 朝鮮, 밝아진 今日, 궁해진 朝鮮에는, 얼마나 만흔 珍職業이 생겻슬……新年號紙上에 열니는 이 珍奇한 展覽會를 보라! 形形色色의 意想밧긔 職業이 잇슴을 보라, 이일은 다만 興味잇는 일에만 긋치지는 안을 것이다
京城各商店看板品評會
朝鮮사람의 商業智識이 얼마나 느럿나? 商業에 對한 眼目이 얼마나 열녓나? 안저서 임으로만 『죽네사네』들지말고 假頭로 假頭로 작고나가야할 것이다. 머리에 감토, 주둥이에 긴담배, 商店압헤가래춤탁탁뱃는 케케묵은 녯날 商業術로는 오늘의 經濟界ㅔ서 失敗를 보고 말 것은 定則이다. 그런면 엇더한 意匠과 定策으로 商業을 經營하고잇는가? 고로고로 市街를 도라 實地視察한 이 品評會를 보라 經濟破滅의 오늘에 한 意味업는 일은 아니리라.
名人名士女流諸家白面觀
朝鮮에서 움직이고 잇는 朝鮮사람! 敎育界ㆍ實業界ㆍ文藝界ㆍ宗敎界ㆍ外國留學生ㆍ甚至於花柳界지의 人物이란 人物을 白面百樣으로 批評, 調査, 觀察, 諷剌, 讃掦한 것. 宛然히 朝鮮士女의 一大寫眞岾을 展覽하는 感이 잇슬 것이다. 엇더한 人物의 엇더한 모양이 紙上에낫허나는가 궁금한이는 깃버히 기다리라. 엇더케 宏壯昭詳한가를……….
