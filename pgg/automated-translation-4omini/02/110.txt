기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Editorial Note)  

珍寫眞募集  
If any of our readers have rare photographs of people, landscapes, or other fascinating subjects, please send them to our editorial department at Byeolgeongon. If they are good, we will introduce them in this magazine and present a small token of appreciation. <27>  

**Summary**  

This article invites readers to submit unique photographs to be featured in the publication, emphasizing the value of diverse visual representations. This call for contributions not only reflects the cultural interests of the time but also illustrates the growing importance of photography in documenting life during the colonial period in Korea. Historians may find this indicative of a societal shift towards recognizing and valuing local culture and experiences through visual media, amidst the complexities of colonial rule.
