Notice of Apology  

The articles "문단풍문" (Literary Gossip) and "수구문" (Conservatism) have been removed due to the authorities' taboos, so they could not be published, which is deeply regrettable for our readers. We shall certainly strive to include such articles in the future, so please wait for these kinds of pieces to appear. <10>  

**Summary**  

The article expresses regret over the censorship of specific content deemed inappropriate by the authorities, highlighting the challenges faced by the publication in freely expressing ideas. This note reflects the restrictive environment in colonial Korea, where the freedom of press and speech was heavily curtailed under Japanese rule, pointing to the broader implications of censorship on cultural and intellectual discourse during this period.
