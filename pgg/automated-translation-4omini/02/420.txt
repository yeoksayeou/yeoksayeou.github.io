Announcement

It has been seven years since our company was established, and the foundation we have built today is the result of the painstaking efforts of our staff and the fervent support of comrades throughout the world.

Unfortunately, after the misfortune of our publication, "Kaebik" (開闢), being banned, our company's business operations may have given the impression to the general public that we faced a temporary setback. However, we have resolved to continue our endeavors without any changes and to expand our business operations. 

Thus, we held a staff meeting this time to further solidify the foundation of our company and to organize a Board of Directors for agile management, having selected seven directors as listed below. 

We sincerely hope that all comrades throughout the world will further support our company's endeavors so that we can achieve our desired objectives.

Lee Dunhwa, Kim Gidan, Min Yeongsun, Cha Sangchan, Bang Jeonghwan, Park Dacheong, Jeon Joonseong

December, Byeongin Year

Kaebiksa <137>  

**Summary**

This article serves as an announcement celebrating the seventh anniversary of the publication and responding to the challenges faced after the banning of their earlier publication. It expresses gratitude towards staff efforts and reader support while outlining steps to stabilize and expand the organization. Historians may find interest in the context of censorship during the colonial period in Korea, showcasing the resilience of Korean intellectuals amidst adversity as well as the significance of publication in shaping national identity.

본사가 창립된지 7개 星霜에 모든 것과 싸우고 싸워나와 오늘 이만한 토대를 쌋케된 것은 다만 본사 사원일동의 苦心慘憺한 결정물이요 만천하 동지여러분의 열열하신 응원의 덕택이라고 하지 아니할 수 업슴니다.
불행히 『開闢』 지가 발행금지의 厄을 당한 후, 본사의 사업경영이 일시 좌절된 듯한 감을 일반인사에게 주엇슬넌지 모르지만 본사로서는 하등 변함이 업시 여전히 나가며 사업을 확장하기로 결심이외다. 그리하야 금번에 사원회를 열고 한층더 본사의 기초를 공고히 하고 사무를 機敏히 하기 위하야 이사제를 조직하고 이사 7인을 좌와 여히 선정하엿사오니
만천하 동지 諸씨도 더욱 본사의 사업을 원조하야 소기의 목적을 달하도록 하시기를 간졀히 바라나이다.
