One Person, One Article: Sand Prevention Work  
						小春  
On some day, while traveling in the Northern Peaks, I came across the sand prevention work visible in various places in the deep valleys, and I asked a nearby boy about what empowered this sand prevention in the mountains.

While passing through the Sambo Valley on the Gyeongwon Line, I observed large-scale forestation that appeared as if grains were being sown in the deep mountain peaks above. I had also experienced questioning its meaning.

The seemingly meaningless sand prevention in the rugged mountains and the strange forestation in the high plateau. What is it that truly empowers this? It is the power of politics, devoid of any political power at all. Undoubtedly, the political power of today has extended into the high mountains and manifested itself in the deepest valleys, affecting all matters big and small. Unlike the past when political action was merely seen as an eruption of power, it has now become so that the effective mechanics of the economy have become the mainstay of politics.

The ignorance and indifference toward politics! I feel very regretful about this, while at the same time it reminds me of the need for modern understanding and concrete efforts regarding it. This is something I diligently ponder about. <29>  

**Summary**  

The article reflects on the role of political and economic mechanisms in societal development, using the metaphor of sand prevention work in the mountains as a means to illustrate the broader implications of both natural and political landscapes. The author emphasizes the importance of understanding modern political dynamics, especially in the context of colonial Korea, where political indifference was prevalent. This is particularly revealing when considering the socio-political struggles under colonial rule and how environmental and infrastructural projects were linked to broader political strategies.

一人一文, 砂防工事
						小春
어느 때 어느 날인가 北岳山에 울나가다가 그 幽谷의 處處에서 눈띄우는 砂防工事를 보고 가티 가든 소년을 향하야 이것이 이 산속의 砂防이 대체 무엇의 힘인가를 試問해 본 적이 잇섯다.
京元線의 三防幽峽을 통과하다가 그 머리우의 深山峻嶺에 마치 무슨 곡식을 줄줄히 심은 것과 가티 뵈여지는 대규모의 식림을 보고 가튼 의미의 무름을 시험해 본 적이 잇섯다.
깁흔 산골 속의 의미 업는 듯한 砂防, 놉흔 高原地帶의 이상한 식림. 이것이 정말 무엇이 힘이냐. 정치의 힘 틀님업는 정치의 힘이다. 두말할 것업시 오늘날의 정치의 힘이라는 것이 놉흔 산니마에도 올나가고 깁흔 골바닥에도 드러가는 것이며 기타의 안과밧 크고 적은 일체에 干與하는 것이다. 단순히 권력적 무엇의 발작이 정치라고 인정하던 녯날과도 달나서 도로혀 경제적 기구의 유효한 움즉임이 정치의 주가 되여진 오늘에 잇서는 더욱 그러함이 잇다.
정치에 대한 無理解 無關心! 이것을 퍽 유감스럽게 생각하는 동시에 이에 대한 근대적의 이해 구체적의 노력 이것이 생각키운다 열심으로 생각키운다.＜29＞
