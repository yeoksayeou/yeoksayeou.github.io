某專賣局男工의 日記  
이 一篇은 不得한 事情으로 싯지못하게되여서 섭섭 未安함을 筆者와 讀者 謝함니다.  

Summary  

This article reflects on the author's experiences as a male worker at the monopoly bureau, conveying feelings of disappointment and unease due to certain unavoidable circumstances that hindered their work. Historical context indicates that this reflection is situated within Korea under Japanese colonial rule, highlighting the struggles of workers during this period. Historians may find interest in how personal narratives illustrate the broader social and economic challenges faced by Koreans during the early 20th century.
