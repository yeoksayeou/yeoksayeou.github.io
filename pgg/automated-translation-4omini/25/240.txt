저자	-  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

名案  

A "Where is the inn you are staying at this time?"  

B "It is right on the tramline at 12 Jongno 2nd Street."  

A "Isn't it noisy with the sound of the tram?"  

B "At first, it can be quite loud, but after about 4 or 5 days, you won't notice it anymore, according to the owner’s wife. So I think I’ll just stay at Aunt’s place for about 4 or 5 days!" ＜57＞  

 
**Summary**  

This dialogue reflects on the experience of staying in an inn and the initial discomfort caused by the noise of the tramway. Interestingly, it highlights personal adaptations to urban living in a rapidly modernizing Seoul during the colonial period. Historians might find value in this everyday exchange as it illustrates the shifts in social behaviors and the cultural impacts of modern infrastructure within the context of colonial Korea.
