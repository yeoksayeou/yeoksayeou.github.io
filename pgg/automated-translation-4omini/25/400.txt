What Caused Such a Chill?  
Lim In  

1. Plan  

The warm breeze of spring melted people's hearts like the breath of a seductive woman, causing an unknown longing to fill the air of April.  

A man, once he reaches fifty, tends to distance himself from sexual life.  

As S approached fifty, aiming for forty, his spring was filled with a fervent anticipation, especially considering his sexual desires were both fervent and serious.  

April 1. ... On this day, S was to take his daughter to transfer her to an XX kindergarten in the city.  

His daughter, until now, had been attending another kindergarten. S used the distance of that school as an excuse to deal with his husband. ... He was a powerful man in certain ways. It was thus that his daughter was to transfer to XX kindergarten.  

However, a significant plan was hidden in S's mind.  

S wasted two hours preparing her makeup. She often felt a tinge of annoyance at the reflection of her face in the mirror, which seemed devoid of charm. Nevertheless, she exerted all her strength and means to enhance her appearance.  

In the spring breeze, she was vexed by her unsatisfying beauty and threw aside a hairpin adorned with jade and a brooch studded with gems.  

She finely adorned her fingers with jewelry and platinum.  

What should she wear? Should she put on this outfit or that one?  

Which parasol should she take with her?  

After preparing, adjusting, and checking her appearance numerous times, she finally hailed a taxi at noon, taking her daughter with her, who had also undergone considerable effort to dress up. Before going to XX kindergarten, S sought out H.  

H ... Indeed, he represented the whole of S's existence, the object of her eager longing that filled her spring nights with dreams.  

S had encountered this H once a year ago at an unknown place.  

The handsome appearance of H had left a considerable impression in S's heart.  

Since then, S had been attempting in every possible way to have some sort of interaction with H.  

For a year, she quietly and subtly created chances, but such lukewarm measures could not achieve her desires.  

S adopted a more aggressive approach.  

Ultimately, her daughter was transferred to XX kindergarten in hopes of making contact with H.  

2. Teacher H  

H, a seemingly solemn husband, greeted S politely.  

In her heart, S might have wished to embrace the small, handsome H at once, but he was not the type of person to reveal any such feelings in that moment.  

S acted as merely a polite housewife or mother in front of H.  

Taking this circumstance into account, S's initial plan of seduction succeeded.  

S visited XX kindergarten several times a month and once a month invited H to her home under the pretext of hosting the teacher who received her daughter.  

A year passed. After a year, S tried glancing suggestively at H.  

However, whether H, who had a loving wife, was aware of S's hints or not remained unknown; he showed no signs of moving.  

When S learned that H would be moving away, she couldn't help but smile to herself, immediately inviting H to move into the lower portion of her house, assuring him not to worry.  

Despite being hesitant to accept, H, hearing about the move, did so with his wife B, arriving on the same day.  

S was satisfied with the prospect that living in the same house would facilitate their encounters, but, contrary to her expectations, H and B went straight back to school and paired up in the evening as well, going out as a couple.  

Nonetheless, eventually, the opportunity presented itself.  

3. An Unexpected Opportunity  

One day, B had some prior engagements and was out, leaving H in his room feeling a bit weak.  

An once-in-a-lifetime opportunity suddenly arose.  

S went to visit H in the guise of caring for him.  

That night, the woman assaulted the man (乙丑甲子).  

In terms of physiological science, although it is said that women cannot directly assault men, it can happen if that man does not truly feel committed to a woman.  

Once a man tastes the joy, he cannot let go of it.  

After that night, H frequently visited S.  

In the bustling city of Kyungsung, there were few places where S and H did not indulge in their affairs.  

During their time together, H also began to genuinely become attached to S.  

As this unfolded, a third person became involved in the situation—B.  

Although H's feelings leaned towards S, he couldn't bring himself to betray his good wife, B.  

S's ambition, however, was not easily contented. She had laid a trap ahead of time. This involved sending letters to B signaling that "H is spending time with another woman."  

On the side, she pressed H to divorce B and officially marry her.  

At that time, H was significantly troubled, uncertain of how to resolve the issue.  

Seeing H's hesitation, S devised another plan.  

One night, after S and H returned from a night out, H discovered B in his room nestled with a man named X.  

H's fury was intense. Although B explained that her actions were unintentional and forced, this had no effect. S had orchestrated the entire situation, though H was blind to this at the time.  

Ultimately, H found a plausible excuse to divorce B.  

B, drenched in tears, had no option but to let go.  

However, H was not satisfied even with that and spread rumors throughout the school, leading to B facing dismissal.  

In the face of such adversity, B found herself helpless, despite her nonintentional actions, and she formed a relationship with the man X. (Even now, they live harmoniously together.)  

4. A Dream of Rose-colored Mornings  

Between S and H, after dealing with B, a blissful paradise without concerns came to reality.  

No one could halt the free-spiritedness of their relationship.  

Although S’s husband Y sensed something was amiss, he had been keeping a secret about a life he had caused to be lost in his daughter's womb years ago, and since S wielded absolute power through her wealth, he dared not oppose her and turned a blind eye.  

After the storm passed, loneliness emerged, and when sensuality waned, boredom followed. Especially in the case of the lustful seductress.  

After a year of indulging in carnal pleasures, S found herself losing interest in H.  

Thinking of a way to shake him off, she sent H to a hot spring in Montan-yu (溫陽溫泉), saying she would join him in a few days.  

H, unsuspecting, complied and went down to the hot spring. Yet, after waiting two days, three days, four days, five days, S never came.  

Anxious, he sent a telegram but received no reply.  

After roughly ten days, a letter arrived like a bolt from the blue.  

"I can no longer spend my life with you, no matter how much I think about it. Most importantly, I am unable to bear children due to a surgery I underwent years ago. Marrying someone like me would only bring you great misfortune. Furthermore, I have difficulties separating from my current husband, Y."  

This summarized S's letter.  

H, furious, rushed back to Seoul.  

Cold winds blew from S’s nose. Initially, he begged at her hem, but S was not one to be trifled with. There was no chance of rekindling their connection.  

Seeing S grow distant, H began reflecting on his past.  

He realized he had been languishing under S's yoke, playing the role of her puppet.  

He attempted to threaten her and weepingly plead, but he failed to achieve his desires and ended up divorcing B.  

No matter how he apologized to B, she had already moved on to another man.  

Seeing S's situation brought tears to B's eyes, yet she could not save H.  

Now, H found himself with nowhere to go in this world. Like the old saying goes, he had lost everything.  

What little conscience he had left tormented him without mercy.  

Holding a poison vial, H committed to return to S.  

S, though outwardly advising him to let go and rebuild his life, inwardly wished he would not return to this world.  

H took the poison and went to his father's house, saying, "Father, I am dying right now."  

The old man, stern in his principles, replied, "You fool, I have never sired such an ugliness."  

Holding a bundle of love letters received from S, H's life faded away alone on a hospital bed.  

**Summary**  

This article explores the tumultuous romantic entanglements of S, a woman, who uses her daughter to orchestrate an affair with a schoolteacher, H, leading to dramatic consequences, including deception, divorce, and eventual despair. The narrative serves as a reflection on the lengths individuals will go for love and desire, alongside the societal pressures faced by women in early 20th-century Korea. Historians might find it revealing how themes of gender, power, and societal expectations interplay within the context of Korean colonial society, highlighting the struggles and aspirations of women at the time.

S의 딸이라는 애는 그 동안까지는 달은 유치원에 다니고 잇섯다. 그러한 것을 S는 그 학교가 집에서 멀다는 것을 口實삼어 그 남편을 달내여 ... 그는 某방면에 잇서서 有力家이다. XX유치원에 뎐학을 식히게 된 것이엇섯다.
그러나 S의 腦中에는 달은 깁흔 게획이 숨어 잇섯다.
S는 두시간을 허비하여 화장을 하엿다. 어느 구석인지 귀인ㅅ성이 업는 무엇이 숨어 잇는 듯한 자긔의 얼골을 거울에 빗치어 보며 짜증을 내인 것도 한두번이 아니엇스나 그러타고 어엽부지 못한 얼골이 갑작이 어엽버질 배도 업고 그는 그저 잇는 힘과 수단을 다 부려서 화장을 하엿다.
봄ㅅ긔운에 알마즌 비취비녀와 보석이 백힌 귀위비개가 눈에 선뜻 띄우도록 만족지 못한 것을 恨하여 집어꼬잣다.
손ㅅ가락도 보석과 백금으로 장식을 주밀하게 갓추엇다.
옷은 어느 것을 입을가? 이 놈을 입어야 그가 선뜻 치어다 불가 저 빗을 입어야 할가?
양산은 어느 것을 가지고 갈까?

2. H先生님
낫몰으는 점잔한 부인을 마진 H는 공순히 인사을 하엿다.
S는 속마음으로는 대번에 조고만한 H를 덥숙 끄러 안고도 십헛스나 그러타고 그는 조곰치라도 그러한 긔색을 그 자리에서 내어보일 적은 인물은 아니엇섯다.
그는 단지 점잔한 집 부인으로써 또는 딸의 어머니로써 H에게 대하엿다.

3. 乙丑甲子
어느날인가 B는 무슨 모힘이 잇서서 나아가고 업고 H가 가벼운 감긔로 혼자 방에 누어 잇섯다. 千載一遇의 기회는 오고 말엇다.

4. 槿花一朝의 꿈
B를 처치한 S와 H사이에는 아모 거릿김 업는 지상천국이 現實되엿다. 아모도 그들의 自由謠奔한 생활을 막을 사람이 업섯다.＜103＞

이것이 S에게서 온 편지의 大略이엇섯다. 눈이 뒤집힌 H는 바로 서울로 뛰여 올나왓다.
S의 코ㅅ등에서는 찬바람이 확확 돌앗다. 처음에는 치마ㅅ자락에 매어달녀 애걸을 하엿다. 그러나 S가 누구라고. ... ... 다시 맛나볼 도리조차 업섯다.
S의 冷冷하여진 것을 본 H는 차차 자기의 과거를 반성하여 보앗다.
순전히 S에게 롱락을 바드며 그의 인형 노릇을 하여 온 것을 깨달엇다.
또 그는 H에게 위협도 하고 눈물로 애원도 하다가 뜻을 이루지 못하고 B를 차젓다.
B에게 아모리 사죄를 하나 B는 이미 남이 사람이엇섯다. S의 情景을 보고 B도 눈물을 흘니엇스나 H를 구하는 수는 업섯다.
인제야 H는 넓은 이 땅우에 갈 곳이 업시 되엿다. 속담과 가티 게도 구럭도 다 놋치엇다. 그나마 양심은 그를 사정업시 苛責을 하엿다.
毒藥를 손에 들고 H는 마즈막 S를 차저갓다.
S는 표면으로는 그러지 말고 다시 마음을 잡어 압날을 개척하락 권고는 하나 심중으로는 H가 이 세상 사람이 아니기를 바라고 잇섯다.
H는 S의 압헤서 독약을 마시고 그의 부친에게로 달녀가서
「아버지 저는 지금 죽음니다.」하엿다.
녯 노인으로 대의를 직히는 H의 아버지는 一言之下에,
「이놈 나는 너가티 못생긴 자식을 둔 적이 업다.」고 몰아 내여 바렷다. S에게서 바든 한뭉치 艶書를 손에 쥔채 H의 목숨은 병원 침대우에서 외로히 사라지고 말엇다.＜104＞
