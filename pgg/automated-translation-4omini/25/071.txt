The articles on the politics of America, Russia, and China in 1930 could not be published due to unavoidable circumstances. I apologize for the inconvenience to the authors and readers.

**Summary**

This article serves as an apology for the absence of articles discussing the political landscapes of the United States, Russia, and China in 1930, attributing this to unavoidable circumstances. It reflects the challenges faced in disseminating information during colonial times and hints at the political dynamics of the era that were considered significant enough to warrant discussion. Historians might find the omission of these analyses particularly interesting as it showcases the period's journalistic struggles, especially under colonial governance.
