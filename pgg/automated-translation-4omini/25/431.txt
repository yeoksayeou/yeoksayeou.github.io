**Summary**  

This article serves as an acknowledgment regarding the absence of a poem titled "동무여락심마라" by Park Ro-eul in this particular issue of the magazine due to unforeseen circumstances. This reflects the challenges faced by writers and editors during the colonial era in Korea, where external and institutional pressures could impact the publication of literary works. Historians might find it interesting as it hints at the constraints on creative expression during a period of cultural suppression under Japanese rule.
