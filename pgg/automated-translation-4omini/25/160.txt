컥텔의 由來  

Once upon a time in some country of Europe, whether it was a king or a farmer, if it was a king, he would be more significant than a queen, and if it were a farmer, more valuable than a field, there was a proud rooster (雄鷄) that one day suddenly became furious. 

So, the owner of the rooster, along with the people living nearby, tried to catch it; their eyes were wide with eagerness to seize it. Despite their best efforts, this rooster, which might have been an athlete in its past life, was so fast that no one could catch up with it. Among the crowd, one person thought to himself, "If I could attract it with just one more bit of feed, it would surely not escape," and leapt forward. 

At that moment, right at the edge of a precipice that neither man nor rooster could avoid, he grabbed the rooster's proud tail. 

As for the rooster's owner's friends, they gathered together to celebrate with a toast. They brought all kinds of liquor they could find and filled their cups with combinations of these drinks, carelessly mixing them. When they tasted it, they could hardly describe the flavor, so they whimsically named that drink "cocktail" (from cock-수ㅅ닭 and tail-꼬리).  

**Summary**  

This article explores the whimsical origin story of the cocktail, tying it to an ancient European tale featuring a proud rooster. The narrative is a blend of humor and folklore, illustrating how something as modern as the cocktail can have quirky and imaginative backstories. Historians might find it interesting to see how European cultural elements were transposed into the Korean context during the colonial period, showcasing the merging of global influences with local storytelling traditions.

컥텔의 由來
녯날녯적 구라파의 어느 나라엿든지 그러고 임금이엿든지 농부이엿든지 임금이라면 여왕보다도 농부이면 田庄보다도 더 중하고 귀하게 녀기엿든 숫닭(雄鷄)이 어느날 별안간 성이 나서 다라낫드랍니다.
그래 그 주인은 물논 그 근방에 잇든 사람까지도 눈이 시ㅅ벍어져 잡으랴고 쪼차 단엿섯스나 이 닭이 전생에 말손 선수이엿섯는지 여간 달음하는 사람은 잡기는 커녕 따라가지도 못하엿드랍니다. 그 중에 한사람이 쪼차가서 한 거름만 더 내여드듸면 닭도 사람도 차저보지 못할 낭떠러지에 와서 소위 危機一髮의 순간에 그 닭의 자랑ㅅ거리인 꼬리를 붓잡앗드랍니다.
그래 그 주인의 깁벗든 말이야 우리는 상상도 못할 깃븜이엿서 그는 여러사람을 모아놋코 祝盃를 난호든 중 술이라는 술은 가지각색으로 잇는대로 다 내여 놋코 술잔에 이 술 저술이 함부루 석기인 것을 무심코 마시고 본즉 그 맛이라니 무어라 할 수 업서 그래 그 술을 컥(cock-수ㅅ닭) 텔(tail-꼬리)이라고 일흠 지엇담니다.＜36＞
