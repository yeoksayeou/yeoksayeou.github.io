기사형태	잡저 (miscellaneous writing)  

A king named Philip of Macedonia once declared to the two most despised criminals, "One of you must flee with all your might beyond the borders, and the other must also use all his strength to chase and capture the fleeing one."

Thales, one of the Seven Sages of Greece, was without a wife well into his years. His mother, feeling sorry for him, urged him to marry. Each time, he would refuse, saying, "I am still young." When he reached middle age, his mother again recommended marriage, but he said, "I have already passed that time."

The philosopher Aristotle had a very strong dislike for his own son. His wife, feeling uneasy, rebuked him, "Why do you hate a child born from your own body?" To which he spat on the ground and replied, "This too is something that came from my body, but it serves me no purpose."

A philosopher named Seneca called over one of his disciples who exhibited a very poor appearance and scolded him. The disciple, showing no shame, defended himself by saying, "It’s no concern if there’s money to spend on something like that." The philosopher then retorted, "If so, then there's plenty of beef! Would that mean that just because there’s a lot of it, it tastes any good?"

Hwang Hee, Hwang Jeongseung was walking down the road in spring when he asked a farmer, "Good sir, which cow is more fruitful, the black one or the yellow one?" The farmer didn’t respond immediately and continued plowing and managing his cows. After some time, he ran over to Hwang Hee and whispered in his ear, "Honestly, the yellow cow is indeed the better one." Hwang Hee asked him, "Why did you not immediately respond, and instead come all the way over to whisper in my ear?" The farmer replied, "While the yellow cow is indeed praised, the black one is of no use in terms of yield." This made Hwang Hee very enlightened.

A tavern keeper came out and was chasing a pig while scolding Jeong Sudong, saying, "Oh my, you’re so useless that you just let this pig eat your food!" Jeong Sudong naturally replied, "I thought those pigs eat by paying cash." The tavern keeper laughed so much that she ended up giving him a few cups on credit.  

**Summary**  

This article consists of a series of humorous anecdotes featuring various historical and philosophical figures, highlighting their quirks and idiosyncrasies. The interactions typically explore themes of value, perception, and human relationships, showing the absurdities and wisdom contained within everyday exchanges. For historians studying Korea during the colonial period, this article's playful depiction of philosophical thought illuminates the complex dimensions of societal norms and individual behavior within Korean culture, reflecting on how such narratives may have been a response to the tensions of the time.

驚句
마게트니아의 왕 후립뜨가 가장 미운 악한 두놈에게 이러케 선언하얏다. 『한 사람은 전속력을 다하야 국경외로 도망을 하고 한 사람은 그 전속력을 다하야 그 도망하는 놈을 따라가 붓잡으라』고.

希臘 七賢의 일인 타레쓰는 과년하도록 안해가 업섯다. 그 어머니가 민망하야 안해 엇기를 권하얏다. 그럴때마다 『아직도 나희가 젊슴니다』하고 거절하여왓다. 나희 중년쯤 되야 그 어머니는 또 장가들기를 권하얏더니 그때는 그만 나희가 지내첫슴니다』고.

철학자 아리스지뜨쓰는 자기의 아들을 몹시도 미워하얏다. 그 안해가 너무 불안하야 『자기의 몸에서 갈러나온 자식을 웨 그리 미워하느냐』고 비난을 한즉 그는 지면에 침을 탁 배트며 「이것도 내 몸에서 갈러 나간 것이지만 나에게 소용이 업다」고.

철학자 「스에노」가 제자 중에 모양 몹시 내고다니는 놈을 불러 꾸지젓더니 제자는 부끄럽지도 안케 『그만한 돈이 잇서서 그만콤 쓰는 것이야 상관 업지요』하고 항변을 하는지라 『그러면 소곰은 얼마던지 잇다』고 요리인이 고기에 소곰을 막 집어너어도 맛이 조탄 말인가?』

黃喜 黃政丞이 봄철에 길을 가다가 엇던 밧가는 농부에게 「여보 농부님 검정소가 부릴성이 좃소? 누런 소가 부릴성이 좃소?」하고 무럿더니 농부는 듯고도 대답을 안하고 갈든 밧이랑을 다 갈고 소를 세우고 나서야 멀직이 뛰여나와 黃喜의 귀에 다 입을 대고 가만히 『사실로 부릴성이 좃키는 누런 소가 조와요』하는지라. 그래 『그러면 뭇든 즉시로 곳 대답을 할게지 무어 굉장한 말이 되어서 이러케 멀리 따라나와 귓 속말을 하느냐』하닛가 그 농부 대답하되 『소들 듯는데 이약이하면 칭찬듯는 누런소는 조와 하지만 검정소는 실여할것 안이요』 黃喜선생 그만 大覺.

酒婆가 나오더니만 「두-두」하고 도야지를 쫓츠며 鄭壽銅을 보고 「여보 이량반 거긔안젓스면서 도야지가 술밥 먹는 것을 그대로 두엇소 에이 에이」하고 책망을 하니까 壽銅은 천연스럽게 「안이 나는 그 도야지들은 맛돈(現金)을 내고 먹는줄 알엇지」 酒婆가 어이가 업서 깔깔 웃으며 외상 이나마 서너사발 주드란다.<13>
