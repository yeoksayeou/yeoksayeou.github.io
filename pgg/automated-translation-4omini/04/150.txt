기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Advertisement and Editorial Note)  

학생漫畵募集  
How strange the world has become! As the most hopeful and most playful students, there are many who only engage in trivial antics. We invite university students, vocational students, middle school students, both male and female, to submit cartoons that satirize such trifling behaviors. If your work is good, we will select it for publication and offer a small token of appreciation. <47>  

**Summary**  

This article is a call for student-created cartoons that humorously criticize the foolish behavior seen among students in various educational contexts. It reflects a contemporary cultural trend where satire and humor serve as tools for social commentary. Historians may find the emphasis on youth and the hope for societal improvement particularly interesting, as such sentiments often arose within the youth movements of the colonial period in Korea.
