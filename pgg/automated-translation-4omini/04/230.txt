夫婦맛  

A couple who usually gets along well had a quarrel over a trivial matter. After a heated exchange, the wife, feeling aggrieved, exclaimed, "Well, let's get a divorce. I will go back to my countryside home, and you can live with the woman you like." The husband felt a tightness in his chest but, putting on a brave face, took out his wallet and said, "Ah, that’s a good idea. Let’s get divorced. Here’s five won, enough to get you back to your home." After tossing her five one-won bills, the wife hesitated for a moment, then picked up the money, saying, "Dear, if you’re going to give me money, you should give me ten won. What am I supposed to use to get back when five won is only enough for my fare?"

  

Modern  

This modern couple, who were not particularly close, ended up arguing over something insignificant and decided, "Since the things you bought belong to you, and the things I bought belong to me, let’s get a divorce." The husband was packing his desk, drawers, and travel bag, while the wife was hastily gathering bedding and a sewing machine. In the end, she lunged at him, saying, "Your things are mine too, so I’m taking them." <83>  

  

**Summary**  

The article humorously depicts a quarrel between a couple over trivial domestic issues, leading to comical attempts at divorce negotiations. The interaction highlights the absurdity of their situation, reflecting the struggles of couples during the 1920s amid societal changes. Particularly interesting for historians is the portrayal of evolving marital dynamics during the colonial period in Korea, showcasing contrasting views between traditional and modern relationship expectations.

구식

정답게 지내는 부부가 조고마한 일로 싸홈을 하엿것다. 주거니 밧거니 말다툼을 한참 하다가 마누라가 획 도라 안즈면서 『그래요. 시원하게 리혼을 해 주어요. 나는 곳 시골집으로 아조 가 버릴터이니 마음에 맛는 년을 어더 살어요』 남편이 가슴이 딱금하엿스나 싀침이를 딱 떼고 돈지갑을 끄내들고
『아이그 잘 생각햇소. 우리 아조 헤여집시다. 자아 이것 오원이니 본집까지 긔차삭은 넉넉하니 어서 가지고 가시요』하고 일원짜리 다섯장을 내여 던지닛가 마누라 한참 머뭇거리다가 돈을 집더니
『여보 돈을 주면 십원을 주어야지 오원만 주면 갓다올때에는 무얼로 타고 오란말요』

신식

이 조흔 신식내외가 대단치 안흔 일로 말다툼을 하고나서 야속하게 알고 네가 산 물건은 네가 가저가고 내가 산 세간은 내가 가지고 아조 헤여지자』하엿것다.
남편은 책상 문갑 려행가방을 묵고 잇는데 부인은 이부자리 재봉틀을 주섬주섬 싸더니 맨 나종에 남편을 싸러 덤비면서 하는 말
『당신도 내 세간이닛가 내가 가지고 갈테요』<83>
