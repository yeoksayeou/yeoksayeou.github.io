Submission Welcome

Regional characteristics! This will be a taste of the local readers’ pages. Write and submit freely as you please. In all corners of the thirteen provinces, there are various strange customs, odd habits, and bizarre historical legends to be found. Characters are interesting. Landscapes are interesting. Local products are interesting. Write about them! Anyone can write and send in whatever pertains to their locality. However, a few requests and restrictions must be stated.

1. Do not write in a clichéd introduction; instead, make it stylish and interesting to read.

2. The manuscript should be approximately 60 lines with 15 characters per line.

3. Write with clear breaks between phrases.

4. It would be even better to attach photos.

Editing and selection will be left to this office, but contributors whose works are published will receive a token of appreciation.<103>  

**Summary**  

The article invites submissions from local readers about unique customs, habits, and legends from their respective regions, emphasizing that writing should be engaging and well-structured. It encourages contributing not only narratives but also supporting photographs. This piece reflects the cultural push in colonial Korea to generate a sense of community and pride in local identities at a time when many aspects of daily life were under foreign influence. Historians may find it of interest as it highlights local engagement with identity formation during Japanese colonial rule.

投稿歡迎
지방색! 이것은 지방독자의 페지로 맛겨둔다. 멋대로 잇는대로 투고하라. 십삼도 방방곡곡에는 가지각색의 이상한 풍속, 야릇한 습관, 해괴한 역사전설이 만흘 것이다. 인물도 좃타. 산수풍경도 좃타. 풍토산물도 좃타. 그것을 쓰란 말이다. 누구나 자기 지방의 것을 얼마던지 써 보내라. 그러나 요만한 所請과 제한은 드러 주어야 한다.
일. 진부한 소개문가티 쓰지 말고 좀 멋잇시 흥미잇게 읽을 수 잇도록 쓸 일.
일. 원고지 일행십오자 육십행 내외로 쓸 일.
일. 구절을 똑똑 떼여서 쓸 일.
일. 사진가튼 것을 첨부하여 보내는 것은 더욱 조흔 일.
첨삭과 취사는 본사에 일임하되 게재되는 분에 대하야는 薄謝를 드리기로 함니다.<103>
