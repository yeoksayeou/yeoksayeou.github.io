Submission Welcome

Local Color! This is to be prepared with the waste of local readers. Submit freely as you please. There will undoubtedly be various peculiar customs, strange habits, and mysterious historical legends in the various districts of the Thirteen Provinces. People, landscapes, and local products should all be included too. Write about whatever related to your local area. However, there are some requests and limitations that should be observed.

1. Do not write boring introductory descriptions; write it in a lively and interesting way so that it can be read with enjoyment.  

2. Write on manuscript paper with fifteen characters per line and within around sixty lines.  

3. Make sure to use line breaks appropriately.  

4. It is even better to attach photographs or similar materials.  

Editing and selection will be entrusted to our company, but for the parts that are published, a small remuneration will be offered. <72>  

**Summary**  

This article invites submissions from readers, particularly focusing on local customs, legends, and landscapes across the Thirteen Provinces of Korea. The guidelines provided encourage creativity and personal engagement with local culture. Importantly, this reflects the growing interest in regional identity among Koreans during the colonial period, showcasing the struggle to capture and preserve local traditions amid the overarching influence of Japanese colonial rule. Historians may find it revealing how even minor publications sought to empower local voices and histories during a time of cultural erasure.
