**Summary**  

This article lists the names and contributions of new members who joined the Opening Society Expansion Committee, showcasing the growing support for the organization, primarily from Mokpo and Jeju. This reflects the networking efforts among Koreans during the colonial period and highlights differing levels of social and economic capital in various regions, which can be crucial for understanding social dynamics under colonial rule.
