Big Changes in Life Before and After Marriage  
Mrs. Li So-san  

I received a request from the Kaehwa Society to write about my feelings before and after marriage. Given the nature of the topic, if anyone reflects on it, many memories will undoubtedly come to mind. It would truly not be uninteresting to revisit and depict the time before marriage and to reflect on my experiences after marriage.  

However, now that I pick up the pen to write again, the feelings of the past and present intertwine and my mind becomes confused. When I glance at my husband, he is fully immersed in reading without a care.  

For anyone married, this experience is quite common, but for me, the most significant change was my outlook on life before and after marriage. To put it simply, before marriage, I always felt emptiness and silence when I interacted with people and the world, which led to a tendency to nihilize everything. However, after the turning point of marriage and starting to live as a human being, my observations of the human world changed entirely.  

That is, all the tears of disappointment about life! The lamentations! The hatred! All the feelings of sorrow disappeared, and instead, I discovered that life is most realistic and declared my path as a modern person. I became actively engaged in the struggle for the advancement of my vocation, filled with new ideals and hopes, thereby naturally dispelling fanciful daydreams.  

Secondly, it is also a fact that I transitioned from instability to stability. In reality, the future was uncertain, but based on my experiences up to now, I had never experienced such chaotic thoughts as before marriage. There were times when I was so absorbed in worry that I couldn't sleep and spent entire nights awake. Looking back on it now, it seems like childishly foolish daydreaming, yet at that time, I could not help but think so. However, after marriage, my mind became very simple and orderly. The reason was that the entire fate became scientifically understandable.  

Looking back, we established a simple home on a certain autumn day last year as the sun set. Of course, initially, we joined hands empty-handed, owning nothing. Thus, until we began this simple home life, both of us experienced considerable fear. However, fortunately, through our mutual understanding and loving life, we could eliminate all the fear and discomfort.  

In short, only when the man bestows complete love upon a woman and the woman bestows complete love upon a man can true human life be appreciated, and in doing so, we can overcome this world that is like cold, gray withered wood. Truly, love is the essential condition of our lives. I believe that the genuine union of love between men and women who understand each other is the only factor for the enhancement of personality, the advancement of society, and the increase of happiness. Without this genuine loving life based on mutual understanding, true effort cannot arise, and where there is no true effort, there cannot be true life. Thus, without hesitation, I urge those in the stage of marriage to marry (of course, based on affection).  

History no longer allows a man to be the only actor; therefore, it also prohibits the one-sided development of women. The human society cannot exist separately. True men and women must join together as individuals to establish ideals and cooperate to realize them. Only then can happiness come to human life and advance human culture.  

Having written to this point, I seem like a proponent of marriage, a monopolizer of happiness. However, this may resonate with the readers' imaginations, and that’s enough.  

  

**Summary**  

This article reflects on the transformative experiences of a woman before and after marriage, detailing how marriage shifted her perspective from nihilism to a proactive engagement in life. It emphasizes the importance of love between partners as the foundation for personal and societal development. The discussion intertwines personal feelings with broader societal observations, indicating a shift in women's roles during the colonial period in Korea. Historians may find this work noteworthy as it provides insight into gender dynamics and the evolving nature of marriage during a time of significant social change in Korea.

결혼 전과 결혼 후의 감상! 쓰라는 부탁을 몃츨전 開闢社로부터 바덧다. 문제가 문제이라 누구나 이에 대한 회상을 한다면 적지 안은 추억이 환기될 것이다. 참으로 지금에 다시 昔日에 歸하야 결혼 전 당시를 그려보고 결혼 후 지내온 일을 다시 회상하야 보는 것도 그다지 흥미업는 일은 안일 것이다.
그러나 이제 『펜』을 들고 새삼스럽게 쓰자 하니 今昔의 감회가 교체하야 머리가 공연히 산란하여진다. 그리하야 겻방에 안즌 남편을 바라보니 그는 오즉 정신업시 독서만 하고 잇슬 뿐이다.
기혼자면 누구나에게든지-잇슬 체험이지마는 나는 무엇보담 결혼 전과 결혼 후의 인생관이 달라진 것이 제일 큰 사실이엿다. 간단이 말하자면 결혼 전 나는 온 사람과 세상을 대할 때에 늘-공허와 적막을 늣기게 되야 모든 것을 허무시하는 경향이 업지 안이 하얏다. 그러나 결혼을 일전기로 하야 인간으로서의 실제생활을 하게 된 후부터 인간세상에 대한<88> 관찰이 일변하고 말엇다.
즉 종래의 인생에 대한 실망의 눈물! 탄식! 증오 등 모든 비애적 감상적 감회는 어대로 사러저 버리고 그 대신 인생은 가장 현실적임을 발견하는 동시 현대인으로서의 진로를 천명하고 그의 속진을 위하야 적극적으로 투쟁함으로써 사적사명을 만일이라고 달하여 보려는 새로운 이상과 희망을 갓게 되엿다. 따라서 실제를 떠난 공상망상은 자연이 염두에서 사라지게 되엿다.
둘재는 불안정으로부터 안정을 엇게 된 것이 또한 사실이다. 실로 후래사는 알 수 업스나 지금까지의 경험으로는 결혼 전과 가티 생각이 조잡혼란한 적은 업섯다. 어떤 때는 공연이 번민에 번민을 거듭하야 한잠도 이루지 못하고 通夜한 적이 업지 안이 하엿다. 그를-지금와서 회상하면 지극히 어리석은 한 몽상에 불과하지마는 그때에는 생각지 말재야 안할 수 업섯다. 그러나 결혼 후 나는 매우 머리가 단순하고 정일하게 되엿다. 그 리유는 모든 운명이 아는 까지는 과학적으로 알어지는데 까닭이엿다.
회고하면 우리는 일작년 만추 어느날 석양에 이 족으만 세집에 가정이란 간단한 휴식소를 設하게 되엿다. 물론 최초에는 서로 빈 손이 빈 손을 제휴한 것 외에는 아모것도 소유한 것이 업섯다. 그럼으로 이 족으만 가정이나마 시작하기 전까지 두 사람은 적지안은 공포를 늣기게 되엿다. 그러나 다행이 두 사람의 이해깁흔 愛的 생활로 그 모든 공포와 불편을 배제할 수 잇섯다.
