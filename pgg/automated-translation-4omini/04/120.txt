기사제목	한손웃길 (One-Handed Smile)  
기사형태	잡저 (Essay)  

The husband, upon hearing rumors that he went to a theater with another woman, is confronted by his wife. 

Wife: "Dear, why did you go with my friend to the theater?"  

Husband: "I know you went to the theater the other day with my friend, so what's the fuss?"  

Wife: "Even so, I bought my ticket from that person. Did you pay for that woman's ticket?" <44>

  
**Summary**  

This article presents a satirical dialogue between a husband and wife concerning suspicions of infidelity related to attending a theater. The exchange highlights themes of jealousy and misunderstandings in relationships during the colonial period. Historians might find interest in how public and private life were intertwined, reflecting societal norms and marital dynamics amid colonial pressures.
