Prize Recruitment  
Creative Folk Songs  
We are recruiting new creative folk songs from modern people.  
○ Up to five verses per person.  
○ A small prize will be given for published works.  
○ There is no deadline.  
Selection Committee: Li Hun-gak

Traditional Folk Songs  
Every region has its unique traditional folk songs. We are gathering traditional folk songs that the youth of Korea have been singing for long.  
(A small prize will be given.)  

Creative Folk Songs  
We are collecting creative folk songs emerging from the daily lives of modern Koreans. (Revisions of traditional forms are also allowed.)  
○ The number of lines is free.  
○ A small prize will be awarded for the selected works.  
Selection Committee: Ba-in Kim Dong-hwan <79>  

Summary  

This article announces a competition for the submission of traditional and creative folk songs, inviting contemporary Koreans to contribute to the cultural heritage. The flexibility in the number of verses and the lack of a deadline suggest an effort to encourage wide participation. This illustrates a significant aspect of cultural expression during a time of colonial oppression, highlighting the revival and modernization of Korean cultural forms. Historians may find the initiative reflective of a broader movement to preserve and adapt Korean identity amidst Japanese rule.
