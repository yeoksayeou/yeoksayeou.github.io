大建物 구경(其4), 徽文高等普通學校 (Visiting Grand Buildings, No. 4: Hwimun Higher Common School)
소식 (News)

Visiting Grand Buildings

This photograph is of Hwimun High School. The site of the school is the old Kwansangam (觀象監) site, and its history is quite significant. The location is also good, and the school’s athletic field and building are the best among Korean-managed schools.

Land Area: 5741 pyong
Main Building: Brick, 3 stories, total 360 pyong
West Building: Brick, 2 stories, total 262 pyong
Lecture Hall and Special Classrooms: Brick, 2 stories, total 210 pyong
Ancillary Buildings: Wooden, single story, 78 pyong
Athletic Field: 4000 pyong
Construction Date: The first school building was constructed in August of Kwangmu (光武) 10th year. After that, it was expanded three times in July of Taisho (大正) 2nd year, April of the 11th year, and June of Showa (昭和) 2nd year. The construction cost was approximately 100,000 won.
