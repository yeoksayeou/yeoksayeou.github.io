Number of Marriages and Divorces in Korea Last Year

190,090 marriages and 9,077 divorces

According to the number of marriages and divorces, spousal relationships, and regional conditions in 1930, the total number of marriages performed last year was 190,090 (197,563 Koreans, 1,683 Japanese, 135 foreigners, averaging 546 per day), an increase of 5,016 compared to the previous year. The number of divorces was 9,077, averaging 25 per day. The regions with the highest number of marriages are Gangwon, South Pyeongan, North Pyeongan, and South Hamgyong, while the region with the lowest rate is South Gyeongsang, as detailed below.

Number of People Eating Bean Rice in Korea, as of the End of February This Year

As of the end of February this year, the number of inmates in each prison (형무소) and its branch offices (同支所) is 16,013 Koreans, 507 Japanese, <12> 199 foreigners, totaling 16,719. Classified by type of sentence, there are 14,037 convicts (受刑者), 1,918 criminal defendants (형사피고인), and 594 people detained at labor camps (勞役場留置人).

The important ideological offenders (思想犯) are: Violation of the Maintenance of Public Order Law (治維法違反) 229 men, 2 women; Violation of the Security Law (保安법위반) 44 men; Sedition (소요죄) 15 men, etc.

Comparing the above inmate statistics with 7,021 in 1910 (明治 43년), the year of the annexation, there is an increase of 9,698.

As the number of prison inmates increases every year, exceeding the capacity of 11,000 by 6,000, it is becoming cramped, with 6 people per pyeong (평) in some prison cells (1 person 3 bu (分) per pyeong in Japan, 1 person 4 bu (分) in Taiwan).

As a countermeasure, expanding prisons or budget allocations is not easy, so starting from 1931, a solitary confinement cell for unconvicted prisoners (미결수) will be newly built in the Seodaemun Prison (서대문 형무소) area to alleviate the situation somewhat, but this will not be very effective. Therefore, bail will be encouraged, and about 1,000 people are expected to be bailed out in 1931.

People indebted to detention centers, their types of crime

In 1930, the number of crimes occurring in each police station in Korea was 177,806, an increase of over 18,000 compared to the previous year, and more than double the number of occurrences in 1921, which was 82,900.

The content of the crimes is theft (절도) in the first place with 46,000 cases, followed by violations of administrative law (행정법위반) with 28,000 cases, and fraud (사기) with 24,000 cases.

The increase in unscrupulous crimes such as theft and fraud is common in all countries, but the large increase in violators of administrative law is a unique phenomenon in Korea, a threefold increase compared to 7,017 in 1922 (the increase in theft is only 19,000 cases, a little over double).

Due to the still prevalent police rule (경찰정치) in Korea, there are many harms suffering from false accusations (寃罪). In 1927 (statistics after that have not yet been announced), out of 32,220 people sent to the prosecutor's office (검사국) by each police station with opinions for indictment, only about half of them, 16,776, were indicted, and the rest were released due to suspension of indictment, non-indictment, or suspension of prosecution. <13>

유치장에 신세 끼친 사람, 그들의 범죄종류
昭和 4년중 조선내 각 경찰서에서 취급한 범죄발생 건수는 17만 7,806건으로 그 前年과 비교하면 1만 8,000여 건의 증가이며 大正 10년의 발생 건수 8만 2,900여 건과 비교하면 배 이상이다.
범죄의 내용은 절도가 首位로 4만 6,000건 그 다음이 행정법위반 2만 8,000건, 사기가 2만 4,000건이다.
절도, 사기 등 파렴치죄가 증가함은 각국의 通例이나 행정법위반자의 다수 증가는 조선의 特有한 현상으로 大正 11년의 7,017건과 비교하면 3배의 격증이다(절도의 증가는 1만 9,000건 2배 强에 불과).
조선은 경찰정치가 아직 성행되는 관계상 寃罪로 신음하는 폐가 만타. 昭和 2년도(그 차후의 통계는 아즉 발표가 업다)에 각 경찰서에서 기소의견을 붓치어 검사국에 송치한 인원 3만 2,220인중 기소된 자 약 그 반수인 1만 6,776인에 불과하고 그 남어지는 기소유예나 불기소나 기소중지로 석방되얏다.＜13＞
