Modern Biographical Sketches

Chŏn Pong-jun was from Kobu, Chŏlla Pukto. Thirty-seven years ago, in February of 1894 (Kap-o year), at the age of 41 (born in the Kap-in year), as a strong young man, he led the Tonghak (東學) (today's Ch'ŏndogyo) Rebellion, the forefront of the Korean people's movement. In just a few months, he had occupied almost all of the Chŏlla and Ch'ungch'ŏng provinces. However, he was unfortunately defeated by the combined government and Japanese forces at Kongju and, after disbanding his troops at Nonsan, he took refuge in Sunch'ang, Chŏlla Pukto. He was captured by government troops in December of that year and executed in Seoul Prison in February of the following year. This photograph was taken by Mr. Mo, from the Tenyūkyō group (天佑俠) of Japanese, when he was captured by the government army. This precious photograph, secretly kept by Mr. Ch'a Sang-ch'an of this company, is the first publication of Chŏn's photograph in Korea. (Chŏn is the one with the topknot in the center.)

近代人物畵報
全琫準씨는 全羅北道 古阜 사람이다. 距今 37년전 甲午 2월에 41세(甲寅生)의 壯年으로 朝鮮民衆運動의 최선봉인 東學(今天道敎)亂을 이르켜서 불과 몃달에 全羅 忠淸 양도를 거의 점령하얏다가 公州에서 불행히 官日 聯合軍에게 大敗하고 論山에서 散兵하야 전북 淳昌에 피신하다가 그 해 12월에 官兵에 捕虜되야 翌年 2월에 경성 감옥에서 사형을 당하얏다. 이 사진은 官軍에게 잡혀올 때에 일본인 天佑俠 일행중 모씨가 박엇던 것으로 본사 車相瓚씨의 秘藏한 珍寫眞이니 全씨의 사진발표는 조선에서 本誌가 처음이다(중앙으로 상투 짠이가 전씨)
