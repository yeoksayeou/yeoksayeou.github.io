Mussolini's Ideal Village

The "parasol-lo-tero-stella" village (파라쏠로·테로·스텔라촌) in Italy is a village of high birth rates, just as Prime Minister Mussolini wished. There are 168 households in total, and 3 houses have 16 children, 1 house has 14 children, 8 houses have 13 children, 11 houses have 12 children, and 22 houses have 11 children, and 34 houses have 10 children. On average, each household has 9 children, so they are happy that Mussolini's ideal is gradually becoming a reality.
