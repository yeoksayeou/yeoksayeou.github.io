Great Satire: New Year's Roundtable Discussion
Attendance by Notable Figures from Various Fields
Attendees (Fictional Characters)
Moderator: Songjak-saeng

Education: Mr. C

Religion: Reverend S

Journalism: Reporter K

Medicine: Dr. Y

Business: M Bank Head

Law: Lawyer L

Multiple Love Affair Expert: Miss H

Critic: Ch'adol-i

Love Problems

Moderator: I am truly grateful that you all attended despite the cold weather like today. Other newspapers or magazines hold extravagant roundtable discussions at good restaurants, but I apologize for inviting you all to this journal so coldly. However, I believe you will understand and forgive me, so I will skip the usual greetings and go straight to the main topic to hear your opinions. First of all, I would like to talk about love, the biggest concern of young men and women these days. What do you think about the issue of love, and how can we guide it so that it can be established without flaws? Please share your thoughts on this.

Mr. C: That's a good question. Leaving young men and women aside, I am always troubled by this issue, even as an educator. I also had an affair recently, and the sweetness of that taste is truly more than honey. However, I cannot do as I please because of social appearances. When I go to my lover's house, I always take a rickshaw, and when I take a walk with my lover, I always go to discreet alleys like Chŏngdong or hidden restaurants like Asogŏn. I cannot divorce my legal wife and marry my lover because circumstances do not allow it, so I am just worried. Furthermore, because my lover has the same job as me, she was transferred to the Kinki region, so I can only see her once a week on Saturdays. How can I make love eternal without a cleft lip? I am frustrated. I hope you all have good things to say on this occasion to relieve my feelings. <39>

Miss H: Why worry about such a love problem? Love should be approached in a multiple and diverse way, just like me. Even if Mr. A goes on a business trip or spends a long time in prison, I have countless lovers like Mr. B, C, and D, so I don't feel lonely. Whether the seed is from Mr. A or Mr. B, it is all the same since it is born from my womb and becomes my child. The only difficult time is when Mr. A, Mr. B, or even Mr. C come to my house at the same time. Even I, who am famous for being resourceful, feel embarrassed when facing family members or colleagues at that time. And another concern is what if I get a boring disease.

Dr. Y: With the development of medicine today, what's the worry about such venereal diseases? A few doses of Salvarsan (606號) and a few bottles of sandalwood oil (白檀油) will cure it all. As a school doctor, I secretly give abortion pills to female students, and I even built a new Western-style house thanks to that. I hope that love affairs will continue to arise among students.

Reverend S: Yes, that's right. I agree. Our church is also crowded with young men and women because of love. If there were no love, the church would be empty, and I would have sold the church bell myself to buy alcohol and run away to hell. However, as love increases every year, the number of young men and women in our church increases, and as a result of love, modern-style weddings are becoming popular. The fees I receive for officiating weddings fill my pockets, and my wife and daughter get new clothes several times a month, even if it is just for receptions without alcohol, and we go to restaurants several times. For me, love is more sacred than Jesus. However, love usually does not last long and breaks down in the middle, which is a boring thing.

Lawyer L: Oh, you are a religious person, so why are you worrying about that? As far as I'm concerned, love should break down in the middle. That way, lawsuits for consolation money (慰藉料), alimony (扶養料) claims, divorce lawsuits, paternity suits (私生子 確認 訴訟), and virginity violation lawsuits (貞操 蹂躪 訴訟) will arise more often.

Reporter K: As for me, either way is fine. Whether love succeeds or fails and leads to heartbreak and suicide, it's all good as long as it provides a lot of news material. Also, sometimes I visit to gather material for the article and accidentally fall in love with a woman due to the issue, so I get unexpected benefits. It is truly no exaggeration to say that a reporter's fate is like a cow lying in a ditch.

M Bank Head: From my experience, love seems useless in this world. With just money, it seems like I can violate the chastity of someone else's pure virgin as I please, and I can have female students or courtesans as concubines several times a month. I can have Japanese (日妾) and Western (洋妾) concubines as I please, and even if I look like a rotten peanut (洛花生) or have bad breath, they all welcome me. Love is all about money these days. What is sacred love? <40>

Ch'adol-i: Listen, everyone! Even if this world lacks public morality and loyalty, you shouldn't think of love that way just for your own ambition. Love is a serious issue in our lives, so we shouldn't take it lightly. If you want to know the details of the Love Law, read the Love Constitution that was published in this magazine sometime ago.

Problem of Birth Control

Moderator: Recently, regardless of East or West, the issue of birth control has been a major topic, and in Japan, it has recently been allowed for the lower classes under certain conditions. How should we handle this issue in Korea in the future? Please share your opinions without hesitation.

Mr. C: I am absolutely against it. If we limit births, first of all, the number of students will decrease, and at the same time, our monthly tuition income will decrease, which will easily put a strain on our food supply.

Miss H: I am absolutely in favor of it. As a woman who practices diverse and multiple love affairs, if there is no birth control, how can I handle all those children, and it gives me a headache to find the father?

Dr. Y: If birth control is allowed, it seems like I will have another Western-style house, so I am very much in favor of it. If I publicly sell abortion pills that I used to sell secretly, who will argue?

M Bank Head: If we can limit births well without affecting the mother's life, it will not have any impact on our business world, especially life insurance companies, so I am not strongly against birth control.

Reverend S: Limiting births is morally unacceptable. In my opinion, we should let children be born as they are, but if the population becomes surplus and creates livelihood difficulties, we can send the obedient children to heaven and the naughty children to hell.

Reporter K: As a fair media organization, I cannot say yes or no definitively. To gather general opinions, I will solicit essay contests on the topic and use them to fill the pages every day when there is no news.

Lawyer L: Regardless of the theory, right or wrong of the issue, if I receive good lawyer fees from either side, I will support that side, even if it pricks my conscience.

The Problem of Modern Wedding Ceremonies

Moderator: Looking at the so-called modern wedding ceremonies these days, not only is the ceremony inconsistent, but it all seems like crazy antics and monkey imitations. What kind of ceremony should we have? Please share your thoughts on this occasion.

Reverend S: Any ceremony is the same, so what can we do? No matter who changes it in what way, I hope that the good custom of giving money to the officiating pastor will remain the same. There is a saying in ancient language that "Without material, there is no sincerity," and "Etiquette comes from emotion." <41>

Miss H: It seems like the pastor is only thinking about his own greed. What is such a ceremony? Marriage is a once-in-a-lifetime event, but how can I perform the ceremony every time I get married several times a year like me? Just a handshake... then that's it.

M Bank Head: No matter how the ceremony is done, marriage is a major event in life, so it would be good to have a grand reception, no matter how difficult it is. If you don't have money, just bring your house deed or land deed to our bank, and we will give you a loan with a special mortgage.

Lawyer L: Recently, after the reception, there is always a second round, and many people are driven to eat on credit (無錢取食) and are wrongly accused. This is because the police authorities do not know the custom of teasing the groom in the Joseon era. If such an incident occurs at anyone's wedding, I will charge a special fee and advocate for a peaceful resolution, so please introduce me.

Reporter K: No matter how the ceremony is done, if you send the wedding invitation and the bride and groom's photos to the newspaper, we will introduce them well.

Lawyer L: Ceremony issues like this should be judged and enforced by the state through laws, otherwise, even if there is a good ceremony, it will be difficult to implement it in general.

Mr. C: We cannot discuss such things here, so how about gathering the educators from each school and discussing it once?

Regarding the Prevention of Early Marriage

Moderator: In Korea, the harmful practice of early marriage has been greatly reduced recently, but what methods should we use to fundamentally prevent this harmful practice? Please share your opinions.

Mr. C: Recently, at a certain girls' school, to prevent this, they refuse admission to married women and strictly prohibit currently enrolled students from marrying, and it seems to be very effective. I think it would be good if general boys' schools also imitated this and absolutely prohibited married people from entering.

Reporter K: We always seem to be criticizing others, but I don't know if it would be good to implement that thoroughly, but in reality, at some girls' schools, the daughter of the head of academic affairs who advocates for it gets married openly while still enrolled, and for the daughters of wealthy families, instead of prohibiting marriage, the principal officiates the wedding, and the general staff are busy preparing for the wedding ceremony like wedding contractors, even closing the department (廢止). It remains to be seen if it will be well implemented.

Lawyer L: I seem to be talking about the law too much, but it won't work even if prohibited by law, how can it be done with school rules? The public must be aware of it.

Reverend S: Whether it is early marriage or late marriage, I officiate them equally, so I don't particularly advocate anything. I just do as it goes, as God does.

Miss H: You can only have love marriages when you are older, so early marriage would be boring. <42>

M Bank Head: We need early marriage to create many widowers and widows, so they can remarry and borrow money from banks like ours, so from a business perspective, I secretly wish for early marriage to continue.

Dr. Y: I am a money-making person, so I am in favor of early marriage. That way, the constitution of men and women becomes weak, and many descendants become disabled, so business is good.

Ch'adol-i: Such issues are not even issues today. Early marriage is a story for when you are full and comfortable, but now that we are about to abandon even our wives and children, who would force anyone to marry early in the future? Who would do it if you told them to? It will be prohibited by itself.

The Problem of Abolishing Licensed Prostitution

Moderator: From now on, I would like to hear your opinions on the pros and cons of abolishing licensed prostitution (公娼), which is a major issue in our society. Please speak very frankly.

Mr. C: I think it is a natural thing to abolish licensed prostitution. As for me, there is no need for licensed prostitutes. There are issues with social appearances when going there, and recently the number of female students has been increasing every year, so even if we go on Sunday walks or field trips to places like Kŭmgangsan together, we can easily break the monotony.

Miss H: I have been advocating for the abolition of prostitution since a long time ago. I think it is even more necessary for our private business than for the women's liberation movement.

Dr. Y: I am in favor of the abolition. If licensed prostitution is abolished, private prostitution will increase, and at the same time, venereal diseases will become prevalent, and my business will flourish.

Reverend S: As religious people, we especially advocate for the abolition of prostitution. It is not only for humanitarian reasons, but also necessary from the perspective of church prosperity. If we abolish prostitution, wouldn't all the lonely people in the world who want comfort flock to the church?

M Bank Head: I am against it. If we abolish licensed prostitution, the number of vagrants will decrease, which will easily affect our high-interest loan business.

Reporter K: I am worried that if we abolish licensed prostitution, there will be no place for our club members to go. However, as so-called uncrowned kings, we can find a place to go somewhere. We will just write the articles as they are based on public opinion.

Lawyer L: If a lawsuit arises between the business owner and the licensed prostitute over accounting issues when abolishing licensed prostitution, I will take on the case. Also, if there is a beautiful woman among the licensed prostitutes and I can have her as a concubine without paying a ransom, I am conditionally in favor of the abolition of prostitution. <43>

戀愛問題
司會 일긔가 이와 갓치 치운 때에 여러분이 만히 出하야 주신 것은 참으로 감사함니다. 다른 신문사나 잡지사에서는 조흔 요리집에서 굉 하게 좌담회를 하는데 우리눀” 이럿케 冷淡하게 이 誌上으로 여러분을 초청하게 되니 더욱히 미안함니다. 그러나 여러분이 잘 양해하시고 용서하실 터이닛가 예사 인사의 말슴은 그만두고 본 문제에 드러가서 여러분의 고견을 듯고자 함니다. 맨 먼저 말슴코자 하는 것은 現下 청춘 남녀간에 제일 번뇌하는 연애 그 문제올시다. 여러분은 그 연애 문제를 엇데케 생각하시며 또 엇지 지도하면 缺陷이 업시 잘 성립이 되게 할 지 거긔에 대하야 잘 말슴하야 주십쇼.
C 先生 참 조흔 문제올시다. 청춘 남녀는 그만 두고 나는 명색 교육자 지위에 잇스면서도 항상 고민하는 문제올시다. 나도 근년에 戀愛個나 하야 보왓지만은 그 맛의 달기야 참 ― 꿀보다 以上이지요. 그러나 사회의 체면이 잇서서 마음대로 하지는 못하고 남몰내 하너라고 애인의 집에를 가랴면 꼭 인력거만 타고 다니고 애인과 산보를 하야도 貞洞 가튼 으식한 골목이나 雅叙遠 가튼 은근한 요리집으로 나단이엿슴니다. 本妻 離婚을 하고 애인과 결혼을 하자니 사정이 허락지 안어서 그저 혼자 고민이지요. 그럴산아 애인의 직업이 또한 나와 가튼 직업이기 때문에 近畿 지방으로 전근을 하게 되야 近日에는 겨우 토요일에 한번식 만나볼 뿐이니 더우 煩悶이 생김니다. 엇지하면 연애도 뀌여맨 언청이처럼 缺裂이 생기지 안코 永遠順成 할는지요. 나도 각갑함니다. 이 기회에 여러분이 아모조록 조흔 말슴을 하시여서 내의 속까지＜39＞ 시원하게 하야주십쇼.
H 孃 그까진 연애 문제야 걱정할 것이 무엇잇슴닛가. 연애는 그저 나처럼 多角的으로 해야만 됨니다. 甲이란 남자가 잠시 출장을 가던지 무슨 일에 長時 감옥 생활을 하더라도 乙, 丙, 丁 無數의 愛男이 잇스닛가 孤寂을 늣길 까닭도 업고 子息은 甲의 씨(種)던지, 乙의 씨던지 내 배를 빌어나서 내 자식 되기는 일반이니 그것도 문제될 건 업슴니다. 다만 곤란한 때는 甲男乙男 혹은 丙男까지 일시 집으로 차저올 때임니다. 그 때에는 아모리 비우조키로 유명한 나로도 집안 사람이나 동모를 대할 때에 얼골이 확확함니다. 그리고 또 한가지 염려는 그러다가 자미업는 병이나 걸니면 엇저나 하는 그것임니다.
Y 醫師 의학이 발달된 오늘에 잇서서 그까진 화류병쭘이야 606號 몃대나 白檀油 몃 甁이면 다 곳칠 터인데 무슨 걱정이야요. 나는 校醫로 잇스면서 처녀 학생에게 비밀하게 落胎藥을 주는 덕분에 양옥까지 새로 짓고 보닛가 학생간에도 연애가 작구 생겻스면... 합니다.
S 牧師 암 그럿코 말고요. 나도 동감이요. 우리 예배당도 원래가 연애 덕분에 그만치 청춘 남녀 敎徒도 열심으로 단이는 것임니다. 만일에 연애가 업다면 예배당이 아주 텅 비이고 나부터도 벌서 벌서 예배당 종을 팔어서 술 사먹고 지옥으로라도 도망을 갓겟슴니다. 그러나 연애가 해마다 늘어가닛가 청춘 남녀의 敎徒가 늘고 연애의 결과로 또 신식 결혼식이 성행하야 주례비 밧는 내의 주머니가 두둑하게 되고 우리 마누라와 딸은 들너리 서너라도 한달에 의복이 몃 벌식 생기며 술 업는 피로연이나마 료리집도 몃 번식 가게 됨니다. 나로서는 연애가 예수 이상으로 더 신성하게 생각함니다. 그러나 연애가 대개는 길지가 못하고 중간에 破裂이 생기니 그것이 한가지 자미업는 일로 생각함니다.
L 辯護士 아이구 당신은 宗敎家가 되여서 그 걱정까지 하십닛가. 나로 보면 연애가 중간 破裂이 생겨야 되겟습듸다. 그래야 소위 慰藉料, 扶養料 請求 訴訟, 離婚 訴訟, 私生子 確認 訴訟, 貞操 蹂躪 訴訟 가튼 사건이 만이 생길 것이 안임닛가.
K 記者 나 가터서는 이래도 조코 저래도 좃습듸다. 연애가 잘 성공을 하던지 不成功하야 失戀自殺을 하던지 그저 記事거리만 만히 제공하야 주면 그만일 것 갓슴니다. 또 어던 때에는 그 기사의 재료를 蒐輯하랴고 방문 갓다가 그 문제로 여자와 우연이 정이 드러서 座收漁入之功의 염복이 떠러저도 좃슴니다. 眞所謂 기자의 팔자란 두렁에 누은 소란 말이 무리가 안이지요.
M 頭取 내 경험 가터서는 이 세상에 연애란 소용이 업슬 것 갓습듸다. 그저 돈만 가지면 남의 순결한 처녀의 정조 유린도 맘대로 할 것 갓고 여학생이나 기생도 마음대로 한 달에 몃 번식 作妾을 할 수 잇고 日妾 洋妾도 맘대로 둘 수 잇스며 얼골이 썩은 洛花生갓치 생겻던지 입에서 썩은 악취가 나도 다 환영을 하는 것 갓슴니다. 지금의 연애는 돈이면 그만이지요. 신성한 연애가 다 무엇임닛가.＜40＞
차돌이 여보시오. 여러분! 아모리 公德도 의리도 모르는 이 세상이기로 자기의 야심만 위해서 연애를 그럿케 알어서 되겟소. 연애는 우리 인생의 중대 문제이닛가 그리 소홀이 녁일 것은 안임니다. 여러분이 만일 戀愛法의 자서한 것을 알으시랴면 언제인가 本誌에 발표한 戀愛憲法을 한 번 더 읽거 보시요.

産兒制限問題
司會者 근래 동서양을 勿論하고 산아 제한 문제가 매우 화두에 올으는 중 日本에서도 최근에 벌서 細民階級에 한하야는 엇던 조건하에 역시 산아 제한을 許하게 까지 되엿슨 즉 우리 朝鮮에서는 장차 그 문제를 엇지 取扱하여야 올흘 지 여러분의 고견이 기시면 숨김 업시 말슴하야 주십시오.
C 先生 나는 그것을 절대 반대임니다. 산아를 제한하면 첫재에 학생수가 줄고 따러서 月謝金 수입이 감소하는 동시에 우리의 밥 주머니가 겁분하게 되기가 쉬우닛가요.
H 孃 나는 절대 찬성임니다. 多角愛 하는 여자로서 산아 제한이 업다면 그 놈의 아희를 엇지 다 주처하며 또 아비를 바로 차저주기에 머리골이 압푸닛가요.
