Modern - Fortune Room
Contrast between the New and Old New Year's Days

When it becomes the first day of the solar New Year, the merchants in Jingogae put up signboards, gather in the tatami room, sit down, cheer "Yosshikora! Choe no tsui!" and stumble around drunk on toso (屠蘇) sake. But in the Jongno area, they sneer, saying, "Humph! That's just the Japanese New Year!" and try their best to sell even one more needle. Then, on the first day of the lunar New Year, as if it's their turn, they lock the shop doors and go out to pay New Year's greetings. Then, this time, friends from across the water scoff, "Is it Chosenjin no oshōgatsu (朝鮮人のお正月)?" (Korean New Year)? And Jingogae is like another world, with brilliant lily-of-the-valley-shaped electric lights and a loud sound of geta (下駄). Who is being laughed at? No matter how you look at it, aren't we losing out? How can the solar New Year be the Japanese New Year? How can double New Year's days make sense?

The Appearance of the Haechi

After the Governor-General's stone palace clumsily tore down Gwanghwamun and settled into the inner room, the haechi, not knowing where to go, had been in a dirt cave for a long time. Now, after washing its body neatly and grooming its appearance, it proudly stood tall in front of the new government building. At first glance, it seems to be living well, but in the face of wind and rain, its appearance is rather haggard, and thinking of its immeasurable sorrow as a fallen king, tears seem to well up in its eyes, and sighs seem to be heard. It wasn't a haechi that came out to shed tears...

Different from "Nero"

In Pocheon County, Han Yeong-su, a beggar boy who was caught as a terrible arsonist for setting fire to the grand houses of the wealthy and was sentenced to three years in prison, suddenly appeared. The burning anger of resenting the wealthy people in the village who showed no sympathy for his poverty and hunger could not be quenched without seeing the flames rising and the wealthy people panicking and the roof tiles flying like bullets. This is completely different from the tyrant Nero playing the lyre and reciting his own poems while setting fire to Rome. Different tastes, different purposes... "King" and "beggar"! Even the names are different...

Great Panic for Children

It has been a long time since women in labor have been calling for birth control, and in various European and American countries, many women are using various contraception methods. However, recently in nearby Japan, this issue has suddenly emerged. Not only will the fatalism of our grandmothers, who said, "How can we control the children given by the Sansin (三神) (birth goddess) with human power?" be greatly impacted, but if everyone keeps practicing contraception, children will become as precious as stars in the dawn sky in ten years.

However, in our Joseon, they are still ignoring it. Someday, those future "de-san de-su (ですます)" citizens, the botchan (坊ちゃん) and jo-chan (嬢ちゃん), become pitiful streams of dead blood and flow downwards. Even as they eagerly pound the belly skin with their fists and kick with their feet, 조선 (Joseon) mothers will be happy but also worried. Living will be hard, too!

Almost Got a Glimpse of Paradise

Like Jesus's Second Coming, wherever he went, believers and ordinary patients gathered like clouds to see the miracles of Kim OO. Pastor Kim was the most famous disciple of Jesus. How honored and proud were the believers of Joseon to have him at that time. And sadly, his miracles were exposed before long. The illness, which they thought would be cured by praying fervently for ten days or fifteen days without sparing money or sincerity, returned. A blind man opened his eyes wide at his burning prayer. But it turned out that he was not blind from the beginning. He had only closed his eyes for a moment while eating the pastor's money and praying. After that, he became a con man. Now, wherever he goes, stones and clubs fly at him. For several years, it seemed that everyone in the world had forgotten him, and his news was unclear, so they thought he was dead, but it turned out that he was still alive and kicking. Last time, he was giving a lecture at the Cheorwon Namgam Methodist Church and was attacked by young people with blows and iron fists, scattering in confusion. He should have learned his lesson after narrowly escaping death in Gando and Haeju by sneaking out the back door at night. How can there be miracles in this world? Even the most foolish people are not fooled for long. Even if they seem foolish, don't they know the savory taste of cracking pumpkin seeds with their mouths!?

The Great Trend of Round-Table Discussions

Round-table discussions, which once became very popular in Japan as a magazine sales strategy, seem to have crossed over to Joseon on the connecting ship. They proudly display the names of so-called myeongsa (名士) (famous people) or myeongsacheoreom (鳴士처럼) (noisy people), feast on sukiyaki all night, and the results of the discussions are published in print the next day or the following month. It seems to be a very helpful and convenient method for the *side. But if it's not just rambling nonsense, who would say anything? If it's beneficial, why can't we get angry! <91>

新舊元旦의 對照
양력 正月 초하로가 되면 오쇼-가쯔라고 진고개 장사치들은 판장문을 잡아 걸고 다다미ㅅ방에 모혀 안저서 욧시꼬라 쪼-이노쯔이 하고 도소주에 취해서 버둥덩그리는 판인데 鐘路 일대에서는 「홍! 그 까진 일본 설!」 하고 빈정그리면서 바늘 한 개라도 더 팔세나 애를 애를 쓴다. 그리다가 음력 초하로에는 인제 우리 차레라는 듯이 자물쇠로 商店문을 채우고 세배를 나선다. 그리면 이 번에는 「센진노 오쇼가쯔까네!」 하고 물 건너 친구들이 비소를 한다. 그리고 진고개는 딴 세상 가티 령란(鈴蘭) 형 電燈이 휘황하고 개다 소래가 요란하니 대체 누가 비웃음을 밧는 셈인고! 아모리 따저 보아도 우리가 밋지는 셈이 아닌가. 어데 양력설이 일본 설이여야 말이지. 시방 二重過歲가 말이 되여야 말이지.

해태의 出現
총독부 돌궁궐이 덜넝 光化門을 뜨더 내고 안ㅅ방 차지로 들어 안즌 뒤로부터 갈 곳을 알 수 업든 해태가 오래 土窟 세레를 밧다가 인제 말숙하게 몸 씻고 形容을 갓추어 新廳舍 정면에 웃둑 벗틔고 올나 안젓다. 얼는 보면 괜찬히 호강을 하는 것 갓기도 하지만 풍마우세에 오히려 그 형용이 파리하고 인변셩이에 강개가 무량한 듯 생각을 하고 보면 그 눈에는 눈물도 어리운 듯 한숨도 보히는 듯 잡혀간 王子의 신세와도 갓구나. 눈물을 흘니려고 나온 해태는 아니엿건만…….

「네로」와는 다르다
抱川郡에서는 부호들의 고루거각에다 처처에 불을 노아 무서운 放火犯에 걸여 삼 년 징역 언도를 바든 韓永洙란 乞人 소년이 돌현하엿다. 헐벗고 굼주린 자긔에게 추호 동정이 업는 당디 부호들을 원망하는 타는 듯한 분노는 훨훨 타올르는 불꼿과 쩔쩔매고 낭패하는 부자들의 꼴과 총알 가티 튀여 날느는 기와ㅅ장을 보지 안코는 꺼낼 재조가 업섯든 모양이니 폭왕 「네로」가 羅馬城 중에 불을 놋코 키타를 잡아뜨드면서 自作詩를 읇프든 것과는 생판 다르다. 취미가 다르고 목뎍이 다르고…「님군」과 「거지」! 일흠부터 다르기야 하지만…….

어린애 大恐慌
산거- 婦人이 산아제한(産兒制限)을 부르지즌지도 임이 오래요 歐米 각국에서는 여러 가지 피임(避妊)법을 써서 그것을 실행하고 잇는 부인들이 적지 안치만 갓가운 일본에서는 요새 와서 밧작 이 문제가 머리를 처들게 되엿단다. 「삼신이 임의로 하시는 자식을 人力으로 엇지 할 수가 잇서야지」 하든 우리 할어머니들의 숙명론이 대타격을 마즐 뿐 아니라 너도나도 하고 작구만 피임을 하고 보면 한 십 년 지난 날에는 어린애가 새벽 하늘에 별과 가티 귀해질 것이다.
그러나 우리 조선에서는 아즉도 들은 체도 아니하고 잇다. 장차 저 쪽에서 데삼 데사 국민이 될 봇쨩, 죠-쨩들이 가엽게도 한 줄기 死血이 되여 아래로 흘너 바릴 때 빨니 나와 무슨 일을 해 보구 십허 갑갑한 듯이 주먹으로 뱃가죽을 두들기고 발로 포득포득 차덩기는 설내에 조선의 어머니들은 깃분 가운데도 성이 가시겟지. 살어 나가기가 고달푸기도 하겟지!

하마터면 天堂 구경
