To Organize a Life Improvement League, A Plan to Abolish Double New Year's Observance
						Han Gi-ak

Even now, people over middle age, steeped in old customs, think that they truly get a year older only after celebrating the lunar New Year, and this seems especially true for women. It seems very difficult to abolish customs that have been followed for a long time and are deeply ingrained, and to observe the solar calendar all at once. However, the double life we are currently living, even though we are clearly aware of it, is due to lingering customs and the inability to abolish them. If we want to improve our lives, the first thing we need to improve is this double New Year's observance (二重過歲). Our attitude is very unclear. When we go out into society, we hold banquets for the solar New Year, perform New Year's greetings (年始禮), and observe the solar New Year in spirit and form, but when we return home, we still have to bow to elders on the lunar New Year and eat rice cake soup (떡국), which is the responsibility of the housewife. Nowadays, some families realize the need for it and cook rice cake soup and play games on the solar New Year, but when the lunar New Year comes, the neighbors cook rice cake soup and the children come and go in new clothes, so we cannot uniquely spend it lonely in our own house. Also, we have to serve the so-called New Year's greeting table (세배상) to those who come to bow, and we cannot say that we don't have a New Year's greeting table or tell them not to come because we observe the solar New Year. So, we naturally end up observing the lunar New Year again, even though we say we won't. As of yet, we don't know what to do. I think it is very difficult to do it only among ourselves in an individual family. An organization should be formed, perhaps called the Life Improvement League (生活改善同盟), and only a structured group like that can generally promote it. First, we need to change the housewife's perception, so the head of the family should constantly enlighten them and dress the children in new clothes on the solar New Year and create New Year's games to make them feel the New Year's spirit. However, it seems impossible to abolish the lunar New Year and celebrate the solar New Year as a holiday all at once. <74> In the future, an organization should be created to promote improvement not only in New Year's observance but also in all aspects of life. If we live like that, and if our thoughts and surroundings gradually change, natural improvement will occur. <75>
