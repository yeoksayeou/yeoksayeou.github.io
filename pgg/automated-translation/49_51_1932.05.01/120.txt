The Economics of Spring Excursions by Mobo-Moga
						I Seo-gu

When a hole appears in a sock, you mend it and wear it. However, when going to meet a lover, even mendable socks are discarded, and new ones are bought. Herein lies the development of economic issues.

"Are you going to meet another beautiful woman today?" His wise wife, while ironing his dress pants, opened her eyes in suspicion. The young man, her husband, who was matching a tie to his new shirt, felt a pang in his heart and said,
"What are you talking about?"
He feigned innocence. But his wife's questioning became even more intense.
"Then why are you changing into a shirt that isn't even dirty, shaving a beard that isn't that long, and making such a fuss about getting your pants ironed?"

He had nothing to say to this. Helpless, he just laughed.
"Oh, you're too suspicious. Is there any reason for that?" Leaving behind a final cry, half appeasing, half angry, the neatly dressed young gentleman left the house and stepped out onto the street. The feeling of shaking off new clothes and flaunting one's appearance under the bright spring sun, while going to meet a beautiful woman, is as clear and refreshing as a newly polished mirror.

How should I play with my lover?
Herein lies the beginning of detailed plans. At that time, one must first consult with the money in one's pocket.
What kind of money would it be? Pledges are made. Monthly salaries are obtained. One might even raid their mother's purse or, worse, break into the piggy bank where their wife has been saving every penny.

Then, how much money should one have? I solemnly conclude my lecture on the classification of social classes and the strengthening of spring economics, and boast:

First Lesson: When you only have one won

It's truly hopeless to try to enjoy a day in spring with a lover with only one won. You might even want to take off and pawn the suit you're wearing. But if you take off your suit, you won't be able to walk alongside your lover, so even if you're sweating, you must create a budget plan that can be covered with one won.
一. Bread 20 jeon (four pieces)
一. Sugar 5 jeon (powdered)
一. Apples 10 jeon (two pieces)
一. Bananas 20 jeon (ten pieces)
一. Rock Candy 5 jeon (a handful)
一. Mat rental 10 jeon (one piece)
一. Ramune two bottles 10 jeon (one bottle per person)
一. Streetcar fare 10 jeon (for the return trip)
Total 1 won
If you use one won this thoughtfully, you can certainly act as the Minister of Finance of a country. So, how do you play with one won?

First, go out into the street with your lover, carrying only a wrapping cloth (bojagi). Buy bread, sugar, apples, bananas, and rock candy there and wrap them up. Then, go straight up to Namsan Park. Leaving behind the dreary spring scenery of Gyeongseong (경성) that melts in the corners between brick walls and tiled roofs, start climbing up the back road of Gyeongseong. As you climb higher and higher, you'll get breathless, so take out a piece of rock candy and put it in your mouth one by one. Climb a little further, and the presence of people will gradually disappear, and the city of Gyeongseong will start to shrink. First, a world of only two people is unfolded. Sit on the grass or on a rock <24> and sing songs. It's good for the young lady to eat the bananas, and for the young master to eat the apples, skin and all. Skillfully peel a banana and say,
"Open your mouth."
Who would say anything if the young master puts it in the young lady's mouth? However, if you overeat in the middle where you are resting, you'll run out of food later. If you keep walking up without resting, you will reach the old site of Namsan Beacon Fire Station (Namsan bongsu) and Guksadang (국사당). Here, you get the exhilarating taste of seeing the entire Gyeongseong at a glance.

The gently flowing Han River stretches out to the south, and Changdeokgung (창덕궁) and Gyeongbokgung (경복궁) palaces are submerged in the haze. Here, Guksadang lives. There's delicious spring water, which is better than cheap cider. There is a large pagoda tree that generously wraps the youth of love in its embrace. Eat lunch here.
Bread
Sugar
Fruit
are eaten in turn. After enjoying themselves here for a while and drinking another bowl of spring water, take the road west, and descending along the old fortress, you will reach the pine forest of Jangchungdan (장충단). If the journey up to here isn't too much, the young lady shouldn't get sore legs. Anyone can go because the road is very smooth and easy to find. When you come down to Jangchungdan, children carrying cider, ramune, and kopi-lu (커피루) (coffee liquor) come looking for the youths in love. Since your legs hurt, buy a mat for ten jeon, spread it out, and sit down. The children stubbornly keep asking you to buy things from beside you. How can the youths who are looking for their own world of two endure standing there and nagging until the end? Helplessly, spend 20 jeon out of the remaining 30 jeon to buy two bottles of ramune and wet your throats. You can play here until you are hungry. Since there are still ten jeon left, isn't there a streetcar waiting when you come down in front of Jangchungdan?

Second Lesson: When you have three won

One hundred and fifty nyang (냥) is three times one won. You can splurge a little more.
一 25 jeon streetcar fare
二 20 jeon Changgyeongwon (창경원) admission ticket
三 80 jeon lunch
四 20 jeon bottle of cider
五 15 jeon two muskmelons, one mat
六 60 jeon admission fee
七 10 jeon cushion fee
八 50 jeon late-night snack
九 20 jeon two cups of parfaits
Total 3 won
My beloved, let's enjoy the spring.
I have three won. I will appear at the gate of Changgyeongwon at 2 p.m. this Saturday. Let the person who waits first have priority in courtship.

A single letter moved a beauty. As soon as two o'clock struck, a young man and a beautiful woman appeared in front of Changgyeongwon gate.
Man: Hey!
Woman: Did you wait long?
Their eyes met. First, they have to buy tickets. All the flower colors of Changgyeongwon seem to be in full bloom for the two of them. Strolling side by side through the cherry blossom tunnel, whispering to each other <25> is something that only those who have a lover can enjoy. After entering the zoo first and looking at the flowers and people, they turn back to the museum and enter the botanical garden. The ripples of Chundangji (춘당지) and the songs of mandarin ducks swimming in pairs will not be seen indifferently.

Standing side by side by the water, looking at this scene, the young lady quietly grabbed the young master's hand. The young master, who had been absentmindedly watching the harmonious pleasure of the mandarin ducks, knows the meaning of why the young lady is holding his hand. The young master silently showed a smile.
"It's very interesting, isn't it?"
From here, the chatter begins.
"Just like us."
If the words that come and go are like this, what worries or concerns can be mixed in the world?
"Aren't you hungry?"
People in love get hungry more than others. Well...
The young lady's answer becomes blurred. It's neither "yes" nor "no."
"Then I'll go buy something to eat. Even Mt. Geumgang (금강산) is only worth seeing after you've eaten."
Two lunch boxes for 80 jeon
One bottle of cider for 20 jeon
Two muskmelons for 10 jeon
One mat for 5 jeon
He bought 1 won and 15 jeon worth of items. Since each person paid for their own streetcar fare when coming to Changgyeongwon, only 5 jeon for streetcar fare was included in the young master's prepared money, so even after buying admission tickets, 1 won and 60 jeon still remains.

Spreading the mat under the cherry blossom branches, they start eating the lunch boxes first. They drink cider in turns. They peel and eat the muskmelons.
"Oh, my legs!"
The young lady stretches out her legs. Only then is it discovered that there is a hole in her artificial silk socks.
"There's a hole in your socks, hahaha."
"Oh, my goodness, hohoho."
What's so funny about this, but for two young people whose hearts are inexplicably lifted, it's more of a laughing matter than Don Quixote.
After playing to their heart's content in Changgyeongwon, they take the streetcar and enter Honmachi (本町) (present-day Chungmuro). They are looking for the spring colors of the city. Spring colors in every window. Parasols, silk scarves, spring hats, spring outfits, spring fruits. After completely conquering Honmachi 1-jeongmok to 5-jeongmok, they go into Meiji Confectionery (명치 제과) or Agata Tamae (아까 다마에) to drink a parfait. Then the cost is 20 jeon! There is still 1 won and 40 jeon left. When it gets close to seven o'clock when the sun sets, they go into Huirakgwan (희락관) or Daejeonggwan (대정관). If they go to the Korean film theater (Joseon hwaldong sajin-gwan), it's too crowded, so they go to the Japanese film theater (Ilbon hwaldong sajin-gwan). They buy the lowest class tickets with the excuse that taking off their shoes on the second floor is inconvenient and that the lower floor is the best if they're going to a Japanese movie.
"Adults 3rd class 40 jeon"
"Students 3rd class 20 jeon"
It is written. It doesn't matter if she graduated from school or not, Korean women can pretend to be students as long as they have a tremeori hairstyle, so the young master buys an adult ticket, and the young lady buys a student ticket and goes in. In the face of gentlemanly pretense, he has no choice but to lay down a cushion, so another 10 jeon goes out. Now, only 60 jeon is left. After watching the show, they each eat a bowl of 25-jeon Western-style rice, and with the remaining 10 jeon, they go to find a place to sleep.

Third Lesson: When you get lucky and have ten won to play with

If just two people put ten won in their pocket and go out, they will have such confidence that even the head of a bank would kneel before them.
"Honey, what are you going to do with all ten won?"
"Let's cut back on the fabric and go to the zoo to see the flowers."
The respectable wife, who had been begging him to see the flowers until yesterday, grabs the wrist of her husband, who is about to go out with all ten won.
"Let's have a day of fun, even if we don't have much. Flower viewing is not every day. On public holidays, the fallen petals will be gone, so let's spread our wings and go out to play."
The regret has not yet completely disappeared, but it is difficult to go against her husband's determined decision, so she sits down and starts planning how to spend the money.
Onyang Hot Springs
Soyosan
Gwanaksan
Ui-dong
are all far away. Everything was cumbersome. Even if they go to Onyang Hot Springs, the round-trip travel expenses will be half price on Saturdays. Even if they go to Soyosan, if they get off at Dongducheon (동두천) on the Gyeongwon Line, they will encounter a strange landscape that pierces through the winding scenic valleys. There are waterfalls, hermitages, rough roads, shady forests, and outstanding peaks, and it has received the praise of "Little Geumgang (소금강)". If you go early in the morning, you can play all day and come back in the evening. The temples even have preparations for alcohol and snacks, and if you pay 50 jeon, they will set out a lavish feast. Gwanaksan is also ten ri (리) along the mountain road from Anyang Station (안양정거장) on the Gyeongbu Line. Climbing along the clear stream, the azaleas, which are luxuriant on the mountains and sell laughter, welcome the travelers. Yeombulam (염불암), Yeomju-dae (염주대), and Sammaksa (삼막사) are full of things to see. Sammaksa is a great temple in Gyeonggi, and Baeknyeonam (백년암) of the teacher Ji Baeg-nyeon (지백년) is famous. From Sammaksa, you can see the sea of Incheon. Ui-dong is famous for its cherry blossoms, and there is a villa of Yeon Dong-gyoju Go Son Uiam (연도 교주 고손 의암) . Samgaksan (삼각산) soars high through the shade of the flowers, and flower petals embroider the waves flowing between the valleys, making it an excessive scenic spot to play in for a day. Gyeongwon Line to Chang-dong, car to Ui-dong - there are plenty of places to play with ten won. However, after a long period of discussion, they decided to take their feet to a place close to the city, in other words, the suburbs of the city. First, the two went out to Jongno Intersection and raised their hands to the passing 80-jeon taxi. It is common to get into a car worth money. They ordered to go to Namsan Park. The 1931 Buick luxury car passed through Yeongnakjeong (영락정) as smoothly as oil, and went to Gyeongseong New Temple.
"To Hanyang Park!"
"To the wide yard of Joseon Shrine!"
Here, you can stop the car for a while and admire the cherry blossoms that are luxuriant on Gyeongseong Namsan to your heart's content. There seems to be a rule that cars that do not worship at Joseon Shrine cannot stay in this wide yard, so it would be good to leave the car in front of Hongyeomun (홍여문) and walk slowly to see the view. After drinking in the spring scenery to your heart's content, put the car in full speed again and head to the Han River Iron Bridge. The thrill of the car running with the spring breeze while turning the Samgakji Curve (삼각디 카부) is worth a thousand gold. Get off at the Han River Bridge and
Car fare 1 won 60 jeon
is readily handed over. The wife tried to bargain a bit, but her beloved husband
"We got off and got on again, so we have to pay twice," and quietly silenced her.
This event led to a story later on, and after that, whenever there was a need to use small change urgently in the family, the wife always lamented and complained that it was good to give 1 won 30 jeon or 50 jeon at the Han River Bridge, but he gave 60 jeon.
The two who got off at the Han River Bridge immediately rented a boat. The boat is loaded with

The beloved man rolls up his sleeves and takes the oars, and the wife covers her back with a light green parasol and faces her beloved man, taking on the role of captain. On the gentle waves of the Han River, a small boat is launched, loaded with love, alcohol, and food. At this rate, it seems like they can go anywhere. It's crowded if you row down to the lower reaches, and it's hard to come back, so the principle is to go up to the upper reaches. After rowing for a while, you will see a grassy field with luxuriant pine trees. Here, they throw the boat and land decisively. Picking wildflowers is extraordinary.
"Honey, pour me a drink."
"Oh, my goodness, why am I a kisaeng (기생)?"
"Instead, I'll peel this fruit for you."
"Forget the fruit, then pour me some cider."
The two can forget about paying the electricity bill, the firewood running out, and paying the monthly installment for the suit, and spend the most enjoyable time embraced by the great nature. The clock strikes five in the afternoon.
Boat rental 50 jeon
is paid, and they call a car again and get in. Instead of giving 80 jeon, they give 1 won and ask him to go around behind the Iron Railroad Park. There are many aspects of this back alley's secluded scenery that cannot be abandoned. The car returns to the city and stops in front of Samwol Obokjeom (삼월 오복점). As if they were about to make some big deal, they take the "elevator" and go down in front of the fourth-floor restaurant, and after looking at the samples for a while like the judges of a food exhibition, they eventually

are consumed. As he comes out of the restaurant with a toothpick in his mouth, his wife suddenly

makes an urgent proposal.
"Oh, already? There's still 3 won and 75 jeon left, but you're answering as if I can't go home without spending all ten won."
"Then where are you going again?"
"Where are you going? We have to go to Changgyeongwon to see the night blossoms."
The wife looked at the bracelet watch she received as a souvenir when she got married and said,
"It will still be an hour before they open."
"Oh, it's not hard to spend an hour. Let's slowly walk around and look at Mitsukoshi (미쓰코시) Jeongjaok (정자옥) and see the sights."

"Honey, that's good."
"Well, it's 38 won."
"When will we ever buy one?"
"Well, maybe we'll buy it with the summer bonus."
In a place where tailors have spread out their wares, the two cannot bear to turn their feet, and among them, they grab the cheapest one and struggle.
"Really, you need to buy a spring coat."
"My coat is fine, but you need to buy an opera bag."
During this time, the remaining 3 won and 70 jeon seemed to move more than once or twice. However, they didn't want to dampen each other's mood, so
"Then let's buy it."
They couldn't make a decision. Finally, the time has come. The two again got into a car, entered Changgyeongwon, followed the path of the second lesson, and completely consumed the remaining 3 won and 70 jeon. When they came home, the mother came out and said,
"They came to urge us to pay the rent."<27>

모뽀모껄의 新春行樂 經濟學
						李瑞求
양말 구녁이 뚜러지면 지어 신는다. 그러나 애인을 차저 가게 되는 경우에는 지어 신을 수 잇는 양말이라도 버서 바리고 새 양말을 사신는다. 여긔에 비로소 경제 문제가 발달된다.
오늘은 또 엇던 미인을 맛나러 가시는게로구려. 그의 현명한 안해는 양복 바지에 금을 내면서 시긔의 눈을 떳다. 새 와이샤쓰에 빗갈을 맛처 넥타이를 매고 잇든 그의 남편되는 청년은 가슴이 선뜻하야
「그것은 또 우슨 소리요.」
시침을 딱 띄엇습니다. 그러나 부인의 추궁은 한층 더 맹열하얏습니다.
그러면 왜 채 더럽지도 안은 와이샤쓰를 가라입고 그리 자라지도 안은 수염을 깍그시고 바지에 금까지 내라고 성화를 하십니까.
이 말에는 애시 대답할 거리가 업서 젓습니다. 하는 수 업시 허허 우스며
온참 의심도 만소 그럴 니가 잇나. 달내는 듯 성내는 듯 마즈막 비명(悲鳴)을 남기고 깨끗이 차린 청년 신사는 집을 떠나 거리로 나섯습니다. 봄날 양명한 태양 아래 새 옷을 떨뜨리고 단장을 휘두르며 미인을 차저 가는 긔분이야 말맛다나 금시에 닥거노흔 거울가티 맑고 상쾌한 것입뉀ˆ다.
애인을 맛나 엇더케 놀까.
여긔에 비로소 뎨이단뎍 게획이 석워지게 됩니다. 그 때에는 무엇보다도 몬저 주머니 속에 든 돈과 상의를 해야 합니다.
돈은 왼 돈이겟는가. 뎐당도 잡힙니다. 월수도 엇습니다. 어머니 주머니터름도 하고 심하면 안해되는 이의 한푼 두 푼 모와 둔 벙어리도 개트려 가지고 나스게 됩니다.
그러면 돈이 얼마나 잇서야 되는가 나는 상중하게 계급에 난호와 신춘 경제학 강화를 맛치고 자랑합니다.

일원을 가지고 이 만콤 유의하게 쓰면 그야말노 일국의 대장대신 노릇은 족히 할 것입니다. 그러며 일원을 드러 어떠케 노느냐.

을 내고 자동차를 다시 불너 탐니다. 팔십전 줄 것을 일원을 주고 텰도 공원 뒤로 도라 드러가자고 합니다. 이 뒤골의 한아한 풍치는 버릴 수 업는 점이 만습니다. 자동차는 다시 시중으로 도라드러 삼월 오복점 압헤 가 노힙니다. 아조 무슨 큰 흥정이나 하실 것가티 「엘리베이터」를 모라서 사층 식당압헤가 나려 두 분은 음식품 전람회 심사 위원 모양으로 한참 견본을 드려다 보다가 결국은
미쓰마메 二十錢
스시 三十五錢
양요리 六十錢
소-다수 二十錢
의 소비를 하기에 이릅니다. 이쑤시개를 물고 식당에서 나아오다가 안악네가 뭇득스며
여보 인제 고만 집으로 갑시다.
긴급 제의가 나아옵니다.
아-발서가 아즉도 三圓七十五錢이 남앗는데 맛치 십원 돈을 다-쓰지 안코는 집에 도라가지 못할 사람가티 대답이 나아갑니다.
「그럼 또 어대를 간단 말이요.」
「어띄를 가다니 이 길로 창경원 밤꼿구경 가야지.」
안악께서는 결혼할 때에 긔념품으로 밧은 팔둑 시계를 드려다 보더니
「아즉도 한 시간은 더 잇서야 문을 열 터인데.」
「아- 그까짓 한 시간 보내기야 무에 어렵소. 자 미쓰고시 뎡자옥을 천천히 도라다니며 구경이나 합시다그려.」
이리하야 두 분은 약 한 시간동안 미쓰고시 정자옥을 방방곡곡이 시찰을 하기에 이르럿슴니다.
「여보 저 것 좃치.」
「글세 삼십팔원이로군.」
「언제나 하나 사나.」
「글세 녀름 상여금에나 살가.」
양복장이 느러노힌 곳에서 두 분은 발길을 차마 못돌니고 그 중에서도 제일 갑싼 놈을 잡고 몸부림이 낫습니다.
「참 당신 봄외투 하나 사야지.」
「내 외투야 괜찬치만 당신 오페라 빽이나 사야지.」
이리하는 동안에 남은 돈 三圓七十전이 움즉움즉하기 한 두 번이 아니엇습니다. 그러나 피차에 긔분을 저상하기를 끄려서
「그러면 삽시다.」
하는 결정을 짓지는 못햇습니다. 긔어코 때는 왓습니다. 두 분은 또 다시 자동차를 모라 창경원으로 드러서서 제이과의 경로를 발바 남은 돈 삼원 칠십전은 완전히 소비햇습니다. 집에 도라오니 어멈이 나스며
「저- 집세 재촉을 하고 갓서요.」＜27＞
