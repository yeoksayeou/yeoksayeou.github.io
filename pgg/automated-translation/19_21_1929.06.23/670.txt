기사형태	시 (Poem)

Rice Transplanting

A field of rice paddies
A male farmer
Near the house, every seedbed
A female farmer

Carefully picking up the seedlings
Hoping to plant them
A swallow flying low over the water
Kisses and goes

Wearing worn-out bamboo hats
They were cheerful
In the muddy rice paddies
Night rain falls

Even the silver rain and golden rain
Are not so beautiful
But the song of rice transplanting
Flows out more sweetly. <161>
