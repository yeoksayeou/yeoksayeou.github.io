필자	春園
기사형태	문예평론 (Literary Criticism)

Youth
						Chunwon

◇ ◇ ◇
One day in late April. I went to Ch'anggyŏngwŏn to see the cherry blossoms. It had been perhaps five years since I had been bedridden. It was the day the flowers were at their peak and falling. Petals fell like a shower of snow, touching my sick body.

The falling petals, each petal, mark the passing spring.
How could my sick body not cherish them?
Youth seems like yesterday, so I cannot remain indifferent.

It was a day off for schools and companies, and many male and female students and other young men and women came to see the falling flowers. The flushed faces of those youths, the daughters of youth stepping on the falling flowers without a care, their feet damp. They are the masters of life and the masters of spring.
◇ ◇ ◇＜156＞
Amidst the pouring blossoms, the youths come and go,
Their flushed faces are the masters of spring,
Treading on the fallen flowers without irreverence.

It seems deeply irreverent for a sick person like me to tread on the fallen flowers. However, for the beautiful flowers of life to be trampled under their feet would be a wish for the fallen flowers as well. Moreover, how many petals would touch their flushed cheeks among the countless fallen flowers?
However, youth passes. The young grow old, the beautiful become ugly, and the strong become weak. Life's endeavors and joys are all the work of youth, but youth is like a dream, like the blink of an eye.
◇ ◇ ◇
In the morning, I sought spring and lay in the shade of the flowers,
When I awoke, all the flowers in the pavilion had fallen.
Let the passing spring pass, let the new spring come.

The ending is, without a doubt, a stubborn refusal.
I wanted to say, "Everyone has scattered, and I am alone,"
But I once flaunted a burst of heroism.＜157＞

靑春
						春園
◇ ◇ ◇
四月末 어느날. 昌慶苑에 벗꼿을보다. 年來 病席에 잇는모양으로 아마 五年만인가. 꼿이 한창 지는 날이다 펄펄  펄펄 눈가티 소낙비 가티 꼿은 날려 病든 몸을 따린다.

펄펄 날리는 꼿 송이송이 가는 봄을
날 가티 병든 몸이 아낀다 하랴만은
청춘이 어젠듯하여 무심못해하노라

이날이 學校, 會社 노는날이어서 男女學生과 其他 젊은 男女들이 만히 지는 꼿을 보러 오다. 그 靑春에 上氣한 어엿분 얼골들, 앗가운줄모르게 지는 꼿을 밟는 靑春의 딸이 축촉이 저젓슬 발들. 그들은 人生의 主人이오  봄에도 主人 이다
◇ ◇ ◇＜156＞
보라 치는속에 오고가는 젊은이의
상긔해 붉은 얼굴 봄에도 주인이며
락화를 짓밝고가도 무엄하지아너라

날가티 병든자가 落花를 짓밝는것은 심히 무엄해보인다. 그러나 아름다운 人生의 꼿들의발에 밝히는것은 落花에게도 所願일것이다. 하물며 그들의 上氣한 빰을 따려보는이야 億兆만흔 落花中에 몃치나되랴.
그러나 靑春은 간다. 젊은이는 늙고, 아름답던것은 미워지고 힘잇던것은 弱하여진다. 人生의 事業과 歡樂이 모도 靑春의 일이어늘 靑春은 꿈과갓고나 눈깜작할새로고나.
◇ ◇ ◇
아츰에 봄을 차자 그늘에 누엇더니

가는봄 가라하여라 새봄 오라하여라
