Folk Song from Seopyeon - A Loyal Heart
						Han Jeong-dong

—Han Sik-nyeo's Lament—

The willows by the Yalu River,
When the mother is angry,
All day today,
I've only drawn water.
Oh dear, oh dear,
What a bother.

Passing by while fetching water,
I dropped the water jar lid (아리는물동이니는 道具).
Even if I passed by without seeing it,
It would have been fine.
Oh dear, oh dear,
What a bother.

The fate of picking it up,
I've digested it in my heart,
I've digested it in my heart,
My sin of not speaking shamefully, what is it?
Oh dear, oh dear,
What a bother.

At that time, at this time,
Spring has passed,
At that time, at this time,
My heart is the same.
Oh dear, oh dear,
What a bother. <159>
