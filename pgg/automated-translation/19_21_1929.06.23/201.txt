Special Announcement

Regarding issues of this magazine that are out of print: No. 5 (Shōwa 2, March issue), No. 7 (Shōwa 2, June issue), No. 10 (Shōwa 2, December issue), No. 11 (Shōwa 3, February issue), No. 17 (Shōwa 3, December issue). We are making this special announcement because they are needed for preservation. Any of our readers who have the issues listed above should send them to our company by the tenth of next month. We will exchange them for the current month's or next month's issue as requested. <45>
