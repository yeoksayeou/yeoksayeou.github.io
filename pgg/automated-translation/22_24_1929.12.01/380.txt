별건곤 제24호
1929년 12월 01일
四時名物 平壤冷麵, 珍品·名品·天下名食 八道名食物禮讚 (Famous Pyongyang Cold Noodles, A Praise of Famous Local Foods of the Eight Provinces, Delicacies, Masterpieces, World-Famous Foods)
金昭姐
문예기타 (Essays and Others)

Spring

In March and April, when the spring breeze blows gently and trees sprout leaves and flowers bloom on Moranbong (牧丹臺), I enjoy the long days in the mood of spring. When my tired legs are set down in the two-story building in front of Taedongmun (大同門), I let go of the fatigue of the day along the blue waters of the Taedong River (浿江), and satisfy my hunger with a bowl of cold noodles filled to the brim!

Summer

In Pyongyang, where the heat is quite high in the summer due to the continental influence, when the heat is particularly severe, large chunks of ice are buried in a white wide-mouthed bowl, and the cold noodles are coiled around each other! I ward off the heat with ice and shake off the weariness with mustard and sourness. <68>

Autumn

When I meet a friend I have longed for years in Pyongyang (浿城), and as we face the moonlight shining through the willows of Nangna Island (綾羅島), we open our hearts and talk about old times! The long strands of cold noodles that are difficult to cut off seem to speak of their friendship!

Winter

Just as Koreans often miss kimchi when they go abroad, there is a force that makes Pyongyang people suddenly miss Pyongyang when they are away from home. This is the taste of cold noodles in winter. When heavy snow falls, only the voice of my mother, sewing and telling stories of the Romance of the Three Kingdoms (三國誌), echoes quietly in the room. When the letters in front of my eyes appear as two or three, and my mother's voice gradually becomes faint, a loud voice shouts, "Noodles!" and what is brought in through the door is a stack of cold noodles. The taste of piercing the frozen kimchi broth and eating it with one or two pairs of chopsticks in the chilled kimchi soup with ice floating on top, and then rushing into the warm ondol room (溫突房)! Is there anyone who hasn't tasted this taste of Pyongyang cold noodles! How is your imagination! <69>

春
봄바람이 건듯 불어 잠자든 牧丹臺에 나무마다 닙트고 가지마다 꼿필는 3,4월 기-ㄴ해를 春興에 겨워 즐기다가 지처진 다리를 大同門압 드놉흔 2層樓에 실어놋코 浿江 풀른물 딸아 종일의 피로를 흘녀보내며 그득 담은 한그릇 冷麵에 시장을 맷출 때!

夏
大陸的 影響으로 녀름날 熱度가 상당히 늡흔 平壤에서 더위가 몹시 다를 때 흰 벌덕대접에 주먹갓흔 어름뎅여를 *여 감추고 서리서리 얼킨 冷麵! 어름에 더위를 물니치고 芥子와 酸味에 倦怠를 떨저버리＜68＞

秋
數年을 두고 그리든 知己를 浿城에 마저다가 綾羅島 버들사이로 빗치여오는 달빗을 마즈며 胸襟을 혜처놋코 古懷를 說話할 때 줄기줄기 기-ㄴ 冷麵이 물어 끈키 어려움이 그들의 友情을 말하는 듯할 때!

冬
朝鮮사람이 외국 가서 흔이 그리운 것이 김치 生覺이라듯이 平壤사람이 타향에 가잇을 때 문득문득 平壤을 그립게 하는 한 힘이 잇스니 이것은 겨울에 冷麵 맛이다. 함박눈이 더벅더벅 나리울 때 방안에는 뱌느질하시며 三國誌를 말슴하시는 어머니의 목소래만 고요히 고요히 울니고 잇다. 눈압해 글자 하나가 두흘셋으로으로 보이고 어머니 말소래가 차차 가늘게 들너올 때 『국수요--』하는 큰 목소래와 갓치 방문을 열고 들여놋는 것은 타레타레 지은 冷麵이다. 꽁꽁 어른 김치죽을 뚜르고 살어름이 뜬 진장김치국에다 한저(箸) 두저(箸) 풀어먹고 우루루 떨여서 溫突房 안렛묵으로 가는 맛! 平壤冷麵의 이 맛을 못본이요! 상상이 어떳소!＜69＞
