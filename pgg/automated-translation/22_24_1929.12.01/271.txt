기사형태	시 (Poem)

King Sukjong's Dream Composition

In the third month of Changan, the grass is like a carpet,
A piece of Fukuzhou and Anjeongju.

Iron horses come to the Han River bank,
Pitifully facing each other, the old lord and ministers. <59>

**Named Entities**

長安	Changan
福州	Fukuzhou
安定州	Anjeongju
