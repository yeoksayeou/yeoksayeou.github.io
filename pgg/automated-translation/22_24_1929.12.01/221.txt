Spring Dream

						㟢參

Last night in the bridal chamber, a spring breeze arose,
In a brief moment amid a lingering dream on the pillow,
Remotely recalling the beauty of the Xiang River waters,
Having traveled thousands of li throughout the south of the river. <53>

Named Entities

湘江	Xiang River
江南	Kangnam
