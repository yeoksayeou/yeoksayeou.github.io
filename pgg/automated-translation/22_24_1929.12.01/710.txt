기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Notice and Postscript)

Offering a Sack of White Rice: A Big Prize Problem
Something easy for anyone to know.
Hurry and submit by the deadline.
Please look closely at the photograph at the very beginning of this book. The photograph above is of the five women of the Toyolhoe Dance Troupe (土月會舞踊部) in the photograph below in disguise. Figure out which person is disguised as which person, and if, for example, you think number one is "ga," then write on the answer sheet: 1 is ga… 2 is na… and so on, in order, and send it in.

▽Those who know the answer should cut out the paper below with scissors, write the answer on the paper, clearly write your address and name, put it in an envelope, and send it in by December 25th.

▽We will send a special prize made by our company to 100 people who get it right (if there are too many, we will draw lots), and we will also draw one person from among them to give a sack of high-quality white rice. (If you are in an area without a branch office, we will send the equivalent amount of money.)
