기사제목: 雜誌注文할 (When Ordering Magazines)
기사형태: 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Post-Editorial)

When Ordering Magazines

1. Write your address and name clearly without blurring.
(The magazine might be returned.)

2. Clearly specify which month or issue number you want.
(If you don't write the month, the wrong issue might be sent easily.)

3. Don't forget to add postage when sending payment.
(Sending a postcard to request the 부족 (pujok, deficiency) is more troublesome and a loss.)

4. Instead of sending postal money orders or stamps, please send payment by postal transfer whenever possible.
(There is no concern about intermediate loss, and the remittance fee is cheaper.) <105>
