Big Prize for 100 People

Question
Look at the photographs in this February issue of Pyŏlgŏn'gon (別乾坤) and tell us which photograph you like the best. If it's a good photo, write down what page it's on and what kind of photo it is, or whose photo it is. ◇ Please write your name and address clearly on the answer sheet.

Prizes
First Prize (1 person) = Fountain Pen, Second Prize (2 people) = Japanese Novel (日文小說), Ordinary Prize (97 people) = Magazine

Rules
◇ Please write your name and address clearly on the answer sheet.
◇ Please attach the reader's certificate (讀者證) at the end of this page.
◇ Deadline is February 24th.＜37＞
