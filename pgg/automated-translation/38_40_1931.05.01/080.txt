Statistics Office

Korean Rice Exported Overseas
Last year, the total amount of Korean rice exported overseas was 4,915,090 *seok*. Of this, 2,584,906 *seok* was brown rice (玄米), 2,169,684 *seok* was polished rice (精米), 1,259 *seok* was *jeongjo* (正租, likely a type of tribute rice), 99,313 *seok* was broken rice (碎米), and 59,928 *seok* was other rice. Compared to the previous year, this is a decrease of 625,865 *seok*, and the main destinations were various parts of Japan.

Raw Silk Production
The production of raw silk within Korea last year was 232,542 *kwan* of machine-made silk, 39,485 *kwan* of hand-reeled silk (*jaguri saengsa*, 座繰생사), totaling 271,027 *kwan*. The amount exported was 1,968,727 *keun* through customs, valued at 16,834,355 won, and 48,416 *keun* exported in small packages, valued at 424,692 won.

How Much Barley This Year?
The authorities expect about 10 million *seok* of barley this year. If the harvest is as good as expected, it will be a much larger increase than last year's actual harvest of 9.96 million *seok*. Eating barley rice tends to produce a lot of gas, so the countryside, where barley rice is eaten a lot, will likely have a stronger smell of gas than last year.

Tobacco Consumption
The Chŏnmae Bureau (專賣局, Monopoly Bureau)'s tobacco sales for March were 510,300 won at the Kyŏngsŏng headquarters of the original sales association, 200,200 won at the Pusan branch, 197,300 won at the Taegu general branch, 171,500 won at the Chŏnju general branch, 142,400 won at the P'yŏngyang general branch, 131,000 won at the Mokp'o branch, 121,600 won at the Sariwŏn branch, and 105,500 won at the Taejŏn branch. In addition, the total amount, including 17 branches with sales under 100,000 won, was 2,622,000 won. The cumulative total since April of last year <12>, i.e., this year's total sales, is 32,580,000 won. When this total sales amount is divided by the jurisdictional monopoly bureaus, the Kyŏngsŏng bureau accounts for 12,398,000 won, the Chŏnju bureau for 7,801,000 won, the Taegu bureau for 6,409,000 won, and the P'yŏngyang bureau for 5,972,000 won. This statistic shows which regions consume the most tobacco.

Total Number of Prisoners in All Korean Prisons
As of the end of February this year, the number of inmates in each prison and its branches was 16,013 Koreans, 507 Japanese, and 199 foreigners, for a total of 16,719 people. Classified by type of sentence, there were 14,027 convicts, 1,918 criminal defendants, and 594 people detained for labor or fines (노역윀¥ 유치인).

Comparing the above statistics of inmates with 7,021 people in 1910, the year of annexation (合병 當年), i.e. Meiji 43, there is an increase of 9,698 people.
As the prison population increases every year, it exceeds the capacity of 11,000 by 6,000. As a result, it is becoming cramped, with some cells having 6 people per p'yŏng (坪, approx. 3.3 square meters) (in Japan proper, it is 1.3 people per p'yŏng, and in Taiwan, it is 1.4 people per p'yŏng).
A solution to this is difficult due to prison expansion and budget issues. Therefore, starting in Showa 6 (1931), individual cells for detainees will be newly built within the Sŏdaemun Prison (서대문 형무소) to alleviate the situation somewhat, but this will hardly have any effect. Therefore, bail will be encouraged, with about 1,000 people expected to be released on bail in the sixth year (of Showa). <13>

朝鮮米外地輸出
작년중에 조선의 쌀이 외지로 수출된 것은 총계 491만 5,090석인데 그중 玄米는 258만 4,906석, 精米는 216만 9,684석, 正租는 1,259석, 碎米 9만 9,313석, 기타 米가 5만 9,928석이다. 이것을 그 前年 즉 재작년에 비하면 62만 5,865석이 감소되고 수출지는 대개가 일본 각지엿다.

生絲의 生産高
조선 내에서 작년중 생사의 생산고는 기계생사가 23만 2,542관, 座繰생사가 3만 9,485관, 計가 27만 1,027관인데 그중 移出高는 稅關通移出額이 196만 8,727근, 가격이 1,683만 4,355원, 小包移出이 4만 8,416근, 가격 42만 4,692원이다.

今年에 보리는 얼마?
당국에서 예상하는 금년의 보리는 약 1,000만 석인데 다른 해만 업고 예상대로 잘 수확이 된다면 작년 實收 996만 석보다는 훨씬 增收이다. 보리밥을 먹으면 방구가 잘 나오는 법이닛가 결국 보리밥을 만히 먹는 농촌에는 방구 냄새도 작년보다 훨신 더할듯.

煙草消費高
專賣局 3월중의 煙草賣上高는 元賣捌會 경성본점 51만 300원, 부산지점 20만 200원, 대구 총지점 19만 7,300원, 전주 총지점 17만 1,500원, 평양 총지점 14만 2,400원, 목포지점 13만 1,000원, 沙里院지점 12만 1,600원, 대전지점 10만 5,500원을 주로 하고 此外에 10만 원 이하의 각점 17개소를 포함한 합계액은 262만 2,000여 원으로 작년 4월 이후 누계＜12＞ 즉 금년도의 매상총액은 3,258만 원인데 此 총매상액은 관할 전매지국별로 구분하면 경성지국 1,239만 8,000원, 전주지국 780만 1,000원, 대구지국 640만 9,000원, 평양지국 597만 2,000여 원이다. 이 통계로 보면 어느 지방 사람들이 담배를 잘 먹는지 알 수 잇다.
