Fighting Friends
A and B were fighting friends who would fight whenever they met. One day, they ran into each other on the street after a long time.

A: "It's good to see you. I've been praising you a lot lately."

B: "Oh, really?"

A: "Yes, really!"

B: "What did you say about me?"

A: "First of all, I said that your monthly income is never less than 1,000 won!"

B: "What else?"

A: "Then I said that you have a lot of irregular income too."

B: "Who did you say that to?"

A: "To the tax office manager..."

B: "What... what?"

Liar
A man walking over a bridge saw a man fishing under the bridge and asked,

"Are you catching any fish?"

"Oh, yes. The other day, I caught forty carp here." <26>

"What! Who do you think you're talking to? I'm the local prosecutor, and this area is all my land."

"Oh, is that so? Then do you know who I am?"

"No."

"Well, I'm the most famous liar in this area."

To Use as a Candlestick
A wife who hated alcohol went for a walk in the countryside with her husband one Sunday. When she opened the picnic basket, she found a whiskey bottle. She became furious and rushed at her husband as if she would devour him.

"What is this?"

"Well, I was going to take the empty bottle home and use it as a candlestick stand... You don't understand me."

To Hear a Sermon
Patrolman Haengsun caught a drunkard passing by at 3 a.m.

"What are you doing out so late?"

"I'm going to... to..."

"To what?"

"I'm going to hear a ser-sermon."

"A sermon? Which pastor is giving a sermon at this time of night?"

"The sermon I'm going to hear isn't from a pastor, but from my wife." <27>

싸홈동무
A와 B는 맛나기만하면 싸호는 싸홈동모엿다. 그런데 둘이는 오래간만에 우연히 길에서 맛낫다.
A 『자네 참 잘 맛낫네. 나는 요새 자네를 굉장히 층찬하고 단엿네.』
B 『아 여보게. 그게 정말인가.』
A 『아- 정말이고말고-』
B 『그래 뭐라고 층찬을 햇단말인가.』
A 『우선 자네의 月수입은 천원 이하 될 때가 업다고 그랫지!』
B 『또 그 다음은!』
A 『그 다음은 여러 가지 不定수입도 굉장히 만타고 그랫네.』
B 『대체 그 말은 누구에게 햇단말인가.』
A 『저-저 稅務所 관리에게...』
B 『무엇이 엇재...』

『네 그럿습닛가? 그럿타면 당신은 또 나를 누군지 아십닛가?』
