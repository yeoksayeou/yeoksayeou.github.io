Call for Submissions

Local color! We entrust this to the pens of local readers. Submit freely as you wish. In the thirteen provinces (十三道) and countless towns and villages, there must be various unique customs, strange habits, and bizarre historical legends. People are good, mountain and river scenery is good, and local specialties are good. Write about them. Anyone can send in as much about their own region as they want. However, you must adhere to the following modest restrictions:

1. Do not write in a trite, descriptive manner, but write in a way that is interesting and enjoyable to read.

2. Write on manuscript paper with fifteen characters per line and around sixty lines.

3. Separate phrases clearly.

4. It is even better to include photographs.

The company reserves the right to edit and select submissions, and a small token of appreciation will be offered for published works. <129>
