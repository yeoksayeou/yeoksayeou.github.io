The Next Issue Will Be the 3rd Anniversary Edition

Our next issue will be a special 3rd anniversary commemorative edition. As this magazine has always been a pioneer in the field with its novel editing, readers can expect even greater improvements with this special edition. Not only the articles but also the cover, frontispiece, and even the appendix will be specially added to decorate the third anniversary issue in various ways. We will increase the number of copies printed in advance, so please subscribe early to avoid the disappointment of being unable to obtain a copy later.

Marriage Ceremony and Discussion Also in the Next Issue

The marriage ceremony, which was originally scheduled to be published in this issue, will also be included in the next commemorative issue due to the limited space in this issue, which is dedicated to Kyŏngsŏng (京城) and articles related to Kyŏngsŏng. We apologize for the delay, but we ask for your patience until the next issue. ＜171＞
