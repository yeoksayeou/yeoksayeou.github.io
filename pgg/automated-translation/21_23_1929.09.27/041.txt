If You Come to Kyŏngsŏng

Be sure not to forget, and be sure to find the Kaebyŏksa (開闢社).

If you come, not only will you see all of the company's business, but you will also be able to see the best building in Kyŏngsŏng (京城) other than Kaebyŏksa (開闢社), and the largest institutions in Kyŏngsŏng (京城).

If you have anything you want to know or any items you want to buy while you are in Kyŏngsŏng (京城), come to our company and discuss it so we can guide you to avoid being deceived. Be sure to find us. <5>

Named Entities

京城	Kyŏngsŏng
開闢社	Kaebyŏksa
