Magazine Title: 別乾坤 第六十一號
Publication Date: 1933년 03월 01일 (March 1, 1933)
Article Title: 新聞에도 發表안된 現代의 『루팡』 女怪盜, 白晝鍾路에서생긴 大膽한 犯行 (The Modern "Lupin" Female Phantom Thief, Not Published in Newspapers, A Daring Crime That Occurred in Jongno in Broad Daylight)
Article Type: 회고·수기 (Recollections and Memoirs)

This is a true story, a recent crime that actually happened at a certain Seoul gold and silver shop. It is so daring and skillful, even more so than the phantom thief Lupin in novels, that one can only be amazed. Moreover, the fact that the perpetrator is a woman makes it a crime story with 100% interest. -筆者- (Author)

It happened in broad daylight—moreover, at Jongno intersection, right next to the police station—at a certain gold and silver shop said to be the largest in Seoul.

The crime was so daring and skillful, and the amount of damage was as much as 1,500 won, that the victimized store vehemently denied the facts, making it a secret story that was not even published in the newspapers.

January 20-something, at 1 PM!
Unlike other stores, this particular time was the quietest. A middle-aged woman, who looked well over thirty and close to forty, arrived at the XX gold and silver shop in a taxi. Judging by her attire, she looked like a wealthy man's mistress, but she was a very dignified woman.
As soon as she entered, she bargained for only metal ornaments—brooches, rings, finger rings, bracelets, and earrings—worth a total of 1,500 won.
The owner, satisfied with the huge deal of 1,500 won in these recessionary times, was very flustered.
The woman in question, after picking what she wanted and rejecting what she didn't, arranging all the items she wanted to buy to one side, opened her mouth with a composure that showed no awkwardness.
"Well, the price for all of this is 1,500 won, right?"
"Yes, yes, that's right."
"Since it's all metal, there's no need to weigh it. Actually, I came from XX-dong 00 Pharmacy (XX동 00약방), and the owner is having his 60th birthday soon, so I'm buying these to give as gifts to the family members. It would be good to bring the money, but the owner says that unlike men, it's dangerous for women to carry such large sums of money, so he told me to just bargain and have the store staff bring it over, and he'll pay right away. Is that possible?"
The owner, who was already half out of his mind, asked this, and especially since it was such a famous house that everyone knew about the ****** tendencies, he readily agreed without any ****.
"Yes, that's fine. I'm especially grateful that you came to this store and made such a big deal. Such trouble is nothing. I'll have someone from the store go with you now, so take them and send them back. Then…"
"Yes, then please do that."
"I'm sorry, but please call a taxi for me."
"Yes, yes, I'll call one for you."
The owner readily agreed, seeing the woman's calm and composed attitude, and called a taxi and sent a clerk with the items.

In front of the XX-dong OO Pharmacy (XX동 OO약방)!
The woman who got out of the car paid the taxi fare with a leisurely attitude. She approached the pharmacy entrance and told the clerk who brought the goods,
"Here, give it to me and wait there for a moment. I'll go inside and show the items to the people who will receive them, and if they all like them, I'll pay the price without any problems."
And she extended her hand. The young clerk also thought that since he had come all the way to the front of the house, there wouldn't be any problems, so he handed the bundle of gold to the woman.
The woman, taking this, went inside. Before going to the store, she visited the owner, a herbal medicine doctor, of the pharmacy with whom she had made an appointment in advance.
"I'm sorry I'm a little late for the appointment. As I said earlier, I forced my sick child to come with me, and he's outside the door now. But as I said before, he just demands money from anyone he sees. If you don't, he'll demand the metal back. Don't pay any attention to it and just give him everything and tell him to come in and give him a thorough examination... And if I'm here, I don't know what kind of trouble that child will cause again, so I'll go into hiding for a while and come back in an hour or two, so please examine him in the meantime and prepare some medicine. No matter how much it costs, as long as that child gets better, I won't spare anything."
Originally, she had visited and spoken with the pharmacy owner before going to the store, and since the woman looked like a very dignified noblewoman, he waited without any doubt. And since she said she brought her son who was mentally ill, he readily agreed without any further thought and opened the back door of his house to let the woman escape for a while.
A little later!
The owner sent someone out to call the clerk who was waiting outside.
"I'm sorry to have kept you waiting so long."
"Oh, you're very welcome."
The owner saw that he was a fresh-faced young man who was barely twenty years old, and thought to himself,
"He's handsome and looks pitiful because of his age."
And compassion welled up.
"Come in this room for a moment."
"No, there's no need to go in. Just give me the money and I'll leave."
The owner thought he must have heard the woman's story.
"Hmm, so he's definitely crazy about money!"
"Well, I'll give you the money, so come in here for a moment."
The clerk thought that since it was a considerable amount of money, he must be calling him into the room to calculate it properly, so he couldn't refuse and went in without hesitation.
The owner, after letting the clerk in, gave him a seat and asked,
"You want money, so how much should I give you?"
"How much? Didn't we agree on everything beforehand?"
"Oh, we agreed... Yes, we must have agreed!"
The owner mumbled, trying to soothe the patient.
"So, what are you going to do with that money?"
"What do you mean? I'm going to give it to the store, what else?"
"The store! The store! ... If I don't give you the money..."
"What? You're not giving it to me? Are you trying to deceive me? Why did you take someone else's metal and not pay? Then I'll take the metal back."
"So, as that woman said, he's definitely crazy!"
The owner clicked his tongue and said,
"Well, let's stop joking around... When did it start?"
And he grabbed his wrist.
"Is this man crazy? What do you mean when did it start... Give me the money quickly. I'm a busy man."
"Well, I'll give you the money. But you have to answer my questions well... If you don't, I won't give it to you..."
"What? I came to get the price of the goods, not to the police station. Why are you asking me questions? What am I supposed to answer?"
"You speak well... What a pity!"
The owner checked the clerk's pulse.
"What are you doing? Am I here to see a doctor?... If you're not going to give me the money, give me back the metal."
The clerk was panicked, increasingly flustered by the pharmacy owner's suspicious behavior. The owner, however, interpreted it as purely pathological.
"Well, I'll give you everything you ask for, so just be patient for a moment."
"...By the way, where did that woman go?"
"You mean your mother... She'll be back later, so don't worry... When did the illness start?"
"What? Illness? Is this man trying to frame me?... What kind of illness are you talking about? Why are you talking about illness to someone who came to sell goods and get paid?"
The clerk's face turned red and blue as he made a fuss, but the owner, who still thought he was a mentally ill patient, decided that this wouldn't work and signaled to a few people nearby to forcibly restrain his entire body and check his pulse.
The clerk then realized that some trickery was involved and foamed at the mouth, making all sorts of struggles, but they continued to see it as the behavior of a crazy person.
However, since he was a healthy person without any illness, there was no way there would be any significant change in his pulse! However.
Due to shock, disappointment, and excitement, the owner interpreted the rapidly beating pulse as purely pathological.
After a while, when the owner was definitely considering it as an illness and even thinking about prescribing medicine, what came out of the patient's body was the store's receipt.
The owner's surprise when he saw this!
I don't have the ability to describe it here as it is, but anyway, the owner's surprise was not small.
Having only listened to the words of an unknown woman and realizing that what he had been doing was actually crazy, the owner, faced with the problem of the reputation of a large pharmacy, and speechless in front of the clerk, didn't know what to do.
In this way, the identity of the matter was revealed, but the gold and silver shop's 1,500 won and the OO Pharmacy (OO약방) owner's crazy act! This will forever—until the woman is caught—remain an unsolved mystery. <21>
