Magazine Title	別건곤 제36호
Publication Date	1931년 01월 01일
Article Title	朱題 (Red Title)
Author	하윤
Article Type	시 (Poem)

In the darkness-engulfed vacant house,
A star twinkles faintly.
You, who have bowed your head all day,
Lift your feet and leave somewhere. <151>
