Consultation Column on the Magazine
We have newly established a consultation column on the magazine. Please make much use of it according to the following regulations.

1, Consultation matters - "Things that are difficult to solve alone in our daily life," excluding special problems such as law and medical care.

2, Paper - Official postcard

3, Others - Inquiries should be simple and clear, and names may be anonymous. <146>
