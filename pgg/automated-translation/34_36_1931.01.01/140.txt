One Question, One Answer Record 7

Yu Il-jun

Reporter: What is the biggest issue in the medical field recently?

Yu: A French medical scholar named Calmette has researched and announced a novel tuberculosis prevention method. After animal testing, and even human trials, it has shown considerably positive results, causing quite a sensation in the medical community. The method involves feeding people tuberculosis bacteria. It seems this method may also be used for typhoid fever and dysentery, replacing vaccinations with oral ingestion of the bacteria. <56>
