기사형태	잡저 (Miscellaneous Essay)

It's not something to be proud of, but Chosŏn is a land full of bedbugs. And there's no pest quite as intractable as the bedbug. After a long day, how great is the loss of energy and ability of those who cannot get a good night's sleep due to bedbugs, especially with the already short nights? And if you add up the losses of many people, how much greater would that be? It is a shameful thing that for 10 years, even 100 years, we have suffered these painful attacks without any effective medicine or extermination method.

We ask that any of our readers who have successfully exterminated bedbugs write down their method and send it to us. We will then widely publicize the method.

1. If it is a medicine, please write down the ingredients, amounts, and timing in detail so that anyone can read it and follow it (we must also consider those without money).

Prize Money: Special Selection: Cash 50 Yen. Ordinary Selection: 5 Yen Each.

Submission deadline: End of July (winning entries will be announced in the August issue) <51>
