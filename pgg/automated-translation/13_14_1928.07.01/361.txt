To Our Readers,

The terrible heat, where sweat pours even when sitting still, is approaching. What will you do to ward off the heat? Without a doubt, order this August's issue of 本誌『大秘密公開號』 (This Magazine: "Grand Secret Unveiling Issue") in advance. Anticipate how refreshing it will be... <135>

Named Entities

八月	P'arwŏl (August)
