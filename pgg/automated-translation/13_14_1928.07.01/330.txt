懸賞募集 理髮所投票 (Prize Contest: Barbershop Vote, Miscellaneous)

Prize Contest: Barbershop Vote
Is there any man in the city these days who doesn't go to the barbershop? The story of the barbershop is something you can never just pass by as if it's someone else's story.

Which barbershop is the best in Kyŏngsŏng? Which place is the best in Taegu, P'yŏngyang, Wŏnsan, Kaesŏng, Inch'ŏn? Please vote.

By doing so, let's get to know the best, second, and third places in each location, Kyŏngsŏng, P'yŏngyang, and Taegu separately. Doing this is not only about making well-known places even more widely known, but also about making them even better in the future, so please don't take it lightly and everyone cast your vote.

Voting Regulations

One person can only vote for one place, and you must cut out the ballot located at the corner of the last page of this book and write on it.
Voting is from July 7th to August 20th (The number of votes until July 21st will be announced in the August issue first, and the votes until August 20th will be added and the results will be announced in the September issue).
Limited to the above 6 regions: Kyŏngsŏng, P'yŏngyang, Kaesŏng, Inch'ŏn, Wŏnsan. Please only write and send one barbershop in your place of residence.

Prize Regulations

The 1st to 7th place will be determined for each of the 6 cities (total of 42 stores in 6 cities), and the winning barbershops will be introduced in the magazine with articles and photos.
For those who voted, the votes from each city will be gathered separately and lottery will be drawn to select 1 person for 1st place (6 people in total for 6 cities), 1 person for 2nd place (6 people in total), and 10 people for 3rd place (60 people in total) as winners.
1st place will receive a high-quality razor (plus 2 kinds of additional prizes)
2nd place will receive high-quality hair oil and dandruff medicine (plus additional prizes)
3rd place will receive a special gift from the company. <124>

Named Entities

京城	Kyŏngsŏng
大邱	Taegu
平壤	P'yŏngyang
元山	Wŏnsan
開城	Kaesŏng
仁川	Inch'ŏn

懸賞募集 理髮所投票
지금 도시 남자치고 이발소에 안다니는 이가 잇겟슴닛가 이발소 이약이는 결코 결코 남의 이약이 하듯 지내처 버리지 못할 것임니다.
京城 안에서 어느 이발소가 제일나은가. 大邱, 平壤, 元山, 開城, 仁川, 서는 어는 곳이 제일 나흔가?......투표하여 주십시오.
그리하야 京城 따로 平壤 따로 大邱 따로 그곳 그곳에서의 일등 이등 삼등을 알고 지내게 하십시다. 이리하는 일은 현재 잘 아는 집을 널니 알게 되는데만 긋치는 것이 안이라, 압흐로 더욱더욱 조와지게 하는 일이니 결코 등한히 녁이지 말고 다 가티 1票씩을 던저 주시기 바랍니다.

投票規定

한 사람이 한 곳만 쓰시되 반듯이 이 책 맨 끗장 구텽이에 잇는 투표용지를 가위로 오러서 거긔에 쓰실 일.
투표는 7월 7일로부터 8월 20일까지(7월21일까지의 투표 수를 우선 8월호에 발표하고 그 후 8월 20일까지의 것을 가산하야 당선 결과를 9월호에 발표함)
京城, 平壤, 開城, 仁川, 元山
상기 6地方에 限하되 각각 자기 거주지방 이발 한 곳만 적어 보낼 것.

賞品規定

상기 6都市 각 지방마다 1등으로부터 7등까지 定하고(6都市 都合 42店) 당선된 이발소를 기사와 사진으로 誌上에 소개함.
투표하신 이에게는 각 都 투표 따로따로 모아서 제비를 뽑아 1등 1인씩(6都 合6인) 2등 1인씩(合6인) 3등 10인씩(合60인)을 정하야 당선자로 하고
1등에는 上等면도 1個進呈(외 副賞品 2種 添呈)
2등에는 上等香油 비듬약 進呈(외 副賞品 添呈)
3등에는 본사 특별상품 進呈<124>
