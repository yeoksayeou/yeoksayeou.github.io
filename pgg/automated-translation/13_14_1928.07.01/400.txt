잡저 (Miscellaneous Essay)

Kaebyŏksa Mark Design Contest Extension

Kaebyŏksa, which was founded in the summer of Kyŏngsin year and has been striving for eight years by publishing *Kaebyŏk*, *Puin*, *Sin Yŏsŏng*, *Ŏrini*, *Pyŏlgŏn’gon*, various magazines, occasional books, lectures, and other activities, and which will continue to fight even harder in the future, is holding a public contest for its "mark" throughout the country and will select the best design. We hope that you will send us many good designs with your special ideas, and we will present a specially made gold watch and a two-year subscription to this magazine as a souvenir to the winning design.

The original deadline was June 20th, but the company has extended it to the end of July in order to receive more of your good ideas. The announcement on the back of the table of contents is to provide you with a reference by selecting the relatively excellent entries from those submitted so far, and even those who have already applied may apply again if they have better ideas.

◇Reference Items
O There are no restrictions on the content to be displayed in the mark, so it is completely free.
O The shape can be a circle, octagon, or hexagon, and the size can be adjusted later, so it can be drawn large. The color can be a single color, or if necessary, two colors (if it is a single color, it must be in ink).
O One person can submit several types (but draw one type on each sheet and write your address and name on each sheet).
O Deadline extended to July 30th. <153>

開闢社 마-크 圖案募集延期
庚申年 녀름에 창설되야 開闢, 婦人, 新女性, 어린이, 別乾坤, 諸 잡지와 또 수시 필요한 도서 출판과 강연 기타로 8년간 고전하여온 또 압흐로 더욱 健鬪하여 나아갈 開闢社의 『마-크』를 특히 天下에 공모하야 그 중 우수한 도안을 採定하기로 하엿슴니다. 특별하신 생각으로 조흔 도안을 만히 보내 주시기 바라오며 採定되는 도안에는 기념품으로 特製 금시계 하나와 本誌代 2년分을 贈呈하겟슴니다.

처음 기한은 6월 20일 까지로 하엿스나 본사에서는 좀더 여러분의 조흔 考案을 다시 엇기 위하야 7월말일까지 연기하엿슴니다. 목차 후면에 발표한 것은 그 동안 응모한 중에서 비교뎍 우수한 것을 뽑아 여러분의 參考에 供하는 것이오니 이미 응모하신 이도 보다 더 조흔 考案이 잇스면 다시 응모하서도 좃슴니다.
