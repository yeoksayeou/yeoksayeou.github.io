**Summary**

This short announcement informs readers about the reopening of the Kangnŭng branch office of the Kaebyŏk-sa publishing company. It lists the location of the branch and the names and roles of its key staff members, including the branch manager, a general affairs manager/reporter, and three other reporters. For historians, this piece indicates the operational reach and personnel structure of a significant Korean publishing house during the colonial period. It shows Kaebyŏk-sa's efforts to maintain a regional presence, specifically in Kangnŭng, and identifies individuals involved in its local operations in 1926.
