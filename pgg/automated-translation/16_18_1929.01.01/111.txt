別건곤 제18호
1929년 01월 01일
秘密命令歡迎 (Secret Command Welcome)
소식 (News)

We welcome secret commands.

We hope that all readers of this magazine will become editors and questioners. The secret investigation team reporters of this magazine are always ready to be dispatched and are awaiting the orders of our readers. Whether it is to investigate the mischief of a 독갑 (tokkap, some sort of goblin or mischievous spirit) in a certain place or house, or to investigate the inside of a strange magic cave (魔窟) in a certain place or house, or if there is a strange person or a suspicious incident in a certain place, please earnestly notify the "Pyŏlgŏn'gon Investigation Department" (別乾坤調査部) and we will immediately dispatch a secret investigation and report it in the magazine.

Even if secrecy is maintained on the magazine pages, please clearly state the location address, and if it is a person, their name and age at our headquarters. Also, please let us know the address of the person submitting the letter so that we can contact them during the investigation. <68>

**Named Entities**

別乾坤調査部	Pyŏlgŏn'gon Chosabu

秘密命令歡迎
本誌를 닑으시는 讀者가 모다 編輯願問이되여주십시오 本誌의 秘密調査班記者가 恒常出動準備를하고 讀者諸氏의 命令을 기다리고잇슴니다 아모곳 아모집에 독갑이 작란이잇스니 調査하라던지 아모곳 아모집에 怪異한 魔窟이잇스니 그 內面을 調査하라던지 어느곳 어느곳에 怪常한 人物이잇거나 疑心나는 事件이잇스나 調査하라고 딘즛이 『別乾坤調査部』로 通知해주시면 卽刻出動 秘密調査하야 誌面으로 報告하겟슴니다.
誌上에는 秘密을 직히드라도 本社아지는 그 場所番地 人物이면 姓名年齡을 明記하시고 投書하시는이의 住所도 알려주서야 調査上連絡할수잇겟슴니다.＜68＞
