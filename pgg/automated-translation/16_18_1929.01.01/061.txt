Announcement of Rice Sack Prize Winner
The photograph age-guessing contest in the December issue of this magazine was truly popular, with people whispering about it everywhere. We received a total of 3,293 responses, and while everyone got the photos mixed up correctly, only forty people guessed the ages correctly. Most people guessed the woman's age too high. From these forty people, we drew a lottery (we asked a young boy from the printing house to draw the lottery), and we will send a sack of white rice to Mr. Han below. We will also send small prizes to the following thirty-nine people.
Han Ŭng-yu, 27 Yejidong, Kyŏngsŏng-bu

Correct Answers
1. The photos of both the man and woman were reversed, which made it difficult.
2. Man: 24 years old, Woman: 18 years old

白米한섬 懸賞當選發表
本誌十二月호에 낫든 懸賞問題 寫眞나희마치기는 참으로 人氣가 大端하야 가는곳마다 그이약이로수근거리고 모다드러온 대답이 三千二百九十三枚엿는데 틀닌 것은 다마치엿스나 나희를 마친이가 겨우 四十人밧게 되지안엇슴니다. 大槪는 女子의 나희를 더만히처서틀엿슴니다. 마침 이 四十名에서 제비아서(제비는 印刷所에서 第一적은 少年한분을 請하야 게하엿슴니다)左記韓氏에게 白米한섬을 보내드리게되엿슴니다. 그리고 下記三十九人도 어엽분 賞品을 보냄니다.
京城府禮智洞二十七番地 韓應裕氏

正答
一, 男女가 모다 寫眞이뒤집히여 외로된 것이 틀닌 것
二, 南二十四歲 女十八歲
