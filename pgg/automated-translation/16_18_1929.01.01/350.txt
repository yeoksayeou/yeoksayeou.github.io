별건곤 제18호
1929년 01월 01일
紙上封切 벙어리 三龍이(2), 羅雲奎 主演 (Paper Release: Mute Samyong (2), Starring Na Un-gyu)
문예기타 (Literary Miscellaneous)

"How could she be so pretty!"

"Looking at the groom, it's a waste!"

"The bride is two years older!"

"No matter how pretty she is, it's useless. Being from a widow's household, they say the groom's family already gave 50,000 yang!"

With such gossip from the villagers, the marriage ceremony of Kwitong ended. The mischievous gang of the village, who had gained something rare, dragged Kwitong out and took him to a bar.

The drunken gang ridiculed and teased him, saying that the bride was prettier and smarter than he was. Immature Kwitong developed resentment and hatred towards his bride. Therefore, he disliked being in the same room and tormented his wife with fault-finding and spite. The gentle wife would secretly shed tears, but never offered a word of resistance. Kwitong, who had grown up without discipline and was immature, ended up chasing away the harmony and laughter of the family by hating his wife. <131>

『아모리 입분들 소용이 잇나. 과부집 살림이라 벌서 신랑집에서 五萬兩이나 주엇다는 데!』
이런흉 저런흉와 말 만흔 동리사람 입술끗헤서 貴童이의 婚姻式은 끗이 낫다. 다시 업슬 그희를 어든 동리의 작란 패들은 貴童이를 잡아내여 술집으로 끄을고 갓다.
自己보다도 신부가 잘 생기고 똑똑하다고 비웃고 조롱하는 술취한 작란패의 말에 철업는 貴童이는 신부에게 대한 분한 마음과 憎惡가 생겻다. 그래서 한방에 잇기를 실혀하고 트집과 심술로 안해를 구박하얏스나 온순한 안해는 남모르게 홀로 눈물은 흘릴지언정 일언의 반항이 업섯다. 버릇업시 자라낫고 철업는 貴童이가 안해를 미워하는 것이 결국은 집안의 和氣와 우슴을 쫏차바렷다.<131>
