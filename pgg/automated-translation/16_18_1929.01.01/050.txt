기사제목	各界各級 白紙 한 겹 關係者間의 新年 訴願 (New Year's Wishes from Various Classes on a Blank Sheet of Paper)
기사형태	문예기타 (Literature and Other)

New Year's Wishes from Various Classes on a Blank Sheet of Paper

Between Old Women and New Women

Old Woman Mrs. Lee

What's the use of looking at other people's daughters; let's look at my own! It's truly heartbreaking. She goes out every day to watch moving pictures or something, and claims to be catching mice (a believer in something). Even when I tell her to get married, she doesn't listen, and I don't know what she does catching those mice day and night! When she comes home, would she ever dip a finger in the sink, or wipe the floor once! How can I keep up with the daughters of rich families when she wants me to buy her shoes and a '텀록도리' (teomnokdori - a type of hat) even though we don't have money? Still, she gets her hands on whatever she wants somehow, and goes around with it, it's truly amazing, a trick. She eats 연앤지비터 (yeon-ae-ji-biteo - a type of candy/confectionary, thought to corrupt people); it's going to ruin her. Can't the police get rid of that 연애 (yeon-ae - romance/dating)? Men come to visit her day and night, and she doesn't even feel ashamed! Having one daughter is a worry. Is it just my daughter who's like this? It seems all so-called New Women are like that? Women should just take care of their husbands and be good at managing the household to be womanly! But now, I can't tell if they are women or men. Why do some women cut their hair short like they are becoming nuns? How can they buy only thread to sew while they cannot even mend one sock hole, and they only talk about Western food while they don't even know how to cook rice properly, and they only talk about hygiene while they don't even wash one piece of clothing? It's truly absurd. I hope they become useful in at least one thing in the New Year.

New Woman Kim In-suk

You aristocrats who only think of your times when you rode 교군 (gyogun - a type of palanquin) and wore 장옷 (jangot - a type of coat worn by women) and only find fault with others, do you even know that time is passing? But that's not your fault… Instead of being confined day and night, wake up and go to night school to get an idea of where the world is going and how! Stop being dolls and open your eyes! Cooking and sewing are not the only things women should do.

Between Pastors and Believers

Pastor C

I hope that our church members will be more sincere in general. Since the Christian church is not a gathering place or a place for leisure activities, what's the use of just blindly coming and going and staring at other people's faces? It's concerning that some young men only look at the faces of young women, and some young women only look at the noses of young men, and think of love as the path to heaven. And the ideological inclination of the general believers seems to be outdated, so how can you go to heaven crawling? I hope you will believe more sincerely and practically, and work in accordance with the times.

Believer Choe Yo-han

We foolish people believe and imitate the words and actions of the pastors more than believing in the Lord. Pastors, don't you have the important and sacred mission of leading our humanity, which is trapped in sin and doesn't know how to live, to heaven? You are sacred messengers who make us abandon our carnal desires and purify our souls, so please be more sacred. I don't mean that all of you are like this, but it seems that some of you treat church work as a business. If you are going to do it as a job, wouldn't selling bean sprouts be more profitable? Pastors, please free yourselves from materialism first, sacrifice everything, and become holy to save us sinners. And don't worry about young men and women looking at each other, and don't look at the noses of Westerners too much. If you only like things that are '洋' (yang - Western), you won't even be able to eat Western rice. Amen.

Between the Rich and the Poor

Mun, Who Lives Inside the Gate

The Korean rich are the ones who can't live even with their own things! Why do you look at the rich like 蛇蝎 (sasaek - snakes and scorpions)? Where did you steal it from? Of course, I will pay if you ask me, but some shameless people who seem to be healthy and strong come and say that they are poor and have nothing to eat, so give them some money. Why do they shamelessly ask for other people's things when their own hands and feet are fine? From the New Year, I hope that there will be no such lazy people and that there will be more people who earn their own living.

Poor Man Paeng Sam-son

I am not poor because I am lazy. I am about to starve to death, so how can I even think about being lazy? I work hard day and night, sweating blood, but I can't make a living, so maybe it's the sin of being poor. It's sad enough to be born poor, but it's even sadder to be treated with contempt. Should the poor die? I ask the rich. Let's all live together. I'm not asking for anything for free. I'm repeating myself, but I hope you understand what the justice of this world is.

Between Automobile Drivers and Passengers

Cho O Automobile Kwon O-taek

Our only goal is to make money! But at least 80% of the passengers ride for pleasure, so I don't think that everyone thinks of it as a Korean. I hope more people will ride it for practical purposes, then it will be good for making money and for my heart. And there are a lot of complaints about the fare being too expensive or too cheap. Japanese drivers get paid without saying anything!.

Regular Passenger Yi Seong-ok

I pay good money to ride the car to get there faster, but when I call a car in a hurry, it's so slow that I waste time waiting and ruin my business. Who would ride a car then? I hope the drivers will be more friendly and have a sense of time.

Between Bus Girls and Drivers

Bus Conductor Kim Ki-ho

Complaints? Hopes? How can I say it all? Not all drivers are like this, but I don't know why they are so mean. If they don't like something, they get upset. Even though the bus ahead of them has just left, they follow closely behind, so there are no passengers on the bus behind them. Since the conductor has a lot of commission from selling tickets! Also, some passengers speak harshly to the conductor, even cursing at them, but I just feel sad that I don't have money. But somehow, even though we suffer together on the same bus all day, in the heat or cold, it seems like we enjoy it. What kind of affection is that? Even if we only ride together for an hour, we might hate the person if we don't like them, but is there any reason to deliberately be mean and cause passengers to fall when starting or stopping? Why else would I be doing this job, hearing all kinds of strange things like "bus girl! bus girl!"? Even if the passengers are like that, shouldn't the drivers and conductors sympathize with each other? Especially since I'm a weak woman, shouldn't you help me more? I hope these points will be corrected in the New Year.

Bus Driver Yi Ki-seong

I don't have any special hopes. Since conductors and drivers just want to earn money to eat, we have to earn even one more penny. The scheduled time is from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, but if we go out for 30 minutes more than the scheduled time, we get paid for one hour. We get monthly payments for gasoline, mileage, time, and passengers, so if we want to go out for 30 minutes more than the scheduled time, the conductors don't listen to us! We get paid too, and they get paid too, so why do they do that when we want to earn even one more 분 (bun - a monetary unit)? I want to say that it's not right, and I want to scold them. This is another story, but I hope our bus drivers will unite….

Between Telephone Operators and Installers

Kwanghwamun Bureau Kim O-suk

How can I express my complaints? Also, if I say something, the adults who see and hear it will scold me! But I will only say one thing. Do you think that operators just sit and say "Number, hello!" all day? You may think it's easy and simple, but it's actually very busy. If we are a little slow, they curse at us, saying "You b*tch! Are you sleeping?" And sometimes they just pick up the phone for no reason and ask us to talk to them, but we can't say anything, and sometimes we want to cry. The adults who know the situation of the weak operators understand us, but I wish the adults who don't know the situation and only scold us would actually come and see it! And I hope you will read the precautions on the first page of the phone book carefully.

Telephone Installer Yi Chang-yeol

We install phones so that they can be used for urgent matters when they are needed, but these days, why are the operators so unkind and don't connect the phone quickly when we call? Sometimes they just say "하나시주 (hanashiju - a Japanese phrase, likely meaning "please speak")," so how can we make a phone call? Hey, telephone operator ladies! From the New Year, please answer quickly and don't connect us to the wrong place and make us look foolish. And when you say something, please say it energetically. Don't speak in a dying voice like someone who hasn't eaten for days! If your voice is not energetic when we are angry, we will get even angrier, right? Please pay attention to these points.

Between Teachers and Students at Girls' Schools

OO Girls' High School Teacher Kim Kong-gyeong

As I always say in the classroom, female students should never forget that they are different from the old women at home and of course different from the ordinary women on the street. Recently, students seem to have forgotten that their status is that of a student and are ruining themselves by being overly free and frivolous. The reason why Korean families think it is dangerous to send their daughters to school and lock them up in their rooms is because of the influence of such a small number of mistakes on the entire female student community. This is what the school is always wary of, but the students themselves should be careful first. What I especially want from students in the New Year is to not forget their status as students above all else. <27>

OO Girls' High School Kim O-ja

Male teachers should show more respect to female students than female teachers. They should be strict and fair. Of course, it's okay to treat students with good grades with favor, but it seems that there is a severe problem in girls' schools. Especially young teachers seem to be dangerous somehow. I hope they won't be too biased towards only one person in the playground or in the classroom. It's really boring to touch and tease students, pretending to be joking. I wonder if you should pay special attention to these things.

Between the Dormitory Supervisor and the Boarders at Girls' Dormitories

OO Girls' High School Dormitory Supervisor S

1. If you have any complaints about the supervisor, please don't just whisper among yourselves, but come and tell me directly.
2. Please follow the dormitory rules well.
3. Please study hard from normal times, and don't keep the lights on until late at night only during exams.
4. Please live harmoniously in a home-like atmosphere.
5. Please keep the room clean at all times so that you won't be ashamed when unexpected guests come.
6. Please don't forget that it's not individual life, but communal life.

OO Girls' High School Boarder Kim O-ja

1. I think it would be good to write down how the students' meal expenses were used and show them to us, so that we can learn how to manage the household and know the details of the calculations.
2. I don't know if the phone call is urgent or not without hearing the contents, but the phone calls that come to the students are often put down and cut off, and we can't even answer them, which is frustrating, so please connect us well!
3. Please let the students buy everything that is used in the dormitory so that we can learn how to bargain.
4. Please show us a useful movie once or twice a year.
5. Please let us participate in useful meetings such as lectures and debates.
6. Please show us the night scenery of Seoul at least four times a year, at the time of each festival. We don't know the world too well, so it's like we're in a monastery.
7. Please don't make it so that we have to lie to avoid being punished if we deviate from the rules a little bit due to unavoidable circumstances. <28>

Between Publishers and Readers

Local Reader Yun U-yeong

When I send money to a company or store to send me books, why don't they send them quickly? They don't even notify me of the situation by letter, and they don't even reply when I ask, so I can't expect communication between publishers and readers, and it seems that they are ignoring local readers too much. I can't just run up to Seoul, and I'm so frustrated and irritated. How can a local guy buy books then? I hope you will think about local readers and handle things more kindly and quickly.

Gyeongseong (Seoul) OO Company Kim O-seong

1. Firstly, some people send money orders (小爲替, sowitae) when ordering books, and it would be safer to attach it to a document or send it by mail, but some people put the money order certificate directly in the letter, which is very annoying.
If you send it by mail with the money order attached to a document, the postage will be expensive, and if you send the money order certificate in an ordinary letter, it is easy to lose. These readers don't know their own mistakes and only complain that they haven't received the books, so how can we send the books if we haven't received the payment? We wish they would ask us about this by letter, but some people don't ask and submit a petition to the police or something, saying that we haven't sent the books. That's useless and only creates resentment. Anyway, if you want to send the payment safely and have a good relationship, it is cheaper, clearer, and very convenient to use transfer remittance (振替, furikae).
2. Secondly, if a magazine or something is not published on the publication date, the company or bookstore will notify you, but the subscribers should also ask the place where they ordered the magazine as much as possible by sending a return postcard or attaching a return postage. They will answer you kindly. <29>

舊女子와 新女子間

舊女子 李氏夫人

남의 집 딸의 숭 볼 것잇나요 내 딸 숭이나 보지요! 참 속이 푹푹 상함니다. 활동사진인지 팔동사진인지 구경은 무슨 구경을 그리 하너라고 날마다 돌아단이며 무슨 쥐라나줘잡네(主義者임네) 하고 시집을 가래도 안가고 밤낫 그까지 쥐는 잡아 무엇하는지! 집에 오니 개수통에 손꾸락 하나 담글까요 걸레질 한번을 칠까요! 업는 돈에 구두를 사내라 텀록도리를 사내라고 부자집 딸과 가티 하랴니 엇더케 뒤를 댐니ㅅ가? 그래도 어서 생기는지 가지고 십다는 것은 다 가지고 단이니 참 조화ㅅ 속임듸다. 연앤지비터 먹는 것인지ㅣ 사람버리겟습듸다. 경찰서에서는 그 놈의 연애 좀 업새지 못하는지! 밤으로 낫으로 사네들이 차자오니 귀치도 안코! 딸 하나 잇는 것이 걱정꺼리임니다. 이것이 엇지 내 집 딸만 그럿켓슴니가 소위 新女子란 다 그런 모양지요? 그저 녀편네는 남편 뒤나 잘 보아주고 살임이나 잘하야 녀편네다워야 쓰지요! 온 이건 녀편넨지 사낸지 알 수가 업서요 그중에 엇던 여자는 女僧이 되랴는지 단발은 왠 단발일가요 버선구멍 한아 못기우면서 짜겟인가 맨긴다고 실만 사뒤리고 밥은 질줄 모르면서 양료리 이약이가 일수요 옷 한아 빨지 안으면서 위생소리만 하니 참 기가 맥히지 안슴닛가 새해에는 한가지라도 실지 잇게 하얏스면 조흘 것 갓슴니다

신여자 金仁淑

교군 타고 장옷 쓰고 단이든 자기네들 시절만 녁이고 남의 흉은 잘 보나 세월이 가는지 오는지 몰으는 양반들아! 그러나 그것이 당신에의 죄는 안이지만…밤낫 갓처만 잇지 말고 정신을 차려 야학이라도 단여서 세상이 어듸로 엇더케 돌아가는 것이나 짐작하고 게시요! 인형 노릇만 하지말고 좀 눈을 번쩍 뜨기 바라오! 밥 짓고 옷 뀌여 매는 것만이 여자의 일이 안이외다.

牧師와 信徒間

목사 C生

우리 교인은 일반으로 좀더 진실햇스면 조흘가 함니다. 예수교회가 <24> 결코 무슨 대합소나 소일꺼리의 집합소가 안인 이상에는 그냥 맹목적으로 왓다갓다하며 남의 얼골이나 치여다 보면 엇지 하겟슴닛가 그중에 젊은 남자는 젊은 여자의 얼골만 보고 젊은 여자는 젊은 남자의 콧등만 보아 연애의 길을 천당의 길로만 아는 것 가트니 그 안이 한심치 안슴닛가. 그리고 일반 신도의 사상 경향을 보면 대개는 시대에 떠러진 것 가트니 천당인들 가재거름으로야 엇지 갈 수가 잇슴니가 좀더 진실하고 실지가 잇게 믿고 시대에 맛치워서 일을 하얏스면 조흘 것 갓슴니다.

신도 崔約翰

우매한 우리는 主를 밋는다는 것보다 목사님네의 말슴과 행실을 밋고 배호고 함니다. 목사님들은 죄악에 빠져서 살 길을 모르는 우리 인류를 닛끌어 천국으로 인도하시는 중대하고도 거룩한 사명을 가즈시지 안으섯슴니ㅅ가. 여러분은 육신의 慾을 바리게 하고 영혼을 깨끗게 하야 주시는 거룩한 使者이시니만콤 좀더 신성해 주십시요 그야 다 그러시다는 것은 안이나 중에는 교회의 일을 한 영업적으로 알으시는 이가 게신 것 갓슴니다. 직업적으로 하시랴면 찰아리 콩나몰 장사가 利가 남지 안을가요. 목사님 자신이 먼저 물욕에서 버서나 모든 것을 희생하시고 신성하서서 죄만은 우리 무리를 구원해 주십시요 그리고 젊은 남녀가 서로 치여다 보는 것을 걱정 마르시고 당신네는 서양 사람의 코등을 너무 치여다 보지 마십시요 양字만 조와하다가는 양쌀밥도 못 어더자시리다. 아-멘.

富者와 貧者間

門안 사는 文보도

제것 가지고도 못 살 놈은 조선의 부자놈이요! 부자놈!, 부자놈하고 蛇蝎視를 하니 어듸서 도덕질을 해 온 것이란 말이요 툭하면 내라니 그도 낼 것이야 당연히 내겟지만 신수가 멀ㅅ정하고 근력이 튼튼해 보이는 사람이 와서 나는 가난해서 먹고 살 것이 업스니 얼마를 내여라. 구걸이 안이라 바보 빗을 졸으러 온 것 가티 으로 딱딱어리는 철면피가 잇스니 왜 제손발이 멀ㅅ정하야 가지고 남의 것을 상관업시 달나느냐 말이요. 새해부터는 그런 惰怠한 사람이 업서지고 제 힘으로 버러먹는 사람이 만헛스면…….

貧人 彭三孫

나는 결코 게을러서 가난한 것이 안이외다. 당장 굶어 죽게 되엿는데 게으른 생각이 몬지 만콤이 나잇겟슴니까 밤낫으로 피땀을 흘리며 죽도록 버러도 살 수가 업스니 이것이 가난한 사람의 죄일지 모르겟슴니다 가난한 사람으로 태여난 것도 설은데 더구나 천대를 밧으니 더욱 설습니다 가난한 놈은 죽어야 할까요 부자님네께 바라나이다 다 갓치 좀 살아봅시다. 결코<25> 거저 달라는 것이 안이외다. 다시 말하지만어도 알으시겟지요 이 세상의 公理가 엇더한 것인지…….

自動車夫와 乘客間

朝O自動車 權O澤

저희야 돈만 버는 것이 목적이지요! 그러나 승객의 팔할은 여흥으로 타시니 조선 사람의 하나로서 생각하는 배가 업지안슴니다 좀더 실용적으로 만히 타시게 되엿스면 맛찬가지 돈버는데도 마음에 좃켓슴니다그리고 요금에 대해서 너모 빗사네 싸네 말이 만슴니다 日人 운전수면 딴 말 업시 내주면서!.

常乘者 李成玉

귀한 돈을 주고 자동차를 타는 것은 좀더 速하게 가자는 것인데 급한 때에 자동차를 부르면 엇지나 그리 더뒤인지 기다리다가 시간을 다 보내고 볼 일을 낭패하니 그래서야 자동차 탈 사람이 어듸 잇겟슴닛가 운전수들은 좀더 친절하고 시간관념이 잇섯스면 조흘가 함니다.
