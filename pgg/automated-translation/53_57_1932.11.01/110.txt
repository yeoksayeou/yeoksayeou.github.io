Advertisements Are Useless

"I hear your son has left home and you don't know where he went. Why don't you put an advertisement in the newspaper today and look for him?"

"But, as you know, that child is only six years old and hasn't even started school yet, so it's useless because he wouldn't be able to read the newspaper."
