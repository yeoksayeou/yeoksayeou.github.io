別件坤 第五十七號
1932년 11월 01일
百貨店見學 (Department Store Observation)
문예기타 (Literary Miscellaneous)

A group of students on a school trip were looking around a department store, and one student stopped alone in front of the restaurant and said, "Ah—this is cutlet (가쓰레쓰)…vegetable salad (야사이사라다)…curry rice. Let's take a moment to record it."

<13>

**Named Entities**

가쓰레쓰　Katsuretsu
야사이사라다　Yasai sarada
