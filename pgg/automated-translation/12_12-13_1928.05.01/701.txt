Serial Storytelling Morning
A Great Read Above Novels, Above Essays, A Great Read Among Reading Material!!
The great serial storytelling by famous figures, which has been highly anticipated by everyone, will finally be published starting with the June issue. What kind of incident will they bring about and what will they suggest...? As the author is an author and the reading material is reading material, I dare to say in advance that no one will be able to simply pass it by.

To be published starting from the June issue, first installment by Ch'oe Namsŏn
This storytelling is infinitely interesting in that it is a creation by someone who is not a creator and a novel by someone who is not a novelist, and it is also deeply meaningful in that the writers are responsible figures from various fields. First of all, what kind of person will appear and what kind of incident will unfold in the first episode by Yuktang Ch'oe Namsŏn... Be sure to order the June issue in advance.

連作講談 아츰
小說以上ㆍ論文以上ㆍ讀物中의 大讀物!!
天下萬人의 多大한 期待中에잇는 問題의 大讀物名士諸氏의 連作大講談은이제 六月號부터 揭載되게되엿다. 그들은 果然 무슨 事件을 促來하야무엇을 提示하려하는가………作者가 作者요 讀物이 讀物인만큼 아모도 等閑히지나처 버리지못할것임을 미리 斷言한다.
六月號부터 揭載第一回 崔南善作
創作家아닌이의 創作 小說家아닌이의 小說인 點에서 이 講談은 興味無窮한 것이요 執筆者가 各方面의 責任잇는 名士인 點에서 意味한 深長한 것이다. 우선 六堂崔南善氏의 第一回發端에엇던 人物이 上場되고무슨 事件이 展開될는지……六月號는 미리미리 注文해두라
