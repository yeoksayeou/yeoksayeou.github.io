기사형태	잡저 (Essay)

The Beauty of Korean Women as Seen by a Painter
						Lee Cheong-jeon

I don't know how the neatly folded skirt of a modest maiden's short jacket (kkoetdong jeogori) makes her figure look more beautiful, softer, cleaner, and more precious. Moreover, the black hair and bright red ribbon (danggi) that hang down from the jacket's collar, swaying below the waist, are indescribable in words. (Lee Cheong-jeon)
