기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Notice/Post-Editing Note)

Seeking Designs for Kaebyoksa's Mark, Recruiting Reader's Ideas.

In the summer of the year of Gyeongsin, Kaebyoksa (開闢社) was founded and has been struggling for 8 years with the magazines Kaebyok (開闢), Buin (婦人), Orini (어린이), and Byolgeonggeon (別乾坤), as well as occasional book publications, lectures, and other activities. To ensure Kaebyoksa (開闢社) continues to fight even harder going forward, we are publicly soliciting designs for the company's "mark" from the world and will select the best design. We hope that you will send us many good designs with your special ideas, and the selected design will be awarded a special-made gold watch and a two-year subscription to this magazine as a souvenir.

Reference Information

There are no restrictions on the content to be included in the mark, so absolute freedom is allowed.

The shape can be a circle, octagon, or hexagon (the size can be adjusted later, so it can be drawn large). The color should be monochrome if possible, or two colors if unavoidable (if monochrome, it must be black).

You can submit as many types as you want (but draw one type on each sheet and write your address and name on each sheet).

Deadline: June 20th

Hopes

It would be good if the meaning of Kaebyok (開闢) could be expressed as much as possible, and Byolgeonggeon.

If possible, it should be easy to spot from a distance when making the company flag.

If possible, it should be simple with neat lines. <85>
