Strange Cases and Clever Answers

A student in Seoul needed to ask for money, but if he didn't specify a clear reason, they absolutely wouldn't send it. So, he said his ear was frostbitten and he was hospitalized, so send money for medicine. He injured his arm while exercising. He sprained his ankle, so send money for treatment. He used every excuse he could think of, one after another. Finally, he said he broke a school window while playing and had to pay for it, so send 2 won. He even said he broke the blackboard and needed 5 won.

This went on for about half a year. He ran out of excuses and was at a loss. One day, while doing gymnastics on the playground, they were lined up in a horizontal row and moving forward. Because he took a step too slow, the gymnastics teacher's command was like thunder:

"列ヲ落シジヤイカソ (Retsu o otoshijiya ikaso - Don't fall out of line!)."

He thought he had it! That day, he wrote home, "Today, while doing gymnastics, I fell out of line (렬을 떠러트려서) and have to pay for it, so please send 7 won by postal money order."

The father in the countryside, who didn't know what "렬 (ryeol)" was, went to the local elementary school teacher and showed him the letter. The teacher looked at it and laughed, saying, "Don't worry. There's a way to fix it without spending money." He immediately wrote a telegram: "一步ススメ (Ippo susume - Take one step forward!)." <19>

奇案妙答
서울 류학생이 돈을 보내 달나고 해야겟는데 분명한 용처를 적어 보내지 안으면 절대로 안 보내는지라. 치위에 귀가 얼어서 병원에 단기니 약갑을 보내라. 운동하다가 팔을 상햇다. 발목을 삐엿스니 치료비를 보내라. 생각나는 핑계를 차례차례 모조리 써먹고 나종에는 작난하다가 학교 유리창을 깨트려서 물어노아야 하겟스니 2원만 보내라. 칠판을 깨트려 노앗스니 5원만 보내 달나고까지 하엿다.
이러케 한 반년 동안 지나닛가. 더 써먹을 핑계가 업서서 쩔쩔매게 되엿다. 하로는 운동장에서 톄조를 하는 중에 엽흐로(橫隊)로 쭉 느러서서 전진을 하는데 한 거름 더듸 거럿드니 톄조선생의 호령이 벽력갓다.
「列ヲ落シジヤイカソ 렬을 떠러트리면 안된다.」 올탓구나 하고 그는 그날 집에 편지하되 「오늘 톄조하다가 렬을 떠러트려서 곳 물어노아야 겟스니 7원만 뎐환으로 부처 주서야 겟슴니다.」하엿다.
그 편지를 밧은 시골 아버지가 렬이라는 것이 무언지 몰나서 동리 보통학교 선생에게 가서 편지를 보엿더니 선생이 보고 우스면서 「럼려 마십시요. 돈 안드리고 물어 놋는 방법이 잇슴니다.」하고 곳 뎐보문을 써주되 「一步ススメ。 한 거름 더 나스라!」고 하엿다.<19>
