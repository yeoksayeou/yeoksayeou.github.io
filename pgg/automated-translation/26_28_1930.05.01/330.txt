I Came to Learn

Teacher (During class): "Lee Gae-ttong... What is the highest mountain in Joseon?"

Gae-ttong: "I know, but I won't say."

Teacher: "Why?"

Gae-ttong: "I came to learn, not to teach the teacher." <115>
