A story titled "Two Hundred and Nine Pieces"

Physiology Teacher: "There are about two hundred and odd bones in the human body."

Student: "Teacher, I have two hundred and nine."

Teacher: "How can that be!"

Student: "I swallowed a fish bone at lunchtime today." <78>
