『Cafe Pandarai』

Fire

Fire
A moving landscape painting under the colored lights.

A waitress with rouged pretty lips
blurring the reverie drawn in the blue peppermint
with jazz dance.

Over here, next to the curtain,
a long-haired youth in a red tie
carelessly pounding the piano keys,

Over there, at the table,
two drunken XX·XX people
spending the night in endless discussion again. <125>

Oh, a Russian young lady, dozing like a cat,
chewing on a yawn and sitting,
XXX XXX Your shaking XX

Tell me about the XX that happened in XXX many years ago.
Today, a sorrow arises for no reason.
A sorrow imbued with alcohol, love, and the scent of Chosŏn.

Oh, give me bitter curry tea.
In the midst of the purple Corona smoke,
your flushed face is also beautiful. (130.2.23) <126>
