A Clever Wife

A couple lived together. One day, the husband, having worked all day, hurried home, expecting his wife to have prepared a warm meal and be waiting for him at the door. But upon arriving, he found the door tightly locked with a padlock. The usually gentle husband, unable to contain his frustration, broke the padlock with a stone in a fit of anger and entered the house.

He stood there, blankly staring at the ceiling.

Suddenly, he noticed a piece of paper on the desk with his wife’s handwriting. It read:

“I’ve gone to see a motion picture. I’ve locked the door, and the key is hidden under the eaves above the entrance.”

The husband remarked,

“What a clever wife!” <139>
