Drawing a Rabbit Picture

Drawing a rabbit picture. Drawing a rabbit picture. Call the painter. I called the painter. Drawing a rabbit picture.

The painter who drew a phoenix on Yi Baek’s (李謫仙) Bonghwangdae (鳳凰臺). The painter who drew the sun and moon on the Namguk Heavenly Emperor’s (南國天子) Neungheodae (凌虛臺). The painter who drew horses on King Yeonso (燕昭王)’s Hwanggeumdae (黃金臺). Grind ink on Dongjeong (洞庭) crystal blue-yellow inkstone, Geobuk (거북) ink drop on Geumsu (錦水) autumn wave. Call an octopus to grind the ink. Untangle both painting brushes, draw this and that on white silk snowflake paper. Draw the eyes that saw the scenery among famous mountains and scenic spots under the heavens. Draw the ears that heard the sound when parrots and peacocks chirped. Draw the nose that smelled the best in the clouds and mists of Mt. Bangjang (方丈) and Bongrae (蓬萊). Draw the mouth that picked and ate orchids and all kinds of fragrant herbs. Draw the feet that jumped around in the Manhwa (萬和) and Bangchang (方暢) flower forest. Draw the fur that protected against the cold in the great cold of midwinter.

The left is a green mountain, and the right is green water. Deep in the green water and blue mountains, under the shade of the cassia tree, softly jump and leap. The two ears are perked up, the two eyes are rolling. The waist is slender. The tail is stubby. The front feet are short. The hind feet are long. Springing. Leaping. Could the crescent moon rabbit of Mt. Emei (峨眉山月半輪兎) be better than this? Ah, here you go. Byeoljubu (鱉主簿), you carry this. <125>

톡기화상
톡기 화상을 그린다. 톡기 화상을 그린다. 화공을 불너라. 화공을 불넛소. 톡기 화상을 그린다. 李謫仙鳳凰臺에 鳳 그리든 환쟁이. 南國天子凌虛臺에 日月 그리든 환쟁이, 燕昭王의 黃金臺에 말 그리든 환쟁이, 洞庭琉璃靑黃硯, 錦水秋波거북硯滴, 烏賊魚 불너 먹을 갈아, 兩頭畵筆 다북 풀어, 白綾雪花簡紙上에 이리 저리 그린다. 天下 名山勝地間에 景槪보든 눈 그리고. 鸚鵡 孔雀 지적울 제 소리 듯든 귀 그리고. 方丈蓬萊雲霧 중에 내 잘 맛든 코 그리고. 蘭草芝草 온갓 香草 꼿따 먹든 입 그리고. 萬和方暢花林 중에 펄펄 뛰든 발 그리고. 大寒嚴冬寒 중에 防風하든 털 그리고. 좌편은 靑山이요 우편은 綠水로다. 綠水靑山 깁흔 곳에 계수 나무 그늘 속 앙금조춤 펄펄 뛰여. 두귀는 쫑굿, 두 눈은 도리. 허리는 잘눅. 꽁지는 몽툭. 압발은 짜르고. 뒤발은 길어. 깡청. 뛰여가는 峨眉山月半輪兎인들 이에서 더할소냐. 아나 옛다. 鱉主簿야. 네가 지고 잇거라.＜125＞
