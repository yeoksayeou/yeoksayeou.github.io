New Word Dictionary
(ㅋ) (고) section

Carnival: A festival before Lent in the Roman Catholic Church. It is also commonly used for general banquets and festivals.

Girl Scouts: A new movement to help girls understand the meaning of social life, similar to the Boy Scouts.

Kapsel: German word. It is called a capsule (膠匣) in Korean. A capsule used to take bitter medicine that is difficult to swallow.

Carat: A unit for the purity of gold. Since pure gold is set at 24 carats, 18-karat gold means that only 18/24 is gold, and the rest is mixed with other metals.

Kleptomania: In Japanese, it is called "shoplifting" (萬引), which is petty theft committed in stores or department stores.

Kreosot: A type of disinfectant that sells well these days.

Clover: A plant that looks like pasture grass. It creeps in gardens or ranches, and the three-leaf clover shaped like a club, and the four-leaf clover is a symbol of love. Lovers put it in love letters and enjoy giving and receiving it.

Radical Bill: Abbreviation for the Radical Social Movement Control Bill (過激社會運動取締法案). A bill to severely crack down on the propaganda of communism and anarchism.

Crowd Psychology: Even if one person cannot think of doing something separately, the crowd does it. It is a type of herd mentality in which people lose their ability to reflect and are easily influenced by feelings and enthusiasm.

Kokusui-kai (國粹會): An anti-socialist movement group in Japan, organized by some comrades of traditional chivalrous men, veterans, and civil engineering contractors. It is also famous for its skill in swordsmanship and violence.

Internationalism: Opposition to imperialism. A doctrine of conducting national activities based on the cause of coexistence of all nations in the world.

Collotype: A type of photographic plate made by coating a copper plate with glue and applying a photosensitive drug on it for exposure.

Croquette: A Western dish made by mincing meat and potatoes (馬鈴薯) into round shapes and frying them.

Contra dance: A dance in 2/4 time in which dancers face each other. It originated in France, and at first, there were two dancers, but now it is usually eight people.

Delusion of Grandeur: A mental illness that often occurs during times of strong nerve stimulation. The mentally ill person falsely believes that they have become very great and has grandiose delusions of that kind.

Kodak: A portable and lightweight film camera for pockets. It is often used by women and reporters.

Concrete-ship: Recently devised due to a shortage of iron materials. It is a ship surrounded by concrete, and not only is the material inexpensive, but it also has the advantage of limiting damage to one part even when attacked by a naval mine. ＜51＞

新語大辭典
(ㅋ) (고)部
카-니봘(Carnival)  羅馬敎의 四旬祭 前에 잇는 祭로 一般 宴會와 祝祭에도 通用되는 말이다.

껄-스카우츠(girl-scouts)  小女義勇團 小女斥候隊로 뽀-이스카우츠와 가치 少女들에게도 社會生活의 意義를 理解식히고자 하는 새로운 運動.

캎셀(Kapsel)  獨語. 우리말로 膠匣이라 한다. 먹기 거북한 쓴 藥을 內用하는대 쓰는 膠囊.

카랏트(Carat)  金의 品位에 대한 單位. 純金을 24카랏트로 정햇슴으로 18金이라면 24分의 18分만 金이고 남어지는 다른 金屬이 석기인 것이다.

크레프트메니어(Cleptomania)  日本말로 소위 「만빗기」(萬引)라는 것으로 商店이나, 데파-트멘트 店頭에서 行하는 鼠賊..

크레오소-트(Kreosot)  요사이 잘 팔니는 殺菌劑의 一種.

크로-붜-(Clover)  牧草와 가티 생긴 植物이니 庭園이나 牧場가튼데 匐生하야 크라부形의 입분 三葉草 四葉 크로-붜-는 愛의 象徵으로 러브레터-속에 너어서 戀人들이 주고 밧고 하면서 즐거워하는 풀이다.

過激法案  過激社會運動 取締法案의 略稱. 共産主義. 無政府主義 等의 宣傳을 嚴重히 取締하기 위한 법안.

群衆心理  한 사람 한 사람 따로 떼여서는 생각할 수 업슬 만한 일이라도 群衆은 實行한다. 卽 反省力을 일코 感覺的으로 흘너서 熱하기 쉬운 附和雷同的 心理作用의 一種.

國粹會  日本에 잇는 反社會主義運動團體로 古來의 俠客, 在鄕軍人, 土木請負人 等의 一部 同志로써 組織된 것이다. 칼질 잘하고 暴力 잘 부리기로도 有名하다.

國際主義  帝國主義의 反對. 世界 萬國 共存의 大義에 의하야 國家活動을 營爲해 나가자는 주의.

코로타이푸(Collotype)  銅板에 膠칠을 하고 그 우에 感光藥을 써서 燒光하는 一種의 寫眞板.

코록케(Croquette)  잘게 썰은 肉. 감자(馬鈴薯)가튼 것을 동그랏게 만들어서 지저 낸 西洋料理의 일흠.

콘트라딴스(Contra dance)  舞踏하는 사람이 마조 보고 추는 2/4 拍子의 딴스. 佛國에 始作된 것인데 처음에는 춤추는 사람이 두 사람이든 것인데 시방은 普通 여들 사람으로 되엿다.

誇大妄想狂  神經刺戟이 强한 時節에 만히 생기는 精神病者. 自己가 대단히 훌륭하게 된 것처름 妄信하고 그런 類의 誇大忘訖을 하는 狂人.

코닥크(Kodak)  懷中用 輕便 필름 寫眞械. 婦人用. 記者用으로 잘 쓰인다.

콘쿠리-트船(Concrete-ship)  鐵材缺之으로 하야 最近 考案된 것인데 콘쿠리-트로 둘러싼 汽船인데 材料가 低廉한 것은 勿論이나 水雷의 攻擊을 밧고도 그 被害가 一局部에 긋치는 이익이 잇다. ＜51＞
