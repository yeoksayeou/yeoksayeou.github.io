Prize Quiz
The last prize quiz was very interesting. I will present another interesting one this time.

"What is between spring and summer?"

Think carefully. If you guess correctly, you will find it very interesting.

How to Answer the Quiz
★ Must arrive at the company by the 30th of next month (May).
★ Write the answer simply on a postcard-sized paper, and be sure to write your address and name clearly.
★ Be sure to attach the reader certificate at the bottom of this page to the answer sheet. It will be invalid if not attached.
★ Write '京城開闢社別乾坤懸賞係 (Kyŏngsŏng Kaepyŏksa Pyŏlgŏn'gŏn Hyŏnsanggye - Kyongsong Publication Company Pyŏlgŏn'gŏn Prize Section)' on the envelope and also write '原稿在中 (wŏn'go chaejung - Manuscript Inside)' in red ink next to it, and seal one corner and attach a 2-jeon stamp. If you don't write 原稿在中 (wŏn'go chaejung - Manuscript Inside), you will be charged a fine (deficiency).

Prizes
First Prize (One Person): Special Made Artistic Table Clock
Other Prizes (Ninety-Nine People): One Book Each

Regarding Last Month's Prize Answer
More than 60,000 people applied for this prize, but there were not many plausible answers.
Therefore, the correct answer chosen by the prize committee was "Adding one drop of water to one drop of water makes one drop of water."
And the next most ingenious was the character "王 (wang - king)." Doesn't 一十一 (il-sip-il - one-ten-one) make the character "王 (wang - king)?" So, we drew two second-place winners from this "王 (wang - king)." After that, we couldn't fill all 97 with those who guessed similarly.

懸賞問題
지난번 현상문제는 매우자미가 잇섯습니다.
이번에도  자미잇는 것으로 내겟습니다.
「봄과여름사이에 잇는 것이 무엇입니?」
잘생각해보십시오. 알어 맛치기만하면 참자미가잇습니다.

현상해답하는법
★내달(五月)三十日지 본사에 와닷도록되여야합니다.
★엽서만한 조회에다 대답을 간단히 적어보내되 반듯이자긔주소와 성명을 이써야합니니다.
★이페지(頁)한해잇는 독자증을 답안용지에다 반듯이 부처야합니다. 안붓치면 무효입니다.
★피봉에는 반듯이 『京城開闢社別乾坤懸賞係』라쓰고 엽헤다 『原稿在中』이라고 안잉크로쓰고 한주둥이를 고 二錢우표붓처보내주십시오. 原稿在中이라고 안쓰면 벌금(不足)을 물게됩니다.

賞品
一等一人 特製 美術卓上時計
等外九十九人 書籍一部式

前月懸賞解答에 對하야
이번현상은 응모가 도합 六만명이상이엇스나 그중에 그럼직한 해답은 얼마되지 아니하엿습니다.
그리하야 현상에게서 정해로 골나 추첨한 것이 「물한방울에 물한방울을 보태면 물한방울」이라는 것입니다.
그리고 그다음으로 진기한 것은 왕「王」자입니다. 一十一하면 「王」자한자가 되지아니합니. 그래서 이 「王」자에서 이등을 둘만 앗습니다. 그런다음에 근사하게 마즌분으로 은 것이 九十七이 다 차지못하엿습니다.
