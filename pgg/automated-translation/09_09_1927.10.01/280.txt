사고·편집후기 (Advertisement/Editorial Postscript)

Foreigners choose and buy books for their sons and daughters to read at home.

The most reliable magazine in Korea for parents to let their children read is none other than "Eorini (어린이)," published monthly by our company, as universally acknowledged. Those who cherish their children can afford to buy it by saving just one pack of cigarettes, as the price is only 15 jeon (錢). <95>
