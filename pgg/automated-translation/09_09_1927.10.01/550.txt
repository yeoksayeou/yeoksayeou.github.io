1927년 10월 01일
中國秘話 旅中의 老子 (Secret Story of China: Lao Tzu on a Journey, Novel)
代老子
소설

Life is full of changes. The more you think about it, the more mysterious life's changes are.

I was nearing 60, and due to the decline of my family fortune, I had to set out on a wandering path again with my young wife, Hwangyeop. Deep down, I wanted to live a life of prosperity again, so I headed towards the royal capital, Nakyang (洛陽).

Traveling with a sick wife, what need is there to mention the hardships along the way! Sometimes riding a horse, sometimes taking a cart, we arrived at Yeongyang (榮陽) after three or four days. I wanted to climb Songsan (嵩山) a bit along the way, but I stopped because of my sick wife and took the road north of Songsan.

Yeongyang is a very bustling place these days. I looked for a suitable place to rest comfortably with my wife. We found Joanru (照顔樓), which had the best reputation, and entered.

The pavilion overlooked a stream and was beautifully situated amidst lush trees. I chose the most serene room and took off my shoes. It wasn't surprising, but my wife, inexperienced in travel, fell ill from fatigue, making the journey difficult again. Inevitably, we had to stay here for several days.

The owner is Jang-o (長梧), a handsome man in his 50s, and there is also a handsome man named Munryang (文良) who works as a servant at this pavilion. He looked to be about 40 years old, and his physique and eyes were extraordinary.

I was truly grateful for the kindness that the owner and servant showed us. Especially at night, they took turns coming into our room to talk about the world and comfort my sick wife, for which I was very thankful. We had already stayed here for seven days.

A clear breeze, passing over green waters and brushing against green leaves, shook the bells on the railing; it was a summer evening. At this time, Munryang came in and said,

"Sir, it is truly a fine season," he greeted.

I replied cheerfully, "Indeed! It is truly a fine season."

"How is your strength?" he asked Hwangyeop again.

"Thanks to your concern, I am much better. I am sorry for causing so much trouble," Hwangyeop also looked much better.

"Not at all! You must be very uncomfortable, but it seems like there are no major issues... In any case, illness while traveling is a great difficulty."

"That's right, traveling with a woman is certainly difficult," I interjected.

Munryang then began to speak to me again.

"Sir, I am sorry to say this, but are you from the Yangban class in Jeong (鄭)?"

"Yes..."

"Are you from Jeong?"

"No, I was born in Jin (陳)."

"Is that so? These days, there is a great man named Jasan (子産) in Jeong, and his reputation is very good."

"Is that so? Jasan, yes, he is a well-reputed man."

"And there is also a famous man named I Dam (李聃) in Jeong."

At this moment, I was startled. But I nonchalantly said, "Is that so?" and remained silent.

"Now that I think about it, excuse me, but you don't seem like an ordinary person either."

"Haha... What kind of person do you think I am?"

"You don't seem like an ordinary person. From the first time I saw your face, and from the way you speak, in every way, you are not an ordinary person."

"Is that so? How can you say I am not an ordinary person when we are talking so openly?"

"In any case, you are not an ordinary person. I even wonder if you eat rice."

"Ah, what a strange thing to say. Where is there a person who doesn't eat rice...?"

"Excuse me, but you don't seem like someone from this world. Do you feel the cold and heat?"

"Of course! When it's hot, I feel hot, and when it's cold, I feel cold."

"Is that so? Do you know when you taste good food?" <162>

"If you give me good food, of course, I eat it deliciously. But where do you give me delicious food? Hahaha..."

"This is too presumptuous to say, but have you ever been in love when you were young?"

"Haha... Of course. Even now, I have such a young and pretty wife, don't I?"

"Ah, is this lady your wife? I thought she was your daughter. I am sorry."

"Is that so? You must be a bit dim-witted."

"Yes, she is your wife."

"There's no need to be so surprised."

"No, there's no need to be surprised, but the age seems a bit inappropriate. It seems a bit unsuitable for an old man to have such a young wife."

"That is also fate, what can I do?"

"You can't just say it's fate. There are at least two things an old man should avoid when he has a young wife."

"An interesting story... Two things to avoid..."

"First, because your health is not average, there is a risk of the man dying early. Second, there is a risk of an old man being easily deceived. I have said something rude."

"What, what rudeness? Whether you are deceived or deceiving, it all depends on the person. Have you ever been deceived?"

"Ah... This is too much of an uncomfortable counterattack. But I don't need to hide anything. I have recently had the experience of being greatly deceived."

"Ha... This is a very interesting story. Tell me. Let me hear it."

"Haha, if that is your wish, I will tell you."

From then on, Munryang, with a deeply moved expression, began to reveal his own experience.

I was originally born in Bakgeo (柏擧), not far from the capital of Cho (楚). Because my father failed in business, our family fortune was extremely poor, and I lived a life of considerable hardship from a young age.

Having grown up in such circumstances, I never even dreamed of getting a wife, whether in my youth or adulthood. I felt that living a poor life with a wife would be the greatest hardship. So, instead of getting a wife, I decided to gather a lot of money.

At that time, there was a man named Seo Seong (徐盛) in my neighborhood who had made a great success as an oil merchant. I became an employee at this store from the age of 20. The salary wasn't that high, but I didn't spend a penny of it anywhere else and saved it bit by bit. <163>

In any case, determination is a scary thing. I had saved almost several thousand gold. My acquaintances advised me to get married. But I was not moved at all and single-mindedly focused on gathering money. So, my friends even gave me the nickname Gold Dung Beetle (金糞虫).

I continued to do this until I passed 30, passed 35, and reached the age of 40. In the 20 years from 20 to 40, I had saved almost two thousand four or five hundred gold. This was not a small amount of money, but I was not satisfied and decided to gather tens or hundreds of thousands of gold. However, as soon as I turned 40, I began to feel a sense of loneliness in my heart from time to time. I couldn't easily figure out what this loneliness was.

One summer evening, I went to visit my friend Heo Baek-won (許白遠), who lived in the suburbs. Next to my friend's house, a clear and clean stream flowed. Thinking it was a very nice view, I looked at the riverbank and unexpectedly saw the back of a woman doing laundry. At first glance, the woman was a bewitching beauty. As someone who had never even greeted a woman in my 40 years, I unknowingly stood there blankly. If her back view is so bewitching, what does her face look like? Out of curiosity, I wanted to go forward and see her face. Suddenly, a kind of caution arose in my heart. What is the point of a middle-aged man in his 40s looking at women? So, I forced that thought down and went to Heo's house. Unexpectedly, Heo was out, so I turned back. On the way back, I also looked at the stream. She had already finished her laundry and was walking slowly, looking at the sunset sinking into the Western Mountains. This time, I saw her face head-on. She was truly an absolute beauty. I almost lost myself, wondering if there could be such a beauty in this world. Sir, please laugh. In the meantime, the woman's shadow gradually disappeared among the trees, and I returned aimlessly, as if I had lost something.

I think this was a heartbreak for me.

The next day, I went to visit Heo again. In fact, there was no particular reason. I just wanted to see the beautiful figure I had seen yesterday. But no matter how much I looked at the riverbank, I couldn't see her shadow. I was disappointed.

Just as I was about to go to Heo's house and ask for an introduction, I heard the sound of a door opening next door. Without realizing it, I looked in that direction, and what a strange thing this was. The beauty from yesterday was coming out of this house. I watched her bewitching figure walking slowly. At this time, I was also in a state of oblivion.

That day, Heo was at home, so we met and talked about our feelings. And more than anything, I was curious about the woman and asked about her circumstances first. "Heo, who has come to live in your neighbor's house, where you have been acquainted for a long time?"

Heo laughed heartily and said,

"Oh, someone has come, and she is an extraordinary person, but it wouldn't be interesting to talk about her to someone like you, who doesn't have a wife."

"No, it's not like that. Tell me who has come."

"Then I'll tell you. The person who came to our neighborhood... Haha."

"Stop laughing and tell me quickly."

"Okay, okay, since you're in such a hurry, I'll tell you. There is an absolutely stunning beauty, the kind you would die for if you saw her once, a girl who seems to be her daughter, about ten years old, and an old man who seems to be the woman's father, a total of three people."

"Is that so? Did she come to your house to greet you?"

"Since we are neighbors, of course, she came to greet me. Listening to her story, she is a very pitiful woman. After her husband died, she had nowhere to rely on and wandered around looking for a place to live. Somehow, she came all the way here. It seems she came from a city called Yeong (郢). Anyway, she is in such a difficult situation that she said it would be good to have her young daughter work as a servant or send her as an adopted daughter somewhere."

"Is that so? She is a very unfortunate woman."

"But if she can take care of that young girl well, the woman herself is excellent and will remarry and have a happy life again."

"Ha! Is she that beautiful?"

"Haha. You don't even have the right to talk about beauty. May I tell you something for reference? In terms of age, she is about 30 years old, and if I were to describe her as a fully bloomed flower, that would be enough, but it would be too disappointing to describe this woman with just one word. A light pink peach blossom color, no, a face that is more beautiful than a begonia, and eyes as clear and full of emotion as daffodils. Lips as lovely as a half-open peony. Haha, I don't have the eloquence, and I don't have the knowledge of beauty, so I can't describe it to your satisfaction. Okay, stop it. Anyway, she is a beauty that you can't see in this area."

"You are also quite the connoisseur."

"No, no matter how many adjectives I use, I can't even get close to her. But there is one thing I am thinking about. You are not going to get married after all, so there's no point in talking about it, but isn't it too unfortunate to be without children when you get old? How about you adopt that girl and raise her well to do chores for you when you get old...?"

"Well, that might be a good idea. There's nothing particularly bad about being an adopted daughter, but let's think about it a bit."

"That's right, everything should not be done too hastily..." <165>

After this conversation, I returned home. Why is this? The woman's beautiful figure did not fade and gradually began to stain my heart. Eventually, it felt like a seal had been engraved in my heart.

I had never paid attention to women before. I was so attached to gold that I didn't even understand myself. But now, after seeing that woman once, I wanted to entrust my entire being to her. No matter what, I couldn't forget her figure.

While I was in this state, one day, someone called from outside the door.

"Excuse me."

I went out and saw an old man of about 60 and a girl of about ten years old.

"I am sorry. I am visiting you with an introduction from Heo Baek-won, who lives next door to Heo."

When I heard this, my heart suddenly started pounding.

"Is that so? Please come in."

"Excuse me," said the old man, bringing the girl in. We sat down in a room together. The old man slowly said,

"I am sorry for visiting you so suddenly, but it is actually an introduction from Neighbor Heo.

"Ah, Heo is my friend."

"Heo said that you are living as a bachelor and looking for an adopted child. So, I told Heo about my situation... It is embarrassing to say, but that is why I came to visit you."

"Is that so? As you can see, I am living a bachelor life. I have also heard a little about your family from Heo."

"Regarding that, my situation is so difficult that I am asking you to take this girl in. So, I have brought her with me today without being ashamed."

"Ah, it is not that I don't want to take in a child, but I haven't decided to take in a child from your house yet."

"That may be so, but my situation is so difficult that I am taking this unreasonable action. My nephew is currently a minister in Cho, and until recently, I was staying there, but I came here to find a place for the poor woman to settle down. If you take this child in, the woman will also have a place to rely on. Please take her in out of pity."

When I heard this, I unconsciously said to myself,

"Okay, let's do that."

Then the old man was very happy and said, "Thank you. I am very grateful," and looked back at the child.

"Child! From now on, this gentleman is your father. Greet him."

"The child's face is truly beautiful and lovely. She immediately gets up and performs the ceremonial bow. Like a dream, this matter went so smoothly. The old man said,

"Then, tomorrow, I will come with the child's mother to thank you again. For today, I will take her with me and come back early tomorrow," and left.

I was truly stunned and didn't know what to do. But thinking that the child's mother would come tomorrow, my heart started beating again as if I couldn't wait. Thinking about it now, my psychology at this time was extremely crazy.

I couldn't sleep at night.

As soon as day broke, I got up early and busied myself preparing to welcome guests. At the same time, I was immersed in pondering over future matters. As time passed, the mother and daughter still hadn't come. The waiting was very impatient.

When it was well past noon, the voice of the old man was heard, asking for an introduction at the gate. I hurried out and saw that the three people had indeed come together.

I led them to the inner room and greeted each other. At this time, I spoke with the child's mother, the absolutely stunning beauty, for the first time. It was different from flowers smiling brightly through the leaves and peonies blooming before my eyes. This was the direct cause of my mental state going crazy. I was intoxicated by the fragrance of the woman's soul.

The old man's name was Tu Ja-seon (鬪子先), the beauty's name was Na-jeo (羅姐), and the girl's name was So-hong (小紅).

That day, they simply asked me to take care of the child and left. From now on, only the child was separated from me. The child was so cute and lovely that I received great comfort from that day on. I had only known that money was precious, but I finally realized the cuteness of a child.

During these two or three days, I naturally became a bit negligent in my work. Feeling guilty, I went to the store. The owner saw my diligent performance over the past 20 years and didn't scold me too much.

I spent several days like this. In the meantime, the old man came to visit and greet me every other day. He became very kind. But I was truly disappointed that the mother rarely came to visit.

Almost a month later, one day, the old man came to visit with a more troubled look than usual, sighing as he sat down. I was so strange that I said,

"Is there any difficult situation?" As soon as I asked the question, he finally said with a troubled look, "Ah, since yesterday, another difficult thing has happened. It is not another thing, but someone has come from my nephew in the capital of Cho, saying that I must return. Then, what will happen to Na-jeo? It is truly not a small difficulty. I have been trying to find a good match for her, but how easy is that? If I go to the capital now, she will be truly lonely. How can a young woman live alone? It is something I can't do anything about..."

After saying this, he looked at my face intently. I felt truly sorry for his situation and said,

"Is that so? That is truly a difficult thing." Then the old man said, "But," "But," as if he was about to say something but couldn't dare to, and finally, gathering his courage, he said this.

"But since things have come to this point, will I hide it? In my opinion, I think we must choose one of two things. One is the child that I have given to you, but I must return her to her mother, but that would be too much to abandon your kindness, so we dare not do it. The other is... please listen calmly. I am sorry and presumptuous, but what if you take Na-jeo as your wife? I have truly said something difficult to ask. What do you think? If you grant our wish, our problem will be solved completely, and if you can't, everything will be ruined. Please consider it once."

Here, I thought. If I were to reveal my heart at this time, it wouldn't be the adopted child that I desired, but the adopted daughter's mother. It wouldn't make sense to just accept the child and let her mother go wherever she wanted. So, as soon as I heard the old man's words, without any hesitation, I immediately agreed to the marriage.

The old man was also very happy and said, "Then everything is solved. Please choose a good day to hold the ceremony."

"Let's do that as soon as possible."

"For anything celebratory, the sooner the better. That's right, that's right. The day after tomorrow is a good day. It will be simple, but please come to my house to hold the ceremony. I will be the officiant."

Here the conversation ended. So, two days later, I learned what human happiness was for the first time in my life. At 40, I tasted the pleasure of the opposite sex for the first time, and I truly lost myself as if in a dream.

I quit my job at the store and spent day and night enjoying myself with my new wife. The old man saw our marriage and returned to the capital.

The store owner and my friend Heo were all surprised when they heard about my marriage. But they understood that men and women cannot live alone and rejoiced.

In this way, I lived a luxurious life for about a month. But unknowingly, my wife was worried about money,

"I am sorry to ask, but what will we do if we just keep spending like this? How much capital do we have?"

"There's no need to worry. Even if we just leave it at the store, it's two thousand several hundred gold, so even if we live a somewhat luxurious life, there's no need to worry too much. Don't worry."

"Is that so? That's very good. Then can we take it out of the store whenever we want?"

"Of course."

"But now that I think about it, I have another worry."

My wife's beautiful face clouded over for a moment.

"What worry?"

"Well, money is a worry whether you have it or not. What if the store owner doesn't return it honestly?"

"There's no way that would happen. Don't worry about that."

"But is there any proof that you deposited it?"

"There is no proof, but it is safe."

"No, you can't trust people so easily."

"If you are so worried, then let's take it all out."

"That's right. There is nothing more certain than keeping money in your own hands. If possible, please take it out soon."

I sympathized with my wife's plausible request and said,

"Then let's do that. I will take it out in a day or two," and reassured my wife.

Two or three days later, I requested all of my savings from the store owner. The owner frowned and said,

"Take it if you want to take it. But this money is what you have worked hard to gather for 20 years. Don't spend it carelessly."

"I will be very careful about that."

In this way, I took out everything. Two thousand several hundred gold is truly not a small amount of money. I wrapped it up nicely and showed it to my wife. My wife was overjoyed and said,

"Wow! That's so good. Let's keep it safe. From now on, I won't worry."

"Is that so? From now on, our life will be guaranteed to be comfortable."

My wife and I spent another month happily amidst joy. But I had to go on a business trip for a while because of something at the store. The number of days was scheduled for about 20 days. I carefully instructed my wife on household matters and left. Unexpectedly, there were many things to do, so I added 5 days to the scheduled date and hurriedly returned after 25 days.

Being away even for a short time made my longing for my wife even greater. I came running as fast as I could, wanting to see my wife's lovely face as soon as possible.

Sir, don't be surprised. What is this? When I returned, the door to the house was tightly locked. No matter how much I called, there was no answer. So, amidst immense doubt, I asked the neighbors, and they said that my wife had left with the child about 10 days ago.

I can't express what my heart was like at this time. As anger surged to the top of my head, I kicked down the door and rushed in to investigate the furniture. Oh my, what is this? There was not a single piece of furniture worth looking at. Of course, the gold bullion, the culmination of 20 years of blood and sweat, could not be seen either.

I truly cried out to the sky and resented people. Like waking up from a nightmare, I felt chills all over my body and cold sweat flowed.

After this happened, could I still be called a person? I smashed all the remaining furniture with my fist and immediately left this place, setting out on a wandering path without a destination.

Like a lost person, I aimlessly wandered here and there, and finally met this owner here and am living this life. This is the path of my suffering. Sir, please laugh. Later, I heard that she was a prostitute from Yeong who had heard somewhere that I hated women and only loved money, so she tried to deceive me and stole the money as compensation for deceiving me.

After telling this long and funny story, Munryang let out a long sigh. I also found the story very interesting and said,

"Munryang, when did that happen?"

"Yes, it was in June of last year."

"So it's been a year. How do you feel now?"

"It's not that big of a deal, but there is still resentment in my heart."

"Ha... Then that won't do. Forget about it. People work hard all their lives to know and remember things, but not studying how to forget things is a big disease."

Munryang was startled and looked at me with his eyes wide open,

"Is there a study of forgetting?"

"Of course. People live their lives in darkness because they can't forget what they have put in their minds. The study of forgetting is very necessary. Only by forgetting can there be light."

"Then is there a separate method for forgetting?"

"Of course, don't you know the method yet?"

"I don't know yet. I originally have a strong memory, so even if I read a book, I don't forget it, and even if something happens, I can't forget it." <170>

"That won't do. Forget everything. Forget everything between good and evil, fortune and misfortune. If you pile up too many things in your small head, worries and pain will arise from its fermentation. Empty your head."

"Ha... I understand. I would like you to teach me the method."

"If you want to know, I'll tell you. When gathering money, one to two, three to four, when getting older, ten to twenty, thirty to forty, isn't everything built up from one? There is a human attachment there, so you have memories like you."

"Haha, that's right." Munryang looked at my face intently.

"But to forget anything, you have to erase the numbers like one or three."

"Ha ha..." Munryang still had a face that said he didn't quite understand.

"Erasing the numbers, do you understand?"

"I haven't thought about that before."

"You are also in the way of righteousness! That is also good. But erasing the numbers is ultimately zero. Zero is ultimately nothing. Go all the way there."

When I said this, Munryang exclaimed, "Ha!" and sat silently with his head bowed for an hour, then slowly raised his head and looked at my face,

"Are you not Master I Dam (李聃先生)?"

"Haha... I am I Dam."

After this conversation, Munryang finally became my disciple. He is Munja (文子). Not only Munja, but also the owner of the inn, Jang-o (長梧), also became a comrade. How is my journey? <171>

中國秘話 旅中의 老子
						代老子
인생이란 변화가 만흔 것이다. 생각하면 생각할사록 불가사의의 것은 인생의 변화이다.
나는 60이 近當해가지고 가산의 영낙으로 해서 젊은 안해 黃葉을 더리고 다시 유랑의 길을 떠나게 되얏다. 마음에 은연히 다시 번화의 생활을 하고 십허서 왕도 洛陽을 향해 거름을 옴기게 되얏다.
약처와가티 떠낫스니 도중의 고통이야 말해 무엇하랴! 타지안어도 可할 말도 타고 수레도 타고하야 이렁저렁 삼사일만에 榮陽이란 곳에 니르럿다. 도중에 嵩山을 좀-올나 보았으면 조켓스나 그 亦 약처땀으네 중지하고 嵩山의 북으로 길을 취햇섯다.
榮陽이란 곳은 이즈음 매우 번화한 곳이다. 나는 어디든지 좀 - 적당한 곳을 가리여 유쾌히 좀 - 쉬여가랴고 말군과 가티 한참이나 차젓다. 맛츰, 照顔樓라는 가장 평판 조흔 곳을 어더 들게 되얏다.
누는 소천을 임하야 수림이 울창한 가운데 퍽 유수하게 되얏는데 나는 그 중에서 가장 청초한 一室을 택하야 신발을 끌럿섯다. 의외라고 할 수는 업지만 여행에 경험업는 약처는 그만 피로병으로해서 다시 행로가 어렵게 되얏다. 부득이 이 곳에서 멧츨간 두유하게 되얏다.
주인은 長梧라고 하는 50 남즛한 好男子이고 거긔에 文良이라고 하는 풍채조흔 好人物이 하나 잇다는데 그는 이 樓의 使喚格으로 잇는 사람이다. 나희는 40가량 되야보이고 체격과 안광이 아울너 비범한 산아희엿다.
주인과 사환이 엇지도 우리 부부에게 친절을 주는지 나는 실로 감사함을 마지 안엇다. 그 중에＜161＞ 특히 야간이면 양인이 번가러 우리의 방에 드러가지고 세상 니약이를 하며 병처를 위로해 주는데는 여간 감사하지 안엇다. 이렁저렁 발서 7일간이나 이곳에 머물넛섯다.
녹수를 건너고 靑葉을 슷처온 청풍이 난간의 방울을 흔들고 지나간 初夏의 석양이다. 이때 文良이 드러오며.

『아지 못함니다. 나는 본래 기억력이 강대해서 책을 닑어도 닛지 안코, 일이 잇서도 닛지를 못함니다.』＜170＞
『그것은 안되야. 一切 니저버리게. 善惡禍福간 全혀 니저버리게. 적은 머리에 만흔 사물을 싸어두면 그것의 發酵로 해서 煩惱苦痛이 작구 생기게 되네. 머리를 비여두게.』
『하...... 그럿켓슴니다. 잠간 그 방법을 가리처 주섯스면 조켓슴니다.』
『알고 십흐면 말해주지. 돈을 모으는데도 하나로 둘, 셋으로 넷, 나희를 먹는데도 열로 스물, 설흔으로 마흔, 모든 사물이란 一로부터 수를 쌋는 것이 안인가. 거긔에 인간의 집착이라는 것이 잇서서 자네와 가치 기억이 남어 잇게 되는 걸세.』
『하하, 그럿켓슴니다.』文良은 나의 얼골을 유심히 처다 보앗다.
『그런데 엇던 사물이든지 닛고거 하면 一이라든가 三이라든가 하는 수를 업새 버려야 되네.』
『하 하 ......』文良은 아직도 확실히 몰으겟다는 얼골을 가지고 잇섯다.
『수를 업새 버리는 것, 알겟나.』
『아직 그런 일은 생각해 본 일이 업서서요.』
『역시 자네도 인의의 도인가!, 그도 좃치. 그러나 수를 업새 버리는 것은 결국 - 零일세. 零은 결국 - 無일세. 거긔까지 철저히 드러가 보게.』
이럿케 말해 들녀 주닛가, 文良은 『하!』하고 한마듸 탄사를 하더니 한시간 동안이나 머리를 숙이고 묵묵히 안젓다가 슬적 머리를  드러 나의 얼골을 처다보면서,
『선생님은 李聃先生이 안이심닛가?』
『하하...... 나는 李聃일세.』
