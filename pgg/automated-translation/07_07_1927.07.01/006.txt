Request for Return of March Issue

Although every issue of Pyŏlgŏn’gon (別乾坤) is out of print, the March issue in particular sold out in just five days, and orders flooded in to the point where we even ran a newspaper advertisement saying, "Please stop ordering." However, we have no copies left in our archives. We will exchange the March issue if returned to our office by July 10th with either the July or August issue of this magazine.
<19>
