Special Column for Female Students
						Myeongchogcho (Nameless Grass)

After all the friends have left and the bothersome cleaning is all done, the school becomes quiet like an empty house, and only the duty students gather and sit, chattering nonsense.

甲: "Hey, which do you like better, male teachers or female teachers?"

乙: "Of course, female teachers are more kind and considerate, so I like them better..."

甲: "What about you?"

乙: "Everyone feels the same way, what's the point of asking?"

丁: "That's right! There's no need to even ask that."

甲: "Then, among the female teachers, which do you like better, the married ones or the unmarried ones?"

乙: "Well... I've never thought about that."

甲: "What about you?"

丙: "I like the unmarried ones better!"

甲: "Why?"

丙: "Married teachers are bound to have household matters on their minds. Unmarried teachers are always on the students' side because they are single, and they pour all their affection only on the students, so aren't they more kind and affectionate?"

乙: "But, it's the married teachers who really understand the students' situations and truly care for the students. Since they've experienced marriage, they sometimes tell us what married life is like and what men are like, and they sincerely care for us and worry about our future, don't they? Honestly, and this is a bad thing to say, but some of the unmarried teachers are hysterical, so they may seem sensible at times, but they get angry often."

丙: "That's the same with married teachers. <125> It seems like married teachers care more when they care for the students, but if they have disagreements with their husbands, they frown even at the students and find everything annoying. When they are bored, they brag about their husbands' businesses in the classroom, or knit their babies' socks or gloves in the office, isn't that right? Anyway, it's true that the circumstances or mood of a teacher's married life affects the office or classroom even a tiny bit."

丁: "That's true."

甲: "Why are there so many hysterical unmarried teachers?"

丙: "That's a bit hard to say..." "Hahahahahaha"

When the conversation gets to this point, they become hungry, pick up their books, and quietly walk down the quiet stairs. <126>

乙 『누구던지 그럿치 무러보아 무얼 하니.』
丁 『그럼! 그런 건 무러 볼 것도 업는 거야. 』
甲 『그러면 녀선생님 중에는 싀집 간 이가 더 조흐냐? 싀집 안간 이가 더 조흐냐』
乙 『글세…그런 생각 못해 보앗는 걸 』
甲 『너는?』
丙 『나는 싀집 안간 이가 더 좃타!』
甲 『웃재서』
丙 『싀집 간 선생님은 암만해도 집 안 일에 마음이 껄니지 무어냐. 싀집 안간 이는 자긔가 처녀닛가 언제던지 학생들 편이 되지 정을 학생들께만 포 쏫아 주는 마콤 더 친절하고 정이 붓는 것 아니냐?』
乙 『그럿치만 정말 학생들의 사정을 잘 알아주고 정말 학생들을 앗겨 주기는 싀집 간 선생님이란다. 자긔가 우선 싀집을 가 보앗스닛가 싀집사리란 엇던 것이고 남자란 엇던 것이라는 것을 갓금갓금 이약이 해주시면서 진정으로 우리를 앗겨주고 장래를 걱정해 주시는 것 아니냐? 참말 말이지 낫븐 말이지만 처녀선생님 중에는 히스테리가 잇서서 상략할 때는 상략스러워도 성을 자조 내신단다.』
丙 『그거야 싀집 간 선생님도 마찬가지＜125＞지 싀집 간 선생님이 학생을 앗길 때는 더 앗기는 것 갓지만 자긔 남편과 불화한 일이 잇스면 학생들에게 까지 얼골을 찡그리고 만사를 귀찬아 하지 자미가 나는 때는 교실에서 남편의 사업 자랑을 하거나 그럿치 안으면 사무실에서도 아기의 양말이나 장갑만 짯는 이도 잇슬 것 아니냐? 엇젯던지 결혼생활을 하는 선생님은 그 가뎡의 형편이나 긔분이 눈꼽짝이 만치라도 사무실에나 교실에 영향하는 것이 사실이지 무어냐.』
丁 『그건 사실은 사실이야』
甲 『처녀 선생님 중에 히스테리가 만타는 것은 무슨 까닭일가?』
