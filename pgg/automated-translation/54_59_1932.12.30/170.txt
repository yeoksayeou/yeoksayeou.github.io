別乾坤 第59號
1932년 12월 30일
臨時自殺 (Temporary Suicide)
金奎澤 (Kim Kyu-taek)
文藝其他 (Literary Miscellaneous)

The whole world is in a slump. It's impossible to live.
There's nothing to eat, and I'm drowning in debt.
Damn it, I'm going to commit suicide.
With this resolution,
as the father was about to commit suicide,
his son, shedding tears, dissuades him.
It is right for a son to dissuade his father from committing suicide.
However, the reason for dissuading him is quite plausible:
"Father! If you commit suicide, I will have to shoulder the debt, so please don't do that, but rather commit temporary suicide (臨時自殺)."
