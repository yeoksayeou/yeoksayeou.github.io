Song of the Ama, Jeju Island Folk Song

Iheo Island, Iheo Island
Iheo, Iheo, Iheo Island
If I go to Iheo Island, tears fall
Don't talk about Iheo, just go

If you go crying, others will cry
Go with a song on the main road (大路)
When leaving, it seems like going in glory (榮華)
But returning, it is like a flower mat (花旃)

The waves (波) of the island are worrying even when they cry
The waves of the sea are worrying even when they cry
Hey, younger sibling, let the water overflow and play
I'm worried that you might have an illness (病)

Worried, I fall into sleep
Not even knowing when day breaks
On the day the bird flies, my mother comes
On the month the bird flies, will my father come?

The boat house (船家) bride is pitiful
Like a duck, sitting in the rapids
Waiting for the wind to blow the cloth

On the day of birth, where will rain fall?
The rain falling on Jeju Yeongsan (濟州瀛山)
Is like the tears of a sad young woman
Setting sail on a tearful swamp (沼)
Let's row away with sighs

When my mother was thirty and carrying me
What seaweed (甘藿) from which sea did she eat?
Every time the wind blows, every time the waves crash
Saying she couldn't live because she was twisted
The vast sea of Yeonghae
On what day will all this labor end? <3>

Named Entities

海女의 노래, 濟州島民謠
이허도(島)러라 이허도러라
이허 이허 이허도러라
이허도 가면 나 눈물난다
이허말은 말어서 가라

울며 가면 남이나 운다
대로(大路)한길 노래로 가라
갈때보니 영화(榮華)로 가도
도라올 때 화전(花旃)이러라

섬(島)의 절(波)은 울어도 근심
바다절도 울어도 근심
한아 동생 물넘겨 노하
벼(病)이 잇슬 근심이러라

근심겨워 안이든 잠은
날이 샌줄 몰나라 한다
새는날에 어머니오며
새는 달에는 아버지오랴

뱃집(船家)각시는 적실해라
오리가티 여울에 안저
베갈바람 기다리더라

벳난 날에 어데 비오리
제주영산(濟州瀛山) 드리운 빗발
설은 情女 눈물이러라
눈물 소(沼)에 배란세워
한숨으로 즈어가세

서른 어머니 날 배힐 적에
어느 바다의 메역(甘藿)을 먹어
바람일적 절일적마다
구을니여 못사라서라
영해바다 가업는 바다
어느날 온갓 이라살이＜3＞
