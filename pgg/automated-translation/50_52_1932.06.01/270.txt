號外의 號外 (Extra Edition's Extra Edition)

The Extra Edition's Extra Edition

The Chungang Ilbo (中央日報), which had been problematic since its resumption, has been suspended again due to management difficulties after only a few months.

Although the name was changed several times from Sidae Ilbo to Chungoe Ilbo, and from Chungoe Ilbo back to Chungang Ilbo, the management difficulties remained the same. Ultimately, the problem lies not in the name, but in the character of the manager or financial issues. Whoever manages it in the future, I hope they will at least leave the name as it is.

A key figure at the Chosun Ilbo was imprisoned due to an incident, and a new management team was formed. Shortly after the new president, Yu Chint'ae (兪鎭泰), took office, he was sued for fraud and embezzlement in connection with the Min Pyŏngju (閔丙疇) case.

It is impossible to know how that case will turn out, but it seems that the position of president of the Chosun Ilbo is not a very good one.

Speaking of the president of the Chosun Ilbo, former president Shin Sŏku (申錫雨) was unable to see his expiration date for a long time, but these days, perhaps because he is so bored, he occasionally visits the Kyŏngsu Automobile Company (京水自動車會社), which he manages.

It must be disappointing to be deprived of the plush cushion of the newspaper president's seat and to occupy a corner of the hard-floored automobile company, but the "Teacher," "Old Man" calls from the pretty bus girls who call out "all right, stop" might be some consolation.

Ubo Min T'aewŏn (牛步 閔泰瑗), a veteran in the newspaper industry, went to Manchuria (滿洲行) recently. According to reports, his trip to Manchuria was not just an ordinary sightseeing trip, but he went with a plan to publish a new newspaper, and that plan is progressing steadily and will be realized soon. <36>

The emergence of that newspaper is expected to create ripples in the Korean newspaper industry, causing personnel changes in each newspaper, and even important reporters from certain newspapers are said to be restless. Details will be in the next extra edition. <37>

號外의 號外
속간 당시부터 말성만턴 중앙일보(中央日報)는 불과 몃달에 경영곤난으로 또다시 정간이 되엿다.

시대일보를 중외일보로 중외일보를 또다시 중앙일보로 몃번식 명의를 곳첫스나 경영곤난은 맛찬가지인즉 결국 문제는 명의란 그것보다 경영자의 인물문제 또는 재정문제인즉 이 압흐로는 누가 다시 경영하던지 제발 명의만은 그대로 두엇스면 조흘 것 갓다.

조선일보의 중요간부가 불상사로 감금이 되고 새간부가 조직되더니 새사장 유진태(兪鎭泰)씨는 취임한지 미구에 민병주(閔丙疇)사건으로 사긔횡령(詐欺橫領)의 고소를 당하얏다.

그 사건이 엇지 귀결될지 알 수는 업거니와 조선일보의 사장 자리도 그리 조흔 자리는 못되는 모양

조선일보 사장 말이 낫스니 말이지 전사장 신석우(申錫雨)씨는 한참동안 연명을 볼 수 업더니 요즘에는 너무 심심한 탓인지 그의 경영하는 경수자동차회사(京水自動車會社)로 각금 나온다고 한다.

푹신푹신한 우단방석의 신문사장 자리를 빼앗기고 딱딱한 장판바닥의 자동차회사 방구석 차지를 하게된 것은 섭섭한 일이지만은 오라이 스텁을 부르는 어엽분 뻐쓰껄들의 선생님 령감님 소리에 적이나 위안될 듯 하다.

신문계에 로장(老匠)인 우보 민태원(牛步 閔泰瑗)씨는 요전에 만주행(滿洲行)을<36> 하얏다더니 전하는 말을 드른즉 그의 만주행은 보통의 유람이 안이요 모신문의 발행할 계홱을 가지고 간 것인데 그 계홱이 착착 진전되야 미구에 실현하게 되리라 한다.
