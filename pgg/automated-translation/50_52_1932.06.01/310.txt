1932년 06월 01일
말 잘쓰는 며누리 (A Daughter-in-law Good at Using Words)
開豐 玄錫夏
잡저 (Miscellany)

Mother-in-law: "Hey, from now on, don't use extra words (겻말)."

Daughter-in-law: "Are extra words (엽말) extra words (겻말)?"

The mother-in-law, annoyed:

"You little thing, I told you not to use extra words (것말), and you're using extra words (겻말) again?"

And she flicked her forehead.

Daughter-in-law: "You're taking away chariot, cannon, soldiers and starting by hitting my face?"

Mother-in-law: "... ..." <45>
