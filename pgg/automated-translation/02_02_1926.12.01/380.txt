Customs Cartoon, Modern Mother-in-law and Old-fashioned Daughter-in-law

1. What misfortune is this, that I, an old-fashioned daughter-in-law, must serve a modern mother-in-law and complain: "Oh my! Other people's mothers-in-law, when they are busy morning and evening, even watch the children and tend the brazier for the stew, but what sin did I commit to meet a modern mother-in-law who, morning and evening, only puts on makeup and washes her face like a kisaeng (妓生) ... Is she trying to look young by applying powder at her old age? She goes around selling tickets to other people's social gatherings day and night, and does she think that's being modern? ... ..." <112>
