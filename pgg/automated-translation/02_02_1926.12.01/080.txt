各人各色 (Everyone is Different)

His preferences (嗜好) are something no one can quite figure out. He says that for an old man like me, the favorite spot is the warmest part of the floor (아름목) near the hearth, but that's just his usual playful talk. Some say he likes Go (바둑) and reading, but those are all things from his past boyhood days; in reality, the only particular preference (嗜好) he has now (只今) is smoking tobacco in a long bamboo pipe (長竹). Country Christians (基督敎人) who haven't reached enlightenment might hear this and be shocked, saying, "Mr. Yi Sangjae smokes tobacco? But alcohol and tobacco are forbidden for Christian believers!" However, he smokes it well without any aversion (忌嬚). His long bamboo pipe (長竹통) never has a moment for the fire to go out. It's a good thing Heaven (天堂) doesn't exist; if it did, it would be smoked black by his tobacco smoke and become a veritable Bronze Heaven (烏銅天堂). Also, a characteristic of his is that he's always playing pranks (괘사) and making people laugh whenever he speaks. Since his work is widely known to the people of the world, I will stop here and lay down my brush (擱筆).<24>

**Summary**

This short article from 1926 humorously describes the personality and habits of Yi Sangjae (李商在), a prominent elderly figure. It focuses on his particular fondness for smoking a long bamboo pipe, noting this habit might surprise some Christians for whom tobacco was often proscribed. The author highlights Yi's playful nature, wit, and penchant for jokes, concluding that his public contributions are already well-known. For historians, the piece offers a valuable contemporary, personal glimpse into Yi Sangjae, an important independence activist and social leader, beyond his formal roles. It reflects societal attitudes towards personal habits, religion (specifically Christianity in Korea at the time), and public figures during the Japanese colonial period, showcasing a lighter, more anecdotal style of reporting.

各人各色
그의 嗜好는 누가 잘 알수 업다. 그는 나와 같은 늙은이의 嗜好는 땃듯한 아름목 구석이 제일이라고 하지만은 그것은 그의 보통으로 괘사 부리는 말이다. 또 바둑도 조와하고 독서도 조와한다는 사람도 잇스나 그것은 다 過去 소년시대의 일이요 실상 只今에 유독 기호라는 것은 긴 長竹에 담배 피우는 것이다. 解脫못한 시골의 基督敎人들은 이말을 드르면 기독교 신자에게는 酒草가 禁物인데 李商在씨 담배라니 하고 놀날것이지만. 그러나 그 눈 아모 忌嬚업시 잘 피운다. 언제든지 長竹통에 불거질 틈이 엄다. 天堂이 엄기에 망정이지 만일에 천당이 있다하면 그의 담배연긔에 끄실너서 아주 烏銅天堂이 될 것이다. 그리고 그는 말할 때 마다 괘사 잘 부리고 웃키기 잘 하는 것이 특색이다. 그의 일은 세상 사람이 만히아는 터이닛가 그만 하야 두고 擱筆한다.＜24＞
