Company Announcement

It has been seven years since this company was founded, fighting and struggling against all odds. The fact that we have built such a foundation today is solely due to the painstaking efforts (苦心慘憺) of all employees of this company and the enthusiastic support of our comrades throughout the world.

After the *Kaebyŏk* journal unfortunately suffered the misfortune of being banned from publication (發行禁止의 厄), the general public may have felt that the company's business management was temporarily frustrated, but the company has not changed in any way and is determined to continue and expand its business. Therefore, we have held a general meeting of employees this time and have decided to organize a board of directors to further consolidate the foundation of the company and to expedite affairs. We have selected seven directors as follows, and we earnestly hope that all comrades throughout the world will further assist the company's business so that it can achieve its intended purpose.

I Tŏnhwa, Kim Kit’ŏn, Min Yŏngsun, Ch’a Sangch’an, Pang Chŏnghwan, Pak Talsŏng, Chŏn Chunsŏng

December, Pyŏngin Year

社告
본사가 창립된지 7개 星霜에 모든 것과 싸우고 싸워나와 오늘 이만한 토대를 쌋케된 것은 다만 본사 사원일동의 苦心慘憺한 결정물이요 만천하 동지여러분의 열열하신 응원의 덕택이라고 하지 아니할 수 업슴니다.
불행히 『開闢』 지가 발행금지의 厄을 당한 후, 본사의 사업경영이 일시 좌절된 듯한 감을 일반인사에게 주엇슬넌지 모르지만 본사로서는 하등 변함이 업시 여전히 나가며 사업을 확장하기로 결심이외다. 그리하야 금번에 사원회를 열고 한층더 본사의 기초를 공고히 하고 사무를 機敏히 하기 위하야 이사제를 조직하고 이사 7인을 좌와 여히 선정하엿사오니
만천하 동지 諸씨도 더욱 본사의 사업을 원조하야 소기의 목적을 달하도록 하시기를 간졀히 바라나이다.
李敦化, 金起田, 閔泳純, 車相瓚, 方定煥, 朴達成, 田畯成
丙寅 12월
