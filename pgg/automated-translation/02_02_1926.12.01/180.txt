STUDENT CARTOON SUBMISSIONS WANTED

Whether you are a middle school student, female student, or professional school student, please send us cartoons (manhwa, 漫畵) that are humorous (滑稽) or satirical (諷剌). If they are good, we will publish them in the pages of *Pyŏlgŏn’gon* (別乾坤) and present a modest remuneration (薄謝). Please apply in great numbers. <49>

**Summary**

This brief notice is an open call for cartoon submissions from various types of students (middle school, female, professional school) for publication in the magazine *Pyŏlgŏn’gon*. The magazine encourages humorous or satirical works and promises publication along with a small payment for selected entries. For historians, this article highlights the magazine's effort to engage with the student population and cultivate reader participation during the colonial period. It also points to the growing presence and perceived importance of cartoons (manhwa) as a medium for commentary and entertainment in 1920s Korea. The specific mention of different student categories offers a glimpse into the educational structure and target demographics of the time.
