Submissions Welcome

Local color! This is entrusted to the pages of our regional readers. Submit freely, just as things are. Throughout the thirteen provinces, in every nook and cranny, there must be many strange customs of all kinds, peculiar habits, and astonishing historical legends. People are good too – write about them. Anyone should write and send in as much as they want about their own region. However, you must heed these few requests and limitations.

1.  Do not write like a stale introductory text; write so it can be read stylishly and with interest.
2.  Write roughly 60 lines, with 15 characters per line of manuscript paper.
3.  Write with clear separation between phrases/clauses.
4.  It is even better to attach things like photographs.

Editing (添削) and selection (取捨) are entirely at the discretion of this company (本社), but we will offer a small token of appreciation (薄謝) to those whose submissions are published.<87>

**Summary**

This editorial note from *Pyŏlgŏn'gon* magazine invites readers across Korea's thirteen provinces to submit articles showcasing their local culture, customs, legends, and notable figures. It sets guidelines for submission style (engaging, not dry), length (~60 lines), clarity, and encourages including photographs. The magazine reserves editorial rights and offers a small payment for published contributions. For historians, this article is interesting as it reflects a conscious effort by a popular Korean magazine during the colonial period to document and disseminate diverse regional identities and folklore. It highlights an interest in preserving local particularities and potentially fostering a sense of national cultural tapestry, even under colonial rule, by relying on contributions from ordinary people across the peninsula.

投稿歡迎
地方色! 이것은 地方讀者의 폐지로 맛겨둔다. 멋대로 잇는대로 投稿하라. 13도 방방곡곡에는 가지각색의 이상한 풍속, 야릇한 習慣, 駭怪한 역사 전설이 만흘 것이다. 인물도 좃타 그것을 쓰란 말이다. 누구나 자기지방의 것을 얼마던지 써 보내라. 그러나 요만한 所請과 制限은 드러주어야 한다.
1. 진부한 소개문 가티 쓰지 말고 좀 멋잇시 흥미잇게 읽을수 잇도록 쓸 일.
1. 원고지 1행 15자 60행 내외로 쓸 일.
1. 구절을 똑똑 떼여서 쓸 일.
1. 사진 가튼 것을 첨부하여 보내는 것은 더욱 조흔 일.
添削과 取捨는 본사에서 일임하되 게재되는 분에 대하야는 薄謝를 드리기로 함니다.＜87＞
