Explanation of the Cover Picture

The picture on the cover of this issue of the magazine depicts a beautiful woman playing the pipa (琵琶) from about 1,500 years ago, during the Three Kingdoms period. Please note how different her hairstyle and clothing are from today's customs. <105>

表紙그림說明
