New Myth "Before Creation"
						Yare

One day, an old man named Muheon翁 came to me, begging me to tell him just one story. I originally didn't know how to tell stories and had no stories to tell, so I stubbornly refused. But the old man kept begging me to tell him just one story. Unable to bear his persistence, I got angry and said,

"Hey, old man - even if a person has a good temper, there are limits. Isn't there a saying that even the P'yŏngan governor (平安監司) would quit if he didn't want to do it? What are you going to do if someone doesn't want to tell a story and you keep begging?"

Then Muheon翁 smiled and, approaching me in a subdued manner, said this:

"No, it's not that I'm asking you for an ordinary story. Isn't there a secret story that you've known alone in your heart your whole life? That word, that secret word, I'm just asking to hear a little of it. Even if my body is torn into ten pieces, I can't leave without hearing that story. Maybe I'll even commit suicide."

It wasn't that there wasn't a secret story that I hadn't told anyone my whole life. The reason I couldn't tell this secret story to others wasn't for any other reason than that the words were so enormous and <168> grand that if others heard them, everyone would easily think it was a lie. I was afraid of being called a liar even with such a good story, so I hadn't told it until now. But Muheon翁 had heard this story somewhere and was asking me to hear this real story that seemed like a lie. So I retorted to Muheon翁 like this:

"Hey, old man, if I speak a little, unable to resist your request, you mustn't think of it as a lie if you hear it. If you even think of it as a lie in your heart, your life will be gone. You must be sure to understand it as the truth, really. It's truly awe-inspiring. It's not that the words themselves are awe-inspiring, but if you think of these words as a lie, an accident will definitely happen from that day on. Even Hwata and P'yŏnjak (華陀 扁鵲) wouldn't be able to cure it, so that's why I'm saying this. That's why I can't say these words carelessly. Do you still want me to speak? Answer carefully."

Muheon翁 listened to my words, then laughed again, saying,

"Hey, stop saying such harsh things and tell me quickly. How could a guy who's about to commit suicide because he can't hear the story die from hearing it? Even if I die, isn't it better to die having heard it than to die without hearing it? My heart is so heavy I can't bear it. Tell me quickly. Quickly, quickly? I'm about to die. Quickly, quickly?"

I was about to take out the story. But I was afraid of catching an innocent person if Muheon翁 heard it as a lie, so I decided to leave out the enormous words and only tell the story that would be most easily accepted by the ears of modern people. Still, it was terrifying, so I decided to slowly tell Muheon翁 the preface to the story first.

"Hey, old man Muheon翁, this story is neither a story of now, nor an ancient myth, nor a future story. It is truly a ground-breaking (破天荒) story that transcends time and space, past and present (古往今來). You must first listen carefully to the preface to understand the essence of the story. Old man, have you heard of the Buddhist theory of reincarnation (輪回轉生說)? But even the Buddhist theory of reincarnation is like a religious myth, so we can't know it as a fact. Let's start with the story using the eternal recurrence theory of the great philosopher Nietzsche. Nietzsche's philosophy establishes a view of the universe and life based on the principle that the universe eternally recurs. To put it simply, no matter how infinite the universe may be, it seems infinite to our small eyes, but from the perspective of the infinite universe itself, infinity is like our lifetime or a single year, constantly rotating and recurring as the universe exists. For example, this place, this time, this shape, this action that we are sitting in right now can recur at any time in eternal infinity. This scene of you and I telling stories will also happen again in the exact same way in eternal infinity."

Muheon翁 listened to these words and, as if some emotion had arisen in his heart, nodded his head and <169> looked at my face, saying,

"That's absolutely right. It's truly so." I continued, "So, what I mean is, the universe and the world that we think of today, when seen from the perspective of this infinite universe, have repeated themselves countless times. I don't know how many eons it has been since this universe was created, but before the creation, there were countless universes like this universe and this world. So, the fact that you and I are sitting here today telling this story must have happened countless times in the universe before this universe was created. So, the story I'm about to tell is not a story after the creation of this universe. I'm telling the story of the universe before this universe was created. Hey- old man, stay focused. Do you understand? In other words, from the perspective of the theory of evolution, this universe began with stellar nebulae, but the story I'm about to tell is the story of the universe before stellar nebulae, before the cycle of trying to create this universe again. Do you understand?"

Muheon翁 listened to these words, his mouth wide open and his eyes wide, saying,

"That's truly enormous. But how can you know about what happened back then?"

"Didn't I warn you from the very beginning? It's easy to think of these words as a lie, and if you think of them as a lie, something terrible will happen, so be careful. If you already have doubts in your mind, I'll stop telling this story."

"No. How could that be? I'm just saying it's enormous. Who said it was a lie? I'll definitely listen as if it's the truth, so don't doubt and tell me. It's truly interesting."

"Old man, it must be hundreds of millions of years ago. Before the universe we live in now was created, that is, before the stellar nebula period that scholars talk about now. At that time, this universe had completely evolved, and people's lives were very different from now. On the surface, all the solar systems and stars seem the same as today, but people's lives were much grander than today.

Now- I can't tell you everything in detail, so I'll tell you specific events one by one. Huh- what should I start with? Should I talk about transportation first?

Hey, old man Muheon翁- you've ridden trains, ships, streetcars, cars, and even seen airplanes, haven't you? You can see how much this world has progressed. When we think about what happened a hundred or two hundred years ago and compare it to today's world, it's unbelievable, isn't it? But, old man, with this much civilization, it's impossible to compare it to the world I'm talking about now. Since I started talking about transportation first, let's talk about it from there. As far as we can see in our world, airplanes are the most amazing, aren't they? <170> But in that world (the world before creation), that's nothing. It's not even as good as children riding wooden horses in the yard. In that world, such dull machines as airplanes are nothing more than children's toys.

To give an example of the transportation machines used in that world, there was something called the radio car (라듸오車). It's an enormous thing that allowed you to travel around this universe. You could ride the radio car and go to the Big Dipper, the Southern Dipper Six Stars, and even go to the Milky Way to watch Kyŏnu (견우) and Chiknyŏ (직녀) get married."

Muheon翁 listened to these words, raised his hands and shook them left and right, turned his face and looked at me straight on, saying,

"Hey, just let me say one thing and then continue talking. First of all, what is this radio car? Does it ride on the air like an airplane, flying with the vibrations of the air?"

"No. No. An air car can only run where there is air. How can it run in the airless universe? A radio car is not a car that rides on the air, but an enormous car that rides on electricity and escapes even millions or billions of miles in a short time with the vibrations of electricity."

"Hey, old man, you've heard of the radio that's been so popular in recent years, haven't you? You can hear sounds from London or New York with just one hole like a trumpet, without any lines or connections. The reason for this is that the universe is full of electrical energy, which is why wireless telegraphy was created using this. Just as ordinary telegraphy sends electricity through wires, wireless telegraphy vibrates the electricity in the air to communicate with each other. Just as wireless telegraphy can be done wirelessly, wireless telephone can also be done wirelessly, can't it? The radio was created by extending this principle. In other words, the radio uses the propagation of electrical waves in the air to send sound. Even the words that you and I are saying now will go everywhere in the world. Not only in the world, but everywhere in the Great Three Thousand Worlds (大三千世界). The reason why we don't hear the sound directly is that our ears can't gather the waves into one hole, so we make a machine like a trumpet to hear it. The radio car was made using this propagation of electrical waves in the air. But the radio car is not as big as an airplane. It's made simply in the shape of a bird's wing, and if you attach it to your armpit, it will fly away. It's like the words you hear in fairy tales, with wings sprouting from your armpits. When you fly, it looks like a person with wings floating in the air like a bird, but it's faster than an arrow, and when it really runs fast, it becomes as fast as air. The reason why it's so fast is that the radio car is designed to react to electrical waves in the air at will, and the wire machine in the car body advances into the air according to the electricity, so it can go anywhere in the world in a short time like a radio. It can also easily reach the lunar world or the Martian world in just a few days. People in that world traveled the Great Three Thousand Worlds (大三千世界) in these radio cars more safely and easily than we now travel around the world by train."

Muheon翁 listened to these words and, feeling very refreshed, clapped his knees with his hands, saying,

"Hey, just listen to me for a moment and then continue talking. But could people in that world live without breathing air?"

"Yes, they could. To compare, there are amphibians among animals, aren't there? Just as amphibians can live in water and on land without water, people in that world could live both in the air and in the ether (에-톌) without air because their physiological bodies had evolved to the end."

"Then, did that world also have a distinction called a country?"

"No. In that world, the distinction between your country and my country had completely disappeared. Wouldn't that be the case? Think about it. People in that world knew that this small Earth was like a neighborhood, so there would be no need for multiple countries in one neighborhood. Or there would be no need for a country. In other words, the lives of people in that world were such that our lives could not even be imagined. Now, should I tell you a story about a marriage in the Martian system as an example?"

"In the world we live in now, if an Eastern person marries a Western person, wouldn't everyone think it's a strange thing? But in that world, the Earth was scaled down like a neighborhood, so it wasn't just that people in the same neighborhood married each other. There were many cases where people from this planet married people from that planet. (To be continued) <172>

新神話 「開闢以前」
						夜雷
하루는 無何翁이라는 늙은이가 와서 나다려 꼭 이약이 한마대만 하야 달나고 졸읍니다. 그래서 나는 본래 이약이를 할 줄도 몰우고 할 이약이도 업노라고 자바떼이닛가 늙은이는 그래도 한마듸만 하야달나고 엇더케도 졸으는지 성화에 견딀 수가 업서서 나는 성을 내며
「여보 령감- 사람이 비위가 조와도 분수가 잇지 平安監司도 저 하기 실으면 고만둔다는 말이 안잇소. 남이 이약이 하기 실타는 것을 그러케 졸으면 엇저잔 말이요.」
한즉 無何翁은 우서가면서 축은축은하게 달여부터 가지고 이런 말을 함니다.
「안이요. 내가 당신에게 보통 이약이를 하야 달나는 게 안이라 당신의 가슴속에 평생 혼자 알고 잇는 비밀 한 이약이가 잇지 안소. 그 말삼 그 비밀한 말삼, 그것 조곰만 들여 달나는 말이요. 몸이 열조각이 나도 그 이약이를 듯지 안코는 갈 수가 업소 엇저면 자살이라도 할넌지 몰우겟소.」
안인 것이 안이라 내에게는 평생 남에게 말하지 안는 비밀 한 이약이 한마대가 잇겟다. 이 비밀 한 이약이를 내가 남한테, 하지 못하는 것은 달은 연고가 안이라 말이 하도 엄청나고＜168＞ 굉장해서 남이 들으면 백이면 백이 다 거즛말이라 하기 쉬음으로 그러케 조흔 이약이를 가지고도 남한데 거즛말장이 소리를 들을가 겁이 나서 여제것 못하든 것이엿다. 그런데 無何翁은 어대서 이 말을 들엇든지 나한테 이 거즛말 가튼 實말을 들여 달나는 것이엇다. 그래서 나는 無何翁에게 이러케 따젓다.
「여보 영감 그러면 내가 영감의 간청에 못익여 말은 조곰 하겟는데 영감이 만약 이 말을 듯다가 거즛말로 알면, 안됨니다. 만약 마음으로라도 거즛말이거니 하면 영감의 생명이 업서지는 것이니까 꼭 實말로만 알고 듯겟소. 단단히 알아챌이우, 정말 위엄합니다. 말 자체가 위엄하다는 것이 안이라 이 말을 거즛말로 아는 날이면 그날부터 영낙업시 탈이 납니다. 그 탈은 화태편작이라도 곳칠 길이 업스니까 하는 말이오. 그래서 내가 이 말을 함부로 못하는 것이오. 그래도 말을 하란 말이오. 단단히 말슴하시오.」
無何翁은 내 말을 듯더니 다시 허허하고 웃고 나서
「여보 딱한 말슴 마시고 어서 들여주오. 말을 듯지 못해서 자살하겟는 놈이 들어서 죽을 리가 잇소. 설사 죽는다 할지라도 듯고 죽는 것이 안듯고 죽너니보다 나은 것이 안이오. 가슴이 답답하야 견딀 수 업소. 어서 들여주오. 어서 어서 금방 죽을 디경이오. 어서 어서?」
나는 이약이를 꺼집어 내겟다. 그러나 無何翁이 거즛말로 들으면 생사람 하나 잡을가 무서워서 엄청난 말을 다 빼여바리고 그중에서 제일 현대사람의 귀에 들어갈만한 이약이만 하기로 작뎡하엿다. 그래도 무시무시해서 나는 無何翁에게 이약이의 서문부터 천천히 들여주기로 하엿다.
「여보 無何翁영감 이 이약이는 지금 이약이도 안이오. 녯날 신화도 안이오. 장래의 이약이도 안임니다. 古往今來라는 시간 공간을 떠나서 진실로 破天荒의 이약입니다. 이것은 먼저 서문을 잘 들어야 이약이의 본체를 이해할 수 잇슴니다. 영감 불교의 輪回轉生說을 들엇는지요. 그러나 불교의 윤회전생설도 종교적 신화 갓흔 것이니까 사실로 알 수 업지요. 「니체」라는 대철학자의 영원윤회설을 가지고 먼저 이약이를 합시다. 「니체」의 철학은이 우주는 영원히 윤회한다는 이치로 우주관 인생관을 세운 것인데 그 뜻으로 말삼하면 아무리 무궁한 우주라 할지라도 우리 조고만 눈으로 보면 무궁하지만은 무궁한 우주 자체로 보면 무궁이란 것이 우리의 한해나 또는 일생이나 가태서 한 이치가 돌우돌우 윤회하야 가면서 이 우주가 존재하야 잇다는 것임니다. 가령 우리가 지금 안저 잇는 이 처소 이 시간 이 모양 이행동이 영원무궁의 새이에는 어느 때에든지 다시 윤회할 수 잇다는 것임니다. 영감하고 나하고 이약이하는 이 모양도 영원무궁한 시간속에는 이와 꼭 가튼 사실이 다시 올이라는 것입니다.」
無何翁은 이 말을 듯고 마음에 무슨 감동이 일어낫든지 고개을 꺼덕꺼덕하더니＜169＞
「참말 올흔 말삼이올시다. 진실로 그러치요.」 하고 내 얼골을 처다 보앗다. 나는 다시 말을 니어 「그러니까 말이올시다. 우리가 오늘날 생각하는 우주라든지 이 세상이라는 것은 이 무궁한 우주로 보면 멧萬劫인지 몰우게 꼭 가튼 사실이 繰返되고 繰返되얏다는 말임니다. 이 字宙가 개벽된 지가, 멧억만년인지는 몰우지만은 개벽되기 이전의 우주에도, 이 우주의 세상과 가튼 세상이 멧만겁 잇섯다는 것임니다. 그러니까 영감이나 내가 오늘 안자 이러한 이약이를 하든 사실도 이 우주 개벽되기 이전의 우주에서 멧번재 지나갓슬 것이올시다. 그러니까 내가 지금 말하고저 하는 이약이는 이 우주 개벽 이후의 이약이가 안이오. 이 우주 개벽되기 이전의 우주의 이약이를 하는 것임니다. 네- 영감 정신 찰이시요. 알아듯겟소. 다시 말하면 이 우주는 進化學의 이치로 보아 星霧로부터 시작이 되엿다는 것인데 지금 내가 할 이약이는 星霧 이전에 먼저 우주가 시작하야 가지고 이 우주를 다시 맨드러 노흐려 하는 윤회기 이전 이약이라는 말삼이올시다. 알아 듯겟소?」
無何翁은 이 말을 듯더니 입이 떡 벌이고 눈이 둥그래지며
「참 엄청남니다. 그런데 그때 일을 엇더케 알까요.」
「그러니까 내가 첫 번부터 당부하지 안습데까? 이 말을 거즛말로 알기 쉽고 거즛말로 알면 큰 탈이 날터이니 조심하라고 영감 마음에 벌서부터 의심이 나면 나는 이 말 고만 두고 말겟소.」
「안이오. 그럴 理이가 잇소. 거저 엄청난다는 말이지요. 뉘가 거즛말이라고 햇서요. 꼭 實말로 들을 것이니 의심말고 하야 주시요. 참 자미남니다.」
