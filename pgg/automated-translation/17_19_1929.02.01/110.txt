The Way of the Married Couple
						Cho Chae-ho

A person who goes against the way of humanity will always feel the emptiness of life. The lives of old maids, old bachelors, and widowers, and when we look into their hearts, there is much to pity. They are like travelers walking alone in a desolate desert. No matter how far they go, it is a desert, and all they have as a companion all day long is their own shadow. Old maids, old bachelors, and widowers each have their own sad stories, but because they defy the great affairs of humans and go against the path of people, they naturally feel the greatest emptiness given by nature. The old saying goes, "The union of two surnames is the source of all things," so the meaningful union of men and women must be the great path of human life. Therefore, I am not advocating for love above all else, but I seem to realize anew the heart of the poet Browning when he concluded "Love is best" in "Love among the Ruins." Truly, those who have not tasted love and slept in the nest of love will find their lives lonely whether awake or asleep.

Lord Avebury said, "Love is the light and sunshine of life." Truly, it would not be an exaggeration to say that love is the glory of life. It is said that experiencing and embodying this "love" for the first time begins with mutual longing between the sexes. Physiologically and psychologically, when controlling the development of a person's mind and body, it is said that after all the conditions necessary to preserve one's individual life have developed, all the conditions necessary to preserve the species begin to grow. Children who have lived selfishly, subjectively, and self-centeredly throughout their infancy and youth begin to show clear sexual differences physically and to discover social consciousness and awareness of others mentally when they reach early adulthood. Young people whose hair begins to lengthen or whose mustaches begin to darken start to stir on behalf of the species, seeking the essence of the source of all blessings (萬福之源). <36> In doing so, they become aware of the existence of people other than themselves and learn the cooperation and fusion of social life, which is said to begin first with love for the opposite sex. Indeed, the saying that "the union of two surnames is the source of all blessings" seems all the more plausible.

Thus, men and women are born into this world with mutual longing and assistance as fundamental conditions. Therefore, the view that sexual desire and appetite are the warp and weft of life, from which all activities and conflicts begin, seems valid to some extent. How can people live as virgins and bachelors? Men and women, who are halves, unite to become complete people, with love, marriage, and then becoming a married couple. Therefore, it is true that young people devote themselves to discovering and refining their vocation, but it is also true that they dream of an ideal couple and an ideal family while seeking their spouse, which is truly a great event in human life. There may be exceptions like Herbert Spencer, who entrusted his lover to a friend, but I cannot help but sympathize with Basparus, who swam across Asia and Europe to meet his lover. It may be a bit of an exaggeration, but I think it would not be an exaggeration to say, "To live is to love."

Thus, young people must seek their spouse passionately, but how many truly become ideal couples by uniting with their spouse and praising love as supreme and marriage as the flower of life? Even if they are united as an ideal couple, how many manipulate their domestic life properly to have a truly married life that is renewed day by day and longed for day by day? Here, I would like to consider a few perspectives that men and women should pay attention to in the process of becoming a married couple and a few duties they should perform when living a domestic life as a married couple.

Love is not pleasure, not a game, not entertainment, not a riot. It is the spark (火花) where the most sincere personalities collide with all their heart and soul in search of an ideal spouse. Truly, love is never just sweet. It is also bitter, salty, and spicy. When viewing or reminiscing about love, it is infinitely sweet and nostalgic, but the period of actually being in love is truly painful and bitter. Therefore, I believe that those who riot in love with a hedonistic mindset never imagine marriage and start from a temporary ambition, and they are sex maniacs and figures who need to be separated from social life. Those who keep concubines while having a legal wife or women who open the back door while having a husband will surely never cease to shed tears of emptiness in their lives. However, there are not a few men and women who truly and genuinely love each other but ruin their marriage and go astray from the path of a married couple, falling into unhappiness. There may be various reasons, but I believe it mainly originates from a lack of established views on love, marriage, and the couple relationship. Therefore, I always offer this advice to men and women who are passionately in love. <37>
Starting from the passion of saying, "If it's not that person, I will live a single life and concentrate my life on my career," analyze your lover with a cold attitude, a sharp mind, and a fair perspective to consider whether they are compatible with you. First, examine their intelligence in terms of mental aspects to judge their superiority or inferiority, reflect their personality on yourself, look at their appearance, heredity, and health in terms of physical aspects, and then look at the presence or absence of their parents, siblings, and their individual personalities, family lineage, relatives, and wealth in their family, and finally, when examining their environment, look at their past relationships with the opposite sex, education level, and social status before comprehensively assessing their trustworthiness and financial capacity (not inherited wealth, but activity).

When they are compatible with you, accept them in your heart and reveal each other's strengths and weaknesses to associate with each other. This is truly what love should be. After seriously comparing and weighing each other again in this love life, it would be a truly beautiful human affair to get engaged and then move one step further to associate with each other before marrying at a considerable time (when they have economic independence). This kind of marriage is truly the flower of life. I believe that couples who start from this kind of marriage are truly renewed day by day and are truly missed day by day. As Lord Avebury said, "Love, true love, grows and deepens with time." Even if couples are formed from this kind of union, if they manipulate their lives improperly, they are not gods but emotional animals. I think there are not a few cases where they feel the ennui of marriage and, if they do wrong, are attracted to new curiosity, causing a rupture in a life that should be beautiful. Therefore, there are a few things that couples should observe with each other. First, they should create many opportunities to recall and reminisce about their first impression and their love days. If eating three meals a day is necessary to preserve the body, then I believe that recalling the old love with a thankful heart every day is the first source of marital harmony. In this sense, photo albums of the two from the days of love and each other's letters in the home will be materials for reviving love that is about to run dry until children are born. (2) Next, in addition to being careful about address and language to respect each other, as for men, they should not try to fabricate trust with a consistently sincere and reliable attitude but should hint at it to their wives, and as for women, they should have the capacity and attitude to row through any waves and storms with a soft, diligent, clean, and unhurried demeanor, being careful and striving to do all household matters as well as internal affairs before speaking with a smile. And (3) finally, husbands should have the warmth to praise their wives' unique efforts, and wives should have the attentiveness to prepare everything before their husbands speak. In other words, when a wife who always wears a Western hairstyle puts her hair up in a chignon (쪽), the husband should praise the good points, leaving out the bad ones, and the wife should be the kind of wife who finds a match when her husband takes out a cigarette. Couples who always observe even these three things can build a paradise on earth and create the driving force for all activities in a constantly new and always longed-for state of mind where spring breezes always blow. <38>

이와 가티 남자와 여자는 天性相思相助를 근본 조건으로 이 생에 난 것이다. 그럼으로 성욕과 식욕이 인생의 經緯가 되어 모든 활동과 갈등이 시작된다고 보는 견해도 어느 점으로 보아서 해당하다는 감이 일어난다. 이러한 사람으로서 엇지 처녀 총각 그대로 살어갈 수가 잇스리요. 반쪽인 남녀가 결합하야 완전한 사람이 되고저 연애가 잇고 결혼이 잇고 그리고 부부가 되는 것이다. 그럼으로 청춘은 자기의 천직을 발견하고 鍊磨하기에 전력을 쓰는 것도 사실이지마는 한편으로 자기의 배필을 구하야 이상적 부부, 이상적 가정을 꿈꾸는 것도 사실이며 참으로 인간에 한 大事일 것이다. 자기의 애인을 友人에 맷기인 Her-bert Spencer 가튼 예외도 잇겟지마는 애인을 맛나고저 亞細亞와 歐羅巴를 헤염질하야 건너단이든 Basparus의 심정을 참으로 동정하야 마지 안는다. 조곰 과장에 지나는 소리인지도 몰으겟지마는 To live is to love. 하고 하야도 과언이 안이라고 생각된다.
