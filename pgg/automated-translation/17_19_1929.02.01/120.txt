Male and Female Students Seen from the Lectern: How Are Men and Women Different from Their Student Days?
Men are born to be great thinkers, women are born to be great beauties.
Cho Chae-ho

Male and female students are the same in that they are students, but they differ in that they are men and women.
Therefore, if the fundamental reasons for the distinction between men and women are clearly defined physiologically or psychologically, the differences between men and women will naturally become clear. However, I do not intend to start from that reason and explain the similarities or differences between male and female students deductively or inductively. I will only bring out a few things that we usually witness on the surface without order.

Regardless of whether they are male or female, upper or lower grades, their lives seem to be within the scope of these four issues: study problems, ideological problems, love problems, and employment problems. The central issue seems to change according to age and grade level. Up to the first and second grades of middle school, all life seems to be concentrated on studying, regardless of gender. However, as they begin to encounter all the complex problems of this world and begin to feel wonder and doubt, ideological problems are conceived there, and as they begin to feel the impulse of the instinct of the species that age creates, love problems torment their hearts. This seems to be mostly around the third and fourth years of middle school. However, from the moment these ideological and love problems shock the lives of male and female students, the central issues of student life seem to gradually become different for men and women. Male students seem to be more interested in ideological problems with a self-righteous feeling than in love problems. As this grows, they think about themselves and contemplate their vocation, and the problem gradually changes into a career. However, female students seem to be more interested in love problems with a desire to be the greatest beauty in the world (in the modern sense) than in ideological problems. <39> As this grows, they think about themselves and become aware of the path of women, and the problem gradually changes into family. This tendency is most evident in the upper grades of middle school. Regardless of gender, students who look at their future more far-sightedly concentrate all their student life on the entrance exam to higher-level schools for a while, but for students who look at the real society, men seem to think about employment problems first, and women seem to dream about marriage first. Of course, this only points out the central issue, and it does not deny all other problems. This is the superficial and general tendency of male and female students that I have seen.
However, as a teacher, when I look at the school life of male and female students, there are many interesting issues and points that I want to inform the students themselves and their parents.

First, let's take a peek into their classrooms and look at their learning status. Female students accept all problems emotionally and swallow them without saying anything. If a professor teaches with an unpleasant feeling, they see it as it is. While watching the teacher's mood with an unpleasant expression, they learn as if they were treading on thin ice (如履薄氷的) swallowing what is given without knowing whether it is salty or bitter.

Of course, male students may have this to some extent, but it is more evident in female students. However, male students do not swallow all problems as they are without saying anything while watching the teacher's mood. They criticize and chew on it, and accept it while complaining that it is salty or bitter. It seems that their attitude of examining the problem provided by the teacher from the front, back, side, externally, and internally, and chewing it after they have clearly understood it to know its taste, is more evident than that of female students.
Next, let's take a peek at their dormitory life and look at their friendship. Female students seem to make friends starting from affection. They rarely make friends with a calculating attitude as good or bad friends or in a relationship of loyalty bound by circumstances. When dealing with friends, they transcend all theories and make friends or enemies unconditionally with only heart and heart, and with the sixth sense. Therefore, even in the same class, the closeness of female students' friendships is clearly recognized by third parties. Looking at their close friendship, it can be said that they would truly cut their flesh to feed each other and that they would definitely share life and death together. However, once they fall out of each other, they treat each other like enemies. They can be endlessly close friends over trivial matters, and they can also be mortal enemies. It can be said that it is a friendship that is truly easy to ignite but also easy to cool down.

However, male students are not like that. Therefore, even though they are truly close friends, it is not uncommon for them to appear to a third party as if they have never even met. This seems to be an attitude that comes from trusting and relying on each other with compassion. Therefore, they rarely show their true selves to their friends. Therefore, on the one hand, they seem to be friendly to everyone, but on the other hand, <40> they seem to be not friendly to anyone.
Starting again from the learning attitude and friendship mentioned above, when we enter the employment and love views of male and female students and examine their tendencies, it will be of great reference not only to educators but also to various fields.

Four Differences Between Male and Female Students
Chang Ŭng-jin

It is a common understanding among parents who have raised children that there are various differences between men and women in terms of character, constitution, function, and other points. However, when comparing men's schools, where only men are gathered, and women's schools, where only women are gathered, the differences are even more apparent. Of course, if we look at individuals separately, it goes without saying that each individual, regardless of gender, has their own unique personality, but I would like to mention a few differences between male and female students that are generally observed.

1. Differences in Temperament
Men are originally characterized by strong and unwavering will, while women, especially as young women, are characterized by extremely gentle and beautiful nature. Men's temperament is progressive. Their nature is cheerful and outgoing, so if they have something in mind, they do not hesitate to express their opinions or take action in front of anyone.
Their nature is so uninhibited that it can be said that they do not care about trivial matters (不拘小節). They often neglect small things, and their actions are often close to sloppiness and crudeness (粗暴). In other words, it seems that they lack meticulousness in thinking and are not attentive to details (周到). Therefore, they seem to lack perseverance and the tenacity to concentrate their mental energy on one thing for a long time. In other words, their minds seem to wander easily. In contrast, women's nature is conservative. They are cautious in everything, attentive to details, and their intuitive observation of things is particularly meticulous. They also have strong tenacity and perseverance. Men's actions are governed by reason, but women's emotions are extremely sensitive, so their actions seem to be governed more by emotion than reason. They laugh suddenly. That is, the expression of joy, anger, sorrow, and pleasure is quick. When they fail at something or suffer injustice, men quickly give up and forget, but women have a strong attachment and never forget it. Thus, even if a man does something wrong and receives excessive scolding from a teacher, so much so that his ears fall off, or even if he is punished in front of the crowd (衆人이 環視하는 中에서 責罰을 당할지라도), he only cares about it at the time, and then forgets about it, or it does not seem like he feels ashamed and remembers it for a long time. In other words, it could be said that he has a big belly or lacks a sense of shame (廉恥心). However, because women are timid and have sensitive emotions, they cry and are afraid when they hear even the slightest scolding or nagging. This is probably what is meant by "tears are a woman's weapon" <41>. Therefore, even if they have done something wrong, it seems that a better effect is achieved by not announcing their shortcomings in front of many people but by calling them aside and calmly scolding them or explaining the situation before and after with gentle words. Men tend to be silent and indifferent, while women tend to be talkative and passionate. Men have a large capacity and do not care about small things, but women's minds are somewhat narrow, making them prone to suspicion and jealousy (嫉妬心). For example, if a teacher designates a particular individual in a class and praises them or shows them special affection, it is easy to incur unfavorable antipathy towards that teacher or individual. When announcing exam results at the end of the semester, it is not good to post the rankings or announce them generally. Even when handing out report cards to individuals, the principle is that they should never show them to each other. Of course, this seems to be because they are somewhat jealous of the winner and have a strong sense of shame about their own shortcomings. In any case, it seems that women's flaw and shortcoming is that they are suspicious and jealous of those who are superior to them in anything. Women should pay particular attention to this point.

2. Differences in Talent
Men have more intellectual power. Therefore, they have more reasoning and research power. It seems that women's intellectual power is inferior to men's. Therefore, subjects such as mathematics and science are not their forte. On the contrary, they have strong memory, so memorization subjects, literature, music, and handicrafts are their most special talents.

3. Differences in Diligence in Studying
Men generally seem to lack diligence in studying. Of course, there are not a few diligent people as individuals, but when viewed as a whole, the attitude, sincerity, and diligence of male students in studying do not match those of female students. Among male students, there are not a few students who have the attitude and appearance of being told to study by their parents or of going to school because other people are going. However, although there are not many parents in Korean families today who think that women should definitely be educated, many women themselves overcome all kinds of difficulties with extraordinary sincerity and diligence to attend school and study. Therefore, they are extremely sad and heartbroken when they have to temporarily take a leave of absence or drop out of school due to illness or unavoidable family circumstances. Also, female students obey all the rules set by the school and the teacher's orders much better than male students. They generally obey and follow the teacher's instructions and commands, whether it is to do homework or even to clean the classroom. It may be that the reason why there is such a difference in the desire to learn between men and women is related to the fact that women's awareness comes earlier than men's <42>, but I think that this is what the past circumstances and the current situation have created. The biggest reason for men is that they think that there are difficulties in entering higher-level schools, employment problems, etc., in other words, that there is little hope for the future. Women, who are just beginning to open their eyes in the darkness, do not yet have the time to think about such problems. They only look back on the sorrow of ignorance and the treatment they have received in the past due to their lack of education. Therefore, they are making efforts to improve their qualifications as human beings by gaining as much knowledge as possible, even if it is just a little, and becoming better people. This is why their zeal for studying is so strong.

4. Differences in Dress and Appearance
There is a growing sentiment in the world that students' extravagance, especially that of female students, is increasing. It is a general principle that both men and women, when they reach the age of around 20 and begin to open their eyes to the opposite sex, dress up and try to attract the attention of the opposite sex. Among them, young women have an even more intense longing for beauty. Aesthetic life (美적 생활) (this does not only mean tangible beauty expressed externally) is a woman's life. It can be said that women are the expression of beauty. It is a woman's characteristic to try to express beauty in everything. Therefore, even if men and women wear the same cotton or wool clothing, women make sure to make it look neat and stylish. This tendency is especially evident in young women. Therefore, even if they wear clothing made of the same material, they appear more extravagant compared to men. Among them, male students generally have uniforms in any school, so their attire is uniform, and the quality of the uniforms is almost entirely produced in Japan, so Korean and Japanese students use the same ones. However, even if female students have something similar to uniforms depending on the school, the current attire of female students is a small modification of traditional clothing, using white jackets and black skirts or all black. Unfortunately, materials that are most suitable for female students' attire in terms of quality, dyeing, style, and durability are not yet produced much in Korea. Therefore, the current attire of female students allows them to use various kinds of materials as they please. Although all schools prohibit things like silk, the use of woolen serge in skirts seems to be increasing recently. Male students do not care at all even if they wear torn shoes and socks, and tattered uniforms, and they do not seem to feel ashamed at all, but female students do not have the courage to go to a public place wearing tattered skirts and stockings that show their skin, and they feel ashamed. In men's middle schools, the principle is that students are not allowed to grow their hair long. However, male students cut their hair as long as possible and shave neatly within the range that can evade the school rules. I have never gone to a barbershop and paid 20 jeon (전) just to get a shave, but recently I have seen many students going to barbershops just to get a shave. Especially when they become fifth graders and graduation is approaching, they start growing their hair long from around the second semester and start preparing for "high collar." Therefore, the supervising teacher worries about the overall atmosphere of the entire school and makes a lot of noise and effort to prevent this, but in any higher common school, if you look at the fifth graders who have entered the third semester, you can see that they are all growing their hair long and making basic preparations for "high collar." Another funny thing is that especially in the summer, the jackets of suits are shorter than women's jackets these days, and moreover, they are torn up to the shoulders, so when the wind blows, the dirty underwear is visible, which makes the appearance inferior and even makes the new clothes look cheap. However, when the students themselves look proud as if they are wearing the most stylish clothes because it is a Japanese or American trend, I cannot help but laugh. Although schools prohibit female students from wearing makeup or wearing rings, which are useless extravagances, it seems that there are many people who secretly apply makeup that is not visible and take off their gold rings only when they come to school and put them back on when they go home. Recently, there has been a significant increase in the number of female students wearing wristwatches. This is a kind of decoration and extravagance that comes from vanity rather than necessity. In any case, young male students have a tendency to dream of external splendor rather than substance. Taking advantage of these weaknesses, merchants skillfully manufacture and sell unnecessary items to satisfy their vanity, further encouraging students' vanity. Regarding this extravagance issue, I, as an educator, am always paying attention to it, but this is not a problem that can be solved by the school alone. Unless there is leading caution from parents at home and general guidance and supervision from society, it is impossible to achieve any effect. <44>

Named Entities

남자는 천하思想家 여자는 천하美人
曺在浩

남학생과 여학생은 학생이라는 점에 잇서서는 일치하지만 남녀라는 점에 잇서서 차이가 생기는 것이다.
그럼으로 남녀의 차별을 생리학적으로나 심리학적으로 그 근본 所由를 明瞭히 하면 남녀의 차이도 자연히 명백하여질줄 안다. 그러나 나는 그러한 이유에서 출발하야, 演繹的으로 또는 귀납적으로 남학생 여학생의 일치 또는 차이를 설명하고저 안이한다. 다만 표면에 나타나는 우리가 항용 목도하는 몃가지를 순서업시 드러보려 한다.
남녀학생을 물론하고 상급 하급을 물론하고 그네들의 생활은 공부문제, 사상문제, 연애문제, 취직문제, 이 네가지 범위를 버서나지 못하는 듯하다. 연령과 학년을 따라 그 중심 문제는 변동이 되는 듯하다. 중등정도 1, 2년급까지는 남녀를 물론하고 공부문제에 모든 생활이 집중되는 것갓다. 그러나 복잡한 이 세상의 모든 문제에 부다치여 경이와 의혹을 늦기기 시작하면서 거긔에 사상문제가 胚胎되며 연령이 자아내는 종족본능의 충동을 늣기기 시작하면서 거기에 연애문제가 가삼을 괴롭게 하는 모양이니,  그는 대개 중등정도 3, 4년기인 듯하다. 그러나 이 사상문제와 연애문제가 남녀학생의 생활을 충격하면서부터 학생생활의 중심문제에 남녀가 조곰식 색달나지는 듯하다. 남학생은 연애문제보담도 유아독존적 기분으로 사상문제에 더 흥미가 집중되는 듯하다. 그리하야, 이것이 점점 자라나서 자기를 생각하며 자기의 천직을 사색심구하야 문제는 차차 사업으로 변하야 가는 듯하다. 그러나 여학생은 사상문제보다도 천하미인적 기분(현대적 의미에 잇서서)으로 연애문제에 흥미가 더 집중되는 듯하다.＜39＞ 그리하야 이것이 점점 자라나서 자기를 생각하며 여자의 행로를 자각하게 되야 문제는 차차 가정으로 변하야 가는 듯하다. 이러한 경향이 현저히 발현되기는 중등정도의 상급이다. 남녀를 물론하고 자기의 前途를 좀 더 長遠하게 내다보는 학생들은 일시적으로 상급 학교의 입학문제에 모든 학생생활이 집중되지만은 實社會를 내다보는 학생들에게는 남자는 취직문제를 먼저 생각하게 되고 여자는 결혼문제를 먼저 몽상하는 듯하다. 물론 이것은 그 중심문제를 지적하엿슬 뿐이요, 다른 모든 문제를 부정하는 것은 결코 안이닊�. 이것이 내가 본 표면적 일반적 남녀학생의 경향이다.
그러나 특히 교원인 나로서 남녀학생의 학창생활을 삷혀 볼 때에 여러 가지 흥미 잇는 문제와 학생 자신과 학부형모제에게 알니고 십흔 점이 不少하다.
爲先 그네들의 교실을 엿보아 그 학습상태를 살펴보자. 여학생들은 모든 문제를 감정적으로 呑下的으로 말업시 감수한다. 교수자로서 불쾌한 기분을 띄우고 교수를 하면 그네들은 그대로 본 바다. 불쾌한 기색으로 선생의 기분을 엿보면서, 如履薄氷的 태도로 주는 그대로 짠지 쓴지 맛도 모르고 呑下하듯이 학습을 하게 된다.
물론 이러한 점은 남학생에게도 다소 잇슬는지 모르겟스나 여학생에게는 이것이 현저히 나타난다. 그러나 남학생들은 모든 문제를 그대로 말업시 선생의 기분을 엿보면서 呑下하지는 안이한다. 비판적으로 咀嚼하야 짜다 쓰다는 불평을 말하면서 감수한다. 선생이 제공하는 문제를 전면으로 후면으로 측면으로 그리고 외적으로 내적으로 두다리고 십흔대로 두다리여 그것을 확실히 이해한 후에 咀嚼하야 그 맛을 알려고 하는 태도가 여학생에 비하야 현저한 듯하다.
다음에 그네들의 舍宿생활을 엿보아 우정관계를 살펴보자. 여학생들은 동무를 사귀여도 애정에서 출발하야 사귀는 듯하다.  善友 損友로는 타산적 태도로 사귀거나 事情에 억매인 의리관계로 동모를 사귀는 일은 드믄 듯하다. 동무를 대할 때에 모든 이론을 초월하고 다못마음과 마음으로 제六감으로 무조건하고 朋友를 원수를 사귀는 듯하다. 그럼으로 여학생들의 우정관계는 가튼 학급일지라도 그 親不親을 제삼자로서 명료히 의식하게 된다. 그 친한 友誼를 보면 참으로 살이라도 비여 먹일 듯하고 생사를 반듯이 한가지할만한 친교라고 할 수 잇슬 것이다. 그러나 한번 사이가 틀니기만 하면 무슨 원수를 대하듯 한다. 대스럽지 안은 일로서 한업시 친한 동모도 될 수가 잇고 다시 업는 원수도 될 수가 잇다. 참으로 타기도 쉽지만은 식기도 쉬운 우정관계라고 할 수 잇는 것이다.
그러나 남학생들은 그러치 안이하다. 그럼으로 진실로 친한 붕우간이지만은 제삼자가 볼 때 아조 당초부터 사귀여보지도 못한 사히 가티 보히는 편이 적지 안타. 그것은 아마 哀心으로 相信相依하는 데서 울어 나오는 태도인 듯하다. 그럼으로 여간하야 동모에게 자기의 정체를 보이지 안는다. 따라서 일편으로 누구에나 친한 듯 하지만은 또 일편으로는＜40＞ 누구와도 친하지 안이한 것가티도 보이기도 한다.
우에 말한 학습태도와 우정관계에서 다시 출발하야 남녀학생들의 취직관, 연애관에 들어가서 그 경향을 통찰할 때에 교육자뿐만 아니라 여러 가지 방면에 만흔 참고가 될 뜻하다.

남학생 여학생 네가지 차이
張膺震

남녀가 성질상 체질상, 기능상, 기타 종종의 점에 대하야, 여러 가지의 달은 점이 잇는 것은 가정에서 자녀를 길너보신 부모들의 다 가티 짐작하는 바이지만은 특히 남성만을 모아 노흔 남학교와 여성만을 모하 노흔 여학교에서 양성을 대조하야 볼 때에는 그 차이가 더욱 현저함을 보겟슴니다. 無論 개인 개인을 떼여노코 보면 남녀를 물론하고 개인마다 제각기 특수한 개성이 잇는 것은 말할 것도 업지만은 일반적으로 관찰한 남녀학생의 차이점을 멧가지 말하랴고 함니다.
