白菱生
문예기타 (Literary Miscellaneous)

It's a story without thorns or poison. It's just a story that sounds plausible as a human being.

The time is four years ago, early September. The place is Sogyeok-dong in Gyeongseong. The main characters are Kwon O-dal, a 5th-grade student at XX Higher Common School at the time, Mr. P who is in the same room, Kwon's father, the neighbor, and the pale pink nude beauty, a student in the 3rd grade of a certain Women's Higher Common School.

P went to a friend's house last night and came back to the boarding house at five in the morning.

He thought the door wouldn't be open yet, but for some reason, the gate was wide open, and Kwon, who should be sleeping in the room, was nowhere to be seen. <95>

If he hadn't slept at home, the room wouldn't be like this, but the mosquito net was up, there was a hastily folded blanket, and what's more, Kwon's school uniform and hat were there!

Thinking he might have gone to the bathroom, he was about to enter the room when the landlady, looking flustered as if she had been surprised by something, came out from inside and said,

"Where have you been? It's a disaster," she exclaimed loudly.

P's sixth sense must have been working because a bad premonition made his heart pound.

"Yes? What's the matter?"

"O-dal has been caught."

"Caught?"

"Caught... to Jongno Police Station..."

"Why?"

"He jumped into the house next door..."

At these words, what flashed through P's mind was the pale pink nude of the neighbor girl who slept naked inside a pale pink mosquito net every night.

In his haste, P tried to ask the landlady about the details of the incident, but the landlady didn't know the details either, except that she heard shouts from the house next door and commotion, and when she looked over the window, she saw O-dal being tied up by a policeman.

That was not even as much as P had expected...

P immediately ran out and woke up the post office worker who wasn't supposed to be working and sent an urgent telegram to Kwon's father.

Attempted rape - this is a crime not easy for a student to commit, and therefore, P could not take action on his own.

O-dal's attempted rape is nothing special. If he is human, he is a vigorous youth. Unless he is disabled or a fool, he is bound to be caught.

It happened in this way.

That day at three in the morning, Kwon woke up needing to pee.

Rubbing his eyes and holding his head, he went out to the veranda and went to the bathroom.

As he climbed back onto the veranda, Kwon thought, "Again today?" Kwon could look down into the room of the house below (the house below was located so that it was separated from Kwon's room and because they were close to each other, the inside of that house could be seen from the veranda.).

In the pink gauze mosquito net, faintly illuminated by the soft light, <96> a plump, pale pink nude girl was fast asleep. Kwon saw (what P and Kwon called) the pale pink nude again.

가시도 업고 독도 업는 이약이다. 다만 사람으로써 그럴듯한 이약이다.
때는 지금으로부터 4년전 9월초생 장소는 경성시내 昭格洞 인물은 주인공으로 당시에 XX고등보통학교 5年級에 다니는 權O達군과 가튼 방에 잇는 P군 그리고 權군의 아버지 이웃집 주인 그러고 某여자고등보통학교 3년에 學籍을 두엇다는 연분홍 裸體 미인-이가 중심인물이다.
P군은 어제밤에 친구의 집에 가서 잠을 자고 새벽 다섯시에 하숙집으로 도라왓다.
아직 문을 열어노치 아니하엿슬 줄 알엇는데 웬일인지 대문이 훤하게 열니고 방에는 응당 잠을 자고 잇슬 權군이 보이지 아니한다.＜95＞
집에서 자지 아니하엿스면 방안이 그러치 아니할터인데 모기帳이 치여저 잇고 업헛다가 거더친 홋이불이 잇고 더구나 權군의 교복과 모자가 잇지 아니한가!
혹시 변소에나 갓나하고 방으로 들어가랴고 하는데 하숙주인이 무슨 일에 놀냇는지 얼골에 황당한 빗츨 띄우고 안으로부터 나오며
「어데갓다 인제오시요? 야단낫소.」하고 장히 들네인다.
P군은 제육감이 움직엿든지 불길한 예감에 가슴이 울ㅅ적하여진다.
「네? 야단이라니요?」
「O달이가 잽혀갓서요.」
「잡혀가다니?」
「잽혀갓서 ... 鍾路경찰서로 ... ... .」
「웨?」
「이 엽헤ㅅ집에 뛰여들어갓다가 ... ... .」
이 말에 P군의 머리에 번개가티 떠올으는 것은 밤마다 연분홍 모기장 속에서 옷을 활신 벗고 잠을 자는 이웃집 처녀의 연분홍 裸體다.
P군은 급한 마음에 사건의 내용을 주인에게 불어보앗스나 주인도 자세한 것은 알지 못하고 다만 이웃집에서 고함을 질으는 소리가 나고 야단이 일어나더니 들창으로 넘어다 보니까 O달군이 순사에게 꼭꼭 묵기어 가더라는 것밧게는 더 알지 못한다.
그것쯤으로야 P군의 예상만도 못할 것 ... ... .
P군은 바로 뛰여 나가서 아니이러나는 우편국원을 뚜드려 깨여 權군의 아버지에게로 至急電報를 처 노앗다.
强姦未遂- 이것은 학생의 신분으로 용이치 아니한 범죄이며, 따러서 P군 독단으로는 조처할 수가 업기 때문이엇섯다.

O달군의 强姦未遂는 달은 것이 아니다. 만일 사람이라고 하면 혈기 방장한 청춘이요. 병신이나 골샛님이 아닌 이상 첩경 면치 못하고 돗에 걸니게 된 사실이다.
그것은 이러한 경로로 생기게 되엿다.
그날 새벽 세시에 權군은 오좀이 마려워서 잠이 깨엿다.
골머리를 후어잡고 눈을 부비면서 툇마루에 나서 변소를 다녀왓다.
