A Person with a Light Mouth

Two people were gossiping to their heart's content about others when, unfortunately, a friend with a light mouth (입 가뱌운 사람) came by and overheard everything. "You're the kind of person who has to tell others whatever you hear, but please, just this once, keep this to yourself," they said, and offered him one won to sweeten the deal. The friend with a light mouth was overjoyed and said, "Since you're even giving me money, how could I possibly say anything?"

He put the money in his pocket and left. However, around midnight that night, someone knocked loudly on the outer gate. "Who is it?" he asked, annoyed, and opened the gate to find it was none other than the friend with the light mouth. "What brings you here at this hour?" he asked, and the friend threw the one won back at him, saying, "I brought this money back." "Why?" "Well, no matter how much I think about it, I just can't keep what I heard to myself!" <63>

입가뱌운 사람
어대 두 사람이 모여서 실컷 남의 험구를 하고 낫는데 맛침 그곳에 입 가뱌운 친구가 와서 그 소리를 다 드러노앗다. 「자네는 무슨 말을 드르면 긔어코 남에게 말하야만 사는 사람이지만 이번 말만은 좀 꼭 참어주게.」
하고 엿을 먹이기 위하야 돈 일원을 내 주엇더니 입 가뱌운 친구도 아조 조와서 『이러케까지 돈을 주는 데야 나인들 그런 말을 할 리가 잇나 』
하고 돈을 호주머니에 느코 갓다. 그런데 그 날 한 밤ㅅ중쯤 하야 밧문을 통통 두다리는 사람이 잇슴으로 『누구여』하고 골을 내며 문을 열고 본 즉 앗가 그 입 가뱌운 그 친구인 고로 『이밤ㅅ중에 무슨 일로 왓나』하고 무른 즉 앗가 그 돈 일원을 내 던지며 이돈은 도로 가저왓네.『왜』 『글세 암만 생각해 보아도 드른 말을 참을 수가 잇서야지.』<63>
