Prize Competition

Creative Sijo (創作時調)
We are seeking new creative sijo of modern people.
○ Up to five poems per person
○ A small token of appreciation will be presented for published works.
○ There is no deadline.
Judge: Karam Yi Hunch'uk (가람 李薰崎氏)

Ancient Folk Songs (古來民謠)
Each region has its own unique folk songs. We are seeking ancient folk songs that Korean youths have been singing for a long time.
(A small token of appreciation will be presented.)

Creative Folk Songs (創作民謠)
We are seeking creative folk songs that originate from the lives of modern Koreans. (Adaptations of old melodies are also welcome.)
○ The number of lines in the title is free.
○ A small token of appreciation will be presented for outstanding works among those published.
Judge: Pa'in Kim Tonghwan (巴人 金東煥氏)
