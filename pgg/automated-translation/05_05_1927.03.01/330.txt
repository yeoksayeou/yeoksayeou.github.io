월강죄 (외 1편) (Transgression of Crossing the Border (and 1 other poem))
豆滿江가에서 汀月
시 (Poem)

Transgression of Crossing the Border

Even if I call out to the reeds
That the butterflies nestle in on the border,
For my anxious heart,
If only they would tell me.
But if this body crosses,
It is a transgression of crossing the border (越江罪).

Every time the geese go,
I tell them and send them off,
Even if I always go with you in my dreams,
If this body crosses,
It is a transgression of crossing the border. <68>

Waiting (Folk Song)

Even though three springs have passed,
My love has not even sent a farewell letter (寄別).
Until the letters (鴈信) arrive on autumn nights,
How can I possibly
Endure it?
The ice on the Tuman River (豆滿江)
Has all melted away.

Even if three springs do not come,
Even if it passes thirteen springs,
I can't bear it,
Has my departed love
Forgotten me...?
The swallows in the Gangnam (江南)
Have all returned to find their nests. <69>

Named Entities

豆滿江	Tuman River
江南	Gangnam
