기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editor's Note)

New Year March Grand Special Edition, "Pride of Chosŏn" Issue

Pride of Chosŏn! Pride of Chosŏn! Yes, we too have pride. Shining pride! The power to boast! It is a sacred power to overcome all misfortunes of a time and create new life. Yes! We have that sacred pride. Stop crying, wipe away your tears, and read this book. Let us gather our pride!!

A great record to be kept in every household and read for ten or even a hundred years!
An unprecedentedly large issue with a significant increase in pages, special price seventy jeon.
Not only should you read this book yourself, but also have the elderly read it, have women read it, have workers and servants read it, and read it aloud to growing boys and girls.

There may have been and will be others who write about "Pride of Chosŏn!" However, how uniquely and pleasantly, how remarkably, the editorially distinctive *Pyŏlgŏn'gon* will handle this, I won't boast in advance, but having gathered the pride of a half-millennial history vertically and the pride compared to various countries and races around the world horizontally, and furthermore, packed with remarkable articles of pride that can only be found in a magazine of taste, I will only say that the reporters, more than the readers, will create a record that they will keep and read over and over again. <59>

**Named Entities**

朝鮮	Chosŏn
別乾坤	Pyŏlgŏn'gon

기사형태	사고·편집후기

新春三月 大大特輯 朝鮮자랑號
朝鮮의 자랑! 朝鮮의 자랑! 그럿타 우리에게도 자랑이잇다. 빗나는 자랑이잇다! 자랑 자랑 자랑할수잇는힘! 그것은 한의 모든 不運을닉여치우고 새로운 生命을지어낼 거륵한힘이다. 그럿타! 우리에게 그거륵한자랑이잇다. 울기를긋치라 눈물을거두라 그리고 이 冊을 닑자 우리의 자랑을거두자!!

家家戶戶에 必備하고 十年  百年傳해닑을 大記錄!
空前特大號 頁數大增加 特價七十錢
自身이닑는 것은 勿論이요 이 冊은 老人에게도 닑히라 婦女에게도 닑히라 職工에게도 머슴에게도 닑히라 그리고 자라가는 少年少女에게도 닑어들려주라

『朝鮮의 자랑!』은 前에도 한가잇섯고  이압흐로도 하는이가 잇슬 것이다. 그러나 編輯獨特한 『別乾坤』이 엇더케 愉快하게 엇더케 奇拔하게 이것을 取扱할가 그것은 미리 자랑하지안커니와 縱으로 半萬年歷史中의 자랑을 거두고 橫으로 世界各國各族에 比한자랑을 모은우에 로히 趣味誌아니면 엇지못할 奇拔한 자랑 記事를 滿載 讀者보다도 記者가먼저 두고두고 내여닑을 記錄을 지을것만말해둔다.＜59＞
