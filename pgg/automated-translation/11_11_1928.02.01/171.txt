Welcoming Orders

We ask readers in Kyŏngsŏng (京城) to occasionally order our reporters to go out and investigate. If there is such-and-such incident in such-and-such place, or a strange person, please notify us to investigate and publish it. We will immediately mobilize as soon as we receive reliable notice. (Regardless of the size of the incident, please send it in a sealed letter to the 『Pyŏlgŏn’gon』 (別乾坤) Editor-in-Chief.) <102>

**Named Entities**

京城	Kyŏngsŏng
別乾坤	Pyŏlgŏn’gon
