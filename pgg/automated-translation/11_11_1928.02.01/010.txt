Waiting
Shin Dong-shik (Composer), Jeong Gi-hwan (Lyricist)

Waiting, each step is so slow,
Even when the moon rises and becomes a full score, you don't come,
Will you come creeping, creeping only when the sun rises?
Passing over even a small basket (장래 하나 넘기),
Are you that busy?

In the parsley field,
The sound of tree frogs,
Even the tree frog
Has a hoarse voice.
Did I come here to listen to this sound,
And stay up all night?
Dew is falling
In the mulberry field.
Morning dew is falling. <1>
