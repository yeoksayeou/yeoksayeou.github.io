預言 十年後의 朝鮮如何? (A Prediction: What Will Korea Be Like in Ten Years?)
논설 (Essay)

What Will Korea Be Like in Ten Years?
If you don't know, you won't know what will happen tomorrow or the day after. If you know, you can know what will happen ten or a hundred years from now, that is the human mind. "What will Korea be like in ten years?" At first glance, it seems like a vague question and an irresponsible task. But upon further thought, it is neither so vague nor irresponsible. As modern people, anyone can foresee ten years into the future. Whether it will coincide with reality is questionable, but I am confident that it will be mostly accurate. Now, I would like to make a ten-year agreement with the readers and introduce the ten-year perspectives of those who have foresight. Please understand that I have avoided political issues for a moment. And since views may vary from person to person, I hope you will not criticize them if they do not match your own opinions. (Editor)

I Believe a Korean Spirit Will Surely Arise
Choe Nam-seon

It is said that people's dull eyes cannot see even an inch ahead, but in Korea, where there are divine sages (神誌), Taoist sages (道誌), Jeong Gam (鄭鑑), and Tojeong (土亭) who can see a thousand or ten thousand years into the future, there may be those who say that things ten years ahead are as familiar as the past. <14> It's just that I am not one of them.

As a fool has many dreams, and as there is no boundary between reality and concept for primitive people, might I not have scraps of dreams left over in my head? Even when I search and scrape them together, there is still nothing to be found. I know I am being honest in saying there is nothing, but I wonder if "Byeolgeonkon" will accept this honesty and excuse me from the responsibility of the question. It has been a long time since honesty has been valued, so it is uncertain whether "Byeolgeonkon" truly likes this honesty.

Well, ten years from now? You ask me to speak only of what I see in terms of thought or scholarship, but my mind is like a restless monkey (心猿) that cannot be easily caught, and my thoughts are like a lost sheep (亡羊) that cannot be easily found! As I twist and turn this way and that, I see that my thoughts and intentions of the past ten years are simply transforming their value and appearing ten years from now. If the three worlds (三世) are interconnected, perhaps the future and the past are originally one (原來不二).

Surely, in ten years, the world of thought will have some direction (歸端).
Generally speaking, the period of the return to reason (回歸熱時代) and the various joys and sorrows of the past ten years will naturally lead to composure and adjustment, establishing at least the mainstream (本流正幹) of the era's trends. The days when those who are good at begging, those who are good at cursing, those who are good at arguing illogically, those who are good at imitating, those who have parrot's tongues, those who are good at speaking deceptively, those who are good at catering to others, those who are good at deceiving, and those who are skilled at flattery will already be over. Because people have been deceived so much, they will no longer be able to gain general approval unless they have depth, solidity, prudence, and practicality. Empty, superficial ideas, no matter how much they borrow the clothes of others' weddings and wear fake agate and false pearl ornaments, will no longer attract attention. In an era where everything is processed based on the principle of life (生活本位), real-life principle (實生活本位), and self-life principle (自己生活本位), what is required before poetry, philosophy, or idealism is realistic economics (現實的 經論)—the thought that clearly indicates and guides the transformation of reality (現實改化) on both ends of its enduring existence will hold authority and power as the central thought of that time.

Looking back on the days of conceptual isms, trends, movements based on signboards (看板至上), and strategies, the warriors of that time will regret the excessive playful mood. Having gone through so much, they will realize how ridiculous it was to struggle with irreconcilable divisions between themselves and others, joining parties within parties, and each carrying a hundred thousand trillion repulsive elements even within themselves, unable to even achieve self-unification. <15> With the great reality blocking the way for Koreans, everyone, no matter who or what, must break through that iron wall in a single-minded way, or all else is in vain. They will realize how ridiculous it was to argue, tear apart, and criticize each other, denying this. Even if their views were truly different, it would not matter, as they are all simultaneously confronting the same reality. The immediate perspectives may differ slightly, and the degree of understanding may vary, but it is a natural agreement that no one can have a completely different objectivism or a completely different economic policy. They will bite their lips every time they see how they roared at each other as if they could not coexist under the same sky (不共戴天), simply because they used different words to express themselves.

Why were they so confused? Why did they wander on a clear path, and why did they try to turn their backs on each other while walking the same path? Why did they try to forcibly conceptualize, playify, and fantasize about life, which is practical? Why did they rely on the neighbor to the south in the morning and try to lean on the neighbor to the north in the evening, doing this for Kim (金之) and that for Lee (李之), without establishing a center of "me for me," and without even realizing that this was wrong? Why did they not realize that their brothers were fighting each other to the point of breaking their skulls because of the enemy's (도적놈) discord, causing even the foundation to be uprooted? Why did they leave the wasteland even more desolate and all the fertile land barren while looking up to the sky with their hands tied? The only things they did were to conflict with each other, twist, roar, and strangle. There was no thought of preserving harmony and working together with one mind. If you listened to their words, there was progress, amazing success, and enormous harvest every year, month, day, and hour, but in reality, it was the opposite. Why was that era so long? Looking back now, this difference (異趣), which is so clear, was somehow so obscure back then. It's funny that even if they had these thoughts back then, they would have found these words offensive. Oh, now that they have deep awareness and great change, it doesn't matter what happens in the future, but why did they waste so much of the good ten years? Many people will say this.

The central axis of thought at that time will be, in short, the absolute uniqueness of Korean-ness (朝鮮的 朝鮮의 絶對把特). This will dominate each sector with its formidable penetrating power and universality. Thus, the light of enduring Korea (久遠 朝鮮) will radiate in all directions at that time. Of course, there will be various things and some may even exhibit greater eccentricity, but the central thought and guiding principle will be clearly formed with the focus on Korean-ness (朝鮮的). The "Stirner"s, the "Marx"s, the "Holtzendorff"s, and whoever and whatever else that once played sharp-angled roles will have already undergone complete fusion in the Korean <16> crucible, and we will mostly see only the haloed moon on the summit of Mount Taebaek (太白山頭의 輪孤月) before us.

Generally, there will be less talk and more work. There will be a lot of talk, but even more work. Because they only talked and easily criticized others, the cooperative spirit could not emerge. But after realizing that nothing, not even retying a loosened coat string, can be done without work and effort, work will gradually take precedence over talk. The result of this will further shrink the world of words, and we will see the taste of spring gradually increasing in the dark and chaotic times.

The power of absurdity, nonsense, instability, and unreliability will unfortunately diminish. Grand theories of the world (世界大勢論) and righteous theories of humanity (人道正義論), like a comb with missing teeth, will also lose their color. The consciousness that "I can only succeed if I am good and do well in any world" will firmly establish itself in the hearts of all. The so-called New Korea (新朝鮮) will begin to form its placenta within itself. And its first expression—the very first effort (멘 몬처의 努力形式)—will manifest as "Let's make one thing at a time (무엇이든지 한아씩 만들자)." No matter what chaos occurs in other worlds or what images appear in the distant future, we will first think and strive to "become something here now (시방 여긔서 한 무엇이 되자)." While constantly excusing themselves as being in a transitional period (過渡期), they will gradually move from a state of clumsiness where nothing fits together or harmonizes, to a phase where they become something, even if it is insignificant, and where that becomes a step upon which further construction can take place. Thinking about how everything crumbled and became entangled during the 甲申 year (Gapsin Year) as a transition (過渡), the 甲午 year (Gapo Year) as a transition, this as a transition, and that as a transition, the theory of blaming the transition itself (過渡自怒論) will completely disappear.

At that time, rather than "Marx" or "Lenin," Dangun (檀君) will be the pillar of the spiritual world—Dangun (檀君) as the only vessel capable of fully containing the temporal value of human experience and the specific inner workings of the Korean mission will be advocated, embraced, and revered by all as the comprehensive and supreme expression in all fields. There is no need to say more.

In one word, it would be sufficient to say that the scientific atmosphere is increasing, but there is no time to delve into academic matters, which are not so simple in reality.

The past is a mirror of the future, they say. Well, if that is true, then anyone without amnesia would be a considerable prophet. Well, if our observation is based even slightly on the actual beauty and ugliness reflected in our mirror, then perhaps we have finished the rites of Gwanak (冠岳) correctly to some extent. Whether it fits or not, it's all about the Eight Trigrams (八卦), so I will bring out the words of Guo Pu (郭璞) and Li Chunfeng (李淳風) of the present age, acting as a childish jester to make a few absurd remarks that are honest yet dishonest and dishonest yet honest, hoping to avoid the blame of the "Byeolgeonkon" writer. <17>

Religious Changes and Civil Law Reforms
Na Yong-hwan

What will Korea be like in ten years? At first glance, it seems vague, but can't we predict ten years into the future? I am confident that none of today's things will remain in ten years. Ten years is not a long time, but compared to the past, it can be said to be as long as a hundred or a thousand years. One year today is like a hundred years in the past. I assert that thoughts, logic, customs, habits, and all things will change dramatically from what they are today. The distinctions between old and young (老小의 別), seniority (長幼의 別), men and women (남녀의 別), and all other forms of discrimination will disappear, and unity and equality will prevail. Topknots (상투) will be impossible to find, white clothes (白衣) will be impossible to wear, the arrogance of the nobility (양반의 거드름), the pretenses of the old (늙은이의 假藉勢), and the separation of men and women (여자의 內外)—all these will disappear. The reason is:

First and foremost, religion, which dominates the minds of all and controls the customs and habits of human society, will change. Religion will inevitably change due to the demands of the times and the welcome of all. Consequently, customs, habits, and general systems and cultures will all change. Religion is the master of civil law. In the past, during the Buddhist era, the general culture of society was Buddhist, and during the Confucian era, it changed to Confucian, and in Christian countries, the general culture became Christian. Now, in Korea, Confucianism and Christianity are already behind the times (時代遲) and a new religion that is adapted to the new era is being demanded. Therefore, all cultures will change. It will inevitably happen that way.

In the past, during the Donghak (東學) era, the heads of corrupt officials (貪官汚吏) were cut off and the testicles of nobles and the rich (양반과 부자의 불알) were crushed. In the past, during the 甲辰 era (Gapjin Era), people cut their hair (斷髮) and wore black clothes (黑衣) all at once. The power of religion is truly immense. In ten years, a new religion will newly dominate general society in Korea. To put it bluntly, it will be the era of Cheondogyo (천도교). Even now, there are three million followers, and this year alone, five or six thousand households have been added. In ten years, at least half of Korea will be Cheondogyo believers. Its main principle is "Man is Heaven (人乃天)," its goal is "Protect the Nation, Secure the People, Spread Virtue Throughout the World (保國安民 布德天下)," and its culture is the establishment of new logic suitable for the times. And in Korea, it already has a central power with history, projects, and organizational strength. Therefore, under the teachings of "Treating People as Heaven, Equality for All (事人如天 萬民平等)," gender discrimination, age gaps (老小間隔), and the distinction between noble and common (兩常尊卑) will all disappear. Also, superstition and licentiousness will disappear, and topknots and white clothes will all disappear. I assert that we will live new lives with a renewed system and culture under the equality of all people. <18> Without the change of religion, civil law, that is, the customs and habits of the people, will not easily change. Even the power of the state cannot do it. No matter how powerful politics and laws may be, they do not have as much power as religion in preserving or eliminating the good and bad customs of the people.

I assert this before all people without hesitation. I see and answer like this even when questioning ghosts and asking heaven and earth. I will say once again that religion will change in Korea in ten years, and everything will change accordingly, and then I will stop.

The Korean Women's World in Ten Years
Lee Gwon-won

1
Expecting a prediction from someone who is not a prophet is a funny thing from any perspective. And for someone who is not a prophet to respond to a request to make a prediction is a scary thing from any perspective. Therefore, in response to the question of what the Korean women's world will be like in ten years, I believe that predicting that it is impossible to know in detail is the most appropriate prediction for someone who is not a prophet.

However, all things change constantly. Therefore, it is accurate to state definitively that the Korean women's world in ten years will not be the same as the Korean women's world today. Then, in what direction will it change, and to what extent will it change? Only in regard to this does social science allow us a certain degree of predictive possibility.

2
The Korean women's world is dependent on Korean society. And the direction of change in Korean society is merging with that of the global society. Therefore, to discuss the direction of change in the Korean women's world, our perspective must first focus on the world as a whole.

Across the entire world, all feudal orders have already been almost completely conquered by the capitalist new order. However, the capitalist modern order has also reached its limit and is now rapidly moving towards a better order. This is the global trend, following the inevitable rules of historical evolution. However, Korean society, even without completely liquidating the feudal old order, has been forced to join the global trend, so Korean society is currently in a bibimbap-like (비빔밥적) state. <19> However, each element of that "bibimbap" represents a different historical process. Both the feudal and capitalist elements are merely receding elements in relation to the next element, but the capitalist element has a value of one step forward compared to the feudal element.

Considering from such a viewpoint, the direction of future change in the Korean women's world is clear. The feudal chains (鐵鎖) that still strongly bind the Korean women's world will gradually break, and capitalist individualism will gradually develop. However, at the same time, the elements of the proletarian class will gradually gain dominance.

3
Then, to what extent will such changes reach in the next ten years? We can think about this in two ways. First, if there is a landmark, major change in society during that time. Second, if there is no such change, and it changes at the usual rate we see daily. If the first case is realized, the Korean women's world will be completely renewed along with general society. All unreasonable constraints, both feudal and modern, will have already lost their power, and our sisters will be busy building true new freedom. The sorrows of sex due to sex, and the sorrows of class due to class, etc., will disappear rapidly. However, in the second case, the state in ten years will be merely a quantitative expansion of the state of today. The number of educated people will increase, the number of those who have awakened to the freedom of individuality will increase, or the consciousness of proletarian women will develop, and the socialist women's movement will become active, etc., are all imaginable, but there will be no qualitative change in the basic state from today.

But I don't know whether Korean society can have a major leap in the next ten years.

What Will Be the Economy of Koreans in Ten Years?
Seonwoo Jeon

It is impossible to predict even a few days into the future, so how can we know about things ten years from now? Just as each person changes their thoughts, principles, and policies while guessing their own future, society, which is made up of individual elements, changes according to the fluctuations of the times. However, whether those changes in thoughts, principles, and policies will ultimately lead to a certain result cannot be predicted by either the person's subjectivity or another's objectivity (continued on page 13). <20> (Continued from page 20) Unexpected changes will certainly occur, such as something thought of as a square transforming into a circle, or something thought of as a circle transforming into a square. Or, optimism may turn into pessimism, and pessimism may turn into optimism. Therefore, in the current situation, what we should be careful about is that we should not change our thoughts and principles this way and that way too much according to the current situation and the fluctuations of the times, ultimately reaching a dangerous situation. Rather than being pessimistic about the present, we should always look for future opportunities to lead to optimism. And if it is something that should be done reasonably and that conforms to justice, even if there are some obstacles and difficulties, if we continue to do it, even if we cannot achieve all of the good fortune, we believe that we will obtain a result of good fortune that will be a source of pride and joy to ourselves. In light of the fluctuations of the times and the changes in principles and ideologies, we who are in a dangerous transitional period should pay even more attention to the above, and we simply think that we will also taste economic good fortune as a result of such attention and care. <13>

預言 十年後의 朝鮮如何?
모른다 하면 내일일 모레일도 모를 것이요. 안다 하면 십 년 백년 후도 알 수 있는 것이 인간의 머리이다. 『십 년 후의 조선 如何?』얼핏 생각하면 막연한 문제요. 동시에 무책임한 일 갓트나 그러나 다시 생각하면 그닥 막연치도 안코 무책임한 즛도 안이다. 현대인으로 10년 預見은 누구든지 알 수 잇는 일이다. 사실과 부합될넌지는 잠간 의문이나 그러나 대개는 적중 될 것을 확신한다. 玆에 讀者 諸氏와 10년을 預約하기로 하고 先見 諸氏의 십년관을 소개한다. 정치 문제는 잠깐 피하야스니 諒解하라. 그리고 사람에 따라 보는 바도 母論 각각일 것이니 각자의 의견에 맛지 안는다고 그는 責하지 말기를 바란다. (編者)

반듯이 朝鮮心이 생길 줄 아오
崔南善

조희 한 겹으로 내다보지 못하는 것이 사람의 鈍眼이라고 하지마는 천년만년도 눈압헤 닥아서 보이든 神誌도 잇고 道誌도 잇고 鄭鑑도 잇고 土亭도 잇는 조선에서는 십 년쯤 압서의 일은 지난 일갓치 안다고 할 이도 잇기야 하겠지.＜14＞ 다만 내가 그이가 아닐 따름.
癡人에게 꿈이 만코 원시인에게는 현실과 관념과의 경계가 업다는 셈으로 내 머리엔들 살아지다가 남은 꿈ㅅ부스럭이가 업술가하야 뒤지고 끍어 모아도 의연히 걸리는 것이 업다. 업는 것을 업다고 말미 정직한 줄이야 알건마는 이러한 정직을 『別乾坤』이 용납하야서 맛긴 문제의 책임을 면해 줄는지가 의문이다. 정직이 통용 아니 된지도 오랜 세월이매 『別乾坤』이라고 꼭 이 정직을 조하하는지 未可必이다.
글세 십 년 압? 사상이나 학술상으로 보는 바만은 말슴하라 하시마는 六窓의 心猿이 만만히 손에 잡히며 九逵의 亡羊이 얼는 눈에 띄워야 말이지! 이리 굴이고 저래 뭉치매 결국은 지난 십 년 동안의 心念意圖가 그대로 십 년 압흐로 가치를 전환하야 나옴을 본다. 三世가 圓通이라 하면 미래와 과거가 原來不二리라고나 할가.
설마 십 년 후에는 사상계도 좀 歸端이 잇겟지.
대체로 보아서 回歸熱시대라고 할 其間 십 수년의 甘苦 여러 가지 경험이 저절로 沈靜과 整調를 나하서 줄잡아도 시대 사조의 本流正幹이랄 것만은 確立하겟지. 떼 잘 쓰는 자가 장사요, 욕 잘하는 자가 장사요, 억설 잘하는 자가 장사요, 숭내 잘 내는 자가 장사요, 鸚鵡부리 가진 자가 장사요, 言足사이비하는 자가 장사요, 영합 잘하고 속임수 조코 발라마치기에 得妙한 자가 장사일 시절은 벌서부터 아니겟지. 원체 만히 속아 본지라 인제는 깁히와 탄탄과 알심과 실사커 잇는 것이 아니면 일반의 승인을 바들 수 업습으로 뷘거플的 허수아비的 사상은 아모리 남의 혼인 의복을 빌어 닙고 造瑪瑙假眞珠의 가진 노리개을 차고 나서도 그제는 다시 남의 눈을 끄을지 못하게 되겟지. 生活本位, 實生活本位, 自己生活本位로 모든 것이 처리되는 철이매 시 보담 철학 보담 이상론 보담 압서서 요구되는 것이 현실적 經論-그 야무른 久遠의 이짝끗친 現實改化를 밝히 또 바로 지시하고 導化하는 사상이 그때의 중추 사상으로 권위와 세력을 가진 것이다.
槪念的의 主義니 경향이니 看板至上의 운동이니 方略이니 하든 일을 回顧하고는 넘어 과한 遊戱 기분을 애닯어하는 소리를 當年의 용사들에게 골고로 드를 것이다. 그만큼 지내보아도 不相容할 것가치 彼我가 대립하고 또 黨中에 입당하고 또 각개 스스로의 속에도 百千無量의 反撥分子를 지녀서 自己 統一부터 못하야 애쓰든 일이 실로 우스운 것을 깨다랏슬 것이다. 조선인에게는 유일한 大現實이 압을 꽉 막은 바에 ＜15＞ 누구든지, 무엇으로든지, 다 한가지, 그저 외골스로 위선 그 철벽을 깨터러지 아니하면 다른 모든 것이 虛幻空妄인 것을, 이것을 아니라 하야 서로 다토고 찟고 발기고 하든 일이 어떠케 우스운 지를 잘 알앗슬 것이다. 그남아 서로 소견이 아조 틀렷으면 어쩌리오 마는 실상은 동일한 현실을 동시에 對觸하는 바에 或前或後하야 보는 당면은 다를지 모르고 或深或淺하야 데미다보는 정도는 갓지 아니할지 모르되 아모에게고 아주 다른 對象觀과 아주 딴판의 經論策이 잇슬 수 업슴이 自然한 약속이어늘. 다만 或此或彼라하야 形喩하는 言辭가 다를 뿐으로 不共戴天할듯키 으르렁거리든 일을 들이보는 족족 입슐을 깸을 것이다.

턱업슴과 엉터리업슴과 주착업슴과 가늠업슴 등의 세력권은 불상하게도 축소될 거이다. 휑뎅그렁한 世界大勢論, 얼레빗살 가튼 人道正義論도 안색이 퍽 減損될 것이다. 어떠한 세계에서든지 제가 잘나고 제가 잘해야 제가 잘된다는 의식이 퍽 선명하게 만인의 중심에 보금자리 칠 것이다. 닐은바 新朝鮮이란 그 속으로서 비로서 胎盤을 지을 것이다. 그런데 그 第一表現―멘 몬처의 努力形式은 『무엇이든지 한아씩 만들자』로 나타날 것이다. 남의 세계에는 무슨 야단이 낫든지, 먼 압헤는 무슨 영상이 보이든지, 우리는 위선 『시방 여긔서 한 무엇이 되자』를 생각하고 또 힘 쓸 것이다. 밤낫 과도기라고 스스로 寬怒하는 중 밤낫 아모 것도 몽킈고 어울리는 것이 업는 어섧힌 상태로부터 차차 대단치 아니헌 것으로라도 무엇 하나식이 되고 그것이 계단이 되어 그 우에 또 다른 建造가 잇는 修理 잇는 국면을 보게 될 것이다. 甲申年도 過渡, 甲午年도 過渡, 이때 이것도 過渡, 저때 저것도 過渡하는 동안에 모든 것이 뭉그러지고 얼크터지든 일을 생각하고는 過渡自怒論은 완전히 그림자를 거둘 것이다.

예언자 아닌 사람의게 예언을 기대하는 것은 무슨 방면으로 보든지, 우서운 일이요. 또 예언자 아닌 자로서 예언을 해보라는 부탁에 응하는 것도 똑가티 무슨 방면으로 보든지, 무서운 일이라. 고로, 십 년 후의 조선 여성계가 엇지 되겟느냐는 물음에 대해서도, 자세히 알알수업다고 예언하는 것이 예언자 아닌 자의게 잇서서는 가장 정당한 예언일 줄 안다.

조선 여성계는 조선 사회에 의존하여 잇다. 그리고 또 조선 사회의 변천의 방향은 세계적 사회의 그것과 합류하여 잇다. 그러닛가 조선 여성계의 변천의 방향을 의논하려면, 우리의 시각은 爲先 세계 전반에 떠러지지 아니할 수 업다.
전 세계에 亙하야, 봉건적 모든 질서는 벌서 자본가적 신질서에 의하야 거진 완전히 정복되엇다. 그러나 자본적 현대 질서도, 벌서 界根점에 달하야 이제 바야흐로, 급속히 그보다 더 나흔 질서로 향하야 간다. 이것이 역사 진화의 必然要則에 따르는, 세계 경향이다. 그런데, 조선 사회는 봉건적 구질서를 미처 완전히 청산하지 못하엿슴에도 불구하고, 세계 대세에 합류하지 아니하지 못하게 되엇나니 조선 사회는 방금 비빔밥적 상태에 빠저 잇다.＜19＞ 그러나 그 『비빔밥』의 제요소는 다 각각 다른 역사적 과정을 대표한 자이니 봉건적 요소와 자본가적 요소는 다가티 다음 요소에 대하야 물러가는 요소에 불과하지마는 자본가적 요소는 봉건적 요소에 대하여는 도로혀 일보 전진의 가치를 가지고 잇는 것이다.
如斯한 의견에서 생각하면, 조선 여성계 금후 변천의 방향은 명백한 일이다. 조선 여성계를 아즉 강하게 구속하고 잇는 봉건적 鐵鎖는 점점 끈어지고 자본가적 個性主義가 점점 발달될 것이다. 그러나, 그것과 동시에, 무산 계급적 요소가 점점 우세를 가질 것이다.

그러면, 그러한 변천이 금후 십 년이면 얼마만한 정도에 달할 게이냐? 우리는 그것에 대하야 두 가지로 생각할 수 잇스니 第一은, 그 동안에 사회의 劃時期적 일대 변동이 잇슬 경우 第二는, 그것이 업시, 일상에 보는 보통 정도로 변천헐 경우이다. 만일 第一의 경우가 실현된다 하면 조선 여성계는 일반 사회와 한가지 面目一新해 질 것이다. 봉건적, 현대적 모든 불합리한 구속력은 벌서 위력을 일코 자매들은 진정한 새 자유의 건설에 奔忙할 것이다. 性의 의한 性의 설음, 계급의 의한 계급의 설음 등은 가속도로 사라질 것이다. 그러나 第二의 경우이면 십 년 후의 상태는 금일의 상태의 양적 확장에 불과할 것이다. 교육바든 자의 수가 는다든지 따라서 개성의 자유에 대하야 눈뜬 자의 수가 는다든지 또는 無産婦人의 의식이 발달된다든지, 따라서 사회주의적 부인 운동이 활기를 가지게 된다든지 등은 물론 상상할 수 잇는 일이지마는 그러나 기본적 상태에 잇서서는 금일과 별 질적 변동이 업슬 것이다.
그런데 금후 십 년 동안에 조선 사회가 일대 비약적 변동을 가질 수 잇슬가 업슬가에 대하여는 나는 몰으겟다.
