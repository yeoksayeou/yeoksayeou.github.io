기사형태 잡저 (Miscellany)

Mother: "Hey! Jeongsun, marriage is something you should think about very carefully before doing, or it won't work. Lately, the trends among young people are gradually getting worse."

Daughter: "Oh dear, then I need to do it quickly, even a day sooner. What if it all gets completely worse?"

A Dream Story, Let Me Hear About It

A: "You look very good today. Did something good happen?"

B: "Yeah! My school expenses came from my hometown."

A: "That's great then. But you should pay me back a little now too."

B: (Suddenly acting innocent) "Oh, you understood me for real. That's the kind of dream I had last night."

Anything, Convenience First

Landlady: "In our house, everyone has a habit of not talking unnecessarily. When I wave my hand, know that it only means 'Come here.'"

New Maid: "Yes, that's a very good habit. If I shake my head sideways, know that it only means 'No, I won't go.'"

Where the Rabbit Went

A gentleman who went to the countryside to visit a mountain village came running and asked a child,

"Hey, hey, did you see a rabbit running this way?"

"Yes, I did."

"When did it run? Did you just see it?"

"Was it the winter before last?"

Please Give Me a Penny

A beggar chased after a gentleman in a suit,

"Sir, didn't you lose your wallet?"

"No, I didn't."

"Then please give me a penny."

I Squeezed My Towel

A young man coming from the bathhouse with a towel in his hand was heading towards a dark side alley and was about to urinate there when a patrolling police officer suddenly approached.

"Hey, hey, you can't urinate there. Where do you live?"

The policeman slowly reached into his pocket, intending to take out his notebook to write it down, so the young man thought, "This won't do," and quickly stood next to the police officer and said, "No, no. I'm just coming back from the bathhouse and I was squeezing my towel, that's all."

"What? Squeezing your towel? Then show me your towel."

"How can I? It's all wet."

"If that's the case, then forget it. But from now on, don't squeeze it on the street and try to go home and squeeze it in the bathroom."

수건 짯슴니다
목욕 갓다 오는 한 청년이 수건을 한 손에 들고 컴컴한 엽 골목을 이것 되얏다 하고 압흘 향하야 절절 오줌을 누고 잇는 판에 때 맛침 순행하던 순사가 쫏차왓다.
『여봐 여봐 그런데서 오줌누어서는 안 되여. 어듸 살어?』
하고 슬멍슬멍 포켓트에 손을 늣터니 그 의례 적는 수첩을 내랴함으로 젊은 친구 「이것 안 되얏구나」 생각하고 밧작 순사 겻흐로 닥어서 서며 『천만의 소리요. 나 지금 목욕탕에서 도라오다가 수건을 짯는데 그럽쇼.』
『무어 엇재. 수건을 짯서. 그럼 어듸 수건 좀 내노아 봐.』
『이러케 물이 저젓는데 그럽쇼.』
『그랫스면 그만두어. 그럿치만 이 다음부터는 이런 길가에서 짜지 말고 될 수 잇는 대로 집에 도라가서 변소에 가 짜아.』
