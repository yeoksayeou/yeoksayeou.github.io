To Those Who Donated Prizes

We extend our deepest gratitude to the following individuals for their sincere generosity in donating valuable prizes to encourage the grand undertaking of our company and the enthusiastic response of our many readers, in connection with the New Year's Grand Prize of One Thousand Names announced in the Byeolgeongon (別乾坤) March issue and the prize announced in the "Children" (어린이) March issue. Furthermore, we offer our best wishes for the future endeavors of the businesses operated by these individuals.

The Prize Department of Gaebyeoksa (開闢社)

懸賞品寄贈한 諸位에게
別乾坤三月號에 發表한 新年一千名大懸賞과 『어린이』三月號에 發表된 懸賞에 對하야 左記諸位로부터 本社의 壯擧와 愛讀者多數의 響應을 鼓勵키 爲하야 貴重한 賞品을 寄贈하신 그 誠意의 懇曲하심을 仰謝하오며 아울너 諸位의 經營하시는 事業의 前途를 爲하야 祝願함을 말지안슴니다.
