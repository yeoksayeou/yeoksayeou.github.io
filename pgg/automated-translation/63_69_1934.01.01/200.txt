Children's Poem, Snowman
						Yun Sŏk-chung

The snowman
I made here yesterday,
Oh dear,
No matter how much I look, it's gone.
No matter how much I look, it's gone.

Did it go
To the stream
To skate on the ice?
No, no, did someone clear it away
To someone's hearth
To warm itself by the fire? <46>
