기사제목	李忠武公時調 (Yi Ch'ungmu-gong Sijo - Poem of Admiral Yi Ch'ungmu)
기사형태	시 (Poetry)

On Hansan Island on a bright moonlit night,
I sit alone at the watchtower.
Wearing my great sword and heaving a long sigh,
From where does the sound of a northern flute (一聲胡笳) break my heart?
