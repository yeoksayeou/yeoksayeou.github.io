One Hundred Percent Modern

By Dongin

-Modern Chastity-
If lips are beautiful,
Kisses, even a hundred aren't enough.
Lovers, even a thousand aren't enough... even repeated a hundred times, love is still something new.

A butterfly seeking flowers, blown by the storm,
Only when my mouth dips into the honey of the hive,
Will those who have passed through before be aware.
Swallowtail butterfly, painted lady butterfly, yellow butterfly, white butterfly…
-30, 5, 10-
