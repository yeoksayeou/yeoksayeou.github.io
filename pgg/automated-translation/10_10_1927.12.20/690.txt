설문 (Questionnaire)

Hopes Two: Criticisms and Expectations for Byeolgeon-gon
						Gang-u of the Labor Movement Company

It is truly a lovable and welcome thing to have the uniquely beautiful monthly magazine Byeolgeon-gon (別乾坤), which brings laughter and interest to our bitter society crying out in painful lives.

Moreover, the general public feels relieved and grateful that it not only thoroughly examines the sinister figures of past dynasties but also exposes the evils hidden within society to the world, for which the general public cannot help but be grateful. At the same time, what we ask of your magazine is a reduction in the magazine price and an easier writing style. <109>
