A Year of Great Harvests: How was the life of Koreans this year? A comparison of last year and this year from various perspectives
						Dong-A Reporter I Kil-yong

Time flows and flows, and the year of Chongmyo (丁卯年) is already drawing to a close. How much harvest did the strong men of the Peninsula, who made resolutions of "During this year?" (今年 一年동안엔?) actually reap, and did they decorate the pages of Korean sports history? Now I will introduce a few words.

Originally, since it is Korean land, no matter how much progress is made, there is a feeling of imperfection, but during this year, there was a truly epoch-making great harvest even among the imperfections. If this grows reasonably, this year was truly a year of generational harvest. Why? This year, the 8th Far East Olympics, the hall of Eastern sports, was held in Shanghai, and Japan took second place in soccer for the first time in its life, and on the way back rejoicing, they made a stopover in Kyŏngsŏng (京城) and were miserably defeated by Chosŏn soccer (朝蹴) at Yŏnhŭi College (延專), which was also one of the great harvests. And in the sports day and the Shinkyo Grand Assembly (神競大會) held in Kyŏngsŏng (京城) this fall, Korean students showed superior performance in running compared to the Japanese, proving that the Korean nature is by no means inferior in sports. This is only two of the great harvests, but in any case, it firmly believed that soccer and soccer skills are the most suitable sports for Koreans and Korean land, and a sport with deep ties. What about tennis? This year's tennis reaped the second-best harvest after soccer, and that is even more so for women than men. This year's competition can be seen as the first year that Korean women's tennis skills have left the cradle stage<157>, and the performances of Kim-Ri team (金李組) of Ewha Girls' High School (梨花女高) and Hosudon (好壽敦) and the players of Chinmyŏng (進明) are worthy of praise. And in track and field, in this year's new records, in the middle and long distances of the track and in the first regular marathon, that is, running the 26 ri 4 pun 1 (26哩4分1) , the Korean Ma Pong-ok (馬鳳玉) won first place, which was a delightful victory.

In this way, we made the first record with our own hands, and we also made 8 new records with our own hands, achieving the first great event in track and field. Now, it is difficult to introduce each example in detail on a narrow page, so I will postpone it to the next opportunity, and finally, I will add one more word to the last page of this year's sports history, which has been made even more brilliant with dreams, and conclude. That is, in the sense of promoting ssirŭm (씨름), the most traditionally popular sport in Korea, in a unified manner, the organization of the Chosŏn Ssireum Association (朝鮮씨름協會) by those who have had intentions since peacetime, and the holding of the 1st All-Chosŏn Ssireum Competition (全朝鮮씨름大會) hosted by the Central Christian Youth Association, make the ending of this year's competition history even more meaningful. <158>

필자	東亞記者 李吉龍

收穫 만흔 一年이엿다, 今年一年 朝鮮사람의 生活은 엇더하엿나, 여러 方面으로 모아 본 昨年과 今年의 比較
						東亞記者 李吉龍
세월은 흘르고 또 흘러 丁卯年도 어느듯 기우럿다. 『今年 一年동안엔?』 하고 決心튼 半島의 健兒는 과연 얼마마한 收穫을 어덧스며 또 朝鮮의 體育史의 페이지를 裝飾하엿는가? 이제 몃마듸 紹介를 하야보려고 한다.
원래가 朝鮮땅이라 얼마쯤 進步된댓자 不具의 感이 업지 안흐나 그러나 今年 한해 동안에는 不具한 그 중에서도 자못 劃時期的의 큰 收穫이 잇섯스니 만일 合理로 이것이 자라난다면 今年이야말로 隔世의 收穫을 거둔 해이엇다. 왜? 今年에는 東洋 스포스의 殿堂인 제8회 극동올림픽대회가 上海에서 열렷고 이에서 日本이 蹴球에 평생 처음으로 2위를 차지하야 가지고 조하서 돌아가는 길에 京城에서 轉戰하야 朝蹴에게 延專에게 無慘히 敗하고 돌아간 그것도 큰 收穫의 하나이오 今年 가을에 京城에서 열린 體育데이와 밋 神競大會의 兒童競技에 朝鮮人 學童이 日本人 그거보다 越等한 好成績으로 走力을 나타내여 결코 朝鮮人 本質이 體育에 劣等아닌 것을 歷歷히 發明케 하엿다. 이것은 큰 收穫 중의 둘에 불과하지만 엇잿든 蹴球와 밋 蹴球技 이것이 얼마나 朝鮮사람과 朝鮮땅에 알마즌 運動이며 因緣 깁흔 運動이라는 것을 굿게 밋게 하엿다. 그 외에 庭球는 엇더한가. 今年의 庭球는 蹴球 다음가는 收穫을 거두엇스니 그것은 男子보다도 女子에게서 더욱 그러하다. 今年 大會에는 거의 朝鮮女子의 庭球技術이 搖籃의 때를 버러난 처음 해이라고<157> 볼 수 잇게 되엇스니 梨花女高의 金李組와 好壽敦 밋 進明의 選手들의 活躍은 稱讚할 갑이 잇다. 그러고 陸上競技에 今年度 新記錄 중 트랙의 中長距離와 今年 最初의 定規마라손 즉 26哩4分1의 走破에 잇서서 朝鮮人 馬鳳玉君이 一着을 하엿슴은 痛快한 勝利이엇다.
그리하야 最初記錄을 우리 손으로 지엇고 또 8종의 新記錄을 우리 손으로 지어 陸上競技의 初有盛事를 이루엇다. 이제 좁은 紙面에 낮낮치 例를 들어 紹介하기는 어려움으로 다음 機會로 밀우고 끄트로 今年 運動史의 마즈막 페이지를 더욱 燦爛하게 꿈이어진 것을 한마듸 더붓치고 말련다. 그것은 朝鮮에 가장 傳來되는 民衆的 競技 씨름을 統一的으로 獎勵하자는 意味에서 이에 平素부터 뜻을 둔 이들의 發起로 朝鮮씨름協會를 組織한 것과 또 中央基督敎靑年會主催로 제1회 全朝鮮씨름大會를 열게 된 것은 今年 競技史의 막음을 더욱 意味잇게 하는 것이다. <158>
