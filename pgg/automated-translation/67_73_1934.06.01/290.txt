종달새의 동리 (Lark's Village)
影童 (Yŏngdong)
시 (Poem)

Lark's village
beneath the flower bushes—

Dropping, dropping
Dew decorates the scenery.

Blossom, blossom
Red flower nameplates decorate.

Lark's village
Beneath the flower bushes.
-(March)-<41>
