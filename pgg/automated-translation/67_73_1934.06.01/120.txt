흘르는 人生, 漫文 (Flowing Life, Essay)
金陵人
문예기타 (Literary Miscellaneous)

Is life inherently something that flows?
Is it wandering aimlessly like a drifting cloud in the sky?
Today, leaning against the window, I gaze at the distant sky and sigh. - Oh! The sorrowful traces of life!

When I learned of Yǒng-ae (永愛) not by chance, and when I felt grateful beyond measure for receiving Yǒng-ae’s love - my sincerity towards Yǒng-ae was by no means trivial.
If a young woman's pure heart is precious and important, is a young man's sincerity any less? If love transcends everything, what use are status and wealth? My liking for Yǒng-ae was not for her beauty.
Not for her learning, nor her wealth.
Not for her family background, nor her reputation.
Was it not for her heart as clear as autumn water?
Was it not for her character as precious and graceful as a jewel? Yǒng-ae's attitude towards me was the same as mine. At least, I believed so.
In the place where pure character and character, clear heart and heart, unite - a love redder than a poppy bloomed.
In this vast world, if only two people firmly believed in each other and lived on, even if the whole world abandoned us, we would never be lonely!
Oh! Heaven, smile and
Earth, dance!
Since the creation of heaven and earth, there has never been a more touching scene than a young man and woman loving each other to their heart's content. - But time flows.
As time flows, life changes.
Even the impartiality of heaven and earth turns hot summer into cold autumn, so what foolishness to believe that the passion of love will last forever!
Yǒng-ae (永愛) turns away, casting a cold smile!
Yǒng-ae (永愛) shakes her hand and leaves! I felt like I was going mad. I was so shocked that my vision went dark.

How could this be, even if the world is vain! But thinking again, I originally had no right to love Yǒng-ae.
To love Yǒng-ae (永愛), the daughter of a noble family, beautiful, cultured, and modern - to take care of her afterwards - to satisfy her desires -
First, I had to have a lot of money!
Second, my health had to be 100% perfect!
Third, I had to have plenty of free time!
But I lacked all of these things.
Yǒng-ae (永愛) left.
She cleanly abandoned me.
While resenting Yǒng-ae, I sincerely wished for her happiness.
Time flows. A year passed.
Yǒng-ae (永愛) graduated from women's school this spring and married a wealthy medical doctor.
I received an invitation.
That day, I sent a telegram and sat in my dim boarding room, imagining the bustling scene of Yǒng-ae's wedding.
A feeling beyond description!
But I bit my lip and endured it.
I wished for Yǒng-ae's happiness again.
The flowing time -
Another two or three years passed quickly.
As I walked lonely through this rough world, my face reflected in the mirror looked haggard even to my own eyes.
But at this time, I was very depressed to hear that the married life of Yǒng-ae (永愛), whom I believed to be happy, had failed, no matter how my body had become. Why did that married life have to break down!
Why did Yǒng-ae's family have to fall and flow to the northern countryside!
Then what would become of Yǒng-ae (永愛) in the future? <20>
Leaving everything as an unsolvable riddle, time and life flow silently.
As a year or two passed, forgetfulness gradually came.
Then, just last summer! I was on a business trip to Kǔmgangsan (金剛山) on behalf of a record company I worked for.
After thoroughly exploring the world-renowned Mt. Kǔmgang, I stayed in Ŏnjǒng-ri (溫井里) for about a week to finish organizing my manuscript, and when I was waiting for the train coming from Kosǒng (高城) direction at Oegǔmgang Station (外金剛驛) on a rainy and clear morning, a haggard young woman among many people! That woman holding a child!
Ah! Isn't that definitely Yǒng-ae (永愛)?
With a feeling that I couldn't distinguish whether it was surprise or joy, I tried to move towards Yǒng-ae's side, but Yǒng-ae (永愛) quickly looked away! Then am I mistaking her for someone else?
Only then did I realize that my eyes were mistaken. And I shuddered at the thought of almost being embarrassed by a stranger.
That's right. "There's no way a woman as healthy as Yǒng-ae (永愛) could be so haggard. Yǒng-ae (永愛), the athlete! She was full of energy and had a tender body that seemed like blood would spurt out if you poked her. She was not the owner of a pale face like that woman.
And Yǒng-ae's expression was clear and full of life.
She wasn't someone timid and afraid of other people's eyes like that woman."
I was thinking this when I got on the train.
The train runs close to the vast East Sea right under my hand, heading towards Anbyǒn Station (安邊驛).
I was staring intently outside at the strange scenery that I had never imagined. The train passed Songdae Station (松田驛) in no time, and it seemed to be running east, but even that was none of my concern.
"Sir!"
I suddenly turned around at the sound of someone calling from behind. It was that haggard woman I saw earlier.
I didn't know what was going on and just blinked my eyes.
"It's Yǒng-ae (永愛)!"
I didn't know. Then is this woman really Yǒng-ae (永愛)?
"When I saw you recognize me earlier, I pretended not to know you on purpose! So you were completely fooled into thinking I was someone else."
I still couldn't say anything.
"But since we've met so coincidentally, if I just pass by without acknowledging you, I might never see you again... Who knows, maybe we'll never be able to see each other again..."
I was only overwhelmed with mixed emotions and couldn't speak.
"I saw you several times in Ŏnjǒng-ri (溫井里) . So I even found out why you came here. But I pretended not to know until the end!"
"Please sit here."
I finally opened my mouth and made room.
"No, I won't."
"Why not?"
"I have to go to that other car soon."
"Why?"
Yǒng-ae (永愛) smiled sadly and didn't answer. Ah! This sad expression! It wasn't the Yǒng-ae (永愛) of the old days.
"What brings you to Kǔmgangsan (金剛山)?"
"Please don't ask."
"Where are you going?"
"Please don't ask that either?"
"Why not?"
"Please don't ask that either."
I was speechless at Yǒng-ae's attitude.
"Sir! You've deteriorated a lot."
Yǒng-ae (永愛) looked at my face with deep emotion, and her eyes seemed to fill with tears.
"You too, Yǒng-ae-ssi (永愛氏)!"
"It's only natural for me, though!"
Yǒng-ae (永愛) replied and quietly sighed.
I also felt a feeling of wanting to cry welling up from my chest.
Even if the resentment I had towards Yǒng-ae (永愛) was buried in my heart, seeing Yǒng-ae's lonely face, which seemed to hold a thousand unspeakable circumstances, the resentment seemed to melt away like spring snow.
"Please sit down!"
I couldn't stop Yǒng-ae (永愛) from leaving next to me. As I held down my heart that wanted to cry - the train arrived at Anbyǒn Station (安邊驛).
"I'm getting off here."
Yǒng-ae (永愛) came next to me again, left these simple words, and tried to get off the train.
"Then aren't you going straight to Kyǒngsǒng (京城)? Is Wǒnsan (元山) your hometown?"
"Well, please don't ask about things like that."
Yǒng-ae (永愛) got off the train.
The train left again.
As the train turned a mountain corner, the figure of Yǒng-ae (永愛) looking at this place from afar on the platform was faintly visible.
I stared blankly out the train window.
Clouds floated in the darkening western sky.
Ah - is life also flowing endlessly like those clouds?
A riddle! That's right, isn't everything an unsolvable riddle? <21>
Why did Yǒng-ae's appearance change like that?
What on earth is Yǒng-ae doing now?
Whose child is that child? Was Yǒng-ae dreaming of living with another man again?
Why did she come to Kǔmgangsan (金剛山)? It's all unknowable. It's something I can't ask anyone.
But it's something I don't even need to ask.
That too, the flowing time has passed another year. Today, even the late spring rain is falling softly on the asphalt.
The sound of spring rain seeping into the heart of a sorrowful life! The sound of spring rain whispering faintly even when I try to block my ears to avoid hearing it!
Strangely, the sound of spring rain draws out a strand or two of memories.
But what's the point of thinking!
Since life is originally changing and flowing-. <65>
