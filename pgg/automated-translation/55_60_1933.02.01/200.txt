Humor Collection

Excessive Kindness
Shopkeeper: "Madam, if you make a dress out of this and wear it, you will look 10 years younger."
Female Customer: "What are you saying? ... I'm only twenty years old. If I look 10 years younger, will I look like a ten-year-old child?"

Because He is Small
At a circus, a dwarf applied to be a lion tamer.
Staff: "How can someone as small as you handle such a large lion?"
Little Man: "That's right. Being small will rather be helpful."
Staff: "Why is that?"
Little Man: "When the lion sees me, it will think I'm a child and think about eating me when I get bigger."

Ignorant Clever Plan
A: "What is that... a map!"
B: "Um- I was thinking of traveling a bit..."
A: "Then why are you looking at it upside down?"
B: "Oh, oh... No, I'm looking for the way to return now..."

My Silly Father's Cuteness
Child: "Father! Please help me with this math homework." <40>
The father, getting angry!
Father: "What, what... There is liquor that costs 1 won and 50 jeon per doe, and if you mix five hop of water into it and sell it, what will be the price of one doe of liquor? Hey, you damned son! If there is a liquor store like that, you should quickly report it. Didn't the school teach you first not to buy liquor from such a place?"

White Labor
Manager: "Have you had any labor experience before?"
Job Seeker: "Yes, of course. I've done labor that makes me turn white every day!"
Manager: "Huh- labor that turns you white? You must be misSpeaking and mean labor that turns you pitch black!"
Job Seeker: "No, you're mistaken. I used to work at a rice mill."

Awkward Hospitality
Husband: "Honey, if guests come over and you're going to serve snacks, you should buy something better. Why did you buy something like this?"
The wife's face only turned red and she remained silent! ... .
This is not unreasonable. The snacks were brought by the guest who just arrived... . <41>
