李愛利秀의 情死騷動, 빅타專屬 流行歌手 李愛利秀, 劇團의 名花이든 李愛利秀 (The Suicide Attempt of Yi Ae-risu, Yi Ae-risu, the Exclusive Popular Singer of Victor Records, and Yi Ae-risu, the Celebrated Beauty of the Theater Troupe)
春郞生
문예기타 (Literary Miscellaneous)

Like the sound of a nightingale flowing from a mountain valley, Miss Yi Ae-risu is good at singing popular songs – Yi Ae-risu, who showed off her seductive figure on the stage, and her beloved, Bae Dong-pil (裵東必), a student at Yonhi College (延禧專門學校), took "Calmotin" at 3:30 on January 9th, lost their minds, and then, resenting every second of their remaining lives, cut their arteries with a sharp knife and desperately ended their lives. Yi Ae-risu committed suicide. Embracing her lover, she left this world. But fortunately or unfortunately, Yi Ae-risu, who had left the world, revived in the dimly lit ward of the university hospital, so how will their future unfold after their suicide (情死) turns into survival (情生)? Everyone who knows them is anxiously wondering, so let's dig up the facts leading up to Ae-risu's suicide and discuss them together.

Ae-risu, born and raised in Gaeseong, always harbored dissatisfaction in her young heart due to her unstable and troubled family, and after joining the theater troupe, Ae-risu's nature, which was good at distinguishing and understanding, raised its head and became like this, so she only had men to rely on. Therefore, she became close to men (actors), and her life became rough.

Back then, it was a time when old-style theater troupes! Shinpa theater! Korean theater had not yet established itself, so there was no one to guide Ae-risu's path, so she wandered without finding her way, not knowing the path to take as an actress or as a woman. So Ae-risu became ill as a person.

Time passed as men's shadows never left Ae-risu, and when the **'s theater troupe finally established itself, she became a popular actress. As Ae-risu grew and the Korean theater troupe also progressed, Ae-risu became an exclusive popular singer for "Victor Records" (빅타 專屬) after going through "Columbia Records" (컬럼비아), monopolizing popularity, and Ae-risu's life became admirable. However, on the other side of Ae-risu, there is a sad story hidden. Mother - Ae-risu, who cannot raise her child with her own hands, becomes a weeping mother. A weeping mother, a laughing Ae-risu, her life of weeping and laughing.

About five or six years ago, when she appeared on stage and captivated the audience, Yi Gwang-ryeol (李光烈), a young wealthy man with the power to support the theater troupe, newly joined, and their eyes met by chance, unraveling the thread of love. However, unable to resist the people of the theater troupe surrounding the beautiful Ae-risu, the people who tried to get close to her with money, and the confusing environment, the thread of love with Yi became entangled.

"I don't want to do theater anymore."

"Why?"

"I want a quieter life."

"Why?"

"To put it simply, I miss having a family."

The two left the theater troupe and formed a new family. But Yi already had a wife and a young child.

"I have a wife and a young child, but don't think of me as a concubine."

"When I first fell in love with you, I didn't think about having a wife, a young child, or a concubine. Why are you saying that now?"

"Then it's my fault."

The two lived happily together again, living an idyllic life with the main wife and a small house under the same roof in front of Yi's mother. So she recorded songs and had a child. Yi, unable to handle household matters or the things that came his way, brought discord to the family. Yi went bankrupt. As a result, the love between Ae-risu and Yi also drifted apart. Seeing Ae-risu, who smiled at the world, who would say she was crying? <14>

"You and I are done here."

"Why?"

"Live happily with your wife who resents me."

Yi's wife, a pure and innocent wife, loved her husband so much that she was willing to accept anything her husband did and decided to give love and sacrifice to the two. However, Ae-risu's past, which was not smooth, and her feelings as she tried to start again were quite complicated.

Yi decided to divorce his wife and expressed his intention to make Ae-risu his legal wife on the family register (戶籍上), and then wandered around trying to rekindle the broken love, so Yi became the most miserable person, and Ae-risu became bored and longed for a new life. As Yi Ae-risu's popularity as a popular singer increased, a huge mass called vanity pressed down on her light body. Ae-risu running with vanity! Ae-risu going out with a child in her arms!

"I'm leaving. Goodbye."

Yi's legal wife, holding a child, blocking her way!

"Why are you leaving? If you leave, I'll leave. What's the point of a loveless couple living for thousands of years? So don't leave and live happily."

Ae-risu took her hand and said,

"I have to go. What's the point of staying longer when I'm in a position to leave?"

"I'm divorced. But I'm leaving without saying anything. It's because I love him. Ae-risu should love the person I love."

A pitiful wife trying to sacrifice! A concubine as a mother cutting off love!

A fight between two women! But Yi, unable to abandon his beloved wife and unwilling to part with Ae-risu, grabbed Ae-risu and begged, pleaded, and made various final appeals.

"Ae-risu! I really didn't know that such misfortune would befall us when we first met."

"... ..."

Silent Ae-risu! Yi blocking her! Beside them, the wife hoping! The sound of two different children crying, breaking the silence in the room, the parents' breaths!

"Ae-risu, if you abandon me, I'll go crazy, so if you pity me, don't abandon me, hold on to me, huh."

After that, Ae-risu gathered money from records and moved her house from Gaeseong to Seoul, living a solitary life, forgetting the past. But the child she couldn't raise with her own hands is now growing up healthy in Yi's house, calling for her mother, so how can others fathom the longing between mother and child.

Everyone could see Bae Dong-pil (裵東必), a promising student at Yonhi College (延禧專門學校), and Ae-risu walking side by side on the night streets. Bae, who had been married twice, came to love Ae-risu as a mother, so their rough love was roughly tied, but could it be said that the remaining pure heart (純情) firmly tied the bond? Anyway, they vowed to live together for a hundred years. Unable to control the burning, hidden feelings, Ae-risu, weary of the past and present life, complained to Bae.

But Bae's father refused and rejected the idea of living with Ae-risu. A fight with his father over love! Their relationship grew distant, and the two became pathetic.

Ae-risu crying and laughing! Bae wandering around, unable to suppress his lingering feelings as the days and months passed, cursing his current life, which was not economically permitted, hating his father, and Ae-risu was tormented by the rising shadows of the past.

It was a winter night, January 9th. The two walked shoulder to shoulder, moving their heavy legs, ate dinner at Jeongjaok (精z玉), pawned their watches for the last time, bought six and a half "Calmotins" (칼모린), and returned home in heavy silence, closing the door.

"Ae-risu, I'm going to die."

"... ..."

Ae-risu didn't want to die. How could she take off the yoke of vanity that wouldn't come off? Bae, a young student seeking love, decided to end his life to escape the cursed environment.

"Ae-risu, I'm going to die."

Ae-risu rushes in as he tries to take the medicine! "I'm going to die too."

They lost their minds. And cut their arteries. Their tlarud (???) to end their lives, which would not die easily, must have been simple.

"Why does dying solve everything?"

The last day of the two men and women who left a few words of farewell to the world.

"Ah! I'm dizzy."

"Now it's our world."

"Love, love, our love is..."

Ae-risu lost her mind and rolled around embracing Bae as if falling off a cliff. Yi Ae-risu and Bae Dong-pil committed suicide.

The two people who died and came back to life have become living corpses and are now counting each other's pulse on the bed of the university hospital, so how will they face the world?

Suicide, dying with love. Then, love and death, was there such a heartbreaking, pure love? The disease obtained from the vanity that shakes the heads of Korean men and women today makes them think of suicide as a modern thing and stage a comedy. It may be a sad thing for those who committed suicide, but those who see it from the side cannot help but point fingers and laugh.

The fact that these people had no other path to take but the path of love is because Koreans lack the common sense they should have. How grateful it would have been if they had fulfilled the responsibilities and duties they should have as members of society (社會). It is embarrassing to our neighbors. Are we, who wander the streets without night, now going to laugh and cry with love and eventually end our lives?

In conclusion, I hope that love, like suicide, has died, so I hope that they will cut off the love that springs up again and become young people who learn the responsibilities and duties that Koreans should have and move forward.

산골작이에서 흘너오는 꾀꼬리 소리와 가티 류행노래 잘하는 리애리수양(李愛利秀孃)-무대 우에서 요염한 자태를 보혀주든 리애리수양이 그의 사랑하는 사람 연전학생 배동필(延傳學生裵東必)과 지난 일월 구일밤 세시반에 「카루모징」을 난호아 먹고 정신을 일흔 후 다시 일분 일초라도 목숨이 부터 잇는 것을 원망하면서 날카로운 칼로 동맥을 끈코 악착하게 목숨을 끈엇다. 리애리수가 정사를 햇다. 사랑하는 사람과 얼사안고 이 세상을 떠나갓다. 그러나 다행이라할가 불행하다할가 세상을 떠나갓든 리애리수가 대학병원 어슴푸레한 병실에서 다시 사러낫스니 정사(情死)에서 정생(情生)을 한 그네들의 압길이 장차 엇더케 전개될 것인가. 그네들을 아는 사람이야 누구나 할 것업시 안타가히 얼녀고 하는 문제이니 애리수가 정사를 하기까지의 사실을 들추어서 다가티 비판을 갓자.
곳은 개성 개성에서 나고 개성에서 자란 애리수는 안존하지 못하고 순조롭지 못한 가정에서 어린 가슴에 항상 불평을 품엇다가 극단에 몸을 내놋케 된 그 후부터 압가림 잘하고 이해를 잘 가리는 애리수의 성질이 고개를 들고 이러스게 되엿스니 다만 외로운 몸을 의지할 곳은 남자들에게 잇섯다. 그래서 남자들(배우들)과 갓가히 하게 되엿시 때문에 생활이 거칠게 되엿다.

애리스는 정신을 빼앗기고 낭떠러지에서 떠러지는 거와 가티 배(裵)를 얼싸안고 딍구럿다. 이애리스와 배동필이 정사를 햇다.

죽엇다 사러난 두 사람은 산송이 되어 지금 대학병원 침대우에서 서서로 맥박의 소리를 세이고 잇스니 엇더케 세상 사람들을 대할 것인가.

정사, 정을 안고 죽는다. 그러면 사랑과 죽엄 그러케도 안타가은 순결한 사랑이 잇섯든가 현재 조선 남녀들의 머리를 흔드러 놋는 허영에서 어든 병이 정사를 한 개의 모던으로 생각하기 때문에 희극을 연출하게 되는 것이다. 정사를 한 사람들에게 슯흔 일이겟스나 엽헤서 보는 사람은 손고락질해서 웃지 안을 수가 업다.

이 사람 사람의 거러갈 길이 사랑의 길밧게 업섯든 것이 너무나 조선 사람이 가저야할 상식이 업기 때문이다. 좀 더 사회(社會)인으로서 가저야할 책임(責任)과 의무(義務)를 다하여 주엇드면 얼마나 고마웟슬가. 이웃 나라 사람들 보기 붓구러운 일이다. 밤이 업는 거리를 헤메는 우리들이 지금 사랑을 안고 웃고 울고 나종엔 목숨까지 끄너야 할 것일가.
나종 끗흐로 바라는 것은 정사와 가티 사랑도 죽엇스니 다시 샘솟는 사랑을 끄너버리고 조선사람이 가저야 할 책임과 의무를 배우며 압길을 향개 나가는 젊은이가 되기를 빌고 바란다.
