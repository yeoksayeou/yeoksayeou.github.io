Editor's Notes

As we welcome the New Year, our company will further refresh and expand its business, and at the same time, we will republish the women's magazine *Sinyeosong* (新女性) and move the company building to the upper floor of the Cheondogyo Memorial Hall (天道敎紀念館), which is newly built within the grounds. We hope that everyone will be happy about the company's daily progress.

We intended to publish this February issue earlier than the New Year's issue, but due to the busy schedules of the writers at the end of the year, the manuscripts were delayed, and as mentioned above, the editing schedule was also slightly delayed due to the relocation of the company building, so it did not go as planned.

However, this issue deals with many *Joseon* (朝鮮) current affairs articles, including the debate over the dissolution of the *Singanhoe* (新幹會), which is a major issue in present-day *Joseon*, as well as the three major joint issues of Christianity, Cheondogyo, and *Gyeongin Chwido* (京仁取), and protests to the Nanjing government of China for the one million Korean compatriots in *Manchuria*. The content is quite substantial, so anyone who wants to know about *Joseon* and worries about *Joseon* must read it at least once. (Cha)

As for international articles, the activities of the Chinese Communist Party, which are once again disturbing the unified China, a meeting with the *Lama* Law King, and the movements of the Japanese *Minseito* (民政黨) cabinet are all worth reading. In addition, we recommend *Nettsson's* (넷썬스) true story collection and *Nettsson's* roundtable discussion as leisure articles. (Chae)

In addition, the *Chungoe Ilbo* (中外日報) issue, the Practical Women's School (實踐女學校) issue, and the suburban tram issue, which have been unresolved since last year and are 궁금 (gunggeum) to everyone, as well as the issues related to the Pre-Employment Frontline (就職前哨戰) written for this spring's graduates and the lifestyle improvement issue, are also articles that need to be read at least once. (Park)

Apology

We apologize to the writers and readers that we could not publish the full text of *Yang Heon-su's* (梁憲洙) "Critique of the Dissolution of the *Singanhoe*" (新幹會解消批判論) and *Buk Heung-saeng's* (北熊生) "Lament of the *Joseon* Infantry" (朝鮮步兵隊 新世타령) in the Debate on the Dissolution of the *Singanhoe* due to unavoidable circumstances. (Company Announcement) <178> <178-178>

잡지명	별건곤 제37호
발행일	1931년 02월 01일

기사형태	사고·편집후기

編輯落書
新年을 마지하며 본사는 사업을 일층 刷新확장하야 여성잡지 신여성을 또다시 續刊하는 동시에 사옥도 構內新築한 天道敎紀念館 上層으로 이전하게 되엿슴니다. 여러분도 다갓치 본삿 社運의 日益進展됨을 깃버하야 주시옵소서.

이번 2월호는 신년호보다도 발행을 좀 더 일즉이 하랴고 하얏더니 연말연시에 필자 여러분의 多忙한 관계로 원고도 느저지고 우에 말슴한 바와 가티 사옥의 이전관계로 편집 시일도 조금 느저저서 여의하게 되지 못하얏슴니다.

그러나 이번호는 『現下朝鮮에서 一大문제되는 신간회의 解消贊否論을 비로하야 기독교, 천도교, 京仁取等 三大합동문제와 在滿백만동포를 위하야 중국 남경정부에 항의 등등.... 朝鮮實事情記事를 만히 취급하니만큼 내용이 자못 충실하야 조선을 알고자하고 조선을 걱정하는 이로는 누구나 한번 꼭 일어야만하게 되엿슴니다.(車)

국제기사로서 現下南北통일된 중국의 천하를 재차 소란케하는 중국공산당의 활약과 라마법왕회견기, 일본민정당내각의 운동...은 누구나 또 일을만하고 그 외 넷썬스 실화집, 넷썬스 좌담회는 취미기사로 또한 일독을 권하고 십슴니다.(蔡)

그리고 昨年來로 미해결하야 누구나 궁금하게 녁이는 中外日報문제, 實踐여학교문제, 교외전차문제 등과 今春의 졸업생을 위하야 쓴 就職前哨戰에 관한 문제, 생활개선문제 등도 또한 한번 읽을 필요가 잇는 기사임니다.(朴)

謝告
