I Mourn My Pale Heart

Roa

Like a cold butterfly
Teasing my eyes
Faintly, faintly dancing white snowflakes <173>

The dark gray sky that swallowed the stars...
Like helpless hands that haven't held anything
Trembling, hidden in coat pockets.

The frozen breath of a frozen corpse
Hangs heavily on my back!
Thinking of the attached wallpaper Lenin
Seeking a little rest
The sound of footsteps returning to the pillow, covered in frost of worries
Thud, thud, thud....
Digested matter passed through the large intestine ten hours ago.

A goblin fire-like fantasy
Leaping from head to intestines.
Fragments of thought leaping from fragment to fragment.
Oh, the state you're in!

Today, too,
On the vast battlefield
You were a dazed soldier
Without a single iron bullet.

Still, you <174>
Leaving behind the bleeding, fallen XX war dead
Seeking a flower with a beautiful dream
Racing across the wilderness
Oh, pale heart!

Shall I commit suicide
Or take a nap

The sentimentality of an intellectual is boundless.
There was a time when a single gray beard of emotion
Dominated creative desire.
An old monkey suckling a baby monkey
Taught art with tearful emotion.

Resonates with the tears of mana
Who learned animal motherhood and
Charity.

Why hesitate?
Since life has left your attention
Your heart has turned pale.......

Do not attempt to apply
Dangerous colors with clumsy skill <175>

Isn't there a belt here?
In the factory
A wide leather strap where the gears and machines
Spin the wheels.....

Do you not have the courage
To hang this belt over your life and head?
Do you not have the courage
To temper your pale heart
In a boiling iron cauldron?
