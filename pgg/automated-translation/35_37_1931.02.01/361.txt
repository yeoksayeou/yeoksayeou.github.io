Welcoming Submissions

Please send in many works of literature such as poems, Sijo (時調), novels, and plays. We greatly welcome works from anyone.

Please write "Submission Manuscript (投稿原稿)" in red ink on the envelope.

Once a manuscript is received, it will absolutely not be returned. <163>
