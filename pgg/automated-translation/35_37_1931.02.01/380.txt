A One-Act Play, A Woman Hospitalized
						P'yŏngyang Son Yŏng-ryŏl (Contribution)

Woman: "Doctor! If I have pleurisy, will I be hospitalized?"

Doctor: "No, no. Even if it's pleurisy, it's very mild, so there's no need to be hospitalized. Just go home and rest for about ten days."

Woman: "No! I'm definitely going to be hospitalized."

Doctor: "No! There's no need to be hospitalized... do as you wish."

Woman: "Hey, come in here and give me a compress."

Pharmacist: "Yes, I'm coming. I have to finish making the medicine first."

Woman: "Hey, please attach this 5 won here."

Pharmacist: "By the way, is this a one-won bill?"

Woman: "When I came from home, I had 200 won, but this money is left over, so I'm sending it back to my mother."

Pharmacist: "Is it possible to have money left over? By the way, don't you have a father?"

Woman: "This person really likes to talk! My father died when I was young."

Pharmacist: "I'm sorry, but let me ask you one more thing. Who was the person you called on the phone a while ago?"

Woman: "This person! Are you being nosy?"

Pharmacist: "What's wrong with that?"

Woman: "To be frank, the secretary at XX Transportation is my fourth cousin, and the driver at XX Automobile is my sixth cousin."

Pharmacist: "Oh my! You have so many cousins!"

Woman: "Hey, Miss! Is the person who works at the pharmacy married?"

Maid (bringing a meal to the ward): "No."

Woman: "Does he have money at home?"

Maid: "I don't know."

Woman: "Is the student from Higher (General) school who comes here... who is he?" <168>

Maid: "That... he's the director's nephew."

Woman: "Oh! Is that so? Where is his house?"

Maid: "I think it's somewhere in the city."

Woman: "In the city?.....Does the director here have a concubine?"

Maid: "Oh my goodness! You ask all sorts of things! Why would he have a concubine?!"

Woman: "............"

Woman: "How old was the person who just left when he got married?"

Pharmacist: "About twenty-four or five years old."

Woman: "You seem to know him very well!"

Pharmacist: "Of course! Of course I know him well... Is he your cousin too?"

Woman: "Of course! He's my maternal fourth cousin."

Pharmacist: "What does he do?"

Woman: "Him?.......He is a teacher at XX Province XX Public Elementary School. He said he's coming again this evening."

Pharmacist: "You must be happy to have so many cousins?"

Maid (mentioned earlier): "Hey, did you see something strange?!"

Child of the Director: "What?"

Maid: "Ah! In the ward, a woman with a high collar and a hospitalized woman were rubbing their foreheads together!"

Child who understands: "Really?"

Maid: "Really! And when I was about to go in with water, they turned off the light! I don't know why they turned it off.... Anyway, I don't know who it was?"

Child who understands: "Hey, the pharmacy said that he is her cousin."

Maid: "Oh, a cousin?! I wonder?!"

Woman: "Ah, I need some money. What should I do?"

Man (Teacher mentioned earlier. And the protagonist of the children's conversation): "Didn't you get 5 won yesterday?"

Woman: "I used that money for medicine and spent it all."

Man: "That's all the money I have!"

Woman: "Hmph! What am I supposed to do with that small amount of money?"

Man: "Then...why don't you withdraw some of your savings?"

Woman: "Oh my! If I lose that 200 won, what will become of me?"

Man: "Oh, don't say that. More money will come along."

Woman (pouting)

Man (Teacher mentioned earlier): "Hey! Where did the woman who was hospitalized in that room go?"

Pharmacist: "I heard she was discharged last night."

Man: "What?"

Pharmacist: "Yes! Last night........With some man."

Man (licking his lips): "Oh!"
-(1931. 1. 1)- <169>
