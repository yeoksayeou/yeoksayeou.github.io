잡지명	별건곤 제4호
발행일	1927년 02월 01일
기사제목	米國, 中國, 日本에 다녀온 女流人物評判記 ,해외에서는 무엇을 배웟스며 도라와서는 무엇을 하는가? (A Critique of Female Figures Who Have Returned from America, China, and Japan: What did they learn abroad and what are they doing now?)
기사형태	대담·좌담 (Dialogue/Symposium)

A. Let’s talk about the female figures who have returned from abroad! Which country has the most returnees and who is the most active?
K. In terms of both quality and quantity, I think those who returned from Japan are the best. The next would be students from America, and then a few who studied in China. What else is there? Anyway, as far as I know, the students from Japan are the best.

A. Then, please tell me about those who returned from Japan.
K. What is there to say? The world already knows. But roughly speaking, I can see it like this. First, there are seven or eight graduates of women's higher normal schools. In terms of seniority and fame, Son Chŏng-gyu at Kyŏngsŏng Women's High School, Sŏng Ŭi-gyŏng at Sukmyŏng are well-known. Then there is Cho Kyŏng-hŭi at Sukmyŏng, Chŏn Chi-ja at Chinmyŏng, Pak Sŏng-hwan and Kim Sun-yŏng at Tongdŏk. Im Suk-jae moved far away to Taegu Women's High School, and Song Kŭm-sŏn moved to Chinju Ilsin Women's High School. Besides them, there is Hwang Kwi-gyŏng, who graduated from a women's commercial school in Tokyo and is now teaching at Kyŏngsŏng Women's Commercial School; Im Sun-bun, a gymnastics school graduate who teaches gymnastics at Kyŏngsŏng Women's High School; Pang Sin-yŏng from Chŏngsin, who returned from the Young Nutrition Institute; Chang Sŏn-hŭi, a Tokyo Art School embroidery graduate at Kyŏngsŏng Women's Art School; and Pak Chŏng-sik, who doesn’t teach but works in administration at Kŭnhwa School after graduating from Sakurai Women's School. Then there are about four or five medical graduates: Hŏ Yŏng-suk, Hyŏn Tŏk-sin, I Tŏk-yo, and Yu Yŏng-jun, who graduated from Tokyo Women's Medical College, and Kil Chŏng-hŭi, who spent a year in a research lab at Tokyo Imperial University. Then there are Ch’oe Ŭn-hŭi, Hwang Sin-dŏk, and I Hyŏn-gyŏng, who graduated from the Women's College of Social Studies, and Kim Sŏn, who graduated from Kobe Theological Seminary.

A. That sounds like a lot! Is there anyone else?
K. Of course, there are more. There's Pang In-gŭn and Tŏn Yu-dŏk, who is someone's wife, who went to Japan, but I don't know which school they attended. And Kim Wŏn-ju and Kim Myŏng-sun, who are famous in the women's world, both tasted a bit of Japan. That's all I know.

A. Which teacher is considered the best?
K. How can I say who is better or worse? They are all talented and qualified, so they must have considerable skill as educators. The very fact that they are teachers makes it a suitable profession for women. But how can I judge who is better or worse on an individual basis? I can't rely on the students' gossip. Anyway, Son and Sŏng are already famous, and Im Suk-jae, who went to Taegu, is said to have gained considerable trust.

A. Why did Song Kŭm-sŏn, who was supposed to study in America last year, go to Chinju? And is Hwang Kwi-gyŏng the wife of Yŏn Hak-nyŏn, the activist? I saw in the newspaper that she was enjoying her life as a housewife, but now she's a teacher at the Women's Commercial School. That's news to me.
K. Song was ready to go with her travel documents, but she couldn't go because she had some illness. Hwang's transition from home to the classroom can be seen as a growing professional awakening among Korean women.

A. That's right. Women's professional awakening is the front line of liberation. But I feel sorry for her since she has two or three children.
K. Yes, it’s a problem for mothers with children to be working women.
A. Are there only five medical graduates from Japan? I know those five well, but Hŏ Yŏng-suk seems to have set sail on a course that deviated from her original path in academia. <21> Seeing her working as a newspaper reporter now, she must have considerable literary talent and cultivation. As Ch’unwŏn's wife, she must have had a lot of literary tastes, so why did she study medicine? She even opened a hospital in Sŏdaemun-dong 1-chome for a while, but now her friend Kil Chŏng-hŭi's husband, Kim T'ak-wŏn, has opened a mental hospital there. Anyway, that family has a strange connection. Most female doctors have fulfilling family lives, but Hŏ and Ch’unwŏn had a scandalous love affair from the beginning, and now they work together in the new Dong-A Ilbo building and live comfortably in a new mansion in Sungsam-dong. Hŏ has been infertile for over ten years of marriage, but now she is happily expecting a child. Hyŏn Tŏk-sin is also living a happy life with Ch’oe Wŏn-sun of Dong-A Ilbo near Tongdaemun Hospital, and her pastime is holding her chubby son. I Tŏk-yo is living a cozy life with Han Wi-gŏn of Dong-A Ilbo in a rented room in Kantong. Kil's husband, Kim, has opened a hospital, and she works at Tongdaemun Hospital. They have similar interests in their work. Yu Yŏng-jun is living a lonely life as a doctor at the Ehwa Hakdang and Taehwa Clinic, trying to save patients struggling in suffering. Happiness will come to her as well. And Ch’oe Ŭn-hŭi, Hwang Sin-dŏk, and I Hyŏn-gyŏng! These three are university graduates and social researchers, so their activities seem meaningful. Ch’oe is considered a good female reporter. Some people worry that she might fail because she is too aggressive in the male-dominated world. Hwang seems more composed. We'll have to wait and see how Ch’oe of Chosŏn Ilbo, Hwang of Chungoe, and Hŏ of Dong-A perform. I works at the Women's Association and must be working hard. And Kim Sŏn! I've heard a lot about her, but what is she doing now? I haven't heard from her lately.
K. Kim Sŏn is enjoying family life with I Wŏn-sik, a teacher at Paejae School in Hŭngjŏng-dong.

A. You mentioned Tŏn Yu-dŏk earlier. It seems she is coming. Tŏn was very active in the Chosŏn Mundan magazine and even became the printer of the magazine for a while. When writers gathered at her house in Yongdu-ri, outside Tongdaemun, she was busy entertaining guests, raising children, and writing articles. Lately, Chosŏn Mundan has been transferred to another place, and I don't know what she is doing now. Why are Kim Wŏn-ju and Kim Myŏng-sun called famous figures in the women's world?
K. If they aren't famous figures, then who is? Having four or five love affairs and getting married once a year, that's enough to be famous. What other conditions do you need? They brazenly express their opinions at social gatherings, publish new views on chastity in newspapers, write poems, write novels, and work as reporters. Isn't the world too ridiculous?

A. Don't they deserve some sympathy? Society is partly to blame.
K. What blame is there for society? It's all self-inflicted. I don't think they deserve any sympathy.
A. You are too harsh! You were worried that I would be critical.
K. It's not criticism, anyone would say the same.
A. I heard that Kim Myŏng-sun has joined the Maeshin Daily as a reporter. With her literary talent, she will be quite successful. And she cut her hair.
K. I don't know about her. But it's good. It will be another topic for men to talk about.

Chinese Students

A. Now, can you tell me about the students who studied in China?
K. There is not much to say about Chinese students. The first one would be Yu Kak-kyŏng. Yu Yŏng-jun and Pak Kyŏng-sik went to China for a while. Yu speaks Chinese quite well, maybe even better than Japanese. Pak Ho-jin might have gone too.

A. Oh! Yu Kak-kyŏng is a Chinese student. I didn't know she was from the Chinese academic world. What school did she graduate from? Does Yu Yŏng-jun speak Chinese that well? He doesn't seem to show it. I thought Pak Kyŏng-sik was a Japanese student, but he went to China too? What is Pak doing now?
K. Pak Kyŏng-sik may not be well known, but he is very solid. He may seem cold on the outside, but he is very friendly when you talk to him. He stayed in Japan for a while too. Since last year, he has been working on the "New Woman" (Sin Yŏsŏng) magazine at the Kaebyŏk Publishing House. The magazine is temporarily suspended, so he is probably resting at home. Recently, women's section reporters from various newspapers have been bothering him, and his opinions and impressions have been published a lot.

A. There are not many Chinese students, so they are not very noticeable. But Yu Kak-kyŏng must be the leader! She teaches children and students at the Anguk-dong kindergarten and Taehwa School, and she must be working hard. She is also very active with the Young Women's Christian Association and is well-respected in the women's world.
K. Of course.

American Students

A. There seem to be quite a few women who came from America?
K. There are more than from China, but not that many, and they don't seem to be doing much. It's obvious. Kim Mi-ri-sa, the principal of Kŭnhwa School, Kim Ae-sok, Kim Hwal-lan, and I Ŭl-ra of Ehwa Women's College, and Hong Esther of the Women's Theological Seminary. And Yun Ch'i-ho's daughter, Hwal-lan. There might be more in the provinces.

A. Kim Mi-ri-sa probably came from America a long time ago. And establishing the Kŭnhwa School was a good thing.
K. It wasn't that long ago, but she can be considered a veteran among those from America. Establishing the Kŭnhwa School was a good thing, but if she doesn't hold on to it and see it through, it will be a failure, and she will be criticized by society.

A. That's right. But she seems sincere, but she has financial difficulties. If she does well, it will be good. What is the reputation of Kim Hwal-lan?
K. I haven't heard much about her. She was once touted as a female orator, but these days there is not much freedom for speeches and lectures in Korea, so I haven't had a chance to hear Kim's oratory.

A. What is Yun Hwal-lan doing? When she first came, she couldn't speak Korean and only knew how to say hello. When she learned to say "goodbye" to adults, it became a joke. Is she just staying home to learn Korean properly?
K. She is working at an English newspaper in Tokyo to make money.
A. I didn't know that. She is good at English, so it is perfect for her to work at an English newspaper. Are there any students from other countries? Like England or Germany?
K. I haven't heard of any.

Final Thoughts

A. I understand the situation of foreign students well. It seems that the students from Japan have the best performance in every aspect, and the students from America and China are so-so, but America is better than China. But shouldn't students from each country have their own unique characteristics?
K. What unique characteristics? They just imitate the country they came from. Those who came from Japan smell like Japan, and those who came from America smell like America. That's all.

A. That's the problem. When they come back, they should compare and contrast the situations and become role models in every way. They should be Korean women and global women at the same time. Students from Japan, students from America, graduates of higher normal schools, medical graduates, should form groups and exchange opinions, present research, and promote friendship.
K. I don't know if they have that spirit. They are only concerned with their own well-being.
A. That's a shame. Men don't do it either. But at least foreign students, who can be considered the intellectual class in the Korean women's world, should create an organic organization to study and present issues such as lifestyle improvement, marriage, love, and childcare. If that becomes the central idea and guiding force of the new women, then there might be some light shining on the Korean women's world, which has a long way to go. But what do you think?
K. That's a good idea. But I doubt that something on that scale will happen. It is hard for women, who are good at finding faults and are jealous, to do that! But it's only a matter of time. It will happen someday.
A. It's been too long. Thank you for your hard work.
K. You're welcome. I also learned a lot from your words... (End)

A 그러면 日本서 도라오신 분들에게 대하야 좀 말슴해 주시요.
K 무엇 따로 말슴할 것이 잇슴니가? 그저 빤히 세상이 다 아는바와 갓지요. 그러나 대강 말슴하면 이러케 볼 수 잇슬가 함니다. 첫재 女子고등사범출신이 칠팔명 되는데 년대로나 聲名으로나 京城女高普의 孫貞圭씨, 淑明의 成義敬씨가 세상에 알게 되고 淑明의 또 한분 趙慶姬씨, 進明의 全智子씨, 同德의 朴聖煥, 金順英 양씨고 멀니 大邱女高普로 옴겨간 任淑宰씨와 晋州日新女高普로 옴겨간 宋今璇씨가 그고 그 외로 색다른 학교를 졸업하고 도라와서 교편을 잡고 잇는 이로는 東京서 어느 女子商業學校를 맛치고 와서 지금 京城女子商業學校에 교편을 잡고 잇는 黃貴卿씨와 체<20>조학교출신으로 京城女高普의 체조과를 담임한 林順分씨, 榮養硏究所로 부터 도라온 貞信의 方信榮씨, 東京美術學校刺繡科 출신으로 京城女子美術校의 張善姬씨, 그리고 또 한분, 교편은 안잡엇지만 槿花學校의 서무를 보는 朴貞植씨가 櫻井女塾을 맛치고 도라왓지요. 고다음으로는 의학출신이 한 사오인 되는데 東京女子醫學專門을 맛친 許英肅, 玄德信, 李德耀, 劉英俊 四氏와 東京帝大연구실에서 일년 게시다가 온 吉貞姬씨가 한패 女子대학사회과출신으로 崔恩喜, 黃信德, 李賢卿 三氏가 또 한패, 그러고 神戶神學校를 맛친 金善씨가 잇지요.

K 宋씨는 여행권까지 어더노코 가랴다가 무슨 병이 잇서서 못가고 마럿다던지요. 黃씨가 가정으로 부터 교단에 나서게 된 것도 아마 朝鮮女子도 직업적 각성이 생긴 경향으로 볼 수가 잇겟지요
