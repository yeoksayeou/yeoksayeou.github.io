시조 (Sijo, Poem)

Magazine Title: 別乾坤
Publication Date: 1927년 02월 01일 (February 1, 1927)
Article Title: 시조 (Sijo, Poem)
Author: Various
Article Type: 시 (Poetry)

On an old plum tree stump, springtime returns,
On the old branches, it seems flowers will bloom,
But with the swirling spring snow, it's uncertain if they will bloom or not. (Unknown)

The snow and moon fill the courtyard, wind, do not blow.
I clearly know it's not the sound of slippers,
But with a longing and regretful heart, I wonder if it might be him. (Yi Ting-jin)

Is the flower hidden in the eastern pavilion an azalea (躑躅) or a rhododendron (杜鵑花)?
The world is covered in snow, how dare it bloom?
It must be a flute, for who else but the plum blossoms have both white snow and spring? (An Min-yeong)

Snow falls on the pine forest, each branch is a flower.
If I break off a branch and send it to where my love is,
How would it be if it melted after my love sees it? (Unknown)

Across the way, Mt. Naluo, black and rugged in the snow,
Old gnarled tree stump, with what power
Do branches grow and flowers bloom like that?
Even if the rotten belly (썩은배) is only half remaining, how can it ignore the meaning of spring? (An Min-yeong)

Who would say that the bamboo bent by the snow is crooked?
If its joints were crooked, would it be green in the snow?
Perhaps the lofty integrity (歲寒高節) of winter is only for bamboo. (Won Cheon-seok)

시조
매화 녯 등걸에 春節이 도라오니 녯피든 가지에 피염즉도 하다마는
춘설이 난분분하니 필동말동 하여라. (無名氏)

雪月이 滿庭한데 바람아 부지마라. 曳履聲 안인줄을 판연이 알것마는
그립고 아쉬운 마음에 행여 긘가 하노라. ( 李廷藎 )

東閣에 숨은꼿이 躑躅인가 杜鵑花인가 乾坤이 눈이어늘 제엇지 감히
피리 알괘라 백설양춘이 매화 밧게 뉘 잇스리. (安玟英

松林에 눈이오니 가지마다 꼿이로다. 한가지 꺽거내여 님게신대 드리
괘자 님게서 보오신후 녹아진들 엇더리. (無名氏)

저건너 羅浮山 눈속에 검어웃둑 울퉁불퉁 대등걸아 네무삼 힘으로
가지도쳐 꼿조차 저리 되엿는다. 아모리 썩은배 반만 남앗슬 망정
봄뜻을 어이 하리요. (安玟英

눈마저 휘여진대를 뉘라서 굽다턴고. 굽은節이면 눈속에 푸를소냐.
아마도 歲寒高節은 대 뿐인가 하노라. (元天錫) <1>
