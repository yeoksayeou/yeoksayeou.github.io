The Secret of Ordering

The April issue of *New Woman* is 20 jeon. The Children's Commemorative Issue is 10 jeon. It is more advantageous to order them together, as it saves on shipping costs. Be sure to order them together.

Also, *Byulgun'gon* costs 5 jeon, but if you only order that, the shipping cost will be higher than the price of the book. Therefore, please order it together with other books, or send 50 jeon in advance for a one-year subscription. <25>
