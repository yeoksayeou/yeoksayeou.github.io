Rumors

Miss Kim Wŏn-sun, a teacher at the Women's Art School (女子美術學校), has been suffering from a severe illness and has been hospitalized for treatment in a certain hospital. Her progress has been very good, and she has gone down to her family home in Oro-ri (五老里), Kyŏnghŭng Line (京咸線). A letter received by one of her acquaintances the other day said that she had completely recovered from her illness and returned to her original state. We offer our blessings for Miss Kim's future. ＜79＞

Named Entities

金元順 Kim Wŏn-sun

風聞帖
女子美術學校 교원 金元順孃은 그간 사나운 병마에 걸려서 모병원에 입원 치료 중이더니 경과가 매우 양호하야 京咸線 五老里 자기 본가로 나려 갓는데 일전에 엇던 그의 친지에게 온 편지를 본즉 병이 아주 완쾌되야 원상회복을 하얏다고 한다. 孃의 前途를 위하야 祝福不已＜79＞
