A Series of Questions and Answers (1)
						Yi Dun-hwa

Question: What is the most difficult thing about being on the stage (演壇)?

Yi: More than anything, it is how to make the audience listen well. If one doesn't care whether people listen or not when speaking, that's that. But to make them listen well is the most difficult thing. <23>
