The Traffic Survey (November Issue Prize Winner) Announcement

Because this prize question was so interesting, it received extraordinary popularity from the general public, reaching an unprecedented success with over 19,000 applicants. However, because it was an ambiguous question that was originally difficult to answer, no one among them was able to get the correct answer. Therefore, regrettably, the first prize was not awarded, but 20 people with answers close to the correct one were selected and are announced below.

交通調査(11월호 현상 당선) 發表
이 懸賞問題가 흥미잇는이만치 일반의 인기가 비상하야 응모자 1만 9천여 인에 달하는 空前의 대성황을 보게 되엿스나 원래 答案을 엇기가 어려운 막연한 문제이기 때문에 그 중에 한사람도 정답을 맛치지 못하게 되엿다. 그리하야 유감이나마 1등상은 뽑지를 못하고 답에 근사한 이로 20인을 뽑아서 下記와 가티 발표한다.
