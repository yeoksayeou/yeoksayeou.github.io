劉道順 (Yu Tosun)
시 (Poem)

Azalea Flower
Yu Tosun

When the azalea spring comes,
You said you would come,
Even when the azalea spring leaves,
You do not come.
Every time the azalea blooms,
Grief thoughts arise,
Every time the azalea falls,
Sorrow accumulates.

Even in Nyŏngbyŏn, on Yaksan's lighthouse,
On rugged rocks,
A single azalea flower
Sways gently.
Knowing full well you wouldn't come
Once spring had left,
Deceived and deceived, even after ten years,
I cannot forget. <5>

劉道順	Yu Tosun
