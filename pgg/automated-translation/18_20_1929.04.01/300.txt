Which Pocket?

When the train was passing through a tunnel without the lights on, a man suddenly thought, "Theft is scary at times like this." He put his hand in his suit pocket. Oh my! Another guy's hand was already in it.
"Hey, you thief! Take your hand out!" he said.
"Hey, you take your hand out! Who are you calling a thief?" the other guy shouted. In the midst of wondering what was going on,
"Hey, you guys! Both of you take your hands out of my pocket! Even if it's dark, how can two guys stick their hands in someone else's pocket at the same time?" a gentleman next to them shouted.

Reason for Returning to Husband

A wife had a fight with her husband and ran away in anger, living in an inn. But in less than four days, she crawled back to her husband.
When her friends asked why she came back so quickly, she said, "Everything else was fine, but one thing written next to the inn door kept catching my eye every time I went in and out, so I came back." When they asked what was written that made her do that,
she said, "Haven't you forgotten anything?"

Strange Law of Nature

A Chinese man and a Korean man went hunting with shotguns. The Korean man was very clumsy with the gun, so the sly Chinese gentleman came up with a plan to try to trick him.
"Hey, if you shoot a bird from a distance of more than 50 gil (丈), I'll give you 5 won. Of course, if you don't hit it, you have to give me 5 won."
"Okay, let's do it."
As he answered, a large bird was flying high in the sky above their heads. The Korean man aimed his gun and fired. Bang! As the smoke billowed, the bird fell dead.<82>
"Well, how is it? Now I've earned 5 won!" he shouted. The Chinese man, whose calculation was wrong, was dumbfounded and speechless.
"Wait a moment. Thinking about it carefully, even if it's just a bird, if it falls from that high, it's bound to die even if it's not hit by a bullet, isn't that right?"
Indeed, he was a Chinese gentleman.

Free Will

Two strangers were fishing in the same place. Gap (甲) caught small fish one after another, while Eul (乙) didn't catch a single one until sunset. One of the men was so frustrated with the situation that Eul (乙) started a conversation.
Eul (乙): "Hey, I bought the most expensive bait (멕이) with money, but nothing is biting. What kind of bait do you have on your hook?"
As if he was annoyed by the question, Gap (甲) didn't answer. When Eul (乙) kept pressing him, he reluctantly replied,
Gap (甲): "I just tied a 50-jeon silver coin to the end of the hook."
Eul (乙): "Does a fish (魚) bite a 50-jeon silver coin?"
Gap (甲): "It doesn't eat the silver coin, but if you hang it with money, they take it and go buy whatever bait they want."
Eul (乙): "......"<83>
