西道民謠 五月女탄식 - 江西의 메나리曲에 마추어 (Song of a May Girl in the Seodo Region - To the tune of a Menari Song from Gangseo)
韓晶東
시 (Poem)

Song of a May Girl in the Seodo Region - To the tune of a Menari Song from Gangseo
Han Jeong-dong

Maksaretaewanan
This sorrowful body
In the morning, fetching water
In the evening, pounding grain.
Aigong aigong
What a bother-.
Battered by harsh wind and rain
Nearing forty
Though my looks are poor
My heart is red.
Aigong aigong
What a bother-.

Whether I am hated or loved
Depends on what you see
The white powder I put on
Is like Mireuk.
Aigong aigong
What a bother-.

If I wear my mother's baby clothes
I look pretty
On Dano, Nandu
Goes to ride the swing.
Aigong aigong
What a bother-. <1>

Named Entities

江西	Gangseo
端午	Dano
