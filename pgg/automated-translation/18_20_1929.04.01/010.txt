The Color of the Water Mill - Set to the Melody of the Gangseo Menari
Han Jeong-dong

Is this body a water mill
That grinds rice?
Each grain of rice being ground
Is my autumn heart (秋心).
Aigong aigong
What a fuss-.

The rice grains collide
Collide, only being polished
My heart collides
Only filled with sorrow (僽心).
Aigong aigong
What a fuss-.

When the sun sets, the water mill
Takes a rest
But at night, my heart
Fashions itself even better.
Aigong aigong
What a fuss-.

The water mill turns
Turns and dies
Will this body, longing
Longing, die?
Aigong aigong
What a fuss-. <1>
