We welcome submissions

Local Color: If you send us unique customs or general news from your region, we will revise the wording and publish it.

Postcard Communication: If you write down local news, light satire, or your own thoughts on a postcard and send it to us, we will publish it as is (must be on a postcard).

Rewritten Folk Songs: We welcome original folk songs. Furthermore, we welcome not only original folk songs but also existing folk songs with revised lyrics, and we will publish them.

No Deadline: Send them anytime, and they will be published in the issue after next. <36>
