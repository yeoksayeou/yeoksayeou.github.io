Rumor Mill

Speaking of occupations, I think being Mrs. I Yagi is better. U Pong-un (禹鳳雲), of Kŭn'u-hoe (槿友會), has become a new type of working woman as a sales representative for Singer Company (씽거會社) (a company specializing in the sale of sewing machines). The world is all about finding something to eat (食觀局), and despite the rainy season and scorching heat, she visits house after house every day to get orders. Being an aging woman, it must be very tiring, and because everyone living there is poor, it is also difficult to get orders.

It is said that Chŏng Ch'il-sŏng (丁七星) has always been weak, and recently developed an illness, making it difficult for him to handle the affairs of Kŭn'u-hoe (槿友會) due to his health, so he will be going to his hometown in Taegu (大邱) for a while to recuperate.
Should we pray to the Big Dipper for Chŏng Ch'il-sŏng's (丁七星) health? <174>

風聞帖
직업 이약이가 낫스니 말이지 槿友會禹鳳雲氏는 요새이 씽거會社(裁縫機械販賣專門業)의 外交員인 新職業 夫人이 되엿다. 世事는 食觀局(먹고볼판)으로 빵 문제 때문에 장마와 炎天을 불구하고 그 注文者를 엇기 위하야 매일 이 집 저 집 방문을 하는데 半나미 늙으신 몸이라 매우 고달푸기도 하려니와 와 사는 사람이라고는 모든 無産者이기 때문에 注文者가 업서서 또한 곤난하다고요.

丁七星씨는 원래 약한 몸에 근래에는 또 병까지 생겨서 그 건강으로는 槿友會 일을 보기 어렵기 때문에 잠시간 大邱 鄕第에 가서 요양을 한다고 한다.
북두칠성님께 丁七星씨의 건강축원이나 할거나.＜174＞
