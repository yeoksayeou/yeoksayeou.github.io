Article Title: 新婦 候補者 展覽會,諧謔·諷刺·奇拔,(第三回),入場無料... (Exhibition of Bride Candidates, Humor, Satire, Extraordinary (Third Round), Free Admission...)

**Body Text Translation:**

Exhibition of Bride Candidates, Humor, Satire, Extraordinary,
Ss Sponsored

Miss Lee
20 years old. Although her chin protrudes a bit, she is a slender beauty. People who admire this beauty have nicknamed her "Ice Cream Beauty." Her face is sharply pretty, her figure is slender, and her personality is as sharp as a knife, so a cold wind blows even in the summer.
She graduated from O Girls' High School with honors, and her family is educated, so her knowledge is not lacking. My respected senior, Mr. Mo, opposes her because her lower jaw protrudes, so there is no place for virtue to accumulate, but I admire her clean and incredibly beautiful appearance and her knife-like clear personality, which rather makes her single-minded, so I have been thinking very earnestly about marrying her.
However, this woman has an unexpected flaw: she always worries about her clothes and hairstyle, asking, "How is it? Isn't it funny?" After asking that, she says, "Why are you staring at my face so intently? Don't I embarrass myself?" As if she thinks I have the ability to tell whether her style is funny or not without looking... In the end, when I think about it, the words she says every time we meet, "How is it? Isn't it funny?" really mean, "How is it? Isn't it really pretty?"
Once, when I went for a walk with her, she stopped in front of a bookstore display case and looked inside, so I looked inside with her for a moment. There was nothing eye-catching among the books on display, so she soon left the glass case and started walking. <86>
"Wasn't the picture on the Library Reading Week poster in that display case a good one?" When I brought up the topic, she replied, "Where? Was there such a thing?" as if she had never even passed by the glass case, let alone looked inside. This woman wasn't looking at the inside of the glass case; she was looking at her own face reflected in the glass.
Although it disappointed me, she is a rare enthusiastic woman among Korean women.

Miss Seo
21 years old. She is the beloved daughter of a wealthy family in Cheongjin-dong, Seoul. She graduated from O Girls' High School with good grades and is now attending XX Home Economics School, a gentle young lady. She is not as beautiful as Miss Lee, but a young man who is a freshman at a certain private vocational school was rejected by this woman and ran away to Shanghai in anger, and two medical students have failed a grade each because they didn't have enough time to follow her around. Even now, when she goes out, she can't go to concerts or theaters alone at night because of the men who follow her, so she sometimes orders me to accompany her, which shows that she certainly deserves to be on the list of beauties.
Perhaps because she was raised by a single mother, she is somewhat spoiled and coquettish, and on the other hand, she is somewhat stubborn, but her heart is as gentle as her flawless face. According to phrenologists, there is no vicious person with a round face. This is true, and even if it weren't, Miss Seo is definitely a kind-hearted woman. She wears new trendy colored skirts boldly, not too short and not too long, demurely down to her mid-calf.
However, this woman has learned nothing but "Of course," "I don't know," and "I hate it." She never expresses any particular opinion. So, this woman's beauty is ultimately nothing more than a pretty doll (人形).
If you ask, "What do you like? Reading (讀書)? Housework (家事)?" she will answer, "I like housework the most. I also enjoy reading."
However, if you look at the room of this young lady who says she likes housework and is currently attending the home economics department, the old woman in our inn would faint and collapse. In the corner of the room, hair is tangled like a scouring pad and thrown away, and mamekong (mamekong) shells and apple peels are overflowing in torn bags and left on top of discarded socks... Only the desk is barely tidied up, but even the flowers in the vase are arranged in a terrible way, with one hibiscus, two lilies, three laurel flowers, and even a kisaeng flower (기생꼿) all mixed together, as if this <87> person admires the complexity of a trash can in her head. This kind of taste is even more pathetic than putting fake flowers (假花)(人造花) that have no vitality, no fragrance, and no life in them. And the walls are covered with photos of movie actors and actresses, which is a sacred taste.
"What kind of books do you read? These days, it seems like 'Candy is Shouting' is being read a lot. Have you read it?" No, no, what she is reading now is Rochun's "Eternal Daydream." "It's very interesting. His writing is always so interesting that I buy it as soon as it comes out," she says, shrugging her shoulders. I was impressed by her head, which is so childish yet doesn't know any shame, and I was immediately chased away by her bold courage.

Miss Baek
20 years old. She is a woman as slender as a willow branch. If the world hadn't changed, this woman would have been treated specially as the most outstanding beauty, especially in her slender figure. Unfortunately, because the world has changed, plumpness is considered modern beauty rather than slenderness, and such women are considered weak (貧弱). Yes, that's right, but this woman is so filial that she always accompanies her old mother when she goes out, and it's very unwise to say "This is my mother." An unplump old woman is truly indescribably weak, so a clever woman doesn't walk around with her stooped mother, saying, "I'll look this ugly in a few years."

Miss Gil
29 years old. She is a woman who became famous suddenly after finishing her studies in the West and cutting her hair. She may not be a beauty in terms of appearance or figure, but she boasts a desirable and abundant figure. She is famous for her good speeches, famous for her good socializing, and even more famous for crying in the West, so she is a woman who works and saves like a ghost. Her face is characterized by a slightly low nose, but it is difficult to find a comparison for this woman's education and knowledge among other women. If you marry her, you will never be embarrassed or frustrated by your wife. Women who have been to the West or old maids (老處女) who want to go to the West often say, "There are no suitable men to marry in Korea," and say things that mean they will never get married. I don't know if the views of women who say such things are wider or narrower than the eye of a needle, but at least this woman, as educated as she is, doesn't say such dull and narrow things. However, she did say to her students, "There is no one in Korea that I want to sit down and discuss with, so I will not associate with anyone at all." This is her holy educated bonus. <88> Saying that there is no husband material for her in Korea and saying that there is no one to discuss with in Korea is the same thing, but it is worth the holy education that she has been to study abroad to say it so politely. If this woman had studied abroad a little longer and studied a little more, she would never have returned to Korea and would have thrown away all the Korean blood flowing in her body and said, "I am the daughter of a Westerner," and would have come to Korea for inspection and then left. If that had happened, the woman's education would have been even more sacred... I cannot help but regret it. I cannot help but regret that I heard that a student in a certain girls' school dormitory called her father, who had come to visit her from her hometown, "our house's servant," and I was very angry. Even this highly educated woman, if she met a certain Westerner, would surely call her husband her family's servant.

Miss Myeong
26 years old. She is a beautiful woman who writes poetry and has a bobbed haircut and wears Western clothes. This woman is probably the most loyal to literature and art among Korean women. She is charming, quick-witted, and very talented, but she laughs for no reason and cries for no reason. She laughs so often and cries so often that it's hard to tell when and how her laughter turns into tears. It would be rude to say that her tears are like large chicken droppings when talking about a beauty, but her pearl-like tears can't even roll down her powdered face, and she just keeps laughing with those tears on her face, which looks very cute in a good way.
Once, when she was working at a certain company, a messenger brought her a cup of coffee, and she got very angry and said, "How can you be so ignorant? You know that women have their periods once a month, so how could you give me coffee?"
This woman is clearly overly hysterical, so if you marry this woman, she will blab about everything, even the things her husband faintly anticipates.

Miss Kang
23 years old. She is an incredibly active woman among Korean women who grew up in a foreign land and returned to Korea and are working hard for the women's movement. She is a scary woman who casually does unbelievable things like going into a bar with five or six young men following her around like a hunter leading dogs.
I have often thought that living a married life with this kind of woman would be quite refreshing and pleasant.
However, I heard that this woman had been close to a man who had grown up with her in a foreign country since she was young, but recently that man suddenly turned away and got engaged to another woman. So, when I met her the other day, I brought up the story and comforted her feelings of heartbreak.
"What are you talking about? Do you think I'm going to despair over such a thing and <89> commit suicide or something? At-tsetse, no way. If I had that kind of thought, I could grab another new man right now, it's as easy as eating porridge. Don't you know that 'discard the old and take the new!' is the motto of modern people?" She says she'll just grab one.
Even while saying these things, this woman sticks out the tip of her tongue every time she speaks. It's fortunate that the tip of her tongue isn't pale but bright red, but still, if you tell her to "please get rid of the habit of sticking out the tip of your tongue," she laughs ha-ha every time.
I still haven't figured out what that ha-ha means.
"Ssang, Mr. S, if you want to marry me, tell me quickly before you miss the chance and regret it."
I was shocked and jumped out like someone who had been electrocuted.

Miss Ma
20 years old. Even though her surname is Ma (馬), she is not like a horse but a gentle and docile young woman like a sheep (羊). She graduated from O Girls' High School and is going to learn English from a private tutor. I first met her when I went to the house of that private tutor. She is a very gentle and calm young woman, as if she had been raised only sitting down, like a maiden in the inner quarters. Her face is also a simple and unadorned face. Her family is also quite principled, her eldest brother is a doctor of medicine and runs a hospital, and her second brother is studying abroad in Germany. However, the left hand of that gentle and simple young woman is always supporting her left neck with a handkerchief. Men, and especially women, hide their physical defects from others, so it is difficult to discover the woman's outward defects even after meeting her briefly. However, I secretly know one way to do it: "You can know everything just by looking at where the woman's hand goes often." This is a sure-fire method. For example, if the woman has buck teeth or a missing front tooth, her hand will always be near her mouth, and if there is a scar on her ear or cheek, her right hand or left hand will definitely not leave the vicinity of her ear or cheek. As someone who knows this method, when I saw that this woman's left hand was always supporting her left neck at our first meeting, I couldn't help but suspect that there was something wrong with her neck. I paid close attention, but I couldn't get a chance to see that part in detail, but when she was putting on her shoes, I glanced at it and saw that there was indeed a scar from a neck disease as big as a thumb. It was a pity. It was a pity, like a flaw in a truly good jewel.

Miss Oh
22 years old. This woman's family is not well-off, but she is very fashionable. Her skill in doing makeup, her skill in doing her hair, and her skill in dressing well are not awkward at all, so she always dresses sharply. Her face has no special features, but she looks pliant and gentle. If I were to force myself to pick out a special feature, it would be that her nose is a little bit high, but this is a good feature and not bad at all. This woman will, of course, manage her household sharply. It is also assumed that her personality will not be too stuffy, as she was a tennis player.
However, this woman is famous for snacking often. It is not too strange that her friends can always find chocolate in this woman's handbag, but it is a famous story that she dropped her handbag while taking out her train ticket on the train, and roasted chestnuts poured out of it instead of chocolate, embarrassing her. As I said before, if this woman runs out of change, it would be a big problem if a certain middle school physical education teacher sold her husband's shoes like a high-collar wife to buy taffy. <91>

Miss Bae
21 years old. She is a quite argumentative woman who graduated from a physical education school in Japan. Everyone will agree that she is a good mother to bear good children. However, at a friend's wedding reception, when they decided to put on a farcical show of having the bride and groom perform a traditional wedding ceremony, this woman was designated as the bride's attendant or bridesmaid. This farce is likely to fizzle out midway because the bride's side doesn't readily agree to it, so hundreds of attendees were hoping that this farce would be completed smoothly. The bride and groom readily agreed and it was progressing well, with the bride and groom even facing each other, but this woman started insisting that "I will never make the bride bow unless the groom bows first."
Even though the bride's side said that the original traditional custom was for the bride to bow first, this woman stubbornly insisted and ruined the long-planned farce at the very last moment, ruining the mood of hundreds of people.
It would be good if this woman's will were so strong. However, this kind of stubbornness at such times is nothing more than something that looks as dense as if you could plow her forehead with a shovel and not see a drop of blood. It would be a big problem if this woman were to be stubbornly persistent about unreasonable things in her married life.

Miss Gu
19 years old. She is a very reticent woman who does not easily express her opinion. I don't know if she is shy or if she doesn't do it on purpose, but she is a taciturn woman who does not easily show whether she agrees or disagrees, whether it is yes or no.
She is a pitiful woman who grew up in front of her grandparents after losing her parents and graduated from school. However, because her grandparents raised her with special affection and care, she knows the etiquette of a woman, albeit in an old-fashioned way, her studies were excellent, and she is also skilled in sewing, so she is praised in the neighborhood. <91> However, compared to modern girls who have graduated from modern girls' schools these days, she is very shy and overly gentle. So, I have been struggling to know this woman's opinion.
Not only this woman, but most women generally do not readily reveal their inner thoughts to others. Even though they may speak as if they are pouring out everything that is in their hearts, like the truth, they often keep their true thoughts separate. So, lies naturally increase. I don't know if it's a bad thing to say, but they spew out lies like an octopus squirting ink when in danger. This may be an instinct (本能) that Heaven has given to women out of pity. Whether that is the case or not, it is easy to be deceived by people who lie so well and easy to be deceived by people who say nothing. In these cases, there is a way to guess the woman's opinion. Unless she is an unusually perverted woman, you can usually find out with this method. What is it? "You can tell by looking at the woman's feet."
While carefully telling the story that you want to tell, if you look at the feet of the woman who is hesitating to answer, and if she puts her feet forward and stretches her legs slightly without bending them, and spreads her feet in the shape of the number 8, that means "agreement," "sympathy," and "yes," so the man can rest assured and be very satisfied. If, on the other hand, she brings her toes together and bends her legs, that means "disagreement," "opposition," and "no," so you should give up.
Generally, I learned her opinion using this method. And her family, her grandparents, also welcome me whenever I go. "Mr. Ssang S says that he wishes she would study English a little when he has time, but he is so busy..." they say.
However, one flaw is that if I marry this woman, I must be prepared to be very stuffy, and another flaw is that I must bring this family's grandparents into our family as well, which is no small problem.

李孃
20세. 턱이 조곰 빠르기는 하지만은 날씬한 미인이라 『아이쓰크림 미인』이라고 이 미인을 숭배하는 사람들이 일홈을 지엇다. 얼골이 날카롭게 어엽부고 자태가 날씬한데 성질까지 터분한 구석이 업시 칼날가터서 녀름에도 찬 바람이 홱홱 도는 까닭이다.
O화를 우등으로 졸업하엿스며 집안이 유식한 집안이니 견문(見聞)이 그리 빠지지 안는다. 나의 존경하는 선배 모(某)새는 그가 하관이 빨러서 덕이 고인 곳이 업다고 반대하지만은 나는 그 깻긋하고 정신나게 어엽분 용모와 칼날가티 분명한 성질이 도리혀 단길성이 잇서서 이와 결혼하기를 매우 렬심으로 생각하엿섯다.
그러나 이 녀자가 의외의 단점을 한가지 가진 것은 『애그 엇대요. 우습지 안어요?』하고 옷맵시 머리 맵시만에 머리를 썩히는 버릇이다. 그러케 물어놋코는 『왜 그러케 남의 얼골을 뚜러지게 보세요. 제가 면구하지 안어요?』한다. 눈으로 보지안코도 맵시가 우스운지 안이 우스운지 보아 줄 재조가 잇는 줄 아는지...결국 생각해보면 『읏대요. 우습지 안어요?』하고 맛날 적마다 하시는 그 말슴은 『읏대요.참말 어엽부지요?』하는 소리인 것이다.
언제인가 한번 나하고 산ㅅ보를 가티 나섯슬 때에 이 녀자가 어느 서점(書店) 진렬장 압헤서 발을 멈추고 서서 드려다 보기에 나도 잠간 가티 드려다 보앗섯다. 거긔 진렬해 잇는 책이 눈에 뜨이는 것이 엄섯던지 금방 그 유리창 압흘 떠나서 것기 시작＜86＞하엿다.
『앗가 그 진렬장 속에 걸녀잇는 도서관 독서주간(讀書週間)포스타-의 그림이 그럴 듯하게 잘 되엿지요?』하고 내가 이약이를 꺼내보닛가 『어데요. 어데 그런 것이 잇섯슴닛가?』하고 대답하는 것이 전혀 그 유리창을 드려다 보기는 커냥 그 압흐로 지나가지도 안흔 사람가텃다. 이 녀자가 그 유리창 속을 드려다 본 것이 안이라 유리창에 빗치는 자긔 얼골을 드려다 보고 잇섯든 것이엿다.
나를 낙심식이기는 하엿지만 그러나 조선 녀자치고는 드물게 보는 렬심가이다.

徐孃
21세. 서울 청진동 어느 과택의 맛따님으로 O명을 조흔 성적으로 졸업하고 지금은 XX가사과(家事科)에 다니는 얌전한 령양이다. 李孃만큼 미인은 안이나 어느 사립 전문학교 1년생인 청년 한명은 이 녀자에게 퇴박을 맛고 화ㅅ김에 상해로 다름박질을 해가고 어느 의학전문학생 2명은 이 녀자의 뒤따르기에 시간이 부족하여서 한 해씩 락뎨를 하엿고 지금도 길에 나스면 뒤따르는 남자들 때문에 밤에는 음악회에나 연극장에를 혼차 나슬수가 업다고 갓금 나에게 배종(陪從)을 명령하는 것을 보면 학실히 미인명부에 오를 자격이 잇는 이다.
홀어머니 압헤서 길니운 탓인지 모르되 다소 응석과 어리광이 잇고 또 한편으로는 다소 고집이 잇스나 신덕만은 그 험잡을 곳 업는 얼골처럼 무던한 녀자다. 골상(骨相)학자의 말에 의하면 얼골이 둥근 사람치고 마음이 악독한 이가 업다 한다. 이 말은 올흔 말이어니와 설혹 그 말이 올치 안타고 하드래도 이 徐孃은 분명히 심덕이 조흔 녀자다. 옷 가튼 것 제법 대담스럽게 새로 류행하는 빗갈치마를 짧지도 안케 길지도 안케 정강이 중*까지 되게 얌전히 닙고 단인다.
그러나 이 녀자는 『그럼요』 「나는 몰나요 나는 실여요」 소리밧게 다른 말을 배호지 못햇다. 아모 이러타할 의견도 말하는 적이 업다. 그래서 이 녀자의 아름다움은 결국 어엽분 인형(人形)밧게 되지 못한다.
『당신은 무얼 조와함닛가. 독서(讀書)임닛가. 가사(家事)임닛가.』하고 무르면 『가사가 제일 좃쿠요. 독서도 즐겨해요』한다.
그러나 가사를 조와한다 하고 현재 가사꽈에 단이는 이 처녀의 방을 보면 우리 려관집 로파는 긔절을 하야 쓰러질 것이다. 방구석에는 머리카락이 쑤세미 가티 엉켜서 내던진 채로 잇고 마메콩껍질, 사과껍질이 찌저진 봉지에 넘친채로 그냥 노여서 버서 던진 양말짝우에 노여잇고...간신히 책상 위만 가즈런히 정돈되여 잇는대 그나마 화병에 꼿을 꼬진 꼴이란 무궁화 한 가지, 백합 두 송이 월계꼿 세 송이 거긔다 기생꼿까지 한데 모아서 꼬자 노앗스니 이＜87＞ 사람의 머리가 쓰레기통 속가티 복잡한 것을 찬미하는 모양이나 이 따위란 생긔도 향긔도 생명도 업는 가화(假花)(人造花)를 꼬자놋는 것보다도 더 한층 천덕스러운 취미다. 그러고 벽이란 벽에 활동사진 남여배우의 사진을 느러 부친 것은 거륵한 취미다.

이 녀자가 의지가 이러틋 굿세이다고 하면 조흔 일이다. 그러나 이런 때에 이따위 고집이란 이마를 부삽으로 헷처노아도 피 한방울 보이지 안케 빽빽한 것 밧게 더 보이는 것이 업다. 이 녀자가 부부생활에 잇서서 당치도 안흔 일에 외고집을 쓴다면 큰일날 일이다.

具孃
19세. 몹시 입이 떠서 용이히 의사표시를 안이하는 녀자다. 수집어서 못하는지 일부러 안이하는지 그것은 모르겟스되 엇잿던지 찬성하는지 불찬성하는지 예-쓰인지 노-인지 용이히 의사를 보이지 안는 묵중한 녀자다.
량친은 업고 조부모님 압헤서 자라서 학교까지 졸업한 가엽슨 녀자다. 그러나 조부모님이 남달니 귀엽고 가엽슨 생각으로 정성드려 양육한 만큼 다소 구식이나마 녀자의 레의를 알고 공부가 우등성적이엿고 또 침선이 능난하야＜91＞ 둥리에서도 충찬밧는 녀자다. 그런데 다만 요새의 녀학교 출신인 모던-처녀들에 비하야 수접음이 만코 너머 얌전하다. 그래서 나도 이녀자의 의사를 알기에 저윽이 고심하엿섯든 것이다.
이 녀자뿐 안이라 누구던지 녀자는 대개 자긔의 속을 남에게 얼는 알니지 안는다. 진정의 참말처럼 자긔 속에 잇는 고대로를 다쏘다 놋는 것처럼 말하는 것 가태도 그래도 정말 속에 잇는 말은 따로 간직해 두는 때가 만타. 그래서 저절로 그짓말이 만허진다. 욕 먹을 말인지 모르나 낙지가 의험한 때 먹물을 풍긔듯 그짓말을 뱃는다. 이것은 한울이 녀자들이 가엽시 녁여서 한가지 더 준 본능(本能)일넌지도 모른다. 그랫던 저랫던 이러케 그짓말을 잘하는 삼람에게도 속기가 쉬운 것이요 아모 말도 안하는 사람에게도 속기가 쉬운 것이다. 이런 때는 녀자의 의사를 짐작할 수 잇는 비방이 잇다. 여간 변태뎍으로 된 녀자가 안이면 대개 이 방법으로 알 수가 잇는 것이다. 무언고 하니 『그 녀자의 발을 보면 안다.』하는 것이다.
이 편에서 할 이약이를 일일이 정성스럽게 이약이 하면서 그 대답을 주저하고 잇는 녀자의 발을 보아서 그 녀자가 두 발을 압흐로 놋코 다소 두다리를 오구리지 안코 펴는 눈치요 발을 여덜 8자형상으로 벌니면 그것은 『찬성』이요 『동감』이요 『예-쓰』니 남자는 안심하여도 조토 만족해하여도 조타. 만일 그와 반대로 발 끗을 모으로 다리를 오그리는 눈치면 그것은 『불찬성』이요 『반대』요 『노-』이니 단념해야 된다.
대톄로 나는 이 방법에 의하야 그의 의사를 알엇다. 그러고 그 집 그의 조부모님도 내가 가기만 하면 환영한다. 『쌍에쓰 선생이 틈만히 잇스면 저 애가 영어공부를 좀 햇스면 조켓다고 그러는대 워낙 밧버하시닛가...』이런 말슴을 한다.
그러나 한가지 험은 내가 이 녀자와 결혼을 한다면 속이 무던히 갑갑할 것을 각오해야 하겟고 또 한가지 이 집 조부모님까지 우리집 식구로 모서야 하겟스니 이것이 적지 안히 문뎨가 된다.
