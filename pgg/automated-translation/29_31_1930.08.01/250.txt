Postcard Correspondence

White Craze

Korea invariably has a rainy season in the summer. It is why the saying that a man should own a raincoat and galoshes came to be. But these days, I often see so-called police officers (巡査 냥반) wearing white shoes and white serge pants, splashing through puddles under other people's umbrellas, and I feel sorry for them. It's not someone else's business. But what are white serge and white shoes for on a rainy day anyway? Considering our situation, things like white shoes, white serge, and mosu overcoats are out of place. Why don't you take a look at what people in other countries do? You should quickly sell those serge pants and use the money to buy a pair of rubber shoes that don't let water in and an umbrella, or you will be ruined! (An Patriot from Gyeongseong)

Do They Teach Mahjong at German Universities?

At a certain hospital in Susong-dong (壽松洞) , it seems that German students gather every day, and I assumed they were doing some kind of scientific research or talking about politics, but it turns out they are having mahjong parties. It seems that they even have a gisaeng (妓生) named Yi X-saek (李X色) attend, as it would be too boring with only men. I don't know if they teach mahjong at German universities, but if they do, those people will probably become mahjong doctors within this year. If so, the gisaeng (妓生) can't go to Germany but will learn mahjong from German students, so she will become a master. I am notifying you of only this for the first dispatch (第一信). (A student from Susong-dong (壽松洞) in the city)

Why Don't You Establish a Korean Language Department?

At Jinseong Professional School (晉成專門學校) in the city, Korean is now taught for three hours a week, and the teacher in charge is Principal Park Seung-bin (朴勝彬). Why not thoroughly establish a Korean Language Department at the school? (A hopeful student in the city)

World's Worst Busan Restaurant

When you arrive at Busan (釜山) port, everyone will be bothered by the persistent boys at the restaurants, like a fly that sticks and won't leave. "Our place has clean rooms and the food is delicious. Come and eat with us." If you can't resist their persistence and follow them as if being dragged, don't be surprised. Inside the room, a foul smell stings your nose, and if you order noodles, they bring you jang-gukbap (장국밥) as if they didn't hear you. They put a lump of cold rice in rotten doenjang-guk (된쟝국) and boldly charge you 25 jeon (錢). Perhaps the restaurant operators in Busan (釜山) have no intention of ever seeing a customer again. Even if they are people with no future, why do they act like they are trying to crack open a pumpkin seed and put it in their mouth all at once? For the sake of your business, please be more honest. (A Passerby through Busan (釜山))

Disgusting Advertising Boards

There are so many advertisements in the mountains, fields, rice paddies, and farms that you can't even see the natural scenery; they cover the sides of the train. Even if it is a commercial society, I can't stand it. It's as if they think we don't know what things are or how to buy them. If you look at the mountains, or if you want to look at the fields, there are only advertising boards everywhere, and it's so disgusting that I could die. Judging by how things are going, I should protest to the railroad bureau to get them to remove them, but that would be like fighting in the criminal court. I have to travel, but I hate the advertising boards, so it's a mess. Thinking about how these advertising boards are making our lives difficult makes me even angrier. Fellow travelers who hate advertising boards, let's devise a plan to get rid of the advertising boards. (Train Passenger) ＜93＞

白色狂
여름이면 으레 장마지는 조선이다. 사내가 우비와 거진말은 징겨야 한단 말도 그래서 생긴게지. 그런데 요새 보닛가 소위 순사 냥반이 백구두 백세루바지를 그냥 질벅 질벅 빠지면서 남의  우산 아래 부쳐가는 분을 雨中에 만히 보게 되니 딱한 생각이 난다. 그게 남의 일갓지가 안흐니 말이다. 그리고 도대체 비오는 날 백세루 백구두란 다 무엇인고? 우리 처지를 생각해보면 여름에 백구두 백세루 모수 두루막 가튼게 본래 당치가 안타. 남의 나라 사람 본을 좀 보아? 냉큼 그 세루 바지 버서 팔어가지고 물 안 울너오는 고무신 한커레 우산 한개부터 살 생각을 해야지 안하면 망한다 망해!(京城一愛國之士)

왜 朝鮮語專門科를 設置하지 안나요
시내 晉成專門學校에서는 매주 조선어를 세 시간식 교수 하게 되엿는데 담임선생임은 교장 朴勝彬씨라고 한다. 이왕이면 철저하게 그 학교에 아주 조선어전문과를 설치하는 것이 엇더 할는지요.(시내 一希望生)

世界無類釜山飮食店
釜山 埠頭에 나리면 누구나 녀롬 淸쉬-파리와 가티 한 번 붓터 떠러질 줄 몰으는 음식점 뽀-이들의 성화를 밧겟다.「저의 집은 방이 깻끗하고 음식 맛이 좃슴니다. 갓치가서 잡숙고 가시요」 하두 성화에 못 익이여 끌니는듯 쫏차가면 여러분 놀내지 마십시요. 방안에서는 구린내가 코를 지르고 음식은 국수를 청하면 딴청으로 장국밥을 가저다 주는대 그나마 썩어진 된쟝국에 찬밥 한 덩이 너헛서 주고 갑은 25錢也라를 당당히 청구하겟지요. 아마 釜山의 음식점 영업자들은 한 번 본 손님은 다시 안볼 작정이야. 아무리 장래가 끈어진 사람들이라구서 그럿케  호박씨 까서 한 입에 너으려는 영업자의 행동이 어대잇는가. 당신들의 영업을 위하야 좀 더 정직하게 하여 주엇스면.(一釜山通過客)

보기실흔 廣告板
산에도 들에도 논에도 밧테도 그거 무슨 광고가 그리 만흔지 자연 경치조차 볼 수가 업시 막 기차 沿邊을 덥퍼 노앗스니 아무리 상품사회라고 할지라도 그 꼴은 볼 수가 업다. 뉘가 物名을 몰르거나 살 줄을 몰나서 못사는 줄 아는 모양이야. 산을 바라볼 여면 산에도 들을 볼나면 전답에도 왼통 광고판뿐이니 딱 보기 실허 죽을 지경이요. 결나는 것 보아서는 철도국에다 좀 치워버리라고 대들면 조켓스나 그것은 刑房간에 지울니는 격이 될 터이니 여행은 하야겟고 그에 광고판은 보기 실코 야단이야. 이 광고판이 우리를 못 살게 하는 것을 생각하니 더욱 결이 벗적난다. 여행하는 동반 광고판 보기실흔 *侔들 광고판 업시하여 터리는 策을 강구합시다.(기차통행인)＜93＞
