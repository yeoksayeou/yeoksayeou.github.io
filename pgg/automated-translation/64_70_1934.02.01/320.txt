First Selection of Popular New Songs
The "New Popular Songs" (新流行小曲) contest, announced in the previous issue, received as many as 250 entries, of which the following two were selected. The lyrics and detailed stories will be included in the next issue. Please wait.

Two Selected Entries: An Unclaimed Ferry (임자 업는 나루배: Kyŏngsŏng Kim Chong-han), I Don't Know Either! (나도 몰라요!: Kaesŏng Ko Ma-bu) <51>
