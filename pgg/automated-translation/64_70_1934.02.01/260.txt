Postbox and Beggar
						Yun Sŏk-chung

Postbox
The postbox at the intersection
Big letters, small letters
The postbox that keeps eating them

A beggar sits next to the postbox
Begging until his hands wear out
"Please, miss, give me a penny
Please, sir, have some mercy"

Into the postbox's closed mouth
Food keeps going in
Into the beggar child's outstretched hand
Not a single coin comes all day
-From a lost ribbon- <41>
