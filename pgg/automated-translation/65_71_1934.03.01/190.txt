First Grand Prize Announcement for New Popular Songs
Ownerless Ferryboat
Kyŏngsŏng Ŭl Paso

A
An ownerless ferryboat, when the wind blows,
Waves push it along, wave after wave.
If there were sails in the fair wind,
The abandoned, sorrowful thing would drift along.

B
This ownerless body is also a sorrowful ferryboat,
Whether the wind blows or it drifts,
So yearningly missing the days gone by,
I weep at the bright moon reflected in the water.

C
An ownerless ferryboat, without a fixed destination,
Today it's here, tomorrow where?
Though the heart is swept away and the body drifts,
The dream returns to the old days. <36>

I Don't Know Either
Kaesŏng Sŭngma-bu

A
Willow branches fluttering in the breeze of spring,
Hearing the lark's song alone,
Sighing faintly with an unknown feeling,
I don't know why my heart cries for no reason.

B
Forsythia blooming brightly under the fence in spring,
Watching butterflies fluttering endlessly above the flowers,
Turning away shyly, face reddening,
I don't know why my heart smiles idly.

C
Beautiful green grass by the roadside in spring,
Meeting him every time I pass by,
Heart pounding, bowing my head,
I don't know why my heart trembles secretly. <37>

Post-Selection Thoughts
As I said in the recruitment advertisement, the popular songs of today are increasingly leaning towards the vulgar, and the impact of this has become a matter that requires great consideration from us, as the wise readers are well aware. Therefore, the emergence of popular songs with good content is something that not only some intellectuals but also the entire public eagerly desires. So, with this in mind, we have been soliciting the newest and most artistically refined songs from readers since the New Year's issue, and we have been more successful than expected, and we have selected the above two songs as the first winners. These two pieces were selected responsibly from a total of 300 entries, after the most rigorous selection process. Of course, there were many good songs that were too good to be rejected, but we had no choice but to exclude all those that did not meet the standards that this magazine had specifically set and requested. <36> It is regrettable that there was no special selection, but we believe that even better works will appear soon, and we will continue to pursue our original goal, so please submit many entries. The two winning entries have been entrusted to the two companies that we trust the most, Pak'ŭm and Sieron, and are already being composed, so the day when these songs will be spread across the country is not far off. In the future, we will continue to cooperate with these two companies, and the winning works will be distributed through the scores of these two companies, so we hope that you will put in more effort and send us the results of your hard work.
In this selection, not only the editorial department of this magazine but also Kohan Yi Kangg'u representing the two record companies participated. Anyway, as you can see by reading it, we are very pleased to have obtained even just two new songs - clean popular songs - that will overwhelm the conventional records. <37>

選後感
임의 모집광고에도 말슴한 바와가치 지금의 유행가는 점점 야비하게만 치우처 그 영향이 크게 우리네들의 고려를 요하기까지에 이른 것은 현명한 독자 여러분이 더 잘 알고 게실 문제이겟습니다. 그리하야 조흔 내용을 갓추운 류행소곡의 출현은 일부 지식게급 뿐 아니라 민중 전체가 이를 갈망해 마지 안는 바입니다. 그래서 우리는 뜻한 바 잇서 지난 신년호부터 만천하 독자께 가장 새롭고 또한 예술적으로 고아(高雅)한 노래를 모집하엿든 바 의외에 예거 이상으로 성황을 보게 되야 우선 제1회로 우에ㅅ 두분의 노래를 입선으로 뽑앗습니다. 응모총수가 300여 편에서 가장 엄선의 엄선을 하야 책임잇게 선정한 것이 이 두 편입니다. 물론 이 중에는 락선식히기에 앗가운 조흔 노래가 만엇습니다만은 본지가 특별히 생각하고 모집한＜36＞ 규정에 어그리진 것은 읏절수 업시 죄다 빼여버렷습니다. 특선이 업섯슴이 유감이나 머지안어 더 조흔 작품이 출현될 것을 밋고 우리는 게속하야 초지를 관철코저 하오니 만히 응모해 주시기 바랍니다. 이번 당선된 두 편은 우리가 가장 신임하는 박타와 시에론 두 회사에 부탁하야 임의 작곡중에 잇스니가 이것이 노래가 되야 전선적으로 퍼저나갈 날도 머지 안엇습니다. 압흐로도 게속하야 이 두 회사와 협약하고 당선작품은 이 두 회사의 보반(譜盤)을 통하야 퍼저나갈 것이오니 여러분은 좀더 분발하야 만흔 노력의 결정을 보내주시기 바람니다.
이번 고선에 잇서 본사 편즙국은 물론이요 두 레코-드회사를 대표해서는 孤帆 李堈求씨가 참례를 하엿습니다. 하여간 열분이 읽어보셔도 알겟습니다만 종래 레코-드를 압도할 새로운 노래- 깨끗한 류행소곡을 단 두 편이라도 엇게 된 것을 크게 깁버하는 바입니다.＜37＞
