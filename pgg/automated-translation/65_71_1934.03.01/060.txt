World's Number One Collection

The world's largest flower is the Rafflesia of Nanyang - its diameter is 3 *cheok* (尺).

The world's longest bridge is the Brooklyn Bridge in the United States, with a length of 6,500 *cheok* (尺).

The world's fastest car is in the United States, with a speed of 2.6 *ri* (哩) per minute.

The world's most accurate clock is that of the British Parliament - it is off by 1 second in 200 days.

The world's tallest tree is in Australia - its height cannot even be measured.

The world's largest woman is an American woman named "Mat Hang," whose height is 8 *cheok* (尺) 2 *chon* (寸).

The world's oldest tree is a Japanese cypress (檜) in Taiwan - over 2,000 years old.

The world's largest statue is the Statue of Liberty in the United States - 151 *cheok* (尺).

The world's fastest streetcar is in Berlin, Germany - a speed of 2.6 *ri* (哩) per minute.

The world's largest book is in London, England - a 9 *ch'i* (呎) Dutch map. <13>

세계제일의 큰 여자는 「맛항」이라는 米國女子인데 그 키가 8尺 2寸
