기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Announcement/Editorial Postscript)

Some people criticize "Byeolgeongon" as a frivolous magazine. But that is not true. Absolutely not. Do you know how much poor and uneducated friends in Joseon welcome this?

Students! Doesn't it occur to you to buy a copy of "Byeolgeongon" and send it to your family in the countryside? And frankly write about what you feel in school, in society, and on the streets and send it in. Let's publish them and learn from each other. <15>
