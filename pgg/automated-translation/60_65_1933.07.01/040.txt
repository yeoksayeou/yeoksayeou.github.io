A Tale of Being Caught
						雙S

I'm going to skip the commonplace stories of being caught stealing and taken to the police station by a detective, or of children skipping school to go to the movies and being spanked by their parents.
Although it's a bit vulgar, just like the saying, "Any room is good, but the bedroom is the best, any house is good, but your wife's house is the best," the best stories are about "Eroticism."
I'd like to briefly introduce a few "episodes" in that area below.

Trying to pick someone up
Whether it's a department store, a movie theater, a tearoom, or a concert hall, it seems like I'm always craving women, and everywhere I go, there are beautiful and desirable women galore.
The question of whether to chase after them always arises when I'm standing there, my eyes glued to the back of a woman walking along the pavement, wondering where I might meet her.
Go ahead and get her! After making up my mind, I started chasing after her. If she was a local train, I could be a express train several times faster, but I matched my pace to hers and followed her slowly at a snail's pace.
These days, women wear long skirts and walk briskly, so no matter how hard you stare at their buttocks from behind, you can't find much of a curve, but instead, you become enamored with their slim figure.
Her face is somewhat plain, but her style is good, and since she's so good at wandering around like that, she must have been followed by men before. From the moment I entered "Yajugae," our eyes met several times. It seemed she had noticed me as a suspicious character.
Well, I don't have any particular goal in following her. I have a grand ambition to gradually develop some kind of relationship with this beautiful woman I'm currently following, but for now, I just want to know where she lives. And I'm only worried about being tricked by a woman and ending up in a shameful situation like that friend who was completely fooled.
The woman walked slowly, then suddenly ran quickly, then pretended to buy something at a store. Something was definitely different about her. But I was determined to persevere and followed her steadily from "Yajugae" to "Tongui-dong."
At that moment, someone blocked the path between the woman and me. It was an old classmate from middle school.
"Oh, what a coincidence!"
I greeted him like that, but I was so anxious that I wanted to shake off his hand. Because if I didn't go and see which house that woman had disappeared into, I was afraid I wouldn't be able to tell which house it was. In a fluster, I just laughed awkwardly and came near the place where the woman had disappeared, looking around here and there. Right in front of my eyes, the woman reappeared. Looking at her female friend following her out and greeting her, it was clear that the place she had gone into wasn't her house, but her friend's house.
When she saw me, she seemed to frown slightly. But I couldn't help but continue to pretend to look for a house, looking around here and there.
"Crazy bastard!"
I clearly heard those words. I felt like I was falling off a cliff.
When the woman went back down to "Yajugae," I didn't have the courage to follow her again. It seemed she had deliberately dragged me all the way to "Tongui-dong" and then gone to her real house. But I couldn't just stand there forever, so I slowly walked down, and when the friend who had come out to see her off saw me, she clearly smirked and closed the gate with a bang. My face must have been redder than a beet. But that would have been fine, but when the classmate I had met earlier, who had gone down and come back up, saw me looking bewildered, he grinned and ran towards me.

Taking her away
I was coming home late from the In'guk Theater (仁극장) when it suddenly started to rain. It wasn't raining that hard, so I could have just walked in the rain, but I was so worried about my new yellow silk overcoat and the McGreggor hat I had bought the other day that I stood under a dimly lit awning, looking up at the dark sky. Then, a woman came along with an umbrella, glanced at me as she passed.
I immediately recognized that she was that type of woman from her clothing, her gait, and her demeanor, although something about her face also gave it away.
It would have been fine if she had just glanced at me and passed by, but she went down a ways and then came back up, this time looking at me very intently. Of course, it was late at night and it was drizzling, so even though it was a summer night, it was perfectly quiet and there were no other people around. I became even more convinced that she was a suspicious woman, and I thought that if I messed with her, something would definitely happen.
Ah! And then, the woman who had gone back up came past me for the third time, and this time she smiled at me and stood a little ways away. Without hesitation, I went next to her and said,
"Excuse me."
But the woman didn't change her expression at all, as if she were talking to someone she knew well, and told me to come under her umbrella.
As I walked along the dark street with the woman, sharing an umbrella, I thought to myself. First, we'll go to a bar and drink until we're tipsy, then we'll hug each other, and then when we clear the table... I was so curious that I looked at the woman's face, which I didn't find unattractive. The woman smiled, and the dimples on her cheeks made her look cute, and her slender body and tall height aroused my sexual desire.
If we go, there's a police box in the middle. When the woman saw the red light of the police box, she stopped walking and said,
"We should have gone through the alley!"
She said that she had forgotten. But since we had already come this far, there was nothing we could do, and since I said it was okay, she seemed to gain courage. We walked on again.
But it seemed like she was still uneasy, so when we came right near the police box, she had me stand on the side of the police box and switched places, then crossed the main street and went up the other side.
During that time, I was quiet and the woman didn't say anything either, so we were walking along silently when, sure enough, we heard someone calling us. I looked back and saw a policeman waving his hand in front of the police box.
It was natural that we would be questioned, but even after being questioned, I wouldn't be able to take the woman with me as I pleased. When I saw the bright light (illegible 1 line), my feelings suddenly faded. And then, I started to feel uncomfortable, and the way she talked back to the policeman was so vulgar that I eventually gave up.
Since it seemed like they didn't have any more questions to ask me, I asked if I could go, and the policeman happily said yes.
As I left the prostitute I had been taking with me at the police box, I smiled to myself. Because she had put on so much makeup on her dark face, she was being questioned for too long, and I had lost interest and came alone first. I repeated this to myself as I walked...

Looking at her
If you go to the old houses on the other side of the street, they're very shallow, and the windows are long, so if you open the windows, everything inside the room is visible to passersby.
But what's the point of looking at the poor furnishings of the room, the rice container placed high up, the empty cookie boxes of unknown origin lined up crookedly, the dirty bedding, the dark floor?
I wasn't interested in looking at those things, but because they were visible, I always looked at them without thinking as I passed by. One time, as I passed by, the room was bright. I saw that the walls had been newly papered in white, a random "Millet" painting was hanging on the wall, and a loafer's suit and Panama hat were hanging there, and I knew that the residents of the room had changed.
Later, I found out that a young couple had moved in. The husband worked at some company, and the wife never went out, but she was a modern woman. I suppose there wasn't anything special about being a modern woman, but when I found out that she was a very young and beautiful young lady, my interest soared to 100%.
Because they always left the windows open during the day, it was easy to look into the shallow room and see the beautiful and attractive wife spending time in her house. But I wanted to see how the young and affectionate couple spent their nights during the deep summer nights. However, they always closed the windows at night, so I couldn't do as I pleased. I could only pass by, looking at the brightly lit windows, feeling disappointed. Even if they left the door open and put up a screen, you wouldn't be able to see anything during the day, but at night, the streets were dark and the room was bright, so you could easily look in. But they kept the window covered with paper, as if telling me not to look, so I couldn't achieve my goal, and eventually I even got the urge to punch a hole in the window with my fist (illegible 1 line).
But one night - I met some friends and had a few drinks, and I was very drunk on the way home. It must have been around two in the morning. Since it was a summer night, dawn would break and it would be light in just two or three hours.
As I came up and passed by that house, I suddenly remembered and looked over that way. For some reason that night, the window that was always closed was wide open. My heart suddenly started pounding. I craned my neck like a crane and looked back, and sure enough, the two men and women had thrown off their blankets and were sound asleep.
But unfortunately, I could only see the husband who was lying on the far side, and I couldn't see the wife because she was on the side of the room. I couldn't just stand there in the middle of the night in front of someone else's open window, so I pretended to pass by, but I heard the sound of shoes right across from me.
I was startled and tried to slip away, but the person went into the house near where the sound of shoes had come from and closed the gate. Then I became greedy again. I thought that it was a golden opportunity to observe the sleeping form of the beautiful woman I had been longing to see, so I slowly went back to the window and this time I got close to the window and was about to look in (illegible 1 line).
"Hey, who's there?"
"You crazy bastard!"
I heard those words. The husband who had been sleeping on the right side jumped up - of course, I ran for my life.

Going over the wall
In our uncle's house, there was a girl who they had brought in to work and help out, and they had promised to arrange her marriage. She used to live a comfortable life and was raised as a precious only daughter under the care of her parents, but now she was in this pitiful situation. However, even though our uncle's house was someone else's house, it seemed that the girl's father and our uncle had been very close friends, so they treated the girl with the utmost respect and loved her very much.
One day, I went down to my uncle's house, and the next morning, I found out that all the family members had gone out to work in the fields, leaving the girl alone in the house to watch the house. Of course, that night when I came home, I couldn't sleep and kept thinking about it. That's because I had been in love with the girl for a long time and had been secretly pining for her (illegible 1 line).
Since they were going to work in the fields early in the morning, I knew that there wouldn't be anyone left in the house by late morning, so I ate breakfast and slowly went down to my uncle's house. Even though I knew that no one would be there and the girl would be alone, I pretended not to know.
Ah, but the gate was locked from the inside. I called out to open the gate, but there was no answer from inside. It seemed like she was thinking that if I called out two or three times and there was no answer, I would just leave. I couldn't ask her to open the gate any more.
But as I was about to go back, I suddenly became worried about the girl being alone in that big house. I don't know if there are "dog"-like incidents like the ones that are popular in the United States these days, but who can guarantee that a thief won't break in with the current situation, and who can know for sure that the girl won't encounter an unfortunate incident? And I had to deliver the letter that I had written to her with all my heart the night before. So I decided to go over the wall just to deliver the letter, and I was about to climb over and jump inside when.
"Who's there?"
I heard a voice. Startled, I looked around and saw that the voice wasn't coming from inside the house, but from the house next to the wall. An old man came out and shouted again. I was embarrassed that I was climbing over the wall, so I quickly jumped down. But I had to explain myself, so I said,
"This is my uncle's house."
"So what?"
He said that and just walked on. It seemed like he was saying that even if it was my house, why was I going over the wall instead of going through the gate? He just left after I came down. But I didn't have the courage to climb over again and felt frustrated. I was about to tear up the letter, but I couldn't bring myself to do that, so I thought that if I went to the post office right now and sent it by special delivery, it would reach the girl's hand in two hours, and I ran towards the post office.
