Magazine Title	別건곤 第15號
Publication Date	1928년 08월 01일
Article Title	民謠, 님의 배 (Folk Song, My Love's Ship)
Author	劉道順
Article Type	시 (Poem)

Every day I climb the mountain and look at the sea,
The vast blue waves stretch a hundred *li* north and south,
But there's still no news of the ship my love is on,
And I live alone in the riverside village for years.

Eheya deya, the sound of oars,
Rilniri kungttang, the sound of drums,
Salt ships and fishing boats all come in,
Why doesn't the ship my love is on come?

In two or three years, I'll fulfill my wish,
And return home in silk clothes.
My love left on a boat for thousands of *li* to a foreign land,
And today, only white herons are seen on the sea.

Eheya deya, the sound of oars,
Rilniri kungttang, the sound of drums,
Salt ships and fishing boats all come in,
Why doesn't the ship my love is on come?

The setting sun slants over the mountain's waist,
Colorful clouds float in the sky,
Where is a sacred famous temple?
I'd pray for my love there.

Eheya deya, the sound of oars,
Rilniri kungttang, the sound of drums,
Salt ships and fishing boats all come in,
Why doesn't the ship my love is on come? <1>

오늘도 바다에는 白鶴만나네

릴니리 꿍땅 북소리나네
