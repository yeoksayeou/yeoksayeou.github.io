Call for Branch Offices and Sub-Branches of Kaebyŏksa

We are seeking individuals with a passion for local culture to establish branch or sub-branch offices of our company and cooperate in our endeavors. By becoming a member of this great and esteemed enterprise, you will not only elevate your own status and acquire knowledge that keeps you abreast of the times, but also become a noble figure who pioneers local culture.

If interested, please send a ten-chon (拾錢) postage stamp to our headquarters to request an information booklet. We will send you the detailed regulations. ＜55＞

開闢社의 支分社大募集
地方文化를 爲하야 有志하신이는 本社의 支社나 分社機關을 設하시고 本社의 事業에 協力活動을 하여주십시오 좃코 尊貴한 큰 事業 큰 機關의 一員이 되는 同時에 러서 自己의 地位가 向上되고 世上에 뒤러지지안는 智識을 갓게되는한편으로 地方의 文化를 開拓식혀주는 高貴한 人物이될것입니다.
有意하시면 拾錢切手封入하야 本社로 案內書를 請求하십시오 자세한 規定을 보내드리겟습니다.＜55＞
