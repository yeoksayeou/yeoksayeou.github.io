朝鮮歷史日誌 第一回 一月錄
靑吾生

January 1st (Old December 2nd)
◇ Yi T’aejo attacked Tongnyŏng-bu (東寧府) in Liaodong and achieved a great victory (Koryŏ Kongmin Wang's year Kyŏngsul)
◇ Korean Prison Regulations were promulgated (Kwangmu 2nd year)

January 2nd (Old December 3rd)
◇ Koryŏ Ch’ungsŏn Wang was exiled to T’ubŏn (Ch’ungsuk Wang's year Imshin)

January 4th (Old December 5th)
◇ Koryŏ Yi Mogŭn-saek's (李牧隱穡) father and son and Yi Ch’ung-in (李崇仁) were exiled (Kongmin Wang's year Kisa)

January 6th (Old December 7th)
◇ The External Affairs Ministry (外部) was abolished and the Foreign Affairs Bureau (外務局) was established (Kwangmu 10th year)
◇ Khitan troops invaded Anbuk (安北) (Koryŏ Hyŏnjong's year Kyŏngsul)

January 7th (Old December 8th)
◇ Hongwijŏk Mao Gŭg-yŏng (毛居敬) crossed the frozen Amnok River and invaded (Koryŏ Kongmin Wang's year Musul)
◇ The Chŏlyŏngdo Lease Agreement was concluded (Joseon Foundation 494th year)

January 8th (Old December 9th) <86>
◇ Regulations for Issuance of Residence Permits to Russians were promulgated (Kwangmu 11th year)
◇ Ch’ing T’aejong led an army of 130,000 across the Amnok River at night and invaded the country (Injyo Pyŏngja year)

January 9th (Old December 10th)
◇ Silla Kim Yu-sin led nine generals to P’yŏngyang (Munmu Wang's year Kyeyae)
◇ The King received congratulations through the Crown Prince (Yŏngjo Ŭlmi year)

January 10th (Old December 11th)
◇ The Forest Law was promulgated (Yunghŭi 2nd year)
◇ Koryŏ established Susŏwŏn (修書院) for the first time (Sŏngjong Kich’uk year)

January 11th (Old December 12th)
◇ Silla Kyŏngsun Wang Kim Chŏn surrendered to Koryŏ (T’aejo Ŭlmi year)

January 13th (Old December 14th)
◇ Injyo moved to Namhansansŏng (Injyo Pyŏngja year)
◇ Kings Wang U and Wang Ch’ang of Koryŏ were harmed (Kongyang Wang's year Kisa)
◇ Cho Hwi-t’ak and Ch’ŏng rebelled in Hwaju and submitted to the Mongols (Koryŏ Kojong Muo year)

January 14th (Old December 15th)
◇ Koryŏ Yun Kwan greatly defeated the Jurchen army in Chŏngju (Yejong Chŏnghae year)
◇ Taegu Kwŏn’ŏp Model Farm was established (Yunghŭi 2nd year)

January 15th (Old December 16th) <87>
◇ Hong Kyŏng-nae raised the flag of rebellion in Pakch’ŏn (Sunjo Sinmi year)
◇ The officials of Kae phủ (改府) in Koryŏ began to wear their hair up (Ch’ungyŏl Wang Kapsul year)

January 16th (Old December 17th)
◇ Regulations on Printing, Construction, Bricks, and Customs Duties were promulgated (Yunghŭi 2nd year)
◇ Ch’ing troops besieged Namhansansŏng (Injyo Pyŏngja year)
◇ King Kongmin of Koryŏ visited Andong Yŏngho Bridge and enjoyed boating (Kongmin Sinch’uk year)
◇ Sunjong made a western tour (Yunghŭi 3rd year)

January 17th (Old December 18th)
◇ Kasan County Magistrate Chŏng Yak was harmed by Hong Kyŏng-nae's army (Sunjo Sinmi year)

January 18th (Old December 19th)
◇ A certain person in Taegu initiated the National Debt Redemption Fund Raising (Kwangmu 11th year)
◇ Sunjong arrived in Ŭiju (Yunghŭi 3rd year)
◇ Seven Japanese warships arrived in Pusan (Kojong Pyŏngja year)

January 19th (Old December 20th)
◇ Cho Chŏng-am Kwangjo was exiled to Nŭngju (Chungjong Kimyo year)

January 20th (Old December 21st)
◇ Taewŏn’gun was born (Sunjo Kyŏngjin year)
◇ Taewŏn’gun's tomb was moved to P’aju (Yunghŭi 2nd year) <88>

January 21st (Old December 22nd)
◇ Kim Yong-am returned to the Ming Dynasty (Kongyang Imsin year)
◇ Regulations for the Industrial Training Institute (工業傳習所) were first established (Kwangmu 11th year)

January 22nd (Old December 23rd)
◇ Chŏng Mong-ju was born in Yŏngch’ŏn (Koryŏ Ch’ungsuk Chŏngch’uk year)

January 23rd (Old December 24th)
◇ The Agreement on Employment of Police Advisor was signed (Kwangmu 9th year)
◇ Japanese envoy Kuroda Kiyotaka docked at Namyang (Kojong Pyŏngja year)

January 24th (Old December 25th)
◇ Ming General Li Ju-song (李如松) crossed the Amnok River (Sŏnjo Imjin year)

January 25th (Old December 26th)
◇ Yi Yulgok was born at Ojukheon in Kangnŭng (Chungjong Pyŏngsin year)

January 27th (Old December 28th)
◇ The 율준時 (Yuljunsi) was revised.

January 28th (Old December 29th)
◇ Koryŏ Kim Pang-gyŏng (金方慶) conquered Japan together with the Mongol army (Ch’ungyŏl Wang Kapsul year)
◇ Liquor Tax, Tobacco Tax, and House Tax Law were promulgated (Yunghŭi 3rd year)
This diary will be continued monthly. I am afraid that there may be some omissions due to the busy investigation. I would be grateful if anyone could write and send us what they know. <89>

朝鮮歷史日誌 第一回 一月錄
						靑吾生
一日(舊十二月二日)
◇李太祖가 遼東의 東寧府를 襲擊하야 大破하다(高麗恭愍王庚戌)
◇韓國監獄規則이 頒布되다(光武二年)

二日(舊十二月三日)
◇高麗忠宣王이 吐藩에 被流되다(忠肅王壬申)

四日(舊十二月五日)
◇高麗李牧隱穡의 父子와 李崇仁이 流配되다(恭讀王己巳)

六日(舊十二月七日)
◇外部를 廢하고 外務局을 置하다(光武十年)
◇契丹兵이 安北에 來宼하다(高麗顯宗庚戌)

七日(舊十二月八日)
◇紅衣賊毛居敬이 鴨綠江結氷을타서 來宼하다(高麗恭愍王戊戌)
◇絶影島借約이 成立되다(朝開四九四年)

八日(舊十二月九日)＜86＞
◇露國人居住券交付規則이 頒布되다(光武十一年)
◇淸太宗이 十三萬大軍을 率하고 鴨綠江을 夜渡하야 國境에 侵入하다(仁祖丙子)

九日(舊十二月十日)
◇新羅金庾信이 九將을 率하고 平壤에 赴하다(文武王癸亥)
◇正朝가 世孫代理로 受賀하다(英祖乙未)

十日(舊十二月十一日)
◇森林法이 頒布되다(隆熙二年)
◇高麗가비로소 修書院을두다(成宗己丑)

十一日(舊十二月十二日)
◇新羅敬順王金傳가 高麗에 歸降하다(高太祖乙未)

十三日(舊十二月十四日)
◇仁祖가 南漢山城에 播遷하다(仁祖丙子)
◇高麗의 王禑王昌이 被害하다(恭讓王己巳)
◇趙暉卓靑이 和州에서 叛하야 蒙古에 附하다(高麗高宗戊午)

十四日(舊十二月十五日)
◇高麗尹瓘이 女眞軍을 定州에서 大破하다(睿宗丁亥)
◇大邱勸業模範場이 設立되다(隆熙二年)

十五日(舊十二月十六日)＜87＞
◇洪景來가 博川에서 反逆의 旗를 들다(純祖辛未)
◇高麗의 改府大官이비로소 頭髮을하다(忠烈王甲戌)

十六日(舊十二月十七日)
◇印刷ㆍ建築ㆍ煉瓦ㆍ關稅規程이 頒布되다(隆熙二年)
◇淸兵이 南漢山城을 包圍하다(仁祖丙子)
◇高麗恭愍王이 安東暎湖橋에 幸行하야뱃노리를하다(恭愍辛丑)
◇純宗이 西巡하다(隆熙三年)

十七日(舊十二月十八日)
◇嘉山郡守鄭箬가 洪景來軍에게 被害하다(純祖辛未)

十八日(舊十二月十九日)
◇大邱에서 某某人이 國債報償金募集發起를하다(光武十一年)
◇純宗이 義州에 着하다(隆熙三年)
◇日艦七隻이 釜山에 來泊(高宗丙子)

十九日(舊十二月二十日)
◇趙靜菴光祖가 綾州에 流配되다(中宗己卯)

二十日(舊十二月二十一日)
◇大院君誕生(純祖庚辰)
◇大院君墓를 坡州에 移葬(隆熙二年)＜88＞

二十一日(舊十二月二十二日)
◇金龍岩■가 明國에 還入(恭讓壬申)
◇工業傳習所官制始定(光武十一年)

二十二日(舊十二月二十三日)
◇鄭夢周가 永川에서 生하다(麗忠肅丁丑)

二十三日(舊十二月二十四日)
◇警務顧問聘協調調印(光武九年)
◇日使黑田淸隆이 泊南陽(高宗丙子)

二十四日(舊十二月二十五日)
◇明李如松이 渡鴨綠江(宣祖壬辰)

二十五日(舊十二月二十六日)
◇李栗谷이 江陵烏竹軒에서 生(中宗丙申)

二十七日(舊十二月二十八日)
◇溧準時를 改定하다

二十八日(舊十二月二十九日)
◇高麗金方慶이 元兵과갓치 日本을 征伐하다(忠烈王甲戌)
◇酒稅ㆍ煙草稅ㆍ家屋稅法이 頒布(隆熙三年)
