Men's Views on Women's Points of Reform
Traditional Subservience

Lee Seong-hwan

Women's reform! I see it as a problem of human nature reform. I think the scope of discussion related to it will also be large. However, avoiding complex and multifaceted stories, I just want to assert one thing urgently: I want the traditional subservience of women to be eradicated. Women are also human beings, so why don't they have the spirit of independence and self-reliance as human beings? Some people say that women cannot be independent physiologically. They say that in their psychological life or in their structural form, they will inevitably be no more than a dependent object in the support of men. But I don't think so. In today's era, when civilization is developing at an accelerated rate, we should not only look at men-centered civilization, but there is plenty of room for women to create a women's culture adapted to women. A bird trapped in a cage will freely leap into the vast air if it is released. In the past, making women a subordinate object of men was an unnatural act like caging a bird that can fly. It is ridiculous that this unnatural prank has somehow become a tradition for women, and that women themselves have passed it off as if they were satisfied with it, without any resistance or reform. As long as women think that food and clothing come only when men loosen their purse strings, they have lost their qualifications as human beings. Also, even if men are engaged in external affairs and women are in charge of household affairs, dividing duties by function, it is believed that new people of the new era cannot create an ethical view that says that they are internal support and live in a master-subordinate relationship in terms of personality. In short, what I want to emphasize as important in the issue of women's reform is that all women in the world must cleanly abandon their traditional subservience to men. Materially as well as spiritually, there must be a new determination for independent self-reliance. In the eyes of the old days, it may have been good for women to flirt and play, but modern people, especially new people trying to create a new society, cannot help but demand women as human beings rather than women as machines. Finally, I repeat: Women have plenty of time to create a women's culture, so please come out to the streets of reform as soon as possible... ... . <70>

Women Also Have a Sense of Responsibility
Kim Ki-jeon

It's not that all women are like this, but in general, when it comes to household affairs, childcare, or matters of society or the nation, it seems that women think they have no responsibility at all, and when something goes wrong, they pass the responsibility entirely to men and think of themselves as irresponsible. To give an easy example, if a woman has any dissatisfaction with her household affairs, she only blames the man, saying that she met the wrong man, and when it comes to educating her children, she completely pushes it to the man, and if she can't educate them, she says it's always the father's fault. Everything else is the same. This comes from the habit of women not having any responsible life under the traditional male supremacy, but from now on, it would be good to realize that men and women have joint responsibility.

Two Things I Want to Say
Park Dal-seong

Whether dealing with my own wife or someone else's wife, I sometimes have these thoughts. In other words,
"I wish she wouldn't do that, why is she like that? Is it because she's originally <71> a woman, so I can't help it... ... Can't I fundamentally fix that? If I were the creator... ..." I think. To repeat,
One, cases where they can say it but don't say it and just turn around and sob; and
Two, cases where they stubbornly avoid doing things that they can do alone and make the husband take full responsibility.
These two cases are the most frustrating, and if I were the creator, if I could give women's tears in a word, I would take away nine and give them about one, and if I gave women's independent self-reliance, I would add nine and give them about ten. and
If I were the creator, I would abolish menstruation and, in terms of childbirth, I would make it so that they could only have a fixed number of two or three children in their lifetime.

Self-Control and Economic Common Sense
Park No-eul

First of all, in a situation where I can't even manage my own personality or life, let alone reform it, saying something about women's reform is already an absurd and excessive act, but since I still have senses to see with my eyes (not just to see women) and hear with my ears, I will still make some requests. <72>
First, I want to say that there is a lack of self-control, and this is revealed even in the smallest aspects of women's daily lives. For example, without considering the reality of Joseon and their own economic power, they are unnecessarily drawn to extravagant trends and try to indulge in luxury beyond their means, or they get married and before they can even understand their husbands well, their hearts become clouded by other men (even their husband's friends) and they confuse kindness with intimacy. I think it's all because of a lack of solidity in life and always longing for other people's lives.
Next, it is said that they have no economic sense or common sense. To give a nearby example, there seem to be quite a few pitiful noble women (貴婦人) who, as the so-called housewives of a family, do not know how much a bag of rice costs or how much a pound of meat costs, let alone daily necessities, and yet they think of it as a luxurious boast. You can easily guess the rest. The accounting that comes from their heads cannot be scaled or complete, and the husbands cannot trust and feel at ease.
As the saying goes, if you ride a horse, you want to put your servant ahead of you, and there are many more things I want to ask for, but I will watch the situation for now and submit these two things first. <73>

Five Things in Order of Preference
Seongbuk-dong Person

One, I wish they would save some of their tears... ... Because humans are emotional animals, it seems a bit cold and heartless if they don't have tears. But women have so many tears that they cry even when they are a little happy, cry even when they are a little upset, cry even when they are a little sad, and cry whenever they see other people's problems. They cry like this and cry like that, and it seems like they can't do anything because of crying.
Two, I wish they wouldn't force themselves to smile when they don't have a smile... ... It's better to smile than to be angry in terms of emotions. However, it is common for women to force themselves to smile when they don't have genuine emotions. It would be better to have more genuine interactions.
Three, I wish they would talk a little less... ... Women are descendants of sparrows and talk more than men. It doesn't matter if the words are useful, but they talk pointlessly even about useless things. They talk even when someone else makes rice cakes, when their father-in-law's back is bent, when their mother-in-law's heels are round, when they see a man wearing clothes. There are more of these words than pebbles on the riverbank or stars in the sky. I wish they could fix that somehow.
Four, I wish they would save some of their nagging... ... There is no one who doesn't nag, whether man or woman, and if there is no nagging at all, it's boring. However, nagging recklessly is despicable and incomprehensible. It's not that all women are like this, but usually, when you look at women these days, they nag without reason even if a man only has a picture of another woman or is seen going out with another woman. I wish they could fix that too.
Five, I wish they would consider the man's situation... ... It depends on the education problem and various circumstances, but women usually don't know the man's situation and always bother the man. Regardless of the man's situation, whether it's clothes or household goods, there are many people who only think about themselves and keep asking for things. That is a big deal in terms of friendship.

Sex Change
Born in the Year of Im-in

I really want all women to go to an internal medicine doctor and get sex reassignment surgery, but if that happens, they will have to get old sex restoration surgery again in the construction period, and my eyes will be lonely for a while, so I will hold back and not open my mouth.
It's boring to ask for something that's ordinary, so should I just ask them not to exchange marriage certificates and business cards?
In general, women abandon their business cards (occupations) that they had until the wedding ceremony. <75>

女子도 責任感을

여자라고 다 그런 것은 안임니다만은 대개 보면 가정의 살임사리로나 子女敎育 문제로나 사회 또는 국가의 일에 대하야 全然히 여자는 책임이 업는 것 갓치 생각하고 무엇이나 잘못되면 그것을 모도 남자의 잘못으로만 책임을 넘기고 자기는 무책임한 것처럼 생각하는 것 갓다. 쉬운 예를 드러 말하자면 여자가 집안 살님사리에 무슨 불만이 잇스면 자기는 남자를 잘못 맛낫느니 하고 남자만 稱怨하며 자녀의 교육을 식히는 것도 全然 남자에게만 밀어버려서 교육을 못 식히면 반듯이 그것은 아비의 잘못이라 한다. 그 외 다른 일도 모다가 그럿타. 그것은 在來 男性專制下에 여자는 하등 책임생활을 하지 못한 인습에서 생겨난 것이지만은 이 압흐로는 그것도 좀 깨다러서 남녀가 다 공동책임이 잇는 것을 인식해야 조흘 것 갓다.
