Apology/Notice

The serial narrative "Ach'im (아츰)" which has been published monthly in this magazine, was supposed to be written by Hong Myŏng-hŭi this time. However, due to an unexpected sudden incident during the editorial process, he was unable to write it, so it will not be included in this issue. <163>

**Named Entities**

洪命憙	Hong Myŏng-hŭi

謝告
