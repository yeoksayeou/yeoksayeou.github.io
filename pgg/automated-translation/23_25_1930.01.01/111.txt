Chronological Record of Gyeong-o Years in 4200 Years of World History
Gyeong-o Year, 3rd Year of Dangun Joseon

◇ 27th Year of Emperor Yao of China - 2331 BC (From the Gyeong-o Year, 3rd Year of Dangun Joseon, to the Gyeong-o Year of the 7th Year of King Gojong of the Joseon Dynasty, there are a total of 70 Gyeong-o years in world history over a period of 4200 years. However, since there are few historical facts worth mentioning before the 28th Gyeong-o year, the 8th Year of King Cheollo of Gija Joseon, they are omitted here, starting from the 8th Year of King Cheollo.)

Gyeong-o Year, 8th Year of King Cheollo of Gija Joseon
◇ Built Guseondae on Heulgol Mountain with patterned stones
◇ 1st Year of King Xiang of Zhou = 651 BC - Duke Huan of Qi swears an oath with the feudal lords at Guqiu

Gyeong-o Year, 4th Year of King Bongil
◇ 16th Year of King Ding of Zhou - Lu attacks Qi, and in July, King Zhuang of Chu dies
◇ 591 BC - The king of Media battles with the king of Lydia

Gyeong-o Year, 30th Year of King Suseong
◇ 40th Year of King Jing of Zhou = 531 BC - Confucius becomes an official of Lu, and in November, Chu destroys Cai.

Gyeong-o Year, 36th Year of King Jese
◇ 5th Year of King Yuan of Zhou = Duke Ai of Lu pays tribute to Yue
◇ 471 BC = The Roman People's Assembly is established, opposing the aristocratic military council, and the historian Thucydides is born.

Gyeong-o Year, 3rd Year of King Hyeokseong
◇ 15th Year of King Weilie of Zhou = Qi captures Lu City
◇ 411 BC = The Athenian aristocratic party organizes the Council of Four Hundred and seizes control of the state.

Gyeong-o Year, 11th Year of King Gyeongsun
◇ 18th Year of King Xian of Zhou = Qin attacks Wei, and Wei returns Handan to Zhao and swears an oath at the Zhang River. Shen Buhai becomes the prime minister of Han.
◇ 351 BC = Roman commoners obtain the right to be elected as prosecutors.

Gyeong-o Year, 25th Year of King Hyeonmun
The king abdicates the throne to Crown Prince Seok and calls himself King Samno.
◇ 24th Year of King Nan of Zhou = Qin attacks Han and captures Wan.
◇ 291 BC = Death of Greek comedic playwright Menander (born 342 BC).

Gyeong-o Year, 2nd Year of King Jongtong
◇ 16th Year of Qin Shi Huang = Han offers Nanyang to Qin
◇ 231 BC = Rome suppresses the rebellion in Casilinum.

Gyeong-o Year, 19th Year of King An of Mahan
◇ 9th Year of Emperor Wen of Han = Great drought in spring
◇ 171 BC = Third Macedonian War

Gyeong-o Year, 3rd Year of King Hyo
◇ 6th Year of Emperor Wu of Han = Suppressed the Western Qiang, Southern Yue, and Southwestern Yi
◇ 111 BC = Jugurthine War of Rome <76>

Gyeong-o Year, 8th Year of King Won (7th Year of King Hyeokgeose of Silla, 9th Year of King Dongbuyeo)
◇ 3rd Year of Emperor Xuan of Han, Ganlu = In the first month, Huhan Yadan comes to the court, and in the third month, Chancellor Huang Ba dies, and Yu Dingguo is appointed.
◇ 51 BC = Cleopatra, sister of the Egyptian Ptolemy XIII (brother - 47), jointly reigns.

Gyeong-o Year, 7th Year of King Namhae of Silla (10 BC)
Appointed Seok Talhae as Daebu (大輔).
◇ Appointed Seok Talhae as Daebu.
◇ 29th Year of King Yurimyeong of Goguryeo = Built a detached palace in Dogokcheon
◇ 28th Year of King Onjo, founder of Baekje = Appointed Dasu as crown prince
◇ 2nd Year of Wang Mang's Xin Dynasty = In the second month, Mang deposes the feudal lords of Han and renames the Xiongnu chief as "Submissive Slave." In the twelfth month, he changes the treasures.

Gyeong-o Year, 14th Year of King Talhae (70 AD = Reference: Silla's Hyeokgeose Year 1 is 68 AD)
◇ 43rd Year of King Dasu of Baekje = Invades Silla
◇ 18th Year of King Taejo of Goguryeo = 29th Year of King Taejo of Gyeongnak
◇ 13th Year of Emperor Ming of Later Han = Built embankments on the Bian River in the He River, and in November, deposed King Ying of Chu

Gyeong-o Year, 19th Year of King Jima
◇ 78th Year of King Taejo of Goguryeo = 3rd Year of King Gaeru of Baekje
◇ 5th Year of Yongjian of Emperor Shun of Later Han = Ban Shi, son of Ban Chao, is executed
◇ 130 AD = Emperor Hadrian rebuilds Jerusalem

Gyeong-o Year, 7th Year of King Beolhyu
◇ Baekje invades, and Gu Do is defeated and demoted.
◇ 12th Year of King Gogukcheon of Goguryeo = 25th Year of King Geunchogo of Baekje
◇ 1st Year of Chuping of Emperor Xian of Han = Dong Zhuo assassinates King Hongnong, and in March, Zhuo moves the capital to Chang'an

Gyeong-o Year, 4th Year of King Chamhae
◇ 3rd Year of King Jungcheon of Goguryeo = 17th Year of King Guir of Baekje
◇ 13th Year of the Later Ruler of Shu = Wei defeats Wu greatly at Jiangling
◇ 250 AD = Seventh persecution of Christians in Rome

Gyeong-o Year, 1st Year of King Gilhae
◇ 11th Year of King Micheon of Goguryeo = 7th Year of King Biryu of Baekje
◇ 4th Year of Yongjia of Emperor Huai of Jin = In July, Han ruler Yuan dies, and his son He rebelled, but was assassinated by his brother Cong
◇ 310 AD = Arius is excommunicated, and Constantine kills Maximian.

Gyeong-o Year, 15th Year of King Naemul (370 AD)
◇ 40th Year of King Gogugwon of Goguryeo = Yeon Taejeon Gong Yongpyeong flees to Goryeo to avoid the rebellion of Qin King Meng, and Goryeo arrests and sends him back.
◇ 25th Year of King Geunchogo of Baekje
◇ 5th Year of Emperor Fei of Eastern Jin = In November, Fu Jian enters Ye, and Former Yan collapses

Gyeong-o Year, 14th Year of King Nulji (430 AD)
◇ 4th Year of King Biyu of Baekje = Song envoys arrive
◇ 18th Year of King Jangsu of Goguryeo
◇ 7th Year of Yuanjia of Emperor Wen of Song = In October, four-shu coins are cast, and in March, Wei is attacked, and Wa envoys arrive.

Gyeong-o Year, 12th Year of King Soji (490 AD)
Rebuilt Biraseong, and for the first time, opened a market in the capital to trade goods from all directions <77>
◇ 78th Year of King Jangsu of Goguryeo = Sent envoys to Wei
◇ 2nd Year of King Dongseong of Baekje = Built the two fortresses of Sahyeon and Eisan, and the king was defeated at Sasaewon
◇ 8th Year of Emperor Wu of Qi = In July, Prince Xiang of Badong is executed

Gyeong-o Year, 11th Year of King Jinheung
◇ 28th Year of King Seong of Baekje = 6th Year of King Yangwon of Goguryeo, Baekje captures Dosalseong of Goryeo, and Goryeo captures Geumhyeonseong of Je, so Silla takes advantage of the situation to attack and capture the two fortresses, and Je also re-establishes relations with Qi
◇ 28th Year of Emperor Jianwen of Liang = In May, Qi claims the title of emperor, and Eastern Wei collapses
◇ 550 AD = Slavs and Huns invade the Eastern Roman Empire

Gyeong-o Year, 32nd Year of King Jinpyeong
◇ King Yeongyang of Goryeo = 11th Year of King Mu of Baekje
◇ 6th Year of Emperor Yang of Sui = Dredged the Jiangnan Canal and attacked and killed the King of Ryukyu
◇ 610 AD = Muhammad begins to preach Islam

Gyeong-o Year, 10th Year of King Munmu
Earthquake
◇ Geomuseum of Goguryeo tries to restore the old country and establishes Anseung as king, requesting to be a border region of Silla. Silla confers the title of King of Goryeo to Seung and places him in Geummajeo, fighting with Tang and capturing more than 80 fortresses of Je.
◇ 1st Year of Emperor Gaozong Xianheng of Tang = Xue Rengui attacks Tubo but is defeated.

Gyeong-o Year, 29th Year of King Seongdeok (12th Year of King Mu of Balhae)
Sent envoys to Tang for the second time.
◇ 10th Year of Kaiyuan of Emperor Xuanzong of Tang = In June, the Xi attack the Khitan
◇ 730 AD = Pope Gregory excommunicates the Eastern Roman Emperor

Gyeong-o Year, 6th Year of King Wonseong (54th Year of King Mun of Balhae, 790 AD)
◇ Great drought
Bigu Gulje = Increased the construction of the dike and sent Ilgilchan Baek-eo as an envoy to the Northern Kingdom.
◇ 6th Year of Zhenyuan of Emperor Dezong of Tang = Tubo occupies Anxi, and the Uighur Zhongzhen Qaghan is killed.

Gyeong-o Year, 12th Year of King Munseong
Amnesty within the country
◇ 4th Year of Emperor Xuanzong of Tang = Tubo plunders Hexi, and Linghu Chu becomes prime minister
◇ 850 AD = Christianity begins to enter Denmark and Sweden

Gyeong-o Year, 14th Year of King Hyogong
◇ 10th Year of Gyeon Hwon of Later Baekje = 6th Year of Qin Feng - Gung Ye greatly defeats the Later Baekje naval forces in Naju
◇ 4th Year of Emperor Taizu of Later Liang = In February, Huainan claims the title of Wu
◇ 910 AD = Founding of Oxford University

Gyeong-o Year, 21st Year of King Gwangjong of Goryeo (970 AD)
◇ 3rd Year of Kaibao of Emperor Taizu of Song = In the first month, Wang So-seo becomes a Guozijian (國子監) professor

Gyeong-o Year, 21st Year of King Hyeonjong
Heung Liao tried to request soldiers but it was not permitted. In this year, Heung Liao collapsed. 500 people from Balhae, Geolsae, and Khitan came to surrender, and the Eastern Jurchens offered bows and horses.
◇ 8th Year of Emperor Renzong of Song = Reinstated the Haeguan (解■) trading law.

Gyeong-o Year, 7th Year of King Seonjong
Song sends a collection of "Essence of Literature in the Garden of Letters (文苑英華集)," Lee Ja-ui is sent to Song, and the King goes to Samgaksan with the Queen Mother. The Sinheung Warehouse and Boje Temple are destroyed by fire.
◇ 5th Year of Emperor Zhezong of Song = Wen Yanbo retires, and Su Zhe becomes the Vice Director of the Censorate.

Gyeong-o Year, 4th Year of King Uijong (1150 AD)
The King visits Namgyeong, and sends Secret Emissary Park Sun-chung to the Jin, but upon hearing of the political turmoil in Jin, he stops.
◇ 4th Year of Emperor Gaozong of Song = Shi Quan attempts to assassinate Qin Hui but fails. <78>

Gyeong-o Year, 6th Year of King Huijong
Crown Prince Rin of King Myeongjong is summoned from Ganghwa. The powerful official Choe Chung-heon builds a large residence and claims to ward off evil spirits from construction, secretly capturing boys and girls, dressing them in five-colored clothes, and burying them in the corners of the roof, causing children to flee.
◇ 3rd Year of Emperor Ningzong of Song = The Jin army invades Gyu-ju and the Mongols invade Jin
◇ 1210 AD = German Emperor Otto IV attempts to conquer Western Sicily

Gyeong-o Year, 11th Year of King Wonjong
The Mongols invade. Bae Jung-son leads the Sambyeolcho and revolts from Ganghwa Island, relocating to Jindo, where Kim Bang-gyeong suppresses them. The Crown Prince returns to the country from Mongolia.
◇ 6th Year of Emperor Duzong of Song = Wen Tianxiang is dismissed from his post. The Mongols build a fortress in Wansan.
◇ 1270 AD = Louis IX of France launches the Seventh Crusade

Gyeong-o Year, 17th Year of King Chungsuk
Yuan exiles Emperor Mingzong to Daecheong Island. The King visits Namgyeong. The King and Princess come from Yuan. A system is initiated where candidates must recite legal poems, novels, and rhyme dictionaries in order to take the civil service examination.
◇ 1st Year of Emperor Wenzong of Jin = In March, Yunnan revolts
◇ 1330 AD = Paper is first made in Northern Europe

Gyeong-o Year, 2nd Year of King Wang Chang (1390 AD, 23rd Year of Hongwu of Ming)
The Office of Royal Lectures and the Palace Guard Command are established. Yi Jo-jeong of the Joseon dynasty greatly defeats the Japanese pirates in Eumjuk. The capital is moved to Hanyang, and Ryukyu comes to pay tribute

Gyeong-o Year, 32nd Year of King Sejong of the Joseon Dynasty
In February, the King ascends to heaven. Jeong Yeo-chang and Shin Su-geun are born
◇ 1st Year of Jingtai of Emperor Jingzong of Ming = The Oirat invade again and negotiate peace
◇ 1450 AD = The Bible is first printed with movable type

Gyeong-o Year, 5th Year of King Jungjong
Yu Dam-nyeon suppresses the Japanese pirates in Sampo. The law of cutting tendons is abolished, and the Yongsan Reading Hall is repaired. Kim Rin-hu is born.
◇ 5th Year of Emperor Wuzong of Ming = Prince Anhwa Zhu Zhifan revolts and is suppressed
◇ 1510 AD = Afonso de Albuquerque, Portuguese Governor of India, captures Goa

Gyeong-o Year, 3rd Year of King Seonjo
The wrongly accused of the Eulsa and Giyu years are exonerated, Kim Sang-heon is born, Yi Toe-gye dies, and the Jeonggong Dogam (正供都監) is established to oversee the payment of taxes from each district.
◇ 4th Year of Emperor Muzong of Ming = Prohibited lascivious sacrifices
◇ 1570 AD = Reconciliation between Buddhist and Christian sects

Gyeong-o Year, 8th Year of King Injo
Min Yu-jung, Song Gyu-ryeom, and Yi Se-hwa are born. Yi Seo and Jeong Chung-sin suppress the rebellion of Chen Jigan, a Ming person on Dando Island
◇ 3rd Year of Chongzhen of Emperor Yizong of Ming = Yuan Chonghuan is falsely accused and dies
◇ 1630 AD = Third phase of the Thirty Years' War. King Gustavus II Adolphus of Sweden invades Germany. Founding of Boston

Gyeong-o Year, 16th Year of King Sukjong
The musical score for the Confucian shrine is selected. Min Ik-su and Yi Gi-ji are born, and Kim Su-heung and Yi Xiang die. A fox enters Injeongjeon and cries for seven or eight days and nights
◇ 29th Year of Emperor Shengzu of Qing = In April, the Great Qing Code is completed
◇ 1690 AD = July 11 Battle of the Boyne, William III defeats James II

Gyeong-o Year, 26th Year of King Yeongjo
The Gyeonggi cavalry and the Gyunyeokcheong (균역청) are established. The King visits Onyang Hot Springs. Shin Gyeong-jun compiles the "Huminjeongeum (訓民正音) Diagram Explanation."
◇ 15th Year of Qianlong of Emperor Gaozong of Qing = Suppression of the Tibetan rebellion
◇ 1750 AD = Franklin's discovery regarding electricity, England begins manufacturing felt

Gyeong-o Year, 10th Year of King Sunjo
Yi Ji-yeong presents the "Comprehensive Treatise on the Five Rites (五禮通論)," earthquake
◇ 15th Year of Jiaqing of Emperor Renzong of Qing = In February, the suppression of the rebellion
◇ 1810 AD = Mexican Revolution, founding of the University of Berlin. France annexes Holland

Gyeong-o Year, 7th Year of King Gojong
Japanese envoy Hanabusa Yoshitada arrives with a national letter and requests trade, which is rejected. The "Five Rites Compilation (五禮便考)" is revised.
◇ 9th Year of Tongzhi of Emperor Muzong of Qing = In Tianjin, rioters massacre French missionaries, and government forces suppress the Hui rebels
◇ 1870 AD = The Franco-Prussian War begins, Italy annexes Rome, and in December, the German Empire is re-established <79>
