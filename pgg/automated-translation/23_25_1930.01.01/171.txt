Postcard Correspondence Welcome

The "Postcard Correspondence Column (葉書通信欄)" is gradually becoming more interesting. As I have said before, the content is absolutely free: short impressions, satire, magazine reviews, drama reviews, news, or anything else is acceptable (only postcards are accepted).

○ Please be sure to write "Postcard Correspondence (葉書通信)" on the envelope.＜98＞
