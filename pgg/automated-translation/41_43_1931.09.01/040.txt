What is the Hoover Boom?

Recently, the term "Hoover Boom" has become popular. It even appears in exam answers. So, what is the Hoover Boom, and what kind of results will it bring?

U.S. President Hoover advocated for a one-year moratorium on reparations payments from Germany to alleviate the global economic downturn and a one-year suspension of war debts owed by European nations to the United States. This proposal has been met with various observations, and each country is considering its own interests, either supporting it or supporting it conditionally. Regardless, it seems that the related countries generally agree with it now, with no objections. Perhaps because of this, rice prices have risen, stocks have risen, and other commodity prices have risen, giving the market a bit of vitality. The most significant effect is that newspapers, which had been struggling with a lack of news material this summer, have gained new material. Japanese newspapers, for example, were completely filled with the Hoover Boom issue at one point. Because of that, the person Hoover gave the newspaper companies news material.

Germany lost the war. That is a well-known fact. The nations that participated in the war took territory and colonies from Germany and, still not satisfied, decided to extract enormous reparations. However, Germany did not have the ability to pay the reparations. So, it was decided to receive a certain amount each year over many years. But Germany's devastated economic state could not handle it. So, the United States lent money to Germany to promote industrial recovery, intending to receive payments from the resulting strength, and lent a huge amount of money to Germany. That was the so-called Dawes Plan. As a result, Germany's economy gradually recovered. This economic recovery meant an increase in Germany's power, and that increasing power would resist unreasonable oppression. Furthermore, France and England could not be happy to leave Germany in the hands of the United States. England, of course, and France, which is considered one of the financial powers (金融資本國) in Europe, did not want to leave neighboring Germany in the hands of the United States forever. So, the Young Plan was created. All the more, Germany's power is increasing.

Now, what are the interests of the related countries due to the Hoover Plan?

England has no problems since it pays as much in war debts as it receives from Germany. France pays half of what it receives from Germany as war debts to foreign countries, and the remaining half remains in its own pocket, so there are interests involved. The United States benefits immensely because the more Germany's economic condition improves, the more American capital grows. So, the troubled country is France.

Kyŏngsŏng Electric's Municipalization Approved

Kyŏngsŏng Electric Company (京電), which had been making huge profits, was approaching the expiration of its licensed business period. As a result, the Kyŏngsŏng Municipal Council and the residents had intense opinions and activities centered on municipal management. Within the Kyŏngsŏng Municipal Council, there were two opposing factions: one faction wanted to approve municipalization, and the other faction, called the investigative faction, wanted to investigate and study whether municipalization would actually be beneficial before making a decision. These two factions were in intense opposition, but on the 9th of last month, after a long debate, the municipalization plan was approved with 31 votes for the municipalization faction and 19 votes for the investigative faction.

Educational Laborers' Union

Recently, incidents related to ideology have been continuously discovered, prisons have become crowded, and there are now specialized judges and prosecutors for ideological cases. Among them, the Educational Laborers' Union (敎育勞働者組合) case, whose publications in newspapers and magazines have recently been lifted, has particularly attracted public attention. It is said that it had its headquarters in Tokyo and a branch in Korea, and their attitude towards Korean education was, firstly, opposition to XXX education, abolition of salary increases for Japanese teachers (六활가봉을 철페) and improvement of the treatment of Korean teachers. The person in charge of the Korean branch was a person named Kamikōbe Yonosuke (上甲米太郞), who used only 30 won of his salary for his own living expenses and used the rest to cook rice and feed students who came to school hungry because they were poor, and he also vehemently opposed the joint cotton sales system, interfering with the authorities' policies, and was highly trusted by parents and residents in the area. It is said that he organized the Educational Laborers' Union and, to promote it among teachers at each common school, first sowed the seeds at Kyŏngsŏng Normal School and was active until he was discovered and arrested. A student at Kyŏngsŏng Normal School named Cho P'an-ch'ul (趙判出) was involved as a Korean. The aforementioned Kamikōbe Yonosuke was the principal of Konmyŏng Public Common School and had previously served as a lieutenant (小尉) in the army.

Second Round Table Conference Between England and India

Although the date has not yet been confirmed, the second Round Table Conference (圓卓會議) between England and India will be held in London this autumn, and all representatives are required to attend by the end of September, so it is expected to be held around October. Before the formal conference, there will be two important subcommittee meetings: the Federal Structure Committee to discuss India's federal organization plan and a committee to handle minority issues, which will be held in September. Not only Gandhi but also representatives from the National Congress Party will participate, increasing the number of people from 89 at the previous conference to 108 this time. In particular, Mrs. Sarojini Naidu will also attend the conference.

Ch'en Yu-jen's Visit to Japan

Ch'en Yu-jen (陣友仁), the foreign minister of the Kwangtung government, came to Japan, met with the foreign minister and the leaders of the army, and has been attracting much public attention. Currently, various factions opposing Chiang Kai-shek's Nanking government have gathered in China and formed a government in Kwangtung. Recently, they have even engaged in military action. In the north, Shih Yu-san has risen up against Chiang Kai-shek, and Feng Yü-hsiang and Yen Hsi-shan have risen up, creating a rapidly changing situation. Moreover, China is facing complex internal and external issues such as the recovery of concessions, the abolition of extraterritoriality, and the Manchuria-Mongolia problem. In this situation, Ch'en Yu-jen's visit to Japan to meet with government officials is by no means a trivial matter. According to newspapers, he is trying to buy weapons from Japan and wants to seek understanding from the Japanese because Ch'en Yu-jen has previously engaged in intense anti-Japanese activities, giving a bad impression to the Japanese. However, considering that Ch'en Yu-jen's visit to Japan was arranged by the Kwangtung consul and considering China's internal and external circumstances, it is difficult to simply dismiss it as such.

후-버- 景氣는 무엇
요사히 후-버- 경기란 소리가 유행한다. 시험문제 답안에까지 나온다. 그러면 후-버- 경기란 무엇이며 그는 엇떠한 결과를 가저올가?
미국의 대통령 후-버-는 주창하야 가로되 오늘의 세게적인 불경기를 퇴치하는 때는 렬국이 독일에서 바들 배상금을 일년간 유예하고 그 대신에 렬국이 미국에 지불하여야 할 전채(戰債)를 또한 일년간 유예한다고 햇다. 그러한 주°½이 세상에 나오자 여러 가지로의 관측이 잇고 각국은 또한 자기의 리해관게를 생각하고 그 입장에서 찬성하기도 하고 찬성은 하되 조건부로 하는 나라도 잇다. 그는 엇지 되엿건 이제 와서는 관게 렬국이 대개는 그에 찬성하고 이의(異議)가 업는 모양이다. 그 까닭인지 쌀갑도 오르고 주식도 오르고 그 타의 물건갑이 오르며 시장에는 다소 활기가 띄여온 것도 사실이다. 그보다도 가장 그 효과가 현저한 것은 이 여름철에 긔사 재료는 업고 하야 곤경을 치루고 잇든 신문사가 수가 생겻다는 것이다. 일본 신문과 가튼 것은 후-버- 경긔 문제가 한때은 전면을 덥고도 남엇다. 그것으로 해서 후-버-란 사람은 신문사에도 긔사 경긔를 주엇다.
독일은 전쟁에 젓다. 그는 다- 아는 사실이다. 그 전쟁에 참가햇든 렬국은 드러부터 독일의 령토를 빼앗고 식민지(植民地)를 빼앗고 그리고도 부족하야 막대한 배상금을 바더내이기로 햇다. 그러나 독일은 그 배상금을 지불할 힘이 업다. 그래서 여러 해를 두고 일년에 얼마씩 배정하야 밧기로 햇다. 그러나 독일의 파멸된 경제 상태는 그를 감당하는 수 업섯다. 그래서 미국은 독일에는 돈을 대여주고 그것으로 산업의 부흥을 도모하야 거긔에서 나는 힘으로 밧기로 하고 거대한 돈을 독일에 빗으로 주게 되엿다. 그가 이른바 또-스 안(案)이란 것이엿다. 그로 해서 독일의 경제게는 점차로 회복되여 간다. 그럿케 경제 상태가 회복하여 간다는 것은 곳 독일의 힘의 증대이니 그 증대되는 힘은 무리한 억압에 반항하게 되는 것이다. 뿐만 아니라 불란서와 영국이 독일을 미국의 손에 너코는 유쾌하여 할 리가 업는 것이다. 영국은 물론이요 불란서도 구라파에 잇서 금융자본국(金融資本國)으로 손곱기에 드는 처지에 린방의 독일을 그대로 미국의 손안에 언제나 두고 십지는 안은 것이다. 그래서 영안이란 것이 생기엿다. 그럴사록 독일의 힘은 증대하고 잇는 것이다.
이제 후-버- 안으로 인하야 관게 렬국의 리해관게는 엇떠한가?
영국은 독일에게 밧는 그 만큼 전채로 지불하는 것이 잇스니 아무런 문제가 업고 불란서는 독일에서 밧는 액수의 절반을 전채로서 외국에 지불하고 남어지의 절반은 자긔 주머니에 남든 것이니 그에는 리해관게가 잇는 것이다. 미국은 그리하야 독일의 경제 상태가 증진하고 잇슬사록 미국의 자본이 크고 잇는 것이니 그야 물론 리익이 막대한 것이다. 그러니 골는 나라는 불란서이다.
