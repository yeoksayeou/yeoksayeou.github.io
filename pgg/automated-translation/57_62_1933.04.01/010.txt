별건곤 第62號
1933년 04월 01일
龍岡 民謠, 님노래 (Yonggang Folk Song, Songs of Love)
洪鍾仁
시 (Poetry)

Are you really leaving me?
You're like the spirit of a rough-legged buzzard.
Even if you leave, can you really leave?
Even if you really leave, can I forget you?

When burning ten thousand volumes of poetry,
Why was the character for "separation" (離別離字) left behind?
Having burned the character for "separation,"
Let's write the character for "meeting" (맛날逢字).

2
Why is love like this?
I can't forget you before I sleep.
Even when I'm eating,
Every sip reminds me of you.
Even when I'm sleeping,
I think of you even in my dreams.
Life is so uninteresting,
Let's grow some bottle gourds.

3
Through the four seasons, spring, summer, autumn, and winter,
I only think of you.
Fetching sweet water,
I place it before the poison.
Turning around, I sigh.

4
When I sit, do you come?
When I lie down, do you come?
In the whispers of the back room, in the sound of the crickets (蟋蟀聲에),
They melt a person's heart.

5
The lark soaring in the bright blue sky
Is as restless as my heart.
The bonfire in the yard
Burns only my insides, like my heart.
