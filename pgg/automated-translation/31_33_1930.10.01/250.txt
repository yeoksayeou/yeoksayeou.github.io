Daegu March

By Sanghwa

Daegu March

In front, Mt. Piseul, behind, Mt. Palgong,
And flowing through the middle, the Geumho River waters, ah!
How much bitter tears and long sighs have been shed,
That night and day, night and day, it weeps like this?

Half-ruined on the ruins of Dalguseong fortress,
Or in the lush shade of Dosuwon park,
Even if many people come and go,
How many are as withered as the old trees of the embankment?

However good the wide Daegu Gamyeong (Daegu Governor's Office) may be,
For us, deprived of laughter and hope,
As lonely beings who cannot even have their beloved,
Even if we wander all over the front and back fields, our hearts are heavy.

If there is a beautiful one like a star on an autumn night,
Pour her good and lovely wine,
Let's not sleep on this stifling night,
But get drunk until the moon sets and the sun rises.<66>

大邱行進曲
						尙火
大邱行進曲

압으로는 琵瑟山 뒤로는 八公山
그 복판을 흘너가는 琴湖江 물아
쓴 눈물 긴 한숨이 얼마나 쌧기에
밤에는 밤 낫에는 낫 이리도 우나

반남아 문허진 達句城 넷터에나
숩그늘 욱어진 刀水園 노리터에
오고 가는 사람이 만키야 하여도
防川뚝 고목처럼 여윈 이 얼마랴

넓다는 大邱 감영 아모리 됴래도
웃음도 소망도 빼앗긴 우리로야
님조차 못가진 외로운 몸으로야
압뒷들 다 헤매도 가슴이 답답타

가을 밤 별가티 어엽분 이 잇거든
착하고 귀여운 술이나 부어다고
숨갑븐 이 한밤은 잠자도 말고서
달지고 해 돗도록 취해나 볼테다<66>
