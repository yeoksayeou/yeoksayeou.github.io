기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Apology/Notice, Editor's Note)

The political situation in China changes like the autumn weather. In this issue, we were going to publish an article titled "The Establishment of the Northern Government of China" (中國北方政府의 成立) by Mr. Cho Yo-han (朱요한), regarding the establishment of the Northern Government in China. However, while proofreading, we received an external report that the Northern Government had collapsed again. Therefore, the author, Mr. Cho Yo-han, requested that the entire article be removed. However, we do not know how the Chinese political situation will change again in the future, and the article, although late, is very helpful in understanding a process of the Chinese civil war, so we are publishing it as it is and apologize to the readers and the author.

We regret to inform the writers that the articles on the issue of the massacre and expulsion of Koreans in Manchuria (在滿同胞 虐殺驅逐事件), a major issue currently, and the opinions and countermeasures of various prominent figures, could not be published due to circumstances. We apologize for the inconvenience to the writers. - Editor <55>

謝告
中國의 政局은 가을 날가티 變化가 無雙함니다. 今番에도 中國의 北方政府가 成立된 데 대하야 朱요한氏의 玉稿 「中國北方政府의 成立」이라는 記事를 本號에 揭載하게 되엿는데 校正을 보는 中에 다시 北方政府가 瓦解되엿다는 外報가 왓슴니다. 그리하야 筆者 朱요한氏는 그 記事全部를 빼여 달나고 要求하엿스나 그러나 압흐로 中國政勢가 다시 엇덧케 뒤박뀌려는지 모를 뿐 안이라 該記事가 비록 때는 느젓슬지언정 中國內亂의 一過程을 보는데 만이라도 매우 參考가 되겟기에 그대로 실으면서 讀者와 밋 筆者에 謝하나이다.

現下 大問題되는 中國人의 在滿同胞 虐殺驅逐事件에 관하야 各方面 名士 諸氏의 高見과 對策에 관한 記事는 事情에 의하야 揭載치 못하게 되엿삽기 筆者諸氏에게 未安을 謝함니다.-編輯者<55>
