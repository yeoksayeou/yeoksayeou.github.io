Editorial Notes

October 1st - It's the 10th day of the 8th month in the lunar calendar. Only five nights remain until the Mid-Autumn Festival (8月), the best holiday of the year. Farmers in the countryside are preparing to celebrate this holiday by making early rice cakes (올벼송편) and brewing new clear rice wine (新淸酒), but we are releasing this special autumn issue for you all.

This special autumn issue is relatively more substantial in content than before. "A Loafer in Haste (忙中閒人)" on the Japanese Privy Council, a dissection of Mr. Shin T'ae-wik, "The Driving Force of Joseon's Economic Change," Mr. Chu Yo-han, "Ten Days Under Heaven of China's Northern Politics," Mr. Kim Se-yong, "The Red Movement of Chinese Farmers," Mr. Yang Yun-sik's "Explanation of the Theft Prevention Law," etc., are all commentaries worth reading at least once. In particular, Mr. Yang's "Explanation of the Theft Prevention Law" is a must-read to understand the content of this strange law, which has only been in effect for a few days. (Cha)

The "Exhibition of Bride Candidates (新婦 候補者 展覽會)" by Mr. and Mrs. Double S, which has been serialized in this magazine every month, ended in the previous issue after receiving enthusiastic welcome from general readers. In this issue, "Exhibition of Groom Candidates (新郞 候補者 展覽會)" will be serialized monthly in its place. General readers, as well as single men and women looking for marriage, should read this not only for enjoyment but also as a reference. (Pak)

The "Local March (地方 行進曲)" articles, which started from the previous month's issue and are published every month, have been greatly welcomed, especially by those in the regions. Just like the "Pyeongyang March (平壤 行進曲)" in the previous issue, this "Daegu March (大邱 行進曲)" was written entirely by people from Daegu, so it discusses and reviews the past and present state of Daegu in detail without any errors or omissions. People from Daegu, as well as those who want to know the real situation of Daegu, should definitely read this. (Kim)

We have decided to publish the "Gaeseong March (開城 行進曲)" in the next month's issue. We hope that people from Gaeseong will provide a lot of material for the article, directly or indirectly, and that readers who have article material related to Gaeseong will submit it. (Ch'ae) <176>

編輯落書
10月 1日- 陰曆으로 8月10日이다. 1年中 第一 조흔 名節인 8月가외도 겨우 닷새밤을 隔하얏다. 시골 農家에서는 올벼송편에 新淸酒를 비저서 이 名節마지를 하지만은 우리는 이 新秋特輯號를 내여 여러분에게 드리게 되엿다.

이번은 新秋特輯號이니만치 內容이 前보다 比較的 充實하다.『忙中閒人』의 日本 樞密院의 解剖 申泰翊 氏의 朝鮮 經濟 變動의 原動力 朱耀翰 氏의 中國 北方政 十日 天下記 金世鎔 氏에 中國農民의 赤色 運動 楊潤植 氏의 盜難防止法 解釋 等等은 누구나 한번 읽을 만한 評論인 중 特히 楊氏의 盜難防止 法解釋은 그 怪法이 實施된지 不過 몃칠인 오늘에 잇서 그 內容이 어떠한 것인지 반드시 읽어 알아야만 될 記事다.(車)

本誌에 每月 續載하든 雙S君의 新婦 候補者 展覽會는 一般 讀者에게 白熱的 歡迎을 밧다가 前號에 끗을 마치고 이번 號에는 그 대신으로 『新郞 候補者 展覽會』가 또 每月 續載하게 되엿다. 一般讀者는 勿論이어니와 求婚中에 잇는 處女 總角 等은 滋味보다도 參考로 꼭 읽어야 만 될것이다. (朴)

前月號부터서 始作하야 每月 揭載하게 된 地方 行進曲  記事는 特히 地方여러분에게 大歡迎을 밧게 되엿다. 前號 平壤 行進曲도 그랫지만은 이번 大邱 行進曲은 執筆者가 全部 大邱人士이니만치 大邱의 今昔實事情을 조곰도 틀님업고 숭김업시 자서히 들어서 討論 論評하얏다. 大邱의 여러분은 勿論이어니와 大邱 實況을 잘 알고저 하는 이 또한 이것을 꼭 읽어야 될 것이다. (金)

來月號에는 또 開城 行進曲을 揭載하기로 內定이 되엿다. 開城 人士는 直接 間接으로 記事의 만흔 材料를 供給하야 주기를 바라거니와 讀者中으로도 開城에 關한 記事 材料가 잇스면 投稿하야 주기를 바란다. (蔡)<176>
