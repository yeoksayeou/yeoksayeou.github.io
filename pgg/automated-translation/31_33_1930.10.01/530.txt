벗생각 (Thoughts of a Friend)
시 (Poem)

Thoughts of a Friend
李裕宿 (Yi Yu-suk)

Once when I fell into injustice,
My friend saw and smiled.
More than words, receiving that smile,
How frightened I was.

When my friend started walking down a difficult road,
I grabbed both wrists and tried to stop him,
But my friend didn't seem to listen.
Without hesitation, he went down the difficult road.

When my friend said he would live as a beggar,
He crossed the sea, laughing as he went,
But when news came that he was sick in bed,
I sought all kinds of medicine and offered it to him.

Today, my friend, having come with great ambition,
In a happy voice, calls and grabs me,
But being ill, I only worry.
Remembering the past, I shed tears.

How far is Cheolli (千里, a unit of distance)? When night comes,
This dream goes to my friend's house.
The song of peace of the three family members,
Only after hearing it, do I return.

How could I ever forget my friend,
But being busy, I cannot see him often.
I wait, anticipating his arrival.
Longing for him, I open the door (살압은 삽작문) and go to meet him.
-30. 1-

벗생각
						李裕宿
언젠가 내가 불의에 빠젓슬 때
벗은 보시고 빙그래 웃더이다.
말드름보담 그 우슴밧고
얼마나 내가 겁내엿든지

벗이 엇길노 거르시기로
두손목 잡고 말여봣스나
벗은 안이 드른체 하시더이다.
서슴안코 엇길노 가시더이다.

벗이 배화야 사시겟다고
바다 건너서 웃고가더니
병드러 누웟다는 기별잇기로
왼갓약을 구하여 밧처드렷네

오늘날 벗이 큰 뜻을 품고 와서
깁분소리로 나를 작고 부르되
알는 몸이라 애만탐니다.
지난 생각에 눈물짐니다.

철리 머다하리 밤만 오면은
이 꿈은 벗님의 집 가고 맘니다.
세식구 불은 평화의 노래
듯고 나서야 돌아듬니다.

벗인들 아주 이즈시랴만
일매여서 자조 못뵙네
하마 나오실까봐 기대림니다.
그리워 살압열고 마중감니다.
(살압은 삽작문)-30. 1-
