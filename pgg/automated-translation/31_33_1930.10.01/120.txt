A question and answer record (9)
Yi Kwang-su

Reporter: If a woman comes on to you, wanting only a small romance, how would you handle it?

Yi: I have no choice but to avoid it. Since I'm not able to have romance anyway, meeting would be boring - But if an absolutely beautiful woman (絶世美人) came chasing after me like that, I might think about not avoiding her -
