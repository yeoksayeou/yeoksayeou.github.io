기사형태	잡저 (Miscellany)

The Chosŏn Chi Kwangsa (朝鮮之光社) and Yŏsŏng Sidae Sa (女性時代社) are located close to each other, but both offices do not have restrooms. Therefore, the two companies jointly use the public restroom next to the Jae-dong (齋洞) police box. One day, Pak Yŏng-hŭi (朴英熙) of Chogwang (朝光) and Yun Paek-nam (尹白南) of Yŏsŏng Sa (女性社) went to the restroom at the same time. The errand boy of Yŏsŏng Sa (女性社), having some urgent matter, came all the way to the restroom and called out, "Sir?" Yun (尹) answered "Yes" from one stall, and Pak (朴) answered "Yes" from the other stall (thinking that someone from their company was calling them), so the sounds of "Yes" kept echoing. A passerby said, "Is that public restroom some kind of school office? Why are there so many teachers?" Ha ha... ...<158>
