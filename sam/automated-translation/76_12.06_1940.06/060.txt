Thoroughness of Wartime Life
Various Outlines Expected in the New Year

The Spirit Movement Korean League (精動朝鮮聯盟) recently held a board meeting at the Communications Business Center. Under the attendance of Director-General Kawashima (川島), Chairman Shiobara (鹽原), and other directors, all stood and solemnly performed Worship towards the Imperial Palace (宮城 遙拜) and offered a silent prayer (黙禱) of gratitude to the Imperial Army (皇軍). Afterwards, Director-General Kawashima gave opening remarks, and under the chairmanship of Chairman Shiobara, they entered into the agenda (議事). A report was given on the activities of the 1939 Shōwa year (昭和 14년도), which had already begun in earnest. Then, based on the already decided movement policy for the 1940 year (15년도), the new fiscal year activity plan proposal (attached separately), which had undergone careful review and planning by the administrative authorities, was presented. Following inquiries from each director and earnest expression of opinions, the main points are as follows:

1. Exaltation and Elucidation of the Great Spirit of the Nation's Founding (肇國 大精神)

(a) Commemorative publication of the 『Mirror of the National Polity』 (國體 明鑑) and 『League Orthodox Canon』 (聯盟 正典) (Tentative Title)
(b) Publication of a Korean translation (or Chinese translation - 漢譯) of 『The Essence of the National Polity』 (國體의 本義)
(c) Holding of commemorative festivals
(d) Holding of commemorative lecture tours
(e) Continuous dissemination and propaganda regarding the purpose and progress of the construction of the New East Asian Order

2. Promotion of the Completion of the Unity of Japan and Korea (內鮮一體)

(a) Labor service for the nation (勤勞 報國) work for the honorable construction (御造營) of the Puyŏ Shrine (扶餘神宮)
(b) Measures towards the Mainland (對內地 施設)
① Dispatch of lecturers
② Holding of round-table discussions and film screenings
③ Propaganda for the increase and settlement of immigrants to Korea
④ Publication and distribution of pamphlets
⑤ Nationwide radio broadcasts
(c) Measures within Korea (對鮮內 施設)
① Diffusion of the national language (국어 - Japanese)
② Holding of training sessions
③ Constant propaganda for the volunteer soldier system (지원병 제도)
④ Commendation of successful volunteer soldier applicants
⑤ Thorough dissemination of the name-changing system (창씨 제도)
⑥ Encouragement of and provision of convenience for marriage with mainland Japanese (內地 結婚)
⑦ Propaganda and enlightenment for emigration to Manchuria (滿洲), North China (北支), and the Mainland (內地)
⑧ Fusion of Japanese and Korean customs (內鮮 풍속)
⑨ Fusion of Japanese and Korean habits (內鮮 습관)
⑩ Guidance towards Japanese tastes (日本 취미)
(d) External measures (대외 시설)
① Propaganda regarding Korean affairs throughout Manchukuo (滿洲國)
② Propaganda regarding Korean affairs to the peninsula people in North, South, and Central China (北南中支)
③ Propaganda to the people of ① and ② regarding the spirit of Hakko Ichiu (八紘 一宇 - Eight Corners of the World Under One Roof) and the purpose of constructing the New East Asia

3. Thoroughness of Wartime Life

(Through printed materials, lectures, broadcasts, round-table discussions, slogans, etc.) <54>
(a) Thorough strengthening of awareness of the current situation
(b) Strengthening the dissemination of national defense ideology
(c) Comforting the Imperial Army (皇軍)
(d) Dissemination of the Spirit Movement (精動) spirit to vibrant industrial organizations
(e) Establishment of Life Improvement Committees
(f) Cherishing and conserving materials
(g) Increasing production and overcoming the effects of drought damage
(h) Encouragement of savings
(i) Mandatory execution of one commemorative project per person
(j) Encouragement of labor service for the nation work

4. Promotion of the Implementation of All National Facilities

To be implemented according to the situation at the time.

5. Mobilization of Cities

(a) Promote further strengthening regarding the Kyŏngsŏng Prefecture League (京城府 연맹).
(b) Strengthening promotion regarding other city leagues
① Hold provincial city league promotion coordination meetings
② Strengthen the activities of various city leagues

6. Advancement of Movement Efficiency

(a) Strengthening the structure of the Korean League
(b) Strengthening local league structures in accordance with the above
(c) Thorough strengthening of Patriotic Neighborhood Associations (애국반)
① Reorganization of Patriotic Neighborhood Associations
② Training of Patriotic Neighborhood Association leaders
③ Commendation of outstanding Patriotic Neighborhood Associations
④ Publication of 『Patriotic Neighborhood Association Leadership Essentials』 (애국반 지도 必携)
(d) Reorganization of school leagues
(e) Guidance of Patriotic Neighborhood Associations by school teachers and local leagues
(f) Establishment of women's groups for movement promotion
(g) Establishment of Spirit Movement (精動) dissemination departments in youth groups
(h) Coordination meetings for persons in charge of provincial league affairs and training sessions for local league personnel
(i) Provincial leader coordination meetings
(j) Conference of league chairmen from Prefecture, County, and Island levels and above
(k) Training sessions for promotion agents
(l) Guidance for labor service corps dispatched to Manchukuo (滿洲國)
(m) Establishment of Spirit Movement (精動) investigation committees in Provincial Leagues
(n) Inspection and guidance of local leagues and Patriotic Neighborhood Associations

7. Guidance towards National General Training

(a) Concentrate the goals of various events on this point
(b) Dissemination and propaganda of the slogan 『National General Training』 (국민총훈련)
(c) Thorough dissemination of morning exercises and Patriotism Day
(d) Encouragement of simple physical exercises at Patriotism Day gatherings
(e) Utilization of the radio's 『Spirit Movement Hour』 (精動의 시간)
(f) Production and circuit screening of films
(g) Dissemination of the league anthem
(h) Group training for the league and Patriotic Neighborhood Associations

8. Other

(a) Holding of grand ceremonies commemorating the Incident [likely referring to the Second Sino-Japanese War] and the league's formation
(b) Holding of commemorative ceremonies in each Prefecture, Town, Village, or Neighborhood/Hamlet league
(c) Cooperation with general annual events
(d) Publication of pamphlets introducing the Spirit Movement (精動)
(e) Publication of various lecture and training session records
(f) Improvement and enhancement of 『Total Mobilization』 (총동원) and 『Dawn』 (曉) <55>

**Summary**

This article outlines the extensive and detailed plans of the Spirit Movement Korean League (精動朝鮮聯盟) for the year 1940, as decided in a recent board meeting. The plans focus heavily on promoting Japanese imperial ideology ("Spirit of the Nation's Founding," "Hakko Ichiu"), accelerating Korean assimilation into Japanese culture ("Naissen Ittai," language diffusion, name changing, intermarriage), mobilizing the populace for the wartime effort (labor service, savings, defense ideology), and strengthening the league's organizational structure down to the neighborhood level (Patriotic Neighborhood Associations). For historians, this document provides a clear insight into the specific mechanisms and propaganda strategies employed by collaborationist organizations under the Japanese colonial government to enforce assimilation policies, control society, and support the Japanese war effort during a critical period. The sheer breadth and depth of the planned activities reveal the totalizing nature of Japanese wartime mobilization in colonial Korea.
