Editor's Postscript

Today's extra edition reports that the German army has advanced into Poland (白蘭). As the war in the East and West progresses further and enters a full-scale stage, we must once again gain a new awareness and awakening. We must not neglect the duties and responsibilities of the people on the home front (銃後), and especially, we believe we must defend the home front by striving for temperance and saving.

We hope you will freshly read the long-unseen essay by Mr. Kim Myŏngsik, "History of the Rise and Fall of Poland (波蘭興亡史)," and the essay by Mr. In Chŏngsik, "Korean Peasants and Literary Expression (朝鮮農民과 文學的 表現)," which are difficult-to-obtain writings. Also, the article covering the roundtable discussion held with the visiting Korean Women's Observation Group from Shanghai (在上海 朝鮮婦人視察團), who have returned to their homeland for the first time in decades or since birth, is believed to be another unique feature of this month's issue.

Notably, this month's literary section features a special on New Chinese Literature (支那 新文學 特輯), translating and publishing poetry and novels by leading, established, and emerging male and female writers of the current Chinese literary scene. Critical biographies (評傳) of active writers Chou Tso-jen (周作人) and Kuo Mo-jo (郭洙若), as well as going back to ancient times with critical biographies of Li T'aebaek (李太白), Tu Fu (杜于美 - likely 杜甫), and Po Lo-t'ien (白樂天), are also among the prides of this month's issue. Furthermore, reminiscences by various renowned figures in related fields about their time studying abroad at prominent Chinese universities are also articles worth reading.

Publishing Department Correspondence

The previously announced "Collection of Patriotic Speeches (愛國演說集)" was published (上榟) in elegant binding and sales began on May 18th; it has gained immense popularity right from the start of sales. Continuing, "Peninsula Rivers and Mountains (半島山河)," written by 16 contemporary literary masters (當代文豪), and "Selected Famous Chinese Poems (支那名詩選)," which are currently being printed, will begin sales shortly. Decorate your bookshelf in early summer with these famous works. Going forward, we will continue to publish works by leading and established writers of the literary world, such as the "English Translated Collection of Korean Poems (英譯朝鮮詩集)," "Collection of Doctoral Dissertations (博士論文集)," "Collection of Representative Korean Children's Stories (朝鮮代表童話集)," "Ten Business Leaders of Korea (朝鮮財界十傑)," "Ten Great Educators of Korea (朝鮮十大敎育家)," and others, striving to present conscientious books before you that will not disappoint your expectations.<300>

**Summary**

This editor's postscript from June 1940 highlights the magazine's content amid the escalating war (WWII). It urges readers towards awareness, duty, temperance, and saving for the "home front." Featured articles include essays on Poland's history, Korean peasants in literature, and a roundtable with Korean women visiting from Shanghai. A special section focuses on contemporary and classical Chinese literature and writers (Chou Tso-jen, Kuo Mo-jo, Li Bai, Du Fu, Bai Juyi). The publishing department announces recent ("Patriotic Speeches") and upcoming books ("Peninsula Rivers and Mountains," "Selected Famous Chinese Poems," English translations of Korean poetry, etc.), emphasizing their commitment to quality publications. For historians, the text clearly reflects the wartime mobilization rhetoric ("home front duties," "temperance," "saving") imposed on colonial Korea. The selection of topics, including the focus on China and the promotion of "patriotic" works, alongside standard literary fare, illustrates the complex cultural and political landscape under Japanese rule during the war period.

編輯後記
오늘 號外에는 獨逸軍이 白蘭에 進擊하였다고 합니다. 東西에서 戰爭은 일층 더 진전되여 本格的 舞臺에 오르고 있는 이때에 우리는 다시금 새로운 認識과 覺醒을 가져야 하겠나이다. 銃後의 국민의 義務와 책임을 缺하지 말아야 할 것이오며, 특히 節制와 貯蓄에 노력하여 써 銃後를 지켜야 할 줄 아나이다.

오래간만에 붓을 드신 金明植씨의 論文 「波蘭興亡史」와 印貞植氏의 論文 「朝鮮農民과 文學的 表現」은 얻기 어려운 글로, 여러분의 新讀을 바라오며, 이번 몇 十年만에 혹은 生後 처음으로 故土를 찾아 오신 在上海 朝鮮婦人視察團一行을 모시고 座談會를 연 記事도 이 달의 한 異彩인 줄 아나이다.

특히 今月號의 文藝欄을 支那 新文學 特輯으로 하여, 支那 現文壇의 大家, 中堅, 新進 男女作家의 詩와 小說을 번역하여서 揭載하였사오며, 現役 作家 周作人, 郭洙若의 評傳과, 다시 古代로 거슬러 올라가 李太白 杜于美, 白樂天의 評傳도 今月號 本誌의 자랑 중 하나인 줄 아나이다. 그와 및 斯界 名士 諸氏의 支那 有數大學 留學 當時의 回想記도 一讀할 記事일 것이외다.

出版部 通信

이미 豫告하였던 「愛國演說集」은 單雅한 裝幀으로 上榟되여 5월 18일부터 發賣를 개시하였는데 發賣 壁頭부터 절대한 인기를 博하고 있으며,  繼續하여 방금 印刷 中에 있는 當代文豪 16氏의 執筆인 「半島山河」와 「支那名詩選」은 不遠에 발매를 開始할터 이옵니다. 初夏의, 貴下의 書架를이 名著로서 裝飾하실지며 앞으로 계속하여 『英譯朝鮮詩集』, 『博士論文集』, 『朝鮮代表童話集』 『朝鮮財界十傑』 『朝鮮十大敎育家』, 其外에도 文壇의 大家 及 中堅作家의 작품을 계속 出刊하여써 여러분의 기대에 어그러지지 않는 良心的인 書籍을 여러분 앞에 내여 놓고저 노력하나이다.<300>
