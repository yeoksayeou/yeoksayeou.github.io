**Body Text Translation:**
Urgent Notice to Our Esteemed Readers
						Samch'ŏlli Company Sales Director

As you can see, the magazine "Samch'ŏlli" continues to be published steadily every month, and we assure you that going forward, it will be published every month without missing even a single issue, so please rest assured and continue reading it diligently.

However, as you may already surmise, due to recent controls on printing paper, there are cases where it is difficult to publish copies beyond the planned number. Therefore, we kindly request that you send payment in advance for either a half-year or a full year's subscription fee (誌代). If you do so, we will undoubtedly continue sending you the issues (送冊).

If you see the advertisement for that month's issue and order it then, there is a concern that the magazine may be sold out (品切). This past May issue also sold out (賣絶) within a week of publication, and we were completely unable to send copies for orders placed after that, instead sending the next month's issue as a substitute (代送).

We anticipate that such situations will occur frequently in the future. We request advance orders with payment. As we plan and print specifically for the advance-paid orders, there will absolutely be no instances where we cannot send them.<46>

**Summary**

The Sales Director of Samch'ŏlli Company informs readers that while the magazine will continue monthly publication without fail, recent government controls on printing paper make it difficult to print extra copies beyond the planned amount. To avoid missing issues, as happened with the May issue which sold out quickly, readers are strongly urged to pay for subscriptions (half-year or full year) in advance. This ensures their copies are included in the print run and guaranteed for delivery. This notice highlights the practical difficulties faced by publishers under resource rationing during the late colonial period. For historians, it illustrates the direct impact of wartime economic controls on cultural activities like magazine publishing in Korea and the strategies (like appealing for advance subscriptions) employed by businesses to cope.

讀者 諸賢에 急告
						三千里社 營業局長
보시는 바와 가치 「三千里」 잡지는 매월 꾸준히 계속하여 발행되오며, 금후로도 한 달이라도 缺하는 일이 없이 매월 발행하겠사오니 안심하시고 강독하여 주심을 바라나이다. 그런데 이미 짐작하시겠지만 근래에 인쇄 용지의 통제 때문에, 예정 이외의 부수는 발간하기 곤란한 경우가 있사오니, 원컨대 미리 반년분이고, 1년분이고 誌代를 先送하여 주시면, 틀님없이 계속 送冊하겠나이다.
만일, 그 달에 나온 책 광고를 보고, 그 때에 주문하신다면 잡지가 품절될 염려가 있나이다. 이번 5월호도 발행 후 1주일만에 賣絶되여 그 이후의 주문分은 전혀 보내드리지 못하고, 그 다음달 호로 代送하였나이다. 如此한 경우가 금후는 많이 生할 줄 아나이다. 미리 선금 주문을 바라나이다. 선금 주문 分만은 미리 예정하여서 인쇄하는 고로 보내드리지 못하는 일이 절대로 없겠나이다.<46>
