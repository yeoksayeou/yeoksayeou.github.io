Financial World, Gold Mining Industry Insider Report
1.2 Million Won Came Out
For Gold Mining Funds to the Korean Side

Who among the Koreans received the low-interest funds for gold mining promotion? Attention from both the capital and the provinces is focused on this.
According to this magazine's investigation, the gold mining funds are being loaned out through the Chosen Industrial Bank (殖銀) and the Oriental Development Company (東拓) (Tōtaku's share is 5 million won), and the total amount has already reached 10.4 million won across about 40 applications. The interest rate is 6% per annum, meaning even if one borrows one million won, the annual interest is only about 60,000 won, which is quite cheap. Among this 10.4 million won, large companies like Mitsubishi Mining (三菱鑛業), Kobayashi Mining (小林鑛業), and Nippon Mining (日本鑛業), which operate with capital in the tens or hundreds of millions of won, are included, but on the other hand, many mining operators working with several hundred thousand won are also included. The number of applications and the amounts requested are said to be much higher, so more loans are expected in the future. The places where Koreans have already used gold mining funds are still few, and among them, the following three are considered large:

500,000 Won to Taedong Mining Co., Ltd. (大同鑛業株式會社)
Taedong Mining is the company managed by Mr. Yi Chong-man <13>, whose Hamnam Changjin mine (咸南 長津鑛山) is already widely known as one of the leading gold mines in the Orient, second to few. Possessing such a superior mine, it's not that the Government-General and the Chosen Industrial Bank would refuse if he requested more funds, but it is said that 500,000 won was deemed sufficient for now, hence it stopped there.

Pak Yong-un (朴龍雲) 450,000 Won
He owns many mines in the Unsan (雲山) area of P'yŏngbuk (平北) and gained fame years ago for selling a mine for 1.3 million won and contributing to mining donations along with Ch'oe Ch'ang-hak (崔昌學), Pang Ŭng-mo (方應謨), and Wŏn Yun-su (元胤洙). This time, he borrowed 450,000 won at low interest.

Mr. Kim Chun-sang (金浚相) also borrowed 240,000 Won
Mr. Kim Chun-sang, famous as the owner of the Samhwa Gold Mine (三和金鑛) located on the border of Anju (安州) and Sunch'ŏn (順川) counties in P'yŏngnam (平南) <14>, borrowed 240,000 won this time. In Shōwa 11 (1936), even without particularly sufficient facilities, the Samhwa mine, which produced 170,000 won annually, was rumored to be worth 2 million won.

Total Korean Side: 1.21 Million Won
It is still unknown whether Messrs. Ch'oe Ch'ang-hak, Wŏn Yun-su, Pang Ŭng-mo, Pak Ki-hyo (朴基孝), Yŏm Kyŏng-hun (廉璟勳), who also own mines, are attempting to borrow funds. It is only certain that the banks have not yet offered these low-interest funds to these individuals.
These low-interest funds are expected to come out in even larger amounts once the organization of the 50 million won gold mining development company is completed. It is said that out of the 300 million won expected to be released over the next approximately five years until Shōwa 17 (1942), 190 million won will be allocated to Korea alone. This means an average of about 40 million won will be distributed annually, truly a surprisingly large amount of money flowing into the gold mining sector.

Inner Workings of General Society
Drastic Changes in Seoul's Organizations
The constant transformation of organizations and figures is a characteristic of Seoul society these days, as everything moves along the lines of national policy under the [China] Incident. Looking at the recent changes in Seoul society, one can perceive that changes were particularly numerous within Christian society.

Chōsen Central Christian Young Men's Association (朝鮮中央基督敎靑年會)
Recently, there were frequent changes among its key executives, and it merged with the Japanese Christian church located in Hasegawa-chō (長谷川町). The Chongno Youth Hall (鐘路青年會館), with its building and land valued at 700,000-800,000 won, up to 1 million won, will also see its assets transferred following the merger.

Chōsen Sunday School Union (朝鮮主日學校聯合會)
The office of the Sunday School Union, located in Chongno 2-chōme (鐘路 2丁目), has ceased operations. As schools are generally ceasing management <16>, Sunday schools also face the same fate.

Chōsen Diligence Young Men's Association (朝鮮勉勵靑年會)
This organization also disbanded in accordance with the times. Until now, it was famous for its large number of members, its founding year, and the number of affiliated groups.

Chōsen Epworth Young Men's Association (朝鮮엡웻靑年會) [Likely Epworth League]
This organization disbanded last month in accordance with the times. This association was also famous for its large number of members and affiliated groups.

Christian Report Company (基督申報社)
Though it happened some time ago, this newspaper, which served as a reporting and press organ for Christian society, temporarily ceased publication due to various circumstances.
Fundamentally discovered from these various points is that whereas previously, Christian society in Korea sent representatives to international Christian conferences abroad, such as the World Christian Conference in the holy land of Judea, "Jerusalem," where they participated, spoke, and made proposals, from now on, direct dispatch will cease. Instead, they will affiliate with Tokyo and attend and speak only through Tokyo. That is, all routes connecting "Seoul and Rome" or "Seoul and Jerusalem" are cut off, and henceforth, only the direct route of "Seoul and Tokyo" will remain.

Mr. Pak Hŭng-sik's (朴興植) Social Project Rumor - Said to Begin in the Near Future -
Mr. Pak Hŭng-sik, president of Hwasin (和信), marks his 20th year in the business world this year and is concurrently planning the establishment of a 10 million won company for the construction of the greater Hwasin. Taking this opportunity, Mr. Pak, who previously had no experience in education or other charitable works, has decided to invest a large sum into society at once, establish a foundation, and strive for the welfare and advancement of societal figures. The amount, type of project, and timing of the investment are still "heavenly secrets not to be divulged" (天機不可洩) and will be announced later, but according to one source, it is said to be a considerable sum, likely exceeding several hundred thousand won. <17>

Singer Sewing Machines Annually 5 Million Won - Rumor of 2-3 Million Won Profit from Korea Alone
Regarding the Singer Sewing Machine Company, an American monopoly business that has operated in Korea for several decades, there are rumors that due to changing times, the Foreign Exchange Control Law, and other circumstances, doing business in Korea is becoming increasingly difficult, and they might withdraw their business and return to the US. Whether this will happen is unclear, but according to an investigation by a certain source, the annual sales of Singer sewing machines in Korea alone reach 5 million won. It is said that about half of this, 2.5 or 2.6 million won, was sent back to the US annually. While it's unclear if the net profit was truly that high, it is evident that they have reaped significant exclusive profits over many decades.

How Many Anglo-American Missionaries Have Returned Home?
The current number of missionaries in Korea is about 300, of whom about 90% are American, with a very small number being British. About one-third of the 300, around 100, are in Seoul, while the rest are distributed in places like P'yŏngyang (平壤), Sŏnch'ŏn (宣川), Taegu (大邱), Hamhŭng (咸興), etc. Recently, due to various complex issues <19> in Christian society, rumors have spread that many Anglo-American missionaries are returning to America one after another. However, according to our investigation, apart from about 12 or 13 missionaries who went home for their sabbatical year (occurring once every six years) to rest for this year, no one has yet returned home due to the recent problems.

Taiwan Volunteer Soldier Issue - Remarks by Taiwan Governor-General's Chief of General Affairs Morioka (森岡)

In Taiwan, the amount of national defense contributions collected from the islanders during this incident reached 4 million won. When the volunteer soldier system was implemented in Korea, voices demanding this system also grew louder among the islanders. Originally, Taiwanese served in the military for three years starting from Meiji 33 (1900), and this issue also arose 7 or 8 years ago, but due to various circumstances, it could not be implemented. In any case, it is true that there is fervent hope among the islanders for its implementation.

Korean Language Exclusion Impermissible - Governor-General Minami Rebukes Misguided Individuals
Governor-General Minami's (南) 11th meeting to hear the people's opinions took place on the 8th at 3 PM at the Waesŏngdae (倭城臺) official residence, where he met with 7 visitors. The main topics for this meeting were strengthening Japan-Korea unity (內鮮一體) under the emergency situation and thoroughly implementing austerity living to make the spirit of diligence the foundation of life. The opinions of each individual were as follows:
First, after Mr. Koizumi Shōhei (小泉昇平) presented his views on forestry in the emergency, Mr. Hyŏn Yŏng-sŏp (玄永燮) (Green Flag League - 綠旗聯盟) stated:
"Unifying the world has historical precedent, but it has never been realized. When thinking of such a global ideal, the issue of Japan-Korea unity is extremely small. However, for Koreans to become complete Japanese, it must start from unconscious fusion, which is already complete Japan-Korea unification (內鮮一元化). Therefore, it must be achieved through Shinto (神道), which has not been experienced before, or through the complete abolition of the use of the Korean language."
Thus arguing for the abolition of the Korean language, Governor-General Minami responded:
"Excluding the Korean language is impermissible. It is permissible to disseminate the national language [Japanese] as much as possible, but this national language dissemination movement is often misunderstood as a movement to abolish the Korean language, which is unacceptable."
He thus rejected it outright. <22>

Artists whose European/American tours were cancelled entirely due to the Sino-Japanese Incident include Mr. Itō Michio (伊藤道郞), and also dancer Nimura Kyōichi (仁村京一). This hand of persecution has now reached Ch'oe Sŭng-hŭi.
Ms. Ch'oe, unable to do otherwise, is silently practicing intensely every day in a studio. It is said that under a contract with N.B.C. this autumn, she will perform twice in the Americas before crossing over to Europe.

Two Accomplishments of General Ugaki (宇垣)
A certain gentleman remarked that among the various administrative achievements of the departed former Governor-General Ugaki, there were two particularly good things. He prefaced by saying:

Statement
We, the undersigned, were once members of the Hŭngsadan=Suyang Tonguhoe. In light of the current changes in internal and external affairs, we have realized the fundamental flaws and errors in the principles and assertions we previously held. Simultaneously, we articulate the views and assertions we have newly grasped, informing our old acquaintances and offering them for the consideration of gentlemen throughout the land.
Considering the rapid changes in the current internal and external situation and the epoch-making progress and development of Japan, these circumstances undeniably demand that we, the Korean populace, carve out a new destiny based on entirely new insights and awareness regarding future development.
We were previously mired in the concept of "so-called nationalism," firmly believing that the development and advancement of the Korean populace lay solely in "national self-determination." All our past ideological and practical efforts were dedicated entirely under this creed. This was because we Koreans misinterpreted Japanese rule in Korea as "colonial policy" and failed to recognize the true intentions of the Empire and the new East Asian historical mission of both the Japanese and Korean peoples.
However, the various leap-forward phenomena appearing in the administration of Korea in recent years, especially the call for Japan-Korea unity (內鮮一體) emphasized by the recent Governor-General Minami, have suddenly awakened us from our delusion. Particularly in the context of the current Incident, the fraternal trust placed in the peninsular populace by the mainland Japanese and the reformist momentum brewing under the great national ideal of the new Japan are more than sufficient to dispel all the doubts and anxieties we held until today.
Observing the trends of the current world situation, numerous small ethnic groups are in an inevitable process of being integrated under the umbrella of large nations. The lives of imperial subjects can only expect development and advancement through societal rather than individual means, and through the harmonious cooperation of all peoples rather than ethnic isolation. The so-called "independence" of a single nation already signifies, contrarily, reactionary isolation. Moreover, witnessing the glorious state of Japan, striving to fulfill its historical mission of building a new East Asia under the ideal of globally propagating the Japanese spirit as the leader of Asian peoples, guaranteeing happiness and freedom to the relevant nations – and observing the epochal phenomena aiming to sublimate the so-called "colonial Korea" and make Korea a powerful component in the formation of the new Japan – it is clear that the so-called national self-determination ideology is now meaningless for the Korean populace and a reactionary concept ignoring the new direction of East Asian historical development.
Hereby, envisioning the future form of Imperial Japan based on the Way of the Emperor (皇道), we have managed to cleanly liquidate our previously mistaken views on nation, state, and the world. We have finally become convinced that the eternal happiness of the Korean populace lies in forging the Japanese and Korean peoples into one solid entity (打 一丸), forming the great nation of Japanese people, and constructing a new East Asia with this as the core subject.
Now, the interests and fortunes (利害休戚) of the Korean populace completely coincide with those of Japan as a whole. The future of prosperous Japan will definitely not exclude the Korean populace. Looking back, the Japanese and Korean peoples share the same roots and ancestors (同根同祖); they were one in origin. The various conditions that differentiate them today need not inherently be clung to. Henceforth, following the development and advancement of national life and the deepening and thorough permeation of fraternal feeling, these differences will gradually disappear, and finally, complete unification into one nation is by no means impossible. The history of human societal development, progressing from division to unity, inevitably leads to this conclusion. For the two peoples sharing the origins of blood and culture, standing before the great mission of creating a new East Asian history, to completely unify is truly the will of God (神) assigned to us.
However, because the ideal of national unification cannot be completed hastily, it is often thought to be impossible. Conversely, national self-determinationism, pressed by long inertia, seems easily realizable. Our fascination with national self-determinationism until today is ultimately due to this, and the reason many people cling to the concept of nation is also due to this. However, the reality is entirely the opposite: the trend towards Japan-Korea unity and national unification has recently made giant strides, and the general situation, regardless of our preferences, increasingly demands its realization. Its reality is inevitable; it has already become a matter of time.
Based on the theoretical grounds above and the various trends of reality, we recognize complete Japan-Korea unification as the only path forward for the Korean populace. Under the national pride and aspiration of building a new Japan, fulfilling our role as one wing of that effort is the only thing that truly promises future glory and development for the Korean populace. This is indeed the fundamental spirit of the Taedong Minuhoe <27>, and it is the mission we must strive for with even greater effort from now on. That is, we are now filled with the most honest and bold spirit of abandoning everything to gain everything.
The current China Incident, as is already well known, is a holy war aimed at fulfilling Japan's great national mission, liberating the peoples of Asia from the shackles of White oppression. From now on, we must be prepared for any sacrifice, endure with perseverance, and become one united nation to achieve our objective.
The world is already weary of the calamities of Red communism and the ugliness of individualistic, utilitarian White civilization. The spirit of the East, Japanese-ism (日本主義), which aims for universal harmony (八紘一宇) through moral bonds, is truly the principle to save East Asia and guide the world's peoples. Therefore, we feel the honor and responsibility of being apostles of the glorious Japanese spirit. Consequently, we pledge to strive and exert ourselves henceforth under the beliefs and assertions stated above.

**Summary**

This 1938 article from "Samch'ŏlli" magazine provides a snapshot of Korean society under Japanese rule, covering diverse topics. It reports on government-backed, low-interest loans for gold mining, highlighting specific Korean recipients like Yi Chong-man and Pak Yong-un. It delves into social gossip, discussing rumors about large donations for an art school (possibly by Ch'oe Ch'ang-hak) and Pak Hŭng-sik's planned philanthropy. Significant attention is given to the restructuring and dissolution of Korean Christian organizations under pressure to align with Japanese policies and routes through Tokyo. The article includes updates on various public figures, notes Governor-General Minami's public stance against abolishing the Korean language despite calls for it, discusses Ch'oe Sŭng-hŭi's difficulties performing in the US due to anti-Japanese sentiment, and reports on the influx of Koreans into occupied North China. A major feature is the public declaration by 18 former members of the nationalist Suyangedonguhoe, renouncing their past beliefs and fully embracing Japan's "holy war" and the policy of Naisen Ittai (Japan-Korea Unity).

For historians, the article is valuable for several reasons. It illustrates the colonial state's economic policies (promoting gold mining for Japan's benefit while selectively funding Korean entrepreneurs), the social dynamics among the Korean elite (wealth, philanthropy rumors, connections), and the tangible effects of Japanese assimilation policies on cultural and religious institutions (Christian groups, language debate). The inclusion of the lengthy pro-Japanese statement by former nationalists provides direct evidence of collaboration and the ideological justifications used by some Koreans during this period of heightened wartime mobilization. The reports on Korean migration to North China also shed light on demographic shifts driven by the expanding Japanese empire.

그런데 본사가 조사한 바에 의하면 産金 자금은 식산은행과 東拓(東拓分 5배만원)의 손을 거처 대출되는데 벌서 그 총액이 1,040만원에 약 40口에 달하였다. 연 6분의 이자이니 백만원을 쓴대야 1년에 6만원 정도의 이자를 물면 되니까 이자는 싼 폭이다. 이 1,040만원 중에는 三菱광업이나 小林광업이나 일본광업 같은 몇 천만원 몇 백만원의 자금을 가지고 하는 대 회사도 끼워 있겠지만 그 반면에 또한 몇 십만원을 가지고 하는 광업가도 여렀이 끼엇는데 申込한 口數와 금액은 훨신 만타함으로 앞흐로 더 융자될 것이다. 조선 사람측으로 産金 자금을 이미 쓴 곳은 아직은 불과 몇 곳이 아니되며 그 중에 크다고 볼 것이 아래의 3口이다.

성명
不肖等이 일즉 흥사단=수양동우회의 일원이든바 현하 내외정세의 변전에 鑑하야 종래 抱懷하야 오든 주의주장에 근본적 缺陷과 誤謬가 있음을 悟하고 단연 차를 청산하고 금회 신국민적 자각 에 대동민우회에 입회함에 당하야 不肖 등의 거취와 동향을 명백히 하는 동시에 오인의 新히 파악한 견해와 주장을 披瀝하야 舊知諸兄에게 소하고 廣히 강호제현의 一考에 供하는 바이다.
惟컨대 현하 내외정세의 급격한 변전과 아 일본의 劃世적 약진발전의 제 계기는 오등 조선민중으로 하야곰 장래의 발전에 대하야 전혀 신견해와 자각 하에 신운명을 개척케 하기를 요구하야말지 아니하는 바가 있다.
오인은 종래 「소위 민족주의」의 관념에 抱泥되야 조선민중의 발전과 향상은 단지 「민족자결」에 있을 뿐이라고 固信하야 과거의 오인의 사상적 급 실천적 제 노력은 전혀 차 신조하에 捧하여왔다. 大蓋 오등 조선인은 조선통치를 「식민지정책」으로 곡해하야 제국의 진의와 내선 양족의 東亞史的 신사명을 인식치 못하였든 까닭이다.
然이나 최근 수년래의조선통치상에 現한 비약적 제 현상 특별히 최근 南총독에 의하야 강조되는 바 내선일체의 聲은 홀연이 오인의 迷妄을 각성케 하는 바가 있다. 특히 今回의 시국에 제하야 내지인이 반도민중에 寄하는바 동포적 신뢰와 신일본의 국가적 大理想 하에 醞釀되어가는 革新機軍은 금일까지 오인의 抱懷한 一切의 의구와 불안을 일소하고도 餘함이 있다.
현금 세계대세의 추이를 觀함에 다수의 약소민족군은 점차로 대국가의 산하에 통합되는 필연적 정세에 있다. 제국민의 생활은 개인보다도 사회적으로 민족적 고립보다도 제민족의 團圓合作에 의하야 비로소 그 발전향상을 期할 수 있으니 일민족의 소위 「독립」이라는 것은 이미 도리어 반동적 고립을 의미하는 것이다. 況 방금  아제민족의 지도자로서 일본정신의 세계적 선양을 이상으로 신동아건설의 역사적 사명을 達코저 분투하고 있는 新當該민족에게 행복과 자유를 보장하는 것이 아니 오도 일본의 광영있는 자태는 소위 「식민지조선」을 지양하고 조선을 신일본구성의 유력한 부분이 되게 하려는 획기적 제 현상에 鑑하야 종래의 소위 민족자결사상은 이제는 조선민중에게는 무의미하고 東亞史 발전의 신방향을 무시한 반동적 관념인 것이 분명하다.
玆에 오인은 皇道일본의 明日의 姿를 想하야 오인의 종래의 그릇된 민족관 국가관 세계관은 潔히 창산함을 得하였다. 조선민중의 久遠의 행복은 재선양족을 打하야 一丸을 삼아 대국민일본인을 구성하야 차를 핵심주체로한 신동아의 건설에 있음을 드드여 확신하기에 至한 것이다.
今에 조선민중의 利害休藏은 전혀 일본 전체의 그것과 합치되고 興隆日本의 前途 亦 決코 조선민중을 배제할 것이 아니라 회고컨대 내선 양족은 同根同祖 그 源에 있어서 일체이었따. 금일 차를 구분할 차이의 제 조건은 원래 차를 墨守할 필요가 없다. 금후 국민생활의 발전향상과 동포감의 심화철저에 從하야 次第로 소멸하고 마츰내 완전한 일민족으로 일원화함이 又 決코 불가능이 아니다. 분산에서 통일로 발전하여 나가는 인류사회발전의 역사는 필연적으로 차에 귀결케 하는 바 있다. 血과 문화의 淵源을 같이하는 양족이 신동아사 창조의 위대한 사명을 앞에 두고 완전히 일원화함은 실로 신의 의사로 오인에게 課일 것이다.
唯然이나 민족 일원화의 이상은 조급히 완성될 것이 아닌 고로 往往히 불가능함과 如히 思하고 是에 反하야 민족자결주의는 오랜 隋力에 눌리어 容易히 실현할 수 있는 듯이 思하는 일면이 있다. 금일까지 오등이 민족자결주의에 眩惑되여온 것도 필경 이때문이오 다수인이 민족관념에 미련을 가지는 것도 이때문이다. 然이나 시실은 전현 此에 反하야 내선일체민족일원화의 경향은 근시 장족의 진보를 遂하야 대세는 吾等의好不好를 불문하고 益益 此의 具現化를 요구하야 말지 안는다. 그 현실은 필연적이오, 이미 시간 문제가 되었다.
吾人은 如上의 이론적 근거와 현실의 諸 경향을 기초로 하야 內鮮 일체 완전한 일원화로써 조선 민중의 進할 유일한 途 인식하야 新일본 건설의 국민적 긍지와 포부 下에 그 일익적 임무를 달하는 것만이 眞히 조선 민중의 장래의 광영과 발전을 약속하는 것이라고 주장한다. 此 실로 大東民友會의＜27＞ 근본 정신이오, 吾人이 금후 일층 분투 노력하지 아니하면 안될 사명이다. 즉 吾人은 今에 일체를 棄하고 일체을 得하려는 가장 솔직 과감한 의기에 충만하여 있다.
今次의 支那 사변은 旣히 주지하는 바와 如히 日我本의 대 국가적 사명의 수행 亞細亞 諸 민족을 백인 제압의 질곡에서 해방하려는 목적의 聖戰이니 吾等은 금후 여하한 희생도 不厭하고 堅忍 持久 擧國 일체가 되여 목적의 관철을 기하여야 될 것이다.
世는 旣히 赤化 공산의 참화와 개인주의 공리주의적인 백인 문명의 추악에 厭性이 낫다. 八紘一宇 도의적 결합으로 하는 동양 정신 日本主義야말로 眞 東亞를 구하고 세계 인류를 지도함 원리이다. 고로 吾人은 광휘있는 일본 정신의 사도로서의 영예와 責左任 感한다. 因하야 吾人은 爾今 如上의 신념 주장하에 自奮 노력하기를 기하는 바이다.

北支에 朝鮮人 大進-北京, 天津, 濟南, 等地에
北京 = 사변 전에는 약 3,000명 (이 박게 내지인이 약 2,000명 합 5,000명)이든 것이 약 1만 2천여 명 (이 박게 내지인 약 8,000명)에 달하고 그리고도 1개월에 內鮮人 합처서 약 1,000인 씩이나 격증하고 잇다고 한다. 北京 거리에 냉면집, 떡집이 느러서게 되고 한편 카페, 喫茶店, 여관 등도 작고 느러간다.

天津 = 여기에는 사변 전에는 조선인이 1,200명 (이 박게 내지인이 1만 1천명)이나 잇든 것이 현재에는 약 4,000명으로 4배나 증가하엿고 내지인은 만여 명으로 亦 2배나 증가하엿다.

濟南 = 사변 전에는 얼마 업든 것이 현재는 1,100명 (이 박게 내지인 1,600명)에 달하엿다. 아마 금년 연말경에는 1만명을 돌파하게 되리라든가.
