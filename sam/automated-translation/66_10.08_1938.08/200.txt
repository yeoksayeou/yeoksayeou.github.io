Modern Women's Bad Tastes

Sin T'aeak
1. Foreign-style makeup. Especially fixing the hair and making it into a Western hairstyle (洋머리).
2. Motion pictures. The vanity, frivolity, and superficiality of modern women all stem from their liking of foreign motion pictures.
3. Gaudy clothing (亂色의 의상). Their taste in colors has become so disorderly that one cannot distinguish whether they are kisaeng or cafe waitresses.
(The author is a lawyer and social critic)

Kim Munjip
Generally speaking, bad taste is a type of degenerate cultural form that arises in highly cultured societies. Just as hypocrisy (僞惡) follows piety (위선), bad taste emerges after good taste (善취미) has developed. In Chosŏn women's society, however, one cannot yet discern bad taste; indeed, one feels it is still far too early even to speak of its prerequisite stage, the culture of good taste. Look here. First, consider their hairstyles. Everyone attaches a so-called suicidal "hair stone" (머리칼 돌맹이) – smaller than a dried persimmon (乾柿), no, smaller than the dung of a sick donkey foal – right to the middle of the back of their head (後頭), and then parades around intersections, OK concert halls, OK wedding halls... Even if the Amori Fashion Show (아모리 팟쇼) is in vogue, for their aesthetic sense (심미관) to be so cleverly controlled like this, we eternal culturalists have no choice but to initially forgo our right to interfere (容喙). These new women (!), decorating the backs of their heads with what looks like a single goat testicle each and strutting down the main roads, are unparalleled in their insolence and arrogance – this too is the pride of Chosŏn. It would be better to follow the old ways and wear your hair in a bun (쪽찌고) and put on a long skirt (긴치마). If not that, then wear geta (ケタ) and style your hair in a marumage (マルマゲ). I'd like to recommend bobbed hair (단발), but how could one bear to see the pig-like appearance that results? Hehe, I cannot stop sighing to myself (呵呵自歎不己矣).
(The author is a literary critic)

No Chayŏng
While there's nothing particularly egregious to single out as modern women's bad taste,
1. I wish they wouldn't wear those shoes that look like snakeskin (구렁이껍질), are full of gaping holes (숭얼숭얼), and resemble the back of a toad (멱자귀 잔등). I wish they would abandon the light-footed, flighty (輕跳 부박한) type and wear somewhat more modest shoes.
1. I wish they would abandon the habit of frequenting "tea houses" (茶店), paying 20 chŏn for a glass of water without any particular business there.
1. I cannot approve of them wearing fox fur scarves (여호 목도리) everywhere either.
(The author is a poet and newspaper reporter) <135>

**Summary**

Three male critics—a lawyer/social critic, a literary critic, and a poet/journalist—express their disdain for what they perceive as the "bad tastes" of modern Korean women in 1938. They criticize Western-influenced hairstyles, makeup, fashion (gaudy colors, revealing shoes, fox furs), and leisure activities like attending foreign films and frequenting tea houses. Kim Munjip's critique is particularly harsh, using crude analogies to mock modern hairstyles and accusing women of arrogance. The authors lament the perceived loss of traditional aesthetics and modesty, associating modern trends with superficiality, frivolity, and foreign influence. This article is historically interesting as it vividly reflects the anxieties and cultural clashes occurring in Korean society during the Japanese colonial period. It reveals conservative male perspectives on rapid modernization, Western cultural influx, and changing gender roles, viewing women's adoption of new styles and behaviors as a sign of cultural degradation rather than liberation or adaptation. Historians might find the specific criticisms (hairstyles, clothing items like fox furs, tea house culture) and the strong, often misogynistic language useful for understanding social tensions, gender dynamics, and the complex interplay between tradition, modernity, and colonial influence in 1930s Korea.

金文輯
 대저 악취미란 고도의 문화 사회에서 발생하는 변종 교양 형태의 하나입니다. 위선이 있은 후에 僞惡이 있고 善취미가 발달한 후에 악취미가 출동하는 법이올시다. 조선여성사회에서는 아직 악취미를 잡아낼 수 없음은 물론 그의 階段인 善취미 문화를 云爲하기에도 시일이 요원한 바 있을 느낌니다. 보십시요. 우선 그 두발 풍경을 乾柿보다 적쟎은 - 아니 병든 당나귀 색기의 똥보다 작은 可謂 자살적인 「머리칼 돌맹이」 한 개를 누구나 가다 後頭 한 복판에 달아 붙이곤 네거리의 0K음악회 0K결혼식장 0K 아모리 팟쇼가 유행이기로서니 조선여성의 심미관이 요로케도 용하게 통제됐다고 해서야 영원의 문화주의자인 吾人으로선 애당초에 容喙할 권리를 포기하는 수밖에 없구려. 염소 불알 한 개씩을 後頭에 장식하고 大路를 활보하는 此種 신여성(!) 건방지고 거만하기는 천하 무쌍이니 이 또한 조선의 자랑. 차라리 본받아 쪽찌고 긴치마를 입으시오. 不然이면 ケタ끄시고 マルマゲ나 하시죠. 단발을 권하고 싶으나 돼나오는 그 꼴을 또 어찌보나. 呵呵自歎不己矣로소이다.
(필자는 문예 평론가)
