Modern Women's Bad Taste
Kim Ch'angje
Since taste, like preference, differs from individual to individual, I think it's rather unreasonable to objectively judge others' tastes as good or bad. And since taste naturally changes according to lifestyle and environment, how can one speak of good or bad regarding taste alone without delving into that person's life? Therefore, it is difficult to give a responsible answer to your question. However, if I were to vaguely or temporarily note down some impressions:
1. Motion pictures.
2. Chocolate.
3. Perming hair, applying lipstick.
- The author is a professor at Ewha High School (梨花 高普) -

Chu Unsŏng
Regarding the three items of "bad taste" of modern women you inquired about, I have considered them, but I don't recognize enough to enumerate three; I will write just a couple.
1. A common bad taste among modern women is movie addiction, especially favoring European and American (歐米) films. The fervor for worshipping white people has expanded considerably, leading to no small amount of contempt for East Asians. Moreover, they send 100 percent adoration towards Western star actors.
2. It's the habit of liking to follow the cutting edge. Many women abandon moderation and rush madly from one extreme trend to another. Look at their attire! Look at their gestures!
- The author is a social critic -

Ch'oe Yunjŏng
Our society is in a transitional period, and moreover, with everything in its current state, our society lacks a definite guiding spirit. There are more than one or two things we still need to learn and reform, and the women's issue is one of the significant problems. However, when men discuss the women's issue, I believe the prerequisite condition is for men themselves to fundamentally clear away their feudalistic perception of women.
Regarding the three items of modern women's "bad taste" you inquired about, I wish to substitute my answer by making a few requests to women of the modern intellectual class.
1. Thoroughly recognize the times, awaken to the increasing importance of home education and how the era makes women's social duties more significant, face tasks with a consistent spirit from beginning to end, and do not push onto men tasks that can be accomplished by women's abilities <162> but rather gain the rights of gender equality through work efficiency (能率).
2. Avoid falling into vanity by excessively chasing trends.
3. Cultivate economic knowledge, consider income and expenditure, and reduce the husband's hardships (苦勞).
- The author is a social critic -

Pak Kich'ae
1. Nowadays, Korean women are losing traditional beauty in more ways than one. To give an example... why do they all burn their beautiful hair and walk around like that?
2. When they speak, trying to pretend they know things they don't, why do they mix in English words that don't even make sense?
3. There's nothing inherently wrong with women frequenting drinking establishments (酒場), but coming and going from bars late at night with bold strides (活步) is something women should probably reconsider.

Cho Pyŏgam
1. The overuse of "Well..." (그럼) ... Despite the luxury of just one layer of outer clothing and putting Rédo brand items in a Coty compact, still, "Well...".
2. The pretense of "Che-" (체-) ... Pretending not to know even when they don't know.
3. The imitation of "Heung" (흥) ... Unable to say a single word to someone's face, but turning away and saying "Heung."
- The author is a poet -

Na Ung
1. Being abundant in inner strength.
2. Pretending not to know even when one knows.
3. Loving moderation and simplicity.
(Would these suffice as the three items of modern women's bad taste?)
(The author is an actor and drama critic)

Ch'o P'aewang
1. The habit of testing every man as if all men passing by are suitors.
2. The habit of overvaluing her own beauty by about threefold, thinking that she alone is fit to be the protagonist of a modern house (문화 주택), piano, and flower-planted garden.
3. Knowing the names of Western actors too well.
- The author is a literary critic -

Na Kongju
1. Casting flirtatious glances (추파) at men passing by.
2. Following behind a man walking ahead, looking him up and down while mixing in various indecent imaginations about his physical beauty (육신미).
3. Leaning against men more than necessary in a crowded streetcar.
(This is the pseudonym (匿名) of a certain female writer) <163>

**Summary**

This 1938 article from *Samch'ŏlli* magazine compiles responses from various (mostly male) Korean intellectuals, critics, and artists about what they consider the "bad tastes" of modern women. Criticisms range from adopting Western trends like movies, fashion (permed hair, makeup), and using English words, to perceived vanity, addiction to movies (especially Western ones), lack of traditional modesty, financial irresponsibility, superficiality in conversation, and overly forward behavior towards men. One female writer anonymously criticizes women's behavior towards men as well. The article reflects the anxieties and perspectives of predominantly male observers regarding changing gender roles, Western cultural influence, and the definition of femininity in colonial Korea during a period of social transition. Historians might find this article valuable for understanding patriarchal attitudes, the impact of Western culture (particularly cinema), the tensions between tradition and modernity, and the social expectations placed upon women in 1930s Korea. The piece reveals concerns about national identity (critique of Western influence, loss of "traditional beauty") alongside anxieties about female agency and public behavior.

1. 활동사진.
2. 초코레트.
3. 머리 지지기 입설 칠하기.
- 필자는 梨花 高普 교수 -

朱雲成
하문하신 현대여성의 「악취미」 3개조에 대하와 考究해 보았으나 3개조까지는 枚擧할 인식이 없고 두어가지만 씀니다.
1. 현대여성의 공통적 악취미는 영화중독인 바 특히 歐米영화를 기호하며 백인숭배열이 팽창하야 동양인 모멸감이 여간 아니며 서양名優에 대하야는 100퍼센트의 戀慕를 보내는 것이외다.
2. 첨단을 것기 좋와하는 버릇입니다. 중용을 버서나 첨단에서 첨단으로 狂奔하는 여성들이 많습니다. 그네들의 단장을 보시요! 채스추어를 보시요.
- 필자는 사회 평론가 -

崔潤廷
과도기의 우리 사회이요 더욱 모든 것이 현금과 같은 처지에 있는 우리 사회라 일정한 지도 정신을 갓지 못하였으니 우리로써 아직 배워야 할 것과 개혁할 점이 한 두 가지가 안이나 여성문제도 중대한 문제의 一이다. 그런대 남성들이 여성문제를 운운할 제 먼저 남성들 자신이 여성에 대한 봉건적 두뇌의 인식을 근본적으로 청산할 것이 선결조건인 줄로 생각한다.
귀문의 현대여성의 「악취미」 3개조에 대하야 현대 지식인계급의 여성에게 멫가지를 부탁하는 것으로 代코저 한다.
1. 시대를 철저히 인식하야 가정교육의 더욱 중대함과 시대가 여성의 사회적 임무가 더욱  중대하게 하는 것을 각성하야 시종여일한 정신으로 일에 당하야 여성의 능력으로 될 일은 남성에게 밀지 말고＜162＞ 일의 能率로서 남녀평등의 권리를 얻도록 할 것.
2. 지내치게 유행을 좃처 허영에 빠지는 폐해가 없도록 할 것.
3. 경제적 지식을 배양하야 수입 지출을 생각하야 남편의 苦勞를 적게 하도록 할 것.
