Multi-faceted Young Activist
						Provincial Councilor Mr. Kim Kŭn-ho

While in the world there are frustrating individuals who, just because they have a bit of money, seem almost innocently imprisoned in tall mansions (高樓巨閣), wasting precious time, there are also people who appear on the vast stage, utilizing funds not only to make their own future prospects even more brilliant but also to benefit their neighbors. Mr. Kim Kŭn-ho of Kanghwa (江華), whom we are about to discuss, is a vigorous young man of 32 this year with a bright future ahead. He displays his nimble skills most energetically in all sorts of fields: business, education, and politics. Currently, he is a provincial councilor who ran for the Kyŏnggi Provincial Council (京畿道會), chairman of the Kanghwa Public Common School (江華公普校) support association, director of a kindergarten, a director of the incorporated foundation Habil School (合一學校), and furthermore, he is active as a director (取締役) in companies involved in the ginseng business (蔘業), fertilizer, rice milling, and other ceramics industries, as well as being the head of the Grain Association and a director of the Ginseng Cooperative. He is an exemplary figure of good character who, since graduating with honors from Paejae High Common School (培材高普) in Taishō 15 (1926), has strived and labored for Kanghwa society for over ten years. <168>

**Summary**

This article profiles Kim Kŭn-ho, a 32-year-old man from Kanghwa, as a dynamic and multi-talented young activist in 1938 colonial Korea. It contrasts him with the idle wealthy, praising his active involvement and success in business (ginseng, fertilizer, rice milling, ceramics), education (school support, kindergarten director, foundation director), and politics (Kyŏnggi Provincial Councilor). Graduating from Paejae High Common School in 1926, he is lauded as a model personality dedicated to his local community for over a decade. For historians, this piece offers insight into the emergence of a particular type of Korean elite under Japanese rule – individuals who navigated the colonial system to achieve prominence and influence across various sectors, embodying a blend of modern enterprise, community leadership, and political participation within the established framework. It highlights the activities considered praiseworthy and indicative of success for certain Koreans during this period.

多角的 靑年 活動家
						道議員 金根鎬씨
세간에는 돈 푼이나 있다고 고루거각에 무죄 감금당하다 싶히 되어가지고 천금같은 세월을 허송하는 답답한 인간도 있는 반면에 광활한 무대에 등장하여 금전을 활용함으로써 자신의 전도를 가일층 광휘있게 할뿐 아니라 내 이웃까지 이롭게 하는 사람도 있다. 지금 말하려는 江華의 金根鎬씨는 아직까지 전도가 양양한 금년 32세의 씩씩한 청년으로서 실업방면 교육방면 정치방면 이렇게 온갖 방면으로 가장 정력가다웁게 그 쾌민한 수완을 베풀고 있나니 현재 그는 京畿道會에 출마한 도의원이요, 江華公普校 후원 회장, 유치원 원장, 재단법인 合一학교 이사, 그리고 또 蔘業, 비료, 정미업, 기타요업주식회사 취체역, 곡물협회장, 인삼조합 이사 등 방면으로 활약하는 분이다. 大正 15년도에 培材高普를 우수한 성적으로 졸업하고 나온 직후부터 江華사회를 위하여 십有여년간 분투노력해 온 모범적, 인격자이다.＜168＞
