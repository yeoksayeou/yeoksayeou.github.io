Editorial Postscript

The *Sambok* season, when people seek coolness (凉味) in the mountains and waters, has suddenly arrived. If you go to the mountains, you miss the sea, and if you go to the sea, you miss the mountains. Therefore, we repeatedly wish that you find a place where mountains and water meet, pitch a tent, and spend this summer with health as your top priority.

Facing this intense heat, when our thoughts turn to the vast battlefields of North China (北支) and Central China (中支), where the soldiers of the Imperial Army are busy fighting the Holy War (聖戰) for East Asian peace while battling the scorching heat of over 130 degrees [Fahrenheit], we naturally feel a sense of apology. We believe that, to support the nation from the home front (총후의 보국) in any way possible, robust young people should voluntarily step forward to work diligently, develop resources, and strive to promote (振作) the national spirit.

As a new experiment this time, we have decided to seek out newspapers and magazines that were shining cornerstones (주칫돌) in the cultural movement of this land in the past, extract and condense their essence (精華), and publish it in each issue. Thus, for the first installment, we have selected representative essays, travelogues, and poems from various fields within the unforgettable 16 volumes of *Ch'ŏngch'un* (靑春) and published them as a special supplement. Following this, we will extend this to various magazines such as *Kaebyŏk* (開闢), *Chosŏn chi Kwang* (朝鮮之光), and *Tonggwang* (東光), and also one by one to the *Tong-a Ilbo* (東亞日報), *Chosŏn Ilbo* (朝鮮日報), *Maeil Sinbo* (每日新報), *Sidae Ilbo* (時代日報), and *Chungoe Ilbo* (中外日報).

We do this because, in an era like today where culture is stagnant, it seems rather better to re-chew (咀嚼) the good culture of bygone days. Also, since new culture always emerges based on tradition, we believe there are things to be naturally found within the path walked in former days.

To give one more reason, in a place like Korea where publishing culture is not robust, there are too many writings (文籍) that are too precious to be read once and discarded. Therefore, we believe creating such an opportunity now is meaningful.

On the day the autumn wind begins to blow, our company intends to greet you all with new plans and a new appearance.

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the August 1938 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine wishes readers well during the summer heat while simultaneously promoting the Japanese war effort in China, referring to it as a "Holy War for East Asian Peace." It urges Koreans, especially youth, to support the war from the "home front" through diligent labor, resource development, and promoting the "national spirit." The editorial also announces a new feature: reprinting selections from past influential Korean magazines and newspapers like *Ch'ŏngch'un*, arguing that revisiting past cultural achievements is valuable in a time of perceived cultural stagnation and provides a foundation for new culture, especially given Korea's less developed publishing environment. For historians, the article starkly reveals the pressures and rhetoric of wartime mobilization in colonial Korea, blending everyday concerns (summer heat) with overt support for Japan's imperial ambitions ("Imperial Army," "Holy War," "home front support"). The plan to republish older Korean cultural works under these circumstances also presents an interesting juxtaposition, potentially reflecting a complex negotiation between promoting Japanese goals and preserving a sense of Korean cultural heritage, albeit framed within the context of contemporary "stagnation."

編輯 後書
산에 물에 凉味를 찿어 가는 三伏철이 어느덧 다다럿도다. 산으로 가면 바다가 그립고 바다로 가면 산이 그립고 그러니 산수 합처진 곳에 천막이나 치시고 이 여름 건강제일로 지내시기를 거듭 바라나이다.

이 혹서를 당하매 北支, 中支의 저 너른 戰地에서 130여도의 炎熱과 싸우면서, 동양평화의 聖戰에 분주하고 있는 황군장사의 일에 마음이 달니매 저절로 죄송한 생각이 듬니다. 우리들은 아모조록 총후의 보국을 하기 위하여 건장한 청소년들은 스사로 나아가 근로하며 자원개척도 하고 국민정신의 振作에 진력하여야 할 바인 줄 아나이다.

이번에 새로운 시험으로 녯 이 땅 문화운동에 빛난 주칫돌 하나 둘 씩 놓고 지내든 신문 잡지를 찿어 그 精華를 발췌 압축하여 매호마다 실니기로 하였음니다. 그리하여 이번에, 그 제1차로 우리들의 기억에서 떠나지 않는 저 「靑春」 전16권 중에서 여러 방면으로 대표될 論策 기행, 시가를 뽑아 특별부록으로 실엇나이다. 이것은 이 뒤에 開闢, 朝鮮之光 東光 등 여러 잡지에도 미처겠고 東亞日報, 朝鮮日報, 每日新報, 時代日報, 中外日報에도 한 벌 한 벌 씩 미치겠음니다.

이것은 오늘같이 문화가, 답보하는 시대에 있어선 전날의 좋튼 문화를 다시 咀嚼함이 도로혀 나으며 또 한편 새 문화는 언제든지 전통을 태반으로 하고 나오느니 만치 전날에 거러 온 그 속에서 저절로 찾어지는 것이 따로 잇을 줄 알고 그리함이로소이다.

이 밖에 또 한가지의 이유를 들자면 조선같이 출판문화가 왕성치 못한 곳에 있어선 한 번 보고 버리기엔 너무나 아까운 文籍들이 많키 때문에 이제 이리한 기회를 짓는 것이 뜻이 있다고 믿고 그리함이로소이다.

추풍이 떠러지는 날 본사는 새로운 계획과 面容으로 여러분을 對코저 하옵니다.
