Magazine Title: 삼천리 제7권 제11호
Publication Date: 1935년 12월 01일
Article Title: 꿈길 (Dream Path)
Author: 파인
Article Type: 시 (Poem)

꿈길
						파인

그리운 이 보내면서
울지 안는 까닭은
사내 대장부라기보다
꿈을 밋기 때문이외다

가시는 길
고이 보고 두섯다가
어두운 꿈길
길옷지기 말고 오소서 <299>

***

Dream Path
						P'ain

The reason I do not weep
While sending off the one I miss
Is not because I am a stout man
But because I believe in dreams.

Having watched your departing path carefully
Please come back without losing your way
On the dark dream path <299>

**Summary**

This short poem expresses the speaker's feelings upon parting with a loved one. The speaker claims their lack of tears stems not from stoic masculinity but from a firm belief in dreams, implying a hope for reunion through this ethereal realm. The speaker urges the departed one to remember the way and return safely via the "dark dream path." From a historical perspective, the poem's themes of separation, hope placed in dreams, and the desire for return might reflect the broader anxieties and longings of Koreans during the colonial period, possibly hinting at suppressed desires for connection or restoration veiled in personal sentiment. The use of older Korean orthography and grammatical forms is also noteworthy.
