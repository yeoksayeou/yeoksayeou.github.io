Dosan's Personality and Stage
						By Yi Kwangsu

Regarding An Dosan, due to various circumstances, it seems difficult to speak sufficiently about his personality or skill.

The An Dosan I know is, I believe, a man of "truth" and "patience." I see him as someone who has completely overcome lies and false expressions. While there might be things he doesn't reveal, he possesses no duplicity, expediency, or conspiracy. Although some in the world might misunderstand him, paradoxically, this misunderstanding stems entirely from his truthfulness and lack of artifice and expediency. I believe he has more than enough talent to employ the resourcefulness, pretense, and flattery needed to make everyone he meets happy and friendly, but he has made it his life's great vow and lifelong duty to remove this "falsehood" from himself and from the Korean people. Furthermore, regarding his striving for patience, no one has ever seen him express anger, fury, or dislike towards people, matters, or objects.

I believe Dosan's personality stands upon the two pillars mentioned above.<54>

Speaking of Dosan's skill, his outstanding strength seems to be his statesmanship (經倫). In this aspect, he is thoroughly practical. He is by no means a dreamer in this regard.

As for what kind of stage should be provided for Dosan, I cannot say. Why? Because Dosan is a person who creates his own stage with his own hands, whatever situation he may be in.

However, hypothetically, if asked what I would offer Dosan, I would want to entrust him with an educational institution (學園) encompassing everything from kindergarten (幼穉園) to primary schools (普通學校), higher common schools (男女高等普通學校) for boys and girls, specialized schools (男女專門學校) for boys and girls, and universities (大學). I believe this would not only be the stage Dosan would most enjoy but also the one where he would be most adept. It has already been proven at Taesŏng School (大成學校) that even those who study for just a few months at Dosan's school receive distinctive training and become distinctive people, and I believe his capabilities in this regard have become even more perfected (圓熱) now.<55>

**Summary**

Yi Kwangsu portrays An Ch'angho (Dosan) as a man of absolute truthfulness and immense patience, whose lack of pretense sometimes leads to misunderstanding. Yi highlights An's practical statesmanship as his key skill, distinct from mere idealism. While acknowledging An's ability to create his own opportunities, Yi suggests that the ideal role for An would be leading a comprehensive educational institution, from kindergarten to university, believing An's talent for character-building, already demonstrated at Taesŏng School, has only grown. Historically, this article is interesting as it shows the deep admiration Yi Kwangsu, a leading intellectual, held for the independence leader An Ch'angho in 1935, focusing on moral character and education as An's strengths. This perspective is noteworthy given Yi's later controversial path during the colonial period, offering insight into his earlier views and the high esteem An commanded even within the constraints of colonial Korea.

島山의 人格과 舞臺
						李光洙
安島山에 대하여는 여러 가지 사정으로 그의 人格이나 手腕을 충분히 말하기가 어러울 것 갓슴니다.
내가 아는 安島山은 「참」과 「참음」의 사람이라고 봅니다. 그는 거즛말, 거즛 표정을 완전히 극복한 사람이라고 봅니다, 그에게는 발표아니하는 것이 잇을 지언정 表裡도 없고 權變도 없고 陰謀도 없습니다.
세상에서 혹시 그를 오해하는 이가 잇지마는 좀 파라독스 같지마는 이 오해가 다 그의 참되어 꾸밈과 權變없는 데서 온 것입니다. 그에게는 접하는 모든 사람을 다 愉快하게 하고 다 친하게 할 만한 權變과 假飾과 謟曲을 부리라면 부릴 재주는 넉넉하다고 생각합니다마는 이 「거즛」이라는 것을 自己個人의게서 빼어버리고 朝鮮사람에게서 빼어버리자는 것을 일생의 大願을 삼고 또 일생의 職分을 삼고 잇습니다. 또 그가 참음을 힘쓴다 함은 그는 일즉 사람이나 일이나 물건에 대하야 성을 내거나 화를 내거나 싫인 생각을 내이는 것을 본 사람이 없읍니다.
島山의 人格은 이상에 말슴한 두 기둥 우에 선 것이라고 믿습니다.<54>
島山의 手腕에 대하여서 말슴하면 그 중에 特長되는 것은 經倫인가 합니다. 이 점에는 그는 어듸까지나 實際的임니다. 그는 이 점에서 결코 空想家가 아닙니다.
島山의게 어떤 무대를 제공하엿으면 좋겟는가에 대하여서는 말할 수 없읍니다. 왜 그런고하면 島山은 어떤 처지에 잇던지 자기의 무대를 自己 손으로 만드는 사람입니다.
만일, 그래도 假想으로, 만일 島山의게 무엇을 제공해 보라고 하면 나는 幼穉園에서붓어 普通學校, 男女高等普通學校, 男女專門學校와 大學과 갖흔 것 포함한 學園을 맡기고 싶읍니다. 이것이 島山이 가장 질겨할 뿐 아니라 가장 능난한 舞臺일까 합니다. 島山의 學校에서 멧달만 배운 사람이라도 특색 잇는 훈련을 받아서 특색잇는 사람이 되는 것은 이미 大成學校에서 실험된 것이지마는 지금은 그 능력이 더욱 圓熱하엿을 것을 믿습니다.<55>
