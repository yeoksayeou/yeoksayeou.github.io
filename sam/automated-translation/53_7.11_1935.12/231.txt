Wŏnsan General Branch Office Announcement

General Branch Office: Wŏnsan Munhwa Sŏwŏn (元山文化書院)
Branch President: Kim Yongsŏk (金容錫)
Reporter: Pak Miha (朴米夏)
Sales Manager: Pak Yŏngsik (朴永植)
External Affairs Manager: No Ponggwan (魯鳳琯)
Advisor: Kim Hyŏnggu (金烱龜)

Anbyŏn Branch Office: Anbyŏn-gun Sijangt'ong (安邊郡市場通)
Branch President: T'ak Yŏngha (卓永河)
Reporter: Kim Sŭngnyŏl (金承㤠) (Note: 㤠 is rare, potentially 烈)

Tŏkwŏn Branch Office: Wŏnsan-bu Myŏngch'ijŏng (元山府明治町)
Sinp'ung Store (信豊商店)
Branch President: Kim Yŏnghwan (金永煥)
Reporter: Kim Sŏkpok (金石福)

We announce the above.
Samch'ŏlli Company (三千里社) <149>

**Summary**

This article is a formal announcement (社告) from the Samch'ŏlli Company listing the staff and locations for its Wŏnsan General Branch Office and its subordinate Anbyŏn and Tŏkwŏn branch offices as of December 1935. It provides names and titles for personnel, including branch presidents, reporters, managers, and an advisor, along with the addresses for each office. For historians, this piece offers insight into the organizational structure and regional network of a prominent Korean magazine during the colonial period. The specific locations (including a Japanese-named district, Meiji-chō/Myŏngch'ijŏng) and roles illustrate the operational footprint and staffing of such cultural enterprises under Japanese rule.
