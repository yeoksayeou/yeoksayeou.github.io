**Body Text Translation**
The Wheels Listen
						Kim Kirim (金起林)

Presently
In the broken "Metropolis" trampled by the typhoon
The young Sun, like a chick
Will flap its wings and rise.
Having crossed that night's dream
Shaking off countless alarms and chills
It will spread its dew-wet wings to the sky.

When the sturdy Main Road, like Hope
Stretches to that distant Horizon
Our Tomorrow will ride a four-wheeled carriage
Ringing out the sound of wandering horse hooves<286>
And depart on a newly met road.

The distant home of the Sun,
Which makes the heart race more because it is night.

On the endless fields, the green hills
It will be more talkative than many a "Demosthenes."
There, I will snap off my whip
Love like a foal
And romp like a foal.

My eyes, which will never burn with hatred,
Will be clearer than Pearls (眞珠).
I will drive out the Goat (山羊) crouching within me
And, pure like Jehovah (여호와),
Be intimate with my sisters (누의).<287>

My Life is my Rose (薔薇).
Not knowing where it began
Nor when it will end, I am
The Sun burning without end.

Drinking geothermal heat from the roots of the Earth
I, who will shake myself off and rise, am the Phoenix (不死鳥).
My flight, with wings of Wisdom (叡智) attached to my back,
Will cover the Universe (宇宙) like the Sun.

Blooming like light from beautiful Action (行動)
Guided by its own Law (法則)
My flight will be an iron wheel
Endlessly running on a joyful Track (軌道).

Friend—Ah
Like the Sun, let us be fierce<288>
Like the Sun, let us hide the shadows within our own light
Like the Sun, let us swallow our sorrow (슲음).
Like the Sun, let us burn away the darkness.

The next day
On the "mast" of the Weather Bureau (氣象臺)
Let's raise a white flag like a piece of cloud.

Clear Skies (快晴).
The Low Pressure (低氣壓) has disappeared
Near distant "Siberia" (시베리아)
And along the coast of the Pacific (太平洋)
The High Pressure has also scattered.
Cloudiness and showers (소낙비)
Storms (暴風) and the rainy season (장마) have passed
Tomorrow too, and the day after<289>
The weather (날세) will be good.

"Prefectural Bulletin Board (府의 揭示板)"
Citizens (市民)—
Melancholy and jealousy and anger and
Endless sighs and resentment's
Rainy season's moldy (곰팽이낀)
Damp things (雨器), please cast them off
And like wings, light
Change into the Sun's clothes.
(End)<290>

**Summary**

This poem by Kim Kirim uses powerful, optimistic imagery centered around the sun, wheels, and technological progress (Metropolis, four-wheeled carriage, Weather Bureau) to depict a future of renewal and hope emerging from destruction (typhoon-stricken Metropolis). It urges readers to cast off negative emotions ("melancholy, jealousy, anger") associated with a dark past ("night," "rainy season") and embrace a bright, active future characterized by purity, wisdom, and self-driven energy, much like the sun or an endlessly running wheel. For historians, the poem is interesting for its embrace of modernity and Western symbols (Metropolis, Demosthenes, Jehovah, Phoenix) alongside traditional nature imagery, reflecting the complex cultural milieu of 1930s colonial Korea. The call for collective optimism and shedding past burdens, announced via a modern "Prefectural Bulletin Board," subtly hints at aspirations for societal transformation, even if overt political critique is absent.

車輪은 듯는다
						金起林
이윽고
颱風이 지밟고간 깨여진 『메트로폴리쓰」에
어린 太陽이 병아리처름
홰를 치며 일어날게다.
하로밤 그꿈을 건너 댕기든
수없는 놀램과 소름을 떨어버리고
이슬에 젗은 날개를 한울로 펼게다.

탄탄한 大路가 希望처름
저머언 地平線에 뻧이면
우리의 來日은 四輪馬車를 타고
유랑한 말발굽소리를 울리면서<286>
처음맞는 새길을 떠나갈게다.

밤인 까닭에 더욱 마음달리는
저머언 太陽의 故鄕.

끗없는 들 푸른 언덕우에서
마는 『데모스테네쓰」보다도 더 수다할게다.
나는 거기서 채찍을 꺽거버리고
망아지처름 사랑하고
망아지처름 뛰놀게다.

미움에 타는 일이 없을 나의 눈동자는
眞珠보다도 맑을게다.
나는 내속에 업드린 山羊을 몰아내고
여호와같이 깨끗하게
누의들과 親할 것이다.<287>

나의 生活은 나의 薔薇.
어디서 시작한 줄도
언제 끛날줄도 모르는 나는
꺼질줄이 없이 불타는 太陽.

大地의 뿌리에서 地熱을 마시고
떨치고 이러날 나는 不死鳥.
叡智의 날개를 등에 붗인 나의 날음은
太陽처름 宇宙를 덮을게다.

아름다운 行動에서 빛처름 피여나는
스스로의 法則에 引導되여
나의 날음은 즐거운 軌道우에
끗없이 달리는 쇠바퀼게다.

벗 아-
太陽처름 우리는 사나웁고<288>
太陽처름 스스로의 빛속에 그늘을 감추고
太陽처름 슲음을 삼켜버리자.
太陽처름 어둠을 살워버리자.

다음날
氣象臺의 『마스트』엔
구름쪼각같은 힌 旗폭을 올리자.

快晴.
低氣壓은 저먼
『시베리아』의 근방에 사라젓고
太平洋의 沿岸에도
高氣壓은 흩어젓다.
흐림도 소낙비도
暴風도 장마도 지나갓고
來日도 모레도<289>
날세는 좋을 게다.

「府의 揭示板」
市民은
우울과 질투와 분노와
끗없는 탄식과 원한의
장마에 곰팽이낀
추근한 雨器일랑 벗어버리고
날개와 같이 가벼운
太陽의 옷을 갈아입어도 좋을 게다.
(끗)<290>
