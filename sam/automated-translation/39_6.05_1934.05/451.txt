**Summary**

This short text is an official announcement from the Samch'ŏlli Company regarding the establishment of its Wŏnsan Main Branch Office. It lists the appointed Branch Manager, Nam Chŏnggwan, the General Affairs Manager, Ko Kibong, and designates the operational area as the Wŏnsan vicinity. For historians, this notice provides concrete evidence of the geographical expansion and organizational structure of the popular magazine *Samch'ŏlli* during the colonial period, indicating its reach into provincial cities like Wŏnsan.
