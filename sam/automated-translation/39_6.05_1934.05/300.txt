**Body Text Translation:**
Analysis Table of the Ideal Wife

A certain famous writer announced the percentage analysis table of the wife he ideally envisions as follows.

1.  Beauty - Appearance (long like a willow leaf, eyes round and large, teeth white as snow, nose prominent*) 50 points; Beauty of Speech (P'yŏngan Province dialect or Seoul accent is good) 10 points; Beauty of Demeanor (tall and slender figure, delicate, graceful and poised, just like a crane or a white heron) 20 points. Total 80 points
(*Refers to a well-defined, somewhat high nose bridge)

2.  Education - Graduating from primary school is sufficient. Didn't Rousseau marry a completely uneducated woman? The philosopher Laozi also did so. Education is not that important for a housewife. Total 10 points

3.  Wealth - A woman raised in a very poor household lacks cheerfulness due to shadows in her disposition. Therefore, one born into the middle class is enviable. Total 10 points. This completes the 100 points.<64>

**Summary**

This article presents a point-based system outlining one writer's criteria for an "ideal wife" in 1934 Korea. The overwhelming majority of points (80%) are allocated to physical beauty, encompassing facial features, specific regional speech patterns (P'yŏngan or Seoul dialect), and a tall, slender, graceful figure likened to a crane or heron. Education is deemed largely unimportant (10%), with primary school being sufficient, citing Rousseau and Laozi as examples. Modest wealth (middle-class background) is preferred (10%) to ensure a cheerful disposition, avoiding the perceived "shadows" of poverty. For historians, the article offers insight into patriarchal beauty standards and social values in colonial Korea, revealing a strong emphasis on appearance over female intellect, specific preferences for regional accents possibly linked to perceived modernity or status (Seoul) or perhaps regional stereotypes (P'yŏngan), and class-based assumptions about personality.

理想的 妻의 分折表
엇던 高名한 文人이 자기가 理想하는 이해의 百分比分折表를 아래와 가치 발표하엿다.
1. 美-容貌는 (버들입가치 길고, 눈이 어골어골 크고 이가 白雪가치 히고 코이 산둑한 이*) 50점 言語美 (平安道 사투리나 서울말씨가 조타) 10점 姿態美(키가 후리후리 크고 간얄피고, 산눗하야 마치 학두룸이나 백로가튼 이) 20점. 計 80점
2. 學識-普通學校卒業程度라도 無*하다. 룻소는 전혀 무식한 여자를 娶치 안엇든가. 哲人老子도 그러하엿다. 家庭婦人에 學識은 그러케 중요치 안타. 計 10점
3. 財産-너무 가난 집안에서 자라난 여성은 그 심성에 陰影이 잇서 明朗味를 缺한다. 그러기에 中産階級에 태어난 이가 부럽다. 計 10점 이상으로 100점을 完한다.<64>
