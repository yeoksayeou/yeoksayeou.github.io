What is a Wife?

1. A wife is the husband's best or worst property. (British Proverb)

1. It is women and gold that rule the world. (British Proverb)

1. A marriage against one's will is hell. It is the repetition of endless fighting. (Shakespeare)

1. To make a gentleman happy, one must only choose a good wife. (Massinger)

1. Marriage is the flower of happiness for all. It is also a pest. (Byron)

1. Everyone knows that after marriage, one ages seven years in just one day. (Bacon)

1. Only by having one wife can one discover true pleasure. (Hebūryu - possibly Hebel)

1. A rich wife is the cause of dispute. (British Proverb)

1. He who has a bad wife cannot expect<53> happiness. (Spanish Proverb)

1. A husband having talent and a wife having patience is the foundation of domestic safety. (British Proverb)

1. Discord within the home is the dwelling place of the god of poverty (貧神). (British Proverb)

1. The husband is the wife's heaven. (*Book of Rites* 禮記)<54>

**Summary**

This article presents a collection of proverbs and quotes from various sources (British, Spanish, Shakespeare, Byron, Bacon, Massinger, Hebel?, and the Chinese *Book of Rites*) defining the nature, role, and impact of a wife and marriage. The perspectives range from viewing a wife as property or a source of discord to acknowledging her importance for happiness and domestic stability, alongside warnings about ill-suited marriages. For historians, this piece is interesting as it reflects the mix of influences in 1930s Korea: traditional Confucian ideas (husband as heaven) alongside newly introduced Western concepts and literary quotes about marriage, love, and gender roles. It showcases how Western cultural elements were being discussed and juxtaposed with existing societal norms during the colonial period.

안해란 것은?
一. 妻는 男便의 최선 又는 最惡의 家財다.(英國俚諺)
一. 世界를 支配하는 것이 婦人과 黃金이다.(英國俚諺)
一. 뜻에 어기는 結婚은 地獄이다. 끗업는 싸홈의 反復이다.(쎅스피어).
一. 君子를 행복케함에는 오직 良妻를 擇함에 잇다.(맛신쟈-)
一. 結婚은 만인의 행복의 꼿이다. 또한 害虫이다.(빠이론)
一. 누구나 結婚 후 단 하로에 7년이나 늙어진 것을 안다.(베-곤)
一. 오직 한 사람의 안해를 가저야 비로소 眞情한 快樂을 발견알 수 잇다.(헤부-류)
一. 富妻는 爭論의 원인이다.(英國俚諺)
一. 낫분 처를 가진 자는 행복을 기대<53> 할 수 업다.(西班牙俚諺)
一. 男便에개 才智잇고 妻에 忍耐力 잇슴은 家內안전의 기초다.(英國俚諺)
一. 家內의 不和는 貧神의 宿所다.(英國俚諺)
一. 남편은 안해의 하늘이다.(禮記)<54>
