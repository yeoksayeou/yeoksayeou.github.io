**Body Text Translation**
Emergency Notice

1. Starting from this May issue, the price of this magazine has been raised to 30 *chŏn*. Please be aware of this.

2. Each month's magazine will be released simultaneously throughout all of Chosŏn before the 10th of the preceding month. Please place your orders in advance.

3. Each month's magazine will be published as a substantial volume (巨冊) of over 250 pages in crown octavo size (國判), prioritizing rich content.

4. To make up for past mistakes, from now on we will publish steadily every month, so please grant us much patronage.<250>

**Summary**

This notice from the magazine *Samch'ŏlli* announces several changes effective May 1934: a price increase to 30 *chŏn*, an earlier nationwide release date (before the 10th of the prior month), an expanded format (over 250 pages), and a renewed commitment to regular monthly publication to address previous inconsistencies. For historians, this notice reflects the economic pressures and operational strategies of Korean publishing houses during the colonial era. It reveals attempts to balance financial sustainability with reader expectations through price adjustments, improved logistics, enhanced content, and assurances of reliability, likely in a challenging market environment.
