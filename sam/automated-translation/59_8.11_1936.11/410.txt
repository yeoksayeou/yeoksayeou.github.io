Magazine Title: 삼천리
Publication Date: 1936년 11월 01일
Article Title: 編輯後 (After Editing)
Author:
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editorial Postscript)

To our tens of thousands of beloved readers, we wish to make one sincere confession. Starting this month, "Samch'ŏlli" has slightly reduced its page count. Currently, most magazines in Chosŏn operate by disregarding production costs, losing a huge amount of money each month.

Because profitability is disregarded, they publish as long as there is money and cease publication when there is none. The history of the past ten-odd years is entirely this. This is a great disgrace for the cultural enterprise of a civilized nation.

How can a magazine be published for a long time? To do so, one must produce a book that is profitable. Therefore, there is no other way but to create a magazine standardized at around 200 pages and charging 30 chŏn.

Beloved readers, we trust you will forgive the reduction in this magazine's page count for the sake of eternal, long-lasting publication. Instead, we intend to compensate with carefully selected content.
Please continue to read it fondly for years to come. <250>

**Summary**

This editorial from *Samch'ŏlli* announces a reduction in page count due to financial unsustainability. The editors lament the common practice among Chosŏn magazines of operating at a loss until folding, deeming it shameful for a cultural enterprise. To ensure the magazine's longevity, they are adopting a more financially viable model (approx. 200 pages for 30 chŏn) and ask for readers' understanding, promising carefully selected content as compensation. For historians, this piece vividly illustrates the economic difficulties faced by Korean-language publications during the colonial period. It highlights the struggle to maintain cultural platforms amidst financial pressures, reflecting both the precariousness of private Korean enterprise and a pragmatic effort to ensure the survival of a Korean cultural voice against economic odds.

編輯後
數萬 愛讀者諸位께 眞情으로 告 한가지 告白을 하겠음니다. 「三千里는 이 달부터 面數를 조곰 주리었음니다. 只今 朝鮮의 諸雜誌는 生産費를 度外視하고 每月 巨額의 돈을 밋저가며 하여가는 것이 大部分이외다.

수판을 度外視하는 까닭에 돈이 있을 때까지는 하고 없으면 廢刊해 버림니다. 過去 십수년의 歷史가 전혀 이것이로소이다. 이것은 文明民族의 文化事業으론 큰 恥辱이외다.

었더케 하면 雜誌를 오래 할가 그러자면 수판이 맛도록 冊을 만들어 내야 함이외다. 그러자면 2백여面標準으로 30錢 밧는 雜誌를 꾸미는 수밧에 었음니다.

愛讀者諸位여, 永遠한 持久的 刊行을 위하야 本誌面數 주린 것을 용서하실 줄 밋슴니다. 그  代身 精選編輯으로서 當코저함니다.
기리기리 愛讀하여 주옵소서. <250>
