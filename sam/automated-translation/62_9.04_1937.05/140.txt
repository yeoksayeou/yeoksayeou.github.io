Mr. Cho Pyŏnggap Who Donated to Posŏng, Yŏnhŭi, and Severance Colleges; Service to Education

Recently, for educational funds:
Yŏnhŭi Professional School (延禧專門學校) 20,000 wŏn
Posŏng Professional School (普成專門學校) 20,000 wŏn
Severance Professional School (世富蘭偲專門學校) 20,000 wŏn
Total 60,000 wŏn

Who is this Mr. Cho Pyŏnggap (趙炳甲) who moved the public by donating this large sum of 60,000 wŏn to these three schools? We wish to introduce his character and express our gratitude for his sincerity towards education.

Mr. Cho is the second son of Mr. Cho Chŏngyun (趙鼎允), a renowned family of generations in Hwanggŭmchŏng (黃金町), Seoul. After finishing elementary school at Suha-chŏng (水下町) Public School in the city, he graduated top of his class with honors from the First Higher Common School (第一高等普通學校). He then entered the Imperial University (帝國大學) to study in the medical department but withdrew during his second year due to family matters. He has since been active in the financial world and social enterprises, a young businessman currently 29 years old this spring. His sharp intellect meant he always placed first during his school days, astonishing his peers (儕輩). Furthermore, having entered the financial sphere, the fact that he achieved enormous profits this spring, riding the wave of the business boom, has astonished even senior figures in the financial world.

It is said that this donation of 60,000 wŏn as scholarship funds to the three colleges is just the first step, and he intends to show even greater sincerity for the education of talented individuals in the future. In these times of educational scarcity, when truly talented children (英材 駿兒) fill the streets (衒衢), how can this generation adequately express its gratitude upon encountering Mr. Cho?

Yi Yong'ik and Palace Attendant Cho Chŏngyun

Mr. Cho Pyŏnggap's respected father is Mr. Cho Chŏngyun. He previously served as the magistrate of Sŏngch'ŏn county, Changjin, and several other counties, before being promoted to an inner office position. He then served as Vice-Chief Aide-de-Camp (侍從院 副卿) and later Chief Aide-de-Camp (侍從院卿) in the Sijongwŏn, entering the palace to serve Emperor Kojong (李太王). Mr. Cho Chŏngyun, thus active in politics and the royal court, also had another illustrious giant (巨星) in his family: Yi Yong'ik (李容翊), Minister of the Royal Treasury (內藏院卿), was his brother-in-law. Regarding Yi Yong'ik, who possessed great talent for his time, he is too famous to warrant tedious explanation (煩說) here. In any case, at that time, Mr. Cho Chŏngyun and Mr. Yi Yong'ik, as paired attendants related by marriage (brother-in-law and sister's husband), worked diligently for the peace and stability of the state (社稷) both within the court (宮中) and the government (府中). As the sun and moon passed afterwards, Mr. Cho withdrew from the palace, enjoying only leisure like a "leisured cloud and wild crane" (閑雲野鶴), making companions of ten thousand volumes of poetry and books. He is now an old man of 84 this spring.

Furthermore, when the Sŏbuk Hyŏpsŏng School (西北協成學校) was established, Mr. Cho Chŏngyun donated 10,000 wŏn for the school building construction fund. He also showed his passion for educational endeavors by unsparingly (앗김없이) donating large sums of many tens of thousands of wŏn multiple times to private village schools and public schools in his hometown in Hamnam (咸南). Besides (그밧게) that, whenever the calamities faced by his compatriots were severe, such as the Great Flood of 1925 (乙丑年) and the floods in the three southern provinces (3南 水害), he never failed to extend his love for his countrymen with donations of thousands or tens of thousands of wŏn.

Moreover, the esteemed elder's eldest grandson, Mr. Cho Taeha (趙大河), is a famous businessman in South Hamgyŏng Province, active in the Changjin region. The white-haired (鶴髮) elder Mr. Cho [Chŏngyun] is enjoying his remaining years, quietly maintaining his pure integrity (淸節) amidst the prosperity of his descendants.<19>

**Summary**

The article reports on the significant donation of 60,000 wŏn by Cho Pyŏnggap, a 29-year-old businessman, split equally among Posŏng College, Yŏnhŭi College, and Severance Medical College in 1937 Seoul. It details Cho Pyŏnggap's elite education (First Higher Common School, brief study at Imperial University) and recent success in business. The article also profiles his father, Cho Chŏngyun, an 84-year-old former high-ranking court official (Chief Aide-de-Camp) under Emperor Kojong and brother-in-law of the influential Yi Yong'ik (Minister of the Royal Treasury). Cho Chŏngyun himself is noted for past philanthropic activities, particularly in education. Historically, the article is interesting as it showcases a wealthy Korean family navigating the colonial period. It highlights continuity between the old elite (Cho Chŏngyun's court service) and new forms of influence (Cho Pyŏnggap's modern education and business success), alongside a focus on educational philanthropy as a socially acceptable and praised activity for Koreans of means under Japanese rule. It provides insight into the institutions (specific schools, Imperial University), social structures (elite families, business circles), and values (education, philanthropy, respect for elders) of the time.

普專, 延專, 世專에 義捐한 趙炳甲씨 인물, 교육봉사
얼마 전에 교육자금으로서
연희전문학교 2만원
보성전문학교 2만원
셰부란스전문 2만원
합계 6만원
이리하야 6만원의 거금을 셰 학부에 義捐하야 세인을 감격케 한 趙炳甲 씨란 엇던 분인가. 우리는 씨의 인격을 소개하야 그 교육을 위한 성의에 사의를 표할 바인가 한다.

씨는 서울 黃金町의 세대명문 趙鼎允 씨의 차남으로서 일직이 시내 水下町 보통학교를 마친 뒤 第一高等普通學校를 수석의 우등으로 졸업하고 다시 帝國大學에 입학하야 의학부에서 수학하든 중 2학년 때에 가사관계로 퇴학한 뒤 재계와 사회사업에 활약하는 今春 29세의 청년 실업가이다. 두뇌 명석하야 학창시대에 늘 수석으로 지내어 儕輩를 놀래게 하엿거니와 일단 발을 재계 방면에 投하야 또한 今春에 재계 호경기의 물결에 어울녀 거대한 수익을 거두었든 사실은 재계 선배를 놀래이게 한 바이다.
말하자면 이번에 3대학부에 장학 기금으로 이 6만원을 낸 것은 제1보로서 앞으로 영재교육을 위하야 더 많은 성의를 보일 것이다. 교육 기근에 처하여 그야말로 영재 駿兒가 衒衢에 차 잇는 이 때 씨를 맛난 이 세대는 얼마나 감사의 意를 표하면 다 하랴.

李容翊 씨와 더부러 宮中侍臣 趙鼎允

그런데 趙炳甲씨의 엄친은 趙鼎允씨로서, 일즉히 成川 군수와 長津 등 數三 군의 군수를 지내고 다시 內職으로 昇次하엿다가 侍從院 副卿을 거처 侍從院卿이 되어 궁중에 드러가 李太王을 보시고 게섯다. 이와 같이 정계와 궁중에서 활약하든 趙鼎允씨는 그 집안에 또 한 혁혁한 巨星을 가지고 있었으니 內藏院卿 李容翊씨는 씨의 매부라. 당대 偉才을 품은 李容翊씨에 대하여는 너무나 유명함으로 여기에 煩說을 피하거니와 엇잿든 그 때 趙鼎允씨와 李容翊씨는 처남, 매부의 손마진 侍臣으로 宮中 府中에서 사직의 安泰를 위하야 많은 활약을 하엿섯다. 그 뒤 日月이 흘너가매 씨는 궁중으로부터 물너나와 오직 閑雲野鶴을 벗하면서 즐기는 만 권 詩書와 벗을 삼어 지낸다. 今春 84세의 노옹이다.
그런데 趙鼎允씨는 일즉 西北協成學校가 설치되자 校舍 건축 기금으로 1만원을 義捐하엿고 咸南의 고향 사숙과 보통학교에도 屢 巨萬의 大金을 앗김없이 던지어 교육사업에 열의을 보엿스며 그밧게 乙丑年과 3南 水害 등 동포의 참화가 매우 클 때마다 수천 수만의 돈으로서 동포애를 베풀기를 마지 안었다.
더욱 옹의 장손 趙大河 씨는 함경남도의 유명한 실업가로 長津지방에서 활약하고 있는데 鶴髮 노옹의 씨는 자손 번영한 속에서 고요히 깨긋한 淸節을 직히면서 여생을 즐기고 있다.<19>
