Article Title: 東京大會 갓가워온다!! (The Tokyo Games are drawing near!!)
Author: Not specified
Article Type: 잡저 (Miscellaneous writing/Essay)

The Shōwa 15 (1940) Tokyo Olympics are also drawing near. With the competition venue decided as Meiji Jingū Gaien (明治神宮外苑), preparations are busily underway day and night to call the youth of the entire world. When one thinks about it, even considering yesterday's Pŏllim (伯林 - Berlin) Games, despite Germany's national finances being extremely meager at the time, they spent over 30 million Marks to create unprecedented facilities, deeply impressing the youth of the entire world. As we anticipate the success of the Tokyo Games three years from now, we recall the events of the Pŏllim Games, which approach their first anniversary... <26>

**Summary**

This short 1937 article expresses anticipation for the upcoming 1940 Tokyo Olympics. It highlights the designation of Meiji Jingū Gaien as the venue and the ongoing preparations. The author draws a parallel with the 1936 Berlin Olympics, noting how Germany, despite poor finances, invested heavily to create impressive facilities, suggesting a similar expectation of grandeur for Tokyo. For historians studying Korean colonial history, this piece reveals the perspective within Korea (published in a Korean magazine, albeit under Japanese rule) looking towards a major event hosted by the colonial power. It shows an awareness of international events like the Olympics and implicitly positions the upcoming Tokyo Games as a significant, anticipated spectacle, reflecting the broader integration into the Japanese imperial sphere, even in cultural and sporting events.

東京大會 갓가워온다!!
昭和 15년(1940)의 東京 올림픽도 갓가워 온다. 대회장을 明治神宮外苑에 정하고 전 세계의 젊은이를 부르러 주야 분주 중이다. 생각컨대 昨日의 伯林대회를 생각하여도, 국가 재정이 몹시 빈약한 때를 불구하고 독일은 3천 수백만 馬克을 지출하여 未曾有의 설비를 하여, 전 세계의 젊은이를 감격케 하엿든 것이다. 우리는 3년 후 東京대회의 성황을 예상하면서, 1주년이 갓가워오는 伯林대회때 일을 회상한다... <26>
