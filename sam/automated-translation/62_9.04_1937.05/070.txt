5 Million Won Investment by Koreans; Major Chaebols like Messrs. Kong Chinhang, Min Kyusik, Kim Yŏnsu Advance and Invest in the 5 Years Since the Incident

Before Shōwa 7 (1932), there was hardly any land owned by Koreans worth mentioning on the Manchurian plains, nor were there many notable Korean entrepreneurs settled there. However, in the five years since the Manchurian Incident (事變), as public order has gradually been established in various regions, Korean investors carrying "gold" have increasingly crossed the Yalu and Tumen rivers from within Korea. Consequently, not only have many people come to own land in key locations within the city limits or suburbs of major cities like the capital Hsinking (新京), Fengtien (奉天), and Harbin (哈爾賓), but Korean-owned fields, forests, and mines have appeared further inland in places like Mudanjiang (牡丹江), Binjiang Province (濱江省), and even stretching into Sanjiang Province (三江省) and Heilongjiang Province (黑龍江省).

According to a reliable investigation, the total investment from the Korean side amounts to no less than (少不下) 5 million won. Among these, some investments were made through organized companies, while others were by individuals. First, let's examine the major investors.

500,000 Won of Manmong Industrial Co., Ltd. (滿蒙産業株式會社)

The first to be noted is the Manmong Industrial Co., Ltd., established three years ago with 500,000 won, centered around businessmen from Kaesŏng (開城). Although the company's paid-in capital (不入) was 250,000 won, they secured loans doubling this amount, bringing the total investment to about 500,000 won in the vicinity of Hsinking (新京), Heilongjiang Province (黑龍江省), and throughout North Manchuria (北滿). The company president is Mr. Kim Chŏngho (金正浩), and the vice president is Mr. Kong Chinhang (孔鎭恒). Mr. Kong, a young businessman and graduate of Berlin University in Germany, personally handled the investments meticulously, traveling the North Manchurian (北滿) sites wearing straw sandals and white clothes.

200,000 Won of Tongbang Colonization Co., Ltd. (東邦殖産株式會社)

This company, with Mr. Min Kyusik (閔奎植) as the major shareholder, is a business entity focused on advancing into Manchuria. Having focused early on land aspects, it has already invested approximately 200,000 won in the Kando (間島) region, where 600,000 Korean farmers (鮮農) reside.

150,000 Won by Mr. Ha Chunsŏk (河駿錫)

This investment is by Mr. Ha, a major entrepreneur whose name is well-known (선자한) throughout the Kyŏngnam region, primarily in the North Manchuria (北滿) area. The money already invested is said to be 150,000 won.

300,000 Won by Mr. Kim Yŏnsu (金䄵洙)

This is also an individual investment by Mr. Kim Yŏnsu (金䄵洙) of Haedong Bank (海東銀行), focused on densely populated areas of South Manchuria (南滿) such as Yingying [likely Yingkou] (營營) and Fengtien (奉天). Already, 300,000 won has been invested. It is rumored that he intends to invest up to one million won solely on his own.

300,000 Won by Mr. Kim Tongwŏn (金東元)

Already renowned as a rubber industry entrepreneur in P'yŏngyang, Mr. Kim has established a large farm called P'yŏngan Farm (平安農場) near the investment area of Mr. Kim Yŏnsu (金䄵洙). The investment amount is estimated at 200,000 won. [Note: Text says 300,000 Won in the header but 200,000 Won in the description.]

50,000 Won by Mr. Pang Kyuhwan (方奎煥)

Mr. Pang Kyuhwan (方奎煥), a businessman from Sindang-chō (新堂町) [modern Sindang-dong] in Seoul, has personally invested about 50,000 won.

200,000 Won of Tongman Agricultural Development Co., Ltd. (東滿農事開發株式會社)

Originally rooted in the Kando (間島) region, Mr. Hong, as the principal figure, invested proactively after the Incident (事變), primarily in the Kando (間島) area centered around Yanji (延吉) and in Binjiang Province (濱江省). Besides these, although the figures are not precise, Mr. Kim Kidŏk (金基德) has also invested several hundred thousand won in land and forests through a general partnership (合名會社) where he is stationed (주재하는). The famous Mr. Kim Hongnyang (金鴻亮) of Anak (安岳) is also investing large sums in mining and land. Furthermore, former Sidae Ilbo (時代日報) president Mr. An Hŭijae (安熙濟) and other influential businessmen from within Korea have invested large amounts.

What is particularly noteworthy is that as entrepreneurs' interest in Manchukuo (滿洲國) steadily increases, future investment is expected to advance exponentially. It is seen as not merely a fantasy that the total will reach several tens of millions of won within (不出) a few years. <11>

**Summary**

This 1937 article from *Samch'ŏlli* magazine details the significant surge in Korean investment into Manchuria (Manchukuo) in the five years following the Manchurian Incident of 1931. It reports a total investment of at least 5 million won by Koreans, highlighting major individual investors like Kim Yŏnsu, Kong Chinhang, Min Kyusik, Ha Chunsŏk, Kim Tongwŏn, and companies such as Manmong Industrial Co., Tongbang Colonization Co., and Tongman Agricultural Development Co. These investments spanned land, agriculture, mining, and forestry across various regions, including Hsinking, Fengtien, Harbin, Kando, and deeper into North Manchuria. The article anticipates this trend will continue, potentially reaching tens of millions of won soon. From a historical perspective, the article is revealing about the economic activities of the Korean elite under Japanese colonial rule. It shows how some Korean capitalists actively participated in the economic expansion into Manchuria, facilitated by the Japanese occupation, seeking opportunities for wealth accumulation in agriculture, industry, and resource extraction, sometimes operating in parallel or conjunction with Japanese colonial interests. Historians might find the specific names, investment amounts, company structures, and geographical focus valuable for understanding Korean capital's role and strategies within the broader Japanese empire, including aspects of collaboration, opportunism, and the economic dimensions of colonialism.

조선인의 투자 5백만원, 事變之後 5개년 사이에 孔鎭恒·閔奎植·金䄵洙씨 등 큰 재벌  진출 투자
昭和7년 이전에는 滿洲 벌판에 조선인의 토지라고 별로 손꼽을 것이 없고, 거기에 토착한 조선인 기업가라고 또한 별로 업더fj니, 滿洲事變 후 5개년 사이에 각지의 치안이 점점 확립하여감에 따라서 압록강과 두만강을 건너 황금』을 질머진 조선내의 투자가가 작고 건너 드러가고 있다. 그래서 지금은 수도 新京의 시가지와 또 奉天, 哈爾賓의 주요도시마다 시가지나 교외의 樞要地의 토지를 가지고 있는 사람도 만해젓슬뿐더러 牡丹江이고 濱江省이고 좀 더 드러가 三江省, 黑龍江省, 일대에까지 조선인 소유의 전답이 있고 삼림이 있고 광산이 있게 되엿다.
이제 엇든 확실한 측의 조사에 의하면 조선 사람측의 투자총액이 少不下 5백만원을 헤인다는데 그 중에는 회사를 조직하고서 투재해 논 곳도 있고 또는 개인으로 투자한 이도 있는데 爲先 중요 투자가를 조사하건대 이러하다.

滿蒙産業株式會社의 50만원

첫재 손꼽을 것은 開城 사업가들이 중심되여 3년 전에 50만원의 滿蒙産業株式會社를 조직하엿는데 이 회사의 불입은 25만원이나 불입에 倍되는 차입금을 어더 합계 50만원 정도의 투자를 新京 부근과 黑龍江省 등지와 北滿 일대에 하엿는데 회사 사장은 金正浩 씨요, 부사장은 孔鎭恒 씨라. 孔씨는 독일 伯林大學 출신인 청년 실업가로서, 北滿 현지에 메투리신고 힌옷 입고 다니며 제손으로 꼭꼭 투자한 터이다.

東邦殖産株式會社의 20만원

이 회사는 閔奎植 씨를 대주주로 한 滿洲 진출의 사업회사로서 일즉 토지방면에 착안하여 60만 鮮農이 살고 있는 間島 방면에 벌서 약 20만원을 投하엿다.

河駿錫 씨의 15만원

경남 일대의 큰 기업가로 그 일홈이 선자한 河씨의 투자로서 주로 北滿 방면인데 이미 집어 너흔 돈이 15만원이라한다.

金䄵洙 씨의 30만원

이것도 개인 투자로서 海東銀行의 金䄵洙씨가, 營營, 奉天 등 南滿의 인수 밀집에 투자한 것으로, 벌서 30만원을 너헛다. 들리는 말에 백만원까지의 투자는 독력으로 할 작정을 하고 있다든가.

金東元 씨의 30만원

평양서 고무공업의 기업가로 이미 저명한 씨는 平安農場이란 큰 농장을 역시 金䄵洙씨의 투자지 부근에 셰어 두엇는데 투자액 20만원을 헤인다고 한다.

方奎煥 씨의 5만원

서울 新堂町의 실업가 方奎煥씨가 개인적으로 약 5만원 돈을 투자하엿다.

東滿農事開發株式會社의 20만원

원래 間島지방에 뿌리박고 있든 洪씨가 주되어 事變 후에 약진적으로 투자한 것으로 주로 延吉을 중심으로 한 間島 일대와 濱江省 등지이다. 이밧게 數字는 자세치 못하나 金基德 씨도 주재하는 合名會社의 손으로 토지, 삼림 등에 수십 만원을 투자하엿고, 安岳의 유명한 金鴻亮 씨도 광산방면과 토지방면에 거액을 投하고 있으며, 전 時代日報 사장 安熙濟 씨 등 기타 유력한 조선 내 실업가로서 거액을 投한 이가 만타.
더욱 주목할 바는 기업가의 滿洲國에 대한 관심이 점점 많어저 감에 따라 금후는 그 투자가 기하급수적으로 약진하야, 不出 수년에 수천 만원을 數하게 될 것은 공상이 아니라고 보여진다. <11>
