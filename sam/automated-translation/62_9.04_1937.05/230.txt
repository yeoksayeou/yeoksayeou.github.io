Article Title: 환락의 서울, 新裝한 사교장 白馬를 차저! (Seoul of Pleasure, Seeking Out the Newly Equipped Social Club Paengma!)
Article Type: 잡저 (Miscellaneous writing)

Seoul of Pleasure, Seeking Out the Newly Equipped Social Club Paengma!

The dreary winter season has somehow vanished, and it's the boom of life when sweet sap rises in the dry branches of the desolate wilderness! The boom that calls to us from stuffy rooms, amidst the azaleas and forsythia between the green grass! Spring, when the drooping willows embrace the East Wind and dance gracefully! Even the sound of the stream water flowing ceaselessly makes our hearts leap, and from the low sky, the skylark also calls to our souls in this bright season!

Spring, when the peach blossoms tempt us ticklishly, and swallows begin to chirp in our restless hearts carried by the wind! Breathing in this season, I turned my steps toward the blooming pleasure district. First, to Pukch'on, I went seeking the social club Paengma (白馬), luxuriously newly equipped by purely Korean hands. Paengma was designed by Pak Kilyong (朴吉龍), the darling of the architectural world, using high-grade materials and techniques born of modern scientific civilization, built with the not insignificant gold sum of 70,000 won.

Having broken ground last August and completed construction only on March 5th this year, anyone who sees the appearance of this Paengma will marvel at how splendidly brilliant and elegant it is on the outside. Not only that, but when one thoroughly surveys the sanitary toilets, steam (스틤) heating facilities, emergency exits, and all various interior facilities, one cannot help but marvel again. Four stories including the basement! Just the electrical decorations cost the large sum of over 8,000 won, so can one not imagine how dazzlingly beautiful it must have become? On the upper floor veranda, lush green trees make the customer's heart green, and they say they will soon build a fountain on the veranda as well, which will provide an even cooler feeling. The beautiful melody (멜노듸) flowing from the electric phonograph and, under the variously brilliant neon signs (네온싸인) like a Fairyland (仙境), the affectionate and kind service (써비쓰) of the beautiful professional women swimming like white fish (白魚)! Among these women, there is said to be a dancer (땐사-) who was at a certain foreign dance hall, a lovely 18-year-old girl who was part of dancer Pae Kuja's (裵龜子) troupe, a considerably intellectual woman, and also a kind-hearted woman who, to ensure her younger brother's success, diligently saved the small sums tossed her way by customers and enrolled him in a certain university in Tokyo.

Newly equipped great nature! Newly equipped Paengma, the sound of music flowing within Paengma! Whispers of love! The group of about 50 beautiful waitresses (女給群)—the gentle waves created by the light skirts of women who make a man's heart race. Lips like roses. Their teeth, white as gourd flesh, revealed whenever they smile softly. Their eyes, clear as a lake. Their fragrant hands, like powder, pouring drinks for the customers. Ever since opening, it's always full! It's said that the waitresses (워트레쓰) and other employees are busy with no time to catch their breath, so can one not easily imagine the state of the social club Paengma? <35>

**Summary**

This 1937 article enthusiastically describes the opening of "Paengma," a luxurious new social club in Seoul's Pukch'on area, designed by architect Pak Kilyong and built entirely with Korean capital and modern techniques. It highlights the club's opulent exterior, modern amenities like steam heating and electrical decorations, and the planned addition of a veranda fountain. The author paints a vivid picture of the club's atmosphere, emphasizing the beautiful music, neon lights, and especially the charm and diverse backgrounds of the approximately 50 waitresses, portraying it as a pinnacle of modern entertainment and social life in Seoul. From a historical perspective, the article is interesting for its depiction of burgeoning modernity and consumer culture in colonial Seoul, showcasing Korean entrepreneurship (Korean-funded and built) within the colonial context. It also offers a glimpse into the changing social landscape, particularly the presence and diverse circumstances of women working in new service industry roles, reflecting both opportunity and necessity during that era.

환락의 서울, 新裝한 사교장 白馬를 차저!
우울한 겨울철도 어느듯 스러지고 적막한 황야의 마른 나무가지에 단물이 오르는 생명의 붐! 푸른 잔듸 사이사이 진달내꼿 개나리꼿 침침한 방에서 우리를 부르는 붐! 휘느러진 수양버들이 東風을 안고 멋드러지게 춤을 추는 봄! 철철 흘너 내리는 시내물 소리도 우리의 마음을 뛰게 하고 나즉한 天空에서 종달새도 우리의 넋을 부르는 화창한 계절이다!
복사꼿이 우리를 간즈럽게 유혹하고 바람을 타고 도는 들석한 우리의 가슴 속에는 제비떼가 지저귀기 시작하는 봄! 나는 이 시절을 호흡하는 꼿피는 환락가로 발길을 옴겨 보앗다. 먼저 北村으로, 순 조선인의 손으로 호화롭게 新裝한 사교장 白馬를 차저 갓다. 白馬는 건축계의 총아 朴吉龍 씨의 설계로 현대과학문명이 나은 고급 재료와 기술을 이용하여 7만원이라는 적지 안은 황금으로 만드렀단다.
작년 8월 달에 기공하여 금년 3월 5일에야 낙성한 이 白馬의 면모는 누가 보던지 감탄하리만치 것흐로 화려 찬란하고 아담할 뿐 아니라 위생변소와 스틤설비, 비상구 제반 내부설비를 이모저모 속속드리 휘둘너 불 때 또 다시 감탄을 금할 수 없으리라. 지하실까지 4층! 전기장식만 해도 8천여 원 이란 큰돈이 들엇으니 얼마나 분부시게 아름답게 되엿는가를 짐작할 것이 아닌가! 우층 베란다에는 푸르청청한 나무가 손님의 마음을 푸르게 하고 不遠間 베란다에 또 분수구를 만들이라 하니 한층 더 시원한 늣김을 줄 것이다. 전기축음기에서 흘너 나오는 아름다운 멜노듸와 仙境과 같은 각색 찬란한 네온싸인 아래 白魚와 같이 헤염치는 꼿다운 직업 여성들의 情이 뚝뚝 덧는 친절한 써비쓰! 이분들 중에는 외국 모 스땐홀에 있든 땐사-도 있고 무용가 裵龜子 일행 속에 끼엿든 어엽분 18의 소녀도, 또 상당한 인테리 여성도, 내 동생을 출세식히기 위하야 손들이 한두닙 던저주는 적은 돈을 알뜰살뜰이 모아가지고 東京 모대학에 입학식힌 맘 착한 여성도 있단다.

新裝한 대자연! 新裝한 白馬, 白馬 속에 흐르는 음악소리! 사랑의 속삭임! 50여명의 아름다운 女給群-사나히 심장을 뛰게하는 여성들의 가벼운 초마자락에 생기는 부드러운 물결. 장미같은 입술. 가많이 우슬 때마다 드러나는 박속같은 하얀 그들의 니빨. 호수같이 맑은 그들의눈동자. 손님들에게 술을 따러 주는 분길같은 그들의 향기러운 손길. 개업 후로 늘상 만원! 워트레쓰 외 다른 종업원도 눈코 뜰 새 없이 밧부다 하니 사교장 白馬의 면모를 가이 짐작할 것이 아닌가. <35>
