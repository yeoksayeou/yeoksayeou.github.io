Samch'ŏlli Special Edition: Seoul Rumors

*Tonga Ilbo* Lifting of Ban Period?

Various Speculations and Subsequent Circumstances

What has become of the *Tonga Ilbo*'s suspension, which has lasted for the long period of 10 months from August last year until this month? Whereas the first, second, and third suspensions all lasted around 40 days to 4 or 5 months, this time it has reached nearly double that, 10 months, and it is unknown what will happen going forward.
Looking at the recent situation, the issue seems to hinge on the selection of a president, the representative figure who will preside over and lead the newspaper company. That is, during this time, the newspaper company has been requesting understanding from the authorities (當局) to quickly save the large number of employees from collective unemployment and to revive the newspaper, a public instrument (公器), as soon as possible. Each time, following the resignation taking responsibility by former executives like Mr. Song Chin-u (宋鎭禹), the selection of a president to take responsibility and break through this difficult situation has become the issue. Thus, the newspaper company attempted to nominate Mr. Yang Wŏn-mo (梁源模), but after this effort failed due to the implication that Mr. Yang was also a former executive, difficulties also arose in the selection of Messrs. Paek Kwan-su (白寬洙) and Kim Pyŏng-no (金炳魯). Recently, Mr. Mun O-u (文O宇) of the Haedong Bank (海東銀行) was recommended, but he declined. Therefore, recently, Posŏng College (普專) professor Ok OO (玉OO) and Kim O-mu (金O茂) were put forward, but it remains to be seen whether the authorities will show understanding towards these two gentlemen.

Ultimately, Will Kim Sŏng-su (金性洙) Step Forward? - Isn't This the Only Way to Resolution?

According to a certain influential source, it is observed that the lifting of the ban on the *Tonga Ilbo* seems unlikely unless Mr. Kim Sŏng-su, the benefactor who built the newspaper company into the great enterprise it is today, steps forward. It appears that the only person who can resolve the current emergency is Mr. Kim himself, who would not only bear the legal responsibility as editor and publisher but also take full spiritual and financial responsibility and strive for the resumption of publication. Mr. Kim Sŏng-su has the trust within the company, social standing, and is a figure whom the authorities would understand to some extent. For this reason, those close to Mr. Kim are making various efforts to persuade him to step forward, drawing attention to what his state of mind might be.

Rumor of Mitsukoshi Branch Office on the Site of Anguk Pŏlgyung Palace

Attention on Big Conglomerate's Advance into Pukch'on

It was previously reported that the old site of Anguk Pŏlgyung Palace (安國別宮), formerly owned by the Office of the Yi Royal Household (李王職), was decided to be sold off to the private sector, and Mr. Ch'oe Ch'ang-hak (崔昌學) purchased 600 p'yŏng at an auction price of 201 won per p'yŏng. Now, according to news heard, the department store "Mitsukoshi" (三越) located in Honmachi (本町) plans to acquire this site and build a Mitsukoshi branch office, intending to advance into the Pukch'on (北村) area. Rumors of Mitsukoshi advancing into Pukch'on have been around for several years already, stating they would construct a new store on the site of the former Chongno Police Station near the Hwasin Department Store (和信) at Chongno Intersection. Then, that talk fizzled out. Now, it is being reported with certainty. This talk is highly believable because, starting this year, it is included in the Government-General's budget; the Governor-General's official residence located at Waesŏngdae (倭城臺) below Namsan is planned to be relocated and built behind Kyŏngbok Palace, and all official residences for Bureau Director-level officials of the Political Affairs Superintendent are also set to be moved and built. Thus, the government office district (官衙街) is moving from Waesŏngdae to Pukch'on, including Anguk-tong (安國町) and Hyoja-tong (孝子町). Accordingly, major commercial districts will naturally move to Pukch'on, and it is presumed that following Mitsukoshi's lead, there will be a successive advance of Honmachi merchants into Pukch'on from now on.

Personal Deposit of 2.5 Million Won

Mr. Wŏn Yun-su (元胤洙) at the Chŏksan Bank

The Paengnyŏn Gold Mine (百年金鑛) in Koksan (谷山), Hwanghae Province (黃海道), known for producing tungsten (당그스뎅), i.e., graphite (흑연), which was highly valued as war material, has long had its reputation grow. It was recently sold for 2.5 million won to Kobayashi Mining (小林鑛業所), and its owner is the wealthy Mr. Wŏn Yun-su of Namdaemun-an, Seoul. As the president of the Chŏksan Bank (殖産銀行), Mr. Ariga Mitsutoyo (有賀光豊), was involved in arranging this mine sale, Mr. Wŏn deposited the entire sale proceeds of 2.5 million won into the Chŏksan Bank. Depositing such a huge sum as 2.5 million won all at once by a private individual is likely a rare occurrence. <34>

"Nagŭnae" Profit of 20,000 Won

Production Cost Around 9,000 Won

The talkie Chosŏn film "Nagŭnae" (나그네 - The Wanderer), which premiered and is showing at Seoul's Umi-gwan (優美舘) and Meiji-za (明治座) theaters, drawing extraordinary popularity, originally cost only about 9,000 won to produce. In just 8 or 9 days in Seoul, it has already seen a profit (利) of over 20,000 won. Furthermore, sources say that if it is shown in about 40 domestic movie theaters in places like Taegu (대구) and P'yŏngyang (평양), and also screened throughout Manchukuo (滿洲國), it will likely earn a profit of almost 40,000 to 50,000 won. Regarding this film, in mainland Japan (內地), the Shōchiku-affiliated (松竹系) S-system will screen it throughout the mainland, while screenings in Chosŏn (朝鮮) and Manchukuo are to be handled by Seoul's Koryŏ Film Distribution Center (高麗映畵配給所), presided over by Mr. Yi Ch'ang-yong (李創用). Due to the success of this work, figures in the film industry are greatly stirred (動心), anticipating that Chosŏn cinema will make epochal (劃時期的) progress henceforth.

Advertising Expenses of 2,000 Won

Unprecedented Just for Seoul

Furthermore, according to sources, not only was the film itself a masterpiece, but unprecedented effort was also put into advertising "Nagŭnae," spending 2,000 won on advertising, including airplanes, in Seoul alone.

Publication by T'aeyangsa

Minch'on's "Human Training"

Mr. Yi Ki-yŏng's (李箕永) "In'gan Suŏp" (인간수업 - Human Training), for which Hansŏng Books (漢城圖書) had already obtained publication permission with the intention to publish, has recently changed plans. It is now set to be published by T'aeyangsa (太陽社) at 110 Kyŏnji-dong (堅志町), Seoul, which newly appeared, and is expected to be released in May.

Chongno Main Street: 1,000 Won Per P'yŏng

Rising Seoul Land Prices

Seoul land prices can be seen as 폭등 (skyrocketing). Compared to the time after the European Great War when they were cheapest, they have risen threefold on average. One reason might be the favorable condition of the financial world (財界), but the city planning, involving spending 6 million won to整備 (organize/improve) the new road network of Great Kyŏngsŏng (大京城), and the expansion of Kyŏngsŏng Prefecture (京城府) to 700,000 people, are also reasons fueling the land speculation (土地 景氣). Enumerating the places with expensive land prices within Seoul:
Around 1,000 won per p'yŏng = Vicinity of Posin-gak Pavilion (普信閣) in front of Hwasin (和信) at Chongno Intersection; the initial entrance (於口) to Honmachi-dōri (本町通) from the Bank of Chosŏn (朝鮮銀行); Namdaemun Intersection.
Around 300 won per p'yŏng = Hwanggeum-chŏng 3-chōme (黃金町 三丁目); the area near the Meiji-chō Stock and Rice Market (明治町株式期米市場); entrance to Uk-chŏng (旭町); the roadside from Hinode Elementary School (日之出小學校) towards Uk-chŏng; Anguk-tong Intersection; and near Sŏdaemun.
Furthermore, noteworthy is that the Yŏngdŭngp'o (永登浦) area, newly emerging as a factory zone, now has places ranging from 60 won up to 150 or 160 won per p'yŏng.

Yŏ Un-hyŏng (呂運亨) Round Trip to Tokyo

Traveled for Some Reason

Mr. Yŏ Un-hyŏng, who was the president of the Chungang Ilbo (中央日報), hastily went to Tokyo in early April and returned recently. The reason is not known in detail, but according to his younger brother, Mr. Yŏ Un-hong (呂運弘), his son graduated from school this spring, so he took him there to enroll him in a certain professional school (專門學校). Upon hearing this, some remark that if it were for the child's school admission, he could have asked an acquaintance in Tokyo to arrange such a matter without having to go himself... <35>

**Summary**

This article from May 1937 compiles various rumors and news items circulating in Seoul (Kyŏngsŏng). It discusses the prolonged suspension of the *Tonga Ilbo* newspaper and the difficulties in appointing a new president acceptable to the authorities, speculating that founder Kim Sŏng-su might be the only solution. It also covers real estate developments, such as the rumored plan for the Mitsukoshi department store to build a branch on the former Anguk Palace site due to the northward shift of government offices, and the general rise in land prices, particularly in Chongno and newly developing areas like Yŏngdŭngp'o. Other news includes a large mining deal involving Wŏn Yun-su, the financial success and advertising push of the Korean film "Nagŭnae," a planned publication by Yi Ki-yŏng, and a recent trip to Tokyo by former newspaper president Yŏ Un-hyŏng, the purpose of which is questioned. From a historical perspective, the article offers insights into the tight control exercised by the Japanese colonial authorities over the Korean press (*Tonga Ilbo* suspension), the economic dynamics involving Japanese capital (Mitsukoshi, Chŏksan Bank, Kobayashi Mining) and Korean elites (Kim Sŏng-su, Wŏn Yun-su, Ch'oe Ch'ang-hak), the nascent Korean film industry's struggles and successes, and the socio-political movements hinted at by Yŏ Un-hyŏng's activities.

東亞日報 解禁期?

구구한 추측과 其後 정황

작년 8월 이래 이달까지 10개월이란 長期에 미치는 東亞日報의 停刊은 그 뒤에 엇지되었는가. 제1차 때나, 제2차, 제3차 때에 모다 40일 내지 4, 5개월 정도었는데 이번은 거지 반 배가 되는 10개월의 장기에 미치었고 또 앞으로도 엇더케 될는지 모른다.
이제 최근의 정황을 살펴 보건데 문제는 신문사를 主宰 통솔할 대표자인 사장 人選 여하에 달녀있는 듯하다. 즉 그동안 신문사로서는 다수한 사원의 집단적 실업을 하로 급히 구하고, 또 신문이란 公器를 하로 급히 살니려고 당국에 향하야 양해를 청하여 왓다. 그럴 때마다 宋鎭禹 씨 등 前 간부의 引責 총사직 뒤를 이어 책임을 지고서 이 난국을 타개할 사장의 人選如何가 문제되여 왓다. 그래서 신문사편으로서는 그동안 梁源模 씨를 추대하려 햇스나 梁씨 亦 前간부란 의미에서 공작이 不如意하게 된 뒤를 이어 또 白寬洙, 金炳魯 兩氏의 인선에도 난점이 있어 오다가 최근에 이르러 海東銀行의 文O宇씨를 薦하엿스나 자퇴한 배 되고, 그래서 최근에는 普專 교수 玉OO와 金O茂씨를 推하엿는데 당국에서 兩氏에 대한 양해 여부는 아직 미지수이라고 볼 밧게 업다.

결국 金性洙 씨 出馬乎?-해결의 길은 이밧게 업지 안을가

일부 유력한 모 소식통의 전하는 바에 의하면, 東亞日報의 解禁은 결국 그 신문사를 금일의 大로 키워 노은 은인 金性洙씨가 출마하여, 자기가 편집 겸 발행인이란 법적 책임을 負함은 물론, 정신적 재정적으로 전 책임을 지고 속간의 노력을 하지 안는 한 다른 길이 좀처럼 보이지 안을 것 같이 관측된다. 金性洙씨면 사내의 신망도 있고, 사회적 지위도 있고, 또한 당국에서도 어느 정도로 이해하여 줄 인물로 당면의 急을 구할 분은 결국 氏 1인에 止할 모양인데 그런 까닭으로 씨의 측근의 人은 씨를 출마식히고저 여러 가지로 애쓰고 있는 바, 씨의 심경이 엇더케 될는지 주목을 끄은다.

安洞 別宮터전에 三越出張所說

대재벌의 北村 진출 주목
