Magazine Title: 三千里 第九卷 第四號
Publication Date: 1937년 05월 01일
Article Title: 돈을 먹는 出版, 「靑春」 「東光」 「朝鮮之光」 「開闢」 「朝鮮文壇」 「高麗時報」들은 얼마? (Money-Eating Publishing: How Much for "Ch'ŏngch'un," "Tonggwang," "Chosŏn chi Kwang," "Kaebyŏk," "Chosŏn Mundan," "Koryŏ Sibo"?)
Author: 木兵丁 (Mok Pyŏngjŏng)
Article Type: 잡저 (Chapchŏ - Miscellaneous writing)

Mr. Shōriki, president of the Yomiuri Shinbun (讀賣新聞), reportedly said he invested 10 won to gain one newspaper reader. This means that to obtain one monthly subscriber (月極讀者), he incurred a loss of 10 won before finally balancing the books.

From the perspective of newspaper and magazine operators, he spoke the absolute truth. Therefore, looking at the past situation within Korea, Yuktang (六堂) squandered over 30,000 won to build a readership of 2,000 for "Ch'ŏngch'un" (靑春). "Tonggwang" (東光) spent around 8,000 won before and after during the Chu Yo-han (朱耀翰) era, "Chosŏn chi Kwang" (朝鮮之光) around 20,000 won, "Kaebyŏk" (開闢) reached about 30,000 won, "Chosŏn Mundan" (朝鮮文壇) spent over 10,000 won just during the Pang In-gŭn (方仁根) era, and furthermore, the large-format "Koryŏ Sibo" (高麗時報) spent about 3,000 won over 4 or 5 printings.

As an aside, for our "Samch'ŏlli" (三千里) to obtain its current 30,000 readers, according to Shōriki's calculation, it would mean spending 300,000 won. In reality, it might even be more than that—

Even for newspapers to reach the point of breaking even as they do today, one cannot know how much dead money (死金), like fertilizer (거름), was thrown away. The Chosŏn Ilbo (朝鮮日報) required the most fertilizer; if you add up all the eras of Song Pyŏng-jun (宋秉畯), Sin Sŏk-u (申錫雨), Ch'oe Sŏn-ik (崔善益), An Chae-hong (安在鴻), Yi Ch'am-gyo (李參敎), and Im Kyŏng-nae (林景來), up to the Pang Ŭng-mo (方應謨) era, it would likely be at least 1,000,000 won by a conservative estimate. The Tonga Ilbo (東亞日報) also, combining the Pak Yŏng-hyo (朴泳孝) and Kim Sŏng-su (金性洙) eras, likely had fertilizer investments of no less than 100,000 won. The Chungang Ilbo (中央日報) too, across the various eras of Yuktang (六堂), Kyo(?), No Chŏng-il (盧正一), Yun Hŭi-jung (尹喜重), and Sŏng Hŏn-gyŏng (成憲慶), probably spent 500,000 won. From this, esteemed members of the public, you should appreciate with tears how much cultural enterprises demand. Do not take it lightly! (休得할진저) (Mok Pyŏngjŏng 木兵丁) <41>

**Summary**

The article discusses the immense financial investment and losses required to establish and sustain major Korean newspapers and magazines in the 1930s. Citing the example of the Yomiuri Shinbun president investing 10 won per reader, the author details the significant capital poured into publications like "Ch'ŏngch'un," "Tonggwang," "Kaebyŏk," "Chosŏn Ilbo," "Tonga Ilbo," and "Chungang Ilbo," often amounting to tens or even hundreds of thousands of won, likening this expenditure to "fertilizer" or "dead money." The author emphasizes the financial sacrifices inherent in cultural publishing, urging readers to appreciate these endeavors. For historians, this piece offers valuable insight into the precarious economics of the Korean publishing industry under colonial rule, quantifying the capital needed and naming key figures and publications involved. It highlights the reliance on substantial funding, likely from wealthy individuals or backers, to simply survive, let alone achieve profitability, in the cultural sphere of colonial Korea.

잡지명	삼천리 제9권 제4호
발행일	1937년 05월 01일
기사제목	돈을 먹는 出版, 「靑春」 「東光」 「朝鮮之光」 「開闢」 「朝鮮文壇」 「高麗時報」들은 얼마?
기사형태	잡저

돈을 먹는 出版, 「靑春」 「東光」 「朝鮮之光」 「開闢」 「朝鮮文壇」 「高麗時報」들은 얼마?
讀賣新聞 正力사장은 신문독자 한 명을 엇기 위해 10원식 투자햇다고한다, 이 말은 한 장의 月極독자 었으려고 밋지기를 10원식 한 뒤에 겨우 수지를 마처놋는단 말이다.
신문 잡지 업자가 보기에는 참으로 올흔 말을 햇다, 그렇기에 이제ㅡ조선 내의 과거 사정으로 볼지라도 六堂 은 「靑春」의 2천 독자를 만들기 위해 3만 여만을 탕진햇고 「東光」이 朱耀翰시대에만 전후 8천원, 「朝鮮之光」 약 2만원, 開闢에 이르러는 약 3만원, 朝鮮文壇이 方仁根시대에만 만여원, 더구나 「高麗時報」 대형 4, 5회에 약 3천원,
딴말이나 우리 「三千里」가 현재의 3만 독자를 엇기 위하얀 正力계산에 의하면 30만원을 쓴 셰음. 실제는 오히려 그 이상될는지 모르지만-
신문도 오늘날같이 수지마즐 정도로 이르기에는 참으로 그 거름(비료)으로 엇더케나 많은 죽는 돈(死金)을 날던젓는지 모른다. 가장 많이 거름이 들기는 朝鮮日報로 宋秉畯시대, 申錫雨시대, 崔善益, 安在鴻, 李參敎시대, 林景來시대, 方應謨시대까지 모다 친다면 아마 적게 처서 100만원은 될 것이요 東亞日報 亦 朴泳孝代 金性洙시대 합해서 비료적 투자가 10만원은 不下햇겟고, 中央日報도 옛날 六堂, 敎, 盧正一
尹喜重, 成憲慶의 여러 대에 50만원은 썻슬 것이다. 이로써 얼마나 문화사업이란 눈물로써 감사해야할 것임을 민중 諸君, 休得할진저.(木兵丁) <41>
