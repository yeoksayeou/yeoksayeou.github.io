**Guest Talk Room** (客談室)

On a corner of Myŏngch'ijŏng (明治町) in Seoul, after seeing the Blue Sky with a White Sun flag (청천백일기) before the Chinese Consulate General, and walking just a few steps further to Namdaemun-t'ong, the five-colored flag of Manchukuo (滿洲國旗) flutters atop the Commercial Bank (商業銀行) building. This is where Mr. Pak Yŏngch'ŏl (朴榮喆), the Head of the Commercial Bank (商銀頭取), is located, having become the honorary consul general for Manchukuo and set up a temporary office here. In any case, the sight of the new flags of old and new nations fluttering just a stone's throw (相距咫尺) from each other presents a unique scene.

While on the ferry returning from Tokyo, Mr. Yŏ Unhyŏng (呂運亨) reportedly grabbed a certain acquaintance and emphasized the great power of the film industry to influence society, lamenting the withered state (凋殘함) of the peninsula's film scene.

Among the executives (諸氏) of the Tonga Ilbo (東亞日報), <3> there is a kind of gentlemen's agreement. It is that until the newspaper is put right, they will mutually refrain from publishing writings or making statements socially. Therefore, even when visitors come, they avoid all political or social discussions, simply making topics like flowers, birds, wind, and moon (花鳥風月談) the subject of conversation.

Unable to resist the artistic impulse amid the deepening green of early summer, Ms. Na Hyesŏk (羅惠錫), who set out carrying her canvas (칸파스) from the old fortress of Suwŏn (水原古城) on a pilgrimage to the south, ended up at Haeinsa Temple (海印寺) in Hapch'ŏn (陜川), South Kyŏngsang Province. Having unpacked her bags here, she is said to be diligently searching for the beauty of the valleys daily and concentrating wholeheartedly (一心不亂) on producing masterpieces?

It is said that Mr. Kim Sŏngsu (金性洙) of Inch'on (仁村) paid for Mr. Chang Tŏksu's (張德秀) wedding expenses, amounting to about a thousand won. Those who heard this news admire Inch'on's friendship.

Describing Mr. Kim Yaksu's (金若水) walk: he walks gracefully with his eyes cast down like a bride, holding a stick (스텍) in his right hand. Some sharp-tongued person described his walk as "a walk that hasn't given up ambition towards female students," to which [Kim Yaksu apparently replied]:
"No. It's an 'unstained by dust' (不染塵的) walk; because I dislike walking in this dusty world (틔끌세상), I walk quietly so as not to stir up dust (몬지)."
And further commenting:
"My walk is so peculiar that even if I'm mixed in a crowd far away, the plainclothes detectives (尾行) spot me just by my walk, say 'Ah, that must be Kim Yaksu,' and follow me. Hahaha." <4>

Ms. Pae Kuja (裵龜子), a young widow who has donned the black veil in her youth, having lost her health following her sorrow (哀歎), is currently confined to a room in Severance Hospital (셰부란스 병원) near Namdaemun, laying her frail body down and merely sighing at the beautiful spring flowing outside her window (窓外).

The poet Ansŏ (岸曙), who loves his drink (愛酒), recited Li Bai's (李太白) "Drinking Alone Poem" (獨酒詩) at a drinking gathering, intoxicating the attendees with its poetic charm (詩趣).
If Heaven loved not the wine,
No Wine Star would be in Heaven.
If Earth loved not the wine,
The Earth would have no Wine Springs.
Since Heaven and Earth love wine, <5>
I, loving wine, need feel no shame before Heaven.
I have heard that clear wine is like the Worthy.
And again, that浊 wine is like the Sage.
Since Worthy and Sage have been drunk,
Why seek for Spirits and Immortals?
Three cups penetrate the Great Way (大道).

Mr. Yu Sang'ŭn (柳相垠), the world penniless traveler who passed through Seoul some time ago, traveled pennilessly for 6 years through China, Russia (露西亞), Taiwan (臺灣), and the South Seas (南洋), returning to Korea last autumn. To fulfill his initial aspiration (初志) of thoroughly exploring the entire world, Mr. Yu set off on the path of wandering again this spring. Having toured South and North Manchuria and the Mongolia region during that time, he recently arrived in Peiping (北平). His future travel course (코-스) is said to be as follows: From Nanhua, Guangxi Province, Republic of China (中華民國廣西省 南華) to French Indochina (佛領 인도) (Hanoi - 하이노), Siam (*羅) (Bangkok - 반고크), Malay Peninsula (馬來반도) (Singapore - 신가뽀-ㄹ), Borneo Island (보르네오島) (Kuching - 크칭), taking a ship from Tawau (타와오) to Java Island (쟈바-島) (Surabaya - 스라바야), Malay Peninsula (페낭), India (인도) (Calcutta - 칼같다, Bombay - 폼페이), British Aden (영국 아덴), Ethiopia (에치오피야) (Abyssinia - 아비시니야), Larbaa? (라르바) (Ouled Bellel? - 우벨베), Port Sudan? Arabia? (포-토스탄소-지아리비야) (Mecca - 멕카), Egypt (埃及) (Jerusalem - 에르사렘), Turkey (토르코) (Tripolis? - 리-타리스), Bulgaria (풀가리야) (Constantinople - 콘스탄치노-불), Czechoslovakia (첵코슬로바키야), Switzerland (西瑞) (Bern - 베른), France (佛蘭西) (Paris - 파리-), England (영국) (London - 론돈), Denmark (丁抹), USA (米國), South America (南亞米利加), Brazil (쁘라질) (Pernambuco - 페라낭코), Uruguay (우르구아이), Argentina (亞爾然丁), Peru (秘魯), Panama (파나마), Mexico (멕시코), Hawaii (布哇), Yokohama (橫濱), Return to Korea (歸鮮).<6>

**Summary**

This article presents a collection of short news items and anecdotes about various figures and scenes in Seoul and beyond in 1937. It notes the juxtaposition of Chinese and Manchukuo flags in Seoul, Yŏ Unhyŏng's concern for the Korean film industry, a self-imposed silence agreement among Tonga Ilbo executives, Na Hyesŏk's artistic retreat to Haeinsa Temple, Kim Sŏngsu's generosity towards Chang Tŏksu, Kim Yaksu's distinctive walking style and awareness of being tailed, Pae Kuja's illness, the poet Ansŏ reciting Li Bai, and the ambitious global itinerary of the penniless traveler Yu Sang'ŭn. From a historical perspective, the article offers glimpses into the colonial reality, such as the visible presence of the Manchukuo flag signifying Japanese influence, the commentary on the state of Korean arts (film), the subtle pressures possibly felt by newspaper figures, and the global awareness and ambition demonstrated by Yu Sang'ŭn's travels despite the constraints of the era.

客談室
서울 明治町 한 모통이의 중국 총영사관 문전에서 청천백일기를 보고 몃 거름 옴기지 안어서 남대문통에 나오면 거기엔 商業銀行 삘딩 옥상에 오색 滿洲國旗가 휘날니고 있다. 여기는 商銀頭取 朴榮喆씨 있는 곳으로 滿洲國 명예총영사가 되어 여기에 임시 사무소를 둔 것이다. 아무튼 新舊국가의 새 깃발이 相距咫尺 사이에서 휘날니고 있는 것은 독특한 일풍경.

東京으로 갓다 오든 길의 呂運亨씨 연락선 중에서 某知友를 붓잡고 영화 사업이 사회에 영향을 주는 힘이 큰 것을 高調하면서 반도의 영화계가 凋殘함을 痛恨하더라고.

東亞日報 간부 諸氏 사이<3> 한 가지 紳士條約이 있다.
그것은 신문이 바로 잡히기 전에는 피차에 사회적으로 글이나 말을 발표하지 말자고 그래서 或有 訪客하여도 政治談 社會談은 일체 피하고 한갓 花鳥風月談으로 화제를 삼는다고.

신록이 무르녹는 初夏에 예술적 충동을 못참어 「칸파스」를 메고 水原古城을 떠나 남방순례를 나섯든 羅惠錫여사 가노라고느간 곳이 경남 陜川 海印寺. 여기에 여장을 풀고 요지간은 매일 계곡의 미를 차저다니며 명작? 을 一心不亂 제작중이라든가.

張德秀씨의 결혼비용 천여원을 仁村 金性洙씨가 내었다고. 이 소식을 드른 이들은 仁村 의 우정에 경복한다고.

金若水씨 거름을 묘사하건대 색시가치 눈을 내리깔고 오른손에 스텍을 들고 운치있게 거러간다. 어느 險口가 그 거름을
「여학생에 대한 야심을 버리지 안는 거름」이라고 햇더니
「아니오. 不染塵的 거름이지 틔끌세상에 것기 실허 몬지 날니지 말려 조용히 것는 이야」
그리고 또 주석하여
「내 거름이 하도 유별한지 尾行들이 아모리 먼 곳 군중 속에 끼어있을 것일지라도 거름만 보고 응 저것은 김약수로군 하고 뒤따러선대. 하하하」<4>

청춘에 거문 베일을 쓰게 된 젊은 미망인 裵龜子씨 哀歎의 뒤끗에 건강을 이러 요지간은 남대문 셰부란스 병원의 一室에 연약한 몸을 가르 눕히고서 窓外에 흐르는 꼿다운 봄을 탄식만하고 안젓다든가.

酒星不在天
