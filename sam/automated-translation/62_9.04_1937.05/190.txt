The Deepening Dream of Newlyweds
In Human Life, Happiness Flows Eternally

Mr. Chang Tŏksu (張德秀), former vice president of the Tong-a Ilbo (東亞日報), who had been diligently studying abroad in the United States (美國) and the United Kingdom (英國) for 13 years, held his wedding ceremony in mid-April at the Sinch'on Church (신촌 교회당) outside Sŏul (서울). The bride is Ms. Pak Ŭnhye (朴恩惠), a professor at Ewha Womans Professional School (梨花女子專門學校). When both were studying together in America, the seeds of love began to sprout. Since then, they spent many years in mutual affection and longing (相思相愛). Ms. Pak finished her studies first and came to Sŏul, quietly suppressing her passionate heart during beautiful days and nights (花朝月夕) while waiting. Now, Mr. Chang Tŏksu has also returned home (還歸鄕), and thus, the ceremony was held on this day when the green foliage is deepening.

The ceremony hall, cleansed with water and beautifully decorated with flowers, was crowded with several hundred figures from Hanyang Sŏng (한양성 - Sŏul) who came to wholeheartedly bless the happiness of the couple. Among them, the faces of Messrs. Yun Ch'iho (尹致昊), Hŏ Hŏn (許憲), Song Chinu (宋鎭禹), and Kim Sŏngsu (金性洙) were visible, and nearly all male and female professors from Ewha Womans Professional School also attended.

The couple has since established the sweetness (쓰윗홈) of their newlywed life in a charming blue-tiled Western-style house (청기와인 양옥) on a small hill (小丘) in front of Posŏng Professional School (普成專門學校), outside Tongdaemun Gate (동대문 밧). From time to time, they take walks in nearby places overgrown with green grass (靑草)... <25>

**Summary**

This news item reports on the April 1937 wedding of Chang Tŏksu, former Tong-a Ilbo vice president, and Pak Ŭnhye, an Ewha Womans Professional School professor. It details their long courtship, which began while studying in the United States, and describes the well-attended ceremony at Sinch'on Church, Sŏul, graced by prominent Korean figures like Yun Ch'iho, Hŏ Hŏn, Song Chinu, and Kim Sŏngsu. The couple set up their new home near Posŏng Professional School. For historians, this article is interesting as it highlights the social networks among the Korean elite during the colonial period, showcasing individuals educated abroad (US/UK) and figures associated with prominent nationalist newspapers (Tong-a Ilbo) and educational institutions (Ewha, Posŏng). The attendance of significant political and cultural figures underscores the social standing of the couple and the interconnectedness of this elite stratum.

신혼의 꿈, 깁흐다
人世엔, 행복이 기리 흐르다

13년동안 미국과 영국에 건너가서 열심히 硏學중에 잇든 전 東亞日報 부사장 張德秀 씨는 4월 중순, 서울 시외 신촌 교회당에서 결혼식을 거행하엿다. 신부는 梨花女子專門學校, 교수로 잇는 朴恩惠양으로서 일즉 미주에서 두 분이 다 함께 學窓에 노일 때, 사랑의 싹이 트기 시작하여 이래 여러 해를 두고 相思相愛의 사이로 지내오다가, 朴양 몬저 學窓을 끝내고 서울 와서 花朝月夕에 정열의 가슴을 고요히 누르고 대기중이러더니, 張德秀씨 마저 還歸鄕하야, 이에 신록이 무르녹는 이날 예식을 거행한 것이다. 이날 물로 닥고 꼿으로 아름답게 장식한 식장에는 두 분의 행복을 마음것 축복하는 한양성 인사가 수백여씨 몰려왓다. 그중에는 尹致昊, 許憲, 宋鎭禹, 金性洙 씨 등의 얼골도 보엿스며 梨花女專의 남녀 교수들도 擧皆 오섯다.
두 분은 그 뒤 신혼의 쓰윗홈을 시외 동대문 밧, 普成專門學校 압, 小丘 우 아담한 청기와인 양옥에 정하고, 잇다금 근교의 靑草 욱어진 곳으로 산책한다... <25>
