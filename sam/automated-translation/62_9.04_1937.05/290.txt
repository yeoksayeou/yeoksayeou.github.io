Government-Made Couples: 700,000
A Cheerful Yet Suggestive Report on German Marriage Finance

Hitler, in June 1933, the first year of his administration, promulgated a law establishing a government "Marriage Preparation Loan System" to implement marriage financing. According to Mr. Reinhardt's report, 700,000 couples have benefited from this system over the past four years, with an average loan of 600 Marks per couple, totaling 620 million Marks lent out. The number of children born is 500,000, and the birth rate among these couples is said to be twice that of couples unrelated to the preparation fund. This is because one-quarter of the loan is forgiven each time a child is born, reducing the need for birth control. The amount forgiven alone is said to reach 700,000 Marks, truly astonishing figures.

These loans are entirely interest-free, and repayment is made in monthly installments of 1/100th of the principal. Hitler intends this not only to increase births but also to provide unemployment relief. Firstly, through new marriages, working women leave their jobs, handing them over to other unemployed individuals. Secondly, the manufacture of marriage furnishings flourishes, increasing the demand for workers in this sector. Through this, 150,000 people are employed annually, and already a total of 650,000 people have benefited.

Additionally, it is reported that Italy recently decided at the Fascist Grand Council meeting to implement a marriage fund provision system, and in the US state of Ohio, a plan is being studied for the state government to lend $1,500 to newlywed couples at an annual interest rate of 3 percent. <42>

**Summary**

This 1937 article from the Korean magazine *Samch'ŏlli* reports on Nazi Germany's successful "Marriage Preparation Loan System," initiated by Hitler in 1933. It details how 700,000 couples received interest-free loans averaging 600 Marks, with debt reduction incentivizing childbirth (leading to 500,000 births and double the birth rate compared to non-participating couples). The program also aimed to reduce unemployment by encouraging women to leave jobs upon marriage and stimulating demand for household goods, reportedly creating 650,000 jobs. The article notes similar pronatalist/economic stimulus policies being considered in Fascist Italy and Ohio, USA. From the perspective of Korean colonial history, this article is interesting as it shows Korean readership being informed about state-led social engineering and pronatalist policies in fascist Europe and even the US. Historians might find it valuable as evidence of the circulation of ideas about population control, economic stimulus through social policy, and the perceived successes of authoritarian regimes during a period of global economic depression and rising geopolitical tensions, potentially influencing or reflecting contemporary discussions within colonial Korea or Japan.

官製夫婦 70만
명랑하고도 暗示 풍부한 독일 결혼 금융보고

힛틀러는 시정 제1년인 1933년 6월에 결혼금융을 정부의 「결혼준비금 貸付制」에 의하야 실행한다는 것을 법률로써 이 제도를 발포하엿다. 라인할트씨의 보고에 의하면 과거 4년간에 70만조의 부부가 이 제도의 은혜를 입었다는대 1조 평균 600말크 총액 6억 2천만 말크가 대여되엿다 한다. 출생한 어린 아해도 50만인으로 준비금에 관계하지 안흔 부부에 그 출산율이 2배나 된다고 한다. 그것은 어린애 하나 날 적마당 貸付金의 4분지 1이 면제되기 때문에 산아제한의 필요가 적은 까닭이며 그 면제를 바든 금액만도 70만 말크가 된다고 하니 그야 참 놀나운 數字이다.
이 貸付金은 전연 무이자이며 상환도 월 100분지 1의 월부로 되엿다. 힛틀러는 이것을 가지고 출산 증가만 아니고 실업자 구제도 할려 한다. 그것은 1은 신혼에 의하야 직업 부인이 職을 떠나 다른 실업자에게 그 직장을 물려준다. 2는 결혼 調度의 제조가 성하야 이에 수요되는 사람이 많어지는 것이다. 이로 하야 연 15만인이 취직되야 벌서 총합 65만인이 이어덧다고 한다.
이 외에 伊太利에서도 최근 팟시스트대회에서 결혼자금급여제를 실시하기로 하엿고 米國 오하이오州에서도 신혼부부에 1,500돌을 주정부에서 이자 연 3分으로 貸付할 안을 연구하고 있다고 전한다. <42>
