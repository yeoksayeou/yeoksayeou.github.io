Publication Date	1942년 01월 01일
Article Title	文壇往來 (Mundan Wangnae - Literary World Comings and Goings)
Article Type	소식 (Sosik - News/Updates)

**Literary World Comings and Goings**

Hyŏn Chin-gŏn (Novelist): Mr. Hyŏn, who had been spending a long time raising chickens at the foot of Paegak Mountain outside Ch'angŭi Gate, has left Puam-jŏng and moved to Sinsŏlchŏngsan No. 13-1, outside Tongdaemun Gate.

An Hoe-nam (Novelist): Mr. An recently left Kyŏngsŏng (Seoul) and moved with his entire family to his hometown in the countryside of Ch'ungch'ŏng Province.

Yi Sŏk-hun (Novelist): Mr. Yi resigned from the Kyŏngsŏng Branch of the Nihon Kōgyō Shimbun (日本工業新聞 - Japan Industrial News) and has now begun working as the Standing Director of the Writers' Association (文人協會) and concurrently as a commissioned staff member (屬托) of the "Nokki Yŏnmaeng" (緑旗聯盟 - Green Flag Alliance).

Yun Sŏk-chung (Children's Poet): Mr. Yun has decided to publish a "Home Song Collection" (家庭歌謠集), something never before published in Chosŏn (Korea). He has already completed 40 sets of lyrics and mobilized about 10 authoritative composers from our music circle <108> to take charge of the music, with plans to release it by the New Spring at the latest. Simultaneously, he plans to publish "Kullŏngsoe" (Hoops) as his 5th collection of children's poems.

Chŏng Pi-sŏk (Novelist): Recently joined the Photo Tri-monthly Report Department (寫眞 旬報部) of the Maeil Sinbo newspaper company.

Kim Yŏng-su (Novelist): Resigned from the Propaganda Department of Koryŏ Film Company (高麗映畵社) and joined the Propaganda Department of Yuhan Corporation (柳韓洋行).

Kim Sa-ryang (Novelist): Mr. Kim, who spent the summer at his home in P'yŏngyang, traveled to Tokyo (渡東) about a month ago and is now staying at Kamakura Onsen (鎌倉溫泉 - Kamakura Hot Springs). He plans to stay until this coming March and then travel again. It has been decided that his second collection of short stories, "Kohyang" (故鄕 - Hometown), will be published by a certain bookstore in Tokyo in early December.

Ch'oe Yŏng-hae (Writer): Mr. Ch'oe (son [令息] of Mr. Ch'oe Hyŏn-bae), who was the editor at Sin Sidae Sa (新時代社 - New Era Company), celebrated his wedding (華燭의 典) with Miss Han Po-dŏk on November 15th.

Ch'oe Yŏng-su (Writer): Joined Koryŏ Film Company (高麗映畵社) last month as the Head of the Propaganda Department.

O Yŏng-jin (Film Person): Mr. O (son [令息] of Mr. O Yun-sŏn), who was an assistant director (助監督) at Tokyo Hassei Eiga Sha (東京發聲映畵社 - Tokyo Talking Picture Company), had been recuperating at his home in P'yŏngyang and recently joined the Chosŏn Film Stock Company (朝鮮映畵株式會社).

Cho P'ung* (Writer): Mr. Cho, who was in charge of editing "Munjang" (文章 - Literature) magazine, has now joined the Maeil Sinbo newspaper company as a reporter in the Society section. <109>
<108-109>

**Summary**

This article provides brief updates on the activities of various Korean writers, poets, and film personnel in late 1941/early 1942. It details changes in residence, new job positions (often within media organizations like Maeil Sinbo, film companies, or advertising departments), publications (including upcoming poetry collections and a short story collection published in Tokyo), and personal events like marriage. The movements reflect the professional lives of cultural figures during the colonial period. For historians, the article is interesting as it shows figures moving into positions at organizations like the Maeil Sinbo (a major Japanese-run newspaper), the Chosŏn Film Stock Company (consolidated under colonial policy), and the mention of the "Nokki Yŏnmaeng" (Green Flag Alliance), likely a pro-Japanese entity, suggesting the increasing integration or co-option of cultural figures into the wartime colonial structure leading up to and during the Pacific War. Kim Sa-ryang's activities in Japan also reflect the complex relationship between Korean intellectuals and the colonial metropole.

文壇往來
玄鎭健氏(小說家) 오랫동안 彰義門 밖 白岳山麓에서 養鷄하며 지나시던 氏는 付岩町을 떠나 東大門 밖 新設町山 13番地 1號로 移舍하였다.
案懷南氏(小說家) 氏는 얼마 전에 京城을 떠나 온 가족과 함께 고향인 忠淸道 시골로 移舍하였다.
李石薰씨(小說家) 氏는 日本工業新聞 京城支局에서 辭任하고 今番 文人協會 常務幹事겸 「綠旗聯盟」 屬托으로 일보게 되였다.
尹石重氏(童謠詩人) 氏는 아직 朝鮮에서 출판된 일이 없는 「家庭歌謠集」을 출판키로 하고 이미 가사 40篇을 완성하여 我樂壇의 권위의＜108＞ 작곡가 10여 명을 동원시켜 담당케 하였는데, 늦어도 新春에는 발매하리라 한다. 그리고 그와 동시에 동요집 第5集으로 「굴렁쇠」를 출판하리라 한다.
鄭飛石氏(小說家) 今般 每日新報社 寫眞 旬報部에 입사.
金永秀氏(小說家) 高麗映畵社 宣傳部를 사직하고 柳韓洋行 宣傳部에 입사.
金史良氏(小說家) 여름을 平壤 자택에서 지나던 氏는 月餘前에 渡東하였다가 지금은 鎌倉溫泉에서 지나는데 오는 3月까지 留하고는 다시 여행하리라 한다. 그런데 12月上旬에 東京 某 서점에서 第 2短篇集 「故鄕」을 출간키로 되였다.
崔暎海氏(文筆家) 新時代社 편집인으로 있던 氏는(崔鉉培氏 令息) 지난 11月 15日 韓寶德孃과 華燭의 典을 이루었다.
崔永秀氏(文筆家) 지난달에 高麗映畵社의 宣傳部長으로 입사.
吳泳鎭氏(映畵人) 東京發聲映畵社의 助監督으로 있던 氏(吳胤善氏 令息)는 平壤 자택에서 그간 휴양하다가 今般 朝鮮映畵株式會에 입사.
趙豐*氏(文筆家) 「文章」편집을 맡아보던 氏는 今番 每日新報社 社會部 記者로 입사.＜109＞
