Wives of Key Figures in the Nationalist Government

Politically speaking, the status of Chinese wives should not be lightly regarded. The fame of figures like Madam Song Qingling (宋慶齡女史) or Madam He Xiangning (何香凝女史) is likely known by anyone who understands the situation in China around the time of the Northern Expedition. The Nationalist Government holds considerable respect and provides significant treatment for Madam Song Qingling, the widow of Mr. Sun Wen (孫文氏). While Madam He Xiangning's fame is not what it once was, it is generally well-known that the remains of her husband Liao Zhongkai (廖仲凱), who recently died tragically, were buried near the Zhongshan Ling (中山陵) (Sun Wen's Mausoleum) in Nanjing (南京), and he is revered as one of the pioneers among revolutionaries. More famous than these two ladies are the three women often called the "Three Heroines": Madam Jiang Jieshi (蔣介石氏) (Chiang Kai-shek's wife) Song Meiling (宋美齡), Madam Wang Jingwei (汪精衛氏) Chen Bi (陳壁女史), and Madam Huang Fu (黃郛氏) Shen Jingying (沈景英) (though some say Xu Huizhen (徐蕙貞), it is unclear).

Next, one cannot omit Madam Song Ailing (宋靄齡 女史), wife of Minister of Finance Kong Xiangxi (孔祥熙氏), who is admired by all women in China as the "leader among the women's corps" (娘兒隊中翹楚) and is one of the three Song sisters.
Madam Song is said to be wiser than Mr. Kong, and considering she is the sister of Song Qingling and Song Meiling, Madam Song's significance is naturally considerable. The reputation of Madam Chen Shuying (陳淑英 女史), wife of the President of the Legislative Yuan Sun Ke (孫科氏), is certainly high. Recently, her health has been poor, and she has been recuperating while traveling between Honolulu (호놀누) and Qingdao (靑島等地).

The name of Madam Yu Fengzhi (于鳳至 女史), wife of Mr. Zhang Xueliang (張學良氏), is also known both domestically and internationally. Considering the rumors of Mr. Zhang Xueliang's scandalous affairs (艶聞) with actresses, she is someone about whom there are no less significant rumors on the wife's side; she is said to be five or six years older than her husband.

Madam Chen Shuzi (陳淑子女史), wife of Mr. Hu Hanmin (胡漢民氏), is someone about whom there were once rumors she might be from the Japanese mainland (日本內地) because her name resembles a Japanese name.

Madam Li Dequan (李德全女史), wife of Mr. Feng Yuxiang (馮玉祥氏), is known for being virtuous and famous. Like Mr. Feng, she is known as a person of the common people. Even among Chinese people, there are none who speak ill of Madam Li. There isn't much talk about Madam Xu Lanxin (許蘭心女史), wife of Mr. Yan Xishan (閻錫山氏).

The names of Madam Wang Wenxiang (王文湘女史), wife of Mr. He Yingqin (何應欽氏); Madam Mo Xiuying (莫秀英女史), wife of Mr. Chen Jitang (陳濟棠氏); Madam He Dagu (何大姑女史), wife of Mr. Chen Gongbo (陳公博氏); and Madam Ren Bi (任壁女史), wife of Mr. Gu Mengyu (顧孟餘氏) are also not widely known.

Although there are many among the wives of key figures who have had romances (로멘쓰), the current wife of former Minister of Communications Wang Boqun (王伯群氏), Madam Bao Zhining (保志寧女史), is one of them. When Mr. Wang was Minister of Communications, he saw Miss Bao Zhining, who was still in school, and married her at once, surprising the world.<79>

**Summary**

This 1935 article from the Korean magazine *Samch'ŏlli* profiles the wives of prominent figures in China's Nationalist Government. It highlights the political significance and public image of women like the Song sisters (Qingling, Ailing, Meiling), He Xiangning, Chen Bijun, and others, discussing their reputations, relationships, and sometimes scandals. The article acknowledges their influence and fame, contrasting well-known figures with those less prominent. For historians of colonial Korea, this piece is interesting as it shows a Korean readership's engagement with contemporary Chinese politics and the emerging roles of elite women, even if viewed through the lens of their husbands' positions. It reflects an awareness of China's turbulent modernization and the public profiles of its leading female figures, potentially offering points of comparison or aspiration for Koreans under Japanese rule.

國民政府 要人들 令夫人
政治上으로 보면 中國夫人들의 지위는 업수히 역일 것이 아니다. 宋慶齡女史나 何香凝女史의 명성은 적어도 北伐 전후의 中國事情을 짐작하는 이면 대개 알 것이다. 孫文씨의 未亡人인 宋慶齡女史에 대해서는 國民政府는 상당한 경의와 대우를 하고 잇다. 何香凝女史의 명성은 예전보담 자랑할 것은 업고 최근 비명으로 죽은 夫君 廖仲凱의 유골을 南京 中山陵(孫文의 墓所) 근방에 장사하고 혁명가 중 先進者의 일인으로 섬기게 된 것은 일반이 다 잘 아는 바이다. 이상 두 부인에 대하야 유명한 것은 蔣介石씨 부인 宋美齡
汪精衛씨 부인 陳壁女史 黃郛씨 부인 沈景英(徐蕙貞이라는 말도 잇스나 不明)등의 3부인으로서 3女傑이라고도 한다.
그 다음으로 빼노치 못할 분은 『娘兒隊中翹楚」라 하야 全中國 부인들의 흠모를 밧고 잇는 宋家의 3姉妹 중의 한 사람인 財政部長 孔祥熙씨 부인 宋靄齡 女史이다.
宋女史는 孔씨보담도 현명타는 말을 듯고 잇스며 宋慶齡 宋美齡 등의 妹姉인 것을 보드라도 宋女史의 상당한 것은 물론이다. 立法院長 孫科씨 부인 陳淑英 女史의 명성은 과연 놉흔 것이다. 최근은 몸이 약해서 「호놀누」와 靑島等地에 巡遊하면서 靜養 중에 잇다.
張學良씨 부인 于鳳至 女史의 일홈도 역시 內外國에 모르는 이가 업다. 張學良씨에게 女優들의 艶聞이 전하여짐을 생각할 때 부인편에도 거기에 못하지 아니한 소문이 잇는 분으로 나히는 夫君보다 5, 6세 위라고 한다.
胡漢民씨 부인 陳淑子女史는 일홈이 日本內地名과 近似함으로 한때는 日本內地 사람이 아닌가하는 소문까지 잇섯든 분이다.
馮玉祥씨 부인 李德全女史는 정숙하고 유명한 이다. 馮씨와 가치 평민적인 사람으로 알려젓다. 中國人들도 李夫人의 흉을 보는 사람은 업다. 閻錫山씨 부인 許蘭心女史의 소문은 그리 만치 안타.
何應欽씨 부인 王文湘女史, 陳濟棠씨 부인 莫秀英女史, 陳公博씨 부인 何大姑女史, 顧孟餘씨 부인 任壁女史 등의 일홈도 널리 알려지지 못한 편이다.
要人 令夫人들 중에 「로멘쓰」를 소유한 분들도 만치만 元交通部長 王伯群씨의 現夫人 保志寧女史도 其中에 한 사람으로 王씨가 交通部長으로 잇슬 때 아직 학교에 잇는 保志寧孃을 보고 단번에 결혼을 하야 세상을 놀래인 것이다.＜79＞
