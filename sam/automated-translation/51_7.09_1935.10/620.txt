**Body Text Translation**
Gratitude and Apology

The world's wind and clouds (風雲) have become extremely pressing now. Perhaps by the time this magazine reaches the streets, the various nations (列國) will already be covered in the smoke of cannons and the rain of bullets. At this time, we consider it a matter of deep joy to have obtained a roundtable discussion on "The Sudden Arrival of the Great War and the Situation in the Far East" from gentlemen well-versed in international affairs.

With this month's issue, this journal (本誌) celebrates its full 7th anniversary. Initially, we had many preparations and plans for the editing, but due to our lack of ability (微力) and various circumstances, we are ashamed that we could not achieve even one-tenth of our intentions. However, please watch over us. Henceforth, we will exert our full effort to make each monthly issue splendid. <300>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the October 1935 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* expresses gratitude for a roundtable discussion on the tense international situation ("The Sudden Arrival of the Great War and the Situation in the Far East") while apologizing for the 7th-anniversary issue not meeting the editors' own expectations due to unspecified limitations. The editors promise renewed effort for future issues. For historians, the piece reflects the palpable anxiety about impending global conflict (likely referencing tensions leading to WWII, possibly the Italo-Ethiopian War which started around this time, or rising tensions in Asia) felt even in colonial Korea. The apology for the anniversary issue might hint at resource constraints or censorship pressures faced by publishers during the colonial period.

感謝와 謝罪
지금 세계의 風雲은 몹시 급박하여젓슴니다. 혹은 이 책이 거리에 나갈 때쯤은 벌서 列國은 砲煙彈雨속에 덥펴 잇슬는지 모르겟습니다. 이 때에 잇서 국제정세에 通한 諸氏로부터 「大戰急來와 極東形勢」의 座談會를 어덧슴을 심히 깁분일로 암니다.
本誌는 이 달 號로써 滿 7週年紀念號를 맛습니다. 처음 생각에는 이것저것 편집에 대한 用意와 계획이 만헛습니다만은 微力의 所致와 諸般事情으로 뜻의 10分1을 다하지 못하엿슴을 붓그러워함니다. 그러나 두고 보아주옵소서. 今後는 每月號를 精彩잇게 꾸미기에 全力을 다하겟나이다. ＜300＞
