First Announcement of New Writers' Works

For the sake of all you new writers (新人 諸氏) who lack a stage and opportunity and thus cannot display your natural talents (天分), our company (本社) previously promised to open our pages as a means of providing that stage. Shortly after the announcement, over 200 new works, like those listed below, were gathered. Thus, these have first been delivered to the various writers whom the submitters "privately admire" (私淑하는). If there is a recommendation from these literary figures, we will publish the work along with the recommendation letter in the upcoming December issue. (The deadline is October 20th; works submitted until that day will still be accepted, so please send many. When sending, be sure to clearly specify that it should be sent to Mr./Ms. X, whom you privately admire.)

**Summary**

This article from the October 1935 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine announces the results of its first call for submissions from aspiring writers ("New People"). It lists numerous works received across genres (fiction, drama, essay, poetry), along with the submitting author's name and the established literary figure they chose as a mentor ("privately admired teacher"). The magazine explains that these works have been sent to the respective mentors for review, and recommended pieces will be published in the December issue. It encourages further submissions before the October 20th deadline. Historically, this piece reveals the active literary scene in colonial Korea and the structure of mentorship where established writers played a crucial role in legitimizing and promoting new talent. It highlights *Samch'ŏlli*'s function as a significant platform for literary debutants during a period of limited opportunities under Japanese rule.

新人作品 第一回發表
무대와 기회가 업서, 자기의 天分을 발휘하지 못하는 新人 諸氏를 위하야 本社에서는 그 무대를 제공하는 의미로 紙面을 公開키로 약속하엿더니, 發表未久에 아래와 가치 200여 편의 新作品이 모여젓슴니다. 그래서 이것만 爲先 各其 「私淑하는」 諸作家에게 전달하엿는 바 文士諸氏의 추천이 잇는 것이면 그 推薦文과 아울너 來12月號 誌上에 발표하여 드리겟슴니다. (10월 20일까지 마감하오니 아직 작품을 더 보내도 그날까지는 밧겟스니 多數히 보내주시압. 보낼 때 私淑하는 某先生에게 보내달나고 반드시 詳記하시오)
