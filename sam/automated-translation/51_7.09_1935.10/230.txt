Record Singer Popularity Poll
Final Results Announcement

Last spring, our company began a Record Singer Popularity Poll, which received an extraordinary welcome. Votes flooded in from music-loving masses on the streets, and indeed, by the end of this September, not yet the 8th round, the momentum showed signs of surpassing ten thousand ballots. Thus, the voting ends today, and we publicly announce the results as follows. Furthermore, we are deeply grateful for the support from various quarters for this grand event (牡擧), allowing it to conclude with such success. (Additionally, the lottery results will be announced later, and a commemorative concert will also be held).

Five Selected Male Singers
1st Place: Ch'ae Kyu-yŏp (蔡奎燁) (Columbia Records) 1,844 votes
2nd Place: Kim Yong-hwan (金龍煥) (Polydor Records) 1,335 votes
3rd Place: Ko Pok-su (高福壽) (Okeh Records) 674 votes
4th Place: Kang Hong-sik (姜弘植) (Columbia Records) 468 votes
5th Place: Ch'oe Nam-yong (崔南鏞) (T'aep'yŏng Records) 333 votes
Runners-up: Kim Yŏng-gil (金永吉) (313), Yun Kŏn-yŏng (尹健榮) (313), Yi Il-nam (李一男) (307), Im Hŏn-ik (林憲翊) (301), etc. Others include Kim Hae-song (金海松), Kim Chu-ho (金周鎬), Kim Il-song (金一松), Yi Sang-ch'un (李相春), and various other gentlemen (諸氏).
Total votes counted above: 5,888 votes

Five Selected Female Singers
1st Place: Wang Su-bok (王壽福) (Polydor Records) 1,903 votes
2nd Place: Sŏnu Il-sŏn (鮮于一扇) (Polydor Records) 1,166 votes
3rd Place: Yi Nan-yŏng (李蘭影) (Okeh Records) 873 votes
4th Place: Chŏn Ok (全玉) (Polydor Records) 387 votes
5th Place: Kim Pok-hŭi (金福姬) (Victor Records) 348 votes
Runners-up: Kang Sŏk-yŏn (姜石燕氏) (344 votes), Yi Ae-ri-su (李愛利秀氏) (309), Kim Sŏn-ch'o (金仙草) (306), Ch'oe Ch'ang-sŏn (崔昌仙氏) (306 votes), Son Kŭm-hong (孫錦紅氏) (301 votes)
Others include: Na Sŏn-gyo (羅仙嬌), Ch'oe Myŏng-ju (崔明珠), Ch'oe Yŏn-yŏn (崔姸姸), Kang Nam-hyang (江南香), Cho Kŭm-ja (趙錦子), Kim Chŏng-suk (金貞淑), Miss Korea (미스코리아), Namgung Sŏn (南宮仙), Pak Pu-yong (朴芙蓉), Han Chŏng-hŭi (韓晶姬), Kim Yŏn-wŏl (金蓮月), An Myŏng-ok (安明玉), Yun Ok-sŏn (尹玉仙), Chang Il-t'a-hong (張一朶紅), Yi Ŭn-p'a (李銀波), Sim Ok-ch'ŏn (深玉泉)

**Summary**

This article from the October 1935 issue of Samch'ŏlli magazine announces the final results of a record singer popularity poll conducted earlier that year. It lists the top five male and female singers based on over 10,000 votes received from the public, along with their affiliated record labels and vote counts. Ch'ae Kyu-yŏp and Wang Su-bok topped the male and female categories, respectively. The article expresses gratitude for the public's enthusiastic participation and announces plans for an awards ceremony, a commemorative concert, and a lottery prize draw. For historians, this article provides valuable insight into the burgeoning popular music scene and recording industry in colonial Korea during the 1930s. It reveals the leading stars, the dominant record companies (Columbia, Polydor, Okeh, T'aep'yŏng/Taihei, Victor), and the engagement of the public through media-driven popularity contests, reflecting cultural trends and consumerism under colonial rule.
