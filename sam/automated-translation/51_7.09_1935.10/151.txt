本誌는 벌서부터 한글 綴字法에 依하야 編輯, 印刷하려하엿스나 印刷所의 設備未了로 延期하여오든중, 未久에 新綴字法에 依하야 印行하겟슴니다
三千里編輯局＜89＞

Public Notice

This magazine has intended for some time now to edit and print according to the Korean orthography (한글 綴字法), but due to the printing press facilities being incomplete (設備未了), we have been delaying. However, we will print according to the new orthography (新綴字法) shortly (未久).
Samch'ŏlli Editorial Department <89>

**Summary**

The Samch'ŏlli magazine's editorial department announces a delay in adopting the new standardized Korean orthography for editing and printing, citing incomplete facilities at the printing press. They assure readers that the magazine will implement the new spelling system soon. This notice is historically significant as it reflects the practical challenges faced by publications in adopting the 1933 Unified Spelling System for Korean (한글 맞춤법 통일안) during the Japanese colonial period, highlighting the intersection of language standardization efforts and technological/logistical constraints.
