Joint Statement by Madame Song Qingling and Mr. Chen Youren

There is probably no one in the world who does not know Sun Wen's (孫文) widow. Madame Song Qingling (宋慶齡女史), she is a heroine of China with the most left-wing ideology, who long ago turned her back on the Chiang Kai-shek (蔣介石) government, managed Sun Yat-sen University (孫文大學) in Moscow, and is raising many fighters.

Mr. Chen Youren (陳友仁氏) is also a talented man who once made a name for himself with his diplomatic skills during the Wuhan Government (武漢政府) era, possessing a most proactive and progressive mind. (Photo is of Madame Song Qingling)

An unpleasant rumor circulated concerning the relationship between this Mr. Chen Youren and Madame Song Qingling, causing a sensation not only in China but throughout the entire East for some time.

Greatly concerned that such a completely baseless rumor was circulating, one day Madame Song and Mr. Chen consulted and issued a statement under their joint names declaring that the rumor was unfounded.

Once this statement (聲明書) was reported in the Chinese newspapers, it is said that all 400 million Chinese people cleared up their misunderstandings.

Observing this psychology of the Chinese people, a certain lady remarked that our Korean people, who only know how to slander others, have something to learn. <93>

**Summary**

The article reports on a joint statement issued by Song Qingling (Sun Yat-sen's widow, noted for her left-wing views and opposition to Chiang Kai-shek) and Chen Youren (former diplomat of the Wuhan Government) to deny rumors about an inappropriate relationship between them. The statement effectively quelled the rumors in China. The article concludes with a comment attributed to an unnamed Korean woman contrasting the Chinese reaction with a perceived tendency among Koreans for slander, suggesting Koreans could learn from the Chinese example. From a historical perspective, the article is interesting as it reflects Korean interest in contemporary Chinese politics and notable figures during the colonial period. Furthermore, the concluding remark offers a glimpse into potentially prevalent self-criticism or comparative social commentary among Koreans under Japanese rule.

宋慶齡女史와 陳友仁氏의 共同聲明書
孫文의 未亡人이라면 아마 世界人으로 모를 사람이 없을 것이다. 宋慶齡女史 그는 가장 左翼的 사상을 가진 中國의 女傑로써 일즉붙어 蔣介石政府와 등진 다음 莫斯科에서 孫文大學을 경영하며 만은 鬪士를 길느고 있는 이다.
陳友仁氏 역시 武漢政府時代에 外交的 수완으로 한때에는 일홈을 날니든 才士로써 가장 적극적이며 진보적 두뇌를 가진 이다.(사진은 宋慶齡女史)
이 陳友仁氏와 宋慶齡女史와의 사히에 자미롭지 못한 風聞이 떠돌아 단녀 한동안 中國은 물론 全東洋에 센세이슌을 일으키는 사실이 있다.
사실이 없는 너무나 엉뚱한 風聞이 떠돌아 단임을 크게 우려하든 나머지, 하루는 宋女史와 陳氏가 相論하고 共同名義로 사실이 無根하다는 성명서를 발표하였다.
이 聲明書가 한번 中國新聞紙上에 報導되자 4억만 中國사람들은 모다 誤解를 一掃하였다 한다.
이러한 中國人의 心理를 보면 남을 中傷할 쭐만 아는 우리 조선사람들도 좀 배울만한 점이 있다고 某女史는 말하더라.＜93＞
