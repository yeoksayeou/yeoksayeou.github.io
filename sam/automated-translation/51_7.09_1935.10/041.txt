Company Announcement

△ Hŭich'ŏn General Branch Office
Location: 233 Ha-ri, Hŭich'ŏn-ŭp, North P'yŏngan Province (平北熙川邑下里二三三)
Branch Manager: Yun Sŏk-kyŏng (尹石卿)

△ P'yŏngyang General Branch Office
Location: 100 Sangsugu-t'ong, P'yŏngyang-bu (平壤府上水口通一○○)
Branch Manager: Kim Si-kwŏn (金時權)
Reporter: Pak In-sik (朴仁植)

General Branch Offices have been established as above.
Samch'ŏlli Company <40>

**Summary**

This is a company announcement from the Samch'ŏlli magazine stating the establishment of two new general branch offices (總支社). It lists the locations and managers for the Hŭich'ŏn branch (in North P'yŏngan Province) and the P'yŏngyang branch, also including a reporter for the P'yŏngyang office. This brief notice is significant as it documents the expansion of the Samch'ŏlli magazine's network and business operations across different regions of Korea during the colonial period in 1935. Historians might find this useful for tracking the reach and organizational development of Korean media under Japanese rule.
