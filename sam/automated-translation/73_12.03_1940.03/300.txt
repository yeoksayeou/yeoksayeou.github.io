Magazine Title: 삼천리 제12권 제3호
Publication Date: 1940년 03월 01일
Article Title: 吉林 同胞의 生活 (The Lives of Compatriots in Chilin)
Author: 中樞院 參議 朴容九 (Chungch'uwŏn Chamŭi Pak Yong-gu)
Article Type: 논설 (Editorial/Essay)

The living situation of Korean farmers (鮮農) not only results in reduced harvests due to annual floods, droughts, and other natural disasters, but the gradually rising cost of daily necessities also threatens the livelihoods of those engaged in farming and those residing in cities. Consequently, few lead comfortable lives, with most living in conditions almost the same (同樣) as Manchurians (滿人). However, among city dwellers, the main occupations are various businesses, especially inns (宿屋), restaurants, general stores, and rice milling operators. Additionally, some are employed in government and public offices. Therefore, compared to rural life, their standard of living is higher, and some possess considerable assets and lead affluent lives.

As side businesses for these Koreans (鮮人), some in rural areas use the agricultural off-season to make rush mats (菰苞) or straw bags (繩叺), or engage in pig and poultry farming to supplement their income, showing quite good results. About 80% of the Koreans in Chilin Province are as described above, but since they all operate with small capital, there is no remarkable development potential. However, the rice milling business solely managed by the Chilin Industrial Co., Ltd. (吉林 産業 株式會社) is of a vast (宏大) scale, demonstrating superior business conditions (業態) among all rice millers in Manchuria. This company also functions as a financial institution (金融 取扱 機關), and quite many resident Koreans utilize it. Other than this, some free laborers are engaged in labor for the Songhua River (松花江) hydroelectric power construction. Apart from the local farmers, the industrial sector is not yet <277> particularly noteworthy. The living situation and industrial status of Koreans in Chilin Province are generally as described above, and it is said that other regions are also largely the same with minor differences (大同小異).<278>

**Summary**

This article by Pak Yong-gu, a councillor in the Japanese colonial government's Privy Council (Chūsuin), describes the economic conditions of Korean migrants in Jilin (Chilin) Province, Manchukuo, in 1940. It portrays farmers struggling due to natural disasters and inflation, living lives similar to local Manchurians. Urban Koreans fare better, running small businesses (inns, restaurants, shops, rice mills) or holding official positions. Some supplement income with handicrafts or animal husbandry. A notable exception is the large-scale Chilin Industrial Co., Ltd. rice mill, which also serves as a financial institution for Koreans. Some Koreans also work as laborers on the Songhua River hydroelectric project. The author concludes that the situation is likely similar for Koreans in other parts of Manchuria. For historians, the article offers insights into the stratified economic reality of Korean settlers in Manchukuo, differentiating between struggling farmers and a relatively better-off urban population engaged in specific trades. The mention of a significant Korean-run enterprise (Chilin Industrial Co.) providing financial services, the specific types of labor (including major infrastructure projects like the Songhua dam), and the observation of assimilation ("almost the same life as Manchurians"), all framed by a colonial official's perspective, are particularly revealing aspects of the Korean experience under Japanese expansionism.

吉林 同胞의 生活
						中樞院 參議 朴容九
鮮農의 생활 상황은 매년과 같이 襲來하는 수해, 旱害 기타 천재 등으로 인하야 수확의 감소를 초래할 뿐 아니라 점차 고등하는 일용품은 此等 영농 방면 及 도시 在住者의 생계를 威脅함으로 여유있는 생활을 영위하는 자 소수로 殆히 滿人 同樣의 생활을 하고 있는 상황이나 도시 거주자로서는 각종 영업, 특히 宿屋 음식점, 잡화상, 정미업자 등이 其 主되는 자이고 또 일부는 관공서에 봉직하고 있다. 고로 차등은 농촌 생활에 비하야 생활 정도도 高하고 일부에서는 상당한 자산을 有한 유복한 생활을 하고 있는 자도 있다.
此等 鮮人의 부업으로는 일부 농촌 방면에 농한기를 이용하야 菰苞 기타 繩叺을 造하며 혹은 양돈 양계 등으로 생활의 일조를 삼고 있는데 상당히 好성적을 나타내고 있다. 吉林省 내의 朝鮮人의 약 8할은 이상에 말한 바와 같으나 차등은 모다 소자본으로 영위하고 있음으로 볼만한 발전성은 없으나 단독히 吉林 산업 주식회사 경영인 정미는 규모가 宏大하야 전 滿洲 정미업자 중 우수한 業態를 示하고 있다. 此 회사는 금융 취급 기관으로 在住 朝鮮人 간에 이용하는 자가 상당히 多하나 기타 일부의 자유 노동자는 松花江 수력 전기 공사의 노동에 종사하고 있다. 지방 영농자를 제하고는 산업 방면은 아즉 <277> 볼 만한 것이 없다. 吉林省 내의 朝鮮人의 생활 상황 及 산업 상태는 대체 이상과 같으나 타지방 亦 大同小異라 한다.<278>
