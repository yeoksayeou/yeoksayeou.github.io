**Body Text Translation:**
Commemorative Address for the Fourth Year of the Holy War, Expressing Gratitude to the Officers and Soldiers of the Imperial Army, and Calling for Self-Restraint from the People Behind the Guns
						Samch'ŏllisa

The China Incident (支那事變) was an inevitable course firmly promised together with the founding of Manchukuo (滿洲國). Why is this so? Because the establishment of Manchukuo is the first stage of world renewal (世界 維新). The unique ideal of Kingly Way politics (王道政治理想), tested and perfected here, its embodiment must gradually spread to neighboring states to save the world's peoples, trampled by Capitalism, Liberalism, and even Communism, allowing them to lead humanity's original life of happiness and glory. To achieve this, it was first necessary to rescue neighboring China (隣邦支那) from their [the aggressors'] clutches (괴수).

This Holy War (聖戰) has unfolded and already reached its fourth year. The Chungking government (重慶政府) and Chiang Kai-shek (蔣介石), failing to comprehend the true meaning (眞意義) of the Incident, are calling for long-term resistance, uselessly increasing the sacrifices. Without an inch of territorial greed, but rather striving to rescue China from the clutches of aggressors (侵略者의 괴수), sacrificing lives and wealth<32> while fighting fiercely amid hardships (惡戰苦鬪), the state of mind of the officers and soldiers of the Imperial Army (皇軍 將兵) is noble, like that of martyrs.

However, in the not-too-distant future, the day will come when the stubborn Chungking government and Chiang Kai-shek regret their wrongs, and the wise 400 million populace will awaken (覺하게) to their error. Then, the new order in East Asia (東亞의 신질서) will progress step by step, and the completion of the East Asian federation (東亞 聯邦) will arrive. There is an old slogan, "Light comes from the East." This signified the proclamation of the Imperial Way (皇道). There, the relationship of exploiter and exploited does not exist; there is the construction of a Kingly Way society (王道 社會). Now, as we greet the fourth year of the Incident, we once again reflect on the noble significance (거륵한 의의) of the Incident, express our gratitude to the officers and soldiers of the Imperial Army, and earnestly hope for the self-restraint and self-discipline (自肅自戒) of the People Behind the Guns (銃後國民). We offer this as the commemorative address for the fourth year of the Holy War.

Showa 15
Kyŏngsŏng-bu Chongno
Samch'ŏllisa<33>

**Summary**

This editorial from the Korean magazine *Samch'ŏlli* commemorates the fourth year of the Sino-Japanese War (termed the "Holy War"). It justifies Japan's actions as a necessary step towards establishing Manchukuo and a "New Order in East Asia" based on the "Kingly Way," supposedly liberating China from Western ideologies and internal strife. The article praises the sacrifice of the Japanese Imperial Army, condemns the Chinese resistance led by Chiang Kai-shek, and calls upon the Korean population ("People Behind the Guns") to demonstrate gratitude and exercise self-restraint in support of the war effort. For historians, this piece is a stark example of collaborationist media during the colonial period, illustrating how Japanese imperial propaganda framing the war as a righteous crusade for regional liberation was disseminated within Korea, demanding Korean complicity and loyalty to the Japanese Empire's expansionist aims.

聖戰 第四年 記念辭, 皇軍 將士에 感謝하며, 銃後 國民의 自肅를 期함
						삼천리사
支那事變은 滿洲 건국과 함께 굳게 약속되어 있는 예정의 코-쓰이었다. 왜 그러냐하면 滿洲國의 창건은 세계 維新의 제1 단계이니 여기에서 試練되고 완성된 독창적의 왕도정치이상은 그 權化가 점차로 연방에 파급하여 資本主義, 自由主義 내지 共産主義에 유린된 세계 인류를 구제하여 행복과 영화를 갖게하는 인류 본연의 생활을 하게 함에는 먼저 隣邦支那를 저들의 괴수에서 건저 내이는 것이었다.
이러한 聖戰이 전개되어 임이 第四年을 맞이하게 되었다. 사변의 眞意義를 해득하지 못하는 重慶정부 及 蔣介石은 장기 항전을 부르짖어 부즈럽시 희생을 크게하고 있다. 寸土의 영토욕이 없고 오히려 中國을 침략자의 괴수에서 구원하고저 생명을 버리고 재화를 희생하면서<32> 惡戰苦鬪하고 있는 皇軍 장병의 심경은 그가 거륵하기 순교자와 같다.
그러나 머지않은 장래에 완고한 重慶정부 及 蔣介石은 자기의 非를 뉘우치는 날이 올 것이요 현명한 4억의 민중은 저들의 우를 覺하게 될 것이니 그 때에는 東亞의 신질서가 步一步進展하여 東亞 연방의 완성이 온다. 예로부터 「광명은 동방으로」라는 표어가 있다. 그는 皇道의 선포를 의미함이었나니 거기에는 착취 피착취의 관계도 없고 왕도 사회의 건설이 있다. 이제 사변 제4년을 맞는 우리는 다시금 사변의 거륵한 의의를 생각하고 皇軍 장병에게 감사하는 동시에 銃後國民의 自肅自戒를 기하여 마지안는 바이다. 이로써 聖戰 第四年의 기념사를 삼노라.
昭和 15년
京城府 鐘路
