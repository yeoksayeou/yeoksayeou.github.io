The Urgent Need for Life Improvement
						Yi Kwangsu

Life improvement is a desperately urgent matter for the people of Chosŏn. If we, in our current insufficient state, are to have an improved tomorrow, the only path is to innovate our lives. It means discarding the irrationality of the old and adopting the rationality of the new. There is no other way. Therefore, the Life Innovation Movement is an urgent matter that must sweep through all of Chosŏn as quickly as possible.

My respected friend, Mr. O Ōk (吳億), has long held a deep interest in this issue, particularly accumulating much research on the nutritional improvement of food. Now, he has resolutely stepped forward to the forefront of the Life Improvement Movement. His recent work, "The Path of Life" (生活進路), and its accompanying diagrams (圖解) are its first voice.

This one book, this one sheet of diagrams, are truly fine works that every household must have and every person must read. I believe they are especially necessary for housewives, who can be said to be entrusted with the life and health of the family, to the point of needing memorization.

The nutritional improvement of food truly affects not only human health but is also a major issue concerning lifespan. Rationalizing this scientifically is indeed the key secret to disease-free longevity and having hundreds of children and thousands of grandchildren, largely because proper nutrition is the best of all medicines (百藥의 長). Thus, Teacher O Ōk's work "The Path of Life" and its diagrams easily teach us the conclusions reached by today's science on this matter.

I, too, obtained this book and diagrams, and even my children read and listened with interest, gaining much from them. I use the diagrams for daily reference, morning and evening. I dare to recommend them to the general public and also pray that Teacher O Ōk's great undertaking—this great work of the Life Improvement Movement—will gain sympathy from various fields and achieve great success.<65>

**Summary**

Yi Kwangsu argues passionately for the necessity of "life improvement" (生活改善) for the Korean people (朝鮮人), framing it as the sole path to a better future. He emphasizes replacing old irrational ways with new, rational ones, specifically highlighting nutritional improvement based on scientific principles. He strongly endorses the work of his friend O Ōk (吳億), whose book "The Path of Life" (生活進路) and accompanying diagrams provide practical guidance on nutrition, which Yi claims is essential for health, longevity, and national progress. For historians, this article is revealing as it shows a prominent intellectual like Yi Kwangsu engaging with discourses of modernity, rationality, and self-improvement prevalent during the colonial period. The focus on scientific nutrition as a key to national betterment reflects broader colonial-era campaigns aimed at 'improving' the populace, often aligning with assimilationist goals, though presented here as a self-driven necessity for Koreans.

生活改善의 急務
						李光洙
생활개선은 朝鮮人을 위하여서는 焦眉의 緊急事입니다. 부족한 금일의 우리로서 향상의 明日을 가지랴면 그 유일한 길은 우리의 생활을 혁신함입니다. 舊의 불합리를 버리고 합리의 新을 취하는 것입니다. 이 밖에는 도리가 없읍니다. 그럼으로 生活革新運動은 하로 바삐 全朝鮮을 風靡하지 아니하면 아니될 긴급사입니다.
畏友 吳億씨는 일즉부터 이 문제에 깊은 관심을 가지시어 특히 음식물의 영양적 개선에 많은 연구를 쌓으시다가 이제 분연히 生活改善運動의 진두에 나서시니 氏의 近著 「生活進路」와 및 圖解는 그 第一聲입니다.
이 한 권 글, 한 폭 그림은 실로 家家必備, 人人必讀할 양서요, 특히 일가의 생명과 건강을 맡았다고 할 만한 주부로서는 암송할 필요가 있는 것이라고 믿습니다.
음식물의 영양학적 개선은 실로 사람의 건강에만 영향할뿐더러 수명에 관한 대문제입니다. 이것을 과학적으로 합리화하는 것이야말로 無病長壽, 百子千孫의 요결이 되는 것이니, 대개 정상한 영양이야말로 百藥의 長인 까닭입니다. 그런데 吳億 선생의 저인 「生活進路」와 그 圖解는 금일의 과학이 도달한 이 문제에 대한 결론을 알기 쉽게 우리의게 가르처 줍니다.
나도 이 책과 圖解를 얻어 아이들 까지도 흥미있게 읽고, 듣고 소득이 많았으며 그 圖解는 조석의 참고로 상용하옵니다. 감히 일반에 推獎하오며, 아울러 吳億 선생의 이 대사업-生活改善運動의 대사업이 각 방면의 공명을 얻어 크게 성취하기를 축원하옵니다.<65>
