Congratulations, on the Occasion of the National Foundation Festival, Kōki 2600
						Kim Tonghwan

As our Japanese Empire, which reveres the Imperial Line of eternal continuity above and where one hundred million subjects below live and work in peace, now greets the Kigensetsu [National Foundation Day] marking 2600 years since the nation's founding, we are profoundly moved in our hearts to be descendants of this land, which we can boast of to all the nations of the world. Furthermore, embodying His Majesty's great will, we, the one hundred million subjects, pledge ourselves to henceforth redouble our devotion with sincerity in loyal service to the state.

Now, as the Holy War (聖戰) reaches its fourth year, although a million officers and soldiers continue to punish the Chiang regime (蔣政權) resisting Japan and embracing communism in over 400 provinces of the Central Plain (中原), the tide in East Asia is already advancing towards the formation of a new order with our Japanese Empire as the leader. Thus, the nation's authority truly resounds across the four seas. For the construction of this new order, and also for the prosperity and happiness of this Asia, we <30> will not forget to offer our last drop of blood and our last bead of sweat to the nation. Now,
We reverently offer congratulations for the brilliant 2,600-year National Foundation Festival of the Rise of Asia (興亞).

Events of the day = On February 11th, starting at 9:00 AM, all of Chosŏn will simultaneously observe a time of national celebration, performing worship from afar towards the Imperial Palace (宮城遙拜). At the same time, at the Government-General (總督府), Governor-General Minami (南總督) and all officials will gather in the plaza before the front entrance of the Government-General to solemnly conduct the Imperial Palace Worship Ceremony (宮城遙拜式). All government offices, schools, banks, companies, and other organizations throughout Chosŏn will respond in kind and conduct the Worship Ceremony (遙拜式) simultaneously.

▲On the same day at 9:20 AM, at the Government-General, the Ceremony of Worship before the Imperial Portrait (御眞影奉拜式) will be held in the first conference room. After the ceremony concludes, all officials will gather in the main hall, raise cold sake, and thrice shout "Long live His Majesty!" (聖壽萬歲).

▲The Kigen 2600 Commemorative Exhibition, hosted by the Government-General, will be held from February 7th to 16th on the rooftop of Chōjiya (丁子屋). It will display historical diagrams and dioramas making the essence of our national polity (國體), whose Imperial Line (皇統) is continuous and unparalleled in the world, clear at a glance (一目瞭然). Also displayed for public viewing will be swords (刃劍), photographs of Imperial Tombs (御陵), and treasures from Chosŏn Shrine (朝鮮神宮) that are normally not allowed outside its gates (門外不出).

Kōki 2600, February 11th
Samch'ŏlli Publisher Kim Tonghwan <31>

**Summary**

This article, written by Kim Tonghwan, the publisher of Samch'ŏlli magazine, is a celebratory piece marking the 2600th anniversary of the mythical founding of the Japanese Empire (Kōki 2600). It expresses fervent loyalty to the Japanese Emperor and Empire, pledges utmost devotion ("last drop of blood") from the Korean subjects (referred to as part of the "one hundred million subjects" of Japan) towards Japan's "Holy War" in China and the establishment of a Japanese-led "New Order in East Asia." The article details the mandatory participation of all sectors of Korean society, led by the Government-General, in Emperor worship ceremonies (palace worship from afar, worship of the imperial portrait) and mentions a commemorative exhibition designed to showcase the "uniqueness" of the Japanese Imperial line and national polity. For historians, this article is a stark example of collaborationist rhetoric and pro-Japanese propaganda disseminated through a Korean publication during the late colonial period. It explicitly documents the forced mobilization of Koreans into Japanese imperial rituals and the internalization or public performance of loyalty demanded by the colonial regime, particularly the promotion of the Kōki 2600 celebrations aimed at solidifying imperial ideology and the war effort.

祝, 建國祭, 皇紀 二千六百年을 際하야
						金東煥
萬世一系의 皇統을 우으로 奉戴하옵고 아래로 일억의 臣民이 安居樂業하는 我 日本帝國이 今에 건국 二千六百年을 마지하는 기원절을 당하옴애 世界萬邦에 자랑할 이 국토의 자손되옴을 중심으로 감격하오며 아울러 御大心을 體하야 일억 臣民은 금후 더욱더 盡忠報國의 赤誠을 다하옵기를 自期하겟나이다
이제 聖戰은 四個星霜에 及하야 백만의 장병이 中原 400여 주에서 抗日容共의 蔣政權 응징을 계속하고 있기는 하오나 이미 東亞의 대세는 我日本제국을 지도자로하는 신질서의 편성에 약진하고 있슴으로 정히 國威 四海에 떨치는 때라, 이 신질서 건설을 위하야 또한 이 亞細亞의 번영과 행복을 위하야 우리는<30> 최후의 血一適까지 최후의 汗一粒까지 국가에 바치기을 있지 안켓나이다, 이제
삼가 興亞의 빛나는 2,600년의 건국제를 봉축하옵나이다
당일의 행사=2월 11일 오전 9시를 기하야 전 朝鮮 일제히 국민 봉축의 시간으로서 궁성 遙拜를 행하고 가튼 시각에 총독부에서는 南總督 이하 전직원이 총독부 정면 현관 前 광장에 叅集하야 엄숙한 궁성 遙拜式을 거행하고 전 조선의 관공서, 학교, 은행, 회사, 기타 모든 단체도 이에 호응하야 일제히 遙拜式을 거행한다.
▲仝日 오전 9시 20분, 총독부에서는 제일회의실에서 御眞影 奉拜式을 거행하고 식이 끗난 후 큰 홀에서 전직원은 냉주를 들어 聖壽萬歲를 삼창하기로 되었다.
▲총독부 주최의 기원 2,600년 奉讚 전람회는 2월 7일부터 16일까지 丁子屋 옥상에서 皇統이 連綿한 것이 세계에 비유가 없는 國體의 정화를 一目瞭然하게 국사 圖解와 드라마를 비롯하야 刃劍, 御陵사진과 門外不出의 朝鮮神宮 보물 등을 진열하야 일반 관람에 내놓기로 되였다.
皇紀 二千六百年 二月十一日
삼천리 사장 金東煥<31>
