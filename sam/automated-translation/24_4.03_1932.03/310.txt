**Body Text Translation:**
Queen's Jewels (皇后의 寶石)

It is reported that former Spanish Queen (前 西班牙皇后) Her Majesty Victoria (빅토리아陛下) has begun negotiations to part with her famous jewel collection (코레크숀), causing a great sensation (센세이순).

The value of the collection (코레크숀) is said to be about 4 million won (約 400만원). Included within it are a splendid crown (왕관) set with diamonds (金剛石), a beautiful emerald (美麗한 예메라루드의) coronet (冕冠), and a diamond-studded turquoise piece (금강석을 박은 토루고 玉) that the queen always cherished and wore on her breast, which she inherited (물녀바드신) from her godmother (敎母), the French Empress Eugénie (佛蘭西의 유-디에니-황후), along with other items mixed in (석기어 잇다). These are all rare treasures and famous jewels (珍品名寶) of the world, possessing immense value (絶大한 가치) and historical significance (歷史적 由緖), and are said to have a value beyond their price. <74>

Furthermore, although the value of these jewels is being assessed (評定하고 잇다고 하나) by the P'ondo Street Jewelers' Association (폰도街 寶石商組合), regarding their disposal (處分), because the Bavarian crown jewels (바리아의 왕관) were recently sold at an unexpectedly low price (案外의 安價로 競落이 되게 되엿슴으로) when put up for auction, it is said they will be sold privately (非公式으로 賣買할 것이라고) instead of being put to auction (경매에 붓치지 안코). <75>
<74-75>

**Summary**

This article from 1932 reports on the impending sale of the former Spanish Queen Victoria's renowned jewel collection, valued at approximately 4 million won. It highlights specific valuable pieces, including diamond and emerald crowns and a diamond brooch inherited from French Empress Eugénie, emphasizing their historical significance. The article notes the decision to pursue a private sale rather than an auction, influenced by the recent unexpectedly low price achieved for the Bavarian crown jewels at auction. From the perspective of Korean colonial history, the article demonstrates Korean interest in European affairs and aristocracy, awareness of international market dynamics (jewel auctions), and the use of the Korean won as a high-value monetary reference point. The mix of Hanja, Hangul (including older spellings), and loanwords reflects the linguistic landscape of the period.

皇后의 寶石
前 西班牙皇后 빅토리아陛下는 그의 유명한 보석 코레크숀과 작별하게 되여 交涉이 시작되엿다고 전하자 큰 센세이순을 일리키고 잇다.
同코레크숀의 가격은 약 400만원이라고 한다. 그 속에는 金剛石을 박은 화려한 왕관 美麗한 예메라루드의 冕冠 황후가 그의 敎母인 佛蘭西의 유-디에니-황후로붓터 물녀바드신 金剛石을 항상 사랑하시며 가슴에 달고 게시든 금강석을 박은 토루고 玉 등이 석기어 잇다. 이것은 모다 천하의 珍品名寶로써 絶大한 가치와 역사적 由緖를 가저고 잇는 물건으로 가격 이상의 가격이 잇다고 말한다.＜74＞
그리고 이 보석들의 가격은 폰도街 寶石商組合의 손으로 評定하고 잇다고 하나 그 처분에 대하야는 바리아의 왕관이 최근 경매에 붓칠 때 案外의 安價로 競落이 되게 되엿슴으로 경매에 붓치지 안코 비공식으로 賣買할 것이라고.＜75＞
＜74-75＞
