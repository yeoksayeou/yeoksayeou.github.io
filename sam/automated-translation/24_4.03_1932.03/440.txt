**Body Text Translation:**
Gratitude and Apology

O You must have seen that this magazine's editorial policy—which has been devoting its full efforts to addressing Korean problems rather than global problems, the life-and-death issues of our white-clothed brethren (白衣형제) rather than those of all humanity, and moreover, the handling of policies and immediate various strategies (諸方略) rather than the investigation (討究) of principles—has become even clearer in this month's issue. We promise ourselves that we will continue to boldly fulfill this special mission from now on.

O Our magazine's April issue is planned as a grand Spring Special Issue (陽春特大號), and all staff members are already working hard so that it may appear before the peninsula's (반도) readership with a majestic, vigorous, and lively appearance. Through this April issue, we hope to gain social recognition that the "Samch'ŏlli staff members" were all figures "much more useful than white bones" (白骨보다 훨신 有用). Please look forward to it very much. (Special price 30 Chŏn (錢))

O "Peace and Freedom" (평화와 자유) is receiving enthusiastic responses from home and abroad. We ask that you please order many copies. <112>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the March 1932 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine thanks readers and reaffirms the publication's focus on practical, immediate issues concerning Korea ("Chosŏn") and Koreans ("white-clothed brethren") rather than abstract principles or global matters. It promotes the upcoming April special issue, expressing the staff's desire to be recognized as practically useful ("more useful than white bones"), and also encourages orders for another publication titled "Peace and Freedom." For historians, this piece offers insight into how a Korean magazine under Japanese colonial rule articulated its mission, emphasizing pragmatic concerns possibly as a way to navigate censorship while still addressing Korean readers' interests. The language used, including archaic spellings and the idiomatic expression about usefulness, reflects the linguistic context of the era.

感謝와 謝罪
O 범세계적 문제보다도 조선문제를, 전인류적 사활문제보다도 白衣형제의 死活문제를, 그리고 또 원리원칙의 討究보다도 정책과 당면한 諸方略의 취급에 全主力를 傾倒하여 오든 본지의 社是는 今月호에 이르러 그 色彩가 더욱 鮮明여젓슴을 여러분은 보섯슬 것이외다 압흐로도 과감하게 이 특수한 사명을 다하려고 自期하나이다.
O 4월호의 본지는 陽春特大號로하야 雄雄하고 勁勁하고 활발한 자태로 반도 독서층에 나타나고저 벌서부터 전사원은 노력중이외다. 이 4월호를 통하야 우리들은 「삼천리사원」들이 모다 「白骨보다 훨신 有用」한 인물이엇다는 사회적 공인을 엇고저 기대하나이다. 만히 기다려주시압.(특가 30錢)
O 「평화와 자유」는 海內海外의 熱呼를 밧고 잇습니다. 여러분께서 만히 주문하여 주시압.＜112＞
