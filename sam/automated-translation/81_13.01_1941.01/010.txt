**Body Text Translation:**
Praying for the Emperor's Eternal Longevity on the Occasion of Kōki 2601

Living in our Japanese Empire, where we respectfully uphold the Imperial line, unbroken for ages eternal, and below which the one hundred million subjects all live peacefully and work happily like one family, as we now greet the year 2601 of the Imperial Era (皇紀), we reverently pray for nothing less than the eternal longevity of:

His Majesty the Emperor (天皇陛下)
Her Majesty the Empress (皇后陛下)
Her Majesty the Empress Dowager (皇太后陛下)

Now, as the Holy War (聖戰) enters its fifth year, the military might (武威) of the Imperial Nation grows ever stronger day by day. Not only have the footprints of the Imperial Army reached nearly every part of the vast 400-odd provinces of China (支那), but they have also advanced far into French Indochina (佛印), punishing <44> the tyranny of Britain and France. Thus, the New East Asian Co-Prosperity Sphere (新東亞共榮圈), with our Japanese Empire as its leader (盟主), will surely be completed before long.

We, the subjects (臣民), pledge ourselves to serve with our last ounce of strength for the prosperity and happiness of this Asia, and we humbly pray once more for the eternal longevity of Your Majesties and the Imperial House.

Events of the Day - On January 1st, at 9:00 AM, all of Korea (朝鮮) will simultaneously observe a time of national celebration, performing worship towards the Imperial Palace from afar (宮城遙拜). At the same time, at the Government-General (總督府), Governor-General Minami (南總督) and all staff will gather in the plaza before the main entrance of the Government-General building to solemnly conduct the Palace Worship Ceremony (宮城遙拜式). All government and public offices, schools, banks, companies, and other organizations throughout Korea will respond in kind, conducting the Worship Ceremony simultaneously.

On the same day at 9:20 AM, at the Government-General, a Ceremony of Worshiping the Imperial Portrait (御眞影 奉拜式) will be held in the No. 1 Conference Room. After the ceremony concludes, all staff will raise cups of cold sake in the main hall and perform three cheers of "Long Live His Majesty!" (聖壽 萬歲).

Kōki 2,601, January 1st
Kyŏngsŏng Samch'ŏlli-sa <45>

**Summary**

This article from the Korean magazine *Samch'ŏlli* marks the beginning of the Japanese imperial year Kōki 2601 (1941). It fervently expresses loyalty to the Japanese Emperor and Imperial family, praying for their long lives. It celebrates Japan's military successes in China and French Indochina as part of the ongoing "Holy War" and anticipates the imminent completion of the Greater East Asia Co-Prosperity Sphere under Japanese leadership. The article concludes by detailing the mandatory events planned for Koreans on New Year's Day, including synchronized worship towards the Japanese Imperial Palace and ceremonies involving the Governor-General and officials. For historians, this piece vividly illustrates the intense level of ideological mobilization and forced emperor worship imposed on colonial Korea during the wartime period. The language used, blending celebratory tones with explicit directives for participation in state rituals, highlights the mechanisms of colonial control and the enforced assimilation policies aimed at erasing Korean identity and integrating Koreans as loyal subjects of the Japanese Empire.

奉禱 聖壽無疆, 皇紀 2601年을 際하야
萬世一系의 皇統을 우으로 奉戴하옵고 아래로 一億臣民이 모다 한글가치 安居樂業하는 我日本帝國에 生을 亨하야 今에 皇紀 2,601年을 마지하옴애 우으로 惶悚하오나
天皇陛下
皇后陛下
皇太后陛下
의 萬壽無疆을 奉禱하여 마지 안나이다.
이제 聖戰은 第5年을 迎하야 皇國의 武威는 날로 날로 더욱 떨쳐서 이제는 넓은 支那 400餘州에 皇軍의 자최 미치지 않은 곳이 別로 없게 되엇슬 뿐더러 멀리 佛印에까지 進駐하야 英佛의 暴虐을 膺懲하고＜44＞ 잇사온지라 未久에 我日本帝國을 盟主로 한 新東亞共榮圈이 完成될 것이로소이다.
我等 臣民은 이 亞細亞의 繁榮과 幸福을 爲하야 最後의 心力까지 奉仕할 바임을 自期하오며 삼가 다시 더 한번 陛下께옵서와 皇室에 萬壽가 잇삽기를 받들어 奉禱하나이다.
當日의 行事 - 1月 1日 오전 9時를 期하야 全朝鮮 一齊히 國民奉祝의 時間으로서 宮城遙拜를 行하고 같은 時刻에 總督府에서는 南總督以下 全職員이 總督府 正面 玄關前 廣장에 參集하야 엄숙한 宮城遙拜式을 擧行하고 全朝鮮의 官公署, 學校, 銀行, 會社, 其他 모든 團體도 이에 呼應하야 一齊히 遙拜式을 擧行한다.

仝日午前 9時 20分, 總督府에서는 第1會議室에서 御眞影 奉拜式을 擧行하고 式이 끝난 後 큰 홀에서 全職員은 冷酒를 들어 聖壽 萬歲를 三唱하기로 되었다.
皇紀 2,601年 1月 1日
京城 三千里社＜45＞
