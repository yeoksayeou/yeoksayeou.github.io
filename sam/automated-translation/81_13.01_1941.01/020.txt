Gratitude to the Million Soldiers of the Imperial Army, Welcoming the Fifth Year of the Holy War

The China Incident (支那事變), which began five years ago on July 7th, Shōwa 11 (1937), is truly a sacred undertaking to build a Co-Prosperity Sphere in New East Asia with our Japanese Empire as the leader. During this time, the one million soldiers of the Imperial Army who have gone to war have upheld the sacred will of His Majesty above and have not betrayed the trust of the people below. Not only have they fought well throughout the 400-odd provinces of China, achieving brilliant military exploits, but now they have even advanced far into French Indochina (佛印), a French territory (佛蘭西領), and the might of our Japanese flag (日章旗) shines day and night east of the West Pacific. We, the 100 million subjects on the home front (銃後), are filled with boundless joy and pride, and at the same time, our hearts are full of gratitude.

Perhaps there has never been a time when our nation's prestige (國威) has resounded throughout the world as it does today. Externally, we have shaken hands in alliance with the great powers of Germany (獨逸) and Italy (伊太利), which seek to break the Versailles system and establish a new order in Europe (歐洲). Internally, in East Asia,<46> we are on the path to realizing the great ideal of Hakkō Ichiu (八紘一宇, Eight Corners of the World Under One Roof) by uniting the various peoples who cried under the exploitation of Britain and America (英米). All of this is truly thanks to the soldiers who have risked their lives (身命) and fought valiantly. The gratitude and trust of the people on the home front (銃後國民) are realized to be growing ever stronger.

Now, with the long-awaited establishment of the Nanjing Nationalist Government (南京國民政府) headed by Wang Ching-wei (汪精衛), our country has concluded the Basic Treaty between Japan and China (日華基本條約). It is said that the life of the traitor Chiang (蔣逆, Chiang Kai-shek) will not last many more days. However, as the malicious schemes of the nations supporting Chiang (援蔣諸國) still exist, it is difficult to predict that the future path will be without turbulence. At this time, we earnestly pray that all members of the Imperial Army will advance further in unity.

As it is the season of severe cold with harsh snow and wind (雪風), when we think of their hardships, we the populace resolve to practice self-restraint and self-discipline (自肅白戒) and cooperate further with national policy. Finally, we offer a reverent silent prayer to the spirits of the fallen soldiers (戰歿將兵).

January 1, Shōwa 16 (1941)
Samch'ŏlli-sa, Chongno, Kyŏngsŏng<47>
<46-47>

**Summary**

This editorial from the Korean magazine *Samch'ŏlli*, published on New Year's Day 1941, expresses profound gratitude to the Japanese Imperial Army as the war in China enters its fifth year. It hails the war as a "sacred undertaking" to establish a Japanese-led "Co-Prosperity Sphere" in East Asia. The article celebrates Japan's military successes, its advance into French Indochina, its alliance with Germany and Italy (Tripartite Pact), and the establishment of the Wang Ching-wei regime in Nanjing, predicting the imminent downfall of Chiang Kai-shek. It urges the Korean populace ("100 million subjects") on the home front to practice self-restraint and fully support the national (Japanese) policy, concluding with a prayer for the war dead. For historians, this piece is a stark example of pro-Japanese propaganda disseminated through a Korean publication during the colonial period. It illustrates the extent to which imperial ideology, including concepts like *Hakkō Ichiu* and the justification of the "Holy War," permeated Korean society and the pressure on Koreans to demonstrate loyalty and support for Japan's war effort, even using language that denigrated other Asian nations like China and figures like Chiang Kai-shek.

皇軍百萬將兵에 感謝, 聖戰第五年을 마지하며
5年前인 昭和11年 7月7日에 始作한 支那事變은 이 實로 我日本帝國을 盟主로 하야 新東亞에 共榮圈을 建設하려는 聖事業인 바 그 동안에 出戰한 皇軍將兵 100萬은 우으로 陛下의 거룩한 뜻을 잘 밧들고 아래로 國民의 信賴를 저버리지 않어서 支那 400餘州, 到處에서 善戰하야 써 赫赫한 武勳을 빛내었을 뿐 아니라 지금에는 멀리 佛蘭西領인 佛印에까지 進駐하야 我日章旗의 威武가 西太平洋以東에 日夜 빛나고 있으니 銃後에 있는 我等 1億臣民은 끝없는 기쁨과 자랑을 가진 同時에 感謝하는 마음을 가슴 가득히 가지고 있나이다.
아마 今日만치 我國의 國威가 世界에 떨친 적이 없을가 하나이다. 밖으로는 歐洲에서 벨사유體制를 打破하고 新秩序를 樹立하려 하는 獨逸, 伊太利의 諸强國과 同盟의 誼로써 握手를 하였고, 안으로는 東亞에＜46＞ 있어 英米의 搾取에 울든 諸民族을 紏合하야 써 八紘一宇의 大理想을 顯現하는 途程에 있으니 이 모다 一로 將兵諸氏가 身命을 睹하야 力戰하여 주신 德이라, 銃後國民의 感激과 信賴는 加一層 더하여 짐을 깨닷나이다.
이제 待望하는 汪精衛를 首班으로 한 南京國民政府와의 사이에 我國은 日華基本條約이 締結되어 蔣逆의 生命이 不多幾日이라 하겠으나 아직도 援蔣諸國의 惡踈한 策謀가 있는지라 그 前途에 波瀾이 없을 것을 豫想키 어려우니 이 때에 오직 皇軍各位는 더욱 一致邁進하기를 빌어 마지 안나이다.
때는 雪風이 甚한 酷寒의 節인지라 그 勞苦를 생각할 때에 우리들 民衆은 自肅白戒하여 국책의 一層協力할 것을 期하며 끝으로 戰歿將兵의 英靈에 삼가 黙禱를 드리나이다.
昭和16年 1月1日
京城鐘路 三千里社＜47＞
＜46-47＞
