Article Type: 논설 (Editorial/Opinion Piece)

Defense Speech by the Army Commander
						Chōsen Army Commander Nakamura Kōtarō

The national organization of Chōsen under the new system has finally seen the establishment of the National Total Mobilization League (國民總力聯盟), reorganized and expanded from the National Spiritual Mobilization League (國民精神總動員聯盟), and it has been decided to strive towards the total training of the nation and the enhancement of total national power under the current situation. Accordingly, on the 16th, an ad-hoc Provincial Governors' Conference was held at the headquarters, and simultaneously, it was decided to launch the National Total Mobilization Movement throughout all of Chōsen. On this day, Army Commander Nakamura issued the following statement, clarifying the military's expectations for the National Total Mobilization Movement.

With the conclusion of the Tripartite Pact, the world now clearly shows the confrontation of two major camps, the new and the old, and the resistance and pressure of the old camp against us have become even more blatant and have intensified.

The Empire (皇國), facing this extraordinarily critical situation, must strive to the very end to thoroughly implement the great ideal of constructing the Greater East Asia New Order. The way to handle this lies nowhere else but in firmly maintaining the strength to boldly repel the resistance and pressure of the old camp, enriching our military preparedness, and establishing an unshakeable national defense posture. Now, a close alliance between Japan (日), Germany (獨), and Italy (伊) has been established, and the solidarity of Japan (日), Manchuria (滿), and China (支) has also been further strengthened. If our people cooperate unanimously and make epoch-making efforts towards the points mentioned above, the great enterprise of Raising Asia (興亞) cannot fail to be achieved.

At this time, coinciding with the establishment of the new system in the Mainland (內地), in our Chōsen, the National Spiritual Mobilization League has been reorganized and expanded, leading to the establishment of the National Total Mobilization League. Adopting a renewed system for demonstrating national total power, striving towards the total training of the nation and the enhancement of total national power under the current situation, and especially holding the ad-hoc Provincial Governors' Conference today to launch the National Total Mobilization Movement throughout all of Chōsen simultaneously, is truly a matter of immense celebration from the perspective of national defense, and the military holds the greatest expectations for its future results. What is urgently needed now is to reap the fruits of national training so that we can establish a posture of national power and resources unshaken by the challenges of the old camp, concentrate the Empire's total power swiftly towards a leap forward in reinforcement, cast aside old habits, <72> and resolutely implement epoch-making measures here. Especially in our Chōsen, which serves as a logistics base (兵站基地) for national defense and an forward operational base (前進據點) for continental construction, I keenly feel its urgent necessity.

It is hoped that, taking the establishment of this League as an opportunity, under the unified leadership of the Governor-General (總督), an iron-like solidarity between the government and the people centered around the Governor-General will be forged, further reaping the fruits of Japan-Korea unification (內鮮一體), overcoming all difficulties, maintaining an unshakeable resolve, swiftly completing the training of the people to enhance their full potential, renewing the spirit of both upper and lower classes, pouring forth human wisdom, maximizing human power to the highest degree, exhausting all measures, implementing them swiftly and nimbly, daring to carry out a leap forward in strengthening both the material and spiritual aspects in Chōsen, taking the lead ahead of the Mainland and other regions, demonstrating the reality of being the base for the construction of the Greater East Asia New Order, and striving swiftly towards the establishment of a high-level national defense posture (高度國防態勢).

Furthermore, the military sincerely prays for the development of this League and the achievement of its intended objectives, and at the same time, pledges to cooperate wholeheartedly, pouring all its efforts into contributing and advancing the demonstration of total national power based on the new system.

That we now see such a vigorous development of the National Total Mobilization Movement in Chōsen is greatly indebted to the spiritual movement of the past National Spiritual Mobilization League. In particular, the military expresses deep gratitude and respect for the fact that, for the past two years, General Kawashima (川島大將) served as President-General (總裁) and earnestly devoted himself to its guidance, and that all members of the League also embodied his intent and worked hard to cultivate the foundation for this Total Mobilization Movement, and also expresses thanks for their labor. <73>

**Summary**

This article is a statement by Nakamura Kōtarō, the commander of the Japanese Army in Korea (Chōsen), published in December 1940. It announces and endorses the launch of the National Total Mobilization Movement (國民總力運動) and the formation of the National Total Mobilization League (國民總力聯盟) in colonial Korea, replacing the previous National Spiritual Mobilization League. Nakamura emphasizes the urgency of this mobilization in light of the Tripartite Pact and the escalating confrontation with the "old powers." He calls for unwavering national defense, strengthened military preparedness, and the complete unification of government and people under the Governor-General to realize the "Japan-Korea as One Body" (內鮮一體) policy. The goal is to transform Korea into a highly efficient logistics and forward base for Japan's "Greater East Asia New Order," demanding total effort and sacrifice from the Korean populace.

For historians, this text is significant as it directly reflects the Japanese military's perspective and directives regarding the intensification of colonial control and resource mobilization in Korea during a crucial phase of WWII. It starkly illustrates the implementation of the "Naisen Ittai" policy, the rhetoric used to justify total mobilization under the guise of constructing the "Greater East Asia New Order," and the explicit framing of Korea as a strategic military base ('兵站基地', '前進據點') supporting Japan's expansionist aims. The mention of General Kawashima's previous role highlights the continuity and escalation of mobilization efforts.

軍司令官의 國防演說
						朝鮮軍司令官 中村孝太郞
신체제하의 조선의 국민조직은 마츰내 國民精神總動員聯盟을 改組擴充하여 國民總力聯盟의 성립을 보게되어 時局下 국민의 總訓練과 總國力의 昻揚에 향하여 매진하기로 되었다. 그리하여 16일에는 본부에서 臨時道知事會議가 개최되는 동시에 全鮮 일제히 國民總力運動을 전개하기로 된 바 이날 中村軍司令官은 左와 如한 담화를 발표하여 國民總力運動에 대한 軍의 기대하는 바를 명백히 하였다.
三國條約의 체결에 의하여 玆에 세계는 判然히 新舊二大陣營의 대립을 보아 舊陣營의 我에 대한 반항 압박은 더욱 노골케 되고 또한 강화하게 되었다.
皇國은 此 非常重大時局에 대처하여 끝까지 大東亞新秩序建設의 大理念貫徹에 매진치 않어서는 안되는데 이에 處하는 道는 他엔 없고 一로 舊陣營의 抗壓에 대하여 敢然히 이를 배격할 수 있는 실력을 견지하고 軍備를 충실하여 부동의 國防態勢를 확립함에 있다. 이제 日, 獨, 伊의 긴밀의 제휴가 성립되어 日, 滿, 支의 단결은 또한 더욱더 鞏固를 加했다. 我國民으로서 一致協力 敍上의 점에 향하여 劃期的 노력을 함에 있어서는 興亞의 위업이 성취되지 아니치 못 할 것이다.
此時에 당하여 內地에 있어서 신체제의 수립과 相俟하여 我朝鮮에서는 國民精神總動員聯盟을 개조 확충하여서 玆에 國民總力聯盟의 성립을 보아 時局下 국민의 總訓練과 總國力의 昻充에 향하여 매진한 國民總力 발휘의 一新體制를 취하고 今日 특히 臨時道知事會議를 개최하여 全鮮 일제히 國民總力運動을 전개하기로 된 것은 국방상 실로 경축이 不堪하는 동시에 今後의 성과에 대해서는 軍이 가장 기대하는 바이다. 모름지기 現下의 급무는 舊陣營을 도전에 대항하여 미동치 않는 國力資源의 태세를 확립할 수 있도록 國民訓練의 實을 걷우어 帝國의 총력을 擧하여 속히 비약적 증강에 집중하고저 舊排를 奪하여＜72＞ 玆에 획기적인 대책을 단행함에 있다. 특히 국방상의 兵站基地로서 大陸建設의 前進據點인 我朝鮮에서 더욱 그 緊切한 바를 통감한다.
바라건대 本聯盟의 성립을 機로 하여 總督의 일원적 지도에 의하여 總督을 중심으로 하는 官民鐵石의 단결을 결성하여 더욱 內鮮一體의 實을 걷우고 萬難을 박차며 부동의 결의를 견지하여 그 全能을 앙양할 국민의 훈련을 속히 완성하여 上下 그 心을 새로히 하여서 人智를 傾倒하고 인력을 최고도로 發揚하여 온갖 대책을 다하고 機敏히 이를 실시하여 조선에 있어서 물심양면의 비약적 강화를 감행하여 內地 기타의 지방에 솔선하여 大東亞新秩序建設의 基地인 實을 발휘하고 속히 高度國防態勢의 확립에 매진하기를 바란다.
그리고 軍은 本聯盟의 발전과 소기의 목적달성을 哀心으로 祈念하며 서도 協心戮力 全力을 傾倒하여 新體制에 基해서 總國力의 발휘에 寄與 邁進할 것을 期하는 바이다.
今般 이렇게 조선에 있어서 國民總力運動의 潑刺한 전개를 보게 된 것은 과거에서의 國民精神總動員聯盟의 精神運動에 因할 바 甚大한 바 있다. 특히 軍은 과거 2년간 川島大將이 總裁로서 銳意 그 지도에 盡瘁하였고 聯盟의 各位 역시 그 旨를 體하여 노력하여 이 總力運動의 기초를 배양한 점에 대해서는 깊이 사의와 경의를 표하며 또 그 勞를 謝하는 바이다.＜73＞
