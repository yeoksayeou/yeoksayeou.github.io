Magazine Title: 三千里 제12권 제10호
Publication Date: 1940-12-01
Article Title: 朝鮮新制와 吾人의 緊張 本誌革新에 際하야 (The New System in Korea and Our Tension: On the Occasion of This Magazine's Reform)
Author: 三千里社長 金東煥 (Kim Tong-hwan, President of Samch'ŏlli)
Article Type: 논설 (Editorial)

1
This Great Imperial Rule Assistance Movement (大政翼찬運動), which the 100 million subjects of our nation aim to achieve through firm unity and the practical application of the way of the subject (臣道), is truly occurring at an extraordinary time both internally and externally. As declared by Premier Konoe, it is indeed a great, decisive action surpassing the Meiji Restoration and a profoundly significant nationwide movement.

2
Accordingly, in Korea (朝鮮 - Chosŏn) as well, starting with the declaration by Governor-General Minami (南總督 - Nam Ch'ongdok) on October 16th, the 23 million populace united firmly, the National Total Mobilization League (國民總力聯盟 - Kungmin Ch'ongnyŏk Yŏnmaeng) was organized, and various constructive movements on the peninsula for the establishment of a high-level national defense state were decided. The Korean populace (朝鮮民衆 - Chosŏn Minjung) must renew themselves completely and strive with selfless devotion (滅私奉公 - myŏlsa ponggong) for the completion of this New System (新體制 - Sinch'eje) this autumn.

3
At this time, this magazine (本誌 - Ponji), now marking 12 years since its founding, will naturally advance towards the great national objective by further adhering to the administrative policies of the authorities (當局 - Tangguk). Moreover, we hereby publicly declare that we will exert all efforts henceforth especially for the operation of the New System (新體制 - Sinch'eje).
And at this time, this magazine especially intends to devote its entire strength to:
One, The realization of Japan-Korea Unification (內鮮一體 - Naeseon Ilche)
One, The thorough inculcation of the "Volunteer" Spirit (「志願兵」精神 - "Chiwŏnbyŏng" chŏngsin)
One, The rapid establishment of compulsory education (義務敎育 - Ŭimukyouk)
<19>

**Summary**

This editorial by Kim Tong-hwan, the president of the magazine *Samch'ŏlli*, published in December 1940, wholeheartedly endorses Japan's wartime "New System" policies being implemented in Korea. It praises the Imperial Rule Assistance Association (Taisei Yokusankai) and the formation of the National Total Mobilization League in Korea, urging the Korean populace towards "selfless devotion" (myŏlsa ponggong) for the state. The magazine explicitly pledges to dedicate itself to promoting key Japanese colonial objectives: Naiseon Ilche (Japan-Korea Unification), the militaristic "Volunteer" soldier spirit, and the establishment of compulsory education (likely intended as a tool for assimilation and state control). This article is a significant example of Korean collaborationist rhetoric during the height of Japanese wartime mobilization. Historians would find its unreserved support for Japanese imperial policies and its commitment to actively propagating assimilationist and militaristic ideologies within Korean society particularly revealing of the pressures and choices faced by Korean intellectuals and media outlets under late colonial rule.

朝鮮新制와 吾人의 緊張 本誌革新에 際하야
						三千里社長 金東煥
1
我國의 一億臣民이 굳은 단결과 臣道의 실천화를 통하여써 달성하려 하는 이번의 大政翼찬運動은 이 실로 內外時運이 非常한 때이니만치 近衛總裁의 聲明한 바와 같이 확실히 明治維新 이상의 大英斷이요 의의깊은 전국민적 운동이라 할 것이다.

2
이에 따라 朝鮮에서도 10월 16일의 南總督의 聲明을 발족으로 하여, 2천3백만 민중이 굳게 단합하여 이에 國民總力聯盟이 조직되엇고 또 高度國防國家의 건설을 위한 반도의 건설적 諸운동이 규정되었으니 朝鮮民衆은 更新一番, 이 新體制의 완성을 위하여 滅私奉公하여야 할 秋이다.

3
이때에 있어 창간 12개년을 閱하는 本誌는 더욱 더욱 當局의 施政方針을 존수하여, 국가 大目的의 달성에 邁進할 바는 물론이며 특히 新體制의 운행을 위하여 今後 온갓 노력을 다 할것임을 이에 공언하는 바이다.
그리고 이때에 있어 本誌는 특히
一, 內鮮一體의 實現
一,「志願兵」精神의 徹底化
一, 義務敎育의 速成
을 위하여 全心力을 경주코저 하노라.＜19＞
