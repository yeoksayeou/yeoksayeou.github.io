Patriotic Anecdotes from the Home Front

I. A Patriotic Woman's Beautiful Story of Donation
(Kyŏngsŏng) The Namdaemun branch of the Patriotic Women's Association (애국부인회) and the Namdaemun branch of the National Defense Women's Association (국방부인회), located at 9, 5-chōme, Namdaemun-t'ong, Kyŏngsŏng Prefecture, pooled money among members to commemorate the glorious 2,600th year of the Imperial Era (황기). On September 21st, they donated 2,600 wŏn to the Korean Army Patriotic Department (조선군 애국부) as funds for the mechanization of national defense within Korea, greatly impressing the related officials, it is reported.

II. The Patriotic Fervor of the "Soldiers of the Hoe"
(Ŭijŏngbu) The 60 students of the 9th graduating class of the Kyŏnggi Province Short-Term Farmer Training Center (京畿道 短期農民訓練所), after their completion ceremony on the 25th, returned to their respective hometowns to take their first valiant steps in agricultural service to the nation (農業報國) under the New Order (新體制) as "Soldiers of the Hoe" (鍬의 戰士), supporting the Great Administration (大政翼贊). During their enrollment, they saved money by cutting back on tobacco and other petty expenses, raising a sum of 12 wŏn, which they contributed as a National Defense Donation (國防獻金).

III. A Martyr-like Beautiful Story Amidst Air Defense Drills
(Pusan) Mr. Paek Hak-su (白鶴壽), a member of the 4th Headquarters Unit of the Pusan Air Defense Corps (경방 제4본단), works as a weaver at the Asahi Silk Textile Factory (旭絹織物工場) located at 252 Pujŏn-ri within the prefecture. While serving during the recent air defense drill, his younger sister, Paek Yun-sŏn (白潤先), fell ill with appendicitis. At 11 PM on the night of the 4th, he was nursing his sister at home. However, at 7 AM on October 5th, believing that public and private affairs should not be confused, he left his critically ill sister and went out to participate in the drill activities. After the drill ended at 11:50 AM, while listening to the critique by Governor Sant'aek (山澤), he received the news that his sister had finally passed away. Although the officials repeatedly urged him to return home, he stated that he could not disrupt his public duties for the private matter of his sister's life or death in an emergency. Only after the unit was dismissed at 3 PM did he finally return home to console his sister's spirit. Upon receiving this report, the Air Defense Headquarters widely informed all unit members of the young man's spirit of self-annihilation for the public good (滅私奉公), leaving them deeply moved.

IV. A Peddler's Beautiful Story of Donating a Large Sum
(Hamhŭng) Under the wartime situation, the Peninsular people's <126> new lifestyle system and fervor for national defense are impeccable as subjects of the Imperial Nation (黃國臣民). On September 13th, a donation of 50 wŏn was entrusted to the Hamhŭng Police Station (咸興署). This came from a man who makes a living selling corn snacks in Hamhŭng city. Although barely making ends meet (僅僅得食) daily, he possesses a strong spirit of saving (貯蓄精神). Cooperating with his wife, they saved 20 chŏn every day starting from the 1st day of the 1st lunar month, accumulating 47 wŏn and 80 chŏn. He was considering how best to use this money. In August, he installed a radio ('라디오') hoping to alleviate some of his daily fatigue. However, realizing this was inappropriate for his situation and his duty as a citizen in wartime, he sold the radio, added some money to the proceeds, and requested that the total of 50 wŏn be used to support national defense efforts. His address and name are as follows:
I. Permanent Domicile: Kŭpkwan-ri (及貫里), P'yŏngyang Prefecture
Current Address: 41, 1-chōme, Kŭm-chŏng (錦町), Hamhŭng Prefecture
Cho Sun-jŏng (趙淳貞) (46)

V. Wedding Expenses Saved for National Defense Donation
(Yangyang) Mr. Kim Hae-su-myŏng (金海秀明) (Former name: Kim Ŭm-bae 金蔭培) of Nammun-ri, Yangyang Township (襄陽面), held his eldest daughter's wedding ceremony on the 18th. By saving on various ceremony expenses, he donated 20 wŏn as a National Defense Donation (國防獻金) to the Yangyang Police Station (襄陽警察署) on the 20th, it is reported.
-End- <127>

**Summary**

This 1940 article from the magazine *Samch'ŏlli* presents five anecdotes showcasing Koreans' "patriotic fervor" and contributions to Japan's war effort. These include women's associations donating money symbolic of the Japanese imperial year, farmer trainees donating savings, an air defense volunteer prioritizing duty over his dying sister, a poor peddler selling his radio to donate savings, and a father donating money saved from his daughter's wedding. The article highlights acts of financial sacrifice and prioritizing imperial duty over personal matters, framing these as "beautiful stories" (美談) of loyalty. For historians, this article is a clear example of wartime propaganda aimed at mobilizing Korean support (or portraying such support) for the Japanese empire under the "New Order" and "Na선일체" (Japan and Korea as One Body) policies. It reveals the pressures and narratives used to encourage Korean participation in the war effort, portraying self-sacrifice for the empire as the highest virtue, even among the poor or during personal tragedies.

銃後愛國美談
一. 愛國婦人의 獻金美談
(경성) 경성부 남대문통 5정목 9번지의 애국부인회 남대문 분구와 및 국방부인회 남대문분회에서는 빛나는 황기 2,600년을 기념하는 뜻으로 회원끼리 모은 돈 2,600원을 조선내 기게화 국방비로서 9월 21일에 조선군 애국부에 헌납하여서 관게관을 크게 감격시컸다고 한다.

二. 鍬의 戰士의 愛國熱
(의정부) 京畿道 短期農民訓練所 第9回生 60명은 지난 25일 修了式 후 各各 鄕里에 돌아가 新體制 하에 鍬의 戰士로 大政을 翼贊하여 農業報國에 勇躍의 첫거름을 밟게 되었는데 入所 중에 煙草 등 잔돈을 절약하여 모흔 돈 一金 12원을 醵出하여 國防獻金으로 獻納하였다.

三. 防空訓練下의 殉國的 美談
(부산) 경방 제4본단원 백학수(白鶴壽)군은 부내 부전리(釜田里) 252번지 욱견직물공장(旭絹織物工場)에 종공으로 근무하면서 이번 방공훈련에 봉사하든 중 그 누이동생 백윤선(白潤先)이가 맹장염으로 앓게되어 4일 밤 열한 시에는 자택에서 그 동생의 병구원을 하며 간호 중이든 바 10월 5일 오전 일곱시에는 그 위독한 누이를 남겨놓고 公私를 혼동하면 아니된다 하여 출장을 하여 활동을 한 다음 열한 시 50분에 그 훈련이 끝난 다음 산택(山澤)지사의 강평을 듣는 사이에 드디어 세상을 떠났다는 부고가 오게 되어 간부들이 귀택하기를 재삼 권하였으나 비상시 사사로운 누이의 생사문제로 공무를 어그러트릴 수 없다하여 오후 세시 해산을 한 뒤에야 비로소 집으로 돌아가 누이의 영혼을 위로하였다. 이 보고를 받은 방호본부에서는 그 청년의 멸사봉공심을 전단원에게 널리 알리어 감격함을 마지 아니하게 하였다.

四. 行商人의 巨金獻納美談
(咸興) 전시하의 반도인(半島人)＜126＞ 생활신체제와 국방열은 황국신민으로써 손색이 없는데 지난 9월 13일에도 헌금 50원을 함흥서(咸興署)에 의탁하였다는 바 이는 함흥(咸興)부내에서 옥수수과자장사로서 매일 근근득식(僅僅得食)하여 가면서도 저축정신(貯蓄精神)만 뚜렀한지라 자기 처와 협력하여 지난 음 일월 일일(陰 1月 1日)부터 매일 20전식 저축한 돈 47원 80전을 적당히 쓸랴고 연구하든 바 지난 8월에 『라디오』를 가설하고 종일피로(終日疲勞)를 다소 위안할랴 하였으나 자기 처지와 전시하에 국민된 도리가 아니라고 자각하고『라디오』를 팔고 그 돈에 다소 보태서 50원을 국방 사업에 보태여 써달라 하였는데 주소와 씨명은 다음과 같다.

趙淳貞(46)

五. 婚費節約 國防獻金
(양양) 襄陽面 南門里 金海秀明(舊名 金蔭培)씨는 지난 18일 長女의 결혼식을 거행하였는데 식의 諸般費用을 절약하여 20일 襄陽警察署에 國防獻金 20원을 기탁하였다 한다.
