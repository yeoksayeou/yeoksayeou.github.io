Become the Matrix of World Culture
						By Kishida Kunio (岸田國土), Cultural Director of the Imperial Rule Assistance Association (大政翼贊會)

Regarding the issue of culture, I intend to interpret this in an extremely broad sense. Dealing with the issue of culture inherently differs from peacetime. That is, the cultural control demanded by the National Defense State cannot but suppress certain tendencies that might have been considered acceptable (可타) in peacetime. However, on the other hand, we, bearing the significant role of a leading nation (指導民族) with a New Order (新秩序), recognize that the so-called emergency period (非常時局) is but a single moment compared to the ten-thousand-year life of the nation. The characteristics of the national culture fostered during this period must not become a source of misfortune (禍) for the eras that will follow. It would be a grave matter if the cultural heritage (文化的 遺産) we leave to our descendants (子孫) is unusable outside of this emergency period, or if it debases (떠러치고) and impoverishes national life.

I believe the ideal is for the creation of modern Japanese culture itself to become the matrix (母體) of a truly global culture that will shine upon other peoples (諸民族). In this sense, I intend to strive in various ways so that today's cultural policy, conceived with thorough preparation (周到한 用意), provides clear guidelines (指標) and executive power (實行力) over the national ideology and daily life.

In this domain [of culture], individual citizens need not endure poverty (貧乏). It would be good to stretch out (벋을 수 있는 대로 삗치고) as much as possible and obtain as much as possible. Abundant joy—therein will the people's spirit (意氣) and steadfast strength be shown (뵈여질 것이다). <201>

**Summary**

Kishida Kunio, Cultural Director of Japan's Imperial Rule Assistance Association, argues that while wartime necessitates cultural controls, Japan must simultaneously create a vibrant national culture. This culture should not be merely a product of the "emergency period" but should serve as a lasting heritage and, ideally, become the "matrix" for a new world culture, reflecting Japan's role as a "leading nation" in the "New Order." He encourages richness and joy in the cultural sphere, suggesting this will demonstrate national spirit and strength. From a Korean colonial history perspective, this article is significant as it exemplifies the Japanese imperialist ideology of cultural assimilation and dominance being promoted directly to a Korean audience through a Korean magazine. It reveals the Japanese justification for cultural control while simultaneously outlining ambitions for Japanese culture to supplant others and become a universal standard, framing this dominance as a benevolent act of providing a cultural "matrix" for other nations, including colonized Korea.

世界 文化의 母體가 되라
						大政翼贊會 文化部長 岸田國土
문화문제에 대하여 나는 이것을 極히 넓은 意味로 해석하려고 한다. 本來부터 文化問題를 取扱하는데는 平時와는 다르다. 즉 國防國家의 要求하는 文化統制는 平時에 있어서는 可타고 하든 一部의 傾向을 抑制하지 않을 수 없다. 그러나 한쪽으로 우리들은 新秩序를 가지고 指導民族으로써의 重大한 役割을 스스로 지고 있어서 所謂 非常時局은 國家 千萬年의 生命에 比하여 이것은 한 순간에 지내지 않는다. 이 期間에 양성되는 國民文化의 特質이 그 後에 올 時代를 위하여 禍가 및일 것이여서는 안된다. 우리들의 子孫에게 남겨줄 文化的 遺産이 非常時 以外에 通用되지 못할 것이라던가, 國民生活을 떠러치고 가난하게 하는 것 等이여서는 重大한 일이다. 現代 日本文化의 創造는 그 自體로써 다른 諸民族 우에 비치여 줄 眞正한 世界的 文化의 母體가 되는 것이 理想이라고 믿는다. 이 意味에 있어서 今日의 文化政策이 周到한 用意를 가지고 생각하여 國民思想과 日常生活 우에 明確한 指標와 實行力을 얻게 하도록 나는 여러 方面에 努力하려고 한다. 國民 各自는 이 領域에 限하여 貧乏을 참을 必要는 없다. 벋을 수 있는 대로 삗치고 얻을 수 있는 대로 얻는 것이 좋을 것이다. 풍부한 기쁨, 거기에는 國民의 意氣와 굳센 힘이 뵈여질 것이다. ＜201＞
