Traces of the Egyptian Queen, Impressions of the New Battlefield

Alexandria, which received the fierce bombing of the German army, is a city commemorating the great enterprise (霸業) of Alexander the Great, and indeed, Shakespeare's tragedy "Antony and Cleopatra" used this Alexandria as its stage. Cleopatra, who came to rule Egypt (埃及) with the help of Julius Caesar, went afar to Rome seeking him<170> out. It seems she became acquainted with Antony at this time.

Even after Cleopatra returned to her home country, Antony could not forget Cleopatra, abandoned his wife, and crossed over to Egypt. Shakespeare sent his pen to a room in Cleopatra's palace in this Alexandria, unfolding a scene of unparalleled enchanting beauty. However, Antony incurred the resentment of Caesar's son, Augustus, was attacked and defeated, and suspecting Cleopatra, committed suicide.

After his death, Cleopatra tried to get close to Augustus, but her looks having already faded, Cleopatra met her tragic end by letting a poisonous snake bite her breast. It is upon this land of Antony and Cleopatra's exhausted love (戀愛) that the German army (獨軍) has delivered what amounts to a fierce bombing of justice (정의의 猛爆).<171>

**Summary**

The article recounts the story of Antony and Cleopatra, set in Alexandria, Egypt, referencing Shakespeare's tragedy. It summarizes their relationship, Antony's conflict with Augustus, and both their tragic deaths. The author then connects this historical drama to the contemporary WWII context, framing the German bombing of Alexandria as a "fierce bombing of justice" upon the land associated with their "exhausted love." For historians, this piece is notable for its explicit pro-Axis stance, reflecting the wartime propaganda environment in colonial Korea under Japanese rule, where Allied locations were depicted negatively and Axis military actions were justified, even using historical and literary allusions.

埃及女王의 자최 新戰場의 感懷
獨逸軍의 猛爆을 받은 알렉싼도리아는 알렉싼다-大王의 霸業을 記念하는 도시인데, 쎅쓰피어 悲劇 「안토니와 크레오파트라」는 실로 이 알렉싼도리아를 舞臺로 한 것이다. 쮸리아쓰, 씨-자의 도음으로 埃及를 覇하게 한 크레오파츄라는 그를 찾어서<170> 멀리 로-마로 갔다. 이 때에 안토니와 알게 되었든 모양이다. 크레오파츄라가 고국에 도라가서까지 안토니-는 크레오파츄라를 잊지 못하고 妻를 버리고 埃及으로 건너갔다. 쎅쓰피아는 이 알렉싼도리아의 크레오파츄라의 宮殿一室에, 붓을 보내여, 妖艶하기 짝이 없는 장면을 開展하였다. 그렇나 안토니-는 씨-자의 아들, 아우구쓰타스의 원망을 사게 되여, 공격을 받아 패하고, 크레오파츄라를 의심하며 자살한다. 그의 死後 크레파츄라는, 아우구쓰라스와 가깝자고 했으나 容色이 임의 衰退해 번린 크레오파츄라는 가슴을 毒蛇에 빨리며 悲劇的 最後를 마줬다. 이 안토니-와 크레오파츄라의 疲勞해진 戀愛의 地에 獨軍은 정의의 猛爆을 받은 셈이다.<171>
