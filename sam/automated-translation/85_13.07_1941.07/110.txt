Korean Movie Theater in Shanghai

Acquired and Operated for 450,000 Won

Mr. Kim Kyŏng-jae (金璟載), who founded the Shanghai Times (上海時報社) and is actively engaged in the Peace and National Construction Movement (和平建國運動), has recently decided to enter the film business. As a first step, he invested 450,000 won to purchase and operate the "Willy Grand Theatre" (威利大戱院), a 3-story Western-style movie theater previously run by a Chinese individual. In the future, he plans to expand into production and strive forward through film in the movement for the construction of Greater East Asia (大東亞). Furthermore, a recent greeting letter from Mr. Kim is as follows.

Manuscript Fees at Kyŏngsŏng Maeil Sinbo - How Much for One Installment of a Newspaper Novel?

Compared to Tokyo below, manuscript fees (稿料) in Korea are very low. Omitting the manuscript fees of various magazine companies <64>, taking only the Kyŏngsŏng Maeil Sinbo (每新), which pays the highest manuscript fees in Korea, it is 80 chŏn per 400-character manuscript page. This 80 chŏn is also a recent increase. Calculating based on this, one day's installment of a newspaper novel receives 4 wŏn. This is said to be a considerable promotion compared to 5 or 6 years ago when various newspapers like Maeil Sinbo, Tonga Ilbo (東亞), and Chosŏn Ilbo (朝鮮) paid from 1 wŏn 50 chŏn to 2 wŏn, 2 wŏn 50 chŏn for a daily novel installment, with Mr. Yi Kwang-su (李光洙) receiving the highest at 3 wŏn.

Tokyo Kaizōsha Manuscript Fees - Monthly "Bungei" Magazine Prints 8,000 Copies -

The monthly magazine "Bungei" (文藝), published by Tokyo's Kaizōsha (改造社), is said to print and issue 8,000 copies per month. The magazine's manuscript fee is reportedly a minimum of 2 Yen per 100-character manuscript page, up to a maximum of 8 Yen paid to master-class writers (大家級).

Two Poems by Wang Ching-wei (汪精衛氏)

High songs reach distant misty clouds
The stroke of the brush emits gold and stone
A bright heart determined to save the times
Ultimately becomes the rule for the world

Striving relentlessly for forty years
A ray of light hangs before my eyes
As if sitting attentively in the spring breeze
Please wield the whip fiercely for these difficult times

These two poems were composed instantly when Mr. Ken Inukai (犬養健) showed Chairman Wang (汪主席) calligraphy by Elder Moktang (木堂翁) [Inukai Tsuyoshi]. Chairman Wang was moved by the patriotic sincerity of the 78-year-old Elder upon seeing the line "Even in old age, still embracing the heart to save the times" (老來猶抱濟時心) in one of the poems, and immediately took up his brush to write them.

Overseas Information - Various News Heard -

Tolstoy's Granddaughter Marries

It has already been 30 years since the Russian literary giant Leo Tolstoy passed away. His granddaughter, Miss Sonia Tolstaya (쏘-니아·톨스-트 孃), married Raphael Lopukhin (라풸·로포-크린), also from a noble Russian family, in a small Orthodox church in Clamart near Paris on April 29th, celebrating their splendid wedding ceremony.

Best-Selling Books in America

At the annual convention of the American Booksellers Association, the winning works for the customary American "National Book Award" were announced. The winner in the fiction category was the debut work "How Green Was My Valley" by the young British author Richard Llewellyn (레에링). This work, a reminiscence of enjoyably spent past times in the Welsh coal mining region, was published in Britain the year before last and became a bestseller after being published in America last year. In the non-fiction (other writings) category, Hans Zinsser's (진사아) "As I Remember Him" (나의 回想記) was selected as the winning work. Zinsser was a medical scientist who studied infectious diseases and visited China and our Japan, traveling around the world. These records of medical journeys across the globe formed "As I Remember Him." This book also became one of the bestsellers in America starting last year and sold extremely well.

600,000 Workers in One German Factory

The Göring Works (케-링크 공장) [Hermann Göring Werke], founded by Field Marshal Göring (케-링크 元帥), has become Germany's largest armaments factory, currently employing 600,000 workers. Furthermore, it also encompasses mining, shipbuilding, etc., under its umbrella.

Top 10 Masterpiece Novels

A certain club in America asked 159 critics to select the masterpiece novels of the past year. The result was that "For Whom the Bell Tolls" (누구를 위하여 죽엄의 종이 우는가) by Ernest Hemingway (아-네스도·해딘쿠에이) overwhelmingly took first place. Second place and below were "New England: Indian Summer" (뉴-·잉구란드 인도의 여름) [by Van Wyck Brooks], "Oliver Wiswell" (우리우아·우이스엘) [by Kenneth Roberts], "How Green Was My Valley" (우리골은 어떻게 파래졌나), "Native Son" (黑人의 子) [by Richard Wright], "You Can't Go Home Again" (집에 다시 돌아오지 않는다) [by Thomas Wolfe], "Audubon's America" (오-태유본의 아메리카), "Sapphira and the Slave Girl" (사후이라와 少女奴隷) [by Willa Cather], in that order, but the 10th place was not decided. The "New Republic" (뉴·리빠브릿크) magazine reportedly criticized this, asking who selected the 159 critics, whether these critics had seen all the good books of the past year, and what exactly constitutes a masterpiece.

British Military Service Up to Age 40

In Britain, those over 30 were previously unrelated to military service, but now it has been decided to conscript energetic individuals up to the age of 40. Conscription is expected within the next 2-3 months. Furthermore, those engaged in munitions industries are expected to be excluded.

French Author Maurois' Recent Situation

André Maurois (앙드레·모-로아), who raised Japanese paper prices <66> with "Why France Fell" (佛蘭西는 敗하였다), is said to be Jewish. He went to America while watching France fall. As the pen name André Maurois somehow evoked memories of the past, he seems to have completely erased it and returned to his real name, Émile Herzog (에미-ㄹ 헬츠오-끄). He is reportedly a very wealthy man who owned several factories in Rouen, France. However, the French authorities, citing his Jewishness and his flight abandoning his homeland after its defeat, confiscated the factories and turned them into state-run factories.

Americans Do Not Demand War

Major General S. L. A. Marshall (S·쫀·손 장군) [Translator's note: The original gives S. Jjon. Son changgun. Likely S.L.A. Marshall, a prominent Scripps-Howard military correspondent], a military commentator for the Scripps-Howard (스크리푸·스하와-드) newspaper chain, recently discussed the Japan issue in an editorial. He argued that 99 percent of the American people oppose intervention in the Far East conflict, and 80 percent have no understanding of the situation there, stating as follows:
"Japan, Germany, and Italy promised mutual defense if another country wages war against one of them. This type of agreement is nothing new. Britain and France once concluded a similar agreement with Poland. So why is it being said that US-Japan relations have worsened due to this Tripartite Pact, as if there is now a risk of war? Does America demand war? Absolutely not; 99 percent of the American people do not demand war. And 80 percent understand nothing of the Far East conflict. In short, these arguments for war are not the will of the American people, nor the will of Congress, but merely the noise made by professional war provocateurs," thus attracting attention by calling out the Far East debate.

Germany's Food Problem

In the First World War, Germans suffered from starvation for nearly 5 years, with the number reaching over 800,000, and the damage was particularly severe for women and children. Due to the lack of livestock feed, millions of pigs were slaughtered at once, which was one of the causes of defeat. However, this time, Germany established the Food Rationing Association (獨逸食糧配給聯盟) and started watertight rationing. As a result, the monthly ration includes 9.5 kg of bread, 2 kg of meat, 1,080 grams of fat, etc., and those engaged in heavy physical labor generally receive double this amount. They receive 900 grams of sugar, about 2.5 times the amount in the Mainland (Japan 内地), and even 600 grams of marmalade. A family of five receives over 35 eggs per month. Even more surprising is that the quality of food items is said to be exactly the same as during peacetime.

Comedies Arising from US Conscription System

In America, which is rushing headlong into military expansion, the conscription system was recently implemented nationwide. However, they decided to exempt only "conscientious objectors" (곤웬샤스·옵젝타-), such as pastors from seminaries and particularly devout pacifists. Suddenly, seminary enrollments surged, and the number of fake believers increased. Deciding this wouldn't do, state governors established numerous review boards to severely punish fakers as draft evaders. Recently, however, "Ero-Hans" [lewd men] who attack women have frequently appeared in Broadway and other places. Furthermore, they openly approach young women <67> in broad daylight in front of others, demanding kisses. When police promptly arrested them for indecent assault against women, the men just laughed. Finding their laughter too strange, an investigation revealed they were doing it to obtain exemption from conscription, based on the rule that "criminals have no right to be drafted."

Death Penalty for Robbery in France Too

The world was surprised when Germany executed black market dealers (暗取引者), but in Paris, France, too, it has been decided to impose the death penalty to eradicate wartime crime for offenses like illegal house trespassing after people have evacuated to air-raid shelters during blackouts or night air raids, or when the perpetrator carries a weapon. It is said that legal procedures will be expedited, and appeals will not be permitted.

UK Mobilizes 700,000 Women

In Britain, 700,000 women are said to have participated in the home front (銃後) national defense movement. These women are participating in the Home Guard (鄕土軍), fire brigades, as bus conductors, and also in the Air Force and Navy. <68>

**Summary**

This article from the July 1941 issue of "Samch'ŏlli" presents a collection of news snippets covering domestic Korean affairs under Japanese rule and international events. Topics include statistics on Japanese-Korean marriages, comparative life expectancies, the activities of pro-Japanese Koreans in Shanghai (establishing media and businesses), colonial policies regarding settlers in Manchuria, personal anecdotes like Yun Ch'i-ho's motto, comparisons of manuscript fees in Korea and Japan, and updates on the Nanjing government. International news covers cultural items (Tolstoy's granddaughter, US bestsellers), war-related developments (German industry, UK conscription, André Maurois' situation, US public opinion on war involvement, German food rationing, French crime penalties, UK women's mobilization), and Soviet demographics. From a Korean colonial history perspective, the article is revealing about the promotion of Japanese-Korean intermarriage (Na선일체 policy), the encouragement of Korean settlement in Manchuria, the activities of pro-Japanese collaborationist figures like Kim Kyŏng-jae in occupied China, the relatively low compensation for Korean writers compared to Japanese counterparts, and the inclusion of Koreans (though a small number) in Soviet census data, hinting at the diaspora caused by earlier migrations and potentially the recent forced relocations. Historians might find the marriage statistics, Manchurian settlement details, Shanghai activities, and the brief mention of Koreans in the Soviet census particularly interesting.

第1次세계대전에는 겨우 5年간에 饑餓에 허덕인 獨逸人은 거의 80萬人餘의 多數 達하였으며 그 被害는 특히 부인과 자녀에게 많았다. 그리고 가축사료의 缺乏 때문에 數百萬頭의 돼지를 일시에 屠殺한 일도 있어서 敗戰原因의 하나였었다. 그런데 이번 獨逸食糧配給聯盟을 創設하여서 물도 샐틈없는 배급을 開始한 결과, 月次定量은 빵 9.5키로, 肉 2키로, 지방 1.080그람 등이며 過度筋肉노동자는 대체로 이 2배의 배급을 받는다. 砂糖은 內地(日本)의 약 2배 반 9百그람을 받으며 「마-마레-드」까지 6百그람을 얻는다. 鷄卵도 5인의 가정에서는 月 35個餘며 더욱 놀라운 것은 식량품의 품질도 平時와 조금도 다름없는 동일한 것이라 한다.

米國 徵兵制度로 因한 喜劇

軍擴에 狂奔하는 미국에서는 요새 徵兵制度를 전국 一齊히 施行하였는데 다만 「곤웬샤스·옵젝타-」 즉 양심적 반대자로서의 神學校 출신의 牧師와 특히 신앙심이 깊은 平和論者만을 제외하기로 했더니 갑자기 神學校 입학자가 激增하였으며 거즛 敎徒가 增加되였다. 이래서는 안되겠다고 各 州知事는 多數의 審査官을 설치하여 僞物은 모조리 徵兵忌避者로 嚴罰에 處하기로 했다. 그런데 최근에는 「뿌로-드웨-」를 비롯하여 各處에 婦女를 습격하는 에로漢이 瀕瀕이 出沒하며 더욱이 白晝에 他人 앞에서 공공연하게 妙齡婦女에게<67> 달려들어 키쓰를 請하므로 警官이 곧 逮捕하여 婦女侮辱罪에 돌렸는바 *漢은 웃고만 있었다. 웃는 것이 너무 이상해서 조사해 본 결과, 「범죄자는 징병될 권리가 없다」는 징병면제자격을 얻기 위해서라고.

佛蘭西에서도 强盜에 死刑
