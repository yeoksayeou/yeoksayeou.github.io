Soldiers' Favorite Books
Eagerly Reading "Mein Kampf"

A recent investigation looked into what German soldiers on the French front are reading, based on statistics from the first 40 days after book sales began at the front lines. According to these statistics, books with valuable content sell much better than entertainment materials. Hitler's "Mein Kampf," Rodenberg's "The Myth of the Twentieth Century," Nietzsche's "Thus Spoke Zarathustra," etc., sell like hotcakes every day, with almost no remaining stock. Trivial pamphlets generally do not sell well; instead, works of high literary value sell better. For instance, Hans Grimm's "People Without Space," other African-themed works, Kolbenheyer's novels, Friedrich Pulk's "Great Voyage," and novels by Emil Strauss, Theodor Fontane, and Knut Hamsun sell well. Among poetry collections, those by Weinheber, Brunk Schumann, etc., are popular, and "Letters of German Students Killed in Action" and Walter Flex's "Wanderer Between Two Worlds" are reportedly sold out. Small paperback series like Reclam, Insel-Bücherei, and Kleine Bücherei are welcomed, showing the surprising reading capacity of soldiers at the front.

Playwrights, Actors Become Soldiers

Jackie Coogan, 26 this year, reportedly underwent a draft examination in Hollywood and was assigned to the U.S. Air Force. Simultaneously, Sidney Kingsley, the playwright famous for the work "Dead End," also passed the physical examination and was reportedly assigned to the army as a private. Kingsley is said to have resolved to use this opportunity to write a play about the U.S. Army.

Germany's Ticket System

The clothing ticket system implemented in Britain seems to be an imitation of one already implemented in Germany. Germany's clothing ticket system is a so-called "point system," with each person, male or female alike, allocated one hundred points per year. That is, one is assigned 100 tickets, and for example, for women's items, one pair of stockings costs four points (four tickets), a handkerchief costs 2 points. For men's items, a Western suit costs 60 points, a collar 26 points, and a pair of gloves 7 points. Within this 100-point limit, one selects and buys what one needs, but there are various rules.<113>

**Summary**

This article from July 1941 reports on three topics: the reading preferences of German soldiers on the French front, the conscription of American entertainment figures, and Germany's clothing rationing system. German soldiers reportedly favor serious philosophical and literary works like "Mein Kampf," Nietzsche, and novels by Hamsun over light entertainment. American figures Jackie Coogan and Sidney Kingsley were drafted, with Kingsley planning to write about army life. Germany's point-based clothing rationing system, which Britain seemingly copied, allocated 100 points per person annually for purchasing items like suits or stockings. From a historical perspective, the article is interesting as it presents news from Axis Germany and the Allied USA side-by-side in a Korean colonial context. The focus on German soldiers' intellectualism and the detailed description of the German rationing system could serve subtle propaganda purposes, portraying Germany as cultured and organized, while the American news focuses on celebrities entering military service.

兵士의 愛讀書
「나의 鬪爭」을 熱讀

佛蘭西戰線에 있는 獨逸兵士는 무었을 읽고 있나 하는 것을 요사이 조사하였는대, 이것은 전선에 있어서 서적판매를 개시한지 40일간의 통계이다. 이 통계에 의하면 오락물보다도 내용적으로 가치있는 책이 제일 많이 팔린다 한다. 히틀러의 「나의 鬪爭」, 로오덴벨크의 「二十世紀의 神話」, 니체의 「쯔아라토쓰토라는 이렇게 말한다」 등은 매일 날개를 돛인 듯이 팔려 그진 殘部가 없고 際物의 팜푸렛수는 대개 팔리지 않고 외이려 문학적 가치가 고상한 것이 잘 팔린다고 한다. 즉 한스·크림의 「土地없는 백성」 기타 아푸리카 것인 골벤하이야냐의 小說, 푸리도리쯔히·뿔크의 「大航海」, 에밀슈도파우스, 데오톨·후온타네구누-로·함슨 등의 소설이 잘 팔린다. 詩集에는 와인해파-, 뿌릉크슈망의 것 등이며 「獨逸戰歿學生의 便紙」와 왈타-·후레쿠스의 「두 世界를 彷徨하는 者」는 切品이라고 한다. 레구람이나 인젤 文庫라든지 구라이네 문고 등과 같은 小型文庫가 환영받는다는데 戰線의 병사의 讀書力은 놀라웁게 보여지고 있다.

劇作家, 俳優 軍人되다

今年 26세의 쟈쯔끼·구캉은 하리웃드에서 徵兵檢査를 받어 米國空軍에 편입되게 되였다 한다. 이와 동시에 「뎃트·엔트」의 作으로 유명한 劇作家 시도니이·도킹구스레이도 체격검사에 합격되여 一兵卒로써 육군에 편입하게 되였다 한다. 킹구스레이는 이 기회를 이용하여 米國陸軍에 관한 劇을 써볼 결심을 하였다 한다.

獨逸의 切符制

英國에서 실시되는 衣類切符制는 임이 獨逸에서 실시된 것을 模방한 것인듯 하단다. 獨逸의 衣類切符制는 소위 「点數式」이라는 것으로 남녀가 꼭 같이 한 사람 앞에 年百点이다. 즉 切符가 百枚라 정하고, 가령 여자의 것이면 양말 一足四点(切符四枚) 한카치는 2点, 남자의 것이면 洋服一着에 60点, 칼라 26点, 장갑 한 벌에 7点, 이렇게 100点限을 해서 자기의 필요로 하는 것을 선택하여 사는 것인데, 여기에 여러 가지 규칙이 있다.<113>
