Magazine Title: 삼천리 제13권 제7호
Publication Date: 1941-07-01
Article Title: 獨逸兵士의 노래 (Songs of German Soldiers)
Author: 金善夫(譯) (Kim Sŏnbu [Trans.])
Article Type: 시 (Poetry)

Advance into France

One. When we advanced into France
There were three of us
One was a Jäger, one was a sniper
And I was the standard-bearer of the heavy cavalry.

Two. We three comrades, three hearts
Marched following the military flag
When our flag fluttered on the Liège front line
Oh, Heaven, please help this country.

Three. When we advanced again
There were two of us left,
One was the Jäger of Budgebul
And I was the standard-bearer of the heavy cavalry.

Four. Two comrades and two hearts
Greeted each other in the morning
But by evening, in the Ronjereoseu trench (론제러스 塹壕)
He bid farewell to the flagpole.<178>

Five. As he bid farewell to the flagpole
The remaining comrade also died
Then, beloved Jäger
Receive the farewell of me, the heavy cavalryman.

Six. Ah, Mother, beloved Mother
I can only pray to Heaven,
The military flag entrusted by the Captain
Now flutters alone in my hand.

Raise High the Flag

One. Raise high the flag
Marching in step
The Stormtroopers march strongly
The souls of comrades fallen due to the Reds and Reactionaries
March together with us.

Two. The wide road is for the Brownshirt Brigade (褐色쌰쓰隊) as they please,
The wide road is liberated for the Stormtroopers,
Behold, millions
Look up hopefully at the Hakenkreuz (하-겐, 쿠로잇)
The day we gain freedom and bread
The dawn of joy shall come.

Three. The assembly bugle sounds,
Preparations for battle are complete,
As Hitler's (힛틀너) flags flutter on every street
The fate of former days is already ending.

Four. Raise high the flag
Arms linked firmly
The Stormtroopers march proudly<179>
The souls of comrades fallen to the Reds and Reactionaries
Walk together with us.

Comrade

One. I had a comrade
A comrade like no other!
When the drum beat boom boom, signaling the advance
He marched in step together with me.

Two. A bullet flew by
And struck him down in an instant,
His body, in that moment,
Just collapsed.

Three. When I ran to him
He still extended his hand
But it was already too late, I could not grasp your hand,
You, my cherished comrade
Rest peacefully, close your eyes quietly.<180>

**Summary**

This article presents Korean translations of three German songs attributed to soldiers. The first, "Advance into France," depicts the dwindling numbers of comrades during wartime, focusing on sacrifice and the standard-bearer's duty. The second, "Raise High the Flag," is a translation of the Nazi Party anthem (Horst-Wessel-Lied), celebrating the Stormtroopers (SA), Hitler, the Hakenkreuz, and the fight against "Reds and Reactionaries" for "freedom and bread." The third, "Comrade," is a lament for a fallen friend in battle, likely based on the German song "Der gute Kamerad." Published in colonial Korea in 1941, these translations served to promote the martial spirit and ideology of Nazi Germany, Japan's Axis ally, to a Korean audience. Historians might find the direct translation and dissemination of Nazi propaganda, including the Horst-Wessel-Lied, in a mainstream Korean magazine during this period particularly revealing about the extent of Axis ideological promotion under Japanese rule.

獨逸兵士의 노래
						金善夫(譯)
佛蘭西進擊

一. 佛蘭西에 進軍하였을 적에
우리들은 셋이었네
하나는 獵兵, 하나는 狙擊兵
그리고 나는 重騎兵의 旗手였네,

二. 우리들 세 동무, 세 心臟은
軍旗를 따라 行進하였었네
리에-지 戰線에 우리旗 번득일제
오오, 하늘이여 이 나라를 도와주소서,

三. 다시 우리가 進擊할 때에
우리들은 둘이 되어 섰네,
하나는 붇게불의 獵兵
그리고 나는 重騎兵의 旗手였네,

四. 두 동무와 두 心臟은
아츰에는 서로 인사하였더니
저녁이 되자 론제러스 塹壕 속에서
그만 旗때를 向해 告別하였네.<178>

五. 旗때를 向해 告別할 때에
남어지 戰友마저 죽었었네
그러면 사량하는 獵兵이여
重騎兵인 나의 고별을 받으라.

六. 아아, 어머니여, 사랑하는 어머이여
다만 하늘에 빌뿐이네,
隊長이 맡겨준 軍旗가
지금은 혼자 내손에서 번득이네.

旗때를 높이 들어

一. 旗때를 높이 들고
발을 마추어
突擊隊는 힘세게 行進을 하네
赤과 反動 때문에 너머진 同志들 魂은
우리와 함께 걸어 나간다.

二. 넓은 길은 褐色쌰쓰隊의 마음대로다,
넓은 길은 突擊隊에게 解放되었다,
보라, 幾百萬人이
希望에 넘처 하-겐, 쿠로잇을 처다보네
自由와 빵을 얻어내는 날
기뿜의 새벽이 오리라.

三. 集合나팔은 울리여온다,
戰鬪의 준비는 다되었다,
모든 거리거리에 힛틀너의 깃때 번득이자
前날의 운명은 이미 끈어저간다.

四. 旗ㅅ때를 높이 들고
팔을 힘굳게끼고
突擊隊는 堂堂히 行進한다<179>
赤과 反動에 너머진 동무의 魂은
우리와 함께 것는 것이다.

戰友

一. 나에게 戰友 있었거니
둘도 없는 戰友가!
북이 둥둥 울려 進軍하랄제
그는 나와 함께 발마처 나갔거니.

二. 총알이 날려와
삽시간에 그를 께둘렸네,
그의 몸은 그순간에
그만 쓰러지고 말었네.

三. 달려갔을 적에
그는 아직 손을 내밀었으나
때는 벌써 늦어 내 그대 손을 잡지 못하였네,
그대여 그리운 나의 전우여
평안히 잘자라, 고요히 눈감으라.<180>
