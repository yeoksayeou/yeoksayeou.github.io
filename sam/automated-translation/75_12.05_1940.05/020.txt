Korean Compatriots in Wuhan
						By Yu Sang'ŭn

Everyone is doing well.

The Wuhan Three Towns (武漢三鎭) refers to Wuchang (武昌), Hankou (漢口), and Hanyang (漢陽). The climate here is mild, making it a very good place to live. Local products are mainly rice, hides, and fish. Currently, the highest governing body for the construction of the New China (新中華) here is the Advisory Council (叅議府), and besides that, there are the Hubei Provincial Office (湖北省 公署) and the Wuhan Municipal Government (武漢市政府). Each institution has established training centers (훈련소) to cultivate talented individuals who will be active in the New China. The number of people coming and going on the streets is so numerous that walking is difficult due to the congestion. Amidst this complexity, and despite being under a wartime system, countless groups of blind beggars (맹인 걸인) are scattered everywhere (散散步步) on street corners, seeking a living. The number of compatriots residing in the Wuhan Three Towns is about 6,000 mainland Japanese (내지인) and about 2,000 peninsular people (반도인) [Koreans].

Their living conditions are generally affluent (裕富하며), and more than half have accumulated substantial wealth due to the Sino-Japanese Incident (日支事變). People's occupations are varied, but mainly consist of trading businesses (무역상), general stores (잡화상), innkeeping (여관업), restaurants (음식점), and comfort station businesses (위안소업). Besides these, there are also individuals managing orchards and people who have established educational institutions.

However, as it is a war zone, although the economy is booming (호경기), prices for various goods are very expensive (빗싸다).
The business with the best economic conditions is the comfort station business. A single woman's daily income (수입금) is over 50 or 60 won.
Also, traders generally have their main office in Hankou and set up branches in front-line areas (일선지대) like Xinyang (信陽) or Yuezhou (岳州), transporting goods from Hankou to sell there.

In the future, if public order (치안) is maintained along the Yangtze River (楊子江), I believe this Hankou will become a base (근거지) for the activities of our compatriots.
Regarding the climate, the period between heat and cold is very short, so the cool season (凉節) is long, and it can be seen as a suitable place (적당지) for us Japanese people (日本人) to live.
The weather (일기) is hot during July and August, and cold only during the periods of Major Cold (大寒節) and the Beginning of Spring (立春節); otherwise, the climate is the same as Korea's autumn season (秋節).
I wish that during this state of emergency (비상 시국), people would not only be active within the country (국내) but would take a step further<23> and advance overseas (해외) to be active.

Hankou Peace Japanese Language School (漢口 和平日語學校)

This school was founded on September 1, Shōwa 14 (1939).
The purpose (趣意) of this school is as written below (左記와 如하다).
一, At this time of constructing the New Order in East Asia, to complete its great mission, it is necessary to foster the firm unity of the Chinese and Japanese peoples and strive to fulfill one part of that power. (際玆建設東亞新秩序完成其大使命要助長中日兩國民團結力之堅固企充其一分部之力量)
二, For the harmonization work (融和工作) between the two peoples of the same script and same race (同文同種), rather than relying on written characters, it is better to rely on spoken language; therefore, it is necessary to establish an institution for language dissemination. (同文同種兩國民間融和工作比於固文字寧可固言語所以要成立一個語學普及之機關)
三, Regarding the fusion (熔合) of the inherent cultures of the two peoples, promoting sound ideology, establishing the hundred-year plan for East Asia, we hope to make even the slightest contribution. (對干熔合兩國民之固有文化發揮健全之思想樹立東亞百年大計期有綠毫之貢獻)
End.
The number of students is about 60 or 70 in total, including those in the Japanese Language Department (日語科) and the Beijing Mandarin Department (北京官話科).
By occupation, the students are police officers from the Chinese side (中國측), employees (雇人) of Japanese (日本측) commercial and industrial companies (상공회사), and company employees (회사원).<23>

**Summary**

This 1940 article by Yu Sang'ŭn describes the situation of the approximately 2,000 Korean residents in Japanese-occupied Wuhan, China. The author notes their general affluence, attributing much wealth accumulation to the ongoing Sino-Japanese War. Common Korean occupations included trade, general stores, inns, restaurants, and notably, comfort station businesses, which are described as the most profitable. The author views Wuhan as a potentially important base for Korean activity if Japanese control is maintained and encourages Koreans to seek opportunities overseas in occupied territories rather than staying in Korea or Japan proper. The piece also details the establishment and purpose of the Hankou Peace Japanese Language School, aimed at promoting Japanese language and ideology for Sino-Japanese "harmonization" under the New Order, attended by Chinese police and Japanese company employees.

From a historical perspective, the article is revealing about the complex roles Koreans played within the Japanese empire, particularly the economic opportunism (including involvement in the 'comfort women' system via 'comfort station businesses') that existed alongside colonial subjugation. It also showcases the Japanese ideological justification ('New Order in East Asia', 'Same Script, Same Race') used to frame expansion and the efforts to impose Japanese language and culture in occupied areas, involving Korean participation. Historians might find the explicit mention of Koreans running 'comfort station businesses' and the encouragement for Koreans to move into occupied China particularly noteworthy.

武漢의 朝鮮 동포
						柳相垠
모다들 잘 있습니다

武漢三鎭이란 것은, 武昌, 漢口, 漢陽을 稱함이다. 當地는 기후가 온화하여 거주하기 매우 좋은 지방이다. 산물로는 주로 米, 皮物, 어류 등이다. 현재 當地의 新中華建設 최고 기관은 叅議府, 其外에 湖北省 公署, 武漢市政府 등이다. 그런데 각 기관에서는 新中華에 활약할 인재들 양성키 위하여 각 기관에는 훈련소가 설치되여 있다. 그런데 가로상에 왕래하는 인간은 어찌나 다수한지 보행하기 어려울만치 복잡하다. 이러한 복잡한 중 또한 전시 체제하에 있는 데로 불구하고 거리거리 모퉁 모퉁이에는 맹인 걸인들 떼가 해아릴 수 없을만치 散散步步하며 생을 구하고 있다. 그런데 武漢三鎭에 거주하는 동포의 수는 내지인 약 육천 명 반도인 약 이천 명이다.
其 생활 상태는 대개 裕富하며 또한 日支事變으로 의하여 반수 이상은 막대한 물질을 적립하고 있다. 각 人의 직업은 各色各種이나 주로 무역상 잡화상 여관업 음식점 위안소업 등이다. 其外에 과수원을 경영하는 인사도 있고 교육기관을 설치한 사람도 있다.
그런데 戰地인 것 만치 경기가 호경기이지만 각종 물가가 매우 빗싸다.
제일 경기 좋기는 위안소업이다. 여인 一人이 매일 수입금이 5,60원 이상이다.
또한 무역상인은 대개는 본점을 漢口에 두고 지점을 일선지대 信陽이나 岳州 방면에 설치하고 漢口에서 물품을 운반해 갖다 판매한다.
장래 楊子江 연안의 치안의 유지된다면 이 漢口는 우리 동포의 활약의 근거지가 되리라고 생각한다.
爲中 기후의 한서간의 기간이 매우 짧으므로 凉節이 길고 우리 日本人의 생활에 적당지라고 볼 수 있다.
일기가 뜨겁기는 7,8월간이고 춥기는 大寒節과 立春節 기간 외에는 朝鮮의 秋節과 동일한 기후이다.
원컨대 이 비상 시국에 국내에서만 활약하지 말고 좀 더 일층 나아가<23> 해외에 진출하여 활약하는 것이 좋다고 생각한다.

漢口 和平日語學校

본교 창립은 昭和 14년 9월 1일이다.
본교의 趣意는 左記와 如하다.
一, 際玆建設東亞新秩序完成其大使命要助長中日兩國民團結力之堅固企充其一分部之力量
二, 同文同種兩國民間融和工作比於固文字寧可固言語所以要成立一個語學普及之機關
三, 對干熔合兩國民之固有文化發揮健全之思想樹立東亞百年大計期有綠毫之貢獻
이상
그런데 학생 수는 日語科 北京官話科生 합하여 약 6,70명이다.
학생 직업별은 中國측 경찰, 日本측 상공회사의 雇人, 회사원 등이다.<23>
