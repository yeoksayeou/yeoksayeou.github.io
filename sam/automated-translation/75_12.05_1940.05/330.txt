Literary Scene Comings and Goings
						Samch'ŏllisa

Mr. Yi Hŏn'gu (Novelist) has reportedly moved from the Arts and Sciences Department of the Chosŏn Ilbo Company to the Publishing Department and taken charge of editing "Yŏsŏng" (Woman) magazine.

Mr. Chang Hyŏkchu (Novelist), who resides in Tokyo, recently returned to Taegu due to his father's passing and briefly visited Kyŏngsŏng on his way back.

Mr. Ch'ae Man-sik (Novelist), who postponed the publication celebration for his works "T'angnyu" (Muddy Stream) and "Ch'ae Man-sik Short Story Collection" indefinitely due to illness, occasionally comes up to Kyŏngsŏng from Kaesŏng while recuperating. When I met him the other day:
"Good heavens, telling an old man like me to write about views on love all of a sudden, what's this all about?"
"Old? You still look like you're in the prime of youth."
"Don't say that. My appearance may be so, but I'm old inside. Worn out by the hardships of life. If I try to get a job, I can't make a living on that paltry 60 or 70 wŏn, and if I try to live off manuscript fees, in Chosŏn, you're bound to starve to death. Truly, I'm old inside." Nevertheless, he produced a manuscript recording the youthful sentiment that "Love is a flower."

Mr. Pak Yŏnghŭi (Critic) - Recently met him by chance at Ewha Women's College:
"What brings you here? In this world of women..."
"What brings *you* here?"
"I came in my usual capacity as a reporter, but..."
"My daughter is enrolling."
"In what department?"
"In the most splendid department..."
"The most splendid department?"
"The Music Department."
"Haha, an artist raises only an artist's daughter. Just listening to your daughter's music, novels must surely flow effortlessly from your pen."

Mr. Kim Chŏnghyŏk (Film Person) - Having moved from Cho Yŏng (Chosŏn Film Co.) to Koryŏ Film Company, Mr. Kim is always bustling about and is said to be planning to launch a film magazine this time.

Mr. Chŏng Hyŏnu (Painter) - Mr. Chŏng recently bought a new house on Mado Island and seems to be starting a new life and savoring the taste of newlywed life for the first time with the pianist, Mrs. Namgung Yoanna.

Mr. Im Hwa (Poet) - Joined Omun Publishing House, which focuses on publishing Korean classic literature, funded by Mr. Sŏl Wŏnsik.<191>

**Summary**

This article provides brief updates on the activities and personal lives of several prominent Korean cultural figures in May 1940. It notes job changes (Yi Hŏn'gu, Kim Chŏnghyŏk, Im Hwa), recent travels (Chang Hyŏkchu), struggles with health and finances contrasted with creative output (Ch'ae Man-sik), family milestones (Pak Yŏnghŭi's daughter enrolling in college, Chŏng Hyŏnu's new marriage and home), and new ventures (Kim Chŏnghyŏk's film magazine, Im Hwa's work at a classics publishing house). The snippets offer a glimpse into the Korean literary and artistic scene during the colonial period. For historians, the article reveals the interconnectedness of the cultural world (publishing, newspapers, film, academia, arts), the economic precarity faced even by established writers like Ch'ae Man-sik under colonial rule, and the continuation of cultural production and personal life events amidst the broader political context. The mention of specific publications, companies, and personal details provides concrete data points for cultural history studies of the era.

						삼천리사
李軒求씨(소설가)는 朝鮮日報社 학예부에서 출판부로 자리를 옮겨 「女性」 편집을 맡게 되였다 한다.
張赫宙씨(소설가) 東京에 계신 氏는 얼마 전에 부친상을 당하여 大邱로 귀향하였던 길에 京城에 잠간 다녀가다.
蔡萬植씨(소설가) 병으로 氏의 작품 「濁流」「蔡萬植短篇集」 출판 기념회를 무기 연기시킨 氏는 病後의 몸을 이끄시고, 종종 開城에서 京城에 올라 오신다. 그런데 일전에 만나저,
「아아니, 늙은 날더러 새삼스럽게 연애관을 쓰라니 그 무슨 일이오?」
「늙다니? 아직 새파란 청춘으로 뵈는데.」
「말마슈. 겉은 그래도 속은 늙었다오. 생활고에 부댁겨서. 그런데 취직하자니 그깐 놈의 6, 70원 돈으로는 생활할 수가 없고, 稿料로 살자니 朝鮮서는 똑 굶어 죽게 마련이니까. 참말 속이 늙소.」하면서도 「戀愛는 꽃이니라」하는 새파란 청춘의 감정을 기록한 원고를 내여 놓으시다.
朴英熙씨(평론가) 日前 梨花專門學校에서 氏를 우연히 만나
「이게 웬일이십니까? 이런 女國世上에서...」
「당신은 웬일이시우?」
「난 例의 기자의 직책으로 왔지만...」
「나는 딸년이 입학하게 돼서」
「무슨 과에요?」
「제일 찬란한 과에...」
「제일 찬란한 과라니?」
「음악과」
「하하, 예술가는 예술가의 딸만 두시누먼. 따님의 음악만을 들으셔도 소설은 저절로 술술 씨여지시켔군요.」
金正革씨(영화인) 朝映에서 高麗 映畵社로. 자리를 옮기신 氏는 늘 분주히 돌아다니시며 금번 영화 잡지를 발간하기로 한다고.
鄭玄雄씨(화가) 氏는 금번 *島에다가 새집을 하나 사서 피아니스트 南宮요안나 부인과 처음으로 새 가정을 이루시고 신혼의 滋味를 이제부터 시작하시는 모양.
林和씨(시인) 薛元植씨의 출자로 朝鮮 고전 문학 출판을 爲主하는 梧文出版社에 입사.<191>
