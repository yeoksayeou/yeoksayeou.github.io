**French Literary Giant Zola's Centennial**

On April 11th, to commemorate the 100th anniversary of the birth of the literary giant Mr. Emile Zola, it was decided to hold a ceremony at the Panthéon, initiated by Mr. Herriot (Speaker of the Chamber of Deputies), president of the "Zola Friendship Society" (조라 友會). The Minister of Education also agreed to cooperate. However, due to the wartime situation, the ceremony will be held simply. Meanwhile, Zola's daughter, Ms. Denise Le Blond-Zola, wishes for Zola enthusiasts in Japan (日本) to send Japanese literature concerning Zola for the Zola museum.

**Romeo's Tomb**

In the Italian city of Verona, the setting of Shakespeare's famous tragedy "Romeo and Juliet," there is a small church. There, the tomb of Romeo—thought to be a fictional character by Shakespeare—currently exists. Beside this tomb stands a bust of Shakespeare, and next to that, there is a post labelled "Juliet's Letters." According to local legend, if young women troubled by love place letters detailing their painful circumstances into this post, Juliet will lend them good wisdom from the other side (幽明의 彼岸).<135>

**Summary**

This bulletin board style article presents two brief cultural notes from Europe. The first reports on the planned centenary celebration for Emile Zola in Paris, noting its simplification due to the war and a request from Zola's daughter for Japanese materials related to Zola for a museum. The second describes the purported tomb of Romeo in Verona, Italy, associated with Shakespeare's play, and a local legend about a mailbox where troubled lovers can seek advice from Juliet. For historians of colonial Korea, the article highlights the continued interest in and reporting on European high culture within Korean intellectual circles, even during wartime. The mention of Zola's daughter requesting Zola-related materials specifically from *Japan* is particularly revealing, suggesting Japan's perceived status as a significant center for the study or appreciation of European literature in East Asia at the time, potentially acting as a conduit or filter for such cultural information reaching Korea.

三千里 揭示板
佛文豪 「조라 百年祭」

4월 11일, 문호 에밀 조라씨의 生誕 백년제를 기념하려고, 「조라 友會」의 회장 에리오씨(하원의장)의 발기로 「판테온」에서 개최키로 되었으며 文部大臣도 협력하기로 하였다. 그런데 전시 下이여서 祭典은 간소하게 행하며, 조라의 여식 또니-스 루부론 조라씨는 日本에 있는 조라 애호자에 대하여 조라 박물관을 위해 조라에 관한 日本의 문헌을 보내주기를 원한다고.

로미오의 묘

섹쓰피아의 名비극 「로미오」와 「쥬리엩」의 배경지인 伊太利 베에로나 시가에 자그마한 교회당이 있는데 거기엔 이 名비극-섹쓰피아의 가공 인물이라 생각하는 로미오의 묘가 현존해 있다. 그리고 이 묘 곁엔 섹스피아의 흉상이 있고 또 그 한 옆엔 「쥬리엩의 편지」라고 하는 掛札한 포스트가 서 있다. 이 토지의 전설에 의하면 연애에 번뇌하는 젊은 여성들이 그 괴로운 사연을 쓴 글발을 이 포스트에 넣으면 幽明의 彼岸에서 쥬리에트가 좋은 지혜를 빌려 준다고 한단다.<135>
