						Samch'ŏlli Editorial Department

1. Reform of the Christian Literature Society of Korea
The Christian Literature Society of Korea (朝鮮基督敎書會), a joint institution of the Korean Presbyterian Church (朝鮮長老會) and the Methodist Church (監理會), possesses a long history spanning 51 years since its founding. However, it has traditionally been under the control of foreign missionary influence. Recently, recognizing the current trends (大勢), there has been a move to completely eliminate foreign influence.

Following a resolution at the regular board meeting on March 16th to transfer the management rights of its business to Koreans, the matter was delegated to its executive committee. The main points of the policy decided upon on March 28th are as follows:
1. Select Mr. Yang Chu-sam (梁桂三氏 - presumed typo for 梁柱三), the President of this Society's board, as General Secretary (總務) to exclusively oversee the business of this Society.
2. Select the following 9 individuals to organize a Special Committee (特別委員部) to act as advisors to the General Secretary in the management of this Society's business and provide support: Yun Ch'i-ho (尹致昊), Paek Nak-chun (白樂濬), Yi Ch'un-ho (李春昊), Chŏng In-gwa (鄭仁果), Chŏng Ch'un-su (鄭春洙), O Kŭng-sŏn (吳兢善), Yi Myŏng-jik (李明稙), Yu Ŏk-kyŏm (兪億兼), Sin Kong-suk (申公淑).
3. The real estate and foundation capital owned by this Society shall be managed by this Society's Incorporated Foundation (本書會財團法人). The profits generated therefrom, after deducting necessary management expenses, shall be entirely handed over to the General Secretary to be used for this Society's operating expenses (經常費).
4. The management period shall be set for three years, from May 1, Shōwa 16 (1941) to April 30, Shōwa 19 (1944). Upon expiration, this Society's Incorporated Foundation, this Society's Board of Directors, and the Special Committee shall convene together to discuss future policy again.
Furthermore, the Special Committee members gathered on April 24th, formally organized the committee, and determined the leadership structure as follows:

2. Transformation of the General Conference Office of the Korean Methodist Church
The highest administrative body of the Korean Methodist Church (朝鮮監理會) has traditionally been called the "General Conference Office" (總理院), and its highest position was called "Bishop" (監督). Recently, it has been renamed the "Headquarters of the Christian Korean Methodist Church" (基督敎朝鮮監理團本部), and the Bishop has been renamed "Superintendent" (總理者), simultaneously undergoing internal organizational changes.

A major transformation has occurred in the organization of the General Conference Office. The names of the former staff and the newly appointed staff are listed as follows:

**Summary**

This 1941 article from Samch'ŏlli magazine details significant organizational and personnel changes within two major Korean Protestant institutions: the Christian Literature Society of Korea (CLSK) and the Korean Methodist Church. The CLSK transferred management control from foreign missionaries to an all-Korean leadership, appointing Yang Chu-sam as General Secretary and establishing a special advisory committee of prominent Korean figures. The Methodist Church renamed its governing body from "General Conference Office" (總理院) to "Headquarters of the Christian Korean Methodist Church" (基督敎朝鮮監理團本部) and changed the title of its leader from "Bishop" (監督) to "Superintendent" (總理者), also restructuring its central administration. These changes reflect the broader trend of eliminating Western influence and increasing Korean (and implicitly, Japanese) control over Korean institutions during the late colonial period. For historians, the article is significant as it documents the concrete steps taken within the Korean Christian community to comply with or adapt to Japanese colonial pressures, notably the removal of Western personnel and the adoption of Japanese-style names (Sōshi-kaimei) by prominent Korean Christian leaders, clearly indicated parenthetically in the committee lists. The renaming of positions and organizations also points towards attempts to align religious structures with the prevailing political environment.

1. 基督敎書會의 改革
朝鮮基督敎書會는 朝鮮長老會와 監理會의 聯合機關으로 창립 51주년이라는 장구한 역사를 가지고 있으나, 在來는 외국인 선교사의 세력의 지배를 받아오던 바, 今番 대세에 자각함이 있어 外人의 세력을 일체 배제하는 동시에
지난 3월 16일 정기 이사회에서 그 사업의 경영권을 조선인에게 양도하자는 결의가 있은 후 그 실행위원에게 위임하였던 바 3월 28일에 결정한 方針大要는 如左하다.
1. 本書會理事長인 梁桂三氏를 總務로 擇하야 本書會事業을 專任 視務케 할 것.
2. 左記 9人을 擇하야 特別委員部를 組織하고 本書會事業經營에 總務의 顧問에 應하고 贊助케 할것. 尹致昊, 白樂濬, 李春昊, 鄭仁果, 鄭春洙, 吳兢善, 李明稙, 兪億兼, 申公淑
3. 本書會의 所有인 不動産과 基本金은 本書會財團法人이 此를 管理하고 거게서 生産하는 利益金은 管理에 必要한 經費만 除하고 其餘 全部를 總務에게 引渡하야 本書會經常費에 使用케 할 것.
4. 經營期間은 昭和 16년 5월 1일부터 昭和 19년 4월 30일까지 3년간으로 作定하고 滿期時에 本書會 財團法人과 本書會 理事會와 特別委員部가 一齊히 會合하야 將來 方針을 다시 協議할 것.
