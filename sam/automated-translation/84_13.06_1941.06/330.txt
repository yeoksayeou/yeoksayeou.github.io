Publication Date: 1941년 06월 01일
Article Title: 朝鮮의 映畵興行收益 (Film Exhibition Revenue in Korea)
Author: None listed
Article Type: 잡저 (Miscellaneous Writing/Article)

The total box office admission revenue for movie theaters during Shōwa 13 [1938] was 3,765,639 wŏn, and this figure is also increasing year by year. <192>

**Summary**

This short article notes that the total revenue from movie theater admissions in Korea during 1938 (Shōwa 13) reached over 3.7 million wŏn and was on an upward trend annually. For historians, this snippet provides a specific data point on the economic scale of the film industry in colonial Korea during a period of increasing mobilization and wartime footing, indicating the growing popularity and commercialization of cinema despite the political climate.
