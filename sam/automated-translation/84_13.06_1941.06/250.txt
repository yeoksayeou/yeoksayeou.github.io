<On-Site Report> Hankou and the Recent Situation of Koreans
						By Yu Sang-ŭn, Principal, Hankou Hwap'yŏng Japanese Language School

The Situation in Hankou
As roughly mentioned before, Hankou's population has become very dense due to the recent Incident (事變). The streets of Hankou are long like a coiled snake.
Regarding the concessions (租界), the French Concession has already been dissolved, and currently, it is divided into the Japanese Concession, the remaining French Concession, the Chinese District, the Refugee District, and so on.
The Refugee District is the general term for the dwelling place of Chinese refugees who have gathered from various regions due to the recent Incident.
Among the Chinese residing in Hankou, some have started businesses, but more than half are generally unemployed. So, how do these numerous unemployed people waste their days? They spend every day going to places like movie theaters, playhouses, large hotels, amusement centers, etc. In Hankou city, the movie theaters on the Japanese side are the "Kwangmyŏng" (光明) and "Tonghwa" (東和) theaters, and on the Chinese side, there is the "Myŏngsŏng" (明星). Besides these, on the Westerners' side, there are the "Chungang" (中央) and "Victoria" (ウイクトリヤ) theaters. Other playhouses are too numerous to count.
Also, speaking of large hotels (大旅社), places like the Hyejung Hotel (惠中飯店) and the Kyot'ong Hotel (交通飯店) are large buildings (大樓) with 400-500 guest rooms (客房) alone. Generally, Chinese people take their close friends to large hotels and grand restaurants day and night (主夜로) and play "Fuchan" (フチヤン). If not that, they spend their time from 12 noon to 12 midnight in teahouses (茶社) or playhouses.

Famous Place: Sin Sijang (New Market)
The Sin Sijang (新市場) is completely different (딴판이다) from markets in Korea. This place called Sin Sijang is a name for an entertainment and amusement venue. The construction of Sin Sijang was in the 7th year of the Republic (民國), and its current capital is 2 million yuan. The general manager is Mr. Wang Mun-myŏng (王文明氏), and its employees number in the hundreds. The most famous person at this venue is the magician Mr. Han Kyŏng-mun (韓敬文氏). Mr. Han's magic is truly the work of an elusive (神出鬼沒) genius. The popularity of Sin Sijang is entirely drawn by Mr. Han alone.

Breakdown Within Sin Sijang
The theater venues include the Kyŏnghŭijang (京戱場 - Beijing Opera Theater), Hanhŭijang (漢戱場 - Hankou Opera Theater), Ch'ohŭijang (楚戱場 - Chu Opera Theater), P'yŏnghŭijang (平戱場 - Ping Opera Theater), and others like the Movie Theater (電影劇場), Dance Theater (跳舞劇場), Martial Arts Performance Hall (武舞術場), and Amusement Hall (遊戱場). It is said that the daily number of visitors to Sin Sijang reaches tens of thousands, and the daily revenue also reaches several thousand yuan.

What are Koreans Doing in Hankou?
Korean compatriots operating under the influence of the Imperial Army (皇軍) are mainly involved in trading businesses (무역상), general stores (잡화상), innkeeping (여관업), restaurants (음식점), and comfort station businesses (위안소업). The Korean inns in all of Hankou are "P'yŏnghwa Yŏgwan" (平和旅舘), "Tonghwa Yŏgwan" (東和旅舘), "Kŭmsu Yŏgwan" (金水旅舘), "Hank'ou Yŏgwan" (漢口旅舘), "Samjin Yŏgwan" (三鎭旅舘), Pando Hasugok (半島下宿屋 - Peninsula Boarding House), <148> etc.
Because business in the war zone is good, every inn is in a state of being full (만원의 상태) every day. For restaurants, there is the first-class cuisine (갑종 요리) "Tongil Kwan" (東一舘) and the second-class cuisine (을종 요리) "Taesŭng Kwan" (大勝舘). Others include Kyŏngsŏng Sikdang (京城食堂), P'yŏngt'aek Sikdang (平澤食堂), Chosŏn Sikdang (朝鮮食堂), Tonggwang Sikdang (東光食堂), etc. Besides these, cafes run by Koreans are also numerous (不少하다).

Price List for Korean Food
Japanese Sake (日酒) 1 hop (合) 50 chŏn, Takchu (탁주) 1 hop 20 chŏn, Beer (ビール) 1 bottle 85 chŏn, Cider (サイダー) 1 bottle 50 chŏn, Naengmyŏn (냉면) 1 bowl (器) 60 chŏn, Paekpan (백반) 1 bowl 30 chŏn, Yukt'ang (肉湯) 1 bowl 40 chŏn, Pulgogi (燒肉) 1 serving (人分) 70 chŏn, Kalbi (갈비) 1 serving 70 chŏn, Kalbit'ang (갈비탕) 1 serving 70 chŏn, Pibimpap (비빔밥) 1 bowl 60 chŏn, Manduguk (만두국) 1 bowl 60 chŏn, Hoegat (회갓) 1 serving 60 chŏn, Ttŏkkuk (떡국) 1 bowl 50 chŏn, Changgukpap (장국밥) 1 bowl 50 chŏn, Tray meal (쟁반) 1 set (床) 1 wŏn and up. The prices for meals (飯食) are generally like this.
As for comfort stations (위안소), there are the "Army Comfort Station" (陸軍慰安所), "Navy Comfort Station" (海軍慰安所), and other general comfort stations. The comfort station business is also doing well, with one woman's daily income exceeding 50 or 60 wŏn.
As (如히) described above, the situation in Hankou is roughly like this. <149>

**Summary**

This 1941 report by Yu Sang-ŭn describes Hankou under Japanese occupation. It notes the city's dense population, including many unemployed Chinese who frequent entertainment venues. A popular spot is the "Sin Sijang" amusement center, famed for magician Han Kyŏng-mun. The report details the economic activities of Koreans in Hankou, who primarily run trading businesses, general stores, inns, restaurants, cafes, and crucially, comfort stations, under the auspices of the Japanese military. It lists specific Korean establishments and provides a detailed price list for Korean food and drinks, indicating thriving businesses, including the comfort stations where women reportedly earned significant daily incomes.

From a historical perspective, the article is revealing for its candid description of Korean businesses flourishing in occupied China, directly tied to the Japanese war effort. The explicit mention of "Army Comfort Stations," "Navy Comfort Stations," and general "comfort station businesses" (위안소업) run by Koreans, along with details of their profitability, provides contemporary evidence regarding the operation and economic context of the comfort women system during the Sino-Japanese War. Historians would find the specific names of businesses, the price lists reflecting the wartime economy, and the casual mention of comfort stations as a type of Korean business particularly noteworthy.

漢口의 事情
漢口라 함은 전번 대략 말하였거니와 今般 事變으로 의하야 매우 인구가 밀도되여 있다. 漢口의 거리는 長蛇와 같이 긴 거리다.
租界를 든다면 法租界는 벌써 해산되고 현재는 日本租界 佛租界 中華區 難民區 등으로 분열되여 있다.
難民區라 함은 今般 事變으로 의하야 각 지방에서 모혀온 중국 피난민들의 거처지를 통칭함이다.
漢口에 거주하고 있는 中國人들은 其中 사업에 착수한 자도 있지만은 全半 이상은 대개 실업자들이다. 그러면 이 多數한 실업자들은 어떠한 所事로 그날그날을 허송하느냐 하면 映畵館, 戱院, 大旅社, 遊戱場 等 處로 매일 단니고 있다. 漢口 시내에는 映畵館이 日本人側으로 「光明」 「東和」 두 戱場이고 中國人側으로 「明星」이 있다. 其外 西洋人側으로 「中央」 「ウイクトリヤ」의 두 戱場이 있다. 기타 戱院은 이루 헤아릴 수 없이 많다.
또한 大旅社라 하면 惠中飯店, 交通飯店 같은 데는 客房 數만 하드라도 4, 5백간의 大樓다. 대개 中國人들은 大旅社 大飯店에 가서 주야로 자기의 親友들을 끌고 가서는 「フチヤン」을 놀고 있다. 그렇지 아니면 茶社나 戱院에서 正午 12시부터 夜 12시까지 세월을 보내고 있다.

名所 新市場
新市場이라 함은 朝鮮의 시장과는 딴판이다. 이 新市場이란 곳은 오락과 연예장의 명칭이다. 新市場의 건설은 民國 7年度며 자본금은 현재 200만원이다. 총 경리는 「王文明氏」며 其 종업원은 수백명에 달한다. 當場에서 제일 명성이 有하기는 마술사 「韓敬文」씨다. 韓씨가 마술함은 실로 神出鬼沒의 천재이다. 新市場의 인기는 전혀 韓씨가 끌고 만다.

新市場內 分別
戱院場은 京戱場, 漢戱場, 楚戱場, 平戱場, 其他 電影劇場, 跳舞劇場, 武舞術場, 遊戱場 등이다. 그런데 新市場의 매일 입장자가 수만명이며 매일 수입금도 수천원에 달한다고 한다.

漢口에서 朝鮮人은 무얼 하는가
皇軍의 세력 하에 활동하고 있는 朝鮮동포들은 주로 무역상 ,잡화상 ,여관업 ,음식점 ,위안소업이다. 全漢口에 있는 朝鮮人 여관은 「平和旅舘」 「東和旅舘」 「金水旅舘」 「漢口旅舘」 「三鎭旅舘」 半島下宿屋＜148＞ 등이다.
戰地의 경기가 좋아서 어느 여관이나 매일 만원의 상태에 있다. 음식점은 갑종 요리 「東一舘」과 을종 요리 「大勝舘」이 있다. 기타 京城食堂 平澤食堂 朝鮮食堂 東光食堂 등이며 其外에 朝鮮人이 경영하고 있는 카페도 不少하다.
