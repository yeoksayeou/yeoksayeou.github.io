**Body Text Translation:**
Regulations for Issuing Skill Certificates to Film Personnel

Article 1: Pursuant to the provisions of Article 8, Paragraph 2, Item 2 of the Enforcement Rules of the Chosŏn Film Ordinance, the issuance of Skill Certificates (hereinafter referred to as skill certificates) shall be governed by these regulations.

Article 2: Persons wishing to receive the issuance of a skill certificate shall submit an application for skill certificate issuance stating the following matters, along with a fee (手数料) of 2 Wŏn, to the Chosŏn Film Professionals Association (조선 영화인협회).
1. Address, full name (創氏者 - those who have changed their names [under the Sōshi-kaimei policy] should also write their former name), and date of birth.
2. Type of work.
The following documents shall be attached to the application form mentioned in the preceding paragraph.
1. Curriculum vitae (to be filled out according to the attached form) <213>
2. Photograph (cabinet card size [手札型], without hat, half-body shot, taken within six months of the application; name should be written on the back [裏面]).
3. Persons who have engaged in the work of directing, acting, or cinematography shall provide their career history; directors who do not have the experience mentioned in the preceding item shall attach a script (脚本) authored by themselves.

Article 3: The Chosŏn Film Professionals Association shall establish a Skill Screening Committee to screen the skills of applicants for skill certificate issuance.

Article 4: The Skill Screening Committee shall be organized with one Chairperson and no more than 11 Committee Members.

Article 5: The Chairperson shall be appointed by the Association President, commissioning the Head of the Library Section of the Police Affairs Bureau (圖書課長 - *Interpreter's note: text reads 图书谏长 which is likely a typo or older form for 课长*) of the Government-General of Chōsen (朝鮮總督府 警務局 圖書諫長).

Article 6: The Committee Members shall be filled by the persons listed below.
1. Two officials from the Government-General of Chōsen related to film.
2. Two directors.
3. Two actors.
4. Two cinematographers.
5. Three persons with knowledge and experience.
The committee members mentioned in the preceding paragraph shall be appointed by the Association President. <214>

Article 7: The term of office for committee members who are government officials shall be the duration of their official position, and for others, it shall be one year. However, they may be reappointed.

Article 8: The Chairperson shall oversee the affairs of the committee.
When the Chairperson is unable to attend to duties due to circumstances, a committee member designated by the Chairperson shall act on their behalf.

Article 9: The Committee shall have Secretaries (幹事).
The Secretaries shall be appointed by the Association President.
The Secretaries shall handle general affairs under the direction of the Chairperson.

Article 10: The Committee shall be convened by the Chairperson.

Article 11: The skill screening shall be conducted according to the following methods.
1. For applicants for skill certificate issuance who intend to engage in the work of directing:
First Examination: Script Examination (脚本 考査) (conducted on the submitted script).
Second Examination: Conducted for those who passed the First Examination in the following order:
(1) Character Examination (性格考査) (Principles [志操], Personality [性格], Talent [才幹], Judgment [判斷], etc.)
(2) Academic Subject and General Knowledge Examination (學科 及 常識 考査) (Regarding National Language [國語 - likely Japanese], National History [國史], National General Knowledge [國民常識], etc., by oral questioning [口答] or written test [筆記]) (Continued on page 194) <215>
(3) Examination of Knowledge Required for Directors (Regarding script, continuity [코티뉴티-], film, sets [裝置], costumes [衣裳], makeup [扮裝], acting [演技], cinematography [撮影], lighting [照明], sound recording [錄音], development [現像], editing [編輯], art [美術], literature [文學], and regulations concerning theater and film [演劇, 映畵에 관한 法規], as well as the film business [映畵事業], etc., by oral questioning or written test)
(4) Continuity Examination (콘티뉴티- 考査) (Applicant is required to create a continuity)
2. For applicants for skill certificate issuance who intend to engage in the work of acting:
(1) Character Examination (性格 考査) (Principles, Personality, Talent, Judgment, etc.)
(2. Academic Subject and General Knowledge Examination (學科 及 常識 考査) (Regarding National Language, National History, National General Knowledge, Film General Knowledge [映畵 常識], etc., by oral questioning or written test)
(3) Examination of Aptitude Required for Actors (素質 考査) (Voice production [發聲], Movement [所作], Expression [表情], Makeup [扮裝], etc.)
3. For applicants for skill certificate issuance who intend to engage in the work of cinematography:
(1) Character Examination (性格 考査) (Principles, Personality, Talent, Judgment, etc.)
(2) Academic Subject and General Knowledge Examination (學科 及 常識 考査) (Regarding National Language, National History, National General Knowledge, Film General Knowledge, etc., conducted by oral questioning or written test.)
(3) Examination of Knowledge Required for Cinematographers (Regarding photographic optics <194> and photographic science [寫眞科學], cameras [撮影機], lenses [렌즈], film [필림], filters [필타], dollies [도릭크], lighting, sound recording, development, editing, etc., conducted by oral questioning or written test, or by having the applicant operate a camera. Furthermore, if deemed particularly necessary, the applicant may be required to submit sample work [試作品].)

Article 12: For those who have obtained registration under the provisions of the Film Law, etc., and are recognized by the Committee as having no particular issues (別無), all or part of the screening according to the provisions of the preceding article may be omitted.

Article 13: The screening of persons who, at the time of the enforcement of this ordinance, are currently engaged professionally in the work of directing, acting, or cinematography, or who have previously engaged professionally in these types of work, shall be conducted based on the documents submitted according to the provisions of Article 2. However, if the Committee deems it necessary, all or part of the skill screening may be conducted according to the provisions of Article 11.

Article 14: The method for determining the passers of the skill screening shall be decided by the Chairperson and Committee Members through deliberation (議定).

Article 15: Those who have received a decision of acceptance from the Committee shall be issued a skill certificate according to the attached form. -(End)- <195>

**Summary**

This article outlines the detailed regulations established in June 1941 for issuing mandatory "Skill Certificates" to film personnel (directors, actors, cinematographers) in colonial Korea, as stipulated by the Chosŏn Film Ordinance. Applicants had to submit an application with personal details, career history (or a script for new directors), photos, and a fee to the Chosŏn Film Professionals Association. A Skill Screening Committee, including Government-General officials and industry representatives chaired by the Head of the Library Section of the Police Affairs Bureau, would assess applicants through a multi-stage process covering technical skills, general knowledge (including Japanese language and national history), and crucially, a "Character Examination" focused on ideological suitability (principles, personality, judgment). Provisions existed to potentially simplify the process for established professionals.

From a historical perspective, these regulations are significant as they demonstrate the tightening state control over the Korean film industry under the Japanese colonial administration during wartime. The requirement for skill certification, administered by an association heavily influenced by government officials (especially the police bureau involved in censorship), served not only to regulate professional standards but also as a mechanism for ideological vetting and control over who could participate in filmmaking. The emphasis on "Character Examination" and knowledge of Japanese language/history highlights the assimilationist and authoritarian policies of the late colonial period.

2. 연출자 2인
3. 연기자 2인
4. 촬영자 2인

(1) 性格 考査(志操, 性格, 才幹, 判斷 등)

第15條 위원회에서 합격의 결정을 받은 자에 대하여서는 별지 양식에 의한 기능 증명서를 교부함. -(끝)-＜195＞
