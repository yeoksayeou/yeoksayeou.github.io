**Summary**

This article from the June 1941 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine presents a list of prominent figures in Korean cinema and theater, revealing their real names alongside their well-known pen names (for writers/directors) or stage names (for actors). It categorizes them into general figures and actors. For historians, this list serves as a valuable primary source documenting key cultural personalities of the late colonial period, their public personas (stage/pen names), and their actual identities. The extensive use of Hanja for names reflects the linguistic environment of the time, and the list itself offers insight into who was considered notable in the entertainment field just before the intensification of wartime mobilization.
