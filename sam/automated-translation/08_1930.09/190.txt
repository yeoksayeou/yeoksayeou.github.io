Grand Undertaking and Support, From the Manager of Samch'ŏlli Company

Recruiting Members for Samch'ŏlli Company!

Japan's Kaizōsha (改造社) and China's Commercial Press (商務印書舘) were the most effective institutions in advancing the culture of their respective countries. Now, our company also intends to devote its full strength to reviving and creating our culture, becoming the Kaizōsha of Chosŏn, the Commercial Press of Chosŏn. Therefore, in addition to publishing the magazine, we will also actively publish books in the future. However, undertaking such a grand cultural endeavor, we know it will be truly difficult to expect development without the support of the wise figures in society (江湖諸賢). Thus, we request your material and spiritual support. After reviewing the regulations below (左規), we hope many of you will become members.

Membership Regulations (社友規程)

Member (社友) = For those who contribute Two Won (金 貳圓)
1. One-year free subscription to the magazine "Samch'ŏlli" (「三千里」)
2. 10% discount on books published by the company, all postage waived for brokered book orders (但 注文要 先金 - However, prepayment is required for orders)
3. Special privileges offered for various events hosted by the company

Special Member (特別社友) = For those who contribute Ten Won (拾圓) or more
Lifetime subscription to this magazine
Other privileges of regular members offered

**Summary**

This article is an announcement and appeal from the manager of Samch'ŏlli Company, publisher of the magazine "Samch'ŏlli". It outlines the company's ambition to become a major cultural force in Korea (Chosŏn), akin to Japan's Kaizōsha and China's Commercial Press, by expanding into book publishing alongside the magazine to "revive and create" Korean culture. It solicits financial and moral support through a membership program, detailing benefits for regular (2 Won) and special (10+ Won) members, and lists the names of individuals who had already become members by August 1, 1930. For historians, this piece is interesting as it reflects Korean cultural aspirations and nationalist sentiment under Japanese rule, positioning publishing as a key tool for cultural revival. The comparison to prominent publishers in neighboring colonizer (Japan) and major regional power (China) highlights the scale of their ambition, while the membership drive and list of supporters offer insight into the social networks and financial realities of Korean cultural enterprises during the colonial period.

大事業과 聲授, 三千里社 責任者白
三千里社友 募集!
日本의 改造社와 中國의 商務印書舘은 그 나라의 文化를 躍進식힌데 가장 有効한 機關이엇사외다. 이제 本社도 朝鮮의 改造社, 朝鮮의 商務印書舘으로 우리의 文化를 復興식히고  創造하는데 全力을 기우릴가 하나이다. 그래서 雜誌發刊 以外에도 압흐로 圖書出版을 盛히 할터이온 바 이러틋 巨創한 文化的 大事業을 하자면 江湖諸賢의 聲援이 업시는 到底히 그 發展을 期키 어러울 줄 아옵고 이에 諸氏의 物質的 精神的 聲援을 請하오니 左規를 보신 뒤에 만히 社友되여 주심을 바라나이다.

社友規程
社友=金 貳圓을 내시는 분에게
1, 雜誌 「三千里」 1個年間 無代 進呈
2, 本社 出版 圖書 1割引, 取次書籍郵稅 一切 免除(但 注文要 先金)
3, 本社 主催 諸般事業에 特典 提供

特別社友=拾圓以上을 내시는 분에게 本誌 永久 進呈
其他 普通社友의 特典 提供
