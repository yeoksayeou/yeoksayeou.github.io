Editorial Postscript

The New Year's bells ring boomingly, and the castle gates are opened. Friends across the land, let us run out to the wide fields and shape 1930 meaningfully. For those who suffer, joy may come quickly, but in return, "old age" also comes quickly. This tense attitude does not last that long, so let us quickly and diligently accomplish the tasks 맡은 (mat'ŭn - 맡은) we have each undertaken. Therefore, this humble person (小人) has resolved to diligently publish the *Samch'ŏlli* (三千里) magazine this year, so I ask for much scolding and help from everyone. Also, starting from the New Year, we have organized the *Samch'ŏlli* Membership System (三千里 社友制); this arises from the desire to somewhat solidify the financial foundation of this company (本社) and also from the humble wish (微意) to gain your spiritual support, so please approve of it greatly.

The next issue (來號) will be specially featured as the "Golden Age Issue" (全盛時代號) and will be published on February 1st. As for why this month's New Year issue turned out so poorly (貧弱하게), you likely know, but due to a certain atmosphere (엇든 雰圍氣) that suddenly arose around early to mid-December, which was the magazine editing period, the question of the magazine itself seemed impossible, so we could not devote our full effort to editing. Also, key figures who were writing for us—they ended up that way, so inevitably it became this mess, but we will restore it starting from the next issue. (P'ain) <41>

**Summary**

The editor, writing under the pen name P'ain, welcomes the New Year 1930, urging readers to be diligent. He announces his renewed commitment to publishing *Samch'ŏlli* magazine and introduces a new Membership System (社友制) to secure financial and moral support. He apologizes for the current issue's poor quality, attributing it to an unspecified "atmosphere" in December 1929 that disrupted editing and prevented key contributors from submitting articles, but promises improvement with the next "Golden Age Issue." For historians, the article is revealing due to its subtle reference to an "atmosphere" hindering publication, likely alluding to censorship or political pressure under Japanese colonial rule, which impacted journalistic freedom and content creation. The introduction of a reader membership system also points to the financial struggles faced by private Korean publications during this period.

編輯餘言
새해의 종소리는 둥둥 울니고 성문은 열어짐니다, 천하의 벗들이어 너른 들로 내다라 1930年을 뜻이 잇게 꾸밉시다. 고생하는 사람에겐 樂도 빨니 오겟지만 그대신 「늙음」도 빨니 오는 법이외다. 이 긴장한 태도가 그리케 오래가는 것이 아니오니 어서 하로 급히 제 마튼 일을 꾸준히 이루워 놋습시다. 그래서 小人도 올해에는 「三千里」 雜誌를 꾸준히 하여 나갈 決心이니 여러분도 만흔 꾸지람과 도움을 주옵소서. 또 新年부터 「三千里」 社友制를 꾸미엇는데 이것은 多少라도 本社의 經濟的 基礎를 굿게하랴는 뜻과 또한 여러분의 精神的支持를 엇고 십흔 微意에서 나온 바니 아모쪼록 만히 讚同하여 주옵소서.

來號는 「全盛時代號」로 特輯하여 2月 1日에 發行하겟사외다. 그러고 이 달 新年號가 이가치 貧弱하게 된 것은 여러분도 아실 터이나 雜誌 編輯時期이든 12月 初中旬에 이르러 홀연히 이러난 엇든 雰圍氣 때문에 雜誌 云云의 문제가 도모지 아닐 것 갓하여 編輯에 全力을 할 수 업섯스며 또 執筆하여 주시든 重要諸氏-그만 그러케 되여 不得己 이 꼬리 되엇사오나 來號부터는 다시 復舊하겟사외다. (파인) <41>
