Company Announcement

In the New Year, our company's external affairs representative, Mr. Kim Ilhŏn (金日憲君), will be dispatched to various places in Hamgyŏng North Province (咸北各地) to handle subscriber recruitment, advertising, and other matters. We request that the people of the Kwanbuk region (關北人士) show him special favor and support.
January, Gyeong-o Year (1930)
From the Samch'ŏlli Company (三千里社白) <41>

**Summary**

This is a company announcement from the Samch'ŏlli magazine informing readers, particularly those in the Hamgyŏng North (Kwanbuk) region, that their representative, Kim Ilhŏn, will be visiting the area to manage subscriptions and advertising. The announcement requests the local populace's support for him. For historians, this brief notice illustrates the magazine's active efforts to expand its readership and business operations geographically into the northern provinces of colonial Korea during this period. It highlights the role of travelling representatives in print media business development at the time.
