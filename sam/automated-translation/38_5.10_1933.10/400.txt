Article Title: 滿州國行의 廉想涉氏 (Mr. Yŏm Sang-sŏp Bound for Manchukuo)
Article Type: 소식 (News/Tidings)

Mr. Yŏm Sang-sŏp Bound for Manchukuo
Mr. Yŏm Sang-sŏp recently revealed his large frame (巨軀) and undertook a journey to Manchuria (滿洲行), returning in recent days. It is the talk among the capital's children [rumor in the capital] (京童의 이약이 소리) that on this trip, Mr. Yŏm must have returned with material for a remarkable work (괄목할 制作).

Ms. Na Hye-sŏk's Feature-Length Novel
The painter Ms. Na Hye-sŏk (羅蕙錫女史), who during her time as a student studying abroad in Tokyo (東京留學生 時代) founded the magazine called *Yŏjagye* (女子界) and wrote many short stories under the pen name (號) Chŏngwŏl (晶月), recently, in the spare moments from holding her painter's brush (畵筆을 드는 餘暇), could not endure her surging creative desire (솟아오르는 창작욕을 참을 길 업서) and has been writing a feature-length novel titled "Kim Myŏng-ae" (金明愛). It is said that she has already almost half finished writing it (거지 반 탈고하야) and sent it to Ch'unwŏn. This corresponds to an autobiography of Ms. Na, and if one seeks a parallel example, might it not form a matching pair (雙璧) with Miyake Yasuko's (三宅*す子) piece, "The False Widow" (僞はれろ未亡人)?
If this novel were to be published, the history of love and desire (愛慾史) concerning the various men (諸男牲) who surrounded this talented female artist (才華의 이 여류화가) would also be implicitly and explicitly revealed (隱現할 것이어서), which would surely be intensely interesting (흥미진진할 것이라). <67>

**Summary**

This news brief covers two prominent cultural figures. First, it notes that the writer Yŏm Sang-sŏp has recently returned from a trip to Manchukuo, sparking rumors that he gathered material for a significant new work there. Second, it reports that the painter and writer Na Hye-sŏk is working on an autobiographical feature-length novel, "Kim Myŏng-ae," comparing it potentially to Miyake Yasuko's "The False Widow," and anticipates it will generate excitement by revealing details about the men in her life. From a historical perspective, the article is interesting for highlighting Manchukuo as a place of interest and potential inspiration for Korean intellectuals during the colonial period. It also sheds light on the activities and public perception of major literary and artistic figures like Yŏm Sang-sŏp and Na Hye-sŏk, particularly the intense public interest (and perhaps scrutiny) surrounding the personal life and relationships of a prominent female artist like Na.

滿州國行의 廉想涉氏
滿州國行의 廉想涉 氏
涉想 氏는 얼마 전에 巨軀를 드러 滿洲行을 하고 近日 도라왓다. 이번 여행에 氏의 일이라 괄목할 制作의 재료를 가지고 도라왓스리라 함은 京童의 이약이 소리.

羅蕙錫女史의 長篇
東京留學生 時代에 女子界란 잡지를 창간하야 晶月이란 號로써 단편소설을 만히 쓰는 畵家 羅蕙錫女史는 최근에 畵筆을 드는 餘暇에 솟아오르는 창작욕을 참을 길 업서 「金明愛」란 장편소설을 집필하야 벌서 거지 반 탈고하야 春園에게 보내엇다 하는데 이것은 女史의 자서전에 해당한 것으로 예를 찻자면 「三宅*す子」의 「僞はれろ未亡人」의 1편에 雙璧이 되지 안을가.
이 소설이 발표된다면 才華의 이 여류화가를 싸고도든 諸男牲이 愛慾史도 隱現할 것이어서 흥미진진할 것이라 할진저. ＜67＞
