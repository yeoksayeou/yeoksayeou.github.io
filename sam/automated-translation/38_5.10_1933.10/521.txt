Branch Office Recruitment

We are recruiting Samch'ŏlli branch offices in various locations. Those who are interested, please request the internal regulations. We will treat you favorably by offering special discounts.

October 1933
Samch'ŏlli Company<92>

**Summary**

This is an announcement from the Samch'ŏlli magazine company seeking individuals or groups to establish branch offices in various locations. It encourages interested parties to inquire about the internal rules and promises preferential treatment, including special discounts. From a historical perspective, this brief notice illustrates the magazine's business expansion efforts during the Japanese colonial period, aiming to broaden its distribution network across Korea. It also showcases the typical mixed script (Hanja and Hangul) and older Korean grammatical forms common in publications of the 1930s.
