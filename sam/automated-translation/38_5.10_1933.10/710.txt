**Body Text Translation**
Publishing the October Issue

Thank you for loving the September issue so warmly.

The October issue has also come out with content that does not fall short of the previous one. Please give it much attention. We will strive even harder going forward.

We would be grateful if our Esteemed Readers (讀者諸氏) would provide their impressions after reading this journal. And may you be well during this fine season of yellow chrysanthemums and crimson maples (黃菊丹楓의 佳節). <120>

**Summary**

This brief editorial postscript thanks readers for their positive reception of the previous (September) issue of the magazine *Samch'ŏlli*. It introduces the current October issue, promising continued quality and effort, requests reader feedback (reading impressions), and offers seasonal greetings appropriate for autumn. From a historical perspective, the text is interesting for its use of early 20th-century Korean orthography (e.g., "감사함니다", "떠러지지 안는", "나왓슴니다", "압흐로") before standardization. It also reflects typical editorial practices of engaging with readers and the use of Sino-Korean expressions for seasonal descriptions (黃菊丹楓).

十月號를 내보내면서
9월 호를 그처럼 열열히 사랑하여 주시니 감사함니다.
10월 호도 전에 떠러지지 안는 내용을 가지고 나왓슴니다. 만히 돌보아 주시압. 압흐로 더욱 분투하겟슴니다.
讀者諸氏께서 본지의 독후감을 주신다면 감사하겟슴니다. 그리고 黃菊丹楓의 佳節에 내내 안녕하옵소서. ＜120＞
