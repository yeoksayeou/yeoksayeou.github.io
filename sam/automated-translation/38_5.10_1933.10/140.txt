**Body Text Translation:**
Captain Amakasu and Manchuria

Since the Manchurian Incident, it has become an open secret that former Captain Amakasu (甘粕元大尉) was secretly active under the alias Naitō (內藤). After being paroled, former Captain Amakasu immediately went to France (佛蘭西) to study nationalism (國家主義). Without returning to Japan (日本) afterwards, he is said to have immediately set foot in Manchuria (滿州) and been active there.

The ¥30,000 Villa

Anyone who has been to Inch'ŏn (仁川) will know it, the luxurious Western-style building rising prominently like an old castle atop the hill of the multinational park. This house was the villa (別莊) of former Governor-General Saitō (齊藤). Saitō used to escape the summer heat here during the three peak summer periods (三伏盛夏). Since he left to become Prime Minister, it has become a desolate house without an owner and remains vacant. Recently, rumor is high that Mr. Pak Yŏngch'ŏl (朴榮喆), president (頭取) of the Commercial Bank (商業銀行) and a famous Honam tycoon (湖南甲富), will purchase this house for 30,000 yen.

Regrettable Pubyŏngnu

Pubyŏngnu (浮碧樓), the tragic stage where Yi Suil (李守一) kicked Sim Sunae (沈順愛) in *Changhanmong* (長恨夢); Pubyŏngnu, famous for Inspector Min's (閔監司) lavish enjoyment; Pubyŏngnu in P'yŏngyang (平壤), which the poet Hŭkk'aek (黑客) always praised first when extolling the Taedong River (大同江)—one side of it collapsed in the rain on September 12th. This building was originally constructed 1,000 years ago by King Yŏngmyŏng (永明王) and rebuilt in the 5th year of King Kwanghaegun (光海君), the 15th king of the Yi Dynasty (李朝). It is a historic building named Pubyŏngnu when King Yejong (睿宗) of old Koryŏ (고려) held a clear banquet (淸宴) here. Talk of reconstruction (重建說) is currently circulating among the people of P'yŏngyang. <22>

**Summary**

This article compiles three short news items: 1) The activities of former Japanese Captain Amakasu Masahiko in Manchuria under the alias Naitō after being paroled and studying nationalism in France. 2) The rumored sale of former Governor-General Saitō Makoto's vacant villa in Inch'ŏn to Pak Yŏngch'ŏl, a prominent Korean banker and businessman, for 30,000 yen. 3) The partial collapse of the historic Pubyŏngnu pavilion in P'yŏngyang due to rain, noting its historical significance and ongoing discussions about its reconstruction. From a historian's perspective, these snippets reveal the continued shadowy influence of controversial Japanese figures like Amakasu in Manchuria, the economic interactions between the Japanese elite and wealthy Koreans (Saitō's villa sale to Pak), and contemporary concerns over the preservation of Korean cultural heritage like Pubyŏngnu during the colonial period.

甘粕大尉와 滿洲
甘粕大尉와 滿洲
滿州事變 이래 甘粕元大尉는 內藤이란 變名으로 비밀리에 만히 활동하엿든 일은 지금도 공연한 사실이 되엿다. 甘粕元大尉는 가출옥이 된 뒤 곳 佛蘭西에 건너가서 國家主義에 대한 연구를 하고 그 길로 日本에 오지 안코 곳 滿州의 땅을 밟고 활동하고 잇섯다고 한다.

別莊의 3萬圓
누구든지 仁川에 가본 이는 알겟지만 각국 공원의 정상에 古城가치 웃둑 소슨 호화로운 洋製建物 이 집은 齊藤 前總督의 별장으로 3伏盛夏마다 齊藤 氏는 여기에 피서하든바 총리대신이 되어 간 뒤로보터는 주인 업는 적적한 집이 되어 空家대로 그냥 잇다.
이 집을 최근에 商業銀行의 頭取요 유명한 湖南甲富인 朴榮喆 氏가 3만원으로 매수한다는 소문이 놉다.

앗가운 浮碧樓
長恨夢에 李守一이 沈順愛를 발길로 차든 비극의 浮碧樓 閔監司가 호탕하게 놀기로 유명하든 浮碧樓 詩人 黑客이 大同江을 찬미할 때 반드시 그 압잡이로 찬사를 보내든 平壤의 浮碧樓가 9월 12일의 비에 한 쪽이 문허젓다. 이 건물은 지금부터 1,000년 전 永明王이 건립하고 李朝15代 光海君 5년에 再建한 것으로 녯날 고려의 睿宗께서 이곳에 淸宴을 베풀고 浮碧樓라고 일홈부친 역사적 건물이다. 平壤人士間에는 방금 重建說이 行하고 잇다. ＜22＞
