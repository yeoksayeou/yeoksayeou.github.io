Article Type: 잡저 (Miscellaneous writing/Essay)

High-Rise Building

Mr. Pak Hŭngsik (朴興植), owner of the Hwasin Department Store (和信百貨店), recently intends to build a 7-story large building on the wide site opposite the police station at Chongno Intersection, and is currently securing the land and buildings in that area. When asked what he would do with such a large building, it is said that, besides using it as a large department store, the top floor will feature a large hall to be opened to the public for wedding ceremonies and the like, and additionally, social rooms will be installed to serve concurrently as modern social facilities. Isn't it delightful just to imagine a 7-story building on Chongno Avenue?

Department Store in Sŏgyŏng

Seoul's Chongno Hwasin Department Store (和信百貨店), the only one managed by a Korean and also the largest, is steadily developing, dispelling the anxieties of some individuals who worried about its management. Now, it is said that an uncle of the department store owner, Mr. Pak Hŭngsik (朴興植), has decided to open a large department store in P'yŏngyang as well, on Sŏmun T'ong (西門通), where foot and horse traffic is most bustling. He has reportedly invested 150,000 Wŏn and is currently constructing a 3-story building. Thus, the department stores of the East and West seem poised to be monopolized by the Pak clan.

Aliases of Exiles

Looking back at previous history, political figures of the Former Korean Empire who were in exile in places like Tokyo (東京) mostly used aliases. Kim Okkyun (金玉均) went around as Iwata Shūsaku (岩田周作), and Song Pyŏngjun (宋秉畯) as Noda Heijirō (野田平次郞).
On another note, Sun Yat-sen (孫逸仙) also used the name Nakayama (中山) during his exile in Tokyo (東京), and after achieving success, he continued to use Nakayama (中山) as his sobriquet. <21>

**Summary**

This article reports on the ambitious construction plans of Pak Hŭngsik, owner of the Hwasin Department Store, who intends to build a 7-story multi-purpose building (department store, event hall, social club) on Chongno, Seoul. It also mentions his uncle establishing a 3-story department store in P'yŏngyang (Sŏgyŏng), suggesting a growing dominance of the Pak family in the department store business. A separate note discusses the historical practice of Korean political exiles like Kim Okkyun and Song Pyŏngjun using Japanese aliases while in Tokyo, drawing a parallel with Sun Yat-sen using the alias Nakayama. For historians, the article provides insights into Korean entrepreneurship and the modernization of urban spaces (department stores as symbols of modernity) during the colonial period, highlighting the success of figures like Pak Hŭngsik despite the challenging environment. The brief mention of historical exiles and their aliases offers a small window into the political realities and connections of the preceding decades.

高層建物
高層建物
和信百貨店主 朴興植 氏는 최근에 鍾路네거리 경찰서 압 너른 基地에다가 7층 대건물을 지을 작정으로 방금 그 等地의 基地와 건물들을 마련하고 잇다는데 그러케 큰 건물을 지어 무얼하겟느냐 하면 大百貨店으로 쓰는 것은 물론 최상층에는 큰 홀을 꾸미어 일반에게 결혼식장 등으로 개방하리라 하며 그밧게도 사교실 가튼 것을 두어 근대식 사교기관으로도 겸하여 설치하리라 한다. 鍾路大街의 7層樓를 상상만 하여 보아도 유쾌하지 안은가.

西京의 百貨店
朝鮮사람 경영으로는 오직 하나이요 또 가장 큰 것인 서울 鍾路 和信百貨店은 그 경영이 어럼지 안을가 하고 근심하는 일부 인사의 杞憂을 물니치고 착착 발전되어 가는데 이번에 또 百貨店主 朴興植 氏의 親叔되는 이가 平壤에도 人馬의 왕래가 가장 번잡한 西門通에다가 큰 백화점을 내기로 되어 15만원인가 投하야 방금 3層樓를 건축중이라고. 이리하야 東西의 백화점은 朴氏門中으로 獨占之勢.

亡命客들의 變名
이전 역사를 뒤저보면 舊韓國의 政客들로 東京等地에 망명하고 잇슬 때는 대개 變名을 썻다. 金玉均은 岩田周作이라 하고 宋秉畯은 野田平次郞이라 하야 도라다녓다.
딴 말이나 孫逸仙이도 東京亡命時 中山이라 하엿는데 功을 이룬 뒤에도 中山을 그대로 號로 섯다. ＜21＞
