Mr. Yi Kiyŏng's Full-Length Novel

It is said that Mr. Yi Kiyŏng (李箕永) has arranged to write a full-length novel for a certain newspaper (某紙) this time, and for over a month now, he has retired to a certain place (某處) in the countryside, engrossed in writing without respite. Thus, Mr. Yi's full-length novel will soon debut before tens of thousands of readers.

However, for a proletarian writer (푸로 작가) to serialize a novel while receiving genuinely favorable treatment (優遇) in terms of page space, Mr. Yi is considered the pioneer (燆矢). Hitherto, proletarian novels (푸로 소설) were typically stiff, forced, full of dry (밧삭밧삭) descriptions, and mostly consisted of works where the author alone gets excited and starts delivering agitation speeches (아지 연설) from the second line—this was the norm. Consequently, it was customary for 10 readers to flee after 3 installments, 100 to flee after 10 installments, and over 1,000 to flee after 100 installments.

Mr. Yi Kiyŏng must rescue himself from this failing (幣). This will simultaneously be the way to rescue himself and the way to rescue the full-length proletarian novel. <57>

**Summary**

This news item reports that the prominent proletarian writer Yi Kiyŏng is secluded in the countryside, working on a full-length novel to be serialized in an unnamed newspaper. The article highlights this as a significant event, noting Yi is the first proletarian writer to receive such favorable treatment for a serial. It contrasts this with the perceived failings of previous proletarian novels—criticized as dry, forced, and overly focused on "agitation speeches" that alienated readers—and expresses hope that Yi's work will overcome these issues, thereby saving both his own reputation and the genre of the proletarian serial novel. For historians, this piece offers insight into the status and reception of proletarian literature in 1930s colonial Korea, revealing the pressures on writers like Yi Kiyŏng to balance ideological commitment with popular appeal and the contemporary critique of the genre's typical style.

李箕永 氏의 長篇
李箕永 氏는 이번 某紙에 장편소설을 쓰기로 되어 벌서 月餘前부터 시골 某處로 가서 은거하여 집필에 여념이 업는 중이라 하니 未久에 氏의 장편소설이 수만 독자의 압헤 데뷰-할 것이다.
그런데 푸로 작가로서 본격적으로 지면의 優遇을 밧으면서 연재소설을 쓰는 것은 氏로써 燆矢를 삼는다. 在來 푸로 소설이라 하면 뻑뻑하고 억지가 만코 밧삭밧삭 마른 묘사에다가 작자 혼자 흥분하여 둘재 줄부터 벌서 아지 연설이 나오는 그런데-마로 一貫한 作이 대부분이엿기에 3회에 독자 10이 다라나고 10회에 100이 다라나고 100회에 1,000이상이 다라나는 것이 통례엿다.
李箕永 氏는 이 幣에서 자신을 구출하여 내어야 하겟다. 이것은 자신을 구출하는 동시에 장편 푸로 소설을 구출하는 길이 될 것이다. ＜57＞
