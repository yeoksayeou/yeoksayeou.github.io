On May 15th of this year, Chiang Kai-shek (蔣介石) delivered the following speech regarding the Red Army (紅軍) at the Jiangxi Provincial Affairs Committee meeting (江西省務委員會席上).

"The territory of the Communist bandits (共匪) spans merely 6 counties (縣). If we count the number of able-bodied men (壯丁) in each county as 50,000, the total is 300,000. However, since the 18th year of the Republic (民國 18년, i.e., 1929), 100,000 have been killed in battle, and 150,000 or 160,000 have died from disease or been murdered. Therefore, those currently mobilized on the front lines number merely 50,000 or 60,000. Furthermore, although there is a difference like heaven and earth (天壤의 差) between us and them in terms of economy and weapons, where lies the reason they have not yet perished despite four or five years of subjugation efforts? It hinges solely on one point: 'strictness of organization (조직의 嚴密)'. Therefore, if we had an organization as strict as theirs, there would be no worry about annihilating them." <35>

**Summary**

This article reports on a speech given by Chiang Kai-shek in May 1933 regarding the ongoing conflict with the Communist Red Army in Jiangxi province. Chiang acknowledges the Communists' resilience despite their numerical and material inferiority, attributing their persistence solely to their highly disciplined organization. He argues that if the Nationalist forces could achieve similar organizational rigor, defeating the Communists would be easily achievable. For historians studying Korean colonial history, this article is interesting as it shows a Korean publication reporting on internal Chinese affairs, reflecting awareness of regional power dynamics. Chiang's emphasis on 'organization' as the key to overcoming material disadvantages might have implicitly resonated with Korean readers under colonial rule, potentially offering insights into contemporary views on resistance and state power.

三千里 壁新聞, 蔣介石의 演說
蔣介石은 금년 5월15일 江西省務委員會席上에서 紅軍에 대하야 如左한 연설을 하엿다.
「共匪의 地盤은 僅히 6縣壯丁의 人數는 各縣 5만인으로 치면 합계 30만이나 民國 18년 이래 戰紀10萬 病死 又는 살해된 자 15, 6만 잇슴으로 현재 前線에 출동하여 잇는 것은 겨우 5, 6만명에 불과하다. 또 경제 及 무기에 잇서 우리들에게 及치 못하야 天壤의 差가 잇슴에 불구하고 그네들이 4, 5년의 토벌을 지나도록 아직 멸망치 아니하는 이유는 어대에 잇는가. 그것은 오직 「조직의 嚴密」이란 一點에 걸니워 잇다. 그럼으로 우의들이 만약 彼等과 갓튼 엄밀한 조직이 잇스면 彼等을 討滅하기는 아무 걱정 어업다. ＜35＞
