Favored Talents of the Medical World

Several doctors from within Seoul city went to Manchuria, saying the Manchurian economy (滿洲景氣) is good. However, upon actually going and seeing, some found it wasn't satisfactory and are returning. Also, many who were in Seoul are returning to the countryside. Mr. Yang Ponggŭn (楊奉根氏), who was the director of Hyŏphwa Hospital (協和病院長) and the Chairman of the Inspection Committee for the Sin'ganhoe (新幹會檢査委員長), went to Hoeryŏng (會寧) in Hamgyŏng North Province (咸北) and opened a practice. Mr. Yi Kapsu (李甲秀氏) of Susong-dong (壽松洞) has suspended his practice, and besides these, there are many other changes.

However, on the other hand, there are also those for whom the times are good. Dr. Im Yunjae (任允宰 博士), a favored talent (寵才) of the medical world (刀圭界), is said to be earning a monthly salary reaching 3,000 Won, and Dr. O Wŏnsŏk (吳元錫 博士) 2,000 Won (though of course, expenses within this must also be many).<111>

**Summary**

This news brief reports on the movement of doctors in Seoul during 1933. Some physicians moved to Manchuria seeking better economic prospects ("Manchurian Boom") but found the situation disappointing and returned, while others relocated to the Korean countryside. Examples include Yang Ponggŭn opening a practice in Hoeryŏng and Yi Kapsu suspending his Seoul practice. In contrast, the article highlights the success of Dr. Im Yunjae and Dr. O Wŏnsŏk, described as "favored talents," who are reportedly earning exceptionally high monthly salaries of 3,000 and 2,000 Won respectively. Historians might find the article interesting for its snapshot of professional mobility driven by perceived economic opportunities (the "Manchurian Boom") versus the reality, the stark contrast in economic fortunes within the Korean medical profession during the colonial era, and the mention of a former high-ranking member of the recently dissolved nationalist organization Sin'ganhoe (Yang Ponggŭn) relocating.

刀圭界의 寵才
서울 시내의 의사 중 여러 분이 滿洲景氣가 조타고 滿洲로 갓다. 그러나 막상 가서 본 즉 시언치 못하야 도로 오는 이도 잇다. 그리고 서울 잇든 이로 시골로 낙향하는 이도 만타. 協和病院長이요 新幹會檢査委員長으로 잇든 楊奉根氏 咸北會寧가서 개업하고 壽松洞의 李甲秀氏는 휴업하고 그 밧게도 변천이 만타. 그러나 한편 세월 잘 맛는 분도 잇스니 刀圭界의 寵才 任允宰 博士는 월급 3,000원 吳元錫 博士는 2,000원 (물론 이 중에 지출도 만겟스나)에 달한다고 한다.)＜111＞
