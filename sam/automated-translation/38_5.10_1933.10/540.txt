Article Title: 가올이 먼길을 떠나랴 하나니 (Autumn is about to depart on a long journey)
Author: 辛夕汀
Article Type: 시 (Poem)

Autumn is about to depart on a long journey
						Sin Sŏkchŏng

Led by a wind transparent as mica (雲母),
Autumn came over some distant constellation;
In the broad daylight of the blue sky, the white moon comes and goes silently,
And at night, like Go stones brushed by waves,
Blue stars flowed through the gaps between thin white clouds.

The yellow ginkgo leaves of the Southern Land (南國)
Make a pilgrimage to the blue sky.
When the long journey begins, in the deep forest where mountain birds sing,
Carefully treading on leaves piled with chestnuts,
When the ripe red persimmons of the old mountain lodge quietly gaze at the sunset sky,
The clear cry of a crow was heard coming over the mountain.

Mother—today, under the warm sunlight of that Back Garden (後園)
Where the cat drowses,
Shall we not split and eat pomegranate seeds, red as a white rabbit's pupils,
And tomorrow, stroll along that forest path red with wild rose (野薔薇) berries,
And talk of these dazzling scenes that autumn leaves behind?
For autumn is now about to depart on a long journey—. <96>

**Summary**

This poem by Sin Sŏkchŏng captures the beauty and transience of autumn. It describes the season's arrival with vivid imagery of the sky, moon, stars, and wind, then shifts to the tangible signs of late autumn—falling leaves, chestnuts, ripe persimmons, and migrating birds—evoking a sense of impending departure. The speaker addresses their mother, suggesting a moment of shared reflection on the fleeting, dazzling beauty of nature before winter arrives. From a historical perspective, the poem's focus on lyrical naturalism and personal sentiment, rather than overt social or political themes, is characteristic of much Korean literature during the colonial period (1930s), when artists often navigated censorship by turning inward or towards nature. Historians might find the poem's use of purely Korean natural imagery and its gentle, reflective tone interesting as a subtle expression of cultural identity and emotion within the constraints of the era.

가올이 먼길을 떠나랴 하나니
						辛夕汀
雲母처럼 투명한 바람에 이끌려
가을이 그 어느 머언 星座를 넘어오드니
푸른 한울의 대낮을 힌 달이 소리 없이 오고가며
밤이면 물결에 스처 나려가는 바독 돌처럼
힌 구름 엷은 새이새이로 푸른 별이 흘러갑데다.

南國의 노-란 은행 입새들이
푸른 한울을 순례한다.
머-ㄴ길을 떠나기 비롯하면 山ㅅ새의 노래 지튼 숲엔
밤알이 싸힌 입새들을 조심히 밟고
묵은 산장 붉은 감이 종용히 夕陽 한울을 바라볼 때
가마귀 맑은소리 산을 넘어 들려읍데다.

어머니- 오늘은 고양이 조름 조으는
저 後園의 따뜻한 볓 아래서
힌 톡끼의 눈ㅅ등자같이 붉은 석류알을 쪼기여 먹으며
그리고 래일은 野薔薇 열매 붉은 저 숲길을 거닐며
가을이 남기는 이 현란한 風景들을 이약이 하지 않으렵니까?
가을이 지금은 머언 길을 떠나랴 하나니-. ＜96＞
