Literati Village of Sŏngbuk-tong
Literati Village of Sŏngbuk-tong

The vicinity of Seoul is entirely renowned for its beautiful mountains and clear waters (山紫水麗), and everyone marvels at the foresight of Great Master Muhak (無學大師) who chose this place as the capital in 옛날 (olden times). Among these areas, Sŏngbuk-ri (城北里) outside Tongsomun (東小門) is famous for the beauty of its springs and rocks (泉石) and its clean air, leading many people from within the city walls to move here recently.
Moreover, being such a tranquil suburb with topography beautifully shaped like a duck's nest (오리 둥주리), many writers who take art as their companion have moved out here to live, forming a scene resembling a kind of Literati Village (文人村).
As of today, those who have already gone out there, built houses, and settled permanently include the poet Kim An-sŏ (金岸曙), the female literary figure Kim Il-yŏp (金一葉), the critic Kim Ki-jin (金基鎭), the novelist Yi T'ae-jun (李泰俊), the critic Hong Hyo-min (洪曉民), and the old polemicist Yi Chong-nin (李鍾麟), among others.

Literati Village of Se'gŏm-jŏng
Just as there is Sŏngbuk-tong (城北洞) in the east, one might say there is also Se'gŏm-jŏng (洗劍亭) in the west; like the two sides of a drum, Se'gŏm-jŏng competes with Sŏngbuk-tong, spreading out in the east and west as a Literati Village (文人村). That Se'gŏm-jŏng outside Chaha-mun (紫霞門) possessed fine mountains and waters is a fact clearly evident (昭然) in history.
Among those who have chosen this place for permanent residence is the novelist Hyŏn Chin-gŏn (玄鎭健)氏, and recently the critic Kim Kyŏng-jae (金璟載)氏 also relocated here, while the political figure Elder Sŏ Chŏng-hŭi (徐廷禧翁) also lives out there. It seems an inevitable trend (必然의 勢) that in the future, many writers and polemicists will increasingly move out to live near Se'gŏm-jŏng.

The Death Age of Yang Guifei
If asked at what age and how Yang Guifei (楊貴妃), the most beautiful woman of the East favored by Emperor Xuanzong (玄宗皇帝) of the Tang (唐) dynasty, died, there might be some who cannot readily answer.
During the rebellion of An Lushan (安祿山), Emperor Ming of Tang (唐明王) abandoned Chang'an (長安) and went into exile (蒙塵). On the way, they stopped at a small village nearby. At that time, the Noble Consort (貴妃), riding in a carriage with Emperor Ming (明王), had a toothache (이(齒)가 압허) and rested midway. It was then that Gao Lishi's (高力士) troop, who were escorting the emperor, believing that eliminating this enchantress (妖婦) who overshadowed the brilliance of this wise ruler (明君) was the way to save the Tang, killed Yang Guifei. At that time, the Noble Consort's age was 38. <119>

**Summary**

This article from 1933 highlights the trend of Korean writers and intellectuals establishing "Literati Villages" in the scenic suburbs of Seoul, specifically Sŏngbuk-tong in the east and Se'gŏm-jŏng in the west. It lists prominent figures like Kim An-sŏ, Kim Il-yŏp, Yi T'ae-jun, Hyŏn Chin-gŏn, and others who had moved to these areas, seeking tranquility and natural beauty outside the bustling city. The piece also includes a short, unrelated historical anecdote about the death of Yang Guifei. For historians, the article provides valuable insight into the lifestyles and residential choices of the Korean cultural elite during the colonial period, possibly reflecting a search for community and respite from urban life under Japanese rule. The list of names offers a snapshot of the contemporary literary scene and their social geography.

城北洞의 文人村
城北洞의 文人村
서울 부근은 모다 山紫水麗하여 녯날 이곳을 수도로 잡은 無學大師의 선견지명에 누구나 경탄하거니와 그 중에도 東小門 밧 城北里는 泉石의 미관, 공기의 청정으로 유명하야 근래에 城안 사람들이 이주함이 만타.
그런데 이러케 한적한 근교인데다가 오리 둥주리가치 지형이 아름다워 예술을 벗삼는 문인들이 만히 나가 사라서 一種 文人村인 듯한 광경을 이루고 잇다.
오늘까지 벌서 그곳 나가서 집 짓고 永住하는 이에 시인에 金岸曙 女流文士에 金一葉 평론가에 金基鎭 소설가에 李泰俊 평론가에 洪曉民 老論客 李鍾麟 등의 諸氏가 잇다.

洗劍亭의 文人村
東에 城北洞이 잇다면 西에 또한 洗劍亭이 잇노라 하리만치 장구의 량쪽 모양으로 東西에 벌너서 文人村을 다투는 곳이 洗劍亭이다. 紫霞門 밧 洗劍亭이 山水 조왓든 것은 역사에 昭然한 사실이다.
이곳에 永住하려고 터전을 잡은 이에 소설가 玄鎭健氏가 잇고 최근에 또 평론가 金璟載氏가 이주하엿스며 政客 徐廷禧翁도 나가서 산다. 아마 자차로도 文人論客이 洗劍亭 부근으로 만히 나가 살니라 함이 필연의 勢일 것 갓다.

楊貴妃의 死齡
唐나라 玄宗皇帝가 총애하든 東洋最高의 미인 楊貴妃는 몃 살에 엇더케 죽엇는고? 하면 얼는 대답을 못할 이가 잇섬즉 하다.
安祿山의 叛에 唐明王이 長安을 버서가 蒙塵 가든 길가에 그 부근 조고마한 촌락에 갓다. 그때 貴妃는 明王과 가치 수레를 타고 가다가 이(齒)가 압허 中途에서 쉬엇다. 그럴 때에 이 明君의 빗을 덥는 요부를 업새는 것이 唐을 구하는 길이라고 황제를 호위하여 가든 高力士의 一軍이 楊貴妃를 죽이엿다. 그때 貴妃의 나이 38세. ＜119＞
