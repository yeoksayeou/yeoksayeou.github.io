Company Announcement
						Respectfully from Samch'ŏllisa Representative Kim Tong-hwan

This past spring, in May, this magazine declared its intention to publish twice a month as a prerequisite for becoming a weekly publication, and we have been implementing this since then. However, as you are also aware, under the current regulations, the manuscript censorship takes time, making it extremely difficult to publish smoothly by the promised dates.

Therefore, until the Publication Law is revised (which we understand will happen in the near future (不遠)), our company hereby announces that we will temporarily suspend this plan and revert to publishing once a month as before (Price 20 chŏn, Format 4x6 BAE판). Furthermore, we intend to publish sister magazines titled "Samch'ŏlli Munhak" (삼천리문학) and "Man'guk Puin" (萬國婦人) when the opportunity arises, in response to the kind support (厚護) of our readers.

August 1st
Respectfully from Samch'ŏllisa Representative Kim Tong-hwan <1>

**Summary**

Samch'ŏllisa announces it is reverting its magazine *Samch'ŏlli* from twice-monthly back to monthly publication due to delays caused by manuscript censorship under current laws. Representative Kim Tong-hwan states this is temporary until the Publication Law is revised. The company also plans to launch two new sister magazines, *Samch'ŏlli Munhak* and *Man'guk Puin*, in the future. This announcement clearly reveals the practical difficulties faced by Korean publishers under Japanese colonial rule, specifically how government censorship directly impacted publication schedules and business plans. Historians would find this a direct piece of evidence illustrating the constraints on press freedom and the publishing industry's adaptation strategies during that period.

社告
						三千里社 대표 金東煥 拜
今春 5월에 本誌는 週刊前提로 월 2회 발간할 것을 선언하야 以來 실행하여 왓사오나 여러분께서도 아시다십히 현재의 법규아래에선 원고검열에 시일이 걸니게 되어서 도저히 약속대로 기일마다 순조롭게 발행하기 곤란하게 되엇나이다.
그러기에 이제 本社는 출판법이 개정(不遠에 개정될 줄 알거니와) 되기 전까지는 일시 此 계획을 중지하고 종전과 如히 월 1회刊(定價20錢, 軆裁 46倍版)으로 복구하올 것을 告하오며 따로 「삼천리문학」과 「萬國婦人」이란 姊妹잡지를 기회보아서 발행하와 讀者 諸씨의 厚護에 答하고저 하나이다.
8월 1일
三千里社 대표 金東煥 拜＜1＞
