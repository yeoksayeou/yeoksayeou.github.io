Magazine Title: 삼천리 第四卷 第八號
Publication Date: 1932년 08월 01일
Article Title: [曙海의 死를 슬퍼하는 애끗는 글월은…] (Heart-wrenching writings mourning the death of Sŏhae...)
Author: 편집자 (Editor)
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editorial Postscript)

There were several other heart-wrenching writings mourning the death of Sŏhae (曙海) besides these, but we have postponed them for another occasion and published only these for now. <94>

**Summary**

This is a brief editorial note explaining that due to space limitations or other reasons, only a selection of writings mourning the death of "Sŏhae" could be published in this issue, with others deferred to a future opportunity. From a historical perspective, this short note reflects the public expression of grief and remembrance for cultural figures (likely the writer Na To-hyang, pen name Sŏhae, who died in 1926) within the colonial Korean press, indicating the significance attributed to literature and its creators during that period.
