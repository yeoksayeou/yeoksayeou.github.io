訂正
Correction

本誌 7월 특대호에 게재된 신문기자 10년 波瀾 史중 金東進씨의 原橋는 5년전 某신문기자 잡지에 게재되얏든 것을 再揭한 것으로 該 文意중 신문사에 대한 비평은 東亞사를 云爲한 것이 절대로 아닌 것으로 만일 此기사 때문에 金씨에게 미안을 끼친 점이 잇다면 본인의 過失임을 告하나이다.
Regarding the manuscript/article (原橋) by Mr. Kim Tongjin (金東進) included in the "Ten-Year Turbulent History of Newspaper Reporters" section published in the special July issue of this magazine (本誌), it was a republication (再揭) of something that had been published in a certain (某) newspaper reporters' magazine five years ago. The critique of a newspaper company within the meaning (文意) of the said (該) text absolutely did not refer to (云爲) Tonga-sa (東亞社). If any offense was caused to Mr. Kim due to this article, I state (告하나이다) that it is the fault (過失) of myself (the editor).

(편집자)＜61＞
(Editor) <61>

**Summary**

The editor of Samch'ŏlli magazine issues a correction regarding an article by Kim Tongjin, republished in the July special issue. This article, originally from five years prior, contained criticisms of a newspaper company. The editor clarifies that these criticisms were not directed at Tonga-sa (likely the Tonga Ilbo newspaper) and apologizes for any trouble caused to Kim Tongjin by the republication, taking personal responsibility for the error. This notice highlights the sensitivity surrounding public criticism, especially potentially involving major entities like the Tonga Ilbo, during the colonial period. Historians might find it interesting as an example of editorial accountability and the implicit pressures faced by publications when handling potentially controversial content from the past.
