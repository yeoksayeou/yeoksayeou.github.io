**Body Text Translation:**
Patriot News

This year marks exactly 50 years since the death of General Giuseppe Garibaldi, the leader of the Red Shirts Party (赤샷스黨), whose name shines brightly in Italian history as a meritorious figure in the Italian independence movement. Therefore, Italy held a nationwide 50th-anniversary memorial ceremony for this patriotic hero. At the same time, the remains of the general's beloved wife (愛妻) Anita, who, in the words of her husband General Garibaldi, was "the possessor of the most perfect individuality known to mankind," were also moved from their reburial site in Genoa initially, and like the General, were immersed in the fervent gratitude and reverence (念) of the entire nation.

The location where she was buried this time is beneath her own bronze statue (銅像) rising on the Janiculum Hill (쟈니그람 丘) overlooking Vatican City (빠디간 市). Furthermore, it is a historic site not far from the old battlefield (古戰場) where General Garibaldi led his personal troops (手兵) and fought against the French Army (佛蘭西軍) and the Papal Army (法軍), making it the most fitting place for the eternal resting place (永久한 휴식소) of a woman famous in Italian history. Anita's remains (遺骸) were transported from Genoa to Rome aboard a special train, escorted by her grandson General Ezio Garibaldi and his wife (夫妻), and the brave soldiers of the "Garibaldi Legion" who gained renown (勇名) serving (從軍) in the World War. Anita was originally Brazilian and thus entirely a foreigner (外國人) in Italy, yet she received more respect (尊敬) than any other woman (他女性) in Italian history. Anita was not only admired for her beauty (美貌) and fine heart (곱다란 맘세). As the wife of a brave general who walked a thorny path (踈棘의 길) for the fight for freedom (自由의 싸홈), she was a woman of great courage (여장부) who remained unyielding (不屈) even amidst cannon fire (砲火), never bringing shame upon her role.

Following her husband General Garibaldi, she rushed into gruesome battlefields (戰場) stained with blood numerous times. Finally, while Garibaldi's army was retreating (浪却) from Rome and fighting desperately (苦戰) against a large pursuing enemy force (敵大軍), she ended her eventful life (多端하든 생애) in some farmhouse (農家).

Amidst the chaos of war (陳馬倥偲중), General Garibaldi had buried her remains (遺骸) in Nice; they were later moved to Genoa, and now, finally, they have been laid to rest in Rome. <25>

**Summary**

This 1932 article from the magazine *Samch'ŏlli* reports on the 50th-anniversary commemoration of Giuseppe Garibaldi's death in Italy. It highlights the concurrent reburial of his Brazilian wife, Anita Garibaldi, in a place of honor in Rome, emphasizing her bravery, loyalty, and status as a heroine revered by Italians despite being a foreigner. The article details her life, participation in battles alongside her husband, and eventual death during a retreat. For historians of colonial Korea, this piece is interesting as it presents a prominent example of Western nationalism and hero worship, focusing on figures who fought for national unification and independence. Publishing such a story in 1932 Korea could subtly evoke parallels or contrasts with the Korean situation under Japanese rule, potentially inspiring nationalist sentiment by showcasing foreign patriots and the reverence they received, especially highlighting the role of a courageous female figure like Anita.

愛國者 消息
伊太利 독립운동의 공로자로써 그의 성명이 燦然히 伊太利 史上에 빗나는 赤샷스黨 首領 쥬젯페 가리빨디 장군이 死歿한지 금년이 꼭 50년임으로 伊利太에서는 전국적으로 이 애국적 영웅의 50주년 기념제를 거행하는 동시에 남편인 가리빨디 장군의 말을 빈다면 「무릇 인류에게 알니어진 가장 완성된 개체의 소유자」인 장군의 愛妻 아니다의 遺骸도 또한 제노아 이장지로부터 당초에 옴기어 장군과 갓치 전국민의 熱誠인 감사이 念이 념치엿다.
이번 埋葬된 장소는 빠디간 市를 나려다보는 쟈니그람 丘에 소사잇는 자신의 동상아래이요 더욱 가리빨디 장군이 그 手兵을 인솔하야 佛蘭西軍 及 法軍과 交戰하든 古戰場에서 멀지아는 유서깁흔 곳으로 伊太利史에 유명한 여성의 永久한 휴식소로 가장 적당한 곳이다. 아니다의 遺骸는 그의 손자 예치오 가리빨디 장군 夫妻 及 세계대전에 從軍하야 勇名을 낫하내든 「가리빨디 군단」의 용사들에게 호위된 특별열차에 탑승되여 제노아로부터 로마에 옴기엿다. 아니다는 本是 부라질사람으로 伊太利에 잇서서는 전혀 외국인이나 伊太利 史上에 잇서서는 如何한 他여성보다도 만흔 존경을 바덧다. 아니다는 그 미모와 곱다란 맘세 뿐만이 아니다. 자유의 싸홈을 위하야 踈棘의 길을 걸어온 勇장군의 妻로써 붓그럽지안을 만콤 砲火속에서도 不屈하든 여장부이다.
남편 가리빨디 장군을 따러 피에 적는 무서운 戰場에 馳驅한 일이 數度 드듸여 羅馬로부터 浪却하는 가리빨디 軍이 추적중 敵大軍때문에 苦戰하는 중 엇든 농가에서 그 多端하든 생애를 맛치엿다.
가리빨디 장군은 陳馬倥偲중에서도 그 遺骸를 니스에 무덧섯든 것을 제노아로 이번은 최후적으로 羅馬에 뭇게 된 것이다.＜25＞
