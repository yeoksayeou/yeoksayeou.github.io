Editor's Postscript
						P'ain

As the editor, I will honestly state one sentiment (所懷). The articles we put our main effort (主力) into for this November issue were three:
1. Criticism of student participation in political movements
2. White-robed officers active overseas (海外活약의 白衣士官들)
3. Impressions of guests from home and abroad (內外賓客의 印象)
Therefore, we intended to make these the great pride of "Samch'ŏlli".

However, for the first topic, we were forbidden from even using the character '政' [politics], resulting in the entire manuscripts of the authors, Mr. Cho Man-sik (曺晩植), Sŏ Sang-il (徐相日), Wŏn Se-hun (元世勳), and four others, being unpublishable. Furthermore, because of that, over 30 other articles, about 200 pages of manuscript submitted together with those manuscripts for censorship, met a certain fate (엇던 變).
Also, the second topic was mostly killed, and parts of the third topic were also omitted.

We learned that the entire 200 pages of manuscript had ended up this way on the morning of October 26th. The manuscripts had been submitted to the authorities for censorship on October 8th. Faced with this, we inevitably had to stay up all night from the 26th to the 27th to hastily prepare (急作) 200 pages of supplementary manuscript to undergo re-censorship (재검열).

Therefore, the content of this month's issue is embarrassingly thinner than planned, and we couldn't increase the page count. Also, since the editing was finished on the 27th, this month's issue has come out a little late. I am not telling this story to seek your sympathy. I am merely informing you of one aspect of the reality facing magazine editors.

Next month is the December issue. As it is the month to wrap up 1932, we plan to make good edits in that sense for this month, and then there is the New Year issue. For this New Year issue, we will greet you with a good structure (체재) and content that can stir the world (一世에 격동을 줄 수 잇도록).

As I prepare to put down my pen, I hear news that the Chungang Ilbo is resuming publication. What could be more joyful for us than the revival (부활) of a media outlet (언론기관)? We pray that the Chosŏn Ilbo also resumes publication soon, so that the forum (論壇) of the Peninsula (半島) may flourish greatly with newspapers and magazines. (P'ain) <100>

**Summary**

The editor, P'ain, explains the significant difficulties faced in publishing the November 1932 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine due to severe censorship by Japanese colonial authorities. Major planned articles, including one critically examining student political involvement, were entirely blocked or heavily censored, leading to the loss of around 200 manuscript pages just days before the deadline. This forced a last-minute scramble to create replacement content, resulting in a thinner, delayed issue. Despite the setbacks, the editor expresses hope for future issues, particularly the upcoming New Year's edition, and welcomes the news of the *Chungang Ilbo*'s revival, praying for the restoration of other outlets like the *Chosŏn Ilbo* to strengthen the Korean press. For historians, this postscript offers a direct, contemporary account of the harsh realities of censorship under Japanese rule, the specific targeting of political content, the practical challenges faced by publishers, and the persistent desire for a free press within colonial Korea.

編輯後記
						파인
편집인으로서 한가지 所懷을 정직하게 말슴하겟슴니다. 이번 11월호에 主力를 너흔 記事는
1. 정치운동에 학생참가의 비판
2. 해외활약의 白衣士官들
3. 內外賓客의 印象의 세가지엇슴니다. 그래서 이것은 「三千里」의 큰자랑거리로 삼으려 햇슴니다.

그리하엿더니 第1題는 정치운동의 「政」字도 못쓰게 되여 筆者이신 曺晩植
徐相日
元世勳, 기타 4氏의 원고 全文이 못실니게 되고 더구나 그것이 까닭이 되야 그 원고와 함께 검열에 너헛든 30餘 題 약 2백매의 원고 전부가 엇던 變을 당하엿슴니다.
그리고 第2題도 대부분이 죽엇고 第3題도 빠진 것이 잇게 되엿슴니다.

이와 갓치 2백매의 원고 전부가 그러케 된 줄을 안 것이 10월 26일 아츰이엿슴니다, 원고 검열은 10월 8일에 당국에 제출하엿던 터이외다. 이레케 되자 부득이 26일부터 밤을 새어 27일까지 사이에 그 보충될 원고 2백매를 急作하여 재검열을 밧게 되엿슴니다.

그러기에 이번달호의 내용은 예정보다 붓그러우리만치 여위고 頁數도 증가치 못하엿슴니다. 그리고 27일에 편집을 끗맛첫스니만치 이번달호가 조곰 늣게 나왓슴니다. 나는 이 사정 이약이를 하여 여러분의 동정을 결코 사자는 것이 아니외다. 다만 잡지 편집자들의 직면하고 잇는 현실의 一面을 알려드릴 뿐이외다.

來月은 12월호이외다. 1932년을 총결산하는 달이니 이달은 그런 의미로 조흔 편집을 할가하며 그리고는 新年號이니 이 新年號만은 一世에 격동을 줄 수 잇도록 조흔 체재와 내용을 가지고 뵈옵겟슴니다.

붓을 노흐려하매 中央日報가 나온단 소리가 들님니다. 언론기관의 부활갓치 우리에게 깃분일이 어대잇스릿가. 어서 朝鮮일보마저 나와서 半島의 論壇이 신문으로 잡지로 크게 떨처지이다 하고 비나이다. (파인)＜100＞
