Paris Dispatch
						Ch'oe Sŭnghŭi

Having arrived in Europe last year, I am achieving results beyond expectations in performances across various European locations. I am pleased to be writing this second letter to you all.

My first performance in Paris on January 30th at the Salle Pleyel in Paris was held in Paris's premier venue with a capacity of 2,700, and I am delighted that it was a great success, receiving tremendous praise from both the audience and critics.
Anyway, Paris is the center of arts in Europe, and as such, the success or failure of my debut (데브-) there greatly influences the success of performances elsewhere in Europe, so I was quite anxious at first. However, I believe that concluding it successfully is due to the experience gained from a year of performances in America.

The first performance in Belgium, Brussels on February 6th was held at the Palais des Beaux-Arts, hosted by the Philharmonique Société (퓌하모닠스사에데-), and this too was a sell-out success.<150> Again, on February 26th, I held a dance recital at the Théâtre Municipal (미읍시플·데아타) in the world-famous scenic spot of Cannes in Southern France (南佛). On March 1st, there was a performance in Marseille, followed continuously by performances in Geneva and Lausanne in Switzerland (西瑞). At the end of the month, I completed performances in Milan, Florence, and Rome in Italy (伊太利), hosted by the Centro Lirico (센트로·리리코), an Italian national management bureau (伊太利 국립 마네-지멘트·뷰로-). Starting from April, there will be performances in five locations in Ireland (愛蘭), and at the end of the month, a second performance in Brussels, Belgium, as well as performances in Antwerp and two other Belgian cities.

Performances in Germany (獨逸) are not yet scheduled, but I trust they will be confirmed sometime in May. The various Scandinavian countries and the United Kingdom (英國) will follow afterwards. Besides these, there are many requests for performances across Europe, but as I must proceed through each region in order, the dates are not yet definitively known.
Thus, the European tour is proceeding extremely smoothly (至極 願調), achieving success beyond expectations.

Furthermore, I have been selected as one of the judges for the 2nd World Dance Competition to be held in Brussels from April 30th to early May, despite my lack of experience, and will be going to Brussels. As you may know, the 1st International Dance Competition was held several years ago in Vienna (윈), and this second one is said to be larger in scale than the previous one. Around two thousand or more dancers and dance groups are expected to participate. While in Brussels for the dance judging, I plan to hold my second recital there.

Also, I have established a research institute in Paris, and whenever I return from performances in various countries, I use the spare moments to practice new works and continue my dance research. This is because Paris is the center of Europe, and also because the headquarters of the "Organisation Artistique Internationale" (오르가니자숑 알데이데이크 안데르내쇼낼), which handles my entire European contract, is located in Paris.

Early May, Paris <151>
<150-151>

**Summary**

This article is a dispatch from the renowned Korean dancer Ch'oe Sŭnghŭi, detailing her highly successful European performance tour in early 1939. She reports on sold-out shows and critical acclaim in major cities like Paris, Brussels, Cannes, Marseille, Geneva, Lausanne, Milan, Florence, and Rome. She outlines upcoming performances in Ireland, Belgium, Germany, Scandinavia, and the UK. Ch'oe also mentions her selection as a judge for the 2nd World Dance Competition in Brussels and her establishment of a research institute in Paris, managed by the Organisation Artistique Internationale. From a historical perspective, the article is significant as it showcases a Korean artist achieving major international recognition and success during the Japanese colonial period, expressing pride in her accomplishments on a global stage and strategically basing herself in Paris, the European cultural capital. This provides insight into the transnational careers pursued by some colonial subjects and their navigation of the international arts scene.

巴里通信
						崔承喜
작년 歐洲에 도착하여 歐洲 각지 공연에서 예상 이상의 성과를 있고 있읍니다. 즐거히 여러분께 제2信을 쓰고 있읍니다.
1월 30일 「巴里의 살-브레이에르」에서의 巴里 제1공연은 定員 2,700의 巴里 제일의 會場이였으나 대성황으로 관객과 비평가에게 대단한 칭찬을 받어서 기뻐합니다.
었쨋든 巴里는 歐州에서 예술의 중심지, 그런만큼 데브-의 성공 如何가 歐洲 각지 공연의 성공 如何에 영향을 미치게 하는 것임으로 처음엔 퍽 불안해 했읍니다. 마는 成功裡에 끝을 막은 것은 1년간 아메리카에 있어서의 공연에서 어든 경험이라고 생각함니다.
2월 6일 白耳義, 브랏세르에서의 제1공연은 「파레·보자르」에서 퓌하모닠스사에데-의 주최 하에 했는데 이것 亦 滿員의 盛況이고＜150＞ 다시 2월 26일 南佛의 세계적 명승지 칸뉴의 「미읍시플·데아타」에서 무용 발표회를 가질 3월 1일에는 마르세유의 공연, 게속해서 西瑞의 쭈네바, 로-잔수 공연, 月末에는 伊太利의 「센트로·리리코」라는 伊太利 국립 마네-지멘트·뷰로-주최에서 미라노, 푸로렌쓰, 로-마 공연을 마치고 4월부터 愛蘭 5ケ所 공연과 월말에는 白耳義 부랏세르 제2공연과 並 안토와-부와 白耳義 3도시에서 하게 됩니다.
獨逸에서의 공연은 日字가 未定이나 5월중에는 확정되리라 밋습니다. 스칸데어나비아 각국과 英國은 그후에 하게 됩니다. 그밖께도 歐洲 각지 공연 신청이 만읍니다 마는 각지를 순서대로 해야겠음으로 아직 日字는 確定히 알 수 없읍니다.
이러케 歐洲 공연은 至極 願調로 예상외의 성공을 보게 되였읍니다.
그리고 4월 30일부터 5월 초순에 이르기까지의 부랏세르에서 열니는 제2회 세계 무용콩쿨에 미숙한 내가 심사원의 한사람으로 선정되여 부랏세르에 가게 되였읍니다. 여러분도 아시겠지마는 제1회 인터내쇼낼 딴스·콩쿨은 수년전 윈에서 열였는데 이것은 그 제2회로 이번 것이 前回보다 대규모라 함니다. 약 2천 이상의 무용가와 무용단체가 참가한다고 합니다. 나는 부랏세르 무용심사에 간 길에 부랏세르 제2회 발표회를 할 작정니다.
그리고 巴里에 연구소를 두고 각국 공연에 갓다 와서는 틈틈이 新作 연습도 하고 무용 연구도 게속합니다. 巴里가 歐州의 중심지요 또 내 全歐州 契約을 引受하고 있는 「오르가니자숑 알데이데이크 안데르내쇼낼」 본부가 巴里에 있는 까닭입니다.
