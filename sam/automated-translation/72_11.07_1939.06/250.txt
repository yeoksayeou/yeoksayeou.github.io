Notes on Figures Elected to the Kyŏngsŏng Municipal Council

Mr. Kang Yongp'yo (康容杓) (Incumbent) 50 years old.
Mr. Kang is currently the chief of general affairs at Yangjŏng Middle School (養正中學) and is a rational person with the enthusiasm to act as a spokesperson for the citizens.
Revealing his aspirations upon running for re-election, he stated firstly that while the two-shift system in elementary education is unavoidable under current conditions in Chosŏn (朝鮮), it should remain merely a temporary emergency measure, and normalization should be rapidly pursued. Another point is the urgent establishment of arterial roads in the old and new city domains to complete the整備 (improvement/consolidation) of transportation systems, among various other things.

Mr. Kang Yŏngho (姜永晧) (Incumbent) 49 years old.
Having been elected in the last supplementary election, he has been very active during that time.
This time, he says he will strive for the expansion of elementary education, the promotion of urban planning, and the realization of covering the Ch'ŏnggye Stream (淸溪川) or creating a canal (運河).
He also expressed his firm conviction to focus particularly on improving social facilities, as Kyŏngsŏng is weak in this area.

Mr. Yi Sin'gu (李信龜) (New) 43 years old.
Mr. Yi taught for a long time at Osan Middle School (五山中學) and is currently the chairman of the board of Samsan Elementary School (三山小學校), dedicating himself to education.
His stated aspirations are the expansion of education, including the establishment of a municipal middle school (府立中學), and the promotion of civil engineering and sanitation facilities. As life begins at 40, and this is his first run for office during his active years, great expectations can be placed on his future.

Mr. Pak Sŭngsŏng (朴勝城) (Incumbent) 42 years old.
Mr. Pak told the reporter:
1. Establishment of autonomy for the municipality (府制).
2. Urgent establishment of free maternity clinics <144> and tuberculosis sanatoriums.
3. Establishment of daycare centers and homes for the elderly.
4. Founding of gymnasiums.
There are so many things to do, but will they proceed as intended? In any case, I am resolved to strive for the clarification [transparency/integrity] of municipal administration.

Mr. Yang Yunsik (楊潤植) (New) 38 years old.
Mr. Yang is a young lawyer who, despite his physique, possesses the spirit and strategy to accomplish great things decisively.
A part of the platform he puts forward includes, firstly, the urgent establishment of a municipal middle school (府立中學); secondly, management of municipally run housing (府營住宅), establishment of communal laundries, expansion of public pawnshops (公益質屋), and promotion of the Ch'ŏnggye Stream (淸溪川) covering project.

Mr. So Wan'gyu (蘇完奎) (New) 38 years old.
Although he is now involved in the legal world, Mr. So Wan'gyu spent a turbulent youth transitioning from literature to law, traveling between Peking (北京) and Tokyo (東京). When asked about his feelings on entering the municipal election race:
"It is about providing integrity [lit. character assistance] to clarify municipal administration. I don't want to make noise before doing the work. If there is work we must do, I intend to devote all my fervent passion (熱) and sincerity (誠) solely to its realization."

Mr. Itō Ponggyu (伊藤奉圭) (New) 37 years old.
Mr. Itō says the following:
"Firstly, the educational discrimination between the children of Chosŏn people and the children of mainland Japanese must be abolished. To do this, there is no other way than to integrate the current first and second shift systems into a single-shift system. True Naesŏn Ilch'e (內鮮一體 - Japan-Korea Unity) cannot be hoped for without abolishing such discrimination. What does it mean to demand Naesŏn Ilch'e while leaving the un-enrolled children of Chosŏn people as they are?"

Mr. Yi Kangbong (李康鳳) (New) 38 years old.
Mr. Yi previously graduated from Pharmacy College (藥學專門) with excellent results and is now the owner of a substantial large store. Currently holding positions as Vice Chairman of the Kyŏngsŏng Pharmacists Association (京城 藥劑士會) and Chairman of the All-Chosŏn organization, the Koryŏ Pharmacists Association (高麗藥劑士會), one can well infer his skill and capacity.
Among his many aspirations, including the issue of establishing a compulsory education (義務敎育) system and expanding and strengthening social work, Mr. Yi says he will especially strive for the completion of sanitation facilities.

Mr. Ye Hŭngsu (芮興洙) (New) 46 years old.
Mr. Ye is currently the Representative Director of Samhŭng Trading Co., Ltd. (株式會社 三興商會) and President of T'aep'yŏng Curio Co., Ltd. (太平 古物*株式會社長), <145> a heavyweight in the business world demonstrating unique shrewdness (敏腕). Having won the honor of election in the 1935 general election for the Municipal Council, he strives for industrial promotion and resource enhancement, stating that the urgent task for Kyŏngsŏng's municipal administration, learning from the development history of advanced cities like Tokyo (東京), is to develop it from a consumer city into a producer city.

Mr. Han Manhŭi (韓萬熙) (Incumbent) 44 years old.
Mr. Han, possessing both a rigid character and a passionate side, exudes fervor, saying, "I have no aspirations or ideals. I will simply faithfully represent what the 700,000 citizens demand and serve (奉公) them." One can predict the future path of Mr. Han, who is full of fighting spirit that transcends all self-promotion and pretense.

Mr. Yi Hongjong (李弘鍾) (New) 49 years old.
Serving as the Standing Director of the Chosŏn Bar Association (朝鮮辯護士協會) and having been active in the legal world for over a decade, Mr. Yi Hongjong was sought out and asked about his aspirations. "The most urgent task currently is, above all, the expansion of elementary education. The difficulty in accommodating the overflowing number of school-age children each year can only be resolved by expanding facilities with national treasury subsidies (國庫補助). Next," he advocates, "a Municipal Industry Department (府産業部) should be newly established for the municipality to encourage household industry among the citizens."

Mr. Cho Pyŏngsang (曺秉相) (Incumbent) 49 years old.
Mr. Cho Pyŏngsang is recognized by himself and others as such a key figure that one associates him with being a council member. Since the implementation of the local autonomy system, Mr. Cho has been involved in everything, and his arguments are thorough. A part of the platform Mr. Cho has put forward this time includes:
1. Equality in tax payment (稅納).
2. Expansion of social work.
3. Promotion towards complete autonomy (自治).
Whatever his principles and assertions, he promises energetic (彈力 있는) activity with firm conviction.

Mr. Yi Chaewŏn (李在元) (New) 41 years old.
Currently active as a heavyweight in the legal field, Mr. Yi is someone who achieved success through extraordinary talent (特才) and effort.
As a fighter (鬪士) who once engaged in youth movements in his hometown of Hwangju (黃州), his future activities are further promised.
Mr. Yi stated he would dedicate his body and soul to fighting as a true representative of the citizens, primarily <146> focusing on resolving the housing problem as part of urban policy.

Mr. Im Hŭngsun (任興淳) (Former) 46 years old.
Mr. Im previously held executive positions (重役) at companies like Tong-A Trust (東亞信託) and Hŭngŏp Housing (興業住宅). After serving as a Kyŏngsŏng Municipal School Councilor (京城府學校 評議員) and a Kyŏngsŏng Municipal Council Member (京城府協議會員), he was elected in the first Kyŏngsŏng Municipal Council election in 1931 (Shōwa 6). He was a fighter who fiercely battled on the front lines at that time, advocating for the expansion of elementary education and public ownership of electricity. Even now, he continues to work steadily, holding various positions such as the First Section Leader of the Kyŏngsŏng East District Defense Group (京城府 東區 防護團 第一分團長), Firefighter Chief (消防員部長), Sakuraoka Town Representative (櫻丘町會總代), and others.
A part of the motto Mr. Im advocates this time is, firstly, to have daily necessity markets directly managed by the municipality (府直營) to promote stability in citizens' lives, and secondly, abolishing the independent accounting system for the first and second education shifts, thorough provision of sanitation facilities in poor residential areas (細民地帶), and utilization of the municipal hospital (府民病院), among other considerable aspirations.

Mr. Ch'oe Paeksŏn (崔白洵) (New) 42 years old.
Currently a key figure in the Chosŏn legal world, he previously served as the President of the First Kyŏngsŏng Bar Association (第一京城 辯護士會長) and is currently a Town Representative (町總代) and an officer in various other organizations.
Mr. Ch'oe has come forward with considerable aspirations, including:
Rapidly establishing a municipal middle school (府立中學), completing the tuberculosis sanatorium (肺結核療養所) and striving for its prevention, and addressing edification (敎化) issues, among others.

Mr. Pak *Myŏng (朴*明) (Incumbent) 41 years old. (*Partial character)
Mr. Pak, who strives to carry out the general will of the citizens concerning various projects, is already running for the third time. He must have gained much experience and refinement (硏磨) during this period. Great expectations are placed on his future activities. He passionately insists (절규한다) that they must strive for the establishment of a so-called "Complete Autonomy System" (完全 自治制) where the Mayor (府尹) is popularly elected (民選), allowing them to fully implement their aspirations and plans (抱負 經倫).
He holds considerable aspirations regarding the expansion and strengthening of social work, thorough completion of urban sanitation facilities, education issues, and others.

Mr. Ch'a Sangho (車相鎬) (Incumbent) 46 years old.
This is Mr. Ch'a's second run. He holds quite a few positions: President of Saeng'ŏp Mutual Aid Co., Ltd. (株式會社 生業共助社長), Auditor of Sŏdaemun Financial Cooperative (西大門 金融組合 監査), Ahyŏn 1st District Representative (阿峴 第一區 總代), Kyŏngsŏng Municipal Council Member (京城府會議員), Post Office Director (郵便所長), among others.
A part of Mr. Ch'a's political views:
1. Promote the encouragement of small and medium industries.
1. For urban beautification, urgently establish relief and guidance organizations (救濟指導機關) for the poor (細民) and beggars (乞兒) <147>, etc.

Mr. Yang Chaech'ang (梁在昶) (Incumbent) 55 years old.
Mr. Yang previously served as a County Magistrate (郡守) for 18 years and is now an executive (重役) at Chosŏn Life Insurance (朝鮮生命). During that time, he was elected once to the Chamber of Commerce Council (商評), twice to the School Council (學評), and twice to the Municipal Council (府議).
Mr. Yang holds many significant aspirations, including: firstly, based on his insights gained from over ten years of public service life, expecting complete autonomy (自治); secondly, implementing compulsory education as quickly as possible, even by a single day; and others.

Mr. Kim Minsik (金敏植) (New) 36 years old.
Mr. Kim is a young fighter (鬪士) with great shrewdness (敏腕). He previously studied in Tokyo (東京) but returned to *Chosŏn (*鮮) after the Great Kantō Earthquake (關東震災), then specially researched politics and economics. During that time, he held positions in education, administrative offices, and the press, and was elected as a Kyŏnggi Provincial Councilor (京畿道會議員) in 1936 (Shōwa 11).
Mr. Kim's views on municipal administration:
1. Re-examination of the municipal administrative structure (府政機§‹ - likely 機構).
1. Balancing and rationalizing the burden on citizens.
1. Fundamental reform of the education problem.
1. Resolving the water supply issue in newly incorporated districts (新編入區) and increasing* the number of municipal hospital branches (府民病院分*增設 - *partial characters), etc.

Mr. Pak Wŏnsin (朴元信) (Incumbent) 51 years old.
Mr. Pak was previously a government-sponsored student (官費留學生) from the Government-General of Chosŏn (朝鮮總督府), graduated from Kyōto Imperial University's Medical College (京都, 帝大, 醫科大學), returned to Chosŏn (歸鮮), established a medical facility at his home, and for over 20 years has solely dedicated himself to serving the patient population in a volunteer capacity. During that time, he served as a School Councilor (學校評*員 - *partial character), township head (面長), and was elected as a Kyŏngsŏng Municipal Council Member (京城府會議員) in Shōwa 11 (1936), acting considerably as a representative for the citizens.
Mr. Pak did not wish to state his aspirations this time, citing the potential嫌 (dislike/suspicion) of self-promotion (自*宣傳 - *partial character). However, he stated that he intends to calmly study (硏討) the current state and future progress of municipal administration, focusing on the various pending issues he has participated in planning (參劃) and the diverse important problems that are immediate concerns (當面의 關心事), and conform to the demands of the era with a sound and faithful (穩健忠* - *partial character) spirit.

Mr. Kang Ch'anghŭi (姜昌熙) (New) 46 years old. (*Note: Article text incorrectly lists him as 新/New, but says he is currently 府議員/a council member).
Mr. Kang is currently a Municipal Council member (府議員); his questions in the council are earnest and to the point. His political views:
1. Establish a 100-year plan (百年大*) for Greater Kyŏngsŏng, whose development potential is unknown.
1. Merge the special budget for education expenses into the general budget to achieve fairness in burden (負担). <148>
1. Establish relief measures for peaceful industries and reinforce* measures to alleviate* the housing shortage (*화책을 강*하라 - *partial characters).
Etc. Currently, he is the Managing Director (專務取締役) of Chosŏn Transportation Co., Ltd. (朝鮮運輸株式會社) and a Director (取締役) of Chosŏn Transport Co., Ltd. (朝鮮運送株式會社).

Mr. Kim Chongyang (金宗亮) (New) 29 years old.
Mr. Kim Chongyang seems familiar even at first meeting. He previously finished Tokyo Higher Technical School (東京高工) and the Ministry of Education's Temporary Teacher Training Institute (文部省 臨時 敎員養成所), served (奉職) in the Government-General of Chosŏn (朝鮮總督府), and then transitioned into the civil engineering and construction business (土木建築業). His political views concern the housing problem for the poor (細民), reform of civil engineering facilities, and many others.

Mr. Kim Ŭngsun (金應純)
Judging solely from the fact that since becoming a special member of the Yongsan Branch of the Imperial Military Reservists Association (在鄕軍人會 龍山分會) in Taishō 3 (1914), Mr. Kim has dedicated himself to various activities such as School Affairs Committee Member (學務委員), Councilor of the Chosŏn Civil Engineering and Construction Association (朝鮮土木建築協會 評*員 - *partial character), Auditor of the Yongsan Financial Cooperative (龍山金融組合監事), Standing Committee Member of the Yongsan Edification District (龍山敎化區 常務委員), Head of the Yongsan Savings Cooperative (龍山貯蓄組合長), Advisor to the Defense Group (防護團相談役), and others, one can well guess Mr. Kim's character and qualifications.

京城府議 當選 人物記
康容杓씨 (現) 50세.
氏는 지금 養正中學 庶務主任으로 또는 府民의 대변자로 활약하는 열성이 있는 理智의 人이다.
이번 재출마의 포부를 드러보니 첬재 초등교육의 二部制는 現下 朝鮮에 빛취여 부득이한 일이나 다만 臨時應急策에 그치게 할 것이고 急速 정상화를 期할 것이라 한다. 그리고 또 한 기지는 新舊府域에 幹線道路를 急設하여 교통기관의 정비를 완성식킬 것 등 여러 가지라 한다.
姜永晧씨 (現) 49세.
지난번 증원선거에 당선되어 그 동안 만흔 활동을 하여 왔는데
이번은 초등교육의 확충, 시가지 계획의 촉진을 비롯하야 청게천의 암거 또는 運河의 실현에 매진켓다 한다.
그리고 경성은 사회시설이 박약한 만큼 특히 이 방면의 개선에 주력하겟다고 굳은 신념을 말하고 있다.
李信龜씨 (新) 43세.
氏는 오랬동안 五山중학교에서 교편을 잡앗고 현재는 三山소학교의 이사장으로 역시 교육에 몸을 받치고 있다.
씨의 말하는 포부는 교육확충에도 府立中學의 설림과 토목위생시설의 촉진인데 인생은 40부터라고 이제 활동기에 첬 출마인지라 앞으로 기대가 크다 할 것이다.
朴勝城씨 (現) 42세.
氏는 기자에게 一府制의 사치제 확립
1. 府制의 자치제 확립
2. 무료산원,＜144＞ 결핵 요양소의 급설
3. 탁아소, 양노원의 설치
4. 체육관의 창설
등등 할 일이야 만치많은 어데 뜻대로 됩니까. 어찌되었던 부정 명랑화에 매진할 각오입니다.
楊潤植씨 (新) 38세.
氏는 체구보다 의기와 책약으로 큰일을 한 목 단단히 이루고야말 소장 변호사다.
씨의 내세운 주장의 일단은 첬째는 府立中學의 급설 둘째 府營住宅 경영 共同洗濯場 설치 공익질옥의 증설 淸溪川 암거공사의 촉진 등을 부르짖는다.
蘇完奎씨 (新) 38세.
지금은 법조게에 뭄을 던지고 있지만 문학에서 법률에로 전환하기까지 北京으로 동경으로 파란 많은 젊은 시절을 보낸 소완규씨 부전선에 발을 내드디려는 소감을 물은 즉
府政 명랑화의 人格補助입니다. 일하기 전에 떠드러 노코 싶지 안허요. 우리가 해야만 될 일이 있다면 뜨거운 熱과 誠을 다하여 다만 그 실현에 매진하려고 함니다.
伊藤奉圭씨 (新) 37세.
이등씨는 이러케 말한다.
첫째 조선인 자제와 일본 내지인 자제의 교육적 차별을 철폐할 것이다. 그러자면 현재의 1, 2부 경제를 1반 경제로 편입하는 것밖에 없는데 이런 차별를 페지 안코는 진정한 내선일체는 바랄 수 없다. 현재 조선인 자제의 미취학 아동을 그대로 두고 내선일체만 요구하는 것은 무었인가.
李康鳳씨 (新) 38세.
氏는 일즉이 藥學專門을 우수한 성적으로 나왔으며 지금은 당당한 大店舖의 주인이다. 현재 京城 藥劑士會 會副長 또 全鮮的 단체인 高麗藥劑士會 會長이란 자리까지 차지한 것으로 보아 그의 手腕力量을 가히 推察하겠다.
氏는 義務敎育制度의 制定問題 社會事業 擴充 强化, 등 많은 포부를 가지고 있는 중에서도 가장 위생시설의 완비를 위하여 더욱 힘써 보겠다 한다.
芮興洙씨 (新) 46세.
氏는 지금 株式會社 三興商會 代表取締役이요. 太平 古物*株式會社長으로서＜145＞ 독특한 敏腕을 보이고 있는 實業界의 重鎭이다. 昭和10년(1935년) 府會 總選擧戰에 출마하여 당선의 榮冠을 획득한 그는 京城府政의 급선무는 東京과 같은 선진도시의 발전사에 鑑하여 消費都市로부터 生産都市에로 발전식히지 않으면 안된다고 産業助長 資力增進에 노력하고 있다.
韓萬熙씨 (現) 44세.
강직한 성격에다가 열정적 반면를 갖훈 씨는 나에게는 아모런 포부와 이상도 없읍니다. 70만 부민의 요구하는 바를 충실히 代辯하고 그들에게 奉公하는 것뿐입니다. 라고 열을 내뿜는다. 모든 자기 선전과 허식을 초월한 투지만면한 씨의 앞 길을 예측할 수 있다.
李弘鍾씨 (新) 49세.
朝鮮辯護士協會 常務理事로 있으며 십수년간 법조게를 위하야 활약하고 있는 李鍾弘씨를 찾고 소회의 포부를 물으니 현재의 급무는 첬재 무엇보다도 초등꼬육 확충이다. 매년 넘치는 학령아동의 수용난은 國庫補助로 시설을 확충하여야 비로소 구할 수 있으며 다음은 府産業部를 신설하야 府로서 부민의 家庭工業을 장려할 것을 부르짖는다.
曺秉相씨 (現) 49세.
조병상씨 하면 부회의원을 연상할 마큼 중진적 존재임을 자타가 인정한다. 지방자치제 실시이래 모주리 씨가 관게하엿고 논지도 철저하다. 이번 씨가 내걸은 포부의 일단은
1. 稅納의 균등
2. 사회사업의 확충
3. 완전한 自治에의 촉진
