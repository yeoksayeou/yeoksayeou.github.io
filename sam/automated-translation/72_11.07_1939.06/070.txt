Re-debut in the Literary World
						Chŏn Yŏng-t’aek

They say even mountains and rivers change in ten years, so it is truly surprising that *Samch’ŏlli* (三千里) has reached its full 10th anniversary since its founding. Moreover, in a place like Korea (朝鮮) where the publishing business is extremely difficult (困難), the fact that it has been run independently without any social or financial backing truly compels one to bow one's head and express respect (敬意). The contributions *Samch’ŏlli* has made to our society and culture over the past 10 years can be said to be great.

Now, upon this 10th anniversary, as I am asked to speak about my connection with *Samch’ŏlli*, I will say a word.

As mentioned above, *Samch’ŏlli*’s contributions over 10 years are great, but its contribution to the Korean literary world (文壇) is truly the greatest. No newspaper or magazine could match *Samch’ŏlli*. Along with Kim Tong-in (金東仁) and Chu Yo-han (朱요한), I was involved in founding *Ch’angjo* (創造) and also had some relation to the Korean literary world, having published a few works (作品), so at that time (當時), I was considered part of the so-called literary figures. However, afterwards (其後), I put down my creative (創作) pen, went abroad (海外) for a while, and threw myself into religious movements, thus losing contact with the literary world. I had become forgotten by the literary world (especially by those who emerged later). It was *Samch’ŏlli*’s editor-in-chief (主幹), Mr. Kim Tong-hwan (金東煥), who occasionally included me in the ranks of literary figures or requested creative works. Over the past five or six years, amidst my busy schedule with religious movements, I couldn't refuse his earnest requests and ended up publishing one or two creative pieces. Some thought I was a newcomer, some refreshed their past memories, and some came to know who I was for the first time.

In any case, *Samch’ŏlli* reintroduced (再紹介) me to the literary world, encouraged me to take up the creative pen again, and thus enabled me to make a re-debut (再出發) in the literary world.

The words, "Somehow, I feel that the writings of the literary figures from the time of *Ch’angjo* (創造) and *P’yehŏ* (廢墟) carry more weight, so I prefer those works," gave me strength and encouragement. <60>

**Summary**

Chŏn Yŏng-t'aek expresses deep gratitude towards the magazine *Samch'ŏlli* on its 10th anniversary. He highlights its significant contributions to Korean culture and especially its unparalleled role in the literary world. Chŏn recounts how, after co-founding the influential literary group *Ch'angjo* and publishing some early works, he drifted away from literature due to overseas travel and involvement in religious movements. He credits *Samch'ŏlli*'s editor, Kim Tong-hwan, for persistently encouraging him and publishing his new works, effectively reintroducing him to the literary scene and prompting his "re-debut." Historically, the article underscores the vital role *Samch'ŏlli* played as a cultural platform during the difficult colonial period, nurturing literary talent and maintaining connections within the community. It also offers a personal glimpse into the career trajectory of an early modern Korean writer, showing how personal circumstances and external encouragement shaped literary participation.

文壇에 再出發
						田榮澤
10년이면 강산이 변한다는데 「三千里」가 창간한 지 만 10년의라니 참 놀라옵읍니다. 더구나 朝鮮같이 출판사업이 극히 困難한 데서 게다가 아모 사회적 재정적 배경이 없이 獨力으로 해왔다는 것은 과연 머리를 숙여 敬意를 표하지 아니할 수 없읍니다. 三千里가 과거 10년에 우리 사회문화에 끼친 공적은 크다 하겠옵니다.
이제 10주년 기념을 당하여 「나와 三千里」의 인연에 대하여 말슴을 하라니 한 마디 하겠읍니다.
우에도 三千里의 10년 공적이 큰 것을 말하였거니와 三千里의 朝鮮文壇에 대한 공적은 진실로 가장 큼니다. 어느 신문이나 잡지가 三千里를 당할 자가 없을 것이외다. 나는 金東仁
朱요한과 같이 창조를 하고 또 朝鮮文壇에 관계하여 약간의 작품을 내놓은 일이 있어 당시에는 소위 文人측에 들었으나 其後에 창작의 붓을 놓고 잠시 해외에 갓다가 몸이 종교운동에 던저 지났기 때문에 文壇과 교섭이 없고 文壇에서 (그 중에도 그 후에 새로 나오신 이들께서) 잊어지게 되었든 것을 三千里 主幹 金東煥 형이 가끔 나를 文人班列에 넣어주고 혹은 창작을 청하여 과거 五六 동안에 역시 종교운동에 총망한 틈에 그 간청을 이기지 못하여 써 놓은 창작 1, 2편을 내놓게 되어 어떤 이는 新人으로 안 이도 있었으나 어떤 이는 과거의 기억을 새롭게 하고 어떤 이는 비로소 누군줄을 알게 하어 주었읍니다.
여하간 三千里는 나를 文壇에 다시 再紹介를 하시고 나도 다시 창작의 붓을 들도록 권장해 주서서 나로 하여 文壇에 재출발을 하도록 해 주섰읍니다.
「나는 아모래도 創造 廢墟 당시의 문인들이 쓴 것이 무게가 있는 것 같아서 그때 것이 좋아 보여요」하는 말이 나에게 힘을 주고 권장해 주었읍니다.＜60＞
