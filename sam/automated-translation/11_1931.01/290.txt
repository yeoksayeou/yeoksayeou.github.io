**Body Text Translation:**
Gratitude and Apology
						P'ain

The Sin'ganhoe (新幹會), which was formed and grew under such fervent nationwide cheers, has now suddenly seen the beacon fire of dissolution arise from one corner of its organization. That Chosŏn's largest association, boasting 200 branches and 20,000 members, has come to face talks of dissolution is truly a grave matter, and I believe its conclusion can only be reached after sufficient earnest and sincere discussion and criticism from the entire populace.

Thus, although our company had absolutely no time during editing, we decided to first feature the guiding theories from the perspectives of both dissolution proponents and opponents, as well as third parties on the front lines of the social movement. However, it is most regrettable that we were unable to obtain and include the articles clarifying the theoretical grounds of the dissolving branches, such as P'yŏngyang (平壤), Iwŏn (利原), and Pusan (釜山), which were most needed, due to time constraints. Nevertheless, regarding this dissolution issue, this journal promises to provide ample space hereafter for the theoretical struggle among intellectuals.

The issue of selecting representatives of the Korean nation (朝鮮民族)—this, too, I know is a grave matter eagerly seeking the opinions of intellectuals ahead of this autumn's Pacific Conference (太平洋會議). And aren't "A Comprehensive View of Foreign Capital" (外來資本總觀) or "The Great Concessions Departing" (떠나가는 大利權) issues that are all too real in our daily lives? I believe it is right for us to think truthfully and come to understand these things with a mutually research-oriented attitude.

Might not "The Crowd of Exiles Wandering All Nations" (萬國에 流轉하는 亡命客무리) also be a text worth reading on a deep winter night, resting one's head on a folded arm?

However, I (生 - *saeng*, humble self-reference) deeply apologize before you all. When *Samch'ŏlli* was preparing its New Year issue for its third year, I never dreamed it would turn out to be this poor in quality. If it was only ever going to be this much from the start, I would have broken my brush and gone down a different path. However, last December truly brought an utterly unexpected calamity (厄變), placing us in a difficult situation with not even two or three days' leeway to make the New Year issue or anything else, so we were unable to handle important issues, not even addressing a tenth of what we had considered. We couldn't even publish the December issue, and now the New Year issue has also ended up like this, truly making me sweat profusely (汗出沾背 - *hanch'uljŏmbae*), but if this was an unavoidable action preceding a great leap forward, might you, esteemed readers (諸氏 - *chesi*), perhaps find it in your hearts to forgive...?

The December issue was almost completely printed and bound when that calamity struck, so we could not send it out. For that reason, since a portion of the December printing was used directly in this New Year issue, there are many places where the editorial order is also mixed up. Please overlook all of this.

And one more matter for apology and notification: regarding the issue of a wife's fidelity while her husband is in prison in the previous month's issue, Mr. Yu Yŏngjun (劉英俊) expressed the general sentiment, "If a husband is imprisoned for perhaps 10 years or more, wouldn't it be somewhat difficult to maintain fidelity?" This was recorded incorrectly due to a mishearing by our company's reporter, so we respectfully correct that passage.

Now the old year has gone, and the new year has come. Facing the shining light directly, I only hope that your struggles, along with your love for this journal, will grow even deeper. (P'ain) <69>

**Summary**

The author, P'ain (likely Kim Tong-hwan), apologizes profusely for the poor quality and delayed publication of the *Samch'ŏlli* magazine's New Year issue, citing an unspecified "calamity" (厄變) in December 1930 that disrupted production and even prevented the December issue's distribution. Despite this, the editorial highlights pressing contemporary issues: the sudden emergence of calls to dissolve the major nationalist organization Sin'ganhoe, promising future coverage of the debate; the upcoming Pacific Conference and the need to select Korean representatives; and the economic impact of foreign capital and concessions. It also includes a correction to a previous article concerning a sensitive social issue. From a historical perspective, the article offers a glimpse into the operational difficulties faced by Korean publications under colonial rule (the "calamity" possibly referring to censorship or suppression), the internal conflicts within the Korean nationalist movement (Sin'ganhoe dissolution debate), and the key political and economic concerns occupying Korean intellectuals in early 1931.

발행일	1931년 01월 01일

기사형태	사고·편집후기

感謝와 謝罪
						파인
全民族적으로 그러케도 열열한 환호 아래에 결성되어 生長하든 新幹會가 이제 그 조직체의 一角에서부터 돌연 解消의 烽火가 이러낫슴니다. 200支會, 2만 會員을 擁한 朝鮮 최대의 結社가 解消說에 직면하게 되엇다 함은 실로 중대문제로 이것은 全 民衆의 熱心하고도 眞麾스러운 충분한 討議, 批判이 잇슨 뒤에서만 비로소 그 결론을 지을 것인 줄 아나이다.
그리하야 本社는 도저히 編輯時日이 업슴에도 불구하고 爲先 解消論者와 反解消論者와 또 사회운동선상에 선 제3자로부터 各各 자기 시각에서 바라보는 그 指導的 이론을 싯기로 한 바 다만 가장 필요하엿든 平壤利原, 釜山 등 解消支會의 이론적 근거를 究明한 論文이 그만 時日관계로 어들 수 업서서 並載치 못하엿슴이 遺憾千萬이외다. 그러나 이 解消문제에 대하여는 本誌는 압흐로도 識者의 理論투쟁을 위하야 충분한 지면을 提供할 것을 약속하여 두나이다.

朝鮮民族的 대표의 迀¸出문제―이것 또한 今秋의 太平洋會議를 압두고 識者의 의견을 熱求하는 중대문제인 줄 암니다. 그러고「外來資本總觀」이나「떠나가는 大利權」은 우리들 현실생활에 너무나 逼眞한 문제가 아니오리까. 이런 것은 彼此에 硏究的 태도를 가지고 眞實하게 생각하며 깨달어 감이 올홀 줄 아나이다.

「萬國에 流轉하는 亡命客무리」도 깁흔 冬夜, 팔벼개 뵈고 可히 읽을 만한 글월이 아닐는지요.

그러나 生은 여러분 압헤 깁히 謝罪함니다. 三千里가 제 3년 맛는 新年號를 꾸밀 때에 결코 이와 가치 貧弱한 것이 되어 지리라고는 꿈에도 생각하지 안엇슴니다. 애초에 이것 밧게 아니 될 것이엇다면 生은 붓을 꺽고 다른 길로 갓슬 것이외다. 그러나 작년 12월은 실로 千萬意外의 厄變으로 新年號 만들고 엇저고 할 2,3일의 여유도 업는 難局에 처하게 되엇스매 생각하든 바의 십분 일에도 차지 안케 중요한 문제들을 취급하지 못하엿슴니다. 12월호도 못내고 이제 또 新年號도 이것 뿐이 되어서 실로 汗出沾背외다만은 이것이 大飛躍을 前期로 한 부득이한 所行이엇다면 諸氏-幸혀 용서 못하실가...... .

12월호는 거지반 印刷製本이 다 되는 판에 그 厄이 잇서서 發送치 못하엿슴니다. 그런 까닭에 12월 印刷分을 이 新年號에 一部 그냥 流用한 관계로 編輯順序도 뒤석긴 곳이 만슴니다. 모다 눌너보아 주시압.

그러고 또 한가지 謝告할 것은 前月號 男便在獄中妻의 守節문제에 대하야 劉英俊씨가『10여년 이상이나 만일 남편이 在獄하는 경우에는 좀 守節하기 困難치 안을가』하는 大旨의 말슴을 本社記者의 誤聞으로 그리 記載되엇스니 該一文을 삼가 訂正하나이다.

이제 묵은 해는 가고 새해는 왓슴니다. 照耀하는 光明을 直面하야 諸氏의 奮鬪와 아울너 本誌에 대한 사랑이 더욱 두터워지기를 바랄 뿐이외다.(파인) ＜69＞
