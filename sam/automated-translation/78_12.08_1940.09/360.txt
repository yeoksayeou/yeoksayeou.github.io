Magazine Title: 삼천리 (三千里)
Publication Date: 1940년 09월 01일
Article Title: 밤이면 밤마다 (Night After Night)
Author: 李庸岳
Article Type: 시 (Poem)

Night After Night
						Yi Yong'ak

This one who steps on my chest and walks like mad
This one who comes along the gloomy alleyway

It feels as if an unwanted heavy hand will be placed on my shoulder
Covering my eyes with a red cloth (붉은 보재기)
You
It seems you will enjoy the end of a blind man,
Sobbing and feeling it

This one who received the black blood of Meletus <196>
Night after night
Is the narrow path I return to anxiously
Though I could lightly cast away all souls by sucking on the wine cup
The place I return to in the end
Is the house of illusions. It is a pitch-black room

There, I have locked them up with your Zeus
The various sins of mine that you wish to peek at

But please retreat now
On the day the blue, truly blue sky serves me
Finding you
I will cross many rivers
I will cross without pride or tears <197>
<196-197>

**Summary**

This poem by Yi Yong'ak expresses intense anxiety and a sense of being haunted or persecuted. The speaker describes an ominous presence ("you") that approaches "night after night," evoking feelings of dread and impending judgment, symbolized by references to Meletus (accuser of Socrates) and Zeus. The speaker feels trapped with their "sins" in a dark, illusory space but retains a defiant hope for a future day of liberation ("the blue sky serves me"), when they will confront this tormentor after crossing "many rivers." From a historical perspective, the poem's heavy atmosphere of fear, guilt, and anticipated judgment, while framed personally, could reflect the oppressive psychological climate of late colonial Korea. The use of classical allusions might be a strategy to universalize personal angst or subtly critique the situation without direct political commentary, making it interesting for historians studying cultural expression under censorship.

밤이면 밤마다
						李庸岳
가슴을 밟고 미칠듯이 거러 오는 이
음침한 골목길을 따라 오는 이

바라지 않는 무거운 손이 어깨에 놓여질 것만 같슴니다
붉은 보재기로 나의 눈을 가리우고
당신은
눈 먼 사나이의 마지막을
흑흑 느끼면서 즐길 것만 같슴니다

메레토스의 검은 피를 받은 이＜196＞
밤이면 밤마다
내 초조로히 돌아가는 좁은 길이올시다
술잔을 빨면 모오든 영혼을 가벼히 물닐칠수 있었으나
나종에 내 돌아가는 곳은
허깨비의 집이 올시다. 캄캄한 방이올시다

거기 당신의 쩨우스와 함께 가두어 뒀음니다
당신이 엿보고 싶을 가지가지 나의 죄를

그러나 어서 물러가십시요
푸른 정영코 푸르른 하눌이 나를 섬기는 날
당신을 찾어
여러 강물을 건너 가겠음니다
자랑도 눈물도 없이 건너 가겠읍니다 ＜197＞
＜196-197＞
