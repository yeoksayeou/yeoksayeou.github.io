Baron Sumitomo's Income: 5 Million Won

Taxes alone are 4 Million Won, Survey of Other Hanshin Financial Circle Incomes

The president of the Sumitomo Company (住友會社), famous for owning much forest and mining land even in Korea, Baron Sumitomo Kichizaemon (住友吉左衛門), who is under 30 years old, has an income this year of 5.5 million won. The income tax paid to the state amounts to 3.57 million won, and combined with a surtax (賦課稅) of 500,000 won, it reaches 4 million won. In the cultural field, the famous painters Hashimoto Kansetsu (橋木關雪) and Takeuchi Seihō (竹內栖鳳) both earn 70,000 won (last year it was 30,000 won), and the painter Dōmoto (堂本畵伯) earns 30,000 won.

In the theater and film world, Bandō Tsumasaburō (阪東妻之助) earns 50,000 won, Kataoka Chiezō (片岡千惠藏) 40,000 won, Ichikawa Utaemon (市川右太衛門), Arashi Kanjūrō (嵐寬壽郞), and Ōkōchi Denjirō (大河內傳次郞) each earn 30,000 won. 550,000 Won from 1 Million Won

Besides this, about 500,000 won is deducted through source taxation (源泉課税) on bank interest and dividends, totaling 4.1 million won. Hŭngsadan Dissolves After Thirty Years (*Translator's Note: text says 20 years later*)

Property Worth 100,000 Won Offered for Korean Education

Recently, the Hŭngsadan (興士團), a Korean ideological group in Shanghai organized around the late An Ch'angho (安昌浩), announced its dissolution. The aforementioned Hŭngsadan's Far East Branch (遠東支部) was organized about 20 years ago, in Taishō 9 (1920), establishing its headquarters at No. 30 Dadeli, Xiafei Road (霞飛路大德里), French Concession (佛租界), and continued its ideological activities. In April, Shōwa 7 (1932), several of its leaders were arrested, but it persisted somehow. Then, in the autumn of Shōwa 12 (1937), upon hearing the news that its sister organization, the Suyang Tong'uhoe (修養同友會), had been dissolved, they sought an opportunity to dissolve themselves, finally doing so on July 16th.

Meanwhile, the group owned about 1,800 p'yŏng of land in Nanking, worth 100,000 won at market price, which served as the foundation for operating an elementary school called Tongmyŏng Academy (東明學院). As the guiding organization, Hŭngsadan, was dissolved, they donated the land to the Shanghai Agency of the China Expeditionary Army General Headquarters (支那總軍司令部 上海機關) on July 8th. Subsequently, on July 16th, they donated all the organization's documents and a cash donation of 300 won as well, simultaneously announcing the dissolution of the Hŭngsadan Far East Branch. Consequently, the Shanghai Agency of the China Expeditionary Army General Headquarters entrusted this matter to Secretary Harada (原田) of the Shanghai Branch Office of the Government-General of Chōsen (朝鮮總督府 上海出張所), requesting that the funds be used for the education of fellow Koreans on the peninsula. Currently, specific methods for this are under consideration.

Hŭngsadan Far East Branch Dissolution Statement

Around Taishō 9 (1920), the Far East Branch of the Hŭngsadan, a nationalist group, was organized under the leadership of the late An Ch'angho (安昌浩). Its office was located at No. 10 Meirenli, Beidilu (貝締鏖路 美仁里) in the Shanghai French Concession (上海法界), later moved to No. 30 Dadeli, Xiafei Road (霞飛路 大德里), where it operated. After An Ch'angho was arrested (被捉) in April, Shōwa 7 (1932), the association's activities stalled and remained dormant (有耶無耶) for 3-4 years. In the autumn of Shōwa 12 (1937), <8> upon hearing the news that its sister organization in Korea, the Suyang Tong'uhoe (修養同友會), had been dissolved, we also decided to voluntarily dissolve (自行解消) the Hŭngsadan Far East Branch. Accordingly, having swept away past mistaken ideologies through self-awakening (自覺), reaffirming our status as subjects of the Great Japanese Empire (大日本帝國), and striving forward on the true path of Imperial Subjects (皇國臣民), we hereby publicly declare in writing the renewal of the ideology of the aforementioned members and the complete dissolution, in name and reality, of the said organization.

**Summary**

This article from the September 1940 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine provides a list ranking the annual incomes of prominent Korean businessmen, landowners, and figures in Seoul (Kyŏngsŏng), contrasting them with the significantly higher incomes of Japanese counterparts in Seoul and major Japanese cities like Ōsaka and Tōkyō. It details high income tax rates in Japan, noting Baron Sumitomo paid roughly 75% tax. Other news includes Pak Hŭngsik acquiring a significant stake in Tongil Bank, the dissolution of the nationalist Hŭngsadan group in Shanghai and donation of its assets, the erection of a monument to the Paekche scholar Wani in Tōkyō, and a breakdown of foreign residents in Korea, highlighting the small number of British nationals amidst espionage concerns. For historians, the article offers valuable insight into the economic hierarchy and disparity between Koreans and Japanese during the late colonial period, the structure of the Korean elite (businessmen, landowners, educators), Japanese economic power, the suppression of Korean nationalist organizations abroad under Japanese pressure, and demographic data on foreign residents. The income list provides specific data points on the wealth accumulated by certain Korean collaborators and entrepreneurs operating within the colonial system.

海外, 海內各方面
三十年만에 興士團이 解散
財産十萬圓은 朝鮮人敎育에 提供
최근에 上海에 있는 朝鮮人의 사상단체로 고 안창호(安昌浩)를 중심으로 조직된 흥사단(興士團)은 해산의 성명을 발표하였는데 전기 흥사단은 지금으로부터 20여년 전인 대정(大正)9년(1920년)에 원동지부(遠東支部)가 조직되어 불조게하비로대덕리(佛租界霞飛路大德里)30호에 본부를 두고 사상운동을 게속하다가 소화7년(1932년) 4월에 그 간부 수명이 검거되자 그런대로 지속하여 나려오든 중, 소화12년(1937년) 가을에 그 자매단체인 修養同友會가 해산되고 말었다는 소식을 듣고 해산의 기회를 찾다가 마츰 지난 7월 16일에 해산하고 말었다고 한다.
그러는 한편 그 단체의 소유로 南京에 있는 1천 800평 가량의 토지 시가 10만원를 기본으로 하여 東明學院이란 소학 정도의 학교를 경영하여 왔든 것이 지도기관인 흥사단이 해소되게 되자 그 토지를 지난 7월 8일 支那總軍司令部 上海機關에 헌납하고 이어서 7월 16일에는 그 단체의 서류 전부와 헌금 3백원까지도 헌납하는 동시에 흥사단원 동지부 해소성명을 발표하였다는 것이다. 그리하여 지나 총군사령부 상해기관에서는 조선총독부 상해 출장소 原田사무관에게 이 일을 일임하여 반도동포 교육기금으로 써달라고 부탁하게 되었음으로 방금 그 구체적인 방법을 고려 중이라 한다.

興士團 遠東支部 解消聲明書
大正9년(1920년)경에 故 安昌浩의 주동으로 민족주의단체인 興士團 遠東支部가 조직되어 사무소를 上海法界 貝締鏖路 美仁里 10號에 두었다가 其後에 霞飛路 大德里 30號로 移轉視務하여 오다가 昭和7년(1932년) 4월에 安昌浩가 被捉된 후에는 會務가 정체되여 유야무야르 3,4년간 경과하던중 昭和12년(1937년) ＜8＞ 秋에 朝鮮에서 本團의 자매단체인 修養同友會가 해산되었다는 소식을 듣고 우리도 또한 興士團遠東支部를 自行解消키로 하였다. 따라서 과거에 그릇된 사상을 一掃 自覺하고 大日本帝國의 臣民인 것을 재인식하며 皇國臣民의 참다운 길로 매진하여 오던 중 茲今에 右團員 思想의 更新과 그 단체가 名實이 완전 해소되었음을 文字로써 公的으로 聲明하는 바이다.
