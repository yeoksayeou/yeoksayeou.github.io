Editor's Postscript

With the birth of the new Konoe Cabinet, raising the banner of a new political platform encompassing the unification of political parties, autonomous, active, and constructive diplomacy, an economy shedding foreign dependence, increased production and conservation of daily necessities, and educational reform, and with the increased demand for national self-awareness, we believe the populace on the home front (銃後국민) must cooperate further. Thus, we fervently pray to see the swift completion of the handling of the Incident (事變).

We are immensely grateful that Consul General Pak Sŏk-yun, despite his lack of time after returning from Poland (波蘭), has especially contributed an article for this journal. We also believe that the articles by Mr. Yi Kap-nyŏng and Mr. Han Sang-nyong on the development status of Koreans in North China (北支) and Central China (中支) are points of pride for this journal and worth reading.

While the roundtable discussion on the film "Festival of Nations" (民族の祭典) and the "Female Writers' Roundtable" (女流文人座談會) article are likely refreshments for readers during the summer heat, the poem by Mr. Chu Yo-han, who has taken up his pen after a long time, is also undoubtedly a pleasure for us. Furthermore, the dialogue between Manchukuo Film Association (滿映) star Miss Yi Hyang-nan (李香蘭), who came from Manchuria (滿洲), and our journal's reporter will also be a special feature of this month's issue.

Notably, this month's literary section is a special feature titled "Selection of Masterpieces by World Female Writers" (世界女流作家名作選), carrying unique works by Ding Ling (丁玲), Pearl S. Buck (팔.뻑), and Keyes (케이스 諸氏), the most active rising talents in the female creative world. Likewise, in the critique section, we are confident that publishing renowned essays under the theme "War Literature and National Literature" (戰爭文學과 民族文學) will not disappoint our esteemed readers (讀者 諸彦). Besides these, articles such as "The Great Achievements of Dr. Wang In" (王仁博士偉績), "The Thoughts of Kim Ok-kyun" (金玉均의 생각), and "A Dialogue on Korean Literature and Arts & Crafts as Seen by Mainland Japanese" (內地人이 본 朝鮮文學과 美術工藝에 對한 對談會) are also pieces that are difficult to come by. <250>

**Summary**

This editor's postscript from the September 1940 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine highlights the content of the current issue while framing it within the contemporary political context. It begins by referencing the new Konoe Cabinet in Japan and its wartime policies, explicitly calling for cooperation from the Korean populace ("home front populace") to conclude the "Incident" (Sino-Japanese War). The postscript then promotes various articles, including contributions about Koreans in China, cultural roundtables (film, female writers), poetry, an interview with the famous actress Yi Hyang-nan (Ri Kōran), a special feature on international female writers, critical essays on war and national literature, and historical/cultural pieces on Wang In, Kim Ok-kyun, and Korean arts as seen by Japanese.

For historians, the article is revealing in its explicit alignment with Japanese wartime political rhetoric, urging cooperation with the Konoe government's "New Political Platform" and the war effort. The juxtaposition of promoting Korean cultural figures (Chu Yo-han, Yi Kap-nyŏng, Han Sang-nyong) and history (Wang In, Kim Ok-kyun) alongside overt calls for support of the Japanese war effort and featuring Japanese/Manchukuo figures (Konoe, Yi Hyang-nan/Ri Kōran, Mainland Japanese perspectives) exemplifies the complex cultural landscape and pressures faced by Korean publications during the late colonial period. The specific mention of "War Literature and National Literature" as a theme also points to the pervasive influence of the war on cultural discourse.

編輯後記
近衛新內閣이 탄생하여 정당의 단일화, 자주적 적극적 건설적 외교, 외국의존 청산의 경제, 생활필수품의 증산과 절약, 敎學쇄신 등의 新政綱의 기치를 들고, 국민의 자각을 일층 요망함에 있어 銃後국민은 더욱 협력하여야 할 줄 아나이다. 이리하여 事變 처리의 완성을 하루속히 보기를 빌어마지 않나이다.

波蘭에서 돌아오신 朴錫胤총영사께서 없으신 시간에 특히 본지를 위해서 글을 주신 것은 감사하기 짝이 없으며, 李甲寧씨와 韓相龍씨의 北支, 中支의 朝鮮人 발전상도 一讀할 본지의 자랑인줄 아나이다.

영화 『民族의 祭典』 좌담회와 『女流文人座談會』의 기사는 銷夏의 讀書子에게 보내는 청량제려니와, 오래간만에 붓을 드신 朱耀翰씨의 詩도 우리의 즐거움이 아닐 수 없습니다. 더욱이 滿洲로부터 온 滿映스타-李香蘭양과 본지 기자와의 대담회도 이달 본지의 異彩일 것이외다.

특히 今月號 문예란은 『世界女流作家名作選』특집으로 하여, 女流創作界의 가장 활발한 신예인 丁玲, 팔.뻑, 케이스 諸氏의 특이한 창작을 실렸으며, 평론 역시 『戰爭文學과 民族文學』이란 題밑에 名논문을 게재한 것은 讀者 諸彦의 기대에 어긋나지 않으리라고 자신하나이다. 그 외, 『王仁博士偉績』 『金玉均의 생각』, 內地人이 본 『朝鮮文學과 美術工藝에 對한 對談會』 등도 얻기 어려운 기사일 것이외다.＜250＞
