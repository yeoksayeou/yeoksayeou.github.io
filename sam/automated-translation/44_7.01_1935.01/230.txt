Scenic Spots for Mountain and River Excursions

Don't just lie huddled in a heated room because it's cold; brave the cold snow, just like the German people (獨逸 國民), we too should shoulder our packs and set out on mountain and river treks to cultivate a grand spirit and robust physique. Here are some suitable places.

Vicinity of Suwŏn= A place of purple mountains and clear waters (山紫水明), renowned for the beauty of its waters and curious rocks (泉石). The Hwasŏng Fortress site from the time of King Chŏngjo of the Yi Dynasty, with Sŏjangdae, Yŏnmudae, Hwahongmun, Chang'anmun, etc., are worth seeing once, and especially as works of Yi Dynasty art, they are worth seeing three times over.

Pusosan= Located in Ch'ungnam Province. It is the site of the royal palace of Sabi Castle (泗泚城), the last capital of Paekche, and faces the Paengma River, a lower tributary of the Kŭm River. On the opposite bank are deep mountains, and crossing the river leads to Taejaegak Pavilion, offering extremely beautiful scenery (風致). Not only are the mountains bright and waters beautiful (山明水麗), but the day our descendants step upon this land, they will surely be filled with deep emotions.

Paegyangsa= Located in Chŏllanam-do Sagari. It is the head temple (總本山) of the Imje Order (臨濟宗), founded 1,300 years ago by the Koryŏ monk Yohwan Sŏnsa (如幻禪師). More than ten large and small halls (堂宇) are situated in deep mountain valleys, creating outstanding scenery in all four seasons. It is not only an old temple (古刹) but also a famous temple (名刹) according to Buddhist tradition.

Muyŏl Wangnŭng= Located in Kyŏngju. Beneath the peak of Sŏak Mountain, it is the grandest among the countless ancient royal tombs, where the 29th King of Silla, Taejong Muyŏl, rests eternally. An elegant stone structure, the tombstone stele has its inscription carved amidst the intertwined outlines (惕結한 輪廓) of six dragons; the body of the stele (碑身) rests upon a large stone turtle.

Pulguksa= Located in T'oham Mountain southeast of Kyŏngju, the ancient capital of Silla, it is a representative example of our ancient architecture. Founded in 539 AD. The temple complex (伽藍) maximizes external beauty. In the front are the two bridges, Ch'ŏngun (Blue Cloud) and Paegun (White Cloud), near where they lead to Chahamun Gate (紫霞門).

Haeju= A place of beautiful scenery nestled between Suyangsan Mountain to the north and Namsan Mountain to the south. Many famous spots (名所) include: Suyangsanjin Fortress North Gate, Kwangjosa Temple, Nine-story Pagoda, Ch'ŏngsŏngmyo Shrine, Yongch'a, Puyongdang Pavilion, Kujai Yusŏngbi Stele, Sin'gwangsa Temple, Munhŏn, Sohyŏn's Ch'ŏngyang, etc. <121>

**Summary**

This 1935 article encourages Koreans to emulate the "German people" by undertaking physically demanding excursions to scenic and historical sites across Korea, rather than staying indoors during winter. It lists several notable locations: Suwŏn (Hwasŏng Fortress), Pusosan (Paekche capital site), Paegyangsa Temple, Muyŏl Wangnŭng (Silla tomb in Kyŏngju), Pulguksa Temple (Kyŏngju), and Haeju, highlighting their natural beauty and historical significance (Yi Dynasty, Paekche, Silla). The article emphasizes appreciating Korea's heritage and fostering a strong spirit and body through travel. From a historical perspective, the article is interesting for its promotion of Korean historical sites during the Japanese colonial period, potentially reflecting a form of cultural nationalism or a managed tourism initiative. The explicit comparison to the "German people" for physical robustness might reflect contemporary global trends or Japanese militarist influences on physical culture prevalent at the time.

山川遊의 勝地
춥다고 온돌방 속에 업듸어 잇지 말고 寒雪을 무릅쓰고, 마치 獨逸 國民가치 우리네도. 배이하여 질머지고 山川跋涉으로 나서서 웅대한 정신과 강건한 육체를 단련하여 가저야 하겟다. 이제 그 적당한 곳으로.
水原附近= 山紫水明의 地로, 물과 泉石의 美觀으로 저명하다. 李朝正朝 때의 華城址에 西將臺, 練武臺, 華虹門, 長安門 등은 한 번 볼 만하며 특히 李朝예술로써 세 번 되도라 볼 것들이다.
扶蘇山= 忠南에 잇다. 百濟 최후의 國都이든 泗泚城의 王宮址로서 錦江의 하류인 白馬江에 臨하여 잇다. 對岸에는 深山이 잇고 강을 건너 大哉閣이 잇서 風致 심히 佳麗하며 山明水麗 할 뿐더러 우리들 후예가 한 번 이 땅을 밟어 브는 날 감개를 밧음이 만흘 것이다.
白羊寺= 全南四街里에 잇다. 臨濟宗의 總本山으로 천 삼백년 전 高麗僧如幻禪師의 창건이다. 大小十餘의 堂宇가 심산계곡 속에 잇서  四時絶景을 이룬다. 고찰일 뿐더러 불가에서 이르는 명찰이다.
武烈王陵=慶州에 잇다. 西岳의 산봉오리 밋헤 수 만흔 녯 왕릉 중에도 가장 장대한 것으로 新羅 29世 武烈大王이 영원히 잠드신 곳이다. 우아한 석조물로서 능비는 六龍의 惕結한 輪廓 사이에 비명을 삭엇는데 碑身은 크다란 돌거북 우에 잇다.
佛國寺= 新羅 古都 慶州의 東南 吐含山 중에 잇는 우리의 고건축의 대표물이다. 서기 539년의 창건이다. 가람은 외관의 미를 極하엿다. 정면에는 靑雲 白雲의 두 다리가 잇서 紫霞門에 통하는 부근에 잇다.
海州= 북으로 首陽山, 남으로 南山을 낀 풍광 아름다운 곳, 首陽山 城北, 廣照寺, 九重塔, 淸聖廟, 永車, 芙蓉堂, 九齋遺城碑, 神光寺, 文憲, 紹賢의 靑陽 등 명소 多.＜121＞
