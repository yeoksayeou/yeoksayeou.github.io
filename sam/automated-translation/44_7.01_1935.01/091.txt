Magazine Title: 三千里 제7권 제1호
Publication Date: 1935년 01월 01일
Article Title: 支社募集 (Branch Office Recruitment)
Author: None Specified
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Announcement/Editorial Postscript)

We are recruiting branch offices in every region (地方) where a Samch'ŏlli (三千里) branch office has not yet been established (設置되지 안은). Please request the regulations (規程), which have simple procedures (手續 簡單) and offer benefits (利益 잇는), review them, and become a branch office. <75>

**Summary**

This short piece is an advertisement by the *Samch'ŏlli* magazine soliciting applications for new branch offices in regions without existing ones. It highlights the simplicity of the process and the potential profitability for those who sign up. For historians, this notice provides insight into the business expansion strategies of a prominent Korean magazine during the colonial era, indicating its ambition to widen its distribution network across the peninsula. It reflects the commercial aspects of publishing under Japanese rule.
