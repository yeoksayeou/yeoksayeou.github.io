Hot Spring Guide

Paekch'ŏn Hot Spring (白川溫泉)= As the closest hot spring to Sŏul, it is most suitable for a weekend trip, going on Saturday and returning on Sunday. The third-class round-trip fare is 1 Wŏn 83 Chŏn. Take the Kyŏngŭi Line and transfer at T'osŏng Station; it takes about two hours. Lodging fees at "Ch'ŏnilgak" (天一閣) start from 3 Wŏn, and at Paekch'ŏn Hot Spring [Inn? likely referring to a specific inn named after the spring], from 4 Wŏn 50 Chŏn. The Pando Inn (半島旅館) and Chosŏn Inn (朝鮮旅館) generally charge 1 Wŏn to 1 Wŏn 50 Chŏn per night.<120>

Onyang Hot Spring (溫陽溫泉)= Take the Kyŏngbu Railway to Ch'ŏnan, transfer to the Kyŏngnam Railway, and it goes directly to the station. The water quality (溫質) is good (良好). The Hot Spring Hotel costs 4 Wŏn per night, and the Chosŏn Inn starts from 1 Wŏn. The round trip from Kyŏngsŏng (Seoul) is 2 Wŏn 78 Chŏn with a 30% discount on weekdays, and 1 Wŏn 98 Chŏn on weekends.

Tongnae Hot Spring (東萊溫泉)= About 40 ri from Pusan, there is a railway. Hotels start from 3 Wŏn 50 Chŏn, Chosŏn Inns offer meals from 1 Wŏn 50 Chŏn, and entertainment is available. The spring quality (泉質) is Simple Saline Spring (單純含監泉), equipped with indoor baths (內湯), etc., making it the best in all of Korea (全鮮 第一).

Haeundae Hot Spring (海雲臺溫泉)= A place with beautiful coastal scenery (風景佳麗), famous from the travelogue of the writer Ch'unwŏn. 40 ri from Pusan, 20 ri from Tongnae. Contains a high alkaline (아루까리性 多分含) content. Also renowned as a seaside bathing spot in summer. Inn expenses are more or less the same as others (他와 대동소이).

Chuŭl Hot Spring (朱乙溫泉)= 30 ri from Chuŭl Station on the Hambuk Line, reachable by car. Located by a mountain stream (山間溪流邊), the scenery is exquisite (風景佳絶). There are many villas (別莊) belonging to Russians (露國人). Sŏnsŏn'gak (鮮仙閣), Ch'ŏnse'gwan (千歲館), etc., start from 3 Wŏn. The Hot Spring Inn also has indoor bath facilities (內湯設備), costing 1 Wŏn 50 Chŏn per night. The water outflow (湧出量) is abundant (豊當).

Yonggang Hot Spring (龍岡溫泉)= 70 ri north from Chinnamp'o, 50 ri west from Chinjidong. A historic hot spring discovered 300 years ago.

Sinch'ŏn Hot Spring (信川溫泉)= Get off at Choch'ŏl (Chosŏn Railway) Sinch'ŏn Station. Especially effective (特效) for skin diseases (皮膚病) and nerve ailments (신경병). Abundant water outflow. Inn costs are roughly the same.

Onjŏngni Hot Spring (溫井里溫泉)= Located within the world-famous scenic Kŭmgangsan (金剛山). There are sightseeing spots (探勝地) nearby. Car service connects from Wŏnsan and Changjŏn. The low temperature of the hot spring is a drawback. Besides these, there are also P'yŏngsan Hot Spring (平山溫泉) about 80 ri from Namch'ŏn on the Kyŏngŭi Line, Yusŏng Hot Spring (儒城溫泉) about 20 ri from T'aejŏn, Talch'ŏn Hot Spring (達泉溫泉) about 50 ri from Sinch'ŏn, Songhwa Hot Spring (松禾溫泉), and many others. <121>

**Summary**

This 1935 article provides a guide to various hot springs across Korea, detailing their location, travel instructions (train lines, transfers, distances in 'ri'), costs (fares, lodging), water quality, notable features (scenery, amenities like indoor baths, nearby attractions), and specific benefits (e.g., for skin or nerve ailments). It covers well-known spots like Paekch'ŏn, Onyang, Tongnae, Haeundae, and Chuŭl, as well as several others including Yonggang, Sinch'ŏn, Onjŏngni (in Kŭmgangsan), P'yŏngsan, Yusŏng, Talch'ŏn, and Songhwa. For historians, the article offers insights into the tourism and travel infrastructure of colonial Korea, including the railway network (Kyŏngŭi, Kyŏngbu, Kyŏngnam, Hambuk lines), travel costs and affordability, the perceived hierarchy of hot springs ("best in all of Korea"), and mentions of foreign presence (Russian villas near Chuŭl Hot Spring). The blend of Korean and Japanese (like 아루까리性) terms and the specific mention of Chosŏn Inns alongside hotels also reflect the period's cultural context.

溫泉場 案內
白川溫泉= 서울서 가장 갓가운 온천으로 토요일에 갓다가 일요일에 도라오는 주말여행에는 제일 적당하다. 차비 三等 왕복 1원 83전, 京義線 타고 土城서 가라타면 되는데 두 시간쯤 걸닌다. 「天一閣」 숙박료 3원부터, 白川온천은 4원 50전부터. 半島여관 及 朝鮮여관 등은 대개 1박에＜120＞ 1원내지 1원 50전.
溫陽溫泉= 京釜鐵道로 天安가서 京南鐵道 가라타고 바로 역까지 간다. 溫質良好, 溫泉호텔은 1박에 4원, 朝鮮여관은 1원부터, 京城 왕복에 평일은 3割引으로 2원 78전, 주말이면 1원 98전.
東萊溫泉= 釜山서 약 40리, 철도 잇다. 호텔은 3원 50전부터, 朝鮮여관 1원 50전 요리, 유흥도 된다. 泉質은 單純含監泉, 內湯 완비 등 全鮮 第一.
海雲臺溫泉= 문호 春園의 기행문으로 유명한 해변의 風景佳麗한 곳, 釜山서 40리, 東萊에서 20리, 아루까리性 多分含. 여름이면 해수욕장으로도 저명, 여관비는 他와 대동소이.
朱乙溫泉= 咸北線 朱乙驛에서 30리 자동차 有, 山間溪流邊에 잇서 風景佳絶, 露國人 별장 多, 鮮仙閣, 千歲館 등은 3원부터 有, 溫泉여관에도 內湯設備 有, 1박 1원 50전, 湧出量 豊當.
龍岡溫泉= 鎭南浦에서 북으로 70리 眞池洞에서 西便 50리, 3백년 전 발견된 유래 잇는 온천.
信川溫泉= 朝鐵 信川驛서 하차, 피부병, 신경병 특효, 湧出量 多, 여관비 대략 同.
溫井里溫泉= 천하명승 金剛山 내에 在, 부근에 探勝地 有, 元山과 長箭서부터 자동차 연락, 溫泉 저온도가 흠. 이 밧게도 京義線 南川서 80리 許에 平山溫泉,  太田 20리 許에 儒城溫泉, 信川서 50리 許에 達泉온천, 松禾온천 등 多有하다. ＜121＞
