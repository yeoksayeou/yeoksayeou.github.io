The Imperial Nation and My State of Mind
						Yŏnhŭi Professional School Professor Yu Ok-kyŏm

International relations are now plunged into a state of chaos, writhing ceaselessly, sometimes in fierce armed conflict, sometimes in severe economic warfare, sometimes in sinister ideological battles. The situation is truly one of rapid, precipitous change, an emergency period where one cannot even turn one's head. Under this situation, I believe it is the greatest joy and pride of a subject of the Imperial Nation (皇國臣民) to know the great principle of loyalty and filial piety as one foundation (忠孝一本) within the eternally boundless community, to dedicate one's full wisdom and strength to the realization of the nation-founding ideal (肇國理想), the execution of national policy (國是), and the establishment of a highly advanced national defense state system, practicing self-annihilation for the public good (滅私奉公).

In such an emergency period, if there are those caught up in narrow-minded nationalist thought (民族思想) or other subversive ideas (不穩思想) who misunderstand the great national enterprise, it is absolutely unforgivable. I too was once caught up in the narrow-minded concept of national self-determination (民族自決). However, this was entirely because I failed to recognize the cultural mission of both the Japanese and Korean peoples (內鮮兩族).

Observing the changes in internal and external affairs since the Manchurian Incident (滿洲事變) in September of Shōwa 6 (1931), I came to recognize the errors of my past principles and assertions and gained conviction as a subject of the Imperial Nation. Thus, the current international situation we witness shows that the self-determination of one nation no longer protects the happiness and freedom of that nation; rather, it signifies isolation and weakness.

I want to tell the people of the Peninsula<174> that they must liquidate the delusion (迷妄) of national self-determination, while being convinced that the interests, welfare, joys, and sorrows (利害休戚) of our Peninsular populace are entirely aligned with the Imperial Nation (皇國) as a whole, and that the future of the advancing Imperial Japan (躍進皇國日本) certainly does not exclude the Peninsular populace.

I cry out that whether one is Japanese (內地人) or Peninsular (半島人), as children (赤子) of His Majesty (陛下), we must respectfully embody the Imperial will (聖旨), become perfectly unified (渾然一體), and strive forward (邁進) to assist in the completion (翼成) of the great Imperial enterprise.

We must firmly bear in mind that one of the duties imposed upon our Peninsula's 23 million people is the Naesŏn Ilch'e Movement (內鮮一體運動 Japan-Korea Unity Movement). I know this movement, considered from the perspective of the eternal grand plan (大計) of the Imperial Nation and the complete accomplishment (完遂) of the sacred task (聖業) of establishing a new order (新秩序) in East Asia and the world, is above all a vital national imperative.

Governor-General Minami (南總督) once stated, "The core of the construction of the Orient by Orientals lies in the perfection of Naesŏn Ilch'e (內鮮一體), and the foundation of Naesŏn Ilch'e lies in fostering the substance of loyal and good subjects of the Imperial Nation (忠良한 皇國臣民)." This clearly shows where the purpose of Naesŏn Ilch'e exists, and I feel we must embody that spirit in our daily lives.

Furthermore, this movement is by no means for temporary convenience; it is a national movement considering the eternal prosperity of the Imperial Nation, the realization (實現) of the nation-founding ideal, and the happiness of the Peninsular populace. Therefore, I believe that whether in the home, in society, or in the workplace (職域), each of us must exert our total effort towards the strengthening of this movement and the embodiment (具現) of Naesŏn Ilch'e. In other words, by firmly recognizing that embodying the mission of Naesŏn Ilch'e is the only true path for the Peninsular populace, we should carry out the tasks bestowed (賦與) upon us with the pride (矜持) and aspiration (抱負) of great citizens building a strong Imperial Japan. Accordingly, in whatever sphere of duty one occupies, one must strive to practice the way of the subject (臣道) with this conviction and resolve. I too, though unworthy (不肖), vow (誓) to dedicate myself wholeheartedly to public service (專心奉公) and repay even one ten-thousandth of the state's benevolence (國恩).<175>

**Summary**

Yu Ok-kyŏm, a professor at Yŏnhŭi Professional School, argues that in the current chaotic global situation, the concept of Korean national self-determination is an outdated and dangerous delusion. He states that he himself once held such views but now recognizes that the true path for Koreans lies in becoming loyal subjects of the Japanese Empire (`皇國臣民`). He emphasizes the importance of the `Naesŏn Ilch'e` (Japan-Korea Unity) movement as the core mission for Koreans, essential for the prosperity of both the Empire and the peninsula, and for establishing a new order in East Asia. He calls on all Koreans to embrace this identity, practice self-sacrifice (`滅私奉公`), and contribute wholeheartedly to the Imperial cause in all aspects of life. For historians, this article is a significant example of pro-Japanese collaborationist discourse by a prominent Korean intellectual during the height of wartime mobilization and assimilation policies, illustrating the ideological pressures and arguments used to justify abandoning Korean nationalism in favor of imperial subjecthood.

皇國과 余의 心境
						延禧專門學校 敎授 兪億兼
이제 國際關係는 混沌狀態에 빠저, 혹은 猛烈한 武力戰으로, 혹은 深刻한 經濟戰으로, 혹은 陰險한 思想戰으로, 停止할 줄 모르고, 꿈틀거리고 있다. 그 情勢 實로 急轉直下로, 머리를, 돌릴 수 없는 非常時局이다. 이 時局下에 天壤無窮의 團體로 한 中考一本의 大義를 알어, 肇國理想의 具現에, 國是의 遂行에 高度國防國家體制整備에, 全智全力을 다하여, 滅私奉公하는 것은, 皇國臣民의 最上의 喜悅인 同時에, 자랑이라고 나는 믿는다.
이러한 非常時局에 있어서 偏狹한 民族思想 其他의 不穩思想에 잡펴서 國家大業을 誤解하는 이 있다면 단연 容恕할 수 없는 일이다. 나도 일즉이, 偏狹한 民族自決의 觀念에 잡핀 일이 있었다. 그러나 이것은 전혀 內鮮兩族의 文化的 使命을 認識하지 못했기 때문이었다.
昭和 6年 9月 滿洲事變以來, 內外情勢의 變遷을 살펴서 從來의 主義主張의 誤謬를 認定하고 皇國臣民으로서의 信念을 가지게 되었다. 그리하야 一民族의 自決이란 것은 벌써, 當該民族의 幸福과 自由를 保護하는 것이 아니고 되려, 獨立과 微弱을 意味하는 것은 우리들이 현재 目擊하는 바의 國際情勢다.
我半島民衆의 利害休戚은 전혀, 皇國全體에, 合致하여 躍進皇國日本의 前途도, 역시, 半島民衆을 결코 排除하는 것이 아니라는 것을 確信하는 同時에, 民族自決의 迷妄을 淸算하지 않으면 안되는 것을 半島民衆에게＜174＞ 告하고 싶다.
나는 內地人이나, 半島人이나, 陛下의 赤子로서, 聖旨를 奉體하여, 渾然一體가 되고, 皇國大業의 翼成에 邁進하지 않으면 안된다고 부르짖는다.
我半島 2,300萬民衆에게 課한 임무의 하나로서, 內鮮一體運動이 있는 것을 銘記하지 않으면, 안된다. 이 運動은 皇國永遠한 大計의 견지로부터, 東亞와 世界 新秩序樹立의 聖業完遂의 견지로부터, 고찰해서, 무엇보다도 긴요한 國家要請인 줄 안다.
일즉이 南總督이, 「東洋人의 東洋建設의 핵심은 內鮮一體의 完璧이고, 內鮮一體의 根底는 忠良한 皇國臣民인 實質을 양성하는 데 있다」라고 述한 것은 內鮮一體의 목적이 奈邊에 존재한 것을 명시한 것으로 우리들 일상생활에 그 정신을 구현하지 않으면 안된다고 느낀다.
그런데 이 운동은 결코 一時的 便益을 위한 것이 아니고, 皇國永遠의 繁榮肇國理想의 實現, 半島民衆의 행복을 생각한 國家的 運動이다. 그런 고로, 혹은 가정에 혹은 사회에 혹은 職域에 있어서, 우리들 각자가 이 운동의 강화, 內鮮一體의 具現에 총노력을 하지 않으면 안되리라 믿는다. 다시 말하면 內鮮一體의 使命을 구현하는 것이, 半島民衆의 유일한 진로라는 것을 굳게 인식함으로서, 굳센 皇國日本建設의 大國民的 矜持와 抱負아래에, 賦與된 任務를 遂行할 것이다. 따라서 어떠한 職域에 있어서든지, 이 신념과 각오로서 臣道의 實踐에 노력하지 않으면 안된다. 나도 또한 不肖하나마, 專心奉公 國恩의 만일이라도 報할 것은 誓한다.＜175＞
