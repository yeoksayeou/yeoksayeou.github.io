Editor's Postscript

Under the increasingly urgent current situation, in order to have the Korean populace (朝鮮民衆) exert double their previous total effort (總力) and to continue strengthening the establishment of the All People Assistance System (萬民翼贊體制), we have this time published writings under the title "A Letter to the Korean Populace" from figures beginning with the Government-General's (總督府側) Korean high officials (朝鮮人高等官), the Kyŏnggi Province Industrial Director (京畿道産業部長), and gentlemen secretaries of the Central Council (中樞院書記官諸氏). We believe these are truly the most appropriate writings for the lives of the people under this war situation (戰局下), and we hope for the earnest reading of the peninsula's populace.

Furthermore, for the promotion of local folk art (鄕土藝術振興) and the revival of local folk entertainment (鄕土娛樂復活), the treatises requested from over ten of our folklorists (民俗學者) and authorities on folk crafts (民藝) are confidently presented as truly valuable documents for the improvement and development of Korean local folk culture (朝鮮鄕土文化).

Having received overwhelming acclaim from the capital and countryside for the "Special Feature on Korean Theater Culture (朝鮮演劇文化特輯)" published in the previous issue, even achieving the honor of selling out, our company has again prepared a "Special Feature on Korean Film Culture (朝鮮映畵文化特輯)" in this month's issue. We aim to promote the improvement of Korean film culture under the New System (新體制下), while simultaneously providing content that does not disappoint the expectations of our esteemed readers.

In literature and art as well, decorating the spring season (陽春) is the "Southern Folk Poetry Collection (南方鄕土詩集)"; furthermore, the powerful work (力作) "Chigimi (지기미)" by the emerging writer Mr. Kim Sa-ryang (金史良氏), who is energetically making his mark on the Tokyo literary scene (東京文壇); and "Ch'ŏnmaek (天脈)," which can be called the author's greatest masterpiece (最高傑作) as its concluding chapter (終結篇), are presented as the pride of the spring literary world.＜300＞ ＜300＞

**Summary**

This editor's postscript from the April 1941 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine highlights its recent content. It emphasizes articles promoting cooperation with the Japanese colonial authorities and the war effort, featuring contributions from high-ranking Korean officials. It also mentions special features on Korean folk arts/entertainment and Korean film culture, intended to align with the "New System." Lastly, it promotes literary works, including poetry and fiction by Kim Sa-ryang. For historians, this postscript is revealing as it explicitly states the magazine's role in mobilizing Koreans for Japan's war effort under the "All People Assistance System." It showcases how cultural content (folk art, film, literature) was framed within and co-opted by the colonial political agenda during a period of heightened wartime mobilization.

編輯後記
더욱 緊迫해 가는 時局下에 朝鮮民衆으로 하여금 倍前의 總力을 發揮케 하여써 萬民翼贊體制의 確立을 繼續强化시키기 위해서 今番 「朝鮮民衆에게 告하는 書」라는 題下에 總督府側 朝鮮人高等官을 위시하여 京畿道産業部長, 中樞院書記官諸氏의 글을 실린 것은 실로 이 戰局下에 國民의 生活을 위해서 가장 적절한 글이라 믿는 바이며 半島民衆의 熱讀을 바라는 바입니다.

그리고 鄕土藝術振興과 鄕土娛樂復活을 위해서 우리 民俗學者와 民藝의 權威 십수명으로부터 청한 論文은 朝鮮鄕土文化의 향상과 발전을 위해 실로 귀중한 문헌이라 자신하나이다.

前號에 발표한 「朝鮮演劇文化特輯」으로 京鄕의 절대한 激讚을 博하여 絶版의 光榮까지 입은 弊社에서는 다시 今月號에 「朝鮮映畵文化特輯」을 꾸며서 新體制下의 朝鮮映畵文化의 향상을 꾀하는 동시에 讀者諸彦의 企待에 어그러지지 않는 내용을 제공하는 바입니다.

文藝에 있어서도 陽春을 裝飾하는 「南方鄕土詩集」을 위시하여 東京文壇에서 활발한 氣焰을 吐하고 있는 新進作家 金史良氏의 力作 「지기미」와 終結篇으로써 作者의 最高傑作이라고 할 수 있는 「天脈」은 陽春 文壇의 자랑으로 삼는 바입니다.＜300＞ ＜300＞
