Article Title	奉天軍官學校 (Fengtian Military Academy)
Author	
Article Type	소식 (News/Report)

What is the full picture of the Fengtian Military Academy, which nurtures the strong defenders of the nation, now that Manchukuo (滿洲國) is 10 years old?

The Military Academy, in other words, is Japan's (日本) Officer Academy.
After spending 0 years and 0 months in this educational life, they are already promising young men who will shoulder the future of the military state as fine officers of the Manchukuo Army.

A mere 30-minute drive by car from Fengtian (奉天) Station brings one before the gates of the Army Military Academy, which occupies a scenic spot by XX Lakeside (00湖畔).

In the very center of the wide barracks yard, piercing the winter sky, flutters the Five-Colored Flag. On both sides of the yard stand rows upon rows of barracks buildings. Behind them, on the hill, the fierce north wind (朔風) seems to blow, making the pines cry out. The outskirts of Fengtian City (奉天市), an industrial city with a population over 1 million, were hazy and faintly white.

This school has the School Headquarters, the Education Department, the XX Department (XX部), and also something called the 00 Unit (00隊). Under the Education Department, Officer Cadets, Second Lieutenant Candidates, and Military Academy Students are receiving rigorous training, forged like hot iron, according to their various military branches.

One afternoon, guided by a Japanese-system officer (日系將校), I visited this Military Academy. Upon receiving the rifle (*銃) check at the gate and entering the barracks yard, just then, a unit of Second Lieutenant Candidates was heading out for field exercises, their military boots sounding loudly.

At the command of the leading officer (引卒將校)—wasn't that Japanese?—the young men passed by in dignified, orderly ranks.
With intense spirit (身魂一微?), fierce *kiai* shouts (기합) and the sounds of bamboo swords (竹刀) were heard together. The guiding officer laughed, saying, "There are some quite decent fellows among the Manchurian-system (滿系) personnel too."

Peeking into Lecture Hall No. 0 (第0講堂), cavalry branch students were listening intently to a lecture. Not a single person turned to look back at our group; they were that focused.
On the front wall of the lecture hall, the following were posted in the Principal's name:
One, Strictly observe time (嚴守時間)
One, Maintain neat appearance/bearing (嚴整儀容)
One, Listen quietly to instruction (靜聽敎授)
One, Strictly prohibit smoking (嚴禁吸煙)
One, Strictly prohibit spitting (嚴禁吐啖)

Exiting the school gate, virgin snow (處女雪) lay scattered here and there on the red earth (赤土) of the heavily undulating meadows. On the nearby XX Heights (XX高地), artillery branch students were practically studying the operation of new weapons. Looking down from this height, students from all branches, without exception, were diligently engaged in various exercises. From 0 Ponmokki Heights (0本木高地), the sound of gunfire from a light machine gun squad—*tat-tak, ta-dak, ttok, ttak*—rose with vigor (氣勢).

Kicking the frozen (얼붚는) ground, their spirit (意氣) soared to the heavens. Japanese-system (日系) and Manchurian-system (滿系) personnel, mixed together, regardless of the flesh-biting cold wind (寒風), are honing their skills diligently through tactical exercises and field exercises (實地演習)—a trustworthy attitude. The future path of these future generals (將星), charging forward to achieve the great mission (大使命) of Japan-Manchukuo Joint Defense (日滿共同防衛), shines brightly. -End-<187>

**Summary**

This article reports on a visit to the Fengtian Military Academy in Manchukuo in 1941. It describes the academy's facilities, the rigorous training cadets undergo (including field exercises, lectures, and martial arts), and the strict discipline enforced. The author emphasizes the dedication of both Japanese-system and Manchurian-system personnel training together, highlighting their role in the "Japan-Manchukuo Joint Defense" and portraying their future as bright. From a historical perspective, the article is significant as propaganda showcasing the Manchukuo military's development under Japanese guidance, reinforcing the narrative of cooperation and shared destiny between Japan and its puppet state. Historians might find the description of the training environment, the explicit mention of Japanese officers and language use, the rules emphasizing discipline, and the overarching theme of joint defense useful for understanding the nature of Japanese control, the construction of the Manchukuoan military identity, and the way such institutions were presented to a Korean audience during the colonial period.

기사제목	奉天軍官學校
기사형태	소식

奉天軍官學校
滿洲國建國이 10年이 되는데 護國의 壯士를 養하는 奉天軍官學校의 전모는 어떠한가?

軍官學校, 말하자면, 日本의 士官學校다.
이 敎育生活을 0년 0개월을 보내고 맞으면 벌서, 훌융한 滿州國軍의 將校로서, 軍國의 장래를, 억개에 질머질 囑望이 두터운 젊은 사람들이다.

奉天驛에서, 겨우 자동차로 30분만 달리면 00湖畔의 景勝地를 점령한 陸軍軍官學校 門前에 선다.

넓은 營庭 맨 가운데, 겨을 하늘을 찌를 듯, 퍼득이는 五色旗, 營庭兩側에 疊疊히 느레선 營舍, 그 배후, 언덕에는 사나운 朔風이 불어 소나무 우는 소리가 들리는 듯하다. 人口 100萬餘, 工業都市 奉天市의 外廓이 뿌-연이, 히게 흐려 있었다.

本校에는 學校本部, 敎育部, XX部 거기 또 00隊라는 것이 있다. 敎育部아래, 軍官候補生, 少尉候補生, 軍官學生이, 각종의 兵科別로 熱鐵鍜鍊敎育을 받고 있는 것이다.

어느 오후, 日系將校의 안내로, 이 軍官學校를 찾었다. 교문에서 「*銃」을 받으며 營庭에 들어선즉, 마침, 軍靴소리 높으게, 少尉候補生의 一隊가 野外演習을 나가는 길이였다.

引卒將校의 號令-에? 日本語가 아닌가. - 으로 젊은이들은 威儀正列히 앞을 지나갔다.
身魂一微, 激烈한 기합과 竹刀소리 합처서 들려왔다. 안내하는 將校가 「滿系에두 꽤 괜찮은 者가 있어요」하고 웃었다.

第0講堂을 디레다 본 즉, 바루 騎兵科學生이 證義를 듣고 있다. 우리 일행을, 한사람 뒤도라다 보는 일 없이 열심들했다.
講堂正面에는
一, 嚴守時間
一, 嚴整儀容
一, 靜聽敎授
一, 嚴禁吸煙
一, 嚴禁吐啖
이리해서 校長名으로 고쳤다.

校門을 나오니 赤土의 굴곡이 심한 초원에, 處女雪이 군데군데 쌓여있다. 멀지않은 XX高地에서는 砲兵科學生이 新兵器의 操縱法을 실지로 연구하고, 이 고지에서 내려다 보니까, 各 科學生이 어느거 할 것 없이, 諸種의 연습을 열심히 하고 있었다. 0本木高地에서 輕機分隊의 총성이 타딱 타닥, 똑, 딱, 氣勢를 올렀다.

얼붚는 대지를 차며 그 意氣는 하늘에, 오를 정도다. 日系 滿系 서로 석기여 살을 어이는 寒風을 가리지 않고, 作戰演習, 實地演習에, 孜孜한 연마를 싸코있는 믿엄즉한 態度, 日滿共同防衛의 大使命 達成에, 突進하는 미래의 將星들의 전도는 빛난다. -끝-＜187＞
