Cultural Organizations of Central China
						Shanghai Scholar (上海學人)

Sino-Japanese Cultural Association (Nanjing)
Although the Nanjing Government itself calls this a reconstruction of an old organization, it is more accurate to say it is an entirely new organization. Its grand inaugural ceremony was held in Nanjing on July 28th last year. The promoters are prominent figures such as Wang Ching-wei (汪精衛), Chu Min-i (諸民誼), Lin Po-sheng (林柏生), Fu Shih-shuo (傳式說), Chao Cheng-p'ing (趙正平), and Chu Ch'ing-lai (諸靑來). The association presidents are former Ambassador Abe on the Japanese side and Chairman Wang Ching-wei on the Chinese side; it is literally a general mobilization of prominent Japanese and Chinese figures. That alone gives it a strong bureaucratic air. Its purpose is described as achieving the goal of good neighborliness and friendship based on the three great principles of Sino-Japanese cooperation, good neighborliness and friendship, and joint defense against communism, through the mutual understanding of Japanese and Chinese culture, the reconciliation of public and private figures from both sides, and the promotion of East Asian civilization.

Chūka Den'ei Kōshi (China Film Company) (Shanghai)
The planner of this company is Mr. Yūki Reiichirō, a civilian attached to the military (軍囑託). It was established through the efforts of Mr. Negishi Kan'ichi and others based on Mr. Yūki's plan, and it is the only Japanese film production studio in Central China (中支). It was established in July of Shōwa 14 (1939) with a capital of 1 million yuan. The Managing Director (專務取締役) is Mr. Kawakita Nagamasa, who is the head of the famous Kawakita Distribution Center. Recently, it completed a cultural film production studio and has vigorously begun production.

Wuhan Youth Association (Hankou)
This is a cultural organization established under the guidance of the Army Information Department immediately after the occupation of Hankou. Currently, the leaders are Mr. Kishi Tomizō, Mr. Yoshida Kichisaburō, and others. Besides publishing the magazine "Bungei" (Literature), it also publishes a Youth Book Series (靑年叢書).

China Women's Advancement Association (Pudong)
This is an edification group (敎化團體) primarily for women, managed independently by Yang Chia-hsiang (楊嘉香) and Ms. Yamagishi Takako under the protection of the Navy. Besides operating a workshop (授産所) in Pudong, it runs a Japanese language school called "Chia-hui Japanese Language School" (嘉惠日語學校) on Nanjing Road in the British Concession. Recently, they brought products made at the Pudong workshop to Japan, held a bazaar, and have been actively engaged, bringing several Chinese women to hold round-table discussions and lectures in Tokyo, Osaka, and other various places. <145>

Tairiku Shinpō (New Shen Bao)
Established in Shanghai under the guidance of the Army Information Department. Mr. Fuke Shun'ichi assumed the position of President. The *Xin Shen Bao* (新申報) can be considered its Chinese language version (中語版). It receives subsidies from the Kōain (興亞院 - East Asia Development Board).

Yōsukō Sha (Yangtze River Company) (Shanghai)
This is a publishing company established to publish the general interest magazine "Yōsukō" (Yangtze River). The president is Mr. Sakana Shinzō. Since December of Shōwa 13 (1938), it has been publishing the magazine and has also published monographs such as "Shanghai Gekisen Tōkakan" (Shanghai Fierce Battle 10 Days), "Senjō Shishū Isho" (Battlefield Poetry Collection - Last Testament), and "Tōa no Kaihō" (Liberation of East Asia). It receives subsidies from the authorities.

Suzhou Literary Society (Suzhou)
Established last summer, around the same time as the Changjiang Literary Society in Shanghai, in Suzhou, which lacked the benefits of a cultural environment, through the enthusiasm and efforts of Mr. Kuroki Seiji and others. -End- <146>

**Summary**

This article from the April 1941 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine introduces several cultural organizations operating in Japanese-occupied Central China. Penned by "Shanghai Scholar," it details the establishment, leadership, purpose, and activities of groups like the Sino-Japanese Cultural Association in Nanjing (led by Wang Ching-wei and a Japanese ex-ambassador), the Chūka Den'ei Kōshi film company, the Wuhan Youth Association, the China Women's Advancement Association (involved in vocational training and cultural exchange), the Tairiku Shinpō newspaper, the Yōsukō Sha publishing house, and the Suzhou Literary Society. Many of these organizations were explicitly established under the guidance or protection of Japanese military (Army Information Department, Navy) or governmental bodies (Kōain) and received subsidies, aiming to promote Sino-Japanese cooperation, anti-communism, and Japanese cultural influence under the banner of "East Asian civilization." From the perspective of Korean colonial history, the article is interesting as it was published in a Korean magazine during Japanese rule, showcasing the expansion of Japanese cultural and political projects into occupied China, mirroring similar efforts in Korea. It reveals the network of collaborationist figures (like Wang Ching-wei) and Japanese entities involved in shaping the cultural landscape to align with Japanese imperial objectives, a process familiar within Korea itself, offering historians insights into the mechanisms and scope of Japanese wartime cultural policy across its empire and occupied territories.

中支文化의 諸團體
						上海學人
中日文化協會(南京)
이것은 南京政府 自身은 舊組織의 再健이라고 하지만 전연 新組織이라는 便이 正當하다. 작년 7월 28일 南京서 성대히 發會式이 거행듸였다. 發起人은 汪簀¾衛, 諸民誼, 林柏生, 傳式說, 趙正平 及 諸靑來 等 당당한 巨頭이다. 協會長은 日本側은 阿部前大使, 支那側은 汪精衛主席인데 문자 그대로 日支名士의 總動員이다. 그것만도 官僚的인 氣香이 높다. 其 目的은 日支提携 善隣友好 共同防共의 3대 原則에 基하여 中日 兩國文化의 疎通, 雙方朝野人士의 融和와 東洋文明의 發揚으로부터 善隣友好의 목적에 達하는 것이라고 설명하고 있다.

中華電影公司(上海)
이 會社의 立案者는 軍囑託 結城禮一郞氏이다. 同氏의 立案으로 根岸寬一氏 등의 힘으로 설립되었는데 中支 유일의 日本人의 映畵製作所이다. 설립은 昭和 14년 7월 자본금은 100萬원. 專務取締役은 川喜多長政氏인데 同氏는 유명한 川北配給所의 所長이다. 최근 文化映畵製作所를 완성하여 盛히 製作에 着手하였다.

武漢靑年協會(漢口)
漢口占領 直後에 陸軍報導部의 指導 下에 성립된 文化團體이다. 目下 主宰者는 岸富造氏 吉田吉三郞氏 등인데 雜誌 「文藝」를 발행하는 한편 靑年叢書도 출판하고 있다.

中國婦女協進會(浦東)
海軍의 保護 下에 楊嘉香과 山岸多嘉子女史의 獨力經營하고 있는 女性을＜145＞ 주로 하는 支那民衆의 敎化團體이다. 浦東에서 授産所를 경영하는 외 英祖界 南京路에서 「嘉惠日語學校」라는 日語敎習所를 열고 있다. 최근은 浦東授産所의 製作品을 가지고 日本에 와서 빠자-를 開催하고 또 支那女性 數名을 다리고 東京, 大阪 기타 각지에서 座談會, 講演會를 개최하는 등 盛히 活躍하고 있다.

大陸新報(新申報)
上海에 있어서 陸軍報道部의 指導下에 성립되었다. 社長은 福家俊一氏가 취임하였다. 新申報는 그의 中語版이라고 해도 좋다. 興亞院 방면에서 補助金이 지급되여 있다.

揚子江社(上海)
綜合雜誌 「揚子江」을 발행하기 위하여 성립된 出版社이다. 社長은 坂名深臟氏로 昭和 13년 12월 以來 잡지를 발행하며 또 「上海激戰10日間」 「戰場詩集遺書」 「東亞의 解放」 등 單行本을 출판하였다. 當局의 補助金이 나온다.

蘇州文學會(蘇州)
작년 여름 上海의 長江文學會와 전후하야 文化的 環境에 혜택을 입지 못한 蘇州에서 黑木淸次氏 等의 열심과 노력으로써 만드른 文學會이다. -끗- ＜146＞
