Patriotic Anecdotes
						Selected by the Editorial Office

Patriotic Youth with Burning Blood Petitions in Blood to Become a Volunteer Soldier
Applications for volunteer soldiers in the military have gained momentum this year, flooding in like the tide. On the 21st, a spirited young man appeared bravely at the Security Section of the Chongno Police Station, carrying a petition written in blood. This youth is Kyehun (桂薰), the eldest son of Mr. Matsubara Genshoku (松原元植) of 385 Puam-jŏng (付岩町) within the city. Aspiring to become a volunteer soldier this time, he wrote in his own blood:
"My life is not regrettable if it is for His Majesty the Emperor (天皇陛下)."
Separately, on a white piece of cloth, he also drew the 'Japanese flag' (일장기) in blood, laying bare his sincerity and deeply moving the concerned authorities.

Gold Watch Received as Commendation Prize, etc., Donated
(Yongin) Mr. Iwamoto Seiichi (岩本誠一) of Kŭmnyangjang-ni, Yongin-myŏn, Yongin-gun, donated a single 18-karat gold pocket watch, received as a commendation prize for 10 years of continuous service, along with 30 Won and 26 Chŏn in cash saved by putting aside the cost of one postcard daily for 1,756 days from April 21, Showa 11 (1936) to February 10, Showa 16 (1941). He entrusted these to the local Police Station (警察署) to be donated as National Defense Funds in commemoration of Kigen Day (紀元節), Year 2601 of the Imperial Era (皇紀).

Torrent of Sincerity: Over 80,000 Won in Weapons Donated in Chŏnnam
(Kwangju) The 2.5 million residents of Chŏnnam Province (全南), as citizens of the home front since the outbreak of the China Incident (支那事變), have continuously donated weapons to the army, such as anti-aircraft guns (高射砲), starting with the donation of five naval aircraft. Aiming for the construction of an advanced national defense state, funds for weapon donations were collected from various counties and islands. As of the end of last December, the total reached 84,436 Won, showing the sincerity (赤誠) of the people of Chŏnnam.

The Hot Blood of Patriotism Boils: Entire Student Body of Sinhŭng Civic School Volunteers as Soldiers
(Hamhŭng) Military-spirited youths<188>—at Sinhŭng Public Agricultural Civic School (新興公立農業公民學校), on the 18th, all students, led by An Ch'ŏnsŭngung (安川勝雄) and 38 others (not a single person missing from the first-grade roster), declared "We will become volunteer soldiers!" and applied to the school authorities. The school reportedly processed their volunteer applications immediately. It is said that this school might be the only one in all of Korea where the entire student body has applied jointly.

Wages of Five Won from Laboring in Severe Cold Donated
Sincerity of the Women's Association of Ch'igung-ni, Songjŏn-myŏn
(T'ongch'ŏn) The Women's Association (婦人會) of Ch'igung-ni (致弓里), Songjŏn-myŏn (松田面), T'ongch'ŏn-gun (通川郡), braving the extreme cold on the 14th and 15th, performed labor at a sardine factory. They brought the 5 Won earned as wages to the local Police Substation (駐在所) and entrusted it, saying although it's a small amount, please send it as a National Defense Donation, deeply moving everyone.

Gathering One Straw Mat Each Month, Donating the Proceeds as National Defense Funds
Sincerity of the Residents of Kumi-ri First District Village
(Kwangju) The members of the Patriotic Squad (愛國班) of the Village Total Mobilization League (部落 總力聯盟) in the First District of Kumi-ri (九美里), Nakseng-myŏn (樂生面), Kwangju-gun (廣州郡), thinking of the hardships of the Imperial Army (皇軍) officers and soldiers active on the front lines, gather one straw mat (가마니) for fertilizer use, which they had set aside during their spare time, every month on Patriotic Day (愛國日). They brought the proceeds from the joint sale, 27 Won and 50 Chŏn, via the Village League Director, Mr. Yu Sŏngnyŏl (柳成烈), to the Social Affairs Section (社會係) of the Nakseng-myŏn Office (樂生面事務所) on the 7th, asking for it to be used as a National Defense Donation despite being a small amount, deeply impressing the section staff.

Machine Gun Donation
Sincerity of the P'och'ŏn Sinŭp Patriotic Squad Members
(P'och'ŏn) In view of the current situation (時局), the activities of Patriotic Squad (愛國班) members everywhere are striving for the perfection of Home Front Defense (銃後守護) with a spirit of Selfless Service (滅私奉公). On Patriotic Day (愛國日), the 1st, the P'och'ŏn Sinŭp (抱川新邑) Patriotic Squad members, following a motion by Director Kim (金理事長), decided that each household in the squad would save 1 Chŏn daily to overcome the national crisis and purchase and donate weapons (a machine gun) helpful for the construction of the New Order.

Over Seven Thousand Comfort Bags Alone
Patriotic Fervor of Kangwŏn Province Residents Reaches High Tide
(Ch'unch'ŏn) Since the outbreak of the Incident (事變), the patriotic fervor in Kangwŏn Province (江原道) has reached its peak. The comfort goods and relief money/items (慰問恤兵金品) handled by the Provincial Military Support League (道軍事後援聯盟) over the past year are as follows, clearly reflecting the utmost sincerity (至誠) of the provincial residents' patriotism. Specifically, the comfort bags sent to the Imperial Army (皇軍) officers and soldiers fighting bravely and dedicating their lives on the front lines numbered 7,341, amounting to 22,498 Won and 72 Chŏn. Cash donations contributed as comfort funds, national defense funds, and soldier relief funds reached a total of 24,101 Won and 63 Chŏn.<189>

**Summary**

This article compiles several anecdotes from across Korea in early 1941, showcasing public displays of patriotism and support for the Japanese Empire during the Sino-Japanese War. These acts include young men petitioning in blood to join the army, individuals donating valuable items like gold watches and savings, communities raising funds for weapons (anti-aircraft guns, machine guns), students volunteering en masse, women donating meager wages from labor, and province-wide efforts collecting large sums of money and thousands of comfort bags for soldiers. The article emphasizes the "sincerity" (赤誠) and "patriotic fervor" (愛國熱) of Koreans supporting the war effort and the Emperor. For historians, this piece is interesting as it exemplifies the propaganda prevalent during the late colonial period, highlighting (and likely exaggerating) Korean loyalty and participation in Japan's war mobilization. It reflects the intense pressure and organized campaigns (like the Patriotic Squads) used to extract resources and demonstrations of allegiance from the colonized population, blurring the lines between voluntary support and coerced compliance under the banner of "patriotism" towards the Empire.

愛國의 熱血兒 志願兵되기를 血書로 嘆願
영에 있는 지원병의 지원은 금년에 드러 더욱 기세를 올려 조수같이 밀려드는 중인데 21일 마츰 종로서 경무게에 『혈서』를 가추은 지원 청년이 씩씩하게 낱아났다. 이 청년은 부내 부안정(付岩町) 385번지 송원원식(松原元植)씨의 장남 계훈(桂薰)군으로 이번에 지원병이 되려고 혈서로서
「천황폐하(天皇陛下)를 위하는 바이면 내 몸은 아까웁지 않도다」라고 쓰고 따로히 힌 헌겁에 또한 피로서 『일장기』를 만들어 가지고 적성을 그대로 내어놓은 것으로 관게당국을 감격케 하고있다.

褒彰賞으로 받은 金時計等獻納
(龍仁) 龍仁郡 龍仁面 金良場里 岩本誠一氏는 10년간 勤續褒彰賞으로 받은 18金 懷中時計 한개와 昭和 11년 4월 21일부터 同 16년 2월 10일까지 1,756일 간을 두고 매일 葉書 한장값씩 貯蓄한 현금 30원 26전을 皇紀 2,601년 紀元節을 기념하기 위하야 當地 警察署에 國防金으로 獻納하야 달나고 寄托하였다 한다.

赤誠의 奔流 全南에 兵器獻納 8萬餘圓
(光州) 전남 250만 도민들은 지나사변이 발생한 이래 총후의 국민으로써 해군비행기 5대 헌납한 것을 위시로 육군에 고사포(高射砲) 등의 병기를 헌납한 후 게속하여 고도국방국가건설을 목표로 병기헌납자금을 각부군도로부터 모흐든 바 지난 12월말 현재루 게를 보면 84,436원으로 전남도민의 적성을 보이고 있다.

殉國의 熱血은 뛴다 新興公民校 全校生이 志願兵自願
(咸興) 한 학교에서 생도 전부가 지원병을 지원하였다는＜188＞ 군국소년들-신흥공립농업공민학교(新興公立農業公民)에서는 지난 18일에 그 학교생도 안천승웅(安川勝雄) 외 38(1급제명에 한사람도 빠지지 않고 『지원병이 되겠읍니다』하고 학교당국에 지원하야 학교에서도 즉시 지원수속을 하였다고 하는데 전교생도가 련명으로 지원한 것은 조선전도에서 이 학교뿐일 것이라고 한다.

嚴寒中勤勞作業 품삭 五圓을 獻金
松田面 致弓里 婦人會의 赤誠
(通川) 通川郡 松田面 致弓里 婦人會에서는 지난 14, 15, 양일 酷寒을 무릅쓰고 鰮工場에서 勤勞作業을 하야 받은 풀삯 5圓을 當地駐在所로 가지고 와서 많지 않은 금액이나 國防獻金으로 보내달라고 寄托하여 일반을 감격케 하였다.

每月 가마 한입式 모아 代金을 國防獻金
九美里 第一區 部落民의 赤誠
(廣州) 廣州郡 樂生面 九美里 第一區 部落 總力聯盟 愛國班員 一同은 전선에서 활동하는 皇軍將兵들의 노고를 생각하고 每月 愛國日이면 틈틈에 처두었든 肥科用 가마니 1枚式을 모아 共販한 代金 27圓 50錢을 部落聯盟理事長 柳成烈씨를 통하야 지난 7일 樂生面事務所 社會係에 가지고 와서 적은 금액이나 國防獻金을 하여 달라고 하야 係員을 감복케 하였다 한다.

機關銃獻納
抱川新邑 愛國班員의 赤誠
(抱川) 現下時局에 鑑하야 各地 愛國班員들의 활동은 滅私奉公의 精神으로 銃後守護의 만전을 기하고 있는데 抱川新邑 愛國班員은 지난 1일 愛國日에 金理事長의 動議로 班員은 每戶마다 매일 1錢式 저금하야 國難을 타개하며 新秩序建設에 도음이 되는 兵器(機關銃)를 買入獻納하기로 되었다고 한다.

慰問袋만 七千餘個
江原道民의 愛國熱高潮
(春川) 事變勃發以來 江原道의 愛國熱은 고조에 達하야 昨年 1년동안에 道軍事後援聯盟에서 취급한 慰問恤兵金品은 如左해서 道民의 愛國의 至誠을 여실히 반영하고 있다 한다. 즉 제일선에서 身命을 밧치어 奮戰하고 있는 皇軍將兵에게 보내달라고 밧친 慰問袋가 7,341개로 금액으로는 22,498圓 72錢에 달하며 慰問, 國防, 恤兵金으로 보태 써 달라고 현금으로 헌납한 것이 실로 24,101圓 63錢에 달하였다는 것이다.＜189＞
