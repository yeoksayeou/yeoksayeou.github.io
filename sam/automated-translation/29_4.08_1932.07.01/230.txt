Publication Date: 1932년 07월 01일
Article Title: 편집후기 (Editorial Postscript)
Author: Unspecified (likely the editor)
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editorial Postscript)

From this company, we will launch "Samch'ŏlli Munhak" (三千里文學) starting in September. This is done purely in the spirit of service for the prosperity of Chosŏn literature, setting aside profit.

After a whirlwind (旋風) occurred at a certain (某某) newspaper company, the forum for discussion (論壇) has become desolate (蕭條), which makes one's heart heavy (압흡니다). As the saying goes, "When the pine flourishes, the cypress rejoices" (松茂栢悅), we should all prosper together.

Starting July 8th, in the spirit of a summer break (暑中休暇), we will publish once per month. <54>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript announces the upcoming launch of a new literary magazine, "Samch'ŏlli Munhak," by the publishers of "Samch'ŏlli," emphasizing its non-profit motive aimed at fostering Korean literature. The editor expresses concern over the state of public discourse following recent turmoil at an unnamed newspaper company, using an idiom to wish for mutual prosperity. Finally, it informs readers that the main magazine, "Samch'ŏlli," will switch to a monthly publication schedule temporarily for a summer break. For historians, this piece offers insight into the active literary scene and publishing efforts in colonial Korea, the awareness and veiled commentary on contemporary events (like potential censorship or conflict within the press indicated by the "whirlwind" and "desolate discourse"), and the operational adjustments magazines made.

편집후기
본사에서 「三千里文學」을 9월부터 창간함니다. 이것은 영리를 떠나서 조선 문학의 번영을 위하여 봉사하는 정 신으로 하는 것이외다.
某某 신문사에 旋風이 잇슨 뒤 논단이 蕭條하여 가슴이 압흡니다. 松茂栢悅이라고 서로 다 잘 되여야 하겟는데요.
7월 8일은 暑中休暇의 의미로 월 1회식 발간하겟슴니다.＜54＞
