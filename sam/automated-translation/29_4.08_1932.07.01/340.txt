Mussolini's Yellow Peril Theory

Since the Manchurian Incident occurred, one peculiar by-product has been Mussolini's Yellow Peril theory. The gist of the Yellow Peril theory, as is typical, is that Japan, in collaboration with China, will rise up to annihilate the White race. He says that Japan, the giant among the Yellow race, leading its 50 million people—as he puts it—will equip China's 400 million people with modern industry and training, subsequently seizing hegemony in the East, while the White people are engrossed in strengthening their fraternal walls (兄弟城壁) and cannot see this disgraceful state (醜態). He offers the grand conclusion: "Arise! White race, awaken! White race." The world judged that Mussolini had either gone mad or, if not, had said this as part of his policy towards Europe, particularly towards France (對佛政策). If the latter is true, the meaning would be self-evident, but if the former, one can only feel pity.

It is true, as Mussolini says, that the White races are fighting amongst themselves. However, it is also true that the Yellow races are fighting amongst themselves at the same time. The current situation between Japan and China is precisely that. This is because the interests of each nation itself have a more intimate connection to its life than race does. Mussolini is someone who forgets this great fact and paints illusions according to his own fancy. Furthermore, the Shanghai Incident was, in reality, China's struggle against the Various Powers (列國), ideologically a struggle against the White race. Japan happened to fire the shots as an accidental result.

What does Mussolini, who speaks of the Yellow Peril, make of this crucial fact? If stimulated by these two facts, one cannot help but reach the sudden conclusion, "Racial distinctions lack authority in times of urgent national need. Speaking of a Yellow Peril is an anachronism (時代錯誤)," arriving at a conclusion completely opposite to Mussolini's. This is why one wonders if he might be mentally unsound.

The Yellow Peril theory was advocated by the deposed Emperor Wilhelm II (廢帝 월헤룸 第 2世) with political significance, and revenge, guided by God's providence (攝理), was unexpectedly carried out through the World War. Of course, we are not so slow-witted (頓才) as to pay attention to or sincerely listen to anyone who speaks of the Yellow Peril. Nor are our minds confused. We merely want to advise Mussolini that it is extremely dangerous to so lightly put forth the Yellow Peril merely as some grand strategy (大策) right before our eyes. <19>

**Summary**

This editorial critiques Benito Mussolini's recent revival of the "Yellow Peril" theory, which posited that Japan would lead China against the White race. The author dismisses this as either political maneuvering (especially regarding France) or madness, pointing out the ongoing conflict between Japan and China (Manchurian Incident, Shanghai Incident) as clear evidence against Yellow racial solidarity. The author argues national interests supersede racial ties and calls Mussolini's theory an outdated anachronism, originally promoted by Kaiser Wilhelm II. The article concludes by warning Mussolini against frivolously using such dangerous rhetoric. From a historical perspective, this piece is interesting as it shows a Korean viewpoint under Japanese rule analyzing international politics. It engages with global racial theories (Yellow Peril) while simultaneously navigating the complex reality of Japanese imperialism and its conflict with China, subtly critiquing both Western alarmism and potentially the hypocrisy inherent in racial theories given Japan's actions in Asia.

뭇소리니의 黃禍論
滿洲事件이 이러나게 되자 한가지의 怪奇한 副産物은 뭇소리니의 黃禍論이다. 黃禍論의 要領은 例에 의한 例와 갓치 日本이 中國과 공동하야 白人種에 대하야 討滅을 일으킨다고 하는 것으로 그는 黃色人種 중의 巨人 日本이 그 5,000萬人-그는 말한다-을 引率하야 支那 4億萬民에게 近代式의 산업과 훈련과를 갓게 하고 딸아서 동양의 覇權을 잡으려고 하는대 白人은 兄弟城壁을 굿게 하기에 열중하고 그 醜態를 볼 수 업다. 「일어나라! 白人種 깨워라. 白人種」이라고 豪壯한 結語를 내리고 잇다. 세계는 뭇소리니가 狂人이 되엿거나 그러치 안으면 對歐洲政策 그 중에도 對佛政策으로써 이런 말을 한 것이라고 鑑定을 내리엿다. 만약 後者라하면 의미는 自明될 것이로대 前者라면 실로 가련한 感을 가질밧게 업다.
뭇소리니의 말과 갓치 白人種이 서로 相爭하는 것은 사실이다. 그러나 이와 동시에 黃人種이 相爭하는 것도 또 사실이다. 日本과 中國과의 現狀이 그것이다. 이것은 人種보다도 각 국민 자신의 利害關係가 더 密接한 관계를 그 생활에 잇기 때문이다. 이러한 크다런 사실을 忘却하야 버리고 자기 마음대로 幻像을 그리여 내는 것이 뭇소리니다. 그러고 또 上海事件은 實에 잇서서는 支那對 列國, 主義로써는 白色人種과의 抗爭이다. 때때 日本이 偶然한 결과로써 銃聲을 울니게 된다.

黃禍를 말하는 뭇소리니는 이 중대한 사실을 어떳케 보는 것인지 이상 두가지 사실에 剌戟된다 할 것 갓흐면 突然의 結語로써 「人種의 차별은 國家緊急의 필요한 때에는 權威가 업다. 黃禍를 말하는 것은 時代錯誤이다」라고 하지 안할 수 업스며 뭇소리니에는 全然 정반대의 결론에 도달할 것이다. 그가 혹 정신이상이나 아닌가 하는 것이 이곳에 잇다.
黃禍論은 廢帝 월헤룸 第 2世가 정치적 意義를 가지고 唱道한 것으로 神의 攝理에 의한 復讐는 期치 안코 世界大戰에 의하야 遂行된 것이다. 물론 我等은 누구가 黃禍를 말하드라도 그런대 着心하거나 진실하게 들을 그러한 頓才는 아니다. 또 두뇌가 혼란하야 잇지도 안타. 다만 우리는 너무나 눈압헤 大策만으로만 黃禍라고 하는 것을 그러케 가벼웁게 내논는 것은 대단히 위험한 것이라는 것을 뭇소리니에게 향하야 충고하고 십다.＜19＞
