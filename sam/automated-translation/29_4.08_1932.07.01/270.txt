**Body Text Translation:**
A Korean Genius Aviator Active on the Chinese Revolutionary Front, Nationalist Government Aviation Lieutenant Colonel Mr. Kim Yŏn-gi
						Kim Yŏn-gi

The story of the genius aviator Mr. An Ch'ang-nam, who once participated in Chiang Kai-shek's Northern Expedition revolutionary army, fighting from Wuchang and Hanyang to various other places, achieving great military successes, and receiving much respect from the 400 million Chinese people, only to be shot by Yen Hsi-shan's troops mid-revolution and depart forever for the other world, is still a sad memory that brings tears to our eyes. However, there is now another genius aviator who, no less than Mr. An Ch'ang-nam, is gaining the trust of the Nationalist Party (國民黨) and achieving great military success while fighting across places like Shashi, Changsha, Nanchang, P'ing-hsiang (萍卿), Chumatien (駐馬居), Zhengzhou, Peking, Bengbu (蚝埠), Kaifeng, etc. – he is Mr. Kim Yŏn-gi. Mr. Kim, a young man not yet 30 this year, once attended Sungsil Middle School in P'yŏngyang. In the 2nd year of the Republic (1927?), Mr. Kim was appointed as a Flight Officer in the Military Government Aviation First Squadron by the Nationalist Government Chairman Chiang Chung-cheng (蔣中正 - Chiang Kai-shek), and has since been repeatedly promoted, now reaching the rank of Aviation Lieutenant Colonel.

The Military Government Aviation First Squadron is located in Zhengzhou, Henan Province, where numerous flight officers invited from Britain and America are stationed, training several hundred young Chinese men. Mr. Kim Yŏn-gi is there, sometimes loading bombs and sortieing to the actual front lines, and at other times taking up the chalk to teach piloting skills to his juniors. Therefore, it is said that there are currently no young Chinese officers who do not know Mr. Kim Yŏn-gi's name, and the trust of the Nationalist Government in him grows deeper by the day.

Furthermore, it is said that Mr. Kim also sortied during the recent Shanghai Incident. We will omit all description of this here and only wish for the well-being of the young aviator Mr. Kim Yŏn-gi, who is breathing fire with immense spirit.
Finally, we publish below Mr. Kim's own account, obtained through the painstaking efforts of our company. Hurrah, Hurrah, Mr. Kim!

Mr. Kim Yŏn-gi's Account

I pray for the well-being of my homeland compatriots through the pages of "Samch'ŏlli."
In Korea, where the machine industry is underdeveloped, what could possibly be satisfactory? But speaking of the aviation field, it is even sadder that it has not yet reached the embryonic stage.

During My Seven Years in Aviation
Due to various circumstances in 1930, I was unable to carry out the planned long-distance flight between Tokyo and Sinŭiju, which I consider regrettable for our nation.
In 1926, I dropped out of Sungsil Middle School in P'yŏngyang midway and went to Tokyo around October.
At that time, I was 17 years old. As you all well know, learning to fly requires a huge fortune, so my grandfather opposed it and did not give his permission.
At that time, my father gave his permission, and I was barely able to go study abroad in Tokyo.
At that time, I immediately entered the Tachikawa Japan Flight School (立川日本飛行學校) in Tokyo, and although I obtained my 2nd Class Pilot's License in 1929, I wanted to make a homeland visit flight to my hometown, Ŭiju, but due to economic difficulties, the long-distance flight between Tokyo and Sinŭiju could not be carried out.
In 1930, when I was 21 years old, through an introduction from Consul Chu (朱領事), the Chinese Consul in Sinŭiju, Korea, I was introduced to <10> the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in Nanjing, which then introduced me to the Aviation Bureau (航空署).
At that time, after a test flight at the Aviation Bureau, I was inducted into the service as an Aviation Major (航空少佐).
I joined as a Pilot (飛機師) with the rank of Major (少校) in the Nationalist Government Military Government Aviation First Squadron.
At that time, the station of the Aviation First Squadron was Hankou, so I came to Hankou.
Currently, the Central Army (中央軍) has over 200 aircraft, and new fighter planes and bombers bought from Britain and America number over 200.
The Air Corps consists of Aviation Squadron One through Squadron Seven, and the number of pilots reaches over 200.
Because China has many internal wars, since coming to Hankou in July 1930, the places I have departed for include Shashi, Changsha, Nanchang, P'ing-hsiang (革鄕 - likely typo for 萍鄕), Chumatien (駐馬店), Zhengzhou, Peking, Bengbu (蚪埠 - likely typo for 蚌埠), Kaifeng, and Nanjing. I traveled to these various places over two years, and I also departed for Nanjing during the past Shanghai Sino-Japanese War.
During my two years in the Chinese Air Corps, I have had quite interesting experiences. I have served in reconnaissance flight duties until now, and I have piloted over 10 types of aircraft so far. I have been promoted to Chief Pilot Aviation Lieutenant Colonel (總飛行航空中佐).
Our An Ch'ang-nam sadly passed away at the hands of Yen Hsi-shan's army, but I am striving harder and harder to further enhance my skills.
With the passionate spirit of becoming an Aviation Lieutenant Colonel at the age of 23 this year, having accumulated over 700 flight hours during my 7 years in the aviation field, I am striving and striving to bring satisfaction to the future aviation field in Korea, where the youth machine industry is underdeveloped.
June 8, 1932
From the Aviation First Squadron, Nanchang (南難州 - likely typo for 南昌州/Nanchang), China
Respectfully, Kim Yŏn-gi <11>

**Summary**

This 1932 article from the Korean magazine *Samch'ŏlli* introduces Kim Yŏn-gi, a young Korean aviator serving as a Lieutenant Colonel in the Chinese Nationalist Air Force. The editor highlights Kim's achievements, comparing him to the late An Ch'ang-nam, another Korean pilot famed for his service in China. Kim's own account follows, detailing his path from dropping out of Sungsil Middle School in P'yŏngyang, training in Japan, and joining the Chinese air force in 1930. He mentions his extensive combat experience across China, including participation in the Shanghai Incident, and expresses his ambition to contribute to Korea's future aviation development despite its underdeveloped industrial base. For historians, the article offers a valuable glimpse into the transnational careers pursued by some Koreans during the colonial period, particularly their involvement in China's military and political struggles against both internal factions and Japanese encroachment. Kim's story, alongside An Ch'ang-nam's, illustrates a path of resistance and seeking opportunity abroad, reflecting the complex realities and aspirations of Koreans under Japanese rule.

中國革命戰線에 活躍하는 朝鮮人 天才 飛行家, 國民政府航空中佐 金鍊器氏
						金鍊器
일즉 天才 飛行家 安昌男씨가 蔣介石의 北伐革命軍에 참가하여 武昌 漢陽을 爲始하여 각지로 轉戰하면서 크게 戰績을 나타내어 4억만 中國 國民으로부터 만흔 존경을 밧고 잇다가 革命中途에 閻錫山軍隊에 사격을 당하야 영원히 저 세상으로 떠나고 만 일은 아직도 우리들의 눈물을 적시게 하는 슬푼 기억이다. 그런데 이제 또한 安昌男씨에 못지지 안케 國民黨으로부터 신임을 밧고 沙市, 長沙, 南昌, 萍卿, 駐馬居, 鄭州, 北京, 蚝埠, 開封 등지로 轉戰하면서 크게 戰績을 나타내고 잇는 天才 飛行家 한분이 잇스니 그는 金鍊器씨라. 氏는 금년 30未滿의 청년의 몸으로 일즉 平壤 崇實中學을 단니든 분이다. 김씨는 民國 2년에 國民政府 主席 蔣中正으로부터 軍政府航空 第一隊의 飛行將校로 임명되어 이래 屢進하야 그 지위가 지금 航空中佐에 미치엇다.
軍政府航空 第一隊는 河南省 鄭州에 잇는데 거기에는 英米國으로부터 招聘된 多數 飛行將校가 駐在하야 數百의 중국 청년을 敎練하고 잇는 바 金鍊器씨는 거기에 잇서서 혹은 폭탄을 실고 實地로 戰線에 출동하며 또는 敎鞭을 들고 後進에게 비행술을 가르치기도 한다. 그래서 지금 中國의 청년 장교치고 金鍊器씨의 일홈을 모르는 이가 업고 그만치 國民政府의 신임도 날로 두터워간다고 한다.
그리고 氏는 今番의 上海動亂에도 출동하엿다 하는 바 우리는 여기 對한 기술을 전부 생략하고 오직 萬丈의 氣焰을 토하고 잇는 청년 비행가 金鍊器씨의 健在를 바랄 뿐이다.
끗흐로 本社가 苦心하야 入手한 氏 자신의 手記를 아래에 발표하노라. 푸레, 푸레, 金氏여!

金鍊器氏 手記
故土同胞의 健安을 『三千里』들 통하야 비나이다.
기계공업이 유치한 朝鮮에 무엇 한아 만족한 것이 잇스련만 항공계로 말하면 더욱 아직 배태期에도 이르지 못함이 슬푸외다.

나의 航空生活 七年 동안에
1930년은 나에게 여러 가지 사정으로 인하여 東京 新義州間 長距離 飛行 계획한 것을 決行치를 못하여 우리 민족에게 원통으로 생각함니다.
내가 1926년에 平壤 崇實中學校를 中途에서 퇴학하고 10월경에 東京으로 가게 되엿슴니다.
그때에 내가 17세 때인대 물론 여러분도 잘 아시겟지만 비행기를 배울나면 수만흔 資産이 들기 때문에 그때에 나의 祖父님께서는 반대를 하여 허락을 아니하엿슴니다.
그때에 부친님께서는 허락을 하여 겨우 東京留學을 가게 되엿슴니다.
그때에 바로 東京 立川日本飛行學校에 입학하여 1929년에 2等 飛行士 免許狀을 得하엿지만은 나의 고향인 義州까지는 故國訪問飛行을 할냐고 하엿지마는 경제곤란으로 東京 新義州간 長距離 飛行은 決行치를 못하게 되엿슴니다.
1930년 나의 21세 되는 때 朝鮮 新義州 中國領事 朱領事의 소개를 엇어가지고 南京 外交部에＜10＞ 소개하여 航空署로 소개하엿슴니다.
그때에 航空署에서 試驗飛行을 한 결과에 航空少佐로 入隊하게 되엿슴니다.
國民政府 軍政府航空 第一隊 小校 飛機師로 入隊하엿슴니다.
그때에 航空 第一隊住地가 漢口가 되여 漢口로 오게되엿슴니다.
현재 中央軍에 飛機는 200餘 臺며 英米國에서 사온 新戰鬪機와 爆擊機가 200臺 이상에 달함니다.
航空隊는 航空 第一隊에 第 七隊까지 잇스며 飛機師는 200여명에 달함니다.
中國은 內戰이 만흔 때문에 내가 1930년 7월에 漢口로 온 이후 出發地方은 沙市, 長沙, 南昌, 革鄕駐馬店, 鄭州, 北京, 蚪埠, 開封, 南京 各 地方으로 2년 동안에 이와갓치 출발하게 되엿스며 지나간 上海 日中戰爭에도 南京까지 출발하엿슴니다.
나의 中國航空隊 2년 동안에 퍽 滋味만히 보앗스며 나는 지금까지 偵察飛行에 服務하고 잇스며 지금까지의 비행기의 종류는 10여종의 비행기를 조종하엿스며 總飛行航空中佐로 昇格되엿슴니다.
우리 安昌男군은 閻錫山軍隊에게 可惜하게도 세상을 떠나지만는 나는 더욱 기능을 助長식히려고 노력 중에 노력하나이다.
나의 7년동안 항공계에 비행시간 700여시간에 달하여 금년 23세의 航空中佐가 된 血氣之勇으로 淸年 機械工業의 유치한 朝鮮에 장차 항공계에 만족을 끼치려고 노력중에 노력함니다.
