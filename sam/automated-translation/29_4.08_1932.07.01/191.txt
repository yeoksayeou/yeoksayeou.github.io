Company Announcement

Kang Chehwan (姜齊煥), Head of the South Manchuria General Branch Office, and Yi T'aehwa (李泰和), South Manchuria Investigative Reporter, will be traveling on business to various places in South and North Manchuria this summer. We ask for the special support (愛顧) of everyone in those regions.

July 1st
Kyŏngsŏng, Samch'ŏlli Company Announcement<49>

**Summary**

This is a brief company announcement from Samch'ŏlli Sa (Samch'ŏlli Company), the publisher of Samch'ŏlli magazine. It informs readers that Kang Chehwan, head of their South Manchuria branch, and Yi T'aehwa, an investigative reporter for the same region, will be traveling throughout South and North Manchuria during the summer. The announcement requests the support and cooperation of people in those areas for the two representatives. From a historical perspective, this notice highlights the operational scope of a prominent Korean cultural enterprise like Samch'ŏlli during the colonial period, extending its activities and information gathering into Manchuria, a region of significant importance to Korean migration, business, and nationalist movements at the time. It demonstrates the magazine's network and engagement beyond the Korean peninsula.
