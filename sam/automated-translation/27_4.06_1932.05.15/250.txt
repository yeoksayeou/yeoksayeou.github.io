Longing
						Mo Yun-suk
The sound of the wind swims over the fields, <38>
My empty room, restless heart, wanders alone,
Over the darkness, those waves, a pitiful thought,
North Castle (北城), the night sky is so desolate.

A long sigh mixed, a painful breath fills me,
Oh, that cloud, divide and come to me.
Upon this soul of mine, bound with no way to untie.

Even this day, deeply yearned for, is setting.
Mist settles on the distant mountain ridge, only stars rise in the blue sky.
How can I calm this anxious heart, wondering if we will meet?
Shedding tears, undoing the coat ties, will sleep please come?

My love, if beyond ten thousand *li* this night is lonely for you too,
Turn the blowing wind into wings and come floating down,
My crying heart by the window sill, placing its sorrow in your hands,
Let’s meet as if in a dream! This too, a fleeting thought. <39>

**Summary**

This poem by Mo Yun-suk expresses intense feelings of longing and loneliness for a distant beloved. The speaker observes the desolate night scene—wind, an empty room, the dark sky over "North Castle" (北城)—which mirrors her inner turmoil. She desperately wishes for reunion, even asking the clouds or the wind to bring her love, acknowledging the seeming impossibility even as she yearns. The poem captures a mood of sorrow, anxiety about meeting, and the solace sought in dreams. From a historical perspective, the poem offers insight into personal expression during the colonial period, particularly the voice of a prominent female intellectual articulating deep emotion. Historians might find interest in the blend of traditional lyrical themes (longing, nature imagery) with the specific context and sensibilities of the 1930s, and the use of older Korean orthography.

그리움
						毛允淑
바람소래 들 우에 헤염질 치고,＜38＞
비인방 설넨 가슴 홀노 떠돌아,
어둠 우로 그 물결 가엽는 생각이어,
北城이라 밤 한울은 쓸쓸도 하이.

긴 한숨 석긴 괴로운 숨이 차고나,
저 구름 갈나저 내게로 오렴.
풀 길 업시 매치인 이내넉우에.

마음 깁히 사모되는 이 날해도 저물건만.
먼 산허리 안개 끼고 푸른 한울 별만 돗네.
맛날가 조바심튼 마음 어이 진정할가나,
눈물지고 푸는 고름 잠이 부듸 들어가요?

님아 너도 만리 넘어 이 밤이 쓸슬커든,
부는 바람 날개 돌여 휘영정 나려오렴,
창문 턱에 우든 가슴 네 손길에 시름 언고,
꿈인 듯 맛나보자! 이 또한 뜬 생각이.＜39＞
＜38-39＞
