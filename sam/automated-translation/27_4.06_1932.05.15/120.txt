**Body**

Jokes

The Reason for Composure

Several men who lived dominated by their wives gathered and resolved that from now on, they would definitely dominate their wives instead. Then, to celebrate this, they were having a toast when, having heard the noise from somewhere, their wives came rushing in as a group.

Where did all the bravado from moments ago go? The men scrambled to be first and scattered, fleeing in all directions. Among them, only one man remained completely composed and did not move.

"Aigoo, the leader is different. How admirable!"

The others, surprised by his courage, came back to look, and ah! That man had fainted.

Kim Hyesuk's Prayer

Miss Kim Hyesuk (金惠淑), who is a telephone operator and a devout Christian, offers a prayer every night before sleeping.

"Hello? Hello? Is this Heaven? This is Hyesuk— Our Father who art in Heaven..."

Her older brother, listening to this, remarked,
"Habits die hard, I see."<19>

**Summary**

This article presents two short humorous anecdotes. The first tells of henpecked husbands whose resolve to stand up to their wives instantly evaporates when the wives appear, except for one man who stays put only because he fainted from fear. The second describes a Christian telephone operator whose prayers begin like a phone call. From a historical perspective, the jokes offer glimpses into social dynamics and gender roles (the trope of the henpecked husband), as well as the penetration of Western influences like Christianity and modern technology (telephones) into Korean society during the colonial period, even becoming subjects of light humour.

泰然不動의 까닭
녀편네의 궁뎅이에 깔니워 지내는 사내 사오인이 모여 이후에는 단연 녀펜네의 궁뎅이에 깔고 지낼을 결의 하엿다. 그러고는 그 축하로 축배 한잔씩 드는데 이 소리를 어대에서 듯고 달려왓는지 안해들 사오명이 와-쓰러왓다.
지금까지 호언장담은 다 어듸로 갓는지 사내들은 압흘 다토야 *히 도망히엿다. 그 중에 한사람만 태연 자약하게 움지기지 아는 남자가 잇섯다.
「에이구, 수모자라 달느군. 훌융하다」
하고 여럿은 그 용긔에 놀내면서 도라 와보니 앗! 그 사내는 긔절하고 잇섯다.

金惠淑의 祈禱
電話交換手이자 基督敎를 信實하게 밋는 金惠淑孃은 밤에 잘때마다 就寢前의 祈禱를 올닌다.
「여보세요, 여보세요, 거기는 하늘님임까, 저는요 惠淑인데요- 하늘에 게신 아바지서……」
이것을 듯고 잇는 그 옵바가
「習慣을 할 수 업군」＜19＞
