Miscellaneous Observations on Speeches

Hŏ Hŏn

It must have been about five or six years ago now. At the Ch'ŏndogyo Memorial Hall (天道敎紀念舘) in Kyŏngun-dong (慶雲洞), the headquarters of the Sin'ganhoe (新幹會) invited some doctor from Czechoslovakia (첵고슬로바기아), an authority on the study of minor nationality issues at the time, who had come to Korea (朝鮮), to give a lecture.

The person who presided over that speech event as Master of Ceremonies was Mr. Hŏ Hŏn (許憲), who was then the chairman of the Sin'ganhoe, and the person who gave the opening remarks was also Mr. Hŏ Hŏn. That was the first time I encountered him as someone on the speaker's platform.

Mr. Hŏ Hŏn's speech was not "agitation-style"; it was thoroughly dignified and forceful. Having frequented the courts as a lawyer for over ten years, his eloquence (辯舌) should have been close to fluent oratory, but in reality, it wasn't the smooth, flowing torrent style of fluent oratory, but rather appeared solely "dignified" and weighty. It would be an apt assessment to say that each word and phrase, uttered unhurriedly (悠悠히), carried the weight of a thousand kŭn (천근).

His voice volume (聲量) had a metallic quality that resonated far, so his words carried well throughout the hall (場內).

His posture while speaking was such that he stood in one place without moving much, and he didn't use many gestures (체스체아).

Yi Kwang-su

The first time I heard Mr. Yi Kwang-su's (李光洙) speech was a couple of months ago at the Ch'ŏndogyo Memorial Hall. He generally doesn't appear often at events like speech meetings. Therefore, even people who have encountered his writing thousands of times have likely not heard his speeches much. Although I hear that when he goes to places like P'yŏngyang (平壤), he often attends lecture meetings hosted by the Tongjihoe (同志會)—

Speaking of the lecture I heard at the aforementioned Memorial Hall, at that time, Ch'ŏndogyo was hosting a Denmark Study Group Meeting (丁抹講習會), and he was seated there as the Editor-in-Chief of the Tong-A Ilbo (東亞日報) and gave remarks by way of congratulations, but it still lasted about 30 minutes one way or another.

Considering his appearance or soft speaking voice, one might imagine his lecture would also be like a scholar's academic lecture. However, in reality, he was an "agitation" speaker with a strong element of incitement. The extreme highs and lows in his speech, the energetic bulging of veins on his face, the piercing (炯炯한) gaze as if shooting something, and the way he pounded the table with his fist at key points! He certainly seemed capable of success even if put forward for recruitment speeches (募兵演說) or fundraising speeches (義金募集演說).

When speaking, he tended to move his upper body relatively more. And as proof of his sharp intellect (두뇌), the content of his speech was also rich and logically organized (조리가 정연하엿다). Just as there was nothing to fault in his writing, there was nothing to fault in his speech either.

Chang Tŏk-su

I hear that Mr. Chang Tŏk-su (張德秀) will be returning to Korea this spring, so after a long time, we will be able to hear his impassioned speeches. In the period immediately after the March First Movement (己未 卽後), when speeches became highly popular in Korean (朝鮮) society, there was the name "Peninsula's Three Great Orators (半島 三大雄辯家)". This referred to Mr. Pak Il-byŏng (朴一秉), Mr. Kim Ch'ang-je (金昶濟), and this Mr. Chang Tŏk-su. The characteristics of the three were: Mr. Pak Il-byŏng's speeches were grandiloquent eloquence (雄辯宏辭), ending in agitation from the first word to the last; Mr. Kim Ch'ang-je was renowned for logical coherence (조리정연); and Mr. Chang Tŏk-su, for his part, was characterized as a speaker consistently driven by passion (정열).

I only heard Mr. Chang Tŏk-su speak exactly once, which must have been about ten years ago, before he went to America (渡米前).

He stood on the platform at the Chongno Youth Hall (鍾路靑年會舘) along with Messrs. Kim Myŏng-sik (金明植), Pak Il-byŏng (朴一秉), Ch'oe Tu-sŏn (崔斗善), and others. I forget the speech topic (演題), but I remember him delivering a very fiery speech. His voice (聲音) was rich, and his bearing (몸가짐) was nimble (輕捷), skillfully capturing the audience's psychology and keeping them tense until the end.

His rhetoric (修辭) could not match Mr. Pak Il-byŏng's, and his logic (條理) could not match Mr. Kim Ch'ang-je's, but in a way that suggested more than was explicitly said (言外有言式), he threw something significant to the audience. (Continued in the next issue) <7>

**Summary**

This article from the May 1932 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine offers observations on the public speaking styles of three prominent Korean figures of the time: Hŏ Hŏn, Yi Kwang-su, and Chang Tŏk-su. It describes Hŏ Hŏn as dignified and weighty, Yi Kwang-su as surprisingly impassioned and agitational despite his scholarly image, and Chang Tŏk-su as a fiery orator known for passion, contrasting him with Pak Il-byŏng (grandiloquence) and Kim Ch'ang-je (logic). The author reflects on past speeches and anticipates Chang Tŏk-su's return to Korea. For historians, this piece provides valuable contemporary insight into the public personas and oratorical techniques of key nationalist and intellectual figures during the Japanese colonial period. It highlights the importance placed on public speaking in Korean society at the time and offers subjective but revealing comparisons between different leadership styles within the nationalist movement.

演說雜觀
許憲

지금부터 아마 5, 6년 前일은 될 줄 안다. 慶雲洞 天道敎紀念舘에서 그 때 약소민족 문제연구의 권위인 첵고슬로바기아의 무슨 박사인가 朝鮮에 온 것을 新幹會 본부에서 초청하여 강연을 식힌 일이 잇섯다.
그 때에 그 연설회를 司會한 이가 新幹會 위원장으로 잇든 許憲씨요 개회사를 한 이가 亦 許憲씨라. 壇上人으로 氏를 대하기는 그 때가 처음이엇다.
許憲씨의 연설은 「아지」式은 아니엇고 어대까지든지 정중하고 힘이 잇섯다. 변호사로서 법정을 출입한 지 10여 년에 미첫슴으로 그의 辯舌은 오히려 快辯에 각가울 터인데 실제는 그러케 유창한 청산유수식의 快辯은 아니고 오직 「정중」하고 무게 잇서 보엿다. 悠悠히 토하는 一言一句는 천근의 무게가 잇다함이 適評이리라.
聲量은 멀니 울니어 나가는 금속성을 띄엇슴으로 場內에 말이 잘 徹底되엇다.
연설할 때의 체격은 한 곳에 서서 별로 움지기지 안엇고 「체스체아」도 별로 쓰지 안엇섯다.

李光洙

李光洙씨 연설은 두어 달 전에 天道敎기념관에서 드른 것이 처음이다. 氏는 대체로 연설회 가튼데 잘 나서지를 안는다. 그러기에 氏의 문장에는 천번 만번 접촉하엿든 사람일지라도 그의 연설은 별반 들어보지 못하엿슬 것이다. 드른 즉 平壤 가튼데 가서는 同志會 주최의 강연회에 잘 출석한다 하지만―
내가 드른 前記 기념관의 강연으로 말하면 그때는 天道敎 주최로 丁抹講習會를 개최하는 그 석상에 東亞日報 編輯局長으로 와 안젓다가 축사삼아 한 것이엇는데 그래도 이럭저럭 30분은 하엿스리라.
氏는 외모라든지 부드러운 말소리를 통하여 생각할 때는 그 강연도 학자의 학술강연 모양이리라고 상상하리라. 그러치만 실제는 선동성을 다분히 띈 「아지」연설가엇다. 그 말에 고저장단이 격심한 것 얼골에 핏줄이 긔운잇게 뻣은 것 눈빗이 무엇을 쏘는 듯 炯炯한 것 그리고 포인트, 포인트에 가서 탁자를 주먹으로 때리는 樣! 확실히 募兵연설이나 義金모집연설 가튼데 내어 노아도 성공할 듯 하엿다.
氏는 연설할 때에 비교적 상반신을 움지기는 편이엇다. 그리고 두뇌가 명석한 증거로 말의 내용도 풍부하고 조리가 정연하엿다. 그 문장에 흠잡을 것이 업는 모양으로 그 말에도 흠잡을 것이라고 업섯다.

張德秀

드르니 올 봄에 張德秀씨는 조선에 도라 나오리라하니 오래간만에 우리들은 氏의 열변을 드를 수 잇슬 것이다. 己未 卽後에 朝鮮사회에는 연설이 대유행할 때에 「半島 三大雄辯家」라는 일홈이 잇섯다. 그는 朴一秉씨, 金昶濟씨, 그리고 이 張德秀씨를 가르처 한 말이엇다. 세 분의 특색은 朴一秉씨의 연설은 雄辯宏辭로 첫마듸부터 끗마듸까지 선동에 끗치고 金昶濟씨는 조리정연으로 일홈낫고 그리고 張德秀씨에 至하여는 또 정열로 일관한 演說客으로 그 특징을 삼엇다.
張德秀씨의 연설은 꼭 한 번을 들엇슬 뿐인데 氏가 渡米前이니 약 10여 년이 되리라.
鍾路靑年會舘에서 金明植, 朴一秉, 崔斗善 등 諸氏와 가치 연단에 서섯는데 演題는 이젓스나 퍽으나 열변을 토하든 기억이 난다. 聲音도 풍부하고 몸가짐도 輕捷하여 청중의 심리를 용하게 붓잡고 끗까지 긴장식혓다.
修辭는 朴一秉씨를 따르지 못하엿고 條理도 金昶濟씨를 따르지 못한엿지만 言外有言式으로 청중에게 무엇인가 크게 던저주는 것이 잇섯다. (以下次號續) <7>
