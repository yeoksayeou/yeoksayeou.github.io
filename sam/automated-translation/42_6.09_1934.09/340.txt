近讀短評 (Short Reviews of Recent Readings)

Pak Yŏnghŭi
As the weather is hot, I cannot read much. Generally, to forget the heat, I read books that have little relation to literature. As for literary works, I read Mr. Chang Hyŏkchu’s 「Kwon to iu Otoko (權と云ふ男)」 and Mr. Yi T'aejun’s 「Talpam (달밤)」.

Both authors showed that their unique writing styles and thoughts were established. If the former is masculine, the latter is feminine; if the former is realistic, the latter gives the feeling of lamenting the unavoidable hardships of life, subject to fate, with light humor. As the title is "Short Reviews," I cannot say more. <215>

Pak Chonghwa
Reading the poetry of Master Tasan Chŏng Yagyong (茶山 丁若鏞先生), I realized that Master Chŏng was not only a great economist and legal scholar of Chosŏn but also a great poet of the humanist school (人生派的 詩人) in Chosŏn. For a poet writing in classical Chinese (漢詩人), who could easily drift into merely appreciating nature (吟風弄月) or pure moral philosophy (純道德哲學), the fact that he reflected the miserable aspects of the lives of the common people (民衆) of that era, cried out against the evils of misrule (惡政), and intended reform (改革) is enough to make him a presence emitting a great, distinct ray of light for his time. Looking from this perspective, one can seek out and glimpse his representative work in "Chŏksŏng Ch'onsa (積城村舍)". <217>

No Chayŏng
Recently, I read Kuo Mo-jo's (郭沫若) "Luoye (落葉)". As it is written in vernacular Chinese (白話體) Hanmun, it is difficult to grasp the true literary flavor (文章的 眞味), but overall, it can be called a well-structured work. However, perhaps because it is the work of a Chinese person, there is some exaggeration here and there, and also the flaw of trying too hard to use ornate language (美辭麗句). Although the content is somewhat sentimental, I think it well represents the minds of young Chinese people these days. <219>

Yang Paekhwa
There isn't any one particular work, but among the popular novels (大衆小說) I've been reading with interest lately are the following three:
"Kŭmch'ŏk ŭi Kkum (金尺의 꿈)" (Lacks refinement of writing)
"Simya ŭi T'aeyang (深夜의 太陽)" (Signs of painstaking effort are visible)
"Im Kkŏkjŏng Chŏn (林巨正傳)" (Will discuss separately) <221>

Kim Unjŏng
It's been a long time since I last saw you. I always admire your diligent efforts (奮鬪). Regarding the matter you notified me about, I apologize that I could not obtain the materials (材料) to answer your inquiry, so I am sending back a blank reply (白紙). I have no time to read because I'm busy earning a living (밥버러 먹기). Please forgive me (容恕). <223>

**Summary**

This article compiles five short reviews or comments by different Korean intellectuals in 1934. Pak Yŏnghŭi briefly comments on works by Chang Hyŏkchu and Yi T'aejun, contrasting their styles. Pak Chonghwa praises the Joseon scholar Chŏng Yagyong as a socially conscious poet. No Chayŏng reviews a work by Chinese writer Kuo Mo-jo, noting its strengths and weaknesses. Yang Paekhwa lists three popular Korean novels of interest. Finally, Kim Unjŏng humorously apologizes for not contributing a review due to being too busy working to read. For historians, the article offers a glimpse into the literary tastes and critical discourse among Korean intellectuals during the colonial period, showing engagement with contemporary Korean fiction, rediscovery of historical Korean figures, awareness of modern Chinese literature, and the reading of popular novels. Kim Unjŏng's comment also poignantly reflects the economic pressures faced by intellectuals that limited their time for literary pursuits.

近讀短評
朴英熙
日氣가 더운 關係로 讀者도 많이 못합니다. 대게 文學과는 關係가 적은 冊을 더위를 이즈려고 봄니다. 作品으로는 張赫宙氏의 「權と云ふ男」와 李泰俊氏의 「달밤」을 보앗습니다.
兩氏가 다 獨特한 筆致와 思想이 確立하여 보엿습니다. 前者는 男性的이면 後者는 女性的이며 前者가 事實的이면 後者는 運命的 不可抗力의 生活苦를 그대로 가비야운 「유모어」로서 詠嘆한 듯한 늣김을 줌니다. 題가 短評이 매더 말할 수 업습니다.＜215＞

朴鍾和
茶山 丁若鏞先生의 詩를 읽고, 先生이 朝鮮의 큰 經濟學者와 法律家뿐만이 안이라 朝鮮의 큰 人生派的 詩人임을 알엇다. 다만 吟風弄月이 안이면 純道德哲學에 흘으기 쉬운 漢詩人으로써, 그 時代民衆의 慘憺한 生活相을 反映하고써 惡政의 弊害을 부르지져 改革을 意圖하았다는 것만으로도 先生은, 當時 한 큰 異彩의 光芒을 發하는 存在이다. 이러한 點으로 보아 그의 代表作을 「積城村舍」에 求하야 엿볼 수 있다.＜217＞

盧子泳
最近 郭沫若의 「落葉」을 읽었음니다. 白話體의 漢文이라 文章的 眞味는 알기 어려우나 全體로 보야 整頓된 作品이라 하겠음니다. 그러나 中國人의 作品이니 만치 어데인지 誇張이 조곰 있고 또는 억지로 美辭麗句를 쓰려 한 缺點이 있음니다. 內容은 다소 感傷的이나 요새 中國靑年들의 마음을 잘 나타내였다고 생각됨니다.＜219＞

梁白華
어느 한 作品이라고 할 것은 업고 이즈막 興味잇시 닑는 大衆小說에 左記三種이 잇습니다.
「金尺의 꿈」 (文章의 洗練이 업슴)
「深夜의 太陽」 (苦心의 자취가 보임)
「林巨正傳」 (別論)＜221＞

金雲汀
오래 보압지 못햇슴니다. 恒時 奮鬪하심을 羡望함니다. 通知하신 問題는 貴問에 對答할 材料를 엇지 못하야 未安하오나 白紙로 返信을 보내 드림니다. 밥버러 먹기에 讀書할 時間이 업슴니다. 容恕하시지요.＜223＞
