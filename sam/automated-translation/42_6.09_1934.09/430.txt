近讀短評
						李孝石
Today I discovered an old postcard (葉書) that had been sitting on my school desk (書卓) for who knows how many days, and finding it a waste to throw away the reply card (返信紙), I write these few words (몃자). Lately, while idly reading through the literary sections (文藝欄) of Tokyo magazines (東京雜誌), I have been astonished by the development in the technique (手法) of the short story (短篇小說). Regarding technique (技術), I think it is almost world-class (世界的). In description (描寫), there is not a single wasted or insufficient character. It treads the peak (極致) of realism in technique (手法). I consider something like Kafū's (荷風) "Hikage no Hana" (「ひかげの花」) to be an outstanding work (出色의 作) of recent times. I tend to appreciate the way it adheres closely to reality (現實에 則하야), drawing things as they are plainly (淡淡히) without literary exaggeration (文學的 誇張), rather than intentionally adopting a literary pose (文學的 姿勢) and bending facts (事實) to fit a literary composition (文學的 構成). It seems I myself have only just recently come to understand literary realism (文學의 리알리즘).<247>

**Summary**

Yi Hyosŏk reflects on the advanced state of short story writing technique in contemporary Japanese magazines, praising its efficiency and realism. He specifically admires Nagai Kafū's "Hikage no Hana" for its straightforward portrayal of reality without literary artifice. The author concludes by noting his own recent appreciation and understanding of literary realism. From a historical perspective, this piece reveals the significant influence of Japanese literary trends, particularly realism, on prominent Korean intellectuals like Yi Hyosŏk during the colonial period. Historians might find his explicit admiration for Japanese technical prowess and his reference to a specific Japanese author and work indicative of the cultural dynamics and artistic standards being negotiated by Korean writers under Japanese rule.

학교 書卓우에 몃츨동안인지 묵은 葉書를 오늘에야 처음 發見하고 返信紙가 버리기 앗가워 몃자 적슴니다. 最近 漫然히 東京雜誌文藝欄을 通讀하면서 短篇小說의 手法의 發達에 놀나고 잇슴니다. 技術에 關한 한 거이 世界的이라고 생각함니다. 描寫에 한 字의 浪費도 不足도 업는 것임니다. 手法의 리알리즘의 極致를 것고 잇슴니다. 荷風의 「ひかげの花」갓튼 것은 근래의 出色의 作이라고 생각함니다. 일부러 文學的 姿勢를 지어 文學的 構成으로 事實을 굽히지 아니하고 現實에 則하야 잇는 그대로를 文學的 誇張이 업시 淡淡히 그려간 곳에 好感을 가지려 함니다. 내 自身 요사이에야 겨우 文學의 리알리쯤을 알게 된 모양 갓슴니다.＜247＞
