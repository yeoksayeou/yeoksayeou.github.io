A Stroll Down Record Street

The popularity of the record world is currently monopolized by P'yŏngyang kisaeng. Since Polydor's exclusive soprano singer Wang Su-bok appeared like a comet, Sŏnu Il-sŏn has also jumped out and is monopolizing popularity.

Sŏnu Il-sŏn has a good vocal volume, and the slight mix of humming carries a folk song sentiment, making her a young lady poised for further development. Some praise her as the "Katsutarō" (勝太郞) or "Ichimaru" (市丸) of Chosŏn.

As the Victor Arts Department became independent, it is fiercely advancing onto the scene (江湖), and recently, an Arts Department member appeared by the Taedonggang River and reportedly scouted Kim Pok-hŭi, a kisaeng from the Kisŏng Kwŏnbŏn (箕城卷番).

As a countermeasure to this, Columbia is said to have recruited Miss Yulnia, born in Chinnamp'o.
P'yŏngyang, the colorful hometown with drooping willows, now occupies the throne through the record world.

Okeh has now partnered with Teichiku (帝蓄), expanding further, and is striving greatly with a plan to sell even more than the 20,000-plus records sold each month.

Mr. Yi Sŏ-gu, the Arts Department Head at Cielon, is planning to provide high-quality records (譜盤) that even respectable households can enjoy listening to now, but what field should be pioneered? A genius mind needs to emerge for this.

Vocalist Hong Nan-p'a is exclusive to Victor, Mr. Hyŏn Che-myŏng is exclusive to Columbia, Chŏng Hun-mo and Sin Pu-ch'ul are exclusive to Okeh, Sŏnu Il-sŏn is exclusive to Polydor, and Cielon and T'aep'yŏng also each have influential figures under exclusive contract. By the way, regarding exclusive contracts, it's heard that depending on the company, an exclusive contract fee of 20 wŏn to 200 wŏn per month is paid.

Mr. Yi Sŏ-gu of Cielon is reportedly in the midst of great activity trying to build a recording studio (吹入所) in Chosŏn. Has a site already been secured? <79>

**Summary**

This 1934 article highlights the intense competition and trends in the Korean record industry during the colonial period. It notes the dominance of P'yŏngyang kisaeng singers like Wang Su-bok and Sŏnu Il-sŏn, comparing the latter to famous Japanese singers. Record companies like Victor, Columbia, Okeh, Polydor, Cielon, and T'aep'yŏng are actively scouting talent, forming partnerships (Okeh-Teichiku), and signing major Korean classical musicians (Hong Nan-p'a, Hyŏn Che-myŏng) and popular entertainers (Sin Pu-ch'ul) to exclusive contracts with varying monthly fees. Cielon aims to produce "high-quality" records for respectable homes and is planning to establish a local recording studio. For historians, this piece reveals the burgeoning popular music market, the central role of kisaeng entertainers, the structure of the colonial record business involving both Korean and Japanese companies/influences (Teichiku, comparisons to Japanese singers), the star system with exclusive contracts, and early efforts towards establishing music production infrastructure within Korea.

레코드街 散步
레코-드界의 人氣는 지금 平壤妓生들이 獨占하는 중이야 포리돌의 쏘푸라노 專屬歌手 王壽福이 헤성가치 나타난 뒤 또 鮮于一扇이 뛰여 나와 人氣를 獨占하는 판.

鮮于一扇이는 聲量이 조코 반쓱 콧노래 석긴 것이 民謠情調를 띄어 더 발전할 아가씨야 朝鮮의 「勝太郞」요 「市丸」라고 一部에서는 稱頌이만하.

삑타 文藝部가 獨立되면서 江湖에 무섭게 進出중인데 요지간 또 大同江가에 文藝部員이 나타나 箕城卷番의 妓生 金福熙를 뽑아갓다나
여기에 對策하여 컬넘비아에서는 또 鎭南浦出生의 율니아孃을 뽑아갓다고
수양버들 느러진 色鄕 平壤은 지금 레코-드界를 通하야 王座를 占領.

今番 오-케는 帝蓄과 結綠 더욱 擴大하고 每月 2萬餘枚 팔니든 것을 더 팔닐 안을 세우고 大熱奮中

시에론의 文藝部長李瑞求氏는 이제는 점잔은 家庭에서도 즐겨 드를 만한 高級의 譜盤을 提供하여야 하겟다고 벌느는데 엇는 방면을 開拓하여야 할가 天才的 머리가 나와야 할 일.

聲樂家 洪蘭坡 삑타-에 專屬, 玄濟明氏 콜넘비아에 專屬, 오-케-에 鄭勳模 申不出專屬 포리돌에 鮮于一扇專屬, 시에론 太平에도 모다 有力한 諸氏專屬 그런데 듯건대 專屬에 대하여는 各社에 따라 다르지만 每月 20圓 乃至 200圓式 專屬料를 준다고 한다.

시에론의 李瑞求氏, 朝鮮에 吹入所 만들고저 大活動中 벌서 기지까지 잡엇다든가.＜79＞
