**Body Text Translation**
Manchukuo Bulletin Board

How many children of Korean compatriots who have migrated to Manchuria are there, and what is the extent of the educational facilities for them? According to this magazine's investigation, there are 508,000 Korean children residing in Manchuria (在滿朝鮮人兒童), and the school facilities for these children are extremely few, showing the following figures.

Manchukuo's National Budget

Manchukuo (滿洲國), born on March 1, Shōwa 7 (1932), based on the ideals of building a Realm of the Kingly Way (王道樂土) and Harmony of the Five Races (五族協和), marks its 10th year this year (Shōwa 16 / 1941). Manchukuo's national budget, which was merely 110 million won at the time of its founding (建國當時), saw a great leap (大飛躍) to 12.5 billion won in the Shōwa 15 (1940) fiscal year.

Countries That Have Recognized Manchukuo

To date, a total of 8 countries have recognized Manchukuo: Japan (日本), Germany (獨逸), Italy (伊太利), Spain (西班牙), Poland (波蘭, although it currently has no country), Hungary (항가리), Slovakia (슬로바키아), and El Salvador (살바돌國).<55>

**Summary**

This short news bulletin from March 1941 reports on two aspects of Manchukuo: the situation of Korean children and the state's development. It states there are over half a million children of Korean migrants in Manchukuo but very few schools for them. It also highlights Manchukuo's 10th anniversary, touting its massive budget increase from 110 million won at its founding (1932) to 12.5 billion won by 1940. Finally, it lists the eight countries that had formally recognized Manchukuo by that time, primarily Axis powers and their allies. For historians, this piece is interesting as it reflects contemporary Japanese/colonial narratives promoting Manchukuo's growth ("Great Leap" in budget) while acknowledging, albeit starkly, the significant lack of educational infrastructure for the large Korean population that had migrated there. The list of recognizing nations clearly shows Manchukuo's geopolitical alignment just before the Pacific War escalated.

滿洲國回覽板
滿洲에 이주한 조선동포의 자제는 얼마나 되며, 및 그들을 위한 교육시설은 어느 정도에 이르렀는가. 이제 본사에서 조사한 바에 의하면, 在滿朝鮮人兒童은 50만 8천명이며 이들 아동을 위한 학교시설을 극히 소수이어서 다음과 같은 數字를 보여 주고 있다.

滿洲國의 國家經費

昭和 7년 3월 1일, 王道樂土의 건설과 五族協和의 이상을 기조로 탄생된 滿洲國은 금년(昭和 16년)으로써 10년을 마지하게 되었는데 滿洲國의 국가예산은 건국 당시에 1억 1천만원에 지나지 않었으나, 昭和 15년도에는 125억원이라는 大飛躍을 보게 되었다.

滿洲國을 承認한 나라

지금까지 滿洲國을 승인한 나라는 전부 8개국인데, 日本, 獨逸, 伊太利, 西班牙, 波蘭(현재는 나라가 없지만), 항가리, 슬로바키아, 살바돌國이라.<55>
