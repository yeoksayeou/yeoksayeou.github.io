Cut-out: Han Hongt'aek - Precautions When Visiting Tuberculosis Patients
						By Okabe Taku, Doctor of Medicine

When visiting someone suffering from lung or pleural disease, the thought of fearing infection tends to come first, and it's easy for the mind to focus solely on that point. However, since the purpose of making the effort to visit is out of kindness to meet and console the patient, one must be careful not to cause even the slightest hindrance to the patient's recuperation.

Time and Topics

Most tuberculosis patients experience a fever daily from around 2 PM until the evening. Conversation, while insignificant to a healthy person, constitutes considerable exertion for a patient, and this further induces fever. Therefore, one should avoid the times when fever is likely to occur. Ideally, visits should be between about 1 PM after lunch if in the afternoon, or between 10 AM and 11 AM if in the morning. Even among close acquaintances, care must be taken not to let the conversation time become too long.
Next, regarding the content of the conversation, since patients are generally nervous, one should refrain from shameless rambling such as talk that directly affects the patient's interests, talk that confuses the mind, or discussions that cause excitement even if interesting. Also, getting too close to the patient, shaking hands, disturbing the patient's rest, or providing triggers for fever, insomnia, etc., should be avoided.

Good Consolation Gifts

When intending to give some sort of consolation gift, one should choose something the patient would find most appreciated. For example, foodstuffs like sweets, fruits, and canned goods (鑵詰) are generally not good due to dietary restrictions. Fresh flowers are rather safe. While gift certificates might be good in cities, something that can be bought immediately regardless of whether in Korean rural areas (朝鮮農村地方) or cities, and is good for any patient, young or old, male or female, like "Wakamoto" (와까모도) (25-day supply for 1 Won 60 Chŏn), can truly be called a good consolation gift.

Success of Recuperation

This single preparation contains various nutrients, as if combining multiple nutritional supplements. Specifically, its comprehensive action—including a powerful Vitamin B complex that stimulates appetite, amino acids that prevent weakness, nuclein (누구레인) that increases white blood cells and enhances phagocytic action, lipase (리빠-제) that dissolves the capsule of the tubercle bacillus, abundant calcium (카루슘) which plays a major role in the deposition of tuberculosis lesions, and Vitamins A and D which enhance resistance to disease—establishes a new system for fighting illness and creates the foundation for successful recuperation.
Furthermore, due to what is medically termed the cell protoplasm activation effect (細胞原形質賦活作用), it fundamentally strengthens the function of the stomach and intestines. Consequently, common issues for tuberculosis patients like indigestion, gastrointestinal catarrh (胃腸가다루), and constipation (便秘) are easily prevented, the whole body's nutrition is enhanced, and resistance to disease is further strengthened, leading to the natural healing of tuberculosis symptoms. Moreover, there are no contraindications when used concurrently with medicine; rather, it enhances the effectiveness of the medicine. Therefore, even from a physician's standpoint, it can be recommended as a suitable consolation gift. <85>

**Summary**

This article advises visitors on etiquette when seeing tuberculosis patients during the colonial era. It recommends visiting during times when fever is less likely (mid-morning or early afternoon) and keeping visits short. Visitors should avoid sensitive or overly stimulating topics and physical contact. Regarding gifts, it discourages food items due to dietary restrictions and instead heavily promotes the Japanese product Wakamoto as an ideal, universally suitable gift that aids recovery by providing various nutrients and improving digestion, claiming it helps establish a "new system for fighting illness." From a historical perspective, the article is interesting as it blends seemingly practical public health advice with the overt promotion of a specific Japanese commercial product (Wakamoto) to a Korean audience, presenting it almost as a medical necessity endorsed by a doctor. This reflects Japanese commercial and cultural influence in colonial Korea, framing consumption of Japanese goods as part of modern health practices.

컷트 韓弘澤-結核患者를 訪問할 때 注意
						醫學博士 岡部卓
肺나 肋膜을 알는사람은 訪問할때는 어찟든 그 感染을 두러려워하는 生覺이앞서 마음으론 그 點에만 注意를 두기쉬운것입니다만, 모처럼 訪問함의 뜻은 病人을 만나 慰勞해주려는 親切心에 있는터이니까 患者의 療養上 조금치라도 障害가되지 않도록 注意를하서야 할것입니다.

時間과 話題

大部分의 肺病患者는 每日 午後二時頃붙어 저녁때에 걸처 熱이납니다. 談話같은 것은 健康한 사람에는 아무렇지도 아는것이지만 患者에있어는 相當히 運動을한것이되어 이것이 發熱을 一層誘發하는것입니다. 그러므로 發熱하기쉬운 時間은 避하여 午後이면 晝食後一時쯤부터 午前이면 十時붙어 十一時頃까지의 사이가 가장 理想的이고 談話의 時間도 넘우길지안케 親한사리라도 注意하서야합니다.
다음에 談話의 內容도 患者는 大槪 神經質이니까 直接患者에 利害關係가 믿치는말 머리를 어즈럽게하는말 假令滋味스럽드래도 興奮을 일으키는 論議등의 念恥업는 放談이라든가 넘우 患者에 接近하여 握手라든가 患者의 安靜을 妨害하며 發熱 不眠等의 動機를 주는 것은 삼가서야 할것입니다.

좋은 慰問品

무슨 慰問品같은 것을 드리려할때에도 가장 患者가 고마웁게 알 것을 選擇하서야할것입니다. 이를테이면 菓子, 果實, 鑵詰等의 食料品類는 食事이 制限등 關係로 좋지못한편이고 生花등은 無難한편이며 都會에서는 商品券같은것도 좋지만 朝鮮農村地方이나 都會를 不問하고 卽時 살수 있고 老幼男女엇더한 患者에도 죠혼『와까모도』(二十五日量一圓六十錢)같은 것은 實로좋은 慰問品이라할 수 있습니다.

療養의 成功

이것은 一劑로서 여러 가지 營養劑를 兼한 거와같이 各種의 營養素를 含有하고있습니다 卽食慾을 增進하는 强力한비타민B複合體 衰弱을 막는아미노酸 白血球를 增殖하여 喰菌作用을 높이는누구레인 結核菌의 被膜을 녹이는리빠-제 結核病所의 沈着에 큰 役割을하는카루슘이 豊富한外 抗病力을 높이는비타민A와D등의 綜合作用으로서 抗病의 新體制를 確立하여 療養成功의 基礎를 만듭니다.
또 醫學上 細胞原形質賦活作用이라고하여 胃腸의 機能을 根本的으로 强化하는 作用이 잇음으로 結核患者에많은 消化不良 胃腸가다루 便秘같은 것은 쉽게 防止되어 全身의 榮養은 增進되고 抗病力은 一層 强化됨으로 結核症狀은 自然히 治癒됩니다. 또 醫藥과 倂用하여 禁忌가없을뿐 아니라 오히려 醫藥의 效果를 助長함으로 醫師의 立場으로서도 慰問品으로서 適當함으로 勸할수있습니다.＜85＞
