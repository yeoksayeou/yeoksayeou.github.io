Brief History of Theater Troupe Gohyeop

In February, Showa 14 (1939), the theater troupe Gohyeop (高協) was formed, centered around Chu In'gyu, Pak Ch'anghwan, Sim Yŏng, Yu Hyŏn, Yi Chaehyŏn, and several others who were members of the filming crew for "Pokchi Malli" (福地萬里), produced by the Koryŏ Film Association (高麗映畵協會).
The goal of our stage activities is to perform the most progressive and developmental plays.
The troupe's financial management and operations are conducted independently.
On March 1st of the same year, Showa 14 (1939), to establish a financial foundation for the troupe, we first held provincial performances, opening the inaugural performance with "K'waegŏl Ŭi" (快傑읭) at the Wŏnsan'gwan in Wŏnsan.

The effort, passion, and cooperation of all twenty-two members are Gohyeop's greatest assets.

Through 40 days of provincial performances in North Hamgyŏng Province (北鮮), we established a complete foundation both theatrically and financially, and solidified our organization.

In May, Showa 14 (1939), Gohyeop Village (高協村) was established through the special favor (特志) of Mr. Han Haksu. Communal living <184> swept away (一消) our previously scattered and unhealthy lives.

"Good theater comes from good living."
The growth of Gohyeop Village is directly proportional to the growth of Gohyeop. We weigh life and the stage on the same scale.

In March, Showa 15 (1940), we held "Ch'unhyangjŏn" (春香傳) as our first-anniversary performance and, with the support of masters Yi Yŏsŏng and Yu Chahu, opened the "Ch'unhyangjŏn Exhibition" at Hwasin Gallery, drawing 200,000 visitors over one week.

In May of the same year, Showa 15 (1940), aiming for comfort performances for the Imperial Army (皇軍慰問) and the continental advance (大陸進出) of new Chosŏn theater, we held performances in North China (北支). This was a first for a Chosŏn theater troupe.

In July of the same year, Showa 15 (1940), starting with the east coast, we toured fishing and farming villages in North and South Hamgyŏng Provinces (咸南北), performing on makeshift stages in places without theaters. This is the sincerity of Gohyeop, aiming for the thorough implementation of serving the nation through theater (演劇報國).

In September of the same year, Showa 15 (1940), we spent 10,000 won on production costs to stage "Muyŏngt'ap" (無影塔). It was a landmark event for Chosŏn classical theater, paying meticulous attention to costumes, props, and stage design (裝置). <185>

**Summary**

This article from March 1941 outlines the history, structure, and activities of the Korean theater troupe Gohyeop (高協), founded in February 1939. Penned by its general manager, Sim Yŏng, it highlights the troupe's founding principles of progressive theater and financial independence, its establishment through provincial tours, the creation of a communal living space ("Gohyeop Village"), and key performances like "Ch'unhyangjŏn" and "Muyŏngt'ap." It also details the troupe's personnel structure as of early 1941, including actors, staff, guest members, award judges, and advisors. For historians, the article is significant as it showcases a Korean cultural organization navigating the late colonial period. It reveals efforts towards self-sufficiency ("Gohyeop Village"), the performance of Korean classics ("Ch'unhyangjŏn," "Muyŏngt'ap") alongside activities mandated or encouraged by the colonial regime, such as "comfort performances for the Imperial Army" (皇軍慰問) and promoting "serving the nation through theater" (演劇報國), illustrating the complex blend of cultural assertion and political accommodation characteristic of the era. The listing of prominent literary and cultural figures involved as members, judges, or advisors further maps the network of intellectuals operating under Japanese rule.

劇團 高協略史

昭和 14年 2월, 高麗映畵協會 제작 「福地萬里」 撮影멤버이든 朱仁奎 朴昌煥 沈影 柳玄
李載玄 외 몇 사람의 중심으로 극단 高協을 결성함.
가장 진취적인 발전적인 연극을 할 것을 무대행동의 목표로 한다.
극단 經理방법과 및 운행을 자주적으로 한다.
同 昭和 14年 3월 1일 극단 經理의 지반을 갖이기 위해 지방공연을 먼저 갖이고 元山 원산관에서 「快傑읭」으로 창립 공연의 막을 열었다.

전원 스물 두사람의 노력과 정열과, 협조는 高協의 최대의 미천이다

北鮮 40일 지방공연으로 우리는 연극으로 경리로 완전한 토대를 갖었으며 그 조직을 공고히 했다.

昭和 14年 5월, 韓學洙씨의 特志로 高協村을 건설했다. 집단생활은 우리들의 <184> 잇때 산만하고 불건강했든 생활을 一消했다.

「좋은 연극은 좋은 생활에서부터」
高協村의 장성은 高協의 장성의 正比例한다. 생활과 무대를 우리는 한 저울에 단다.

昭和 15年 3월, 「春香傳」을 1주년기념공연으로 갖이며 李如星 柳子厚선생의 후원으로 화신갸라리에 「春香傳展覽會」를 열어, 1주일간 관람객 20만을 동원했다.

同 15년 5월, 皇軍위문과 朝鮮신극의 대륙진출을 꾀하고 北支공연을 갖었다. 이것은 朝鮮극단으로서 嚆矢이다.

同 15년 7월 동해안을 위시해 咸南北, 어촌, 농촌에 극장 없는 곳에 가설무대 순회공연을 갖었다. 演劇報國의 徹底를 기한 高協의 성의다.

同 15년 9월 공연비 1만원을 듸려 「無影塔」을 상연했다. 衣裳, 소도구, 裝置 등에 충실을 다해 한 朝鮮고전극의 획기적 擧事였다. <185>
