**Body Text Translation:**
On the Publication of the "Special Theater Issue," We Expect the Advancement of the Korean Theater Association, Commemorating the Formation of the Korean Theater Association, Special Feature: "Theater and the New Order"

At this time when all 23 million people are vigorously embarking on the path toward the New Order (新體制) as one, the "Korean Theater Association" (朝鮮演劇協會) was founded on December 22nd at the Kyŏngsŏng Civic Hall (京城府民舘), through the total consolidation of theater personalities, particularly in the field of "Theater," which holds affection and influence among the greatest number of the populace among cultural spheres. We publish this special feature with the intention of blessing the lively march of the Korean theater world along the lines of the New Order. —

Association's Aims and Objectives

It aims to contribute to cultural advancement by planning the sound development of theater and the improvement of the qualities of theater professionals, and concurrently expects to achieve the reality of Japan-Korea Unification (內鮮一體, Naisen Ittai) through theater, further carrying out the following activities: 1). Guidance and control (統制) of theater 2). Facilities related to the training (養成) and guidance of theater professionals 3). Guidance and improvement regarding provincial tours (地方巡業) 4). Commendation (表彰) of meritorious members, publication of an organ journal (機關紙), and others.
Members consist of Regular Members, Special Members, and Supporting Members:
I. Regular Members: Playwrights and members belonging to theater troupes, directors, actors, and others engaged professionally in acting, technical skills, or administration.
II. Special Members: Those who agree with the aims of this Association and offer assistance.
III. Supporting Members: Those with special merit concerning the Association's activities or possessing scholarly knowledge and experience. <158>

For the Commemorative Performances

Furthermore, from December 27th, commemorative performances by various groups were held and received great acclaim in various locations. **Summary**

This 1941 article from *Samch'ŏlli* magazine announces the publication of a special theater issue celebrating the recent formation of the Korean Theater Association (朝鮮演劇協會) on December 22nd (likely 1940, given publication date). It explicitly states the association's establishment aligns with the Japanese colonial "New Order" (新體制) and aims to contribute to cultural advancement, improve theater professionals, and crucially, achieve the "reality of Japan-Korea Unification" (內鮮一體, Naisen Ittai) through theater. The article details the association's structure, objectives (including guidance and control of theater), membership types, leadership (listing prominent figures, many with both Japanese and Korean names), affiliated theater troupes with their founding dates, representatives, key members, and office locations, as well as listing recent commemorative performances.

From a historical perspective, the article is significant as it provides a clear example of the co-option and reorganization of Korean cultural fields under the intensified assimilation policies of wartime Japan. The explicit linking of theater development to the "New Order" and "Naisen Ittai" highlights the political instrumentalization of culture. The listing of prominent theater figures, many using Japanese names (창씨개명, Sōshi-kaimei), alongside their Korean names, and the detailed roster of troupes affiliated with this state-sanctioned body, offer valuable data for historians studying cultural policy, collaboration, and the performing arts during the late colonial period.

2,300萬 民衆이 모도다 한글같이 新體制의 길을 향하여 활발하게 一路發足하는 이때에 있어 가장 다수한 민중에게 친애와 영향력을 가진 文化部面 중 특히 「演劇」視野에 있어 이번에 劇界人士의 總結合으로 「朝鮮演劇協會」가, 12월 22일 京城府民舘에서 創立되였다. 吾人은 新體制의 線에 沿하야 朝鮮演劇界가 活潑한 行進을 축복하는 뜻에서 이 특집을 발간하노라.―

協會趣旨目的

연극의 건전한 발달과 연극인의 자질향상을 圖하여서 문화향상에 기여함으로써 목적하며 아울러 연극을 통하여 內鮮一體의 實을 擧하기를 期하며 더욱 左의 사업을 行한다. 1). 연극의 지도, 並 統制 2). 연극인의 養成 並 指導에 關한 施設 3). 地方巡業에 대한 指導改善 4).  有功會員의 表彰, 機關紙의 발행, 其他.
회원은 正會員, 特別會員, 贊助會員인 바
一. 正會員 脚本作家 及 演劇團 所屬員, 演出家, 俳優 其他演技, 技術 又는 사무를 業으로 하는 者.
二. 特別會員 本會의 趣旨에 贊同하며 援助를 與하는 者.
三. 贊助會員 本會 사업에 대하여 특히 功勞있는 者 又는 學識 經驗을 有한 者. <158>
