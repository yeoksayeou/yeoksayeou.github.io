Astonishing Debt of 100 Million Won

Among the small farmers and small merchants of Chosŏn (조선), it is rare to find someone who does not owe a debt to the Financial Cooperatives (金融組合). Whether mortgaging a humble six-kan thatched house (六間斗屋) or mortgaging farmland (田土), many are borrowing heavily from the Cooperatives. Now, looking at the total debt as of the end of May (5月末日), it has surpassed a total of 100 million Won (一億圓). This means they are carrying 100 million Won in debt from these semi-governmental lending institutions (半官設 貸金業機關), and the interest alone, calculated at ten percent per year (년 一割), amounts to 10 million Won. How astonishing is this?

The number of Cooperatives, combining cities and villages, is 621. This is an increase of 24 cooperatives compared to the same period last year, and an increase of 47 cooperatives compared to the same period two years ago.

The number of cooperative members is 594,231, an increase of 55,283 people compared to the same period last year, and an increase of 92,113 people compared to the same period two years ago.

The total amount loaned (貸付高) is truly 100,016,080 Won (1억 1,608圓). This is an increase of 15,511,000 Won compared to the same period the previous year (前年 同期), and an increase of 21,421,000 Won compared to the same period two years ago.

The average loan amount per cooperative member is 185 Won, which is an increase of 10 Won compared to the same period the previous year, and an increase of 9 Won compared to the same period two years ago. <59>

**Summary**

This 1930 news report from the magazine *Samch'ŏlli* highlights the rapidly increasing debt burden carried by Korean small farmers and merchants, owed primarily to Financial Cooperatives (金融組合). Total debt surpassed 100 million Won by May 1930, with significant year-over-year increases in the number of cooperatives, members, total loan amounts, and average debt per member. The article expresses astonishment at the scale of this indebtedness and the associated interest burden, estimated at 10 million Won annually. For historians, this article provides quantitative data on the economic distress and growing dependency on semi-governmental lending institutions in colonial Korea during a period of economic hardship. The figures illustrate the penetration of these financial entities into rural and urban society and the scale of economic pressure faced by ordinary Koreans.

놀라운 一億圓의 負債
조선의 소농민, 소상인들로 金融組合에 채무를 아니진 분이 드물다. 혹은 六間斗屋을 잡히고 혹은 田土를 잡히고서 組合에서 빗을 만히 어더쓰고 잇는 터인데 이제 5月末日 현재의 채무총액을 보면 모다 一億圓을 돌파하엿다. 즉 半官設 貸金業機關으로부터 一億圓의 빗을 지어 쓰고 잇는 셈이니 그 이자만 년 一割 잡어도 一千萬圓이다. 얼마나 놀라운 일인가 즉
組合數는 도시와 촌락을 합하야 621조합 작년 同期에 비하야 24조합 재작년 同期에 비하야 47조합움˜ 증가이다.
조합원은 594,231人이니 작년 同期에 비하야 55,283人 재작년 同期에 비하야 92,113人의 증가이다.
貸付高는 실로 1억 1,608圓이니 前年 同期에 비하야 1,551만 1천圓 재작년 同期에 비하야 2,142萬 1千圓의 증가이다.
조합원 一人 평균 貸付高는 185원이니 前年 同期에 비하면 10원 재작년 同期에 비하야 9원의 증가이다.＜59＞
