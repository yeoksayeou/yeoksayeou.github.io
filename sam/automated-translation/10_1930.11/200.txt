**Body Text Translation**
Lighting Up a Cigarette

If only one didn't see, didn't hear, didn't speak, one might have already attained Buddhahood, but because this world is fundamentally one where Buddhas cannot live, let's try speaking of things as we see and hear them.

The Osŏng School (五星學校), deeply connected with figures like Yi Tong-hwi (李東輝), Yu Tong-yŏl (柳東悅), Yi Kap (李甲), and Yi Chong-ho (李鍾浩), was closed down around the time of the March First Movement (己未前後), and the Hyŏpsŏng Industrial School (協成實業學校) has moved into its building, currently in the process of organizing as a foundation with 300,000 won. However, they say they are soliciting funds only from people of the Western Provinces (西道人士); is this a case of 'same price, red skirt' (同價紅裳格)? A school is a well dug at a crossroads; the residents of all four villages—east, west, south, north—should draw water from it equally. Beat the drum! To break down regional fervor—

The Chungoe Ilbo (中外日報) has suspended publication. Although the sheets of newspaper spewing from the rotary press have been cut off, is there any reason its principles should be cut off? However, few know what "Chungoe Ilbo-ism" is. What has it been doing for several years?

Money can make one a president, and the talent (才操) for gathering Japanese advertisements <57> can make one a key executive, but alas (嗟乎), money and talent are ultimately not expressions of character.
Oh, you many presidents, vice presidents, and executives of the human world! Don't think your character shines upon the world; know that it's the few pennies your father earned like a miracle that emits the radiance. Late awakening is a disaster (禍哉)!

It's unsightly, it's unsightly—there's nothing I hate seeing more than the "so-called gentlemen" (曰紳士) who hire rickshaws and ride around the main streets of Chang'an (長安大道) bobbing their heads. One fellow pulls from below the feet, and one fellow sits supported above the feet. Hey, there should be limits even to class confrontation.
Indeed, the people riding in rickshaws all look like antiques. If not, is the person in the rickshaw a coffin loaded onto a funeral bier (상여에 실닌 관짝)? Mr. Song (宋某), Mr. Sin (申某), Mr. Pak (朴某), and others who hire first-class rickshaws day and night (밤낫) and ride around bobbing their heads—get down quickly!

"The county magistrate of Kilchu (吉州) banned the youth association's athletic meet. He must have wanted to become the Provincial Governor (道長官)." "The Government-General (총독부) distributed an errata sheet for textbooks. They should distribute a governance errata sheet too." The former is a famous line published in the rambling column of some paper 7 or 8 years ago, the latter more recently. Writers who pen such memorable phrases have become rare these days.

It's written in "History of Korean Rule Seen from Behind" (裏から見た朝鮮統治史) that the corruption case involving former Governor-General Yamanashi (山梨) was first exposed to the judiciary (司直) through a fight over a certain beauty (美姬) from Chin'gogae named Tamijijo (多美之助). There are surprising histories of rule indeed.

It is said that 400,000 won was paid to Song Pyŏng-jun (宋秉畯) by Hasegawa Yoshimichi (長谷川好道), the Commander <58> of the駐韓司令官 (Japanese forces stationed in Korea) at the time, to manipulate the Ilchinhoe (一進會). Of course, it was secret funds (機密費).

Reading the book "Recent Russia" (최근의 露西亞) written by Oze Keishi (尾瀨敬止), it seems that the lives of writers there are not affluent either. It says there are only 25 novelists or poets making a decent living in Moscow (莫斯科) and 45 in all of Russia (全露西亞). Although the rest might be much better off than us, the manuscript fee there is 200 Rubles for 16 pages of 4x6 format. For the poet Mayakovsky (마야 콥스키), poems are 3 Rubles per line.

If a fly falls into a beer mug, an Englishman throws away the fly and pours the beer into another mug; a Frenchman throws away both the drink and the mug; a German gently flicks the fly out and drinks as is; a Russian gulps it down with the fly still in it. This is a comparison of the national character of each country; where would Korean people (조선 사람) fit in? As for me, I envy the German.

Where is Silch'ŏn Women's School (實踐女學校) going? Lonely butterfly (胡蝶), no matter how weary you are, do not alight on a poisonous flower.

There are many requests to know the actual circulation numbers of each newspaper in the city, and while it's hard to say exactly, you wouldn't be too far off knowing that Tong-a (東亞) and Chosŏn (朝鮮) average 2,000 to 3,000 copies per province, and Chungoe (中外) is around 1,000 copies. (Recorded by Ch'obyŏngjŏng 草兵丁) <59>

**Summary**

This article is a collection of critical observations on contemporary Korean society and events in late 1930. The author laments the closure of nationalist-linked schools, the suspension of the Chungoe Ilbo newspaper, the perceived arrogance of the wealthy elite riding rickshaws, and the decline of sharp social commentary in the press. It touches upon historical scandals involving Japanese officials and Korean collaborators, compares the meager earnings of Russian writers to implied worse conditions for Korean writers, uses an anecdote about national reactions to a fly in beer to ponder Korean character, expresses concern for the Silch'ŏn Women's School, and concludes with estimated circulation figures for major newspapers. The piece offers historians glimpses into social stratification, media landscape (including circulation numbers), colonial-era scandals, nationalist sentiments (regarding schools and regionalism), and everyday observations reflecting the socio-political climate of 1930s Korea under Japanese rule.

담배 한 대 피어 물고
보지 말고, 듯지 말고, 말하지 말엇스면 벌서 成佛이라도 하엿스련만 이 세상이 원악 붓처님은 못살게 된 세상이기에 이제 보는대로 듯는대로 말하여 보기로 하자.
李東輝, 柳東悅, 李甲, 李鍾浩씨 등과 인연이 깁흔 저 五星學校는 己未前後에 마서지고 그 校舍에는 協成實業學校가 비러드러 방금 30만원의 재단법인으로 조직하여 가는 中. 그런데 그 자금을 西道人士에게만 청한다 하니 同價紅裳格인가 학교는 네거리에 판 움물이라 동서남북 四村住民이 골고로 길어 먹게 할 일, 북을 울려라. 地方熱 打破의-
中外日報는 휴간하엿다. 輪轉機로부터 쏘다저 나오든 신문조히는 비록 끈처젓스나 그 主義야 끈처질 理 잇나? 그러나 『中外日報이즘』이 무엇인지 아는 이 드물다. 몃 해를 무엇하엿노?
돈이 사장은 될 수 잇고 일본광고＜57＞ 모으는 才操가 중요 간부는 될 수 잇지만은 嗟乎 돈과 才操 필경 인격의 표현이 아닌 걸
人世의 만흔 사장 부사장 중역들이여 당신네의 인격이 세상에 빗나는 줄 아예 알지 말고 아비가 기적가치 벌어 노앗든 그 돈푼이 광채를 발하는 줄 아시오. 晩覺은 禍哉
눈꼴이 틀닌다 틀닌다 하니 인력거 잡숩고 長安大道 고개짓하며 다니는 『曰紳士』가치 보기시른 것이 업다. 한 놈은 脚下에서 끄을고 한 놈은 脚上에서 버틔고 에라 계급대립도 분수가 잇지.
아닌게 아니라 人力車上人은 모다 골동품가치 보인다. 그러치 안으면 車上人이란 상여에 실닌 관짝인가. 宋某 申某 朴某 등 밤낫 인력거 일등 잡숩고 고개짓하며 다니는 이여. 어서 냉큼 내려라.
『吉州군수가 청년회의 운동회를 금지하엿다. 道長官이 되고 십든게지』 『총독부에서 교과서의 正誤表를 돌렷다. 시정정오표도 돌닐 일』 전자는 7, 8년 전에 후자는 최근에 某紙의 횡설수설란에 실닌 명구다. 이러케 기억에 남는 글을 쓰는 이 근래엔 드물어 젓구나.
山梨 前總督의 疑獄事件도 처음에는 多美之助라는 진고개 엇든 美姬의 爭奪戰으로부터 司直에 발각이 되엇다고. 『裏から見た朝鮮統治史』에 씨여 잇다. 놀라운 統治史도 잇다.
一進會를 조종하기 위하여 당시 駐韓司令官長＜58＞ 谷川好道로부터 宋秉畯에게 40만원이 지급되엿섯다 한다. 물론 機密費로 말이지.
尾瀨敬止가 쓴 『최근의 露西亞』란 책을 보면 거기서도 文士의 생활은 有餘치 못한 모양으로 소설가나 시인으로 상당한 生計를 하는 자 莫斯科에 25인 全露西亞에 45인뿐이라고 그 남어지 사람도 우리보다는 만히 낫겟지만은 그곳 稿料는 4,6版 16枚에 200留. 詩人 마야 콥스키-詩는 1行에 3留式.
영국사람은 맥주잔 속에 파리가 바젓스면 그 파리를 버리고 다른 맥주 잔에 따라마시며 佛國人은 술과 잔을 모다 동댕이 치며 독일인은 파리만 살작 버리고 그대로 마시며 露國人은 파리빠진 채로 그냥 드리킨다. 이것은 各國 各民性의 비교어니와 조선 사람은 어듸에 해당할는고. 나만은 독일인이 부럽다.
實踐女學校는 어대로 가나. 외로운 胡蝶아 아모리 고단하더래도 毒잇는 꼿 우엔 안지 마라.
시내 각신문사의 發賣實部數를 좀 알어달나는 요구가 만으나 참아 엇더케 말하랴만 대략 東亞와 朝鮮은 每道 평균 2,000내지 3,000部요. 中外는 1,000부 내외인 줄 알면 과히 틀니지 안을 걸.(草兵丁 記)＜59＞
