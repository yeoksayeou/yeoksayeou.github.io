Article Title: 海岸 (押韻) (Coast (Rhyme))
Author: 金岸曙
Article Type: 시 (Poem)

Coast
						Kim Ansŏ

One
O you who came out, having depicted a sorrowful scene,
Rolling up all of Wŏnsan last night,
Today, embracing the sands of Changjŏn,
How lovely is the heart of your splashing song.

Two
A frog sits alone in the muddy water,
Croaking, croaking, its song full of excitement,
As if treasuring the bright stars in the sky,
They reflect in the murky water, watching over it.

Three
The way the cicada cries out, *maem maem*,
The excitement welling up as the cricket chirps, *sseureuram*,
It is all quite stylish, all pleasant to hear,
O you, sing out your boiling heart!

Four
Lonely stands the willow tree, born by the riverside,
Its shadow reflected upon the water,
As the autumn wind whistles and whips around its body,
Does it mean even my beloved has left? Where is the path to go?
―In 22 an, Auguslo. 1930―<62>

**Summary**

This poem by Kim Ansŏ uses imagery from the Korean coast (Wŏnsan, Changjŏn) and nature (frogs, cicadas, crickets, willow trees) to evoke sounds and moods. It moves from the splashing waves to the croaking frog and chirping insects, urging an expression of "boiling" emotions. The final stanza introduces a tone of loneliness and loss, questioning the departure of a loved one as the autumn wind blows. For historians, the poem offers insight into the artistic expression and prevailing sentiments among Korean intellectuals during the colonial period. While not overtly political, its focus on Korean landscapes (Wŏnsan, Changjŏn) and the expression of personal, somewhat melancholic emotions in the Korean language, using distinctively Korean natural imagery (like specific insect sounds), represents a quiet assertion of cultural identity and feeling within the constraints of the era. The use of older Korean orthography is also of linguistic interest.

海岸
						金岸曙
一
어제밤은 元山을 모다뒤말아
설은 景 그러놋코 나온 이들아,
오늘이라 長箭선 모래를 안고
찰래~네노래 맘씨곱구나.

二
구적물에 개고리 혼자들안자
으앙으앙 제노래 興이 놉흘제,
하늘이라 밝은 별 貴해함인지
흙물에 어려들며 직혀를주네.

三
매암이 매암매암 울부짓는양
쓰르람이 쓰르람 소사나는 흥,
格이로세 모도다 듯기 죠흔양
그대여, 노래하라 둬끌는 心情.

四
외롭다 버드나무 江까에나서
물우에다 그림자 어리고섯네,
갈바람은 우수수 몸을 휘들제
님조차 진단말가 갈길이 어데.
―In 22 an, Auguslo. 1930―＜62＞
