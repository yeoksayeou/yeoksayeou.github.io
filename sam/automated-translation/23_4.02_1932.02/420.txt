Magazine Title: 삼천리 제4권 제2호
Publication Date: 1932년 02월 01일
Article Title: 新春有感 (Thoughts on the New Spring)
Author: 영업국 (Business Department)
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editorial Postscript)

It is spring already. Inner shoots sprout on the burnt grass, and the frozen river melts and flows; surely the ninety days of spring scenery (90春光) have come to visit the mountains and rivers of the peninsula (半島山河).

The major collection of essays published by our company (本社), "Peace and Freedom" (平和와 自由), is indeed selling surprisingly well with the absolute welcome (絶大한 歡迎) of the public (江湖). This is a book our company proudly sells. Also, "My Korean Peninsula" (나의 半島山河), which was previously announced, will be published in the near future. We hope for your fond readership (愛讀). (Business Department) <112>

**Summary**

This short editorial postscript from the *Samch'ŏlli* magazine's business department celebrates the arrival of spring in Korea and promotes two publications. It announces the successful sales of a collection titled "Peace and Freedom" and the upcoming release of another book, "My Korean Peninsula." For historians, the piece offers a glimpse into the publishing activities and commercial strategies of a Korean magazine during the colonial period. The titles "Peace and Freedom" and especially "My Korean Peninsula" (나의 반도산하) might be of interest as potentially coded expressions of national sentiment or identity within the constraints of colonial censorship.
