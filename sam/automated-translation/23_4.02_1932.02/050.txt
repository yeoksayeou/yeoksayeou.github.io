**Body Text Translation**
Company Announcement

Regarding the discussion on abolishing factionalism and regional fervor, we announce that we regrettably could not publish the long, 57-page manuscript by Mr. Wŏn Sehun (元世勳), which historically discussed factionalism, along with manuscripts from several other authors.

Samch'ŏlli Company Editorial Department <11>

**Summary**

This brief notice from the Samch'ŏlli magazine's editorial department informs readers that several manuscripts, including a lengthy 57-page one by Wŏn Sehun discussing the history of factionalism, could not be published in the current issue focused on "abolishing factionalism and regional fervor." For historians, this notice is interesting as it points to editorial decisions possibly influenced by the sensitivity of discussing internal Korean divisions like factionalism (파쟁) and regionalism (지방열) during the colonial period. It hints at the constraints or self-censorship magazines faced when dealing with potentially controversial socio-political topics.
