交叉點
Intersection

If there is anything anyone wishes to ask someone, please submit it to this column. Our magazine's standard-bearer (旗手) will forward the letter to the recipient and publish their reply in this column.
1. Use a postcard for the paper.
2. Keep the meaning concise.
3. The content should relate to matters of public social interest.

To Master Ch'unwŏn (春園):
I hear that you sent your son to some commercial school in P'yŏngyang. Why did you choose a commercial school? Please instruct me on the reason, and also, when educating children, what method should be used to select a school? Regardless of its character, considering the current situation in Chosŏn, what kind of school would be best to choose? Also, should one choose between public and private (公私立)?
(Respectfully from Kim Chong-sŏ (金鍾瑞) of Mungyŏng (聞慶))

He chose the commercial school in P'yŏngyang of his own free will. I merely considered his health and my financial capacity (資産能力) and only suggested, how about attending an agricultural school?
I think that the education of children cannot help but be focused on "making a living" (벌어먹기 위주), but I also think one cannot force them to choose any particular field. I can only state my opinion and hope as a reference and let them choose according to their own wishes, as far as financial resources (資力) allow.
(Yi Kwangsu (李光洙))

Are there no publishing houses in Chosŏn that issue lecture notes for Girls' Higher Common Schools (女子高等普通學校)?
If there are, please inform me (下敎).

In Chosŏn, there are currently no places that publish lecture notes at the Girls' Higher Common School level.
(Reply from Ch'obyŏngjŏng (草兵丁))

To Master Chŏng Sŏk-t'ae (鄭錫泰):
My face has many pimples and looks unsightly. Is there any medicine I can take orally to completely cure it (全治)? Please instruct me (下敎) on a method for a complete cure. I wish for this inquiry to be written in the Samch'ŏlli magazine.
(From a reader in Taegu (大邱))

Greetings. Pimples appearing according to age are necessary when they occur, so if it's not excessive, it is natural to leave them as they are.
However, in severe cases or when they never seem to end, there are various medicines and injections available, but for home use, trying a powdered medicine (散藥) called "Edainogisil" (에다이노기씰) might not be unrelated [to a solution].
(Respectfully from Chŏng Sŏk-t'ae (鄭錫泰))

I congratulate you on your prosperity. Regarding the special sale notice section in your esteemed magazine this time, please reply in detail whether the short story collection by Ch'unwŏn (春園) contains the piece titled Hyŏlsŏ (血書). If so, I plan to order it (注文 爲計), so I greatly hope (千萬大望) for your guidance without fail (忘旁).
If perhaps it is not in the collection (集中), please also inform me whether Ch'unwŏn's novel Hyŏlsŏ is available for sale.
(I saw Hyŏlsŏ in some student magazine)
Kim Ho-ch'ang (金鎬昌) from Pukch'ŏng (北靑)

"Hyŏlsŏ" (血書) is in the short story collection titled "Chŏlmŭn Kkum" (졂은 꿈 - Young Dream), published by Pakmunsŏgwan (博文書舘).
Yi Kwangsu (李光洙)

To Master Kim Yŏng-hwan (金永煥):
I also have a lot of interest (취미) in music, but I cannot become proficient like others as I wish. What should I do to become good? Also, what is your address (주소)?

Receive instruction from a good teacher and practice a lot.
(Kim Yŏng-hwan (金永煥))

Home Inquiry Form (家庭質問書)
I am currently a 20-year-old wife, and there is a serious divorce issue. Being originally an old-fashioned woman (古女性), I am unenlightened about academics. I barely know our national script (언문 - ŏnmun, i.e., Hangul). I married 2 years ago; the groom was 16 and I was 18. The legal marriage law (法律婚法) requires 17 [for males] and 15 [for females]. He wants to divorce ignorant me. Moreover, our hearts do not match. We are not registered in the family register (戶籍) and are propertyless (無産). Can I claim alimony (慰藉料)?
(From Pae Chin-hŭi (裵鎭姬))

Reply... If the marriage registration (婚姻 屆出) was not filed in the family register, then the marriage is not legally established, so it cannot be a matter of divorce. If the man does not comply with the marriage registration, you can claim damages (損害賠償) for breach of the marriage promise and alimony (慰藉料) for violation of chastity (貞操蹂躪).
(Lawyer Kim Pyŏngno (金炳魯))<109>

**Summary**

This article is a Q&A column from the February 1932 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine, where readers pose questions to experts or public figures via the magazine. Questions cover topics like choosing schools for children (addressed to Yi Kwangsu), the availability of educational materials for girls, remedies for acne (addressed to Chŏng Sŏk-t'ae), locating a specific short story by Yi Kwangsu, advice on learning music (addressed to Kim Yŏng-hwan), and legal advice regarding an unregistered marriage and potential separation (addressed to lawyer Kim Pyŏngno). The responses provide practical advice, information, and legal counsel. For historians, this column offers valuable insights into the everyday concerns of Koreans during the colonial period, including anxieties about education pathways (commercial vs. agricultural), access to resources (lecture notes), personal health, literary interests, and navigating legal rights, particularly for women in complex marital situations under the Japanese legal framework applied in Chosŏn. The interaction between readers and prominent figures like Yi Kwangsu and Kim Pyŏngno highlights the role of magazines as platforms for public discourse and guidance.

1. 용지는 엽서에
2. 의미는 간단하게
3. 내용은 사회공중에 관계되는 것

春園선생에게
선생은 자제를 평양 엇든 상업학교에를 보내시엿다구요. 엇지하야 상업교를 택하시엿슴니까? 그 이유를 교시하는 동시에 자녀를 공부식힘에 학교는 엇더한 방법으로 택하여 그 성격을 불문하고 현하 조선 형세에 빗치여 엇던 학교를 택하여 주는 것이 좃켓슴니까. 또 公私立間 何를 취하리까.
(聞慶 金鍾瑞 拜)

평양의 상업학교는 자기의 자유로 택한 것입니다. 나는 다만 그의 건강과 또 나의 자산능력을 酌量하야 농업학교에 다님이 어떠냐고 권하엿슬 뿐입니다.
자녀의 교육은 「벌어먹기 위주」를 할 수 박게 업다고 생각하나 강제로 어느 科든지 택하게 할 수는 업다고 생각합니다. 내 의견과 희망은 참고로 말하야 자기의 소원대로 택하게 할 수 박게 업슴니다. 資力이 許하는 한에서.
(李光洙)

朝鮮에 女子高等普通學校 강의록 발행소는 업슴니까.
잇삽거던 下敎하세요.

朝鮮에는 아직 女子高等普通學校 정도로 강의록을 발행하는 곳이 업슴니다.
(草兵丁 답)

鄭錫泰 선생께
顔面에 여드름이 만히 나서 보기 흉하오니 복용하야 全治할 약은 업슴니까. 全治될 방법을 下敎하시요. 此 질의를 삼천리 지상에 써서 주시기를 바람니다.
(大邱 一독자)

拜上 연령에 따라 여드름이 날 때에는 나는 것이 필요하온즉 과하지 안커든 그대로 두는 것이 당연합니다.
然이나 심한 경우라든가 언제든지 끗칠 줄 모르는 경우에는 여러 가지 약과 주사도 잇슴니다만은 가정용으로서는 「에다이노기씰」이라는 散藥을 시용하시는 것도 무관일 것임니다.

귀하의 번창을 축하하옵내다. 弟 금반 貴誌 특가발매급고란에 春園 作인 단편소설집에 血書라는 부분이 有하는지 자세히 回示하시면 주문 爲計하오니 忘旁 교시를 千萬大望 합니다.

北靑 金鎬昌

家庭質問書
저는 지금 20세 된 부인이온대 중대한 이혼문제가 잇슴니다. 본래 古여성이깃 때문에 학문에 대하여서 눈 못떳서요. 겨우 우리나 언문이나 좀 암니다. 결혼은 2년 전이온대, 신랑 16세이고 저는 18세인 즉 법률혼법은 17 대 15이라는대요. 무지한 저를 이혼할나고 합니다. 더구나 마음도 안 마저요. 호적에 안 올니고 無産임니다. 위자료 청구할 수 잇서요?
(裵鎭姬 上)

답...  호적상 혼인 屆出이 되지 못하엿스면 법률상 혼인이 성립되지 못한 것이니 이혼문제가 될 것이 업슴니다. 남자가 혼인 屆出에 응치 안니하면 혼인예약 불이행에 인한 손해배상과 정조유린에 인한 위자료를 청구할 수 잇슴니다.
(辯護士 金炳魯)＜109＞
