人物短評 吳相淳
						黃錫禹
O Sangsun (吳相涥) was not a socialist thinker. He was a philosopher of spiritualism, a young sage (哲人) who became the sole opposing idol to many pioneering materialist youths. Mr. O was a well-matched rival (好對手) to Mr. Yu Chin-hŭi (兪鎭熙); whenever they met, they would growl and fight.

As a spiritualist thinker, Mr. O possessed deep and abundant academic cultivation. Even the materialist camp paid considerable respect to his level of cultivation.

The hot-tempered Mr. Yu, with his strong sense of superiority, would often lash out at Mr. O with insults like, "You damn praiser of phantoms (幽影), you iron-legged one (鐵脚者)!" (キサマタイナ幽影예찬ノ鐵脚者ガ). Yet, if he hadn't seen Mr. O for several days, he would say, "That Mr. O, I want to see that fellow, he's really a good person" (アノ 吳군 彼奴ニ逢 ヒタイナー, 彼奴ハ本當 ニ人ヨシダ). This seems to have been precisely because he respected Mr. O's profound cultivation.

If Mr. O had possessed some foundation (地盤) in terms of published works at that time, he would now likely be an idol in literary circles, like Yi Kwangsu (李光洙) and others who became targets of attack by the proletarian literature youth. Mr. O was the greatest idol in the intellectual world of that time. Now, Mr. O wanders from mountain valley to mountain valley (山間), like a certain Mr. Wŏn (元). This is in order to build the intellectual culture of the future to come.

It seems Mr. O's future days are also worth anticipating. Oh, Mr. O! What kind of sage (哲人) will you become, coming out onto the streets (街頭) to cry out in the future? Oh, Mr. O, the one spiritualist thinker serenely detached (超然) from all the realities of Chosŏn (朝鮮), living freely and contentedly (悠悠自適)!

Mr. O is not only a thinker but also a poet. His personality is gentle, compassionate (惻), and affectionate, like that of an old, loving mother (老慈母).
He is also a man of sentimental disposition (感傷質); he possesses the sentimentality of a religious person. He seems to be one of Chosŏn's young lofty scholars (高士).<19>

**Summary**

This 1932 article by Hwang Sŏgu portrays O Sangsun as a respected spiritualist philosopher and poet, contrasting him with the materialist thinkers of the time, particularly the combative Yu Chin-hŭi. Despite lacking published works that might have solidified his status like Yi Kwangsu, O was considered a major intellectual figure. Hwang notes O's gentle, motherly personality and his current wandering life, expressing anticipation for his future contributions. The article highlights the intellectual landscape of colonial Korea, particularly the tension between spiritualism/idealism and materialism/socialism. For historians, it offers a glimpse into the reputation and characterization of a significant non-socialist intellectual figure outside the mainstream literary or political movements, the dynamics between different intellectual factions, and the casual use of Japanese phrases even in Korean intellectual discourse.

人物短評 吳相淳
						黃錫禹
吳相涥군은 사회주의의 사상인은 아니엿섯다. 君은 만흔 유물론의 선구청년의 유일의 敵對偶像이 되든 정신주의의 철학자 젊은 哲人이엿다. 吳군은 兪鎭熙군과 맛나기만하면 으르렁거리고 싸호는 好對手인물이엿섯다.
吳군은 정신주의의 사상인으로서는 學적 교양이 깁고 풍부한 사람이엿다. 그 교양에 잇서서는 유물론군의 진영에서도 상당한 경의를 밧치어 왓섯다.
성미겁겁하고 우월감이 굿센 兪군이 吳군더러 툭하면
「キサマタイナ幽影예찬ノ鐵脚者ガ」
엇저는 욕을 함부로 퍼부어 노코는 멧칠만 吳군을 못맛나면 「アノ 吳군 彼奴ニ逢 ヒタイナー, 彼奴ハ本當 ニ人ヨシダ」라 하든 것도 곳 吳군의 그 기픈 교양을 존경하기 땜은이엿든 듯 하다.
吳군이 그 당시에 무슨 저서상의 地盤이라도 갓고 잇섯드면 吳군은 지금은 문단에 잇서서 푸로문학청년의 공격 대상인이 되든 李光洙 등과 가튼 우상이 되엿슬 것이다. 吳군은 그 때의 사상계에 잇서서는 가장 큰 우상이엿섯다. 지금의 吳군은 아나의 元군과 가티 山間으로 山間에 방랑하고 잇다. 장차 오는 압 날의 사상문화를 세우기 위하여
吳군의 뒷날도 가히 기대할 것인가 한다. 오- 吳군이여! 그대는 장차 街頭로 나와 무엇을 부르짓는 哲人이 될 것인가? 오- 朝鮮의 모든 현실에 초연한 단 하나의 정신주의의 유유자적의 사상인인 吳군이여!
吳군은 사상인 뿐이 안이라 시인이다. 그 성격은 老慈母와 가티 은은하고 惻하고 다정스럽다.
君 역시 감상質의 사람 그는 종교인적 감상기질을 가진 사람이다. 그는 朝鮮의 졂은 高士의 1인일가한다.＜19＞
