Magazine Title: 삼천리 第四卷 第十號
Publication Date: 1932년 10월 01일
Article Title: 鍾路네거리 (Chongno Crossroads)
Article Type: 잡저 (Miscellaneous writing/Gossip column)

Whether the Chosŏn Ilbo will come out first or the Chungang Ilbo will come out first is always a topic of discussion among the leisurely intellectual class (閑暇한 인테리層). Indeed, which one will come out first?<45>

The Chungang Ilbo (中央일보) is said to be taking a solid path (典實한 進路), like tapping even a stone bridge before crossing, deciding not to start (着手) until it has secured the full 150,000 won in cash (現金). Although it is currently undergoing a difficult birth (難産中), it is expected that a newspaper with correspondingly substantial content will inevitably emerge. The strategists (策士) on this side, debating whether it's Ch'u or Han (楚냐 將이냐), are said to be Cho Man-sik (曹晩植) and Kim Tong-wŏn (金東元) in P'yŏngyang, and Cho Pyŏng-ok (趙炳玉) and others in Seoul. It is the speculation of newspapermen that if the Chungang Ilbo emerges based in the Kwansŏ (關西) region, the foundation (地盤) of the Tonga Ilbo will be somewhat shaken.

There is talk that the Chosŏn Ilbo (朝鮮일보), having left the hands of Im Kyŏng-nae (林景來), will come out under the hands of former executives (舊幹部) such as Yu Chin-t'ae (兪鎭泰) and Han Ki-bu (韓基缶). Because of this, there is a rumor (風說) that a chaebŏl (財閥) worth 40,000 won or some amount has even backed it. In any case, it is thought that Messrs. An Chae-hong (安在鴻) and Yi Sŭng-bok (李昇馥) at least will not appear on the surface.

Many people, after watching Na Un-gyu's (羅雲奎) film "Imja ŏmnŭn narutpae" (임자업는 나룻배 - The Ownerless Ferryboat), are newly marveling (驚歎) at Mr. Na's artistry. Truly, "The Ownerless Ferryboat" is a recent masterpiece (近來의 傑作). May the petty filmmakers (群小영화인들) not kill this genius (天才) out of jealousy and hatred for one who surpasses them (勝己者厭).

The Society for the Promotion of Local Products (物産獎勵會) is divided into the faction (派) of Mr. Chŏng Se-kwŏn (鄭世權) and the faction of Mr. Myŏng Che-se (明濟世), and the disputes (紛爭) are incessant. What is to be done? What is to be done? (將奈何 將奈何)<46>

**Summary**

This short column from October 1932 discusses several topics circulating in Seoul (specifically around Chongno). It touches upon the anticipated launch of the Chungang Ilbo newspaper, its cautious financial approach, key figures involved (Cho Man-sik, Kim Tong-wŏn, Cho Pyŏng-ok), and its potential rivalry with the established Tonga Ilbo. It also mentions rumors surrounding the Chosŏn Ilbo's potential revival under former management (Yu Chin-t'ae, Han Ki-bu) with possible financial backing, noting An Chae-hong and Yi Sŭng-bok's likely non-involvement. The piece then praises Na Un-gyu's film "The Ownerless Ferryboat" as a masterpiece and warns against professional jealousy. Finally, it laments the internal factional disputes within the Society for the Promotion of Local Products between followers of Chŏng Se-kwŏn and Myŏng Che-se. For historians, this article offers valuable insights into the competitive dynamics of the Korean press under Japanese rule, the financial hurdles faced by new ventures, the key personalities active in media and nationalist movements, contemporary cultural reception (film), and the internal struggles within Korean organizations like the Product Promotion Society, reflecting the complexities and challenges of the colonial period.

鍾路네거리
조선일보가 몬저 나오겟느냐 중앙일보가 몬저 나오겟느냐 하는 것은 閑暇한 인테리層의 늘 토론거리다. 과연 어느것이 몬저 나올는고＜45＞
中央일보는 現金15만원을 다 만들어 노키전에는 着手치 안는다고 돌다리라도 두다려 보고야 건너는 典實한 進路을 취하기로 되어 難産中에 잇스나 그만치 내용충실한 신문이 필연코 나오리라 기대된다는데 楚냐 將이냐 하고 울니는 이편 策士는 平壤에 曹晩植
金東元 서울에 趙炳玉씨 等이라고 關西를 地盤으로 하고 나오는 중앙일보가 되면 동아일보地盤에 多少 動搖가 생기리라고 함은 신문인의 추측

朝鮮일보는 林景來손을 버서저 舊幹部인 兪鎭泰
韓基缶씨 등의 손으로 나오리란 말이 잇는데 그 때문에 4만원인가 얼마의 財閥까지 섯다는 風說이 잇다. 엇지햇든 安在鴻
李昇馥 兩氏만은 표면에 나서지 아니하리라니 생각한 일이다.

羅雲奎씨의 「임자업는 나룻배」의 영화를 보고 새삼스럽게 羅씨의 예술에 驚歎하는 이가 만타. 實로 「임자업는 나룻배」는 近來의 傑作이다. 이 天才를 群小영화인들은 질투와 勝己者厭으로 죽이지 말지어다.

物産獎勵會는 鄭世權씨 派와 明濟世씨 派로 갈나저서 紛爭이 不絶 將奈何 將奈何＜46＞
