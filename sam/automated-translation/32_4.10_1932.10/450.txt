From the Publication Department

A new sister magazine called "Man'guk Puin" (萬國婦人) has recently come out. Please read it widely.

However, there are one or two matters the Publication Department must apologize to everyone about. First is "P'yŏnghwa wa Chayu" (平和와 自由). Due to unavoidable circumstances, the reprinting of this book has been delayed, and we apologize for disappointing everyone's expectations. However, immediately following the printing of the October issue of "Samch'ŏlli," we began printing it, so it will definitely be out within the 15th of this month. We ask for the deep understanding of those who ordered it.

The printing of "Sin Munhak Sŏnjip" (新文學選集) and Mr. Kim Tong-in's (金東仁) "Kamja" (감자) has also been delayed due to certain circumstances (某사정), but we will begin work soon, so they too will likely be out by the 20th of this month. Please forgive us.<100>

**Summary**

The Publication Department announces the launch of a new magazine, "Man'guk Puin," and requests readers' support. It then apologizes for delays in publishing three books: the reprint of "P'yŏnghwa wa Chayu" (Peace and Freedom), and the new publications of "Sin Munhak Sŏnjip" (New Literary Selection) and Kim Tong-in's "Kamja" (Potato). New publication dates within the month are promised, citing "unavoidable" or "certain circumstances" for the delays. For historians, this notice offers a small window into the challenges faced by Korean publishers during the colonial period, where delays attributed vaguely to "circumstances" might hint at censorship, resource scarcity, or other external pressures affecting cultural production.

出版部에서
「萬國婦人」이란 新姊妹잡지가 새로 나왓슴니다. 만히 愛讀하여주시압.
그런데 出版部로서 諸씨에게 謝告할 일이 1, 2件이 잇슴니다. 첫재 「平和와 自由」외다. 이 책은 부득이한 사정으로 再版이 延期되어 諸씨의 기대에 어그러젓사와 죄송하엿슴니다. 그러나 10월號 「三千里」 인쇄 卽後를 바더서 곳 인쇄에 着手하엿스니 今月 15日 以內로는 반드시 나옴니다. 注文諸氏의 深切한 諒解를 바라나이다.

「新文學選集」과 金東仁씨 「감자」도 그동안 某사정으로 인쇄가 느저젓스나 곳 着手하겟스니 그것도 아마 今月 20日까지는 나오겟슴니다. 용서하시압.＜100＞
