Magazine Title: 삼천리 제4권 제10호
Publication Date: 1932년 10월 01일
Article Title: 그러키때문에 (Because of That)
Author: None specified
Article Type: 잡저 (Miscellaneous writing/Anecdote)

Pastor—Liquor is not just your enemy, it is the enemy of all humankind!

Drunkard—What was that? Isn't it in the Bible (聖經)? It says to love your enemy.<71>

**Summary**

This short piece recounts a humorous exchange between a pastor condemning alcohol as the enemy of humankind and a drunkard who cleverly retorts using the biblical injunction to "love your enemy." The anecdote likely served as a brief, amusing filler item in the magazine. From a historical perspective, it reflects the presence and social commentary surrounding Christianity and temperance movements in colonial Korea. The use of older Korean orthography (e.g., "그러키때문에", "술리란", "잇지안소") is also of linguistic interest to historians studying the evolution of the Korean language during this period.
