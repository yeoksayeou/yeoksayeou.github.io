Article Title: 編輯後記 (Editorial Postscript)
Author: Not specified
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Announcement/Editorial Postscript)

Time flies, and already this month marks the 4th anniversary of this magazine's founding. Thinking of the four years we have spent dearly, weeping and laughing each month with tens of thousands of readers (讀者諸氏), we feel anew a desire to respectfully greet you.

We intended to make the October issue (10월號) of this magazine (本誌) a splendid double-sized issue (倍大號), but as not everything (萬事) goes according to our wishes, we unavoidably kept the page count (百數) as usual. A widow (寡婦) knows a widow's sorrow, wouldn't you say?

However, in its place (代身), we will carry out major, 'blockbuster' editing (大巨彈的 編輯) continuously for the three months of the November, December, and New Year issues (新年號). May your patronage (愛護) become even deeper.

Now is the time when yellow chrysanthemums and crimson maples (黃菊丹楓) are also having their final moments in the frosty wind (霜風). We pray for your continued well-being (健安).
＜100＞

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the October 1932 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine marks its fourth anniversary. The editor expresses deep gratitude to the readership for their support over the years. They apologize for being unable to publish a planned special double-sized issue for October due to unspecified difficulties ("a widow knows a widow's sorrow"), but promise "blockbuster" issues for November, December, and the New Year instead. The piece concludes with seasonal greetings referencing autumn chrysanthemums and maples, wishing readers well. For historians, the article reveals the operational challenges faced by Korean publications during the colonial period, hinting at financial or logistical constraints preventing a special issue. It also highlights the magazine's commitment to its readers and its ambition to produce significant content despite these hurdles, reflecting a strategy for survival and engagement within the colonial context.

編輯後記
歲月이 빨너서 벌서 本誌 창간 4주년을 이달로서 마지하엿슴니다. 數萬讀者 諸씨와 달마다 울고 우스며 4년을 親愛하여온 생각을 하옴애 새삼스럽게 敬拜하고 십사외다.

10월號 本誌는 찬란하게 倍大號로 하려하엿스나 萬事가 우리 뜻과 갓지못한지라 부득이 百數도 보통모양으로 하엿슴니다. 寡婦서름을 寡婦야 아러주겟지요. 그러치만 그 代身 11, 12, 新年號의 3개월을 계속적으로 大巨彈的 編輯을 하겟슴니다. 여러분의 愛護가 더욱 두터워지압소서.

이제는 霜風에 黃菊丹楓도 마지막 가는때외다. 여러분의 내내 健安을 비나이다.＜100＞
