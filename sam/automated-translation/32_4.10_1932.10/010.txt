Holding the "Key (열대) of the Peninsula," on the Occasion of This Magazine's Fourth Anniversary

We are people entrusted with the key (열대) given by our Peninsular brethren. What does this mean? For example, it is said to be customary for the mayors of cities like London or New Yŏk [New York] to receive the key (열대) of London or New Yŏk [New York] and then, when guests from other places or great artists visit, to present that key (열대) to them upon their first step ashore. The meaning is said to express welcome and respect, signifying, "With this key (열대), please feel free to open and explore any place in our city as you wish."

Now, we too have received this key (열대) signifying welcome from the personages (人士) of the Peninsula, and for the full four years since our founding, we have strived with all our might, traveling to every corner of the 13 Provinces, to unearth the brilliant culture of this land and present a vision for the future.

Indeed, during that time, we have thoroughly examined both the interior and exterior of the Peninsula. In this way, striving to fulfill our duty entrusted with the key (열대), we have offered the utmost sincerity (犬馬의 誠) and have now reached today, the fourth anniversary of our founding.

Thinking back, our path has been quite arduous. Among the magazines of the Peninsula, there are almost none without massive backing behind them, while this magazine alone, leading a solitary, lonely single horseman, has repeatedly endured hardships in passing through various efforts and barriers. We have fought against financial power, and we have fought against [lack of] foundation.

However, now that the affection of readers throughout the land grows deeper by the day and the foundation of our company has become unshakable, standing upon bedrock, from now on we shall truly refresh our brushes and inkstones and advance further (更進一番). We ask that you, our readers, entrust the key (열대) of the Peninsula to us forever and look forward to our future endeavors. (Pa'in)<5>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript celebrates the fourth anniversary of the magazine *Samch'ŏlli*. The author uses the metaphor of being given the "key to the Peninsula" (symbolizing trust and access) by the Korean people. They reflect on the past four years of diligently exploring Korea (the 13 Provinces) to showcase its culture and future prospects, despite facing significant hardships, particularly financial struggles and lack of powerful backing compared to other publications. With growing readership and a now stable foundation, the magazine pledges to redouble its efforts and asks readers to continue entrusting it with the metaphorical "key" and to anticipate its future contributions. For historians, the article offers insight into the challenges faced by independent Korean publications during the colonial period, highlighting the struggles against resource disparity ('financial power' and 'foundation'). The nationalistic undertone, framed within the acceptable bounds of the time (focusing on culture and future hope), and the use of the "key" metaphor to represent a mandate from the Korean people ('Peninsular brethren', 'personages of the Peninsula') are particularly revealing of efforts to maintain a Korean voice and identity under colonial rule.

『半島의 열대』를 쥐고서, 本誌創刊 四週年에 際하야
우리는 半島형제가 주는 열대를 마튼 사람이외다. 이 말이 무슨 말인고 하면 가령 倫敦이나 紐育시장갓흔 사람은 倫敦 혹은 紐育市의 열대를 마타 가지고 잇다가 다른 곳의 賓客이나 위대한 예술가가 내방하면 그 열대를 상륙 제1보에 내어 맛기는 것이 예이라함니다. 그 뜻은 이 열대를 가지고 당신이 우리 市의 어느 곳이든지 마음대로 열고 드려가 보십시요 하는 환영과 敬待하는 뜻을 표하는 것이라함니다.
이제 우리도 半島人士로부터 이 환영을 표시하는 열대를 밧어 쥐고 창간 이래 만 4주년동안에 13道 방방곡곡을 차저다니며 이 땅의 찬연한 문화의 발굴과 압날의 희망의 제시에 잇는 힘을 다하느라고 노력하엿슴니다.
실로 그동안 우리는 半島의 내외를 잘도 삿삿치 드비어 보앗슴니다. 이리하야 열대 마튼 소임을 다하느라고 犬馬의 誠을 다하면서 이제 창간 4주년의 今日에 至한 것이외다.
생각건대 우리의 길은 상당히 험하엿섯나이다. 半島의 잡지로 그 背面에 거대한 박크를 아니가진 것이 거지반 업슨 속에 본지 혼자 孤影蕭然한 單騎를 잇글고 가진 노력과 관문을 통과하기에 고심을 거듭하엿든 것이외다. 金力과도 싸웟고 地盤과도 싸워왓나이다.
그러나 이제 천하의 애호가 날로 두터워 社의 기초가 요지부동하야 반석 우에 서게 되옴애 이제부터는 實로 筆硯을 새로히 하야 更進一番하오리니 청컨대 여러분은 半島의 열대를 우리에게 길이길이 맛겨 두시고 압 날을 기대하여 주옵소서. (파인)＜5＞
