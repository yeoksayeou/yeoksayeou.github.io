**Body Text Translation:**

To Mr. Sin Hŭngu: Please Go to America and Collect One Million Won for Cultural Projects on the Peninsula

Sir, as you have more connections with America than perhaps any other Korean, please go across to America once more and return with about one million Won in donations.

Please use it to invigorate the various educational projects and industrial movements within Korea that cannot be realized due to lack of funds, for the future of our people.

As far as I know, you have probably crossed over to America some ten-odd times. With such a broad stage and such good opportunities, especially since you traveled with important titles and positions each time you went to America, I believe you must have formed friendships with many great tycoons and famous figures in the Americas. If you resolve to pour all your sincerity and effort into obtaining funds from the Americas, I believe it would be possible. Therefore, I hope you will strive to quickly gather the money and contribute it to projects within Korea.

I hear that large amounts of charitable funds from America's great conglomerates are spread annually to various countries and places, even speaking of the Orient. Just about 5 or 6 years ago, wasn't there a donation of 4 million dollars from the American Rockefeller Foundation to Tokyo? The city of Tokyo received that money and built a large charity hospital (施療病院), creating a paradise (樂地) for the poor and sick. I believe it would not be such a difficult matter for organizations possessing colossal fortunes of hundreds of millions, like Carnegie or Rockefeller, to give one or two million Won for the cultural projects of a people. How advantageous would it be for them as well, to win the favor of an entire people with one or two million Won?
Even just looking at China, how much clean money (淨財), devoid of aggressive or political intentions, comes in greatly from America to help the cultural projects of the Chinese state? Isn't it because they possess individuals like Wang Zhengting (王正廷) and Dian Weijun (顚維均) [likely Gu Weijun/V.K. Wellington Koo] who focus <7> on and are active in that field that millions of dollars in clean money are gathered annually? I would like to hear your profound determination regarding this point and your intentions about it.

Mr. Sin Hŭngu's Reply

How could I myself lack such passion and sincerity? Indeed, there were more than one or two occasions when I strove in various ways, wanting to obtain clean money (淨財) donations—not just 1 million Won, but even several million Won.
During the time of the People's University (民立大學) project and other instances, however, we are placed in special circumstances. Money truly devoid of political intentions does not come easily. Now, I don't know how this will be interpreted, but in the meantime, enormous sums of money have indeed come into the Peninsula annually from American religious organizations or businessmen. Do you know how many cultural projects rooted in Christianity are progressing through that money? Only, because the money comes through various channels, I cannot say with my own mouth what the total amount is, but in any case, numerous church workers and schools are growing under that shadow.
Now, you ask me to collect about 1 million Won from America to be used for social cultural projects. The argument is good, but it is difficult to give a definite affirmation readily, though I am always thinking about it in my heart and will try to strive for its realization. <7>
<7-8>

**Summary**

The article consists of a plea to Sin Hŭngu (a prominent Korean figure with US connections) to fundraise one million Won in America for Korean cultural and educational projects, citing successful examples of US philanthropy in Japan (Rockefeller) and China (enabled by figures like Wang Zhengting). Sin Hŭngu responds, acknowledging his past efforts and desire but highlighting the difficulty due to Korea's "special circumstances" (implying colonial restrictions hindering politically neutral foreign aid). He notes, however, that significant funds already flow from US religious groups for Christian-affiliated projects in Korea, and while hesitant to promise success for broader cultural funds, he commits to considering and striving for it.

For historians, the article is revealing about Korean aspirations for self-strengthening through cultural and educational development during the colonial period and their awareness of international philanthropic avenues. It highlights the perceived importance of US connections and funding. Sin Hŭngu's response subtly points to the constraints imposed by Japanese rule, suggesting that purely "clean money" (淨財) without political implications was hard to secure for non-religious, potentially nationalistic, Korean initiatives, contrasting this with the relative success of US religious funding streams. The comparison with China and Japan underscores Korean awareness of their disadvantaged position in attracting such international support.

기사형태	문예기타

申興雨氏에게 米國가서 百萬圓 거더 오십시요, 半島의 文化事業에=쓸 資金으로
선생은 朝鮮사람 중 누구보다도 米國과 퍽으나 인연이 만흐니까 이제 다시 한 번 米國건너가서 돈 백만원쯤 기부금을 어더 가지고 도라나오십시요.

그래서 돈이 업서서 이루어지지 못하고 잇는 朝鮮內의 諸교육사업이나 산업운동 등을 활발하게 이르켜 同族의 장래를 위하여 주십시요.

내가 알기에는 선생은 아마 十數次나 渡米하엿던줄 압니다. 그 너른 무대와 그 조흔 기회을 가지고 더구나 渡米할 때마다 중요한 職名과 지위를 가지고서 출입하엿스니까 그만하면 米州의 큰 부호와 명사들을 만히 친교하여둔 줄 암니다. 선생이 모든 정성과 역량을 부어서 米洲의 돈을 어더오러고 결심하신다면 가능한 일일 것이니 어서 돈을 끄으려다가 朝鮮內의 사업에 던지어 주도록 힘써주기를 바람니다.

나는 듯건대 米國 큰 재벌의 자선적 돈은 東洋으로 말할지라도 각국 각처에 거액이 年年히 펴진다함니다. 약 5,6년 전만 하여도
東京에 米國 록펠라 재단으로부터 400만불의 기부가 잇섯지 안엇슴니가. 그 돈을 東京市에서 밧어서 큰 施療병원을 만들어 가난한 病傷者의 樂地를 만들엇지요. 카-네키나 록펠나가튼 屢億의 巨財를 가진 단체에서 1,2백만원쯤 한 민족의 문화사업을 위하야 내어준다함이 그다지 곤란에 속한 일이 아니라고 봄니다. 1,2백만원으로 一민족의 환심을 산다함이 엇더케나 저네들로도 유리하릿까.
中國만 하여도 침략적, 정치적, 의미를 뛰지안는 淨財가 엇더케나 亞米利加로부터 만히 드러와서 中國국가의 문화사업을 돕고 잇슴니까 한 사람의 王正廷, 한사람의 顚維均가튼 그 방면에＜7＞ 着目하고 활동하는 人士를 가젓기 까닭에 年 수백만불의 淨財가 모여지는 것이 아님니까. 선생의 이 점에 대한 深甚한 각오와 또 그에 대한 의향을 듯고 십슴니다.

申興雨氏 答
내 자신에게 엇지 그러한 열과 정성이 업섯스리까 그야 100만원은 아니고 數 백만원이라도 기부하여주는 淨財를 엇고 십허서 각 방면으로 애쓴 때도 한 두번이 아니엿슴니다.
民立대학때라든지 기타 경우에, 그러치만 우리는 특수한 처지에 노여 잇슴니다. 원악 정치적 뜻이 석기지 아닌 돈이란 잘 오지 안는 법이외다. 이제 이러케 말하면 엇더케 해석할는지 모르나 그동안 亞米利加의 종교단체에서나 사업가의 손으로부터 半島에 거대한 금액이 年年히 드러왓지요. 그 돈을 통하야 기독교에 근간을 둔 諸문화사업이 엇더케나 만히 진행되어가는 줄 암니까, 다만 여러 계통을 통하야 드러오는 돈이기에 그 금액 전체가 얼마나 된다고 내입으로 말할 수 업슴니다만은 엇지햇든 다수한 사원과 학교가 그 그늘 아래에서 자라가고 잇슴니다.
이제 날더러 사회적 문화사업에 쓰일 돈 100만원쯤 米國서 거두어 오라니 그 論이 조흐나 얼는 確言 하기 어럽지만 心中 에 늘 생각하고 실현하기에 노력하여 볼가함니다.＜7＞
