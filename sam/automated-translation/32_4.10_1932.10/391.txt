Urgent Notice

The magazine "Samch'ŏlli" (三千里) must be present wherever our white-clad compatriots (白衣同胞) reside, but if by chance your region (貴地方) does not yet have a branch office (支社), please immediately request the regulations (規則書) and strive to establish a branch office yourself.

Samch'ŏlli Company Sales Bureau (三千里社營業局) <92>

**Summary**

This short text is an advertisement or notice from the Samch'ŏlli magazine's sales bureau, urging readers to help establish local branch offices or distributorships if one doesn't exist in their area. It appeals to Korean national identity ("white-clad compatriots") and emphasizes the magazine's importance, encouraging readers to take the initiative in expanding its circulation network. For historians, this notice reflects the magazine's business strategy and efforts to broaden its reach across Korea during the colonial period, relying on reader initiative and local networks. It also highlights the use of nationalist sentiment ("white-clad compatriots") in commercial appeals.
