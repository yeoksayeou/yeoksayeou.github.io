Great Actors and the Stage

If we are to count the great actors possessing masculine strength and passion in our theater world, it would be Mr. Ch'unsa Na Un'gyu (羅雲奎). Mr. Na first gained the popularity of the peninsula by directing and acting opposite Sin Il-sŏn (申一*) in the movie "Arirang." Following that, he continuously released masterpieces such as "Chal Itkŏra" and "Sarangŭl Ch'ajŏsŏ," and today reigns in the position of the prince of the film world.

However, although it hasn't been long since he set foot in the theater world, his masculine acting is also receiving fervent acclaim on stage, and today his name is renowned as a great figure in the theater world.

Among his works, the most famous are likely his role in [the play] "P'onora" *** at Tansŏngsa last autumn, and "The Count of Monte Cristo" in [the production] Haewangsŏng (海王星). His masculine acting reached its peak in this Haewangsŏng. The scene where he meets the abbé [Abbé Faria], the scene where he takes revenge—the great actor Mr. Na Un'gyu achieved a complete 100 percent stage effect in these scenes. <12>

**Summary**

This 1933 news article highlights the acclaimed actor Na Un'gyu (Na Un-gyu), known as Ch'unsa. Initially famous for directing and starring in films like "Arirang," "Chal Itkŏra," and "Sarangŭl Ch'ajŏsŏ," he successfully transitioned to the stage. The article praises his "masculine" acting style, particularly his performance as the lead in "The Count of Monte Cristo" (part of a production possibly titled "Haewangsŏng") at the Tansŏngsa theater, citing the scenes meeting Abbé Faria and enacting revenge as peak moments achieving full stage effect. For historians, the article illustrates the vibrant cultural scene in colonial Korea, the popularity of figures like Na Un'gyu bridging film and theater, the adaptation of Western narratives like "Monte Cristo," and the significance of venues like Tansŏngsa during this period. The mention of specific films and plays provides insight into popular entertainment of the time.

名優와 舞臺
남성적 힘과 열을 가진 명배우를 우리 극단에서 손을 곱자면, 춘사 라운규 씨리라. 씨는 처음 영화「아리랑」을 신일선(申一*)을 상대적으로 하야 연출하야서 반도의 인긔를 끄으럿다. 그 뒤 게속하야 「잘 잇거라」「사랑을 차저서」등의 명작을 내여, 오늘 영화게의 왕자의 지위에 군림하고 잇다.
그러치만 극게에 씨가 투족한지는 얼마 되지 안치만은 그의 남성적 연긔는 역시 무대 우에서도 열열한 환영을 바더 그는 오늘 극게의 대자로서 일홈이 놉다.
그 중에서 가장 유명한 것은, 작년 가을에 단성사에서 *** 「포노라」의 인역과, 해왕성의 「몬테크리스트 백작」이리라. 그의 남성적 연긔는 이 해왕성에 이르러 절정에 달하엿다. **법사를 맛나든 장면, 복수하든 장면, 명우 라운규씨는 이 장면에서 완전히 100퍼-센트의 무대효과를 내엿다.＜12＞
