Famous Actors and the Stage

The play called "Oh, Heaven's Will" (嗚呼天命) is an adaptation derived from the Japanese "Soga Brothers" (曾我兄弟). The theme of the play is a masterpiece centered on the loyalty that arises between brothers who sold military secrets (軍事機密) to the enemy. Mr. Yi Kŭise did the translation, and the one who played the role of the protagonist, an army captain, most splendidly and received acclaim was Mr. Kim Sorang. Kim Sorang is from Sŏul. He was once part of Im Sŏnggu's troupe, and later became the leader himself, organizing the Kim Sorang troupe.

Kim Sorang, Im Sŏnggu, and Kim Tosan can all be seen as meritorious figures who were pioneers of the Korean new school drama (sinpa) scene, and the three each had their own theater troupes. Kim Sorang is still active in the theater world even now. <7>

**Summary**

This short news piece reports on the actor Kim Sorang's acclaimed performance as an Army Captain in the play "Oh, Heaven's Will," an adaptation of the Japanese "Soga Brothers" story, staged at the Tansŏngsa Theater. The play, translated by Yi Kŭise, deals with loyalty between brothers involved in espionage. The article also contextualizes Kim Sorang as a pioneer of Korean *sinpa* drama alongside Im Sŏnggu and Kim Tosan, noting that Kim Sorang remained active in theater at the time of writing (1933). For historians, the article is interesting as it shows the direct adaptation of Japanese popular narratives for Korean audiences during the colonial period, reflects the themes (loyalty, military elements) considered suitable or popular, and names key figures in the development of early modern Korean theater (*sinpa*), highlighting their individual contributions and continued presence. The specific venue, Tansŏngsa, is also significant as a major cultural site.

名優와 舞臺
「오호, 천명」이란 연극은 일본의 「증아형제」(曾我兄弟)를 번한번안한 것이다. 극의 주제는 적군에게 군사긔밀(軍事機密)을 판 형제 사이에 이러나는 의리를 주제 삼은 명작이다. 이것을 번역하기는 리긔세 씨가 하엿고, 이 작의 주인공인 륙군대위의 역을 가장 훌륭하게 마터 환영을 반은 이가 김소랑 씨엿다. 김소랑은 서울 사람이라 그는 일즉 림성구 일행에 다니다가 나종에 자기가 수령이 되여 김소랑 일행을 조직하엿다.
김소랑이나 림성구나 김도산이나 모다 조선 신파극게의 원조라 볼 수 잇는 공로자들로, 세 사람이 각각 극단을 가젓섯다. 김소랑은 지금까지도 극게에 발을 멈추고 잇다.＜7＞
