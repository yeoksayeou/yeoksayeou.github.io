Famous Actors and the Stage

It was last February. The Theater Arts Research Association (劇藝術硏究協會), organized by literary figures, held a performance at the city's Public Hall (公會堂). The stage that drew the most popularity then was the German playwright Kayser's "Friendship" (友情). The plot of "Friendship" has poignant aspects, so to write it down: When word arrives that a boatman went out to sea and died because his boat was wrecked, his wife weeps but then marries her husband's irreplaceable best friend. However, the husband who was thought to be dead was not dead, and actually returns alive to this newlywed home. Here, the two friends fight over the one woman. At the end of the fight, the new bride, thinking that if she were gone there would be no reason for their friendship to crumble, commits suicide by poisoning herself. The friendship of the two is restored before the corpse of their beloved. It is a one-act play. Taking on the role of this new bride and captivating the entire audience was the poetess Mo Yunsuk (毛允淑氏). It is said that Ms. Yang [referring to Mo Yunsuk] had also appeared on stage during her student days at Rihwa Haktang (梨花學堂) and received much acclaim for her passionate performance.<8>

**Summary**

This news report covers a performance of the German play "Friendship" (Ujŏng) by the Theater Arts Research Association (Kŭgyesul Yŏn'gu Hyŏphoe), a group formed by literary figures, at the Public Hall (Konghoedang) in February 1933. The article highlights the performance of the poet Mo Yunsuk in the lead female role, noting her popularity with the audience and mentioning her previous acting experience as a student at Rihwa Haktang. For historians, this article offers insight into the cultural activities of Korean intellectuals during the colonial period, specifically the intersection of literature and theater, and the participation of prominent female figures like Mo Yunsuk in public performances.

기사형태	소식

名優와 舞臺
지난 2월 일이다. 시내 공회당에서 문인들로 조직된 극예술연구협회(劇藝術硏究協會)에서 공연을 하엿다. 그때 가장 인긔를 끄는 무대가 독일 카이자작 「우정」이엿다. 우정의 강개는 심각한 점이 잇기에 적어 보면 뱃사공이 바다에 나갓다가 배가 파선되여 죽엇다는 말이 전할 때, 그의 안해는 울다가 남편의 둘도 업는 친우와 결혼하여 버렷다. 그런데 죽은 줄 알엇든 그 남편은 죽지 안코, 실상은 이 신혼 가정에 사라 도라왓다. 여기에서 한 녀자를 가운데 두고 두 친구는 싸운다. 싸우든 끗헤 신부는 자기가 업스면 우정이 스러질 까닭이 업스리라 하면서 음독자살 하여 버린다. 둘의 우정은 애인의 시체 압헤서 다시 복구하여진다는 일막물이다. 그런데 이 신부의 역을 마터가지고 만장의 관객을 뇌살식힌 이에 녀류시인 모윤숙씨가 잇섯다. 양은 일즉 리화학당 학생 시대에도 출연하여 그 정열적의 연출에 만흔 환영을 밧엇다든가.＜8＞
