Famous Actors and the Stage

Miss Pok Hyesuk, who had drifted away to Shanghai (上海), recently returned to Sŏul and is managing a bar called "Venus" (삐너쓰) in Rakwŏndong. Before becoming a film actress, this Miss Pok Hyesuk first appeared on the stage of the T'owŏrhoe (土月會) and was one of the famous actresses who achieved great success.

The most popular production staged by T'owŏrhoe after its founding was "Katyusha" (카쥬샤), and Miss Pok Hyesuk took on the role of that Katyusha. Above all else, Miss Pok Hyesuk cries well and laughs well. Scenes such as agonizing in prison as a prostitute (賣笑婦) probably could not have achieved such effect if not for Miss Pok Hyesuk. Just as Matsui Sumako (須磨子) gained the name of a historically famous actress by playing the role of Katyusha well, wouldn't this Miss Pok Hyesuk be the Katyusha of Korea, if Matsui Sumako is the Katyusha of Japan? <11>

**Summary**

This short news piece reports on the return of actress Pok Hyesuk to Sŏul from Shanghai, where she is now running a bar named "Venus." It reminisces about her earlier stage career with the T'owŏrhoe troupe, highlighting her acclaimed performance as Katyusha in their popular production of Tolstoy's "Resurrection." The article praises her emotive acting, particularly in scenes depicting Katyusha's suffering, and compares her portrayal favorably to the famous Japanese actress Matsui Sumako's renowned interpretation of the same role, suggesting Pok Hyesuk is Korea's equivalent Katyusha. From a historical perspective, the article is interesting as it shows the transnational connections (Shanghai, Japan) of Korean performers in the colonial era, the significance of stage adaptations of Western literature (Tolstoy's "Resurrection"), the influence and comparison point of Japanese popular culture (Matsui Sumako), and the early career trajectories of Korean stars who moved between stage and screen.

名優와 舞臺
상해(上海)에 흘너갓든 복헤숙양은 요즈음 다시 서울에 도라와서 락원동에서 「삐너쓰」란 「빠-」를 경영한다. 이 복혜숙 양은 일즉 영화배우로 나서기 전에 맨 처음 토월회의 무대에 올나서서 만흔 성공을 한 명배우 중의 한 사람이엿다.
토월회가 창립되여 가장 인긔를 끄는 상영작품은 「카쥬샤」인데, 그 「카쥬샤」의 역을 복헤숙씨가 마터 하엿다. 복헤숙 양은 무엇보다도 잘 울고, 잘 웃는다. 매소부로서 감옥에 드러가 고민하든 장면 가튼 것은 복해숙씨 아니고는 그러케 효과를 내지 못하엿슬 것이다. 마쓰이스마꼬(須磨子)가 「카쥬샤」의 역을 잘 하야 력사적 명녀우의 일홈을 들엇거니와, 일본에 카쥬샤를 마쓰이스마꼬라 하면, 조선의 카쥬샤는 이 복헤숙 양이 아니될가.＜11＞
