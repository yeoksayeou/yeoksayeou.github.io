Famous Actors and the Stage

Every year, as the early summer arrives and flowers grow lush, Ewha Hakdang (梨花學堂) in Chŏngdong holds a student play. Among these, the one that produced the greatest masterpiece and received high praise was likely "Jeanne d'Arc," performed in 1930. The director at that time was Mr. Yŏn Haknyŏn (延鶴年). "Jeanne d'Arc" is a famous play, familiar to everyone, based on the patriotic girl of France. During the Hundred Years' War between England and France, the activities of the *girl of Orléans, Jeanne d'Arc, saved her homeland, whose entire territory (국코) was about to be trampled under the horses' hooves under the rule of the Grand Duke of England (영길리) at the time.

The protagonist of this play, Jeanne d'Arc, was performed by Miss Pak Sahye (朴思惠). Miss Pak previously finished Ewha Women's College and worked at Ahi Saenghwalsa (아히생활사), and is now studying abroad in America.＜9＞

**Summary**

This short news report highlights a student play performance of "Jeanne d'Arc" at Ewha Hakdang (Ewha School) in 1930, directed by Yŏn Haknyŏn. The lead role was played by Pak Sahye, an Ewha alumna who later worked at Ahi Saenghwalsa before going to study in America. The article praises the 1930 performance as a masterpiece. From a colonial history perspective, the choice of "Jeanne d'Arc," a story of a national heroine resisting foreign domination (England), performed at a leading Korean women's institution like Ewha, is notable. Historians might find this interesting as a potential expression of veiled nationalism or commentary on Korea's own situation under Japanese rule, using a historical European parallel. The mention of studying in America also points to the transnational aspirations of educated Korean women during this period.

기사형태	소식

名優와 舞臺
해마다 화초가 욱어지는 첫 녀름철을 잡으면 정동에 잇는 리화학당(梨花學堂)에서는 학생 연극을 한다. 그 중에도 가장 걸작을 연출하여 호평을 밧든 해는 1930년에 한 「짠, 따-크」이리라. 그때 연출지도자는 연학년(延鶴年)씨엿다. 「짠, 따-크」는 누구나 잘 아는 불란서의 애국의 소녀를 주제로 한 유명한 연극이라, 영불 백년전쟁때 오투레안의 *소녀 「짠, 따-크」의 활약은 당시 영길리의 대공(大公)의 지배 하에 모-든 국코가 말발굽 아래 짓밟히려 하는 조국을 구해 내엿든 것이다.
이 연극의 주인공「짠, 따-크」를 박은헤 양이 하엿다. 양은 일즉 리화전문을 마치고 아히생활사에서 일을 보다가 지금은 아메리카에 가서 류학하고 잇는 중이다.＜9＞
