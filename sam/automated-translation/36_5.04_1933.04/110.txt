Article Title: 三千里壁新聞 (Samch'ŏlli Wall Newspaper)

**Body Text Translation**

Samch'ŏlli Wall Newspaper

The Reality of Newspapers
The annual advertising revenue of the Ōsaka Mainichi Shinbun and Ōsaka Asahi Shinbun is around 51 million yen. However, the Tonga Ilbo, said to bring in the most advertising revenue in Korea, earns only about 100,000 won. Incidentally, the annual advertising revenue of the Keijō Nippō is said to be about double that of the Tonga Ilbo. Also, the number of types of Japanese newspapers entering Korea is about 500. Among them, the ones with the largest circulation entering are the Ōsaka Mainichi and Ōsaka Asahi at 6,000 copies each. While this is the case for the 400,000 Japanese residential areas, the civilian newspapers serving 20 million people [Koreans] should rightfully have many times that circulation. Yet, it is a regrettable fact that the circulations of the Tonga, Chosŏn, and Chungoe papers are smaller than those of the Ōsaka Mainichi and Ōsaka Asahi entering Korea.

The Agony of the Christian Church
If one were to count the pro-American faction in Korea, one would count the various gentlemen of Christian society. And if one were to count pro-American enterprises, they would be churches, schools, hospitals, missionary organizations, etc. Recently, as the Great Panic (全*恐慌) struck America, banks closed their doors, and money dried up in the markets, causing funds for missionary work in Korea to not flow as abundantly as before, which is said to be a headache.
Consequently, there have been salary reductions for teachers in various Christian schools, and church workers are also feeling the effects. Does the rise and fall of the New York market thus impact the East Asian Peninsula (亞細亞東半島) so frightfully?

Yi Kap's Distribution of Wealth
The story of Yi Kap (李甲), who took back the 300 *sŏk*-worth (*turak*) of land confiscated [due to XXX] from his paternal uncle (伯父) by entering X's house with a pistol, is known by everyone in the world. But there is a fact not yet fully known: at that time, not only did he receive the land back, but he also received 30,000 won in cash separately. Yi Kap then set aside 2,000 won and distributed it among the house steward (廳直이) and female servants (어멈들) of Mr. X's house, and used the remaining money to establish entities such as the Osŏng School (五星學校) and the Sŏbuk Association (西北學會). <16>

**Summary**

This article from April 1933 presents three short news items: 1) A comparison of newspaper advertising revenues and circulation, highlighting the dominance of Japanese papers (Ōsaka Mainichi, Ōsaka Asahi, Keijō Nippō) over major Korean papers (Tonga Ilbo, Chosŏn Ilbo, Chungoe Ilbo) in colonial Korea. 2) The financial difficulties faced by American-funded Christian institutions (schools, churches) in Korea due to the Great Depression impacting funding from the US. 3) An anecdote about a man named Yi Kap who, after recovering confiscated land and extorting money using a pistol, used the funds to establish a Korean school and association. For historians, the article offers insights into the economic realities of the Korean press under Japanese rule, the tangible impact of global economic events like the US Depression on colonial Korea via missionary channels, and anecdotal evidence of individual resistance and the funding mechanisms for Korean nationalist or educational movements during the period.

三千里壁新聞
신문의 실정
大阪每日新聞과 大阪朝日新聞의 1개년 광고 수입이 오천백만원 내외, 그런데 조선서 가장 광고료가 만히 드러온다는 동아일보가 약 십만원 내외, 딴말이나 京城日報의 年廣告收入은 약 동아일보의 배가 된다 한다. 또 일본 신문이 조선에 들어오는 種類數가 약 오백종. 그 중에 가장 多部數가 드러오는 것이 大阪每日, 大阪朝日의 육천부라 하니 사십만 일본인 住接處에 이러할 때 이천만이 사는 민간신문은 그 여러 십배가 되어야 올켓는데 可歎할 事 東亞, 朝鮮, 中外의 各紙가 조선에 드러오는 大每, 大朝보다 그 부수가 적은 것이 사실이다.

基督敎會의 苦悶
조선에서 親米派를 헤자면 기독교 사회의 諸氏를 헤일 것이요. 또 親米系 사업을 헤자면 교회*학교병원, 포교기관 등인데 최근에 아메리카에 全*恐慌이 닥처와서 은행이 문을 닷고 시정에 돈이 말너드는 바람에 조선의 포교사업에는 전과 가치 裕足하게 돈이 나오지를 안어서 두통거리라 한다.
그래서 기독교계 각 학교에서는 교사 월급의 감봉이 잇섯고 교회 교역자들도 그 영향을 입고 잇다하니 뉴욕시장의 성쇠는 亞細亞東半島에 이러틋 무섭게 미치든가.

李甲의 分財
XXX때문에 伯父의 토지 삼백석 斗落을 빼앗긴 李甲이 권총을 들고 X의 집에 드러가서 토지를 도로 차젓다하든 이약이는 세상에서 다 알지만 아직 채 알니지 못한 사실이 잇스니 그것은 그 때 토지의 반환을 밧엇을 뿐더러 따로 현금 삼만원을 밧엇다. 李甲은 그 때 이천원을 떼어 X씨집 廳直이와 어멈들에게 논아주고 남어지 돈으로 五星學校 西北學會 등을 이르키는 데 썻다. ＜16＞
