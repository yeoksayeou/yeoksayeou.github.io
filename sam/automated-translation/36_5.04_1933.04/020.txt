Great Actors and the Stage

If we were to count the person deserving the laurel crown as the "progenitor of our Chosŏn *sinp'a* drama world," everyone would point to Mr. Lim Sŏnggu (林聖九). Mr. Lim Sŏnggu initially appeared on insignificant stages in theaters in Sŏul and Japan, then washed his feet [made a fresh start] and founded a theater troupe called the "Lim Sŏnggu Troupe," making his name known throughout the peninsula (半島). Like Messrs. Kim Sorang (金少浪) and Kim Tosan (金陶山), he was born in Sŏul and did not receive advanced formal education (高等 學問) early on, but his enterprising spirit (進取的 氣像) that hated losing to others and his talent (材質) surpassing others ultimately made him succeed as a great actor (名優).

His most signature role is "Wŏrhon" (月魂). It is, of course, a Japanese *sinp'a* play, also called "Getsukon" (月魂) in Japanese. This masterpiece (名作), taking a love triangle (三角戀愛) as its theme (主題), greatly wrung tears from the hearts of the youth (青年) of the time (當時).

It is said that Mr. Lim Sŏnggu still holds great dreams for the theater world.＜6＞

**Summary**

This article introduces Lim Sŏnggu as the progenitor of Korean *sinp'a* (new school) drama. Despite lacking higher education, his ambition and talent led him to establish the successful "Lim Sŏnggu Troupe" after early minor stage appearances in Korea and Japan. His most acclaimed role was in the Japanese play *Wŏrhon* (also *Getsukon* in Japanese), a tear-jerking love triangle story popular among contemporary youth. The article notes he still harbors aspirations for the theatre. From a historical perspective, the article highlights the significant Japanese influence (*sinp'a*, the play *Wŏrhon/Getsukon*) on early modern Korean theatre during the colonial period. It also underscores the emergence of individual star actors like Lim Sŏnggu and the formation of travelling troupes as key elements in the development of popular theatre in Korea.

名優와 舞臺
우리 「조선 신파극게의 원조」라는 월개관을 드릴 분을 손곱자면 누구든지 모다 림성구 씨를 가르칠 것이다. 림성구 씨는 처음 서울, 일본 어느 극장의 시언치 못한 무대에 출연하엿다가 발을 싯고 다시 「림성구 일행」이란 극단을 창설하여 그 일홈을 반도에 날리든 분이다. 김소랑, 김도산 량씨와 가치 서울출생으로 일즉 고등한 학문을 배우지 못하엿스나, 원래 남에게 지기 시려하는 그 진취적 긔상과 남보다 뛰여나는 재질은 끗끗내 그를 명배우로 성공시키고 말엇다.
그의 가장 마친 역은 「월혼」(月魂)이다. 무론 일본 신파극인데 일본 말로도 「月魂」이다. 이것은 삼각연애를 주제삼아 당시 청년의 가슴의 눈물을 몹시 짜내든 명작이다.
림성구 씨는 지금도 극단에 대한 크다란 꿈을 안고 잇다든가.＜6＞
