Famous Actors and the Stage

Kim Tosan! This name echoed like thunder in the early Korean new drama scene. Everyone likely remembers the fervent welcome he received when he led the Kim Tosan troupe around P'yŏngyang, Taegu, Chŏlla Province, and Hamgyŏng Province, presenting stages like "Oh, Heaven's Mandate" (오호, 천명) or "Changhanmong" (장한몽) as gifts. Kim Tosan was born in Sŏul. From a young age, he loved theatre. Moreover, his personality and talent led to his success as a stage actor.

He appeared on several dozen stages, but among them, his most fitting work was *Changhanmong*, and within *Changhanmong*, it was the role of Paek Nakkwan. Paek Nakkwan, the incomparably close, chivalrous friend of Yi Suil! Just thinking of Paek Nakkwan, doesn't Kim Tosan's true character come vividly to mind? <10>

**Summary**

This short piece reminisces about the fame of Kim Tosan, a prominent actor in early Korean *sin'gŭk* (new drama). It highlights his widespread popularity across Korea while touring with his troupe, performing plays like *Changhanmong*. The article specifically praises his portrayal of Paek Nakkwan in *Changhanmong* at the Tansŏngsa theater as his defining role, emphasizing how the character embodied the actor's own spirit. For historians, this article offers a glimpse into the vibrant theatre scene of 1930s colonial Korea, showcasing the popularity of *sin'gŭk*, the celebrity status of actors like Kim Tosan, and the cultural significance of specific plays like *Changhanmong* (itself an adaptation of a Japanese novel, indicating cultural exchange and adaptation).

名優와 舞臺
김도산! 이란 일홈은 초대의 조선 신극게에 우뢰가치 들니든 일홈이다. 그가 평양이고, 대구고, 전라도고 함경도로 김도산 일행의 극단을 끄을고 「오호, 천명」이나 「장한몽」의 무대를 선물삼아 도라다닐 때에 렬광적 환영을 밧든 일은 누구나 긔억 할 것이다. 김도산이는 서울서 낫다. 그는 어릴 때부터 연극을 조와하엿다. 더구나 그의 성격과 재일은 무대의 배우로서 성공하엿다.
그가 출연한 무대는 여러 수 십장이 되엿스나, 그 중에 가장 마친작은 장한몽이요, 장한몽 중에서도 백락관이엿다. 리수일과 다시 둘도 업게 친한 의협남아 백락관! 백락관을 생각만 하여도 김도산의 전면목이 약여하지 안흔가!＜10＞
