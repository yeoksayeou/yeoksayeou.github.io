社告 (Company Notice)

一, 電話 (Telephone)
本社의 電話는
光化門五五七番이외다
(Our company's telephone number is Kwanghwamun 557.)

二, 振替 (Transfer)
本社振替口座
京城二九四九番이외다
(Our company's bank transfer account number is Kyŏngsŏng 2949.)

三, 支社 (Branches)
地方各處에 支社를 設置함니다 有意
한분은곳 內規를 請하시압
(We are establishing branches in various regions. Interested individuals should please request the internal regulations.)

三千里社＜224＞
(Samch'ŏlli Company <224>)

**Summary**

This brief notice from the Samch'ŏlli Company provides essential contact and business information: their telephone number (Kwanghwamun 557), their bank transfer account number (Kyŏngsŏng 2949), and an announcement that they are seeking to establish regional branches, inviting interested parties to inquire about the internal rules. For historians, this notice offers a glimpse into the basic operational details of a Korean publishing house in Seoul (Kyŏngsŏng) during the colonial period, including its communication methods (telephone), financial transactions (bank transfer), and expansion plans (regional branches). The use of specific location identifiers like Kwanghwamun and Kyŏngsŏng grounds the business firmly within the colonial capital.
