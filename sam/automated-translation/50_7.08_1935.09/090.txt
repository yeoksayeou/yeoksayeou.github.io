드른 風月記,
人生不睦記

兩社長非親和由來

There is a newly revealed secret story concerning the Chosun Ilbo and Dong-A Ilbo, which had a fiery clash not long ago. It relates to the discord between Dong-A President Song Chin-u and Chosun Ilbo President Pang Ŭng-mo. It was said that even when invited by the Governor-General to events at places like the Chosun Hotel where presidents of various newspaper companies were seated together, the two would turn away from each other even if they met in the hallway. Once seated, it was said one would sit as if in the Arctic Ocean and the other in the Antarctic Ocean, tens of thousands of *ri* apart. Although one might doubt if it was really so, not long ago, on a narrow side street leading to the Ewha Womans College's new building dedication ceremony in Sinch'on, Seoul, the two happened to meet directly, but both averted their gaze without greeting and passed by. Many people who happened to be passing by witnessed this and were surprised. Also, one evening at the home of a certain technical college principal, where 7 or 8 prominent figures like Yun Ch'i-o were gathered for dinner, President Song, perhaps sensing President Pang's presence, ultimately remained absent. This gave the strong impression to everyone seated together that evening that the two were indeed on bad terms, just as rumored. Furthermore, there were many witnesses who saw the two occasionally meet at Chongno Intersection or Kwanghwamun Intersection, only to look up at the clear blue sky and pass by, pretending not to see each other. Therefore, people speculate about the cause of this discord. Some suggest it stems from resentment Pang Ŭng-mo felt during his difficult times (失意時代) back in his hometown Chŏngju, when he ran a Dong-A Ilbo branch office, failed, and faced compulsory execution (강제집행). However, according to what I have heard (드른풍월), that is not the reason. Shortly after Pang became president, at a gathering of 3 or 4 individuals associated with Dong-A Ilbo at Myŏngwŏlgwan restaurant,<64> Pang's name came up. At that time, a certain individual insultingly referred to him as "that mere mine owner fellow" (광부작자) and advised others not to associate with such a person. A *kisaeng* who was present at the banquet that day relayed this remark to Pang. Consequently, Pang thought, "I am a mine owner. So what if I am a mine owner? Weren't you idle sons of Chŏlla Province landlords? How are you any better?" From then on, it is said, the two became completely estranged – so some people confidently relate the story.
However, a matchmaker (月下氷人) recently appeared. It was Cho Man-sik (曺晩植), who came up from P'yŏngyang. Shortly after the clash between the two newspaper companies, he held a tea party (茶菓會) at the Chosun Hotel one day, inviting Kim Sŏng-su, Song Chin-u, and Kim Pyŏng-no from Dong-A, and Pang Ŭng-mo and Sŏ Ch'un from Chosun, to work towards reconciliation. The main outcome was that they managed to show smiling faces to each other after a long time. However, onlookers referred to this smile as the "Stone Buddha's Bitter Smile Curtain" (石佛苦笑幕). In any case, have the two become friendly nowadays?

金若水氏와 兪鎭熙

Both are giants in their respective fields. Moreover, called "comrades" (컴렛드), they were bound by the same ideological ties, even going to villas together, eating from the same pot, and drinking from the same well for 5 or 6 years. To outsiders, they might seem like blood brothers who would cut off their own flesh for each other, but in reality, it is not so. The two are not that close, nor do they fight when they meet. It is merely worth mentioning that the degree of their intimacy (親密濃度) differs somewhat from what people perceive.

李光洙氏와 兪億兼氏

The discord between Yŏnhŭi College Vice Principal Yu Ŏk-kyŏm and Yi Kwang-su is also famous. To trace its cause, one must go back many years to the factional strife (派爭) overseas. That is, when Ch'unwŏn (Yi Kwang-su's pen name) returned from Shanghai, he was associated with the Tosan (An Ch'ang-ho's pen name) faction, while Yu Ŏk-kyŏm was with the Yi Kwang-su and Syngman Rhee faction. Thus, the two stood as sharp points (尖銳) of opposing systems, which harmed even their friendship. Anyone with some knowledge (識見聞) of the history of the overseas movement knows about the factional strife between Tosan and Syngman Rhee in Hawaii (布哇) and America (米洲), so I will not write further.<65>

**Summary**

This article discusses several notable personal feuds among prominent Korean figures in 1935. It details the cold relationship between Song Chin-u (Dong-A Ilbo president) and Pang Ŭng-mo (Chosun Ilbo president), citing specific incidents and dismissing rumors about its origins, suggesting instead an insult ("mine owner fellow") as the cause. An attempted reconciliation by Cho Man-sik is mentioned. It also touches upon the less-than-close relationship between Kim Yak-su and Yu Chin-hŭi despite their past association, and the well-known animosity between Yi Kwang-su and Yu Ŏk-kyŏm, tracing it back to overseas Korean independence movement factionalism (An Ch'ang-ho vs. Syngman Rhee). For historians, this piece offers valuable insights into the personal dynamics and rivalries among key intellectuals and media figures during the Japanese colonial period, revealing divisions and personal animosities that existed alongside broader nationalist sentiments. The mention of specific incidents, rumors, and the connection to overseas political factions provides a nuanced look at the complexities within the Korean elite at the time.

兩社長非親和由來

얼마 전에 불이 벗적나게 한바탕 싸홈을 치르고 난 조선일보와 동아일보 사이에 한가지 새삼스럽게 밝혀진 秘話가 잇다. 그는 동아사장 송진우씨와 조보사장 방응모씨의 불목이라 들리는 말에 가령 조선호텔 가튼 데서 총독의 초대가 잇서 각 신문사상이 열석할 적에도 두 분은 복도에서 맛나서도 외면하고, 자리에 드러가서도 한분은 북빙양(北氷洋)에 한분은 남빙양에 상거 수만리로 논아 안는다 하는 말이 잇서 설마 그려라 하엿더니, 얼마전에 서울 신촌, 이화전문 신축낙성식으로 가는 외골목 길에서 두분이 서로 딱 맛낫건만 양편이 다 인사업시 외면하고 지내더란 것을, 마츰 그리로 지나가든 여럿이 보고 놀냇다 하며 또 모전문학교장 집에서 하롯 저녁 윤치오 등 명사 7, 8분이 모여 저녁을 자시기로 되엇슬 적에도 방사장이 온 눈치를 알엇슴인지 송사장은 끝끝내 결석하여, 듯든 말과 가치 두 분이 정말 불목하구나 하는 인상을 그날 저녁 동석한 여러분에게 주엇다하며 또 종로 네거리나 광화문 네거리에서 각금 두분이 맛나건만 피차에 청천하늘만 처다보며 본체 만체 지나가버리든 광경을 본 사람이 여럿이 잇섯다. 그러기에 세상에선 그 불목된 원인을, 방응모씨가 고향 정주에서 실의시대(失意時代)에 동아지국을 하다가 실패보아 강제집행을 맛난 그 원한 때문이라고 아는 듯 이약이 하는 이 잇건만 그런 것 아니고 나의 드른풍월을 긔하면 방씨 사장 된 뒤 미구에 명월관에서 동아계의 모모씨 등 3, 4인이 모힌 석상에서<64> 방씨 이약이 낫는데 그때 모씨가 『그까짓 광부작자』라고 모욕하는 말을 하며 그런 사람과 친하지 말나고- 이 말을 그날 그 연회석상에 가치 안젓든 긔생아씨가 그만 방씨에게 일너바첫거든 그래서 방씨는 『나는 광부다. 광부면 엇더냐, 너히는 놀고 먹는 전라도 지주 아들이 아니엇더냐 무에 나으냐』하는 생각이 드러 그 뒤부터는 아조 두분이 토라진 사이가 되엇다고 본드시 이약이 하는 사람도 잇다.
그러나 월하빙인(月下氷人) 한분이 근래에 나타낫다. 그는 평양서 올나온 조만식(曺晩植)씨라 두 신문사 싸홈 즉후에 어느날 조선호텔 다과회(茶菓會)를 열고 동아로부터 김성수, 송진우, 김병로 삼씨, 조선으로부더 방웅모, 서춘, 량씨를 오게 하여 화친공작을 하도록 한 바, 요는 오래간만에 피차에 웃는 낫츨 지어 보게 하엿슬 뿐 이 우슴을 世人은 「石佛苦笑幕」이라고 엇잿든 이즈음은 두분은 친하여 젓는지?

金若水氏와 兪鎭熙

두분 다 일방의 거인이다. 더구나 『컴렛드』라 하여, 그 사이에 갓튼 노내끈에 얽매어 별장에도 함께 가서 한솟헤 밥과 한움물의 물을 마시기를 5, 6년씩 하든 사이라. 남들이 보기에는 두분은 골육을 서로 버혀줄 친형제 가튼 사이라 하겟스나, 실상은 그러치 아녀서, 두분은 그리 갓가운 사이도 아니고, 그러타고 맛나서 싸우는 사이도 아니다. 다만 두분의 친밀농도(親密濃度)가 세상사람 각과는 다소 차이 잇슴을 말하여 둘 뿐이다.
李光洙氏와 兪億兼氏 延禧專門副校長 兪億兼氏와 李光洙氏의 不睦이란 또한 유명하다. 그 까닭을 따저 보자면 벌서 여러해 전, 海外의 派爭에까지 溯及하지 안으면 안되겟스니, 즉 春園이 上海로부터 드러올 때 島山系로, 李光洙, 李承晩系로 兪億兼 이리하야, 두분은 대립된 두 系統의 尖銳로써, 그 우정까지 害하엿든 것이다. 布哇와 米洲에 잇서 島山 對 李承晩의 派爭이란, 海外運動史에 다소 識見聞이 잇는 사람은 다 알 일이기 더 길게 쓰지 안으련다.<65>
