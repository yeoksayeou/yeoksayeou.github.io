Article Title: 春의 小曲 (Small Song of Spring)

Article Type: 시 (Poem)

Small Song of Spring
						No Ch'unsŏng

One, Spring Breeze
Spring breeze, spring breeze, descending upon the earth
Comes dancing with the pussy willows
Comes smiling with the pasqueflower blossoms

Girl foraging for greens, gather the greens though you may
Do not pick the pasqueflower plant
Boys playing the flute, play the flute though you may
Do not break the pussy willow branches

Spring breeze, spring breeze, descending upon the earth
Into the flower basket of the girl foraging for greens
It quietly nestles and flows away

Spring breeze, spring breeze, descending into the stream <94>
Into the willow flute of the playful shepherd boy
It is carried serenely and flows away

Two, Azalea
At the foot of the winding slope of Pugaksan (北岳山) Mountain
While the azalea flower sleeps in the night dew

The flowing crescent moon, longing for its form,
Watches over that sleeping flower! <95>
<94-95>

**Summary**

This two-part poem by No Ch'unsŏng celebrates the arrival of spring. The first part, "Spring Breeze," personifies the breeze as it interacts gently with nature (willows, pasqueflowers) and children (a girl foraging, a boy playing a flute), urging preservation of the delicate spring elements. The second part, "Azalea," paints a brief, evocative image of azaleas sleeping under the moonlight on Pugaksan mountain. From a historical perspective, the poem offers a glimpse into the appreciation of nature and traditional spring imagery in Korea during the colonial period. Its gentle, pastoral tone, focusing on natural beauty and innocent childhood activities, contrasts with the often harsher political realities of the time, perhaps representing a form of cultural refuge or a continuation of traditional lyrical themes despite the colonial context.

春의 小曲
						盧春城
一, 봄바람
봄바람 봄바람 땅우에 나려
강아지 버들과 춤추며 오네
할미꼿 송이와 웃으며 오네

나물캐는 少女여 나물은 캐도
할미꼿 나무는 캐지를 마소
피리부는 少童들 피리는 불어도
강아지 버들은 꺽지를 마소

봄바람 봄바람 땅우에 나려
나물캐는 少女의 꼿바구니 속에
가만히 안겻다 흘너가네

봄바람 봄바람 시내에 나려＜94＞
장난치는 牧童의 버들피리 속에
고요히 실녓다 흘너가네

二, 진달래
北岳山 굽으러진 언덕밋에
진달내꼿은 밤이슬에 잘때

흘으는 조각달 머옴 그리워
잠자는 그 꼿을 보고 잇서라! ＜95＞
＜94-95＞
