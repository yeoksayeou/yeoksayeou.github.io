We will be establishing Samch'ŏlli branch offices (三千里支社) and sales outlets (販賣所) in various locations (各處), so those who are interested (希望하시는분), please request the internal regulations (內規).

Business Bureau (營業局) <119>

**Summary**

This brief announcement from the *Samch'ŏlli* magazine's Business Bureau, dated April 1, 1932, states the intention to establish branch offices and sales outlets in various places. It invites interested individuals to request the internal regulations governing these potential partnerships. From a historical perspective, this notice highlights the commercial expansion efforts of a Korean cultural publication during the Japanese colonial period. It suggests the magazine was actively seeking to broaden its distribution network, which could be of interest to historians studying Korean media, business practices, and cultural resilience under colonial rule.
