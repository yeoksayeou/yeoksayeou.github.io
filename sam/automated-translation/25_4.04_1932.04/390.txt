Publication Department Announcement

Because the voices seeking "Peace and Freedom" have become impassioned everywhere (坊坊谷谷), we have now released a budget edition (廉價版) so that even those without money can all read it. Please order many copies.

The Danish Gymnastics Method (丁抹 체조법) will be released on April 13th, Mr. Kim Tongin's (金東仁) "The Sleeping One" (잠자는) on April 15th, the New Literature Anthology (新文學 選集) on April 25th, and the Folk Song Collection (민요집) will also begin sales from late April.

Mr. Yi Kwangsu's (李光洙) "A Revolutionary's Wife" (혁명가의 안해) sold out (賣盡) its 3,000 copies in just one year, and now a second edition (再版) has been published.

From now on, our company will pour all its efforts into the publishing department and will publish 3 or 4 standalone books (단행본) each month.<120>

**Summary**

This is an announcement from the publishing department of the Samch'ŏlli company. It reports the release of a budget edition due to popular demand for "Peace and Freedom." It lists upcoming publication dates for several books: "Danish Gymnastics Method," Kim Tongin's "The Sleeping One," a "New Literature Anthology," and a "Folk Song Collection." It also notes the successful sell-out and reprinting of Yi Kwangsu's "A Revolutionary's Wife" and states the company's plan to publish 3-4 new books monthly. For historians, the mention of widespread desire for "Peace and Freedom" necessitating a budget edition offers insight into the socio-political climate and readership interests of 1932 Korea. The active publishing schedule, including works by prominent authors like Kim Tongin and Yi Kwangsu alongside practical guides (gymnastics) and cultural collections (folk songs), highlights the vibrant, albeit constrained, cultural production during the colonial period.

出版部告
「평화와 자유」를 찻는 소리가 坊坊谷谷에서 白熱化하엿삽기 이제 廉價版을 발매하여 돈 업는 이들까지 모다 보시도록 하엿슴니다. 만히 주문하여주시압.

丁抹 체조법은 4월 13일에 발매되고 金東仁씨의 잠자는 4월 15일에 또 新文學 選集은 4월 25일에 발매되오며 민요집도 4월 하순부터 발매하겟슴니다.

李光洙 씨의 「혁명가의 안해」도 겨우 1個年 간에 3,000부를 賣盡하고 이제 再版을 발행하엿슴니다.

압흐로 본사는 출판부에 전력을 너허 매월 단행본 3,4冊式은 발행하겟슴니다.＜120＞
