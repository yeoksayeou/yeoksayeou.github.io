Sending Out the April Issue

Open the window, and it is already spring. To say "Spring has come, but it doesn't feel like spring" (春來不似春) are words to be cursed (咀呪), but anyway, the trees on Namsan and Pugak have begun to green, and the cherry blossoms (벗꼿) in Ch'anggyŏngwŏn have started to bloom, so what could this be if not spring? Gazing upon the ripening spring colors atop the walls of Hanyang (漢陽城頭) like this, our mood as we prepare and send out this Special Expanded Issue (特大號) is carefree (悠悠蕩蕩) and vast (浩浩蒼蒼), making us endlessly happy.

However, these are just words put together intentionally. In reality (實狀), we are endlessly sorry. Although we called it a Special Expanded Issue (特大號), we are ashamed, realizing ourselves that it was somewhat lacking in page count (頁數) and that the content did not amount to much that would cause a sensation of the generation (一世의 센세슌).

In the initial editorial plan, we intended to have 180 pages (頁數), 8 pages of illustrations (畵報), and splendid content, but midway, we ended up with quite a few characters "unable to have the freedom to be put into movable type" (活字化하는 자유를 가지지 못하는), and suddenly everything was reduced, resulting in this. Please understand (諒解) this sorrow, and do not excessively scold the inadequacy of this issue.

Instead, next month's issue will be increased in page count (頁數) compared to a regular issue as a service. The fixed price is 20 Chŏn. <120>

**Summary**

The editor expresses mixed feelings about releasing the April 1932 issue. While celebrating the arrival of spring in Seoul (Hanyang), they apologize profusely for the issue falling short of the planned "Special Expanded Issue" status in terms of page count and sensational content. The editor explicitly blames this reduction on encountering material that was "unable to have the freedom to be put into movable type," a clear euphemism for censorship under Japanese colonial rule. They ask for the readers' understanding and promise an enlarged issue next month. For historians, the article is a candid, albeit veiled, admission of the direct impact of Japanese censorship on Korean publications, revealing the constraints faced by editors and the strategies (apology, promise of future compensation) used to manage reader expectations under such conditions.

四月號를 보내면서
창문을 여니 벌서 봄이외다. 春來不似春이라 함은 咀呪할 文字이지만 아무튼 南山과 北岳에 나무입 푸르기 시작하고 昌慶苑에 벗꼿이 피기 시작하니 이것이 봄이 아니고 무엇이리까. 이러틋 漢陽城頭에 무르녹는 春色을 바라보면서 이번 特大號를 꾸미어 보내는 우리의 기분은 悠悠蕩蕩하고 浩浩蒼蒼하야 유쾌하기 짝이 업슴니다.

그러치만 이것은 일부러 지어서 하여보는 말이지요. 實狀은 죄송하기 끗이 업슴니다. 特大號라 하여노코 頁數에 잇서 좀 적엇고 내용에 잇서 一世의 센세슌을 이르킬 것이 만치 못하엿슴을 스사로 깨닷고 붓그러워 함니다.

처음 편집계획에는 頁數 180頁 畵報도 8頁, 내용도 훌융하게 하려고 하엿든 바 中途에 「活字化하는 자유를 가지지 못하는」 글자를 다분히 가지게 되어 돌연히 전부가 축소되어 이러케 되어버렷슴니다. 이 서름을 여러분은 諒解하시고 이번 號의 빈약을 과히 꾸짓지 마르소서.

그 대신 來月號는 보통호보다 頁數를 증가하여 奉仕하겟나이다. 定價는 20錢. ＜120＞
