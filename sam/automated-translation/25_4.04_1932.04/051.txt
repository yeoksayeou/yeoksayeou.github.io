Company Notice

Regarding the Analysis of the Structure of the Big Capital Camp:

○ The thesis by Mr. Ch'anghaegaek on "The Contents of Tongch'ŏk (東拓)" and ○ the thesis by Mr. Kim Tongjin on "The Outline of the Hyangch'u Fishing Grounds (香推漁場)" could not be published at this time.

Samch'ŏlli Editorial Department <27>

**Summary**

This brief notice from the Samch'ŏlli Editorial Department informs readers that two scheduled articles, one analysing the Oriental Development Company (Tongch'ŏk) by Ch'anghaegaek and another on the Hyangch'u Fishing Grounds by Kim Tongjin, were unable to be published in the current issue. These articles were part of a larger feature on "Big Capital". The notice is significant for colonial history as it likely points towards censorship or external pressures preventing the publication of potentially critical analyses of major colonial economic institutions (like the powerful Tongch'ŏk) and resource control (fishing grounds). Historians might see this as evidence of the challenges faced by Korean publications attempting to discuss sensitive economic and political topics under Japanese rule.
