Birth Control Clinic/Office
						Medical Doctor MB

Question: Recently, there have been many newspaper advertisements promoting certain medicines and devices for contraception (避姙). Are these truly reliable?
(Pak Ok-sun, P'yŏngyang)

Answer: While some might be trustworthy, most are likely not. What requires particular attention is that contraceptive devices (避姙器具) have no effect (効力) just by purchasing the device itself. The method of use absolutely must be scientific, so to speak. Let me give an example. Even with the Dutch pessary ("닷지펫싸리"), considered the safest and most reliable for women, it is ineffective unless the rubber cup-like pessary is inserted very well into the cavity (腔) to completely cover the head of the uterus (子宮). As you may know, the structure of a woman varies somewhat from person to person. Therefore, one must use a device suitable for them. That is, the goal of contraception can only be achieved after accurately determining things like size (大小) and shape (形體) without error before use. To practice this completely and train women to the point where they can use it proficiently on their own, it is necessary to receive medical guidance. In foreign countries, especially large cities in the UK (英國) or the US (美國), there are "Pregnancy Consultation Offices" (姙娠相談所). These are places that teach the methods of using and selecting contraceptive devices. I believe that only when such facilities are established quickly in Korea (조선) can birth control (産兒調節) be carried out completely.<59>

Question: I keep hearing about the "Dutch pessary" ("닷지,펫사리"). How much does this device cost, and where is it sold? I wish to remain anonymous, so please just provide the address and the fixed price.
(Frustrated Woman, Taegu)

Answer: It is currently available in Japan (日本). There are three types: one used by women who have not yet given birth (small size - 小形), one used by those who have given birth (medium size - 中形), and also a very large one (large size - 大形). The price is 1 won 50 jeon each.

Due to requests from our readers (讀者諸氏), our company's agency department (本社代理部) has decided to handle the sale (販賣) of the "Dutch pessary."

Question: Regarding temporary, voluntary contraception methods (任意 避姙法), please inform me about harmless, safe, and reliably effective methods concerning washing (洗滌法) and medications.
(A Reader, Kyŏngsŏng)

Answer: Regarding uterine washing (子宮洗滌法), that is, rinsing with water afterward, its effectiveness is still considered to be under testing, and there are opinions that it is not very reliable. Regarding medications, we are not yet at liberty to speak. Similarly, for men, the rubber condom ("곤도-무") and for women, things like the "Dutch pessary" are relatively simple to use and their effectiveness can be guaranteed. Termination of pregnancy (姙娠中絶) can be done with Roentgen rays ("렌도겐").

Question: Are there any statistics compiled on maternal mortality due to childbirth?
(A Doctor, Kyŏngnam)

Answer: I don't know if the Government-General (總督府) has them. The example from Japan (日本) is said to be four deaths per thousand.

Question: If the implementation of birth control laws (産兒制限法) is encouraged, what will generally happen to the morals (風紀) of our society? Won't young men and women (靑年男女) think it's great and public morals become disordered (紊亂)?
(A Moralist, Puyŏ)

Answer: Do not worry. Young men and women (靑年男女) understand that healthy social development begins with appropriate sexual lives (性慾生活), and they possess sufficient self-control (自制力).<60>

**Summary**

This 1931 article from "Samch'ŏlli" magazine, presented as a Q&A with a medical doctor (MB), discusses contraception in colonial Korea. It addresses the unreliability of advertised methods, strongly recommending the Dutch pessary for women and condoms for men, emphasizing the need for proper fitting, scientific use, and medical guidance for the pessary. It mentions the availability of pessaries in Japan and that the magazine will begin selling them. Uterine washing is deemed unreliable, comment on drugs is withheld, and X-rays are mentioned for abortion. The article touches upon maternal mortality (citing Japanese statistics) and dismisses concerns that birth control access will lead to moral decline among youth, arguing for responsible sexual lives and self-control. Historically, the article is interesting as it shows the introduction and discussion of modern contraceptive methods (pessaries, condoms) in Korea during the colonial period, referencing practices in the West (UK, US) and Japan. It highlights the role of print media in disseminating health information, the nascent demand for contraception, the mention of the Government-General concerning statistics, and the societal tension between adopting modern health practices and traditional concerns about morality (風紀).

産兒調節所
						醫學博士 MB
問 近來 新聞廣告에 避姙을 하랴거든 이 藥品과 이 器具를 쓰라고 宣傳하는 것이 만흔데 그 것이 果然 밋을만한 것이 됨니까.
(平壤 朴玉順)
答 그중에는 安心하고 밋을만한 것이 잇기도 하겟지만 大槪는 밋을 것이 못됨니다. 더욱 注意하실 것은 避姙器具라 하는 것은 器具만 사와서는 何等 効力이 업슴니다. 그 사용법이 그야말로 科學的이 아니면 안됨니다. 내가 例를 한 가지 듬니다. 第一安全하고도 確實하다는 婦人用의 「닷지펫싸리」로 말할 지라도 고무로 만든 茶盞가튼 그 닷지펫싸리를 腔속에 아조 잘끼어서 完全하게 子宮의 머리를 업퍼버리는 것이 아니면 効力이 업슴니다. 아시겟지만은 婦人의 그 構造는 各人에 따라 모다 多少의 差異가 잇슴니다. 그럼으로 거기에 適合하는 器具를 써야함니다. 卽 大小라든지 그 形體란다든지 하는 것을 틀님이 업시 正確히 決定한 뒤에 使用하여야 避姙의 目的을 達할 수 잇슴니다. 이것을 完全히 練習하여 婦人들이 저흔자 使用할 수 잇는 程度로 熟練식히자면 아모래도 醫學의 指示를 밧는 것이 必要함니다. 外國 특히 英國이나 美國의 큰 都市로 가면 「姙娠相談所」라는 것이 잇슴니다. 이것이 곳 避姙器具의 使用方法과 選擇등을 가르처주는 곳이니 조선서도 이러한 施設까지 어서 잇게 되어야 産兒調節을＜59＞ 完全하게 하여갈 수 잇는줄암니다.

問 「닷지,펫사리」 「닷지 펫사리」하는데요, 그 긔구는 갑이 얼마나 가며 어듸에서 팜니까. 저는 일홈을 내기실흐니 그저 주소와 정가만 가르처주세요.
(大邱 답답女性)
答 日本에도 現在 잇슴니다. 그런데 아직 아히를 나어보지 안은 부인들이 쓴는 것(小形)과 아해를 나은 분들이 쓰는 것(中形)과 또 아조 크게 만든 것(大形)등 세종류가 잇는데 갑은 1원50전씩

讀者諸氏의 要求가 잇삽기에 本社代理部에서 「닷지 펫사리」의 販賣를 하기로 하엿슴니다.

問 一時的 任意  避姙法에 洗滌法과 약품에 대하야 無害安全 効果確實한 方法을 알려주시오.
(京城 一讀者)
答 子宮洗滌法 즉 그 뒤에 물로 씻는데 대하여는 그 効果를 아직 試驗中이라 할 것으로 그리 미들만한 것이 못된다, 하는 說도 잇슴니다. 藥品에 대하여는 우리는 아직 말할 自由가 업슴니다. 亦是 男子는 고무로 만든 「곤도-무」 녀자는 「닷지 펫사리」가튼 것이 使用하기에 比較的 簡單하고 効果도 保障할 수 잇다 할 것이지요. 姙娠中絶은 「렌도겐」으로 할 수 잇슴니다.

問 出産 때문에 죽어가는 母性의 統計를 調査한 것이 업슴니까.
(慶南 一醫師)
答 總督府에는 잇는지 모르겟슴니다. 日本의 例는  千名에 네명은 죽어간다고 함니다.

問 産兒制限法을 작고 實施하라고 하면 우리 社會의 風紀가 大體 엇더케 되겟슴니까. 靑年男女들은 차라 조타고 風紀가 紊亂하게 되지 안으리까요.
(扶餘 一道學者)
答 근심마십시요. 靑年男女들은 適當한 性慾生活을 함으로부터 비로소 社會의 健全한 發達을 할 줄 알고 그만한 自制力이 잇는 터입니다.＜60＞
