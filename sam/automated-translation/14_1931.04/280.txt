Gratitude and Apology

How should we relate the "movement for acquiring immediate benefits (當面利益의 獲得運動)" to the overall movement? Also, if there are immediate benefits we should obtain, how should we define them? As this is a topic that figures from both left and right factions (左右 各派 人士) who pay attention to our current situation (情勢) are equally concerned with and researching, this journal, to fulfill its preliminary scouting mission (前哨的 任務), has dealt with this issue in this month's issue. We hope that various figures in society will continue to discuss this proposed issue in the future. Finally, we introduce the writers. Mr. Yi Hangbal (李恒發) is a current central executive of Shin'ganhoe (新幹會), Mr. Kim Seyong (金世鎔) is a social critic, and Mr. Yun Hyŏngsik (尹亨植) is the former chairman of the Chosŏn Youth General Alliance Central Committee (前朝鮮靑總中央委員長).

Regarding the expansion of this journal as promised last month, we intended to include gravure plate photo reports (「그라비아」版 寫眞畵報), but because printing technology in Seoul is rudimentary (幼稚한), it would be difficult to achieve the expected effect (豫期의 効果), so we have suspended this for the time being. Instead, we have decided to increase the number of pages by approximately ten.

We are publishing the "Complete Collection of Modern Literature (近代文學全集)." It is worthy of treasured possession (珍藏의 値), so please order many copies. Also, because the company office was cramped (狹溢하기에), we have now moved the address to 22 Kyŏnji-dong (堅志洞 22番地). (End)

**Summary**

This editorial note from *Samch'ŏlli* magazine addresses the strategic debate within Korean movements regarding balancing the pursuit of immediate, tangible benefits with broader, long-term goals, inviting further discussion from both left and right factions. It introduces contributors discussing this topic, including figures associated with Shin'ganhoe and the Chosŏn Youth General Alliance. Practical updates include canceling planned gravure photo pages due to Seoul's limited printing capabilities, increasing page count instead, promoting a new "Complete Collection of Modern Literature," and announcing the magazine's office relocation. From a historical perspective, the article is interesting as it openly references the strategic discussions and ideological spectrum (left/right) within the nationalist and social movements during the colonial period, mentions prominent organizations like Shin'ganhoe, and highlights the practical constraints (like printing technology) faced by publishers in 1930s Seoul.

感謝와 謝罪
우리들은 「當面利益의 獲得運動」을 全體運動에다가 엇더케 關聯식혀야 할가. 또한 우리가 어더야 할 當面利益이 잇다면 그 規定을 엇더케 지어야 할가. 이것은 오Š˜날 우리 情勢에 留意하는 左右 各派 人士치고 한글가치 關心且硏究할 題目이기에 本誌는 그 前哨的 任務를 다하기 위하야 今月號에 이와 가치 이 問題를 取扱한 터이외다. 바라건대 提出된 이 問題에 대하야 압흐로도 社會의 諸氏 繼續 討議하여 주시압. 끗흐로 執筆者의 人物 紹介를 함니다. 李恒發씨는 現 新幹會中央幹部 金世鎔씨는 社會評論家 尹亨植씨는 前朝鮮靑總中央委員長.

前月에 約束한 바 本誌 擴張을 위하야 「그라비아」版  寫眞畵報를 실으려 하엿든 바 印刷術이 幼穉한 서울에선 豫期의 効果를 나타내기 어럽겟기에 當分 中止하고 그 代身 頁數를 約十頁 增加하기로 하엿슴니다.

「近代文學全集」을 發行함니다. 珍藏의 値가 잇스니 만히 注文하여 주시압. 그리고 社屋이 狹溢하기에 今에 住所를 堅志洞 22番地로 옴기엇슴니다.(파인)＜75＞
