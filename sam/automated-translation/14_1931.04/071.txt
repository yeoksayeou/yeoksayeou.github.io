Magazine Title: 三千里 第14號
Publication Date: 1931년 04월 01일
Article Title: (무제-국편) (Untitled - Editor's Note)
Author: 編輯人 (Editor)
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Apology · Editor's Postscript)

We feel regretful that the profile of the vanguard fighter (前衛鬪士), the essay on Yi Chuyŏn penned by Mr. Chungnim Wŏn'gŭn, and the contributions of several other individuals could not be published due to unavoidable circumstances (不得已한 事情).

—(Editor)—<30>

**Summary**

This brief editor's note apologizes for being unable to publish several articles, including a profile of a "vanguard fighter," an essay on Yi Chuyŏn by Chungnim Wŏn'gŭn, and other contributions, citing "unavoidable circumstances." This notice is interesting to historians as it likely points towards censorship or external pressures faced by Korean publications under Japanese colonial rule, particularly concerning potentially nationalist or leftist figures like a "vanguard fighter" or individuals like Yi Chuyŏn. The vagueness suggests sensitivity around the reasons for non-publication.
