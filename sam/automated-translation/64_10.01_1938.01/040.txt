Facilities and Their Reasons

Current Educational Facilities in Siberia = According to a survey as of September 1, 1935 (Shōwa 10), the number of schools ranging from elementary level to university/specialized colleges reached a high count of 785, with approximately 32,000 students. Propaganda education is emphasized for most of these students.

The Reason for Making Them Relocate

That is, firstly - as modern Koreans' sentiment towards the Soviet Union gradually became one of alienation (離反), especially due to the Manchurian Incident and the China Incident, feelings towards Japan deepened, and they came to celebrate the Manchukuo of the 'Kingly Way and Paradise' (王道樂土), leading to concerns about a succession of people communicating with Japan and Manchukuo (日滿) - it seems there were many reasons related to counter-espionage (防牒上), anticipating the risk that they could be utilized by Japan and Manchukuo in the event of an emergency. Secondly - it appears they intend to give the Far East region, whose industrial value has increased, to Soviet people, while relocating Koreans to regions not yet cultivated to utilize them for industrial development. This relocation plan seems to have been largely carried out in September. At the time of relocation, to prevent schemes such as Koreans fleeing or causing disturbances, the Red Army (赤軍) and GPU (게페우) were deployed in advance to the area, enforcing strict vigilance. The preparation period was no more than a week, forcing them to relocate leaving almost all their property behind.
Furthermore, using sweet talk like claiming the relocation destination was a fertile region, or that staying in the Far East region posed a risk of poison gas (毒瓦斯) damage due to the Japanese army, they completed the relocation. Under the surveillance of the GPU (게·페·우) from various places, groups ranging from tens to hundreds of people were moved to assembly points, then loaded 30-40 people at a time onto freight trains (貨物列車) and transported under the watch of armed GPU personnel, just like escorting criminals. <9>

**Summary**

This 1938 news report describes the state of education in Soviet Siberia and focuses on the purported reasons and methods behind the recent forced relocation of ethnic Koreans from the Soviet Far East. The article claims the Soviets initiated the deportation due to Koreans' increasing anti-Soviet sentiment, growing affinity towards Japan and Manchukuo following regional conflicts, and Soviet fears of potential espionage or collaboration in wartime. An economic motive is also suggested: transferring Koreans to undeveloped lands for exploitation while reserving the developed Far East for Soviet citizens. The report details the harshness of the deportation, including minimal notice, property confiscation, heavy surveillance by the Red Army and GPU, deceptive justifications, and transport in freight cars under armed guard. For historians, this article offers a contemporary, albeit likely influenced by the Japanese colonial perspective, account of the 1937 Soviet deportation of Koreans (Koryo-saram). It sheds light on the geopolitical anxieties of the era, Soviet internal security concerns and ethnic policies, and the traumatic experiences of the Korean diaspora caught between empires.

施設과 其原因
현재의 西伯利亞 교육상 시설= 소화 10년 9월 1일 현재의 조사에 의하면 초등정도로부터 대학전문에 이르기까지의 각 학교수는 785교의 다수에 달하야 그 생도수는 약 3만 2천여명이라고 이 학생들에는 대부분이 선전교육에 주력하고 잇다.

이주식히는 그 원인
즉 첫재는 -근대 조선인이 쏘비에트에 대한 감정이 점차 이반(離反)적 태도로 되고 특히 만주사변, 지나사변에 의하야 일본에 대한 감정이 두터워지고 또 왕도낙토(王道樂土)의 만주국을 구가하게 되어 일만(日滿)에 통첩하는 사람이 속출하는 것을 념려하고 - 즉 방첩상(防牒上)의 원인이 만흔듯하며 일단 유사한 때에는 일만간에 이용될 염려가 잇는 것을 미리 생각한 것이다. 둘째는 -산업적 가치가 증진된 극동지방을 쏘비에트인에게 주고 조선인에는 아직 개간하지 안흔 지방에 이주시켜 산업개발에 이용하랴는듯하고 이 이주게획은 9월중에 대체로 수행된 듯한데 이주할 때에는 조선인의 탈주, 소요등의 책모(策謨)를 방지하기 위하야 미리 그 지역에 적군(赤軍)과 게페우를 배치하야 경게를 엄중히 하고 준비기간도 일주일에 지나지 안는 상태이어서 거의 재산도 그대로 버리고 이주하지 안흐면 안되게 맨드럿다.
더욱이 이주하는 지방은 비옥한 지방이라든지 극동지방에 잇으면 일본군대 때문에 독와사(毒瓦斯)의 피해가 잇다는 감언으로써 이주의 실시를 마치고 각지로부터 게·페·우 감시하에 수십명내지 수백명씩 집합지에 이주시킨 후 화물렬차(貨物列車)에 3,40명씩 실어서 무장한 게·페·우의 감시로 마치 죄인을 호송하듯 이수송하엿다. ＜9＞
