**Body**

Public Notice
						Samch'ŏllisa

Regarding the Chosŏn New Economic Construction 3-Year Plan, Mr. Pae Sŏngnyong's (裵成龍氏) discussion concerning the Movement to Encourage Chosŏn Products (朝鮮 物産獎勵運動) could not be published due to circumstances (事情).<27>

**Summary**

This brief notice from the publisher, Samch'ŏllisa, states that an article by Pae Sŏngnyong about the "Movement to Encourage Chosŏn Products," part of a larger economic plan, could not be published due to unspecified "circumstances." This highlights the constraints faced by Korean publications during the Japanese colonial period. Historians might find this notice interesting as subtle evidence of censorship or external pressures preventing the discussion of nationalist economic movements like the promotion of domestic goods, which implicitly challenged Japanese economic dominance.
