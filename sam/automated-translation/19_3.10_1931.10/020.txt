**Body Text Translation:**
The Cultural Advancement of the Korean People and the National Grand Plan
						Shanghai An Ch'angho

Observing the current state of Chosŏn from afar overseas today, it is clear from statistics appearing in various newspapers and magazines that it has fallen into difficult economic circumstances, with the vast majority of the entire nation feeling the hardship of life and even the extremely small capitalist class rapidly declining. Nevertheless, it is deeply regrettable that, on the other hand, the National Grand Plan to overcome this national suffering and expand the nation's livelihood and political status is not being expressed with the same degree of concentrated focus. Class activists tend to become absorbed in overly subjective movements far removed from the reality of Chosŏn by emphasizing the political assertions unique to their class, while nationalist activists adopt an overly regressive and passive attitude, failing to concentrate unified political assertions and political views. <2> (omitted)

However, on the other hand, it is a very joyous phenomenon that recently, publications guiding and expressing the cultural enlightenment and political will of the masses have appeared in greater numbers with surprisingly improved content, both quantitatively and qualitatively, compared to the past.

This exemplifies that even though Chosŏn today may be in extreme poverty, it has culturally advanced much more than before, and the desire of the masses to rush towards a specific goal has become stronger than before. At this time, I hope that all leaders of Chosŏn will depart from their respective narrow views and subjective standpoints, unify and concentrate the strength and demands of the entire nation in a different form than before, based on a more profound National Grand Plan, so that a new momentum for the Chosŏn movement may unfold. In doing this work, I expect the magazine *Samch'ŏlli* (三千里) to also play a part, and knowing that it is doing so, I am glad. Now, celebrating its third anniversary, I commend the efforts of publisher Kim Tonghwan and wish him well for the future. (Remainder omitted - original text) <3>

**Summary**

From Shanghai, An Ch'angho observes Korea's dire economic situation and laments the lack of a unified "National Grand Plan" (民族的 大計) to address it. He criticizes both class activists for their subjectivism detached from reality and nationalist activists for their passivity and lack of cohesive political vision. However, he finds hope in the recent surge of publications aimed at cultural enlightenment and fostering political will, seeing it as evidence of cultural progress despite poverty and a growing popular desire for focused action. He urges leaders to transcend factionalism, consolidate national strength around a profound plan, and expresses hope that the magazine *Samch'ŏlli* will contribute to this new phase of the Korean movement. This article is interesting to historians as it reveals An Ch'angho's perspective as a leading nationalist figure in exile, highlighting the internal ideological divisions within the Korean independence movement (class vs. nationalism) during the colonial period. His emphasis on the need for both cultural development and a unified political strategy offers insight into the complexities of nationalist thought at the time.

朝鮮民族의 文化向上과 民族的 大計
						上海 安昌浩
오늘날 朝鮮의 現狀을 멀니 해외에서 觀察하건대 經濟的으로 어러운 處地에 빠지어 잇서서 全民族의 대부분이 생활의 곤란을 늣기고 극소 부분의 자산계급도 급속도로 몰락하여 가는 현상이 각 신문 잡지 등의 통계에 나타나는 바에 의하야 명백함에 불구하고 他面 이러한 민족적 수난를 타개하야 민족의 생활과 정치적 지위를 신장하려는 民族的 大計가 이와 동일정도로 집중 표현되지 못하고 계급 운동자는 계급 독자의 정치적 주장를 강조함으로써 조선의 현실 내용과 거리가 먼 넘머나 주관적 운동으로 열중하는 경향이 잇고 민족 운동자는 너머나 退嬰的 소극적 태도를 취하야 통일된 정치적 주장과 정치적 견해를 집중치 못함과 갓틈은 심히 憫着하는 바이다.＜2＞(중략)
그러나 一面 최근에 이르러 군중의 문화적 계몽과 정치적 의욕을 지도 표현하는 출판물이 과거보담 양적 질적으로 놀나운 내용을 가지고 만히 나타나는 것은 심히 깁버할 현상이다.
이것은 오늘날 조선이 아모리 빈궁한 가운데 잇다 할지라도 문화적으로 전보담 훨신 진보되고 또한 대중이 일정한 목표를 향하야 돌진하려고 하려는 욕구가 전보다 더욱 강하여짐을 例證하는 바니 이때에 잇서 조선의 모든 지도자는 각자 편협한 견해와 주관적 입장을 떠나 전민족의 힘과 요구를 전과는 다른 형식으로 좀더 深刻한 民族的 大計 우에서 통일 집중하야 조선운동의 新機運이 전개되기를 企望하고 이러한 일을 하는데 잇서 잡지 三千里도 일부의 역할을 演할 것을 기대하고 또한 그리하고 잇는 것을 알고 깁버하며 이제 三週年을 맛는 주간 金東煥氏의 노력을 稱揚하야 그 前途를 祝하는 바이다(下略-원문)＜3＞
