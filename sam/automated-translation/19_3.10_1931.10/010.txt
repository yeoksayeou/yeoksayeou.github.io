잡지명	삼천리 (三千里) 제3권 제10호
발행일	1931년 10월 01일
기사제목	最初의 任務, 本誌 3過年에 際하야 (The Initial Mission, On the Occasion of This Magazine's Third Anniversary)
필자	金東煥 (Kim Tong-hwan)
기사형태	사고·편집후기 (Notice / Editorial Postscript)

最初의 任務, 本誌 3過年에 際하야
						金東煥
1
Recently, a tense "Young Chosŏn" (청년조선) calls upon many people and busily entrusts them with tasks. Some are assigned the Singanhoe, some farmers' unions, labor unions, some newspaper companies, churches, banks, companies, and even down to sowing grain in the earth and distributing a single leaflet (삐라) to each house – all are entrusted. Among these, is it wrong to take pride in the fact that publishing the magazine "Samch'ŏlli" (삼천리) is also a solemn mission thrown upon us?

Only, as "Chosŏn" (조선) has been eventful during the three years since our founding, this magazine has also been eventful; and as the growth of our peninsular brethren has not been smooth, accordingly, the development of this magazine's members has also not been smooth, so we can only lament that our contribution to our culture has been small. However, had we only possessed money and a certain degree of freedom from censorship, we would not have appeared in this arena today with such a meager history. Come to think of it, like the numerous figures on our land who, though full of fighting spirit (투지만신), uselessly spend their time in small, humble houses (삼간두옥) with long sighs (장탄), our lonely strength has also passed three years of lamentation. Nevertheless, please watch over us for the long future ahead! That is all we can say.

2
Even so, we are resolved. The duty of publishing a magazine is, so to speak, the "initial task" (초긔의 임무) that "Chosŏn" has entrusted to us. Now, as all movements progress upwards, we know that new tasks of the second and third stages will be entrusted to us.

Already in Manchuria, clashes between the Japanese and Chinese armies (일중양군) have begun. The general situation in the Far East is racing towards some endpoint at extraordinary speed. Within the complex shadows emerging and disappearing on the international stage, might there not be a day when we discover our own situation?

Amidst this, our future path, one that does not violate justice and humanity, will surely be revealed. This magazine, too, intends to make steady efforts towards the discovery of that path.

Must those who possess an "unfortunate yesterday" (불행한 작일) always weep only in that "unfortunate yesterday"? Rather, we intend to cry out that the purpose of living creatures lies in striving for the happiness of today and tomorrow (명일).<1>

**Summary**

This editorial by Kim Tong-hwan reflects on the third anniversary of the magazine *Samch'ŏlli*. He frames the magazine's publication as a solemn duty assigned by "Young Chosŏn" amidst numerous other tasks given to Korean intellectuals and activists. He laments the magazine's limited achievements over three difficult years, attributing this to lack of funds and censorship, comparing their situation to frustrated patriots sighing in humble dwellings. However, he expresses resolve, viewing the magazine as an "initial task" and anticipating greater responsibilities as national movements progress. Referencing the escalating conflict in Manchuria, he hopes the magazine can help discover Korea's path forward amidst international turmoil, emphasizing the need to strive for future happiness rather than dwelling on past misfortunes.

Historically, the article is revealing for its candid mention of censorship and financial struggles faced by Korean publications under Japanese rule. It reflects the constrained yet persistent intellectual activity and nationalist sentiment of the era. The reference to the Manchurian Incident ("clash between Japanese and Chinese armies") and its potential implications for Korea highlights the acute awareness of regional geopolitics among Korean intellectuals. The framing of the magazine's work as a "mission" bestowed by "Young Chosŏn" underscores the sense of collective purpose and responsibility felt by cultural figures during the colonial period.

기사형태	사고·편집후기

最初의 任務, 本誌 3過年에 際하야
						金東煥
1
최근 긴장된 「청년조선」은 여러 사람을 불너서 분주히 일을 맛김니다. 누구는 신간회를, 누구는 농민조합, 노동조합을 누구는 신문사와 교회와 은행 회사를 하다 못해 땅에 곡식 뿌리고, 집집에 삐라 한 장 돌니는 일까지 모다 맛겨줌니다. 그 가운데 잡지 「삼천리」를 발행하라는 것도 또한 우리들에게 던지어진 엄숙한 사명이라고 자부하는 것이 그른 일이리까.
다만 창간이래 삼년동안 「조선」이 다사하엿슴애 본지 또한 다사하엿고 반도 형제의 성장이 순조롭지 못하엿슴애 따라 본지 동인의 발육이 또한 순조롭지 못하야 우리 문화에 공현한 바 적엇슴을 한탄할 밧게 업슴니다만은 그러나 돈과 어느 정도의 검열의 자유만 잇섯더라도 이러케 빈약한 력사를 가지고 오늘 이 마당에 나타나지 안엇슬 것이외다. 생각건대 투지만신 이면서도 부질 업시 삼간두옥에서 장탄으로 보내는 우리 땅 인사 다수한 모양으로 외로운 힘이 또한 탄식의 삼년을 보던냇 것이외다. 그러치만 이제 길게 압날을 두고 보아주십소! 할 뿐이외다.

2
그러터라도 우리는 각오하고 잇슴니다. 잡지 발행의 소임은 이를터이면 「조선」이 우리에게 맛기어준 「초긔의 임무」라고, 이제 모든 운동이 상향됨에 따라서 우리에겐 제2단계 제3단계의 새로운무가 맛기여 질 줄로 암니다.
벌서 만주에는 일중양군의 교전이 시작되엇슴니다. 극동의 대세는 비상한 속도로 어느 종점을 향하야 다름질을 치고 잇슴니다. 국제적 시야에 출몰하는 착잡한 그 그림자 속에 또한 우리 자신의 서태를 발견할 날이 잇지안으리까?
이 가운데서 정의와 인도을 어기지 안는 우리들의 장차 거러갈 길이 보여지겟지요. 본지 또한 그 길의 발견에 꾸준한 노력을 할가 하나이다.
「불행한 작일」을 가진 사람은 언제든지 「불행한 작일」에서만 울것이겟슴니까. 오히려 금일과 명일의 행복에 대하야 노력하는 데에 생물의 목적이 잇다 할 것을 웨치려 하나이다.＜1＞
