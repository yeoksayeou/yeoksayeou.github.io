Thoughts and Hopes
						Kim Ch'ŏl (金澈) in Shanghai (上海)

Although the number of those who devote their bodies and give their utmost sincerity for the Chosŏn (朝鮮) movement, both domestically (海內) and overseas (海外), cannot be said to be very small, the lack of an institutional publication expressing and exchanging unified will and concentrated theory up to the present day is a flaw. As someone who has been overseas for a long time and has not directly encountered the real situation in Chosŏn, I particularly regret that both those domestically and those overseas each tend to lack an accurate perception of Chosŏn's reality due to their one-sided observations. Of course, it is also true that realizing such a thing is difficult under the current situation in Chosŏn, but if it could be operated even within the minimum possible scope to suit the overall goal of national interest, I believe that the wise leaders of the populace or knowledgeable individuals involved in publishing within Chosŏn need to weigh the pros and cons regarding this issue.

Furthermore, although recently the issue regarding the relationship between nationalist activists (民族運動者) and class activists (階級運動者) tends to be interpreted by class activists as if it has been resolved extremely simply, I think that regarding this issue too, both sides are still governed only by their respective subjective perceptions. It seems they have not yet reached a proper understanding that plans for the greater scheme of the Chosŏn movement based on a cool, objective perspective. Therefore, rather than being mechanically dominated solely by the influence of some advanced country, there is truly a need, unchanged from the past, for individuals from various circles with differing views to broadly exchange and express their intentions in order to establish the reality of our nation and the best path forward for each movement. This also necessitates the existence of a conscientious, wise, and unbiased media institution.

Among the newspapers and magazines coming from Chosŏn that I read as attentively as possible, the good impression I received particularly from the magazine *Samch'ŏlli* (三千里) has given me considerable anticipation for realizing the couple of points I felt, as mentioned above. Therefore, I substitute this for congratulatory remarks while looking forward to the splendid view of the 3rd-anniversary commemorative issue, which will be produced as a crystallization of even more diligent effort. <92>

**Summary**

The author, Kim Ch'ŏl writing from Shanghai, laments the lack of a unified communication platform for the Korean independence movement, leading to fragmented perspectives between activists inside Korea (海內) and those overseas (海外). He criticizes both nationalist and class-based activists for subjective viewpoints hindering a unified strategy, stressing the need for objective discussion and an unbiased media outlet. He praises the magazine *Samch'ŏlli* for potentially fulfilling this role and expresses hope for its continued development. For historians, this piece offers insight into the internal divisions (geographical and ideological) within the independence movement during the colonial period, the challenges of communication under Japanese rule, and the perceived role and importance of journalism in fostering national unity and strategy.

所懷와 企望
						上海 金澈
朝鮮運動을 위하야 몸을 밧치고, 성의를 다하고 잇는 이들의 數는 海內海外을 통하야 그리 적다고는 할 수 업스나 오늘날까지 통일된 意思와 집중된 이론을 표시 교환하는 기관 출판물이 업슴으로 항상 海內는 海內대로 海外는 海外대로의 각자 一面的 觀察로써 朝鮮의 현실을 정확히 인식치 못하는 결함이 잇는 것은 海外에 오래 잇서 朝鮮의 實情에 직접 부듸치지 못하는 나로써는 특히 유감으로 생각하는 바이다. 물론 이러한 일은 現下의 朝鮮정세 下에서는 실현키 곤란한 일인 것도 사실이지마는 그러나 최소한도의 가능한 범위 내에서라도 이것을 민족적 이익의 전체목표에 적합하도록 운용할 수가 잇다 하면 朝鮮 內地에 잇는 현명한 민중의 지도자 또는 출판에 관계잇는 識者들은 이 문제에 대하야 이해득실을 權衡할 필요가 잇다고 생각한다. 더욱이 근간에 民族運動者와 階級運動者와의 관계에 대한 문제는 극히 간단히 해결된 것과 갓치 階級運動者들은 해석하는 경향이 잇스나 나는 생각건대 이 문제에 대하야도 어느 쪽이나 역시 각자의 주관적 인식만에 지배되야 냉정한 객관적 견해에 의한 朝鮮運動의 大計을 企圖하는 정당한 인식에는 아즉도 이르지 못한 것과 갓흠으로 기계적으로 엇더한 선진국가의 영향에만 지배되지 말고 진실로 우리 민족의 현실과 각자 운동의 最善進路를 規範키 위하야 見解相異한 각계의 인물이 널이 意思을 교환 표시할 필요는 녯이나 지금이나 달음업슴으로 이 또한 偏頗치 안코 양심 잇고 현명한 언론기관의 존재를 필요케 하는 것이다. 나는 朝鮮에서 오는 신문 잡지을 될 수 잇는대까지 주의하여 보는 중에서 특히 잡지 「三千里」에서 밧은 조흔 인상이 이상과 갓흔 두어 가지의 늣긴 바륊� 실현함에 잇서 상당히 余를 기대케 한 바 잇슴으로 더욱 꾸준한 노력의 結晶으로써 산출될 3週年 紀念號의 盛觀을 기대하면서 祝辭에 代하는 바이다.＜92＞
