○Urgent Notice
We are recruiting Samch'ŏlli branches within the country and overseas. Those interested, please request the internal regulations (enclose a 2-sen postage stamp).
Kyŏngsŏng Samch'ŏlli Sa <117>

**Summary**

This short notice is an announcement from the Samch'ŏlli Company, publishers of the Samch'ŏlli magazine, seeking individuals interested in establishing branch offices both within Korea ("海內") and abroad ("海外"). Prospective applicants are instructed to request the company's internal regulations by mail, enclosing a 2-sen postage stamp. This brief text reveals the magazine's ambition to expand its network and distribution during the colonial period, suggesting a degree of business activity and outreach extending potentially beyond the Korean peninsula. The requirement of a stamp for information highlights the common communication methods of the era.
