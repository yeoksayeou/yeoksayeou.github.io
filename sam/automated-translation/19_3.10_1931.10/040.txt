Samch'ŏlli Wall Newspaper (No. 1)
Doctoral Dissertations

Yi Sŭngman received a Doctor of Philosophy degree from Princeton University in the United States in 1911 for his dissertation titled "The Influence on the Neutrality of the United States of North America."

Chŏng Han'gyŏng received a Doctor of Philosophy degree from American University in 1921 for his dissertation titled "The Korean Incident."

Kim Yŏsik received a Doctor of Philosophy degree from American University in 1924 for "Early International Relations Between Korea and the United States of North America."

Yi Hun'gu is said to have received a Doctor of Philosophy degree from the University of Wisconsin in 1929 for "History of Korean Land Law and Policy." <6>

Samch'ŏlli Wall Newspaper (No. 2)
The amount Japan has invested in Manchuria is said to be about 1.5 billion won, 800 million won in Korea, and 300 million won in Taiwan.

The recently newly constructed and completed Korean Products Exhibition Hall of the Association for the Promotion of Native Products (物産獎勵會), located behind Pagoda Park in Sŏul, is a three-story brick building, and the construction cost was 15,000 won.

The four-story concrete Western-style building newly constructed next to the Hwasin Department Store on Chongno, Sŏul, is said to be funded by the Min Kyusik line, with construction costs of approximately 300,000 won. Regarding such large investments for a "building," there was previously the wealthy Yi Yongmun's building in Hwan'gŭmjŏng (黃金町), and now there is the Min Kyusik line's Chongno building. <7>

Samch'ŏlli Wall Newspaper (No. 3)
The Ten Richest People of Kaesŏng

In Kaesŏng, famous for having many rich people, until 2 or 3 years ago when the price of ginseng was good, there were more than 10 people with assets over 300,000 won, referred to as the Ten Great Magnates.

Kim Wŏnbae
Ch'oe Sŏnik
Ko Hansŭng
These gentlemen were all among the big rich people at that time. However, as the ginseng price sharply dropped, it is said that the number of Kaesŏng millionaires with over 300,000 won has now somewhat decreased. <8>
<6-8>

**Summary**

This article, presented as short news items, covers three topics: notable Koreans who received PhDs from American universities in the early 20th century, listing their names, universities, dissertation topics, and years; Japanese investment figures in Manchuria, Korea, and Taiwan, alongside recent construction projects in Seoul (a new exhibition hall and a large private building); and the changing fortunes of wealthy merchants in Kaesŏng, known for its rich populace, noting a decline in the number of top millionaires due to falling ginseng prices. For historians, the article offers insights into the early achievements of the Korean elite seeking education abroad (particularly future president Yi Sŭngman), the scale of Japanese economic penetration in its colonies and sphere of influence, urban development and capital investment in colonial Seoul, and the economic basis (and vulnerability) of regional wealth, specifically Kaesŏng's reliance on the ginseng trade.

三千里 壁新聞(제 1호)
博士論文
李承晩氏는 1911년에 米國 프린스톤대학으로부터 「북미합중국의 중립성에 대한 영향」이란 논문으로 철학박사의 학위를 엇고

鄭漢景氏는 1921년에 아메리칸대학에서 「조선사건」이란 논문으로 철학박사의 학위를 엇고

金麗植씨는 1924년에 아메리칸대학에서 「조선과 북미합중국과의 초기 국제관계」로 철학박사의 학위를 엇고

李勳求氏는 「조선 토지법 及 정책사」로 위스칸산 대학에서 1929년에 철학박사 학위를 엇엇다 한다.＜6＞

三千里 壁新聞(제2호)
日本이 滿洲에 투자한 금액은 약 15억만원이요, 조선에는 8억만원, 臺灣에는 3억만원이라 한다.

최근에 新築落成된 서울 「빠고다」공원 후편의 物産獎勵會의 朝鮮物産 진열관은 벽돌 삼층인데 건축비 1만 5천원이엇다.
서울 종로, 화신백화점 겻헤 새로 신축되는 4층 콩그리드 양옥은 閔奎植계의 출자로 건축비 대략 30만원이라 한다, 「삘딩」을 위하야 이러케 거액의 투자를 한 이는 전에 부자 李容汶의 黃金町 건물이 잇섯고 今에 閔奎植계이 종로삘딩이 잇다.＜7＞

三千里 壁新聞(제3호)
開城 10富者
부자 만키로 유명한 開城에는 2 ,3년 전까지 인삼시세 조홀 때는 30만원 이상의 부자가 10人 이상이나 잇서 十大부호라 하엿다.
金元培
崔善益
高漢承
等諸氏가 모다 당시 큰 부자들 중 1人이엇다 그리든 것이 인삼시세가 탁 떠러지면서 인제는 30만원 이상의 開成富者가 다소 줄어들엇다 한다.＜8＞
