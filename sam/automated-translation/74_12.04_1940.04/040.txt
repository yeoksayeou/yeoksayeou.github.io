Author: None specified
Article Type: 소식 (News/Report)

What are the main clauses of the Nine-Power Treaty that European and American nations frequently bring up, interpreting the purpose of Our Empire's Holy War (聖戰) with malicious intent?

Article 1. The signatory powers, other than the Republic of China (支那國), agree as follows:
1. To respect the sovereignty, independence, and territorial and administrative integrity (保金) of China (支那).
2. To provide the fullest and most unembarrassed opportunity to China (支那) to develop and maintain for herself an effective and stable government.
3. To use their influence for the purpose of effectually establishing and maintaining the principle of equal opportunity for the commerce and industry of all nations throughout the territory of China (支那).
4. To refrain from taking advantage of conditions in China (支那) in order to seek special rights or privileges which would abridge the rights of subjects or citizens of friendly States, and from countenancing action inimical to the security of such States.

Article 2. The contracting powers, other than China (支那國), agree as follows, for the purpose of more effectively applying the principle of the Open Door or equality of opportunity in China (支那) for the trade and industry of all nations:
a. Not to seek, nor support their respective nationals in seeking, any arrangement which might purport to establish in favour of their interests any general superiority of rights with respect to commercial or economic development in any designated region of China (支那).
b. Not to support any nationals in seeking any monopoly or preference which would deprive other nationals of the right of undertaking any legitimate trade or industry in China (支那), or of participating with the Chinese Government, or with any local authority, in any category of public enterprise, or which by reason of its scope, duration or geographical extent is calculated to frustrate the practical application of the principle of equal opportunity. <54>

**Summary**

This article outlines key provisions of the Nine-Power Treaty, specifically those concerning the respect for China's sovereignty, independence, territorial integrity, and the principle of equal opportunity ("Open Door") for commerce and industry within China. The article frames these treaty clauses as the "pretext" used by Western powers (歐米 諸國) who allegedly misunderstand the true purpose of Japan's "Holy War" (聖戰) in China. From a historical perspective, this piece is significant as it directly reflects Japanese wartime propaganda disseminated in colonial Korea. It attempts to justify Japan's actions in China, which violated the Nine-Power Treaty, by portraying Western criticism as malicious misunderstanding and positioning the treaty itself as merely a convenient excuse for Western interference, rather than a legitimate international agreement Japan was bound by. Historians would find this useful for understanding how Japan rationalized its expansionism and attempted to shape public opinion in its colonies regarding international relations and the war effort.

기사형태	소식

三千里 緊急 揭示板, 九個國 條約이란, 聖戰의 意義를 誤解하는 歐米 諸國의 口實은 잇든 것인가?
我 제국의 聖戰 목적을 악의로 해석하여 걸핏하면 歐米 諸國이 끄집어 드는 9개 조약의 주요 조항이란 어떤 것인가.
제1조. 支那國 이외의 체결국은 여좌한 약정이다.
1. 支那의 주권, 독립과 그 영토적 及 행정적 保金을 존중할 일.
2. 支那가 스스로 유력 또 안고한 정부를 확립 유지하기 위하여 가장 완전하게 그리고 장애 없을 기회를 줄 일.
3. 支那 전 영토에 각 국민의 상공업 상의 기회 균등 주의를 유효하게 확립하며 또는 유지하기 위하여 노력할 일.
4. 우호국 신민 또는 시민의 권리를 減殺할 특수 권리 혹은 특전을 獻得하기 위해서 支那에 있는 政情을 이용치 않고 又 우호국의 안녕을 해하는 행위를 지지할 일.
제2조. 支那國 제외한 각 체약국은 모든 국민의 상공업 때문에 支那에 있는 문호 개방 혹은 기회 균등 주의를 일층 유효하게 적용할 목적으로 여좌히 약정한다.
가. 支那 여하한 특정 지역에 있어서도 상업 혹은 경제 발전에 관해서 자기 이익 때문에 하등, 일반적 우월권을 설정시킨다는 협력을 구하지 말고 또는 인민의 이러한 협력을 구하는 것을 지지말 일.
나. 타국민이 支那에 있어서 적당한 상공업을 영위하는 권리 又는 그 종류의 여하를 물론하고 공공 기업을 支那 중앙 정부 혹은 지방 관권과 공동 경영하는 권리를 빼았는 일 같은 것, 그리고 규모, 기간, 또 지리적으로 기회 균등의 실제적 적용을 무효하게 하는 등, 여하한 독점권 혹은 인민이 이것을 구함을 지지하지 말 일.＜54＞
