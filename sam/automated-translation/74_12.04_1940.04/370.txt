We believe the roundtable discussion article titled "The People and Landscapes of the 400 Provinces (4백여 州의 人物과 山河)" is a topic that everyone will read with interest in this April of warm spring (陽春), when the new central government is established in China (支那). We hope you will read it thoroughly. Also, at a time when military fervor (軍國熱) is truly swelling (澎脹) on the peninsula, with nearly 80,000 applicants for the volunteer soldier system, we invited the military instructors (敎官 諸氏) within Kyŏngsŏng City, who are devoted to the military training (軍事 敎訓) of young students through the "Military Instructors' Conference (軍事敎官會議)." This roundtable discussion article will also prove to be an essential (緊要) article for these times.

Mr. In Jŏngsik (印貞植)'s "Letter Sent to Wang Jingwei (汪精衛에 送하는 書)" and General Tada's (多田軍 사령관) "The Empire's Policy Towards China (帝國의 對支 政策)" are both essays concerning national policy (國策的). Besides these, "Character Sketches of Provincial Governors (道知事의 인물평)" and others are all writings essential for the current times (時下), and we recommend a careful reading (精讀).

As excellent writings that stand out in the current trends (時流), Mr. Pak Chonghwa (朴鍾和)'s travelogue "Nammoksansŏng (南漠山城)" and the conference of newspaper novelists are all fine literary works (文藻) highly anticipated by poets.

Furthermore, this time, we have mobilized all the female literary figures (女流 文場 諸家) of Chosŏn to create a kaleidoscope (萬華鏡) of poems, songs, novels, travelogues, and elegant prose (詞華). We ourselves anticipate (自期) this will likely be a magnificent undertaking (壯擧) that will long remain in the cultural history of this land. We are considering attempting such special editions around the spring and autumn seasons (春秋兩節) in the future (압흐로).

These have become times when paper goods (紙物) are extremely precious, and we anticipate various further severe difficulties for newspapers and magazines going forward (今后). For that reason, reprints (再版) of this magazine have become utterly unthinkable (夢想도 못하게 되엇스니), so we hope you will place advance subscription orders with payment upfront (先金).

Many standalone books (單行本) from our company will be published starting from the warm spring festival (陽春節), and we hope that you, esteemed readers (諸賢), will read them avidly.

**Summary**

This editor's postscript from the April 1940 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine highlights key articles, reflecting the wartime atmosphere. It promotes pieces on the new Wang Jingwei regime in China, the surge in Korean volunteer soldier applications and military training, and essays supporting Japanese imperial policy. The editor also mentions contributions from prominent writers like Pak Chonghwa, a special feature mobilizing female writers, and expresses concern over paper shortages, urging readers to subscribe in advance. For historians, the text vividly illustrates the magazine's role in disseminating pro-Japanese, militaristic ideology during the late colonial period, showcasing the promotion of "national policy" essays alongside cultural features, and revealing the practical constraints (paper shortages) faced by publishers. The mobilization of female writers for a special feature is also a point of cultural interest within this context.

編輯後記
「4백여 州의 人物과 山河」란 좌담회 기사는 支那에 신 중앙 정권이 수립되는 陽春 4월에 누구나 관심을 가지고 읽을 好題인 줄 아옵니다. 玩讀하여 주기를 바라며, 또 반도에는 8만명 갓가운 지원병 응모자가 잇서 軍國熱이 正히 澎脹한 이때에 「軍事敎官會議」를 開하여 청년 학도의 군사 敎訓에 열중하시는 경성시 내의 敎官 諸氏를 請邀 하여어든 좌담회 기사도 또한 이 시절에 緊要한 기사가 될 줄 아옵니다.

印貞植 씨의 「汪精衛에 送하는 書」 多田軍 사령관의 「제국의 對支 정책」 모다 국책적의 논문이며 그 박게 「道知事의 인물평」 등등 모다 時下 긴요의 글인 줄 알고 精讀을 권합니다.

時流에 뛰여난 조흔 글로 朴鍾和 씨의 「南漠山城」 기행과 신문 소설가 회의 모다 시인에 줌이 만흔 文藻들인 줄 아오며
더구나 금번에는 조선의 여류 문장 諸家를 총동원하여, 시와 노래와, 소설과 기행, 詞華 등으로 萬華鏡을 이루엇슴니다. 아마 이 땅 문화사상에 기리 남기어 질 壯擧인 줄 自期 하옵나이다. 압흐로는 春秋兩節 쯤 이런 특수한 편집도 하여 볼가고 생각합니다.

紙物이 몹시 귀한 시절이 되야 신문 잡지의 今后는 여러가지로 一層 심한 난관을 예상하고 잇슴니다. 그런 까닭에 本誌의 再版 가튼 것도 도저히 夢想도 못하게 되엇스니, 미리 미리 先金으로 예약 주문이 잇서 주시기를 바라나이다.

本社의 단행본 陽春節부터 만히 나오니, 諸賢께서 만히 애독하여 주시기를 바라나이다.＜350＞
