Respectfully Celebrating the Emperor's Birthday (天長佳節), on the Occasion of Reaching the Fortieth Year of His Precious Age (寶齡 四十)
						Samch'ŏlli (三千里) President Kim Tong-hwan (金東煥)

April 29th marks the Emperor's Birthday (天長佳節), when His Majesty reaches the fortieth year of his precious age (寶齡). We respectfully celebrate His Majesty's sacred longevity (聖壽) and pray that the national fortunes of His Majesty's nation, Our Empire of Japan (我 日本帝國), may prosper and the Imperial virtue (皇化) reach the eight corners of the world (八絃).

Considering that the great Emperor Meiji (明治大帝), after carrying out the Restoration (維新), paid attention to the expansion of military preparedness and the promotion of culture, building the foundation (基) for a wealthy nation and strong army (富國強兵), and as a result, achieved decisive victories in the two great wars, the Sino-Japanese (日淸) and Russo-Japanese (日露) wars, thereby laying the foundation (礎) for peace in East Asia (東洋平和), now that the Chiang (蔣) regime has adopted a mistaken anti-Japanese, pro-communist policy and reached the point of resisting the Empire<30>,

His Majesty the current Emperor (今上 陛下) dispatched the loyal and brave, peerless Imperial Army (忠勇無雙 皇師) far away to over 400 provinces to deal with the China Incident (支那 事變). However, during the four years (四星霜) of the Holy War (聖戰), the rout (敗走) of the Chiang (蔣) regime is already evident, and we know that its end, from which it cannot recover, is imminent (近迫하온).

This is all due to His Majesty's Imperial authority (御稜威), and the 100,000,000 subjects (臣民) celebrate without end. Furthermore, as Mr. Wang Jingwei (汪精衛氏), a friend (友) of justice, has recently commenced the work of establishing a new central government (新 中央 政權) to resolve the current situation, we know that before long (不遠하야), it will cooperate with the Empire and strive towards the construction of a new order in East Asia (東亞 新 秩序). The rise and prosperity (隆興과 繁榮) of Asia (亞細亞) truly begins from now.

Now, we respectfully pray for His Majesty's limitless sacred longevity (聖壽無疆).

Shōwa (昭和) 15, April 29th<31>
<30-31>

**Summary**

This editorial by Kim Tong-hwan, president of the popular Korean magazine *Samch'ŏlli*, celebrates Emperor Hirohito's 40th birthday in April 1940. It heaps praise on the Emperor and the Japanese Empire, crediting Emperor Meiji with Japan's modernization and military victories, and justifies the ongoing Sino-Japanese War ("China Incident") by blaming Chiang Kai-shek's "anti-Japanese, pro-communist" policies. The author expresses confidence in Japan's ultimate victory, attributes military success to Hirohito's divine authority, and welcomes the establishment of Wang Jingwei's collaborationist regime in China as the beginning of a new order and prosperity for Asia under Japanese leadership. The article concludes with fervent wishes for the Emperor's long life. For historians, this piece is a stark example of pro-Japanese collaborationist writing by a prominent Korean cultural figure during the height of colonial assimilation policy and wartime mobilization. It demonstrates the pressures and/or choices faced by Korean intellectuals and the extent to which imperial ideology was promoted through Korean media.

奉祝 天長佳節, 寶齡 四十을 마지하심에 際하야
						三千里社長 金東煥
4월 29일은 寶齡 40을 마지하시는 天長佳節이 온지라, 삼가
폐하의 聖壽를 봉축하오며 아울너 폐하의 나라인 我 日本帝國의 국운이 隆昌하야 皇化 八絃에 미치시기를 비나이다.
생각하옵건대 明治대제께서 유신을 단행하신 뒤 군비의 확충과 문화의 진흥에 유의하시와 부국 강병의 基를 修하서 그 결과 日淸, 日露의 양대 전쟁에 결승하신지라 그리하야 동양 평화의 礎를 노흐섯든 중 이제 蔣 정권이 그릇된 항일 용공 정책을 들고 제국에＜30＞ 반항함에 至하자,
금상 폐하께서는 忠勇無雙한 皇師를 멀리 400여주의 派하사 支那 事變을 보섯사오나, 聖戰 4星霜동안에 蔣 정권의 패주는 이미 현저하야 재기 불능의 말로가 근박하온 줄 아나이다.
모다 폐하의 御稜威의 소치로 100,000,000 신민은 경축하야 마지 안나이다. 더구나 근자에 정의의 友 汪精衛씨가 이제 시국을 수습하기 위하야 신 중앙 정권 수립 공작을 개시하온지라, 不遠하야 제국과 협조하여 동아 신 질서의 건설에 매진하게 되겟사오니 亞細亞의 융흥과 번영은 실로 이제부터인 줄 아옵나니다.
이제 삼가 聖壽의 무강을 비옵나이다.
昭和 15년 4월 29일＜31＞
＜30-31＞
