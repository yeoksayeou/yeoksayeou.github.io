**Samch'ŏlli Emergency Bulletin Board, Scene of the Meeting Between North Korean Army Chief of Staff Ōba and Russian General "Major General Ka"**

On the occasion of the significant 35th Army Memorial Day in Hoeryŏng, a rare photograph has emerged. It captures a scene from 34 years ago, in late August of Meiji 38 (1905), when a division of the North Korean Army (commanded by Lieutenant General Miyoshi) entered Hoeryŏng and negotiated ceasefire conditions with the Russian Army at Saŭllyŏng. When the North Korean Army entered Hoeryŏng, Commander Miyoshi dispatched Engineer Major Shibo (芝生) of the staff as the first military envoy on September 8th to the Russian Army commander, Major General Kasagovskiy (가사고푸스키-). For the second and subsequent military missions, Chief of Staff Ōba (大庭), then a Lieutenant Colonel (later a General), was dispatched to negotiate the details of the ceasefire conditions at Saŭllyŏng (in P'arŭlmyŏn, Hoeryŏng County). This photograph captures the scene when the representatives of both armies were conferring outdoors and raising a toast during lunch. <58>

**Summary**

This 1940 news brief from the magazine *Samch'ŏlli* reports on the rediscovery of a photograph taken 35 years prior, in August 1905. The photo shows Japanese and Russian military officers meeting near Hoeryŏng in northern Korea to negotiate ceasefire terms during the Russo-Japanese War. Specifically, it depicts Japanese Chief of Staff Ōba (representing Lieutenant General Miyoshi) meeting with Russian Major General Kasagovskiy at Saŭllyŏng. The article provides details about the sequence of envoys sent by the Japanese side. For historians, the article is interesting as it casually uses "North Korean Army" (北韓軍) to refer to the Japanese forces operating in northern Korea during the Russo-Japanese War, reflecting the geopolitical perspective of the time under Japanese rule. It also documents a specific moment of interaction between opposing forces on Korean soil during a major international conflict that significantly impacted Korea's fate, leading eventually to annexation.

三千里 緊急 揭示板, 北韓軍 大庭 參謀長과 露將 「가少將」 會見의 光景
제35회의 의의있는 육군 기념일을 맞이한 會寧에 희귀한 사진 한 장이 나타났다. 그것은 34년 전인 明治 38년 8월 하순, 北韓軍(三好 중장 지휘)의 일개 사단이 會寧에 입성하여 沙乙嶺에서 露軍과 휴전 조건을 협의한 당시의 광경을 촬영한 것인데, 北韓軍이 會寧에 입성하자 三好 사령관은 9월 8일 露軍 사령관 가사고푸스키- 소장에게 제1회 軍使로서 참모의 芝生 공병 소좌를 파견하였으며, 제2회 이후의 군사로서는 참모장인 大庭 중좌(후에는 대장)를 파견하여 沙乙嶺(會寧郡 八乙面)에서 休戶 조건의 세목을 협정케 하였는데 이 사진은 그 때 양군의 위원이 야외에서 협의하고 점심 먹을 때 건배를 들었던 정경을 촬영한 것이다.＜58＞
