Article Type: 소식 (News/Notice)

On the occasion of Army Commemoration Day on March 10th, we reprint here the *Kukmin Sinmun* extra from March 10, Meiji 37 (1904), which reported the victory of our army. Closing one's eyes, the scene from that year seems to appear before them. <109>

**Summary**

This short notice from the April 1940 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine announces the reprinting of a newspaper extra (*Kukmin Sinmun*) originally published on March 10, 1904 (Meiji 37). The extra reported a victory by "our army" (referring to the Imperial Japanese Army) on Army Commemoration Day. The notice evokes nostalgia for this past Japanese military success. For historians, this piece is interesting as it reflects the propaganda efforts during the late colonial period, promoting Japanese military achievements and fostering identification with the Japanese Empire ("our army") among Korean readers, specifically recalling the Russo-Japanese War era as a foundational moment of Japanese imperial strength.
