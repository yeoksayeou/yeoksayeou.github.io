Magazine Title: 三千里
Publication Date: 1935년 03월 01일
Article Title: 社告 (Company Notice)
Author: 三千里編輯局
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Postscript)

Because the articles for the March issue were extremely congested (輻轃), we unavoidably had to postpone approximately nine topics, amounting to over fifty pages of planned manuscripts, to the upcoming April issue. We express our deep apologies to the writers and readers.

Samch'ŏlli Editorial Department <24>

**Summary**

This brief notice from the Samch'ŏlli Editorial Department informs readers that due to a high volume of content ("congestion") planned for the March 1935 issue, approximately nine articles totaling over fifty pages had to be deferred to the April issue. The notice apologizes to both the writers whose work was postponed and the readers. From a historical perspective, this notice highlights the practicalities and constraints of periodical publishing in colonial Korea. It suggests the magazine had a significant amount of submitted or commissioned content, perhaps exceeding available space due to paper costs, printing limitations, or even potential censorship delays requiring layout changes. It points to an active literary or journalistic scene contributing to the magazine.
