Apology Notice
						Samch'ŏlli Company

We deeply apologize that this March issue was delayed due to unavoidable circumstances at the printing office. We will strive to ensure the upcoming April issue is definitely released for sale on March 25th. <89>

**Summary**

This brief notice from the Samch'ŏlli Company apologizes to readers for the delay in the publication of the March 1935 issue, attributing it to problems at the printing office. It assures readers that the next issue (April) will be released promptly on March 25th. For historians, this notice offers a small window into the practical challenges of publishing in colonial Korea, specifically the reliance on external printing services and the potential for delays impacting distribution schedules. It also reflects the publisher's direct communication and accountability to its readership.
