Ireland's Revival Movement (愛蘭의 復古運動)

Recently in Ireland (愛蘭), a new cultural movement has arisen nationwide. It involves collecting all remaining things such as legends (傳說), old proverbs (古諺), and folk songs (民謠).

Once this plan from the Government (政府) was announced, all people of intention welcomed this policy. Already, a certain farmer (農夫) has written two books of old stories (녯날 이약이冊), and a certain pastor (牧師) has written five or six books of myths (神話) and legends (傳說). Looking at the nationwide statistics, it has already surpassed 50,000 pages, and since it is being organized as a 10-year plan, it seems likely to reach several hundred thousand pages. This revivalist trend (復古思潮) is currently drawing attention from the world (世界). <256>

**Summary**

The article reports on a government-supported cultural revival movement in Ireland focused on collecting folklore like legends, proverbs, and folk songs. The initiative has received widespread popular support, with individuals like farmers and pastors contributing significantly, resulting in over 50,000 pages collected already as part of a ten-year plan expected to yield hundreds of thousands of pages. This Irish "revivalist trend" is noted as attracting international attention. For historians of colonial Korea, this article is interesting as it highlights a contemporary example of a nation actively working to preserve and revive its traditional culture, likely resonating with Korean intellectuals and nationalists concerned with maintaining Korean identity under Japanese rule. The focus on collecting folklore mirrors similar efforts undertaken by Korean scholars during this period.

愛蘭의 復古運動
愛蘭에서는 요지간 全國的으로 새로운 新文化運動이 이러나고 잇는데 그것은
傳說, 古諺, 民謠-
가튼, 아직까지 나머잇는 것을 모다 蒐集하는 중으로 政府로부터의 이 計劃이 한 번 發布되자, 뜻 잇는 人民들은 모다 이 政策을 歡迎하야 벌서 엇던 農夫는 녯날 이약이冊 두 冊을 써냇고 엇던 牧師는 神話와 傳說 5, 6冊을 써내어서 全國的으로 統計하여보면 벌서 50,000頁을 돌파하엿는데, 10개년 계획으로 꾸미는 중이라 한즉 아마 수십만頁을 이룰 모양이다. 이 復古思潮에 대하여는 지금 世界에서 注目중이다. <256>
