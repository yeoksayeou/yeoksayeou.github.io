Recent Reading and Creation

Kim Pokchin=Lately, I am reading works written by Chosŏn writers as much as possible.

Chŏng Insŏp=I am studying a bit of Latin and striving to quickly complete the English translation of Chosŏn poetry.

Kim Sang'yong=I am trying my hand at translating some English poetry, recently tackling several famous works from the English poetry scene, and intermittently, I am endeavouring to translate Persia's "Rubaiyat" (「루바니얏트」).

Hong Kimun=These days, I am mainly trying to research "Linguistics" (言語學), so I am reading various books on "Linguistics" published in the T'okyo area. Also, I recently started studying the Turkish language. And, whenever I have time, I occasionally read the works of the French writer Balzac (빠르작크).

Son Chint'ae=I get to read many things concerning Chosŏn History (朝鮮史) and Folklore (民俗學). Besides this, I am always paying attention to collecting various handicrafts such as Ancient Chosŏn artifacts (朝鮮古事) and pottery (陶磁器).

Sŏ Hangsŏk=Recently, I read a lot of Poetry (詩) and Drama (戱曲). As I am also involved with the "Kŭgyŏn" (劇硏 - Drama Research Society), I am particularly interested in Drama. <229>

**Summary**

This short article surveys the recent intellectual activities of several Korean figures in 1936. It reveals their diverse interests, ranging from reading contemporary Korean literature (Kim Pokchin), translating Korean poetry into English and studying Latin (Chŏng Insŏp), translating English and Persian poetry (Kim Sang'yong), studying linguistics, Turkish, and French literature (Hong Kimun), researching Korean history, folklore, and collecting artifacts (Son Chint'ae), to focusing on poetry and drama, particularly due to involvement in a drama society (Sŏ Hangsŏk). Historically, this piece is interesting as it showcases the intellectual currents among Korean elites during the Japanese colonial period. It highlights engagement with both Western and traditional Korean culture, an effort to engage with global knowledge (linguistics, foreign languages, translation), and a continued focus on Korean identity through history, folklore, and literature, despite the colonial context.

最近의 讀書와 制作
金復鎭=나는 요지간 朝鮮文人들이 쓴 작품을 될 수 잇는대로 읽는 중이외다.
鄭寅燮=라톈語를 좀 공부하며 그리고 朝鮮詩歌의 英譯을 完成석히려고 애쓰고 잇슴니다.
金尙鎔=英詩飜譯을 좀 하여보려고 최근 英詩壇에서 저명한 몃몃 작품에 손을 대이고 잇스며, 간간히 波斯의 「루바니얏트」번역을 하여 보려고 힘씀니다.
洪起文=나는 요사히 주로 「言語學」에 대한 것을 좀 연구할야고 해서 東京방면에서 출판되는 「言語學」에 대한 여러 가지 冊을 봄니다. 그리고는 土耳其語 공부를 최근에 와서는 하기 시작하지요. 또 佛國 作家 빠르작크의 작품을 틈 잇는 대로 각금 봄니다.
孫晋泰=나는 朝鮮史에 관한 것, 民俗學에 관한 것 등을 만히 보게 됨니다. 이 박게도 朝鮮古事와 陶磁器 등 여러 手藝品의 蒐集에도 늘 留意하고 잇지요.
徐恒錫=최근 나는 詩와 戱曲을 만히 봄니다. 戱曲은 내가 「劇硏」에도 관계하고 잇니만큼 關心하지요. <229>
