Does Sambang Mineral Water Truly Have Efficacy?
						By Pak Ch'anghun, M.D.

Not just Sambang mineral water, but generally when we speak of "mineral water" (약수), it contains chemical components like aluminum, and is said to be very good for neuralgia, stomach ailments, skin diseases, and the like.

However, regarding whether the "mineral water" itself is truly effective for the various illnesses mentioned above, I would say that is highly doubtful.

While we commonly say that "mineral water" is very good for the various illnesses mentioned above, the reason we also doubt the effectiveness of the mineral water itself is that places with mineral water generally have good scenery (산수), clean air (공긔), and quiet surroundings, so one finds direct physical benefits from recuperating in such a scenic place (전지료양).

Then, does "mineral water" itself have no direct effect whatsoever? That is not the case either. Of course, one can say it does have some effect.

However, there are various kinds of "mineral water." That is, there are those with many chemical components characteristic of "mineral water" and those with few. Therefore, every person must drink "mineral water" suited to their own body's strength or weakness (强弱). If a person with a weak constitution drinks "mineral water" containing high-intensity (强度) aluminum components, it will likely be rather harmful to the body.

Some years ago (연전), I had the experience of drinking Sambang (三防) "mineral water," and I saw that there were three types of Sambang mineral water. Accordingly, various people should choose and drink the "mineral water" appropriately.

That is, it would be very good for those with strong stomachs to drink the "new mineral water" (신약수) and for those with weak stomachs to drink the "old mineral water" (구약수).

The "mineral water" of Sambang is comparatively good among mineral waters. Moreover, it is a scenic spot (승지) with good scenery and clean air. From late spring through the summer season and early autumn, those who are frail, if only they can afford the money and time, would do well to go and recuperate there, even for just a short while. <191>

**Summary**

Dr. Pak Ch'anghun questions the direct medicinal efficacy of mineral water (yaksu), specifically Sambang mineral water, for ailments like neuralgia, stomach problems, and skin diseases. While acknowledging some minor direct effects due to chemical components like aluminum, he attributes the primary health benefits associated with mineral water sites to the environmental factors: good scenery, clean air, and tranquility, which aid recuperation (chonjiryoyang). He cautions that individuals should choose mineral water appropriate to their constitution, noting Sambang has different types, and warns stronger waters could harm weaker individuals. He concludes that Sambang is a good place for recuperation for those with the means. From a historical perspective, the article reflects the intersection of traditional health beliefs (efficacy of yaksu) and modern medical skepticism in colonial Korea. It highlights the emergence of health tourism and leisure culture among affluent Koreans, focusing on a specific Korean natural site (Sambang), and shows a medical professional navigating popular beliefs with scientific reasoning, albeit cautiously acknowledging some minimal direct effects.

三防藥水는 果然 效力이 잇슬가?
						醫學博士 朴昌薰
삼방약수만이 아니라 일반적으로 「약수」라 하면, 화학성분인 알미늄가튼 것이 들어 잇서서, 신경통, 위병, 피부병 등에 대단히 조흔 것임니다.
그러나 이상에서 말한 여러 가지 병에 「약수」 그 자체가 그러케 효과가 잇는냐 하면, 그것은 심히 의문이라고 하겟슴니다.
우리가 흔히 「약수」가 이상에서 말한 여러 가지 병에 대단 조타고는 하면서, 또 한편 약수 그 자체의 효과를 의심한다는 것은, 「약수」 그것보다도 약수가 잇는 곳이면 대체로 산수가 좃코 공긔가 맑고 모든 주위가 조용하여서, 「전지료양」 한다는데서 즉접 신체의 리로운 점을 발견한다는 것임니다.
그러면 「약수」 그 자체는 즉접적으로는 아무런 효력이 업느냐 하면 그러치도 안슴니다. 물론 다소의 효과야 잇다고 할 수 잇슴니다.
그런데, 「약수」 가운데는 여러 가지 종류가 잇슴니다. 즉 「약수」로서의 화학적 성분이 만히 들어잇는 것과 적게 들어 잇는 것이 잇슴니다. 그럼으로 모든 사람은 제각긔 자긔의 신체의 강, 약, (强弱)에 맛춰, 「약수」도 마서야 하는 것임니다. 만일 약한 육체를 가진 사람이 강도(强度)의 알미늄 성분이 들어 잇는 「약수」를 마시게 되면 도리혀 몸에 해로울 것임니다.
내가 연전 三防 「약수」를 마서본 일이 잇는데, 삼방약수는 세가지 종류가 잇는 것을 보앗슴니다. 여긔에 맛춰서 여러 사람들도 적당하게 「약수」를 택해 마서야 할 것임니다.
즉 위장이 튼튼한 사람은 「신약수」 위장이 튼튼치 못한 사람은 「구약수」를 먹도록 하는 것이 매우 조켓습니다.
삼방의 「약수」는 비교적 「약수」 중에서는 조흔 편임니다. 더구나 산수 좃코 공긔가 맑은 승지임니다. 느즌 봄부터 녀름철과 일흔 가을까지는, 몸 약한 분들은, 금전과 시간의 여유를 낼 수만 잇스면 다만 얼마 동안만이라도 가서 수양하는 것이 조흘 것임니다. <191>
