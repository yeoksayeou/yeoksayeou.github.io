Notes from Travels Abroad
						Kim Hwallan

Gathering the impressions remaining in my head, the feelings gained during several years of travel abroad are generally as follows.

I once visited the capital of Annam and observed the lives of the Annamese people. They possessed no high degree of civilization (高度 文明), and their living conditions (生活狀態) were impoverished (貧寒). The men often had dishevelled hair (散髮), their complexions were pale (蒼白), and at first glance, they seemed a listless (無氣力), worn-out people (民族), casting the darkest shadow (暗影) in my mind during my world travels. Generally, even visiting the cities (都市) in Annam, the ones holding the large shops (商店), factories (工場), and other economic institutions (經濟機關) were either Indians (印度人), Chinese (中國人), or French (佛蘭西人); the native Annamese (土着安南人) had no power whatsoever, causing this traveler at sunset (夕陽遊子) to have pitiful thoughts.

On that trip, I also visited Egypt (埃及) and saw the Sphinx and pyramids, admiring the splendor (燦爛) of the ancient civilization (古代文明) they possessed. The Egyptians (埃及人) in its capital, Cairo (카이로), seemed quite listless (無氣力), but judging by the people and the outward appearance (外觀) of the city, it seemed far better than Seoul. <39>

During my travels abroad, I visited Denmark (丁抹) and thought that Denmark was truly an ideal (理想) country. Moreover, the women there—pure (純), truthful (眞實), diligent (부즈런하며), and healthy (健康)—I thought were the best (第一) among the women of all the countries (世界 各國 女性) I had seen. Tasks like milking cows and sideline businesses (副業) in the farming villages (農村) were mostly (大槪) handled by women (女子), and in the cities (都會), policewomen (女巡査) stood watch, maintaining public order (治安); thus, it is an ideal country of gender equality (男女平等).

Also, though the world (世上) mistakenly understands Paris (巴里) only as a frivolous (輕薄), glamorous (華麗), and extravagant (奓侈) place, from what I saw, it is by no means like that. Rather, they seemed to have integrity (着實性) and sincerity (眞實), and while there was certainly a portion (一部) of men and women with showy (華美) lifestyles (態度), the vast majority (大多數) appeared to be sincere citizens (國民).<41>

**Summary**

Kim Hwallan recounts her impressions from travels abroad. She describes Annam (Vietnam) negatively, noting the poverty and perceived listlessness of the people, contrasting them with the foreign control of the economy. Egypt's ancient wonders impressed her, but the modern people seemed similarly listless, though Cairo appeared more developed than Seoul. Denmark stood out as an ideal nation, particularly praised for its hardworking, healthy women actively participating in the economy and public life, achieving gender equality. Lastly, she challenges the stereotype of Paris as purely frivolous, finding the majority of its citizens sincere and grounded despite some extravagance. From a historical perspective, the article offers a valuable glimpse into the worldview of a prominent Korean female intellectual during the colonial era. Her observations on other colonized nations (Annam), her high praise for gender equality and women's roles in Denmark, and her nuanced view of Western centers like Paris provide insight into contemporary Korean perspectives on modernity, colonialism, and gender.

外遊旅草
						金活蘭
내 머리에 남아 잇는 印象을 주서 모으면 여러 해 外遊 중에 어든 所感이란 大體로 아래와 갓슴니다.
安南首都에 들너 安南人의 生活을 본 일이 잇슴니다. 그네들은 高度의 文明도 못가젓고 生活狀態도 貧寒하더이다. 男子들은 散髮이 만코 얼골빗갈은 蒼白하고 一見에 無氣力한 낡은 民族으로서 世界旅行 중 가장 내 머리속에 暗影을 던저주더이다. 大體로 安南은 都市에 가보아도 큰 商店, 工場 등 經濟機關을 쥐고 잇는 것은 印度人이 아니면 中國人이나 佛蘭西人들로 土着安南人은 아모 힘도 업섯고 夕陽遊子로 하여곰 처참한 생각을 돌게 하더이다.
그 길로 埃及까지 들너 보앗는데 스핑쓰, 피라밋을 보고 그네가 가진 古代文明의 燦爛을 讚歎하엿슴니다. 그 서울인 「카이로」 市街의 埃及人은 퍽으나 無氣力하게 보여지는데 人間들로나 都市의 外觀으로 보아 서울보다는 훨신*은 듯하더이다. <39>
나는 外遊 중 丁抹에 가서 보고 丁抹은 진실로 理想의 나라라고 생각했습니다. 더구나 그 純하고 眞實되고 부즈런하며 그러고도 健康스러운 그곳 女性은 내가 보든 世界 各國 女性 中 第一이라 생각했습니다. 소젓 쨔는 것이라든지 農村의 副業은 大槪 女子들이 마터하고 都會에선 女巡査들이 서서 治安을 직히고 그러면서 男女平等한 理想의 나라 엇슴니다.
그리고 世上에서는 巴里라 하면 곳 輕薄하고 華麗, 奓侈한 곳으로만 잘못 알어오나 내가 보기에는 결코 그러치 안코 오히려 着實性 잇고 眞實하며 生活의 態*도 華美한 一部 男女가 업는 바는 아니나 大多數는 眞實한 國民들로 보이더이다.<41>
