**Body Text Translation:**
The Palace of Film Art 『Meiji-za』 Calling the 600,000 Citizens of Great Kyŏngsŏng, The 『Shōchiku』 First-Run Movie Theater Completing This Autumn-

Seoul (서울), anticipating the Great Kyŏngsŏng Urban Plan, gathers all the luxury and essence of modern civilization to showcase the face of "Cultural Kyŏngsŏng" widely to the world; this is the appearance of Great Kyŏngsŏng these days.

Indeed, Seoul is different yesterday and different today. How it will change tomorrow is an unknown variable. Cultural houses are constantly increasing, and all sorts of entertainment venues are popping up here and there. On the other hand, 4- or 5-story buildings for banks, companies, etc., are being designed and constructed, several each day.

While this is Seoul, so it might not be that surprising, it is true that another big "news" item that draws our attention is emerging.

The location is precisely the crossroads point of Myŏngch'i-jŏng (明治町), which could be called the heart of Great Kyŏngsŏng. Those with the luxury of time<102> may go there and see; a large construction site undertaken by the Miki Gōmei Kaisha (三木合名會社) under contract will immediately catch the eye. This is precisely the Palace of Film Art that will soon appear dazzlingly before our eyes.

So, who is the protagonist constructing this Palace of Film Art? It will soon come into being from the hands of an individual named Mr. Ishibashi Ryosuke (石橋良介氏), whose permanent domicile (原籍) is in Nagasaki Prefecture (長崎縣) and who came to Korea (渡鮮入京) early on, being very active in various fields such as the business world.

According to Mr. Ishibashi, construction began around November last year on a vast site of 856 *pyeong* (坪), investing a truly astonishingly large construction cost of 67,000 Won (圓) for a state-of-the-art movie theater of reinforced concrete (鐵筋 콩크릿트) with 2 basement floors and 4 floors above ground, for a total of 6 floors. Everything is proceeding such that it will be completed by July or September of this autumn at the latest.

So, what kind of film art will be premiered (封切) in this magnificent Palace of Film Art, the likes of which Great Kyŏngsŏng has never seen in its extreme splendor? Mr. Ishibashi also states that it has already been decided (作定되엿스며) to make it the premiere theater for the so-called Manchuria-Korea Shōchiku Films (滿鮮松竹映畵), and it will not cater only to the Japanese residents in Kyŏngsŏng but will go a step further to cater to all 600,000 citizens of Great Kyŏngsŏng, so one can well imagine how grand the business plan is.

However, the plan is to make the admission fees (料金) popular (大衆的) so that anyone can come and visit this Palace of Film Art. Furthermore, one astonishing fact is that on the day this project is completed this autumn, for the first public opening commemorative performance (公開祝賀記念興行), there are plans to invite Ms. Ch'oe Sŭng-hŭi (崔承喜女史) from faraway Tokyo (東京) and Osaka (大阪), bring over "Kabuki" (歌舞伎), and even mobilize the Takarazuka Revue (寶塚레뷰-) and the Shōchiku Revue Troupe (松竹레뷰-團) to dazzle the eyes (眼目) of the 600,000 citizens of Great Kyŏngsŏng. Accordingly, even after that, they plan to invite such "Kabuki" revue troupes from Tokyo and Osaka 4 or 5 times a year to provide excellent service (써비스) to the men and women of the entire capital (滿都).

So, what will this theater be named? It was decided to give it the most splendid name based on a public vote (投票), and it was recently decided upon as 『Meiji-za』 (明治座).

As a vigorous (壯年) businessman of 43 this year who manages cafés and the "Maru Biru" (丸ビル), Mr. Ishibashi's future skill and activity (手腕活動) will be worth watching, and the future of this literally Kyŏngsŏng-first Great Palace of Film Art will indeed be worth seeing. (Advertisement)<103>

**Summary**

This 1936 article from *Samch'ŏlli* magazine announces the upcoming completion of a large, modern movie theater named Meiji-za in the heart of Gyeongseong (colonial Seoul), specifically in Meiji-chō (Myeong-dong). Funded by Japanese businessman Ishibashi Ryosuke with origins in Nagasaki, the 6-story reinforced concrete building is contracted to Miki Gōmei Kaisha and will serve as the premiere venue for Shōchiku films from Japan and Manchuria. While intended to target the entire 600,000 population of Gyeongseong with popular pricing, the grand opening plans include famous Japanese acts like Kabuki, the Takarazuka Revue, and the Shōchiku Revue, alongside renowned Korean dancer Ch'oe Sŭng-hŭi. The theater aims to be a major cultural landmark. For historians, the article illuminates the infusion of Japanese capital and popular culture into colonial Seoul's urban landscape, the targeting of both Japanese settlers and the Korean populace (albeit with primarily Japanese entertainment), and the scale of modernization projects during this period. The mention of Ch'oe Sŭng-hŭi alongside top Japanese performers highlights her transnational stardom.

기사형태	잡저

大京城 六十萬府民을 부르는 映畵藝術殿堂 『明治座』, 今秋에 完成되는 『松竹』 映畵封切舘-
大京城都市計劃을 압둔 서울은 모든 現代文明의 豪華와 精粹를 한데 모와 「文化京城」의 面貌를 널니 세상에 빗내이랴고 함이 昨今의 大京城의 外觀이다.
실로 서울은 어제가 다르고 오늘이 달으다. 來日은 어떠케 변하여저 갈지 그것은 알 수 업는 未知數의 일이다. 文化家宅이 작고 늘고 온갓 娛樂場이 여긔저긔에 생겨진다. 그런가 하면 또 한편에는 銀行會社 등의 4,5층 건물이 하루에도 몃 개ㅅ식 設計되고 建築된다.
이러한 서울이라 그리 놀나울 것은 업스나 우리들의 視線을 모으게 할 또한 커다란 「뉴-쓰」 하나가 생겨지고 잇슴이 사실이다.
바로 場所는 大京城에서도 心臟이라고 할 만한 明治町의 十字路 點. 時間의<102> 裕잇는 분은 한번 그리로 가보면 아리니 그 곳에는 三木合名會社의 請負로 진행중에 잇는 커다란 工事場 하나이 언뜻 눈에 띄이게 될 것이다. 이것이 바로 將次 머지 안허 우리들 눈 앞에 황홀 찰난하게 나타나게 될 映畵藝術의 殿堂이다.
그러면 이 映畵藝術의 殿堂을 建設하는 主人公은 果然 그 누구인가? 原籍을 長崎縣에 두고 일즉이 渡鮮入京하여 實業界 등 多方面으로 크게 활동하고 잇는 石橋良介氏란 個人의 手中에서 장차 생겨날 것이다.
石橋氏의 말을 들으면 建坪數 856坪이나 되는 廣大한 땅에다 67,000圓이라는 실로 놀나울만한 巨大한 工事費를 던저 地下 2층, 地上 4층, 合 6층의 鐵筋 콩크릿트의 最新型 映畵劇場으로서 昨年 11월 頃에 着工하얏는데 느저도 今秋 7월 乃至 9월까지에는 竣功되게 모든 것을 進行식히고 잇다는 것이다.
그러면 이럿틋이 굉장하고 大京城이 아직도 가저 보지 못한 華麗極致한 映畵藝術의 殿堂에서는 어떠한 映畵藝術를 封切할 것인가? 또 氏는 말한다. 소위 滿鮮松竹映畵의 封切場으로 하기로 임이 作定되엿스며 在京日本 內地人만을 相對로 한이 아니고 한거름 나아가서 大京城 60萬府民 全部를 相對로 할 것이라 하니 그 事業計劃이 如何히 큰가함을 가히 알만하다.
그러나 料金은 大衆的으로 누구나 이 藝術殿堂을 차저 오도록 할 計劃이라는 것이다. 더욱이 한가지 놀나운 사실은 今秋 이 事業進行이 完成되는 날에는 第一回 公開祝賀記念興行으로  멀니 東京, 大阪 等地에서 崔承喜女史를 招待하고 「歌舞伎」를 불너오고 寶塚레뷰-와 松竹레뷰-團까지 動員식혀 大京城 60萬府民의 眼目을 황홀케 할 作定이라고 한다. 따라 그 뒤에도 年 4, 5回는 東京 大阪에서 이러한 「歌舞伎」 레뷰-團을 招請해다 滿都男女에게 絶好한 써비스로 提供하리라 한다.
그러면 이 劇場의 이름은 어떠케 지어질 것가? 일반의 投票에 의하야 가장 훌융한 名稱을 부치기로 되엿는데, 얼마 전 『明治座』로 決定지엇다.
今年 43세의 壯年 實業家로서 카페-, 「丸ビル」를 經營하는 氏의 今后의 手腕活動도 볼 만 하려니와, 이 文字 그대로의 京城初有의 大映畵藝術殿堂의 앞날이 가히 볼만하다 할 것이다. (廣告)<103>
