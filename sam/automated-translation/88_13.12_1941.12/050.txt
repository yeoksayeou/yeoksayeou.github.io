Overseas Information
Selected by the Samch'ŏlli Editorial Office

Nude Beauty New Tactic

According to hard-to-believe Soviet propaganda regarding the war against Germany, it was reported even in the United States that German forces, in order to cross a river under enemy fire, employed a bizarre tactic where they put nude women at the forefront. The aim was for Red Army soldiers to be distracted by the sight of the nude women, allowing the Germans to execute a blitzkrieg river crossing during that gap. It is said that several hundred women participated in this Nude Beauty New Tactic.
However, the Red Army side announced that they did not let their eyes wander to the group of nude women but fired upon the German soldiers.

Income of the Seven Highest-Paid Individuals in the United States

The Treasury Department announced the highest earners in the United States for the year 1939, in order. First was the actor Mr. "Cary Cooper" (케-리·쿠-파), with $482,819. Second, third, and fourth places were occupied by businessmen. In fifth place was William Knudsen (윌리암·쿠누-드센), currently of the Office of Production Management, with $372,366.<48> Sixth place went to "James Cagney" (쩨임스·꺄그니이) with $368,333, and seventh was the film director "John Ford" (쬰·포-드) with $320,000.

Twelve-Year-Old Boy Conducts Symphony Orchestra
A 12-year-old boy named "Lorin Maazel" (로링·마앗첼) conducted the NBC Symphony Orchestra, which Toscanini (토스카니이니) has also conducted. He is said to have memorized 22 orchestral pieces. After his first performance, the boy stated, "I tried conducting to see if I could make the musicians my own." Born in Neuilly (뉴이리이) near Paris (巴里), he studied music from childhood and, prior to NBC, had conducted seven orchestras, including the Los Angeles Symphony Orchestra.

Hopes of American Boys

A public opinion survey was conducted among 10,000 American boys aged 8 to 13, asking, "What food do you enjoy?" and "Will America enter the war?" The results showed that half preferred "dessert" (떼자-트) for the first question, and 28% were immediate interventionists. Among them, 71% acknowledged being raised with spankings on the bottom, but 82% did not fear their parents. When asked about their hopes for when they grow up, 20% wanted to be pilots, while only 2% wanted to be president. The majority of girls hoped to become housewives.

Poll of Ten Popular Actors in the United States
The renowned film newspaper company, "Motion Picture Herald" (모오숀·피챠·헤랄드), recently mobilized 13,000 movie theaters across the United States to conduct a popularity poll for movie actors worth promoting as future stars. There were 130 in total, and the top 10 are as follows:
Laraine Day (라레인·띠-) (MGM - 메도로)
Rita Hayworth (리다·헤이와스) (Columbia - 콘론비야)
Ruth Hussey (루스·핫시이) (MGM - 메도로)
Robert Preston (R·푸레스톤) (Paramount - 파라마운트)
Ronald Reagan (로날드·리-깐) (Warner - 와-나)
John Payne (죤·베인) (Fox - 폭스)
Jeffrey Lynn (쩨푸리이·링) (Warner - 와-나)
Ann Rutherford (안·루자포-드) (MGM - 메도로)
Dennis Morgan (떼니스·몰간) (Warner - 와-나-)
Jackie Cooper (쟉키·쿠-바-) (Columbia - 콘론비야) <49>

**Summary**

This article compiles several short news items from overseas, primarily focusing on the United States, as reported in a Korean magazine in late 1941. It includes a Soviet propaganda piece about alleged German war tactics using nude women, lists the highest-paid individuals in the US for 1939 (mostly entertainers and businessmen), highlights the debut of a young conductor Lorin Maazel, summarizes a survey on American children's preferences and aspirations, and presents the results of a poll identifying rising Hollywood stars. From a historical perspective, the article is interesting as it shows a Korean audience under Japanese colonial rule being fed information about Allied nations (USA, Soviet Union) and global events, including war propaganda and American popular culture (salaries, child surveys, movie stars). It reflects the complex flow of information and cultural interest during a period of intense global conflict and colonial control.

裸體 美人 新戰術
믿기 어려운 蘇聯의 對獨戰宣傳에 의하면 獨逸軍은 敵前渡河를 위해서 裸體의 여자들을 내여세워 赤軍將兵이 裸女에게 눈을 빼았기는 틈을 타서 電擊渡河를 결행한 珍戰法을 썼다고 米國에서까지 보도되였다. 이 裸體美人新戰術에는 수백명의 여자가 등장하였다고 한다.
그러나 赤軍측은 裸女群에 눈팔지 않고 獨兵에게 사격했노라고 발표하였다.

米國의 最高俸給者 七人의 收入

1939년도에 米國 최고의 봉급을 받은 者를 順次로 재무성에서 발표했는데 첫째가 俳優「케-리·쿠-파」君으로서 482,819弗이다. 둘째와, 셋째, 넷째는 實業家가 점령했고, 다섯째에는 현재 國防生産管理局의 「윌리암·쿠누-드센」이 372,366弗을＜48＞ 받았다. 6위는 「쩨임스·꺄그니이」인데 368,333弗, 7위는 영화감독의 「쬰·포-드」로서 320,000弗이다.

十二歲 少年이 交響樂團을 指揮
「토스카니이니」가 지휘한 일이 있는 NBC교향악단을 「로링·마앗첼」이라고 하는 금년 12세의 소년이 지휘하였는데, 그는 오-케스트라의 곡목 22를 암기한다고 한다. 第一回의 연주가 끝난 뒤에 그 소년은 「나는 樂士들을 나의 것으로 만들어 볼려고 지휘해 보았다」고 말하였다. 그는 巴里근교 뉴이리이 출생으로, 유년시절부터 음악을 공부했으며, NBC의 이전에는 로스앤젤스 교향악단 등 일곱 개의 「오-케스트라」를 지휘하였다.

米國少年들의 希望

8세로부터 13세까지의 米國소년 10,000명에게 「너는 무슨 음식을 즐기느냐?」, 「米國은 參戰할 것인가?」라는 질문을 하여 아이들의 여론조사를 하였다. 그 결과 第一問에는 「떼자-트」가 좋다는 者가 절반, 즉시 參戰派가 28%였다. 그 중의 71%는 궁둥이를 얻어맞고 成育된 것을 인정케 되였으나 82%는 兩親을 두려워하지 않었다. 어른이 되여서의 희망은 무엇이냐는 질문에, 飛行士가 20%, 대통령이 겨우 2% 그리고 소녀들의 희망의 大半은 가정의 주부가 되겠다는 것이다.
