Establishment of a Branch Office

Unggi Branch Office (雄基支社)
Chief Manager (主幹) O Yŏngguk (吳永國)
Advisor (顧問) Kim Ch'angjun (金昌俊)

As stated above (右와 如히), a branch office has been established.
Samch'ŏlli Company (三千里社) <99>

**Summary**

This is a brief announcement from the Samch'ŏlli Company, publisher of the magazine *Samch'ŏlli*, stating the establishment of a new branch office in Unggi (now Sŏnbong in North Korea). It names O Yŏngguk as the Chief Manager and Kim Ch'angjun as an Advisor for the new branch. From a historical perspective, this notice indicates the expansion of the magazine's operations and reach into the northeastern region of Korea during the colonial period. It provides specific names associated with this expansion, potentially useful for mapping business and intellectual networks of the time.
