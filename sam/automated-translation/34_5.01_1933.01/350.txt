**Body Text Translation:**
Literary World "Indépendants" Special Feature = Call for Manuscripts

As the year ends and the new year begins, it is a customary, cyclical habit both within and outside the literary world to lament (라멘크) the stagnation and decline of the literary scene over the past year. Therefore, we believe that excavating the objects of our ambitions and hopes from the strata beneath the surface of the literary world and mobilizing the hidden forces within it will be a great help in eliminating the lamentations that will surely confront us again at the end of this year. Accordingly, we intend to make the upcoming "Samch'ŏlli April Issue Literary Section" a "Literary World Indépendants Special Feature" (文壇앙데펜당. 特輯) and send the resounding voices of newcomers into our literary world, which is starved of vitality (生活氣). All unknown writers, whatever your tendency or position may be, appear with your own originality, boldly and individually. (Samch'ŏlli Literary Department (三千里文藝部))

Submission Guidelines
Types: Poetry, Conte, Radio Drama, Essay, Criticism, Research, Novel
Deadline: Until February 20th
Standard for Publication: Above the level of the current literary world.
Enclose the author's photograph and address with the manuscript, and clearly write "Literary World Indépendants Manuscript" (『文壇 앙데펜당』원고) on the envelope (皮封).<93>

**Summary**

This announcement from the magazine *Samch'ŏlli* calls for submissions from unknown writers for a special "Indépendants" feature in its April 1933 issue. Lamenting the perceived annual stagnation of the literary scene, the editors aim to discover and promote fresh, bold voices regardless of their literary style or affiliation, setting the publication standard as "above the level of the current literary world." Historians might find this article interesting as it reflects a conscious effort by a major colonial-era Korean magazine to invigorate the literary field by actively seeking out and providing a platform for new, potentially non-establishment writers, suggesting both a critique of the existing literary circles and a desire to foster new talent during a period of cultural suppression. The use of the French term "Indépendants" also hints at an awareness of international artistic movements and a desire to apply similar principles of openness in Korea.

文壇 「안데펜당」 特輯=原稿募集
年未年頭를 당하야 문단의 내외에서는 예에 의하야 循期的으로 지난 한 해 동안의 문단의 침체 쇠퇴를 「라멘크」하는 습관이 잇다. 그래서 우리는 우리들의 야심과 희망의 대상을 문단의 표면 이하의 지층에서 파내여 문단에 숨은 세력을 동원시키는 것이 금년 말에 또 닥처올 우리들의 「라멘크」를 업시는 데 큰 힘이 되리라고 밋는다. 이에 우리는 오는 「三千里 四月號 文藝欄」을 「文壇앙데펜당. 特輯」으로 하야 生活氣에 주런 우리 문단에 우렁찬 신인의 소리들을 보내려고 한다. 모-든 무명작가는 그대의 경향이나 입장이 무엇이지든 가장 대담하게 각각이 독자성을 가지고 등장하여라.(三千里文藝部)
