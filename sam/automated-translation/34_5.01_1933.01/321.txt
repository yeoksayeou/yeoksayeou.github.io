Everyone, please look forward to the February issue of Samch'ŏlli (三千里). It will be released on January 25th with substantial content.<89>

**Summary**

This brief text is an announcement from the editors of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine, urging readers to anticipate the upcoming February issue, which is promised to have rich content and will be available from January 25th. From a historical perspective, this short notice illustrates the routine operations and promotional efforts of a popular Korean magazine during the colonial period, highlighting its publication cycle and engagement with its audience. It shows the continuation of cultural production and the magazine's efforts to maintain reader interest.
