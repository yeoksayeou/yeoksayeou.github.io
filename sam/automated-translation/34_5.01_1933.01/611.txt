Preface

◇ We knew the editing of the 『Overview of Korean Thinkers』 would become a great achievement in the history of humanities on the Peninsula and intended to begin already, but unable to find the right time, we postponed it until today's publication. However, due to the pressing deadline and insufficient investigation, it has become like "drawing a tiger but ending up with a cat" (虎畵爲猫格 - i.e., an ambitious project poorly executed). We intend to continue the investigation, and as soon as it is completed, we plan to publish it as a separate volume at a later date.
◇ Due to circumstances, thinkers currently in prison, deceased, or in exile have been excluded.
◇ The editorial order follows age order.
◇ Furthermore, please understand that many details written among the items collected and recorded in this outline had to be erased due to unavoidable reasons. Also, we deeply apologize that several dozen individuals were omitted because we could not investigate them in time. We hope to supplement this at a later opportunity. In any case, please view this as being better than having nothing at all.
1933, February 1
From the Kyŏngsŏng Samch'ŏlli-sa Editorial Bureau
Kim Tong-hwan

前記

◇『朝鮮思想家總觀』의 編輯은 半島人文史上의 큰 事績이될줄압고 벌서부터 着手하려하엿스나 때를 엇지못하야 延期하여오다가 오날에야 發行합니다 다만 日字가 促迫하고 調査가 不充分하야 虎畵爲猫格의되엿스나 다시 繼續하여 調査完了 되는대로 後日單行本으로 出版하려함니다.
◇思想家中 現在獄中에잇는이, 死亡한이, 亡命한이는 事情으로 除外하여슴니다.
◇編輯顧序는 年齒顧으로하여슴니다
◇더욱本綱에 收載記錄한중 여러 가지 事項을썻다가 不得已그만지워버린 個所가 퍽으나만엇스니 諒解하시며  미처 調査를 못하야 트린분이 數十氏가잇섯슴을 深謝함니다 後機會에 補充함가하는바 어재던업기보다 낫다는 程度로 보아주옵소서.
