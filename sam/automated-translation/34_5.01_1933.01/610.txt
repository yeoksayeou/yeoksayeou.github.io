Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editorial Postscript)

Facing the New Year, we intend to grow 『三千里』 (Samch'ŏlli) with doubled courage, so we ask for much love from all of you.

We will definitely publish the February issue early. <124>

**Summary**

This short editorial postscript from the January 1st, 1933 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* expresses the editors' resolve to improve and expand the magazine in the new year, asking for readers' continued support. It also promises the early publication of the next (February) issue. For historians, the text offers a glimpse into the operational commitments of a Korean cultural magazine during the colonial period, showcasing the standard polite address to readers and reflecting contemporary Korean orthography and grammatical forms of the early 1930s.
