**Company Announcement**

Samch'ŏlli branches are being established in various locations domestically and overseas. Those who are interested, please request that the company regulations be sent.

Samch'ŏlli Company <112>

**Summary**

This is a brief announcement from the Samch'ŏlli Company stating its intention to establish branch offices both within Korea (海內) and internationally (海外). It invites interested individuals to contact the company to receive its regulations (社規). From a historical perspective, this short notice indicates the ambition and expanding reach of the Samch'ŏlli magazine and publishing company during the colonial period, seeking to build a network beyond the Korean peninsula despite the constraints of Japanese rule.
