Wall Newspaper 1, December 1932 Diary

December 1st: Inauguration ceremony of Ecuador's new president, Martínez Mera (Liberal Party); Eurasian Commodity Conference held in Copenhagen; Manchukuo independence bonds issued; Japanese naval officers transferred; Shimonoseki-Pusan undersea telephone cable opened to the public; Wireless telegraph service opened between Japan and Argentina; League of Nations Committee of Nineteen meeting opens.

December 6th: Republic of Finland Founding Anniversary; German Parliament (Berlin) opens; League of Nations General Assembly opens (Japan-China Issue discussion).

December 9th: 16th anniversary of the death of Japanese novelist Natsume Sōseki (夏日소石).

December 15th: Due date for Britain's war debt repayment to the United States; Ch'ŏngjin-Tsuruga sea route, direct operation by the Ministry of Railways begins.

December 17th: Birthday of His Majesty King Alexander I of Yugoslavia.

December 24th: 34th Imperial Diet convened.

December 26th: Same Diet's opening ceremony. <32>

**Summary**

This article presents a concise diary of notable international and Japanese events for December 1932. It includes political events (Ecuadorian presidential inauguration, German parliament opening, League of Nations meetings concerning the Manchurian Incident), economic activities (Manchukuo bond issuance, Britain's war debt payment), infrastructure developments (Shimonoseki-Pusan cable, Japan-Argentina wireless, Ch'ŏngjin-Tsuruga sea route), and commemorative dates (Finnish independence, Natsume Sōseki's death anniversary, Yugoslavian King's birthday). From a Korean colonial history perspective, the entries detailing Japan's actions—issuing Manchukuo bonds, naval officer movements, opening communication lines like the undersea cable to Pusan and the sea route from Ch'ŏngjin, and the convening of the Imperial Diet—alongside the League of Nations discussion of the "Japan-China Issue" (Manchurian Incident), are particularly revealing. They highlight Japan's expanding influence and infrastructure projects directly involving Korea, framing these within a broader context of global events and solidifying Japan's imperial presence.

壁新聞 1, 1932년 12월 日誌
12월 1일 『에콰도-르』 新대통령 『마-피넷즈, 메라』(自由黨)의 취임식, 歐亞商品會議 『코쁘ㅡ노』에서 개최, 滿洲國 독립 채권발매, 日本 해군장교이동 下關釜山間 해저전화 공개, 日本 『아르젠티나』間 無線電信 개통, 연맹19개국 위원회 開會.
6일  芬蘭共和國 건국 기념일, 獨逸國會(白林)開院, 聯盟總會開會(日中問題 토론)
9일  일본소설가 夏日소石의 16週忌
15일  米國에 대한 英國의 戰債返濟期日, 淸津敦賀間 항로, 鐵道省 直營開始
17일  『유고슬라비아』국왕 『알렉싼더-』1세 陛下의 탄생일.
24일  34帝國 의회소집
26일  同開院式 ＜32＞
