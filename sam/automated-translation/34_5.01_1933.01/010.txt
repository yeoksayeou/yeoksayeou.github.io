年頭感 (New Year's Thoughts)
Let's uphold these three things starting from the new year.

First, let us never tear down the forces that are already formed.
Anything is easy to tear down. Even a great mansion or tall building (大廈高樓) can be easily torn down with a single matchstick, but to build again what has been torn down is truly difficult. We are those who insist on the absolute necessity of representative organizations (表現團體), whatever the circumstances may be. Therefore, we do not accept the reasoning that other aspects of the movement wither away because of those representative organizations. To speak concretely, we support the view of the experienced majority of knowledgeable people (經驗派的 多數識者) who regret the dissolution (解消) of the Sin'ganhoe (新幹會). In short, the Sin'ganhoe is a living example that it is easy to tear down but difficult to build.
We must be wary of the destructionists who go around holding matchsticks looking only at one side of things, wanting to set fires. Therefore, let's adopt the motto that, although what is already torn down cannot be helped, we must never, ever tear down the various forces that are currently formed.

Second, let's cease all internal strife (內爭).
Recently, even an organization dedicated to denouncing a certain religion/teaching (某敎) has emerged. However, is this the time to engage in such mutual hostility and conflict over trivial matters (蝸牛角上)? The situation we collectively face is too large and somewhat more critical. Raising these one or two internal issues is, for knowledgeable people (識者) to consider, merely a microscopic fact fraught with peril (危懼). This year, let us stop these petty internal conflicts.

Third, let's discard utopian collectivism (공상적 전체주의).
A large contingent (大部隊) is needed for any task. In this respect, everyone is captivated by collectivism rather than the union of a small group. However, there are two ways to form a collective whole. There is the path where one unit gathers ten thousand times to become ten thousand, and there is also the path where one unit first gathers into a hundred, and that hundred then becomes the unit, and a hundred of those combine to become ten thousand. Considering our characteristic traditions and various circumstances, the latter organization seems more feasible and solid (堅窂) than the former. That is, shouldn't we strive for methods that skillfully bind together scholars as scholars, thinkers as thinkers, businessmen as businessmen, and besides those, the natural associations formed regionally, both vertically and horizontally (종으로 횡으로)? At least, that path seems possible. Therefore, I believe making a great effort towards collective formation through such methods is the right thing to do this year. <5>

**Summary**

This 1933 editorial urges Koreans at the start of the new year to focus on three principles: preserving existing organizations, ceasing internal conflicts, and pursuing pragmatic, bottom-up unity rather than abstract collectivism. The author specifically laments the recent dissolution of the Sin'ganhoe as an example of easily destroying something valuable and difficult to rebuild. The piece stresses the need for solidarity and practical organization in the face of larger challenges. For historians, the article offers insight into the mood of Korean intellectuals following the collapse of the Sin'ganhoe, highlighting the debate on national unity strategies under colonial rule. The explicit mention of internal strife, including conflict over religious or ideological matters, and the advocacy for a federated, functional approach to organization (by profession, region) reveals the practical difficulties and strategic thinking within the Korean nationalist movement during this period.

年頭感
새해부터 이 세 가지를 직혀가자.
첫재, 이미 결성 되어 잇는 세력을 결코 헐지마자.
무엇이나 헐기는 쉽다. 大廈高樓라도 성양갑 한 대면 헐기가 족하지만 다시 그 허러진 만들자면 실로 어렵다. 우리는 엇던 정세하에 잇슬지라도 表現團體의 절대필요성을 주장하는 자이요. 따라서 그 표현단체 때문에 타일면의 운동이 萎微하여저 간다는 논거를 승인치 안는 자이다. 구체적으로 말하면 新幹會의 解消를 후회하는 經驗派的 多數識者의 견해를 지지한다. 요컨대 新幹會는 헐기는 쉽고 만들기는 어렵다는 산 예의 其 一이다.
우리는 일면만 보고 성양갑을 쥐고 불질느려 다니는 파괴주의자들을 경계하여야 하겟다. 그래서 적어도 이미 허러진 것은 할 수 업스나 현재 결성되어 잇는 諸 세력은 결코 결코 헐지 말기를 못토-로 삼자.
둘재, 일체의 內爭을 중지하자
최근 某敎를 紏彈하는 결사까지 생겻다. 그러나 지금 蝸牛角上의 이러한 反目相爭을 일삼을 때 일가. 우리가 전체적으로 당면하여 잇는 형세는 너무 크고 좀 더 중대하다. 이러한 내부의 하나, 둘의 문제를 치키어들문 識者는 고려할 危懼한 현미경적 사실에 불과하다.  올에는 우리들은 조고마한 이런 內爭을 중지하자.
셋재, 공상적 전체주의를 버리자.
일에는 大部隊가 필요하다. 이러한 점에서 소수 층의 결합보다 전체주의에 누구나 도취한다. 그런데 전체를 결성하는 방법에 두 가지가 잇다. 단위 一이 萬이 모여 萬이 되는 길도 잇고, 一이 百으로 爲先 모히고 그 百이 비로소 단위되여 그것이 다시 百이 합처 萬이 되는 길도 잇다. 그런데 우리의 성격전통 또 諸 정세로 보아 전자보다 후자적 조직이 더 가능하고 堅窂 할 것 갓다  즉 학자는 학자, 사상가는 사상가, 실업가는 실업가로 그밧게 지역적으로 자연결합이 이루어저 잇는 그것을 종으로 횡으로 잘묵는 방법에 힘 쓸 것이 아닐가. 적어도 그 길이 가능할드시 생각된다. 그러기에 이러한 방식을 통하야 전체 결성의 大노력을 하는 것이 올흘 일인가 한다. ＜5＞
