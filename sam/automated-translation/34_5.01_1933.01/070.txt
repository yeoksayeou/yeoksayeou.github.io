Publication Date: 1933년 01월 01일
Article Title: 壁新聞 2, 新年에 나올 新獨立國 (Wall Newspaper 2, New Independent Nation to Emerge in the New Year)
Author: Not specified
Article Type: 소식 (News/Report)

France (佛蘭西), having experienced 10 years of difficult rule, is finally trying to withdraw from Syria (씨리아). Therefore, it is said they are preparing to grant independence to this ancient nation.

France (佛蘭西), considering the governments organized in Syria (씨리아) and Lebanon (레빠논) to be stable in light of the conditions of the Mandate (委任統治) received from the League of Nations (國際聯盟), has had the Mandates Commission (委任統治委員會) conduct the requested investigation, and it is said the League Council (聯盟理事會) will be able to declare the 'freedom and independence' of Syria (씨리아).

Among the League's mandated territories, the first country to become independent will likely be Iraq (이라크), which was under British (英國) rule. Iraq (이라크) is now one of the member states of the League (聯盟加入國).

During the 10 years of rule, it is said France (佛蘭西) suffered no small headache due to the nationalist movement in Syria (씨리아).<33>

**Summary**

This news report states that France is preparing to grant independence to Syria, a League of Nations mandate territory, after ten years of difficult rule marked by nationalist movements. This follows the precedent of Iraq gaining independence from British mandate rule and joining the League. The League of Nations Mandates Commission has investigated, and the League Council is expected to declare Syria's freedom and independence soon. From the perspective of Korean colonial history, this article is interesting as it shows awareness among Koreans of international affairs concerning mandated territories and independence movements. Reporting on the anticipated independence of Syria, following Iraq, could implicitly highlight the contrast with Korea's situation under Japanese rule and reflect aspirations for self-determination prevalent during the period.

壁新聞 2, 新年에 나올 新獨立國
10년간의 곤란한 통치를 격거 나온 佛蘭西는 필경 『씨리아』로부터 손을 끈으려고 한다. 그래서 이 오래인 나라에 독립을 주려고 준비중이라고 한다.
佛蘭西는 國際聯盟으로부터 밧은 委任統治의 조건에 빗최서 『씨리아』와 『레빠논』에 조직된 정부는 안정된 것으로 생각하엿슴으로 委任統治委員會는 요구바든 조사를 행하게 되여 聯盟理事會는 『씨리아』의 『자유와 독립』을 선언할 수 잇스리라 한다.
聯盟의 위임통치국 중에서 최초로 독립된 나라는 英國의 치하에 엿던 『이라크』일 것이다. 『이라크』는 지금 聯盟加入國의 한 나라다. 통치 10년간 佛蘭西는 『씨리아』의 국민운동 때문에 적지 안케 머리를 아럿다고 한다.＜33＞
