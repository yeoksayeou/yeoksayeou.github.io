Chongno Crossroads

The leader of Ireland's (愛蘭) subversive elements (?), Éamon de Valera, reportedly displayed his usual subversive attitude (?) even during the proceedings of the League of Nations Council meeting, causing Mr. Drummond and the representatives of the major powers to frown. One wonders if the police officers on Easter night hastily crossed the 'ttoŏ' (?) to extend an emergency cordon around the city of Geneva.

Rumors are circulating among the 'intelli' women of Kyŏngsŏng city that they are plotting the formation of a women's athletic organization. Soon we might see female champions competing in ssirŭm (Korean wrestling) tournaments. Perhaps newspaper companies, currently in a state of war, could compete to hire female guards, mobilizing both brute strength (腕力) and charm (愛嬌) as weapons for 100 percent efficiency. However, husbands who enjoy nightlife, husbands who interfere with their wives' outings, husbands stingy with cosmetic expenses—a pessimistic era will soon arrive for this type of husband, so be warned, be warned.

General Schleicher has risen to shoulder the difficult burden of the German cabinet, succeeding the fallen Papen. <42> The sound of sabers clatters chaotically in the streets of Berlin amidst the height of militarism. This epidemic influenza seems to have taken on global symptoms. What France feared was not ten million Nazis, but indeed the leader of the German Reichswehr, General Schleicher. I want to check Mr. Herriot's heart.

Last year, the new French President, Mr. Lebrun, entered the Elysée Palace saying he would become a servant of the constitution and just nap for seven years. This spring, the new US President, Mr. Roosevelt, a slave to 120 million people, moves into the White House (白煙舘) to spend four years without closing an eye, shaking hands with thousands of people daily, receiving thousands of phone calls, and writing hundreds of reply letters. That's why former President Mr. Harding reportedly grabbed a friend and cried out, "You know, being president is truly a difficult job." We pray for Mr. Roosevelt's well-being. <43>

**Summary**

This article from 1933 offers brief, critical commentary on various contemporary events. It touches upon international affairs, noting tensions involving Ireland's De Valera at the League of Nations, the rise of militarism in Germany under Schleicher, French anxieties, and the contrasting burdens of the French and incoming US presidents (Lebrun and Roosevelt). Domestically, it satirically discusses the potential rise of women's athletics and physical power in Kyŏngsŏng, warning traditional husbands of changing gender dynamics. For historians, the piece is interesting as it reveals Korean awareness and perspectives on major global political shifts (especially the rising tensions in Europe) and reflects evolving social norms and anxieties regarding women's roles within colonial Korean society, albeit presented with a somewhat mocking or cautionary tone.

鐘路네거리
愛蘭不穩分子(?)의 總師 『떼발레라』 연맹이사회의 사무 진행에 잇어서까지 例의 不穩態(?)를 발휘하야 『쭈러몬드』 君과 밋 大國側의 얼골을 찡그리게 하엿다고. 復活祭날 밤의 警官諸君 황망이 『또버』를 뛰여 건너서 『쥬네브』市에 非常線을 느리지나 안는지.

京城 市內의 『인텔리』여성들 사이에 여성 체육단체의 결성을 책략한다는 소문이 들린다. 이윽고 씨름대회에 여장사의 출전을 볼 것이고 요지음 전시상태에 잇는 신문사쯤에서는 다토아 女守衛를 쓴다면 腕力愛嬌함께 무기로 동원할 수 잇어서 능률 『100퍼-쎈트』일 것이다. 그러나 밤노리 잘하는 남편, 안해의 외출을 간섭하는 남편, 화장료 지출에 吝嗇한 남편─이윽고 이 종류이 남편에게는 悲觀時代가 올 것이니 주의주의.

『슐라이헬』장군이 『파-펜』의 꺼꾸러진 뒤를 니어 難産의 病兒인 獨逸내각을 질머지고 이러섯다.＜42＞ 伯林街頭에 『싸-벨』 소리 어지럽다. 『밀리타리즘』의 全盛에-. 유행성 感冒는 세계적 징후까지 띠고 잇다. 佛蘭西가 무서워한 것은 천 만의 『나치스』가 아니고 실로 獨逸國防軍의 總師 『슐라이헬』 장군이엿다. 『에리오』 君의 가슴을 지퍼보고 십다.

去年 프랑스 新대통령 『루붑렁』 君은 『헌법의 종이 되여 7년간 낫잠이나 자기 위하야 『엘리제』의 궁전으로 드러가더니 今春에는 1억 2천만 人民의 奴隷인 米國 新대통령 『루즈벨트』 君이 4년 동안 한 눈도 감을 새 업시 날마다 수 천명과 악수하고 수 천통의 전화를 밧고 수 백통의 편지 회답을 쓰기 위하야 白煙舘으로 이전한다. 그러니가 전 대통령 『하딩』씨는 동무를 붓잡고는 「자녜하고 니말이지 대통령이란 참 해먹기 어려운 노릇이야」 하고 비명을 올렷다고. 『룻즈벨트』 君의 건재를 빈다. ＜43＞
