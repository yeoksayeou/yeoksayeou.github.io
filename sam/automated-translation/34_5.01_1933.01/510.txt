Publication Date: 1933년 01월 01일
Article Title: 睡眠에 보내는 祈願, 現代英詩壇 (A Prayer Sent to Sleep, Modern English Poetry Scene)
Author: R·W·케튼크레머(作), 金珖燮(譯) (R. W. Ketton-Cremer (Author), Kim Kwang-sŏp (Translator))
Article Type: 시 (Poem)

O Sleep, come hidden by the cedar's shade,
And close these heavy, unclosing eyes.
With the wind that, when the moon rises, from the cherry tree laden with flower-burdens,
Sends a stream of blue petals flowing over the night,
Sleep, O Sleep, come silently.
And like sunlight resting upon the grass,
Descend upon me and lie still as sunlight.

And like fallen petals upon the dew
Exuding the last fragrance of their mother tree,
O beautiful Sleep, exude your fragrance.
Until the cock-crow at dawn from the sleeping farm awakens the eyes,
And the grand morning light shines beyond the sea's
Cold, grey plains,
Upon the few dark hours. -End- <106>

**Summary**

This article presents a Korean translation by Kim Kwang-sŏp of a poem titled "A Prayer Sent to Sleep" by the English writer R. W. Ketton-Cremer. The poem is a gentle invocation asking sleep to come quietly and restfully, bringing peace and fragrance like nature (cedars, cherry blossoms, sunlight, fallen petals), lasting through the short hours of darkness until dawn. From a historical perspective, this piece is interesting as it showcases the translation and dissemination of contemporary Western literature (specifically English poetry) within Korea during the Japanese colonial period (1933). It highlights the cultural activities of Korean intellectuals like Kim Kwang-sŏp and the role of popular magazines like *Samch'ŏlli* in introducing foreign culture to a Korean audience, reflecting ongoing cultural exchange despite colonial rule.

睡眠에 보내는 祈願, 現代英詩壇
						R·W·케튼크레머(作), 金珖燮(譯)
오 잠이여 삼나무(杉) 아지로 숨겨서 오라.
그리하야 이 무거우면서 안이 감기는 눈을 감게 하라.
달이 뜰 때 꼿짐을 실은 櫻花나무에서
파-란 꼿닙의 시내를 밤 우에 흘러 보내는 바람으로 더부러
잠이여 잠이여 잠잠히 오라.
그리하야 해ㅅ비가 풀 우에 머물드시
내 우에 내려서 해ㅅ비가치 고요히 누우라.

그리하야 떠러진 꼿닙이 이슬 우에
어머니 나무의 마지막 향기를 뿜드시
아름다운 잠이여 향기를 뿜으라.
잠자든 농원에서 닭 우는 새벽소리 눈을 띄우고
웅장한 아츰 빗이 바다의
차듸찬 회색 벌판 저편에 빗날 때까지
얼마 아니되는 컴컴한 시간 우에. -끗- ＜106＞
