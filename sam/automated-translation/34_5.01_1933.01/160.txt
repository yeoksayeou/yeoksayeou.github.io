Korea's Last Army and Defense Plan
						Elder Kwŏn Tong-jin Dictated

Before the Year of Kapo [1894], when the winds and clouds of East Asia were stirring with great enigmas, the Korean government, along with many other nations, also deployed troops to the central region, border areas, and rugged terrains under a superficially well-organized defense plan.

Although the Military Headquarters (軍部 - Kunbu) did not have something like the current General Staff Headquarters, the Minister of Military Affairs (兵曹判書 - Pyŏngjo P’ansŏ) among the Six Ministries (6曹 - Yukcho) served as the highest military authority, overseeing military discipline. Below him were the following Five Military Camps (五營門 - O Yŏngmun) protecting the capital (京師 - Kyŏngsa).

Minister of Military Affairs (兵曹判書 - Pyŏngjo P’ansŏ)
Military Training Command (訓練都監 - Hullyŏn Togam) - Commander-in-Chief of Military Training (訓鍊大將 - Hullyŏn Taejang)
Royal Guard Command (御營廳 - Ŏyŏngch'ŏng) - Commander-in-Chief of the Royal Guard (御營大將 - Ŏyŏng Taejang)
Capital Garrison Command (禁衛營 - Kŭmwiyŏng) - Commander-in-Chief of the Capital Garrison (禁衛大將 - Kŭmwi Taejang)
Royal Escort Command (護衛營 - Howiyŏng) - Commander-in-Chief of the Royal Escort (護衛大將 - Howi Taejang)
General Garrison Command (總戎廳 - Ch’ongyungch’ŏng) - General Garrison Commander (總戎師 - Ch’ongyungsa)

At that time, the Minister of Military Affairs was Min T’ae-ho (閔台鎬). Below him, Cho Yŏng-ha (趙寧河), the Commander-in-Chief of Military Training, commanded 5,772 troops. It is well known that around the Year of Imo [1882], when four of the Five Military Camps were abolished, he alone held his ground, selling his own farmland to pay his subordinate soldiers' salaries and defending the solitary fortress. He was one of the victims of the Kapsin Reform [1884]. Besides him, I vaguely recall Sin Chŏng-hŭi (申正熙) serving as Commander-in-Chief of the Royal Escort. In addition to the 5,772 men of the Military Training Command (訓練廳 - Hullyŏnch'ŏng), each camp had several thousand soldiers. These forces were deployed inside and outside the capital city to defend the central region. In the provinces, there were Provincial Army Commanders (兵使 - Pyŏngsa) in Anju and Hwangju, and North and South Provincial Army Commanders <48> in Hoeryŏng and Pukch’ŏng respectively, with several hundred to several thousand troops assigned to each to counter foreign invasions from the north. In the Samnam region [Ch’ungch’ŏng, Chŏlla, Kyŏngsang Provinces], there were three Provincial Army Commanders in Ch’ŏngju, Kangjin, and Ulsan. For the navy, there were Provincial Navy Commanders (水使 - Susa) in Ongjin (Hwanghae) and Tongnae, and Left and Right Provincial Navy Commanders in Yŏsu and Haenam.

Above them, the Supreme Naval Commander (統提使 - T’ongjesa) in T’ongyŏng commanded the land and sea forces of the Samnam region. The Supreme Naval Commander was granted not only military authority but also a tiger head emblem (虎頭 - Hod’u) and a sword. If he sent out the sword inserted into the tiger head emblem, he had the immense power to arrest even a Provincial Governor (監使 - Kamsa) and execute first, report later (先斬後戒 - Sŏnch’am Hugye).

Besides these, there were Defense Commanders (防禦使 - Pangŏsa) in three locations: Sŏnch’ŏn, Kilchu, and Ch’ŏrwŏn. In today's terms, the Defense Commander would correspond to the rank of Lieutenant General, along with the Vice Commander (中軍 - Chunggun) of the Five Military Camps.

Furthermore, in the strategic locations of Kanghwa, Suwŏn, Kwangju, and Ch’unch’ŏn, there were Special Governors (留守 - Yusu). Civil or military officials of the first rank or higher concurrently managed both land and sea military affairs and supervised the magistrates of neighboring counties (隣郡 - Ingun). Not only that, but local magistrates (수령 - Suryŏng) in border regions were mostly appointed from military officers to prepare for contingencies. However, corrupt officials in the capital (京師 - Kyŏngsa) disliked being assigned to border regions, considering them remote outposts. Although the system appeared orderly on the surface, it is needless to say that the military preparedness and system did not match in name and reality.

In each military camp and naval command post, there was an ordnance workshop (車器廠 - Kŏgich’ang) where weapons were taken out, repaired, and maintained twice every five years. Although large-scale exercises for land and sea forces were supposed to be held in spring and autumn, they were rarely conducted in the late Joseon period. Soldiers returned home to farm and served in rotation (番 - Pŏn), so training was naturally insufficient.

After the Year of Imo [1882], the position of Minister of Military Affairs (軍部大臣 - Kunbu Taejin) was created, and the aforementioned systems were abolished. Left and Right Camps (左右兩營 - Chwa U Yangyŏng) were newly established, and later, Front and Rear Camps (前後兩營 - Chŏn Hu Yangyŏng) were added. After the Year of Kapo [1894], Left and Right Training Regiments (左右訓練隊 - Chwa U Hullyŏndae) and two Royal Guard Units (근위대 - Kŭnŭidae) were established, each with 500 soldiers.

Later, referencing Japan's new military system, the deployment of central and local military forces was revised as follows. However, on the night of July 31st, Lung-hŭi 1 [1907], the then Minister of Military Affairs, Yi Pyŏng-mu (李秉武), received the imperial edict for the disbandment of the army. On August 1st, the disbandment ceremony was held at the Hullyŏnwŏn (훈련원 - Military Training Ground), and thus, Korea's last army ceased to exist.

**Summary**

This article, dictated by Elder Kwŏn Tong-jin, recounts the structure of the Korean military before the Kapo Reforms of 1894, focusing on the traditional Five Military Camps system centered around the Minister of Military Affairs. It then describes the subsequent changes, including the establishment of new camp formations and later, regiments modeled after the Japanese system. The core of the article details the final organization and deployment of the Korean army, both in the capital and provinces (the Chinwidae garrisons), immediately before its forced disbandment on August 1, 1907, under Japanese pressure, ordered by Minister of War Yi Pyŏng-mu. The piece provides a valuable first-hand perspective on the state of the Korean military in its final years, highlighting the superficiality of earlier reforms and the ultimate dismantling of Korea's defense capabilities leading up to annexation. Historians would find the detailed breakdown of unit names, locations, officer ranks, and troop numbers just before disbandment particularly useful for understanding the scale and structure of the Korean Empire's last military force and the process of its dissolution.

韓國最後의 軍隊와 國防計劃
						權東鎭 老述
甲午年이전 東洋의 풍운이 바야흐로 큰수수걱기를 품고 움직일 때에 韓國政府도 또한 뭇 나라와 함끼 表面整然한 국방계획 아래서 중앙과 국경지대와 惡險地에 各各 군대를 배치한 것이다.
사관시대의 권동진
軍部에는 지금 참모본부와 가튼 것은 업서스나 6曹中 兵曹判書가 軍部 최고기관으로서 군기를 統轄하엿다. 그 아래 아래와 가튼 5營門이 잇어서 京師를 지키고 잇엇다.

兵曹判書
訓練都監 - 訓鍊大將
御營廳 - 御營大將
禁衛營 - 禁衛大將
護衛營 - 護衛大將
總戎廳 - 總戎師
당시의 병조판서은 閔台鎬엿고 그 아래 훈련대장 趙寧河가 5,772명의 수하 군대를 거느리고 잇다가 壬午年인가 五營門中에 네營門이 업서질 때에도 그는 호을로 버티고 잇스면서 자기 소유의 田地를 파라서 부하 군졸의 급료를 지불하면서 孤城을 수호한 것은 일홈노푼 일이다.  그는 甲申改革의 희생자의 한 사람이다. 그밧게 申正熙가 호위대장으로 잇슨 것을 히미하게 기억한다. 訓練廳의 5,772명 이외에 各營에는 각각 수천명식의 군인이 잇섯다. 이상의 군대가 중앙을 護守하기 위하야 都城의 내외에 배치되엿고 지방에는 安州병사, 黃州병사, 會寧, 北靑兩地에 南北병사가＜48＞ 잇어서  북방의 外寇侵略에 各各 수백수천의 군대가 배정되여 잇섯다. 三南에는 淸州, 康津, 蔚山에 3兵使가 잇섯고 수군으로는 甕津(黃海)束萊에 각각 水使가 잇섯고 麗水, 海南에 左右水使가 잇섯다.
그우에 統營에 統提使가 잇어서 三南의 수륙대군을 통솔하엿다. 統提使는 군사뿐 아니라 虎頭와 칼을 하사밧고서 虎頭에 칼을 박어서 내보내면 監使라도 포박하여서 先斬後戒하는 큰 권한이 부여되여 잇섯다.
그밧게 宣川, 吉州, 鐵原 세 곳에 防禦使가 잇섯다. 지금말로 하면 防禦使는 五營門의 中軍과 함끼 中將의 지위에 해당할 것이다.
그러고는 要害의 지대로서 江華, 水原, 廣州, 春川 네 곳에 留守가 잇서서 일품 이상의 문무관이 수륙 양방의 군무를 겸해보며 隣郡의 군수들을 그 감독 아래 두엇다. 그뿐 아니라 국경지방의 수령으으는 대개는 武將을 파견하야 만일에 備하엿스며 京師의 貪官輩는 外地라고 하야 국경지방에 出仕하는 것을 꺼렷다. 그러치만 표면상 제도는 이러케 정연하나 실상에 잇서서 軍備와 兵制가 명실이 상부하지 못하엿던 것은 물론이다.
各營門과 水使가 잇는 곳에 車器廠이 잇서서 5년에 두번식 軍器를 꺼내서 수리정돈하며 春秋로 수륙군에 各各 대 연습이 잇섯다고하나 韓末에는 거의 실행한 일이 업고 군인은 도라가서는 농사를 일삼고 番을 갈라서 복무하니 훈련이 부족하엿슴은 물론이다.
壬午年 이후에 軍部大臣이 생기고 前述한 각 제도는 업서지고 左右兩營이 신설되엿다가 그후에 前後兩營이 더 생겻드니 甲午年 이후에는 左右훈련대와 근위대 2부대에 각각 500명식의 군인이 배치되여 잇섯다.
그후 일본의 新兵制를 참작하야 아래와 가치 중앙과 지방의 군비배치를 개정하엿스나  隆熙 元年 7월31일밤 당시의 군부대신 李秉武는 군대해산의 詔를 밧고 8월1일 훈련원에서 해산식을 거행하니 이에 한국 최후의 군대도 업서지고 만 것이다.
