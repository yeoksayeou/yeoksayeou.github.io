**Header Section**
Magazine Title: 삼천리 (三千里)
Publication Date: 1935년 11월 01일
Article Title: 社告 (Company Announcement)
Author: None
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Announcement/Editorial Postscript)

**Body Text Translation**
Company Announcement

From the next issue, many good children's songs (童謠) and children's stories (童話) will be published.<204>

**Summary**

This brief announcement informs readers that the magazine *Samch'ŏlli* will begin publishing a significant number of children's songs and stories starting with the subsequent issue. For historians, this notice highlights the magazine's content strategy and potentially reflects a growing interest in or perceived need for children's literature and cultural content within Korean society during the colonial period, possibly aimed at education or shaping young minds.
