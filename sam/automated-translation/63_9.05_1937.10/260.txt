**Body Text Translation:**
The centennial festival for the death of Pushkin (1799-1837), the great national poet Russia (露西亞) boasts to the world, was recently held on a large scale in the Soviet Union (소聯) as a grand national event (盛事). On this day, not only was there a magnificent festival (祭典) in Moscow's (모스코바) Pushkin Square, but also all factories, collectives, farms, and schools simultaneously held a "Pushkin Evening." Besides this, all cultural institutions such as newspapers, radio (라듸오), and theaters were fully mobilized. Furthermore, the Soviet authorities designated this entire year as the "Year of Pushkin Commemoration," planning various commemorative projects such as an All-Russian (全露) Pushkin exhibition at the Moscow History Museum, the publication of 18.4 million commemorative books, and the erection of monuments at Pushkin's historic sites. Additionally, it was decided that Pushkin's grandson would be given an annual pension of 300 rubles from the national treasury (國庫).

Furthermore, the Pushkin commemorative festival in Shanghai (上海) corresponded with that in Moscow. The Shanghai branch of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Association (中소文化協會) took the lead, and on the Chinese side, Mr. Sun Fo (孫科氏), president of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Association, and Mr. Cai Yuanpei (蔡元培氏), head of the Academia Sinica (中央硏究員長), along with the Soviet Consul General in Shanghai and over one thousand several hundred people gathered at the "Isis" (아이시스) movie theater (映畵舘) for a grand commemorative festival with music and other events. Also on that day, an unveiling ceremony for a new Pushkin statue was held in Shanghai's "Pushkin" Square by the hands of the Shanghai White Russian Association (上海 白系露人團).<31>

**Summary**

This article reports on the extensive celebrations for the 100th anniversary of Alexander Pushkin's death in 1937. The Soviet Union treated it as a major state event, mobilizing all cultural resources, planning year-long activities including exhibitions and publications, and even granting a pension to Pushkin's grandson. Simultaneously, a large commemorative event was held in Shanghai, organized by the Sino-Soviet Cultural Association and attended by prominent Chinese figures like Sun Fo and Cai Yuanpei, alongside Soviet officials. Notably, the White Russian community in Shanghai also erected a statue of Pushkin. For historians, this piece offers insights into Soviet cultural diplomacy and its projection of national pride through literary figures during the 1930s. It also highlights the complex international dynamics in Shanghai, involving the KMT government, the Soviet Union, and the White Russian émigré community, all finding common ground in celebrating a Russian cultural icon, even amidst the backdrop of colonial Korea observing these events.

푸시킨 百年祭, 소聯文化기관을 總動員, 上海에 문호의 동상이 서다
露西亞가 세계에 자랑하는 국민적 대시인 푸-시킨(1799-1837)의 사후 百年祭가 지난번 소련에서 국가의 盛事로 대규모로 거행되엿다. 이날 모스코바의 푸-시킨 광장에서 성대한 祭典이 있었음은 물론 각 공장 집단, 농장, 학교에서는 일제히 「푸-시킨의 저녁」을 하였다. 그밖에 신문, 라듸오, 극장 등 모든 문화 기관을 총동원하였다. 더욱 소련 당국에서는 금년 1년을 「푸-시킨 기념의 해」라 하여 모스코바 역사 박물관에서 全露 푸-시킨 전람회, 1,840만 책의 기념 출판, 푸-시킨 유적에 기념비를 세울 일등 여러 가지 기념 사업이 계획되고 있다. 그리고 푸-시킨의 손자에게 금번에 年額 300留식의 연금을 국고에서 주기로 되였다.
더욱 上海의 푸-시킨 기념제는 모스코바와 호응하여 上海의 中소文化協會 상해지부가 주체가 되여 中國 측으로 中소文協 회장 孫科氏, 中央硏究員長 蔡元培氏, 소련으로 상해 총영사 기타 일천 수백 명이 映畵舘 「아이시스」에 모여 음악 등을 하면서 성대한 기념제를 하엿다. 또 그 날 上海 白系露人團의 손에 依하여, 上海 「푸-시킨」 광장에서 새로운 푸-시킨 동상 제막식이 있었다.＜31＞
