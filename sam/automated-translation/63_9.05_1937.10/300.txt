**Body Text Translation:**
A Grand Stadium of 6 Million Yen, Luxurious Facilities for the Tokyo Olympics

Stadium Decision Amidst Difficulties
Preparations are steadily progressing for the Tokyo Olympics scheduled to be held three years from now in Shōwa 15 (1940). Among these, the locations for the main stadium and swimming pool, which have consistently drawn public attention, have recently been finalized. Specifically, it has been decided to renovate Jingū Gaien (神宮外苑), and Dr. Kishida's (岸田博士) renovation master plan has been approved. The designs for the main stadium and swimming pool are as follows.

Main Stadium
Total cost: 4 million Yen; Total area: 20,000 *pyeong*; Location: Same as present
1. The exterior walls will be of a bright white type, avoiding dark colors. At the northernmost end, a white Olympic Tower, Marathon Tower, and Holy Fire Cauldron (聖火聖) will be established. Inside the Olympic and Marathon towers, facilities such as radio and motion picture filming equipment, lighting fixtures, etc., will be installed, with the lower part serving as entrances/exits for stands, tunnels, etc.
1. The flagpole stand will be established on the mountain side (*山側) opposite the Olympic Tower.
1. For the main stand, a roof covering 23 tiers will be built over the uppermost section for about 100 meters in the central part.

Swimming Pool
Total cost: 2 million Yen; Total area: approx. 4,000 *pyeong*; Location: Same as present; Length: East-West; Capacity: 30,000 people
1. A tall tower will be erected in the center of the main stand, equipped with motion picture filming facilities, radio, and lighting equipment.
1. The flagpole tower will be at the easternmost end.
1. The stands will be 16 meters high, 27 meters deep at the base, totaling 44 tiers.
1. For the pool, a 25-meter square diving pool (-* 풀-) will be established on the east side, and directly in front, a racing pool (競泳풀-) 25 meters wide and 50 meters long will be set up. The diving ( -힝구) and racing pools will be approximately 5 meters deep. <37>

**Summary**

This 1937 news article reports on the finalized plans for the main venues of the 1940 Tokyo Olympics. The Jingū Gaien area was chosen for renovation based on Dr. Kishida's plan. The main stadium, costing 4 million Yen, would feature a white exterior, integrated towers for broadcasting and filming, and a partially roofed main stand. The swimming pool, costing 2 million Yen, would accommodate 30,000 spectators and include a central tower, separate diving and racing pools. For historians, this article is interesting as it details Japan's ambitious preparations for the Olympics amidst rising militarism and just before the outbreak of the Second Sino-Japanese War, highlighting the large investment and intended scale of an event ultimately cancelled due to the war. It reflects Japan's efforts to project international prestige during the colonial period, an endeavor reported on even in Korean magazines like *Samch'ŏlli*.

난관 중이든 경기장 결정
3년 후인 昭和 15년에 열니기로 된 올림픽 東京大會는 모든 준비가 착착 진행되여 가고 있다. 그 중에도 늘 세인의 주목을 끌어오든 주경기장과 수영장의 장소는 요즈음 아조 결정이 되었다. 즉 神宮外苑을 개조하기로 하고 岸田博士의 改造 原案을 승인하였는데 이제 주경기장과 수영장의 설계를 보면 이러하다.

주경기장
총 경비 4백만 원 총면적 2만평 위치 현재 同樣
1. 외벽은 명랑한 白色系로 暗色을 掛하며 최북단에 白色系의 올님픽탑 마라손탑, 及 聖火聖을 設하여 올님픽, 마라손 兩塔 내부에 라디오, 활동사진 촬영설비 조명기 등의 설비을 하여 그 하부는 스탄드, 톤널 등 출입구
1. 국기 게양대는 올님픽탑 반대측의 *山側에 說하며
1. 메인스탄은 중앙부 약 百米突間의 최상부 23단의 지붕을 만든다.

水泳場
총 경비 2백만 원, 총면적 4천坪位 場現在와 同樣 東西에 길이 收客人員 3만인
1. 메인스탄드 중앙부에 高塔을 세우고 활동사진 촬영설비, 라디오, 조명기의 설비를 함.
