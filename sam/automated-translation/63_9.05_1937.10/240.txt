**The "Korean Language Usage Ban" Issue Discussed in the Diet, Q&A between the Prime Minister, Minister of Colonial Affairs, and a Diet Representative**

On August 18th, the Tong-A Ilbo newspaper reported that the Government-General's Bureau of Educational Affairs authorities were abolishing "Korean Language and Classical Chinese" courses in Higher Common Schools and Girls' Higher Common Schools. The following day (August 19th issue), the Chosŏn Ilbo newspaper, even revealing a statement from the *former Director of the Bureau of Educational Affairs*, reported that such a thing was a false rumor. Thus, it was truly a case of truth versus falsehood, leaving people hesitant about which newspaper's report to believe regarding the outcome. In any case, since this spring, after the issue of banning the use of Korean by government officials arose, many people have been agitated once again by this "Issue of Abolishing Korean Language Courses in Middle Schools." However, just now, along with the statement from the Director of the Government-General's Bureau of Educational Affairs, there was good material allowing a glimpse into the government's fundamental policy. This was the discussion of this issue between a member of the House of Representatives, the Prime Minister, and the Minister of Colonial Affairs during the 71st Imperial Diet, which extended into early August, letting the Korean populace know the government's stance on the matter.

**Question Regarding the National Language Issue in the Outer Territories**
Member of the House of Representatives, Tōgō Minoru

**Part 1: The Issue of Banning the Use of Korean in Chosŏn**
1. Regarding the banning of Korean language use in schools: According to common rumors, the use of Korean is to be banned in Common Schools and Higher Common Schools, and those who use Korean will be punished with fines or other penalties. Is this true?
2. If it is true, when and in what form was such a prohibition issued? Also, what are the reasons and objectives necessitating such a policy? Please explain in detail.
3. I have heard that the use of Korean has been banned in public assemblies such as the Central Council (中樞院), Provincial Councils (通會), Prefectural/Municipal/Town Councils (府邑會), and Township Consultative Councils (面協議會). If this is true, since when was this policy of prohibition adopted?
4. It is said that the Government-General's Documents Section recently decided that Government-General officials are prohibited from using Korean not only in government offices but also at home, and that those who do not comply with this order will be punished without leniency. Is this true?
5. If it is true, when and in what form was the prohibition order issued? Also, what are the reasons and objectives necessitating such a policy? Please explain in detail.
6. Please indicate, broken down by gender, the number of people in Chosŏn's total population who can understand the National Language (Japanese) and their percentage relative to the total population.

**Part 2: The National Language Issue in Taiwan**
1. It is said that the Classical Chinese columns in daily newspapers in Taiwan were recently completely abolished. Please explain in detail the reasons and objectives for this abolition, as well as the process leading up to it.
2. Does the Government-General of Taiwan intend in the future, similar to Chosŏn, to ban the use of the Taiwanese language in schools, government offices, homes, etc.? Or not?
3. Please indicate, broken down by gender, the number of people among the total Main Islander (本島人) population who can understand the National Language (Japanese) and their percentage relative to the total population.

1. & 2. That is not true. However, concerning school education, a directive outlining the policy for thorough National Language education and encouragement (勵行) of National Language use was notified to the supervisors, the Provincial Governors, dated May 20, Shōwa 12 (1937).
3., 4. & 5. That is not true. However, a notification was issued to each affiliated government office, dated June 18, Shōwa 12 (1937), stating that the National Language should be used as much as possible while on duty in government offices.

**Summary**

This 1937 news article reports on a Q&A session in the Japanese Imperial Diet concerning rumors of a ban on the Korean language in Korea (Chosŏn) and the status of Taiwanese language in Taiwan. Diet member Tōgō Minoru questioned Prime Minister Konoe Fumimaro and Minister of Colonial Affairs Ōtani Takeyuki about alleged prohibitions in schools, public assemblies, government offices, and even homes. The officials denied any explicit ban on Korean or Taiwanese, stating that policies merely encouraged the use of Japanese (the "National Language") to promote assimilation ("Nae-Sŏn Yunghwa" / "Dōka"). They confirmed directives were issued in 1937 to promote Japanese usage in schools and government offices in Korea. Regarding Taiwan, they explained the recent voluntary abolition of Classical Chinese newspaper columns stemmed from a desire to aid Japanese language dissemination. Historians might find this article interesting for its documentation of official denials contrasted with widespread public rumors about language suppression, illustrating the tension between stated policy (encouragement) and perceived reality (prohibition). It also highlights the explicit goal of assimilation through language policy in both colonies and provides specific dates for directives aimed at increasing Japanese usage in official spheres.

8월 18일의 東亞日報 紙上에는 總督府 學務 當局에서 高等普通學校 及 女子高等普通學校에 대하야 「朝鮮語 及 漢文」科를 폐지한다 하였고, 그 翌日(8월 19일부)의 朝鮮日報 紙上에는 *原學務局長의 언명까지를 드러 그런 일이 虛說임을 보도하였기 때문에 그야말로 虛虛實實, 양지의 어느 所報를 믿었으면 좋을는지 世人은 그 歸趨에 망서리게 되어있다. 어쨋든 今春 이래로 官公吏의 朝鮮語 使用 禁止 問題가 있은 후, 多數한 世人은 今番 「中等學校朝鮮語科 廢止 問題」에 또 한번 충동을 받고 있다. 그런데 이제 마츰 總督府 學務局長의 언명도 있었거니와 정부의 근본 방침을 엿볼 수 있는 好자료가 있었으니, 그는 즉 8월 초순에까지 미친 제71 帝國議會에서 이 문제가 衆議院 代議士와, 內閣 總理大臣 及 拓務大臣과의 사이에 論議가 있어 조선 민중으로 하여곰 同 문제에 대한 정부 방침을 알게 한 바가 있었다.

外地의 國語 問題에 關한 質問
衆議院 議員 東鄕實

제1 朝鮮에서의 朝鮮語의 使用禁止問題
1. 학교에서의 朝鮮語의 사용 금지 世間所傳에 의하면 普通學校 及 高等普通學校에서는 朝鮮語의 使用을 禁止하게, 朝鮮語를 사용하는 자에 대하여는 罰金 기타의 형식으로서 처벌을 가한다 하는데 과연 그가 사실인가.
2. 만약 그가 사실이라면 何時에 如何한 형식으로써 如斯한 禁止金을 發하였는가, 또 如斯한 방침을 필요로 하는 理由 及 其 目的은 奈邊에 在한가. 상세히 설명하라.
3. 中樞院, 通會, 府邑會, 面協議會 등의 公會에서 朝鮮語의 使用을 禁止하였다고 들었는데 과연 사실이면 언제부터 禁止 方針을 잡었는가.
4. 최근 總督府 文書課에서 總督府의 官公吏는 官公廳에 있어서는 물론이요, 가정에서도 朝鮮語의 使用을 禁止하며 該 命令에 복종치 안는 자에 대하여는 가차없이 처벌하기로 결정하였다는데 과연 그가 사실인가.
5. 만약 사실이라면 언제 如何한 형식으로 금지령을 발하였는가. 又 如斯한 방침을 필요로 하는 理由 及 其 目的은 奈邊에 在한가. 상세히 설명하여 달라.
6. 朝鮮 總人口 중 능히 國語를 이해할 수 있는 자의 數 及 其 總人口에 대한 비율을 남녀별로 示하여 달나.

1. 臺灣에 잇서서 최근 일간신문의 漢文欄을 全廢하엿다는데 其 廢止의 理由 及 目的 並 廢止에 至하기까지의 경과를 상세히 설명하여 달나.
2. 臺灣總督府는 장래 조선에 있어서와 同樣으로 학교, 관공서, 가정 등에서서의 臺灣語의 사용을 금지하려 하는 의도를 가지고 있는가, 어떤가.
3. 本島人 總人口 중 능히 國語를 이해할 수 있는 자의 數 及 總人口에 대한 비율을 남녀별로 示하여 달나.
