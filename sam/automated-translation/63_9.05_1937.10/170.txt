Corrigenda

In the previous issue, page 8, paragraph 1, line 39, 'Labor Union Newspaper' (勞働組合新聞) was a misprint for 'Commerce and Industry Newspaper' (商工新聞).

In the same [issue], paragraph 2, lines 27-8, what was printed as 'Past experiments and future prospects' (Past expert ments and prospects) was a misprint for 'Past experiences and future prospects' (Past experiences and future prospects).

In the same [issue], page 9, paragraph 1, line 13, 'Social Science' (사회과학) was a misprint for 'Political Science' (정치과학).

In the same [issue], paragraph 6, lines 11-2, 'non-resistant' (무저항적) was a misprint for 'irresistible' (불가항적).

In the same [issue], paragraph 4, line 3 and line 18, 'Dictatorshiig' was a misprint for 'Dictatorship'. <18>

**Summary**

This article is a list of corrections (corrigenda) for errors found in the previous issue of the Samch'ŏlli magazine (Vol. 9, No. 4, published September 1937). It rectifies misprinted names of newspapers (Labor Union Newspaper vs. Commerce and Industry Newspaper), Korean terminology (experiments vs. experiences, social science vs. political science, non-resistant vs. irresistible), and a typographical error in the English word 'Dictatorship'. For historians, this brief notice reveals the types of publications and concepts (labor newspapers, commerce newspapers, social/political science, dictatorship) being discussed in the magazine, reflecting intellectual and social concerns under Japanese colonial rule. The specific corrections highlight areas prone to error and the importance placed on precise terminology in contemporary discourse.
