The Business World and New Figures

A New Face in the Textile Industry, Mr. Hong Chae-ryong
Mr. Hong is a very gentle, talented, and quick-witted individual. After graduating from Nihon University (日本大學) in Shōwa 10 (1935) and returning to his hometown, he constructed a modern factory on a total area of about 5,000 *p'yŏng* with an investment of around 40,000 won. This is the Choyang Textile Company (朝陽紡織會社), truly the pride of Kanghwa (江華). Construction began in the spring of Shōwa 11 (1936) and was finally completed just this April. Currently, the weaving machinery alone is worth over 23,000 won, employing 130 female workers and about 20 other employees. While daily production is only 6,000 yards for now, he is reportedly planning to increase the number of machines by 200-300 in the near future to create a large-scale factory.

Furthermore, receiving a subsidy of over 9,000 won from the Government-General (總督府), Mr. Hong also manages a pottery company (窯業會社) with a capital of 200,000 won. (This company manufactures earthenware vessels for storing alcohol, soy sauce, etc. The raw materials are rare nationally in Chosŏn, but abundant only in Kanghwa. Until now, these vessels were mostly purchased at high prices from mainland Japan.) It is said this company will also increase its capital to 500,000 won in the near future. It currently has over 110 workers, and its annual production value is said to be over 90,000 won. Mr. Hong is not only involved in business but also enjoys Chinese poetry (漢詩) and possesses political skill (政治的 手腕), having become a candidate for the Provincial Council (道評議員) this past spring. At just 28 years old, he is truly a young and vigorous industrialist, rich in years ahead of him.

Education and the Mining Industry - Mr. Kim Nŭng-su of P'yŏngyang
Upon first glance, Mr. Kim is a very dignified gentleman. His character is generous, and he has a broad understanding of worldly affairs.
After graduating from the Political Economy Department of Meiji University (明治大學) in Taishō 13 (1924), he spent about 10 years as a trader and has now been involved in the mining industry for a full 10 years as of this year.
Mr. Kim is currently a Provincial Councilor (道議員), an officer in the Chamber of Commerce and Industry (商工會議所), a director of Sungin Commercial School (崇仁商業學校), and is also involved with the City Council (府會). During this time, he has served four terms in the Chamber of Commerce and Industry and four terms in the City Council. When asked what he would do if he made about ten million won from mining, he replied with a most friendly expression that he would use it for educational endeavors, industrial development, and social projects. Still only 41 years old, with a weight of 25 *kwan* [approx. 94 kg], he possesses a truly imposing physique. While Mr. Kim is already dedicating significant effort to educational projects, he realizes that education is indispensably the most urgent priority for the advancement of the Korean people and plans to focus even more effort in this area in the future. Truly, much is anticipated from his future endeavors. <21>

**Summary**

This article profiles two young Korean entrepreneurs in 1937: Hong Chae-ryong, a 28-year-old textile and pottery factory owner in Kanghwa, and Kim Nŭng-su, a 41-year-old trader and mine operator in P'yŏngyang. Both are graduates of Japanese universities (Nihon and Meiji) and are actively involved not only in business but also in colonial administrative and economic bodies like the Provincial Council and Chamber of Commerce. Hong received a Government-General subsidy for his pottery business, while Kim expresses aspirations to use potential wealth for education and social development. For historians, this piece offers a glimpse into the activities and mindset of the emerging Korean capitalist class under Japanese rule, highlighting their engagement with the colonial system (education, administration, subsidies) alongside their business ventures and stated goals of contributing to Korean society, particularly through education.

事業界와 新人物
紡織界의 新人, 洪在龍씨
씨는 매우 부드럽고 多才敏活한 분이다. 昭和 10년에 日本大學을 졸업하고 고향에 도라온 후 총면적 약 5천 평에다 4만여 원이란 돈으로 근대식 공장을 건축하였는데 이것이 곳 江華에서 가장 자랑할 만한 朝陽紡織會社이다. 이것이 기공되기는 昭和 11년 봄, 금년 4월에야 겨우 落成되였거니와 현재 방직기계 갑만도 2만 3천여 원 이상 여공이 130명 기타 종업원이 20여명인데 지금은 매일 생산고 6천 야루 밖에 아니 되나 不遠하여 기계 2, 3백대를 더 증가시켜 가지고 규모 있는 큰 공장을 만들고저 계획 중이라 한다.
씨는 또 한편으로 總督府에서 9천여 원의 보조를 받어 가지고 자본금 20만원의 窯業會社를 경영하는데(술, 간장, 류를 담는 사긔그릇을 제조하는 회사, 원료는 조선적으로 희귀하나 오직 江華는 만히 난다. 지금까지 일본 내지에서 대부분 고가로 사다 쓰든 그릇) 이 회사도 머지 않은 장래에 50만원으로 증자하리라 한다. 현재에 110여 명의 작업원이 있으며 年産額은 9만여 원이라고 씨는 또 실업 방면 뿐 아니라 漢詩도 좋아하고 政治的 手腕도 있어 今春에는 道評議員 입후보자가 되였었다. 씨의 나히는 지금 겨우 28세 참으로 춘추 富한 小壯 實業家이다.

敎育과 鑛業界-平壤 金能秀씨
씨는 한번 보아 매우 근엄한 신사다. 그리고 그 성격이 寬厚하고 世事에 이해가 넓다.
大正 13년에 明治大學 政經科를 畢業한 후 무역상으로 10여 년 또 鑛業界에 投足한지가 금년채 10년 동안이나 된다.
씨는 현재 道議員으로서 일방 商工會議所 役員이며 崇仁商業學校 理事요 府會에도 관계를 가지고 있다. 그동안 商工會議所에 4차 府會에도 4차나 등장하였었다. 鑛業으로 일천만원 가량 생기면 어데다 쓰겠느냐고 하니까 그는 가장 다정한 표정으로 敎育事業 實業 方面 社會事業 등에라 한다. 아직도 41세 체중이 25貫 참으로 위대한 체격이다. 씨는 현재도 교육사업에 다대한 힘을 쓰고 있는 중이지만 조선 사람이 향상하여 가자면 不可不 교육이 급선무임을 깨닷고 앞으로는 일층 더 이 방면에 주력하리라 하니 진실노 앞날이 기대되는 바이다. ＜21＞
