Preview of the Coming October Issue

1. An Analysis of the Background and Base of Mr. Ch'oe Rin (崔麟)

2. Record of a Conversation with Mr. Hong Myŏnghŭi (洪命憙)

3. Successive Prominent Leaders Full of Fighting Spirit: Leaders of Ch'ŏndogyo (天道敎), Christianity (基督敎), Buddhism (佛敎), Successive Chairmen of the Labor Federation (勞總), Farmer Federation (農總), Youth Federation (靑總) <92>

**Summary**

This text is an announcement previewing articles scheduled for the October 1933 issue of the magazine *Samch'ŏlli*. It highlights upcoming features focusing on prominent Korean figures Ch'oe Rin and Hong Myŏnghŭi, and a piece covering the historical leadership of major religious (Ch'ŏndogyo, Christianity, Buddhism) and social/political organizations (Labor, Farmer, and Youth Federations). For historians, this preview is interesting as it indicates the magazine's engagement with contemporary influential individuals and major organizational structures within colonial Korean society, reflecting the issues and personalities deemed significant for public discourse at the time.
