**Body Text Translation**

Samch'ŏlli Wall Newspaper

Impressions from a World Tour
×
Mr. Bernard Shaw (너드, 쏘-翁), who recently returned to London (英京「론돈」) after finishing his world tour, said,
"Through this trip, I finally clearly understood that while people living in civilized countries (文明國) are unhappy and have many worries, conversely, people living in uncivilized countries (非文明國) live peacefully (泰平) and comfortably (安樂)."
Regarding his impression of the United States (米國), he mentioned being surprised by the idiotic (白痴) questions from over 100 newspaper reporters who rushed to his hotel, asking things like,
"Mr. Shaw (쏘-氏), essentially, who is greater, you or Shakespeare (쐭스피어)?"
(London Dispatch)

Duel Revival
Held at Germany's Heidelberg University
As soon as Hitler (힛틀러) received the seal of office <35> as Chancellor of the German Federation (獨逸聯邦宰相), the very first new policy he implemented was the order to revive dueling (決鬪復活令). Saying that vitality and vigor are what Young Germany, which will bear the burden of implementing the "National Revolution" (國民革命), needs most, and that dueling (決鬪) is the best way to cultivate (涵養) this, the order was given to revive dueling, which had been prohibited for eight years since the German Republic (獨逸 共和國).
×
Therefore, at Heidelberg University (하이델, 벨히大學), which possesses a 550-year old tradition (녯傳統) since its founding, the first dueling match (決鬪試合) since the revival order was held in the training hall. Starting with the university president, professors (敎援博士諸氏 - *Note: 敎援 might be a typo for 敎授*), and even the Chief Prosecutor (檢事正) attended. Alumni (同窓生) from various places, Iron Bar Corps (鐵棒隊), Storm Troopers (突擊隊), and many women gathered, and the enthusiastic (熱狂) atmosphere was greatly auspicious (*大瑞 - likely meant 大盛, 'very lively'*). The university students all stood up and praised (稱誦) Chancellor Hitler's decisive action (英斷). (Heidelberg Dispatch)

Number of Sŏdang
How many *sŏdang* (書堂), which are important educational institutions (敎育機關) second (次하야) to primary schools (普通學校), are there in total? <36>
Kyŏnggi Province (京畿道): 1,245 locations, 14,000 students

South P'yŏngan Province (平南道): 1,367 locations, 18,676 students
North P'yŏngan Province (平北道): 1,593 locations, 34,708 students (*Note: 300k seems high, likely a typo for 34,708*)

**Summary**

This article presents three unrelated news briefs: Bernard Shaw's observations contrasting "civilized" and "uncivilized" societies after a world tour and his bemusement at American reporters; the revival of dueling in Nazi Germany, celebrated at Heidelberg University as a means to foster national spirit; and detailed statistics on the number of traditional Korean village schools (*sŏdang*) and their student populations across the provinces of colonial Korea in 1933. The *sŏdang* data is particularly interesting for historians, offering a quantitative snapshot of the state of traditional education alongside the colonial primary school system (Pot'ong Hakkyo). It highlights the persistence and regional distribution of these indigenous institutions during Japanese rule, suggesting their continued importance in Korean society despite modernization efforts.

三千里壁新聞

世界漫遊感想
×
世界漫遊의 旅行을 마치고 日前에 英京「론돈」에도라온 너드, 쏘-翁은
「이번 旅行으로 비로소 分明히 안일을 文明國에사는 사람들이 不幸하고 煩悶이 만흔 反面에 非文明國에사는 사람들이 泰平하고 安樂하게 살고잇는 것을 보앗다」
라고하엿다.  米國의 印象에 對하여는 新聞記者 一百餘名이 호텔로 몰려와서
「쏘-氏여 大體 당신과 쐭스피어와 어느편이 더 훌융해요?」
하는 위의 白痴가튼 質問에 놀낫다고 말햇다.(론돈電)

決鬪復活
獨逸「하이델벨히」大學에서 擧行
힛틀러가 獨逸聯邦宰相의 印＜35＞綬를밧자, 맨몬저 實行한 新政策이 決鬪復活令이다. 「國民革命」의 實踐을 負擔할 靑年獨逸이 가장몬저 必要로 하는 것은 精力과 元氣다, 이것을 涵養하는데는 決鬪가 第一이라하야 獨逸 共和國以來 八個年間 禁止하여오든 決鬪의 復活令이 나린 것이다.
×
그래서 創立以來五百五十年의 녯傳統을 가지고잇든 하이델, 벨히大學에서는 復活令後 最初의 決鬪試合을 道場에서 擧行하엿는데 學長을 爲始하야 敎援博士諸氏, 그리고 檢事正지 參列하엿스며, 各地의 同窓生, 鐵棒隊, 突擊隊들과 만흔 女性들이 모아 그 熱狂하는 모양은 大瑞하엿다, 大學生들은 모다 이러서서 힛틀러 宰相의 英斷을 稱誦하엿다. (하이델, 벨히電)
