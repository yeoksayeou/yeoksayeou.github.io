University Student Temperament
Would you take a million dollars or perfect love?
Proposed by Professor William Marston of William College, USA

Dr. William Marston, a university professor, posed the question written above to American university students.
1. All the male students (truly, without a single exception) wrote "Money" as their answer.
2. Among the female students, 92 out of 100 wrote "perfect love," and although the remaining 8 wrote "Money," without exception, there was an added note to the effect of, "Because with that much money, one can certainly control love."

Flaws women see in men. Flaws men see in women.
Regarding this, it was revealed that male students look down on female students, and conversely, female students look down on male students.
And the male students listed 26 characteristics they consider to be the <38> merits of themselves, men, and acknowledged 8 things considered flaws or bad habits.
However, concerning the opposite sex, female students, they counted 29 flaws and acknowledged only twelve merits.

What about the female students? The female students found twenty-one flaws in the male students and acknowledged eleven merits, and regarding themselves, the female students, they listed fourteen merits and acknowledged 18 weaknesses.

Listing the important ones among them—
Merits of female students recognized by male students

1. Charm (Charmingness)
2. Being affable and gentle.
3. Being warm-hearted.
4. Unselfish (沒利我的 - Mollidaejŏk).
5. Mysterious (神祕的 - Sinbijŏk).
6. Alluring (蠱惑的 - Kohokchŏk).

Merits of male students seen by female students

1. Being adventurous.
2. Being diligent in work.
3. Not being startled by trivial matters (瑣事 - Sasa).
4. Superior physical strength.
5. Being democratic (테모그라직 한 것 - likely a transliteration of 'democratic').
6. Being sincere (誠意 - Sŏngŭi) and having strong endurance (耐久力 - Naeguryŏk), etc. <39>

Flaws of female students pointed out by male students

1. Materialism (拜金主義 - Paegŭmjuŭi)
2. Being emotional and lacking decisiveness.
3. Being liars.
4. Being less trustworthy.
5. Being childish.
6. Lacking tolerance, etc.

Flaws of male students acknowledged by female students

1. Being selfish.
2. Being arrogant/putting on airs (웃줄한다).
3. Being moody/temperamental.
4. Being cruel.
5. Being impudent/cocky.
6. Being simple-minded.
7. Being easily deceived/fooled, etc. <40>
<38-40>

**Summary**

This article reports on a survey by Dr. William Marston of William College (USA) asking American university students whether they would choose $1 million or perfect love. All male students chose money, while 92% of female students chose love (the other 8% chose money believing it could secure love). The article also details the perceived merits and flaws each gender identified in themselves and the opposite gender, revealing mutual disdain. Men valued female charm and gentleness but criticized their materialism and emotionality. Women admired male adventurousness and diligence but faulted their selfishness and arrogance. From a historical perspective, this piece is interesting as it reflects the transmission of Western social surveys and gender stereotypes into colonial Korea during the 1930s. It offers insights into how Koreans perceived American university students and potentially mirrored or contrasted these views with their own societal norms regarding love, money, and gender roles under Japanese rule.

大學生 氣質
百萬弗과 완전한 戀愛의 어느 쪽을 取하겟는가?
米國 윌리암 大學敎授 提出

대학교수 윌리암, 마-스톤 박사는 아메리까 대학생에게 우에 쓴 것과 가튼 질문을 發하엿더니.
1. 남학생들은 전부(實로 한 사람도 빼지 안코) 「金」이라 대답을 썻고.
2. 여학생은 100명중 92명까지는 「완전한 연애라고 썻스며 남어지 8명도 「金」이라고 쓰기는 하엿스나 한 사람 빼지 안코 「그 만큼 한 돈이 잇스면 연애는 확실히 지배할 수 잇스니까」하는 의미의 添書가 잇섯다.

여성이 본 남성의 결점. 남성이 본 여성의 결점
이에 대하야 남학생은 여학생을 경멸하고 반대를 여학생은 남학생을 경멸하고 잇는 사실이 드러낫다.
그리고 남학생은 자기네들 남성의 ＜38＞ 美點이라고 생각하는 特長 26點을 들고 결점 又는 惡習癖이라고 생각되는 것을 8개를 승인하엿슴니다.
그런데 상대방의 여학생에 대하여는 결점 29를 헤이엇고 美點은 열두 가지만 승인하엿슴니다.

여학생들은 엇더하엿는가. 여학생들은 남학생에게서 결점 스물 한 가지를 차저내엇고 美點은 열 한 가지를 승인하엿고 그리고 자기들 여학생 자신에 대하여 美點 열넷을 들고 약점 18을 승인하고 잇슴니다.

그중 중요한 것을 들건대―
남학생이 인정한 여학생의 美點

1. 참잉네스(사랑스러운 점)
2. 싹싹하고 부드러운 점.
3. 온정이 잇는 것.
4. 沒利我的.
5. 神祕的.
6. 蠱惑的.

여학생이 본 남학생의 美點

1. 모험적인 것.
2. 일에 부즈런한 것.
3. 瑣事에 놀나지 안는 것.
4. 육체력이 勝한 것.
5. 테모그라직 한 것.
6. 誠意하고 耐久力이 강한 것 등등. ＜39＞

3. 거짓말쟁이
4. 미듬성이 적은 것.
5. 어린애 갓다.
6. 관용성이 업는 것 등등.

4. 잔인하다.
