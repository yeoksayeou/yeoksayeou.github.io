Magazine Title: 삼천리 제7호
Publication Date: 1930년 07월 01일
Article Title: 한강직히자. (Let's Guard the Han River.)
Author: 파인 (P'ain)
Article Type: 시 (Poem)

1
Though showers fall from the blue sky
And lightning splits the rocks,
The Han River flows steadfastly on,
Like the spirit of this land's brave men.
Comrade, arise, let's guard the Han River!
That river is our blood and milk.

2
Those living corpses who know not how to weep
At injustice and corruption—bind them and throw them in that river!
Wash the bloodstained tip of the dirty sword,
And let us go forth together to the sea of joy.
Comrade, arise, let's guard the Han River!
That river is our blood and milk.

3
Flow on, flow on, O river of freedom!
As you have nourished these descendants for thousands of years,
Let us live together for myriad years to come.
Our spirit resides in you.
Comrade, arise, let's guard the Han River!
That river is our blood and milk.

**Summary**

This poem by P'ain uses the Han River as a powerful symbol of the enduring Korean spirit and national identity. Despite hardships (storms, lightning), the river flows persistently, representing resilience. The poet calls for purification, urging comrades to cast out the corrupt ("living corpses," "injustice") and cleanse the nation ("wash the bloodstained sword"), ultimately aiming for a future of freedom and joy associated with the river. The repeated refrain emphasizes the river's vital importance as the nation's lifeblood ("blood and milk") and serves as a rallying cry for its protection. For historians, the poem is a significant example of nationalist sentiment during the Japanese colonial period. It reflects a yearning for freedom, a call for collective action and purification, and the symbolic appropriation of a major geographical feature (the Han River) as the core of national identity and persistence against oppression, likely coded references to the colonial situation.

한강직히자.
						파인
1
푸르든 하늘에 소낙비오고
번개불 바위를 째어노아도
한강물 굿굿히 흘너나간다
이땅의 쾌남아 정신과갓치
동무야 이러나 한강직히자
저강은 우리의 피와젓일세

2
불의와 불정에 울줄모르는
산송장 묵거서 저강에너코
더러운 피무든 칼끗싯고서
깁붐의 바다로 함깨나가자
동무야 이러나 한강직히자
저강은 우리의 피와젓일세

3
흘너라 흘너라 자유의 강아
수천년 이자손 양하여주듯
압흐로 억만년 함께살자야
우리의 정신이 너에게잇다.
동무야 이러나 한강직히자
저강은 우리의 피와젓일세
