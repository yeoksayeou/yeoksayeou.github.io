Mental Test of a Notable Figure (Part 4)
						Yu Chint'ae, Director of the Chosŏn Education Association

Even setting aside political significance, in any country's society, whether it be China with Li Hongzhang (李鴻章) and Liang Qichao (梁啓超), or Japan with Ōkuma Shigenobu (大隈重信), there are always one or two individuals who hold the stature of elder statesmen. In Chosŏn too, after the passing of Wŏllam Yi Sangjae (月南 李商在翁), without anyone needing to recommend him, Mr. Yu Chint'ae (兪鎭泰氏) naturally came to occupy the position of an elder. To that extent, Mr. Yu possesses hidden contributions and virtues recognized by himself and others, receiving the respect of all people. Now, on a certain autumn day in September, amidst the yellow chrysanthemums and crimson maples (黃菊丹楓), I sought out the elder—renowned throughout the world for his integrity (淸廉) and uprightness (剛直)—at the Education Association (敎育協會) building in Sup'yogyo (水標橋) and attempted a test of his mind.

"What do you think our Chosŏn's population will be in 10 years?"
"It will increase significantly. Not just the population. Industry, education, everything will increase significantly. It will grow, it will grow, why wouldn't it grow... That time will truly be good.."
"Then you are very optimistic about the future."
"Of course! One lives because of optimism! Even in these circumstances, one must live on."
A gleam of some suggestion flows in the depths of his piercing eyes (炯炯한 그 眼底). Turning the question again,
"Sir, until a few years ago, you cultivated talent by sending many students to study abroad (留學), but why have you not put much effort into that area in recent years?"
"The wealthy don't give money anymore. Even if I recommend someone, those with money don't listen."
And like a lecture from the old doctor, he calmly explained (說示) the prevailing trends of thought among the wealthy in society these days. His words were apt. I turned the question a third time,
"Do you have any desire to travel around the world?"
"You need money to go. They say it costs at least twenty or thirty thousand won (數三萬圓)."
"If you had the money, would you leave right away?"
"Even so, perhaps when I'm a bit older!"
'When I'm a bit older!' The elder's spirit (氣骨) was vividly apparent. Seeking practical achievements from the elder is unreasonable. Rather, one should seek his presence in correcting the adverse current (庰流) of today's society, which rushes headlong (滔滔狂奔) seeking virtue (德) and righteousness (義)! Today’s score: 9 points, Excellent.<18>

**Summary**

This 1929 article interviews Yu Chint'ae, identified as a respected elder statesman of Korean society following the death of Yi Sangjae. The interview, framed as a "mental test," reveals Yu's strong optimism about Korea's future growth in population, industry, and education despite the current difficulties under colonial rule. He laments, however, that the wealthy elite are no longer willing to fund initiatives like sending students abroad, indicating a shift in social attitudes or priorities among the affluent. The author concludes by emphasizing Yu's importance not for practical achievements, but for his moral standing and integrity as a corrective influence in society. For historians, the article offers insight into the recognition of certain figures as national elders during the colonial period, the persistent hope for national development among intellectuals, and contemporary commentary on the perceived changing social responsibility of the Korean economic elite in the late 1920s.

名士의 멘탈테스트(其四)
						朝鮮敎育協會 理事長 兪鎭泰氏
政治的 意味를 떠날지라도 中國에 李鴻章, 梁啓超, 日本에 大隈重信 等 엇든 나라의 社會든지 그 社會에는 반드시 元老格 되는 분이 한 두분은 게시는 법이다. 朝鮮도 月南 李商在翁이 떠난 뒤 누가 薦하는 사람이 업시 저절로 兪鎭泰氏가 元老格에 안저 게시다 그토록 氏는 自他가 共認하는 숨은 功勞와 德望이 만허서 모든 사람의 敬待를 밧고 잇다. 이제 黃菊丹楓의 秋 9月 어느날 淸簾과 剛直으로 一世를 울니는 翁을 水標橋의 敎育協會 會舘으로 찻고 頭腦試驗을 試하여 보다.
「10년 후에 우리 조선의 인구가 얼마나 될 것 갓슴니까」
「훨신 늘지 人口뿐일가. 産業도 敎育도 무에든지 훨신 늘지. 늘지 늘어 웨 안늘어… 그 때는 정말 조흐리..」
「그러면 압날을 대단히 樂觀하심니다 그려」
「암! 樂觀하기에 살지! 이 속에서도 긔어히 살어가지」
炯炯한 그 眼底에는 무슨 暗示의 빗이 흐른다. 質問을 再轉하여
「선생께서는 몃 해전까지는 人材를 海外에 만히 留學식혀 養成하시드니 近年에는 엇재서 그 方面에 別로 힘을 아니 쓰심니까」
「부자들이 이제는 돈을 안내는구려. 내가 누구를 薦하여도 돈 가진 이들이 말을 듯지 안는구려」
하며 老博士의 講演가치 近日 돈 가진 이의 社會에 흐르는 思潮를 순순히 說示하신다. 適切한 말슴이다. 나는 다시 質問을 三轉하여
「世界一週하고 십흔 생각이 업스심니까」
「돈이 잇서야 가지 .적어도 數三萬圓은 든다는데」
「돈만 잇스면 곳 떠나시겟서요?」
「그러터래도 좀 더 늙거든!」
좀 더 늙거든! 하는데 翁의 氣骨이 躍如하다. 그러고 翁에게서 事業上의 功績을 求하는 것은 無理다. 그보다도 德과 義를 차저 滔滔狂奔하는 지곰 社會의 庰流를 바로 갑는데 翁의 存在를 자저야 할 것이다! 今日採点 九点으로 優等<18>
