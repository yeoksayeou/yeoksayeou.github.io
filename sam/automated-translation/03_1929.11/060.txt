Anecdotes

Speech

In his prime, when Mr. Ch'undo Pak Ilbyŏng (春濤 朴一秉), renowned alongside figures like Kim Myŏngsik, Chang Tŏksu, and Kim Ch'angje as one of the great orators of the era, was once giving a speech, employing his unique voice and gestures (제스치아) energetically, a certain high-ranking Japanese police official watching nearby couldn't help but admire him, saying, "If we had him give speeches for raising national bonds during wartime, it seems like it would be a splendid success."

Female Students' Luxury

While the extravagance of American female students is famous, a certain female student at a New York state girls' high school reportedly wore one hundred twenty pairs of socks in a year, all of them expensive silk socks, the value amounting to 410 dollars, which is 820 won in our money. If there were just ten young ladies like that, the country would surely go bankrupt.

Theater in a Train

On the train between London, the capital of England, and Glasgow, they recently established a theater inside and allowed general passengers to view performances. As the results were very good, they say that in the future, they will install theaters on trains throughout all of England.<7>

**Summary**

This article presents three brief, unrelated anecdotes: one praising the oratorical skill of Pak Ilbyŏng, even admired by a Japanese official; another criticizing the perceived excessive luxury of an American female student buying expensive socks; and a third noting the novel introduction of theaters on trains in England. From a historical perspective, the anecdotes offer glimpses into colonial Korean society's interests and anxieties. The admiration for a Korean orator by a Japanese official is notable, highlighting individual recognition despite the colonial power dynamic. The piece on American student luxury reflects concerns about Western influence and extravagance, possibly contrasting with perceived Korean frugality or economic hardship. The mention of train theaters in England shows an awareness of and interest in modern Western developments and conveniences.

揷話
演說

한참 당년은 金明植, 張德秀, 金昶濟 氏등과 가치 一世의 雄辯客으로 일홈을 날니든 春濤 朴一秉 氏가 어느 때에 氏 獨特의 음성과 「제스치아」를 盛히 써가며 연설하고 잇슬 때에 겻헤서 그것을 바라보고 잇든 경찰측의 모 일인 고관이
「戰時가튼 때에 國債募集하는 연설을 식혓스면 훌융히 성공할 것 갓다」
하고 탄복하기를 마지 안엇다고.

女學生의 奢侈

미국 녀학생들의 사치는 유명한 것이지만 「뉴-욕」 주립고등녀학교의 엇든 녀학생은 일년에 양말 일백 스무 커레를 신엇는데 그것이 모다 갑가는 비단양말로 그 갑이 410불 죽 우리 돈으로 820원 어치를 신엇다 하니 그런 색시 열만 잇다가는 나라가 망할 일이야.

汽車內에 劇場

영국 서울 「론돈」과 「구라스고-」간의 긔차속에는 이번에 재로 연극장을 설하고 일반 승객에게 관람케 하엿는데 그 성적이 매우 조흠으로 장차는 영국 전톄의 긔차에 극장설치를 하리라고.<7>
