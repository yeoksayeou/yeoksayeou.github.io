Mental Test of a Notable Figure (Part 4)
						Sin'ganhoe Central Committee Chairman, Mr. Hŏ Hŏn

President of Posŏng Chŏnmun Hakkyo (Posong College), President of the Chosŏn Pyŏnhosa Hyŏphoe (Korean Bar Association), Central Committee Chairman of the Sin'ganhoe (New Stem Association)! Thus, his footprints have been immense, involving the 48 Persons Appeal Dismissal Case, attendance at the Peijing (北京) International Lawyers Conference, and participation in the Belgium (白耳義) National Congress. Moreover, the stones he cast on each occasion have created exceedingly large ripples throughout the world and Chosŏn (Korea). Also, the stage upon which he will leap forward is extremely wide and fertile, like the Namuri plains in Chaeryŏng (載寧). Therefore, his every move and action will surely become the focus of ten thousand watchful eyes. However, let us wish, as with all pioneers, not to seek accomplishments from him too hastily, but rather to first feel the greatness in the vast inception of his work, and let it end there! In any case, upon meeting him, a warm breeze blows from his demeanor, and one comes to know he is somehow a magnanimous person of virtue and a gentleman of spirit, evoking feelings of affection and respect as if people "flock to him upon seeing his banner" (望風而集). Catching him for a moment while he is busy with the Sin'gan Movement:

"In which area should we exert the most effort?"

"To exert effort means striving to survive; we must exert effort evenly in all aspects of society, plan, pioneer, and be active. If the whole prospers, the parts also prosper, but it is also true that if the parts prosper, the whole also prospers..."

As a lawyer of 10 years, his answer was clear and logical, leaving no room for further questions. Again,

"Then, is it a good thing that Koreans keep flowing overseas, and is it something that cannot be stopped?"

"Well, it would be good if they lived well even if they go (excerpt - original text)... As things stand now, it seems stopping them from going would be impossible."

And his political views, seen from a broad perspective, flow滔滔 (abundantly). Lacking the freedom to publish his valuable words on this page, I quickly turned the topic to humor (笑話) to conclude.

"When you went to Europe (歐羅巴) this time, did you happen to get close to any Western beauties?"

"Get close?"

"Sexually."

"Good heavens, absolutely not!"

And a smile like a young bride's flowed across his once stern face. He still possesses a childlike innocence (童心). Lastly, we hope that within his great physique, like Mount T'ae (泰山不動 - immovable as Mt. Tai), there is much courage and wisdom, so that he may not be shameful as our leader (O首). Today's score: 8 points, a pass (及第). <14>

**Summary**

This article is a brief interview with Hŏ Hŏn, a prominent Korean nationalist figure holding multiple leadership positions in 1929, including Chairman of the Sin'ganhoe. The interviewer highlights Hŏ's significant past activities and future potential, praising his character. Hŏ emphasizes the need for comprehensive societal effort for national survival and comments on the unstoppable trend of Korean emigration, wishing emigrants well. The interview concludes with a lighthearted question about encounters with Western women during his trip to Europe, revealing Hŏ's reserved nature, and assigns him a "passing grade" of 8 points in this "mental test."

For historians, the article offers a glimpse into the public persona and thoughts of a key figure in the Korean nationalist movement during the colonial period. Hŏ Hŏn's cautious optimism, his views on unified national effort, and his acknowledgment of emigration as an unavoidable reality reflect the complex challenges faced by Koreans under Japanese rule. The slightly playful "mental test" format and the deference shown towards Hŏ provide insights into contemporary journalistic styles and the public perception of nationalist leaders.

名士의 멘탈테스트(其四)
						新幹會 中央委員長 許憲氏
普成專門學校長, 朝鮮辨護士協會會長, 新幹會中央委員長! 그래서 48人控訴不受理事件, 北京國際辨護士大會出席, 白耳義民族會議叅席 등 氏가 거러온 발자취도 거대하엿거니와 기회마다 던지엇든 돌도 심히 큰 파문을 세계와 朝鮮에 이르키고 잇섯다. 또 압흐로 비약할 무대도 載寧나무리벌가치 극히 넓고 沃土로 되어 잇슨 즉 氏의 일거수일투족은 萬目注視의 초점이 될 터이라. 다만 우러는 모든 先驅者에서와 가치 氏에게서 功績을 구하기를 너무 급히 하지 말고 그 사업의 巨創한데서 爲先 偉大를 늣겨두는 것으로 끗치자! 엇잿든 대하여 보면 풍모에 땃듯한 바람이 불고 어대인지 寬厚하고 德의 人, 氣槪의 士임을 알게 하여 「望風而集」的 親愛의 情과 尊敬하는 念을 이르키게 한다. 新幹運動으로 분주하시다는 것을 잡간 붓잡고
「우리들은 엇더한 방면에 가장 힘을 써야 하겟슴니까?」
「힘쓴다는 것은 생존하자는 노력인데 사회 각 방면의 모든 것에 골고로 힘쓰고 계획하고 개척하고 활약하어야 하겟지요. 전체가 잘 되면 부분도 잘 되는 것이나 부분이 잘 되어도 또 전체가 잘 되는 것도 진리니까...」
변호사 10년인 그의 대답이 明浙하엿슴과 條理잇슴은 더 무를 여지 업는 일이리라. 다시
「그러면 조선사람이 해외로 작고 흘너 나가는 것은 조흔 일임니까, 또 막을 수는 업는 일임니까」
「글세 나가더래도 잘 살면 조켓는데(畧-원문) 지금 갓하여는 가는 것을 막을 수도 업겟지요」
하며 大局으로부터 보시는 정치적 견해가 滔滔히 호른다. 이 지면에 그 귀한 말슴을 계재할 自由가 업서서 얼는 화제를 笑話에 돌니어 끗을 맷기로 하다.
「이번 歐羅巴 갓섯슬 때 혹 서양미인들과 갓가히 지나섯서요」
「지내다니?」
「性的으로요」
「원 천만에!」
하며 근엄하든 그 얼골에 숫삭시가튼 미소가 흐른다. 아직 童心을 가지섯다. 끗흐로 泰山不動가튼 그 거구에 만흔 용기와 지략이 잇서 우리의 O首됨에 붓그럽지 말기를 바라며... 今日採点 八点으로 及第. <14>
