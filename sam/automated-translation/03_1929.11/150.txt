Eight Scenic Views of the Peninsula
						Kim Tongin (金東仁)

Through the project of the Samch'ŏlli Company (三千里社), Kŭmgangsan (金剛山), Taedonggang (大同江), Puyŏ (扶餘), Kyŏngju (慶州), Myŏngsa Simni (明沙十里), Haeundae (海雲台), Ch'oksŏngnu (矗石樓), and Paektusan (白頭山), these eight places were selected as the Eight Scenic Views of the Peninsula (半島八景).

Among them, a natural yet curious phenomenon is that there was not a single person who did not recommend Kŭmgangsan. That is how much Kŭmgangsan is our source of pride and an outstanding landscape beyond compare. However, when reflecting again on how Kŭmgangsan differs from all other sceneries, one cannot help but tilt one's head. Mountains are all sorts of mountains, waters are all sorts of waters; several unusual rocks (奇岩) and a few clear streams (淸溪) are merely the elements (要素) constituting the so-called superb view (絶景) of Kŭmgangsan, and Kŭmgangsan simply has these elements layered upon each other. In other words, a scenic spot (勝景) is not just about having unusual rocks and clear streams, but about them being large and numerous. I have no dissatisfaction whatsoever regarding Kŭmgangsan being chosen as the first place.

However, I cannot help but wonder about the second place, Taedonggang. Anyone who has traveled even a little in the mountains and fields will discover places everywhere that far surpass the scenery of Taedonggang and the Moranbong (牧丹峰) area. It is likely just because the Taedonggang area includes the city called P'yŏngyang (平壤) and thus transportation is convenient, that it caught the eyes of many. I believe there must be many places that, despite having scenery far surpassing Taedonggang and the Moranbong area, remain merely napping spots for nearby farmers (農夫) simply because they haven't caught the eyes of many.<22>

That Paektusan barely received 8 votes, becoming the 8th place, must be for the same reason. That Myohyangsan (妙香山) and Tadohae (多島海) were eliminated with only 2 votes each must be because the selectors' eyes did not reach them. It is also unsatisfactory that Changsu-san (長壽山), also a place that should not have failed to be chosen, received only 3 votes.

Places like Puyŏ, Kyŏngju, and Ch'oksŏngnu are ordinary landscapes (平凡한 경치) with no particular wonders (奇) if you remove the concept (觀念) of their historical background (歷史的 背景) and man-made structures (人工).<24>

**Summary**

Kim Tongin critiques the popular selection of the "Eight Scenic Views of the Peninsula" organized by the Samch'ŏlli Company. While agreeing with Kŭmgangsan's top position due to its sheer scale of natural beauty, he questions the high ranking of Taedonggang, suggesting its popularity stems from proximity to P'yŏngyang and accessibility rather than superior scenery compared to lesser-known places. He laments the low ranking or omission of Paektusan, Myohyangsan, Tadohae, and Changsu-san, attributing it to their remoteness. He further argues that Puyŏ, Kyŏngju, and Ch'oksŏngnu derive their fame primarily from historical significance and man-made elements, lacking inherent natural wonder. From a historical perspective, the article is interesting as it reflects contemporary discourse on national landscapes during the colonial period, highlighting the tension between natural beauty, historical importance, and the practical influence of accessibility (transportation infrastructure) on public perception and the construction of national symbols. It also offers insight into Kim Tongin's aesthetic criteria and potentially subtle nationalist sentiments in valuing certain types of Korean landscapes.

半島八景
						金東仁
三千里社의 事業으로 金剛山, 大同江, 扶餘, 慶州, 明沙十里, 海雲台, 矗石樓, 白頭山, 이러케 여덟 곳이 半島八景으로 뽑히엇슴니다.
그런데 그 가운데 當然하고도 奇異반 현상으로는 金剛山을 推選치 안은 이는 하나도 업는 것이외다. 그만치 金剛山은 우리의 자랑꺼리이며 出衆한 경개에 다름 업슴니다. 그러나 다시 도리켜 모든 다른 경치들과 金剛山이 어듸가 엇더케 다른냐 할 때에는 머리를 기우려 보지 안을 수 업슴니다. 山이면 온갓 山 물이면 온갓 물에 몃 개의 奇岩과 몃줄기의 淸溪는 絶景이라는 金剛山을 搆成한 要素로서 다만 金剛山은 그 要素가 겹겹이 싸혀잇는 것 뿐이겟슴니다. 다시 말하자면 勝景이라는 것은 奇岩과 淸溪가 잇는 것이 아니고 크고 만흔 것이외다. 金剛山이 第1位로 當選된데 대하여는 아모 不滿이 업슴니다.
그러나 第2位의 大同江은 좀 엇던가 하지 안을 수 업슴니다. 山野를 조금이라도 旅行하여본 이는 大同江과 牧丹峰 一帶의 경치보다 百勝한 곳을 到處에서 發見하겟슴니다. 다만 大同江 一帶는 平壤이라 하는 都會를 끼고 따라서 交通이 便함으로 만흔 눈에 뛰인 까닭이겟슴니다. 만흔 눈에 띄이지 못한 까닭에 大同江과 모란봉 一帶보다 百勝한 경치를 가지고도<22> 다만 근방의 農夫의 낫잠터가 되여잇는 곳이 만흘 줄 아나니다.
白頭山이 겨우 8票로 第8位가 된 것도 그와가튼 리유-겟지오. 妙香山과 多島海가 겨우 2票로 落選된 것도 選者의 눈이 미처 가지 못한 까닭이겟슴니다. 長壽山도 또한 뽑지 못할 곳이 3票밧게는 엇지 못한 것도 不滿이외다.
扶餘, 慶州, 矗石樓 等地는 歷史的 背景의 觀念과 人工을 제하면은 아모러한 奇도 업는 平凡한 경치외다.<24>
