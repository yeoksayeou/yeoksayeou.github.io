Article Title: 編輯後記 (Editor's Postscript)
Author: 파인 (Pa'in)
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editor's Postscript)

We are proud that the previous 'Chosŏn Pioneers' (朝鮮先駈者) issue was indeed sold amidst much high praise.

We had intended for this issue too to come out into the world with very substantial content, but as those in the know may know, we lament that due to various unavoidable circumstances, it has come out in this state.

Indeed, we are only ashamed that we could not even put together one-third of what we had planned.

Above all, we must apologize for not being able to put out the October issue.

You may call it cowardly if we blame this failure on surrounding circumstances, but truly, in magazine management, the three 'impossibles'—unavoidable (不可抗), inescapable (不可避), and impossible (不可能)—clearly lay before us, and thus we were forced to suspend publication.

However, we believe these reasons will mostly disappear starting from the next issue, so please understand.

From next month's issue, we will definitely publish regularly on the first of each month, so please understand widely.
(Pa'in)<40>

**Summary**

The editor apologizes for failing to publish the October 1929 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* and for the diminished content of the current (November) issue. This failure is attributed to vague but severe "circumstances," described as the "three impossibles" (unavoidable, inescapable, impossible), likely referencing external pressures such as colonial censorship or financial difficulties. The editor assures readers that these problems are expected to lessen and promises a return to regular monthly publication starting with the December issue. This postscript reveals the precarious environment for Korean publishers during the colonial era, hinting at the constant struggle against external controls and resource limitations. Historians might find the coded language used to describe the difficulties particularly interesting, reflecting the constraints editors operated under.

編輯後記
요전번 朝鮮先駈者 號는 실로 만흔 激讚아래에서 發賣되엇슴을 자랑으로 아옵니다. 이번 號도 매우 充實한 內容을 가지고 世上에 나오려 하엿삽든 바 아는 분이나 아실는 지 마지못할 여러 가지 事情으로 이 모양이 되어 나가게 되엇슴을 슬퍼함니다. 실로 豫期하든 三分의 一도 꾸미지 못하여 붓그러울 뿐이외다.

무엇보담 謝罪하올 것은 10월호를 못나인 것이외다. 이 잘못을 周圍의 事情에 민다하면 卑㥘하다 하실는 지 모르지만 실로 잡지경영에는 불가항 불가피 不可能의 세 「不可가 뚜렷하게 가르노여 不得已 休刊한 터이외다. 그러나 이 理由도 來號부터는 대부분 消滅될 줄 아오니 諒解하여 주압소서.

來月號부터는 定期로 每月 一日에 꼭 發刊하겟사오니 널니 諒解하시압.(파인)<40>
