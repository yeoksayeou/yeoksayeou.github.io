More Fruit than Flowers
More Fruit than Flowers

In front of a beautiful woman's nude painting lined up at an exhibition hall, a certain gentleman stopped and asked the painter:
"Excuse me (실례올시다만은), but did you paint this nude (라체화)?"
"Yes."
"How much would you sell it for?"
"It is 500 won."
"Then, I will give you just 100 won. Please sell the painting to someone else, and just give me the address of the model woman."

The Pig Wearing a Hat
Guest: "Hey boy, where is your father?"
Seven-year-old child: "He's fixing the pig pen right now. There are two in the pen; one is a pig, and one is father. The one wearing a hat is father."

The Eternal Question
A young daughter asked her father:
"Father, where were you born?"
"In Taegu (大邱)!"
"Then mother?"
"In P'yŏngyang (平壤)."
"And me?"
"You were born here in Sŏul."
"Heh, that's funny. How come the three of us live together like this?"<52>

Even Better
Mother: "Hey, Ok-hŭi (玉姬)ya, if you marry that man, I will never cross the threshold of your house as long as I live."
Ok-hŭi: "Oh, mother, if you do that, Kyŏng-sik-ssi (京植氏) and I will like it even more."

Two Roles in One Body (一身兩役)
Visitor: "Doctor (醫師)! You said that if I bring one patient (患者) each to this hospital, you would treat my illness for free as a reward, didn't you?"
Doctor: "That's right."
"Well then, it worked out! I am the patient (患者)."

A Dull Marriage
An 18-year-old maiden, leaning her forehead on the man's shoulder most happily:
"Hey, Ch'un-myŏn-ssi (春眠氏)! How did you come to decide to marry me?"
"Well, because my mother at home said I had to..."

An Escape Route
To a young man fishing for goldfish by a pond:
"Are you catching any?"
"No luck today, but yesterday I caught over 50 right here."
"What? Do you know who I am to be saying such things?"
"I don't know."
"I'm the owner of this pond. What are you planning to do now? Coming to someone else's pond and catching all the fish."
"Old Master Owner, then do you know who I am?"
"You're asking me who you are?!"
"Hmph, I'm famous even in Sŏul for being a good liar. Yesterday's story was all a lie too."

The Merit of Flaws (缺點의 德)
Groom (新郞): "Now that we are married, shall I point out a few of your flaws (흉점)?"
Bride (新婦): "Stop it. I know very well that I have flaws (欠點). It's because I knew that I came to you; otherwise, I would have married into a better place."<53>

**Summary**

This article from the May 1931 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine presents a collection of eight short, humorous anecdotes or jokes. They cover various themes including pragmatic (or lewd) views on art, comical misunderstandings in family life, cynical takes on marriage proposals and relationships, clever wordplay to get out of trouble, and a self-aware acceptance of personal flaws in marriage. The humor often relies on twists, misunderstandings, or bluntness, reflecting everyday social interactions and attitudes of the time. For historians, this collection offers glimpses into social norms, humor styles, and perhaps underlying societal views on topics like marriage, family dynamics, and authority in colonial Korea during the early 1930s. The simple, relatable scenarios provide insight into common people's perspectives and the transition of certain social values.

「얼마면 파시겟서요.」

「그러면 100원만 드릴터이니 그림은 다른 분에게 파시고 모델 여자의 주소만 가르켜 주시지요.」

모자 쓴 도야지
손님 「얘야 아버지는 어듸게시냐?」
일곱 살 먹은 아히 「지금 도야지 울안을 고처요. 울안에 둘이 잇는데 하나는 도야지고 하나는 아버지얘요. 모자쓴 이가 아버지얘요.」

千古의 疑問
어린 딸이 아버지더러
「아버지는 어듸서 낫서요.」
「大邱에서!」
「그럼 어머니는?」
「平壤서.」
「나는요.」
「너는 서울서 낫지.」
「에그 우수워라. 그런데 엇더케 우리 셋이 이러케 갓치 사러요.」＜52＞

차라 더 조와
어머니 「얘야, 玉姬야 네가 그 사내허고 결혼하면 나는 생전 네 집 문특을 안넘을란다.」
玉姬 「에그, 어머니두 그러시면 京植氏와 저는 더 조와하게요.」

一身兩役
訪問者 「의사선생님! 이 병원에 환자 한 사람씩 대려오면 사례로 병을 거저 보와 주신다고 하섯지요.」
醫師 「그럿소.」
「올치 되엿군, 제가 患者올시다.」

슨거운 結婚
18세 처녀가 사내의 억개에 이마를 다이고 가장 행복스럽게도
「이봐요, 春眠氏! 엇더케 되어서 당신은 저와 결혼하시려고 결심하섯서요.」
「그거슨 우리 집 어머니가 가야한다니까...」

도망질 칠 구멍
연못가에서 금붕어를 낙는 청년더러
「좀 잡히나뇨?」
「오늘은 틀녓서요, 그러나 어적게는 50여마리를 여기서 잡엇는 걸요.」
「무에 내가 누군지 알고 그런말 해.」
「나는 몰느지요.」
「이 연못 주인이야. 장차 엇더케 할 셈이야. 남의 연못에 와서 고기를 쨍그리 잡아 버렷스니.」
「주인령감, 그럼 나는 누군지 아시오.」
「네알을 날더러 뭇는담!」
「흥 나는 거짓말 잘 하기로 서울서도 유명하다두. 어적게 이약이도 다 거짓말이야.」

缺點의 德
新郞 「우리 둘이 이제는 결혼햇스니 어듸 당신 흉점을 몃가지 말해볼가요.」
新婦 「그만 두어요. 내게 欠點잇는 줄 내가 잘 알어요. 알엇기에 당신한테로 왓지 그러찬으면 더 조혼 데로 시집가게요.」＜53＞
