How Did Chaplin Rise to Fame?

Speaking of Ch'yap'uring (Chaplin), who is currently traveling around Rondon (London) and P'ari (Paris) drawing extraordinary popularity, he was not famous from the beginning.

One day, the American (美國) entertainment manager (興行經營者) "Senet'ŭ" saw a comedian (喜劇俳優) acting (演劇) the part of a drunkard, completely intoxicated and staggering, on the stage (劇場舞臺) of a small theater (劇場) outside New York (紐育) City. He made a contract (契約) with that actor (俳優) for 125 dollars (弗) per week (一週). That actor was Ch'yap'uring.

A few days later, Ch'yap'uring, visiting Senet'ŭ's office, picked up large shoes, a worn-out bowler hat ("中山帽"), and a short stick (스텍) that were in a corner of the room and tried moving around (擧動) a bit. At this, the manager Senet'ŭ applauded loudly (박장하엿다), saying "Well done! Well done!"

Ch'yap'uring's fate (運命) began to open up from the sound of this "loud applause" (박장).<8>

**Summary**

This short article recounts the story of Charlie Chaplin's discovery by American film producer Mack Sennett. It highlights that Chaplin wasn't initially famous but was found performing as a drunkard in a small theater outside New York. Sennett hired him, and Chaplin later created his iconic Tramp persona using props found in Sennett's office, leading Sennett to applaud enthusiastically, marking the beginning of Chaplin's rise to fame. For historians of colonial Korea, the article demonstrates the reach of global popular culture, specifically Hollywood cinema, into Korea during the 1930s. It shows how international celebrities like Chaplin were presented to a Korean audience, reflecting cultural interests and transnational information flow during the colonial period, noticeable also in the contemporary Korean spellings and loanword usage.

챠푸링은 엇더케 出世하엿나
론돈, 파리를 도라다니며 지금 비상한 인긔를 끄을고 잇는 챠푸링으로 말하여도 처음부터 유명하엿든 것은 아니다.
미국 興行經營者 「세넷트」가 엇든 날 紐育 市外의 조고마한 劇場舞臺 우에서 잔뜩 취하여 곤드레맨드레된 주정꾼질을 演劇하는 喜劇俳優 한아를 보앗다. 그는 一週에 125弗을 주고 그 俳優와 契約하엿다. 그 俳優가 챠푸링이엇다.
몃칠 뒤 「세녯트」 사무소를 방문한 챠푸링은 방 한쪽 구석에 잇는 큰 구두 낡어 빠진 「中山帽」에 짤막한 스텍을 집고 조곰 擧動을 하여 보앗다. 여기에 經營者 「세녯트」는 잘한다 잘한다! 하고 박장하엿다.
챠푸링의 운명은 이 「박장」 소리에서부터 트기 시작하엿다.＜8＞
