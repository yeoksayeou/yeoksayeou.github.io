社告 (Company Notice)

식민지 자치운동에 대한 左의 4편 논문은 부득이 못 실게 되엇슴니다.
1. 인도 민족운동과 國民會議派 ---- 金璟載
2. 무산계급과 의회운동 ---------- 徐相日
3. 자치운동비판 ---------------- 柳海松
4. 애란 민족운동과 신펜당 ------- 金東煥 ＜5＞

We regret that we were unavoidably unable to publish the following four articles concerning the colonial autonomy movement:
1. The Indian National Movement and the National Congress Party ---- Kim Kyŏngjae
2. The Proletariat and the Parliamentary Movement ---------- Sŏ Sangil
3. A Critique of the Autonomy Movement ---------------- Yu Haesong
4. The Irish National Movement and Sinn Féin ------- Kim Tonghwan <5>

**Summary**

This editorial notice from the March 1933 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine announces the unavoidable cancellation of four planned articles dealing with various aspects of colonial autonomy movements. The specific articles listed cover the Indian National Congress, the proletariat's relationship with parliamentary movements, a critique of autonomy movements in general, and the Irish Sinn Féin movement. This notice is historically significant as it clearly indicates the pressures, likely censorship by the Japanese Government-General or self-censorship by the magazine, faced by Korean publications attempting to discuss politically sensitive topics like nationalism, anti-colonial movements, and self-governance during the colonial period. The specific cancellation of articles comparing the Korean situation to movements in India and Ireland, as well as Marxist perspectives, highlights the restricted intellectual and political discourse allowed at the time.
