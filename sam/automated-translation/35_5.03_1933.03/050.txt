Self-Portrait of a Beautiful Woman, Bright-Spirited!
						Ch'oe Ŭisun

The request to draw myself!
I hesitated quite a bit.
Fundamentally, it's because I still lack any sharp points or prominent features, and there's nothing particularly noteworthy I can pinpoint and present.

After rowing diligently for ten-odd years in fair winds and ten-odd years in storms, perhaps today my formerly just-round shape has become pointed like an egg (게란). However, the habit of looking at the hidden heart behind others' faults (惡) to share their tears, and worrying if my shortcomings might cause excessive pain to others, seems to remain ingrained in my very core (골세).

Naturally having little greed and no ambition, and thus with self-importance bowing its head, my disposition (心氣) is always on the bright side. Therefore, I am someone who finds enjoyment in all matters. It's just that my senses are somewhat keen and my sensitivity (感受性) is strong, making my emotional reactions quick. But like waves rushing in, hitting a rock, and then receding after one surge, a strangely strong reason (리지) always pushes back at my shoulders, bringing order.

My heart always yearns for the active and positive side, yet it possesses a nature that cannot hate the opposite. Therefore, in all things, I often cannot make quick judgments and may fail to be a vanguard spearheading the charge, but once I step forward, I seem certainly qualified as a member of a do-or-die squad (결사대원) who doesn't stop until the very end.

It is a fact that this has been the sole mast (돗대) guiding my past life. <3>

**Summary**

The author, Ch'oe Ŭisun, reflects on her personality in this self-portrait requested by the magazine. She describes herself as lacking outstanding features but having developed a 'point' over time through life's experiences. Key traits include empathy, a lack of greed or ambition leading to a generally cheerful disposition, high sensitivity balanced by strong reason, and a nature that, while not suited for leading charges, possesses unwavering determination once committed. This quiet resolve has been her guiding principle. From a historical perspective, the article offers a glimpse into the self-perception of an educated Korean woman during the colonial era. Her emphasis on inner qualities like empathy, rationality, and steadfastness, rather than outward ambition, might reflect both personal inclination and the societal constraints faced by women (and Koreans generally) at the time. The term "do-or-die squad member" (결사대원) used metaphorically for commitment is particularly striking.

麗人自畵像, 心氣明朗!
						崔義順
나를 그리라는 청!
퍽 주저되엿다.
도대체 나에게는 아직 뽀죽한 곳이나 두두러진 곳이 업시 이러타하고 신통하게 꼬집어 내세울 거리가 업기 때문이다.
순풍에 10여 년 폭풍에 10여 년 꿉벅꿉벅 노질해 온 오늘날 나는 전일의 동글기만 하든 모양이 마치 게란가치 뽀죽해젓다고 할지. 그러나 남의 악(惡)을 보면 그 뒤에 숨은 심경을 살피어 눈물을 난우고 내게 부족이 미치드라도 남에게 쓰라림이 과할가 염려하는 습관만은 그대로 내 골세 백인 채 잇는가 한다.
본래 욕심이 적고 야심이 업스며 따라서 자만심이 머리를 숙이고 잇스니 항상 심기가 명랑한 편이다. 그럼으로 범사에 재미를 잘 부치는 사람이다. 단지 좀 감각이 예민하고 감수성(感受性)이 강하야 감정 작용이 빠르나 밀려드러 오든 물결이 돌에 부듸처 한고비 물은 물려 나가 버리듯이 이상하게 세찬 리지가 항상 억개를 밀어내어 정돈을 시키어 준다.
나의 맘은 항상 능동적이고 적극적인 편을 동경하고 잇스면서 그와 반대편을 미워하지 못하는 성질이 따른다. 그럼으로 모든 일에 속단을 못하야 압잡이 돌격대원은 못될 때가 만흐나 한번 나선 거름이면 최후까지 멈추지 안는 결사대원으로서의 자격은 확실한 듯 하다.
이것이 나의 과거 생활을 지배한 유일한 돗대 이엇슴은 사실이다. ＜3＞
