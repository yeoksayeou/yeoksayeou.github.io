A Beautiful Woman's Self-Portrait, Though Lacking Angular Beauty
						Song Kŭmsŏn

Russian refugee! That is the dishonorable gift of a name people observed when I wore Western clothes. Also, some people call me the mistress of the house (대방마님)! This, needless to say, is the glorious name given to me when I wear Korean clothes (조선옷). In any case, perhaps because my ears are big, or perhaps because I like people (or maybe because I'm foolish), I listen well to what others say. Hearing such names, perhaps because of that, even when I think about it, it doesn't seem like a comparison to a complete dung pot (똥단지). No, it really seems quite plausible! Whichever way you look, not a trace of angular beauty (角線美) can be found. Being 5 Cheok (尺) 4 Pun (分) tall, I suppose I'm a short woman by modern standards!! In proportion to that, weighing 14 Kwan (貫), I surely can't escape being called plump. My face is round and broad, and my features, one by one, are large, so I console myself (自慰) thinking I probably don't look hateful. Compared to my physique, my hands and feet (手足) are pale, small, and graceful, so whether looking from the perspective of physiognomy (骨相學) or physiology, far from being a lower animal (하등동물), among humans who are the lords of all creation (만물지영장), I am not a big-handed, big-footed thief or lowborn person (쌍놈), but rather quite a noble person (귀인), I console myself (自慰) – truly a naive (메데다이) person!! Though terribly stubborn, I am often moved to compassion in unnecessary matters and, trying to be chivalrous, I often end up sitting down in someone else's eye discharge [idiom: getting into trouble because of others], so perhaps I am foolish! I am famously fond of my friends, so it seems I have much camaraderie. If I wrote more, there would be plenty, but as I have filled the requested page space, I have no choice but to stop. <9>

**Summary**

The author, Song Kŭmsŏn, presents a self-deprecating yet proud self-portrait. She notes how her perceived identity changes based on wearing Western ("Russian refugee") or Korean ("Mistress of the house") attire. She describes herself physically as short and plump, lacking "angular beauty," but consoles herself by noting her relatively small, pale hands and feet signify nobility according to physiognomy, contrasting with stereotypes of the "lowborn." She characterizes her personality as stubborn but also foolishly compassionate and fiercely loyal to friends. For historians, this piece offers insight into the self-perception of an educated Korean woman during the colonial period, touching on identity tied to clothing (Western vs. Korean), contemporary beauty standards ("angular beauty"), references to traditional units of measure and physiognomy (尺, 分, 貫, 骨相學), and class consciousness ("lowborn," "noble person"). The casual use of the Japanese loanword "메데다이" (medetai) also reflects the linguistic environment of the time.

						宋今旋
로서아 피란민! 여러 사람들이 양복 입은 나를 관찰한 명예롭지 못한 선물의 명칭이다. 또 어느 부분 사람들은 말하되 대방마님이라고! 이것은 말할 것도 업시 조선옷 입은 내에게 준 영광의 명칭임니다. 좌우간 나는 귀가 커서 그런지 사람이 조와 그런지 (못나 그러켓지) 남에 말을 잘 듯는다. 그런 명칭을 듯고 보니까 그런지 내가 생각해도 아조 똥단지 가튼 비유는 아닌 것 갓다. 아닌 것이 아니라 따는 그럴 듯 해! 어드루 보든지 角線美는 하나도 차자볼 수 업다. 키가 5尺 4分이면 적은 녀자겟지 현대에서는!! 그 비례에 14貫이나되니 뚱뚱보 소리는 면치 못할 것. 얼골이 둥글넙적하고 구먹구먹 생김생김이 큼직큼직하니 그리 얄밉지는 아늘 것이라고 自慰하고 잇스며 체격에 비하야 手足은 히고 적고도 얌전하니 骨相學上으로든지 생리학상으로 보든지 하등동물은커녕 만물지영장인 사람 중에서도 손발 큰 도적과 쌍놈은 아니요 아조 귀인이라고 自慰하니 참으로 「메데다이」한 사람!! 굉장한 고집장이면서도 쓸대업는데 동정 잘하고 의협심 내다가 남의 눈 똥에 주저안끼를 잘하니 아마 미련한가바! 유명하게 동무들을 조와하니 우애는 만은가 보이다. 쓰고 보면 만켓지만 써 달나는 지면을 채왓스니 그만 둘 밧게 엄슴니다. ＜9＞
