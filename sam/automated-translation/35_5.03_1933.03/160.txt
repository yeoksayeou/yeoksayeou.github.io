Samch'ŏlli Wall Newspaper, The Land Boom Wind of Najin

Although the Government-General (總督府) is also there, making Seoul the political center of the peninsula, it is something acknowledged by all knowledgeable people (識者) that the future economic center will become places like Najin (羅津) and Unggi (雄基). Regarding these places like Najin (羅津), Ch'ŏngjin (淸津), and Unggi (雄基), even looking at the recent situation before the main boom (本景氣) begins, there are surprising aspects. Specifically, in the maritime transport network between Ch'ŏngjin (淸津) and Unggi (雄基), five major steamship companies (汽船會社) are competitively assigning ships (配船), and on land (陸上)—be amazed! (勿驚)—45 companies are applying for transport permits (輸送出願) for trams (電車), automobiles (自動車), airplanes (飛行機), and the like. <27>

**Summary**

The article reports on the growing anticipation that Najin and Unggi in northeastern Korea will become the peninsula's future economic center, surpassing Seoul. Even before the expected main economic boom, there is already intense activity, evidenced by five major steamship companies competing on the Ch'ŏngjin-Unggi sea route and 45 companies applying for land transport permits (trams, cars, planes) in the Najin, Ch'ŏngjin, and Unggi area. From a historical perspective, this article highlights the colonial administration's focus on developing specific regions, particularly ports like Najin, for strategic economic and logistical purposes (likely related to accessing Manchuria). It reveals the tangible excitement and investment rush associated with this planned development, showcasing the dramatic shifts in infrastructure and economic focus occurring under Japanese rule.

三千里壁新聞, 羅津의 陸上景氣風
總督府도 잇서 정치적으로는 반도의 중심지가 서울이 되겟지만 장차 경제중심지는 羅津 雄基 등지가 되리라 함은 識者들이 다가치 인정한는 바이다. 그런데 이 羅津 淸津 雄基 등지로 말하면 本景氣가 나기 前인 최근 형편만 보아도 놀라운 바가 잇다. 즉 淸津 雄基 間의 해상 교통망에는 5大 汽船會社가 경쟁적으로 配船하고 잇스며 陸上은 勿驚하라. 45회사에서 전차, 자동차, 비행기 등의 輸送出願을 하고 잇다. ＜27＞
