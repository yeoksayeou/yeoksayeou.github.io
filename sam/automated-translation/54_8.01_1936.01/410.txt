필자	嶺雲 毛允淑
기사형태	시 (Poem)

思慕
						嶺雲 毛允淑

A night embraced in the shade west of the detached building (별당)
On the quiet altar (壇), the flame burns blue
Only the X house
Cries upon the wall.
Weeds sway in the midnight wind (삼경바람)
Clouds tremble and pass under the moon
Pointlessly, the blue smoke of memory
Crawls up the armor and helmet in the closet.

The fragrant purple haze wine (紫霞酒) in the white jade cup (白玉盃)<273>
That you couldn't bear to drink upon leaving
I now hold in my hand and pray
The shadow of a sword is still visible in the wine cup
Surely, even tonight, my Lord (님)
Holding the Yongch'ŏn sword (용천검) askew, must be traveling a thousand-league road (千里길).

When my Lord, cradled in colored clouds,
Calls me from beneath that shade

Without hiding my tear-stained face
Embracing this soul within a blue will
You gave me a sharp sword, telling me to split this heart.

My Lord knew
That blue eyebrows and red cheeks
Could perhaps never please you for a lifetime
On the day the journey through dusty earth (塵土의 行旅) ends<274>
The blue moss on a few pieces of white bone (白骨)
Will laugh at life's joys (人生歡樂).

Frost falls beneath the moon
The brilliant morning star awakens the sky
O Lord, this deep and clear night
Unravel the infinite creation (無窮한 조화) with wisdom
Drive out the dark formations (검은 陣)
And turn the bright spirit (밝은 靈) toward XX.

O Lord, with your iron-like will (鐵氣로운 意志)
Annihilate the treacherous energy of this sky
Let the blue light pierce through a thousand autumns (千秋)
Let the fallen souls rise<275>
And dance beneath the eternal white light.

Building the banks of XX River, those hands were rough
Sitting guard at the castle gate (城門), there was no peaceful sleep (安眠)
Though that leg was broken by another's sword
The way you returned, bleeding, and laughed
I picture it clearly even now.

The shaggy dog barks outside the village entrance (동구 밖)
Is it announcing the path my Lord will come?
The resting place of living life (산생명)
How could this body cease where my Lord's breath is?
If I grind my frail bones to repay that grace
Let me become an offering (제물) in this place where my Lord lives.
-(From the novel *The Death of Ich'adon* (小說 異次頓의 死에서))<276>

**Summary**

This poem by Mo Yun-suk, titled "Samo" (Yearning), expresses intense longing and devotion towards an absent figure referred to as "Nim" (Lord/Beloved). The speaker recalls shared moments, the beloved's strong will and sacrifice (likened to a warrior with a sword), and pledges unwavering loyalty, even unto death, wishing to become an offering. The poem uses martial and spiritual imagery, referencing armor, swords, battles, souls, and eternal light, culminating in an explicit comparison to the Silla martyr Ich'adon, suggesting the "Nim" embodies selfless sacrifice for a greater cause. From a historical perspective, the poem, written during the Japanese colonial period, is significant. The intense yearning for a powerful, righteous figure ("Nim") who fights against darkness ("treacherous energy," "dark formations") and whose sacrifice inspires devotion could be interpreted as a veiled expression of nationalist sentiment or longing for liberation, using the historical figure of Ich'adon as a culturally resonant symbol of martyrdom for a cause (in Ich'adon's case, Buddhism; in the 1930s context, potentially national identity or sovereignty). The poem's blend of romantic devotion, martial imagery, and historical allusion offers historians insight into how sentiments of loss, loyalty, and perhaps resistance were articulated through literature under colonial rule.

별당 서편 그늘에 품긴 밤
고요한 壇에 불꽃 푸르고
X집만 외로히
벽 우에 울고 있다.
잡풀은 삼경바람에 흔들니고
구름발 달아래 떨고 간다
부지럽시 記憶의 푸른 연긔는
벽장 속 갑옷 투구에 긔여 오르네.

떠날 때 참아 못 마시든
白玉盃의 香 빠진 紫霞酒<273>
내 이제 손에 들고 비나이다
술잔 속 칼 그림자 지금도 보이오니
님은 정영 이 밤에도
용천검 빗겨들고 千里길 行하시오리.

님이 채색 구름에 안겨
저 그늘 아래 저를 부르실 때

흠진이 얼골 개림 없이
푸른 意志 속에 이 魂을 포옹하고
날낸 검 주시며 이 맘 갈나 하시다.

님은 아시더이다
푸른 아미 붉은 두 뺨이
하마 平生 님을 깃브게 못하올 것을
塵土의 行旅를 맟이는 날<274>
두어조각 白骨의 푸른 잇기
人生歡樂을 비웃을 거슬,

달 아래 서리 날고
황황한 샛별 하늘을 깨우거니
님이여, 이 밤이 깊고 맑은 밤
무궁한 조화를 지헤로 풀어내여
검은 陣을 내몰고
밝은 靈을 XX으로 向하리이다.

님이여, 님의 鐵氣로운 意志로
이 하늘의 간사한 긔운을 멸하고
푸른 빛이 千秋를 꿰뚤으게 하소서
떠러진 魂들이 니러나<275>
永遠한 힌빛아래
춤추게 하소서

XX의 江邊을 쌓으매 그 손길 거칠엇고
城門에 직혀앉어 安眠이 없엇거늘
그 다리 남의 칼에 꺽기여도
피흘니며 도라와 우스시든 양
지금도 그양 자푸러냄니다.

삽살개 동구 밖 우러
우리님 오실 길 아룀인가
산생명의 안식소
님의 숨결잇는 곧에 이몸 어이 그히리
잔약한 뼈를 갈아 그 은헤 갚을진대
님 사옵시는 이 곳에 제물이 되여지다.
-(小說 異次頓의 死에서)<276>
