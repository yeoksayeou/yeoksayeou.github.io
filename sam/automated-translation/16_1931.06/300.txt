Company Announcement

We are recruiting for branch offices in regions where branch offices have not yet been established. Those who are interested, please enclose 4 chŏn (錢) worth of postage stamps and request the rulebook.

June 1st
Samch'ŏlli Company Business Department <77>

**Summary**

This is a brief company announcement from the Samch'ŏlli Company's Business Department, published on June 1, 1931. It calls for applications to establish branch offices in areas where they do not yet exist. Interested parties are instructed to send 4 chŏn worth of postage stamps to request the company's rulebook. For historians, this notice reflects the magazine's business expansion strategy during the colonial period and the practicalities of communication and application processes, relying on the postal service and specifying costs in the contemporary currency unit (chŏn).
