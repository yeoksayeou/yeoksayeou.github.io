The Returned Labor-Peasant Masses and Organization
						By Song Pong'u

After the Sin'ganhoe (新幹會) dissolved, it is a clearly visible fact that the masses attached to the Sin'ganhoe will return—peasants to peasant unions and laborers to labor unions. If existing organizations capable of absorbing the returning labor-peasant masses do not exist, new ones will be organized. In this way, they will likely not establish vague struggle objectives as they did during the time the Sin'ganhoe existed, nor will their fighting spirit become deadened and fixed.

Facing the issues at hand, they must also strive to secure their respective interests. The vanguard must not follow behind them but lead them well from the front. By doing so, I believe the movement in each sector will expand and strengthen.

However, there is something I must say. It is about the strategy regarding this, but my words keep stopping. In short, what I have said above are only the easy things to say. I cannot speak of detailed strategies. (Remainder omitted - original text) <10>

**Summary**

This editorial by Song Pong'u discusses the anticipated reorganization of peasants and laborers following the dissolution of the Sin'ganhoe. The author predicts these masses will naturally gravitate towards specific peasant and labor unions, needing new organizations if existing ones are insufficient. He emphasizes the need for these groups to pursue concrete interests and avoid the vague objectives and stagnant fighting spirit seen previously. The author calls for a "vanguard" to actively guide these movements but explicitly states an inability to elaborate on detailed strategies, likely due to the political climate. From a historical perspective, the article is interesting as it reflects the immediate aftermath of the Sin'ganhoe's dissolution, a significant event in the Korean nationalist and socialist movements. It highlights the strategic thinking about channeling mass energy into more focused, class-based organizations (unions) and reveals the cautious self-censorship prevalent under Japanese colonial rule, hinting at deeper, unstated plans.

還元된 勞農大衆과 組織
						宋奉瑀
新幹會가 解消한 뒤에는 新幹會에 附着된 大衆을 農民은 農民組合에로 勞働者는 勞働組合에로 도라갈 것은 환-하게 보이는 事實임니다. 還元되는 勞農大衆을 吸收할 旣成體가 업스면 새로히 組織될 것이외다. 이리하야 이들은 在來 新幹會存立當時와 가치 漠然한 鬪爭目標를 세운다든지 그러치안으면 戰鬪意識이 死化固着되지는 안을 것이외다.
當面當面하야 各自의 利益戰取에도 努力하여야 할 것이외다. 前衛는 그들의 뒤에 따라갈 것이 아니라 압서서 그들을 善히 指導하여야 함니다. 이러케 하면 各 部門運動이 擴大强化하리라고 생각됨니다.

그러나 말하여 둘 것이 잇슴니다. 다름이 아니라 이에 대한 方略을 말슴하랴 하니 말이 작구 멉추어 짐니다. 要컨대 以上에 말한 것은 하기 容易한 말만을 하엿슴니다. 仔細한 方略은 말하지 못하겟슴니다.(下略-원문)＜10＞
