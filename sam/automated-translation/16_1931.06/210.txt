Declaration
						China League of Left-Wing Writers

Art in a transitional period of change can only be true art when it exists as a weapon for the struggle for XX [revolution]. Why is this so? Because only art that keeps pace with the development of history can achieve truly great completion.

Insofar as the mission of art aims at organizing the X命的 [revolutionary?] direction of the proletariat's life sentiment, the artist must simultaneously be an organizer (올가.나이자) in the political sense as well. The old system (舊制度) has already become shackles (桎梏) on human development. (Omitted - original text)

Thus, we must develop all oppressed progressive elements through a daring struggle (鬪爭) against all feudalistic (封建的), capitalistic (資本主義的), and reactionary (反動的) toxins (毒素). This is the mission we must naturally bear as collaborators (協力者) of the proletariat.

All our art must be dedicated to the struggle of reality (現實).
Our art must become a means (手段) to elevate and organize the XX的 [revolutionary?] pulsation (鼓動) of all the feelings (感情) of the proletariat that beat in the lowest stratum of this dark century (暗黑의 世紀).
We must participate (參加) in the construction movement (建設運動) of proletarian culture (文化). We must indicate the correct direction (正確한 方向) of the movement through theory (理論) and at the same time actively move in reality to strengthen its development.
Actively developing the raising and examination (提起와 檢討) of issues (問題), the presentation (發表) of works (作品), and concrete criticism (具體的 批評), while simultaneously carrying out merciless criticism (假藉업는 批判) of "salon" (살론) art and petit-bourgeois (뿌지, 뿌르) art, and perfecting a close contact network (緊密한 連絡網) with proletarian writers abroad (國外푸로레타리아作家), are pressing issues (當面의 問題).
We, as political organizers (政治的 올가나이자), must actively participate (積極的 參加치) in the struggle against the reactionary forces (反動勢力) of the XX [revolutionary?] movement (運動).

Reference - The League of Left-Wing Writers is a joint formation (綜合結成) of:
Creation Group (創造派), Sun Group (太陽派), Mass Literature Society (大衆文藝社), Modern Youth Society (摩登靑年社), Art Theater Society (藝術劇社), *Hwangwŏlgansa (*荒月刊社), Sprout Society (萌芽社), Modern Novel Society (現代小說社), etc.
Writers (作家) and Theorists (理論家) include:
Messrs. Kuo Mo-jo (郭沫若), Lu Hsun (魯迅), Feng Nai-ch'ao (馮乃超), Chu Ching-wo (朱鏡我), P'eng K'ang (彭康), Ch'ien Hsing-ts'un (錢杏邨), Hung Ling-fei (洪霍非), Chiang Kuang-tz'ŭ (蔣光慈), Kim Man-sŏng (金滿成), Cheng Po-ch'i (鄭伯奇), etc. <57>

**Summary**

This document is the declaration of the China League of Left-Wing Writers, arguing that art in the current era must serve as a political weapon for the proletarian revolution. It asserts that true art aligns with historical progress and demands artists become political organizers. The League advocates for abandoning feudal and capitalist influences, dedicating art to reflecting and mobilizing proletarian consciousness, building proletarian culture, engaging in theoretical debate and practical criticism (including denouncing salon and petit-bourgeois art), and forging international connections. The declaration calls for active participation in the struggle against reactionary forces. From a Korean colonial history perspective, the publication of this Chinese leftist manifesto in a major Korean magazine like *Samch'ŏlli* in 1931 is highly significant. It demonstrates the transnational flow of radical socialist and communist ideas into colonial Korea and suggests a degree of solidarity or shared interest among East Asian leftist intellectuals, despite the complex political environment and Japanese censorship. Historians would find this valuable for understanding the ideological currents influencing Korean intellectuals and artists during this period and the international dimensions of leftist movements in East Asia.

宣言
						中國左翼作家聯盟
變革의 過渡期의 藝術은 XX을 위한 鬪爭의 무기로하여 존재하는 경우만 진실한 藝術이 될  수 잇다. 엇재 그러냐하면 歷史의 發展과 同時에 同一한 步調를 가지는 藝術만 眞實로 偉大한 完成을 가지는 까닭이다.
藝術의 任務가 푸로레타리앗트의 生活感情의 X命的 方向에의 組織化를 目標로 하는 以上 藝術家는 同時에 政治的 意味에 잇서서도 올가.나이자가 안되면 안된다. 舊制度은 이미 人類發展의 桎梏이 되엇다. (中略 - 원문)
이리하야 우리들은 一切 壓迫當하고 잇는 進步的 要素를 모든 封建的 資本主義的 反動的 毒素에 대한 果敢한 鬪爭을 通하야 發展식히지 안으면 안된다. 그것은 우리들의 푸로레타리아의 協力者로서 當然히 負擔할 任務이다.

우리의 藝術은 모다 現實의 鬪爭때문에 밧치지 안으면 안된다.
우리들의 藝術은 暗黑의 世紀의 맨아래層에 脈을 치는 푸로레타리아의 一切의 感情의 XX的 鼓動을 놉히고 組織하는 手段이 되지 안으면 안된다.
우리들은 푸로레타리아 文化의 建設運動에 參加하지 안으면 안된다. 理論에 의하여 運動의 正確한 方向을 指示하고 同時에 現實로 積極的으로 움지기는서 그 發展을 强大케 하지 안으면 안된다.
問題의 提起와 檢討, 作品의 發表及 具體的 批評을 活潑하게 展開함과 同時에 「살론」藝術及 뿌지, 뿌르藝術의 假藉업는 批判及國外푸로레타리아作家와의 緊密한 連絡網의 完全은 當面의 問題이다.
우리들은 政治的 올가나이자(組織者)로서 XX運動反動勢力과의 鬪爭에 積極的 參加치 안으면 안된다.
