**Body Text Translation:**
Literary World March

Among writers' groups, there are three: the Chosŏn Artists Association (朝鮮文藝家協會), the Proletarian Art Alliance (푸로藝術同盟), and the Ballad Association (歌謠協會). The Artists Association was a group formed in unity by over 40 writers, motivated by the desire to receive manuscript fees of 15 chŏn per page for novels and 3 wŏn per piece for poetry, but with the collapse of magazines like *Chosŏn Mundan* (朝鮮文壇), *Tonggwang* (東光), and *Hyŏndae P'yŏngnon* (現代評論), it has become somewhat desolate now. As for the Proletarian Art Alliance, whatever work they do internally, there is little noticeable activity apparent from the outside. The Ballad Association also made a grand initial pronouncement, even receiving editorials from three newspaper companies, but remains quiet and unheard from. Fishermen catch fish, writers write texts, and organizations must work ceaselessly for their name to be established, so why is it so quiet?

Some time ago, the Chosŏn Ilbo hosted a writers' roundtable discussion at a certain hermitage outside Tongsomun (東小門). At that gathering, literary giants (巨星) such as Sŏnghae (星海), Sŏhae (曙海), Hwaewŏl (懷月), Ir'yŏp (一葉), P'albong (八峯), Tokkyŏn (獨鵑), Sŭng'il (承一), Ŭnsang (殷相), Chŏkku (赤駒), Tayŏng (多影), etc., gathered truly like clouds. However, their mouths were not refined, and when taking a commemorative photo, they unbearably pestered the photographer, saying "Take it quickly!" "Just take it quickly and be done with it!" and then looked at each other and laughed "Hahaha." Perhaps understanding the meaning behind their excessive laughter and pestering, the face of Lady Ir'yŏp (一葉女史), who stood in the middle as the sole woman among many men (萬錄叢 중 紅一点), suddenly turned red like an autumn maple leaf in the ninth or tenth month. Oh, sinful males.

If asked who among writers received the most manuscript fees, it would likely be Ch'unwŏn (春園) who comes first, having both seniority (年祖) and many readers, followed by Sangsŏp (想涉), Tokkyŏn (獨鵑), Sŏnghae (星海), Sŏhae (曙海)... or so it would seem. However, did even Ch'unwŏn, who earns much, exceed 10,000 Wŏn (萬圓)?

If we were to pick orators of the literary world, Yohan (요한) would be mentioned first, and Mr. Yi Ryang (李亮氏) would be counted next as a provocative, eloquent speaker (煽動的 能辯家). P'albong's (八峯) speeches have weight but are not fluent; Hwaewŏl (懷月) speaks in a scholarly manner without moving his body the slightest bit; Ch'unwŏn (春園) is not an orator but acknowledged as eloquent (能辯); Sangsŏp (想涉), with his masculine voice volume and large build, is famous for captivating the audience with a bold and lively spirit (豪活한 패기), gesturing smoothly with both arms and legs on the podium (演壇); Sŏnghae (星海) is skilled at speaking smoothly without interruption; Yŏsu (麗水) is quiet but renowned for being logical and orderly (調理整然); Ansŏ (岸曙) is also famous for reciting poetry in the style of the P'yŏngyang Susimga (平壤愁心歌)... As for others, I have not yet heard them speak, so I cannot write more.

P'osŏk (포석), who once dominated the proletarian literary scene (푸로문단) with his single work "Naktonggang" (洛東江), went north and further north, and now there is no news of him. Lady Kim Myŏngsun (金明淳女史), whose name became famous when she published "Ŭimun ŭi Sonyŏ" (疑問의 少女 - Questionable Girl) in *Ch'ŏngch'un* (靑春) as an eighteen-year-old girl, also has no news. Wherever they are, it would be good if they are making steady "advancement" (전진) through their pens (붓대).

The school song of a certain school, composed by a certain person (某氏) who was our pride 10 years ago but has already become our shame today, is reportedly something the students of that school have recently decided collectively not to sing. Because of this, the person who can neither tell them to sing it nor tell them not to sing it, unable to sit or stand, is a certain Mr./Ms. So-and-so (某氏) who serves as the dorm supervisor (舍伯) for that person [the composer] at that school – what a surprising dorm supervisor! <29>

**Summary**

This article from 1929 provides gossip and observations about the Korean literary scene. It notes the relative inactivity of major literary organizations like the Chosŏn Artists Association and the Proletarian Art Alliance. It humorously recounts a lively gathering of prominent writers (listing many by pen name), speculates on who earns the most royalties (naming Ch'unwŏn first), and ranks various writers based on their public speaking abilities. The author also laments the disappearance of writers P'osŏk and Kim Myŏngsun from the public eye and concludes with an anecdote about a school song written by a now-disgraced figure, causing awkwardness for a school official. For historians, the article offers a valuable glimpse into the colonial-era literary world, identifying key figures, organizations (including the leftist P'ŭro Yesul Tongmaeng), publications, social interactions within the male-dominated literary circle, economic concerns (royalties), and the real-world consequences of political or personal disgrace within that community. The mention of specific pen names, royalty figures (even speculatively), and the school song controversy provide concrete details about the period.

文壇行進曲
문인들의 모임으로는 朝鮮文藝家協會와 푸로藝術同盟과 歌謠協會의 세 개가 잇다. 文藝家協會는 소설 한 장에 15錢, 시 편 장에 3圓식 원고료를 밧자는 것이 동기되어 文士 40餘氏가 단합결성한 단체엇스나 朝鮮文壇 東光 現代評論 등이 꺽구러지는 통에 지금은 다소 적막하게 되엇고 푸로藝術同盟은 안에서는 무슨 일을 하는지 바갓헤 나타나는 일로는 별로 눈에 뜨이는 일이 업고 歌謠協會도 선소리만 굉장하여 3신문사의 사설까지 밧더니 아직 寂寂無聞하다. 어부는 고기를 낙고 문인은 글을 쓰고 단체는 부절히 일을 하여야 그 일홈이 서거든 웨이리 한산할고?

얼마 전에 조선일보사 주최로 東小門밧 어느 암자에서 문인좌담회를 열엇는데 그 석상에는 星海 曙海 懷月 一葉 八峯 獨鵑 承一 殷相 赤駒 多影 等 巨星이 그야말로 구름가치 모앗섯스나 모다 입이 점잔치 못하여서 기념촬영을 할 때에 「어서 박어요!」「어서 시언히 박아버리서요!」하고 사진장이더러 못견디게 졸라떼고는 서로 얼골을 처다보며 하하하 웃엇다. 너무 여러 번을 웃고 졸느는 통에 그 뜻을 알엇슴인가 萬錄叢 중 紅一点으로 가운데 섯든 一葉女史의 얼골은 불시에 구십월 단풍입가치 빨개젓다. 죄만흔 남성들이어.

문인가운데 누가 제일 원고료를 만히 밧엇술가 하면 아모래도 年祖와 독자가 만흔 春園이 첫손가락에 꼽히겟고 그 다음이 想涉 獨鵑 星海 曙海... 그러케 돠리라고. 그러나 만타는 春園이 萬圓을 넘엇슬가?

문단웅변가를 꼽자면 요한을 처음에 들겟고 선동적 능변가로 李亮氏를 그 다음에 칠 것이다. 八峯의 연설은 무게잇스나 流鴨치 못하고 懷月은 몸집하나 깟닥아니하는 학자식으로 하며 春園은 웅변은 못되나 능변으로 許하고 想涉은 남성적 성량에 거구에 아울러 演壇에서 팔도 능청능청 다리도 능청능청하여 豪活한 패기로 청중을 끄느는 것이 유명하고 星海는 굴곡이 업스나 말이 막히지 안는 것에 능하고 麗水는 고요하나 조리정연으로 일홈잇고 岸曙도 平壤愁心歌調讀詩가 유명하고... 그 밧게 분은 아직 들어보지를 못하여서 더 쓸 수 업다.

「洛東江」 1편으로 푸로문단을 위압하든 포석은 북으로 북으로 가드니 이내 소식이 업고 열여덜의 색시때에 「疑問의 少女」 1편을 靑春에 발표하야 그 일홈이 놉흐든 金明渟女史 亦 소식이 업다. 어데에 잇든지 붓대를 통하야 꾸준한 「전진」을 하고 잇다면 조흐련만은.

10년 전엔 우리의 자랑거리더니 10년 후의 오늘엔 이미 우리의 치욕거리가 되어버린 某氏가 지은 모학교의 교가는 요지음에 와서 그 학교 학생들이 일제히 아니부르기로 결정하엿다나. 그러타고 부르랄 수도 업고 아니 부르랄 수도 업서서 안지고 못하고 서지도 못하 잇는 분은 그를 舍伯으로 가진 그 학교의 某氏라니 놀나운 舍伯이로군.＜29＞
