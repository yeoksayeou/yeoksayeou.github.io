**Body Text Translation**
President Has His Son Work as Someone Else's Servant, Says It's for Practical Education

Mexican President Oljisŭru-bio (올지쓰루-비오), thinking that his two sons should receive practical education rather than school education—social education rather than book education—had them complete three months of apprenticeship service (見習 奉公) in New York during the past summer vacation.<48>

Regarding the field in which they intend to pioneer their future lives, the two sons spoke with humble tones as follows.

The older brother, Kip'elmo Oljjŭ Ru-bio (기펠모.올쯔.루-비오) kun, now 19 years old, said he wants to become an excellent banker in Meksŭk'o (멕쓰코) before long.

His younger brother, P'eljinandŭ Oldeijjŭ Ru-bio (펠지난드.올데잇즈.루-비오) kun, now 10 years old, expressed his intention to become a civil engineer, saying that when he returns to Meksŭk'o, he will build necessary bridges (橋), waterways, and other roads.<49>

**Summary**

This article reports that the President of Mexico, Ortiz Rubio, sent his two sons (aged 19 and 10) to New York for three months of "apprenticeship service" during their summer vacation. The President prioritized practical, social education over formal schooling. The older son aims to be a banker in Mexico, while the younger aspires to be a civil engineer building infrastructure there. For historians studying colonial Korea, this piece offers a glimpse into international news filtration and interests of the time. It presents a non-traditional educational approach by a foreign leader, potentially contrasting with or implicitly commenting on the educational aspirations and opportunities (or lack thereof) for Koreans under Japanese rule, particularly the emphasis on practical skills development.

大統領이 제 아들을 남의 使喚 노릇 식혀, 실지 교육을 식힌다고
멕쓰코 대통령 올지쓰루-비오는 두 아들에게＜48＞ 학교교육 보담 실지교육―서적교육 보담 사회교육을 밧게 하겟다는 생각에서 지난 여름 방학을 이용하야 뉴-욕에서 3개월간 見習 奉公을 맛추게 하엿다.
그 두 아들은 장래에 엇더한 방면에서 자기의 일생애를 개척하야 나가겟다는데 대해서 겸손한 어조로 다음과 가튼 말을 하얏다.
형되는 기펠모.올쯔.루-비오 군는 금년 19세인데 오라지 안어서 멕쓰코에서 훌융한 은행가가 되겟다고 말하엿고.
그 동생되는 펠지난드.올데잇즈.루-비오 군는 금년 10세인데 토목기사가 될여는 생각으로 멕쓰코에 도라가면 필요한 다리(橋) 수로 기타 도로를 만드러 노켓다고 이약이하엿다.＜49＞
