Company Notice

Mr. An Chaehong's (安在鴻氏) piece, "Britain's Attitude Towards the Indian Movement (印度運動에 對한 英吉利의 態度)," is not being published due to unavoidable circumstances (不得已한 事情).

From the Editorial Bureau <7>

**Summary**

This brief notice from the *Samch'ŏlli* magazine's editorial bureau informs readers that a scheduled article by An Chaehong discussing British policy towards the Indian independence movement has been withdrawn due to unspecified "unavoidable circumstances." This likely indicates censorship or self-censorship, possibly due to the politically sensitive nature of discussing anti-colonial movements (like India's) and the implicit comparison to Korea's situation under Japanese rule. Historians might find this notice significant as evidence of the constraints on press freedom and the careful navigation required by Korean intellectuals and publishers when addressing topics related to imperialism and national self-determination during the colonial period.
