中外日報의 近況
						三千里 壁新聞 제4호

The Chungoe Ilbo (中外日報), which had been suspended for a long time, causing a desolate wind to blow in one corner of the Korean press world, was granted permission for a change in publisher name to Mr. Kim Ch'ansŏng (金贊成) of Kyŏngnam (慶南) on October 15th. A group of new managers centered around Mr. Kim purchased the two-story Western-style building at Kyŏnji-dong (堅志洞) 60, the former site of the Chosŏn Tosŏ Hoesa (朝鮮圖書會社), for ₩12,500, and are preparing to purchase a rotary press and type. Furthermore, they are investing about ₩8,000 to construct a factory on the plot of land adjacent to the rear of the company building.

According to what is heard, the newspaper is expected to begin publication around mid-November, and there is talk that it will be a low-priced 4-page newspaper; its name has reportedly been changed to Chungang Ilbo (중앙일보).

Also, according to informed sources, the former figures Mr. An Hŭi-je (安熙濟) and Mr. Yi Sang-hyŏp (李相協) have severed their connections, and new figures such as Mr. No Chŏng-il (盧正一) are said to be becoming active, et cetera. <27>

**Summary**

The article reports on the impending revival of the Chungoe Ilbo newspaper after a lengthy suspension. New management, led by Kim Ch'ansŏng, has secured publishing rights, purchased a building (the former Chosŏn Tosŏ Hoesa site), and is investing in printing equipment and a new factory. The paper is set to relaunch in mid-November under the new name Chungang Ilbo, likely as a low-cost, 4-page publication. Former key personnel An Hŭi-je and Yi Sang-hyŏp are no longer involved, with figures like No Chŏng-il taking active roles. For historians, this piece illustrates the operational challenges, financial aspects (building/factory costs), and personnel shifts within the Korean private press during the colonial period, highlighting the ongoing efforts to maintain Korean-language media despite difficulties and potential political pressures hinted at by the change in leadership and name.

中外日報의 近況
						三千里 壁新聞 제4호
오랫동안 휴간중에 잇서 朝鮮 언론계의 일각에 적막한 바람을 불게하든 中外日報는 慶南 金贊成씨 명의로 10월 15일에 발행인 명의의 변경이 허가되어 金씨를 중심으로한 신경영자의 일파에서는 堅志洞 60번지 녜전 朝鮮圖書會社터의 2층 양옥을 1만2500원으로 매입하고 윤전기와 활자를 구입준비중이며 더욱 사옥에 인접한 후편 지면에 약 8000원을 投하야 공장을 건축중에 잇다.
드른즉 신문은 11월 중순경부터 발행되리라는데 정가가 나즌 4부신문으로 하리란 설이 잇스며 명칭은 중앙일보라고 변경하엿다한다.
又 소식통이 전하는바에 의하면 전일의 安熙濟,李相協씨등은 관계를 끈코 새로 盧正一씨등이 활약하리라 云云.＜27＞
