Production of Famous Russian Film, Commemorating the 15th Anniversary of the October Revolution

According to a telegram sent from Haesam (海參 - Vladivostok) on the 6th, the famous Russian film director Mr. Eijesyut'ain (Eisenstein), at the invitation of America's Mr. Esen Sink'ŭrea (Essen Sinclair), completed a major film themed on his three-year stay in America and life in Meksŭk'o (Mexico) after being invited there. This film is currently enjoying favorable reviews in Rŏsia (Russia). Mr. Eijesyut'ain plans to return to Rŏsia (歸露) at the end of October to begin production of a grand film commemorating the 15th anniversary of the October Revolution (10月革命).<49> That film is rumored to be planned on a larger scale than the original film "October," which had caused all Russians to acclaim it as the world's greatest film, and it is said he intends to pour all his energy into it, causing a great sensation in the film world (映畵界).<49>
<49-50>

**Summary**

The article reports that renowned Soviet film director Eisenstein, after completing a film about his time in America and Mexico funded by "Essen Sinclair" (likely Upton Sinclair), is returning to Russia. He plans to commence production on a new, large-scale film commemorating the 15th anniversary of the October Revolution, expected to surpass his acclaimed earlier work, "October." From a historical perspective, this piece is interesting as it shows Korean audiences in the colonial period were kept informed about international cultural figures like Eisenstein and major events like the October Revolution anniversary. It reflects an awareness of and potential interest in Soviet Russia and its cultural output, despite Japanese colonial rule and censorship. The use of the older Korean name "Haesam" for Vladivostok is also noteworthy.

露西亞 名映畵 製作, 10月革命 15周年 紀念으로
지난 6일에 海參에서 보낸 전보에 의하면 러시아의 명영화 감독 에이제슈타인씨는 아메리카의 엣센·신크레아씨의 초빙으로 3년간 아메리카 滯在와 멕쓰코 생활을 주제로 한 一大영화를 완성해서 목하 러시아에서 호평을 날니고 잇는데 同氏는 10월 말에 歸露하야 10월혁명 제 15주년 기념 대영화의 제작을 시작하려고＜49＞ 한다. 그 영화는 세계 제일의 영화라고 전러시아인을 떠들게 만들던 본래 영화 「10월」이라는 영화보담 대규모의 계획으로 전력을 주입한다는 소문이 잇슴으로 영화계에 일대 센세이슌을 니르키고 잇다고 한다.＜49＞
