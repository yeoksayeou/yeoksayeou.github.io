Company Announcement

We are widely recruiting branch offices both domestically (海內) and overseas (海外). Those interested (有意하신분) are requested to enclose a two-chŏn postage stamp (貳錢郵票) and request the regulations document (規定書).

Samch'ŏlli Company Business Department Regards <78>

**Summary**

This is a brief company announcement from the *Samch'ŏlli* magazine's business department seeking individuals interested in establishing branch offices both within Korea and abroad. Prospective applicants are instructed to send a two-chŏn stamp to receive the application guidelines. From a historical perspective, this notice reveals the magazine's ambition to expand its reach and distribution network during the colonial period, indicating a degree of business activity and outreach. The requirement of a stamp for information also provides a small glimpse into the administrative and economic practicalities of the time.
