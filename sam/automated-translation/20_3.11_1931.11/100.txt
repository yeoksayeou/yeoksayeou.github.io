**Body Text Translation:**
The Manchuria-Mongolia Independence Movement and Korean Interests, the Manchuria-Mongolia Regime and Our Future
						Kwŏn Tongjin

Today, all the complex relationships surrounding the Manchuria-Mongolia (滿蒙) issue are seen not merely confined to the problem between Japan (日本) and China (中國), but also widely spreading to and relating to the entire Far East (極東) political situation and also globally. Although there is no need to recount the historical narrative leading up to this point, in any case (엇젯든), we cannot overlook the fact that after the Nationalist government based on the Three Principles of the People (三民主義) was established in China, and consequently, the diplomatic authority over the Three Eastern Provinces (東三省) under the Chang Hsüeh-liang (張學良) clique was transferred into the hands of the central government, the relationship between Japan and China became much more complex and fraught with difficulties.

Now, looking at the recent situation in both countries, wasn't it the case that following China's movement to recover its national sovereignty, there was an economic boycott against Japan, and as the anti-Japanese movement reached its peak, Japan, for its part, could not help but see its vested rights and interests (기득권익) in Manchuria and Mongolia also being shaken, ultimately leading to a fated clash (천벌의 相交)?

However, a situation deserving great attention at this juncture is the report that warlords and key economic and political figures like Aiginkai (哀金凱), who held sway in the four provinces of Manchuria and Mongolia (滿蒙四省), are plotting a Manchurian-Mongolian independence movement. (Four lines omitted)

When forecasting the Manchuria-Mongolia independence movement, considering all conditions and stating a conclusion, in my view, the feasibility of its independence seems extremely weak. The actions of politicians, warlords, etc., holding sway in the Three Eastern Provinces seem mostly to be the impure professional techniques of political profiteers (모리배). Even considering the regional relationships, its feasibility seems very slim.

Therefore, until this sees a definitive outcome, it seems very difficult for Koreans in Manchuria (在滿朝鮮人) or for us (吾人) to discuss the relationship of interests with the Manchuria-Mongolia independence movement. (Omitted text)<26>

The general trend unfolding ahead—it provides us, at least, with material for observation. It seems to be a matter requiring more time.<27>

**Summary**

The author, Kwŏn Tongjin, analyzes the burgeoning Manchurian-Mongolian independence movement in late 1931, viewing it as a consequence of escalating Sino-Japanese tensions following the rise of the Nationalist government in China and Japan's efforts to protect its interests in the region. Kwŏn expresses strong skepticism about the movement's success, attributing it largely to the opportunistic maneuvering of local warlords and political figures rather than genuine conditions for independence. He concludes that the situation is too uncertain to determine the implications for Koreans (both in Manchuria and broadly), urging careful observation as events unfold. For historians, this article offers a valuable Korean perspective from the colonial era, highlighting concerns about regional instability driven by Japan and China, skepticism towards Manchurian power players, and the precarious position of Koreans caught amidst these larger geopolitical shifts, needing to cautiously assess how any outcome might affect their interests and future.

滿蒙獨立運動과 朝鮮人 利害, 滿蒙政權과 吾人의 將來
						權東鎭
금일 滿蒙문제를 중심삼고 싸고도는 모든 복잡한 관계는 비단 日本과 中國과의 문제에 끄치지 아니하고 極東政局 전반에와 또한 세계적으로 파급,또 관련되는 점이 심히 만타고 봅니다.이러케 되기까지의 역사적 서술은 다시 기록할 필요가 업스나 엇젯든 中國에 三民主義에 의한 국민정부가 수립되고 따라서 張學良계의 東三省 외교권이 중앙정부의 수중에 옴긴뒤부터 日中관계는 훨신 복잡하여젓고 다단하여진 것을 우리는 간과할 수가 업슴니다.
이제 최근의 양국정세를 살펴보면 中國의 국권회수운동에 따라서 對日경제절교가 잇서 반일운동이 그 절정에 달함과 동시에 또한 日本으로 말하면 滿蒙에서의 기득권익이 또한 동요를 입지아니치 못하야 필경 천벌의 相交에까지 이른 것이 아님니까?
그런데 이즈음에 대단히 주목할 사태는 哀金凱등 滿蒙四省에 할거하고 잇든 군벌과 경제,정치상 요인들이 滿蒙독립운동을 획책하고잇다는 보도외다.(四行略)
滿蒙의 독립운동을 전망할때에 제반조건을 예상하고 결론을 말하라면 내가 보기에는 그 독립의 실현성이 극히 박약한줄암니다.東三省에 할거하고 잇는 정객,군벌등의 행사는 거지반 정치적 모리배의 불순한 직업적 기교가 대부분일줄 암니다.지역상의 관계를 생각하여도 그 실현성이란 대단히 희박한줄로 암니다.
그리기에 이것이 결정적 귀결을 보기전에는 在滿朝鮮人 또는 우리로 안저 滿蒙독립운동과의 이해관계를 말하기 대단히 곤란할줄압니다.(中略)＜26＞
압흐로 전개되는 대세-그는 吾人에게만흔 주시의 재료를 던저줌니다.좀더 시간을 필요로하는 문제인줄암니다.＜27＞
