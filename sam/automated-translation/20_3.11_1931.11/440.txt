**Body Text Translation:**
World Diary, What Made the Mother Abandon Her Child?
						Song Kye-wŏl

October Xth
As the alarm clock placed on the desk by my head indicated seven o'clock in the morning, I customarily picked up my washing implements and opened the door, just as the sound of chopping firewood in the inner courtyard was loudly echoing through the house. The young man chopping the firewood, looking like someone not yet fully awake, muttered as if angered or agitated by something, "Hmph, I've seen it all now. Couldn't even sleep properly because of that darn thing." Given the iron rule that "males and females should not sit together after the age of seven (男女七歲不同席)," as I am already a maiden of 20 and he is a man over 20, asking him what he meant felt a bit forward. This wasn't just my perspective, but also because I sensed he blushed excessively whenever he saw a woman. At that moment, from inside, as if startled:
(Hey, you there? Did you get robbed in your room last night? Why all the commotion? Really, how could anyone sleep in the house next door? Gosh!) As if the grandmother's anger was justified,
"Ma'am, I've truly seen strange things in this world."
"Why, what happened?"
"While sleeping at night..." it's impossible for a baby's crying sound to come from our house, but I heard the loud cry of a newborn baby, didn't I? So, I immediately opened the door, held up the electric lamp, and looked towards the main gate; the gate was about half open, and the sound of a baby crying was coming from a black bundle."
"Oh my goodness. And then?"
"Well, I couldn't just go and look at it because I was scared."
"..."<97>
"So, having no choice, I abruptly picked up that black thing, and it cried even more. I woke up the mistress of the main house, and when we looked at the baby, it was a boy, and incredibly handsome, like a polished stone." "Oh, what a pitiful thing. It must be the doing of some female student slut or a widow slut again. Those wretched, disgraceful bitches. So, what did you do?" From this point, the grandmother, becoming furious, glared at the young man, who also spoke as if he had been angry too.
"But the birthdate was all written down, wasn't it? June 25th. But the female student slut must have been desperately poor; the baby's clothes were rags. Gosh, frankly, they were worse than a beggar's clothes on the Chongno crossroads."
"Did you report it to the police box?"
"Yes, I went to the police station immediately. A policeman came and looked, too. He investigated and took notes; they'll probably catch her today or tomorrow. Those damn bitches."
"How many children does the landlord have?"
"They probably sent it to our house knowing there's only one little girl who attends kindergarten. Anyway, they say raising a child that comes to you brings good fortune."
Whether it was the doing of a female student or a male student, since we did not witness it directly, let's treat it as a separate issue. But what exactly made the mother abandon this child? We must first consider this and cannot avoid analyzing it.
If the truest love in this world is a mother's love, where could the mother of this child find a resting place where her heart could be at ease after abandoning her child?
For those chased by the hardships of life, struggling for even one meal a day, another kind of love, unbearable for a mother, arises. This is the mother's sorrowful psychology of wanting to lay the child in the warm hearth corner of a somewhat wealthier home to be raised, rather than raising it herself with milk that doesn't even flow a single drop! This is a shuddering fact that one cannot even imagine unless one has experienced a household that has starved for a meal or two. Then, why did this mother have to make this child shiver for hours at the gate of a rich house? Who dares to refute that the ultimate cause is a sin arising from the irrationality of the social system?<98>

Because I am a Woman!
October Xth
I was on my way to Chedae Hospital (帝大病院) for urgent office matters. Just then, a classmate named B appeared, so we decided to cut through "Sutnaetk'ol" (likely a local path name) and were engrossed in conversation. From the other direction, two female students from X Myŏng Girls' School (X明女學校), identifiable by the white stripe below, came running, unable to speak and just gesturing backward. B and I, not knowing what was happening, stood near the two approaching girls to ask what was wrong. At that moment, a gentlemanly man, a man wearing a square cap, and another man dressed somewhat like a Russian youth in a rubashka (루바시까), these three people, came running after them like tiger hunters. B, standing beside me, and I also thought something serious might have happened, but seeing the two of us, their attitude changed completely. They started staggering this way and that, like people half-drunk, and approached us. The young man in the rubashka said, "Hey there, I have something to say to you."
He addressed the two female students. The shorter female student, summoning a bit more courage than the taller one, said,
"Why! Say it."
"Well, why do you run away when you see people? You should be studying at school, yet you wander around Changgyŏngwŏn like delinquent girls (부량녀) and then run off when you see men, making it seem like we deliberately followed you. Huh?"
However, as the female students tried to flee again, the young man in the suit said,
"Hey! I have a younger sister just like you, too. When sightseeing is preferred over studying, quite a few unfavorable things can happen. How about I become your older brother and look after you?"
Then the man in the square cap standing next to him also said,
"Hey man, what's the point of talking to such frivolous bitches (알부량자 계집년)? Let's just go. Huh."
As they were grabbing the female students' sleeves and making such remarks, about ten people from the neighborhood gathered around, as if it were a mutual affair. At this time, an XX Specialized School student (XX專학생), with five gold buttons lined up, raised his hand like an advertiser and proclaimed,
"We cannot just leave such female students be. They need to be taught a lesson. They should study. Otherwise, we felt the need to admonish and guide these female students who only wander around playing, which is why we detained and pursued them."
Perhaps moved by his speech, curses also began to emerge from the mouths of the people.
"That's why our daughters should be raised well and married off to the countryside. Letting them grow up unrestrained like that won't do. They're ill-bred."
"That's right. Acting like they know everything."
I believe the readers, with their sharp minds, will dissect and critique this issue, but the psychology that leads to venting the frustration of unfulfilled desires from playing around with women onto innocent female students, and even ordinary housewives, instilling the perception that "educating girls is throwing them away"—"I am a man, the strong one." This is the title men have claimed for themselves, while "the weak, the ignorant" is the title they attach to women. How much does this harm originating from them rebound back onto themselves? Shouldn't they think about this a bit more?
To give the most pertinent example here, consider the propertyless (무산자) who occupy one-third of this society. Although it is said they have already left the world called home and work shoulder-to-shoulder with men, how much wage disparity is created due to the stigma men have placed upon them? Rather than saying the author finds the conduct of this world's young men hateful (continued on page 103)<99> by writing this, it is written because such behavior is frequently committed. For those innocent ones who are just now trying to bud, a sense of inexplicable despair arises—"I am weak. Also, because I am a woman." This causes many ills, naturally leading them to sing a song of retreat. Lastly, women, isn't it right to powerfully overcome these trials of the transitional period? By learning how women like Rosa Luxemburg (로-자 룩센부룩) and Liebknecht (니푸쿠넷트), who are guiding us from the front, conducted so much activity above the heads of men, and how much they fought and led in the entire proletarian liberation movement, shouldn't we cultivate the strength to overcome this rotten morality created by men! <103>

**Summary**

The article consists of two diary entries by Song Kye-wŏl from October 1931. The first recounts the discovery of an abandoned baby boy at her home's gate, leading to reflections on the desperate poverty and societal judgment (specifically blaming female students or widows) that might force a mother into such an act, ultimately attributing the root cause to flaws in the social system. The second entry describes witnessing three young men publicly harassing two female students, justifying their actions as "guidance," while bystanders join in condemning the girls. The author criticizes this misogynistic behavior, the double standards imposed on women, the wage gap faced by working women, and the self-defeating notion that women are inherently weak, urging women to find strength and overcome societal limitations by looking to examples like Rosa Luxemburg and Karl Liebknecht. From a historical perspective, the article offers insight into the social conditions of colonial Korea, particularly the economic hardships, the precarious position of women (especially students and the poor), prevalent misogynistic attitudes even among educated men, and early feminist consciousness critiquing patriarchal structures and advocating for female empowerment, referencing international socialist figures.

여기에 대한 문제비판은 독자 여러분의 명석한 두뇌로 해부하여 주리라고 생각하지만 자긔들이 다리고 놀여다 그 욕심을 다 못채인 그 마음이 결국 순진한 녀학생들에게 일반 가정부인들에까지.
「공부식히는 것은 버린다. 이러한 인식을 너어주는 그 심리 나는 남자이요 강자로다.」 이것이 남자 그들의 자칭 마튼 명사요 「약자요 무지자」 그것이 여자의 명사로 부치여준 그들로 말미아마 이 페해가 그들 자신에까지 얼마나 미치는 것을 좀 더 생각하여줌이 엇덜는지?
가장 여기에 적절한 례를 하나 들어본다면 이 사회에 삼분지이를 점령한 무산자? 이들은 가정이란 세계를 이미 버서나 남자 그들과 한가지로 억개를 마추어 일을 한다고 하지마는 남자들이 씨워준 오명 그것으로 인하야 임금에도 얼마나 차를 두게 하엿는가? 필자가 이 세상 청년의 소행이 미웁다고 이(제103頁에)＜99＞ 말한다는 것보담도 이러한 행동이 자저짐으로써 지금 겨우 움틀려고 하는 순진한 그들에게는 나는 약자다. 또한 여자이니까.」 이러한 까닭모를 자포자긔가 생하게 됨으로써 자연 퇴진곡을 부르는 페가 만흠에 쓰는 것이다. 끗흐로 여성 여러분은 과도기의 이러한 시험을 힘잇게 익여 우리 압헤서 지도하고 잇는 로-자 룩센부룩과 니푸쿠넷트와 가튼 여성이 남자들의 머리 우에서 얼마나 만흔 활약을 하엿스며 이들은 전무산자해방운동에 잇서서 얼마나 만히 투쟁하엿스며 지도하고 잇섯다는 것을 알무로써 남성 그들이 만들어 노흔 이러한 썩은 도덕을 극복할 힘을 양성함이 올치 안을가!＜103＞
