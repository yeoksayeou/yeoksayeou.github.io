**Body Text Translation**
A Divorce Tragedy Seen in Court: The Husband is an Idiot, the Wife is a Peerless Beauty
						Lawyer Kim Pyŏngno

Three years ago, while visiting the Haeju District Court for a lawsuit, I happened to observe a divorce trial.
The plaintiff was a young man of about 23 or 24 years old; he was the husband. The defendant was a woman of about 20.

Now, I have seen many divorce trials unfold in court, but this particular one drew my interest because it had many points that differed from what I usually saw. That is, the plaintiff, the young man <33>, was so remarkably ill-favored in appearance as to be called extremely bizarre, and additionally short in stature, resembling an idiot (白痴). In contrast, his wife, speaking of her, possessed a beauty rare in the world, a slender figure, and eyes shining with intelligence, more than enough to convey her cleverness. The contrast between this couple was already dramatic, but another peculiar thing was that neither party had legal counsel.

The presiding judge's hearing began.
The husband, resembling an idiot, repetitively insisted on divorce.
The wife, in a clear tone, opposed the divorce.
Considering the usual practice in such situations, based on the assertions of both parties, who ought to have initiated the divorce claim first?

Feeling a somewhat morbid curiosity, I looked around at the general audience and the presiding judge handling this case.
Indeed! Did they perhaps share my thoughts? Faint smiles flickered across their faces.
The judge, perhaps trying to suppress a laugh, briefly rose from his chair, then settled back into his seat and
asked the plaintiff again for the reason why he wanted a divorce. The plaintiff, again mumbling something, kept repeating words whose point could not be grasped at all.

However, the woman, with a composed and clever demeanor, <34> vehemently opposed her husband's words.
The hearing ended thus, and without understanding the deep reason why they wanted a divorce, I left the place with perplexed thoughts.

It was several days after that. At a certain gathering where many people were present, I heard the story of this bizarre divorce case and finally understood the truth, receiving a deep impression.
The gist was as follows.
As mentioned above, the husband was extremely dull in addition to having a coarse appearance, while the wife, conversely, had a cheerful personality. As often happens to women with beautiful faces, she could not fend off the temptations of outside men, leading her to commit acts deviating from the path of matrimony (夫婦道). The forbidden path (禁斷의 길) the woman once stepped onto! Yes. That woman's feet gradually sank deeper into a secret world, one might call it the deviant path (邪道) of hidden sexual pleasure. Whether this was a justifiable attitude for the woman or not is beside the point here. The husband noticed and became aware of his wife's misconduct. With the disposition often peculiar to dull individuals, he severely threatened (脅威) his wife. However, the wife merely responded coolly.
The man, unable to restrain his burning anger, was stomping furiously when, even enlisting (使囑) the people around him, he thereupon filed for divorce (henceforth, original text missing - National Institute of Korean History) <35>

**Summary**

Lawyer Kim Pyŏngno recounts observing a strange divorce trial in Haeju around 1928. The husband, described as unattractive and dim-witted, was suing his exceptionally beautiful and intelligent wife for divorce, contrary to expectations. Both parties lacked legal representation. The husband struggled to articulate his reasons, while the wife confidently opposed the divorce. Puzzled, Kim later learned the truth: the wife had been unfaithful, and the seemingly dull husband, upon discovering her infidelity, reacted with intense anger and, after consulting others, initiated the lawsuit. This article offers a glimpse into marital conflicts and legal proceedings in colonial Korea, highlighting social perceptions tied to appearance versus reality, gender dynamics, and the accessibility of the legal system. Historians might find valuable the portrayal of social attitudes towards infidelity, the description of the characters reflecting certain stereotypes, and the procedural detail (or lack thereof, like missing lawyers) within the colonial court system.

法庭에서 본 離婚悲劇, 男便은 白痴, 안해는 絶世의 美人
						辯護士 金炳魯
나는 3년 전에 소송사건으로 海州지방법원에 가서 우연히 이혼재판을 방청하게 되엿다.
原告는 23, 4세 되는 청년인데 그가 남편이요 被告는 한 20세 쯤 되는 여자이엿다.
그런데 나는 법정에서 전개되는 이혼재판을 만히 보앗스나 특히 이 이혼재판만은 보통때에 보든 것과 달는 점이 만허서 나의 흥미를 끌엇다. 그것은 원고되는＜33＞ 청년이 그 용모가 지극히 기괴하다 할 만치 못생기엇고 거기다가 키는 짜르고 마치 백치 갓흔데 비하야 처 되는 그 여자로 말하면 세상에 드문 미모에다가 날신한 자태며 이지에 빗나는 눈빗 등이 그의 영리한 것을 말하고도 남는다. 이와 가튼 부부의 대조가 벌서 극적이거니와 또 한가지 특이한 것은 쌍방에 모도 변호인이 업는 것이다.
재판장의 심리는 시작된다.
백치 갓흔 남편은 重言復言 이혼를 주장한다.
처는 똑똑한 언조로 이혼을 반대하엿다.
이 쌍방의 주장이 常例로 보아서 이갓흔 경우에 누가 먼저 이혼을 주장해야 올흘 것인가?
나는 다소 엽기적 흥미까지 띄면서 일반 방청객과 이 사건을 담당 심리하는 재판장을 둘너 보앗다.
과연! 그들도 나와 갓흔 생각을 가젓던가. 그들의 얼골 우에는 빙글빙글 미소가 흐르고 잇섯다.
재판장은 우슴을 참으려 함인지 의자에서 잠간 이러낫다가 다시 자리를 곳처 안지며
원고에게 다시 엇재서 이혼하겟느냐 하는 그 이유를 뭇는다. 원고는 역시 우물쭈물 무엔지 도모지 要領을 잡을 수 업는 말을 작고 되푸리하여 직거린다.
그러나 그 여자난 의연히 영리한 태도로＜34＞ 남편의 말을 극력 반대한다.
심리는 이럭저럭 끗나고 나는 그들의 이혼을 할녀고 하는 깁흔 원인을 알지 못한 채로 의아한 생각을 가지고 그 자리를 떠낫다.
그 뒤 몃칠 뒤이다. 여럿이 모인 엇던 좌석에서 나는 기괴한 이 이혼사건 이약이를 듯고 비로소 그 진상을 알고 깁흔 인상을 어덧다.
그 大要는 이러하엿다.
우에 말한 바와 갓치 남편되는 사람은 용모가 추잡한데다가 극히 우둔하고 처는 그 반대로 경쾌한 성격에 흔이 얼골 어엽분 여자에게 닥처오는 외간 남자의 유혹을 물니찔 수 업서 夫婦道로서의 탈선적 행동을 짓게 하엿다. 그 여자가 한 번 듸듼 禁斷의 길! 그러타. 그 녀자는 남모르게 숨기는 성적 쾌락 邪道라고 할 비밀의 세계에 발은 점점 더 깁히 드러갓든 것이다. 이것이 그 여자로 하야금 과연 정당한 태도라 할 것인가? 안인가? 이것은 여기서 論外로 하고 남편되는 사람은 처의 이 비행을 눈치 채여 알엇다. 흔히 우둔한 자에 특유한 기질로서 처를 몹시 脅威하엿다. 그러나 처는 냉연히 대할 뿐.
사나히는 분노에 타오르는 감정을 抑制치 못하야 펄펄 뛰든 중 주위의 사람들 조차 使囑하야 그는 이에 이혼소(이하 원문 결 - 국편)＜35＞
