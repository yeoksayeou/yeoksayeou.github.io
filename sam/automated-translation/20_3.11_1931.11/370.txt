Article Title: 交叉點 (Crossroads/Intersection Point)

交叉點
(Crossroads/Intersection Point)

If anyone has a question they wish to ask someone, please submit it to our company. Our company's reporter will deliver the letter to the intended recipient, receive a reply, and publish it in this journal.
1. Use a postcard for the paper.
2. Make the meaning concise.
3. The content should relate to the public interest.

1. Korean Language Dictionary

I hear that a dictionary written for Koreans to learn Korean has not yet been published. It's truly regrettable!
However, I heard that the Korean Language Compilation Committee (조선어편찬회) was organized the year before last. How far has that project progressed, when will it be published, and what will the approximate price be?

2. Literary Magazine
Is there an independent literary magazine in Korea?
If there is, please inform me.
Kim Sŏngtaek, Kyŏngju

Reply
1. Establishing the scientific foundation of the national language is not an easy task. Since the establishment of the Korean Language Dictionary Compilation Committee (朝鮮語辭典編纂會), specialists in this field <66> have been continuously working hard, but we cannot yet state the publication date or the book's price.
When the book is ready to be published, there will be an advance announcement in the newspapers, so we hope you will wait until then.
Yi Kŭngno, Korean Language Dictionary Compilation Committee

2. In the past, magazines have appeared and disappeared, disappeared and appeared, but in recent years, more complete magazines have emerged, and we believe that the pitfalls of the past will be fewer in the future.
Currently, there are no purely literary magazines being published, but we hear that a place called Munye Wŏlgansa (文藝月刊社) plans to publish a magazine centered on literature and arts soon.
Samch'ŏlli Research Department Reply

To Mr. Kuk Kiryŏl
Sir, you were previously the Editorial Director at the Tonga Ilbo (東亞日報). What are you observing [doing] these days? Also, where is your residence? What is your age?
Kuk Hyŏngsik, Ich'ŏn

Political Department Director
Outside Seoul City, Puk-ri 38
Age 40
Respectfully, Kuk Kiryŏl, Tonga Ilbo

To Mr. Yang Chudong
Will the magazine Munye Kongnon (文藝公論) resume publication? Has it stopped? Also, <67> when will your poetry collection "Chosŏn Maekp'ak" (朝鮮脈搏 - Korean Pulse) be brought into the world [published]?
Yang, Sunch'ang..., Sunch'ang, Chŏnbuk

We intend to resume publication of "Munye Kongnon" when the time is right, but for the time being, due to various circumstances, it is effectively suspended. The poetry collection is still undecided, but I intend to publish it next spring by all means. As the person responsible, I feel extremely apologetic. I humbly ask that you wait for a while (姑竢).
Respectfully, Yang Chudong, P'yŏngyang

A couple of questions
1. Are P'yŏnghŏ, Sangsŏp, and Tongin novelists?
1. Is Mr. Yu Kwangnyŏl from Chŏlla Province?
1. Was Mr. No Chayŏng's article ["Love's Flame"]
a repeat of something? Or is it孔雀의 비애 (Kongjak ŭi piae - The Peacock's Sorrow)?
If this inquiry is not published in Samch'ŏlli, Mr. Sŏk is a cowardly gentleman. That's all.
Yi X-s, Cha'ya-dong, Seoul

1. It's rather pitiful that you don't even know P'yŏnghŏ, Sangsŏp, and Tongin are novelists.
2. Mr. Yu Kwangnyŏl is not from Chŏlla Province but from Kyŏnggi Province.
3. The piece Mr. No Chayŏng wrote in the previous issue of this magazine was a recently written essay.
Reply from Sŏk Pyŏngjŏng (Stone Soldier)

Provincial Governor
Among the 13 provinces of Korea, how many provincial governors are Korean, and <68> what are their names? In Korea's 218 counties, how many county magistrates are Korean? Please let me know in detail.
An Tŏkhu, Nagoya

Among the 13 governor positions, five are offered to Koreans. They are Kim Sŏgyu of Kyŏngbuk, Hong Sŭnggyun of Ch'ungbuk, Namgung Yŏng of Kangwŏn, and Han Kyubok of Hwanghae. The number of county magistrates is still under investigation.
Reply from Ch'o Pyŏngjŏng (Grass Soldier)

Korean History
I wish to learn a bit about our history. Please introduce the most suitable books for us! Where is the publisher, who is the author, and what is the fixed price?
An Sŭngnyŏl, Kirim (吉林)

I recommend Mr. Yi Sŏn'gŭn's "Choegŭn Ses회사" (最近世史 - Recent History). The book price is 60 chŏn. Orders can also be fulfilled (酬應) through our company.
Reply from Ch'o Pyŏngjŏng (Grass Soldier)

Receive my greetings. How are you? Dust flies gently, and if one is slightly careless, collisions occur. What kind of thing is a "good Korean" (선량な 鮮人)? What is the work they do? Please teach me in some detail. I wish it.
Ŭngjogi, An Tong-hyŏn (安東縣)

Only those who know know about the "good Korean" (선량な 鮮人), and considering the nature of the person asking, please try to guess and understand. Goodness gracious。
Reply from Hwa Pyŏngjŏng (Fire Soldier) <69>

To Mr. Chŏng Segwŏn
The Korean Products Promotion Association (朝鮮物産獎勵會), which advocates for the use of our native products, has carried on for several years (幾個 星霜) through the propaganda of its fervent committee members. However, in the contributor's humble opinion, if you go out onto the streets, not even 1 in 100,000 people wears purely Korean-made clothes. Have you perhaps been promoting foreign goods all this time? How about having the families of the committee members first promote the use of domestic products?
Pak Hanik, Ŭiju

The Korean Products Promotion Association has not promoted foreign goods, but the power of certain forces promoting foreign goods is stronger than our power promoting Korean products, which is why the results of the Korean Products Promotion Association have been weak. However, both spiritually and practically, the achievements of the Korean Products Promotion Association cannot be ignored. We will take note of your suggestion to have the committee members' families use Korean products first as reference and strive to implement it. First of all (爲先), we ask that you, the inquirer, become a member of the promotion association to help invigorate the association and spur on (鞭撻) the executives.
Respectfully, Chŏng Segwŏn, Korean Products Promotion Association

Yŏ Unhyŏng
Reading "Brother Yŏ Unhyŏng in Taejŏn Prison" published in the last September issue, it says he is weaving fishing nets (魚網) in prison. I cannot help but feel a certain emotion.
So, the claim that his treatment in prison is different from other prisoners is nothing more than groundless talk (野說). Right?
And how much longer until his release date?
Ch'oe Yŏngha, Kyŏngju <70>

He receives no special treatment different from other prisoners whatsoever and is just weaving nets. His release date is December of next year.
Reply from his younger brother, Yŏ Unhong

To Teacher Yi Kwangsu
Sir, I have an interest in literature but worry because I don't know its composition method (其 作法). Please teach me somehow the method for composing Sijo (時調作法).
Yi Tonghwa, Ŭiju, P'yŏngbuk

(Reply) A book titled "Sijo Composition Method" has not yet been published. If you thoroughly read many sijo composed by others, you will naturally grasp it. Receiving guidance from seniors is good, but reading sijo seems to be the best method.
Respectfully, Yi Kwangsu

To Teacher Chŏng Sŏkt'ae
Sir, because my nose (코鼻) is flat and misshapen (惡形), I always lived in pessimism. Then, in a recent newspaper, I saw an advertisement for a nose-raising device (隆鼻器) from the Fujisawa Research Institute (藤澤硏究所) in Tokyo city. Sir, can that nose-raising device achieve the goal of perfectly raising the nose (隆鼻) for someone like me, at home? If that nose-raising device has no effect, are there none elsewhere?
Pak Chun, Kyŏngsŏng

To put it simply, I disapprove of you ordering it. The reason, of course, is that it's impossible for a home treatment device to have any effect, but injecting a certain liquid into the bridge of the nose (鼻背) to raise it is the safest way, yet even that <71> shape can change in hot weather or when entering a hot bath. Therefore, if it's not excessively unsightly, please just live with it as it is.
Respectfully, Chŏng Sŏkt'ae

Our writings in China
Is it possible to subscribe within Korea to Korean books, magazines, and newspapers published in places like Shanghai (上海), China (中國)?
Yi Inho, Pukch'on

It seems that most will probably be impossible to purchase. Their contents are likely not "mild" (軟) enough to be allowed in openly.
Reply from Ch'o Pyŏngjŏng (Grass Soldier)

After the Singanhoe dissolution

Please inform us of the recent activities of the Dissolution Faction (解消派) since the Singanhoe (新幹會) was dissolved...
Pukchae, Anju

All subsequent activities of the Dissolution Faction (解消派) are being prohibited by the police, so I myself do not know.
Reply from Sŏk Pyŏngjŏng (Stone Soldier)

Mr. Ch'oe Namsŏn
There has been no recent news from Mr. Ch'oe Namsŏn, who is famous as a historian (史家). What is he doing, and where is he working?
Chŏng Sunil, Tongsong-dong, Chinyup, Kyŏngnam

It is said that the Lay Scholar Yuktang (六堂居士), at the Central Council's Historical Materials Compilation Committee (中樞院史料編纂會), eating (食于) the generous government salary (至厚之官祿而), is diligently (日夜孜孜) compiling (編成于) the "History of Korea" (朝鮮史), Your Excellency.
Reply from Mok Pyŏngjŏng (Wood Soldier) <72>

**Summary**

This article from the November 1931 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine features a Q&A section titled "Crossroads" (交叉點), where readers pose questions to the magazine or specific public figures, and responses are published. The inquiries cover a wide range of contemporary concerns: the progress of the Korean Language Dictionary project, the availability of literary magazines, the whereabouts and activities of intellectuals like Kuk Kiryŏl and Yang Chudong, clarification on literary figures and works, details about colonial administration (number of Korean provincial governors), recommendations for Korean history books, the meaning of the term "good Korean" (likely referring to collaborators or assimilated individuals), the effectiveness of the Korean Products Promotion Association, the prison conditions and release date of nationalist leader Yŏ Unhyŏng, advice on writing traditional *sijo* poetry, opinions on cosmetic surgery devices, the possibility of obtaining Korean publications from China, the activities of the Singanhoe dissolution faction, and the current work of historian Ch'oe Namsŏn. The responses provide direct answers (or sometimes evasive ones, particularly regarding sensitive political topics or censored materials) from the individuals addressed or from the magazine's staff under pseudonyms like Sŏk Pyŏngjŏng (Stone Soldier).

For historians, this Q&A offers valuable insights into the everyday concerns, cultural interests, political landscape, and information networks of Koreans during the colonial period. It reveals anxieties about cultural preservation (language dictionary, native products), engagement with literature and history, curiosity about public figures, the realities of colonial governance (limited Korean representation, imprisonment of activists like Yŏ Unhyŏng), censorship (publications from China, activities of political groups), and even personal issues like cosmetic surgery. The cautious or indirect nature of some answers also hints at the constraints imposed by Japanese colonial rule. The mention of Ch'oe Namsŏn working for the colonial government's history compilation project is also historically significant.

1. 朝鮮語辭典

조선 사람이 조선말을 배우고저 쓴 사전은 아즉 출판되지 못하얏다고 그러지요. 매우 유감함니다 그려!
그러나 재작년도에 조선어편찬회가 조직되엿다드니 그 사업은 얼마나 나아갓스며 출판은 何時 그러고 가격은 얼마나 될 모양임니까?

2. 문예잡지
조선에 문예잡지란 독립된 것이 잇슴니까?
잇삽거든 下敎하시요.
慶州 金成澤

答
1. 국어의 과학적 기초를 세움은 그리 용이한 일이 아님니다. 朝鮮語辭典編纂會가 성립된 이후로 斯界의 전문학자들이＜66＞ 계속 노력한는 중이나 아즉은 출판시기와 책 가격을 말할 수는 업슴니다.
책이 나게 되는 때에는 미리 신문에 광고가 될 터이니 그때까지 기다리시궀°를 바람니다.
朝鮮語辭典編纂會 李克魯

2. 과거에 잡지가 낫다가 업서지고 업서젓다가 나고 하여 왓섯는데 근년에 와서는 완전한 잡지가 나와 今後로는 과거와 가튼 폐단이 적을 줄 밋슴니다.
현재엔 발행하여 나가는 純문학잡지가 업고 최근 文藝月刊社라는 곳에서 문예중심잡지를 발행한다고 함니다.
