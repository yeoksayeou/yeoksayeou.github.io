**Body Text Translation:**
Legal Consultation for Women
If any of you ladies have questions about the law you don't understand, please do not hesitate to ask immediately. Samchŏlli-sa will obtain answers from renowned contemporary legal experts and lawyers and publish them here.

Regarding Having an Abortion
My body is extremely weak, and I happen to be pregnant. If I want to abort this pregnancy, what are the legal implications?
Kim Okpun, P'yŏngyang

Abortion and Legal Relations
Lawyer Yi Ch'anghwi
Abortion has long been regarded as a sin according to human morality, and furthermore, it has been completely prohibited based on policies for population growth.<100>
Even today, strict legal provisions regarding abortion are stipulated in the criminal code.
However, it is true that the perception has become considerably weaker compared to the past. How much do all the methods related to abortion, frequently appearing in the newspaper and society pages recently, and all the detective-story-like facts surrounding them, startle the public eye?
Nevertheless, when we see such newspaper articles, why do we tend to simply overlook them as ordinary events occurring in some corner of the wider society?
This is because, as the trends of the times change, the standards of sexual morality are shifting, and due to changes in social circumstances, we see the fact that nowadays, the impoverished cannot help but practice birth control.
However, statistically examining crimes related to abortion reveals that many stem from illicit sexual relationships, and while some are due to illness or poverty, many abortions are also performed as a result of the inner sexual lives of young widows, the negative consequences of sexual dalliance among unmarried individuals, and adultery by married women.
Of course, when life is endangered due to illness, or in cases of extraordinary difficult childbirth where the mother's life is at risk, it falls within the scope permitted by law for a doctor to perform an abortion taking responsibility. However, if a pregnant woman secretly uses drugs (continued in upper column) to induce an abortion, causes an expectant mother to have an abortion, or pays money to a doctor, midwife, or pharmacist to perform an abortion, these all constitute the crime of abortion. Please be careful regarding all these points. (Responsibility for text lies with the reporter)<101>
<100-101>

**Summary**

This article presents a question from a reader, Kim Okpun from P'yŏngyang, asking about the legality of abortion due to her weak health, and a response from lawyer Yi Ch'anghwi. Yi explains that abortion is illegal under the criminal code, rooted in traditional morality and population policies. However, he notes that societal views are changing, evidenced by frequent media reports and the perceived necessity of birth control among the poor. While medically necessary abortions performed by doctors are legal exceptions, self-induced abortions or those performed for payment by doctors, midwives, or pharmacists are criminal acts, often stemming from socially disapproved situations like extramarital affairs or premarital sex. The lawyer warns readers to be aware of the legal prohibitions. For historians, the article is interesting as it reveals the tension between established law (likely based on the Japanese legal system imposed on colonial Korea) and changing social mores regarding sexuality and family planning in the 1930s. It highlights the social pressures, including poverty and shifting moral standards, that led women to seek illegal abortions despite the legal risks, offering a glimpse into women's health issues and social conditions during the colonial period.

女人法律相談
여러분께서 법률이상에 몰을 점이 잇서 무르실 것 잇스면 조곰도 주저하지 마시고 곳 무러주시압. 삼천리사에서는 당대에 일홈 잇는 법률가 변호사에게서 대답을 밧어서 여기에 실어드리겟슴니다.

墮胎를 하자면
저는 몸이 몹시 허약하온 바 마츰 임신하고 잇는데 이를 타태하자면 법률상 엇더한 관계가 잇슴니까.
平壤 金玉粉

墮胎와 法律關係
辯護士 李昌輝
타태는 자고로 인류도덕상 죄악인 듯이 녁일 뿐더러 국민증가정책상 아조 금하여 왓든 것임니다.＜100＞
금일에 잇서서도 타태에 관하여 형법상 준엄한 법률조문이 규정되여 잇슴니다.
그러나 그 관념이 과거에 비하야 퍽 희박해진 것이 사실이니 近者에 瀕瀕히 신문, 사회면에 나타나는 타태에 관한 모-든 방법과 이것을 싸고도는 모든 탐정소설적 사실이 어떠캐나 만히 세인의 이목을 놀내임니까?
그러컨만 우리는 이와 가튼 신문기사를 볼 때 한갓 넓은 사회 한구통이에서 발생되는 예사로운 사실로 그냥 간과하려 함은 무슨 까닭인가?
이것은 시대사조가 변천됨에 따라서 성도덕의 표준이 달너저감과 사회사정의 변환으로 말매아머서 근래에는 빈궁자들이 산아제한을 하지 아니치 못하는 그러한 사실이 잇게 되는 것을 보는 까닭임니다.
그러나 타태에 관한 범죄를 통계적으로 고찰한다면 성적으로 不義한 관계에서 기인된 것이 만코 질병, 빈궁 등으로 기인된 것이 잇스나 젊은 미망인의 내면 성생활의 결과, 미혼자들의 성적 유희의 餘弊와 유부녀의 간통 등―으로도 타태를 감행하는 일이 만슴니다.
물론 질병으로 인하야 생명에 관계되는 때나 産時 비상한 난산으로 산모의 생명이 위급할 경우에 의사가 책임지고 타태를 시키는 것은 법률이 허용하는 범위에 속하는 것이나 임신한 부녀가 약품을(상단으로 계속) 사용하야 비밀히 타태를 하거나 임부로 하야금 타태케 하거나 의사나 산파, 약제사 등을 금전을 주고 타태를 하는 등 모다 타태죄가 구성되는 것이니 이러한 모든 점은 주의하십시요.(文責在記者)＜101＞
