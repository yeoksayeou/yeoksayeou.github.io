Urgent Notice

Due to the numerous requests from readers on the Peninsula (半島), starting this month, our Company (本社) has established a Book Agency Department. Please order many booklets (册子). (Payment is strictly in advance---Cash on delivery (代金引換) is refused).

◇

Occasionally, people from the provinces (地方) ask us to send magazines (雜誌) via cash on delivery. Please be aware that magazines are not sent unless paid for in advance (先金).

◇

For monthly subscribers (月定讀者), when your advance payment is depleted (盡하면), we will stamp it with a "Advance Payment Depleted (先金盡)" stamp (圖章). Please continue to send money (送金).

Samch'ŏlli Company respectfully informs (三千里社白) <57>

**Summary**

This notice from the Samch'ŏlli Company announces the establishment of a Book Agency Department to meet reader demand for various books, emphasizing that all orders require advance payment and cash on delivery is not accepted. It also reminds magazine subscribers, especially those in the provinces, that magazines are only sent upon prepayment and that a stamp will indicate when their subscription payment needs renewal. From a historical perspective, this brief notice illuminates the practical business operations of a Korean magazine publisher during the colonial period, highlighting the challenges of distribution (especially to provincial areas) and payment collection, reflecting the developing print market and reader engagement despite colonial constraints. The insistence on prepayment might suggest difficulties with reliable payment or postal systems for COD.

急告
半島讀者諸氏의 要望이 만삽기에 이달부터 本社는 書籍代理部를 設置하엿스니 무슨 册子든지 만히 主文하여주시암(代金은 一切先金---代金引換은 謝絶함니다)
◇
각금 地方에서 雜誌를 代金引換으로 보내달라고 함니다. 雜誌는 先金이 아니면 發送치안사오니 그리아시압.
◇
月定讀者로 先金이 盡하면 先金盡이란 圖章을 사오니 繼續送金하여주시암.
三千里社白＜57＞
