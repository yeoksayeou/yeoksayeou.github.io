**Body Text Translation**

Conditions of Beauty, The Type of Woman I Want to Feature in My Novel (1)
						Ch’oe Tok-kyŏn

I intend to make such a woman the protagonist of a novel I will write in the future, so my well-connected elder brother, as you travel here and there, if you happen to find a model anywhere, please be sure to find just one for me.

1. Physical Beauty

1. Height 5 ch'ŏk 2 ch'on to 3 ch'on [approx. 157-160cm]
2. Weight 12 kwan 50 mun [approx. 45.2kg]
—In any case, a woman so slender that her weight seems very light compared to her height, possessing a slight consumptive quality like "Marguerite Gautier" (춘희) or "Namiko" (Namiko (德富蘆花), the heroine of *Hototogisu*).
3. A woman whose face and all other beauty would be specially selected without discount for "Samch'ŏlli's Finest Beauty" (삼천리의 일색)
—For that standard, I once again borrow the efforts of the Samch'ŏlli publisher and the honorable teachers who made the selections.—
(Hereafter abbreviated - original text)—

2. Spiritual Beauty

1. A silent nature that transcends melancholy
—For example, a woman who loves him until the moment of death without saying the words "I love you."—
2. Lively behavior that does not seem frivolous.
3. A woman who may weep for others but does not know how to shed tears for herself.
4. An affectionate woman removed from sentimentality.
5. A woman who may regret her own mistakes but does not know despair.
6. A woman who seems weak but is strong, rather than a woman who pretends to be strong but is weak.
7. A woman whose mind is more mature than her body
—Ah, how many older sisters there are in this world whose minds run wild, younger than their bodies!)
8. A woman possessing the same kind of ideals as the daughter in "Pulgun Sarang" (붉은 사랑) and the chastity in practice like Ch'unhyang's (춘향).
9. Also, a woman who does not become curious even after hearing a story.
(Hereafter abbreviated - original text)<99>

**Summary**

The author, Ch’oe Tok-kyŏn, outlines the physical and spiritual characteristics of the ideal woman he wishes to portray as the protagonist in his future novel. Physically, she should be tall and very slender, almost frail, evoking heroines like Marguerite Gautier and Namiko, and beautiful enough to be selected for the magazine's beauty feature. Spiritually, she should be stoic, silently devoted, active yet composed, selfless, resilient against despair, internally strong despite appearing weak, mentally mature, chaste like Ch'unhyang, and lacking in curiosity. From a historical perspective, this article reveals specific aesthetic ideals prevalent in 1930s Korea, blending influences from Western (Marguerite Gautier) and Japanese (Namiko) literature with traditional Korean figures (Ch'unhyang). Historians might find the explicit preference for a 'consumptive' physique, the detailed list of desired inner traits emphasizing silent suffering and unwavering chastity, and the juxtaposition of foreign and native female archetypes particularly interesting as reflections of colonial-era cultural negotiations and gender expectations.

美의 條件, 내 小說에 올니고 십흔 女性의 타입(1)

나는 이러한 여자를 장차 쓸 나의 소설의 주인공을 삼으려는데 발 넓으신 형께서 이리저리 다니다가 어데서든지 그 모델을 하나만 꼭 구해주시구려.

1. 肉體美

1. 신장 5척2촌 내지 3촌
2. 체중 12관50문
―엇젯든 신장에 비하야 체중이 매우 가벼우리만치 후리후리하야 「마루그리트.코쳬」(춘희)나 「나미꼬」(德富蘆花)의 不如婦의 여주인공)처럼 폐병 소질을 약간 가진 여자
3. 얼골 其外의 모든 미는 「삼천리의 일색」에 할인 업시 특선될 여자
―그 표준은 삼천리 주간과 考選하신 諸선생께 또 한 번 그 수고름 빔니다.―
(이하 략-원문)―

2. 精神美

1. 우울을 초월한 침묵성
―一例를 든다면 「나는 당신을 사랑하여요」 라는 말을 하지 안코 죽는 순간까지 그를 사랑하는 여자―
2. 경솔해보이지 안는 활발한 행동
3. 남을 위하야는 울지언정 자기를 위하야는 눈물을 흘닐 줄 모르는 여자
4. 쎈치멘탈에서 버서난 다정한 여자
5. 자기과오를 뉘웃치기는 할지언정 실망을 모르는 여자
6. 굿세인척 하고 약한 여자보다 약해보이면서 굿세인 여자
7. 육체보다 마음이 숙성한 여자
―아아 이 세상에는 몸보다 마음이 젊어서 날뛰는 누님들이 그 얼마나 만흔가?)
8. 「붉은 사랑」에 나오는 그 딸가튼 관념과 춘향이의 실행가튼 정조를 가진 여자
9. 또 이야기를 듯고도 호기심을 가지지 안는 여자.
(이하 략-원문)<99>
