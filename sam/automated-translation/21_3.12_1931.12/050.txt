Article Title: 三千里 「壁新聞」 (제5호), 露西亞의 新聞部數 (Samch'ŏlli "Wall Newspaper" (No. 5), Newspaper Circulation Figures in Russia)
Author: None specified
Article Type: 소식 (News/Information)

While the circulation figures of newspapers within Chosŏn, such as the Chosŏn Ilbo, Tong-a [Ilbo], Chungang [Ilbo], Ōsaka Mainichi, and Kyŏngsŏng Ilbo, are something the public very much wants to know, one also desires to know the circulation figures for all newspapers and magazines in Russia (露西亞), which talks about eradicating illiteracy, has established a national publishing house, and is making rapid strides in the New Culture Movement.

First, looking at the newspapers:
"Pravda" (뿌라부다) has 2 million copies, surpassing 100,000 copies in the city of Leningrad (레닌그라드市) alone, and the "Izvestia" (이즈우에스쟈-) newspaper has 1.8 million copies.
While the above are central newspapers, it is said that provincial newspapers also generally print and sell 40,000-50,000 copies each, showing that the fervor for newspaper readership is fierce. Next, looking at magazine publications, among publications, those holding primary significance (第一義的 의의) at the current stage are mainly literature concerning the mass propaganda (푸로파칸다) of Marxist-Leninist theory. Within this year alone, it is said that Lenin's (레-닌) works exceeded 5 million copies. Calculating the total since the revolution, it is said that over 75 million copies of Lenin's writings alone have been sold. Furthermore, regarding textbooks related to the Party, whereas 48 types totaling 4 million copies were published in 1930, this year it seems they will surpass 120 types and 18 million copies. <11>

**Summary**

This short news piece from 1931 contrasts the opaque circulation numbers of major newspapers in colonial Korea (including Korean and Japanese papers) with the reportedly massive circulation figures of newspapers and political literature in Soviet Russia. It highlights the high readership of papers like *Pravda* and *Izvestia* and the extensive publication of works by Lenin and other Marxist-Leninist texts, emphasizing the Soviet focus on literacy and mass propaganda. For historians, the article offers a glimpse into Korean interest in the Soviet Union as an alternative model of development and mass mobilization during the colonial period. It also reflects contemporary concerns about media access and the scale of state-sponsored publishing and ideological dissemination.

三千里 「壁新聞」 (제5호), 露西亞의 新聞部數
조선 안에 잇는 朝鮮日報나 東亞, 中央, 大阪每日, 京城日報 등의 발행부수는 세간에서 퍽으나 알고저 하는 바이지만 문맹타파를 운운하고 국립출판소를 창설하여 新文化運動에 약진하고 잇는 露西亞의 모든 신문잡지의 발행부수도 알고 십다.

몬저 신문을 보면
「뿌라부다」가 200만부로 레닌그라드市에만 10만부 이상을 돌파하고 잇스며 「이즈우에스쟈-」지가 180만부.
이상은 중앙신문이지만 지방신문도 대개 4,5만부 식은 인쇄발매된다 하니 신문구독열이 맹렬한 것을 알만하며 그 다음 잡지출판물를 보면 출판물 중 현단계에 잇서 第一義的 의의를 가진 것은 맑쓰, 레-린주의 이론의 대중적 푸로파칸다에 관한 문헌이 주로 금년 안에 잇서 레-닌 저작물만 500만부를 초과하엿다 하며 이것을 혁명 후 전통계를 따저보면 레-닌 저서만 7,500만부 이상이 팔니엇다고 한다. 또 당에 관계하는 교과서는 1930년에 48종, 400만부를 출판하엿든 것이 금년에는 120종 1,800만부를 돌파할 모양이라 한다 <11>
