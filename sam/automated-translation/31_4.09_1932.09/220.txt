The Tragic Current Situation of Pongch'ŏn Refugees
						By Ch'oe Ch'iŏk, Residing in Pongch'ŏn

Since last July until today, the astonishing number of accommodated fellow refugees has reached 3,700 to 3,800. Unexpectedly, adding frost to snow, they have contracted cholera (호렬자), and the average number of daily deaths exceeds 15. This situation is truly unbearable to witness (目不人見), and it's so appalling that words fail to describe the current state. Although the authorities provided vaccinations in the meantime, it seems that too was an unavoidable circumstance, and deaths continue daily for a long time.

The current state of survival for these refugees is that each person is given two *hop* of millet, calculated for five days, meaning one *sŭng* per person for five days. For vegetables, they are given 10 *chŏn* worth for five days, which is insufficient, and it is certain that they have no leisure to care for their own sick family members.

Beyond this, another unspeakable situation is that they have erected reed mat shelters (삿틀막) in the yard. Others may sleep in houses, but seeing those who cut grass because they have no mats (삿) and weave them to sleep on, as fellow members of our people (동족), I cannot suppress my burning affection and sorrowful tears.

I convey this unjust and tragic situation to the 30 million brothers and sisters residing at home and abroad (海內海外).<67>

**Summary**

This article by Ch'oe Ch'iŏk describes the dire conditions of 3,700-3,800 Korean refugees accommodated in Pongch'ŏn (Fêngt'ien/Shenyang) as of September 1932. A cholera outbreak is claiming over 15 lives daily despite vaccinations. Refugees survive on minimal rations of millet and a small amount of money for vegetables, lacking the means to care for the sick. Many live in crude reed mat shelters or sleep on woven grass due to a lack of proper mats. The author expresses deep sorrow and appeals to Koreans everywhere for awareness. Historically, the article highlights the suffering of Korean migrants in Manchuria amidst the turmoil following the Manchurian Incident (1931), revealing the inadequate relief provided and the strong sense of ethnic solidarity and shared hardship felt by Koreans during the colonial period.

慘憺한 奉天避難民의 現狀
						在奉天 崔致億
去7월 이후로 今日까지 수용된 피난동포은 3천7,8백명의 놀나운 수자 중 의외에 설상가상으로 호렬자에 글이여 평균 매일에 사망자가 15인 이상되니 이것이야 참으로 目不人見이고 참 긔막히여 말못할 헌상이다. 그 간에 당국으로부터 예방주사도 주엇지만은 그도 역시 할 수 업는 시정이라구만 둔대도 오래두고 사망자는 연일 게속되는 중이다. 그들 피란민에 연명하는 현상은 좁살 두 홉식 1인에게 주고 즉 5일간식 삭정하여 一升式 주는대 채소는 5일간 金 10전식 주니 넉넉지 못하고 어느 결을에 자기 가족 병자 구호할 여가가 업난 것은 틀임업다. 그 외에도 말못할 현상은 마당에다가 삿틀막을 치고 잇고 그 외에는 혹은 집에 잠을 잔다 하지마는 삿도 업시 풀을 비여다가 삿을 삼고 자는 것을 볼때에 이것이야 참으로 우리 동족이 되엿다가 불갓흔 애정과 쓸푼 눌물노 금치못하겟나이다. 이 억울한 참상을 海內海外에 게신 3천만 동포 형데자매님게 전하나이다.<67>
