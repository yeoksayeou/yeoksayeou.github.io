Urgent Notice

The upcoming October issue will be published as a special edition commemorating the 4th anniversary of this magazine's founding, so please look forward to this luxurious, splendid, profound, and fresh issue! <60>

**Summary**

This short notice announces that the upcoming October issue of the magazine 'Samch'ŏlli' will be a special edition celebrating its 4th anniversary. It encourages readers to anticipate a high-quality issue described as luxurious, splendid, profound, and fresh. For historians, this notice reflects typical magazine promotional practices of the era and highlights how publications like 'Samch'ŏlli' presented themselves and sought reader engagement during the Japanese colonial period, emphasizing their longevity and quality even within the constraints of the time.
