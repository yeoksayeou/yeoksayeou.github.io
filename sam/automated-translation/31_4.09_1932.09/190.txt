Literary World Miscellany

Kim Pok-chin (金復鎭), a graduate of the Tokyo Art School (東京美術學校) and the peninsula's sole sculptor, was imprisoned four years ago due to the XX Party Incident (XX당 사건). Since then, he has always been in good health, keeping busy with reading and net-making. Recent news indicates he is expected to be released upon completion of his sentence in just a few months, which is joyous news.

Playwright Kim Yŏng-p'al (金永八) recently quit the Kyŏngsŏng Broadcasting Station (京城放送局) and went to Manchuria (滿洲). What he will do there remains to be seen, but won't the peninsula's theatrical circles, which should be busy preparing for autumn, become even more desolate having lost Mr. Kim?

Among literary figures with experience of divorce are Messrs. Yi Kwang-su (李光洙), Kim Tong-in (金東仁), Sim Hun (沈薰), Kim Il-yŏp (金一葉), Kim Yŏng-p'al (金永八), the late Ch'oe Sŏ-hae (故崔曙海), and Ch'oe Sŭng-il (崔承一). Among them, the late Sŏ-hae (曙海) married four times during his 32-year life due to divorce and bereavement.

Dancer Ch'oe Sŭng-hŭi (崔承喜) gave birth to a beautiful baby girl about 20 days ago. This year<64> seems to be a bumper year for lovely children; among those involved in the arts, whenever someone gives birth, it's a beautiful child. For example, Mr. Sim Hun (沈薰) and Mr. Kim Yu-yŏng (金幽影) are both overjoyed with their lovely children.<65>

**Summary**

This article compiles short news items from the Korean literary and arts scene in September 1932. It reports on the anticipated prison release of sculptor Kim Pok-chin, imprisoned for political reasons; the departure of playwright Kim Yŏng-p'al to Manchuria after leaving the Kyŏngsŏng Broadcasting Station; lists prominent literary figures who have divorced, noting Ch'oe Sŏ-hae's multiple marriages; and celebrates the recent birth of children to dancer Ch'oe Sŭng-hŭi, writer Sim Hun, and film director Kim Yu-yŏng. From a historical perspective, the article is interesting for its casual mention of political imprisonment (Kim Pok-chin and the "XX Party Incident"), the noted migration of a cultural figure (Kim Yŏng-p'al) to Manchuria (a common destination during this period), and the glimpse into the personal lives (divorce, births) of major figures in colonial Korean culture.

文壇雜話
東京미술학교 出로 半島 유일의 조각가이든 金復鎭은 XX당 사건으로 4년 전에 入監 이래 항상 건강하여 독서와 그물뜨기에 분주하다더니 요지음 들니는 소식에 이제 멋 달 안니잇스면 滿期出獄 이 되여 나오리라하니 깁분 일이다.

극자가 金永八씨는 얼마 전에 京城방송국을 그만두고 滿洲로 갓다. 가서 무엇을 할는지 그것은 아직 두고 보아야 할 일이지만 가을의 준비에 분주하여야 할 半島의 극단은 氏를 일코 더욱 한산하여지지 안을가.

문인들로 이혼한 경험이 잇는 이에 李光洙, 金東仁, 沈薰, 金一葉, 金永八, 故崔曙海, 崔承一,의 諸氏가 잇다, 그 중에도 故 曙海는 이혼과 사별로 32년의 일생동안에 부인을 네 번 마지하엿다.

무용가 崔承喜여사가 20여일 간에 玉童女을 나엿다. 今年에는 玉童자녀 풍년모양으로<64> 예술에 관계한 이로 해산만 하면 玉童자녀이다. 曰 沈薰씨 曰 金幽影씨 모다가 玉童자녀로 滿悅.<65>
