A Person Who Enjoys the Character 閉 (Closed)
						By I Ŭng-su (李應洙)

I am a person who enjoys the character 閉 (closed).
Yesterday evening too, I went out to the seashore
And went before the firmly closed villa of some foreigner
And enjoyed the inner world (內界) of the character 閉 (closed) before coming back.
Though how much that house tempted me, wanting me to enter,
I merely toyed a couple of times with my hand that touched the door latch (문걸새)
And just enjoyed the closed-off interior before coming back.

I am a person who enjoys the character 封 (seal).
Carefully sealing away the fidelity (정조) of a beloved person,
Enduring one month, enduring two months, within that process
I am a person who tastes a curious pleasure.
Leaving a letter (글월) from my beloved sealed as it is,
Putting it deep inside my small chest (괴),
I make my soul, anxiously, anxiously,
Linger around that chest for 2 or 3 days.
I am a strange person who enjoys the character 封 (seal). ―End―<91>

**Summary**

The poem describes a speaker who derives a peculiar pleasure from things that are closed off or sealed – symbolized by the Hanja characters 閉 (closed) and 封 (seal). The speaker reflects on enjoying the imagined interior of a closed villa without entering and delaying the gratification of opening a sealed letter from a loved one, finding satisfaction in the anticipation and the contained nature of things. For historians, the poem offers insight into an aesthetic of introversion or perhaps suppressed desire during the colonial period. The act of finding pleasure in the "closed" might reflect a coping mechanism or a turning inward in response to external constraints. The specific mention of a "foreigner's" closed villa subtly points to the presence and perhaps the inaccessible nature of foreign elements within Korean society at the time.

다들 閉字를 亨樂하는 사람
						李應洙
나는 다들 閉字를 亨樂하는 사람이외다.
어제 석양에도 해변에 나갓다가
엇던 외국인의 굿이 다친 별장 압헤 가 서서
다들 閉字의 內界를 亨樂하다 왓슴니다.
그 집이 얼마나 내게 들어가기 십흔 유혹을 주엇스릿가만
나는 다만 문걸새에 대엿든 내 손을 두어번 히롱하여보고
그만 닷어진 속만을 亨樂하고 왓슴니다.

나는 봉할 封字를 亨樂하는 사람이외다.
사랑하는 사람의 정조를 고이 봉해두고
한 달 참고 두 달 참어가는 속에서
야릇한 즐거움을 맛보는 사람이며
님에게서 온 글월을 봉한채로
나의 조그만 괴속에다 깁숙이 너어두고는
나의 영혼으로 하여금 안탑갑게 안탑갑게
2,3日이나 그 괴의 주위을 감돌게 하는
封할 封字를 亨樂하는 괴기한 사람이외다. ―끗―<91>
