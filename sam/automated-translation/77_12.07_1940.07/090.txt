잡지명	三千里 제12권 제7호
발행일	1940년 07월 01일
기사제목	上海에서 近信, 金璟載氏로부터 (From Shanghai, a Recent Letter from Mr. Kim Kyŏng-jae)
기사형태	문예기타 (Literary Etc./Miscellaneous)

Since Mr. Yi Ryang and Mr. Pak Mun-hŭi came to Shanghai, whenever we stroll together through the French Concession (佛租界) or the International Settlement (共同租界), we always end up talking about you, hyŏng, and the steady persistence and well-being of the Samch'ŏlli Company (三千里社).

For someone like me, wandering in this foreign land (異域), what is never forgotten is the homeland's mountains and rivers. However, when I actually set foot there, boredom sets in before two days pass. Yet, on moonlit nights or rainy mornings, the mountains and rivers of my home country become newly missed in my thoughts.

Shanghai is pleasant right now. Today is Sunday. I am currently on my way out for a walk with Mr. Pak to Jessfield Park (「제스필드」공원). Using the time before that, unable to forget the missed voices of you, hyŏng, and all the respected colleagues at Samch'ŏlli Company, I am sending these few words as news.

Shanghai, North Szechuan Road (北四川路), Kim Kyŏng-jae
To Kim Tong-hwan hyŏng <96>

**Summary**

Kim Kyŏng-jae writes a personal letter from Shanghai to Kim Tong-hwan, likely associated with the magazine Samch'ŏlli, expressing feelings typical of an expatriate. He mentions meeting fellow Koreans Yi Ryang and Pak Mun-hŭi, walking through Shanghai's concessions, and discussing news from home, including the Samch'ŏlli company. He reflects on his complex feelings of homesickness, noting that while he misses Korea deeply from afar, the reality of being there quickly leads to boredom, yet the longing returns with specific triggers like moonlit nights or rain. The letter provides a snapshot of Korean intellectual/expatriate life in Shanghai in 1940. For historians, this text offers a glimpse into the personal networks among Koreans in Shanghai, their connection to cultural production back in Korea (Samch'ŏlli magazine), and the nuanced emotional landscape of displacement during the colonial period.

上海에서 近信, 金璟載氏로부터
李亮군과 朴文熹군이 上海로 왔기에 때때로 佛租界, 共同租界에 산보동행하는 길에 형의 이약이와 三千里社의 꾸준에 健在를 늘 話題에 올니군 함니다.
이렇게 異域에 유랑하는 나에게는 언제나 이치지지 않는 것이 고향산천이외다. 그러나 막상 드러서면 잇틀이 가기전에 실증이 남니다. 그러나 달밝은 밤, 비오는 아츰, 나의 머리에는 고국산천이 새삼스러히 그리워짐니다.
上海는 지금이 조흔때외다. 오늘은 일요일임니다. 나는 朴군과 함께 지금「제스필드」공원에 산보나가는 길임니다. 그 전의 시간을 이용하야, 兄과 아울너 三千里社 諸兄의 그리운 聲音 잊이못하야 두어자 소식드리나이다.
上海 北四川路 金璟載
金東煥 兄 ＜96＞
