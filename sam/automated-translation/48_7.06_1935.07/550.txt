A Discourse on Rational New Home Life
						Song Ok-hyŏn
Let Us Live Anew Like This

As our lives gradually changed from self-sufficiency, the industrial revolution brought about a revolution in home life. Household labor transformed into factory labor, and numerous convenient daily necessities and materials for food, clothing, and shelter produced in factories were imported into the home, making home life significantly more convenient. This has given housewives, who manage home life, much leisure time. I believe it is increasingly necessary for women to use this time meaningfully. Looking at current society, one can see that not a few people misuse their leisure time in home life. In this current era where home life can be made convenient in many ways, if women use various convenient institutions to simplify home life but then waste a great deal of leisure time satisfying their own base hobbies, it not only affects their families but also becomes a cause of social disorder (社會紊亂). For example, if a housewife, without considering the hygiene and nutrition of her family, goes to watch movies she likes, wasting much time, and consequently uses a lot of canned food (罐詰料理) to simplify meals, this is an instance of using convenient goods of the current era but misusing leisure time. <250>

We should seek as much leisure time as possible that comes back to us, and by utilizing that time, we should strive to make our lives more rational.

We hope to become housewives who strive for a more solid and fulfilling (堅實圓滿) life, a meaningful life as cultured individuals, or become dedicated wives devoted to social work, or steadfast wives engaged in a specific profession to support the family livelihood.

It is said that Western women, compared to Eastern women, have more developed general knowledge and advanced cultivation, enough to enlighten men. We Eastern women too must become aware of our own responsibilities, and at the same time, make good use of leisure time to broaden our reading and other aspects of self-cultivation, enrich our lives, and strive for improvement.

We should apply and put into practice the education we received in school to improve our lives into ideal ones. Since schools teach a wide range of home economics within a limited time (矩時間), it is difficult for theory and practice to go hand-in-hand, thus making it hard to cultivate skilled techniques. Understanding this shortcoming, one must adapt appropriately in real life. Such skill (手腕) is necessary for one who becomes a housewife. If a housewife becomes a straggler in society, it not only has enormous harmful effects (弊害) on children's education but also becomes a cause of damaging the peace and harmony of the entire family.

We need to strive for intellectual improvement regardless of gender distinction, and at the same time, endeavor for the development of home life.

To survive in current society, 8 hours of sleep per day are *****, and another 8 hours *** *** labor are an unavoidable economic duty, so the remaining * hours ** 6 hours of leisure are necessary for self-cultivation (修養), rest (慰安), recreation, etc., for modern people, for cultured individuals, regardless of gender. Women, due to their physiology or innate nature, are considered suited for handling household affairs, and we hear that managing the home is a woman's duty, her heaven-sent vocation (天職). This is a fact everyone acknowledges (認證). Therefore, as women cannot avoid housework, their heaven-sent vocation, they must handle their duties smoothly. Women should improve the household affairs of a family into a scientific life, a social life, an artistic life, achieve a rational home life, and attain a meaningful human life as cultured individuals.

Traditional home life was an experiential life, a life based on common sense, so deep scholarship was not necessary, nor was broad insight or observation required. However, times have changed, and experience alone can no longer satisfy our lives.

Modern women who realize this need cultivation, insight, superior judgment (見識), and ideals. Focusing on this point (此点), we should consider our birth as a member of society meaningful, become individuals who contribute to social development, fully fulfill our responsibilities as women, and hope to become possessors of a meaningful life. <251>

**Summary**

This editorial by Song Ok-hyŏn argues that modern conveniences brought by industrialization have given housewives leisure time, which should be used rationally for self-improvement, efficient home management based on science and art, and potentially social contribution or professional work, rather than frivolous pursuits like excessive movie-watching. The author emphasizes the need for women to gain knowledge and cultivation, comparing Eastern women unfavorably to Western women, and stresses that managing the home is a woman's "heaven-sent vocation" (天職), which must now be approached with modern knowledge and ideals, not just traditional experience, to ensure family well-being and contribute positively to society. From a historical perspective, the article is interesting as it reflects the complex interplay of modernization discourse, the adoption of Western ideals (rationality, efficiency, education for women), and the simultaneous reinforcement of traditional gender roles within the context of 1930s colonial Korea. The call for women to become "cultured individuals" managing homes "scientifically" while still seeing domesticity as their primary, inherent role highlights the tensions and transformations occurring in Korean society at the time.

우리들은 이러케 새로 살자

우리생활이 자급자족의 생활로부터 次次 변하야 지금의 우리 생활은 산업혁명에 의하아 가정생활의 혁명이 일어나 家內勞動이 공장노동으로 변하여 공장에서 제조한 多數의 편리한 일용품과 의식주의 자료가 가정에 수입되여 가정생활이 대단이 편리하여짐으로 가정생활을 관리하는 주부에게 만은 여가의 시간을 주게 되엿음니다. 이러한 시간을 有意義하게 이용함이 여성으로서 더욱 필요하다고 생각함니다. 현 사회를 보면 가정생활에 잇어 여가에 시간을 악용하는 자도 적지 안타고 볼 수 잇음니다. 가정생활을 얼마던지 편리화식힐 수 잇는 현시대에 여성이 여러가지 편리한 기관을 이용하여 가정생활을 간단이 하며 여가에 시간을 자기자신의 야비한 취미을 만족식히고저 多大한 시간을 허비한다면 자기가족에게 밋치는 영향은 물론이고 社會紊亂의 원인도 됨니다. 예를 들면 가정의 주부가 가족의 위생영양을 생각지 안코단지 자기 조와하는 영화관 구경을 가서 多大한 시간을 허비한 관계로 식사를 간단이 하기 위하야 罐詰料理를 만히 사용한다면 此는 지금시대에 잇어서 便利品을 이용하고 여가의 시간을 악용한 것임니다.<250>
우리에게 도라오는 여가의 시간을 될 수 잇는 한도에서 만이 구하며 其시간을 이용함으로써 우리 생활을 더욱 합리화 할 수 잇도록 노력할 것임니다.
우리 생활에 잇서 더욱 堅實圓滿한 생활 문화인으로서 의미잇는 생활을 하도록 노력하는 庭家부인이 되며 혹은 사회사업에 盡瘁하는 봉사적 부인이 되며 또는 일정한 직업에 종사하여 일가의 생계를 하는 착실한 부인이 되기를 바람니다.
서양부인은 동양부인보다 일반상식이 발달되고 교양이 진보되엿음으로 남자를 계발식힐만 하다고 함니다. 우리 동양여성도 자신의 책임을 자각하며 동시에 여가의 시간을 善히 이용하야 독서 기타 수양방면을 넓히도록 하며 우리 생활을 충실이 하며 향상에 노력할 것임니다.
우리가 학교에서 밧은 교육을 응용하며 실용화하야 우리 생활을 이상적 생활로 개선할 것임니다. 학교에서는 矩 시간에 범위 넓은 가사를 敎授함으로 이론과 실습이 병행되기가 어렵고 따라서 숙련한 기술을 양성키 곤란함으로 그 결점을 이해하고 실생활에 잇서 適知할 것임니다. 그 手腕이 주부되는 자는 필요함니다. 주부가 사회에 낙오자가 된다면 자녀교육에도 막대한 弊害가 잇을 뿐더러 일가족의 평화단락을 손상케 하는 원인도 됨니다.
우리는 남녀의 구별이 업시 지식향상에 노력함이 필요하며 동시에 가정생활발전에 힘쓸 것임니다.

在來의 가정생활은 경험적 생활 상식적 생활임으로 깁흔 학문도 필요치 안코 별로 넓은 見識*察도 필요치 안엇슬지라도 시대는 變遷되여 경험으로만은 도저이 우리 생활을 만족식힐 수 업서지엇음니다.
이것을 자각하는 현대의 여자는 교양이 필요하며 통찰력과 우수한 見識이 필요하고 理想이 필요함니다. 우리는 此点에 착안하고 사회의 一分子로서 출생함을 의미잇게 생각하고 사회발전에 도음이 되는 자가 되며 여자로서의 책임을 충분이 達하며 뜻잇는 생활의 소유자 되기를 바라는 바임니다.<251>
