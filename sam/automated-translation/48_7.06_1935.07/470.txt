Magazine Title: 삼천리 (三千里)
Publication Date: 1935년 07월 01일
Article Title: 一億名이 본대요 (One Hundred Million People Watch, They Say)
Article Type: 잡저 (雜著) (Miscellaneous writing)

The number of people watching movies is constantly increasing, and it is a very interesting question how many people in the world enjoy watching movies each week. The result produced by a certain statistician, who investigated while sweating, is said to be 185 million people.<225>

**Summary**

This short piece from 1935 notes the growing popularity of cinema worldwide, citing a statistic that 185 million people watch movies weekly. For historians of colonial Korea, this reflects the awareness and interest in global mass culture trends, specifically the rise of film as entertainment, within Korea during that period. The use of large, global statistics indicates an engagement with modern forms of information and a positioning of Korean readership within a wider world context, even amidst colonial rule.
