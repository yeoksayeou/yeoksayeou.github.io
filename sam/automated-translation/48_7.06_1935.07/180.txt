기사제목	美人 머리까까락은? (What About a Beauty's Hair?)
기사형태	잡저 (Miscellaneous writing)

Everyone, do you know this fact? The fact that no matter how beautiful a woman (美人) is, her lustrous, flowing black hair only lasts about 4 or 5 years. Therefore, human hair doesn't last 10 or 20 years, but can only sustain itself for about 4 or 5 years. After 4 or 5 years, it falls out, and new hair grows.<102>

**Summary**

This short piece states that human hair, even that of a beautiful woman, only lasts for about 4 to 5 years before naturally falling out and being replaced by new growth, debunking any notion that it lasts for decades. From a historical perspective, this brief article offers a glimpse into the kind of general knowledge or trivia shared in popular Korean magazines during the 1930s. Its focus on "beauty" (美人) and the simple explanation of a biological process might reflect contemporary interests and the magazine's aim for a broad readership.

美人 머리까까락은?
여러분 이런 사실을 아심니까. 아모리 미인이라도 그 윤 흐르듯 까만 머리깔이 겨우 4, 5년박게 못간다는 사실을. 그러기에 사람의 머리털이린 10년 20년 가는 것이 아니고 겨우 4, 5년간박게 지탱 못한담니다. 4, 5년 면 빠져버리고 또 새털이 나는 것이지요.<102>
