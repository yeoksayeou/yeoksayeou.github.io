Gratitude and Apology

It is now a fine season to seek the shade of green trees and read poetry and books. We hope that all of you, during this midsummer heat, will find scenic spots in the beautiful mountains and rivers, use a stone for a pillow, lie on the grass, listen to the sound of cicadas, and read enjoyably.

As announced separately by our company, we have decided to publish the collected works of our proud literary master, Mr. Yi Kwangsu (李光洙). The first volume, the novel 『The Life of That Woman』 (그 女子의 一生), will be released on June 20th. We hope you will read it avidly.

Mr. Chang Hyŏkchu's (張赫宙) novel 『Rainbow』 (무지개) has already received its publication permit and is currently being printed. The 『Selection of Korean Masterpieces』 (朝鮮名作選集) will also be published on June 25th. We promise that our company will put great effort into publishing endeavors going forward.

We promise that the August issue of Samch'ŏlli will be placed upon the desks of our esteemed readers before July 15th, so please continue to cherish it.<298>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript from the July 1935 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine expresses gratitude to readers and makes several announcements. It encourages reading during the summer and promotes upcoming publications by the magazine's company: the first volume of Yi Kwangsu's collected works ("The Life of That Woman"), Chang Hyŏkchu's novel "Rainbow," and a "Selection of Korean Masterpieces." It promises continued dedication to publishing and assures readers the next issue will arrive by mid-July. From a historical perspective, the article highlights the active literary and publishing scene in colonial Korea, featuring prominent (and later controversial) figures like Yi Kwangsu. The promotion of specifically *Korean* literary works ("朝鮮名作選集") and authors, despite the colonial context, is noteworthy for historians studying cultural production and national consciousness during this period. The use of mixed script and older orthography also provides linguistic data.

感謝와 謝罪
녹음을 차저 詩書를 읽을 조흔 철을 당하엿슴니다. 여러분께서 이 삼복 여름에 산수조흔 勝地江山을 차저 돌벼개 벼고 잔듸로 배를 깔고 매암소리 들어가면서 유쾌하게 독서하여 주시기를 바람니다.
본사에서는 이번에 別紙社告와 如히 우리의 자랑할 문호 李光洙씨의 전집을 발행키로 되여 그 제1권인 소설 『그 女子의 一生』은 6월 20일 발매하옵니다. 만히 애독하여 주시기를 바람니다.
張赫宙씨의 소설 『무지개』도 벌서 출판허가까지 마터 방금 인쇄 중이며 朝鮮名作選集도 6월 25일 발행되옵니다. 압흐로 본사에서는 출판사업에 크게 힘쓸 것을 예약하여 두옵니다.
삼천리 8월호는 7월 15일전으로 諸賢의 案頭에 여여질 것을 약속하오니 더욱 애호하여 주옵소서.<298>
