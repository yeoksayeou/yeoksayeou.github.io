**Body Text Translation**

Samch'ŏlli "Wall Newspaper" No. 8

Speech by the Chief of Naval Operations

Admiral Pratt (부랏트大將), the current American Chief of Naval Operations, stated in a speech sometime ago when the London Naval Treaty was submitted for deliberation in the US Senate:
"It may be that before one hundred years have passed, we will have to aid China (中國) and fight Japan (日本)."

Manchurian Railway Guard Military and Police Expenses

It is said that Japan (日本) spends 15 million yen annually for military and police expenses to guard the Manchurian Railway (滿鐵).

Mr. Chang Tŏksu (張德秀氏), Vice President of the Tong-A Ilbo (東亞日報), who has been in the United States (米國) for a long time, is scheduled to return to Korea this autumn in October and is expected to board a ship departing from San Francisco (桑港) soon.

Dancer Ch'oe Sŭnghŭi (崔承喜氏) will hold her 4th new works presentation performance at the Tansŏngsa Theater (團成社) in the city in early September, and will subsequently tour all of Korea (全朝鮮).

Mr. Ch'oe Rin (崔麟氏) of Ch'ŏndogyo (天道敎) has been receiving treatment for a gastrointestinal illness at Iwaii Internal Medicine (岩井內科) of Tokyo Imperial University (東京帝大). As there has been some improvement, word has arrived that he will return around mid-September.

The memorial ceremony for the eternally righteous kisaeng (義妓) "Non'gae" (論介) was held on August 12th on the Namgang River (南江) in front of Ch'oksŏngnu Pavilion (矗石樓) in Chinju (晋州). All the kisaeng of Chinju attended, along with over 3,000 citizens, and the ancestral rite was held in a very grand manner.<32>

**Summary**

This "Wall Newspaper" presents several news briefs from September 1931. Key items include a quote from the US Chief of Naval Operations predicting a future conflict with Japan alongside China, the annual cost Japan incurs for guarding the Manchurian Railway, the impending return of Tong-A Ilbo's Vice President Chang Tŏksu from the US, upcoming performances by dancer Ch'oe Sŭnghŭi, news of Ch'ŏndogyo figure Ch'oe Rin's medical treatment in Tokyo, and a report on a large memorial ceremony for the historic figure Non'gae in Chinju. For historians, the article is interesting as it reflects contemporary geopolitical tensions (US-Japan-China), Japanese colonial expenditures in Manchuria, the international movements and cultural activities of Korean elites under colonial rule, and the continuation of traditional cultural observances like the Non'gae memorial. Pratt's statement is particularly notable, foreshadowing future conflict less than a decade before Pearl Harbor.

三千里 「壁新聞」 第8號
軍令部長의 演說

亞米利加 現軍令部長 부랏트大將은 얼마전 倫敦軍縮條約이 米國上院에 上程審議될때에
『今後 百年이 차지 안어서 吾人은 中國을 援助하여 日本과 싸우지 안으면 안될는지 모른다』
라고 演說하엿다.

滿鐵護衛의 軍隊及警察費

日本은 滿鐵 護衛을 위하야 每年 1,500만圓의 軍隊及警察費를 支出하고 잇다한다.

오랫동안 米國에 가잇든 東亞日報 副社長 張德秀氏는 今秋 10월에 歸國하기로 內定되어 不遠에 桑港 出帆의 배에 올나타리라고.

舞踊家 崔承喜氏 第4回 新作發表의 公演을 9月 初旬에 市內團成社에서 하고 계속하여 全朝鮮을 一週하리라고.

天道敎의 崔麟氏는 그동안 東京帝大의 岩井內科에서 胃腸病 治療를 하고 잇든 중 多少 差効가 잇슴으로 오는 9月 中旬頃에 도라나오리란 寄別이 잇다.

千古義妓 『論介』의 祭典을 8월 12일에 晋州 矗石樓압 南江에서 지내엇는데 晋州의 妓生은 全部요 市民 3천여명이 參加하여 非常한 盛況으로 祭祀를 지내엇다고.<32>
