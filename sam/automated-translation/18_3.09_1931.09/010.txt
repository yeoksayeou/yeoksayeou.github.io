Let Us Gather Our Eyes and Ears on Manchuria and Mongolia.

Recently, upon reading the Japanese book titled "The Significance of Manchuria and Mongolia and the Invocation of Force" (滿蒙の重大性と實力發動), it was observed that Japan strongly advocated for a resolute military solution to protect its one billion [yen] investment in the South Manchuria Railway (滿鐵投資), its 200,000 residents, and all other rights and interests in Manchuria and Mongolia (滿蒙). Particularly, within it, the section titled "Sino-Japanese War and the US and Russia" (日中交戰と米露) draws much of our attention.

It points out the fact that out of the United States' (米国) total annual trade volume of 18 billion yen, exports and imports with China (對中輸出入) amount to merely 540 million yen, concluding that the US is unlikely to participate even if Japan and China (日中) go to war over Manchuria and Mongolia, a region where its interests are weaker than even Canada (加奈陀), the Philippines (比律賓), Central America (中米), South America (南米), or Hawaii (布哇). Next, regarding relations with Russia (對露關係), it explains the dangerous possibility that Russia (露國), for various political and economic reasons, might engage in joint operations with China (中國), and it concludes only by comparing military strengths as a countermeasure. We do not intend to comment on the appropriateness of his predictions, but in any case, it is a fact that recently, the great powers such as China, Japan (日), the US (米), Russia (露), and Britain (英) have all become tense, centering on the hegemony over Manchuria and Mongolia.

At this time, we must pay even closer attention to the future of Manchuria and Mongolia. This is because, at a minimum, the fate of one million overseas Korean residents (百萬僑民) hangs in the balance, and more broadly, it is predicted that matters of grave importance and interest (至重한 利害關係) will affect us all.

In any case, does history not tell us? Either our own strength must be greater to overwhelm them, or we must rely on the power of diplomatic relations between third parties, or failing that, because our own strength is insufficient, we must eternally seek their pity (憐憫). The tide of the general situation will push us forward onto one of these three paths. In this respect, truly, today is the time, I say, to gather our eyes and ears (耳目) on the shift in hegemony (覇權移動) in Manchuria and Mongolia.<1>

**Summary**

This 1931 editorial urges Koreans to pay close attention to the escalating tensions surrounding Manchuria and Mongolia (Manmong), particularly Japan's stated intent to use military force to protect its interests there. The author analyzes a Japanese book predicting low US intervention but potential Russian alignment with China. The article emphasizes the critical importance of the Manmong situation for the million Koreans living there and for the Korean nation's future, outlining three potential paths: achieving dominance through strength, relying on diplomacy between major powers, or seeking mercy due to weakness. For historians, this piece is interesting as it reflects Korean awareness and anxiety regarding Japanese expansionism just before the Manchurian Incident (September 1931). It highlights the precarious position of Korean migrants in Manchuria and the limited agency perceived by Koreans under colonial rule, framing the international power struggle's direct impact on their fate.

기사형태	논설

吾人의 耳目을 滿蒙에 모히자.
最近 『滿蒙の重大性と實力發動』이란 日本人 著書를 보건대 日本은 滿蒙에 대하야 10億의 滿鐵投資及 20萬의 주민과 其他一切의 權益을 擁護하기 위하야 斷然 武力解決할 것을 高調한 바 잇섯는데 特히 그중에 『日中交戰と米露』라는 一文은 吾人의 注目을 끄으는 바가 만타.
그는 米國全體의 年貿易額 180億圓中 對中輸出入이 僅僅 5億4千萬圓인 사실을 指摘하야 오히려 加奈陀, 比律賓, 中米, 南米, 布哇만도 利害關係에 잇서 薄弱한 滿蒙을 위하야 米國이 日中이 開戰하더라도 參戰할 것 갓지 안타고 論斷하고 그다음 對露關係에 이르러 露國은 政治的, 經濟的 여러가지 理由로 中國과 協同作戰할 危險性이 잇슴을 說破하고 오직 그 對策으로 軍事上 實力比較로써 끗을 매젓다. 우리는 그의 豫想의 適否를 말코저 안커니와 아무튼 最近 滿蒙의 覇權을 中心삼고 中, 日, 米, 露, 英等 列强이 모다 緊張되엇슴은 事實이다.

우리는 이때에 잇서 滿蒙의 明日을 더욱 注視하여야 할 것이다. 적게는 百萬僑民의 運命이 달렷고 크게는 우리 全體에게 至重한 利害關係가 미처질 것을 豫測되기 때문이다.
아무튼 歷史는 말하지 안느냐. 제힘이 그보다 더 强하야 그를 壓倒하거나 第三者와 第三者사이의 外交關係의 힘에 의하거나 그러치 못하면 제힘이 부족하니까 그의 압헤 永遠히 憐憫을 求하든가 그래야 大勢의 趨移는 우리들에게 이 세길 중 어느 한길로 邁進식힐 것이다. 이 点에서 實로 오늘은 우리의 耳目을 滿蒙의 覇權移動에 모일 때인가 하노라.<1>
