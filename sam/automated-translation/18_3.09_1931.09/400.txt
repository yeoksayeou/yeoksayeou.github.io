Gratitude and Apology

Above all, we are most sorry and offer our profound apologies (叩謝) for the point of not being able to publish the August issue of this magazine. Although it happened due to unavoidable circumstances (엇절수 업는 事由), the fact that we missed a month is no small mistake (失手) on our part, and for this, all of us in the editorial department apologize (謝過함니다). Henceforth, such a thing will not happen again.

We intended to make the contents of the September issue even more splendid (훌융히), but as you know, due to the extreme heat of the hottest summer days (三伏 酷暑), many writers (執筆 諸氏) were away in the countryside (地方) or had other circumstances, resulting in points contrary to our expectations (豫想).

As the upcoming October issue is the 3rd Anniversary Commemorative Issue, we will greet you again with substantial content, both internal and external, that we truly won't be ashamed (붓그럽지 안을만한) to show you. From now on, our entire staff will charge forward (突擊함니다) with the editing of the special issue. Please stay well (안녕히 게시압) in the meantime.
-The Editorial Department Staff

We feel terribly sorry (罪悚하게 암니다) that the August issue could not be published. Also, we regret (未安하옴니다) that we were unable to respond individually (一一히 回答하여 드리지 못한 곳도 잇서) to the inquiries (督促) from branch offices (支社), sales outlets (販賣所), and other readers (讀者 諸氏). This happened because we were so busy (奔走한 탓으로), so please forgive us (용서하시압).

For the next month's issue only, the fixed price (定價) will be 35 *chŏn* (錢). As the number of pages (頁數) will be doubled (倍加) and, in a first trial (젓 試驗) for the Korean printing world (朝鮮印刷界), many gravure photo plates (「그라비아」 寫眞版) will be included (넛는), we unavoidably (不得已) raised the price (올녓슴니다). Furthermore, for this special issue, we plan to print a predetermined number of copies (部數를 작정하고) with the policy (方針) that not a single extra copy will remain, so those who need it, please place your orders (注文하여 주시압) in advance.

For monthly subscribers (月定讀者) whose advance payment (先金) has run out (盡한), we have affixed a stamp (圖章을 찍엇사오니) indicating 'Advance Payment Exhausted' (先金盡), so please continue (繼續하여) to remit payment (送金하여 주시압).

We are recruiting (募集함니다) branch offices (支社) in various locations. Interested individuals (有意하신 분) in places still without a branch office, please enclose a 2 *chŏn* (錢) postage stamp (郵票封入하여) and request (請求하여 주시면) the terms and conditions booklet (規約書), and we will send it. (Business Department) <96>

**Summary**

The editorial staff of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine apologizes profusely for failing to publish the August 1931 issue due to unspecified "unavoidable circumstances." They also acknowledge the September issue didn't meet expectations due to writers being unavailable during the summer heat. They promise a high-quality, expanded 3rd-anniversary special issue for October, featuring gravure printing for the first time in Korea, but announce a temporary price increase to 35 *chŏn*. They urge readers to pre-order the limited-run special issue and ask subscribers whose payments have lapsed to remit funds. The business department also seeks applications for new branch offices. For historians, this notice provides insight into the practical challenges of periodical publishing in colonial Korea, including potential external pressures ("unavoidable circumstances"), seasonal workflow disruptions, the introduction of new printing technologies like gravure, and the economics of magazine distribution (pricing, subscriptions, branch offices). The formal, apologetic tone highlights the publisher-reader relationship dynamics of the era.

感謝와 謝罪
무엇보다도 未安하옵고 또 叩謝할 일은 8월호 本誌를 내지 못한 點이외다. 비록 엇절수 업는 事由로 그리 되엇다 할지라도 한달을 闕하게 된 것만은 우리들의 失手가 如干아인 것으로 이에 編輯局員 一同은 謝過함니다. 以後에는 그러케 될 일이 업겟습지요.

9월호는 그 內容을 더욱 훌융히 하려 하엿사오나 아시다십히 執筆 諸氏가 三伏 酷暑 때문에 모다 地方에 가게시거나 其他의 事情으로 그만 豫想에 反한 點이 잇섯슴니다.

來10월호는 3週年 紀念號인 것만치 여러분에게 보여 드려도 정말 붓그럽지 안을만한 充實한 內外容을 가지고 다시 뵈옵겟슴니다. 지금부터 우리 一同은 特別號의 編輯으로 突擊함니다. 그동안 안녕히 게시압.
編輯局一同

8월호가 못 나왓슴을 罪悚하게 암니다. 그리고 支社와 販賣所와 其他 讀者 諸氏의 督促에 대하여도 一一히 回答하여 드리지 못한 곳도 잇서 未安하옴니다. 奔走한 탓으로 그리 되엇스니 용서하시압.

來月號만은 定價를 35錢으로 함니다. 頁數도 倍加하고 또 朝鮮印刷界에서는 젓 試驗인 「그라비아」 寫眞版도 多數히 넛는 關係上 不得已 定價를 올녓슴니다. 그러고 이번 特別號는 미리 部數를 작정하고 한 部도 더 남지 안케 印刷할 方針임으로 必要하신 이는 미리미리 注文하여 주시압.

月定讀者로 先金이 盡한 분에게는 先金盡이란 圖章을 찍엇사오니 繼續하여 送金하여 주시압.
各處에 支社를 募集함니다. 아직 支社 업는 곳에서 有意하신 분은 2錢 郵票封入하여 規約書를 請求하여 주시면 보내 드리겟슴니다. (營業部) <96>
