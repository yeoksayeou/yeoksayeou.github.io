History of America's Great Crime King, 'President of the Night' 'Capone' Earns 220 Million Won Annually from Bootlegging and Illicit Sales
Chicago's President of the Night

Chicago (市俄古)!
Here, there is a great slaughterhouse boasting of being the world's largest, where tens of thousands of cows and pigs are butchered daily. On the other hand, it is the surprising city of crime, number one in America, where the "President of the Night," Capone, has pitched his camp, engaging in countless murders, illicit prostitution, and the import of bootleg liquor.
In Chicago, the annual number of
Murder cases is 367.
Here, day and night, human blood flows from pistol shots. Human corpses litter corner after corner. What a dreadful city of spiritual bankruptcy!

Bombing incidents: 150 cases.
Violent gangs run rampant, destroying buildings of banks, companies, and wealthy individuals 150 times, with damages amounting to 400,000 Won.
Extortion, murder, illicit prostitution, bombings—.
Into the hands of Capone, the crime king who creates all these dark aspects of humanity, a huge sum of 210 million Won rolls in annually.
- Income from smuggling and brewing liquors like whiskey: 120 million Won.
- Income from gambling dens and dog racing tracks: 50 million Won.
- Income from illicit prostitution and dance halls: 20 million Won.
- Income from intimidation and extortion: 20 million Won.
Total 210 million Won.<55>
There would hardly be any rich person, aside from Carnegie, with this much income.
Capone! Capone! At the mention of his name, even crying children stop crying. Passersby on the street lose their senses. The citizens of Chicago and the people of all America tremble.
More than that, more than that, the judges and prosecutors of the American courts, the police chief and his officers who should be catching murderers, the Chicago city officials who crack down on bootlegging, and even newspaper reporters can only fold their hands and tremble. That's because he spends tens of millions of Won to bribe all these officials. Having bought them off, whether it's the mayor, the chief justice, the police chief, or the newspaper president, he directs them with his fingertips and commands them with his toes.
The amount American officials receive annually from this crime king Capone in
Bribes is 60 million Won.

The Face of the Crime King

So, what kind of man is Capone? Is he truly evil? A hero? A demon?
Even when he sleeps at night, he has his subordinate soldiers carry guns and stand guard at the gate and in front of his bedroom. When he goes out, he rides in an armored car (裝甲自動車) with bodyguards, darting through the streets like lightning.
Furthermore, when attacking others, he uses weapons and ammunition exceeding that of an army. It's truly war!
Once, when attacking a certain place in Chicago, Capone loaded eight cars full of machine guns (機關銃) and revolvers (陸血砲), filled with his subordinates, and fired upon all the nearby hotels, shops, and restaurants, finally occupying the target location. The president of a Chicago newspaper who witnessed this scene trembled, saying,
"It's completely like a war."
Indeed, it is war. Capone's thievery is a form of war. It is that cruel and large-scale.
He is still a young man, only thirty-two years old. A flat nose, thick lips, two eyes rolling like cannonballs; his body bears bullet scars here and there. There are knife scars too.
His home is inside a three-story brick building hotel (三層煉瓦建物인 「호텔」) on Cicero Avenue. Using this as a fortress (要塞), every window is fitted with steel shutters thick enough that bullets cannot penetrate. At West 22nd Street, number 4823 (西22番街 4,823番), there is an armory (武器庫) where firearms and ammunition (銃器彈藥) are piled up like mountains.
Around Capone's fortress, fourteen or fifteen strong men (壯漢) always stand guard. If police or other enemies approach, hundreds of armed subordinates can gather immediately from where they sit via a dedicated telephone switchboard (電話交換臺).
Capone likes horse racing (競馬). It's quite easy for him to go out to the racetrack when bored and win about 200,000 Won. Conversely, if he loses at the races, he cleanly pays out tens or hundreds of thousands of Won in cash.
The money he earned just from horse racing during the nine years he was in Chicago is
20 million Won!
20 million Won!
20 million Won!
According to one newspaper reporter's investigation, among Capone's subordinates, there are

6,000 Officials.

Among them is even a judge from the New York Court (紐育裁判所 判事). Once, Capone killed two people and was tried in the New York Court, but the judge acquitted him.<56>
Besides this, whenever Capone's subordinates are caught, they somehow manage to escape prison (脫獄) easily, and somehow, they are acquitted and released.
Thus, the emperor (帝王) of the crime kingdom (犯罪王國) sweeps across all of America day by day. This is truly a bizarre phenomenon in the modern 20th century.

Also Active Politically

His entry into the military, his appearance on the political stage (政治舞臺) where he fought a battle against the Mayor of Chicago, and the records of his long-standing rivalry with the enemy faction (敵黨) "Torio" for supremacy in the crime world are dazzling, but these will be introduced gradually—.
Recently, Chicago telegrams report that Capone has been arrested. People all over the world were astonished once again. How could a crime king with such astounding power be caught?
In any case, it seems Capone was not merely a simple villain. Recently:
1. Capone intended to become the Mussolini of the labor union world (勞動組合界). He already holds real power (實權) in the Plumbers' Union (鉛管工組合), Street Cleaners' Union (街路掃除夫組合), Newspaper Deliverymen's Union (新聞配達夫組合), City Hall Clerks' Union (市廳事務員組合), and Marble Setters' Union (大理石工組合).
2. Capone is building a political organization (政治的 機關) to seize the right to appoint public officials (公職任命權) from city council members (市條事會員) and city authorities (市當局者). Capone is actively working to make City Auditor (市監査役) "Seridera" the Road Supervisor (道路管理人), thereby gaining control over 14 million Won annually, 3,000 officials (吏員), and supervisory authority (監督權) over 10 million Won in road improvement funds (道路改良費).
3. Capone is planning a grand unification (大同團結) of criminals. He reconciled with "Eiro," who has 5,000 subordinates (部下), plotting (畫策) some major undertaking.
His recent efforts to extend his demonic influence (魔力) into political and social spheres are drawing even more attention.

America has two presidents.
The president of the day (박주) is Hoover.
The president of the night (밤) is Capone.<57>

**Summary**

This 1931 article from the Korean magazine *Samch'ŏlli* details the immense power and wealth of Chicago gangster Al Capone, dubbed the "President of the Night." It outlines his estimated annual income of 210 million Won (likely Yen) from bootlegging, gambling, prostitution, and extortion. The piece emphasizes his control over officials through bribery (60 million Won annually), his fortress-like headquarters, large armed force, violent methods compared to warfare, and his surprising acquittal in a New York murder case. It concludes by noting his recent arrest and his ambitions to extend his influence into labor unions and city politics, aiming to control appointments and public funds. For historians studying Korean colonial history, this article is interesting as it shows the global reach of news and fascination with figures like Capone, even in colonized Korea. It reflects how international events and sensational stories were consumed and interpreted for a Korean audience, potentially offering insights into contemporary Korean perceptions of America, crime, power, and corruption, possibly drawing implicit parallels or contrasts with their own colonial situation. The use of Korean phonetic renderings for American names and places, along with older Korean orthography, is also of linguistic interest.

犯罪王의 面目

그러면 「카보네」란 엇더한 사내인가? 그는 과연 악한인가? 영웅인가? 귀신인가?
그는 밤에 잘때에도 부하의 병정들을 총메이게하여 문깐과 침실압헤 파수세이고 잔다. 밧갓으로 나단일때에는 장갑자동차(裝甲自動車)에 호위병을 세위노코 번개가치 거리거리를 피하여 다라난다.
그뿐더러 남을 숩격할때도 군대이상의 무긔탄약(武器彈藥)을 사용한다. 바로 전쟁이다!.
한번은 시카고의 엇든 곳을 습격할때에 「카보네」는 여덜대의 자동차에 긔관총(機關銃)과 륙혈포를 가득 실고 그리고 부하가 가득 안저서 그 부근의 호텔과 상점과 음식점까지 모조리 사격(射擊)하면서 마츰내 목젹한 곳을 점령하고 말엇다. 이 광경을 보든 시카고신문사장(市俄古新聞社長)은
「완연히 전쟁이구나」
하고 전률하엿다. 실로 전쟁이다. 「카보네」의 도적질은 한 개의 전쟁이다. 그만치 잔혹하고 대규모이다.
그는 인제 겨우 서른둘밧게 아니된 청년이다. 평퍼짐한 코 두터운 입 대포알가치 구으는 두눈동자 그의 일신에는 총알자죽이 처처에 잇다. 칼마진 자죽도 잇다.
그의 집은 「시세로」거리의 삼층련와건물인 「호텔」속이다. 이것을 요색(要塞)으로 하고 창문이란 창문에는 모다 총알의 마저도 께둘늘수 업스리만치 두터운 강철창을 하엿고 서삼이십이번가 사팔이삼번(西22番街 4,823番)에는 무긔고(武器庫)가 잇서 총긔탁약(銃器彈藥)이 태산가치 싸히여 잇다.
가보네의 요색부근에는 십사오명의 장한(壯漢)들이 늘 직히고 잇다. 만일 경관이나 다른 장적이 달려들면 즉시 전용인 전화교환대(電話交換臺)를 통하여 안즌 자리에서 수백명의 무장한 부하가 모여들게 되엿다.
「카보네」는 경마(競馬)를 조화한다. 경마장에 심심하면 놀너나가서 이십만원쯤 따오는 일은 아조 쉬운 일이다. 그대신 자긔가 경마에 지면 까긋하게 몃십만원이든지 몃백만원이든지 현금으로 지불한다.

政治的으로도 活躍

그가 군대(軍隊)에 드러가고 그가 청치무대(政治舞臺)에 나타나 시카고 시장(市長)을 상대로 한바탕의 싸홈을 하엿고 또 다년의 적당(敵黨) 「도리오」와 범죄왕의 패권을 다투든 긔록등이 실로 눈부시게 만치만은 그것은 차츰차츰 소개하기로 하고-.
최근은 시카고 전보는 「카보네」가 잡히엇다고 전한다. 전세계의 사람사람들은 다시 한번 놀내엇다. 그러케 놀라운 세력을 가지고 잇는 범죄왕이 잡히다니?
좌우간 「카보네」는 단순한 악한만이 아니엇든 모양으로 최근에는
1. 카보네는 勞動組合界의 뭇소리니 되려하야 그는 벌서 鉛管工組合, 街路掃除夫組合, 新聞配達夫組合, 市廳事務員組合, 大理石工組合等의 實權을 掌握하고 잇다.
2. 카보내는 政治的 機關을 建設하여 市條事會員及市當局者로부터 公職任命權을 奪取하려 하고잇다. 市監査役 「세리데라」을 道路管理人으로 하여 年 1천4백만원과 吏員 3천명의 支配權及道路改良費 1천만원의 監督權을 엇으려 카보네는 活躍中이다.
3. 캇보네는 犯罪者의 大同團結을 計劃하고 잇다. 5천명의 部下를 가진 「에이로」와 화목하여 무슨 큰일을 획착하려 하엿다.
그는 근래에 정치적 사회적방면으로 그 마력을 페이려함이 더한층 죽목된다.
