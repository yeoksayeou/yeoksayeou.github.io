**Body Text Translation:**
Departure
						Kim Okpong

In life's endless solitude, though there might be the joy called a smile,
When I close my eyes and feel the distinct quietness of a poor, flower-bloomed courtyard, imagining it before my eyes,
Why, sadness seems to overwhelm me.
Having now lost even the will to tear off the calendar page, I stare distantly at the ceiling, and finding transient life plausible,
I laugh hollowly.

Trying to endure even for a moment the burning pent-up frustration (愛鬱)—still unable to stop yearning for the painful reality—
I suddenly, like someone seized by madness (穿發狂), opened the door and rushed out.
At that moment—outside the door, wasn't the delivery man standing unexpectedly?

"I shall depart with flower seeds and farming tools (농구)—"
Within the most ordinary words, more extraordinary words must be hidden.
Ah, upon seeing just a few characters written on a trivial piece of paper (조히쪽우에),
I realized how significant this brief moment of departure, this single letter, was.
I realized anew that I would welcome the knowing you.
(1931.6.16) <84>

**Summary**

This poem by Kim Okpong depicts a shift from deep despair and ennui to a sudden spark of hope and purpose. The speaker, initially lost in the solitude and apparent futility of life, symbolized by staring blankly at the ceiling, experiences a moment of revelation upon receiving a simple letter delivered unexpectedly. The letter's mundane message about departing with seeds and tools takes on profound meaning, representing a new beginning or a call to action. For historians, the poem offers insight into the emotional landscape of individuals during the colonial period. The initial feelings of powerlessness and poverty ("poor, flower-bloomed courtyard," "transient life") contrasted with the abrupt hope found in a simple message might reflect the constrained circumstances and the yearning for agency or change under Japanese rule, finding meaning even in small, everyday events.

出發
						金玉峰
인생의 끗업는 고독속에 미소란 깃븜도 잇스련만
가난한-- 꼿핀 집뜰이 별로 고요함을 눈감고 느끼며 눈아페 그려볼때
웨 슬픔이 엄도들가합니다.
나는 지금 掛曆떼는것조차 일코 천정만 먼히 처다보다 덧업는삶이 그럴듯하
야 헛청대고 웃소이다.

불붓는 愛鬱을 잠시라도 참어볼가하야--괴로운 현실을 그래도 그려마지못
하야--나는 별안간 穿發狂이처럼 문열고 내달렷소이다.
그때--문박겐 배달부가 문듯 서잇지안습니까.

『꼿씨와 농구를가지고 출발하리라--고』
가장 평범한 말속에 보다 비범한말이 숨엇스리라.
아아 하챤흔 조히쪽우에 멧字안되는 글발을볼때
이편지한장의 출발의 잠시가 얼마나 큰가를 알엇소이다.
아는그대를 마지할것으로 새삼시리 깨달엇소이다.
(1931.6.16) <84>
