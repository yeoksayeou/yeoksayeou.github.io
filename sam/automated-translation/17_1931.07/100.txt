**Smoking Room**

Ch'oe Rin and the Pig Blood

When the Ch'ŏndogyo Leader Son Pyŏnghŭi was still alive, one day he went to Yŏngwŏl in Kangwŏn Province, taking along several followers such as Kwŏn Tongjin, O Sech'ang, Ch'oe Rin, and Chŏng Kwangjo. At that time, there were many wild boars in Yŏngwŏl, and it was said that drinking the blood of these boars was beneficial for the body, so they went in large numbers intending to drink wild boar blood.

Finally, they went into the mountains and one day shot and caught two wild boars. The group, with the two boars laid out before them, was greatly satisfied. They first dealt with one wild boar whose life had just moments ago been extinguished, and from it gushed clean, bright red fresh blood. Son Ŭiam looked towards Kwŏn, O, Ch'oe, Chŏng, and the others and strongly urged them, saying, "Come now, please drink this blood quickly. I will catch and eat the remaining one," telling the others to drink the blood of the first-caught boar. Those beside him urged the Teacher (선생) to drink first instead, but he ultimately wouldn't listen. At that time, Ch'oe Rin said to Son Ŭiam,
"Teacher. Please drink this blood first. With wild boar blood, if the bullet doesn't strike the heart perfectly and happens to damage the liver (간), it becomes unusable. Fortunately, this one is fresh, so please drink it first. We will catch and eat the remaining one."

Thinking there was reason in those words, Ŭiam drank the blood of the first-caught boar as Ch'oe Rin recommended.
Then, they dealt with the next boar. Its blood was blackish. What was the reason? Just as they had worried might happen, the bullet had lodged in the chest and damaged the liver (간), causing the blood to spoil. Thus, they couldn't drink that blood. Only then did everyone present (一座) admire Ch'oe Rin's words.

An Chaehong's Chairmanship Connection

An Chaehong was the chairman at the founding convention of the Sin'ganhoe Kyŏngsŏng Branch, and he was also elected chairman at the association's dissolution meeting held last spring at the Youth Hall downtown. Thus, it's said he forged a very close connection (因緣) with the rise and fall (明滅) of the Sin'gan Branch.<28>

**Summary**

This article shares two brief anecdotes from the "Smoking Room" column. The first describes a hunting trip where Ch'oe Rin astutely advises Son Pyŏnghŭi, the Ch'ŏndogyo leader, to drink the blood of the first wild boar they caught, correctly predicting the second boar's blood might be spoiled due to the bullet damaging the liver. The second anecdote notes that An Chaehong served as chairman for both the inaugural meeting and the final dissolution meeting of the Sin'ganhoe's Kyŏngsŏng (Seoul) branch. From a historical perspective, these vignettes offer glimpses into the personal interactions and beliefs (like the health benefits of boar blood) of significant nationalist figures during the colonial period, as well as their involvement in key organizations like the Sin'ganhoe, highlighting both cultural practices and political activities of the time.

喫煙室
崔麟氏와 도야지 피

天道敎主 孫秉熙氏가 아직 在世하엿슬 때 하로는 權東鎭, 吳世昌, 崔麟, 鄭廣朝 等 여러 교인들을 다리고 江原道 寧越으로 갓다. 그 때 이 寧越에는 멧도야지가 만헛는데 그 도야지 피를 먹으면 몸에 유익하다하야 산도야지 피 먹으려 大擧하여간 것이엇다.
급기야 산에 가서 엇든 날 멧도야지 두 마리를 쏘아 잡엇다. 두 마리를 압페 노흔 여러 사람은 크게 만족하여 목숨이 금박 끈어진 멧도야지 한 마리를 위선 잡으니 거기에선 깨긋하고 샛빨간 선지피가 소삿다. 孫義菴은 權, 吳, 崔, 鄭 等 여러 사람을 바라보며 「자, 어서 이 피를 자시오. 나는 남어 잇는 한 마리를 잡어 먹을터이니」 그리면서 먼저 잡은 도야지 피를 여러 사람에게 먹으라고 강권한다. 겻헤 사람들은 도로 선생부터 잡수라고 권하엿스나 종시 듯지 아니한다. 그 때 崔麟氏가 孫義菴더러
「선생님. 이 피를 먼저 잡스세요. 산도야지 피란 총알이 아조 심장을 마치어 만일 간을 범하는 것이면 못쓰는 법인데 다행히 이것은 생신하니 위선 잡시서요. 남저지것을 저이들이 잡아 먹겟슴니다」
그 말에 일리잇는 듯 하여 義菴은 崔麟氏의 권대로 먼저 잡은 도야지 피를 마시엇다.
그러고 그 다음 또 도야지를 잡엇다. 그 피는 까마햇다. 무슨 까닭이냐 만일을 염려하든 바와 가치 총알이 가슴에 박이어 간을 범한 까닭에 피가 상한 것이엇다. 그래서 그 피를 먹지 못하고 말엇다. 그제야 一座가 모다 崔麟氏의 말에 탄복하더라고.

安在鴻氏의 議長因緣

新幹會 京城支會 창립대회 때 의장이 安在鴻氏엇는데 지난 봄 시내 청년회관에서 열닌 동지회 해소대회 때의 의장으로도 亦 推選을 밧엇다. 이래 新幹支會의 明滅과는 매우 갓가운 인연을 맷게 되엇더라고.<28>
