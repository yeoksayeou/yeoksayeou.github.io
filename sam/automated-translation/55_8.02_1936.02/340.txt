**Body Text Translation:**
What Does Mr. Yi Su-tak Plan to Do If He Gets His Million Won?

From death to life (生)! Yi Su-tak (李洙倬), cleared of the false charge of being a patricide (弑父犯)! How did the land worth a million gold (百萬金) slip from Yi Su-tak's hands during those eight years, through what course, and into whose hands did it fall?
Can this money be recovered? Unable to contain my curiosity, I sought out Mr. Yi at 45 Samgak-chŏng (三角町), downtown (市内), on a mild early January (一月初旬) day.

The room, decorated in pure Korean style (純朝鮮式), was filled with cozy warmth.

I: How much you must have suffered, and how happy you must be now?
Yi: How could I possibly describe it in words (形言)? Just as my mother says, she says she "gave birth to me anew." I, too, am not the Yi Su-tak of old, but a newly born person seeking a new "life" (삶) rather than the old life (生活). I have changed my name from Yi Su-tak to Yi Kŭn-t'aek (李根澤) here, cast off the actions (行動) of the old Yi Su-tak, and intend to do the work of a true person. Was I a dead man? If I die now while doing human work, so be it. Like a sparrow (!쨘발잔갓치), I intend to turn my feet from the path of wrongdoing (不良) to goodness (善良), from vanity to truth, from the path of foolishness, and tread the best path (最善) worthy of a human life (人生).

I: Then what about that million gold (百萬金)? What happened to it?
Yi: You would know from seeing the complaint submitted to the Chŏnju District Court (全州地方法院) reported in the newspapers (新聞紙上). While I was in prison (獄中) for those 8 years, scoundrels (不良輩) transferred it here and there using forged documents (僞造文書).<162> Its assessed land value (地價) alone was over 253,000 won, but calculated at the current market price (現時價), who knows how many times greater it would be.

I: How many people are implicated in this case (事件)?
Yi: 443 people.

I: If you win (勝訴) the lawsuit (訴訟), what do you intend to do with that money?
Yi: I am Yi Kŭn-t'aek, not the Yi Su-tak of former days... Therefore, after observing society (社會) a little, I intend to try using it valuably (價値잇게) according to various fields (各方面). It's money that was lost, after all.

I: Then how do you plan to make a living?
Yi: I plan to build a humble three-room thatched house (三間草屋), farm the land in a quiet area (地方), and spend my leisure time (餘暇) reading books.

Mixing casual conversation, asking and receiving his thoughts for about an hour, I then stepped out the door. The red glow of sunset hung over the peak of Pukak Mountain (北岳山).<163>

**Summary**

This 1936 interview features Yi Su-tak, recently exonerated after serving eight years for a patricide he did not commit. While imprisoned, his valuable land, worth perhaps a million won, was illicitly transferred via forged documents. He has filed a lawsuit against 443 individuals at the Chŏnju District Court to reclaim his property. Having changed his name to Yi Kŭn-t'aek to symbolize a fresh start, he expresses a desire for a simple, quiet life farming in the countryside, while planning to use any recovered fortune for societal benefit rather than personal luxury. For historians of colonial Korea, the article illuminates the vulnerability of property rights, the potential for legal recourse (albeit complex, involving hundreds of defendants), and the personal struggles and aspirations for renewal under Japanese rule. The specific details about the court case, the large number of defendants, and the protagonist's name change offer tangible insights into the social and legal landscape of the period.

기사제목	李洙倬氏는 무얼 하려는고, 百萬圓 돈차지면?
기사형태	대담·좌담

李洙倬氏는 무얼 하려는고, 百萬圓 돈차지면?
죽엄에서 生으로! 弑父犯의 누명을 벗은 李洙倬! 백만금의 토지는 8년동안 李洙倬의 손에서 버서나 엇더한 결로를 밟어 엇더한 사람의 손으로 들어갓던가!
또 이 돈을 도로 차즐 수 잇는가! 筆者는 궁금함을 금치 못하여 市內 三角町 45番地로 氏를 찻기는 겨을날도 포근한 一月初旬 어느 날이엇다.

순조선식으로 꾸민 방안에 후군한 더운 기운이 가득하다.
나!얼마나 고생하섯스며 이즘 얼마나 깁븝니가?
李-엇더타고 形言할 수 잇겟슴니가. 그저 우리 어머니 말슴처럼 그저 나를 「새로 나핫다고 하지요」 나도 녯날에 李洙倬이가 아니고 새로난 사람으로서 모든 녯날 生活보다 새로운 「삶」을 엇고저 합니다, 여긔서 李洙倬을 李根澤으로 일홈을 곳치고 녯날에 李洙倬의 行動을 저바리고 참다운 사람의 일을 하렴니다. 내가 죽은 사람이니가? 지금 내가 사람다운 일을 하다가 죽으면 그만이다 하고 저! 쨘발잔갓치 나의 할 일을 不良에서 善良으로 헛된 데서 참으로 어리석은 길 우에서 발을 돌녀 人生다운 最善의 길을 발부려고 함니다.
나-그러면 저 百萬金! 엇지된 것임니가?
李-그것은 全州地方法院에 제출한 고소문을 新聞紙上으로 보시여서 알겟습니다. 내가 8년동안 그 옥중에서 잇는 동안 불량배은들 위조문서로서<162> 이리저리 넘겨간 것이 위선 地價로만 25만3천여원인데 現時價로 計算한다면 몃 倍가 될지 모르지요.
나-몃 사람이나 이 事件에 걸녓습니가.
李-443명임니다.
나-그 訴訟에 勝訴되면 그 돈은 엇더케 하렴니가?
李-전날의 李洙倬 은아니고 李根澤임니다... 그러니 좀 社會를 살펴 보아서 價値잇게 각 方面에 따라 써 보려고 함니다. 일헛든 돈이니가 말이지요,
나-그러면 엇더케 살님사리를 할렴니가
李-三間草屋이나 지여서 땅 파며 한적한 地方에서 여가에 책이나 읽고 지나렴니다
나는 잡담 석거 주고 밧으며 그의 의사를 뭇기를 한 시간이나 하고 이여 문을 나서니 北岳山 머리에 저녁 붉은 노을이 걸처 잇다.<163>
