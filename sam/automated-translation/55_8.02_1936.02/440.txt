今年에 하고 십흔 文學的 活動記
(Record of Literary Activities I Want to Do This Year)

Chang Hyŏkchu
"Yemyŏnggi" (黎明期 - Dawn Period), "Kokkan ŭi Chŏngnyŏl" (谷間의 情熱 - Passion of the Valley)

Yŏm Sangsŏp
Following Mr. Chŏn Yŏngt'aek, I am supposed to write a full-length novel for the M Newspaper (M新聞), so I intend to devote my energy to that.

Chŏn Yŏngt'aek
I want to write a creative work based on "Jesus" (예수), but I dare not touch it. And I intend to put together a collection of short stories.

Yu Chino
I haven't devised any particular work, but starting this spring, I am determined to try writing good works, leaving behind all the noise.

Ch'oe Sangdŏk
I intend to try making "Yŏnae Pisangsŏn" (戀愛非常線 - Love Emergency Line), which I am currently writing for the Chosŏn Chungang Ilbo (朝鮮中央日報), into a newspaper novel worthy of the name.

Yang Chudong
Hyangga Annotations (鄕歌註釋)
Great Compilation of Old Korean Words (朝鮮古語大集成)
These two items above have been my long-cherished desires for years <217>

No Chayŏng
I am thinking of trying to depict recollections of my "younger days" under the title "Tonggyŏng Sidae" (憧憬時代 - Age of Longing), but I don't know if I will actually get to write it.

Chŏng Naedong
"Introduction to the Chinese New Literature Movement" (中國新文學運動槪論)

Yi Muyŏng
I'm thinking of trying to write a three-act play. If that doesn't work out, I might try starting the novel "Thirty Years" (三十年).

Hong Hyomin
Starting the new year, I've reached the respectable age of 33, so I do have the ambition to write creative novels using my unique technique, but will my surrounding circumstances allow it?

Yi Il
I don't know if the new spring has arrived, but I cannot muster an appetite for writing, and consequently, I don't even dream of any creative ideas.

Yu Ch'ijin
Borrowing the character named "Kim Towŏn" (金道元), I am thinking of a play to satirize one of the characteristics of Korean people.<219>

Kim Chinsŏp
I am only ashamed of my continued state of 'not flying, not crying' (不飛不鳴 - inaction).

Sŏ Hangsŏk
I want to write a play. Although I conceived it long ago, I haven't had the chance to pick up the pen, but this year I plan to try writing one or two.

Ch'ae Mansik
I am considering writing a novel titled "Nakcho" (落照 - Setting Sun), but I don't know if it will get written, and even if I start writing it, I don't know if it will take one year this year, or three years, or ten years.

Kim Ansŏ
What will I do in the new spring? I haven't decided yet, but I want to try depicting the life of Cho Sin (調信) as an epic poem. Since mind and execution never align, it's also something unknowable. And I want to consult a bit regarding folk songs (民謠).

Chang Tŏkcho
Nothing in particular.<221>

Kim T'aejun
I intend to devote this year entirely to Research on Korean History (朝鮮史學硏究).
I have no plans beyond that yet.

Sim Hun
I am conceiving something else to write, but it hasn't taken shape yet (lit. eyes and nose haven't formed yet).

Kim Kwangsŏp
There are many things I want to write starting from the new spring, but I hesitate to announce them abruptly here.

Min Pyŏnghwi
I am not trying to write creative works, but going forward, I am striving to write responsible criticism. Therefore, I focus more on reading than writing.

An Sŏgyŏng
This year, I plan to complete the three movie scripts (映畵劇本) I composed last year: "Kalmegi" (갈메기 - Seagull), "Yŏn'ga" (燕歌 - Swallow Song), and "Sanahŭi ŭi Maŭm" (사나희의 마음 - A Man's Heart).<223>

Ham Taehun
From the new year, I will be writing the full-length novel "Strait of Pure Love" (長篇純情海峽) in "Chogwang" (朝光). Also, in the new year, I plan to release one full-length Russian novel through a certain publisher. In any case, I plan to write many full-length and short stories.

Im Hwa
Due to other theoretical "programs," I doubt whether I can proceed with my creative plans. I have two plans, but before they are finished, I cannot say what they are. One is to make a poetry collection about a youth who traveled back and forth across the Genkai Sea (玄海灘 - Hyŏnhaet'an) during the fifty or sixty years from Enlightenment Korea (開花朝鮮) to present-day Korea (朝鮮), and I want to write an epic poem of about 30,000 lines dealing with "Immigration" (移民).<225>

**Summary**

This article from the February 1936 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine presents a survey of prominent Korean writers and intellectuals detailing their literary plans for the year. Responses range from specific projects like novels, plays, poetry collections, historical research, and critical essays to more uncertain aspirations or expressions of creative block. Several mention specific titles, themes (like Jesus, Korean characteristics, historical periods), or publications they are working with. The plans reflect a diverse literary landscape encompassing fiction, drama, poetry, scholarship (history, linguistics, folklore), criticism, and even screenwriting. For historians, this survey provides a valuable snapshot of the Korean literary scene under Japanese colonial rule. It reveals individual ambitions, the types of genres and themes considered relevant, the perceived constraints (personal, environmental, political implied by "雜音" - noise, or Im Hwa's "theoretical programs"), and the engagement with both Korean tradition (Hyangga, folklore, history) and contemporary issues (modern life, emigration, the legacy of the recent past). The mention of specific newspapers and magazines also sheds light on the publishing ecosystem of the time.

今年에 하고 십흔 文學的 活動記
張赫宙
「黎明期」 「谷間의 情熱」

廉想涉
田榮澤氏의 뒤를 이어 長篇을 M新聞에 쓰기로 되엇스니 그것에나 誠力을 다 할가 함니다.

田榮澤
「예수」를 題材로 創作을 하고 싶으나 敢히 손을 대지 못합니다. 그리고 短篇集을 하나 쨔내 보렵니다.

兪鎭午
特別한 무슨 作品을 創案하는 것은 업스나 今年 봄부터야 말로 모든 雜音을 떠나 좋은 作品을 써보랴고 벼르고 잇습니다.

崔象德
朝鮮中央日報에 執筆中인 「戀愛非常線」을 新聞小說다운 新聞小說을 만드러 볼까 함니다.

梁柱東
鄕歌註釋
朝鮮古語大集成
以上 2건은 年來의 夙望 <217>

盧子泳
「憧憬時代」라고 하여 나의 「젊은 날」의 追憶을 그려보려고 생각하는 것이 있으나 과연 쓰게 될는지는 몰으겠읍니다.

丁來東
「中國新文學運動槪論」

李無影
3幕戱曲을 한편 써볼까 합니다. 그것이 안되면 小說 「三十年」을 起稿해 볼까 합니다.

洪曉民
新年부터는 나히도 33세라는 먹을 만한 程度가 되엇으니 나의 獨特한 手法으로의 創作小說을 쓰려고 하는 野心은 잇습니다마는 周圍環境이 어디 許諾합니까.

李一
新春이 되엿는 지 모르지만 執筆食慾을 니르키지 못하고 따라서 創案은 꿈도 안 뀜니다.

柳致眞
「金道元」이란 人物을 빌려서 朝鮮사람의 特性의 하나를 諷刺하랴고 戱曲 한 篇을 생각 中입니다.<219>

金晋燮
依然한 不飛不鳴을 부끄려할 뿐이올시다.

徐恒錫
戱曲 한 篇 쓰고 싶습니다. 構想은 한 지 오래면서 붓을 들 겨를을 얻지 못하엿던 것을 今年에는 하나둘 써볼까 합니다.

蔡萬植
『落照』라고 題目을 부치어 小說을 하나 써볼가 하기는 하지만 써질른 지도 모르겟고 또 쓰기 시작한다더래도 今年 1年이 걸릴른 지 3年 10年이 걸릴른 지 모르겟슴니다.

金岸曙
新春에 무엇을 하겟느냐고? 아직 定치는 아니하엿으나 調信의 一生을 敍事詩로 그려보고 십습니다. 맘과 實行과는 언제나 一致되지 아니하니 또한 알 수 업는 일이외다. 그러고 民謠에 對한 것을 좀 參考하고 십습니다.

張德祚
별노 업슴니다.<221>

金台俊
저는 今年을 純全히 朝鮮史學硏究에 바치려 합니다.
아즉 그 以上 아무 計畫이 없음니다.

沈熏
무엇 하나 또 써볼가 하고 構想 中인데 아직은 눈, 코도 생기지 않엇읍니다.

金珖燮
新春부터 쓰고시픈 것이 만슴니다마는 여기서 當突하게 發表하기에는 躊躇함니다.

閔丙徽
作品을 쓰려고는 하지 안씀니다만은 앞으로는 責任잇는 評論을 쓰려고 애쓰고 있음니다. 까닭으로 쓰는 것보다 읽는데 더 힘씀니다.

安夕影
今年에는 昨年에 「갈메기」와 「燕歌」와 「사나희의 마음」 세 篇의 「映畵劇本」을 構成한 것이 잇는데 이것을 完成식히고자 합니다.<223>

咸大勳
長篇純情海峽을 新年부터 「朝光」에 씁니다. 그리고 新年에 某社出版으로 露西亞長篇 하나를 내일 作定입니다. 어떠튼 長篇 短篇을 많이 쓸 作定입니다.

林和
다른 理論的 「푸로그람」 때문에 創作 上의 計劃을 進行식힐지 疑問임니다. 計劃으로는 두 個가 잇는대 다 끗나기 前에야 무어라 말할 수 업스나 한아는 開花朝鮮으로 今日의 朝鮮에 이르는 五六十年間의 「玄海灘」 上을 往來한 靑年을 한 個 詩集으로 하고십고 「移民」을 取扱한 約 3萬行의 敍事詩를 쓰고 십슴니다.<225>
