World Knowledge

World's Paradise

Iceland's population is 118,000, and this place is truly the Garden of Eden (東山). It is a place where not only are there no unemployed or poor people, nor police officers (巡査), but there isn't even a prison.

Country Without Cultural Facilities

British Somaliland has been a British protectorate (保護領) since 1884. Although its area is 68,000 square miles (平方哩) and its population is 350,000, it is said that cultural facilities such as railways, hotels, banks, and hospitals are still absent.

Copyright Fee of $100,000

An extremely large sum of money was once paid in advance (先拂) from a publisher to an author. This occurred in 1826 when Thomas Macaulay received $100,000 from a publisher under the promise to write "History of England," setting a record.

Marriage Agencies (結婚福德房)

In the United States of America (아메리카合衆國), approximately 150,000 men and women each year find their partners through marriage advertisements, public marriage mediation centers (公設結婚媒介所), and marriage clubs.

Roman Pope

From 885 AD to 1939 AD, 152 individuals became the Roman Pope (로마法皇), but among them, only four Popes received the title of Saint (聖者, Saint).

Lakes as Food Stores

Switzerland, concerned about wartime food shortages, annually seals large quantities of wheat flour (麥粉) in large iron (鐵製) tanks and stores them in lakes throughout the country.<77>

**Summary**

This article compiles several brief, unrelated facts presented as "world knowledge." It covers Iceland as a utopia without police or prisons, British Somaliland's lack of development despite being a British protectorate, a record-breaking $100,000 advance paid to Thomas Macaulay for his history book, the scale of marriage brokering in the US, the small number of Popes canonized as saints, and Switzerland's practice of storing flour underwater as a wartime measure. From a historical perspective, the selection of topics offers insight into the kind of information about the outside world being presented to Korean readers during the colonial period. The focus on Western nations (Iceland, Britain, US, Switzerland, Rome/Vatican), extreme examples (utopia vs. undeveloped), large figures (money, population), and preparedness (Switzerland) might reflect colonial-era interests or attempts to shape perceptions of global affairs and modernity.

世界知識
世界의 樂園

아이스랜드의 인구는 11만 8천인데, 이곳은 전혀 에덴東山이다. 실업자나 빈한한 자나 巡査가 없는 것은 물론, 형무소까지도 없는 곳이다.

文化施設이 없는 나라

英領 소말리랜드는 1884년來, 英國의 보호령이 되여 있는데, 면적은 6만8천平方哩, 인구는 35만이나 있으나 철도, 호텔, 은행, 병원 등 문화시설이 아직까지도 없다고 한다.

十萬弗의 著作料

대단히 많은 거액의 돈이 출판업자로부터 著作者에게 先拂된 일이 있는데, 그것은 1826년에 토-마스·마코-레이가 『英國史』를 쓸 약속하에 출판자로부터 10만弗을 수령한 것으로, 기록을 짓고 있다.

結婚福德房

아메리카合衆國에서는 매년 약 15만人의 남녀가 결혼광고, 公設結婚媒介所, 결혼구락부를 통하여 그 상대를 찾고 있다.

로마法皇

西曆 885년부터 1939년까지 로마法皇이 된 사람은 152명인데, 그 중에서 聖者(쎄인트)의 칭호를 받은 法皇은 네사람 뿐이다.

食糧庫인 湖水

스이스에서는 戰時의 식량부족을 염려하고, 매년 다량의 麥粉을 크다란 鐵製탕크에 밀폐하여 국내 각지의 湖水속에 넣어둔다.<77>
