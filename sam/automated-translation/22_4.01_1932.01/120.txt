**Samch'ŏlli Wall Newspaper**
The Path to the Discovery of the Ōsugi Incident

According to the true story from a reporter of the Tokyo Asahi Shinbun, initially, the Keishichō (Metropolitan Police Department) did not know that Captain Amakasu had murdered Ōsugi Sakae during the Great Tokyo Earthquake. Only when it became impossible to resolve the incident solely through the hands of the Kenpeitai (Military Police) was the Keishichō notified, whereupon the shocked Keishichō immediately reported it up to the Naimushō (Ministry of Home Affairs). The then Home Minister, Gotō Shinpei, became greatly angered and immediately delivered a stern reprimand (嚴談) to the Rikugun Daijin (Minister of War), Tanaka Giichi. Minister of War Tanaka then rode straight away to meet the Martial Law Commander, General Fukuda. During that meeting, the order for the summons (喚問의 傳令) of Military Police Commander Koizumi arose. This eventually reached the ears of reporters covering the Keishichō and was finally relayed swiftly to the world.<46>

**Summary**

This article recounts how the murder of anarchist Ōsugi Sakae by Captain Amakasu during the Great Kantō Earthquake (known as the Ōsugi Incident or Amakasu Incident) became public. It details the initial cover-up by the Kenpeitai (Military Police), the subsequent forced involvement of the Keishichō (Metropolitan Police), the anger of Home Minister Gotō Shinpei leading to a confrontation with War Minister Tanaka Giichi, and the eventual leak of information about the investigation (specifically the summons of the Kenpeitai commander) to the press. For historians of Korean colonial history, the article offers a glimpse into the internal conflicts and workings of the Japanese state apparatus, particularly involving the Kenpeitai, an institution deeply feared in colonial Korea. The incident highlights the potential for state violence and attempts at concealment, as well as the mechanisms (even if accidental leaks) through which such information could eventually surface, providing context for understanding the nature of Japanese imperial power and information control during that era.

三千里 壁新聞
大杉事件 發覺의 經路

東京 朝日新聞 기자의 실화를 드르면 東京大震災 때에 甘粕대위가 大杉榮을 살해한 갓을 처음에 警視廳에서는 몰낫다. 憲兵隊의 손만 가지고는 그 사건을 수습할 수 업게 되자 그때에야 경시청에 통고하엿슴으로 震駭한 경시청에서는 즉시 내무성에 上申하엇다. 그 당시의 內相 後藤新平이 크게 노하여 즉시 陸軍大臣 田中義一에게 嚴談하엇다. 田中 陸相은 그길로 말을 몰고 계엄사령관 福田 대장을 만나려 갓다. 그 회견에서 小泉 헌병대사령관 喚問의 傳令이 일고 이것이 나종에 경시청 출입하는 기자의 귀에 드러가서 끗끗내 세상에 속히 전하여진 것이라 한다.<46>
