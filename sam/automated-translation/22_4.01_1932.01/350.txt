Author: Not specified
Article Type: 잡저 (Miscellaneous writing/Essay)

In America these days, a great debate has arisen among astronomers. Director Ch'ŭkhŭso (측흐소) (?) of Albania, Dr. Pposa (뽀사) (?), discovered that there are mountains on Mars, saying that if one could just go, one could live there, and said that if one travels at a speed of 680 miles (哩) per hour, it would take 8 years and 22 days.

However, the Los Angeles astronomer Dr. Ppŭm'gadŭ (쁘-ㅁ가-드) (?) said the problem is how one would store 8 years' worth of food, and announced that even with the development of speed science (스피-드·싸이엔스), arrival would not be possible within 500 years.

Furthermore, Dr. Tchi-je (찌-제) (?) of the University of California (加酒大學) published an argument that within 500 years, due to the lack of oil and metal, earthlings would not be able to fly to Mars, so a debate was bound to arise.<109>

**Summary**

This article reports on a debate among American astronomers in the early 1930s regarding the feasibility of traveling to Mars within the next 500 years. One astronomer claims Mars is habitable due to the discovery of mountains and calculates an 8-year travel time. Another astronomer counters that storing food for such a journey is problematic and that even with advancements, reaching Mars within 500 years is impossible. A third astronomer argues that resource depletion (oil and metal) on Earth will prevent such a journey within that timeframe anyway. For historians of colonial Korea, this piece reveals an awareness of and interest in international scientific discussions, including speculative topics like space travel. It reflects how global futuristic ideas were received and reported in Korea, while the somewhat unclear transliterations of foreign names and places offer insights into information flow and translation practices during the period.

아메리카 學界에서의 五百年後의 「꿈」, 『火星에 간다』『못간다』는 것으로 論爭
아메리카에선 요새 천문학자사이에 대논쟁이 니러낫는데 알바니아의 측흐소장 뽀사박사는 화성에 산이 잇다는 것을 발견햇슴으로 가기만 하면 샇수 잇다고 햇스며 한시간에 680哩의 스-피드를 다-하면 8년 22일이 걸닌다고 햇다.
그런데 로산젤쓰의 천문학자 쁘-ㅁ가-드박사는8년간의 양식을 엇더케 저축하겟는가하는 것이 문제이라고 하엿스며 스피-드·싸이엔스가 발달해서 5백년이내에는 도착할 수 업다고 발표햇다.
거기에 또 加酒大學 찌-제박사가 5백년내에는 오일과 금속이 업서 지구인이 화성에 나러갈 수 업다는 논설이 발표되엿스니 논쟁이 생길밧게업지.<109>
