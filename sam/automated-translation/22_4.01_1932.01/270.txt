기사제목	笑話, 戀愛情調 (Joke, Romantic Mood)
기사형태	잡저 (Miscellaneous Writing)

Under the clear autumn sky—receiving the gently blowing evening breeze, the two young people headed towards the pleasant outskirts.

Finding a quiet and clean place, the footsteps of the two young people stopped there.

The two sat side by side, but their hearts just pounded, and they couldn't start a conversation. After a while, the woman barely managed to speak.

"Look here. Why is the sky dyed so very blue?"

The counterpart's reply was extremely simple.

"Because it's dyed very blue, that's why."

Fortunately, a topic was brought up, but their conversation couldn't continue for even a minute and was cut off. Again, the woman spoke.

"Why are the birds sitting in the trees so small?"

"Because they are small birds, that's why they're small."

Like this, the topics brought up several times could not continue, and they only maintained silence for a long time.<96>

**Summary**

This short piece describes an awkward encounter between a young man and woman on a date in the outskirts. The woman attempts to initiate conversation by remarking on the beauty of nature (the blue sky, the small birds), but the man provides only blunt, literal answers, causing the conversation to stall repeatedly. They end up sitting in silence. For historians, this article offers a glimpse into social interactions and perhaps dating awkwardness among young Koreans in the early 1930s. It portrays a certain reserve or lack of romantic conversational skill, reflecting everyday life rather than overt political or resistance narratives, which can be valuable for understanding the texture of colonial society beyond major events.

笑話, 戀愛情調
맑게 개인 가을 하늘 아래―산들산들 부러오는 저녁 바람을 마지면서 졂은 두 사람은 공긔 조흔 교외로 향하엿슴니다.
조용하고 깨끗한 장소를 차즌 두 졂은이에 발거름은 그곳에 머물너저 섯슴니다.
두 사람은 나란히 안젓슴니다만은 가삼만 울넝거릴 뿐이고 이야기는 끄집어 내지를 못하엿슴니다. 한참 잇다가 여자편에서 간신히 말을 끄집어 내엿슴니다.
「여보세요. 하늘은 웨 저럿케 새-파랏케 물들엇슬가요?」
상대자의 대답은 몹시도 간단하엿슴니다.
「새-파랏케 물들어스닛깐 그럿치요」
요행 화제를 꺼내엿스나 1분도 게속되지 못하고 그들의 이약이는 끈허지고 말엇슴니다. 또 다시 여자편에서
「나무에 안즌 새가 웨 저럿케 적을가요」
「적은 새니깐 적지요」
이러케 몃 번이나 끄집어낸 화제는 게속되지 못하엿스니 그들은 오래도안 침묵을 직혓슬 뿐이엿슴니다.<96>
