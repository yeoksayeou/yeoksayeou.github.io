Magazine Title: 三千里 제4권 제1호
Publication Date: 1932년 01월 01일
Article Title: 社告 (Company Notice)
Author: 三千里社 (Samch'ŏlli Company)
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Company Notice/Editor's Postscript)

Oh, everyone! Let's increase the readers of Samch'ŏlli. May each one of you persuade just one other person, please increase [our readership].

Samch'ŏlli Company <89>

**Summary**

This brief notice is an appeal from the Samch'ŏlli publishing company to its readership. It urges each current reader to recruit one additional reader to help expand the magazine's circulation. From a historical perspective, this highlights the challenges faced by Korean-language publications during the colonial era in maintaining and growing their audience. It underscores the reliance on grassroots reader support and promotion for the survival and growth of cultural enterprises like magazines.
