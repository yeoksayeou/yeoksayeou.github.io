**Body Text Translation**

Samch'ŏlli "Wall Newspaper"
Prisoners Surviving Winter

Winter is cold. Looking at the number of prisoners spending this cold winter in prisons (according to the Government-General Legal Affairs Bureau (總督府 法務局) survey at the end of October, Shōwa 6 [1931]):
Kyŏngsŏng Prison (京城刑務所) 1,200 people
Sŏdaemun (西大門) 2,100 people
Ch'unch'ŏn (春川) 290 people
Kongju (公州) 560 people
Ch'ŏngju (淸州) 350 people
Taejŏn (大田) 870 people
Hamhŭng (咸興) 750 people
Wŏnsan (元山) 380 people
Ch'ŏngjin (淸津) 570 people
P'yŏngyang (平壤) 1,700 people
Taegu (大邱) 1,400 people
Pusan (釜山) 600 people
Kwangju (光州) 800 people
Total Others Combined 17,534 people

Chosŏn's First Female PhD

Miss Kim Hwallan (金活蘭), famous not only as an "old miss" of Chosŏn but also holding a teaching position (教鞭) at the highest educational institution for women's education, recently obtained a Doctor of Philosophy degree from an American university for her dissertation on rural issues. She is the first Chosŏn woman to hold such a degree (學位).

The Hŏ Yŏngsuk (許英肅) Bobbed Hair Incident

According to recent news sources, Mrs. Hŏ Yŏngsuk, the wife of Ch'unwŏn (春園) Yi Kwangsu (李光洙) and mother of two sons and one daughter, has cut her hair short (斷髮). In the circle of those who bobbed their hair late, she is another addition after Miss Kim Hwallan. Thus, the three women Hŏ Yŏngsuk, Kim Hwallan, and Hyŏn Sindŏk (玄信德) form, in a different sense, a three-person bobbed hair brigade (三人 斷髮隊).<8>

**Summary**

This "Wall Newspaper" section from the January 1932 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine presents three news briefs: statistics on the prison population across Korea as of late 1931, totaling over 17,500 inmates; the achievement of Kim Hwallan becoming the first Korean woman to earn a Ph.D. (from an American university); and the news that Hŏ Yŏngsuk, wife of the famous writer Yi Kwangsu, has bobbed her hair, joining Kim Hwallan and Hyŏn Sindŏk as prominent women adopting this modern style. For historians, the article offers a stark glimpse into the scale of incarceration in colonial Korea under Japanese rule through the specific prison population numbers. It also highlights the evolving social landscape, showcasing both the academic advancement of Korean women on an international stage and the adoption of modern Western styles like bobbed hair by elite women, symbolizing changing gender norms and cultural shifts during the period.

朝鮮 最初의 女博士

朝鮮의 올드.미쓰로 유명할 뿐더러 여자교육 최고학부에 교편을 들고 잇는 金活蘭양이 금번 米國 대학에서 농촌문제의 논문으로 철학박사의 학위를 어덧다는데 朝鮮 여성으로 최초의 학위를 가진 분이다.

許英肅 女史 斷髮事件

근일 소식통에 의하면 春園 李光洙씨의 영부인이요 2남 1녀의 어머니인 許英肅 여사가 단발을 하엿는데 늣게 단발한 축으로는 金活蘭씨 후에 또 한 분 늘은 셈이다. 이리하야 許英肅, 金活蘭, 玄信德의 3여성은 딴 의미로 3인 단발대를 이룬 셈이다.<8>
