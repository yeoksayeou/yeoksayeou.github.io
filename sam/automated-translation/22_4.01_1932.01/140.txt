Samch'ŏlli "Wall Newspaper"
Korean Students Residing in the United States

Currently, the individuals most active in Chosŏn society are those who studied abroad in Tonggyŏng and America.

According to a recent survey, there is still a large number of 340 Chosŏn students studying day and night within the United States. Now, looking at the numbers of various foreign students currently studying at American universities:

China: 1,294
Canada: 1,263
Japan: 987
Philippines: 887
Russia: 338
Germany (獨 日): 397
United Kingdom: 365
Mexico: 350<49>

**Summary**

This short news piece highlights the significant role played by Koreans educated abroad, specifically in Tokyo and the United States, within contemporary Korean society (as of 1932). It reports that 340 Korean students were studying in the US at the time and provides comparative figures for students from other nations like China, Canada, Japan, the Philippines, Russia, Germany, the UK, and Mexico. For historians, the article offers a snapshot of the perceived importance of foreign-educated elites in colonial Korea and provides concrete data on the scale of Korean student presence in the US relative to other nationalities during this period. The comparison implicitly positions Korea within a global context of international education centered on America.
