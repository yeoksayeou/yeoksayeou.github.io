Magazine Title: 삼천리 제4권 제1호
Publication Date: 1932년 01월 01일
Article Title: 年頭感, -新年號 印刷를 마치며- (Year-End Thoughts, -Upon Finishing the Printing of the New Year Issue-)
Author: 파인 (P'ain), 정희 (Chŏnghŭi), 박 (Pak), 신순석 (Sin Sun-sŏk)
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice / Editor's Postscript)

Who is there that is not ashamed before the passage of time? As the New Year's Day arrives, I am especially ashamed, enough to want to dig a hole and enter the ground, for having added another year to my age (馬齡) without having done anything at all. However, as we are young bodies, there must still be much time permitted, so I only pledge to achieve meaningful work in this year of 1932. Now, I lament, asking for funds and time. (P'ain)

1931 has all gone, and the next new year has returned, so new strength arises again, but this is the strength that arises every time one greets the new year. Yet, it has been the usual practice (常例) until now not to have found the results of that strength, so I hesitate to make a promise here again, saying "In the new year, I will do such-and-such." However, I will promise only that I will exert all the strength I have. (Chŏnghŭi)

1931 has gone, having dug a large pit both domestically and internationally (海內海外). What have we seen, and what have we done? There is only shame. Reflection on the past, hope and aspirations for the future to come – surely everyone has these, wouldn't everyone possess them? Now! The morning sun (旭日) of 1932 has risen in the bright sky. Let us all take a deep breath, powerfully and joyfully, together. To powerfully greet the coming year. (Pak).

I only hope that your favor (愛顧) deepens, and together, I offer New Year's greetings (세배) to all you readers (諸氏). (Sin Sun-sŏk) <141>

**Summary**

This editorial postscript features brief reflections from four individuals (P'ain, Chŏnghŭi, Pak, Sin Sun-sŏk) upon completing the New Year's issue of the magazine *Samch'ŏlli*. They express a common sentiment of shame and regret for the lack of accomplishment in the past year (1931), acknowledging the passage of time. Despite this, they convey a renewed sense of determination and hope for 1932, pledging to exert their efforts towards meaningful work, even while hesitating to make specific promises due to past failures. Pak explicitly mentions 1931 as a year that dug a "large pit" domestically and internationally, hinting at significant hardships. For historians, the piece offers a glimpse into the mood of Korean intellectuals during the colonial period – a blend of self-reproach, constrained ambition, and resilience. The reference to 1931's difficulties provides context for the socio-political or economic climate impacting Korea and the world at the time.

年頭感, -新年號 印刷를 마치며-
누가 세월 압헤 붓그럽지 안은 자 잇스리까. 새해 설날을 당하매 더구나 아모 한 일이라고 업시 또 馬齡 하나를 더하게 됨이 땅파고 드러가고 십도록 붓그럽슴니다. 그러나 우리는 졂은 몸이니 아즉 허락된 세월이 만흘 터이매 1932년 이 한해에는 보람잇는 일을 이루고저 맹세할 뿐임니다. 이제 나에게 자금과 시간을 달나고 탄식하면서.(파인
1931년은 다- 가고 그 다음의 새해가 도라오니 또한 새힘이 생겨짐니다만은 이것은 새해를 마즐 때 마다 생겨지는 힘이오나 그 힘의 성과를 찻지 못햇슴이 엿때까지의 常例엿스니 굿해여 또 여기에다가 「새해에는 엇더케 하겟슴니다」고 미루 약속하기는 주저되여 짐니다. 그러나 잇는 힘을 다- 하겟다는 것만 약속해 두겟슴니다.(貞熙)
1931년은 海內海外를 물론하고 커다란 구덩이를 파놋코 갓슴니다. 우리는 무엇을 보앗스며 무엇을 하얏슴니까. 다만 붓그럼만이 잇슬 다름임니다. 과거의 반성, 미래에 올 희망과 포부는 누구나 다 잇슬 것이며 누구나 갓일 것이 안이겟슴니까. 자아! 1932년의 旭日은 명랑한 상공에 떳슴니다. 다갓치 힘껏, 그러고 질겁게 심호흡을 합시다. 압흐로 올 1년을 힘차게 맛기 위하야.(朴).
여러분의 愛顧가 두터워짐을 오즉 바랄 뿐이며 아울너 독자제씨에게 세배를 들입니다.(申順石) <141>
