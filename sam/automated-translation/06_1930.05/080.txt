**Body Text Translation:**
Mr. Yu Chint'ae says = People in other countries, if they are close friends and one says, "This is a secret," sealing their lips, there's no way they would ask again. But observing Koreans, if you request, "This is a secret, so don't ask further," the more it's a secret, and the closer the friendship, the more annoyingly they follow you around, having a habit of persistently asking. This, I know, is certainly a bad habit.

Ms. Na Hyesŏk says = If circumstances permit, I intend to go to P'ari once more for art studies. The last time I went there, I stayed for about 6 months, attending the studio of the famous P'ari painter Mr. Pissei (빗세이), trying hard to learn something, but what I couldn't quite grasp then, upon returning home, I find myself realizing this and that quite clearly (谿然히). It seems like my eyes are truly opening to Western painting only now. So, it feels like I did futile work back then. That is, I regret having painted futile pictures.

Nowadays, I am borrowing a friend's room and painting a landscape to submit to an exhibition, and I am completely cooped up in that studio from ten in the morning until four in the evening. It will probably take about two weeks to complete, and it's the first work I've put effort into since "Ch'ŏnhugung" (天后宮), which depicted scenes from Pongch'ŏn (奉天) long ago. I believe in it, but how will it turn out? <25>
My female student days were already over 10 years ago, and now I am an old woman with four little children, headed by a ten-year-old son. Time flies, doesn't it?

Mr. Han Yongun = Although Paek Yongsong is also sitting here right now, I have a composed mind regarding the matter of life and death – that is, the issue of people dying and living. Death is something I cannot avoid even if I try to flee, and although I don't know when it will strike, I simply do not think that death itself is something fearful.

Mr. Kim Pyŏngno = It is an urgent task for us to strive for the abolition of certain laws and ordinances (某某 법령) that exist only here and not in other countries. I will say no more than this...

Mr. Hŏ Hŏn = We should consider those who merely have enough to eat and wear as rich. We must draw the boundary line between the poor and the rich here. <26>
<25-26>

**Summary**

This "Smoking Room" article from *Samch'ŏlli* magazine presents brief opinions from five prominent Korean figures in 1930 on diverse topics: social habits (Yu Chint'ae on keeping secrets), art and personal life (Na Hyesŏk's reflections on studying painting in Paris and her family), philosophy (Han Yongun's stoic view on death), veiled political critique (Kim Pyŏngno on abolishing specific colonial laws), and socio-economic commentary (Hŏ Hŏn's definition of wealth in a poor society). For historians, the article is valuable as it captures candid snapshots of the thoughts of key intellectuals and activists during the Japanese colonial period. Kim Pyŏngno's carefully worded call for abolishing unnamed laws unique to Korea offers a glimpse into subtle resistance and critique of the colonial legal framework, while Hŏ Hŏn's comment highlights the prevalent poverty.

喫煙室
兪鎭泰氏 曰=남의 나라 사람들은 친한 사이면 「이것은 비밀일세」 하고 입을 담을어 버리면 다시는 뭇는 범이 업는데 내가 가만히 조선 사람들을 지내어 보면 「이것은 비밀이니 더 뭇지 말나」 부탁하면 비밀이라 할사록 또 친한 사이일사록 성가시게 따라 다니며 기어히 뭇는 버릇이 잇스니 이것은 확실히 낫분 버릇인 줄 아오.

羅蕙錫女史 曰=저는 경우만 허락하면 그림공부로 다시 한번 「파리」로 가려고 함니다. 요전 번에 그곳에 갓슬 때는 약 6개월 동안 잇섯는데 파리의 유명한 화가 「빗세이」씨의 화실을 다니며 무엇을 좀 알려고 애를 썻지만은 잘 알려지지 안튼 것이 정작 귀국하여 보니 이것 저것 谿然히 깨닷게 되는 바 잇서 이제야 정말 洋畵에 눈이 떠지는 듯 함니다. 그래서 녯날에는 헛 일을 한 듯 해요. 즉 헛 그림을 그린 듯 후회남니다.

요지음은 친구의 방을 빌어 가지고 전람회에 출품할 풍경화를 그리고 잇는데 아츰 열시부터
저녁 넉점까지 그 화실에 꼭 드러 박이고 잇슴니다. 아마 2주일이나 걸니어야 완성될 듯 한데 녜전 奉天의 풍물을 그린 「天后宮」 이후에 처음 애쓰는 作으로 나는 밋슴니다만은 엇덜는시요.＜25＞
나의 여학생 시대는 벌서 10여년 전으로 지금은 열살 먹은 아들을 머리로 어린애들 녯을 가진 늙은이람니다. 세월은 참 빠르지요.

韓龍雲氏= 지금 이 자리에 白龍城도 안저 계시지만은 나는 생사문제-즉 사람이 죽고 살고 하는 문제에는 태연한 마음을 가지고 잇슴니다. 죽음이란 내가 피하러 하여도 피할 수 업는 것이고 또 언제나 닥처 올 것인지 모르나 死 그 물건이 두리운 것이라고는
도모지 생각되지 안슴니다.

金炳魯氏=우리들은 다른 나라에는 업고 이곳에만 잇는 某某 법령을 철폐하는데 힘 쓰는 것이 急務외다. 이 이상은 더 말치 안커니와...

許憲氏=우리들은 겨우 먹고 입고할 만하면 그를 부자라고 처야 한다. 貧人 富人의 경계선은 우리는 여기에 두어야 할 것.＜26＞
