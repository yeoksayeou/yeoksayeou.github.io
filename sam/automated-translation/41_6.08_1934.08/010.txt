An Evening of Mystery with the XX Newspaper President

Recently, the streets of Seoul are full of many mysterious things, and various rumors are circulating. However, this is not necessarily a true fact, so please listen with that in mind.
One day, Mr. XXX, the president of the XX Daily News (xx日報社長), came to the company and was working in the president's office when a middle-aged couple visited him. They greeted him saying, "We are delighted to meet the teacher (先生) we have always wanted to see," and asked if they could treat him to dinner, requesting his presence at a certain restaurant (料理店) that evening. Mr. X, having a vivacious personality, readily agreed. Mr. X likely thought that although they were strangers, there was no need to refuse since they had come out of their way to show goodwill. At the promised time, Mr. X, with his cheerful and bright steps, went to the agreed-upon restaurant for dinner. The couple who had visited earlier in the day had already arrived and were waiting.
As they settled in the room and engaged in lighthearted conversation, the couple whispered amongst themselves, went out as if to make a phone call, and shortly after, three or four young men, also unknown to Mr. X, opened the door and entered the room. Upon introduction, they were all said to be graduates of certain universities in Tōkyō and Kyōto. After a while, the dinner table was set, and even beer was served. While they were eating, the young men took turns asking Mr. X the following:
"Teacher (先生), how do you view the recent actions of Mr. XX?"
"I haven't given it much thought."
"Basically, we believe this is not the time to remain idle <6> and that it would be good to start the XX Movement. To do so, regarding the person to be nominated as its leader, Mr. XXX has much money but is old and lacks courage, so he won't do. Mr. XXX thinks only his group is the best, and so-and-so is a good figure but lacks money, and yet another has money but lacks skill—after considering this and that, Mr. XX is indeed good, so wouldn't it be best to nominate him and work under his banner? What is your opinion, Teacher (先生)?"
Mr. X, quite taken aback by the unexpected words, said, "Anyway, the purpose tonight was to have dinner, so let's just enjoy the meal and go home."
Thus, Mr. X hastily finished his dinner, drove his car, and returned home.

Results of the Posŏng Chŏnmun Memorial Hall Fundraising

The support committee, which announced the plan to build the Posŏng Chŏnmun School (普成專門學校) Memorial Hall by investing 300,000 wŏn, recently sent Mr. Kim Pyŏngno (金炳魯) to tour South Chŏlla, North Chŏlla, South Kyŏngsang, and North Kyŏngsang provinces.
Mr. Kim Pyŏngno, who braved the heat during the scorching midsummer Sambok period and traveled through the Yŏngnam regions for about a month, spoke about the results as follows:
"Regarding the project to build this hall for our proudest institution of higher learning, the people of the Samnam (three southern provinces) region truly showed enthusiasm that moved me to tears. As a result of visiting thoughtful individuals in various places, it seems about 100,000 wŏn will be collected from this trip. Combining this with the money already <7> gathered, it looks like around 200,000 wŏn will be collected. For the remaining 100,000 wŏn, I will continue traveling to South and North P'yŏngan, Hwanghae, South and North Hamgyŏng, and other regions to find supportive comrades."

Lieutenant Colonel Yi Yang and Nakaoka Kon'ichi

The fact that Lieutenant Colonel Yi Yang (李亮中佐), a Korean, is an important cadre in the East Railway Protection Army (東鐵護路軍) of Manchukuo (滿洲國) is widely known through the travelogue by President Yamamoto (山本社長) published in "Kaizō" (改造). However, many may not yet know the fact that Nakaoka Kon'ichi (中岡艮一) recently visited Lieutenant Colonel Yi Yang.
Specifically, Nakaoka Kon'ichi is the young man who assassinated Hara Kei (原敬), was imprisoned for 13 years, and then released. This time, intending to work in Manchukuo, he decided to seek out Lieutenant Colonel Yi, trusting in him, and passed through Seoul in late June heading to Manchukuo. Since Manchukuo is a vast stage, the developments following the meeting between Nakaoka Kon'ichi and Lieutenant Colonel Yi Yang draw considerable interest.

XX신문 사장과 수수꺽기의 一夕

xx日報社長 xxx氏가 엇든 날 社에 出動하여 社長室에서 執務하고 잇는데 엇든 中年夫婦 두 사람이 차저와서 늘 뵙고 십허 하든 先生을 뵈엇스니 깁부외다 하는 인사말을 하고 저녁이라도 待接하고 십사오니 오늘저녁 某 料理店으로 가 주실수 업겟는가고 請 하엿다. 活達한 性格의x氏라 快히 承諾하엿다. x氏의 생각에는 未知의 사람들이지만은 일부러 차저 와서 好意를 表 하려하는데 拒絶할 것 까지 업다하야 그러함이엇든듯 約束한 時間에 x氏는 그快活 明朗한 거름을 옴겨 約束한 料理店으로 晩餐자시려 갓다앗가 나제 왓든 그夫婦는 벌서 와서 기다리고 잇섯다.
房에 定坐하고 뼈업는 閑談을하고 잇슬제 두夫婦는 지이끼리 쑤군쑤군하더니 나가서 電話거는 것 갓더니 조곰잇다가 그亦 未知의 靑年 三四人이 x氏 房으로 門을 열고 드러선다. 인사한즉 모다 東京과 京都의 某 大學出身들이라 하는데 한참만에 저녁 床이 나왓고 麥酒까지 나왓다. 한참 젓가락질 하는 中途에 그 靑年들은 번가라가며 x氏에게 이러한 말을 하엿다.
「先生께서는 最近에 XX氏의 行動을 엇더케 보시오.」
「나는 別로 생각한 바 업소이다.」
「大體 우리는 이대로 지내갈 때가 아닌즉<6> XX運動을 이르킴이 조흘줄 암니다. 그리함에는 그首領으로 推戴할 사람은 XXX氏는 돈은 만흐나 年齡도잇고 勇氣 업서 틀렷고 XXX氏는 自己 그릅만 제일이라 하고 또 누구누구는 人物은 조흐나 돈이 업고 또 누구누구는 돈은잇스나 수완이 업고-이럭저럭 생각한 끗헤 亦是 XX氏가 조흐니 그분을 推戴하고 우리 그 傘下에들어 일함이 그 아니 좃슴니까. 先生의 意見은 엇더하서요.」
X氏는 하도 意外의말에 左右間 오늘저녁은 晩餐먹자는 것이 目的이니 저녁이나 맛나게 먹고 도라갑시다.」
