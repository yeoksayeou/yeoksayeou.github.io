Arts and Culture News

Ŏm Hŭng-sŏp's Short Story Collection "Kil" to be Published.

Hansŏng Toso Chusik Hoesa (한성도서주식회사), which has completed the publication of the Contemporary Chosŏn Full-Length Novel Collection, will again publish a short story collection by Mr. Ŏm Hŭng-sŏp (嚴興燮), widely known as a short story writer. The title is said to be "Kil" (길), and collected within this creative work are some ten stories including "An'gaesok ŭi Ch'unsam" (안개속의 춘삼), "Pŏn'gyŏn T'alch'ulgi" (번견탈출기), "Kwase" (과세), "Kach'aek" (苛責), "Sungŏ" (숭어), "Him" (힘), "Yumo" (유모), and "Ch'uhwe" (추회), along with "Kil." It is reported that it will be released soon in 4x6 format (46 제), approximately 300 pages, priced at 1 Won 20 Chŏn. Many orders are hoped for.<204>

**Summary**

This news brief announces the upcoming publication of a short story collection titled "Kil" by the author Ŏm Hŭng-sŏp. The publisher is Hansŏng Toso Chusik Hoesa, noted for previously publishing a collection of contemporary Korean novels. The article lists several story titles included in the collection, specifies the book's format, page count, price, and encourages orders. For historians, this article provides concrete details about Korean literary publishing during the colonial period, including the activities of specific authors (Ŏm Hŭng-sŏp) and publishing houses (Hansŏng Toso Chusik Hoesa), book formats, pricing, and the types of works being produced and promoted.

嚴興燮씨 단편집 「길」이 발행되다.
현대 조선 장편소설전집 발행을 완성한 한성도서주식회사에서는 다시 단편작가로 널니 알녀진 엄흥섭씨의 단편집을 발행하리라는데 제명은 「길」이고, 동창작집 가운대 수집된 안개속의 춘삼, 번견탈출기, 과세, 苛責, 숭어, 힘, 유모, 추회, 길 등 10수편으로 불원간 발매되리라는대 46 제 300여공, 정 1원 20전이라고 한다. 만히 주문을 바란다드라.<204>
