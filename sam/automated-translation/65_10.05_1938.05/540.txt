Magazine Title: 삼천리 第10卷 第5號
Publication Date: 1938년 05월 01일
Article Title: 現世의 困苦와 來世의 希望 (Hardship of the Present World and Hope for the Afterlife)
Article Type: 문예기타 (Literary Miscellaneous)

Depending on our spiritual perspective, we can view the world with either optimism or pessimism. However, when one observes human wickedness, suffering (疾苦), and death, it is a world one cannot help but view pessimistically. Then, is this world destined to end entirely in pessimism? Or is there some seed of optimism or point of hope? Examining the true state of these worldly affairs (世事), its foundation was a paradise of happiness, but through the mischievous work (戱作) of sin, sorrow and pain arrived, merely deceiving the future state (영락) with temporary pleasure. Thus, when people are affectionately associating and finding pleasure with those they love, the eternal afterlife (來生) is not so readily believed, and the afterlife seems to belong merely to theory or fantasy, appearing to have no special connection to the joyful and lively present (琨在 - likely 現在).

However, it is amidst hardship (困苦) and sorrow that one first opens one's eyes and comes to acknowledge the afterlife. Of course, there are always those who stubbornly deny the afterlife, but through the impulsive force (파동력) of hardship in the afterlife that was obscured by the fleshly energy (肉氣) of the present world, the clouds and mist (운무) are cleared away, and its real existence (실재) is revealed (발로).

This hardship includes internal hardship (內省의 困苦) and external hardship (외래의 困苦). That is to say, firstly, one confronts internal hardship by delving into the problem of life through self-awareness (자각). Secondly, by encountering external troubles and suffering (우환 疾苦), one comes to realize the hope of the afterlife and simultaneously finds and meets one to trust (신뢰자).

What position are you all in currently (現下), and what problems are you facing?

**Summary**

This article philosophically explores the relationship between suffering in the present world and the hope or belief in an afterlife. It argues that while happiness and pleasure obscure the reality of the afterlife, hardship and sorrow force individuals to confront it. Suffering, whether internal (through introspection) or external (through calamities), acts as a catalyst for recognizing the existence and hope of a future life, leading one to seek faith or a trusted guide. From a historical perspective, this article, published in colonial Korea in 1938, is interesting as it reflects a turn towards spiritual or otherworldly concerns during a period of significant real-world hardship. Historians might find it significant as evidence of coping mechanisms, religious sentiment, or perhaps a subtly expressed yearning for liberation from present difficulties, framed in a non-political, philosophical discourse potentially less vulnerable to censorship.

現世의 困苦와 來世의 希望
우리의 정신주의를 따라서 세상을 낙관할 수도 잇고 비관할 수도 잇다. 그러나 인간의 악성과 疾苦 사망을 본다면 비관할 수밖에는 없는 세상이다. 그러면 아주 비관으로 마칠 세상인가. 혹은 어떤 낙관할 만한 배태이나 희망점이 잇는가. 이 世事의 진상을 살피건대 근본은 행복의 낙원이엿섯지만 죄악의 戱作으로 비애와 고통이 오고 다만 일시적 향락으로 영락을 속이고 잇다. 그리하야 사람들이 그 사랑하는 자로 더부러 친애하고 쾌락할 때에 있어서는 영원한 來生이 그러케 믿어지지 안코 내생이란 한갓 이론이나 공상에 속한 것으로 보여저서 즐겁고 괘활한 琨在에 대하야 아무 특별한 관계가 없어 보이는 것이다. 그러나 困苦와 비애를 당하는 중에서 비로소 눈을 뜨고 내생을 승인하게 되는 것이다. 그야 언제나 완강히 내생을 부인하는 자도 잇지마는 현세의 肉氣에 가리웟든 내생의 困苦의 파동력으로 운무가 걷처지고 그 실재를 발로하는 것이다. 이 困苦는 內省의 困苦와 외래의 困苦가 있으니 즉 말하자면 첫재는 자각으로써 인생 문제에 드러가서 내심의 困苦를 당하는 것이오. 둘새는 외래의 우환 疾苦를 당하야 내생의 희망을 자각하게 되는 동시에 신뢰자를 찾아 맛나는 것이다. 여러분은 現下 어떠한 위치에서 당면하고 잇는 문제가 무엇인가?
