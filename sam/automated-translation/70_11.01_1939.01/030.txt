**Body Text Translation:**
New Year's Address, For the Glory and Prosperity of Asia and the Construction of a New East Asia

A year and a half have already passed since the Empire [of Japan] dispatched the Imperial Army for the eternal peace of East Asia, and already China's central hubs of Shanghai, Nanjing, Guangdong, and the Three Towns of Wuhan have been occupied. With this, the military phase of the Incident has advanced a step, entering the second stage of construction work.

This construction work, in other words, is the new construction of a New East Asian Cooperative Body, aiming for the construction of a self-reliant New Asia through the wholehearted cooperation of the three nations—the Empire of Japan as the leader, Manchukuo, and China. To achieve this,
1. China's participation in the Anti-Comintern Pact
2. The complete liberation of China, which is in a semi-colonial state under Britain and America <32>
must inevitably be accomplished.

Truly, looking upon the 400-odd provinces of China, due to the misguided anti-Japanese and pro-communist policies of the Chiang Kai-shek regime, domestic concessions have largely fallen into the hands of Britain and America, and ideologically, Communism has been welcomed, placing China's sovereignty and the future fate of its 400 million people in a state that is pathetic even to imagine.

Now, we must, of course, dedicate our full strength to serving the accomplishment of the objective of this Holy War for the Empire's construction of a New East Asia, and especially facilitate the mobilization of the National General Mobilization on the home front (銃後). Indeed, all citizens on the home front are warriors in the economic war and warriors in the ideological war. In carrying out a long-term war, every individual must strive with the resolve of a warrior.

From afar, we cannot but pray for the lasting military fortunes of the Imperial Army officers and soldiers active in the lands of extreme cold.

Samch'ŏllisa <33>

**Summary**

This 1939 New Year's editorial from the Korean magazine *Samch'ŏlli* celebrates Japan's military advances in China (capturing major cities) and frames the ongoing conflict as the second stage—"construction"—of a "New East Asia" led by Japan, alongside Manchukuo and China. It calls for China to join the Anti-Comintern Pact and be "liberated" from British and American influence, blaming Chiang Kai-shek's "anti-Japanese, pro-communist" policies for China's woes. The editorial urges Koreans on the "home front" to support Japan's "Holy War" through economic and ideological mobilization, likening them to warriors.

For historians, this article is a stark example of pro-Japanese propaganda disseminated through Korean media during the colonial period. It reveals the extent to which the language of the Greater East Asia Co-Prosperity Sphere and justifications for Japanese imperialism permeated Korean society, framing Japan's aggression as a righteous "Holy War" for regional liberation and stability, while demanding total mobilization and subservience (奉公 - ponggong) from the Korean populace. The explicit denigration of the Chinese Nationalist government and Western powers, coupled with the call for Koreans to act as "warriors" on the home front, highlights the ideological pressures and collaborative rhetoric prevalent at the time.

기사제목	新年辭, 亞細亞의 榮繁과 新東亞 建設을 爲하야
기사형태	잡저

新年辭, 亞細亞의 榮繁과 新東亞 建設을 爲하야
帝國이 東亞 영원의 평화를 위하야 皇師를 進한지 一年有半에 벌서 支那의 중심인 上海, 南京, 廣東과 武漢3鎭까지 점거하게 되여 이에 武力的 事變은 一步進하야 건설공작의 第2段階로 드러가게 되였다.
此 건설공작이라 함은 다시 換言하면 東亞新協同體의 新建設로써 日本帝國을 盟主로 하야 滿洲國과 支那의 3國이 一心協力 자주적인 新亞細亞의 建設을 목적함이니 그러기 위하야
1. 防共協定에의 支那의 참가
2. 英米의 半植民地的 狀態에 있는 支那의 완전한 해방 <32>
을 수성치 않이치 못할 바이다.
진실로 支那 400여 州를 바라볼 때에 蔣介石政權의 그 그릇된 抗日容共 정책때문에 국내의 利權은 擧하야 대부분이 英米의 수중에 歸하고 사상적으로는 共産主義의 迎入이 되야 支那의 주권과 4億萬 인민의 明日의 운명이 상상하기에도 한심한 바가 있게 되였다.
이제 我等은 一意帝國의 東亞新建設을 위한 此 聖戰目的 達成에 총력을 다하야 奉公할 것은 물론이어니와 특히 銃後에 있어 國家總動員의 발동을 至便케 할 바이다. 무릇 銃後의 국민은 모도다 經濟戰의 戰士이요 思想戰의 戰士이라 장기전을 수행함에 잇서 각 개인이 모도다 전사의 각오를 가지고 노력치 않으면 않될 바이다.
곳흐로 멀니 酷寒의 地에 활약하는 皇軍將士의 武運長久를 빌어마지 않노라.
삼천리사 <33>
