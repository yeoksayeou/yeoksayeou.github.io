Resolution of Korean Students in America, at the Eastern Regional Conference

The utmost love for the twenty million Korean people overflows in our hearts. When we hear the cries of our brothers and sisters, when we think of the arduous and perilous future path of our nation, sorrow tears at our hearts, and hot tears cover our faces.

Ah, for our extremely weighty nation, for our extremely beloved Korean people, what shall we offer? Tears of repentance or mere weeping are not what the valiant sons and daughters of our Korean nation should do, and futile sorrow alone cannot be the path to our nation's salvation.

We seek to find the path to the salvation of the Korean people through the discovery of solemn facts, under the light of scientific knowledge, amidst utmost love and sincerity, and with the unwavering spirit of strong men whose resolve does not change even if the heavens fall. By uniting to become a lump like metal and stone, uniting to fight for unwavering justice, uniting to toil our minds and trouble our bodies for pioneering our path of survival, we intend to fulfill our duty of gratitude towards our nation.

What strength could we possibly possess, but perhaps, we might serve as support for the steps of our struggling compatriots, and become a kind of light, however faint, for their dark path ahead. Therefore, we have specifically commissioned research committees to study important issues concerning our nation's future, heard their reports, discussed them sufficiently, established them as the will of this conference, and now announce them widely among our fellow brothers and sisters.

This is firstly to seek the teachings of our predecessors, and secondly to promote deeper research and resolution on these matters among our fellow students. We hope that our seniors will correct our shortsighted views and implement sound views in practice, and that our fellow students will pursue truth to any extent, striving, as time allows and strength permits, solely towards arousing public opinion and casting light. The Second Eastern Regional Conference of the Federation of Korean Students in America resolves this with all love and sincerity.

(This text is the preamble of the full resolution adopted at the conference held by Korean students in America some time ago.) <91>

**Summary**

This article presents the preamble to a resolution passed by Korean students studying in America during their Eastern Regional Conference in 1935. Expressing deep sorrow and love for the suffering Korean nation under colonial rule, the students reject passive grief. They declare their determination to find a path for national salvation through scientific knowledge, unity ("뭉치어"), and dedicated effort. They have commissioned research on key issues and present their findings to seek guidance from elders and encourage further study among peers, aiming to arouse public opinion and bring enlightenment. Historically, this text reveals the active engagement of overseas Korean intellectuals in the nationalist cause during the colonial period. It highlights their awareness of Korea's plight, their belief in modern knowledge and collective action as tools for liberation, and their efforts to contribute despite being far from home. Historians would find the emphasis on scientific knowledge, unity, and the call for broader engagement indicative of the strategies discussed within the educated diaspora for Korea's future.

在米洲朝鮮留學生 決議, 東部大會에서
二千萬 朝鮮民族에 대한 至極한 사랑의 情은 우리가슴에 넘친다. 兄弟姊妹의 우름소리를 드를 때에 우리民族의 艱苦險難한 前程을 생각할 때에 哀痛은 우리의 가슴을 찟고 뜨거운 눈물은 우리의 얼골을 덥는다.
아, 至極히 重한 우리民族, 至極히 사랑하는 우리 朝鮮民族을 위하야 우리는 무엇을 밧칠가. 悔改의 눈물이나 한갓 우름은 우리 朝鮮의 民族의 勇猛한 子女가 할 바 아니오 부즈럽슨 哀痛만은 우리 民族 救援의 길이 되지 못한다.
우리는 嚴正한 事實發見에서, 科學的 知識의 빗 아래에서, 至極한 사랑과 精誠 가운데서, 하늘이 문어지도 變치 안는 줄기찬 丈夫의 氣象에서 朝鮮民族 救援의 길을 찻고자 하며 뭉치어 金石갓흔 덩어리가 되고 뭉치어 百轉不退의 公義을 싸호고 뭉치어 우리 生路開拓에 마음을 썩이며 몸을 괴롭게 함으로서 우리民族에 대한 報恩의 義務를 다하고자 한다.
우리의게 무슨 힘이 잇스리오만은 幸혀나 艱難한 우리同胞의 거름에 부축이 되고 어두운 그 압길에 히미하나마 一種의 빗이 될가 하야 우리民族 前程에 重要한 問題를 특히 調査委員에 委囑하야 硏究케 하고 그 報告를 드리 充分히 討論한 후 本大會의 意思로 定하야 넓히 우리 同胞兄弟 사이에 發表하노라.
이는 첫재 우리 先知者의 가라침을 밧고자 함이며 둘재 우리 同學 여러 벗의 이에 대한 더 깁흔 硏究와 覺悟를 促進케 하고자 함이라. 바라건대 先者은 우리의 短見을 矯正하며 正見을 實地에 行하고 우리 學友는 眞理를 어대까지든지 探求하야 오즉 輿論喚起에 따라 光明投射에 시간이 許하는 대로 힘이 밋치는 대로 努力하기를 바라노라. 在美朝鮮人 留學生總會 東部地方 第 2回 大會는 사랑과 精誠을 다하야 이를 決議하노라.
(이 一文은 얼마 전에 米國잇는 朝鮮人 留學生들이 大會를 開催하고 그 席上에서 決議한 全文의 卷頭다.) <91>
