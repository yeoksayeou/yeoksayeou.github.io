Publication Date: 1938년 12월 01일
Article Title: 編輯后書 (Editor's Postscript)
Author: Not Specified
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editor's Postscript)

We have come to deliver the year-end issue, making us feel once again the rush of the year's affairs (歲事). As the new year approaches, the Holy War (聖戰) will enter its third year, and following the completed capture (攻略) of Hankou (漢口) and Guangdong (廣東), we cannot help but pray solely for the expansion (擴大) of those war results (戰果).

Looking back, the path of this magazine (本誌), which celebrated its 10th year since its founding, was not entirely smooth (順坦) this past year, and we apologize that we occasionally had to skip issues (缺號發行). However, these difficult times (時艱) were not limited to just one magazine company; they affected all organizations and individuals to some degree. We merely intend to push forward (邁進) with new spirit (意氣) starting from the new year.

However, overall, this past year can be seen as a year of cultural advancement (문화약진) for this land. This is because the new publishing culture began to unfold with vigorous momentum starting this year. We earnestly pray that this meaningful trend continues without faltering in the slightest, expanding further and further as we greet the new year. If ten schools opened this year (올에), may there be a hundred next year (明年); if a hundred kinds of newspapers, magazines, and books came out this year, may their number expand to a thousand, to ten thousand next year.

In any case, it was a year of many transformations (轉變). During this year, unable to resist the trend of the times (時流), numerous figures sank (沈落) below the horizon, while conversely, many new faces emerged. Also, among various organizations, many disappeared and many new ones were formed. We naturally recall (回願) that there have rarely been times when the rise and fall (興亡起伏) of our society were as frequent as this year. <300>

**Summary**

This editor's postscript from the December 1938 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* reflects on the past year. The editor apologizes for occasional missed issues due to hardships ("時艱"), acknowledges the ongoing Sino-Japanese War ("聖戰") celebrating recent military victories (Hankou, Guangdong) and hoping for further "expansion of war results." Despite difficulties, the editor views the year positively as one of "cultural advancement," particularly in publishing, and hopes for exponential growth in education and publishing in the coming year. The piece concludes by noting the significant social flux, with many individuals and organizations rising and falling. From a historical perspective, the article is revealing in its normalization and endorsement of Japan's war effort ("Holy War," "expansion of war results") within a Korean publication. It also highlights the editor's focus on cultural development (specifically publishing) amidst wartime constraints and societal upheaval, offering insight into the complex dynamics of colonial modernity and wartime mobilization in Korea.

編輯后書
어느덧 송년호를 보내드리게 되니 歲事의 蒼慌함이 다시 또한번 느껴지나이다. 이제 새해로써 聖戰도 第3年을 마지하게되여 漢口 廣東의 攻略을 完了한 뒤를 이어서 오직 그 戰果의 擴大를 빌어마지 안는 바이외다.

도리켜 생각하면 창간후 10년을 마지한 本誌의 今 1年의 거름은 그리 順坦한 바가 아니어서 間或 缺號發行한 적도 있어 미안하였읍니다. 그러나 이 時艱은 一 雜誌社에 限한 것이 아니라 모든 기관과 개인에게 모다 다소의 영향이 있었든 바라, 오직 새해부터 새로운 意氣로써 邁進하고저 할 뿐이외.

그러나 대체로 보면 이 한해는 이 땅의 문화약진의 해라고 볼 것이니 그것은 새로운 출판문화가 활발한 기세로써 전개되기가 금년으로부터 시작한 때문이외다. 이 뜻있는 긔운이 조곰치나 꺽기는 일이 없이 새해를 마지하면서 더욱더욱 버더저 나가기를 빌어 마지 안습니다. 올에 학교가 열이 되었다면 明年에는 百으로 올해에 신문과 잡지와 서적 百種이 나왔다면 明年에는 그 수가 천으로 만으로 더 버더저 나가기를 바라서 마지 안습니다.

아무튼 轉變이 많은 1년이엇읍니다. 이 한해에 時流에 逆할 수 없어 多數한 人士가 水平線 아래로 沈落하기도 하엿고 그대신 신인의 등장이 또한 많었고 그리고 여러 기관중 없어진 것도 새로된 것도 많었으니 우리 사회의 興亡起伏이 이해가치 자즌 때가 드문 것을 저절로 回願하게 되나이다. ＜300＞
