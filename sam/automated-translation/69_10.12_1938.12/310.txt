Promise of the Beloved/Lord
						Ch'unwŏn

Where is this place? From where did I come here?
What task was I sent to do, and where am I going?
I stand looking at the endless road before and behind me.

I know it was the Beloved/Lord who sent me away,
But having forgotten who that Beloved/Lord is, and even the errand,
I wonder where I have wandered aimlessly to arrive here.

Fumbling, fumbling through hazy old memories,<204>
Wandering, searching this path and that, getting lost,
Yet, I wonder, could there be a path that is not the Beloved/Lord's path? Should I go?

Becoming the sunlight by day, becoming the starlight by night,
Becoming the roadside rock, becoming the water, becoming the water,
Sometimes becoming a traveler, You guide me.

Because there is joy, because there is sorrow,
Met upon the road, they are fleeting scenes.
When I finally enter the Beloved/Lord's house, I wonder if I will see eternity.

The Beloved/Lord whose face I've forgotten, whose name I've even forgotten,
The reason I seek, seek, fumbling along an unknown path is
Because the beautiful promise of the Beloved/Lord is not forgotten.<205>

**Summary**

This poem by Ch'unwŏn (Yi Kwangsu) describes a spiritual or existential journey. The speaker feels lost and has forgotten their origin, destination, and the identity of the "Beloved/Lord" who sent them. Despite this amnesia, the speaker perseveres, believing the Lord guides them through nature and encounters, and driven solely by the lingering memory of the Lord's promise. The journey encompasses both joy and sorrow, viewed as transient aspects before reaching the final destination. From a historical perspective, the poem reflects a common theme in Yi Kwangsu's work: spiritual searching and yearning. Written in 1938, as colonial pressures intensified and Yi's own ideology was complexifying, historians might analyze the ambiguous figure of the "Beloved/Lord" and the theme of a forgotten yet binding promise in the context of Korea's situation or Yi's personal philosophical/religious explorations during that era.

임의 언약
						春園
이곳이 어드메오? 내 어디서 여기 온고?
무슨 일을 보러어  드메로 가는 걸고?
앞 뒤로 끝 없는 길을 보고 섯는 나여라

날 떠내 보내신 이 임이신줄 알건 만은
그 임이 누구신지 심부름도 다 잊고서
어디로 두루 쏘다녀 예 왔는가 하노라

흐린 옛 기억을 더듬더듬 더듬어서＜204＞
이 길 저길 찾아 가다 오다 헤매어도
임의 길 아닌 길 있으리가고 갈까 하노라

낮에는 해 빛 되사 밤이면은 별 빛 되사
길갓 바위 되사, 물이되사, 물이되사
때로는 행인 되시와 나를 끌어 주서라

낙이 있삽기로 괴로움이 있삽기로
노상에 만났으니 있다 없을 풍경이라.
다 가서 임 집에 들때 영원 볼까 하노라

얼굴도 잊은 임을 이름 조차 잊은 임을
모를 길 더듬어서 찾아 찾아 가는 뜻은
고으신 임의 언약이 안 잊혀서 입니다.＜205＞
