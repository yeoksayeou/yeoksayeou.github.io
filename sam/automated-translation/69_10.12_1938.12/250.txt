"Camellia" (椿姬)
Shanghai Kwangmyŏng Film Company (上海 光明影業公司) Production
Directed by I P'yŏngch'ŏng (李萍淸)
Starring Wŏn Miun (袁美雲) as Marŏ(?) / Lilli, Yu Kyŏng (劉瓊) as Taamon / Amon
Coming Soon to Kyŏngsŏng (京城)

It was at a charity ball that Lilli (利利), the flower of Sanghae's (上海) high society, met the young lawyer Tu Amon (杜亞蒙).
Lilli, while recuperating from tuberculosis, was cherished as a substitute daughter by old Mr. Kim (金영감), who could not forget his own daughter who had died of the same illness, and lived a splendid life under his protection.
Knowing she was frail, Lilli threw herself into the social scene with a self-destructive attitude towards life. However, upon realizing young Amon's pure passion, Lilli's heart felt as if it had met spring, becoming happy, and she gained a burning desire to try living again.
One day, while strolling with Amon and friends in the green-wooded suburbs, Lilli thought how nice it would be to leave the bustle of the social world entirely and live in such a quiet countryside. So, Lilli obtained some money from old Mr. Kim, and Amon also got money from his father, calling it travel expenses, and they set up a true love nest in the suburbs not far from Sanghae.
Happy times flowed for the two, but when this story reached the social circles, old Mr. Kim became indignant and cut off Lilli's allowance, causing the couple's life to soon face economic hardship. At Amon's home, not only were there marriage talks for his sister who was of marrying age, but also, thinking of the future of their promising son Amon, his father personally visited Lilli and pleaded with her to break up with Amon. The kind-hearted Lilli was deeply impressed by the parent's love for his child and declared she would separate from him.
Amon, partly out of self-abandonment, tried his hand at gambling, but his luck was poor, and he suffered a crushing defeat. Having no other choice, he returned to their hideaway, only to be met with Lilli's seemingly brusque coldness. Now feeling boundless disappointment, he turned and left. Debts piled up relentlessly, debt collectors pressured her without mercy, coming near Lilli's sickbed, and not only her household possessions but even the house she lived in were put up for auction.
Amon, learning of the situation from his father's letter, rushed back. In silence, their hands grasped each other firmly, and the flame of love flared up again between them. But by then, the painful tuberculosis had already eaten deep into her chest, and her poignant and fleeting life was quietly coming to a close. <177>

**Summary**

This article announces the upcoming screening in Seoul (Kyŏngsŏng) of the Chinese film "Ch'unhŭi" (椿姬), an adaptation of "La Dame aux Camélias," produced in Shanghai. It details the plot: Lilli, a socialite with tuberculosis supported by an elderly patron, falls for a lawyer, Amon. They move to the countryside but face financial ruin and pressure from Amon's father to separate for the sake of Amon's future and his sister's marriage prospects. Lilli sacrifices her love, pushing Amon away. Her health deteriorates severely amid mounting debts. Amon learns the truth and returns for a final reconciliation just before her death.

From a historical perspective, the article is interesting as it shows the importation and promotion of Chinese cinema in colonial Korea in 1938, highlighting cultural flows within East Asia even during the tense period following the outbreak of the Second Sino-Japanese War in 1937. It demonstrates that popular entertainment in Seoul included foreign films, specifically adaptations of Western literary classics produced in other parts of Asia like Shanghai, indicating a layer of shared modern cultural consumption.

上海 光明影業公司 製作

不日京城切

上海의 社交界의 꽃 利利가 청년변호사 杜亞蒙을 맞난 것은 慈善 舞踏會 밤이다.
利利는 폐병으로 요양하는 중에 같은 병으로 죽은 딸울 못 잊는 金영감님으로부터 그 딸의 대신으로 귀염을 받으며 그 보호 아래서 화려한 생활을 하고 있다.
몸이 약한 줄을 알면서도 利利가 사교계에 나서게 된 것은 이러한 생활태도의 自爆自棄的 행동이 있는데 청년 亞蒙의 순진한 열의를 알게 되자 利利의 마음도 봄을 맞난 듯 행복되여저서 다시 살어 보겠다는 불타는 마음을 갖이게 되였다.
어떤 날 산보로 亞蒙과 利利는 友人들과 같이 숲이 푸른 교외에 나갔을 때 利利는 사교계의 번잡함을 아주 떠나서 이런 조용한 싀골에 살었으면 하는 생각을 했다. 그래서 利利는 金영감으로부터 얼마간의 돈을 얻고 亞蒙도 여행비라 하고 그 부친으로부터 돈을 어더서 上海에서 멀지 않은 교외에 그야말로 사랑의 보금자리를 꾸몃다.
행복된 세월이 두 사람에게 흘넜다마는 이런 이얘기가 사교계에 들니자 金영감은 분개하야 利利에게 돈주는 것을 끊인 까닭에 두 사람의 생활은 이내 경제적 고난 우에 서게 되었다. 亞蒙의 집에선 때마침 혼기에 있는 누이동생의 緣談이 있을 뿐 아니라 前述有望한 자식 亞蒙의 장래를 생각하여 그 부친이 친히 利利에게 찿어가 亞蒙과 갈나지기를 간청하였다. 자식을 생각하는 어버이의 마음에 마음 착한 利利는 탄복되여서 갈나질 것을 선언했다.
亞蒙은 自爆自棄하는 마음도 있어서 도박에 손을 대였으나 그도 운수가 시언치 안어 참패를 당하고 할수헐수없이 숨어사는 집에 도라온즉 利利의 손을 번접이 하는 듯한 냉대에 이제는 끗없는 실망을 하고 도라 나가버린다. 싸히고 싸히는 빗에 債鬼는 용서없이 피박하며 利利의 병상 갓가히 와서 家財器物은 물론 쓰고 사는 집깐까지 경매에 부치게 되었다.
아버지로부터의 편지로 사정을 들은 亞蒙이 달녀와서 무언의 속에 두 사람의 손은 굳세게 서로 쥐워젔을 때 사랑의 불길에 둘은 다시 타올났다. 그러나 그때에는 이미 가슴속 깊이 파먹는 쓰라린 페병때문에 쪄르고도 시산한 일생은 고요히 다처지고 마는 때이였다.＜177＞
