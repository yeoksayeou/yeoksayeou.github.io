Article Title: 내가 서울 女市長된다면? (If I Were to Become the Female Mayor of Seoul?)
Author: 張德祚
Article Type: 설문 (Survey/Questionnaire Response)

If I Were to Become the Female Mayor of Seoul?
						Chang Tŏk-cho

1. I would carry out urban district reforms focusing mainly on public health and housing issues.

2. The establishment of city-run nurseries for working women.<58>

**Summary**

Chang Tŏk-cho, responding to a survey question about what she would do as the female mayor of Seoul, states her priorities would be urban reform focused on public health and housing, and the establishment of city-run daycare centers for working women. This brief response is interesting to historians as it reveals a woman's perspective on pressing urban social issues in colonial Seoul during the 1930s, highlighting concerns for public welfare, infrastructure, and the specific needs of working women, suggesting a progressive viewpoint for the time.
