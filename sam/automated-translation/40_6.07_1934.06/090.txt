Life's Bulletin Board

These are the "fathers of sorrow" who are currently in mourning (哀痛中) having lost their beloved sons and daughters. Everyone, please offer your condolences (吊辭).

Poet Yi Ha-yun
Critic Sŏ Ch'un

Above are recent cases, below are older news

Poet Kim An-sŏ
Novelist Yi Kwang-su
Critic Hye Ŭi-sik (薢義植氏) <40>

**Summary**

This short notice lists prominent Korean literary figures—poets, critics, and a novelist—who had recently or previously lost children. It invites readers to offer condolences to these "fathers of sorrow." From a historical perspective, the article is interesting as it publicly acknowledges the private grief of well-known intellectuals during the colonial period. It humanizes these figures beyond their public roles and provides a glimpse into the social custom of sharing such news within the community via print media.
