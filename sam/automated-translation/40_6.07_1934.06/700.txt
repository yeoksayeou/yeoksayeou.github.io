剌客과 나
						나파륜 1世

Today on the training ground, an officer stopped a seventeen-year-old boy, the son of a farmhand, who tried to rush close to my person. He resisted this. Suspicious, the officer searched the boy and found a dagger (短刀) in his bosom. I had him brought before me.

"What did you seek to achieve regarding me?"

"Only to assassinate Your Majesty."

"What wrong have I done to you? Who made you aware that I am the Emperor?"

"I wanted to end the war."

"Why did you not go to the French Emperor?"

"What business would I have with him? I do not place importance on him. Besides, even if he died, someone else would succeed him. But after Your Majesty is gone, the French army will disappear forever from Germany."

"Do you regret it?"

"No."

"Will you attempt to harm me again?"

"Yes."<162>

**Summary**

This short piece, presented as a monologue or recollection by "Napoleon I," recounts an encounter with a seventeen-year-old German boy who attempted assassination. When questioned, the boy states his motive was to end the war by killing Napoleon, believing his death, unlike that of the French Emperor (presumably a distinct figure in this context, or perhaps a misunderstanding/artistic license), would lead to the withdrawal of French troops from Germany. The boy remains defiant and unrepentant. For colonial Korean history, this article is interesting as it explores themes of assassination as political resistance against a dominant foreign power (France in Germany). Published during the Japanese occupation of Korea, the story of a young person attempting to kill a powerful emperor figure to achieve national liberation or end a conflict could resonate with, or subtly comment on, the Korean situation and the motivations behind resistance acts against Japanese rule, even if set in a European historical context.

오늘 연병장에서 머슴꾼의 아들이란 열일곱에 나는 소년이 나의 신변에 갓가히 달려들려 함을 사관이 이를 막엇다. 그는 이에 저항하엿다. 이에 사관이 수상하여 그 소년을 수색하여 본즉 가슴에서 단도가 나왓다. 나는 그를 면전에 대려오게 하엿다.
「너는 짐에 대하야 무엇을 구하려느냐」
「폐하를 살해코저 할 뿐」
「짐은 너에게 무슨 못할 짓을 하엿던가. 누가 짐이 황제인 것을 알니더냐.
「나는 전쟁을 끗치게 하고 십헛소이다.」
「엇재서 너는 불란서 황제잇는데로 가지 안엇든가」
「그와 무슨 때문에요? 나는 그에게 치중하지 안슴니다. 또한 그가 죽을지라도 누가 또 그 뒤를 이으리다. 그러치만 폐하 업슨 뒤는 불란서 군대는 독일에서 영영 업서지리다.」
「너는 후회하느냐?」
「아니오」
