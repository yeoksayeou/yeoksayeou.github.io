Magazine Title: 삼천리 (三千里) 제6권 제7호
Publication Date: 1934-06-01
Article Title: 서울 오서서 몃 번이나 移舍하섯슴니까 (How Many Times Have You Moved House Since Coming to Seoul?)
Author: 이하윤 (Yi Hayun), 김안서 (Kim Ansŏ), 김동인 (Kim Tongin)
Article Type: 설문 (Survey)

Yi Hayun

I lived in Wŏn-dong for a full 4 years, and last spring, I moved to Sŏdaemun-chŏng 1-chome - the house where I live now. Since I had intended to move soon after meeting that year in Wŏn-dong, I felt quite relieved when I left. But as you know, is there anything as troublesome as moving house in Seoul?
It's a huge "uproar" (『날니』). Because the families of both the old house and the new house have to be in turmoil for about half a month each. There's not even anything worth calling an impression. I've already started to get tired of this house too, which I came to with the intention of living in for a while. It's a problem I don't even want to think about, wondering when I'll ever be able to have a residence where I can settle down.
During my student days in Tokyo, I moved lodgings almost every year, or even every semester, but each time I moved, I soaked in the new atmosphere and studied quite diligently (『오씨쓰꾸』) for a few months. Moving being a good thing is a story from when one was single; moreover, with the household affairs of a Korean person, is there anything more troublesome than this?<135>

Kim Ansŏ

Since there is no stability in life, one always ends up wandering around, so there isn't much interest in something like a philosophy of moving for city dwellers. There might be considerable interest in moving voluntarily, but if not, what kind of impression could there be in moving house due to some unavoidable circumstances? If I had to say, it would be the displeasure of self-pity. It's just that the lament of my situation, being unable to avoid wandering around every single one of the 365 days a year as if the earth itself is moving, comes to mind once in a while. Therefore, there is likely no one who hasn't lived in Seoul for the most part.
For a person who is not a snail, the circumstance of being unable to avoid moving house at any time is an extremely distressing matter.<137>

Kim Tongin

Since coming to Seoul, I have not yet moved. If this current house (Haengch'on-dong) sells, I intend to move inside the city gates. I imagine that when I move, I will feel a sense of mystery about the new house.<139>

**Summary**

This article compiles responses from three Korean intellectuals (Yi Hayun, Kim Ansŏ, Kim Tongin) in 1934 regarding their experiences with moving house in Seoul. Yi Hayun finds moving burdensome, contrasting the hassle of family moves in Seoul with the more refreshing experience of changing lodgings as a student in Tokyo. Kim Ansŏ views frequent moving as a sign of life's instability and a source of distress, devoid of any philosophical interest unless done voluntarily. Kim Tongin, having not moved yet since arriving in Seoul, anticipates feeling a "sense of mystery" upon eventually moving. The article reflects the housing instability and difficulties faced by urban dwellers, particularly Korean families, in colonial Seoul. Historians might find the contrasts drawn between Seoul and Tokyo, the references to specific neighborhoods, the explicit mention of the burdens of Korean family life ('살림사리'), and the general sense of precariousness illuminating aspects of urban social conditions and colonial modernity in 1930s Korea.

異河潤

苑洞서 만 4년, 작년 봄에 西大門町 1丁目- 지금 사는 집으로 옴겨왓습니다. 苑洞서는 그해 만나고 곳 옴겨 안즈려든 터이라 떠나고 보니 퍽 시언한 늣김이 잇섯슬 뿐이엇습니다마는 아시다십히 서울 이사저럼 귀찬은게 어듸 또 잇겟습니까.
큰 『날니』지요. 헌집 새집서 반달식은 두 집안 식구가 들끄러야만 되니까요. 감회라 할만한 것조차 업습니다. 얼마동안은 사라보려고 온 이 집도 벌서 실증이 나기 시작햇습니다. 언제나 안주할 주택을 가질 수 잇슬넌지 생각도 하기 실흔 문제올시다.
동경 학창시대에는 거이 해마다 혹은 학기마다도 하숙을 옴겨다녓지만 옴겨안즐 적마다 새 기분에 저저 몃 달 동안은 무척 『오씨쓰꾸』해서 공부를 하고는 하엿습니다. 이사가 조흔 것도 혼자몸 시대이야기지 더구나 조선사람의 살림사리로는 예서 더 귀치안흔게 어듸 잇겟습니까.<135>

金岸曙

생활에 대한 안정이 업는지라 언제던지 떠들아 단이게 되니 새삼스러이 도회인으로의 이사철학이라느 흥미가 잇슬 것도 아니외라. 자진하야 하는 이사에는 적지 안이한 흥미도 잇슬 것이나 그러치 아니하고 엇든 할 수 업는 사정에 집을 옴기는 곳에 무슨 감회가 잇슬 것 입닛가 말하라면 그것은 자기가 자기에 대한 憐憫으로의 불쾌겟지요. 1년 365일을 하로가치 지구가 좀 가는 듯이 떠들아 다니지 아니할 수 업는 신세 타령이 한번씩 생각될 뿐이외라. 그러니 서울서 대개는 살아보지 아니하엿슬 이가 업는 일이외다.
달팽이로 아닌 사람으로서는 은제든지 집을 옴기지 아니할 수 업는 사정이란 대단이 괴로운 일이외다.<137>

金東仁

상경 이래 아직 이사를 못햇습니다. 지금 이 집(杏村洞)이 팔리면 문안으로 이사를 가려합니다. 짐작컨대 이사할 때는 새집에 대한 神秘味를 感할 뜻합니다.<139>
