The Great Lion's Roar of Wartime Patriots!!, Stenographic Record of the Great Oratorical Meeting for Wartime National Service

Our homeland, Asia, has for the past several hundred years suffered exploitation and aggression under the oppression of the Anglo-American powers. China was thus, as were India, Thailand, and the entire Malay Peninsula. In response, our Empire [of Japan] dispatched a million Imperial soldiers, from the Soviet-Manchurian border in the far north down to the 400 provinces of the Central Plains and French Indochina in the south, to expel the savage enemy powers and construct a Greater East Asia based on a new order. Already five years have passed since this holy war began! Still, the clouds of war in the north and south grow more urgent by the day, and we face a critical situation demanding a wartime footing of national armament. In response, the <15> patriots of the peninsula resolutely rose up and, through voluntary civilian will, formed the Wartime Countermeasures Cooperation Association (臨戰對策協力會), delivering its first pronouncements at the Kyŏngsŏng Civic Hall (京城 府民館) on September 4th with the following topics. We hereby record this historic stenographic record of the speeches and widely disseminate it to the world. **Summary**

This article from the November 1941 issue of "Samch'ŏlli" introduces and presents the program of a large pro-Japanese rally held in Kyŏngsŏng (Seoul). The introductory text frames the ongoing Second Sino-Japanese War (extending into the Pacific) as a "holy war" waged by the Japanese Empire to liberate Asia from Anglo-American oppression. It announces the formation of the "Wartime Countermeasures Cooperation Association" by Korean "patriots" to support Japan's war effort and lists the prominent Korean figures who gave speeches at the inaugural event. For historians, this piece is a stark example of high-profile Korean collaboration during the late colonial period, showcasing the rhetoric used to mobilize Korean support for Japan's imperial ambitions under the banner of creating a "Greater East Asia Co-Prosperity Sphere" just before the Pacific War escalated with the attack on Pearl Harbor. The list of speakers includes well-known, influential Koreans openly aligning themselves with the Japanese wartime regime.

우리들의 향토 亞細亞는 과거 수백년간을 두고 英米들 열강의 압제하에 착취와 침략을 받아왔다. 支那가 그리하였고, 印度, 泰國, 馬來半島 모도가 그러하였다. 이에 우리제국은 백만의 皇師를 멀리 북으로는 蘇滿國境에서 남으로는 中原400여주와 佛印에까지 파하여 포악한 敵性세력을 驅遂하고 신질서에 위한 大東亞를 건설코저 성전을 시작한지 이미 5년! 아직도 남북의 풍운은 날이 갈수록 더 긴박하여 국민무장화의 임전체제를 요하는 중대시국에 직면하였다. 이에 반도의 ＜15＞ 애국자군은 단연 궐기하여 민간 자발적 뜻으로 臨戰對策協力를 결성하고 9월4일 京城 府民館에서 아래의 연제로 그 第一聲을 발하였다. 이 역사적 연설의 속기록을 여기 수록하여 널리 世에 공하는 바이노라.
