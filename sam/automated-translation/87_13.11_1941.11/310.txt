Editor's Postscript

At this time when the waves of the Pacific are becoming quite rough as Britain and America further reveal their hostility through the ABCD encirclement (ABCD包圍陣), we believe our nation indeed faces a pressing situation where the entire country must unite, the whole populace must arm itself, and prepare a wartime footing. Meeting such an extraordinary time, our company hosted a large roundtable discussion called the "Emergency Countermeasures Council" (臨時對策協議會) by inviting 120 distinguished figures from the financial, press, ideological, and religious communities, aiming for the practical realization of the people's voluntary public service (義勇奉公化). We consider this a significant gathering under the current circumstances. Furthermore, we are confident that organizing this council as a standing body and holding a "Great Wartime Lecture Meeting" (臨戰大演說會), featuring the powerful orations (獅子吼) of Mr. Yun Ch'iho (尹致昊), Mr. Ch'oe Rin (崔麟), and five others as a special feature, is a rare harvest, and we believe this will greatly contribute to the path forward for the Korean people (朝鮮人).

□

Moreover, as icing on the cake (錦上添花), the report by Mr. Sŏ Ch'un (徐椿), who attended the East Asia Newspaper Reporters Conference held this time in Canton (廣東) and observed the Central and South China (中南支) region, is published exclusively in this journal, and we trust it will be a good read.

□

As the second special feature of our cultural lecture series (誌上文化講座), we mobilized the faculty of Yonhi College (延禧專門學校) to provide new articles on economics, science, law, religion, sports, etc., not seen in last month's cultural lecture special. We are confident this too will be a great help (도음) to our cultural world. Due to page limitations, [some articles] will be published next month.

□

In the literary section, we have specially featured overseas war literature, including war novels and war poems from Germany (獨逸), Italy (伊太利), and France (佛蘭西). (To be continued next issue)

★ Recruiting Samch'ŏlli Branch Offices

We are widely establishing branch offices in Seoul and various provincial locations. Those interested may request our internal regulations, which we will send. We hope many will establish branches.

To Our Readers

As this is a time of extreme paper conservation, we cannot print more than a predetermined number of copies in advance. If you order after the month's issue is released, there is often concern it will be sold out. Therefore, please send payment for a half-year or full-year subscription in advance and become an annual subscriber (年定讀者).
＜156＞

**Summary**

This editor's postscript from November 1941 highlights the magazine *Samch'ŏlli*'s recent activities and content against the backdrop of escalating tensions in the Pacific (the "ABCD encirclement"). It promotes a large pro-war mobilization roundtable ("Emergency Countermeasures Council") involving prominent Korean figures, features exclusive reports from China, and showcases cultural and literary content, including war literature from Axis and occupied nations. It concludes with calls for new branch offices and advance subscriptions due to wartime paper shortages. For historians, the article is significant as it vividly illustrates the propaganda and mobilization efforts undertaken by Korean media outlets under Japanese colonial rule immediately preceding the Pacific War. The explicit mention of the "ABCD encirclement," the call for Koreans to adopt a "wartime footing," and the participation of figures like Yun Ch'iho and Ch'oe Rin in pro-Japanese events clearly reflect the pressures and political climate of the time.

編輯後記
ABCD包圍陣으로 英米는 더욱 敵性을 露骨化하므로써 太平洋물결이 자못 거츠려가는 이때, 實로 我國은 擧國一致, 國民全體가 武裝하고 臨戰態勢를 가추어야할 緊迫한 時局에 直面할줄 아나이다. 이러한 超非常時 에 際會하여 國民의 義勇奉公化의 實踐을 爲해서 本社에서는 財界, 言論界 思想團體, 宗敎團體의 名士百二十名을 招請하여「臨時對策協議會」라는 大座談會를 開催한것은 이 時局下 意義깊은 모임이라 생각하는 바이며, 더욱이 協議會를 常設機關으로 組織하여「臨戰大演說會」를 開催하여 써 尹致昊, 崔麟兩氏外에 五氏의 獅子吼를 特輯한것은 드물게 보는 收穫이라 自信하며, 이로써 朝鮮人의 進路에 貢獻함이 크리라고 생각하나이다.
□
더욱이 錦上添花로 今番廣東에서 開催된 東亞新聞記者大會에 參席하고 中南支一帶를 視察한 徐椿氏의 報告記드 本誌의 獨占記事로 好讀物이라 믿나이다.
□
誌上文化講座 第二回의 特輯으로 延禧專門學校敎授陣을 動員하여 經濟, 科學, 法津, 宗敎, 體育等 前月號文化講座特輯에서 보지못하던 새로운 記事도 또한 우리 文化界에 큰 도음이라 自信하나이다. 頁數關係로 來月揭載
□
文藝欄에 있어서는 特히 海外戰爭文學을 特輯하여, 獨逸, 伊太利, 佛蘭西, 諸國의 戰爭小說과 戰爭詩를 실었나이다. (次號繼續)

★三千里支社募集

京鄕各處 支社를 널리 設置하오니 留意하시는분은 內規를 請하시면 보내드리겠사오니 많이 設置하여 주시기를 바라나이다.

讀者諸氏에게

지금은 用紙를 極度로 節約하는때이므로, 미리 一定한 部數밖에 印刷하지 못합니다. 그달 冊이 나온뒤 注文하시려면 흔히 品切의 念慮있으니 미리, 半年分 又는 一年分의 代金을 보내주시어 年定讀者듸어 주옵소서.
