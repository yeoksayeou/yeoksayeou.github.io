**Beauties' Annals**

Dancer Pae Ku-ja's Movements (裵龜子 動靜)

Dancer Pae Ku-ja (裵龜子), who always received applause and cheers for revealing her lovely figure on stage, was reported to be going to Germany (독일) and France (불란서) to study dance, but changed her path and entered Tōkyō (東京) where she is engrossed in dance research. It is said she will likely return around this autumn and once again show her lovely dancing form before the white-clad gentlemen (白衣 人士).

Miss Ha So-yang's Advance (河小楊여사의 進出)

Mr. Na Un-gyu (羅雲奎) of Ch'unsa (春史), who had not shown his popular presence on the silver screen for a long time, recently completed "Kaehwadang Imun" (開化黨異聞), based on the life story of Kim Ok-kyun (金玉均), which will be screened at Tansŏngsa (團成社) from early June. At that time, Miss Ha So-yang (河小楊) was the sole beautiful woman who captivated the entire audience of fans. Miss Ha is an intellectual woman who once threw herself into the ideological movement in her hometown of Kyŏngsang Province and also engaged in social movements, and who entered the film world after some contemplation.
It is the cry of many fans that they wish for her to continue appearing in various works following the "Kaehwadang" production.

Rumor of Miss Kim Myŏng-sun as a Model (金明淳여사의 모델說)

Novelist Miss Kim Myŏng-sun (金明淳), who gained great fame in the peninsula's literary circles as a female writer after publishing the piece "Ŭimunŭi Sonyŏ" (의문의 소녀 - The Mysterious Girl) in "Ch'ŏngch'un" (靑春), dragged her unfortunate self to Tōkyō (東京) and disappeared. According to recent word, there is a rumor (風說) that she tried to work as a film actress at some motion picture studio, but finding many things not to her liking, she changed course again and has been going around artists' studios working as a model. In any case, this nearby talented person seems to be living quite unfortunately.

Miss Na Chŏng-wŏl's Return Home (羅晶月 여사의 귀향)

Female painter Miss Na Hye-sŏk (羅蕙錫), who went to Tōkyō (東京) and received special recognition at the Imperial Exhibition (帝展), recently returned to Sŏul. It is said she did not submit anything to the art exhibition (미전) held recently at Kyŏngbok Palace.<9>

**Summary**

This article provides brief updates on the activities and circumstances of four prominent Korean female figures in the arts during the colonial period in 1932. Dancer Pae Ku-ja is studying dance in Tokyo instead of Europe. Actress Ha So-yang has made a popular return to film in Na Un-gyu's new movie. Writer Kim Myŏng-sun is rumored to be struggling financially in Tokyo, potentially working as an artist's model after failed attempts at acting. Painter Na Hye-sŏk has returned to Seoul from Tokyo after success at the Imperial Exhibition but did not participate in a recent local exhibition. For historians, the article offers glimpses into the careers, aspirations, and sometimes precarious situations of 'New Women' artists under Japanese rule, navigating opportunities and hardships both in Korea and Japan (Tokyo). It highlights their visibility, the public interest in their lives, and the challenges they faced, reflecting aspects of cultural life and gender roles during that era.

佳人春秋
무용가 裵龜子 動靜

어엽분 자태를 스테-지에 나타내어 늘 박수 갈채를 밧든 무용가 裵龜子씨는 독일과 불란서로 춤 공부를 간다고 전하더니 길을 밧구어 東京에 드러가서 무용 연구에 열중하는 중인데 아마 올 가를 쯔음에 도라나와서 다시 白衣 人士 압헤서 어엽분 춥추는 모양을 보여드리게 되리라고.

河小楊여사의 進出

오랫동안 은막에 그 인기 잇는 자최를 나타나지 안코 잇든 春史 羅雲奎씨는 이번에 金玉均 일대기를 제재로 한 「開化黨異聞」을 완성하여 6월 초순부터 團成社에서 상영한다는데 그 때 유일의 미모의 여성으로 滿堂의 팬을 뇌쇄한 이에 河小楊여사가 잇섯다. 여사는 일즉 고향인 경상도에서 사상운동에 몸을 던저 사회운동도 하여오든 인테리 여성으로 생각한 바 잇서 영화계에 투신한 이다.
「開化黨」작품에 계속하여 또 여러 가지 작품에 출연하여 주기를 바람은 다수 팬이 모다 부르짓는 소리다.

金明淳여사의 모델說

「靑春」에 「의문의 소녀」 一篇을 발표한 뒤 閨秀 작가로써 半島 문단에 盛名을 날니든 소설가 金明淳여사는 불우한 몸을 東京에 잇글어 자최를 감추어 버렷드니 최근 들니는 말에 어느 활동사진 촬영소에서 영화배우로 활약하려다가 뜻과 갓지 못한 일이 만허서 다시 轉身하여 화가의 화실을 도라다니며 모델로 다니드란 풍설이 잇다. 아무튼 갓가운 이 才人 한 분은 퍽으나 불우하게 지내시는 모양.

羅晶月 여사의 귀향

東京에 드러가서 帝展에 특선이 된 閨秀화가 羅蕙錫여사는 최근에 서울로 도라왓는데 이번 경복궁에 열닌 미전에는 아모 것도 출품하지 안엇다는 말이 잇다.<9>
