Magazine Title: 三千里 제4권 제7호
Publication Date: 1932-06-15
Article Title: 편집후기 (Editor's Postscript)
Author: N/A
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editor's Postscript)

Already, the *sambok* (three hottest days of summer) have arrived. At times like these, one wishes to see Paektu Mountain covered in white snow. Thus, we have made the cover a snowy landscape of Paektu Mountain.

We apologize for being unable to publish the early June issue due to unavoidable circumstances. We are now publishing the mid-month issue in advance, and we will also advance the early July issue and release it in late June. Please love 「三千里」 (Samch'ŏlli) even more and also protect it.

The 「詩歌集」 (Poetry and Song Collection) by the Siga Tonginhoe (詩歌同人會 - Poetry and Song Colleagues' Association) was postponed because the membership quota was slightly insufficient, but it will likely be published sometime in July.
<42>

**Summary**

This editor's postscript from the June 15, 1932 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* explains the choice of Paektu Mountain's snowy landscape for the cover, attributing it to a longing for coolness during the summer heat. The editor apologizes for missing the early June issue and announces an adjusted publishing schedule, releasing the mid-June issue early and planning to release the early July issue in late June. It also mentions a delay in publishing a poetry collection by the Siga Tonginhoe due to insufficient membership, expecting it in July. For historians, the text provides insight into the operational challenges faced by Korean magazines under Japanese rule (missed issues), efforts to maintain reader loyalty, the use of national symbols like Paektu Mountain, and the linguistic features (archaic spellings and grammar) of Korean print media in the 1930s. The mention of a poetry association also points to ongoing cultural activities.

편집후기
벌서 삼복이 왓슴니다. 이런 때는 백설이 덥힌 백두산을 가보고 십슴니다 그려. 그래서 표지를 배두산 설경으로 하엿슴니다.

6월 초순호는 부득이한 사정으로 내지 못하여 미안하외다. 중순호를 이제 미리 발행하얏사오며 7월 초순호도 미리 당기여 6월 하순에는 발매하겟슴니다. 「三千里」를 더욱 사랑하시고 또한 직혀주시압소서.

詩歌同人會의 「詩歌集」은 定員이 조곰 부족하야 연기하엿든 바 아마 7월중에는 발행하겟
슴니다. <42>
