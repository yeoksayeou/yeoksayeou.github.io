교차점
(Intersection/Crossroads)

Anyone who has a question they wish to ask someone, please submit it to this column. Our company's representative (旗手) will forward the letter to the recipient and publish their reply in this column.
1. Use a postcard for the paper.
2. Keep the meaning simple.
3. The content should relate to the public society.

To Mr. Chŏng Se-kwŏn (鄭世權):

In the reply in this column of the November issue of "Samch'ŏlli" (三千里), you mentioned that because the power of a certain force promoting foreign goods is stronger than our power promoting Korean products, etc... How about spending the energy used for the Korean Products Promotion Association (물산장려회) to repel this strong force?
Also, isn't it the Korean capitalists who benefit more and more as Korean products are favored?
If you avoid this reply and the "Samch'ŏlli Company" (三千里社) suppresses it, your company's representative (貴社 旗手) is acting improperly.
Cho Man-sik (趙晩植) from P'yŏngyang (平壤)

Reply to Cho Man-sik (趙晩植):

Rather than spending energy to repel the influence of foreign goods, I believe fostering the spirit of self-production and self-sufficiency (自作自給) is the better strategy (得策).
As for who receives the benefit when Korean products are favored, whether capitalists or proletarians, I leave that to individual interpretation; I believe the entire Korean economy will revive if only Korean products are favored.
Chŏng Se-kwŏn (鄭世權)

News of Various People

We enjoy reading Samch'ŏlli (三千里), which you all work hard on. Excuse us, but what happened regarding the incident with Chŏng Chong-myŏng (鄭鍾鳴) and Hŏ Chŏng-suk (許貞琡) of the Kŭnuhoe (槿友會)? Also, are Ms. U Pong-un (禹鳳雲) and Ms. Sim Ŭn-suk (沈恩淑) of Kŭnuhoe still at the Hall and continuing their efforts as before? We have no way of knowing, so it is frustrating.
Pak Chŏng-hwa (朴貞和), Pak Nam-i (朴男伊) from Sŏnsan (善山)

Chŏng Chong-myŏng (鄭鍾鳴) is currently detained awaiting trial (未決監) at Sŏdaemun Prison (西大門 형무소) due to a certain incident, and Ms. Hŏ Chŏng-suk (許貞淑) has completed her sentence and been released from prison (만기 출옥). Also, Ms. U Pong-un (禹鳳雲) and Ms. Sim Ŭn-suk (沈恩淑) are executives (간부) of the Kŭnuhoe (槿友會), and it is said that the Kŭnuhoe has not yet been able to hold a general meeting and its affairs (會務) are rather stagnant (침체).
Mok Pyŏng-jŏng (木兵丁) Respectfully <7>

**Summary**

This article features a Q&A section from the magazine "Samch'ŏlli." The first exchange involves Cho Man-sik questioning Chŏng Se-kwŏn about the effectiveness of the Korean Products Promotion Movement against the strong influence of foreign goods, asking if efforts should focus on directly combating foreign influence and pointing out that capitalists might be the primary beneficiaries. Chŏng Se-kwŏn replies that fostering self-sufficiency is better and that the whole economy benefits regardless of who profits most. The second exchange is an inquiry about the status of several female activists associated with the Kŭnuhoe (a women's organization), revealing that one (Chŏng Chong-myŏng) is imprisoned awaiting trial, another (Hŏ Chŏng-suk) was recently released, and the organization itself is inactive. For historians, the article provides insight into contemporary debates on economic nationalism (Buy Korean movement) under colonialism, including skepticism about its beneficiaries. It also explicitly names activists associated with the Kŭnuhoe and confirms their imprisonment or recent release, reflecting the political suppression faced by nationalist and women's groups during this period.

교차점
누구시든지 어느 분에게 뭇고 십흔 말슴이 잇거든 본란에 투고하여 주십시요. 본사 旗手가 수신할 분에게 그 편지를 分傳하여 그 회답을 바더다가 본란에 발표하겟슴니다.
1. 용지는 엽서에
2. 의미는 간단하게
3. 내용은 사회 公衆에 관계되는 것

鄭世權씨께

「三千里」 11월호 본란 답에 某 세력에서 외래품을 장려하는 힘이 우리가 조선 물산장려하는 힘보다 강하기 때문에 운운하엿스니 이 강한 세력을 격퇴식히기에 물산장려회에 쓰는 정력을 소비함이 如何.
또 조선물산을 애용하게 되면 될사록 이익을 보는 것을 조선인 자본가가 안이겟슴니까?
이 회답을 회피하야 『三千里社』에서 沒書로 한다면 貴社 旗手는 조치 못함니다.
平壤 趙晩植

答鄭晩植

외래품의 세력을 격퇴하기에 정력을 소비함보다 自作自給의 정신을 진작함이 得策일가 함니다.
조선 물산을 애용하야 其 이익을 밧는 사람이야 자본가이거니 무산자이거니 각자의 해석에 맛기고 조선물산을 애용만 하엿스면 조선인 전체의 경제가 회생될 것을 밋슴니다.
鄭世權

諸人消息

여러분 노력하시는 三千里는 잘 봄니다. 미안함니다만는 槿友會
鄭鍾鳴과 許貞琡 사건은 엇지 되엇슴닛가. 그리고 槿友會 禹鳳雲씨와 沈恩淑씨 여러분들은 아직도 會館에 잇서서 여전히 노력함닛가. 도모지 알 수가 업스니 답답함니다.
善山 朴貞和, 朴男伊



鄭鍾鳴은 某사건으로 西大門 형무소 未決監에 구금중이며 許貞淑씨는 만기 출옥되엇슴니다. 그리고 禹鳳雲씨, 沈恩淑씨는 槿友會의 간부로 잇스며 槿友會는 아즉 대회 개최도 못하며 會務가 다고 침체된 중에 잇다 함니다.
木兵丁 拜<7>
