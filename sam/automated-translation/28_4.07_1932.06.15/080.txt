**Literary Scene Fragments**

Tokkyŏn's Wedding

Novelist Tokkyŏn, Mr. Ch'oe Sangdŏk, held his wedding ceremony on June 2nd at the main branch of Myŏngwŏlgwan in Seoul. The bride was a beautiful woman named Hong Hye-yŏn. On that day, many literary figures gathered, making it a great success. However, whether Tokkyŏn's novels will increase or decrease due to the marriage, no one knows except the bride.

Mr. [Pak] Yongch'ŏl's Reception

A wedding reception for Mr. Pak Yongch'ŏl, a poet from the overseas literary scene, was held the other day, also at the Myŏngwŏlgwan main branch. Although the ceremony was omitted, this wedding reception also saw many literary figures gather, reportedly making it a very successful event. That many literary figures are getting married recently seems to be because, moved by the poetic sentiments of the early summer season with its blooming flowers and fragrant grass (花爛芳草), they also feel the excitement of life. It is a joyous thing.

Nosan's Publication Celebration

A party celebrating the publication of the newly published "Nosan Sijo Collection" (鷺山時調集) by Mr. Yi Ŭnsang, the great master of the *sijo* world, was held from the evening of May 27th at Ch'ŏngnyangsa temple outside the city. Ansŏ, Yi Pyŏnggi, Yun Paengnam, Tokkyŏn, I Ha-yun, Sŏ Hangsŏk, Mun Ilp'yŏng, Cho Un, and many other figures from the literary world attended. Among female writers, Ms. Ch'oe Ŭi-sun was the only one who came.
Listening to the pine wind (松風), this pleasant gathering continued until late into the night.

Mr. Yun Sŏkchung's Children's Song Collection

Mr. Yun Sŏkchung, who has shown many clear, clean, and excellent children's song compositions through the pages of the Tong-a Ilbo newspaper, is now having a collection of children's songs published via Sin'gusŏrim bookstore in the city and it is currently being printed. This is quite a joyous event for the lonely field of children's songs.<13>

**Summary**

This article reports on recent social and professional activities within the Seoul literary scene in mid-1932. It covers the weddings of novelist Ch'oe Sangdŏk (pen name Tokkyŏn) and poet Pak Yongch'ŏl, both held at the prominent Myŏngwŏlgwan restaurant. It also details a publication party for *sijo* master Yi Ŭnsang (pen name Nosan) at Ch'ŏngnyangsa temple, listing numerous attendees, and announces the upcoming publication of a children's song collection by Yun Sŏkchung. Historically, the article offers a glimpse into the social networking of Korean writers during the colonial period, highlighting popular gathering spots like Myŏngwŏlgwan and Ch'ŏngnyangsa, the active participation in various literary genres (*sijo*, novels, poetry, children's songs), and the presence (though limited) of female writers like Ch'oe Ŭi-sun in these circles.

文壇片片
獨鵑의 結婚

소설가 獨鵑 崔象德씨는 6월 2일 서울 明月館 본점에서 결혼식을 거행하엿는데 부인은 洪惠艶이란 佳人이엇다. 당일은 문인 諸氏가 만히 모여 대단히 성황을 이루엇다. 但 獨鵑의 소설이 결혼때문에 더 만허질는지 적어질는지 이것은 신부 이외에 아모도 모른다.

龍喆氏의 披露宴

海外 문학계의 시인 朴龍喆氏의 결혼피로연이 前日 亦 明月館本店에서 개최하엿는데 식을 略한 이 결혼 피로연에도 文士諸氏가 모어 非常히 盛會엇섯다고 한다. 근래 문인사이에 결혼하는 이가 만흔 것은 花爛芳草의 初夏節季에 시흥을 感하다가 人生의 흥까지 感하는 까닭인 듯 깁분 일이다.

鷺山의 出版記念

時調界의 巨匠 李殷相씨가 새로 출판한 「鷺山時調集」의 출판기념회는 5월 27일 저녁부터 市外淸凉寺에서 개최되엿는데 岸曙, 李秉岐, 尹白南, 獨鵑, 異河潤, 徐恒錫, 文一平, 曺雲 文壇諸氏가 다수 來會하엿다. 여류작가로는 崔義順씨 한 분이 오섯다.
松風을 드러가며 愉快한 이 회합은 夜深토록 繼續되어섯다.

尹石重氏의 童謠集

東亞日報紙面을 통하야 맑고도 깨끗한 우수한 동요작품을 만히 보혀주든 尹石重씨는 이번에 동요집을 市內新舊書林의 손을 거치어 출판하기로 되어 인쇄중이라는데 寂寞한 동요 방면을 위하야 자못 깃분 일이다.<13>
