Article Type	사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editor's Postscript)

Spring rain falls on the mountains where horses were born, and every valley turns lush and green (푸르靑靑하니). Ah, the fine season of the three spring months has suddenly arrived. Seeing the green grass spread over the blue mountains like that reminds one even more of the feeling in the old saying,
"Spring grass is green year after year (春草年年綠)."
Now, spring is said to be the season of growth (生長의 節季), and this magazine (本誌), too, is preparing to take this season as an opportunity to achieve great progress.

In Tokyo (東京), the wartime Diet (戰時議會) is currently underway, and in Chosŏn (朝鮮), the entire populace is united in tension (緊張一致) for the Week for the Promotion of the Japanese Spirit (日本精神 發揚週間). At this time, we know we must dedicate our full energy (全心力) to overcoming this current situation (시국) by exerting all our strength in industry and culture.

The single stone cast by Wang Ching-wei (汪兆銘), who escaped Chungking (重慶), with his peace proposal (화평제의), and the movements (動向) of Wu P'ei-fu (吳佩孚), who is rising in Peking (北京) bearing the hopes of the people (輿望), give a significant hint of the coming changes (轉變) in Asia (亞細亞). There is a premonition that the eyes and ears (耳目) of the world are now gathering on the tableau of the Far East (極東一幅).

Finally, a word to our esteemed readers (애독자 諸賢): This magazine (本誌) has always been released at the beginning of each month, but this has caused various inconveniences. There have been many requests (요망) to release it around the middle of the preceding month, like Tokyo (東京) magazines. Respecting these opinions, we will henceforth set the publication date as the 10th of the preceding month. We hope you will place your orders well in advance. Accordingly, this February issue has been combined (合倂) with the March issue.<300>

**Summary**

This editor's postscript from the April 1939 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine announces a change in the publication schedule, moving the release date from the beginning of the month to the 10th of the preceding month, citing reader requests and the model of Tokyo magazines. It also frames this change within the context of springtime growth and reflects on the ongoing Sino-Japanese War, mentioning the wartime Diet in Tokyo, the "Japanese Spirit Promotion Week" in Korea demanding unity, and recent political developments in China involving Wang Ching-wei and Wu P'ei-fu, suggesting major shifts are coming in East Asia. The most revealing aspects for historians are the explicit mention of the "Japanese Spirit Promotion Week" and the call for Koreans to dedicate "all strength in industry and culture" to overcome the "current situation," clearly indicating the magazine's alignment (or forced alignment) with Japanese wartime mobilization propaganda aimed at the colonial populace. The change in publication date to mimic Tokyo practices also subtly underscores the increasing integration or assimilation pressure within the colonial publishing sphere.

編輯後記
말 낫든 산에 봄비 내려 山谷마다 푸르靑靑하니 어허 어느덧 三春佳節이 다 닥처 왔오이다. 靑山에 저렀틋 푸른 풀이 깔닌 것을 보면 녯사람이 이르든
「春草年年綠」
의 늣김을 한층 더 알녀지나다이, 이제 봄은 生長의 節季라 하오니 本誌 또한 이 철을 한 기회로 큰 진보를 이루고저 차림하고 있나이다.

東京서는 戰時議會가 바야흐로 전개되여 가고 朝鮮서는 日本精神 發揚週間으로 全國民이 모다 緊張一致하고 있나이다. 이 때에 우리들은 산업으로 문화로 온갓 힘을 다하야 이 시국을 익여 나가기에 全心力을 다하여 가갈 바인 줄 아옵니다.

重慶 탈출한 汪兆銘이 投한 화평제의의 一石과 北京서 輿望을 띠고 蹶起하는 吳佩孚의 動向은 장차 올 亞細亞의 轉變에 중대한 암시를 주는 것으로 세계의 耳目이 極東一幅에 바야흐로 모여진 질 예감이 잇나이다.

끗으로 애독자 諸賢께 고할 말슴은 本誌는 항상 그 달 초에 발매하여 왔는데 그리하여서는 여러 가지로 불편하옵이 東京雜誌들 모양으로 그 前 달 中旬경에 내여 달나는 요망이 만키에 그러한 여러분의 의사를 존중하여 이제부터는 그 前 달 10일로써 발매 期日을 定켓슴니다. 미리 미리 주문하여 주시기를 바라며 따라서 이번 2월호는 3월호와 合倂하였나이다.<300>
