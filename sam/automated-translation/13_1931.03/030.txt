**Body Text Translation:**
Paper Screening of Tearful Chaplin's "City Lights"

This film is said to be decidedly superior even compared to past works produced by Chaplin, the actor of "Tearful Comedy" (눈물의 喜劇), such as "The Gold Rush" (黃金狂時代), "A Dog's Life" (개의 生活), "Shoulder Arms" (밧드러 銃), and "The Pilgrim" (僞牧師). As a scene from "City Lights" (거리의 불), it is truly a work obtained through arduous effort, costing several million won in filming expenses and consuming about three years of time. Its content is as follows:

Chaplin, a poor wanderer without a home, hometown, lover, honor, or gold, met a pitiful blind flower-selling girl in a park. He developed feelings of affection for the girl. Then one day, seeing her drop a bunch of flowers without realizing it, he picked it up for her, and thus they came to know each other. After that, his love for the girl grew fiery hot like a flame. So, eventually, after running around in every direction, he had her admitted to a hospital for dedicated treatment.<4> After that, the girl's eyes were opened, and she opened a flower shop (끗가개). Meanwhile, Chaplin's arrangements for the girl became his misfortune (禍), and he ended up going to prison. Later, after serving his sentence, Chaplin was released from prison and met the girl again, but the girl, not knowing that this man was the great benefactor who had opened her blind eyes, treated him very coldly. Then, when everything became known, they grasped each other's hands, shed tears, and rejoiced. However, this too was brief. A certain wealthy and handsome young gentleman appeared before the flower-selling girl, intercepted her love, and took it away.

At this time, Chaplin, having no choice, leaves the flower-selling girl he loved so much behind and once again becomes a forever, forever solitary wanderer, roaming the endless earth.<5>
<4-5>

**Summary**

The article provides a detailed plot summary of Charlie Chaplin's film "City Lights," describing it as a masterpiece superior to his previous works. It recounts the Tramp's encounter with a blind flower girl, his efforts to restore her sight which land him in prison, their reunion where she initially doesn't recognize him, and the bittersweet ending where, despite her recognition, she is ultimately courted by a wealthy suitor, leaving the Tramp alone again. For historians, this article reveals the early reception and popularity of major Western cultural figures like Chaplin and specific Hollywood films in colonial Korea during the 1930s. The detailed synopsis suggests a strong interest among the Korean readership in international cinema and its narratives, even when presented secondhand through a "paper screening."

눈물의 챠푸링「거리의 등불」紙上上映
이 映畵는 從來 「눈물의 喜劇」의 俳優인 「촤푸린」이 過去에 製作한 「黃金狂時代」 「개의 生活」 「밧드러 銃」 「僞牧師」등에 비하야도 斷然 優秀하다한다. 「거리의 불」의 한 場面으로 撮影費 수백만원과 약 3년의 日字를 虛費하야 그야말로 刻苦慘憺으로 어든 作品이다. 그 內容은
집도, 故鄕도, 愛人도 名譽도, 黃金도 업는 가난한 放浪者「촤푸링」이 公園에서 눈이 먼 불상한 「꼿팔기색시」를 맛낫다. 그는 그 색시에 思慕하는 마음이 생겻다. 그럴때 하로는 그 색시가 꼿떨기를 떨구고도 알지 못하고 잇는 것을 보고 그를 주어 주어서 이 눈먼 색시와 서로 알게 되엇다. 그런 뒤는 색시를 사랑하는 情이 불가치 거워지엇다. 그래서 結局 百方奔走하여 그를 病院에 入院식혀 專心治療하엿다.＜4＞ 그런 뒤 그 색시는 눈이 열어지어 「끗가개」를 開業한다. 한편 촤푸린이 그 색시에 대한 周旋이 禍되여 刑務所에 드러가는 몸이 되엇다. 뒷날 懲役을 다 치르고 촤푸린이 出獄하여 그 색시와 맛나지만 색시는 그 사나히가 자긔의 먼 눈을 뜨게 하여준 大恩人인지도 모르고 크게 冷待한다. 그러다가 모든 일이 알니워 지어서 둘은 서로 손에 손을 맛잡고 눈물 흘니며 깁버한다. 그러나 이것도 잠간이엇다. 꼿파는 색시 압헤는 엇던 돈 만코 外樣잘난 靑年紳士가 가로 나서서 그의 사랑을 가로차가고 만다.
이때 촤푸린은 할수업시 그러케도 사랑하든 꼿파든 그 색시를 뒤에 두고 또 永遠히 永遠히 孤獨한 放浪者가 되여 끗업는 地上을 彷徨한다.＜5＞
