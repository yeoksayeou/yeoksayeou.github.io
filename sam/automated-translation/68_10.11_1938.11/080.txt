Magazine Title: 三千里
Publication Date: 1938-11-01
Article Title: 佳人 往來 (Beauties Coming and Going / News of Beauties)
Author: None specified
Article Type: 소식 (News/Tidings)

**Kim Soyŏng Shedding Red Tears (紅淚)**

Since her affiliated group was dissolved, her contract was also terminated. Now, all alone, she reportedly sheds tears of sorrow (紅淚) deep into the long autumn nights (秋夜 長), wishing only for the speedy and safe release of her husband, who is elsewhere.

**Chi Kyŏngsun, Absorbed in the Piano**

Formerly an exclusive actress with the Ch'ŏngch'unjwa (靑春座) troupe, whose name became widely known, she seems to have had a change of heart. Now, despite the sweltering heat of this summer, she is diligently going daily to the Orion music studio on the second floor of the coffee shop in Myŏngch'ijŏng (明治町) and is absorbed in receiving private piano lessons.

**Mun Yebong Living in a Hotel**

Due to the noisy issues at Sŏngbong Film Studio (聖峰 영화원), she entrusted her children to her parents-in-law in the countryside and came up [to the city] alone. Currently staying at the Tongmyŏng Hotel (東明 호텔), her face looks quite worn out from thinking about her children day and night. She says she will bring the children once she sets up a house soon, so the day when her pale face regains its vitality should not be far off.

**Pak Hyesuk in Her Wedding Chamber (花燭洞房)**

Having completely stepped away from theater and film, Ms. Pak, who found enjoyment only in radio dramas and novel readings on the radio, is said to have recently married a doctor, Mr. Kim XX, according to recent rumors.

**Han Ŭnjin, Who Filmed “Mujŏng” (無情)**

Although the filming of “Mujŏng” (無情) has finished, Director Pak Kich'ae reportedly praises her greatly, saying she comes to work without missing a single day and is a very diligent actress. Before long, we will see her fresh and graceful figure on the screen (소크린).<64>

**Summary**

This article provides brief updates on the lives and careers of several prominent female entertainers in colonial Korea in 1938. It covers Kim Soyŏng dealing with personal hardship after her troupe disbanded, Chi Kyŏngsun shifting focus from acting to piano, Mun Yebong managing family separation due to work issues, Pak Hyesuk leaving entertainment for marriage, and Han Ŭnjin being praised for her diligence after completing a film. For historians, the article offers glimpses into the precarious careers of female performers, the intersection of personal lives (marriage, family, potential political trouble hinted at with Kim Soyŏng's husband) and professional activities, and the functioning of the entertainment industry (troupes, film studios, radio, personal lessons) under colonial rule. The blend of personal anecdotes and career updates reflects the public's interest in these figures.

佳人 往來
紅淚를 짜는 金素英

소속 단체가 해체되였음으로 전속도 해약하고 지금은 오직 혼자서 딴 곳에 게신 부군이 하로 속히 무사히 나오기를 바라면서 秋夜 長 깊은 밤에 紅淚를 짠다고.

피아노에 熱中하는 池京順

靑春座 전속 배우로 그 일홈을 널니 날니드니 무슨 뜻한 바가 있었든지 지금은 날마다 - 이 쩔쩔끌는 염천임에도 불구하고 - 明治町 다방 오리온 2층 음악사에 다니며 피아노 개인 교수 받기에 열중이시다.

호텔 生活하는 文藝峯

聖峰 영화원의 싯끄런 문제로 해서 애기들은 시골 시父님한태 맷기고 혼자 뛰여 올나와서 현재 東明 호텔에 투숙 중이나 밤났으로 애기들 생각에 얼골이 몹시 상하섰다. 이제 곧 집을 작만하면 애기들을 대려오리라 하니 핼슥한 얼골에 화기돌날도 오라지 않으리라.

花燭洞房의 卜惠淑

연극과 영화엔 도모지 손을 대지 않고 라디오 뜨라마 라디오의 소설 낭독에만 자미를 부친 氏는 최근 들니는 소문에 의하면 의사 金XX 씨와 결혼하섰다고 한다.

「無情」을 짜내인 韓銀珍

「無情」촬영이 끝났으나 어느 날 빼는 일 없이 출근한다고 朴基采 감독이 아주 부즈런한 여배우라고 대 칭찬이시란다. 머지안어 그 청초한 앙자를 우리는 소크린에서 볼 것이다.<64>
