Film Observation Deck

Among *Mujŏng* (無情), *Aeryŏnsong* (愛戀頌), *Pokchi malli* (福地萬里), *Sae Ch’ulbal* (새 出發), and *Ŏhwa* (漁火), could there possibly be a work worth anticipating? Apart from *Yŏro* (旅路) and *Han’gang* (漢江), the various other works are a succession of inferior products. The heavens are dark and gloomy.

*Tosaengnok* (圖生錄), *Sunjŏng haehyŏp* (純情海峽), *Kunyong yŏlch’a* (軍用列車) – none of them are bearable to look squarely at. To have jumped into the sacred circle of film culture with merely this level of conception, merely this level of effort, merely this level of talent – their audacity is truly just sad.

With that much money, wouldn't it be better to build even one more school or library? Those few individuals who should retire and take up poultry farming but instead advance and undertake filmmaking are truly pitiful existences.

Just because they've had the good fortune to secure distribution through places like Tōwa Shōji (東和商事), Nikkatsu (日活), or Tōhō (東寶), which have nationwide distribution networks, releasing such inferior works risks cutting short the partnership with Chosŏn cinema. This will tarnish the whole due to two or three incompetent films and block the path for those who follow. I urge serious self-reflection upon those few writers, directors, and actors with the minds of middle school students.

One thing to consider on this occasion is that, at the very least, only films that have first premiered and done well in Chosŏn should be sent to the markets in mainland Japan (內地), such as Tōkyō (東京) or Ōsaka (大阪). This is necessary to maintain our national face (민족적 체면). Entrusting filmmaking solely to two or three producers is dangerous. While production may be free, the results should not also be free [from standards].

At the very least, if it's not of the caliber of *Yŏro* or *Han’gang*, don't even think of making it in the first place. If, unfortunately, it has been made, the second-best option is to quickly screen it only in Chosŏn, for fear of it catching others' eyes, and then put it away. This is the voice of heaven (天聲).

Are there still producers who blame the inferiority of their work on a lack of facilities? Many of France's (불란서) famous films are made under conditions more wretched than ours. Even in Paris (파리), over 20 types of newspapers are printed at a single printing house. With capital as small as a deer's tail, stop thinking of each having independent studios (스타리오), sound recording rooms, and cameras – become one. Rationalize the production apparatus. The path lies here. The light shines here. Think thrice.<91>

**Summary**

This article is a harsh critique of the Korean film industry in 1938, lamenting the generally poor quality of films being produced, with only *Yŏro* and *Han’gang* cited as exceptions. The author deplores the perceived lack of talent, effort, and planning among filmmakers and producers, suggesting some should leave the industry. There's a strong concern that distributing these subpar films to Japan ("Naeji") will damage the reputation of all Chosŏn cinema and hinder future opportunities. The author advocates for the consolidation of resources and rationalization of production facilities, pointing to the French film industry as an example of success despite limited means. For historians, this piece is revealing about the internal criticism and anxieties within the colonial Korean cultural sphere, particularly regarding artistic standards and national representation ('minjokjŏk ch’emyŏn') in relation to the Japanese metropole. It also sheds light on the material conditions and proposed solutions for the fledgling film industry under colonialism.

映畵 展望臺
「無情」 「愛戀頌」 「福地萬里」 「새 出發」 「漁火」 이 가운데서나 기대할 作이 있을가 「旅路」 「漢江」이외의 諸 作은 타작의 연속, 天日이 암담하다.

「圖生錄」 「純情海峽」 「軍用列車」 어느 것이나 참아 正視할 것이 못된다. 겨우 요만 구상, 겨우 요만 노력 겨우 요만 才分을 가지고 성스러운 영화 문화의 圈내에 뛰여든 그 대담, 오직 슲은 일이다.

그만 돈이면 차라리 학교, 도서관 한 개라도 더 만들미 올치 않을가. 退하야 양계나 할 분들이 進하야 영화에 착수하는 그 몇몇 분은 可謂 애닯흔 존재.

東和商事 日活, 東寶 같은 전국적 배급망 가진 이런 곳에 배급할 수 있는 好運을 맛낫다고 이런 타작이 나와서는 조선 영화와의 제휴가 단명될 우려 있다. 이것은 2, 3 劣能 영화 때문에 전체를 흐리게 하고, 또 뒷사람 진로를 막음이리라. 중학생 級 머리의 작자, 감독, 배우 몇몇 분에게 猛省을 促한다.

이 까리에 一考할 일은 적어도 동경 大阪 등 내지 시장에는 조선서 몬저 封切하여 잘 된 것만 보낼 것이다. 그래야 민족적 체면을 유지하겟다. 영화를 2, 3 제작자에게만 맷겨두는 것은 위험한 일이다. 제작은 자유래도 그 결과까지 자유여서는 안된다.

적어도 「旅路」「漢江」 급이 아니거든 맨들 생각 애초에 말 것이요, 불행히 맨드렀거든 행여 남의 눈에 뛰울세라 조선서만 얼는 해 버리고 치워 두는 것이 차선이다. 이것은 天聲인저.

타작의 죄를 아직도 설비 부족에 돌리는 제작자가 있는가. 불란서의 그 많은 명화도 우리보다 더 참담한 조건 속에 되는 것이 수두룩하다. 신문조차 파리서는 한 인쇄소서 20여 종을 인쇄해 낸다. 노루 꼬리만한 자본을 가지고 제각금 독립한 스타리오 녹음실 카메라 가질 생각말고 일체 되라. 제작 기구의 합리화를 하라. 길은 여기 있다. 빛은 여기 빛인다. 三考할 일.<91>
