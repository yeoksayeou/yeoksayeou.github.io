「The Fragrance of the Tale of Ch'unhyang」
						By Ch'oe Sŭnghŭi, In the United States

The Tale of Ch'unhyang
(Performed by the Tokyo Shinkyō Troupe)

Cast

Writer      Chang Hyŏkchu
Director      Murayama Tomoyoshi

I am very glad that the Shinkyō Troupe chose "The Tale of Ch'unhyang" for the script of their performance tour in Korea. Why is that? It is because the tragedy of these two people, Ch'unhyang and Mongnyong, reveals our unique traditional character and style within this work.

From afar, thinking of the performance scene on the stage at the Pumingwan Civic Hall in Seoul now, I dearly wish I could go and see it. Who is playing the role of Ch'unhyang? Ch'unhyang, who endured the hardships of prison for three years <93> and defended her chastity even unto death, is the figure I admire most as a model for East Asian women.

Finally, I hope that the members of the Shinkyō Troupe find an even richer artistic fragrance in Korea on this trip and bring it back with them. <93-94>

**Summary**

Writing from the United States, the renowned Korean dancer Ch'oe Sŭnghŭi expresses her pleasure that the Japanese Shinkyō Theatre Troupe has chosen to perform the classic Korean story "Ch'unhyangjŏn" during their tour of colonial Korea. She praises the story for embodying traditional Korean character and style, specifically highlighting the virtue of Ch'unhyang. Ch'oe expresses a longing to see the performance in Seoul and wishes the troupe success in experiencing Korea's artistic essence. For historians, this article is interesting as it shows a prominent Korean artist endorsing a Japanese cultural group's engagement with Korean heritage during a period of intense assimilation pressure. Ch'oe's focus on the "traditional character" and "artistic fragrance" of Korea, expressed through a positive view of this specific cross-cultural event, offers a nuanced perspective on cultural identity and interaction under colonial rule.

작자      張赫宙
연출      村山知義
新協 극단이 渡鮮 공연의 각본에 「春香傳」을 택한 것은 나는 매우 깁부게 생각함니다. 었째서 그런가 하면 春香과 夢龍의 이 두 사람의 비극은 우리들 독특의 전통적 성격과 스타일이 이 작품 속에 낱아나서 있는 까닭임니다.
멀니서 이제 서울 府民舘 우의 공연 무대 광경을 생각하고 한끗 가보고 싶은 생각 간절함니다. 春香역은 어느 분이 하심니까. 3년 동안이나 그 옥중 고초를 겪으면서도<93> 자기의 정조를 죽엄으로서 직힌 春香 동양 여성들의 표본으로서 제가 가장 조와하는 분이올시다.
끝으로 新協 극단 여러분들이 이번 길에 조선에서 더 좋은 예술적 향기를 찿어 갖이고 도라가기를 빔니다.<93-94>
