Publication Date: 1938년 11월 01일
Article Title: 編輯後書 (Editorial Postscript)
Author: Editor/Editorial Staff
Article Type: 사고·편집후기 (Notice/Editorial Postscript)

The sound of falling leaves in late autumn is heard outside the window, signaling that this year, too, is nearly drawing to a close. As it was a year to remember for its many eventful and difficult affairs in this land, myriad emotions crowd the mind.

It cannot be only us whose myriad emotions crowd the mind. Would it not be the case for all people of East Asia (東洋)? Looking at the changing map of Asia (亞細亞), each person will be experiencing joy and sorrow in the dreams and illusions they individually paint.

How could joy and sorrow be limited only to Asia? When looking at the war clouds over Europe (歐羅巴) surrounding Czechoslovakia (첵코), the hearts of the various nations and peoples bordering the Mediterranean Sea (地中海) must also be anxious. In this way, the entire world is surging, embracing the confusion and excitement that preceded the Paris Peace Conference (巴里媾和會議) 20 years ago.

If one cannot know an individual's tomorrow, how much less can anyone predict the rise and fall of a nation or a people? We can only open our eyes wide and squarely face the unfolding international situation.

There is no way to fully express our deep gratitude for the enthusiastic reception and readership of the past October issue. Different from other times, publishing even one volume of a magazine is no easy task. The price of paper (조히갑) is dear, printing fees (印刷費) have soared, and furthermore, the number of writers has decreased. If society does not recognize the suffering involved in the existence of this press organization (言論機關), breaking through all the economic and social difficulties (難關) amidst these circumstances, would it not be too cold-hearted? We sincerely ask for even greater readership always.<300>

**Summary**

The editorial postscript reflects on the end of 1938, an "eventful" year, acknowledging the widespread anxiety felt across East Asia and Europe due to geopolitical shifts like the changing map of Asia (likely referencing the ongoing Sino-Japanese War) and the crisis in Czechoslovakia. The editor emphasizes the uncertainty of the future for nations and stresses the importance of observing international affairs. The piece concludes with gratitude for reader support for the previous issue while highlighting the severe economic challenges facing the magazine—soaring paper and printing costs, and fewer contributing writers—implicitly appealing for continued understanding and readership amidst these hardships. For historians, this article offers a glimpse into the atmosphere of Korea in late 1938, showing awareness of international crises and their perceived impact on Asia. It also underscores the precarious existence of Korean-language press under Japanese colonial rule, facing both economic pressures and likely unstated political constraints, while expressing a sense of shared destiny or concern among East Asians during a period of major upheaval.

編輯後書
殘秋의 落葉聲이 창박게 들니니 이 해 또한 거지반 기우러 가느구려. 이 땅의 多事多端하든 記憶할 해이니만치 萬感이 交集된다.

萬感이 交集될 것은 우리뿐 아니랴. 全東洋人士가 모다 그럴 일이 아닐가 변하여 가는 亞細亞의 저 地圖를 바라서 사람사람들은 여러 가지로 저마다 그리는 꿈과 幻影에 喜悲하고 있을 것이다.

喜悲하고 있을 것이 엇지 亞細亞에만 끈치랴. 첵코를 싸고도는 歐羅巴의 戰雲을 바라볼 때 地中海를 끼고 도는 여러 國家, 民族의 胸中 또한 썰넬 것이라. 이리하야 全世界는 20년전 巴里媾和會議以前의 그 混雜과 亢興을 싸안고 용소슴치고 있다.

한 개인의 明日을 모르려든 하물며 一國家 一民族의 흥망을 누가 점치랴. 눈을 크게 뜨고 우리들은 이 國際情勢 도라가는 것을 오직 正視하여 나감이 잇슬 뿐인저.

지난 10월호를 그처럼 歡呼愛讀하여 주시니 감격한 말슴 다 할 길 없다. 다른 때와도 달나 1권의 雜誌를 낸다는 것이 여간 어려운 일이 아니다. 조히갑 빗싸고 印刷費 暴騰하였고 加之 글쓰는 이 줄어 들엇고 이런 속에서 모든 經濟的 社會的의 難關을 꾀뚫고 나오는 言論機關의 이 存在 그의 苦哀을 몰나 준다면 社會가 너무 冷酷치 않으랴. 늘 더 한층의 愛讀을 빌어 마지 않노라.<300>
