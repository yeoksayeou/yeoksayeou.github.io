Before and After Marriage
						Cho Ch'un'gwang

P'ain (巴人) of Samch'ŏllisa (三千里社), who asks me today, when it's nearing 10 years since I married, to speak of my impressions before and after marriage, is quite the "mischievous fellow." Also, although it has been 10 years since I married, lest the esteemed readers think me an old man and I risk losing my youthful spirit, I feel the need to let it be known that I am still a vibrant young man (?) whose "jaw (턱아리)" has yet to sprout a beard.

In any case, since speaking about this issue without mincing a single word is likely to touch upon the taboos of the Minister of Internal Affairs (my wife), I will speak only as follows, within the limits of not disturbing the public peace.

Before Marriage - Fanciful Idealism
After Marriage - Idealistic Realism <24>

**Summary**

The author, Cho Ch'un'gwang, humorously reflects on being asked about his feelings before and after marriage, nearly ten years into it. He playfully calls the requester "mischievous" and insists he's still a young man. Acknowledging the sensitivity of the topic, especially regarding his wife (whom he jokingly calls the "Minister of Internal Affairs"), he states he will speak cautiously "within the limits of not disturbing the public peace." He concludes by characterizing the pre-marriage state as "fanciful idealism" and the post-marriage state as "idealistic realism." From a historical perspective, the article provides a glimpse into personal reflections on marriage and gender roles in 1930s Korea. The author's humorous self-censorship and reference to his wife as an "authority" figure, along with the phrase "not disturbing the public peace," might subtly echo the broader political constraints of the colonial period, repurposed for domestic humor, which historians might find interesting as an example of how societal pressures manifest in personal writing.

結婚前과 結婚後
						趙春光
내가 결혼한지 벌서 10년이 갓가워 오는 오늘날에 날더러 결혼전과 결혼후의 감상을 말하라는 三千里社의 巴人도 『작난꾼』이다. 또 결혼한지 10년이 되얏다하나 讀者諸氏가 날노 하야금 늙은이로 아라서는 나의 젊은 의기를 상실할 염려가 엄지 아니하겟기로 나는 아즉 『턱아리』에 수염도 나지 아니한 새파란 청년(?)이다는 것만은 알게 해둘 필요가 잇다.
하여간에 이 문제를 한마듸도 사리지 안코 말랴면 內務當局(妻)의 忌휘에 저촉하기 쉬운 문제이니 치안을 방해하지안는 정도 안에 잇서서 다음과 갓치만 말하겟다.
