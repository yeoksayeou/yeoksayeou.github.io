Yi Sangjae Memorial Special Feature

In the February issue of this magazine, Samch'ŏlli <67>

**Summary**

This brief announcement in the January 1937 issue of Samch'ŏlli magazine previews a special feature dedicated to the late Yi Sangjae, scheduled for the upcoming February issue. It indicates that the feature will include memorial writings from various contributors, plans for commemorative projects, and information regarding memorial services. For historians, this notice points to the continued public interest and efforts to commemorate prominent Korean nationalist figures like Yi Sangjae nearly a decade after his death (d. 1927) during the colonial period, and shows the magazine acting as a platform for organizing or publicizing these efforts.
