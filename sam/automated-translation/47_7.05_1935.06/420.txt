						By Hong Unbong

Marseille's erotic films and prostitute district are extremely famous. Not knowing Marseille's history in detail, I am unclear from roughly which era these things came to exist. In any case, I came to see their origin from the time the city of Marseille began to swallow and spit out numerous ships as a trading port. Just before leaving the Old Port through Rue de la République from Joliette Dock, the narrow alleys on the left and right sides are entirely that district.

Stone buildings, looking like they have passed through several centuries, stand neatly lining a road barely wide enough for one person, dark even in broad daylight. If people were not passing through, it would look like ruins. That is the street one step into the secluded side of Rue de la République. It lies beneath the magnificent ancient fortress facing Château d'If. Enough with the preface, let's see the cinema first.

Just as there are no travelers visiting Marseille who haven't savored Bouillabaisse, it's no exaggeration to say that there are no crew members of ships docking at Marseille who haven't seen this cinema. (Bouillabaisse is a type of stew costing 3,000 Won [likely refers to Francs or a comparative value], a famous specialty dish of Southern France's Marseille).

The cinema shows rare films unseen elsewhere, <244> though of course, it's not openly operated as public entertainment. It is secretly projected on the second floor of a house of prostitution (淫賣家). When we wander around that uneven (凸凹한) street, an old woman looking out the door tells us to come inside. Once inside, she immediately guides us to the second floor. The woman who guides us locks the door and disappears.

There is only a hole about one foot square in the right wall. Once closed, it's absolutely impossible to get out. The room is a rectangle of about 10 py MSR:"p'yŏng" eong (approx. 33 sq meters).

Even if it's the first time experiencing such a strange situation, having heard about it often onboard the ship, one isn't particularly surprised. Beside us is a sofa, and at a right angle to it, another sofa is placed along the wall. A white screen hangs on the front wall, and the floor is covered with carpet (카-벳트).

It's a good room that gives rise to many thoughts.

Shortly after, the side door opens, and a young man in civilian clothes (세비로 服) with a black bow tie (蝶넥타이) comes out.

He has the style of a handsome waiter (boy).

"Will you watch the cinema? Or will you dance?"
He asks in English.

"Cinema," we reply, and the man bows politely and goes into the next room. Then, all the electric lights turn off, and the room becomes dark. Only by the glow of cigarettes can one tell they are not alone. Soon, a bluish-white beam of light starts flowing above our heads and hits the screen in front.

The scene is a place far from human habitation in some village. The background shows a spring flowing, birds singing, and butterflies flying among flowers.

Two young maidens are picking fruit on an olive tree more than an armful around. They are beautiful, pure maidens. A young man passes by, sees the maidens, exchanges a word or two, and as one maiden comes down to a lower branch, he takes the chance to lift her skirt (쥬-부, likely jupe/skirt) and teases her. The women jump down towards the man, roll around on the soft grassy field, and after kissing and embracing to their heart's content, the three man and women finally eat the fruit of the forbidden (禁斷) tree.

It lays bare all of humanity's [sexual acts?] and most hedonistic [pleasures?] on film (휘룸). Who could see such a picture and not feel an uncontrollable impulse (衝動)? Generally, one pays [Francs?] per person to watch two reels, but the other reel is largely the same (大小同異), just with a different order of events. When the projection ends and the lights turn on, five or six young maidens emerge from behind the screen. They are a group of pretty dolls dressed in light, thin (열분) dresses (도레스) showing glimpses of skin. Their beautiful hair—blonde, brown, jet black, etc.—sparkles under the brilliant chandelier (샨떼리).

With bright tones, they flirt in French and English, and with light steps (스테-ㅍ), they run to us and embrace us.

The last woman to come out lifts the piano cover and starts tapping the keys—pong pong. If we respond in French to the women who don't know English well, they chatter away completely unintelligibly, like lighting oil paper on fire. One cannot understand unless they adjust the speed of their speech. Not being skilled dancers, we lacked the courage to resist the women's honey-like <245> words and began to take steps. After 2 or 3 minutes, following the women who became our partners (파-드나-), we were pulled dancing behind the screen into the women's rooms.

The old women take turns bringing whiskey and gin, etc., by the jar (罎채로). "Won't you drink?"
"We can't drink. Take it away. Quickly!"
We have no choice but to shout (高喊). But by then, our necks are already entwined in the soft arms of young, blonde beauties. And the woman whispers, pressing her lips to our ear,
"Hello, [Sir?], I like you very much. You are the kindest [Asian?] person. It's true. Here, drink up, my knight. And just as I love you, you must love me too."

Oh, why are you so lovely? Listen to me. Drink up. Raise your glass. You're a kind gentleman, right? Absolutely. Yes—give me [Francs?]. And..."

Becoming intoxicated by such passionate words, forced to drink the liquor costing [Francs?] per glass, and becoming completely captive to sexual sensation, the women flash a satisfied smile of victory and guide us to the second or third floor.

말세-유(Marseilles)의 에로 映畵와 娼妓街는 너머나 有名하다. 말세-유의 歷史를 仔細히 모르는 나는 大抵 어느 時代부터 그걷이 존재하엿는지 불명하다. 좌우간 馬耳塞市가 一貿易港으로써 多數의 船舶을 呑吐하기 始作한 때의 起原을 보게 되엿다. 죠리엘드독크(Joviettedock)로부터 레뷰리쿠 街(Ruederepulique)를 통하야 舊港을 나가기 좀전 左右便 좁은 모통이가 전부 그거시다.
數世紀를 經過하게 보이는 石造建物이 僅僅 一間쯤 되는 道路를 俠하야 가즈런이 서 白晝라도 컴컴하다. 만일 사람이 통행치 안으면 廢墟과 갓치 보인다. 그거시 「레뷰리쿠」街의 一步幽側의 거리이다. 레아도, 듸푸(Lhateaudif)와 상대하야 싸여잇는 豪牀한 固城下이니 前言은 이만하고 키네마부터 보자.
키네마는 말세-유에 들니는 遊行者로(Borillabass)를 玩味하지 안은 者 업는 거와 가치 馬耳塞에 寄港하는 船舶의 乘助員은 그것을 보지 안은 자 업다고 하여도 過言이 안이다-(Boilabasse) 3,000圓의 일종인대 南佛殊이 말세유의 名物料理-
키네마는 他處에서 보다 못한 珍奇한 映畵인대＜244＞ 물론 그것은 興行物로서 공개되여 잇지는 안하다. 淫賣家二層에서 공연한 祕密로서 映寫되여 잇다. 우리들이 例의 凸凹한 길을 기웃기웃하면 문밧끌 내여다 보든 할미가 안으로 드러오라고 한다. 드러가면 곳 2層으로 안내한다. 안내한 게집은 門에 鎖채우고 자쵀를 감춘다.
