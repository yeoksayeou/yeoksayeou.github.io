江南風月 (Affairs South of the River; Anecdotes)

Yŏ Unhyŏng's (呂運亨氏) Crisis
While in Shanghai (上海), one cannot know how many times Mr. Yŏ Unhyŏng (呂運亨氏) narrowly avoided being arrested by Chiang Kai-shek (蔣介石). It is said he earned much of Chiang's enmity because he constantly opposed Chiang's policies (政策). Therefore, listening to Mr. Yŏ's evaluation (評) of Chiang, from the first word to the last, it is harsh criticism upon harsh criticism (惡評又惡評).

High Tobacco Prices and Mr. Kim Pyŏngno (金炳魯氏)
Mr. Kim Pyŏngno (金炳魯氏) of the legal circles, about whom rumors of becoming the president of the Dong-A Ilbo (東亞日報) are currently circulating in some quarters, is a famous smoking enthusiast (愛煙家). He blows 5 or 6 packs of Asahi (朝日) cigarettes into the air each day. However, due to the aftereffects (余澤) of the Majang (馬場) tax increase finance policy, Asahi packs have suddenly jumped by 3 *chŏn* each. This price hike (値上額), even figuring just five packs a day, amounts to a loss of 15 *chŏn* daily, totaling over 15 *wŏn* over 365 days a year—these days, he gazes at the purple smoke (紫煙) and gives a lonely, bitter smile (苦笑).

A Poem by Mr. Yang Kŭnhwan (梁權煥氏)
Mr. Yang Kŭnhwan (梁槿煥씨), who toured places like Harbin (하爾賓) and the banks of the Songhua River (松花江邊) enjoying the scenery and local customs before returning, composed a poem (一首詩) out of emotion (感激) upon returning to his native land (故土) as he crossed the Amnok River (鴨綠江).
*Man san kang yŏp kŭm su chik* (萬山江葉錦垂織 - Ten thousand mountains, river leaves, brocade hanging woven)
*Ch'ŏn kok ki kŭm myŏng nan pi* (千谷奇禽鳴亂飛 - A thousand valleys, strange birds crying, flying chaotically)
*Ha ch'ŏ kang san pi chu t'o* (何處江山非住土 - What place's rivers and mountains are not land to live?)
*Pu chi ko t'o to yu yŏ* (不知故土賭猶麗 - Unknowing, the native land seems even more beautiful when seen)

The Brotherly Love (友愛) of the Yŏ Brothers
For good brotherly love (友愛), it is rare to find a relationship like that between Yŏ Unhyŏng (呂運亨) and Yŏ Unhong (呂運弘). Every lunchtime, the elder brother purposefully comes from the newspaper company (新聞社) and the younger brother from the sewing machine company (미신 會社) to meet at Paekhabyŏn (百合園) on Chongno Intersection (鍾路 네거리) and have lunch together at the same table (食卓).
However, these days a rumor (풍설) is circulating in Seoul (서울). It is the story that Mr. Yŏ Unhyŏng is being brought in (迎入) as the General Secretary (總務) of the Central Youth Federation (中央靑年聯合會) to straighten out the Korean Christian society (朝鮮基督敎社會), which is said to be in a state of tangled hemp (亂麻).
Hearing this story, the younger brother, Mr. Unhong, said, "Crazy talk! My elder brother, a socialist (社會主義者) who has believed in historical materialism (唯物史觀) for decades, is going to handle Christian affairs? That's blatant slander (中傷)."<39>

**Summary**

This article presents four short anecdotes about prominent Korean figures in 1936. It mentions Yŏ Unhyŏng's past troubles with Chiang Kai-shek in Shanghai due to political opposition, lawyer Kim Pyŏngno's financial lament over rising cigarette prices affecting his heavy smoking habit, Yang Kŭnhwan's patriotic poem composed upon returning to Korea, and the close bond between brothers Yŏ Unhyŏng and Yŏ Unhong, concluding with the dismissal of a rumor that the socialist Yŏ Unhyŏng would lead a Christian youth organization. For historians, these snippets offer glimpses into the personal lives, political leanings (Yŏ's anti-Chiang stance, socialism), economic pressures (tobacco tax impact), and social dynamics (rumors, brotherly ties) among Korean intellectuals and activists during the Japanese colonial period. The mention of Yŏ Unhyŏng's troubles with Chiang Kai-shek is particularly notable.

江南風月
呂運亨氏 危難
上海있을 때 呂運亨氏는 蔣介石에게 몇 번 逮捕되려다가 避했는지 모른다. 蔣의 政策을 늘 反對한 때문에 그의 미움을 많이 삿더라고, 그러기에 呂氏의 입으로 나오는 蔣의 評을 드르면 첫마디부터 마즈막까지 惡評又惡評.

煙草高價와 金炳魯氏
지금 東亞日報社長說이 一部에 흘르고 있는 法曹界 金炳魯氏는 有名한 愛煙家라 하루에 朝日 5,6匣式은 공중에 날려버린다. 그런대 馬場增稅財政의 余澤으로 朝日匣이 매통 3錢식 폭등, 이 値上額이 하루 다섯갑만 잡아도 15錢式 1년 365일에 15余圓의 손실―요지간은 紫煙을 바라보면서 외로히 苦笑.

梁權煥氏의 一首詩
하爾賓과 松花江邊 등지를 두루도라 산천, 풍물 구경하고 도라온 梁槿煥씨, 鴨綠江을 건늘 때 故土에 오는 感激으로 一首詩를 지었다.
萬山江葉錦垂織
千谷奇禽鳴亂飛
何處江山非住土
不知故土賭猶麗

呂氏 兄弟 友愛
友愛 좋기로는 呂運亨 呂運弘 兄弟 같은 사이가 드물다. 점심마다 兄은 新聞社로 동생은 미신 會社로 일부러 鍾路 네거리 百合園에 모아 한 食卓에서 점심을 같이한다.
그런데 요지간 서울에 풍설 한 가지 돌아다니는대 그것은 朝鮮基督敎社會가 亂麻같어가니 그를 바로잡기 위하야 中央靑年聯合會總務로 呂運亨氏를 迎入한다는 說이 그것이다.
이 소리를 들은 동생 運弘氏 「미친 말들, 社會主義者로 唯物史觀을 信奉하여 數十年에나 하는 내 兄이 基督敎 일을 보아? 빨간 中傷이지.」＜39＞
