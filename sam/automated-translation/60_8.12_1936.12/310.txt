Consolation Money for the Violation of Chastity?
						Defense Attorney Yang Yun-sik

"Violation of chastity" (貞操蹂躪) is a fact that frequently occurs in any society, at any time. It can also be said to arise in various forms and various circumstances.

There seems to be no need to explain anew what "violation of chastity" is in this place, nor is this perhaps the place for it. However, when the fact of a "violation of chastity" occurs, exactly how much money should be paid as its price (代價) to be appropriate? What degree of monetary amount would indeed be the most suitable recompense for a "violation of chastity"?

Though it is a deeply awkward and difficult problem, I find myself in a regrettable situation where I cannot remain silent, and so I intend to jot down a few words.

In Chosŏn (朝鮮), how much should be paid as the price for a "violation of chastity"? Of course, even if a crime is established under the charge of "violation of chastity," the severity (重輕) will be decided by taking into account various points such as the time (時日), circumstances (境遇), place (場所), etc., and it will also differ based on the status, reputation (名望), etc., of both parties involved.

However, while we don't know about several decades ago or several years ago, in recent times, even setting it at the maximum, the price for "violation of chastity" is likely at most around 1,000 wŏn. Judging from precedents a few years ago, perhaps as society gradually develops, the concept of compensation for "violation of chastity" is weakening; it is a fact that the amount of the price decreases year by year. A few years ago (年前), it was not unheard of for a "violation of chastity" price of 2,000 wŏn to result from a trial, but recently, 1,000 wŏn is the maximum. Then, in the case where the lowest amount is paid, how much would it be? Just this spring, something occurred within the city of Sŏul (서울), and there was indeed a case where the small sum of 100 wŏn was paid as the price for "violation of chastity."

Meanwhile, a few years ago in Tōkyō (동경), there was a case where a sum of 10,000 yen was paid as the price for "violation of chastity," but generally in Tōkyō and Ōsaka (대판), amounts exceeding 5,000 yen and nearing 10,000 yen seem to occur often. <169>

**Summary**

The article, written by a defense attorney in 1936, discusses the monetary compensation ("consolation money") awarded in cases of "violation of chastity" in colonial Korea. The author notes that the amount has been decreasing over time, recently ranging from 100 to a maximum of 1,000 wŏn, suggesting a weakening social concept of compensation for such acts. He contrasts this with Japan (Tokyo and Osaka), where compensation amounts were significantly higher, often between 5,000 and 10,000 yen. For historians, this article offers insight into social attitudes towards sexual purity and its perceived monetary value, legal practices regarding compensation in civil cases related to sexual transgressions, and the socio-economic differences or perceived disparities between colonial Korea and Japan during that era. The declining value in Korea might reflect changing social norms, economic hardship, or the specific legal interpretations within the colonial context.

貞操蹂躪의 籍慰料?
						護辯士 楊潤植
「정조유린」이란 어느 사회 어느 때를 물론하고 자조 일어나고 있는 사실이다. 또한 여러 가지 형태로 여러 가지 경우에 생겨지고 있다할 것이다.
「정조유린」이 어떠한 것인가 함은 이 자리에서 새삼스레 이야기할 필요조차도 없을 것이며 또 그럴 자리도 아닌가 한다. 다만 「정조유린」이란 사실이 있을 때 도대체 그 대까(代價)로서 얼마마한 돈을 물면 적당할 것인가? 「정조유린」에 대한 가장 적당한 보답은 과연 얼마나한 정도의 금액이면 될까?
심히 쑥스럽고 어려운 문제이나 말치 않을 수 없는 딱한 경우에 부닷치게 되여 멫마디 적어 보려 하는 바이다.
조선에 있어서 「정조유린」의 대까(代價)로서 얼마나 물면 될까? 그야 「정조유린」죄명으로 죄가 성립된다 하드라도 시일(時日), 경우(境遇), 장소(場所)등 여러 가지 점으로 참작하여 중경(重輕)이 결정될 것이며 또한 양편 당사자들의 지위, 명망(名望) 등에서 달러질 것이다.
그러나 멫십년 전이나 멫해 전은 몰라도 최근에 와서는 최고로 잡는다 하드래도 「정조유린」값이란 깃껏해야 1,000원 정도의 것일 것이다. 멫해 전 의례에 미루워보면 아마도 사회가 점점 발달되여 나갈수록 「정조유린」에 대한 보상관념이란 엷어가는지, 해를 따라 그 대까(代價)액이 적어지는 것이 사실이다. 년전에는 2,000원의 「정조유린」값이 재판 결과 없었든 것이 아니였으나 근자에는 1,000원이면 고작이다. 그러면 가장 적게 물게 될 경우라면 얼마나 하면 될까? 바로 금년 봄 서울 시내에서 일어난 일인데, 「정조유린」 대까로서는 100원이라는 적은 금액을 지불한 사실도 있었으니까요.

그런데 년전 동경에서는 「정조유린」값으로 일금 10,000원을 물게된 사실도 있으나 대체로 동경 대판에서는 5,000원 이상 10,000원 가까운 금액이 종종 잇는 듯 하다. ＜169＞
