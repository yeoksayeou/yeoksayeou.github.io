Appreciation and Study of Classical Literature, A Study of the Tale of Changhwa and Hongnyŏn
						Kim T'aejun (金台俊)

1. Synopsis (梗槪)
In Ch'ŏlsan (鐵山) County, P'yŏngan-do (平安道) province, lived a local elder named Pae Chwasu (裵座首). His wife fell ill and died, leaving behind two beloved daughters, Changhwa (薔花) and Hongnyŏn (紅蓮). Pae Chwasu took a second wife, and this newly arrived stepmother also gave birth to two sons. As the days passed, the stepmother grew to hate the two daughters from the previous wife, looking for any opportunity to kill them. One day, the stepmother devised a wicked plan (奸計). Intending to kill Changhwa first, she caught a rat, skinned it, and made it look convincingly like a miscarried fetus (落胎). She secretly placed this under Changhwa's bedding. Then, accusing Changhwa of immoral conduct (*行), she told Pae Chwasu it was right to kill the girl who would ruin the household. Late one night, she took Changhwa out, threw her into a certain pond (池), and killed her. Hongnyŏn, who sensed what had happened, followed Changhwa and also threw herself into the pond, dying. Thus, the resentful (寃恨) deaths of these two maidens (處女) manifested as ghosts (幽靈) that haunted the dreams of the Ch'ŏlsan County Magistrate (郡守). Every magistrate who came subsequently fell ill and died. Consequently, the Royal Court (朝廷) selected a man of outstanding civil and military ability named Chŏn Tongil (全東屹) and dispatched (派遣) him as the Ch'ŏlsan County Magistrate. Magistrate Chŏn investigated the wrongful deaths (寃死) of the two daughters, had the stepmother arrested and executed, and appeased their vengeful spirits (怨魂).

2. Study (考究)
The distinctive features appearing in this work are that its stage (舞臺) is P'yŏngan-do and that it is a "stepmother novel." Originally, P'yŏngan-do was a place outside the cultural sphere of the Yi Dynasty (李朝文化圈), so it was uncommon (境遇가 없었다) as a setting for novels. However, as this novel's events were truly factual incidents from the time of King Sukchong (肅宗), it is a case where a true story (事實談) became a narrative book. My respected friend (畏友) Sin Ch'ŏlhoe (申澈會) went to Yŏju (驪州) and obtained a book titled *Chŏn Ka\*chip* (全嘉*集). According to it, Ka\* Chŏn Tongil (嘉* 全東屹) had an official career (官歷) during King Sukchong's reign, even becoming the Commander-in-Chief of the Eight Provinces (八道都統使). The fact that he went to Ch'ŏlsan as County Magistrate and appeased the two vengeful spirits of Changhwa and Hongnyŏn is also recorded. The end section of his collected works (<218> 文集大尾) describes the facts of Changhwa and Hongnyŏn's murders, clearly (昭然히) giving the feeling of reading the "Tale of Changhwa and Hongnyŏn." That there were many murder cases (殺獄) in P'yŏngan-do can be well understood by looking at the *Annals of the Yi Dynasty* (『李朝實錄』) and the *Hong\* Chŏnsŏ Simnirok* (『弘*全書審理錄』), among others. Particularly, the stepmother's cruelty (繼母的 殘*) is a common product of the East Asian family system (東洋家族制度), and we cannot vaguely blame only the person who became the stepmother without examining (吟味) that system. We must consider what drove the stepmother to commit such a crime (犯行), rather than assuming there is a seed of 'wickedness' (『奸惡』). The 'fact' that the ghost (幽靈), the 'incarnation of the soul' (『魂의 化身』), 'frequently appeared at the magistrate's official residence (官舍)' is likely a fictional construct (架空的 搆假). The magistrate probably investigated separately but attributed it to this [the ghost]. It is a terrifying scene, like the ghost in "Macbeth" (『막그베스』). However, the 'ghost' is an ideological product (관념적 산출) created by the human brain (두뇌), and it is clear (歷然) that its traces fade as we advance into the scientific century (科學的 世紀).
The writing is in pure Korean. It seems likely that this event was recorded (긔록) in Hangul as a storybook sometime after the reign of King Ch'ŏljong (哲宗). The exact pathway (經路) is not yet well understood.
I express my gratitude (謝한다) for the kindness (好意) of Mr. Sin Ch'ŏlhoe, who discovered this material. <219>

**Summary**

This article by Kim T'aejun provides a synopsis and analysis of the classic Korean story, "Changhwa Hongnyŏn chŏn." Kim recounts the plot involving the tragic deaths of two sisters caused by their cruel stepmother and their subsequent haunting, leading to justice delivered by a new magistrate, Chŏn Tongil. Kim notes the P'yŏngan-do setting as unusual for Yi Dynasty narratives and confirms the story's historical basis in an incident during King Sukchong's reign, citing evidence found by Sin Ch'ŏlhoe in the *Chŏn Ka\*chip*. He interprets the stepmother's cruelty not just as individual evil but as a product of the traditional family system, while dismissing the ghost element as fictional. For historians of colonial Korea, the article is interesting as it showcases Korean intellectuals engaging with their classical literature, attempting to link folklore with historical records (*Yi Cho Sillok*, etc.), and offering social commentary on traditional family structures, all within the context of developing modern scholarship during the Japanese occupation. The mention of regional cultural differences (P'yŏngan-do vs. the Yi cultural sphere) and scholarly collaboration (Kim and Sin) also provides insight into the period.

						金台俊
1. 梗槪
平安道 鐵山 땅에 사는 裵座首의 妻가 病으러써 귀여운 두 딸 薔花와 紅蓮을 남기고 죽었다. 裵座首는 後室을 聘하니 새로 들어오 繼母는 또한 아들 형제를 낳었다. 繼母는 날이 갈사록 前室의 所生인 두 딸을 미워하야, 기회만 있으면 죽이랴고 하였다. 繼母가 하로는 奸計를 생각하야 우선 薔花를 죽일 작정으로 쥐를 잡아 가죽 뱃을 겨서 怡似히 落胎한 모양으로 구미여 슬그먼히, 薔花의 니불 속에 넣고 그리고는 薔花의 *行을 責하다가 裵座首더러 이 집안 亡칠 계집을 죽의는 것이 올타 하야 어느 날 밤 말께 태여 가지고 가서 어느 못(池)에 던저 죽여 버렸다. 이 일을 눈치 챈 紅蓮은 薔花의 뒤를 딸라서, 또한 그 못(池)에 몸을 던지고 죽었다. 이리하야 두 處女 * 寃恨스러운 죽엄은 幽靈으로 나타나서 鐵山郡守의 꿈을 襲擊하였다. 郡守는 오는 대로 병이 나서 죽었다. 그리자 朝廷에서는 全東屹이라는 文武出衆한 人物을 擇하야 鐵山郡守로 派遣하였다. 全郡守는 이 두 딸 寃死한 것을 調査하고 그 繼母를 삼아 사형에 처하고 그 원魂을 풀어 주었다.

2. 考究
이 作品에 나타난 特色은 舞臺가 平安道라는 것과 繼母 小說이라는 데 있다. 원래 平安道는 李朝文化圈 外에 있든 곳이라 小說의 舞臺로는 境遇가 없었다- 하나 이 小說은 그 사실이 정말 肅宗 當時의 事實이였든 만큼 事實談이 니약이 책으로 化한 것이다. 畏友申澈會君이 驪州에 가서 全 嘉*集이라는 一 책을 얻어 왔는데 거긔에 依하면 嘉* 全東屹은 肅宗朝에 八道都統使까지 된 官歷이 있고 鐵山 郡守로 가서 薔花紅蓮의 두 寃魂을 풀은 사실도 있고 그 ＜218＞ 文集大尾에는 薔花紅蓮의 被殺事實이 적혀 있어 昭然히 「장화홍련뎐」을 읽는 늦김 준다. 平安道에 殺獄이 많은 것은 『李朝實錄』, 『弘*全書審理錄』 등을 보 보아도 잘 알 것이지만 特히 繼母的 殘*은 東洋家族制度의 共通的으로 있을 産物로서 우리는 그 制度에 대한 吟味가 없이 漠然히 繼母된 處女만을 責할 수도 없다. 『奸惡』한 사람이 種子가 있는 게 아니라 무었이 繼母로 하야금 그러한 犯行까지에 닐으데 하였는가를 생각지 않으면 않된다. 『魂의 化身』인 幽靈이 『자조 郡守의 官舍에 나타났다는 事實』만은 架空的 搆假일 것이다. 郡守는 따로히 조사했을 것이나 이에 假託한 것이다. 『막그베스」의 幽靈처럼 무시무시한 場面이다. 하나 『幽靈』은 인간의 두뇌가 맨들어 내인 관념적 산출이라 科學的 世紀에 나아갈소록 그 자취를 감추는 것은 歷然한 것이다.
文章은 純한 글로 썼다. 이 사실이 이야기 책으로 한글로 긔록된 것은 哲宗 以後의 일일 것 같다. 아즉 그 經路는 잘 알 수 없다.
