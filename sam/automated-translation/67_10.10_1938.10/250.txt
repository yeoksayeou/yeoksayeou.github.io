**Sino-Japanese War** (日淸 戰役)

- Meiji 27 to Meiji 37 (Each) July 24th - Telegram from Kyŏngsŏng published in the Tōkyō Nichi Nichi Shimbun (Dispatched immediately by special correspondent Kuroda in Kyŏngsŏng at 5 PM on the 21st) Minister Ōshima urged the Korean Royal Court (韓廷) the day before yesterday, the 19th, regarding the construction of barracks. Also, yesterday the 20th, to clearly demonstrate Korea's de facto independence, he proposed to the Korean Royal Court that the Qing soldiers (淸兵) stationed in Korea be made to withdraw outside its borders, pressing for a decision within 3 days, stating that depending on the response, a major decision would be made.

O Hard-liners Rally (Dispatched from Mito at 9:10 PM on the 22nd) Messrs. Kusumoto Masa*, Yamada Inotarō, Ōkuma Shūma, Ōi *tarō, Shiga Shigetaka, Shutō Rikusō, Magaki Nyochō(? 曲木如長), Fujimura Shirō, Nakajima Yūjin(?) attended the hard-liner (硬派) rally. There were over 400 attendees. Mr. Ōkuma and four others spoke assertively about a hard-line foreign policy, and also declared that candidates would be selected from the hard-line faction.

O Come forth, Liu Yongfu! Liu Yongfu (劉永福), the leader of the Black Flag Army (黑旗軍) who made his name during the Sino-French War (淸佛 交戰), upon the outbreak of the recent Sino-Japanese negotiation incident, frequently sent letters to Li Hongzhang (李鴻章) saying, "My brothers (the term 'brothers' refers to the subordinates allied with Liu since he was a general of the long-haired rebels), after 10 years from now, will not be fit for combat. I wish to fight the Japanese soldiers and repay the special grace (特恩) of the Celestial Court (天朝)," but Li has not yet permitted it. However, if peace between the two countries is broken, Li will surely grant Liu's wish. A certain Qing person (淸人) said that Liu's course of action determines the reality of Qing China's (淸國) stance on peace or war. They intend to bring out figures well-known among many of our countrymen (邦人 - Japanese), such as Liu Mingchuan (劉銘傳), Wu Dazheng (吳大徵 - assuming 大微 is 大徵), and Liu Yongfu, to use them for empty threats (虛喝). How capable are they? One or two are insufficient. Select all the brave generals and come forth all at once. We will show you the true strength (實力) of the Japanese soldiers.

**Russo-Japanese War** (日露 戰役)

O Recent Tidbits (近事 片片) The people attentively listened (正聽) to Admiral Tōgō's words, conveying his intention to strive regarding the future of the war situation and requesting this be communicated to the nation (國民).

O Audience of Minister Hayashi On the 22nd at 0:00 PM, dispatched by special correspondent Shimura in Kyŏngsŏng. Plenipotentiary Minister Hayashi and Secretary Hagiwara had a very long meeting (from 4 PM on the 21st for 2.5 hours). His Majesty [the Korean Emperor] earnestly showed his intention to trust Minister Hayashi and Our Country (我國 - Japan), and the Minister reported on the overall current situation, while at the same time repeatedly reporting (耬耬 奏上) so that there would be no misunderstanding by the Korean Royal Court (韓廷) regarding Our Empire's (我 帝國) policy towards Korea. As a result, it seems a cabinet reshuffle (內閣 改造) may be seen soon.

O Naval Battle of Yŏsun (旅順) (Dispatched at 9 AM on the 23rd by correspondent Ōmi(?) in Chihfu (芝罘)) According to news from a junk (장크) that entered this port (當港) this morning from Ch'ŏngunwa (靑雲窪), from midnight on the 21st until 3 AM this morning, there were over 40 Japanese warships (軍艦) and torpedo boats (雷艇) in the Yŏsun sea. Among them, large warships attacked the Hwanggŭmsan (黃金山) Battery, and torpedo boats (水雷艇) were fighting Russian ships (露艦), which could be discerned from afar by the illumination of searchlights (探海燈). Based on this, it is thought that our torpedo boat and destroyer flotilla (逐艦隊) must have carried out a night attack (夜襲) under the cover (援護) of the warships. <140>

**Summary**

This article from the October 1938 issue of *Samch'ŏlli* magazine compiles brief news reports (presented as telegrams) about the First Sino-Japanese War (1894) and the Russo-Japanese War (1904). The reports cover Japanese diplomatic pressure on Korea to expel Chinese troops, a rally of Japanese hardliners supporting expansionist policies, a dismissive challenge to Chinese military figures like Liu Yongfu, and accounts of Japanese diplomatic maneuvering in Korea and naval actions against Russia near Port Arthur (Yŏsun). From the perspective of Korean colonial history, the article is revealing in how it retrospectively frames these past conflicts from a 1930s Japanese imperial viewpoint. It highlights Japan's assertive actions as decisive and justified, portrays Korea as a passive entity subject to Japanese guidance (e.g., Minister Hayashi's audience), and expresses confidence in Japanese military superiority, particularly mocking Chinese figures. Historians might find the selection and tone of these "telegrams" useful for understanding how Japan's past interventions in Korea and conflicts with China and Russia were presented and historicized for a Korean audience during the height of Japanese colonialism, reinforcing narratives of Japanese leadership and power in East Asia.

戰爭의 電報
日淸 戰役
-明治 27년 동 37년(各) 7월 24일- 東京日日新聞所載 京城來電 (21일 오후 5시 在 京城 특파원 黑田 卽發) 大島 公使는 一昨 19일 병영 건축을 韓廷에 催促하다. 又 昨 20일 조선 독립을 사실상으로 明證키 위하야 在韓 淸兵을 境外에 퇴거식히도록 韓廷에 申込하고 3일 안으로 決答을 재촉하여 그 決答 여하에 의하여 크게 결단할 바 잇스리라고 한다.
O 硬派 대회 (22일 오후 9시 10분 水戶 發) 楠本正*, 山田猪太郞, 大隈秀磨, 大井*太郞, 志賀重昻首藤陸三, 曲木如長, 藤村紫郞, 中島祐人 諸氏 硬派 대회에 臨席하다. 출석자는 400여 인 大隈씨 외 4명 對外 硬主義 說述하고 또 후보자는 硬派로부터 선출할 것이라고 연설하다.
O 오라 劉永福 淸佛 교전시 其 名을 擧한 黑旗軍의 수령 劉永福은 這回 日淸 교섭 사건 起하자 자조 李鴻章에게 書를 보내여 我兄弟兄 (弟란 말은 劉가 장발군의 將이든 때부터 제휴한 부하를 가르침) 自 今 10년을 지나면 전투의 用에 서지 못할 것이외다. 원컨대 일본병과 싸와서 天朝의 특은에 답코저 한다고 원하지만 李는 아직 不許 然이나 양국의 和 깨여지면 李는 반듯이 劉의 원을 許하리라. 劉의 진퇴는 써 淸國  和戰의 實을 決하는 것이라고 모 淸人은 말하다. 劉銘傳, 吳大微, 劉永福 이런 다수 邦人 간에 알리워진 자를 擔出하여 虛喝의 資에 供하려 한다. 彼等 얼마나 되는가. 1인 2인은 부족하다. 모든 용장을 택하여 한꺼번에 오라. 일본병의 실력 여하를 알리워 주리라.

日露 戰役
O 近事 片片 戰局 장래에 관하여 노력을 期하는 此 旨 국민에 傳知하히라고 東鄕 대장의 言 국민 正聽하다.
O 林 公使의 알현 22일 오후 0시 在 京城 志村 특파원 發 林全權 公使 萩原 서기관은 (21일 오후 4시로부터 2시간 반 동안이나 장시간의 회견을 하다. 폐하는 懇篤히 林 公使와 我國을 신뢰하시는 意를 示하옵시고 公使는 시국 전체에 대하여 奏上한 바 인자 동시에 我 제국의 對韓 방침에 관하여 韓廷의 오해가 없도록 耬耬 奏上하 바 있다. 그 결과 근간 내각 개조를 볼 것 갔다.
O 旅順의 海戰 (23일 오전 9시 在 芝罘大彌 통신원 發) 22일 朝靑雲窪에서 지금 當港에 입항한 장크로부터 소식에 의하면 21일 夜半으로부터 今朝 3시까지 旅順海에 日 군함 及 雷艇 40여 척이 있다. 其中 큰 군함은 黃金山 砲臺를 공격하고 水雷艇은 露艦과 싸호고 있음으로 멀리 探海燈의 照射에 의하여 알 수가 있었다. 此에 의하여 보면 我 水雷艇 及 逐艦隊는 군함 援護 하에 야습을 단행하지 않었는가 생각한다.＜140＞
