Article Title: 米國 할리웃드 風景 (Scenery of Hollywood, USA)
Author: 「聖林」에서 崔承喜 (Choe Sŭnghŭi from "Sŏngnim" [Holy Forest, i.e., Hollywood])
Article Type: 문예기타 (Literary/Arts Misc.)

Greetings to my brothers and sisters in the homeland.

Thanks to everyone's help and love, I crossed the Pacific Ocean, came to the United States (米國), and after already visiting Los Angeles (로샌젤스), Washington (華盛頓), and New York (紐育), I arrived a few days ago in Hollywood (할리웃도), the movie capital of the world.

Since coming to the United States (米國), I cannot cease to be amazed at how astonishingly grand the movie business is. They say that movies, like the automobile business, are likewise one of the three major industries of the United States (米國).

I can only marvel at how Hollywood's (할리웃드) movie facilities are extremely exquisite (極美), having exhausted human effort.

Today, I met Robert Taylor (로- 버트, 테일너) here, and also Franchot Tone (푸랜죹, 톤), who was familiar from "The Lives of a Bengal Lancer" (벤갈의 創騎兵). They were all cheerful and fine men (好男兒).

I will save the details for a separate letter (別便) and end here for now (爲先).＜162＞

**Summary**

This brief article is a letter from the famous Korean dancer Choe Sŭnghŭi, written from Hollywood in 1938. She greets her compatriots back home, recounts her travels across the US (Los Angeles, Washington D.C., New York) culminating in Hollywood, expresses awe at the scale and sophistication of the American film industry, and mentions meeting movie stars Robert Taylor and Franchot Tone. For historians, the article is interesting as it showcases a prominent Korean cultural figure traveling and engaging with the West, specifically Hollywood, during the Japanese colonial period. It reflects an awareness of global popular culture and provides a glimpse into the experiences and perspectives of a Korean celebrity navigating the world stage at a time when Korea itself was under colonial rule, offering a subtle counterpoint to the restrictions faced within the peninsula.

米國 할리웃드 風景
						「聖林」에서 崔承喜
고국 게신 여러 형제께 문안드리나이다. 저는 여러분께서 도와주시고 사랑하여 주시는 속에서 태평양을 건너 米國에 와서 이미 「로샌젤스」와 「華盛頓」과 「紐育」을 두루 걸처 몇칠 전 세계의 영화의 서울인 「할리웃도」로 왔나이다.
米國 와본즉 영화 사업이 놀납게 굉장한데는 놀나기를 마지 않었읍니다. 자동차 사업이나 마천가지로 영화가 米國의 3대 산업의 하나라고 합니다.
할리웃드의 영화 시설이 인력을 다한 극진 極美한 데는 오직 경탄할 뿐이외다.
저는 오늘 여기에서 「로- 버트, 테일너」를 맞났고 또 「벤갈의 創騎兵」에서 나치 익든 「푸랜죹, 톤」을 맞났읍니다. 모다 쾌활하고 好男兒들이더이다. 자세한 것은 別便으로 돌리기로 하고 爲先 여기에 끈침니다.＜162＞
