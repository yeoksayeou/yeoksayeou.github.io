Principle of National Sanctity

The newspaper *Giornale d'Italia*, based on the advocacy of the Italian Ministry of Culture, published "The Fascist Racial Creed," written by Italy's Summer University professors. According to this, Italy will soon not recognize Jews as genuine Italians. This racial creed consists of the following 10 articles:

(1) The nation exists in human society. (2) Among nations, there exist great nations and small nations. (3) The concept of the nation must stand purely on a biological basis. (4) The majority of the Italian people are of the Aryan race. (5) The migration of peoples is purely legendary in history. (6) A pure Italian nation strictly exists. (7) The time has arrived for the Italian people to clearly declare themselves as nationalists. (8) A clear distinction must be drawn between Western races, Eastern races, and African races. (9) Jews do not belong to the Italian nation. (10) Italians must maintain, to the very end, the qualities of being purely Western both physically and mentally.

Furthermore, this Fascist racial creed emphasizes that it is not an import of Germany's racial policy. While Germany's policy stands on philosophical and religious grounds, Italy's nationalism is built purely on a biological foundation. <105>

**Summary**

This article reports on the "Fascist Racial Creed" published in Italy in 1938, outlining its ten points. These points assert the biological basis of nations, identify Italians as primarily Aryan, deny historical migrations, claim the existence of a pure Italian nation, call for nationalist self-declaration, mandate racial distinctions (Western, Eastern, African), explicitly exclude Jews from the Italian nation, and demand the preservation of "pure Western" traits. The article concludes by highlighting the Italian claim that this policy, unlike Germany's, is based purely on biology, not philosophy or religion. For historians of colonial Korea, this article is interesting as it shows Korean awareness of contemporary fascist ideologies and racial theories spreading globally. It provides context for understanding how ideas of race, nation, and purity were being discussed internationally, potentially influencing or offering points of comparison with Japanese colonial rhetoric and policies regarding race and national identity in Korea and the wider empire.

民族神聖原則
죠나래, 듸따랴紙는 伊太利 文化省 提唱에 依한 伊太利의 夏季 大學敎授가 執筆한 『파시스트의 民族信條』라는 것을 게재하였는데 이것에 의하면 이태리도 不遠하여 유대人을 진정한 이태리인을 인정치 안는다는 右 민족신조는 左의 10개조로 되었다.
(1) 인류사회에 민족이란 것이 존재해 있다. (2) 민족 중에는 대민족, 소민족이란 것이 존재한다. (3) 민족이란 관념은 順然한 생물학적 근거 우에 서지 않으면 아니된다. (4) 이태리 국민의 대부분은 아리안 인종으로 되었다. (5) 민족의 이동이란 것은 역사상 순수한 전설적인 것이다. (6) 순수한 이태리 민족이란 것은 엄연히 존재하고 있다. (7) 이태리 국민이 민족주의자로서의 자신을 명확왕게 선언할 시기가 도래하었다. (8) 서양 인종, 동양 인종, 아푸리까 인종이란 것의 사이에 명확한 구별이 지어지지 않으면 아니 된다. (9) 유대인은 이태리민족에 소속하지 않었다. (10) 心身 共히 순수한 서양인의 소질이란 것을 이태리인은 끗까지 유지치 않으면 아니 된다.
더욱히 이 파시스트 민족신조는 독일의 민족정책을 수입한 것이 아니다. 독일의 그것은 哲學的 宗敎的 근거 우에 있지마는 이태리의 민족주의는 순수한 생물학적 토대 우에 축성되었다는 점을 강조하고 있다. ＜105＞
