Publication Date: 1930년 10월 01일
Article Title: 印度民族運動 (Indian National Movement)
Author: Not specified
Article Type: 소식 (News/Report)

India is currently groaning under the exploitation and oppression of being a British colony, and thus a revolutionary movement is underway seeking to break free from the rule of British Imperialism (英帝國主義) and achieve national liberation. Since the Great European War (歐洲大戰), this movement has become extremely fierce, with riots, strikes, boycotts, etc., frequently erupting throughout all of India. The slogans of the Indian national movement are:

1.  The establishment of complete national independence and a democratic republic based on universal suffrage (一般決議權).
2.  Confiscation (沒收) of large landholdings.
3.  Abolition (廢止) of land rent (地租), tenant fees (小作料), and indirect taxes (間接稅), and the imposition (負課) of a high-rate progressive income tax (高率累進所得稅).
4.  Modernization of agriculture with state aid (補助).
5.  Nationalization of assets related to the public interest and industrialization of flatlands (平地) with state aid.
6.  Establishment (制定) of an 8-hour workday and minimum wage (最低賃銀).

**Summary**

This 1930 news report in the Korean magazine *Samch'ŏlli* details the ongoing Indian independence movement against British colonial rule. It notes the intensification of the movement after World War I, characterized by riots, strikes, and boycotts. The article lists the movement's key demands: complete independence, a democratic republic with universal suffrage, land reform (confiscation of large estates), tax reform (abolition of certain taxes, progressive income tax), state-supported agricultural modernization and industrialization, and labor rights (8-hour day, minimum wage). For historians of Korean colonial history, this article is interesting as it reflects Korean awareness of and potential inspiration from other anti-colonial movements, particularly India's struggle against the British Empire. The specific demands listed, covering political, economic, and social reforms, likely resonated with Koreans facing similar issues under Japanese rule, providing a comparative perspective on anti-imperialist goals in Asia during this period.

印度民族運動
인도는 지금 영국의 식민지로 착취와 억압에 신음하고 잇슴으로 英帝國主義의 지배로부터 버서나서 민족해방을 달성하려고 하는 혁명운동을 하는 중으로 歐洲大戰 后 매우 치열하야 全印度의 폭동 파업 불매동맹 등이 작고 이러나고 잇다. 인도민족운동의 슬로-간은
1. 완전한 민족적 독립 幷一般決議權을 기초로 한 민주주의적 공화국의 수립.
2. 大土地所有의 沒收
3. 地租, 小作料, 間接稅의 廢止 及 高率累進所得稅의 負課
4. 국가의 補助의 의한 농업의 근대화
5. 공익에 관계한 재산의 민족화 국가의 보조에 의한 평지의 공업화
6. 8시간 노동과 最低賃銀의 제정
