文豪 떠스터엡스키 作의 映畵, 罪와 罰 (Film adaptation of Literary Giant Dostoevsky's work, Crime and Punishment)

Raskol'nikov was a talented university graduate, but because his skills were unsuited for the world, he fell into poverty. He devoted himself to the study of criminology and sent the manuscript fees to his mother and sister. Pressed for rent money, he went to pawn his late father's watch, and there he came to know the unfortunate young woman, Sonya. His sister Antoniya, due to money, could not avoid marrying a dull and rough government official. Raskol'nikov, loving his sister, resolved to break off that unhappy marriage against all odds. With the confidence that "I alone am capable of perfectly executing a crime," he brutally murdered the old pawnbroker woman and seized her money. However, contrary to his cold intellect, a fierce conscience began to awaken.

The detective knew he was the criminal but could not arrest him due to lack of evidence. While despising the detective, Raskol'nikov agonized over the pangs of conscience. He went to Sonya, who was weeping in distress. Sonya, who loved him, gently explained and advised that liberation from that guilt would come from confessing everything and receiving punishment for the crime. Raskol'nikov, who had initially refused, ultimately could not overcome the torment in his heart and Sonya's true love, and confessed. On the day he was exiled to Siberia, Sonya also followed behind him like a shadow.<253>

**Summary**

This article summarizes the plot of the film adaptation of Dostoevsky's "Crime and Punishment." It recounts the story of Raskol'nikov, an impoverished but brilliant former student who commits murder based on his theories, his subsequent battle with guilt, his interactions with the detective Porfiry and the virtuous Sonya, and his eventual confession and exile to Siberia, followed by Sonya. From a historical perspective, this piece is interesting as it demonstrates the consumption and discussion of major Western cultural works (Russian literature via film) in colonial Korea during the 1930s. It highlights the themes of intellectualism, poverty, morality, and redemption resonating with the Korean audience of the time, showing cultural exchange and engagement despite the political situation.

文豪 떠스터엡스키 作의 映畵, 罪와 罰
라스콜니코프는 천재적 대학졸업생이였스나 기교가 世에 能치 못함으로 貧困에 陷落되엿다. 그는 범죄학 연구에 전념하야 그 원고료를 어머니와 누이에게 보내엿다. 그는 하숙비에 쫄니여 아버지가 물녀준 시계를 典當잡히려 갓다가 그곳에서 薄倖한 처녀 소-니야와 서로 알게 되엿다. 그의 누이 안토니아는 돈으로 말미아마 無味하고 粗暴만 一官吏와 결혼하지 않을 수 없게 되엇다. 누이를 사랑하는 라스콜니코프는 萬難을 무릅쓰고 그 불행한 결혼을 破棄할녀고 「범죄를 완전히 실행할 수 잇는 자는 내 혼자이다」라는 자신을 가지고 그는 전당포 노파를 慘殺하고 돈을 奪取하엿다. 然이나 그의 냉정한 理智에 反하야 激烈한 양심이 눈뜨기 시작하엿다.
탐정은 그가 범인인 줄은 아나 증거가 없어 捕縛하지 못하엿다. 라스콜니코프는 탐정을 경멸하면서도 양심의 가책에 煩悶하엿다. 그는 고민에 우는 소-야에게 갓다. 그를 사랑하는 소-야는 그 가책에서 해방됨에는 모든 것을 고백하야 죄에 대한 벌을 받음이라고 淳淳說明하며 勸告하엿다. 처음에는 그것을 거절하야오든 라스콜니코프도 끈내 마음의 번뇌와 소-니야의 진정한 사랑에 익이지 못하야 자수한다. 그가 시베리야에 流刑되는 날 소-니야도 또한 그림자와도 같이 그의 뒤를 딸어간다.<253>
