						Samch'ŏllisa
Ideological and Journalistic Circles

The Day Sin Tanje Came to Namdaemun Station Head

The remains of Tanje Sin Ch'aeho arrived at Namdaemun Station at 2 PM on February 24th and immediately departed for his hometown of Ch'ŏngju. The various figures who came to greet him at the station that day were as follows:
Kwŏn Tongjin, Hong Myŏnghŭi, Yŏ Unhyŏng, Sin Sŏgu, Sŏ Ch'un, An Chaehong, Kim Tongwŏn, Pak Tonsŏ, Sin Sang'u, Yi Kwan'gu, Chŏng Inbo, Wŏn Sehun, Yi Taewi, Kim Yaksu, Hyŏn Tongwan, Chu Ik, Yu Chint'ae, Sŏ Chŏnghŭi, Kim Tonghwan, and several dozen others. It is said that among them were many who had lived together during the Peking (北京) and Shanghai (上海) days. Furthermore, Mr. Wŏn Sehun escorted the remains down to the Ch'ŏngju hometown. Those who went to Ryojun (旅順 - Port Arthur) were his wife, Madam Pak Chahye, his eldest son Su Pŏm, and Sŏ Sech'ung as a representative of his friends.

Condolence Money from Various Figures

Upon hearing the news of the passing of historian Sin Tanje,
Sin Sŏgu: 250 Won
Tong'a Ilbo's Song Chinu: 50 Won
Chung'ang Ilbo's Yŏ Unhyŏng: 50 Won
Chosŏn Ilbo's Pang Ŭngmo: 20 Won
Samch'ŏllisa's Kim Tonghwan: Several Won
Condolence money was received from these figures, and the funeral<26> was held.

Government-General's Monthly Salary: Three Million Won

The place in Chosŏn that pays the most in monthly salaries (or annual salaries) is the Government-General (總督府) on Kwanghwamun Street in Seoul. How much does the total amount come to (this naturally combines all Chosŏn government officials and public servants)? It is 3,330,000 Won per month, about 40,000,000 Won per year. How many people receive this? About 46,000 people. However, the amount of salary received by Chosŏn officials among them is about 8,000,000 Won out of the 40,000,000 Won.

Ideological Converts and the Paegakhoe

Who Gathered?

Recently, an organization called "Paegakhoe" (白岳會) was formed in Seoul. This is a group where ideological converts gather, and simply put, its platform is based on repudiating things like Communism. Listing the names of its members will make everything clear, so let's list the names first:
Leaders are Yi Kakchong (former Government-General commissioned staff (囑託)), Kim Kyŏngsik (former Wŏnsan Labor Federation Chairman, released after serving prison time back then), Yi Hangbal (former Sin'ganhoe committee member, imprisoned several times), An Chun (served time as a Communist party member), Yu Kongsam (former Koryŏ Revolutionary Party member, served time), Han Sinkyo, Sin Pong'ok (Lawyer), Yi Pang (under preliminary trial (豫審) for an ideology case), Chŏng Hongkyo (formerly involved with the Boy Scouts (少年軍)). Besides them, Yun Hongjung, Yi Sŭngwŏn, Yun Kwiyŏng, Song Kwan,
Kim Yŏnhak, Pak Insŏn, Yi Kyŏngsik, Kim Inch'ang, Im Nakpin, Kim Tuhyŏng, and 2 others.
Looking at the activities of this Society:
One. Protection for temporary residents (寄留保護)
Two. Protection for returning residents (歸住保護) a. Arrangement (斡施) of place to return to b. Travel expense assistance
Three. Care for recuperation (療養保護) a. Arrangement (斡施) of recuperation b. Medical expense assistance
Four. Vocational guidance (職業輔導) a. Arrangement (斡施) of employment b. Support (助成) for business ventures c. Job skills training d. Loan and supply (貸補給) of industrial funds
Five. Arrangement (斡施) for returning to school/entering school
Six. Sending items into prison (差入)
Seven. Family relief a. Personal counseling b. Comfort visits c. Industrial guidance
Eight. Introduction and arrangement (斡旋) of defense lawyers
Nine. Holding self-cultivation meetings (修養會) and discussion meetings
Ten. Management of farms and agricultural schools (農園, 濃塾)
Eleven. Other related activities

How Much Funding Is There?

So where does the money needed to carry out these activities come from? For now, <27> I will answer that I know but don't know. Word has it that currently, they are using money procured and brought by Mr. Yi Kakchong.

Tosan's Itinerary

An Tosan (安島山) has been touring the mountains and rivers of the Honam and Ch'ungch'ŏng regions (湖嶺山川). He is reportedly planning to travel the mountains and rivers of the Hamgyŏng and P'yŏngan regions (關北山川) around May.

Five Thousand Students Studying in Tokyo

According to a Government-General survey, the total number of Chosŏn students studying abroad in Tokyo (東京) is 5,050. By school level, it is as follows:
University students: 1,151
Professional school students: 1,490
Secondary school students: 2,399

Manuscript Fees Surpass One Thousand Won

The literary scene (文運) in Chosŏn society is now greatly prosperous (大通), and various magazine companies and book publishers are all showing vitality. Investigating the amount of manuscript fees (原稿料) paid out monthly to writers and critics by several magazine companies publishing in Seoul:
Chosŏn Ilbo's "Chogwang" (朝光): Approx. 350 Won monthly
Tong'a Ilbo's "Sindonga" (新東亞): 180 Won
Same paper's "Sin Kajŏng" (新家庭): 120 Won
Chung'ang Ilbo's "Chungang" (中央): 160 Won
Sahae Kongnon (四海公論): 150 Won
Samch'ŏlli (三千里): Several Won
Including other magazines, it is said the total easily surpasses 1,000 Won.

Ewha, Now Fifty Years Old

Ewha Women's College (梨花女子專門), which has newly built splendid and luxurious school buildings in suburban Sinch'on and is beautifully raising the daughters (令孃) of the peninsular land, now (於焉) celebrates its 50th anniversary this spring. The school is currently underway with all sorts of preparations to hold a grand commemorative celebration around the end of May.

Financial World and Others

Three Hundred Thousand Won Gold Mine Goes to Noma

These days, many figures from Tokyo's (東京) financial and political circles are extending their hands into Chosŏn to engage in mining operations. Just recently, Noma Seiji (野間淸治), president of the Tokyo Hōchi Shinbun (東京報知新聞) and president of Kōdansha (講談社), bought a gold mine (金鑛) in Chŏngp'yŏng, South Hamgyŏng Province (咸南定平) for 300,000 Won and is currently operating it. <28> Mr. Noma reportedly owns 3 or 4 other mines in places like Ch'ungch'ŏng Province (忠淸道).

Ms. Ch'oe Sŭnghŭi in April

Dancer Ms. Ch'oe Sŭnghŭi will return to Chosŏn in early April, perform in five locations—Pusan, Taegu, Kyŏngsŏng, P'yŏngyang, and Sinŭiju—and then, invited (招聘) by the South Manchuria Railway (滿鐵), will immediately undertake a dance tour (舞踊行脚) in major cities like Fengtian (奉天 - Mukden), Dalian (大連), and Xinjing (新京 - Changchun). In the early summer month of May, she is invited (招聘) by Taiwan (臺灣) and will reportedly perform passionate tropical dances in places like Keelung (基隆), Tainan (臺南), and Taipei (臺北).

Six Thousand Won for the Film "Counterattack"

The Chosŏn film "Counterattack" (逆襲), produced with Ms. Pok Hyesuk as the lead actress and currently being screened in various locations, cost 6,000 Won to produce for its full 10 reels.

Prodigal Squandering: One Hundred Sixty Thousand Won

A sad trend these days that wrings tears from the eyes of concerned citizens is the scene in the pleasure quarters (花柳界) of Seoul's inner city (서울 장안). Seoul citizens, seemingly gripped by a fin-de-siècle mentality (世紀末적 思潮) where "Life is a three-foot zither" and "Worldly affairs are a cup of wine," seem determined to live only for today and weave spiderwebs in their mouths from tomorrow onwards. Whether they earn 10 Won or 20 Won, it's off to the restaurant, as prodigals indulge in idle dissolution (醉生夢死) in the streets of the pleasure quarters (狹斜의 거리). Look at the entrances of Myŏngwŏlgwan, Sikto'wŏn, Ch'ŏnhyangwŏn, Kugilgwan, Chosŏngwan, etc., these days. The phrase "a market forms before the gate" (門前成市) refers to this; as soon as evening falls (日暮西山), several dozen automobiles line up, and the sound of singing and strings (歌絃의 소리) continues ceaselessly until dawn.
Therefore, among the city's 800 registered gisaeng (4券番妓生), unless physically incapacitated, all are fully mobilized for banquets.
Because of this, how much money has flowed out for drinking parties? According to reliable sources, just the money spent on gisaeng fees (妓生花代) for about 10 days from around the 20th of the twelfth lunar month to the 10th of the first lunar month reached the huge sum of 160,000 Won. Adding the costs for food and automobiles, it is said to be at least (不下하리라 한다) three to four hundred thousand Won. Looking at this trend, how many people must weep for it to be right? The nights of Great Kyŏngsŏng (大京城) come and go amidst the deplorable waves of fin-de-siècle pleasure.

Number of Automobiles in Seoul Inner City

How many automobiles are there really in Seoul's inner city (서울 장안)?<29> Everything is lacking in this society. There should be several thousand, several tens of thousands!
However, even in the United States, they say the ratio averages one car per two people, and laborers drive their own automobiles home from the factory. Compared to such Western countries, it's nothing, but even in our Chosŏn, automobiles have increased quite a lot in just the past few decades.
Even since the March First Movement year (己未年 - 1919), Chosŏn people, especially the general populace, couldn't even think of riding in an automobile. But recently, the number has grown year by year, and now in Seoul's inner city alone, with things like the "80 Chŏn Flat Rate" taxis, anyone of moderate means can afford to use automobiles. According to what was recently heard from certain quarters, the number of automobiles in Great Kyŏngsŏng is now approaching nearly 500. Compared to 20 or 30 years ago, it's a world of difference (天壤之差). Should we say that "Seoul" too is gradually receiving the benefits (惠澤) of civilization?<30>

**Summary**

This 1936 Samch'ŏlli article compiles various news snippets covering politics, society, culture, and economics in colonial Korea. It reports on the return of nationalist historian Sin Ch'aeho's remains, details Government-General salaries (highlighting the disparity between Japanese and Korean officials), profiles the newly formed Paegakhoe group for ideological converts from communism, lists the number of Korean students in Tokyo, notes rising manuscript fees indicating a lively publishing scene, mentions Ewha Women's College's 50th anniversary, reports on Japanese investment in Korean gold mines (Noma Seiji), outlines dancer Ch'oe Sŭnghŭi's upcoming tour, gives the budget for a Korean film, laments extravagant spending in Seoul's gisaeng houses, and tracks the increasing number of automobiles in Seoul. For historians, the article offers valuable quantitative data (salaries, student numbers, spending) and qualitative insights into colonial administration, the handling of dissent (ideological conversion), cultural activities under colonialism, Japanese economic penetration, and emerging modern social trends and anxieties in 1930s Seoul. The details on the Paegakhoe and the stark depiction of elite spending versus underlying societal conditions are particularly revealing.

申丹齊오는 날의 南大門驛頭

丹齊 申采浩의 유해는 2월 24일 오후 2시에 남대문역에 도착하엿다가 그 길로 그냥 淸州 고향을 향하야 출발하엿는데 당일 驛頭에 出迎한 諸人士는 아래와 갓했다.
權東鎭, 洪命熹, 呂運亨, 申錫雨, 徐椿, 安在鴻, 金烔元, 朴敦緖, 申常雨, 李寬求, 鄭寅普, 元世勳, 李大偉, 金若水, 玄東完, 朱翼, 兪鎭泰, 徐廷禧, 金東煥 등 수십氏로서 그 중에는 北京 上海 시대의 寢食을 함께 하든 분도 여러분이 잇섯다고 한다. 더욱 元世勳씨는 淸州故里까지 유해를 모시고 내려갓는바 旅順에 간 이는 그 부인 朴慈惠 여사와 長子 秀範, 友人代表로 徐世忠의 諸氏엇다고.

總督府의 월급三百萬圓

朝鮮 안에서 월급(혹은 연봉)을 가장 만히 지불하는 곳은 서울 光化門통에 잇는 總督府라, 그 총액이 모다 얼마나 되는가 하면(全朝鮮 官公吏 것을 모다 합하엿슴은 물론이다) 매월 3,330,000圓 1년 약 40,000,000圓이다. 이것을 몃사람이 타먹는가 하면 약 46,000여 명이다. 그런데 그 중에 朝鮮인 관리가 타먹는 봉급액은 40,000,000圓 중 약 8,000,000圓이다.

思想轉向者와 白岳會

누구누구가 모엿는가

蕩兒散財十六萬圓

요지간 有志者의 눈물을 쨔내게 하는 슬푼 경향은 서울 장안의 花柳界 光景이라. 서울 市民은 마치 「人生은 琴3尺」이요 「世事는 酒1杯」란 世紀末적 思潮에 모다 잡혀서 오늘만 살고 내일부터는 입에 거미줄 칠 작정인지 10圓이 생겨도 요리점, 20圓이 생겨도 요리점, 이라하야 蕩兒들은 모다 狹斜의 거리로 醉生夢死를 일삼는다. 보라 요지간의 明月舘, 食道園, 天香園, 國一舘, 朝鮮舘 등의 문전을, 門前成市란 이것을 가리친 것으로 日暮西山하기 밧부게 自働車가 여러 數10臺와 다이고, 그리고는 歌絃의 소리는 새벽까지 끈칠줄 모른다.
그래서 장안 800名의 4券番妓生은 병신이 아닌 이상 모다 宴席으로 총출동이다.
이 까닭에 얼마나한 돈이 酒宴代로 흘넛는고-확실한 측의 조사에 의하면 음력 섯달 스무날께부터 정월 열흘까지 약 10일 동안에 妓生花代로 나간 돈만 160,000圓의 巨額에 달하고 거기 料理代, 自働車代 합치면 三四十萬圓을 不下하리라 한다. 이 경향을 바라보고, 몃사람이 울어야 올흘고, 大京城의 밤은 歡樂의 世紀末의 可歎할 물결 속에 오고가고 한다.

서울 長安의 自動車數

서울 장안에 있는 自動車數는 과연<29>얼마나 되나? 모든 것이 변변치 못한 이 社會이다. 많으면 멫1,000臺, 멫10,000臺가 되가야 하련만!
그러나 米國만 하드래도 平均 두 사람 앞에 한 臺式의 比例는 된다고 하며 로동자들이 工場에서 자긔집으로 돌아갈 때도 제 自動車를 운전해서 돌아가는 형편이라고 하니, 그러한 西洋의 나라들에는 比할배 아니지만 우리 朝鮮만 하드래도 이제 不過 數十年來에 自動車가 퍽으나 많이 늘어난 셈이라.
己未年以來만 하드래도 朝鮮사람 더구나 一般民衆들까지 自動車를 탈야고는 생각지도 못하든 것이 요사히에 와서는 해마다 늘어서 이제 서울 長安만 하드래도 「80錢均」이니 해서 웬만한 사람이면 누구든지 이 自動車의 身勢를 지게 되게끔 되엿는데 近者에 어떠한 方面에서 들은 바로는 大京城의 自動車數는 인제 近近 500대 가까히 되여간다고 하니 2, 30년 전과 비하면 天壤之差이다. 이것을 가지고 「서울」도 차츰 文明의 惠澤을 입는다고나 할는지?<30>
