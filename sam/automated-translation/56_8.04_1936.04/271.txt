기사제목	나비의 탄식 (Sigh of the Butterfly)
필자	金岸曙
기사형태	시 (Poem)

나비의 탄식
						金岸曙
송이송이 장다리 노란닙에
사운사운 하로를 실비가오네.

비에걸녀 이몸은 그리쓸쓸이
어득어득 이저녁 헤매노라고
나비는 날며날며 탄식을하네.＜135＞

Sigh of the Butterfly
						Kim Ansŏ
On the clusters of yellow petals (노란닙) of the shepherd's purse (장다리),
Softly, softly (사운사운), a light rain falls all day long (하로).

Caught in the rain (비에걸녀), this body, oh so lonely (그리쓸쓸이),
Wandering dimly, dimly (어득어득) through this evening (이저녁),
The butterfly flies and flies (날며날며), letting out sighs (탄식). <135>

**Summary**

This short poem by Kim Ansŏ depicts a butterfly caught in a gentle, persistent rain falling on shepherd's purse flowers. The butterfly expresses its loneliness and despair as it struggles to fly through the dim evening. The poem uses simple nature imagery to evoke a mood of melancholy and helplessness. For historians, the poem offers a glimpse into the lyrical sensibilities and common themes (nature, melancholy) in Korean poetry during the colonial period. While not explicitly political, the feeling of being trapped and desolate might be interpreted by some as reflecting the broader atmosphere of the time.
